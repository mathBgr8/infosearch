Droon
1526

Droon was an Imperial admiral who helped Frija Torlock try to rule Corulag by killing Wrenga Jixton. After discovering the plot, Jixton handed Droon to Darth Vader who forced him into a duel with a lightsaber, where he was defeated by Vader and executed. *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy admirals of the Galactic Empire Category:Males
