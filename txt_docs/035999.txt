Kas tank
1549

The Kas tank was a repulsorcraft tank model employed by the Wookiees. It had a crew of two, a pilot in the front cockpit and a gunner in the rear cockpit. It was armed with a heavy laser cannon mounted outside the cockpit, and was effective against enemy armor. The Kas tank also had room for transporting more troopers. In 1 ABY, when Chief Attichitcuk assisted Leia Organa and Echuu Shen-Jon in retrieving the Vor'Na'Tu during the Krantian Civil War, his contingent of Wookiee warriors included Kas tanks. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Hovertank models Category:Wookiee vehicle classes
