Turu-grass
760

Turu-grass was a vegetal species from planet Shili. The wild scrub lands of Shili were covered with meter-high turu-grass, which was red on one side and white on the other side. This color combination provided many species, including the Togruta, with excellent cover and camouflage. *Star Wars Insider 67 *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species *The Force Unleashed Campaign Guide Category:Grasses Category:Togruta culture
