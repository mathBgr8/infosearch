Zu Pike
3807

Zu Pike was a female Epicanthix and the twin sister of Zan Pike. The only way to tell them apart was the fact that Zan had green eyes and Zu had a green eye and a blue eye. thumb|left|180px|Zu Pike The Pike sisters were born in 23 BBY, the daughters of famous Teräs Käsi fighter Dux Pike. He died when they were infants, just before the start of the Clone Wars. Their mother was a noblewoman of Bunduki. She placed the twins, as children, in the hands of Followers of Palawa for their education. Zan and Zu went on to become Teräs Käsi masters themselves, and criminals for the highest bidder. They competed in illegal fighting matches across the galaxy. In 3 ABY, Xizor had them hired to work for Black Sun. Following his death, they signed on to work with Spinda Caveel, who sent them to acquire A-OIC and, later, to re-acquire him and the Human replica droid Guri. On this latter expedition, they encountered the Heroes of Yavin, and decided to cut their losses, quitting their employment with Caveel. When he drew his blaster to keep them on task, they killed him.
