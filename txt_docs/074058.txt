Chaos trooper
683

Chaos troopers served the Krath during the time of the Great Sith War. During the rise of the Krath cult, many followers were brainwashed by the teachings of Satal Keto to become willing suicide warriors who would pilot the Chaos Fighter into enemy ships. They served as living missiles and this they did gladly. *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 2 Category:Krath culture
