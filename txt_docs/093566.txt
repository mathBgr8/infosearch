Cranscoc
1101

The Cranscoc were an insectoid species from the planet Cartao who controlled Spaarti Creations. They reproduced slowly, and communicated by changing colors. A sub-species known as Twillers controlled the strange fluid technology that earned Spaarti Creations their profits. *The New Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Expansion Region sentient species Category:Insectoid sentient species
