Grodo
1059

Grodo was the Hutt physician of Aruk Besadii Aora and he had advised the elderly crimelord to keep better care of himself by exercising and eating nutritiously. Unfortunately, Aruk had been poisoned by Teroenza acting under orders from Jabba and Jiliac.
