Fidge Tadrazan
1508

Fidge Tadrazan was a young Biituian who befriended R2-D2 and C-3PO at the time of their encounter with the Great Heep on the planet Biitu. He had a reptilian pet and companion named Chubb. He aided the droids in rescuing their owner, Mungo Baobab, from the Imperials stationed on the planet to monitor the Great Heep. After freeing Mungo, they worked to destroy the Great Heep and drive the Imperials from Biitu. *George Lucas: The Creative Impulse *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Biituians Category:Males
