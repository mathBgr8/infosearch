|subscribers=November 23, 2004 |pages=100 |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=79 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 79 was the seventy-ninth issue released of Star Wars Insider. It was released in January and February in 2005 under two separate covers. It began shipping to subscribers on November 23, and hit newsstands on December 7. *"Letter from the Editor" *"Comlink" *"Skywalking" *"Jedi Library" &mdash; One Picture at a Time *"Drawn by the Force" &mdash; Clone Wars Villains Face Their Fates: Days of Reckoning *"Technical Readout" &mdash; Theed Royal Palace *"Ask the Master" &mdash; Six Degrees of Antilles, and You Must Unlearn What You Have Learned *"Scouting the Galaxy" &mdash; Would I Take a Trip On The Way-Back Machine? *"Anchorhead Arcade" Star Wars Galaxies: Jump to Lightspeed *"Jabba's Collection" &mdash; Episode III Toys: The Frenzy Is About To Begin *"Best of Hyperspace" &mdash; "Didn't We Just Leave This Party?" *Bantha Tracks Vol. 10 *"Laugh It Up, Fuzzball" *"Prequel Update" *:After a busy summer of filming, the postproduction crew at Industrial Light & Magic can officially begin to take care of business. *"The Wookiees Walk Amongst Us" *:Finally, five films and 27 years later, the Wookiees get their due on the big screen.
