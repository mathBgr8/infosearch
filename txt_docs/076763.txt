Hutt Consulate
967

The Hutt Consulate was the diplomatic embassy of the Hutt Cartel to the New Republic. It was located in Valorum Tower. Golga Besadii Fir was the final Hutt ambassador to Coruscant. When the Hutts announced that they would be working with the Yuuzhan Vong, the Hutt Consulate was targeted for a series of attacks, ranging from the water and power to get cut off, to bomb threats. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse Category:Coruscant government buildings Category:Embassies Consulate
