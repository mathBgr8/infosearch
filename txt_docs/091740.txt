Dreambeast
1309

Dreambeasts were strange creations made through the dark side of the Force. The appearance of such beasts was never the same, as they took on the shape of what the opponent feared most in their mind. This allowed it to take on a wide variety of appearances. When the creatures were killed, the physical wounds they left on their opponent would seem to disappear, suggesting that the creatures inflict a form of telepathic pain that borders on the real and the imaginary.
