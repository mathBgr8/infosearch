Razoral Reefs
557

The Razoral Reefs were a collection of artificial reefs that formed in the ruins of Derem City, on the planet Kamino. Anchored by the city's sunspires, the Razoral Reefs provided a refuge for some of Kamino's aquatic lifeforms, especially those that were preyed upon by the ocean world's larger predators.Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Kamino locations
