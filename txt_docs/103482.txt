Abridon Government Center
1445

The Abridon Government Center was the capitol building of Abridon and home of the Abridon Government Council. Three towers joined by a central disk, it sat on a hill named Government Mound in the middle of the capital city. Behind it was a sheer, craggy cliff face that led down to a river that curved around the hill. In front of it lay well-tended gardens. Its gardens were marred, however, when General Brenn Tantor set up an Imperial command center at the foot of the hill after retaking the Government Center from Rebels who had captured it only days before.
