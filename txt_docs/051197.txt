Squibbian
1151

Squibbian was the native language of the Squib species from Skor II. Most Squibs knew both Squibbian and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. However, they only managed to get a limited grasp on Basic. As a result, a Squib seems inarticulate when speaking Basic. Their sentences in Squib have surface and deeper meanings, and at least one of these (usually the surface meaning) is lost when translated to Basic, except for another Squib.
