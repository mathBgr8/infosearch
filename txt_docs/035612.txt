Troy Vigil
2390

Troy Vigil is an artist who has worked on a number of Star Wars works including several Essential Guides, trading cards, portfolio sets, and posters. Troy began drawing and was inspired by the Star Wars trilogy at the age of eight to pursue an art career. His professional career started in print production before he became a freelance computer artist. Troy currently lives in Venice Beach, California with his partner, Stacey.
