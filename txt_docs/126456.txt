Mawshunba Zee
1355

Mawshunba Zee was a male Dug who served under Sebolto's employ during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He also worked for Sesstian Hesser. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by the Galactic Wildlife Liberation Front for the illegal breeding of wild Nexu.
