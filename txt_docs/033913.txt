CSL Danenberger
1555

CSL (SEE eh sel) Danenberger was a Force-sensitive Human from the planet Kyrouac. thumb|right|CSL Danenberger in a bar brawl. CSL Danenberger had always dreamed of becoming a Jedi Knight, though the youth was thought of as crazy by other Kyrouaquians. When the Rebel Alliance sent diplomats to Kyrouac in an attempt to get them to supply them with food supplies, it was Danenberger who warned them of the treachery of one of the Kyrouaquian High Council members. After Danenberger informed them of this, they agreed to take the youth off the planet.
