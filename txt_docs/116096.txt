Naboo Underground
2550

The Naboo Underground was a Resistance cell filled primarily by students from the Royal House of Learning. It opposed the Trade Federation during their occupation of Naboo. The group conducted several operations within the city of Theed during the occupation, and eventually joined forces with Queen Amidala to participate in the Second Battle of Theed. Many of the members worked together long after the Naboo's victory against the invasion. The Naboo underground conducted a number of successful operations against the occupying forces, primarily involving weapons theft and liberation of prisoners. In one daring operation, they liberated the Naboo Minister of Culture, Kyu Tane.
