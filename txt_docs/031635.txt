The Arch
682

The Arch was one of the major skyways found within the city of Efavan, capital of the planet Vorzyd V. It circled the Old Bronzan district of the city, and carried passengers from the Farain Skyway to other parts of the city. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Roadways Category:Vorzyd V locations
