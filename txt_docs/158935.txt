|terrain= |fauna=Rock beetles |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} The Rock Beetle Refuge was an area in the Dune Sea of Tatooine where a large population of rock beetles lived. During the Galactic Civil War, Porcellus hired a spacer to go to this location in order to kill several rock beetles and bring him their carcasses to cook beetle broth for Jabba the Hutt. The Rock Beetle Refuge was a location in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The Rock Beetle Refuge was added to the game as part of the revamped "Jabba's Theme Park," released with the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
