Pho Ph'eahese
918

Pho Ph'eahese was the native language of the Pho Ph'eahian species from Pho Ph'eah. The language was composed of squeaks, squeals and high-pitched barks. Its written form used an intricate set of characters to create a syllabary. Most Pho Ph'eahian were fluent in both Pho Ph'eahese and Basic, being literate in both languages. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
