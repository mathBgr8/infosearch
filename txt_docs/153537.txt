Vohis mold
1526

Vohis mold was a local export found on the planet Gelgelar available at Gelgelar Free Port. It was used to enhance the flavor of various dishes, including nerf casserole, Ruugian noodles, and Vohan bean stew. It grew in the swamps and was harvested by local farmers. They sold it to Brack who would hire people to ship the moist mold to companies like Nebula Consumables and Falvin Foods who would dry and process it.
