Bordhell-type fuel slug

Bordhell-type fuel slugs were a form of fuel used extensively by the empire of Xim the Despot. Though long-obsolete by 0 BBY, in their time they provided Xim's warships with unmatched range and speed. *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Fuels Category:Xim's empire
