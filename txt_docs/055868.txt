Imperial Bounty 82,471
1224

Imperial Bounty #82,471 was the designation number of the Imperial bounty that was posted for the head of Adar Tallon shortly after the Battle of Yavin. The bounty was issued by Captain Ranif Parlan, and the bounty hunters who were attempting to collect on it included Jodo Kast, Zardra, Puggles Trodd and IG-72. However, Tallon managed to escape all of them, eventually joining the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Captain Parlan was executed by Darth Vader because of his failure to ensure Tallon's capture. *Tatooine Manhunt Category:Legal documents
