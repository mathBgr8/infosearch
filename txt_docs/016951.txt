*ArrowheadThe Client States and Miscellaneous Regions map in The Essential Atlas places the entirety of the grid square M-10 portion of the Core Worlds in the Arrowhead. |sector=Demophon sector |system=Demophon systemSupernova |coordinates=M-10 |xyz= |routes= |distance= |orbits=#Demophon #*Nestro #Zenobah #Jatee #Enarc #Derr |stations= |asteroids= |other= |class=Formerly red giant |diameter= }} Demophon was the primary star of the Demophon system. A red giant near the end of its life, it exploded as a supernova, destroying the system during the Galactic Civil War. In 22 BBY, scientists at the University of Sanbra analyzed the star, with the findings released 13:3:14. Cosmology Professor Deakis Ronse explained that they found Demophon to have chromospheric instabilities and spectrographic readings of iron and carbon ejecta. Its life expectancy was deemed short, to end in a supernova within the century (50 to 75 standard years).
