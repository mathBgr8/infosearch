thumb|right|250px|The Tenebrous The Tenebrous was a Unidentified warship class (Sith Empire) of the Sith Empire that served as the base of the remnants of Imperial Intelligence during Cipher Nine's efforts to bring down the Star Cabal. Operating illegally after the dissolution of Imperial Intelligence, Imperial military databases contained no crew or mission records for the ship. *Star Wars: The Old Republic Category:Starships of the resurgent Sith Empire
