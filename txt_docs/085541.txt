Night Lady
1980

The Night Lady was a starship belonging to the Yaka pirate known as Redstar. Six years after the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War, Jaina Solo, Lowbacca, and Tesar Sebatyne boarded the Night Lady. After the crew failed in their attempt to turn the Jedi away, they pulled blasters on the Jedi, and were subdued by their lightsabers. The Jedi then brought Redstar to the maximum security space station MaxSec Eight to stand trial in front of a Reconstruction Authority inquisitor, Athadar Gyad, who asked about what had occurred on the Night Lady.
