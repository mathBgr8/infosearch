27th Denarian Fleet
1118

The 27th Denarian Fleet was a formation of the Imperial Navy which included 520 Victory-class Star Destroyers. When the Empire decided to sell off all the Victory-class starships in the fleet to make room for the newer Imperial-class Star Destroyers, the ships were stripped of weapons and sold off to the Corporate Sector Authority's Picket Fleet. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia 27
