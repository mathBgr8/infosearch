Malrev IV was a focal point for the dark side that served as a vital dark side hub for the Sith. It was the site of several battles during the New Sith Wars. In 1750 BBY, the Jedi Order and Mandalorian mercenaries defeated the forces of the Dark Underlord on the planet. Several centuries later, another battle took place at the beginning of the Light and Darkness War.The Essential Atlas It had a Sith Temple on it visited by Palpatine.
