|pages=22 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Infinities |issue=4 |next=last }} Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope 4 is the fourth and final issue in the Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope series of comics. It was published on November 21, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. In this altered Star Wars timeline, Luke Skywalker fails to destroy the Death Star, and the Rebellion is seemingly crushed. Overcoming his raging emotions, Luke escapes to become a Jedi Master after finding Yoda and completing his training. Imperial forces thrive throughout the galaxy, and Leia Organa undergoes a dark transformation while Luke plunges headlong into the heart of the Empire in a final attempt to rekindle the Rebellion. Brother battles sister, and a dying Jedi Master squares off against an entire world in this final issue.
