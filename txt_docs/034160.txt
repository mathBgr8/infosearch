Christopher Neame
1449

Christopher Neame played Jerec in Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II. Most depictions of Jerec are based on his appearance. The only source that depicts Jerec as somewhat thin&mdash;at least thinner than Neame&mdash;is the New Essential Guide to Characters, which also depicts Jerec as completely bald although Neame was only partially so.
