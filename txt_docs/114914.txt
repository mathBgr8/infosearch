Fondor (star)
2092

Fondor was the primary star of the Fondor system. In 26 ABY, Centerpoint Station was fired by Thrackan Sal-Solo, sending a colossal, thousand-kilometer wide beam of gravitic energy through the Fondor system. Had the beam struck the star, a supernova would have occurred.
