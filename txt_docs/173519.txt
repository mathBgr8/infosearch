C440
1017

C440 was a group of three Class-C cargo containers near the Calamari Cruiser Lulsla. During the first phase of the assault by the Galactic Empire and according to order by the Cloaked Figure, pilot Maarek Stele identified their contents and found them to contain stolen Imperial war equipment. They were eventually destroyed along with the cruiser.
