Nazaar
1305

thumb|right|200px|Maarek Stele inspecting Shuttle "Nazaar 1" Nazaar was a group of two Lambda-class T-4a shuttles stolen by Ripoblus refugees during the Sepan Civil War. Nazaar 1 and 2 escorted IPV-1 System Patrol Craft Glas as the refugees requested asylum from Admiral Harkov's forces. The shuttles, along with Maarek Stele, and other Imperial starfighters, protected SPC Glas from Ripoblus and Dimok attackers while it docked with Victory-class Star Destroyer Protector.
