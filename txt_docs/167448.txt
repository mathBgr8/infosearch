Wartaki
694

Wartaki was the name of an island continent on the planet of Kashyyyk. It was most likely a large part of the Wartaki Islands. The dialect Xaczik, a difficult dialect, was developed on Wartaki to confuse and/or fool the Wookiees' Imperial slave masters in the Galactic Civil War. Category:Continents Category:Kashyyyk locations
