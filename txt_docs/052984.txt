Wrokix Works
1209

Wrokix Works was a company that produced powered armor, specifically the AV-1 power armor. Another of their products was the Deluxe Boarding Armor which was equipped with multiple respiration systems, self-healing patches, communication devices, and armor plating to prevent possible breaches. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Personal gear companies
