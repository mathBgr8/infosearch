Muchardis Mulaxis
1236

Muchardis Mulaxis was a male Ayrou Senator of the New Republic who represented Maya Kovel and the Moddell sector up until 19 ABY. During the Caamas Document Crisis, he was skeptical of Borsk Fey'lya's claims that the Bothans did not know which clans bore responsibility for the destruction of Caamas. *Specter of the Past Category:Ayrou Category:Males Category:New Republic Senators
