Dalus Othona
2683

|masters= |apprentices= }} Dalus Othona was the Provost of the University of Alderaan in 22 BBY. is set on the date 13:3:14, which corresponds to 22 BBY per the reasoning here. That year, the university's Dean, Halcor Raystel, caused a three-month scandal when it came to light that he was addicted to ryll spice and had offered class credits in exchange for sexual favors to two female students. Following an investigation into the matter, Othona censured and suspended Raystel from office. Dalus Othona was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, an in-universe website published on March 14, 2002. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content.
