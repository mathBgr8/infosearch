Salespeak 7
673

The Salespeak 7 was a small protocol droid specifically designed to translate Shyriiwook into Basic. While useful in Wookiee galactic relations, misapplication by various nefarious factions occurred nonetheless. A group of aggressive Wookiee con-artists led to a scam utilizing the Salespeak 7's abilities to market a subscription to the Coruscant Journal. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Translator droids
