Yopaxtul
2276

A Vratix from the planet Thyferra, Yopaxtul was an athlete who was active during the last years of the Galactic Republic. In 22 BBY, Yopaxtul participated in the prestigious Mobquet Presents: Fastest Land Beings racing meet on Teyr, winning the one hundred meter hexaped race, setting a new speed record of 6.132 seconds in the process. Originally from the planet Thyferra in the Polith system, Yopaxtul was a member of the six-limbed, insectoid Vratix species and a sprinter active during the last years of the Galactic Republic. In 22 BBY, Yopaxtul competed in the prestigious Mobquet Presents: Fastest Land Beings racing meet, held annually in the city of Pyre Folas on the planet Teyr. The Vratix won the meet's one hundred meter hexaped race, setting a new speed record of 6.132 seconds in the process. For the Vratix's victory, Yopaxtul was awarded a medal, and later posed in a picture along with the meets' biped and quadruped race winners&mdash;the Teek Yeep and the Selonian Pumav, respectfully.
