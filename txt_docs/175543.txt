Terry (transports)
868

The designation Terry referred to a group of two Imperial cargo ferries. The ferries were assigned as part of the convoy Rope 17, which was dispatched to aid in the upgrading of the Deep Space Manufacturing Facility ZA-13. Terry carried foodstuffs for the personnel of the factory.
