Razor Coast
988

thumb|300px|The aftermath of the [[Battle of Razor Coast.]] The Razor Coast was a rocky shoreline on Jabiim. During the Clone Wars in the Battle of Jabiim, it was the headquarters for Confederate forces under Alto Stratus.
