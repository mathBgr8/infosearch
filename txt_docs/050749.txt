ChikatLik
1105

ChikatLik was the capital city of Ord Cestus. The name was a X'Tingian word meaning "the center." The city contained thousands of roads, but little aerial traffic, and was designed to blend its artificial structures perfectly with the volcanic terrain. Notable localities within the city included the Cleft Head wake-up house, the Night Shade, Ore Boulevard, and the Grand ChikatLik prison.
