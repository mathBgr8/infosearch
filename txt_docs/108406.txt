Zann Consortium Droid Works
1739

The Zann Consortium Droid Works was a droid manufacturing company operated by the Zann Consortium led by Tyber Zann. The Droid Works was a blending of Clone Wars-era Geonosian technology and new enhancements that allowed the production of droids such as the Droideka Mark II. The majority of the factory was underground, allowing the droids to be moved around through tunnels that exited to the surface.
