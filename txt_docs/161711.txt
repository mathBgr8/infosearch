Salliche Student Militia
567

The Salliche Student Militia was a military program on the planet Salliche designed to train young people. The students learned to fly Z-95 Headhunters during training, and could win scholarships to Imperial institutions. Ranna Gorjaye was once a member of the Student Militia, and earned a scholarship to the Raithal Academy. *The DarkStryder Campaign Category:Military units
