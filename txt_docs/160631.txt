Rodian batwing
1153

The Rodian batwing was a flying creature native to Rodia. Xaverri used a flock of Rodian batwings in her magic act, transporting them from on glassine cage to another in a burst of smoke and flame.The Hutt Gambit One of Palpatine's Senatorial critics claimed to be being chased by a flame-eyed, snake-tongued Rodian batwing when he was found on the roof of the Imperial Bank on Coruscant.
