The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 153
1301

The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 153 is the one hundred fifty-third issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Monthly series of comic reprints, published in January 1982. This issue features one of the few original comic stories in the series, "Dark Knight's Devilry".
