Star Wars Weekly 60
1275

Star Wars Weekly 60 is the sixtieth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 18, 1979. This issue includes "Pursuit!", the black & white fourth and final part of "The Kingdom of Ice". First published in color in Pizzazz magazine, "The Kingdom of Ice" was incomplete until its final part was printed here. It wasn't colored until its reprint in the first Marvel Illustrated Books volume from where Dark Horse Comics took it for its Star Wars Omnibus series. *"Pursuit!" *"The power of Warlock Part 7: Zero" *"Micronauts Part 5: A hunting we will go!" *"Tales of the Watcher: The unreal!" 60
