Electro photo receptor
3840

An electro photo receptor (EPR) was the simplest kind of sensor. It integrated information from normal light, ultraviolet, and infrared telescopes to create a composite hologram or two-dimensional image. The electro photo receptor was useful only at comparatively short ranges and was often used in targeting sensors.
