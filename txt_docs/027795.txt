thumb|right|180px|[[Ysanne Isard, last Director of Imperial Intelligence under the Galactic Empire before its collapse.]] Director of Imperial Intelligence, also known as the Internal Security Director, was a title held by the leader of Imperial Intelligence of the Galactic Empire's . The position was considered a successor to that of Director of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence, for when Palpatine established the Galactic Empire three years after the First Battle of Geonosis, the bureau became Imperial Intelligence. Under the Empire, this post&mdash;considered a part and in command of the Ubiqtorate&mdash;was the highest level of command within Imperial Intelligence, one of much power and prestige. The post is known to have been held by only three persons: Armand Isard, who was appointed to his position under the Galactic Republic by then-Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, Emperor's Hand Cronal, who filled in as an interim director after Armand's daughter Ysanne Isard executed her father, and eventually Ysanne Isard who was appointed in his place by Emperor Palpatine after Cronal's exile to the Outer Rim.
