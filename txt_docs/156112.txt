Gla
537

Gla were buildings used by the Yuuzhan Vong to breed amphistaffs. They were shaped like hexagons and had mica roofs. One such building resided on the Vongformed Coruscant in 28 ABY. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Building types Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
