Hyperspace pulsemass generator
1919

The hyperspace pulsemass generator was an experimental hyperspace weapon developed by the Galactic Empire. Developed by the IDMR, the hyperspace pulsemass generator worked by creating tiny spheres of hyperenergy and scattering them over a region of space. If a ship traveled through the area the generator was affecting, the hyperenergy spheres would quickly overwhelm its deflector shield and destroy the ship. While effective, the flaw in the technology was that the hyperenergy spheres had a life span of only minutes and would dissipate quickly.
