Kauri
1708

Kauri was an Agave-class picket ship assigned by the New Republic to assist in the capture of the Teljkon Vagabond. It was attacked and badly damaged. Kauri was also the name of a server &mdash; or "Galaxy" &mdash; in Star Wars Galaxies which happens to be a mostly French server (during some parts of the day).
