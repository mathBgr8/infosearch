|interest=Toola Spaceport |population= |demonym= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Galactic Empire }} Ithaqua Station was a spaceport town on the icy world of Toola. It was known for its large central market and for being the site of the planet's main spaceport. Ithaqua Station was a small spaceport town/station, that floated on-top of a half-frozen body of water located somewhere on Toola. The town was home to a bustling central market, where rich slave owners were known to buy merchandise. The station was also home to the planet's main spaceport which could only be accessed by a lift, as it was located on top of a large cliff. Shortly after the Clone Wars, when Order 66 was issued by Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, a garrison of clone troopers, led by clone trooper commander Keller, was established in Ithaqua Station. The garrison was sent there in order to capture two Jedi fugitives who escaped the contingency order. Shortly after arriving at Ithaqua Station, Keller's unit began to thoroughly search the spaceport town and raid several locations, including a bar which was celebrating the end of the war. When commander Keller and his troopers raided the bar, an all out brawl began between the clones and citizens after one of the Jedi fugitives, Kai Hudorra, convinced the bar patrons to attack the clones.
