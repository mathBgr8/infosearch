Chris Parsons
2644

Chris Parsons (born 1960) is an actor who portrayed K-3PO, 4-LOM, R-3PO, and E-3PO, as well as Chase Wilsorr in Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back when he was 18 years old. In addition, Parsons acted as C-3PO's double and stand-in in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. He is also listed on a call sheet as a stand-in Y-wing pilot for Return of the Jedi.
