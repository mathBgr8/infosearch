Gymsnor-2 light freighter
1793

The Gymsnor-2 was the second ship in the Gymsnor series produced by CorelliSpace. Though they were considered obsolete by 12 BBY, some were still in use as late as 19 ABY. It was considered difficult to keep them even moderately up-to-date. *Scupper Bantoo Though it has made at least one novel appearance, technical details for the Gymsnor-2 have only been found in one West End Games book. The listing was for a Gymsnor-2 with "extensive modifications," and the statistics listed here may differ significantly from a baseline example of the class. *Specter of the Past *The Kathol Outback Category:Gymsnor-series freighter classes
