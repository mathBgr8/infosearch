Curovao
641

Curovao was both one of the Brentaal Houses and one of the cities on Brentaal IV. The Curovao family was both noble and rich, and it founded the homonym city. In some time, the city of Curovao became one of the largest cities on Brentaal, and the family used it to control much of its business during the times of the Old Republic. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Brentaal IV cities
