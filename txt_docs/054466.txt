Dorr-Femi-Bonmi
1921

Dorr-Femi-Bonmi was a Cerean male senator who represented the planet Cerea in the Galactic Senate of the Galactic Republic and served in the Cerean Council of Elders. In 33 BBY, he was involved in a conspiracy with the Chevin smuggler Ephant Mon to sell precious Cerean malium to Jabba Desilijic Tiure in exchange for banned technology, such as swoops. Secretly, he played the movement for information and sold illegal Cerea technologies. Dorr was one of the only Elders on the Council in favor of joining the Galactic Republic, and expected the influx of technology to win the people in his favor.
