Clan Fenn
1197

Clan Fenn was a famous and influential Twi'lek family on Ryloth. Among its members were Ro Fenn and Kh'aris Fenn. Ro Fenn was in the Twi'lek Clan Council and when Pol Secura died, Ro was sent into the Bright Lands. Another member was Rh'ajah Fenn. Swilja Fenn was a member during the New Jedi Order.
