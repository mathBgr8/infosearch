Jennifer Roberson
1692

Jennifer Roberson is a Star Wars author who wrote about the deadly, mysterious, and long-lived Anzati with her two short stories based on the character of Dannik Jerriko. "Soup's On: The Pipe Smoker's Tale" appeared in the Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina anthology, and "Out of the Closet: The Assassin's Tale" in the Tales from Jabba's Palace anthology. Bantam Spectra published the respective collections in 1995 and 1996. Outside of Star Wars, Roberson is known for her Sword-Dancer saga, the Chronicles of the Cheysuli, the Karavans universe, and the urban fantasy series Blood & Bone.
