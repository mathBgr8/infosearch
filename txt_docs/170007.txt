Doctor Farnsworth was an elderly Ithorian male scientist who did a study of galactic history. During his historian career, Farnsworth had a painting of an Old Republic clone trooper dressed in Phase I armor commissioned. After his tenure as a historian was finished, Farnsworth journeyed to the Wookiee homeworld of Kashyyyk to study the local environment around the city of Kachirho. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, five of his environmental survey devices stopped sending signals, likely due to atmospheric interference. As a result, someone needed to go into the forest and download the memory from the devices by hand, but the area around the devices was far too dangerous for the aged Ithorian to travel through, being filled with a variety of deadly predators, including varactyl and bolotaurs. However, Farnsworth soon met a passing spacer, who&mdash;after Farnsworth explained his predicament&mdash;agreed to go collect the data from the survey equipment and bring it back to the Ithorian. After the spacer retrieved the information, Farnsworth gave the spacer 10,000 credits and his painting of the clone trooper as payment for the spacer's services. Doctor Farnsworth was very polite when he conversed with the spacer, a being he had never seen before. When the spacer retrieved his survey data, Farnsworth both graciously thanked and paid the spacer for his helpfulness. The Ithorian also cared about the spacer, hoping that his helper would not get hurt while searching for the data. Farnsworth admired Kashyyyk's nature and forests, believing them to be a fascinating study and a lovely landscape. Farnsworth had a wispy white and brown beard, brown skin, and brown eyes. Doctor Farnsworth was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Farnsworth made his only appearance in the 2005 expansion pack Rage of the Wookiees. In the game, he had a quest that the player could complete for experience points and other rewards.
