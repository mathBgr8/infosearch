Kamida
1784

Kamida was an Ewok princess during the era of the Galactic Civil War. Kamida was courted by Tippet, a respected Ewok warrior, in 4 ABY, when Hiromi saboteur Hirog&mdash;looking to incite conflict between the Ewoks and the Lahsbees&mdash;framed Kamida for having an affair with a Lahsbee delegate to the Alliance of Free Planets. The scandal resulted in an outbreak of violence between Ewoks and Lahsbees on Endor, but Hirog's treachery was soon exposed and the conflict quashed. *Star Wars (1977) 94 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ewoks Category:Females Category:Royalty
