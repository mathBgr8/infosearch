Slag Flats
3362

Slag Flats was a female Ithorian crime lord on Tatooine. In 85 BBY, she came to Tatooine on the colony ship that founded Mos Eisley after she was exiled from Ithor. Over the years, Slag carved a place out for herself in the criminal underworld of Mos Eisley, but not one big enough for Jabba the Hutt to take notice. Her criminal organization operated out of an abandoned water silo in the city. When Admiral Adar Tallon defected from the Empire shortly after its creation by faking his death in a staged pirate attack, he fled to Tatooine and sought help from Slag Flats. Slag assisted Tallon with disappearing and maintained infrequent contact with him over the years. When the Empire discovered Tallon was alive and hiding on Tatooine during their search for the Death Star plans, a bounty was placed on his head and a large manhunt commenced. Slag was fond of the honorable admiral and tried to help him escape Tatooine. She hired a Devaronian information broker named Labria to look out for a trustworthy and capable group that could help her extract Tallon. When a group of rebel agents confronted Labria for information on Tallon's whereabouts, the Devaronian agreed to arrange a meeting with Slag Flats. Before the meeting, Slag messaged Old Arno, the only other founding colonist still alive, and instructed him to link up with the rebels and lead them to Tallon. Unbeknownst to Slag, Labria had been bought out by the bounty hunter Jodo Kast. After the Devaronian tipped him off about the meeting, Kast and a squad of bounty hunters assassinated Slag Flats and her associates.
