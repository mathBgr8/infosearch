Moss Deevers
1168

Moss Deevers was a Bothan smuggler who paired with Twingo. The two were known for their wild boasts. They set the record for running the Lando's Folly course. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothans Category:Males Category:Smugglers
