|height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents=Mazie |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Berri was a female Twi'lek child from Tatooine who worked in the kitchens at Krayn's palace. She and her mother Mazie were slaves in the spice-processing facilities on Nar Shaddaa. They helped Anakin Skywalker to provoke Krayn's slaves into revolting, resulting in their freedom. Berri herself, in her kitchen role, was able to retrieve Siri Tachi and Anakin Skywalker's lightsabers from Tachi's quarters (where the Jedi had hidden them beneath her sleep-couch), slipping the weapons through a food-delivery panel in the door of the high-security cell in which Berri's master had incarcerated the Jedi.
