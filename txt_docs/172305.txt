Bujiboi
2269

The Rebel Alliance employed the cargo ferry Bujiboi during the Galactic Civil War. Bujiboi arrived near XQ3 Platform Pondut during the "Arms Race" after Imperial assault forces had successfully disabled all Galactic Electronics craft in the system. Imperial starfighters attacked Ferry Bujiboi while Assault Transports stripped the platform of all technology. In Star Wars: TIE Fighter Bujiboi has orders to dock with a Galactic Electronics container containing magnetic pulse equipment. The TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide does not elaborate on the ultimate fate of Ferry Bujiboi, stating only that Maarek Stele's wingmates attack the vessel.
