captain, to a rebel ship.|}} Warlord was a Victory II-class Star Destroyer in service in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The Warlord entered in service some years after the beginning of the New Order. During the Galactic Civil War, the ship was assigned to the Allied Tion sector defense fleet. Some time after the Battle of Yavin, the Santhe/Sienar Technologies began to develop a new cloaking device technology for the Empire, the NOVA project. At this time, the Warlord was often used by Lord Rodin Hlian Verpalion, Imperial Advisor of the project, as his personal ship.
