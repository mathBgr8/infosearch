Felpajh 10A
1960

The Felpajh 10A was a luxury suborbital shuttle manufactured by Larjh StarCorporation. These shuttles were commonly used to transport passengers from the ground to a waiting liner. The Felpajh 10A could accommodate up to eight passengers, in addition to five metric tons of cargo, and provided its users with a high level of luxury during flight. It required a single, dedicated pilot to operate, and could achieve a maximum speed of 750 kilometers per hour. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology Category:Larjh StarCorporation products Category:Shuttle classes
