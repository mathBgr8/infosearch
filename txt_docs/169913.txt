thumb|250px|A seismic tank's seismic driver is fired during the [[Battle of Dantooine (Clone Wars)|Battle of Dantooine.]] The seismic driver was the primary weapon of the seismic tank, a mining vehicle built by Haor Chall Engineering that was repurposed for planetary assaults by the CIS. It consisted mainly of a gargantuan metal piston that would extend rapidly from the underside of the vehicle, generating colossal seismic shockwaves upon impact with the ground. *The Clone Wars: Act on Instinct *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Mining technology Category:Vehicle weaponry
