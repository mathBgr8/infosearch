Corrupter Task Force
3477

The Corrupter Task Force was an Imperial Navy task force that had been placed under the command of Admiral Garreth Holtz during the Galactic Civil War. The unit was named for Holtz's command ship, the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter. While the Corrupter itself participated in the Battle of Hoth, having been one of the ships damaged by the Rebel Alliance's ion cannon, the location of her task force at this point in time was unknown. However, it was possible that the task force was only assembled after the battle, when Holtz was presumably given his orders to hunt down and eliminate any surviving Rebels still hiding within the Anoat sector. The task force discovered the presence of the Rebel Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Defiance and hunted it relentlessly. Its flagship, however, was somehow badly damaged, putting it out of action long enough for the Alliance forces to escape into hyperspace.
