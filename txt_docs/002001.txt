|climate=Temperate |gravity=Standard |terrain=Barren desert |water=Dry |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species=Bpfasshi |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government=Representative council |population=50 million |demonym=Bpfasshi |cities= |imports=*High technology *Mid technology |exports=*Metals *Natural resources |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Confederacy of Independent Systems *Galactic Empire *New Republic }} Bpfassh was an arid double planet in the Sluis sector orbited by a complicated system of moons, located near Praesitlyn and Sluis Van. It was the homeworld of the Bpfasshi. The main body of the Bpfassh system in the Sluis sector, Bpfassh was a double planet orbited by a complicated system of moons. The twin planets took the name of the system's star, Bpfassh, and were neighbored by two systems with names unpronounceable to Humans. Both were temperate worlds with Type II atmospheres, a dry hydrosphere, and standard gravity. The Bpfassh worlds had barren terrain, and were home to a native population of 50 million Bpfasshi, who reached a space-age technology level. The double worlds were used for the mining of natural resources, and exported metals and other resources while importing high- and mid-level technologies. Bpfassh was the site of a Jedi Praxeum. Near the start of the Clone Wars, Nejaa Halcyon chased Zozridor Slayke's Scarlet Thranta there in his ship, the Plooriod Bodkin. Slayke disabled the Thranta and stole the Bodkin at the price of only one of his men, leaving Halcyon and his crew stranded on Bpfassh for months, disgracing him in the eyes of many. Bpfassh's Praxeum was the site of a Dark Jedi uprising later during the Clone Wars. The Bpfasshi Dark Jedi were responsible for a rampage of terror and destruction throughout the sector before the insurrection was quelled by a Jedi task force led by Yoda. The Bpfasshi held a strong dislike for all Jedi since that episode. This uprising would be distorted by legend; some versions placing Jorus C'baoth on the front line, a Jedi Master who had died several years before the event.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia thumb|left|150px|[[Han Solo/Legends|Han Solo, Leia Organa and Wedge Antilles inspect a crater after a planetary bombardment on Bpfassh.]] Decades later, Grand Admiral Thrawn feinted at this system to draw ships away from Sluis Van. Han Solo, Leia Organa Solo, Wedge Antilles and Rogue Squadron were later attacked by Grand Admiral Thrawn's Noghri here. Jorus C'baoth was originally set as the Jedi Master who defeated the Dark Jedi; however, after the date of the Clone Wars became clear, this became impossible. The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia reconciled this by stating that legend had distorted truth. The PC game Star Wars: Rebellion mistakenly places the Sluis sector and, consequently, Bpfassh, in the Galactic Core.
