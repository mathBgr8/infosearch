Jakan Arms
927

Jakan Arms was a company that manufactured anti-vehicle defensive emplacements. They produced the 222 Blaster Cannon, which was used by the military of the Galactic Empire during its reign over the world Goroth Prime. *Goroth: Slave of the Empire *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Arms manufacturing companies Category:Jakan Arms products
