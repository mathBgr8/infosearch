It is intended for four Level 9 players, and follows a group of spacers who are hired to kill the Osaji Syndicate triplets. A group of spacers were hired to kill the triplets in charge of the Osaji Syndicate. They hunt down the first two brothers, and when they arrive on Muskree for the third sibling, Osaji Hux reveals that she was responsible for the bounty.
