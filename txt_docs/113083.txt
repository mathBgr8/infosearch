Memory droid
1194

The Memory droid was created by SoroSuub as a droid library. It stood about 2 meters high, and was hexagonal. They contained computers detailed enough to store a multitude of information. They were used by the Galactic Empire to record information such as the Death Star blueprints. *Before the Storm *Shield of Lies *Tyrant's Test *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:Class one droid models Category:Droid models of the Galactic Empire Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
