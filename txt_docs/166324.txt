The Number Five External Hard Dock was a dock onboard the Glorious. Lando Calrissian convinced Lieutenant Ijix Harona to let him dock the Lady Luck there after mentioning that after talking with Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt, he would ask him to conduct a review of the bridge procedures and staff, and find out why a flag officer and the Fleet Operations delegation to the mission were kept waiting while the officer of the day, Harona, was thumbing through a regulation manual. *Before the Storm Category:Spacecraft locations
