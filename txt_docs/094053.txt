Rycrit stew
1314

Rycrit stew was a common Twi'lek dish, made from rycrit meat. During the Galactic Civil War era, Mal'aa's Kitchens was a restaurant in the city of Kala'uun which served rycrit stew. Bowls of rycrit stew were often served with fermented fungus ale, munch-fungus bread, and raw munch-fungus soaked in a warm broth. A full rycrit stew meal could be had for about five credits. Many spacers prized Mal'aa's stew, often buying pots of stew to take with them on their trips. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Soups and stews Category:Twi'lek food
