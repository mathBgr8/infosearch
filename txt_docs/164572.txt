Agara
775

Agara was a CR90 corvette. Transporting repair kits along with a group of other civilian vessels, the ship was attacked by the Imperial Star Destroyer Accuser and the Immobilizer 418 cruiser Grappler. When the Interdictor was driven out by Rebels in X-wings and A-wings, all of the ships made a run for their hyperspace junctions through the fire of the Accuser. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:CR90 corvettes
