Kanchen Sector Conference
659

The Kanchen Sector Conference was an annual conference of the Kanchen sector's leaders held on Pakrik Major. It was led by the Grandiose Elector of Pakrik Major. Han and Leia Organa Solo attended the conference as New Republic representatives in 19 ABY. The 19 ABY meeting lasted for at least fifteen days. *Vision of the Future Category:Events of the Galactic Civil War
