Ulzepdi
1734

The Ulzepdi was a Modular conveyor belonging to the Galactic Empire. The Ulzepdi was attempting to escape the attack on an Imperial cargo transfer site in the Yrrna system made by TIE Defenders duplicated by Nami pirates.
