DFS-1104
1494

DFS-1104 was a Vulture-class starfighter assigned to the Saak'ak. The image used to represent DFS-1104 is a frame captured from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace which was slightly augmented by artists at Decipher The same frame was also used to represent DFS-1308.
