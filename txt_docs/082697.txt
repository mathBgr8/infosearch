Heavy Single Trooper Aerial Platform
2165

The Heavy Single Trooper Aerial Platform, also called the Heavy STAP, was developed by the Trade Federation after the Invasion of Naboo. Sporting larger laser cannons and a missile launcher than the STAP-1, the Heavy STAP was used to scout dangerous enemy territory and to eliminate retreating enemy forces.
