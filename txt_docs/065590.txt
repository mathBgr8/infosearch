Mo' Moolee Rah
892

Mo' Moolee Rah or "Mo' Money" in Basic, was a small open market in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It offered used weapons and technical boutiques, and also offered weapon maintenance and customizing services. It was located in the city's Tar Mass neighborhood. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Mos Eisley locations Category:Weapon stores
