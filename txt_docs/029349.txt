DF76-882 was a New Republic Intelligence datafile that cataloged the various starfighters, capital ship classes, and support craft used by the Yevetha. The datafile DF76-901 was later appended to DF76-882 when new data became available. *Cracken's Threat Dossier Category:Intelligence reports and documents
