Mothma Squadron

Mothma Squadron was a starfighter squadron that was assigned to the Galactic Alliance warship Bounty during the Second Galactic Civil War. Equipped with X-wing starfighters, it participated in Alliance operations monitoring Bothan fleet movements in the Bothawui system. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance starfighter squadrons
