Spicy Lady
1549

The Spicy Lady was a ship designed after the blue prints of the freighter Millennium Falcon, crossed with an A-wing. The A-wing part of the ship was able to detach and distract a fighter while the Falcon part, which carried the cargo, could escape. They could later be reattached. It was owned by the smuggler Jarril until his death in 17 ABY. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Freighters Category:Smuggler starships
