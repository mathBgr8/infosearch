Imperial worker
1934

thumb|An Imperial worker. Imperial workers were engineers used by the Imperial Remnant that wore hazardous environment suits as the conditions they had to work in were often dangerous. Primarily used in operating machinery and conducting repairs, therefor were not armored, though their toughened suits could absorb mild blast damage, and were armed with a DH-17 blaster for personal protection. Many of them were slain by Kyle Katarn when he encountered them on his adventures.Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast'Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy Although the workers worked in the Imperial Remnant, they could be sent to aid other factions, as Jaden Korr discovered when he encountered a small group in the employ of a Hutt crimelord on Nar Kreeta and found corpses on Bakura during his mission there, apparently of Bakuran workers who had resisted the Remnant. The Imperial worker is available as a multiplayer character in both the Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast and Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy video games. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy *Star Wars: Tiny Death Star Worker
