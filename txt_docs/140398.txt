Andel Tanner
2130

}} Andel Tanner was a prominent inventor who lived during the early Manderon Period, an era that lasted from 7000 BBY to 5000 BBY.The Essential Atlas It was said that a pair of slugthrower pistols, which were more than 6,000 years old by the time of the Galactic Civil War, had been created by Tanner on the Core Worlds planet Brentaal IV. That claim was mentioned in a 35:7:29 news report by TriNebulon News covering the theft of the pistols from the famed musician Fitz Roi. Andel Tanner was mentioned in the "Galaxywide NewsNets" feature of the fourth issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine, which was published in November 1994.
