QuietLift
1827

|products=Repulsorlift engines |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} The manufacturing company QuietLift produced repulsorlift engines that did not produce the shimmer between a vehicle using the engine and the planetary surface it was pushing against, which was produced by some short-range repulsorlifts. In the current Star Wars canon, QuietLift was mentioned in the article "Repulsorlifts," which was published in the "Secrets of Spaceflight" department of the thirty-eighth issue of De Agostini's Star Wars: Build the Millennium Falcon magazine around September 23, 2015. In the Star Wars Legends continuity, the QuietLift 1400 was a repulsorlift engine that was first mentioned in The Star Wars Sourcebook, a 1987 supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Bill Slavicsek and Curtis Smith and published by West End Games.The Star Wars Sourcebook Category:Manufacturing companies Category:Technology companies
