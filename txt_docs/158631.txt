Apocia
1428

Apocia was a kind of tree whose white wood was used to make luxury furniture. It was native to the planet Bethal. They were very vulnerable to a greddleback attack and took over two hundred years to mature. The desk in Dooku's private shuttle was made out of polished apocia wood.
