Bat Squadron
661

Bat Squadron was an Airam fighter squadron composed of R-41 Starchasers. It was the resident defense unit at Goff Platform and participated in the Imperial attack there. Tasked with defeating Avenger Squadron, it and the rest of the Airam defense was outwitted by the Empire's pincer attack.
