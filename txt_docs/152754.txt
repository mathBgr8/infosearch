"Screamer" active jammer
2421

The "Screamer" active jammer was a sensor jammer manufactured by Bertriak, and was commonly found installed on Incom Corporation's T-65 X-wing starfighter, where it was commonly paired with a Carbanti United Electronics universal transceiver package. The "Screamer" disguised the starfighter's ion drive emissions, in turn disrupting the sensor systems of enemy fighters,The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology such as TIE/LN starfighters.
