|pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=10 }} Star Wars Tales 10 is the tenth issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. It was published on December 12, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. Ever wondered what it would be like to be a stormtrooper? Working for the Empire, never sure where you're going next, following orders without question, ready to lay down your life for the Emperor? Writer Garth Ennis (Preacher, Hitman) and penciller John McCrea (Hitman, Superboy) take us on a journey that shows in agonizing detail what it's like for those troops in white. Also in this issue are New Zealand newcomers Christian Read and Chris Slane offering the story of a Sith Lord, a lightsaber, and a bit of innovation&hellip; This and MUCH, MUCH more in an all-new, action-packed Star Wars Tales! Star Wars Tales editor Dave Land and medic droid 2-1B are shrunken in size and inserted into the body of a Jedi Knight to search for midi-chlorians and learn their appearance. Land and 2-1B discover, to their horror, that the midi-chlorians resemble assistant editor Philip Simon, and Land chases them off, ordering them back to work.
