When Ulic Qel-Droma revealed the location of Exar Kun's base on Yavin 4, the Republic Rim FleetThe Essential Atlas arrived at the jungle moon. As the fleet arrived, Exar Kun watched from atop his temple and ordered Kalgrath to go to the isolation chambers Naga Sadow had built beneath the temples. There, Exar Kun altered the mutated Massassi warrior even further. He caused his tough hide to harden until it could block most conventional energy weapons, made his skin color go from crimson to deep green, to better camouflage in the catacombs and jungles of Yavin 4, and rendered Kalgrath mute. Kalgrath was put in a deep sleep by Kun using Sith alchemy and left behind there by the Dark Lord as a guardian.The New Essential Guide to Characters Kalgrath would remain in hibernation for millennia after most of his people were exterminated, and some fled into the parts unknown. thumb|left|220px|Kalgrath attacks Luke Skywalker and Han Solo During an attack of Admiral Amise Griff, Kalgrath was awakened from his millennia-long sleep by a crashed TIE/sa bomber in the isolation chambers, cracking his suspended-animation capsule open. He then went to look for his people believing they were still alive, not knowing the Massassi had been killed in Kun's mad bid for supernatural power. Instead, Kalgrath discovered the Rebel base in the ancient temple. Thinking they were his ancient enemies, he attacked them. However, he was stopped by Luke Skywalker and R2-D2. thumb|right|180px|The Night Beast on [[Yavin 4/Legends|Yavin 4.]] Skywalker managed to communicate with Kalgrath through the Force. Kalgrath was able to calm himself down, allowing his insanity to fade. Luke agreed to help him to find his people. Kalgrath was put aboard a GR-75 medium transport programmed with an auto-jump to the former location of the ancient Sith Empire to find the remaining members of his people. One of the random jumps performed by the transport brought the beast back to Alliance space. This time, through the Force, Luke explained that the beast would eventually find its former masters by getting back onto the transport. After Luke founded his Jedi academy, Tionne Solusar did some research into Kalgrath's fate, and have been able to track down the creature to the deserted world of Ziost before the trail ended. She was informed by researchers on Ziost that they had seen a dark shadow creeping around their camp. Despite this, it remains unknown whether the one-of-a-kind creature found his way to the fallen Sith Empire, with little more than rumours to back up Tionne's hypothesis. Later, on the planet Seidhkona, the Mind-witch S'ybll conjured up an illusion of Kalgrath from Luke Skywalker's memory. Kalgrath, identified only as the "Night Beast," first appeared in The Night Beast, a comic strip story arc written by Archie Goodwin, illustrated by Al Williamson and published in 1982. The character was identified as Kalgrath in the 2001 reference book The Essential Guide to Alien Species.
