Noa Briqualon's hut
778

Marooned explorer Noa Briqualon lived in a hut on the forest moon of Endor. A homemade table was by the door and a stove was also near the door. Animal hide rugs carpeted the wooden floor. Also there was Noa's bed and assorted tables, hollows, and crevices in which junk was stored. There was also a small second level with many interesting articles. *Ewoks: The Battle for Endor Category:Endor locations Category:Residential buildings
