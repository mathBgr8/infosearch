Rebels and the Galactic Empire would fight over the volcanic planet Vernost, the only source of a natural liquid known as Ceela that served as an efficient fuel source and medicine. Some players would guide an X-wing starfighter through the canyons of the planet and gather as much Ceela as possible. Other players would play as Imperial turrets. The game would have been played on the "Mirage" flight simulator, similar to those that teach pilots. Category:Computer and video games Category:LucasArts games
