Ambrostine
1152

Ambrostine was an alcoholic beverage. Over-consumption was known to cause loss of inhibitions, followed by sleepiness, these symptoms noted particularly in a way indicative of greater effect than the standard effects of alcohol. It was popular among Imperial dignitaries. *Shadow Games *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alcoholic beverages
