Lifting claw
1767

thumb|right|250px| A lift claw in [[Kaerobani's base]] Lifting claws were a type of device used to move cargo from place to place within buildings. They were often employed in docking bays, and could be used to store various starfighters. Certain examples ran on straight tracks mounted high on walls, while others were fixed in position.
