|type=Paperback |pages=112 pages |dimensions=28.6 x 21.6 x 0.6 cm |weight=318 g |isbn=0761533087 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Demolition: Prima's Official Strategy Guide was a strategy book for Star Wars: Demolition by David Hodgson. It was published by Prima Publishing and released on November 16 2000. The guide detailed coverage of both the PlayStation and Dreamcast versions of the game. It contained detailed maps, including all of the Power-up locations, generators and combat droids to assist you through out the game. Also included were comprehensive level descriptions and environment overview along with topographical studies. A combat tips section was included for help to beat the more difficult foes of the game, as well as tips on exploiting weaknesses. All weapons and vehicles, and their effects, were covered later in the book. The last section contained game secrets from the "masters". Demolition: Prima's Official Strategy Guide
