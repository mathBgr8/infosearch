Mike Vilardi
6057

Mike Vilardi was an illustrator for numerous West End Games roleplaying books, articles, and short stories. Vilardi enjoyed creating new alien species, and repeated a number of original designs throughout his work, such as an unidentified stalk-eyed species. Others involved in Star Wars products spoke highly of his work: Dan Wallace described his work as "constantly a joy," and Peter Schweighofer called Vilardi's work "incomparable."
