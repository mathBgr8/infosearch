Iry Danta
1448

Iry Danta was an Imperial soldier. Impoverished after the events in of 19 ABY, he planned to sabotage the wedding of Luke Skywalker and Mara Jade with his compatriots, Chala Venan, Chik Apla, Banner Sumptor, Anlys Takkar, and Moff Derran Takkar. He and Venan were stopped by Chewbacca before they could do any harm.
