|type=Paperback |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0874315034 |isbn2= |isbn3= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Black Sands of Socorro is a supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. It was written by Patricia A. Jackson and was first published in 1997. The book was written from an in-universe perspective, and provided information on the Outer Rim planet Socorro and the rest of the Socorro system. The author attempts to create an Arabian-like ambience for the locations and characters encountered on the planet. A frontier world far from the tyranny of the Empire, Socorro offers opportunity, wealth&mdash;and danger! Travel the Doaba Badlands, learn the secrets of the infamous Black Bha'lir smuggling coalition... explore a world as distinctive as its unforgettable ebony surface. *Prologue *Chapter One: Socorro
