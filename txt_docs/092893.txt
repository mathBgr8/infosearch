Skannbult Likker
1430

Skannbult Likker, also called Fire Liquor on Elshandruu Pica, was considered to be one of the most volatile intoxicants in the galaxy. It originated from Skann in the Astal sector, distilled in rock basins by local farmers from that planet's surplus of Dust-Corn and Dry Gene Wheat. Good quality Likker was rare even on its planet of origin, primarily distilled on smaller rural farms; most traders only encountered an inferior quality Likker found in Skann's spacers' bar. Kina Margath acquired a small supply of quality Skannbult Likker for her establishment, where a double measure went for as much as twenty-five credits. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Alcoholic beverages
