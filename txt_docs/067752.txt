Freighter Type H
2232

The Freighter Type H was a medium-sized cargo freighter manufactured by Galarne/Ferges Industries. The craft's unusual design and slightly smoother than usual handling characteristics allowed this small manufacturer to squeeze into the crowded freighter market. Unarmed and only lightly armored, the Type H was best deployed as part of a defended convoy, as its maximum speed was easily no match for even the most primitive starfighter. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Freighter classes Category:Starship classes of the Alliance to Restore the Republic Category:Starship classes of the Galactic Empire
