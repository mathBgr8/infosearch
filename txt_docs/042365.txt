Hijata
2157

Hijata was a Human. He was a student at the Sith Academy on Korriban in the year of 3956 BBY. It is likely that Hijata was an error, given that the player can't have any dialog with him and because he is identical to the other students and guards at the Academy and has the same lines as them. On the PC release there are four lines of dialog in the file kor35_hijata.dlg (which sits in the korr_m35aa_s.rim module) and all four are assigned Ithorian voice files.
