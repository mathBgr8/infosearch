Mount Dagger
1180

Mount Dagger was a mostly-eroded shield volcano on the planet Dagobah. It was the only prominent natural formation on the planet. It stood at 10 km tall and was created during the birth of Dagobah, before 30,000 BBY. The only major geological feature on Dagobah, Mount Dagger stood ten kilometers tall and towered over the jungle canopy and the fog bank that shrouded the planet. A shield volcano, most of the shield had eroded away, leaving a craggy igneous spire that jutted above the clouds. The hardened lava-interior remained intact and ripe for geological exploration and discovery. Small plants and lichens found no where else on the planet thrived in the soil around Mount Dagger. *Nexus of Power Category:Dagobah locations Dagger
