Draflago
1033

The Draflago were a sentient species from the Draflago system that was active in the galaxy in the early years of the Galactic Empire. The Tion Hegemony's sole university, the University of Rudrig, had a number of luxury mud tanks available for visiting Draflago.Han Solo and the Lost Legacy, 24.
