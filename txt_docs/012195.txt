Redhaven
645

Redhaven was the capital city of Rhommamool. Tamaktis Breetha was once its mayor. It was the site of the Square of Hopeful Redemption, a pit located at the local spaceport's former Portmaster's Pavilion where the Red Knights of Life, led by Nom Anor, stoned droids before destroying them. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital cities
