Lendova
1442

thumb|right|250px|[[EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate/Legends|Nebulon-B frigate Lendova during the Conflict at Mylok IV.]] Lendova was a Habeen Nebulon-B frigate that was involved in a diplomatic mission to bring the Habeen into the Galactic Empire some time during the Galactic Civil War. At the rendezvous with Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin aboard the Imperial Nebulon-B2 frigate Shamus, the Lendova was attacked by the four Nharwaak CR90 Corellian corvettes of Phazer group. Thanks in large part to the efforts of Maarek Stele's starfighter combat skills, the Lendova escaped the wrath of the Nharwaak and the diplomatic mission ended in success.
