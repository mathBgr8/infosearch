Dreyf
2699

Commander Dreyf was an Imperial Intelligence officer who served aboard the Star Destroyer Chimaera in 19 ABY. Loyal to Supreme Commander, then-Fleet Admiral Gilad Pellaeon, Dreyf was tasked with investigating the misappropriation of funds in the records of the planet Muunilinst concerning Moff Vilim Disra, Lord Graemon (whom he referred to as "a snake"), and General Jaron Kyte. Dreyf later escorted Pellaeon to Disra's palace on Bastion in order to confront the Moff concerning the misappropriated funds, which were found to have been channeled to the Cavrilhu Pirates.
