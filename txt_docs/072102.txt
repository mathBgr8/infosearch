Old Patch
1281

thumb|Floatboats travel the canals of the Old Patch. The Old Patch was a district located in the southeastern part of Abregado-rae Spaceport on the planet of Abregado-rae. Divided by several canals that were fed by the Spaceport Gusher, the Old Patch took its name from the fact that its buildings were far older than those in the more modern spaceport district. Floatboats carrying passengers and cargo down the canals were a common sight in the district, while pedestrians were relegated to using sidewalks and bridges to get around. Smugglers, local dissidents against the Tundei Tribunal and other less-than-lawful types congregated on the outskirts of the Old Patch in places like the LoBue Cantina. *Heir to the Empire *Heir to the Empire 4 *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Old Patch Old Patch
