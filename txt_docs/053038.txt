Armed transport
2825

Armed transports were long repulsorcraft capable of carrying large amounts of cargo. They were usually armed with a single laser cannon. The Barkhesh Resistance employed five of the transports, escorted by three combat vehicles, in a convoy to transport supplies to the Rebel Alliance shortly after the Battle of Yavin. The transports were escorted by the T-65 X-wing starfighters of Rogue Squadron. thumb|left|220px|The Barkhesh convoy. Later, the Galactic Empire employed the vehicles as decoys for any possible rescue missions on the planet Kessel. They were rigged to emit Human life readings to deflect attention away from a nearby armored hovertrain that was actually carrying the prisoners and give it time to reach its destination, though this attempt didn't work. The armed transports appear in the 1998 video game Star Wars: Rogue Squadron.
