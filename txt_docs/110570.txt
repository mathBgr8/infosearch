Medal of Destiny
919

The Medal of Destiny was an Imperial starfighter pilot medal awarded for successfully gaining the upper hand in an arms race, which was won by the Empire when Admiral Zaarin acquired new weapon technologies (such as the beam weapon system). Like most TIE pilot medals, the ribbons were colored with red and blue stripes. The medal itself was arrowhead-shaped, and had a gold wedge at the center. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:TIE fighter medals
