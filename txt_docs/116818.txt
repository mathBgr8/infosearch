Parla Tentase
1205

Parla Tentase was a Kiffar passenger aboard the luxury liner Kuari Princess when it was attacked by the Riders of the Maelstrom. Tentase was a "veteran vacationer," and had a vague feeling of strangeness about the voyage. Riders of the Maelstrom calls Tentase a "Kiffu" rather than a "Kiffar." *Riders of the Maelstrom Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Kiffar
