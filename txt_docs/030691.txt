Task Force Summer
1201

}} Task Force Summer was one of five task forces of the New Republic Defense Fleet's Fourth Battle Group. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Summer was placed under the temporary command of the Fifth Battle Group, paired with Task Force Apex and led by Commander Farley Carson.
