Radell Mining Corporation
1251

Radell Mining Corporation, commonly abbreviated RMC, was a mining company that worked primarily in the Elrood sector. Although headquartered on the planet Elrood, the corporation owned many other planets in the sector, such as Halbara, Acatal and Dega and had offices located in the Elrood Bazaar. During the time of the Galactic Civil War, Radell Mining Corp. struggled with Imperial Mining, Ltd. for economic control of the sector.
