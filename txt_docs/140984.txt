Chilab
661

A chilab was a Yuuzhan Vong "neural grub." It was inserted through the sinus cavity into one's brain, where it would interface with the optic nerve and record everything the subject saw through a period of time. Nom Anor used one to spy on the Bilbringi Shipyards. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Galaxy of Intrigue Category:Data storage and processing Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
