LesTech
1715

LesTech was a small droid manufacturer based in the Core Worlds that specialized in the production of explorer droids. The company manufactured the PackTrack 41LT-R MULE droid, the ER-1 amphibious probe droid, the M38 explorer droid and the S19 astromech droid.
