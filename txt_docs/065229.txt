Tamaktis Breetha
1607

Once the mayor of Redhaven, Tamaktis Breetha quickly became a member of an independent senate on Rhommamool run by Nom Anor. He, along with Shok Tinoktin escorted Leia Organa and Jaina Solo, Mara Jade, and C-3PO to a meeting with the costumed Nom Anor.
