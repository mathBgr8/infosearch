Lance (Nebulon-B2 frigate)
1949

The Lance was a Nebulon-B2 frigate belonging to the Alliance to Restore the Republic. She, along with the Lion and the Ornik, were to commence an attack on the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Conqueror. They had T-65 X-wing starfighters, RZ-1 A-wing interceptors and B-wing starfighters as fighter support, and BTL Y-wing starfighters as back-up.
