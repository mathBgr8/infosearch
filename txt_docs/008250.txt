Inexorable (Neutron Star-class)
1823

The Inexorable was a Neutron Star-class bulk cruiser that served in the Imperial Navy. Inexorable was assigned to Admiral Greelanx for the assault on Nar Shaddaa. Inexorable, along with Enforcer, formed the rear of the assault force's wedge formation. *The Hutt Gambit *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Neutron Star-class bulk cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
