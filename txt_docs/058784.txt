Gungis X Weapons
858

Gungis X Weapons was an arms manufacturing corporation in operation before the era of the Jedi Civil War. They were known for being credited with the development and manufacture of the first blaster carbine. Personal blaster carbines used by the famous mercenaries Jurgan Kalta and Jamoh Hogra were supposedly this model originally. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Arms manufacturing companies
