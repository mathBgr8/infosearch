In 33 BBY, they flew as Echo Six in the attack on Station TFP-9. When the adversaries received reinforcements, Till ordered Harlaan and others to keep concentrating against the fighters. Keela Egast noticed that the fresh Z-95s were equipped with missiles. Harlaan said they were concussion that were not dangerous for their shields, and could be fired at. Dren Melne turned against them and started firing at their own ships in an attempt to persuade Essara Till to surrender. Bravo Leader attempted to regroup the flight, and Harlaan reported that they were battered during the dogfight.
