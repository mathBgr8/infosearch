Free People
565

The Free People were a group on Wayland who resisted the invasion of their homeworld by the Yuuzhan Vong. The Free People was organized of the locals of Wayland including Humans, Myneyrsh and the Psadans. Their leader was Txer and aided Uldir Lochett when he came to Wayland in search of Klin-Fa Gi. *"Emissary of the Void" Category:Outer Rim Territories organizations
