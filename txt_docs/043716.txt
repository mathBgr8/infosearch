Sinjaffe
588

Sinjaffe was a childhood disease that sometimes struck young members of the Bitthævrian race. It was almost always fatal.Alliance Intelligence Reports Aul Tarrus Vishav suffered sinjaffe as a child; however, due to his personal endurance and physical condition, he overcame the illness in only days. His arm since kept some large bands as a memory. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Diseases and maladies
