Artoo's Day Out
3003

"Artoo's Day Out" is an 8-page comic written by Ryder Windham from Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 1 which was published by Topps in Fall 1994. C-3PO, under the ownership of Baron Pitareeze, assists the Baron as a translator for his business negotiations, leaving R2-D2 behind to watch the landspeeder. During this time, R2 is stolen by spice smugglers and transported off-planet.
