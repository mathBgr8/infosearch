Vikas Sleer
975

Vikas Sleer was the Quarren owner of a pawn shop on Phyrstal Island in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae during the Galactic Civil War. He loved to haggle and would spend hours bargaining over the most mundane of items. Category:Abregado-rae Category:Business owners and executives Category:Males Category:Quarren
