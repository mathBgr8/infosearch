Triege
1000

Lord Triege was an Imperial official who commanded a small fleet consisting of three Imperial-class Star Destroyers and an Immobilizer 418 cruiser in temporary orbit over Nar Shaddaa. According to Galaxywide NewsNets, the fleet's purpose was to search for the mysterious smuggler Cynabar.
