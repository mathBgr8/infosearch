White Wolf Inphobia 53
1116

White Wolf Inphobia 53 was a roleplaying magazine from 1995. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "Wanted by Cracken..Update" and was written by Timothy D. Jacques. Category:White Wolf Magazine
