As Vandyne was within the region of the Ciutric Hegemony and was located in the same "corridor" of space as Axxila, it was a target for the space pirates known as Kavil's Corsairs. During the Clone Wars, the Ciutric Hegemony joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems, and so the planet became a member of the Separatist Alliance.
