Sauvax (language)
835

The Sauvax language was the native language of the Sauvax species from Leritor. Their mouths were advanced enough to pronounce most of the needed sounds for usual languages such as Basic.Ultimate Alien Anthology Due to the infrequent contact with the galactic community, some Sauvax were fluent in both Sauvax language and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages Category:Sauvax culture
