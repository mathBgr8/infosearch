Outrider-class cruiser
684

Outrider-class cruisers were produced just before the time of the Clone Wars. Although they were designed as exploration ships, they packed the weaponry of warships. The Order of the Silver Jedi owned at least one of these ships, which they trapped the Force Vampire on until he was found by Captain Korsu's picket ship. Category:Starship classes
