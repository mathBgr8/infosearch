Consorts' Sitting Room
363

The Consorts' Sitting Room was located near the entrance to the Royal Residence in the Fountain Palace on Hapes. It was where the potential suitors to the Queen Mother would wait for her to call on them. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest Category:Hapes locations Category:Rooms
