Technobeast Jedi
1634

|masters= |apprentices= }} The "technobeast Jedi" was a male Jedi Knight who lived during a subset of the New Sith Wars called the Sictis Wars. As a combatant in this conflict, the Jedi Knight became infected by the nanogene spore, a virus capable of transforming living beings into part-machine, part-organic mutants known as technobeasts. Through the power of the Force, he prevented the virus from lobotomizing the frontal lobes of his brain.
