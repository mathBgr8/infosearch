Evader-GT Ion Drive
1519

The Corellian Engineering Corporation Evader-GT Ion Drive was a scaled down military grade ion drive for commercial availability. It costs only 50,000 credits, plus a 5,000 credit fee, for those that would license the engine with the Empire, and weighed 16 metric tons. *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters, Second Edition *The Far Orbit Project Category:Corellian Engineering Corporation products Category:Ion engines
