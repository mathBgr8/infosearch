Chituhr
1753

Chituhr was a member of the Travelling Jindas on the Forest Moon of Endor. He served as the tribe's animal trainer. He was large, well-muscled, and frightening in appearance. He wore his fur in a pom-pom atop his head and sported a blue jumpsuit. Chituhr shared his name with Chituhr, an Ewok deity. Kenner planned to produce a Chituhr action figure as part of its Star Wars: Ewoks toy line, but the figure never got beyond the prototype stage.
