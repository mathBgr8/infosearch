Century (Random House)
704

Century is an imprint of Random House Publishers Group. They are responsible for Random House's hardback Star Wars fiction publications in the United Kingdom. Paperback publications are handled by Arrow Books Ltd., and factual books, such as The Making of Star Wars Revenge of the Sith, are published under the Ebury Press label.
