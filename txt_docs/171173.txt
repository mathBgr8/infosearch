Gestation bin
470

Gestation bins were birthing chamber biots used in Yuuzhan Vong grashals. Developed by the Shaper caste, these bins were used to grow clones of the species that were native to their galaxy. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
