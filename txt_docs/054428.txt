Observateur
1578

Observateur was a Broadside-class cruiser in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Observateur is French for "observer" or "observant." It is also the name of multiple French-language newspapers and magazines, and was the name of at least one French naval vessel. *Star Wars: Empire at War Category:Broadside-class cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
