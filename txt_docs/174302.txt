Camen Spaceport
2067

Camen Spaceport was a civilian space station. Two members of Dunari's coalition escaped the Hurrim's asteroid base in the the Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Merte under heavy pursuit of Preybird starfighters. Alliance High Command sent a strike force of two A-wings to cover the retreat until the shuttle reached hyperspace.
