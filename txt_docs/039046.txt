Intergalactic Federation of Musicians
940

The Intergalactic Federation of Musicians (IFM) was a trade guild organization whose function was to protect and promote the business interests of singers, songwriters and performance artists across the galaxy. The popular Tatooine-based jizz band known as the Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes were members of the Intergalactic Federation of Musicians. Category:Musicians' guilds
