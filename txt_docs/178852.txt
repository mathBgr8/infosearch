|description= |animals= |plants= |other= |edible=Humans |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects=Ability to keep pilots awake |dishes= |affiliation= }} SoroSuub Stimchew was a sweet snack manufactured by SoroSuub Corporation. The treat was used by pilots to keep awake on long flights. The Human pirate Quist always carried a supply of SoroSuub Stimchew and was perpetually found to be chewing it and leaving the wrappers wherever he had been. Initially, the product was known simply as "SoroSuub Chew" in the first edition printing of Tatooine Manhunt,Tatooine Manhunt but the named was changed to SoruSuub Stimchew with the release of the second edition printing of the adventure, which was included in Classic Adventures: Volume Three. *Tatooine Manhunt Category:Food Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
