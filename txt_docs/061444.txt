Rysais Producers Co-operative
461

Rysais Producers Co-operative was a large rysais-producing agricultural co-operative on the planet Kothlis. Despite their large production quantities, the co-operative made very little profit and had to rely on government subsidization. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Agricultural companies Category:Kothlis locations
