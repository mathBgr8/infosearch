Espionage droid
6055

A MerenData espionage droid.|thumb|200px|right Espionage droids were a classification of spy droids that, to all outward appearances, resembled a standard droid model, but had hidden programming and equipment used to record information and relay it to the droid's owner. While anyone could stick a recording device into a droid, it required a professional to design an espionage droid which could get past the various security measures typically guarding against such intelligence-gathering. Manufacturing espionage droids was therefore limited to top-secret groups operating as part of larger organizations, such as the Galactic Empire and New Republic.The Essential Guide to Droids Technically espionage droids could look like any of the thousands of different models which existed in the galaxy, though the most successful could pass off as one of the more common models which most individuals would never give a second thought upon seeing. For example MerenData produced an espionage droid using the frame of a Cybot Galactica 3PO-series protocol droid, which proved highly effective due to the widespread use of 3PO units across the galaxy as diplomats and interpreters.Scavenger's Guide to Droids Arakyd Industries also produced a highly successful espionage droid for the Empire, the RA-7 protocol droid, which was refitted by the Imperial Security Bureau to function as espionage droids equipped with recording devices and surveillance equipment, including sensor jammers disguised as weld marks.The New Essential Guide to Droids Variations on the typical espionage droid included models like the RM-2020 espionage droid, which was not designed to infiltrate polite society but for battlefield intelligence gathering. *BL-17 *Imperial espionage droid *R-3PO *RM-2020 espionage droid *R3-S6 *TC-SC infiltration droid *IR-9X Replicator Droid *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Rise of the Hutt Cartel *Star Wars (1998) 44 *Star Wars (1998) 45 *Star Wars: Rebellion *Star Wars (1977) 75 *Star Wars (1977) 76 *The Mandalorian Armor *Slave Ship *Hard Merchandise *Queen of the Empire *Prophets of the Dark Side *Dark Forces: Rebel Agent *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II *Dark Force Rising *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith *Specter of the Past *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *Wanted by Cracken *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, Second Edition *Cracken's Rebel Operatives *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Kathol Outback *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids *Star Wars: Rebellion: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Essential Guide to Droids *Hero's Guide *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Rebellion Era Campaign Guide *Scavenger's Guide to Droids *Fly Casual *Endless Vigil }} *LEGO Star Wars Character Encyclopedia Category:Droid classifications *
