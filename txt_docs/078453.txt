Testan ke Harran
1573

Testan ke Harran was an Adumari guide of Jacen Solo and Ben Skywalker during a Mission to Adumar in 40 ABY. Balding, black-bearded ke Harran toured the two young Jedi around the Dammant Killers concussion missile factory, covering up whole assembly lines of missiles. Young Skywalker told ke Harran his lightsaber was a practice weapon. When Skywalker later used it to cut a hole in a wall panel, Solo explained to ke Harran that Skywalker had been telling the truth, from a certain point of view: Skywalker did practice with the lightsaber. The two Jedi then were forced into a confrontation with tens of Adumari guards - but ke Harran's attempt to hide the secret assembly line was a failure, as Skywalker was able to send a holo recording of the assembly line back to Coruscant. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Adumari Category:Humans Category:Males
