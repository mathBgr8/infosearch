thumb|250px A Rebel Alliance scout ship was a ship used by Princess Leia Organa and Luke Skywalker while scouting out locations for a new Rebel Alliance base shortly after the Battle of Yavin. Imperial stormtroopers found the vessel on Jarnollen and destroyed it, though the Rebels were not inside. *"The Bounty Hunter of Ord Mantell" Category:Scout starships Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
