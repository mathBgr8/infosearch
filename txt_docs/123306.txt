The Newcomers were one of the three major social groups of the planet Nam Chorios, the others being the Oldtimers and Latecomers. The Newcomers were the descendants of the settlers who arrived on the planet after the last century sometime in 44 ABY. *Planet of Twilight *Fate of the Jedi: Conviction *The Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Culture of Nam Chorios Category:Political factions
