Borzu Nale
3037

Borzu Nale was a male Fluggrian crime lord, and father of Kam Nale. During or prior to 32 BBY, Nale was assassinated by the Glymphid hit-man Aldar Beedo, who was hired by Nale's rival, Orin Nyell. This caused Kam Nale to endure a long lasting hunt for Beedo which inlcuded the Fluggrian changing his name to "Elan Mak," becoming a Podracer pilot, and eventually reclaim his father's criminal empire.
