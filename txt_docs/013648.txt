It was homeworld to the Soomans. The droids C-3PO and R2-D2 were dispatched to Sooma as diplomatic couriers in the decade prior to the Battle of Yavin. Around ten months after the Emperor's defeat at the Battle of Endor, Sooma was attacked by the Stenax in the conflict known as the Stenax Massacres.
