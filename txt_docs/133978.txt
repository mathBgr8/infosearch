Garron
1487

Garron was a male Sullustan pilot under the employment of crime lord Jabba the Hutt. Garron worked with the female Human Vareth. Garron used his recently deceased parents' life insurance in the purchase of the freighter Startled Circuit. They made their way to Tatooine, and Jabba's majordomo Bib Fortuna was impressed enough with Garron and Vareth's skills that he offered them employment as spacers in the Hutt's criminal organization. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Category:Males Category:Pilots Category:Spacers Category:Sullustans
