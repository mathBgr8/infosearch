It occupied the first orbital position around the star Prak and had a barren rock surface. Rake was first mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 3: Prakith," the third entry in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 21, 2005. Rake first appeared in Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil, the final novel of the Star Wars: Darth Bane Trilogy written by Drew Karpyshyn and published on December 8, 2009.
