Ord Mynock Freeport
1367

Ord Mynock Freeport was the only spaceport on Ord Mynock. It was originally part of the protective fortress built by the planet's early settlers, which became a bustling modern spaceport. Ord Mynock Freeport had a variety of goods, services, specialists, mechanics, and other businesses. The Galactic Empire reported that their regulations were a little lax in the Freeport due to Ord Mynock's distance from the Core.
