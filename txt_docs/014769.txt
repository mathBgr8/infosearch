Senator Jonas Wallen, who represented a sector in the Galactic Republic's Senate, hailed from Darlon. For a year leading up to the date 13:4:11, Wallen and their fellow Senator Char Bibbeck from the Outer Rim Territories &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Colunda Prime system astronomical object Colunda Prime engaged in betting on the likelihood of specific planets to either secede from the Republic or remain a part of that government. The gambling was eventually revealed to the public, and on 13:4:11 it was reported in an issue of the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. Darlon was introduced in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #51, a representation of an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published on the HoloNet News website on April 11, 2002. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Darlon system, and therefore the celestial body itself, in grid square R-7.
