9000 Z004 landspeeder
2940

The 9000 Z004 landspeeder was the more popular successor of the 9000 Z001 landspeeder. thumb|left|200px|A [[WED Treadwell repair droid/Legends|WED Treadwell repairs a 9000 Z004]] Although it kept a similar, spherical hull, the design was not marketed solely to Ugors, but rather to sport-vehicle lovers of all species, especially speed-loving young adults. The craft was 2.46 meters in diameter. It carried one pilot, one passenger, and 30 kilograms of cargo. It hovered only 1.5 meters off the ground, but could achieve 300 kilometers per hour. The unique design, while low-riding, was able to remain stable over rough terrain.
