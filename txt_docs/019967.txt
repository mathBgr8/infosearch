Bawoonka
1064

The bawoonka was a horn used by the Gungans. Its name was taken from the loud, bass, resonating sound it would make when blown. *Episode I Adventures 10: Festival of Warriors *Star Wars: Tiny Death Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gungan music Category:Wind musical instruments
