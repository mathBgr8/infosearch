Mission to Zonju V
3014

The mission to Zonju V was carried out in 14 ABY by Jaden Korr for the New Jedi Order. Korr was sent to Zonju V to meet a spaceport worker who was in possession of important information about the Disciples of Ragnos, a Sith cult which was causing problems around the galaxy. Korr was to meet the worker in an abandoned outpost three kilometers outside of Zoronhed. thumb|left|200px|One of the locations that [[Jaden Korr passed while being chased by the Disciples of Ragnos.]]Korr purchased a Flare-S swoop for his journey to the outpost. On the way he was attacked by members of a swoop gang working for the Disciples of Ragnos. He managed to kill or evade some of them, using mounted lightsaber combat techniques. When he finally reached the outpost, the worker was assassinated by a sniper before he could tell Korr anything. Korr reckoned that he should leave immediately so he stole one of the gang's swoops. Being chased by the gang, Korr resorted to both mounted lightsaber combat and daring swoop stunts to shake off the pursuers, including piloting his swoop to jump across a deep chasm. Eventually, he managed to lose them and get to the spaceport and off the planet. There is no set number of swoop gang members in this level of the Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy game.
