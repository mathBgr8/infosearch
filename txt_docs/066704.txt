Pri-Andylan Propulsion Systems
1145

Pri-Andylan Propulsion Systems was a major corporation located on the planet Duro. They specialized in the production of repulsorlifts and other propulsion systems. Their strength was such that they owned several orbital shipyards including a major ship-producing platform above Duro. The company prospered under the Empire and maintained several expensive contracts under this government. After the Battle of Endor, the Empire ended almost all their contracts with Pri-Andylan, and the company suffered, eventually being purchased by the Corporate Sector Authority.
