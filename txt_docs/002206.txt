Vua'spar interdictor
2547

The Vua'spar interdictor was a Yuuzhan Vong warship equipped with multiple weapons and a full communications suite consisting of several villip choirs. They were spicular in shape and resembled two cones pushed together at the base. Where these cones meet, there were three large dovin basals used to interdict hyperspace travel.
