Sereno
2426

Prince Sereno of the Tofs was the leader of the Tof Kingdom who led their galactic invasion of 4 ABY. The Tofs were pursuing the Nagai, a species they had formerly conquered and enslaved. After encountering them at Trenwyth and establishing a base at Saijo, the Tofs invaded Zeltros. Repelled at Zeltros by a combined force, Sereno allied his forces with Dark Lady of the Sith Lumiya to defeat their mutual enemy: the Alliance of Free Planets, which had since reorganized into the New Republic. During the Battle of Saijo, Sereno stayed with his royal court as his forces engaged in trench warfare with Republic forces on the planet's surface. As Sereno held audience with Lumiya, an enemy task force infiltrated Sereno's Saijo palace. In an isolated firefight, Sereno panicked, yelling to his guards to protect him. Unfortunately for the Tof leader, the Jedi Knight Luke Skywalker, armed with dual sabers, took the leader captive.
