Rakrir
2199

|affiliation= }} Rakrir was the homeworld of the Rakririan species. A highly refined and meticulous species, few left their homeworld, finding the rest of the galaxy to be too unsophisticated. The planet was sometimes known as Ballikite.Alien Encounters Two notable expatriates from the planet were Mogoyu, who came second in the decaped-plus category of the Mobquet Presents: Fastest Land Beings races,HoloNet News Vol. 531 #52 and Sabodor, who set up a boutique petstore on Etti IV.
