|subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=Lanky, with a sour face |lifespan= |origin= |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Poldts were lanky sapient bipeds that superficially bore some resemblance to Gungans and the Geonosians. The Poldts were skinny, long-snouted humanoids who had thin limbs, a flattened head. Because of their lanky appearance, they bore a superficial resemblance to the Gungans of Naboo, while their heads more closely resembled that of the Geonosians, an insectoid species from Geonosis. In 32 BBY, a male Poldt named Fanta was on Tatooine, visiting the Mos Espa Grand Arena on the occasion of that year's Boonta Eve Classic podrace. He had a quarrel with a Gungan named Jar Jar Binks, who constantly tried to look over his shoulder to peek at his hand-held viewscreen.
