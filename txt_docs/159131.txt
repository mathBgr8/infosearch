G'Neeznow
2073

G'neeznow was a Strike-class medium cruiser that was an escort ship for Task Force Whirlwind. The task force was supposed to rendezvous with a convoy of Missile Boats that were used as bait to lure Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's forces into a trap. However, Zaarin sent the Maelstrom to destroy the task force but they were repelled.
