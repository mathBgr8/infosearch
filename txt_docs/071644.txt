Alabard's Comets
1253

thumb|right|350px|Alabard's Comets base on the moon of [[Sheva]] Alabard's Comets were a Rebel Alliance military unit operating in Tapani sector. They maintained a base on the barren moon of the planet Sheva in the Freeworlds Territory. The base was used as a listening post from where they could use a captured HoloNet receiver to intercept and analyze Imperial orders throughout the sector. The information gathered was then relayed back to Alliance Intelligence. The base also contained a bacta tank. The unit maintained eight Y-wing fighters, two X-wing fighters and one shuttle. They would occasionally strike at targets along the Shapani Bypass, posing as pirates. The base personnel were highly versatile, they flew and maintain their ships while operating the HoloNet receiver. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic military units
