Flight Officer Esege "Tuke" Tuketu was a Narvath pilot who served in the New Republic Defense Fleet's Fifth Battle Group. thumb|left|270px|Esege Tuketu's K-wing starfighter While serving as a pilot for the New Republic, Esege Tuketu was part of Task Force Aster in the Fifth Battle Group. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Tuketu piloted a BTL-S8 K-wing assault starfighter with his gunner Skids, and commanded Red Flight at the Battle of Doornik-319.
