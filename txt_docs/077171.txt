Reeven Clan
1484

thumb|right|250px|[[Jannik (Rodian)|Jannik the White, Nym and the Reeven Clan.]] Reeven Clan was a Rodian clan under the leadership of Jannik the White prior to the Clone Wars. In 24 BBY, Jannik and his clan fought an honor duel against Evo the Blue's Cairn Clan. They won due to the actions of Nym, who had been hired by Jannik. Members of the clan then went on to help Nym in his adventure on Maramere.
