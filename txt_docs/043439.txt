Tabor (Aleena)
1530

}} thumb|left|Tabor is killed by Silus. Tabor was a Force-sensitive Aleena who participated in a fighting sport involved in the ways of the Force on an unnamed planet in the Outer Rim Territories. When Darth Sidious sent Darth Maul to kill the champion of the sport, Silus, Tabor engaged Silus in combat. However, the Aleena was quickly killed by the Drovian.
