Choobies
2235

Choobies was a slang term for testicles. It was also used as a metaphor for courage and confidence: After downing a glass of Port in a Storm, Greer Sonnel proudly asked, "How's that for choobies?"Bloodline The phrase "pain in the choobies" referred to someone that is very annoying.Battle of Jakku — Last Stand 3 The word "choobies" is akin to the vulgar real-world term "balls," which in a literal sense refers to testicles, but is also used as a rude term for bravery and self-assurance. "Pain in the choobies" is similar to the real-world offensive idiom "pain in the ass/butt." *Lost Stars *Battle of Jakku — Last Stand 3 *Bloodline Category:Phrases Category:Slang
