|published in=Tales from the New Republic |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=4 ABY (39:5 GrS) |series= |preceded by="Day of the Sepulchral Night" |followed by="Handoff" }} "Gathering Shadows" is a short story by Kathy Burdette that details the experiences of a Rebel commando named Jai Raventhorn and a freelance mercenary named Dirk Harkness sharing a cell together after an Imperial interrogation. The story takes place on Zelos II in 4 ABY, two months after the Battle of Endor. It was originally scheduled to be published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 16, but the Adventure Journal ceased publication before it could be featured, so it was later published in Tales from the New Republic by Bantam Spectra in December 1999. Mercenary Dirk Harkness and New Republic Intelligence agent Jai Raventhorn sit in a pitch-black Imperial prison cell recovering from interrogation. They are rescued by smugglers and Alliance sympathizers Platt Okeefe and Tru'eb Cholakk, who braved the eerie landscape of Zelos II to find a secret Imperial garrison. Jai, who is disillusioned by the New Republic, seriously considers Dirk's offer to become a mercenary and continue the fight against the Empire. The quartet of protagonists featured in this story consisted of recurring characters from roleplaying game resource material published in the Star Wars Adventure Journal from West End Games.
