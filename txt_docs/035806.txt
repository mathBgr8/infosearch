Twirrling
1291

thumb|right|180px|A Naboo monarch riding a gualama while twirrling. Originating on Naboo, twirrling was the name given to the sport practiced by nobles throughout the galaxy. Participants in the hunt, riding domesticated gualamas, used trained twirrls to seek out and recover prey, which included a variety of small mammals and waterfowl. One of Naboo's Queens following the rise of the New Republic participated in this activity while utilizing her own private herd.
