Nul was a city on the planet Zelos II. It was the second largest city on the planet after Kryndyn, and was named in honor of Mandated Commander of the People of Zelos Galleros Nul. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Cities on Zelos II
