Star Wars Weekly 8 is the eighth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on March 29, 1978. *Star Wars (1977) 4 *"Man-Gods from Beyond the Stars" *"The Sword in the Star!: Stave 1: Alas, the Seeds of Man!" *"Spacemail Depot Sector 3" *"Why Kenny Baker Nearly Shunned Star Wars!" - An interview with Kenny Baker, the operator of the R2-D2 prop, conducted by Audrey Smith. *A "Pick Your Own Prize" competition, where ten winners can choose from a set of Star Wars miniature figures; Star Wars Dip Dots and poster painting set; a Star Wars Play Doh and paints set; or two Star Wars keel kites and a set of five Star Wars masks. All prizes were provided by Palitoy. To enter, readers must answer six simple Star Wars related multiple-choice questions. 08
