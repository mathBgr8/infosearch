Fekk Trunkel is a non-player character (NPC) in Star Wars Galaxies that is spawned somewhere outside of Keren when the player accepts Kritus Morven's first mission. As in many of the game's old quests, the NPC's gender, species and appearance is dynamically generated from a wide variety of possible appearances every time it is spawned.
