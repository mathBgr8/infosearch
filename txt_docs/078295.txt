Courteous
598

Courteous was an Imperial cruiser of unknown class dispatched from the Centrality to blockade the Starcave. It was destroyed just before the Battle of ThonBoka when a negotiating party of Oswaft shouted at it. *Lando Calrissian and the Starcave of ThonBoka *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
