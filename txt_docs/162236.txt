Vhell Kursoc
897

Vhell Kursoc was a member of the Blood Raptors gang on Genarius. He was ready to take Scar Blenner's place if Scar ever screwed up. Vhell Kursoc was simply a rename of Scar Blenner for a later encounter of The Price of Business, in case the players had killed Scar in the first encounter.
