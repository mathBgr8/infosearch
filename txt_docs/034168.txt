Polyhedron 94
949

Polyhedron 94 was a roleplaying magazine from April of 1994. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "The Luminous Bantha: Adventures for the Star Wars: New Republic Campaign" and was written by Bill Slavicsek and Michele Carter. Category:Polyhedron
