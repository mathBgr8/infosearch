Xucphra Rose
862

Xucphra Rose was one of three Bacta Tankers hijacked by Rogue Squadron during the Ambush of the bacta tankers from a small convoy escorted by the Victory II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter. After having surrendered, the ship accepted to carry its bacta to Halanit. During this trip, it was escorted by Corran Horn, Ooryl Qrygg and two Twi'lek Chir'daki starfighters. The ship was captained by Bors Kenlin. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bacta Tankers
