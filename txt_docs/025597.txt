Dorajan
1543

Dorajan was a jungle planet. Platt Okeefe was shot down by TIE fighters over the planet while smuggling Imperial-issue heavy blasters. She crash-landed in its jungles, and was forced to repair her ship with only her ASP-7 labor droid, SeeVee.
