Republic-class cruiser
2682

The Republic-class cruiser was a medium-sized combat vessel introduced during the Yuuzhan Vong invasion. Cheap and easy to produce, they were manufactured in large numbers during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Republic-class vessels were seen at the Second Battle of Obroa-skai, and at the fleet assembled around Mon Calamari near the end of the war.
