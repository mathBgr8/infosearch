Etheric rudder
2806

The etheric rudder was a device for maneuvering starfighters. It allowed them to make sharp turns without the use of attitude thrusters, allowing for precision thrust vectoring and enhanced maneuverability; in the case of 4L4 fusial thrust engines, maneuverability was so increased that pilots likened it to flying a T-16 skyhopper in atmospheric flight.X-Wing: Rogue Squadron Etheric rudder prototypes were also used on Recon-Xs.Star Wars Handbook 1: X-Wing Rogue Squadron The term was created by Timothy Zahn for Heir to the Empire.
