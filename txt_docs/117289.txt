Unidentified Shistavanen Jedi
1784

A Shistavanen Jedi fought on Kamino, when the Separatists invaded the planet. The Jedi fought on the side of the Republic alongside a group of clone troopers in the corridors of the Security Sector Six. The droid forces bested the clones and the Shistavanen and killed the troopers. The Jedi's lightsaber was destroyed and their right arm injured.
