It was located in a nebula near the Red Twins. Deneter was the site of devastating battles during the Clone Wars which rendered the planet virtually uninhabitable. Due to the extensive damages caused by the war, the population left everything behind and emigrated to the Core Worlds, especially Coruscant. A few months after the fall of the Jedi Order, Ferus Olin, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Trever Flume were on the run from the bounty hunter Boba Fett, who was working for Inquisitor Malorum at the time. The trio outmaneuvered Fett and tricked him into crash-landing his ship, the Slave I, on one of the satellites of Deneter. The planet Deneter first appeared in the novel The Last of the Jedi: Dark Warning. Although the novel placed the planet in the Red Twins' star system, the 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas, written by Jason Fry and Daniel Wallace created a separate Deneter system.The Essential Atlas This discrepancy was later fixed as the Deneter system was removed from The Essential Atlas Online Companion.
