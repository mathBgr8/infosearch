The Arra Desert was a vast desert located on the planet Sorrus. It was formed in 53 BBY when the government dammed up a nearby water source, draining the area of water for ten years. *Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment *Jedi Apprentice: The Dangerous Rescue Category:Deserts Category:Sorrus locations
