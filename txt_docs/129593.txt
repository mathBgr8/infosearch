Unidentified Jedi (Brink of the Celestial Wake)
2917

|pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Blue |skin=Green |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Jedi Order *Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} A green-skinned individual was a male Jedi who served the Galactic Republic and the Jedi Order during the Clone Wars. A Force-sensitive, this individual received instruction in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Knight. Reporting to Coruscant at the start of the Clone Wars, this Jedi took up the rank of General within the Grand Army of the Republic and accepted missions from the Jedi High Council. The Jedi was placed in command of a Jedi-clone trooper task force created to investigate the mysteriously abandoned space station known as the Brink of the Celestial Wake. Boarding the station, the Jedi and clones were quickly attacked by mass amounts of pink blobs and all perished excluding Jedi Serra Keto. When Jedi Anakin Skywalker received Keto's distress code, he boarded the ship and found the body of the unfortunate Jedi task force leader. His body would be destroyed when the space station was destroyed by the combined efforts of the two surviving Jedi. A green-skinned humanoid, this deceased Jedi belonged to a species which had three-fingers on each hand, and two curving cranial horns which sprouted from the back of his head. The Jedi was created by the Fillbach Brothers, a pencillers of The Brink, a story in the fourth volume of the Clone Wars Adventures line.
