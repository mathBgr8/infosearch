Modo So
1064

Modo So was a male Aqualish who managed the Nar Shaddaa swoop circuit owned by Lupo Shar in 3951 BBY. If the the player character chooses to help the female Twi'lek Borna Lys in buying the swoop circuit from Lupo Shar, Modo will still manage the circuit. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Aqualish Category:Males
