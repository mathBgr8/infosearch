InQuest 39
1135

InQuest 39 was a roleplaying magazine from July 1998. It contained many articles, including an addition to the Star Wars Customizable Card Game (CCG) called "Mirror, Mirror," which was written by Michael Mikaelian and Jason Robinette, as well as an article called "Terminators: Five Movie Monsters to Torment Your PCs," written by Kenneth Hite, with suggestions for using movie monsters in the West End Games Star Wars D6 system and non-Star Wars gaming systems. In "Mirror, Mirror," Wizard Entertainment gave an explanation for the Star Trek and Star Wars universes being joined, so that CCG players could use the two systems together. InQuest Gamer 039
