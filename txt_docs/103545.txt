Fleet shuttles were used for routine ferrying of personnel between nearby New Republic warships. During combat, some pilots, such as Second Lieutenant Plat Mallar aboard the Mandjur, were commanded to fly fleet shuttles to recover dead or extravehicular pilots. Even other shuttles, such as the Tampion, were used for long-range interstellar VIP personnel transport.
