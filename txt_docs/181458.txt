Chromosphere
1001

The chromosphere was a layer of atmosphere of a star. It laid between the photosphere and the corona. Magnetic loops sometimes occurred in the chromosphere and stellar flares were formed here. In 3986 BBY, Vima Sunrider hijacked an ion mining ship from Exis Station and flew it perilously close to the chromosphere of a nearby star. *Star Wars (1977) 58 *Luke Skywalker and the Shadows of Mindor *Champions of the Force Category:Stellar layers
