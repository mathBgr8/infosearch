Mavikk Jumka was arrested sometime during or before 32 BBY and sentenced to the Oovo IV prison facility to carry out his prison time. Jumka provided information to the authorities about his partners, resulting in the Outland Police Force issuing a bounty on the Nikto's head and wishing him to die a terrible death.
