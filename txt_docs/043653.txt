Hesken Fever
1405

Hesken Fever was a non-fatal disease caused by bacteria that thrived in vacuum. Alliance to Restore the Republic pilot Wes Janson contracted the disease suddenly while on a scouting mission, preventing his participation in the assault on the first Death Star.
