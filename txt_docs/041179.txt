4th Squad
1708

4th Squad was a squad of Wilderness Fighters in the Alliance Military. At the Battle of Hoth, it was part of the battalion commanded by Major Bren Derlin, and was commanded by Corporal Jobin, who was Mon Mothma's son. They were stationed at Echo Station 5-7. Other soldiers included Roj, Jielu, Grond, and Sora. They noticed that the ion cannon was under attack by snowtroopers and an All Terrain Scout Transport. They then stormed the cannon and were able to save Rebel shuttles by disabling an Imperial Star Destroyer. While most were killed in the battle, at least two soldiers managed to escape to deliver a message to Mon Mothma. Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic squads
