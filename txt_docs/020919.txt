Catharese
1211

Catharese was the written and spoken language of the Cathar. Communication in Catharese included remarking some spoken words with a growl. Cathars were usually also fluent in Basic. In Knights of the Old Republic, the Cathar Jedi Juhani speaks Basic, albeit with an accent that includes rolled "r"s and "w"s that sound like "v"s.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:Cathar culture Category:Languages
