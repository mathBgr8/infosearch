Windsor, Ontario, Canada |death=December 23, 2024 |listedpronouns= |nationality=Canadian |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Angus MacInnes (October 27, 1947 &ndash; December 23, 2024) was a Canadian actor who played Gold Leader in Rogue One: A Star Wars Story and Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. MacInnes was born in Windsor, Ontario, Canada on October 27, 1947. In 1977 MacInnes appeared Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, as the Y-wing pilot Gold Leader. Although the character is only identified by his call sign and the nickname "Dutch" in the film, he was later given a full name, Jon Vander. Archive footage of MacInnes was used in Rogue One: A Star Wars Story, again showing him as Gold Leader. MacInnes also recorded additional lines for the film, including the line "Keep it tight and watch out for those towers." MacInnes passed away on December 23, 2024, at the age of 77.
