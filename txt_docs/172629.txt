Spike Squadron
706

Spike Squadron was an Airam-operated starfighter squadron that flew T-wing interceptors. The squadron was quartered aboard the Airam Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser Anat. When the vessel engaged Imperial forces near the Blair Cluster, the squadron participated in the battle that followed, but was wiped out by Avenger Squadron. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Starfighter squadrons of the Airam
