The atmosphere of Drackmar was composed of methane, requiring native Drackmarians to wear special methane helmets when traveling off-world.The Courtship of Princess Leia On the other hand, oxygen-breathing species could use environmentally-sealed vehicles, such as the Orbitblade-2000, to visit the planet.The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Drackmar was the homeworld of the Drackmarian warlords who conquered several neighboring worldsThe Truce at Bakura Sourcebook in the Quelii sector, including Dathomir and Morseer. During the Mandalorian Wars, Drackmar and the Drackmarians joined the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders. The Drackmarians were eventually defeated by the Galactic Empire and the Drackmar system was integrated in the Quelii Oversector. The system was later part of Warlord Zsinj's empire and, after the death of Zsinj, was annexed by loyal Imperial forces. The Drackmar system was conquered by the Yuuzhan Vong in the early stages of the Yuuzhan Vong War.
