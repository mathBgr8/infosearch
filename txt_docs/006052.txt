It was the only gas giant in the Beheboth system, which was located in the Sanbra sector, a part of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Behenui itself was circled by nine moons. Behenui was first mentioned in "Return to Prosperity," the first article in the Planet Hoppers series Beheboth: Blood and Water. The article was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on June 3, 2004. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Beheboth system, and by extension Behenui, in grid square O-17.
