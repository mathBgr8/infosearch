Empress' Diadem
1840

Empress' Diadem was one of the starships in Talon Karrde's smuggling operation. It was a modified YT-1210 light freighter and during the Bacta War it was captained by Melina Carniss. A disk-shaped ship, it was armed with a blaster cannon and concussion missile launch tubes. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Car'das smugglers Category:YT-1210 light freighters
