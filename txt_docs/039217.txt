Grawley family
1050

The Grawleys were a Human farmer family living on the moon Sulon. They consisted of Grif Grawley, his wife Carole, and their daughter Katie. Following the Imperial raid on Sulon in 1 BBY that killed Katie, Grif and Carole emigrated to Ruusan.
