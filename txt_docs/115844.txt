Thaylian bog-slime
3116

|date= |discovered= |creators= |created= |type=SlimeStar Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |color= |properties= |uses= |cost= }} Thaylian bog-slime was a type of slime that originated in the Thaylia system of the Outer Rim Territories' Sprizen sector. The Human spacer and mechanic Matton Dasol was familiar with it. In 3956 BBY,The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic to 3956 BBY. Jedi Padawan Revan visited the Mid RimThe Essential Atlas planet Kashyyyk. There, he met both Dasol and the Human merchant Eli Gand, who had secretly murdered the mechanic's fellow crewmembers and held Dasol as an indentured servant in order to pay back the debt that the crew supposedly still owed Gand. Eventually, Revan uncovered evidence of Gand's deeds that he presented to the pair. After Dasol subsequently exposed Gand's crime, the mechanic called the trader a Thaylian bog-slime while complaining to Revan. Thaylian bog-slime was introduced in the 2003 BioWare video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. In the game, the bog-slime can be mentioned in an optional conversation the player character, Revan, can have with Matton Dasol.
