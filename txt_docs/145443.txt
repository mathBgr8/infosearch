Bore bang
1010

Bore bangs were long red sticks of explosive that could bury themselves several meters underground. They were used by clone commando Darman to great effect during Omega Squad's mission on the planet Qiilura. When he and Jedi Padawan Etain Tur-Mukan were being chased by battle droids, he used bore bangs to blow away a hillside to eliminate his pursuers. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Explosive charges
