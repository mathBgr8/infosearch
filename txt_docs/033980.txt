Adam Hughes

}} Adam Hughes is a comic book artist. He has drawn many famous characters including Princess Leia, Wonder Woman, Vampirella, Cat Woman, Lara Croft and Storm. He has drawn covers and art for such titles as Classic Star Wars movie adaptations, The Justice League, Star Trek, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Tomb Raider, Legionnaires, Ghost, and Gen¹³, to only name a few. Hughes also began creating designs for Wizards of the Coast roleplaying games such as the Star Wars: Invasion of Theed Adventure Game. For Theed, Hughes was asked to introduce fans to new heroes through his illustrations of the teen Wookiee scout Rorworr and the female Jedi Guardian Sia-Lan Wezz.
