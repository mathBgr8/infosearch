Armalat
1084

The Armalat species was a giant, green-skinned non-humanoid race that weighed several hundred kilos. Each one of their hands only had three fingers. Some Armalat settled on Ansion, and were represented by Tolut on the Unity of Community's Unity Council. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Sentient species
