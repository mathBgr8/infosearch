Orca
809

Orca was a pair of CR90 corvettes belonging to the Rebel Alliance. The ships were part of a convoy containing supplies for a new base that was attacked by Assault Gunboats and the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Red Wind. Despite this pressure they made it into hyperspace by Keyan Farlander in an A-wing. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
