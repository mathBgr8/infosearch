Proton beam cannon
3917

thumb|right|230px|[[Firmus Piett/Legends|Piett's Accuser fires its proton beam cannon at a Mon Calamari Cruiser.]] Proton beam cannons were powerful energy weapons used most prominently by the Galactic Empire. They were able to deal massive amount of damage to hulls. thumb|left|180px|Two [[Galactic Empire/Legends|Imperial Lancets attack a Rebel MPTL-2a with their proton beam cannons.]] The most famous of these were the planetary proton beam cannons used to make up superlasers such as the ones found on the first and second Death Stars.Death Star Designer These could completely obliterate a target world. There also existed smaller composite beam superlasers that were capable of making a planet uninhabitable by destroying its core. An anti-capital ship proton beam cannon was also constructed on the surface of Dubrillion during the height of the Galactic Civil War.Star Wars: Rogue Squadron III: Rebel Strike Capital ship-scaled cannons were a feature of both the Eclipse-class and Sovereign-class Super Star Destroyer models. These guns could sear entire continents in a moment, but not pulverize an entire planet.Dark Empire Sourcebook At the Empire's height, a much smaller proton beam cannon was mounted on the modified Imperial-class Star Destroyer, the Accuser.Star Wars: Empire at War Decades later, Booster Terrik had his private Star Destroyer Errant Venture fitted with a similar weapon said to be based on the Darksaber project.The New Jedi Order Sourcebook A fighter-scale proton beam cannon was fitted to the Lancet Aerial Artillery vehicles used by the Empire.Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Jedi Quest: The Way of the Apprentice *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: X-Wing *Star Wars: Rogue Squadron III: Rebel Strike *Star Wars: Return of the Jedi novelization *Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi junior novelization *Darksaber *Death Star Designer *The Technical Book of Science Fiction Films *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Energy cannons Category:Starship weaponry
