Zark Squadron
1457

Zark Squadron was a Chiss Clawcraft squadron that participated in the Battle of Tenupe during the Swarm War. The squadron was under the command of Captain Jagged Fel and engaged in battle with the Jedi Joiners Jaina Solo and Zekk. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Chiss military units Category:Starfighter squadrons
