Hunter (aquatic vehicle)
1201

The Hunter was an aquatic destroyer used by the Rebel Alliance chiefly to approach and destroy enemy vessels and shore emplacements. It was armed with powerful short-range energy pummels which were put on the front center of the vehicle. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Aquatic vehicle models Category:Vehicle classes of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
