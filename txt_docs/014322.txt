Humans from Brentaal colonized the planet in 4000 BBY, barely avoiding conflict with the native Muzaran people, who were also known as the Muza. The first Human settlement was built on the top of a high plateau, and was organized by the Brentaal League, which hoped the world would make a profitable agriworld. The Humans brought harvester crawlers to Muzara, automated farming machines which tilled, sowed and harvested crops.
