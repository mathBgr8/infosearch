Tony Russo
2031

Anthony P. Russo wrote the short story Blaze of Glory for the Tales from the Empire anthology. Russo wrote fan fiction Star Wars stories in high school "long before it was considered cool." Russo's first published short story appeared in Aboriginal Science Fiction magazine. He devoted development to the Star Wars Live Action Roleplaying System and was reportedly finishing his first full-length novel by the time Tales from the Empire was published in 1997. *A Taste of Adventure (Countdown to Disaster, Rebel Escape, Silent Fury, and The Battle for Gap Nine) *Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal (The Pentastar Alignment) *Gamemaster Toolkit: Live-Action Adventures *Live-Action Adventures *Operation: Elrood *Tapani Sector Instant Adventures *The Best of the Star Wars Adventure Journal, Issues 1-4 (The Free-Trader's Guide to Sevarcos) *The Kathol Outback Category:Short story authors
