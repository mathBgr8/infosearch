The industrial planet Ank Ki'Shor was targeted by the Galactic Empire because it was a key supplier to the Alliance to Restore the Republic in its formative years. During the battle, the Rebel forces made a show of force with their Y-wing starfighters to defend the population against Imperial bombardment. The good use of these fighters saved countless thousands of lives by holding off Imperial TIE bombers.
