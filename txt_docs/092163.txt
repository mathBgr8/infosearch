UR-1
914

UR-1 was a droid similar to C-3PO in voice and vocabulary. He helped the Tatooine nerf herder Domo Jones, the Twi'lek waitress JillJoo Jab, and the Bith musician Blerx sabotage an Imperial purchase of proton torpedoes.
