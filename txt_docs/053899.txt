Qarl
1390

Captain Qarl was a male Mon Calamari officer who served in the Rebel Alliance during the early part of the Galactic Civil War. He was the commanding officer of the cruiser Mantan Wanderer, which was flagship of the 14th Roving Line. The commander of a battle line in the Alliance Fleet would be expected to hold the position of line admiral.
