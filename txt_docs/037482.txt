Raim Tibekk
3508

Raim Tibekk was a Human male police officer living on the planet Coruscant. He served in the Coruscant Police Force during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Raim Tibekk was a Human male who lived on the planet Coruscant. He became a police officer and joined the Coruscant Police Force. At one point, Tibekk was involved in a raid in the Entertainment District against the Hutt Cartel. During the raid, Tibekk killed a Cartel operative. In response, the Hutt Cartel placed a bounty for Tibekk's death in the amount of 2,000 credit. In 32 BBY, Mandalorian bounty hunter Jango Fett found Tibekk in Coruscant's Financial District. Fett managed to shoot and kill Tibekk and collected on the bounty. Tibekk was a Human male with dark eyes and light skin. When on duty, he wore his CSF uniform and carried a blaster. Raim Tibekk first appeared in Star Wars: Bounty Hunter a single player game released on December 5, 2002. The player can choose to pursue Tibekk's bounty though it is not required to move to the next level.
