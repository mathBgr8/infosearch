thumb|right|300px|Republic forces landing in the Lycinder Plain during the [[Stark Hyperspace War/Legends|Stark Hyperspace War.]] The Lycinder Plain was a flat area on the equator of Troiken, located in front of Mount Avos. In 44 BBY after the First Battle of Qotile Ranulph Tarkin's paramilitary landed there to evacuate into the mountain.
