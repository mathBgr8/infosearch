Supreme Strategist
1800

The Supreme Strategist was a high-ranking official of Mandalore and a Clan Chieftain. He was of an unknown species, and commanded a Keldabe-class cruiser. Some time before the Battle of Yavin, Tyber Zann's fledgling organization managed to commit piracy above Mandalore by kidnapping the Supreme Strategist.
