Ssamb
998

thumb|250px The Ssamb were large sapient arachnids indigenous to Kathol. They were created by the DarkStryder to serve as its guardians. They appeared as headless creatures which moved on its six legs and attacked with large fangs. Within the DarkStryder's fortress, there were swarms of these creatures that would move across the many surfaces, constantly in search for any intruders. They were genetically engineered to serve their master and would do so at the cost of their own lives.
