Bethrogg
1067

|masters= |apprentices= }} Bethrogg was an admiral in the Imperial Starfleet. Sometime prior to the Battle of Endor, Bethrogg commanded the Imperial star destroyer Behemoth and spearheaded Operation Venom, a military operation aimed at eliminating the Pegasus Strike Force, an element of the Rebel fleet, which was active in the Mid Rim.
