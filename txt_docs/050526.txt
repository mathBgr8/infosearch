"Arc of Fire" Bar & Grill
2322

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} "Arc of Fire" Bar & Grill was a cantina that was located on the world of Selenius VII, within the Selenius system. At some point by 3 ABY,Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters is set before the events of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. Since "Almost Had 'Im" establishes that Renlo first entered "Arc of Fire" Bar & Grill at least fifteen months prior to the conversation taking place in the narrative, the former event must have happened at some point by 3 ABY. a bounty hunter known as Renlo entered the bar, claiming that he would be the one to hunt down and capture the smuggler Han Solo. After making this claim, Renlo bought everyone present in the cantina drinks, then left. After fifteen months, Renlo returned, having failed to capture Solo, and having lost much of his belongings. He worked as a janitor in the bar afterwards, often muttering "Almost had 'im" repeatedly as he swept up glass. After Renlo began to work in the bar, an anonymous hunter who was a client of the cantina told Renlo's story. The "Arc of Fire" Bar & Grill was created by Rick D. Stuart, and introduced in the story Almost Had 'Im of Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters, a 1994 supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. Category:Cantinas
