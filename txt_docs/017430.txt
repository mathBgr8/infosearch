It contained the world of Artus Prime. At some point during the Cold War Darth Jadus's dreadnaught was stationed in Artus system. His ship was in orbit of gas giant Artus Five when it was infiltrated by Cipher Nine. Darth Jadus then reveled his plans to take over control of the Empire. In 12 ABY, Kyle Katarn and Jan Ors went on a mission on Artus Prime to investigate New Republic mining facility that was captured by Imperial Remnant forces.
