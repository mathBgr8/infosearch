Dragon's Spine (mountain chain)
1273

Dragon's Spine was a large chain of snow-capped mountains on the forest moon of Endor at the edge of the Dragon's Pelt. Condor dragons lived in caves inside the high peaks. In 3 ABY, Noa Briqualon, Cindel Towani, Wicket Wystri Warrick, and a group of Ewoks from Bright Tree Village escaped from Terak's castle in these mountains. *Ewoks: The Battle for Endor *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Endor locations Category:Mountain ranges
