Joe Corroney
7825

}} Joe Corroney is an illustrator who has provided Lucasfilm with official Star Wars artwork for related books, games, trading cards and magazines since 1996. He also penciled Star Wars: Empire for Dark Horse Comics. Other comic books he has illustrated include Crimson Dynamo for Marvel Comics, Green Lantern for DC Comics, Parts Unknown for Image, and Blood and Roses for Blue Line Pro. Along with developing his creator-owned comic-book series, Death Avenger, Corroney recently illustrated Spike Vs. Dracula for IDW Publishing. Currently, he is creating new Star Wars artwork for Lucasfilm and Star Wars Insider. Corroney is the creator of the Star Wars characters Kai Justiss and Ur-Sema Du, who are based on his fellow artists Justin Chung and Jan Duursema, respectively.
