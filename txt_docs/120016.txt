Survival armor
1520

thumb|right|200px|Luke Skywalker wearing survival armor, in a TIE fighter's ejector seat Survival armor was a type of spacesuit commonly used among both large corporations and independent spacers. Zero-G workers for the Tagge Mining Company wore survival armor, and Han Solo kept at least one such suit aboard the Millennium Falcon. The suits were equipped with a jetpack. Luke Skywalker used Han's survival armor to infiltrate a group of Tagge workers and learn of the plans to destroy the Rebel fleet using the Omega Frost. Luke later wore the suit when he escaped a Mining Explorer in a TIE fighter eventually abandoning the small craft via ejector seat before it crashed into an asteroid.
