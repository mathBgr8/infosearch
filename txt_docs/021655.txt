Team Ralltiir
1921

|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Cup of Limmie }} Team Ralltiir was a limmie team that played for the planetThe Essential Guide to Planets and Moons of Ralltiir in the Galactic Cup of Limmie. In 13:3, during the Separatist Crisis, they defeated the planet of Tralus's team in an upset, with a single score in the Cup's opening round. The result was then published in an article in the CoCo District edition of the HoloNet News. Team Ralltiir was mentioned in Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
