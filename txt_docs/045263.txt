Kristoffer Tabori
2155

Kristoffer Tabori voiced HK-47 in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. He voiced HK-47 again in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, as well as the HK-50 series assassin droids (some of which were cut from the final game), and all the HK droids in Star Wars: The Old Republic. {| !
