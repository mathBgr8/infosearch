Daren Horley
516

Daren Horley is a Star Wars fan who designed the Amphi-Hydrus in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the 16 years and older category. He was 29 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
