Planet of the Mists
7480

"Planet of the Mists" is an adventure supplement published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. It was written by Nigel D. Findley and published in 1992. Rebel Agents accidentally discover an Imperial factory plant run by the Dark Jedi Vost Tyne.
