Rhea
1758

Sergeant Rhea was a male Human Corellian Security Force officer, stationed in Coronet City. He was responsible for training CorSec starfighter pilots and assigning duty missions to them. Sergeant Rhea was a NPC in Sony Online Entertainment's MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies.
