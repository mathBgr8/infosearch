Unidentified Force-sensitive Human
1532

A male Human Force-sensitive had traveled with a young Yoda hundreds of years before the Battle of Yavin and the two crash-landed on an unknown swamp world. They were found by the Hysalrian Jedi Master N'Kata Del Gormo, who started instructing them in the ways of the Force. Yoda was fully trained and became a Jedi Knight. *The Official Star Wars Fact File Part 2 (2013) Category:Humans Category:Jedi trainees of the Jedi Order Category:Males
