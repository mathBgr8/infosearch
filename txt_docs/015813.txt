Confederacy of Independent Systems forces under General Sev'rance Tann arrived on Tatooine after she had been informed by Count Dooku that Boorka, a Hutt living on Tatooine, had valuable information for the Separatists. After stocking up on supplies on the neighboring Kaer Orbital Platform in the Siskeen system, Tann's forces arrived on Tatooine. thumb|left|200px|[[Sev'rance Tann/Legends|Sev'rance Tann standing in front of the burning spaceport.]] First, Tann fought her way through Boorka's guards, impressing him greatly. Boorka agreed to give Tann his information concerning the Decimator, but only if she destroyed the Galactic Republic occupied spaceport nearby. Tann and her droid forces pushed through and overpowered the Republic defenses and demolished the spaceport, succeeding in Boorka's task.
