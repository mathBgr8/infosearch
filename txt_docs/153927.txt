Korta Sarc
1161

Korta Sarc was a Shistavanen Alpha Premier who lived during the of the Imperial Era. Sometime by 8 BBY, Sarc suddenly disappeared and the Uvena System Constabulary assigned two Shistavanen scouts, Lak Sivrak and Yurdak Fav, to join the search.
