Traveller's Tales
2155

Traveller's Tales is a British video game developer founded in 1989. Traveller's Tales has developed seven LEGO Star Wars games. Traveller's Tales was founded by Jon Burton. In 2004, development began on LEGO Star Wars: The Video Game with Giant Interactive Entertainment. In 2005, Traveller's Tales merged with Giant Interactive Entertainment, forming TT Games.
