|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=25 ABYThe Essential Atlas |series=Star Wars: Chewbacca (2000) |issue=4 |next=last }} Chewbacca 4 is the fourth and final issue in the Star Wars: Chewbacca series of comics. It was released on April 19, 2000 by Dark Horse Comics. The history of the galaxy's greatest Wookiee concludes with stories from those closest to him. Luke Skywalker recounts the final moments of Chewbacca on the burning, heaving surface of Sernpidal, and marvels at his friend's sacrifice. And Han Solo, his grief locked deep inside, tells a story of Chewbacca's heroic rescue of Han's daughter. Don't miss the opportunity to say farewell to this beloved character!
