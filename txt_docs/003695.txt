The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook (Second Edition)
35521

The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook was a roleplaying book published by West End Games for the second edition of Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game in early 1994. First published in 1990, it was written by Paul Murphy,The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook and was revised for the Second Edition by Peter M. Schweighofer. The book covers the history, organization, technology, and tactics of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. The book itself lists it release date as April 1994, while the the StarWars.com Cargo Bay later listed the release date as January 1, 1994. A terrible civil war burns throughout the galaxy. A rag-tag group of freedom fighters has risen from beneath the dark shadow of a tyrannical Empire: against all hope, they have dealt the oppressors a mighty blow. Throughout the vastness of space, heroic beings engage in a desperate struggle against the overwhelming might of the Empire. They are ill-equipped and pitifully few, but their courage and determination has fostered new hope for freedom in the galaxy.
