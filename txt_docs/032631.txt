Oryon
2996

Oryon was a male Bothan spy who worked for the Republic Intelligence during the Clone Wars and later the Erased. thumb|[[Dexter Jettster/Legends|Dexter Jettster, Curran Caladian, Oryon and Keets Freely.]] After Chancellor Palpatine reorganized the Republic into the Galactic Empire, Oryon joined the Erased. When a group of stormtroopers attacked a base of the Erased, he helped save his friends, but not all of them. He traveled with Jedi Master Fy-Tor-Ana to rescue Ferus Olin, and with them helped temporarily rid Naboo of Imperial presence. Oryon later helped Fy-Tor-Ana rescue two of Ferus's friends, Roan Lands and Dona Telamark, from the government on Bellassa, with help from The Eleven. Oryon was killed when Darth Vader destroyed the asteroid.
