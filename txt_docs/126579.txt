Tray'la Sheek
1373

Tray'la Sheek was a female Twi'lek who was an agent of the so-called "Freedom Convoy," and was affiliated with Eela Valotta during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, she was wanted by Jabba Desilijic Tiure for kidnapping several Twi'leks from the Hutt's personal harem.
