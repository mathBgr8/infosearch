Kara'Teel was a female member of the Singing Mountain Clan, a coven of witches native toThe Courtship of Princess Leia the Outer Rim TerritoriesThe Essential Atlas planet Dathomir. A member of the Djo family, she was the youngest daughter of the clan's Human leader Augwynne Djo and the younger sister of Gethzerion, Barukka, and Allaya Djo. Over time, Gethzerion sought to take control of the Singing Mountain Clan and allied with the witch Baritha as part of a plot to kill her mother. Sometime by 8 ABY, in Gethzerion's attempt to kill Augwynne, Kara'Teel sacrificed herself so that her mother could live. Baritha and Gethzerion were then banished by Mother Augwynne, and the two re-formed the coven of the Witches of Dathomir known as the Nightsisters.
