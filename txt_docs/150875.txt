Zuliria Museum of Antiquities
1086

The Zuliria Museum of Antiquities was a permanent institution in Zuliria dedicated to acquisition, conservation, research, communication and exhibition, for purposes of study, education, enjoyment, the tangible and intangible evidence of people of Zuliria and their environment.Alliance Intelligence Reports Arcona student Ther-das worked in the Ancient Weapon department of this museum while trying to be re-accepted at Lerct Historical Institute to get a degree in ancient weaponry. Ther-Das eventually became acquiition manager of the whole Museum. Duelist aficionado Cene Gilvent also worked in this Museum, in property maintenance, and she spent seven years using the museum's resources to learn different fencing styles. Gilvent met Ther-Das while working in the museum. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Museums
