Tarrack
710

The Tarrack were the colonists of the planet Utharis.. The Tarrack feature in the novel Shield of Lies. They are also mentioned in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia. Neither source reveals whether the term Tarrack refers to a sentient species or some other social group. *Shield of Lies Category:Undefined social groups
