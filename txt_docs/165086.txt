Separatist cruiser
2950

A type of cruiser was used by the Separatists during the Clone Wars. thumb|left|150px|A ship of this class over [[Kashyyyk/Legends|Kashyyyk.]] These cruisers also bore a slight resemblance to an elongated Venator-class Star Destroyer from the opposing forces of the Republic Navy and the Providence-class Dreadnought utilized by Separatist forces. Little is known about the class itself, except that it was capable of operating inside an atmosphere and that it was armed with at least two dual turbolaser cannons. Ships of this class were present at the battles of Geonosis and Kashyyyk. thumb|left|150px|A ship of this class over [[Geonosis/Legends|Geonosis.]] This class of ship first appeared in Star Wars: Republic Commando during missions on Geonosis and Kashyyyk. It is also briefly seen in Star Wars: The Force Unleashed in the versions developed for PS3/PC, Xbox 360, and Wii. It appears in the Raxus Prime level. After defeating a Junkyard Titan, Starkiller uses his Force abilities to create a bridge out of one such ship, lodged in the piles of scrap that cover the planet.
