It was the site of a Trade Federation laboratory producing the illegal chemical weapon trihexalon, prior to the Clone Wars. The planet was heavily defended by orbital defense cannons and powerful deflector shield systems. The defense system was compromised by the pirate forces of Nym, following the Hex attack on Maramere. Aided by Mere cruisers, Nym punched a hole through the orbital defenses, and staged an assault on the Hex factory planetside. The facility was destroyed by Nym and the Lok Revenants, halting production of Hex weapons for Cavik Toth's mercenaries.
