Ithorian Invasion additionally denotes a March 1998 release date. |pages= |isbn=0-590-91611-4 |issn= |genre= |issue=7 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 7 is the seventh issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in March 1998. Issue 7 included part two of the serial comic X-Wing Marks the Spot, written by Mike W. Barr. Star Wars Kids 7 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book. Star Wars Kids 7 came packaged with the game's seventh adventure book, Ithorian Invasion. 07
