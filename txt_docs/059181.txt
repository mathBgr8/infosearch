Multiple Missile Launch Turret
2245

The Multiple Missile Launch Turret, or Trandoshan MMLT was a turret manufactured by Arakyd Industries that fired anti-personnel rockets. During the Battle of Kashyyyk, Trandoshans used these weapons against Wookiees and clone troopers. It shot small rockets at foes at a high velocity and with a delayed explosion. They were effective against Katarn-class commando armor and Wookiees. It was manned by one Trandoshan. It was armored with a blast shield in the front so the only way to infiltrate it was with detonators, anti-armor rounds, or LJ-50 concussion rifle rounds. It first appeared on the Star Wars: Republic Commando website, where it was only described as a "Trandoshan MMLT", though the meaning of the acronym was not given.
