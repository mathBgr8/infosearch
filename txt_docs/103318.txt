Tentacle-cactus
581

The tentacle-cactus was a species of plant that grew in arid climates. The plants, which could become quite large, sported thick stalks that were covered with long spines. The spines of the tentacle-cactus housed within the Skydome Botanical Gardens were used to serve hors d'œurves during special receptions. *Jedi Search *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cacti Category:Plants of Coruscant
