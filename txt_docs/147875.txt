Michael Ralph
980

Michael Ralph (born 1963) provided the voice for the Steadfast and Torpedo Launcher pilots in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds. He also voiced Rebel Wingman 5 in Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader and provided additional voiced for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.
