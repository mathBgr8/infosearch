thumb|right|250px|[[Sith Lord/Legends|Sith Lord Darth Stryfe and Sith Lady Darth Talon, prominently displaying their tattoos]] The term Sith tattoo referred to a number of different types of body markings that were used by followers of the dark side order and its factions. The One Sith under Darth Krayt were prominent practitioners of Sith tattooing.
