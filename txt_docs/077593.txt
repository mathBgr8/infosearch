Gort
1245

Gorts were small creatures with blue feathers. They had a flat beak, three toes, a thin tail, and three eye stalks with one eye on top. They were kept as pets, and their eggs (whose shells were a translucent pinkish-pearl) were considered valuable in many cultures.
