Taborah Johnson
1242

Taborah Johnson (born March 21, 1953) is a Canadian voice actress who was the voice of Latara in the first season of the Ewoks animated television series and Jessica Meade in the Droids animated television series. Her other appearances included Air Waves, A Holiday Romance, Blood Brothers, One Night Only, Utilities, The Big Comfy Couch, Mythic Warriors and Polka Dot Door as well as narrating trailers for PBS Kids. She is the elder sister of Clark and Molly Johnson. Category:Star Wars: Droids voice actors Category:Star Wars: Ewoks voice actors
