MultiNode communications link
1712

The MultiNode communications link was a cheap comlink, commonly found across the galaxy. Produced by the BCC corporation, the MultiNode communications link was a smaller, less powerful version of the BCC OmniNode communications set. The comlink was small enough that it could be mounted on a set of goggles or in a helmet. This was especially useful as the unit could beep or vibrate to notify the user of an incoming message.
