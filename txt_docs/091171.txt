Battle of unidentified planet (Confederacy of Independent Systems)
1816

A battle was fought on a planet during the Clone Wars, resulting in the natives' enslavement by the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Following a fierce battle between the planet's defense forces and an invading CIS army, an unnamed city was badly-damaged and still smoking from fires caused by the battle. Separatist destroyers, as well as droid tri-fighters could be seen overhead, blockading the planet against Republic intervention. The resident humanoid species on the planet were captured and held under guard by B1-Series battle droids. Category:Battles of the Clone Wars
