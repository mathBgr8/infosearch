Douglas Kaufman
503

Douglas Kaufman is a writer and game designer who wrote and developed a number of roleplaying game books and board games from West End Games, including Battle for the Golden Sun, Otherspace II: Invasion, Star Warriors: Starfighter Combat in the Star Wars Universe, and Starfighter Battle Book: X-wing vs TIE Interceptor. Category:Role-playing game authors
