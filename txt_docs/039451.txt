In 4 ABY, the Pride of Aridus dropped off a Rebel commando team on an Imperial world. When the team came back late and under heavy fire, second-mate Alfonso Luiz Obota mutinied against Nord, who wished to leave the team behind. Five of the Aridus crewmen and all but three commandos were killed in the rescue attempt. Obota was court-martialed and imprisoned as a result.
