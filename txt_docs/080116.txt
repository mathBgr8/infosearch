Collapsium
678

Collapsium was an extremely dense and volatile substance that was used in Void-7 seismic charges. When ignited, it had the peculiar property of creating a powerful and destructive implosion. *Shatterpoint *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Substances
