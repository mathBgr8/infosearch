Charles Dennis
1110

Charles Dennis (born December 16, 1946) is a Canadian actor who voiced Ulic Qel-Droma in Star Wars: The Clone Wars (video game). He also voiced Davik Kang in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic and Dol Grenn in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords.
