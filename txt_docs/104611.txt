Galinda Norsh
2682

Galinda Norsh was a female Jedi Initiate within the Jedi Order who lived at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant several decades before the start of the Clone Wars circa 89 BBY. A Force-sensitive female, Galinda Norsh was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, studying at the Coruscant Jedi Temple's academy at the same time as the infamous Dooku of Serenno. During her training, Norsh was assigned to Blue Team during an urban tracking exercise beginning at the Temple and leading into the All Planets Market to retrieve a specific fruit. Assigned to the same team as Dooku and Hran Beling, Dooku took command of the team and was determined to beat his former friend and leader of Gold Team Lorian Nod. Deposited in the Nova level of Galactic City, Norsh suggested using the many alleyways leading to the Market to move about undetected and hide so as not to be tagged by the other team as they all strove to get the fruit first. Suggesting that the Gold Team was most likely staking out Blue Team and awaiting them so as to tag them all and move easily to the Temple, Norsh and Beling aided Dooku in infiltrating the market. Beling was tagged by one of the opposing team members but Norsh was able to grab the fruit without any disruption.
