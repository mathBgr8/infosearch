Javyar's Cantina
1713

Javyar's Cantina was a cantina located in the Lower City of Taris. An atrium with a set of pazaak tables led into the main bar. A bounty office owned by Zax the Hutt was located in one wing and a stage with dancers and music was in the wing opposite. It was a popular hangout and was frequented by such people as Lyn Sekla, Uriah, Holdan, Gelrood, Mission Vao, Zaalbar, Canderous Ordo, and Calo Nord. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cantinas Category:Lower City locations
