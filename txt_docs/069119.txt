Mission to Nar Shaddaa (disambiguation)
2807

There were a number of specific missions to Nar Shaddaa during the history of the galaxy. *Rescue of Luke Skywalker - A mission undertaken by Han Solo and Leia Organa to rescue Luke Skywalker shortly after the Battle of Yavin. *Mission to Nar Shaddaa (War of the Bounty Hunters) - A mission undertaken by the Alliance to Restore the Republic and two bounty hunters, Beilert Valance and Dengar, to gain information on Boba Fett. *A young Jedi Knight's mission during the Desolator crisis. *Meetra Surik's mission during the First Jedi Purge, part of her attempt to track down the Lost Jedi and to stop the Sith Triumvirate. *Shae Vizla's mission during the Third Galactic War *A mission undertaken in 32 BBY by Mace Windu and Depa Billaba. *Mission to Nar Shaddaa (Clone Wars) &ndash; apart of Quinlan Vos's attempt to infiltrate the inner circle of the Confederacy of Independent Systems, 21.5 BBY *Mission to Nar Shaddaa &ndash; A mission conducted by Galen Marek to locate Rahm Kota at The Undertow on Nar Shaddaa in 2 BBY. Special agent Kyle Katarn went on several missions to Nar Shaddaa on behalf of the Rebel Alliance: *Mission to Nar Shaddaa (Dark Trooper campaign) &ndash; Katarn's effort to steal a nava card detailing smugglers involved the Imperial Dark Trooper Project, 1 ABY *Mission to Nar Shaddaa &ndash; Katarn's later attempt to discern the details of his father's death from the droid 8t88, 5 ABY *Mission to Nar Shaddaa (Empire Reborn) &ndash; Katarn's personal war against the Empire Reborn, 12 ABY *Mission to Nar Shaddaa (Disciples of Ragnos crisis), carried out by the New Republic Intelligence Service
