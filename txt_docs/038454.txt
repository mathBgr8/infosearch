Dvakvar Grahrk was a Force-sensitive Devaronian Jedi who hunted down members of the Sith. Though the Devaronian was a member of the Jedi Order, Grahrk also learned the power of the Dark side of the Force. Grahrk spent time on the planet Dantooine,Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords where they fought Kath hounds at the ruined Jedi Enclave and fought kinrath on the plains near the Khoonda government building. Grahrk also visited the planet Korriban, where in the abandoned Sith Academy, they fought and killed Sith assassins that aided the Sith Triumvirate. Grahrk had orange skin and yellow eyes. A user of the Force, Grahrk used the powers Force lightning and Force crush to kill Sith assassins. While on Dantooine, the Devaronian wore flex heavy armor with a head device and duel wielded an ion blaster and a heavy blaster pistol. As they fought kath hounds and kinrath, Grahrk also wore Neo-Crusader assault armor, a head visor, and used two purple-colored vibroblades. On Korriban, they wore dark-colored robes and wielded a cyan-bladed lightsaber. During the development of the 2004 video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, the character of Dvakvar Grahrk was created as a substitute for the then unfinished Disciple, a companion of the player character.
