Atallo
2689

Lieutenant Atallo was a male officer in the Telos Security Force following the Jedi Civil War. He oversaw the Czerka landing bay Urban 38-3 on Citadel Station, and used his position to cover up the illicit Czerka weapons trade on the station. At the request of TSF Lieutenant Yima, Meetra Surik confronted Atallo and convinced him to turn himself in, but Atallo was killed by officers from the Czerka Security Division before he could give his confession. Atallo was originally part of a subquest in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords entitled "Yima's Suspicion", in which the player character&mdash;Meetra Surik&mdash;could help Yima uncover Atallo's crimes and either persuade him to confess or blackmail him. Regardless of which path Surik took, Atallo would be killed by the Czerka Security Division after the player met with him. Like many other aspects of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, this sidequest was eventually cut from the final product, and Surik meets neither Yima nor Atallo in the game&mdash;although the former is mentioned by TSF Lieutenant Dol Grenn.
