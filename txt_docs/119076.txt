Ninth Fleet (Galactic Republic)
1462

The Ninth Fleet was one of the primary naval units of the Galactic Republic during the height of the Clone Wars. During the war, the Ninth Fleet received a distress signal from the merchant vessel Deyvus and dispatched the freighter Twilight to rescue the crew. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons 9
