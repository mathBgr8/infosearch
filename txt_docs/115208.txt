Genogri
1399

Genogri was the leader of the warlike Wind Raider tribe of Humans on Taloraan. Before he assumed chieftainship, the Wind Raiders were a peaceful people, but Genogri was ambitious and power hungry and transformed his tribe onto a band of warriors. He did not care much for peaceful negotiations, tending to prefer violent solutions to all conflicts. He possessed a blaster pistol that he had looted from a Rebel operative whom he had betrayed and killed. He was later tricked by other Rebels who acquired the starship of the murdered operative&mdash;also in Genogri's possession.
