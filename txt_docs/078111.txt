Pengauani
2007

thumb|right|250px|A Pengauani ready for action The Pengauani was an infantry unit employed by a faction during the Galactic Civil War. Consisting of members of an avian alien species standing approximately one meter tall, the Pengauani were equipped with a pair of goggles and a large rifle. They were also fitted with a harness bearing an electronic device.Star Wars: Empire at War The Pengauani's only appearance is as a set of model and animation files in Star Wars: Empire at War. There is no usable code in the game XML files to enable it, and its intended purpose remains unclear. It may have been used internally by the developers as an equivalent for Simon and the Bongo Marauder, for debugging or demonstration purposes, or simply as a joke. It is speculated that the Pyn'gani from EAW were originally intended to use the Pengauani model, and had this been the case, would have explained a few uncertainties in the game in regards to them. Particularly, why the Pyn'gani (who are made up of the human snow-dweller model, yet do not spawn from the typical snow-dwelling human structure, but rather their own igloo-like ones) are the only "humans" in the expansion game that can be enslaved by the Zann Consortium. The similarity of the words "Pyn'gani" and "pengauani" are also notable. The name of the Pengauani's model is "NI_PENGAUANI.ALO". The "NI" is a sorting descriptor that represents "Neutral Infantry". *Star Wars: Empire at War Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Military personnel
