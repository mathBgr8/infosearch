Level 3301 was a docking level in Byblos Starport Tower 214. The local Imperial Customs office was situated at this level, as was docking bay 789012634. The protocol droid U-THR created some commotion at this level when he attempted to free his owner from the Imperial authorities. Category:Byblos locations
