Hawkbat (starship)
2007

Hawkbat was a supply ship in the fleet of Warlord Zsinj. In 7 ABY, the ship was commanded by Bock Nabyl. Shortly before the Battle of Talasea, the ship was ordered to transport supplies to Night Caller, but had to do so under quarantine procedures due to an outbreak of Bunkurd Sewer Disorder contracted by crewmembers on leave on the planet Storinal. The captain and crew apparently never learned that this outbreak was deliberately caused by Wraith Squadron, who were trying to avoid direct contact with personnel of Zsinj's empire. The class of the Hawkbat remains unknown, but it was large enough to carry at least two shuttles, the Hawkbat's Perch and Hawkbat's Vigil. *X-Wing: Wraith Squadron *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of Zsinj's Empire Category:Transports
