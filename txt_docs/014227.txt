Qretu 5
1376

Qretu 5 was a bacta refinement planet in the Inner Rim's Qretu system, loyal to Ysanne Isard during the Bacta War. The planet hosted the Q5A7 Bacta Refinement Plant, which was destroyed by a punitive strike conducted by Rogue Squadron soon after the Battle of Halanit. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in the Inner Rim
