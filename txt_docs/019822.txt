|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=3.5 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire |issue=4 }} Shadows of the Empire 4 was the fourth issue in the six-part Shadows of the Empire series of comics, and was released on August 7, 1996 by Dark Horse Comics. What would it take for you to step into the den of a rancor? What treasure would tempt you to stare death in the face? Aboard Boba Fett's ship is a carbonite-encased Han Solo. Jabba's willing to pay plenty for Solo, making him a tempting treasure for every bounty hunter in the galaxy. But tempting enough to board the Slave I? Apparently, somebody thinks so!
