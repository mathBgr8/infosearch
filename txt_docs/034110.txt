Qel-Droma
1565

The Qel-Droma was a frigate once captained by Tash Scrambas during the Alderaan Ascendancy Contention. According to What's The Story? author Aidan Hennessy, the Qel-Droma was originally called the Ulic Qel-Droma, and was specified as being an Alderaanian War Frigate. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alderaanian starships Category:Frigates
