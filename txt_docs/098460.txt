Kif
1789

Kif was a high-energy ore mined at Gadon 3. The name of kif was introduced via a mention of "Kif miners" in Steven Barnes' 2004 novel The Cestus Deception of the Clone Wars multimedia project. 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia subsequently introduced the name's decapitalized form, "kif," and confirmed that it was in fact the name of the type of ore that the novel had associated with the miners. *The Cestus Deception Category:Ores
