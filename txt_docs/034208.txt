White Wolf Magazine 15
834

White Wolf Magazine 15 was a roleplaying magazine from April/May of 1989. It contained many roleplaying articles, including a rules supplement for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "Let the Dice Decide" and was written by Sean Stevenson. Category:White Wolf Magazine
