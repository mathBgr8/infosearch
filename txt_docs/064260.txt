Sagebrush Corporation
1138

thumb|right|150px Sagebrush Corporation is a publishing company that republishes many Star Wars books for younger readers. Demco Media's Turtleback books were acquired by Sagebrush on March 1, 2006. On July 26, 2006, Follett Software Company acquired the Sagebrush Corporation. Category:Publishing companies (real-world)
