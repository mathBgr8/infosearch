Aola
1246

Aola was a type of cooking oil commonly found on the planet, Kashyyyk. Stored at room temperature, its culinary value was ideal for basting roasts such as Bantha rump. The chef Gormaanda, provided instructional holovids including tips on how to properly use this food additive for various meals. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the Wookiee, Mallatobuck, used liberal helpings of this item when preparing a special Life Day Bantha surprise dinner for her husband, Chewbacca. Fried Endorian chicken was fried in Aola and then served. In the Star Wars Holiday Special, Cooking Oil was used as Aola.
