|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=c. 19 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=*Star Wars: Dark Times *Star Wars: Vector |issue=11 (Republic #94) }} Dark Times 11 is the eleventh issue in the Star Wars: Dark Times comic series. It is the first issue of Dark Times to feature the Star Wars: Vector cross-over story arc and is the fifth issue in the overall arc. The biggest Star Wars story of 2008 comes to the era of the Empire as Darth Vader's pursuit of power releases a force unlike any he's ever witnessed! Beginning the second phase in a yearlong, galaxy-changing storyline, part five of "Vector" puts the smuggling crew of the Uhumele in their toughest predicament yet, puts Darth Vader in a traitor's sights, and potentially puts the entire galaxy at risk! Be there as Star Wars history is made!
