Imperial Customs Frigate 517
1078

Imperial Customs Frigate 517 was an Imperial Customs Frigate which once chased Hawker Bryce-Kelley's starship Queen's Victory to the Reginard system. When they arrived in the system, however, they were met with a Rebel ship and soon sent Customs Inspector Buudro to board the ship with two stormtrooper guards. The Rebels managed to convince Buudro that they had not seen any ship enter the system, and so 517 left the system. Category:Imperial Customs Frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
