Bel Att
1240

Bel Att was a male Iotran who served as a member of the Black Sun and an enforcer for the crimelord Sprax during the time of the Galactic Civil War. He was killed by the mercenary Limna Yith while trying to protect valuable Black Sun data during a dispute on the mining colony Yen-2. *Secrets of the Sisar Run *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Black Sun members Category:Enforcers Category:Iotrans Category:Males
