Pentabenzedralyne
733

The drug pentabenzedralyne was created during the Clone Wars, designed to heighten the aggression of combatants. Years later, after the Battle of Endor, the Camarina Connection gang renewed production of pentabenzedralyne on a massive scale aboard the factory ship Black Lotus, as part of a scheme to destabilize the galaxy&mdash;and make themselves rich in the process. The only mention of pentabenzedralyne occurs in the French roleplaying-game magazine Casus Belli 89. Category:Stimulants
