Reedox-stew
1436

Reedox-stew was a kind of stew. Around 10 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion a young Human pilot named Han Solo ate a bowl of reedox-stew in an infirmary located in Colony One, the capital of the planet Ylesia, after coming to be a cargo pilot for the Cult of The One and The All, a bogus religious organization set up by the Besadii Hutt clan. Solo found the stew to be filling, but he was rather indifferent about it. Reedox-stew was mentioned in The Paradise Snare, a 1997 novel written by A. C. Crispin as the first book of The Han Solo Trilogy. *The Paradise Snare Category:Soups and stews
