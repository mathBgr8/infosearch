It had room to carry up to 40 passengers. The craft were produced sometime around 4 ABY, when Star Tours referred to them as "new." thumb|left|250px|Technical specifications for the StarSpeeder 3000 The StarSpeeder 3000s were very similar in to their predecessors, the StarSpeeder 1000s, both in shape and equipment. The craft was still boxy, with a downwardly angled front side, and still featured the usual five automated doors on both starboard and portside. With its five seating rows, the 3000 could still be boarded by no more than forty passengers at a time. Like with the StarSpeeder 1000, an astromech droid was loaded in a socket at the bow of the ship. Unlike the StarSpeeder 1000, however, the StarSpeeder 3000 lacked several features such as the IC-360 camera droid and multi-terrain skids. Those transports were normally piloted by an RX-Series pilot droid, assisted by its astromech droid navigator. The ship was considered fast and maneuverable for a transport. Given the lowered risk usually involved in interplanetary tourism, the StarSpeeder 3000 was equipped only with minimal deflector shields and two simple laser cannons, the latter of which were nevertheless as abnormally powerful as the concealed cannons on the StarSpeeder 1000, being able to destroy a TIE Fighter with a single shot. However, the tour brochure indicated that the hyperdrive, or "warp drive," was fairly advanced. Despite the agency's claim that the ships themselves were highly advanced, they were often in need of repair. thumb|right|250px|A StarSpeeder 3000 on Endor The StarSpeeders were used by Star Tours starting around 4 ABY, and eventually used during their push to capitalize on the new found notoriety of Endor at the time with the Endor Express route. After a series of mishaps in the months following, including a StarSpeeder's accidental involvement in a space battle, the tour company shut down. The vessels were used for years afterward by other entities, including Corellian Translines in 17 ABY. The new ship, called the StarSpeeder 1000, is white with an orange pinstripe, while the StarSpeeder 3000 is white with a blue pinstripe.
