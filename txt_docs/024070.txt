Ylesian Republic
2677

The Ylesian Republic was the authority established on Ylesia after it was granted to the Peace Brigade by the Praetorite Vong, following the capture of Ylesia, the Ylesian Republic had nominal independence but the Yuuzhan Vong exercised a great deal of influence and it was effectively a collaborationist government. It was led by a President, and governed by a Senate. Thrackan Sal-Solo was appointed to be the first President of the Ylesian Republic and Commander-in-Chief of the Peace Brigade. The Ylesian Republic and the Peace Brigade were eventually defeated during the Battle of Ylesia. *The New Jedi Order: Ylesia Category:Hutt Space governments Category:Yuuzhan Vong organizations
