thumb|right|250px|[[General Grievous was Supreme Commander of the Droid Army]] Supreme Commander was a high rank borne by military and paramilitary officers in the service of most of the major galactic powers of the Imperial and post-Imperial eras. It was similar to the title of Commander-in-Chief. As most major states and powers of these eras used at least one version of this rank, full information is divided among several pages: *Supreme commander of the Imperial military &mdash; used by the senior-most officer of Imperial High Command, and held direct control over the Imperial Armed Forces. It was once held by Grand General Cassio Tagge. **Supreme commander of the Imperial fleet &mdash; the title used by the Sith Lord Darth Vader following the Cylo Hijacking. *Supreme Commander &mdash; used by the King of Mon Cala, who led the defense forces of the monarchy. *Supreme commander &mdash; used by an official within the New Republic. *Supreme Commander of the Alliance Armed Forces &mdash; used by an official within the Alliance Armed Forces who, along with only the Alliance Chief of State, could award the Kalidor Crescent. *Supreme fleet commander &mdash; used by Admiral Gial Ackbar in the Alliance Fleet. *Supreme Martial Commander of the Separatist Droid Armies &mdash; used by the leader of the Confederacy of Independent Systems armed forces. It was held by General Grievous throughout the Clone Wars. *Alliance Supreme Commander &mdash; title used by the Chief of State of the Rebel Alliance. *Supreme Allied Commanders &mdash; title used by senior Rebel Alliance military personnel that led one of the branches of the Alliance Military. **Supreme Commander of the Rebel Fleet &mdash; a title used by General Carlist Rieekan. *Supreme Commander &mdash; used by the Jedi Order during the Galactic War. *Supreme Commander &mdash; used by the King of Mon Cala. *Supreme Commander &mdash; used by the Yuuzhan Vong's warrior caste. *Supreme commander of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Force &mdash; used by the Chiss Ascendancy for the leader of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Force. *Supreme Commander of the Confederation military &mdash; title used by the leader of the Confederation Fleet. *Supreme Commander of the Corellian Defense Force &mdash; title used by Wedge Antilles as leader of the Corellian Home Fleet. *Supreme Commander of the Droid Armies &mdash; used by Separatist Alliance General Grievous during the Clone Wars. *Supreme Commander &mdash; used by the leader of the Vagaari armed forces. *Supreme Commander &mdash; used by the Galactic Empire. *Supreme Commander of the Republic Military &mdash; the title given to the leader of the Republic Military. *Commander-in-Chief *Commander-in-Chief/Legends Category:Military ranks
