Byhdee Kees
1407

Byhdee Kees was a male Gran who was active during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by the Coruscant Police for questioning, relating to the smuggling of death sticks on Malastare. He had known connections to the RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate. A bounty was posted on him, 3000 credits alive, 1500 dead.
