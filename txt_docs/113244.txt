Mon Ubris
992

Mon Ubris was a floating city located on the planet of Mon Cala. The city was poor and had a reputation for its criminal activity and a large homeless population. In 1 BBY, Chewbacca and his partner, Han Solo, came to the city in order to find the fabled Yavin Vassilika. *Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika 3 *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Cities on Mon Cala
