Plo Smarx
1204

Plo Smarx was a scientist working for Hydrospeare Corporation. The leader of Design Team Beta, Dr. Smarx worked in several projects to develop aquatic military technology for the Galactic Empire. In 1 ABY, Dr. Smarx was arrested, accused of sedition and espionage.
