*Dave Marshall |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=August 16, 2006 |published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 6 |reprinted in= |pages=18 |timeline=c. 19 BBYStar Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 6 dates its four stories to just before and during the events of , which The Essential Atlas dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Means and Ends" is a comic story in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 6. It features Plo Koon and Kit Fisto trapped in an underwater prison. In an M'Bardi prison, a riot has broken loose. Jedi Masters Kit Fisto and Plo Koon arrive to sort things out. The prisoners immediately unite against the two Jedi, and lightsabers are drawn. During the battle, the Jedi realize the difference in their views: while Fisto believes in the value of all life, Koon argues that "scum like this can't be rehabilitated". Defeating the rioters using their own personal methods, the two descend into the sewers, where they meet a two-headed thug divided into two personalities: one peaceful, one argumentative. Koon finally gives in to Fisto's arguments and chooses to knock the two out cold rather than kill them. Their escape is broken short, however, when the bounty hunter Durge appears, explaining that it was he who started the riot in order to lure Jedi into his trap. He is temporarily defeated when a boulder falls upon his head, an event triggered by the actions of the two-headed prisoner.
