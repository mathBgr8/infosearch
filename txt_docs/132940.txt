Interdictor (disambiguation)
2195

Interdictor, or Interdictor-class, could refer to several things: *Interdictor, a general term for any ship capable of generating a gravity well **Immobilizer 418 cruiser, a class of cruiser and Interdictor vessel **Interdictor-class Star Destroyer, a class of Star Destroyer derived from the Imperial-class Star Destroyer that was 1,600 meters in length, serving as an Interdictor vessel **Interdictor line, a line of ships used by the Galactic Empire ***Interdictor-class heavy cruiser, a class of heavy cruiser of the Star Destroyer design family that was an Interdictor vessel ****Interdictor, a specific Interdictor-class Star Destroyer *Interdictor ship, a term for ships that could produce a interdiction field **Immobilizer 418 Interdictor-class Heavy Cruiser, built by Sienar Fleet Systems, the most widespread Interdictor type class of ships in service during the Galactic Civil War. The terms Interdictor Cruiser or Interdictor usually refer to the Immobilizer 418 **Interdictor-class cruiser, the original hyperspace interdiction cruiser. It served in the Old Sith Wars, thousands of years before the Galactic Civil War **Interdictor-class Star Destroyer, built by Sienar Fleet Systems using the hull of an Imperial-class Star Destroyer, and designed to offset the Immobilizer 418's combat shortcomings **Vua'spar interdictor, a Yuuzhan Vong warship *TIE/IT Interdictor starfighter, a bomber class
