The name Chertyl Ruluwoor has been used for two different characters. You may either be looking for: *The Selonian who was sent to Corran Horn's CorSec unit as a cultural exchange. *The Human Corellian involved in an investigation about a series of terrorist attacks aimed at Selonian dens sometime after the destruction of the first Death Star.
