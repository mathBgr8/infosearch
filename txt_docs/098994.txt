Coffee
2223

}} Coffee was a non-alcoholic beverage that was most often served extremely hot and made from a specific type of bean. Emperor Palpatine owned farms on various planets that provided the beans to produce high quality coffee and vine-coffee, solely for the use of his court. Coffee could be prepared and dispensed from a coffee machine, and was usually served in a coffee cup.
