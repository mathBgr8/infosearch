The book was written by Gunray's alleged mistress, Lora Besh, as a provocative tell-all on the Viceroy's life in the aftermath of his numerous trials in the Galactic Republic's Supreme Court following his failed invasion of the planet Naboo in 32 BBY.The New Essential Chronology The book was released on the same date as another TriPlanetary Press product, former Supreme Chancellor Finis Valorum's autobiography, Holding Back the Tide. While Valorum's book floundered, Gunray On Top was met with wild enthusiasm and spent at least fourteen weeks on the top-ten bestseller list. The impressive disparity in popularity between the two works was reported on in the CoCo District Edition HoloNet News two months after the book's release date. Gunray on Top was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News published on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
