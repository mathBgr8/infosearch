Joint Chiefs of Staff
1561

The Joint Chiefs of Staff were the leaders of the various branches of the Galactic Alliance's military forces. They included the Chief of Army Operations, the Chief of Naval Operations, and the Chief of Starfighter Command. The Supreme Commander served as chair of the joint chiefs. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Galactic Alliance organizations
