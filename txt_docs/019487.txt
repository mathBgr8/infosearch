Tetsu Clan
3673

thumb|right|200px|Greedo, arguably the most famous member of Tetsu Clan }} The Tetsu Clan, alternatively known as the Tetsus, was a clan of Rodians from the planet Rodia. They were forced to flee during the Rodian Clan Wars and some colonized a jungle planet. When Navik the Red ordered his own Chattza Clan to exterminate them, some Tetsus fled first to Rodia's moon Enak, Navik's killers eventually tracked them down, killing a number and dispersing the rest. Navik also personally attacked them on the jungle planet in 4 BBY. A group moved to Nar Shaddaa to hide in the densely populated underworld. The most notable member of the Clan was the infamous bounty hunter Greedo, killed by Han Solo. It also included his cousin Beedo and his uncle Avaro Sookcool.
