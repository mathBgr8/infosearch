Cleaning droid
4610

thumb|right|150px|An [[A-LT Utility Droid]] Cleaning droids or custodial droids were a type of droid whose purpose was to maintain cleanliness. During the reign of the Galactic Empire, a cleaning droid cleaned the windows of some houses on Level 3204, including those of the apartment belonging to Anandra Milon's family. Infiltrator demolition droids could disguise themselves as sweeper droids for the purpose of covert operations. Custodial droids were used to perform minor tasks aboard the starship Legacy Run. Hedda Casset noticed one such droid shortly before her ship's destruction.The High Republic: Light of the Jedi Custodial droids appeared in the 2021 novel The High Republic: Light of the Jedi.
