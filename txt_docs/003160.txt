Star Wars Adventure Journal 6
3332

The Star Wars Adventure Journal 6 is the sixth issue of the role-playing magazine Star Wars Adventure Journal. It was published by West End Games in May of 1995 and contained several articles, role-playing adventures and short stories. Take a fascinating voyage through the Star Wars galaxy with the Star Wars Adventure Journal. Each issue features exciting adventures, source material and tales from the Star Wars universe! New York Times-best-selling author Kathy Tyers returns to the adventures of Tinian I'att, the former industrial heiress whose life was ruined by ambitious Imperial Moff. On the run from the Empire, she and her friends find shelter on Silver Station, where Tinian hopes to find somebody who can use the c-boards stolen from her family's prototype energy shield. Join Tinian, her friends, Rebel supporter Una Poot and Wookiee bounty hunter Chenlambec in To Fight Another Day. Other features in this issue include: *Alex Winger's final Rendezvous With Destiny. *A rough and tough speeder ride with Swoop Gangs. *The misadventures of Galactic News Service reporter Kella Rand. *A quest for a long-lost artifact in Relic. *"Wanted by Cracken" *"Scouts' Dispatch" *"Galaxywide NewsNets" *"The Greel Wood Haven" *"To Fight Another Day" *"Rendezvous with Destiny" *"Ringers" *"Swoop Gangs" *"Finder's Fee" *"Kella Rand, Reporting..." The summary on the back cover of this issue reuses the sentence "The misadventures of Galactic News Service reporter Kella Rand." that was mistakenly included in the previous issue's summary. 06
