Kyrotech Corporate Combine
677

The Kyrotech Corporate Combine was a company which produced the Kyrotech Blademaster X-260 series of assassin droids. They programmed all of their droids with a sub-routine which would not allow them to act against the interests of the Combine. Category:Droid manufacturers
