Duman Yaght
1905

Duman Yaght was a Yuuzhan Vong Commander of Domain Yaght during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The commanding officer of the Exquisite Death, he received seventeen Jedi captives from Fitzgibbon Lane, who was in truth Lando Calrissian, in the plot to deliver the Jedi to Myrkr for the Mission to Myrkr in 27 ABY. After torturing the Jedi aboard, he later held Ganner Rhysode hostage when the Jedi hijacked the ship.
