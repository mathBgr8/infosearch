Chuck Sperati
832

Chuck Sperati wrote the short story "Droid Trouble" for the Star Wars Adventure Journal 3 in 1994, which was later archived on Hyperspace. Chuck also wrote the article "A Deal Gone Sour" for Star Wars Adventure Journal 4.
