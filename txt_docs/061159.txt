Boor-Daa
1472

Boor-Daa was a male Kajain'sa'Nikto who was one of the co-directors of the Stark Commercial Combine. In 44 BBY, he thought Iaco Stark too ambitious and forward-thinking for both his own and the Combine's good, but advocated allowing him to continue to lead during the Stark Hyperspace War.
