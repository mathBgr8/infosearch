Miv'rah Firestorm
3173

Miv'rah Firestorm was a highly modified Nestt-class light freighter owned and operated by the Sullustan smuggler Gaor Tembon. The Miv'rah Firestorm was a Nestt-class light freighter that had been extensively modified. It had numerous hidden smuggling compartments above and below the desks of the ship. This required rearrangement of most of the interior life support systems. It was equipped with two quad laser cannons that could be retracted under the external hull plating to discourage custom inspectors. Due to the increased power requirements for the weaponry, the Nepton power core had to be reworked and six Koensayr TLB power converters had to be installed to handle the excess power. These modification meant that the ship produced much more energy than was legal.
