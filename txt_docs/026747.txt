Bartam
1780

Bartam was a Human Grand Moff of the Galactic Empire. He conspired with Grand Moff Trachta and Moff Kadir to kill Emperor Palpatine and Darth Vader, but tensions amongst the conspirators led to infighting, and Bartam had Gauer, another of the conspirators, shoot Trachta.
