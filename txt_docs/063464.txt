Kavil
1783

Kavil was a pirate who led the Kavil's Corsairs pirate group. He was a Human male with blue eyes, balding brown hair, and a heavily-scarred face. In the months after the Battle of Endor he became an Imperial privateer through the hope of attaining social promotion for his duties.
