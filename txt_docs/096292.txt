It played an important role in the outcome of the war while in the hands of Anakin and many others after his death. thumb|left|230px|Anakin with his lightsaber shortly before his death in [[27 ABY/Legends|27 ABY.]] During his early Jedi training, Anakin was part of a group of Jedi that retrieved Obi-Wan Kenobi's third lightsaber from Orloc.Junior Jedi Knights: Kenobi's Blade While the Yuuzhan Vong occupied Yavin 4, Anakin added a Lambent crystal that he harvested into his lightsaber. This allowed Anakin to use "Vongsense," which allowed him to perceive the Vong and their creations. Anakin Solo's lightsaber had a special affinity to the light side of the Force.Legacy Era Campaign Guide In 27 ABY Anakin led a team of Jedi on a mission to destroy the Voxyn cloning facility on the Koros-Strohna Baanu Rass. Though he fought valiantly, he died in combat with Yuuzhan Vong to protect his siblings and fellow Jedi. His lightsaber was soon after taken by Nom Anor. thumb|right|170px|Ganner Rhysode wielding Anakin's lightsaber at the entrance to the World Brain. During the Mission to Myrkr Jacen Solo was also captured and taken to the Yuuzhan Vong occupied Coruscant. While in Vong captivity, Jacen was offered Anakin's lightsaber in an attempt to turn him to the True Way. Jacen accepted, but deceived them when he lied of his conversion. Ganner Rhysode came to rescue Jacen and used Anakin's lightsaber to kill countless Yuuzhan Vong warriors, while Jacen sabotaged the World Brain. Ganner was killed in combat and the lightsaber was taken yet again by Nom Anor. Later was used by Master Shaper Qelah Kwaad to demonstrate Supreme Overlord Shimrra Jamaane improvements of vonduun armor on her newly shaped Yuuzhan Vong elite soldiers then again taken by Nom Anor, who presented it to Shimrra. During the Second Battle of Coruscant, Luke Skywalker confronted Shimrra. As they fought, Shimrra mocked Luke with his nephew's lightsaber. To the astonishment and dismay of Shimrra, Luke used the Force to pull the lightsaber to him, and then used it to decapitate the Dread Lord. With Shimrra dead, the Yuuzhan Vong surrendered and left for the Unknown Regions with Zonama Sekot.
