Eickarie language
741

Eickarie was the language spoken by the reptilian Eickarie. In their culture, "left-handed" was associated with evil and deceit while "right-handed" was associated with good and truth. *Shak was the Eickarie word for ready. *Ha-ran mish-ra hee-sae sha-kae drof-si-shae-ral translated to "I am Ha-ran of the Family Mish-ra, Clan Sha-kae, prince of the Tribe Si-shae-ral." *Sha-mees craa shes-ayi translated to "I have praised their valor in your name; I trust that is acceptable." *Fool's Bargain *Survivor's Quest Category:Languages
