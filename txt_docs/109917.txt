It was piloted by a driver in an open seat. The vehicle was armed with two rotating blaster cannons and used a large rudder to steer. During the Galactic Civil War, the Security Scout was used by Rebel Alliance personnel on Endor, including Princess Leia Organa and Jedi Knight Luke Skywalker. The Security Scout vehicle was created as part of Kenner's line of Body-Rigs in 1985. In Europe it was released in a closed box, while in the US it was packed on a blister card.
