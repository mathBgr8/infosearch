Stendaff
1685

Stendaff was a Sacheen-class light escort vessel that served in the New Republic Defense Fleet during the Black Fleet Crisis. The warship was a part of New Republic Intelligence Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt's task force stationed at Gmar Askilon, following the departure of the Teljkon Vagabond into hyperspace. *Shield of Lies *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sacheen-class light escorts Category:Starships of the New Republic
