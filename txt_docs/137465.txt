|masters= |apprentices= }} Gray Wing was the call sign of a Rebel Alliance pilot who flew as a subordinate of Gray Leader Colonel Horton Salm with Gray Squadron, one of the four main Rebel battle formations participating in the Battle of Endor. "Gray Wing" was among the earliest fatalities of the historic battle. The 1983 novelization of uses the term "Wing" to refer to both starfighter units and individual pilots.
