Kettrifee Air Mover
1377

The Kettrifee Air Mover was a model of troop transport employed by Wookiees. thumb|left|180px|Air Mover at various angles. It was maneuvered by repulsor jets and propelled by a single main engine mounted to the rear left. The Air Mover displayed the Wookiees' characteristic love of asymmetry, as did most of their vehicles. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Repulsorcraft troop transport classes Category:Wookiee vehicle classes
