Yuza Bre
985

Yuza Bre was a high class restaurant built on Coruscant after the Galactic Alliance liberated the planet from the Yuuzhan Vong. It was said that reservations took months in advance to get, and it was one of the favorite restaurants of Chief of State Cal Omas. During the Swarm War, Aryn Dro Thul bought the restaurant after she had been denied repeated requests to see Chief Omas. Madame Thul mused that Omas would suddenly find his request for reservations repeatedly denied. The Yuza Bre was owned by Bornaryn Trading as of the Swarm War.
