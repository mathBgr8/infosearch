Planka
2079

Planka was a Rebel Alliance MC40a Light Calamari Cruiser. It placed Mine Type Bs in an attempt to capture the corvette group Dagger. However, two Missile Boats arrived and destroyed the mines, allowing a transport to dock and repair the two corvettes. It was presumed that they destroyed the Planka.
