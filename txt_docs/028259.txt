Following the outbreak of occult violence, Dorosii attempted to convince Republic officials that intervention in the situation was unnecessary, as local Hutt authorities would deal with the M'dweshuu attacks. Despite the Hutt's reassurances, the Jedi Council planned to investigate the murders anyway and, according to HoloNet News, likely intended to send a task force into the Periphery themselves. An edition of HoloNet News CoCo District Edition later documented on both the M'dweshuu cult murders and Dorosii's exchanges with Republic officials. Dorosii believed that it was not necessary for the Republic to get involved in ending the M'dweshuu murders, though the Jedi Council did not share these views.
