Jacen (stuffed creature)
458

Jacen was a stuffed tauntaun that Jacen Solo gave to his daughter, Allana, on a visit to Hapes. Allana named it Jacen so that she could talk to it when Solo did not visit. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Millennium Falcon Category:Entertainment
