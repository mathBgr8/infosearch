Kedorzhan pub
1440

Sometime in 3964 BBY during the Mandalorian Wars, a Kedorzhan pub in the Lower City of the planet Taris was visited by Zayne Carrick and Marn Hierogryph when they were on the run from the First WatchCircle led by Zayne's Jedi Master, Lucien Draay. While Carrick was skeptical of hiding there, Hierogryph reassured him that the bar was mainly visited by Kedorzhans who had blurred vision for hours after working long shifts in the mines. Hierogryph then jokingly stated that they only had to worry if the news broadcasted smells on the transmitters.
