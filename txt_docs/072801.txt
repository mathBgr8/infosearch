Battle of the Angor system
1040

The Battle of the Angor system occurred shortly before the start of the Yuuzhan Vong War. In the battle, the Dozen and Two Avengers under Jedi Knight Kyp Durron attacked smugglers in the Outer Rim Territories' Angor system.
