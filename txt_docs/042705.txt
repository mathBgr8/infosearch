Honka was a Rodian male who frequented Dex's Diner in the CoCo Town district of the planet Coruscant during the years leading up to the Clone Wars, a conflict between the Galactic Republic and Confederacy of Independent Systems that started in 22 BBY.The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. He was often in the company of his female friend Sidewa. Before the start of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY, Honka sat in a booth with Sidewa and a Human inside the diner as Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi arrived to ask diner owner Dexter Jettster about a Mk I SaberdartEndless Vigil he acquired after an attempted assassination on Senator Padmé Amidala. After Jettster and Kenobi sat down in a booth, Honka took a drink from a cup and talked with his companions. Honka had green skin and black eyes.
