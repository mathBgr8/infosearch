C-3 protocol droid
681

The C-3 protocol droid was a household protocol droid that was manufactured on the factory moon of Telti by Cybot Galactica. It was programmed with many different functions. It was the predecessor of the C-4 protocol droid and the successor of the C-2 protocol droid. *Children of the Jedi *The New Rebellion Category:C-series protocol droid models
