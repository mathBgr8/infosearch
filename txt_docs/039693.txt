Cracken's Rebel Operatives |prerequisites= |upgrades= |first awarded= |last awarded= }} The Nebula for Bravery was a military decoration awarded to Alliance to Restore the Republic staff and Special Forces members. The award was given twice to General Airen Cracken, and one was waiting to be presented to an agent known at the time only as Jan Strange (later known to be Jan Ors), who infiltrated Imperial Intelligence to engage in counter-intelligence, sabotage, and covert extraction. *Cracken's Rebel Field Guide *Cracken's Rebel Operatives Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic awards
