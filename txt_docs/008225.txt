Giant Flog
2114

|pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes=Red |skin=Red |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=The Cauldron |masters= |apprentices= }} "Giant" Flog was a gladiator owned by Kesivo. Kesivo purchased his prize gladiator during a trading mission in the Moddell sector. Flog survived in the gladiator arena for two years. He wielded a huge flail consisting of a craggy hunk of rock on a chain. His origins remained unknown, and he had little command over any language. Rumors abounded that Flog was the result of an occult summoning by a demon, or a crude amalgamation of the parts of dead gladiators. When Asajj Ventress entered The Cauldron to challenge the Rattatakan gladiators, Flog joined the fight. Flog killed a Cauldron wampa that was about to attack Ventress. They then engaged, she severed Flog's flail, sending it flying through the air to smash into the viewing box housing Kesivo. Flog's benefactor was killed instantly.
