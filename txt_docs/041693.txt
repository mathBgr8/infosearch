During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. the Gran attended the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall on the planetStar Wars: Complete Locations Coruscant. During Whoma's visit, the bounty hunter Zam Wesell ran into the club trying to escape from the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker. When Skywalker separated from his partner to survey the crowd, Whoma was stood with a group of clubgoers, including the Ithorian Ror Ithh. When Skywalker walked near the group again, Whoma and Ithh interacted with two Rodians. Whoma had orange skin and black eyes. Being a Gran, the gambler added to the interplanetary feel of the club on the night of their visit. Whoma wore gray armor during their visit to the club. Nic Whoma was created for the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002.Star Wars Year by Year: A Visual Chronicle The character's costume, partially created by special effects artist Nick Dudman, was reused from the character Mawhonic, who appeared in the previous prequel trilogy film, 1999's Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The Outlander Club scenes, where the character was portrayed by an uncredited extra, were shot on July 17, 2000 at Fox Studios, Sydney.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones Prior to the release of Episode II, the character made their first appearanceStar Wars: Episode II — Attack of the Clones 1 on April 17, 2002, with the release of the first issue of the Dark Horse Comics adaptation of the film. The issue was written by Henry Gilroy and illustrated by Jan Duursema. The character's name was first mentioned in an image in the May 2, 2003 StarWars.com web article "Cantina Roll-Call: Shedding Light on Some Alien Aliases", where it was applied to an image of Mawhonic. The name was first tied to the character appearing in Episode II through the StarWars.com Image Attack feature on November 6, 2003.
