|intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Paperback |pages=128 |isbn=9780553486520 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline=3 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure |preceded by=A New Hope |followed by=Return of the Jedi }} Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: The Empire Strikes Back is a non-canon Legends junior novel written by Christopher Golden and illustrated by Phil Franké. It is the second Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure book, and it was published on August 10, 1998 by Bantam Skylark. The novel adapts the 1980 film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, with the reader taking the role of Luke Skywalker's best friend and choosing which storyline to follow. What price would you pay to learn the ways of a Jedi knight? The fight against evil rages on. You have the power to decide your future&mdash;and the fate of the galaxy. You could go to Dagobah and learn the ways of the Force from Yoda, but Darth Vader tempts you with the power of the dark side. Will you help your friends in the Rebellion fight the Emperor, or will you betray Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, and all who love you to seize unimagined power for yourself? Although the first book allowed for multiple different endings, this book goes on as if all the events from A New Hope took place. You are Luke Skywalker's best friend and a lieutenant in the Rebel Alliance currently stationed at Echo Base on the planet Hoth. When the Empire discovers your base, you are given the choice to either join Luke and head for Dagobah to further your Jedi training, or you can go with Princess Leia and Han Solo on the Millennium Falcon to evade the Empire. *Leave Luke to die by the wampa and try to get back to Echo Base, but freeze to death (non-canon). *Do not go with Han's plan to cut open the tauntaun and try to walk back while everyone dies (non-canon). *Join Han and the others, meet Lando, get captured by the Empire, and escape. **Try to fight Darth Vader and get sent to Kessel (non-canon). **Han is encased in carbonite and taken by Boba Fett. ***Let Lando help you escape but fail to rescue Han.
