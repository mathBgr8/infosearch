The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 150
1291

The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 150 is the one hundred fiftieth issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Monthly series of comic reprints, published in September 1981. As the cover tease shows, this issue was intended to include part one of "The Pandora Effect", but a note on the first page says deadline woes made it impossible to include in this issue. Instead, it reprints Star Wars (1977) 16. The Pandora Effect was printed entirely in The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 151. *Star Wars (1977) 16 Category:The Empire Strikes Back Monthly issues
