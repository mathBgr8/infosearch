Khlor
1794

Khlor was a highly skilled bounty hunter who specialized in live capture and tracking. Adorned in dark clothes and a full-body cloak, and with a jet-black Velusian fursnake named Sting coiled around his neck and shoulders, he presented a harrowing image to client and prey alike. He specialized in nonlethal weaponry, employing stun grenades, magna bolas, electro-nets, blowguns, and a modified R-88 suppressor riot rifle to capture his marks alive.
