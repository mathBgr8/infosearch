Hontho
1983

Hontho was a male Gamorrean henchman of 8t88, along with Trox and Rol after the Battle of Endor. In 5 ABY they served 8t88, a dignitary on a personal mission for Jerec on Sulon, and they were with him in the Government House in Barons Hed. They coped with the demented droid's various eccentricities.Dark Forces: Rebel Agent Grontho was present when Jerec's majordomo attempted to explain why the droid was assigned to a humble room; when the droid asked him to approach to listen him better, Hontho exchanged looks with Rol, knowing that his amplifiers could hear the slightest sound from a distance. Shuffling forward, he was grabbed by the droid and urinated, causing the human guards to wrinkle their noses, except Hontho, who didn't notice.
