Taxtro Grave
2423

|masters= |apprentices= }} Taxtro Grave was a Human male who served the Galactic Empire as a Stormtrooper sniper. Grave was stationed aboard the Reprisal in 0.5 ABY. He used a T-28 repeating sniper rifle, and joined the Hand of Judgment after he and his friends, Daric LaRone, Korlo Brightwater, Saberan Marcross, and Joak Quiller were forced to leave (on a TL-1800 Suwantek Systems freighter) after the accidental death of Imperial Security Bureau agent Major Drelfin. He then, after leaving the Reprisal, went to Drunost and stopped a swoop gang from attacking a farmer with brand new equipment. They then fled from Drunost and went to Janusar where they had their speeders confiscated and attacked Cav'Saran to get them back.
