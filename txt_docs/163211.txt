Concussion grenade mortar
748

thumb|right|250px|A concussion grenade mortar mounted on a Mobile Defense Unit The concussion grenade mortar was found on Imperial Mobile Defense Units. Also, the Hutt Cartel would use them as automated turrets on worlds like Nal Hutta. It would fire a volley of eight grenades at an enemy target until it or its target is destroyed. The grenades had low accuracy but did do damage to enemy units in the surrounding area. *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Category:Grenade launchers Category:Vehicle-mounted launchers
