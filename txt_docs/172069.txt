Doppler sensor array
675

A Doppler sensor array was an array of sensors keyed to the Doppler effect. At least one such sensor was found on Prince Xizor's Coruscant castle. However, this array was shattered by an erratically-piloted Millennium Falcon. Any surviving remnants of the device were subsequently destroyed when the building collapsed. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel Category:Emplacement sensors Category:Sensor arrays
