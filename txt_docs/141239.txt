Pandor's Hydrospanner
691

Pandor's Hydrospanner was a chain of repair and maintenance shops. They sold equipment for starships and starfighters, among other items. Most Pandor's Hydrospanner shops also traded in smaller starships. One of their most popular shops was situated in Sector Alpha on Zirtran's Anchor. They also had a store on Ord Mynock. *Gamemaster Toolkit: Live-Action Adventures Category:Repair and salvage yards
