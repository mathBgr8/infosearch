Jiktha
1447

Jiktha was a male Jawa junk peddler who lived in Mos Espa during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Jabba the Hutt for selling him a faulty droid. Eventually, Jango Fett claimed the bounty on Jiktha some time after the Battle of Naboo.
