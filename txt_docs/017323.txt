|system=Tirahnn system |coordinates=N-8 |xyz= |distance= |suns=Tirahnn |orbited= |position=4 |moons=24 |routes=Perlemian Trade Route |lengthday= |lengthyear= |class= |diameter= |atmosphere= |climate= |gravity= |terrain=Gas giant |water= |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Ijol was a gas giant with twenty-four moons. The planet was fourth in orbit around the Tirahnn sun. Ijol resided in the Tirahnn system of the Zeemacht Cluster in the Inner Rim. The Tirahnn system, and therefore Ijol, lay along the Perlemian Trade Route hyperlane. By 17,000 BBY, Ijol's system also resided along a hyperspace route that ceased to exist by 3976 BBY.Per The Essential Guide to Warfare, by approximately 17,000 BBY the Tirahnn system, and therefore Ijol, was on a hyperspace route that The Essential Atlas shows linked it to the Filordis and Ktil systems. Per The Essential Atlas, that route had ceased to exist by 3976 BBY. Ijol was introduced in "Triplet Threat," a roleplaying mini-adventure written by Jason Fry for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game and published on Wizards.com on January 22, 2004. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas subsequently placed the Tirahnn system, and therefore Ijol, in grid square N-8.
