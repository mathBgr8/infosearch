Galidon Okanor
1141

Galidon Okanor was a Human male art appraiser and fence who lived on Corellia. He liked to haggle, but was willing to pay high prices. When Han Solo returned to Corellia after the destruction of the Bria, he sold a paledor statue and a black Krayt dragon pearl to Okanor for nearly 10,000 credits. *The Paradise Snare *The Hutt Gambit *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corellians Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Merchants
