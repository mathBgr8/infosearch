Benevolence
1350

Benevolence was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate modified as a medical frigate. The captain of Benevolence was a Herglic called Melchi who discovered it drifting in orbit around the planet Pelagon after the Mecetti Purge of House Pelagia. He assumed control of the vessel and began to mount a medical relief effort. He expanded his efforts to disaster zones across the Tapani sector and later got funding from the Great Council on Procopia. The frigate had 550 beds, 12 Bacta tanks and five operating theaters. The medical personnel included 25 medics and 50 Emdee medical droids.
