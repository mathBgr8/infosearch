Zraii
2014

Zraii was a Verpine who was in charge of repairs and maintenance for Rogue Squadron's fleet of X-wings, as well as other Alliance ships. He also mind wiped and upgraded Wedge Antilles' droid, R5-D2 (commonly known as Mynock), turning it into an R5-G8 named Gate.
