Productivity Day
908

Productivity Day was a holiday in the Tapani sector. The celebration highlighted the strong work ethic present in the sector, and celebrated hard work and the contribution of previous generations who made the sector prosperous. It fell between the months of Nelona and Helona. *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector holidays
