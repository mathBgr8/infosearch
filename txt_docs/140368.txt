She attended the Boonta Eve Classic that was held in 32 BBY, during which she was widowed when Ratts died in a fatal crash. She had recently been released from a local hospital, presumably after giving birth to her youngest son, Doby. Some time following her husband's death, her own brother-in-law sold her daughter, Djulla, into slavery, to be bought by the notorious podracer Sebulba. Djulla would later be rescued by Anakin Skywalker. Mrs. Tyerell appears in the Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace "Complete Podrace Grid Sequence" deleted scene from the film's 2001 DVD release.
