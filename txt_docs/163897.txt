|model= |type=Medium power armor |culture= |creators= |created= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners= |locations= |cost= |value= |structure= |size= |color= |material= |length= |width= |height= |weight= |protection= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation= }} DZ 17X Nemesis was an extremely rare powersuit battle armor model manufactured by Mili-Corp. It was commonly found in the day of the Old Republic but around the time of the Galactic Civil War, the number of intact Nemesis units was very small. The armor provided full body protection against kinetic and energy attacks and came with an inbuilt Multi-Frequency Targeting and Acquisition System and a 180 degree macrobinocular vision providing sensor system. A DEMP gun was installed in the left forearm armor and a light repeating blaster on the right one. A climate-controlled body glove was also standard equipment. The Nemesis armor was designed with corporate militias and security forces in mind. While common prior to the Clone Wars, most Nemesis units were destroyed in the many conflicts leading up to the end of the Old Republic. Around the time of the Galactic Civil War, it was believed that there were only six intact and fully operational Nemesis powersuits around.
