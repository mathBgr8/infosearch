Ramoan relocation project
945

The Ramoan relocation project was a project headed by the New Jedi Order to use a former Yuuzhan Vong slaveship, the Maledoth, in order to relocate the population of a dying world to a more suitable place. The project came to a standstill when Lowbacca left to answer a call to the Unknown Regions from Raynar Thul. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King Category:Galactic Alliance organizations
