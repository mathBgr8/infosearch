Daxis Outpost
902

Daxis Outpost was a New Republic research installation that was located in the Jandolhoon system in the Outer Rim of the galaxy. The outpost was used by the Bureau of Scouting and Exploration Services as a staging ground for expeditions into the Galactic Rim. Doctor Maxina Sensis was the Daxis Outpost's Chief Researcher.
