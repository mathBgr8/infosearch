Managing Council
1615

The Managing Council, was the legislature of the Outbound Flight Colony. The Council, by the requirement of its founding mandate, consisted of member-representatives from its two factions: the Survivors and the Colonists. It shared power with the Colony's Director, and both worked together to set policy. Brace Tarkosa and Jobe Keely represented Survivors on the Council.
