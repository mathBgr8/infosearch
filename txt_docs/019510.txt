Extolled
680

The Extolled was the new name for the lowest Yuuzhan Vong caste known as the Shamed Ones. Even though they were considered to be extolled, some Yuuzhan Vong (especially the Warrior caste) still did not show them the new respect they had gained and treated them as untouchables. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *X-Wing: Mercy Kill *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Yuuzhan Vong castes
