Ninth Fleet (Galactic Alliance)
1785

The Ninth Fleet was one of the nine main battlegroups of the Galactic Alliance Defense Fleet, serving the Alliance government during the Second Galactic Civil War in 40 ABY.Legacy of the Force: Tempest During the crisis, the Ninth Fleet was based at Crix Base in the Coruscant system, but it was still enigmatic to high-ranking Alliance figures like Grand Master Luke Skywalker of the New Jedi Order. In fact, it was the sole GA fleet under Galactic Alliance Guard command. Elements of the Ninth Fleet took part in the Battle of Gilatter VIII. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Exile *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas 9th Fleet
