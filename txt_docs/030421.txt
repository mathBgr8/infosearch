Rejuvenator-class Star Destroyer
2002

The Rejuvenator-class Star Destroyer was a class of warship used by the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances. These Star Destroyers were created by the New Republic during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The Rejuvenators continued in service of the Galactic Alliance and were part of major campaigns in 29 ABY, during the last months of the war against the Yuuzhan Vong. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Star Destroyer classes Category:Starship classes of the Galactic Alliance Category:Starship classes of the New Republic
