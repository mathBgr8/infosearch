Acme Robopolish
774

Acme Robopolish was a window-cleaning company that did business during the Galactic Civil War. Utilizing droid models such as the INS-444 and the CLE-004, the company was accused of plagiarism by the jizz musician Fitz Roi. The name is probably a reference to the fictional Acme Corporation from the Looney Tunes universe, which was famous for producing nearly all kinds of imaginable gadgets. *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Vendors and sellers
