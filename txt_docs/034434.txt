Battle of Corbos
5107

The Battle of Corbos, also called the Rout of Corbos, was a battle fought between the Jedi Order and Dark Jedi. It was the last battle of the Hundred-Year Darkness. Having fought for nearly a century, the Jedi and their rival Dark Jedi made a final stand at Corbos, a mining world. Ajunta Pall, the leader of the Dark Jedi, personally slew more than a dozen Jedi before the Dark Jedi were defeated at the battle's end. Karness Muur also participated in the battle and was one of the few surviving Dark Jedi. The surviving Dark Jedi were exiled to uncharted space, where they discovered Korriban, home to the primitive but unusually Force-sensitive Sith people.
