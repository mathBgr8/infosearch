Outpost (Carrack-class)
1694

Outpost was a Carrack-class light cruiser of the Imperial Navy. It was part of the Imperial squadron present at the Battle of Nar Shaddaa during 4 BBY. Outpost was deployed as a rear guard, and thus escaped the battle undamaged. *The Hutt Gambit *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Carrack-class light cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
