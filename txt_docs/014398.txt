It was an ocean-covered planet. The sentient Inleshat species were once indigeonous to the planet. C-3PO aided the Rebel Alliance on Drexel II during the period of 0 ABY and 3 ABY.The New Essential Guide to Characters During the Yuuzhan Vong invasion, a group of Gluss'sa'Nikto established a colony on Drexel II. "Drexel II" was an invention by Rich Handley and Joe Bongiorno for their article The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series published in 2000's Star Wars Gamer 1.
