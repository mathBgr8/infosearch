Star Wars Weekly 61 is the sixty-first issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 25, 1979. This is the first issue to start printing US stories in three parts instead of two, making room in the book for more pages than usual from the other non-Star Wars stories. *Star Wars (1977) 27 *"Micronauts Part 5: A hunting we will go!"
