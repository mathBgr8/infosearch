Troska had a minor Imperial outpost which was grudgingly supported by the ruling Kyber Royal Family. Locations on Troska included Refinery Six-Two-Two, and Imperial officers stationed on the world included Sev Buzk, Treyz Manech, and Kelmont. During the Galactic Civil War, Manech hired Boba Fett to depose the Kyber Monarchy, resulting in the Battle of Troska. King Natas Kyber bribed Fett to spare him as the Imperial forces approached the palace, leaving the attack to stop Fett from killing the King pointless, and much of the Imperial force was destroyed.
