Jhosa
1614

Jhosa was a male Selkath who lived during the Jedi Civil War. He was a judge on the Ahto City Civil Authority court. A moderate supporter of the Galactic Republic, Jhosa believed the Republic was in the right to defend against the aggression of the Sith Empire, although he had doubts as to their ability. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Legal professionals Category:Males Category:Selkath
