Nuffin
926

Nuffins were sapient humanoids, noted for their cleanliness and gentle nature. Princess Nampi's ship was a converted Nuffin freighter, and she kept a Nuffin slave on board in order to fool would-be pirates into boarding, whereupon they would be ambushed by the princess's soldiers. *Jabba the Hutt: The Hunger of Princess Nampi *Jabba the Hutt: The Dynasty Trap *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
