- Bon'nyuw-Luq sector |stellar= |endpoints= *Sharlissia system *Triton |transitpoints= |otherobjects=Triton - Xagobah - Kabal - Nellac Kram - Sharlissia system |junctions=Rimma Trade Route - Sanrafsix Corridor }} The Sharlissian Trade Corridor was a trade route located in the Mayagil and Bon'nyuw-Luq sectors of the Outer Rim Territories. The Sharlissian Trade Corridor connected the Mayagil sector and the Bon'nyuw-Luq sector, two sectors of the Outer Rim Territories. This trade route began at Triton, passed through Xagobah, Kabal and Nellac Kram, and ended at the Sharlissia system. It intersected the Rimma Trade Route at Triton, and the Sanrafsix Corridor at Kabal. In the years following the Invasion of Naboo, the trade route was heavily taxed by the Galactic Republic. As a result, the Trade Federation discontinued operations to Kabal, leading to food shortages.
