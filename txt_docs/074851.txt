Jalan
1496

Jalan was a male Human and associate of Captain Arwen Cohl. He went with Cohl to Dorvalla, on a mission for the Nebula Front, to steal a shipment of aurodium ingots from the Trade Federation freighter Revenue. Though the mission was successful, and the crew escaped through an arm pod, Jalan was hopelessly caught underneath a computer console when the pod they were in was shot down. Most other members of the crew had died.
