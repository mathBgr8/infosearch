Teks
1557

Teks was a city on the agricultural planet Reytha. In 0 ABY, Teks and its food storage facilities was one of several Reythan population centers that were taken by the Rebel Alliance to disrupt the food supply. Teks was subsequently recaptured by a strike force led by Darth Vader during the Reytha campaign. Teks appears during the Darth Vader campaign in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
