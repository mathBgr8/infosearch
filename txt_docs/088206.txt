They inhabited the Orellan plains and relied on speed as a means of hunting and survival as they had to virtually race their prey. The spotted folk were essentially semi-nomadic with multiple dwellings in various locations of the savannas. They constituted the majority of Kentra farmers. Category:Kentra Category:Kentra culture
