Lig sword
1382

thumb|Kaleesh warrior Qymaen jai Sheelal wielding a Lig sword The Lig sword was a weapon used by the Kaleesh of Kalee, named for the Lig tribe. A product of dedication and artistry, a Lig sword took several days to create. Lig swords began with a pointed tip, and the blade grew wider as it neared the pommel. They were commonly used for mumuu hunting.
