The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 9 is the ninth issue of The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine, later rebranded as the Star Wars Insider. Issue 9 was published in Fall 1989. *"The Ultimate Collection" by Adam Schultz *"Sean Connery: Discovering the Bonds Between Father and Son" by Dan Madsen and John S. Davis *"Inside ILM: The Abyss" by John S. Davis *"Sound Makes a Difference: A Look at Lucasfilm's Sprocket Systems" by Lisa E. Cowan *"Keeping Up with Professor Jones" by R. Tomasic 009
