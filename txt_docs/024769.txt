|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=96 |isbn=1569714924 |isbn2= |timeline=5 ABY |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issues= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron: Mandatory Retirement collects issues 32 through 35 from the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron series of comics. It was released December 13, 2000. When Imperial Intelligence chief Ysanne Isard discovers that acting-Emperor Pestage has cut a secret deal with the Rebel Alliance to sell out the Empire, it appears that the Rebels' ticket to Imperial Center has been punched. But when Pestage is captured in his sanctuary on a far world, Rogue Squadron is assigned to go after him. If the New Republic wants to claim Imperial Center and turn the tide of war, it is imperative that they rescue Pestage, now wanted by the Empire for high treason. Unfortunately, Isard is also going after Pestage&mdash;with a brace of Star Destroyers and her own designs on the Imperial throne!
