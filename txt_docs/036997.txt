Ravaging Wardog Cantina
655

The Ravaging Wardog Cantina was a run-down cantina in the decrpit part of Kig Dannen Spaceport on the planet Gellefon. It was in this cantina that a group of Rebels met with Watcher after ordering seven Green Galaxy drinks and putting an end to a barfight which was caused by Watcher. During that meeting, Borsk Fey'lya pushed his way into the cantina and joined Watcher and the group of rebels. Category:Cantinas
