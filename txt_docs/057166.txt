D3RM3 was a Class-A cargo container in use by the Rebel Alliance Fleet during the Galactic Civil War.X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide During the evacuation of Yavin, D3RM3 was filled with a cache of grain and loaded aboard the Rebel BFF-1 bulk freighter Op Mus. When it broke down and was ambushed in the Lesser Plooriod Cluster, Op Mus was forced to dump the cargo container to flee the attack. While the Alliance sought new sources of food, it dispatched another freighter, Trips, escorted by two X-wing starfighters, to secure the grain container for immediate use. Keyan Farlander and his wingmate succeeded in defeating several TIE fighters and the corvette Assassin 1, protected Trips during the two minute transfer process, and escorted it back to the Alliance. *Star Wars: X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Class-A cargo containers
