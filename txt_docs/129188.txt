Subsonic grenade
472

Subsonic grenades were a type of grenade that utilized sound waves rather than explosives to inflict damage. As the name suggests, it utilizes sound waves in the sub-sonic range to physically knock over an adversary, causing limited physical damage. *Dark Force Rising Category:Grenades Category:Sonic demolitions
