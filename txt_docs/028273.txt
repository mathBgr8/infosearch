"Heavy Metal Jedi" is a 28-page comic featured in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 1, written by Haden Blackman, drawn by the Fillbach Brothers, colored by Sno Cone Studios, and lettered by Michael David Thomas. It takes place five months after the First Battle of Geonosis. Saesee Tiin and Mace Windu command Galactic Republic forces at the Battle of Iktotch, while debating their contrasting uses of the Force&mdash;Tiin's preference for the "broadsword," Windu's for the "rapier."
