Renatasia III was a lost Human colony world located in the Open Sea. It was one of fourteen colonization projects sponsored by Queen Tasia of Grizmallt in 3950 BBY. By the time of rediscovery, the local inhabitants were using Steel, Titanium, and Organoplastics, were capable of wringing useful amounts of energy from the core of an atom; and had also profitably colonized six bodies in their system.
