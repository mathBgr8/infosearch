Frak (expletive)
858

Frak or frack was a profanity popular on the planet of Cularin. It was also used in modifiers such as "frakkin". It was also used by one of the guards at Cog Hive Seven in 32 BBY. It was also sometimes shouted by clone troopers, rebel troopers, stormtroopers, and Imperial Navy pilots in certain situations, such as when getting hit during a battle. *Maul: Lockdown *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *Shield of Lies *Legacy (2006) 2 Category:Slang
