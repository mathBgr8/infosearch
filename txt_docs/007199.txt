Nemesis
3620

Nemesis was an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer. At some point prior to the Battle of Endor, the Nemesis was seen undergoing weapons testing at the Imperial Academy at Carida. Later, it was a part of Grand Admiral Thrawn's personal armada some five years after the Battle of Endor (9 ABY). It participated in the Battle of Sluis Van in a failed attempt to capture unoccupied New Republic vessels. It would later be tasked with an assault on the planet Chrondre as part of Thrawn's over-all plan to capture the Ukio system.
