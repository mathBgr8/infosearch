Zap'Ulga
1279

Zap'Ulga was a male Dug under Sebolto's employ and was also involved with pirate activity and Nexu poaching. He was wanted by Senator Ask Aak for connections to a series of raids on Republic freighters carrying relief supplies to Malastare.
