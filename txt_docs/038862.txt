Alku Vordu
1653

Alku Vordu was a male senator who represented Daroon in the Imperial Senate of the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Following the dissolution of the Imperial Senate in 0 BBY, Imperial stormtroopers led by Lieutenant Falto Dragen tried to arrest the senator on Daroon for suspected Rebel ties.
