Zantay Caves
753

The Zantay Caves were on Ord Cestus, near the Zantay Hills. They were the home of the Dashta eels, and retreated to the cave after the X'Ting began spreading across Cestus. The Caves were mapped by Kilaphor Dashta around 422 BBY.
