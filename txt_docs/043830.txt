Jon Trux
1326

Jon Trux was credited as editor for issues 2 through 15 of the Star Wars Official Poster Monthly magazine, and may have held the same position for the first issue as well. He also contributed articles to the following issues: *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 2: "Choreography of a Dogfight" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 3: An interview with John Stears *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 4: "The Building of Artoo" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 5: "The Power Behind the Throne" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 6: "The Spaceships of Star Wars" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 7: "Beams, Blasters, and Battlestations!" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 13: "The Shooting of Star Wars Part Two&mdash;On the Floor" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 14: "Star Wars and the Effect Pioneers" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 15: "Orbiter 102&mdash;First Space Freighter" Category:Editors Category:Non-fiction authors
