Mon Casima
1894

Mon Casima was a Mon Calamari Star Cruiser in service to the New Republic during the Galactic Civil War. It was part of a hastily assembled force of New Republic warships dispatched to Adumar in 13 ABY. The Mon Casima is never described as a specific class of Mon Calamari cruiser. The "Mon" aspect of its name has been used on both MC80a Star Cruisers and MC80B Star Cruisers.
