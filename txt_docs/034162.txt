Polyhedron 93 was a roleplaying magazine published in April 1994. It contained an article, "Crystal Web Space Station: A Setting for the Star Wars: New Republic Campaign," for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The article was written by Bill Slavicsek and Michele Carter. Like all supplements printed in Polyhedron prior to 2002, the article is considered ambiguously canon. Category:Polyhedron
