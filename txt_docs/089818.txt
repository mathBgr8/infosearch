Liberation of Clak'dor VII
3099

The liberation of Clak'dor VII was fought at Clak'dor VII between the New Republic and the forces of Warlord Sander Delvardus in 5.5 ABY. After the unsuccessful attack of Delvardus on Sullust, the New Republic led by the Sullustan captain Sien Sovv launched a counterattack campaign for the control of territories of the Eriadu Authority. The New Republic fleet dispatched to Lwhekk to deal with the Ssi-ruuvi was ordered by Mon Mothma to aid in the liberation of Clak'dor VII; the fleet included the Shriwirr, twelve Nebulon-Bs, and smaller support ships.
