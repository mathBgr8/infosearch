Len Wein
633

Len Wein (June 12, 1948 &ndash; September 10, 2017) was an accredited comic book author and editor for both DC and Marvel Comics, best known for creating Swamp Thing and the All-New X-Men, including Wolverine. He likewise contributed his talents to the independent comic book company, Blackthorne Publishing. *Star Wars 3-D 1 *Star Wars 3-D 2 Category:Comic authors
