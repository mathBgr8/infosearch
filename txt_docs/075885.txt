Jewel of Churba
899

The Jewel of Churba was a Dairkan Starliner inspired by the Queen of Empire. It transported Wedge Antilles, Pash Cracken, Corran Horn and Erisi Dlarit to Coruscant for Rogue Squadron's mission to disable the planet's shields. *X-Wing: Wedge's Gamble *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dairkan Starliners
