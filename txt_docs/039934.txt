Leeno
2558

Leeno was a Human male stormtrooper who was assigned to join Jerec's fleet during the Galactic Civil War. In 5 ABY, Leeno was a rating who joined Patrol 341 on Ruusan, led by Lieutenant Aagon. He was the gunner at the front of the skimmer Z-19 and his helmsman was Sergeant Forley.Dark Forces: Jedi Knight The patrol deserted its position in order to have fun hunting some indigenous Bouncers, when it was attacked by a collection of settlers. Aagon and his troops exited the skimmer while Forley was ordered to round the rocks and pin the Rebels. Leeno at first shot randomly at the rocks, until Sergeant Forley had the idea of concentrating on certain rocks and make them explode in order to force the Rebels out of their hiding. After some time there was no visible result, while the two realised they lost their last speeder bike escort, while Aagon was not replying; Kyle Katarn led the Rebels behind the troops and had killed the Lieutenant. While Forley was contemplating their next move, Leeno saw a bouncer behind him, which immobilized the Sergeant and swallowed his head. In his panic, Leeno swiveled the cannon around and shot, killing both of them. Realizing what he did, and the grotesque image haunting his mind, he jumped out and ran screaming. The bouncers who were around fell on him and threw him down.
