Liberator (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
3333

The Liberator was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that saw service with the Rebel Alliance Fleet during the Galactic Civil War.X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide During a Rebel operation with the objective of obtaining important information from the Imperial Navy CR90 corvette Talon, the Liberator, along with X-wing starfighters from Red and Blue Squadrons engaged the Imperial TIE Fighters running as escorts, while Y-wings from Gold Squadron disabled the Talon. In that battle, Red Leader Lieutenant Hamo Blastwell's X-wing was damaged and his astromech droid was disabled, forcing him to dock with the Liberator to return to the Independence through hyperspace. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
