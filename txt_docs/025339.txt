Bronell
1736

Bronnel was a Mon Calamari bounty hunter. During the Galactic Civil War, Bronell accepted the bounty placed on a Rebel Alliance officer by Jabba the Hutt for debts owed. After capturing the officer, Bronell allowed the Alliance, Jabba, and a group of pirates also owed money, to bid on the officer's life. Bronell was a non-player character in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
