|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=10 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Dark Empire II |issue=5 }} Dark Empire II 5 was the fifth issue of the 6 part Star Wars: Dark Empire II series of comics. It was released by Dark Horse Comics on April 18, 1995. Lando Calrissian's X-1 Viper War Droids have painted a target on the Emperor's citadel, but an army of monstrous creatures are making it their own mission to stop the New Republic fighters. Meanwhile, on the world of Ganath, Han Solo has to equip his damaged Millennium Falcon with antique weaponry. Will it be enough to escape from Boba Fett? Don't miss the penultimate chapter of this landmark Star Wars series! The events of this issue were adapted in the Dark Empire II (audio) radio drama, which featured the music of John Williams' score from the films.
