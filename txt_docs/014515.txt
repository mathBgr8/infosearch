Galtea Run
1046

The Galtea Run was a hyperspace route located in the Kathol sector in the Outer Rim Territories of the galaxy. The Galtea Run, along with the Sebiris Run, crossed the Marcol Void into the Kathol Outback. The Galtea Run connected Galtea to Timbra Ott.
