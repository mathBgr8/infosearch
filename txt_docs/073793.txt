Battle Group of Yun-Q'aah
1068

The Battle Group of Yun-Q'aah, was one of the five primary Battle Groups used by the Yuuzhan Vong during the Battle of Ebaq. Under the command of Commander Droog'an, the Yun-Q'aah Battle Group worked with the Battle Group of Yun-Txiin to initially engage the squadron under the command of General Keyan Farlander. Their working together to attack the enemy was in homage of the Lover Gods they were named in honor of. When the Smugglers' Alliance fleet arrived, the Battle Group of Yun-Q'aah engaged them. When Warmaster Tsavong Lah ordered the fleets to withdraw, the Battle Group of Yun-Q'aah received enough of a warning about the minefield the New Republic had established to ward off critical damage. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way Category:Yuuzhan Vong fleets
