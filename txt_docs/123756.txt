Kuat's father
1428

Kuat's father held the position of Kuat of Kuat in the period in between Onara Kuat and his own son, Kuat of Kuat. Although Kuat's father's reign as head of the Kuat family and the Kuat Drive Yards appears to have been short, his words of advice were noted by his son during the latter's reign.
