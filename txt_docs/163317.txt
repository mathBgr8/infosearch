To-Sharr Uuta Shipworks
1037

The To-Sharr Uuta Shipworks or To-Sharr for short, was a Hutt Space based trader's guild, much like the Corellian Merchants' Guild. It provided discounts and assistance to members with port facilities and astrogation. In reality, however, the To-Sharr was a front for Black Sun, co-founded by Sprax and Durga, two vigos with interests in the region. It provided intelligence and money laundering services to the criminal cartel, as well as organizing and funding archaeological research and recovery on ancient starships from the Expansionist Era and the Sith War.
