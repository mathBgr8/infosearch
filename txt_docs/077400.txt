Troob
852

Troobs were one of the two variants of the Tahlbooreans, the others being the Hobors. The two races were constantly at war. The Troobs had blue skin, and embraced technology and Galactic society. Around 5.5 BBY their leader was General Krax. *The Protocol Offensive *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient subspecies
