Zeena
1475

Zeena was a yellow-skinned female Twi'lek living in Ahto City on the planet Manaan in and around 3956 BBY. As an amateur swoop racer, she would refuse to talk to anyone unless they were a racer as well. If the player does choose to race on Manaan's swoop track, then Zeena will talk, albeit grudgingly.
