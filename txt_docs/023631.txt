|birth= |death= |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns= |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Hawkbat Battalion |masters= |apprentices= }} Twelve, designated CT-12/12-0068, was one of the numerous clones created from bounty hunter Jango Fett's DNA on the planet Kamino to serve in the Grand Army of the Republic. Like Fett, Twelve was a Human male who stood 1.83 meters tall. During the Clone Wars, Twelve served the Galactic Republic as a clone trooper major and led Hawkbat Battalion, a unit of 576 troops. Formed during the buildup to the Battle of Geonosis in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology Hawkbat Battalion was one of the four battalions that made up the 101st Regiment, which fell under the 7th Legion, 327th Star Corps, 2nd Sector Army, and Systems Army Alpha. Twelve was first mentioned in Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic, an article written by Karen Traviss and Ryan Kaufman and published in Star Wars Insider 84 in September of 2005. In 2008, the clone trooper was mentioned in Hawkbat Battalion's entry in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia.
