|release date=April 2, 2005 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Return of the Jedi |followed by= }} Revenge of the Sith was the tenth and final expansion made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game produced by Wizards of the Coast. It is based on Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith with the expansion symbol being of Darth Vader. The set contains 110 cards including 40 rare, 30 uncommon and 40 commons. Revenge of the Sith added a new type of card: Equipment. These cards could be added to Character, Ground or Space units expanding their abilities or power. Each Equipment cards build and equip cost is paid with Build Points or (equip cost) Force Points. The expansion was released on April 2, 2005 and was sold in boosters containing 11 (7 common, 3 uncommon, 1 rare) or 5 cards. Disrupt now can be used to missions and activated abilities.
