Star Wars Weekly 94 is the ninety-fourth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on December 12, 1979. It included the original story "Way of the Wookiee!" by Archie Goodwin. *"Way of the Wookiee!" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 6: Wasted by the War-Wolf!" *"Guardians of the galaxy! Part 21: At war with Arcturus!" *"Tales of the Watcher: Sabotage Mission Mars!" 94
