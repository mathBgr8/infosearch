President of the Senate
2546

The President of the Senate, known also as President of the Galactic Senate, or Senatorial Presidency, was the official chair of the Galactic Senate. In this capacity, the Supreme Chancellor, elected leader of the Galactic Republic, was vested with this title. The title of President of the Galactic Senate was given to the Supreme Chancellor of the Galactic RepublicStar Wars: The Visual Dictionary as the official leader of the Galactic Senate. The title President of the Senate was restored and given to the Chief of State of the New Republic in their capacity as leader of the New Republic Senate.Darksaber In Star Wars Legends sources written before the prequel trilogy, the term "President of the Senate" was used to refer to the head of state of the Galactic Republic; but the Prequel trilogy revealed the Republic's head of state to be called the Supreme Chancellor. The Essential Chronology and The New Essential Chronology identified the leader of the Senate during Ulic Qel-Droma's trial as President of the Senate while the databank entry for Ulic Qel-Droma describes the same character as Supreme Chancellor, retconning "President of the Senate" to be one of the Chancellor's titles. *Darksaber *Before the Storm *Shield of Lies *Star Wars Technical Journal of the Rebel Forces *Cracken's Threat Dossier *Star Wars: Behind the Magic *Star Wars: The Visual Dictionary Category:Political titles
