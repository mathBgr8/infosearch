The Galactic Empire based a SLD-26 planetary shield generator on the forest moon of Endor.Ultimate Star Wars The shield provided protection for the Empire's powerful DS-2 Death Star II Mobile Battle Station superweapon and, so long as it was active, made the weapon invulnerable to the attacks of the Rebel Alliance forces.
