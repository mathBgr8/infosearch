Qeimet fleet
2370

The Qeimet fleet was a unit of the Imperial Navy. The Qeimet fleet shared its name with the Qeimet system of the Juris sector. It was stationed near the Hook Nebula, a strategic Outer Rim sector where the Alliance to Restore the Republic had a strong presence. The fleet's primary objective was to quell the rebellious worlds in the nebula and bring them under Imperial control. The fleet's flagship was the Victorious, but it was perhaps most famous as the command in which the Scimitar assault wing acquired its reputation, aboard the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Retribution.
