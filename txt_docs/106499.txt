Qaestar Town
1420

Qaestar Town was the largest city on the planet Talus and the Twin Worlds. It was dominated by its enormous spaceport, located on the Qaestar Ridge two kilometers above the city proper. Twenty-meter wide stone promenades called the Qaestar Steps led up from the city to the ridge; the steps were ancient and worn and somewhat treacherous. The spaceport was run by gangs that preyed on offworlders. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Suns of Fortune Category:Cities on Talus
