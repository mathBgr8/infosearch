Bavos-II heavy-duty military space platform
2490

The Bavos-II heavy-duty military space platform was a space station developed by Rendili StarDrive. The second line of defense platforms, the Bavos-II was an improvement over its predecessor, the Bavos-I. At 2,500 meters in length, they were armed with 100 turreted medium turbolaser cannons, 100 anti-starfighter turbolasers, and three turreted concussion missile launcher banks. It also possessed a hangar bay capable of launching up to six squadrons of starfighters, and a squadron-and-a-half of bombers.
