Sanger
1748

A sanger was a low-profile defensive position that could be constructed from rocks, logs, or other available objects. Clone commando RC-1136 was taught how to build a sanger by Mandalorian combat instructor Kal Skirata and built one from half-decayed logs while on a mission to the Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Qiilura in 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact in 22 BBY. A sanger appeared in the 2004 novel Hard Contact, the first novel in the Republic Commando series written by Karen Traviss. In real life, a is a similar fortification, albeit with a different spelling. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Infantry tactics
