Marspa
2527

Marspa was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic sometime after the Ruusan Reformations that served as the catalyst for the Golden Age of the Old Republic. A Force-sensitive male, Marspa was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. During his years of service to the Galactic Republic, Master Marspa selected the Initiate Imina as his Padawan. Traveling the galaxy together, Master Marspa tried to instill the meaning of the Jedi Code on Padawan Imina over the years. While traveling, Padawan Imina grew confused as to why the Jedi could intervene to prevent domestic violence on Ord Mantell but not slavery on Nal Hutta. Master Marspa explained that because Ord Mantell was a member of the Galactic Senate, the Order could intervene and assist the government.
