Confessor's whip
1134

The confessor's whip was a serrated barb tipped whip of Geonosian make, used in the martial arts form known as Petranaki. The barb was capable of inflicting major lacerations on a target, and was often coated in poison. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Geonosian weapons Category:Whips
