Rin Shuuir
2069

Rin Shuuir was a Sith sorcerer active around 4000 BBY.Tales of the Jedi Companion is set 4,000 years before the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which translates to 4000 BBY per The New Essential Chronology. At one point, he accompanied the Sith warrior Utris, who became incapacitated. To revive his companion, Shuuir used a Sith healing amulet, which in turn forced the sorcerer to temporarily impair his ability to access the Alter class of Force abilities. Rin Shuuir was introduced in the 1996 roleplaying game sourcebook Tales of the Jedi Companion, written by George R. Strayton and published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. In the book, the character was mentioned in an example describing the in-game usage of a healing amulet.
