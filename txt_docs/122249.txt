Fecklen
2806

*HornsStrongholds of Resistance |lifespan= |origin=Chandrila |habitat= |diet=Balmgrass |language= }} Fecklen were stumpy, squat, horned, gracile-necked creatures that lived in herds on their homeworld Chandrila. They were the primary ranching animal on the planet and were raised for their meat and milk. Though quite peaceful, they were known to block traffic and were capable of producing a loud booming noise that served to frighten off predators and locate other members of their herd. Fecklens roamed widely and grazed on balmgrass. In 26 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth to 26 ABY. while New Republic general Wedge Antilles met with the Jedi Kyp Durron and Jaina Solo, a herd of fecklen near the shore of Chandrila's Silver Sea began to angrily make booming and whirring noises at a nearby group of squalls,The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth members of a small quadrupedal species also native to Chandrila.
