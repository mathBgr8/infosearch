|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire **Imperial Army }} The Ruac Outpost was a base of the Imperial Army of the Galactic Empire that was located in the Ruac system of the Outer Rim Territories' Dominus sector. Several years prior to some point in 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Humans Derembus Sitnalta and Clenna served together at the Ruac Outpost. Years later, Sitnalta noted their shared service in a personal addendum to a Rebel Alliance Task Force on Alliance Security SecuriDex datafile on Clenna. The Ruac Outpost was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Dominus sector locations Category:Imperial Army military bases
