Borealis (cruiser)
1603

The Borealis was a New Republic cruiser captained by Ioa, it served as the flagship for Chief of State Leia Organa Solo during her secret diplomatic mission to Nam Chorios in 13 ABY. It, along with its escort, the Adamantine, was infected by the Death Seed plague in 13 ABY.
