1833

A 3D-4 administrative droid was a 3D-4 administrative droid from the planet Ando. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, the Aqualish began destroying droids en masse to appease the Yuuzhan Vong. In 26 ABY, Jedi Knight Dorsk 82 tried to save a number of these droids. The 3D-4 administrative droid was in a group of 38 that he was protecting from a mob in the city of Imthitill. However, all the droids were destroyed when Dorsk's rescue ship was destroyed, along with its pilot Hhen.
