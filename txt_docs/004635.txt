Kre'fey family
1405

The Kre'fey family was a Bothan family to which several promimnent Bothan military officers belonged. It indirectly involved Borsk Fey'lya, the Chief of State of the New Republic, and three other Bothans that served in the New Republic Defense Force and the Galactic Alliance Defense Force.
