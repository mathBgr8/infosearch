Bethal
2827

*Altoona Prefecture |imports= |exports=Apocia hardwood |affiliation=*House Pelagia *Tapani Empire *Tapani Federation *Galactic Republic *Galactic Empire }} Bethal was a planet located in Pelagia Province of the Tapani sector in the Colonies region. It was the primary planet in the Bethal system. It housed the headquarters of Bethal AgriCorp, and its main export was apocia hardwood timber. An infestation of greddleback termites caused the economy of the planet - which was based on apocia hardwood timber - to go under. Imperial Moff Tendd declared the planet a disaster world, and helped bring Imperial funds to the planet in an effort to restore the apocia.
