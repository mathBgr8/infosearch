Holt Cross
975

The Holt Cross was one of the highest honors bestowed on Anaxes. It was named after the Holt family, who lived in the Sirpar Hills for nearly seven centuries, serving continuously in the Republic Navy and later the Imperial Navy. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Awards
