The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 155
1246

The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 155 is the one hundred fifty-fifth issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Monthly series of comic reprints, published in April 1982. This issue includes one of the few original comic stories in the series, "Dark Lord's Conscience".
