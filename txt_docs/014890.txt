|flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Yerphonia was a terrestrial celestial body located in the Yerphonia system of the Core Worlds. It was the site of the prestigious All Science Research Academy. The HumanThe Essential Reader's Companion future entrepreneur Granta Omega, who hailed from the moon Nierport VII in the ColoniesThe Essential Atlas Eeropha system, attended the institution on Yerphonia thanks to sponsorship by the Galactic Republic senator Sano Sauro. Omega graduated from the academy around 32 BBY, shortly before the Battle of Naboo,Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise, the events of which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 25 BBY, establishes that Granta Omega graduated from the All Science Research Academy seven years prior. In addition, further narrows down the time of the graduation to shortly before the Battle of Naboo, which took place in 32 BBY per The New Essential Chronology. a fact that archivist Jocasta Nu of the Jedi Order's ArchivesStar Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary uncovered seven years later and subsequently relayed to Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi, who was searching for Omega at the time. Yerphonia was first mentioned in The Master of Disguise, the 2002 fourth entry in Jude Watson's Star Wars: Jedi Quest series of young reader novels.
