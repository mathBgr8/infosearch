Fire grenade
2878

Fire grenades or flame grenades were a special type of hand grenade that released a quick burst of an incendiary agent which was very quick to ignite. The burns generally incapacitated the victims for a short while, but tough or well protected targets were known to ignore them. Such was the case with Jedi SentinelsStar Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Jedi Sentinels are immune to those effects. and soldiers with many years of experience, like Canderous Ordo, the first leader of the Mandalorians following the Mandalorian Wars.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Mandalorian Courage feature description. A Special Fire Grenade was found by Revan in Tomb of Naga Sadow on Korriban, during his quest for the Star Forge. A cache of fire grenades were found on Tatooine when the Jedi Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi was attempting to rescue Queen Amidala. In the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, the player can use the Special Fire Grenade on a pool of acid. The result would be death by noxious fumes.
