Yibikkoror
2853

Yibikkoror was a planet of the Yinchorri system. It was a small world with a dense atmosphere and possibly no solid surface. The Yinchorri built at least one balloon city on the planet, and its residents used flutter-packs for transportation. In 33 BBY during the Yinchorri Uprising, it was the site of the Battle of Yibikkoror.
