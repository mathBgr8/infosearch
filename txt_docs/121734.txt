Imperial base (Yinchorr)
1679

An Imperial base belonging to Darth Krayt's Galactic Empire was located on the planet Yinchorr. During the Second Imperial Civil War, it was the headquarters of many Imperial military units, including the 407th Stormtrooper Division. The based consisted of several low, domed structures on the surface of the planet. The HQ was established in 130 ABY and served as the division's headquarters for more than seven years.
