Adolfo Buylla
491

Adolfo Buylla pencilled the Star Wars comic The Pandora Effect, which was first published across The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 150 and The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 151 by Marvel Comics. It would later be republished in Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 2 by Dark Horse Comics. Category:Comic book pencillers
