Sturdimoss
1583

Sturdimoss was a moss that was used as a living carpet for the large transparisteel atrium known as the Morning Court, which was located in the New Jedi Temple on the Core WorldThe Essential Atlas of Coruscant. As the secret Sith Lord Jacen Solo left the Morning Court to join the Galactic Alliance's Fourth Fleet, the heels of his boots dug into the sturdimoss floor.
