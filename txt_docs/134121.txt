Swoopbike Speedway
1426

thumb|right|250px|The proposed Swoopbike Speedway The Swoopbike Speedway was a proposed attraction located on the surface of the Death Star. The track featured grandstands capable of holding over one-hundred thousand beings, luxury boxes for Imperial officers and Moffs, floating holocameras, food vendors and a main seating area, where enlisted personnel sit. Swoop bikes raced around the oval at high velocities.Death Star Designer Leland Chee has stated that, while most items, like the supercoaster and Ferris Wheel are entered for fun, much of the other information is included in the Holocron continuity database, and is canon. Regarding the Swoopbike Speedway itself, Chee has also stated that its best to consider it non-canon until or unless it resurfaces elsewhere. *Death Star Designer Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station locations Category:Swoop tracks
