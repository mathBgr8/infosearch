Dashta Mountains
769

The Dashta Mountains were a mountain range on Ord Cestus, 180 kilometers west of the Zantay Hills and 75 kilometers northwest of Clandes. They were named after Kilaphor Dashta, the first person to map them around 422 BBY. *The Cestus Deception *The Cestus Deception abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mountain ranges Category:Mountains of Ord Cestus
