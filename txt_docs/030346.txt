Ord Cestus Squad
1667

The Ord Cestus Squad consisted of an Alpha ARC trooper and four clone commandos, which included a communications expert (Seefor), a pilot (Xutoo), a logistics expert (Sirty), a physical training expert (Forry), and their leader, (Jangotat). They aided Jedi Masters Obi-Wan Kenobi and Kit Fisto on their mission to stop the droid foundry on Ord Cestus. This squad has never been officially named; the placeholder title is derived from the location of the squad's only known mission. *The Cestus Deception *The Cestus Deception abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Republic commando squads
