Rennie Gallou
1760

Rennie Gallou was a female individual who served as a supply officer in the military arm of the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. Shortly after the Alliance victory at the Battle of Yavin, Gallou worked at the Massassi Outpost on Yavin 4.
