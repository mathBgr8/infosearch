Morcanth was an obscure terrestrial planet located in the Morcanth system of the Unknown Regions. thumb|right|200px|Morcanth was located in the Unknown Regions. Morcanth was situated in an area of space over which, by the time of the reign of the Galactic Empire, three powerful spacefaring alien species had been waging a centuries-long war. At some point after the establishment of the Empire in 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology the Jedi student Winslau Da'k, who had become corrupted by the dark side of the Force, attacked and overpowered his Jedi Master. Da'k then imprisoned the older Jedi at a hidden fortress on Morcanth, where he subjected him to torture. At one point, the planet's name was changed, with its original name subsequently only appearing in very old star charts. During the Galactic Civil War between the Empire and the Rebel Alliance, Da'k's Jedi Master reached out telepathically to a group of Alliance agents, pleading with them to rescue him. The Rebels knew the Jedi was imprisoned on a planet called Morcanth but were initially unable to find it on any star charts. However, they eventually discovered the world and, making peace with one of the species warring over the planet's cosmic environs, recruited the aliens to launch an assault on Da'k's fortress while they mounted a successful rescue of the fallen Jedi's Master. At the fortress on Morcanth, where he lived in wealth and comfort, Winslau Da'k was accompanied by a small retinue of servants and guards. Da'k's small private army included advanced starfighters. Morcanth was the site of a hidden underground fortress, the defenses of which included heavy blaster emplacements. Morcanth is the setting for "The Last Knight," a roleplaying adventure hook written by Bill Olmesdahl published in the 1992 supplement Star Wars Gamemaster Screen for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. "The Last Knight" suggests an optional modification of its events where Winslau Da'k employs a more significant military force, thereby forcing the player characters to seek aid from at least one of the alien species engaged in conflict over Morcanth and its galactic neighborhood.
