Ma'aood
1142

The Ma'aood were beings who built wondrous temples. The chief designer of these funerary temples ensured that all obvious passages were filled with traps that appeared inviting to beings that entered the temples. The cyborg Lobot was familiar with this Ma'aood practice. The Ma'aood were first mentioned in Shield of Lies, a 1996 novel written by Michael P.
