Harf
1669

. |lifespan= |origin= |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Harf were a sentient species with an elongated snout and massive arms. They had goggle-like eyes that shined red. In 4 ABY, a drunken Harf was present in the Mos Eisley cantina when the Trandoshan bounty hunter Bossk and the Mandalorian Boba Fett had a face-off. Bossk won, with Fett paying him a large number of credits. The Harf were created by K. W. Jeter for his novel Hard Merchandise, published by Bantam Spectra in 1999 as book three of The Bounty Hunter Wars trilogy. They later received an entry in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia, published by Del Rey in 2008. *Hard Merchandise *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
