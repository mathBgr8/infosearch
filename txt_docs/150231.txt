Itching powder

}} Itching powder, also referred to as itching dust, was a substance which induced itching after coming in contact with the skin of an individual. It was employed by the shamans of Bright Tree Village to ward off potential enemies. The Ewok Teebo once used itching powder to ward off a glok monster when it tried to attack his sweetheart Latara during a trek through the glop pits. Category:Potions Category:Powders
