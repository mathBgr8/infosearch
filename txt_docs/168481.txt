Ujin Voli
1365

Ujin Voli was a male Gotal who was the leader of the Antar Band, a criminal organization active in the area of space around the Sisar Run. Voli had a strong rivalry with Black Sun Vigo Sprax, another crime lord active on the Sisar Run, though Sprax's operations were much more vast than Voli's. Voli owned a small hideout in an abandoned religious temple in Temptation Canyon on Sriluur.
