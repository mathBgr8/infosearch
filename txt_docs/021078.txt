|schedule=Monthly |format=Completed |issues=4 |timeline=28 BBYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Jedi Quest was a four-issue series of comics published by Dark Horse Comics between September 12, 2001 and December 12, 2001. The same story was also told in the Jedi Quest series of young adult novels, in the Jedi Quest: Path to Truth special edition. This series has not been collected as a trade paperback, but was eventually collected in Star Wars Omnibus: Menace Revealed. Although the 2001 young reader novel Jedi Quest: Path to Truth depicts Rashtah with a scar running through his eye and wearing an eye patch,Jedi Quest: Path to Truth in the comic series he is depicted with both eyes intact.Jedi Quest 1Jedi Quest 3Jedi Quest 4 Another contradicting detail is the Colicoid diplomatic ship, which is depicted in the comics as a standard Consular-class cruiser,Jedi Quest 2 yet in Path to Truth is stated to be a typical Colicoid-designed starship.
