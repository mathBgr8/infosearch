Privitt
1382

Privitt was an Imperial stationed at a communications outpost on the planet Vaal. Having been stationed only a few weeks before the destruction of the first Death Star, Privitt was confused by the relaxed attitude of the two other officers there. Still reluctant, he finally embraced their views.
