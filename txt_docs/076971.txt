Evanrue
3241

Evanrue was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in the New Republic Defense Fleet. In 9 ABY, it was part of the fleet assigned to defend Coruscant, and participated in the Siege of Coruscant. Near the end of the battle, Evanrue collided with a cloaked asteroid which had been launched by the Imperial Star Destroyer Chimaera, and suffered heavy damage. *The Last Command *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the New Republic
