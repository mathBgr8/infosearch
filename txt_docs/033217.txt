SHADIS Magazine 18.5
832

SHADIS Magazine 18.5 was a roleplaying magazine from April of 1995. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an unlicensed adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called The Spark Before the Flame, and was written by Sean Patrick Fannon with illustrations by Matt Wilson and Richard E. Edmond. Category:Shadis
