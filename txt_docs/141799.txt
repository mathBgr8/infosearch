Gilbert Taylor
4019

}} Gilbert Taylor (d. August 23, 2013) was a cinematographer who worked on Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. }} After six years of wartime service as an Officer in the Royal Air Force Volunteer Reserves, he became an Operational Cameraman flying in Lancaster bombers documenting the damage after British bombing raids. He worked on a number of films noted for their stylish cinematography, notably Stanley Kubrick's Dr. Strangelove and Richard Lester's A Hard Day's Night, featuring the Beatles. Taylor died on August 23, 2013. The camera droid RX-91L, also known as Gil, was named in honor of Taylor. {| ! Year !! Title !!
