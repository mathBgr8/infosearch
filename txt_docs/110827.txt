C-8 protocol droid
498

The C-8 protocol droid was a household droid programmed with many functions and forms of communication. It was manufactured by Cybot Galactica on the factory moon of Telti. It was the predecessor of the C-9 protocol droid and the successor of the C-7 protocol droid. *The New Rebellion Category:C-series protocol droid models
