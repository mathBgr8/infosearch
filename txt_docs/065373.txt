Gladiator walker
2593

The zZip Motor Concepts Gladiator walker was a personal bipedal walker vehicle used in gladiatorial combat. Designed from the All Terrain Scout Transport and All Terrain Personal Transport blueprints, each walker boasted shields and a light blaster cannon. The SecuriTech made a similar design. They were equipped with an emergency shut down system, which would cut power if an occupant was in danger of being injured. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition *Arms & Equipment Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Walker models Category:ZZip Motor Concepts products
