Aargau Medical Observer Corps
594

The Aargau Medical Observer Corps was a neutral organization that was active during the Clone Wars. The Observer Corps was tolerated by both sides of the conflict and allowed access to war-torn areas in order to investigate potential atrocities committed during the fighting. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Medical organizations
