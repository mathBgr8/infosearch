Lady Starstorm
1767

The Lady Starstorm was a YV-100 light freighter owned by the New Jedi Order. She had been previously owned and was bought by the Order to fly incognito. She was used for the preliminary scouting mission to Myrkr before the Jedi strike team. Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order Category:YV-100 light freighters
