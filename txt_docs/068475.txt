Titavian IV
2879

The Titavian IV was an exploration ship used by the Intergalactic Zoological Society. It was named after the titavian species of Naboo. The vessel had holding pens for alien wildlife and was built with several landing bays. Around 20 BBY, it was attacked and its crew killed, possibly by pirates or Separatist privateers. Some time later Quinlan Vos, being hunted by Asajj Ventress and Tol Skorr, found the wreck when he emerged from hyperspace, hiding on it.
