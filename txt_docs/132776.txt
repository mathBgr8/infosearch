EWHB-10
2219

The EWHB-10 was an E-Web heavy repeating blaster produced by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. It was designed to be carried and operated by two soldiers, and had a set-up time of fifteen minutes. It was possible to carry the weapon with the tripod attached, but it was not a recommended maneuver. Power for the weapon came from an Eksoan Class-4T3 power generator with a Gk3 Cryocooler, while targeting fire control was provided by Starvision and IR for operation at night and in adverse environmental conditions.
