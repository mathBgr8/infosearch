Foxcatch
1983

The Foxcatch was a heavily modified Lambda-class T-4a shuttle owned by Jodo Kast. Its tail was emblazoned with Kast's personal emblem. All three wings were shortened considerably, and six Incom 4L4 fusial thrust engines were mounted on its wings and body. The ship's armament was increased by adding two additional laser cannons on the wings, for a total of twelve.
