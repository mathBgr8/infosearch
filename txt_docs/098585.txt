Laser scalpel
3851

thumb|196px|A laser scalpel A laser scalpel was a laser-based surgical tool, and could be used to make highly accurate incisions. At Rimsoo units, laser scalpels were used for a variety of surgeries. The servogrip pinchers on 2-1B surgical droids were gentle enough to manipulate a laser scalpel. IT-0 interrogation droids were equipped with laser scalpels.The Essential Guide to Droids Ton Phanan of Wraith Squadron carried a laser scalpel as a holdout weapon. The model he used was almost indistinguishable from a writing implement when deactivated. This made it easily missed during a weapons search, and he could use his medical credentials as justification for carrying it if discovered.
