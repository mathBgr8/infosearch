Bryn Apice
1281

Bryn Apice was a male Human. Bryn, his brother Wes, and their friend Rayt were in the midst of journeying off-planet with when they discovered a funds transfer in a local bank. Donning clone armor, the three located the funds but were caught, though they still appeared to be troopers. They were stationed to move the funds onto the Republic starship, and remained there as it blasted off, journeying to an unknown destination. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males
