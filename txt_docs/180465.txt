Unidentified Bakuran prisoner
1180

An unidentified prisoner was a Human male from the world of Bakura, who had been sentenced by the Galactic Empire to a life term on the prison-world of Despayre. During the trip down to the penal colony there, he attempted to force a relationship with Teela Kaarz, who simply pretended that her last "bunkmate" had died of "natural causes".
