|published in=Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=0 BBY (35 GrS) |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Soup's On: The Pipe Smoker's Tale" is a short story about Dannik Jerriko that takes place in 0 BBY. Jennifer Roberson wrote the story for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina, which Bantam Spectra published in August 1995. Dannik Jerriko, a thousand-year-old Anzati vampire, coolly eyes potential prey in the Mos Eisley cantina.
