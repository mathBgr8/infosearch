Sterilization spore probe
457

Sterilization spore probes were equipment carried aboard the inquisition module of a modular taskforce cruiser. Their main job was to be used for mass sterilization on a planet as a form of population control or even extermination. The Galactic Empire often used these devices extensively on small, rebellious planets. *Dark Empire Sourcebook Category:Technology
