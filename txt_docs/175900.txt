She was a member of the Sith Shadows on Dathomir. Kylaena Haley was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Kylaena Haley was added to the game along with the "Axkva Min" Heroic Encounter, introduced with the release of the "Chapter 7" update, on November 1, 2007.
