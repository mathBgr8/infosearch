Del (Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport)
1938

Del was a group of 3 Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports in the service of the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Transports Del were a part of an assault on XQ1 Platform NL-1 during the Battle on the Frontier. With the combined effort of starfighters from Nebulon-B2 frigate Ludwick and Imperial Aces such as Maarek Stele, the rebel attack failed, and the Del group was destroyed. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
