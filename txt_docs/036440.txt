Jesse Jensen
925

Jesse Jensen played Saesee Tiin in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. He is the brother of Zachariah Jensen who played Kit Fisto in Attack of the Clones. Since working on Star Wars he has gone on to become a Production Designer for the reality TV series 'Survivor'.
