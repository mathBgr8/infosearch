Tyresi Gurtt
1409

Tyresi Gurtt was Captain of Bolt and part of the Khuiumin Survivors. A female Human, Gurtt was a Eyttyrmin Batiiv pirate serving along Jacob Nive. She later became a Colonel and became a crew member of the Invidious after the Second Battle of Xa Fel, under the command of Moff Leonia Tavira.
