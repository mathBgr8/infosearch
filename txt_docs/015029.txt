Burna Trade Route
1139

The Burna Trade Route was a hyperspace trade route that was used to cross the Lesser Plooriod Cluster, which was located in the Mid Rim. This hyperlane along with Entana Run formed the boundary of Urce Space. During the Galactic Civil War, High Inquisitor Mox Slosin patrolled through this route, looking for Rebel agents in his area. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Mid Rim hyperlanes Category:New Territories hyperlanes
