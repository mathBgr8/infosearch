Sodrab
1888

Sodrab was an Imperial Carrack-class light cruiser that was featured in an Alliance flight training simulation. The cruiser was featured in an Alliance training simulation designed to familiarize new recruits with deep space strike missions. The Sodrab was docked at the Damorian Repair Yard. Pilots were tasked with destroying it as part of the assessment.
