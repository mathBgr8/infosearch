Steve Niles
460

Steve Niles (born June 21, 1965) wrote the comic "Planet of the Dead" in Star Wars Tales 17. His most famous work is the comic 30 Days of Night, which was illustrated by Ben Templesmith, who also worked on a story in Star Wars Tales 17. Category:Comic authors
