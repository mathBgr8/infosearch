Her family owned a galactic transportation company. From years of travel, Gandish learned many loopholes in spaceport protocol. In 32 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace to 32 BBY. she attended the celebration that united the Naboo and the Gungans after the Battle of Naboo. A good-natured woman, Gandish had light skin, brown hair and wore a pink outfit. Phylo Gandish first appeared in the 1999Star Wars Year by Year: A Visual Chronicle prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The character was played by an uncredited actor.
