Anglu
4279

Anglu was a Twi'lek who was a swoop pilot for the Hidden Beks. They attended the Tarisian Season Opener and talked to the former Sith Lord Revan there. They fled shortly thereafter when a fight broke out between Black Vulkar leader Brejik and Revan. Anglu was a Twi'lek female who operated as a swoop pilot for the swoop gang Hidden Beks on the Outer Rim planet Taris during the Jedi Civil War in 3956 BBY.The Essential Atlas places the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, which includes Anglu's appearance, in 3956 BBY. During 3956 BBY, they attended the Tarisian Season Opener in which then amnesiac former Sith Lord Revan competed against the Black Vulkar swoop pilot Redros for Jedi Bastila Shan as a prize. During the Season opener, Revan talked to Anglu, with them admitting to not understanding why new swoop riders make their starts in the biggest races of the year. Anglu then told Revan about his swoops chance to explode due to the prototype swoop accelerator installed on it. After Revan won the swoop race, Black Vulkar leader Brejik accused Revan of cheating in the race. Cutting Brejik's boasting short, Shan was able to escape from the cage she was kept in and defeated the guard near her. Brejik called for Shan and Revan to killed and Anglu fled shortly thereafter. Anglu had blue-eyes and orange skin. Anglu found racers making their competitive start in the Tarisian Season Opener confusing. Anglu wore a Krath Holy Battle Suit and a grey headdress. Anglu first appeared in the 2003 BioWare video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. If the player chooses to side with the Black Vulkars and race for them, Anglu will be the Hidden Beks racer and claim that the player does not stand a chance with the experienced racers.
