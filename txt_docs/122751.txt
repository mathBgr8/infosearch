Bandigo
1431

Bandigos (also called BandingosLords of Nal Hutta) were a species of vicious omnivore commonly found within Sriluur's Temptation Canyon. They resembled large, dog-like creatures with four clawed-legs and wrinkled skin. They were widely known for their long, sinister howls&mdash;which many Weequay falsely believed to be produced by Water Nymphs living within the canyon.
