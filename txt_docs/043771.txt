Vira606
775

Vira606 was a synthetic virus manufactured for the Imperial Security Bureau. It was always fatal, and although it was not contagious, there was no cure. It could be kept in remittance through regular injections of a serum developed to counteract its effects, however. Vocis Kenit infected the Solar Terrors with Vira606 and rationed out the serum as the pirates performed requested tasks. Category:Diseases and maladies Category:Weaponized plagues and viruses
