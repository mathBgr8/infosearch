Yun-Q'aah
1538

Yun-Q'aah was a female Yuuzhan Vong deity and one half of the Lover Gods, with Yun-Txiin being the other. Having a relationship outside one's caste was forbidden, and disobeying automatically led to sacrifice to the Lover Gods. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The New Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Q'aah
