Wookiee Guided Rocket Launcher
2196

The Wookiee Guided Rocket Launcher was a gigantic homing rocket launcher, created by the Master Wookiee Crafters from Kashyyyk. It was effective against droidekas, OG-9 Homing Spider droids and other large enemies. The rocket launcher used guided warhead-tipped rockets and could hold five rockets at a time. They were fired from the shoulder and could either be "dumb"-fired or locked onto a target. Republic clone commandos from Delta Squad used this launcher to destroy the bridge on Kachirho village to protect it.
