It was the homeworld of the rare spidery nightgliders. Discovered sometime between 3000 BBY and 1000 BBY, the planet was located in Galactic Republic space, and later the Galactic Empire. Following the fall of the Empire, the planet was engulfed in the campaign against the New Republic launched by Grand Admiral Mitth'raw'nuruodo. After Thrawn's defeat, the planet fell to the Republic and later the Imperial Remnant.
