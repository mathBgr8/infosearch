|climate= |gravity= |terrain=Urban |water= |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies=*Humans *Ishi Tib *Devaronians *Ithorians *Pinurquian *Stenax |socialgroup= |language=Galactic Basic Standard |government=Controlled by the Boss |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Keyorin, also known as the Hunter's World, was a planet in the Outer Rim Territories, and known as a common destination for retired or semi-retired smugglers seeking second careers as entrepreneurs or bank-rollers of those who still flew. Smugglers who decided it was time to go dirtside but couldn't bear getting out of the game sometimes ended up on this planet. The most respected such retiree was typically given informal rule over Keyorin, and known as the Boss. Boss Tinketh Fo controlled the planet as of 4 ABY. Alas, retirement rarely meant escaping from one's past indiscretions, due to the many available bounties which earned Keyorin its nickname. Following his presumed death on Sulon, the droid infochant 8t88 was seen on Keyorin with the head of a BL-series Battle Legionnaire droid.
