|type=Miniature Hardback |pages=128 |dimensions=7 x 8.5 x 1.5 cm |weight= |isbn=0762405198 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Episode I Who's Who: A Pocket Guide to the Characters of The Phantom Menace is a small book which provides information about the characters in the film, The Phantom Menace. A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away, a boy named Anakin Skywalker dreamed of leaving his desert home to become a brave Jedi Knight. . . . Now the early adventures of Anakin and Obi-Wan Kenobi come to life in this pocket guide to George Lucas' Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace! Inside you'll find familiar faces such as R2-D2, Yoda, and Jabba the Hutt. You'll also meet for the first time the new heroes and villains—from Qui-Gon Jinn and Jar Jar Binks to Nute Gunray and Darth Sidious—who populate The Phantom Menace.
