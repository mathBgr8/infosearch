CoruFresh Farm Produce was a Coruscant-based company that supplied fresh fruit and vegetables. Its main distribution division was in Quadrant F-76 on Coruscant. This was the location of a major anti-terrorism operation by Omega and Delta Squads, 385 days after the First Battle of Geonosis. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero Category:Agricultural companies
