Soammei clan
900

The Soammei clan was a Rodian clan from the species' homeworld of Rodia. Its homeland was west of the Chattza Bay on the Encheeko continent of the planet. Through its domination of all other Rodians, the Soammei clan was the first to institute the title of "Inta'si'rin'na," or Grand Protector of the Rodians. However, during the course of the following centuries the clan was challenged and defended its leadership claim, until eventually it met its destruction at the hands of its enemies. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Rodian clans
