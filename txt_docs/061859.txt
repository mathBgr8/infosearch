1138 (CR90 corvette)
1974

1138 was a CR90 corvette, serving in the Imperial Navy as part of Task Force Vengeance. During the Galactic Civil War, 1138 was destroyed by the MC80 Star Cruiser Liberty as a diversion by the Rebel Alliance during the Skirmish near Almaran. 1138 is a recurring number in Star Wars.
