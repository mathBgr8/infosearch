Roger Stern
1244

Roger Stern was the owner of Stern Traders, as well as being a Rebel sympathizer. He was meant to meet with a group of rebels on Greyman's Planet but was unfortunately captured by the Empire when they discovered his rebel ties. He was held captive in the Imperial garrison outside of the city containing Da'Harrin Spaceport while his associate, Ral Yekkiv, attempted to free him.
