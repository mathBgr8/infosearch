QuasarFire Turbolaser Turret
1295

The QuasarFire Turbolaser Turret was a ground-based emplacement weapon produced by Taim & Bak. These turbolasers were produced with their own power generators. Six QuasarFires were used by the Corporate Sector Authority to defend the Stars' End prison. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Category:Ground-based turbolasers Category:Taim & Bak products
