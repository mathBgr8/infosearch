Landing platform NL1922

Landing platform NL1922 was one of the many landing platforms on the ecumenopolis of Coruscant. It served as the location of a small skirmish when Order 66 survivors Drake Lo'gaan and Ekria attacked an Imperial Lambda-class shuttle that had docked there. *Evasive Action: End Game Category:Coruscant docking facilities
