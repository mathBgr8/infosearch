Rendeel Industries
615

Rendeel Industries, located on Rendili, was a large food-manufacturing corporation. In addition to conventional plants, they also built facilities both in space and underwater, in order to process a variety of foods such as Quekka fish. These underwater processing plants were composed of several modules, engineered to withstand deep ocean pressures. Rendeel also was a large provider of starship consumables. *Hideouts & Strongholds Category:Foodstuff manufacturers
