Warl Thimbus
1446

|masters= |apprentices= }} Warl Thimbus was a quixotic Jedi serving with the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. Stationed at the Rebel outpost on Taul, Thimbus led Parody squad when the Victory-class Star Destroyer Dominator assaulted the planet. Armed with a blaster pistol and a lightsaber, Thimbus was Force-sensitive but displayed no Force powers.
