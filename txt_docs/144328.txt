Esther M. Friesner
758

Esther M. Friesner is a science fiction author of novels and short stories. She won the Nebula Award for Best Short Story in both 1995 and 1996. Also in 1996, she wrote "That's Entertainment: The Tale of Salacious Crumb," a short story for the Tales from Jabba's Palace anthology. Category:Short story authors
