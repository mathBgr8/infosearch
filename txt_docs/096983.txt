Ravagor
1886

The Ravagor was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer operating in 0.5 ABY. One mission in the months following the Battle of Yavin was a strike against an underwater Rebel base on the planet Aguarl 3. The destroyer launched TIE/sa bombers, including one piloted by Lieutenant Ranulf Trommer, which damaged the base and caused it to flood.
