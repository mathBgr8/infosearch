Qe'u
1878

Qe'u was a Shamed One sent to Yavin 4 after the Yuuzhan Vong conquered the small moon. When Vua Rapuung faked his death and disappeared, Qe'u was given his trawling duties and his vangaak. When Vua suddenly reappeared and found Qe'u, he killed the Shamed One to prevent him from telling anyone about Anakin Solo.
