Pa'hir'al City

|climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Pa'hir'al City was a settlementAlliance Intelligence Reports located in the Pa'hir'al system, a part of Urce Space of the Mid Rim. Around 1 BBY,Alliance Intelligence Reports, which establishes that Tirranna was captured in Pa'hir'al City "approximately two and a half years" prior to its setting, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Wookiee operative Tirranna of the Rebel Alliance was captured by the Galactic Empire in Pa'hir'al City, which led to her interrogation by Imperial High Inquisitor Mox Slosin.
