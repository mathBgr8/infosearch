Crix Masst
2376

*New Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} General Crix Masst was a former member of Alliance Special Operations and a brilliant tactician who believed strongly in the ideals of the New Republic. He was killed on Sinded by the assassin Morteos, who wired a bomb into the repulsorlift generator of the general's personal speeder.
