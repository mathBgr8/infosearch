Anlage
880

Anlage was a massive capital ship of the New Republic Defense Force. It neared completion in the Orbital Shipyard 1321 construction dock at Fondor when the shipyard was attacked by the Yuuzhan Vong. The vessel was destroyed when coralskippers rammed the slipway where it was being worked on. It was important enough for Commodore Brand to identify it by name when reviewing footage of the attack. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital ships Category:Starships of the New Republic
