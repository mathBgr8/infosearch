Michel Lacombe
851

Michel Lacombe is a French Canadian artist who has illustrated several Star Wars comics. He was the penciller on "Lucky" and Walking the Path that's Given in Star Wars Tales, and subsequently did the interior line art (both pencils and inks) for the following comics: *Empire 34 *Rebellion 0 *Rebellion 3 *Rebellion 6 *Rebellion 7 *Rebellion 8 *Rebellion 9 *Rebellion 10 *Star Wars: Luke Skywalker, Last Hope for the Galaxy Category:Comic book pencillers Category:Comic book inkers
