Morr Wenn
1122

Morr Wenn was Adar Tallon's servant. Wenn carried out household tasks on Tallon's home while he was living on Tatooine with his wife Kay. Cala Wenn worked at the same home. He was not a brave man, but was loyal to Tallon. Years later, Adar Tallon decided to leave Tatooine with his wife and droid LN-26.
