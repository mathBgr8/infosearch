Slaver permit
691

Slaver permits were legal documents issued by the Galactic Empire to slaver guilds for a fee, and were designed to regulate the slave trade throughout the galaxy. Some slave trading organizations, such as the Zygerrian Slavers Guild, were able to acquire permits at a reduced cost due to their size and structure. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations Category:Legal documents Category:Slavery
