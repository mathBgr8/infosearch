Relekin Confidential
1059

Relekin Confidential was a collections agency based on the planet of Danadine. When the company was sold to the Tenloss Syndicate, Relekin become the primary collections agency for the criminal syndicate, presumably using coercive methods that would have been shunned in its earlier, supposedly legitimate form. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations Category:Financial and holding companies Category:Tenloss Syndicate companies
