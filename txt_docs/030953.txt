Gruvian
1849

The Gruvians were the native sentients of the planet Gruvia in the Expansion Region. Gruvians were tall, green, mound-shaped beings who shuffled along without visible legs or feet. They had two mouths, eight eye stalks, and four thin arms with three-fingered hands.Platt's Starport Guide Two well-known Gruvians were Benja and Olall Da'aapp, two tourists who were often seen at the Kuat Passenger Port during the Imperial era.
