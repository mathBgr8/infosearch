The Voyages of the VSD Protector
779

The Voyages of the VSD Protector was a holo-serial produced during the time of the Galactic Empire. It featured the exploits of the crew of the eponymous Victory-class Star Destroyer, and its content was approved by the Imperial Security Bureau. The program featured fictionalized versions of the dreaded Sliviliths as its primary antagonists. *Alien Anthology Category:Holodramas
