|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=1 }} Classic Star Wars 4 is the fourth issue of the Classic Star Wars series, published by Dark Horse Comics. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson from the early 1980s. Williamson supplied new panels and art to flow the stories for traditional comic format. When Luke's spy mission in Imperial territory turns out to be a trap set by none other than Darth Vader, his only hope for escape is a beautiful thief named Tanith Shire.
