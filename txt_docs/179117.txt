|lifespan= |origin=Despayre |habitat=Dried evergreen branches |diet= |language= }} Scorpion slugs were a non-sentient species native to Despayre, an Outer Rim Territories planet.Star Wars: Rebellion They were named for the stinger on the end of their tail, which could cause weeks of agony for many humanoid species until the victim either recovered or perished due to the potent venom. Meanwhile, a scorpion slug's sting was usually lethal to smaller creatures. The slugs liked to live among dried evergreen branches. By some point between 3 BBY and 0 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Death Star to between 3 BBY and 0 BBY. prisoners such as Celot Ratua Dil often used the branches for sleeping mats, though they frequently changed branches to avoid the creatures.
