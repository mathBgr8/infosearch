224th Recon Patrol Squad

on Corellia |formation=Squad |commander= |second= |staff= |subunit= |strength= |vehicles= |headquarters= |location=Corellia |battles= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Corellian Security Force }} The 224th Recon Patrol Squad was a reconnaissance unit of the Corellian Security Force active during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 ABY, one of the unit's soldiers went missing after a wilderness survival exercise on Corellia. A transponder check showed that he was still stuck out in the Corellian wilderness, with a broken leg and many hungry animals gathering. Because the unit was due to be shipped offworld soon, their Imperial Intelligence liaison remained uncaring who would be left behind. But as the 224th had always worked from the principle of leaving no one behind, they went outside the usual channels to rescue their comrade, hiring experienced hunters to dispose of the animals while one member of the 224th did the rescue work. The 224th Recon Patrol Squad was mentioned in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The 224th Recon Patrol Squad was mentioned in the description of a mission called "Rescue Recon Buddy From Animals" available to players at mission terminals on Corellia.
