Ghorman's Honor
1465

The Ghorman's Honor was a DP20 gunship in the service of the Alliance Fleet. It was named after the victims of the Ghorman Massacre. It fought at the Invasion of Bakura as part of the Bakura task force. *The Truce at Bakura *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bakura task force ships Category:DP20 gunships
