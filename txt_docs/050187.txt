During the time of the Galactic Civil War, waged between the Galactic Empire and the rebellious Alliance to Restore the Republic, Rageon Vart was a member of the Death Watch, an ancient splinter group of Mandalorian mercenaries. Following the Battle of Yavin, Vart held the position of Ghost and was stationed at the Death Watch's hidden bunker on the forest moon of Endor. In 1 ABY, a group of intrepid spacers associated with the bounty hunter Boba Fett attacked the bunker. While the facility was soon locked down, Rageon Vart was in possession of a pass key granting access to the Section A of the Death Watch Bunker. Then, he was confronted by at least one individual seeking entrance to the depths of the base, and was eventually killed for his pass key. Rageon Vart, as a Mandalorian soldier, wore a dark gray-colored Mandalorian armor made from alum metal. His helmet was equipped with a mounted rangefinder, and yellow trim outlined his HUD-equipped visor. In addition, he wore heavy combat boots. During his time at the Death Watch's bunker on Endor, the Ghost made use of a blaster rifle as a weapon. Rageon Vart was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
