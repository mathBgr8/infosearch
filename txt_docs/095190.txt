**Metharian Nebula Territories Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three |sector=Elrood sector |system=Gelgelar system |coordinates=M-20 |xyz= |orbited= |distance= |routes= |role=Medical facility |affiliation=*Alliance to Restore the Republic *Galactic Empire |constructed= |destroyed= |retired= |battles=*Galactic Civil War **Evacuation of Gelgelar **Attack on the Gelgelar system |owners= |namedcrew= |captains= |registry= |aliases= |population= |interest= |services= |sectors= |modules= }} Hormuuz was the name of an XQ5 Platform used by the Rebel Alliance as of 3 ABY, located in the Gelgelar system. It served as a hospital station. When Imperial Task Force Vengeance was deployed in the Elrood sector, the Alliance was forced to withdraw from their assets there, including Hormuuz. In the middle of the evacuation, the Empire attacked with Assault Gunboats, the Victory Star Destroyer Stormhawk and the Imperial Star Destroyer Rage, trying to destroy the fleeing Rebel transports. Eventually the evacuation was complete, Hormuuz however fell into Imperial hands. Shortly later, the Alliance staged a raid against their former station in an effort to reclaim supplies left behind during the evacuation.
