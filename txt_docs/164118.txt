D.Star Emperor's Revenge Site One
857

D.Star Emperor's Revenge Site One was a made-up system in the Kathol sector. Moff Kentor Sarne made reference to this system in a datacard possessed by Lieutenant Palme on Shintel. The datacard was passed onto the crew of the New Republic CR90 corvette FarStar in an effort to spread disinformation concerning Sarne's assets in the sector. The datacard also possessed a computer program that would trigger an overload in the engines of the FarStar when they attempted to jump to hyperspace. Category:Fictional locations
