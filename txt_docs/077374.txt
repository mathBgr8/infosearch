Therefore, Full of Surprises must also take place in this year. |series= |issue=1 |next=Most Precious Weapon }} Full of Surprises was the first Hasbro/Toys "R" Us&ndash;exclusive comic published in 2002. Obi-Wan Kenobi is heading towards a meeting on Albecus in his Delta-7 Aethersprite-class light interceptor when he is ambushed by Jango Fett. Flying Slave I, the notorious bounty hunter attempts to shoot down Kenobi. He is forced to open his hidden missile launcher to destroy the Jedi. After his ship is damaged by asteroids, Obi-Wan converts his starfighter to fight mode, and uses the hidden laser cannons to damage Slave I and force Fett to flee.
