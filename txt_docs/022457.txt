Jess Harnell (born December 23, 1963) is an American voice actor who provided voices for several Star Wars enterprises. *Star Wars: Episode I Jedi Power Battles &ndash; Ki-Adi-Mundi *Star Wars: Demolition &ndash; Darth Maul, Malakili *Star Wars: Obi-Wan (video game) &ndash; Gran Thug, Ki-Adi-Mundi, Wounded Naboo Soldier *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds &ndash; Darth Maul, Gungan bolo trooper, Hannoon soldier, STAP driver **Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns &ndash; IG-88, Ki-Adi-Mundi, Stam Reath *Star Wars: Starfighter &ndash; Ric Olié, Pirate Ground Forces *Star Wars: Racer Revenge &ndash; Darth Maul *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast &ndash; Gran 1, Rogue Leader *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic &ndash; Additional voices *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy &ndash; Gran, Trandoshan He also voiced Officer Demon Mott in the fan film TROOPS and had a role in with Mark Hamill.
