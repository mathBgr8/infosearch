Drolan Plasteel
1750

Drolan Plasteel was a small company that manufactured archaic plasteel weapons. It was named after its founder and chief designer, Malia Drolan, who was known to scour the galaxy for inspiration. The company was most well-known for its P-71 repeating crossbow, which was based on the Wookiee bowcaster, as well as for its QuickShot wrist-caster.
