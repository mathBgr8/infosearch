Tormak Rodan
1214

Tormak Rodan was the older brother of New Republic Senator Fyor Rodan. Rodan was a smuggler who worked for Jabba the Hutt until Jabba's death and later operated out of his native Commenor working for himself; many believed that his brother was also involved in the trade.
