The Architects
1912

The Architects were an ancient group that saved the planet Livno III through the construction of Breeze, Rain, and Solstice Globes. Together, the globes powered a machine which regulated the planet's environment. The Architects were first mentioned in the 2018 junior novel Lando's Luck, which was written by Justina Ireland.
