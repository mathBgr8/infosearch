Alliance Recruiting Center
581

Alliance Recruiting Center was a committee seeking and recruiting new members for the Rebel Alliance. Ace Azzameen received an e-mail from the Recruiting Center inviting him to join the Alliance, due to their small numbers. The Azzameens joined the Alliance soon after but it is not known if that e-mail had a role in his decision. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic military units
