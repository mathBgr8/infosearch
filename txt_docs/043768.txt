Veizen fever
635

Veizen fever was a malady that was rumored to cause the Moochers of Abregado-rae to fly into fits of madness. An outbreak of Veizen fever was rumored to have affected one particular colony of Moochers, who were terrorizing other denizens of their homeworld by plundering food supplies and attacking anyone who wandered too close to their den.Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Diseases and maladies
