La'okio
644

La'okio was a village on Zonama Sekot, founded by the Yuuzhan Vong after the Yuuzhan Vong War. Despite the Yuuzhan Vong's intentions to move beyond their caste system progress was rather slow despite the attempts by Bava to make it functional. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Cities Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture Category:Zonama Sekot locations
