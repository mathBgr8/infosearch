Adarakh
1959

Adarakh was a male Noghri who served as the bodyguard of Leia Organa Solo alongside Meewalh, following the Battle of Duro during the Yuuzhan Vong War. When Coruscant was attacked by the Yuuzhan Vong, Adarakh and Meewalh escorted Leia, Han Solo and Ben Skywalker to Millennium Falcon. However, they were approached by the treacherous Senator Viqi Shesh, whose disguise fooled even Adarakh. YVH 1-507A revealed her true identity, causing Shesh to attack the Solo party. She stabbed Adarakh in the heart with a vibro-shiv, killing him instantly.
