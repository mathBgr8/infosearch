All the Vorzydiaks on Vorzyd IV were dedicated to manufacturing products and were disdainful of fun and games. The Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn and his apprentice, Obi-Wan Kenobi, went to Vorzyd IV in 39 BBY and discovered that their monitoring systems were being sabotaged by the newest generation of Vorzyd IV, the Freelies, who wanted to change their parents' "work-only" attitudes.
