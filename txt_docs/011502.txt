Prophetess could refer to three different individuals: *Merili, a Prophetess of the Dark Side *Valara Saar, a skilled Force user and teacher of Force Adepts on Yashuvhu, who was given the title Prophetess by her people *Sariss, a Dark Jedi during the Galactic Civil War who assumed the title Prophetess during a spying mission on Tatooine *Prophet (disambiguation)
