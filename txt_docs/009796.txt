Jeh
975

Jeh was a H'kig glyph that represented a mystical ideal of the peaceful religion. Considered too sacred to be written in full, the glyph was often abbreviated as J, as in the planet J't'p'tan. The symbol could be translated roughly as "the imminent" in Basic, and was a mystical reference in the H'kig religion. *Shield of Lies *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Languages Category:Religions
