HT-2200
2643

A HT-2200 was a model of starship that was capable of transporting cargo. In 3 ABY, a HT-2200 carrying volatile cargo traveled to the space station Hauler's Keep located in the Khepi system of Wild Space. A message instructing the station's repair droids to standby to assess the ship was broadcast over the station's speaker system. HT-2200s were first mentioned in canon continuity in the "A Pirate's Fortune" expansion for the Massive Entertainment video game Star Wars Outlaws, which was released on May 15, 2025. The ship type was mentioned in one of several ambient announcements that players can hear while on Hauler's Keep. The ship was first introduced in the Star Wars Legends continuity as the HT-2200 medium freighter in "Wildfire", an adventure in the 1996 supplement book, The Kathol Outback, for the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. *Star Wars Outlaws Category:Starship classes
