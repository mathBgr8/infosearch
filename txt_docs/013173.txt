Roger MacBride Allen
1803

Roger MacBride Allen is a US science-fiction author of Star Wars: The Corellian Trilogy, consisting of Ambush at Corellia, Assault at Selonia, and Showdown at Centerpoint. He was born on September 26, 1957 in Bridgeport, Connecticut. He grew up in Washington D.C. and graduated from Boston University in 1979. The author of a dozen science-fiction novels, he lived in Washington, D.C., for many years. In July 1994, he married Eleanre Fox, a member of the U.S. Foreign Service.
