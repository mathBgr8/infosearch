Starshine Surprise
2313

A Starshine Surprise was an intoxicating beverage, potent enough that it was recommended only for non-Humans and the most hardened of Human drinkers. It allegedly got its name because, after consuming it, drinkers would discover themselves lying flat on their backs, looking at the stars. It was infused with fruit and often lit on fire before being consumed.
