Following the Battle of Endor, Throsen arranged for many major officers and bureaucrats to be replaced by his own staff, and ordered stormtroopers to take over from corporate troops. He also sent the equivalent of a garrison and two Star Destroyers to each of the eighteen systems under Sallice Ag's control.
