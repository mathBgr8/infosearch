KT6 Heavy Blaster Cannon
1176

The KT6 Heavy Blaster Cannon was created by Taim & Bak and equipped on Gamma-class assault shuttles. Control of the weapons was split between two of the assault shuttle's three gunners, and they were sometimes used during boarding actions to cripple the target spacecraft. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Starship-mounted blaster cannons Category:Taim & Bak products
