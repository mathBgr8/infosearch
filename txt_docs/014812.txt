Ladarra
2380

Ladarra was a desert planet in the Gordian Reach along the Pinooran Spur. It was the location of the Human colony of New Hope Settlement. The planet was colonized during the days of the Old Republic and ended up becoming a failed chromium mine. By the time of the Galactic Empire, the planet's people were dying out. All that was left were poor farmers, hermits and fugitives, as well as some dust balls from the rust-colored desert. The only incident of note in the planet was when Boba Fett came to settle some personal business in The Ellstree Bar, with the Storyteller. The Storyteller was an old mark who fled here to escape him and took a new identity to entertain kids.
