Pixelux Entertainment
1094

thumb|right|247px|Pixelux Entertainment logo Pixelux Entertainment is a software developing company founded in October 2003. On May 8, 2006, LucasArts announced its partnership with Pixelux Entertainment, through which LucasArts will incorporate Pixelux's (DMM) technology into all internal titles currently in development for next-generation video game consoles, including Star Wars: The Force Unleashed.
