Muunilinst (star)
2493

Muunilinst was was a starSpecter of the Past located within the Outer Rim Territories' Muunilinst system.The Essential Atlas establishes that Muunilinst was located in the Outer Rim Territories and in the Muunilinst system. As the sun orbited the planet, it must be located in the Outer Rim's Muunilinst system. It was orbited by the planet Muunilinst, the headquarters of the InterGalactic Banking Clan, from which it could be seen.
