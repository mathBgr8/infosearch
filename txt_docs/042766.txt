Elders (Skakoan)
1012

The Elders were the holy men of the Skakoan religion. They resided on the Power Mounds of the Elders on Skako and read from the Book of the Boolmide. During the Clone Wars, the chief Elder was Clongor. Category:Core Worlds organizations Category:Religious titles Category:Skakoan religion
