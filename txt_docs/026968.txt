Rylith power cell
906

A Rylith power cell contained a rylith crystal that collected and stored solar energy. This feature allowed for the cell to be a popular choice in the design of many advanced weapon power supplies; the energy output of blasters, blaster rifles, and bowcasters was greatly increased. This type of power cell came in a range of energy output capability: basic, standard, advanced, superior, and pure. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Energy cells and power cells Category:Weapon attachments
