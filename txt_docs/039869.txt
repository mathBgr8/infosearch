Naboo Diplomatic Corps
1222

The Naboo Diplomatic Corps was a governmental organization set up by the Royal House of Naboo during the rule of the Galactic Republic. The diplomatic corps was a branch of the Naboo government based in Theed, designed to provide diplomatic relations with other worlds in the Chommell sector and beyond. Senator Palpatine was the Ambassador of Naboo prior to the Invasion of Naboo, and later Padmé Amidala took up the position.
