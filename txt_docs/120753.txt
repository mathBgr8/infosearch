Sidi Driss Inn
2448

The Sidi Driss Inn was an elegant hotel located in Anchorhead, on Tatooine. It was a moisture farm in the final days of the Galactic Republic. Dama Whitesun Brunk and her newly-married husband transformed it into an inn, which grew more luxuriant over the years. During the early years of the New Republic, it was still run by Dama, who was assisted by a Pa'lowick named Keesa. The Inn contained a special suite designed specifically for the use of Hutts. Jabba and Gardulla the Elder met there on occasion. It was here that New Republic members Han Solo, Leia Organa Solo, C-3PO, and Chewbacca stayed to avoid being recognized by any locals or Imperial forces. They were recovering from exposure to a severe sandstorm, during their mission to recover Killik Twilight and the hidden Shadowcast key it contained. Imperial forces, led personally by Grand Admiral Thrawn (disguised as a stormtrooper), discovered the Solos here, leading to a firefight and the Solos' escape. This would mark the first occasion that the Solos came across Thrawn in his final campaign against the New Republic.
