Bacrana Shipyards
1321

The Bacrana Shipyards was an Imperial shipyard located in the Bacrana system of the Brak sector. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, eight space docks had been constructed, with more capacity planned so that the shipyards could accommodate up to fifteen capital ships as large as Imperial-class Star Destroyers. The largest of the docks was Bacrana Orbital Spacedock A. *Flashpoint!
