Doaba Badlands
894

The Doaba Badlands was a vast desert on Socorro, covering almost three-quarters of the planet's surface. It was named for Iann Doaba, a self-exiled warrior. During late dawn and early dusk, mysterious lights were visible at the edge of the desert. *The Black Sands of Socorro Category:Deserts Category:Socorro locations
