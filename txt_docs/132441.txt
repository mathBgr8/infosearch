Entertainment Promenade
1832

The Entertainment Promenade, which was located in the Refugee Sector of Nar Shaddaa, had two major rooms: a cantina and a Pazaak Den. The Jedi Exile may have played Pazaak in the Pazaak Den. In the cantina, the Exile purchased Juma Juice to knock out Vogga the Hutt's kath hounds. She also met with Vogga the Hutt's Twi'lek majordomo, who asked her to dance for Vogga. She accepted his offer and was taken to dance for Vogga. Atton Rand went to the Entertainment Promenade to relax, but was attacked by the Twin Suns, whom he knocked out. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Nar Shaddaa locations Category:Roadways
