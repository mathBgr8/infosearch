With the rise of the Galactic Empire, the planet pledged support to the Imperial Senate, but peace was not to last on Aguarl. After the first Imperial attack on Dac, a number of Mon Calamari and Quarren Rebels established a base on the world. Princess Leia Organa of the Alliance to Restore the Republic was preparing to leave Kalla's Stanchion to visit Aguarl 3 to meet with Prime Minister Yenley approximately six months after the Battle of Yavin, but was delayed by a sighting of Imperial forces which forced the rebels to scatter their fleet temporarily. Later the same month, Organa was inspecting the Rebel base on the planet when a Quarren spy betrayed its location to the Empire. Four TIE bombers from the Ravagor attacked the planet, but only one survived the defense system, piloted by Ranulf Trommer.
