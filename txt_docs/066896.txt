PharmCorp
703

PharmCorp was a local company on the planet Nubia. It was the largest corporation on the planet and specialized in producing a wide variety of agricultural and pharmaceutical products. The company was owned by Prall the Hutt, but in an effort to not hurt business, this fact was not revealed to the general Nubian public. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Essential Atlas *Suns of Fortune Category:Agricultural companies Category:Hutt organizations Category:Medical companies
