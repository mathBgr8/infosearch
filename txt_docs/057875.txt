Jatayus Trading Consortium
983

The Jatayus Trading Consortium, named after the Nalroni goddess of prosperity, was a large company with its headquarters on the planet Celanon. It began life as a normal company, but was taken over by Sprax, who used the conglomerate and its branches, including Jatayus Outbound, as a front for his business as a vigo of Black Sun. *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Secrets of the Sisar Run Category:Conglomerates Category:Front companies Category:Organizations of Celanon
