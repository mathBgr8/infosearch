Star Wars: Short Story Collection
1730

The Star Wars: Short Story Collection is a printed booklet packaged with many Hasbro toys, published in July 2003. It features three original short stories, as well as a guide to more toys. It was also given away at some conventions, and in October 2003 was made available for free downloading online. *Clone Wars Timeline *"Storm Fleet Warnings" by Jude Watson *"Equipment" by Matthew Stover *"Duel" by Timothy Zahn Category:Legends eBooks Category:Legends short story anthologies
