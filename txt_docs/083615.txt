Lorshan Pass
990

Lorshan Pass was an area of volcanic caverns on Haruun Kal. During the Summertime War it was the location of an Upland Liberation Front headquarters and Korunnai refugee camp, and contained a hyperspace transmitter. It was located 50 kilometers southeast of Oran Mas, and was the site of the Battle of Lorshan Pass. *Shatterpoint Category:Caves Category:Haruun Kal locations
