|end date=October 20, 2004 |schedule= |format= |era= |timeline=0 ABY The date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series= |issues=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Empire: Idiot's Array was a 2-issue story arc of Star Wars: Empire published by Dark Horse Comics. It ran from September 08, 2004 to October 20, 2004. On a supply run for the Rebellion, Han Solo runs into old flame Sheel Odala and invites her for a game of Sabacc. Unbeknownst to Solo, Odala is indebted to information broker Jib Kopatha; she betrays Han for his knowledge of the location of the Rebel Alliance fleet in order to clear her debt. Eventually, she saves Han, who offers her a space in the Rebel Alliance. She declines and goes her own way.
