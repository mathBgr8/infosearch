In 22 BBY, Crockagor fought and died as a gladiator in the Cauldron arena on the planet Rattatak. Crockagor was a gladiator who served the Dark Lord of the Sith Count Dooku. In 22 BBY, during the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and Dooku's Confederacy of Independent Systems, Crockagor fought in a ritual battle to determine Dooku's new apprentice at the arena known as the Cauldron on the planet Rattatak. During the fight, he faced off against the insectoid gladiator Krutch, whom he attempted to crush with his mace, only for the other fighter to block the blow with his shield. Krutch then ensnared Crockagor with a net and attacked him with a spiked club, following which Crockagor was killed. Crockagor belonged to a reptilian species with green, hairless, spiny skin and green eyes. The species was humanoid, with a horn on each limb, three digits on each hand, and four digits on each foot. They also possessed teeth. Crockagor served Dooku loyally, always carrying out his master's bidding. Crockagor wore a brown loincloth and fought with a brown and black spiked mace while in the Cauldron arena. Crockagor first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003. The character was later given a name in Ultimate Sticker Book: Star Wars: Clone Wars, a sticker book written by Simon Beecroft and released in 2004.
