Narsacc Habitat
696

Narsacc Habitat was one of the six triangular luxury pods of Toryaz Station. It was 40 meters thick and 250 meters across, and connected to the main station by a 250 meter long spoke. It contained Kallebarth Way and Varganner Way. In 40 ABY, the meeting between Gilad Pellaeon and Aidel Saxan took place on Narsacc Habitat. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Kuat locations Category:Space stations in the Core Worlds
