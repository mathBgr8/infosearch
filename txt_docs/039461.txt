Thune (creature)

|class=Mammal |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor=Gray |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Dantooine |habitat= |diet=Herbivore |language= }} The thune was a large herbivorous creature with gray, wrinkled, and hard hides. Living in small herds on the grasslands of Dantooine, they were typically not aggressive; only when angered did they became dangerous opponents. Thunes were the size of a small shuttle. From the center of the thune's face hung a huge, noselike appendage, while thin, full ears circled its head like a halo. The thune could flap its ears&mdash;filled with thousands of blood vessels&mdash;in order to cool down.
