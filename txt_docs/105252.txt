R2-V6
1200

R2-V6 was an R2-series astromech droid produced by Industrial Automaton. Owned by Gryseium Incorporated, this droid was used by Sabel Axis Corazon to hold a computer virus she was going to introduce to the Premier Provisions' central computer. Rebel Alliance agents intercepted the droid and used it to foil the plans of Corazon and infiltrate Gryseium. Category:R2-series astromech droids
