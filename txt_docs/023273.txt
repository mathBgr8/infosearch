|birth= |death= |species=Human (clone) |gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Talon Squad |masters= |apprentices= }} Green, designated CT-53/21-8778, was a clone of the Human male bounty hunter Jango Fett, one of the many such clones created on the planet Kamino to fight in the Grand Army of the Republic. Like Fett, Green was 1.83 meters tall. At the height of the Clone Wars, he served the Galactic Republic as a clone trooper sergeant in charge of Talon Squad, a nine-man detachment. According to the Grand Army's order of battle, Talon Squad fell under the 2nd Platoon, Bacta Company, Hawkbat Battalion, 101st Regiment, 7th Legion, 327th Star Corps, 2nd Sector Army, and Systems Army Alpha. Green was first mentioned in Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic, an article written by Karen Traviss and Ryan Kaufman and published in Star Wars Insider 84 in September of 2005. In 2008, CT-53/21-8778 received an entry in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia.
