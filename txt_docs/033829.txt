T-12
1596

T-12 was a service droid used by the Jedi to catalog Naddist artifacts being shipped to Ossus following the Freedon Nadd Uprising. During the Conclave at Deneba, T-12 was present, assisting Cay Qel-Droma and the other Jedi. When the Krath attacked Deneba during the conclave, T-12 was one of the droids subjected to the Krath programming trap, thus turning T-12 from a humble servant droid into a deadly assassin. *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 1 *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 3 *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droids of the Jedi Order Category:Maintenance droids
