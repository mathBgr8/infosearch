A'roFilter
1431

A'roFilter was a mining company operating primarily on gas planets including Bespin. The company had ties to the Rebel Alliance, with Willrow Hood overseeing secret supply of discounted tibanna from Bespin to Rebel hands. A'roFilter was introduced by Benjamin Christopher Norton Dawe in his Willrow Hood What's The Story? entry. He had originally called the company AëroFilter, though the name was changed in editing. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mining companies
