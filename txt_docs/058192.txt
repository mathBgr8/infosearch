After 4 ABY's Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon, where another Hutt crime lord, Jabba Desilijic Tiure, was killed on the planet Tatooine, one of Jabba's bodyguards, the Klatooinian Umpass-stay, went on to work for Jocoro on Du Hutta. Jocoro was introduced in 1997's Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition, a supplement for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition The Hutt was first named as "Jocoro" in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003. 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia would later identify the Hutt by the full name of Jocoro Desilijic Tiure. *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Crime lords Category:Desilijic kajidic Category:Hutts Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Individuals with unspecified pronouns
