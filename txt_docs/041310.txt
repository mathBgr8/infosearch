Seaweed Forest
553

The Seaweed Forest was an undersea region located on the watery planet known as Dac. Several distinctive landmarks were established in this area including the Whaladon graveyard, and the Whaladon Processing Center. The hunter known as Captain Dunwell frequently operated in these waters with a small crew of Aqualish laborers, hunting the valuable Whaladon mammals. *The Glove of Darth Vader Category:Mon Cala locations
