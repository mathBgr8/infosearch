White *Heather Antos |publisher=Marvel Comics |release date=December 28, 2016 |pages= |upc= |timeline=*c. 40 BBY Star Wars: Timelines *0 ABY The frame story seen in Star Wars (2015) 26-30 occurs immediately after the events of Star Wars (2015) 25, which Star Wars: Timelines dates to 0 ABY. |series=Star Wars (2015) |issue=26 }} Star Wars 26 is the twenty-sixth issue of the Marvel comic book series Star Wars. Written by Jason Aaron with art by Salvador Larroca, it is the first issue in the story arc Book VI: Yoda's Secret War that features the character Yoda, set prior to Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The story was published on December 28, 2016. *With one of our rebel crew captured and imprisoned&hellip;' *&hellip;we turn once more to the journals of Ben Kenobi&hellip;' *&hellip;and a legendary adventure of Jedi Master&hellip;Yoda!' Aboard the Devastator, C-3PO is being interrogated, but the troopers of Scar Squadron find his constant barrage of information pointless and annoying. Worst of all? None of it reveals the location of the Rebel Fleet or their potential new base. Sergeant Kreel suggests scouring through C-3PO's memory banks, but Darth Vader orders him and his squadron to scrap the droid for parts, threatening a similar future for them if they continue to fail him. But before they embark on that, the other troops convince Kreel to send a message to the Rebellion that their "beloved" droid is in their hands, hoping to lure them into a trap. C-3PO assures the squadron that his friends would leap at the chance to rescue him. Back aboard the Rebel Fleet, Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa, Han Solo, and Chewbacca all agree that a rescue attempt would be suicidal, especially with the recent theft of a Star Destroyer. He was assigned to Luke Skywalker, following R2-D2's mission to rescue a captured C-3PO. This comic book depicts the Delta-7 Aethersprite-class light interceptor existing before the Invasion of Naboo.
