Dermot Power
2136

Dermot Power is an illustrator, comic book artist, and concept artist who worked on Star Wars films such as Attack of the Clones and The Force Awakens. He started as a children's books artist. Some of his unused early concept artwork for the Attack of the Clones Sith Lord was later used to become Asajj Ventress in Star Wars: Clone Wars and other works. In the 2015 reference book The Art of Star Wars: The Force Awakens, Power's concept work on a chrome stormtrooper is featured, a character which eventually became Captain Phasma in The Force Awakens film. {| !
