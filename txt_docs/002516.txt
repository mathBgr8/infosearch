Walerv
1650

The Walerv was a DP20 gunship in the service of the Alliance Fleet. It fought at the Battle of Endor, and shortly afterward at the Invasion of Bakura as part of the Bakura task force. It was the only ship sent to Bakura with a primarily non-Human crew (including seventeen Mon Calamari). *The Truce at Bakura *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bakura task force ships Category:DP20 gunships
