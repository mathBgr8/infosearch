Sebiris Run
889

The Sebiris Run was a hyperspace route located in the Kathol sector in the Outer Rim Territories of the galaxy. This hyperlane along with the Galtea Run originated at Pembric II at the terminus of the Trition Trade Route. These two hyperroutes were the only spacelanes that crossed the Marcol Void.
