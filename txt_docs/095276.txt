Transport Ship (Galactic Empire)
1887

The Transport Ship was an aquatic repulsorcraft used by the Galactic Empire to transport troops or personnel across bodies of water. Transport Ships under the command of Darth Vader were used during the Reytha campaign in 0 ABY. Transport Ships appear in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds and its 2002 expansion pack Clone Campaigns.
