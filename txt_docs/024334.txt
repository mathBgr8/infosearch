Calline system's first planet
1370

The first planet of the Calline system orbited so close to the primary, it was hazardously hot and uninhabitable. The world had a dense metal core, making it, in theory, suitable for mining with extensive precautions. Calline I was surveyed by Rebel Alliance scouts searching for possible alternate bases around 2 ABY. *Rebellion Era Sourcebook Category:Calline system locations Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories
