Wannschok
1968

thumb|250px|A River of Fire at dusk The Wannschok was a hot, rocky desert on the Nikto homeworld of Kintan. It was home to the Kajain'sa'Nikto, or "Red Nikto." The word Wannschok, translated to Galactic Basic, meant Endless Wastes. Another name for the region was the Fire-desert of Kinklath. Once, the Wannschok was covered with beautiful vegetation. Eventually, however, the Endless Wastes were completely desertified and dried by the sun. Only dried wood from the old forests which once stood there were left, though a few plants adapted to the extreme heat. Within the dangerous and jagged mountains of the Endless Wastes were many large cave structures and freely flowing lava rivers (or Rivers of Fire, as the natives called them). Members of the Morgukai cult spent a month fasting in the desert.
