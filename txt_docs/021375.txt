Circarpous X
2536

Circarpous X was a frozen planet in the Circarpous system with a traffic monitoring station. The tenth planet in the Circarpous system, and was home to a small outpost colony with possible Rebel Alliance sympathizers. There was also a monitoring station there, which interrogated all large traffic moving through the system.
