Cirra
1804

The Cirra was one of the four akias of Aramandi soceity that shared the planet Aram. They colonized the Cirra system, which was named after them. They were the second of the akia to settle their system. The Cirra were the most militant and aggressive of the akias, always believing that they should be ready for conflict despite the fact that, by the time of the Galactic Civil War, there had been no inter-akia wars for generations. Members of the Cirra made up the largest percentage of volunteers to the Taler, the Aramandi military forces. Members of the Cirra typically dressed in loose jerkins and carried at least one weapon. In about 9 BBY, the Cirra Mace, an important artifact associated with the clan, was stolen by a Seela and sold.
