In 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:3:14, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. the inexperienced Team Coruscant competed in the Galactic Cup of Limmie tournament. Coruscant took on the planetThe Essential Guide to Planets and Moons of Fondor's team in the opening round, losing three to one in their own home stadium, the BlasTech Gikosphere. In an interview published in the CoCo District edition of the HoloNet News, Numifolis commented that the team had hoped to win in front of the home crowd. They added that as the sport was gaining popularity among local toddlers, Coruscant's team would likely be a feared competitor in about twenty years time. Team Coruscant was mentioned in "Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round," an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002. The article includes an illustration drawn by Joe Corroney depicting Coruscant's game against Fondor. Although one of the two teams is visible, it is not clear which.
