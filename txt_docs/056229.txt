Khaaza'k
856

Khaaza'k was a Rebel Lambda-class T-4a shuttle that ferried the captain of the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Maximus after it was crippled and abandoned before its destruction. The Khaaza'k and other transports carrying crew from the Maximus docked with the Nebulon-B frigate Anvil. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Lambda-class T-4a shuttles Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
