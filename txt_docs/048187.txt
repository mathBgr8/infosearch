Arroyo
1560

Arroyo was a group of three Lambda-class shuttles used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic to evacuate Alliance High Command personnel from the base on Brigia during Operation Strike Fear. They were protected during the battle by X-wings from Red Squadron assisted by Y-wings from Gold Squadron. Thanks to the strong Rebel defense, all three shuttles escaped, ensuring the survival of a significant portion of the Alliance chain of command. *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic shuttle groups
