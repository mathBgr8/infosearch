Paul Hecht
1980

Paul Hecht (born August 16, 1941) is an English-born Canadian stage, film, and television actor. Born in London, England, Hecht graduated from the National Theatre School of Canada in 1963. He made his Broadway debut as "The Player" in Rosencrantz & Guildenstern Are Dead, for which he was nominated for the Tony Award for Best Performance by a Featured Actor in a Play. Hecht has numerous other Broadway, TV, and film credits to his name, and he has recorded extensively for the CBC. In Star Wars media, Hecht performed the role of Emperor Palpatine in the NPR radio dramatizations of The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi.
