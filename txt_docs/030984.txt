Liquidator (Neutron Star-class)
2345

The Liquidator was a Neutron Star-class bulk cruiser in the Imperial Navy, commanded by Admiral Winstel Greelanx. It participated in the Battle of Nar Shaddaa, where it was destroyed by Mako Spince flying the Dragon Pearl, causing its broken hulk to plummet into Nal Hutta, Nar Shaddaa's host planet and homeworld of the Hutts.
