Artery worm
2804

Artery worms were tiny, vicious worms native to Dathomir. During the time of the Galactic Republic, artery worms were a torture method favored by the Nightsisters of Dathomir. The torture involved force-feeding or injecting the victim with liquid containing artery worms.Star Wars (1998) 24 When ingested, an artery worm would burrow through its host's veins, "turning them to molten fire" (according to the Witches) as it digested them. A Nightsister would survive after a day of torture; but others would die, unless they were given a potion containing witch blood, which would rid the victim of the worms, provided the creatures hadn't reached their heart. In 31 BBY, the Jedi Knight Quinlan Vos was sent on Dathomir to inquire about the sudden disappearance of planet Ova.Star Wars (1998) 23 When Queen Zalem discovered his identity, she decided to "charm him to death" by injecting worms in his arteries.
