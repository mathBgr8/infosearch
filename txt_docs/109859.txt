Unidentified Star Tours droid
1309

A droid worked at Star Tours' Tomorrowland Starport. The droid ran on tracks, much like the model of MSE-series droids also at the starport. Fans have nicknamed the droid "LGB" in reference to a standard acronym for Lehmann Gross Bahn, a model railroading system that is used to operate the droid in the theme park attraction Star Tours, and it's updated sequel. *Star Tours: The Adventures Continue *Star Tours Category:Individual droids Category:Star Tours personnel
