Adonar Dellox
1634

Adonar Dellox was a Rebel and Chief Technician at Reginard Base on the planet Reginard during the Galactic Civil War. He was one of the commanding officers of the base and was known to be a determined man who preferred to work alone. This did not stop him having a relationship with his fellow technician Kim Yeasson, although this relationship ended a month before his death. While he was working on the passive sensor array to be deployed on the other side of Reginard, he discovered an Imperial device which had been planted there by Major Tanya Madera. This caused Madera to assassinate him with her needler pistol which contained the deadly triflexia virus.
