PTL-05 Launch Platform
722

The PTL-05 Launch Platform or Proton Torpedo Turret was a turret fitted with four proton torpedo launchers. They could be used to attack from a fairly long range and were especially effective against infantry. Its torpedoes had higher yield than that of the MPTL-2a but had reduced range. *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Launcher emplacements
