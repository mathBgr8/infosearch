The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 154
1125

The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 154 is the one hundred fifty-second issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Monthly series of comic reprints, published in February 1982. This issue includes one of the few original comic stories in the series, "Tilotny Throws a Shape".
