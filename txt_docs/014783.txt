In 2 ABY, jatz musician Fitz Roi began his concert tour on Gambolla. Two months later, the art collection of Whiphid business tycoon Baron Galrowk, one of the most prominent in the region, was robbed by the art and jewel thief known as the Tombat. Inspector Zanza Gata of the Imperial Office of Criminal Investigations was assigned to the case.
