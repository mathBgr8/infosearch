The planet supplied food to both sides during the Galactic Civil War. Galactic Republic scout Arnoth Draenell discovered the planet. The first settlements on Draenell's Point were established in about 150 BBY by traders using the Terr'skiar Pass. The planet was ruled by the Draenell Planetary Council but control was relinquished to the Galactic Empire to spare an invasion.
