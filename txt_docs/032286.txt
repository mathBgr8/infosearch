Eirrauc
1999

|lifespan= |origin=Eirrauus |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Eirraucs were a sentient species from Eirrauus. They were easily recognizable from their six limbs and long, four-eyed faces. Eirraucs moved with a "leaping run," using their rear and middle pairs of limbs to swiftly travel the plains of their homeworld. Their middle limbs could also be used as arms, supplementing their upper limbs. Eirraucs were peaceful omnivores, feeding on grasses, fruits, and carrion. The Eirraucs were ill-prepared when the Galactic Empire arrived to conquer their homeworld. Unable to resist, they were subjugated and enslaved.
