thumb|right|The original "Design an Alien" submission. Matthew Berry is a Star Wars fan who designed Brock Lar, Gryphon, and Martika along with Craig Farr, in the "Design An Alien" contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the 16 years and older category. He was 18 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
