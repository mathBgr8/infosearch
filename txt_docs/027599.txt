Zakul
2586

Zakul was a Sanyassan Marauder and the new King of Endor. He was one of the sons of Terak, leader of the stranded Sanyassan Marauders. When King Terak was killed in 3 ABY,Ewoks: The Battle for Endor General Yavid tried to take the Marauder throne and tried to become King. But he was not able to match Terak's feat of keeping order over such a rabble, and the Marauders turned on one another. But eventually Zakul emerged victorious in this struggle, re-established order, and became the new King. Nevertheless, when the New Republic later sent agents to scout the Endorian forest, they found no trace of the Sanyassan Marauders. The character of Zakul was first mentioned in the roleplaying game module Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds, published in March 24, 2004.
