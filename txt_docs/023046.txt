The twins were dancers on Ord Mantell. A Twi'lek businessman named Quatrain Pudundruh became their manager. After working with them for a year, he sold the sisters into slavery to make up for his failing assets. They were purchased by the Black Hole Gang, a group of pirates from Delphon.
