*Christopher Perkins *Michael Mikaelian *Scott Chernoff |cover artist= |contributors= |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=June 14, 2001 |subscribers=May 18, 2001 |pages=96 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=54 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 54 is the fifty-fourth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was released to newsstands on June 14, 2001. This marked the final Star Wars Insider issue produced by Jeff Quick, who had assumed chief editorial duties of the magazine with issue 52 upon Wizards of the Coast becoming the new publisher of Star Wars Insider. Issue 54 featured a special flip cover on the back of the magazine with related content celebrating the twentieth anniversary of the Indiana Jones film Raiders of the Lost Ark. Issue 54 also came packaged with a special LucasArts preview CD, while the newsstand version was available in an exclusive polybag.
