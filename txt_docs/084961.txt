Trevor Insevade
1570

}} Trevor Insevade was an Alderaanian male member of the Legislative Youth Program during the beginning of the Separatist Crisis. In 22 BBY, Insevade missed the annual mock Senate session on Chandrila due to having nerf-pox. Naboo delegate Dashé Borreno believed he would have voted against the mock Military Creation Act.
