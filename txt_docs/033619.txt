High Hermit
612

The title of High Hermit was held by the leader of the Ancient Order of Pessimists, a religious group based on Maryx Minor. The last individual known to have gone by that title died in 3 BBY following an Imperial orbital bombardment. *Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Religious titles
