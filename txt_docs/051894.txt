Bulior Repulsor Systems
788

Bulior Repulsor Systems was a New Republic corporation that developed a starfighter crash-restraint system suitable for installation into small cockpits. Small repulsorlift units would hold the pilot in an invisible cocoon if his starship crashed. The corporation got a boost in the public relations sector when Derek Klivian, the famous Rogue Squadron pilot, endorsed the system. *Star Wars Handbook 1: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Category:Starship technology companies
