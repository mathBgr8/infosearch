Omicron 5
1803

Omicron 5 was an Imperial Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttle which escorted BFF-1 bulk freighters Degathem and Anterab to deliver research supplies to Grand Admiral Zaarin's XQ6 Platform Research during the Galactic Civil War.
