Monofilament wire
997

Monofilament wire was a fiber so strong and thin that its cutting power was greater than that of the sharpest vibroblade. Practically anything that touched it would be cut in two. These wires were favorite weapons of assassins. A Rodian named Sniquux used this wire to assassinate several t'landa Til priests on Ylesia, scaring them until they ran into his deadly trap. Monofilament wires could be combined into a monofilament net to create an even deadlier trap.Young Jedi Knights: Return to Ord Mantell *Rebel Dawn *Young Jedi Knights: Return to Ord Mantell Category:Manufacturing technology Category:Substances
