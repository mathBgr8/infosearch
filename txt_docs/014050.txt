An industrial world, it served as the lackluster sector capital of Thesme sector, on the Thesme Trace. Thesme was represented in the Galactic Senate by Silya Shessaun. In the years leading up to the Clone Wars, the Senator was often called upon to return to the world to mediate territorial disputes in the sector. During the Galactic Civil War, a bounty was put up on Thesme for the capture of the Zygerrian slaver Marko Tyne.
