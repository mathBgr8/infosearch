|gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes=Blue |skin=Brown |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Onyeth Canavar was a Tarnab male who roamed the alleys and visited the streets of the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetInside the Worlds of Star Wars: Attack of the Clones Coruscant. During a night on Coruscant in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. the Tarnab wandered along Vos Gesal Street in the district with the Human partier Kristoff Vestswe. As they did so, the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi caught up with the fellow Jedi Anakin Skywalker at the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall. Canavar later walked into the club, alone, and stood near the entrance while Kenobi drank at the club's bar. During his night on Coruscant, Canavar wore a gray jumpsuit. The Tarnab had brown skin, blue eyes, and brown hair. Onyeth Canavar first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The Outlander Club interior scenes including Canavar, who was portrayed by an uncredited extra, were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney on July 17, 2000. The club's exterior scenes were also shot at the studio.Creating the Worlds of Star Wars: 365 Days The character was first identified on July 15, 2003 through the Image Attack feature on StarWars.com.
