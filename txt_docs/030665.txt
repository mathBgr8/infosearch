Legislature
1946

Legislatures were governmental organizations that drafted laws, and often elected the Heads of State and/or Government. Depending on the specific government, a legislature might be referred to as a Council, High Council, Great Council, Congress, Ruling Council, Senate, or Parliament. Examples of legislatures included: *Bothan Council, legilsature of Bothawui and Bothan Space. *Chandrilan House, legislature of Chandrila. *Council of Elders, legislature of Cerea. *Council of Moffs, de facto legislature of the Imperial Remnant. *Duros High House, legislature of Duro. *Elders' Council, legislature of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium. *Galactic Senate, legislature of the Galactic Republic. *Great Council, legislature of the Tapani sector. *Great Council, legislature of the Yuuzhan Vong. *High Council of Alderaan, legislature of Alderaan. *Hutt Grand Council, legislature of Hutt Space. *Jabiim Congress, legislature of Jabiim. *Lannik High Court, legislature of Lannik. *Mon Calamari Council, legislature of Dac. *Sullustan Council, legislature of Sullust. *Twi'lek Clan Council, legislature of Ryloth. *Typha-Dor High Council, legislature of Typha-Dor.
