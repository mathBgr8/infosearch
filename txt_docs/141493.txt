Outpost Cinder
1063

Outpost Cinder was an Alliance Special Forces outpost base located on the planet Bid'jerma. It was established on a ridge overlooking the Empire's Bid'jerma Detention Block, in an effort to liberate the high concentration of Alliance prisoners at the facility. Outpost Cinder was established by Team Razor and staffed by Team Blade, commanded by Lieutenant Teracca Torrh. *Hideouts & Strongholds Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic military bases
