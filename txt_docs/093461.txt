Jal Shey Mentor armor
914

thumb|Jal Shey Mentor armor Jal Shey Mentor armor was a type of armor worn by Jal Shey mentors. The mentors were considered to be unparalleled in their intellect and were known to often serve as widely respected teachers and advisors. Though tougher than the similar armor used by neophytes and advisors, this armor was still lightweight and did not interfere with the usage of Force powers. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Armored robes
