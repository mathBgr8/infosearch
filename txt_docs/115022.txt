Stand and Deliver
6176

"Stand and Deliver" is an article published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 5 by John J. Richardson III, which describes the pirate Drek Drednar, his starship the CR90 corvette Sable III and its crew. The pirating operations conducted by Drednar were based on Taraloon. The first mate upon Anto's Star, renamed Sable III was the Rodian, Chillo Sanpona. The chief astrogator was Tron Nixx.
