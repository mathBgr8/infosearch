N'dru Suhlak
2390

N'dru Suhlak was a hunt saboteur during the Bounty Hunter Wars and former Rebel. Despite being an accomplished pilot having learned from both Wes Janson and Jek Porkins, Suhlak was removed from the Tierfon Rebel Outpost for excessive insubordination and found work elsewhere ferrying bounties. Twice, he intervened in Boba Fett's business, and he once teamed up with the fearsome bounty hunter. Fortunately for him, Suhlak was able to escape the partnership with his life after he pulled a blaster pistol on Fett, Dengar, and Neelah. thumb|right|250px|N'dru Suhlak in his Z-95 Headhunter Suhlak piloted a modified Z-95 Headhunter that had the space bubbled out to be able to fit more passengers in it.
