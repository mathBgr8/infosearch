Plaza of the Daders
1209

thumb|The Plaza of the Daders The Plaza of the Daders was an open area in the New Galactic Empire capital of Alderaan, where the Emperor Cos Dashit and the Supreme Tribunal reviewed Imperial troops and gave speeches. Thousands of troops could fit into its expanse. Stardestroyers performed barrel rolls above as part of the pomp and circumstance. The Emperor conferred the Governorship of the Aquilaean system on Crispin Hoedaack here, and Clieg Whitsun, a spy reported on this to Bail Antilles in an Alderaanian nightclub. The Plaza of the Daders was renamed the "Plaza of the Donns" in the first draft, and was located on Granicus. *The Star Wars 1 *The Star Wars rough draft *The Star Wars first draft Category:Alderaan locations Category:Plazas
