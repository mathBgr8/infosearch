Executor (Yuuzhan Vong rank)
1819

thumb|right|180px|Nom Anor, Yuuzhan Vong Executor Executor was a rank of the Yuuzhan Vong Intendant caste. Executors handled various day to day measures, and also managed valuable "living resources"&mdash;slaves, Workers, and Shamed Ones. They were also sent out as spies and advance scouts. Nom Anor and Eshin Worr held the rank.
