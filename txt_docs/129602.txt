Intrepid (CR90 corvette)
2383

The Intrepid was a CR90 corvette that saw service with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. It was the consular ship of a Rebel ambassador that had been sent on a mission to win the support of the Airam at an Airam Platform. However, during Operation: Clean Sweep the Galactic Empire sent a strike force with orders to disable and board the Intrepid to capture the ambassador. This incident took place prior to the formation of Imperial Task Force Vengeance, but could be seen as prelude, or perhaps first step, in the conflicting Imperial and Rebel Alliance campaigns in the Airam sector. While the occurrence of the space battle depicted in this mission, as well as the participants, is C-Canon, the outcome depends on the actions of the player and is therefore indeterminate.
