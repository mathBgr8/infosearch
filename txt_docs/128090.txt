|plating=Tan |armament= |equipment= |affiliation= |firstmade= |retired= }} Rolo droids were a type of droid that rolled on a singular wheel. One rolo droid was present in Mos Espa on Tatooine in 32 BBY,Star Wars: Galactic Atlas and passed by the Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn and the young slave Anakin Skywalker. The rolo-droids first appeared, briefly, in George Lucas's Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. Category:Droid models
