Emdee
1520

Emdee was an MD-series medical droid who served the First Order. While loading crates aboard the shuttle Two-Seven-Oh-Five on a First Order space station, Emdee was approached by Finn and Poe Dameron, members of the Resistance who were attempting to escape the station disguised as medical workers. Emdee investigated the two "workers," but was electrocuted by the astromech droid BB-8 and left in a crate. *Choose Your Destiny: A Finn & Poe Adventure Category:Droids of the First Order Category:MD-series medical droids
