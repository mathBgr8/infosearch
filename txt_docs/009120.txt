|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=136 |isbn=978-1-56971-020-3 |isbn2= |timeline=4000 BBY |series=Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi miniseries |issues=TPB |preceded by=Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire trade paperback |followed by=Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising trade paperback }} Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi – Knights of the Old Republic is a graphic novel collecting the five-issue miniseries Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi. It was originally released as simply Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi &ndash; The Collection with the same cover art, except that the words "The Collection" were directly beneath the "Tales of the Jedi" logo, rather than at the bottom of the cover. It also included the covers of each of the initial comics. Four thousand years before Luke Skywalker stood as the final Jedi, his predecessors wandered the universe, each playing his own role in keeping the peace and holding back the forces of the dark side. This 136-page collection focuses on Ulic Qel-Droma and Nomi Sunrider, two young novices training to become Jedi warriors.
