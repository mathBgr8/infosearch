Technical Services Division
1118

The Technical Services Division, also known simply as Technical Services, was a subdivision of the Imperial Navy's Fleet Support Branch. It was charged with all matters relating to power distribution, environment, and other services not covered by the Engineering Division. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition *Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide Category:Imperial Navy divisions
