Kadok Resources
511

Kadok Resources was a Bitthævrian-controlled company. After some unfortunate experiences with Imperial-backed mining companies wanting to operate in Guiteica (particularly Vulca Minerals adopting a pushy attitude about yttrium mining), Kadok Resources was assigned to manage all of Guiteica's exports. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Mining companies
