|pages= |upc= |timeline=c. 19 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Obsession |issue=5 |next=last }} Obsession 5 is the fifth and final issue of the Star Wars: Obsession comic series, released May 18, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics, and the final piece of Star Wars media released before Revenge of the Sith. On the bleak cemetery world of Boz Pity, the Jedi fight what they hope will be a decisive battle against the Separatists. As a team of powerful Jedi take on Count Dooku, Mace Windu goes one-on-one with General Grievous in a spectacular duel that requires both parties to pull out all the stops! But Obi-Wan Kenobi cares for none of this. He is concerned with only a single target&mdash;the enemy he has pursued across the galaxy. But Obi-Wan doesn't realize that his greatest triumph might also lead to his greatest failure, and destroy everything that makes him a Jedi and a hero. The exciting conclusion of this story sets the stage for Episode III Revenge of the Sith! Adi Gallia was originally killed off in Obsession 5 (2005), but her death was later overridden by the Star Wars: The Clone Wars television series. Her death was instead depicted in the episode "Revival" (2012). Leland Chee, the Keeper of the Holocron continuity database, provided confirmation that any parts of the comic relating to her death should be treated as non-canon.
