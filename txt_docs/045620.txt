B4711 was an Imperial stormtrooper present during the Blockade of Kashyyyk in 1 ABY. B4711 was the designation of a stormtrooper who served the Galactic Empire in 1 ABY. During the Empire's Blockade of Kashyyyk, B4711 and another trooper raided the home of Chewbacca's family alongside a commander and a technician. However, after their search turned up no sign of Rebel activity, the other Imperials left the home, assigning B4711 to stay and keep watch. The trooper soon discovered a device which the young Wookiee Lumpy had made to fool the others into returning to base, which he destroyed. B4711 then chased Lumpy out onto the front deck of the home, where he was met by Chewbacca and Han Solo, who had just arrived for the Life Day celebration. Solo tripped the trooper, and B4711 fell to his death in the forest below. Later on, when the Empire began looking for the trooper, Saun Dann gave an Imperial officer the idea that B4711 had raided the home, stolen much of the food, and fled. The Empire then proceeded to send out a search party. }} B4711 appears in 1978's The Star Wars Holiday Special, where he utters a Wilhelm scream as he falls to his death.
