Eiattu pirates
2592

The Eiattu pirates, also known as TIE Pirates, were a pirate group of Imperial starfighter pilots who were hiding out on the planet of Radix in the Abraxas system in the wake of the Battle of Endor. They committed several raids on New Republic freighters and convoys that they saw as trespassing in their home system while using Radix as a depot to refuel and rearm their ships. The group was created in 4 ABY after the debacle at Endor and was led by Lieutenant Harme Kiela. A former Imperial Navy captain named Kine later betrayed the group during a battle between the TIE Pirates and forces of the New Republic.
