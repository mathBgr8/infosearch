Lorrd City
1085

Lorrd was the capital city of the planet Lorrd. The city was named for the planet itself, and the home to that planet's famed university. It was the headquarters of the Lorrd Security Forces, and the Lorrd City Spaceport was one of the planet's largest. Fiolla, later assistant Auditor-General of the Corporate Sector Authority, hailed from the city of Lorrd. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Category:Capital cities City
