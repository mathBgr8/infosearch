Strike the Moons Flightknife
975

Strike the Moons Flightknife was a unit of Adumari Blade-32 superiority fighters, commanded by Captain Liak ke Mattino in 13 ABY. They were the first Adumari unit to challenge Wedge Antilles' Red Flight in an honor duel, using only simulated lasers and pigment-filled missiles. The pilots learned several lessons from Wedge's Red Flight following the action. Several days later, Strike the Moons helped Red Flight escape from Cartann by firing upon Lords of Dismay Flightknife, justifying their action as refusing to allow "some honor-grubber" stop Wedge.
