House Halliikeenovich
1359

House Halliikeenovich or House Hallikeenovich was an unimportant Ancient House based on their homeworld of Dramassia in the Juvex sector.The Essential Atlas During the Clone Wars, a member of the house, Són Halliikeenovich, hoped to gain his house prestige by marrying Neelanon's senator Terr Taneel of the House Taneel and joining their houses. If she returned his interest, it was not obvious to the public. *Dressing a Galaxy: The Costumes of Star Wars *Star Wars Chronicles: The Prequels *The Essential Atlas Category:Ancient Houses
