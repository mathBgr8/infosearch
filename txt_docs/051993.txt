*1996 Star Wars Technical Journal of the Rebel Forces, pg. 11, notes that the magazine's three Special Edition volumes were published prior to September 15, 1996. |subscribers= |pages=83 |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=1 |series=The Official Star Wars Technical Journal }} Star Wars Technical Journal of the Planet Tatooine, also styled as Star Wars Technical Journal Vol. 1, was the first of three volumes in The Official Star Wars Technical Journal magazine series from Starlog Communications International. It was published in October 1993. The three volumes were later compiled into the compendium title Star Wars Technical Journal. Volume One was reprinted in July 1994 with a new foil-embossed cover to herald the release of the series' second and third volumes later that year. In 1996, to celebrate the upcoming release of The Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition, Starlog reprinted all three volumes as "Special Edition" versions. Each Special Edition volume came in a sealed poly bag along with bonus content in the form of an eight-page gatefold supplement.
