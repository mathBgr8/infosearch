New Coronet
1109

New Coronet was a city on the swamp world of Trigalis. A rundown haven for criminal activity, the city was at one time a bustling trading post. During the Clone Wars, it was controlled by Black Sun assassin Xist. *Obsession 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Black Sun locations Category:Cities
