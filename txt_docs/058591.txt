Eyes (Rodian)
1061

"Eyes" was a Rodian who had once been imprisoned on Kessel. However, when a smuggler came to the planet, "Eyes" stowed away on board the smuggler's YT-1300 freighter, paying the crew 50 credits per day to keep him safe. *Star Wars Gamemaster Handbook Eyes
