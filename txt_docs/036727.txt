Dryanta
2020

Dryanta (meaning "old judge" in ShyriiwookGalactic Campaign Guide) was a Wookiee and cousin of Chewbacca. During the Imperial occupation of Kashyyyk, Dryanta served in the Wookiee resistance under Katarra. When Chewbacca returned to his homeworld to attend Lumpawarrump's Test of Ascension, he left the Millennium Falcon in the care of Dryanta and Jowdrrl, another one of Chewbacca's cousins. Dryanta participated in the rescue of Han Solo from the Pride of Yevetha.
