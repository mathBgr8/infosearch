|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=19 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The New Essential Chronology dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=81 }} Republic 81 is the eighty-first issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comic books. It was published on January 4, 2006 by Dark Horse Comics. The first issue of a three-part story that begins during the events of Revenge of the Sith, and reveals a battle that was not shown in that film! The Separatist attack on the Wookiee homeworld of Kashyyyk demands a strong response from the Republic, and Jedi Masters Luminara Unduli and Quinlan Vos arrive with their clone armies. But there may be more behind the Separatist attack&mdash;and more to the Wookiee's defenses than the Jedi know. Old friends, new enemies, and lots of action!
