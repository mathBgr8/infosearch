Force mask
1199

thumb|150px|A [[Human/Legends|Human female wearing the mask.]] A Force mask was a device that was utilized in order to protect its wearer from the effects of Force powers. A complicated device with unknown origins, the mask was effective in blunting mental attacks. If a Force-sensitive was to don this piece of equipment, they would find themselves suddenly disoriented, as device's effects would blunt a Jedi or Sith's own connection to the Force.
