Remote Terminator
865

The Remote Terminator was an extremely strong alcoholic beverage, the consumption of which was viewed by many in the galaxy as an extremely risky proposition. Before mixing a Remote Terminator, it was common for a bartender to ask whether the patron wanted the ingredients mixed using a preprogrammed recipe, or for the ingredients to be mixed together randomly. The latter option was considered to be riskier than the former. *Young Jedi Knights: Shadow Academy Category:Cocktails
