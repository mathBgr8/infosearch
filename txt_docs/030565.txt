*George Lucas *Stan Lee |penciller=*Howard Chaykin *Steve Leialoha *Rick Hoberg *Bill Wray *Ernie Chan |inker= |letterer=*Jim Novak *Tom Orzechowski *Carol Lay *Mike Royer |colorist= |cover artist=*Rick Hoberg *Dave Cockrum |editor=Roy Thomas |publisher=Del Rey |release date=November 1977The Marvel Comics Illustrated Version of Star Wars |other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=128 |isbn=034527492X |isbn2= |timeline=0 BBY&ndash;0 ABY |series=Star Wars (1977) |issues=1&ndash;6 |preceded by= |followed by=The Empire Strikes Back }} The Marvel Comics Illustrated Version of Star Wars is a Legends trade paperback that collects the first six issues of the Marvel comic-book series Star Wars, reprinted in a smaller size in black and white. The book was adapted by Roy Thomas and Howard Chaykin from George Lucas's screenplay for the film Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. It also includes a special introduction written by Stan Lee, as well as a gallery of pin-ups illustrated by Chaykin and Ernie Chan. The Marvel Comics Illustrated Version of Star Wars was published by Del Rey in November 1977.
