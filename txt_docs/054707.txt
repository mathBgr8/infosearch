Army of the Wookiee Trade Guild
743

The Army of the Wookiee Trade Guild was a group of Wookiees that defended the Wookiee Trade Guild. They fought in the Kashyyyk Trade Rebellion that took place in 28 BBY, at the beginning of the Separatist Crisis. The army was completely destroyed by the Trade Federation's battle droids and the Sith Lord Darth Tyranus, who led the attack. *Star Wars: Battlefront Army of the Wookiee Trade Guild Category:Organizations of Kashyyyk
