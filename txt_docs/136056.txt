Unidentified boneless species
1410

A species of boneless sentients with crystalline eyes existed during the time after the Imperial Period. They spoke through their prehensile trunks. These trunks had fine tendrils at the ends, and members of the species had at least five trunks. They formed a colony on Chalcedon, and were its bureaucrats. One member of the species, who worked in the center of a glass building for a strange water pool, was known as the Indexer. *The Crystal Star *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
