Oejoe Hitewa
2157

Oejoe Hitewa was a Human smuggler who, along with his accomplices, Stu Hemphawar and Ruceba Ahid, worked out of a tiny apartment close to the Uscru Entertainment District of Coruscant. Active during the years prior to the Battle of Naboo, the Senate Bureau of Intelligence issued a bounty for their arrest, when the group was implicated in trafficking illicit material throughout the Core Worlds.
