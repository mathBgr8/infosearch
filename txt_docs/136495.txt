Ram's Head
2325

The Ram's Head was a Rebel Alliance CR90 corvette, the first and only ship to be outfitted with the experimental Shield X deflector shield. thumb|left|200px|The Ram's Head, about to smash into an [[Imperial-class Star Destroyer/Legends|Imperial command tower.]] Prior to its capture by the Rebellion, it was an Imperial corvette known as Blade 5. It was stolen and used to ram an array of Star Destroyers docked in a shipyard. Technicians aboard the CR90 Corvette Libra modified it to lock coordinates on the shipyard, thus destroying all of the Star Destroyers in a row.
