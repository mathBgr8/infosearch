Renquet
1932

Renquet was an Imperial Moff during the Galactic Civil War. On sampling the cooking of Yeoman Vimran Trell, of the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Adjudicator, Renquet was so impressed with the food, Fromirian roast queg, that he requested the starship became the flagship of his Sector Fleet.
