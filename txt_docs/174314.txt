Minelayer (Beta-class ETR-3 escort transport)
817

Minelayer was an Imperial Beta-class ETR-3 escort transport equipped with one or more minelayers. It was tasked with laying a mine field around Comm Center, and was presumably destroyed by either the Rebel Alliance pilots who attacked that station or the Azzameen family, who hacked into the base's systems. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Beta-class ETR-3 escort transports Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
