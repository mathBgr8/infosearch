Sergio Aragonés
1649

Sergio Aragonés is a cartoonist and writer best known for his contributions to Mad Magazine and creator of the comic book Groo the Wanderer. His Star Wars&ndash;themed comics include Sergio Aragonés Stomps Star Wars, "Spare Parts," and "Junkheap Hero." He also illustrated the Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide.
