Colby Hoff
3841

Colby Hoff was a male and formerly a pesky business competitor of Prince Xizor,Star Wars Encyclopedia the leader of the Black Sun crime syndicate. After a failed gamble to outsmart Xizor left him ruined, Colby committed suicide by firing a blaster into his mouth and blowing off the back of his head. Seeking to avenge his father's death, Hoff's son later ambushed Xizor inside a protected corridor on the planet Coruscant in 3.5 ABY.The New Essential Chronology dates the events of the Shadows of the Empire novel to 3.5 ABY. Xizor calmly subdued his assailant and snapped the young man's neck after remarking that Colby Hoff was foolish to have challenged him. Colby Hoff was first mentioned in the 1996 novel Shadows of the Empire, written by Steve Perry.
