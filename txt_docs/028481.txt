Ambak
1267

Ambak was a Mon Calamari physicist. Ambak proposed that there was a distance from a planet beyond which moons could exist or be created by the accretion of left-over matter. Inside of this distance, moons would not form due to the mother planet's gravitational pull. The distance was relative to the planet's size and natural gravity.
