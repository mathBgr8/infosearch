Blazing Rockets
956

Blazing Rockets was the autobiography of poet, scholar, and philologist Ebenn Q3 Baobab. In it, Baobab described the many adventures of his lifetime, all "with breathtaking lyricism and unflagging power," according to his Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Journals and memoirs
