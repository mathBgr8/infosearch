Aradian pulse-lifter
729

Aradian pulse-lifters were an atmospheric drive system. Developed around 15 ABY, pulse-lifters were an alternative to the commonly used repulsorlifts. The pulse-lifters created massive shockwaves and sonic disruptions, preventing their use in high-traffic areas. They were sufficiently rare to draw the interest of New Republic military officials when used on Yevethan Aramadia-class thrustships. *Before the Storm *Cracken's Threat Dossier Category:Antigrav technology
