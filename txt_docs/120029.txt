Feswe Corridor
3698

The Feswe Corridor was an uninhabited star system, located in the Gordian Reach sector of the Outer Rim Territories. The Corridor was full of closely-packed asteroids, which made navigation difficult. It was named after Feswe, the Rebel Alliance scout who blazed a hyperlane from Ladarra to the Corridor. thumb|left|200px|The [[Millennium Falcon/Legends|Millennium Falcon attacks the Executor in the Feswe Corridor.]] The Rebels used the Corridor to smuggle supplies from Feriae Junction to Yavin 4, avoiding the Imperial blockade of the Gordian Reach.
