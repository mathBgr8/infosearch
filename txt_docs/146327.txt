Karl Kesel
1193

Karl Kesel (born January 7, 1959) is an artist who illustrated the Jedi Prince series of young reader novels. Raised in Victor, New York, Kesel read comic books as a child. He later decided that he wanted to become a cartoonist. He eventually worked for DC Comics and Dark Horse Comics. *The Glove of Darth Vader *The Lost City of the Jedi *Zorba the Hutt's Revenge *Mission from Mount Yoda *Queen of the Empire *Prophets of the Dark Side Category:Comic book pencillers
