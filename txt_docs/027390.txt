Ahazi
1953

The Ahazi was a Hajen-class fleet tender of the New Republic Fifth Fleet. During Operation Hammerblow, the Ahazi exited hyperspace in the planet's atmosphere, disintegrating the ship and its crew instantly. The ship's destruction marked the fleet's first loss.
