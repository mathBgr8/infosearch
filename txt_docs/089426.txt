Rutanian
1450

Rutanians were the native species of the planet Rutan. Their species had an offshoot called the Senali who inhabited Rutan's own moon Senali. The rivalry between the two species was provoked by the Yuuzhan Vong into war, leaving both Rutan and Senali weak and vulnerable to invasion.
