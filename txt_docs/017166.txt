It was the second planet in the Marcellus system, which resided in the Marcellus Nebula. Glauheim circled the yellow sun Marcellus. The gas giant, along with the rest of the its star system, was located in the Meerian sector of the New Territories portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Glauheim was first mentioned in Planet of the Mists, a 1992 adventure supplement authored by Nigel D. Findley and published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
