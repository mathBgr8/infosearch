Specter of Thrawn

}} Specter of Thrawn was a proposed comic series that would tie-in with Star Wars: The Hand of Thrawn Duology, set between Specter of the Past and Vision of the Future, in 19 ABY. It was to be co-written by Timothy Zahn and Michael A. Stackpole. *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Legends comic miniseries
