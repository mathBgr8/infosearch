Jal Shey Mentor
894

The Jal Shey Mentor was the highest rank of the Jal Shey order, and the equivalent of the Master in the Jedi Order. They were highly respected for their wisdom and understanding of the Force, and were considered to be the brightest minds in the known galaxy. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: The Clone Wars novelization *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:Religious titles
