Harrier
2563

Harrier was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser under command of the New Republic. The Harrier was one of the six Katana Dreadnaughts used by General Garm Bel Iblis. It was later part of the Coruscant defense fleet by the time Grand Admiral Thrawn attacked the planet using his camouflaged asteroids to block the system.
