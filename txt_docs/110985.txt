|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters=Dantari Village |locations=Dantooine |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} }} The Kunga were a Dantari tribe from Dantooine. Most of the Kunga lived in their village and the tribe also established a stronghold in a cave. The Kunga were in conflict with the Janta and the Mokk, another Dantari tribes. During the Galactic Civil War, the Galactic Empire considered the Kunga as a threat for their troops.
