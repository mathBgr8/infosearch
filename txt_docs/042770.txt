N'ildwab
1589

N'ildwab was a rocky planet in the Outer Rim with many canyons. The inhabitants of N'ildwab lived very simple lives. Because of this, in an effort to control the people, Nobam Nol terrorized the inhabitants and claimed to be a Sith. He was able to mimick Force powers because of his wired gloves. Because of his terrorism, the people of N'ildwab hired Boba Fett to kill Nol.
