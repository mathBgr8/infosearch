Chopstick
2335

Chopsticks,Queen's Peril also known as eating sticks,Heir to the Jedi were a type of eating utensil that originated from and commonly used in the Aakaash system of the Oplovis sector. Foreigners of the star system found chopsticks difficult to use. On Naboo, Captain Quarsh Panaka and his wife Mariek, both of the Royal Naboo Security Forces, used chopsticks to eat a late-night dinner shortly after Padmé Amidala had been elected Queen. They were also used to eat Corellian buckwheat noodles or nerf nuggets with onions. If one were to order food from the dining establishment on Denon, Sakhet's Noodles, they would receive their food in flimsicard boxes with disposable eating sticks to eat it. In 0 ABY, Luke Skywalker and Nakari Kelen dined at Sakhet's Noodles, and they used disposable eating sticks to consume their food.
