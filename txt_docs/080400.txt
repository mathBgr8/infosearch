Piloted by Civé Rashon, who operated under the call sign "Howlrunner" and the designation OS-72-1, Obsidian 1 at one point flew over Cloud City on the planet Bespin. Obsidian 1 originated as a TIE/LN starfighter prop for the 1980 original trilogy film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back.Star Wars Chronicles The starfighter was later brought into Star Wars Legends canon by a card in the 1998 Special Edition Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
