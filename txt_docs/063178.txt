thumb|250px|[[Armand Isard/Legends|Armand Isard's office, in the Imperial Intelligence Headquarters]] The Imperial Intelligence Headquarters was located in the Imperial Palace on Coruscant. It was used as a base of operations for Armand Isard's Imperial Intelligence.
