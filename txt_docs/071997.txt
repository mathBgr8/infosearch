Nicholas Read
812

Nicholas "Nicky" Read is an actor who played the Ewok Nicki in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. He was credited as Nicki Reade in the film. Read also appeared in such films as Labyrinth and Without Work: The Drive Away. Category:Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi actors
