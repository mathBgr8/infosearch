Dehno
1955

Dehno was a Human male patrol captain who worked for the Czerka Corporation branch on Kashyyyk. Dehno was a ruthless man, who would kill (or in his terminology, "put down") any Wookiee slaves that became rebellious. He eventually encountered Revan who persuaded him not to enslave any more Wookiees and to request a transfer. If the player fails to persuade otherwise then the player may have to kill him. They will still get light side points, but not as much.
