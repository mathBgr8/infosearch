Sector Alpha
595

Sector Alpha was a sector in the bizarre space station known as Zirtran's Anchor. Sector Alpha was the central sector of the station and housed, among other important locations, The Hub - the original Zirtran barge - as well as the gambling den, Chabak's, and the local Pandor's Hydrospanner repair shop. The Geelans resided primarily in this sector. Alpha, Sector
