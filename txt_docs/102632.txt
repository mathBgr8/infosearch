Zindra
1181

Zindra was a female Rodian. She worked for Hyperspeed Express as an office manager at the Bilbringi Shipyards, and was very helpful with requests. When the Jedi trainee Dal Konur went rogue and was being pursued on the request of Master Luke Skywalker, Zindra supplied Konur's pursuers with information regarding his movements on the planet.
