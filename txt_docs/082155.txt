Fel Swoop
1416

The Fel Swoop was a cantina located in Treasure Ship Row in Coronet City on Corellia. The Fel Swoop was located in Treasure Ship Row, an open air market inside Coronet City's Blue Sector on Corellia. It was often packed with members of swoop gangs and served Corellian whiskey. In 11 ABY, Corellian Security members Gil Bastra and Iella Wessiri took Corran Horn to the Fel Swoop after his father died.
