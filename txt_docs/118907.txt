House of Tagge merchant ship
2599

House of Tagge merchant ships were large freighters built and used by TaggeCo. Such vessels carried profits from the Wheel to more coreward banks. Many such vessels were piratized by the Imperial forces of Sector Commander Strom. The wealth was taken for the Empire and the ships left destroyed.
