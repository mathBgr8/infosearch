Suuv Ban D'Krid
1983

The Suuv Ban D'Krid was a Yuuzhan Vong warship. A Suuv Ban D'Krid boasted similar firepower to the smaller Uumufalh gunship but also carried 36 coralskippers. It had broad red and green arms which served as docking bays for the coralskippers. Each Suuv Ban D'Krid was 740 meters in length and had 40 Yaret-Kor.
