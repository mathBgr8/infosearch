Koong Wars
811

The Koong Wars were a series of conflicts perpetuated and participated in by the Hutts and possibly the Moras sometime prior to the Battle of Naboo. A notable veteran of these campaigns was Ebenn Q3 Baobab, who was forced into service by the Hutts. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Conflicts during the Great Peace of the Republic
