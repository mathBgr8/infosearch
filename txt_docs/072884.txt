Edan tiger
1045

Edan tigers, also known as snowcats, were large predatory felines native to the colder regions of Edan II. They were covered from head to spined-tail in white fur with black stripes which helped conceal them in their natural habitat, both during warmer weather and the colder snow-covered months.
