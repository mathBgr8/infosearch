During the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic, a Human clone served in the Imperial Military as a stormtrooper and was designated "KE-829." He was one of the soldiers stationed on board the first Death Star in the days prior to its destruction at the Battle of Yavin, and was on guard duty near Tractor Beam 12 by the time the Millennium Falcon arrived on the battle station. When the Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi deactivated the tractor beam to allow the Millennium Falcon to leave Docking Bay 327, he overheard KE-829 and another trooper conversing about the BT-16 perimeter droid. After accomplishing his objective, Kenobi used the Force to confuse the troopers and escape the area. Specifically, Kenobi tricked KE-829 into thinking there was something behind them, eventually attributing it to nothing more than outgassing.
