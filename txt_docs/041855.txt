Reezen
2707

Reezen was a Force-sensitive Human male who served as a corporal within the faction of the former Galactic Empire that served under Warlord Zsinj in 8 ABY. As a young teenager, Reezen attended a parade with his father on Coruscant in which Darth Vader was present. Vader felt that the boy had some power in him, so halted the parade and patted Reezen on the head, telling him to always trust his instincts. Unaware of the significance of this encounter, Reezen never realized he was Force-sensitive. He had limited powers of divination and at times was able to feel where enemies would send their ships. He later worked in military intelligence under Warlord Zsinj. It was Reezen, through his powers of reason and subtle use of the Force, that recognized a pattern in the hit-and-run attacks orchestrated by Han Solo designed to draw Zsinj's forces away from the planet Dathomir.
