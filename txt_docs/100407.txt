|role=Repulsorlift vehicle manufacturing |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=*Kolatill Council *Moff Kentor Sarne's forces }} Kal'Shebbol Transport was a repulsorlift vehicle manufacturing company located on the planet Kolatill in the Kathol sector of the Outer Rim Territories. The corporation's plants also manufactured repulsor parts for Ikas-Adno, a major repulsorlift manufacturer that also had factory-complexes on Kolatill. In 0 BBY,"Omens" states that martial law on Kolatill was "declared eight years ago". The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The DarkStryder Campaign to "eight years after the Battle of Yavin," for a date of 0 BBY. martial law was enacted on the world by the local Imperial governor, Tetrum, who was operating under the orders of Moff Kentor Sarne, ruler of the sector. Under the terms of the martial law, all local repulsorlift manufacturing corporations were required to build combat speeders for the Moff's forces. Kal'Shebbol Transport was mentioned in the RPG adventure Omens, which was published in The DarkStryder Campaign by West End Games in 1995. Category:Repulsorcraft manufacturers
