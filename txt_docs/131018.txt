Meridian (shuttle)
623

The Meridian was a sleek, black-hulled shuttle used by New Republic Intelligence to ferry the bodies of Doctors Kroddok Stopa and Josala Krenn, several recovered Qella bodies and artifacts back to Coruscant. She was captained by Wagg. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shuttles Category:Starships of the New Republic
