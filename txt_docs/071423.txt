Bomb (Immobilizer 418 cruiser)
2451

The Bomb was an Immobilizer 418 cruiser loyal to Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. In 3 ABY, it was stationed at Zaarin's Supply Base in the Gwar system, The Bomb was one of the main targets of an Imperial assault led by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Courageous and attempted to destroy the vessel as part of the ambush Zaarin had laid for his enemies.
