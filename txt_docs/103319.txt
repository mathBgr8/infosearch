Collo Fauale Pass
1452

Collo Fauale Pass was an area of the Collo Fauale mountain range on Phaeda. It became a redoubt for the underground New Republic forces on Phaeda in 11 ABY. Following an attack by Colonel Xexus Shev, who sought to capture Kir Kanos for Carnor Jax, Commander Mirith Sinn ordered an evacuation to the redoubt. However, the traitor Tem Merkon informed Jax of the hideout. Jax soon inadvertently captured Sinn and threatened orbital bombardment of Collo Fauale, which he nevertheless ordered.
