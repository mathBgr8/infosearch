Riknak
2334

Riknak was a Weequay saboteur working for Gardulla the Hutt who was wanted dead by Hurleydo Travels for hijacking tourist speeders (and their occupants) employed by the agency. His bounty of 1,500 credits dead was claimed by Jango Fett. Riknak appeared in the 2002 LucasArts video game Star Wars: Bounty Hunter. He is the final secondary bounty of the level Gardulla's Palace (and the final secondary bounty in the entire game overall). In 2012, the incarnation of Star Wars Insider Rogues Gallery from Issue 131, "An Audience of Aliens," identified individuals in Watto's viewing box from the Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace film (1999).
