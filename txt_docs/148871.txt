Halabar
1426

Halabar was an Mon Calamari professor during the Galactic Civil War. A respected teacher on his homeworld, Halabar was captured during the early stages of the Empire's occupation of Dac. Forced to live as a pet to a minor Imperial official on Coruscant, Halabar soon broke free from his captivity and escaped the planet on a smuggling vessel. He hid out in the Outer Rim and established a small tapcafe.
