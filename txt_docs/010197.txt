Ken Steacy
784

Ken Steacy is a renowned comic artist from Canada. His artwork is featured on the covers of Star Wars Galaxy #1 and The Art of Star Wars Galaxy. He also provided the illustrations for Podracing Tales, Imperial Spy, and R2-D2's Mission: A Little Hero's Journey.
