Argor
1595

Argor was a Human male and a Lesser Prophet of the Dark Side, one of the Emperor's Mages at the time of the Battle of Yavin. Unlike many higher-ranking Prophets who stayed on Imperial Center and Dromund Kaas, Argor served as a field agent of the Prophets of the Dark Side, foiling Rebel activity in the Outer Rim Territories. *Rebellion Era Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Prophets of the Dark Side Category:Sith acolytes and apprentices
