The Kashirim were the native people of the planet Kashir. The group included Humans. According to Obi-Wan Kenobi, Kashirim were known throughout the galaxy as thieves and pick-pockets, since stealing was an honored tradition and practiced art at Kashir. *Star Wars: The Ultimate Visual Guide Category:Planetary cultural groups
