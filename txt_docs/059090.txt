Kol Yabu
2744

Kol Yabu was an apostle of the Undying Flame, the cult of the Lover Gods, and a reader. Kol Yabu's body had been ritually burned and scarred, so that one side appeared male, representing Yun-Q'aah, and another side female, representing Yun-Txiin. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, Yabu served under the priestess Vaecta aboard Warmaster Tsavong Lah's flagship Sunulok. The apostle was present in the Glory Room on the Sunulok for Lah when Executor Nom Anor returned from the planet Coruscant after addressing the New Republic's Senate. When Anor relayed the revelation that the young Jedi Jacen and Jaina Solo were twin siblings, Yabu pressed him to confirm it. Anor went on to ask how Yun-Txiin and Yun-Q'aah would view the death of only one twin. Initially, the Undying Flame apostle avoided the question, saying only that the Lover Gods did not demand sacrifice. When pressed by Lah, however, Yabu admitted that such an event would cause the Great Dance to become unstable.
