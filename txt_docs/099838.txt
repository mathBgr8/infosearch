Tiranga
999

Tiranga was a Hutt who owned and operated Tiranga's Loft. A spinal cord injury as a young worm left him partially paralysed from the waist to the end of his tail. The injury stunted his growth at only two-point-five Imperial standard meters. *The Black Sands of Socorro Category:Hutts Category:Males
