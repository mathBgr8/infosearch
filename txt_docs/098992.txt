Miasra sauce
728

thumb|Trimpian served in a seasoned miasra sauce Miasra sauce was a yellow-swirled sauce that was considered the perfect accompaniment to trimpian. Miasra sauce appeared in the video game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sauces
