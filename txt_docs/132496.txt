Ministry of Defense
3327

The Ministry of Defense, also referred to as the Defense Committee, was the agency responsible for oversight of the Republic Military and was headed by the Minister of Defense. It was dissolved during the Ruusan Reformation, along with its Minister. During the Great Sith War, Netus was its head. During the Mandalorian Wars, Koa Delko was Minister and Catronus Steffans was Director for Public Information. Admiral Saul Karath and his forces were given information from the Defense Ministry that Mandalorian forces were going to invade the Ottega system.
