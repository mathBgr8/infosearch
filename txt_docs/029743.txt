|regulator=Umgullian Racing Commission |associated= |participants= |teams= |players=Blob 14 |affiliation=*Dack TrackJedi Search *Fondine Raceways }} A blob race was a contest of speed between protoplasmic, gelatinous Umgullian blobs through a course of obstacles&mdash;from fine mesh screens to a bed of nails. It was a major betting sport in Umgul City on the planet Umgul. The syrupy masses, usually grayish green but laced with bright hues, rolled, slithered, and oozed their way through.
