Rob Caswell
874

Rob Caswell was an illustrator and graphic artist for West End Games who worked on many Star Wars books. Some of his more notable and enduring pieces of vehicle visual design are the Imperial AT-PT one-man walker and the Wild Karrde (Corellian Action VI Transport). He provided art for: *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook *Cracken's Rebel Field Guide *Wanted by Cracken *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *Twin Stars of Kira *Death Star Technical Companion *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two Category:Roleplaying game artists
