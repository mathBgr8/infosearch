Anti-Air Super Battle Droid
1683

The Anti-Air Super Battle Droid was a battle droid used by the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars. This unit had no arms, copper-colored armor plating, and was armed with one or two air defense missile launchers on its shoulders. This weapons platform was very effective against all air units, however was not as hardy as Hailfire droids, and Geonosian Fighters. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns B2-Z Anti-Air Category:Droid models of the Confederacy of Independent Systems
