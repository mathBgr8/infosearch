Goroth Beta
1760

Goroth Beta was the brown dwarf companion of Goroth Alpha. It orbited Goroth Alpha beyond the orbits of the Goroth system's planets. Goroth Beta had a 227,575-kilometer diameter. The canonical diameter for this brown dwarf is scientifically impossible. Real world brown dwarfs are slightly smaller than gas giants, because they are compressed by their much stronger gravity.
