Sernpidal City
1137

thumb|right|200px|[[Chewbacca/Legends|Chewbacca's death in Sernpidal City.]] Sernpidal City was the capital city of the planet Sernpidal. It was destroyed in 25 ABY, as the Praetorite Vong used the Yo'gand's Core tactic, pulling Dobido, one of Sernpidal's moons, into the planet. It had a population of 50,000 but its only spaceport was a fenced-in field.
