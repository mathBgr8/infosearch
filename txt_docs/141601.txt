Ganner Squadron
624

Ganner Squadron was a squadron temporarily established by Corran Horn and Wedge Antilles during the Second Galactic Civil War. It was named after fallen Jedi Knight, Ganner Rhysode. The squadron consisted of Horn, Antilles, Luke Skywalker and Mara Jade Skywalker. *Legacy of the Force: Exile *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance starfighter squadrons
