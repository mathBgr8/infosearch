Brejik's belt
784

Brejik wore a personal belt during combat which, when used in conjunction with his arm band, produced a mild, though effective, barrier against melee attacks.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic In 3956 BBY, his belt was recovered by Revan and Bastila Shan after they slew Brejik and the rest of the Black Vulkars at the Tarisian Season Opener swoop race. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Belts
