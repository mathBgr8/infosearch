Fasgo
1501

Fasgo was a male spacer with red hair. Fasgo once worked for Roa, and was able to give him and Han Solo information on Reck Desh's involvement with the Peace Brigade. He later was captured by the Yuuzhan Vong along with Roa at the Battle of Ord Mantell by a qworlth skaal sucking at the Jubilee Wheel, a station orbiting Ord Mantell and taken as yammosk-tending slave aboard the Crèche.
