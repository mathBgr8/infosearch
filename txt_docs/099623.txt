Blood tree
912

The blood tree was a species of tree that was native to the Colonies planet Batorine. Along with their characteristic red wood, they were famous and widely known for being the material from which Blood Carver sculptures were crafted. *Rogue Planet *The Essential Guide to Alien Species *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Blood Carver culture Category:Trees
