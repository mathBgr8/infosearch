Nardo
1552

Nardo was the chief of Lady Tarkin's security forces at Port Tarkin on Phelarion. When Princess Leia Organa was stranded on the planet and forced to work as Lady Tarkin's housemaid, Nardo did not trust her. After watching her for a few days, he discovered her using the subspace transponder to get a message to Han Solo. Nardo fired on her, but missed, striking a nearby megonite moss display case, which exploded, killing him.
