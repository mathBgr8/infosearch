Energy pudding

*Chagrians *MirialansThe Approaching Storm *Ansionians |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Energy pudding was a type of foodstuff that was frequently found in survival and ration packs in dehydrated form. Energy pudding was edible by a wide array of species, including Humans, Chagrians, Ansionians, and Mirialans; however, it was widely considered to lack a pleasant taste. The food, which came in wrapped bars and was brown in color, was available as early as the Cold War between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire, and the combat medic Archiban Kimble survived almost entirely on pudding. In 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion the Jedi Knight Barriss Offee discovered the Ansionian Tooqui raiding her supplies during a mission to the planet Ansion, and the thief tried to threaten her with packs of energy pudding as weapons.
