Siringana
2599

The siringana were vicious, reptilian predators native to the planet N'zoth. These beasts had four three-taloned legs, a pair of scythelike arms, a straight, spiked tail, a mouth full of razor-sharp teeth and a face similar to that of a Yevetha. Their tough, leathery brown skin allowed them to blend into the desert environment.Coruscant and the Core Worlds They would turn on other siringana easily so there were never more than three in a group at a time, and they seemed to relish instilling fear in their prey before moving in on the kill. They reproduced by laying eggs, which were considered a delicacy in the Koornacht Cluster. The siringana were considered to be as merciless and brutal as the Yevetha, who were the only sentients native to N'zoth.
