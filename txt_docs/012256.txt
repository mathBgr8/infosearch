Laric wielded a green lightsaber. Niquon once sent Laric to take the remains of an assassin droid discovered by Borta to Lowghen Badra on Yabosta. An unknown Dark Jedi apprentice of Von Totha waylaid Laric to get him out of her way so she or her allies would be free to assassinate Niquon, but Lowghen Badra helped Laric escape.
