NK-3
2645

NK-3 was the designation given to two IG-100 MagnaGuards that operated as the personal bodyguards of the N-K Necrosis droid on Kashyyyk. The assassin droid IG-72 captured two MagnaGuards on behalf of the scientist Treun Lorn, to be used as part of Nycolai Kinesworthy's research with the remains of General Grievous. The result was N-K Necrosis, a killing machine in the vein of Grievous himself. The NK-3s were used to train the Necrosis droid in the lightsaber forms Grievous had once instructed his guards. The NK-3s and Necrosis itself were ultimately defeated by a rag-tag band of spacers in the Myyydril Caverns on Kashyyyk. The droids' remains were stripped of all valuable possessions.
