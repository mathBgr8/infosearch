Triumph (Marauder-class)
2098

The Triumph was a Republic Sienar Systems Marauder-class corvette in the Kathol Republic Navy. The ship was commanded by Hendric Faile, and carried a complement of marines onboard. In 8 ABY, the Triumph responded to a distress call from the Bravado III alongside her sister ships Juno II, Mirandine, Quillion, Siroma, and Solidarity. Together, they intercepted the modified New Republic corvette FarStar, and marines from the Triumph boarded and captured the vessel.
