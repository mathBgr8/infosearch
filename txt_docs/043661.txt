Knytix Pox

Knytix Pox was a disease communicated by the knytix insect of Thyferra. It was highly contagious, and could be acquired by simply breathing in a concentrated area of knytix. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Poxes
