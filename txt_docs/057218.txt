Tassida Judrelle
1492

Tassida Judrelle was a Jedi Knight who was assigned to a team of Jedi to break up a salt smuggling ring in an asteroid belt near Cona. Judrelle joined Jedi Knights Stass Allie and Quarmall in the capture of the leader of the smuggling ring, Lojrak Shrag. The Jedi team was led by the smuggler Billey to the smugglers' secret base, where they engaged the criminals.
