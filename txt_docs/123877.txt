Dagger (Assassin-class)
1003

Dagger was a group of two Assassin-class corvettes used by the Galactic Empire. They were attacked by type 2 mines deployed by the Rebel MC40a Light Calamari Cruiser Planka. However, two Missile Boats arrived and destroyed the mines, allowing a transport to dock and repair the two corvettes.
