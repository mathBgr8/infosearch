LifeLine Technologies
1465

LifeLine Technologies was a company that manufactured various enviro-suits and protective gear for the crews of starships.Their range of products included the Model 11 Space Suit, the Immediac Model 10 spacesuit, and the TechMaster II vacuum suit. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *Platt's Smugglers Guide *The Far Orbit Project *Millennium Falcon Owner's Workshop Manual Category:Personal gear companies
