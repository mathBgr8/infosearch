|type= |term= |powers=Leader of the Trade Federation |duties= }} }} The Viceroy of the Trade Federation was the senior executive position of the Trade Federation conglomerate. The Sith Lord Darth Sidious manipulated Nute Gunray's rise to the position, that would allow the megacorporation's invasion of the planet Naboo in the following year,Star Wars: Galactic Atlas Gunray served as Viceroy of the Trade Federation during the invasionStar Wars: On the Front Lines and, despite facing several trials in the Supreme Court, kept the title during the subsequent Clone Wars. The Viceroy was killed on Mustafar in 19 BBY by Sidious' new apprentice, Darth Vader.
