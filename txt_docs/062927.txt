The eopi Elrod was the pet of a Human civilian who lived on the remote planet Nadiem. In 22 BBY,Star Wars: The Comics Companion dates the events of "Hide in Plain Sight," which depicts the Battle of Nadiem, to twenty-two years prior to those of , which corresponds to 22 BBY per The New Essential Chronology. five months into the Clone Wars conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Nadiem was attacked. The civilians of the world were evacuated by the Republic as the Separatist Droid Army, commanded by General Grievous, invaded the planet. Elrod and his owner were in the process of evacuating into a Low Altitude Assault Transport from a settlement, when the Mirialan Jedi Luminara Unduli instructed the civilians to leave their belongings behind, as they did not have space. Elrod's owner inquired to Unduli why he should leave the eopi behind, as he considered him family. The Jedi explained that Elrod would take up the space of three people and asked him which of his neighbors should be denied passage. With no response to Unduli, Elrod's owner instructed him to run away, told the eopi that he would be safe and said that he would look for him. Elrod gave a grunt and left. Elrod had yellow eyes and green skin. His owner considered him to be apart of his family. During the evacuation of his owner's settlement, the eopi wore a leash around his neck which his owner held.
