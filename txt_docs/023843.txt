Saras
1767

Saras was the name of the Killik hive of weavers and shapers. At one point settled on Ruu, the hive and its Joiners settled on the planet Woteba in the Utegetu Nebula after the Qoribu Truce. The Saras were an artistic colony, building replicas of the X-Wing Luke Skywalker flew at the Battle of Yavin, as well as the Millennium Falcon, though the Dark Nest's influence had the Saras unconsciously insert tiny Gorog into the replicas. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King abridged audiobook *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen abridged audiobook *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Killik hives
