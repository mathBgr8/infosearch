OPS third sector precinct house
870

The third sector precinct house of the Office of Peace and Security was located on the west side of Gadrin. Probably the smallest precinct house on Cularin, it was a square one-story structure with only one door. It had a small front room with a pair of desks and half a dozen chairs, a holding area, and a supply closet. Hidden behind the supply closet was a stairway which led down to a secret meeting room which had a round table and nine seats. The room was used by the Cularin resistance, and resistance leader Gerta Haman had OPC agent Corporal Ortin, secretly also a resistance member, bring a group she was recruiting here to meet with her.
