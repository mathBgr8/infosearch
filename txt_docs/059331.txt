Respectable
757

Respectable was an Imperial cruiser that was a part of the blockade of ThonBoka. It functioned as a picket cruiser, challenging Lando Calrissian when he arrived at the blockade. Lando was brought aboard the vessel. He posed as a traveling salesman and gained the trust of a security chief onboard, who gave him contacts on other ships on the blockade he could sell his wares to. *Lando Calrissian and the Starcave of ThonBoka *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
