Lucky Star (Elerion)
719

The Lucky Star was a casino in Kushal Vogh on Elerion. It was owned by Gogol, and was shut down by the Galactic Empire after their attempt to get Nescan Tal'yo's holocube failed. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Casinos
