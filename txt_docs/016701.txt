Shiritoku Spur
876

The Shiritoku Spur was an area of Wild Space that included such worlds as B'Knos, G'rho, Cattamascar, Gymelo, Kammia, and the Bakura system. Also running through the region was the Shiritoku Way hyperlane, which began at the Imperial world of Timora. The Spur was invaded by the Yuuzhan Vong. Most of its worlds chose never to join the New Republic. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Essential Atlas Category:Wild Space regions
