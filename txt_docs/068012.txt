Abregado-rae Spaceport
1399

thumb|The water canals of Capital City thumb|left|200px|Abregado-rae Spaceport Abregada-rae Spaceport was the capital and largest spaceport of Abregado-rae. It contained many water canals which were used for transportation. The older part of the city was referred to as the Old Patch.
