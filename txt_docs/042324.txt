Clezo

Clezo was a Rodian Vigo of Black Sun during the time of Prince Xizor. Clezo provided funding for House Tresario of the Bounty Hunters' Guild. He was present in a Vigo meeting where Guri publicly executed Vigo Green for spying for an outsider organization.Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel After Xizor's death, Clezo was seen in a Vigo council meeting about the future of the Black Sun. He and Wumdi did not like when Lonay started acting as head of the council, and Clezo was not happy seeing an attractive woman supporting him, stating he would not be ordered by some human and female.Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 3 During a later meeting, the Heroes of Yavin showed up after the human female's invitation. Then the room was invaded by thugs, who were quickly slaughtered. The human female identified the bodies as Vekker's soldiers; nobody knew that the mysterious human who was actually Savan in disguise, and the one responsible for the attack to incriminate Vekker. Savan claimed that Clezo's and Wumdi's troops would soon be after Vekker. Clezo was a subdued, but well-tailored dresser. He was considered short for his species.Shadow Games He was especially skilled at selecting the proper personnel for success, and endeavored to provide his part of Black Sun with diverse interests and holdings.
