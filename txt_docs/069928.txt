thumb|right|200px|[[Teemto Pagalies/Legends|Teemto Pagalies with his digit droids]] Digit droids were droids primarily used in the game Digotto. They were round and red, with a black center that displayed a number. The game of Digotto was based on mathematics. Teemto Pagalies was allegedly fond of this game, and described the droids as "cute." *Star Wars Math: Jabba's Game Galaxy Category:Class five droid models
