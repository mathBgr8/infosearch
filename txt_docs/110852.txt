Plasma Defense Satellite
999

right|thumb|A Plasma Burst Satellite Plasma Burst Satellites were similar to laser defense satellites. They were loaded with powerful plasma blasts, shot from two turrets stationed on top of the satellite. One round was enough to destroy even the strongest fighter craft. Multiple shots could take down the shields of corvettes and frigates. It was not nearly as effective against multiple squadrons of fighters and frigates. *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Defense platform classes
