Kryndyn
1065

Kryndyn was the capital and most populous city of Zelos II. Like the planet's other cities, it was surrounded by a 10-meter high wall with six gates. It had a garrison of 50,000 troops. In addition to a thriving commodities market, the city had a wide variety of bars, casinos, entertainment centers, libraries, and hotels. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Capital cities Category:Cities on Zelos II
