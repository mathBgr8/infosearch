release on November 3. |pages=5 |published in=Star Wars Insider 27 |reprinted in= }} "A Star Wars CELibration" is an article by Jon Bradley Snyder that appeared in Star Wars Insider 27. The article discusses Nelvana's Droids and Ewoks animated series and includes quotes from an interview with Clive Smith, a co-founder of Nelvana. The article also includes a Droids/Ewoks Episode Guide which gives short, informal summaries of each animated episode. *Star Wars Insider 27 Category:Star Wars Insider 27 articles
