Makrin City
1557

Makrin City was the capital city of Shelkonwa. It billed itself as the Second City of Spires, a reference to ancient Coruscant. It contained the Nightowk Repository and a governor's palace. Farfarn District was an area of the city. It had three spaceports: Makrin Main Spaceport, Greencliff Regional Spaceport, and the palace port.
