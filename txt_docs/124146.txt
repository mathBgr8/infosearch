|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair=Black |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Light |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} A Tatooine moisture farmer was present in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in Mos Eisley when Luke Skywalker met Han Solo for the first time in 0 BBY. He was engaged in conversation with one of the Tonnika sisters, whom unknown to him was actually Karoly D'ulin in disguise. This moisture farmer first appears in the 1977 film Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, portrayed by an uncredited actor, conferring with one of the Tonnika sisters. Many have often identified this moisture farmer as character Ariq Joanson, though Leland Chee clarified this wasn't Joanson.
