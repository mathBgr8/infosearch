Abridon holding camp
2114

This holding camp was located on Abridon in its capital city. In 3 ABY, following the planet's failed attempt to join the Alliance to Restore the Republic, the Galactic Empire constructed a holding camp on the outskirts of the planet Abridon's capital city and began placing Abridons in it. The camp was referred to as a refugee camp, but its inhabitants were essentially prisoners of the Empire. Most of the inhabitants were likely linked with the Abridon Nationalist movement. Shortly before the Battle of Endor, Rebel generals Tyr Taskeen and Brenn Tantor returned to the planet and drove out the Empire. In the process, Tantor discovered the existence of the camp when a prisoner in the Government Center pled for the release of his family from the facility.
