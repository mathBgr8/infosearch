Tall (Human)
845

"Tall" was a Human male smuggler operating out of Tholatin. Privy to information on where the Yuuzhan Vong would attack next, he operated a scam with his partner, "Short," where he would take paying refugees on their freighter, Trevee, from refugee settlements to worlds that would soon come under Vong attack.
