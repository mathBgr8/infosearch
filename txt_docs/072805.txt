Shunta Osarian Dharrg
1749

Shunta Osarian Dharrg was the leader of the planet Osarian in 25 ABY and an opponent of Nom Anor's actions on Rhommamool who viewed Leia Organa Solo's meeting with Anor as giving him unacceptable legitimacy. To that end, Dharrg assigned Captain Grappa of the Osarian First-Force to attempt to prevent her from reaching Rhommamool.
