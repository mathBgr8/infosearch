Kyakhta
2365

|masters= |apprentices= }} Kyakhta was a male Ansionian enforcer for Soergg. He had ragged red hair and had a crude prosthetic to replace his missing right arm. He was an Alwari who had been cast out from his Tasbir clan to the cities as a child due to his mental illness. In 22 BBY, before the Clone Wars, Kyakhta worked with another Ansionian named Bulgan to kidnap Jedi Padawan Barriss Offee, but instead aided her after she cured his and his companion's mental illnesses.
