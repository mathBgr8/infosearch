Jord Kraas
1709

Jord Kraas was an officer serving as a lieutenant in the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Lieutenant Kraas and Captain Mordaph Clafdatha compiled the report code-named V492771R225Y, detailing the capabilities and specifications of various starships used by the Empire, Rebel Alliance and other neutral parties for Darth Vader.
