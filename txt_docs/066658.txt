R4-C9
1314

R4-C9 was an R4-series agromech droid, with an R2-series dome, used by Jedi Master Saesee Tiin, when piloting his Jedi interceptor during the Battle of Coruscant in the late stages of the Clone Wars. It was colored yellow and gray, unlike the starfighter's hue. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droids of the Jedi Order Category:R4-P astromech droids
