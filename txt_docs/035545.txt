Omeesh

Omeesh was a Hutt who lived on Sleheyron at some point prior to the Jedi Civil War. He enjoyed torturing his slaves. He was eventually murdered by one of them, a Twi'lek girl named Yuthura Ban, who stabbed him in his bedchamber. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Hutts Category:Males
