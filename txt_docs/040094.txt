Sakarie
2013

Sakarie was a Human smuggler who met Meetra Surik in the Iziz Cantina on Onderon. She was seeking a starport visa, which she needed to transport permacrete explosives for the powerful Exchange criminal organization. She was willing to offer a unique sword, a rare Qixoni crystal, or five thousand credits for a visa. She was voiced by Elizabeth Conboy. The player character is only able to learn that Sakarie is an Exchange member and needs the visa to transport explosives, if they have a high enough persuasion skill or by using Mind trick.
