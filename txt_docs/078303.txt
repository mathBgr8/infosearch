Renatasian Confederation
913

The Renatasian Confederation was a loose association of human exiles from the destroyed nation-states of the Renatasia system. Led by Klyn Shanga, these fighter aces sought to hunt down the Butcher of Renatasia. They eventually killed Osuno Whett, the man responsible for betraying Renatasia to the Centrality and the Empire.
