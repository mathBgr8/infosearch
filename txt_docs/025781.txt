|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters= |locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Grand Army of the Republic }} Clone Engineers were clone trooper engineers of the Galactic Republic that were active during the Clone Wars against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Clone Engineers made sure that the Republic's vehicles were in top condition, and were equipped with specialized Phase I clone trooper armor and later Phase II clone trooper armor.
