Omiddelon III
1416

Omiddelon III was a planet in the Mid Rim whose rotational plane and orbital plane were perpendicular to each other. Its weather consisted of an intensely hot climate, followed by a brief mild season, then an extremely freezing winter, followed by another mild season. The planet orbited a very bright and hot star and so was subjected to intense light, heat, and radiation.
