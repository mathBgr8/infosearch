Megaphone disk
566

A megaphone disk was a device used to increase the volume of one's voice. Cupped in the speaker's hand, the disk could make the voice loud enough to boom across an entire area. Grand Admiral Thrawn used a megaphone disk to address the populace of Wayland. *Heir to the Empire *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Communications
