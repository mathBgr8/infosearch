Yinchorri flutter-pack
976

thumb|250px Yinchorri flutter-packs were unique packs with wings which allowed Yinchorri to fly. These only could be used for one hour, then they needed to be recharged for eight hours before another use. They used them to move about on the balloon city on the colony world of Yibikkoror, as well as in their attack on the Jedi Temple. *Jedi Council: Acts of War 2 *Jedi Council: Acts of War 3 *Jedi Council: Acts of War 4 *Invasion: Revelations 4 *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Air transportation technology Category:Yinchorri culture
