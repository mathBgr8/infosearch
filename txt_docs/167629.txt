Admiral Jerjerrod's Testimony
1847

"Admiral Jerjerrod's Testimony" is a short dialogue written by Greg Gorden, originally published in the Imperial Sourcebook and later archived on Hyperspace. Admiral Jerjerrod is interviewed by the Procurement Panel of the Senate Naval Subcommittee. Senators Gopple and V'troren question Jerjerrod regarding critiques he has made of the Panel's decisions regarding procurement procedures for the Republic Navy.
