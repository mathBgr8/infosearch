L. Fraig
1192

L. Fraig was once a member of Cherit's gang until Jaing Skirata killed Cherit. Working from the Tekshar Falls Casino, he maintained some legitimacy in his business and gathered many contacts. When Boba Fett came looking for Skirata, he questioned Fraig on how to contact the clone by dangling Fraig from a building.
