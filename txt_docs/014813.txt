Lansono
3607

}} Lansono was a celestial body located in the Lansono system, a part of the Southern Core portion of the Core Worlds. By the date 13:5:16, Senator Candabrine Bu represented Lansono in the Galactic Republic's Senate. Bu supported the passing of the Military Creation Act that proposed the restoration of a centralized Republic military, which had been dissolved as part of 1000 BBY's Ruusan Reformation event.The Essential Atlas Lansono was introduced in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #56, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published on the HoloNet News website on May 16, 2002. 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia misspelled the celestial body's name as "Lansano."
