Christian Read
610

Christian Read is an Australian comic book writer, best known for his work on Dunwich, a take on the Cthulhu mythos, and The Watch. His most recent work includes weird western The Eldritch Kid: Whisky and Hate, ongoing supervillain series Unmasked, and urban fantasy novel Black City. Read has written three stories for Dark Horse's Star Wars Tales series: "Nameless," "Apocalypse Endor," and "The Duty." Category:Comic authors
