Kvarm Jia
1689

Kvarm Jia was a male Human Senator of the New Republic who represented the Tapani sector circa 27 ABY. At the bidding of Leia Organa Solo, he convened the Corruption Panel that investigated Viqi Shesh prior to the Appeasement Vote.
