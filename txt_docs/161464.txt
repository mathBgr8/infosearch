Arky's Emporium of Forgotten Treasures
1076

Arky's Emporium of Forgotten Treasures was an antique store ran by Seb "Arky" Arkos. Located in the city of Xakrea, Arky's Emporium got caught up in the four-way agenda of Hal Horn, Ysanne Isard, Garm Bel Iblis and Moranda Savich around 0 BBY. Among its stock were a Weequay chin-harp (sans Chord hammer) and a feline statue made of Ithorian toal wood. However, the shop was intended as a front for forged identification.
