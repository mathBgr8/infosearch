|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Black Sun Nexu" is a scenario for the Star Wars Miniatures game, written by Sterling Hershey and published on the Wizards of the Coast website on June 15, 2006. It depicts a battle between Black Sun and Rebel Alliance forces. The Black Sun syndicate, in an effort to eliminate rival smugglers, has released a nexu. Unfortunately, the smugglers are working with the Rebels, who proceed to fight back. Black Sun Nexu
