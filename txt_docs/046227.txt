Dor Reder *Lieutenant Niclara Varnillian |registry= |aliases= }} Pulsar was a Star Destroyer which was present at the Battle of Endor. Commander Dor Reder served aboard the ship as a junior officer, and Lieutenant Niclara Varnillian was a member of the ships's gunnery crew.
