Kaudine
732

Kaudine was one of five key fortifications created by the Freedom's Sons around the Intergalactic Communications Center during the Battle of Praesitlyn. Located on the flank of Zozridor Slayke's army, the guns placed at this outpost could be swept around to cover any direction.
