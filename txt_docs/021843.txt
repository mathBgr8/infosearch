It was a part of the Seswenna sector of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Dzass IV was the site of the prestigious Lunis-Medix Medical Academy. At some point, the Anomid businessman Yin Vocta graduated from the academy at the top of his class. Dzass IV was first mentioned in the 1997 sourcebook Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy, authored by Paul Danner and published for West End Games's Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
