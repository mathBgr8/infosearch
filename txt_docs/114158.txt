Ashala Ma-dred
1396

Ashala Ma-dred was one of the four wives of King Sha-Mar Ma-dred of Kashir. When the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker were asked by the King to help in protecting his wives, Ashala stole Skywalker's lightsaber while he was distracted by her beauty to lure him to her to test his integrity.
