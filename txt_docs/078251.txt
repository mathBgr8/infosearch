Yamnoss
1430

Yamnoss was a H'nemthe patron at Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. M'iiyoom Onith was originally going to be named Yamnoss as a pun on "Yam Nose", her production nickname. This character did not appear in the final cut of The Star Wars Holiday Special. The official Star Wars website established Yamnoss as an official name for this H'nemthe.
