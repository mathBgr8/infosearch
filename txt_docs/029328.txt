Scarab Mark VI assassin droid
2236

The Scarab Mark VI assassin droid was an assassin droid designed to look like a small insect. It was one of the eighteen variants that comprised Sienar Intelligence Systems' scarab droid line. They were designed to kill through stealth, not brute force. It had enough capacity for 2 uses. The droid had magnetic feet which allowed it to climb metallic surfaces and advanced sensors that would allow it to track a specific target if it was supplied with a sample of biological fluid.
