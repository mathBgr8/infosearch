|affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Duskhan League |navy=Imperial Navy |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases=Destiny of Yevetha }} Redoubtable was an early-model Imperial I-class Star Destroyer. It was later renamed the Destiny of Yevetha. After taking significant damage in the retreat from the Battle of Endor, she was sent to the Black-15 shipyard at N'zoth. The ship was seized by Nil Spaar of the Yevetha during their uprising, and renamed the Destiny of Yevetha. After the Yevetha completed her repairs, she served in the Yevethan fleet during the Black Fleet Crisis.
