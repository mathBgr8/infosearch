|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Artist |companies=Dark Horse Comics |website= }} }} Steve Crespo is an artist who worked as one of the pencillers on the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron comic series. He has also contributed to a number of other comics for Dark Horse Comics. Born in Manhattan, Crespo attended New York's School of Visual Arts. He has also drawn for Marvel and DC, and he worked as a character illustrator with Nickelodeon Creative Resources, eventually going on to lead their East Coast illustration team as Director of Character Art and Illustration. There, he worked on such properties as Rugrats and SpongeBob SquarePants.
