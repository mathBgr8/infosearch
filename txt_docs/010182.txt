|subscribers= |pages=72 |isbn= |issn=1081-1362 |genre= |issue=10 |series=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine }} Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 10 is the tenth issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in January 1997. This issue's theme focused on the then-upcoming Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 10, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with two promotional trading cards, one featuring Jabba the Hutt for Topps' Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition Widevision set and another highlighting issue 10's cover art by Joe Jusko. This issue also included the fold-out poster article What's New in the Trilogy. *Rebel Report *Communiqués *Around the Galaxy *The Galactic Bazaar: The Cardboard Galaxy **Super Sleuths **Design An Alien *Game Room: Launching a New Rebellion *Cyber Notes *Use the Force: Special-ized Trivia Quiz *Ordinary Joe *Renewing the Universe *The Force Is Back *Sounding Off *Back to the Drawingboard *What's New in the Trilogy *Sandbound on Tatooine *The Ice World Cometh *Star Wars: Shadow Stalker *The Essential Reader's Companion 10
