U'Han Swinol
1355

U'Han Swinol was an out-of-work male Bith musician who lived the during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Haugg Nerf Industries for defaulting on a loan and had a bounty of 2,000 credits alive and 1,000 credits dead.
