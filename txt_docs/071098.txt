The Anthropology of Engineering
2743

The Anthropology of Engineering was a work authored by the Incom Corporation engineer Jo Ewsli. At some point by 0 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Rebel Force: Trapped, which includes the appearance of an I-7 Howlrunner, to 0 ABY. Ewsli had designed the I-7 Howlrunner starfighter, which was named after the omnivorous howlrunner creature of the Wild Space &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Kamar system planetThe Essential Guide to Alien Species Kamar. In The Anthropology of Engineering, the engineer, who at one point had visited Kamar, narrated how he had observed a pack of hunting howlrunners from inside a safari dome. In that passage, Ewsli also noted that he had experienced a moment where he felt like a part of the pack, and that he had subsequently strived for his entire life to design a starfighter that would help its pilot recreate the feeling. The Anthropology of Engineering was mentioned in the 2003 reference book The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels by W.
