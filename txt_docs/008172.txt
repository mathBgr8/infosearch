*Vosadii Techno-Palace, Sleheyron |locations=*Ansion *Dirha *Kwenn Space Station *Llanic *Sleheyron |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Clans of the Ancients *Hutt Cartel }} Vosadii was a Hutt kajidic. Soergg Vosadii Bezhin, a Bossban on Ansion, was a member of this clan. The Vosadiis were among the few clans that could trace their lineage back to Varl, the home of the ancient Hutt Empire from before the Galactic Republic; thus, they were known to be one of the Clans of the Ancients, along with Bareesh, Gorensla, Desilijic, and others.
