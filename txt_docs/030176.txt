|publisher= |release date= |media type= |pages= |published in= |genre= }} History of the Galaxy, Parts One, Two and Three was a popular three-part story that Mark IV architect droid Huyang told to the Jedi younglings at the Jedi Temple. The former Jedi Ahsoka Tano considered the first part to be the best. During their journey to another galaxy along the Pathway to Peridea, Huyang and Tano discussed the story. Category:Histories
