|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=0.5 ABY |series=Star Wars: River of Chaos |issue=4 |next=last }} River of Chaos 4 was the fourth and final issue in the Star Wars: River of Chaos series of comics. It was released October 24, 1995. The war of good against evil has been waged for centuries. It began long before Ranulf joined the Imperials, long before he was ever born, and the war will continue long after he is dead. But now Ranulf is forced to fight one battle of this war, a battle that rages in his soul. When Governor Grigor's Imperial forces clash with Princess Leia's Rebels, Ranulf must choose between his Imperial allies and the Rebel he has come to love&hellip;and the outcome of his personal battle may change the current of this River of Chaos!
