Handree
1696

The Handree was a DP20 gunship that was in operation with the Rebel Alliance sometime during the Galactic Civil War. The vessel was stationed on Isis and supplied medicalstuffs to guerrilla rebels on Polmanar. The Imperial survey crew of the Wanderer hijacked the vessel, transporting it to Bespin. Alliance operatives recaptured the ship before the Isis location could be exposed. *The Isis Coordinates *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:DP20 gunships Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
