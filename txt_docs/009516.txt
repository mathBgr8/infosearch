High Inquisitor
3165

thumb|right|250px|High Inquisitor Antinnis Tremayne High Inquisitor was a senior rank in the Inquisitorius, a shadowy branch of Imperial Intelligence consisting of dark side Force-sensitives agents who worked under the command of Emperor Palpatine himself. They were often employed as interrogators, though their primary task was to seek out potential Jedi candidates and investigate Force activity during the reign of the Galactic Empire. The only higher rank in the Inquisitorius was that of Grand Inquisitor, who was the head of the Inquisitorius.
