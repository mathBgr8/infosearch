*Rated 16 RU |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Rebel Alliance *Azzameen family *Various private, commercial, and pirate interests }} The Cargo Canister was a tube-shaped freight container manufactured by Xizor Transport Systems. The canister was used to store cargo during transit in both realspace and hyperspace. For transport, the container was able to be carried by freighters, like the YT-1300 light freighter. The smallest of the container designs in the line, the cargo canister was often used for emergency deliveries or transportation of small but valuable cargoes. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Cargo container classes Category:Xizor Transport Systems products
