Sony
1722

Sony Corporation is a multinational corporation that manufactures various home video, music, video gaming and audio equipment. Corporate headquarters are located in Tokyo, Japan, and the US subsidiary, Sony Corporation of America, is based in New York City. Sony Online Entertainment (now Daybreak Games) is responsible for the company's development and maintenance of massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPGs). The company has released two Star Wars&ndash;themed MMORPGs since 2003, Star Wars Galaxies and Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures.
