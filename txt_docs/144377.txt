Wk'ou melon
738

A wk'ou melon was a double-bulbous, pale red melon from Morishim. Wedge Antilles was harassed by a Morish female attempting to sell him a melon of this sort while he was on Morishim. Lando Calrissian also purchased one. *Specter of the Past Category:Melons Category:Plants of the Outer Rim Territories
