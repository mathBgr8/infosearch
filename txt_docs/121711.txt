Vadid Shenvehr
1368

Lieutenant Vadid Shenvehr was a hangar controller who worked at Byblos Starport Tower 214 hangar 217-11. He was impartial in the Rebel Alliance's fight against the Galactic Empire. Shenvehr was knowledgeable in all aspects of Imperial flight regulations and Byblos traffic regulations. He was also a fully qualified pilot. He didn't care who won the Galactic Civil War as long as they didn't disrupt his hangar operations.
