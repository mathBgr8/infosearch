Coruscant News Network
1334

The Coruscant News Network, also known as the Coruscant News Net, was a news agency in the later days of the Old Republic and in the age of the Galactic Alliance. It was the premier source for news on Coruscant. Some years before the Battle of Caamas, Coruscant News Network journalists accompanied Gar Stazi and a Tynnan diplomat on Kuthard. They asked an old Dimok matriarch about the centuries lasting conflict with the Ripoblus.
