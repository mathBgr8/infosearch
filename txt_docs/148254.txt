Buldobeast
1001

}} Buldobeasts were creatures found on the planet of Vryssa. Imperial scout troopers patrolling the Vryssa garrison once spotted something moving in the nearby trees. Thinking it was just a buldobeast, only one trooper set out to investigate. What the trooper thought was a buldobeast was actually Boba Fett. Fett killed the trooper with ease.
