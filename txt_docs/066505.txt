Jeff Coopwood (born June 29, 1958) is an American actor who provided the voice for Captain Quarsh Panaka in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace video game, Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, and Star Wars: Episode I: Battle for Naboo.
