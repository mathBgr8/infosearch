Mark Cotta Vaz
772

Mark Cotta Vaz is the author of several Star Wars "Behind the scenes" books. He also compiled the Star FX section of the Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. *From Star Wars to Indiana Jones: The Best of the Lucasfilm Archives (with Shinji Hata) *The Secrets of Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire *The Art of Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones *The Complete Star Wars Trilogy Scrapbook *The Galactic Empire Scrapbook *The Rebel Alliance Scrapbook *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace The Official Souvenir Magazine (with Marc Patten) *The Making of Star Wars: The Force Awakens Category:Non-fiction authors
