Interceptor (Guardian-class light cruiser)
681

The Interceptor was an Imperial Customs Guardian-class light cruiser that had been assigned to patrol the Elin Roe system at some point during the Galactic Civil War. It was commanded by Captain Hallyn Phlynne. Category:Guardian-class light cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
