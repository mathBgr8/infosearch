*Western Reaches places the Vulcusion sector in what The Essential Atlas identifies as the Western Reaches. |sector=Rocantor sector |system=Yabosta system |coordinates=L-15 |xyz= |distance= |suns= |orbited=Yabosta |position= |moons= |routes= |lengthday= |lengthyear= |class=Terrestrial |diameter= |atmosphere= |climate= |gravity= |terrain=Mountains, forests |water= |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species=Dolandu |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities=Stomorr |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Skalokor was the second moon of the world Yabosta. The moon was covered in forests and treacherous mountains, and was inhabited by the Dolandu, a sentient species of white-furred humanoids. One trading settlement on the moon was Stomorr. It was once attacked by forces under command of the Dark Jedi apprentice Von Totha who wanted to kill a Jedi Master that lived near the village. That Jedi Master, Niquon, defeated all of Totha's forces except for a drethi, a winged creature with Force-targeting poison. The drethi was finally defeated by Niquon's Padawan learner Nason Laric.
