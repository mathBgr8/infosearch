Sarlacc's Peak
990

Sarlacc's Peak was a location in the Carbon Score Wastes on Socorro, surrounded by the city of Killee-Kor. During the timeframe of the Second Imperial Civil War, the pirate Rav docked his ship, the Crimson Axe, within the peak. A sarlacc was said to live at the top, but it died centuries before the rise of the Galactic Empire. *Legacy (2006) 11 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sarlaccs Category:Socorro locations
