Zoochberry juice
2825

Zoochberry juice was a beverage containing zoochberry that was potable by Humans. In 5 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Zorba the Hutt's Revenge, which includes Zorba Desilijic Tiure's arrival in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina, in 5 ABY. the Hutt Zorba Desilijic Tiure barged into Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina on the planet Tatooine, desperate to learn why he could not enter his son Jabba's palace. The Barabel Tibor recommended that Zorba calm down and consume some zoochberry juice, which only exacerbated his rage. In the same year, zoochberry juice was served in significant quantities at a housewarming party at the Human Han Solo's sky house on the planet Bespin. Solo spent the party ensuring the zoochberry glasses were full while interacting with his guests. A short time later, Solo and the Human Leia Organa traveled to the space station Hologram Fun World to elope. After their arrival they were given glasses of zoochberry juice by their friend and the station's baron administrator, Lando Calrissian, who also partook of the beverage.
