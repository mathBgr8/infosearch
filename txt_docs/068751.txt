Brommstaad Mercenaries
1266

The Brommstaad Mercenaries, also referred to as the Bromstaad boys [sic], were a mercenary outfit hired by Black Sun for work on Corellia. There, they worked for Zekka Thyne before he was sent to Kessel. While on Corellia, they got into a fight with Riij Winward and Rathe Palror, a fight they nearly won if not for the timely arrival of Hal and Corran Horn. Most known members were either killed or captured when the Imperials raided Thyne's fortress. *"Side Trip" Category:Black Sun organizations Category:Mercenary units
