David Alan Grier
1369

David Alan Grier (born June 30, 1956) is an American actor, voice actor and comedian. He is known for being a cast member on In Living Color and Martin in the 1990s. He later won the Tony Award for Best Featured Actor in a Play at the 74th Tony Awards for leading the Broadway revival of A Soldier's Play. In Star Wars media, Grier provided additional voices for the radio dramatizations of Star Wars and The Empire Strikes Back.
