Molo shrub
643

Molo shrubs grew in the shade on Tatooine. They could be found in the canyons of the Jundland Wastes. During the day, the shrub resembled a simple scrub bush, however during the night time, the shrub erupted with leaves and flowers. *The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Plants of Tatooine
