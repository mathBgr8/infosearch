January 23
2023

January 23 is the 23rd day of the year. 342 days (343 in leap years) remain in the year after this day. *1939 &ndash; Greg and Tim Hildebrandt are born. *1948 &ndash; Ann Sachs is born. *1952 &ndash; Klaus Janson is born. *1970 &ndash; David Rabbitte is born. *1979 &ndash; Star Wars (1977) 22 published. *1980 &ndash; Star Wars Weekly 100 published. *1981 &ndash; Julia Jones is born. *1981 &ndash; Pepe Larraz is born. *1993 &ndash; The Star Wars Trilogy reprinted in mass market paperback format. *2002 &ndash; Star Wars (1998) 38 released. *2002 &ndash; The Official Star Wars Fact File 4 published. *2003 &ndash; High Alert! roleplaying mini-adventure published by Wizards of the Coast. *2007 &ndash; The five finalists of the Darth Who Contest are revealed. *2009 &ndash; "Defenders of Peace" airs on Cartoon Network. *2013 &ndash; Star Wars: Blood Ties: Boba Fett is Dead trade paperback published. *2019 &ndash; Star Wars (2015) 60 published. *2019 &ndash; Star Wars Battlefront II Count Dooku update released. *2021 &ndash; Trisha Noble dies. *2024 &ndash; The Courtship of Princess Leia unabridged audiobook released as part of The Essential Legends Collection. *2024 &ndash; Yoda: Dark Rendezvous unabridged audiobook released as part of The Essential Legends Collection.
