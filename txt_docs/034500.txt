Trenwyth was supposed to be a dead world, but when a large group of Tof suddenly appeared strongly entrenched on the world, the Nagai mounted an attack on their old enemy. The Tof destroyed most of the Nagai force, but there were some survivors, including Wing Leader Taikotelai Akikoma. The battle continued to rage in space. Princess Leia Organa's transport was caught in the crossfire, and forced to make an emergency landing on Trenwyth.
