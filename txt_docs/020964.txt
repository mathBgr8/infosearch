Despite its official classification as semi-sentientThe Essential Guide to Alien Species and inclusion in the "Non-Sentient" league, the Taurill species was in fact fully sentient.The Essential Chronology On the date 13:3:14, a news story titled "Yet Another Greenputt League" was published by the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. It reported on the formation of the Tour and how it joined the prevously established greenputt leagues. The Non-Sentient Professional Greenputt Tour was first mentioned on the HoloNet News website in the in-universe issue HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, published on March 14, 2002 and written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
