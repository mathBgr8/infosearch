Galactic Fair
1770

|conc= |next= |image= |name=Galactic FairImperial Fair |observedby=Galactic Empire |significance= |celebrations= |observances= |place= |begin= |end= |date= }} The Galactic Fair (called the Imperial Fair in the reign of the Galactic Empire) was the highlight of Fete Week, and was a traditional fair held annually on the planet Coruscant. The Fair was held along Glitannai Esplanade and incorporated dancing, acrobatics, shopping, technology exhibits, food pavilions, and a massive parade of military might. From the surrounding spires, thousands watched as the parade ended in Pliada di am Imperium. There, at the base of the Imperial Palace, dignitaries would bestow blessings on the crowd from an open-air balcony.
