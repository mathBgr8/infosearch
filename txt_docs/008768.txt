Soothfast
1293

Soothfast was a retrofitted Proficient-class light cruiser of the New Republic Defense Fleet that served during the Yuuzhan Vong War under the command of Captain Skent Graff. Six months into 25 ABY, Soothfast was patrolling the space near Durren in the Meridian sector when a pair of biotic warships entered the system. Soothfast and its complement of starfighters engaged the yorik coral spawn ship that moved towards them.
