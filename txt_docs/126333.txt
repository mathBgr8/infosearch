Croig Jacamden
1278

Croig Jacamden was a male Duros and the owner of Croig's Fix-It Barn in the capital city of Hweg Shul on Nam Chorios. While on Nam Chorios in a search quest for Callista Ming in 13 ABY, Luke Skywalker took up work as a mechanic at the Fix-It Barn for a short time.
