Sub-space image transmitter
1137

The sub-space image transmitter was a prototype communications device created by Little Kessel's Renn Volz. It utilized subspace technology, giving it a much longer range than the standard comlink. It also transmitted fully color, lifelike images, unlike most holoprojectors of the time. *"The Second Kessel Run" *Rebellion Era Sourcebook Category:Holographic communications Category:Subspace transceivers
