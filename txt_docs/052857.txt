Kendamari Casinos
1710

The Kendamari Casinos was a chain of high-class casinos located on worlds in the Lol sector of the Outer Rim Territories. The Kendamari organization had its headquarters on the planet of Kendamar. Kendamari Casinos were part of the Tenloss Syndicate, though some of the larger establishments were owned by the Hutts. They bribed a courier named Fellan to turn over evidence of Narloch's affiliation to Jabba the Hutt so they could take over his casinos on Reaper's World. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Casinos Category:Tenloss Syndicate companies
