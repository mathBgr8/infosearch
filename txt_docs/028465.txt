Danga family
1107

The Danga family were a family of Human farmer colonists that lived on Sulon. They owned the farm south of Morgan Katarn. In 1 BBY, their farm was attacked by a unit of Ghost Battalion in an attempt to frame Katarn's assassination on the Rebel Alliance. The family consisted of at least four people: the father/husband Don, his wife, and two children. All were killed in the raid. *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire Category:Families Category:Sulonese
