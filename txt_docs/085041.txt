Night scopes,Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear also spelled nightscopesJedi Quest: The Master of Disguise or referred to as night-vision scopes, were targeting scopes that allowed an individual to aim better in low-light conditions.Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition They could be affixed to blaster weapons to increase their accuracy. Boba Fett had a night scope on his EE-3 blaster carbine. *Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition Category:Targeting scopes
