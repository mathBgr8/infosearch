Maikel Das
703

Maikel Das is a German writer, artist, editor, and publisher and a regular contributer for the Official German Star Wars Fan Club magazine. He also contributed reports and illustrations to the US fanzine Blue Harvest (Zine) from 1994 to 2000. The illustrations of Ishin-Il-Raz appearing in Star Wars Insider 66 and 84 were based on Das.
