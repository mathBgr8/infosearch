Swori Mdimu
1515

Swori Mdimu was an Etti spacer who shipped weaponry to the Peace Brigade. His vessel, the Tinmolok, was captured by the Millennium Falcon (under the guise of Princess of Blood), and the Tinmolok was confiscated. Mdimu and his crew were then jettisoned in an escape pod.
