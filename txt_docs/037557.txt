Isced
2226

The Isced were one of three prominent tribes of the Ugnaught species from the planet Gentes. The other two known tribes were the Botrut and the Irden. Isced Ugnaughts were known for having a particularly distasteful sense of humor&mdash;a personality trait that often earned them the enmity of their brethren among the Botrut and Irden tribes.
