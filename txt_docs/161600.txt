Daryl F. Mallett
1050

Daryl F. Mallett (born May 3, 1969) is the author of Tongue-tied: Bubo's Tale, a short story appearing in 1996's Tales from Jabba's Palace. The story was less than six pages long and told the story of the Frog-dog, Buboicullaar. Mallett is also an actor, and has appeared as an extra in such Star Trek projects as The Next Generation, Deep Space Nine, and The Undiscovered Country. Category:Short story authors
