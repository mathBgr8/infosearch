Intergalactic Zoological Society
1326

The Intergalactic Zoological Society (IZS) was devoted to gathering and studying lifeforms from all over the galaxy. It was based on the planet Mycroft and worked with the Jedi Exploration Corps. During the Clone Wars, the Society refused to take sides, insisting that politics should remain out of science and that knowledge belonged to everyone. The Society operated a number of exploration ships, including the Titavian IV.
