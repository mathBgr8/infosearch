Endorian snail
898

Endorian snails were tiny creatures with orange/white coiled shells that lived in grassy hill areas on the Forest Moon of Endor. These tiny creatures can be found in some regions of Endor in Star Wars Galaxies, but contrary to other creatures in this game, players can't target or attack them nor otherwise interact with these snails.
