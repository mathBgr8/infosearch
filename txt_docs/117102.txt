Wyvern Toriq
712

Wyvern Toriq was the leader of Dragon Squadron, a group of elite Rebel X-wing starfighter pilots. Among his squadron-mates he was considered to be the most "normal" and survived as a result of pure skill, rather than drugs or a psychotic nature as many of the other pilots did.
