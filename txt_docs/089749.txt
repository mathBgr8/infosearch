Kerred Santhe
2070

Kerred Santhe was a Human male industrialist from Lianna and a member of the Santhe family. He acquired a controlling share in Sienar Technologies and its subsidiary Republic Sienar Systems around 99 BBY. As a result, Sienar Technologies was renamed Santhe/Sienar Technologies and made a subsidiary of Santhe Corporation. He was Darth Plagueis's first murder under the tutelage of Darth Tenebrous.
