Belleau-a-Lir
906

Belleau-a-Lir was an island city on the planet of Alderaan, located on Lir Lakir. This city was once considered one of the world's artistic and cultural centers during the days of the Galactic Republic, prior to the Galactic Civil War. It, along with Alderaan itself, was destroyed by the Death Star in 0 BBY. Category:Cities on Alderaan Category:Islands
