Red Bush Grove
1452

Red Bush Grove was an Ewok village several days' travel south of Bright Tree Village on the Forest Moon of Endor. All of its denizens, minus Leektar and Keoulkeech, were killed in a devastating fire that was the result of a lightning storm. The fire occurred some time before the Battle of Endor, and destroyed the entire village. Red Bush Grove was a location developed by Arthur Papadam as part of Leektar's What's The Story? entry. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities on Endor
