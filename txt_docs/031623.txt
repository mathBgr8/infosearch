Sylo family
1059

The Sylo family was a family of farmers on Dantooine. At the time of the Clone Wars, the family had lived on the Sylo farm for ten generations, and used it to harvest tritacale and yot beans. As of 22 BBY, the family consisted of: *Griggen Sylo *Tashna Sylo **Paxi Sylo **Six older children By the time of the Empire, the family had been forced to abandon their farm, and their fate after that is unknown.
