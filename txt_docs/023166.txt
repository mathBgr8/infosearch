Covepi'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located on the planet's western continent, situated between the capital of Drev'starn (to the northwest) and Navshe'starn (to the southeast). *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
