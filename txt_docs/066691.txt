Olanet droid factory
925

The planet Olanet was the site of a massive Separatist droid factory. It was said to be one of the largest producer of battle droids besides Geonosis. In 21 BBY, the factory was sabotaged by clone troopers N-5 and RC-3222. Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems locations Category:Droid factories
