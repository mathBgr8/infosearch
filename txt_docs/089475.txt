Starlifter
801

The Travis Motors Starlifter was a cargo loader used by Alfex Cargo Stacks on Brentaal.Coruscant and the Core Worlds Piloted by a humanoid in its one-seat cockpit or a droid in a special socket, the Starlifter carried cargo with four landing claws that emerged from the corners of its flat bottom. Alfex, as a front for Black Sun, made its own special modification to the Starlifter, hiding a pair of blasters in each vehicle. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Ground vehicle models
