Rakririan Burnout Sauce
1651

Rakririan Burnout Sauce was a spicy sauce, capable of burning mouths and throats if used in sizable quantities, due mainly to the use of "meltdown spice", which was actually made of a liquid petrochem fuel. As such, most sentients used it in trace amounts. In spite of its effects, the Jedi Knight Plo Koon was once known to have used a large amount to sabotage a dish in order to create a diversion while on a Jedi mission. *Star Wars Galaxies: Jump to Lightspeed: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Guide: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Sauces
