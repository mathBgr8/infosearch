Brosin
1277

Brosins were Near-Humans from Brosi. Brosins shared their native planet with Old Republic settlers for centuries. After the discovery of Zinsian veins on the planet, the Corporate Sector Authority government began displacing thousands of Brosins and "hiring" the youngest ones to work in their dangerous Zinsian processing plants.
