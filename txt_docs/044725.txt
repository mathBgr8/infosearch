Abyssin (language)
1123

Abyssin was the official language of the planet Byss, home of the Abyssin species. Abyssin had both a spoken and written form. The spoken form used mainly growls and grunts that many species considered primitive-sounding. The written form of language used only a small number of simple hieroglyphs. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Abyssin culture Category:Languages
