Benedict Taylor
1293

Benedict Taylor is an English actor who played Porro Dolphe (or Bravo 2) in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. His sister is Femi Taylor, who played Oola in Return of the Jedi. He also played the part of Prince Sixtus of Bourbon-Parma in an episode of George Lucas' TV series The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles. {| ! Year !! Title !!
