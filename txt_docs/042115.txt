Casandra Mateil
2161

Casandra Mateil was a female yellow-skinned Twi'lek who worked as a professional swoop racer during the Jedi Civil War. In 3956 BBY, Revan met her in Ahto City. Mateil was often considered to be somewhat egotistical; she would refuse to sign personal autoprints, instead the autoprints would be created, and fans had to pay for them. She held second place at the Ahto City swoop track until Revan helped Queedle Molto enhance his swoop to become Sector Champion. Mateil's time fell to third place, and Hukta Jax was moved to second.
