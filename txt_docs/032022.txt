Nebula Room
1175

The Nebula Room was an expensive restaurant onboard the luxury liner Star of Empire. Governor Dol Donar II with his wife Rissa and their son, freshly commissioned officer Nathan, dined there, following his graduation from the Academy of Carida. His classman Kyle Katarn was also invited. They had green salad, fresh scone, runyip stew, leaves with some berries; and candied insects, a specialty of the chef, who brought the bowl to the table himself. Hearing that the young officer's father was allegedly murdered by Rebels, the Governor started shouting curses to them, making some customers to turn to stare, and his wife feel uncomfortable, worried that some of them might be Rebels or sympathizers. The night ended with Lando Calrissian inviting the governor for a game of sabacc. *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire Category:Dining establishments Category:Spacecraft locations
