Gadget
1371

Gadget was the astromech droid assigned to Ton Phanan during his service with Wraith Squadron. Gadget remained with Phanan until his X-Wing was destroyed, leaving both pilot and droid without a vehicle. The droid was later transferred to Myn Donos when his own astromech, Shiner, was destroyed.
