During this battle, the Alliance lost important agents. The lead pilot of Scandium Team, Jouffer Talamiin, was injured and became permanently handicapped. Due to this, he was replaced with fellow Scandium Aven Cholus. Princess Leia Organa of Alderaan intervene in the battle.
