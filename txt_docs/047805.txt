It was assigned to Red Squadron and stationed at the Rebel base on Yavin 4. Red 6 was piloted by Jek Porkins with R5-D8 as the craft's astromech droid. Prior to the Battle of Yavin, the craft skipped its most recent inspection of its computer and flight control systems. During the Battle of Yavin, Porkins made several successful strafing attacks on the Death Star with Red 6. During the battle, Red 6 was hit by debris causing a cascade of malfunctions in the fighter's computer and flight control systems. The systems returned incorrect information, leaving Porkins unable to judge his altitude.
