Tracy Duncan
983

Tracy Duncan, also known as Dark Spork, Dunc or Snarkel, has been active in online fandom since 1995. Her vocal presence in the online fan community led her to be tuckerized as Dunc T'racen in Tish Pahl and Chris Cassidy's short story "Hutt and Seek." She is the webmaster of Club Jade and a former moderator on StarWars.com forums. She also runs the Livejournal communities "" and "."
