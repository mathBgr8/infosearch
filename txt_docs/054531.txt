Phoedris Bos
1410

Phoedris Bos was a male Nalrithian sympathizer who served the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Early in his life, his eggmate Cypher Bos killed his weaker siblings but avoided Phoedris because he was stronger then the others.
