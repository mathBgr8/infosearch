Bredis
1989

Bredis was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle owned and operated by Ripoblus forces during the Sepan Civil War. Imperial forces under the command of Admiral Harkov encountered Bredis as Dimok starships staged a raid on a Ripoblus supply convoy that was secretly keeping Imperial munitions that were ironically sold by Harkov for profit. Bredis aided Imperial starfighters in pushing back the Dimok attackers but the shuttle was likely destroyed by the Dimok forces, if the Imperial fighters didn't destroy it after discovering the illegal shipment and decided to seize it. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Lambda-class T-4a shuttles Category:Starships of the Ripoblus government
