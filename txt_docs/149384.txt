Skutch was present when Reen's group freed important Rebel captives Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa from the clutches of Imperial Captain Ryko Vant&mdash;it was Skutch who later discovered that Vant had left a tracking device on Skywalker's droid R2-D2, leading to conflict between the Rebels and Reen's guerrillas. Skutch was later killed with the rest of Reen's group, attempting to blow open a bunker supposedly holding a relic known as the "Eternity Crystal."
