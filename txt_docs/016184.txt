It was a backwater in the Freeworlds Territory. It had a liberal government that did little to threaten smugglers and other criminals. Sheva had one barren moon, the base of the Alabard's Comets' listening post. Shortly after the Battle of Hoth, the base was discovered by Jinjur Tomas in the Shindra Lady.
