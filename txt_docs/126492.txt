Bodwae
1192

Bodwae was a male Laerdocian who worked as a sensor officer on the Errant Venture as of 19 ABY. He spoke with his species' characteristic accent, inserting J's before I's so that, for example, "ship going to get hit" became "shjip gojing to get hjit."
