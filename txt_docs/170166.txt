Abek's Station
1955

thumb|A ship docks at Abek's Station Abek's Station was a Shadowport owned by the Nikto pirate Nim Abek. It resided on the Sisar Run just before the Ac'fren Spur, though it could not be found on any maps. The only method of finding it was by invitation. The male Near-Human Blalock was in charge of security on the shadowport.
