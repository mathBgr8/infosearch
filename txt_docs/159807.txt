Charbi City Spaceport
1091

Charbi City Spaceport was a spaceport on Vulpter. It was the location of the meeting between Cal Omas and Dur Gejjen. Gejjen was later assassinated by Ben Skywalker on behalf of the Galactic Alliance Guard. Captain Lon Shevu and Lieutenant Jori Lekauf also spyed on the meeting, and the information they captured allowed Jacen Solo to arrest Omas. After the assassination, everyone inside the spaceport was searched. This lead to a showdown between Lekauf and the police outside the spaceport, where Lekauf killed himself by destroying his shuttle. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Spaceports Category:Vulpter locations
