Gorimn wine
601

Gorimn wine was a kind of alcoholic beverage made and ingested by Wookiees. Han Solo consumed gorimn wine and graling juice on Chewbacca and Mallatobuck's wedding day. Wookiees thought it mild, though it was very strong to Humans. *Rebel Dawn *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Wines Category:Wookiee food
