A blue-skinned Tefaun, Petikkin served as a persuasive recruiter for the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. He used his contacts in the crime lord Jabba the Hutt's desert palace on the planet Tatooine to find support for the Alliance. Soth Petikkin possessed limited precognition, and also had a level of attunement to the Force. Soth Petikkin's origins date back to the 1983 film Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi production stages. A number of alien maquettes were designed for possible inclusion in scenes taking place in Jabba's Palace.
