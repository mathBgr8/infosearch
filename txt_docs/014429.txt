The planet's lush forests were home a wide variety of plant life, and also to a unique species of gundark known as the long-necked gundark.The Essential Guide to Alien Species Princess Leia Organa and C-3PO scouted the planet for possible new location for the Rebel base after the Battle of Hoth, but they they quickly fled after coming under attacked by long-necked gundarks. Kharzet III first appeared in Star Wars 51: Resurrection of Evil, written by Archie Goodwin and released in 1981.
