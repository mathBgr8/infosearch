Xucphra Meander
1121

The Xucphra Meander was one of three bacta tankers that was hijacked by the former members of Rogue Squadron during the Ambush of the bacta tankers, as part of their strategy of deposing the former head of Imperial Intelligence, Ysanne Isard, from her rule of Thyferra. Belonging to a small convoy escorted by the Victory II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter, after having surrendered, the ship agreed to carry its bacta to Coruscant. During the trip it was escorted by Rhysati Ynr, Inyri Forge and two Twi'lek-piloted Chir'daki starfighters. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bacta Tankers
