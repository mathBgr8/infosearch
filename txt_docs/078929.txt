Hardpoint Squadron
1719

Hardpoint Squadron was a starfighter squadron that was entirely composed of Jedi pilots flying XJ6 X-wings. The unit was in existence as of 40 ABY. Including Luke Skywalker, Mara Jade Skywalker and Toile Senn, Hardpoint Squadron was assigned as "Team Womprat" during their role in Operation Roundabout. The squadron engaged Corellian attack fighters, leading to the death of Toile Senn, and escorted Jaina Solo's teams off Corellia. It later was a part of the Tralus Ground Occupation Forces, under the command of Solo and Zekk. It participated in the Battle of Tralus.
