|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population=70 (originally 4,000) |demonym= |affiliation= }} Mindori was a city on Kallistas during the time of the Empire. A mining town centered around some pits, it was the second oldest settlement on the world, once with over 4,000 inhabitants, but reduced to some 70 of them during the rule of Prefect Dengless Rinn. Although nominally under Imperial control, the people were unhappy with Rinn and admired the local pirate, Jondrell Inx. Category:Cities Category:Kallistas locations
