Pentastar Talks
1503

The Pentastar Talks were a series of meetings held on the Reaper in 4 ABY between Moff Ardus Kaine and various political and business leaders from the Velcar Free Commerce Zone. Elta Besk, of the Dynamic Automata, Ib Dekeet, an Imperial Governor, Gregor Raquoran, the Commerce Master Commissioner of the Velcar Free Commerce Zone, and Wyrn Otro of Galentro Heavy Works were all present in the talks. They resulted in the signing of the Pentastar Alignment Treaty and the establishment of the Pentastar Alignment. *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Events of the Galactic Civil War Category:Meetings Category:Pentastar Alignment
