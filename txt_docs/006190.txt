This planet was attacked during the Caamas Document Crisis. Sif'kric was home to the Sif'kries, a people who were constantly feuding with the Frezhlix who shared the Sif'kric system with them. Sif'kric was one of the outer worlds in the Sif'kric system. An inner world was a hot-world where pommwomm plants were grown and exported to Sif'kric annually. These plants were the basis of the Sif'kric economy. During the Caamas Document Crisis, in 19 ABY, the Frezhlix blockaded the planet Sif'kric. The Frezhlix justification was the Sif'krie's opposition to the Drashtine Initiative in their sector assembly, demanding that the Bothans pay reparations for the destruction of Caamas.
