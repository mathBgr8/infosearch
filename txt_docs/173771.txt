|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=137 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=*Star Wars: Legacy *Star Wars: Vector |issue=30 }} Legacy 30 is the thirtieth issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. It is the third issue of Legacy to feature the Star Wars: Vector cross-over arc and is the eleventh issue in the over-all Vector story arc. Celeste Morne, a Jedi of the Old Republic, and Cade Skywalker, descendant of the legendary Skywalkers, have joined with a group of Imperial Knights and Cade's bounty hunter crew for an assassination attempt on the Sith Emperor, Darth Krayt. But Celeste has been struggling with the spirit of Sith Lord Karness Muur for over 4,000 years&mdash;there is no way to determine how much control he maintains over her actions. Can Cade's plot succeed, or will he find himself at the mercy of another Sith?
