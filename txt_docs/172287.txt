|establishedby= |dateestablished= |datesuspended= |datereestablished= |dateabolished= |government= |organization=Oviedo Engineering |type= |term= |powers= |duties= }} Director General was a title used by Oviedo Engineering, a corporation that manufactured military equipment. Around 21 BBY, is set approximately a year after the events of and therefore around 21 BBY per The New Essential Chronology. Lorca Oviedo, the director of Oviedo Engineering and a member of the Confederacy of Independent Systems, held the title. After Galactic Republic clone commandos discovered Oviedo's allegiance to the Confederacy on the Expansion Region &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Asturias system moon Asturias, they brought him to the Republic's Core Worlds &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Coruscant system capital planet Coruscant for questioning, where he died of respiratory failure. The title of Director General appeared in the flashbacks of the comic story "Honor Bound," which was published by Dark Horse Comics on February 23, 2005 as part of the twenty-second issue of the Star Wars Tales comic-book series. The story was written by Ian Edginton and illustrated by Steve Pugh. In the real world, a Director General is the chief executive or administrator of an organization. Category:Titles
