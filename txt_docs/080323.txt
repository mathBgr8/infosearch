Demmings
2537

Demmings was a Human male who served as a commander in the Imperial Navy of the Galactic Empire, as well as an intermediary between the Empire and the Tiss'sharl League on Tiss'sharl. Following the Battle of Yavin, Commander Demmings was serving in the Outer Rim Territories, primarily as an adjutant to the Supreme Commander, Darth Vader, and as captain of the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Reprisal, the current command ship of his superior. In this capacity, he played a prominent role in the hunt for the elusive Rebel Fleet , and the final pacification of Jabiim. Commander Demmings accompanied Lord Vader on his third visit to the Tiss'shar on Tiss'sharl.
