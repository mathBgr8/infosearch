Patrol Plaza
1250

Patrol Plaza was a square on the planet Taris. In 3964 BBY, Taris Holofeed interviewed Constable Sowrs of the Taris Civil Authority about the role of the Jedi in law enforcement on Taris at Patrol Plaza. Patrol Plaza first mentioned in the "The Taris Holofeed: Prime Edition," an in-universe issue of the Taris Holofeed written by John Jackson Miller that introduced the first arc of the Knights of the Old Republic comic series. The mention of Patrol Plaza in the article had made some fans consider that the square was the location of the Jedi Tower but this over-interpretation had been denied by John Jackson Miller.
