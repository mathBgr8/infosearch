thumb|right|150px|[[Kar Vastor was said to be a member of Ghôsh Windu]] In Korunnai society, a Ghôsh was essentially a tribe or family. One such example was Ghôsh Windu, the tribe to which Mace Windu belonged. Another was Ghôsh Rostu. Members of the same ghôsh were referred to as Dôshalo.
