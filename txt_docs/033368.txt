Chancellery Walkway
665

}}The Chancellery Walkway was a street in the Jrade District of Coruscant. It was lined with the monuments and memorials constructed in remembrance of the most influential Supreme Chancellors. It was the location of the Finis Valorum Memorial. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant roadways
