Ferrocongregate was an explosive material used in core bombs. When combined with magnopium, ferrocongregate was capable of creating a heavy blast with a wide radius. *Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Compounds
