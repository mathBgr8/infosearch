Gates of Grontessiant
1108

thumb|250px|Wat Tambor opens the Gates of Grontessiant. The Gates of Grontessiant were a mythical alternate realm spoken of in the Book of the Boolmide, the holy text of the Skakoan religion. It could only be opened when the Elders read the Book on the Power Mounds of the Elders when the chosen individual was present. The Gates were the key to the mythical Eye of the Albino Cyclops. In 19 BBY, the Gates opened for Wat Tambor, permitting him to enter into the Threshold of the Hidden Realms. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mythological places Category:Religions Category:Skakoan religion
