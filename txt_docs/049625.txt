Shi'idese
572

Shi'idese was the native language of the Shi'ido species from Lao-mon. Shi'idos venturing from their homeworld became quickly fluent in Basic. Some xenobiologists were still discussing if there was a written form of Shi'idese as of 0 BBY. It was clear that Shi'ido easily learned to write and read other languages. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
