Fiery One
1024

Fiery Ones were mysterious sentient photonic beings indigenous to Kathol. Unlike most of the planet's inhabitants, they were apparently not creations of the DarkStryder. In appearance, they looked like balls of light. Communicating through incredibly powerful telepathy, they could use the Ta-Ree energy that came from the planet, and could use it as a weapon if needed. *Endgame *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Kathol sector sentient species
