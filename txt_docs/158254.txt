Sorbiss Valley
1128

The Sorbiss Valley was located on the planet Kal'Shebbol in the Kathol sector. During Moff Kentor Sarne's reign as ruler, he built a secret hangar in the valley, and berthed the CR90 corvette Renegade there. He used the hangar facilities to heavily modify the Renegade in preparation for a foray into the Kathol sector to retrieve the DarkStryder technology. In 8 ABY, when the planet was liberated by the New Republic, the location of the corvette and valley was divulged to Lieutenant Judder Page by Jessa Dajus.
