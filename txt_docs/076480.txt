Gardulla Besadii the Younger
1207

Gardulla Besadii the Younger was the son of Gardulla Besadii the Elder and the brother of Decca Besadii Diori. He was a Hutt advisor to Borga Besadii Diori during the Yuuzhan Vong War, who suggested giving information of the Vong to the New Republic. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Besadii kajidic Category:Hutts Category:Males Category:Yuuzhan Vong collaborators
