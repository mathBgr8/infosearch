She stole the Kashi Mer talisman, an heirloom of the Kashi Mer Dynasty that was rumored to possess a dark power. The talisman was a dark gray stone in the shape of a prism. However, several months after stealing it, Jalooz became convinced her actions were wrong, and personally returned to Kashi to beg for forgiveness.
