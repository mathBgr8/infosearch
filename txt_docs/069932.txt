Rimrider
1283

The Rimrider was an Imperial scout ship stolen by Halagad Ventor in order to escape Darth Vader. He flew the ship to Trinta, and there it stayed permanently in the swamps. *Domain of Evil *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Scout starships Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
