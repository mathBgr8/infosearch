Herglese
2194

Herglese or Herglic was the language of the Herglics from the planet Giju. Most Herglics also learned to speak Basic. The language had a written form, which was also known as Herglese. Due to the influence of Herglic trading within the Tapani sector several Herglic words were incorporated into the local vocabulary. *chakk - a naïve person. Literally a young Herglic that was not weaned from its mother *hauum - sound of a Herglic clearing his blowhole.
