Cleft Head
1186

The Cleft Head wake-up house was a bar located in the immigrant section of ChikatLik on Ord Cestus. It served a thousand legal stimulants from a hundred worlds, such as Tatooine H'Kak bean tea. The Cleft Head's clientele consisted predominantly of office workers seeking to enhance their stamina. It was speculated that within the bar access to illegal substances was also easily arranged.
