|designer= |homeworld= |line= |model= |class=Battle droid |degree=Class four droid |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |sensor= |plating= |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=Galactic Empire |firstmade= |retired= }} The AP-1-C attack droid (Armored Platform, model 1, command) was a battle droid used by the Imperial Remnant. It was created by Arakyd, makers of the Arakyd Viper. The AP-1-C was armed with a repeating blaster, but more commonly was used, as its name suggests, as a commander for its ally AP-2 and AP-3 attack droids. The attack droid saw combat during the Battle of Cadinth. The "Imperial Attack Droid B" model was designed for the abandoned The Epic Continues story line. The prototype model was made by kitbashing 2-1B and 4-LOM figures, and was given Zuckuss' gun. The droid later appeared in the Legends continuity where it was officially named the AP-1-C attack droid.
