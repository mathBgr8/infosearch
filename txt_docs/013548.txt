Talfaglio
2212

Talfaglio was a reddish-orange planet in the Corellian sector. It was located in Talfaglio system, the least-populated section of the Corellian sector, this planet was where Human League agent Pharnis Gleasry was able to meet Mara Jade and give her a message cube from Thrackan Sal-Solo for Princess Leia.
