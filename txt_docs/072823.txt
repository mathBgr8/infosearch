Rath Kelkko
2118

Rath Kelkko was an Anzati assassin who was recruited to train and lead the uprising army on Saleucami of Morgukai. Sora Bulq hired him and his fellow Anzati assassins to train a "shadow army" for the purposes and wills of Count Dooku. His final hour was to be used to assassinate Jedi General Oppo Rancisis. When he was the last assassin of his team he charged the Jedi in a vain attempt. He was Force pushed into an energy barrier, thus causing his death.
