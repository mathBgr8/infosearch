Trever Flume's father
2243

Trever Flume's father was the father of Trever Flume. He was killed right after the Clone Wars ended. A doctor during the war, he lived on Bellassa and worked in the city's defense department. However, Imperial troops arrived after the end of the war in 19 BBY, wanting to take over the defense system.
