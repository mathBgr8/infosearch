Beidlo (Tatooine)
1684

Beidlo was a young boy who grew up with his parents on Tatooine. When his parents were killed by Tusken Raiders, he joined the B'omarr monks and they took care of him. One day, Mammon Hoole came to Jabba's Palace to research the B'omarr monks with Tash and Zak Arranda. Beidlo informed Tash and Zak about the B'omarr monk's culture. At one point during Hoole's and the Arranda children's long visit, Beidlo befriended Tash and Zak, and he got caught in the middle of one of Jabba's wicked plots. His brain was placed in a monk brain spider and his body was used by one of Jabba's minions.
