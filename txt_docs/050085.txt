Tyrannic
3235

The Tyrannic was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer of the Imperial Starfleet, as part of the Braxant Sector Fleet. Under the command of Captain Nalgol, Tyrannic was brought into the forces of Moff Vilim Disra and Major Grodin Tierce after Nalgol was convinced of Flim's performance as Grand Admiral Thrawn. In that capacity, Tyrannic was outfitted with a cloaking shield and took the lead in the assault on Bothawui.
