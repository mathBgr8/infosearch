Battle Platform
790

Battle Platforms were military-grade vehicles used by the Second Imperium in 23 ABY that could be used as both landing craft and aerial assault vehicles. It resembled a large, flattened sail barge with rounded corners. A battle platform had two levels with an upper command deck that would open automatically when they reached the atmosphere.
