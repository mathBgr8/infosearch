Aldereenian
2494

The Aldereenians were a sentient species of insectoids. They were native to the Aldereen system, located in the Inner Rim Territories. By 8 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of The Courtship of Princess Leia, which includes the existence of the Aldereenian Consulate on the planet Coruscant, to 8 ABY. they maintained a consulate on the planet Coruscant. The consulate was located almost 200 kilometers from the Alderaanian consulate. On the day of the wedding of the Human couple Han Solo and Leia Organa, their best man, Luke Skywalker, was delivered to the Aldereenian consulate by an errant shuttle driver.
