Mika Dorin
2072

Mika Dorin was a Rodian male who worked as the manager of The Drunk Side, a cantina in Dreshdae on Korriban, in 3956 BBY during the Jedi Civil War. He, along with his partner Ziagrom, learned of the amnesiac Revan's true identity after the Ebon Hawk capture by the Leviathan. He offered Revan a look at his premium items and constantly referred to him as Lord Revan.
