|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood |issue=3 }} Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 3 was the third issue from the Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood series of comics, and was released on January 13, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. While Grappa's pirates turn up the heat on the Rebel cargo routes, the Interim Council sinks to unprecedented depths under the leadership of Xandel Carivus. Mirith Sinn finds herself the victim of the greatest deception of all when an old "comrade" finds her amidst Grappa's thugs. Meanwhile, where has Kir Kanos gotten himself to?
