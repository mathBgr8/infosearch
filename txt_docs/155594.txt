Prayer of Redemption
599

The Prayer of Redemption was a chant used by the Shamed Ones Yuuzhan Vong who were involved with the Jeedai heresy. This prayer was used to gain favor and as a means to better the circumstances of the Shamed Ones involved in the Jedi Heresy. It was formed on Coruscant after it was conquered by the Yuuzhan Vong empire and gained a high amount of popularity when the prophet Yu'shaa came into power. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy Category:Religious songs Category:True Way
