Paffen
635

Paffen was an illusion of an Imperial officer used by Akanah to further trick Jedi Master Luke Skywalker. Luke attacked the illusionary Imperial and cut his arm off. After Luke and Akanah left Ialtra the body of Paffen dissipated into nothingness. Paffen was created by the Dopplegänger Force power or a similar technique. *Before the Storm *Shield of Lies Category:Fallanassi powers
