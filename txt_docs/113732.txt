|other date= |media type=Hardcover |pages=56 |isbn=1593077629 |isbn2= |timeline=1000 BBY |series=Star Wars: Jedi vs. Sith/30th Anniversary Collection |issues=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Volume 2: Jedi vs. Sith is the second of the Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection TPBs, released on April 4, 2007. It is a hardcover version of Star Wars: Jedi vs. Sith. Volume 2 gathers the epic Jedi Vs. Sith, by Darko Macan, Ramón F. Bachs, Raul Fernandez, Chris Blythe and Andrew Robinson. A thousand years before Luke Skywalker embarked on his fateful confrontation with the Death Star, the Jedi Army of Light met the Sith Army on the planet Ruusan for a climactic showdown that would forever change the galaxy. Read about the rise of Darth Bane, and witness the terrible war on Ruusan from the eyes of orphans caught between enemy lines.
