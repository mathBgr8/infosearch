Clawcraft Spin
1075

The Clawcraft Spin was an evasive combat tactic that was utilized by pilots of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Force. The pilot of a clawcraft would throw his or her craft into a wild, erratic spin that was centered around the ball cockpit, constantly firing and moving unpredictably. It was popular among clawcraft pilots for use in space, but was considered incredibly dangerous to use within an atmosphere.
