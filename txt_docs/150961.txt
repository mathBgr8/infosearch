Wartaki clay pipe
696

The Wartaki clay pipe was a smoking implement favored by the Wookiees,Alliance Intelligence Reports and made with clay from the Wartaki Islands on the planet Kashyyyk. A Wookiee gave a Wartaki clay pipe to Professor Callow Batta as a present. Batta used the pipe to smoke wroshyr cavendish. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Smoking
