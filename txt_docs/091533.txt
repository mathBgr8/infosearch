Nanilas
553

Nanilas was the most widespread of the several Rellarin root languages. The eight languages of the Riha Archipelago were derived from Nanilas, though it is not clear if it was spoken on its own as a living language. As there were at least 57 Rellarin cultural groups, and each had their own language, most Rellarin were multilingual, speaking their own languages and those of neighboring tribes. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Languages
