BABYgnon was first mentioned as "Babygnon" in the Star Wars Roleplaying Game sourcebook Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds, which was published by Wizards of the Coast in 2004. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas, authored by Daniel Wallace and Jason Fry, placed the Dathomir system, and therefore BABYgnon, in grid square O-6.
