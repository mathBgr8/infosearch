The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 10 is the tenth issue of The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine, later rebranded as the Star Wars Insider. Issue 10 was published in Winter 1990. *"The Force to Return in the '90s!" *"Villains of the Last Crusade" by Dan Madsen *"Star Tours: A Grand Opening" by Adam Schultz *"Inside ILM: Back to the Future Part II" by John S.
