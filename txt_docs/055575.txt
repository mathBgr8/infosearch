Dillian Pressor
2253

Dillian Pressor was one of the crewmen of Outbound Flight. His wife, their daughter Katarin, and their son Jorad accompanied him on the flight. He survived the crash of Outbound Flight on the planet known to the Chiss as the Redoubt, an event he was able to foretell due to his latent Force-sensitivity. Even moments after the crash it attracted the suspicion of Chas Uliar.
