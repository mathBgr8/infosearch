CRM908A
1428

The Pakuuni Pirates used Container CRM908A as a storage container during the Galactic Civil War. As part of an expansion project, Imperial forces, left by Grand Admiral Thrawn raided a Pakuuni Pirate outpost during the Battle on the Frontier. Imperial starfighters destroyed as many Pakuuni vessels as possible, along with any cargo vessels in the area, including containers from group CRM908A. Maarek Stele, present at the battle, inspected the cargo containers prior to their destruction, locating Rebels onboard.
