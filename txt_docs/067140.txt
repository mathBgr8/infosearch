Stalking onsonker
1328

A stalking onsonker was (allegedly) a species of ambulatory, plantlike carnivore. Vilmarh Grahrk had a pet of the species, named "Fluffy." Grahrk claimed he had infected Bobo with Fluffy's spores. According to Vilmarh Grahrk, the babies would grow out of Bobo's stomach. Fluffy sniffed out Bobo by the scent of her young, and, to brood the babies within her, ate Bobo. Many beings had never heard of such a beast and doubted the species existed.
