Cattarash mushroom
748

The cattarash mushroom grew on the planet Gandle Ott. They were thin lime-green fungi that stood over one meter tall, and could be brewed to make a tea. They were allowed to grow in huge groves in Montrol City. Every year on Gandle Ott, several cattarash mushrooms were cut down as part of the Ceremonial Mushroom Cutting.
