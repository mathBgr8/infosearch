Hideouts & Strongholds
24298

Hideouts & Strongholds is a paperback supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. It was written by Sterling Hershey and published in 1998. The book is a detailed look at twenty different bases; each is presented as a specific example of a general type, allowing the gamemaster to adjust them as needed for their campaign.
