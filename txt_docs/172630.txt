Mallet Squadron
905

Mallet Squadron was an Airam starfighter squadron made up of R-41 Starchasers. It was attached to the Airam dreadnaught Anat. When its mothership engaged Imperial forces near the Blair Cluster, it participated in the battle, being tasked with driving off Interdictor Compellor, and was wiped out by Avenger and Gamma squadrons. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Starfighter squadrons of the Airam
