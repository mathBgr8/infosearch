Star Wars 2: World of Fire
1407

Star Wars 2: World of Fire is the second of two Star Wars Marvel Illustrated Books reprinting stories first printed in the Marvel UK Star Wars Weekly comic. It reprints the World of Fire! story arc previously available only in Marvel UK's Star Wars Weekly 107&ndash;115. The story is reprinted in black and white. The story is split into three parts: #World of Fire! #The Word for World is Death! #The Guardian of Forever! Category:Marvel Illustrated Books
