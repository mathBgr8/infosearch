The Star Wars first draft
7871

The Star Wars first draft was the first revision to the rough draft, written by George Lucas in July of 1974. Most of the revision centered on names, and many of these changes were later rejected when George Lucas wrote the second draft, entitled Adventures of the Starkiller, Episode I: The Star Wars, in early 1975. *The names of about half the characters, organizations and locations changed throughout the script. *The sky of the Fourth Moon of Ogana changed from "bleak" to red. Lakes were present on that moon, some green. *The clouds of the Royal capital of Granicus have become yellow oxide instead of white oxide. *The second handmaiden, Alana, was omitted. Mina remained as a decoy princess at the Chathos Academy instead.
