Thrasher (drexl)
1006

Thrasher was Oron Kira's personal drexl which he took to Empress Teta in an attempt to rescue Ulic Qel-Droma. The Jedi tried to force Ulic onto Thrasher, but Nomi Sunrider forbid it. *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 6 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Named pets
