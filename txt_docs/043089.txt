Victory Fleet
1994

Victory Fleet was a unit of the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars, and the first unit to use Victory I-class Star Destroyers. It was launched in 20.1 BBY, six months ahead of schedule, as a result of the Separatist victory at the Foerost Siege and the launch of their Bulwark Fleet. Victory Fleet was divided into two task forces, one under the command of Captain Jan Dodonna and the other under the command of Captain Terrinald Screed.
