Bijo Hammax
2054

Lieutenant Bijo Hammax was a male Narvath New Republic Intelligence operative who fought during the Black Fleet Crisis. An experienced warrior, Hammax honed his skills in the Narvath resistance and in the last year of Rebellion era fighting alongside with members of Rebel Alliance during Galactic Civil War. His experience and abilities earned him command of an NRI assault team assigned to Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt. During the search for the Teljkon Vagabond, Hammax was responsible for penetrating and boarding the mysterious vessel, but after Lando Calrissian prematurely landed on the ship, Pakkpekatt canceled Hammax's mission.
