|death=July 24, 2003Glen Ridge, New Jersey |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Illustrator |companies=*Harvey Comics *Marvel Comics / Star Comics |website= }} Warren Kremer is an illustrator who has worked as artist/penciller on every issue of the Marvel Comics/Star Comics Ewoks comic book series. He also provided art for many other Star Comics series. Born in The Bronx, New York in 1921, Kremer worked for Harvey Comics for thirty-five years, during which he co-created the popular character Richie Rich in 1953, and updated the design of Casper the Friendly Ghost.
