Far Distance
602

Far Distance was the area on Zonama Sekot where clients were bonded with their prospective seed-ships. The Northern Sea was located there. After Nom Anor attacked Sekot with a biological weapon in 29 ABY, areas of the Far Distance melted and became islands. *Rogue Planet *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Zonama Sekot locations
