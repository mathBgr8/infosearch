Abolisher (escort carrier)
1799

The Abolisher was an Imperial Ton-Falk-class escort carrier that was part of Task Force Vengeance. The Abolisher was part of the group of reinforcements that were pulled out of hyperspace by the Rebel Modified Strike Cruiser Peregrine. In the ensuing battle, the ship was destroyed by the Rebels along with the rest of the reinforcing fleet. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Starships of Task Force Vengeance Category:Ton-Falk-class escort carriers
