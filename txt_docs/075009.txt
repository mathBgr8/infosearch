Groola's Place
980

Groola's Place was a large underground establishment on Corellia owned by Groola the Hutt. It was located in the city of Tyrena near the city spaceport and contained various levels including a bar, restaurant, casino, games and amusements, private halls, and Groola's Den. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Hutt locations Category:Tyrena locations
