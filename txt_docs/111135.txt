Para-wing glider
3579

thumb|right|200px|A paraglider in the high atmosphere. A para-wing glider or paraglider was a very light, single person, winged vehicle. It consisted of little more than a pair of wings and a repulsorlift engine, which could be attached to a pilot's back. These gliders were used by tourists on Cloud City. They were modified with a radar beacon, comp-controlled navigational equipment and emergency thrusters, making the gliders slower and less maneuverable than their unmodified counterpart but safer for tourists to use.Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition The downtrodden of Coruscant's lower regions arranged para-wing races that utilized the tractor fields of the city's garbage pits.
