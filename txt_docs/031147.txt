Clone heavy assault trooper
5089

The clone heavy assault troopers, also known as clone heavy troopers and anti-air clone troopers were heavy weapons specialists in the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. thumb|left|200px|Clone anti-air troopers on standby. The clone heavy assault troopers were the heavy weapon specialist specialized in using mobile emplacement weapons.Shatterpoint Clone heavy troopers often carried Merr-Sonn PLX-1 portable missile launchers, DC-15S, V-1 thermal detonators, HX2 antipersonnel mines and other ARC trooper equipment. The heavy troopers handled the anti-vehicle, anti-airborne, and anti-armor duties of the Grand Army of the Republic. The units were not as resilient as AT-TEs or LAATs but offered ground units some protection against vehicles and air attacks.Star Wars: Battlefront II A similar unit was the clone heavy gunner.Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith video game Some Commanders, such as Commander Bacara, were equipped with clone heavy trooper armor such as the cheek plating, and Kamas. Early during the Clone Wars, anti-air clone troopers fought alongside General Echuu Shen-Jon during the Decimator campaign. Captain Four-Nine commanded a heavy assault trooper platoon during the Battle of Haruun Kal. Later, clone heavy trooper of the 501st Legion fought on Mygeeto, Felucia, Kashyyyk, Utapau and during Operation: Knightfall. The clone heavy trooper eventually became the heavy weapon specialists of the Imperial shock troopers.
