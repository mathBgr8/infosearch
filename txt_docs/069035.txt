Ice Edge Flightknife
998

Ice Edge Flightknife was a unit of Blade-32 Beta superiority fighters in the Adumari Union's air force. It fought in the Battle of Adumar in 13 ABY, and played an important role in the combined Adumari/New Republic force's attack on the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Agonizer. In coordinated missile attacks with two other Blade-32 flightknives and a New Republic Y-wing squadron, the fighters of Blades of the Ice Edge managed to break through the Agonizer shields and caused substantial hull damage, forcing the Star Destroyer to retreat. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Adumari flightknives
