CX-5011 PeopleMover
2099

The CX-5011 PeopleMover was a personnel transport that was manufactured by Incom Corporation and utilized by the Galactic Empire. Measuring forty-two meters long, the ship carried one pilot, up to twenty troops, seventy-five metric tons of cargo, and one month's worth of consumables. It had a Class 2 hyperdrive as well as a backup of uncertain class.
