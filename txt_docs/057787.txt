Ganrite Yards of Elrood, Inc.

Ganrite Yards of Elrood, Inc. was an Elrood-based starship manufacturing corporation with offices located in the Elrood Bazaar. They primarily produced clones of other starshipwright's established designs, including the Bratilia light freighter. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Shipwrights
