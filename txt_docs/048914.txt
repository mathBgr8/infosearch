Louis Mellis
1037

Louis Mellis is a Scottish voice actor, who provided the voice of Darth Sion in the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords. Louis also works as a scriptwriter, having co-written the feature film Sexy Beast, and currently can be heard in the United Kingdom on BBC Radio 1 as a one of the main imaging voiceover talents. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, Darth Sion Category:Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords voice actors
