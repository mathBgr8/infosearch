M-4D0
1106

M-4D0, also known as Fourdee, was a golden protocol droid that served as a porter droid aboard the luxury liner Star of Empire. In 0 ABY, the Systems Infiltration Manager took over the liner and as a result many of the on-board droids attacked the passengers and crew. M-4D0 used a blaster to shatter a transparisteel viewport and was instantly sucked out. *Galaxy of Fear: The Doomsday Ship *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Protocol droids
