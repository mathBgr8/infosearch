Red Threxa
557

Red Threxa was a Suiraon who owned a boarding house in Gelgelar Free Port on the planet Gelgelar. He was a quiet keeper of the house who left his clients alone to do what they wanted, provided they paid their rent. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Business owners and executives Category:Males Category:Suiraons
