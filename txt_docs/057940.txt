Orfa Olfactory Entertainment, Ltd.
708

Orfa Olfactory Entertainment, Ltd. was a small company that manufactured portable olfactory masks, which provided wearers with a wide range of scents with the holofilms they watched. They were slightly popular during the Galactic Civil War, however they did not go far beyond that time. The company had established its headquarters on the planet of Kidron and had offices located in the Elrood Bazaar. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Entertainment companies
