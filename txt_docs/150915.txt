Fru Kalg
1160

Fru Kalg was a Quarren who served as an operative in the Rebel Alliance. Kalg was captured while on the shipyards of Dac sometime between 1 ABY and 2 ABY. Talas Piran of the Mytaranor Slaving Council was suspected of being the person responsible for Kalg's disappearance. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Agents of the Rebel Alliance Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Quarren
