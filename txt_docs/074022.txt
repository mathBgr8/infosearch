Totolaya
1089

Totolaya was a hostage taken by the Yevetha during the attack on the Kubaz colony Doornik-319. Along with the other hostages taken during the Yevethan Purge, Totolaya was forced to record a message begging the New Republic not to fire on Yevethan ships carrying prisoners. The Yevetha played the messages during the Battle of Doornik-319, causing hesitation from the New Republic ships and forcing them to withdraw. *Shield of Lies *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Kubaz
