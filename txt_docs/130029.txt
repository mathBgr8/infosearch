Indomitable (Majestic-class)
2783

The Indomitable was a Majestic-class heavy cruiser that served as command ship of Task Force Aster in the New Republic's Fifth Battle Group. It participated in the campaign against Zsinj. Years later, it fought at the Battle of Doornik-319,Shield of Lies and at the Battle of ILC-905 during the Black Fleet Crisis.Tyrant's Test Indomitable was directly commanded by Commodore Brand, the task force commander; Captain Theb Tobbra served as first officer, and Lieutenant Threld was another member of the bridge crew. The starfighters included the E-wings of the 16th Fighter Squadron and the K-wings of the 24th Bombardment Squadron. Indomitable was first identified as a Majestic-class heavy cruiser in the at author Michael P. Kube-McDowell's website. In the trilogy, she is described as a "star cruiser" on her first mention in Shield of Lies in 1996 and subsequently referred simply as a "cruiser". This information was later supported by The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia.
