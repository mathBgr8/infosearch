Unidentified Blenjeel shipwreck survivor
2539

This fair-skinned Human male with dirty-blond hair served on a merchant vessel in 14 ABY and survived a crash in the starship. The incident occurred when the vessel crossed paths with an Imperial Interdictor cruiser and was shot down on the desert planet Blenjeel. The Human Jedi Apprentice Jaden Korr was sent by the New Jedi Order to rescue the crew of the downed freighter. However, by the time he arrived, he found that the blond Human man was the lone survivor. Shortly before Korr could rescue him, the man was eaten by a native creature known as a sand burrower.
