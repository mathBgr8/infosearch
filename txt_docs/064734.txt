thumb|250px|The Starseeker in Docking Bay 10 of the [[Spaceport/Legends|spaceport on Lamuir IV.]] The Starseeker was a freighter used by the House Pelagia agent Pellas Agri to travel to Lamuir IV where he was to deliver information to an associate, Janna Pallask. The ship landed in Docking Bay 10 at a spaceport on the planet Lamuir IV. Category:Freighters Category:Starships of House Pelagia
