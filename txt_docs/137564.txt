Solo Project
1363

The Solo Project was the attempt, headed by Nom Anor, by the Yuuzhan Vong to break the will of Jacen Solo and convincing him into accepting the True Way. Theoretically, this would have allowed the Vong to hasten their conquest of the galaxy by opening the way for 'peaceful' conversion of the rest of the population. However, with the help of Vergere, Jacen not only thwarted the project's goals, but tricked his would-be masters into believing that he had accepted, only to then betray them by bonding with the World Brain of Yuuzhan'tar and using that bond to sabotage the Vongforming of Coruscant. *The New Jedi Order: Traitor *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
