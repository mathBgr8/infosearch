|pages= |upc= |timeline=c. 5.5 BBYStar Wars Omnibus: Droids |series=Star Wars: Droids (1995) |issue=3 }} Droids 3 is the third issue of Star Wars: Droids (1995), a Dark Horse Comics comic book series starring R2-D2 and C-3PO. It was published on June 13, 1995 by Dark Horse Comics. Trapped on a drone barge headed for a recycling center, See-Threepio is about to have his memory wiped clean! Artoo races to rescue his old pal, but will Threepio even recognize the plucky little droid by the time he arrives?
