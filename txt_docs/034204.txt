SHADIS Magazine 42
748

SHADIS Magazine 42 was a roleplaying magazine from 1996. It contained many roleplaying articles, including campaign settings for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called ""Well, When I Blew Up the Death Star...": 3 Alternative Campaigns" and was written by Steven Marsh. Category:Shadis
