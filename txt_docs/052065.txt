|media type=Paperback |pages=8 + 4 pages of stickers |isbn=0789439646 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Ultimate Star Wars: Episode I Sticker Book is an 8-page book filled with four additional pages of stickers of heroes, villains, spacecraft, vehicles, droids, and equipment from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. It was originally published by DK in 1999 and additionally offered as part of the Star Wars Episode I: Limited Edition Gift Pack. Learn about Star Wars: Episode I with this fun and informative sticker book. Choose the stickers to create your own book of Star Wars: Episode I. Easy-peel stickers can be used more than once. *; May 1999; DK Publishing, Inc.; US paperback Category:Legends Ultimate Sticker Collection books
