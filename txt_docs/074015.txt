Tomuon
1340

Tomuons were long-horned, woolly herd animals that were native to the planet Askaj. Their coats were turned into a highly prized fabric. Askajians also used their meat, milk, hooves, fat, and bones for food and other essential items. It was rumored that Palpatine's robes were made of tomuon wool.
