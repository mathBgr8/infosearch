Firebrand (lightsaber hilt)

|culture=Jedi |creators= |created= |modifier= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners= |locations= |cost= |value= |structure= |size= |material= |length= |width= |height= |weight= |protection= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=New Jedi Order }} The firebrand was a style of lightsaber hilt with a thin black body that was ribbed along the lower half to offer a stable grip. One end of the hilt was flared to store power cells with the activation switch situated on top. The other side featured a thin deflector ring to protect from energy backwash. By 14 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy to 14 ABY. the hilt was typical among Jedi trained by Luke Skywalker, founder of the New Jedi Order.
