Sensislug
958

Sensislugs were sluglike insects used by the Yuuzhan Vong for spying. Sensislugs released a chemical composition that was able to render most humanoids unconscious for a short period of time. This made them useful defensive measures, and the Yuuzhan Vong would also use a milder form of the sensilug's compound on prisoners that were less than grateful to be granted the "honor" of being sacrificed to the Yun'o.
