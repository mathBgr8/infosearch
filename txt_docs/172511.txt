Convoy LMC-55c
892

Convoy LMC-55c was a small fleet of ships owned by the Lant Mining Corporation. Its flagship was LMC Hauler One-Six and the convoy included a small Imperial Navy escort which protected the fleet. The Galactic Empire ships were destroyed, and the corporation convoy was captured by a squadron of Rebel Alliance Y-wings led by Riane. *Flashpoint! Brak Sector Category:Fleets
