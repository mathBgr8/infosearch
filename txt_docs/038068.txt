Hanging Moss Village
756

Hanging Moss Village was inhabited by cruel and vicious Ewoks on the forest moon of Endor. It was an isolated location and its inhabitants were known to torture the creatures they caught. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities on Endor
