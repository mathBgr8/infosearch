It allowed a Force-sensitive to harness the Force to shatter the Force powers another used or in order to convince others that they had been cut off from the Force itself.Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil As such, it served to diminish an enemy's Force resistanceStar Wars: The Old Republic and to negate a vast range of other Force abilities, including but not limited to Force speed, Force valor, Force barrier, Force fury, Inspire, Energy resistance, and even Battle meditation. When subjected to certain uses the victim might be unable to focus enough to touch the Force,The Courtship of Princess Leia or could not control objects bigger than molecules when affected by Dampen Force. However, it did not block use of the Force entirely and only had temporary effects, though sometimes lasting for some days. Advanced mastery of this technique was referred to as Force breach during the Old Sith Wars. During the Cold War, that name was instead applied to the ability to deal catastrophic damage against targets by rupturing their shatterpoints. Hethrir was a known user of this technique, using it to control the children he kidnapped for his Empire Reborn movement. He would often dampen the abilities of non-human "students" at critical moments to perpetuate the movement's humanocentric agenda. These unfortunates would be removed as "failures" and sold into slavery as a source of income. In Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic and its sequel, the power would be first unlocked as Force Suppression before being able to be upgraded to Force Breach at level 15, the difference being Force Breach was capable of removing higher-level versions of the same Force Powers. Mechanically, it acted as a sort of "buff removing" power, canceling active Force Powers that provided benefits to the target.
