Shenbit bonecrusher blow
1078

Shenbit bonecrusher blow was a Teräs Käsi move, named after the shenbit bonecrusher. During the Palawa Band tournament in 22 BBY, Teräs Käsi practitioner Dux Pike was killed by Phow Ji with a shenbit bonecrusher blow. Category:Teräs Käsi
