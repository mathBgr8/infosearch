Rim Merchant Einem
2375

The Rim Merchant Einem was a Trade Federation cargo hauler in the Trade Defense Force. The vessel served under Captain Mignay. After the failed Invasion of Naboo by the Federation, it was one of the ships transferred to the Republic Judicial Department, where it served as Commander Wilhuff Tarkin's flagship at the Second Battle of Zonama Sekot.
