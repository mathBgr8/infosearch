Primary Shaping
477

Primary Shaping was the educational system for the Shaper caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. In this system, crechelings would be instructed on the various aspects of the Shaping protocols. The dormitories of the young Shapers were guarded by Warriors. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy Category:Places of learning Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
