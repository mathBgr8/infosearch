|location=Gansett systemThe Essential Atlas |interest=Reptile house |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Gansett Zoo was a zoo in the Gansett system within the Mid Rim which notably contained a reptile house. At some point, the hunter Sam Heggs had visited the zoo's reptile house, which he was not particularly fond of. Around 3 ABY,Star Wars Omnibus: Early Victories dates "Tales from Mos Eisley: Heggs' Tale" to approximately three years after the Battle of Yavin, which equates to around 3 ABY per The New Essential Chronology. Heggs was present during a shootout at Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in the city Mos Eisley on the planet Tatooine. After the altercation had ended, Heggs left the cantina, exclaiming that it was worse than the Gansett Zoo's reptile house. The Gansett Zoo was mentioned in the comic "Tales from Mos Eisley: Heggs' Tale," written by Bruce Jones and published by Topps in the second issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine in January 1995.Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 2 indicates it was published in winter 1995, and as issues 3 and 7 were published in April 1995 and 1996 respectively, issue 2 must have been published in January 1995.
