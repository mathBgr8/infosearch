|habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Chestrashi were a sentient species originating from Wild Space's Chestrashus system. They were ruthless and made extensive use of biological warfare. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, the company Charendohl Exports, Limited manufactured the Chestrashi void spear, a weapon characteristic of the species. The void spear, which was available in extremely limited quantities, was a metal throwing spear with a tip containing thousands of microorganisms. Upon striking the target, the weapon's payload would attack their bone and central nervous systems.
