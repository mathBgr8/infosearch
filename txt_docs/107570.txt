Malagarr battle droid
1497

Malagarr battle droids were used as security droids on Yavin 4 to protect Sith treasure in the numerous Massassi temples. They had three arms, with each wielding a vibro-ax. In 0 ABY a Rebel soldier chanced upon several of them, and later, Chewbacca destroyed one. *Star Wars Missions 1: Assault on Yavin Four *Star Wars Missions 17: Darth Vader's Return Category:Battle droid models
