Opur
1676

Opur was male individual who served as security officer on the Wild Karrde in 26 ABY during Talon Karrde's mission to rescue the Jedi trainees from a Peace Brigade attack at the Jedi Praxeum on the moon Yavin 4. During the operation, Karrde ordered Opur to make sure the children were secured, a task which he hurried to complete.
