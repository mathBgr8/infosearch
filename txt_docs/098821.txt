Missile Attack Launcher
2570

The Missile Attack Launcher (MAL) was a repulsor artillery unit used by the Zann Consortium during the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|180px|A Missile Attack Launcher. It was capable of configuring into a long-range missile launcher; however, when it did this, it was rendered immobile. It was armed with three turreted concussion missile launchers and was capable of shooting them in rapid succession; however, this resulted in reduced range. When deployed, it would utilize its missile system for increased range attacks against enemy forces.
