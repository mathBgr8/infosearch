|author=Palpatine |editor= |illustrator= |artist= |performer= |publisher= |release date=5:3:13 GrS |media type=Book |pages= |published in= |genre=Political }} The Paths to Power was a political text written by Supreme Chancellor Palpatine shortly after his election to the office in 32 BBY. The book was released on 5:3:13 GrS (30 BBY) and topped the best-seller lists. The release of The Paths to Power was later included in a listing of Palpatine's triumphs included in the Inaugural Edition of HoloNet News, celebrating the Chancellor's appointment as Galactic Emperor. The text was considered a modern masterpiece in the time following Palpatine's death in 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology during which Imperial Admiral Gaen Drommel had it highly ranked on his reading list. The Paths to Power was first mentioned in the in-universe article, Republic HoloNet News Special Inaugural Edition 16:5:241, published in Star Wars Insider 84. The book was created by author Daniel Wallace, who later noted on his blog that it was but one example of Palpatine's career as a writer, as the Emperor had been established as the author of the Dark Side Compendium in the Star Wars: Dark Empire comic series.
