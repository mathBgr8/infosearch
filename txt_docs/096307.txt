Lus-ma
1117

Lus-ma was a form of lightsaber combat which Count Dooku taught General Grievous and his IG-100 MagnaGuards. Like Sokan, Lus-ma was not part of the classic combat forms of the Jedi, but its lightsaber moves and ideals are akin to those of Form III. Next to nothing is known about Lus-ma. When Grievous trained with his guards in Labyrinth of Evil, it was the only time Lus-ma was mentioned: that Dooku observed they used Lus-ma to answer a Soresu move. *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Lightsaber combat
