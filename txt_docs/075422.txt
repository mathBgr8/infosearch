Wrede, based on a screenplay and story by George Lucas. It adapts the film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, and it was first published in May 1999 by Scholastic,. The novel was collected in Star Wars: Collector's Edition (2005), Star Wars Boxed Set: Episodes I–VI (2007), and Star Wars: Prequel Trilogy (2014).
