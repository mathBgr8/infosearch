Antilles BioGen
1180

|products=L-hand 980 |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Antilles BioGen (ABG), according to the medical droid 2-1B, was the manufacturer of the L-hand 980, which was used as a prosthesis for Luke Skywalker after he lost his duel with Darth Vader. While the arm which goes with the hand was discontinued, the 980 continued being produced. ABG was first mentioned in the Dear 2-1B column of Star Wars Insider 51. Category:Medical companies
