|intl release= |pages=37 |issue=1 |featured= |series=The Official Star Wars Fact File }} The Official Star Wars Fact File 1 was the first issue of The Official Star Wars Fact File, released on December 27, 2001. The issue was authored by Darren Allanson, David Bailey, Chris Gardner and Iain Lowson.
