Liquid Galaxies
1133

Liquid Galaxies was a beverage which poured out of its bottle in thick droplets, each appearing to be a tiny galaxy of stars. In addition to its unique appearance, it was also supposed to taste great. Ace Azzameen once received a bottle of Liquid Galaxies after he and his surviving brother Emon and sister Aeron narrowly evaded an ambush orchestrated by his uncle, Antan Azzameen. The Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance data file containing the picture of "Liquid Galaxies" is named "SPICE.CBM," possibly indicating that the drink was meant to be an intoxicant, but the in-game description does not state this. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Beverages
