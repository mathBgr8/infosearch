Pleasure cruiser
1498

This pleasure cruiser was a civilian luxury craft piloted by Captain Quasar. On one voyage near the Merson asteroid belt during the final years of the Galactic Republic, a group of Merson pirates attacked the cruiser. Fortunately one of the passengers aboard was Obi-Wan Kenobi who used his skill to destroy the aggressors even with the modest firepower of the cruiser. *Star Wars (1977) 24 Category:Liner classes
