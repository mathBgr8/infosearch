Buster rocket
656

A buster rocket was a small high-explosive rocket designed for use with personal weapon systems. The weapon was made to pierce light vehicles and heavy droids. Buster rockets were known to have been used shortly after the Jedi Civil War by the bounty hunter Mira. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Wrist rockets
