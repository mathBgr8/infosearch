Valorous (MC80 Star Cruiser)
2346

The Valorous was a MC80 Liberty type Mon Calamari Star Cruiser in service to the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. The Valorous attempted to pick up cargo containers in the Esyram system while the Imperial Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser Warlover was attempting to destroy the cargo containers.
