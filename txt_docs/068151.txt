Sulfaro plant
716

The sulfaro plant was a plant native to Kirdo III. It was the chooba's favorite dish. The plant had a very meaty interior. Kitonaks often mimicked sulfaro plants. They would lie down and wait for a chooba to climb up to its head, looking for the plant's interior. The Kitonak would then swallow the chooba whole. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Plants of Kirdo III
