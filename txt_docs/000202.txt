Corellia StarDrive
3116

|subsid=Akin-DowerTales of the Jedi Companion |headquarters= |locations= |role=Starship manufacturer |products=*Coruscant-class heavy courier *Thranta-class corvette |founding=c. 5000 BBY |dissolved=c. 3000 BBY |affiliation= }} Corellia StarDrive was a successful, if short-lived, starship manufacturer. The company was founded by a former Corellian Engineering Corporation executive one millennia before the outbreak of the Great Sith War. Corellia StarDrive was well known for both its civilian and military vehicles; many warships of the Republic Navy were constructed by Corellia StarDrive much to the concertation of Corellian Engineering Corporation. Corellia StarDrive was the parent company of Akin-Dower. During the Galactic War, the Sith Empire invaded Corellia. In this time, Corellia StarDrive used its corporate finances to fund the rebellion against the Sith. As a result, the Empire destroyed their headquarters in order to deal a crippling blow on the company. Corellia StarDrive's supremacy did not last long; the whole of the company's senior design team was killed in a freak shuttle crash.
