Lanigiro
1943

The Lanigiro was YT-1300 transport used by the Rebel Alliance. She was carrying medical supplies to the Redemption while the Nemesis attacked the frigate. Upon completion of the transfer of the medical supplies, the Lanigiro stayed and backed up the Z-95 Headhunters against the Imperial fighters.
