Shela Jalahafi
1363

Shela Jalahafi was a dark-haired female Human and a Dark Side Adept who was part of a team entrusted by Emperor Palpatine to infiltrate spies and agents into the ranks of the Rebel Alliance. Her brother, Teles, always lacked her strength, but still felt that he was more powerful then her. They subverted the Rebel underground which rose up on Edan II until the Imperials attacked them and the Rebels. *Introductory Adventure Game *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dark side adepts Category:Females Category:Humans
