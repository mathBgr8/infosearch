Varat'k Snarap
591

Crafted by the Gree Varat'k and on the transport ship Rokak'k Baran, the Varat'k Snarap was not a blaster weapon, but rather manipulated gravity to fire raking force field blasts that could vaporize another starship. Category:Gree technology
