Kendrick Machill'Tr
692

Kendrick Machill'Tr was a lieutenant with New Republic Intelligence assigned as the Administrative Aide to General Airen Cracken. Lieutenant Machill'Tr was resposnible for gathering all the appropriate intelligence reports related to the Corellian Incident and the Human League before submitting them to General Cracken for review. *Cracken's Threat Dossier *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Machill'Tr, Kendrick
