New Plympto resistance (Imperial Period)
2032

The New Plympto resistance was the Nosaurian resistance against the forces of the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars and, following the Battle of New Plympto, the forces of the Galactic Empire took control of New Plympto. They held out for a month with the help of Jedi General Dass Jennir but were finally wiped out by the 501st Legion at the Battle of Half-Axe Pass.
