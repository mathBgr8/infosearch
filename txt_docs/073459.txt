Iliox Argunn
1110

Iliox Arguun was a male Anx, who worked at the University of Sanbra for a number of years as a Professor of Anatomy. From his knowledge of anatomy, physiology, and his experience with creatures, he was able to draw the skeleton of any living creature, without looking inside the body. When the New Order was instituted, Iliox became increasingly withdrawn and reserved. Category:Anx Category:Biologists Category:Educators Category:Males
