Insignia of the New Republic
2109

thumb|150px|Insignia of the New Republic The seal adopted by the New Republic Provisional Council as the Insignia of the New Republic was based upon the symbol of the Alliance that preceded it, the blue crest of the AllianceHeir to the Empire Sourcebook -which was in fact often featured in red. It was set within a circle of stars that represented the galactic community. The circle is trimmed in gold, symbolizing the right of the people to govern themselves.
