Manda-Roon Merchant Route
2005

The Manda-Roon Merchant Route was a hyperlane that ran from the world of Manda to the the planet of Roon, which was located within the region of the galaxy known as The Cloak of the Sith. The route was blazed by Mungo Baobab, and was one of only two routes that ran through that treacherous area of hyperspace, the other being the less known Death Wind Corridor. Although heavily traveled, the route was a challenge even for the most seasoned astronavigators and was marked by a series of nav-beacons attached to "lightstation" space stations to assist in navigating the route successfully.
