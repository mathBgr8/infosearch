Fizzy-Bip
1120

Fizzy-Bip was a beverage that existed in the galaxy. The drink was sold in bottles at Kwik-E-Mart on the planet Coruscant,Evasive Action: Prey and it was also served in cartons on the starship Reasonable Doubt. Fizzy-Bip first appeared in the 2004 novel, Yoda: Dark Rendezvous written by Sean Stewart. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous *Evasive Action: Recruitment *Evasive Action: Prey Category:Effervescent beverages
