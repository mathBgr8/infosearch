The Night Beast
6027

"The Night Beast" is a comic strip written by Archie Goodwin and illustrated by Al Williamson, published by the Los Angeles Times Syndicate from March to May 1982. It was later reprinted in Amazing Heroes magazine #24&ndash;26. It was republished and colorized by Dark Horse Comics, beginning on page 16 of Classic Star Wars 8, and completing in Classic Star Wars 9. It was later republished on StarWars.com's webstrips section of Hyperspace.
