Annually, the comet would find its path through the hazardous spaceway on its way to briefly orbit the system's sun. It moved in a way that resembled a ricochet. In 20 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of MedStar I: Battle Surgeons to 20 BBY. the protocol droid I-5YQ compared his travels throughout the galaxy to the motion of the Roon Comet, which by that time had become legendary. Explorer Mungo Baobab gained much notoriety from following the comet as a means of rediscovering the Manda-Roon Merchant Route.
