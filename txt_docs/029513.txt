AthleticGal

}} thumb|158px|Aquawear advert in the HoloNet News AthleticGal was a company that sold the bathing tog line Aquawear. At some point, an advertisement was put up by AthleticGal on the HoloNet News to promote Aquawear, including exclusive holos, model data, and species gallery. AthleticGal first appeared in the HoloNet News, an in-universe website originally published on February 28, 2002.Per , HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47 was released on March 14, 2002. As HoloNet News segments were published weekly, and was published two issues prior, it must have been released on February 28 of the same year. The company was featured through one of the interchangeable advertisements seen on the website. Category:Personal gear companies
