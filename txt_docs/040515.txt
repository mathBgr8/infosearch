Anx (language)
1021

Anx was the language of the Anx of Gravlex Med, referring either to the spoken or written form. The Anx spoken language was comprised of words spoken in low-frequency tones produced by the sinus cavities found in an Anx's large headcrest. These tones could be heard across great distances. *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Savage Spirits Category:Languages
