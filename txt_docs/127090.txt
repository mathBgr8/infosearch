Giant thranta
826

The giant thranta&mdash;not to be confused with the great thranta&mdash;was an oversized breed of common thranta, otherwise identical in appearance to the smaller variety. It survived the destruction of Alderaan, its homeworld, on planets such as Bespin. *Alien Anthology Category:Thranta species
