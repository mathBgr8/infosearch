She was the daughter of Heff, one of only three surviving members of the original colony ships to settle Tatooine in the early years of the Galactic Empire. Tebbi was a Rebel sympathizer, and when her father was killed by the bounty hunter Jodo Kast, who was hunting Adar Tallon, she shared what she knew with a group of Rebel agents who were also seeking Tallon. A couple of seasons later, Tebbi sold her father's shop, Heff's Souvenirs, to the Sullustan Moplin Jarron.Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Tebbi was described in Tatooine Manhunt as a "rabbit-like alien".
