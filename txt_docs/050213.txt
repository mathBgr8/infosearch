Jorak
2059

}} Jorak was a member of the Sanyassan Marauders from the forest moon of Endor. During the Galactic Civil War, he paid an individual 25 credits to eliminate a bloodseeker mite which was attacking the Sanyassan's livestock. After this, he again paid the individual 30 credits to eliminate a blurrg which had eaten stinking verminweed, causing it to go into a fury and knock over trees, homes, and even children. Finally, he paid the individual 40 credits to take out a mantigrue, which earned them his respect.
