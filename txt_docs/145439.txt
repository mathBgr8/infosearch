Emperor's Wisdom
668

Emperor's Wisdom was a Victory-class Star Destroyer of the fleet of the Ciutric Hegemony which participated in the Battle of Ciutric under the command of the self-proclaimed "Prince-Admiral" Delak Krennel. It was destroyed in the Battle of Ciutric by Garm bel Iblis. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Ciutric Hegemony Category:Victory-class Star Destroyers
