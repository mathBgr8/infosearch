Nescan Tal'yo
1487

}} Nescan Tal'yo was a wolf-like information broker. His bodyguard was Hermos. He killed a Rebel courier on Farwell Station and captured a holocube detailing the locations of the Rebellion's main bases. He planned to sell the cube to the Galactic Empire on Elerion to make himself rich, but his plan was foiled by Leia Organa and the Quetzal Sisters. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Information brokers Category:Males
