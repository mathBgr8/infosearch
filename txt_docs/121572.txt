Brian Schomburg
2799

thumb|right|250px|Cover illustration of [[Stock Ships by Brian Schomburg]] Brian Schomburg was an illustrator and graphic artist for West End Games who worked on many Star Wars books from 1994 to 1998. He has continued to produce many other works since then. He also provided graphic design for the Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Beginner Game from Fantasy Flight Games in 2012. *Alien Encounters (Interior Artist) *The Breath of Gelgelar *Classic Campaigns *Cracken's Rebel Operatives *Creatures of the Galaxy (Graphics) *The DarkStryder Campaign (Graphics/Interior Artist) *The Far Orbit Project (Cartography/Graphics) *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations (Graphics) *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies (Graphics) *Hideouts & Strongholds (Cover Design/Graphics) *Instant Adventures (Card Art/Cover Design/Graphics) *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook (Cover Design/Graphics) *The Last Command Sourcebook *Lords of the Expanse *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook (2nd Edition) *Rules of Engagement (Cover Design/Graphics/Interior Artist) *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook (Interior Artist) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 (Graphics) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 2 (Graphics) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 4 (Graphics) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 6 (Graphics) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 11 (Cover Design/Graphics) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 13 (Graphics) *Star Wars Adventure Journal 14 (Graphics/Interior Artist) *The Star Wars Planets Collection (Graphics) *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded (Artistic Design/Graphics) *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition (Interior Artist) *Stock Ships (Art Director/Cover Design/Graphics) *Tapani Sector Instant Adventures (Card Art) *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook (Graphics) *The Truce at Bakura (Interior Artist/Graphics) *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy (Cover Design/Graphics/Interior Artist) *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Beginner Game (Graphic Design) Category:Roleplaying game artists
