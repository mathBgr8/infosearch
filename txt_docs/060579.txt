Abolisher (Assault Frigate Mark II)
1783

The Abolisher was an Assault Frigate Mark II in the service of the Rebel Fleet during the Galactic Civil War. In the 2006 game: Star Wars: Empire at War and it's expansion, Forces of Corruption; the Abolisher is a common name for the Assault Frigate MkII.
