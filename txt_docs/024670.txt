Vaufthau Shipyards Ltd.
1321

Vaufthau Shipyards Ltd., in cooperation with Rendili StarDrive, was the manufacturer of the Invincible-class Dreadnaught Heavy Cruiser. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, the Vaufthau shipyards had been incorporated into the Corellian Engineering Corporation conglomerate. *Tempest Feud *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Shipyard companies
