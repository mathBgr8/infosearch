Jedi!
1025

Jedi! was a critically acclaimed play, written and performed by actor Palleus Chuff. The story featured Yoda (as portrayed by Chuff). In act three scene two, Yoda set his ship's hyperdrive to self-destruct before fleeing in an escape pod to elude pursuing Tholians. It was very popular and could be watched during the Clone Wars. By the time of the Mission to Vjun, Chuff had performed Jedi! 1,437 times.
