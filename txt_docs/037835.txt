Thrawn Simulator
775

The Thrawn Simulator was a program developed after the defeat of Grand Admiral Thrawn to test ship commanders on their tactics against one of the galaxy's greatest military geniuses. Admiral Nek Bwua'tu, a Bothan, was the first New Republic and then Galactic Alliance commander to consistently defeat the Thrawn Simulator in tests. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Simulator technology
