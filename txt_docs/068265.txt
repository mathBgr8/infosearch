Spetbecca
999

Spetbecca was a Wookiee who ran the Great Hoary Mastmot Trading Station on Toola shortly after the formation of the Galactic Empire. Spetbecca rescued a Human named Haaken Baranar after they were mauled by a snow demon. While Baranar recovered, Spetbecca was captured by bounty hunters.
