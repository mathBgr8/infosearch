Unidentified red pachydermoid species
2871

A red pachydermoid species of sentients lived on a planet in the Outer Rim, near the border of Galactic Republic space. They had short trunks which ended in white tendrils, and had a large tusk on each side of this trunk. The species also had large ears and three-fingered hands.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Flashpoint thumb|left|150px|The species fights against [[Mandalorian/Legends|Mandalorians.]] The species was represented in the Galactic Senate from at least the time of the Exar Kun War.Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War 3 Their homeworld was conquered by the Mandalorians during the Mandalorian Wars, before the Battle of Vanquo. Afterwards, at least two members of the species joined the Mandalorian forces, and were present on Taris.Knights of the Old Republic 24 This species first appeared in the comic Tales of the Jedi: The Sith War, published in 1995, as a senator for an unnamed region. It was not seen again until Star Wars Knights of the Old Republic: Flashpoint, published in 2006.
