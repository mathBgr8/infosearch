Tactical Analysis Bureau
1059

The Tactical Analysis Bureau was a branch of New Republic Intelligence Service responsible for analyzing data and compiling reports for senior personnel and government representatives regarding the tactical capabilities of pro-Imperial, neutral, or friendly forces. Commodore Kursk Mal'ia was just one of multiple officers who was a part of this division. *Cracken's Threat Dossier *Galaxy of Intrigue Category:New Republic intelligence organizations
