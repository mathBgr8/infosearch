Star Wars (Marvel Illustrated Books)
1591

Star Wars is the first of two Star Wars Marvel Illustrated Books reprinting stories first printed in the Marvel UK weekly Star Wars comic. This edition reprinted and colorized three full stories from the UK comic. It also reprints the concluding part of The Kingdom of Ice, the beginning of which was printed in Pizzazz #10&ndash;16 before the magazine was discontinued. The four stories are: *Way of the Wookiee! *The Day after the Death Star! *The Weapons Master! *War on Ice! Category:Marvel Illustrated Books
