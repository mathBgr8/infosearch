Running Crimson Flightknife
642

Running Crimson Flightknife was a unit of Adumari Blade series starfighters from the planet Adumar. Personally commanded by General Wedge Antilles, along with his wingman Tycho Celchu, they led the assault on Cartann during the Adumari Civil War. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Adumari flightknives
