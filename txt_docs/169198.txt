Victor Jun
1341

Victor Jun was a male Human Force-sensitive who suffered from schizophrenia, and he thought the voices in his head were those of gods and obeyed what they told him to do&mdash;which occasionally involved murder&mdash;and in turn, the gods gave him glimpses into their power. In reality, these "glimpses" were Jun using the dark side of the Force. *Ultimate Adversaries *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Force-sensitives Category:Humans Category:Males
