M'yet Luure
1347

M'yet Luure was a male Exodeenian Senator of the New Republic who represented the planet Exodeen. He was a major supporter of the New Republic amnesty bill in the New Republic Senate. Luure was killed in 17 ABY during the Bombing of the Senate Hall; and was replaced as senator by R'yet Coome. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Exodeenians Category:Males Category:New Republic Senators
