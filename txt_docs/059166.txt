Bardictol was a Habassan Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport that carried the Habassan envoy to the Independence in order to attain a diplomatic meeting with the Rebel Alliance Due to their involvement in previous assistance operations, the Habassa asked the Bardictol to be escorted by Red Squadron. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports Category:Habassan starships
