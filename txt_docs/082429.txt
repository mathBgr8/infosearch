Star Destroyer White
882

Star Destroyer White was the name given to the pigmentation of paint that was applied to the hulls of Star Destroyers in Imperial service. It was the only color of paint that was produced in enough quantities to be reasonably-priced for applying to Imperial II-class Star Destroyers.
