Heritage Council
1171

The Heritage Council was a council of Hapan nobles led by the Duchas AlGray and Galney who allied themselves with the Five Worlds during the Second Galactic Civil War. Their goal was to ensure the independence of the Hapes Consortium from the Galactic Alliance by overthrowing Queen Mother Tenel Ka and assassinating Chume'da Allana. To this end they hired the bounty hunter Aurra Sing and attacked the Hapan Royal Navy with their fleet.
