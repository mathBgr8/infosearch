Homogoni
694

Homogoni was an expensive wood used to make tables. Like all things, its cells were bound together by the Force. In this wood's case, the cells were bound in long lines, which gave it its grain. During training session Jedi Knight Jaina Solo successfully practiced shatterpoint technique, shattering the wood. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Invincible *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hardwoods
