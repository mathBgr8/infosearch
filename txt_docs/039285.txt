He ran a small stand in the town, but dealt only with Jabba the Hutt and those who worked with the crime lord. He hated Valarian very much, and often hired people to slay her men. Jowir Ar'Lensa appeared as a non-player character in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
