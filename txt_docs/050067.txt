Ewoks (1985) 5
2783

Ewoks 5 is the fifth issue of Marvel's Ewoks series of comic books and was published under the Marvel Comics Star Comics imprint on October 1, 1985 with a cover date of January 1986. This story is split into three chapters: "The Terrible Machine," "The Discovery," and "The Battle."
