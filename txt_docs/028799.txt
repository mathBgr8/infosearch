Bok may refer to any of the following: *Bok Askol, a Pacithhip with an unusual genetic makeup *Bok Anbrin, an Utai jockey *Aidan Bok, the Jedi charged with guarding the ancient Jedi library on Nespis VIII. *Captain Bok, the Mon Calamari Rebel in command of the Grey Damsel *Bok, a Nikto member of the Morgukai warriors *Bok Askol, a Pacithhip boasting an unusual genetic makeup *bok, a creature native to Bothawui It is also a common misspelling of Boc, a Twi'lek Dark Jedi.
