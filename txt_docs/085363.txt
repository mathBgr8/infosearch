Dorgum
1290

The dorgum was a species of domesticated animal that was raised and utilized by the Alwari of Ansion. Gwurran were known to use the creature's name derisively. In 22 BBY, the bold Tooqui, before launching a personal campaign to rescue his captive Jedi friends from a Qulun camp, wished himself a piece of dorgum dung when confronted by a pair of snuffling kholot after he'd escaped down their burrow-hole from his pursuers. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook Category:Creatures of Ansion Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Domesticated creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
