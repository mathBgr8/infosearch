Deleta
1248

Deleta was a Human female participant of the Katharsis, the game founded by the failed Jedi Xanatos and the Telosian treasurer Vox Chun. As Telos fell to corruption the second time, Deleta was one of those that lost hope. When she heard about the BioCruiser, Deleta decided to be one of the first to leave the planet and find a new settled home.
