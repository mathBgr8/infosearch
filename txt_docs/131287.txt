Hapan Home Fleet
1163

The Hapan Home Fleet was a fleet that was part of the naval forces of the Hapes Consortium that guarded the capital of Hapes. During the Second Galactic Civil War, Jacen Solo implored Queen Mother Tenel Ka to allow him to use the Home Fleet against the Confederation, which she allowed on the condition that Jacen make peace with his uncle, Luke Skywalker. When Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo persuaded her that Jacen needed to be stopped, however, the Home Fleet was deployed against Jacen at the Battle of Kashyyyk. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *Legacy of the Force: Invincible *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Hapan fleets
