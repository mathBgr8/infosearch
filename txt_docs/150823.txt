The u'Tal'ac Range was a mountain range at Upper Bal'ta'ran Cluster, in Guiteica.Alliance Intelligence Reports Slave Aul Tarrus Vishav was imprisoned there before his intended execution, but he escaped, killing his guards and becoming a legend among the slaves of Bal'ta'ran mines. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Guiteica locations Category:Mountain ranges
