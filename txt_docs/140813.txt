Gavin Bocquet
2427

Gavin Bocquet first worked in the Star Wars saga when he served as a draftsman on Return of the Jedi. He later served as production designer for five more George Lucas productions: the 1992 television series The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles, the 1994 feature film Radioland Murders, and the Star Wars prequel trilogy films.
