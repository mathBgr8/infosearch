|sector=Veragi sector |system=Licha In system |coordinates=L-2 |xyz= |distance= |suns= |orbited= |position= |moons= |routes= |lengthday=24 standard hours |lengthyear=364 local days |class=Terrestrial |diameter= |atmosphere=Type I |climate=Temperate |gravity=Standard |terrain=*Island continents *Mountains *Plains *Forests |water=Moderate |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies=*Gree *Human *Various aliens |socialgroup= |language= |government=Local city-states |population=500 million |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation=Gree Enclave }} Licha In was a world of the Gree Enclave and the first to suffer from the degeneration of Gree society. This resulted in the reputation that this world's inhabitants were both wild and backward; an example of what happened to the Gree during their decline, an example at least seen by the Gree of other worlds. Licha In was a nexus for the Hypergate transportation mechanism allowing individuals to reach the farthest regions of the Enclave. As time went on and Gree society gradually became more advanced, the native Gree divided into many city states. Each competed for supremacy and wars were made for food, slaves and powerful devices left behind. The planet was a temperate world with large islands. The world itself rotated on its axis every 24 standard hours and its year lasted 364 local days.
