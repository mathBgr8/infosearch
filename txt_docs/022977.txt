|figures=60 |cards= |isbn=0-7869-4041-7 |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Champions of the Force was the fifth set of Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Miniatures game. It was released on June 6, 2006. Before its release it was often announced as the set containing the most lightsabers ever (25, beating Clone Strike's record of 22). The set includes figures from all eras and also introduces two new factions, Old Republic and Sith. The Yuuzhan Vong are not included in this set. The box artwork is by Chris Trevas.
