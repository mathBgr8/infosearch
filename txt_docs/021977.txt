Rodney Thompson
3297

Rodney Thompson is an RPG Designer for Wizards of the Coast. He managed production of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game books from 2007 to 2010, when Wizards discontinued their license. He was previously a freelancer, writing game books and online supplements. Rodney currently works as a Senior Designer at Bungie in Bellevue, Washington.
