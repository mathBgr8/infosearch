Star Wars Annual 1981
1642

Star Wars Annual 1981 was a hardcover comic book released in the United Kingdom that reprinted the stories from Star Wars (1977) 48, Star Wars (1977) 45 and Star Wars (1977) 47. The stories were first printed in the UK in The Empire Strikes Back Weekly 136-139, The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 140 and The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 142. *Star Wars (1977) 45 *Star Wars (1977) 47 *Star Wars (1977) 48 Category:Marvel UK specials and annuals
