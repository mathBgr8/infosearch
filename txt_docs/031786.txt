Sutu
1272

Sutu was a Human male stormtrooper who served in the Imperial Navy of the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, he was assigned aboard the Star Destroyer Imperator where he served as a scout in the platoon, led by Cadet Leader Kyle Katarn.
