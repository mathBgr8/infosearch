Rhees-Verk breathing
523

Rhees-Verk Breathing was the medical term used to describe a form of syncopated breathing rhythm that sometimes caused ventricular fibrillation in the heart of a being. It was especially dangerous for races like the Zabrak, which had multiple hearts that needed to operate synchronously. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Diseases and maladies
