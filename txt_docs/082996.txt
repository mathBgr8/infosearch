A rich mining world on the Kismaano Bypass, Kismaano had been part of the Kingdom of Cron and Xim's Empire, and by the era of the Galactic Empire it was a member of the Cronese Mandate. Throughout its history, it had always stood apart from the rest of Cron, which had never trusted the planet's merchants. A windswept world, Kismaano's oldest settlements were built into the sides of canyons and rifts.
