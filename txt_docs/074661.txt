XJ6 X-wing starfighter
1849

The XJ6 X-wing starfighter was a variant of the classic X-wing starfighter produced by Incom Corporation. In service with the Galactic Alliance Navy as of 40 ABY, it saw action in the Second Galactic Civil War.
