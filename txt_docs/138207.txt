Gwig
4331

Gwig was a young male Ewok who lived on the forest moon of Endor. He came of age and received his hood&mdash;together with Malani and Mookiee&mdash;at the Festival of Hoods. He was also amongst a group of young Ewoks and woklings to whom Chirpa told the story of Logray, Morag and the Sunstar. Gwig sought to join older Ewoks on their many adventures. He was named for a legendary scout from Ewok mythology.Galactic Campaign Guide thumb|left|Gwig plush toy Gwig first appeared as one of six 8-inch plush woklings made by Kenner as part of their Return of the Jedi toy line, and then as a figure in the Wicket the Ewok toy line from the company's Kenner Preschool division. Gwig's first appearance in fiction was as a background character with no spoken lines in the Ewoks television series. His name appears only on two of Nelvana's animation model cels, one of which shows him wearing a green bandana and the other a blue hood. The blue-hooded design matches the unnamed character in "The Cries of the Trees."
