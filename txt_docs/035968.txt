Republic freighter
1789

The Republic freighter was a planetary trade vessel employed by the Grand Army of the Republic. During the Clone Wars the Clone Army used these transports to trade with other factions. It was a small transportation ship that was mainly used to transport supplies and ammunition to ground troops.
