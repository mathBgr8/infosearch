Yammka's Mount
915

Yammka's Mount was the flagship of Warmaster Nas Choka during the final days of the Yuuzhan Vong War. The warship first saw action during the Defense of Mon Calamari, when the Yuuzhan Vong launched a massive attack on the Galactic Alliance capital.
