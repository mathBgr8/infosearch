Alliance Needler carbine
2098

The Alliance Needler carbine was a small carbine firing small sharp darts effective against personal shields. Used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War, it had a maximum range of 50 meters. Uff Wogo owned at least one of these before he was killed by "Project Durge", who then gave the weapon to a spacer working for Boba Fett.
