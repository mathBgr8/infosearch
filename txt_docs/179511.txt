Tom M. Christopher
1645

Tom M. Christopher (commonly abbreviated T.M. Christopher) was an audio engineer for Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, and later went on to edit episodes of the Special Editions of the original Star Wars trilogy, and the compilation films of the Star Wars Droids animated television series and Lucasfilm's Young Indiana Jones Chronicles. He began work with Lucasfilm Ltd. in 1982. Category:Star Wars original trilogy production staff
