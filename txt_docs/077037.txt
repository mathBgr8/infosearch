|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=c. 5.5 BBYStar Wars Omnibus: Droids |series=Star Wars: Droids (1994) |issue=2 }} Droids 2 is the second issue of Star Wars: Droids (1994), a Dark Horse comic book series starring R2-D2 and C-3PO. It was published in May 1994 by Dark Horse Comics. A gang of interplanetary thieves steal See-Threepio and Artoo-Detoo from the Pitareeze family, and take the droids to Kalarba's moon, Indobok. The droids meet a nefarious character from their past, and realize that their troubles have just begun!
