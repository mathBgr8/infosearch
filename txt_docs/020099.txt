Jizz-wail
1847

Jizz-wail was a music genre. A quartet performed some jizz-wail in a crowded square in the capital of the planet of Euceron during the Galactic Games of 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games to 27 BBY. During the Imperial Era, the spacer Gideon heard jizz-wailing in an establishment on the planet Centares, played on a red ball jett organ. Jizz-wail was first mentioned in the roleplaying game source article "Shape-shifters," written by Pablo Hidalgo for the twelfth issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine and published in February 1997. *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games Category:Music genres
