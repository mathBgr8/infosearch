Following the death of Emperor Palpatine at the Battle of Endor, the Imperial Military withdrew from the sector, retreating to Coruscant. Jewett turned to the New Republic for support, and managed to retain the services of several capital ships when their crew mutinied on learning about Palpatine's demise. Jewett managed to keep trade and communications lines open between the star systems in the sector, maintaining services and economical ties.
