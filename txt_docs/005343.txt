Silas Carson
3984

Silas Carson is an English actor who played Nute Gunray, Lott Dod, Ki-Adi-Mundi and Antidar Williams, the pilot who died at the beginning of Episode I. Outside of Star Wars, Carson also made a guest appearance on the UK series Hustle, Strike Back, The Bill and Spooks. He provided the voice of the Adherents of the Repeated Meme for the Doctor Who episode The End of the World and voices the alien race Ood in the episodes The Impossible Planet, Planet of the Ood, The End of Time and .
