Ryn (language)
1303

Ryn language was the native language of the Ryn species. It used tones and inflections and was easily described as melodious by anyone who heard it. Since childhood, Ryns were taught both Ryn language and Basic, to get more easily integrated into the galactic society, although they were usually only literate in their own language. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species *Scum and Villainy Category:Languages Category:Ryn culture
