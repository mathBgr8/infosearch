Harrid Sendo
1571

General Harrid Sendo was a Human male. He was a non-combat officer in the Destab Branch of Imperial Intelligence, and could access all kinds of information from where he worked He had dealings with Black Sun; each month Xizor would give him a 10,000 credit notes in a plastex envelope for informing Black Sun. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Platt's Smugglers Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Intelligence personnel of the Galactic Empire Category:Males
