Season scepter
1582

}} thumb|250px|The Snow King uses the season scepter. The season scepter was a magical staff that controlled the seasons of the Forest Moon of Endor. Four siblings&mdash;the Sun King, the Leaf Queen, the Snow King, and the Flower Queen&mdash;rotated possession of the scepter. The season scepter had immense power, including the ability to create snow and ice, change the moon's temperature, and propagate the growth of flowers.
