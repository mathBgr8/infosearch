Carmilla Vandangante
2151

Carmilla Vandangante was a Human female who was a Deputy Viceprex of the Rigahuerr Publishing Combine. She gave birth to her son and only child, Klaus Vandangante, on Coruscant. Carmilla was a widow and a doting mother leading her to raise her son in a luxurious, privileged manner. She would provide Klaus with a droid nanny as well as multiple private tutors. Her household was serviced by house cleaning droids and included a living room with a vase that was used as a stockpile for unwanted foods by a young Klaus. Carmilla also possessed natural qualities conducive to leadership roles, a trait which would later be passed on to her granddaughter Jessa. Carmilla Vadangante was first mentioned in Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook by Michael Allen Horne, a roleplaying guide published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
