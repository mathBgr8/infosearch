Phobium
2595

thumb|right|150px|Phobium Phobium was an metal alloy mined on the planets Gargon, Dor, and Bonadan.Star Wars Missions 19: Bounty on Bonadan It was first discovered many millennia before the Galactic Civil War for use in power cells. Jedi Knights would sometimes use phobium power cells and emitters for their lightsabers.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords During the Galactic War, between Galactic Republic and Sith Empire, phobium was used to build armors and weapons.Star Wars: The Old Republic During the early years of the Galactic Civil War, Emperor Palpatine stationed work crews on Gargon to mine the element.
