Pookie Baobab
1044

Pookie Baobab was a female Human who was the wife of renowned author, poet, and philologist Ebenn Q3 Baobab. Together on Manda, Pookie and Ebenn raised four children, who went on to have eighteen grandchildren. Pookie's pride and joy was their pet miniature bantha named Nuke. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Baobab family Category:Females Category:Humans
