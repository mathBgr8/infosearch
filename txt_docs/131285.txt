House AlGray
801

House AlGray was a powerful noble family of the Hapes Consortium. Based in the Relephon Moons, they controlled a dozen Battle Dragons. In 40 ABY, under Ducha AlGray, they allied with the Heritage Council. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hapan families
