Fencing emitter
604

A fencing emitter was a general type of lightsaber blade emitter used mainly for heightening the blade's usefulness when parrying enemy attacks. The result, however, is a blade that is less capable of deflecting blaster bolts. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Lightsaber emitters
