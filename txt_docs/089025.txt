True Guild
759

The True Guild was a faction of the Bounty Hunters' Guild during the Bounty Hunter Wars led by Gleed Ontondon. It was composed of the elders who had run the Guild and reaped the profits, igniting the resentment of the younger, bounty-earning members who formed the Guild Reform Committee. *Slave Ship *Hard Merchandise *Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Bounty Hunters' Guild
