Xucphra City
1763

Xucphra City was the site of Thyferra's primary starport and served as the planet's capital. Established as Zalxuc City at the height of the New Order to house the Human population of the planet, the residents were responsible almost solely for the production of bacta even though they numbered just 10,000. When Ysanne Isard took control of Xucphra and bacta production, many Zaltin families fled the planet. The city was then renamed Xucphra City.
