Orr Agg R'orr
1394

Orr Agg R'orr was a Tusken who served the Sand People and was known for sniping and disabling Teemto Pagalies' Podracer at the Boonta Eve Classic on Tatooine in 32 BBY. Later that year, bounty hunter Jango Fett hunted down and killed the Tusken Raider, who had a bounty of 3000 credits on his head posted by the Pagalies Family. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Tusken Raiders
