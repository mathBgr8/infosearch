Liberation of Harte Secur
2640

The Liberation of Harte Secur was a retaliatory battle where forces led by Boss Rugor Nass attacked Camp Six and the occupied city of Harte Secur to rescue their Fambaa beasts of burden. The Gungans destroyed a Federation command there, and getting their attention, drew them out to the Great Grass Plains, thus liberating the city. The Liberation of Harte Secur appears during the Gungan campaign in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
