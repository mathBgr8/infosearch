Jamie Hyneman
1974

Jamie Hyneman worked for ILM on the prequel movies The Phantom Menace and Attack of the Clones. He co-hosted the MythBusters show on the Discovery Channel, along with Adam Savage. In 2014, they hosted a MythBusters Star Wars Special episode. Hyneman voiced a member of the Galactic Empire along with Savage on Phineas and Ferb: Star Wars.
