|class= |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=DathomirBook of Sith: Secrets from the Dark Side |habitat=Lairs |diet= |language= }} Moogs were a small non-sentient species of creatures indigenous to the planet Dathomir that were the favored food of the reptomammalian rancors. They were known to be quite difficult to catch as they were both swift and fast-moving animals. They made a snuffling sound and lived in lairs. Mother Talzin of the Nightsister coven of Dathomir Witches mentioned the creatures in her manifesto, Wild Power, comparing their snuffles to the echoes of the Fanged God, a being of the dark side of the Force who resided in the realm of Mortis and was worshipped by the Nightsisters as a deity. Wild Power was one of many writings collected in the Book of Sith, a Sith historical book assembled by Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Sidious sometime between 19 BBY and 18 BBY.
