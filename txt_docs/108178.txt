Afterburners
764

The Afterburners was a swoop gang based on the planet Entralla during the formative years of the New Republic. They were led by an individual called Ace. The Afterburners were commonly found lurking in the streets of the Overhang, near Nexus City's starport, and would attempt to protect citizens from con men and other local criminals. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Swoop gangs
