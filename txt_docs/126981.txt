Clem Vaneer
1337

Clem "Trigger" Vaneer was a male Clantaani working for Longo Two-Guns. He earned a bounty on his head by Jabba Desilijic Tiure the Hutt because he owed the Hutt sixty percent of a generous loan. The bounty was claimed by Jango Fett, who was able to kill him. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Clantaani Category:Criminals Category:Males
