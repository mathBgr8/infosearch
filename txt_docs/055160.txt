Sith medical kit
1111

thumb|200px|[[Palpatine/Legends|Darth Sidious's medical kit.]] A medical kit was a case of special potions and medical equipment engineered for use by the Dark Lords of the Sith. This medical kit was used by Darth Sidious to treat his wounded apprentice, Darth Vader, following his duel with Obi-Wan Kenobi on Mustafar. The contents of the kit included vials of injectable bacta and bota, injector power cells, wireless defibrillator, cardiovascular monitors, injector handle and head, resuscitating ventilator, adaptors for pressor field generator, and filtration transpirators. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: Beware the Sith Category:Medkits Category:Sith artifacts
