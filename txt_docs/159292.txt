Artom's Crest
542

Artom's Crest was the highest rock spire rising from the molten surface of Rennokk, a moon of Cularin, rising more than 20,000 kilometers. Reidi Artom landed here while conducting her survey of the Cularin system. It was the only named location on Rennokk. *Living Force Campaign Guide Category:Cularin system locations Category:Rock formations
