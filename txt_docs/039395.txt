Punctil
874

Punctil was a spice used to enhance the flavor of various meals, including Bantha rump. The four-armed chef, Gormaanda, provided instructional holovids including tips on how to properly use this food additive for various meals. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the Wookiee, Mallatobuck, used liberal helpings of this item when preparing a special Life Day Bantha Surprise dinner for her husband, Chewbacca. *The Star Wars Holiday Special Category:Cooking spices Category:Wookiee food
