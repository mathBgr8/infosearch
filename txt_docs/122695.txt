Tarasin Skintone Language
1007

Tarasin Skintone Language was a complex form of communication thought to be one of the most beautiful languages in the galaxy. With this method, the Tarasin expressed their emotions and feelings&mdash;as well as thoughts and ideas&mdash;through a system of skin color changes. Jorus Trass, a Jedi linguist, and other such scientists deciphered and detailed the intricacies of the language in the document, Shades of Meaning. *Living Force Campaign Guide *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages Category:Tarasin culture
