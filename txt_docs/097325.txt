Lord High Admiral
1310

Lord High Admiral was a military title and rank which Princess Leia Organa believed that the former Imperial Admiral Harrsk had awarded himself. In Children of the Jedi, the rank of "Lord High Admiral" is attributed to Harrsk only in Leia's thoughts, where she thinks of him as "the self-styled Lord High Admiral Harrsk" and later simply as "High Admiral Harrsk". He is also referred to as "Admiral Harrsk" by Gial Ackbar, and as "Grand Admiral Harrsk" by C-3PO.
