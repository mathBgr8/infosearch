Attack on Coruscant
1833

The attack on Coruscant was an offensive by the Duinuogwuins during the Duinuogwuin Contention that ravaged the planet. The attack occurred in 15,500 BBY when scouts of the Galactic Republic discovered the Duinuogwuins. Hundreds of Duinuogwuins followed the scouts back to Coruscant, where they began attacking the city. A peace treaty was soon worked out between the Republic's Supreme Chancellor, Fillorean, and the Duinuogwuin philosopher, Borz'Mat'oh.
