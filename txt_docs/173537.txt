Star Wars Weekly 95 is the ninety-fifth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on December 18, 1979. It included the original story "Chewbacca Fights Alone!" by Archie Goodwin. *"Chewbacca Fights Alone!" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 7: New adventure!" *"Guardians of the galaxy! Part 22: Fulfilled!" *"Tales of the Watcher: One of our spacemen is missing!" 95
