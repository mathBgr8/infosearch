Galleros Nul
1864

Galleros Nul was the Mandated Commander of the People of Zelos, after declaring martial law, and the governor of Zelos II in 4 ABY. The Galactic Empire had a secret garrison on the planet in the Valley of Umbra. New Republic operative Jai Raventhorn was captured by the base and interrogated. However, she escaped her imprisonment and took the commanding major captive. She forced the major to call Nul and expose the Imperial presence. It was likely that Nul then called an air strike on the garrison.
