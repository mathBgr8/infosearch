Ethmane
589

Ethmane was a dense gas found on Kr in the form of frozen ice jungles. It also vaporized into the atmosphere of the moon. It could block scanners or sensors, and thus became a problem for any flying among the ice structures on Kr. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Compounds Category:Gases
