She was killed in Nashal by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|220px|Nomi's corpse Nomi was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Nomi's corpse was added to the game with the revamped "Rebel Theme Park," released with the "Chapter 6" update, on May 22, 2007. Nomi herself was later introduced with the revamped "Imperial Theme Park," released with the "Chapter 6.12" update, on September 13, 2007.
