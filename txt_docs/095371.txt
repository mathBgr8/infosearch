Mag-ball,Rebel Dawn also known as magball,Star Wars: The Old Republic was a spectator sport. Mag-ball was first mentioned in A. C. Crispin's 1998 novel Rebel Dawn, the third entry of The Han Solo Trilogy. The 2011 BioWare video game Star Wars: The Old Republic introduced an alternate spelling of the sport's name. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Rebel Dawn Category:Ball sports
