Flying serpent
1466

The flying serpents were giant flying reptiles&mdash;essentially winged snakes&mdash;native to the planet Ophideraan. The serpents were hatched from eggs in a breeding ground within the caverns under the planet's surface. The serpents were used as riding beasts by the group known as the Serpent Masters. The warriors found the beasts responded to certain ultrasonic tones and pulses.
