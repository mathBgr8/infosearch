Imperial Biological Research Center
1264

An Imperial Biological Research Center was based on the planet Najarka, as part of the Imperial Complex. After Yearo Seville kidnapped Crying Dawn Singer and his entourage, they were taken to the center to be interrogated by Imperial Security Bureau interrogators. While Crying Dawn Singer was transported to Braig Farool, the rest of the entourage was killed in the facility. *The Abduction *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Empire scientific facilities
