Governor of Caluula
1106

The Governor of Caluula was a female Galactic Alliance governor of Caluula during the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 29 ABY after the Battle of Caluula, she made a deal with the Yuuzhan Vong that would allow them to occupy Caluula in exchange for not using surge-coral and allowing scientists onworld to witness the Nocturne of the Winged-Stars. The decision made her extremely disliked among the populace, and she retreated behind her walled headquarters.
