Tagren

Tagren was a Mandalorian male warrior under Mandalore the Preserver on Dxun. In 3951 BBY, he was a strong Mandalorian and a great warrior, but he failed to defeat Meetra Surik in the Battle Circle within the camp even though the fight was unarmed and Surik wasn't allowed to use the Force.
