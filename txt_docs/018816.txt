Clan Vos
5735

The Clan Vos was a Kiffar family. For a time they were the rulers of the planets Kiffu and Kiffex. The Clan's insignia was a gold five-point star enclosed by a gold circle, the same symbol used by the Kiffu GuardiansJedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force.
