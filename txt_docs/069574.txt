Long Tan
1620

thumb|right|200px|[[CR92a Assassin-class corvette|Assassin-class corvette Long Tan under attack from Imperial TIE Fighters.]] The Long Tan was a Corellian Engineering Corporation Assassin-class corvette operated by the Dimok forces. During the Sepan campaign, the Imperial Escort Carrier Tropsobor delivered a consignment of TIE Avengers to Admiral Harkov's flagship, the Victory-I class Star Destroyer Protector, near Gerbaud 2 the Long Tan was part of a joint Dimok/Ripoblus assault force that attempted to disrupt the starfighter transfer.
