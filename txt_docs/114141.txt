Rory
1261

Rory was a Human male member who served the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. He was one of the survivors of the attack on the transport Bright Hope during the Battle of Hoth and assisted Toryn Farr when she took command of the surviving passengers. He was the first to sight the approaching ship Mist Hunter, and was rescued with the other survivors by Zuckuss and 4-LOM. Category:Alliance Military members Category:Humans Category:Males
