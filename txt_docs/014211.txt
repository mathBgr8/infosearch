It had only one moon and was covered almost completely by oceans interrupted by long archipelagos. These islands many beaches were covered by carnivorous flagellate microorganisms. The Galactic Empire depopulated the planet of its natives as punishment for rebelling against Imperial authority, then used the planet as a penal colony for fugitive Wookiees. In 3 ABY, the Imperial Governor of the system, Io Desnand, planned to stage an attack on the penal colony, thus baiting Rebel ships into a rescue attempt.
