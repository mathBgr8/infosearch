Entha Kandela was a skilled scout who served as animal-control officer for the city of Mos Eisley, under the orders of Mayor Mikdanyell Guh'rantt. She was severely underfunded by the time of the Battle of Yavin. Because of this, the vermin outside the city walls became a major health hazard. In 1 ABY, she hired an individual to deal with the problem. While affiliated with Jabba the Hutt's criminal organization, the spacer acted on behalf of the Mayor Mikdanyell Guh'rantt. The spacer eliminated a number of womp rats, kreetles, worrts, tempest rills and rockmites for 2,150 credits, satisfying Kandela. Entha Kandela was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Entha Kandela was added to the game with the release of the "Publish 17," on May 26, 2005. She used to be a specialized trainer providing quests to the beginner players who had chosen a "scout" career.
