Hunter (game)
488

Hunter was a hide-and-seek game played by the youth on Coruscant. Emperor Palpatine encouraged the children of workers at the Imperial Palace to play it inside the Palace. The game was thought to have originated with these children. These games were rumored to last as long as three days. *Tyrant's Test Category:Games
