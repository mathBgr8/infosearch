Teta (disambiguation)

Teta or Empress Teta could mean several things: *Teta, according to Qel-Droma Epics, Empress of the Empress Teta system *Empress Teta, an astronomical object in the Deep Core *Empress Teta system, the star system hosting Empress Teta *Teta, a Duke *Tetarick sea urchin, a spiky creature *Teta, Empress of the Empress Teta system *Empress Teta, the planet formerly known as Koros Major, renamed after the above's death *Empress Teta system, also named after Teta *Teta, a Duke from the planet Coruscant *Empress Teta's Crown Jewels, a collection of weapons, armor and jewelry
