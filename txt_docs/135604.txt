Itipiniwi
1014

Itipiniwi was a wilwog that won Best in Show at the Annual Mid-Rim Domesticated Sub-Sentient Show in 22 BBY. Upon winning, Itipiniwi and its owner celebrated, annoying the runner-up bolstyngar named Boshuda, which devoured Itipiniwi. Because this had never occurred before in competition history, the bolstygar was awarded Best in Show by forfeit on part of the devoured. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Named pets
