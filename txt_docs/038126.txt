A man working in the galaxy during the time of the New Republic, Stridus Darkov owned a fringe enterprise during his life. At some point during his career, Darkov came to trust one of his employees, a young female named Alara Fax. To her own ends, Fax used Darkov's trust, friendship and his credits to bankroll her own endeavors until she had the means to start her own company, Fax Ventures.
