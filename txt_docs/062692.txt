All-Terrain Attack Vehicle
1355

All-Terrain Attack Vehicle, or AT-AV for short, was the nickname of a female pittin that was a pet belonging to Tia Organa, one of Princess Leia Organa's adoptive aunts on Alderaan. As a child, Leia had named the creature herself. AT-AV had a pale candy-pink color and was small enough to fit in Leia's cupped hands. She also liked to roll on her back and bat at Leia's braids and starblossom petals.
