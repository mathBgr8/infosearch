Unidentified spider droid
1108

This class of spider droids were small, floating combat droids that were employed by the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Battle of Amaltanna. During this battle, several of these droids attacked the Jedi Knight Bultar Swan; however, she was able to destroy all of them.
