Jurec Tre
1190

Jurec Tre was a male Spiner criminal imprisoned on the Spice Mines of Kessel for several counts of murder on Corulag. He was the toughest and most aggressive prisoner in his block and a natural bully. He had intimidated a small group of prisoners to act as his personal thugs.
