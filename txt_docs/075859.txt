Wotson
2031

Wotson was a large male in the Peace Brigade, a band of galactic citizens that cooperated with extra-galactic invaders called the Yuuzhan Vong during the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 25 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion alongside Ven, Wotson guarded Reck Desh, the leader of the Brigade, when he traveled to Kuat City on the planet of Kuat. There, the trio visited a diner called the Nebula Orchid to meet an informant, secretly Viqi Shesh. Wotson and Ven sat at a nearby table while Shesh told Desh of a captured Yuuzhan Vong priestess. Once the meeting was over, the trio pursued the informant into the streets, but lost sight of the person.
