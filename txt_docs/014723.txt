While it had a variety of natural environments, the native Rellarin people were concentrated in coastal and island settlements in the frigid polar regions. The Rellarins had a primitive, stone-age culture, and rarely left their homeworld. The only export of Rellnas Minor was the wisdom of the Rellarin tribal elders (called un'Yala.) These sages were revered offworld, especially by the Swaze people of the Jidlor system, who sent their leaders to consult with the un'Yala every year.
