The two closest orbital positions around Faarlsun were occupied by the searing-hot rock planet Jun and the asteroid belt known as the Zossi Belt. Further out, the star was circled by two terrestrial worlds, the poisonous Weken with its two moons and the habitable Pochi with three natural satellites of its own. The outer orbital positions in the Faarlsun system were occupied by the gas giant&ndash;type planets Koss and Calipp, which had eight and twelve moons, respectively. Pochi was a dry planet that was home to the semi-intelligent Tulvaree avians. At some point by 7 ABY,Wanted by Cracken is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor. Since The New Essential Chronology dates that conflict to 4 ABY, the placement of the sourcebook therefore translates to 7 ABY. the Human pirate and warlord Faarl arrived in the uncharted star system. Faarl established his base of operations on Pochi and named the system and its star after himself. Faarlsun was mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, written by Louis J.
