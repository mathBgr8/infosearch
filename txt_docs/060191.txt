X2-a series sensor node
2135

The X2-a series sensor node designed by MicroThrust Processors was a piece of equipment for the battlefield used during the time of the Galactic Civil War by both the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance. Sensor nodes placed throughout the battlefield collected and monitored specific locations for any kind of activity and transmitted the data back to field troops.
