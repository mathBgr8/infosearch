Malevolent
1376

Malevolent was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that saw service with the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. During Operation: Clean Sweep, the vessel challenged the Rebel Alliance frigate Endeavor to a ship-to-ship duel which included both warships' contingents of starfighters. While the occurrence of the space battle depicted in this mission, as well as the participants, was C-canon within the Legends continuity, the outcome depends on the actions of the player and is therefore indeterminate.
