Scanner Jammer
1646

The Scanner Jammer was a mobile platform produced by Loratus Manufacturing and used by the Rebel Alliance during the early stages of the Galactic Civil War. The Scanner Jammer was designed with a variety of countermeasures to disrupt and jam enemy scanners and sensors.
