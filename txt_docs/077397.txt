Ket Krax
1023

Ket Krax was a male Troob. He was the son of General Krax and Stigrit Krax. He was secretly in love with Larka Nimondro, the daughter of the enemy Chief Nimondro. Jake Harthan murdered Ket with a vibro-shiv, blaming it on Larka in hopes of provoking a major war and stealing control of the sky cannon. *The Protocol Offensive thumb|left|180px|Ket Krax, killed by a vibro-shiv.
