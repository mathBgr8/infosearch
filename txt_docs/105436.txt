|armament= |equipment=*Three wheeled legs *Retractable heavy grasper arm *Extendable 0.3 meter video sensor *Electric arc welder *Circular saw *Video display screen *Holographic projector/recorder *Fire extinguisher *Internal cargo space |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic *Reekeene's Roughnecks **Green squad }} R2-V0 was a R2-series astromech droid with purple adornments. R2-V0 was owned by the Ithorian Ixsthmus, a captain of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. The droid served the Green squad of the Reekeene's Roughnecks, a team of the Alliance, and was stationed aboard Ixsthmus's ship, the Long Shot. The unit was disassembled over fifty times by the ship's co-pilot, Siene Symm. This was to repair the continuous stream of nonsense that came from the units' actuator. Symm was unable to repair it, because the sounds were not a result of a defect.
