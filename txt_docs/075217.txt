Acid storms were a weather phenomenon that occurred on Mordis VI's surface. The bounty hunter Zam Wesell once claimed that, due to it being designed for operation on wasteland worlds with thin, low-oxygen, or corrosive atmospheres, her stolen Koro-2 all-environment exodrive airspeeder could withstand the effects of an acid storm on Mordis VI. Mordis VI was mentioned in the 2003 reference book The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels, which was authored by W. Haden Blackman.
