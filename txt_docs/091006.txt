Fefar Blackeye
1570

General Fefar Blackeye was a one-eyed Nosaurian, who led a rebellion on New Plympto against the Galactic Empire, shortly before the Battle of Yavin. However, he was captured by the Empire in 1 ABY. Popular legend had it that an Imperial commander trapped him in a copse and waited until sunset. When it came, Blackeye, in the manner of his people, was overcome with an urge to bray loudly, thus revealing himself.
