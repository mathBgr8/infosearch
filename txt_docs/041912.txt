Hammer (Imperial-class)
2595

Hammer was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer in service with the Imperial Navy. The Hammer was dispatched to the space station D-34 in the Javin system after the Battle of Hoth, to bring relief to the forces there, who had come under attack by Rebel ships, allied with the local Mugaari pirates. The EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Fogger later arrived in the system to relieve the Hammer, which remained in the area to assist D-34 in dealing with the sudden increase in enemy activity in the sector.
