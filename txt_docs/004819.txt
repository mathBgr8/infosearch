Ison Trade Corridor
1261

The Ison Trade Corridor, also known simply as the Ison Corridor, was a hyperspace route passing through the Ison system in the Anoat sector. The planet Mataou served as an entry point to the Ison Corridor for starships leaving the Corellian Trade Spine. *Star Wars: Uprising *The Art of Star Wars: Uprising *Star Wars: The Rebel Files Category:Greater Javin hyperlanes
