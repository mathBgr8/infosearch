Feathered dog
1069

thumb|250px The feathered dog was a domesticated reptavian kept as a pet on Taris prior to and during the Mandalorian Wars. Any of these creatures still living on-planet at the time of the Jedi Civil War were likely killed when Darth Malak subjected the planet to an orbital bombardment. This creatures looks similar to the varactyl, a species native to Utapau. It also looks similar to a mooka.
