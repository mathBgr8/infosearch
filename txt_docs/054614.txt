Cubber Daine
3715

|affiliation=New Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Cubber Daine was a male Human who served as a mechanic to Wraith Squadron, a New Republic commando unit, shortly after their formation as a temporary squadron. Cubber worked with Wraith Kell Tainer to get and keep the squadron's X-wings, TIE fighters, and their CR90 corvette in running order. During the Galactic Civil War, Cubber Daine served for a year on Hoth. As a mechanic, he relied especially on a treadwell droid to assist him in his repairs, as he found the many appendages and arms of this type of droid highly useful. He would show his treadwell to others, informing them about its usefulness. He was known for doing favors, such as modifying R2 units, in return for pilot training. He also hated factory-new X-wings, because they had a tendency to malfunction. He did, however, have a tendency to lie about how much it would take to fix them, so that commander Wedge Antilles would think he was working harder than he was. He had a six-year old daughter, who he claimed could lie better than Kell Tainer.
