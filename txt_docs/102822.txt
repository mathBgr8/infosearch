Duggan Station
479

Duggan Station was an area of Bburru Station. The headquarters for CorDuro Shipping was located there. Port Duggan was a docking area here; one that the Jade Shadow used at one point in the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Spacecraft locations
