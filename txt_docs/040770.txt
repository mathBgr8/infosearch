Zalk't
1318

Zalk't was the Vratix senator of Thyferra in the Galactic Alliance Senate during the Swarm War. He was on Coruscant at the time of the Tarhead Coup. In The New Jedi Order: Star by Star, the Thyferran senator outlines a nine-point plan for peace with the Yuuzhan Vong.
