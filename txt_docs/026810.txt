Measure 4213.0410 was a proposal brought to the senate by Jedi Master Jorus C'baoth, and passed by Supreme Chancellor Palpatine in 8:2:11. The Measure was used in order to sponsor and launch the Outbound Flight Project, in addition to providing full funding for the project. Category:Galactic Republic laws and acts
