Alyson Court
1562

Alyson Court was born in Toronto, Canada on November 9, 1973. At the age of 11, she voiced the Ewok character Malani in the 1985 Ewoks animated television series. Her best-known appearance was The Big Comfy Couch. She also appeared on such television shows and movies as The Care Bears, Madballs, The New Archies, X-Men, Timothy Goes to School, Stranger in My Bed, Beetlejuice, Drop Out Mother, Silver Surfer and Clifford the Big Red Dog. She also voiced Mira Clay, Soon, and other characters in Bakugan. Her favorite movie appearance was a live-action video called My Pet Monster (also an animated TV series) in which she costars with Yannick Bisson, Colin Fox and Sunny Besen Thrasher.
