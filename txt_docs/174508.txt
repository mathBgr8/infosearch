Voshan Containers
890

Voshan Containers was a freight shipping company that operated during the time of the Clone Wars. They shipped goods along the Corellian Run. The company was easily identified by its bright orange livery. One of their ships was involved in an incident with an unmarked Republic traffic interdiction vessel at the Corellian&ndash;Perlemian hyperspace intersection 367 days after the Battle of Geonosis. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero Category:Transport companies
