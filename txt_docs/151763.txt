Corellian Vehicle Reclamation
797

Corellian Vehicle Reclamation was Corellia's leading landspeeder repossession company. It was repeatedly attacked by local gangs that were hired by enraged customers whose vehicles had been repossessed. The company's CEO often hired armed individuals to eliminate those gangs to prevent further attacks. This company is mentioned in the mission "Stop Rampages Through My Officer" that is available at Mission Terminals on Corellia in Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided.
