Futor's Network
862

thumb|right|200px|[[Futor with some clients]] Futor's Network was an information network company founded by the Sullustan Futor on Omze's Incredible Traveling Starport. It was located next to Con'varra's Datawork Central. The office contained a couch for customers to sit on while Futor looked up the data they had requested. It also had a counter and work table covered in datapads. Futor's Network would provide information and access to newsnets and other services to the patrons of the Starport. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:News networks and companies Category:Omze's Incredible Traveling Starport locations
