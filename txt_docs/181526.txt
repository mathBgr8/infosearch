Alan Rosenberg
728

Alan Rosenberg (born October 4, 1950) is an American actor who supplied the voice for Boba Fett on the NPR adaptation of The Empire Strikes Back. He has played numerous other roles, most notably Eli Levinson in both Civil Wars and L.A. Law. He was the president of the Screen Actors Guild from 2005 to 2009.
