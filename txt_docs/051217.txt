Talz (language)
3017

}} Talz was the native language of the Talz species from Alzoc III. It was mainly composed of buzzes and chirps, with variable pitch being an essential feature.Ultimate Alien Anthology The Talz throat and proboscis structure made them unable to speak most languages of the galaxy except for their own. Most Talz were not fluent in Basic and had no understanding of it.
