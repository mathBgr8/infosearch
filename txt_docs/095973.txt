The Locans were a sentient species of near-Humans that shared its name with the Wild Space &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Loce system celestial body Loce. They had pointed ears, prominent incisor teeth, and distinctive light purplish-blue skin. One Locan, Talas Piran, had a thick body frame and a heavy build with large arms the skin on which was black and mottled. Piran had short black hair.
