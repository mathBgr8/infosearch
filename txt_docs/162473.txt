Astrogation buffer
1270

The astrogation buffer was a data storage device installed in many astromech droids. It held a limited number of hyperspace coordinates, and allowed the droid to plot courses for smaller starships that were not equipped with nav computers. Different buffers held different numbers of jump coordinates, ranging from the single set a stock R1 could hold to the R7's ability to hold fifteen different destinations.
