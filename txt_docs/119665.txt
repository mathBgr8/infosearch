Blaze of Glory
1742

The Blaze of Glory was a modified YT-1930 light freighter owned and operated by the Bimm smuggler Rycar Ryjerd. The Blaze of Glory was at one time docked in Rycar's hidden asteroid base in the Obana Belt. Category:Smuggler starships Category:YT-1930 transports
