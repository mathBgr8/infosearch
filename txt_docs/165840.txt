Raije
1111

Raije was a ZeHethbra bounty hunter active during the Galactic Civil War. He was known to carry a Wookiee bowcaster, which he is said to have claimed from his very first target. He once attempted to find the Devaronian Brinaloy N'Vaari in Le Yer on Abregado-rae with the mission to kill her. His employer was a rival of the Le Yer gangsters Malkoi and A'jidre Skrigatov.
