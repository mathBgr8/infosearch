Redkihl Rokk's spider droid
1107

Redkihl Rokk's spider droids were droids used by Redkihl Rokk to attempt to kill Han Solo, Leia Organa, C-3PO, and Chewbacca while they were aboard his ship. Before the Empire, Redkihl Rokk used these to stun opponents on a ship so he could loot the ship of valuable items. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Battle droid models
