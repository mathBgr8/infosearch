S-4
2557

S-4 was a droid in service to the Star Tours travel agency as a quarantine inspector. He cleaned the baggage claim area with his associate, P-6. He was equipped with radar, a flyswatter, and a loupe. Both were supervised by a blue R2 unit. He required locomotive pads to move. The background for this droid comes from a "Spot the Droid" poster displayed at the Tokyo Disneyland Star Tours, which was translated at the fan site, as well as the Complete Guide to Star Tours booklet. The droid was present during the opening day of the first ride, but he and his counterpart, P-6, were eventually removed.
