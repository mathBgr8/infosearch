Rallo
1760

Rallo was a Human male who was the leader of a Rebel cell on the Corellian moon of Gus Talon. Living incognito as a local mechanic workshop owner, Rallo and his daughter Mala were firm opponents of the Galactic Empire and smuggled goods to the fledgling Rebellion. When the biased Imperial captain Turrant tried to arrest Rallo and his group without cause, a battle soon ensued. The young officer surviving Rallo's defense immediately called for reinforcements; unexpectedly, they arrived in the form of TIE bombers which totally annihilated the small settlement and its population.
