Tree fisher
825

Tree fishers were carnivorous plants from the jungle world of Port Haven. It consisted of a large body with a collection of vines. The small vines were used to attach to trees while the larger ones were used to entrap prey from the forest floor. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Carnivorous plants Category:Vines
