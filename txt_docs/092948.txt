Prowler
1588

The Prowler, or Rebel Prowler, was an aquatic cruiser used by the Rebel Alliance. It was a vessel with large mounted cannons, developed for shore bombardment and shelling of enemy fleets. The Heavy Prowler was an upgraded model of the standard Prowler.
