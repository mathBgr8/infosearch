Beta Cruiser
794

A Beta Cruiser was a type of battleship used by the Hapan Royal Navy. It was much smaller than the Nova-class battle cruiser, but also more maneuverable. The Hapan fleet that fought in the Battle of Fondor contained many ships of this class, but few survived that engagement.
