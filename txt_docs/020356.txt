|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4.5 ABYThe date of this story is established by in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire: Evolution |issue=1 }} Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 1 was the first issue in the Shadows of the Empire: Evolution series of comics, a sequel to Shadows of the Empire and was released on February 11, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. Xizor, the leader of the Black Sun organization, has been destroyed, and Guri, a Human replica droid and Xizor's personal assassin, is left wondering what her future will hold now that she no longer has anyone to answer to. Throughout the ongoing struggle for underworld supremacy, Guri endeavors to uncover hints of her Human side &ndash; if it even exists. Her search first takes her to the far side of the Rim looking for droid programming genius Massad Thrumble, in the hope that he can alter her programming. The cover of this issue states it as the first out of six, though the series only consists of five issues (as stated on the covers of the other issues).
