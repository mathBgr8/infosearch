District 3054 was an urban district on Vorzyd V. Luke Skywalker went through it on his way to rendezvous with Natala Vanden in 1 ABY. Skywalker had been surveyed by Imperial agent O-625 until losing track of him in 3054. Another group of Imperials quickly dispatched a number of spy eyes which followed Skywalker through the district. *"Gambler's World" Category:Districts Category:Vorzyd V locations
