Team SoroSuub
1126

Team SoroSuub was a swoop racing team. It was presumably sponsored by the SoroSuub Corporation. As of 22 BBY, it had become the second-highest-ranking swoop team. During the Clone Wars, the team participated in the Cularin Classic swoop race on the planet Cularin. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Swoop racing teams
