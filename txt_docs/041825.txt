Christopher Moeller
640

Christopher Moeller is an acclaimed comic book painter, who primarily works on covers and special projects. For Star Wars, he provided cover and interior art for the Battle of the Bounty Hunters pop-up book and cover art for Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi &mdash; The Golden Age of the Sith&mdash;both published by Dark Horse Comics.
