It had at least one spaceport. At some point during the Galactic Civil War, Yesgar's pirates, who had a hideout on the Esstran sector &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Antared system astronomical object Antared III, were spotted in Durollia's spaceport. Durollia was introduced in Star Wars Gamemaster Handbook, a 1993 sourcebook published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. In the game, the celestial body was mentioned in a hypothetical example in a section advising gamemaster players on the use of various paper handouts in their games. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Durollia system in grid square P-5. The book's co-author Jason Fry has stated that the star systems listed in the Appendix section of The Essential Atlas are named after the most prominent planets or celestial bodies located in those systems.
