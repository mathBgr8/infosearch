IBC Arcology (Aargau)
1156

The IBC Arcology was a massive pyramidal structure on Aargau owned by the InterGalactic Banking Clan. The Galactic Republic garrisoned the structure early in the Clone Wars, despite the technical neutrality of Aargau. It claimed that Hailfire droids seen on Geonosis were produced in this arcology.
