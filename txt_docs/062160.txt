Vercanne Mineral Technologies
877

Vercanne Mineral Technologies was a company that specialized in producing devices for explorers. Its most popular pieces of equipment were devices which could extract oxygen and other necessary elements from subsurface deposits such as the FOE-2000 oxygen extractor. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology Category:Personal gear companies
