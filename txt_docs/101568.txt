Requilisant
568

Requilisant was a chemical compound that reduced the dexterity penalty associated with operating in low or zero gravity. It cost 200 credits per dose and the effects lasted for 6 standard hours. Requilisant could be applied by the Cyduct E-23 Booster interdermal injection system. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Far Orbit Project Category:Compounds Category:Stimulants
