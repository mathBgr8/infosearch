Captain's Pride
1659

The Captain's Pride was an Ugor Black Hole-class Salvage Dreadnought. It gathered debris in the wake of the Imperial Armored Transport Elusive, near the devastated planet Dankayo. There, the Captain's Pride competed aggressively with a rival scavenger vessel, the Squib Momship Thrifty (which had arrived first) and with Elusive Alliance to Restore the Republic pursuers aboard the shuttle Bonderium. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Black Hole-class Salvage Dreadnoughts Category:Ugor culture
