For nine standard years, he posed as a Lannik for the New Osler Flejj Beasts, a Pugil team. Exposed by a random blood test in 22 BBY, he immediately fled the planet Lannik. Until his blood test, he was the highest-scoring and highest-paid player in Lannik history. Teammate Kova Naan, interviewed in the aftermath, quipped to a reporter that even he might be Noviee.
