Romi Moola
1774

Romi Moola was a female Twi'lek who formerly served Jabba the Hutt during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, a bounty was placed after she escaped Jabba's Palace while attempting to kill him by poisoning his chuba stew, only to have it kill his loyal chef instead. She was captured by Jango Fett, who claimed the bounty.
