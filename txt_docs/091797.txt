|birth= |death=33 BBY, Naboo system |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Royal Naboo Security Forces **Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps ***Echo Flight |masters= |apprentices= }} Kerl was a young male Human from Naboo who served as a starfighter pilot in the Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps' Echo Flight. In 33 BBY, Station TFP-9 was attacked by what was assumed to be pirates consisting of Z-95 Headhunters with a Hornet-class carrier serving as their capital ship. He was assigned as Echo Two for a squadron of twelve N-X Police Cruisers. He would be watched over by two N-1 starfighters from Bravo Seven, piloted by Essara Till and Eight, piloted by Dren Melne, who was secretly contracted by the Agamar Governor Challep to steal both N-1s and some of the cruisers. When the Naboo fighters arrived both squadrons engaged and drove the Z-95s to their carrier with Echo flight taking minor damage. Till then asked Echo One, piloted by Evenyl Yob, and Kerl, to join her and Dren for an attack run on the Carrier with proton torpedoes. Kerl was so excited he would be challenged against a capital ship that he screamed. It was then that more Z-95s arrived, surprising Yob abd Kerl; it was a second Hornet-class Carrier, Velumina, that launched fresh Z-95s. The first carrier dropped Droid starfighters and Till ordered her group to concentrate on them. It was then that Bravo Eight, Dren Melne, betrayed Naboo by breaking formation and opened fire. Kerl didn't know where the attack came from, Yob told him it was Melne. Kerl could only watch as his shield generator. Till tried to shoot at Bravo Eight, as Kerl's right nacelle exploded and started spinning; his dome crashed on Echo One's hull, destroying her astromech, and careened away. Till attempted to regroup the flight, and everyone was surprised when Echo Two reported back, with their fighter floating in pieces. Till promised they would rescue the pilot.
