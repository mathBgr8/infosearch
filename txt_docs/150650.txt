Mapuzo break-in
1576

|outcome= |keyparties= |participants=Vadon Lenitor }} The Mapuzo break-in was an event that occurred on the world Mapuzo by or in 2 ABY. The Task Force on Alliance Security believed that the Rodian bounty hunter Vadon Lenitor had been present during the events that led to the Mapuzo break-in. Thus, they wanted to find Lenitor and interrogate her, although she was not considered as a wanted fugitive. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Battles of Mapuzo Category:Events of the Galactic Civil War
