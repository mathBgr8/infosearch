The Movie Trilogy Sourcebook
38173

The Movie Trilogy Sourcebook was a roleplaying guide published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. It was written by Grant S. Boucher and Michael Stern, and was first published in September 1993. The book summarizes and details the events of the original trilogy of Star Wars movies.
