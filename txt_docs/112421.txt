Birok's gang
949

This gang operated on the planet Diado under the leadership of Birok, during the Clone Wars. It consisted of five members: Birok, a Devaronian, and three Humanoid males. One was choked to death and the others were shot by Saesee Tiin. Category:Criminal organizations Category:Mid Rim organizations
