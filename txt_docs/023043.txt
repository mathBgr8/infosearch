Tri-Nebula Entertainment
893

Tri-Nebula Entertainment was a media company owned by TriNebulon News. It was based in the Minos Cluster. Tri-Neb specialized in creating fantastic documentaries based only loosely on real events, combining both real holographic footage and the work of their own artists. It was responsible for at least three stories concerning the adventures of Han Solo. One of these was Eight for Aduba-3: The Saga of the Starhoppers, telling the Corellian's adventures on Aduba-3.
