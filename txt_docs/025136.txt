Chokk was a Klatooinian bodyguard and explosives expert who was in the paid service of the Hutt crime lord Jabba Desilijic Tiure during 32 BBY.The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 32 BBY. Chokk took pride in his work. He had dark-colored eyes and dark red skin. The Klatooinian was very good at his work. During the Boonta Eve Classic in 32 BBY, Chokk wore dark blue pants and a crimson tunic fastened with a belt that had an array of pouches. Chokk first appeared in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, which was released in 1999. Chokk was later identified in Star Wars: Episode I The Visual Dictionary as one of Jabba Desilijic Tiure's bodyguards during the Boonta Eve Classic.Star Wars: Episode I: The Visual Dictionary However, this depiction was contradicted by Star Wars Customizable Card Game and the Young Jedi Collectible Card Game, both of which were released in 2001.
