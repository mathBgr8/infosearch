In 32 BBY, she was present during the celebrations held in the wake of the Invasion of Naboo. Fay David stood in as Padmé's handmaiden for some pickup shots that were used for the celebration scene at the end of The Phantom Menace. While Fay was probably just standing in for an already existing handmaiden, she surmised that the handmaiden was "perhaps named Fé," a new character entirely.
