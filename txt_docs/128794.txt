High Castle
1399

The High Castle on Jomark had been built by the original inhabitants of Jomark, who had long-since vanished by the time of the Galactic Civil War. It was a short, dirty building with sometimes ill-fitting stonework, built on what was left of a volcanic cone overlooking Ring Lake 400 meters below in the center of the cone's crater.
