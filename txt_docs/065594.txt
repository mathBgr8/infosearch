Buzzee's
1925

Buzzee's was the name of a restaurant that was located in Mos Eisley spaceport on the planet Tatooine during the reign of the New Republic. The Human scholar Ebenn Q3 Baobab claimed that the food at Buzzee's, all of which was insect-based, was unique and reasonably priced in the Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide he authored in 6 ABY. Baobab also declared in the book that the larvae served at the restaurant were the best available in the Outer Rim Territories and included the establishment in a list of what he thought were the finest eateries in Mos Eisley.
