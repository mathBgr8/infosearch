Symponik
898

Symponiks were epic historical musical works composed by Gungans that were one of the pillars of Gungan oral history. Major events in Gungan history, both triumphant and tragic, inspired and formed the basis of the symponiks. Musikagungs performed such works at Boss coronations, Sacred Feasts, and other major events in Gungan life. The "Symponika Crunchen-Grand," composed by Beezar Pert, became the definitive symponik of the formation of the Gungan Grand Army. Category:Gungan music Category:Music genres
