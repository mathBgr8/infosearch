Hunt saboteur
853

A hunt saboteur was a profession that disrupted the work of bounty hunters. A saboteur would intercept a bounty, agreeing, for a fee, to ferry the sentient safely out of the range of their pursuer. N'dru Suhlak was an accomplished hunt saboteur. *Slave Ship *Hard Merchandise *The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Category:Occupations
