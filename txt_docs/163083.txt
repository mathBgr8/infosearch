Judith Reeves-Stevens
1828

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=Canadian |occupation=*Author *Producer |companies= |website= }} Judith Reeves-Stevens is a Canadian author who, alongside her husband and co-author Garfield Reeves-Stevens, wrote two Star Wars short stories in the 1990s. The pair penned "A Bad Feeling: The Tale of EV-9D9" for Tales from Jabba's Palace and "One Last Night in the Mos Eisley Cantina: The Tale of the Wolfman and the Lamproid" for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina. They are also well-known for writing numerous books in the Star Trek franchise, including a series co-authored by William Shatner. Category:Short story authors
