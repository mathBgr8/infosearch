Museum of the Old Republic
1148

The Museum of the Old Republic was a museum established by the Republic Senate on Centares. It contained an aviary. Following the Battle of Yavin, it was shut down by Emperor Palpatine and used by Darth Vader to interrogate Thurlow Harris, a captured Rebel pilot. It was there that he first learned of Luke Skywalker's name. *Vader's Quest 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Centares locations Old Republic
