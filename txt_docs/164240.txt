Crimpler
1511

Crimpler was an undefeated Lorellian kickboxer who was drafted into the Hapan Royal Navy during the Yuuzhan Vong War. He saw defeat at the Battle of Fondor and defected to become a pirate. During an attempt to capture the Hapan Jedi Knight Tenel Ka, he was defeated in unarmed combat and imprisoned by Hapan forces.
