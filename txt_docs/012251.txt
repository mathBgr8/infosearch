Dav-Wes Renno
1947

*Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} A Force-sensitive, Dav-Wes Renno was a Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order in the final decades of the Galactic Republic. During the Separatist Crisis, which plagued the Galactic Republic during its final decades, the former Jedi Count Dooku reemerged as a political idealist for the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Renno and Chief Librarian Jocasta Nu assisted the journalist Harris Sanseon compile data for the HoloNet News Special Feature entitled Who is Count Dooku?.
