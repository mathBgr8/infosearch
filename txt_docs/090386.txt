Harme Kiela
1546

Harme Kiela was a male Human and a lieutenant of the Eiattu pirates who attempted to trap Air Marshal Von Asch into rejoining the Galactic Empire. Asch quickly rejected the offer, though, claiming that the pirates were a disgrace to the Empire. Kiela pointed his blaster at Asch's head, but was immediately shot at by Rebel General Lando Calrissian.
