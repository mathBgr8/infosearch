Conclave on Zonama Sekot
2548

A conclave was called by Jedi Master Luke Skywalker at the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War. Held on Zonama Sekot in 29 ABY, it gathered together virtually all the Jedi, including the students onboard the Errant Venture and those stationed at the Shelter in the Maw. Allowing everyone a day to mingle and catch-up, Skywalker convened the meeting in a forest clearing, delivering an opening address discussing the repercussions of the war and directions for the future of the Order. At the meeting it was decided to relocate the Jedi Order to Ossus, a move suggested by Kam Solusar.
