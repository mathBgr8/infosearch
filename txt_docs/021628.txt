|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: The Last Command |issue=3 }} The Last Command 3 was the third issue in the Star Wars: The Last Command comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel The Last Command. It was released February 4, 1998. Approaching the midpoint of the climactic story in Timothy Zahn's exciting Thrawn series, all the story lines are moving toward a convergence: the scheming smugglers, Mara Jade's mysterious past, Grand Admiral Thrawn's plans for using clones to help defeat the New Republic, and Leia's growing concern for the protection of her newborn twins.
