Liane appears in the directory tree files for the 2003 massively multiplayer online roleplaying game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided. According to these files, Liane was a handmaiden to Queen Kylantha. However, Liane is not known to have appeared on the video game's live servers as a proper non-player character.
