It featured the large island continent Klypash. The Shatras, a species of four-eyed sentient reptilians, evolved on Trascor. The species was divided into five races, including the Hy'tras and the dominant Y'tras, and the Shatras fought extensive racial wars centuries prior toAlliance Intelligence Reports 2 ABY.Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. After the conflicts, the Hy'tras willingly reverted to a more primitive level of technology and made a bargain with the Y'tras in which the former surrendered all of their accumulated wealth in return for the latter leaving them alone. Subsequently, the Hy'tras only resided on Klypash&mdash;with the other three Shatras races inhabiting other regions of Trascor&mdash;while members of the dominant Y'tras eventually left their homeworld for the wider galaxy. The Shatras bounty hunter Ssach'thirix hailed from Trascor. Trascor was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
