Garrik Trier
1222

Garrik Trier was an Admiral in the Imperial Navy. He commanded the fleet stationed in the Brak sector during the Galactic Civil War. Trier was known as a confident commander, who spent a great deal of time analyzing naval tactics used by the Rebel Alliance. Garrik's last name is also the name of a city in Germany. *Flashpoint!
