He developed counterintelligence tactics at Echo Base, including setting loose the flamboyant red protocol droid R-3PO in search of Imperial spy droids. Torshan formerly served under General Jan Dodonna on Yavin 4 and sat in on the conferences planning the Rebellion's assault on the Galactic Empire's Death Star battle station for the Battle of Yavin. Major Palo Torshan was an officer of the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. He served under General Jan Dodonna at the Rebel Alliance headquarters on Yavin 4 and attended the strategy conferences planning the Battle of Yavin, in which the Rebels assaulted and destroyed the Galactic Empire's first Death Star battle station.The New Essential Chronology Torshan later continued to serve with Alliance High Command at Echo Base on the ice planet Hoth. thumb|left|200px|Palo Torshan (left) stands beside a tauntaun in Echo Base. Torshan coordinated Rebel security for Echo Base and developed counterintelligence techniques. He suggested that the Rebels reprogram R-3PO, one of Echo Base's more temperamental and troublesome protocol droids, and set it loose to search for Imperial espionage droids. Torshan also suggested not giving R-3PO a memory wipe or repainting its red body, since no one would suspect such a flamboyant and moody droid of being a spy. R-3PO proceeded to foil several Imperial infiltration attempts at Echo Base. In 3 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of , which includes Palo Torshan's service at Echo Base, to 3 ABY. just before the Battle of Hoth, Torshan was present in Echo Base's tauntaun stable on the night Rebel Commander Luke Skywalker went missing during a routine scouting mission of the base perimeter. As Torshan and another soldierStar Wars: The Action Figure Archive were struggling with all their physical strength to calm one of the agitated tauntauns that was trying to break free,Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back novelization Han Solo passed their position and departed the base in search of Skywalker. Palo Torshan's instincts concerning R-3PO's value as a spy droid proved correct after R-3PO snuffed out several Imperial infiltration attempts at Echo Base and, later, with Alliance High Command. Torshan also possessed a certain level of Force-attunement. A Human, he had light skin. During his service at Echo Base, Torshan donned the cold-weather armor standard of the Rebel personnel on Hoth. The Torshan character was first named and given a brief backstory on the as part of the November 1998 Special Edition Limited expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game. Torshan's card identifies the character as "Force-attuned."
