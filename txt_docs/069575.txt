124 group was a pair of Imperial Corellian Engineering Corporation Assassin-class corvettes involved in supplying Admiral Wooyou Senn's Task Force Vengeance in the Outer Rim Airam sector during the Galactic Civil War. During a scouting mission, a Rebel Alliance A-wing revealed these modified corvettes to be carrying generators and warheads.
