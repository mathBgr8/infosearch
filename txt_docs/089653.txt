|cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |pronouns=new |sensor= |plating= |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=QT-3PO's master }} QT-3PO was a protocol droid. Shortly before the Battle of Yavin, in 0 BBY, QT-3PO and its master set down in Mos Eisley on the planet Tatooine in their freighter. While the freighter was being repaired, QT-3PO wandered off to explore, but was forcibly abducted by the Jawas of Nebit's clan at blaster-point. There, QT-3PO observed Jawa society and the interior of their sandcrawler. The sandcrawler later recovered two other droids in the desert&mdash;C-3PO and R2-D2. By this point, QT-3PO had been extensively damaged, but was still able to relate the story of his time on the sandcrawler to C-3PO. Shortly after C-3PO and R2-D2 were sold to the Lars family, QT-3PO was discovered and purchased back by their master, who paid the Jawa's asking price.
