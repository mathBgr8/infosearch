|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=137 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Legacy |issue=16 }} Legacy 16 is the sixteenth issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. The origin of A'Sharad Hett's descending into Darth Krayt is revealed in this issue. Much has been told about the legacy of the Skywalker family, but until now little has been said of the legacy of Obi-Wan Kenobi! If you think you know the whole story, prepare yourself for a shock. In the wake of the Clone Wars, Obi-Wan fought a lonely battle&mdash;the outcome of which rocks the future of the Star Wars galaxy! Another layer of the Legacy mystery is revealed! A'Sharad Hett's duel with Obi-Wan Kenobi is expanded upon in the young adult novel The Life and Legend of Obi-Wan Kenobi.
