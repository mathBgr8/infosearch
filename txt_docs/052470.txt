Crustai asteroid base
2079

Crustai asteroid base was the asteroid headquarters of Picket Force Two of the Chiss Expansionary Fleet, on the borders of the Chiss Ascendancy. The base was actually hidden inside the asteroid. Force Commander Mitth'raw'nuruodo was in charge of the base for some time.
