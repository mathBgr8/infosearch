Zlato's Place
869

Zlato's Place was a restaurant and lounge on Coruscant run by the Sljee Jeseej. It was Toydarian-themed, with the staff, food, and decorations all Toydarian. Its signature dish was terratta. The restaurant was always half empty. However, this was not a problem for Jeseej, as the true purpose of Zlato's Place was a front for his side businesses, forgery and soothsaying. Access to these special services was only gained by giving a passcode to the restaurant's maitre d' and ordering a particular menu item based on another password.
