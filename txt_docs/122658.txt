Unified Ruurian Colonies
865

The Unified Ruurian Colonies were a group of planets in the Tingel Arm of the Outer Rim Territories controlled by the Ruurian species from Ruuria. In 19 ABY, the region was headed by Amisus, who surrendered it to the Imperial Remnant, which was being represented by an imposter of Thrawn during the Caamas Document Crisis. *Vision of the Future *The Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Planet groups
