Lion (Nebulon-B2 frigate)
2147

The Lion was a Nebulon-B2 frigate belonging to the Rebel Alliance. Along with the Lance and the Ornik, the Lion was to commence an attack on the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Conqueror. They had T-65 X-wing starfighters, RZ-1 A-wing interceptors and B-wing starfighters as fighter support and BTL Y-wing starfighters as back-up. The Conqueror had TIE Avengers from Avenger Squadron, TIE/IN interceptors and TIE/sa bombers.
