Arthur Baker
665

Arthur Baker is the calligrapher for William C. Dietz' Dark Forces series of novellas. Arthur Baker was born on the West Coast, and as of 2005, lives in Andover, Massachusetts. As a renowned calligrapher, he has designed many typefaces and published over twenty books on the subject. Examples of his work include the use of his Signet Bold font for the "Coke" on Coca-Cola products and his ITC Tiepolo on Lipton products. Category:Comic book letterers
