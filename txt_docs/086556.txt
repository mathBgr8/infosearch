Outland Regions
1388

The Outland Regions was a vast area of space in the Outer Rim Territories of the galaxy, including the space around the planet Eriadu. In the last decades of the Galactic Republic, the Outland Regions were patrolled by the warships of the Republic Outland Regions Security Force, and under the Galactic Empire, Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin of Oversector Outer could have been styled as Governor of the Imperial Outland Regions. *Cloak of Deception *Rogue Planet *Coruscant Nights I: Jedi Twilight *Star Wars Souvenir Program *The Essential Guide to Characters *The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film Category:Outer Rim Territories regions
