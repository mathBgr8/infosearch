|pages=14 |timeline=8 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |campaign=The DarkStryder Campaign |preceded by=Shintel Downtime |followed by=Traitor in our Midst }} "Crisis" is an adventure in The DarkStryder Campaign. It is set in 8 ABY. Seeking a slicer to purge the FarStar system of programming trapdoors, the corvette heads to Pembric II, a world run by the crime lord, Crev Bombaasa. With information gained from Tanquilla Beach, they were directed to Breslin Drake, the manager of the ThrusterBurn tapcafe. Running afoul of Bombaasa, the crew of the FarStar, aided by Drake, rescued the young slicer Gaelin from his clutches and escape the planet.
