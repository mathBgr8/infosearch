|continent= |location= |climate= |interest=Viper Tower |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Hpaq was a city located on the planet Vulpter that was home to Viper Tower, headquarters of Viper Sensor Intelligence Systems. Hpaq was originally created for HoloNet News Vol. 531 50, a HoloNet News article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens with illustrations by Joe Corroney and released on April 4, 2002.
