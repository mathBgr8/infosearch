They were able to consume nuts, bolts and other spare parts, including rusted droid parts.Complete Guide to Star Tours This model was one of the earliest of the MSE series.Star Wars: The Ultimate Action Figure Collection Star Tours, a short-lived travel business, had two of these droids. One was named MSE-1T, while the other unfortunate unit was damaged and found a new use as a table for G3-5LE. The droid models frequented the Droidnostics Center of the spaceport. In 2005, Hasbro released MSE-1T as an exclusive Star Tours action figure, but its model name has not been revealed. The droids are not exclusively found in the Disney's Hollywood Studios version of Star Tours, but also at Tokyo Disneyland.
