Jefferson Starship
2927

Jefferson Starship is a rock band formed in the 1970s. The group performed the song "Light the Sky on Fire" as an unnamed band for a musical sequence in The Star Wars Holiday Special. The members involved in the special were lead singer Marty Balin, drummer John Barbata, lead guitarist Craig Chaquico, keyboardist David Freiberg, rhythm guitarist Paul Kantner, bass clarinetist Steven Schuster, and bassist Pete Sears. In the early 1970s, Tom Veitch, who would later write Star Wars comics, wrote two issues of a comic book for Jefferson Starship's vanity label Grunt Records. In June of 1977, the group was photographed with Darth Vader (as played by touring model Kermit Eller). Sometime later, Grace Slick met Mark Hamill backstage at a concert, where she introduced herself as "Darth Vader's wife." By 1978, Creach and Slick had departed from the group. It was during this period that the group performed in The Star Wars Holiday Special. Balin and Barbata soon departed the group as well.
