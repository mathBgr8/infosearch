|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=0.5 ABY |series=Star Wars: River of Chaos |issue=1 }} River of Chaos 1 was the first issue in the Star Wars: River of Chaos series of comics. It was released June 6, 1995 by Dark Horse Comics. Several months after the destruction of the Death Star, Emperor Palpatine sends an Imperial pilot to spy on the governor of a distant planet, M'haeli. Although Palpatine knows of certain Rebel activity on M'Haeli, he does not know of the presence of one particular Rebel: Princess Leia Organa! Can Leia undermine the enemy from within, or is she doomed to drown in a river of chaos? This ground-breaking four-issue series takes readers behind enemy lines, to see the Rebellion through the eyes of an officer of the Empire!
