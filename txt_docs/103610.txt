Oph Nar Dinnid
1585

Oph Nar Dinnid was a Lyunesi comm handler on the run from bounty hunters after a huge bounty was placed on his head following an affair with a member of a supreme liege-lord's family in the Narrant system. He was murdered on Circumtore by Gheeta while seeking shelter with Shell Hutts before the team of bounty hunters led by Boba Fett could arrive to capture him; his body was found in a state of decay several weeks after his death. *The Mandalorian Armor *Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cyborgs Category:Lyunesi Category:Males
