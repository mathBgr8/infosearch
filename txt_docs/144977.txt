The system's primary planet was Sluis Van. The system withdrew from the Republic to join the CIS before the Clone Wars. Its senator at that time was Corlissi Ludar. In 9 ABY, the system was the site of a battle with Grand Admiral Thrawn during his campaign against the New Republic.
