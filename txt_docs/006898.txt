|pages=22 |upc= |timeline=33 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Republic - Prelude to Rebellion (3 of 6) |issue=3 }} Star Wars 3 is the third issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on February 17, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. As Ki-Adi-Mundi gets closer to the truth of who is seeking to destabilize his homeworld, and why, he comes closer and closer to his own doom, and only the dimly remembered teachings of his diminutive Jedi Master can save him! Shortly after the death of the Swoop-gang leader, Skeel, Ephant Mon decides it is time to move their hiding place. It turns out to be none-too-soon, as Ki-Adi-Mundi is hot on his trail. Just as he is attacked by Mon's merc droid, a messenger from the Elders arrives with his lightsaber. While barely escaping with his life, the Jedi Council authorizes to pursue his charges off-world.
