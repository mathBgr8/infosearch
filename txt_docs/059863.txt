RH Mining Corporation
520

RH Mining Corporation was a large mining corporation that existed during the final years of the Old Republic. RH Mining was formed by Rees Haslip but was later inherited by his daughter, Keriin Haslip, upon his death. Keriin sold the corporation to the Empire, who presumably nationalized it.
