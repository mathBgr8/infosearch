Malakin Enterprises
1103

Malakin Enterprises was a Lesu-based corporation that dabbled in several criminal venues. Rumored to be secretly controlled by Hutts, Malakin was part of the Tenloss Syndicate, and was the largest stockholder in the Natori Association. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations Category:Tenloss Syndicate companies
