Doolis Podrace Arena
1496

|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} Doolis Podrace Arena was a Podracing course within the capital city of Doolis on the planet Caprioril. When the efforts of the anti-Podracing organization known as the Ratts Tyerell Foundation lead to Podracing being outlawed on Caprioril in 22 BBY, proconsul Shren Whist announced the arena would be shut down. There were plans for the facility to then be used for another purpose, although what exactly was not immediately determined. The closing of the arena was later reported on by HoloNet News. The arena was first mentioned in the in-universe HoloNet News Vol. 531 45 article "Podracing Banned on Caprioril", which was released in 2002 on the HoloNet News website. Category:Podracing courses
