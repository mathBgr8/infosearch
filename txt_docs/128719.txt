Arhul (disambiguation)
1697

Arhul was a Human name. In Star Wars Legends, it was of Brentaalan origin and became popular after it appeared as the alias of Sival Brentioch in The Kallea Cycle. It could refer to: *Arhul Corubalni, scout in the Galactic Republic's Scout Service who wrote the initial survey log for the planet Vandor. *Arhul Hextrophon, Rebel officer and namesake of the Hextrophon Collection. **Arhul Hextrophon School of Embedded Journalism, school located on Lerct attended by Beaumont Kin. *Arhul Nemo, a lieutenant in the Commenor Underground. *Arhul Narra, commander of Red Squadron. *Sival Brentioch, who went under the alias of Arhul Habea. *Arhul Crace, a lieutenant in the Stormtrooper Corps. *Arhul Hextrophon, a Rebel and New Republic historian. *Arhul Holt, an officer in the Imperial Navy who later defected. *Arhul Kurumenga, a general in the Imperial Department of Military Research. *Arhul Manaxa, scholar emeritus at the University of Rudrig. *Arhul Narra, commander of Red Squadron.
