|letterer=Ed King |colorist=*Christie Scheele *Bob Sharen |cover artist=Bill Sienkiewicz |editor=*Michael Higgins *Mary Jo Duffy |publisher=Marvel Comics |release date=August 2 1983Marvel Age 5 |pages=23 |upc=071486029922 |timeline=4 ABYThe Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Return of the Jedi 2 to 4 ABY. |series=Star Wars: Return of the Jedi (1983) |issue=2 }} Return of the Jedi 2 is the second issue of the Legends comic book miniseries Star Wars: Return of the Jedi, which adapts the film of the same name. Based on a screenplay by Lawrence Kasdan and George Lucas, it was adapted by Archie Goodwin, illustrated by Al Williamson and Carlos Garzón, and first published by Marvel Comics in November 1983. The issue features the story "The Emperor Commands," which opens with the Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon. Luke Skywalker returns to Dagobah while Emperor Palpatine plots to bring him to the dark side of the Force. This issue includes a scene cut from the original film, featuring Princess Leia attacking a deck gunner preparing to fire on the skiff carrying Han Solo, Chewbacca and Lando Calrissian.
