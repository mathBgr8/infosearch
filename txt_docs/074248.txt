Bakura Truce
3061

The Bakura Truce, also known as the truce at Bakura, was a temporary cessation of local hostilities between the Galactic Empire and Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War was declared in 4 ABY for the duration of the Bakura Incident as the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium invaded Bakura. Crafted and signed by Imperial Governor Wilek Nereus and Leia Organa, the truce allowed the Alliance aid fleet and the Bakuran defense force to collaborate against the Ssi-ruuk invasion.
