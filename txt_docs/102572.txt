Dino Entertainment
1476

thumb|right|Dino Entertainment logo Dino Entertainment was a German-based publishing company responsible for printing the German translations of the Star Wars young reader novels and some comics. Dino was bought out by the Panini Group (Panini Verlag) in 2003. After this, Panini kept using the name Dino as a label until early 2007, when all of Dino's Star Wars Publications were moved to Panini itself. *Jedi-Padawan series *Jedi Quest series *Boba Fett series *Knights of the Old Republic Volume 1: Commencement *The Art of Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace *The Art of Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones *The Art of Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith Category:Publishing companies (real-world)
