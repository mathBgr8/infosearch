Brace Tarkosa
2576

Brace Tarkosa was a Human male who worked as a crewman on Outbound Flight. He was a strongly built man and one of the first hundred people recruited to the project at its start in 29 BBY. He was two years older than his friend on the crew, Chas Uliar. Tarkosa later represented the Survivors on the Managing Council in the Redoubt, where Outbound Flight crash-landed.
