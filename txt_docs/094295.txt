Groogrun
983

Groogrun, also known as Clan Groogrun, was a clan of Gamorreans. Nine of their members, led by Ortugg, guarded Jabba Desilijic Tiure at his palace on the planet Tatooine. Snogrutt, a master weapons maker and builder of the Clan Groogrun vibro-ax, was a member of this clan, and were known to produce these axes for sale to gamorreans such as Ortugg. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Gamorrean clans
