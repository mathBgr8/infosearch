The New Jedi Order: Knightfall II: Jedi Fire
1168

thumb|Preliminary cover art by Terese Nielsen The New Jedi Order: Knightfall II: Jedi Fire was the second book in the canceled Knightfall Trilogy of the New Jedi Order series. It was to be written by Michael Jan Friedman, with the cover art by Terese Nielsen. The plot would have dealt with Danni Quee and Jorallen seeking to stop the Yuuzhan Vong from murdering the Jedi. C3
