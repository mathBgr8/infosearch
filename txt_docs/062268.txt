Blaylock effect
589

The Blaylock effect projected images into the atmosphere of a planet. The Galactic Empire was rumored to have been in development of this technology, possibly to project as many as 10,000 battlecruisers into the sky. However, as of 1 BBY, they were rumored to be having trouble developing it. *Empire 8 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Holographic technology Category:Physics
