Ba'tra
1189

General Ba'tra was a Bothan officer in charge of the mine activities over Coruscant during the Battle of Coruscant. He was onboard the Orbital Defense Headquarters before it was destroyed, but escaped on the Lady Luck with Lando Calrissian and helped rescue Ben Skywalker. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothans Category:Males Category:New Republic Defense Force admirals and generals
