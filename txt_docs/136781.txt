Ubrikkian star yacht
1891

The Ubrikkian star yacht was a sleek light cruiser produced by Ubrikkian Industries. Built for quick getaways, the yacht came equipped with a hyperdrive, yet carried no weapons, apart from two concealed proton torpedo shafts. The Slams, a gang from Mamendin led by Roper Slam, owned and traversed their corner of the galaxy in a Ubrikkian star yacht, before their arrest in 24 BBY. The Slams had heavily modified the cruiser with a large number of compartments, hidden within various deck platings of the ship, in which they stored their illegal contraband.
