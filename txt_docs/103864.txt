Lanah Malo
1441

Lanah Malo was a member of the Corellian Resistance during the early years of the Galactic Empire. She accompanied her friend, Bria Tharen on a mission to Devaron to make contact with the local resistance and to assassinate the t'landa Til priest, Veratil, who was conducting a revival there to recruit pilgrims to work as slaves in the spice processing factories of Ylesia. The plan failed when Veratil caught sight of Han Solo in the crowd and alerted his guards.
