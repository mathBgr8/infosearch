Nova Nova
854

The Nova Nova was a restaurant in Coronet City, said to be the finest restaurant on Corellia. Reservations had to be made several months in advance. It served its food "techno style," with small portions arranged artistically, with sensors in the plates and utensils providing holographic displays telling the diner the ingredients and history of the dishes they were eating at the particular time. *I, Jedi Category:Coronet City locations Category:Dining establishments
