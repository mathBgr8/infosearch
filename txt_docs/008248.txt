Inexorable (Victory-class)
3679

Inexorable was a Victory-class Star Destroyer in the Imperial Starfleet during the Galactic Civil War. The Inexorable came to the aid of the Imperial space factory Drekker, where it deployed TIE Interceptors and TIE Bombers of Gamma and Beta Squadrons, respectively, to defend against a Rebel Alliance raid. The Inexorable was present in the Zhar system but was not able to prevent Rebel pilot Ace Azzameen's Otana and Rebel fighters to steal the Tydirium. While the occurrence of the space battle depicted in this mission, as well as the participants, is Canon, the outcome depends on the actions of the player and is therefore indeterminate.
