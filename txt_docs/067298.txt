Rwook
19479

Rwooks were the most common Wookiee subspecies, native to the planet Kashyyyk. They largely inhabited the tree-city Rwookrrorro. Rwooks were distinguished by their fur, which commonly had more earth-like tones than albino Wookiees, such as red, brown, or chestnut.
