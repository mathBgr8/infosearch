Silkweed
766

Silkweed was a plant native to the planet Kushibah, the homeworld of the simian Kushibans. It was used by the natives to produce hand-woven textiles which made them famous. This involved harvesting and then combining the silkweed and with fur they brushed from their coats daily and then spinning it with thread.
