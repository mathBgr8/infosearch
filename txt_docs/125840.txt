It also participated in the Alsakan Conflicts, a series of conflicts between the Galactic Republic capital planet Coruscant, the world Alsakan, and their respective allies that lasted from 17,018 BBY to 3017 BBY. During the Alsakan Conflicts, the Gilagimar was one of the few large capital ships used by Coruscant.
