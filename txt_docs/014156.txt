Antiquity was a a muddy world of shallow seas and sheer mountains. It was a minor agriworld located on the Belsavis Run and ruled by House Hax. Sparsely inhabited, the towns of Antiquity were very similar, collections of plasticrete shelters. Most of the planet's economy was based upon on the growth of Teggi fungal root. An Imperial Colonel hid on the planet after the Battle of Endor.
