Magenge
945

Magenge was a light green, sweet-tasting fungus that was the basis of the Verpine diet. The Verpine cultivated magenge on grow walls in agrifactories on their asteroid colonies in the Roche asteroid field. *Strike Force: Shantipole *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Edible fungi
