Gregg Berger
3742

Gregg Berger is an American voice actor who voiced Jedi Master Plo Koon in the video games Star Wars: Episode I Jedi Power Battles and Star Wars: Obi-Wan. He also voiced Darth Maul in Jedi Power Battles, and provides the voices of various characters in other Star Wars games. He voiced Kalani in Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Star Wars Rebels.
