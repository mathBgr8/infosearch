Drever Corporation
2094

*"Phoenix II" plasma disruptor *"DN bolt caster" plasma disruptor |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Drever Corporation was a weapons manufacturing corporation in the Parmic sector. Around the time of the Clone Wars, Drever Corporation created the "Phoenix II" plasma disruptor for special forces as a tool to break through sealed doors. However, these soldiers saw its effectiveness as a weapon as well and began using its devastating electrical charges against battle droids. Because of this, Drever produced the "DN bolt caster" plasma disruptor, which saw use by many clone pilots during the Clone Wars. During the New Order, the corporation was led by Jenson Drever II.
