Catherine Taber
15306

Catherine Anne "Cat" Taber is an American actress appearing in films, television and video games. She has done voice work since 2003, participating in several Star Wars projects. Her main works included voicing Mission Vao in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic'Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, Padmé Amidala in the Star Wars: The Clone Wars film and series,Star Wars: The Clone Wars film and Star Wars Forces of Destiny (a role she reprised in Lego Star Wars: The Skywalker SagaLEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga), Vette in Star Wars: The Old Republic'Star Wars: The Old Republic, and Numa in Star Wars Rebels.
