Glazer
1842

Glazer was a CR90 corvette that the Nharwaak used to ferry weapons technology to the Rebel Alliance Nebulon-B frigate Xerxes. The Glazer was escorted in this mission by two other corvettes, the Coffey and the Janson. In the ambush staged by Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's attack from the Imperial Nebulon-B2 frigate Shamus on the Xerxes, Glazer and its escorts were also attacked. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Nharwaak starships
