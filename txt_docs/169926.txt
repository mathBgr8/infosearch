Jedi Academy (H'ratth)
2122

This academy was used by the Jedi Order during the reign of the Galactic Republic and staffed by Jedi healers. Located on the lush planet of H'ratth, this praxeum was a premiere academy that taught the healing arts. An ancient facility, this academy specialized in the instruction of the healing arts. As of 4001 BBY, Jedi Master Chamma served as an instructor at the facility; Andur Sunrider was one of his prized students.Tales of the Jedi Companion Closed during the Jedi Civil War, the facility reopened following the Ruusan Reformations of the Galactic Republic. According to Darth Sidious's notes in his stolen copy of The Jedi Path: A Manual for Students of the Force, the H'ratth academy was one of many that he had destroyed during the Great Jedi Purge.
