Slythor
1333

Slythor was a Squib crime lord and owner of the R4-series agromech droid R4-B11. He worked from a base on Korad during the height of the Galactic Civil War. He often considered himself the "Highest Exalted Ruler of Korad." Many people doubted that because nobody wanted to live on the junk-covered planet.
