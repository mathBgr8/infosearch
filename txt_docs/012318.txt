|pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Jedi Order **Almas Academy **Almas Council *Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Ti-Amun Tiro was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as a member of the Almas Academy, a board of Masters and instructors who oversaw operations of the Order's academy on Almas. A Force-sensitive male, Ti-Amun Tiro was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, ascending to the rank of Jedi Master. A noted philosopher, Tiro was appointed as an instructor at the experimental academy on Almas, a planet located in the Cularin system. By the time the Clone Wars erupted across the galaxy, a violent conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Tiro had been appointed to the Almas Council, the academy's governing body which served as a satellite to the Jedi High Council on Coruscant. When Headmaster Lanius Qel-Bertuk called a meeting of the Council, the Coruscant envoy, Master Darrus Jeht, announced that the High Council was recalling most of the Academy's instructors to the Jedi Temple where they would be assigned ranks in the Grand Army of the Republic and lead clone troopers against the Separatist Droid Army. Furious by the High Council's decision, Tiro stormed out of the chamber in protest.
