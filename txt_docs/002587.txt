Ghôsh Windu
1868

thumb|right|200px|Mace Windu, the most famous member of the tribe Ghôsh Windu was the Korunnai tribe/family to which Mace Windu belonged. It was once loosely affiliated with Ghôsh Rostu. Windu and his "brother" Kar Vastor were the only known remaining members of the ghôsh by 22 BBY. All other members were killed in the Summertime War.
