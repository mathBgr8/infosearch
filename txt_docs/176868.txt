Galactic Power Brokers is an article appearing in Star Wars Gamer 10. It gave a brief description and stats for several powerful individuals in the Star Wars galaxy, including Senators Mas Amedda, Bail Organa and Orn Free Taa; Separatists Shu Mai, Nute Gunray, Passel Argente, Wat Tambor and San Hill; and underworld power brokers Elan Sel'Sabagno and Dexter Jettster.Star Wars Gamer 10 *Star Wars Gamer 10 Category:Star Wars Gamer 10
