Bio-energy array
925

The bio-energy array was a weapon used by the Qektoth Confederation and, like many of their designs, it was a biological creation. The array fired a blue lightning-like blast that was capable of piercing conventional shields in a manner similar to ion cannons. However, it required a large amount of power and as a result, shield strength would often suffer when the weapon fired. The weapon was mounted on their Qektoth attack cruisers, and was the cruiser's main weapon. *The Kathol Outback Category:Biological weaponry Category:Starship weaponry
