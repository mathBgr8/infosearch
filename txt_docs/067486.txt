It was home to a population of the sentient Baragwin species, members of which tended to be cautious but friendly. Denuhi-Eight was mentioned in the 1995 West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game sourcebook Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies, in an entry authored by Jean Rabe.
