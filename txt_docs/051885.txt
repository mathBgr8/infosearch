Tanass Brila
1436

Tanass Brila owned and operated Brila Industrial Supplies. Along with his wife, Renia, he was a member of the Rebel Alliance. He originally utilized peaceful protests to fight the Galactic Empire, which caught the attention of the local Rebel cell, and eventually Tanass gave up on his peaceful approach and joined the Rebellion. It was rumored he was a money launderer for Black Sun.
