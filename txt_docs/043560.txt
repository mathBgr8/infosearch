John May
2170

John May was credited as a contributing editor for issues 2 through 15 of the Star Wars Official Poster Monthly magazine, and as editor for issues 16 through 18. He may have been a contributing editor for the first issue as well. He also wrote several articles for the magazine: *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 2: "Darth Vader Lives" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 3: "Han Solo&mdash;Rogue Spacer!" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 4: "Luke Skywalker&mdash;From Youth to Man" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 5: "Chewbacca&mdash;Star Wars' Friendliest Alien" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 6: "The Lowdown on Artoo" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 7: "George Lucas&mdash;A Man and His Movies" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 8: "The Special Effects Wizards" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 9: "Ralph McQuarrie: Visualizer" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 10: "The Star Wars Snowball" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 11: "The Men Behind the Masks" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 12: "The Soundmaster" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 13: "The Model Squad" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 14: "The Machines That Make Lines Move" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 15: "'Empire' Latest!" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 16: "Fan Club Facts" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 17: "Introducing the Intergalactic Inker...Russ Manning" *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 18: "The Empire Gets Closer" Category:Editors Category:Non-fiction authors Category:Reference work authors
