Xal 3 Armorers' Confederacy
932

The Xal 3 Armorers' Confederacy, originally known simply as the Armorers' Confederacy, was a band of outlaw armorers and weapons technicians that was originally based on the world of Xal 3. They were forced off of their homeworld and forced to seek refuge in the Krasho Salt Rifts on the planet of Sarafur during the last years of the Galactic Republic. The Confederacy was known for its skills in repairing almost every form of combat armor, and their custom armor designs were considered some of the best in the Outer Rim Territories.
