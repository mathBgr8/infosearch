On Coruscant, Mace Windu explains to Yoda how his talks with the Er'stacians went... Windu arrived on Er'stacia to find a war in progress between rival clans. After negotiating a cease-fire with the help of his force abilities, Windu discovered the clans are fighting over ownership of a large statue known as the Venerable One. A warrior accidentally shot at Windu with his blaster, desecrating the statue, and he was torn apart by his fellow Er'Stacians.
