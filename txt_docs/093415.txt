Naga Sadow's poison blade
1385

Naga Sadow's poison blade was a Sith sword once owned by the Sith Lord Naga Sadow. Centuries later, the sword was found in the Tomb of Naga Sadow on Korriban by Revan during his infiltration of the Sith Academy during the Jedi Civil War. As its name would suggest, the blade was poisoned and would inflict poisonous effects upon its victims.
