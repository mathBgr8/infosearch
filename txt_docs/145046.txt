Pembric Security Legionnaires
1023

The Pembric Security Legionnaires were Pembric II's spaceport and possible planetary security force under the Pembric Security Legion. Each Legionnaire wore a white uniform, blast helmet and vest. Primarily Human, they patrolled in groups of five or six, and were heavily armed. In 8 ABY, Chief Magistrate Kebbo led the Legionnaires. They enforced the will of the Bombaasa Cartel on Pembric II, and were used to quell civilian protesters at Erwithat Spaceport.
