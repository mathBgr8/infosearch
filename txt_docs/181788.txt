Portminian
580

The Portminian was a civilian transport ship. The ship was used by the Galactic Empire to transport Memah Roothes, Rodo, and several other civilian workers to the Death Star I while it was under construction at Despayre. The ship shared its name with the Portminia system. *Death Star Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire Category:Transports
