Exocron Combined Air-Space Fleet
1437

The Exocron Combined Air-Space Fleet was the primary military force of the Exocron system. It was the successor of the Exocron Airfleet, established after the ruling council obtained data on spaceflight and advanced technology. Supreme Admiral Horzao Darr had command over the fleet, with Admiral Trey David as second-in-command. When Talon Karrde and his group of smugglers landed on Exocron to visit Jorj Car'das, the Exocron Combined Air-Space Fleet provided them with an escort as they landed. When Talon Karrde left Exocron, he was attacked by Rei'Kas's pirate fleet.
