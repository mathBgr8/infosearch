Rutian septuplets

200px|thumb These unidentified Rutian septuplets were a group of seven turquoise-skinned Rutian Twi'leks who were en route' to the Outer Rim Territories to be given to a Hutt. However, before they could be successfully delivered, their "transport" was hijacked and they were taken by Krayn.
