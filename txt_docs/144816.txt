Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 5
1870

Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 5 was a Republic Mobile Surgical Unit stationed on the jungle planet Drongar during the Clone Wars in 20 BBY. It serviced the personnel of the Galactic Republic at the Battle of Drongar alongside other surgical units under the command of Colonel D'Arc Vaetes. It, with all the other Republic forces on the planet, left when the bota mutated and became useless. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Republic Mobile Surgical Units
