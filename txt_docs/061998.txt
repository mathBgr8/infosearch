A Force-sensitive, Vaas Ga was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, achieving the rank of Jedi Master by the start of the Clone Wars. The war, a galaxy-spanning conflict between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, saw Master Ga pushed into the role of General within the newly created Grand Army of the Republic. Placed in command of Sarlacc Battalion A and B of the 41st Elite Corps, he saw battle on many fronts. During the war, Master Ga and his troops were assigned to take the planet Dinlo, working closely with Senior Clone Commander Gree. Overwhelmed during the fighting, Ga and his men were rescued by General Etain Tur-Mukan and the clone troopers of Improcco Company during the skirmish.
