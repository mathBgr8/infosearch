Cranan 12
1859

Cranan 12, or simply Cranan, was a planet in the Arkanis sector of the Outer Rim. Cranan 12 was a thriving planet, rich in ores and minerals. It was the only world controlled and exploited by the Mining Guild in the Arkanis sector.
