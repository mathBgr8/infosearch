Lucre
1499

Lucre was a small transport that was used by Corran Horn during the Yuuzhan Vong War. It was destroyed when Corran, Anakin Solo and Tahiri Veila set it on a preprogrammed evasion course in a Yuuzhan Vong fleet and escaped to a nearby asteroid in vacsuits. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order Category:Transports
