Model MilBar C-427
1737

The Model MilBar C-427 was a training facility designed by Delvin Constructs, that was used by the Galactic Empire. A prefabricated building, it could be set up quickly by Imperial commanders on any planet to provide a home for a limited Imperial garrison. This facility was most often used by stormtroopers and scout troopers.
