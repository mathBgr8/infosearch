Based in the Jedi Temple on the planetThe Essential Atlas Coruscant, he was revered for being an expert in lightsabers' inner workings as well as the weapons' construction. He could repair almost any damage done to a lightsaber that had been used in a fight. In 23 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Jedi Quest: The Final Showdown to 23 BBY. after Jedi Padawan Darra Thel-Tanis died in battle due to a faulty lightsaber, Jedi Padawan Ferus Olin brought the lightsaber to Hing, who found that the flux aperture and power cell's fusing needed a slight adjustment.
