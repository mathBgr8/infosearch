Phonane Mimesis Studios
635

Phonane Mimesis Studios was a corporation that manufactured animatronic puppets and other movable models. Their puppets were very lifelike in appearance. Because of this skill in design, the Khoss of Knylenn commissioned the company to create an intricate life-support system for the long dead Knylenn elder. *Slave Ship Category:Entertainment companies
