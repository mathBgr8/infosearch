Star Wars: The Official Magazine 1 was the 1st issue of the Star Wars: The Official Magazine, covering April and May 1996 and containing 66 pages. *Feel the Force *New Hopes *Q&A *Jawas' Corner *Second Trooper: Deborah Lee Carrington *:The first of our regular interviews with the Star Wars trilogy's unsung heroes finds us catching up with an Ewok! *"They Call Him the Sith Lord" *:The full low-down on Darth Vader, including interviews with Dave Prowse, James Earl Jones, Brock Peters and Sebastian Shaw, who have all had a hand in bringing the character to life. *"The Making of Star Wars" *:Introducing our major regular feature on the making of a legend. *Interview: Rick McCallum *:The Executive Producer of the new Star Wars trilogy brings you up to date on developments. *"Interview: Jeremy Bulloch" *:Step out with Boba Fett himself, a bounty hunter without equal! *Boba Fett: Bounty on Bar-Kooda Part 1 *:Part one of a three-part new comic strip story from Dark Horse Comics. Boba Fett is hired by a love-struck Gorga the Hutt! Story by John Wagner with art by Cam Kennedy. 01
