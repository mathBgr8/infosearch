Type-12A Anti-Personnel Rocket
2642

The Type-12 A Anti-Personnel Rocket was an ammunition type produced by Locris Syndicates and Locris Syndicated Securities. An anti-personnel rocket, the Type-12A employed an explosive contact detonator. The Type-12A was compatible with Locris Syndicates' Model RDP-12 Rocket Launcher, and the MM9 rocket system produced by Kelvarex Consolidated Arms.
