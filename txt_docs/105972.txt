Coruscant civilian transport
520

Coruscant civilian transports were small freighters used by the citizens of Coruscant to get to other areas of a city, or another part of the planet. They could each hold a fair amount of people, and were usually piloted by only one or two pilots. They were outfitted with light armor plating, to protect from heavy damage in the event of a crash. *Evasive Action: Recruitment Category:Freighter classes
