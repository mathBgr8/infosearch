Sebolto's Palace
1592

Sebolto's Palace was an underworld palace located on Malastare. It was home to the crime lord Sebolto. The complex itself is a huge structure with outposts that stretched far out into the surrounding jungle and even below it. Guards would patrol each outpost with Nexu. Below the main palace lay a huge death stick factory, a place that many of Sebolto's enemies and ironically himself would end up. thumb|left|180px|Sebolto's throne room There was an elaborate series of tunnels running under and around the palace in case of attack or a raid all, of which were heavily patrolled.
