Brogar's Safe House and Cantina
2729

Brogar's Cantina and Safe House, also known as simply Brogar's, was a combination of a cantina and safe house operated by Brogar on the planet Lok. He provided his patrons with security that included bouncers, Z95 patrol guard droids, and at least one Gotal door attendant; he always had a door attendant monitoring the electronic eye and entry intercom. thumb|right|250px|Inside view In 137 ABY, following the Sith–Imperial War, the Bothan Jedi Hosk Trey'lis sought sanctuary and transportation there at the same time as Naxy Screeger, who had a bounty placed on him by the pirate Rav. Cade Skywalker, Deliah Blue, and Jariah Syn tracked Screeger there to capture him and collect the bounty. In the process, they spotted Trey'lis, who also had a bounty that applied to any Jedi, dead or alive. The cantina and safe house appeared in issue two of Star Wars: Legacy.
