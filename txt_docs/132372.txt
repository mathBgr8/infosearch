Tarm
3045

Tarm was a Human male villager residing on the planet Jomark under insane Dark Jedi Joruus C'baoth's rule. In 9 ABY,Dark Force Rising states that it takes place five years after the events of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 5 ABY. Therefore, Dark Force Rising must take place in 9 ABY during Grand Admiral Thrawn's campaign, Svan, another Human villager, attempted to kill Tarm with a slugthrower and Tarm attempted to defend himself with a knife; however, C'baoth and a Jedi who he was training, Luke Skywalker, arrived after they sensed the "evil" in the building the villagers' confrontation was taking place in in and stopped it. Ultimately, C'baoth considered Tarm in the right following his and Svan's motives, and punished Svan with Force lightning and ordered him to pay Tarm the full wages they had set. Tarm had dark skin and a beard. Tarm first appeared in the 1992 novel Dark Force Rising, the second installment in Timothy Zahn's Thrawn Trilogy.
