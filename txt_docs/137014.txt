In 28 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion when the Remnant was engaged with a Yuuzhan Vong fleet in the Borosk system, the Dreadnaught-class cruiser Braxant Bonecrusher&mdash;after having successfully rescued prisoners from a Yuuzhan Vong slaveship during the fighting&mdash;was directed to dock at the Hale Return by Captain Arien Yage of the Imperial Navy so that the rescued prisoners could receive urgent medical attention. Hale Return appeared in 2003's Force Heretic I: Remnant, a novel written by Sean Williams and Shane Dix as part of The New Jedi Order series.
