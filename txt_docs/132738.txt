Raid on Bilbringi
4066

The Raid on Bilbringi was a raid by the newly established Smugglers' Alliance on the part of smugglers Mazzic and Ellor against the Galactic Empire at the Bilbringi Shipyards in retaliation of the Ambush on Trogan. After the Imperial ambush during the smuggler meeting on Trogan, Mazzic and Ellor needed to teach the Empire a lesson and attacked the Bilbringi Shipyards to avenge the death of the Gotal smuggler Lishma. Around twenty of Mazzic's freighters penetrated discreetly the system and destroyed a nearly completed Imperial-class Star Destroyer within the construction docks of the shipyards. The ships were attacked by squadrons of TIE Fighters, which were easily destroyed by Ellor's two Corellian Gunships that arrived to cover their escape. Grand Admiral Thrawn was very angry about the loss of the Star Destroyer and the implication of the smugglers in the war. He tried to convince them that he wasn't responsible for the ambush on Trogan but failed because they quickly discovered that Ferrier was an Imperial agent and the organizer of the ambush.
