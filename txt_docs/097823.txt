Ram-tractor
798

A Ram-tractor was an object that a Rebel starfighter crashed into inside the Death Star II during the Battle of Endor in 4 ABY. Wedge Antilles mentioned it to Cilpar resistance leader Elscol Loro when he was trying to convince her of his honesty that he and Rogue Squadron were there to help her. He described flying down an unfinished service corridor in the battle station, and "I lost a good friend who ran into a ram-tractor... we had to fly seat-of-the-pants...". *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 1 Category:Technology
