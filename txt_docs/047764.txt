Dupas Thomree
2335

Dupas Thomree was a Human male Diktat of Corellia who lived during the reign of the Galactic Empire. Thomree made a deal with Emperor Palpatine so he could govern the Corellian sector as he wished as long as the Corellian paid taxes, war materiel, and homage to the Emperor while leaving Corellia mostly free of Imperial mandates. He was assassinated in 2 BBY.
