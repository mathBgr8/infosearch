Opuh was a Phuii frequenter of Dex's Diner in the CoCo Town district of the planet Coruscant. The Phuii visited while the diner was active during or after 28 BBY to between 19 BBY and 18 BBY. states that Didi Oddo gave up his café four years after Qui-Gon Jinn's death on Naboo. The New Essential Chronology dates the events of , which includes Jinn's death, to 32 BBY. Therefore, Dexter Jettster must have owned the building some point during or after around 28 BBY. The Last of the Jedi: Underworld states that Dex's Diner was destroyed by the Galactic Empire, which The New Essential Chronology establishes was formed in 19 BBY. The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of the novel to around 18 BBY. Therefore, Dex's Diner was destroyed between 19 BBY and 18 BBY. N.J. Opuh was stated to appear in Attack of the Clones, but did not make to the final cut.
