Pulga
1764

Pulga were a type of creature native to the forest moon Endor. The sentient Ewoks also native to the moon would sometimes ride pulga. The term pulga was first used in the Star Wars Legends continuity and was used to refer to a type of horse found on Endor.The Essential Guide to Characters Gaupa were confirmed to be canon by the sourcebook Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know in 2015, which used the term independently from pony and so suggested the creatures were a separate group. *Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know *Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know, Updated and Expanded *Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars Bestiary, Vol. 1: Creatures of the Galaxy Category:Creatures of Endor Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Domesticated creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
