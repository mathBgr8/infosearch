Paul Blake
2528

Paul Blake is an English actor who portrayed the bounty hunter Greedo in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. A production photograph related to the scene with Jabba the Hutt in Docking Bay 94 shows Blake putting on a Rodian costume on the set.
