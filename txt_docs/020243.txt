Quetarra
2646

The quetarra was a Zabrak musical instrument that had eight strings and mutiple holes. The mad genetic terrorist Zeta Magnus received a double-necked quetarra as payment for assisting in the cybernetic reconstruction of a corniculate half-bot.SkyeWalkers: A Clone Wars Story Zan Yant played one in the casino at Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7 on Drongar in 20 BBY.MedStar I: Battle Surgeons thumb|right|A double-necked quetarra. In a forum post on the Jedi Council Forums, SkyeWalkers author Abel G. Peña revealed that the intended identity of the corniculate half-bot Zeta Magnus reconstructed and received a quetarra as payment from was Darth Maul. This double-necked quetarra he received is a reference to the double-necked electric guitar Darth Maul plays in Randy Martinez's "" illustration created for Star Wars Celebration IV. *SkyeWalkers: A Clone Wars Story *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *Coruscant Nights I: Jedi Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:String musical instruments Category:Zabrak culture
