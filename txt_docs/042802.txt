Dragon slug
2039

Dragon slugs were large, fire-breathing creatures who ate lumni-spice, living on both Hoth"A New Beginning" and Corulag. The fungus that created the spice was the slug's favorite meal; this diet often made them grow to 20 meters and develop armor-like skin.
