During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Logg visited the club, talked with the socialite Sel Maa DVD-ROM Depth Commentary and stood next to the Human Daro Willits while the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered the club, searching for bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Logg continued to stay with the two when Skywalker searched around the club. Logg had light skin and dark hair and wore black clothing.
