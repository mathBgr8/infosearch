Yu'nar
615

A yu'nar was a ruling tribunal of the Trianii society. The tribunals were all female, reflecting the matriarchal culture of the Trianii. Over the centuries, the yu'nars formed a strong religious moral code. Spirituality permeated trade, industry, and every aspect of Trianii society. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Alien Encounters *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Galaxy at War Category:Trianii culture
