Nebula Queen
1613

Nebula Queen was a cruise liner owned by Event Vistas. The liner was one of the newest and most beautiful ships in the Event Vistas fleet in 7 ABY. It made regular runs to Coruscant. Warlord Zsinj's Project Funeral kidnapped and brainwashed her Sullustan captain, Rostat Manr, and ordered him to crash the ship into Coruscant's surface. New Republic Intelligence placed an operative on the liner, who ordered one of Manr's subordinates, Nurm, to stop him. The liner then made an uneventful landing on the planet. *X-Wing: Solo Command *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Liners
