Jared Rutledge
1163

Sir Jared Rutledge was a minor House Mecetti noble from the Tapani sector. He had a brooding nature and was said to be a loner. Jared was a covert operative for House Mecetti. He was equally competent in working the underbelly of society as well as mixing with the noble elite. A patient man, Jared did not like to take unnecessary risk.
