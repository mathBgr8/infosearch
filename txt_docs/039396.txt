200px|thumb|Celonslay Celonslay was a green vegetable that was popularly used as part of a broth mixture for Bantha rump roasts. The chef, Gormaanda, provided instructional holovids including tips on how to properly use this food additive for various meals. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the Wookiee, Mallatobuck, used liberal helpings of this item when preparing a special Life Day Bantha Surprise dinner for her husband, Chewbacca. *The Star Wars Holiday Special Category:Fruits and vegetables Category:Wookiee food
