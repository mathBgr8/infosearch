Bonestar Pirates
2484

Bonestar Pirates were a group of Corellian rogues, once led by Loka Hask. During this time, they were pursued by the Corellian Security Forces for several crimes. An interstellar chase resulted in the pirates landing on Gus Treta Inner-System Market Station to create a diversion. The diversion involved the destruction of a fuel depot, owned by the Antilles family. The devastation, caused when the pirates lifted off while still coupled to the fuel dispensers, destroyed the Antilles' depot and killed Jagged and Zena Antilles. Wedge Antilles was safely off the station when the "accident" occurred.
