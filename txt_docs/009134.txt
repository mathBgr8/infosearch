|end date=September 19, 2001 |schedule=Monthly |format=Completed |issues=6 |timeline=1000 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Jedi vs. Sith was a series of comics published by Dark Horse Comics from April 18, 2001 to September 19, 2001, then compiled as a trade paperback on May 1, 2002. It tells the story of the end of the New Sith Wars, the Battle of Ruusan, and how the Rule of Two came into fruition.
