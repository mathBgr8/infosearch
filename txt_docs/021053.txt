Tolan Navy Yards
1185

Tolan Navy Yards, also known as the Tolan Naval Installation, was a Imperial Navy facility on planet Tolan, located relatively close to Dankayo. When the Imperial Navy destroyed the Rebel Alliance base on Dankayo, the Imperial Armored Transport Elusive was charged with gathering and transporting wreckage from the facility, including sensitive datapacks. On leaving Dankayo, the Elusive was attacked by the Rebel shuttle Bonderium, but while she escaped, her captain ordered her to divert to Tolan for repairs. While at the Navy Yard, the Elusive not only had her battle damage repaired, but was also fitted with an external rack carrying a force of TIE/LN starfighters.
