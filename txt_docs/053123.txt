HC-100
1899

HC-100 was the droid companion and homework checker of Ken in the Lost City of the Jedi. HC-100 had a humanoid body with silver plating and blue photoreceptor eyes. HC-100 was built by DJ-88. He was made to resemble C-3PO, and he walked and talked like a drill sergeant.
