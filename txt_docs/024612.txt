Star Wars Blog
1499

frame|Star Wars Blog logo The Star Wars Blog was the official blog on StarWars.com. It has since been merged into the official site's "News + Blog" section, with most posts being re-published as articles. The last blog post was made on June 24, 2014 with the blog shutting down shortly thereafter. The subdomain now redirects to the News section on StarWars.com. It featured contributions by Albin Johnson, Ashley Eckstein, Jenn Fujikawa, Bryan Young, Casey Pugh, Dave Filoni, Jennifer Landa, Jonathan W. Rinzler, Katie Cook, Leland Chee, Mary Franklin, Pablo Hidalgo, Pete Vilmur, Stephen J. Sansweet, and the StarWars.com Team. It replaced the Star Wars: Blogs prior to the 2011 website revamp, where multiple users could create their own blogs on StarWars.com. Starting on September 9, 2013, the blog began running a top-ten list called "The StarWars.com Ten," which involves the top ten aspects of a category of Star Wars chosen by the blog's staff. The first top-ten list featured lightsaber duels and the second featured starfighters. Category:StarWars.com Category:Web articles
