Micro Applications, Inc.
552

Micro Applications, Inc. or MicroApp was a computer chip manufacturing corporation that was based on the Bothan colony world of Kothlis, in the city of Tal'cara. Its generic chipsets were known to be used by a wide range of datapad and pocket computer makers. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Computer and software companies Category:Tal'cara locations
