Galactic Society of Recording Artists
1156

The Galactic Society of Recording Artists was a group that managed the collection of royalties due to be paid to its members for the performance of their music by other entertainers. C-3PO, a 3PO-series protocol droid with access to a myriad of songs for playback in his memory core, factored in these royalties when determining if he could earn enough money for passage off the planet of Nim Drovis with his counterpart, an astromech droid designated R2-D2,Planet of Twilight in 13 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion The Galactic Society of Recording Artists was briefly mentioned in Planet of Twilight, a 1997 novel by Barbara Hambly. *Planet of Twilight Category:Musicians' guilds
