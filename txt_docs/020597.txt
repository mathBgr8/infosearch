Swedlan
1318

Swedlan was one of the three primary planets that made up the Pimbrellan League. Located in Kathol sector within the Outer Rim Territories, Its atmosphere could not sustain most forms of humanoid life. Sealed domed cities were built in underground cave systems, financed with the profits gained from trade with nearby Dolstan. *The Kathol Outback Category:Kathol Outback locations Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories
