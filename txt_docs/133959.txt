Fang was a group of four CR90 corvettes that served with the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. They attempted to prevent the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Cathleen from escaping into hyperspace, however all four of the vessels were destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Category:Galactic Empire starship groups
