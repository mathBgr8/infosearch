|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYDate established in narrative text. |series=Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand |issue=4 }} Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 4 was the fourth issue in the Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand series of comics. It was released on November 4, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. After the death of Emperor Palpatine, Mara Jade escapes from Imperial Center and throws herself headlong into the underground of the Empire's underworld. Though her master is dead, Jade must complete her final mission&hellip; even if it costs her life!
