Rodian Galactic Drama Troupe
1582

The Rodian Galactic Drama Troupe was the most famous all-Rodian traveling group of Rodian Theater, and the only dramatic corps allowed by the Grand Protector to travel off-planet. Run by Entukan Yesosko, it consisted of 200 elite performers, directors, agents, musicians and other technicians. Technical staff sometimes participated in minor roles.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide It circulated along the galaxy's most prestigious entertainment districts, performing the best of Rodian drama, notably portraying accurately (and shockingly) all forms of violence. It used to stay to a certain location for several weeks, performing a variety of shows. The Grand Protector Navik the Red, having learned that rogue Rodians attended their performances, assigned some bounty hunters to shadow the group; Yesosko was tasked to inform the hunters about their movements at all times. However, this has been discovered by some group members, who, opposing Navik, try to sabotage the process; other members were aware but were afraid of Yesosko's reaction. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Rodian organizations Category:Theatrical organizations
