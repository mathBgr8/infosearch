457 was the designation for a group of three CR90 corvettes that belonged to the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The warships had been stationed near Vergesso Base during the Second Battle of Bajic; as a result of the action, all three vessels were destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
