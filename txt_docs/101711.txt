The Ghostling Children also depicts Anakin as older than the eight-year-old Arawynne, putting the books in 33 BBY. |series=Star Wars Adventures (Scholastic) |preceded by=Capture Arawynne |followed by=Danger on Naboo }} Star Wars Adventures 10: Trouble on Tatooine was the ninth installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. This book is a reprint of Episode I Adventures 8: Trouble on Tatooine. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 10 in July 2003.
