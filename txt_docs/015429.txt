|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=19 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The New Essential Chronology dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=83 |next=Dark Times 1 }} Republic 83: "Hidden Enemy, Part 3" is the eighty-third and final issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on February 8, 2006 by Dark Horse Comics. Palpatine's Order 66 has changed everything. As Jedi are slaughtered across the galaxy, one has managed to cheat fate&mdash;but for how long? Badly wounded by the same troops he previously led to victory, Master Quinlan Vos retreats into the jungle wilderness of the Wookiee homeworld Kashyyyk. As the clones close in, Vos must choose&mdash;stay and fight, or run and put those he loves in danger of the newborn Empire's wrath? According to Randy Stradley, the fate of Quinlan Vos at the end of the comic came directly from George Lucas. John Ostrander had initially considered killing Vos off, and an interview with Jan Duursema in Star Wars Insider before the issue's release claimed that he died.
