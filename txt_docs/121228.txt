Necr'ygor Omic wine
852

Necr'ygor Omic wine was an expensive alcoholic beverage from the Necr'ygor system. The LoBue Cantina in the Abregado system was known to serve it, and it was a favorite of Lando Calrissian's, whose preference was a '49 vintage. *Heir to the Empire *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Wines
