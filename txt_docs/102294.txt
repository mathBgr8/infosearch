Cutlass-class corvette
1116

The Cutlass-class corvette was a corvette type used by the time of the Second Galactic Civil War.Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines The Imperial Remnant used it as a capital ship during the years following the Swarm War.Legacy of the Force: Invincible In 40 ABY, at least one ship of this class appeared among the Bonadan forces supporting the Corellians during the Blockade of Corellia. *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *Legacy of the Force: Invincible *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corvette classes
