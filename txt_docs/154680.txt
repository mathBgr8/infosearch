Unidentified Twi'lek bounty hunter (Tatooine)
1303

A male Twi'lek bounty hunter was present in the cantina when Zorba Desilijic Tiure learned of his son's death. The Twi'lek also ambushed Luke Skywalker, but was killed along with another bounty hunter.
