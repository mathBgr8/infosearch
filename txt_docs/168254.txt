Eyeshine
1760

Eyeshine was a modified Corellian Engineering Corporation Barloz-class freighter that was owned by the Shistavanen smuggler Mal Biron. The vessel had been upgraded with the addition of an X10-D droid brain, which helped Biron to operate and maintain the ship effectively.
