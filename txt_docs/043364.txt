Vasarian brandy
1908

}} Vasarian brandy was an alcoholic brandy made from fruits and berries from the planet Talus. It was popular on Mid Rim worlds such as Lok. The pirate Nym enjoyed drinking from large mugs of Vasarian brandy while entertaining potential business associates.
