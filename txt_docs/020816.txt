Fiction
9335

thumb|250px Fiction was a feature on the Star Wars fan club website, Hyperspace. It was composed largely of short stories previously published in Star Wars Gamer, Star Wars Insider, Star Wars Galaxy Magazine, Star Wars Adventure Journal, etc., but more recent additions were original contributions. The 2008 update to StarWars.com saw many of the Fiction stories become unavailable to paying Hyperspace customers for an extended time. The stories were later fully returned to StarWars.com, though the subsequent 2011 update of the site saw the discontinuation of this feature. In all, 82 stories, including eight original, were published on Hyperspace.
