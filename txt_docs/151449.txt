At first she didn't understand the traded insults between Kempo, Sully Tigereye and Hugo Cutter until she figured it was just how the old friends worked together, she also believed that Hugo should have been in a psychotrauma ward. While on Gabredor III she killed a large reptoid mount after it threatened to kill her. While they attacked the base of the Karazak Slavers Cooperative, Kempo ran a repulsorcart full of explosives at their command center and tried to jump getting his foot stuck in the process.
