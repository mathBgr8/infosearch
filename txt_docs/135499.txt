The Free-Flight Dance Dome
2295

The Free-Flight Dance Dome was an elegant bistro on the planet Etti IV. The establishment served as a meeting place between Han Solo, Chewbacca and Ploovo Two-For-One and his guards, but unfortunately the meeting ended in a blaster fight. Han Solo was able escape using a gravity-shifter that the club used to lighten the gravity and allow its patrons to float. Han Solo turned the gravity on three-times normal gravity while in a protected area. With every patron flattened on the ground from their own intense weight, he and Chewbacca were able to escape through a path unaffected by the change in gravity.
