Survivor
1896

The Survivor was a Corellian Engineering Corporation&ndash;manufactured class of lifeboat. They had enough fuel for six hours of spaceflight, and a week's worth of consumables. Emergency gear included tents, thermosuits, portable hand scanners, substance analyzers, and a hunting rifle for small game. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Category:Corellian Engineering Corporation products Category:Escape pod classes
