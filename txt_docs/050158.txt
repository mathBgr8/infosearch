K'rom wars
513

The K'rom wars were a series of conflicts involving the Xi'Dec people. During these wars, several new sexes of Xi'Dec arose which were specialized for combat. These Xi'Dec had specialized forelimbs used as spears or sabers. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition Category:Battles in the Xappyh sector Category:Conflicts of the Outer Rim Territories
