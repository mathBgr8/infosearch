Nejma
2818

The Nejma were an aquatic species originally native to Eriscot. The Nejma relocated to the water-covered planet Iskalon to escape a cataclysm that struck Eriscot around five millennia before the Galactic Civil War, states that the species constituting the Iskalonian cultural group were brought to Iskalon approximately five millennia before the Galactic Civil War. Since "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series" establishes that the relocation of the Iskalonian species was prefaced by the cataclysms on the species' original homeworlds, the cataclysms must also have occurred around that time. and became a part of the Iskalonian School, a cultural group of several aquatic species that had traveled to Iskalon with the aid of spacefaring benefactors. A very tall, brown-skinned race that typically had wild orange hair, red eyes and a long dorsal fin on their backs, the Nejma were known as the "Honored Ones" by the rest of the School, and largely kept themselves isolated from wider Iskalonian society.
