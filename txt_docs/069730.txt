Battle of Borleias (holodocumentary)
929

Battle of Borleias was a holodocumentary about the Siege of Borleias during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Narrated by Wolam Tser and recorded by Tam Elgrin, the holo portrayed the forces under General Wedge Antilles&mdash;and the rest of the New Republic Defense Force in general&mdash;in a positive manner, boosting the sagging morale that the New Republic had experienced during the Battle of Coruscant. The holo was a huge success, and helped increase enlistment in the Defense Force. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way Category:Holodocumentaries
