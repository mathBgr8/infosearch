|published in=*Star Wars Insider 68 *Star Wars Insider 69 *Star Wars Insider 70 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=22 BBYThe Essential Reader's Companion'The New Essential Chronology, p. 67 (14:5 GrS)The New Essential Chronology, p. 67 places the events of the story 12 months after the First Battle of Geonosis, which is dated to 13:5 by The Essential Atlas, p. 149. |series= |preceded by=MedStar II: Jedi Healer |followed by=Yoda: Dark Rendezvous }} "Hero of Cartao" was a serialized short story written by Timothy Zahn and illustrated by Douglas Chaffee that appeared in Star Wars Insider magazine. It was separated into three parts: "Hero's Call" (Issue 68), "Hero's Rise" (Issue 69), and "Hero's End" (Issue 70). It featured such characters as Jafer Torles and the pre-existing Zahn character of Kinman Doriana. It took place in 22 BBY and was essentially the story of how the Spaarti cloning cylinders came to Wayland.
