|end date=June 11, 2008 |schedule= |format= |era= |timeline=0 ABY The date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series= |issues=4 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Rebellion: Small Victories was the third story arc in the Star Wars: Rebellion series of comics. After participating in several nearly disastrous missions, Deena Shan is ready to give up, go home, and leave the Rebellion to the real heroes. But instead, a failed hyperspace jump puts Deena, Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa and Tungo Li in orbit above the Imperial refueling station, Bannistar Station. Knowing that escape is futile, and discovery is imminent, they lead a crew on a suicide mission to strike against overwhelming odds, and destroy Bannistar Station.
