Fora Mardec
1132

Fora Mardec was a Human female who lived in Jalor City with her parents Riic and Lina, and a sister Sale. In 2 ABY, all of the Mardecs save Riic died in the crossfire when the Galactic Empire attacked a Rebel cell. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Females Category:Humans
