Grungeon Block
1202

Grungeon Block was a a twenty-square-kilometer area of The Works on Coruscant. It had once housed production centers for Serv-O-Droid, Inc., Hunvicko, and Nebula Manufacturing. The manufacturers declared bankruptcy and abandoned the Block. The Grungeon Block was first mentioned in Coruscant and the Core Worlds, which placed it in the Factory District. However, later sources moved the Grungeon Block and Sidious's hangar to the Works in the Dacho District, near the Senate District. *Labyrinth of Evil *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant districts Category:The Works locations
