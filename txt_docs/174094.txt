Ardees Rabang Jundis
1783

Ardees Rabang Jundis was an "assistant to controller" for Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and was one of two US Assistants to Rick McCallum during the production of Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones and Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. She later served as assistant to McCallum again for George Lucas' 2012 film Red Tails. The beverage ardees was named after her. *Star Wars Chronicles: The Prequels Category:Star Wars prequel trilogy production staff
