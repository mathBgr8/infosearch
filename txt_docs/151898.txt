Huberdink Bufflin III
1068

Huberdink Bufflin III was a male being and the husband of Milli Bufflin. Both natives of Corulag, the couple were vacationing on the world of Amfar during the growing Separatist Crisis. Due to the steep drop in tourism, the Bufflins found that they had many of the resorts facilities to themselves. Category:Corulag residents Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males
