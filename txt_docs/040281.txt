Gluss'elta Archipelago
734

The Gluss'elta Archipelago, also known as the Gluss'elta Islands, was an area on Kintan containing twelve islands and many rocky inlets that spanned four thousand kilometers of the Gluss'elta sea. The islands were home to the Pale Nikto, and also served as hideouts for smugglers, pirates and bounty hunters. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Islands Category:Kintan locations
