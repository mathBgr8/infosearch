thumb|right|250px|Luminara Unduli stands before Mondo-Mod in the Arena of Doom The Arena of Doom was a gladiator arena owned by Mondo-Mod the Hutt. During the Clone Wars it was shut down by Luminara Unduli and Barriss Offee after a winning ten million credit bet cleared the owners accounts, resulting in the closure. It had many different contestants including giant aliens and robots. One of the warriors at the arena was the battle droid Evil Supreme. Category:Gladiatorial arenas Category:Hutt locations
