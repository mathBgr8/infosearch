She commanded the Namana-class light cruiser Watchkeeper. During the First Corellian Insurrection, the Watchkeeper was one of four ships that penetrated the interdiction field encompassing the Corellian system using an experimental device called the hyperwave inertial momentum sustainer. Unfortunately, Captain Mantrony's vessel was damaged in the process and lost its propulsion systems.
