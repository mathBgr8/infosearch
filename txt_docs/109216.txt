Rogue Alpha

Rogue Alpha was the callsign used by pilots who were temporarily added to Rogue Squadron when the squadron was already full. Nrin Vakil held the position when he flew a T-65R X-wing reconnaissance starfighter with the Rogues in the campaign against Delak Krennel.
