Cemas
1572

The Cemas were a species of sentient beings that had feet. At least one member of the species had entered the galactic community by 17 ABY. That year,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Rebellion to 17 ABY. the smuggler Han Solo helped a Cemas to its feet in the Crystal Jewel casino, on the planet Coruscant, after a bomb went off in the New Republic Senate Hall nearby.
