thumb|right|250px|Mar Dunn laying [[Mine Type A|mines around Escort Carrier Hininbirg.]] The Mar Duun was a CR90 corvette used by the Rebel alliance. The Mar Dunn, along with the Vop Hui, attempted to disrupt a meeting involving TIE Defender prototypes being transferred to the Escort Carrier Hininbirg by deploying mines around the transfer point.
