|end date=April 9, 2003 |schedule= |format= |era=Rise of the Empire era |timeline=22 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion (10 weeks ABG) |series=Star Wars: Republic |issues=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Republic: The New Face of War was the thirteenth story arc in Star Wars: Republic. Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi, as well as Master Glaive and his Padawan Zule Xiss and the ARC trooper Alpha-17 were sent to the moon of Ohma-D'un, over Naboo, as they were led to believe Spices Miners had just attacked a Gungan colony. But when they arrive near the colony, the Jedi discover that the Separatists have used the Gungans as an test, with a lethal new gas produced by Jenna Zan ArborIn author James Luceno's 2005 novel Labyrinth of Evil, Jenna Zan Arbor was identified as the crazed Separatist scientist who created the lethal swamp gas used on the Gungans on Ohma-D'un at the start of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY. Zan Arbor's character was originally created by author Jude Watson in her Jedi Apprentice and Jedi Quest series published by Scholastic. on Queyta. The Separatists plan on crashing a ship filled with the gas onto the moon, to frame the miners for the atrocity. When the Jedi and clone arrive, they find a contingent of droids. They destroy all the droids, but when they assist the miners, two unknown characters arrive: the Gen'Dai bounty hunter Durge, who wants to kill all the clones because they are offsprings of a Mandalorian (and he just hates Mandalorians), and the Sith apprentice Asajj Ventress. Ventress kills Glaive while Anakin and Alpha are helping the miners, but in the end Obi-Wan and Zule defeat the two enemies, who flee from the moon, and Anakin and Alpha destroy the ship the Separatists want to crash on the planet.
