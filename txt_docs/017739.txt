|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=c. 10 ABYStar Wars Omnibus: Boba Fett |series=Star Wars: Boba Fett |issue=One-shot }} Boba Fett: Agent of Doom is a comic written by John Ostrander and illustrated by Cam Kennedy. It was released on November 8, 2000 by Dark Horse Comics as part of the Boba Fett series of one-shot comics. Some jobs are too much of a risk for even the best bounty hunters&mdash;but that never stopped Boba Fett! The Imperial "Death Ship" Azgoghk, designed for the sole purpose of exterminating alien species, continues its duties long after the Empire has fallen apart. Now it's time for justice and REVENGE. When the last member of a dying species seeks to exact revenge on the Azgoghk and its crew, there's only one man he can turn to&mdash;BOBA FETT! Slique Brighteyes, the last survivor of the Gulmarid species, wants revenge on the commanders of the Imperial ship Azgoghk, namely Admiral Mir Tork and Doctor Leonis Murthé, for capturing members of the species and making them suffer until their extinction. He hires Boba Fett for the job, not only for justice and the small fee offered, but so the bounty hunter can prove that he is still the best in the galaxy. The Emperor is dead, but Tork and Murthé are at it again, finding species to exterminate, and Fett catches up with them on Malicar 3.
