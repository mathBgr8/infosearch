Dex (Peace Brigade)
1053

Dex was a Corellian member of the Peace Brigade who tried, with Roxi Barl and Gad Sluggins, to kill Jedi Master Eelysa on Corellia, where he ran into Leia Organa Solo in 26 ABY.
