Dar Bille
1611

Dar Bille was a male Yevethan commander. During Nil Spaar's operation to seize Black Sword Command from the Galactic Empire in 4 ABY, Dar Bille was the future Viceroy's second-in-command. Dar Bille led Spaar's backup assault team through Section 21 in the seizure of the Super Star Destroyer Intimidator.
