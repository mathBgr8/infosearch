|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Save the Mantaris!" is a Wizards of the Coast web supplement written by Steve Miller. It is a side adventure based on the sourcebook Secrets of Naboo, where the players investigate sabotage on the Mantaris voyage. Following the invasion of Naboo, there is a new era of cooperation between the Naboo and the Gungans. They are launching the Mantaris transport to colonize the moons, but there are factions that want to prevent that.
