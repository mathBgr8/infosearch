|type=Hardback |pages=208 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0761511768 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire: The Official Strategy Guide was a strategy guide published by Prima's Secrets of the Games for both the PC and Nintendo 64 versions of Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire. With Shadows of the Empire: The Official Strategy Guide, you'll learn how to defeat Boba Fett and IG-88, blast Imperial baddies, and get the secrets you'll need to ultimately destroy Prince Xizor and his Skyhook, including: *All the locations of the secret Rebel challenge points *Step-by-step walkthroughs for every mission *Jet-packed flight skills that will propel you to victory *Maps for selected levels Official Strategy Guide, Shadows of the Empire Shadows of the Empire: The Official Strategy Guide
