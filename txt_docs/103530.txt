Nil Posondum
1525

Nil Posondum was a former head accountant for Trans-Galactic Gaming Enterprises Corporation and a thief who found a bounty of 12,500 credits on his head from the Hutts after switching allegiances and stealing private data. He escaped Bossk and Zuckuss, only to be caught and transferred through Boba Fett via Kud'ar Mub'at.
