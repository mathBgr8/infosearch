Jaren's planet
1425

Jaren's planet was a habitable world in the Outer Rim Territories near Molavar. It was home to hoars and was sparsely populated with such Human settlers as Ament and Jaren, but had at least one spaceport. The local police force rode swoop bikes. During his hunt for Lycan, Darca Nyl crashed onto the world around 31.5 BBY. The world was unique in that it was considered to be alive and telepathic. It amplified people's thoughts emotions and could grant people on its surface visions of their past when they opened their minds to it.
