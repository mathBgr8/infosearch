Imperial pilot (disambiguation)
914

Imperial pilot may refer to: *Imperial Starfighter Pilot &mdash; pilots that flew TIE/ln space superiority starfighters for the Galactic Empire. *Imperial pilots &mdash; pilots of the reconstituted Sith Empire. *TIE pilots &mdash; pilots that flew TIE/LN starfighters for the Galactic Empire. Notable examples include Soontir Fel and Tycho Celchu. *Shuttle pilots &mdash; pilots who flew shuttles like the Lambda-class T-4a shuttle for the Galactic Empire. *Vehicle Command Crewers &mdash; pilots of land vehicles such as AT-ATs for the Galactic Empire.
