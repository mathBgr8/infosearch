Deluvian fatworm
3997

|habitat= |diet=Parasitic |language= }} The Deluvian fatworm was a type of worm that hailed from the Deluvia system, a part of the Outer Rim Territories' Klina sector that was situated close to Hutt Space. places the Deluvia system in grid square S-13 as part of the Klina sector, which and The Essential Atlas both depict as situated near Hutt Space. It led a parasitic lifestyle, attaching itself to the back of members of the sentient Hutt species. In 3956 BBY,The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic to 3956 BBY. Jedi Padawan Revan visited the Mid RimThe Essential Atlas planet Kashyyyk. There, he met the merchant Eli Gand and his indentured servant Matton Dasol. When Gand attempted to interest Revan in his wares, Dasol warned the latter that the interest rates of the former's loans would "drain" him in a manner comparable to a Deluvian fatworm parasiting on the back of a Hutt. In response, Gand, who took offense at the comparison, claimed that the Hutts were, in fact, "ALL backside." The Deluvian fatworm was introduced in the 2003 BioWare video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. In the game, the creature can be mentioned in an optional conversation the player character, Revan, can have with Eli Gand and Matton Dasol.
