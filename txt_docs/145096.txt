Battle of Exocron
2442

The Battle of Exocron was a battle fought between Rei'Kas and his pirate fleet against the Exocron Combined Air-Space Fleet, the Aing-Tii and the Wild Karrde. The battle started and ended after the pirate fleet chased the fleeing Exocron fleet too close to the world, causing them to be wiped out to the very last by the Aing-Tii monks and their ships. *Vision of the Future *Vision of the Future abridged audiobook Category:Battles of Exocron Category:Battles of the Hunt for Jorj Car'das
