Even with Republic reinforcements, the Basiliskans were outmatched and poisoned their own planet in a last ditch effort to drive away the invaders. thumb|left|170px|The planet Basilisk after its devastation. The Mandalorians abandoned the planet, but succeeded in capturing a large group of Basiliskans. These creatures would later become the Lagartoz War Dragons. The battle claimed the life of Jedi Master Sidrona Diath. The Mandalorians would eventually move from Basilisk to the planet of Kuar, where Mandalore the Indomitable later dueled the Jedi Ulic Qel-Droma. The Essential Atlas placed the invasion of Basilisk in 3997 BBY, during the Great Sith War but The Essential Guide to Warfare shifted it 20 years earlier, in 4,017 BBY.
