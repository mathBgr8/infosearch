Hutt Guardsman's Guild
943

The Hutt Guardsman's Guild was a society of bodyguards that served the various Hutt Lords. In 4 ABY, with the aid of Quaffug, Lando Calrissian joined this group, under the alias Tamtel Skreej in order to infiltrate Jabba Desilijic Tiure's organization in preparation for the rescue of Han Solo. *The Bounty Hunters: Scoundrel's Wages *Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters *The New Essential Guide to Characters *Star Wars: The Comics Companion Category:Commerce and labor guilds and unions Category:Hutt organizations
