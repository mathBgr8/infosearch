Torpris
1424

Torpris was a planet located within the Torpris system of the Core Worlds. It was a planet of moderate urbanization and housed the slaving consortium headquarters of the Drach'nam Chay Praysh. It had a decent number of large cities, and some of which (those under Praysh's control) were poverty-ridden. A dozen or more different non-native species inhabited the planet. *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in the Core Worlds Category:Uoti sector locations
