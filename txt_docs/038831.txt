Kare Fontin
1514

Kare Fontin was a male Senator of the Galactic Republic and a diplomat of the New Republic. Kare Fontin served as a Senator in the Galactic Senate during the time of the Galactic Republic. During the reign of the New Republic, Fontin was over 100 years old, and operated as a diplomat. Although his body was frail and his hearing impaired, Fontin's mind and wit were intact.
