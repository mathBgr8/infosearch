Sa-Di
2138

Sa-Di was a male Human Dark Side Adept and an advisor to the reborn Emperor Palpatine. Following the destruction of the Emperor's cloning tanks, Sa-Di believed Palpatine to be gone for good, and declared himself the ruler of Byss.
