She was the captain of her own starship, which her fellow Ensos crewed, and she used the vessel to ship hydroponics around the galaxy, including the planet Tatooine. Teyora Rekab hailed from the planet Ensolica&mdash;a cold world covered with tundra and ice. Like the rest of her people, she became an expert on the matter of plant hydroponics. Rekab was the captain of a starship, crewed by fellow Ensos. She and her crew made their living by selling hydroponics equipment and seeds. They regularly visited the arid world of Tatooine, where their customers were the local moisture farmers. While visiting the world, Rekab and her crew were forced to don rented coolth suits&mdash;the biological makeup of the Ensos made them abhorrent to the heat of Tatooine, and if exposed to the planet's twin suns, they would be susceptible to heat stroke. In 32 BBY,According to The New Essential Chronology the events of take place in 32 BBY. Rekab was present in Mos Espa around the time of Boonta's Eve. She later attended the annual Boonta Eve Classic Podrace at the Mos Espa Grand Arena on Tatooine. Rekab watched as the Dug Podracer Sebulba completed the first of three scheduled laps, and at least at one point, she also removed her helmet. Considered to be odd-looking, Teyora Rekab was a female Enso, a species of Near-Humans. Rekab had blonde hair, blue eyes and light skin. In addition to providing herself and her crew with hired protective coolth suits, Rekab had the foresight to wear polarized goggles while visiting Tatooine, as the suit's bubble did not protect against the glare of the planet's twin suns. Teyora Rekab first appeared in the 1999 prequel trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, as an unnamed background extra in Mos Espa.
