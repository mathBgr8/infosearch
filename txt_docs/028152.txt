Saarai-kaar
1296

thumb|250px|The Saarai-kaar The Saarai-kaar was the sole master of the Jensaarai order of Force-usersThe Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia. The term Saarai-kaar meant "keeper of the truth" in the Sith. Like the Dark Lords of the Sith, they believed there could only be one Saarai-kaar, who would wear the same cortosis-covered armor that their Jensaarai brethren would. The first Saarai-kaar was Nikkos Tyris. After his death, he was succeeded by one of his disciples, the wife of an apprentice slain during an attack by the Jedi. Her name was not recorded.
