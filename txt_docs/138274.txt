Thruncan Insurrection
1254

The Thruncan Insurrection, also known as the Thruncon Insurrection, was a rebellion that took place prior to 12 BBY.According to the 1995 sourcebook Alliance Intelligence Reports, Xenon Nnaksta graduated from the Galactic Outdoor Survival School in around 12 BBY. Instigated by Vodrans living in the city of Thruncan, on the world of Vodran against the Imperial presence there, the results of the insurrection were unclear.
