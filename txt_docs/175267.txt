"Shape-shifters" was an article for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game that was published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 12. It was written by Pablo Hidalgo and illustrated by Steve Bryant, and provided details on four shapeshifting species: the Proteans, Polydroxol, Stennes Shifters, and Shi'ido. *Star Wars Adventure
