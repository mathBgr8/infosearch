Umboo Racing Team
1770

The Umboo Racing Team was a team for the Roon Colonial Games lead by Auren Yomm. It was consisted of Yomm, her friend Gee Long, and the droid BIX. They specialized in the Drainsweeper event. When Governor Bisad Koong poisoned Long and sabotaged BIX one year during the games, they were replaced by Mungo Baobab and C-3PO that year. *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded Category:Sporting teams
