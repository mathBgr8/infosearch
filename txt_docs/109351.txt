Ripoblus government
2988

The Ripoblus government was the ruling body of the planet Ripoblus and its people. The Ripoblus government opposed the Dimok government in the Sepan Civil War until the Galactic Empire intervened in 3 ABY.The New Essential Chronology thumb|left|255px|The Galactic Empire [[Battle of Sepan 8|hoped to capture the leaders of Dimok and Ripoblus near Sepan 8]] The Ripoblus possessed a sizeable fleet, consisting of BTL Y-wing starfighters, Z-95 Headhunters, Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttles and Lambda-class T-4a shuttles. For capital ships, they used EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates.
