Goat (Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport)
625

Goat was the name of a group of two Rebel Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports that carried Rebel engineers and workers away from Yavin 4. The Red Squadron oversaw their exit from the Imperial blockade. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
