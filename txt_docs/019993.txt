Chordokeylo

The chordokeylo was a musical instrument that could be modified by inserting datacards that each allowed it to play different kinds of music. In 19 ABY Talon Karrde brought Ettian tonal card to Valin Horn as a birthday present. *Specter of the Past Category:Musical instruments
