Dagbat
1991

Dagbats, also known as Dagles, were a species of winged creatures native to Dagobah. Dagbats were first seen in 1991's The Empire Strikes Back video game, and named in the game's manual. A different, more elaborate version of the creature was seen in Super Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back.
