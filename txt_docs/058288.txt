Super-vaporator
1244

The super-vaporator was a highly experimental version of the standard moisture vaporator used to wrench moisture from Tatooine's air. Captain Egoreg was in charge of an effort to enrich Tatooine with moisture in the midst of a severe drought. To that end, the team began building a super-vaporator. Due to an untimely Jawa raid, the project was temporarily delayed. *The Mystery of the Rebellious Robot Category:Agricultural technology
