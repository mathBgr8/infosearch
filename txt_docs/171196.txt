Guardian (Tendrando Arms)
752

Guardians were battle droids produced by Tendrando Arms. An improved version of the Defender Droid, it had thick weapons-equipped arms, gray laminanium armor, higher than military standard mag-shielding, and a downward-pointing vocabulator. Without proper authorization, the droid could not be shut down. One was used to keep Cal Omas under house arrest. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Battle droid models Category:Tendrando Arms products
