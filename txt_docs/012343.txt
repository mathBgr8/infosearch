Dozen Rules
794

The "Dozen Rules" were a code of conduct created by Urias Xhaxin and followed by the crew of the privateering ship Free Lance, and was seen as typical of a privateer's code of conduct. The Rules included: *Keep mobile, be flexible *Never pick a fight on someone your own size, and don't fight fair *Never hunt in your own backyard *Gamble *Make a name for yourself *Stick to the shadows *Stick to the job *Plan obsessively, but allow for the unexpected *There are no sure things *The prize's crew is not the enemy *Maintain initiative *Keep your crew well *Pirates & Privateers Category:Doctrines Category:Piracy and privateering Category:Privateering
