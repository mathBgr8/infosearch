Arapia
2301

Arapia was the Kadri'Ra adoptive homeworld. It was located in the Arapia system. During the years of the Galactic Republic, Arapia had more than one hundred million Kadri'Ra individuals. The Galactic Empire decided that Kadri'Ra were not sentients and, due to their slavery and other measures, reduced the number to 14,000 individuals as of 0 BBY. Crime in Arapia was controlled by the Kadri'Ra crimelord Saadoon-Kauldi, originally a native of Arapia who left the system after one assassination attempt.
