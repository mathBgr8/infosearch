Tonqu flower
1723

Tonqu flowers were a type of flower known to the extra-galactic Yuuzhan Vong species as of 29 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion They used their sweet scent to lure insects to alight upon them, before trapping them there as the tonqu flower's long petal rolled up. In 29 ABY, former Yuuzhan Vong Executor Nom Anor compared the sentient planet Zonama Sekot to a tonqu flower, as it attracted beings and did not let them go. The tonqu flower's only mention was in 2003's The Final Prophecy, written by Greg Keyes as part of The New Jedi Order series. Though the flower's effect suggests a carnivorous nature, no source confirms this, so this article does not treat the flower as such. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy Category:Flowers Category:Species of undefined sentience
