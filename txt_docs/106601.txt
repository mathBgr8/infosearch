Raggold
2004

Raggold was as human male who served as one of the leaders of the Solay resistance against King Blackart, spending 50 years of his life fighting Blackart's tyrannical rule. He and another freedom fighter named Mary unsuccessfully led the resistance until the arrival of Jedi Knight Luke Skywalker, hero of the Rebel Alliance, who led the rebellion to overthrow Blackart and briefly seize control of Solay. However, Raggold betrayed the planet to the Galactic Empire, and consumed by guilt over his betrayal, committed suicide.
