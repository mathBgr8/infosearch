Millennium Falcon (fake)
3330

A fake Millennium Falcon was used by a team of Noghri Death Commandos on Bpfassh in 9 ABY in an attempt to capture Leia Organa Solo and her unborn twins for the Dark Jedi Joruus C'baoth. In their continuing efforts to kidnap her, the Empire, under Grand Admiral Thrawn, had managed to find another working YT-1300, using it in an attempt to trick the Solos into coming aboard and into the hands of their would-be captors. The deception was realized when Organa Solo noticed that the Millennium Falcon was not using its underside swivel blaster and that she could not sense the presence of Chewbacca inside. In order to drive off the kidnappers, Han Solo used his wife's lightsaber to disable the ship by cutting the coolant lines to its main reactor.
