The Empire Strikes Back Weekly 118
1391

The Empire Strikes Back Weekly 118 is the one hundred eighteenth issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Weekly series of comic reprints, published on May 29, 1980. Starting with this issue, the series' title was changed from Star Wars Weekly as the adaptation of Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back began. The numbering continued from the previous title, though this issue released on Thursday, unlike the prior and subsequent issues. *Star Wars (1977) 39 *"Heroes of the galaxy: Man-Wolf Part 5: Crash landing on the moon!" *"Tales of the Watcher: The impossible spaceship!"
