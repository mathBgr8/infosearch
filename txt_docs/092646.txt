|result=Dark Empire victory |side1=Dark Empire |side2=New Republic |side3= |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2=*Captain Tekba&dagger; *Green Leader |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |forces1=Galaxy Gun |forces2=*Pelagia *T-65 X-wing starfighters |forces3= |forces4= |casual1=None |casual2=Pelagia and all 100,000 troops aboard |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} The Destruction of the Pelagia was a brief battle between the New Republic troopship Pelagia and a missile, shot from the superweapon Galaxy Gun. Having received word of impending attack by the dreaded cannon, Captain Tekba ordered a full defense of his ship, as it was incapable of escaping the missile in the ten minute time-span given. Pelagias complement of X-wings and defensive guns bombarded the missile the moment it exited hyperspace, but it was all for naught. The barrage failed to penetrate the missile's extensive shielding, and it hit the troopship, destroying it and killing all aboard. Back at Byss, Palpatine reveled in yet another victory.
