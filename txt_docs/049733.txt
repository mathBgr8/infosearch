Dagga Marl
3418

Dagga Marl was the bodyguard of Thrackan Sal-Solo during his brief time as President of the Ylesian Republic. Sal-Solo chose her for her skill&mdash;something his official guard lacked in abundance&mdash;and her mercenary nature. In exchange for one kilogram of glitterstim&mdash;which had virtually replaced other currency on Ylesia&mdash;every week, plus a bonus if he required her to kill someone, Marl kept a close eye on Sal-Solo, which she considered a challenge. Earlier in her career, she had worked as an assassin for the Ylesian Senate, and as a bounty hunter.The New Jedi Order: Ylesia Marl and Sal-Solo were both captured by the New Republic at the Battle of Ylesia in 28 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of The New Jedi Order: Ylesia, which includes the Battle of Ylesia and Thrackan Sal-Solo's use of "Dagga Farglblag," to 28 ABY. Assuming the mantle of Ludus Fazum, the Peace Brigade leader claimed himself and his companion to be a couple who were refugees from the Mid Rim worldThe Essential Atlas&mdash;Based on corresponding data for Falleen system of Falleen that were taken to the Hutt Space worldThe Essential Atlas of Ylesia to work as house slaves; Sal-Solo had, in fact, been appointed President of Ylesia by the invading Yuuzhan Vong. The ruse failed when Sal-Solo's cousin and Jedi Knight Jaina Solo arrived on the scene and arrested Sal-Solo and Marl.
