Hume (officer)
3249

Hume was a Human male officer and pilot who served in the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. During the Clone Wars, Hume was an officer and pilotThe Last of the Jedi: Death on Naboo in the Galactic Republic. After the Galactic Empire was formed by Emperor Palpatine in 19 BBY, Hume was among the others that stood against the Empire and erased all records of himself when he joined the Erased. He travelled with Ferus Olin, Trever Flume and other members of the Erased to the place of Solace for protection in anticipation of an attack against the Erased from the Empire. They eventually reached the place and learned that Solace was not a place, but a person. During a subsequent attack, Hume died trying to save a group of people that were surrounded by stormtroopers that were attacking the place of Solace. Hume first appeared in the Last of the Jedi: Underworld. There is a discrepancy between Underworld and the Last of the Jedi: Death on Naboo. In Underworld Hume is described as being a officer in the Republic Army who was not a clone by Dexter Jettster, while in Death on Naboo, Hume is described as having been a pilot in the Republic Army.
