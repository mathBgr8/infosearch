Diva
878

Divas were a religious order of female Theelin singers. Toward the final years of the Galactic Republic, most Theelin females were given the title of Diva, regardless of their vocal talents in order to preserve the culture of a dwindling population. *"Podracing Tales" *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Religious organizations
