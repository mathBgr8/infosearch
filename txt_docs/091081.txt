Invisible Star
1468

Invisible Star was a modified Action V transport owned by Qual'om Soach, a Twi'lek crime lord in the Brak sector. Soach used the transport as his mobile base of operations. While served as his home it also was used to make both legal and black market runs. The ship was equipped various transponder overlays and extra legal and forged credentials.
