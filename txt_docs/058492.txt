|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=1 }} Classic Star Wars 6 is the sixth issue of the Classic Star Wars series, published by Dark Horse Comics. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson from the early 1980s. Williamson supplied new panels and art to flow the stories for traditional comic format. Pages 1 to 6 reprint the end of the The Serpent Masters comic strip, while page 6 appears to be new panels and art. Pages 7 and onwards reprint the beginning of the Deadly Reunion comic strip. After his adventures with Tanith Shire and the Serpent Masters, Luke rejoins Leia on Kabal just as the planet falls under Imperial attack.
