Unidentified child (Coruscant)
1267

A child lived in the slums of Coruscant in 27 BBY. Spiz's hoodlums chased the child with intent to kill for no apparent reason, calling their victim "brat" until they were scared off by a rancor illusion created by Jedi Master Yarael Poof.
