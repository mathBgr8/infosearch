Jedi Guardians
7478

|figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Battle of Yavin |followed by=The Empire Strikes Back }} Jedi Guardians was the fifth expansion card pack made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game produced by Wizards of the Coast, based on the film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, though also containing elements from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and Expanded Universe material set during this timeframe. The Expansion icon was that of the Republic. The expansion was released in July 7, 2003 and contains 105 cards including 35 rare,35 uncommon and 35 common cards. New abilities: *Overkill - Hits in excess of the defending unit's health may be allocated to another unit in the same arena. *Reserves - Actions that can be performed while the unit is still in your build zone.
