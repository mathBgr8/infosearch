Brast'alshi'barku
2183

Brast'alshi'barku, also known by his core name of Talshib, was a Chiss male of the Brast family who held the rank of Captain in the Chiss Expansionary Defense Force. In 22 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion he served aboard the diplomatic ship Chaf Envoy as a line commander. He wore green facings on his black uniform, whereas his superior General Drask bore red trim.
