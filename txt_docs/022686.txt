Findos served as a lieutenant with the Separatist Droid Army during the siege of Cato Neimoidia, where he gave an order to send more B1-series battle droids against Jedi Master Kit Fisto. Findos later became acting Viceroy of the Trade Federation immediately after the deaths of Nute Gunray, Rune Haako, and Rute Gunnay on Mustafar. After a few days of resisting the Empire, the viceroy was reluctantly forced by stormtroopers on Neimoidia to sign a treaty granting the new self-appointed Emperor Palpatine all of the Trade Federation's resources following the end of the Clone Wars in 19 BBY. He was then escorted to an unknown location by a squad of stormtroopers.
