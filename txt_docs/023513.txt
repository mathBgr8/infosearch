During the Separatist Crisis, he was a member of the Loyalist Committee. However, fellow senator Onaconda Farr accused him of holding ties to the Confederacy, and after a Judicial Department investigation confirmed it, Looruya was censured and removed from the Senate, with Farr taking his place on the Committee.
