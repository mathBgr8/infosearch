Bulano serpent
608

The Bulano serpent was a snake that had no natural defenses at all. However, it had the ability to inflate itself to make it seem that it was five times larger than it actually was, and as a result few predators attacked it. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Snakes Category:Species of undefined sentience
