Mandalorian dungeon ship
2225

The Mandalorian dungeon ship was a wartime transport used by various Mandalorian organizations. The Mandalorian dungeon ships were used by the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders during the Mandalorian Wars. The class was used as a basis for the Lictor-class dungeon ship thousands of years after the conflict ended.
