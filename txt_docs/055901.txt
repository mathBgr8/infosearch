Diputs group
1239

Diputs was a group of two BFF-1 bulk freighters used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Alliance Intelligence discovered that Imperial ships were using the base at Kalla VII for supplies and repairs on their way to the first Death Star. General Jan Dodonna proposed that if the base was destroyed, any passing ships heading for the Death Star would be stranded there, leaving them open to capture. Diputs group arrived at the repair dock while it was being destroyed by the Rebel X-wings of Red Squadron.
