Rak (substance)
1082

Rak was a substance that the gang boss Cherit supplied to Kuati nobles before his death sometime around the year 40 ABY. Afterward, a man named L. Fraig continued Cherit's operation. Rak was briefly mentioned in 2007's Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice, a novel by Karen Traviss. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Substances
