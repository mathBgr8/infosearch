|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYDate established in narrative text. |series=Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand |issue=5 }} Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 5 was the fifth issue in the Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand series of comics. It was released on December 16, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. Fed up with being hunted by her enemies, Mara Jade decides it's time to become the hunter. First on her payback list: the evil Dequc, Black Nebula crime lord&mdash;the man who survived her final assignment for the Emperor! Now, Mara must infiltrate his hidden base, take him down, and escape! Could she finally be in over her head?
