Misanthrope
1297

The Misanthrope was a cargo ship that crash-landed on D'vouran, a planet that devoured anything it came in contact with. The Misanthrope was captained by Bebo, who was, with Lonni and eighteen other people, a survivor of the encounter. *Galaxy of Fear: Eaten Alive *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Transports
