Meditation of Immersion
634

Meditation of Immersion was a Fallanassi Force technique that stilled the mental and physical state of the Force-user who performed it to a profound level. This allowed the user to attain an almost direct contact with the White Current. In this state, the Fallanassi could become completely invisible, or project images around them with vivid clarity. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Meditation
