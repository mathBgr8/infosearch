Patricia Wynne
2697

|companies= |website= }} Patricia Wynne is a freelance artist and illustrator with a career of over forty-five years. Specializing in scientific illustration, she has illustrated numerous scientific papers in collaboration with various scientists. She has also illustrated a number of children's books, including seven Star Wars storybooks published between 1978 and 1980. The children's book Hello, Bumblebee Bat, which Wynne illustrated, was a Theodor Seuss Geisel Award Honor book. As the artist-in-residence at the Department of Mammalogy of the American Museum of Natural History for over forty years, Wynne was honored as the namesake of a new species of bat in 2014&mdash;Thyroptera wynneae, or "Patricia's disk-winged bat." *Star Wars: Punch Out and Make It Book (1978) *Artoo Detoo's Activity Book (1979) *Chewbacca's Activity Book (1979) *Darth Vader's Activity Book (1979) *Luke Skywalker's Activity Book (1979) *The Wookiee Storybook (1979) *The Empire Strikes Back: A Pop-Up Book (1980) Category:Cover artists
