Kim Klib
1074

Kim Klib was a young man who lived in Gadrin. His day job was as a messenger and stockboy for one of the local emporiums, but on the side he was an underworld mechanic. He was developing contacts in the underworld of Gadrin and Hedrett. He enjoyed stealing and stole from his employer, but was overconfident in his abilities.
