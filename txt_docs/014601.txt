It was a source of ulikuo gemstones. It was a damp mud-ball riddled with caves. Zorba Desilijic Tiure was imprisoned on Kip in 22 BBY, shortly before the Clone Wars. After the Battle of Endor, in 5 ABY, Ysanne Isard arranged for pirates to take over Kip and free Zorba.
