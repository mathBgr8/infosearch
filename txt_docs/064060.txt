Vlassy Nature Preserve
1144

The Vlassy Nature Preserve was a secluded wildlife preserve, located along the Vlassy River on Garqi. Located eight hundred kilometers southeast of Pesktda, the Preserve was a popular destination for Garqians and off-world tourists alike. Due to the Preserve's forested bluffs and canyons, the region was deemed unsuitable for farming, and has been a nature preserve since the planet's founding. At some point, the Preserve's rdava-birds were dying off due to a nerve agent released by Chine Gabbard's research station.
