A Force-sensitive Human, Slatka and her brother Sladak were native to a seafaring world without forests. In 1000 BBY, the siblings were recruited into the Jedi Order's Army of Light by Jedi Knight Torr Snapit to augment the Army's forces on Ruusan. While descending through Ruusan's atmosphere, Snapit's ship was attacked by Sith buzzards of the Brotherhood of Darkness and ultimately destroyed.
