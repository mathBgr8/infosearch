LSS 1000 Automatic Stun Cuffs
584

The LSS 1000 Automatic Stun cuffs were a pair of modified handcuffs used during the period following the Yuuzhan Vong war. They were known as a more sophisticated version of the LSS 401 Stun Cuffs. Han and Leia Organa Solo kept a pair of these powerful stun cuffs aboard the Millennium Falcon. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen Category:Restraining devices
