Swyy'm-Ee wielded twin fi'langs against the other combatants but was later defeated by assassin Asajj Ventress. Swyy'm-Ee was a reptilian Trogodile from the harsh world of Rattatak. Shortly after the outbreak of the Clone Wars,The New Essential Chronology Swyy'm-Ee ended up in the Cauldron arena. It was the largest gladiatorial pit on the planet, filled with violent warriors, where war barons and generals sought out the best combatants who would win their wars. Therefore, Swyy'm-Ee, like many other gladiators, entered a deadly free-for-all battle,The New Essential Guide to Droids but it was a rare gladiator indeed who could survive multiple fights there. 280px|left|thumb|Swyy'm-Ee and the other gladiators preparing to attack Asajj Ventress During one battle, the Sith Lord Count Dooku attended the match, hoping to find a new apprentice among the fighters. Swyy'm-Ee fought Flalios when the Dathomirian Asajj Ventress entered the arena and, in a short time, defeated most of the remaining gladiators. The survivors united for a single attack against Ventress, but she used the the Force to crush stone debris from the ceiling onto their heads. Swyy'm-Ee belonged to a predatory reptilian species and fought using their traditional crooked blades called fi'lang. During the fight in the Cauldron, Swyy'm-Ee wore a red dress. Swyy'm-Ee first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003. The character was later given a name in Ultimate Sticker Book: Star Wars: Clone Wars, a sticker book written by Simon Beecroft and released in 2004. In the episode, the death of the character was depicted twice in a mere few seconds. The first time, Swyy'm-Ee was shown to be crushed by a falling piece of ceiling right after Anchor Blue.
