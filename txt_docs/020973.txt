Alderaanian War Frigate
2623

The Alderaanian War Frigate was a warship that served the Royal Alderaan Civil Fleet during the Clone Wars. Later models of this type appeared in service of the Alderaanian Resistance, the Rebel Alliance, the Alliance of Free Planets, and the New Republic. It was the pride of Alderaan's fleet and the most powerful ship of its time.
