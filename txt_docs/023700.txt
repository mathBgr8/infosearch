Ord Dorlass was a terrestrial world located in the Ord Dorlass system, a part of the Iskin sector in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Mid Rim. It lay at a distance of no more than seven days' hyperspace travel with a Class 1 hyperdrive from both the Outer Rim Territories celestial body Dahvil and the Expansion Region astronomical object Tintel. Ord Dorlass served as an Ordnance/Regional Depot outpost for the Galactic Republic during the Pius Dea Era, a period of time that lasted between approximately 12,000 BBY and 11,000 BBY, despite it being located outside the borders of Republic space. When the Republic launched the Twelfth Pius Dea Crusade against the Teirasan and Zarracine species in 11,660 BBY, Republic forces advanced through Ord Dorlass toward the Zarracina system. During the Galactic Civil War, Ord Dorlass served as the first of the three mid-race stopping points of the popular annual Star Rally interstellar racing route known as the Dahvil-Fodro Hyperspace Promenade. At one point, the racer and undercover Rebel Alliance operative Siro Simito was tasked by the Alliance to deliver a data module from Dahvil to a contact on the Mid Rim astronomical object Fait d'Fait while participating in the Dahvil-Fodro Hyperspace Promenade. Similarly to other such missions, the Alliance arranged for the agent Kaini to hand the data module to Simito. On Dahvil, the starting point of the race, the former agent posed as a holovid reporter from Ord Dorlass who pretended to interview Simito about his feud with the fellow racer Seeg. As part of the Rebels' pre-arranged act, Simito&mdash;pretending to lash out in annoyance at the repeated mentioning of his rival&mdash;used his vibro-ax to slice off the supertelephoto lens of Kaini's cam droid, and the racer subsequently picked up the casing of the lens, which contained the hidden data module. After the Dahvil-Fodro Hyperspace Promenade began, Simito intentionally decreased the hyperdrive efficiency of his starship on the first legs of the race's route so that he would not later make a suspicious delay on the segment of the route where the racer planned to make a detour to Fait d'Fait. During a stop on Ord Dorlass, Simito and Seeg staged for publicity purposes a fistfight at a nightclub in the world's capital city. In the process, the latter accidentally broke the former's nose, and Simito also threw Seeg over the establishment's bar. On the next morning, after the two racers were relased from the local jail they had been incarcerated at, the Star Rally promoter Pann Tefilous notified them that their popularity among the audience on Ord Dorlass had risen by six points. At some point by the time that shortly followed the Battle of Yavin, Rebel Alliance General Airen Cracken included Simito's report, which mentioned the racer's visit to Ord Dorlass during the Dahvil-Fodro Hyperspace Promenade, in a datafile detailing various Rebel operatives. Ord Dorlass was a suitably colonized world with active entertainment media. The Human Rebel Alliance agent Kaini once posed as a reporter forming one half&mdash;complemented by her cam droid&mdash;of a holovid reporting crew from Ord Dorlass. The capital city of Ord Dorlass was a cosmopolitan settlement that featured multiple nightclubs as well as a jail. Ord Dorlass was created by Chuck Truett and was introduced in Cracken's Rebel Operatives, a 1994 West End Games sourcebook published for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
