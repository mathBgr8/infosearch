Golan Space Colony 3
1823

The Golan Space Colony 3, commonly known as Space Colony 3, was manufactured by Golan Arms. Its predecessor was the Golan Space Colony 2. It was becoming quite popular throughout the galaxy around the time of the Battle of Endor.
