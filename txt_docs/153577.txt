|type=Paperback |pages=96 pages |dimensions=10.9 x 8.5 x 0.3 inches |weight=10.6 ounces |isbn=0761533052 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Starfighter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide was a strategy book for Star Wars: Starfighter by Prima Publishing and was released on February 21, 2001. The guide detailed coverage of the game. It contained detailed maps, including every pilot and their ship to assist you throughout the game. Also included were comprehensive dogfighting techniques for exploiting weaknesses. Starfighter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide
