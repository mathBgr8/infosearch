Eermik
1090

Eermik was a male Bith bounty hunter who was among the group who agreed to help Risso Nu eliminate Boba Fett on Ma'ar Shaddam. Unfortunately, Fett got the drop on Eermik and managed to drive a knife through the fuel canister on the Bith's back as he accidentally fired upon a droid. The droid fired back, blowing up his leaking canister, along with most of the bounty hunters in the room. Category:Bith Category:Bounty hunters Category:Males
