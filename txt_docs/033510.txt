Ch'no
1939

Ch'no was a male H'drachi who joined the Rebel Alliance. A scrounger and collector of junk, he adopted an infant Human named Mora after finding her in the rubble of her ruined home after her family was killed. As a seer, Ch'no knew that the young girl would later be revealed as the sole living heir to the ruling house of M'haeli, but he kept that secret for a long time. Other H'drachi did not enjoy the presence of Humans on M'haeli, seeing them as a scourge to the planet.
