Stinksalts
508

Stinksalts were prepackaged minerals that came with a standard medical first-aid kit. Their extremely foul and potent scent was used to rouse people from unconsciousness after they had fainted or had become incapacitated via some other means. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *Legacy of the Force: Inferno Category:Stimulants
