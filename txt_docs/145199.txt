Battle Dog
2427

The Battle Dog was a Quasar Fire-class cruiser-carrier in the New Republic Defense Fleet. The Battle Dog served in Group Two of the Solo Fleet during the hunt for Warlord Zsinj. During this period, its starfighter complement included two Y-wing squadrons, one of which was Lightning Squadron; Shadow Squadron, flying X-wing fighters; and a squadron of CloakShape fighters.
