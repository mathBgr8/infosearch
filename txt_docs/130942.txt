Hall of the Wind Crystals
660

The Hall of the Wind Crystals was the entrance to the Royal Residence in the Fountain Palace on Hapes. A wind-crystal chandelier the size of an A-wing hung from the ceiling. During a skirmish in the Palace, one in which Aurra Sing attempted to kill Tenel Ka, Jedi Leia Organa Solo brought down the light fixture on a group of guards. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hapes locations Category:Rooms
