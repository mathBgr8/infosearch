Whitesun family
3713

The Whitesun family was a family that lived on the planet Tatooine. For three generations as of the Clone Wars they had been moisture farmers and were known as the best of them in the area around Mos Eisley. The Whitesun family bloodline included the siblings Beru, Dama, Haro, and perhaps Coolie. When Anakin Skywalker became Darth Vader, his son, Luke Skywalker, was cared for by Beru and her husband, Owen Lars.
