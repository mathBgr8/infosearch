They were made by Slayn & Korpil and Roche. 11-17s were equipped with a large plasma jet, a telescoping circular saw and a blow torch. To protect it against rockslides, the 11-17 had durasteel armor plating. Such droids were used on Hosk Station. They had well-developed personality, and would have strong loyalty to their owners. The droids were known to rescue miners trapped in cave-ins.
