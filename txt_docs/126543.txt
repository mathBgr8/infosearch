|height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Cedo Partu was a pioneer and early settler of Mos Eisley, a town on the planet Tatooine. When Mos Eisley was a boomtown, Partu, a town leader, built a blockhouse that would serve as a fortification against Tusken Raiders, a sentient species indigenous to Tatooine. Generations later, the blockhouse would become Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. Sariss, a Dark Jedi who went by the name Prophetess, once claimed that one of the cantina's bartenders, the Human male Wuher, was Partu's reincarnated soul. The name "Cedo Partu" first appeared in 1989's Mos Eisley Cantina Adventure Set to identify the male bartender in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. In later sources, however, the bartender was instead identified as "Wuher."
