thumb|right|250px|Booma Troopers standing by. The Gungan Booma Trooper, also known as a "Grenade Trooper", were artillery units used by the Gungan Grand Army. The trooper used a miniature catapult to lob boomas into the desired attack area, similar to Gungan energy catapults. These units were very effective against shielded and mechanized units, however ineffective against buildings and non-mechs. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns Category:Gungans Category:Soldiers
