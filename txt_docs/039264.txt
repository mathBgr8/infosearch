Snow falcon
1839

|habitat= |diet= |language= }} The snow falcons were avian creatures native to the frigid planet Rhinnal, where they built eyries within glaciers. During the Galactic War, the endangered snow falcons of Rhinnal were considered a rare and prized delicacy, worth a small fortune to those willing to risk life imprisonment by hunting them. These creatures became extinct prior to the rule of the New Republic.
