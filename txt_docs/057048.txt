Body Calculus
1296

The Body Calculus was a governmental body on Yag'Dhul. It was composed of the most revered mathematicians of the Givin people, and based its decisions on mathematical modeling. The Givin government was referred to by outsiders as a theocracy at the time of the Clone Wars.
