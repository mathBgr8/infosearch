Four-Eighteen
2556

Four-Eighteen was the designation of a stormtrooper who served in the Galactic Empire's Stormtrooper Corps during the Galactic Civil War between the New Republic and the Galactic Empire. In 6 ABY, the individual was killed by a booby-trap left by New Republic forces shortly after a raid intended to eliminate Rogue Squadron, an elite New Republic starfighter squadron, on the planet Talasea. Four-Eighteen's death, along with the death of fellow stormtrooper Four-Twenty, were later reported to Imperial Intelligence's agent Kirtan Loor. The stormtrooper was first mentioned in the 1996 novel X-Wing: Rogue Squadron, the first book in the Star Wars: X-Wing novel series written by Michael A.
