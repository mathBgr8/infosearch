D'kee-class landing ship
3440

The D'kee-class planetary lander, also known as the D'kee-class landing ship, was used by the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium during its invasion of Bakura in 4 ABY. It was used to transport troops into enemy territory and was equipped with paralysis canisters which could be dropped over a major population area from an altitude of 3,000 meters. They could cover an area of nearly 9 square kilometers. The vehicle's maximum atmospheric speed of 750 kilometers per hour and was armed with 6 light ion cannons and 2 laser cannons. Due to his fragile construction, the ship could not withstand repeated atmospheric entries so it had a mesh of heat-conducting filaments on the hull to divert heat into a rear exhaust system. Some of the heat was converted to energy and stored in onboard batteries for use. The D'kee-class planetary lander carried a complement of ten P'w'eck "collector barges" which would be deployed after the target area was bombed with paralysis gas.
