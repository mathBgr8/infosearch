Westrex
488

Westrex was a casino district in the city of Efavan on Vorzyd V. The Port Line passenger conveyor tube connected Westrex to the city's spaceport, Efavan Port. The casinos were located along the Casino Line. One such casino was called the Hand Pot. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Vorzyd V locations
