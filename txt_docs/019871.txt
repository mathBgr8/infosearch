Arbor Industries
1217

Arbor Industries was a scientific research company founded by Jenna Zan Arbor. It produced the antidotes to many deadly diseases that plagued countless worlds&mdash;most of which Arbor Industries started itself. Its headquarters were located on Zan Arbor's homeworld of Ventrux, but were shut down when she moved her studies to Simpla-12. It was not completely gone, however, when it was discovered near the end of the Clone Wars that Arbor Industries was behind a deadly gas used to devastate the Naboo moon of Ohma-D'un as well used in Nute Gunray's mechno-chair as an emergency defense mechanism. *Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment *Jedi Apprentice: The Dangerous Rescue *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Scientific companies
