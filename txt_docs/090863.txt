Bloodletter
2289

Bloodletter was the nickname of a corporate spy hired in 31 BBY by the Vratix Ashern group to infiltrate the Bacta Cartel. Surviving a close encounter with a Mandalorian in blue and silver armor, Bloodletter discovered the existence of the hybrid kolcta. Soon after the Bacta War, Bloodletter released a memoir of the Thriving Season, Thrive or Die. Though an infamous figure, the Bloodletter's identity and even gender remains unknown.
