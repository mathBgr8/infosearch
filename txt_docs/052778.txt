Nrross
1279

Nrross was a Trandoshan pirate hired by Captain Naz Felyood of the ship Jynni's Virtue. He served as added intimidation and a yes-man to the captain. When Jynni's Virtue came under fire from an Imperial scout patrol, a desperate leap to hyperspace destroyed the ship's astromech droid. The explosion killed Nrross, despite the Trandoshan's natural regenerative abilities. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Pirates Category:Trandoshans
