Ithor (corvette group)
633

Ithor was a group of three CR90 corvettes in the Rebel Alliance. They arrived to pick up a shipment of warheads stolen from Viraxo Industries by Container Transport group Magnum lead by Ace Azzameen in the YT-1300 light freighter Sabra. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
