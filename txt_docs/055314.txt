Director of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence
2458

The Director of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence, also known as the Director-General of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence, was the head of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence. The SBI was an organization of the Galactic Republic Senate and was separate from its military counterpart, Republic Intelligence. Immediately prior to and during the Clone Wars, this office was held by Armand Isard, who also held the position of Director of Republic Intelligence.
