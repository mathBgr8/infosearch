LU-L-N
2093

}} LU-L-N was an 8D smelter droid who served as a reporter for The Emperor's Court. After a trial for the murder of Greedo, LU-L-N interviewed the defendant, Han Solo, who lost the case and was sentenced to life frozen in carbonite. After the short discussion, LU-L-N directed him to the chamber and Boba Fett. The plaintiff, Neela, the mother of Greedo, was interviewed second. Afterwards, LU-L-N concluded the segment with a tease for the next episode.Star Wars Tales 14 LU-L-N wore a red suit and sported fiery red hair. LU-L-N appeared in "The Emperor's Court," the second story in Star Wars Tales 14. The issue was released on December 11, 2002, and was written by Jason Hall, with art by Ramón F. Bachs. LU-L-N is a parody of reporter Doug Llewelyn, who reported for the television series The People's Court. Category:8D smelter droids Category:Journalists
