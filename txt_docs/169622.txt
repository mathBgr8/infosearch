Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Handbook
13811

The Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Handbook is a one-shot issue in the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series of comics. Published by Dark Horse Comics this one-shot comic is a guide to the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series. For a thousand generations, the Jedi kept peace and order in the galaxy--but things don't seem peaceful or orderly for Padawan Zayne Carrick, falsely accused of murder and thrust into the Mandalorian Wars! And here, readers can get the back-story on the scene, 3963 years before the Battle of Yavin! Check out illustrated profiles on Zayne and his outlaw friends; Lucien and the Jedi Covenant; Squint and the Revanchists; the Mandalorian invaders; and more! Written by series writer John Jackson Miller, the Handbook is perfect for catching up with the series so far--while offering new information for regular readers, including an incredible cross-section of everyone's favorite junk hauler, The Last Resort, by series artist Dustin Weaver! Around late 2006 or early 2007, John Jackson Miller pitched the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Handbook as he had quit his job at magazines in light of the sales of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic going really high.
