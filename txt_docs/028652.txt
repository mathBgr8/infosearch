|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Scott Lawrence (born September 27, 1963) is an American actor best known for playing Sturgis Turner in the legal drama television series JAG. He is known for his voice role as Darth Vader in many Star Wars games, including Star Wars: TIE Fighter, Star Wars: Rebellion, Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, Star Wars: Force Commander, and Star Wars Episode III Revenge of the Sith. In several of these vocal appearances, Scott Lawrence's voice was used over that of actor C. Andrew Nelson. He is considered to be the official "audio double" for the character voiced by James Earl Jones. He also voiced the character Jarek Yeager in the animated series Star Wars Resistance. {| ! Year !!
