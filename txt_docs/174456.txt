Cloak Group
856

Cloak Group was a group of four Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports in the Alliance to Restore the Republic. These ships were part of the attack on Sensor Relay Station IOS 24. Their role was to jam the communications to prevent the station from calling in reinforcements. Whatever became of them afterwards is unknown. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
