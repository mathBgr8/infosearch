Tapani Royal Spaceport
1197

The Tapani Royal Spaceport was a spaceport located in the Commercial District of Estalle Island on Procopia in the Tapani sector. Although the spaceport was compact it had some extremely advanced facilities and was quite exclusive. Only the upper echelons of government, a member of the noble houses, the Empire or another favored group were granted permission to land at the Tapani Royal Spaceport. Those that lacked the clearance to land were diverted to the major commercial spaceport on Destato Island.
