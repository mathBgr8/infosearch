Zarfeen
1048

Zarfeen was a female Rodian bounty hunter. Employed by the Black Sun crime syndicate's Clezo, she was sent by the group to retrieve Celis Mott's lost treasure when word got out that Marka Durn had discovered the horde. Sadly, it was too little too late, and the treasure eluded her. Category:Black Sun members Category:Bounty hunters Category:Females Category:Rodians
