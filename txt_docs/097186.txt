Petro (pilot)
1512

Petro was an Imperial TIE/LN starfighter pilot in Crimson Squadron, stationed at Kiidan on Cilpar in 4 ABY. Petro insulted Rogue Squadron's Tycho Celchu (who was undercover as an Imperial pilot at the time) as an Alderaanian coward upon meeting him. Tycho jumped on Petro and struck him several times to demonstrate that he was not a pacifist.
