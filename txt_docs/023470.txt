Confederate Third Fleet
1310

|affiliation=Confederacy of Independent Systems *Confederate Navy }} The Third Fleet was one of the three main fleets of the Confederate Navy during the Clone Wars. For a time, it was based at Thyferra; it later participated in Operation Durge's Lance and the Battle of Duro. Given its location in the Core, it presumably also participated in the Battle of Coruscant. Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems fleets
