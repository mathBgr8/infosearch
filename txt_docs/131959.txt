Ariela
1267

Ariela was a Human female from Alderaan who later lived on Tatooine. Ariela supported Ariq Joanson's idea to make peace with the Tusken Raiders and the Jawas. She was in love with Eyvind, who, at their wedding, was killed by Tuskens.
