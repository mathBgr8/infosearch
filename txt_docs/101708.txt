At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. a Rebel Alliance force carried out training movements on Fislan, at which time the Ocsin swordmaster Cene Gilvent, a member of the Zulirian Swordmasters group of melee weapon afficionados, attacked and nearly killed one of the Rebel soldiers. Alliance Major Bren Derlin subsequently mentioned the encounter in a Task Force on Alliance Security SecuriDex datafile on Gilvent. Fislan was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
