Lim tree
598

Lim trees were native to the original Yuuzhan'tar, and were stored in the Qang qahsa. They had fine silver limbs that actively searched for food. Other than asthetics, they had no value. A close relative of the lim tree grew on Zonama Sekot, a point which led Nen Yim to consider the planet's true origins. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Trees Category:Yuuzhan Vong plants
