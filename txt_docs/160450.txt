Canyonade
573

The Canyonade was a location on Cejansij. It was a large canyon that had been turned into a city, similar to those on Belsavis. It contained the Grand Rim Promenade, Docking Rectangle 16, and Tranquility Common. *Specter of the Past *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Canyons Category:Cejansij locations Category:Cities
