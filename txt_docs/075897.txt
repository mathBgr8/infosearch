Saiga Bre'lya
1192

Saiga Bre'lya was a Bothan female. She was one of the New Republic Intelligence Service guards covertly stationed aboard Queen of Empire to watch over Elan and Vergere to oversee their transfer from Myrkr to Coruscant. She was killed by blaster fire from Darda and Capo. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothans Category:Females Category:New Republic Intelligence personnel
