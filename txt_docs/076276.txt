Kathy Burdette
1239

Kathy Burdette contributed two source articles to the Star Wars Adventure Journal and wrote the short story "Gathering Shadows" for the Tales from the New Republic anthology. She also illustrated several articles for the journal and was the featured artist for issue 11. She lives in Williamsburg, Virginia and is Senior Project Editor of Book Publications for the Omohundro Institute of Early American History and Culture.
