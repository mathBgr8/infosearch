|pages= |isbn= |issn= |issue=159 |series=Star Wars Monthly }} Star Wars Monthly 159 is the one hundred fifty-ninth issue of the Star Wars Monthly series of comic reprints, published in June 1982. Starting with this issue, the series's title was changed from The Empire Strikes Back Monthly. The numbering continued from the previous title. This issue includes one of the few original comic stories in the series, "Blind Fury!".
