The Anglebay Station droid was a silver droid serving on the staff of Anglebay Station medical facility on Telos IV during the Galactic Civil War. The droid was present when bounty hunter Beilert Valance and his crew attacked the station to destroy it and wipe out the records of Valance's past stay at the station. The droid was shocked when confronted by the attackers, and tried to explain that Anglebay Station was neutral in the war before he was gunned down by the bounty hunters.
