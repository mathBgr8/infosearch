She was the grandmother of Han Solo and Thrackan Sal-Solo. She was married to Denn Solo. After she gave birth, the planet was attacked by pirates, forcing the family to flee. Splitting up in the confusion, Tira took Tiion while Den fled with Jonash, never to be seen again.
