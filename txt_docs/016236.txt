Skip 6
696

Skip 6 was an asteroid located in the same asteroid belt as the infamous Smuggler's Run. It was used as a headquarters for the Glottalphib crime boss known as Nandreeson. He chose to fill the caves with pools of water filled with algae, in order to make the asteroid more comfortable to his fellow Glottalphibs. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Smuggler's Run
