|species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Brown |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Mechis III |masters= |apprentices= }} Hekis Durumm Perdo Kolokk Baldikarr Thun was the administrator of the Mechis III droid factories. One of the few Humans living on the planet, Thun was in charge when IG-88A arrived after escaping from Holowan Laboratories. A vain man, Thun added new, grandiose titles to his name whenever he felt unappreciated by his superiors, increasing the apparent importance of his own signature. His administrative droid, 3D-4X, delivered his master's afternoon stim tea and production reports at the same time every day, and Thun saw it as the high point of his schedule. On the day that IG-88A arrived in orbit of Mechis III, 3D-4X delivered the tea and reports at the appointed time, but while Thun sipped his tea, 3D-4X shot him twice with a blaster pistol. Thun and the other seventy-two Humans on the planet were killed in an uprising as IG-88A and his fellow IG-88 assassin droids reprogrammed the droids and took over the planet. *The Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males
