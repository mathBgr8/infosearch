|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=38 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 32 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express |issue=2 |next=last }} Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express 2 is the second issue in the Star Wars: Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express series of comics. It was released June 12, 2002. Some days being a hero can be more trouble than it's worth, but for Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi, that's just part of what being a Jedi is all about. As the luxury cruise liner they're trapped on sinks deeper into the crushing atmosphere of Yorn Skot, their only hope of restoring the ship's controls is to identify the criminal mastermind among the list of rich, suspicious passengers. Unfortunately, their prime suspect has leapt to his death, and there are only 43 minutes left to save the day! Adventure soars high in the exciting conclusion to "THE AURORIENT EXPRESS"!
