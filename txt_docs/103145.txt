Snuzzleguff
816

[[Bnugor and his pet snuzzleguff, Snowball.|thumb|right|250px]] Snuzzleguffs were round, furry animals about the size of a hubba gourd. Carnivorous, snuzzleguffs utilized a poison to paralyze their prey before devouring it. They were also capable of gliding short distances by using their small, wing-like arms.
