Gladean State Parks
1987

The Gladean State Parks were a group of parks on the planet Chandrila known for their beautiful gardens, which were beloved throughout the galaxy.Star Wars: Force and Destiny Core Rulebook Funded through a tax on industry, the Gladean State Parks were created by the Emita Governor Gladean several millennia before the rise of the Galactic Empire. This tax was distributed to the governors of Chandrila to fund local parks and reserves. The State Parks brought tourism from across the Core Worlds, and were staffed by the Chandrilan Park Ranger Service's scouts, hunters, and survivalists. *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Force and Destiny Core Rulebook *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Emita locations Category:Life exhibition areas
