As with all other Z-95t Headhunters, Tala 1 had a class 3 hyperdrive, which was added to allow the starfighter to allow it to operate independently of capital ship support. In addition to the class 3 hyperdrive, the Z-95t Headhunter model also had increased maneuverability compared to the Z-95 Headhunter, and Tala 1 was individually a heavily modified Headhunter. By 4 ABY, is set around the time of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, the events of which are dated to 4 ABY by The New Essential Chronology. Tala 1 was serving in the Alliance's Tala Squadron. Operationally assigned to support Alliance Special Forces commandos led by Crix Madine, an Alliance general, Tala 1 was piloted by Colonel Airen Cracken, an Alliance spy and scout. Cracken often flew support missions for Madine's commando team.
