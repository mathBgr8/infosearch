Indomitable (Strike-class)
2544

Indomitable was a Strike-class cruiser used by the Galactic Empire. Along with Strike cruisers Formidable, Triumph and the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Grey Wolf it was to ambush a strike force, sent by Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin, that was supposed to ambush a convoy of what was supposed to carry Missile Boats, when in reality they carried munitions. The strike force was destroyed and the ships went into hyperspace. It was then an escort ship for Task Force Whirlwind. The Task Force was supposed to rendezvous with a convoy of Missile Boats that were used as bait to lure Zaarin's forces into a trap. However, Zaarin sent the Maelstrom to destroy the Task force but they were repelled.
