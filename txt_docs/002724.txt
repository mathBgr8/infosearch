|intl release= |pages= |issue= |featured= |series=Star Wars Encyclopedia }} "Princess Leia Organa" is a volume in the Star Wars Encyclopedia series of reference books published by De Agostini. It was published on November 30, 2021. Senator, princess, rebel, jedi? Leia Organa was many things throughout her life, but above all, she was always someone who fought for freedom wherever she was. Long oblivious to her true lineage (she was the daughter of Anakin Skywalker), Leia was guided by the lofty principles instilled in her by her adoptive father, Bail Organa, which would lead her first to join the Rebel Alliance and then, decades later, to create a resistance group against a threat, that of the First Order, whose danger only she seemed to grasp.
