Plague (Marauder-class)
2263

Plague was a standard Marauder-class corvette. Owned by a mercenary group which operated out of the former Azzameen family space station, the Plague was destroyed by a strike force consisting of Emon Azzameen, Aeron Azzameen, and elements of the Alliance Fleet, including Ace Azzameen. Plague and its accompanying starfighters were not successful in defending the mercenary position, and the base was retaken by the Alliance. The Plague was featured in the 1999 video game Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance.
