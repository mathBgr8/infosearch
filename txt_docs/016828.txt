It occupied the third orbital position around the star Beshqek and had one moon. Polos featured a rocky surface and a toxic environment. Polos was first mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 1: Byss," the first entry in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 14, 2005.
