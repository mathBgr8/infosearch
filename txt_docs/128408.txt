Z-Jumimi
1981

The Jumimi was a Carrack-class light cruiser used by Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. The Jumimi was part of an attempt to destroy the Deep Space Manufacturing Facility ZA-13. However, the cruiser was destroyed by Tan Maarek Stele.
