Preparation Center
1583

The Preparation Center was a Galactic Republic facility on Yaga Minor where the crew of the Outbound Flight trained for two years prior to the launch of the Project in 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. It was located several kilometers from the Yavvitiri Spaceport.
