In 17 BBY, Skee and his family fled their native world during the Rodian Clan Wars to start a new life for themselves on U-Tendik.The Essential Reader's Companion Skee discovered a predatory species of animal known as a Manka cat and became a prosperous Manka Cat hunter. He particularly enjoyed hunting them late in the year during mating season and would hang Manka pelts from the limbs of the Tendril trees as a testament to his skill. In 4 BBY, raiders from Navik the Red's Chattza Clan found the Tetsus on the jungle planet and began slaughtering them.
