Tharb
1104

Tharb was the codename of a Galactic Republic informer on Tarhassan. His species is unknown, he was short though, with a deep voice. He worked at Corgan's Gustatorium restaurant as a server, which is where he gave information to Republic Intelligence officer Edbit Teeks.
