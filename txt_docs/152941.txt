Tor Aramatha
2046

Tor Aramatha was a male Human and the governor of Aldera, Alderaan's capital, from the era of the Clone Wars up until the planet's destruction in 0 BBY. A contemporary of Senator Bail Organa, Aramatha was an influential member of Alderaan's society and government, promoting the Alderaanian ideals of pacifism and coexistence with all.
