Tatooine Bureau of Ethnicity and Socialization
1334

The Tatooine Bureau of Ethnicity and Socialization was an adjunct of the mining colony that was operating on Tatooine in the sixth century BBY. Among those who worked for the bureau was Alkhara, who studied the Tusken Raiders as an anthropologist, who allied with them for a short time before later betraying them and establishing his own criminal group. Even hundreds of years after Alkhara's terror reign had ended, the Bureau still had to deal with small pockets of Alkhara's followers who still believed in the anarchy that Alkhara had preached.
