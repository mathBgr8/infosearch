Ransar Rhal Insignia
379

The Ransar Rhal Insignia was a Bitthævrian decoration. Heroic Bitthævrian Aul Tarrus Vishav was decored with this insignia after saving Quaal Tavier Catharius's life, which almost guaranteed Vishav's place as Catharius's successor. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Awards
