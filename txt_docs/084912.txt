Mintri
850

Mintris was a plant that grew on the Mid Rim planet Naboo as well as its moon Ohma-D'un. They were eaten by Muudaboks. The plants were used by Gungans to make drinking straws, blow darts and were also used in Gungan weaving. *Star Wars Episode I: The Gungan Frontier *The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide Category:Plants of Naboo
