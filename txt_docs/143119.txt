Landric
1256

Landric was a male Human and the Imperial Moff who governed Emmo sector during the Galactic Civil War. During a vacation on the resort world of Elshandruu Pica, the corusca gem necklace that belonged to Moff Landric's wife was stolen, then returned, by the infamous art and jewelry thief known only as "Tombat." The Moff ordered the Imperial Office of Criminal Investigations to immediately capture the criminal, though they believed Tombat to be male.
