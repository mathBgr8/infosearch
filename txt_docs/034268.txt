Bantha Burrows
872

Bantha Burrows was a deep-desert canyon maze located on Tatooine. It consisted of thousands of huge caves, possibly used by banthas retreating from dangerous sandstorms. In 8 ABY, Han Solo sought shelter there from a large sandstorm. *Tatooine Ghost *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Canyons Category:Tatooine locations
