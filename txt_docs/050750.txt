thumb|right|250px|The Doneeta and [[Triumph (Acclamator-class)|Triumph attack Separatist starships during the Battle of Rendili]] The Doneeta was an Acclamator-class assault ship in use by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. Near the end of the Wars, it, along with several other Acclamators, accompanied High Generals Saesee Tiin and Plo Koon to the Rendili system, where they would prevent the defection of Rendili's home fleet.
