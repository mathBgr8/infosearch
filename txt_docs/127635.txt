Diehard
1421

Diehards were burrowing, predatory insects native to Atraken. Diehards had ten legs and a tough, chitinous shell. They had a dangerous set of mandibles, which they used to attack their prey. Diehards were very resistant to energy weapons such as blasters. They were also immune to radiation, poison, and various viruses. They were non-sentient, and very aggressive. They attacked their prey in swarms and once attached, were difficult to detach. Diehards were the only species to survive their homeworld's poisoning during the Clone Wars, earning them their name.
