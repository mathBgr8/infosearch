Slitherhorn
1697

thumb|right|150px|A slitherhorn The slitherhorn, also called the traz, was a musical instrument common among jizz bands during the Galactic Civil War. It was similar in appearance to the kloo horn, though smaller in size. Rodian musician Doda Bodonawieedo played the slitherhorn in the Max Rebo Band. *Star Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition *Star Wars: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided Quick Reference Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Wind musical instruments
