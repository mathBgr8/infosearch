The Pentastar Alignment
8286

"The Pentastar Alignment" was an article for the West End Games Roleplaying Game. It was written by Anthony P. Russo and illustrated by Chris Gossett. It was published in the Star Wars Adventure Journal 3, and was reprinted in Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal. Some of the illustrations were also reprinted in color in The Best of the Star Wars Adventure Journal, Issues 1-4.
