Palpatine's office
1269

Palpatine's office was an office in the Galactic Senate Building on Coruscant used by Naboo Senator Sheev Palpatine. It was decorated with pieces from his personal art collection, and deliberately laid out in such a fashion as to subtly discomfort visitors and discourage them from staying, as Palpatine was not interested in encouraging guests. It did not have a view of the Jedi Temple. *Queen's Peril Category:Offices Category:Senate Building locations
