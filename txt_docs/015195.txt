*Michael Mikaelian *Michelle Vuckovich *Mary Franklin |cover artist= |contributors=*David West Reynolds *Justin Lambros *David Maddox *JD Wiker *Aaron Allston *Daniel Wallace *Kevin Rubio |publisher=Paizo Publishing |release date=January 21, 2003 |subscribers=January 3, 2003 |pages=*98 *108 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=65 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 65 is the sixty-fifth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was released to newsstands on January 21, 2003. The subscriber-exclusive edition of issue 65 included the second volume of Bantha Tracks, the Star Wars Fan Club newsletter. This issue also came packaged with a fold-out map of the Star Wars galaxy. *From the Editor's Desk *Star News: From the Worlds of Lucasfilm *The Jawa Trader *Behind the Magic &mdash; Lesser is More *HoloNet News **CIS Shadowfeed Dispatch 14:2:14 Edition **Republic HoloNet News Core Edition 14:2:12 *Gamescape &mdash; Star Wars Galaxies Update *Bookshelf &mdash; Finally, Two There Are *Comics Link &mdash; Boba Fett's Sacrifice *Set Piece: The Outlander Club *Scouting the Galaxy &mdash; Become 100,000 Baht Richer *Crossword &mdash; Who Dooku Live? *Star Wars Q&A *The Last Page *"Prequel Update" *:It's the calm before the storm, but Rick McCallum gives us a few hints about starting production on Episode III. *"Inside Attack of the Clones" *:For centuries, the abandoned Dacho factory in one of Coruscant's Dead Sectors has been the final resting place of millions of unfortunate workers.
