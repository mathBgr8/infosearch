Ansta
1507

The Ansta was a warship employed by the Galactic Alliance during the Second Galactic Civil War. The Ansta was part of the blockade of Corellia by the Alliance's Third and Fifth Fleets. It was assigned to the Fifth Fleet, under the command of Admiral Cheb. *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
