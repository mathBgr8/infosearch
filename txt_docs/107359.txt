Ciuray Sea
704

The Ciuray Sea, known colloquially as the Slurry Sea, was a body of water on the planet Metellos. The sea lay close to the Moridebo District. It was stagnant and inky gray-brown. Its water was processed for drinking by powerful sterilization plants on the shoreline.
