Natori Shock Troops
600

The Natori Shock Troops were a mercenary unit allied with the Natori Association. Graduates of the Natori Association's training programs usually paid for the cost of their training by serving a tour with the Natori Shock Troops, while entrants who paid for the training up front went on to serve in other units.
