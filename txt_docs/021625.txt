|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: The Last Command |issue=1 }} The Last Command 1 was the first issue in the Star Wars: The Last Command comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel The Last Command. It was released November 26, 1997. The decorated pair come together to adapt Timothy Zahn's best-selling novel The Last Command, in which Grand Admiral Thrawn creates an army of clones for the Empire and Leia gives birth to twins, Jacen and Jaina Solo, destined to become powerful Jedi.
