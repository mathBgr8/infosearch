Tieress
563

Tieress was the title held by the spouses of the Satabs of Ventooine. Their duties were unknown, but they were members of the royal family and held power over the royal guard. The only known Tieress is Chrysalla, who held the title sometime prior to 0 BBY. She betrayed her husband and helped end the Satab rule on Ventooine. *Star Wars Annual (1977) 2 Category:Titles of nobility
