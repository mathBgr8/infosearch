Tor Difusal
2128

Tor Difusal was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as a member of the Jedi High Council, the leading body of the Order. Trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, the Force-sensitive Tor Difusal eventually attained the rank of Jedi Master. Distinguished from his peers, Master Difusal was selected to sit on the Jedi High Council by 89 BBY, joining eleven other esteemed members on the august body. Sitting alongside such luminaries as Grand Master Yoda and Oppo Rancisis, Difusal was a vocal member of the Council, interjecting comments while Master Yoda spoke.
