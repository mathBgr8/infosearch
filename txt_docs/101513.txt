Intamm clan
506

The Intamm Clan was a Rodian clan on the planet of Rodia. It was based on the Betu continent where it owned the small Intamm Enclave. It owed its loyalties to the Chekkoo clan. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Rodian clans
