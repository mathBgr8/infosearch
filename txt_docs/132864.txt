Imperial Communique 2734.19g
818

Imperial Communique #2734.19g detailed the defection of Imperial Army infantry units on Wazta. The ISB member General Nasda referenced this communique and cited that various Imperial Navy vessels and their crews had deserted afterwards, which alongside the results of the Battle of Yavin, made more recruitments necessary and yet at the same time they had to make sure any new recruits were not Rebel sympathizers. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Imperial Communiques
