Seer Lonnel
1674

Seer Lonnel was a male Ugnaught engineer who worked for the Helmet Squad in Desolation Alley during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Around 32 BBY, he was a snitch for the Helmet Squad and the Correctional Authority, which was why Sebolto posted a bounty on his head to 'shut up' permanently.
