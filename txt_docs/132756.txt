Dokus
1911

|masters= |apprentices= }} Dokus was a member of the Imperial Army. Whilst on deployment to Tatooine, Dokus used a DEMP gun on work droids belonging to a moisture farmer that were denying access to their master's evaporation station. Dokus later served in the Mos Eisley police, participating in an investigation into possible Alliance to Restore the Republic agents with ties to Voren Na'al during the Galactic Civil War.
