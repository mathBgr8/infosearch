Betsy Mitchell
1053

Betsy Mitchell has been the editor-in-chief of Del Rey Books since 2002. Previously, she was the Associate Publisher of Bantam Spectra when they held the license to publish Star Wars novels in the 1990s. She edited many of the books from this period, including Timothy Zahn's Heir to the Empire. Category:Editors
