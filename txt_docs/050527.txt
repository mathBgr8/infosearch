Visvia
2164

Visvia were Chiss units of distance, corresponding to approximately 1.6 kilometers each. In 27 BBY,The events of Outbound Flight take place five years after the Battle of Naboo, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 32 BBY. Therefore the novel must have taken place in 27 BBY. the commander of the Chiss Picket Force Two engaged a Trade Federation task force with a Chiss fighter. The Trade Federation's droid starfighters fought the Chiss ship, however, they were close enough to Thrawn's Chiss assault cruiser Springhawk, and were jammed after Thrawn inquired a Human aboard his cruiser if ten thousand visvia was the correct operating range for the droids.
