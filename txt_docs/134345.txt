Gilling
1394

Gilling was one of the Imperial Security Bureau officers serving on the Reprisal under Colonel Vak Somoril. He accompanied the Colonel in an investigation into a group of defecting stormtroopers. Along with his partner Brock, he was dispatched to Somoril to track Mara Jade, an Emperor's Hand.
