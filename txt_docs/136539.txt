Carbanti United Electronics
1965

|founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Carbanti United Electronics,Star Wars Outlaws: Low Red Moon or simply Carbanti, was the producer of Carbanti transceiver packages, the main setup for a T-65B X-wing starfighter's sensor system. The Carbanti transceiver package was comprised of a Fabritech ANs-5d "lock track" full-spectrum transceiver, a Melihat "Multi Imager" dedicated energy receptor, and a Tana Ire Electro-photo receptor. *Star Wars Outlaws: Low Red Moon *Star Wars: Squadrons *Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes *Special Modifications *Star Wars: X-Wing Deluxe Book and Model Set *Star Wars: The Rebel Files *YT-1300 Millennium Falcon Owners' Workshop Manual *Star Wars: Smuggler's Guide Category:Manufacturing companies
