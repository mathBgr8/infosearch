Sovereign (Sovereign-class)
3209

Sovereign was the lead ship of the Sovereign-class Super Star Destroyer line. It was still under construction during the time of the Dark Empire. The Star Wars Encyclopedia, A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded, and finally The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia all incorrectly states that the Sovereign was under Warlord Zsinj's command,Star Wars Encyclopedia, p. 281A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded, p. 503 when other sources such as The Essential Atlas state that Zsinj only had one Super Star Destroyer.The Essential Atlas This error might come from the German translation of The Courtship of Princess Leia. In the final confrontation between the heroes and Zsinj his ship the Iron Fist was erroneously translated as Souverän, the German word for Sovereign. In addition both the English and German version of the novel describe the Iron Fist in the final confrontation as a mere Star Destroyer, not a Super Star Destroyer.The Courtship of Princess Leia, chapter 26 Somehow this error might have made it into the Star Wars Encyclopedia in 1998, which first described the Sovereign as one "of Warlord Zsinj's Star Destroyers", while the later A Guide to the Star Wars Universe published in the year 2000 describe it as a "Star Destroyer in Warlord Zsinj's fleet". Both sources attribute this information to The Courtship of Princess Leia. Finally The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia of 2008 mixes these two entries with the Sovereign mentioned in the Dark Empire Sourcebook. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sovereign-class Super Star Destroyers
