Tyko Thul
1992

Tyko Thul was a Human male who was the younger brother of Bornan Thul, and he used his share of the family fortune to buy and run the droid factory on Mechis III. There he found the shell of the abandoned IG-88A, and had it refitted as a personal bodyguard. He staged his own capture by IG-88 in an attempt to lure his brother out into the open.
