Knave Three
1954

Knave Three was the callsign of a male Human pilot serving in the New Republic Starfighter Corps during the later stages of the Galactic Civil War. A Human male joined the New Republic as a pilot and was assigned to Knave Squadron, a squadron of E-wings which was part of the New Republic Starfighter Corps. Going by the callsign Knave Three, the bearded Human flew his craft during the battle over Dac in 10 ABY. Attacking the enemy craft of the Galactic Empire, Knave Three's starfighter sustained damaged during the battle over the Calamari Sea.
