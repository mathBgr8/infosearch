Briessen
966

Briessen was a male Human and a member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic who served as a commander in the Rebel SpecForces. He was Koris Haslam's superior officer, and assigned Aurin Leith to Haslam's team for a mission to Selnesh. Category:Alliance Military officers Category:Humans Category:Males
