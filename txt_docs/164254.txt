Onderon Soldier
1027

thumb|right|250px Onderon Soldiers performed both the functions of army and law enforcement on Onderon. Ranks below commissioned officer wore brown uniforms with a cap and a mask that covered the lower half of their face, with officers wearing a very dark shade of blue-gray. Both classes of uniform were offset with gold piping and topped with a white wrap-like garment covering the shoulders. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Culture of Onderon Category:Soldiers
