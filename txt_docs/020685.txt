It was the origin point of the art form known as participlays, which the Art group of the Coalition for Progress of the Galactic Empire's Commission for the Preservation of the New Order reviewed and judged. By the time shortly after the Battle of Yavin, Imperial Intelligence noted Besn's participlays in a report prepared for an Imperial Grand Moff, which in turn was later added by Rebel Alliance historian Major Arhul Hextrophon to a compilation of Imperial documents for Alliance Supreme Commander Mon Mothma and other Rebel officers. thumb|right|250px|Voyages SF 13 placed Besn in the Taldot sector. Besn was first mentioned in Greg Gorden's 1989 Imperial Sourcebook that was published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.Imperial Sourcebook "Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star, How I Wonder Where We Are," a 1990 roleplaying game source article published in the thirteenth issue of the Voyages SF magazine, placed Besn in the Taldot sector. Since that article was released outside of the Lucas Licensing process, it is considered non-canonical with respect to the Star Wars Legends continuity.
