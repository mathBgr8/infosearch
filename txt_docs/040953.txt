Invincible (Imperial-class)
2021

The Invincible was an Imperial Star Destroyer. During the reborn Palpatine's return, Invincible was commanded by Zasm Katth. On Nar Shaddaa, while giving chase to the Millennium Falcon, the Star Destroyer met its final fate when it was impaled by the Air traffic control structure dismantled by its own tractor beam. *Dark Empire II 2 *The Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial-class Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Dark Empire
