ST 178
1149

ST 178 was a Lambda-class shuttle in the Imperial Navy. The "ST" in the shuttle's designation stood for "space transport". A black Imperial logo and the ship's numeric designation were painted on the tips of the port and starboard wings. Although most Imperial shuttles boasted better defensive systems, ST 178 was armed with both proton torpedoes and turbolasers (weapons usually installed on Imperial patrol vehicles) in addition to the laser cannon emplacements standard for its class.
