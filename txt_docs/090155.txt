Quetzal Sisters
964

The Quetzal Sisters were a squad of Rebel Alliance femmes fatales used for covert missions. Under the command of General Orma Hundeen, they were composed of Zeela Nall, Nuri Pravada, and Sheyne Tyndos. Leia Organa once worked with them on Elerion. Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic squads
