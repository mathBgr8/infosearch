December 25
1147

December 25 is the 359th day (360th in leap years) of the year. There are 6 days remaining. In-universe, this day is represented by Life Day, a Wookiee celebration of joy and harmony. *1975 &ndash; Daxiong is born. *1977 &ndash; Star Wars released as La Guerra de las Galaxias in Mexico. *1979 &ndash; Star Wars (1977) 33 published. *1987 &ndash; Joe Kaye dies. *2002 &ndash; The Official Star Wars Fact File 52 published. *2015 &ndash; Jason Wingreen dies. *2020 &ndash; Disney Gallery: The Mandalorian: Making of Season Two premieres on Disney+. *2020 &ndash; Han Solo published in France. *2023 &ndash; Richard Franklin dies. *2024 &ndash; Battle of Jakku &mdash; Last Stand 1 published.
