RO-D-series droid
1994

The RO-D-series droid was designed and manufactured by Balmorran Arms to serve as both a labor droid and security droid for touring musicians throughout the galaxy. A jack-of-all-trades droid, the RO-D droid could function as a labor droid to help move equipment and set up a stage for a performance and could also act as a mechanic to repair the same equipment. It also served as a bodyguard for the performers. It cost 14800 credits new.
