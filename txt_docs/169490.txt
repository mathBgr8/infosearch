Glue stat
572

A glue stat was a medical substance developed shortly before the Clone Wars and was made from cloned tissue and an adhesive made from Talusian mussels. It could be placed on a laceration, and would dissolve, sealing the opening. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bandages Category:Substances
