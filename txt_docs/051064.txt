Qurang Lah
1761

Qurang Lah was a male Yuuzhan Vong warrior and a crèche-brother of Warmaster Tsavong Lah. Lah commanded the attack on the Yag'Dhul system. However, Qurang initially opposed the assault, as it would be better to hold the present "truce" for the moment so that the Yuuzhan Vong could reorder their forces. He was suspicious of Nom Anor when the Executor claimed that the Jedi (Corran Horn, Tahiri Veila, and Anakin Solo) killed the rest of the warriors when in fact the Intendant did it himself so the other Yuuzhan Vong would not find out he had backed down from a challenge by one of the Jedi.
