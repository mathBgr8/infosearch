|inventor= |affiliation=*Jedi *SithDarth Vader (2017) 20 *Knights of Ren }} Force Stasis was a telekinetic Force power used to restrain and immobilize people or objects. Force Stasis was an ability of the Force to forcibly restrain and immobilize people or to halt objects headed in a Force-user's direction. The ability allowed its users to cancel out the motion of a target, not only reducing its speed but even suspending it in place. The effect could at times be temporary, allowing the target to regain its motion, but could also be so powerful that, unlike normal applications of telekinesis, continuous conscious effort and physical gestures were not always necessary to maintain it. Additionally, the most skilled or powerful users could even exert this paralyzing grip on non-material objects, such as blaster bolts. thumb|left|250px|Master Yoda holds back Asajj Ventress's strike against King Katuunko.
