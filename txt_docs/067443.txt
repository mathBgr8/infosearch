Zolander
1680

Zolanders were the native species of Zolan. They were adapted to the extreme cold of the planet. As their sun's radioactive emissions increased, the Zolanders created a skin-altering gene, inadvertently creating a genetic offshoot of the Zolanders, the Clawdite.
