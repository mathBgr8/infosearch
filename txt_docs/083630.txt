Tiltanka took part in the Nagai invasion of the galaxy in 4 ABY. He led the invasion of Iskalon and became known as the "enslaver of Iskalon". Kob designed the Nagai Kob sonic blaster,The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology and favored using it on its highest setting, able to kill sentient beings. Many other officers felt this was a dishonorable way to kill but Kob disagreed.
