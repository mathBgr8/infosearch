Savrip (language)
1012

Savrip was the language of the Mantellian Savrips, an intelligent species native to Ord Mantell. It was composed of roars, shrieks, and growls.Threats of the Galaxy It was a difficult language to learn, incomprehensible to other species. While modern translation equipment and droids had partial success in deciphering some simple linguistic concepts, the only way for a non-Savrip to fully learn the language was with the help of the Force. Conversely, few Savrips ever bothered to learn Basic, and even in those cases, they rarely spoke it. *Alien Anthology *Ultimate Adversaries *Threats of the Galaxy Category:Languages
