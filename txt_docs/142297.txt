|pages=11 |timeline=c. 25&ndash;29 ABYThis story is set during the Yuuzhan Vong War, which spanned from 25 ABY to 29 ABY per The Essential Atlas. |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Hive of the Infidel" is a Star Wars Roleplaying Game adventure published in Star Wars Gamer 8. It was written by David Noonan and Peter M. Schweighofer, and was illustrated by Derek Thompson. The players take on the role of Yuuzhan Vong infiltrators. They are hunting a Jedi aboard the Chilastra.
