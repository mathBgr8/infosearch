Rel Farley
1973

Rel Farley was a male who served as the assistant manager of the Brodsport Mining Corporation in Trid, on Danuta. He was also a sympathizer for the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Preparing for his mission to Danuta on the Moldy Crow, Kyle Katarn was told to mention Farley's name when checked by the customs. Indeed, approaching the planet, X-Ray 291 demanded his business, and Katarn claimed that his ship was bringing spares for Brodsport, mentioning also Farley, adding a friendly remark for him. Katarn was not told more about Farley, but supposedly he was to confirm the ship's business.
