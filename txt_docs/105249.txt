R4-M17
1763

R4-M17 was a hardworking, but stubborn astromech droid. Her Arc welder had an intermittent short, and her third leg could no longer retract, but otherwise she was a skilled worker. The unit was owned by a moisture farmer, who bought it from the Jawa Traders shop in the center of Mos Eisley. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droids with feminine programming Category:R4 astromech droids
