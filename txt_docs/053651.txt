Kyrf
708

Kyrf was a fruit grown on Korbin, and, if fermented, an alcoholic beverage. Cross-Galactic Mining, although primarily a mining corporation, handled the exportation of kyrf, which was produced exclusively by Drelmar Bin's kyrf plantation. The kyrf fruit was grown on green trees that somehow thrived in arid conditions. The fruit itself was metallic gold in color and oval in shape. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Fruits and vegetables
