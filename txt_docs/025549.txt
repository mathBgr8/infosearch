In 19 BBY, during the war's final days, the Clone Commander was stationed on the Republic capital world of Coruscant, where he was assigned to a task force of clone commandos under the command of High Jedi General Mace Windu. In the hunt for Darth Sidious, he led the commandos of Aurek Team and Bacta Team. He and his teams accompanied Mace Windu and Shaak Ti during their search for Sidious in the LiMerge Building in The Works. Their search led to the lower levels of 500 Republica, where Valiant remained as the invasion of Coruscant began. There, they discovered Sidious. He and Aurek Team were killed beneath 500 Republica by Geonosians armed with sonic blasters and droids armed with blasters, guarding the Sith Lord.
