Fel estate
1220

The Fel estate was a large estate on Corellia, located near Coronet City. It was granted to Soontir Fel by Palpatine after the former was granted the title of Baron of the Empire following the Battle of Derra IV. Fel's entire family lived there. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 25 *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 26 *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Corellia locations
