MED-47
2004

The MED-47 was a model of medical droid used by the Trade Federation. Many of their qualities were incorporated into the later ADK-25-MED medical droids. The droids often replied to their commander with "sedatives are not an option", "torture, or repair?", as well as "you organics are so fragile," indicating their enjoyment of painful operations. During the Clone Wars, MED-47s served alongside their ADK-25-MED counterparts, especially in scenarios the Trade Federation focused in. Years after the war, the Galactic Empire used their shells for suicidal explosive droids-ironic, considering their original purpose as healers. Like all the medical droids used by the "bad guys" in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, the MED-47 has a sadistic love of torturous surgery. This droid cannot be normally be built during gameplay, as the Trade Federation uses droids and has no need for medical droids.
