Trade Federation Regional Commander

Regional Commander was the title given to officers of the Trade Federation who oversaw particular areas of the galaxy under Federation control. The Neimoidian officer Harro Ruuk was Regional Commander of the Karthakk system as of 22 BBY, and directed Federation forces against Nym's Lok Revenants from a sensor station on Maramere. *Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter Category:Trade Federation titles
