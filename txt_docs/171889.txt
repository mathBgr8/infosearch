AGWS-04

AGWS-04 was a Loronar Corporation Strike-class medium cruiser serving the Alliance to Restore the Republic during her attempt to wrest power from the Galactic Empire in the Airam sector. During the Battle of Nocto, both the AGWS-04 and AGWS-03&mdash;an identical Strike Cruiser&mdash;were waiting in line behind the Sagina at the shipyard Calenz&mdash;the facility where the Airam engineers planned to refit further vessels to carry a pair of gravity well projectors, providing interdiction capabilities according to the Modified Strike Cruiser design pioneered by the Peregrine. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic Category:Strike-class medium cruisers
