Vor-cal
1716

thumb|right|250px|Vor-cal Festivities The Vor-cal was an annual safari hunt attended by each house of the Tapani sector as well as other prominent institutions. Each year a different house sponsored the Vor-cal and hosted the pre-hunt festivities on board their orbital hunting lodge. A popular place to hold the Vor-cal was on Vycinyth's moon of Vilhon. The night before the Vor-cal, the grand Vor-cal Feast was held by the hosting house, whose representative opened with a speech that introduced the participants and announced the target prey for that year. When House Melantha hosted the event prior to the Battle of Endor, the target prey was the Rodian Karstag. On this occasion the hunt was staged at the House Melantha Orbital Hunting Lodge.
