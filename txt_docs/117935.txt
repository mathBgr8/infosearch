Michael Stemmle
497

Michael Stemmle wrote the storyline for various LucasArts projects. He was one of the writers for the video game Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast. He is also credited as a writer for Star Wars: Rebel Assault II and Star Wars: Battlefront II. Category:Game screenwriters
