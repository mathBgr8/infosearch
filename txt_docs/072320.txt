House Sizhran
4023

House Sizhran was a family of Falleen nobles from the planet of the same name. The family included Haxim and his wife, two sons, including the Dark Prince Xizor, and two daughters. Xizor became the Underlord of the Black Sun crime syndicate. Xizor was off-planet when most of his family was killed when Darth Vader ordered Falleen to be "sterilized" in 7 BBY.
