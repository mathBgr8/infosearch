Yorlin
2057

Yorlin was a stormtrooper that served aboard the Iron Fist under Warlord Zsinj. When members of Wraith Squadron&mdash;under the guise of the Hawk-bat Independent Space Force&mdash;visited the Iron Fist, Yorlin was one of the stormtroopers that made sure the visitors were not armed before meeting Warlord Zsinj. Yorlin searched Dissek&mdash;the alias used by Kell Tainer&mdash;and was knocked unconscious by the pilot. Dissek claimed that it was just his reflex action at being prodded. Yorlin suffered a concussion and damage to some of his teeth at the encounter.
