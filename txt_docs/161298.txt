Juuus
1310

Juuus was one of three Trandoshans living in Edic Bar. He worked as a street sweeper, which he claimed let him know what was going on without being noticed. He also sometimes worked as hired muscle for SoroSuub, paying visits to those who tried to manipulate the corporation. It was said that he didn't leave bruises. Juuus claimed that he used to be a pilot, but left that career after an accident nearly killed him (though nothing of this has been confirmed and Juuus is not a reliable source).
