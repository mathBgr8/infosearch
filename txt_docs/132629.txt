Imperial Spaceport
592

The Imperial Spaceport was an Imperial occupied city on Kile II, and part of the Imperial Enclave situated on the planet. The spaceport was surrounded by a turbolaser defense. The spaceport was completely destroyed during bombing runs performed by Rogue Squadron during the Galactic Civil War. *Star Wars: Rogue Squadron Category:Imperial military bases
