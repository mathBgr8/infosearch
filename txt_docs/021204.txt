Photo-lipids in brazened fatty-acid sauce
598

Photo-lipids in brazened fatty acid sauce could be served as a food delicacy enjoyed by Ugors. The chef droid L9-G8 instructed the rebel crew of the Bonderium to cook this dish for an Ugorian feast, in order to bribe ArrGack and GrrKack for admission to the Paradise system. Lipids are fatty chemicals. Photo-lipids may be fats that are affected by light. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Sauces Category:Ugorian food
