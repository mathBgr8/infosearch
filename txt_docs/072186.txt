Drake Theatre
764

The Drake Theatre was located in the Commercial District of Estalle Island on Procopia (Tapani sector). It was a beautifully designed theatre with two stages and it tended to run more contemporary plays and operas than the Leocald Opera House, also located on the island. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Procopia locations Category:Theatrical venues
