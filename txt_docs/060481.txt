Vorol was an alien who lived on the Wheel space station during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, Rebel leaders Lando Calrissian and Chewbacca, who were searching for information on the whereabouts of the bounty hunter Bossk, were led to believe that Vorol knew their target's whereabouts.
