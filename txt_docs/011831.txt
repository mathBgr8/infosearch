|last date=June 6, 2000 |media type=Novels |timeline=25 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |num books=2 |preceded by=The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime |followed by=Agents of Chaos Duology }} The Dark Tide duology was written by Michael A. Stackpole and contained the novels The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught and The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin. The duology is part of the New Jedi Order series. It was originally going to be a trilogy, but The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide: Siege was removed. In June of 2000 both novels in the duology were published in single volume Hardcover exclusively by the Science Fiction Book Club. |part2=Ruin }} *1
