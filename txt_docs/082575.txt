In the year 32 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace to the year 32 BBY. the Rodian watched the Boonta Eve Classic podrace from the Toydarian junk dealer Watto's private box in the Mos Espa Grand Arena on the planet Tatooine. In 0 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope to the year 0 BBY. As Super Star Wars is an adaptation of the events of A New Hope, it can be determined that Super Star Wars must also take place in 0 BBY. Breedo was at Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in Tatooine's Mos Eisley spaceport when Greedo was killed by the Human smuggler Han Solo. 200px|thumb|left|Breedo in Super Star Wars Breedo first appeared as multiple enemies in the Super Star Wars video game, during the Cantina level. The character appears to be a recolored version of the game's Greedo character without ears or head spikes, more resembling a Duros with its red eyes and blue skin.Super Star Wars The game's manual does not show or mention the character, but the Super Star Wars Official Game Secrets book names the enemy and suggests they are Greedo's cousin.
