Klirun
1044

The klirun was a high-tension projectile bow that was the traditional hunting weapon of the Tunroth. It became so revered by the species that it was considered a sacred weapon. Weighting around 3.5 kg, the powerful hunting bow was built with a recurve so that it remained bow-shaped even when unstrung. Designed for Tunroth strength, the bow had a heavy pull, and was capable of piercing stormtrooper armor.
