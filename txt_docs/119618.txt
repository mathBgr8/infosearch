Gk3 Cryocooler
1584

The Gk3 Cryocooler was an advanced cooling unit. These unit was installed in Eksoan's Class-4T3 power generator which provided power to the E-Web heavy repeating blaster. The Gk3 Cryocooler was intended to prevent overheating in the power generator and to reduce the likelihood of an explosive overload.
