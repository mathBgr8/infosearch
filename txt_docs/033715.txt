In 0 BBYThe New Essential Chronology she was in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina during Luke Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi's visit, where her multifacted eye was scoping out Kal'Falnl C'ndros' brood of eggs with covetous intent. She later followed Kal'Falnl C'ndros outside, hoping she would lead her to her nest. Keed'kak was friends with Swilla Corey during those visits. Kitik Keed'kak had a bad temper and could be made angry very easily. She was strong given her tiny frame, and was known for her stealthiness. Keed'kak was also very good with technology.
