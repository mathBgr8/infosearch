Mercy (Lambda-class)
1415

Mercy was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle in service to the Rebel Alliance. The shuttle was used in a rescue operation near Stars' End to board the BFF-1 bulk freighter Hampton and rescue two Rebel pilots, while two Alliance-flown Y-wings held off the escort frigate Furious attacks long enough for the Mercy to rescue the two pilots and escape into hyperspace. The briefing in the mission address the ship as Oola, while the game mission and the mission objectives identify it as Mercy.
