Med Unit
1700

Athakam Medtech's Med Unit was a mobile medbed that employed a repulsorlift engine; patients were often pushed to hospitals on such units. The unit was capable of diagnosis and treatment of its patient. It analyzed the patient and displayed any information it found on a screen. It would then begin treatment with its robotic arms and internal supply of medical materials. The Med Unit came with enough supplies for ten treatments.
