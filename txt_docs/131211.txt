Royal Intelligence Service
1117

The Royal Intelligence Service was the primary intelligence agency of the Hapes Consortium. At some point, the Hapan Intelligence Security was tasked with identifying the Lady Morwan as well as her superiors. Trista and Taryn Zel worked for Hapan Intelligence at some point.
