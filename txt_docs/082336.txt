Selonian glaive
962

thumb|90px|A Selonian glaive The Selonian glaive was a heavy combat melee weapon developed by the Selonian species. The shaft of the weapon was formed from heavy wood with one end containing an obsidian blade while the other end possessed obsidian hooks and barbs. Each glaive was specially crafted by a Selonian female, much like young Jedi are required to build their own lightsaber. It was a mark of worthiness to use this melee weapon should the wielder be able to locate the appropriate materials for herself. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Suns of Fortune Category:glaives Category:Polearm weaponry Category:Selonian culture
