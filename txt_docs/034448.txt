thumb|Tag with his Darth Tater. Darth Tater was a version of Mr. Potato Head, made to look like Darth Vader. Tag Greenley owned such a toy when he was still a baby. Bink's parents discovered his Force-sensibility when he let his toy float in the air. He was born on "Potatooine." Darth Tater originally was a real-life toy released by Hasbro in February 2005. He came complete with lightsaber, cape, helmet, shoes, eyes, nose, and teeth.
