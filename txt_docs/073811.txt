Andrew Foley
373

Andrew Foley is a Star Wars fan who designed the Hepsalum Tash in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the sixteen years and older category. He was twenty-five at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
