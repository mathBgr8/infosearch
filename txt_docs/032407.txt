Zi-Kree Sector
949

Zi-Kree Sector, or Sector 4805, was one of the many sections of the planet-wide city sprawling across the face of Coruscant. It was one of the oldest sections of the city and contained the Crimson Corridor in its Third Quadrant. It was located along the equatorial strip of Coruscant.
