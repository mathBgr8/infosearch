Kels Turkhorn
1502

Kels Turkorn was a female Human thief living around 0 ABY who partnered herself with Cecil Noone. She, along with Noone, the Sluissi slicer Hass Sonax, and the Tynnan demolitions expert Dawson double-crossed their Hutt employer after stealing a Hapan Gun of Command and made off with the luxury yacht Asaari Wind. Later, on Kabal she led the rescue of Noone from the estate of big game hunter Tyro Viveca after negotiations for the sale of the Gun of Command went sour. *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Thieves
