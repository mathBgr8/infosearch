Kn'ik Th'ronik
2357

Kn'ik Th'ronik was a Fnessian of that species' thos gender who smuggled droids and weapons. At thirty-five years old, Th'ronik was wanted by the SoroSuub Corporation for the alleged theft of their freighter, the Morgannier, when it was docked on Ord Mantell. SoroSuub Corporation believed that Th'ronik may have installed an advanced security system on the starship to prevent anyone else from intruding, and they suggested that confirmed sightings could be reported to Imperial officials to receive assistance with apprehending the alien. The bounty was set at 4,000 credits, dead or alive, with a bonus of gems worth 10,000 credits if the Morgannier was returned in good condition. Th'ronik was said to have an aversion to violence, and instead of battling bounty hunters, the being used deception and trickery to evade capture.
