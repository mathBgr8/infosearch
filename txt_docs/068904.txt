Domain Dal (worldship)
750

The worldship Domain Dal was possessed by the Yuuzhan Vong Domain Dal. After the Battle of Coruscant, the Domain Dal became Warmaster Tsavong Lah's base of operations during the Vongforming of Coruscant.
