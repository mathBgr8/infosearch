Jouffer Talamiin
1157

Jouffer Talamiin was a Rebel infiltrator. Talamiin was the lead pilot of infiltrator unit Scandium Team. Unfortunately for her, during the battle of Gendrah-Narvin, Talamiin was shot by the enemy and she suffered injuries that left her permanently handicapped. She had to retire from the lead pilot seat, and was replaced by Aven Cholus.
