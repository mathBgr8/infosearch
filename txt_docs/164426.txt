Clint Young
2823

|companies=*LucasArts *BioWare |website= }} Clinton Young, best known as Clint Young, was a senior artist for LucasArts and Lucasfilm, and worked as the lead for several projects. He has also been credited as a writer on several LucasArts titles including Star Wars: Episode I Racer (where the character Cy Yunga was named after him) and Star Wars: Bounty Hunter. Clint Young was also credited with creating 3D models in Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II. Young was also a voice actor for B1-Series battle droids and super battle droids in Star Wars: Republic Commando in addition to several other characters in the Lucasfilm / LucasArts umbrella. He has several conceptual sites including blog sites where his art work is displayed, most recent as a senior lead concept artist on the video game Star Wars: The Old Republic. {| ! Year !! Title !!
