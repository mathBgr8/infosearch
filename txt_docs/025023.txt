Jord Stokk
1457

Jord Stokk was a male Human prison guard in Desolation Alley from Corellia who lived during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted dead by the Galactic Podracing Circuit Underwriters' Union for the pre-race crippling of a visiting podracer.
