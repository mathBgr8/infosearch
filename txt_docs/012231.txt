Lo-Jad
2190

Lo-Jad was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic in the decades leading up to the Invasion of Naboo; an event which would later be recognized as part of the end of the Golden Age of the Old Republic. A Force-sensitive trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, Lo-Jad was raised to the rank of Jedi Master in the final decades of the Galactic Republic.
