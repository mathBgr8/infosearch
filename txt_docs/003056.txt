|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest=*Dex's Diner *Menaya's Sweet ShopThe High Republic: A Valiant Vow |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Galactic EmpireAftermath *New Republic }} CoCo Town, also known as the Collective Commerce DistrictStar Wars: Scum and Villainy: Case Files on the Galaxy's Most Notorious or more simply as the Coco District, was a dilapidated industrial area of the planet Coruscant. It was also the site of Dex's Diner, a local eatery owned by Dexter Jettster during the Republic Era. Hard-working laborers visited CoCo Town to congregate at the diner. During the Galactic Civil War, the Galactic Empire and the New Republic fought for control of the region.
