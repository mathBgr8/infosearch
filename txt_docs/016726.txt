Roonadan
3116

Roonadan was the fourth planet in the Corporate Sector's Bonadan system. On Roonadan, passengers could board the Lady of Mindor. During the Second Galactic Civil War, Boba Fett went to Roonadan looking for Taun We. However he soon realized that she was not on the planet, but instead on Vohai. It was similar to Bonadan, but its ecosystem was not as ruined as Bonadan's. In some of the cities there were even constructed "rivers" and greeneries to add a level of appeal to the planet's appearance.
