Deej Warrick
5137

Deej Warrick was an adventurous Ewok warrior. Deej and his wife, Shodu, had four children: Weechee, Widdle, Wicket and Winda. Deej's paternal grandfather was Erpham Warrick, the Ewok who made the famed Ewok battle wagon. One day, when Deej had taken his sons fishing, he cut himself on some sharp rokna tree fungus, which caused him to fall deathly ill. He was nursed back to health by his loving wife, Shodu, while his children helped to find the ingredients for Logray to create an antidote to cure him.
