Skylane T-534D
730

Skylane T-534D was a Skyways Flyer skylane in the Fobosi District of Coruscant. In 22 BBY, an accident was caused by a malfunctioning automated pubtrans flitter that went the wrong direction. Five speeder vehicles avoiding the flitter collided into an unmanned water silo, which was breached and flooded the apartments below. Trade Federation Senator Lott Dod was one of the six accident victims and was rushed to a medical unit. Category:Coruscant roadways
