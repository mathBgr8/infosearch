Ryll kor
1257

Ryll kor was the rarest grade of ryll. It made up approximately three percent of all ryll. Ryll kor absorbed light except in the ultraviolet range. Airen Cracken possesed gear that was able to sort true ryll kor from other grades of ryll that had been dyed black.
