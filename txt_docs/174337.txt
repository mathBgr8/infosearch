Edallia Monotheer
1635

Edallia Monotheer was a Human female, and the wife of Dalls Petothel. She was also the mother of Gara Petothel. Monotheer was trained as an actress and also as an Imperial Intelligence agent working for Director of Imperial Intelligence Armand Isard.
