Kid DXo'ln
1807

Kid DXo'ln was a male smuggler who was an acquaintance of Han Solo and took him to his first visit to Little Kessel onboard his starship, the Starfire. In 17 ABY he joined Han and Chewbacca in their attempt to rescue Lando Calrissian from the crime lord Nandreeson, along with Zeen Afit, Wynni and Sinewy Ana Blue. Unknown to Han, Kid's true loyalty lay with Nandreeson. Once the rescue party reached Nandreeson's chamber, Kid turned on Han along with Afit and Wynni.
