After Bultar Swan and Tsui Choi survived Order 66, they retreated to Eriadu. Clone stormtroopers were on the alert and searched the planet for any signs of the hiding Jedi. Led by their Clone trooper commander, a patrol squad scouted the slums of an Eriadu city for the hiding Jedi. Tsui Choi, after ditching his starfighter in the area, jumped off a rooftop and slaughtered the team.
