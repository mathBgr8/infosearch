|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=3963 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |issue=24 }} Knights of the Old Republic 24 is the twenty-fourth issue in the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series of comics. The third and final part of the Knights of Suffering story arc, it was released on December 26, 2007, and included "Galactic Republic Defense Ministry Daily Brief KD0092" as a backup news organ. Zayne Carrick's murderous masters have hunted him halfway across the galaxy. Now, his run ends with him cornered in the very place where it began&mdash;in the Jedi Temple of Taris, the current headquarters of invading Mandalorian leader Cassus Fett! Zayne's escaped before&mdash;with help from his friends and the Force&mdash;but not this time! His only choice is to fight for his life, in mortal combat with his far more talented teacher! It's the shattering conclusion to the yearlong "Days/Knights" sequence&mdash;and not everyone makes it out alive! The Knights of the Old Republic series&mdash;will never be the same again!
