Jaaruls Street
897

Jaaruls Street was a road in Cuipernam, the capital city of Ansion. It was the location on an unnamed building in which Kyakhta and Bulgan, the clanless Ansionian enforcers of Soergg the Hutt, took the kidnapped Jedi Padawan Barriss Offee in 22 BBY, during the Jedi mission to Ansion. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ansion locations Category:Roadways
