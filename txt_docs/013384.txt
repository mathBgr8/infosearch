Around the year 10 ABY,Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations is set around six years after Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which takes place in 4 ABY, according to The Essential Reader's Companion. it served as the homeworld of the Tenloss Coalition, a criminal organization specializing in contract kidnapping. Verisin was mentioned in the 1994 sourcebook Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations, written by Rick D. Stuart for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
