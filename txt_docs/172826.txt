Anuto
1877

The Anuto was a GR-75 medium transport used by the Rebel Alliance. It saw service in the Airam sector. When the decision was made to construct a Rebel shipyard in Airam space, the Anuto and its sister-ship Anuti were part of a convoy sent with the needed supplies and personnel. Anuto itself bore the construction workers who would assemble the shipyard.
