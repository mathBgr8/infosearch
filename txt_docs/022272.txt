During the Galactic Civil War, the Galactic Empire began to use armed patrols to enforce a high technology ban there. Around the same time, the rivals xenoarchaeologist Doctor Angrail Ryhl and the antiquities dealer Nardo Sau were both searching for the Um'Tal, or "Sky Stone," an ancient artifact that originated with a lost civilization and was believed to possess mystical powers. Their search led both men to Teh'Jar II, with Ryhl hiring the best fixer in the Minos Cluster to help him deal with the Imperial technology ban. Teh'Jar II was mentioned in the 1995 sourcebook Heroes & Rogues, authored by Rick Stuart and Paul Sudlow and published for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
