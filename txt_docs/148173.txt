SC-4 blaster pistol
1900

The SC-4 blaster pistol was a blaster pistol design created by SoroSuub for Imperial use. Inside the grip of each blaster, there was a tracking device, which transmitted when and where the blaster was fired through a planet's global positioning network to an Imperial tracking computer. *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *Arms & Equipment Guide *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Blaster pistols Category:Imperial Munitions products Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
