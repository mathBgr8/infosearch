Pugil
877

Pugil was a popular sport on the planet Lannik. Teams included the New Osler Flejj Beasts. The sport underwent something of a scandal in 22 BBY when Rolsat Noviee, the sport's highest-scoring and highest-paid player, was revealed to be a Clawdite changeling disguised as a Lannik. *The name "pugil" is likely a reference to "pugilism," another term for boxing.
