**Galactic Senate *Confederacy of Independent Systems |masters= |apprentices= }} Navi was a male Sephi senator of the Galactic Republic who represented Thustra and joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars. thumb|left|180px|Navi being scolded by his uncle In 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion as corruption in the Galactic Senate went along, Navi's uncle, Alaric noticed that his nephew had become narrow-minded and ignorant. Alaric elected to work against the Republic's corruption and made a decision to join the Confederacy of Independent Systems under the rule of Count Dooku. During the Battle of Thustra, Navi and his aide Moje tricked the Republic into attacking the Sephi forces, making them think that they killed Yoda. While attempting to declare to the Republic Senate that Yoda was killed during the Battle of Thustra, he was interrupted by Yoda himself.
