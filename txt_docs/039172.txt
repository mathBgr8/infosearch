Temple of the Creator
1080

The Temple of the Creator was the temple that was built to Yun-Yuuzhan, the Creator God of the Yuuzhan Vong, after the Vongforming of Coruscant into Yuuzhan'tar. When the Yuuzhan Vong dedicated the temple shortly after Supreme Overlord Shimrra's arrival, they planned to sacrifice hundreds of captives that had been captured during the Battle of Coruscant, including Senators, their families, and high ranking members of the New Republic Defense Force. However, Maw luurs began regurgitating the waste they had been consuming, causing sewage to leak onto the ground where the thousands of Yuuzhan Vong had been gathered.
