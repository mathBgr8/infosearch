|line= |model=KPR security droid |class= |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |pronouns= |sensor= |plating=Green |armament= |equipment= |affiliation= }} 6Y-2KPR was a green-domed KPR security droid manufactured by Lerrimore Droids. In 0 BBY,The Essential Chronology establishes that the event of take place in 0 BBY. this droid was in the possession of Nebit's Jawa tribe and it was stored on the same sandcrawler that held R2-D2 and C-3PO captive after they escaped from the Tantive IV. When the sandcrawler arrived at Owen Lars' moisture farm, the Jawas lined up several droids to sell, including R2-D2 and C-3PO. 6Y-2KPR was not one of the droids selected to be shown.
