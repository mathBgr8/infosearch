XJ-7 airspeeder
668

The Narglatch XJ-7 was a prototype airspeeder produced by Narglatch AirTech following the end of the Clone Wars. A Black Sun operative was known to have used a prototype XJ-7 during the early years of the Galactic Empire. It was said that Black Sun's security was so good, when he left the speeder unattended in the Wicko District, he returned three days later to find it had not been touched. *Evasive Action: Prey Category:Airspeeder models Category:Narglatch AirTech products
