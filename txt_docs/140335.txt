Malubi
666

Malubi was the malvil-tree bound to the family of the Xamster Xeran on Xagobah. It was a hundred meters tall and had lived for a thousand years. Malubi's spores allowed the young bounty hunter Boba Fett to enter into Wat Tambor's Mazariyan Citadel. Malubi had various tendrils that stemmed from its fungus stalk. These tendrils could detect body temperature and heart rate. *Boba Fett: A New Threat Category:Individual trees
