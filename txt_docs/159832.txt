Republica House
1035

Republica House was a building that housed the elite of the Galactic Alliance including Cal Omas's residence. At the building, Chief of State Omas was arrested by Colonel Jacen Solo, accompanied by Trooper Limm and Sergeant Wirut. The Republica Building was built to replace the 500 Republica after the Battle of Coruscant. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant residential buildings
