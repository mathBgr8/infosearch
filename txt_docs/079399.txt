The group oversaw the major leagues of greenputt, a ball game played on a field.Planet of Twilight which included the Professional Greenputt Tour, the Female Professional Greenputt Tour, the Asexual Professional Greenputt Tour, the Not-Dead-Yet Professional Greenputt Tour, and the Non-Limbed Professional Greenputt Tour. The committee had been formed by 22 BBY. That year, it added the Non-Sentient Professional Greenputt Tour (NSPGT) to the list of recognized greenputt leagues after certifying the Taurill Bortis and three Ambrian neeks as having consistent skill in playing the sport. The Greenputt Governing Council was first mentioned on the HoloNet News website in the in-universe issue HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, published on March 14, 2002 and written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
