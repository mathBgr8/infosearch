During his travels around the lava world, Pwwoz found a strange crystal and then later claimed to have seen the spirit of an old Human and wasn't sure what happened to him after that as he became possessed by the powers of the crystal. When Pwwoz lost the crystal, he requested that a passing spacer retrieve it for him. Upon the spacers return with the crystal, Pwwoz took and smashed it on the ground, taunted the spirit, and attempted to kill the confused spacer. When the spacer subdued Pwwoz, who later recovered and apologized for his actions, they took a shard of the crystal for themselves. Pwwoz Pwwa was a non-playable character in the massively-multiplayer online roleplaying game Star Wars Galaxies prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
