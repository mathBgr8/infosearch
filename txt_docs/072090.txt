Starlight Theater
555

The Starlight Theater was an ampitheatre on Phyrstal Island in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae. It saw many performers take the stage. Yorl Niba and Brinaloy N'Vaari were two such performers, although a rivalry formed when Brinaloy attracted larger audiences. Category:Abregado-rae locations Category:Theatrical venues
