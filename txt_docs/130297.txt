Titan Magazines
1841

Titan Magazines, also known as Titan Comics, is the world's leading publisher of officially licensed film and television magazines. The company has published Star Wars Comic UK, Star Wars: The Clone Wars Magazine (both edited by Jon Chapple), Star Wars Magazine, and Star Wars Rebels Magazine. It also published Star Wars: The Official Magazine until it began publishing Star Wars Insider (edited by Jonathan Wilkins through issue 174) during Celebration IV in May 2007.
