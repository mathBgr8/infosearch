The planet was a gas giant and had eight moons. It was located within the Kira sector in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Expansion Region. Lazerian VI was found along the trade routes known as the Harrin Trade Corridor and the Kira Run. At some point, it was surveyed by the Lazerians of Lazerian IV, another planet in the Lazerian system. Lazerian VI was created by John Terra and introduced in Twin Stars of Kira, a 1993 supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
