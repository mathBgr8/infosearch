Esau's Ridge
474

Esau's Ridge was a smuggler's hideout on Tholatin, located in a crevasse between rock walls. In 25 ABY its security chief was Plaan. Its earlier supervisors included Bracha e'Naso and Formayj. *Tyrant's Test *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities
