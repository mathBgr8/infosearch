Elya Omas
1374

Elya Omas was a Human female who was the daughter of Cal Omas, Galactic Alliance Chief of State. When Cal was held captive, he was allowed by sergeant Jonat to use her comlink to contact his daughter. The conversations were recorded and used by Darth Caedus to misinform Ben Skywalker that Omas had killed his mother. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno Category:Females Category:Galactic Alliance individuals Category:Humans Category:Individuals with she/her pronouns
