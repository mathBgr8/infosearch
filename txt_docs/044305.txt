Butcher of Renatasia
704

Butcher of Renatasia was the title given to Vuffi Raa by the survivors of a Centrality-Imperial assault on Renatasia in the early years of the Galactic Empire. They believed Raa provided information to the Imperials and their Centrality allies that led to the attack, though the true "Butcher" turned out to be an individual named Osuno Whett.
