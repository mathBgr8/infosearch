Scarab Mark 3 Assassin Droid
1846

The Scarab Mark 3 Assassin Droid, most commonly known as sleeper droids, was a product of Sienar Intelligence Systems, a division of Santhe/Sienar Technologies. These pseudo-assassin droids were held aloft by three repulsorlifts, armed with a stun blaster and a dart launcher, and were protected by an electrically-charged covering. The Mark 3 was designed to capture rather than kill its prey, in compliance with the laws of the Galactic Republic, which prohibited the use of assassination droids.
