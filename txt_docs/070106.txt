Sack of Ko Vari
1619

The sack of Ko Vari was a battle which saw the forces of Xim the Despot lay waste to the once pristine Hutt colony of Ko Vari. However, the Hutts would soon have revenge after they decimated Xim's fleet at the First Battle of Vontor. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Battles of Boonta Category:Battles of the Xim Wars
