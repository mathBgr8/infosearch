The Preventers
515

The Preventers were a vocal minority group of Carosites. They argued that their species should overcome their tradition pacifism and fight aggressively against the Galactic Empire. In this way, it was argued, more lives would be saved in the long run. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection *Alien Encounters Category:Political factions
