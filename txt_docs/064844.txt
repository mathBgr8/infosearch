TL-118 "StarHammer" Short-range Attack Ship
2911

The TL-118 "StarHammer" Short-range Attack Ship was a heavy assault starfighter manufactured by Subpro Corporation. It was 35 meters long, it carried 1 pilot, 1 gunner, and 1 week's worth of consumables. The craft could achieve 300 kilometers per hour and had a Class 3 hyperdrive. The craft's armament included 2 heavy laser cannons, 2 light laser cannons, 2 fire-linked twin laser cannons, 1 concussion missile launcher, and air-to-surface bombs. It was also shielded. Subpro hoped to sell the craft to the Galactic Republic, but the Republic transformed into the Galactic Empire. The Empire rejected the seemingly impressive craft due to its unfortunate faults: a single blow in combat to the ship's ion engines caused them to send the ship out-of-control at maximum speed. The craft was also lacking in maneuverability. Subpro's single production run was sold to local militias, private security forces, and even criminals.
