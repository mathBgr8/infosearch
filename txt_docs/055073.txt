Luma (device)
2172

A luma was a portable industrial glowrod, commonly used in mining and construction operations. In 2 ABY, Luke Skywalker and Princess Leia Organa each used a luma illuminate their path through a coway shaft on Mimban, though they quickly turned their devices off after realizing the tunnel was naturally lit by bioluminescent lichen.Splinter of the Mind's Eye Later, in 4 ABY, Leia carried a pocket luma when she rescued Luke and Dev Sibwarra from the Ssi-ruuvi Shree-class battle cruiser Shriwirr.The Truce at Bakura *Knight Errant *Star Wars Adventures 1: Hunt the Sun Runner *Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader *Splinter of the Mind's Eye *Razor's Edge *The Truce at Bakura *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Illumination devices Category:Technology
