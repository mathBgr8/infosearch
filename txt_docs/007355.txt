T3-B heavy attack tank
3744

The T3-B heavy attack tank was a heavier complement to its predecessor, the T1-B. The T3-B was used by the Rebel Alliance during the early stages of the Galactic Civil War. The T3-B's ordnance consisted of long-range proton missiles and heavy blaster cannons, making the tank relatively strong against larger targets. The T3-B was a tracked tank manufactured by Yutrane-Trackata, and was one of the first vehicles fielded by the Rebellion capable of defeating AT-AT walkers in open combat. Yutrane-Trackata later designed the T4-B heavy tank, an upgraded form of the older T3-B. Though the new T4-B was very powerful and effective during the Galactic Civil War, its implementation did not completely surpass the T3-B, and both tanks were put to great use by the Alliance Army and their other ground forces during the war. T3-B tanks were later used by the New Republic during the Seizure of Coruscant.
