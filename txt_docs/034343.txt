Neuvian sundae
1943

thumb|250px|A Neuvian sundae held by Plooz. A Neuvian sundae was an immense dessert from the Neuvia system. It consisted of frozen cream and fruit and was served in a wide bowl. Dex's Diner on the planet of Coruscant served them for 2.7 credits where they consisted of two scoops of Denta bean ice cream and a choice of Trammistan chocolate or zoochberry topping as well as atomically-aerated cream and a zherry.
