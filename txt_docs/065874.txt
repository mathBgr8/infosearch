Cato Neimoidia |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns= |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Grand Army of the Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} CT-8910 was one of many clones of the Human male bounty hunter Jango Fett created on the planet Kamino to fight in the Grand Army of the Republic. Like Fett, CT-8910 stood 1.83 meters tall. During the Clone Wars, CT-8910 served the Galactic Republic as a clone trooper. In 19 BBY, they participated in a battle on the planet Cato Neimoidia and was stationed in a clone staging area located in one of the planet's bridge cities. During the battle, the clone troopers on Cato Neimoidia were issued Order 66, a command that told them to kill their Jedi General Plo Koon. Koon's Delta-7 Aethersprite-class light interceptor was shot down and crashed into the clone staging area, killing CT-8910 and 140 other clone troopers. Moff Marcellin Wessel reported on CT-8910's death to Emperor Palpatine of the Galactic Empire that had replaced the Galactic Republic.
