Cavalcade
1578

Cavalcade was an HT-2200 medium freighter used by the BloodScar pirates in 0.5 ABY. Shakko was its captain and Tannis was its pilot. Vickers and Bisc were other crew members. Mara Jade infiltrated the ship and was forced to kill Shakko.
