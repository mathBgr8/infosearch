Pizzazz 11
998

Pizzazz 11 is the eleventh issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated August 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. The issue also includes the article "The Heroes," discussing science fiction movie heroes and featuring Luke Skywalker and Han Solo. 11
