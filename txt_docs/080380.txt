Jebble-Vanquo-Tarnith line
1587

thumb|right|250px|The Defense line during the Mandalorian Wars. The Jebble-Vanquo-Tarnith line was a boundary between Galactic Republic and Neo-Crusader forces in the Outer Rim in 3964 BBY, during the Mandalorian Wars. It was likely formed in 3966 BBY as a result of Lhosan Industries lobbying the Republic to defend Taris's resource worlds. The line was located near the planets of Taris, Jebble, Vanquo and Tarnith.
