Killing House
1090

The Killing House was a location in the clone military education complex on Kamino, used for the training of clone commandos in anti-terrorist operations. Niner recalled that the layout was changed constantly, so the trainees never knew which doors the "terrorists" were behind, and they were never told whether the ammunition they were using was live or simulated. The Killing House was also known as Arena a17. *Star Wars: Republic Commando *Republic Commando: Hard Contact *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tipoca City locations
