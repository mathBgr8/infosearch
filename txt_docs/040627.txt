Damutek of the Intendants
725

The Damutek of the Intendants was a location on Coruscant after its Vongforming into Yuuzhan'tar. The massive Damutek was built for the Intendant caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. It housed their offices, where they handled the bureaucratic matters of the day to day running of the Yuuzhan Vong empire.
