Director of Harlequin Station
1303

The director of Harlequin Station was a male individual who was in charge of deliveries to and from Harlequin Station during the Galactic Civil War. He was also in charge of Station defenses. When the station came under attack by the Viraxo, he called upon the aid of the Azzameen family, who were in the system on a delivery run. Siblings Ace and Aeron Azzameen, flying the family's two identical YT-1300 transports managed to chase off the invading ships.
