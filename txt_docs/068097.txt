*Western ReachesThe Client States and Miscellaneous Regions map in The Essential Atlas places the entirety of grid square K-18 in the Western Reaches. |sector=Tungra sector |system=Yerej system |coordinates=K-18 |xyz= |orbited= |distance= |routes= |role= |affiliation=Drekker Industries |constructed= |destroyed= |retired= |battles=Battle of Yerej |owners= |namedcrew= |captains= |registry= |aliases= |population= |interest= |services= |sectors= |modules= }} Drekker was an Imperial X7 Factory Station owned by Drekker Industries used for the production of valuable crystals in the Yerej system. During the Galactic Civil War, it came under attack by a Rebel Alliance Nebulon-B escort frigate, the Meteor, and its squadrons of Z-95 Headhunter starfighters. Fortunately, the Victory-class Star Destroyer Inexorable was able to intervene and protect Drekker. The factory had two Lambda shuttles called Fubarium and Tymtogo that were to be used to evacuate the station should it fall under heavy attack. Because it appears in one of the missions of the Rebel Alliance Operation: Quick Strike, the factory itself and the Rebel attack are canon. However, the outcome of the attack depends on the player's actions; there is no canonical answer to it.
