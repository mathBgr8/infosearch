Tallaga Garrison
609

Tallaga Garrison was a garrison located in the city of Tallaga on the planet Sucharme. The garrison contained one wing of starfighters, led by Major Veril Acamma. The Trade Federation shut down the Tallaga Garrison in an attempt to prevent armed resistance after the Federation's occupation of the planet. *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Imperial garrisons
