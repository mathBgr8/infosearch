Galactic Cantina
1468

thumb|right|250px|The proposed Galactic Cantina. The Galactic Cantina was a proposed cantina to be built on the surface of the Death Star. The cantina had a large green sign towering over the structure that read, in Aurebesh, "Cantina". Stormtroopers and Moffs alike relaxed on their breaks. The bar was high-tech and capable of synthesizing almost any drink in the known galaxy, as well as over 16,000 well-known recipes. The building also featured a soundstage for live entertainment.Death Star Designer Leland Chee has stated that, while most items, like the supercoaster and Ferris Wheel are entered for fun, much of the other information is included in the Holocron continuity database, and is canon. Regarding the Galactic Cantina itself, Chee has stated that it is best to consider it non-canon until it resurfaces elsewhere. *Death Star Designer Category:Cantinas Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station locations
