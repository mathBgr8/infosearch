Senator Organa (disambiguation)
870

Senator Organa may refer to at least two senators from Alderaan or the Alderaan sector: *Viceroy Bail Organa represented the Alderaan Sector in the Galactic Senate (32&ndash;19 BBY) and in the Imperial Senate (19&ndash;1 BBY). *His adopted daughter, Princess Leia Organa, later Leia Organa Solo, was his successor in the Imperial Senate (1&ndash;0 BBY). She later became a member of the New Republic Senate (4&ndash;11 ABY).
