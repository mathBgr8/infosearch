Borokii Council of Elders
880

The Borokii Council of Elders was the ruling group of the Borokii overclan of the Ansion Alwari. It consisted of twelve old female and male Borokii who were guarded by handpicked warriors of the tribe. The Council sat in an intricately decorated meetinghouse. Each of the Borokii subclans had a representative; that of the Situng Borokii was female. After peace was established with the Januul in 22 BBY, the Borokii Council became a part of the Council of Alwari Elders. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ansionian organizations Category:Legislatures
