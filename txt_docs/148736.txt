|plants=Dry wheat bread |other= |edible=Humans |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes=Dustcrepes and nausage |affiliation= }} Dustcrepes, also called dustcrêpes were a light Tatooinian food that consisted of dried bread and a herbivore meat filling, commonly served with nausage. Luke Skywalker was known to enjoy them. This light crepe was made of a dry type of bread, consisting of wheat. It also had a filling of herbivore meat. Dustcrepes appeared in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies. They could be made by traders, a profession that specialized in the creation of various goods, including food.
