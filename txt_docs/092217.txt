Yortal's Emporium
2794

Yortal's Emporium, also known as Yortal Ixlis's Junk Shop,Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide was a small shop located in the eastern part of Ahto City on the planet Manaan in 3956 BBY. It mainly sold used mechanical merchandise, ranging from armor plating for swoop bikes, to special droid weaponry such as flamethrowers. The store was owned and maintained by the Ithorian Yortal Ixlis. The store was originally opened when Ixlis was placed on Manaan by the Czerka Corporation. He was instructed to sell used merchandise to pay a debt he owed due to the failure of a task given to him by a Czerka executive. Sith troopers from the Sith Embassy located near the store would often visit the shop to harass the shopkeeper. In 3956 BBY, the amnesiac Jedi Revan visited the shop while trying to find the Rakatan Star Map hidden on Manaan. After Revan proved that the Sith were kidnapping young Selkath, he was offered a discount at the store for his actions. Yortal's Emporium was featured in the 2003 BioWare video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic..
