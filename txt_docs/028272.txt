*Jeremy Barlow |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=July 7, 2004 |published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 1 |reprinted in= |pages=38 |timeline=22 BBY (5 months ABG) |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Blind Force" is a 38-page comic featured in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 1. It was written by Haden Blackman and drawn by Ben Caldwell, with lettering by Michael David Thomas. It takes place five months after the First Battle of Geonosis. Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi are sent to the planet Nivek, which was shrouded in perpetual darkness. Mace Windu believes a Separatist ambush is being prepared there, but when the Jedi arrive, they are unable to see anything. Anakin and Obi-Wan then come under attack from the native Shadowmen. Because of the darkness, they can't see and are beaten back. However, by covering their eyes, they end their reliance on sight. This leaves the Force for their sight.
