Bavakar Medical Research Labs
1372

Bavakar Medical Research Labs was a medical corporation operated by the Bavakar. The company existed during the latter part of the Old Sith Wars. It was considered to have been at the forefront of developmental cybernetics research, developing a variety of medicines and cybernetic implants for personal use. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Medical companies
