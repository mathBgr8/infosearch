Loronar navy

The Loronar Navy was the defense space fleet of Loronar. They were known for their search for the pirate Reginald Barkbone. The swashbuckling freebooter subsequently recalled this pursuit as his "worst spell in space", although he claimed it was because he was stuck with a vegetarian galley droid. *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Military units
