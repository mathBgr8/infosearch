Broadside (Wookiee)
1424

Broadside was the Wookiee bartender at the Starboard Broadside Club, a cantina aboard Omze's Incredible Traveling Starport. He was overweight, and several patches of hair were missing due to scars from injuries sustained during his life as a gladiator. Rumor was he had fought alongside Omze in the gladiator pits of Loovria.
