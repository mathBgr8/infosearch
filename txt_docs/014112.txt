Battle of Althir (disambiguation)
596

The Battle of Althir can refer to two separate battles that occurred during the Mandalorian Wars: *The first Battle of Althir was a naval engagement fought over Althir III in 3976 BBY, the first battle of the Mandalorian Wars. *The second battle, also known as the Battle of Althir, was a ground battle around 3960 BBY that saw the annihilation of most of the Mandalorian ground forces.
