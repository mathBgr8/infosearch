|published in=Star Wars Insider 74 |reprinted in=*Star Wars: The Official Magazine 56 *Hyperspace |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Pearls in the Sand" is a short story written by Voronica Whitney-Robinson for Star Wars Insider 74. It takes place shortly before the events of Star Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine,The Essential Reader's Companion presumably in 1 ABY. Dusque and Tendau follow rumors of a fabled krayt dragon graveyard into the wilderness of Tatooine and are nearly done in by Zabrak robbers. Left to die in the desert, the two scientists persevere and discover the krayt spawning ground – a place where the beasts die after laying their eggs.
