Bavo Six
1796

Bavo Six was a notoriously powerful yet easy to produce truth serum. It was used by many organizations, from the Bartokk assassins to the Galactic Empire. Bavo Six would induce visual exaggeration and paranoia&mdash;shapes and outlines became distorted, and the victim became afraid of everything.
