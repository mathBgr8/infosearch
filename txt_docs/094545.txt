Gorgt
541

Gorgt trees were plants that grew on the Gamorrean homeworld of Gamorr. They were favored by the Gamorreans for the dense thick timber they provided. The wood was used to manufacture the handle of the thogk, a traditional Gamorrean club. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Plants of Gamorr Category:Trees
