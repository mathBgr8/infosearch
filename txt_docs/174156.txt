Ground-eel
1565

Ground-eels were a species of animal found on the planet Qiilura that made fluting courtship calls when attempting to find a mate. In 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, which include the mention of ground-eels, in the year 22 BBY. the Jedi Padawan Etain Tur-Mukan heard the mating calls of several ground-eels while she was hiding in a field of barq on Qiilura.
