K3, otherwise known as Kaythree, was the personal protocol droid of Mara Jade when she served as an Emperor's Hand. thumb|left|250px|Kaythree with [[Mara Jade Skywalker|Mara Jade]] During the Empire Day festivities of 1 ABY, Kaythree visited Theed with Mara Jade to issue out jobs to various non-combat Imperials, such as traders and entertainers. Kaythree made appearances as a non-player character (NPC) in the 2003 MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies. During the Empire Day live events of 2009, 2010, and 2011, he could be found in Theed handing out quests to non-combat players, such as traders and entertainers. In the game, Kaythree's appearance differed slightly in comparison to the comic.
