Baragwin Shadow Armor
690

thumb|right|200px A unique armor designed by the Baragwin, Baragwin Shadow Armor was a modifiable suit of armor that enhanced the capabilities of most stealth fields, allowing the wearer to almost seamlessly blend in with their surroundings. Though generally sold only to respected governmental agencies, during the Jedi Civil War a few units popped up in the black market. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Light armor
