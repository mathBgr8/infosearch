Mind control device
1267

thumb|Sundown's mind control device (after being disabled by [[Orin Bold).]] A mind control device was a brain-wave projection machine that allowed its user to bend the will of others, much like Jedi and other Force-users could do with the Force. It was worn about the head in a manner similar to a circlet or crown, and was small enough to be concealed by many headdresses. The first known mind-control devices were commissioned by the Baroness Omnino in the years leading up to 37 BBY as part of her plot against the Galactic Republic and the Jedi.
