Sector Four (Coruscant)
1195

thumb|250px|Sector Four under Confederate siege. }} Sector Four was a district on Coruscant. During the Battle of Coruscant of the Clone Wars, Captain Fordo and his team defended the plaza from droid fire, but were overrun. Fordo ordered the troopers to fall back, but Master Yoda soon arrived and countermanded those orders. Mace Windu managed to control a captured droid starfighter and provide support for the dwindling forces. *The Old Republic: Fatal Alliance Category:Coruscant districts
