XJ5 X-wing starfighter
2229

The Incom XJ5 X-wing starfighter, more commonly known as the ChaseX, was a model of X-wing starfighter used during the Chiss-Killik conflict by the New Jedi Order and the Galactic Reconstruction Police. It had numerous improvements to its weapons and shielding over the T-65XJ3 X-wing starfighter.
