|pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=16 }} Star Wars Tales 16 is the sixteenth issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. It was published on June 25, 2003 by Dark Horse Comics. As hard as it is to believe, Yoda wasn't always a wise old Jedi. At one time he was a wise YOUNG Jedi. Now for the first time ever, Star Wars Tales is proud to present a story from Yoda's past, telling of a near fatal brush with the dark side during his first journey to the swamp planet Dagobah.
