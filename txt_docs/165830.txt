NAVCOR Command
537

NAVCOR Command was an command unit of the Imperial Navy. Admiral Kemel Trowe was assigned to the command when they released a statement indicating that Alderaan was destroyed due to Bail Prestor Organa initiating a biowar project in association with the Alliance to Restore the Republic.
