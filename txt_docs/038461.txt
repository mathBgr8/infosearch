At some point long before 3956 BBY,The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic to 3956 BBY. Eralam lightsaber crystals were mined on the moon. When the ancient Sith bombarded the third moon of Erai, much of what remained there became shattered, with the crystals becoming exceedingly rare. The third moon of Erai was first mentioned in the 2003 BioWare video game, Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.
