Daranu
1399

Daranu was an Alcoholic beverage made from terrberries and select spicy nuts on Parein II 4, in the Sarin sector. It was fermented in Parein Sweetgreen Fruit hardnut shells. The taste was said to be very soothing and refreshing until the alcohol hit like of a blaster bolt around thirty standard minutes later. The Rodian Nio, residing on the planet Redcap, was fond of imbibing daranu, even while driving. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Alcoholic beverages
