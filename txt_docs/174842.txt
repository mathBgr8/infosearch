Fordwyn
1158

Fordwyn was a Rebel agent. He masqueraded as an Imperial officer in 0 ABY, appointed Imperial Navy Captain of the Victory-class Star Destroyer Valiant near Togoria. He aided Han Solo by throwing three pursuing bounty hunters off his trail. *Star Wars Missions 8: Togorian Trap *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Agents of the Rebel Alliance Category:Humans Category:Males
