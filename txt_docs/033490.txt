It was a single-seat vehicle with a tri-wing configuration reminiscent of the larger Lambda-class shuttle. It was a fast, compact transport, designed to be deployed from larger shuttles, and it was armed with two defensive blaster cannons for operations in combat zones. It also possessed a rear-view mirror to spot Rebel fighters from behind. A number were also acquired by the Rebel Alliance. The Alliance Intelligence flagship Shadow Raptor carried a dozen in the rear hangar.
