Cerebus (Nebulon-B2 frigate)
2509

Cerebus was a Nebulon-B2 frigate belonging to the Galactic Empire. It was under the command of Captain Monfre and had TIE Avengers from Avenger squadron carried aboard. The name Cerebus is based on Cerberus, the three-headed dog who guarded the underworld in Greek mythology, however is misspelled. The ship only appears in a single mission when the Corrupter is attacking Dunari's Casino.
