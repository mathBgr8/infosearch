Great Council (Yuuzhan Vong)
1726

The Great Council was the leadership council that advised the Supreme Overlord of the Yuuzhan Vong. The Great Council was composed of the members of the various leaders of the Elite, the Ruling Castes. Meeting in the Hall of Confluence, thousands of caste members would also attend the meetings of the Great Council. In theory, it advised the Supreme Overlord, and aided him in ruling the Yuuzhan Vong empire according to the will of the Gods.
