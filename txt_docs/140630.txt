Cadinth supported the Galactic Republic against a militant secessionist faction during the Separatist Crisis,The New Essential Chronology but by 22 BBY a pro-Separatist uprising broke out in the planet's capital city of Saskapeg, leading to Vitan's death. The Cadinth Oligarchy subsequently withdrew its previous support of the Republic, with Prime Oligarch Rankwin Fopow later revoking previous decrees and declaring that the planet was "firmly undecided" between the two groups. Bodis-Ker Vitan was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #55, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published on the HoloNet News website on May 9, 2002.
