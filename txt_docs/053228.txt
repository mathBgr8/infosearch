Bal Jaset
1805

Bal Jaset was a Human male who served as an Imperial Advisor and was the High Lord of House Melantha. Originally, Jaset had served as a Privy Councilor to Emperor Palpatine, though he was generally not considered a member of the Inner Circle. In 7 BBY, Jaset assumed his post as Lord of House Melantha. Because of his close relationship with Imperial Center, he was able to get a great deal of support from the Galactic Empire, and brought Melantha to a position of prominence over House Mecetti. In addition to his support of the Empire, Jaset was also a known womanizer.
