Badji
1235

Badji was a pirate working for Captain Andel in the Shelsha sector. A short time after the Battle of Yavin, Andel's group attempted to pirate a freighter in the space in Purnham system. However, they were stopped by the Hand of Judgment with help from Millennium Falcon. Badji was captured.
