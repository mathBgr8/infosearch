TibannaX
489

TibannaX was the special isotope of Tibanna gas used in StealthXs. The company that produced TibannaX was later purchased by Bornaryn Trading. Its ion emissions cooled in several milliseconds, making the StealthX hard to track. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Substances
