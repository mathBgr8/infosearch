Dangerous Dreams (song)
2093

"Dangerous Dreams" was a song by the band Red Shift Limit from their eponymous compilation album. The album was available in the galaxy by the time period between the years 0 ABY and 4 ABY.Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim is set in the time period between the Battle of Yavin and the Battle of Endor. Those battles are dated to 0 BBY and 4 ABY, respectively, by The New Essential Chronology, which also establishes the Battle of Yavin as the final event before the beginning of 0 ABY. The song was banned by the Imperial Board of Culture due to Red Shift Limit's anti-Imperial stance. "Dangerous Dreams" was mentioned in Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim, a supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Simon Smith and Eric Trautmann and published in 1993. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Songs by Red Shift Limit
