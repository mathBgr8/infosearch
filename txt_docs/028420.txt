Liberator (Mere)
2038

The Liberator was a Mere troop transport used by the Mere Resistance prior to the Clone Wars. The transport was flown by the Chadra-Fan mercenary Kole during the prison break at Spacestation 1138. Escorted by Nym and Adi Gallia, the Liberator docked at the space station and rescued Captain Orsai and his men, who had fled their cells to rendezvous at the station's docking arm.
