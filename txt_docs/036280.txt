Three Statutes of Aargau
1146

thumb|250px|The Statutes on display. The sacred Three Statutes of Aargau were the three principle laws enforced on the banking planet Aargau. Violation of any of the three was considered a capital offense. All visitors to the planet were required to be familiarized with the Statutes. The Statutes were as: On Aargau these crimes are punishable by immediate execution. 1. The unlawful removal of precious metals. 2. The unlawful possession of weapons by non-citizens. 3.
