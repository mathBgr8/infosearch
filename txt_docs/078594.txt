Razor tree
397

Razor trees were a type of tree indigenous to Baskarn. They had razor-sharp leaves and limbs that functioned to deter herbivores and to saw off the limbs of other trees that grew too close. The Yrashu were the only beings who knew how to climb these trees properly. Category:Trees Category:Yrashu culture
