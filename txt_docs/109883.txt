In 1 ABY, the moisture farmer hired a hunter to kill dangerous creatures that haunted the surrounding area of his farm, because he feared that they might move close to the farm and attack him and his children. The moisture farmer was mentioned in the description of a mission called "Help Me...", that was available to players of Star Wars Galaxies at mission terminals on Tatooine.
