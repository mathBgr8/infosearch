Sisar Runners
752

The Sisar Runners were a branch of the Hutt underground, led by Embra, that operated during the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 29 ABY the Runners aided the Galactic Alliance in preparation for the retaking of Coruscant. They were probably named for the Sisar Run, which was a hyperlane located within the Periphery, situated near Hutt Space. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hutt organizations
