&mdash; Based on corresponding data for Kragis system |formed= |founded=By 20 BBYThe New Essential Chronology dates the events of Obsession 2, by the time of which the Bloodboilers of Kragis had attempted to kill Durge, to 20 BBY. |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Bloodboilers of Kragis was an organization from the Expansion Region's Kragis system. At some point by 20 BBY, it attempted to kill the Gen'Dai bounty hunter Durge. Confronted by Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker during the latter's mission to Maramere in 20 BBY, Durge claimed that as with the Sith, Mandalorian, and the Bloodboilers of Kragis in the past, Skywalker would not be able to kill him. However, the Jedi was undeterred, and destroyed Durge by disintegrating him in the star Karthakk.
