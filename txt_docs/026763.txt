Lizard Keeper
1177

thumb|250px The Lizard Keepers were slaves of the Dathomiri witch Zalem. They were assigned to cut off the paws of the native lizards, the Kwi, which were the key to the Infinity Gate. Not every maleling slave became a Lizard Keeper, because that job was a very dangerous one. You only became a Lizard Keeper if you were promoted by Zalem herself.
