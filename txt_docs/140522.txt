Cinnabar Moon Retreat
765

Cinnabar Moon Retreat was a spa on Cinnabar Moon that had been abandoned due to the Yuuzhan Vong War, and was used as the headquarters of the Wild Knights. It was seven levels tall, and offered Ando mud baths, geothermal spring baths, shops, restaurants, and Storm IV Twin-Pod cloud car excursions.
