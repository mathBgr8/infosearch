Emperor's Reach
1691

The Emperor's Reach was an exalted level of Dark Side Adept loyal to Emperor Palpatine&mdash;also known as Darth Sidious, the reigning Dark Lord of the Sith. They had the unique ability to communicate across great distances with the Force. The Emperor's Reach was a position which served in a parallel with the Emperor's Hands, Emperor's Eyes, and Emperor's Voice. Palpatine entrusted the training of one member to the Prophets of the Dark Side, the member having previously been one of their own.
