|pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=22 }} Star Wars Tales 22 is the twenty-second issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. It was published on February 23, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics. The "new" Star Wars Tales continues! The vastness of the Star Wars galaxy hosts an ever-unfolding mythology, filled with character-driven stories of loss and tragedy, of heroism and redemption. This issue contributes three more such tales to the mythos&mdash;the tragic saga of mysterious Jedi Darca Nyl continues to unravel, courtesy of Cla$$war writer Rob Williams and Star Wars: Empire artist Brandon Badeaux; Two enemy soldiers accidentally left behind after Return of the Jedi Battle of Endor must survive a tribe of disgruntled Ewoks and each other in "Marooned" by Lucas Marangon; and we kick off the issue with a tie-in to the highly anticipated LucasArts video game Republic Commando.
