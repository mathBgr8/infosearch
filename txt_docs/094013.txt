Deflection emitter
666

A deflection emitter was a specialized lightsaber blade emitter designed especially for generating a blade well-suited for deflecting incoming blaster bolts. As a result, such an emitter was ideal for practitioners of Form III lightsaber combat. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Lightsaber emitters
