Knolstee
1667

Knolstee was a near-virgin planet in the Corporate Sector owned by Park Holdings Inc. Park Holdings used the planet as a layover for traveling tourists. Sightseers were welcome to stay in small luxurious orbital stations before traveling to Knolstee's surface, where they could take tours lead by Park Holdings that consisted primarily of Ithorians. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Corporate Sector locations Category:Locations along the Intra-Sector Spur Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories
