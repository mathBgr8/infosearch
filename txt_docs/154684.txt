James Anderson is a member of the who wrote a series of short comics titled Bungo n' Rusti. The first story, Bungo n' Rusti Get Carry-Out, was published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 11. Others were originally slated to appear in the Adventure Journal, but, as Dark Horse Comics held the sole Star Wars comics license, the plans were scrapped.
