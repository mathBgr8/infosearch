LEGO City
2535

LEGO City was a gated city in the Known Universe. Despite not being located in the galaxy, the conflict of the Skywalker family was felt there through a Disturbance in the Force. Six Humans lived in LEGO City, as well as three GNK power droids and various plants and non-sentient species. Additionally, several houses and vehicles were located in LEGO City. LEGO City first appeared in the 2006 video game LEGO Star Wars II: The Original Trilogy. It is a bonus level that could be unlocked after collecting 60 gold bricks. The goal of the level is for the player to collect one million studs located in the city.
