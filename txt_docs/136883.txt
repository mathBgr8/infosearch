Starlight Entertainers
518

The Starlight Entertainers were a troupe of performers who were popular in the Outer Rim at the beginning of the Jedi Civil War. Their manager, Bib Surool, was on Taris at the time of its bombardment by Darth Malak. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Entertainment companies
