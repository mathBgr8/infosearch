Dxulininan
1201

Dxulininan was the author of a book entitled Warm Thoughts for Invertebrates. On the date 13:2:28, according to HoloNet News, Dxulininan's book was number-three on a list of best-sellers. Dxulininan was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #45, a 2002 in-universe website. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content.
