Uffel was a droid-manufacturing moon run, inhabited, and owned entirely by droids, whom settled the moon in 90 BBY. Thus, this made it the target of the technophobic Red Knights of Life, an allied group of the Yuuzhan Vong invaders who hated any form of mechanical technology.
