XT labor droid
673

thumb|right|XT-8, a modified XT droid The XT labor droid was a stocky, barrel-chested droid with strong arms which hung to its knees. It was produced around 20,000 BBY and, though its personality matrix was crude by the time of the Galactic Civil War, it was high technology when it was first produced. One such droid, XT-8, had been modified to become an assassin droid. Category:General labor droid models
