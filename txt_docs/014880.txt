While on Challon, Leia Organa tried to convince local Governor Tchai to support the Rebel Alliance. Organa was forced to kill Giles Durane, her "weapons master," in self-defense. The people of Challon often referred to inhabitants of planets in the Outer Rim Territories derisively as "Rimworlders."
