T'iil
2562

T'iil was a fragrant Alderaanian flowering plant. The flower itself was shaped like a trumpet, and bloomed with a vibrant yellow color. On Naboo, shaaks and other large herd animals helped pollinate the t'iil. The extract from the white core of the plant's roots was considered a treatment for sore throats. The leaves of the plant were used to create a soothing tea. The single drop of nectar found in the t'iil was harvested and used to distill l'lahsh.
