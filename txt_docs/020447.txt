Gaeriel Capens
1592

Gaeriel Capens was a musician during the last years of the Galactic Republic. She was a member of the Melody Fellowship. In 22 BBY, it was revealed that her two latest hits, The Sequential Passage of Chronological Intervals and a remix of Shwock Dubllon (originally by Dembaline), were actually produced by RetSpan Audionics TribBots.
