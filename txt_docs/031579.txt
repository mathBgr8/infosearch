Ralltiir tiger
1556

Ralltiir tigers were fierce carnivores native to the planet of Ralltiir. Although they appeared innocent when young, they quickly turned into savage and unpredictable creatures. Ralltiir tigers were prone to fits of blood madness, a craze that overcame them with a desire to kill everything around them.
