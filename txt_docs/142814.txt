Casus Belli 62
782

Casus Belli 62 was a French roleplaying magazine from March of 1991. It contained many roleplaying articles, including one for the Star Wars West End Games Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game called "Hold-up galactique" (in English, "Galactic hold-up") which was written by Jean Balczesak.
