Asilyr
886

The Asilyr tribe was a nomadic clan of Humans on Socorro known for being friendlier than the planet's other tribes. Though initially suspicious of newcomers, the Asilyr would eventually welcome strangers into their tribes and made effective guides through the Western Doaba Badlands. Socorro's sun, Sokor, was considered an enemy by the Asilyr, who carried on the activities of daily life at night. *The Black Sands of Socorro Category:Culture of Socorro Category:Families
