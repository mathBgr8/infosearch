Corrun Falt
4047

|masters= |apprentices= }} Corrun Falt was a male Human employee of Czerka Corporation on Citadel Station who greatly disliked Jana Lorso, his superior. The Human male Corrun Falt became an employee for Czerka Corporation following the end of the Jedi Civil War in 3956 BBY.Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide dates the Battle of Rakata Prime to 3956 BBY. After working against many other employees to get a promotion to a meaningful position, the corporation sent him to the Outer Rim Territories to manage a military reclamation effort. Falt ended up working under Czerka executive Jana Lorso on Citadel Station on the devastated planet Telos IV. There, he lived in apartment A3 in Residential Module 082, spent his time in the cantina in Entertainment Module 081, and had a stagnant career. Falt disliked having Lorso as his superior, as she was the one who took away his position. Meetra Surik convinced Falt to grant her access to the Czerka mainframe, exposing Lorso's illegal dealings and allowing her to thwart Czerka and ensure success for the Ithorians' restoration efforts on Telos. Falt would later order Czerka's mercenaries to keep Surik out of Czerka controlled restoration zones on Telos's surface. Though Falt only has a significant role if Surik sides with the Ithorians, he was also supposed to figure into the Czerka storyline if Surik sides with Lorso. In this version, Falt is also plotting against Lorso. Surik could either help Falt, or report his treachery to Lorso.
