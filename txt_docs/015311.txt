|imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Kodai was a remote planet in the Belderone sector of the Outer Rim Territories. Some time prior to 29 BBY, its entire surface had been flooded save for the city of Rena. thumb|left|Kenobi and Skywalker on their mission to Kodai, soon after which Anakin constructed [[Anakin Skywalker's first lightsaber/Legends|his first lightsaber]]The Jedi Order took advantage of Kodai's oceans to dispose of the holocron of King Adas. The Sith holocron was buried under millions of tons of water, deep in an underwater geyser crater, in the hopes that no one else would fall under Adas's influence and the powerful pull of the dark side.Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force In 29 BBY, the Jedi, with the aid of the Quermian expert on the Sith, Murk Lundi, recovered the holocron from beneath Kodai's oceans.
