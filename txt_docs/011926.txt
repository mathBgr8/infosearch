Katraasii Spaceport
1137

Katraasii was a spaceport city, located on the planet of the same name. During the New Republic era, it was controlled by the Imperial Remnant, and was frequented by criminals like Abron Mar. Mara Jade went there to look for Mar while seeking Takara, but fell into a trap. It was known to have some minor pirate activity, and also contained no fewer than three cantinas. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith Category:Cities Category:Imperial Remnant locations
