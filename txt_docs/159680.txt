Unidentified Phindian captain
1852

An unidentified Phindian captain was an officer of the New Republic Defense Fleet who served as the captain of Pride of Honor during the Battle of Coruscant and later confronted Senators "Sneakaway" and "Scramblefree" on Mon Calamari in 28 ABY. As Luke and Mara Jade Skywalker and a crowd of officers watched, the Phindian accused the senators of causing the deaths of thousands of New Republic officers thanks to their commandeering of Fleet assets during the Battle of Coruscant.
