Ryloth Ventures
1194

Ryloth Ventures was a trade-based corporation located on Ryloth. During the Clone Wars, it was headed by Has Doneeta, with Miles Croft as a business negotiator. Senator Vien'sai'Malloc was interested in having Ryloth Ventures trade on Devaron.
