Eixes Valorum
2235

Eixes Valorum was a member of the Valorum family of Coruscant. He eventually became a senator and later served as Supreme Chancellor, some time prior to 62 BBY. His Chancellorship was first proposed at one of the annual elite gatherings on the Sojourn moon hosted by Caar Damask, and was known for a sense of style not seen again until the chancellorship of Thoris Darus.Darth Plagueis He was the last Supreme Chancellor to give a State of the Republic address prior to Palpatine.Labyrinth of Evil In Labyrinth of Evil, he is named as Valorum Eixes. This is likely an editing mistake, as nowhere else is the family name given first.
