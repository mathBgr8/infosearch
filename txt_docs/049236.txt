Phindian (language)
1052

Phindian language was the native language of the Phindian species from Phindar. Phindian was formed basically of soft sibilants and vowel sounds, which were significantly longer than those of other languages. Most Phindians were fluent in both Phindian and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
