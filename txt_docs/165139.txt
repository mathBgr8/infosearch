Spirited Spirits
1082

Spirited Spirits was a business located in Hedrett that dealt in mixed alcoholic beverages. It was owned by Yves Arsen and its head of research and development was Shayne Gerrare. One of its most famous and successful products was the Double-Dip Outer Rim Rumdrop, which was created by Gerrare. In 31 BBY, Gerrare and most of the stockboys of Spirits disappeared to join the Cularin resistance; Arsen regretted only Gerrare's absence.
