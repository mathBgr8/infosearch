Hard-biscuit
904

Hard-biscuits were common foodstuffs of the Drall and were frequently enjoyed by those of all classes. Rich and flavorful, they were also overbaked in order to produce an incredible hardness that made them all but inedible to Humans. Even for Drall they required diligent chewing to eat; they enjoyed the act of chewing the biscuits as much as the flavor itself. *Assault at Selonia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Biscuits and cookies
