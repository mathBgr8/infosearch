Baanu Rass
3059

The Baanu Rass was a Yuuzhan Vong worldship. It was one of the oldest worldships in the Yuuzhan Vong fleet to survive the trek from their galaxy to the known galaxy. It was also one of the largest worldships, at 120 kilometers across. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, Baanu Rass was placed in orbit around Myrkr, where the Yuuzhan Vong used it as a cloning facility to create the voxyn. Baanu Rass was irreparably damaged by the Myrkr strike team, and was abandoned shortly after the Mission to Myrkr. However, the hulk of the worldship remained in orbit around Myrkr, dead and open to the vacuum of space. In 40 ABY it was visited by Jacen Solo and Tahiri Veila.
