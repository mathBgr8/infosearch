Cattena Squadron
1530

Cattena Squadron was a stormtrooper unit based at the Academy of Carida on the planet Ord Carida. Brenn and Dellis Tantor began to shadow the unit after having failed to be accepted into the Stormtrooper Corps. Impressed by their dedication, Cattena's captain allowed the two to join the unit as "honorary stormtroopers". Though controversial, this decision went unchallenged. Cattena Squadron served in the Battle of Kalaan in 0 ABY. During that battle, one of the squadron's sergeants was killed and Brenn Tantor took command of his unit.
