Ulqib MicroTronics
1952

}} Ulqib MicroTronics was an electronics and custom programming company. The company did not generally manufacture goods, instead analyzing the products of other companies and then producing kits and computer programs. This allowed customers to customize their purchases, such as droids, computers, or even automated machinery, to function at maximum utility and efficiency. Ulqib was one of the contributing sponsors to the Corporate Sector Authority. The company's products included the BX-333 Biorecorder and the droid customization kit. The Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook originally identified the company as Ulqib MacroTronics.
