Stacy
2806

Stacy was an X-wing pilot serving the Rebel Alliance under General Carlist Rieekan around the time of the Battle of Yavin. Shortly after the battle, she was attracted to and flirted with Han Solo, who returned her attention, mostly to antagonize Princess Leia Organa. The appearance of Stacy in the novel Allegiance was an homage to the protagonist of the Pink Five fan films, who harbors an infatuation with Han Solo and exhibits antagonism towards Leia. The first film, which was directed by Trey Stokes and featured Amy Earhart as Stacy. Author Timothy Zahn, a fan of Pink Five," included Stacy in Allegiance as "a little nod to all the fan films in general, but focused on Pink Five."
