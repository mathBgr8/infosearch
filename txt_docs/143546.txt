Paralysis cord
1510

A paralysis cord, also known as stun cord, was a weapon used by IG-88 to paralyze a target for live capture. The cord was similar in function to a whipcord launcher, in that when fired it unspooled a large amount of animated cord, that proceeded to wrap itself around a target. Unlike a whipcord however, a paralysis cord kept on unspooling, until it had completely bound an enemy in its coils. Essentially, this paralyzed the victim, as the tight coils disabled a sentients ability to move. Some models of paralysis cord had conductive metals interwoven through its fabric, in which after enveloping an enemy, a powercell would electrify the cord, and further incapacitate the target. *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:Electrical weaponry Category:Net and cord launchers
