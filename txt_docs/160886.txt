It was written by Scott Pelland and Nathan Bihldorff, Levi Buchanan and Jason Leung. The credited publisher for Star Wars: Episode I Racer Official Nintendo Player's Guide was was M. Arakawa with Yoshio Tsuboike as associate publisher, Leslie Swan as editor in chief, Scott Pelland as executive editor, Jessica Joffe as copy editor and Nancy Ramsey as production coordinator. Pelland was the guide's senior writer, Nathan Bihldorff, Levi Buchanan, Jason Leung were each credited as "Writers/Editors", and Eric Bush, Michael Leslie and Jeffrey Storbo served as game consultants. Kim Logan served as art director, with Jay Wergin as "Design/Prepress Supervisor". The guide's strategic layout was done by Kyle Stanley Hunter and Christopher Inclenrock for V-Design, Inc. under V-Design's art director and design coordinator Yoshi Orimo and Sonja Morris, with art by LucasArts and Lucasfilm. Natt Thangvijit, David Waterworth and Van Williams were credited as responsible for electronic prepress, with Todd Dyment as prepress assistant. The guide was printed in the United States and published by Nintendo of America Inc. Episode I Racer Official Nintendo Player's Guide
