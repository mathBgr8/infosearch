Vidge
682

Vidge was a tall, pale Langhesi male who was living on Zonama Sekot during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 29 BBY, he was the chief shaper of a shipbuilding team that grew and formed Sekotan Personal Starships from seed-partners. He had large, wet eyes and his clothes were wet and sprinkled with bits of glowing slime. *Rogue Planet Category:Langhesi Category:Males Category:Sekotans
