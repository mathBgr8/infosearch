Hoona
1602

Hoona was a female Phlog from the Forest Moon of Endor. She was an adolescent just prior to the Battle of Yavin. During this time, Umwak, the Dulok shaman, made her inhale the fumes of a love potion created by Logray which caused her to fall in love with the Ewok Wicket Wystri Warrick.
