|subscribers= |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue= |series= }} Vader: The Ultimate Guide is a one-shot souvenir magazine about Darth Vader. It features a great deal of photographs, original art, and various other content. It debuted at Comic-Con International in July 2005. *Introduction *"The Story of Anakin" by Daniel Wallace - Extensive biography of Anakin/Vader **Including "The Great Remorse of Vader" by Joe Corroney *"The Path of Evil" by Abel G. Peña - Brief history of the Sith and timeline *"Vader Tech" by Abel G. Peña - Vader's armor, the lightsabers he used, and his vehicles *Bringing Vader to Life - How Vader's appearance was created through concept art and costume design. Also a paragraph on his breathing. *"In His Image" by Karen Traviss - Short story: Vader finds clone templates and fights an assassination attempt. *The Men in Black - Interviews with James Earl Jones and Hayden Christensen, biographies of David Prowse, Jake Lloyd, Bob Anderson and Sebastian Shaw. *Villain with a Thousand Faces: The History of Darth Vader Collectibles by Pete Vilmur *Vader Trivia Category:Promotional magazine issues Category:Vader: The Ultimate Guide articles
