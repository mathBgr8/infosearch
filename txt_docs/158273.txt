Isko Talgal
1380

Isko Talgal was a female Mandalorian warrior who lived prior to and during the Second Galactic Civil War. Talgal was a grim-faced woman with graying black hair. She was present at a meeting between Boba Fett and several other Mandalorians in Zerria's Bar on Drall in 40 ABY.
