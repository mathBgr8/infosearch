Tendler Vek
1436

Tendler Vek was a male Human gambler who cheated a group of nobles in the Core Worlds and was forced to flee to the Rim. He frequented the CrossWind Saloon in Storrd Township on Seregar. Tendler was smooth, suave and well-groomed. He frequently puffed on a cigarra. Born in the Outer Rim Territories, he started out gambling after bad land deals bankrupted him and his family. He traveled through the Core Worlds, winning a fortune before suspicious nobles began to investigate him. He quickly fled, spending most of his wealth in the getaway. Tendler was always friendly and had a positive outlook on life. He was not prone to violence, and preferred to know everything he could about a situation before proceeding.
