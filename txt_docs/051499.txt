BAS Research & Development
849

BAS Research & Development was a division of the Bank of Aargau Security, Ltd., itself a subsidiary of the Bank of Aargau. It developed various scanning and security technologies for the Bank of Aargau Security, including the BAS44 Customs-Grade Holoscanner. Category:Technology companies
