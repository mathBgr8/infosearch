Vulca Minerals
1870

Vulca Minerals was a corporation active during the Rebellion era.Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races During the Imperial era, Vulca Minerals was a major player in the meleenium trade. Each year, they sent a starship to the high-gravity planet of Af'El, the only known source of natural meleenium. They traded fresh foodstuffs to the native Defel in return for meleenium. Vulca Minerals also managed yttrium mining in Guiteica. Ayla Shar worked with Vulca Minerals during this time. Due to the pushy attitude of Vulca, native Bitthævrians (particularly the m'Yalfor'ac Order) considered killing the team commander.
