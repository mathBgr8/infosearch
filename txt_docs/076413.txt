Dometown (Coruscant)
1167

Dometown was a kilometer-wide space of homes and other living areas carved out of Coruscant's lower levels by a bevy of investors, including Lando Calrissian, for the hopes of luring people away from the crowded upper city. When none of the planned hundreds of thousands of families flocked there, it eventually passed into the hands of the New Republic Defense Force. The Defense Force leadership occasionally used it as a meeting area.
