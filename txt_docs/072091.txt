Yorl Niba
1078

Yorl Niba was a male Twi'lek singer at the Starlight Theater on Phyrstal Island in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae during the Galactic Civil War. He was openly hostile to Brinaloy N'Vaari. This was due to his envy over her ability to attract far greater crowds.
