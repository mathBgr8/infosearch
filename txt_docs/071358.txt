Bravo Seven
709

Bravo Seven was the callsign given to a pilot in Bravo Squadron during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. Essara Till served as Bravo Seven prior to the Invasion of Naboo. She was killed in a mercenary attack while protecting Queen Amidala after a failed negotiation with the Trade Federation. *Star Wars: Starfighter Bravo 07
