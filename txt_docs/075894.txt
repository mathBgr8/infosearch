Capo (Peace Brigade)
1315

Capo was a Rodian male. He was a Peace Brigader disguised as one of the NRI guards covertly stationed aboard Queen of Empire to watch over Elan and Vergere to oversee their transfer from Myrkr to Coruscant. He was shot by Showolter but escaped from the cabin alive.
