Yttrium
3753

Yttrium was a rare mineral. It was among the several different ores and minerals that, by the time of the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, were being extracted from the crust of the rocky Hutt SpaceThe Essential Atlas planet Nar Kreeta. Extensive subterranean deposits of yttrium were also located on Guiteica, a planet in Wild Space's Kadok Regions. &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Tertiary Kadok system At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. when the Vulca Minerals company attempted to aggressively negotiate with Guiteica's native Bitthævrian species the rights to mine yttrium, the m'Yalfor'ac Order, the dominant Bitthævrian continental army, nearly killed the company's Team Commander. Since that incident, all of Guiteica's exports were managed by the company Kadok Resources. Yttrium was first introduced to the Star Wars franchise in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
