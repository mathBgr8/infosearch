Baran Do Sages
2503

The Baran Do Sages were an ancient Force-based Kel Dor organization. They protected their homeworld of Dorin and were famous for their well-honed precognition skills, which enabled them to launch early evacuations in cases of deadly storms and advise government officials. For centuries, the Jedi Order recruited Dorin's strongest Force-sensitives, which ultimately caused the Baran Do order to fall into obscurity sometime before the Empire arose. Still, they had a working relationship with the Jedi, who they were allied to. The Sages were entirely subservient to the will of the Force, and sought to only act at precisely the right place and time to avoid undesired consequences. If they did not meditate before making major decisions or acted rashly, this could take a toll on their mental health, yet conversely they were strong in the virtue of discipline and understood how to use lightning without needing to draw on the dark side.
