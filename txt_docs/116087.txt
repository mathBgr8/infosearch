Alakandor
788

The Alakandor was a freighter that landed on Vogga the Hutt's dock on Nar Shaddaa. The landing was delayed however, due to a mix-up of the ID signatures of the Alakandor, the Silver Zephyr, and Toorna's Profits. Fassa, the dock manager, asked the Jedi Exile to correct this. With a little tinkering, the Exile distributed power to the three pylons and assigned priorities for the ships as follows: *1st: Silver Zephyr *2nd: Alakandor *3rd: Toorna's Profits *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Freighters
