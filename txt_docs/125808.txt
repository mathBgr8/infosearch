Planet of the Amazons: The Tale of the Pirate Ship Mourning Glory
1023

Planet of the Amazons: The Tale of the Pirate Ship Mourning Glory was a bestselling memoir of Grov Bricker's capture and time among the Hapans. The book provided one of the few views into Hapan society prior to its formation of diplomatic relations with the New Republic in 8 ABY. Its mysterious topic made the book wildly popular, despite its mix of fact and fiction. Category:Journals and memoirs
