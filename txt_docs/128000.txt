Inquisitor-4
1801

Inquisitor-4 was a World Devastator. It was not as powerful or big as Silencer-7, and would have been a secondary ship in a fleet similar to the one that attacked Mon Calamari. However, it was capable of leading an attack on a less-defended world. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire Category:World Devastators
