Tun Aduban
2010

Tun Aduban was the largest city and only spaceport on the Outer Rim world of Aduba-3. The spaceport consisted of one control tower, manned by a broken-down PK droid. No landing directions were given and there were no landing pads; incoming ships merely found a spot on the desert. The population of Tun Aduban was comprised mostly of drifters and unlucky spacers. The city had little to offer beyond cantinas, brothels, and merchants selling illicit goods.
