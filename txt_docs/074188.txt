Sinick Kritkeen
2200

General Sinick Kritkeen, a top-ranking COMPNOR official, was the planetary overseer of Aruza during its subjugation by the Galactic Empire. He bore a slight resemblance to Han Solo. The Aruzans hired the bounty hunter Dengar to assassinate him. Kritkeen's resemblance to Solo made Dengar enjoy killing him. In fact, while doing the deed, Dengar deluded himself into believing that Kritkeen actually was Solo. The COMPNOR official was killed when Dengar put a concussion grenade in his mouth, threw him off a cliff, and shot him twice in mid-air as he fell.
