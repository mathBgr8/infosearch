The hyperlane known as the Ardan Cross connected it to the Feldwes and Kalishik systems, and another hyperlane linked it to the Capella system. The trading planet Arda II held a neutral stance during the early years of the Galactic Civil War. After the spy Mag Doum reported to Darth Vader that there was a Rebel presence on planet, the rulers of Arda II openly supported the Rebellion in fear of Imperial reprisal. The planet was ruled by the Qualo. During the Stenax Massacres, Arda II was invaded by the Stenax. Tun Wala was a major city on the planet.
