Psychokinesis
811

thumb|right|250px|A [[Human/Legends|Human using psychokinesis to levitate a boulder.]] Psychokinesis was a Ta-Ree spell capable of being wielded by Ascendantss on the planet Kathol. The spell allowed the caster to levitate and move objects at will. Ascendants could levitate several objects at once, and could even use the power to levitate themselves. In emergencies, the power could even be used as a primitive space drive. *Endgame Category:Ta-Ree spells
