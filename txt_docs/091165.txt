Micro-pulse blaster
1211

Micro-pulse blasters were a form of blaster weapons used during the time of the Dark Wars. The weapon worked by firing short controlled bursts of coherent energy rather than a fully powered energy blast. The weapons were usually created from thoranium, allowing them to release more powerful discharges than standard blasters, even larger rifles. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Micro-pulse blasters
