Skoth
791

Skoth was a dried-up riverbed and settlement on the planet Prakith, and was the source for a number of valuable ores. Prall, a mining company controlled by Imperial Warlord Foga Brill, oversaw much of the riverbed excavation. *Shield of Lies Category:Cities on Prakith Category:Settlements
