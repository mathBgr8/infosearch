Star Cruiser (disambiguation)
701

Star Cruiser might refer to several different subjects: Canon: *Star Cruiser: A type of capital ship that came in many varieties but mostly referring to the ships made by the Mon Calamari. Legends: *Star Cruiser: A general term for a broad category of spacegoing capital ships. *Mon Calamari Star Cruiser: a designation for large spaceships produced by the Mon Calamari. *Star Cruiser (Galactic Empire): Several spaceships used by the Galactic Empire. A model of air cruiser and a Super Star Destroyer.
