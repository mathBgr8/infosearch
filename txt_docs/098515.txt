Moras
601

The Moras were a faction that imprisoned Ebenn Q3 Baobab and forced him to serve in the Mora Infantry. The Moras are referenced in Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide. The book fails to note whether the Moras are a species, a government, or something else altogether. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Political factions Category:Undefined social groups
