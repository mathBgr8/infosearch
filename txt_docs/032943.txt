Annealer

Annealer was an Acclamator-class assault ship of the Republic Navy. During the Clone Wars, Annealer was dispatched to defend Duro. There it was destroyed by General Grievous during Operation Durge's Lance. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Acclamator-class assault ships Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
