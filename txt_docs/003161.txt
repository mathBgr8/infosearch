Star Wars Adventure Journal 7
3907

The Star Wars Adventure Journal 7 is the seventh issue of the role-playing magazine Star Wars Adventure Journal. It was published by West End Games in August of 1995 and contained several articles, role-playing adventures and short stories. Take a fascinating voyage through the Star Wars galaxy with the Star Wars Adventure Journal. Each issue features exciting adventures, source material and tales from the Star Wars universe! The Star Wars Adventure Journal gives Star Wars fans original locations, characters and plots in their favorite universe that they cannot find anywhere else. New York Times-best-selling author Timothy Zahn weaves a tale on the origins od one of his most infamous Star Wars characters! When a Victory-class Star Destroyer captain pursues Rebel smugglers into Unknown Space, he and his ground troops get more than they bargained for. Are the attacks on their encampment caused by the smugglers, primitive alien natives, or someone else hiding in the forest? Find out in Mist Encounter. And don't miss the special preview story featuring characters from Rogue Squadron, the first in Michael A. Stackpole's series of four X-Wing novels to be released in 1996! Missed Chance follows X-wing pilot Corran Horn on the run from Imperial agents and the cunning schemes of an insignificant but power-hungry Imperial bureaucrat. Find out more about the upcoming X-Wing novels in an exclusive interview with the author.
