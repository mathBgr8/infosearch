|type=Trade Paperback |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0-7869-1963-9 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Living Force Campaign Guide is a paperback supplement to the Wizards of the Coast Star Wars Roleplaying Game. It was written by Robert Wiese and Andy Collins, published March 2001. The Guide provides an overview of the Cularin system, the setting of the Living Force roleplaying campaign. It details the history, the planets and locations, and the movers and shakers of the system, finishing up with short descriptions of some of the system's mysterious locations. *Introduction **Irolunn
