Nonsu
1176

Nonsu was a company that sold information on the planet Metellos. The company was headquartered in the Stratablock 7 building located in Ektra city. Nonsu also was involved in the production of materials that helped expand the city of Ektra. Nonsu was unchallenged except for the rival company CarsinShare which competed for business with the Nonsu corporation.
