It was home to the lethal predator known as the needle beast, which the Defel miner and undercover Rebel Alliance spy and saboteur Thar'quan once encountered by the time of the Galactic Civil War.Cracken's Rebel Operatives Dithanune's name was introduced via a mention of the "Dithanune needle beast" in Cracken's Rebel Operatives, a 1994 West End Games sourcebook published for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The section mentioning the creature was authored by Craig Robert Carey with J. Michael Denbo. 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia subsequently established Dithanune as a distinct planet.
