*Michael Mikaelian *Stacie Fiorito *Michelle Vuckovich *Scott Chernoff |cover artist= |contributors=Jason Fry |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=April 2, 2002 |subscribers=March 15, 2002 |pages=98 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=59 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 59 is the fifty-ninth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was released to newsstands on April 2, 2002. This issue celebrated the twenty-fifth anniversary of the Star Wars franchise in the lead-up to the theatrical release of the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. *A Word From George Lucas *Rebel Rumblings *Attack of the Clones Update *Star News: From the World of Lucasfilm *Gamescape &mdash; Enter the Arena *Bookshelf &mdash; Living the Rebel Dream *Comics Link &mdash; Attack of the Clones *Set Piece: Rebel Alliance Briefing Room *Scouting the Galaxy &mdash; Midnight Madness: Episode II *Crossword &mdash; The Other Sci-Fi Work *Star Wars Q&A *The Last Page *"Han Solo: Ford Tough" *:It's hard to imagine a world without Harrison Ford as Han Solo or Indiana Jones.
