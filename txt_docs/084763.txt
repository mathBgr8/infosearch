Wedge Antilles Boulevard
2211

Wedge Antilles Boulevard was a street in Coronet City, the capital of the planet Corellia. It was once known as CCC-39, but had been renamed by 40 ABY in honor of Wedge Antilles, one of the most well-known Corellian heroes at the time, although some maps still displayed the former name. Wedge Antilles Boulevard crossed under the wider, elevated Five Brothers Avenue, which had much more traffic than the boulevard. A team of Jedi rode a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle across Wedge Antilles Boulevard in 40 ABY after unsuccessfully attempting to arrest Five World Prime Minister Aidel Saxan. Wedge Antilles Boulevard was created by Aaron Allston for his 2006 novel Legacy of the Force: Betrayal, the first volume in the Star Wars: Legacy of the Force series. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Coronet roadways
