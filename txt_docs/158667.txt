Luminous Gardens
1012

The Luminous Gardens was a health spa on a hill above the Boiling Sea of Drall. It was operated by Drusa, a young female Drall. The spa featured hot spring and mineral baths, mud soaks, aromatherapy, and masseuses.Coruscant and the Core Worlds The Fountain was a restaurant located in the spa.
