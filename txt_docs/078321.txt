Sith regenerator
1260

An implant created by the ancient Sith, the Sith regenerator stimulated cell replication of its "host," allowing them to heal. Spies were often equipped with such devices as being behind enemy lines prevented them from seeking medical treatment. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cybernetic implants Category:Sith artifacts
