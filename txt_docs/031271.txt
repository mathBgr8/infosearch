|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=Irish |occupation=ActressCaravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure |companies= |website= }} Fionnula Flanagan (born December 10, 1941) portrayed Catarine Towani in Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure. Flanagan was one of the few actors (along with Phil Fondacaro, Felix Silla, Clive Revill and Jason Wingreen) to be involved in both a Star Wars film and a Star Trek production. Flanagan played Juliana Tainer in the Star Trek: The Next Generation episode "Inheritance", Enina Tandro in the Star Trek: Deep Space Nine episode "Dax", and Ambassador V'Lar in the Star Trek: Enterprise episode "Fallen Hero".
