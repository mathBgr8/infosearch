Imperial Arms
1163

Imperial Arms was an arms company under the Galactic Empire. It produced mostly small holdout blasters. One of the most popular devices to come from Imperial Arms was the Model 22T4 ISB Special hold-out blaser. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook Category:Arms manufacturing companies
