Ranat (language)
853

Ranat language was the native language of the Ranat species from Rydar II. It had a written form, only known by the Ranats themselves. Although Ranats could learn other languages, such as Basic, most of them could not find any use for that. *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
