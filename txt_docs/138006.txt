Paricha
564

Paricha was a soft-flavored spiced drink made from paricha root. Served warm, it had a pleasing, gentle aroma. During the Siege of Coruscant, Leia Organa Solo feared that she might be having her last taste of paricha for a long time, as the root required to make it had to be imported from offworld. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *The Last Command Category:Beverages
