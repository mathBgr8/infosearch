The Atomic Punk
1141

The Atomic Punk was a closed-down nightclub on the planet Yukka, in the heart of the capital city's dust belt. It was used as a Rebel base during the Galactic Civil War. In this incarnation, bunks and equipment were all over the floor, and the D.J.'s booth was used as a monitoring station, where an engineer would always monitor the system traffic using a large sensor array that the Rebels had acquired. A group of Rebels came to the base after Rebel Intelligence learned of the D-wing, which was being shown at the Cynestra Space Craft Show. They met up with Captain Reeves, who gave them all the information necessary for them to capture the D-wing prototype. Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic military bases Category:Bars and clubs
