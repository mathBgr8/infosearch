Kligson's Moon, also known as Droid World, was a mobile space station inhabited by the cyborg Kligson and the many droids that he had rescued. Due to its high level of technology, it was targeted by the technophobic Yuuzhan Vong. However, the Yuuzhan Vong were repelled by the Iron Knights.
