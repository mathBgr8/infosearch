Murgo Choke
2208

The Murgo Choke was a hyperlane connecting the Utegetu Nebula to the Rago Run. It passed between two binary systems: one consisting of a yellow and an orange star, the other of a blue giant and a crimson dwarf. The intense gravity wells exerted by the four stars prevented any sort of hyperspace travel, hence the name "Murgo Choke".
