TIE Hangar Bay 62-N9
972

thumb|Diagram of TIE hangar bay 62-N9 TIE hangar bay 62-N9 was a hangar aboard Death Star I used as a docking bay for TIE/LN starfighters. This hangar featured a TIE hangar rack capable of holding up to seventy-two TIE fighters in individual, ceiling-mounted berths. It was destroyed in 0 BBY. *Death Star Technical Companion *Death Star Technical Companion, Second Edition *The Galactic Empire—Ships of the Fleet Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station hangars and docking bays
