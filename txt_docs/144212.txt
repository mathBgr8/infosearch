Naima was a native of the planet Bellassa who was a member of the Eleven resistance group. She was a friend of the resistance members Ferus Olin and Doctor Amie Antin. Around 18 BBY, Naima and fellow resistance member Terris were piloting V-wing starfighters when they encountered an Imperial starship near Bellassa's inner atmosphere. The vessel's captain reported to the Sith LordThe Essential Reader's Companion Darth Vader that the starfighters were on their radar and that they were not registered to Bellassa's government, meaning they were possible resistance members. After the captain told Vader that the starfighters had not identified themselves, Vader ordered them to blast the fighters. Although the captain protested, stating they were probably just doing surveillance, they eventually complied, destroying one of the fighters. As the remaining fighter attempted to avoid the Imperial starship, an officer commented on the way it squirmed. The V-wing was destroyed soon after. Olin, who was acting as a double-agent aboard the Imperial starship, felt helpless watching as the fighters were destroyed and thought it was likely he either knew them or their friends or family. Later, on Bellassa, Antin told resistance members Roan Lands and Dona Telamark about the deaths of Naima and Terris. Lands was saddened by their deaths and hoped Olin had not known the starfighters were piloted by friends. Although he was unaware of her identity, Ferus Olin believed that Naima was brave to attack an Imperial starship. Naima piloted a V-wing starfighter.
