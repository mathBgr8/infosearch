Siege of Nulan VI
2143

The siege of Nulan VI was a battle that took place some time after the introduction of TIE bombers into the Imperial Navy and the formation of the Scimitar assault wing. The celestial body Nulan VI rebelled against the Galactic Empire and came under siege. The Scimitar assault wing was instrumental in ending the siege, when they utilized a tactic of deep-atmospheric precision bombing to force Nulan VI's capital to surrender. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles of the Hook Nebula campaign
