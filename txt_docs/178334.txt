Cyborg Separatist commander
2144

}} This unidentified separatist commander was a male cyborg in charge of the Separatist Droid Army on an unidentified planet during the Clone Wars. Following the outbreak of the Clone Wars, the CIS droid army, under the command of the cyborg commander, invaded the planet and began to steal land and enslave citizens. Approximately six months after the First Battle of Geonosis, the commander attempted to invade a city using an army of B1-series battle droids, B2 super battle droids, and DSD1 dwarf spider droids. The commander led the assault on the city from his personal gun platform, but Jedi Master Yoda arrived and quickly defeated the army and cyborg commander, scaring him into fleeing from the scene.
