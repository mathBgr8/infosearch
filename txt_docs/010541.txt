|birth= |death=22 BBY, GeonosisRepublic Commando: Hard Contact |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns=new |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Grand Army of the Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Vin, whose number was RC-1134, was a male clone of the Mandalorian bounty hunter Jango Fett. Born on the planet Kamino, Vin was created by Kaminoan cloners for use in the Grand Army of the Galactic Republic. Trained as a clone commando, he was a member of Theta Squad, and spent his entire life alongside his squad mates Darman, Jay and their sergeant, Taler. The squad was deployed on the planet Geonosis during the first battle of the Clone Wars and fought alongside Delta Squad, another commando unit, against the forces of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. While fighting in a series of caverns under the planet's surface, Theta provided covering fire for Delta as they progressed through a breached blast door. Geonosian warriors then overwhelmed Vin, Jay and Taler; however, and killed them, leaving Darman as the sole survivor of Theta. Like all clone troopers, Vin was 1.83 meters tall. RC-1134 first appeared in Republic Commando: Hard Contact a novel written by Karen Traviss and released in 2004.
