Star Wars Adventures 7: The Ghostling Children
2065

Star Wars Adventures 7: The Ghostling Children was the seventh installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. This book is a reprint of Episode I Adventures 5: The Ghostling Children. Now flashback to the time before Episode I.' The Ghostling children live on a beautiful, isolated world, safe from harm&hellip;until they are captured by an evil trader. Soon they find themselves on the harsh planet of Tatooine, about to be sold to the terrifying Gardulla the Hutt. Their only hope of survival is a young slave named Anakin and his friends.
