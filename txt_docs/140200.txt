CT-307 was created on the planet Kamino by the Kaminoans. Developed from the genetic template of bounty hunter Jango Fett, CT-307 was a Human male clone who had tan skin and a height of 1.83 meters. In 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Utapau to 19 BBY. CT-307 served during the Battle of Utapau in the 212th Attack Battalion led by clone commander Cody and Jedi General Obi-Wan Kenobi. After the failed execution of Order 66&mdash;an order that declared the Jedi enemies of the Republic&mdash;against Kenobi, Commander Cody dispatched several Prowler 1000 exploration droids to search for the Jedi. During the search, the Prowlers encountered a mother nos monster who swallowed the droids whole after they approached too close to her babies. CT-307 reported the loss of Prowlers one through fourteen to his commander and asked for permission to deploy the final batch. CT-307 was mentioned in the reference book The New Essential Guide to Droids, as part of the Star Wars: Essential Guides series.
