Shadowroot soap
802

thumb|right|200px Shadowroot soap was made from a rare plant called the shadowroot plant. When the soap was put into water and washed on an object, the object became invisible, but it could be brought back when washed with clean water. Logray had a bar of shadowroot soap that was stolen by the Duloks thinking it was a bar of Ewok soap. *The Haunted Village picture book *Ewoks Coloring Book *Droids\Ewoks Category:Soaps
