Jante (species)
881

The Jante were a species native to the planet of Jante. They were locked in an arms race against the nearby Freda, who eventually attacked Jante industrial plants on Rett II. The Galactic Empire, led by Moff Haveland, ordered a ceasefire, from which the Freda eventually withdrew. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mid Rim sentient species
