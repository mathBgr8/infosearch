|continent= |location= |climate= |interest=*A safe houseThe New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *Arthen Brutt's warehouse |population=10,000:The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Human *Psadan *Myneyrsh *Noghri |demonym= |affiliation= }} New Nystao was the capital city on Wayland for the Noghri after their world, Honoghr, was devastated. It was named after their capital city of Nystao. Clan Hakh'khar was one of the Noghri families living there, and New Republic Intelligence Service maintained a safe house in the city. Many members of all species could be found there investigating the ruins of Mount Tantiss, and as such it had a thriving hotel and cafe business. In 25 ABY, Elan and Vergere were brought there following their "defection" from the Yuuzhan Vong. In the ensuing Yuuzhan Vong attack on Wayland, New Nystao was devastated.
