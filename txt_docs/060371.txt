Pizzazz 4
1133

Pizzazz 4 is the fourth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated January 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. This issue included an advertisement and order form for Marvel Special Edition Featuring Star Wars 1 and Marvel Special Edition Featuring Star Wars 2. 04
