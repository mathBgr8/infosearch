Attakril Meats
921

Attakril Meats was a small company based on Kidron that provided a variety of meats in bulk quantities to restaurants and grocery stores throughout the Elrood sector. They competed with Tozzin Foods and had offices located in the Elrood Bazaar. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Foodstuff manufacturers
