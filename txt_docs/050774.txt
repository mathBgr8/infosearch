Tauran Nartal
1510

Tauran Nartal was a Kajain'sa'Nikto Special Forces sergeant for the New Republic. Nartal, who was captured by the Twi'lek slaver Slar-dan ti Gardi on Werncin 3 following the Battle of Endor, was sold into slavery. Nartal had been investigating the disappearances of Kashyyyk representatives Krotorra and Lamorrack, who had also been captured by Slar-dan ti Gardi. Although Tauran was able to capture Gardi, he was ambushed by the Twi'lek's old partner Borun Call. Nartal was then sold into slavery on Kessel.
