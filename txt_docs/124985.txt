Ubuugan
1594

Ubuugans were a sentient species from the planet Ubuuga. They had grayish skin, three eyes, and a long snout. The Ubuugans developed a toxic secretion that protected them from the deadly Ubuugan fleshborers. Some of them even considered the fleshborers as a delicacy. The Ubuugan criminal Chung, was captured by the bounty hunter Boba Fett and was en-route to being taken back to Imperial Center when Fett picked up a distress call from the freighter Mingula, which was revealed to be infested with fleshborers that had killed the crew. Fett managed to escape as his armor proved resistant to the deadly parasites.
