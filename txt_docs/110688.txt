|type=Hardcover, Limited Edition |pages=224 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=1933784032, 1933784105 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Sculpting a Galaxy: Inside the Star Wars Model Shop is a 2006 reference book that examines selected models used in the six Star Wars films. It is available both as a standard hardcover and as a Limited Edition.
