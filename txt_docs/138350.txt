Eriann Strathmore
1527

Eriann Strathmore was an arrogant Lady and model Imperial citizen. She detested the weak and had a particular dislike of inferior alien races. Lady Strathmore believed that her only vice was her penchant for gambling. She only frequented the more distinguished establishments in the galaxy such as the Ace of Sabres where she tried to get membership into the exclusive Sabre Club.
