Dawn Rumble
634

The Dawn Rumble or Little Dawn Rumble was a Killik mating dance. After it was performed, Killiks would go to the Harem Cave to mate, and Joiners would go off as well, presumably for the same purpose. Jaina Solo fantasized about Jagged Fel becoming a Joiner, as it would make the Dawn Rumble "more interesting." *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *Legacy of the Force: Tempest Category:Ceremonies Category:Dance Category:Killik culture
