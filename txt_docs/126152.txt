Resonance projector
2541

The resonance projector was a superweapon that fired resonance torpedoes, plasma warheads that could destabilize a star's core. The resonance projector was a weapon that fired resonance torpedoes. The torpedoes were activated by passing through the resonance energizer, and were then fired out of the projector. When fired at a star, a supernova explosion would be triggered, destroying everything in its path.
