Threading the needle
1689

thumb|right|200px|A swoop "threading the needle." "Threading the needle" was an infamous move among the swoop and skyhopper racers of Beggar's Canyon in which they would successfully jump through the Stone Needle. A slight miscalculation in this move would cause certain death.
