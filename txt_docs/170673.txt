It was the second (and last) part of The Lost Jedi adventure game series. The story was later collected along with Jedi Dawn in The Lost Jedi Adventure Game Book. Two years after his first adventure on Toprawa, Havet Storm&mdash;now a wanted man&mdash;attempts to leave the planet, but finds himself once again involved in galactic affairs. Storm is on the run from the villainous bounty hunter Boba Fett in an attempt to find and warn the smuggler Han Solo of the Empire's nefarious plans&mdash;before it's too late. *Arf *Chewbacca
