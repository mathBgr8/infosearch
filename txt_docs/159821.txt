Strip-cam
556

A strip-cam was a flimsi-thick camera the size of a coat-check stub that could be applied to surfaces and under doors for monitoring without being easily detected. After its transmitting was complete, it could be commanded to shred the data it held and then discarded in garbage. *Imperial Commando: 501st *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Cameras Category:Emplacement sensors Category:Surveillance equipment
