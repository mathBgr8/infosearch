Lucas Marangon
2631

Lucas Marangon is primarily known as the artist of the Tag & Bink comic stories, written by Kevin Rubio for Dark Horse Comics, and has illustrated other short pieces, mostly humor-related, in Star Wars Tales, including "Marooned," for which he also wrote the script. He was also the cover colorist for the Star Wars: General Grievous series, and the penciller and cover artist for Star Wars: Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express. Currently, he works as the official Star Wars illustrator for Disney LATAM.
