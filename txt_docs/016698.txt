|sector=Seswenna sector |system= |coordinates=M-18 |xyz= |routes= |distance= |orbits= |stations= |asteroids= |other= |class=Black holePirates & Privateers |diameter= }} The Moshaw Dark Star was a black hole located on the edge of the Outer Rim Territories' Seswenna sector. The swoop gang known as the Dark Star Hellions originated in the space neighboring the black hole. The Moshaw Dark Star was first mentioned in Pirates & Privateers, a 1997 sourcebook written by Timothy S. O'Brien for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
