|date= |race= |creator= |found= |type= |description=A red-colored, strong, slightly bitter wine |animals= |plants=Fruit and wild rice |other= |edible= |inedible= |potable=HumansStar Wars: The Old Republic |nutrition= |effects=IntoxicationDarth Bane: Dynasty of Evil |dishes= |affiliation=Mandalorians }} Mandalorian wine was a type of alcoholic beverage made by members of the Mandalorian culture. Also known as mando papuur'gal in the Mando'a language, Mandalorian wine was created from a combination of fruit and wild rice. The drink was typically very strong, with a slightly bitter taste, and possessed a deep red coloration. Mandalorian wine gained a notable increase in popularity in the years following the end of the Mandalorian Wars. By the time of the Galactic Empire's reign, Mandalorian wine was often kept corked in a glass decanter prior to consumption. An individual who had become intoxicated from Mandalorian wine was commonly known to move with a stumbling gait. Mandalorian wine first appeared in Star Wars canon in the MMORPG, Star Wars Galaxies, released June 26, 2003.
