Lench
2989

Lench was a male captain who served the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. He captained the heavy cruiser By'ynium during the Battle of Praesitlyn, and was killed when the starship was destroyed. Around 22 BBY, during the Clone Wars, the Confederacy of Independent Systems launched an attack on Praesitlyn. Lench was part of a Republic Navy fleet sent to retake the planet, commanding the heavy cruiser By'ynium. As the Ranger, a Centax-class heavy frigate serving as the flagship, launched the first salvo on the Confederacy forces, the By'ynium was hit by the CIS counterattack and destroyed. The captain died in the explosion, his death witnessed by Captain Quegh and Jedi General Nejaa Halcyon on the bridge of the Ranger. Lench was a highly competent captain and commanded an equally efficient crew&mdash;at least, according to fellow officer Quegh, who lamented Lench's death.
