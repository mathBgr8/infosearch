England, United KingdomStar Wars Year By Year: A Visual History, Updated and Expanded Edition |death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=British |occupation=Actress |companies= |website= }} }} Keira Knightley (born March 26, 1985) is an actress who played Sabé in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. Knightley was cast as Sabé, Queen Amidala's handmaiden and decoy in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. She was chosen as Amidala's decoy because of her close resemblance to Natalie Portman, who played the Queen. Many people (including their mothers) had trouble telling them apart during the filming of The Phantom Menace. The character Kiera Swan was a reference to Knightley, whose name was misspelled as "Kiera" in the ending credits to The Phantom Menace, and to her character Elizabeth Swann from Pirates of the Caribbean. {| !
