Daupherm-Boton Conflict
2061

The Daupherm-Boton Conflict, also known as the Discril-Boton Wars,Pirates & Privateers was a conflict between the Botor Enclave and Daupherm Planet States, two adjoining territories in the Southern Core portion of the Core Worlds. The feud between the Botor and the Dawferim began around 490 BBY and continued for five hundred years to at least 10 ABY. It attracted renegades and scavengers from nearby systems.
