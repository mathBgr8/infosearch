Denise Ream
746

thumb|VFX producer Denise Ream Denise Ream was the Visual Effects Executive Producer for Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. She appeared in Revenge of the Sith as a cameo, portraying Onara Kuat in Romeo Treblanc's private viewing box at the Galaxies Opera House. Aidan Hennessy wrote her backstory through the What's The Story? feature on Hyperspace.
