CLL-6 binary load lifter droid
2363

The CLL-6 was a binary loadlifter droid produced by Cybot Galactica during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. The basic processor kept the droid moving and carrying out its orders. It took orders in Basic literally, and as such often required a protocol droid to correctly translate instruction into Binary. Being one of the oldest and simplest droid models in the galaxy, CLL-6 models were known to have participated in Nuna-ball leagues in addition to their load-lifting duties.
