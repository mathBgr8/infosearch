Crypt Master
499

The title of Crypt Master was given to those Sullustans who maintained the burial vaults on their homeworld of Sullust. In addition to this role, they also presided over the tomb-walling ceremonies. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sullustan culture Category:Titles
