Harvey Korman
2126

|listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation= |companies= |website= }} }} Harvey Korman portrayed Krelman, Chef Gormaanda, and Dromboid in The Star Wars Holiday Special. He later played "Jules Cogley" in George Lucas's 1994 film Radioland Murders. Korman was best known for his work on the popular television variety series The Carol Burnett Show and his appearances in Mel Brooks films such as Blazing Saddles. Korman passed away at the age of 81 on May 29, 2008, at UCLA Medical Center. {| ! Year !! Title !!
