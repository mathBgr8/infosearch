Matt Busch
2204

Matthew Busch is an artist who illustrated several West End Games publications as well as sketch cards for Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Trading Card Set. He was also one of two artists for the book You Can Draw: Star Wars along with Thomas Hodges. Busch can be credited with the creation of the droid MB-RA-7, which he featured in Star Wars Kids from 1998 to 2000.
