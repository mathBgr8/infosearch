During the Galactic Civil War, Sarko VI was under the control of the Galactic Empire and home to a colony. Some colonists lived in poverty and survived in ramshackle shelters and makeshift homes on the outskirts of the main settlement. Between 0 ABY and 3 ABY, Imperial troops slaughtered the residents of a squatter camp on the outskirts of the colony. The camp was believed to have a Rebel cell, because of false information provided by an informant.
