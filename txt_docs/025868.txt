P'Lor
1930

P'Lor was a Human male cyborg who served as an aide to Orion Ferret, the owner and operator of the black market space station Bazarre during the era of the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, P'Lor aided Ferret in his attempt to swindle and kill Rebel leaders Lando Calrissian and Luke Skywalker over a starfighter deal. However, their plot was foiled in part due to the help of their Wookiee friend Chewbacca.
