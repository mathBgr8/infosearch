Wandering Flyer 191
1986

The Wandering Flyer 191 was an aerospace transport craft that was manufactured by Mobquet Swoops and Speeders. It was a popular transport used for personal and corporate use within the Corporate Sector. Powered by two Comet Motors ion engines, it measured 7.5 meters in length and seated four. The Wandering Flyer 191 featured the familiar Mobquet fuselage design, but included structural reinforcements. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Category:Airspeeder models Category:Mobquet Swoops and Speeders products
