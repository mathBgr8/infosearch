The Binayre Pirates operated in the Corellian sector and were led by Captain Kath Scarlet. The Binayre maintained two home bases on the twin worlds Talus and Tralus, and were among the lowest criminal elements on those worlds. They were looked down upon as disreputable and untrustworthy even by other criminals. Several of them wore the insignia of Black Sun. On Talus, they operated from their bunker. The Binayre Pirates were tracked by the Corellian Security Force and the Federation of the Double Worlds, and they were also in open conflict with the Aa'kuan anarchist. After the Battle of Yavin, the Binayre Pirates controlled several areas of space in the Corellian system, including the Binayre Razorcat, the Trifecta Star and the Rayless Lantern. In 1 ABY, a commoner in the city of Dearic provided the coordinates of the Binayre Pirate Bunker on Talus to a spacer brave enough to visit it. Later, Jabba the Hutt's bounty hunter Bronell dispatched a spacer to the Talusian pirate bunker in order to retrieve information about a CorSec officer who owned money to Jabba, as well as to the pirates. The same year, an Imperial TIE bomber crash-landed on Talus during a battle between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance. Although the Empire quickly dispatched a rescue ream, the first to find the crash site were a group of Binayre scalawags. Looking for ship parts to salvage, the criminals assassinated the Imperial pilot on the spot. The Binayres then attacked the group of Imperial operatives sent to rescue the pilot but they were all killed. The Imperial later reported to Imperial Officer Ceth Laike. In retaliation, the officer ordered the Imperial operatives to strike at several criminal and pirate groups in the Corellian system. On Talus, the operatives assaulted a Binayre camp and killed 15 of them. Then, Imperial pilots attacked a group of Binayre starfighters in Corellian space. All pirate vessels were destroyed and the Binayre leader "Black Jack" MacDougal was killed in the battle. Troops of the Imperial 419th Infantry were also involved in actions against Binayre Pirates.
