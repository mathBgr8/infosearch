Gallant (escort carrier)
2018

The Gallant was a Ton-Falk-class escort carrier. It was part of a Rebel convoy that contained B-wing starfighters. The ships were attacked by Imperial forces, but thanks to X-wings the entire convoy managed to escape into hyperspace. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic Category:Ton-Falk-class escort carriers
