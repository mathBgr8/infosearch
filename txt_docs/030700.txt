Coruscant Museum of Galactic Cultures
832

The Coruscant Museum of Galactic Cultures was a museum and research center on Coruscant. Its research and displays were centered depictions of the various cultures of the Galaxy, in an attempt to demonstrate how they could live and cooperate together. The museum, which had stagnated during the Imperial era, experienced a resurgence following the recapture of Coruscant by the New Republic.
