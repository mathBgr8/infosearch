Government Mound
814

The Government Mound was a hill in the center of Abridon's capital city. On the hill's peak sat the Abridon Government Center, which served as the capitol of Abridon. Government Mound was an extremely picturesque site with a commanding view of the city. It was surrounded by gardens to the northwest and a river which curved along the cliff faces which made up the south and east sides of the hill. *Star Wars: Force Commander Category:Abridon locations Category:Mountains
