Galactic Gallery
58646

thumb|right|300px|Galactic Gallery logo Galactic Gallery was a feature on Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club that showed sketches and renditions from concept artists. It began on June 1, 2005 with the image "Danger is Afoot". Many of these images were featured in Star Wars Insider Best of Hyperspace articles. Before our favorite Star Wars icons came to be, they underwent initial explorations in the work of amazing concept artists. These visionaries helped translate George Lucas's imagination into reality.
