Mark VII power crossbow
756

thumb|right|250px|Boddu Bocck carrying the Mark VII The Mark VII power crossbow was a crossbow made by Malkanthi. It had an over-stock-mounted magazine capable of holding five crossbow bolts which were each tipped with barbed heads or injector heads filled with tranquilizers. The crossbow was favored by the bounty hunter Boddu Bocck for hunting sentients and wild game. *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook Category:Bows Category:Malkanthi products
