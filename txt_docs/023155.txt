Urval'starn

Urval'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the midlands of the planet's western continent. The city Gre Shev'starn was in the west, and Dreel'starn to the southeast. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
