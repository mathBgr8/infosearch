Jal Shey Neophyte
730

Jal Shey Neophyte was the lowest rank in the Jal Shey order and the equivalent of a Padawan or youngling in the Jedi Order. They studied with their mentors until they were ready to accept investigative and diplomatic missions of their own. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:Religious titles
