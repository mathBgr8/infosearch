Xtib
1270

Xtib was the processing company responsible for the production of the starship fuel TibannaX, an exotic Tibanna isotope used in Jedi StealthX fighter engines to limit the visibility of their ion tails.Dark Nest III: The Swarm War The corporation was founded during the latter end of the Yuuzhan Vong War. During the Swarm War, Bornaryn Trading, under the leadership of co-founder Lady Aryn Dro Thul, took control of Xtib to let Luke and Mara Skywalker know that they were aware of the plan to assassinate Raynar Thul. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Companies
