Alderaan Select Academy for Young Ladies
1432

The Alderaan Select Academy for Young Ladies was a school on Alderaan for upper-class girls, with Madame Vesta as the headmistress. All the daughters of nobility, including Leia Organa, attended. Students wore white and blue uniforms.
