Whippendell Woods
993

}} Whippendell Woods is located near the town of Watford in England. It was used for two short sequences in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace: the opening scenes on Naboo with Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi when they first meet Jar Jar Binks, and the Gungan Sacred Place.
