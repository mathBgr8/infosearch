Shiv
910

A shiv was a knife used by many species. It was considered just a step above a flint knife. It consisted of a sharpened blade, usually of metal, that had no other properties, such as a vibro-shiv would. Kateo killed a squad of Republic soldiers with a shiv. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Death Star *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Fate of the Jedi: Conviction *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Knives
