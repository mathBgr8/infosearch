Cragus 12's bounty hunter armor
925

thumb|[[Cragus 12 donning his armor]] Cragus 12's bounty hunter armor was a unique suit of armor that belonged to the ruthless bounty hunter Cragus 12. It was a full-body battle armor with a helmet that protected and concealed Cragus' head. The battle armor was equipped with a jet pack that had a movement range of 100 meters horizontally and 70 meters vertically as well as a Multi-Frequency Targeting and Acquisition System. Among other things, it also included a climate-controlled body glove, a self-contained air unit with two hours of oxygen, a built-in comlink and a wrist laser. Category:Heavy armor
