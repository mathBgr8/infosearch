Corellian Stock Exchange
1322

The Corellian Stock Exchange (CSE) was a financial institution, located in Coronet City, Corellia, in which all sorts of commodities, securities like stocks and other forms of legal investment were traded among its members. Together with the open-air market of Treasure Ship Row it usually housed a large amount of inhabitants, including black marketeers, and was known to be a pretty intense place for any dealer of its kind.Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations, P. 65. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Coronet City locations Category:Stock exchanges
