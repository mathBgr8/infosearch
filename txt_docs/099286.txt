Eanca Goa-Ato
943

thumb|Eanca Goa-Ato Eanca Goa-Ato, also known as the Grand Protector's Hunters' Guild Hall, was located in the Rodian city of Iskaayuma on Rodia. It was a large complex of buildings that expanded from an ancient Awards Hall, which initially housed written and video records of the greatest hunters, itself added on numerous times over the years. The Eanca Goa-Ato eventually contained the offices of the Goa-Ato. The complex also housed the Grand Protector's Chambers and the Chronicle Room containing records of the hunts of Goa-Ato members. The Eanca Goa-Ato hosted the annual Rodian hunting awards, the Atiang. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Guildhalls
