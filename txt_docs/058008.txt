Charbodian
2103

The Charbodians were the inhabitants of the Charbodia system in the Inner Rim. Jason Fry, co-author of The Essential Atlas, stated his intention to create homeworlds for numerous species based on context implied from their names. Charbodia appears to be one such case. One Charbodian worthy of note was a military cook at a Galactic Republic medical station on Drongar during the Clone Wars.MedStar I: Battle Surgeons, Chapter 18. The Charbodians are mentioned in the novel MedStar I: Battle Surgeons, written by Michael Reaves and Steve Perry and published by Del Rey in 2004. The book does not specify whether the term Charbodian refers to a sentient species or is merely a planetary demonym, such as Corellian or Hapan. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons Category:Undefined social groups
