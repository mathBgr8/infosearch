Umberbool City
2584

Umberbool City was a city in the swamps on the planet Naboo, inhabited by the amphibious Gungan species. In 32 BBY,The New Essential Chronology establishes that the Invasion of Naboo happened in 32 BBY. the Gungans assembled their Grand Army on the Great Grass Plains outside the Human city of Theed to combat the droid army of the Trade Federation, which had invaded Naboo. Umberbool City was one of many settlements that contributed troops to the army, who fought alongside Gungans from many other cities such as Otoh Gunga, Otoh Mandassa and Oxon City. Umberbool City was mentioned in the 2001 LucasArts video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, in the introduction to "The Battle for Theed", the seventh level in the Gungan campaign.
