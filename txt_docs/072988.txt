thumb|right|200px|Sonic detonators Sonic grenades,Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know also known as sonic detonators,A New Dawn were rare and deadlyKnights of Fate sonic-based explosive weapons. The Bith had such sensitive hearing that the sound of such grenades could make their heads explode. The grenades were very powerful, and had the capability to shatter thick glass, crack ferrocrete, and crumple light alloys.
