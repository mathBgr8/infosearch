In 20 BBY,Star Wars: The Comics Companion Jedi Master Ruati was beheaded by an assassin on Nauton IV, and fellow Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi suspected the Dark Jedi Asajj Ventress to be the murderer. Nauton IV was first mentioned in Obsession 1, the first issue of the Star Wars: Obsession comic series, written by W. Haden Blackman released on November 24, 2004 by Dark Horse Comics.
