|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Crimson Empire |issue=2 }} Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire was the second issue in the Star Wars Handbook series published by Dark Horse Comics. It was released July 21, 1999. Dark Horse continues its line of Star Wars handbooks with Star Wars: Crimson Empire! The epic tale of Kir Kanos, Imperial Royal Guardsman, and his one-man war against those who seek to taint the memory of Emperor Palpatine through corruption and greed, has become an instant classic. In encyclopedic format, Crimson Empire Handbook expands on the characters and events of the Crimson Empire and Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood miniseries. No fan will want to miss this!
