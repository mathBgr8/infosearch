Los Angeles Times Syndicate
733

The Los Angeles Times Syndicate was a newspaper publishing syndicate that distributed a number of comic strips. It was the publisher of the Star Wars newspaper comic strips that were published in U.S. and Canadian newspapers from March 11, 1979 through March 11, 1984, alongside the Watertown Daily Times.
