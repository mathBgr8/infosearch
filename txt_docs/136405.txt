Briff (bounty hunter)
1206

Briff was a licensed bounty hunter and black marketeer in the Ord Mantell system. Briff was Czethros's henchman during the Human's early career as a bounty hunter. As such, Briff was sent to Kessel along with Czethros after Han Solo framed the bounty hunter in an effort to rescue Chewbacca.
