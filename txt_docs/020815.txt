Troops
2105

Troops was a popular holodrama viewed by the citizen Mav Gatta. In 3 ABY, the thirteen-year-old Gatta used survival skills she had learned from watching the show to save her homestead from raiders, earning her a full scholarship to an Imperial Academy from the Galactic Empire. Troops was then mentioned by reporter Dorna Parros in coverage of Gatta's story on the HoloNet News later that week. Troops was mentioned in the 2024 Massive Entertainment video game Star Wars Outlaws. It shares its name with Kevin Rubio's 1997 fan film TROOPS, which depicts stormtroopers as parodies of the law enforcement officers featured in the television series COPS. *Star Wars Outlaws Category:Holodramas
