Moss Shelby
2095

on Corellia}} Moss Shelby was an officer of the Corellian Security Force who lived on Corellia during the Galactic Civil War. After the Battle of Yavin, he was a lieutenant in CorSec Regiment Twelve. When, in 1 ABY, large creatures from the Agrilat Swamp wandered into more civilized areas of Corellia and local farmers began to get worried, they asked Shelby for help. As Shelby did not want to waste CorSec resources on this, the lieutenant hired a group of spacers to do the job. Moss Shelby was mentioned in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Shelby was mentioned in a mission called "Large Creatures on the Move" available on mission terminals on Corellia.
