Third Quadrant

The Third Quadrant was one of the divisions of the Zi-Kree Sector of the ecumenopolis of Coruscant. The infamous Crimson Corridor was located in Zi-Kree's Third Quadrant. Jedi Padawan Darsha Assant was sent to the quadrant around 32 BBY to escort the Black Sun defector Oolth back to the Jedi Temple. *Darth Maul: Shadow Hunter Category:Coruscant districts
