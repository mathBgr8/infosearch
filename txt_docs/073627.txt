She made her home on Tatooine and frequented Chalmun's Cantina. Deneb was hoping to leave Tatooine and was often found at the Cantina during Ackmena's shift. In Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, Both was mistakenly identified as Momaw Nadon.
