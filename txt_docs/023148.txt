The conditions on Ibanjji were harsh, and it was home to packs of creatures known as varns. During the reign of the Galactic Empire, it was the site of a special motivational camp used to harden Imperial recruits. On the recommendation of his Imperial Sub-Adult Group neighborhood warden, Titus Klev, a thirteen-year-old SAGroup member from the Core WorldsThe Essential Atlas planet Alsakan, was selected to attend the motivational camp on Ibanjji as part of his training at some point before the Battle of Endor of 4 ABY. During his time at the motivational camp, Klev saved an instructor from a wild pack of varns. As a result, the recruit received approval to join Imperial CompForce despite being four years under the standard age. Ibanjji was first mentioned in the 1993 Dark Empire Sourcebook, which was authored by Michael Allen Horne and published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
