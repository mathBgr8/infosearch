Continuum Void
1059

The Continuum Void was a tapcafe located in the city of Xakrea on the planet of Darkknell and one of the few places in the city that stocked Gralish liqueur. It was here that Garm Bel Iblis was supposed to meet an Imperial defector who was willing to share information on the first Death Star for the right price. Unfortunately for Bel Iblis, the datapack with the information was lifted by master thief Moranda Savich before the deal could be done. It was also pegged by Inspector Hal Horn of CorSec as a likely place for Savich to hang out; he would later visit the tapcafe in search of the woman. Category:Darkknell locations Category:Tapcafes
