Columbia
2641

The Columbia was a MC80a Star Cruiser that participated in the rescue operation against the Imperial prison ship Dargon. It sent Assault transports to rescue the prisoners on board the Dargon before the Victory-class Star Destroyer Callisto could arrive to reinforce the Imperial defenders.
