Model Fac H-121
1816

The Model Fac H-121 was a factory model, designed by Delvin Constructs, that was used by the Galactic Empire. While able to be tooled for the production of several vehicle types, the Empire most often produced the Self-Propelled Medium Artillery and 2-M Saber-class repulsor tank at these facilities.
