Pitareeze Cruiser
932

250px|thumb|[[Hologram/Legends|Holographic plans for the cruiser]] The Pitareeze Cruiser was a class of starship that was developed by Baron Pitareeze and his business partner, Olag Greck. However, Greck betrayed Pitareeze, stealing the plans for the Pitareeze Cruiser while the Baron slept and producing cheaper versions of the design. When the ships became faulty, Greck kidnapped the Baron's grandson, Nak, and forced him into making repairs. Fortunately Pitareeze's droids, R2-D2 and C-3PO, were able to save the boy and foil Greck's plans.
