WED-1016 'Techie' Droid
1665

The WED-15-1016 droid, also known as the WED-1016 'Techie' droid, was created by Cybot Galactica as a starship maintenance droid. It had the ability to repair over 5,000 different onboard systems. One droid of this model was used by the Rebel Alliance on Hoth. *Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back junior novelization *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Maintenance droid models Category:WED Treadwell models
