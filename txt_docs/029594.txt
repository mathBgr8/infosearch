Brilliant (Nebula-class)
2095

The Brilliant was a Nebula-class Star Destroyer of the New Republic Defense Fleet. Assigned to the New Republic Second Battle Group in 16 ABY, the Brilliant was tasked with close planetary patrol of Coruscant. When the Aramadia attempted to make an unauthorized departure and damaged its surrounding ships, the Brilliant was sent to shadow the vessel under the command of Admiral Gial Ackbar.
