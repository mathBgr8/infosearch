AGWS-03
2225

AGWS-03 was a Rebel Loronar Corporation Strike-class medium cruiser. At the outset of the Battle of Nocto, both the AGWS-03 and an identical Strike Cruiser designated AGWS-04 were in line behind the Sagina at the Calenz shipyard, the facility where the Rebel Alliance hoped to refit further vessels according to the Modified Strike Cruiser design pioneered by the Peregrine.
