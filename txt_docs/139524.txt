Perilous laughing spell
981

thumb|250px|[[Teebo/Legends|Teebo suffers the effects of the perilous laughing spell.]] The perilous laughing spell was a magic spell contracted by touching a loonee bird. It caused the unfortunate victim to laugh uncontrollably, a spell that could plague its victim forever and would temporarily affect any who went near them. Only the tears from Mount Sorrow could cure the spell. The Ewok poet, Teebo, was once affected by the spell, but was eventually cured by Mount Sorrow's tears. *Ewoks (1985) 7 Category:Diseases and maladies
