Sullustan wine
1082

Sullustan wine was an alcoholic beverage. It was a claret. Lando Calrissian planned to serve this beverage on the night that he almost proposed to Bresenni. The liquid was also the preferred drink of Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina regulars the Tonnika sisters. *Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil *Darth Plagueis *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope *The Movie Trilogy Sourcebook *Star Wars 100 Objects Category:Wines
