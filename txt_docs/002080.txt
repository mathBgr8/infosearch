A frozen planet, with a local day of 28 standard hours long, and a year of 389 local days. The planet was discovered shortly after the declaration of the New Order. Emperor Palpatine kept its location a secret. The planet was the site of a research base. After the Battle of Bilbringi in 9 ABY, Luke Skywalker and Alexandra Winger led agents of the New Republic to the base on an infiltration mission.
