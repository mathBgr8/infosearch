He specialized in physical training. Along with his fellow clone commandos Seefor and Sirty, Forry attempted to stop Alpha ARC Captain Nate from violating their code, which Nate was doing by not following the instructions that Generals Kenobi and Fisto gave them. When Nate tried to leave, Forry and Nate squared off. Nate won by landing a blow on Forry's jaw. Forry helped Fisto and Nate train the commandos of the Desert Wind terrorist group. He was among the survivors who managed to escape their lair when Asajj Ventress sent a group of infiltration droids to destroy them.
