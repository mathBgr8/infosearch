The Search for Grubba the Hutt additionally denotes a February 1998 release date. |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-91610-6 |issn= |genre= |issue=6 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 6 is the sixth issue Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in February 1998. Issue 6 included part one of the serial comic X-Wing Marks the Spot, written by Mike W. Barr. Star Wars Kids 6 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book.
