Romin (species)
1729

Romin were a humanoid species with a somewhat smaller stature than that of Humans. Romin had golden skin and eyes. Their noses were flat, barely raised on their faces, and their mouths were wide and expressive. The Romin hailed from the planet Romin and were divided into two classes. The ruling class was the minority of Romin who had wealth and status. The majority of the Romin were of the working class who lived in poverty and took up homes in vast shanty towns that were set up outside the richer cities. The rampant disease and decay that dominated the ghettos of Romin gave the working class a much shorter lifespan, with many of their children not reaching their second birthdays. The Romin were ruled by a single dictator who went by the title of Great Leader.
