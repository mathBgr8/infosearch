|subscribers= |pages= |isbn= |issn=1361-5211 |genre= |issue=26 |series=Star Wars: The Official Magazine }} Star Wars: The Official Magazine 26, also styled as Star Wars Magazine 26, was the 26th issue of the Star Wars: The Official Magazine. Actor and stunt man Ray Park discusses the secrets of the Sith, the rigours of battling Jedi, and the challenge of bringing Darth Maul to life. This issue also explores the hidden secrets of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, including cameo appearances, unexpected jokes and other surprises. The magazine talks to the vocal talents behind many of Episode I's characters, and features an interview with Star Wars author Michael A.
