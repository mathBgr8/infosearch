Kitalic Nirasik
1350

Kitalic Nirasic was a male Svivreni miner and mining manager. Nirasic studied in several colleges throughout the Republic, including the Svivren Xenomineralogy Institute. Later, he worked in most areas of mining in several planets, including his native Svivren, Af'El and Vactooine, commonly combining philanthropy and profitability. Nirasic eventually went to Bandomeer and found out that the Offworld Corporation controlled the local mining.
