Startled Circuit
1997

The Startled Circuit was a freighter owned by the Jabba the Hutt-employed spacer duo Garron and Vareth. After losing both his parents to a shuttle crash, the Sullustan Garron used the insurance money to buy the freighter Startled Circuit. Along with his partner Vareth, Garron and the Startled Circuit made their way to Tatooine, where Jabba's majordomo Bib Fortuna was impressed enough with Garron and Vareth's skills that he offered them employment as spacers in the Hutt's criminal organization. In addition to Jabba's employ, the Startled Circuit was also available for hire by others in Mos Eisley. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Category:Freighters
