Laser lasso
583

The laser lasso was a children's toy. Similar in concept to a lightwhip, but in comparison non-lethal, the lasso emitted a "laser" that could wrap around objects and throw them about. In 18 BBY, Lune Divinian used his laser lasso in conjunction with his untrained Force powers to direct a discord missile away from his companions. *The Last of the Jedi: A Tangled Web Category:Toys and merchandise
