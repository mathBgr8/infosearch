Central Committee of Grand Moffs
3656

The Central Committee of Grand Moffs was a collective body of Grand Moffs within the Galactic Empire. In 5 ABY, the majority of the Grand Moffs attempted to subvert Ysanne Isard's claim to rule the Empire. Disregarding the authority of the weakened Imperial Ruling Council, it instead formed an alliance with the warlord Trioculus and his faction. Bertroff Hissa was its organizer and unspoken leader, and moffs Vilim Disra, Dunhausen, Muzzer, and Thistleborn were senior members of its Council. The face of its operations, Trioculus, was a weak figurehead championed as the mutant son of the late Emperor, and was ostensibly the committee's head. They were stationed aboard the Moffship. The committee organized the assassination of Grand Admiral Rufaan Tigellinus, and met on Kessel to advance Trioculus's claim to the Imperial throne.
