Jabiimi
2074

thumb|Enslaved Jabiimi Jabiimi, also known as Jabiimite, were Humans native to the planet Jabiim. They settled on the world around 3000 BBY. The fact that they were widely ignored by the galactic community, even during times of crisis, left them with a distrust of whichever government was in power. This distrust led to an open rebellion when the Jabiimi Loyalists aided the Republic forces and let them land on Jabiim.
