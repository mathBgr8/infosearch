Ilsa
1572

Ilsa was the daughter of Dantooine farmer Jon. In 3956 BBY, she was killed by Mandalorians. Her father called upon the Jedi to seek revenge for his daughter's murder, and the call was answered by Revan, at that time studying as a Padawan under his programmed identity.
