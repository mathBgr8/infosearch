Southern Deserts
807

The Southern Deserts were the deserts of the southern hemisphere of Sevarcos II. During the Galactic Civil War, the Southern Deserts were governed by Spice Lord Xerxes Quintas, who allowed the Empire to mine spice in his territories, much to the dismay of many nomadic Sevari clans.
