At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. a Galactic Empire force led by High Inquisitor Mox Slosin subjugated the Fitca system and Fitca Prime. During the first Rebel Alliance support wave during that conflict, Alliance Colonel Shanda Dok was captured, and she was subsequently interrogated by Slosin. Between 12 ABY and 13 ABY, Fitca Prime fell within the borders of the New Republic. Fitca Prime was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
