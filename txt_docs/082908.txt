|description=Blue noodles with softened or crunchy texture |animals= |plants=*Calarantrum seeds *Slim spring celto *Groundnut oil *Milkened tuber |other=*Chav *Conewere *Dabaroo *Firebud *Shadun *Teltiar noodles |edible=Humanoids |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Glowblue Noodles and Chav was a dish made by chef Gormaanda just before the outbreak of the Clone Wars. The recipe was modular, meaning that one could adapt it to different preferences, diets, and availability of ingredients, and the dish would still be flavorful. For example, one could add meat to it, such as sautéed nerf medallions or lean cooked gornt shank. The basic recipe created a vegetarian dish that was suitable for beings unable to consume meat. Glowblue Noodles and Chav was featured in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #50, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News, written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens, illustrated by Joe Corroney, and published on the HoloNet News website on April 4, 2002.
