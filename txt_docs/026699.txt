Garrick Hagon
2246

Garrick Hagon is a British-born, Canadian actor who portrayed Biggs Darklighter in A New Hope. He was raised in Toronto, Canada and is a stage, television, and film actor. He is also well known to sci-fi fans as Ky in the Doctor Who storyline The Mutants (1972). He reappeared in Doctor Who in 2012, as Abraham in the episode "A Town Called Mercy". Hagon's voice-over credits include the radio drama Judge Dredd: Jihad, as a psychotic clone of Toby Longworth's Joseph Dredd; the 1996 Manga Video dub of Lupin III: The Mystery of Mamo and Lupin III: Bye Bye Liberty Crisis as Goemon Ishikawa XIII; and the 2015 video game Batman: Arkham Knight, as a clone of the Joker (voiced by fellow Star Wars alumnus Mark Hamill). {|class="sortable" |- bgcolor="#CCCCCC" !
