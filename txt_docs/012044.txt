Selnia Harbright
2342

Lady Selnia Harbright was a young Human female senator from Salliche, who served in the Imperial Senate of the Galactic Empire. She followed proudly in the footsteps of her family for nearly three centuries. Energetic, intelligent and confident, Selnia was more interested in getting things done than in bland theory. With Salliche held tight in the Galactic Empire's grip, the Harbright family came to realize that Emperor Palpatine was a tyrant, and that the Rebel Alliance was justified.
