Yus Sh'roth
1574

Yus Sh'roth was a Shamed One living in the Coruscant underworld after it was Vongformed into Yuuzhan'tar. A former shaper, Sh'roth was part of the Niiriit Esh's group of renegade Shamed Ones who followed the Jeedai Heresy, and was one of the older members of the group. As a former shaper, he was a vital member of their community, knowing how to use the chuk'a they had found and grow a good deal of their food. Sh'roth was with Kunra when Yuuzhan Vong Warriors attacked their camp. Kunra fled while Sh'roth tried to fight off the warriors, and he was killed.
