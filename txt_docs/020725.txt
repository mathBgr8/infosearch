During the Galactic Civil War, the Dravian Starport&ndash;based company Topatz Shipping offered free-traders a job of transporting one hundred tons of imitation gemstones to Bazaar for 4,500 credits. Bazaar was mentioned in "Tales of the Smoking Blaster," a roleplaying game adventure written by Bill Smith. The adventure was included in Star Wars Gamemaster Handbook, a 1993 sourcebook published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Bazaar system, and therefore Bazaar itself, in grid square M-6.
