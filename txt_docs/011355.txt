A Force-sensitive male Human, Dalnus Cam trained by the Jedi Order in the ways of the Force. Over time, Cam attained the rank of Jedi Knight. When the Clone Wars erupted across the galaxy, Cam was assigned to assist in the massive defense of Jabiim. During the fighting, Cam was stationed at Shelter Base with fellow Jedi Rallcema Bylissura and Sana-Jis Ilowa.
