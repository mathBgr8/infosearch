Conclave
946

The Conclave was one of two political councils of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium. It was primarily concerned with religious issues, but also appeared to have judicial and legislative powers. The Conclave was occupied by gold Ssi-ruu. Together with the Elders' Council, the Council elected the Shreeftut.
