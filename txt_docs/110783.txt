CorSec Database H-Z

|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The CorSec Database H-Z is the second part of the in-universe CorSec Database for Star Wars Galaxies gamers. It was published on June 16, 2006 on the Star Wars Galaxies official website. The database accounts for the following non-player characters and organizations: *Jabba the Hutt *Janta *Jawa *Kobola Miners *Kunga *Lady Valarian *Lost Aqualish *Maulers *Max Rebo and the Max Rebo Band *Meatlumps *Mummers *Nightsisters *Nym *Lord Nyax Cult *Rori Spice Mining Collective *Sanyassan Marauders *Selonian *Sennex *Singing Mountain Clan *Skaak Tippers *Tusken Raiders The database is a continuation of the first part, which accounts for A-G. Like the first part of the database, CorSec Database H-Z is written from an in-universe perspective, but unlike the first part, the second part does not have any introductory or exit text.
