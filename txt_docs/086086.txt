Trebor was a name shared by a number of individuals: *Trebor, a Human who served as copilot aboard the cargo shuttle Moon Dash. *Coleman Trebor, a Vurk Jedi Master who was killed in the Battle of Geonosis *Epoh Trebor, a Human comedian.
