Laura Cappato
1088

Laura Cappato (born October 13, 1967 in Venice, Italy) worked as the production coordinator in Italy for both Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. Other notable works include both of the Omega Code movies. Category:Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace production staff Category:Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones production staff
