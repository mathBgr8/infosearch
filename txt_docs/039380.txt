Star Wars: The Official Magazine 11 was the 11th issue of the Star Wars: The Official Magazine, published in December 1997. *Feel the Force *Comlink *Second Trooper: The Dancer's Tails *Jawas' Corner *Role-Playing Round-Up *"A New Hope" *:The beginning of a major interview with Irvin Kershner, the man George Lucas entrusted to direct the first Star Wars sequel The Empire Strikes Back. *"Roowarragh!" *:Peter Mayhew, the actor who played Chewbacca in all three Star Wars films, remembers his contributions to the original trilogy. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species: Wookiees *:Wookiees &mdash; the fearsome and loyal species of which Chewbacca is a prominent member. *The Making of The Empire Strikes Back: Striking Back *:Our ongoing production chronicle of the original Star Wars trilogy enters a new chapter. *Anthony Daniels' Set Diary: DP PO'D *:C-3P0 [sic] actor Anthony Daniels remembers the trials of life on location during the production of Star Wars. *"Shadows of the Empire Players Guide" *:We're still putting together the Jedi Knight player's guide we promised last issue - in the meantime here's the first part of a massive guide to this acclaimed PC/N64 game from LucasArts. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron ½ *:A special presentation of this one-off Rogue Squadron story, together with an introduction outlining the history of the acclaimed comic series. 11
