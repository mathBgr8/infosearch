|cover artist=Drew Struzan |illustrator= |editor= |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=September 2001 |type=Hardcover |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0786918373 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Rebellion Era Sourcebook is a supplement to the Star Wars Roleplaying Game, published by Wizards of the Coast. It was written by Bill Slavicsek, Steve Miller, and Owen K.C. Stephens. It was first published in 2001. The book details material from the era of the original trilogy of films and key contemporary Expanded Universe information. It includes characters, adventure hooks, and advice on conducting a Rebellion era campaign. No epic adventure has fed the imagination of millions of fans worldwide quite like the Star Wars series has. Now there's even more great information to digest. Everything that a player needs to know about the Empire and Rebel Alliance from the classic Star Wars universe can be found in this one sourcebook -- from weapon and ship statistics to information on non-player characters. Han Solo, the Death Stars, TIE interceptors, Princess Leia, stormtroopers, Luke Skywalker . . . it's all here in detail. This book will be a must-have for all Star Wars gamemasters. Comprising 159 pages, this book packs a good deal of information about the era of the Galactic Civil War.
