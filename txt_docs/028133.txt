Archai
2861

Archai was a YT-1300 light freighter manufactured by the Corellian Engineering Corporation and owned by the Human scout Coli Carinth. While Carinth operated the Archai, she became one of a few spacers in the galaxies Outer Rim with the coordinates required to travel to the planet Iego through hyperspace. At some point the members of the Diathim species native to the planet subverted Carinth's mind causing her to guide travelers to Iego, subsequently strand them there, and leave without any recollection of her actions. In order to protect the coordinates of Iego from her customers, Carinth would require them to link their starships to the Archais hyperdrive thus protecting the coordinates.
