Xenovet
706

Xenovet, or XV, was a Commenorian bioengineering corporation that primarily produced veterinary supplies for exotic species, especially those kept as pets by image-conscious Imperial officers. After the death of Emperor Palpatine, the company turned toward more philanthropic works, but soon after went out of business.
