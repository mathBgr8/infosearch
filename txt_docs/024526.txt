NB-1 Royal Bomber
2051

The Naboo NB-1 Royal Bomber was a bomber used by the Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps. It was the predecessor of the NB-1S Royal Bomber. It was armed with proton bombs and produced at Naboo airfields. The craft featured chromium plating at the bow and was colored yellow at the aft, and had two engine units.
