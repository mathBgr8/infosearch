Ulan Squadron
1215

Ulan Squadron was a Rebel Alliance starfighter squadron composed of T-65 X-wing starfighters. It saw service in the Airam sector where, in the end, it was destroyed. Ulan Squadron had its final fight in the Rocrin system. When a Rebel convoy was ambushed there by the Galactic Empire, it was sent to help. The ambush itself included Interdictor cruiser Compellor so, recognizing this, Ulan was given a load of torpedoes and charged with driving it away. However, the rescue was a failure.
