P'den Robalt
1310

P'den Robalt was a noted criminal on the planet Taris in the era of the Mandalorian Wars. At some point, Robalt was captured by the Falleen Jedi Padawan Kamlin, one of the Jedi trainees at the local Jedi satellite academy. In 3964 BBY, in a Taris Holofeed article about the local Padawans, the capture of Robalt was named as one of Kamlin's most noteworthy achievements. P'den Robalt was first mentioned in The Taris Holofeed: Prime Edition, an in-universe news report appearing on the last page of the comic The Taris Holofeed: Prime Edition.
