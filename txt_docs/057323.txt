Sentinel (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
2491

Sentinel was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The Sentinel was called to intercept a Rebel attack at the heart of the Imperial surveillance satellite network near Coruscant itself. However, it arrived too late to prevent the lone Rebel X-wing from destroying the satellites and the transports that were mining the area. The Sentinel later guarded a supply depot in Hollan D1 sector.
