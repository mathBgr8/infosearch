Dark Side Squadron
1784

Dark Side Squadron was the designation used by the Dark Side Elite of the Dark Empire during at least one missionDark Empire II audio drama in 10 ABY.The New Essential Chronology dates the events of Dark Empire II audio drama, which features the battle of New Alderaan, to 10 ABY. Piloting I-7 Howlrunner starfighters, the group participated in a raid on New Alderaan to abduct Jacen and Jaina Solo. They were defeated by Luke Skywalker, Kam Solusar and Empatojayos Brand.
