Droidfest
1244

thumb|200px|Droidfest outside [[Jabba's Palace/Legends|Jabba's Palace.]] Droidfest was an annual used-droid sale on Tatooine supported by the combined effort of the Jawa Droid Traders. An outward version of the annual intra-Jawa swap meets, Droidfest attracted a seedy collection of fringers looking to purchase cheap droids.
