|birth= |death= |species=Human |gender= |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=Sylo family |parents= |partners=Tashna Sylo |siblings= |children=*Paxi Sylo *Six children |affiliation=Sylo farm |masters= |apprentices= }} Griggen Sylo was a Human and the spouse of Tashna Sylo. The two owned a large outback farm, which had been in the Sylo family for ten generations, on the Outer Rim planetThe Essential Atlas of Dantooine in the waning years of the Galactic Republic. The couple worked hard to harvest tritacale and yot beans in order to supply a larder to feed their family of seven children and to sell offworld. Between the years 30 BBY and 31 BBY,Paxi Sylo's StarWars.com Databank entry states that Paxi was nine years old when he witnessed the battle on Dantooine, which The Essential Chronology places in 21.66 BBY. Therefore, this article assumes Paxi's year of birth to be either 30 BBY or 31 BBY, nine years earlier depending on the month he was born. the youngest of the Sylo children, Paxi, was born. By the time of the Galactic Empire, which was formed in 19 BBY, the Sylo farm had been abandoned. Griggen Sylo was mentioned as one of the parents of Paxi Sylo in the latter's original Databank entry on StarWars.com, which was first posted in 2004.
