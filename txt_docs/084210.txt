Slag Pit
2753

The Slag Pit was a cantina located on the Outer Rim TerritoriesStar Wars (2015) 8 moon Nar Shaddaa. By the reign of the Galactic Empire, it had gained a reputation as one of the roughest bars in the galaxy. During the early days of the Imperial Era, the Latero pilot Greez Dritus and former Jedi Knight Cere Junda entered into an altercation with smugglers at the Slag Pit and were subsequently thrown out of the establishment. The two bonded over the incident and shared a drink of lum in a nearby alley. In 14 BBY,Star Wars: Timelines dates the events of Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order to 14 BBY. Junda and Greez formed a crew with the Jedi Padawan Cal Kestis aboard the Stinger Mantis luxury yacht. Dritus recounted the event and cited it as the first time he had clicked with Junda. In the same conversation, Junda recounted her own memories of the event, citing it as one of the best nights of her life that she'd had in years. In 3 ABY, the gambler Zana Gatta visited the Slag Pit a few days before visiting the casino cruiser Morenia.Star Wars Outlaws The Slag Pit was canonically mentioned in the 2019 video game Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order during a conversation that may occur during travel between planets.
