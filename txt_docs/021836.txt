Dononter Minor had a Type I breathable atmosphere, and it was the homeworld of the plant known as the widowmaker, which could be used as a weapon. Dononter Minor was mentioned in the article "Endor and the Moddell Sector," which was authored by Craig Robert Carey, Daniel Wallace, and Jason Fry, and was published in Star Wars Gamer 9 on February 22, 2002. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Dononter Minor system, and therefore the world Dononter Minor, in grid square I-16.
