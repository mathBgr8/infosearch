Gauntlet Dartcaster
1368

The Gauntlet Dartcaster or the wrist dart launcher or simply dart launcher is a concealed weapon, usually hidden in the wrist plates or gauntlet of battle armor. Mandalorian Bounty hunter Jango Fett had a Velocity-7The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology dartcaster in his Mandalorian armor, just one of the many deadly tools in his arsenal, which ranged from WESTAR-34 blaster pistol, to a ZX miniature flame projector, a whipcord launcher, and cutting torch. These dartcasters usually carried some form of poison dart, Jango himself used Kamino saberdarts, a very rare artifact. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Dart weapons Category:Personal weaponry
