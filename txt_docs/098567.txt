Juan Schwartz
1709

Juan Schwartz was an alias of Pablo Hidalgo. Hidalgo used it because of short-live rule that prohibited Lucasfilm employees from doing freelance work for publishers. He used the alias in Star Wars Gamer 5 as the author of The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Chiss and as a co-author of The Emperor's Pawns, with Abel G. Peña.
