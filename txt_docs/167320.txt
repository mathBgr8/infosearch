Josh Ling
1480

Josh Ling is an author who has written a variety of Star Wars literature such as the collector's lists for the Topps Star Wars Galaxy Collector magazine, as well as updates for Tomart's Worldwide Price Guide to Star Wars Collectibles. He contributed to Anakin Skywalker: The Story of Darth Vader along with Daniel Wallace, which was published by Chronicle Books in 1998 and co-wrote two books in the Star Wars Masterpiece Edition series: C-3PO: Tales of the Golden Droid with Daniel Wallace and Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters with Ryder Windham, which were published by Chronicle Books in 1999 and 2000, respectively. Josh was also Steve Sansweet's first archivist and collections manager beginning in 1993 through 2000. Josh helped start the cataloging process of Steve's massive collection and helped Steve relocate from Los Angeles to Petaluma in 1998.
