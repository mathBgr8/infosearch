Stinkweed
655

Stinkweed was a type of plant found on Bothawui.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide The term could also be used as an insult. Stinkweed could confused even the krak'jya's keen sense of smell, and hunters sometimes camouflaged by hiding in it. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Plants of Bothawui
