Pizzazz 15
1032

Pizzazz 15 is the fifteenth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated December 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. This issue also include the article "The Seers Catalog of Robots," a humorous look at the history of robots in science fiction films, including R2-D2 and C-3PO. 15
