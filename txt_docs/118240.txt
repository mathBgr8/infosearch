ComNet Corporation
1121

ComNet Corporation was a communications company located on Ord Mynock inside the Ord Mynock Freeport. It took control of a Galactic Republic communication outpost and relay station and held a virtual monopoly on communications in that sector. Their dome was one of the largest buildings in the Ord Mynock Freeport. It was large and studded with transmission dishes, laser-link relays, and other antennae. Since it was such an important and expensive structure, it was heavily guarded by ComNet security forces. *Gamemaster Toolkit: Live-Action Adventures Category:Communication companies
