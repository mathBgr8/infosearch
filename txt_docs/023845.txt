Qoribu Truce
2714

Forged by the Killik Colony and the Chiss Ascendancy at Qoribu in 35 ABY, the Qoribu Truce outlined the withdrawal of the Killik Colony from Chiss space. In exchange for a peaceful withdrawal, the Killiks were granted colonization rights to the Utegetu Nebula worlds. The Chiss Ascendancy would provide the Colony a border guarantee and a promise of non-aggression. The treaty was mediated by Leia Organa Solo. In 36 ABY, the Chiss declared the Truce broken as the Colony, under the influence of Lomi Plo and her Dark Nest, moved into Chiss border again.
