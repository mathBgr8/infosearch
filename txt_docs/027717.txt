Kolaador
2870

Kolaador was a planet in the Kolaador system of the Outer Rim Territories. This planet's landscape had a crystalline structure, which was ideal for pilot training. During the Cold War between the Galactic Republic and the reconstituted Sith Empire, Kolaador was the venue for the annual Kolaador Speeder Derby. Prior to the Battle of Tatooine, Ru Murleen trained Rebel pilots in the R-22 Spearhead, showing them how to avoid the obstacles.
