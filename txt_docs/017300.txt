It was located in the Velm system, a part of the Halori sector in the Trans-Hydian portion of the Mid Rim. Velmooine was mentioned in the roleplaying source article Velmor: Royalty and Rebellion, which was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards.com on March 6, 2003, for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Velm system, and therefore Velmooine, in grid square P-7.
