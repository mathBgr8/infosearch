Ruling Council (disambiguation)
1728

There were several Ruling Councils in the history of the galaxy. *Imperial Future Council&mdash; a group of high ranking Imperial Military officials who met on Akiva to discuss the future of the fragmenting Galactic Empire. *Imperial Ruling Council&mdash; a collective of bureaucrats consisting of Galactic Emperor Palpatine's closest Imperial advisors. *Shadow Council&mdash; a secret advisory council led by Counselor to the Empire Fleet Admiral Gallius Rax to govern the loyalist Imperial forces towards the end of the Galactic Civil War. *Shadow Council (Imperial Remnants)&ndash; another secret advisory council led by the Imperial Remnants forces during the New Republic Era. *Ruling Council (Kamino)&mdash; the council that consisted of the Kaminoan government. *Mandalorian Government Council&mdash; also known as the Ruling Council, it was a council of ministers that advised the New Mandalorian Duchess Satine Kryze until its dissolvement. *Ruling Council (Skako Minor) *Chiss Ruling Circle *Hutt Ruling Council *Imperial Ruling Council *New Republic Ruling Council *Ruling council (Exocron) *Ruling Council (Kamino) *Ruling Council (New Mandalorians) *Council
