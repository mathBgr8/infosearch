It was a rich source of hyperbaride, a mineral that lay beneath Goroth Prime's crust. In 10 BBY, the Galactic Empire had outsourced the mining on hyperbaride to various subsidiary organizations, and many native Gorothites were also used for forced labor. Due to the highly radioactive nature of the mineral, many of the natives became horribly exposed with the damage to them and the planet as a whole being disastrous.
