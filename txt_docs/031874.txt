|class=Humanoid |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor=*Light *Blue mottling |haircolor=Light brown |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=*Bony ridges surrounding eyes *Raised eyebrows *Wider nostrils |lifespan= |origin=Couronne |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Imzig species was a sentient Humanoid race native to the planet Couronne. The species differed from base Humans through bony ridges surrounding the formers' eyes, raised eyebrows, and wider nostrils. At least one was light-skinned, with blue mottles on the face, as well as light brown hair. Utris M'Toc was an Imzig who hailed from hisStar Wars: The Action Figure Archive species' homeworld. When a bloody coup occurred on Couronne, M'Toc, who worked as a member of the monarchical High King's Guard, fled the planet. In 3 ABY, the Galactic Empire occupied Cloud City on the planet Bespin.The New Essential Chronology By that time, M'Toc had joined Cloud City's Wing Guard, becoming a leader in the security force, and he was present on Cloud City during the Imperial takeover. The Imzig species was created by Utris M'Toc's card in the 1997 Cloud City Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game. According to Star Wars author Stephen J.
