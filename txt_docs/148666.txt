Blackhand Annihilator
1565

The Blackhand Annihilator was a slugthrower pistol typically used among scoundrels and outcasts that was developed for use during the Clone Wars. As the developement came too late, it only saw limited use during that conflict. However, it later saw some military use during the time of the Galactic Civil War. This pistol was looted from Moufs in the Kkowir Forest region on Kashyyyk. Also, while the item description and the spelled the item name "Annihilator", the item itself was erroneously named "Annihilater".
