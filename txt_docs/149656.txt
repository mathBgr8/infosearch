Eriadu Governor's Palace
1902

Located in Eriadu City, the Eriadu Governor's Palace was be the home to both governmental power in Seswenna sector and Eriadu's governor. This ziggurat-shaped building&mdash;somewhat similar to many Jedi and Sith temples&mdash;was seemingly located near some sort of haven. Wilhuff Tarkin became "de facto" owner of this mansion by 29 BBY, when Palpatine made him full governor of the Seswenna Sector.
