The Human TIE fighter pilot Creb of the Galactic Empire was born at Bettok in 20 BBY.Dark Force Rising Sourcebook, which is set during the events of Dark Force Rising, states that Creb is 29 years old. The Essential Reader's Companion dates the latter work to 9 ABY, so Creb must have been born in 20 BBY. Bettok was mentioned in Dark Force Rising Sourcebook, a 1992 supplement authored by Bill Slavicsek for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
