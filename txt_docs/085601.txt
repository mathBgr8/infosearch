Biter and Beater
443

Biter and Beater were the nicknames given to a pair of bio-engineered beasts used by the Yuuzhan Vong. Respectively, Biter referred to Sgauru and Beater referred to Tu-Scart. *Invasion 3 *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Aliases, nicknames, and pseudonyms
