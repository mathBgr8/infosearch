Swift (freighter)
1850

Swift was a freighter in service to the Galactic Empire. It was this ship that took Ysanne Isard and her team of commandos to the Bilbringi Shipyards in 9 ABY in her attempt to recapture the Executor-class Star Dreadnought Lusankya. The ship was commanded by Captain Wintle. The Swift appeared in the 1999 novel, X-Wing: Isard's Revenge, written by Michael A.
