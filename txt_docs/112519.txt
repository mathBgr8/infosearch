Princess of the Realm
2234

Princess of the Realm and Empress Apparent-Heir were two titles borne by Isplourrdacartha Estillo of Eiattu 6 after she was called home to take up the reins of government on her homeworld in 4 ABY. The Empress Apparent-Heir's father was Emperor Uthorrferrell Cartha, and she referred to herself as "the Princess of the Realm" to Count Rial Pernon. Princess Isplourrdacartha had a younger brother, Prince Harran.
