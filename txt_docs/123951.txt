Petranaki scimitar
1287

The Petranaki scimitar was one of the five traditional Geonosian weapons that made up the Petranaki array. The Petranaki scimitar was a light weight sword with a gently curving blade, which had been honed to razor-sharpness. It was generally considered the most useful and popular of the weapons in the Petranaki array due to its balance and cutting ability. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Geonosian weapons Category:Swords
