|publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=April 2, 2005 |other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=136 |isbn=1593073119 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series= |issues= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Visionaries is a graphic novel published by Dark Horse Comics. It features 11 stories written and drawn by concept artists who worked on Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. Visionaries was edited by Jeremy Barlow and contains an introduction by Jonathan W. Rinzler, who came up with the concept and pitched it to Dark Horse. The comics constituting Visionaries are also collected in Star Wars Omnibus: Wild Space Volume 2. They've been responsible for some of the most dazzling and awe-inspiring visuals ever put to film, and now the concept artists behind the Episode III Revenge of the Sith bring their considerable talents to comics. The gifted minds of the Lucasfilm art department and the visual effects powerhouse Industrial Light & Magic come together to tell their own Star Wars tales in this compilation of short stories. Given free reign to explore any and every aspect of the Star Wars universe, each artist offers a new twist or a deeper view into that galaxy far, far away.
