Kerritamba Village
3259

Kerritamba Village was one of the ground-level Wookiee settlements on the planet Kashyyyk. Kerritamba Village was a city on the Wookiee homeworld of Kashyyyk. Unlike most cities on Kashyyyk, it was on the forest floor and not in a wroshyr tree.Kashyyyk's StarWars.com Databank The people of the village had charged themselves with protecting the native Kkowir Forest and the Great Tree from the dark powers of the Sayormi species for many generations. At the time of the Galactic Civil War, Chief Kerritamba was in charge of the village. During his tenure, the Great Tree became poisoned by the Sayormi, and he tasked an individual from outside the village with gathering the resources he needed to make a cure. After doing this, he tasked the individual with attacking the Sayormi. *Chief Kerritamba *Athnalu *Wirartu *Warchief Naloriss Kerritamba Village first appeared in the video game Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees prior to the game's closure on December 15, 2011.
