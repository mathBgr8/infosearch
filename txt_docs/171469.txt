Repulsorfield Generator
606

The Repulsorfield Generator was a specialized prisoner restraint built by Cartel Secureworks. It was a manacle that was fitted to a prisoner's left ankle (or equivalent) which caused the convict to be unable to move more than a foot off the ground. This type of restraint was only used on prisoners with a predilection for climbing or flight to cut down on potential escapes.
