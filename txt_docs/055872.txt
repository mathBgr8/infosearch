Daysong
773

Daysong was a splinter group of the Rights of Sentience Party which advocated in 13 ABY that the New Republic Chief of State Leia Organa Solo's Noghri honor guard was a form of humiliation for the already long-suffering species and that they should be replaced by droids. They also objected to synthdroids as guards on the grounds that synthflesh was alive and had rights as well. *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Political factions
