Taloraan City
2707

Taloraan City, also known as Cloud City, was the capital city of Taloraan and one of the sites of the battle above Taloraan. It featured several tall, spire-like buildings atop a massive repulsorlift generator, similar to technologies used on Bespin. Taloraan City,The Clone Wars: The Wind Raiders of Taloraan sometimes referred to as Cloud City,Star Wars: Rogue Squadron: The Official Nintendo Player's Guide was a city floating in the atmosphere of the gas giant Taloraan. The city was home to a series of tibanna gas mining facilities. During the Clone Wars, it was visited by Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker on a diplomatic mission. During the Galactic Civil War, Commander Luke Skywalker led Rogue Squadron to destroy most of the Imperial-owned tibanna gas in an attempt to weaken the local Moff Kohl Seerdon's resources. Taloraan City first appeared in the 1998 video game Star Wars: Rogue Squadron.
