Practice Makes Perfect (comic book)
3231

Practice Makes Perfect was the third Hasbro/Toys "R" Us&ndash;exclusive comic book published in 2002. Jedi Knights Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi are trapped by droids. Jumping into action, the two Jedi quickly disable them. When the lights come on, it is revealed the duel was a test. Skywalker brags about the ease, but is chastised by his master for his overconfidence. This comic briefly shows Plo Koon without his mask for the first time. Fans were initially uncertain whether this image is canon within what is now the Legends continuity. Later this was clarified when Star Wars: Purge: Seconds to Die featured a similar image of Koon on his homeworld of Dorin.
