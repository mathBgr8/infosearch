|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: The Last Command |issue=4 }} The Last Command 4 is the fourth issue in the Star Wars: The Last Command comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel The Last Command. It was released March 25, 1998. Continuing the adaptation of the sequel to Star Wars: Dark Force Rising. Adapted from the best-selling Bantam novel by Timothy Zahn, with fantastic art by a pair of Russ Manning Award-winners, Edvin Biukovic, and Eric Shanower.
