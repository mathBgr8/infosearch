Battle of the Resurgence
2734

The Battle of the Resurgence occurred some time after the end of the Clone Wars, when a group of the Alderaanian Resistance were escaping from a mission on Coruscant. They were ambushed by a unit of the Imperial Navy under the Inquisitorius. Their ship, the Resurgence, was boarded and destroyed; although Inquisitor Valin Draco took Jedi Master Denia captive to the Citadel Inquisitorius on Prakith, most of the rest of the rebels managed to escape through hyperspace to Alderaan.
