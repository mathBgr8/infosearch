Razor moss
1403

The razor moss was a hardy plant indigenous to Tatooine that inhabited the rocks of the planet. It had corrosive roots that broke down the crystalline structure of the sandstone outcroppings to extract the moisture contained within. Sandjiggers fed on the moss, as did cliffborer worms.
