Central Gathering Hall
548

The Central Gathering Hall was a building in Imperial City on Coruscant with a large clock. It would send a ripple of light into the night sky to mark the hour. By the time of the Thrawn campaign, it had been functioning thus for roughly three centuries. *The Last Command Category:Coruscant buildings
