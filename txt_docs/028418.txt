The nebula's three suns gave local space a ruby-red tint, hence the name, Ruby Nebula. The density of the nebula's stars was known to disrupt starship sensors, making it easy for spy ships and starfighters&mdash;and even larger vessels such as landing craft and patrol interceptors&mdash;to remain hidden amid the free-floating asteroids. Jedi Master Adi Gallia met Nym's informant, Reti, in the Ruby Nebula while attempting to set up a meeting with the pirate captain. Reti was followed to the area by droid starfighters, disgorged from a Trade Federation landing craft.
