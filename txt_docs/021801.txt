Dartessex IV
2287

Dartessex IV was a celestial body located in the Dartessex system, a part of the Slice portion of the Inner Rim. The home offices of House Renliss, a chapter house of the Bounty Hunters' Guild, were located at Dartessex IV, leading to the celestial body being considered the homeworld of the house. Dartessex IV was listed as the Renliss headquarters in an entry for the house included in the Bounty Hunters Guild Handbook.The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Dartessex IV was first mentioned in the 1994 West End Games sourcebook Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters, written by Rick D. Stuart for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
