HH-4 grenade launcher
2188

HH-4 grenade launchers were support units manufactured by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. in use by certain smugglers and Wookiee warriors working with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The launchers had a capacity of five grenades, which were set on a timer and were capable of taking out a small Imperial squad.Star Wars: Battlefront II The HH-4 launcher appears in the Star Wars: Battlefront video game series. *Star Wars: Battlefront *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Battlefront II: Prima Official Game Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Grenade launchers Category:Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. products
