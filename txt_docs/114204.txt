Star Wars: The Official Magazine 66
1958

Star Wars: The Official Magazine 66, also styled as Star Wars Magazine 66, was the sixty-sixth issue of Star Wars: The Official Magazine, released on October 19, 2006 and covering November and December 2006. It contained 100 pages. *Com-Scan" *Jump to Hyperspace *Jawa's Corner *Scouting the Galaxy &mdash; The One That Almost Got Away *Q&A *Set Piece &mdash; Escape From Cloud City *Com-Link *"Inspired by Empire" by Pete Vilmur *"Where Did You Dig Up That Old Fossil?" *"ILM's Sizzling Mustafarian Summer" *"The Art of War" *"Underworld: A Galaxy of Scum and Villainy" *"Bricks, Whips, and Spaceships" *"The League of Spies" *"Inside the DVD Menus" by Pete Vilmur 66
