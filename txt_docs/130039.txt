Brakka Barakas
1315

Brakka Barakas was an elder of New Brigia. Brakka taken as a hostage by the Yevetha during the attack on the Brigian colony of New Brigia. Along with the other hostages taken during the Yevethan Purge, Brakka was forced to record a message begging the New Republic not to fire on Yevethan ships carrying prisoners. The Yevetha played the messages during the Battle of Doornik-319, causing hesitation from the New Republic ships and forcing them to withdraw.
