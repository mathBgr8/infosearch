Daniel Zizmor
1509

thumb|right|250px|Zizmor playing [[Kit Fisto/Legends|Kit Fisto.]] Daniel Zizmor is a storyboard and previsualization artist for Star Wars: The Clone Wars, a CGI-based television series that debuted October 3, 2008 on Cartoon Network as a continuation of the film with the same name, released 2 months prior. Zizmor was also a layout artist for Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith and worked in the visual effects department for Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones.
