The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 157
1132

The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 157 is the one hundred fifty-seventh issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Monthly series of comic reprints, published in May 1982. This issue includes one of the few original comic stories in the series, "The Flight of the Falcon".
