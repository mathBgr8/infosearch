G'nabgib
2057

The G'nabgib was an Imperial Lambda-class T-4a shuttle used to evacuate the leading TIE/D Defender scientist and other remaining personnel from a research facility. Part of a convoy that included the Mharsup and escorted by Maarek Stele, it was intended to rendezvous with the Hashim group. When the convoy was attacked by pirate forces, G'nabgib was damaged and captured, with its passengers transferred to enemy transport Ibutho 1.
