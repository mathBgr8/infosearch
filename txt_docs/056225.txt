Tadjyyyk

Tadjyyyk was a group of two Rebel Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports that carried personnel from the damaged Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Maximus before it was destroyed. The transports were able to make it to Nebulon-B frigate Anvil. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
