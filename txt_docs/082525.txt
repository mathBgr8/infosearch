High Marshal
941

High Marshal was a Galactic Republic military rank used during the Clone Wars by the Iron Knights. High Marshals were appointed by the Supreme Chancellor. Ilum and Dragite were High Marshals when they reemerged years before the Yuuzhan Vong War. High Marshal is also the highest rank in the mercenary group, Ailon Nova Guard. *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Jedi ranks Category:Military ranks
