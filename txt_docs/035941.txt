ADK-25-MED medical droid
1524

The ADK-25-MED was a type of medical droid employed by the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars. The droids often replied to their commander with quotes such as '"sedatives are not an option", "torture, or repair?", and "you organics are so fragile", indicating their enjoyment of painful operations. Like all the medical droids used by the CIS in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, the ADK-25-MED has a sadistic love of torturous surgery. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns Category:Droid models of the Confederacy of Independent Systems Category:Medical droid models
