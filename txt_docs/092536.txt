She served in the Rebel base of Dantooine during the first days of the Rebel Alliance. There, she met and fell in love with fellow Rebel Harovan Toth. They married and had a daughter, Samona; after that, Toth became an infiltrator for the Rebel SpecForces. Tisha died during the Battle of Thovinack.
