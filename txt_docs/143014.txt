Nagai expeditionary force
2344

The Nagai expeditionary force was a massive collection of cruisers and starships that became home to the Nagais after there homeworld of Nagi was conquered by the Tofs. The fleet was first seen above Kinooine where they aligned themselves with the Galactic Empire who aided the Nagai to avenge their defeat at Endor at the hands of the Alliance of Free Planets.
