Jake (miner)
2388

Jake was a large Human male miner who worked on Mimban. Jake sold some ancient artifacts to Bwahl the Hutt. Bwahl had Han Solo and Chewbacca deliver the credits to Jake, but the Hutt didn't send the amount Jake was expecting. When Solo and Chewbacca were ambushed by a group of Coway, Jake was wounded. Solo and Chewbacca proceeded to finish off the ambushers.The Essential Guide to Alien Species Years later, Jake made advances at Leia Organa, provoking a brawl with Luke Skywalker. The fight was broken up and the participants placed under Imperial arrest.
