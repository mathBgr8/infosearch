Nouland flare
1635

Nouland flares were nonsentient vines that were used to guard certain restricted areas. They were not very dangerous. They were threatening-looking, flammable, and would burn when hit with a blaster bolt. Governor Choard's estate on Shelkonwa had a number of Nouland flares growing on the property. Nouland flare first appeared in Timothy Zahn's 2007 novel Allegiance. *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Vines
