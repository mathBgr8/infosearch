22-B Nightfalcon
3582

The Ikas-Adno 22-B Nightfalcon was a versatile speeder bike, suitable for city navigation, racing, and military and security uses. It could carry one passenger in addition to the pilot and held four kilograms of cargo. It cost 6,250 credits when new. It was one of Ikas-Adno's most popular models.
