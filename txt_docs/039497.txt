L9 Crestrunner
2140

The L9 Crestrunner was an extreme depth submersible which combined the properties of a conventional underwater vehicle and a walker. As a submersible, it outclassed most military submarines, and could withstand high pressures, allowing it to dive to great depths. Alternately, a pair of retractable walker legs could be used to propel the craft through shallow water or over land as needed. Although a military submarine by design, the Crestrunner was primarily used as an operations vehicle during the clean up efforts after the Battle of Calamari.
