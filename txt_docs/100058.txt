Gottu
1457

Gottu, along with Idow, was an urban combat specialist of Lieutenant Judder Page's special forces team called the Katarn Commandos. In 8 ABY, he was one of the commandos assigned to infiltrate Kal'Shebbol to bring down the planetary shields so that the New Republic could liberate the planet.
