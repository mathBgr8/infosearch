Advb
984

Advb was the official language of Riflor, spoken by the Advozsec. The language had a written form. Written and spoken Advb was the language of choice of most of the Advozsec in the galaxy, with only a few of them bothering with learning Basic. Advb was not spoken by any other species, and since the destruction of all the life on Riflor, Advb was at best a lost language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Legacy Era Campaign Guide Category:Languages
