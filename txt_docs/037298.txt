Magro Slim
1268

Magro Slim was a male Klatooinian criminal affiliated with Eno Arba and Meeko Ghintee. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Administrator Rozatta with a bounty of 1,500 credits alive and 750 dead for damaging many of the Outland Transit Station's bathrooms and sanitary facilities.
