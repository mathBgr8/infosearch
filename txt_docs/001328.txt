Battle of Endor Hero's Medal
837

The Battle of Endor Hero's Medal was an award given to pilots (and possibly ground forces) of the Rebel Alliance who fought in the Battle of Endor. Ace Azzameen was awarded this medal for his valor in the assault against the second Death Star. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic awards
