|death=December 29, 2017 |listedpronouns= |nationality=Scottish |occupation=*Artist *Musician |companies= |website= }} }} Jim Baikie (February 28, 1940&ndash;December 29, 2017) was a Scottish artist who did the inking, penciling, and coloring for Star Wars: Empire's End for Dark Horse Comics. Baikie was most famous for working on Skizz with Alan Moore. He began his career in the 1960s working for several British girls' and tv-related comics, and he was a comics artists for four decades.
