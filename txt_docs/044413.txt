Berserker

The Berserkers and Advanced Berserkers were a caste of vicious Wookiee warriors armed with two Ryyk blades or bowcasters.The Force Unleashed Campaign Guide left|thumb|A Wookiee Berserker. After the Galactic Empire established control of Kashyyyk and began enslaving Wookiees, Berserkers began to fight Imperial Forces, with an ultimate goal of liberating their homeworld, fueling their single-minded fighting fervor with rage at what the Empire had done with them, their planet and their people. During the Galactic Civil War, Berserker groups remained in hiding due to the Empire's Human High Culture. They typically entered battle wielding two extremely large Ryyk blades, although they could also use bowcasters as ranged weapons, and were equipped with light armor for maximum mobility on the battlefield. Advanced Berserkers from Ittummi were used to great effect during the latter parts of liberation of Kashyyyk. Six Advanced Berserkers joined in the raid on Avatar Orbital Platform. Legendary stories told of the Berserkers' exploits, speaking of their ability to single-handedly decimate mercenary cadres.
