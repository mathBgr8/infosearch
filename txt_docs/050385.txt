Chosen One (Lannik)
1937

The Chosen One was a male Lannik cult leader operating on the planet Lannik two decades prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars. A male Lannik, the "Chosen One" was the title of a leader of a band of terrorists operating on Lannik. When the Chosen One took the control, he saw the cult's terrorist activity increase twofold. The cult's work, carried out by the Chosen One's faithful and devout followers, ranged from assassination to mass murder. The Chosen One personally hired the bounty hunter Aldar Beedo to eliminate Jedi High Council member and Jedi Master Even Piell, also a native of Lannik. *Star Wars: Episode I Insider's Guide Category:Lannik Category:Terrorists
