Herstell V
2052

Herstell V was a terrestrial astronomical object situated in the Herstell system of the Colonies. It hosted the headquarters of the "Red & Black League," a criminal organization that was active during the Galactic Civil War and was named after the colors of the Herstell system's Herstellic currency. Herstell V was mentioned in the 1995 sourcebook Heroes & Rogues, authored by Rick Stuart and Paul Sudlow and published for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
