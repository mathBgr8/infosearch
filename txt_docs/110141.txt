Teyan Praxeum
968

The Teyan Praxeum was a Jedi praxeum located on the planet Teya IV. The praxeum was founded around 4600 BBY by a group of Jedi. In 4000 BBY, Mistress Tannis taught Vici Ramunee and Veni Ramunee at the praxeum. Jedi Master Simikarty wrote his Teyan Apologia at the praxeum. *The Essential Atlas Category:Jedi academies
