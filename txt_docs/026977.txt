Nisotsa
2103

Nisotsa was a female Human Jedi who lived in the time of the Mandalorian Wars and the Jedi Civil War. Nisotsa attended a gathering called by Malak, around the time Revan decided to involve the Order in the Mandalorian Wars (circa 3963 BBY), in the presence of Meetra Surik and Bastila Shan, and fellow comrades Cariaga Sin, Xaset Terep, and Talvon Esan. Torn between remaining with the Jedi Order that chose inaction while countless innocents were slaughtered and the Republic's desperate plea for aid, Nisotsa ended up casting her lot in with the rogue Knights, going against the Jedi High Council's wishes, and by doing so, helped to split the Jedi Order in two. In 3951 BBY, Nisotsa would appear to Meetra in a vision Meetra experienced in the Tomb of Ludo Kressh. In the vision, she wielded a yellow lightsaber. In the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, the player is able to choose her appearance as that of the Exile.
