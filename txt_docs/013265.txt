**Ministry of Science *Galactic EmpireDark Empire endnotes, part 2: The Power of the Dark Side *Dark Empire |masters= |apprentices= }} Umak Leth was an Imperial engineer. He was one of the chief weapons engineers on the original Death Star.Death Star During the time leading up to the Clone Wars, Leth was a representative of the Galactic Republic's Ministry of Science. In 22 BBY, Leth participated in a HoloNet debate opposite Nasdra Magrody and Bunt Dantor, where he argued that the mysterious weapon targeting Republic task forces was possibly equipped with cloaking devices. He estimated that the power supply capable of destroying entire task forces of capital ships would require a vessel to be at least 8 kilometers long. He also speculated that a Jenth-class hypermatter core was the possible reactor being used for this. During the Great Jedi Purge, Leth designed the Universal Energy Cage to hold and transfer Jedi captives. He also developed the "resonator" devices, weapons which were used to torture and subjugate the Marasan people.Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies He also began work on the World Devastators as early as 0 BBY, with its development being so secret that Leth only relayed the most basic information to Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin, which he then used in the official field manual Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide.Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide After the Battle of Yavin discredited Bevel Lemelisk, Leth was given the position of Master of Imperial Projects for his creation of an advanced model of molecular furnace.Dark Empire Sourcebook Years later he designed the Galaxy Gun for the reborn Emperor Palpatine, as well as the World Devastators, SD-10 battle droids, and Shadow Droids.Dark Empire II 1 In addition, he presented the cloned Emperor with the XR-85, allowing the tank droids to finally be fully realized. He was killed when Palpatine's flagship Eclipse II fell out of hyperspace and collided with the Galaxy Gun.Empire's End 2 In the audio dramatization of Star Wars: Empire's End, Leth was voiced by John Cygan,Empire's End audio drama and in HoloNet News — A Galaxy Divided, he was voiced by David W. Collins.
