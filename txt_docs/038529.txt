Orthavan
2948

The Orthavan was a Mon Calamari Star Cruiser of the New Republic Defense Fleet and a sister ship of the Liberty. During Thrawn's campaign against the New Republic, Orthavan served as General Garm Bel Iblis's flagship of the fifteen warships assigned to protect Qat Chrystac. The vessel also served as a temporary headquarters for Rogue Squadron. Under the command of a Mon Calamari captain, the Orthavan took heavy damage while exchanging fire with two Victory-class Star Destroyers during the Battle of Qat Chrystac.
