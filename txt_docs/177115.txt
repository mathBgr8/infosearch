Belt of Bodo Baas
1728

thumb|right||Belt of Bodo Baas. }} The Belt of Master Bodo Baas was an article of clothing first found by Jedi Master Bodo Baas. In the centuries after Baas' demise the belt was passed down through various members of the Jedi Order. The last Jedi to own the belt was Jedi Master Ashka Boda, who was also in possession of Master Baas' holocron. It is not known what happened to the belt when Boda was captured by Imperial Sentinels and killed by Palpatine. It later resurfaced after the Battle of Yavin. The Belt of Master Bodo Baas is an item in Star Wars Galaxies, first introduced in Chapter 7. To obtain the item, the player has to collect several Jedi artifacts. It boosts several stats of the player character and, when worn in combination with a Jedi robe, gives skill bonuses to Lightsaber assembly and Lightsaber experimentation.
