Rhan'starn
567

Rhan'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the southern part of the planet's eastern continent. Liah'starn was to be found to the west-northwest, Mesdri'starn to the north, Shevre'starn to the northeast and Sal Vhe'starn to the east. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
