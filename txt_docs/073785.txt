Jack Perkins
353

Jack Perkins is a Star Wars fan who designed the Quariped in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the under 16 years old category. He was 9 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
