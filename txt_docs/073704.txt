Imperial Embassy (Procopia)
1220

The Imperial Embassy on Procopia was located on Estalle Island. Although officially the Tapani sector was part of the Empire, the Empire maintained an Embassy in the sector much like they did with other Imperial client states such as the CSA, Hutt Space and the Centrality. The building was in the contemporary Imperial architectural style seen on Coruscant.
