Shortly after the outbreak of the Clone Wars,The New Essential Chronology the insectoid combatant who went by the name of Krutch fought in the Cauldron on Rattatak. It was the largest gladiatorial pit on the planet, filled with violent warriors, where war barons and generals sought out the best combatants who would win their wars. Therefore, Krutch, like many other gladiators, entered a deadly free-for-all battle,The New Essential Guide to Droids but it was a rare gladiator indeed who could survive multiple fights there. thumb|left|200px|Krutch faces Crockagor in the Cauldron. During one battle, Krutch engaged in a duel with the reptilian Crockagor. The latter attempted to strike Krutch with a mace, but it blocked the blow with its shield, cast a net over his opponent, and swiftly finished him off. Shortly afterward, another gladiator, Kulltu Kutta, rode up to Krutch on own beast and cleaved it in two, causing the insectoid to explode. It scuttled into battle on multiple legs, carrying a net, a mace, and several blades in its six arms. Krutch first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003. The character was later given a name in Ultimate Sticker Book: Star Wars: Clone Wars, a sticker book written by Simon Beecroft and released in 2004.
