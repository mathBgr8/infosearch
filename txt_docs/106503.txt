Nebulas of Osssorck
3041

}} The Nebulas of Osssorck, also known as the Osssorck Nebulae, were a series of nebulosities located in the Core Worlds. During the Galactic Civil War, an aging yet legendary gunfighter resided in the city of Buerhoz on the Outer Rim Territories' &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Colu system planet Clak'dor VII. When a group of individuals encountered the gunfighter at Buerhoz, he claimed to have witnessed the destruction of several cruisers of the Galactic Empire in the Nebulas of Osssorck. The Nebulas of Osssorck were introduced via mention in "A Fistful of Credits," a roleplaying game adventure written by Bill Olmesdahl and included in the 1992 supplement Star Wars Gamemaster Screen for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
