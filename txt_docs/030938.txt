Chancellor Palpatine Commercial Nexus
538

The Chancellor Palpatine Commercial Nexus was a large spaceport on Coruscant built during, and named after, the chancellorship of Palpatine. It was connected to the Chancellor Palpatine Spaceport, and the combined complex dwarfed all other spaceports on Coruscant. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous Category:Coruscant spaceports
