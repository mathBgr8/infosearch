Rekpa De
2939

Rekpa De was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive, Repka De was instructed in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. Trained as a Guardian, Master De was well-trained in the art of lightsaber combat; so much so that he was selected by the acting battlemaster to gain the title of lightsaber instructor at the Coruscant Jedi Temple. After the Jedi High Council approved the appointment, Master De began teaching basic lightsaber techniques at the Temple to the many Initiates that passed through the Temple academy.
