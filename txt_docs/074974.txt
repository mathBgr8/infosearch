Michael Bell
3209

Michael Bell (born July 30, 1938) is an American voice actor, actor and voice director. He provided the voice of General Vanden Huyck Willard in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope who was physically portrayed by Eddie Byrne, although he was uncredited. He would later return to voice Tyr Taskeen in the video game Star Wars: Force Commander, which released in 2000. He also voiced General Jan Dodonna for the 2014 animated television series, Star Wars Rebels.
