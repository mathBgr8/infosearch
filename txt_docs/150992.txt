Midnight Squadron
1169

Midnight Squadron was a SpecForce unit of the Rebel Alliance. It was part of the Eclipse Team. Commander Derembus Sitnalta was the heavy weapons crewer of Midnight Squadron while Lieutenant Colonel Palpatine Essex Yerac served as Midnight Leader. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic Special Forces units
