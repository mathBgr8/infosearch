|end date=March 31, 1987Marvel Age 51 |schedule=Bimonthly |format= |issues=14 |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Ewoks was a fourteen-issue series of comics published by Star Comics, an imprint of Marvel Comics, from 1985 to 1987. The series was a tie-in to the animated series and was aimed at a young audience. The series' first four issues were released before the television show premiered.
