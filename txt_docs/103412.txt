RM-09 Alliance shuttle
2572

The RM-09 Alliance Shuttle was a craft employed by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The shuttle was used to transport various Rebellion units and dignitaries across the galaxy. It was only lightly armed and could not protect itself well against enemy attacks, so it almost always required an escort. The RM-09 was similar in appearance to the Republic Troop Transport, used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars.
