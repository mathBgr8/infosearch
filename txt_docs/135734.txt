Sunnydew flower
1241

The sunnydew flower was a nectar-producing plant native to Endor. The flowers were indigenous to Endor's Zandor region, and its nectar was known for its medicinal properties that kept the rest of Zandor's flora flourishing. The rampaging Ogre Gantu eventually destroyed all of Zandor's sunnydew flowers, rendering Zandor a wasteland&mdash;however, the Ewoks Wicket Wystri Warrick and Teebo eventually chased Gantu off, leading to a regrowth in sunnydew flowers that promised to spread across Zandor. *Ewoks (1985) 1 Category:Flowers Category:Pharmaceutical plants Category:Plants of Endor
