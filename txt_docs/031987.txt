Dendu
1899

Dendu was a Human male stormtrooper who was member of the Stormtrooper Corps during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, he was assigned aboard the Star Destroyer Imperator, along with the platoon led by Cadet Leader Kyle Katarn. He was a part of the battle on AX-456. Late in the mission and already low on ammo, they inspected the comm center, where a small maintenance droid saw the Imperials and squeaked. Dendu tried to shoot it but missed, and Sergeant major Hong reprimanded Dendu for wasting precious ammo.
