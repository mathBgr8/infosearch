Garbage chute
1056

Garbage chutes were used to transport garbage into a garbage compactor. In 1 BBY,Star Wars: Galactic Atlas: Updated Edition on a mission to rescue Princess Leia Organa from the Death Star, farmboy Luke Skywalker, smuggler Han Solo, and Wookiee Chewbacca, alongside Leia, went down a garbage chute into an Imperial garbage masher to escape Stormtroopers.
