Luna (secretary)
938

Luna was Hakon deVille's secretary and bodyguard. She was tall, elegant, efficient, and completely loyal to Hakon. She also appeared to be his companion and was the only person onboard the Ananuru Express to hold a weapon, having a blaster in her handbag.
