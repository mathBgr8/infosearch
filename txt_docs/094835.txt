Krieg
1775

Fleet Admiral Krieg was a Zabrak Imperial stationed at Kachirho on Kashyyyk during the Galactic Civil War. Krieg employed a pro-Imperial spacer to carry out a couple of missions for him&mdash;the escort of an AT-ST, the defense of Kachirho from a Rebel strike force, and the relief of two scout troopers deep within Kkowir Forest. His command was soon taken over by Darth Vader, who sent the spacer another mission, but Krieg remained on Kashyyyk. Krieg appeared as a non-player character in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
