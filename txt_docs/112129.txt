Battle of Storinal
1905

The Battle of Storinal was fought between Imperial Remnant and New Republic forces for the control of the planet of Storinal. During the battle, the Star Destroyer Peremptory bombarded Princess Leia Organa's flagship Rebel Dream, and it was captured along with its crew of 37,000.
