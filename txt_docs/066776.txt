Bay One
1400

Bay One was the name of the main hangar bay located onboard the modified Providence-class carrier/destroyer Invisible Hand. Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker crash-landed in Bay One during the Battle of Coruscant, in their attempt to rescue Supreme Chancellor Palpatine. It was filled with B2 super battle droids and B1-Series battle droids. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization *Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith video game Category:Docking bays Category:Invisible Hand locations
