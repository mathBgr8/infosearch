Around 1500 BBY, during the New Sith Wars, the Sith settled on the planet where they built temples to evil spirits. This group was eventually defeated by the Jedi, who transformed the temples into storehouses of knowledge. During the years of the Galactic Empire, these temples were robbed for their precious artifacts by the Empire, and an Imperial fuel-ore processing plant was built.
