Holorepository
568

The Holorepository was a library on the wheelworld Arkam 13 where Atour Riten once worked as an archivist. It was believed by many to have one of the largest collections of lore on the Old Republic, greater than even the Jedi Archives in the Jedi Temple. *Death Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Libraries
