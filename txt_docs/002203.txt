Uro-ik v'alh
2108

The uro-ik v'alh was a massive, oblong Yuuzhan Vong battleship. It was 2,240 meters on average, and armed with 80 yaret-kor as well as hundreds of dovin basals. It had a crew requirement of 1,640 warriors, and could carry 3,150 troops and 16,480 metric tons of cargo. It could dock coralskippers, and had several docking arms for that task. Like the rest of the Yuuzhan Vong's technology, the Uro-ik v'alh was an organic ship. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital ship classes Category:Yuuzhan Vong starship classes
