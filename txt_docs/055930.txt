Hetrinar assault bomber
1312

The Hetrinar assault bomber was a starfighter used by the Hapes Consortium. Hetrinar assault bombers flew alongside Miy'til starfighters aboard Nova-class battle cruisers for "defensive purposes". *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bomber classes Category:Starship classes of the Hapan Consortium
