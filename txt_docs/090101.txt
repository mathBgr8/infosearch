|published in=Star Wars Tales 15 |reprinted in= |pages=8 |timeline=c. 2-1 BBYFirst Impressions depicts Leia Organa's first visit to Coruscant with her (adopted) father, Senator Bail Organa of Alderaan. The New Essential Chronology dates Leia Organa's birth to 19 BBY. From Children of the Jedi it is stipulated that Leia formally joined the Imperial Senate at 18 years of age. Hence, the timeline of First Impressions is no earlier than 2 BBY as dated from Leia's birth year. |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} First Impressions is a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 15 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4. Senator Bail Prestor Organa, preparing to hand his Senate seat over to Leia Organa, brings her to Coruscant for the first time. Her father allows her to go off into the city with two guards. At first entranced by what she sees, Leia then witnesses a Caamasi being arrested by two stormtroopers for simply trying to buy a drink at a cafe. When she tries to intervene on his behalf, the stormtroopers nearly arrest her as well. Back at the apartment, Leia fumes to Bail, and says that she'll bring it up to Palpatine when she meets him at a reception being held at the Imperial Palace that night. Bail tries to dissuade her, saying that doing so now would jeopardize Alderaan when, once she is a senator, she will have the ability and power to try to make changes. Leia refuses to be deterred. At the Palace, Leia is appalled at how deferential and pandering everyone is to the Emperor, which only hardens her resolve to bring up the issue to him. When they finally meet face to face, however, Leia is struck by the pure evil radiating from him, and is unable to do anything more than mumble thanks to his compliments as Darth Vader watches. Following the reception, Bail assures Leia that it is nothing to be ashamed of, as that is a common reaction to seeing Palpatine, especially for the first time, and that to make changes, one must be subtle. He tells her how proud he is of her, and relates a piece of good news - he has arranged for the Caamasi she saw arrested to be released. Happy, Leia goes to meet him, and the story ends with him introducing himself as Eg'ros Akala. The age of Leia is somewhat ill-defined in First Impressions, as she is still a teenager with a very naïve demeanour and not the hard-headed Rebel yet seen in A New Hope. Luke's and Leia's well-defined birth year is 19 BBY. The same year the Clone Wars ends with Order 66 achieving the murders of nearly all the Jedi Order and the Rise of the Empire with the ascension of Emperor Palpatine. Children of the Jedi established that Leia was 18 years old when she formally replaced her (adopted) father Bail Organa as the Senator for Alderaan in the Imperial Senate. The events in First Impressions would have to place Leia as being around 16 to 17 years old and thus as early as 3-2 BBY; however, 1 BBY is a problematic timeline without more corroborating evidence as to how long Leia spent in the Senate prior to joining the Rebellion and the events in Rogue One that lead to the events of A New Hope.
