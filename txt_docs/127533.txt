Slimy nonakara
1272

An inhabitant of lakes and swamps, the slimy nonakara or whirling snakes was a species that somehow had spread to numerous planets. Their primarily herbivorous diet was supplemented with occasional fish and insects. Nonakara excreted slime that slowed down their prey. They also exchanged slime to mate: being hermaphroditic, both partners would have offspring.
