68-RKO
1354

68-RKO was a droid in the service of Bail Organa during the Clone Wars. He happened to be traveling on the same pleasure cruiser as Obi-Wan Kenobi and followed him in order not to be considered luggage. While accompanying Kenobi he witnessed the Jedi save the ship from Merson pirates, and defuse a hostile mob of passengers.
