Cindar
1252

Cindar was a male Kajain'sa'Nikto who was a high-ranking member of the Nebula Front prior to the Battle of Naboo. He was Eru Matalis' second-in-command of the Nebula Front's militant wing. He was killed on Karfeddion after being hit by a ricocheted blaster bolt while fighting the Jedi Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi. *Cloak of Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Kajain'sa'Nikto Category:Males
