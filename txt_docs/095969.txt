Lexlar
2419

The Lexlars were a species of sentient, hair-covered hominid. Odeon Farnish was a three-feet-tall, Basic-speaking Lexlar who worked as a customs inspector during the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance. At one point, Farnish referred to another being as "hairless one." The Lexlar species was introduced in the 1987 rulebook for the first edition of West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The book compared the appearance of the Lexlar Odeon Farnish to Cousin Itt, a character introduced in the 1964&ndash;1966 television series The Addams Family. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game Category:Sentient species
