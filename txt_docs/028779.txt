Troye Mulleshar
1409

Lieutenant Troye Mulleshar was a male Senate Guard who worked for the Galactic Republic during the Separatist Crisis. One week before the scheduled vote for the Military Creation Act, Mulleshar was one of the Senate Guards assigned to disrupt a protest outside the Senate Building. When he attempted to extricate wounded protesters, he and fellow Guard Anjavay Rosit were clubbed by the crowd and received blunt-force trauma wounds. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Senate Guards
