Bactade
829

Bactade was an edible form of bacta that was introduced some time prior to 9 ABY. A muddy liquid that was dispensed in bottles, bactade possessed good healing qualities, however it had a terrible, chalky taste. During their attempt to retrieve the moss painting Killik Twilight and the code module ensconced within while on Tatooine, both Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo had to take bactade in order to heal their injuries.Tatooine Ghost *Tatooine Ghost *Star Wars: Tiny Death Star Category:Bacta
