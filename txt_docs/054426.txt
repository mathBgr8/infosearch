Imperieuse
1614

Imperieuse was a Broadside-class cruiser in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Impérieux is French for "imperious," and Impérieuse its feminine counterpart. Three ships of the British Royal Navy have been named HMS Imperieuse. *Star Wars: Empire at War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Broadside-class cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
