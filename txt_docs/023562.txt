Battle of Dagary Minor
2220

A battle occurred on the planet Dagary Minor during the Mandalorian Wars, in 3963 BBY. It was one of many early losses for the Galactic Republic as it struggled to fend off the invading Mandalorians. The Jedi general that would come to be known as the Jedi Exile participated in the battle.
