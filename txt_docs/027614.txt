Aludium pu-36
1104

Aludium pu-36 could be used as an ingredient for types of alcoholic drinks. The green substance was used at the Outlander Club. It had a radioactive ingredient, but it was safe enough for humanoid consumption. *Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary Category:Food additives
