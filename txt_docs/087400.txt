Golfhan
1229

Golfhan was a male Caridan who served as ambassador for the government of Carida during the end of the Separatist Crisis. In 22 BBY, he supported the Military Creation Act during the start of the Clone Wars, a galactic conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Caridans Category:Diplomats Category:Males
