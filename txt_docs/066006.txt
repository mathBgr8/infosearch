Rkk'Tl'Kt
1499

Rkk'Tl'Kt was a Tusken Raider in the warband led by Sharad Hett. During A'Sharad Hett's initiation rite, Rkk'Tl'Kt's hand was bitten off by a canyon krayt dragon female. Unable to wield a gaffi stick one-handed, Rkk'Tl'Kt chose suicide. Sharad Hett gave him a dagger and he stabbed himself in the stomach.
