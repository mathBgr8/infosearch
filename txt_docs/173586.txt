Putten Beatus
1837

Putten Beatus was a near-Human female. She was an outcast who found work as a customs inspector at the Mos Eisley Spaceport. She was different from a normal Human by her gray skin and large ears. Because of her looks, she was known to attract quite a few stares. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Category:Customs officials Category:Females Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Near-Humans
