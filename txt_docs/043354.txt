Greg Gorden
2246

Greg Gorden is a writer and game designer. He contributed to several books for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, published by West End Games. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game (credited with "Heroic and Meritorious Service to the Rebellion") *The Star Wars Rules Companion *Otherspace (editor) *Riders of the Maelstrom (editor) *Imperial Sourcebook *Cracken's Rebel Field Guide (editor) *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition (credited with "Original Design" and "Additional Design") *Tales of the Jedi Companion (credited with "Original Force Power Design") *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook (credited, along with Greg Costikyan, with "Additional Design" for the Force powers "Contort/escape," "Force of will," and "Shift sense") *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded (credited with "original design") Three vignettes originally published in the Imperial Sourcebook have been reprinted by Hyperspace: *Death of a Rebel Base *Imperial Spin *Admiral Jerjerrod's Testimony *James Bond 007 (credited as "System Developer") *Torg (credited with "Concept and Design") *DC Heroes Role-Playing Game, 3rd Ed.
