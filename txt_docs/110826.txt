C-7 protocol droid
480

The C-7 protocol droid was a household droid programmed with many functions and forms of communication. It was manufactured by Cybot Galactica on the factory moon of Telti. It was the predecessor of the C-8 protocol droid and the successor of the C-6 protocol droid. *The New Rebellion Category:C-series protocol droid models
