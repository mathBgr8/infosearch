Grey Gabaki
521

A plant named for its drab color when dried, Grey Gabaki gave off a light and relaxing smoke when burned. It was commonly used in cantinas, but some merchants also burned it to make store fronts a more relaxing environment for customers. *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Pharmaceutical plants Category:Plants of Toydaria Category:Smoking
