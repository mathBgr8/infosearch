Casement
1587

Casement was the code-name of a Rebel Alliance undercover agent working with Porter under Winter on Drunost in 0 ABY. While operating in the Shelsha sector, Casement once survived a raid staged by the BloodScar pirates near Ashkas-kov. In this episode, the BloodScars attacked an entire convoy with ten of their own ships, even blowing up the four ships they were primarily targeting after stripping them of goods. Casement survived the attack because his ship had an armored inner hull.
