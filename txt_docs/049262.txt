Yashaka Mountains

The Yashaka Mountains were a range on planet Yashuvhu. They served as an isolated retreat where Force adept and former wanderer Valara Saar, locally known as "Prophetess", placed her humble hut and trained some of her students. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee Category:Mountain ranges
