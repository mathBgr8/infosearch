Grand Imperial Union
1012

The Grand Imperial Union was supposedly a faction of the former Galactic Empire led by Moff Tragg Brathis which was to have alleged to exist by the Duskhan League, however the New Republic did not believe the claim and that it was simply Yevethan propaganda. During a Yevethan pirate broadcast over HoloNet Channel 81, the Grand Imperial Union announced its support of the Duskhan League in its war against the New Republic. This was the the first any of the New Republic's leaders heard of the Grand Imperial Union. Brathis, as well, was an unknown name, leading most of the Republic's leaders to conclude that the Grand Imperial Union was simply manufactured by the Yevetha for propaganda purposes. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Propaganda
