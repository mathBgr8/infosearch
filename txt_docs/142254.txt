Master control signal
766

The master control signal was dispatched to all battle droids and warships of the CIS during the Clone Wars by Lord Vader, who used this signal to deactivate the CIS's droid army and navy after he murdered the Separatist Council, thus paving the way for Palpatine's new Galactic Empire.
