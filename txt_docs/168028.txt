Aurra Sing's starfighter
1612

The bounty hunter Aurra Sing had a starfighter. It was carried by the bounty hunter aboard her cruiser. Sing would leave her cruiser drifting and seemingly helpless to distract her pursuers, then surprise-attack them in her fighter.
