Square of Hopeful Redemption
2398

The Square of Hopeful Redemption was an square built at the city of Redhaven before the Osarian-Rhommamool conflict between the Osarian and Rhommamoolians, before the conflict, it was a primary trading spaceport of the planet of Rhommamool. After Yuuzhan Vong Nom Anor's takeover, the spaceport was turned into a public square in which citizens could stone droids, declare freedom from Osarian, renounce the New Republic and the Jedi, and to cry out for a simpler time with no technology. After Anor's takeover, it was also renamed to the Square of Hopeful Redemption. The citizens came to the square to stone droids and technological objects. In 25 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion Nom Anor and his Red Knights of Life stoned a protocol droid they found, C-9PO which wandered across the city of Redhaven. The Square of Hopeful Redemption appeared in the 1999 novel The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime, written by R. A.
