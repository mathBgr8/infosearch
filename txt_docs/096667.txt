Battlegroup Orion
1542

Battlegroup Orion was an Imperial battle group assigned to Task Force Vengeance. The Star Destroyers Nemesis and Conqueror were assigned to this group. At a skirmish near Almaran, Admiral Senn ordered Battlegroup Orion to take up pursuit of the Calamari cruiser Liberty, alongside with Interdictor Compellor.
