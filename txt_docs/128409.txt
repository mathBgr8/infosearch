Brenn
1053

Brenn was the capital city of Genesia. Brenn is primarily under the control of local crime lord Eelien Kirat, although Soach, a smuggling and black market kingpin, is challenging this rule. Brenn has great logistical value to the Alliance as a busy major port where few questions are asked. *Flashpoint!
