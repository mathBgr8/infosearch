Swaze
802

The Swaze were a technologically advanced people from the Jidlor system. Though they had an advanced culture, with access to starship technology, they revered the wisdom of the comparatively primitive Rellarin people of Rellnas Minor. Every year, the Swaze leaders would go to Rellnas Minor to seek advice from the Rellarin tribal leaders and sages, the un'Yala. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Mid Rim sentient species
