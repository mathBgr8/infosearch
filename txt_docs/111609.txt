Bowlump
936

thumb A bowlump was a hairy, mollusk-like predator native to the oceans of the planet Naboo. These creatures could shoot down prey from overhanging branches with well-placed jets of water. The bowlump was able to unhook its stalk from the muddy shallows and move slowly through the water by using its water jet for propulsion.
