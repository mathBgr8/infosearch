Flechétte missile
514

Flechétte missiles were a type of dart-shaped projectile weapon, typically around eleven centimeters long. They were carried aboard the BTL-S8 K-wing assault starfighter and had the ability to follow their targets. *Before the Storm *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Air-to-air missiles
