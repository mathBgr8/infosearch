Crystal Reef Amusement and Tourism Council
599

The Crystal Reef Amusement and Tourism Council ran the resort city of Crystal Reef on Dac. Crystal Reef was a city for the extremely wealthy. it was along the oceans of Dac's northern polar region. The city has viewing decks that gave its passengers views of icebergs. The Crystal Reef Amusement and Tourism Council was in charge of most real estate and most of the gambling offerings across the city. *Young Jedi Knights: Crisis at Crystal Reef Category:Organizations of Dac Category:Tourism organizations
