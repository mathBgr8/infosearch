House Sreethyn
1300

House Sreethyn was one of the more militant of the noble Ancient Houses that ruled Juvex sector.Children of the Jedi Their homeworld was Juvex, which was the capital of the sector solely in name; only outsiders were concerned with the planet's official title. House Sreethyn also possessed a trio of shabby industrial worlds, Kimm Aurek, Besh, and Cresh. In 12 ABY the House allied with Roganda Ismaren. *Children of the Jedi *The Essential Atlas Sreethyn
