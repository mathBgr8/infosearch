The hull of the fighter craft consisted of two large horizontal wings attached to the main hull, with a smaller diagonal wing on the each underside. It was propelled by three sublight engines, one per wing and a third fixed atop the rear of the starfighter's hull. The front of the fighter consisted of two cockpits, and the fighter was armed with a large ventral turret armed with two dual cannons, and a smaller dorsal turret.
