Sigil (starfighter)
2345

Sigil was a type of starfighter used by the Lok Revenants. The Sigil were a class of single-seat starfighters with two engines. Sigils were equipped with shields, a hyperdrive, and laser cannons. The pirate Nym acquired several of these for his band. They were used to raid Trade Federation shipping around the time of 32 BBY. After Nym was driven from his base, his forces continued to fight against the Trade Federation occupation of their system and continued to use the Sigil. Sol Sixxa, the leader of the Mere Resistance, used one as his personal starfighter.
