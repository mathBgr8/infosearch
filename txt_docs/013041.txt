The Millennium Falcon had a brief encounter with the world nine years after the Battle of Endor. In the early months of the Yuuzhan Vong invasion the Vong priestess Elan was captured by a New Republic task force present in the system. Later during the Yuuzhan Vong War, the Imperial Remnant attempted to protect the world, before it fell to the invaders.
