Lak Jit
2370

Lak Jit was a male Devaronian information broker, who worked indirectly for Talon Karrde. In 19 ABY, he unearthed several old datacards while excavating the Mount Tantiss on the planet Wayland. It was revealed later that he had found a copy of the Caamas Document. Leia managed to catch him with the assistance of Talon Karrde.
