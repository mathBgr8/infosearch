MD-10 medical droid
1444

The MD-10 medical droid was a model of medical droid and a part of the MD-series that was manufactured by Industrial Automaton. The design was a breakout success, and each droid was built to be a specialist within a particular medical field, including cardiology, neurology, obstetrics, and dentistry. *The New Rebellion *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:MD-series medical droid models
