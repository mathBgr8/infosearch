Baragwin assault blade
1040

The Baragwin assault blade was an advanced (for its time) vibrosword produced around the time of the Jedi Civil War. The weapon not only offered improved functionality, but was also fitted with capacitors that would discharge upon the blade's contact with a target. Empty space within the blade was reserved for the installation of common upgrade modules. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Vibroswords
