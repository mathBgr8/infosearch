Brazzo
1680

Brazzo was a Human male bounty hunter on Centares. He was known to have learned the identity of Luke Skywalker. He and his sister, Selle, fled from Darth Vader, who eventually caught them. He fired his blaster at Vader, who deflected the bolt with lightsaber into Selle. Overcome with grief, Brazzo failed to dodge Vader's killing stroke. *Vader's Quest 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia thumb|left|Darth Vader kills Brazzo.
