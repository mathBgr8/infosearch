|location=Cinnagar, Empress Teta |interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} Club Corellia was a nightclub located within the Core District of the city of Cinnagar on the planet Empress Teta. Like many of the attractions within the Core District, the club's architecture represented a specific planet within the Core Worlds region of the galaxy; specifically Corellia. Club Corellia specialized in food, drink, music, entertainment, and dance from Corellia as well as a variety of other planets in the galactic core.
