John Beyer
1004

John Beyer is the author of a number of roleplaying game sourcebooks including Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide, Heroes & Rogues and Operation: Elrood. He has also co-written two short stories with Wayne Humfleet, "Swoop Gangs" and "Lumrunners" for the Star Wars Adventure Journal.
