Wedding of Nrin Vakil and Koyi Komad
842

The Wedding of Nrin Vakil and Koyi Komad took place sometime between the end of the Thrawn campaign and the start of the Yuuzhan Vong War. Anki Pace and Corran Horn were among the attendees, as presumably were other members of Rogue Squadron. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught Category:Weddings
