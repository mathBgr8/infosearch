Annihilator (disambiguation)
2019

Annihilator could refer to several things: *Annihilator, an Imperial Executor-class Star Dreadnought later known as the Liberty's Misrule. *Annihilator, a Star Destroyer that partook in the Battle of Endor. *Scorpenek annihilator droid, a type of droid used by the Pyke Syndicate during the Battle of Mos Espa. *DT-57 "Annihilator" blaster, a type of blaster. **"Grievance Striker," also known as the Annihilator. *Annihilator, an alias used by the droid Bollux. *Annihilator, an Executor-class Star Dreadnought owned by Admiral Gaarn. *Annihilator, a Star Destroyer which patrolled near Hutt Space and the Periphery. *Annihilator, an Imperial command ship. *Annihilator 1, Beri Tulon's personal All Terrain Armored Transport. *Annihilator, a nonexistent superweapon. *Annihilator-class starfighter, manufactured by the One Sith. *Scorpenek annihilator droid, built by the Colicoid Creation Nest. *DT-57 blaster, also known as the "Annihilator." *Hypermatter annihilator, reactor, used in the Death Stars.
