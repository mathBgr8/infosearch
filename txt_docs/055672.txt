|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Zygerrian Takedown" was a free online supplement published by Wizards of the Coast on July 24, 2003. The mini-adventure was written by Jason Fry for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The mini-adventure is set in the Rise of the Empire era and is designed for four player characters in the 8&ndash;10 level range.
