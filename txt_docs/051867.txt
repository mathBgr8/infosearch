Model C-22 flame carbine
2083

*7 meters |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=Corporate Sector }} The Model C-22 flame carbine was an incendiary weapon produced by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc.. The carbine featured an external fuel canister worn on a belt and connected to the weapon by a flexible tube. The C-22 was used by Corporate Sector forces as a riot control device.
