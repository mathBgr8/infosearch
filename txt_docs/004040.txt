|next=*Clone Wars *Great Jedi Purge *Imperial Period conflicts |image=File:ShatterpointCoverArt.jpg |name=Summertime War |begin=c. 52 BBY |end=22 BBY |date= |place=Haruun Kal |result=*Jedi/Republic victory **ULF is disbanded |battles=*Battle of Lorshan Pass *Battle of Pelek Baw *Duel at Pelek Baw |side1=*Galactic Republic *Jedi Order |side2=*Confederacy of Independent Systems *Order of the Sith Lords **Dark Acolytes *Balawai |side3=*Upland Liberation Front&dagger; *Korunnai |side4= |commanders1=*Jedi High Council **High General Mace Windu *Republic High Command *Nick Rostu *Colonel Lorz Geptun |commanders2=*Darth Sidious *Count Dooku *Colonel Lorz Geptun |commanders3=*Depa Billaba *Kar Vastor |commanders4= }} The Summertime War was a cyclically recurring planetary war on Haruun Kal between the Korunnai and the Balawai. Later involvement from the Galactic Republic and Confederacy of Independent Systems turned the Summertime War into a proxy war of the galactic-wide Clone Wars. The war began almost thirty years before the Clone Wars as a disagreement over the use of Haruun Kal's jungles. The Korunnai relied on their grassers to move through, and therefore plow, the jungles to find safety for the winter, while the Balawai needed the jungles intact to harvest and export its output. The Korunnai, fiercely protective of the grassers, began guerrilla war against the Balawai, who responded in kind with air strikes to protect their financial interests. The conflict developed into a brutal all out war that began each year after the snows of winter subsided and lasted until the autumn rains began, gaining its name as the "summertime" war. The situation worsened as the Separatist crisis brewed in the Galactic Republic. Separatist interests began to sponsor the Balawai, providing them with weapons and technology to trump the primitive Korunnai, in hopes of convincing them to join Count Dooku's movement. With the outbreak of the Clone Wars, the Separatists gave the Balawai an offer they could not refuse: the defeat of the Korunnai in exchange for control of the Al'Har system. The Balawai accepted, and Confederacy forces were quick to establish a droid garrison on Haruun Kal. Jedi Masters Mace Windu and Depa Billaba, having learned of the Confederacy's sponsorship of the Balawai, raced to Haruun Kal to save the system from falling into enemy hands. Upon their arrival, the Jedi Masters led the Korunnai in a series of decisive victories at Lorshan Pass and Pelek Baw.
