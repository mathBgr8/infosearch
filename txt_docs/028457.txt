Ghost Battalion
1419

Ghost Battalion, also known as Special Ops Group, was an elite special operations unit of the Imperial Army. As of 1 BBY, Second Platoon, B Company of the Battalion, under the command of Lieutenant Brazack and containing such commandos as Koyo and Santo, was assigned to Inquisitor Jerec. It was this unit that was responsible for the death of Morgan Katarn. *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire Category:Imperial Army battalions Category:Imperial Special Forces units
