Terminator
1476

Terminator was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The Terminator was dispatched to attack the Rebel Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Cathleen while it was recovering battle-weary starfighters returning from a successful attack on Imperial shipping in Corporate Sector. The Terminator launched TIE/sa bombers to destroy the vulnerable Rebel ships, but two T-65 X-wing starfighters and two RZ-1 A-wing interceptors from Red Squadron held off the Imperial fighters while the Cathleen recovered the other Rebel ships and escaped. *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
