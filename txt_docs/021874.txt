Isen Asteroid Belts
607

The Isen Asteroid Belts were three asteroid fields located in the Isen system. The first two, Kisen and Kossan, circled Isen in the first two orbital positions. The third, Kossim, lay in the fourth orbital position. The Asteroids were full of minerals and ores of great value. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One *Star Wars Gamemaster Handbook Category:Asteroid fields in the Outer Rim Territories Category:Isen system locations
