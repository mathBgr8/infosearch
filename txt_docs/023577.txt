Bantha Breakfast Biscuit
965

thumb|right|[[Bungo Bung enjoys a Bantha Breakfast Biscuit.]] Bantha Breakfast Biscuits were a popular food of the Biscuit Baron chain. They were often eaten with blue sauce. The biscuits were also noted to be the source of the Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuit, which were created by the accidental mixing of preservatives in the biscuits with bantha genetic material.
