In 1 ABY, he was the Chief Scientist of the organization and stationed in the Mummer bunker, the organization's local headquarters, located near Theed. During that time, he was responsible of a violent terrorist attack in Theed. Crimsol Vaighn was killed not long after the attack, during a raid on the Mummer bunker by troopers of the Royal Naboo Security Forces led by Sergeant Brunser. Crimsol Vaighn appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
