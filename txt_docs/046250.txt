Flashship
1190

Flashships were rare starfighters built by the Korlier Combine. Though they were very fast, Flashships were built primarily for space combat. Just like all Korlier designs, they had no serial numbers, which made them hard to track and identify. *Vision of the Future Category:Korlier Combine products Category:Starfighter classes
