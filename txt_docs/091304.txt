Helb
1376

Helb was a male Neimoidian broker for stolen tech equipment in a gang called the Tech Raiders on Coruscant. He once hired Fligh to steal a datapad from Senator Uta S'orn. When the Jedi came looking for information, he revealed to Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi that Fligh owed credits to the Raiders. *Jedi Apprentice: The Deadly Hunter *Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Males Category:Neimoidians
