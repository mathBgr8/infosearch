Fevye'starn
812

Fevye'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the southeastern part of the planet's eastern continent, facing the Rhyde'vak ocean. Sal Vhe'starn was located to the west of the city. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities on Bothawui
