Forak

Forak was the capital city of the planet of Trandosha. In downtown Forak, there was an Imperial repair center located on Banir Avenue. Trandoshan mercenary and assassin Nakaron was exiled from Forak after showing anti-social behavior towards the city's Elders. Category:Capital cities Category:Cities on Trandosha
