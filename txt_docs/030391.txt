Imperial hunter (starship)
2098

Imperial hunters were long-range intercept starships developed by the Dark Empire during the reign of Palpatine's clones on Byss. One such ship, Scourge One, carried Executor Sedriss QL, Vill Goir, and a group of Force-enhanced stormtroopers to the ancient Jedi planet of Ossus, in an effort to capture Luke Skywalker for Palpatine.
