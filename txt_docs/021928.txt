|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYDate established in narrative text. |series=Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand |issue=1 }} Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 1 Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 1 was the first issue in the Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand series of comics. It was released on August 5, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. After she fails to eliminate the young Jedi Luke Skywalker, Mara Jade is dispatched to take down a crimelord who seeks to reestablish the criminal organization Black Sun. Little does she know that the Emperor's imminent date with death will change her life forever! Written by Timothy Zahn and Michael A. Stackpole, and illustrated by Carlos Ezquerra.
