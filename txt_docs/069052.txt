Sands in Winter
1969

The Talz thief Muftak released an autobiographical novel entitled Sands in Winter. Despite being regarded as simple on his homeworldThe Essential Guide to Characters of Tatooine, the Talz developed enough eloquence to tell his story about how his larval sack was accidentally loaded onto an Imperial freighter going to the planet.The Essential Guide to Alien Species The book became a best seller and ensured that Muftak and his adopted Chadra-Fan daughter Kabe would never have to worry about credits again. Sands in Winter was first mentioned in the 2001 reference book The Essential Guide to Alien Species, written by Ann Margaret Lewis. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Journals and memoirs
