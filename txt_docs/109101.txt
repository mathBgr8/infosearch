Sounder Flats computer complex
1123

The Sounder Flats computer complex was an Imperial computer facility on Ruul, a planet in the Core Worlds. It was situated on Sounder Flats, between a steep hill and a deep canyon. Two Imperial bases nearby and a network of turrets protected it.
