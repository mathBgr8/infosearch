Battle Group of Yun-Txiin
1040

The Battle Group of Yun-Txiin was one of the five battle groups the Yuuzhan Vong used during the Battle of Ebaq 9. When the fleet arrived, the group worked with the Battle Group of Yun-Q'aah to flank General Keyan Farlander's squadron. However, as the battle progressed, the numbers took their toll on the Yun-Txiin battle group. When Warmaster Tsavong Lah ordered the fleet to withdraw, the Yun-Txiin group and the Battle Group of Yun-Harla were the first to withdraw. However, they were also the first to encounter the minefield the New Republic had set up. The Battle Group of Yun-Txiin, already overtaxed due to the fighting at Ebaq 9, took massive casualties in the minefield. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way Category:Yuuzhan Vong fleets
