Dmaynel Kiph
2573

Dmaynel Kiph was a male Devaronian who lived in the Invisec area of the planet Coruscant. Kiph hated the Galactic Empire for its speciesist policies. Gathering a like-minded band of non-humans, Kiph formed the Alien Combine, an anti-Imperial, anti-Human band of Invisec dwellers. They carried out attacks against the Galactic Empire and, quick to judge Humans as bigoted, killed those they felt to be so and sent their bodies back as warnings to the Empire. One such potential victim was Rogue Squadron pilot Gavin Darklighter, but when Darklighter and his Rogue companions were brought before Kiph, the Empire raided the Alien Combine's headquarters.
