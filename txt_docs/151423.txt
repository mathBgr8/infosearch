Inflexible
2199

The Inflexible was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer. It was constructed at the Kuat Drive Yards shipyards on Kuat in 3 ABY. Shannon Voorson was able to slice its name along with several other Star Destroyers names and gave it to her cousin, Deen Voorson, a member of the Rebel Alliance. The Inflexible also hosted the Tir training school, a training center for TIE pilots. Flight Captain T.
