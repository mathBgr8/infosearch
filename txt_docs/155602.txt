Abel Quiller was an alias used on occasion by Talon Karrde. Dankin often acted as Quiller so as to keep Karrde from being recognized. Quiller was supposedly the impatient captain of the Hab Camber. *The Last Command Category:Aliases, nicknames, and pseudonyms
