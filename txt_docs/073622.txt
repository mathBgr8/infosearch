Caldera Righim
1680

Caldera Righim was a Talz. He resided on Tatooine, and was often spotted enjoying a drink at Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. He was often present during the night when Ackmena was the bartender. Like most Talz, Caldera was a pacifist.
