Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7
3129

Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7,The New Essential Guide to Droids also known as RMSU-7 (pronounced Rimsoo Seven), was a Republic Mobile Surgical Unit stationed on the jungle planet Drongar during the Clone Wars in 20 BBY. The Sea of Sponges was in between RMSU-3 and RMSU-7. It serviced the personnel of the Galactic Republic at the Battle of Drongar alongside other surgical units under the command of Colonel D'Arc Vaetes. It, with all the other Republic forces on the planet, left when the bota mutated and became useless. The medical base contained a cantina managed by the Ortolan, Baloob.
