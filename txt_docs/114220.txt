Garnak Fey
1034

Garnak Fey was the head of the Fey family and the comptroller of the Silica Tab production lines, which were the main source of income for the family-owned organization. He was also a major impact on the government of Utapau. Some time after the Battle of Yavin, Fey was captured by Silri of the Zann Consortium. *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Category:Business owners and executives Category:Humans Category:Males
