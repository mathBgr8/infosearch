Secret police (disambiguation)
743

Secret Police (also Political Police) can refer to: *Palpatine's Secret Police; a term used to describe the Jedi during the Clone Wars. *Imperial Security Bureau; the secret police of the Galactic Empire. *Galactic Alliance Guard; the secret police of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances created during the Second Galactic Civil War. *Xim's GenoHaradan guild of assassins. *K'ghaan, rumored secret police of Ssi-ruu serving the Shreeftut of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium and said to hold black-scaled Ssi-ruuk among its ranks.
