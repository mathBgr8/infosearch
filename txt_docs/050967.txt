Trianii (language)
731

Trianii was the native language of the Trianii species from Trian. Trianii language was formed of purrs and growls, rolling its vocalizations. Instead of an alphabet, Trianii used a syllabary.Galaxy at War Most Trianii were fluent in both Trianii language and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Han Solo at Stars' End *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Galaxy at War Category:Languages Category:Trianii culture
