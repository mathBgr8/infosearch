Kas air cruiser
1474

The Kas air cruiser was a model of atmospherical repulsorcraft employed by the Wookiees. thumb|left|Multiple views Although the vehicle moved and fired slowly, its firepower was immense. One shot could decimate a large area of enemy forces. With its longer than average range, it was able to bombard turrets without being fired upon in return. In well protected groups, several of these impressive ships could lay waste to whole enemy bases. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns Category:Atmospheric repulsorcraft models Category:Wookiee vehicle classes
