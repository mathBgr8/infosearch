Firing chamber
1289

The Firing chamber was the blaster component where the plasma energy was activated and intensified in the microseconds before the weapon was fired. Larger firing chambers allowed for wider energy beams that caused greater damage, so they were highly sought after in the customization of blaster weaponry.
