dates the events of the Star Wars Adventures series to a year before the First Battle of Geonosis, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 22 BBY. |series=Star Wars Adventures (Scholastic) |preceded by=Hunt the Sun Runner |followed by=The Hostage Princess }} Star Wars Adventures 2: The Cavern of Screaming Skulls was the second installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 2 in November 2002. After the fallout of the Sun Runner incident, the Jedi are stranded in the jungles of Nallastia, where hostile natives send them on a quest to recover sacred gems said to hold the key to an ancient prophecy.
