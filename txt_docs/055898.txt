|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=1 }} Classic Star Wars 3 is the third issue of the Classic Star Wars series, published by Dark Horse Comics. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson from the early 1980s. Williamson supplied new panels and art to flow the stories for traditional comic format. Classic Star Wars is set during the period between the first and second Star Wars films.
