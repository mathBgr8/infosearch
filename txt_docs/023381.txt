Fett homestead
1221

The Fett homestead was the home of the Fett family on Concord Dawn and the childhood home of Jango Fett. In 58 BBY, the Mandalorian Civil War spilled on to the property. Jango's father, the Journeyman Protector of Concord Dawn, hid Mandalore Jaster Mereel from the wrath of Death Watch leader Vizsla. In retaliation, Vizsla killed both adult Fetts and destroyed the farm.
