Sander de Lange
8245

Sander de Lange from the Netherlands wrote the backstory of Niai Fieso appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. His alias is exarxan on StarWars.com, and he was known as on Wookieepedia. He is a contributing editor of TeeKay-421, the Belgian Star Wars fan club. At Celebration Europe II he organized the Star Wars Trivia Challenge on Sunday as part of TeeKay-421.
