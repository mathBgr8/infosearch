Dark Sword
1222

Dark Sword was the name of a starfighter squadron made up of Jedi pilots flying StealthX attack fighters and led by Jedi Master Kenth Hamner. It was part of the Galactic Alliance First Fleet and one of the six squadrons compromising the StealthX wing commanded by Grand Master Luke Skywalker during the Second Galactic Civil War.Legacy of the Force: Inferno *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance starfighter squadrons Category:New Jedi Order organizations
