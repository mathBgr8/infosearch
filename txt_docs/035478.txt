Wehttam (group)
1068

The Wehttam were the inhabitants of the planet Wehttam.Before the Storm, Chapter 14. They spoke the language known as Wehttam.Shield of Lies, Chapter 1. Although the planet Wehttam plays a significant role in the Black Fleet Crisis series of novels, only Before the Storm mentions a group known as "the Wehttam." The context leaves unclear whether the term refers to a sentient species native to Wehttam or simply acts as a demonym for the inhabitants of the world.
