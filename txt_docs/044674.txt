Republic Senate rules
997

Republic Senate rules was a gameplay style of pazaak. It drew its name from the bureaucratic nature of the rules of the Galactic Republic's Senate. Under Republic Senate rules, the goal was simply to pass the time; nothing was bet, and no one came out on top. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Card games
