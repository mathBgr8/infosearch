Starflare
805

The Starflare was the space-yacht used by the Fel dynasty on Csilla in around 29 ABY. The Fels' daughter Wynssa enjoyed taking the Starflare helm, but she flew less often than she would have liked, due to the frequency with which her brother Cem took the ship out. The name Starflare commemorated Baroness Syal Antilles Fel's former stage-name, "Wynssa Starflare," but it is not known what the yacht's capabilities were, or even whether she was built to a Chiss or Human design. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee Category:Yachts
