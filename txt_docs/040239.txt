Fere (species)
1541

The Fere were a sentient species of humanoids native to the world of Feree. They were considered to be master shipbuilders of their time. The average Fere was a tall, ivory-skinned being with six fingers on each hand. Around 192 BBY, the entire species was wiped out by a planet-wide plague that struck their homeworld. *The Courtship of Princess Leia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Extinct sentient species Category:Outer Rim Territories sentient species
