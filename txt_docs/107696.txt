Peter McConnell
3037

Peter McConnell is a video game music composer who works for LucasArts. He has worked on video games, usually with other sound specialists like Michael Land and Clint Bajakian. Land, McConnell and Justin Graham are responsible for the evolution of the iMUSE system.Star Wars: Rebel Assault: The Official Insider's Guide He participated to the team of Star Wars: Rebel Assault with Land and Bajakian. Among other things he extracted the sound effects (laser blasts, R2-D2's whistles) from the Skywalker Sound database in order to be used into the game.
