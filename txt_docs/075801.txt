Kashyyyk bladestick
1394

A Kashyyyk bladestick was a polearm or spear used by the Wookiees of the planet Kashyyyk. The handle was made of wood and it had two long, thin blades on both ends. The blades were engraved. During the Galactic Civil War, one of these weapons was owned by the Wookiee Chatook and used by his son, Takook. *Star Wars Galaxies: Jump to Lightspeed Category:Wookiee weapons
