Storm Fleet
1994

The Storm Fleet was a secret CIS fleet made up of destroyers that was meant to conquer planets with their large amounts of firepower. The fleet consisted of at least twelve warships, their number indicated by the fact the fleet was occupying twelve docking different docking bays, bays 1211 through to 1222. They were disguised as freighters to avoid detection by Republic forces. Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi delayed the fleets first mission to Cyphar by destroying two ships at the Llon Nebulae. The remainder of the fleet pushed on only to be defeated in the Battle of Cyphar where nearly half of the remaining ships were lost to the reinforcing Republic fleet. *Labyrinth of Evil *The New Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems fleets
