Magno-neutralizer
535

A magno-neutralizer was a high-tech gadget which could be thrown at a small group of droids, exploding and somehow deactivating them. Llez once used a magno-neutralizer he had gotten from his father to deactivate a pair of droids attempting to imprison his hero, Reddjak. *Droids (1986) 3 Category:Grenades
