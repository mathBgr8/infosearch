Wall of Remembrance
1112

The Wall of Remembrance was a landmark located on the planet Almas. It was essentially a massive wall, similar to the Memory wall on Coruscant, that held the names of all the Jedi that were killed in battle. As part of a ceremony, after the name was engraved on the wall, any surviving students or friends of the slain Jedi were allowed to make a speech on the life of their comrade. During the Clone Wars, the number of names on the wall grew rapidly, which was a fact that alarmed the Jedi that studied at Almas Academy. A similar wall was located on Cularin.
