Zackery
1475

Zackery was a Human male and the head of Imperial Senate security. Before the Great Jedi Purge, Zackery was the head of the Senate security on Coruscant. He threw Keets Freely out of the Senate building on numerous occasions, and even found him in a Senator's office. When the Empire took over, Zackery remained head of security, and, once more, caught Keets in a Senate office, along with Curran Caladian.
