Sebairns
1366

Sebairns was a Human male pilot of the private ship of Admiral Erel Kersos who served the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. In 20 BBY, he served onboard the MedStar under the command of Admiral Erel Kersos over the planet Drongar.
