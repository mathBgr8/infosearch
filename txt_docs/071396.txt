Battle of Yavin (starship)
2330

The Battle of Yavin was an Imperial Customs Frigate which was captured by the New Republic. It was assigned to the Black Curs, a band of smugglers and outlaws led by Dirk Harkness. In 6 ABY, the Battle of Yavin crashed on Hensara III while fleeing the Imperial Strike-class cruiser Havoc. After the Havoc withdrew, Rogue Squadron and a CR90 corvette entered the Hensara system to rescue the ship's crew.
