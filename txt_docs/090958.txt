Lynne Hale
2114

}} Lynne Hale was Vice President of Publicity & Communications for Lucasfilm A thirty-five year veteran of the company, Hale helped to create and manage the return of the Star Wars saga to the public forefront, beginning with The Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition and continuing with the Star Wars prequel trilogy and the Star Wars: The Clone Wars television series. She retired at the end of 2021. Category:Other Lucasfilm employees
