Star Wars: Invasion of Theed Adventure Game
16036

|pages=96 |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Star Wars: Invasion of Theed Adventure Game" is a mini Roleplaying game that was released by Wizards of the Coast in 2000. The adventures involve both established and original characters, and is set during the Trade Federation's invasion and occupation of Theed, Naboo. It is a boxed set that includes a mini rulebook, adventure booklet, character sheets, character tokens and an exclusive Hasbro action figure of Rorworr. The game was meant to be a simplified introduction to the new Star Wars Roleplaying Game released for The Phantom Menace. Wizards of the Coast also released a number of supplementary adventures that used the simplified Adventure Game ruleset, characters, enemies, and map tokens: *Peril in the Ionosphere *The Predators
