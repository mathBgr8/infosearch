Kystes' Spine
903

thumb|right|250px|Kystes' Spine The Kystes' Spine was a mountain range on the planet Talus that bisected the southern continent Aximia. The range was named for Kystes, and the majority of Talus's Selonian population, including the Kurtuln Den, lived within the volcanic tunnels and caves beneath the mountains. The mountains were nearly impassable and were home to a number of deadly native creatures, and were prone to violent storms and groundquakes as well.
