|birth= |death= |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=Corf Binalie |affiliation=Spaarti Creations |masters= |apprentices= }} Pilester Binalie was a Human male who was the father of Corf Binalie, a friend of Jedi Master Jafer Torles, and the owner of Spaarti Creations on the planet Cartao during the Clone Wars, a conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. In 22 BBY, he resided in a mansion near Foulahn City and attempted to remain neutral throughout the war. However, the Confederacy of Independent Systems planned to use the factory for equipment and special purposes throughout the war. This led to a fierce battle when Jafer and the Republic arrived on the planet. Pilester and his son observed as a Republic ship destroyed the factory, blaming the Jedi and thus ending their friendship with Torles.
