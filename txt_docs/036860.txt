Gonrey
1425

Corporal Gonrey was an ISB agent on Imperial Center after to the Battle of Yavin. Along with Vypass and Streln, they helped design the YI-5 probe droid. Ysanne Isard called for the execution of the three when it was revealed that the probes, like her, would have blue and red eyes; however, she later decided to let it go, assuming it was an innocent mistake.
