Maxiti
764

Maxiti or Murk as he was called by some swoop bike gang members, was a Lieutenant in the Pembric Security Legionnaires in 19 ABY. During Talon Karrde's visit to the Kathol Republic, he was stopped by Maxiti who kept him from aiding Shada D'ukal in defeating a group of swoop bike gang members.
