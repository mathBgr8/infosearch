Unidentified Rebel Vanguard (Second Battle of Sullust)
1554

This unidentified Rebel Vanguard was a male human who was recruited by Commander Col Serra to join Renegade Squadron, the Rebel Alliances' new elite fighting unit. During his career as a Rebel he was trained and became a Rebel Vanguard. During the Second Battle of Sullust he was killed along with a Wookiee when IG-88 jumped out of a Sentinel-class landing craft and landed nearby.
