Battle of Nickel One
2009

The Battle of Nickel One was an early conflict of the Swarm War. A team of Jedi Knights were sent to Nickel One to prevent the Killiks and their tarhead and Joiner Verpine allies from staging a coup. Luke Skywalker, Mara Jade Skywalker, and Jacen Solo entered the system to find the Killik forces already landing on the asteroid. The Jedi received a distress call from the Verpine Hive Mother informing them she was under attack.
