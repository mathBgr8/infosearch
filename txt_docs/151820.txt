80-Vag
1559

80-Vag was the starship of Mugaari pirate Icanis Tsur. The ship, manned by a crew of thirty in addition to the captain, was destroyed in the Hoth asteroid field sometime after the Galactic Empire's rise to power. Its wreckage floated among the asteroids for years, often plundered by Squibs and other scavengers. Category:Mugaari starships
