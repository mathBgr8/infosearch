Arrestor was the name of two known starships throughout galactic history: *Arrestor, an Acclamator-class assault ship that fought for the Republic and later the Empire. *Arrestor, a Neutron Star-class bulk cruiser in the Imperial Navy. *Cantwell-class Arrestor Cruiser, a type of cruiser used by the Galactic Empire.
