Eleaor Propulsion
1460

Eleaor Propulsion was the manufacturer of the Twilight Sailer. It was founded by the Kashi Mer Dynasty noble Krispus Eleaor in the Kashi system and built luxury transports including ground vehicles and repulsorlifts. In c. 4000 BBY, it had plans to begin production on star yachts and other spacecraft. Craft constructed by Eleaor often featured rare animal skins, jewels and art obtained by scouts hired by the company. Sometime before 3951 BBY, Exchange leader G0-T0 commissioned the prototype Starscape-class yacht, Visionary, for himself, calculating that a stronger Eleaor Propulsion would aid in his efforts to stabilize the Republic.
