Yankirk Route
1089

The Yankirk Route was a hyperspace route located in the Outer Rim Territories. This hyperlane was one of several routes that intersected at Eriadu, in the Seswenna sector, along with the Hydian Way, the Rimma Trade Route, and the Lipsec Run.
