Saffa (species)
950

The Saffa were a sentient species that were responsible for the development of an important form of art, the Saffa paintings, after making contact with the Thennqora. The Saffa painting reached its height approximately 2219-1569 BBY. *Heir to the Empire Category:Mid Rim sentient species
