Q-6100
1620

The Q-6100 was a model of ground vehicle in the Q-series manufactured by Jadai Motors. Out of production since 28 BBY, during the decades that followed it became popular on backwater worlds throughout the galaxy. The body was made out of metal instead of synthetics which made it a very sturdy vehicle. The assassin Callandri owned one of these vehicles; it was usually kept within the cargo hold of her ship, the Nightcast. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Ground vehicle models Category:Jadai Motors products
