Perry
2282

Perry was an individual who served a term as one of the law-enforcing Peacekeepers of the Outbound Flight Colony, the government established by those who survived the Galactic Republic's survey ship Outbound Flight after its crash in a star cluster called the Redoubt. In 22 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion the Human Trilli mentioned to fellow crewman Jorad Pressor that Perry was assigned with Quinze to guard the Quarantine section, where the colony kept Force-sensitive children under surveillance. That year, Commander Chak Fel was investigating the ruins of Outbound Flight when he discovered the small prison. A female attendant watching over the children initially mistook him for Perry, but quickly realized he was someone else.
