Melisse

Melisse was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate which had a few squadrons of Y-wings under her command. The Melisse arrived at the System Patrol Squadron above Farfeld II and attacked the Imperial frigate Dark Eye and, with the help of Keyan Farlander in a B-wing, managed to destroy it. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
