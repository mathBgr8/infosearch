Farvakine
1690

}} Farvakine was a male Imperial officer and water merchant on the planet of Tatooine. At some point, Farvakine bought the Mon Calamari slave Sissalik, and treated him cruelly. Sissalik later killed him by poisoning his food with Chuba poison, which triggered a deadly stroke.
