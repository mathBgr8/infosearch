DD-19
1374

DD-19 was a masculine DD-19 "Overseer" labor pool droid assigned to the New Republic CR90 corvette FarStar. It was nicknamed "the slavemaster" due to its harsh treatment of other droids. DD-19 was primarily assigned to the droid repair bay on the ship, and had the authority to apply restraining bolts to unruly droids. *The DarkStryder Campaign *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:DD-19 "Overseer" labor pool droids Category:Droids of the New Republic Category:Droids with masculine programming Category:FarStar crewmembers and personnel
