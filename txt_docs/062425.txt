Beltway
1448

A beltway was a moving platform with seats.Han Solo and the Lost Legacy A beltway was a form of transportation used in densely crowded locations on highly urbanized planets. It provided a safe alternative to airspeeders and landspeeders. It was a slightly more upscale version of the standard slidewalk, which moved standing pedestrian traffic. A similar beltway existed on board Super Star Destroyers. It was an octagonal shaft that ran from stern to prow, and had a tracked hauler at the top, which allowed it to move heavy equipment.
