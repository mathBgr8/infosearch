Gorge of Fire
1091

The Gorge of Fire was a long, broad and deep canyon on the forest moon of Endor that separated the forests of the Ewoks from the land of Zandor. Enormous, never-ending flames stood up from its bottom along its entire length, making it impossible to cross unless one was in possession of the bridge crystal. Those that possessed the crystal could conjure the legendary Rainbow Bridge, which allowed passage across the Gorge of Fire. *Ewoks (1985) 1 Category:Canyons Category:Endor locations
