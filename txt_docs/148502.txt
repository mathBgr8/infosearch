Yag'Dhul space station
668

This space station, located over the planet of Yag'Dhul, was used as the base of the Yag'Dhul insurgents around 5 BBY. In that year, the Galactic Empire attacked the insurgents, and during the battle, the space station was destroyed. *Millennium Falcon Category:Orbital stations
