Ditwar Logas
767

Ditwar Logas was a sentientologist of some repute during the Imperial era. Obo Rin once claimed to have studied under Logas at the Three Rings University of Social Sciences. However, Imperial Intelligence Major Herrit claimed in a communiqué to Lord Darth Vader that Three Rings was not a genuine university, but a business that sold worthless diplomas via computer order. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Sentientologists
