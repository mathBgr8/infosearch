Starspin
608

The Starspin was a type of small starship that was manufactured by Kuat Drive Yards. A Starspin was used by the Black Sun operative Mathal during his ill-fated trip to meet with Tarnese Bleyd. The ship was presumably hyperdrive-equipped, however it was relatively small in size. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons Category:Kuat Drive Yards products Category:Starship classes
