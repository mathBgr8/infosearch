Yavin (starship)
795

The Yavin was a Star Cruiser commanded by General Wedge Antilles during the Battle of Almania. This powerful warship was the flagship of a task force sent to deal with Dark Jedi Kueller. Fighting alongside the Tatooine and Calamari, Wedge managed to defeat the trio of Victory II-class Star Destroyers and secure a victory for the New Republic. *The New Rebellion Category:Flagships Category:Starships of the New Republic
