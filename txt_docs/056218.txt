Uzbe'k
814

Uzbe'k was the name given to a group of Rebel Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports that carried Rebel personnel from the exploding Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Maximus. They were able to make it to the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Anvil. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
