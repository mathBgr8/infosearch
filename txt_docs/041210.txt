Alistar Dadefra was a Human male born in 57 BBY. He joined the Imperial Navy, rising to a command level position by 11 BBY. In 1 ABY, Dadefra was in command of the Victory-class Star Destroyer Bombard. Alongside the Crusader, Dadefra smashed the Eyttyrmin Batiiv pirates in the Khuiumin system. Several months later, he was assigned to the Elrood sector, commanding the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Brazen.
