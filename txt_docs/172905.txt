The term Survivors may refer to the following groups or organizations: *The Survivors, a group on Dellalt descended from the crew of Xim the Despot's treasure ship, the Queen of Ranroon. *The Survivors, one of two social factions in the Outbound Flight Colony. *The Khuiumin Survivors, a pirate organization. It may also refer to: *"Survivors", a comic that appeared in Star Wars Tales 13 and was collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4.
