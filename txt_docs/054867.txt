Since The Further Adventures: Droid World adapts issue 47 it must also take place in 3 ABY. |series=Star Wars: The Further Adventures |preceded by= |followed by=Planet of the Hoojibs }} The Further Adventures: Droid World was a book-and-record in the Star Wars: The Further Adventures series. It was adapted from Star Wars (1977) 47 by Jymn Magon as part of the Buena Vista Records' Read-Along Adventures series. It was produced by Ted Kryczko and released in 1983 as book with 7" 33 ⅓ rpm record and Cassette tape It is unknown who narrated the record as credits were not provided along with the book or record. When the Rebel Alliance captures a damaged new model of Imperial warbot on Xeron they decide to ask for assistance from the droid populated space station known as Kligson's Moon in repairing the droid so the Alliance technicians can study it. Kligson agrees to repair the droid, but R2-D2 and C-3PO must accompany the warbot to observe the operation since Kligson intends to keep the droid after it's repaired. However, another droid on Kligson's moon has other ideas as the former Imperial droid Zee-Exthree plans to use parts of the warbot to repair his own war droid and lead a revolt against Kligson. However Kligson had anticipated this and sent a decoy to lure Zee-Exthree and his followers out into the open when he would launch his own warbot against them.
