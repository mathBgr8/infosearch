Slopper
1016

Slopper was a Saurian who worked for Sithless Leethe. He was not very intelligent however, and was rather easy to dupe if someone were to attempt it. He was once assigned to accompany a group of Rebel hopefuls on board the Limping Lady when they delivered Imperial weapons to the Felinian natives of Zoma V.
