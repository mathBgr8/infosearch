Jedi enclave (Cularin)
1478

The Almas Academy maintained a presence on Cularin with a small Jedi enclave located in the northeast corner of Gadrin. It was a satellite facility for the Academy, not being at all like the ancient Jedi Enclaves. When E1-6RA recruited a group to look into the disappearances of people around Gadrin and Hedrett, she had them meet her there. With the start of the Clone Wars and a blockade by Jedi Master Darrus Jeht on the Almas Academy, the facility held the classes that could not take place on Almas. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cularin locations Category:Jedi enclaves
