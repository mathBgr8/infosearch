Kluistar
2464

Kluistar was a planet on the border of the Colonies region, and was considered a comfortable if undistinguished world. Although featuring several small cities and spaceports, the majority of Kluistar's surface was given over to vast tracts of unspoiled wilderness and woodland. These areas of natural beauty gave the planet a sense of primeval and mythic utopia, although despite being considered a paradise on the edge of civilization, the world was accessible via several major trade routes. The Ace of Sabres, owned by the Ace Entertainment Corporation and secretly run by Sibarra the Hutt, was one of the planet's major developments to intrude on the natural wilderness.
