Dama Whitesun Brunk

|children=new |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Dama Whitesun Brunk was a Human female from the planet Tatooine. She was the younger sister of Beru Whitesun Lars. Her brother was Haro Whitesun, and she may have had another sister called Coolie&mdash;though this may have been Luke Skywalker's nickname for Dama herself. Dama owned and operated the Sidi Driss Inn, a hotel in Anchorhead, during the early days of the New Republic. She assisted Leia Organa Solo in attempting to recover the painting Killik Twilight. After 0 BBY, Dama, like her brother, hated the Imperials for killing her sister and brother-in-law, Owen Lars. Those close to Dama considered her trustworthy.
