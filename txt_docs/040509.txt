Lorn Kabul
1498

}} Lorn Kabul, addressed as "Lord Kabul" and "Master Kabul", was the owner of a mining operation on Otunia and the father of Arista Kabul. A loving father, Lorn nevertheless thought Arista was too irresponsible and made her wear a tracking device on her ankle at all times. He was killed by his brother Seth Kabul who, scheming with the Imperial Moff Harsh wanted to take over the mining operation. As Lorn lay dying, he asked the unusually noble Gamorrean, Grissom, to protect Arista and help her make it off-planet. Category:Business owners and executives Category:Humans Category:Males
