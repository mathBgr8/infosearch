Randy Martinez
2333

|companies= |website= }} Randy Martinez is an artist. He drew the program cover for both Celebration IV and Celebration Europe. He did the covers for all books in the Star Wars: Rebel Force series and for the book Darth Vader: Sith Lord. He also worked on the art of several Star Wars pinball machine games, including one for Star Wars: The Mandalorian. *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Comic book cover artists Category:Cover artists
