Unified Factions
1217

The Unified Factions was the planetary government of the Kroctari species, lead by the Lord Superior. Although they initially resisted inclusion to the Galactic Empire, after joining the New Republic, the Unified Factions witnessed its disarray, and welcomed the chance to join the Imperial Remnant in 19 ABY. *Vision of the Future *Vision of the Future abridged audiobook Category:New Republic-aligned governments Category:Planetary governments
