|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=0.5 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Rebellion |issue=13 }} Rebellion 13 is the thirteenth issue in the Star Wars: Rebellion series of comics. With Luke Skywalker and Princess Leia captured by an ambitious Imperial officer, Admiral Ackbar considers sending a squad of new, experimental starfighters to save the Rebellion's greatest heroes from certain death! On an Imperial station, Luke and Leia do their best to distract their cruel captor from the rest of Rebels still loose in the facility. Unfortunately, Imperials have found Deena Shan, the Rebel team's last hope for success. Deena is cornered into a decision of disastrous proportions for herself and everyone around her. By the time Ackbar deems a rescue mission worth the risk, there may be no one left to save. *This new story is set in the months after Star Wars: A New Hope, starring Luke Skywalker and Princess Leia.
