Reactant agitator injector
1734

Reactant agitator injectors were components used on starfighters such as Incom Corporation's T-65 X-wing starfighter. Sarylcorp manufactured "closed loop" reactant agitator injectors that collected fission byproduct from the catalyst injected into the fighter's power converter.
