AEA-500 mineral extractor
1928

The House of Tagge Mining Company AEA-500 mineral extractor was produced during the Galactic Civil War. Twenty meters long, it carried one pilot, one dig operator, and was equipped with tri-beam lasers, which were used for mining. The craft could carry ten metric tons of ore and minerals that were gathered from planetoids. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology Category:House of Tagge Mining Company products Category:Mining starship classes
