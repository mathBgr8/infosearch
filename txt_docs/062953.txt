The station was chosen by the Azzameen family as a convenient place for a rendezvous between them and the scout craft Ravenno, representative of a smuggler dealing in black market bacta. However, the bacta sale was merely a ruse designed by the Loose Cannon pirates, who sought to entrap the Azzameens in an ambush at the cargo transfer area.
