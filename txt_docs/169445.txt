Wriggler
1704

Wrigglers were an amphibious species native to Drongar. The average size was about five meters long and half a meter thick, making them the largest of all known land-based lifeforms on that world. The creatures' cilia could deliver an electrostatic charge that was powerful enough to knock a grown Human off his feet. Like other Drongar lifeforms, they were invertebrates.
