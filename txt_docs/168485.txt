Ar'wa Nonshik
1050

Ar'wa Nonshik was a male Aramandi who worked for the Antar Band, a criminal organization active in the space around the Sisar Run. Nonshik was a bodyguard until his employer was killed by the Gotal Ujin Voli. Voli was impressed by Nonshik and offered him a job. Nonshik accepted the offer and began working as a mercenary for the Antar Band. *Secrets of the Sisar Run Category:Aramandi Category:Males Category:Mercenaries
