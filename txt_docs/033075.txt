Nilko Bwaas
1807

Nilko Bwaas was a male Selkath who lived during the Jedi Civil War. He was a member of Manaan's government, and hired Revan to investigate why the Republic Embassy was hiring so many mercenaries. He offered Revan five hundred credits in recovering any vital information for him.
