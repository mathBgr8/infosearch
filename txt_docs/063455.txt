Hover Transport TC9
3019

The Loratus Hover Transport TC9 was a cargo vehicle. left|202x202px|thumb|A common mercenary model. The TC9 was manufactured by Loratus Manufacturing and was a hover vehicle capable of fast speeds. This armored troop transport had front-mounted anti-armor blasters, and could carry up to six troopers. It was used by the Rebel Alliance during the early stages of the Galactic Civil War and a group of smugglers and mercenaries affiliated with the Disciples of Ragnos, later in the war. The transports were later used by the New Republic to speedily evacuate friendly-personnel, such as escaped prisoners, from enemy bases. They were deployed to evacuate the revolting prisoners and Kyle Katarn during the Battle of Artus Prime. left|200x200px|thumb|Landed freelance TC9s in [[14 ABY/Legends|14 ABY.]] On a mission to Mos Eisley in 14 ABY, Kyle Katarn and Jaden Korr discovered a couple of TC9s.
