Jedi on the Roof
1164

thumb|300px|Signs for Jedi on the Roof and other shows outside the Galaxies Opera House Jedi on the Roof was a musical playing at the Galaxies Opera House in 19 BBY. A sign advertising the show was displayed outside of the opera house during a performance of Squid Lake attended by Palpatine and Anakin Skywalker.
