Tiss'sharl League
1826

thumb|right|300px|The Tiss'sharl League meets with [[Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Lord Vader.]] The Tiss'sharl League was the governing body of Tiss'sharl. A consortium of the Tiss'shar leaders of the five companies that reported the highest profits in the previous five local years,Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies it consisted of a President and his Vice President along with several League Councilors and Officers. It had been established at least since 1000 BBY.Empire 31 Around eight months after the Battle of Yavin, Si-Di-Ri held the position of President of the Tiss'sharl League, while Geor-Dan-Thi was Vice-President, having rapidly climbed to that position following the suspicious deaths of Mayor Antha-Kres, Secretary Lai-O-Sid, and Vice President Astre-De-Kay. Following an visit by Lord Darth Vader and an assassination attempt, Si-Di-Ri resigned, leaving the position open for the ambitious Geor-Dan-Thi.
