Light amplification goggles
1427

250px|thumb|A pair of the goggles Light amplification goggles, also known as infra-goggles, were a type of goggles similar to macrobinoculars. The difference is that these goggles could filter and balance levels of light, allowing the user to see in pitch black areas, though the vision was cast in a greenish hue. The infra-goggles required batteries to function, which drained at a rate comparative to Electrobinoculars.
