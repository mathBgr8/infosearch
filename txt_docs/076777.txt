Integrity (Venator-class)
3864

The Integrity was a Venator-class Star Destroyer that had been assigned to the Open Circle Fleet as part of the Galactic Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. The Integrity was a Republic Navy Venator-class Star Destroyer that was colored light gray with red and yellow Open Circle Fleet emblems painted on either side of dorsal bay doors that were painted a darker gray. While most Venator-class Star Destroyers were marked symmetrically, the Integrity port wing was uniquely painted with a larger maroon delta shape than the one painted on the starboard wing. During the final days of the war, it served as flagship of Generals Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi, leading the Republic charge at the Battle of Belderone and the recapture of Tythe. The warship and the rest of the Open Circle Fleet came to the aid of Supreme Chancellor Palpatine in the Battle of Coruscant. On June 19, 2007, the Integrity was first represented as a miniature in the premier release of WizKids's Star Wars PocketModel TCG.
