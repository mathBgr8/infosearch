Eclipse (sail barge)
2187

The Eclipse was a model of sail barge manufactured by Gefferon Pleasure Craft. It was unarmed and smaller than the Ubrikkian Industries Luxury sail barge, but the interior was considered to be more luxurious for its smaller quantity of passengers, and add-on weapons were also produced. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition *Star Wars Gamemaster Screen, Revised *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded Category:Gefferon Pleasure Craft products Category:Sail barge models
