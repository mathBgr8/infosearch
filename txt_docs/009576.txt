Drew Campbell
536

Drew Campbell wrote Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids with Eric S. Trautmann, and is one of the authors of Cracken's Threat Dossier. He was also the co-writer of the article Cynabar's Droid Datalog, which appeared in Star Wars Adventure Journal 14, and Cynabar's Droid Datalog Addendum for the previous issue of Star Wars Adventure Journal.
