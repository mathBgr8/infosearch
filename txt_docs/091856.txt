Raiders associated with the Confederacy of Independent Systems had been attacking Republic convoys along the Corellian Trade Spine, including five which hailed directly from Coruscant. The raiders' tactic was to use a gravity well projector to pull the ships from hyperspace and into an ambush for which the freighters were unprepared.
