Bollin
1237

Bollin was the oldest platform city on Cularin, and its age showed in its aging technology, low population, and dilapidated buildings. Most of its business revolved around exporting Cularin's exotic woods. Bollin had the best relationship with the Tarasin of any of the platform cities. Indeed, its baron administrator as of 31 BBY was a Tarasin named Ta'Shal.
