|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation=Cadinth Oligarchy }} Saskapeg was the capital city of the Mid Rim world Cadinth and its government, the Cadinth Oligarchy. In 22 BBY, is set on the date "13:5:9," which corresponds to 22 BBY per the reasoning here. when the Galactic Republic was clashing with a militant separatist faction,The New Essential Chronology the Oligarchy took the Republic's side. That changed when a pro-Separatist uprising occurred in Saskapeg, leading to the death of Jedi Knight Bodis-Ker Vitan, the planet's Watchman. The incident prompted the government to withdraw the world's support of the Republic, with Prime Oligarch Rankwin Fopow later recalling those statements and declaring it was "firmly undecided" between the groups. Saskapeg was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #55, an issue of the HoloNet News website that was published on May 9, 2002.
