He, and therefore Cadinth, supported the Galactic Republic during its conflict with the Confederacy of Independent Systems.The New Essential Chronology After the death of the planet's Jedi Watchman, Bodis-Ker Vitan, during a pro-Separatist uprising in Cadinth's capital, the Oligarchy retracted its endorsement of the Republic. Fopow later withdrew all statements, instead declaring that the planet was "firmly undecided" between the two groups. His statement was included in an issue of the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. Rankwin Fopow was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #55, a May 9, 2002 issue of the HoloNet News website. The article that references him, entitled "Cadinth Loyalty Uncertain After Jedi Death," was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
