Leemoth
1333

Leemoth was a Duros male surgeon who served the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars on Drongar. He specialized in amphibious and semi-aquatic species. He survived the attack of the Separatist forces against the medical center, and the RMSU was moved to a new location.
