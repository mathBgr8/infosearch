TIE-wing
2090

The TIE-wing was a type of Rebel ship. Appearing only in the new timeline introduced by the non-canon LEGO Star Wars: Rebuild the Galaxy, the TIE-wing has a main body like a canon X-wing's and wings like a canon TIE fighter's. TIE-wings resemble X-TIE Fighters from Legends, but Rebuild the Galaxy implies that TIE-wings, unlike X-TIEs, are a standard design in the new timeline rather than Uglies made from salvaged parts.
