Adascopolis Financial Exchange
1717

The Adascopolis Financial Exchange was a financial service operated by the Adasca BioMechanical Corporation of Arkania during the time of the Old Sith Wars. An ancient financial service in the city of Adascopolis, the capital of Arkania, the Financial Exchange was run by the Adasca BioMechanical Corporation, a megacorp owned and operated by Arkania's very own House of Adasca. The Adasca family had a leading influence in the the Arkanian Dominion and continued in their dominance in the wake of the Great Sith War.
