Great Downrush
632

The Great Downrush was the largest river on Haruun Kal. It was fed by several tributaries, including the Grandmother's Tears and a stream originating at the Trundur Wall. The river was a kilometer wide when it passed Pelek Baw, and at the south of the city it went into the Downrush Falls. *Shatterpoint *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Haruun Kal locations Category:Rivers
