Challenge 47

Challenge 47 was a roleplaying magazine from December 1990/January 1991. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "The Rebel Air Force: Combat Airspeeders" and was written by Whitney Weston. Category:Challenge
