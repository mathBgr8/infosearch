Lunkar An
2314

Lunkar An was a male Rellarin scout and a veteran of the Galactic Outdoor Survival School. Powerfully-built, An was noticeable because of his thick dark brown skin, his small black eyes and his oversized hands. As a member of the Summit Class, An was successful in the final trek used as a test in the GOSS. He did the trek in 129.1 hours, holding a new record. Proud of his own success, he left GOSS to create his own Jmin Survival Academy, of which he was an instructor. Seven years later, his record was broken by the Twilight Class, by more than one day. An discovered this fact and returned to GOSS as a student, wanting to "redeem" himself. An has since been a personal nemesis of Twilight Class veteran Sisquoc, and has also expressed a personal hatred towards another Twilight veteran Ma'w'shiye.
