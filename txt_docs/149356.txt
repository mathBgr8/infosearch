thumb|right|upright|[[Teebo/Legends|Teebo discovered the blue pearl under the surface of Sunshine Lake.]] The blue pearl was a pearl found by the Ewok Teebo in 3 ABY under the surface of Sunshine Lake on the Forest Moon of Endor. He found the pearl inside a sun shell. When he gave the pearl to the an amphibious sentient species, it replaced the lost red pearl and helped grow the food pearls they relied upon for sustenance. *Ewoks (1985) 9 Category:Pearls
