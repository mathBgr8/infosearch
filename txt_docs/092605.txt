Palisade
1191

|continent= |location= |climate= |interest=Palisade Starport Control |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} A small coastal town on Kabal, Palisade was unaffected by the Imperial bombardment around 0 ABY. The town was the location of eccentric businessman and big-game hunter Tyro Viveca's estate. Spiraling Shape touched down at Docking Pad P13 while Cecil Noone met with Viveca to offload a stolen Hapan Gun of Command. While Noone did this, his cohorts circulated through the town, trying to scavenge needed supplies. At one shop, the Tynnan demolitions expert Dawson found three Quay novelty toys, much to his delight, and the chagrin of partner Kels Turkhorn. Category:Kabal locations
