Hron
1027

Hron was an Abyssin who played as the star shock-back for the Rampa Rancors. Prior to a match between the Rancors and the Dargul Dianogas at the Innerdome Arena of Nwarcol Point, he refused to throw the game for the Black Sun Vigo Sprax.
