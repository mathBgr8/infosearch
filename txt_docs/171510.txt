Alinda Solaris
1734

*New Republic Fleet Intelligence |masters= |apprentices= }} Alinda Solaris was a New Republic Sector Naval Intelligence Officer. Solaris held the rank of Commander of Naval Intelligence in Vilonis sector following the Battle of Endor. Commander Solaris was responsible for giving the briefing to determine the New Republic's response to the Corporate Sector Authority and Galactic Empire's attempt to seize the Kalinda system.
