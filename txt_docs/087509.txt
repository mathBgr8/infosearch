Hilltop Emperor
1060

Hilltop Emperor was a child's game. The goal of the game was to reach the top of a structure, becoming the "Emperor". In 22 ABY,The events of Survivor's Quest are dated to 22 ABY by The Essential Reader's Companion while aboard the Chaf Envoy, Luke Skywalker and Mara Jade Skywalker witnessed a version of the game being played in the background of a transmission from the Geroon Remnant's vessel, with Mara noting the inclusion of a red headband that the winner would wave over their head in victory.Survivor's Quest *Survivor's Quest Category:Games
