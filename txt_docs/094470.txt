thumb|A [[Swokes Swokes/Legends|Swokes Swokes riding a Schinga Shikou.]] Schingas were large, predatory, serpents native to Makem Te. There were several subspecies of these serpents, the most famous and prized of which was the 'serpent-stallion' schinga shikou. These serpents were used as mounts by the Swokes Swokes.
