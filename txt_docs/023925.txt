Ceremony of Waters
820

The Ceremony of Waters was an Alderaanian tradition practiced by the Dro and Thul families. It was a ceremony of peace, so all invited to attend had to leave any and all weapons behind. The ceremony began with one person humming a tune, then placing their hand into a fountain and letting the water run over it, with the others joining in.
