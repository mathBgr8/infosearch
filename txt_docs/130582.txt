Jabiimi Nationalists
2047

The Jabiimi Nationalists were the faction of Jabiimi under Alto Stratus who took control of Jabiim at the start of the Clone Wars. They joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems and were opposed by the Jabiimi Loyalists. Their command center was located first in Razor Coast, and then in Hyber Canyon. Their military arm was the Nationalist Army. After Stratus' death in the Battle of Jabiim, the Nationalists fell under the control of Thorne Kraym.
