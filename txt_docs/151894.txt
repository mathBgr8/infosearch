Byll Shotaleve
2395

}} Byll Shotaleve was a resort owner on the popular vacation world Amfar. When the number of tourists dropped steeply in 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:4:18, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. several beaches on Amfar were reclaimed by redfish. In an interview published in the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News, Shotaleve expressed their discontent with having to cancel half their planned activities due to lack of attendance, as well as the return of redfish where resort-goers normally stayed. Byll Shotaleve appeared in "Leisure Industry Suffers Record Losses," an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #52 on the HoloNet News website on April 18, 2002.
