GA Series Information Analysis Unit
896

The GA information analysis droid was created by Cybot Galactica for peaceful purposes. Scientific- and business-oriented users were often impressed with the speed of the unit. The model was later upgraded with expanded memory and a new analysis software package in order to be used in a military capacity. The GA series was eventually surpassed by the GY-I information analysis droid. *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook Category:Analysis droid models Category:Cybot Galactica products
