Hejaran family
950

The Hejaran family were a very wealthy noble family of House Mecetti who owned a moon of Pella called Nightsinger's Orb located in Mecetti Province of the Tapani sector. They were once considered great hunters and a popular family tradition was to hunt the nightsinger creature. *Baron Lucian Hejaran, brother of Themion, cousin of Brigta, nephew of Galemus *Lady Brigta Hejaran, cousin of Lucian and Themion, niece of Galemus *Themion Hejaran, brother of Lucian, cousin of Brigta, nephew of Galemus *Galemus Hejaran, uncle of Lucian, Themion, and Brigta *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Families Category:Noble houses
