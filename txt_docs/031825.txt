captain |crewmembers=*Kirtan Loor *Mar Barezz |registry= |aliases= }} The Aggressor was an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer of the Galactic Empire. Early in Galactic Civil War, the Aggressor transported the Imperial Security Bureau agent Mar Barezz. Following the death of Gil Bastra in 6.5 ABY, it transported Kirtan Loor to Coruscant.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia The novel X-Wing: Rogue Squadron describes Loor as being onboard a shuttle attached to the SSD Aggressor. This is an error, seeing as this Aggressor had already been destroyed at that point.
