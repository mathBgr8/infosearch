Heavy combat suit
1166

thumb|120px|[[Carth Onasi wearing a heavy combat suit.]] The heavy combat suit was a type of armor that offered more protection than the basic model of combat suit. The heavy combat suit was heavier overall than other combat suits, and not quite as flexible, but many considered the trade-offs worthwhile. It was a good source of defense from physical attacks and light blaster fire.
