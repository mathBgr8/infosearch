Tangle gun
1522

Tangle guns were nonlethal capture devices that were produced by Merr-Sonn Munitions and Salus Corporation for crowd control. These weapons fired plastic webbing that was used to incapacitate a target; in this regard they were similar to Stokhli spray sticks, though without the secondary gas dispenser. *Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil *Rebel Dawn *Vision of the Future *Cracken's Rebel Field Guide *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Heroes & Rogues *Secrets of the Sisar Run *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Far Horizons Category:Crowd control weapons Category:Net and cord launchers
