Delphine (cargo ferry)
1829

Delphine was a cargo ferry used by Dimok forces during the Sepan Civil War. Delphine aided other Dimok starships in raiding a Ripoblus supply convoy in the beginning of the Imperial Sepan campaign. Intending to drive off the Dimok raiders, Imperial TIE starfighters destroyed the Cargo Ferry Mangonel, among numerous Y-wings, forcing Delphine to retreat to hyperspace. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Cargo ferries Category:Starships of the Dimok government
