|cover artist=Steve Chorney |illustrator= |editor= |publisher=Skylark |early release= |release date=August 10, 1998 |intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Paperback |pages=128 |isbn=9780553486537 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline=4 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion dates , which this book adapts, to 4 ABY |series=Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure |preceded by=The Empire Strikes Back |followed by= }} Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: Return of the Jedi trilogy, is a young reader novel by Christopher Golden, published by Skylark on August 10, 1998. It is written from the second person point of view with the reader taking on the role of Luke Skywalker's best friend, the protagonist of the story. The book features a number of points in which the reader can influence the story by deciding which page to turn to, resulting in multiple storylines and endings. The fate of the galaxy is in your hands — May the Force be with you. As with the last book, although it allowed for multiple different endings, this book continues as if all the events from A New Hope and The Empire Strikes Back took place. You are Luke Skywalker's best friend, and you and Lando Calrissian have infiltrated Jabba the Hutt's palace as guards as Luke puts his plan into action to rescue Han Solo from Jabba's clutches.
