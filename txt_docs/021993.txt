Alliance Intelligence Reports
47518

Alliance Intelligence Reports is a 95-page paperback supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. It was written by Bill Smith and published June 1995. It was set two years after the Battle of Yavin. Alliance Intelligence Reports is presented as a compendium of "datafiles" about non-Imperial threats to the Alliance to Restore the Republic. It is divided into the following chapters: *Introduction about how to use the book. *The Task Force on Alliance Security, including information on some of the Rebel characters who had written the datafiles.
