Omega (ATR-6)
1294

thumb|right|250px|[[Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transport|Assault Transports Omega assaulting XQ2 Platform Pondut.]] The Imperial Navy employed various Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports under the callsign Omega during the Galactic Civil War. Assault Transports Omega typically performed capture and assault operations on enemy space stations, such as the assault on XQ2 Platform Pondut during the "Arms Race."
