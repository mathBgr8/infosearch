Tundan
2876

Tundan was the language spoken by the Sorcerers of Tund, a group of Sith-blooded Force users from the verdant Force nexus of Tund. Though the origins of the Sorcerers could be traced to the Kissai outcasts who interpreted the death of Sith King Adas during the 27,700 BBY invasion of KorribanThe Essential Atlas as a sign to reject the dark side, Tundan was considered distinct from the Sith language by the time of the Great Sith War.Despite the Sith origins of the Sorcerers of Tund, the Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide and Karnak Tetsu's Web Enhancement entry list Darth Traya and Karnak Tetsu&mdash;both of whom lived during the Great Sith War&mdash;as fluent in both Sith and Tundan, implying some degree of linguistic differentiation between the languages. Known speakers of the Tundan language with some degree of fluency included the Sith Offshoot Karnak Tetsu, the Croke Rokur Gepta,The Dark Side Sourcebook and the Sith Triumvir Darth Traya.Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Additionally, a Tundan version of Asenec's poem "The Gleam of Kiirium" was used as the source material for the Galactic Basic Standard translation produced by Kiekal Zzoh. *The Dark Side Sourcebook *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:Languages
