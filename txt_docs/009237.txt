Ariana
1691

Ariana was the capital city of Garos IV. It boasted the University of Garos, The Violet Viska, and Chado's Pub. To the south lay the Tahika Cliffs and a mining base at the foot of Mount Usca, to which it was connected by the Currahen Crossroad.
