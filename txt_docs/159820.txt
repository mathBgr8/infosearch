Baka rock
1417

Baka rock was a type of music invented by Gamorreans. Apart from Gamorrean expatriate communities, the genre mostly failed to find a significant appreciative audience across the galaxy, Baka rock can be heard in LEGO Star Wars II: The Original Trilogy as well as LEGO Star Wars: The Complete Saga. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *LEGO Star Wars II: The Original Trilogy *LEGO Star Wars: The Complete Saga Category:Gamorrean music Category:Music genres
