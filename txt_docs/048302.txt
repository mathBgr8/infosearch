Feeorin (language)
1060

Feeorin was the native language of the Feeorin species, although most Feeorin also learned Galactic Basic Standard. The word "Revanchism" was Jurmaarz in Feeorin. Also, Ha'aruk ta was a formal greeting used among the ancient Feeorin people, used when first meeting offworld dignitaries. *Knights of the Old Republic 29 *"The Betrayal of Darth Revan" *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Clone Wars Campaign Guide *Legacy Era Campaign Guide Category:Languages
