Imperial Department of Redesign
1768

The Imperial Department of Redesign, also known as the Imperial Redesign Team, was an elite, highly secretive corps of the Galactic Empire, tasked with subduing or liquidating troublesome non-Human species, which was to be achieved through the secret project "The Heart of Steel." Grand Moff 4-8C, an IG-series assassin droid, was one of their number.
