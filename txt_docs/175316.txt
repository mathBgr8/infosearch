Korkeal Hai
2375

Korkeal Hai was a hyperkinetic smuggler who loved tinkering with her freighter, the modified Gymsnor-3 freighter called the Riff-Raff. She spent her early years in the Shesharile system constructing a light freighter from spare parts with the help of an old high-grade computer from the Clone Wars. She installed the computer in her ship, and it constantly nagged her about her ship's disarray. Korkeal was a slender Human woman, with failing eyesight that she corrected with archaic spectacles instead of corrective medicine.
