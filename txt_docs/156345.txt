Council of Ithorian Elders
848

The Council of Ithorian Elders was the ruling body of Ithor. Momaw Nadon was tried before the Council after surrendering their agricultural secrets to the Galactic Empire. Following the Yuuzhan Vong War the council was reconvened by surviving Ithorians that were abroad during the species' homeworld's destruction. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope, Second Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ithorian culture Category:Legislatures
