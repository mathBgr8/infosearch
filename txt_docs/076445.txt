Verpine bond gauntlets
722

Designed to mimic the exoskeleton of its creators, the Verpine, Verpine Bond Gauntlets were formed from tensor mesh materials which stiffened the forearm of the user. They functioned similarly to the stabilizer gauntlets, allowing the user to safely handle volatile objects. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Gloves
