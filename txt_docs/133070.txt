Nek (resistance)
971

Nek, taking the name from the ferocious battle-dog, was the name of a resistance member on Samaria. She tried to help Ferus Olin by finding information about bribing ministers of Samaria by Bog Divinian. Nek, like her friends, was unsuccessful in doing so. *The Last of the Jedi: Return of the Dark Side *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Resistance fighters
