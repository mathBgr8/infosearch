Goliath (droid)
1293

The Goliath was a monstrous battle droid owned by the Yahk-Tosh Lord Gar-Oth. The droid was remotely controlled by telepathic commands from Gar-Oth only. When newly knighted Jedi Yoshi Raph-Elan attempted to break up the forced wedding between Gar-Oth and Princess Lourdes, Gar-Oth sent the Goliath after him, but Raph-Elan was able to disable it. *Star Wars (1998) 27 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Scavenger's Guide to Droids Category:Battle droids Category:Droids with masculine programming
