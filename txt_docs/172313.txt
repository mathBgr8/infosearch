Greppem Freight
727

Greppem Freight was a transport and supply company that supplied the Liberty Task Force after the Rebel Alliance arranged a contract with Dunari. Craft of that company are seen in mission 5:2 of the game, if the player identifies the craft escorting the Liberty, before proceeding to the hyperspace and the main objective of the mission. They exist purely to add realism and consistency to the game (indeed the previous mission 5:1 concerned the arrangement of supplying procedures between the Alliance and Dunari) *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Transport companies
