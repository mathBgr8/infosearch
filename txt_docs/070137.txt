Duros ale
944

Duros ale was a type of ale available in the galaxy. It was served aboard the luxury repulsortrain Gem of Alderaan and at the annual Vor-cal Feast held aboard House Melantha Orbital Hunting Lodge over the planet Vycinyth. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Ales
