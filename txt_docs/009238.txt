Zila
630

Zila was a resort town on the shore of the Locura Ocean on the planet of Garos IV. Counselor Baro, along with many other high ranking Imperial officials stationed on the planet lived in Zila. Mount Berin was located on the outskirts of the city. *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons Category:Cities on Garos IV
