Junction City cantina
1031

The Junction City cantina of Feriae Junction was a rough place where death was no great novelty. However, in 0 ABY, Beilert Valance killed Marko Tyne so savagely even the normally desensitized regulars were shocked. Valance had been making a habit of capturing patrons with bounties on their heads, so much that it was impacting business. *Star Wars (1977) 27 Category:Cantinas Category:Feriae Junction locations
