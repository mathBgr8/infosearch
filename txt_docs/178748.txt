Temple city
1213

thumb|right|250px|The [[Battle of Aridka|battle on the outskirts of the city.]] }} The temple city was a city on the planet Aridka. The city was surrounded by cliffs, upon which stood the ruins of temples and obelisks. Around 21.5 BBY, nine Galactic Republic clone troopers made their last stand against the Separatist Droid Army on the outskirts of the city. The clones erected a deflector shield at their final position to defend a bridge leading directly into the city. Upon the arrival of Jedi Master Plo Koon, the troops managed to defeat the Separatists and save the city. Category:Cities
