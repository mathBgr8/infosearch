Kiam
1756

Kiam was a group of five BFF-1 bulk freighters used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. After the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Intrepid's hyperdrive was damaged in an accidental collision with an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate, leaving it stranded in the Circarpous system, Kiam group was sent to deliver replacement parts. The Rebel Alliance sent an X-wing, flown by Keyan Farlander, escorted by two R-22 Spearheads, to prevent the supplies being delivered. Despite the Intrepid launching TIE starfighters against the attacking Rebels, Farlander, under orders to destroy the freighters carrying the hyperdrive parts, eliminated Kiam III with proton torpedoes and Kiam V with his laser cannon, before leaving.
