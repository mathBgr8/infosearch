Mecht
1581

Mecht was a middle-aged Human male soldier who served as a member of Red Hand Squadron during the Galactic Civil War. Prior to his service in the Rebellion, he was a slave of the Galactic Empire until he was freed. He participated in the capture of Helot's Shackle.
