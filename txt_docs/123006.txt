Sigil (disambiguation)
661

Sigil could refer to several things: *Sigil, a planet. *Sigil system, a star system within the Chorlian sector containing the planet Sigil. *Sigil (crystal), a lightsaber crystal from the Sigil system. *Sigil, the name of a type of starfighter used by Nym's pirates. *Sigil (Sisterhood), a member of the Sisterhood of the Glorious Radiance.
