Drago
2167

Drago was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. After some Rebel pilots, including Samuel Raider and Toalagar, escaped from Stars' End prison aboard the BFF-1 bulk freighter Genue, the Drago was sent to retrieve them near Kashyyyk.
