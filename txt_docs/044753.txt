White Wings of Hope
1206

The White Wings of Hope were a sacred Ewok item that once belonged to an ancient Ewok warrior. They comprised a headband with tufts of white feathers on each side. Around 3 BBY, prior to the journey to locate and rescue the parents of the shipwrecked Humans Mace and Cindel Towani from a Gorax, the journey's caravan leader&mdash;Deej Warrick&mdash;was presented the sacred item by the Ewok medicine man Logray. *Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe Category:Sacred Ewok items
