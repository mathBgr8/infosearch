When the Domino Falls
6146

"When the Domino Falls" is a short story written by Patricia A. Jackson that was originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 3 by West End Games in August 1994 and was later archived online at Hyperspace on March 4, 2005. Drake Paulsen and his smuggling colleagues break out of an Imperial prison cell on Omman. Returning to Socorro, Drake hears the news that his father, Kaine, died while on a smuggling run to Thrugii.
