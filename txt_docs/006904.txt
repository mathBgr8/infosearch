|pages=22 |upc= |timeline=*33 BBYThe New Essential Chronology *88 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Republic - Prelude to Rebellion (6 of 6) & Vow of Justice (3 of 3) |issue=6 }} Star Wars 6 is the sixth issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on May 19, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics As Ki races to save his daughter from the clutches of the vile gangster Jabba the Hutt, he inadvertently discovers that the Hutt crime lord is secretly procuring munitions for a war fleet massing in orbit above Tatooine. Suddenly, Ki's problems on his homeworld of Cerea seem very small indeed! As Jabba's fighters scramble to intercept Ki-Adi-Mundi, his daughter and her companion wait, terrified, for their rescue. Ki uses a clever feint to ditch his ship and go after the girls just as Jabba launches a shuttle full of contraband bound for a Trade Federation rendezvous. After being reunited with his daughter, Ki returns to Cerea, only to find his formerly infertile honor-wife pregnant. This arc closes with the appointment of Ki-Adi-Mundi to the Jedi Council.
