Father Tree
1092

thumb|200px|The Father Tree The Father Tree was the tallest tree on the forest moon of Endor and was worshiped as a deity by the Ewoks. The tree was protected by the Grudakk, a large creature who lived in a burrow under its roots. Logray, shaman of Bright Tree Village, possessed a branch from the Father Tree that he used to call upon the spirit of the Father Tree. *The Adventures of Teebo: A Tale of Magic and Suspense Category:Ewok deities Category:Individual trees Category:Plants of Endor
