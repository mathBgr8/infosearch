Blood Prince of Thassalia
1215

The Blood Prince of Thassalia was a monarchical title that shared its name with the Thassalia system of the Outer Rim Territories' Albanin sector. The title of the Blood Prince of Thassalia was mentioned in Shield of Lies, Michael P. Kube-McDowell's 1996 first entry in The Black Fleet Crisis trilogy of novels. *Shield of Lies Category:Titles of monarchs
