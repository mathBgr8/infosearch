Iridik'k-stallu
1669

Iridik'k-stallu was a female Senator of the Galactic Republic in 19 BBY. She had multiple hearts, and supported Bail Organa's and Mon Mothma's efforts to limit Supreme Chancellor Palpatine's powers. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Galactic Republic senators Category:Individuals of unidentified species
