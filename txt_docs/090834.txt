Jevax
1307

Jevax was a male Mluki and the Chief Person of Plett's Well on Belsavis in 12 ABY. Jevax was small for his species, although standing up straight instead of with his back inclined would have made him taller, and wore jewelry such as silver and blue shellwork imported from Eriadu.
