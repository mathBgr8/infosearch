Akaga
3105

Akaga was an Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate under command of Admiral Harkov during the Galactic Civil War. After the Battle of Hoth when Admiral Harkov moved against the Empire, Akaga defected from the Imperial Navy to the Alliance to Restore the Republic. When Lord Vader later tracked Harkov's forces down in the Ottega system, he sent the Interdictor Harpax to trap the traitorous Admiral's former flagship Protector, prompting a frenzied retaliation by both the Akaga and the Rebel Calamari Cruiser Warhawk.
