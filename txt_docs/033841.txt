Reliance I
4531

The Reliance I was a Republic command ship used by the Galactic Republic around 4000 BBY. It served as the flagship of Ulic Qel-Droma during the opening stages of the Great Sith War. The Reliance I was equipped with turbolasers and shield generators and a specialized bridge atmospheric containment field. The vessel was equipped with a shipboard infirmary, complete with bacta tanks and medical droids. Under the command of Fleet Captain Orley Vanicus, she was damaged by an assault wave of Chaos fighters at Koros Major during the Battle of Koros Major, when swarms of the small attack starfighters and Krath CX-133 Chaos fighter were used in kamikaze strikes against the Republic fleet and one such craft crashed into the Reliance I bridge viewports. Although the hole was quickly sealed off by an atmospheric force-field and the burning wreckage was cooled quickly, there were several casualties among the command crew.
