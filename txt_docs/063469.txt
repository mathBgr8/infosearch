Rebel Platform
2004

Rebel platforms were space stations constructed by Telgorn Corporation and employed by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Each station was 2,100 meters long, boasting shields and eight turbolaser emplacements. Each station had a hangar for starfighters and also capacity for troops and supplies. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Space station classes Category:Telgorn Corporation products
