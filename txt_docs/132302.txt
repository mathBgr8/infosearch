Ten-Abu Donba

Ten-Abu Donba was a male Talid from Ando Prime who served as a High Priest of the Order of Dai Bendu. thumb|left Ten-Abu Donba was known as one of the wisest Andobi Bendu monks on Ando Prime around the time of the Invasion of Naboo.
