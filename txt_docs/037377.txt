Fire suppression droid Mark I
1315

thumb|Fire Suppression Droid mark I The fire suppression droid Mark I was similar in appearance to the KM1 mining droid and the mining droid Mark I, but its purpose was to put out fires using a carbonite gun. Such droids were used at the Peragus Mining Facility. The Exile, Meetra Surik encountered them on her travels shortly before the destruction of the Peragus Mining Facility, when HK-50 used these and various other mining droids to protect the Exile from the miners, who wished to give the Exile to the Exchange. Unfortunately, the assassin droid underestimated the strength of a Jedi, even an ex-Jedi who had severed her connection with the Force; as a result, the Exile awoke prematurely, and all of the facility's droids that had been commandeered turned on her.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Firefighter droid models
