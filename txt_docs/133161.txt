Rand (BFF-1 bulk freighter)
879

Rand was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Along with Talli, it was part of Operation Grain Snatch. Their role was to swap the fresh grain belonging to the convoy with poisoned grain.
