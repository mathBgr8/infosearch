Cromag
1502

Cromag was a large humanoid beast "owned" by Mosh Pelkan. He was two and a half meters tall, with huge eyes with overdeveloped shoulders and arms. He was described as having a shape like a "V". He was the primary combatant in the gladiatorial arena at Pelkan's Mansion on Questal. Many of his opponents were prisoners of the Imperial Moff Bandor whom Pelkin served.
