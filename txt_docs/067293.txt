Margath's on Elshandruu Pica
1058

Margath's on Elshandruu Pica was a conglomerate of bars, casinos, and restaurants owned and operated by Kina Margath on Elshandruu Pica in the city of Picavil. Among the various places for relaxation, gambling, drinking, and dining was the five-star rated 27th Hour Social Club.
