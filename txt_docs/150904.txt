Kichiir
1557

Kichiir was a Wookiee associate of Rebel agents Xenon Nnaksta and Adazian Liebke.Alliance Intelligence Reports Nnaksta and Liebke asked Kichiir and other associates for help when they were being chased by bounty hunter Ssach'thirix. Kichiir and the others managed to turn the tables on Ssach'thirix, bruising and beating him, but Ssach'thirix survived. Kichiir joined the Alliance Special Forces Eclipse Team's Lightning Tech Squadron and was later injured while attempting to stop the theft of Meleenium from the Qua'Tahc mines on Af'El. *Rebel Dawn *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Alliance Special Forces personnel Category:Males Category:Wookiees
