Dagobah mud cake
702

Dagobah mud cake was a type of food enjoyed by a green bounty hunter who was present at Ban Papeega's interrogation of Thurlow Harris on Centares. It was served with junk beetles. The dish shared its name with the swamp planet Dagobah. *Vader's Quest 1 Category:Cakes
