Cophrigin monkey
1203

This monkey was a small creature from the Outer Rim world of Cophrigin V. One of these creatures was in the company of an exiled Jedi, An'ya Kuro, when Darth Vader arrived on the planet to eliminate her in 1 BBY. As no official name has been given to these creatures, they are conjecturally named for their simian-like appearance.
