Kishi
531

Kishi was an area of Coruscant near Westport that contained the Pillar Zone. In 19.9 BBY, after the crash of an infected ship called the Spinner, Kishi became overrun with stone mites. However, Jedi healers used morichro to destroy the infestation. *The New Essential Chronology Category:Coruscant districts
