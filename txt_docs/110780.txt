CorSec Database
1122

|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The CorSec Database was an online reference guide for Star Wars Galaxies gamers, listing some of the more important criminal NPC characters and organizations featured in the game. It consists of two alphabetically organized parts and is written in an in-universe perspective, representing the CorSec criminal database. The first part was released on June 9, 2006 and the second part on June 16, 2006. Category:In-universe source material Category:Online reference databases Category:Star Wars Galaxies articles
