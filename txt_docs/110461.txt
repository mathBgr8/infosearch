Corellian Merlot
1175

}} Corellian Merlot was an alcoholic beverage from Corellia. The Hutt crime boss Jabba Desilijic Tiure had a taste for this merlot, though his chef Porcellus felt it was not the best type of wine. It was also served in the dining car of the luxury repulsortrain Gem of Alderaan. This drink was mentioned by the NPC Porcellus in one of his quests in Star Wars Galaxies, which are part of Jabba's Theme Park. Category:Corellian alcohols Category:Wines
