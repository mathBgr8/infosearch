Bezil
2281

The Bezil was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle used by Ripoblus forces during the Sepan Civil War. Imperial forces encountered the Bezil as Dimok starships staged a raid on the Ripolus supply convoy Asbo. The shuttle, along with Bredis and Gamu, aided Imperial starfighters in pushing back the Dimok attackers. After the attack was routed, Imperial pilot Maarek Stele inspected the convoy, discovering Imperial munitions likely sold by Admiral Harkov who was, ironically enough, leading the campaign to end the civil war. The shuttle was ultimately destroyed, either by Dimok or Imperial forces. The Bezil appears in the video game Star Wars: TIE Fighter.
