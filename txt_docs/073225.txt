Bothan Martial Academy
1409

The Bothan Martial Academy, also known as the Bothan Military Academy, was a Bothan military academy. Although often mocked, the academy produced many officers and soldiers during the Galactic Civil War to protect the Bothans' neutrality. Peshk Vri'syk, Asyr Sei'lar and Traest Kre'fey were graduates.
