Star Wars Weekly 117
1281

Star Wars Weekly 117 is the one hundred seventeenth issue of the Marvel UK's Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints. Starting with the next issue, the series would be renamed The Empire Strikes Back Weekly, but would continue the numbering used under the previous title. This was first time detailed in Star Wars Weekly 116 by a C-3PO and R2-D2 advert. *Star Wars (1977) 38 *Heroes of the galaxy: Man-Wolf Part 4: Man-Wolf shows his claws! *Deathlok the Demolisher Part 27: The final chapter! *Tales of the Watcher: The treasure of Planetoid 12!
