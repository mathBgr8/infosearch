X10 Groundcruiser
2625

The X10 Groundcruiser was manufactured by the Corporate Sector Authority for use by their Security Police. A six-meter long repulsorlift vehicle, it required a single pilot to operate. It could carry six Espos and 100 kilograms of cargo. Capable of achieving 400 kilometers per hour, the craft had a flight ceiling of two meters.
