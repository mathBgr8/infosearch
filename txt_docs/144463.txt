*New Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Hovarb Devist was a clone of Soontir Fel. He was part of a sleeper cell of Fel clones planted by Grand Admiral Thrawn on the planet Pakrik Minor. He later met all of the other members of their sleeper cell, Dobrow, Carib, Sabmin and Tabric.
