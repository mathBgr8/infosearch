Geoffrey Pierson
619

Geoffrey Pierson (born June 16, 1949) provided voices for the Empire Strikes Back radio drama, and later provided voices for Star Wars: The Old Republic. For the radio drama, he provided the voice of Lobot, among other characters, making him the first actor to perform the voice of the Cloud City cyborg.
