Blue Wings of Strength
1196

The Blue Wings of Strength were a sacred Ewok item that had once belonged to an ancient Ewok warrior. Around 3 ABY, prior to the journey to locate and rescue the parents of Mace and Cindel Towani from the Gorax, Widdle Warrick was presented the sacred item by the Ewok elder Logray. The item comprised a headband with tufts of blue feathers on each side. *Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe Category:Sacred Ewok items
