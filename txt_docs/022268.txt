Tala 9
2352

Tala 9 was located within the Tala system of the Inner Rim. It was the location of a droid-controlled factory. The droids discouraged sentients from visiting by only using droid languages to issue landing codes. The system was discontinued after two fully laden passenger liners collided in orbit.
