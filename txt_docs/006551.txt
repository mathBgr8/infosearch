|publisher=Marvel Comics |release date=February 7, 1978 |pages=18 |upc=071486028178 |timeline=0 ABYThe events of Star Wars 11 take place after the Battle of Yavin and prior to the events of Star Wars (1977) 25, which include the Yavin blockade. Since The Essential Chronology dates the Yavin blockade to 0 ABY, the events of Star Wars 11 must also occur in that year. |series=Star Wars (1977) |issue=11 }} Star Wars 11 is the eleventh issue of the Legends comic book series Star Wars. It was written by Archie Goodwin, illustrated by Carmine Infantino and Terry Austin, and first published by Marvel Comics on February 7, 1978. It features the story "Star Search!," in which Han Solo and Leia Organa continue their search for Luke Skywalker in the Drexel system. Han and Chewie are captured by space pirates while Luke takes on a monstrous Sea-Dragon! Han Solo and Chewbacca leave Aduba-3 in the Millennium Falcon, only to be captured once again by Crimson Jack and his gang of pirates. Princess Leia Organa had also been captured by the pirate while on her way to the Drexel system to find out what happened to Luke Skywalker, and Jack intended to hold the princess for ransom. Han and Leia secretly devise a ruse to trick the pirates into taking them to the Drexel system by claiming that the Rebels have stockpiled a treasure there. While suspicious of Solo's plan, Jack's greed gets the better of him, and he decides to trust the Rebels for the time being. Meanwhile, on Drexel, Luke, R2-D2, and C-3PO are stranded on their wrecked spacecraft floating in the huge ocean that covers the planet and are attacked by a giant sea-dragon.
