}} Cosmohaul Shipping was a cargo transportation company. Cosmohaul Shipping appeared in "Droids Defiant," a roleplaying adventure written by Thomas Bowling and published in the February 1996 ninth issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
