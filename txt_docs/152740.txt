EVA Pod
1348

thumb|right|250px|An EVA Pod in the junkyard behind Watto's Shop The EVA Pod was a type of vehicle present in the junkyard behind Watto's Shop in Mos Espa on Tatooine in 32 BBY. It had previously been a repair and maintenance vehicle pod of unknown origin.Inside the Worlds of Star Wars: Episode I The EVA Pod is actually a replica of a famous prop from the 1968 film 2001: A Space Odyssey, which resembled one of the designs of the then-recently proposed FlexCraft by N.A.S.A.. It was put in the background of Watto's junkyard in The Phantom Menace as a visual Easter egg. *Inside the Worlds of Star Wars: Episode I *Star Wars: Complete Locations Category:Easter Egg articles Category:Space pods
