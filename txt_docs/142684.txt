Arly Bron
1291

Arly Bron was a stocky, aging Human male smuggler with a genial air and sharp tongue who frequented the Corellian Sector during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, Arly Bron was one of the captains who helped the Rebel Alliance where he piloted the Double Echo and served as one of the atmosphere guides during the Battle of Ylesia. He was named after writer R. Lee Brown, founder and original editor of Echo Station, the original Star Wars online e-mag (itself the source of Bron's ship the Double Echo). *Rebel Dawn Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Pilots Category:Smugglers
