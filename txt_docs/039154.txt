Jooj
562

Jooj was a Killik hive. They were less than an inch long, and would create swarms of millions. During the Swarm War, they were used as a weapon by the Killiks, having them crawl under enemy armor and kill enemy soldiers via blood sucking. They were a greenish-brown color. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Unknown Regions Category:Killik hives
