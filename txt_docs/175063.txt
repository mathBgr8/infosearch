|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation=House Naberrie }} }} Amidala's Beach was a private beach island located in Naboo's Lake Country, located in close proximity to Varykino, the ancestral home owned by House Naberrie. 220px|thumb|The small island served as Amidala's private beach. Padmé Amidala stayed there with Anakin Skywalker before the start of the Clone Wars when her life was being threatened by a bounty hunter, who was hired by Nute Gunray to kill her. According to Padmé, when she was in Level Three, she and her fellow classmates frequently swam to the island during school retreat, where an elderly jeweler crafted beautiful vases and necklaces out of glass.Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones novelization This was where Padmé had made plans to raise her child, although she died in childbirth, giving birth to Luke and Leia Skywalker. In 1 ABY, some spacers visited the location. Amidala's Beach was a location in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
