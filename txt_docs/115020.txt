Taraloon Pirate Complex
868

thumb|right|250px|The Taraloon Pirate Complex The Taraloon Pirate Complex was an underground base build on the uncharted planet of Taraloon by the pirate Drek Drednar and his crew. It was equipped with perimeter sensors, maintenance bays, hangars for the ship Sable III and the starfighters under Drednar's command, medical facilities, dining rooms, recreation centers and comfortable quarters. There was also a large cache where the pirate loot was stored. Category:Expansion Region structures Category:Pirate bases
