Organ grafting
1215

thumb|right|200px|A female Shaper performing an organ graft. Organ grafting was a ritual of the Yuuzhan Vong which involved the replacement of removed organs with a new living creature "augmenting" the Yuuzhan Vong biologically. The Executor Nom Anor was known to have removed one of his eyes with the sharpened end of a burning stick and then replaced it with a plaeryin bol, a living creature capable of spitting venom. This was considered one of the most extreme rituals ever committed by a Yuuzhan Vong. Yuuzhan Vong commanders also had their cloaks attached by special growths to their shoulders.
