Raid on Gyndine
1961

The New Republic raided on Yuuzhan Vong-occupied Gyndine in 28 ABY. The goal was to train new recruits in combat that the New Republic was sure to win, in preparation for a decisive major battle later on. The Yuuzhan Vong force was larger than Admiral Traest Kre'fey wanted to attack, but he still managed to completely destroy the enemy force. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Battles of Gyndine Category:Battles of the Yuuzhan Vong War
