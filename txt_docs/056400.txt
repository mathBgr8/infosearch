Alter Damage
1303

|purpose= |inventor= |affiliation=Jedi Order }} Alter Damage was a Force technique used by the Jedi Order to change the damage their lightsaber inflicted upon a target. Mastery of this skill came hand-in-hand with mastery over lightsaber combat, as it was used in conjunction with use of the weapon. When used correctly, a practitioner could manipulate the energy of the lightsaber and reduce the damage caused to the target. Used while fighting both living and non-living opponents, this technique could make the difference between killing an opponent or completely disabling a droid or piece of machinery.
