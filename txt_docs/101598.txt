Fleet Support Branch
2175

The Fleet Support Branch was one of the four organizational arms of the Imperial Navy, consisting of those officers responsible for maintenance and engineering duties.Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide It was divided into two main divisions; the Engineering Division, which was responsible for maintaining a ship's sublight and hyperspace engines, and also for the structural integrity of their hulls, and Technical Services, which was charged with looking after power distribution, life-support and other specialities that were outside of the Engineering Division's remit. In 3 ABY, Commander Brandei, serving in the role of Technical Services Officer aboard the Super Star Destroyer Executor, was responsible for ensuring the readiness of the ship's TIE Fighter squadrons. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition *The Essential Guide to Warfare *Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide Category:Imperial Navy branches
