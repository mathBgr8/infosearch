Sweetblossom
2393

thumb|200px|A jar of thick fluid made from Sweetblossom. Sweetblossom was a flower whose extract produced a significant narcotic effect. While not addicting, the extract was used nonetheless by abusers who preferred to evade life's demands. While completely aware of their environment, users of sweetblossom had an attitude of procrastination and apathy. Rebel Alliance pilot Greglik spoke highly of the drug.
