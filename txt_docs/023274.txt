Born on the planet Kamino from the genetic template of bounty hunter Jango Fett, CT-41/14-0301 was a male clone trooper created to serve the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. CT-41/14-0301 served in the 327th Star Corps and eventually earned the nickname "Barr."The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Early in the war, the 327th, led by Clone Commander Bly, attempted to reap information from the memory banks of a Confederate tactical droid, by setting up a trap in a rocky canyon. They were able to fabricate an information bunker, which attracted the attention of the tactical droid. In turn, the droid ordered his troops to attack the bunker, prompting Barr and the men of the 327th to attack them from the rear. They charged in on jetpacks and began firing, with Commander Bly ordering Barr and Galle to take out the super battle droids. They were able to effectively dispatch the tactical droid's troops, while Jedi General Aayla Secura beheaded the tactical droid. Since the droid had not gone into cyberostasis, they would be able to plunder its memory banks for intel.The Clone Wars: Headgames As the war continued, Barr was eventually promoted to the rank of lieutenant. As a Clone Lieutenant, Barr led the 327th's 2nd Platoon, which comprised of thirty-two clone troopers and four clone medics divided into four squads. Towards the war's end, Barr fought in the Battle of Felucia and continued to serve under the command of Bly and alongside the now lieutenants Galle and Inc. During the battle, Barr accompanied Inc and Jedi General Barriss Offee in an attempt to ambush a battle droid patrol. During the ambush, Inc's blaster goes into carbon flush, causing the clone to get killed. Getting overwhelmed, Offee ordered Barr to go get the rest of the squad. As Barr left to get help, the clones received Order 66, and when Barr returned with help, Galle ordered an AT-TE to fire on Offee, killing her.Reversal of Fortune As a clone of Jango Fett, Barr stood at 1.83 meters tall and had tan skin. During the Clone Wars, Barr wore Phase I clone trooper armor adorned with the yellow markings of the 327th Star Corps. During the battle at the information bunker, Barr utilized a jetpack and a DC-15A blaster rifle. Barr would eventually substitute their Phase I armor for Phase II clone trooper armor, but kept the 327th's markings.
