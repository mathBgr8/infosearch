Telkur Station Cantina
1684

The Telkur Station Cantina was a seedy pirate cantinaLegacy of the Force: Tempest on the Telkur Station. The cantina was a place illuminated by dim blue lightning and filled with the sweet taint of Rekka smoke hanging in the air. The discreet entrance was half-concealed somewhere along the establishment's eight walls. The room's only ceiling was a disorganized web of ventilation ducts. The place featured a filthy bar whose Hapan barkeepers handed out datapads with drink recipes featuring beverages like the Red Cloud and the flat-tasting Gizer Ale, which were both served in mugs to guests. The cantina's clients were exclusively Human and Near-Human who sat clustered in groups of three or four around corroded, rust-stained durasteel tables. The male patrons were handsome but scroungy looking with three-day beards, whereas the Hapan women dressed to look tough and available. The pirate station used to be a prominent hotspot for outlawed ships to pick up supplies because the place was not on the radar of Hapan Security. Although weapons were permitted, droids had to stay outside.
