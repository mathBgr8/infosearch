Curovao ImpEx
594

Curovao ImpEx was an import-export business owned by the Curovao family, based on Brentaal in the city of Curovao. The company specialized in the transportation of military commodities and experimental technologies. The corporation was the target of many civil right activists, who claimed the corporation was exploiting natural resources on underdeveloped worlds, and crushing any opposition. *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Transport companies
