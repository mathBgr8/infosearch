thumb|right|200px|Class VI automated laser projectors Automated laser traps were lasers that were attached to a sensor set for automated targeting. Laser traps were used in the Death Star detention level. The most common kind of laser trap was the BlasTech Class VI automated laser projector. It had a small control computer, a sensor suite, and a small laser.
