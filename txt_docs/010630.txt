Unity Green
1224

Unity Green was a large park on Coruscant, erected following the culmination of the Yuuzhan Vong War. Removed from the rising skytowers of the planet-wide city, Unity Green symbolized the peace treaty between the Yuuzhan Vong and the Galactic Alliance. Yorik coral bluffs rose from the shores of Liberation Lake, while native hawk-bats preyed on bio-engineered yammal-jells and coufee eels. The council room at the top of the New Jedi Temple offered a spectacular view over Unity Green.
