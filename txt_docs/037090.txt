Tecave
558

Tecave was a grass that grew only on the planet Cerea. Tecave was important to the inhabitants because is was used in the production of guilea. It was also in high demand off-planet. However, it could not survive without malium in the atmosphere, a substance rare on most worlds but plentiful on Cerea. *Star Wars (1998) 1 Category:Grasses
