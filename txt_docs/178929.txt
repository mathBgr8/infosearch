Team Four (Noghri)
615

Team Four was a team of Noghri Death Commandos under the command of Thrawn. After the failure of Team Eight to capture Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa Solo, Team Four was sent to attempt their capture. Team Four was defeated at Bpfassh. *Heir to the Empire Category:Imperial military units
