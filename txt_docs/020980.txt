Kevin Rubio
1257

thumb|250px|Kevin Rubio in Tag & Bink Are Dead 2 Kevin Rubio has written for Star Wars in a variety of media. He wrote, directed, and provided a voice for the fan film TROOPS. He was later hired by Dark Horse to pen several comics, including the Tag and Bink series and several stories for Star Wars Tales. Rubio also wrote a number of humorous character interviews for Star Wars Insider.
