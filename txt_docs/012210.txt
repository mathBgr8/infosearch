Binn Ibes was a Force-sensitive trained to use the Force and understand the Path of the Jedi by the Jedi Order. A contemporary of famed Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn, Ibes gained the title of Master as well, at which time he selected the Dressellian Initiate Reeft as his Padawan.
