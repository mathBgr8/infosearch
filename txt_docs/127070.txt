Grizmallt nerf
1856

A subspecies of nerf&mdash;a nonsentient species&mdash;lived on the Core Worlds planetThe Essential Atlas Grizmallt. The subspecies featured a variety of differences to common nerfs, having more resemblance to eopies with antlers. It was speculated in The New Essential Guide to Alien Species, an anthropological text compiled from the notes of Senior Anthropologist Mammon Hoole, that the nerfs on Grizmallt had evolved in body shape and neck length through thousands of years of reaching up to trees to eat berries and leaves.
