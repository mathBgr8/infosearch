Coral Depths City
2412

Coral Depths City was the oldest part of Coral City, the planet Dac's capital city. It was the birthplace of Admiral Gial Ackbar.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Along with Foamwander City and Reef Home City, it was charged with coordinating orbital defense. Many leaders and Senators fled there after the Yuuzhan Vong captured Coruscant. During the attack on Mon Calamari, it came under assault by three squadrons of Admiral Daala's TIE Fighters.
