By the year 4.3 ABY, he was employed by the Star Tours travel agency at their Tomorrowland Starport. Along with F-24-AMTx, F-23 performed the final assembly of StarSpeeder 3000 and serviced their ion engines.Complete Guide to Star Tours He did not mind having to work overtime as the extra credits helped pay for his expensive tuition at the University of the Universe.
