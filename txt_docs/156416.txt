Chance Castle
864

The Chance Castle was an ancient and one of the largest hotel-casinos on Nar Shaddaa. When Xaverri came to Nar Shaddaa, her shows were held at the Castle. When news of an Imperial attack came to Nar Shaddaa, the Castle willingly allowed Han Solo and Mako Spince to use their auditorium for a smuggler meeting to plan for the defense of Nar Shaddaa, as an attack would be bad for business. It had a fifty story atrium.
