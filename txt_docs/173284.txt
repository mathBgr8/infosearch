Jonathan Wilkins
1223

Jonathan Wilkins is the current Editor at Large of Star Wars Insider magazine, published by Titan Magazines. He previously served as editor for issues 107 to 174, from January 2009 through July 2017. He was succeeded by Will Salmon. Wilkins has interviewed many Star Wars personalities, including Mark Hamill, Stuart Freeborn, Nick Dudman, John Mollo, Roger Christian, Dave Filoni, and Dee Bradley Baker. *Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back: Special 40th Anniversary Collector's Edition (2020) Category:Editors Category:Non-fiction authors Category:Reference work authors
