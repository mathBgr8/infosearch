Venom rat
2298

A venom rat was a type of venomous rat that could be found in swamps. At some point no earlier than around 8 BBY,Ewoks (1985) 5 features Wicket Wystri Warrick, who was born around 8 BBY per the Ewok Wicket Wystri Warrick fled from the alien woodsmen Rok and Zol after discovering the alien's plot of cutting down the Ewok's forest on the moon of Endor for processing. During the chase, Wicket stated that the two aliens were as fun as a swampful of venom rats. Venom rats were mentioned in the fifth issue of the Star Wars: Ewoks comic series.
