Airspeeders for Bodyguards and Security Specialists
605

Airspeeders for Bodyguards and Security Specialists was a training course that covered airspeeder piloting. Intended for bodyguards and security officers, it was held on Coruscant. Joram Kithe attended this course at one point. Category:Training programs and exercises
