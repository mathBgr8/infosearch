The planet featured a frozen environment, and its surface consisted of rock. Vulp Minor occupied the fifth orbital position around its star, Vulpter, and it had three moons. Vulp Minor was mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 4: Vulpter," the fourth entry in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 28, 2005. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Vulpter system, and therefore Vulp Minor, in grid square L-10.
