Gray Squadron (Aleph-class)
1083

Gray Squadron was an experimental unit of six Aleph-class starfighters based on Blue Diver in 40 ABY. Syal Antilles, Zueb Zan and their astromech were in the squadron. They fought at the Battle of Tralus. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Galactic Alliance starfighter squadrons
