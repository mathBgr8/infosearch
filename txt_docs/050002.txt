L-23
947

L-23 was a protocol droid who belonged to Byrom and Ensa, two farmers living on the planet of Molavar. During one of the farmers' routine repair runs on their antiquated moisture vaporators, L-23 was sliced in half by Dark Jedi Lycan's lightsaber, as he approached them. Category:Protocol droids
