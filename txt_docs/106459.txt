Martínez Roca
2557

thumb|right|195px|Logo Ediciones Martínez Roca, known commonly as Martínez Roca, is a company formerly responsible for publishing many of the Spanish translations of Star Wars novels. The company prints both fiction and non-fiction. They focus primarily on historic novels, but also work with science fiction and fantasy. They acquired the rights to the Star Wars books in 1992, but sold them to Alberto Santos Editor in 2001.
