Unidentified Devaronian Revenant commando
1201

This Unnamed Devaronian was part of the commando team lead by Nym to disable some shield bunkers protecting the Trihexalon factory on Nod Kartha. When the commando team was under heavy fire at the last shield bunker, Jedi Master Adi Gallia made a hole in the bunker and provide cover for the team to escape just as the timed charges blew. This Devaronian character model was used in the game mission for the unit 'Commando' in the game.
