Hall of Peace
879

The Hall of Peace was a large open hall located in the new Jedi Temple on Coruscant. It was most likely on the first floor as was the Morning Court. The chamber was used to hold great banquets at the Temple for Jedi, and sometimes politicians. In 40 ABY, the feast of remembrance, held after the funeral of Mara Jade Skywalker, took place there. It was located in one of the four newly constructed pyramids around the Jedi Temple. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:New Jedi Temple locations
