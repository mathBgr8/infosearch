|pages= |upc= |timeline=0 ABY"Tatooine Sojourn" is set between the Battle of Yavin and "Princess Leia, Imperial Servant," which The Essential Guide to Characters places in 0 ABY. |series=Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures |issue=4 }} Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 4 was the fourth issue of the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Russ Manning. Luke Skywalker is ordered by Rebel Command to abandon a reconnaissance mission. It seems that appearances of unscheduled transport capsules have coincided with the destruction of Rebel bases, and just such a capsule has landed&mdash;on Tatooine! Luke must return to his home planet, now a bustling locus of trade and a hotbed of rebellion, to discover how the Empire is finding out about the hidden Rebel bases.
