Annoo-dat
1699

Annoo-dat was the name used by two separate species who lived as a single culture on Annoo, an agriworld in the Outer Rim Territories. The original Annoo-dat were four-eyed reptiles indigenous to the original planet Annoo. They abandoned their homeworld and conquered Gelefil, and the Ret natives of that world. Afterward, the Ret called themselves Annoo-dat as well, and Gelefil's name was changed to Annoo. Xenobiologists called the original Annoo-dat "Annoo-dat Prime", and called the Ret Annoo-dat Blue.
