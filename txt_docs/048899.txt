Koorivar (language)
1164

Koorivar was the native language of the Koorivar species from Kooriva. Most Koorivar were fluent in both Koorivar and Galactic Basic Standard, although only literate in their own language. Koorivar language used whispers and soft sibilants, which had to be combined with subtle hand gestures to be fully meaningful. *Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Languages
