Sigma-class shuttle
4414

The Sigma-class shuttle was a Koensayr Manufacturing shuttlecraft that was considered obsolete by 2 ABY, but continued to see use by traders and criminals such as the Granse Confederacy. The Sigma-class was a shuttle of inferior speed and power to the Lambda-class T-4a shuttle fielded by the Galactic Empire. The Mist Laden, a highly-modified Sigma-class shuttle, measured 21.6 meters in length, and was equipped with a Class 1 hyperdrive with Class 15 backup, a navigation computer, deflector shields, sensors, four months-worth of consumables, a laser cannon and heavy turbolaser cannon, and had a top atmospheric speed of 1,000 kilometers per hour. It was crewed by a pair each of pilots and gunners, though it could operate with a single crewmember, and could additionally carry eight passengers and 100 metric tons of cargo. Produced by component and starship manufacturer Koensayr,The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels the Sigma-class was once popular, but was outclassed and largely replaced by craft such as the Imperial Lambda-class shuttle years before 2 ABY.Alliance Intelligence Reports takes place two years after the destruction of the Death Star, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY, two years before 2 ABY. One modified Sigma-class shuttle, the Mist Laden, was owned by an independent trader, and seized by the Gand findsman Lu'daal-ud when he captured its prior owner. Undergoing further alterations by Lu'daal-ud's associates, it proved a reliable vessel, and was among the craft employed by the criminal organization the Granse Confederacy, of which the Gand was a member.
