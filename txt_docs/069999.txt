Chief of State's office
2325

The primary office of the New Republic's Chief of State was located within the old Imperial Palace on Coruscant. Occupying a chamber deep within the heart of the Imperial Palace on Coruscant, the office of the New Republic's Chief of State served as the primary chambers used by the Republic's leader. With red-hued walls and "windows" which presented real time holoimages from the outside, the office was occupied by two different Chiefs during its use.
