Last Stand Casino
1074

The Last Stand Casino was located in Ord Mynock Freeport. It was housed in the Old Republic-era fort, which was the original government building. Because of this, the owner, Mortimon Galli, kept the pre-Imperial appearance of dim lighting with many old wooden tables and chairs. Games of sabacc and other forms of gambling were always taking place in the back.
