Tchaw Fenn
1280

Tchaw Fenn was a Bothan information broker who lived in Friz Harammel on Genarius. He used to work exclusively for Riboga the Hutt, but after the Hutt left the Cularin system, Fenn worked as a free agent. Gathering information came naturally to him, and he reveled in the process. As he got into his middle years, he become somewhat pudgy. He kept his beard in immaculate condition, and he would often preen it when in public. *Living Force Campaign Guide *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothans Category:Genarius residents Category:Information brokers Category:Males
