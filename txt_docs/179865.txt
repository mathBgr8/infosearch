He spent his entire life searching and collecting valuable and rare items throughout the galaxy. In 1 ABY Slaus traveled to the Outer Rim moon, Yavin IV, where he lived in the Mining Outpost for an extended period of time. Here, he would occasionally offer rewards to spacers for gathering rare items for him. Breac Slaus is one of several collector Non-Player Characters (NPCs) in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies. Introduced in the Chapter 7 of November 2007, these Non-Player Characters offer several quests that involve collecting items throughout the game world and killing creatures.
