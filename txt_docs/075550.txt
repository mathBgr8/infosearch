Ixidro Legorburu
1559

Ixidro Legorburu was a male from M'haeli. He was once a member of New Republic Intelligence Service, and had been a tactical aide during the Black Fleet Crisis. By 25 ABY he held the rank of colonel and was the director of the Home Fleet's Battle Assessment Division.
