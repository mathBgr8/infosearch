Python (Assassin-class)
2299

The Python was an Assassin-class corvette warship. It was used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic in the defection of the Imperial officer Commander Zaletta. After Ace Azzameen and others had destroyed the minefield surrounding an Imperial Communications relay Comm Center, the Python was called in to download its logs in which they discovered Zaletta's location in the Eidoloni system.
