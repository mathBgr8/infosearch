Corri

Corri was a gas giant in the Outlier systems of the Corellian sector. This planet was made non-canon when Sacorria's system mentioned in Cracken's Threat Dossier (the Outlier systems) was completely reorganized (as the Sacorrian system) with new planets in the most modern sources. *Cracken's Threat Dossier Category:Gas giants Category:Planets in the Core Worlds
