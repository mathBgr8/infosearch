Golga Besadii Fir
1927

Golga Besadii Fir was a Hutt diplomat that served as the final ambassador on behalf of his government to the New Republic. He was assigned to the Hutt consulate on Coruscant by the time of the Yuuzhan Vong War. When the Hutts allied with the extragalactic Yuuzhan Vong, Golga began to make preparations to leave Coruscant for Nal Hutta. It was at this point he received a visit from New Republic Senator Viqi Shesh who the Hutt imagined what she would look like as a dancer. The Senator hoped to pass informal intelligence to the Hutts of New Republic assets in the hope that this disinformation would be passed to the Hutt's Yuuzhan Vong allies.
