Old Soren

thumb|right|250px|Old Soren Old Soren was an spaceport on the penal colony Ord Vaxal. During the jailbreak 20 years prior to the Battle of Yavin, the entire spaceport was blasted to rubble by Galactic Republic ships in orbit to keep the inmates of Ord Vaxal's prisons from escaping. Afterward, the city of Tof Soren sprang up around the ruins of Old Soren, while the northern rim eventually became the location of the Oubliette shadowport. Category:Ord Vaxal locations
