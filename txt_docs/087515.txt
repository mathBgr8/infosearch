As a TIE/sa bomber, EX-1-2 boasted incredibly precise computers that allowed its pilot to destroy specific buildings or vehicles. In 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology EX-1-2 was secured above a hangar bay floor on the Empire's Death Star II superweapon when the Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Vader arrived to discuss the Death Star's lagging construction schedule with Moff Tiaan Jerjerrod, the Imperial officer in charge of the Death Star. EX-1-2 first appeared in the 1983 original trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. In 2003, it received a name on a card for the Empire Strikes Back expansion set of the Wizards of the Coast Star Wars Trading Card Game. Though that card game is set around the 1980 film, Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, EX-1-2 does not appear in that film.
