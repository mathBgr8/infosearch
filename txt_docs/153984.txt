Civilized
1287

The Civilized was the collective name of a group representing members of specific political, economical, and social classes that lived on the planet New Apsolon. In contrast with the Workers, who were a group representing the poorer inhabitants of the planet, the Civilized were the highest and wealthiest caste, living in houses constructed of stone mined from the outskirts of the capital city.
