|published in=RStar Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 7 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=22 BBY (6 months ABG)The date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Geonosis, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 22 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Impregnable" is a comic story from Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 7 featuring Bultar Swan. After a massive battle on Amaltanna, all of General Bultar Swan's clone troopers are defeated. The Separatist leader remains snug in his fortress. Swan, determined not to fail, enters the heavily protected mansion. Ignoring the taunts of her foe, Swan slices through doors, fends off battle droids, leaps huge chasms, and traverses through mazes, finally to reach the impregnable door leading to the leader's chambers. Quick on her feet, she slices through the control panel with her lightsaber, locking herself out, but locking the Separatist leader in.
