Reenogga Personal Services, Cubed
1151

thumb|The [[Reenogga Sisters - owners of Reenogga Personal Services³.]] Reenogga Personal Services, Cubed, also called Reenogga Personal Services³, RPS Cubed, or just RPS, was a firm of "equalizers" owned and operated by the Reenogga Sisters, former members of the Skine Bounty Hunter College. Their offices were located in the Death Mark aboard the Zirtran's Anchor space station. RPS offered their protection, for a fee, to clients against virtually any threat from illegal bounty hunting to assassination. RPS maintained a number of transport ships and starfighters for off-station duties. They would often help Quink, a friend of one of the sisters, to get his cousin Padda out of trouble.
