Lost Loves Casino
613

Located on Phyrstal Island in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae, the Lost Loves Casino was a run-down gambling house that seemed somewhat out of place in the beautiful city. Its bartender, Malric Blaene was widely known for his gossip. Category:Abregado-rae locations Category:Casinos
