Finn (pilot)
1640

Finn was a male Human who served as an Imperial TIE/LN starfighter pilot who was attached to Crimson Squadron when it was stationed on Cilpar in 4 ABY. He was killed shortly before the arrival of Tycho Celchu, who was operating in an undercover role, in the squadron, shot by a blaster bolt to the thorax. All that was ever found of him were his head and feet.
