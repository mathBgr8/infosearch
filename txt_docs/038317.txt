FLTCH R-1
2234

FLTCH R-1 was a FLTCH-series battle droid created by the Trade Federation. Originally programmed by Ephant Mon and Bron to kill Ki-Adi-Mundi, Ki was able to disable him and escape. However, later when a crew was needed for Ki's personal starship, droids on Cerea were few and far between. The Republic agents outfitting him recovered FLTCH from the Outsider Citadel and reprogrammed him as a pilot. FLTCH accompanied Ki on at least one mission, a rescue mission to Tatooine. There he served not only as his pilot, but his bodyguard.
