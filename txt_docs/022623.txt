Gregory Walker
1314

Gregory Walker is the author of "Dining at Dex's," a StarWars.com Hyperspace exclusive article. He also wrote the backstories of Magaloof, Acros-Krik, and Nar Hida, appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. Outside of Star Wars fandom, Walker ran the official fan club and website for actress Jeri Ryan, who played Seven of Nine on Star Trek: Voyager.
