Targeting scope
1526

thumb|200px|The view through a targeting scope A targeting scope was an electronic device mounted onto blasters, blaster rifles, and bowcasters in order to increase the chances of hitting long range targets. Sniper weapons included scopes but for most other weapons they were a costly addition, especially for the high quality ones.
