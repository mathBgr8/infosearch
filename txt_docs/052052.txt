Ryannar N'on Dikasterar
1793

Ryannar N'on Dikasterar was a male Elomin miner who was the first to discover the Elom. He was friends with Kav Dryfus, one of the first Eloms he met. After the Galactic Empire took many of his people captive, he and Dryfus went on a rescue mission. It was a success. However, Kav was captured. Luckily, Ryannar managed to escape.
