Bror Tower Three
840

Bror Tower Three was a building on Coruscant presumably controlled by the Commerce Guild prior to the Clone Wars. It connected to Bror Tower Four via a walkway at their 166th floors. During the Clone Wars, Shu Mai met Tam Uliss and Mousul here.
