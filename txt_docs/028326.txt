Grey Damsel
2301

The Grey Damsel was a Mon Calamari Star Cruiser under the command of Rebel Captain Bok. thumb|left|150px|The Grey Damsel bridge, following the [[Dark Trooper/Legends|dark trooper's slaughter of the crew.]] The Grey Damsel escaped the attack on Tak Base and jumped into hyperspace, evacuating an unknown number of base personnel. However, its entire crew was killed by a dark trooper that managed to sneak onboard. The Grey Damsel emerged from hyperspace near Tatooine, where it crashed into the sandy dunes. Jawa scavengers recovered the seemingly defunct dark trooper from the crash site, however, its systems came back online and caused devastation among the Jawas and other inhabitants of the planet before the locals finally put it down for good. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mon Calamari cruisers Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
