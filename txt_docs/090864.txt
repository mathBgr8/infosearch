Kolcta
1109

}} Kolcta, a rare hybrid of bacta and kolto, was an extremely powerful curative agent. The fluid was so scarce that full submersion was near impractical, so it was usually sold and applied as a simple soaked cloth. Kolcta treatment could help reattach severed limbs, heal almost any kind of wound and, if ingested, counter the effects of aging. *Star Wars: The Old Republic Category:Bacta
