Lord Being of the Swirblies
878

The Lord Being of the Swirblies was a deity of the Skakoan religion. It lived in the Gates of Grontessiant. It oversaw the See Seers and guarded the Albino Cyclops and its Eye. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Deities Category:Skakoan religion
