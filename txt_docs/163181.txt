Furious (Lancer-class)
1989

Furious was an Imperial Lancer-class frigate. Along with its sister ship, the Black Hawk, it escorted a convoy through the Eidoloni system which, belonging to Xizor Transport Systems, transported a wide range of heavy materials used for the construction of the Death Star II. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Lancer-class frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
