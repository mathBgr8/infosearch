Laantis
3409

Laantis was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that saw service with the Imperial Navy. The warship was to receive a new hyperdrive system from the BFF-1 bulk freighter Rasvin. During that time it had to power down to allow the freighter to board. Before the operation commenced the Rebel Alliance sent a strike force, consisting of A-wings, B-Wings, X-wings and Y-wings, two shuttles Silver, two Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports Logan and two corvettes Glaser and Chiller attacked the Laantis knowing it would be vulnerable during the refit. However, the attack failed as the frigate was defended not just by TIE Fighters, Bombers and Interceptors, it was also defended by TIE Advanced starfighters after the attack was defeated the Laantis successfully received its new hyperdrive. The incident was then recorded into the Combat chamber for future simulation training missions to train future pilots, one of them was Maarek Stele. The Laantis appears in the 1994 PC game Star Wars: TIE Fighter in the 4th training mission for the TIE Advanced called "Hyperdrive Upgrade". This article assumes "Medium" difficulty.
