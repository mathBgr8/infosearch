Geothray Camber
1567

Geothray Camber was a Sector Plexus technician working in Imperial Intelligence. Camber proposed the creation of the Hyperspace Orbiting Scanner, a device capable of eavesdropping on virtually any computer by bypassing its security systems. However, Camber had several theoretical and technical problems, and sent a series of scandocs with specifications to Doctor Lindu Sencker.
