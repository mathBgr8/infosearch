3315

|figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Jabba's Palace Sealed Deck were promotional products created by Decipher in October 2000, as an addition to the Star Wars Customizable Card Game (or SWCCG). They are a product that contains cards which in conjunction with Jabba's Palace and Enhanced Jabba's Palace, chronicle the first portion of Return of the Jedi for the SWCCG. Each box contains six Limited Jabba's Palace expansion packs and 20 new and exclusive cards - ten for each Side of the Force. Revisit and enhance strategies introduced in earlier card sets. Straight ahead is the best Sealed Deck play ever offered on the far side of Tatooine. Meet your opponent head-to-head in the bowels of Jabba's dungeon, or fight it out in the sand dunes. The 20 premium cards are as follows: *Hutt Influence *Jabba's Palace: Lower Passages *Mercenary Pilot *Mighty Jabba *My Kind Of Scum / Fearless And Inventive *No Escape *Power Of The Hutt *Racing Skiff (Dark Side) *Stun Blaster (Dark Side) *Tatooine: Desert Heart *Agents In The Court / No Love For The Empire *Jabba's Palace: Antechamber *Lando With Vibro-Ax *Ounee Ta *Palace Raider *Racing Skiff (Light Side) *Seeking An Audience *Stun Blaster (Light Side) *Tatooine: Hutt Trade Route (Desert) *Underworld Contacts Category:Star Wars Customizable Card Game
