Trenchant
2063

The Trenchant was a Sacheen-class light escort of the inaugural New Republic Fifth Fleet, of Task Force Copperleaf. Trenchant was the first casualty of the Battle of Doornik-319. Its shields were taken down by four calibrated gravity bombs deployed by a Yevethan Aramadia-class thrustship which had microjumped near the Trenchant location.
