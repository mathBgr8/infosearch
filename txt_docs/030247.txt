Poodoo Lounge
1422

|location=Mos Espa, Tatooine |interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Poodoo Lounge was an entertainment and dining venue in Mos Espa on Tatooine. The Pre-Boonta Concert was held here, guest hosted by Fodesinbeed Annodue, starring Boles Roor. The Poodoo Lounge was a cantina inside an adobe structure.
