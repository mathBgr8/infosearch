Combat jumpsuit
1941

thumb|An illustration of a combat jumpsuit. The combat jumpsuit was a heavily padded jumpsuit that provided limited protection against both physical and energy attacks. Weighing only eight kilograms, it was designed with maximum flexibility in mind and did not restrict the wearer's movement considerably.
