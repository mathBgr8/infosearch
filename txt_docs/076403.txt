While repairing trash compactor faults in the Kuat Drive Yards research part of the shipyards, the astromech droid R4-P17 was crushed and left for scrap. Anakin Skywalker later discovered the droid while visiting the shipyards to inspect the Jedi customizations made to the Delta-7 interceptor design.
