|haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Coruscant |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Gartros were small dragon-like avian creatures that lived on Coruscant. They had spiked tails, and jaws filled with sharp teeth. The eggs of the gartro were served as a delicacy at Dex's Diner alongside nerf steak, protato wedges, and Quor'sav-fried steak.
