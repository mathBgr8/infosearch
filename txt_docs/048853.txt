Irksh poison
476

Irksh poison was an instantly fatal Yuuzhan Vong poison. Nom Anor provided his spy At'raoth with irksh poison secreted in a false tooth in the back of her mouth in case of capture. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *Galaxy of Intrigue Category:Poisons
