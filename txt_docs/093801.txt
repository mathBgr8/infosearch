Giant Fungi Forest
3757

The Giant Fungi Forest, also known as the Great Fungi Forest, was a titanic patch of huge, trumpet-shaped fungi located on the planet Little Kessel. They were large and strong enough for the world's inhabitants to build homes and offices inside of them. Several of the large mushrooms were even used as landing pads. Professor Renn Volz did his most advanced research in a laboratory hidden in the stipe of one of the forest's gigantic fungus. In the year 0 ABY,The New Essential Chronology Captain Bzorn of the Imperial Navy, under personnel orders by Darth Vader to located and punish planets sympathetic to the Alliance to Restore the Republic, commandeered Volz's Ionic Ring Ship. When Little Kessel's government refused to surreneder its Rebels, Bzorn attacked the Giant Fungi Forest using earthquakes, which the world had not experienced for eons. The Giant Fungi Forest was originally created for the Star Wars Legends newspaper comic strip The Second Kessel Run, written by Russ Helm and penciled by Russ Manning, which ran from January 1 to February 25, 1980. *"The Second Kessel Run" *Star Wars: The Comics Companion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Forests and woods Category:Little Kessel locations
