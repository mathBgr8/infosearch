Corrupter (also spelled as Corruptor) can refer to multiple subjects: *Corrupter, a Victory-class Star Destroyer used by the Galactic Empire *Corrupter, a Victory II-class Star Destroyer used by the Galactic Empire *Corrupter, an Imperial-class Star Destroyer used by the Galactic Empire *Corrupter Task Force, a task force led by the Imperial class Corrupter *Corrupter repair convoy, a repair convoy assigned to assist the Imperial class Corrupter *Corruptor Zero, a battle droid belonging to the Dread Host *Kinetic corruptor, massive machines used to extract energy from baradium deposits
