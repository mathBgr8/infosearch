Neimoidian transport
1998

The Neimoidian transport was a troop transport employed by the Trade Federation. Unlike C-9979 landing craft, standard Neimoidian transports were originally designed for organic troops rather than battle droids, having been in service in the Neimoidian military for several years. They were not equipped with hyperdrives, but could make short space-hops from orbit to the surface or very quickly from one surface point to another. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Starship classes of the Trade Federation Category:Troop transport starship classes
