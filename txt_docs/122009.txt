Diplomatic pouch

thumb|250px|[[Leia Organa Solo/Legends|Leia Organa's diplomatic pouch, containing the Crown Jewels of Alderaan]] Diplomatic pouches were small cases used by dignitaries to carry sensitive, valuable, weapons, or even illegal items. They were immune to searches by most security forces and checkpoints.
