Forest Father
1345

The Forest Father was a deity worshiped by the Ewok species native to the Forest Moon of the Endor. They believed the spirit watched over them all and listened to their prayers. Ewok younglings were taught about the Forest Father, along with other aspects of the forests they dwelt in, after they first learned to walk. The Forest Father was created by Joe Johnston for his children's book The Adventures of Teebo: A Tale of Magic and Suspense, which was released in 1984. *The Adventures of Teebo: A Tale of Magic and Suspense Category:Ewok deities
