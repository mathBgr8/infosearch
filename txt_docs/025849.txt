New Jedi Order or NJO may refer to the following items: *The Jedi Order, which was named the New Jedi Order under Luke Skywalker after the fall of the Galactic Empire. *The New Jedi Order Era that took place subsequent to the New Republic Era and the First Order-Resistance War *New Jedi Order, Luke Skywalker's iteration of the Jedi Order after the fall of the Galactic Empire *Star Wars: The New Jedi Order, the Legends novel series detailing the Yuuzhan Vong War *The New Jedi Order era, the Legends publishing era
