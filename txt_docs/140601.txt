. The events of that film take place in 4 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology, meaning that all paintings in "Celestia Galactica Photografica," including Hunting Overlook, must have been created after 104 ABY. |media type=Painting |pages= |published in=Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: Legacy Vol. 4 |genre= }} Hunting Overlook was a painting created after 104 ABY that depicted a landing platform overlooking a massive wroshyr tree on top of a large body of water on the planetThe Essential Atlas Kashyyyk. The tree had numerous huts and platforms carved throughout its trunk and branches. Starships were parked on several of its platforms, including two CR90 corvettes and a J-type diplomatic barge on the painting's right side. On the platform in the painting's foreground, five Wookiees and an astromech droid worked on two Raddaugh Gnasp fluttercraft. On the right side of the image, a group of four YT-1300 light freighters with brightly colored stripes on their hull flew toward the wroshyr tree. The left side of the painting included a large aquatic worm-like creature, with several fluttercraft flying in the air above it. The center of the image featured a large floating structure, with several platforms standing above the water below it. Green Coruscant appeared in "Celestia Galactica Photografica," a series of in-universe illustrations by Ryan Church collected in the 2005 graphic novel Star Wars: Visionaries.
