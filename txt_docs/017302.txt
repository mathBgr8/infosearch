It was the second of two gas giants in the Velm system, located in the Halori sector in the Trans-Hydian portion of the Mid Rim. Zeloc was orbited by eleven moons, and it was also encircled by a ring system. Zeloc was mentioned in Velmor: Royalty and Rebellion, a roleplaying source article written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards.com on March 6, 2003 as a supplement for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Velm system, and therefore Zeloc, in grid square P-7.
