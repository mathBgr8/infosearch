Woolly veermok
1039

Native to the planet of Naboo, woolly veermoks were dangerous predators that lived on the frozen swamps of Naboo's northern hemisphere. Once the natural enemies of Gungans during Naboo's ice age, the woolly cousins of the four-legged veermoks were known for their untameable nature, prompting the Naboo government to make attempted capture of the creatures an illegal activity. *Alien Anthology *Ultimate Adversaries Category:Creatures of Naboo Category:Mammals Category:Non-sentient species Category:Predatory creatures Category:Swamp creatures
