Plasmar
1046

The Plasmars were transparent, amoeba-like creatures native to dark world Y'nybeth. Living in an unlit environment, they absorbed any available warmth into their bodies. The first and last mention of the Plasmars occurred in the 1997 novel Planet of Twilight, where the ability of these creatures was used as a point of comparison to the condition of Leia Organa, who felt the presence of the Force with her body. *Planet of Twilight Category:Creatures of the Expansion Region Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Invertebrates Category:Species of undefined sentience
