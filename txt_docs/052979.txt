Chiewab Amalgamated Pharmaceuticals Company
3919

}} Chiewab Amalgamated Pharmaceuticals Company was a medical, chemical and pharmaceutical equipment conglomerate which controlled over six hundred star systems.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Scouts employed by Chiewab laid claim to many newly discovered planets in Wild Space, and the company exploited these planets in the development of new biotech products. After the end of the Clone Wars, Chiewab Pharmaceuticals became a voting sponsor of the Corporate Sector Authority and received a seat on its Executive Directorship Board. Chiewab was seen as the leading manufacturer in the biotechnological field with one of their rivals being BioTech Industries. It manufactured the immensely popular GLiS Emergency Medpac and the GH-7 medical analysis unit.
