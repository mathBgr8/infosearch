Reelo Baruk's RA-7 protocol droid
2045

Reelo Baruk, a Rodian gangster who operated on Nar Shaddaa, owned a masculine RA-7 protocol droid. While searching for Baruk, Kyle Katarn stumbled upon the droid, who assured him that he was not evil, just his master. The droid also told Katarn to watch out for Baruk and asked not to be deactivated. The droid is canonical, appearing in Jedi Outcast, but has never been named. If the player approaches the droid, it will exchange some dialogue with Katarn. The player can choose to destroy the droid, but this does not affect the game in any way.
