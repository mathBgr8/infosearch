Aaron Sinner
649

Aaron Sinner wrote the backstory of Rango Tel appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club. He also co-wrote the backstory of Romeo Treblanc. His alias is Master Ki-Aaron-Mundi on StarWars.com. - August 2005 (co-written by Benjamin Dawe) - September 2005 Category:What's The Story? authors
