Quantum computer
719

A quantum computer was a computer capable of generating a random number that was forty-seven digits long for use as a password. For further protection from slicing, one digit in the number would shift one value lower or higher every six standard hours. It was used to create a number that protected a personal folder of Atour Riten that contained the Death Star plans, which was also protected by a series of pyrowalls and military wards.Death Star *Death Star Category:Computer technology
