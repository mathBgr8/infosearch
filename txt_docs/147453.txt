Geridard Convalescent Center
525

Geridard Convalescent Center was an expensive retirement home on Geridard that provided life care. Sentients could apply to become a resident with a deposit in advance. Augusta I'att was to be admitted, though her execution at the hands of Moff Kerioth prevented that. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Buildings
