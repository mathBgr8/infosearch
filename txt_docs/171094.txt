Kimik
1429

The Kimik was a Nebulon-B2 frigate used by Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's forces to assault Emperor Palpatine's C-3 Passenger Liner Excalibur. With the aid of Maarek Stele and his Missile Boat, the Excalibur escaped into hyperspace, thwarting Zaarin's plans yet again. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter Category:Nebulon-B2 frigates Category:Starships of Zaarin's forces
