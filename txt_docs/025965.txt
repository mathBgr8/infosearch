E'koth Tassjun
2474

E'koth Tassjun was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order during the waning years of the Galactic Republic and he trained Ephaan Kenzon as a Padawan. A Force-sensitive, E'koth Tassjun was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. Selecting the young Initiate Ephaan Kenzon as his Padawan, Master Tassjun had difficulty controlling the unruly apprentice, often clashing on missions. Padawan Kenzon did not agree with the Jedi High Council's stance on several important political issues and frequently questioned their decisions in a very vocal manner. Master Tassjun as well as the Council, discussed the issue with Padawan Kenzon numerous times to no avail. Kenzon's disobedience proved to be too much for the Council to overlook and the Council of Reassignment discussed expelling the young man from the Order. The rift between the Council's views and Tassjun's Padawan came to a climax when the Master-Padawan pair were on a mission in the Zeemacht Cluster.
