IT-4 Warning Droid

Station Gamma |armament=None |equipment= |affiliation=Rak'qua |firstmade= |retired= }} The IT-4 Warning Droid was a type of modified DZ-70 fugitive tracker droid used during the Galactic Civil War. Droids of this kind was unarmed as it primarily filled a reconnaissance role. IT-4 Warning Droids were used by the Rak'qua on Station Gamma above Ord Mantell following the Battle of Yavin. In 1 ABY, a number of spacers who explored Station Gamma fought and destroyed multiple IT-4 Warning Droids. IT-4 Warning Droids appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
