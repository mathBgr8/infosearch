Cyax
1265

Cyax was the Cyax system's primary star. It was the brightest star in the skies of Varl, and early in the history of the Hutts, they created legends about it and its planets. When the Hutts began their expansion from Varl, they left the Cyax system alone, preferring to allow their myths to remain unchallenged. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Stars in Hutt Space
