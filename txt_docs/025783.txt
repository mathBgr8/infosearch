Arrow Books
763

Arrow Books is an imprint of Random House Publishers Group. They are responsible for Random House's paperback Star Wars fiction publications in the United Kingdom, Australia and New Zealand. Hardback publications are handled by Century, and factual books (such as The Making of Star Wars Revenge of the Sith) are published under the Ebury Press label.
