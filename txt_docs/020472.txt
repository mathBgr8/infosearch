Where Did All the Killiks Go?
1077

"Where Did All the Killiks Go?" was the signature song of Dengar and the Destroyers, a band which performed at the club Bantha Traxx on Lianna. "Where Did All The Killiks Go?" was first mentioned in the Star Wars Legends sourcebook Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy, which was written by Paul Danner and published in 1997. *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy Category:Songs
