Falung
1282

Falung was a Yuuzhan Vong female of the Priest caste. A Priestess of Yun-Harla, she was a part of Supreme Commander Zho Krazhmir's force which attacked the living world of Zonama Sekot. When Vergere offered to go with the Yuuzhan Vong, she became Falung's familiar. Falung taught Vergere much of the mythology of Yun-Harla, and Vergere linked herself to the Trickster.
