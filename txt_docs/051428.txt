War Day was a holiday of the Galactic Republic around the era of the Great Sith War. It involved the firing of old weapons into the air. The Battle of Coruscant took place shortly after 3996 BBY's War Day. *Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War 2 *Tyrant's Test Category:Galactic Republic holidays
