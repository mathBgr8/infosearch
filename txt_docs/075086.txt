Crimson Corridor
1671

The Crimson Corridor was a high-crime district in the Third Quadrant of Coruscant's Zi-Kree Sector. It was less than 10 kilometers from the Jedi Temple. One of the oldest areas of the city, the district contained the Follin Restaurant, the Tusken Oasis, the Green Glowstone Tavern, Barsoom Boulevard, and Lorn Pavan's apartment near one end, and was the main territory for the Raptors.
