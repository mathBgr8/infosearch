Shoengen Delta
608

The Shoengen Delta was the mouth of the Nihun River, which connected it to the Nomers Ocean, located on the planet of Brosi. The city of Shoengen was located on the delta itself. The delta was within the sphere of influence of the Brosin Underground around 2 ABY.Alliance Intelligence Reports *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Bodies of water Category:Brosi locations
