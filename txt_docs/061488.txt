Leia Organa's space cruiser
2491

This small space cruiser was used by the Rebel Alliance for general transportation. Princess Leia Organa used this cruiser to leave Yavin 4 in search of Luke Skywalker, who had gone missing while searching for a new location for the Rebel base and was eventually captured by Crimson Jack before she could find him. The vessel required a single pilot to be operated. thumb|left|150px|The ship as illustrated in issue 11. In Star Wars 10, this ship has certain design elements hinting at a Corellian Engineering Corporation origin.
