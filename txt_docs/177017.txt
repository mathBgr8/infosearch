Quenton
1802

Quenton was a commander aboard the Imperial Star Destroyer Chimaera, reporting directly to Captain Gilad Pellaeon. When Grand Admiral Thrawn returned, he eventually took the Chimaera to the planet Tatooine in an attempt to recover the painting Killik Twilight.
