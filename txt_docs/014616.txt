The planet was situated on the Celanon Spur hyperlane, which connected it to the Qiilura and Korvaii systems. Dor Nameth was the homeworld of Brangus Glee, an elder of a dexterous species of renowned travelers. By 12 ABY, the planet fell within the borders of the New Republic. Dor Nameth was created for a card of the 1998 Special Edition Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
