Cluster (Yuuzhan Vong)
480

A Cluster was a military unit among the Yuuzhan Vong Fleet, consisting of roughly 170 ships. Warmaster Tsavong Lah dispatched six clusters from Reecee as part of the fleet for Battle Plan Coruscant.
