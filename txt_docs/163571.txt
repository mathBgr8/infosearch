Vrortic cocktail
602

Vrortic cocktail was a Wookiee dish that combined various meats and layers of wroshyr leaves that had been soaked in potent Grakkyn nectar for weeks. Han Solo deemed it as a "safe" food, fit for Human consumption&mdash;as many Wookiee foods were spicy enough to cause damage to a Human's gullet. *Rebel Dawn Category:Wookiee food
