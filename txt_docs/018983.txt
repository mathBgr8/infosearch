Asanté Vos
1746

Asanté Vos was a female Kiffar, and cousin to Quinlan Vos. She was part of the plot to wipe the memories of Quinlan Vos and his Twi'lek Padawan Aayla Secura. To this end she personally stunned Aayla, and along with Pol Secura fed the two captives glitteryll. Asanté hoped that her part in this plot would give her the money and opportunities necessary to become something other than a Kiffu Guardian. Asanté met with Quinlan on Ryloth as he searched for his Padawan, and once again betrayed him.
