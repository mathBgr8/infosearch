Parck family
1310

A family was a military bloodline from Corulag with a tradition of naval service. Voss Parck was the most well-known member of the family, though he also had a cousin who achieved the rank of vice admiral within the Imperial Navy. Voss Parck's father and grandfather were also naval captains.
