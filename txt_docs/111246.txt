Cuddles
1801

Cuddles was Nightsister Silri's pet rancor. It was trained by Silri to work for the Zann Consortium and attacks only enemies, not Zann Consortium members. Cuddles later helped Tyber Zann, Urai Fen, and Silri raid Palpatine's Imperial Museum on Coruscant to retrieve an ancient Sith holocron stolen from the Zann Consortium by Bossk.
