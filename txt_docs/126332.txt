Calamarian C-tea
563

Calamarian C-tea was a type of tea brewed from plants found in the vast oceans of Mon Calamari. It found popularity with the crews of Mon Calamari Star Cruisers serving the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Mon Calamari culture Category:Teas
