Llez
1331

Llez was the son of Ambassador Zell of Majoor who purchased the droids R2-D2 and C-3PO. During his diplomatic mission to Armath, he ordered the droids to serve as tutors and companions to Llez. Llez had dreams of dismantling C-3PO and selling his parts, as well as using R2-D2's mechanical contraptions for entertainment. Spelled backwards, "Llez" is "Zell", the name of his father. *Droids (1986) 3 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Members of the unidentified Majoor species
