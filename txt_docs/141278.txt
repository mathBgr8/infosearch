Quentin Bartog
749

Quintin Bartog was a crime lord who once resided aboard Zirtran's Anchor in the Laughing Sullustan in Sector Delta. He disappeared mysteriously along with all other life forms aboard the station when it vanished mysteriously, only to reappear lightyears away.
