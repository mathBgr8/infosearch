Shoo bread
1848

Shoo bread was a type of bread found on the planet Coruscant. By 29 BBY,Rogue Planet mentions that Yoda and Obi-Wan Kenobi cooked shoo bread a few months prior to the novel's main events. Since The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Rogue Planet to 29 BBY, Yoda and Kenobi must have cooked shoo bread by that year. the alien Yoda and Human Obi-Wan Kenobi, who were members of the Jedi Order on Coruscant, cooked shoo bread and wurr over a charcoal fire in Yoda's quarters of the Jedi Temple.
