Star Wars Journal: Captive to Evil
7259

Star Wars Journal: Captive to Evil is a novel from the point of view of Princess Leia Organa, surrounding the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. Princess Leia makes regular entries in her data pad, a palm-sized computer hidden in her belt. In each entry she recalls all details of her intergalactic capture and rescue—and how she really feels about Han Solo and Luke.
