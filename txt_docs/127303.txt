Besnian sausage
1318

Besnian sausages were a delicacy from the Besnia system. Jason Fry, co-author of The Essential Atlas, stated his intention to create homeworlds for numerous species based on context implied from their names. Besnia appears to be one such case. often served with orange-Madeira sauce. Porcellus once served Jabba the Hutt such a dish. Category:Sausages
