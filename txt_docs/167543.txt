A Grand Admiral Returns
3189

"A Grand Admiral Returns" is a short story written by Bill Slavicsek, originally published in the Heir to the Empire Sourcebook and later archived on Hyperspace. The story is set 8 ABY. Captain Gilad Pellaeon awaits news from a mysterious contact that has ordered his Star Destroyer, Chimaera, to a meeting in space.
