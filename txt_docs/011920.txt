Z'trop was a tropical world with numerous volcanic islands. The wide beaches and clear waters of these islands made it a popular destination for romantic holidays. Trileaf trees grew on volcanic bluffs on the planet, and seaweed grew in the oceans, fed upon by the vegetarian septapus&mdash;seven tentacled creatures with five glowing eyes&mdash;which swam the oceans. Firmus Kett's family had a retreat on Z'trop. Following the kidnapping of Princess Leia Organa by Trioculus in 5 ABY, Han Solo and Chewbacca flew the Millennium Falcon to the planet for a vacation. Accompanying them were Leia Organa, Luke Skywalker, Ken Palpatine and their droids. However, their vacation was cut short when they discovered an unmanned Imperial Compact Assault Vehicle on a bluff overlooking their location. On investigation of some items inside, they realized it was affiliated with Scardia Station and the Prophets of the Dark Side.
