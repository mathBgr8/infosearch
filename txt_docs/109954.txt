Allynic E'Kles's holocron
606

Allynic E'Kles's holocron was a holocron made by Allynic E'Kles, a Caamasi Jedi Master. He stored his investigative knowledge and many memnii within the holocron, the latter of which could be accessed by telepathic Jedi. *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Jedi holocrons
