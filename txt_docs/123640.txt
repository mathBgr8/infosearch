Filve (CR90 corvette)
896

The Filve was a CR90 corvette under the service of the Rebel Alliance. The Imperial frigate Mayhem attempted to attack the Filve and the Bilbringi believing it to be a secret meeting between the Rebels and the Habassa. However, the Rebels leaked the information intending to capture the Mayhem.
