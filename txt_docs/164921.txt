Jonatha Ariadne Caspian
408

Jonatha Ariadne Caspian is a writer and game designer who wrote for a number of roleplaying game books from West End Games, including Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Classic Campaigns and Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition. Category:Role-playing game authors
