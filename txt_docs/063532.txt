Combat utility vehicle
2767

Combat Utility Vehicles were manufactured by BTS Enterprises and used by the Galactic Empire. During battles, these craft could reload the warhead armament of starfighters and Missile Boats and repair damaged warships. Each ship was 15 meters long and required a single pilot. The craft was equipped with a hyperdrive, two laser cannons, and two ion cannons; however, it lacked shields. In the original TIE Fighter combat chamber and missions, transports (TRN) and tugs were used as repair/rearm vehicles.
