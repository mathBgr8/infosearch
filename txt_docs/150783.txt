Barcarian claw salt
1095

Barcarian claw salt was a commodity from the planet Barcaria. It was said to resemble eyeballs, and had a particularly pungent aroma, one that was even stronger to the sensitive smell of Wookiees. Han Solo and Chewbacca were once running Barcarian claw salt when they were raided by the Natalon Core Bandits. *A Valentine Story Category:Salts
