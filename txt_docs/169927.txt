Salamander (frigate)
775

The Salamander was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that served with the New Republic Defense Fleet during the Galactic Civil War. The vessel was dispatched to the world of Krann in order to investigate a threat on the nova crystal mines located there. Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the New Republic
