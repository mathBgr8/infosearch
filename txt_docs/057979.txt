Hospitality Guide
807

Hospitality Guides were citizens of Kegan who helped introduce tourists and foreigners to the planet. Although Kegan was isolated for several years during the pre-Clone Wars era, the Hospitality Guides were key in introducing Obi-Wan Kenobi, Qui-Gon Jinn, Siri Tachi, and Adi Gallia to the planet of Kegan.
