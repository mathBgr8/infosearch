Polyhedron 90
1017

Polyhedron 90 was a roleplaying magazine published in December 1993. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The article was called The New Republic Campaign: The Battle for Endor from the PCs' Perspective and was written by Bill Slavicsek. Like all supplements printed in Polyhedron prior to 2002, the article is considered ambiguously canon. Category:Polyhedron
