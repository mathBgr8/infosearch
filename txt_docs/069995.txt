|date= |race= |creator= |found= |type=CheeseLords of the Expanse |description=Crimson-colored |animals= |plants= |other= |edible= |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} }} Neonan Red Cheese was a crimson cheese originating on the planet Neona. It was exported from the Tapani sector to the wider galaxy. Neonan red cheese was often served alongside soup and bread. The cheese was served aboard the luxury repulsortrain Gem of Alderaan and at the annual Vor-cal Feast held aboard the House Melantha Orbital Hunting Lodge over the planet Vycinyth. After the Battle of Yavin, the businessman Zoda Burb offered a container of Neonan red cheese to Brantlee Spondoon, the assistant to the Diktat of the Corellian sector Daclif Gallamby, in order to win favors from the Corellian government. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Cheeses Category:Tapani food
