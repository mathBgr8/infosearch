Tedevium
2987

The Tedevium was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in service to the Alliance to Restore the Republic that later served as a training ship for starfighter recruits, and was home to the Screaming Wookiee Squadron, a starfighter training squadron that fought as Gold Squadron. While a training vessel, the ship was commanded by Colonel Atton Repness. After Repness' exposure as a black marketer and thief at the hands of Lara Notsil the ship was placed under the command of General Edor Crespin and eventually converted back into a combat vessel for the New Republic. During the hunt for Zsinj, Tedevium served in General Han Solo's fleet as part of Group One.
