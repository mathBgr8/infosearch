|gender=Female |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Blue |skin=Blue |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Alliance to Restore the Republic **Alliance Intelligence |masters= |apprentices= }} Adery Shrial was a female Twi'lek spy who lived during the Galactic Civil War. Following the Battle of Yavin, she reside onboard Tansarii Point Station, a space station located above Ord Mantell, in the Bright Jewel system. During that time, she was a Rebel Alliance operative, collecting information regarding Imperial activity for Agent Garek. In 1 ABY, Garek encountered a spacer who was seeking to help the Rebellion. To evaluate this new recruit, the Rebel agent tasked the spacer to retrieve datadisks with sensitive information from Adery Shrial and Rund Jesela, another Rebel spy. Although the datadisks only contained Grandma Drake's soup recipes, the spacer achieved the objective successfully and was later assigned to a real mission. Adery Shrial appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Adery Shrial was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
