Galactic Bandits
616

Galactic Bandits was a holodrama that Luke Skywalker watched when he was a young boy. It featured wanted criminals from across the galaxy. One of the featured ones was Evet Scy'rrep. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *The Secrets of Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire Category:Holodramas
