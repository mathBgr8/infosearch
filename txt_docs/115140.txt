thumb|200px|NKik clan's sandcrawler. Nkik clan was a Jawa clan. Wimateeka was the leader of this clan. Het & Jek Nkik were also members of this clan. In 1 ABY the clan was attacked by Humans and aliens and several Jawas were killed. Wimateeka hired a well armed individual to destroy the base of their enemies. on Tatooine On another occasion, Wimateeka contracted a number of hunters to eliminate wild animals that were attacking his clan's supply of hubba gourds. on Tatooine At one time during the Galactic Civil War, the clan's sandcrawler accommodated an unidentified Rebel operative who learnt the art of droid crafting under the aegis of Wimateeka.Star Wars: DroidWorks *Star Wars: DroidWorks *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe Category:Jawa clans
