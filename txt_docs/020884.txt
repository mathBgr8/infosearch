Garqi Agricultural University
1569

}} Garqi Agricultural University, also known as Garqi Ag University or Garqi Ag, was the leading university on Garqi. It was located in the capital city of Pesktda, and was a center of anti-Imperial sentiment during the Galactic Civil War.
