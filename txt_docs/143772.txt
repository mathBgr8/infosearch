Priority Sector High Commander
1317

thumb|right|250px|Priority Sector High Commander rank insignia Priority Sector High Commander was a high rank in the Republic and Imperial Military. During the reorganization of the Imperial Military caused by the destruction of the Death Star over Yavin 4, the temporary rank insignia possessed six blue squares at the top, three red squares on the bottom left, and three orange squares on the bottom right, similar in appearance to one version of the Grand Moff plaque rank. The rank of Priority Sector High Commander was the highest given rank of the Imperial Sourcebook, although its exact powers were not specified. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Galactic Empire ranks Category:Republic Military ranks
