Senatorial Record Digest
565

Senatorial Record Digest was a publication sent out by the Senate cam droids to the personal computers of senators. The transcript summarized the hourly events of the day. It was also available as a durasheet hardcopy sold on the streets of Coruscant. *The Last of the Jedi: A Tangled Web Category:Publications and journals
