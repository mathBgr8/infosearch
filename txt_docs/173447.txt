Unidentified Hutt (Dantooine)
1267

This unidentified Hutt lived on the world of Dantooine during the Jedi Civil War. Calder Nettic was rumored to have had business ties with this Hutt prior to his death in 3956 BBY. His fate during the Sith raid is unknown. The existence of this particular Hutt is only hinted at within the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic through a remark made to the player character by Handon Guld.
