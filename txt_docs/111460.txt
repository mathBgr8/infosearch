Ybella Systems
673

Ybella Systems was a software company that produced the Ybella Systems "Starfighter Training" program, which helped inexperienced starfighter pilots learn how to pilot their ships. The Ybella Systems "Starfighter Training" program is an in-game help and manual for pilots in Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided. It is displayed in the Head-Up Display of starships and explains the basic handling and piloting of ships. Category:Computer and software companies
