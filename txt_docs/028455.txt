Battle of Dubrillion
4865

Returning from their Mission to Sernpidal, Yuuzhan Vong forces followed the Millennium Falcon and the other escaping spaceships, resulting in the Battle of Dubrillion. Lando Calrissian was alerted that the Vong forces were coming, and they prepared for battle. Jacen Solo, Jaina Solo, and Anakin Solo all flew modified TIE Advanced x1s, but were told to stay near Dubrillion's surface, under the shield protection of Belt-Runner I. Above, Kyp Durron, Han Solo, and Leia Organa Solo battled in the Millennium Falcon, along with the fighters that Calrissian had managed to recruit. However, Belt-Runner I was destroyed, taking away the shields of the Solo children. After fighting near the surface for a while, they fought upwards, drawing the Yuuzhan Vong force away from Dubrillion, and towards Lando's Folly. They wove in and out of the asteroids, using the Force to guide them, destroying much of the invasion force. This proved too much for Anakin Solo, who lost his concentration and made a blind hyperspace jump. Fortunately for him, his parents arrived at the system in his ship, Calrissian Runner, came out of hyperspace to bring him back.
