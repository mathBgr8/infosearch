Golden Dreams
705

Golden Dreams was a modified SoroSuub medium transport in Drea Renthal's pirate fleet. The ship was fairly large, but lightly shielded and poorly armed. Its role was to carry some of Renthal's Z-95 Headhunters into battle. Golden Dreams would launch the fighters, then fall back to a safe distance while they and the dedicated warships of Renthal's fleet did the fighting. *The Hutt Gambit Category:SoroSuub Corporation products Category:Starships of Drea Renthal's fleet Category:Transports
