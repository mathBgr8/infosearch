Candice Orwell
1119

Candice Orwell is an English actress/model who played the handmaiden Yané in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. She has been represented by London-based modeling agencies Storm Model Management and Reality Check Management. Orwell was present at the Collectormania 15 convention in 2009.
