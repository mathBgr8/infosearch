Fort Departure
628

Fort Departure was the only permanent settlement on the moon Vilhon orbiting the planet Vycinyth in the Tapani sector. It maintained a simple service spaceport and quarters. Vilhon was maintained as a hunting preserve and many expeditions began at Fort Departure (although there were several plains on the moon where ships could land).
