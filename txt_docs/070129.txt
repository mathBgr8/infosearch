thumb|right|280px|Caserta Palace thumb|right|280px|The interior of Caserta Palace in [[Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace|Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace]] The Caserta Palace (Italian: Reggia di Caserta), located at is a palace in Caserta, a town situated north-east of Naples. Its Palazzo Reale is the largest royal palace in Italy (and, by volume, in the world), designed by Luigi Vanvitelli and built for the Bourbon King Charles III in the 1750s. It boasts over a thousand rooms and the surrounding park is full of fountains and statuary. There is also an English Garden.
