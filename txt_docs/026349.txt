Wyndham Systems
664

Wyndham Systems was the manufacturer of the Razorshark. Wyndham Systems was mentioned in "Paid in Full," a roleplaying adventure written by Lester W. Smith and published in the forty-first issue of the Challenge magazine for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. Category:Shipwrights
