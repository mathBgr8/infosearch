Travale
2027

Travale was a male Human Senate Bureau of Intelligence agent during the last years of the Galactic Republic. Travale was not his true name, but rather a code name, as he had been inserted into the Confederate facility on the asteroid of Escarte near the end of the Clone Wars. He was blonde, fair-complexioned, wide-shouldered, and as tall as Anakin Skywalker. He helped Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi rescue the Bith Thal K'sar, and escape from the facility. He also taught Kenobi how to deactivate tractor beams, predicting that they were the wave of the future.
