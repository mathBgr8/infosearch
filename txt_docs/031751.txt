Jen (Human)
1497

Jen was a teenaged Human female from Sulon who joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, she was one of the twenty-six Rebel resistance soldiers that were led by Morgan Katarn and participated in the missile team during Jerec's attack.
