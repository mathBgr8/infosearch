Acid lizard
1269

Acid lizards were gigantic reptiles who dwelt underground on the planet Tatooine. They possessed a scaled purple hide, mottled with green spots. They are known as acid lizards due to the strong acid inside their large mouths. Gotar was an acid lizard.
