|class= |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin= |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Aggoron were a sentient species. Many Aggorons were traders, and&mdash;along with Devaronians, Humans, Nalroni, and Squibs&mdash;they formed part of the thousands of non-Gree who lived in Satikan, the largest city on Asation, which was the main trading post and access system to the Gree Enclave. The Aggoron were created by Timothy O'Brien for the article "The Gree Enclave," which was published in the magazine Star Wars Adventure Journal 8 in November 1995 by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
