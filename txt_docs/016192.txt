It was the endpoint of the Leisure Corridor, a trade and tourism route that started at the planet Coruscant in the Core Worlds. During the Galactic Civil War, between 0 ABY and 4 ABY, Ebiwaan was considered to be deep in Imperial Space. The Jesoni-class liner Culroon Minstrel operated on the Leisure Corridor from Coruscant to Ebiwaan. Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim originally suggested that Ebiwaan was situated in the Middle Rim.
