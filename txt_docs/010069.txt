Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 5
2435

Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 5 is the fifth issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in fall 1995. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 5, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with promotional trading cards for 1996 Topps Star Wars: Return of the Jedi Widevision, 1995 Topps Star Wars Master Visions, and 1995 Topps Star Wars Galaxy Series 3.
