Kejim Outpost
1642

thumb|left|150px|A schematic outline of the base. The Kejim Outpost was an Imperial Remnant research lab on the planet Kejim. The outpost was used to perform experiments intending to infuse select stormtroopers with Artusian crystals, resulting in the Reborn. This outpost was commanded by the Imperial Remnant officer Stavan and garrisoned with around 140 personnel. thumb|left|150px|left|These bridges were destroyed during the mission. Thought to be abandoned, Kyle Katarn and Jan Ors went to scout the area, but Mon Mothma played a transmission intercepted from the outpost which confirmed Imperial activity. During the subsequent mission, much of the outpost's staff was killed, and the base suffered considerable damage in some areas. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast Category:Imperial military bases
