VDV-100 Holo Display
1798

The VDV-100 Holo Display was an extremely powerful and compact holographic comlink that could receive transmissions from the opposite side of the galaxy. It could also receive HoloNet transmissions. It had security features such as blocking specific holo signals or identification of incoming signals. It could also display an away message and record short messages. The unique feature of the VDV-100 was the ability to match incoming audio transmissions with a pre-recorded holographic image of the caller.
