It was the outermost celestial body in the Beheboth system, a star system that was located in the Sanbra sector, a part of the Trailing Sectors in the Outer Rim Territories. Like its inner neighbor Derenath, Omdrid was classified as an ice planet and had no moons. Omdrid was mentioned in "Return to Prosperity," the first article in the Planet Hoppers series Beheboth: Blood and Water. The article was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on June 3, 2004. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Beheboth system, and therefore Omdrid, in grid square O-17.
