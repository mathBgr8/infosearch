|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand |issue=6 |next=last }} Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 6 was the sixth and final issue in the Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand series of comics. It was released on February 3, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. Mara Jade has destroyed many enemies of the Empire in her time, and has always carried out her missions with forethought and skill. But an unforeseen variable is about to enter the scene of her final mission for her dead employer, and Mara knows that her own survival is second to getting the job done!
