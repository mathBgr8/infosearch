|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=22 BBYThe events of Republic 59 through Republic 68 were originally set roughly sixteen months after the start of the Clone Wars, but with the continuity changes from Star Wars: The Clone Wars, both the Battle of Jabiim from Republic 58 and the Battle of Rendili from Republic 69 have been moved to 22 BBY by The Essential Reader's Companion and The Official Star Wars Fact File (2014). |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=64 }} Republic 64 is the sixty-fourth issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. The comic is presented in reverse chronological order. It was published on April 28, 2004 by Dark Horse Comics. As the fires of revolution sweep across the galaxy, it's impossible to gauge the number of personal tragedies caused by the Clone Wars. But some tragedies are more calculated than others, and when they result in the deaths of a Jedi Master and his Padawan, they deserve a closer look. Jedi Ronhar Kim strikes up a friendship with a rising politician, never dreaming that, years later, the relationship will make him the target of one of the greatest evils the galaxy has ever seen!
