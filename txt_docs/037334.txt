Construction droid foreman
804

The construction droid foreman was a droid model used c. 3951 BBY. It was more dexterous than the construction droid Mark I or construction droid Mark II, having five arms, and was apparently used to supervise the AI of the lesser droids. A typical construction team consisted of one foreman, one Mark II, and multiple Mark I droids. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Construction droid models
