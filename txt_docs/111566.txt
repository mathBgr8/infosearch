Corellian spark grenade
520

Corellian spark grenades were an improvised explosive that caused the conductors in droids and other technological devices to short circuit. In 15 ABY, Han Solo and Chewbacca created one out of a power source and a twisted-pair det to fry Perek's technobeasts. Category:Grenades
