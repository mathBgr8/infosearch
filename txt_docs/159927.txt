Ull
2374

Ull was a Strike-class medium cruiser that served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The warship attempted to destroy the Rebel Alliance Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser Karei, sending out its complement of TIE Avengers, from Avenger Squadron and Talon Squadron, as well as Assault Gunboats for backup. At the same time, the Dreadnaught deployed its own squadrons of X-wings and A-wings to defend itself, as well as B-wing starfighters serving as backup.
