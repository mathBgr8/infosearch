Imperial machinist
585

Imperial machinists were specialized Imperials that were trained to handle and maintain machines and vehicles such as Juggernauts. Some were stationed on the planet Dathomir, where a Juggernaut's drive shaft snapped. Once they requested replacement parts, they were often ignored and wouldn't receive them until after many days. *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Imperial combat driver variants
