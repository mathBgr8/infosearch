Ekisp
1039

Ekisp was a Human male who worked as a servant for wealthy industrialist Bohhuah Mutdah and Mutdah's private estate on Oseon 5792 during the Imperial Period. An elderly man with a long, white beard by 3 BBY, one of Ekisp's duties was reading to Mutdah from his employer's extensive library. *Lando Calrissian and the Flamewind of Oseon Category:Centrans Category:Males Category:Servants
