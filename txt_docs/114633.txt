Quillion
1782

The Quillion was a Republic Sienar Systems Marauder-class corvette in the Kathol Republic Navy. In 8 ABY, the Quillion responded to a distress call from the Bravado III, alongside her sister ships Juno II, Mirandine, Siroma, Solidarity, and Triumph.
