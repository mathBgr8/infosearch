Released sometime prior to the end of the Clone Wars, which concluded in 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology the Plevitz Essential Guide to Species was written by the professional author Plevitz, who had been hired by the anthropologistThe Essential Guide to Alien Species Mammon Hoole to do so. The reference literature included an entry on the Zeltrons, a carefree Humanoid sentient species from the planet of Zeltros, who were known for their red skin and ability to love other beings unreservedly. In the entry, Plevitz stated that Zeltrons could release potent pheromones and sense the emotions of other sentients. The entry noted that while the Zeltron species lacked concern for any military defense of their homeworld, they generally kept themselves physically fit, and thus could become fierce and violent warriors if they found no other way to show their passion. Plevitz Essential Guide to Species was first mentioned in the comic One of a Kind&mdash;published in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 8 on June 20, 2007&mdash;where the reference literature is quoted multiple times over the course of the comic's story. The Plevitz Essential Guide to Species was later mentioned in Plevitz's entry in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia, published December 9, 2008. Category:In-universe guides and reference books
