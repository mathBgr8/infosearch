Orange frilled mushroom
514

Orange-frilled mushrooms were native to the planet Felucia. This mushroom grew to be at least fifteen feet tall. It was a partially-transparent orange color and it had transparent vines hanging off its dome. It lived among many other types of fungi on Felucia. *Star Wars: Battlefront II Category:Fungi of Felucia Category:Mushrooms
