Shree-class battle cruiser
3569

The Shree-class Battle Cruiser was a central warship class of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium fleet, deployed as a command ship in invasions and space battles. thumb|left|Schematics. It was 900 meters long was armed with the Ssi-Ruuvi equivalent of 24 heavy turbolasers, 24 ion cannons, 12 concussion missile launchers, 12 tractor beam projectors, thus making it a formidable warship. The battle cruiser also carried 500 Swarm-class battle droids. Since Ssi-Ruuk place a very high priority on capturing enemy ships, they often used the battle cruiser's tractor beam projectors and ion cannons to capture enemy ships which they dragged into huge landing bays. They then captured the crew of the ships and used a process called entechment to draw the life forces out of their captives and then transfer them into their droids, ships and computers. Most vessels of that class had one large entechment lab for enteching prisoners and giant batteries for storing these enteched energies. Battle cruisers also had five meter tall ceilings since the Ssi-Ruuk were very tall. There were also several tunnels and passage ways for the P'w'ecks, a species conquered by the Ssi-Ruuk, to get around the ship and do their duties. Since the P'w'ecks could not be trusted, battle cruisers had stun traps to ensure that the P'w'ecks followed their orders and keep out of the ship's sensitive areas. These stun traps could cause terrible pain to the P'w'ecks and kill a Human.
