Mitt'swe'kleoni
1531

Mitt'swe'kleoni, also known by his core name of Tswek, was a Chiss Aristocra who led a delegation to the Galactic Alliance in 35 ABY, to protest to Cal Omas and the Masters' Council about the Jedi survivors of the Myrkr strike team allying with the Killiks. Gray-haired and venerable, he was apparently older than Aristocra Chaf'orm'bintrano, and is thus likely to have been born no later than around 45 BBY. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Aristocra of the Chiss Ascendancy Category:Chiss Category:Males
