Mukit
1264

Mukit was an Ubese mercenary who had been hired by the Mandalorian Ghez Hokan to work with him and several other mercenaries on the planet Qiilura. After Cailshh's execution by Hokan for destroying farms with little to no provocation, Hokan ordered Mukit to bury Cailshh in the proper Ubese way. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Mercenaries Category:Ubese
