Bew'a
2334

The Bew'a was an Immobilizer 418 cruiser belonging to the Galactic Empire. The Bew'a was part of the Imperials' efforts to stop the Rebel Alliance cruiser Link from alerting the Rebel Fleet at Sullust, which suffered an engine failure.
