Lanaxa Tark
2129

Lanaxa Tark was the wife of the Imperial Lieutenant Boes Tark and, in 23 BBY,Wanted by Cracken, which notes Moxin Tark's age as thirty, is set three years after the Battle of Endor, which the The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. Wanted by Cracken must therefore be set in 7 ABY and Moxin's birth year must be 23 BBY. they had a son named Moxin on the planet Bandonia. They raised Moxin on the Tark family estate in a life of luxury until, at age eighteen, he ran away to pursue adventures in space, eventually becoming a bounty hunter.Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Lanaxa Tark was first mentioned in Wanted by Cracken, Louis J.
