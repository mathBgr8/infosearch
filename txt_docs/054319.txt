Valrond
857

Captain Valrond was an alias created by Han Solo to match the false ID transponder code that the Millennium Falcon was using to identify the ship as the Victory Ring. During the mission to Ord Mantell, Leia Organa also used the Valrond alias, as Han had never specified a gender for the captain. *Rebel Mission to Ord Mantell *Millennium Falcon Owner's Workshop Manual *The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Category:Aliases, nicknames, and pseudonyms
