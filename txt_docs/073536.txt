Fond Memory
1558

The Fond Memory was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle in the service of the New Republic around 25 ABY. Several months after the Battle of Helska IV it ferried Leia Organa Solo and Elegos A'Kla from Coruscant to Agamar and Dubrillion. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Lambda-class T-4a shuttles Category:Starships of the New Republic
