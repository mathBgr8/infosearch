Aeramaxis Technologies
1357

Aeramaxis Technologies was a small manufacturing company that produced personal equipment. The company's products included the Evasive-13 individual field disruptor, the Evasive-226-R, and the PDW-50 Ionic Tingler. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Personal gear companies
