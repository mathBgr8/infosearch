Majestic (Imperial-class)
2551

Majestic was an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer that served as Emperor Palpatine's private flagship in the months following the Battle of Hoth. At some point after the Clone Wars, it was built over Kuat at Kuat Drive Yards. A couple of months before the Battle of Endor, Grand Admiral Zaarin managed to intercept and damage this vessel with the Emperor aboard in an attempted coup of the Empire.
