Satellite Liberator
1618

The Satellite Liberator was a Bendine XR12 starship in operation at some point prior to the Invasion of Naboo. It was slightly smaller than an average escape pod. The capsule-shaped Satellite Liberator was junked, finding its way into the backyard of the Mos Espa junk shop owned by the Toydarian Watto. *Inside the Worlds of Star Wars: Episode I *Star Wars: Complete Locations Category:XR12
