Elpur
1886

Elpur was the first planet within the Ropagi system, situated in the Kira sector of the Expansion Region. It was a desolate searing rock.Twin Stars of Kira Elpur was created for the West End Games supplement book Twin Stars of Kira as part of Ropagi II article written by John Terra. While the planet log in spelled the planet's name "Elpur", the describing text a page before spelled it "Elpoor". *Twin Stars of Kira *Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Planets in the Expansion Region Category:Planets in the Trailing Sectors Category:Ropagi system locations
