Kalaan City
1003

Kalaan City was a major city on the planet of Kalaan and home to a major Rebel Alliance base before 0 BBY. A bustling city of domed houses and rounded architecture, it boasted a starport and was flanked by large statues of Kalaanites. It was also the site of the Battle of Kalaan, during which it was completely razed by Imperial forces. *Star Wars: Force Commander Category:Cities
