Cobalt Station
1255

Cobalt Station was a Galactic Republic fortification on Jabiim during the Battle of Jabiim in the Clone Wars. It was located just north of Monsoon Mesa. During this battle, Commander Anakin Skywalker brought much-needed convoy of materials and reinforcements to this base.
