David Manak
3070

}} David (Dave) Manak is a comic book writer and artist. He wrote every issue of the Marvel Comics/Star Comics Ewoks comic book series, and all but one issue of the Droids series. In his almost three decades at Marvel, Manak worked on a number of comic books, such as the Rocky and Bullwinkle, Sonic the Hedgehog Comic Books and Weird War series.
