Aiwha Squad
2386

Aiwha Squad was an elite clone commando unit, and was part of the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. This unit was named after the native Aiwhas of Kamino. During a skirmish with the Separatist Droid Army, the Republic accidentally bombed a farmhouse on Garqi. Aiwha Squad was ordered to search for women and children and to bring them back to a refugee camp. While searching a building, Zag found a little boy named Evan. Evan was forcibly escorted by the Aiwhas. When he tried and run away, the little boy was attacked by a homing spider droid, but Sarge, the leader of the squad, threw a rock at the droid, and the rest of the commandos ambushed it. Going to the refugee camp, the commandos dropped Evan off, and conversed with Jedi General Traavis. thumb|left|200px|Aiwha Squad executing [[Order 66/Legends|Order 66.]] At that moment, Order 66 was issued, and the commandos were forced to kill the Jedi General in front of the little boy.
