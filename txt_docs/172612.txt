Grom 2
827

Grom 2 was an Airam Assassin-class corvette, which together with its sister ship, Grom 1, accompanied the Dreadnaught Anat on its ill-fated final trip near the Blair cluster. Upon being pulled from hyperspace by the Empire's police action in the form of Star Destroyer Rage and Interdictor Compellor, Grom 2 was ordered to engage.
