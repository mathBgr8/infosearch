Jeweled Lizard
1186

The Jeweled Lizard was a constellation visible from Commenor. It was shaped as a lizard, complete with a tail that comprised five stars. A cloaked asteroid used by the Galactic Alliance during the Battle of Commenor was detected when one of the stars in the Jeweled Lizard was blocked from the view of a patrolling BTL-S8 K-wing assault starfighter. *Legacy of the Force: Fury *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Constellations
