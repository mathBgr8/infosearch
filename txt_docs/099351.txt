Nessem Shipyards
646

Nessem Shipyards was a large company that specialized in the production of large starships. The company was headquartered on a large space station above the planet Nessem. The shipyards were so large that residence halls for the workers on the station appeared as small towns. Category:Lostar sector locations Category:Orbital shipyards Category:Shipyard companies
