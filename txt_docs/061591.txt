Gogol (patron)
3061

Gogol was a male humanoid criminal who haunted the Dor on Coruscant in the final years of the Galactic Republic, before the Clone Wars. Gogol sold Obi-Wan Kenobi information about the slave-raider Krayn's dealings with the Colicoids, about which ties the Jedi Master had been suspicious since Krayn's attack on a Colicoid cargo ship that Kenobi and his Padawan were escorting, and its captain's rage at Krayn, as if the assault and resultant offense taken had been of a personal nature. Kenobi learned from Gogol that the Colocoids had recently and secretly taken over the Kessel mines and that they were planning a total usurpation of the spice trade, including of its processing factories run by Krayn on Nar Shaddaa, the "Smuggler's Moon"—operations made possible by Krayn's seemingly inexhaustible supply of slaves. Gogol appears to be of the same species as Fay and Princess Lourdes. All these individuals appear to be Human, but with pointy ears.
