Matt Haley
863

Matt Haley is a creative consultant and comic-book artist. His first piece of Star Wars-related art was for the Star Wars Galaxies II trading card set for Topps, depicting Luke Skywalker at Darth Vader's funeral pyre at the end of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. Recently, he provided storyboards for LucasArts' Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith Xbox video game and television commercials, as well as sketchcards for the Episode III Topps trading card set.
