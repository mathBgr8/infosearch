Dorn Tlo
1480

Dorn Tlo was a famous Kel Dor Baran Do Sage Master who contacted the New Jedi Order in 30 ABY and sent them old Republic-era records from the Jedi to attest to the Baran Do's long history. He would later speak of the Baran Do's belief that the dark side of the Force was a necessary evil and that following the Potentium's views could be dangerous for the Order and the galaxy.
