Plasma thrower

A plasma thrower was a device similar to a blaster that fired explosive bolts of plasma. It was used by clone blaze troopers. A much more powerful version known as a plasma burst cannon was used by SD-10's at the Battle of Balmorra. *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:Plasma weaponry
