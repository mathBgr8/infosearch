Xris established Sordaan Xris' Safari Partners and what became known as the Rodian Hunting Grounds, which offered big-game hunting on the Wookiee homeworld. He used the Bocctyyy sub-region of Etyyy as his personal hunting grounds, and often made wagers with fellow hunters pertaining to feats performed within Bocctyyy.
