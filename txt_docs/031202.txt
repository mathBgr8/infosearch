Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back (Second Edition)
34224

The second edition of Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back is a supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, published by West End Games in 1996. Based on the First Edition written by Michael Stern, this edition was revised and expanded by Pablo Hidalgo. The guide features character profiles with game stats for main and some secondary characters from the film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. This book supplies Expanded Universe backgrounds for many characters appearing in the film. This edition expands upon characters only mentioned in the previous edition, and includes a new adventure as the last chapter of the guide. It is illustrated with photos from the film, replacing the artwork from the previous edition. A short story from the first edition, Little Girl Lost, is not included here. Galaxy Guide 3 is the comprehensive guide to Episode V of the Star Wars saga, compiling information and facts from the movie, radio play, novels, and comics. You'll find within these pages a definitive collection of backgrounds, statistics, and information on the heroes and villains from The Empire Strikes Back, many of whom make their first appearance in a roleplaying supplement.
