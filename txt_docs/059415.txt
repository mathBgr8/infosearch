Ta'a Chume's consort
1326

Ta'a Chume's consort was the father of Kalen and Isolder. Described by Ta'a Chume as adventurous, he once took Isolder camping on Dreena. He died while his children were small. Ta'a Chume's consort was first mentioned in The Courtship of Princess Leia, a 1994 Star Wars Legends novel written by Dave Wolverton. *The Courtship of Princess Leia *The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey Category:Hapan royals Category:Males
