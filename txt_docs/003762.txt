Target Identification Network
1001

Target Identification Network, or TIN, was a sophisticated surveillance system and combat package based on those used in assassin droids. Using a system of piercing targeting lasers, TIN projected a sensor bubble ranged at 30 meters around the vehicle it was mounted. Combining advanced audio listening antennae with thermosensors, TIN was able to offer vehicle crews a precise picture of the battlefield. The most useful application for wise vehicle commanders was to identify a target with TIN that would maximize psychological impact while minimizing physical destruction.
