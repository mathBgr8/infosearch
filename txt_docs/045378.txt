Spare Parts (disambiguation)
544

Two unrelated stories entitled "Spare Parts" have been written: *"Spare Parts," a short story written by Pablo Hidalgo included in Star Wars Adventure Journal 11 and on the Fiction section of the Hyperspace website *"Spare Parts," a four-page comic written by Mark Evanier and Sergio Aragonés included in Star Wars Tales 4
