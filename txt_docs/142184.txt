Barthos Okeefe
1494

Lord Barthos Okeefe was a Brentaalan Human of the prominent House Okeefe merchant house. He had a long and successful career as a financier and director of Brentaal's orbital HoloNet relay station, then retired around the age of sixty to a planetside resort villa on the north pole.
