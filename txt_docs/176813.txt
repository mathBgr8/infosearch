Baltke
1931

Baltke was a male Chiss commander of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Fleet who served as a battle surgeon and interrogator. During the Swarm War, he interrogated Jedi Knight Leia Organa Solo about her mission with her husband Han Solo, and the New Jedi Order's plans for the war.
