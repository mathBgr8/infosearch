Brine-eel
1232

Brine-eels were a large non-sentient species of eel that lived in salt water. A species of eel, brine-eel had powerful jaws and were used in warfare. In 23 ABY when Jedi apprentice Jaina Solo heard a whining, gnawing sound that was an Imperial Assault Shuttle breaching the hull of GemDiver Station; she likened the sound to a huge battle-ready brine-eel chewing its way through the station's armor. *Young Jedi Knights: Shadow Academy Category:Eels Category:Non-sentient species Category:Predatory creatures
