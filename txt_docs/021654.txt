Team Ord Mantell
2005

|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Cup of Limmie }} Team Ord Mantell was a limmie team that played for the planetThe Essential Guide to Planets and Moons of Ord Mantell in the Galactic Cup of Limmie. In 13:3, during the Separatist Crisis, the planet of Kothlis's team defeated them with a single point in the Cup's opening round. An article published in the CoCo District edition of the HoloNet News covered the result of the match. Team Ord Mantell was mentioned in Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
