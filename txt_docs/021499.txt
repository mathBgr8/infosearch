In 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:3:14, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. Team Fondor competed in the Galactic Cup of Limmie, where they were matched against the inexperienced team of the planet Coruscant in the opening round. When Fondor ultimately won three to one at the Coruscanti home stadium, the BlasTech Gikosphere, the Coruscant team's captain Jipoly Numifolis expressed their dissatisfaction with the tournament's result in "Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round," a news story published in CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. Team Fondor was mentioned in "Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round," an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002. The article includes an illustration drawn by Joe Corroney depicting Fondor's game against Coruscant. Although one of the two teams is visible, it is not clear which.
