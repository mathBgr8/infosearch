|pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=3 }} Star Wars Tales 3 is the third issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. It was published on March 29, 2000 by Dark Horse Comics. The Galaxy's greatest comics anthology features the world's greatest comics creators and their spins on the Star Wars saga, both within and beyond the continuity! Lando Calrissian gambles for an immense prize: an entire city! Jar Jar Binks discovers that the bonds of friendship can only be stretched so far.
