Lance (CR90 corvette)
1130

thumb|right|250px|The Lance on patrol with the Defiance Task Force Lance was a CR90 corvette in the service of the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. At the time of the Battle of Hoth, it was part of the Defiance Task Force, and after a brief campaign escaped the Anoat sector along with the rest of the task force. It carried a cargo of thermal units, likely either bound for Echo Base or being evacuated from there. Her exploits following the reconstitution of the Defiance Task Force are unknown, and it is likewise unknown whether or not the vessel participated in the Battle of Endor. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Defiance Task Force ships
