Seed Machees
1556

Seed Machees was a male Gran who worked for Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Coruscant Police for questioning, as well as being involved in the smuggling of death sticks on Malastare shortly after the Uprising on Naboo.
