Figg family
1375

The Figg family consisted of hyperspace explorer Ecclessis Figg, his Alderaanian wife Yarith, and their descendants. The family founded the Figg conglomerate prior to the exploration of the Greater Javin, and maintained control over its holdings through the Galactic Civil War. Category:Families
