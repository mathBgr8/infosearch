Devers (Rebel Alliance)
1653

Devers was a male Human who served as an officer in the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War, having achieved the rank of Commander. He was the tactical officer stationed aboard the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Defiance, under the command of Admiral Nammo while the warship was on the run from the Imperial Navy after the Battle of Hoth. Specifically, the vessel was being hunted by Admiral Garreth Holtz. Olin Garn, in order to recruit Ace Azzameen into the Alliance as a starfighter pilot, had to first get approval from Devers about the young man.
