Silviut Corporation
1553

Silvuit Corporation was the manufacturer of the Svelte-class Imperial shuttle and the Vibre-class assault cruiser. Silvuit Corporation was a minor starship manufacturer for the Galactic Empire and often worked as subcontractors with Sienar Fleet Systems on projects such as the Lambda-class T-4a shuttle.
