The TIE fighter was active around 0 BBY.The A New Hope expansion of the Star Wars Trading Card Game takes place around the time of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. The "DS-73-5" name was coined by Wizards of the Coast in their 2003 Battle of Yavin expansion of the Star Wars Trading Card Game. DS-73-5's card uses an image&mdash;taken from the 1977 original trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope&mdash;of a TIE fighter that was previously identified as "Black 4" in the 1996 A New Hope Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game. This article treats the TIE fighter in the Star Wars Trading Card Game DS-73-5 card image as a misidentified Black 4, and further avoids linking the DS-73-5 name to any of the onscreen TIE fighters in the 1977 film.
