Martin Wixted
490

Martin Wixted was the author of Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley. He also wrote two articles that appeared in the science fiction magazine Challenge, "HoloNet Waystation" (in #74) and "New Character Templates: A Supplement to Star Wars' Mos Eisley Galaxy Guide" (in #73), the second of which was an addendum to Galaxy Guide 7.
