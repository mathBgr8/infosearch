Jhank Shel
703

Jhank Shel was a Hutt kajidic. The clan controlled the planet Lirra until the Galactic Empire, led by Moff Heedra, drove them off. The Jhank Shel clan had originally taken control of Lirra in approximately 100 BBY when the Hutts moved enough of their slaves to the planet to such a point that they outnumbered the original colonists. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Kajidics
