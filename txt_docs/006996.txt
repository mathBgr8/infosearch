*The SliceThe Client States and Miscellaneous Regions map in The Essential Atlas places the entirety of grid square P-14 in the Slice. |sector=Thaere sector |system=Cularin system |coordinates=P-14 |xyz= |routes= |distance= |orbits= #Acilaris #Cularin #*Rennokk #*Tilnes #Genarius #*Ulbasca #*Eskaron #*Uffel #*Ostfrei #Cularin asteroid belt #Almas #*Dorumaa #Morjakar #Cularin comet cloud |stations= |asteroids= |other= |class=White dwarf / Binary star |diameter= }} Termadus was the white dwarf companion to the main sequence star Morasil in the Cularin system. Termadus was a much younger star than its counterpart. A gaseous haze arose between the two due to Termadus drawing gases from Morasil's corona. Termadus, Morasil, and their haze gave off a combined light equivalent to a single bright star.
