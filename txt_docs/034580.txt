Lonn Idd's repair station
1842

Lonn Idd's repair station was the space station headquarters of Lonn Idd, who concentrated on repairing droids. In this station, Tig Fromm tried to steal a powerful laser from Idd, but this attempt was stopped by Idd's children Vik and Nikki with help of C-3PO and R2-D2. This space station was introduced in Droids (1986) 2, a comic issue released in 1986 by Marvel Comics, where it appears unnamed. *Droids (1986) 2 Category:Deep space stations
