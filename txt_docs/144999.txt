Hammerhead starship
1382

thumb|Hammerhead starship The Hammerhead starship was a class of ship used by the Galactic Republic during the Mandalorian Wars. In 3963 BBY, at least two of them were present in the Republic fleet that gathered over Ralltiir, and one of them was seen at Camp Three on the planet Serroco, shortly before the Battle of Serroco. The size and function of the Hammerhead starship is not made clear in its appearances. While the ship is evidently smaller than the similarly-designed Hammerhead-class cruiser.
