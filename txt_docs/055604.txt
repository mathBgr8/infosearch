Negra Star
1190

Negra Star was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate which served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Some captured Sullustan technical staff were being transferred to the Negra Star and the Nebulon-B frigate Warspite for transportation to Coruscant. The Rebel Alliance mounted a rescue operation and sent a lone Y-wing, flown by Keyan Farlander, to identify which transports were carrying the Sullustans and to disable them.
