C-5MO
1260

C-5MO was a silver protocol droid that served as a clerk in a droid shop on the Imperial Remnant capital of Bastion. When trying to escape Imperial forces, C-5MO gave Lando Calrissian and Han Solo permission to use several astromech droids to transmit multitonal signals to jam Imperial frequencies. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Protocol droids
