Vibrospade
891

A vibrospade, or vibroshovel, was a digging tool with an internal generator that made the blade vibrate rapidly in the same manner as a vibroblade. This allowed the tool to more easily dig through hard-pack gravel, sand, or dirt. *Galaxy of Fear: The Doomsday Ship *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back, Second Edition Category:Digging tools
