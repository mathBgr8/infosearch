Turumme-warrens
518

Turumme-warrens were large banks of apartment quarters built below the surface of the planet Adumar. These structures were usually owned by and built on top of underground manufacturing plants and were used to house the plants' workers. Turumme-warrens got their name from the Turumme, a reptile that dug elaborate nests in the ground. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Residential buildings
