*Icebreakers |equipment= |affiliation=G0-T0 |firstmade= |retired= }} The Sentry droid Mark I was a floating, spherical droid model equipped with a blaster. Others had flamethrowers and icebreakers, and were identical to G0-T0. G0-T0 used many of them as guards on his yacht, the Visionary.
