Green Forge
901

Green Forge was a group of environmental terrorists who were active in the years preceding the Battle of Naboo. The group was known for their tactics of destroying headquarters or refineries of companies that they felt had committed environmental crime. A notable member of the group was Buck, who would have affairs with wealthy women and use them to achieve his ends. *Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Political factions
