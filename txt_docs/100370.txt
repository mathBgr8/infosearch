Opdoa
958

Opdoa was a male Rodian who lived during the Galactic Civil War. A member of the Goa-Ato, Opdoa served on a committee that matched hunters with bounties. It was he and Nilesk who sent Fask Eelak of the An'yettu clan to apprehend the Wookiee Gerraba. Category:Bounty hunters Category:Males Category:Rodians
