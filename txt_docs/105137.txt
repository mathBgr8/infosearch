Q-E
1823

Q-E was a red Model E, a nanny droid who ran a nursery on Kalarba along with his companions 2-E and U-E. At some point prior to the Galactic Civil War, they were ordered by a townsman named Vuldo to build illegal blasters, but the droids questioned his intentions. Vuldo responded by shooting U-E with a blaster. Q-E and 2-E then received help from C-3PO, R2-D2, and Nak Pitareeze and managed to expose Vuldo's scheme, and also save U-E since they had managed to salvage his cogitative theory unit and memory banks beforehand and thus, R2-D2 was able to install them on the body of a labor droid that had been constructed by Nak. *Droids (1994) 5 *The Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droids with masculine programming Category:Model E baby-sitting droids
