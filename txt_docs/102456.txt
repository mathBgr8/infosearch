Hunchuzuc Den
847

The Hunchuzuc Den was a Selonian den dedicated to ending the rule of the Overden over Selonia. After being tricked into submitting to the Overden, they learned the truth of who truly controlled Selonia's Planetary repulsor and gained more power than before due to the Overden's lies and shame. Its activities were watched by the New Republic Observer Fulin Gor, who watched for any violations in New Republic edicts during their rise to power. *Assault at Selonia *Showdown at Centerpoint *Cracken's Threat Dossier *Star Wars Encyclopedia *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Selonian dens
