Adrick Tolliver
1293

Adrick Tolliver is the author of Death in the Slave Pits of Lorrd, a piece of short fiction appearing on StarWars.com. Tolliver also wrote the backstory of 3B6-RA-7 and co-wrote the backstory of Janu Godalhi appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. His alias is TalonCard86 on StarWars.com and TalonCard on TheForce.net's Jedi Council Forums as well as Wookieepedia.
