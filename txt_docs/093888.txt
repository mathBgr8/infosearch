|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation=Red IaroStar Wars (1998) 15 }} Palesia was a territory on the planet Lannik. During the conflict between the Red Iaro terrorist organization and the Lannik government, the terrorists captured the region from loyalist forces, but offered to cede the territory in Jedi-mediated peace talks on the Mid Rim planet Malastare.Star Wars (1998) 13 Red Iaro representative Myk'chur Finux Zug's negotiation with lands legally belonging to the Lannik crown outraged Lannik Prince R'cardo Sooflie IX, who took offense and discontinued negotiations. HoloNet News journalists reported from Palesia on the discovery that successful pugil athlete Rolsat Noviee, supposedly Lannik, was in fact a Clawdite changeling. *Star Wars (1998) 15 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Lannik Space locations
