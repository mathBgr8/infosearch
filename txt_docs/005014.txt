Assault Transport (Empire)
2295

The Assault Transport was a troop transport vessel used by the Imperial Navy. The Assault Transport was a large troop carrier that was armed with multiple laser cannons, had stronger shields than a Carrack-class light cruiser and a relatively fast hyperdrive.
