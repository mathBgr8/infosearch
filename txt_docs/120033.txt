Horned priest
1262

The horned priest was one of the High Elders of the Order of the Sacred Circle on Monastery. He accompanied the green priest and Domina Tagge to Yavin 4 to bring Luke Skywalker to Monastery. The horned priest was a humanoid with large ears, a short snout, and an overall somewhat feline face.
