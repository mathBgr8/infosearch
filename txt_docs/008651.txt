It was written by Mary Jo Duffy, illustrated by Cynthia Martin, and first published on February 12, 1985 by Marvel Comics. The issue features the story "No Zeltrons," which culminates in a climactic duel between Luke Skywalker and the Dark Lady Lumiya. There's a new Dark Lord in the galaxy, and she's out for Luke's head!
