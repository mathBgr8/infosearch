Corellian Masternav, Incorporation
1184

Corellian Masternav, Incorporation was a Corellia based company that was renowned for producing highly accurate astrogation maps. They used droidships to conduct survey missions. App. 1 year after the Battle of Yavin a Corellian Meatlump gang started firing warning shots at Masternav's astrogation surveyor ships, followed by extortion attempts, demanding payments to "protect" Masternav's ships. In the absence of adequate police involvement, Masternav commissioned an independent agent to destroy the gangster base and protect their business. *Secrets of the Sisar Run *Suns of Fortune Category:Companies Category:Corellian organizations
