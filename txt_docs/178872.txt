|outcome= |keyparties= |participants=Jawas }} }} A swap meet was a means of bartering between Jawas. One, the Great Swap Meet, also known as Dune Sea Haggleday, was held in the Dune Sea. At these meets, Jawas would arrange marriages for clan members and trade between each other.
