|side1=Grievous |side2=Asajj Ventress |side3=Durge |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |ppl1=General Grievous |ppl2=Asajj Ventress |ppl3=Durge |ppl4= |casual1=None |casual2=Asajj Ventress wounded |casual3=Durge wounded |casual4= |civilian= }} The Mission to Trenchant space station was a skirmish fought between Asajj Ventress, Durge, and General Grievous in the Clone Wars. thumb|left|200px|[[Durge/Legends|Durge is defeated by Grievous.]] Count Dooku sent Asajj Ventress and the Gen'Dai bounty hunter Durge on a mission to Space Station Trenchant. The real purpose on the mission was to select the new Supreme Commander of the Droid Armies. The third candidate for the role was the cyborg, General Grievous. The pair started on their mission to identify their common enemy, reaching the trophy room together which was filled with Jedi robes and lightsabers. Armed with an electrostaff Grievous appeared and defeated Durge initially. Asajj then arrived and fought Grievous, who was again victorious. The pair then teamed up, Asajj using her paired lightsabers and Durge unleashing his full arsenal, including his energized bolas. The pair seemed victorious as Asajj knocked the electrostaff away. Grievous, though, was far from beaten, and unleashed his lightsabers. The pair was quickly defeated by his lightsaber skills.
