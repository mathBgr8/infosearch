Foran Tutha star probe
802

The Foran Tutha star probe was an ancient wrecked starship found on Foran Tutha around 500 BBY. It is considered one of the greatest discoveries of the post-Ruusan Reformation era. Scholars were divided on its origins as Celestial, Rakatan, or early Core technology. *Shield of Lies Category:Artifacts Category:Pre-Republic Category:Starships
