Trade Federation cargo hauler
2424

The Trade Federation cargo hauler was a capital ship class used by the Trade Federation. The cargo haulers were "control ships" in the Trade Defense Force and had poor viewing capabilities above and below the ship. Such vessels resembled the larger LH-3210 cargo haulers though they were classified as midsized and were smaller.
