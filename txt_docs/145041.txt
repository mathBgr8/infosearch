Harch (Human)
1256

Harch was a Human male who served in the Galactic Empire. He was the Imperial major who served on board the Chimaera as an engineering officer in 19 ABY. He coordinated repairs on the Chimaera after an attack from Moff Disra's pirates near Pesitiin. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Officers of the Imperial Remnant
