In addition to being a scout, Uutkik was also an experienced thief who pilfered equipment and hijacked vehicles from unwary bystanders in Mos Eisley, a large spaceport on the planet Tatooine. At one point during the Galactic Civil War, a major galactic conflict between the Rebel Alliance and the Galactic Empire, Uutkik stood near a sandcrawler, a treaded vehicle used by Jawas. In 0 BBY,The New Essential Chronology on Tatooine, with other members of Nebit's clan, Uutkik peered at astromech droid R2-D2 from behind a rock wall in Jawa Canyon after the droid was separated from his companion, C-3PO. Uutkik had yellow eyes and wore a brown cloak. They were the considered to be the ugliest cousin of Het Nkik, a Jawa member of the Nkik Clan. Uutkik first appeared in the 1977 original trilogy film Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. While first given a name in the 1998 Special Edition Limited expansion set for the Decipher Star Wars Customizable Card Game, the card did not specify if the character appeared in the film. Uutkik was later retconned into a Jawa that appeared in the film through the Visual Guide feature on StarWars.com on May 30, 2007.
