|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=360 |isbn=1593077769 |isbn2= |timeline=4&ndash;5 ABY |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron/Star Wars Omnibus |issues=Volume 3 |preceded by=X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 2 |followed by=Tales of the Jedi Volume 1 }} Star Wars Omnibus: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 3 is the third Star Wars Omnibus by Dark Horse collecting several issues of the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron comics. It was published on July 4, 2007. The galaxy's greatest starfighters take flight once more in a mission to reclaim the galactic capital Coruscant from the Empire! Whether this becomes the New Republic's finest hour or its darkest defeat lies squarely on the shoulders of Wedge Antilles and his team of ace X-Wing pilots—Rogue Squadron! In full color on high-quality paper for an unbeatable price, this massive volume contains issues #21-35 of Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron, encompassing In the Empire's Service, The Making of Baron Fel, Family Ties, Masquerade, and Mandatory Retirement. Follow the rise of the Empire's deadliest pilot Baron Fel, marvel at the machinations of Imperial power-player Ysanne Isard, and discover the fate of Interim-Emperor Sate Pestage in the book that collects one of the most epic sagas in Star Wars into a single volume for the first time! For the first time in a generation, those opposed to the Empire have a chance to reclaim the galactic capital Coruscant. Whether this becomes the New Republic's finest hour or its darkest defeat lies squarly on the shoulders of Wedge Antilles and his team of ace X-wing pilots - Rogue Squadron!
