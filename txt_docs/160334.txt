Docking Bay 15
734

Docking Bay 15 was a docking bay on the hangar deck of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Errant Venture, owned by Booster Terrik. It was large enough to accommodate Talon Karrde's Action VI transport, the Wild Karrde.
