Jens (student)
1280

Jens was a Human female student at the University of Agamar who had blue eyes and blonde hair. In 25 ABY, she was one of the members of Dr. Anki Pace's team to journey to Bimmiel. When Corran Horn and Ganner Rhysode embarked on their rescue mission for two others students, Jens operated a remote to keep the two Yuuzhan Vong guards preoccupied.
