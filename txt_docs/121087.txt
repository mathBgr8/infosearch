Reina Solov
1010

Reina Solov was a Professor of archaeology at the University of Byblos during the Imperial Era. Solov specialist area of study was ancient cultures and during the Galactic Civil War investigated the Fellowship of Kooroo religion. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Archaeologists Category:Educators Category:Females Category:Humans
