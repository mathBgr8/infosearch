Aether Hypernautics
1859

|products=*Hyperdrives *hyperdrive motivators |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Aether Hypernautics was a company based on the Core World of Corulag. The company pushed academic hyperspace research into the realm of applied science in an effort to create better, safer, faster, and more efficient hyperspace propulsion systems. It manufactured hyperdrives and supplied military-grade hyperdrives to the Imperial Navy. Aether Hypernautics was a contractor and subcontractor for many classified Imperial Military projects and worked closely with Sienar Fleet Systems. The company's hypernautics research was focused on attempting to produce a .1 military hyperdrive motivator, which made them one of the companies that competed to design the fastest engine in existence. *Darth Plagueis *The Far Orbit Project Category:Starship technology companies
