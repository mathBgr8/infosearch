|publisher=Decipher |release date=May 1997 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The First Anthology Star Wars Customizable Card Game was released May 1997. Each set contained two white-bordered Premiere starter decks, two A New Hope expansion packs, two Hoth expansion packs, and the 11-card premium Jedi Pack. Six white-bordered preview cards were included: *Commander Wedge Antilles *Boba Fett *Jabba's Influence *X-Wing Assault Squadron *Hit and Run *Death Star Assault Squadron All six cards were later reprinted as black-bordered cards in the Special Edition set. The First Anthology was released in a long, 800-count card box that was completely black, with the Star Wars Customizable Card Game logo on the lid. Category:Star Wars Customizable Card Game
