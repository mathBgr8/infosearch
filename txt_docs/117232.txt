Battle of Half-Axe Pass
2258

The Battle of Half-Axe Pass was a battle that took place on the planet of New Plympto as part of the Galactic Empire's campaign to kill the planet's Nosaurian population. During the battle, Jedi Master Dass Jennir aided the New Plympto resistance, fighting alongside his friend Bomo Greenbark, one of the Nosaurians. The pair were the only members of the resistance to survive the battle, as the other Nosaurians were killed in the fighting or executed after surrendering. *Dark Times 1 *Suns of Fortune Category:Battles of the Cleansing of New Plympto
