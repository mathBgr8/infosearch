Oolid
1221

Oolids were native to Oolidi and were affiliated with the New Republic. Tolik Yar was their senator. Author Michael P. Kube-McDowell created the Oolid for his novel, Before the Storm, published by Bantam Spectra in 1996. Its sequel, Shield of Lies, also featured the same Oolid senator. In 2000, James Luceno reprized the senator in The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial, published by Del Rey. *Before the Storm *Shield of Lies *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:Expansion Region sentient species
