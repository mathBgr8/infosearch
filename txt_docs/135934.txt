Davrilat
1058

Davrilat was a complicated religion based on the sanctity of harmonics. The Tocoyan Neb Dulo, a native of the planet Tocoya, became one of Davrilat's disciples by the time of the Galactic Civil War. Decipher created Davrilat in 1998 for Neb Dulo's card in the Special Edition Limited expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game. Category:Religions
