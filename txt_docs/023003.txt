|type=Paperback |pages=96 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0-7869-1794-6 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Secrets of Naboo is a paperback supplement to the Wizards of the Coast Star Wars Roleplaying Game. It was written by Steve Miller and J.D. Wiker and published in February 2001. Apart from information and game stats, the sourcebook also includes multiple short "story ideas" and the longer adventure "Peril on Naboo". The adventures "Operation: Clodhopper" and "Save the Mantaris!", published at Wizards.com, are also based on the book. *"A Letter from Chancellor Valorum" *"The Security Leak" &mdash; Story idea
