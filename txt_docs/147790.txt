Arkeld
1300

Doctor Arkeld was a male Human professor at the Mrlsst Trade and Science Academy, before he became infamous for leading a cell of the Justice Action Network&mdash;an anti-Empire terrorist group&mdash;on Mrlsst. Arkeld was known as a gifted speaker, and often denounced the tyranny of the Empire. By the time of the Battle of Endor, he had nearly forty years of research and teaching experience. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Educators Category:Humans Category:JAN operatives Category:Males
