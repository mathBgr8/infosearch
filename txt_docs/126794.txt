Kiatha
1394

Kiatha was a male Jawa who was active during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Coruscant Police for mishandling a Ronto, resulting in extensive property damage. In 32 BBY, the bounty was claimed by the Mandalorian-turned Bounty hunter Jango Fett.
