Bay of Tears
1060

The Bay of Tears was a deep water harbor on Vjun guarded by sudden shoals. The shores of the bay had a network of caves and tunnels that had been carved by the River Weeping. Some of these caves exited into the cellars of Château Malreaux. The Bay was considered the perfect smuggler's port and was used by the first Count Malreaux to launch pirate raids against the nobles in the surrounding territory. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Tears
