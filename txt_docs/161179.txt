Underground
564

"Underground" was the primary cantina on Tilnes. It was located at the center of the tunnel networks of the mining moon, and it served as a gathering place for individuals from the different mining companies. The politics of the companies were forgotten here. "The Underground" was run by a Rodian named Maris Gen. *Living Force Campaign Guide Category:Cantinas Category:Tilnes locations
