Goq Cranna
1279

Goq Cranna was a male Sorrusian who served as chief of a Sorrusian tribe. As Astri Oddo and Obi-Wan Kenobi came to Sorrus to obtain information on Ona Nobis, Goq and his son, Bhu Cranna, showed them where the hiding place of the bounty hunter Ona Nobis was located. Later, he and his son helped Kenobi, Astri, Cholly, Weez and Tup again, when they were trapped in a desert cavern by assisting them out of it.
