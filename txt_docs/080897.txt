Dammant Killers
1146

The Dammant Killers were an armaments company based on Adumar, known for its missiles. The company was decades old as of 40 ABY. Their main factory was in Cartann City. In 40 ABY it was found out by Jacen Solo and Ben Skywalker that they were building missiles beyond Galactic Alliance quota and supplying them to planets threatening to buck GA authority. The findings of the missions were then reported to GA authorities. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Adumari organizations Category:Arms manufacturing companies
