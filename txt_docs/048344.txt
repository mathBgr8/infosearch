Proton missile launcher
1315

The proton missile launcher had miniature proton warheads. Each clip contained five missiles. It was especially useful for neutralizing droids and other machinery. The ammunition for it was particularly hard to find, keeping wielders from overusing them. They were used during the Invasion of Naboo in 32 BBY. *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace video game *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Portable launchers
