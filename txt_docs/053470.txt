Compart Corporation
624

Compart Corporation was a Bothan-run corporation that was based on Kothlis, a colony world in Bothan Space. It mass-produced various types of droid circuitry and components. One of its developments was the circuitry used to manufacture photoreceptors, an important element in the development process of droids. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Droid technology companies Category:Tal'cara locations
