327 (corvette group)
1504

250px|thumb|Corvette 327 1 327 was a group of two CR90 corvettes that served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. They were part of the task force that was led by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Invincible during Operation Strike Fear. While mustering in the Dellalt system they were identified as carrying TIE repair parts by a Rebel reconnaissance mission flown by Keyan Farlander in a lone R-22 Spearhead.
