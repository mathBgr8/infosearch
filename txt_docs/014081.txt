Neelgaimon
2652

Neelgaimon was a hot, drought-ridden planet in the Xappyh sector of the Outer Rim Territories. The planet was famous for its hazardous sandmines, the planet's main industry, which were developed and maintained by slave and droid labor. It was still an Imperial world by 12 ABY. Han Solo visited Neelgaimon once. The planet appears to be a Tuckerization of the name of the British writer Neil Gaiman.
