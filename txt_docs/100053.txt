Urlor Sette
1977

Urlor Sette was a Lusankya prisoner around the same time as Rogue Squadron Lieutenant Corran Horn. A large man, he had lost part of one hand, leaving him effectively crippled. He was a personal assistant to fellow prisoner Jan Dodonna, a Rebel General. During his time at Lusankya, Sette helped Corran Horn escape&mdash;the first person to have done so.
