K'vin
1318

K'vin was a male individual who was the disembodied leader of the Most Perfect Order of K'vin, a Tatooine-based cult he claimed was a sect of the B'omarr Order. K'vin was present when bounty hunter Eron Stonefield ventured into the Order's citadel on the hunt for Han Solo, and requested that she shoot the vat his brain was held in. The bounty hunter complied, but instead of reaching total enlightenment, K'vin's brain soon died as a result of being exposed outside of the nutrient filled jar. *Star Wars Missions 5: The Hunt for Han Solo Category:B'omarr Monks Category:Cyborgs Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Tatooinians
