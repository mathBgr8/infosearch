Mindabaal Custom
2000

The Mindabaal Custom was a type of personal yacht. Designed to meet the needs of individual customers, Mindabaal Customs had a reputation for flexibility. In their stock configuration, they were not equipped with weapons; however, some customers would order armament installed, such as James Ortell Donovan did with the Royal Flower. *Pirates & Privateers Category:Yacht classes
