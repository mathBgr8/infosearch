Suprema (title)
808

Suprema was a title of unknown meaning among the Shimholt species of Kar'a'katok. The only known Shimholt to hold this title was Ampotem Za, who took it as his name upon leaving his homeworld. *Star Wars (1977) 69 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Titles
