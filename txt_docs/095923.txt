In 8 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the wedding of Han Solo and Leia Organa to 8 ABY. a number of Jumerians presented Princess Leia Organa Solo with a singing lamp as a gift for her wedding. Although she disliked the item, she kept it in the Coruscanti apartment she shared with her husband, Han Solo. That year,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of A Forest Apart to 8 ABY. the Wookiees Mallatobuck and Chewbacca visited the apartment with their son Lumpawaroo, who began rummaging through the space. As the parents listened to objects breaking inside, Solo commented that while his wife might be angry about the damage depending on what items were affected, she would probably appreciate if the Jumerians' singing lamp was one of the items broken. Jumerians were first mentioned in the 2003 eBook novel A Forest Apart, written by Troy Denning. *A Forest Apart *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Core Worlds sentient species
