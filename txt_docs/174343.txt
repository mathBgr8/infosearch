Star Wars Weekly 49 is the forty-ninth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on January 10, 1979. This issue includes part three of a four-part black & white reprinting of the story The Keeper's World, originally published in color in Marvel UK' Pizzazz magazine. *"Do You Know What Your Children Are?" *"Caverns of Mystery!" *"Tales of the galaxy: Visitation Part 2: With God on our side!" *"The power of Warlock Part 5: Sacred Empire" *"Tales of the Watcher: The cave of Shaggdorr!" 49
