Yeade

thumb|250px|[[Vonya and fellow witches enjoying some warm yeade]] Yeade was an alcoholic drink enjoyed by the Witches of Dathomir. It was usually served warm and particularly appreciated during cold weather. The Sisters who guarded the malelings at night would often get drunk on yeade. In 31 BBY, the witch Vonya once bullied her fellow Sister Ros Lai by throwing a cauldron of hot yeade at her face. *Star Wars (1998) 23 Category:Alcoholic beverages
