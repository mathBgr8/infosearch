By the Banks of the Warm, Pink Z'gag
863

"By the Banks of the Warm, Pink Z'gag" was a song played by the members of the K'zagg Colony at the Ruurian cocoon-weaving time, during their annual cycle when the larvae gather to grow chrysalis. Simultaneously, last years pupae are emerging from their cocoons as chroma-wings. It is a soft, sad melody played to comfort the individuals in need.Han Solo and the Lost Legacy The tune was sung by Skynx, on his mission to Dellalt, to the members of the party while trapped in the hold of the Survivors. *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy Category:Songs
