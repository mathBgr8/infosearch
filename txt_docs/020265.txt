Those battles are dated to 0 BBY and 4 ABY, respectively, by The New Essential Chronology, which also establishes the Battle of Yavin as the final event before the beginning of 0 ABY. Like the rest of The Emperor's New Clothes' work, "Section 223" was approved by the Imperial Board of Culture.
