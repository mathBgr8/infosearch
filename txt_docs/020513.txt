It was the site of a town that functioned as a mining colony. At some point by 7 ABY,Wanted by Cracken is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor. Since The New Essential Chronology dates that conflict to 4 ABY, the placement of the sourcebook therefore translates to 7 ABY. in response to attacks by a group of pirates, the mining colony hired six mercenaries for protection. The pirates, however, hired the Kerestian bounty hunter Andov Syn, who proceeded to singlehandedly wipe out the mercenaries. The incident on Adim eventually cemented Syn's reputation as a deadly bounty hunter. The New Republic General Airen Cracken subsequently noted the incident on Adim in Wanted by Cracken, a datafile that served as a compilation of reports on various threats to the New Republic. Adim was first mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
