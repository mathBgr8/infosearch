Shebba Kalshi Desilijic
1665

Shebba Kalshi Desilijic was a Hutt of the Desilijic kajidic who lived on the agricultural planet Shawti. The Klatooinian Umpass-stay served as Shebba's bodyguard. Unfortunately, Umpass-stay failed to recognize the danger posed by Shebba's own nephew, Jabba Desilijic Tiure, and was unable to stop him from killing Shebba. Shebba's name is styled with "Desilijic" as the last name, rather than the middle name like Jabba Desilijic Tiure or Zorba Desilijic Tiure. This styling is consistent across references to Shebba.
