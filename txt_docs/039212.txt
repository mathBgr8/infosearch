Ruillia's Insulated Rooms
770

Ruillia's Insulated Rooms was the name of a small hostelry located between Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina and the Lucky Despot Hotel & Casino in Mos Eisley on the planet Tatooine. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hotels Category:Mos Eisley locations
