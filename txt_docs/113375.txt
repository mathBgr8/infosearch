This contender wore full-body armor with a face-concealing helmet and was armed with two melee weapons, one in each hand. 280px|right|thumb|The «second» gladiator who appeared after the death of the «first» This gladiator first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003.
