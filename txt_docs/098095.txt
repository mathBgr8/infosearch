C-4 protocol droid
488

The C-4 protocol droid was a droid manufactured during the time of the New Republic on the factory moon of Telti by Cybot Galactica. It was programmed with many household functions. It was the predecessor of the C-5 protocol droid and the successor of the C-3 protocol droid. *The New Rebellion Category:C-series protocol droid models
