Craddock Karath
1857

Craddock Karath was a journeyman laborer, an officer in the Republic Navy, and the father of five children, the oldest of whom was Saul Karath. He was stationed at the Republic shipyards near the planet Foerost when the Krath and Mandalorian forces arrived and took them over, and was killed during the battle.
