Kayson's Weapons Shop
1681

Kayson's Weapons Shop was based in Mos Eisley and was a distributor of firearms. It was probably the best weapons shop in the city and was run by Kayson, a grizzled alien, who was also very adept at obtaining weapons on the black market.
