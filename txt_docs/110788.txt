Gray Four
1905

Gray Four was a call sign used by multiple pilots of the New Republic and Galactic Alliance. Bothan New Republic pilot Eurrsk Thri'ag was the fourth member of Gray Squadron prior to its adoption of its permanent designation, Wraith Squadron. Another Wraith would use the call sign Gray Four during the squadron's time posing as the crew of the Warlord Zsinj-aligned CR90 corvette Night Caller, flying its complement of TIE/LN starfighters as Gray Flight. "Gray Four" was directed by Commander Wedge Antilles to target the communications systems of the corvette Constrictor in a battle over Talasea. Syal Antilles was designated Gray Four when she piloted an Aleph-class starfighter during the Second Galactic Civil War. *X-Wing: Wraith Squadron *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Squadron callsigns
