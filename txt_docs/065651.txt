Aurorient Express
2260

The Aurorient Express was a luxury cruise liner on the gas giant Yorn Skot owned by Clode Rhoden and captained by a Neimoidian. The ship became a target for the eco-terrorist group Green Forge, who attempted to blow it up as well as have Rhoden assassinated for his insurance money. The plot took place under the guise of an attack by pressure pirates, who coincidentally, were attacking the ship at that precise time. Despite being torn apart by the pirates' Fastlatch-class defense droids, Aurorient Express was saved by Jedi Knights Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi, only to be destroyed by a core bomb left active by disgruntled employees. *Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express 1 *Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Luxury liners
