Lord Superior
581

Lord Superior was a title used by the leader of the Unified Factions, the government of the planet Kroctar and of the Kroctari people. Bosmihi, the leader who negotiated a treaty of alliance with the Imperial Remnant in 19 ABY, was a holder of the title. *Vision of the Future *Vision of the Future abridged audiobook Category:Political titles
