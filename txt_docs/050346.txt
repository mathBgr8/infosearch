Alderaan Biotics
1650

|founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Alderaan Biotics was an Alderaan-based hydroponics company. Their Borleias satellite facility, abandoned before the destruction of Alderaan due to lack of profit, was eventually reactivated by Evir Derricote and later co-opted by the forces of Ysanne Isard prior to the Bacta War. Rogue Squadron would soon recapture the facility.
