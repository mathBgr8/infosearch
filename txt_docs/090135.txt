|published in=Star Wars Tales 15 |reprinted in= |pages=8 |timeline=3 ABY"Slippery Slope" takes place while Luke Skywalker is training on Dagobah, depicted in and therefore placed in 3 ABY per The New Essential Chronology. |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Slippery Slope" is a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 15 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4. The events of the story are mentioned in Star Wars: The Ultimate Visual Guide. While Luke Skywalker is training with Yoda on Dagobah, a droid bounty hunter named Milko arrives, trying to take the bounty on Luke offered by the Galactic Empire. Yoda tells Luke to stop him at all costs, lest he reveal their location and doom the Rebellion. Luke manages to capture Milko, but instead of killing him, lets him go free with the knowledge that if he revealed the location of Yoda, he'd also have to reveal that he groveled before a Jedi instead of capturing him.
