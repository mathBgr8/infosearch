Contumely Club
1095

The Contumely Club was an entertainment location operating during the Galactic Civil War. The Club's policies denied access to Humans and droids. To enforce that rule, Wookiee bouncer Urartu was at the door, pushing out unwanted clients. A small alien translated the Wookiee's native Shyriiwook. The smuggler Roark Garnet once tried to enter the Club but was stopped by Urartu. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game Category:Bars and clubs
