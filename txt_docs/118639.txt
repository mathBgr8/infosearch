Loonee bird
1351

The loonee bird was a creature that lived on the forest moon of Endor. The bird laughed incessantly, and any other creature it touched would fall under its spell of uncontrollable laughter, a spell that could plague its victim forever and would temporarily affect any who go near them. An Ewok legend told of a being at the top of Mount Sorrow whose tears had the power to reverse such spells. When young Teebo came under the spell by plucking a tail feather from a loonee bird, his friends Wicket and Kneesaa journeyed with him to the top of the mountain.
