Rotas V indigenous fighters
2482

A group of fighters were loyal, pro-Galactic Republic soldiers indigenous to Rotas V,Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary a planet in the galaxy's Outer Rim Territories.The Essential Atlas Rotan soldiers wore kama belt-spats, flexible leather half-kiltsLegacy of the Force: Revelation traditionally used by and associated with Mandalorian warriors. When many of the Grand Army of the Republic's clone trooper officers took to wearing kamas due to the army's Mandalorian influence, several Republic leaders were against the proliferation of a symbol from the Mandalorian culture among the clone ranks. However, opinions were swayed into support after it was pointed out that the indigenous Republic fighters of Rotas V had also worn kamas into battle. Referring to the kamas only as "command skirts," these individuals emphasized the influence of the Rotan fighters in the clones' usage of the kama over that of the Mandalorians. The indigenous fighters of Rotas V were first introduced to Star Wars canon in the Visual Dictionary accompaniment to Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. They were later mentioned again in the Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic, an article in the eighty-fourth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine, written by Karen Traviss and Ryan Kaufman. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary Category:Soldiers
