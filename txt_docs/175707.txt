Togri
1675

Togri was a MB-C1 medium transport belonging to the Imperials. Togri was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupters vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor to the Corrupter in the Mev system. Togri's cargo was 'machinery'. The ship was then attacked by Rebel raiders and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:MB-C1 medium transports Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
