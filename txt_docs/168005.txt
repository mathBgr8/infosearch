Dat-an
554

Dat-an was the language of the Annoo-dat Prime. After they left their homeworld of Annoo and conquered Gelefil, the language was also adopted by the planet's other inhabitants, the Annoo-dat Blue, largely replacing Tra'ang'a. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Languages
