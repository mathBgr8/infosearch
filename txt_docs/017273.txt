Uhanayih
3001

Uhanayih was a planet in the Yinchorri system. It was a lifeless rock close to the system's primary star. In the Yinchorri language, its name meant "within world," and it was associated with the spirit realm in the Yinchorri religion. During the Yinchorri technological and military buildup, the High Command built a command center inside the world, giving its name new meaning. In 33 BBY during the Yinchorri Uprising, Uhanayih was attacked by the Navy of the Galactic Republic.
