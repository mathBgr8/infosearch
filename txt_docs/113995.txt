Blue 9
1739

Blue 9 was the callsign of one of the starfighters in the forces of Iaco Stark, whose fleet included many fighters of the same class. Blue 9 was responsible for destroying Nute Gunray's Trade Federation shuttle. The fighter then attempted to destroy the Consular-class cruiser carrying a Jedi delegation. The Jedi used a Force illusion to convince Blue 9's pilot his ship had suffered critical damage from numerous attacks by Republic fighters.
