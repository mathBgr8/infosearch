Farrimmer Cafe
1047

The Farrimmer Cafe was a bar and grill aboard the Mynock 7 Space Station. It was owned by the Filvian H'nib Statermast and his Vaathkree partner, Grosteek, during the reign of Emperor Palpatine. The smoky, dim interior was in stark contrast to the vibrant exterior, with its images of menu items. Treedee greeted visitors to the Farrimmer Cafe, while A5 served as a bouncer. It was here that Bnugor briefly lost his pet snuzzleguff, Snowball. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cafes
