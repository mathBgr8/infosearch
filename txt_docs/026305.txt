Two Jedi Walk Into a Bar

|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Two Jedi Walk Into a Bar" is a scenario for the Star Wars Miniatures game, written by Tim Bresnan and published in 2005. It depicts an alternate version of Attack of the Clones, in which Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker battle Zam Wesell in the Outlander Club. The game offers rules for including neutral characters in the bar. Category:Miniatures Scenarios
