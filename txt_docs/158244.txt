Legal analyst droid

A legal analyst droid, also known as a legal droid or litigation droidGalladinium's Fantastic Technology was a droid that could perform governmental functions, including making laws. A terrorist organization on Lorrd wanted to replace the government with these droids. One of these droids was HM-3, who assisted Jacen Solo in removing Cal Omas from power in 40 ABY. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Legacy of the Force: Revelation *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids *The Far Orbit Project *Strongholds of Resistance 3
