Volu Nyth
1927

Volu Nyth was a Kuati pilot who joined Rogue Squadron during the siege of Borleias and flew in that series of battles. After the Yuuzhan Vong War, she retired from military service; during the Second Galactic Civil War, Wedge Antilles recruited her into Rakehell Squadron, an independent unit supporting Luke Skywalker's Jedi faction.
