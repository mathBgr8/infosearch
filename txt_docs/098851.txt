Teleq
984

Teleq was one of six bounty hunters hired by Granta Omega to capture Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi on a training mission to Ragoon VI. He captured the Jedi Wren Honoran, and intended to use him to capture the Jedi. His plan was foiled, however, after attempting to down the Jedi with waves of poison darts, and he was carried away by angry bird-like creatures when Obi-Wan and Anakin drove him into the birds' guarded nests where they protected their young.
