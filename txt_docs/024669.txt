Renatta Racing Systems
690

Renatta Racing Systems was a ship-manufacturing company that existed during the latter years of the Old Republic. They were best known as the creators of the Renatta Needle Ship, one of the fastest models of its type in the galaxy. However, because of the complexity and high costs associated with these ships (not to mention the inclusion of rare and dangerous components, such as Tobal lenses for the ships' photon drive systems), the company went bankrupt. *The Life and Legend of Obi-Wan Kenobi *Tatooine Ghost Category:Shipwrights
