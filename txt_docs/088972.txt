Dawn (Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport)
2032

Dawn was a Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport used by the Rebel Alliance. It was part of an arms deal between the Alliance and the pirates in a cargo loading area located in the Tungra sector. The Imperials learned of this deal and attacked. Maarek Stele inspected the ship to find it containing Rebel advisers.
