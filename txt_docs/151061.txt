Hound-W2 SPD
2561

The Hound-W2 SPD was a security droid from the Scanning Patrol Detail series developed by Industrial Automaton. They were rectangular shaped and about half the height of an astromech droid. thumb|left|200px|A schematic drawing of an Hound-w2 They moved on wheels and had four extendable arms that could reach out to a distance of 15 meters. The arms were tipped with various sensors, such as a laser scan emitter, a pulse scan emitter, and a thermal imager. They did not have a vocabulator and could only communicate via Droidspeak, which was considered their only negative point. They were among the best security droids on the market. thumb|right|200px|Hound-W2 SPD security droid. Hound-W2s were frequently used at security checkpoint and tasked with detecting illegal materials, like spice or explosives.
