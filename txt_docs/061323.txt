Tesslar
1652

Tesslar was a male Human bounty hunter working for the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. A male Human orphan, Tesslar made a living during his adolescence as a member of the smuggler Shamel's gang. Stealing speeders for the gang throughout his youth, Tesslar eventually learned how to fire a blaster and cut ties with Shamel and ventured into the wider galaxy. Becoming a bounty hunter, Tesslar quickly found work with the Galactic Empire, detesting any hunter aligned with a guild or working independently. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Category:Bounty hunters Category:Humans Category:Males
