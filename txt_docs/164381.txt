Angela Krin
1580

Angela Krin was a female Human Lieutenant Commander who served in the Corporate Sector Authority's fleet. Krin was the commander of the Resolute, an Invincible-class Dreadnaught which enforced the quarantine of Endregaad, in the Tion Cluster. Shortly thereafter she received a promotion to Captain.
