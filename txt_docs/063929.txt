Quentor
1541

Quentor was the false alias, given by Fligh to Obi-Wan Kenobi, for a thief by the name of Aarno Dering, who operated in stolen goods and information for profit. It was also the name of a yellow-tailed summerbird pet of the Galactic Senate. On the planet Euceron, the thief "Quentor" approached a shady information dealer by the name of Fligh during the Galactic Games of 26 BBY. Fligh learned that Alderaan would win the "fixed" swoop obstacle course event, and in turn shared that information with Didi Oddo, who then told it to Obi-Wan Kenobi. Jocasta Nu, at Kenobi's request, did a search of the name in the Jedi Archives and discovered that "Quentor" was a yellow-tailed summer bird. The unofficial pet of the Senate, Quentor lived in the eaves of the building and the Senators provided him with fruit and crumbs to eat. Obi-Wan Kenobi knew, then, that Fligh had lied to him about his source.
