Shipwomb
1014

A shipwomb was a facility used to grow and assemble Yuuzhan Vong ships, analogous to a mechanical shipyard. These facilities were usually carved out of the ruins of destroyed or conquered worlds. Sernpidal held one within its remains after that world's moon crashed into it.
