thumb|right|200px|Whalen's published artwork of Rorr'hn of Drgi Tom Whalen is a Star Wars fan who designed Rorr'hn of Drgi in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the 16 years and older category. He was 21 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
