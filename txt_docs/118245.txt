Adventures Unlimited 2
767

Adventures Unlimited 2 was a roleplaying magazine published in the Summer of 1995. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The article was called "Forbidden Fruit" and was written by Nigel D. Findley. He died a month after submitting it. Category:Adventures Unlimited
