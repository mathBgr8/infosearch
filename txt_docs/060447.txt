Mersel Kebir
2760

The Mersel Kebir was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser that served as the flagship of the Rendili Home Defense Fleet. At the time of the Clone Wars, it was under the command of Captain Jace Dallin. Other officers included Lieutenant Mellor Yago, and Gallan. Following the Battle of Rendili, the Mersel Kebir joined the Republic Navy with the rest of the Rendili fleet. The ship's name is a reference to the real-world Mers-el-Kébir, a Mediterranean naval port that served as the base for the Vichy French navy after the Fall of France during World War II. The UK Royal Navy launched Operation Catapult with the same aim as the Republic's mission to Rendili - to secure the French fleet either by taking them into Allied service or destroying them to deny their use to the Axis.
