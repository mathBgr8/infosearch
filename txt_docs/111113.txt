UDON
1960

UDON, also known as UdonCrew and UDON Entertainment, is an Art collective commissioned to color the comic "Ghosts of Hoth" and illustrated the cover for Star Wars Tales 17. Members include Roberto Campus, who did the cover of Star Wars Insider 87; Eric Vedder, who did linework for artwork accompanying Star Wars Insider articles; Joe Ng and Kevin Yan, who contributed linework and paints respectively for some artwork in The Force Unleashed Campaign Guide; Saejin Oh, who worked on the Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide; and Alvin Lee and Arnold Tsang, who respectively did the lines and colors for the cover of Dark Horse Comics' seventeenth issue of Star Wars Tales. *"Ghosts of Hoth" *Star Wars Tales 17 Category:Comic book colorists
