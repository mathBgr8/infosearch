Vigilant (Carrack-class)
1755

The Vigilant was a Carrack-class light cruiser of the Imperial Navy. thumb|left|The bridge of the Vigilant. Captain Deyd Llnewe was the commander of the ship. Raprice was also an officer on the ship. Vigilant was active in the regions around Nal Hutta, where she was patrolling the planet's rim, looking for any smugglers and other kinds of mischief.
