The details were lost to history, but it appears to have been a civil war in the Tapani sector. The Jedi Order and House Pelagia battled the Sith-influenced Mecrosa Order of assassins, which was sponsored by House Mecetti. During the conflict, House Melissus and House Barong were absorbed by House Mecetti.
