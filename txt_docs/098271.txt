Hunti Pereg
1599

Hunti Pereg was a skilled bounty hunter who, along with five others, was hired to capture Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. He was incapacitated while throwing thermal detonators at the Jedi. Granta Omega posed as an injured Hunti Pereg to trap the Jedi into going back to their ship, but later the two Jedi ran into the real Hunti Pereg.
