Jenatahza
1768

Jenatahza was a male Dug who served under Sebolto's employ during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY,Star Wars: Bounty Hunter is set immediately after the Invasion of Naboo, which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 32 BBY. he was wanted by the Malastare Special Service for being part of a plot to assassinate Representative Ainlee Teem.
