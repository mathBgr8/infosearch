Tree-Bott
803

A Tree-Bott was a small aboreal animal native to U-Tendik, a jungle planet colonized by Rodian refugees. The Tree-Botts were considered ideal hunting game and were valued for their milk and meat. The Tetsu Clan Rodians known as Nok and Teeku were reputable Tree-Bott herders among their people. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Arboreal creatures Category:Creatures of U-Tendik Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Food creatures Category:Hunted creatures Category:Mammals Category:Species of undefined sentience
