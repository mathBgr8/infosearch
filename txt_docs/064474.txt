Janna Pallask
1050

Janna Pallask was a female Human who served as an undercover agent for House Pelagia and was an associate of Pellas Agri ("Coros Telari"). She was involved in attempts to recover a file that would potentially reveal House Mecetti's involvement in anti-Imperial activities. Janna was an intense, honest and idealistic woman who was totally dedicated to House Pelagia. Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Spies
