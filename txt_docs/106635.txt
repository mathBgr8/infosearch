*Dunlavey Studio |illustrator=*Rusel DeMaria *Marian Hartsough Associates *Shawn Morningstar |editor=*Paula Munier Lee *Hartley Lesser *Kip Ward *Becky Whitney *Rick Barba |publisher=*Prima Publishing *Roger Stewart |release date=March 22, 1995 |type=Paperback |pages=580 |dimensions=7.5 &times; 1.2 &times; 9.5 inches |weight=2.2 pounds |isbn=1559587857 |series=Prima's Secrets of the Games |preceded by= |followed by= }} X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide was a computer game strategy book for Star Wars: X-Wing: Collector's CD-ROM, the CD-ROM version of Star Wars: X-Wing, written by Rusel DeMaria, David Wessman, and David Maxwell and published by Prima Publishing. A previous edition of the guide was published along with the game's original release. Like the first edition, it contained much of the story from The Farlander Papers and continued the story of Keyan Farlander throughout. It also included walk-throughs and strategies designed to help players defeat the simulator missions and the missions for the game's first three Tours of Duty, and interviews with the game's creators and design team. Additional material in this version included walkthroughs for the Imperial Pursuit and B-wing expansion packs. Keyan Farlander, one of the greatest Rebel heroes, is back. Once again, he has emerged victorious from a series of daring conflicts with the evil Empire, and in your hands is the book that chronicles his strategic and tactical genius. Rest assured, there's no better guidance in the universe for winning every perilous X-wing scenario.
