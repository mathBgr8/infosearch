Locru's Central Saloon
1621

Locru's Central Saloon was a cantina near the center of Tun Aduban on Aduba-3. It was founded by Locru. It also served as an inn, charging twelve credits a night. Frequent customers included Warto and Azoora. It was here that Han Solo and Chewbacca recruited spacers to fight Serji-X Arrogantus, forming the Eight for Aduba-3. If approached from the direction of Spacer's Hill, it was the third structure to the left past the Town Circle.
