King of the Phosphura Belt Pirates
653

The King or Queen of the Phosphura Belt Pirates, also known as the Pirate King, was the highest ranking leader of the Phosphura Belt Pirates. Pirate Queens and Kings were assisted by Buccaneers. The first Pirate King was Arvo Norstrag. In the Galactic Civil War, their king was Leo Bellsfar. *Pirates & Privateers Category:Belt pirates Category:Titles
