Timbu
758

Timbu were domesticated creatures that were found on backwater worlds such as Bonadan and Caluula. They were quadrupeds the size of a small dewback with long snouts, black eyes, big floppy ears and were nearly as shaggy as a bantha. They were prone to spit when startled or had their reins jerked too hard, their spittle was mentioned as ten times worse than a tauntaun's worst spit wad. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Domesticated creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
