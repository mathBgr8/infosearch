Tito
1066

Tito was a Flakax Joiner who, with Yugi, was assigned by the Squibs (known as the Directors) to assassinate Han and Leia Organa Solo. He failed in his mission, and was convinced by Han and Leia that the Directors and the Fefze were plotting to kill him and the other Flakax. They sent him back to the Directors, where he cut down Krafte and Seneki before being stopped (and presumably killed). *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flakax Category:Joiners Category:Males
