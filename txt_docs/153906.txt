Hauler VI
1094

Hauler VI was a Censian-class transport for the Alliance to Restore the Republic. The Alliance used it to transport SpecForce Marines of its army.Alliance Intelligence Reports During a particular run, the Hauler VI hosted no less than 400 marines. It was then targeted by a crew of 48 pirates from the criminal Fivaran Organization, who were unaware of its cargo. Two of the pirates, the Venithon Twins, discovered the cargo but, instead of warning their partners, they escaped. The other pirates began a raid on the Hauler and were eventually killed or captured. This skirmish led to the Venithon Twins losing the favor of the Fivaran Organization. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Censian-class transports Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
