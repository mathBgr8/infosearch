Sparky (droid)
3126

Sparky was Jaina Solo's R5-series astromech droid during her early tenure with Rogue Squadron in the war with the Yuuzhan Vong. Sparky was destroyedThe New Jedi Order: Balance Point a year into the conflict when Jaina strayed too close to an exploding New Republic ship in her T-65XJ X-wing starfighter at the Battle of Kalarba. Jaina was distraught at the loss of her droid, and eventually replaced the astromech with R2-B3, a droid nicknamed "Cappie" after her fallen wingman Anni Capstan.The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines I: Rebel Dream Sparky first appeared in the 1999 novel The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught, the second book in the series Star Wars: The New Jedi Order and the first installment of the Dark Tide Duology.
