Raaptur
1121

A raaptur was a Gungan battlefield emplacement used to attack enemy airspeeders and spacecraft. Although it was partially organic, the raaptur was not a creature, as many Naboo creatures (fambaa, cerrabore, lerraa) used by the Gungans on the battlefield were. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Gungan vehicle classes Category:Otoh Gunga Defense League products
