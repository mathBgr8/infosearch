Asteroid hopper
2230

An asteroid hopper or Verpine asteroid hopper was an unusual space-capable short-range repulsorlift vehicle designed by the Verpine to operate in dense asteroid belts, such as the Roche asteroid field. Rather than relying on ion engines, a Verpine hopper "bounced" from asteroid to asteroid using its repulsor engines, relying on gyroscopic stabilization to minimize discomfort for its pilot and passengers. thumb|left|150px|Squib in an Asteroid Hopper around [[Korad.]] Though able to ferry its passengers through the vacuum of space, it was dependent on the asteroids' mass for movement, making it unable to travel through deep space. *Jedi's Honor *Strike Force: Shantipole *Operation: Elrood *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Repulsorcraft models Category:Starship classes
