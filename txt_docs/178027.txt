"Corellia Ho!" was a popular Corellian anthem frequently played by militia and military bands. A well known part of the anthem goes like this: Brace up, Corellia, stand firm and tall. None shall subdue you, you'll never fall. Go your own way and let none hold you back, for your future calls you ever ever on-n-n-n-n. Category:Corellian culture Category:Patriotic and military songs
