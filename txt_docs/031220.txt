|type=Hardcover |pages=160 |dimensions=11.0 x 8.5 x 0.5 inches |weight=1.6 pounds |isbn=0786928832 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Hero's Guide is a supplement to the Star Wars Roleplaying Game published by Wizards of the Coast. It was written by Rodney Thompson and J.D. Wiker, first published in June 2003. Follow your own path. Bold individuals from countless star systems aspire to greatness. But the galaxy's real heroes are the dynamic characters who blaze their own way to fame or infamy. Like them, you possess a unique combination of skills, abilities, and talents that enables you to succeed where others fail. With your vision and drive, you will leave your mark on the galaxy. This sourcebook features: *Over 90 new feats, including Blasterslinger, Dark Power, and Kinetic Combat, as well as seven feats that reflect mastery of each of the lightsaber forms. *Over 30 new prestige classes, such as the master duelist, sharpshooter, Bothan master spy, and berserker droid. *Over a dozen factions, such as the Rebel Alliance, Bounty Hunters' Guild, and the Hutt Criminal Syndicate, along with rules for joining each one. *New equipment, including weapons, military hardware, and cybernetics. *Character Creation *Character Archetypes *Skills and Feats *Prestige Classes *Factions *Equipment *Combat *The Force *Droids *Bail Antilles *Kaskutal *Solm *Sei Taria *Marus Timpel *Variise *Ydnen |organisms= |droids= *Berserker droid *Protocol droid **TC-series protocol droid *Spy droid **Espionage droid |events= |locations= *Yanibar |organizations= *Alliance to Restore the Republic *Antarian Rangers *Baran Do *Black Sun **Vigo *Corporate Sector Authority *Corellian Security Force *Galactic Empire **Imperial recruiter **Stormtrooper Corps ***Scout trooper ***Stormtrooper *Matukai *Mistryl Shadow Guard *Sienar Fleet Systems *Zeison Sha |species= *Bothan *Gran *Human *Kel Dor *Noghri *Quarren *Rodian *Trandoshan *Twi'lek *Wookiee *Yuuzhan Vong *Zabrak |vehicles= *74-Z speeder bike *TIE/D Defender |technology= *Cybernetic limbs *Discblade *Shock whip *Telescopic eye *Wan-shen |miscellanea= *Assassin *Aurebesh *Echani (martial art) *Engineer *High Galactic alphabet *HoloNet *Imperial crest *Information broker *Lord of the Expanse *Phase I clone trooper armor *Sniper *Scout trooper armor *Stava *The Force **Soft to Solid *Wrruushi }} Category:Wizards of the Coast sourcebooks
