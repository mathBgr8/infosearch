TD0-2
1560

thumb|right|200px|TD0-2 with Rorworr. TD0-2 was a hovering translator droid belonging to Rorworr. It was similar in function to the later M-TD. Its primary function was to translate Shyriiwook and Thykarann to Galactic Basic Standard, as Rorworr understood Basic, but was unable to pronounce it. Comments in the Production Art Gallery on Wizards labels the droid as "TD0-9." However, the droid is designated TD0-2 in Battle for Theed, a free, 3-page teaser comic released by Wizards of the Coast.
