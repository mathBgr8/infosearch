|published in=Dark Horse Presents Annual 1999: DHP Jr. |reprinted in= |pages=10 |timeline=6 BBYThe events of Luke Skywalker's Walkabout were depicted in a section of A New Hope: The Life of Luke Skywalker dated to 6 BBY by The Essential Reader's Companion. |series=Dark Horse Presents |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Luke Skywalker's Walkabout" is a comic written and drawn by Phill Norwood. It was first published in the 1999 anthology Dark Horse Presents Annual 1999: DHP Jr.. It was later reprinted in color in the 2008 collection Star Wars: Luke Skywalker, Last Hope for the Galaxy. Though Luke Skywalker feels that his life on a moisture farm on Tatooine is boring, the events seen in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope are neither young Luke's first adventure, nor his first meeting with the mysterious Ben Kenobi. In this story, based on events in the Star Wars Radio Drama, Luke and his friend Windy learn for the first time that the Jundland Wastes are not to be traveled lightly... Around 6 BBY, Luke Skywalker and Windy Starkiller attempt a journey to Ja-Mero Ridge, but they get held up by a sandswirl. Then Ben Kenobi rescues them and guides them back to the Lars homestead. This story, originally black and white, was printed in color in Star Wars: Luke Skywalker, Last Hope for the Galaxy. In the original, Windy is referred to as both Windom Starkiller and Windy Marstrap (one can assume that Windy is short for Windom).
