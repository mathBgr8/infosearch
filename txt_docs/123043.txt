Campaign in the Ssi-ruuk Star Cluster
3359

The Alliance of Free Planets strike force led by the Sibwarra engaged in a campaign in the Ssi-ruuk Star Cluster as they fought the remains of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium. Just after the Chiss departed from their punitive raid, a New Republic task force led by the recently renovated Shriwirr (renamed Sibwarra by the New Republic) arrived. They took note of the large-scale damage, and attempted to negotiate a truce with the Ssi-ruuk. When that proved futile, the New Republic fleet engaged in another punitive battle against the Bakura invasion fleet under Admiral Ivpikkis, then departed on Mon Mothma's orders to aid in the liberation of Clak'dor VII. New Republic Intelligence believed that Ivpikkis commanded the Ssi-ruuvi forces in that engagement.
