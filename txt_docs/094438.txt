First Steward
1472

First Steward was a title used by the Geroon Remnant, a secret Vagaari military unit. In 22 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Survivor's Quest to 22 ABY. the Vagaari Bearsh held the title until he was killed by Jedi Master Luke Skywalker and former Emperor's Hand Mara Jade Skywalker.
