Condular
2650

Condular was a celestial body located in the Condular system, a part of the Southern Core portion of the Core Worlds. It was situated between the Chasin and Gandeal systems on the Corellian Trade Spine super-hyperroute. Condular was home to the Grand Astrocartographic Institute, which was active at least several years prior to the Black Fleet Crisis of 16 ABY&ndash;17 ABY.The New Essential Chronology Condular was first mentioned in "," a roleplaying source article written by Jason Fry for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game and published on Wizards.com on January 30, 2003.
