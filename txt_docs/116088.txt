Toorna's Profits
806

The Toorna's Profits was a freighter that landed on Vogga the Hutt's dock on Nar Shaddaa. The landing was delayed however, due to a mix-up of the ID signatures of the Toorna's Profits, the Silver Zephyr, and the Alakandor. Fassa, the dock manager, asked the Jedi Exile to correct this.
