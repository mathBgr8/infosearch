In 0 BBY, he volunteered for his prison detail position, as he took sadistic pleasure in seeing Imperial justice enforced. He was present when Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, and Chewbacca rescued Princess Leia Organa from her prison cell. Prescott was the third casualty of the battle.
