Givens (stormtrooper)
1773

Givens was a Human male who served as a stormtrooper, holding the rank of corporal, during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, he was a member of the platoon led by Cadet Leader Kyle Katarn where they were assigned aboard the Star Destroyer Imperator to take control over AX-456. Along with Morely, he was the team's demolitions expert.
