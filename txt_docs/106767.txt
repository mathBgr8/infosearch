thumb|right|150px|Polish hardcover - Gwiezdne Wojny: Niesamowite przekroje Star Wars: Complete Cross-Sections is a 2007 reference book written by David West Reynolds and Curtis Saxton, with illustrations by Hans Jenssen and Richard Chasemore. Covering the entire Star Wars film saga, it is the fifth installment in the Star Wars Incredible Cross-Sections series. It is a compilation of&mdash;and in some places, expansion on&mdash;the original four cross-section books. Entries in bold denote new additions or updates to this version.
