CDEF blaster pistol
4384

}} The CDEF blaster pistol was a blaster pistol produced by Corellian Arms. 200px|thumb|left|A CorSec officer using a CDEF pistol The CDEF blaster was a basic, cheap, and mass-produced design pistol manufactured by Corellian Arms. CDEF pistols lacked power and precision and were often of poor quality. Although the weapon lacked the stopping power of comparable blasters, the weapon's relatively small size, reliability, and ease of maintenance made it an appreciated sidearm. The CDEF blaster pistol was the standard sidearm issued to CorSec officers and had been kept it in service with the Corellian Security Force since before the Clone Wars. During the Galactic Civil War, CorSec personnel were also known to use variations of in the CDEF line, particularly a carbine and rifle. The Ithorian known as Tendau Nandon was known to carry one, although he was never comfortable with brandishing a weapon.
