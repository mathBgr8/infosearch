Bioto
1297

Bioto was a male Koorivar smuggler from Murkhana, serving as the captain of the Dead Ringer and a rival of Cash Garrulan. Garrulan framed Bioto, resulting in his ship being impounded by the Exactor under the incorrect suspicion of carrying Jedi Purge survivors Roan Shryne and Olee Starstone.
