Force weapon
5189

thumb|right|250px|[[Vodo-Siosk Baas uses a Force-imbued walking stick to duel Exar Kun]] Force Weapon was a Force ability that allowed the user to imbue an unpowered weapon with the Force for a period of time. By channeling energy into the weapon, it allowed the wielder to strike and do more damage than the weapon's simple appearance would suggest. As a useful side effect, a Force-imbued weapon could be used to block lightsabers without damaging the weapon.Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 1 The ancient Je'daii of Tython,Star Wars: Force and Destiny Core Rulebook and later the Jedi, Force-imbued their swordsThe New Essential Chronology using Vur Tepe.Nexus of Power Most of the users of this power were those outside the Jedi Order, many from cultures and Force traditions that were considered quite primitive, such as the Zeison Sha, who used this power to augment their discblades,Hero's Guide though there were some exceptions. Jedi Master Vodo-Siosk Baas used this power in his duels against Exar Kun, using his humble walking stick to counter Kun's lightsaber skills.Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War 3 The Force-sensitive natives of Felucia were also known to employ such an ability, infusing their jawbone swords with the Force to increase their cutting power and allow them to block lightsabers.Star Wars: The Force Unleashed Darth Vader and Starkiller were able to imbue and charge their lightsabers with the Force. In Starkiller's case, his lightsaber emitted Force lightning or Force Push, granting him attack capabilities that opponents were rarely able to predict, much less counter. Force-imbued weapons could also damage and destroy dark side spirits.The Dark Side Sourcebook It is also possible that Kreia might have used this power during her adventures with Meetra Surik.
