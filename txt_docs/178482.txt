Series 401 hyperdrive motivator
1265

The Series 401 hyperdrive motivator was the primary hyperspace propulsion system of the Millennium Falcon following its upgrade from a Isu-Sim SSP05 hyperdrive generator. This was considered to be a "more efficient" unit, but apparently had the same Hyperdrive Rating of 0.5 as the heavily-modified SSP05 (though it is technically possible that the drives were merely swapped back to restore the Falcon's previous 0.5 rating by the end of the Black Fleet Crisis).Cracken's Threat Dossier *Before the Storm *Cracken's Threat Dossier Category:Hyperdrive motivators
