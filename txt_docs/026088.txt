Imperial-class repulsortank
3351

The Imperial-class, or Imperial-series repulsortank, was a line of military vehicles made by Ubrikkian Industries. Three versions of the tank were created; light, medium, and heavy variants. The designation applied to the amount of armament carried by each variant tank as all three tanks of the Imperial-class shared the same chassis design. The heavy variant of the Imperial repulsortank was the standard production model. thumb|right|150px|Imperial Repulsortank 1-L The cheapest version of the Imperial-class repulsortank, the 1-L lacked the main gun found on the heavier variants. It required a crew of only two to operate&mdash;driver and gunner&mdash;and had space for either the sergeant or platoon sergeant major. It mounted a single Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. Mark 3/S Medium Blaster Cannon. thumb|right|150px|Imperial Repulsortank 1-M The medium Imperial repulsortank was produced in limited numbers and intended for use by platoon officers to command their units in the field. It required a crew of three to operate&mdash;driver, gunner, and commanding lieutenant&mdash;with enough space for the sergeant major and communications equipment as well. The 1-M mounted a Merr-Sonn Mark 3/S Medium Blaster Cannon and a heavy repeating blaster. thumb|right|150px|Imperial Repulsortank 1-H The heavy Imperial repulsortank was the most widely produced of the Imperial-class and required a crew of four to operate&mdash;driver, two gunners, and the commanding sergeant. Considered to be a squad level unit in itself for the purposes of the Order of Battle, the 1-H mounted a Merr-Sonn Mark 4e/S Heavy Laser Cannon and a Mark 3/S Medium Blaster Cannon.
