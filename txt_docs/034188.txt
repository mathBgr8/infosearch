Polyhedron 104

Polyhedron 104 was a roleplaying magazine from February of 1995. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The article was called "Dangers from the Dark Side: Villains for the Star Wars: New Republic Campaign, Part Two" and was written by Bill Slavicsek and Michele Carter. Category:Polyhedron
