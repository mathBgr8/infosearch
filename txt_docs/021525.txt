It was written by Louise Simonson, illustrated by June Brigman and Roy Richardson, and first published by Dark Horse Comics on September 19, 1995. Leia has uncovered Ranulf as an Imperial spy! She and her covert Rebel team are holding him prisoner and preparing his execution, but when the Empire has the Rebel Mora in its merciless grasp, it's Ranulf who volunteers to rush to her rescue!
