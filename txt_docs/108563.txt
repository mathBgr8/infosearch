Vibrodagger Killer
1336

The Vibrodagger Killer was a dangerous criminal who committed several murders, presumably with a vibrodagger. The Empire turned a blind eye to his gruesome crimes, because all his victims had been Alliance sympathizers. Members of the Rebel Alliance therefore took matters into their own hands. When they believed to have finally found out the identity of the Vibrodagger Killer, they sent out a holograph and dossier of the suspected individual to their HoloNet slicers to get the information out to their field agents. The Vibrodagger Killer is mentioned in the mission description of a delivery mission called "By Any Means Necessary", that players of Star Wars Galaxies can get at Rebel Mission Terminals.
