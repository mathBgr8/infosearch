Sega
1774

thumb|Sega Corporation logo Sega Corporation is the international videogame corporation based on Japan. In the 1980s and 1990s, it was a major competitor to Nintendo, and manufactured numerous home video game consoles and arcade games. After the commercial failure of the Dreamcast console, Sega exited the home console market and focused on developing third-party software instead, where it remains extremely profitable.
