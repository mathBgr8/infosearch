Malo
1189

Malo was a Rodian smuggler and an associate of Han Solo. He and another Rodian sometimes employed Solo to smuggle spice. Malo and his partner made one such exchange with Solo in 0 ABY, allowing Imperial prisoner Mira Volz to sneak aboard Solo's Millennium Falcon. *"The Second Kessel Run" Category:Males Category:Rodians Category:Smugglers
