Darth Karnage
1688

|masters= |apprentices= }} Darth Karnage was a Sith. As with many Sith Lords throughout history, Karnage's name included the Darth title. Darth Karnage was mentioned in a 2006 blog entry written by Leland Chee, the keeper of the Holocron continuity database. Karnage was the only Sith listed in the blog post who never appeared or received a mention in another source. The entry indicates that Karnage's name is possibly derived from the word "carnage," meaning extensive slaughter or butchery of others.
