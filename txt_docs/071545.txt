One of a Kind (short story)
7184

"One of a Kind" is a short story written by Paul Danner that was originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 5 by West End Games in February 1995. It was made available online at Hyperspace on December 17, 2004. Sienn Sconn, a down-on-his-luck charmer of a thief, is passing time in the Binary Bar pondering a way to get the credits he owes his landlord for rent. Shandria L'hnnar enters the bar and sits at a table after getting a drink from the bartender. When stormtroopers arrive to arrest the rebels and L'hnnar is pursued by them, Sconn dares to rescue the New Republic agent and assists in her escape. L'hnnar carries stolen Imperial data that could save the lives of millions if delivered to the New Republic. Sconn fights off the Imperial forces and the bounty hunter Pentix Graphyt, helping her stay a step ahead of the Imperials in a high-speed speeder chase, escorting L'hnnar to the spaceport, where she gets to her Y-Wing fighter and escapes off-planet.
