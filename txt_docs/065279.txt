Remplish
1151

The Remplish were a clan native to the planet Sullust. After a long holy war on Sullust, they were cast off the planet. Many members of this clan ended up on Ord Mantell and Gamorr. While living there, they generally operated frontier posts, toll posts, space gates, as well as running almost all of the fuel ports on Gamorr. Hepmaga MiOrd was one such being who set up a hunting business in the outer wastelands of Gamorr. The Remplish were created by Jess Lackaff, a Star Wars fan, for the backstory of their Hepmaga MiOrd entry in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. Category:Families
