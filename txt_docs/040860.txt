The Ghostling Children also depicts Anakin as older than the eight-year-old Arawynne, putting the books in 33 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Episode I Adventures |preceded by=The Ghostling Children |followed by=Capture Arawynne }} Episode I Adventures 6: The Hunt for Anakin Skywalker was the sixth installment of Star Wars: Episode I Adventures written by Dave Wolverton and published by Scholastic in 2000. Anakin Skywalker and his friends have helped to free a group of Ghostling slaves. Now their owner, Gardulla the Hutt, wants to find out who's responsible. And the Hutt wants revenge. Some of the best trackers on the planet of Tatooine are searching for Anakin. Will he be caught?
