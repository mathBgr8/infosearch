|cover artist=Al Williamson |editor=*Anina Bennett *Bob Cooper |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=February 1993 |pages=30 |upc= |timeline=0 ABYThe Yavin blockade, which is depicted in Classic Star Wars 7, is dated to 0 ABY by The Essential Chronology. |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=7 }} Classic Star Wars 7 is the seventh issue of the Legends comic book series Classic Star Wars. It reprints the second half of "Deadly Reunion" and the first half of "Traitor's Gambit," which were written by Archie Goodwin, illustrated by Al Williamson, and originally serialized in the Star Wars comic strip from 1981 to 1982. Classic Star Wars 7 was first published by Dark Horse Comics on February 1993. Leia, Luke, Han, and Chewie make it out of Doctor Arakkus' death trap by the skin of their teeth and head for home at long last, only to discover that Rebel headquarters is under Imperial attack!
