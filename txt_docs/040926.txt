Mere Resistance
1636

The Mere Resistance was a small resistance movement led by former pirate Sol Sixxa, in opposition to the Trade Federation operations in the Karthakk system. They fought at many battles including the Nod Kartha, the Geonosis, and the Second Battle of Lok.
