|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYStar Wars Omnibus: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 1 |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issue=23 }} X-Wing Rogue Squadron 23 was the twenty-third issue in the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron series of comics. It was released on October 1, 1997 by Dark Horse Comics. Rogue Squadron brings the battle to the Imperials as X-wings and TIEs zigzag among the lofty minarets of Brentaal. More than one Rogue will be shot down this day, but an X-wing pilot on the ground with a blaster is twice as angry and almost as dangerous!
