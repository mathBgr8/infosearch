WA-2 waitress droid
1311

The WA-2 waitress droid was a line of serving droids. One such model was employed as a waitress at Panky's diner on the planet Barlok in 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. WA-2 waitress droids appeared in Timothy Zahn's 2006 novel Outbound Flight. *Outbound Flight Category:Waiter droid models
