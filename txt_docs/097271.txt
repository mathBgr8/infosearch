Scarification Ceremony
861

The Scarification Ceremony was a Vunakian celebration. During the ritual the Vunakians would cut their faces with sharp implements without the benefit of any sort of anesthetic, while trying to follow a geometrical pattern. The ritual would result in the Vunakian' foreheads and cheeks being covered with protuberant scars and ritual drawings. A Vunakian's caste could be determined based on the configuration of the scarring across their head. This ceremony caused a level of suffering past the Vunakian's pain resistance thresholds, resulting in brave people, capable of resisting extreme situations. The Vunakian Republic Colonel Kohn Doil went through the scarification ceremony. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons Category:Ceremonies
