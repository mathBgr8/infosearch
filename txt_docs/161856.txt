Sometime following the Battle of Yavin in 0 BBY, she operated at the Nightsister Stronghold. There, she served the Nightsisters as a Rancor Tamer. Diax was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Diax was one of the quest givers of the original "Singing Mountain Clan Theme Park." This quest series was removed from the game upon the release of the revamped "Witches of Dathomir Theme Park," introduced in the Game Update 19 on December 14, 2010.
