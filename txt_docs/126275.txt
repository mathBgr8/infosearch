Marcopius
1492

Yeoman Marcopius was one of Chief of State Leia Organa Solo's Honor Guard and a graduate of the New Republic Fleet Academy. Although not initially killed by the Death Seed plague, he survived long enough to take a small lifeboat off of the Borealis with the droids C-3PO and R2-D2 and into hyperspace, where he died.
