Acceptance
2588

The Acceptance was a Judicial Forces Consular-class cruiser piloted by Lieutenant Antidar Williams. It was one of the transports at the Battle of Galidraan, and Jedi Master Dooku's flagship. It was destroyed at the Battle of Uhanayih, though Williams and Maoi Madakor escaped.
