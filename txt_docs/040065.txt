Andrew Gilbertson co-contributed to the backstory of Leektar appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. His alias is Hecko Tenton on Star Wars Hyperspace, Zarm R'keeg on StarWars.com, and Zarm_Rkeeg on TheForce.net. (co-written by Arthur Papadam) - November 2005 Category:What's The Story? authors
