Aba
688

Aba was one of a pair of Wookiee dolls given to Jacen and Jaina Solo by Chewbacca after returning from a visit to Kashyyyk, that had occurred prior to their visit to Munto Codru in 14 ABY. Jacen named his doll Aba while Jaina's was Eba. *The Crystal Star *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded Category:Toys and merchandise
