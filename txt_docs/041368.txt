YV-664 light freighter
1644

The YV-664 light freighter was a modern light freighter model produced by the Corellian Engineering Corporation. It was regarded by many as having a good balance of cargo capacity, durability, and speed. The ship had the saucer-like shape of the YT-series, giving it the ability to move around easily. The YV-664 had many similarities to the YT-1300 light freighter, such as the cockpit style and engine configuration, though a few differences from past models existed.
