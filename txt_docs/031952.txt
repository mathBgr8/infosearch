Sith escort gunship
1770

The Sith escort gunship was an attack craft employed by the Sith Empire. It served as the primary warship of the Sith naval forces. The Sith escort gunship was designed for speed and agility in combat, contrasting the larger, slower Derriphan-class battleships of the era. This gunship was 75 meters long and was armed with six autoblasters, and was capable of carrying 30 troops into battle, plus up to 250 metric tons of cargo.
