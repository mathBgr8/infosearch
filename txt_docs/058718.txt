When your back's up to the wall and your very soul's at stake; when the fate of the universe rests in your hands and the entire Empire's looking to disarm you; you can fold like a house of cards or you can use a little Force. Reprinted from the original Marvel Star Wars series, this is the second of two newly colored, prestige-format issues bringing the first Luke and Leia, the swashbuckling Han Solo and his trusted companion Chewbacca, the dastardly Darth Vader, and the faithful droids Artoo-Detoo and See-Threepio.
