Baden Lathe
1255

Baden Lathe was a captain in New Republic Intelligence Service. A member of the Analysis Bureau, Lathe was responsible for compiling a report on the events leading to the marriage of Leia Organa Solo, including a dossier on Warlord Zsinj and his forces, information on the Hapes Consortium, and as much data as was available about the Witches of Dathomir. Lathe did all he could to overcome the spotty nature of Dathomiri records and the secretive tendencies of the Hapans.
