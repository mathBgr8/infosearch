Skirmish on Nal Hutta
3346

A skirmish on Nal Hutta occurred when Zann Consortium forces decided to kidnap the Hutt spaceport director Tanda to corrupt Nal Hutta to further Tyber Zann's plans. Urai Fen and some Defilers landed on the planet and fought their way past various Hutt forces. They located Tanda on a landing pad and destroyed the shield generator there.
