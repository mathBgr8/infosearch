Telerath Interstellar Banking Initiative
524

The Telerath Interstellar Banking Initiative was the banking system on Telerath. In 3963 BBY it was bought out by both the Draay Trust and the Adasca BioMechanical Corporation of Arkania. The Draay Trust quickly sold most of its portion to Adascorp, as well. *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:Banks
