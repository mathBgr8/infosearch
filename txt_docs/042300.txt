Ralltiir Consolidated Marble
954

Ralltiir Consolidated Marble was a marble mining corporation on Ralltiir. Subsidized by the Galactic Empire, the company did not know that its fiercest rival, Xwizi Marble, was secretly supporting the Alliance to Restore the Republic. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Mining companies
