Jo-Mau-Rinti
1071

Jo-Mau-Rinti was a male Cerean swoop racer who served as a member of Team NaKuda during the Separatist Crisis. He perished in a massive explosion which killed nearly 200,000 swoop race spectators by an assassin droid. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cereans Category:Males Category:Swoop racers
