Oso Nim
1208

Oso Nim was a female Duros with orange eyes who was living on Belsavis in 12 ABY. She was known as something of a gossip; she spoke to Han Solo about Drub McKumb's disappearance while he and his wife visited the planet in search of clues about the Jedi.
