Hirrtu
1022

Hirrtu was a male Rodian communications officer, tail gunner and cook for the Boneyard Rendezvous. He was a typical light green Rodian with the exceptions of several splotches of blue he painted on every fifth scale of his body. He most likely died when the Boneyard was destroyed by Ben Skywalker onboard a Sith Meditation Sphere. *Legacy of the Force: Exile *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Pirates Category:Rodians
