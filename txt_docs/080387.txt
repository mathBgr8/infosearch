They were the most common firearms for stormtroopers around the year 130 ABY, during the Massacre at Ossus, and were still in use as of 137 ABY. The ARC-9965 was a significant upgrade over the antique E-11 stormtrooper rifle, and it was designed with accuracy in mind. To achieve the desired result, designers consulted some of the best sharpshooters in the galaxy.
