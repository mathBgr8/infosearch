Provocateur
2317

Provocateur was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in the service of Warlord Zsinj, commanded by Captain Joshi. In 7 ABY it was part of a task force lured into a trap by New Republic forces in the Morobe system. During the battle X-wings from Wraith Squadron delivered a barrage of proton torpedoes across the ship's midsection, literally snapping it in half.
