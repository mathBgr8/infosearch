Exhibition Day
1033

Exhibition Day was a day where Jedi Padawans and initiates of any age performed exercises while Jedi Masters watched. These exercises included Force exercises, balance, endurance, climbing, jumping and swimming. Sometimes they were split into teams of two or four. It was play but it was also serious.
