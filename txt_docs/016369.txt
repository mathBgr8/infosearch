Nyrvona
1576

Nyrvona was a planet in the Cha Raaba system of the Hutt Space. Like Ylesia, the Besadii kajidic used it as a quasi-religious spice-processing colony. In the real world, Nirvana is a Sanskrit word for "liberation." *Rebel Dawn *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Planets in Hutt Space
