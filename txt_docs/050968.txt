Tunrothese
591

Tunrothese was the native language of the Tunroth species from Jiroch-Reslia. Tunrothese was formed of grunts and snarls, using simple vocalizations, which gave it a primitive-looking aspect. Instead of an alphabet, Tunrothese used pictograms to represent only physical objects. Most Tunroths were fluent in both Tunrothese and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages Category:Tunroth culture
