During the battle, the Galactic Republic employed LIN demolitionmech autonomous minelayer droids to clear the location known as Marrow's Moor. 563 of the demolitionmech units were destroyed during that effort. The Battle of Gligger was mentioned in the 2006 reference book The New Essential Guide to Droids by Daniel Wallace. *The New Essential Guide to Droids Gligger
