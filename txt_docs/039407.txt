Zehr Sea
1013

|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} The Zehr Sea was a sea on the planet Delassin Six. The Vodran Xenon Nnaksta worked as a longshoreman at docks for vessels that traveled on the sea. The Zehr Sea was first mentioned in "The Greel Wood Haven," an article written by C. Robert Carey in Star Wars Adventure Journal 6, which was released in May 1995. Category:Seas and oceans
