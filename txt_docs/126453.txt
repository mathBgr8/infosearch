Rogh'ma Ixsan
1548

Rogh'ma Ixsan was a male Dug who served under Sebolto's employ and was also involved with a crime syndicate called RavinsBlud during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Coruscant Police for the questioning, involved in smuggling death sticks on Malastare.
