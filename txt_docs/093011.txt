The Essential Atlas establishes the Battle of Endor as occurring in 39:3, while the Star Wars Encyclopedia states that the battle of Notak happened eight months after Endor; thus, a date of 39:11 is accurate. |place=Notak |result=New Republic victory |side1=Galactic Empire |side2=New Republic |side3= |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |forces1=Victory-class Star Destroyer Harridan |forces2= |forces3= |forces4= |casual1= |casual2= |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} In 4 ABY, eight months after the Battle of Endor, a battle occurred at the planet Notak. The battle was a rear-guard action by the Galactic Empire; the Victory-class Star Destroyer Harridan was reassigned from its normal duties in the N'zoth system to support Notak and the defensive retreat from the Inner and Mid Rims. The Harridan reassignment, combined with the reassignment of most of Black Sword Command, led to the Yevethan Uprising three weeks later. The fighting at Notak was first mentioned in the novel Before the Storm.
