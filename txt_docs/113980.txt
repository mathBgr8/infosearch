Aurra Sing's blockade runner
2056

Aurra Sing's blockade runner was a small CR-series corvette that had once belonged to the Senex pirates who kidnapped Sing as a young girl. It was comparable in size to a Consular-class space cruiser and had a Corellian Engineering Corporation design aesthetic. After finishing her Anzati training, Aurra Sing took revenge on her kidnappers and stole their blockade runner.
