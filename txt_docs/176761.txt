Nizon resistance
610

The Nizon resistance were an organization of Nazren rebels formed after the Clone Wars to oppose the occupation of Nizon by the Galactic Empire, and the exploitation of their species by Trandoshan slavers. The resistance was led by a charismatic individual who only needed an impetus, provided in the form of the Alderaanian Resistance, to rise up and liberate Nizon. Category:Resistance groups
