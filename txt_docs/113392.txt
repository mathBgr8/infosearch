Battle of Mokk IX
1808

The Battle of Mokk IX was a battle of the Galactic Civil War, sometime before 11 ABY. In it, the forces of the New Republic liberated the Imperial labor world of Mokk IX. Mirith Sinn was a soldier in the New Republic forces of the battle. In the aftermath of the battle, Sish Sadeet enlisted in the New Republic. *Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire Category:Battles in the Inner Rim Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
