It was located in the Outer Rim's Kakani sector. During the Galactic Civil War, Princess Leia crash-landed on Nibiru, which prompted the mission to Nibiru. Nibiru shares its name with a real-life hoax involving a supposed planetary object known as "Nibiru," or "Planet X," which was speculated by some groups to collide with Earth in an apocalyptic cataclysm.
