C-3 passenger liner
3173

The C-3 passenger liner was a passenger vessel in use during the Galactic Civil War. They were very luxurious craft and were often spotted parked near space facilities. A fairly common sight on the spacelanes, even Emperor Palpatine employed a C-3 liner, the Excalibur, as a personal transport. It was ambushed in 3 ABY by traitorous Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin.
