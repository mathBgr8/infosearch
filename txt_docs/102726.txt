Wayland marble
437

Wayland marble was presumably a type of stone from Wayland. The Imperial Palace had tiled floors of Wayland marble. It was presumed to be extremely expensive, with each tile running at about five thousand credits. It had a translucent white appearance, with strands of gold in its surface. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point Category:Marbles
