Kaminoan nano-virus
1723

The Kaminoan Nano-Virus was a Nanogene virus created by the Separatist-aligned Kaminoan Kuma Nai to kill all clone troopers. It was discovered by the Jedi Kit Fisto and Aayla Secura a few months after the Battle of Geonosis, who were staying at Kamino when they passed a squad of dead troops. After confirming a deadly virus, Prime Minister Lama Su issued Clone Master Sayn Ta to create a vaccine to protect all clones. Fisto and Secura tracked the defector and killed her, but could not save Sayn Ta from being killed.
