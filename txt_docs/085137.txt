Kallebarth Way
524

Kallebarth Way was a 250-meter-long hallway onboard Toryaz Station's Narsacc Habitat. It was directly below Varganner Way. In 40 ABY, during negotiations with the Corellian alliance, it was where the Galactic Alliance delegates were housed. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Roadways Category:Spacecraft locations
