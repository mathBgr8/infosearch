Jedi Men From Baronis
896

Jedi Men From Baronis was a Jediboh Opera play written by Master Thespian Damien Tantrellius, who also played the leading role. It brought him fame when he played it on Bespin and was about confused identities when two traveling bantha salesmen were mistakenly thought to be Jedi Knights and were thus forced to defend a small village from Isorpian Raiders. However, during the play, the bantha salesmen encountered a real Jedi, who saved them while he attempted to hide his true identity. It had a sequel, Two Jedi Men From Baronis.
