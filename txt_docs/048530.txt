Green Squad 7
682

Green Squad 7 was one of eight Rebel Alliance training squads who trained at Alliance Training Center-5/Green Squad on the planet Ksift. When the Galactic Empire attacked the base, an initiate of this squad met with a group of trainees from Green Squad who would go on to become Green Squad 3 and told them that their help was needed out front to combat the Imperial forces.
