|publisher=Decipher |release date=January 12, 2000 |figures= |cards=12 cards *8 Dark Side *4 Light Side |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Enhanced Cloud City |followed by=Jabba's Palace Sealed Deck }} Enhanced Jabba's Palace was a line of cards created for the Star Wars Customizable Card Game. The product was created and produced by Decipher before losing the license to Wizards of the Coast. The line was made in an effort to get rid of excess Jabba's Palace packs. EJP (as it is commonly called) was notable for containing a card of Mara Jade, the first live-action depiction of that character. Model Shannon Baksa posed for the image. Can you say Mara Jade? Finally, the fabled character (from the Timothy Zahn novels and 'Tales From Jabba's Palace') comes alive as one of the 12 new Premium cards for this enhanced set. Each of the four Enhanced tuck boxes contains 4 booster packs from the original Jabba's Palace expansion set plus 3 new Premium cards.
