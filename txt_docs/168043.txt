JZ-5 Fusial Thrust Engine
1788

The JZ-5 Fusial Thrust Engine was a type of sublight engine that was developed and manufactured by Slayn & Korpil. While the engine installed on an A/SF-01 starfighter was usually a Quadex Kyromaster, alternate configurations mounted four individual JZ-5 Fusial Thrust Engines.
