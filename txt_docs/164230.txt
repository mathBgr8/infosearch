Solitaire
519

Solitaire was a game meant for one person to play. The term also referred to a situation in which one side was acting as another to give the impression that there was no second side. Solitaire was mentioned in 2002 novel The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey, written by Elaine Cunningham. It is a real-world game. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey Category:Card games
