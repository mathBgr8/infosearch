Palhra Wavren
858

Palhra Wavren was a civilian who was held hostage by Roshu Sune guerrillas when a commuter hopper she was traveling on was hijacked and set down in the swamps outside of Skreeka, on Atzerri, where the Gotal guerrillas had a temporary base. Along with 12 other hostages out of 25, Wavren was rescued by a Jedi task force led by Sarrissa Jeng, although she was injured in the fight, probably by burns or smoke inhalation. Wavren was taken to Talos Port Medicomplex to recover. Wavren, Palhra
