thumb|250px|[[Wedge Antilles/Legends|Wedge Antilles uses wing-tip against a TIE fighter.]] The wing tip was a maneuver utilized by Rogue Squadron's Wedge Antilles during a dogfight over the planet Cilpar in 4 ABY. Getting in close behind an Imperial TIE fighter, he used the starboard S-foils of his X-wing to shear off the port wing of the enemy fighter, sending it slamming into the ground. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 1 Category:Starfighter tactics
