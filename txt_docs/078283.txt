Klol fungus
1434

Klol fungus was a type of fungus that grew on the Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Qiilura and was present in the year 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, which includes the appearance of klol fungus, in the year 22 BBY. It grew quickly, appearing as a pale pink mesh on fallen tree trunks in the span of a few days. Klol fungus appeared in the 2004 novel Hard Contact, the first novel in the Republic Commando series written by Karen Traviss. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Fungi
