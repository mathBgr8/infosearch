Voor Viper
1636

Voor Viper was a modified Lambda-class T-4a shuttle that was owned and operated by Tig Fromm approximately 15 BBY. The vessel was not in prime condition and was prone to malfunctioning. After the destruction of the Trigon One, the Voor Viper attacked Kea Moll's Sand Sloth, but the Fromms were not able to capture the ship.
