Carrion may refer to one of the following: *Carrion, a synonym for the ice scrabbler creature *Carrion sector, a sector in the Outer Rim Territories *Carrion Spike, a wildlife reserve near the Tarkin family's compound on Eriadu *The Carrion Spike, the corvette of Wilhuff Tarkin named after the reserve
