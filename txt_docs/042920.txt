thumb|right|200px|View of Cells 49557Ceta from above. Cells 49557Ceta was a prison complex of the Galactic Republic. Located in the undercity of Coruscant, it was a high-security establishment made to hold humanoid prisoners awaiting shipment to permanent off-planet prisons.
