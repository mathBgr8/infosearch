Beroya
919

Beroya (meaning "bounty hunter" in Mando'a) was a Mandalorian-designed and built Gladiator-class assault fighter that was flown by Goran Beviin. Beviin used it during the Yuuzhan Vong War and the Second Galactic Civil War. He took the ship to Corellia during Boba Fett's plan to break the Galactic Alliance blockade and get onto the planet. *Boba Fett: A Practical Man *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gladiator-class assault fighters Category:Personal starships
