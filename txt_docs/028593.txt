Parjai Squad
2418

Parjai Squad was an elite unit of clone paratroopers in the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars under the command of Sergeant Barlex. The squad was part of the 2nd Airborne Company, itself a unit of the 7th Sky Corps's 212th Attack Battalion, led by Clone Marshal Commander CC-2224 and High Jedi General Obi-Wan Kenobi. Parjai Squad was one of the first squads to land in the Battle of Utapau and later aided in the subjugation of Utapau following the transmission of Order 66. The clone troopers of Parjai Squad wore specialized armor and helmets that allowed for high altitude drops.
