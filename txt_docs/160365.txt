TS-Titan
1647

The TS-Titan was an industrial-grade beamdrill manufactured by Karflo Corporation. It was designed for use on large-scale construction projects, such as starship manufacturing, and building structures and highways. Measuring over ten meters long, the TS-Titan was mounted on a repulsorlift platform. The plasma beam generated by the TS-Titan was ten meters long, and up to three meters in diameter.
