Wookiee Slug-Thrower
1654

}} The Wookiee Slug-Thrower was a model of slugthrower rifle that was mass-produced by Wookiee workshops across the planet Kashyyyk around the time of the Clone Wars. Near the middle of it's body it had multiple cartridges for blaster gas. The weapon was used by various Wookiee warriors during the Battle of Kashyyyk at the end of the Clone Wars. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: Battles for the Galaxy Category:Slugthrower rifles Category:Wookiee weapons
