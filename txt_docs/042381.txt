Hool served under Commander Ozrei N'takkilomandrife and was ordered to lead the attack force in the Second Battle of Endor. He led a squad of Nagai and Maccabree warriors in an assault on the Alliance of Free Planets flight center. The Marvel comic calls this Nagai "Hool", yet both an article in Star Wars Gamer 1 and Ultimate Alien Anthology call him "Hol."Ultimate Alien Anthology It is clear from the context that these are the same individuals.
