Tsuyoshi Nagano
6878

Tsuyoshi Nagano (Japanese: 長野 剛) is an artist who does Japanese cover art versions for the Star Wars novels. In addition to his Star Wars works, he's done illustrations for KOEI PC/Online game series of Nobunaga's Ambition and Romance of the Three Kingdoms, Toho's Godzilla, Mothra and King Ghidorah: Giant Monsters All-out Attack, and covers for Sekai Bunka Publishing Sanguozhi (Three Kingdom Saga). He is a member of the Society of Illustrators and the Japan Publication Artist League. Around 1998, artist Tsuyoshi Nagano saw a Star Wars novel at a bookstore and was inspired to ask his employer, Takeshobo, publisher of the Japanese Star Wars novels, if he could do cover art for the novels. Nagano had been a fan of Star Wars since seeing it in school, and the first laserdisc he bought was Star Wars, so he was "really excited" to take on the project. The first job he got was to do the cover art for a Willow novel, and his boss Junzo Takagi was impressed with his work. Afterwards, Nagano was given the go-ahead to create his first Star Wars novel cover art for the Hand of Thrawn duology.
