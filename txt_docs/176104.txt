Kep
639

Kep was the unit of measurement Adumari pilots used in flight speed instead of the galactic standard klicks. They also use the Adumar hour instead of Coruscant hour. Its origins lie on the exact thousand paces measured by the stride of some long-dead Cartann perator. The Adumari measurement was about eighty percent of the Imperial standard. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Units of measurement
