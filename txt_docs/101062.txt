Michaud Industries
880

Michaud Industries was a droid component manufacturer that specialized in manipulative droid parts, such as heavy-duty arms and retractors. It also produced ship parts such as blaster radiators. Michaud Industries company shares its name with Collette Michaud, who worked as Project Leader, Designer and Writer on the 1998 video game Star Wars: DroidWorks. *Star Wars Math: Jabba's Game Galaxy *Star Wars: DroidWorks Category:Droid manufacturers Category:Vehicle manufacturing companies
