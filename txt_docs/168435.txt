Docking Bay 32
1302

Docking Bay 32 was a starship harbor at Anchorhead, Tatooine. Revan used it when visiting the planet on his search for a Star Map located on Tatooine. Jor Ul Kurax and Mic'tunan'jus Orgu both worked in the docking bay at the time of the Jedi Civil War. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Anchorhead locations Category:Docking bays
