Galentro Heavy Works
1579

|role=Conglomerate of businesses |products=Enforcer-class picket cruiser |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=Pentastar Alignment }} Galentro Heavy Works was a major conglomerate. They owned and operated the Jaemus shipyards, and controlled the government of Bescane. They were most famous for their starship design, the Enforcer-class picket cruiser. Galentro Armaments was a subsidiary company.
