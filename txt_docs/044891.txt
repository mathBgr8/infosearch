Cerean (language)
3241

Cerean was the official spoken and written language of the planet Cerea.Ultimate Alien Anthology It was mainly spoken by native Cereans. Despite their isolationist stance,Star Wars: Prelude to Rebellion the Cereans also learned to speak Basic, teaching it during childhood. "Chu'unthor" was the Cerean word for "academy," and the word "Barsen'thor" translated to "Warden of the Order." Many non-Cereans learned to speak the language, including Follnor Callat,Star Wars Gamer 9 Miltar Dolor,Tempest Feud and Angela Krin.
