Dasobo Meats
772

Dasobo Meats was a meat-shipping company based on Yulant. They distributed for the Salliche Ag Corporation. Sometime before the Clone Wars, Salliche sued Dasobo, claiming Dasobo stole their genetic secrets used to breed gornts, which Salliche discovered by utilizing nanoscopic analyses of their Gornt Cutlets.
