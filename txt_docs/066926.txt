Santhe Security
2356

Santhe Security was a subsidiary company of Santhe Corporation. Santhe Security provided security forces for Imperial outposts and space stations during the time of the Galactic Empire. Santhe Security had its own force of military personnel, referred to as "Santhe Security troopers." They typically operated in squads of five to ten troopers and carried blaster pistols, heavy blaster pistols, or blaster rifles. They were trained in unarmed combat and other types of melee combat as well as repulsorlift operation and operation of vehicle blasters. Santhe Security personnel wore uniforms similar to that of the Imperial Fleet Regiment, except they wore silver helmets and had silver piping running down their shoulders, arms and legs.
