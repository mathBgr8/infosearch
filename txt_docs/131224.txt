Galney family
658

The Galney family was an important Hapan family from Terephon. Members included Lady Galney and Ducha Galney. The family was considered to be a staunch supporter of Queen Mother Tenel Ka, and controlled the Galney fleet. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest Category:Families Category:Hapans
