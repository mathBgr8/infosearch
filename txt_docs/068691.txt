Maggy the Gorgon's
1247

Maggy the Gorgon's was a tavern in a section of Mos Espa on Tatooine that was not controlled by the Hutts. The tasty drink, ruby bliel, was known to have been served there. It was often a dangerous place to enter, home to plenty of brawls. Shmi Skywalker asked her son Anakin to buy his ruby bliel from here, rather than from the Blue Brubb.
