Ganza Park
1402

Ganza Park was a park located in the Historical District of Estalle, a city found on Estalle Island on the planet Procopia. The park consisted of a large preserve made up of rock and flower gardens, rolling hills and isolated groups of trees. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, the area was some of the most undeveloped and valuable real estate on all of Estalle island. By this time a large tea house named the Greenhouse had opened within the park. Built with a view of the sea, the tea house was considered a highlight of the preserve.
