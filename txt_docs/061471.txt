Farboon
1455

Farboon was a planet that appeared green-blue from space. It was the site where Maarek Stele, a TIE/LN starfighter pilot, rescued Imperial Admiral Mordon's shuttle from a Rebel attack of X-wing and Y-wing fighters. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories Category:Tantra sector locations
