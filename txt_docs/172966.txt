|pages= |upc= |timeline=Various |series=Tokyopop's Star Wars Manga |issue=2 }} Star Wars Manga: Silver is a limited edition of the manga One-Shots published by Tokyopop. It is the second issue following Star Wars Manga: Black. Although Leland Chee himself has stated that their canon status is fuzzy, the stories in this issue are all labeled as Infinites in the most recent French collection. *Perfect Evil Part 2 by Hiromoto-SIN-ichi *Oh!! Jawajawa by Hitoshi Ariga *Star Wars -Phantasmagoria- by Baron Ueda *Vader vs. Artoo & Threepio by Makomo Nakatsuka *Dark Jedi by Yasushi Nirasawa left|thumb|Illustration by Yuji Kaida In addition to the stories, this issue contains two colored fold-out illustrations of Darth Vader by Yuji Kaida and Kia Asamiya.
