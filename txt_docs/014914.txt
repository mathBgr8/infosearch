Kidiet Olgo
3569

Kidiet Olgo was a celestial body and a shadowport located in the Kidiet Olgo system, a part of the Northern Dependencies portion of the Core Worlds. A hyperlane connected it to the Mamendin system of the Farrfin sector. Graxol Kelvyyn, an Anx enslaver operating throughout the Outer Rim Territories around 32 BBY, establishes that Graxol Kelvyyn operated as an enslaver at the same time he attended Podraces on Tatooine. depicts Kelvyyn attending the planet's Boonta Eve Classic Podrace, and The New Essential Chronology dates the film's events to 32 BBY. associated himself with a network of enslaved beings' smugglers, some of which made their living at Kidiet Olgo. Kidiet Olgo was first mentioned in "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Anx," a roleplaying source article published in the seventh issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine on October 16, 2001 for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game.
