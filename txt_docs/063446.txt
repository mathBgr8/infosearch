Ryloth (CR90 corvette)
2048

The Ryloth was a Corellian Corvette in service to the New Republic during the Galactic Civil War. It was part of the New Republic fleet during the Seizure of Coruscant. Along with the Devonian, the ship was dispatched by Ackbar to rescue any survivors on a crippled Golan III Space Defense NovaGun. It also joined the task force commanded by Admiral Ackbar during the Battle of Ciutric.
