Slici Canyon
567

Slici Canyon was one of the many surface features of the planet Kamino that were covered with water during the Great Flood. Located on the planet's southern hemisphere, Slici Canyon plunged some 10,000 meters beneath the surface of the water. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Canyons Category:Kamino locations
