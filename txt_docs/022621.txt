Tim Veekhoven
16269

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=Belgian |occupation=*Writer *Webjournalist |companies= |website= }} Tim Veekhoven (born May 5, 1976) has contributed to StarWars.com, the Star Wars Blog, Star Wars Insider, Star Wars: Build the Millennium Falcon, and Star Wars Encyclopedia from De Agostini. Tim Veekhoven is the author of the backstories of Swilla Corey, Tzizvvt, Maxiron Agolerga and Wam Lufba appearing in the original Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. He is co-founder and president of TeeKay-421, the Belgian Star Wars fan club, and Administrator of Yodapedia, the Dutch/Flemish Star Wars wiki. In September 2012 he was honored as a Friend of the Rebellion and in 2015 an Honorary Member by Rebel Legion Benelux. At Celebration Europe II he participated at the International Star Wars Fandom panel with Gerald Home on Friday and organized the Star Wars Trivia Challenge with TeeKay-421 on Sunday. Together with Kevin Beentjes and Sander de Lange, he researched and wrote for the Rogues Gallery feature in Star Wars Insider, starting with issue 145.
