Hedda (Jedi Knight)
1412

Hedda was a female Jedi Knight who served the New Jedi Order during the Second Galactic Civil War. In 40 ABY, she was stationed at the Ossus Academy. Having just been knighted, Hedda was ordered to protect the younglings at the Academy when the Galactic Alliance Guard attempted to "secure" it, holding the students hostage.
