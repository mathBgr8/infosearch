Boozoo Boga
1591

Boozoo "Bogey" Boga was a male Aqualish bounty hunter hijacker who lived during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He recruited criminals into the Galactic Radicals. He was also known as Boozoo Bogs. He was wanted by the bounty hunter Papeh Blangko. He was killed by Jango Fett who claimed the bounty sometime after the battle of Naboo.
