After a lot of hard work, he became one of Borvo's champions. His next mission was to go to the Gungan Sacred Place and wipe out a group of Gungan traders that crossed Borvo. After the Battle of Yavin, spacers learned about the legendary fighting skills of Mordran and challenged him to a duel. Mordran appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Mordran can be found near the Gungan Sacred Place on Naboo, close to Boss Nass.
