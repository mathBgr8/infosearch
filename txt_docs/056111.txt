Obliterator
3029

The Obliterator was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer serving in the starfleet of the Imperial Remnant at least fifteen years after the Battle of Endor. Under the command of Captain Trazzen, this was one of four Braxant Sector Fleet Star Destroyers&mdash;the others were the Relentless, the Tyrannic and the Ironhand&mdash;that pledged its allegiance to a recuperated Grand Admiral Thrawn, who was in fact a con-artist named Flim. Its mission was to exploit the Caamas Document Crisis in order to begin a civil war within the New Republic. In that capacity, Obliterator was outfitted with a cloaking shield.
