Destroy the Liquidator additionally denotes a December 1997 release date. |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-12791-8 |issn= |genre= |issue=4 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 4 is the fourth issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in December 1997. Issue 4 included part four of the serial comic The Rebel Thief, written by Ryder Windham. Star Wars Kids 4 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book.
