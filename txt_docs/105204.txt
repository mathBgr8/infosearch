MuniCenter Records Office
426

The MuniCenter Records Office was a room in the MuniCenter in Plawal Rift on Belsavis. It contained the business and worker records for the three major companies of the Plawal area. In 12 ABY it was run by a Bith. *Children of the Jedi Category:Belsavis locations Category:Offices
