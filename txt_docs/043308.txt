Midakinos was a barren, frigid satellite, and the fourth moon of Ovise V. It was inhabited by 200,000 Humans, though the atmosphere required the population to wear breath masks. A day lasted for 31 standard hours and its orbital period lasts 300 standard days. The moon's gravity was 90% of standard.
