Jervis Gloom
2293

Jervis Gloom was a Human male death stick dealer who lived on Coruscant and had long posed problems for the Coruscant Security Force during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, Jervis Gloom was believed to have grown up in the bowels of Coruscant's rough streets. He made a living peddling death sticks in Coruscant's entertainment districts working for crime lords as they came and went - earning him a 5,000 credit bounty (alive) by the CSF - until he was captured by Jango Fett.
