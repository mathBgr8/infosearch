|birth= |death= |species=Human |gender=Female |pronouns=She/her |height= |mass= |hair=Blonde |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Light |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Palee Ruda was a Human female clubgoer native to the planet Coruscant. During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. she visited the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall on Coruscant. Ruda exited the club accompanied by a Pacithhip escort and the Kajain'sa'Nikto Mois Parcon, just as the bounty hunter Zam Wesell ran into the club from the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker after her assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala. She unwittingly missed Wesell's arm being dismembered by Kenobi. Ruda had light skin and blonde hair. She had a feminine first name of Human origin.Galactic Campaign Guide On that night, Ruda, a poorly dressed individual, wore an intimidating ensemble of white face makeup with painted on eyebrows, a breathable chainmail veil, a top, and black pants. Palee Ruda first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The Outlander Club exterior scenes, which included Ruda, were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney.Creating the Worlds of Star Wars: 365 Days Prior to the film's release, she was first pictured in January 2001 with the "Prequel Update" section of the fifty-first issue Star Wars Insider.
