Slane Vick
1516

Slane Vick was a Human male private who served in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. He was a guard aboard the Ranger, along with Corporal Ram Raders, when General Nejaa Halcyon repelled battle droids boarding the ship. He later served on the ground in a scouting mission with Corporal Raders, Grudo, and Sergeant Omin L'Loxx.
