Thinin
2764

Thinin was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate operating in the Imperial Navy under Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. When the Grand Admiral turned rogue, in 3 ABY, and led a coup d'etat against the Empire, the Thinin remained under Zaarin's command. Zaarin utilized the Thinin as a support vessel alongside the Carrack-class light cruiser Yaai and the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Glory during his attempt to capture a large amount of Missile Boats. The then Vice Admiral Thrawn had, unfortunately for Zaarin, set the missile boats up as bait in a trap to capture Zaarin and destroy his task force. However, Thrawn in turn was thwarted by the fact that the Immobilizer 418 cruiser Whirlwind was stalled by a surprise ambush and thus the trap failed to pin down Zaarin. Yet Zaarin did not make good use of his advantage.
