|media type=Compact Disc |run time= |discs=1 |tracks=13 |upc= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Corellian Edition was initially a promotional CD packaged with some copies of the Revenge of the Sith soundtrack as a Target-exclusive edition. However, it was later re-packaged and sold on its own. It contained a selection of music from each of the six Star Wars films released up to that point.
