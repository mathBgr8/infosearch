Additionally, commandos were allowed to choose the colors of their suits.Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, New Edition The armor shared its name with the Katarn species, which was native to the world of Kashyyyk.Star Wars: Complete Locations The armor was durable and had blast-resistant properties; for instance, clone commando Gregor was able to survive two point-blank shots to his chest-plate during his escape from the Imperial Base on Daro. One variant of the Katarn-class armor was the Mk I Katarn-class Commando Armor. thumb|300px|Many clone commandos wore blank white armor during the Imperial Era Katarn-class commando armor was used by clone commandos throughout the Clone Wars and into the Imperial Era. The members of Clone Force 99 wore modified Katarn-class armor. During the Imperial Era, much like how most standard clone troopers were required to return their armor to a blank white state, many clone commandos returned their armor to its default white appearance.
