|type=Softcover |pages=192 |dimensions=12.0 x 9.0 x 0.7 inches |weight=2.4 pounds |isbn=1593072619 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Panel to Panel is a collection by Dark Horse Comics of a wide array of artwork from their Star Wars comics beginning in 1991. Each page is filled with bright and colorful artwork. The book is broken down into basic categories and while it is by no means authoritative, it does include a large volume of characters and cover art. *Heroes and Allies *Villains and Rogues *The Jedi *Spaceships *The Worlds of Star Wars *Index of Artists *Timeline Category:Dark Horse Books Category:Legends art books
