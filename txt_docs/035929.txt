Bongo Missile
2578

Bongo Missiles were aquatic anti-starfighter vessels of the Gungan fleet. They were based on tribubble bongos, but were armed with a surface-to-air missile launcher to defend against enemy aircraft. They were, however, defenseless against other ships or ground forces. The ship's main body was crescent-shaped and had two separate cockpits, and were driven by two propellers on outriggers. Bongo Missiles were constructed at shipyards for a cost of 100 units of carbon and 35 units of nova crystals, and could be upgraded into a more powerful vessel, the Bongo Heavy Missile. Bongo Missiles saw action against the Trade Federation in 32 BBY during the occupation of Naboo. Bongo Missiles appear in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds and its 2002 expansion pack.
