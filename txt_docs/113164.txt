VeerTa
1179

VeerTa was a female Meerian who was the leader of the Home Planet Party on the planet Bandomeer in 45 BBY. She unintentionally aided the Dark Jedi Xanatos in his plan to destroy Bandomeer and kill Qui-Gon Jinn.
