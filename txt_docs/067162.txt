Coruscant Planetary Defense Force
1477

|restored= |affiliation=*New Republic **New Republic Defense Force }} The Coruscant Planetary Defense Force (PDF) was a part of the New Republic Defense Force during the Yuuzhan Vong War. It was charged with the duty of defending Coruscant; a task which included manning the orbital defense platforms above the planet and setting up ground defenses. Carlist Rieekan was called out of retirement in 27 ABY to command the defense force.
