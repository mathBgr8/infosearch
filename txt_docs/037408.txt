|models= |availability= |role= |firstuse= |retired= |affiliation=Galactic Republic }} The Republic escape pod was a type of escape pod used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY,Star Wars: Timelines Jedi Master Yoda and clone troopers Thire, Jek, and Rys used a Republic escape pod to land on Rugosa where the Jedi Master had a meeting with the Toydarian King Katuunko. In the same year, Kel Dor Jedi Master Plo Koon and clone troopers Wolffe, Sinker, and Boost survived an attacked by the Malevolence in a Republic escape pod after their Venator-class Star Destroyer Triumphant was destroyed. The Republic escape pod first appeared in "Ambush," the first episode of the animated television series Star Wars: The Clone Wars first season, which originally aired on October 3, 2008.
