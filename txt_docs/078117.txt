Steve Buccellato
654

Steve Buccellato is a comic-book letterer and colorist. He worked on the Classic Star Wars series as collected in Classic Star Wars Volume 1: In Deadly Pursuit, Classic Star Wars Volume 2: The Rebel Storm and Classic Star Wars Volume 3: Escape to Hoth.
