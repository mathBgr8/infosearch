The planet was very icy and also had abundant natural resources. The planet also contained a ski resort on Mount Quavaar. The stohl creature, a popular guard pet, was native to Ichtor 8. The species was first discovered by Imperial scouts. Hunters were soon enamored of the inquisitive creatures, and the creatures were exported at a high price. Holovid star Shantee Ree made attack stohls fashionable. When her pet attack stohl Needla bit a fan wanting an autograph, the resulting media coverage made the attack stohl a popular target for daredevil hunters. Ree then started a Save the Stohl campaign. The planet was the site of a battle of the Western Reaches Operation during the Imperial Reconquest of the Rim in 17 BBY.
