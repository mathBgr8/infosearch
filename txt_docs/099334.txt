Wasber
1147

Wasbers were a type of poisonous stinging insect from Tenupe. Their poison worked quickly on male Human sized prey, making them a viable assassin tool, though it lost its effectiveness after a certain period of time. The poison turned the immune system against the body making it almost impossible to stop it once it started.
