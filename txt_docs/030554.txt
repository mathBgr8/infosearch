Steve Leialoha
2503

Steve Leialoha (born January 27, 1952) is a comic book artist and colorist. He inked and colored the early Marvel Star Wars issues with penciller Howard Chaykin and later returned to the title prior to its cancellation, inking Cynthia Martin's pencils. He began in the comic industry contributing to the independent comic anthology Star Reach in 1975. He would later work for Marvel Comics in the 1970s and 80s on several titles including Howard the Duck and Spider-Woman.
