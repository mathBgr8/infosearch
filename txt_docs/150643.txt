Waterspire
2704

The Waterspire was a Mon Calamari Simiyiar-class light freighter. Initially used by a crew of Rebel Alliance operatives whose primary area of operations was located in the Wilpiet area, the ship was stolen by the bounty hunter Milacass at least two months after he joined the Granse Confederacy. In the process, he also damaged Rebel property and became a being wanted by Alliance Intelligence. Milacass's made the Waterspire available to the Granse Confederacy. However, the loose organization of bounty hunters preferred to use their other two starships, the Mist Laden and Nightcast, because they were less striking&mdash;and thus, less noticeable.
