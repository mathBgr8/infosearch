T-21 C-wing
1386

The T-21 C-wing was a high-performance civilian airspeeder produced by Incom Corporation by the start of the Galactic Civil War. The controls of the T-21 were similar to that of the T-16 skyhopper and X-wing which meant that any pilot who learned to use one of the craft was able to easily adjust to the others. The craft is mentioned in the 1998 popup book X-wing: A Pocket Manual. *X-wing: A Pocket Manual Category:Airspeeder models Category:Incom Corporation products
