Temptation Canyon
920

Temptation Canyon was a deep canyon halfway between Meirm City and Quay'kizac on Sriluur. It was called Uurutche'zediev ("Temptation Canyon" in Sriluurian) because of an ancient legend of water nymphs coaxing the moon god Quay into the crevasse, then transforming into beasts and attacking him. There was a large pool of drinkable water at the eastern end but the native bandigos and length of the canyon left it mostly undisturbed by the native Weequay.
