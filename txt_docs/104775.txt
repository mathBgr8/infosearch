Alderaan Alliance
628

The Alderaan Alliance was the political organization of Alderaanians who had survived the Destruction of Alderaan. It compiled a list of all individuals affiliated with the Death Star project, and publicized it, hoping they would come to stand trial. *Children of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Culture of Alderaan Category:Political factions
