Tympanic pulser
464

The Tympanic pulser was a synthesized percussion instrument developed by the Ruurians of Ruuria. It was worn rather than held, allowing the user to play other instruments as well, assuming they had more than one set of arms. *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Category:Percussion musical instruments
