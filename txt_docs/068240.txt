Drallist Front
1393

The Drallist Front, also known as simply the Drallists were an extremist political faction on Drall. During the First Corellian Insurrection they seized power. The faction was relatively weak in itself, being little more than a subsidiary of the Sacorrian Triad and an ally of the Overden and the Human League.
