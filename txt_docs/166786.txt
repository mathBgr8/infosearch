|pages=23 |upc=759606081134 |timeline=1 ABYStar Wars: Timelines dates the events of Star Wars Book X: Mutiny at Mon Cala to 1 ABY. |series=Star Wars (2015) |issue=47 }} Star Wars 47 is the forty-seventh issue of the Marvel comic book series Star Wars. It was written by Kieron Gillen and illustrated by Salvador Larroca. It was released on May 2, 2018. The Rebellion's fight to liberate Mon Cala continues as Han, Luke and Leia undertake a perilous and nigh-impossible rescue. At the Moncaladrome on Mon Cala, Queen Trios and Dors Urtya grow impatient with the Moff Tan Hubi's uncharacteristic tardiness. When he finally arrives, C-3PO notes that the Hubi seems far too happy for the somber occasion, as he watches from the side of the theater while remarking on the tragic tale of the "Faluvian cycle" opera. Unbeknownst to everyone at the opera house, Hubi is being impersonated by the shapeshifter Tunga Arpagion. Meanwhile, underneath the ocean of Strokill Prime, the rebels are in trouble, as a massive fish tries to eat their submersible. Han Solo questions why it would want to eat a metal ship, and Leia Organa explains it's likely targeting their bright lights. Luke Skywalker puts on a suit and gets out a device called the Sled. Solo turns the lights of the submersible off, and Skywalker distracts the massive fish with his lightsaber before Chewbacca rescues him with the Sled at the last possible moment. Back at the opera house, during the intermission, Urtya and Queen Trios discuss "Hubi's" surprising behavior. The rebels work their way back into the Moncaladrome with the real Hubi in tow.
