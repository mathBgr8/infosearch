In the early years of the Galactic Civil War, the planet was the site of the secret Imperial research facility of Moff Jesco Comark's Death Hunter project, transforming captured Humans from nearby Bescane into loyal cyborg assassins. The facility was eventually destroyed by Alliance agents and nearly all of the captured humans were freed.
