Carolyn Watson Dubisch
515

Carolyn Watson Dubisch was an illustrator for Alien Encounters, a roleplaying game book from West End Games. Carolyn received her B.F.A. from New York City's School of Visual Arts in 1993, and has also produced art for children's books, magazines and comics. She is the writer and co-creator of "The People That Melt in The Rain", an award winning comic series with her husband, well known fantasy and RPG artist Michael Dubisch Category:Roleplaying game artists
