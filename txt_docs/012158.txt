Wonetun
2200

Wonetun was a Brubb male from Baros, powerfully built, and had a voice as raspy as his pitted hide. He was one of the Wild Knights, a fighter squadron made up of Force-sensitive pilots, and was personally trained by Saba Sebatyne. He ultimately survived the Yuuzhan Vong War, participating in engagements at Reecee, Coruscant, Borleias, and the final Battle of Yuuzhan'tar. Five years after the end of the Yuuzhan Vong war, Wonetun was an official Jedi Knight, and was one of many Jedi who sided with Kyp Durron and volunteered to fly a StealthX in a mission to rescue Luke Skywalker and Han Solo in Killik space.
