Star Wars: The Official Magazine 65, also styled as Star Wars Magazine 65, was the sixty-fifth issue of Star Wars: The Official Magazine, released on August 17, 2006 and covering September and October 2006. It was promoted as a "Lords of the Sith Collectors' Special Issue," and contained 100 pages. *Com-Scan *Best of Hyperspace *Jawa's Corner *Q&A *Set Piece &mdash; Death Star II Main Hangar *"Morbid Visions" *"Heritage of the Sith" *"Lessons in Light and Darkness" *"Ghosts of the Sith" *"Legacies of Future Orders" *"Virtual Sith" *"MedStar: Intermezzo" *"Betrayal is Just The Beginning" 65
