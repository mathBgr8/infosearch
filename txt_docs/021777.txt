During the Galactic Civil War, it was the site of the main headquarters of the weapons manufacturer Caliban. The Human Corman Jeihn, who hailed from Calabosh-2, was employed with Caliban as a security guard at the time. Since Caliban's marketing plan was sympathetic to the Rebel Alliance, the armed forces of the Galactic Empire soon arrived at the company's headquarters at Calabosh-2. After Caliban was nationalized by Imperial Munitions at some point by 3 ABY, states Corman Jeihn moved to Bespin and became a member of the Bespin Wing Guard after Caliban was nationalized by Imperial Munitions. "Rogues Gallery" also depicts Jeihn serving in the ranks of the Wing Guard during the events of Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 3 ABY. Therefore, Caliban must have been nationalized at some point by 3 ABY. Jeihn moved to the Outer Rim Territories planetThe Essential Atlas Bespin, where he eventually became a member of the Bespin Wing Guard security force. Calabosh-2 was first mentioned in the 2002 Wizards of the Coast sourcebook Arms & Equipment Guide, which was authored by Jeff Grubb and Owen K.C. Stephens for use with the Star Wars Roleplaying Game.
