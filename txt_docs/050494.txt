Allied Grain and Roughage
1390

Allied Grain and Roughage, or AGR, was an agricultural company that ran an agro-combine on Corellia, and possibly other planets. Baron Soontir Fel was the son of a transport flier for the company. The company sponsored an annual Harvest Festival on Corellia to celebrate the completion of a harvest. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 25 *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Agricultural companies
