Authority Officer
509

Authority Officers were the members of the Indu Council's security force, the Council Authority. They were responsible for protecting and maintaining order within the Council. One Authority Officer, Kaleb Darme, was responsible for the assassination of chief councillor Shek Barayel and the subsequent murder of his aide, Tev Aden. Category:Military ranks
