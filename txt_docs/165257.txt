Docking Bay 331

Docking Bay 331 was one of many docking bays in Karfeddion Spaceport on the planet Karfeddion. Prior to the Eriadu Trade Summit in 33 BBY, Cohl, Boiny, and Lope rendezvoused with several Nebula Front members in the bay before embarking on their attempted assassination mission of Galactic Republic Chancellor Finis Valorum. *Cloak of Deception Category:Docking bays Category:Karfeddion locations
