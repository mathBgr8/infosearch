|end date=September 25, 2002 |schedule= |format= |era= |timeline=28 BBY;Star Wars: Rite of Passage'Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force Aayla Secura discovery flashback, 46 BBY |series= |issues=4 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Rite of Passage was a story arc of the comic book series Star Wars: Republic that consisted of 4 issues. It was also the last story arc to be released under Republic former name, Star Wars. The original four issues of Star Wars: Republic: Rite of Passage each stated: "The events in this series take place 30 years prior to Star Wars: A New Hope." However, a quote made by Quinlan Vos within Star Wars (1998) 43 places the events of this series at least a year after the events of a previous arc, Star Wars: Darkness which was also set in 30 BBY. Initial comments made by author John Ostrander via the Jedi Council Forums in 2002 initially placed Rite of Passage circa 29 BBY, while both the first and second edition trade paperback collections, released in 2004, place Rite of Passage: "approximately twenty-eight years before the events in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope." 2007's Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force further corroborated the 28 BBY date through the inclusion of comments, specifically dated at 28 BBY, attributed to Tholme, shortly before Quinlan Vos became a Jedi Master, as depicted in Star Wars (1998) 45. Both Star Wars: The Comics Companion, published in 2006, and 2010's Star Wars Omnibus: Quinlan Vos: Jedi in Darkness once again erroneously placed Rite of Passage in 30 BBY. The in-universe quotes by Quinlan and Tholme, both dictate that Rite of Passage takes place in 28 BBY.
