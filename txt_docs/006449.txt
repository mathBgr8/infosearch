|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=2 BBYAccording to the entry for Soontir Fel in The New Essential Guide to Characters, this event occurs two years after the Battle of Nar Shaddaa. However, later sources shifted the battle’s date to 4 BBY. Works published around the same time as Characters, such as The New Essential Chronology, instead placed the battle in 3 BBY, which aligns with the timeline presented in Star Wars: Empire issue 8, depicting the mutiny shortly before the Battle of Yavin. More recent references, including The Essential Reader's Companion, ultimately standardized the Battle of Nar Shaddaa as occurring in 4 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Empire |issue=11 }} Empire 11 is the eleventh issue in the Star Wars: Empire series of comics. It was published on September 10, 2003 by Dark Horse Comics. While establishing the Rebel base on Yavin IV, General Dodonna clashes with fellow Rebel commander Roons Sewell over plans for the future of the still fledgling Alliance. Sewell is a charismatic leader who has won the hearts and minds of the rebel rank and file, but his flair for the dramatic worries the more pragmatic Dodonna. It's age and experience versus youth and vigor. But with the Empire searching for the new base, and ready to swoop in and destroy the rebels at the first opportunity, the behind the scenes struggle between Dodonna and Sewell could have unexpected - and unwanted - results for the Alliance! The "Darklighter" story line returns with issue #12!
