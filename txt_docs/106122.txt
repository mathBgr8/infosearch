Tommy Mac
1197

thumb|200px|Tommy Mac aka DJ Maul. Tommy Mac wrote the backstory of Cebann Veekan appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. He goes by the alias of DJ Maul: Got Feet? and has a long running Virtual Toy Room Tour blog on StarWars.com. He is a Mobile Disc Jockey, and has an entire room in his basement dedicated to his ever growing Star Wars collection. He attended Celebration 3 in 2005 dressed as "Darth Vader In A Tux" and was the organizer for Celebration IV's "Official Star Wars Bloggers Meet & Greet Party." (species name by Bob Vitas) - September 2006 Category:What's The Story? authors
