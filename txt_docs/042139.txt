Zoriis Bafka
2077

Zoriis Bafka was a female Twi'lek with green skin, who was one of the professional swoop racers Revan met in Anchorhead in 3956 BBY. She considered swoop racing a business, and swoop racers like herself the "product." She used to race under a different name when she was younger, but her career was not spectacular at that time. Revan was successful in beating Bafka's time in a swoop race. If the player takes the dark side path in the game, they can insult or threaten Zoriis.
