ABX-110 Tube-Launched ABC Scrambler
2032

The ABX-110 Tube-Launched ABC Scrambler was an ABC scrambler modified from an Imperial Army anti-ordnance EM probe. It was employed by bounty hunters to disorient targets and by Imperial forces in order to suppress large unruly crowds.Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Each ABC pod normally took one minute to charge up before it could be fired, although it could be rushed into firing in less time. Once launched, the pod would emit full-spectrum ultrasonics, biological irritants and chemical agents over an area up to thirty meters in diameter. Those caught within this area would be incapacitated for several minutes by the massive sensory overload. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Explosive projectile launchers Category:Loronar Corporation products
