Benits Stinex
1544

Benits Stinex, also known as "the Old Man", was a well known architect who designed a series of apartments in the Manarai Hills district of the planet Coruscant. He later became the chief architect and head of interior design and construction board the Death Star. He was featured regularly in Beings Holozine.
