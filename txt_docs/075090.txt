Mulch mold
704

Mulch mold was a type of black fungus from the planet Neimoidia. When marinated in the secretions of the blight beetle once its spores were developed, it was considered a delicacy by the Neimoidians. It was the favorite dish of Nute Gunray. *Darth Maul: Shadow Hunter *Star Wars: Tiny Death Star Category:Edible fungi Category:Neimoidian food
