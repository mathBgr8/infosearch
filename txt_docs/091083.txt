thumb|250px|Bevel Lemelisk, Master of Imperial Projects The Master of Imperial Projects was the title held by the chief Imperial scientist, who headed the development and production of new Imperial technology. Bevel Lemelisk held this position during the height of the New Order; after the destruction of the First Death Star, it was given to Umak Leth. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *Death Star Owner's Technical Manual Category:Galactic Empire ranks
