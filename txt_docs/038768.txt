Thwim
1646

Thwim was a Kubaz security guard employed by the Whiphid, Lady Valarian. He worked at the front reception area of the Lucky Despot Hotel & Casino shortly before the Battle of Yavin. Thwim was acquaintances with the Bith jizz musician known as Doikk Na'ts, and ushered Doikk and the rest of his band, the Modal Nodes, into the Star Chamber Café. Thwim was present when enforcers for Jabba Desilijic Tiure as well as Imperial stormtroopers raided the casino disrupting the wedding of Lady Valarian and D'Wopp. *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Guards Category:Kubaz Category:Males Category:Tatooinians
