Golden Yoke
1699

The Golden Yoke was a customized Tarrvin-on-Kallik Blade-28 fighter belonging to Pekaelic ke Teldan, the Perator of Cartann. The fighter drew its name from its unique solid gold color scheme, and had been flown by Pekaelic during the great military victories of his youth. The fighter was nearly as famous on Cartann as Pekaelic himself, and one of the Perator's titles was "Master of the Golden Yoke." After becoming Perator, Pekaelic stored the Golden Yoke in his personal hangar, a short distance from his palace in Cartann City.
