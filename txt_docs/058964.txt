Carol Lay
1410

Carol Lay (born 1952) is a comic artist who lettered the sixth issue of Marvel's Star Wars series. That issue, the climax of the comic adaptation of the original Star Wars film, was reprinted in numerous collections. Lay's early comic work appeared in the underground publishers like Last Gasp and Rip Off Press in the 1970s.
