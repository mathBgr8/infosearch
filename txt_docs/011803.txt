Ragna III was also home to much larger predators than the Yuzzem, forcing these thick-furred natives to roam in prides in the planet's treetops. The planet's temperature was mild. The Yuzzem were enslaved by the Galactic Empire, sent to serve in Imperial mining facilities. However, the Yuzzem were hard to control, and often caused riots and breakouts, especially when teamed up with Wookiee slaves.
