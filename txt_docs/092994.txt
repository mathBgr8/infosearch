Restrainer
2656

The Restrainer was an Immobilizer 418 cruiser in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The Restrainer along with Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Subjugator ambushed Rebel Frigate Redemption who received defector Commander Zaletta from DX-11a. Forces arriving from Liberty assisted to destroy the Immobilizer and make it to hyperspace. A Rebel training mission in the Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter game depicts Calamari Cruiser Liberty being trapped by the Restrainer and subsequently attacked by ISD Bellicose and VSD Jundland. Rebel fighters must destroy the Restrainer in order for the Liberty to escape.
