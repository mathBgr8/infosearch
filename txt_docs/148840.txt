Obvious Nirvana
1478

The Obvious Nirvana was a starship which was destroyed. The Obvious Nirvana was one of the first ships that was to dock with the newly built Tatoo III space station outside Tatooine. Instead it rushed into and impacted the station, destroying both the ship and Tatoo III. Later investigations showed that the metals used to construct Tatoo III had been magnetic, which had dragged the Obvious Nirvana to its doom. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Category:Starships
