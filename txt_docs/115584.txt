Danuta Uprising
1068

thumb|right|Emperor Katarn orders the extinction of the uprising. Shortly after Kyle Katarn assumed the power of the Valley of the Jedi and became Galactic Emperor around 5 ABY, the Rebel Alliance initiated an uprising against him on Danuta. Emperor Katarn sent Sariss to crush the rebellion. The uprising is mentioned only in the non-canon dark side alternative ending of the game Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II Category:Battles of Danuta Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War Category:Rebellions and uprisings
