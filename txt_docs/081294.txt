Chrissie England
1658

thumb|250px|The now superseded appearance of Chrissie England as Orn Free Taa's aide in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace Chrissie England was the Visual Effects Executive Producer of Industrial Light & Magic for The Phantom Menace. She was also a member of the Administrative Staff for Return of the Jedi. She had a small cameo in the theatrical release of The Phantom Menace, as a Human member of Orn Free Taa's delegation in the Galactic Senate. However, the character was replaced with a Twi'lek aide for the DVD release. In February 2006, she along with George Lucas was awarded a National Medal of Science and Technology by United States President George W.
