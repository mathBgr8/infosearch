It was located within the Kira sector, in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Expansion Region. Along with the rest of the Lazerian system, Acorvus lay on the Harrin Trade Corridor and Kira Run hyperlanes. The planet's surface featured a barren desert, and the world had no moons. It had a Type II atmosphere, in which the use of breath masks was suggested.Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One Acorvus was the closest neighbor to the planet Lazerian IV, and at one point it was surveyed by the inhabitants of that world. Eventually Acorvus came to serve as a penal colony and exile planet for political dissenters and criminals. The planet was off-limits to all non-authorized personnel. Acorvus was created by John Terra and introduced in Twin Stars of Kira, a 1993 supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. In the book, the planet's name was misspelled as "Acoruus" on one instance.
