Legate (card)
630

The Legate was a rank in the Centran sabacc deck. It had a value of 11, but trumped a normal 11 for tie-breaking purposes. When used in fortune-telling, it normally represented a being that was good at heart, with the suit providing more information. *Lando Calrissian and the Mindharp of Sharu *Fate of the Jedi: Apocalypse *Legacy (2006) 17 Category:Sabacc cards
