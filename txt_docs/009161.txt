Odos was located near the Chorios systems. It was also located near the Exodo system and the Spangled Veil Nebula. The planet Odos was surrounded by gas clouds.Planet of Twilight In 13 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion while at Exodo II, Han Solo, Chewbacca and Lando Calrissian were attacked by an Imperial fleet from the nearby Antemeridian sector. They lost their pursuers by flying the Millennium Falcon through the gas clouds of Odos and the fringes of the Spangled Veil Nebula. Odos was introduced in Planet of Twilight, a 1997 novel authored by Barbara Hambly. It was first described as a planet by the 1998 reference book Star Wars Encyclopedia, written by Stephen J.
