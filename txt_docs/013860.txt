Excessive mining resulted in volcanic eruptions that poisoned the atmosphere and destroyed the ecosystem. The inhabitants were forced to retreat to sealed cities. After having been ruled as a despotism for years, in 37 BBY a group of young liberals overthrew the government, resulting in a disorganized democracy. However, in 22 BBY, just before the start of the Clone Wars, the Church of Infinite Perception launched a counter-coup, installing a theocracy.
