Seer (Yuuzhan Vong rank)
1180

Seer was a rank of the Priest caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. A seer was one who took the auguries of the Gods, interpreted signs, and pronounced when the Yuuzhan Vong would be ready for a battle. Seers were often female and were often covered in black Yuuzhan Vong blood.The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force There were variations upon the position of seer among the ranks of the Yuuzhan Vong. One such variation was that of the warrior-seer, who provided both religious and tactical advice to senior Yuuzhan Vong commanders.
