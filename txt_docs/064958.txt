Dalloralla tree
939

The dalloralla tree was a thirty-meter-tall species of tree native to the world of Belkadan. Scientist Danni Quee often watched the planet's sunset as it filtered through these trees. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Trees
