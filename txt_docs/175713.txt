Vo (container transport)
1954

Vo was a Container transport belonging to the Imperials. Vo was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupters vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor in the Mev system. Vo was ferrying a fresh supply of Imperial Probe droids. The ship was then attacked by Rebel raiders in the Battle of Bettel and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Container transports Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
