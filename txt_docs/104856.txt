Babbnod Luroon
1798

Babbnod Luroon, or "Babb" for short, served as first mate aboard Jynni's Virtue under the command of Captain Naz Felyood. When the Virtue crashed near the Valley of Golg on Korriban. After 3 days (which in reality was 2 months), Captain Naz Felyood set out and didn't return. Presuming command of the crew, over the next 3 days (In reality 3 months), they came under attack of Korriban zombies under the command of the return spirit, Dathka Graush. Luroon would create a ship log, explaining her former allies dying and becoming reanimated corpses. Knowing she would soon be overrun by the undead, Luroon had given orders to set charges on the ship engines. Assuming Felyood dead and low on blaster charges and crew, would set off the planted explosives, blowing Jynni's Virtue engines and herself up.
