IntelStar Company
1699

}} IntelStar Company was a highly successful company that specialized in the creation of hyperdrive prime components, as well as navigation and sensor arrays. The company sold to eleven different hyperdrive builders and became very wealthy. As a result, the company used its finances to sponsor the creation of the Corporate Sector Authority.
