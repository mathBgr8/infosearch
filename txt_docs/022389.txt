|type=Hardcover |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=087431268X |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded is a Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game book written by Bill Smith and published by West End Games in August 1996. It supersedes the first and second editions of the core book. The book featured game rules, game master advice, and the standalone adventure The Pirates of Prexiar.
