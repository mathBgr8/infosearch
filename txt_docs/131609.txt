Empire (Imperial II prototype)
2560

|commission= |destroyed= |retired= |battles= |affiliation=Galactic Empire |navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains=Admiral Feyet Kiez |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} The Empire, was a prototype Imperial II-class Star Destroyer that served within the Imperial Navy. It was modified with superior shielding, and was commanded by Admiral Kiez. The Empire was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer with upgraded superior shields . It was the finest warship ever within the Imperial Fleet. Its crew was handpicked, and it was rumored they were the elite of the elite within the fleet. At some point, Feyet Kiez, recently promoted to the rank of Admiral, became the commanding officer of the ship. Within the next few years, the battleship would be used to patrol star systems, and locate military staging areas.
