Frommon longhaul all-purpose transport

thumb|right|200px|The Starkiller, a modified Frommon longhaul all-purpose transport The Frommon longhaul all-purpose transport was one of the first hyperdrive-capable freighters during the early years of the Galactic Republic, as was evidenced by their crude ion coils. It came in at 30 meters and required a crew of two people to operate it. One such ship, the Starkiller, was used by bounty hunter Leshy Drobo. Category:Transport classes
