Sentinel (Wookiees)
2176

The Sentinel was a repulsorcraft mech manufactured and employed by the Wookiees of Kashyyyk. It was armed with a light laser cannon mounted on an arm that protruded from the cockpit. Th Sentinel vehicle was effective at disrupting infantry formations, however, it was weak against heavier armored units such as the AT-ST. It was also fast enough to be used for scouting.
