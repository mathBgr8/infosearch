Kian'thar (language)
1074

Kian'thar was the native language of the Kian'thar species from Shaum Hii. Most Kian'thar were fluent in both Kian'thar and Basic, although only literate in their own language. Kian'thar used mainly croaks, honks and watery bubbling sounds.
