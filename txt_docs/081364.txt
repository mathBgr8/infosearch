Treitamma Political Center
1053

The Treitamma Political Center was on Anchoron. The Assistant Director of the center was Graskt. The center was to be the site of Garm Bel Iblis' first major speech against the New Order and the growing powers of Emperor Palpatine. In an effort to assassinate Bel Iblis, a co-founder of the Rebel Alliance, agents of Armand Isard planted a thermal detonator in the center. Bel Iblis missed the detonation, but the blast killed his wife Arrianya and their two children, as well as Graskt. The explosion destroyed the convention center.
