Morellian Enforcers
805

The Morellian Enforcers were an elite group of soldiers and protectors who guarded the Morellian Commonwealth. Morellian Enforcers used a limited technology, preferring to rely on their natural resources. Usual gear included leather boots, blackstrap gloves, Morellian oilcoat and the Morellian Weapons Conglomerate .48-caliber Enforcer pistol.
