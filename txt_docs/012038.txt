Bakuran National Symphony
1043

The Bakuran National Symphony was the most prestigious and elite musical group on Bakura. As it was the 'national' symphony, it was presumably located in the Bakuran capital of Salis D'aar. Malinza Thanas, the daughter of former Prime Minister Gaeriel Captison and Bakuran Defense Fleet commander Pter Thanas, joined the Symphony at an unusually young age. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Orchestras Category:Organizations of Bakura
