Wookiee warblade
1288

thumb|right|200px|[[Freyyr with his warblade.]] The Wookiee Warblade was a common weapon among Wookiee warriors who would wield the blade in constant motion, allowing for deadly strikes. The warblade was a cumbersome weapon for non-Wookiees but was perfectly wieldy for the Wookiees who carried them. This melee weapon was seen among warriors during the Great Sith War.
