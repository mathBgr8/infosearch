Pantang Scale of Aero-techno Advancement
1001

As an evolutionary scale developed to aid historians and xenoarchaeologists in classifying a civilization's level of technology, the Pantang Scale of Aero-techno Advancement was not a popular classification system. The primary reason this was that it rated technology required to build a landspeeder as equivalent to that required to build an Imperial-class Star Destroyer. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Measurement scales
