Truvvhhurr

|climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Truvvhhurr was a major city on the planet Kashyyyk located in the galaxy's Mid Rim. The city was build around manufacturing facilities that produced goods for offworld trading. Truvvhhurr was mentioned in "Secrets of Kashyyyk," an article written by J.D. Wiker and Craig Robert Carey for the fourth issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine published by Wizards of the Coast on June 19, 2001. Category:Cities on Kashyyyk
