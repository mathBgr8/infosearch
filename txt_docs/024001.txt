Jorga
1583

Jorga was a Duros gunnery officer serving aboard the Admiral Ackbar, under the command of Admiral Nek Bwua'tu during the Dark Nest Crisis. When the Dark Nest attempted to flee its homeworld, its fleet came up against the Galactic Alliance Fifth Fleet in the Murgo Choke.
