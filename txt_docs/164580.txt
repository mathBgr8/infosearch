Exchange Shadow Caster
831

The Exchange Shadow Caster was an advanced stealth field generator mounted on a utility belt, and was manufactured and distributed by the Exchange in the period following the Jedi Civil War. It was of considerable use as an escape tool on planets where agents of the Exchange were outnumbered by local authorities. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Stealth field generators
