|degree= |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender=Masculine programmingDroids (1986) 1 |sensor=White |plating=Green |armament= |equipment= |affiliation= |firstmade= |retired= }} The Ranger X-1 was an antiquated model of masculine defense droid programmed to automatically fight any act of hostility. It had green plating with "X-1" emblazoned in white on its chest, a pair of white photoreceptors, and morphed into a box when deactivated. The series was terminated when the Intergalactic Law Agency was founded during the last century of the Galactic Republic, and the last of the X-1s were collected and scrapped. However, a Ranger X-1 brought to the planet Dodz to protect pioneers was missed by the ILA and found by Jost Ellon near the world's crystal mines. After meeting R2-D2 and C-3PO, Ellon discovered that the X-1 had corroded power cells and kicked the inert droid, accidentally reactivating it. Despite being low on power, Ellon wanted to use the X-1 to aid the merchants of Dodz against Governor Kugg, who used his killer droid, the Destroyer, to murder those who refused to pay his heavy taxes. Although outmatched, the Ranger was able to destroy the Destroyer when R2 tapped into its power and fed it back into the X-1. The defense droid ran out of energy in a permanently heroic pose, remaining on top of a hill as a reminder of its importance to the people of Dodz for ending Kugg's corrupt reign.
