Yovshin Swordsmen
1145

The Yovshin Swordsmen of the Atrisian Empire developed a sword fighting style in which they wielded a dueling sword in each hand. They dubbed the weapons "Jar'Kai" after the Atrisian city where they were first crafted. The Jedi Order would adopt their style into a form of lightsaber combat. *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Rise of the Hutt Cartel *The Jedi Path: A Manual for Students of the Force Category:Military units
