Nap Socker
1012

Nap Socker was a Newcomer employed as a smelter worker on Nam Chorios in 13 ABY. When Luke Skywalker used the Force while stranded there, accidentally creating a Force Storm, Nap and his employer, Tinnin Droo, were severely burned when the smelter overturned on them.
