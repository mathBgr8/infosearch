Astral-8
2364

The Astral-8 was a luxury landspeeder model manufactured by zZip Motor Concepts. It was twelve meters long and could carry a single pilot, eight passengers, and two hundred and fifty kilograms of cargo. It was designed for comfort and luxury. It had a single pilot cockpit that could be isolated with a privacy panel. The rear compartment had a small cold storage unit and drink dispenser and owners often added entertainment units. It had built-in anticoncussion shielding, making it more durable than most landspeeders.
