Chine Gabbard
1624

Chine Gabbard was a native of Rehemsa, but worked as a botany researcher on Garqi. Chine served as an executive on the board of Rehemsa Consumables. He threw lavish parties and was very popular among the native Garqians. He also did research on the hafa vines, a cash crop grown by Imperial order.
