Redhawk
2290

Redhawk was a mercenary bulk cruiser affiliated to the mercenary base Falcon's Nest. After the mercenary assault against the Tal'cara, the mercenary base was sought. After interrogation of a captive by the Rebel Alliance, he gave the information that Redhawk was to rendezvous at the mercenary base. Otana met and boarded Redhawk. Aeron infiltrated the ship and sliced its charts to find the coordinations of the base. However a mistake led them to a weapons testing center in the Carida system. Redhawk was seen again later parked near the Falcon's Nest, when the Azzameens realized it was actually their home.
