He was often seen in the company of his friends Zey Nep, Artuo Pratuhr, and Civ Sila. During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Just, alongside Nep, Pratuhr, and Sila, visited the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall located in the Uscru Entertainment District of Coruscant, as Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered, searching for the bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Just stood near the entrance of the club with a drink in hand and greeted those who entered. Soon after, Just talked to Nep, Pratuhr, and Sila near the club's bar. He debated with Sila as Skywalker walked through the club. Just watched as Wesell was disarmed by Kenobi and taken out of the club. He had light skin, dark hair, and wore dark colored clothing at the club. Dixon Just first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, where the character was portrayed by Justin Dix, a member of the film's Droid Unit crew.
