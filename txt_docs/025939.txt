Tensor 4
1171

Tensor 4 was a world. During the Galactic War, there was a large processing project which caused for thousands of confiscated blasters, vibroswords and other weapons to be processed into useful metallic parts. Tensor 4 was first mentioned in the unlicensed non-canon magazine article "Jet Packs", and was later canonized by its inclusion in the video game Star Wars: The Old Republic: Shadow of Revan.
