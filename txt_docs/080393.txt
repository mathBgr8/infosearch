DX-300 traffic package
2361

The DX-300 traffic package was a traffic regulation device in use around 3964 BBY. Unlike previous traffic regulation packages, the DX-300 could suggest the best track available to commuters by use of regulating signals. A lecture detailing the package was held on the Outer Rim world of Taris,Knights of the Old Republic 0 during which the lecturer claimed the device was an invaluable tool for anyone looking to work as a civil engineer. The lecture was interrupted when Jedi Padawan Zayne Carrick crashed his airspeeder into the building. The DX-300 package's first and only mention was in the second issue of the Knights of the Old Republic comic book series. The author of the comic, John Jackson Miller, commented on his website that he found it ironic for a traffic accident to occur in the middle of a lecture about a device that handled traffic flow. *Knights of the Old Republic 2 Category:Communications
