Dimogog
1017

Dimogog and his fellow Trandoshan, Orix, were a pair of leg-breakers who accompanied the Sullustan Jubieck in trying to obtain the head of L80-RC from Ginder the Bimm. They ended up in a three-way battle for the head with a group of freelancers and a trio of Thaereian soldiers after Orix made a grab for it.
