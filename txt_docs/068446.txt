Blade-28 fighter
1404

The Blade-28 fighter was a combat aircraft built by Tarrvin-on-Kallik on Adumar. By 13 ABY, the Blade-28 was somewhat outdated, being outperformed in nearly every way by the newer Blade-32 superiority fighter. Nevertheless, a large numbers of Blade-28s were still in use, especially by the nations opposing Cartann and by retired pilots who kept the fighters for their personal use. The most famous Blade-28 was the Golden Yoke, the personal fighter of Perator Pekaelic ke Teldan of Cartann. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Blade series models
