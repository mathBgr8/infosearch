Tikulini
1251

A tikulini or "mining worm" was a large, carnivorous worm native to Jazbina that inhabited the sun crystal mining tunnels. They had green blood. "Tikulini" meant "the death of a myriad knives." Their skin was strong enough to withstand a lightsaber strike.
