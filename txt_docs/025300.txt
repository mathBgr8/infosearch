Nagwa

Nagwa was an Agave-class picket dispatched to Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt's task force during the New Republic's attempt to capture the Teljkon Vagabond. After receiving minor damage during the failed capture attempt at Gmar Askilon, Nagwa was ordered back to drydock. *Shield of Lies *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Agave-class picket ships Category:Starships of the New Republic
