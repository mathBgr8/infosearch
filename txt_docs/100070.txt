With the help of R2-RC, he claimed it was a simple transport, although it had a military background. He armed the ship with four plasburst laser cannons and two proton torpedo launchers to defend against unwanted company. The G Cat was used by Cavv and Quillin Arkell on a mission to extract Rivoche Tarkin from Corulag before her cover was blown. After rescuing the spy, Cavv had his droid pilot the G Cat to the top of the Royal Galaxy Hotel so the group could get off the planet.
