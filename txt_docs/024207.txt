In 25 ABY, it was one of several starships that attempted to evacuate the residents of the Outer Rim Territories &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Julevian system planet Sernpidal, which was located in the Julevian system, while the planet was destroyed by the Yuuzhan Vong species by pulling Sernpidal's moon Dobido into the planet. Several Yuuzhan Vong grutchins, insectoid creatures that tore through starships, attacked the Juliupper over Sernpidal, boring through the vessel's hull and attacking its ion engines. After the shuttle's pilot called for assistance, the YT-1300 light freighterMillennium Falcon Owner's Workshop Manual Millennium Falcon moved to assist, killing one of the attacking grutchins with its laser cannons; however, the creatures destroyed the Juliupper. The Juliupper first appeared in the 1999 novel Vector Prime, written by R. A.
