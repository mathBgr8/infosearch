Deep Pockets
1291

Deep Pockets was an underwater mining complex on Varn owned by Lando Calrissian in 19 ABY. Following the start of the venture, Calrissian added on a casino and observation gallery to the facility, with financing from Tendra Risant's family, despite their skepticism. At full capacity, the casino's total revenue could pay for the entire operation, and many customers were actually delighted to pay money to observe Lando's miners at work. However, pirate raids on Varn wore into the operation.
