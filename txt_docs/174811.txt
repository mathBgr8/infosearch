Garqian
529

A Garqian was a non-native being either born on or that claimed permanent residence on the planet of Garqi. It was also used as a proper adjective to refer to anything related to, coming from, or similar to that on Garqi. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook Category:Garqi residents Category:Planetary cultural groups
