It was the farthest planet away from the system's star, Zug, and because of this, it was extremely cold. Uluu possessed a single natural satellite. Uluu was first mentioned briefly in "Three Simple Laws," the first part of the Planet Hoppers article series Aargau: For All Your Banking Needs, which was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on the Wizards.com website in 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Zug system, thereby including Uluu, in grid square L-10.
