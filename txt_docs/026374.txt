Korolev
2770

Korolev was a CR90 corvette that served as a medical vessel in the Rebel Fleet. Korolev transferred wounded from the evacuation of Brigia during Operation Strike Fear to the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Redemption. The transfer was interrupted by the arrival of the Imperial Nebulon-B frigate Warspite which began launching TIEs. The actions of Red Squadron, and particularly Keyan Farlander, protected the transfer and all Rebel ships escaped into hyperspace. A simulation based on guarding the corvette would later be part of the New Republic's training program.
