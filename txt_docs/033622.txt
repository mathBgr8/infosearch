Boog
1291

Boog was a Near-Human trader who served aboard the Port-Esta Queen during the Imperial campaign against the Icarii on Vestar. When Boba Fett was hunting down Abal Karda, he made his way to Port-Esta and spoke with the Port-Esta Queen's captain. Boog, meanwhile, rigged a crate of kneebs to fall onto Fett. Fett realized this and flew out of the crate's way with his jetpack.
