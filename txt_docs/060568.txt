|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 5 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=c. 19 BBYStar Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 5 dates its stories to just before and during the events of , which The Essential Atlas dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "What Goes Up..." is a story from the comic book Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 5. It was written and illustrated by the Fillbach Brothers and colored by Lee Evandon. Aayla Secura and Commander Bly investigate a deserted Separatist outpost in the Endor system. After discovering the base to be empty (and finding the remains of countless clone troopers and battle droids), Secura goes out scouting where she encounters a lone battle droid. Disposing its appendages, Secura is caught unaware by the native Ewoks and is shot with a tranquilizer dart. Waking up in a treehouse, Secura uses the battle droid as a translator, who sarcastically informs her that she is thought to be a forest spirit. As a spirit, it is her duty to vanquish the Beast in the Mouth of the Mountain. Secura, taking the droid along, complies. Reaching the cave, the Jedi Knight trips on a pile of bones, alerting the Beast to her presence. The Beast quickly impales the battle droid with its claw, leaving Secura free to work on collapsing parts of the ceiling upon the monster. Fulfilling her duty as the Ewok's spirit, the Jedi Master returns to the Republic gunship, where Bly is waiting. Dismissing her commander's worries, the pair, along with their squadron, heads to Felucia.
