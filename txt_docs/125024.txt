Qiluiy
781

Qiluiy was a group of 3 CR90 corvettes used by the Rebel Alliance. The three corvettes were part of a convoy which also consisted of 2 BFF-1 bulk freighters called Xroi in the Mennaalii system which became under attack from pirates. However the pirate attack was driven off by Rebel X-wings, Y-wings and A-wings. *Star Wars: X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
