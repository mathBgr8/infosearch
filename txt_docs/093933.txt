Sand-buzzer

thumb|Two sand-buzzers at the [[Lars homestead/Legends|Lars homestead with Owen Lars' Zephyr-G swoop between them.]] The sand-buzzer was a security device that emitted an electronic field to drive away small burrowing pest animals, such as scurriers. Commonly employed on Tatooine, many moisture farms had sand-buzzers surrounding the homestead to damage to the property, particularly the sunken walls of the homestead courtyards. *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Security technology
