A Force-sensitive Human, Jonas Fel'Kona was trained in the ways of the Force, training as a recruiter after achieving Jedi Knighthood. A respected member of the Acquisition Division, Fel'kona was attuned to the Force in such a way that he was able to locate potential Jedi Initiates from afar. Indeed, one of his most prominent finds, Lanius Qel-Bertuk, would go on to be headmaster of the experimental Almas Academy during the Clone Wars.
