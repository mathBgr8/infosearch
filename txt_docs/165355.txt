Reiss
973

Lieutenant Reiss was an Imperial officer assigned to the Marduk under Systems Sub-Commander Jared Brojtal. Following the attack on the Syndaar Military Prep School by the Ho'Din terrorist Ort Hoogra-D'En, Reiss had the unenviable task of informing Sub-Commander Brojtal that his son was killed.
