Rh'ajah Fenn
1207

Rh'ajah Fenn was a male Twi'lek member of Clan Fenn. During the Clone Wars he was loyal to Dooku and an accomplice of his relative, Kh'aris Fenn. Kh'aris was murdered by Quinlan Vos seventeen months after the Battle of Geonosis when Vos disguised himself as Rh'ajah. *Republic 66 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems individuals Category:Males Category:Twi'leks
