Saduu Nagag
1337

Saduu Nagag was a male Aplocaph tourist aboard the Kuari Princess when it was attacked by the Riders of the Maelstrom. He was a very fastidious being, who, in hopes of attracting romance, would make an effort to gleam his skin on an almost hourly basis. *Riders of the Maelstrom *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Aplocaph Category:Males
