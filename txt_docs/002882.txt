Darian Gli
1573

Darian Gli was a Markul member of Jabba Desilijic Tiure's court who was secretly in league with Lady Valarian. He helped Beshka University professors Ra Yasht and Skarten get an interview with Jabba's cook, Porcellus, which they turned into the book Torture Observed: An Interview with Jabba's Cook. For this offense, Jabba consumed him.
