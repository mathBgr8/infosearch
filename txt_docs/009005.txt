It had jagged, windswept mountains and deep caves. It was an isolated world of xenophobic people. With the recovery of the Holocron of King Nakgru, Sorzus Syn discovered that the Sith Empire had struck the Republic world of Draethos at some point after the Sith colonized the Stygian Caldera.
