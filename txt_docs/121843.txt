|description=Liquid |animals= |plants= |other= |edible=HumansThe New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Oorp was a healthy but bland liquid food supplement developed for pregnant beings. While pregnant in 26 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth to 26 ABY. the Human Mara Jade Skywalker dismissed her husband Luke Skywalker's concerns about how far she could walk on an artificial beach on the planet Coruscant, questioning if he really thought it would be better for their child for her to sit around sucking on oorp instead. Oorp was first mentioned in The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth, which was written by Greg Keyes and released in 2001 as the second book in the Edge of Victory Duology, a part of the wider Star Wars: The New Jedi Order series of novels. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Food
