SM-1
745

SM-1 was a sentient controlled droid onboard the New Republic ship Steadfast, and was used to search destroyed vessels for traps before being salvaged. In 16 ABY SM-1 was one of the droids involved in the finding of an operational datacore on the Star Destroyer Gnisnal that allowed for the discovery of the Black Fleet.
