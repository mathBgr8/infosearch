The Rebel Alliance managed to obtain the ship and armed it with two turret-mounted twin laser cannons and one turret-mounted turbolaser cannon. The Rebel command allocated the ship to Scandium Team to use it as its main transport. It would be piloted by lead pilot Jouffer Talamiin, and then by his replacement Aven Cholus after Talamiin was permanently grounded. After Scandium Team was decimated on the Wellte-ir Massacre and then dismantled, Cholus kept The Agent and used it as her personal transport during her brief stay with Gale Team.
