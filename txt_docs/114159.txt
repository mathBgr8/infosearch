Liberty (Yevetha)
1572

The Liberty was one of the capital ships used by the Yevetha of the Duskhan League during the genocidal Great Purge in 16-17 ABY. Along with Honor and the Star Destroyer command ship Devotion of Yevetha, Liberty was one of the three ships of the Yevethan task force sent against Polneye at the start of the Purge. She was responsible for destroying the cities of Eleven South and Eleven North by orbital bombardment, along with one of Polneye's six defending TIE Interceptors. *Before the Storm Category:Cruisers Category:Yevethan starships
