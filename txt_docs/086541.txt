Wazado
2344

Wazado was a male baron. Prior to the plot to assassinate Galactic Republic Senator Padmé Amidala in 22 BBY, Wazado was killed by ASN-121, a custom assassin droid owned by the bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Wesell's next target was Amidala, and during that assassination attempt, she shot ASN-121 down on the planet Coruscant. Fragments of the droid were found in an alleyway in the world's undercity, and when sliced, ASN-121's logs revealed Wesell's involvement in Wazado's death.
