}} Clan Bralor was an ancient Mandalorian clan that dates back since the Mandalorian Wars. The clan had with roots in the hill-fort town of Norg Bral, on the Outer Rim world Mandalore. During the Mandalorian Civil War as the Third Galactic War raged on, Clan Bralor would betray Mandalore the Avenger and side with the Hidden Chain, attacking Port Nowhere with fellow defectors from Shae's cause. thump|left|150px|Bralor Bralor was respected Mandalorian warrior and was the champion of Battle Circle during the Dark Wars. Unlike many of his more aggressive Mandalorians, he respected the Jedi and had study much about them in hopes of understanding them in his quest to best them in combat. Even when being defeated by the Jedi Exile, Meetra Surik, it gained his respect for her. thump|left|150px|Jogo Jogo, also known as Jogo the Carver, was the son of Salus Bralor. Jogo would however make the mistake of insulting the Grand Hunt Champion and was killed by him. thump|left|150px|Salus Salus Bralor was the father of Jogo the Carver. Only to share his fate in being killed. thump|left|150px|Cantis Bralor Cantis Bralor was the leader of the strike-force that assaulted Port Nowhere during the Mandalorian civil war between Mandalore the Avenger's faction and the Hidden Chain. thump|left|150px|Rav Bralor Rav Bralor was among the Mandalorian elite to be hired by Jango Fett to train the Clone army, having been the one to train the Clone Commando Squads, Vevut Squad and Yayax Squad. After her duties as a trainer were over, she went back to Mandalore to help maintain the Kyrimorut. She was present when Etain Tur-Mukan's body was brought back and prepared the body for cremation. By time of the Second Galactic Civil War the leadership of Clan Bralor would fall under Neth Bralor. During the meeting between the clan chieftains, mercenaries, and heads of Mandalorian corporations to discuss Mandalore's position in the war.
