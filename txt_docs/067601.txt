Intergalactic Communications Center
1917

During the Clone Wars, the Intergalactic Communications Center, located on Praesitlyn, played a vital role in communication for the Core Worlds. The Center was located 150 kilometers from the center of a major continent. It was seized by Separatist Admiral Pors Tonith and his droid army. In a desperate measure of lives and deaths, Jedi Generals Anakin Skywalker and Nejaa Halcyon were sent to take back the center and the planet Praesitlyn, in which they succeeded. Its staff consisted of fifty technicians and specialists. Reija Momen became its chief administrator in 27 BBY. Slith Skael was the chief of its communications staff.
