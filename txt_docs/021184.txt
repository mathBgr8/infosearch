|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood |issue=2 }} Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 2 was the second issue from the Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood series of comics, and was released on December 9, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. As the death toll rises, so do the stakes for the future of the Empire. Who is committing these assassinations of Interim Council members and who within the Empire has the most to gain? Why has Mirith Sinn abandoned the New Republic to fulfill a personal vendetta? And how does Kir Kanos fit into this jigsaw puzzle of intrigue?
