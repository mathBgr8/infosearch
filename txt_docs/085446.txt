Lorqual
1653

Lorqual were animals on Ansion, perhaps the world's biggest inhabitants. They were twice as tall and four times as massive as the suubatars, and traveled in herds. They had bristly brown fur, blocky skulls with six bony knobs protuding from them, six feet with six horned toes, but only small eyes. In 22 BBY, the diminutive but brave Gwurran Tooqui incited a lorqual stampede into a Qulun camp, in order to rescue his Jedi friends and their Alwari-outcast guides from the machinations of the fat trader-chief Baiuntu, a pawn of Soergg the Hutt and his majordomo Ogomoor.
