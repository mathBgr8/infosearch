Drallish crown
431

Drallish crowns were units of currency on the planet Drall. The tutor Ebrihim paid 1150 Drallish crowns to purchase the experimental droid Q9-X2, although the sales receipt showed the droid cost 1250 crowns. *Showdown at Centerpoint *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:Currency
