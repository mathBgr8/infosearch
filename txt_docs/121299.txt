Credit game
929

thumb|right|250px|R2-D2 beating the credit game The credit game was a gambling device that required players to match symbols in order to win credits. Color variations of the credit game included blue, green, purple, and red. Level Seven, the game room of the Tahlboor space station, was equipped with at least five credit game machines. Around 5.5 BBY, R2-D2 beat the game, winning enough credits to break the station's bank. The credit game appears to be similar in concept to a slot machine. *The Protocol Offensive Category:Gaming
