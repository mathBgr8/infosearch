|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=1 }} Classic Star Wars 5 is the fifth issue of the Classic Star Wars series, published by Dark Horse Comics. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson from the early 1980s. Williamson supplied new panels and art to flow the stories for traditional comic format. This issue reprints the middle of the The Serpent Masters comic strip. After crash-landing their stolen Imperial ship, Luke and his new-found companion Tanith Shire are captured and enslaved by the Serpent Masters of Tanith's home planet.
