Greelossk
1382

Greelossk was a male Trandoshan inmate who lived in Desolation Alley during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was serving a life sentence for committing high crimes against the Galactic Republic, but he was also wanted dead by an unknown being for committing personal crimes.
