Jorgis
1879

Jorgis was a fighter pilot who served in the Rebel Alliance as a member of Green Group during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, Jorgis was stationed onboard the Mon Calamari cruiser Defiance after joining the Rebellion about same time as Ace Azzameen. The pilot's call sign was Green Seven. Jorgis sent an encouraging e-mail to Azzameen to establish a friendship; however he was killed shortly after.
