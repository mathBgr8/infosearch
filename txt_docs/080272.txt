Hand (One Sith)
3406

A Hand was a member of the One Sith around 130 ABY. They were Darth Krayt's most capable servants, trained and existing to carry out his will. They followed only his orders, and reported only to him. thumb|left|Krayt's Hands on Vendaxa By 137 ABY, Krayt's only Hand was Darth Nihl, having been given the post when his predecessor died unexpectedly during the Massacre at Ossus. That same year, he made Darth Talon, a Twi'lek Sith Lord, into his second Hand, a fact that displeased Nihl.
