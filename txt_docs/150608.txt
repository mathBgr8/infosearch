Nihun Valley
558

Nihun Valley was a valley on Brosi, to the east of Nihun River.Alliance Intelligence Reports Nihun Valley was in the influence area of the Brosin Underground around 2 ABY. The largest team of the Brosin Underground, personally led by Vuraj Marn, operated both in Reyno Valley and Nihun Valley. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Brosi locations Category:Valleys
