Sal Vhe'starn
965

Sal Vhe'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the southeastern part of the planet's eastern continent. Rhan'starn was found to the west, Shevre'starn to the northwest, and Fevye'starn not far to the east. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
