Echo Flight
3661

Echo Flight was a squadron in the Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps paired with Delta Flight. Essara Till of the Bravo Squadron was once the flight instructor for the unit just before the Invasion of Naboo. At one point the Flight was scrambled to prevent a pirate freighter from attacking a farmer's estate and loading his shaak herd. After dealing with the pirates's portable surface-to-air ion missiless, they took control of their weapons, and turned them against them, preventing the freighter from taking off. They flew blue N-X Police Cruisers and fought at the Battle at TFP-9 against Vulture droids at Station TFP-9. Added with the unexpected betrayal by Dren Melne, only five survived and made it back. After the Battle of Naboo, Gungans began the construction of a colony on the Naboo moon of Ohma-D'un. A shipment of machine parts was hijacked by pirates on its way from Oxon to the moon installation, prompting the Royal House of Naboo to assign a group of pilots to escort further shipments. After some escorts, they witnessed another pirate raid. It happened that one of the pirates was a Naboo, disillusioned with their sadistic new leader who ordered the death of all witnesses. Suddenly he changed sides at the height of the battle. The teacherous pirate agreed to guide a group of fighters to their secret base, and Queen Amidala ordered them to capture the leader, as the least bloody option.
