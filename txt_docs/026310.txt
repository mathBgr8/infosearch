|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Father's Heir" is a scenario for the Star Wars Miniatures game, written by Eric Cagle and published in 2005. It depicts an alternate version of Return of the Jedi, in which Luke Skywalker, now Palpatine's apprentice, battles against his old allies. During the Battle of Endor, Emperor Palpatine succeeded in converting Luke Skywalker. His friends fled the battle, no longer sensing him. They were then captured on Cloud City and brought before the Emperor on Coruscant and tortured for a week about the Rebellion.
