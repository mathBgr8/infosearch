Phindar Spaceport
854

Phindar Spaceport was the major spaceport of Phindar. It had a gravity of 0.69g. During the Clone Wars, Yoda along with Jedi Knights Maks Leem and Jai Maruk, as well as their respective apprentices Whie Malreaux and Tallisibeth Enwandung-Esterhazy&mdash;nicknamed "Scout"&mdash;visited Phindar Spaceport on their secret mission to Vjun. Soon after their arrival, they were ambushed by Asajj Ventress and several droids, which resulted in the deaths of Maks Leem and Jai Maruk. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous Category:Spaceports
