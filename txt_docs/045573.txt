|gender=Female |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=Bel Iblis family |parents= |partners=Garm Bel Iblis |siblings= |children=Arrianya and Garm Bel Iblis's daughters |affiliation=Galactic Empire |masters= |apprentices= }} Arrianya Bel Iblis was a Human female who served the Galactic Empire and was the wife of the powerful Corellian Senator Garm Bel Iblis. The daughter of a well-off Core family, Bel Iblis supported Emperor Palpatine for the stabilization he brought to the Core Worlds. This drove a wedge between the married couple, who often argued over Palpatine's use of power. She, along with the Bel Iblis children, perished on Anchoron when a bomb meant for her husband detonated at the Treitamma Political Center.
