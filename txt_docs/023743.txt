E-2 Asteroid Miner
3020

The E-2 Asteroid Miner was a craft used for mining asteroids in 3999 BBY. thumb|left|180px|[[Marauder Starjacker schematics.]] Vehicles of that class, having an insectoid appearance, were common throughout the galaxy as they were a cheaper alternative to using tractor beams to drag asteroids. Pirates were known to use E-2s. E-2 Asteroid Miners had rugged claws which could cling on to the hard rocky surface of an asteroid and plasma drills for cutting through them. E-2s were also equipped with thruster jets for transporting asteroids to mining locations.
