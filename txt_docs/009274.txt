thumb|right|300px|Oroboro Killiks Oroboro was the name of a Killik hive on the planet Alderaan. Oroboro meant "our home" in the Killik language and was a region located across the northern steppes of Alderaan known as the Castle Lands.Dark Nest I: The Joiner King Oroboro Killik Caverns could be found within the Kaamos Territory around 3643 BBY. Vector Hyllus and Pabel Alde were Joiners of the hive. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Killik hives
