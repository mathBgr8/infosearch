NightMight 4NS scope
1089

The NightMight 4NS was a removable scope used with the Czerka Corporation Outland rifle favored on backwater worlds such as Tatooine and Kalee. The scope enabled the user to see heat signatures and possessed a built-in rangefinder, enhancing accuracy in low-light conditions. The Outland rifle had a range of 1,500 meters and an optimal range of 700 meters. Category:Scopes Category:Weapon attachments
