The Menace of the Opera

thumb|300px|Signs for The Menace of the Opera and other shows outside the Galaxies Opera House The Menace of the Opera was a musical playing at the Galaxies Opera House in 19 BBY. A sign advertising the show was displayed outside of the opera house during a performance of Squid Lake attended by Palpatine and Anakin Skywalker.
