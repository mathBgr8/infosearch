The descendants of the colony that C'cHaan founded eventually evolved into a separate species known as the Neimoidians. Chla C'cHaan was first mentioned in The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Duros, an article released in 2000 as part of the second issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine, and he received an entry in the 2008 The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia&mdash;but under the simplified name Chal Haan.
