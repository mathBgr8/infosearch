Valadon
2436

Valadon, a native of the Core world of Mamendin, was a young Humanoid female identity thief, an expert in her field. A founding member of the Slam gang—a notorious gang of expert thieves made particularly infamous by its heist of the Vuma treasury—Valadon added to an already flirtatious nature by dressing in a provocative manner. In 24 BBY, in an attempt to apprehend Jenna Zan Arbor, Siri Tachi took on the identity of Valadon while serving undercover on the planet Romin. That same year, the real Valadon was captured and recommitted to prison after an attempted heist with Roper Slam of a substantial cache of crystalline vertex.
