|pages=23 |upc=759606081134 |timeline=1 ABYStar Wars: Timelines dates the events of Star Wars Book X: Mutiny at Mon Cala to 1 ABY. |series=Star Wars (2015) |issue=46 }} Star Wars 46 is the forty-sixth issue of the Marvel comic book series Star Wars. It was written by Kieron Gillen and illustrated by Salvador Larroca and was released on April 4, 2018. The Rebellion continues their mission to liberate Mon Cala! Princess Leia has a nearly impossible plan to unite the planet with the Rebel Alliance, one that counts on the unique skills and cultural know-how belonging to the one-and-only&hellip;C-3PO?! Aboard the Millennium Falcon, Leia, Luke, and Han Solo are with the rescued Clawdite, Tunga Arpagion. Leia explains that Tunga is a famous shape-shifter with a reputation for impersonating people so well that he once played a dead actress for a week without anyone noticing. She tells Tunga that they need him to impersonate Moff Tan Hubi for just a few hours. When Tunga expresses concern about impersonating the Moff on duty without access to his files, Leia reassures him that all he needs to do is sit through a five-hour opera. She appeals to his ego, suggesting that a successful deception of this magnitude would make him even more famous. On Mon Cala, the rebels watch as the Moff arrives at the Moncaladrome. They implement their plan: the droids, R2-D2 and C-3PO, are smuggled in with catering to drug the Moff's drink, allowing Han and Chewbacca to grab him. They manage to subdue the Moff, but he wakes up during the transfer to Tunga. The rebels escape with the Moff in a submersible Mon Cala vehicle and travel to the water planet of Strokill Prime, where the ultra-secure prison is located just below the surface. After inputting the access codes and bio-marker, they crash into the ocean.
