Happy Failure
878

Happy Failure was a freighter operated by the mother of Odea Aurora. She was killed by Glongfurrp after Toln ne Yerres discovered she was shipping goods stolen from his boss, Jabba the Hutt. The freighter lay abandoned in Port Town and was systemically stripped by scavengers before being converted into a casino.
