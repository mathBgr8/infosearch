Anastasia Heonis
3047

Anastasia Heonis is a model also known as "Acid PopTart." Joe Corroney, who once shared an apartment with her, used her as inspiration for the illustration of two characters: Thrynka Padaunete. and Ailyn Vel. The former was based of photography by Laura Dark, and was featured in Star Wars Insider 71,Star Wars Insider 71 while the latter was based of a photography by Jason Perlman, and appeared in Star Wars Insider 80.Star Wars Insider 80 Category:Real-world people
