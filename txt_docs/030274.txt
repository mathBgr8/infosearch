Lex Lang
2982

Lex Lang is an American voice actor and director who voiced Han Solo in the games Star Wars: Battlefront II and Star Wars: Rogue Squadron III: Rebel Strike. He is the official "audio double" for the character played by Harrison Ford. He was also "Stormtrooper 1" in Star Wars: The Force Unleashed and Poe Dameron in LEGO Star Wars: The Resistance Rises.
