CardSafe
754

The CardSafe was one of the most talked-about and well known restaurants on the planet Coruscant, during the early years of the New Republic. Established by Ortolan chef Handree Braman, the CardSafe was known for its use of local wildlife in the main dishes, including hawk-bats. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Coruscant dining establishments
