Episode I Adventures 9: Rescue in the Core
4166

Episode I Adventures 9: Rescue in the Core was the ninth installment of Star Wars: Episode I Adventures. Jar Jar Binks is in trouble. He is clumsy, and he just caused a big accident. He is being taken to the Quarry, a spooky fortress that few prisoners have survived. Along the way, the bongo carrying Jar Jar is attacked. Can Jar Jar save himself&hellip;and save the day?
