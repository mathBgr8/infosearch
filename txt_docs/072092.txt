Njinska's Tavern
1024

Njinska's Tavern was a popular cantina located on Phyrstal Island in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae. It was entirely made out of wood and its windows were very large and contained stained-glass art. It had a large T-shaped stage which featured many high quality acts and had a large wide bar along one wall. The stage had a trap door that led to a several meter long angled shaft which ran under the stage and to a back door. The tavern was serviced by three bartender droids and had two large Human bouncers for protection. Brinaloy N'Vaari was a frequent performer at the tavern. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Abregado-rae locations Category:Cantinas
