High Haven
660

The High Haven was a New Republic medical frigate supporting the Fifth Battle Group stationed outside the Koornacht Cluster before and after the Battle of N'zoth. Han Solo was transferred there before the start of the battle for healing after his rescue from the Yevetha. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Frigates Category:Medical starships Category:New Republic Fifth Fleet elements
