Equipped with three Elsinore-Cordova Turbodyne engines, the Tri-Mark VII was very fast, and its large cargo capacity allowed for the transport of up to twenty troops and four prisoners, in addition to a crew of three. General maintenance was provided by two astromech droids. Originally designed to be a Republic Security Force interceptor, intended for interstellar pursuit, the Tri-Mark VII came equipped with six auto-targeting dual laser cannon turrets and a ventral concussion missile launcher.
