Trader's Alley
1179

Trader's Alley was a large cash-only bazaar located in Blue Level of Booster Terrik's Imperial-class Star Destroyer Errant Venture. One of the greatest free markets in the galaxy, the Trader's Alley did boast quite a selection of wares, legal or otherwise. Broken war droids, captured Imperial weapons, illegal drugs and spices and unique pieces of art from across the galaxy could be found in the Trader's Alley, no matter how restricted elsewhere.
