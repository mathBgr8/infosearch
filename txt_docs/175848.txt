Entertainer
2583

thumb|right|250px|A Chagrian stage performer. An entertainer was a person who performed in front of audiences for money. It was a general term that could be applied to singers, dancers, musicians and acrobats. One famous band was Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes. For some species, entertaining was a way of life. One well known species who considered this were the Twi'lek.
