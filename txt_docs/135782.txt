Kalindan Deep Space Fleet
849

The Kalindan Deep Space Fleet was the navy commanded by the monarchs of the Kalinda system. The fleet engaged elements of the Imperial Navy during the Battle of Kalinda. The fighting cost both sides numerous assets and casualties, which allowed a fleet from the Corporate Sector Authority to land an Enforcer Battle Group and take control of twenty-five percent of the planet. In the Twin Stars of Kira, the Kalindan Deep Space Fleet is mistakenly referred to as the "Kalidean Deep Space Fleet." Category:Fleets
