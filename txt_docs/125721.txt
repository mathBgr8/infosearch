Ala Kimbo
1303

Ala Kimbo was a male Human from Corellia who was wanted by the Hutt Clan for unknown reasons. He believed that he could escape the Hutt Clan by masquerading as a prison guard at Desolation Alley. In 32 BBY, Jango Fett claimed the bounty and proved him wrong. Ala Kimbo's Bounty is worth 4,500 credits alive and 2,200 dead.
