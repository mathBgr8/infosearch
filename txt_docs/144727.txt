Carbonite Sith Master
1152

thumb|right|175px|The unknown [[Carbonite/Legends|carbonite Sith.]] This unknown Sith was frozen in carbonite and placed in a tomb along with the Carbonite Sith Army. His life support monitors showed that his vital signs were good, and that the Sith was still alive. Next to the block of carbonite holding the Sith sat an urn, whose contents were unknown. Lying in a simple stand at the top of the urn was a lightsaber. The identity of this Sith and the reasons why he and the Sith Army were encased in Carbonite were unknown as of the Galactic Civil War. While not actually seen in the cutscene that reveals the army, the game files include this entry as "Carbonite Sith Master". *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Sith individuals
