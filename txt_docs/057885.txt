Scott Cleverdon

Scott Cleverdon (born July 31, 1969) was the voice of Obi-Wan Kenobi and Nute Gunray in The Phantom Menace game. He also provided the voice for Kenobi in Star Wars: Episode I Jedi Power Battles.
