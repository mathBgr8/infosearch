Ediorung
2327

Ediorung was a planet in the Interior Regions of the Hapes Cluster. The planet was known for the dynamic holosculptures it produced; however, these were strictly judged by the Commission for the Preservation of the New Order Art Group.Imperial Sourcebook, 19.
