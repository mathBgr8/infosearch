Leavesden Studios
1918

thumb|250px Leavesden Studios is situated near Watford in England. It was the primary filming location of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, which started production design in August 1996 with principal photography starting on June 26, 1997, and lasting for four weeks. In all, the production used the studio for two and a half years. *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace Scrapbook *The Star Wars Archives: Episodes I–VI, 1977–2005 Category:Filming locations
