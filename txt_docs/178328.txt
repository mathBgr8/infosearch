Imperial Medallion of Service
1026

The Imperial Medallion of Service was an award granted by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War, to "those who have honored themselves by serving the Empire with excellence and loyalty." Lord Hethrir granted this award to an Imperial agent who thwarted Rebel Alliance advances in the Corellian system. Category:Galactic Empire awards
