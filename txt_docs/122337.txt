Tantt
555

Tantt were a tribe of Humans native to Thracior. They objected to their people marrying members of other tribes such as the Hnsi. If done, it usually ended in the death of the whole family. Floria and Dane were members of the Tantt. *Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tribes
