Minestra
1994

The Minestra was a captured Imperial Customs Corvette that was part of Drea Renthal's pirate fleet. It was upgraded with ion cannons and concussion missiles in addition to its standard turbolaser armament. The Minestra was actually slightly better armed than Renthal's flagship, Renthal's Fist.
