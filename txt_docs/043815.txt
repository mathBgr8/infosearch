Treka Horansi
1659

The Treka Horansi were a subspecies of the Horansi species native to Mutanda. Treka stood on average 2.3 to 2.6 meters. They had the shortest hair of all the subspecies of the Horsani. They were very curious and inquisitive. The Treka were the most peaceful of all the Horsani, due to the isolation of their mountain caves where they lived. They were against the hunting of other Horsani, and would often intervene if they saw other members of their species in trouble. The Treka were the only Horsani that let off-worlders develop on their land, but were very protective of the hunting grounds they used for food.
