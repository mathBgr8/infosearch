Pixelito Art Guild
731

The Pixelito Art Guild was a guild that specialized in art. A Dug named Pixolga claimed that painted paw prints by a tamed nexu did not constitute true art. His accusations resulted in severe profit loss for the guild. The organization in turn posted a bounty on Pixolga's head, which was soon claimed by Jango Fett. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Commerce and labor guilds and unions
