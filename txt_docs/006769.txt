4020 BBY

The following events took place in 4020 BBY. *The Czerka Corporation lays claim to Kashyyyk, renaming the planet "Edean".The Essential Atlas *Andur Sunrider is born around this time.Tales of the Jedi Companion states Andur is a day away from his 12th birthday when Jev leaves. Months pass, and a year later Jev's spirit appears to Andur. He trains for the next six years, and then with Chamma for two further years leading directly into the events of Tales of the Jedi 3. Since that issue is set in 3999 BBY, calculating backwards suggests a birth year of around 4020 BBY for Andur. *Tales of the Jedi Companion *The Essential Atlas Category:Years in the post-Manderon period
