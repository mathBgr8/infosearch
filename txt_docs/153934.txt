Battle of Shaylin 18
1818

The Battle of Shaylin 18 was a crucial battle between the Alliance to Restore the Republic and the Galactic Empire that took place at Shaylin 18 after the Battle of Yavin. The victorious rebel forces were commanded by Captain Vanden Willard. Due to his performance, he would later be assigned the command of Delta Base and of Suolriep sector. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles in the Pakuuni sector Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
