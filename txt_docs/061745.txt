RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate
1021

The RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate was a deadly criminal organization known to have been on Malastare or Hok. It consisted mainly of Gran members, such as Byhdee Kees and Jaw-knee Rise. Due to a number of members possessing bounties on their heads, the group's numbers decreased on Malastare thanks to the coincidental appearance of the notorious Jango Fett. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Criminal syndicates and cartels
