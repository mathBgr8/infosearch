Rooarwaa
1048

Rooarwaa was a Wookiee mercenary who was once hired by a smuggler on the planet Kashyyyk to serve aboard the smuggler's YT-1300 freighter. The cost for his services was 200 credits per day. Rooarwaa's illustration was a re-use of the picture of Cherioer from Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Category:Males Category:Mercenaries Category:Wookiees
