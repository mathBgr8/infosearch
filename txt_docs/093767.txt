Slicer chip
741

The slicer chip was similar to the electronic lock breaker in that it was designed to hack into security systems. The slicer chip could be used to break into computers, droids, and other automated devices. Some were even capable of controlling entire vehicles, navigation systems, security systems or overriding a droid's programming. *Champions of the Force *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook Category:Security countermeasures
