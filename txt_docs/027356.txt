QuickSnackLite
841

QuickSnackLites were popular food bundles sold by the Biscuit Baron chain. They were lighter versions of the QuickSnack. During the Separatist Crisis, Biscuit Baron launched its month-long "Heroes of the Republic" campaign, which consisted of including commemorative holocubes in QuickSnackLite bundles depicting notable historical figures of the Galactic Republic (including Supreme Chancellors Anwis Eddicus and Vaila Pecivas, and the Jedi Masters S'diawae, Ikrit, and Jojighar).
