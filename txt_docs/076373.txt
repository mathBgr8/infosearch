Sith Slayer droid
666

Sith Slayer Droids were battle droid tanks that possessed an organic brain inside. Crafted through Sith alchemy, they were difficult and expensive to produce. Twelve meters in height, the Slayer possessed two shoulder-mounted blaster cannons as well as a single spinning blade on both arms. As a result of the alchemy, they were more capable of shrugging off blows which the Sith's organic warbeasts lacked. Category:Cyborgs Category:Droid tank models
