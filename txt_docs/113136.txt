J. M. Meulenhoff
3901

Uitgeverij J. M. Meulenhoff (*M=sfX), known simply as J. M. Meulenhoff and Meulenhoff, is an Amsterdam-based publishing company founded on September 15, 1895 by Johannes Marius Meulenhoff. J. M. Meulenhoff publishes Dutch and foreign translations of novels, poetry, essays, and non-fiction. The company was responsible for printing the Dutch translations of Star Wars novels after A. W. Bruna stopped publishing them.
