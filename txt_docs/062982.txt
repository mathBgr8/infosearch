Meen Burtak
1474

Meen Burtak was a death stick-addicted male Gran from Malastare, who was known to be a smart, manipulative informant to Jaw-knee Rise and a member of the RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. A bounty, 4000 credits alive, 2000 dead, was posted on his head by Brine Eyes-Li.
