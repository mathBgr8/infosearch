Model Edu A-34
1810

The Model Edu A-34 was a training facility, designed by Delvin Constructs and used by the Galactic Empire to train new Imperial Officers. Imperial commanders could easily construct these facilities on a planet, enabling them to promote and train new officers anywhere in the galaxy before sending them off to Core World academies (such as those at Carida or Corulag) for more formalized training.
