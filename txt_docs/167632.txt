Night Attack
3442

"Night Attack" is a short story written by Michael Stern for the 1990 Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game sourcebook Galaxy Guide 5: Return of the Jedi from West End Games. The story tells of Major Bren Derlin leading the Rebel strike team's insertion into Endor. It was later reprinted in the sourcebook's 1995 second edition, The Movie Trilogy Sourcebook, and released through the StarWars.com Hyperspace feature. After the Battle of Endor, Major Derlin recounted the battle to Voren Na'al. Derlin described how after General Han Solo split the Endor strike team, Derlin kept his team out of sight. At one point, he had to stop Delevar from taking a potshot at a scout trooper. When they set down to rest, their scanners picked up an AT-ST. When it approached their position, Derlin ordered Dansra Beezer to jam communications, then Greeve shot out its searchlights. The walker opened fire, and Delevar approached it with a Caspel launcher, who shot a smoke grenade right into the cockpit.
