SX troop transport
1347

The SX troop transport, also called a L two hundred, was a transport used by the Republic during the Clone Wars and later by the Empire to transport troops. They had broad wings and pointed bows. These transports were deployed during the Battle of Murkhana in the closing hours of the war. During the Great Jedi Purge that followed, ten Jedi survivors managed to steal a transport of this type from Dellalt.
