Allaya Djo
2094

Allaya Djo was the daughter of Augwynne Djo, mother of Teneniel Djo, and grandmother of Tenel Ka Djo. She was the warmaster of the Singing Mountain Clan until her untimely death in the desert. The mother of Teneniel Djo was first mentioned in the 1994 novel, The Courtship of Princess Leia, written by Dave Wolverton.
