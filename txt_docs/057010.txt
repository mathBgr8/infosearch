The Hatred Vector
816

The Hatred Vector was a planned four-part comic miniseries that would have been set prior to The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime and would have detailed the Yuuzhan Vong preparing for their invasion of the galaxy. It would possibly have been a direct lead-in to Vector Prime. *Star Wars Insider 47 Category:Dark Horse Comics comic miniseries
