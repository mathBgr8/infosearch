Bugnaught
1374

Bugnaught was a type of droid gladiator used by the Rattataki as training adversaries. One in particular fought in the Cauldron on Rattatak. This Bugnaught burned Carl to death with a flamethrower installed in his arm, but was ripped to pieces by Blorga. Several other Bugnaughts belonged to Asajj Ventress, which she used as lightsaber fodder. *Star Wars: Clone Wars Cine-Manga *Republic 60 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gladiator droid models
