Hypori droid factory
3492

This droid factory was one of many droid factories owned and operated by the Confederacy of Independent Systems on Hypori, which produced thousands of battle droids for their war against the Republic. thumb|left|250px|[[Zann Consortium/Legends|Consortium mercs and a Droideka Mark II in the Ground Battle on Hypori.]] The factory was discovered by Jedi Master Daakman Barrek and his Padawan Sha'a Gi, ensuing in the Battle of Hypori. This particular droid factory somehow survived the deactivation of the Separatist Droid Army by the Sith Lord Darth Vader in 19 BBY. thumb|right|250px|A overhead perspective featuring a part of the factory's layout. During the Galactic Civil War, the Hutt Cartel took an interest in this factory, and Jabba the Hutt hoped to use it to destroy Tyber Zann and his army. However, his plan was a failure due to Zann's tenacity.
