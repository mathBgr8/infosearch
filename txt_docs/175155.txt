Vennido T'all
1257

Vennido T'all was a male Bith musician who lived on Clak'dor VII. His music was notable for its strong anti-Republic sentiment, and in 21 BBY, he composed a song that contained samples from the Jabiimi separatist commander Alto Stratus' speeches to the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
