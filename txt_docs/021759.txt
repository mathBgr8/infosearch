|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=1 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika |issue=3 }} Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika 3 was the third issue in the Star Wars: Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika series of comics. It was released March 14, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. It's easy to get lost on the high seas of Mon Calamari, and when you're 8-feet tall and covered with hair, that's a prospect second only to Hell... Han, Chewie, and the rest of the Millennium Falcon passenger list scour the floating city of Mon Ubris for the man who can lead them to the fabled Vassilika of Yavin. Unfortunately his trail leads out across the vast ocean plains towards the mysterious Bank of Knowledge. Bossk and his fellow bounty hunters might want to think twice before crossing their paths, however, as there's not much meaner than a water-logged Wookiee!
