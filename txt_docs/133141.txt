Jabesq's surname was shared with Bernie Jabesq, another Theed Palace Space Vessel Engineering Corps member active around the same period.Star Wars: The Phantom Menace: The Expanded Visual Dictionary thumb|right|250px|Simon Jabesq's helmet from Star Wars: The Phantom Menace: The Expanded Visual Dictionary The character Jabesq was created for 1999's Star Wars: Episode I The Visual Dictionary, written by David West Reynolds. Although the caption accompanying Jabesq's helmet in the reference book states that "Jabesq" is the name written on the headgear, 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia established a transliteration table of the Futhork alphabet with which the writing on the helmet can be deciphered as "Simon." The same source also established "Simon" as Jabesq's first name.
