Nova Scout
935

A Nova Scout was a scout who had been inducted into the New Republic's Bureau of Scouting and Exploration Services' Nova Core. Talek Var was the youngest inductee into the Nova Scout "Hall of Fame," and also the first to be inducted posthumously.
