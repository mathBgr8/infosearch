Subelectronic converter
1421

The subelectronic converter was a brain implant invented by Imperial designer Nasdra Magrody that enabled Force-sensitive subjects to control droids remotely. One known recipient was Irek Ismaren, who used the implant to control the Eye of Palpatine.Jedi Academy Training Manual The subelectronic converter allowed Ismaren to visualize the programming of computerized devices, and, through the Force, override it. The subelectronic computer allowed him to apply his existing skill in Alter Mind. It took many months of practice to master. *Children of the Jedi *The Dark Side Sourcebook *The New Essential Guide to Droids *Jedi Academy Training Manual Category:Communications Category:Cybernetic implants
