Nuri Pravada
1324

Nuri Pravada was a female near-Human member of the Quetzal Sisters, a squad of 'femme-fatales' who worked for the Alliance to Restore the Republic. She along with the other Quetzal sisters, Zeela Nall and Sheyne Tyndos served with Leia Organa and General Orma Hundeen on a mission on Elerion to recover the holocube map containing the location of Rebel main bases from Nescan Tal'yo, Hermos, and Gogol.
