Fiver
1793

Fiver was Anakin Solo's modified R7 astromech droid a year into the war with the Yuuzhan Vong. When the Peace Brigade attacked the Jedi praxeum at Yavin 4 a little over a year into the invasion, Fiver flew Anakin's X-Wing to distract the Brigade. Fiver managed to fly the damaged X-Wing back to Anakin, and boarded the transport Anakin had fixed up. Fiver was aboard the transport when Anakin and Tahiri Veila prepared to make their escape.
