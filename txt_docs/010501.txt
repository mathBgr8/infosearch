There have been at least seven battles at the planet of Borleias in galactic history. Fifth Alsakan Conflict :*Battle of the Pyria system Galactic Civil War :*First Battle of Borleias, in 6 ABY. :*Second Battle of Borleias, in 6 ABY. :*Skirmish near Borleias, in 7 ABY. :*Battle of Borleias, in 10 ABY.
