Dorma's Cafe
812

Dorma's Cafe was a dining establishment run by a woman named Dorma during the last years of the Galactic Republic. It was located in the Cloud Lake District of Ussa on the planet of Bellassa. When the Galactic Empire took over the planet after the Clone Wars, the cafe fell on hard times as foot traffic thinned out and neighboring businesses closed.
