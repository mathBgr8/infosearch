Scurrier disease
1153

|transmissiontype=Bite from a scurrier |incubationperiod= |symptoms= |treatments= }} The scurrier disease was a dangerous affliction carried by scurriers. Any scurrier had a ten percent chance of carrying the disease, although the scurriers themselves were immune to it. Individuals bitten by an infected scurrier automatically caught the disease. *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Revised Core Rulebook Category:Diseases and maladies
