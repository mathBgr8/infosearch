Unidentified Jedi General
2524

This individual was a male Human Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order during the final days of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive, this individual received training in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order during the last decades of the Republic. Crafting for himself a green-bladed lightsaber, he attained the rank of Jedi Knight. When the Clone Wars erupted between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, this Jedi accepted the rank of General within the Grand Army of the Republic and led clone troopers in battle. Surviving until the end of the war, this man was standing amongst his men when Supreme Chancellor Palpatine betrayed the Jedi with the issuance of Contingency Order 66.
