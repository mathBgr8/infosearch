Harrowbane
569

Harrowbane was a poisonous weed that grew on the planet of Randorn 2. The native Mizx considered harrowbane a sacred plant and would construct barriers of it to keep away dangerous iblitons. The weed had an acidic burning effect on ibliton tentacles. *Creatures of the Galaxy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Poisonous plants
