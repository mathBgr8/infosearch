L'lahsh
1923

}} L'lahsh was a delicate beverage that was distilled from the nectar of t'iil blossoms produced on the planet Alderaan.A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Each blossom only produced a single drop of nectar, meaning that a single year's vintage took hundreds of thousands of blossoms to create. The nectar had to be picked by hand, and could not be harvested by a machine or droid.
