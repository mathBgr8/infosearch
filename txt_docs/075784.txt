Sith assassin pistol
1235

The Sith assassin pistol was an ancient tool used by the Sith to dispatch people quickly without alerting the target of the person's presence. The power setting could be adjusted to allow for quick or a lingering death. These were the same weapons that were used during the Great Sith War which proved to be quite popular at the time. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Hold-out blasters Category:Sith weapons
