Kristi's Klub
761

thumb|100px|[[HoloNet News/Legends|HoloNet News advertisement.]] Kristi's Klub was a trendy club on Coruscant. Its trademarked motto was "Putting the dive in diversions." Among the amenities offered were good food, attractive staff, and clandestine back-door exits. Droids were not allowed inside, however. Its exact location was either unknown to many, or known to most, as it operated on the maxim that "if you need to ask directions, don't bother coming."
