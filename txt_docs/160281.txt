|figures= |cards= |isbn=978-0-7869-4813-0 |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Battle of Hoth Scenario Pack is a scenario expansion set for the Star Wars Miniatures Game, produced by Wizards of the Coast. It was released in December 2007. It was announced at Celebration IV. It is a Target exclusive.
