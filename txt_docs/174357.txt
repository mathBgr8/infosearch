Star Wars Weekly 58
1206

Star Wars Weekly 58 is the fifty-eighth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 4, 1979. This issue includes part two of a four-part black & white reprinting of the unfinished story "The Kingdom of Ice", originally published in color in Marvel UK' Pizzazz magazine. The final part of the story was eventually printed in Star Wars Weekly 60. *"Death Trap!" *"Snow Fury!" *"The power of Warlock Part 5: The infinity effect!" *"Micronauts Part 4: Death-Duel at Daytona Beach!" (Continuation) *"Tales of the Watcher: I accepted the deadly challenge of Zarkorr!" 58
