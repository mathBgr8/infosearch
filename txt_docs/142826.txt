TDF officer
815

TDF officers were the organic individuals who held a position of authority and responsibility in the Trade Defense Force of the Trade Federation's, usually in command of the private droid forces. They were also stationed on Lucrehulk-class battleships with pilot droids. Their uniform colors varied from red to black to gray, but always had opalescent stripes. The Neimoidian Blox Hatha was one of these officers. *Rogue Planet Category:Soldiers Category:Trade Federation titles
