*7 Jadthus *360 clone troopers *Grassers *Akk dogs |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} The Battle of Lorshan Pass was the first large-scale battle of the Summertime War, and one of the last confrontations of the conflict. It resulted in a humiliating defeat for the Balawai militia at Lorshan Pass, although at a high cost of the Korunnai Upland Liberation Front. The Battle of Lorshan Pass was won mostly due to a risky, strategic maneuver by Jedi Master Mace Windu. *Shatterpoint Category:Battles of Haruun Kal Category:Battles of the Clone Wars
