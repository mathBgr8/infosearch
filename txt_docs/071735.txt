Ukian Torbull tail soup
1040

Ukian Torbull tail soup was a variety of soup made from the tail of the Ukian Torbull. The soup was served aboard the luxury repulsortrain Gem of Alderaan and at the annual Vor-cal Feast held at the House Melantha Orbital Hunting Lodge over the planet Vycinyth. *Lords of the Expanse *The Unknown Regions Category:Soups and stews
