Hokk
2455

Hokk was a member of the Disciples of Ragnos, a cult that wanted to revive the Sith Lord Marka Ragnos, who had died in 5000 BBY.Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 2 In 14 ABY,According to Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy, the events in the game take place ten years after the Battle of Endor, which according to the The Essential Reader's Companion is placed in 4 ABY. Hokk, along with others such as J'aahn, took part in an attempt to disable a cargo train in bustling Coronet City, capital of the planet Corellia. Jedi Padawan Jaden Korr and Jedi Master Kyle Katarn, who were in Coronet City to investigate reports of Disciples of Ragnos activity, heard a distress call from the cargo train. Korr quickly made his way over; when the Padawan arrived, Hokk was going to meet J'aahn at Station Six-C, but Korr stopped the cultists before they could.
