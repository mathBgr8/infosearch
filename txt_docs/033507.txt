Weggit Arpor
2519

Weggit Arpor was a male Human who represented the Tenloss Syndicate's operations within the Vergesso Asteroids. Arpor worked for the Tenloss Syndicate, specifically the distribution department of most of its legal fronts, managing its smuggling operations and contracting with smugglers, but also trying to keep them both happy and fearful to remain loyal to their business; a subtle art in which he gained experience through the years. He rose through the ranks of Ororo Transportation and rose through its ranks, before transferred to Galindas Exports's logistical support.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide He was transferred to the Vergesso Base, a shadowport owned by Ororo; the Life level of the base housed offices for the Galindas branch. His real role was to make sure that the base's leader, Nim Zanti, kept in line with the Syndicate's plans. Officially, he appeared as senior business and operations advisor, enjoying the constant protection of two bodyguards from the Natori Association. Arpor resembled a human reptile; his eyes almost never blinked, and it was difficult to determine his age.
