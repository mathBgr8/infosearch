Sienar Army Systems
1903

|founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Sienar Army Systems was yet another subsidiary of Santhe/Sienar Technologies. It was created during the Reborn Emperor's offensive to supply his new forces with war machines, by then-Director of Santhe/Sienar Technologies, Lady Valles Santhe. Sienar Army Systems never achieved the prestige or renown of the Sienar Fleet Systems, and would go down in history as a failure, despite its production of the TIE Crawler used in the Empire's reconquest of Coruscant. Their goal was to act as the land counterpart of the SFS, and create ground vehicles for the Imperial Army while the SFS provided starships to the Imperial Navy. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Galactic Empire corporations Category:Vehicle manufacturing companies
