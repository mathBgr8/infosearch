Donald F. Glut
2143

Donald F. Glut is a freelance writer and movie director. He wrote some Marvel Star Wars comics, as well as the Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back novelization. He has written numerous film and television scripts including the animated series Spider-Man & His Amazing Friends, Transformers, and G.I.Joe.
