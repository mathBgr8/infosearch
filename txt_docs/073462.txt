Mission to Fuel Station Ergo
4235

The Mission to Fuel Station Ergo was a skirmish in the Galactic Civil War in which Kyle Katarn raided the Imperial Fuel Station Ergo to board a smuggler ship headed for the Executor. After Katarn translated the nava card in the Imperial Security Operations building, Jan Ors understood that only Executor knew the Arc Hammer location at each given time. The Hammer was making leaps to hyperspace to keep its status secret. The only way to reach the factory was via the Executor, and the Super Star Destroyer was known to be supplied from Ergo. The Moldy Crow docked to one of the station's bays. Disguised as a smuggler, Katarn slipped into the Ergo orbital fuel station and proceeded to board the smuggler ship. In the station's cantina he relieved an Imperial officer from his keycard. In the fuel area, he operated the switches that control a rotating walkway in order to make his way to the main control room. He utilised the stolen keycard to access a cache with supplies, including an Assault cannon. From there he redirected the movable fueling arm from the docking bay of the Crow towards the docking bay of the target smuggler ship, which he boarded. Inside the docked ship, he fought several Gamorreans and Trandoshans, and stole one more yellow keycard from an officer, which he used to reach the ship's bridge. He activated the engines, but not before exchanging one last parting message with Ors over comlink.
