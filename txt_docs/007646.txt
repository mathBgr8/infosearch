Utapaun (disambiguation)
788

thumb|right|250px|Pau'ans (left) and Utai (right) were collectively called Utapauns. Utapaun was a collective term to refer to two separate indigenous species on the planet of Utapau: *The languid Pau'ans, who served as the modest patrician class on the planet *The short Utai, who served as the labor class and were humble by their nature There were two distinct species of Utapaun on the planet Utapau: *The short, stocky Utai, who developed deep within Utapau's sinkholes *The tall, gaunt Pau'ans, who developed on the planetary surface but later moved into the sinkholes as well.
