An extortion kingpin, he was affiliated with Klatooinian and Nikto street gangs. By 32 BBY,Star Wars: Bounty Hunter is set immediately after the Invasion of Naboo, which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 32 BBY. a four thousand credit bounty was placed on Sik, as he wanted dead for extortion by an anonymous originator. His last known location was the planet Coruscant and he was listed to be exterminated with "extreme prejudice." In 32 BBY, Sik was standing on a balcony in the Outlander Club, an establishment in the Uscru Entertainment District of Coruscant, when the bounty hunter Jango Fett entered. Sik had green skin and black eyes. When Fett landed on the balcony, Sik began to shoot at him.
