|published in=Wizards.com |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Living Force: Dark Shadows" is a short story in the Living Force campaign for Star Wars Roleplaying Game. It was written by August and Cynthia Hahn and was published on the Wizards of the Coast website on February 16, 2006. It continued the saga of Darrus Jeht, following on from Dark Hope, and followed by Living Force: One Minute to Midnight. The Jedi Master Darrus Jeht receives orders to attack the Confederacy planet Corlax 4, likely to result in the deaths of millions of civilians. While in transit there, Jeht receives a transmission from was Milinae Untaire. The smuggler informs him that Trilinae Untaire has gone missing, as well as that all the Jedi on Almas have been destroyed.
