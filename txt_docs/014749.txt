It was the sister world of the terrestrial planet Gambolla and was the site of the location Greccia. In 2 ABY, dates Fitz Roi's concert at Arcopola to the week of the date 37:8:14, which corresponds to 2 ABY per the reasoning here. the famous jatz musician Fitz Roi gave a rare Outer Rim concert during the Arcopola Baas Music Festival at Arcopola. Later that week, on the date 37:8:14, the Galactic Resorts correspondent Tanda Marelle reported on the concert from Greccia. Arcopola was mentioned in the "Galaxywide NewsNets" feature of the eleventh issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine, which was published in November 1996. The feature was authored by Paul Sudlow. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Arcopola system, and thereby Arcopola itself, in grid square Q-5.
