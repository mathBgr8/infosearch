Brett Rector
6796

*Lucasfilm Publishing |website= }} }} Brett Rector is a producer and editor on various Star Wars projects. He has worked on many video game manuals, and he served as editor-in-chief of Star Wars Insider from 2004 to 2005. He also was an assistant producer for Star Wars: The Force Unleashed, Star Wars: The Force Unleashed: Ultimate Sith Edition, LEGO Star Wars III: The Clone Wars, and Kinect Star Wars.
