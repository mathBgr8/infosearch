Qaresi Squadron
704

Qaresi Squadron was a starfighter squadron, equipped with X-wing starfighters, that was assigned to the Galactic Alliance warship Bounty during the Second Galactic Civil War. It participated in Alliance operations monitoring Bothan fleet movements in the Bothawui system. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance starfighter squadrons
