TT-6 was a Multi-Troop Transport in the Trade Federation Droid Army around 32 BBY. TT-6 was designated as the primary command liaison transport during the Invasion of Naboo, and carried the majority of battle droid officers on Naboo, conveyed to the planet's surface via a C-9979 landing craft. It was given priority to unload as soon as the landing zone was cleared. Category:Multi-Troop Transports
