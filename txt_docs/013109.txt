"The Loop" was a hyperlane located in the Greater Javin region of the Outer Rim Territories' Western Reaches. It looped around the Coreward side of the Kiax Nebula to link the planets Mijos, Shuldene, and Lutrillia, as well as two other star systems. In doing so, it passed through the Yarith and Anoat sectors, while intersecting with the Cerean Reach at Shuldene and forming a four-way junction with the Lutrillian Cross, the Koda Spur, and the Lutrillia–Darlyn Boda trade route at Lutrillia. *The Essential Atlas Category:Greater Javin hyperlanes
