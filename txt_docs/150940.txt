|type=CultureAlliance Intelligence Reports |leader= |subgroup= |origin= |region= |species=Humans |language= |established= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |distinctions=Valued pain and suffering |affiliation= }} The Entuurans were a Human culture that shared its name with Entuur, a celestial body in the Mid Rim's Urce sector, &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Entuur system as well as the procedures known as Entuuran resilience tests. The Entuurans valued pain and suffering. The male Human Mox Slosin, who was born sometime between five and six decades prior to 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports, which establishes that Mox Slosin already served the Galactic Empire approximately two and a half years prior to its setting, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. was an Entuuran from Entuur who had entered service to the Galactic Empire by 1 BBY and had become an Imperial High Inquisitor by 2 ABY. Slosin's sadistic tendencies potentially originated with his Entuuran upbringing. The Entuurans were mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. In the book, the Entuuran Mox Slosin was illustrated by Matt Busch. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Human cultural groups
