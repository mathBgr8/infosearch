Southern Campus
729

Southern Campus was a school of higher education on the planet of Coruscant. It was one of the dozen satellite campuses of the University of Coruscant. In 22 BBY, one week before the scheduled vote of the Military Creation Act, 7,000 students of the Southern Campus camped outside the Senate Building, protesting against the Act.
