Heavy Grav Mountainous Zones
543

The Heavy Grav Mountainous Zones were locations on Thrantin. Although the planet did not have such an area as a natural scape, it was artificially created by planet owner Barosa Warren. Warren spent decades planetscaping the world to create a controlled environment for his Galactic Outdoor Survival School. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Thrantin locations
