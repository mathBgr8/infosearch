Imperial Saboteur
1948

thumb|A saboteur Imperial Saboteurs were specially trained soliders skilled in demolitions and sabotage in the Imperial Remnant. Clad in flexible black armor, they were generally armed with a DL-44 heavy blaster pistol, an E-11 blaster rifle, or a DXR-6 disruptor rifle. Additionally, they were equipped with stealth field generators that rendered them virtually invisible, allowing them to evade enemy defenses. Saboteurs operated at various locations throughout the galaxy during the Galactic Civil War. Occasionally, they worked alongside stormtroopers on worlds of interest to the Disciples of Ragnos, a Sith cult backed by the Imperial Remnant. Jaden Korr, a Jedi Knight and student of Luke Skywalker's Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4, encountered several saboteurs during his missions. He foiled several of the saboteurs' plans, including attempts to destroy a power facility on Bakura, and blow up Tibanna gas tanks on Kril'dor. Saboteurs were also employed to deter Korr as he escaped from an Imperial facility on Dosuun and hamper his search for Rosh Penin on Taspir III. It is not known whether any saboteurs survived the defeat of the Disciples of Ragnos and Imperial Remnant. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy Saboteur Category:Sharpshooters and snipers
