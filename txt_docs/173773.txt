|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=137 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=*Star Wars: Legacy *Star Wars: Vector |issue=31 }} Legacy 31 is the thirty-first issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. It is the fourth and last issue of Legacy to feature the Star Wars: Vector cross-over arc, as well as the twelfth and final issue in the over-all Vector story arc. It is Jedi vs. Sith, and Sith vs. Sith in this, the conclusion to "Vector"&mdash;the year-long event that has shaken the galaxy, shattered lives, and brought about new beginnings ends here! Darth Krayt may finally be captured and defeated in the trap set by Cade Skywalker and Jedi Master of old Celeste Morne. But Emperor Krayt might no longer be the most powerful Sith lord in the galaxy. Will Cade and Celeste have another Sith to defeat... or join?
