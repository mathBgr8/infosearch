By the date 13:4:4, Senator Bufus Ritsomas represented Till Chorios in the Galactic Republic's Senate. Ritsomas was implicated in participating in an Outer Rim slavery ring that saw the senator profit from the activities of Thalassian slavers in the Meridian sector. Till Chorios was introduced in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #50, an issue of the representation of the in-universe HoloNet News that was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published by April 4, 2002. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Till Chorios system, and therefore the celestial body itself, in grid square R-6.
