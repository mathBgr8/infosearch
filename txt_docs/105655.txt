Gamma Fleet
1467

Gamma Fleet was a fleet of the Galactic Alliance Defense Force led by Admiral Traest Kre'fey during the Yuuzhan Vong War conflict. The fleet was supposed to participate Operation Trinity at the Bilbringi system. After jumping in-system, Alpha Fleet was supposed to transmit the system's weak spots to them and Beta Fleet. However, the Yuuzhan Vong launched several Mabugat kan creatures, which crippled several HoloNet relay stations. Alpha Fleet therefore was unable to send out any transmissions. With strict orders not to jump without the transmission from Alpha, Beta and Gamma Fleet held their positions. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy Category:Galactic Alliance fleets
