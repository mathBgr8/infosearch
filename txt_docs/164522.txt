|sector=Krenhner sector |system=Gmar Askilon system |coordinates=L-14 |xyz= |routes= |distance= |orbits= |stations= |asteroids= |other= |class= |diameter= }} Gmar Askilon was a star in the Krenhner sector of the Inner Rim. The star was where the runaway alien vessel known as the Teljkon Vagabond was sighted by New Republic Intelligence in 16 ABY. It was near Gmir Askilon that Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt and his task force intercepted the Teljkon Vagabond, and Lando Calrissian and his team successfully boarded the vessel.
