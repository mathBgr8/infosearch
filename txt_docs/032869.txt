UV-002
1021

The astromech droid UV-002 (or Uvee) was attached to the rescue starship No Luck Required during the Yuuzhan Vong War. He had been reprogrammed to be capable of flying the ship in emergencies, and in the messages he could flash up on the ship's displays, he had picked up first mate Vega Sepen's habit of referring to captain Uldir Lochett as "Boss-boy". *"Emissary of the Void" Category:Astromech droids
