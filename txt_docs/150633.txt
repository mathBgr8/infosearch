Tory's Catcher
2131

Tory's Catcher was an Imperial Immobilizer 418 cruiser. Under the command of Captain Gresia Vleen, the Imperial Immobilizer 418 cruiser Tory's Catcher patrolled the Noonian sector.Alliance Intelligence Reports At some point before 1 ABY, the Tory's Catcher captured smugglers Thi Sawrtin and Zayl Braith, who were performing a "rutinary" smuggling run through Noonian sector. Sawrtin tried to bribe Vleen, which led Vleen to shoot Sawrtin. Then, Vleen impounded Sawrtin's ship and convinced Braith to serve a sentence on Rol II. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Immobilizer 418 cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
