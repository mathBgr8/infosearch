Rayt (smuggler)
1111

Rayt was a male Human smuggler who resembled like a shorter, fatter Han Solo. He lived and worked on StarForge Station and had a Verpine copilot. Six months after the Battle of Endor he was responsible for shipping Tycho Celchu and Winter from Eiattu 6 to Leonia Tavira.
