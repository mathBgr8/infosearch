Kiltirin-class dungeon ship
2242

The Kiltirin-class dungeon ship was a Rendili StarDrive starship that was a scaled-down version of the Lictor-class dungeon ship. The Kiltirin-class measured 150 meters in length and could carry up to 1,250 prisoners. It was armed with five quad turbolaser batteries and a single tractor beam projector.
