|intl release= |pages= |issue=7 |featured= |series=The Official Star Wars Fact File }} The Official Star Wars Fact File 7 was the seventh issue of The Official Star Wars Fact File, released on February 13, 2002. The issue was authored by Darren Allanson, Andrew Darling, Chris Gardner, Iain Lowson and Jim Swallow.
