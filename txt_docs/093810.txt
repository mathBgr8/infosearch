Rylle'vak
715

Rylle'vak, also known as the Quiet Ocean in Basic, was a body of water that was present on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. Velhom'starn was located in this ocean. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothawui locations Category:Seas and oceans
