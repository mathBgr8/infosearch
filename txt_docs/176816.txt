Old Folks' Home
1696

}} Old Folks' Home was a codename used by Jedi to refer to the Jedi Temple, commonly by field agents on assignments or investigations. Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi and Jedi Master Adi Gallia both used the codename while on assignments prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars. Gallia reported from the Karthakk system under the codename "Shooting Star" to report on the conflict against Sabaoth Squadron, while Kenobi used R4-P17 to relay a message to Jedi Masters Yoda and Mace Windu. *Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter *Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones novelization *Star Wars: Episode II — Attack of the Clones 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: The Official Starships & Vehicles Collection 18 Category:Phrases
