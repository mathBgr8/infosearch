Tharen Wayfarer
1996

The Tharen Wayfarer was a starship owned and operated by Kalarba Safari, a Pitareeze family company. The family used the ship to take guests on the "Journey of the Spirit" tour, traveling over the Great Sea and past the Three Peaks of Tharen, Kalarba's symbol of the spirit. The Indobok Pirates, led by Captain Huba, captured the Tharen Wayfarer, but C-3PO, R2-D2, and Nak Pitareeze managed to convince the pirates to return it, in exchange for the location of Olag Greck's treasure trove and a Stenness pie recipe. *Droids (1994) 2 *Droids (1994) 4 *Droids (1994) 6 *Star Wars Encyclopedia *C-3PO: Tales of the Golden Droid *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia thumb|left|The Tharen Wayfarer over the [[Great Sea (Kalarba)|Great Sea of Kalarba.]] Category:Liners
