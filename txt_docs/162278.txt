Daniel
1434

Daniel was a male Mon Calamari who was paired up with a loved one during the Ewok Festival of Love. L-O53 tasked a spacer with finding a loved one for Daniel. After searching the cities of Tyrena and Kaadara during the Festival of Love, the spacer found a companion who shared similar interests with Daniel. Daniel was a Non-Player Character (NPC) in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies. He was in the game during the Ewok Festival of Love celebration of 2009. During the event, players could pair up Daniel, as well as several other NPC's, with companions who shared similar interests. Category:Males Category:Mon Calamari
