Nomad-Damon
1404

Nomad-Damon was a traveler who introduced many Ubese Thorn-Back War Dragons to populated worlds during the Clone Wars. Nomad-Damon was created by Mark Rehkopf, a Star Wars fan, for the backstory of his Ubese Thorn-Back War Dragon entry in the Design An Alien contest held for Star Wars Galaxy Magazine, which was eventually published in the seventh issue of the magazine in 1996.
