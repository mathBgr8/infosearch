Wirut
2300

Wirut, a Human male native of the planet Coruscant, was an officer who held the rank of Sergeant in the Galactic Alliance Guard in 40 ABY. He also served as a soldier of the 967 Commando, a company of elite GAG troopers led by Colonel Jacen Solo. During the Second Galactic Civil War, he arrested Chief of State Cal Omas&mdash;leader of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances&mdash;at the behest of Solo.
