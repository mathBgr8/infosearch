Judlie
912

Judlie was one of the five massively dug-in strongpoints constructed by the Freedom's Sons around the Intergalactic Communications Center during the Battle of Praesitlyn. It was placed as a defense for the rear of Zozridor Slayke's forces, six hundred meters behind his command bunker and the army's main defensive position, and benefited from a three-hundred-sixty-degree field of fire.
