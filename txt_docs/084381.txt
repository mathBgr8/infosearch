Pefederan Lloyn
1218

Pefederan Lloyn was the chairwoman of the Galactic Alliance Finance Council as of 40 ABY. Prior to the Second Galactic Civil War, she sold off her holdings on Corellia and replaced them with new property on Kuat, violating the theory that Financial officers were to be unable to have direct control over their holdings during their time in office. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Financial professionals Category:Galactic Alliance bureaucrats Category:Individuals of unspecified species
