Penumbra Omen
3997

|subgroup= |headquarters= |locations=Area D-512, Naboo system |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} Penumbra Omen was the code-name of a mysterious and hostile force of starships present in the Naboo system during the Galactic Civil War. Around the time of the Battle of Yavin, an unit of the Imperial Navy commanded by Captain Silverline, Imperial Authoritarian, was ambushed in the Area D-512 of the Naboo system and suffered heavy casualties. Captain Silverline reported that these assailants, probably droids, piloted starships similar to those used by Hutt smugglers and indicated that their leader identified itself as the OT-Violator. The Empire then considered the so-called Penumbra Omen as incredibly dangerous and prohibited all travel in the Area D-512, renamed Penumbra Omen Quadrant. Captain Silverline's emergency report was later intercepted by Fghai'g Windrunner, a pilot of the Royal Naboo Security Forces who considered to explore the Penumbra Omen quadrant. Penumbra Omen was mentioned in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
