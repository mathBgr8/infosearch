Keiffler Brothers
970

The Keiffler Brothers were a tech crew that operated from Pembric II during the early years of the New Republic. They were contracted to work for Moff Kentor Sarne on Kal'Shebbol, and, among other projects, designed many of the programming trapdoors that infested the systems of the CR90 corvette FarStar. They were considered the best slicers in the Kathol sector.
