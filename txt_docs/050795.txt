|release date=June 7, 2006 |pages=32 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars: Legacy |issue=0 |next=Legacy (2006) 0½ }} Star Wars: Legacy 0 is the pre-release supplement of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. It was released June 7, 2006, and instead of a story, served as an introduction to the series. It cost twenty-five cents. An expanded issue was later released. Welcome to the beginning of a grand, new chapter in the Star Wars saga! More than a hundred years have passed since the events in Return of the Jedi and the days of the The New Jedi Order. There is a new evil gripping the galaxy, shattering a resurgent Empire and seeking to destroy the last of the Jedi. Even as their power is failing, the Jedi hold to one final hope: the last remaining heir to the Skywalker legacy. It's a brave new galaxy for Star Wars fans, at once familiar and surprising&mdash;interplanetary intrigue and adventure on an epic scale; iconic heroes and villains cast in new roles and caught in surprising new situations. Everything you've loved about Star Wars, but nothing you expect! This special 25¢ comic sets the stage for the launch of the new Star Wars: Legacy series, which began in June 2006!
