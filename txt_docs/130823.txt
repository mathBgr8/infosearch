He operated from his palace on Revkinn V. Linrec fathered a daughter, Cressis Linrec, and he arranged her wedding to Moff Raiss. This infuriated one of Linrec's underlings, governor Dengless Rinn of Kallistas, who planned a coup against Linrec. However, Cressis was unhappy with the wedding and ran away with a pirate, Jondrell Inx. As Inx operated on Rinn's territory, Linrec offered Rinn a few days to save his daughter, or he would personally go to Kallistas with his troops.
