Episode I Adventures 13: Danger on Naboo
5413

Episode I Adventures 13: Danger on Naboo was the thirteenth installment of Star Wars: Episode I Adventures. The planet of Naboo has been blockaded. Obi-Wan Kenobi and Qui-Gon Jinn have been sent as Jedi emissaries to establish peace. But they find war instead. Naboo needs their help.
