While it was the oldest and most battered ship he owned, Gillespee refused to command any other ship. The Kern's Pride was armed with six double turbolasers and two ion cannons, and had a cargo capacity of 10,000 metric tons. thumb|left|150px|Kern's Pride deckplan. When Gillespee attempted to retire on Ukio, he kept the Kern's Pride with him, and later used it to escape the planet during Grand Admiral Thrawn's assault.
