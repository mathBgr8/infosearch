Gilt Gushers
737

The Gilt Gushers of Abregado-rae were a grouping of entirely natural fountain and spring formations located in the planets vast underdeveloped wilderness. They were named such, due to the fact that on rare occasions, flakes of pure Electrum would bubble to the surface.
