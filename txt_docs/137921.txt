Jean Rabe
1062

Jean Rabe was head of the RPGA in 1994* and the author of "The Breath of Gelgelar," "Day of the Sepulchral Night," and "The Farrimmer Cafe," Star Wars short stories originally published in the Star Wars Adventure Journals.
