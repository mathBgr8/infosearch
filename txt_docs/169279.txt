Unidentified stormtrooper (Battle of Maridun)
1167

This unidentified stormtrooper was a stormtrooper in the Imperial Army. He fought at the Battle of Maridun, where he was killed when he was stabbed in the back by an Amanin spear. After seeing the man's death, Janek Sunber realized he would never know the fallen trooper's name. *Empire 18 Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Stormtroopers of the Galactic Empire
