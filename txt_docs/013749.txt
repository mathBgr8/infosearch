|release date= |media type=EncyclopediaStar Wars: The Roleplaying Game |pages= |published in= |genre= }} The Encyclopedia Galactica was a reference book of some renown, published by TriPlanetary Press shortly before the Clone Wars. The entry for Neimoidians consisted of two words: See: Duros.
