The Chunkers were a gang of junk pirates active on the Twin World of Talus during the Galactic Civil War. They primarily stole items that other people had trashed or "chunked away"&mdash;hence their name. They would acquire weapons and equipment solely by looking through abandoned camps and junk piles.
