StarNailer-class fighter
658

The StarNailer-class fighter was a starfighter produced by Subpro Corporation in the waning years of the Galactic Republic. It was a rare and expensive ship that contained a class-two hyperdrive and was introduced as a late-model. Zam Wesell used a StarNailer-class fighter during her hunt for Hurlo Holowan. *Star Wars Adventures 5: The Shape-Shifter Strikes Category:Starfighter classes Category:Subpro Corporation products
