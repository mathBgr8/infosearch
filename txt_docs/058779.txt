Gryseium Incorporated
670

Gryseium Incorporated was a ranch founded by Huegu Eib, a Sullustan, but later sold to Rigis Corazon. It was located in the clouds of Tyed Kant, a planet in the Mid Rim. Corazon attempted to use Gryseium Incorporated to corner the food market on Demophon, but failed due to a lack of a contract with the Demophonian government or the Galactic Empire. His daughter, Sabel Axis Corazon, later took control over the company. Category:Agricultural companies
