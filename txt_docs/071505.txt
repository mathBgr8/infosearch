TDL-501 unipod waitress droid
1483

The TDL-501 unipod waitress droid was a service droid developed by AccuTronics shortly before the outbreak of the Clone Wars. The TDL-501 was manufactured to compete with the WA-7 service unit. It did enjoy moderate success but was later outdated. One droid of this series worked in the cantina of the RMSU-7 on Drongar. When the shield generator broke down, the XY-90 component was required immediately. Teedle had this component and she let the technical experts extract it to repair the breakdown.
