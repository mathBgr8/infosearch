Brogune
2066

The Brogune were a sentient species with four eyes. In 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The Approaching Storm to 22 BBY. a Brogune served as a concubine for the Hutt Soergg Vosadii Bezhin, who was a Bossban&mdash;a criminal leader&mdash;based on the Mid Rim &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Ansion system planet Ansion. The Brogune was one of his many concubines, which included Humans and members of other species. Brogune were among the species whose females the Hutt found attractive.
