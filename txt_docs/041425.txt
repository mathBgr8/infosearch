Nezmi was a location on Qiaxx known for its Bubble Cliffs. Black Nebula owned a casino in Nezmi and was headquartered out of a secret base within the cliffs. *Rebel Force: Renegade *Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 5 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Qiaax locations
