HuntSuit
1522

The HuntSuit was a powersuit that provided protection and was specialized for those in the bounty hunter profession. Bounty hunters of all types found the HuntSuit to work well for their purposes. The popular piece of protection was developed by Corellian Technologies. It was often modified with a power cell recharger, short-range motion sensors, and storage systems for extra equipment and munitions.Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear The suit typically cost 2,900 credits. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Corellian Technologies products Category:Powersuits
