White |publisher=Marvel Comics |release date=September 13, 2017 |pages=32 |upc= |timeline=c. 1 ABYStar Wars: Timelines dates the events of Star Wars (2015) 36 to around 1 ABY. |series=Star Wars (2015) |issue=36 }} Star Wars 36 is the thirty-sixth issue of the Marvel comic book series Star Wars. It was written by Jason Aaron and illustrated by Salvador Larroca. It was released on September 13, 2017. *The Empire has held Threepio captive for far too long.' *There's no way they are prepared to deal with this rescue. *That's right — Artoo is ready to take action!' }} On a solo mission, the resourceful astromech droid R2-D2 is aboard an Imperial Star Destroyer, determined to save his friend, C-3PO. Earlier, Imperial officers located a drifting X-Wing with no signs of life and decided to bring it in with a tractor beam, noting that they must be careful as Darth Vader is on board. When a containment team attempts to seize R2-D2, he electrocutes them with one of his many appendages. An Imperial officer scoffs at the idea that a single droid took down his team and orders more troopers to the hangar, telling them to be on the lookout for the biggest, nastiest droid they've ever seen. R2-D2 slips past the new troopers unnoticed and interfaces with the ship's systems. A technician reports that a second team is headed to the hangar—a team they didn't dispatch—and that they've lost contact with the first. The newly-arrived stormtroopers soon come under heavy fire, and both teams are quickly defeated. As the Imperials wonder what the droid could be after, R2-D2 continues searching for C-3PO's location. C-3PO is in an interrogation room, bolted to a table, and an Imperial officer tells him that he's given all the information he's going to give, so he will be dismantled on the orders of Darth Vader himself. R2-D2 arrives and the Imperial laughs. Soon, R2-D2 and C-3PO are heading back to the hangar as C-3PO remarks that they must have noticed his escape because he was a very important prisoner. The Imperial officer instructs his crew to shoot down the X-Wing holding the droids, but they can't get a lock on it. A technician suggests they must be dealing with a highly skilled pilot, a master spy. Darth Vader appears and Force chokes the technician for his incompetence. Vader orders them not to kill the droid and heads out in his TIE Fighter to intercept the ship. Vader fires upon the X-Wing with the droids on board, but R2-D2 turns the ship around to attack him directly. Suddenly, Luke Skywalker and Han Solo appear in their ships to help. Luke gives R2-D2 coordinates, and the group jumps to hyperspace. C-3PO tells Luke that R2-D2 was trying to get them killed and that his own calmness under pressure kept them alive. Elsewhere, aboard an Imperial shuttle, Scar Squadron discusses having found a Rebel outpost and readies their weapons for an assault.
