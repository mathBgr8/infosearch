The distance between Ather and the Tamarin sector's &mdash; Based on corresponding data for the star system containing Dravian Starport Dravian Starport could be traversed in two days using a starship equipped with a Class 1 hyperdrive. During the Galactic Civil War, the Dravian Starport&ndash;based shipping company Nar Shaddaa Shipping offered free-traders a job of transporting fifty tons of datapad circuit boards to Ather for 3,500 credits. Ather was mentioned in "Tales of the Smoking Blaster," a roleplaying game adventure written by Bill Smith. The adventure was included in Star Wars Gamemaster Handbook, a 1993 sourcebook published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Ather system, and therefore Ather itself, in grid square L-20.
