Forma
1510

Captain Forma was a crewmember and smuggler for the Mining Guild. He commanded their transport Draisini, which he piloted to a diplomatic meeting between the Guild and the representatives of the Galactic Empire and the New Republic. Refusing to compromise, he left to pick up an undocumented cargo.
