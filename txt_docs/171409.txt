Landing platform Nilat-3
1056

Landing platform Nilat-3 was one of the many landing platforms on Coruscant. The Jin'ha used this platform when smuggling weapons to the criminal organization Black Heth, an act that was interrupted by Jedi Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi.
