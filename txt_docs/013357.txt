Located in the Outer Rim Territories, it was located along the Perlemian Trade Route. Under the control of Moff Tol Getelles after the Battle of Endor, the world was home to a facility owned by Loronar Corporation where they produced CCIR needle fighters. Contacted by Seti Ashgad in 13 ABY, Getelles was offered a way to conquer the Meridian sector.
