Ulic Qel-Droma's mesh suit
1586

thumb|A [[Human/Legends|Human female in the armor.]] Ulic Qel-Droma's mesh suit was a suit of medium armor that was utilized by the fallen Jedi Knight Ulic Qel-Droma during the Great Sith War. Qel-Droma abandoned his armor, as well as all his trappings of the dark side after the murder of his brother, Cay. Even so, the dark taint continued to haunt it, with the possibility of affecting whomever wore it. Ulic Qel-Droma's mesh suit was intended to appear in the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic but it was removed from the final version of the game.
