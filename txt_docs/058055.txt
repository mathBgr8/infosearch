Golden Nyss Shipyards catered largely to smugglers, pirates, bounty hunters, and other elements of the galactic fringe. The droid NT 600 was built at this shipyard. During the Yinchorri Uprising against the Galactic Republic, it was one of the Yinchorri's first targets. First, they stole the ships docked there. Then, they destroyed the station altogether.
