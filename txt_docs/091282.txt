Jedi Guardian
2206

Jedi Guardians, also known as a Warrior Masters, were a group within the Jedi Order. The Processional Way in front of the Jedi Temple on Coruscant was terminated in a staircase that was crowned with the statues of two Jedi Warrior Masters and two Sage Masters.
