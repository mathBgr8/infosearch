The Senatorial Suite was a room of the Junavex Hotel on Taris, most likely for Gorravus. During the invasion of Taris, after the loss of its Highpoint office, the Suite was the headquarters of The Taris Holofeed. Category:Hotels Category:Rooms Category:Taris locations
