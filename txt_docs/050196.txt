DXR6 heavy carbine
2344

The DXR6 Carbine had a longer range than any other carbine on the market and dealt a reasonable amount of damage. It could be seen wielded by stormtroopers as well as bounty hunters and the Sith Shadow thugs. During the Galactic Civil War, bounty hunters often used the DXR6 for its long range, firepower, and durability.
