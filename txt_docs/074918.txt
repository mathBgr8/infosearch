Aleph (disambiguation)
866

Aleph was a letter of the Common Sith alphabet. It was also a letter of the Myke alphabet. As a name, "Aleph" could refer to several things: *Aleph-class starfighter, a Sienar Fleet Systems starfighter used during the Second Galactic Civil War. *Bunker Aleph-One, a base on Storinal from which Wraith Squadron managed to steal two Tie/ln starfighterss, destroying two more in the process. *Bunker Twenty-Two Aleph, a location where the shuttles of the starship Hawkbat resupplied. *Task Force Aleph, an element of the New Republic Fifth Battle Group.
