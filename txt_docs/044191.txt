Tulak
1502

Tulak was a male Advozse Warlord. He conquered Koba three centuries before the Battle of Yavin, imprisoning the native Kobans. Jedi Knight Polvin Kut and his Padawan, Yaddle went there to stop him. However, Tulak was more than ready and when the two Jedi arrived, he ambushed them. His forces killed Kut and captured Yaddle. After Tulak had her tortured, his men threw her in a dungeon in hopes she could be of some use. By the time Yaddle escaped, over a century later, Tulak had died, and his son, Kalut was the new Warlord. *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook Category:Advozsec Category:Males Category:Warlords
