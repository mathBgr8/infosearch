Relics of Geonosis
1295

The "Relics of Geonosis" refer to any remaining battle droid or starship left on Geonosis following the First Battle of Geonosis of the Clone Wars. An entire army of marooned B1-series battle droids that failed to respond to the shut-down code following the Mission to Mustafar, an LAAT gunship, and a Jedi starfighter were among the relics. During the battle of Geonosis of the Galactic Civil War, several relics were repowered by Imperial and Rebel forces.
