*Rusel DeMaria *Ocean Quigley *Martin Cameron *John Bell *The Dunlavey Studio |editor= *Roger Stewart *Neweleen Trebnik *Lindy Daily Paulson *Matthew Briere Hansen *Rick Barba *Dave Maxwell *Dave Wessman *Ocean Quigley |publisher=Prima Publishing |release date=June 1, 1993 |type=Paperback |pages=432 |dimensions=7.5 x 0.8 x 9.2 inches |weight=1.6 pounds |isbn=1559583754 |series=Prima's Secrets of the Games |preceded by= |followed by= }} X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide is a computer game strategy book for Star Wars: X-Wing written by Rusel DeMaria, David Wessman, and David Maxwell and published by Prima Games. It contains much of the story from The Farlander Papers and continues the story of Keyan Farlander throughout, along with walkthroughs and strategies designed to help players defeat the simulator missions, the missions for the game's first three Tours of Duty, and interviews with the game's creators and design team. A subsequent edition, X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide, reprinted the original material and added walkthroughs for the Imperial Pursuit and B-Wing expansion packs. Climb into the cockpit of a Rebel fighter craft and join Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo, Chewbacca the Wookiee, and the newest Rebel hero, Keyan Farlander, as they fight the evil Emperor and his minions. Face danger a hundred times — then come back for more! Written by the author of The Farlander Papers, this handbook continues the story of Keyan Farlander's adventures. These further adventures will take X-Wing players through every important detail, as well as each strategy and tactic needed to succeed at the increasingly hair-raising missions. X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide is the ultimate resource for fans of LucasArts' hit game.
