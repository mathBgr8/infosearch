Adam Bareham
922

Adam Bareham (born July 29, 1953 in Bromley, London) is an actor who played Jhoff, a traffic control officer on the Executor, in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. Bareham has appeared in a number of television shows like Inspector Alleyn Mysteries, Wycliffe, Minder, Doc Martin, Pie in the Sky and The Bill.
