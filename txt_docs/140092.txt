Newlife Point
947

Newlife Point was Carosi XII's capital and only city. Its northwestern sector contained the planet's starport, major hospital, asylums, and cybernetics facilities. The Carosite's disdain for alcohol resulted in Newlife Point's small number of bars; where alcohol was available, but it was heavily taxed. Offworlders also found the Carosite tendency to dispense dietary advice annoying.
