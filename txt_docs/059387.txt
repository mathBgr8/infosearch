Identification 43641
1279

Identification #43641 was the identification given to an investor which joined the Dorumaa Investment Group sometime before the Battle of Naboo. The group invested heavily in a deep-ocean landing platform, ostensibly for use as a fishing base, though no sea vessels were ever seen near it. Other investors believed #43641 to be a front for Thaereian military forces in the Cularin system.Living Force Campaign Guide The Dorumaa Excavation, a cave that held presumed proofs of native intelligent life in the Cularin system in ancient times, had been closed to any public access since before Identification #43641 was first noticed. Around 31 BBY, Identification #43641 publicly notified that those proofs were false; however, access to the site was still forbidden. *Living Force Campaign Guide Category:Aliases, nicknames, and pseudonyms
