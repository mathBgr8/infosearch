Trustant
752

Trustant was the title given to the Caamasi who led the individual Caamasi Remnant communities. The Trustant was responsible for maintaining the community's ways, in the hope of one day returning to Caamas, destroyed by the agents of Emperor Palpatine after the Clone Wars. *I, Jedi *Vision of the Future *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook Category:Political titles
