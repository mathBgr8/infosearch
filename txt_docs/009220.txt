Hariz
1300

Hariz was a city on N'zoth, located 150 kilometers southeast of Giat Nor. The city included the starport called Hariz Downside, and outdoor markets. It was connected to Giat Nor by a hovertrain. It was within Hariz that Nil Spaar reappeared after fleeing Coruscant. It was one of the only cities that allowed non-Yevethans to walk around unaccompanied.
