Andevor Javin
1888

Andevor Javin was the author of a book entitled Carving the Republic: The Real Story of Count Dooku. The book concerned the eponymous Jedi Master Dooku, who had resigned from his postition in the Jedi Order to found a militarized confederacy of planets that wished to secede from the Galactic Republic.The New Essential Chronology On the date 13:2:28, according to HoloNet News, Javin's book was number-one on a list of best-sellers. Andevor Javin was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #45, a 2002 in-universe website. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content.
