Typtus of the 33rd Egg
1112

Typtus of the 33rd Egg was a Geonosian aristocrat of the Gehenbar hive. Named after the famed Gehenbar leader Typtus the First, he aimed to bring back the glory of his namesake. As his name suggested, he was born from the 33rd egg in his cr&egrave;che, considered to be a lucky number by Geonosians.
