Nocturne of the Winged-Stars
525

The Nocturne of the Winged-Stars was a natural phenomenon on Caluula that occurred once every 300 years. The event of 29 ABY was disrupted by the Yuuzhan Vong War. The winged-stars were wiped out by the Alpha Red virus. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Events
