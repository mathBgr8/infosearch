T. Lund Phuna
2313

T. Lund Phuna was the local Constable on the mining asteroid Oseon 2795, appointed by the Administrator Senior of the Oseon system during the Imperial Period. A squat, curly-haired Human male iven to chain-smoking cigarettes, Phuna was secretly in the employ of Governor Duttes Mer of the nearby Rafa system and his associate, Sorcerer of Tund Rokur Gepta, who plotted to rope in a traveler stopping in the Oseon to help them acquire an ancient Sharu artifact called the Mindharp. Phuna was known to spend his time playing sabacc&mdash;on one occasion in 3 BBY, Phuna played a game with visiting smuggler Lando Calrissian, whom Mer and Gepta had identified as a likely target. After being cleaned out for over a thousand credits, Phuna planted a cheater on Calrissian and attempted to arrest the man.
