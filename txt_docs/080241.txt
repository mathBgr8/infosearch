Kuvara tree
1727

The kuvara tree was a species of tree that lived on the Mid Rim &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Qiilura system world of Qiilura in 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, including those in which the kuvara tree was involved, in 22 BBY. They produced a fruit, which was named kuvara like the tree itself, that was edible by Humans. The kuvara trees' wood was used in the rare, lavish homes of Qiilura. Kuvara trees appeared in the 2004 novel Hard Contact, the first novel in the Republic Commando series written by Karen Traviss. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Plants of Qiilura Category:Trees
