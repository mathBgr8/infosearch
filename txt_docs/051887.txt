Briletto Company
1366

|products=*AAP blaster box **AAP-II blaster box **AAP-IV blaster box |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Briletto Company was an arms manufacturer that developed a series of strap-on laser weapons known as AAP blaster boxes, which could be affixed to droids without extensive reprogramming. The company's AAP-II blaster box was used by BL-series Battle Legionnaires, while its AAP-IV blaster box was slated for use by Z-X3 experimental droid troopers. *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Arms manufacturing companies
