|rebuilt= |builder= |mayor= |celestialbody=Vulpter |continent= |location= |climate= |interest=Industrial complexes, corporate offices and recreational facilities |population=None |demonym= |affiliation=*Vulptereen *Trade Federation }} Benoga was an abandoned city on the planet Vulpter in the Deep Core. At one time, it was a large, heavily populated city with significant industrial complexes, but after the intervention of the Trade Federation, Benoga became deserted. Benoga was one of the largest abandoned cities on the surface of the planet Vulpter in the Deep Core.The Essential Atlas Once a heavily populated city that supported industrial complexes and corporate offices, Benoga quickly fell into disrepair when the Trade Federation decided to close the manufacturing plants and relocate the company headquarters offworld. Within a few years, the city was deserted – except for the occasional drifter or pack of vermin. Buildings, apartment complexes, factories, recreational facilities, and mass transit systems were left standing in the city, giving the occasional visitor the sense that the entire populace had simply vanished overnight. In time, some structures fell into disrepair and were corroded by acid rain and other pollutants, but for the most part, Benoga remained a city without inhabitants. The fate of Benoga was not unique; since the Trade Federation's rise to power on Vulpter, and given the organization's indifference toward the local population, the planet's surface had become dotted with ghost towns and dying settlements. A brief history of the city of Benoga was included in an article on the planet Vulpter for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game published by Wizards of the Coast in 2005. Benoga was considered as a potential primary setting for the scenario «Ghosts in the Darkness», in which players were tasked with capturing the wanted criminal amid the ruins of an abandoned city, who was rumored to be possessed by mysterious dark powers.
