Antbbianplourr III
1006

Antbbianplourr III was the Emperor of Eiattu 6 during the final days of the Galactic Republic. He bartered with the Galactic Empire to keep the throne and Imperials away from the world. He was succeeded by his only son Uthorrferrell Cartha.
