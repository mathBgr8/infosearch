Chardaan Shipyards
1773

The Chardaan Shipyards were a shipyard affiliated with the Rebel Alliance and the New Republic.Darksaber They produced many starfighters, including A-wings, B-wings, E-wings, X-wings, and Y-wings. Some of their A-wings were used in the Battle of Endor. The shipyards' structure included silver pressurized hangar spheres.
