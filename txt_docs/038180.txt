Shirmar Base
580

Shirmar Base was a staging area and a processing center built on the Bespin moon of Drudonna. It was constructed by the Ugnaughts to allow them easier access to Velsor's Ring, where they maintained mining operations. *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition Category:Buildings
