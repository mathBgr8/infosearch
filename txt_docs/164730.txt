Palace Traffic Control Center
1715

The Traffic Control Center was a multi-story data center located in the upper levels of the Imperial Palace on Coruscant. Established in the Imperial Palace on Coruscant by the time of the Galactic Empire's reign over the galaxy. Located in one of the upper towers of the Palace, the Traffic Control Center was responsible for processing all in-system starships and planetary air-traffic that was head for the Palace. A large facility located high in the Imperial Palace, the control center's central chamber featured a 40-meter wide holographic spherical grid displaying the location of any ships in sensor range that were en route to the Palace.
