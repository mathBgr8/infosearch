Fhnark & Company Exotic Foodstuffs
913

Fhnark & Company Exotic Foodstuffs was an exotic food and delicacies supplier founded by Fhnark. They were noted suppliers of fine nala tree frogs. Borga the Hutt was a large customer.The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse Jabba the Hutt also ordered his favorite trufflites from them.
