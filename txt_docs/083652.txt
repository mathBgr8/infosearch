Wayfarer (Tof starship)
2575

The Wayfarer was a large boat-shaped star cruiser, commanded by Tof forces led by a Supreme in the Battle of Zeltros in 4 ABY. The design of the Wayfarer was reminiscent of earlier styles, such as the Fairwind. It seemed to be of the same design as its fellow Tof warship, the Merriweather, though there were minor differences. Luke Skywalker and a team of Hoojibs and Hiromi infiltrated the Wayfarer during the Battle of Zeltros to rescue the Zeltron aides Bahb, Jahn, Marruc and Rahuhl, that the Tofs had mistaken for important Alliance commanders. The rescue was successful and cemented the Zeltron commitment to the Alliance.
