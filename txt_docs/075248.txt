The Violet Viska was a cantina in the city of Ariana on Garos IV. It was so named due to a large sculpture of a Viska over the entrance. It had a Baragwin bartender and a layer of grease over all of the interior surfaces. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin Violet Viska Violet Viska
