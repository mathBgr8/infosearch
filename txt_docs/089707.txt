Cree'n'aak Palace
1802

Cree'n'aak Palace was the residence of His Potency the Shreeftut, the supreme ruler of the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium. It was located on the Ssi-ruuvi homeworld and Imperium capital of Lwhekk, deep in the remote Ssi-ruuk Star Cluster which was far away from the main disk of the galaxy. Cree'n'aak Palace was nearly a kilometer tall and covered hundreds of square kilometers. It was located on the northern continent of the Ssi-ruuvi homeworld. Within the fortress were hundreds of thousands of His Potency's loyal advisors, supporters, and attendants. It was also the location of the planet's largest military installation, which was on the palace grounds. Housed in the palace complex were all of the planet's weapon and military technology factories, which made control and inventory of their military hardware very simple. The imperial palace also housed the head of the planetary computer and information network.
