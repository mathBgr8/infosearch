G3-5LE
2328

G3-5LE was a droid that worked aboard the Star Tours travel agency's Tomorrowland Starport. It was seen near the MSE droid track plugging and unplugging a fuel tube, using a broken MSE unit as a table to rest it on. G3-5LE was created for the 1987 Disney simulator ride theme park attraction Star Tours, where it was featured in the ride's queue at Disney's Hollywood Studios.
