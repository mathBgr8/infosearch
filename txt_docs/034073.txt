Highland Green Washeteria
1683

The Highland Green Washeteria was a laundromat-cum-eatery on the north side of Pelek Baw, the capital city of Haruun Kal. It was a cover for Republic Intelligence; its owner, Phloremirlla Tenk, and five of its 37 employees, were undercover Intelligence agents. It was made from white tiles with black grout. Possible modifications included durasteel blast shutters.
