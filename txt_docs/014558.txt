Master Shaper
1984

Master Shaper was a rank of the Shaper caste. Once an adept was escalated to Master, they would sacrifice one&mdash;or both&mdash;of their hands, and replace them with Shaper hands. Also, Masters were allowed to view the sixth and seventh cortexes of Shaping protocols. Master Shapers were denied one form of shaping&mdash;that of parenthood. They were supposed to be above the carnal, and could not take mates. Master Shapers were also distinguished by full-body oozhiths. Mezhan Kwaad and Nagme were Master Shapers.
