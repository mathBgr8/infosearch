Joz Larutur
1254

Joz Larutur was a male Human who was the husband of Cira Larutur and one of the hostages that were taken during the Galactic City spaceport hostage siege. In 22 BBY, Joz and Cira were on their way to see their first grandchild when they were taken hostage by Nuriin-Ar for the immediate withdrawal of the Republic forces on Haruun Kal.
