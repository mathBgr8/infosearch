|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: Dark Force Rising |issue=2 }} Dark Force Rising 2 was the second issue in the Star Wars: Dark Force Rising comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel Dark Force Rising. It was released June 25, 1997. With Obi-Wan Kenobi and Yoda both dead, Luke Skywalker is in need of a new Jedi mentor—and he finds one in C'Baoth. Risen from the ashes of a shrouded past, C'Baoth may prove to be just the teacher Luke needs to deliver the final blow to Grand Admiral Thrawn's ailing Empire. Or he may be the Empire's last hope for turning the tide ...
