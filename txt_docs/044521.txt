Spacer Quarter
796

thumb|right|250px|A map of the Spacer Quarter The Spacer Quarter was a section of the city of Aldera that catered to the needs of offworlders. Services offered included several bars, a gambling hall, several hotels, a public park, and a pawnbroker. Ahr's Dive, the Drunken Thranta, the Eclipse, the Tranquility, and Spacer's Luck were located in the Spacer Quarter.
