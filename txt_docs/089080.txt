Drebbin
1537

Drebbin were horned creatures native to Dathomir. In 8 ABY, Teneniel Djo said to Luke Skywalker that they preyed on rancors, though subsequent New Republic sources described them as quarry for the rancor. A major predator on the world, the drebbin had thick hides and evoked fear among the other species of Dathomir.
