|complement= |bays= |escapepods= |crew= |skeleton= |passengers= |capacity= |cargohandling= |consumables= |lifesupport= |communications= |othersystems= |role=*Territorial defense *Command ship |commission= |destroyed= |retired= |battles=Battle of Tammuz-an |affiliation=Tammuz-an |navy=Tammuz-an space navy |fleet= |owners= |captains=King Mon Julpa |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} King Mon Julpa led his planet's fleet from a command ship during the Battle of Tammuz-an. The vessel appeared slightly different from the only other Tammuz-an capital ship seen, lacking a stern protrusion and having several smaller bow protrusions, which might have been connected to its weapons-systems. The bridge area was located underneath the main superstructure, with a command area similar to many other warships. The controllers and technicians were seated at consoles arranged around the main walking area. During the Battle of Tammuz-an, King Julpa's fleet was led by this vessel, which participated in the frontline action against Gir Kybo Ren-Cha's stolen Star Destroyer, the Demolisher.
