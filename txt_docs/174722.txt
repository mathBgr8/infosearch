Slick (drug)
777

Slick was a recreational drug that was made from fermented seaweed oil that otherwise had medicinal properties. The primary effect of the drug was that it induced a temporary state of euphoric delirium. The drug originating from the Outer Rim world of Vaynai; the Bothan Oneron Fil'vye, who ran a shipping business that operated out of Straysel Island on that planet, was a heavy trafficker of Slick. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Euphoriants
