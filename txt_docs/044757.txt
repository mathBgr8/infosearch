Tront
1463

Tront was a Gamorrean pirate who worked for Grappa the Hutt under the command of Sol Mon. Tront hated droids, and never missed an opportunity to damage or destroy one, particularly 4/NG. Tront, like many Gamorrean guards, wore a strap across his chest with an armor uniform underneath.
