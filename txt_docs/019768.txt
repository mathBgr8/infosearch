All Science Research Academy
582

The All Science Research Academy was a prestigious school on the world of Yerphonia. As a young man, Granta Omega attended the school, with the sponsorship of Senator Sano Sauro. Omega graduated from the academy a short time before the Battle of Naboo. *Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Academies
