Vongspawn
1353

right|thumb|[[Hosk Trey'lis and Nei Rin seeing to an infected patient at the Jedi Temple on Ossus]] Vongspawn was a derogatory term for sentients infected with Vongspawn virus as a result of the sabotaged Ossus Project. Many had looked to the Galactic Empire for aid, not aware that the Empire was in league with the Project's original saboteurs, the Sith Order.
