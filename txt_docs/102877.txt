Hasville Terson
1154

Hasville Terson was a male Human living on Coruscant prior to, and during, the early stages of the Yuuzhan Vong War. He and his wife, Adray owned and operated the Terson Comfort Carriers air taxi company. Hasville and Adray had a son, Hasray, who was killed by Denua Ku. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Business owners and executives Category:Coruscanti Category:Humans Category:Males
