Viidaav blaster rifle
794

thumb|250px|A Viidaav blaster rifle. This blaster rifle was in operation during the Clone Wars and used by the native warriors of Viidaav. The gray-hued weapon had a long, wide barrel and a shoulder mount. These blasters were used in combat against Republic clone troopers during the Battle of Viidaav. They proved to be a match for the clones' DC-15A blaster rifles. Category:Blaster rifles
