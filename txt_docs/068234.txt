The Krayt Cantina was a cantina in Mos Eisley, Tatooine. Kyle Katarn was familiar with the place, and, in 14 ABY, he went there to investigate leads on the Cult of Ragnos. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy Category:Cantinas Category:Mos Eisley locations
