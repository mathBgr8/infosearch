Greg Titus
1402

Greg Titus is an illustrator and concept artist. He penciled the comic "All for You," a part of Star Wars Tales 17. While working at Pilot Studio from 2014 to 2019, he developed the art and was the principal illustrator for the Black Series of action figures.
