Zisian
1231

Zisians were Near-Humans with scaly green skin and orange eyes. In their old age, they often suffered from rheumy eyes and wrinkled skin hanging in loose flaps. One individual of the race was Ganar Tos.The Paradise Snare While the physical description of Ganar Tos seems somewhat reptilian, his voice is described as "human,"Rebel Dawn the term humanoid is used in the text rather than Near-Human, and he apparently expected to be able to have children with a Human woman, Bria Tharen.
