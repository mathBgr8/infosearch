|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYStar Wars Omnibus: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 1 |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issue=27 }} X-Wing Rogue Squadron 27 was the twenty-seventh issue in the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron series of comics. It was released on February 4, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. Corellia may have somehow managed to avoid the devastating war between the Empire and the Rebel Alliance, but the back streets of Coronet City have their own brand of villains, and Rogue Squadron can be as heroic on the ground as they are in the sky!
