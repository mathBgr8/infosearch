3-MAL personal comlink
2660

The Crozo 3-MAL personal comlink was used by the Rebel Alliance at Echo Base. It monitored "Standard Clear Frequencies" for civil defense warnings and for important information about local regulations and medical or governmental facilities in the vicinity. The 3-MAL private comlink was manufactured by Crozo Industrial Products. A small cylindrical device with simple controls, it was a military-issue comlink featuring limited encryption modules that were designed to prevent eavesdropping. The comlink was capable of transmitting and receiving on hundreds of channels, and automatically monitored Standard Clear Frequencies for emergency broadcasts. The 3-MAL had a range of fifty kilometers, capable of reaching low orbit. The Alliance to Restore the Republic used the 3-MAL extensively, and issued it to their naval troops and ground forces.
