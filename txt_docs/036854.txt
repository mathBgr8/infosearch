Korval Willic
1408

Korval Willic was a male Human spy for the Alliance to Restore the Republic on Imperial Center during the time of the Galactic Empire. A resident of the Senate District, Korval claimed to spend all of his credits and time in the Outlander Club. He was executed by his own grandson, ISB agent Vune Willic.
