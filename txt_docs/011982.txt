Kolermigon
2486

Kolermigon was a Rebel Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport captured during the Galactic Civil War. Imperial forces encountered Kolermigon in the final engagement of the Battle for Honor, just prior to the capture of Admiral Harkov. It was stationed on Rebel Outpost DS 5 loading Rebel evacuees as the Imperial Navy launched its final assault on the platform. Imperial Alpha-class Xg-1 Star Wing assault gunboats disabled Kolermigon, prior to its capture by Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transport Omega.
