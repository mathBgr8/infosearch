Garve
1449

Garve was a Human mercenary with red hair and flamboyant earrings. He was hired by the Galactic Empire to be stationed at Jellyfish Cove. After the jellyfish statues tried to kill him, he killed his Imperial replacements and took the colony's prisoners to Mos Eisley, where he handed them off to Corporal Phlik as the newest recruits to the Rebel Alliance.
