Davonn
1694

Davonn was a Human male lieutenant who served in the air forces of Naboo. In 31 BBY, just after the Invasion of Naboo, Davonn was the pilot of an N-1 starfighter on Kwilaan spaceport. He was the leader of Guardian flight. The YT-1300 light freighter Majestic Gundark tried to take off without permission, as it was believed that it was holding an illegal cargo. Davonn and another member of Guardian Flight took off and tried to intercept the ship. Although Davonn could not stop the Gundark from reaching hyperspace, Davonn managed to track its hyperspace vector.
