Drossk
1653

Drossk was a male Trandoshan who worked for Gardulla the Hutt during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted alive by multiple entities for questioning in connection to Hutt accounting practices by the Senate Investigative Committee. He was also wanted dead by Jabba Desilijic Tiure for causing considerable damage. He was killed by Jango Fett who claimed the bounty on his head. Drossk can be taken in dead or alive for the same price.
