Frozian (language)
1068

|script= |dialects= |characteristics= |related= }} Frozian was the native language of the Frozian species, natives of the devastated world of Froz. Frozians were usually bilingual, speaking both Frozian language and the predominant language of their adopted homeworld, as Froz was no longer inhabited.
