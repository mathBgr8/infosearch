Sarpazian Rose
1591

The Sarpazian Rose was a ship used by the female Human smuggler Bera Kazan during the Clone Wars. It was somewhat dented and scarred, a trait owing to the extreme conditions in which Kazan operated. The Sarpazian Rose was first mentioned in the manual packaged with Star Wars: The Clone Wars, a video game released in 2002. *Star Wars: The Clone Wars video game Category:Smuggler starships
