Nala tree
602

Nala trees were presumably the staple of nala tree frogs. They were bulbous in appearance and thrived on the tropical planet of Ylesia. Additionally, the trees were quintessential means for hiding used by slaves escaping the depressive rule of the Besadii Hutts. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Trees
