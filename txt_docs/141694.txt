David Tattersall
2078

David Tattersall is a British cinematographer who was one of the primary cinematographers on George Lucas's 1992 television series The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles, for which he won an Emmy Award. He later photographed four of Lucas' films: Radioland Murders (1994), Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace (1999), Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones (2002) and Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith (2005).
