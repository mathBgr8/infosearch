RS Revenge
4314

The RS Revenge was a cruiser in the Rebel Alliance's fleet during their civil war against the Galactic Empire. At some point between 0 BBY and 1 ABY,The New Essential Chronology it was captained by a man named Kazan and stationed at an Alliance base that was hidden in an asteroid field. When the Rebel-allied smugglers Han Solo and Chewbacca left the base to retrieve a mystical artifact known as the Talisman, Kazan recorded a starlog journal entry about their mission from the RS Revenge bridge. His entry, which mentioned the cruiser by name, was later used as narration for a cartoon created about the mission.The Star Wars Holiday Special The RS Revenge only mention in Star Wars canon is in The Story of the Faithful Wookiee, a cartoon written by George Lucas and produced by Nelvana Studios.
