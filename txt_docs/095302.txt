Gathi
1900

The Gathi was a group of which Can Lar was a member. At one point he worked for the CoMar Combat Systems company, where he proposed a design for an anti-orbital ion cannon. After the company rejected the proposal, Lar quit working there and instead presented the design to Kuat Drive Yards, which went on to realize it as the v-150 Planet Defender. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, Lar being a Gathi was mentioned in a compilation of Imperial documents made by Rebel Alliance historian Major Arhul Hextrophon for Alliance Supreme Commander Mon Mothma and other Rebel officers.
