Nayli
2588

A small city on the planet Chandrila, Nayli was known as being one of the three main spaceports on-planet. Nayli was located in Chandrila's equatorial belt on the planet's eastern continent. The city was surrounded by countryside and supported a large hovertrain station. The buildings were mainly conical.Star Wars: Rogue Squadron One year after the Battle of Yavin, the city was the site of Moff Kohl Seerdon's battle with Rogue Squadron, after Seerdon imposed a blockade upon Chandrila. Seerdon's forces attacked the city, but the Rogues brought supplies and repulsed Seerdon's TIEs and All Terrain Scout Transports, allowing citizens to be evacuated on shuttles.
