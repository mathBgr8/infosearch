The station, along with Henlon station was the location of an incident between the Rebel Alliance and the Hurrim. A civilian convoy from a company called Toburik Haulers was attacked by both the Rebels and the Hurrim. The objective was to capture the cargo and leave the ships intact. However, as soon as the ships lowered their shields, the Hurrim showed their true colors and began firing upon the civilians. The Rebels managed to drive the Hurrim away and capture the cargo, but they were worried about the outcome of this incident.
