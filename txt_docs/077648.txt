2-1C medical droid
2185

The 2-1C medical droid was a combat medic droid employed by the Galactic Empire. It was extremely similar in design to the FX-series medical assistant droid, but was designed for independent operation on the battlefield. It had a working vocabulator and a cruel, cynical attitude, a stark contrast to the eager, friendly IM-6 units used by the previous Grand Army of the Republic. 2-1C was also a non-playable character in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace video game. It was voiced by Harvey Jason. It is unknown whether this model of 2-1C matched the one in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds. Like all the medical droids used by the Empire in Galactic Battlegrounds, the 2-1C have a sadistic love of torturous surgery.
