Research Station Shantipole
1882

Research Station Shantipole was an asteroid in the Roche system. It was primarily staffed by Verpine. After the Battle of Yavin, the asteroid was the site of the Shantipole Project, in which Slayn & Korpil worked with Commander Gial Ackbar to develop the A/SF-01 B-wing starfighter.Strike Force: Shantipole When the Empire became aware of this, Governor Bane Nothos blockaded the system.
