Toxus Li
1115

Toxus Li was a male pirate who was hunted down by Boba Fett during the Galactic Civil War. Although many of Li's crew members fought in his defense and were killed by Fett, Li's bounty was the only one Fett was concerned about. When confronted by Fett in person, Li surrendered himself.
