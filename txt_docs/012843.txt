The Plooriods contained many agricultural worlds that supplied food for the Galactic Empire. It also included the planet Corsin, where swoop races took place. The Greater Plooriod Cluster was ruled by Overlord Ghorin, who shortly after the Battle of Yavin, agreed to supply the Rebel Alliance with grain. The grain had been tainted. Following the disaster at Deepspace Besh, the Rebel Alliance stepped up raids and military action in an effort to keep the Imperial Navy off balance. As part of this strategy, the Rebel Alliance launched several raids in the Plooriods, including one in the Corsin system.
