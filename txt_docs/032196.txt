Drone ore shuttle
1388

Drone ore shuttles were used in the Koros system around 5000 BBY. They had no firepower, but had strong armor. Also they were equipped with repulsor-chute. Jori Daragon, while a prisoner on Ronika, stole one in order to find Empress Teta and warn her about the impending Sith invasion. *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 2 Category:Cargo shuttle classes
