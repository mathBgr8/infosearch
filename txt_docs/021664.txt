Hodrix
1206

Hodrix was a male Dug who was a swoop racer and a member of Team NaKuda. As a newcomer to the sport, racing on Caprioril, he quickly upstaged the former favorite, Serji-X Arrogantus. He perished in a massive explosion which killed nearly 200,000 swoop race spectators by an assassin droid. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dugs Category:Males Category:Swoop racers
