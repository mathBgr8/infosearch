Ubote group
1341

Ubote group was a group of three Rebel-operated Corellian CR90 corvettes during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, the corvettes were ordered to destroy Outpost D-34 in orbit around the planet of Javin, which was located within Javin sector.
