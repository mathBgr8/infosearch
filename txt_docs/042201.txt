Sith officer
2594

Sith officers were former Republic officers that joined the Sith Empire during the Jedi Civil War when Darth Revan and Darth Malak turned on the Galactic Republic after the Battle of Malachor V. Sith Officer uniforms may vary in color from light to dark gray, dark gray-green, or black. Generally, a strap connected from a left shoulder epaulet running cross diagonal to a utility belt. Boots and caps usually matched the color scheme of the torso, but may also vary as well. Sometimes, they also wore on their left breast a plate with various colored lines on it. Many, if not all of these officers served in the Mandalorian Wars under Revan and Malak. As many Sith Officers were the commanders of Republic ships such as the Leviathan and the Ravager during the Mandalorian Wars, they took these ships with them when they turned their backs on the Republic.
