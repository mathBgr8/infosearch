Gilly
1609

Gilly was a Human male who joined the Erased along with his twin brother, Spence, after the Galactic Republic was reorganized into the Galactic Empire by Emperor Palpatine. After the brothers engaged a band of robbers in 18 BBY, Gilly showed his skill with weapons to Solace. When stormtroopers attacked one of the bases, Gilly fought alongside his brother to defend the base, but they had no chance and were both shot down by concentrated fire. *The Last of the Jedi: Underworld *The Last of the Jedi: Death on Naboo *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Resistance fighters
