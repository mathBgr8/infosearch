Gespo Bomu
1294

Gespo Bomu was a Rodian soldier. He was a member of the Bomu Clan and occasionally worked form Zonnos the Lesser of the Anjiliac kajidic. Some accounts suggest that Gespo and his relative, Hreeda Bomu, were hired to avenge the death of two other Bomus, pirates Heedron and Ormann, who had been killed when trying to stop the rescue of Zonnos' brother Mika. Gespo and Hreeda went to Endregaad and ambushed the rescuers, trying to "finish the job".
