Tovric
1220

Tovric was a male Human who was employed as an assassin during the Galactic Civil War, a galaxy-spanning conflict between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance. He was hired to assassinate the Sullustan Ambassador Kollrin at the Ace of Sabres, an upscale gambling establishment located on the world of Kluistar.
