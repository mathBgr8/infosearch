Money Lane
904

"Money Lane" was Han Solo's term for the shared field of fire on the Millennium Falcon, where both her upper and lower quad lasers could engage an enemy. Solo and his co-pilot, Chewbacca, placed bets on who could eliminate more enemy targets in this area. *Han Solo's Revenge *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Millennium Falcon Owner's Workshop Manual Category:Phrases
