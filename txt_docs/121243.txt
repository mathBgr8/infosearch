Pine tree
1966

thumb|right|250px|A pine forest on [[Naboo/Legends|Naboo.]] Pine trees were a type of trees. They could be found on a number of planets like Naboo, Krant,Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Endor,How the Ewoks Saved the Trees: An Old Ewok Legend and many others. Pines had needle-shaped leaves that emitted a sweet scent. The painting Alderaan Refugees, created after 104 ABY,"Celestia Galactica Photografica" was included in Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: Legacy Vol. 4, which describes the stories it contains as taking place more than a century after the events of . The events of that film take place in 4 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology, meaning that all paintings in "Celestia Galactica Photografica," including Hunting Overlook, must have been created after 104 ABY. depicted pine trees on the destroyed planet Alderaan.
