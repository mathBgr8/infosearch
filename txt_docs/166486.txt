Sanitary entry station
362

The sanitary entry station was a method of personal sanitation used by the Yevetha onboard their starships. One would enter an airtight hatch which, once sealed, would fill with yellow gas; afterward everything would be washed down by a hot liquid spray. *Before the Storm Category:Hygiene
