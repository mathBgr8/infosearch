|subscribers=May 10, 2000 |pages=82 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=49 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 49 is the forty-ninth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was released to newsstands the week of May 15, 2000. This issue celebrated the twentieth anniversary of the original trilogy film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back.
