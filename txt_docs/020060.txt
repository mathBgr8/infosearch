Galgamok
2142

|publisher= |release date= |media type=Song |pages= |published in= |genre= }} "Galgamok" was a song performed by Evar Orbus and His Galactic Jizz-Wailers, a band formed during the final years of the Clone Wars of 22 BBY&ndash;19 BBY.The New Essential Chronology At some point before Evar Orbus was killed in 4 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale," including the death of Evar Orbus, to 4 ABY. his band made three recordings and a name for themselves with crowd-pleasers such as "Galgamok," which was sung by one of the members, Joh Yowza, who individually quickly became a celebrity. "Galgamok" was introduced in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003.
