L19a freighter
1334

The L19a was a freighter produced by Surron StarTech. It was the initial production run of the Surronian L19 heavy freighter and was much more luxurious than the standard L19. It had a limited production run as was highly desirable. This meant prices for a genuine L19a could be four times the amount of a L19. Moff Aricos Dreleene owned a customized L19, named the First Edition. He had modified this ship to resemble the more expensive L19a. *Stock Ships Category:Freighter classes Category:Surron StarTech products
