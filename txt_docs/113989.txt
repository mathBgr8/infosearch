Trade Federation shuttle
1705

In 44 BBY, then-Senator Nute Gunray used a shuttle of a different class than the Sheathipede-class transport shuttle. The shuttle was capable of transporting numerous HKB-3 hunter-killer droids. It had a similar design to the contemporary Trade Federation bacta ship. thumb|left|150px|The destruction of Gunray's shuttle. Gunray traveled to Troiken aboard this shuttle. The shuttle was destroyed by the forces of the treacherous Iaco Stark along with many onboard droids, but Gunray was not on the shuttle at the time. *Star Wars (1998) 37 Category:Shuttles
