Yoggoy (Hive)
883

The Yoggoy hive was a Killik hive on the planet Yoggoy. In 27 ABY, the Tachyon Flier crash-landed on Yoggoy, with Raynar Thul, Lomi Plo, and Welk onboard. The Yoggoy then absorbed Raynar and became the first hive of the Colony. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Fate of the Jedi: Apocalypse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Killik hives
