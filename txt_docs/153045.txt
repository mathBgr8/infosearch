Hadis Ato
1912

Hadis Alto was a male Human and one of the first cadets to pass through the Corulag Imperial Academy. A level-headed, unremarkable Colonel, Alto served several tours of duty in the Outer Rim Territories, honing his skills as a sniper and VIP bodyguard. In 2 ABY, he returned to Corulag and served as an undercover agent assigned with tailing, monitoring, and discreetly protecting valuable scientists, technicians, and engineers.
