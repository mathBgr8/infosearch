Supreme Commander of the Corellian Defense Force
2077

Supreme Commander of the Corellian Defense Force was the highest ranking officer of the Corellian defense fleet. During the Second Galactic Civil War between the Five Worlds and the Galactic Alliance, Wedge Antilles was appointed to this position after the death of Minister of War Thrackan Sal-Solo. After Antilles resigned, the post was given to Genna Delpin by Five World Prime Minister Dur Gejjen.
