Thruncan was a city on the planet Vodran.Lords of Nal Hutta The Thruncan Insurrection took place there when the native Vodran rebelled against Imperial occupation. Prior to the Insurrection, the city had a population in the millions. Afterward, only a few thousand remained. *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Cities on Vodran Category:Vodran culture
