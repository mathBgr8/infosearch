Redeye (starship)
1447

Redeye was an Airam starship. During the operations of Task Force Vengeance it was misfortunate enough to cross paths with Star Destroyer Rage and Interdictor Cruiser Compellor near the Blair Cluster where the two Imperial vessels were hunting illegal activity.
