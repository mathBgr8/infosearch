Jon would serve under Admiral Firmus Piett during the Battle of Endor. Davod Jon was a Human who served as a colonel in the Galactic Empire. As an officer of the Imperial Army, Jon was promoted for his skills and bravery as a scout. He served as a leader in the Empire's Special Forces unit. Shortly before the Battle of Endor in 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi to the beginning of 4 ABY. Jon was among the many Imperial personnel present when the Dark Lord Darth Vader arrived aboard the second Death Star to coerce the battle station's commander, Moff Tiaan Jerjerrod, to quicken the superweapon's construction, to which Jerjerrod responded that his construction crews would double their efforts. Afterward, Jon stood in one of the Death Star's hangar bays alongside Vader and other officers to await the arrival of Emperor Palpatine aboard the battlestation. Jon went on to serve under Admiral Firmus Piett, the commanding officer of the Star Dreadnought Executor and Death Squadron, Vader's personal fleet, during the Battle of Endor. Davod Jon was known as a proficient leader. Despite the Empire's obvious technological superiority over opponents, Jon advocated the development of wilderness survival training for Imperial troops and convinced his superiors that wilderness survival and recon training were also necessary for military dominance. Jon had brown hair that was graying with age and light skin.
