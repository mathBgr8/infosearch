Golm
3670

Golm, also known as Bothawui Prime, was a planet in the Both system. A purple gas giant, it was the fourth planet in the star system and was orbited by 23 moons, as well as an asteroid belt.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Like it's "twin", Ganash, the planet was named after a hero from Bothan mythology, Golm Fervse'dra. In 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion a battle was fought between the Galactic Republic and Confederacy of Independent Systems in orbit of the planet. A Republic naval task force regrouped in Bothawui Prime's orbit after several vicious confrontations with a Separatist fleet led by General Grievous. They were the only line of defense between him and Bothawui itself. As Grievous's frigates swooped in to attack the Republic cruisers, groups of AT-TE walkers hidden on the asteroids of Bothawui Prime's rings, opened fire on the vessels' unguarded sterns and turned the tide of battle, helping to stop his assault on Bothawui.
