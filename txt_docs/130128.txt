Titania
1828

Titania was a Imperial I-class Star Destroyer which served under Moff Kentor Sarne in the Battle of Kathol System. During the battle, the Titania suffered massive damage in an all-out assault by the Bothan commander, Kursk Mal'ia. Despite this damage, however, the guns aboard the Titania continued to fire, even as other ship systems failed.
