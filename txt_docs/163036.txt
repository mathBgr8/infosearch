The following events took place in 207 BBY. *Reidi Artom departs the Cularin system to explore the Unknown Regions. The Living Force Campaign Guide mentions that Artom left for the Unknown Regions 175 years before the Invasion of Naboo, which is dated to 32 BBY by The New Essential Chronology.
