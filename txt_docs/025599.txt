It was a part of the Expansion Region's Lostar sector, being located both in the Outer Expansion Zone sub-region and the Northern Dependencies portion of that region. Draria lay between the Adin and Viga systems on the super-hyperroute known as the Hydian Way, specifically the segment that was known as the Corsin Run before being incorporated into the larger hyperspace route by approximately 3693 BBY. In a Rebel Alliance Task Force on Alliance Security SecuriDex datafile on the bounty hunter group known as the Granse Confederacy that was submitted at some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Draria was listed as the last known location for five of the group's members: the Humans Callandri and Zayl Braith, the Tunroth Kiran Tatch, the Ubese Arcuse, and the Gand Lu'daal-ud. Between 12 ABY and 13 ABY, Draria was situated within the borders of the New Republic. Draria was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
