Survival school
1475

Survival schools were institutions found on several worlds across the galaxy, helping youngsters learn survival traits for later life. Luke Skywalker attended a survival school during his youth on Tatooine, where he was taught to view dangerous situations as objectively as possible. He attempted to use this thinking during a confrontation with the Tusken Raider URoRRoR'R'R, though he found he was unable to bring these teachings to bear, and instead fainted in the face of danger. One of the more notable of these organizations was the Galactic Outdoor Survival School founded by Barosa Warren on the planet OM813.
