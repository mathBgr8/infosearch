Jatayus Outbound
1122

Jatayus Outbound was a shipping company with local offices on Novor 23. It was known publicly for serving the length of the Sisar Run, but was merely a front for the illicit business operations of Sprax, a Black Sun vigo. Jatayus specialized in bulk and medium interstellar transport in the Outer Rim. It owned one super transport in 3 ABY, the freighter Warren. *Secrets of the Sisar Run *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Front companies Category:Transport companies
