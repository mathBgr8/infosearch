Unidentified Human Revenant commando
683

This Unnamed Human Revenant was part of the commando team lead by Nym to disable some shield bunkers protecting the Trihexalon factory on Nod Kartha. When the commando team was under heavy fire at the last shield bunker, Jedi Master Adi Gallia made a hole in the bunker and provided cover for the team to escape just as the timed charges blew. She wielded twin blaster pistols. This character has not been officially named. *Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Pirates
