Tilark
1035

Tilark was a Xiytiar-class heavy transport belonging to the Imperials. Tilark was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter's vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor to the Corrupter in the Mev system. Tilark's cargo was 'Probe Droids'.
