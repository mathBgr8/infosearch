It was situated on the Sisar Run hyperlane. Risiev Credal was a Human born around 28 BBYAlliance Intelligence Reports, which establishes Risiev Credal to be approximately thirty standard years old by the time of its setting, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. who hailed from Jofoger and had the appearance common among the population of Humans that had long lived in the area of the Sisar Run. Credal eventually joined the Rebel Alliance and traveled offworld. Jofoger was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
