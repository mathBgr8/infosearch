Ganeed
1008

Ganeed was the sister of the King Meenon of Senali. She was married to Garth and had five children; Hinen, Jaret, Drenna, Tinta and Bu. She welcomed Qui-Gon Jinn and Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi when they visited Senali. *Jedi Apprentice: The Shattered Peace *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Senali
