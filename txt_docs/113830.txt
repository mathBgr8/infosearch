Senior Administrative Aide
2777

Senior Administrative Aide, also known as administrator, was a title held by the Umbaran Sly Moore, the Chief of Staff to Supreme Chancellor of the Galactic Republic Sheev Palpatine. Moore continued to use the title when she served the Republic's successor, the Galactic Empire.
