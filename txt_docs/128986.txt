|death=April 3, 2016Berkeley, California, USA |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=*Radio dramatist *Voice actor |companies= |website= }} }} Erik Bauersfeld (June 28, 1922 &ndash; April 3, 2016) was the voice of Admiral Ackbar and Bib Fortuna in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. It only took one hour for Bauersfeld to record Ackbar's lines and thirty minutes for Fortuna's. He also read for the part of Yoda, but lost the role to Frank Oz. He later went on to reprise his role as Ackbar in the computer game Star Wars: X-Wing and Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens. Born in Brooklyn, New York, Bauersfeld knew at 3 years old that he was destined to work in radio when his father showed him a small crystal radio receiver. He would eventually lead the drama and literature department of Berkeley-based radio station KPFA for 31 years. {| ! Year !! Title !! Contribution(s) !!
