Sakhisakh
1759

Sakhisakh was a Noghri bodyguard of Leia Organa Solo. He was one of Leia's highest bodyguards. Sakhisakh came from the clan Tlakh'sar. When Han Solo decided to head to Bastion to retrieve the Caamas Document, Sakhisakh recommended to Han to take him, something that was refused. He joined Leia to meet with Admiral Gilad Pellaeon onboard the Chimaera where he viciously refused the idea of peace with the Imperial Remnant. *Specter of the Past *Vision of the Future Category:Leia Organa Solo's bodyguards Category:Males Category:Noghri
