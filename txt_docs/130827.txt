|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Vexen was a city on the mining colony Kallistas, during the time of the Galactic Empire. It was the center of the extraction of radioactive elements, with more than one hundred workers working on it, although the harshness of the place made most of them leave. Although nominally under Imperial control, the people were unhappy with Prefect Dengless Rinn and admired local pirate Jondrell Inx. Category:Cities Category:Kallistas locations
