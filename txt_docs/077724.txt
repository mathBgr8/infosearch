It was located in the Joralla system, a part of the Mektrun sector in the Western Reaches portion of the Mid Rim. Jaska was orbited by three planets: the hot, rocky Manalin; the temperate Grall; and the terrestrial Joralla. The inner two worlds had poisonous atmospheres. Joralla, considered to be the primary planet of its star system, had breathable air and circled its star once every 310 local days. It was a tropical jungle world that Jaska bathed in crimson light.
