She was the daughter of Mika, a bounty hunter who worked during the Clone Wars and hated Jedi. She was tattooed on her face and abdomen; the lines around her navel were similar to those of a Zealot of Psusan. Years later, a grown up Breela, who along with her fellow hunters Boushh and Zuckuss, were tracking down Jedi Master Kai Justiss, a survivor who escaped Order 66 years ago and had recently sought refuge on the planet Garqi, but was betrayed by local farmers to Breela and the others. After a skirmish, during which Master Justiss was able to defeat at least two other combatants and severed the arm of one of them, Breela, Boushh and Zuckuss surrounded him and held him at blaster point. Breela was a female Farghul from the planet Farrfin who rose to prominence as the leader of Crimson Nova, a faction operating within the Bounty Hunters' Guild during the time of the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance. She was the daughter of the bounty hunter Mika, who had previously commanded the Crimson Nova during the Clone Wars, a galaxy-wide war that lasted for a few years between the Republic and the Separatist Alliance.Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones During that conflict, Mika increasingly devoted the group's efforts to hunting down Jedi, a focus that ultimately led to her capture by a Jedi Order task force led by Jedi Master Mace Windu. Following her arrest, Crimson Nova was dismantled, and its members dispersed. With the end of the Clone Wars and the rise of the Galactic Empire in 19 BBY,Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith Mika's imprisonment came to an end, allowing her to reunite with her daughter and re-establish contact with the bounty hunting underworld. After her release, Mika managed to restore the Crimson Nova and continued to prioritize the pursuit of surviving Jedi who had managed to evade both Order 66 and the Jedi Purge throughout the Imperial Era. During this period, Breela would adopt her mother's hostility toward the Jedi and was gradually introduced into leadership roles within the organization. Once she was considered old enough, Breela succeeded her mother as leader of Crimson Nova and directed its bounty hunters in a campaign targeting Jedi fugitives. Among the hunters with whom she collaborated were Boushh and Zuckuss. Together, they pursued the Jedi Kai Justiss after local farmers on Garqi revealed his location. The encounter escalated into a confrontation between Justiss and the bounty hunters, resulting in several members of Breela’s group being seriously wounded. Breela was a disciplined and determined individual whose outlook had been strongly shaped by her upbringing within the bounty hunting underworld. Raised under the influence of her mother, Mika, she inherited a deep-seated hostility toward the Jedi, whom she viewed as responsible for both her family’s misfortunes and the dismantling of Crimson Nova during the Clone Wars. This resentment hardened over time into a defining motivation, guiding her decisions and priorities as she rose through the ranks of the organization. Breela was pragmatic in her approach, valuing results over ideology and focusing on the successful completion of contracts rather than personal glory. As a leader, Breela was methodical and authoritative, preferring calculated operations over reckless aggression. She demonstrated a willingness to work alongside other bounty hunters when it served her objectives, indicating a practical understanding of alliances within the Guild. While she could be relentless in pursuit of her targets—particularly Jedi—she was not careless with the lives of those under her command and treated serious injuries to her companions as operational setbacks rather than acceptable losses. Overall, Breela was defined by her resolve, her inherited animosity toward the Jedi, and her commitment to maintaining Crimson Nova as a capable and respected force within the bounty hunting community.
