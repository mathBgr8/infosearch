Syn's Shadow
1558

Syn's Shadow was a starfighter-sized black space yacht that belonged to the ruthless bounty hunter known as Andov Syn. It was armed with two laser cannons in a turret. Syn kept it equipped with at least eight transponder codes and its armor was interchangeable. The Syn's Shadow appears in the article Hunting the Hunters in Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 6.
