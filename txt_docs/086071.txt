The Barrows (Trulalis)
1036

The Barrows was the name of a large graveyard on the planet Trulalis. The graveyard was on the outskirts of the Kovit settlement and contained the victims of a massacre by Imperial troops. After returning to Trulalis to find the Kovit settlement destroyed, Adalric Cessius Brandl created The Barrows to honor his former friends and family who had died because of him. The Imperial troops that had massacred the town had been searching for him. The Barrows also contained an elaborate grave for Adalric's wife who died defending their son, Jaalib Brandl from stormtroopers. Category:Trulalis locations
