Esfandia Long-Range Communications Base
1653

The Esfandia Long-Range Communications Base was a mobile base on the planet Esfandia. The base was built on existing AT-AT technology, allowing it to move slowly but steadily around the planet, maintaining and repairing the limited communications relays between the Unknown Regions and the known galaxy. The base was built with narrow but tall corridors and contained an extravehicular bay with five speeder bikes and enviro-suits for additional outside repairs. During the Battle of Esfandia, thirteen of the original forty communications relays on the planet were destroyed as a result of Yuuzhan Vong bombardment, cutting close to Commander Ashpidar's fifty-percent safety margin. The crew of the Millennium Falcon stumbled upon the base and helped the crew escape destruction at the hands of the Yuuzhan Vong and the traitor in their midst.
