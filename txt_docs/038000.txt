Two series of three books each, the Star Wars Journal delve into the lives of some of the major Star Wars characters. They are told from the first-person point of view. *Star Wars Journal: Captive to Evil: *Star Wars Journal: Hero for Hire *Star Wars Journal: The Fight for Justice *Star Wars Journal: Anakin Skywalker *Star Wars Journal: Queen Amidala *Star Wars Journal: Darth Maul Category:Legends young-reader series
