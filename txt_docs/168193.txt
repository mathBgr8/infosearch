Arca Company Barracks
1393

Arca Company Barracks was the barracks belonging to Arca Company, at which Omega Squad and many other clone troopers stayed while on Coruscant. The six Null-class ARC troopers took it over on the return from Geonosis, refusing to be put into stasis tubes as no one could command them during the battle.
