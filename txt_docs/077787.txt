Holosphere

thumb|right|150px|[[Leia Organa Solo/Legends|Leia Organa holding her holosphere with an image of Han Solo.]] A holosphere was an item that could display three dimensional recorded images. It was a hand-held device that was activated by the heat given by a person touching the sphere. The image would persist after the user ceased touching the holosphere, but would fade after a short time. *Star Wars (1977) 61 Category:Holographic technology
