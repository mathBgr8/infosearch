|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=British |occupation=*Actor *Writer |companies= |website= }} Oliver Ford Davies (born August 12, 1939) played Governor Sio Bibble in The Phantom Menace, Attack of the Clones, and Revenge of the Sith. He appeared alongside Ewan McGregor in an episode of the crime drama Kavanagh QC in 1995. Oliver Ford Davies also worked with George Lucas on Lucas's 1992 television series The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles, in which he was credited as "Ship's Captain" in the pilot movie Young Indiana Jones and the Curse of the Jackal.
