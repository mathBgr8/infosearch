Parallel to the Galactic War, the unsolved murder of a popular governor made the ethnic factions of Tandankin accuse one another. The planet was considered to be at the brink of descending into chaos due to the dispute. thumb|left|250px|The landscape of Tandankin After the Battle of Endor, Grand Moff Nivers occupied the planet, but Tandankin was liberated by Wedge Antilles, Luke Skywalker, and Rogue Squadron. Wedge destroyed the Imperial TIE/LN starfighters before they could even launch, by shooting down a tall tower that crushed the TIE fighters. The tower was the most cherished monument of Tandankin, and the populace was outraged, berating Wedge. Luke chastised them for their behavior, and they later apologized to Wedge and thanked him.
