Quatrain Pudundruh
1175

Quatrain Pudundruh was a Twi'lek businessman from Ord Mantell. He managed the dancing duo of Amaiza and Jodelle Foxtrain for a year before selling them into slavery to recover lost assets. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Businesspeople Category:Males Category:Twi'leks
