|conc=Third Great SchismThe New Essential Chronology |next=Freedon Nadd UprisingTales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising 1 |image=File:BeastWarsBegin.jpg |name=Beast Wars |begin=4350 BBY |end=4000 BBY |date= |place=OnderonTales of the Jedi 1 |result=Beast Rider victoryTales of the Jedi 2 |battles=Battle of Iziz |side1=*Beast Riders *Jedi Order |side2=Naddists |side3= |side4= |commanders1=*Modon Kira *Oron Kira *Gobee *Arca Jeth |commanders2=*Freedon Nadd *Ommin *Amanoa *Novar |commanders3= |commanders4= }} The Beast Wars (also called the Beast Wars of Onderon) was a localized conflict lasting approximately 400 years following the arrival of Freedon Nadd on Onderon. The conflict ultimately helped to touch off the Krath Holy Crusade, part of the Great Sith War. The Beast Wars were the product of the unusual relationship between Onderon and its moon Dxun: the moon orbited in such a way that once a year the atmospheres of both bodies would connect. Over the centuries, the Drexls, flying predators native to Dxun, learned that they could use this "oxygen bridge" to migrate to Onderon and settle there. In response to the vicious nature of the Drexls, the Humans of Onderon built a large, walled city that would come to be known as Iziz to protect themselves. Around this time, a Dark Jedi by the name of Freedon Nadd arrived on Onderon, becoming king and creating a long line of darkness within the Onderonian royalty. It was soon decreed that all criminals in Iziz would be sent out into the wildlands of Onderon, to be killed by the Dxun beasts&mdash;though, due to the strong dark-side nature of the royal house at the time, half of these "criminals" were simply those who disagreed with the monarchy. However, in time, many of these exiles learned to tame the beasts and became the Beast Riders. They used them to attack Iziz, thus beginning the Beast Wars of Onderon. In 4000 BBY, the conflict reached new heights. The Force ghost of Freedon Nadd warned the ruler of Onderon, Queen Amanoa, that a war with the Beast Riders would lead to her destruction, so she appealed for Jedi aid to end the Beast Wars.Tales of the Jedi Companion Jedi Master Arca Jeth was assigned to the Onderon system as its watchman. He sent his Padawans, Tott Doneeta and Ulic and Cay Qel-Droma, to settle the problem on Onderon. As they arrived in Queen Amanoa's throne room, a group of Beast Riders staged a daring raid on the city and kidnapped Amanoa's daughter, the Princess Galia. The Jedi ventured into the wildlands to save the Princess, and Tott was able to tame a boma, which led them to the fortress of Beast-Lord Modon Kira. The Jedi entered the building, only to discover an ongoing wedding-feast between Galia and Modon's son, Oron Kira. thumb|left|250px|Arca Jeth upon arriving on Onderon to mediate the Beast Wars Ulic attempted to rescue the Princess, but she claimed that she, in fact, wanted to be married. Modon set up a peaceful banquet to discuss things, where the Kiras revealed to the Jedi that the Onderonian royal family was influenced by the dark side of the Force and the spirit of Freedon Nadd. Galia, Oron, and the Jedi headed back to Iziz to speak with Queen Amanoa, having agreed with Modon that he would launch an attack should they fail. When they arrived, Amanoa unleashed her dark side powers upon them, forcing them to retreat into a secret passageway. Oron informed his father on the comm that they had failed. And so, Modon's massive gathering of Beast Lords from all over Onderon marched towards Iziz, with the Jedi battling Amanoa's royal guard in the throne room&mdash;one of whom used a halberd to cut off Cay's arm, which was later replaced by an arm from an XT-6 droid. Amanoa attempted to turn the tide of the battle using the dark side, but was thwarted by the newly-arrived Arca Jeth's Jedi battle meditation, allowing the beast-riders to take advantage, and soon Iziz had fallen. The Jedi, Oron, and Galia descended into the deepest levels of Amanoa's palace, to the tomb of Freedon Nadd. Amanoa was killed instantly by the sheer light side of the Force emanating from Jeth's body, ending the dark side's half-millennium hold of Iziz. Galia and Oron Kira were married, uniting the beast riders and Iziz. The conflict ended late in the year 4000 BBY. In the hopes of ending the dark side influence over Onderon, Arca Jeth had the coffins of Freedon Nadd and Amanoa removed from Iziz and re-interred on Dxun. However, though his body had been ravaged by his use of the dark side of the Force, King Ommin was still strong in The Force, and fomented an uprising among the Naddists, those Onderonians who still followed Nadd's teachings. Though the revolt was eventually defeated, Ommin was able to pass on much of his dark side knowledge to two Empress Tetan nobles, Satal and Aleema Keto, who had traveled to Onderon in search of Sith artifacts.Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising 2 Following Ommin's defeat, the spirit of Freedon Nadd appeared to the Ketos and introduced them to the dark side of the Force.
