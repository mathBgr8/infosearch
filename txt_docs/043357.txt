|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Artist |companies= |website= }} }} Dan Parsons is an American comic book inker. Born in Rochester, New York on January 10, 1966, he grew up with dreams of being an artist. Parsons became involved with Star Wars comics at the 2002 San Diego Comic-Con when he met penciler Jan Duursema, who was looking for a new inker.
