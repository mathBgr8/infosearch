Jesse Harlin
1902

Jesse Harlin (b. August 11, 1976, Pine Grove Mills, PA) was the Staff Composer for LucasArts, a Lucasfilm company. Most known for composing the soundtrack to Star Wars: Republic Commando, Harlin has been scoring games in the video-game industry since 1999. From 2003 until its closure in 2013, he worked for LucasArts and worked on games such as Gladius, Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith video game, Star Wars: Battlefront II and Star Wars: The Force Unleashed, as well as the cancelled Star Wars: 1313.
