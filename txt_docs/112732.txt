Message drone
1163

thumb|right|250px|An Elegance message drone Message drones were small automated droid ships used to relay confidential messages across interstellar distances in the days prior to the invention of the HoloNet. The Cha'a message pod and the Elegance message drone were two known types with the latter being used in the days of the Old Republic, and to some extent, the Galactic Empire. *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 1 *Darth Bane: Path of Destruction *Darth Bane: Rule of Two *The Truce at Bakura *Ambush at Corellia *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids *The Essential Guide to Droids *
