R'ube was present in the cantina around 1 ABY, when the Imperial curfew went into effect. The Y'bith left the establishment during Ackmena's final performance of Goodnight, But Not Goodbye, together with a drunken Caldera Righim. This alien's face was made from a modified Bith mask.
