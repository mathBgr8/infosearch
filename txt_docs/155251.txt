Ta (MC40a light cruiser)
1960

Ta was a MC40a light cruiser belonging to the Rebel Alliance. Ta along with Re were to go against the Strike-class medium cruisers Eel and Asp. Both ships had X-wings from Rogue Squadron and B wings from Green Squadron.
