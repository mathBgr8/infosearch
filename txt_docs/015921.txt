The Kajiin Swamp can be found there. Spinda Caveel chose that planet for his hideout, both because of its isolated astrographic location, and the dangerous natural environment. His fortress/laboratory stood out in the jungle, but inside a powerful Full-globe class-one Imperial shield. The Pike Sisters, under Caveel's employ, came to the planet to deliver him A-OIC, a medical droid they had stolen.
