|type=Hardcover |pages=224 |dimensions=12.2 &times; 9.4 &times; 1 inches |weight=3 pounds |isbn=0345431251 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Art of Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones includes more than 500 illustrations including sketches, costume designs, set pieces, models, and brilliant full-color paintings; an in-depth look at the new creatures introduced in Episode II; behind-the-scenes accounts and anecdotes related by the artists themselves; visuals of exotic planets, spacecraft, and characters such as Jango Fett, Count Dooku, and Jedi Luminara Unduli; and poster art created especially for Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. It also features the illustrated screenplay. *Alien concepts that appear on page 15 and 38 can be spotted in the background on Coruscant in "Chapter 21" of Star Wars: Clone Wars. {|class="wikitable sortable" !
