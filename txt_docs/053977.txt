Medx homunculi
966

The Medx homunculi was an unsettling little creature, looking uncannily like a bony Human scaled down to thirty centimeters in height. They inhabited the wispy treetops of Gravlex Med and&mdash;while not sentient&mdash;were quite clever for animals. The striking resemblance between these chittering creatures and the species that ruled the Galactic Republic was a private joke among the Anx.
