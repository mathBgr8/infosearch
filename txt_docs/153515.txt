Yavin's Glory

Yavin's Glory was a New Republic patrol cruiser. Its crew once happened upon an abandoned Imperial vessel which had been attacked by the Red Moons group. The Red Moons had used the chemical DX-343 to force the Imperials to abandon ship. Category:Cruisers Category:Starships of the New Republic
