thumb|250px|Ashley Wood's cover art for [[Star Wars Tales 16|Star Wars Tales 16]] Ashley Wood (born 1971) is an artist who has contributed to several Star Wars projects. He uses a blend of painting, digital photography, and graphic software, which gives his artwork a unique feel. Ashley was born in Australia in 1971. He has worked with many major companies including LucasArts, Lucasfilm, DC Comics, Wizards of the Coast, Paizo, Marvel Comics, Activision, Dreamworks SKG, Warner Bros., Hasbro, Random House, McFarlane Entertainment, IDW, and Konami.
