Iritsa
1061

Iritsa was the capital city of the planet Chazwa. Iritsa was composed of a warehouse district, a spaceport, and two markets (north and south, respectively). Situated between the two markets was Gold Plaza, while a living compound, known as the Stones, was located due south of them. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital cities Category:Chazwa locations
