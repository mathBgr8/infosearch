Trade Federation Battleship (aquatic)
1652

thumb|right|250px|A Trade Federation battleship on [[Maramere]] The Trade Federation Battleship, not to be confused with the Trade Federation's space-faring Lucrehulk-class battleship, was a large ship built for the Trade Federation's aquatic naval forces. The battleship was deployed as a starfighter transport, capable of launching waves of Scarab-class droid starfighters, Vulture droid starfighters, and Trade Federation droid bombers. These vessels carried droid control signals with a limited range, meaning that any Vulture droids caught out of its radius were instantly destroyed. thumb|left|250px|A battleship in the [[Jenuwaa Sea on Kalee]] Count Dooku used a battleship to cross the Jenuwaa Sea on Kalee to retrieve Qymaen jai Sheelal following his shuttle crash.
