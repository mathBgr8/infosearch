Armored spacesuit
1506

thumb|An illustration of an armored spacesuit. Though bulky, the armored spacesuit could support a wearer within the vacuum of space or another type of hostile environment for up to ten days at a time. The armored spacesuit cost ten thousand credits and weighed forty-five kilograms.
