Celebration (1999)
2115

Star Wars Celebration was the first Celebration. Intended to celebrate the history of Star Wars and promote the then-upcoming , the event was sponsored by Lucasfilm and organized by Dan Madsen, president of the official Star Wars Fan Club, and held in Denver, Colorado&mdash;where the fan club was based at the time&mdash;at the Wings Over the Rockies Air and Space Museum. It was held over three days from April 30 through May 2, 1999. Category:Official Star Wars conventions
