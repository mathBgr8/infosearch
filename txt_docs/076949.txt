Iceworm
2205

The iceworm, also spelled ice worm, was a burrowing species of annelid native to the ice planet Hoth. The tidal pull of Hoth's three nameless moons caused fissures in the ice-layers of the massive frozen ocean in Hoth's southern hemisphere, sending jets of ocean water into the freezing air. The intense cold froze these jets into spires of ice, suspending primitive ocean algae in these tall columns and glaciers.
