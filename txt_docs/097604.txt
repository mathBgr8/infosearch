Igitz
2277

Igitz were a species of small amphibians hailing from the planet Naboo. Those blue-skinned, omnivorous critters dwelled in the planet's many dense swamps. Because of the environment in which they had evolved, the igitz were well-adapted to both land and water: while their front paws were clawed for treading on land, their hindquarters were finned, allowing it speed when in water. The igitz were a popular pet in most Gungan civilizations. A certain Gungan diplomat of the New Republic owned one.
