Cutter
714

Cutter was a spicer term used to describe a person who prepared spice for use or sale. It was also applied to someone who was close enough to a person that they could be trusted with cutting their spice. The term, often used on Corellia, was also used to describe the lover of a spicer, as in the case between Black Sun criminal Zekka Thyne and Inyri Forge. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *X-Wing: Wedge's Gamble Category:Occupations Category:Slang
