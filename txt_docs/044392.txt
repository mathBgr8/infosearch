|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=224 |isbn=1569716196 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series=Star Wars Tales |issues=4 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Tales Volume 1 is the first trade paperback collection of the Star Wars Tales comic book series. Volume 1 collects Star Wars Tales issues 1-4. It was released on February 20, 2002. Collecting the first four issues of the quarterly anthology smash sensation, Star Wars Tales contains thrilling stories featuring Darth Vader, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Han Solo, Emperor Palpatine, C-3PO, R2-D2, Lando Calrissian and a cast of thousands! Tales explores every corner of the Star Wars galaxy and keeps readers coming back for more!
