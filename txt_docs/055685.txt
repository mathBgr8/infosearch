Judgment Circle
733

The Judgment Circle was a Jedi tradition where if one or more Jedi overstepped their bounds, other Jedi would convene and find a passable solution. Jedi Master Justyn Ma'Ning convened one against fellow Jedi Master Jorus C'baoth, due to his strict actions and placing the Jedi of Outbound Flight above the other crewmembers onboard.Outbound Flight *Outbound Flight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Jedi ceremonies
