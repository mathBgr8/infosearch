It featured corporate complexes, outside of which stretched vast unsettled territories. The Human Clenna grew up on Yalln, spending a lot of time during his youth exploring the wilderness outside the corporate complexes with his trusty tal. At some point, Clenna traveled offworld and served in the Imperial Army of the Galactic Empire, but he eventually returned to Yalln. He grew both restless and tired of his life there, however, and ultimately joined the offworld Galactic Outdoor Survival School as a staff member at some point by 2 ABY.Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Yalln was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
