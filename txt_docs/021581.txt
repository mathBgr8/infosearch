P'w'itthki
686

P'w'itthki was one of the highest deities of the Ssi-ruuvi religion. According to the Ssi-ruuvi holy book, the G'nnoch, P'w'itthki and Ssi hatched from the primordial first egg, while P'w'itthki's offspring hatched from the third egg. Ssi and P'w'itthki eventually fought, with Ssi winning. Ssi allowed P'w'itthki's offspring to live, on the condition that they serve Ssi's offspring for all eternity. Thus came into being the Ssi-ruu (Ssi's offspring) and their slave race, the P'w'ecks (P'w'itthki's offspring). *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook Category:Deities Category:Ssi-ruuvi religion
