Mama (information broker)
2223

Mama was an information broker on Ord Mantell. As a member of the Columi species, Mama possessed a large hairless head, which weighed a fifth of her total body weight and was supported by a tiny bipedal body. She had black unblinking eyes. She was the proprietor of a smuggler's haven. Han Solo contacted her to find out information about Alfreda Goot who had kidnapped Leia Organa; however, Mama was killed shortly afterwards, causing her two sons&mdash;Marvid and Craitheus Qreph&mdash;to spend the next several decades hunting down and "destroying" all possible suspects in her murder.
