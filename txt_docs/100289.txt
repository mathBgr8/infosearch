Exposing the YT-1300 Transport: Secrets of the Falcon
1587

"Exposing the YT-1300 Transport: Secrets of the Falcon" is an article written by Christopher West and appearing in the pages of the gaming magazine Polyhedron 162/Dungeon Magazine 103 with a cover date of October 2003. This extensive article attempts to clear up some of the discrepancies of the Millennium Falcon and provides a history, as well as interior maps by West himself, of the two YT-1300 variations of which the Falcon is an amalgamation. Also included is a full-color, full page interior map of the Falcon itself, as well as a detailed breakdown of the function of each part of all the ships' interiors. *Polyhedron 162 Category:Polyhedron articles
