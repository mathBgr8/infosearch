Bay-Suwe Combine
905

The Bay-Suwe Combine was a large datacard manufacturer at the time of the Galactic Civil War. Secondary product lines included hologram platforms, used to display small decorative holographic images. This company, along with Corellia Digital, is mentioned in the description of most hologram rewards in Star Wars Galaxies. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees *Star Wars Galaxies: Trials of Obi-Wan Category:Technology companies
