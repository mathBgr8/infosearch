Naotebe wingling
1340

Naotebe winglings were invented by Nom Anor and were engineered by Master Shapers to dissolve and eat synthplas and other plastic derivatives. They were fairly large, with white patterns on their wings that resembled eyes. The moths had two distinct stages: one of a worm and one of a moth. During both stages, they could eat synthplast, and after only two weeks of maturation did they metamorphose into the mature moth stage.
