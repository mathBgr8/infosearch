MD-series medical droid
2297

The MD-series medical droid was a line of medical droid, including MD-15C medical droid.Star Wars: The Last Jedi: The Visual Dictionary One MD-series droid, Emdee, served the First Order. The MD-series medical droid first identified in Choose Your Destiny: A Finn & Poe Adventure which was written by Cavan Scott and illustrated by Elsa Charretier. The adventure book was released on October 4, 2019.
