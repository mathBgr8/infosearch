Diet of Imperial Planetary Governors
1256

The Diet of Imperial Planetary Governors was an Imperial organization active by 11 ABY. Its role was to put a positive spin on Imperial edicts to help forestall popular uprisings.Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire In 11 ABY, Diet member Tann Starpyre was placed on the Interim Ruling Council by Carnor Jax to foster an illusion of representative democracy. *Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire Category:Imperial organizations Category:Legislatures
