Battle of Cyphar
1753

The Battle of Cyphar began when the Storm Fleet arrived at Cyphar, attempting to destroy the port. The fleet had been delayed at the Battle of the Llon Nebulae, giving a Republic fleet enough time to arrive. Nearly half of the Storm Fleet was destroyed, and Cyphar remained in Republic hands. *The New Essential Chronology *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Battles of Cyphar Category:Battles of the Clone Wars
