Palace of Gorga the Hutt
1353

The palace of Gorga the Hutt was located on the planet Tatooine, and was similar in form and function to the structure inhabited by his late uncle Jabba the Hutt. It was used primarily as a vacation retreat overseen by Gorga's majordomo, Ding.
