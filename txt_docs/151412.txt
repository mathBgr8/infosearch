He was also the father of Jak and Bern Durasha and the twins Celia and Raine Durasha. Durasha was like many Imperials in the belief that women served no place in the Imperial Army or Navy. As such, when his daughter Celia was attending Raithal Academy, he disallowed her from service. He would later inform her of Raine's death.
