Braze
704

Braze was a shortened term for brown haze. It was a kind of air pollution found in Cloud City, and it was a major byproduct of the Galactic Empire's harvest of metals and tibanna gas from the planet's rethin core. *Zorba the Hutt's Revenge *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Slang Category:Weather
