Skirmish on Saleucami
3383

The Skirmish on Saleucami was a mission to corrupt Saleucami into supporting the Zann Consortium. Urai Fen led his forces into battle, destroying several turrets placed in their path, then enemy infantry and vehicles. After getting past those defenses Urai Fen found Koong Eeth and then severely injured him. The planet was successfully corrupted into serving the Zann Consortium, allowing Tyber Zann to send another defiler to sabotage the shield generator and sending Urai Fen down there again to capture the planet. The player does not have to intimidate Saleucami.
