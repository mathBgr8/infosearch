*The Interior places the Odik system in the area of space Star Wars: The Galactic Explorer's Guide identifies as the Interior. |sector=None |coordinates=K-11 |xyz= |quadrants= |suns= |orbits= |stations= |asteroids= |comets= |nebulae= |routes= |other= |species= |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |population= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} The Odik system was located in the Deep Core and the Interior. It was situated in grid square K-11 of the Standard Galactic Grid. The Odik system was introduced in the current Star Wars canon in "The Deep Core," an article written by Mark Newbold and included in the "Guide to the Galaxy" department of the thirty-sixth issue of the Star Wars: Build the Millennium Falcon magazine that was published around September 9, 2015. It was then identified by name in "Star Systems of the Galaxy," an appendix published with the Star Wars Galaxy Map on StarWars.com on June 24, 2025. Both works were authored by Jason Fry.
