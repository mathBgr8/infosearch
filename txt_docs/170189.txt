HD7 Hyperdrive Activator Unit
1843

The HD7 Hyperdrive Activator Unit was a hyperdrive engine manufactured by Cygnus Spaceworks. The unit was a Class One rated unit, and was used in both the Alpha-class Xg- Star Wing and the Sentinel-class landing craft. The engine was installed on the Sentinel-class landing craft, giving the ship an atmospheric speed of one thousand kilometers an hour, and speed comparable to a BTL Y-wing starfighter in realspace. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *Star Wars: Behind the Magic Category:Cygnus Spaceworks products Category:Hyperdrive units
