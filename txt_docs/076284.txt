He was formerly a merchant while looking for work, although everywhere he went, he turned up empty handed. Rosen served the Alliance to Restore the Republic as a pilot during the Battle of Endor in 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology where he served as an officer. At the battle, the Alliance successfully destroyed the Galactic Empire's second Death Star battle station. Following the battle, Rosen was among the many to dance while celebrating the victory on the Forest Moon of Endor, playing a musical instrument while doing so.Return of the Jedi 4 He also served as a scout.Introductory Adventure Game Rosen wore a helmet that signified a high rank in the Rebel Alliance, offered moderate protection, and had a built-in communication device. He also donned a beige Mon Calamari Crewman uniform.The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, Second Edition Rosen was good-willed even as his search for work was unsuccessful. He had brown-mottled skin and orange eyes. His first name meant "ingenious," while Rosen was a female name translating to "pure."Galactic Campaign Guide Loci Rosen was created for the 1983 original trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. During production he was referred to as "Forest Ackbar." The scenes in which he appeared were filmed between January 20 and 22 of 1982 on Stage 3 in Crescent City, California. Conceptual art that was originally intended to be of Admiral Gial Ackbar was repurposed to become Rosen, or as it was labeled, "Forest Akbar." The art, done by Nilo Rodis-Jamero, was accompanied by instructions and Polaroids on how to wear the character's costume.The Making of Return of the Jedi Rosen was first identified on a card for the 2001 Tatooine Limited expansion for the Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
