Thi Sawrtin
988

Thi Sawrtin was a veteran smuggler when he met newbie Zayl Braith. Sawrtin accepted her as a partner and they began working together in many smuggling runs.Alliance Intelligence Reports At some point before 1 ABY, during a "routine" run through the Noonian sector, Sawrtin and Braith found an Imperial Interdictor cruiser, the Tory's Catcher, and were captured. Sawrtin found that Imperial Captain Gresia Vleen was not a friend of bribes. Vleen shot Sawrtin, impounded the smuggler's ship and imprisoned Braith. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Smugglers
