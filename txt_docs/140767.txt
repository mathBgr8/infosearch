Coronet University
849

The Coronet University was an educational institution in Corellia's capital Coronet City. Dr. Tennet Sionaro was one of the teachers at the Archaeology Department of this university. Coronet University is mentioned in a mission in Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided called "Coronet Archaeology Site Menaced" that was available on Mission Terminals on Corellia. The Darkstryder Campaign mentions the Corellia University, but it is not known whether this is a completely different institution or maybe just another name for the same university.
