Esorp
1682

Esorp was a BFF-1 bulk freighter that transferred mines to the Dellalt system, preparing the Operation Strike Fear. It was destroyed by Keyan Farlander and Hamo Blastwell during the defection of Bixby. The name is an anagram of Prose, going with the other freighter named Orcim.
