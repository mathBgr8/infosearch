Bellek
1531

Bellek was a moisture farmer on Tatooine before and during the Clone Wars. Raala Ponchar was one of his employees. In the first year of the Clone Wars Confederacy battle droids destroyed his homestead and after the attack Tusken Raiders raided the ruins, killing most of the survivors. Bellek lost everything but his life.
