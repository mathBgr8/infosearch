|figures= |cards=180 cards :90 Dark Side :90 Light Side |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Cloud City Limited |followed by=Special Edition Limited }} The Jabba's Palace Limited set was the fifth full expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game, a card game based on the Star Wars universe. It consists of 180 cards (90 Dark Side and 90 Light Side cards) and was introduced in May 1998. This set consists of 80 rare, 50 uncommon, and 50 common cards. This set introduced many of the classic characters from Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi that were involved with the events occurring on Tatooine in the palace of Jabba the Hutt. A complete list of the Jabba's Palace Limited set can be found on the . Because this is an expansion set, it bears an expansion-set symbol, which is located near the top right corner of the card to the right of the lore and below the destiny number. The symbol for this set is Jabba's eye. This set was sold in 9-card expansion packs and 60-pack expansion boxes. Each pack included one rare card, three uncommon cards and five common cards. Starter decks were never produced for this set. The set went out of print when Decipher lost the license to produce the Star Wars Customizable Card Game. There was never an unlimited version produced for this set. This set was the original source for the names of many characters from Episode VI, focused on background characters seen in Jabba's Palace and sail barge, and during the battle at the sarlacc pit. The characters include Bane Malar, Attark, Herat, Tanus Spijek, Laudica, Jess, and Loje Nella. Newly identified skiff guards include Kithaba, Nysad, Vizam, and Vedain. The production name "Wooof" was finally applied here to the Nikto character once mistakenly identified as Klaatu in Kenner's Return of the Jedi action figure collection.
