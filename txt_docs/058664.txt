Battle of the Llon Nebulae
2275

The Battle of the Llon Nebulae was a battle in the Clone Wars. Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi were returning from a mission when they spotted a fleet, which they believed to be the Confederacy's new Storm Fleet, on its way to Cyphar. After receiving data on the ships, the two Jedi took off, trying to stop the CIS fleet. Skywalker led them into an asteroid field and managed to evade the asteroids&mdash;using the Force to perform near-impossible maneuvers.
