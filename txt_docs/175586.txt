Elamm
1527

Elamm was a male Drall wingrider, the traditional Drallish police force and messenger service, and a member of Duchess Sarella's clan. Like most wingriders, he was smaller and shorter than a typical Drall. He had tan fur, which grew longer on his head, where he braided it into elaborate curls with beads. Elamm felt most comfortable above-ground, unlike most Drall, who stayed underground whenever possible. He was also uncommonly quiet for a Drall, rarely attending Ta'sharr sessions. Elamm patrolled the skies mounted on his ibbot named Sha'lott.
