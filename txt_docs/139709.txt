Daplona River
783

The Daplona River was a slow-moving river that passed through the country surrounding the city of Daplona, on Ciutric IV. The Imperial Assault Shuttle used as a decoy by the clone of Ysanne Isard was shot down by Wedge Antilles and plunged into the river, killing the pilot Lorrir during the Battle of Ciutric.
