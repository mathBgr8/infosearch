Blackstar Raiders
600

The Blackstar Raiders were a widely feared and dangerous band of thugs and thieves who plundered dozens of backwater Outer Rim worlds. They were notorious for hitting weak and poorly-defended targets, and became widely targeted when Medara's Outpost hired a group of mercenaries to eliminate the Raiders.
