Trennt Ferinn
944

Trennt Ferinn was a colonel who served in the New Republic Intelligence Service. A member of the Analysis Bureau, Ferinn was attached to the Special Threats Division. Ferinn was responsible for compiling a report on the events surrounding the Corellian Crisis. *Cracken's Threat Dossier *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:New Republic Intelligence personnel
