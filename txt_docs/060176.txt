Model Fac L-113
1776

The Delvin Constructs Model Fac L-113 was a light vehicle factory, modeled after a typical AT-ST manufacturing plant on Balmorra. This model was pre-fabricated, so that it could be set up or taken down relatively quickly to begin manufacturing new vehicles for Imperial forces.
