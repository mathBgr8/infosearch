Xythan force shields were a type of shield newly created by Cestus Cybernetics in 21 BBY. The shields could absorb and return all energy, including the blade of a lightsaber. *The Cestus Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cestus Cybernetics products Category:Shields
