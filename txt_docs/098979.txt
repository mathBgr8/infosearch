Robior Web
2491

Robior Web, a former Guardian on Junction 5, was a male bounty hunter who was active during the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, Web was hired by Count Dooku to assassinate the ruler of Delaluna, Samish Kash, on Null. The bounty hunter Dane, seeing Web running away from the scene of the crime in the Spade Forest, jumped on his swoop and chased after him. Jedi Knights Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker caught up to Dane and took over the chase. Unfortunately, they crashed the swoop into a reclumi spider web and had to return back to Dane and his sister, Floria, at the hunters' inn near the forest. After Skywalker told Dane what happened, Dane remembered who Robior Web was. Web, though he had succeeded in shooting Kash, realized he hadn't killed him. But having missed the opportunity, and with no time to spare, he fled to Dooku's mountain villa landing platform where he met with the Sith Lord. When Kenobi and Skywalker pursued Kash, the Jedi paused to bide their time when they beheld the supremacy of the battle droids that Dooku had brought with him. When the Sith Lord unleashed his droids against both the Jedi and members of the Station 88 Spaceport planetary alliance that had convened at the villa to discuss allegiances, Web, as he took aim at Kash again, was dispatched by Kenobi, who Forcefully hit his chest with both feet, causing the bounty hunter to fly back against a stone wall, where he was impaled by a piece of protruding stone. *Legacy of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bounty hunters Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males
