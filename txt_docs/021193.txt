|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood |issue=6 |next="Hard Currency" }} Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 6 was the sixth and final issue from the Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood series of comics, and was released on April 14, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. As the Baron's forces lay waste to the Interim Council's guarded planet, Sinn and Kanos hurtle toward the heat of the battle in an attempt to thwart the escape of Feena D'Asta's clone. But will they arrive in time? And what happened to the treacherous Grappa the Hutt? Has he escaped unscathed from all the trouble that he raised?
