Pocket Patrol Boat
2181

The Pocket Patrol Boat (PPB) was a Corellian-built starfighter design used by the Corellian Defense Force. It was armed with one turret mounted Heavy laser cannon but the power drain caused by firing the weapon led to a slow rate of fire. The PPB was a rather fast craft, having engaged and damaged the Millennium Falcon.
