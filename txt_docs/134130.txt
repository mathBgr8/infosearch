Combat Arena
1206

thumb|right|250px|The proposed combat arena The combat arena was a proposed addition for the Death Star. The site was loved by Stormtrooper legions for its brutality. The structure contained 100 posts for executions, and 12 enclosed pits for different carnivorous and deadly animals.Death Star Designer Leland Chee has stated that, while most items, like the supercoaster and Ferris Wheel are entered for fun, much of the other information is included in the Holocron continuity database, and is canon. Regarding the status of the Combat Arena itself, Chee has stated that its best to consider it non-canon unless it resurfaces elsewhere. *Death Star Designer Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station locations Category:Sporting venues
