|birth= |death= |species=Vulptereen |gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Lon Wert was a male Vulptereen from the Deep Core's planet of Vulpter. At the time of the rise of the Galactic Empire, Lon Wert was a talented pilot, and among the people of his homeworld he had earned a reputation as the most skilled racer since Dud Bolt. He wanted to see his people rebuild their livelihoods, and for that reason he joined the Rebel Alliance. Among the capable pilots on all of planet Vulpter, Lon Wert was known not only for his daredevil flying skills but also for his unique understanding of how vehicles worked, which helped him earn a name for himself as the most talented racer since Dud Bolt, the famous professional Podracer pilot during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. When the galaxy was under the rule of the Galactic Empire, Lon Wert had more sympathy for the Rebellion movement because he believed that the Empire's overthrow would help his people rebuild their livelihoods. Wert dreamed of joining the Rebel Alliance as a fighter pilot, but he remained on Vulpter to help support the war effort there. Unfortunately, Wert's arrogance had led him to accumulate debts through gambling. As a result, he found himself deeply in debt to a local crimelord. At that very moment, the annual race, the Vulpter Orbital, was taking place, and Lon Wert was one of the contestants. The crimelord wanted to ensure that Wert did not survive the race as a warning to anyone who thought they could avoid paying what they owed. The Alliance, however, did not want to see their contact harmed and sent a party to Vulpter with a dual mission. Not only did the group have to ensure that Wert survived the race and eliminate any of the crimelord's thugs who tried to sabotage him, but they also had to place a significant wager on Wert's victory and make certain it came to pass in order to pay off the Vulptereen's debt in full. left|thumb|The skills of Lon Wert were not inferior in professionalism to those of the famous racer Dud Bolt Lon Wert was a male Vulptereen tech specialist and pilot. Considered one of the most talented pilots on his homeworld, he was a young and impulsive individual with a tendency toward bragging. Known for his daredevil flying skills and his strong understanding of vehicle mechanics, Wert earned a reputation as a highly impressive racer, though his arrogance also led him to accumulate gambling debts. In addition to his piloting abilities, Wert was trained as a mechanic and specialized in starship technology, with particular expertise in crafting and repairing spacecraft. He was proficient in starfighter operation and trained in the use of blaster pistols and simple weapons, favoring a blaster pistol at range while relying on his natural bite in close combat. He spoke and read Basic and Vulpterish, and his standard equipment consisted of a blaster pistol, a comlink, a pilot's uniform, and a tool kit. A character summary of Lon Wert was presented by Wizards of the Coast on the official website in a supplementary article dedicated to the planet Vulpter for their own roleplaying game in 2005. This non-player character served as a key element in the adventure scenario «Racing in the Rings», in which the players took on the roles of members of the Rebel Alliance. The players were tasked with ensuring that Lon Wert, acting as a contact for the organization, was not harmed by a local crimelord during the annual race, as the crimelord sought to ensure that Wert did not survive the competition.
