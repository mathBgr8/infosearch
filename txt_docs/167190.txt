Omonoth
2470

Omonoth was an Inner Rim white dwarf type star in the Omonoth system, ringed with asteroids mined by Adascorp during the Mandalorian Wars. The ring became infested with exogorths, which devoured the droid mining stations. The star was in a region contested by the Mandalorians and Republic. thumb|left|200px|Exogorths devouring the asteroids around Omonoth. The name of Omonoth was based on those of the ancient alien creatures present in the works of H.P.
