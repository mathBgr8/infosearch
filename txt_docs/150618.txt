Wasith
1416

Wasith was a supervisor for the Corporate Sector Authority. The commanding officer of the CSA on Brosi, Wasith was responsible for the Zinsian processing plants. Wasith found trouble when dealing with the Brosin Underground, a resistance group opposing to the CSA's methods. Wasith tried to deal with the Underground first with the CSA Security Police, but they were ineffective due to their unfamiliarity with the local landscape.
