Lucasfilm Animation
6052

|founder=George Lucas |dissolved= |headquarters= |parent=Lucasfilm |subsid= |website= }} Lucasfilm Animation is a division of Lucasfilm that was founded in 2003. It is a digital animation studio that produces Lucasfilm's animated content for films, games, and television. Lucasfilm Animation was responsible for the Star Wars: The Clone Wars 3D animated television series, in conjunction with Lucasfilm Animation Singapore. The studio also produced Star Wars Rebels, the unreleased Star Wars Detours television series, Star Wars Forces of Destiny, Star Wars Resistance, and a variety of television series for Disney+.
