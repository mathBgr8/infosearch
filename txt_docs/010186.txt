HB-9 rifle
2188

The HB-9 rifle,Star Wars Chronicles: The Prequels also known simply as the HB-9 or Utapaun Blaster Rifle,Star Wars: Battles for the Galaxy was a model of blaster rifle manufactured by Zenoti Arms during the Clone Wars era. These rifles were popular with the natives of Utapau. The ornate Zenoti Arms HB-9 was a long-range blaster rifle with an intricately-patterned barrel and an elegant, curving stock and shoulder brace. It featured a front sight over the tip of the barrel and a hand-turned recharge valve. The ignition housing was primitive, although the weapon still delivered a powerful blast.
