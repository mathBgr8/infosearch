Rollo Morsai
2190

Rollo Morsai was a female Human smuggler and former actress who operated in the Minos Cluster during the time of the Rebellion era. Formerly a beautiful holovid actress under the name "Jona Reeten," she was blacklisted by Babel Torsch when he learned she played a Jedi in a holo. Unable to find work as an entertainer, she converted her yacht Gilded Lily to a freighter and started smuggling. Gaunt and lethargic from inactivity and despair, Rollo was prone to depression and focused on getting revenge on Babel Torsch. Tiebo, her pilot and former valet, performed most of the work for her business.
