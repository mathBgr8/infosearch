|last date=February 1999 |media type=Paperback |timeline=c. 0 ABYStar Wars Science Adventures: Emergency in Escape Pod Four |num books=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Science Adventures is a Legends junior novel series written by Jude Watson and K.D. Burkett. The series includes the books Emergency in Escape Pod Four and Journey Across Planet X, which were published simultaneously in February 1999 by Scholastic. Star Wars Science Adventures did not receive a third entry, and its story was left unresolved. The series is set in the year 0 ABY, and it follows droids R2-D2 and C-3PO, science droid 4B-X, and a Rebel kid named Stuart Zissu as they go on a science-based adventure. The novels also include activities which demonstrate physics from the story. *Star Wars Science Adventures: Emergency in Escape Pod Four *Star Wars Science Adventures: Journey Across Planet X Category:Legends young-reader series
