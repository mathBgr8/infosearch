Toli tree
666

thumb|right|200px|A [[skree-skater among toli trees]] The toli tree was a plant unique to the planet Galantos. Consisting of a thick, gnarled trunk that grew up out of the gelatin sea, a toli tree typically had fewer than ten leaves. The plant was nourished by soaking up nutrients from the gelatin through its system of needle-roots, rather than by photosynthesis. The leaves were used as sails to carry seed pods on the wind. *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Plants of Galantos Category:Trees
