Hadocrassk
1780

Hadocrassk was a male Trandoshan and the Speaker-Above-All of Trandosha prior to the start of the Clone Wars. During the Separatist Crisis, Hadocrassk met with the Wookiee representative Yarua in Hsskhor, to discuss a truce between the long-feuding natives of Trandosha and Kashyyyk. Hadocrassk was of the opinion that the Wookiee species received preferential treatment in the Senate, to the detriment of his own people.
