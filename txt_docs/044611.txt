|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=104 |isbn=1569712352 |isbn2= |timeline=4 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars: Return of the Jedi |issues=TPB |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Return of the Jedi - The Special Edition was a reprint Trade paperback collection, collecting both issues of the Classic Star Wars: Return of the Jedi comic book series. Dark Horse presents Star Wars: Return of the Jedi &ndash; The Special Edition, the third thrilling chapter in the greatest space fantasy ever! The Emperor and Darth Vader make the final preparations to destroy the Rebel Alliance once and for all. Meanwhile, Luke Skywalker, now a Jedi Knight, must rescue Han Solo, frozen in carbonite, from the palace of Jabba the Hutt.
