Asp
2040

Asp was a Strike-class medium cruiser used by the Galactic Empire. Asp and Eel were to go up against MC40a light cruisers Ta and Re. Both ships had TIE Avengers from Avenger Squadron and Assault Gunboats from Talon Squadron.
