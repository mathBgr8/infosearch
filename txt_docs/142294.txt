He trained as a member of the Cerean Ner Yan tradition, translating into Basic as "mind chord." This group trained their minds to attune to the Force, linking their minds together. When the Yuuzhan Vong invaded the galaxy, Ka-Tu-Un was one of a number of refugees trying to flee Druckenwell. He tried to escape onboard the MOD-17 container freighter, Chilastra, but realized that he had been mistaken by the invaders as a member of the New Jedi Order. While attempting to escape the Yuuzhan Vong, Ka-Tu-Un was sold out by Doctor Vlohei, which led to his capture and interrogation by the Yuuzhan Vong. In the Hive of the Infidel article, Ka-Tu-Un is presented as a Force-adept of the Ner Yan tradition with no formal affiliation to the New Jedi Order as led by Luke Skywalker.
