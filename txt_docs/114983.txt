The Venaari Strike Speeder was very versatile and considered to be a reliable attack craft. It was primarily used for the transport of strike squads and had room for a strike squad of ten men. The speeder had enough firepower to defend itself in battle. The speeder was used extensively by Imperial forces on Venaari and elsewhere on Imperial-held planets in the Mid Rim after the death of Palpatine at the Battle of Endor. Venaari strike speeders were used in the battle against the New Republic, but splinter groups within the Imperial Remnant would also use it against each other in their internal struggles for power.
