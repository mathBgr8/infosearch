Gorros' Fen
653

A Gorros' Fen was a massive, tubelike worm that extended from the bowels of the Yuuzhan Vong worldship it was grown into. It was used to gather nutrients from a planet's mantle to power the ship, and on some occasions, even deploy warriors.
