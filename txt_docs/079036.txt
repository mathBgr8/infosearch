|class=Mammal |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=Split trunkStar Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine |lifespan= |origin=Lok |habitat=Desert |diet=Herbivorous |language= }} Snorbals were grazing herd animals from the planet Lok. They were found among the arid wastelands of their homeworlds. Snorbal were highly intelligent and docile creatures, rarely attacking other than to defend their herd. Their trunk had two 'fingers' at the end, allowing them to manipulate simple objects. Snorbal had a highly efficient digestive system. The Snorbals were physically very similar to the thunes of Dantooine.
