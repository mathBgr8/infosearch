At some point by 2 BBY,The battle near F'Dann IX took place at least six years prior to the setting of The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook, which corresponds to 4 ABY per The New Essential Chronology. The confrontation must have therefore occurred at some point by 2 BBY. the Carrack-class light cruiser Dominant of the Galactic Empire engaged the Crimson Nova Gang in battle near F'Dann IX, a confrontation in which the Dominant suffered damage heavy enough to warrant repairs in drydock. F'Dann IX was mentioned in The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook, a 1996 sourcebook published for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
