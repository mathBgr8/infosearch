In 19 ABY, during the Caamas Document Crisis, the planet was a member of the New Republic but was willing to join the Imperial Remnant upon the faked resurrection of Grand Admiral Thrawn. In order to discuss their readmission into the Remnant, Captain Dorja ferried the Sinister Triumvirate aboard the Relentless to the Kroctari homeworld where they met their leader, Lord Superior Bosmihi.
