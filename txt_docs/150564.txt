Maashan
2530

Maashan was a male Kamarian Badlander and a Rebel Alliance member. Maashan was born on Kamar, but left the planet before 10 BBY, and thus was not affected by the rise of the Cult of Varn on the planet. A pyrotechnician, Maashan joined the Galactic Outdoor Survival School and became a member of the prestigious Twilight Class, which graduated around 10 BBY. Afterward, Maashan and most of his classmates joined the Rebel Alliance.
