Wotan
1125

Wotan developed the Wotan Weave, a maneuver used by starfighter pilots to avoid enemy fire. Wotan was first mentioned in the glossary of X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide, a guide to the video game Star Wars: X-Wing, written by Rusel DeMaria, David Maxwell and David WessmanX-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide and published in June 1993.
