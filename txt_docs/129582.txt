Jordi
1661

Jordi group was a pair of Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. When the Alliance succeeded in stranding the Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Priam near Kalla VII, General Jan Dodonna gave the order for a "board and capture" operation against the Priam. A strike by Gold Squadron's Y-wings disabled the ship and transports from Assault group delivered commandos from Alliance Special Forces to capture the vessel. Once captured, Lambda-class shuttles from Panda group delivered the new crew for the ship while Jordi 1 delivered supplies and Jordi 2 brought equipment needed to secure and operate the ship. Once all the transports were aboard, the Rebel forces, along with the newly captured frigate, jumped to hyperspace. *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
