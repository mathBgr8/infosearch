Turl Nevil
3485

Turl Nevil was a male ensign serving in the Second Fleet of the Galactic Alliance Defense Fleet during the Second Galactic Civil War. The son of decorated Galactic Alliance Captain Kral Nevil, he served as a weapons officer aboard the frigate Cheesmeer. In 40 ABY, Turl was killed as the Corellian Five World Prime Minister Sadras Koyan ordered the firing of the hyperspace tractor beam onboard Centerpoint Station, an ancient space station in the Corellian system, during a skirmish at a deep-space rendezvous point. The ensuing blast destroyed the majority of the Second Fleet, including the Cheesmeer and all Corellian forces present at the rendezvous point. In the aftermath, Jacen Solo, one of two Chiefs of State of the Galactic Alliance, asked Kral if he had lost any family in the destruction. Kral responded by informing him of Turl's death.
