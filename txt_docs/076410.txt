Double Worlds
4352

250px|thumb|The Double Worlds, Talus and Tralus The Double Worlds was a phrase used to refer to the double planets Talus and Tralus, which orbited Centerpoint Station as it orbited the star Corell in the Corellian system. Double Worlders was the name given to citizens of either world. They were two of the Five Brothers. It was believed that the Celestials moved the planets into Corell's orbit from other places in the galaxy, along with three other planets; all kept in place by the Centerpoint Station. In 500 BBY, the Federation of the Double Worlds was founded—the government of Talus and Tralus. The Federation capital is Dearic on Talus.
