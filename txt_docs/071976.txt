Kevzod Stenir
1084

Kevzod Stenir was a male individual who served as the Fourth Assistant Attorney General of the Galactic Republic during the Separatist Crisis. For several years when the Clone Wars started, he had been prosecuting legal cases against the Commerce Guild.
