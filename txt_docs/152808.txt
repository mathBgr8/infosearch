Galan starfighter
3161

The Galan starfighter was a sleek, small two-seater starfighter used by the Galacians. In 44 BBY, Queen Veda of Gala loaned one of the starfighters to Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn and his Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi, who would pilot it during the Mission to Melida/Daan. More than seventeen years later, a Galan starfighter was used by Obi-Wan Kenobi and his apprentice Anakin Skywalker on their assignments to Euceron and Haariden. *Jedi Apprentice: The Defenders of the Dead *Jedi Apprentice: The Uncertain Path *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games *Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise *Jedi Quest: The Moment of Truth *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starfighter classes
