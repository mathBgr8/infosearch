Takhaff Uul
1801

Takhaff Uul was a member of the priest caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Uul was a High Priest of Yun-Yuuzhan, the Creator. After the Battle of Coruscant, Uul approached Warmaster Tsavong Lah. He said he had a dream in which Yun-Yuuzhan was angry for not receiving any of the glory from the invasion. Uul said the Creator wanted a whole world to be made into a temple for Yun-Yuuzhan. In truth, Takhaff Uul was conspiring with Master Shaper Ghithra Dal to control Lah.
