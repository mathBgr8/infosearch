The Flight of the Falcon
3790

"The Flight of the Falcon" is a comic story originally published in Marvel UK's The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 157, written by Steve Parkhouse. It was later reprinted and colorized in Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 1.
