Jolly Man
1884

Jolly Man was a freighter used by the New Jedi Order as part of the Wild Knights. It was used to transport their non-hyperdrive equipped ships (2 I-7 Howlrunners and 3 A-9 Vigilances). It participated in the attempts to capture a yammosk at the Assault on Arkania, as Corran Horn's flagship at the Battle of Talfaglio, and at the Battle of Coruscant during the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The New Jedi Order: Recovery *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flagships Category:Freighters Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order
