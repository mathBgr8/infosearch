Stripper
1100

A stripper was a type of dancer that specialized in sexual enticement. During a dance routine, the stripper would take off their outer garments in a stylistic fashion, revealing their underwear or semi-nude or nude forms. During his time on Dathomir, Prince Isolder imagined the many Dathomiri witches of Singing Mountain Clan staring at him with lust as though he were a stripper. *Star Wars: Jango Fett *The Courtship of Princess Leia Category:Leisure and entertainment occupations
