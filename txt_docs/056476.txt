N11
1626

N11 was one of twelve equally-sized zones on the northern hemisphere of the Death Star, a massive battle station built by the Galactic Empire. Like the other twenty-three hemispheres on the space station, the zone covered 31.4 kilometers of the Death Star's circumference, was commanded from a bridge, and contained a number of sectors with designated purposes. The Death Star, including zone N11, was destroyed in 0 BBY. *Death Star Technical Companion *Death Star Technical Companion, Second Edition Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station zones
