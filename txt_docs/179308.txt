Anderson. It was adapted by John Whitman and read by a full cast. The audiobook was first released on cassette tape by Time Warner AudioBooks in July 1997. Later on May 5, 2005 it was released on compact disc by HighBridge Audio. Darth Vader was a Dark Lord of the Sith. Discover the terror of the Dark Lords who preceded him in this stunning sequel to Tales of the Jedi. Ulic Qel-Droma and Nomi Sunrider and young Jedi Knights drawn into a battle for the survival of the Old Republic.
