Sith Hall
1233

Sith Hall was an exhibition hall within the Galactic Museum on Coruscant, which was devoted to the history of the ancient Sith Empire. By the time of Satal and Aleema Keto's arrival on Coruscant, the Hall was in the process of erecting a new exhibit, titled The Dark Lords of the Sith.
