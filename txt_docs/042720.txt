They had lovely green eyes. The empress was documented in the annals of the Arkanis Regency. The Pii system's forested planets Pii III and Pii IV were called "Teeda's Eyes" as a reference to Teeda's green eyes. Teeda was first mentioned in "The Greel Wood Haven," an article written by C. Robert Carey and featured in the sixth issue of Star Wars Adventure Journal, a roleplaying magazine that was published by West End Games in May of 1995 for their Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Empresses Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Individuals with unspecified pronouns
