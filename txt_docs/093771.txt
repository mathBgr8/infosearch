Irradiator
844

Irradiators were small round lighting devices that emitted bands of energy to kill micro-organisms on starships to prevent infection of passengers and crew. They also doubled as interior lighting and were commonly found in most spaceports and many ships, including the Millennium Falcon. *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Illumination devices Category:Safety devices
