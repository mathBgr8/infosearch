Wasaka-berry pudding
895

thumb|right|120px|[[Lumpawaroo/Legends|Lumpy enjoys his mother's wasaka-berry pudding.]] Wasaka-berry pudding was a delicacy on Kashyyyk made from wasaka berries that grew in the Shadowlands. Wasaka-berry pudding was a favorite dish of Chewbacca's. Despite Lumpy's valiant efforts to obtain the berries, Chewbacca's wife, Mallatobuck, was unable to make the pudding for his 200th birthday. *The Wookiee Storybook Category:Puddings Category:Wookiee food
