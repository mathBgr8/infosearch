Balmor played Divoran Holochess with Hart Daele via projective telepathy for around forty years. He lived on Damualer Triac with his second wife, Hannah Balmor. Balmor also enjoyed holographic painting. An interview with Balmor was included in Heroes Of The Republic, a book written by Madelein Aurin and published by Sarlain Star Press.
