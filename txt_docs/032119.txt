Gablith masquer
1708

A gablith masquer was a creature bioengineered by the Yuuzhan Vong to function as a living disguise, allowing an individual to pose as Duros or Givins. Gablith masquers were bioengineered creatures employed by the Yuuzhan Vong as organic disguises, similar to ooglith cloakers. They allowed an individual to disguise themselves as a Duros or Givin,The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth rather than a Human. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, the gablith masquer allowed Yuuzhan Vong agents to disguise themselves as Duros.
