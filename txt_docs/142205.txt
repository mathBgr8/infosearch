Mummergy
1040

Mummergy was a type of strong-smelling plant used to make liquor and as a type of spice. Wuher used it to create a drink for Jabba the Hutt. The Mummers took their name from the plant. *Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy *Star Wars: Complete Locations *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alcoholic beverages Category:Plants Category:Spice variants
