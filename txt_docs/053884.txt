MK6 missile launcher
582

The MK6 missile launcher was an infantry weapons system produced by Zoltan Arms. A standard magazine for the system contained six missiles, although the missiles came in anti-personnel and anti-vehicle variants: individual missiles of either type cost 100 and 200 credits respectively, and the launcher system itself cost 800 credits.
