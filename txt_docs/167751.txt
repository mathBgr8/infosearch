Efavan Port was a spaceport located in the city Efavan, which was the capital of Vorzyd V. The Port Line passenger conveyor tube connected the port with the casinos in the city's Westrex district. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Spaceports Category:Vorzyd V locations
