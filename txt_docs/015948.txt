|sectors=Corva - Meram |stellar= |endpoints= |transitpoints= |otherobjects=Betha II |junctions= }} The Daranc Run was a heavily traveled hyperspace route located in the Outer Rim Territories. This route cut directly through the Corva sector, and the planet Betha II marked the halfway point of the route. It presumably also passed through the Daranc system.
