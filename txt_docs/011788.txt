|companies=Ensemble Studio Theatre |website= }} Ann Sachs (born January 23, 1948) is an actress, writer, business owner and theatre specialist. She voiced Princess Leia in all three radio adaptations of the original Star Wars trilogy. Sachs worked as a professional actress, mostly on the stage, beginning in 1969. In 1992 she co-founded a design company, Sachs Morgan Studio, with her husband, working as president and CEO until the end of 2012. Later she served for a time as vice president of the board of directors of the Ensemble Studio Theatre in New York. {| ! Year !! Title !!
