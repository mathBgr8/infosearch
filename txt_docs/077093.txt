Mikan

|haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor=Green |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Mika |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Mikans were a sentient species native to the asteroid Mika, also known as Jellyfish Cove. They took the form of beautiful near-Human women; however, once they had attracted someone to them, they turned into huge jellyfish-like creatures and consumed their targets. They could only come out in night or on foggy days, as when the sun came out, the natural light reacted with their tissue and froze them into statue-like rocks. Bara was a Mikan. Like the Felacatians, the Mikans only had the ability to alternate between two distinct physiologies.
