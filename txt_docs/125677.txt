NovaNetwork
1606

|products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} NovaNetwork, also known as Nova Network, was a news agency during the Civil War. It was the arch-rival of TriNebulon News. NovaNetwork managed to be pro-Imperial and still have sympathy towards individual rights, though many considered them to be "bottom-feeders". They were on the list of legitimate targets for Rebel privateers.
