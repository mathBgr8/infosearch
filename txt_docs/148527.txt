Yuuzhandroids were droids built by Tendrando Arms during the Yuuzhan Vong War for training their YVH 1 droids. Similar to the YVH 1s, they resembled Yuuzhan Vong warriors and were equipped with sensor packages that mimicked their odor, tactics, body heat, and heartbeats. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:YVH-series battle droid models
