Wahl mansion
775

The Wahl mansion, which was owned by Dr. Wahl, was one of the nicest buildings in Hedrett. It was the only residence in either Hedrett or Gadrin that stood over two stories tall. It had well-tended grounds and security droids watched the property. The Wahls had a doorsperson dressed in a red uniform to greet visitors.
