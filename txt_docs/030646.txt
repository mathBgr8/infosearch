Globblin
1514

}} Globblins were carnivores creatures with green skin and four-clawed limbs. They had sharp, white teeth and a jutting lower jaw. Their eyes, located on the sides of their head, glowed blue. They appeared to be capable of mimicking speech, as one was witnessed saying, "Feed me". While these predators were not very fast in terms of running, they did have a means of capturing their prey without usage of their legs: a long, prehensile tongue. Boba Fett once used a globblin to intimidate a captive into providing the bounty hunter with a sought-after possession. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Carnivorous creatures Category:Reptiles Category:Species of undefined sentience
