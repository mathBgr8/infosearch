Advanced Recon Commando heavy gunner
2878

thumb|250px|A squad of ARC heavy gunners wielding [[Z-6 rotary blaster cannon/Legends|Z-6 rotary blaster cannons]] Advanced Recon Commando heavy gunners (ARC heavy gunners) were the vanguards of the Advanced Recon Commandos, making use of powerful heavy weapons. thumb|left|150px|An ARC trooper with a [[PLX-1/Legends|PLX-1 portable missile launcher.]] Wielding high-powered rapid-firing weapons such as the reciprocating quad blaster or the Z-6 rotary blaster cannon, they were the scourge of Separatist vehicles. Their specialized armor was equipped with a direct feed from their helmets' HUDs to their heavy weapons, granting them extra targeting capability. Other ARC heavy gunners were equipped with PLX-1 portable missile launchers, DC-15S, V-1 thermal detonators, and HX2 antipersonnel mines.Star Wars: Battlefront During the Clone Wars, ARC heavy weapon specialists fought on Geonosis, Kamino, Rhen Var, and Kashyyyk. thumb|right|180px|Two ARC heavy gunners during the [[Battle of Hypori/Legends|Battle of Hypori.]] A team of gunners assisted the ARC trooper squad during the rescue mission of the Jedi strike force during the Battle of Hypori. A variation of the ARC heavy gunners were the Rail-class Advanced Recon Commandos. They also wielded Z-6 rotary cannons and were one of the trooper types found among the anti-troopers after the Declaration of a New Order.
