Ghazdik Jah
1430

Ghazdik Jah was a male Dug who was employed by Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he, along with Mazanga, was wanted by Senators Ask Aak and Aks Moe for the murder of special assistants to the Malastarian Senators.
