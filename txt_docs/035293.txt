Commerce Guild corvette
2598

The Commerce Guild corvette was a type of corvette utilized by the Commerce Guild and later the naval forces of the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars against the Galactic Republic. In that time, which saw the Guild side with the Confederacy, the corporation's corvettes were utilized alongside Guild bombers, Diamond-class cruisers and Recusant-class light destroyers to defend strategic worlds located off the Perlemian Trade Route, such as Castell, Escarte, Felucia, Mooga, and Minntooine, which also served as sites for major Commerce Guild interests as well as Separatist strongholds. Commerce Guild corvettes were first mentioned in the new Star Wars canon in Rise of the Separatists, a 2019 supplement to Fantasy Flight Games' series of Star Wars Roleplaying products.
