Ewoks (1985) 3

Ewoks 3 is the third issue of Marvel's Ewoks series of comic books and was published under the Marvel Comics Star Comics imprint in June 4, 1985. The council calls upon Wicket, Kneesaa, and Teebo to fly their gliders to where the forest and the mountains meet to harvest the special wood the Ewoks use to construct their gliders.
