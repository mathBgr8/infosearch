Mastala (DP20 gunship)
1552

The Mastala was a DP20 gunship in the service of the Rebel Alliance Fleet. It was named after the planet of Mastala. It fought at the Battle of Endor, and shortly afterward at the Invasion of Bakura as part of the Bakura task force. *The Truce at Bakura *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook Category:Bakura task force ships Category:DP20 gunships
