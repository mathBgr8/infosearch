The planet orbited the star Doornik-628 and was itself circled by a single moon. Doornik-628B, like its neighbor Doornik-628C, featured acidic clouds. Doornik-628B was mentioned in "J't'p'tan: Temples and Tybis," an article written by Jason Fry and published on Wizards.com on January 30, 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Doornik-628 system, and therefore Doornik-628B, in grid square K-10.
