Star Wars: The Official Magazine 23 was the 23rd issue of the Star Wars: The Official Magazine, released for November/December 1999. It largely reprinted content from Star Wars Insider 46. *Feel the Force *Prequel Update *Jawas' Corner *Star Wars Q&A *Prequel Profile &mdash; Paul Martin Smith *Bookshelf *Comlink *"Anthony Daniels: Nuts and Bolts" *"The Vehicles of Episode I" *"A Jedi Trio" *"The Unseen Planets of Star Wars" 23
