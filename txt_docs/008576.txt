AP-3 attack droid
2112

The AP-3 attack droid (Assault Platform-3), also known as an AP-3 was a battle droid used by the Imperial Remnant. The AP-3 resembled an astromech droid, but it was armed with six missile launchers and micro-proton torpedoes. It also possessed a repulsorlift drive that enabled it to move quicker across the battlefield at the order of an AP-1-C. The attack droid saw combat during the Battle of Cadinth. The "Imperial Attack Droid C" model was designed for the abandoned The Epic Continues story line. The prototype model was made by kitbashing figures of FX-7 and a Power droid.
