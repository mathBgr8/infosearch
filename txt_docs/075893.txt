Darda
1167

Darda was a tall, dark-haired Human male who lived during the time of the New Republic. Darda was a Peace Brigader disguised as one of the NRI guards covertly stationed aboard Queen of Empire to watch over Elan and Vergere to oversee their transfer from Myrkr to Coruscant.
