Donoslane Excursions
2022

Donoslane Excursions was a transport company founded by Myn Donos and Kirney Slane,The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia born "Gara Petothel" and also known as "Lara Notsil."X-Wing: Solo Command Donoslane leased a section of Behareh Spaceport in Coronet on Corellia in 40 ABY. The co-pilot aboard the manager's personal Lambda-class shuttle was a short, furry alien.Legacy of the Force: Betrayal In 44 ABY, Kirney Slane used one of Donoslane's shuttles to rescue Wraith Squadron from Vandor-3. Although never made explicit in the novel, Donoslane takes its name from Myn Donos and Kirney Slane, an alias Gara Petothel took after the destruction of the Iron Fist, who author Aaron Allston has said got together after the events of X-Wing: Solo Command. The co-pilot of Kirney's shuttle, however, is obviously meant to be Kolot.
