|editor=David Land |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=March 7, 2001 |pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=7 }} Star Wars Tales 7 is the seventh issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. Have you ever had one of those days? Your boss won't stop bothering you with petty complaints? Your teacher won't get off your back about your late book report? The neighbor kids won't stop screaming? Well, ESCAPE your everyday troubles with an all new and improved issue of Star Wars Tales! Join Boba Fett, Plo Koon, Micah Giiett, Yoda, Mace Windu, and a fellow named DOMO JONES as they take your mind off your cares and to a place where you're as likely to run into a three-eyed alien as encounter bureaucratic headaches! STAR WARS! TAKE ME AWAY!!! In an attempt to sate reader demand for stories with Darth Maul, Star Wars Tales editor Dave Land presents "Baby Darth Maul Goes to the Dentist," a short comic featuring Maul and artist Lucas Marangon. Despite Land's assurances, Marangon is worried at the prospect, but accompanies Maul to a dentist's office.
