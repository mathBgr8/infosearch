Military Secrets Act
466

The Military Secrets Act was passed by the New Republic Senate during the Yuuzhan Vong War. It allowed the Chief of State to "declare a majority" of the Senate if a military council voted to approve a measure, thus avoiding the security compromise of a full vote. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:New Republic laws and acts
