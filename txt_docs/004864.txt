Zo Howler
2787

Zo Howler was a Anx Senator who represented the Raioballo sector and the Gravlex Med system in the Galactic Senate during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 22 BBY, Howler's term began shortly before the First Battle of Geonosis. Prior to election, he had been President of Gravlex Med. He was of a younger generation than Horox Ryyder, and Ryyder hoped Howler could achieve peace where he felt he had failed, due to the Separatist Crisis. Howler and his aide, Fo Kuna, attended Horox's retirement ceremony.
