Star Forge Robe
3415

The Star Forge Robes were special Jedi robes that were made in an ancient massive automated Rakatan shipyard, the Star Forge. These robes were long, colored sand-brown, and only its chest plate, gloves, and boots broke the ascetic simplicity. Nonetheless, it benefited a Force-sensitive wearer by increasing their abilities. Despite the fact that the Star Forge was a powerful tool of the dark side of the Force, only one immersed in the light side was able to wear the robes. In 3956 BBY, during the final battle of the Jedi Civil War,Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide the Jedi Revan defeated Sith Lord Darth Malak and the Star Forge was destroyed, setting an end to the production of the robes. The Star Forge Robe first appeared in BioWare's RPG video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, released in July 2003. Star Forge Robes can be created at one of the computer rooms on the Star Forge for a large number of computer spikes. The robe gave the wearer an increase in their wisdom and saving throws. In the non-canonical dark side storyline of the game, these robes cannot be created.
