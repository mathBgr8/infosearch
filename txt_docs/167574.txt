Timma Briss
1111

Timma Briss was a Human female from Corulag. She worked as a slicer at Boztrok spaceport until she stole some important files from docking bay owner and crime lord Pok Nar-Ten. She eluded the corrupt businessman by finding work with the starship mechanic Bolabo Hujaan, who worked on Byblos.
