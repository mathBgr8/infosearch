Orotoru G'am
1588

Orotoru G'am was a large city on Mon Calamari off the coast of the Grand Reefs. It was kept afloat by a system of ballast tanks. It was best known as the home of the Hall of Heroes. By 24 BBY, the city hosted the third race of the Galactic Trials on Orotoru G'am's racetrack and the fourt race of the Podracing Open on The Ballast Complex. *Star Wars: Racer Revenge *Star Wars: Racer Revenge: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Fly Casual *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Cities on Mon Cala
