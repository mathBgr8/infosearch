Salinrerian Sea
687

The Salinrerian Sea was presumably located on Mon Calamari. Slaag Lado a male Draag created a seismic shock pulse in the sea. This created a rift, causing large amounts of environmental damage and perpetual rain. For this Slaag had a price on his own head which was claimed by Jango Fett. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Mon Cala locations Category:Seas and oceans
