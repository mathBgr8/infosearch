Harvey Jason
872

Harvey Albert Jason is an English actor who provided the voice for the AT-PT driver and 2-1C medical droid in Star Wars: Force Commander. He also voiced the Imperial transport ship captain and Royal Lifter pilot in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
