Converted drone barge
1692

This converted drone barge was Admiral Mordur's base of operations following the Battle of Endor. Command codes had been installed to enable an entire convoy of automated drone barges to follow this vessel, carrying massive amounts of valuable ores from Vandelhelm. The Admiral planned to leave the system once the last of Vandelhelm's royal family had been killed.
