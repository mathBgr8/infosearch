Adamastor was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas subsequently placed the Adamastor system, and therefore the world itself, in grid square L-9.
