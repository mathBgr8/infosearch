The Rebel assault armor was developed for modern combat and was often worn by a soldier needing to retain their agility while ensuring maximum protection against pulse-wave blasters. In fact, it offered a high degree of protection against kinetic damage but lesser protection from energy-based weapons like blasters. The complete armor set was composed of the helmet, chest plate, left & right biceps, left & right bracers, gloves, leggings and boots. Typical suits had to be biolinked to the wearer to prevent unwanted use; they were also very expensive to create, meaning that such assault armor was a rare sight in battle. A variant of this armor, the Rebel assault forest camouflage armor, was designed to help Rebel assault troops blend with forest environments. Another variant was the Rebel Special Ops Officer armor, best known as the Rebel Special Ops armor. This armor was originally developed for Special Operations officers but it was eventually provided to any Alliance soldier who had reached the rank of commander by proving superior fighting skills on the battlefield. After the Battle of Yavin, Rebel assault armor were sometimes used by Heavy Weapons Specialists. In 1 ABY, certain spacers who allied with the Alliance to Restore the Republic received camouflaged Rebel assault armor for serving and assisting certain high-profile Rebellion leaders while they were in hiding within the Hidden Rebel Base on Corellia. The same year, Rebel assault armor was worn by the Alliance organizer of the Remembrance Day event on Corellia. Also in 1 ABY, a spacer retrieved the nine parts of a Rebel battle armor while visiting multiple worlds in the galaxy and finally assembled the complete armor. During the Galactic Civil War, the Mistryl Assault Troopers used an armor that included an helmet very similar to the Rebel assault armor helmet. The Rebel assault armor appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The Rebel assault armor was one of the Rebel faction armors added to the game with the release of the "Combat Upgrade," on April 27, 2005.
