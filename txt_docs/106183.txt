Sith Destroyer
1800

A Sith Destroyer was a capital ship used by the Sith during the New Sith Wars. The ship type was armed with at least a broadside laser cannon artillery and could overpower several battleships. One such vessel was the Nightfall, Lord Kaan's flagship.
