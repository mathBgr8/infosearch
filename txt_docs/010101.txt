|subscribers= |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=7 |series=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine }} Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 7 is the seventh issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in April 1996. This issue's theme focused on Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 7, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with Topps Finest Chromium Darth Vader and Shadows of the Empire Prince Xizor promotional trading cards, and issue 12 of the LucasArts Company Store catalog.
