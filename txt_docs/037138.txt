|birth= |death= |species=Human |gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Fair |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Galactic Empire |masters= |apprentices= }} Yerka Mig was a Human from the planet Ralltiir who became an Imperial officer, pilot, and bureaucrat. When the Galactic Empire subjugated his homeworld in 1 BBY, Mig resigned and fled in remorse, becoming a fugitive from both Imperial Intelligence and the Imperial Security Bureau and living as a fugitive on Tatooine, where he frequented the planet's Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in 0 BBY. The Human Yerka Mig hailed from the planet Ralltiir. He joined the Galactic Empire, becoming a pilot and an Imperial officer. Also a bureaucrat with high security clearance, Mig was still serving the Empire when his homeworld was subjugated in 1 BBY.Empire 5 dates the subjugation of Ralltiir to three weeks before the Battle of Yavin. According to The Essential Atlas, the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope begin on 35:3:3, which would place the subjugation of Ralltiir in 35:2, which equates to 1 BBY, as explained here. Mig resigned in protest and fled in remorse after Ralltiir's subjugation. With his high security clearance making him a fugitive from both Imperial Intelligence and the Imperial Security Bureau, Mig was forced to live as an outlaw on Tatooine. thumb|left|200px|Yerka Mig (center) at the cantina While on Tatooine, Mig was known to frequent Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in the planet's city of Mos Eisley.Star Wars Encyclopedia In 0 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope to 0 BBY. Mig was in the cantina when a former moisture farmer named Luke Skywalker and a Jedi Master named Obi-Wan Kenobi entered, searching for a pilot to take them and the droids C-3PO and R2-D2 to the planet Alderaan. Mig witnessed the smuggler Han Solo killing the bounty hunter Greedo before Solo left the cantina. Yerka Mig fled from the Empire in remorse after his homeworld's subjugation. While he was living as an outlaw on Tatooine and frequenting Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina, Mig could not help but smirk at Solo quipping "sorry about the mess" to Wuher, the bartender. He had brown hair and fair skin. Yerka Mig first appeared in 1977's , the first film of the original trilogy. Uncredited in the film, he was portrayed by actor George Stock, listed as one of the "bureaucrats."
