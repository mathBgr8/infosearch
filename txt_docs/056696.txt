Drosh Khalii
1593

Drosh Khalii was a member of the Intendant caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. After the Battle of Coruscant and the terraforming of Coruscant into Yuuzhan'tar, Khalii was appointed Prefect of the Geling Sector, becoming very powerful in the process. Nom Anor, as the Prophet Yu'shaa, decided that Khalii should be brought down. Using the Priestess Ngaaluh, he inserted Shamed Ones spreading the Jeedai heresy into Khalii's household.
