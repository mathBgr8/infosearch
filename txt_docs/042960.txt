Groola
1289

Groola was a Hutt and owner of Groola's Place. He insisted that he was not a typical representative of the Hutts. He arrived on Corellia five years before the Battle of Yavin and reopened an old, closed-down bar which he named Groola's Place, he turned it into a large underground complex.
