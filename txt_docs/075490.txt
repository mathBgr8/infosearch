Mif Kumas
1141

Mif Kumas was a male Calibop Sergeant at Arms for the New Republic Senate. In 25 ABY, he quieted everyone down as Director bel-dar-Nolek explained the Yuuzhan Vong attack on Obroa-skai at Coruscant's Grand Convocation Chamber.
