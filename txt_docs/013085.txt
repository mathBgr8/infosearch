- Cadavine - Juris - Bajic - Sarin - Parmel - Quence - Khuiumin - Svivreni - Parmic - Torch - Thuris - Skine |stellar= |endpoints= *Farstine *SkynaraGalaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Svivren |transitpoints= |otherobjects=Farstine - Ninzam VI - Trigalis - Stend - Vergesso Asteroids - Bajic - Vohai - Elshandruu Pica - Suarbi 7 - Svivren - Skynara |junctions=Triellus Trade Route - Llanic Spice Run }} The Five Veils Route, also known as the Five Veils' Tour trade route, was a hyperspace route that ran from Farstine to its terminus at Skynara which was located in the Skine sector. It was the site of the Five Veils Campaign.The Essential Guide to Warfare Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim establishes that Farstine is the head of the Five Veils' Tour trade route, which linked Farstine to Skynara in the Skine sector. In 2009's The Essential Atlas by Jason Fry and Daniel Wallace, no mention was made of the Five Veils' Tour trade route, but a Five Veils Route was included, linking Farstine to Svivren in the Svivreni sector.
