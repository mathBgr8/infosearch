Ice worm
1441

The ice worms were large predatory creatures from Akuria II. Their burrows honeycombed Akuria II's icy surface, making it highly dangerous to walk on. Ice worms were green, slug-like creatures, with mouths filled with sharp teeth and lined with tentacles. They moved through their burrows on a series of small pseudopods.
