Komok-Da
1991

Komok-Da was a Sith Lord of the old Sith Empire, hailing from the Sith species, and a weapons master. His spirit dwelt in Bo Vanda's holocron along with her spirit, and that of Lord Kla. He had great knowledge of various weapons, including Sith poison and lightsabers. He seemed to be an academic, not caring who learned his arts. In truth he was obsessed with his weapons, and would only share his knowledge with those who would kill in cold blood for that very purpose.
