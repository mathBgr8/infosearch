Iotran Police Force
2229

The Iotran Police Force, or IPF, was the leading military and police force on Iotra. The top eight members of IPF made up the Joint Council, the lead ruling body of Iotran. It was formed seven hundred years before the Galactic Civil War by consolidating various military orders, including the Kilandran Royal Forces, the Lurathi Guard, and the Jengardin Millennial Warriors. They handled all the civic duties in the Iotran Territories, including disaster relief, law enforcement, fire fighting, and emergency medical services. All Iotrans were required to serve in the force, giving them a large reserve of highly trained personnel available for use. They were also hired out to corporations and other governments as a source of income for the Iotrans.
