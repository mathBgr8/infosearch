Surf and Turf
517

"Surf and Turf" referred to a dish that contained meat from both land animals and of sea-dwelling creatures. Ladneks were sea creatures that were sometimes found in a "Surf and Turf" meal. In real life, Surf and Turf is an actual main course meal typically consisting of seafood as well as meat. A lot of times it contains lobster as well as beef steak. *Rebel Dawn Category:Meat Category:Seafood
