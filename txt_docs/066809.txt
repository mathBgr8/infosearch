Corrupter repair convoy
1822

A group of Imperial cargo freighters which were trying to bring repair equipment to damaged Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter in the Mev system. They were escorted by Monitor. They were completely destroyed by the Rebel Alliance in the Battle of Bettel, shortly after the Battle of Hoth. The convoy consisted of: *MB-C1 medium transport Tendo, carrying Engineering droids *MB-C1 medium transport Sormus, carrying probe droids *MB-C1 medium transport Togri, carrying 'machinery' *BFF-1 bulk freighter Polis, carrying durasteel *BFF-1 bulk freighter Hilo, carrying durasteel *(2x) Modular Conveyor Benar, carrying 'provisions' *Xiytiar-class transport Kalif, carrying probe droids *Xiytiar-class transport Tilark, carrying probe droids *Container transport Vo, carrying 'hyperdrive parts' *(4x) Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Lonus (1-4), carrying 'technicians' The three transports carrying Probe Droids is likely a reference to an earlier mission, in which the player destroys three sets of probe droids. The droids mentioned in this mission are likely replenishments. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Imperial fleets
