It was a barren world with atmosphere-filled craters and tasar crystals. Tasariq joined the Republic in 22 BBY. It was later part of the Galactic Empire. Barimoq Industries, a Tasari-run corporation that produced a variety of personal projectile weapons, was based on Tasariq.
