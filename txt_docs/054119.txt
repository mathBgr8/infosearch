Damorind Securities
1096

Damorind Securities was a security corporation that manufactured and sold holding devices, such as force cages, during the era of the Galactic Empire and the Galactic Civil War. One of these models was the Model S-3 force cage, which was a portable version of the popular security apparatus. They also produced the RPC-12 restraint capsule. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Security companies
