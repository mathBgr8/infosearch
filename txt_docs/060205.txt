Drixfar
1399

The drixfar was a musical instrument made of podracer engine parts. It can be assumed from the name that it was in the same family as the fanfar. During the Boonta's Eve Race, a group of Bith musicians in the stands played various horns, including the drixfar. *Star Wars: Episode I: The Visual Dictionary *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: The Phantom Menace: The Expanded Visual Dictionary Category:Wind musical instruments
