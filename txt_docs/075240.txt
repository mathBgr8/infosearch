Rade Dromath
1883

Rade Dromath was a tall blond, blue-eyed Human male native to Garqi. His mother was Dynba Tesc and his father was a New Republic Defense Force officer killed in the Thrawn campaign. His mother always planned for an invasion of Garqi by the Imperial Remnant, and so formulated escape and resistance plans and made secret stockpiles of supplies. Although his mother was killed when the Yuuzhan Vong first invaded Garqi, Rade escaped and used her preparations to become a leading member of the Garqi Resistance, later helping a New Republic commando unit during the Battle of Garqi.
