Kei
1299

Kei was a Yevethan female, the first mate of Nil Spaar, and the darna of his family. Her birth-cask produced two nitakka&mdash;males&mdash;and one marasi&mdash;female&mdash;but eventually succumbed to the gray death. Spaar kept a breedery alcove at the royal palaces in Giat Nor empty in her honor and to assuage her jealousy of his many other mates.
