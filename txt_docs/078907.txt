Saber 1 had the serial number 000004, and also bore a bloodstripe, representing a minimum of ten kills. By 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology establishes that the events of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi take place in 4 ABY. is concurrent to the events of Return of the Jedi; therefore, its in-universe year is also 4 ABY. Saber 1 was assigned to the Imperial 181st Imperial Fighter Wing's Saber Squadron. The interceptor was piloted by Baron Soontir Fel, the leader of the 181st Imperial Fighter Wing. Saber 1 was created in 2000 for the Death Star II Limited expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
