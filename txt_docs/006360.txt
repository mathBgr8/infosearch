|series=Star Wars: Droids (1986) |issue=2 }} Droids 2 is the second issue of the Star Wars Droids series of comics. The story is split into three chapters, "The Ultimate Weapon Part 1," "The Ultimate Weapon Part 2: In the Company of Evil," and "The Ultimate Weapon Part 3: Shock Treatment." It was published on March 18, 1986 with a cover date of June 1986.
