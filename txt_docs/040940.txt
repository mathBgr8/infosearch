Battle of Foerost (Jedi Civil War)
2902

The Battle of Foerost was the opening battle of the Jedi Civil War. In a surprise attack, Revan's fleet, swept into the Foerost system. Quickly overcoming the unprepared defenses, Revan and Malak announced to the galaxy that they were the new Sith Lords, and captured the majority of the Republic Fleet based in the drydocks around Foerost, dealing a crippling blow to the Galactic Republic. *The New Essential Chronology *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide *The Essential Atlas *Star Wars: The Old Republic Encyclopedia Category:Battles of Foerost Category:Battles of the Jedi Civil War
