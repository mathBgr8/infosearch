CoCo Penitentiary
1552

The CoCo Penitentiary was a prison located in CoCo Town on the planet Coruscant. In 22 BBY, the CoCo District Constabulary issued a jailbreak alert when three convicts escaped from the Moderate Security Ward of the CoCo Penitentiary. Felons Dannl Faytonni, Achk Med-Beq and Borlovian smuggler Llollulion escaped aboard a supply truck servicing the Penitentiary's laundry wing.
