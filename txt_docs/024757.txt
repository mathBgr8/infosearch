|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYStar Wars Omnibus: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 1 |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issue=31 }} X-Wing Rogue Squadron 31 was the thirty-first issue in the X-Wing: Rogue Squadron series of comics. It was released on June 3, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. Rogue Squadron faces off against the pirates of the Blazing Claw, but no sooner have they sent them packing than an Imperial Star Destroyer makes orbit! It's time for Baron Fel to show his true colors as the fate of Rogue Squadron hangs in the balance!
