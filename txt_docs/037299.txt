Gabo
1336

Gabo, also known as Gabo the Wicked, was an Aqualish, wanted by the Republic Security Force for indecent exposure and illegal hologram distribution. His bounty was 3,000 credits alive and 1,500 dead. He was captured on the Outland Transit Station by Jango Fett for 3,000 credits sometime after the Battle of Naboo. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Aqualish Category:Criminals Category:Males
