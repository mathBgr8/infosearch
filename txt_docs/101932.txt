Four-Twenty
2419

Four-Twenty was the designation of an individual who served as a stormtrooper in the Imperial Stormtrooper Corps during the Galactic Civil War between the Empire and the New Republic. Shortly after a raid on the planet Talasea in 6 ABY, the individual was killed by a trap left by New Republic forces. The raid was intended to eliminate Rogue Squadron, an elite New Republic starfighter squadron. Four-Twenty's death, along with the death of stormtrooper Four-Eighteen, would later be reported to Imperial Intelligence's agent Kirtan Loor. The stormtrooper was first mentioned in the 1996 novel X-Wing: Rogue Squadron, written by Michael A.
