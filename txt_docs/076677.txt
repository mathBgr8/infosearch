Birdigan Nasalle
960

}} Birdigan Nasalle was a swoop racer who was at some point the TransGal Champion Swoop Racer. Nasalle once witnessed a wind race during the Festival of the High Winds on Sevarcos II, commenting on the fact that the participants were engaged in such a dangerous race just for the fun of it.
