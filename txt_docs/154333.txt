Its narrow-beam hyperspace transponder signal was configured to blend into the "background noise" caused by drive emissions, making it virtually undetectable except for dedicated scanners. IG-88B hid two of these scanners aboard Boba Fett's starship, Slave I. IG-88C and IG-88D tracked the ship to Tatooine and sought to steal the carbonite-encased Han Solo, but Fett outsmarted the assassin droids and claimed Jabba the Hutt's bounty on Solo. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Homing beacons Category:Neuro-Saav Technologies products
