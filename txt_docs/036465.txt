TC-3
1704

TC-3 was a TC-series protocol droid belonging to Senator Yeb Yeb Adem'thorn. TC-3 had primarily red plating with silver colored seams. He was distinguished by a silver neck, which almost gave the appearance that his red-colored head was floating. TC-3 was cut from The Phantom Menace.
