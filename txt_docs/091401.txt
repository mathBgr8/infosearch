Steve John Shepherd
968

Steve John Shepherd is an actor who was credited for portraying "Naboo Lieutenant" in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. In Star Wars Legends, the character was later identified as Theomet Danlé in the Databank, a name that was retained in canon. Category:Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones actors
