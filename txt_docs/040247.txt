Courageous (Republic flagship)
1789

Courageous was a flagship in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. The vessel, commanded by Jedi General Plo Koon, operated as the flagship of the task force dispatched to attack the Separatist choke point in the Ywllandr system. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flagships Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
