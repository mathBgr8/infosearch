Rogir-boln

A rogir-boln was a type of fruit native to the planet Zonama Sekot. The fruit was harvested, turned into a white paste and eaten by the Ferroans. Senshi grew them on his plantation. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Fruits and vegetables
