Breathe My Jets
693

Breathe My Jets was the vessel of Uran Lavint. It had enough cargo space to fit several YT-1300 light freighters in its cargo hold. The ship was captured by the Anakin Solo after Uran sold out her fellow smugglers to the Galactic Alliance.
