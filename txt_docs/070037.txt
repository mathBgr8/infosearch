Baloob
1492

Baloob, nicknamed "Big Nose" by Den Dhur, was an Ortolan male who worked as a pubtender of the cantina stationed on Drongar during the Clone Wars. The cantina was opened for the military and medical personnel that the Galactic Republic sent to the planet and was based at Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7. He survived the attack of the Separatist forces against the Rimsoo, and took up again the business in the new location.
