Kollrin
958

Kollrin was a Sullustan ambassador during the Galactic Civil War. He was a frequent visitor of the Ace of Sabres, a luxury gambling establishment on the planet Kluistar. He was the target of an assassination attempt by Tovric, but the plan was foiled by Dunan Par'Ell, head of security for the casino. *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy Category:Ambassadors Category:Males Category:Sullustans
