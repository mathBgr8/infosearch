Atin Attan
2260

Atin Attan was a Sullustan pilot who was a member of the Galactic Outdoor Survival School's Twilight Class who later joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic. A pilot since her childhood, Attan studied Sullustan geology from SoroSuub Extension programs. After graduating, she joined the Galactic Outdoor Survival School, and was a member of the prestigious Twilight Class. Attan led one of the most dangerous parts of the final trek that led to the impressive graduation of the Twilight Class around 12 BBY. After that, Attan joined the Rebel Alliance alongside most of the other members of the Twilight Class. She became a pilot instructor for the Alliance Fleet.
