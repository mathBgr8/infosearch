Janos Marsh
1361

Janos Marsh served as the starship computer expert on board the scout vessel Bright Seeker. Apparently loyal to House Calipsa, Marsh was in fact, a member of the rival House Melantha's Covert Activities Cadre, working to keep House Calipsa from locating a new trade route through Tapani sector. Marsh was forced to kill first officer Quat Ralto when the latter interrupted him contacting House Melantha in the transceiver room of the Bright Seeker.
