Great Temple (Lorahns)
998

The Great Temple was a building on the planet Lorahns that was the center of the Ffib religion. thumb|left|150px|Reess Kairn devastates the Ffib temple. During the time of the Galactic Republic, the Jedi-turned-pirate, Reess Kairn, ransacked the Great Temple&mdash;destroying sacred texts, stealing priceless artifacts, and murdering four Ffib priests during his getaway. This was a major factor in contributing to the Ffib's hatred of the Jedi. *The Bounty Hunters: Aurra Sing Category:Temples
