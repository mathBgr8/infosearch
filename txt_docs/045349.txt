In his youth, he was a reputable freighter pilot, but he fell victim to gambling and alcoholism and had to take up a smuggling career to pay off his many debts. He was known for losing crew members, valuable cargo, and even entire ships. He employed the mischievous but technically skilled Jawa Squig as his very unreliable mechanic.
