Wookiee Chieftain
2526

thumb|right|250px|A Wookiee Chieftain A Wookiee Chieftain, also known as a Wookiee Elder, was a Wookiee with the ability to lead and to command troops on the battlefield, or managing a society of Wookiees. Chieftains also played roles in some government issues such as battles or war. Notably, they had the right to cancel the festivities of Life Day, the most important Wookiee holiday."The Kashyyyk Depths" At the time of the Galactic Republic, a council of venerated Elders accompanied Kashyyyk's representative to the Galactic Senate on Coruscant.Inside the Worlds of Star Wars: Episode I Merumeru was a Chieftain at the time of the Battle of Kashyyyk and, at 250 years old, was one of the youngest to have held the title.
