Droids (1986) 8
5426

Droids 8 is the eighth and final issue of the Star Wars: Droids series of comics. Published under the Marvel Comics, Star Comics imprint, this issue had a cover price of $1.00 U.S. and $1.25 Canadian. It was published March 17, 1987 with a cover date of June 1987. After making it back to the Millennium Falcon, following the death of Obi-Wan Kenobi, C-3PO and R2-D2 are brought to Yavin, where Artoo delivers the Death Star plans to the Rebel Alliance. Upon their arrival, the droids discover and destroy a homing droid tracking their whereabouts. Following this, Artoo joins Luke Skywalker on the Rebel assault on the Death Star. This series was based on the Star Wars Droids animated TV series, but the stories are original and not adaptations of the show.
