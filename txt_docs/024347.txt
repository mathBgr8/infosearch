Arkanian bond armor
1468

Developed by the ancient Arkanians, Arkanian bond armor used a number of special alloys that lost heat at a very slow rate, offering protection from sudden drops in temperature. A bonded form of laminate was used which made them durable though somewhat inflexible. The Arkanian bond armor was intended to appear in the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic but it was removed from the final version of the game.
