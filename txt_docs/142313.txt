Lord Baltharog was a character from Quest for Quasar, first a book, and then released to the big screen in 22 BBY. According to the original sourcebook for Quest for Quasar, Baltharog was 1.89 meters tall. He was to be portrayed by the holoactor Harlan Ottekvar, but reaction to the decision to use him was criticized by fans of the original work.
