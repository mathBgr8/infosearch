InterGalactic Currency Exchange System
821

The InterGalactic Currency Exchange System managed currency distributed by the InterGalactic Banking Clan. The IGCES also allowed the banking clan to register new currencies. The company also streamlined the process of adding new currencies considerably, from two years, to thirty minutes. Category:InterGalactic Banking Clan
