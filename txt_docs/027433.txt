|published in=Tales from Jabba's Palace |reprinted in= |pages=19 |timeline=4 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion (39 GrS) |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Taster's Choice: The Tale of Jabba's Chef" is a short story by Barbara Hambly that tells the story of Jabba the Hutt's chef, Porcellus which was published in Tales from Jabba's Palace December 1995 by Bantam Spectra. The ever-fretful Porcellus is the head chef in Jabba's Palace. He avoids displeasing the Hutt with food and attempts to maintain a low profile and humble kitchen. Until he hears that Jabba suspects him of putting fierfek in his food – a Huttese term that Porcellus takes to mean "poison". He knows that one bad meal would be a death sentence. When the dead body of Weequay sail barge captain, Ak-Buz, turns up near the kitchen, a terrified Porcellus hides the corpse lest he be a suspect. In the midst of Luke Skywalker's rescue of his friends from Jabba's clutches, Porcellus learns the true definition of fierfek from Skywalker's droid.
