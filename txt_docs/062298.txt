Moonshadow (Imperial II-class)
2329

The Moonshadow was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer operated by the New Republic Defense Force during the Galactic Civil War. It was commanded by Admiral Kir Vantai and was often paired with the Swift Liberty, commanded in turn by Admiral Areta Bell. At the Battle of Liinade III, it served as the home base for a wing of B-wing starfighters under the command of General Horton Salm.
