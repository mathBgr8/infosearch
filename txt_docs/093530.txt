Council of Lords
1238

The Council of Lords, also known as the Noble council, was the legislative body of the Onderonian government during the time of the First Jedi Purge. As of 3951 BBY, among its ranks were included Lord Dashel, and some time prior to that, the late husband of Terlyn, who had attempted to assassinate General Vaklu. That same year, they debated the issue of Onderon seceding from the Galactic Republic, which Queen Talia opposed, but Vaklu supported. In 3627 BBY, the council was supportive of the Galactic Republic during King Regalun Petryph's political crisis.
