The Lundi Series
1394

thumb|Professor Lundi The Lundi Series were recorded lectures of Professor Murk Lundi used by the New Jedi Order for insight into the history of the Sith. The Lundi Series were initially met with skepticism by Jedi historians, as Professor Lundi failed to cite specific sources, and some data appeared to be his own inventions to improve his academic reputation. Lundi was considered a better teacher than researcher.
