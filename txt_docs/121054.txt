Tahlboorean language
556

Tahlboorean was the root language of several dialects of the Tahlboorean species, spoken by both Troobs and Hobors. While many Troobs learned to speak Galactic Basic Standard, the Hobors stuck with Tahlboorean. The protocol droid C-3PO translated Chief Nimondro's Tahlboorean circa 5.5 BBY. *The Protocol Offensive Category:Languages
