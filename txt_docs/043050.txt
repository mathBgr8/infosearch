Byss Bistro
1962

The Byss Bistro, also known as the Spacer's Bistro, was a cantina on Byss, the reborn Emperor's throneworld. It was located at the base of the Imperial Freight Complex. Although only pilots hauling Imperial cargo were allowed to land on Byss, Salla Zend was able to recruit a few smugglers with New Republic sympathies at the bistro to help rescue Lando Calrissian, Wedge Antilles, and their team of commandos, whose mission to Byss had been defeated by the Emperor's mutant chrysalides.
