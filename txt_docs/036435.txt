|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=3964 BBY |series=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |issue=3 }} Knights of the Old Republic 3 is the third issue in the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series of comics. After stowing aboard a garbage carrier in the Lower City and surviving a scuffle with a Black Vulkars crew, Gryph leads Zayne to an area known as Junk Junction. Meanwhile, the Jedi pursuing them foolishly fall for a scheme of Gryph's that leads them into the Undercity. In the Junction, the duo are attacked by an Arkanian known as Jarael and ordered to leave. She rationalizes her actions to the duo by informing them that because of the massacre, Lhosan Industries (a major employer in the Lower City) has decided to relocate to a safer planet. At this time, Gryph spots the man he came to see, Camper, and asks for his assistance in smuggling them off the planet. Camper refuses to help the fugitives escape, however, and ignores Gryph's pleas for help. At that moment, Taris Civil Authorities and the Jedi pursuing Zayne and Gryph swoop in on a gunship and order them to surrender. Camper and Jarael rush to get out of the area quickly, not caring that Gryph and Zayne follow them. Revealing the junk shop to be a junk hauler, the four escape the planet. The escape is short-lived, however, as the ill-equipped hauler loses power just as an orbital patrol ship approaches.
