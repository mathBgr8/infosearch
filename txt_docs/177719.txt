Star Wars Kids 11 *July 1998 Star Wars Kids 11 was released packaged with Star Wars Missions 11: Bounty Hunters vs. Battle Droids, for which the StarWars.com Cargo Bay a release date of July 1998. Bounty Hunters vs. Battle Droids additionally denotes a July 1998 release date. |subscribers= |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-51213-7 |issn= |genre= |issue=11 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 11 is the eleventh issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in July 1998. Issue 11 included part one of the serial comic Imperial Spy, written by Mike W. Barr. Star Wars Kids 11 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book. Star Wars Kids 11 came packaged with the game's eleventh adventure book, Bounty Hunters vs. Battle Droids. 11
