During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. the Nikto exited the club accompanied by a Pacithhip and the Human Palee Ruda. Just as the group exited, bounty hunter Zam Wesell ran into the club away from the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker after her assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala. Mois Parcon first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The character was first named in an image in the StarWars.com web article "Cantina Roll-Call: Shedding Light on Some Alien Aliases," published on May 2, 2003.
