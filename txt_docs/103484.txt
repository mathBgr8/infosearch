Tauntaun Feathers A1
597

"Tauntaun Feathers A1" was the name of a datacard created by Kyle Katarn and his apprentice, Jaden Korr, reporting the investigation on the disturbance in the Force on Hoth. Luke Skywalker later donated this report to the New Republic Historical Archive. Category:Datacards
