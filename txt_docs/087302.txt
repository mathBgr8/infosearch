2JTJ
2033

2JTJ, usually referred to as TooJay, was a navigation droid provided by the Jedi Order to Master Tahl after she was blinded on Melida/Daan. 2JTJ helped her with her new life, providing assistance so that she could start getting used to her new condition. However, the failed Jedi Xanatos installed an audio and video spying device inside the droid in order to track Qui-Gon Jinn and Tahl's investigations. When they discovered the trick, they prepared a trap for Xanatos.
