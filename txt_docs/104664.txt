Mawkren
1052

Mawkrens were one of the many animals sold at the Exoticalia Pet Emporium. Mawkrens were reptiles. They resembled small lizards. When raising baby mawkrens, the diet, humidity, temperature, and lighting needed to be perfectly regulated in order for the mawkrens to fully develop. For this reason mawkrens were considered to be exotic pets that were often very expensive both to acquire and keep healthy. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Domesticated creatures Category:Reptiles Category:Species of undefined sentience
