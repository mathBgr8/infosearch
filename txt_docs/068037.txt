War party

A war party (plural war parties) was a military unit among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. War parties would vary in size according the mission profile of the party, but their numbers could range from anywhere to half a dozen to two dozen. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Yuuzhan Vong military units
