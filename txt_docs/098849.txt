He was in command of a platoon of two hundred stormtroopers sent by Moff Wilhuff Tarkin to Kashyyyk, just prior to its Imperial occupation. Unlike those under his command, Janzor was aware that their platoon was only a distraction while Central Command landed a much larger army elsewhere. While briefing his men before the mission, Janzor underestimated the Wookiees, whom he described as primitive and possibly descended from banthas.
