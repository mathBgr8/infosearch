Darnov
1158

Darnov was a Human male Rebel officer who accompanied Bria Tharen aboard the Queen of Empire during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, he he accompanied with Feltran and Bria Tharen on the Queen of the Empire where they were on their way to Nar Shaddaa to meet up with the Hutts.
