H. Hrush
1870

Professor H. Hrush was an individual who was the author of a book entitled The Lost Twenty: Dooku's Rise, though it was published without consent from the proper parties. The book concerned a Jedi Master named Dooku, who resigned from the Jedi Order to found a militarized confederacy of worlds that wished to secede from the Galactic Republic.The New Essential Chronology For a time, Hrush's book was listed as the number-two best-selling book on a list compiled by HoloNet News on the date 13:2:28. H. Hrush was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #45, a 2002 in-universe website. The website's content was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
