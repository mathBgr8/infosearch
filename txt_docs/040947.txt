Paul Youll
2327

Paul Youll is an artist and illustrator who specializes in science fiction and fantasy. He created the cover illustrations for the X-Wing series as well as Tales from the New Republic, the original paintings of which are now owned by George Lucas. Youll lives in the United Kingdom.
