Larjh StarCorporation
1402

Larjh StarCorporation was a corporation that specialized in the manufacture of custom space yachts and suborbital transports. A subsidiary of zZip Product Concepts Limited, Larjh StarCorporation maintained a dealership aboard the luxury gambling liner Cal Ambre.
