Dead Guard
1352

The Dead Guard was the name given to a group of Wookiees that defended Kkowir Forest on their home world of Kashyyyk, also known as the Dead Forest. Dead Guards wore gray robes. Dead Guards appeared as non-player characters in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees *Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Military units Category:Organizations of Kashyyyk
