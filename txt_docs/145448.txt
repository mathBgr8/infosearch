Rust Never Sleeps
3786

"Rust Never Sleeps" is a comic first published in The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 156, written by Alan Moore. It was later reprinted and colorized in Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 2. C-3PO and R2-D2 pose as inert droids and are taken by a junk hauler to Ronyards where countless broken or malfunctioning droids die. They encounter Fivelines, a droid with many disciples that believe Ronyards to be a god. Threepio warns Fivelines that the Empire is planning to strip Ronyards of its metal and use it for their own purposes. Fivelines unfortunately does not listen and is destroyed when an Imperial Star Destroyer arrives. The droids' "God" then destroy the scout group of stormtroopers and eventually the Star Destroyer itself.
