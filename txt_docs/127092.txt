Unidentified flat-bodied creature (Dagobah)
1329

A flat-bodied creature was native to the swamps of Dagobah. Its body had a brown and yellow coloration, and it had four eyes.The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide The creature appeared in The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide', written by Terryl Whitlatch and Bob Carrau and illustrated by Terryl Whitlatch, at the beginning of the Dagobah section of the book. It isn't named, nor is there an entry about it in the book itself. *The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide Category:Creatures of Dagobah Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Species of undefined sentience Category:Swamp creatures
