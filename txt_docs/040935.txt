Harbinger could refer to several things: *Harbinger, an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer used by the Galactic Empire after the Battle of Yavin and hijacked by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. *Harbinger, an Imperial Star Destroyer that served in the Third Fleet under Captain Gilad Pellaeon. *Harbinger, a Tector-class used by the Galactic Empire at the Battle of Endor. *Harbinger, a Resurgent-class Star Destroyer used by the First Order. *Harbinger courier ship, a type of Imperial courier ship carried aboard the Strikefast. *Harbinger, a Sith dreadnaught during the Great Hyperspace War. *Harbinger, a Hammerhead-class cruiser commandeered by Darth Sion following the Jedi Civil War. *Harbinger, a Separatist landing ship. *Harbinger courier ship, a model of courier ship used by the Galactic Empire. *Harbinger, an assault ship piloted by Bel Att during the Galactic Civil War. *Harbinger, a New Republic Mon Calamari Star Cruiser. *Harbinger, an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer used by the Imperial Remnant. *The Harbinger, a model of speeder bike *"The Harbinger", the ninth episode of Star Wars: The Bad Batch third season
