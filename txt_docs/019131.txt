Tef Shabiak
1063

Tef Shabiak was the most senior executive, and Clone Master, at Arkanian Microtechnologies on the planet Vohai. After Darth Vader was attacked at the facility by Sa Cuis's men, he ordered his aide-de-camp, Erv Lekauf, to remove Shabiak's head to encourage the facility to better its security.
