|pages= |upc= |timeline=1 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=1 }} Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 1 was the first issue of the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series. It was published by Dark Horse Comics on August 9, 1994. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Russ Manning. Artoo-Detoo and See-Threepio search for Luke and Leia, but will their quest leave them dismantled on a decidedly anti-droid Gambler's World? Dark Horse has unearthed a series of strips by the famed Russ Manning and presents them here for the first time ever in color! These strips have been reformatted and expertly retouched by Rick Hoberg, who worked with Manning on the original strips.
