Nanaod Engh
1464

Nanaod Engh was the First Administrator of the New Republic's General Ministry during the Black Fleet Crisis. A close ally of Chief of State Leia Organa Solo, whom she affectionately called "Nanny," he tried to convince Chief Organa Solo to "buff" up her public image.
