Andrin Foods
573

Andrin Foods was a foodstuff manufacturing company that smuggled weapons for the Rebel Alliance. Containers of this company can be seen in mission 5:3. If the player inspects them before proceeding to the main objectives, one can see their true cargo, hidden blasters, and hidden weapons. They don't have a role in the mission objectives. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Foodstuff manufacturers
