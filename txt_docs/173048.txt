Drayk
2316

Drayk was a Human male who served as an abbot at the Dim-U monastery during the height of the Galactic Civil War. Drayk was concerned with order and precision, and he controlled the monastery with a military style. Although he was the abbot of the monastery, he was not overly concerned with the belief of Dim-U, and the monks under his command were involved in an illegal transponder-slicing business, using the religious building as a cover. He used a GoCorp JL7 Elixir Hoverscooter for transportation around Mos Eisley, after a speeder accident rendered him paralysed from the waist down.
