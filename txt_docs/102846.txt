Gallie Wover
1437

Gallie Wover was a female Gunnery Sergeant who served with the New Republic Defense Fleet. She was stationed aboard the cruiser Adamantine when the vessel transported Chief of State Leia Organa Solo to Nam Chorios in 13 ABY, and later died of the Death Seed plague.
