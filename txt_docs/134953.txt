Voyages SF 11
1335

Voyages SF 11 was a roleplaying magazine from 1990. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "A Day at the Auction" and was written and illustrated by James B. King. Category:Voyages SF
