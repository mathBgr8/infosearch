Clone-cam
1005

Clone-cam was a service that saw use by the Galactic Republic. The helmets of the clone troopers serving within the Grand Army of the Republic each had a video recorder in them. Through these clone-cam recorders, officers were able to monitor the activities of the clone troopers under their command through various computers and terminals that had been set up to access the broadcast feed. During the Battle of Felucia, Drake Lo'gaan, Zonder, and Ekria witnessed the deaths of Aayla Secura and Barriss Offee during Order 66 through clone-cam. *Reversal of Fortune Category:Cameras Category:Personal sensors
