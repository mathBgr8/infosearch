Tysys Ryx was a Twi'lek who managed pit fighters in the quasi-legal "blood arena" circuit. At one point in his career he owned the Gamorrean Huggtar, an excellent fighter, until his contract was bought by Zhen Mirat. Category:Males Category:Twi'leks
