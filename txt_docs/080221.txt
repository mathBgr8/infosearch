Question of Master Jrul
1602

The Question of Master Jrul was a famous bit of philosophy from Jedi Master Jrul, formulated some time before the Clone Wars. The Master's question pondered the connection between good and evil. Fellow Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi remembered this question during the Battle of Coruscant in 19 BBYThe New Essential Chronology while facing the Separatist Supreme Commander, Grievous. The Question of Master Jrul was first mentioned in the 2005 novel Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith written by Matthew Stover. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization Category:Jedi code and mantras
