Medal of Order
1958

The Medal of Order was an Imperial starfighter pilot medal awarded for acts of service, particularly in the fourth line of duty. thumb|left|170px|Medal of Order award ceremony. The award was presented in a small ceremony, consisting of eight Imperial officers, seven watching the ceremony from overhead, and one directly giving the nominated TIE pilot the award. The ribbon was primarily blue, although there was a red diamond pattern and then after another area of blue a yellow diamond pattern, with the Imperial Crest being prominently displayed in blue. The medal itself was gold and shield shaped with a banner on top, although there were white triangles on each side of the medal that gave the appearance of fangs.
