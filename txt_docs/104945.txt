Teroch-type gunship
2696

The Teroch-type gunship, named for a Mando'a word "pitiless", was a fast-attack gunship used by Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders during the Mandalorian Wars. If they broke through an enemy line they could surround enemy capital ships in moments. thumb|left|150px When the Arkanian mechanic Jarael (while masquerading as a Jedi) was captured by the invading Mandalorians on Vanquo, she was taken prisoner aboard a Teroch-type gunship to be transported to the Flashpoint Stellar Research Station.
