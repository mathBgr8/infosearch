Control Pain
4921

Control Pain was a Control-based Force power that could be used to reduce the pain suffered as a result of injury or of other reason. Force-users could use Control Pain to suppress the effects of a pain-inflicting injury, and continue to function as if they were not wounded. However, even though the pain was reduced, the injury would remain and still needed to be treated, either through the use of Force healing or through medical methods. It could also be used to ignore pain affecting the individual that was not from a wound.
