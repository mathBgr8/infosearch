Starship Outfitters
1287

Starship Outfitters was a small business run by the Rodian Tulagn on the planet Wroona. On one of the main boulevards of Wroona spaceport, it was packed full of supplies to cater to the needs of spacers. While some tough-looking guards watched the front, Tulagn would work the back counter. Tulagn's Starship Outfitters was also a front for the Rodian's fencing business. It was run from a back room accessible only from an entrance at the end of a long winding alley. The back room was stacked with unmarked crates.
