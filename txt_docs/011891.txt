Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment
6574

Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment is a Star Wars Legends junior novel written by Jude Watson. It is the twelfth volume of the Star Wars: Jedi Apprentice series, following The Deadly Hunter and preceding The Dangerous Rescue. It was published in paperback by Scholastic in February 2001. An evil scientist is imprisoning and torturing Jedi to find the secret behind the Force. She taps their emotions, monitors their actions&hellip;and then drains them of their blood. Qui-Gon Jinn is now her captive. Obi-Wan Kenobi is desperately searching for his Master. Meanwhile, Qui-Gon must match wits with one of the most dangerous enemies he has ever encountered. His survival depends on it. Obi-Wan and Astri voyage to Sorrus to track down the mysterious bounty hunter who has kidnapped Qui-Gon.
