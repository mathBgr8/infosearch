Sanvia Vitajuice Bar
487

The Sanvia Vitajuice Bar was a vitajuice bar on Coruscant. Its clientele consisted almost entire of Ementes, who liked to watch their food being prepared for them. Mara Jade Skywalker and Kyp Durron drank here. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant dining establishments
