|stellar= |endpoints=*Coyn *Tantra |transitpoints= |otherobjects=Coyn - Tantra |junctions=Elrood-Derilyn Trade Route - Rimma Trade Route }} The Coyn Route was a hyperlane located in the Outer Rim Territories. This route branched off the Elrood-Derilyn Trade Route at Coyn in the Elrood sector and connected it with the Rimma Trade Route at Tantra in the Tantra sector. The Coyn route was established by traders of the Elrood sector after the Imperial occupation of the sector. A subtle act of defiance, this route isloted Derilyn economically from the rest of the sector's worlds. Now the main connection of the sector with the rest of the galaxy, Coyn became known as the "Gateway to Elrood Sector." *Operation: Elrood *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection *Lords of the Expanse *Starships of the Galaxy, Saga Edition Category:Outer Rim Territories hyperlanes
