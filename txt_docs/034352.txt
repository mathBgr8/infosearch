Ardax
1742

Ardax was a male Human colonel of the Galactic Empire's Imperial Army who served as second-in-command to the Ambassador Furgan. He also commanded the Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser Vendetta. Ardax was killed when the Vendetta was destroyed during the Battle of Anoth. Ardax was created by author Kevin J.
