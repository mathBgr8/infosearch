Motel Nebulus
957

Motel Nebulus, or Motal Nebuli in Huttese, was a motel in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It also had a nightclub, where the band Figrin D'an II and the New Modal Nodes played in a large glass aquarium in the center of the dance floor. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Bars and clubs Category:Hotels Category:Mos Eisley locations
