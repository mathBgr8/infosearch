Trasse Proving Grounds
1152

The Trasse Proving Grounds were a secret Imperial factory complex on Trasse that constructed All Terrain Armored Transports. As part of the manufacturing process, the AT-ATs were tested in outdoor firing ranges by firing on prisoners. There were two of these ranges, one for infantry and one for armor.
