Eagle (corvette group)
759

Eagle was a group of two CR90 corvettes in service to the Galactic Empire. They attempted to stop the BFF-1 bulk freighter Ashanti from stealing TIE Avengers from a secret Imperial base. However the corvettes were destroyed by Rebel starfighter ace Keyan Farlander. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Galactic Empire starship groups
