Gratenite

Gratenite was a white stone which was carved from cliffs and could be used in the construction of buildings. It is unknown whether it was a material only to be found on Terephon, where it was the building material of choice in the construction of Villa Solis, Ducha Galney's private hunting retreat. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Suns of Fortune Category:Rocks
