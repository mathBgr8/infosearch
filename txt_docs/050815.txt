Conil Barje
1354

Conil Barje was a Human male from Corellia. Conil sold all his possessions in order to move to Tatooine with his wife Camelle and their new-born child, to live the simple life of moisture farmers. He was killed when Tusken Raiders raided the trading site at Mochot Steep.
