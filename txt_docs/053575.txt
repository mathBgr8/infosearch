Jace Paol
1989

Jace Paol was a Human male Corellian who served in the Rebellion during the Galactic Civil War. He was Bria Tharen's second-in-command of Red Hand Squadron, under the title of chief, and followed Commander Tharen on her diplomatic stints, as well as participating in the capture of the Helot's Shackle and the Battle of Ylesia.
