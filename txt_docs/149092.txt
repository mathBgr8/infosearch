Tranira Trell
1301

Tranira Trell was the mother of Vimran Trell. When the Empire invaded their homeworld, Yutusk, Tranira and her husband, Francill were executed as suspected Rebel Alliance sympathizers. Their deaths ensured that Vimran used his position as chef on the Star Destroyer Adjudicator to secretly pass information to the Rebel Alliance. *Cracken's Rebel Operatives Category:Females Category:Humans
