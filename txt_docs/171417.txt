Gargon (Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport)
1154

thumb|right|250px|Transports Gargon fleeing Cruiser [[Lulsla (MC40a light cruiser)|Lulsla.]] Gargon was group of two Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Gargon 1 & 2 were apart of two other groups of starships attempting to flee Calamari Cruiser Lulsla before it succumbed to wounds dealt by the Galactic Empire during the Aftermath of Hoth. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
