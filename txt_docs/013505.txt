Arden founded both Bakura and its capital city, Salis D'aar, and served as Prime Minister for eight years. The planet Arden was named after him. After Arden's death, and faced with diminishing returns, the Bakur Mining Corporation faced an uncertain future; this may have been the factor that led to the power shift from the Corporation to the Bakuran Senate.
