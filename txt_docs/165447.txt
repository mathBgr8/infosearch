Last Hope (CR90 corvette)
2286

Last Hope was a CR90 corvette which served with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The corvette attempted to break through the Imperial blockade on Edan II, but was crippled, crashing onto the planet's surface with the loss of all hands. The wreckage of the ship slid precariously close to the edge of a plateau, perched directly above a ravine. The ship was discovered by a small group of Rebels, who salvaged what was left of the corvette's supplies.
