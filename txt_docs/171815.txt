thumb|right|250px|[[CR90 corvette/Legends|CR90 corvettes Phazer attacking the Habeen, Imperial technology transfer.]] Two Nharwaak CR90 corvettes from group Phazer took part in the Conflict at Mylok IV during the Galactic Civil War. The Nharwaak used Phazer group's corvettes during a last ditch effort to stop the transfer of completed advanced hyperdrive technology from the neighboring Habeen species' Frigate Lendova to the Galactic Empire.
