|published in=Star Wars Tales 6 |reprinted in= |pages=7 |timeline=3 ABY |series= |preceded by="Junkheap Hero" |followed by="Thank the Maker" }} "The Hidden" is an 7-page comic featured in Star Wars Tales 6. It was written by Sean Konot and Scott Morse, illustrated by Scot Morse and lettered by Sean Konot. thumb|left|250px|[[Yoda/Legends|Yoda and his new cooking tool.]] Above the planet Dagobah, an Imperial Star Destroyer launches multiple probe droid pods. One of the droids is almost immediately attacked by a native creature but the droid easily kills it. At one moment the droid detects a little green creature and the droid followed it. Unfortunately for the droid, the little green creature is Yoda. Yoda uses his lightsaber to disable the droid with ease. The Imperials in their Star Destroyer are shocked by the sudden attack. The only problem is that they could not receive any transmissions from the planet, so they are unable to see who or what destroyed their scout. The onboard admiral quickly decides to skip this inhospitable planet on their scouting trip because he does not want to enrage Lord Vader.
