|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY (35:11 GrS)The date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Empire |issue=36 }} Empire 36 is the thirty-sixth issue in the Star Wars: Empire series of comics. It was published on October 12, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics. Devotion to duty has put Imperial Lieutenant Janek Sunber once more on the front lines. First as part of an intense ground battle, where the last of a planet's freedom fighters are desperately holding on, then as a glorified prison guard at an Imperial base where prisoners are put to work for the benefit of the Empire. It's not the career Sunber imagined he'd have in the Imperial Infantry, but a Rebel surprise attack on the base may give Sunber another chance to distinguish himself--or die trying!
