Oodonnaa
979

Oodonnaa was a female Twi'lek information broker on Nar Shaddaa. On his first visit to Nar Shaddaa, she provided Han Solo with directions toward the Corellian sector of the moon and instructions on how to contact Mako Spince. *The Hutt Gambit *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Information brokers Category:Twi'leks
