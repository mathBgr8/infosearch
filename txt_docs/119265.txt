|crewmembers=Skud |registry= |aliases= }} The Voidraker was the personal freighter of the notorious Hutt crime lord Jabba Desilijic Tiure during the Imperial Era. In 0 ABY, one of Jabba's lieutenants, the Nimbanel accountant Mosep Binneed&mdash;who occasionally traveled and worked under Jabba's name&mdash; flew the Voidraker to the desolate Outer Rim world of Orleon, where he had tracked a smuggler named Han Solo who owed his master a sizable debt. Binneed and the ship's crew cornered Solo's Millennium Falcon in a cave system, only for Solo's ship to escape by blasting through the cavern's roof. While this was occurring, the Voidraker was set upon by a massive swarm of stone mites, which threatened to chew through the entire ship. The stone mite infestation rendered the Voidraker unusable and killed the entire crew except for Binneed, who made his way to safety thanks to a lift from Solo. The Voidraker first appeared in Star Wars (1977) 28, written by Archie Goodwin and released in 1979. The ship was illustrated by Carmine Infantino. *Star Wars (1977) 28 Category:Freighters
