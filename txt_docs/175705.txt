Sormus
1670

Sormus was a MB-C1 medium transport belonging to the Imperials. Sormus was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter's vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor to the Corrupter in the Mev system. Sormus' cargo was 'Probe Droids'. The ship was then attacked by Rebel raiders and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:MB-C1 medium transports Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
