Mattri asteroid base
2697

The Mattri asteroid base was an asteroid base in the Mattri system that once served as a location for the headquarters of Garm Bel Iblis's private, anti-Imperial resistance movement, dubbed Peregrine's Nest. When asked about the number of bases they had used Sena replied the current one was "Fourteenth..." "...That's if you count Womrik and the Mattri asteroid bases." However, Lando Calrissian later doubted the accuracy of those statements when he realized how permanent a structure Peregrine's Nest had become.
