Hunter (Victory I-class)
2674

The Hunter was a Victory I-class Star Destroyer belonging to the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Along with Immobilizer 418 cruiser Claw, the Hunter attempted to stop a number of Rebels who were heading to Kothlis after stealing a top secret computer from the Imperial freighter Suprosa.
