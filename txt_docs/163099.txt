The Kaal Connection
5886

"The Kaal Connection" is an article from Star Wars Adventure Journal 7, written by Peter M. Schweighofer and Timothy Zahn, and illustrated by David R. Deitrick. It was updated and reprinted in Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal. Set in 6 ABY, it centers on attempts to claim the food planet Kaal from crime lord Tirgee Benyalle by the Galactic Empire (represented by Kermen), warlord factions (represented by Utoxx Prentioch), and the New Republic (represented by the players).
