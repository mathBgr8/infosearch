Around 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Secure Passage seminar was scheduled to take place on Dlor 3. The Morellian scout and the founder of the Galactic Outdoor Survival School, Barosa Warren, planned to attend the seminar. The Rebel Alliance Major Sisquoc and other Alliance members who were formerly Warren's students at the Galactic Outdoor Survival School planned to confront the Morellian, who held a grudge against Sisquoc in particular and the Alliance in general, at the summit on Dlor 3&mdash;a fact which was noted in an addendum to Alliance SecuriDex datafile on Warren. Dlor 3 was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
