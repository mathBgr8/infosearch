Brandon Badeaux
3012

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=*Artist *Author |companies= |website= }} Brandon Scott Badeaux (b. April 9, 1979) is a comic book artist and author. Hailing from Louisiana, he attended the Savannah College of Art and Design. After working on various titles for DC, he was hired by Dark Horse Comics to draw some of their Star Wars titles. Badeaux penciled and inked three issues of Star Wars: Republic and the four-part story Nomad for Star Wars Tales, before launching the new series Star Wars: Rebellion with author Rob Williams.
