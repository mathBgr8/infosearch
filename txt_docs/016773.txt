It was located within the Kira sector, in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Expansion Region. The planet was a frigid, rocky world with no moons, and orbited the yellow star known as Lazerian. Lazerian IX was found along the trade routes known as the Harrin Trade Corridor and Kira Run. At some point, it was surveyed by the Lazerians of the planet Lazerian IV. Lazerian IX was created by John Terra and introduced in Twin Stars of Kira, a 1993 supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
