|death=March 23, 2018 |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation= |companies= |website= }} Debbie Lee Carrington (December 14, 1959 - March 23, 2018) was an American actress and stuntwoman who played the Ewok Romba in Return of the Jedi. She also played Weechee Warrick in Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure, and worked on the stunts in both films. Footage of her Ewok character in Return of the Jedi was used in Star Wars: Battlefront. She also worked on other George Lucas productions; she is credited as playing "Additional Ducks" in the 1986 film Howard the Duck, and "Idee" in Captain Eo. Carrington went on to have a very successful career in film and television acting.
