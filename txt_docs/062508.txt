Farnym
590

Farnym were sapient creatures noted for their roundness. They had close-cut orange fur and small noses. They had a peculiar odor that smelt of ginger mixed with sandalwood. Leia Organa Solo's copilot aboard the Alderaan, Lieutenant Tchiery, was a Farnym. *The New Rebellion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mammalian sentient species
