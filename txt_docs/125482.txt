Disintegration booth
696

A disintegration booth, also known as a disintegration chamber, was a device used for population control or compulsory self-executions following a decree from the government. These instruments were also employed by the Galactic Empire for executions. These booths operated by disintegrating their victims, and left nothing behind. The use of this apparatus was archaic and frowned upon by many cultures. *Lando Calrissian and the Mindharp of Sharu *Dark Empire Sourcebook Category:Death Category:Technology
