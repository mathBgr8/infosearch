Jagg Island Detention Center
998

The Jagg Island Detention Center was a penal facility on Coruscant, located on the eponymous Jagg Island. Davith Sconn was incarcerated here in 4 ABY and remained a prisoner until at least 16 ABY, suggesting that the facility was taken over intact by the New Republic from the Galactic Empire, and indeed that it remained in operation through the repeated liberations and reconquests of Coruscant between 6 and 10 ABY . *Shield of Lies *Shield of Lies abridged audiobook *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant locations Category:Prisons
