Hakin, a Human, was a native of the planet Alderaan and a skiff racer who lived during the Galactic Civil War. He joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic shortly before the Battle of Yavin in the year 0 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Tatooine to 0 BBY. eventually becoming a corporal. That year, Hakin was among the Rebel troopers stationed aboard the Alderaanian Senator Leia Organa's CR90 corvette, the Tantive IV. Rebel spies transmitted the stolen plans of the Galactic Empire's DS-1 Orbital Battle Station superweapon to the vessel, leading to its pursuit and overtaking by the Dark Lord Darth Vader's personal flagship, the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Devastator. The Rebel troopers unsuccessfully attempted to defend the ship from the invading stormtroopers. Hakin had light skin, brown hair, and blue eyes. He sported a thin mustache. Due to his skiff-racing experience, Hakin knew how to bargain on the black market, a skill valued by the Rebel Alliance. Hakin wore the standard uniform for Alliance Fleet troopers and was armed with a DH-17 blaster pistol.
