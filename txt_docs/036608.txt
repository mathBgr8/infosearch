Supreme Allied Commanders
3580

The Alliance Supreme Allied Commanders, or simply Supreme Commanders, were the senior Rebel Alliance military officers commanding the various military branches of the Rebellion. The seven Supreme Allied Commanders and their functions were: *Fleet Command (Fleet)&mdash;the Alliance Fleet of combat warships and attached support units. *Ordnance and Supply (OaS)&mdash;weapons, equipment and supply procurement, and also R&D *Starfighter Command (StarCom)&mdash;administrative oversight of starfighter forces and pilot training; only a few "rogue" squadrons were under the direct operational control of StarCom *Support Services (Support)&mdash;bases, staging areas, supply ships, safeworlds, and manufacturing centers *Intelligence (Intell)&mdash;espionage and analysis *Special Forces (SpecForces)&mdash;the Alliance's ground troops *Sector Command (SecCom)&mdash;oversight over the semi-autonomous Sector Forces controlled by governments and resistance groups that pledged allegiance to the Alliance The identities of the following Supreme Allied Commanders are known.
