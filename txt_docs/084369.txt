Llitishi
1706

Llitishi was a Wroonian male who was the leader of one of the Cestus Cybernetics Families during the Clone Wars. His particular family was descended from the son of a murderer and the daughter of an ore miner. Around 22 BBY, Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi and the Vippit Doolb Snoil managed to make the Regent give up trusting the Families.
