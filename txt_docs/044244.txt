Drayneen
3115

Drayneen was a Human female member of the Imperial Inquisitorius. With her charm and beauty, Drayneen was able to learn countless secrets and excelled at pulling information from captured prisoners by making them believe that she was on their side. She was also well known for being disliked by her counterparts in Imperial Intelligence. Most operatives from the organization were afraid of her ability to easily coerce almost anyone. Drayneen was one of the few members of the Inquisitorius to be female. She was subtle and ruthless, however, during interrogation she was manipulative and seductive, usually strongly portraying confidence and empathy, this often caused her prisoners to like her initally with them even hoping to make her happy. It is only late in an interrogation that she allows her true cruelty and power to take hold. Despite her talent and effectiveness in interrogating, some Intelligence officers refused to call upon her due to her habit of both scaring and exciting them while working. As an Inquisitor, Drayneen had many talents in the Force such as the ability to affect ones mind, enhance her senses, inflict fear, grip her enemies, defend herself through the Force, hide her allignment in the force, determine the magnitude and allignment of an area, person, or object, and communicate telepathicly. Outside of her abilities, she was able to speak, understand, and write in Basic. She was equipped with a force pike, blaster pistol, an encrypted comlink, a datapad, and a Force talisman.
