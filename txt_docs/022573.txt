Jubilee Wheel (game)
2741

Jubilee Wheel was a betting game popular in many casinos including the Aspre Plunge on the planet Spira. It involved players betting on which of 100 numbers would appear after a spin of a giant wheel. Players placed bets on the number they believed the wheel would stop on. The wheel was then spun. Once the wheel stopped, a win indicator pointed to the winning number. Any player who picked the winning number won the pot, which could be split among multiple winners.
