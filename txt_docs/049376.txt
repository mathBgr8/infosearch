Snap bug
914

Snap bugs were projectile biots used by the Yuuzhan Vong. A variant of the blast bug, the snap bug was the Yuuzhan Vong analog of a Flash-bang grenade. When thrown, the snap bug would explode with a bright flash and a sonic boom. The snap bug would temporarily blind or deafen enemies.
