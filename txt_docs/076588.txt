Mandalorian heavy suit
958

thumb|150px The Mandalorian heavy suit was a stronger version of the Mandalorian combat suit, providing protection to melee damage beyond the scope of what would be expected of a normal combat suit of that weight. This version was used by elite scouts and could be outfitted with various upgrades in addition to the combat suit's capabilities. Its popularity continued through into the Dark Wars and was valued at 10,000 credits at that time. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Mandalorian armor
