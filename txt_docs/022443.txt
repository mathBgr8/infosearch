André Sogliuzzo
4254

André Sogliuzzo is an American voice actor who provided the voices of Captain Typho and Commander Cody, as well as the clone troopers, Alpha-77 and battle droids, for the Star Wars: Clone Wars cartoon. He also provided minor voicework for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic and Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, as well as the voices of clone troopers and Cydon Prax for The Clone Wars. He is credited for "Additional Voices" in Phineas and Ferb: Star Wars, and worked on the LucasArts games Gladius and Secret Weapons Over Normandy. He voiced Captain Slavin on Star Wars Rebels in the episodes Hera's Heroes and In the Name of the Rebellion.
