|type=Smythsewn Hardcover |pages=400 |dimensions=15 x 11.75 inches |weight= |isbn=0-9791580-0-1 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Art of Ralph McQuarrie is a 400-page book showcasing the artwork of Star Wars concept artist Ralph McQuarrie. Limited to a release of 2,000 copies, this book was published by Dreams and Visions Press in April 2007 and debuted at Celebration IV on May 24-28, 2007. The 2,000-copy limited edition publication consists of: *1,625 unsigned, slipcased hardcovers *300 (250 numbered copies for sale) slipcased hardcovers signed by Ralph McQuarrie, Doug Chiang and Ken Ralston *75 (52 lettered for sale) leatherbound hardcovers in deluxe presentation boxes signed by Ralph McQuarrie, Doug Chiang, Ken Ralston and Anthony Daniels Ralph McQuarrie
