Falcon (person)
539

Falcon was the leader of a group of thugs who were hired by Gordunga to kill Pothos Fine and retrieve the Nova Crystal from him. His efforts were thwarted however when a group of passengers from a ship which had just landed on Platform 11-33-D were able to kill both him and his female partner.
