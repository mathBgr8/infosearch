R4-P22
2726

R4-P22 was the designation of the astromech droid attached to Anakin Skywalker's modified Delta-7 Aethersprite-class starfighter, Azure Angel, during the Clone Wars. Although part of the R4-series of astromech droids, R4-P22&mdash;and all other Jedi Temple astromechs&mdash;were based on Obi-Wan Kenobi's droid, R4-P17, which had an R2-style dome rather than the usual conical head dome. R4-P22 flew with Anakin Skywalker during the Battle of Muunilinst, but was destroyed on the surface of Yavin 4 when Asajj Ventress destroyed his ship.
