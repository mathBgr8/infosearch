Domain Vorrik
716

Domain Vorrik was a Domain among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. A notable member of Domain Vorrik was Commander B'shith Vorrik. During the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar, Domain Vorrik's vessels were part of the flotilla sent to attack Zonama Sekot. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Vorrik
