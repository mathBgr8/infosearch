Raava
2227

Raava was a Socorran liquor made from the concentrated extract of zsajhira berries and water from Socorro's neighboring planet, Neftali. Ethra Brewery had a monopoly on the production of raava, which it sold in black bottles made from the planet's well-known black sands.
