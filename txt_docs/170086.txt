Thermal detonator trooper (Galactic Empire)
2576

The thermal detonator trooper, also known as a grenade trooper, was an artillery unit used by the Galactic Empire. The trooper used a mortar device to lob thermal detonators into the desired attack area. These units were very effective against shielded and mechanized units, but ineffective against buildings and non-mechs. They wore black uniforms with a helmet. Grenade Troopers were deployed on many battlefields during the Galactic Civil War. Specifically, at least four troopers assisted the Empire during the Occupation of Bespin. Darth Vader ordered the recruitment of additional troopers from troop centers during the war to use in battle against the Rebel Alliance.
