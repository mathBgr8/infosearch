Kyood Vurd
1709

Kyood Vurd was a short Ugnaught businessman who ran a cantina on the moon of Nar Shaddaa around the time of the Invasion of Naboo. He recently had a scuffle with Jedi Masters Mace Windu and Depa Billaba when he attempted to kill a street-vendor on his turf.
