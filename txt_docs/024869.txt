Elix
1457

The elix were rare predatory birds that hunted in flocks. During the Imperial era, their homeworld was threatened by a supernova explosion. In order to preserve the species, a population was established on the Imperial Species Preservation Zone planet of Dar'Or. There, the elix were even deadlier predators, as their new homeworld had much lower gravity than their old world did. The elix preyed on the native sentients, the Ri'Dars.
