Unmanned energy refinery
818

An unmanned energy refinery was a type of deep space industrial plant. The refineries were used to manufacture subatomic knots of space-time essential as components in gravitational devices used throughout the galaxy, like repulsorlift generators, inertial compensators and tractor beam projectors.
