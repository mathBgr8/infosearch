Zavirk
1134

Zavirk was a male Human who served as a trooper in the Galactic Alliance Guard during the Second Galactic Civil War. He professed to have joined the army in order to see the galaxy. On the orders of Colonel Jacen Solo, Zavirk monitored the conversations between Five World Prime Minister Dur Gejjen and Chief of State Cal Omas, and later took part in the mission to Vulpter. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Galactic Alliance Guard personnel Category:Humans Category:Males
