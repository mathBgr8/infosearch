Bantha Clan
1390

The Bantha Clan was an initiate training clan of the New Jedi Order named after the bantha, a creature found throughout the galaxy. In 35 ABY, it consisted mostly of students who were the between the ages of ten to twelve.Legacy of the Force: Inferno In 40 ABY Vekki, a member of Bantha Clan, was saved by Zekk during the raid on the Jedi Academy.
