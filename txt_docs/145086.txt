Garfin

The Garfin was a New Republic warship sent to aid General Garm Bel Iblis, Booster Terrik, and the Errant Venture at Yaga Minor in an attempt to steal a copy of the Caamas Document. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the New Republic
