Chellemi Chuovvick
2641

Chellemi Chuovvick was a female Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Chellemi Chuovvick, as a Force-sensitive, was trained at the Coruscant Jedi Temple. She eventually graduated from the academy and was selected by a Jedi Master to train as a Padawan. After completing her Jedi Trials, Chuovvick was raised to the rank of Jedi Knight by the Jedi High Council. During her time as a Knight, Chuovvick was partnered with fellow Jedi Empatojayos Brand and Bultar Swan and dispatched to the war-torn Sepan system as part of the third Jedi team sent to negotiate peace in the endless civil war. Negotiations dragged on for a month, bringing about a short-lived peace. Following the Jedi's departure however, the world erupted back into violence as debate raged on whether to secede from the Galactic Republic and join the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
