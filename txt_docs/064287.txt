|death=December 11, 2023 |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Illustrator |companies=Dark Horse Comics |website= }} Ian Gibson (1946 &ndash; December 11, 2023) was a comic book illustrator who worked on Star Wars: Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire and Star Wars: Droids: Rebellion. Outside of his Star Wars work, he was known for his contributions to 2000 AD, including Judge Dredd, The Ballad of Halo Jones and Robo Hunter. Category:Comic book pencillers Category:Comic book inkers
