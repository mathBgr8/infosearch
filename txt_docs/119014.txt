Dragon Queen
2044

The Dragon Queen was Queen Mother Tenel Ka's personal Hapan Battle Dragon. It served as her flagship during the Second Galactic Civil War, in the battles of Hapes and Kashyyyk. It also appeared during the battle over Shedu Maad, where a nanovirus was released on it to kill all of those with Hapan Royal Blood.
