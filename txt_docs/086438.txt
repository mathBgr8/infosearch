Natinati
1163

Natinati was a planet in Wild Space. One of the main cities was Poborandurannum, where Jabba the Hutt once rented a secret chamber. By 25 ABY, Natinati boasted a population of less than a million sentients. *Jabba the Hutt: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in Wild Space
