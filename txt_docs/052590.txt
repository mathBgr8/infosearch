Hras'kkk'rarr
2517

Hras'kkk'rarr was the native language of the Srrors'tok, a feline sentient species from the planet Jankok of the Outer Rim Territories' Bozhnee sector. &mdash; Based on corresponding data for the Jankok system It was a very sophisticated combination of sign language and a complex series of growls, snarls, clicks, and other sounds that were easy for the voice boxes of the Srrors'tok to produce&mdash;although that same anatomical structure also made it very difficult for them to speak Galactic Basic Standard. The Srrors'tok bounty hunter Tyionsis Cex, who was active during the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, had developed an ability to throw his voice, projecting apparent growling noises indicative of the presence of other Srrors'tok in his vicinity.Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Hras'kkk'rarr was first mentioned in the 1993 Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game supplement Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim, which was written by Simon Smith and Eric S.
