Rookies: Day Long Remembered
2703

Rookies: Day Long Remembered was the planned third webstrip of the Rookies series. It is to be written by Pablo Hidalgo and was set to begin in 2008. Along with The Force Unleashed webstrip, it was originally announced as being available for free on StarWars.com, but was later revealed to be a Hyperspace exclusive. Hyperspace was shut down in 2011. In 2025, Hidalgo confirmed that a script was written.
