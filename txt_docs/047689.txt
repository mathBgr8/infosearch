Tarhead
437

Tarhead was a derogatory term for someone addicted to black membrosia, referring to the thick consistency and dark color of the substance. During the Swarm War, many of the galaxy's insectoid species became tarheads, and were used by the Killiks to attempt takeovers of their home systems. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War Category:Slang
