Vor-cal Feast
1233

thumb|right|360px|Vor-cal Feast The Vor-cal Feast was held before the day of the Vor-cal annual hunting safari. When House Melantha hosted the Vor-cal prior to the Battle of Endor, the feast was held in the grand hall of House Melantha Orbital Hunting Lodge.
