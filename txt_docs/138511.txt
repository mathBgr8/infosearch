Fernandin Scouting Expedition
770

The Fernandin Scouting Expedition was an exploratory body that inhabited the Great Massassi Temple on Yavin 4 after the Rebel Alliance evacuated the base several months after the Battle of Yavin. The expedition remained there until after the Battle of Endor, when they ran out of funding. *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition Category:Outer Rim Territories organizations
