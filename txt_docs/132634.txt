Stavan
2948

Stavan was an officer under the command of Admiral Galak Fyyar of the Empire Reborn, a faction of the Imperial Remnant. Holding a rank of commander, he was stationed in an outpost located on the planet Kejim around 12 ABY. During that year,Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast places the events in-game 8 years after the Battle of Endor, which takes place in 4 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology. Therefore, the game takes place in 12 ABY. around the time a New Republic agent named Kyle Katarn was investigating the outpost, Admiral Fyyar made a transmission to the outpost, in which he expressed disappointment with Stavan's performance and instructed that Stavan be removed from command and reassigned. During his time investigating the Kejim outpost, Katarn stumbled upon the transmission involving Stavan and several other messages while examining a communications console. Stavan was mentioned in the 2002 video game Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast, in which he did not appear but was mentioned in a transmission. While the communications console containing the message could be accessed by Kyle Katarn&mdash;the player character&mdash;finding the console was optional and did not affect the outcome of the game.
