During the Galactic Civil War, it hosted detention facilities used by the Galactic Empire. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Human smuggler Zayl Braith, who had been imprisoned for several weeks aboard the Imperial Immobilizer 418 Interdictor-class Heavy Cruiser Tory's Catcher after being captured in the Noonian sector, was transferred to the detention facilities on Rol Two. There, however, Braith escaped from the Empire and traveled off-world, with Imperial authorities continuing to hunt for her by 2 ABY. Rol Two was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
