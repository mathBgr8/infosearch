Widow Maker
1639

Widow Maker was a modified Incom Corporation "Explorer" Scout Ship used by brother and sister bounty hunters Alic and Vika Surn. In exchange for their transport, the Surns paid a five percent gap to the Neuvalis syndicate, the ship's true owner. It was armed with one dual laser cannon, and included two restraint capsules for holding the bounties the Surns' apprehended. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Category:Bounty hunter starships Category:Explorer Scout Ships
