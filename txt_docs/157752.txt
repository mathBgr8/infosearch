Adrienne Wilkinson
2818

Adrienne Marie Wilkinson is an American actress of Irish, Scottish, English, Cherokee, Danish, and German heritage. Wilkinson provided the likeness, expressions and voice of Maris Brood in Star Wars: The Force Unleashed as well as in the Nintendo Wii version of Star Wars: The Force Unleashed II, as well as the voices of several ambient characters in Star Wars: The Old Republic, and the Daughter in Star Wars: The Clone Wars.
