Executive Results

|ledby= |associations= |parent=Syndicate One |subsid= |headquarters= |locations=Near the Perlemian Trade Route |role=Front company for Syndicate One |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Executive Results was a front company for Syndicate One. It was responsible for hiring and outfitting mercenaries to perform operations dictated by Saujutta-Ok-Topii's inner circle. Their offices were located in starports near the Perlemian Trade Route in the Expansion Region. Officially they were equipment retailers, but underneath secretly smuggeled weapons inside machinery and vehicles. Executive Results used merceneries as security guards, smugglers, messengers, and diversions. Few involved reailzed their cargoes contained weapons from Syndicate One. General cargo was brought to the planet Valgauth, where weapons were inserted and hidden inside. Once packaged, the cargo and its hidden contraband was shipped to an Executive Results office and then deliverd to the customer.
