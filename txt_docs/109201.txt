Hungry Ghost
2284

}} The Hungry Ghost was a extremely modified Carrack-class cruiser owned by the pirate salvager Redkihl Rokk. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the freighter attempted to salvage scrap from the destroyed Death Star. The cruiser, along with Rokk, were destroyed by Han Solo in 0 ABY.
