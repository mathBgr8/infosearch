Kai-kan
1349

In lightsaber combat, a kai-kan was a reenactment of a famous, usually ancient, very dangerous, lightsaber or even sword battle, which only very well trained Jedi attempted to perform. High Parry Hold the blade horizontal with the blade running just below your chin. Overhead Parry Hold the saber so the blade is parallel to your spine and then shift it slightly to your strong side to make it on that side. Drop Stance Bend your knee and put your weight on it and stick your other leg outward with part of your weight resting on it but keep most of your weight on your bent knee. *Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Lightsaber combat
