Porgo Goo
2084

Porgo Goo was a short, plump, furry native of Chortose. Porgo ran a small repairs shop in the Minos Cluster with his brothers, who all had natural ability for engineering and repairing starships. None of them had the technical understanding to back up their practical ability but the Goo brothers were not embarrassed by this. The Goo brothers charged the standard rate for their work, but if properly motivated (not with credits, but dares or bets), could complete their work in a third of the usual time needed. The Goo brothers were also perfectly willing to install illegal ship modifications so long as they viewed the modification as being 'cool'. Porgo was a playful being and enjoyed social gatherings, especially in dangerous cantinas.
