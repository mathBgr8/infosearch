|masters= |apprentices= }} Tammia Hollusen, also known as Tammi, was a Human who, in her youth, lived and worked for the Galactic Empire on Metalorn. She aided in Princess Leia Organa's escape from Orman Tagge. Tammi planted a single flower on Metalorn to show the first seeds of rebellion to the Empire. Hollusen eventually joined the Peace Brigade, but ultimately left the group.
