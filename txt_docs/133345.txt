Ghrakhowsk
514

In the Trandoshan tradition of the life debt, the being to whom the debt was sworn was referred to as the Trandoshan's ghrakhowsk. Marn Hierogryph became Slyssk's ghrakhowsk after pretending to save Slssyk's life by pushing him out of the way of a falling piece of machinery. *Knights of the Old Republic 13 Category:Trandoshan culture
