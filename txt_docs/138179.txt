Quintama
1193

Quintama was captain of the Melida Liberation Force just before the Twenty-First Battle of Zehava, in which he was killed. Quintama was the husband of Pinani and father of Renei and Wunana. A hologram of Quintama recorded the day before the Twenty-First Battle of Zehava appeared in the Hall of Evidence on Melida/Daan. *Jedi Apprentice: The Defenders of the Dead *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Melida/Daan residents Category:Officers
