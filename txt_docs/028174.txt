Oceanroot
1472

Oceanroot was a type of beverage found at local taverns on the planet Shaum Hii located in the galaxy's Outer Rim Territories. Members of the sentient Kian'thar species native to the planet often socialized over steaming cups of oceanroot. Oceanroot was mentioned in Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies, a supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games in 1995.
