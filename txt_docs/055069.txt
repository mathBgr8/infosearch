J'an Ane Jinder
1490

J'an Ane Jinder was a female Jedi Guardian of the Galactic Republic and somewhat of an archaeologist as well. Jinder traveled the galaxy, seeking out ancient or rare artifacts that might be of some interest to the Jedi Order. On one such quest for an artifact from the planet Malastare, J'an Ane wound up on Mrlsst, homeworld of the Mrlssi. In a Mrlssi spaceport, while attempting to charter a starship, she was offered a discount on the ship if she could solve the Mrlssi steward's riddle. The riddle involved the Mrlssi ship designers, their starships and three offworld clients.
