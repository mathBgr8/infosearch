Speedy
2954

"Speedy" was the nickname of a Sullustan pilot in the Rebel Alliance who served aboard the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Independence during the Galactic Civil War. He was called Speedy by the other pilots as they could not pronounce his real name. In 1 BBY, Speedy completed his training and entered active duty, successfully completing two missions shortly after Operation Strike Fear. The Sullustan was present in the pilot's lounge of the MC80 Home One type Star Cruiser for a conversation between Red Squadron pilots about their preferred Imperial targets, with Speedy mentioning his particular dislike for Assault Gunboats. A cultural divide separated the Sullustan pilot from Red Squadron, with his fellow Rebel pilots understanding neither his jokes nor his references to scarns. Despite this, Speedy returned to the company of Red Squadron's pilots in 0 BBY after receiving special reconnaissance training due to his Sullustan navigational skills, witnessing Keyan Farlander's attempt to use a Force mind trick on Samuel Raider and recruitment of rookie pilots such as Casal Marskan with promises of power through the Force.
