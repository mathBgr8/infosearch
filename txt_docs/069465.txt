Ciran
734

Ciran was the capital city of the planet Cirrus. It was shaped like five pointed star and the Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn described it has having many colorful awnings. The city had two levels, that were linked by turbolifts. In 39 BBY, Qui-Gon Jinn, Obi-Wan Kenobi, Adi Gallia, and Siri Tachi traveled to Ciran to search for Talesan Fry. *Secrets of the Jedi Category:Capital cities
