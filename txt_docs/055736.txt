|published in=Star Wars Tales 16 |reprinted in= |pages=2 |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Long, Bad Day" is a 2-page comic that first appeared in Star Wars Tales 16 and was collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4. The script was written by Mike Denning, art was by Lucas Marangon and letters were by Steve Dutro. It takes place in 0 BBY. As Darth Vader attempts to return to the first Death Star in his TIE fighter following the battle of Yavin, he is confronted by the mocking spirit of Obi-Wan Kenobi. Furiously, Vader thumps his fist on the control panel where Kenobi has appeared, causing one of his wings to fall off. Again Vader attempts to strike the spirit of Kenobi, this time causing him to fire a torpedo into Grand Moff Tarkin's escape pod.
