The Admiral's List
847

The Admiral's List was an official publication of the Republic Navy during the Mandalorian Wars, covering news related to the war effort from the Navy's viewpoint. The Admiral's List is one of a number of "news organs" based on the Taris Holofeed that are included as extras in monthly issues of Knights of the Old Republic starting with issue 13.
