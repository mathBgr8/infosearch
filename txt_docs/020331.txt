During the Waymancy Storm of 7811 BBY, the Neshtabine nest was associated with the Waymancy Hollow, fighting against the Galactic Republic. It was eventually routed by the Republic Rocket-Jumpers at Tantara. An Imperial factory on Neshtab was creating stormtrooper droids until it was destroyed by Luke Skywalker after the Battle of Hoth.
