Symt
836

Symt was the capital city of the planet Darstell 4. The Imperial Governor's palace was located in thecenter of the city. Symt had a fairly high level of technology, with approximately one million beings calling it home. It contained a modest underworld element, as well as a fair amount of crime, although its small size kept things from getting tremendously out of hand. It had a large Imperial presence, with many Stormtroopers patrolling the city whenever Governor Bellows requested it. Category:Capital cities Category:Galactic Empire cities
