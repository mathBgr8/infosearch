Dap Nechel
1295

Dap Nechel was an alien engineer onboard the Kuari Princess in 0 BBY who enjoyed watching B'shingh matches between Celia Durasha and Detien Kaileel. When he learned that Detien had smuggled weapons to members of the Rebel Alliance he aided Detien and Celia's escape from the Princess. *Riders of the Maelstrom *Classic Adventures: Volume Three *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Engineers Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Kuari Princess crewmembers Category:Males
