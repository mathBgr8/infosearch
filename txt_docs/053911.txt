Mon Delindo

The Mon Delindo was a MC80 Star Cruiser of the New Republic Defense Fleet. During the hunt for Warlord Zsinj, the Mon Delindo was one of three Mon Calamari Star Cruisers assigned to General Han Solo's Solo Fleet.
