|pages=92 |upc= |timeline=0 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars Manga: A New Hope |issue=2 }} Star Wars Manga: A New Hope 2 is the second volume in the Star Wars Manga: A New Hope series. It was illustrated by Hisao Tamaki and was based upon the original story by George Lucas. The cover art is by Adam Warren. In August 1999, the comic won the Eisner Award for Best U.S. Edition of Foreign Material.
