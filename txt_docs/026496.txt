|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters= |locations=CoCo District, Coruscant |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Republic }} The CoCo District Constabulary was the local police force of the Collective Commerce District, or CoCo District, on the planet Coruscant. The Constabulary operated during the waning years of the Galactic Republic and was separate from the planet's primary police agency, the Coruscant Security Force. Information on criminals or other tips could be submitted to the Constabulary via their hotline at CDC.403.432.1156.321. In 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:5:16, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. three inmates escaped from the CoCo Penitentiary including the Human con artists Achk Med-Beq and Dannl Faytonni, and a Borlovian smuggler named Llollulion. The CoCo Constabulary put out an alert and encouraged citizens to contact them should the three escapees be spotted. The CoCo District Constabulary first appeared in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #56, an entry of the HoloNet News written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and released on May 16, 2002.
