Siege of Wann Tsir
2005

The Siege of Wann Tsir was a battle of the Galactic Civil War. The Galactic Empire laid siege to the planet Wann Tsir, using at least one Dedicated Siege Platform commanded by Commander Titus Klev. During the engagement, Klev accidentally discovered an Alliance agent attempting to sabotage the platforms deflector shields, earning him commendations from Imperial High Command. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Battles in the Sepan sector Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
