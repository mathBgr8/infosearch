*Jungles *Mountains *Plains *Seas |water= |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species=Palmiens |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Palme du Tigre was a planet with a wide variety of terrain, which included jungles, deserts, plains, mountains, and seas. Its indigenous humanoids, the Palmiens, had a pre-spatial level of technology. The Empire trained numerous non-clone stormtrooper recruits on the planet. The final test of their training was to commit genocide of the native population. "Palme du Tigre" is French for "Tiger's Palm".
