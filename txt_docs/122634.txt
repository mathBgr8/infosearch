Sedan Anang
981

Sedan Anang was a male xenobiology professor who accompanied author Rogar Farnoster when he was writing his book The Slug Named Grendel. Anang was a professor at the University of Sanbra shortly before the Clone Wars and was one of the few beings who saw the 80-meter space slug in the Borkeen Belt.
