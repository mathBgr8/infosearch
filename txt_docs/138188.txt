Twenty-Second Battle of Zehava
1538

The Twenty-Second Battle of Zehava occurred circa 91 BBY, three years after the Battle of Bin. It was one of the many struggles of the Melida/Daan Civil War on the planet of Melida/Daan. During the battle, Renei and Wunana were killed. *Jedi Apprentice: The Defenders of the Dead Category:Battles of the Melida/Daan Civil War
