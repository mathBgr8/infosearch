Nib Maroon
1150

Nib Maroon was a female Rutian Twi'lek. Maroon was a singer who traveled with a group of itinerant musicians in her early career, but later she was hired by Rava Teb'lya to work at the Rose Nebula. She worked at the Nebula as a singer and a member of the nebula's undercover security team. *Ultimate Adversaries Category:Females Category:Musicians Category:Rutian Twi'leks
