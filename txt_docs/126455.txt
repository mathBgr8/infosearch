Xucaabo
1533

Xucaabo was a male Dug who worked for Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He was also employed by the Pixelito Puppeteers Union, and was a skilled and well-known puppeteer. A bounty was placed on his head by the Senator of Malastare, Ask Aak, for falling heavily on debt.
