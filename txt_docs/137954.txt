Amber (smuggler)
965

Amber was a smuggler in the organization of Mazzic. She was the pilot of the Skyclaw.The Last Command In 9 ABY, she escorted Mazzic on the Distant Rainbow, along with Griv, to a smugglers' meeting organized by Talon Karrde on the planet Hijarna.
