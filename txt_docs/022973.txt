First Army
3011

The First Army, also known as Azure Hammer Command or the Coruscant Sector Army, was a Republic and later Imperial Sector Army tasked with defending of the Coruscant sector. At its zenith, this mighty fleet boasted the Executor-class Star Dreadnought Whelm along with 57 other capital ships. Throughout the Galactic Civil War, Anaxes served as the fleet's primary command center. Azure Hammer Command, like all other forces in Sector Zero, was under control by Grand Admiral Osvald Teshik. Teshik's command was revoked after his failure to address the threat posed by the Rebel privateer Far Orbit, which occured shortly after the Battle of Yavin. Despite the Grand Admiral's dismissal, Anaxes successfully fended off the New Republic for numerous years.
