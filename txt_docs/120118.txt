Prior to the Battle of Yavin, the Human male Boelo, a henchman of the crime lord Jabba the Hutt, owned Worra as a pet. Worra was first mentioned in the lore for the Boelo character's card in the 1998 Special Edition Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
