Ryder Windham
13121

Ryder Windham is the author of over sixty Star Wars books. He was also an editor of Star Wars comics for Dark Horse Comics. In the mid-1990s Ryder Windham was Dark Horse's group editor for licensed titles.The Secrets of Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire He was also said to have overseen the Dark Horse series Heir to the Empire, Jabba the Hutt, River of Chaos, Splinter of the Mind's Eye, and Shadow of the Empire, and issues of Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron (which were all also credited as being edited by various other editors at Dark Horse in the books themselves). Windham also edited and wrote scripts for Star Wars: Droids released by Dark Horse. Windham worked closely at the time with Allan Kausch and Sue Rostoni at Lucas Licensing to maintain continuity between the comics, books and other Star Wars properties.
