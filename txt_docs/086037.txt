Wurf'al
1094

Wurf'al was a male Bothan who was a captain onboard the Admiral Ackbar who was related to Avke Saz'ula by marriage, which gave him a disdain for Jedi, and was the great nephew of Admiral Nek Bwua'tu. He was killed by Gorog assassins trying to save a bust of the "great" Admiral. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothans Category:Galactic Alliance Defense Fleet officers Category:Males
