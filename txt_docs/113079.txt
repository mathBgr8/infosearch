Inferno firefighting robo
1788

The Inferno firefighting robo was developed by the Corporate Sector Authority to put out fires. They were very tough and were able to stand even the hottest of flames. They used chemical foam to extinguish fires, and also had a searchlight and a loudspeaker. The Inferno consisted of a carrier unit and four smaller firefighters. The carrier unit would convey the firefighting units to the conflagration where they would deploy. The units could interlock together to provide more effective firefighting. The units were protected by tough armor with heat-resistant coating. They came equipped with a laser cutter to open debris and heavy gripper arms to drag unconscious victims to safety.
