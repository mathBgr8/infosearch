Vojak
1076

Vojak was a Humanoid male, whose brother was assassinated by the assassin droid, C-3PX, on Bonadan. After chasing C-3PX down on Hosk Station in an attempt to repay the droid for what he had done, Vojak was dispatched with a shot from the rear of C-3PX's head, effectively disintegrating the avenger. *Droids (1994) 3 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males
