It had a toxic atmosphere in which Humans required a breath mask. Axtria was first mentioned in the gamemaster’s notes for the standalone solitaire adventure "The Void Terror", published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 3. In the example involving Axtria, the adventure’s rules are explained through how the main character, Selnia Harbright, performs various actions. At one point, Selnia wants to determine whether the atmosphere on Axtria is breathable. Her Planetary Systems skill is 5D, and the gamemaster sets the difficulty number at 15. Selnia’s player rolls five dice and gets a total of 18. Selnia recalls that Humans require a breath mask to survive in Axtria’s atmosphere. Category:Planets in the Core Worlds
