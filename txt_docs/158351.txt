Jester (Carrack-class)
2520

Jester was a Carrack-class light cruiser belonging to the Rebel Alliance. In 3 ABY, the Jester observed an attack on an Imperial cargo transfer area in the Yrrna system by their Nami allies. The Nami were demonstrating the combat superiority of the TIE/D Defender, which they had stolen from the Imperials in an earlier raid with the intent of producing such craft for themselves and subsequently selling them to the Rebels. A retaliatory strike led by Tan Maarek Stele failed to save the cargo transfer area, but saved the Imperial personnel there from certain slaughter by the Nami.
