Battle armor
6583

thumb|An example of battle armor Battle armor was some of the heaviest armor in widespread use as of the Jedi Civil War. Designed for use by heavy militias, it had the protection needed to keep a soldier alive during ranged combat with massive weapons. Battle armor was constructed from a mesh of metal or composite plates and a padded jumpsuit. Variants of the armor included less plates and more padding for a lighter, though less protective armor, and heavier plating with molded pieces to fit the wearer. Though the armor was available unmodified, most users personalized their armor, usually cobbling together gear from various sources.Star Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook Battle armor was also used in other eras. During the Galactic Civil War, battle armor was a classification of armor.
