|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 4 |reprinted in= |pages=20 |timeline=19 BBYStar Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 4 dates its stories to just before and during the events of , which The Essential Atlas dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Descent" was a comic story from Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 4. Shortly after Order 66 was executed, Triton Squad was given a new order: destroy the Wookiees. Approaching a group of elders and pups, the clone troopers were met with an unpleasant surprise as Tarfful and Chewbacca swooped down with a team of warriors. Engaging in ferocious combat, the Wookiee team soon dropped down into the Shadowlands of Kashyyyk, leaving Triton Squad to follow. Setting a maze of traps involving local flora and fauna, the Wookiees soon narrowed the squad down to a few troopers, quickly dispatched by an ambush.
