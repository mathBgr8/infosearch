Alderaanian armed forces
738

The Alderaanian armed forces was the military organization tasked with defending Alderaan. On the day of the end of the Alderaan Ascendancy Contention, fifteen-year old Pello Scrambas joined the armed forces. Years later, the Royal Alderaan Civil Fleet was formed to protect Alderaan during the Separatist Crisis and the Clone Wars. It is unclear what relation it had to the rest of Alderaan's military.
