|publisher=LucasArts |release date=November 14, 2006 |closed= |upc= |engine= |genre=MMORPG |modes= |ratings= |platforms=Microsoft Windows |series= |basegame=Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided |expansions= |director= |producer= |designer= |programmer= |artist= |writer= |composer= |timeline= |prev= |next= }} Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Online Adventures is a compilation release of the massively multiplayer online role-playing game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided. It was released on November 16, 2006. It includes the base game, An Empire Divided, along with all three expansion packs, Jump to Lightspeed, Rage of the Wookiees, and Trials of Obi-Wan, and a behind-the-scenes DVD featuring developer interviews, concept art and various images and videos from the development of the game. As in the previous compilations, it includes an exclusive in-game item, this time an AT-RT vehicle as seen in Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith.
