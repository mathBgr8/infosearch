Quicksilver (Iaco Stark)
2208

|navy= |fleet= |owners=Iaco Stark |captains= |crewmembers=Adi Gallia |registry= |aliases= }} The Quicksilver was a private starship owned by Iaco Stark at the time of the Stark Hyperspace War. During the war, it was hijacked by Jedi Master Adi Gallia, who used it to transport Minister Nute Gunray and Senator Finis Valorum back to Coruscant from Troiken.
