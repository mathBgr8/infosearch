Greg Keyes
1502

Greg Keyes is the author of three New Jedi Order novels as well as the New Jedi Order short story Emissary of the Void. He was born in Meridian, Mississippi, to a large, diverse, storytelling family. He received degrees in anthropology from Mississippi State and the University of Georgia before becoming a full-time writer. He lives in Savannah, Georgia. {| !Title !! Format !!
