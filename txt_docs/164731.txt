Jolee's robe is an equippable item of clothing the player may discover in Obsidian Entertainment's 2004 video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords. Were the player character to wear this garment, it would boost their Charisma attribute, which augments their persuasive air, and it slightly bolsters the regeneration of their personal Force reservoir, which allows the use of Force powers. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Jedi robes
