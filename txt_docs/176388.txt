Gyrocopter
1516

Kleb Zellock owned this gyrocopter, that he mostly used as a transport to sell Nergon-14 to the Galactic Empire illegally. Trying to escape from an exploding Nergon mine, Kleb was shot with his own Mini-stunner. Yorpo Mog, Jann Tosh, Threepio, Artoo, Julpa and Sollag Den loaded Kleb into the cargo hold and threw his Keshels overboard to keep them from weighing the copter down. With the reduced weight, the vessel carried them all out of the mine to safety. *The Lost Prince: A Droid Adventure Category:Skiffs
