|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=10 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Dark Empire II |issue=6 |next=Empire's End 1 }} Dark Empire II 6 was the sixth and final issue of the 6 part Star Wars: Dark Empire II series of comics. It was released by Dark Horse Comics on May 23, 1995. Han Solo and Princess Leia have taken refuge with their twins on the planet New Alderaan. But refuge, it seems, eludes the family when Emperor Palpatine and an AT-AT battalion come hunting for Rebel officers. Worry not, Leia! They don't want your lives&hellip; they just want your children! The events of this issue were adapted in the Dark Empire II (audio) radio drama, which featured the music of John Williams' score from the films.
