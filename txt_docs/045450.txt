Jer'Jo Cam'Co
2126

Jer'Jo Cam'Co was a male Chiss and one of the founding syndics of the Chiss Ascendancy. He proposed the creation of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Fleet after exploration efforts revealed several resource rich worlds. As a result of this, many old records found in the Expeditionary Library held his name. At least seven Chiss vessels were named for him, as well as two star systems, including the Colonial Station Cam'co.The Essential Atlas Jacen Solo encountered the name several times when using the Library to find Zonama Sekot.
