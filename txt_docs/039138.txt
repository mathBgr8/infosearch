The Imperium was peacefully absorbed by the growing influence of Coruscant several centuries before the year 25,053 BBY, when the Galactic Republic was founded, though the Imperium's influence could still be felt in the millennia afterwards&mdash;the Azure sector and the Republic Military's Azure Hammer Command took their names from the Azure Imperium.
