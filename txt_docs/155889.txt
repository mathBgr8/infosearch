The Lovers' Dilemma
800

"The Lovers' Dilemma" was a famous piece of art created by the artist known as Walls who was a member of the Professiona Artists Guild. It was essentially a mixed media mood chamber and holographic painting. The piece was proudly displayed in the guild's permanent residence within the Perma on the planet Elrood. There were two similarly named events on the planet: The Lovers' Conflict and the The Lovers' Embrace. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Paintings and posters
