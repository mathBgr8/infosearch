O-Melie
2455

O-Melie was a female Human from Kegan that was the wife of V-Nen and the mother of Jedi Knight Olana Chion (born O-Lana). V-Nen and O-Melie subvertly contacted the Jedi Council, which then sent Jedi Masters Qui-Gon Jinn, Adi Gallia and their Padawans, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Siri Tachi, to Kegan. However, the leading members of Kegan's government, known as the Benevolent Guides, were Force-sensitive and could see into the future. They saw Kegan being taken over by Masked Soldiers and the Galactic Empire.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia They kidnapped Olana and sent her to the Re-Learning Circle to enforce upon her the ways of The Learning. However, the Jedi were able to rescue Olana after Kenobi and Tachi managed to get captured and sent to the Learning Circle. After finding Olana Force-sensitive, the Jedi brought her back to Coruscant for training, while V-Nen and O-Melie adopted a young orphaned boy by the name of V-Davi who had escaped with Kenobi and Tachi from the Re-Learning Circle. O-Melie and her husband were part of a covert resistance against their leaders, and after the Jedi exposed what was going on at the Re-Learning Circle, the city's populace peacefully ousted their Benevolent Guides. *Jedi Apprentice: The Fight for Truth *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Keganites
