Warra nut
1073

Warra nuts were a green nut provided with alcoholic beverages at many bars on Coruscant and across the galaxy, including the Kragget. They had many different flavors, ranging from Hot 'n' spicy to salt 'n' sour. *RC-1136 a.k.a.
