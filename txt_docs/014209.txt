Its continents were composed of hardened duranium and its shallow oceans of molten duranium. In 2,000,000 BBY, an alien named Ssiskor came to Kriekaal, but perished in a catastrophe wrapped in legend of supernatural forces, which claim the oceans themselves destroyed him. New continents were constantly forming on the planet. The continent Raltez finished its formation only by 3 ABY.
