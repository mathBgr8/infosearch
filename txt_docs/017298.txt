It was located in the Velm system, a part of the Halori sector in the Trans-Hydian portion of the Mid Rim. Zelmaadine was mentioned in Velmor: Royalty and Rebellion, a roleplaying supplement written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards.com on March 6, 2003 for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Velm system, and therefore Zelmaadine, in grid square P-7.
