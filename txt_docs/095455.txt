Steward of the Imperial Personal Archives
522

The Steward of the Imperial Personal Archives was the being who controlled all access to and input in the Imperial Personal Archives. At the time of the Battle of Endor the position was held by Sate Pestage, who used it to seal all records on Palpatine's family and personal relationships. *Dark Empire Sourcebook Category:Galactic Empire ranks
