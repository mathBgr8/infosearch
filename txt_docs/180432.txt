A-23 Treeduster
1188

The A-23 Treeduster was a type of shuttle made by Incom Corporation. The ship had a substantial cargo capacity. One A-23 with a cargo of turbolaser pieces crashed near Cloud Mountain, Cularin, in 31 BBY. Category:Incom Corporation products Category:Shuttle classes
