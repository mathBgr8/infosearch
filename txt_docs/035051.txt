Kronus
707

Kronus was a thug from the planet Mandrine. He and his goons were known to have caused a brawl in a bar called Maizie's while Captain Scarlet Bloodhawk was visiting. He was killed when one of his dupes drew a thermal detonator who was then shot. The detonator was then picked up by Tyric and thrown at Kronus. They were scraping his remains for weeks.
