Star Wars Miniatures Battles Companion |complement= |bays= |escapepods= |crew=Pilot (1) |skeleton= |passengers=1 |capacity=30 kg |cargohandling= |consumables= |lifesupport= |communications= |othersystems= |availability= |role=Combat support |firstuse= |retired= |affiliation=*Alliance to Restore the Republic *Galactic Empire }} }} The XP-38 sport landspeeder was a SoroSuub landspeeder. It was similar in design to the earlier X-34; in fact, it looked much like a slim version of this older model. Its engines were similar to those of the Flash speeders of Naboo. This updated, sleeker model was a popular civilian speeder, and was also often used by Imperial forces as a support vehicle at planetary garrisons. The XP-38 was released shortly before the Battle of Yavin, and customers soon found that its repulsorlift system made travel over rough terrain quite difficult. SoroSuub produced new versions (i.e. the XP-38 All-Terrain Roughrider) for use in rural areas.The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Kenner intended to release a very different-looking version of an XP-38 landspeeder as part of its never-released The Epic Continues toy line.
