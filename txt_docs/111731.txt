Stenn Glidrick was a Human male who served in the Imperial Navy as a pilot. After leaving the service, he became the commanding officer of Galaxy Tours luxury liner Kuari Princess. In 0 BBY, Captain Glidrick had to deliver the news to his navigator, Lieutenant Celia Durasha, that her brother, Raine, had been killed in battle with Alliance to Restore the Republic forces on Ralltiir. Glidrick was in command of the Kuari Princess during the voyage in which the vessel was hijacked by the Riders of the Maelstrom. He was killed in the assault. Stenn Glidrick was middle-aged, with brown hair which was graying. He wore the Kuari Princess uniform, which was a white tunic and blue trousers with a gold stripe down either leg.
