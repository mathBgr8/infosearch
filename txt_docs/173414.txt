|retired= |battles=Battle of the Tungra sector |affiliation=Mugaari pirates |navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Prince was a Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport used by Mugaari pirates. It was part of an arms deal between the Rebel Alliance and the pirates in a cargo loading area located in the Tungra sector. The Imperials learned of this deal and attacked.
