Its surface featured wastelands in which grew the flith flower, and it was also home to the vicious predatory creature bruth. At some point, Lorista was colonized by members of the Zabrak species from the Glythe sector &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Iridonia system planet Iridonia. Wiker's 2003 Wizards of the Coast Star Wars Roleplaying Game sourcebook Galactic Campaign Guide.
