Ted Gagliano
1825

Ted Gagliano is the Post Production Executive at 20th Century Fox Gagliano later worked as the post-production executive on the Special Edition release of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope.. In a 2024 interview with "Tales From The Collection," Gagliano detailed his hands-on approach to the "Star Wars" Special Edition project, discussing his efforts to balance technological advancements with preserving the original film's authenticity. He elaborated on restoring and enhancing specific scenes, including the meticulous work of improving visual effects and sound quality. Category:Real-world people
