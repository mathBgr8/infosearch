Minder (occupation)

A minder was an occupation similar to a therapist or a psychiatrist. Klo Merit, an Equani, was a minder stationed by the Galactic Republic on the soggy world of Drongar. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Medical occupations
