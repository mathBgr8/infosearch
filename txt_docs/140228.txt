Eye of the Pirate
891

The Eye of the Pirate was a red star visible in the sky of Corellia. It was connected to the constellations of the Drall's Hat and the Ronto. Jason Fry, co-author of The Essential Atlas, indicated in June 2015 that the Eye of the Pirate was excluded from The Essential Atlas Online Companion because it was a local name for a visible star, and not its commonly accepted name. *The New Jedi Order: Recovery Category:Constellations Category:Corellian culture Category:Stars
