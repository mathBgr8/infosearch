Ram Raders
1668

Ram Raders was a Human male who served in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars, holding the rank of corporal. During the Battle of Praesitlyn, Raders was stationed aboard the Ranger where he helped Nejaa Halcyon drive off an army of battle droids that boarded the ship. He, along with Private Slane Vick, Sergeant Omin L'Loxx, and Grudo went on a recon mission to the Separatist headquarters, and later fought along Commander Anakin Skywalker, Trooper Odie Subu, and Private Vick to rescue the Separatist prisoners.
