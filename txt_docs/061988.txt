Farr Orjul
1770

Farr Orjul was a Human male Separatist terrorist and a supplier of explosives ordnance for Perrive's terror net on the planet Coruscant. He was apprehended by Omega Squad during a TIOPS mission and was interrogated by the Mandalorian Walon Vau and Jedi Etain Tur-Mukan. During Tur Mukan's first attempt to extract information from him with a simple mind trick, he spat in her face, and instead she broke down his will enough for Vau finish the job.
