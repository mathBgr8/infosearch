Jeodu
1500

The Jeodu were a tall, brown-skinned species. Their heads were topped with a conical white crystal; for this reason, the name "Dequc" (meaning "Whitehead") was common among them. A Jeodu, Dequc became the leader of Black Nebula in 3.5 ABY, in an attempt to resurrect the Black Sun criminal organization, and many Jeodu joined the organization.
