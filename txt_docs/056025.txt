Dweomilis Advisory Foundation
1374

}} Dweomilis Advisory Foundation was one of the original contributing sponsors of the Corporate Sector Authority, and was later upgraded to voting sponsor. The were a military think tank and policy advisory board who worked with other companies, such as BlasTech Industries and Santhe/Sienar Technologies, to help with defense manufacturing.
