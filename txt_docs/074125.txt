Snillik
509

Snillik was a thick liqueur produced somewhere in the Core Worlds. Lando Calrissian absolutely hated it, so he often chose it to drink while gambling, sipping it slowly. This allowed him to "drink socially," while staying sober and keeping his senses keen. *Lando Calrissian and the Flamewind of Oseon Category:Liqueurs
