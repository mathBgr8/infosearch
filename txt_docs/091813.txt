Spukami
1418

Spukami were a common breed of Corellian housecats that hunted vermin. The Fed-Dub Protective Services Spukami pocket blaster, manufactured on the nearby Corellia system worlds of Talus and Tralus, was named after the species. Spukami were mentioned in the 2019 canon Fantasy Flight Games roleplaying game sourcebook Gadgets and Gear.
