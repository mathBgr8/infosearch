Starlog Communications International
668

thumb|right|250px|Starlog — America's SciFi Magazine Starlog Communications International was the publisher of the science fiction magazines Starlog and Starlog Yearbook that featured several behind-the-scenes articles and interviews about various aspects of the Star Wars franchise. It also released The Official Star Wars Technical Journal magazines, the Star Wars Technical Journal compilation and Star Wars Technical Journal Collector's Edition.
