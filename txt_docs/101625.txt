Tower 78
1418

Tower 78 was one of many spires erupting out of the massive pyramid known as the Imperial Palace on Coruscant. A part of the Imperial Palace complex during the rule of the Galactic Empire, Tower 78. Containing several hangars, chambers and over 400 levels, the Tower was the location of a meeting between Darth Vader and Emperor Palpatine in the days following the battle above Yavin Prime. Several years after the death of both Vader and the Emperor, the Imperial Kirtan Loor was taken to level 443, bay 2, to meet with Director of Imperial Intelligence Ysanne Isard. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron Category:Imperial Palace locations
