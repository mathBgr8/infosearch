Glena
1355

Glena was a descendant of the colonists of Eol Sha that were relocated to the world of Dantooine. Glena was the wife of Warton early in the Galactic Empire's rule. She was one of the many settlers who was vaporized by an AT-AT during Admiral Daala's attack on Dantooine in 11 ABY. *Dark Apprentice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Individuals with she/her pronouns
