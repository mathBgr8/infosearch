|gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=White |skin=Gray |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=A trade guild |masters= |apprentices= }} Murr Danod was an Ithorian who lived during the final years of the Galactic Republic. A smuggler, he worked for a trade guild that operated out of an Ithorian Herdship. In 32 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 32 BBY. Danod attended the annual Boonta Eve Classic Podrace at the Mos Espa Grand Arena on the planet Tatooine. From his seat in the audience, the Ithorian watched as the Dug Podracer Sebulba completed the first of three scheduled laps. Danod had white eyes and possessed skin the same color as the morality of the items he smuggled: gray. The Ithorian's name originated from Ithorian culture. His masculine first name was the name of a beast of burden onGalactic Campaign Guide the planetThe Essential Atlas Ithor, where his species originated, and his family name, Danod, translated to "winemaker." He enjoyed such dealings but was nevertheless a peaceful being who was honest to his customers. Danod was Force-attuned and a fan of Podracing. Murr Danod first appeared in the 1999 film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace.
