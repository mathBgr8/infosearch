|pages=24 |upc=761568983203 |timeline=32 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series= |issue= }} Episode I: Qui-Gon Jinn is a one-shot comic book retelling events from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace from the perspective of Qui-Gon Jinn. The issue was written by Ryder Windham, illustrated by Robert Teranishi, and published by Dark Horse Comics on July 7, 1999. Originally scheduled for a June 2 release, it was later swapped with Episode I: Obi-Wan Kenobi, which was released on June 2 instead. Though he has been branded a maverick, a troublesome Jedi with a propensity to follow his own aptitude for the Force in all living things, Qui-Gon Jinn is undoubtedly a Jedi Knight of the first order.
