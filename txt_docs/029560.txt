|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Maarwraawroo was a large city on the planet Kashyyyk. It had been built around manufacturing facility in the intertwined branches of the world's native wroshyr trees. Maarwraawroo was mentioned in "Secrets of Kashyyyk," an article written by J.D. Wiker and Craig Robert Carey for the fourth issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine published by Wizards of the Coast on June 19, 2001 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities on Kashyyyk
