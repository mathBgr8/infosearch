Delpho
1518

Delpho was a Human male who served as a soldier with the Galactic Alliance Guard. He was a member of Major Salle Serpa's battalion, which occupied the Jedi Academy on Ossus during the Second Galactic Civil War. In the ensuing Battle of Ossus, Delpho was responsible for pumping coma gas into Jaina Solo's room; however, she was able to overpower him and knock him unconscious. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance Guard personnel Category:Humans Category:Males
