Artzam Hathan
1451

Artzam Hathan was a male Ugnaught who was a chief engineer at some point. Artzam Hathan was wanted by Sienar Fleet Systems for stealing design schematics and architectural layouts which he intended to sell. His bounty was for 3,000 credits alive and 1,500 credits dead, and was claimed by Jango Fett.
