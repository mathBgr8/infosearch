Whaladon Meat Quality Control Division
450

The Whaladon Meat Quality Control Division was a division of Captain Dunwell's Whaladon-hunting operation that presumably controlled the quality of the Whaladon meat. Meat was shipped there from the Whaladon Processing Center on Mon Calamari after being chopped into slabs. *The Glove of Darth Vader Category:Industrial facilities
