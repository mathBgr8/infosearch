The team was based out of the Grand Khanum Stadium in Capra, a large city on Kinyen, and could accommodate up to 150,000 spectators for Galactic Cup tournament matches. Team Kinyen represented one of the few offworld influences on the world's native Gran, though their team often failed to qualify for the Galactic Cup despite the citizens' enthusiasm. In 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:3:14, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. Team Kinyen was bested by the planetThe New Essential Guide to Alien Species Yag'Dhul's team, which won their bolo-ball game with two unanswered points in the tournament's opening round. The match's result was reported in "Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round," a news story published in the CoCo District Edition of HoloNet News. Upon the Galactic Cup's suspension after the Destruction of Alderaan in 0 BBY, the Grand Khanum Stadium largely fell out of use.
