Domain Yim
799

Domain Yim was a Domain among the Shaper caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Members of Domain Yim were extremely skilled at the Shaping arts, and they were also very devout. However, Domain Yim seemed to be subordinate to Domain Kwaad; Tjulan Kwaad was the lord of Domain Yim. One of the most noteworthy members of Domain Yim was Nen Yim, Master Shaper of Yuuzhan'tar.
