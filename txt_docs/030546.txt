Klev (assistant)
786

Klev was an assistant of Squarn's who worked in Squarn's Junkyard on the planet Muugrah. When a group of Rebels arrived to get parts to repair the hyperdrive of their ship, the Transorb, they were met by Klev who led them to a permanently emplaced military surplus shelter module which served as the junk seller's office.
