Dungeontown
1299

Dungeontown was a makeshift prisoner shantytown on the Imperial prison planet Despayre. Dungeontown accommodated criminal convicts sentenced to the planet. Teela Kaarz was among several hundred prisoners from Dungeontown who were recruited by the Empire seeking additional slave labor for the construction of the Death Star in orbit above the planet, leaving Despayre onboard the prisoner transport ship GLTB-3181 in 3 BBY. Dungeontown was destroyed when the Death Star fired upon the planet in 0 BBY. *Death Star Category:Despayre locations
