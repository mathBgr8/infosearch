Pixolga
1237

Pixolga was a male Dug who served the Anti-Artist Federation during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by the Pixelito Art Guild for claiming that painted paw prints by a tamed nexu did not constitute true art. His accusations had resulted in severe profit loss.
