Cather Taan's Gun Shop
711

Cather Taan's Gun Shop was a weapon shop in Storrd Township on the planet Seregar. It was a small building with stucco walls and a large neon sign. Owned by its eponymous proprietor, it was widely considered the only decent weapons shop on Seregar. Category:Seregar locations Category:Weapon stores
