X'Tingian
725

X'Tingian was the language of the X'Ting, the insectoid native species of the planet Ord Cestus. It replaced a previous language, Old X'Tingian. X'Tingian used buzzes and clicks to convey information in its "oral" form. To create a "written" X'Tingian, scents were to be stored and then smelled when wanting to "read". This was the heritage of Old X'Tingian, which used scents to convey information "orally".
