Citadel 2 was a BFF-1 bulk freighter that served with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. It was sent to rescue Mon Calamari slaves scheduled for transport to the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Warrior. Citadel 2 boarded the freighter Wiggins containing the slaves and rescued them and, along with Citadel 1, boarded container Xi 1, rescued the slaves, and escaped into hyperspace, while the Rebel pilot Halley Kadorto fought of wave after wave of TIE/LN starfighters and TIE/sa bombers that were attempting to destroy the cargo containing the slaves.X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
