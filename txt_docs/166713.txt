Audio telescope
504

An audio telescope was a form of technology that recorded conversations. It was small enough to hide in plain sight and could be attached to walls or ceilings. One was used by Hiram Drayson to eavesdrop on Nil Spaar and Leia Organa Solo. *Before the Storm Category:Audio sensors Category:Emplacement sensors Category:Surveillance equipment
