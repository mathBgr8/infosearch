Three Peaks of Tharen
1077

The Three Peaks of Tharen was a mountain range on the planet Kalarba near the immense equatorial zone. The local Kalarbans believed that their great spirit of strength was embodied within the mountains. It overlooked the nearby Great Sea and the planetary capital Kalarba City and Kalarba City Spaceport. *Star Wars: Droids Special *Droids (1994) 2 *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons Category:Kalarba locations
