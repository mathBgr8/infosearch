Quil Nathan
831

Quil Nathan was a well-dressed employee of an arms manufacturer intent on acquiring the secret Imperial RNA brainwashing virus. He was sent with Havas Rar when it was discovered to be up for auction on the Ananuru Express. Before the auction could begin, they attempted to search through the Express's computer systems to see if they could find it. They did not manage to, and were involved in the bidding until Secesh Trant interrupted it and stole the datatape.
