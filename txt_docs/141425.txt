Trader's argot

Trader's argot was a collection of most of the languages used by the Humans and aliens of the galaxy. Full of slang terms and grammatical errors, not even a protocol droid could comprehend this integration of languages used by the traders. *The Paradise Snare *The Hutt Gambit *Rebel Dawn Category:Trade languages
