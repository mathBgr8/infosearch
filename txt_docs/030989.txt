Abrogator
2002

Abrogator was an Imperial Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser that acted as the flagship of the fleet sent to Saloch 2 to quell a group of Lortans during the Reslian Purge. The sixth squadron of the 37th Imperial Fighter Wing was assigned to the Abrogator, under the command of Captain Lun Tessra.
