Carlos Meglia
763

Carlos Meglia was an Argentinian artist who did the pencils and inking for Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika and "Lando's Commandos: On Eagles' Wings." He died at the age of fifty in August 2008. Category:Comic book cover artists Category:Comic book inkers Category:Comic book pencillers
