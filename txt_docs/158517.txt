Imaharatronics
939

Imaharatronics was a company that produced logic display sensors for use in various models of droids. The astromech droid R2-D2 had a sensor constructed by this company. This company is a reference to Grant Imahara, a controller for R2-D2 in the prequel trilogy. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid technology companies
