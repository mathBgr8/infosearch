Flip of the Credit
2336

}} The Flip of the Credit was a casino owned by the Rodian Avaro Sookcool. It was located in Equator City on the Rodian homeworld and served as a front for money laundering conducted by Black Sun. Leia Organa met with Avaro here in order to solicit the criminal syndicate's aid in finding and rescuing the captured Han Solo.
