|founder= |dissolved= |headquarters=New York City |parent= |subsid= |website= }} The Topps Company, also known as Topps Collectibles, or simply Topps, is a collectibles company based in New York City. It is best known as a leading producer of baseball cards and other sports-related trading cards, but it has also maintained a partnership with Lucasfilm since 1977, regularly releasing numerous series of Star Wars trading cards. Topps also published Star Wars Galaxy Magazine, its successor Star Wars Galaxy Collector, a trading card game titled Star Wars PocketModel TCG (through its subsidiary WizKids Games), and several Star Wars comic books. Starting in 2015, the original trading-card series were reprinted in several hardcover volumes by Abrams Books.
