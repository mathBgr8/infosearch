Glich
1990

Glich was a Rebel transport encountered by Maarek Stele on his first sortie at Outpost D-34. Glich is not specifically described in TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide, but mentioned only by name under mission goals for the first mission of the Hoth Aftermath campaign.
