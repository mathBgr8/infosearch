Gramzee
1474

"Rachet" Gramzee was a male Ugnaught who lived during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. "Rachet" might have been his nickname. The Corporate Alliance placed a bounty on his head for stealing a chemical process used in the production of death sticks. The bounty was claimed by bounty hunter Jango Fett, who caught him.
