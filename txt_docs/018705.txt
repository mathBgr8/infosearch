Cobra
7100

The Cobra was a ship that belonged to Lando Calrissian. Calrissian had many stories of how he acquired the ship and wasn't sure which one he liked the best. The vessel was a modified Mu-2 long range shuttle originally manufactured by Sienar Fleet Systems. thumb|left|150px|Calrissian wagers his title against an Idiot's Array. In 1 ABY, Calrissian used it as part of his wager to Baron Administrator Dominic Raynor in a sabacc game, losing the Cobra in a game rigged by Barpotomous Drebble, but then later winning it back. At this time, the Cobra was notable enough for Raynor to request it by name when Lando proposed to wager a ship in their game. Calrissian later flew the ship off of Bespin while faking his own death in order to uncover a plot to assassinate him. He then traveled to the Yucrales sector and the planet Quilken with it while being chased by the bounty hunter Thune.
