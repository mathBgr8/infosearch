Emperor's Voice (Galactic Empire)
900

The Emperor's Voice was an exalted level of Dark Side Adept loyal to Emperor Palpatine at the time of the Galactic Empire. It served as a parallel to the Emperor's Hands, Emperor's Eyes, and Emperor's Reach.
