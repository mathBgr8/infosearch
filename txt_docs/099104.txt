Briike
963

Briike was a Mandalorian soldier under Mandalore Boba Fett's leadership. In 40 ABY, he was present in a meeting between Boba Fett and other Mandalorians in Zerria's Bar on Drall. His surname derives from the Mando'a word "briik" that means "line". *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines Category:Males Category:Mandalorians
