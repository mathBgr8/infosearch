Vulture (Marauder-class)
1951

Vulture was a Republic Sienar Systems Marauder-class corvette in use by an Outer Rim pirate group. Accompanied by an escort of Y-wings and Toscan fighters, named Pirate and Fury respectively, Vulture led an attack on noted entrepreneur Dunari aboard his pleasure yacht, the Raw Deal.
