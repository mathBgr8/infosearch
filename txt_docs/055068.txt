The Weary Traveler
1283

The Weary Traveler was one of the two small cantinas in Anchorhead on Tatooine during the time of the Clone Wars. Obi-Wan Kenobi frequented this cantina during the initial stages of his self-imposed exile on Tatooine. It featured a HoloNet receiver, a vaporator, and a selection of offworld brandy. It was here that Obi-Wan learned of Darth Vader's survival after leaving him for dead on Mustafar.
