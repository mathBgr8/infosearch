Chack
1012

Chack was the name of a saurian quadruped who sniffed out contraband for the Eriadu Customs and Immigration security forces. This animal was killed by Captain Arwen Cohl's hired mercenaries shortly before the assassination attempt on Supreme Chancellor Finis Valorum at the Eriadu Trade Summit. *Cloak of Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Named creatures
