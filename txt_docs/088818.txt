Lovolan
1504

Lovolan was a droid manufacturer based in the Core Worlds; their name was synonymous with wealth, diginity, beauty, and exclusivity as their products were both expensive and covered in ostentatious ornaments. The company was the manufacturer of the R-10 household droid, a droid favored by Emperor Palpatine.
