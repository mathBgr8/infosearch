|published in=Star Wars Tales 20 |reprinted in= |pages=2 |timeline=3 ABY |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Who's Your Daddy" is a non-canon, humorous short comic from Star Wars Tales 20, involving Darth Vader reciting how he is going to introduce himself to his son during their inevitable duel. Darth Vader woke up one morning and did his daily routine, brushing his teeth and having breakfast. He was constantly rehearsing how he will reveal the truth about Luke Skywalker's paternity to himself all morning.
