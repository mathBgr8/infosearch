Rekker
589

Rekker was a Killik hive. The denizens of the hive were 3 meters tall, extremely energetic and had hard natural armor. They were capable of jumping long distances to strike at their targets. As such, they were used to batter enemy fortifications and soldiers by the Killiks during the Swarm War. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Unknown Regions Category:Killik hives
