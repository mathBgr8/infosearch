In 4 ABY,The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars (1977) 88 to 4 ABY. when members of the slaver group known as the Herdessan Guild working for the Sith Lady Lumiya captured a miscreant, Finn, one of them suggested selling the offender as a slave to work in the lava mines on Vanan. Vanan was mentioned in the eighty-eighth issue of Marvel Comics' Star Wars series, which was written by Mary Jo Duffy and published on July 17, 1984.
