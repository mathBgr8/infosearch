Storm Unit (team)
2027

The Storm Unit was the name of a squad of Rebel Alliance boarding troopers during the Galactic Civil War. The unit was called upon numerous times during the war to board enemy vessels and installations, their mission profiles included: stealing information; planting explosives; rescuing prisoners and capturing certain facilities. Storm Unit was commanded by Lieutenant Paling, who led the Nighthawks. Some of their high profile missions included: *Recovering important information from the Imperial CR90 corvette Talon *Rescuing prisoners of war from Imperial prisoner Dreadnaught VTR-LX *Stealing data from an Imperial factory producing starfighters for the TIE Experimental Project, then destroying the facility *Defusing a bomb planted on the Azzameen Station. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic squads
