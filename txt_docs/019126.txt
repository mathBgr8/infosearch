M-40 light repeating blaster
1414

The M-40 light repeating blaster, also known as the "Thunderbolt", was a repeating blaster produced by Merr-Sonn. It was mounted on a bipod and produced powerful red bolts. They were impractical as close-quarter weapons however. Chalk notably wielded a Thunderbolt as her favorite weapon. They were also carried by the four hooded men sent to kill Darth Vader on Vohai.
