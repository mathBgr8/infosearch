Verpine zal alloy mesh
1805

Verpine zal alloy mesh was a type of armor that was manufactured by the Verpine during the years before and after the Jedi Civil War. The armor offered outstanding protection against most types of attacks due to its utilization of Zal alloy, however, this also increased the price of the armor immensely. This armor can be acquired in the game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic on the planet Lehon as a reward from the Black Rakata war leader outside the settlement for killing the Mandalorian Hunters near the Temple of the Ancients.
