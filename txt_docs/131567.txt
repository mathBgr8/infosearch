Kalstan gangsters
638

The Kalstan gangsters were based in the Xorao system. In 1 ABY, Rebellion Lieutenant Commander Anson Blazer was investigating their activities. The gangsters were alerted to his presence by the Tunroth bounty hunter, Kiran Tatch who was visiting the system. They successfully chased off the Rebels, forcing them to leave behind much valuable equipment. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Criminal organizations
