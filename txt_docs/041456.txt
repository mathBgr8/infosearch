Festival of Great Trade

thumb|right Festival of Great Trade was a holiday on the planet Svivren specifically in Wrils. It involved hordes of merchant-pilgrims marching through cities' trade and negotiation areas. *Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Holidays
