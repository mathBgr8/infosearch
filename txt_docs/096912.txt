Pelgrin (species)
548

The Pelgrin were an extinct species native to Pelgrin. Though primitive, they had the uncanny ability to duplicate advanced technology. They were the creators of the mysterious Oracle at Pelgrin, over two million years before the Battle of Yavin. *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook Category:Extinct sentient species Category:Outer Rim Territories sentient species
