|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYThe date of this story is established by in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: The Bounty Hunters |issue=One shot |prev=Crimson Empire 6 |next=Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 1 }} The Bounty Hunters: Kenix Kil was the third and final issue in the Bounty Hunters series of comics, and was released on October 13, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. Kir Kanos, the last surviving member of the Emperor's Royal Guard, is the most wanted man in the galaxy. How do you hide when every bounty hunter in every system is on your trail? For Kanos, the answer is to disguise himself as one of the hunters. From Star Wars: Crimson Empire, the untold tale of how Kir Kanos became bounty hunter Kenix Kil!
