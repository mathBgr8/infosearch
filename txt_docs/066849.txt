Plexcorp

Plexcorp was a company that specialized in the production of repulsorlift engines. During the height of the Galactic Civil War, agents of the House Mecetti broke into Plexcorp's headquarters and stole a powerful new design of repulsorlift engine from the company. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Technology companies
