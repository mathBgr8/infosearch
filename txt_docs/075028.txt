Independence Week
879

Independence Week was a holiday in the Freeworlds Territory within the Tapani sector. It was celebrated from the 10th to the 15th of Relona. It celebrated the Freeworlds' separation from the Expanse. *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector holidays
