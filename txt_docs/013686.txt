It was situated on the Ootmian Pabol hyperlane between the worlds of Nal Hutta and Du Hutta. Nar Hekka also sat on the Sheela Run. Nar Hekka was a cold world with little indigenous plant and animal life. Despite the hostile environment, Hutts rectified this by erecting large hothouse domes around their cities and planting foreign vegetation within. The domes had artificial wind generators to circulate the air around the many botanical gardens. Nar Hekka was the home and business headquarters of Tagta the Hutt, a subordinate to Jiliac. It was assumedly conquered by Yuuzhan Vong Warmaster Nas Choka during the Yuuzhan Vong War's subjugation of Hutt Space, though the amount of botanical plant life on the planet may have granted it some concessions.
