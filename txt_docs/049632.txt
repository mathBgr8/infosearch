John Dicks

John Dicks (born July 23, 1947) is an actor who played the Imperial officer Captian Lennox in Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, commanding the Star Destroyer Tyrant. The movie credits him only as "Imperial Officer," but the Expanded Universe later named him Captain Lennox. Category:Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back actors
