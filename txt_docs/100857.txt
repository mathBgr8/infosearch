|published in=Star Wars Tales 12 |reprinted in= |pages=10 |timeline=0 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |series= |preceded by="The Revenge of Tag & Bink" |followed by="The Duty" }} "Once Bitten" is a short comic in Star Wars Tales 12 in which Obi-Wan Kenobi tells the tale of his and Qui-Gon Jinn's encounter with Aurra Sing. thumb|left|150px|Qui-Gon Jinn duels Aurra Sing Han Solo is in the cockpit of the Millennium Falcon, contemplating things while flying his ship towards Alderaan. The aged Obi-Wan Kenobi asks to come in and tells a story of his and Qui-Gon Jinn's encounter with the evil Aurra Sing. The Jedi Council had received a distress call from Mana Veridi, a fellow Jedi who had been sent to investigate a disturbance on Kwannot, asking them for assistance. On arrival, Qui-Gon tells Obi-Wan to relax and let the Force guide them. Soon they encounter Mana, only for her to be assassinated by Aurra Sing. Aurra then attacks Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan, mocking Obi-Wan for only being a Padawan and claiming she is after his master. Feeling the duo won't easily be beaten, she retreats, stealing Mana's lightsaber. As Han expresses disbelief at the thought of two Jedi being matched by a mere assassin, Obi-Wan explains that Aurra was a defected Jedi.
