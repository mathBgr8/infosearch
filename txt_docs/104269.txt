Eager for a scrap with the forces of the Galactic Empire, Maal and Wuwuhuul volunteered to accompany a Rebel strike team to Goratak III to recover a downed I2-CG droid with critical information about Rebel starfighter manufacturing operations on Tar Morden. At the time of this engagement, Wuwuhuul was 7 seasons old, stood 1.88 meters tall, and weighed just over 54 kilograms.
