At some point, AthleticGal placed an advertisement to promote Aquawear&mdash;including exclusive holos, model data, and species gallery&mdash;on the HoloNet News. Aquawear first appeared in the HoloNet News, an in-universe website originally published on February 28, 2002.Per , HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47 was released on March 14, 2002. As HoloNet News segments were published weekly, and was published two issues prior, it must have been released on February 28 of the same year.
