Tamora Spice
1871

Tamora Spice was the second wife of Kitster Banai and mother of Ji and Elly Banai. After Kitster stole the painting Killik Twilight during the auction held at Mawbo's Performance Hall, she, Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo went to search for Kitster.
