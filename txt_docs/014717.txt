Jankok was the homeworld of the sentient feline Srrors'tok species, members of which communicated via the Hras'kkk'rarr language and were susceptible to low temperatures. The Srrors'tok primarily lived in tribal hunting parties formed on the basis of familial ties and a common culture, and their homeworld was at a primitive technology level. Few offworlders, save for scouts and the occasional trader, visited Jankok, and the planet had no starports. Not many Srrors'tok left their homeworld, with Ahnjai Rahmma, bodyguard and enforcer of the Galactic Empire's High Inquisitor Antinnis Tremayne,Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim and the bounty hunter Tyionsis Cex and his merchant family being among those who did.Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Jankok was first mentioned in the 1998 West End Games Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game sourcebook Alien Encounters.
