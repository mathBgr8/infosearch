Cave of Elders
1085

The Cave of Elders was the only artificial structure in the ThonBoka, a nebula located within the remote Open Sea in the Centrality. Believed to be the only structure ever constructed by the native Oswaft species, the Cave of Elders served as an important meeting place for the Oswaft.
