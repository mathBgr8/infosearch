Docking Bay 92
1535

Docking Bay 92 was a starship hangar located behind Spacers Row in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was owned by the Human, Dom Antyll. The bay was used almost exclusively for starship repairs as Dom was a skilled mechanic who kept his tools in Docking Bay 92.
