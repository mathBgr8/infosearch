|pages= |published in= |genre= }} "Lightyear" was a song that the RX-Series pilot droid R-3X played on the radio station BSO 401.72 in Black Spire Outpost on the planet Batuu in 34 ABY. The droid introduced the song as something for younglings, grubs, and hatchlings. "Lightyear" was created for the themed land Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, which opened to guests at Disneyland Park and Disney's Hollywood Studios in 2019. It can be heard playing during the land's background radio loop. Category:Songs
