Law Elders
1637

The Law Elders were the ruling planetary council of Bimmisaari, the capital planet of the Halla sector. Made up of members of both the Near-Human and furred races of Bimm, the council met in the Tower of Law. While aligned with the Galactic Empire throughout the Galactic Civil War, the Elders opened negotiations to join the New Republic during the early days of the Thrawn campaign. After negotiations with Leia Organa Solo, Bimmisaari ultimately chose to join the New Republic, and other worlds in the Halla sector such as Danuta followed suit. *Heir to the Empire *Heir to the Empire Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Legislatures
