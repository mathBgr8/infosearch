Its vast oceans were interrupted by some 2 million volcanic islands, of which only a few in the southern hemisphere were active during the Galactic Civil War. There were two northern island-continents: Belshain and Madieri. Some of the planet's mountains were capped with snow and home to snow-wolves. Delgas Medical and Hydrospeare Corporation were headquartered on Kabaira. The capital city was Eponte Spaceport, which was known for Drayhar's Cantina. The local day consisted of 23 standard hours; the local year of 341 local days. Humans and Twi'leks inhabited the planet. There was a Rebel Alliance cell on the planet.
