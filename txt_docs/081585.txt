|editor= |website=Wizards.com |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=*March 6, 2003 *March 13, 2003 *March 20, 2003 *March 27, 2003 |series=Planet Hoppers |preceded by=Arkanian Chill |followed by=Coruscant: Center of the Empire }} Velmor: Royalty and Rebellion was an online supplement for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game published on the Wizards of the Coast website in March 2003 as part of the site's Planet Hoppers series, featuring the planet Velmor and other characters and settings appearing in Marvel Comics' Star Wars series. It was written by Cory J. Herndon. The supplement was split into four separate articles, including the reports and memoirs of Zeta Traal, Chozz Wardle, Debor Fyalko, and Ergric Betos. &hellip;the Empire's diplomatic attaché, the conniving Captain Traal, reports to her superiors on the wealth and resources of the secluded planet Velmor. &hellip;the Royal Physician, Chozz Wardle, delivers a medical, genetic, and psychiatric summary of the man who claims to be Velmor's missing royal heir. &hellip;the Court Swordmaster, Debor Fyalko, offers a tutorial in the use, handling, and care of the archaic Velmorian energy sword. &hellip;Velmor's unluckiest bodyguard, Ergric Betos, bears witness to the events that took place the day of the new king's coronation -- when he failed to protect the life of a Velmorian prince.
