Sektor 242 NewsLine
1531

Sektor 242 NewsLine was a smaller news agency during the Galactic Civil War, but it attained a high standard concerning accuracy and objectivity. This network also had one of the most popular holostrips in the Outer Rim, "The Times and Trepidations of Majnar Roak."
