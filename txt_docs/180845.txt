Tansarii Point Station |species= |gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Arslynd was a male individual who lived during the Galactic Civil War. After the Battle of Yavin, Arslynd resided on board Tansarii Point Station, in the Bright Jewel system. In 1 ABY, Arslynd was in possession of information needed by the bounty hunter Boba Fett about a certain shipment. In the meanwhile, Fett was training a spacer who wished to become a bounty hunter and realized that obtaining information from Arslynd could be a good exercise. Fett therefore tasked the spacer to interrogate Arslynd about the shipment. The spacer finally found Arslynd and his bodyguard in the atrium of the station. Indeed, Arslynd was not willing to share any information and attacked the spacer. The latter thus killed Arslynd and eventually collected his datapad. Unfortunately, the datapad was damaged during the fight and Boba Fett was not able to obtain the information he was looking for. thumb|250px|Possible depictions of Arslynd Arslynd appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Arslynd was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005. Arslynd was found in the tutorial level on Tansarii Point Station and was killed by any player who took the Bounty hunter profession as part of a non-optional class quest for Boba Fett. Arslynd's species and gender would be randomized each time he spawned. Some potential appearances included being a male or female Rodian, or a female Bothan.
