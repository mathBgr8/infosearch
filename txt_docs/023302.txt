The game was released on Disney.com on October 9, 2014. It features the Spectres. The crew of the starship Ghost decided to conduct strikes against the Galactic Empire. They were pursued by Commandant Cumberlayne Aresko. The crew destroyed Imperial armaments and stole supplies in Capital City before attacking Grint himself. The game has an overhead view and control is turn-based, with each character getting two actions per turn.
