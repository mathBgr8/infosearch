Avengeance
1655

The Avengeance was a Bothan cruiser employed by Reh'mwa and other Bothan radicals to locate the hidden world Zonama Sekot. These radicals intended to use the ship to continue the policy of ar'krai and wipe out the relocated Yuuzhan Vong species. However, the Avengeance was soon neutralized and taken into the custody of the Galactic Alliance. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothan starships Category:Cruisers
