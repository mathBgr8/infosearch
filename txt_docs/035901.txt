Graygl
2688

The Graygl were an aquatic species originally native to Danalbeth. The Graygl relocated to the water-covered planet Iskalon to escape a cataclysm that struck Danalbeth around five millennia before the Galactic Civil War, states that the species constituting the Iskalonian cultural group were brought to Iskalon approximately five millennia before the Galactic Civil War. Since "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series" establishes that the relocation of the Iskalonian species was prefaced by the cataclysms on the species' original homeworlds, the cataclysms must also have occurred around that time. and became a part of the Iskalonian School, a cultural group of several aquatic species that had traveled to Iskalon with the aid of spacefaring benefactors. Graygl had a fearsome appearance, with a wide mouth filled with sharp teeth and red eyes,The Essential Guide to Alien Species although this belied their gentle nature.
