|type=Paperback |pages=464 pages |dimensions=9 x 7.2 x 1.1 inches |weight=10.6 ounces |isbn=0761542221 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Galaxies: Jump to Lightspeed: Prima Official Game Guide was a strategy book for the Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided Expansion pack by Prima Publishing and was released on November 2, 2004. The guide detailed coverage of the game. It contained information on the three new space professions, Ithorian and Sullustan, new race specs as well as information on the eight new space zone maps and Shipwright profession tree, with schematics. The section on Naboo provides some historical context for the planet's Human population, which reuses some text directly from the DataBank in the 2001 LucasArts video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
