Grannan
1136

The Grannan were a Near-Human species from Granna. Distinguishing features of the Grannan included skin cuffs that allowed them to retract their fingers and toes, respiratory sacs that allowed the breathing of methanogen and loose eye creases. Grannan naming conventions placed the surname before the personal name.
