However, later sources shifted the battle’s date to 4 BBY. Works published around the same time as Characters, such as The New Essential Chronology, instead placed the battle in 3 BBY, which aligns with the timeline presented in Star Wars: Empire issue 8, depicting the mutiny shortly before the Battle of Yavin.
