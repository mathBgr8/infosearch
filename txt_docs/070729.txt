Lando Calrissian's R5 unit
1426

Lando Calrissian's R5 unit was an old astromech droid into which Lando Calrissian had encoded all access codes to all doors in Cloud City. Kyle Katarn used it to get access to the upper levels of Cloud City during the Battle of Bespin. The droid appears in Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast but has not been named. Lando simply referred to it as "an old R5 unit." In the game, the droid helps the player by following Kyle around and unlocking doors for him. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast: Official Perfect Guide Category:R5-series astromech droids
