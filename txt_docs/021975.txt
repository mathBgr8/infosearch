Michael Allen Horne
2275

}} Michael Allen Horne (1963? - Jan 18, 2015) was an author who wrote material for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game for West End Games. Horne's work on the Dark Empire Sourcebook and Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook has been praised by other Expanded Universe authors like Daniel Wallace and Abel G. Peña, with Wallace calling them the "blue-ribbon standards" of gaming sourcebooks and lauding their amount of detail, color, and universe expansion. When Horne passed away in 2015, Wallace called him "one of the best writers of West End Games and the #StarWars Expanded Universe." {| ! Year !!
