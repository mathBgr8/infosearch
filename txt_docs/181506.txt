Rugin
1634

Rugin was an Imperial Cargo ferry that arrived at the Elliirad Platform to pick up Glavsled while the platform was under attack by a Rebel Alliance strike force consisting of Y-wings that were targeting the freighters, A-wings attempting to provide starfighter support for the Y-wings and a commando unit in a stolen Escort shuttle intending to destroy the station with explosives. The outcome depends on what happens during the mission, it is either destroyed or escapes into hyperspace. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter Category:Cargo ferries Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
