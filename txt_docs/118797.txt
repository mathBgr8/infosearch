Unidentified individual ("Pig-face")
1543

An individual encased entirely in plates of silvery armor with only a glass-encased brain and yellow-goggled eyes visible was in a relationship with a furry female individual, and the two were sometimes seen at Locru's Central Saloon. When Han Solo came into the saloon and found himself in a fight with Warto, the Corellian smuggler crashed into the table seating the silvery-armored humanoid and their girlfriend.
