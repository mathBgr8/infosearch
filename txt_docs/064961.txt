Shlecho newt
800

A Yuuzhan Vong bioengineered creature, the shlecho newt was a small brown-orange amphibian. Coomb spores were a favorite food of such creatures and as such, could detect their presence on another being. As a sign of excitement, the newt would dart its tongue in and out and its head would become a shade of crimson.
