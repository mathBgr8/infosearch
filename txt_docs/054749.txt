A human from the planet Kuat in the Core Worlds, Elsek became a member of the Galactic Empire's Stormtrooper corps following graduation from Corulag Academy. He was partners with Avarik since graduation from the academy. He was often forced to cover for his partner's rash decisions, such as bailing Avarik out of fights with drunks in local bars and enlisted clubs. Sometime during his service, he attained the rank of sergeant. When Avarik was accosted by Han Solo, he signaled Elsek to go for help. As Elsek took off on his 74-Z speeder bike, Chewbacca destroyed it with his bowcaster, and the scout was propelled into the air. Elsek first appeared as an unnamed background extra in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi.
