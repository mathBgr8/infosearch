Data screen unit
1758

A data screen unit (DSU) was a unit of data storage measurement that was based on the amount of data that could be displayed on a standard datapad. Arhul Hextrophon's report on the Alliance to Restore the Republic that had been commissioned by Mon Mothma was 168,000 DSUs in length, and would have taken an individual over seven hundred hours to read in its entirety.
