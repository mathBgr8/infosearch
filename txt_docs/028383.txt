Niner's squad
5247

Niner's squad was a unit consisting of clone commandos in the Galactic Republic's Grand Army who fought in the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. The unit was made up of four clone commandos and led by Niner, and was trained by Sergeant Kal Skirata. In 22 BBY, the squad was deployed to the First Battle of Geonosis, of which Niner was the squad's only survivor. Niner's squad was a group of clone commandos who served in the Galactic Republic's Grand Army from 32 BBY to 22 BBY. Cloned on the Wild Space planetThe Essential Atlas Kamino in 32 BBY, the squad was led by Niner, and consisted of clone commandos Two-Eight, O-Four, DD, and Sev. During the commandos' training under Cuy'val Dar training Sergeant Kal Skirata on Kamino, 28 was killed due to an explosives error of his own doing on a live-range exercise&mdash;an event that would weigh heavily on the squad leader. The squad received either O-Four, DD, or Sev as a replacement in 28's stead, but Niner observed that the new trooper seemed to feel as if he was not accepted by the unit. In 22 BBY the squad was deployed to the Outer Rim world of Geonosis in the first battle of the Clone Wars. In the battle, three of the squad's members were killed in action, leaving Niner as the only survivor to be assigned to Omega Squad shortly after, which he remained as squad leader for the duration of the war. RC-1309's squad was mentioned in the 2004 novel Republic Commando: Hard Contact, written by Karen Traviss.
