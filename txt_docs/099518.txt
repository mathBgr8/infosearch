Forre Musa
1100

Forre Musa was waiter droid in Zerria's Bar on Drall in 40 ABY. Although he was only programmed to serve as a waiter, Musa offered patrons what he called "intelligent entertainment", ranging from political satire to poetry. Musa was a prompt waiter, even though few ever asked for his entertainment.
