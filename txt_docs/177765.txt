Stenness Raider
1838

The Stenness Raider was one of the two ore-raiding ships used by the pirate captain Finhead Stonebone in 3999 BBY to raid Ithullan ore haulers. It was a modified Byblos Drive Yards E-2 asteroid mining vehicle.
