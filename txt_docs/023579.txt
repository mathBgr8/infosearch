S'diawae
2024

*Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} S'diawae was a famous Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic sometime between the Ruusan Reformation and the Separatist Crisis, a period known as the Golden Age of the Old Republic. A Force-sensitive member of the Jedi Order, S'diawae had gained the rank of Jedi Master and served the Galactic Republic with distinction. Postmortem, Master S'diawae was recognized by the Biscuit Baron corporation during the time of the Separatist Crisis, a political schism threatening the Galactic Senate during its final decade.
