Council of Elders (Yinchorri)
1485

The Yinchorri Council of Elders was the ruling body of Yinchorri in Tol-Kachorn on the planet of Yinchorr and were made up of the Yinchorri intelligentsia caste. During the Galactic Civil War, the Galactic Empire held the council hostage in exchange for Leia Organa, who had been on planet.
