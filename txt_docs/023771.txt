Slightly larger than its predecessor at 42 meters, the Warden-class was designed as a general security vessel, capable of completing customs, patrol and engagement missions. It featured more powerful engines than the Guardian-class, as well as improved sensor capabilities. A crew of sixteen operated the vessel with one gunner manning a pair of turret-mounted laser cannons. In addition, the interior could support fourteen troopers and 100 metric tons of cargo.
