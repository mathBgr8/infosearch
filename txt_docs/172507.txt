Pricene
1619

Pricene was a male Human who served as a starship captain for the Lant Mining Corporation. He had been assigned to command of the LMC Hauler One-Six, the lead ship of Convoy LMC-55c, when it was captured by an Alliance corvette and a squadron of Y-wings. *Flashpoint!
