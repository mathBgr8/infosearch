A family with three children, including the Human girl Shanna Kinn, lived there, with Kinn spending time with her two older brothers and learning to repair starships as a result. At some point, military forces of the Galactic Empire arrived at Dorriella and occupied it. During an Imperial raid on a suspected Rebel Alliance hideout, Kinn's family was killed, leaving the girl an orphan. Subsequently, in order to survive on Dorriella's streets, Kinn resorted to thievery and eventually stowed away aboard a freighter that took her off-world. Dorriella was mentioned in "Fizzi's Slightly Used Starships," a roleplaying game source article written by John J. Richardson III and published in the February 1996 ninth issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Dorriella system, and therefore Dorriella itself, in grid square I-17.
