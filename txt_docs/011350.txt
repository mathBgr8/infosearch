Winso Bykart
2861

Winso Bykart was a Jedi Knight who served the Jedi Order around 32 BBY.According to Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi, Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi had their second trip to Ragoon-6 with Winso Bykart shortly before their mission to Naboo, which took place in 32 BBY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Bykart was a pilot who often ferried Master and Padawan pairs to the planet Ragoon-6 for a traditional field exercise, where Bykart would go into hiding and the pair would try to track down their fellow Jedi. Around 32 BBY, one of these pairs were Master Qui-Gon Jinn and his apprentice Obi-Wan Kenobi. Kenobi would later reflect on his time on Ragoon-6 with Bykart while on a trip to the planet with his own learner, Anakin Skywalker, in 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi, including Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker's trip to Ragoon-6, to 27 BBY.
