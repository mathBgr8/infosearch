Dabi
2039

The Dabi were a sentient species that had four arms that they could operate independently. A member of the species could speak Galactic Basic Standard. In 40 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines to 40 ABY. a Dabi worked as a bartender in Zerria's Bar on the planet Drall. When a visiting patron, the Mandalorian Mirta Gev, destroyed the speech circuit of the droid waiter Forre Musa, the bartender applauded her action, claiming the droid had been ruining business at the bar.
