Smalyun
3089

Smalyun was a male Human captain in the Bank of Aargau Security's customs division during the era of the Galactic Civil War. Inexperienced at his post as a security officer at Planetary Customs Security Checkpoint LH48 as of 3 ABY, Smalyun struggled with lateness early in his new role, but impressed supervisors by mastering the checkpoint's holoscanner in record time. Smalyun was secretly a contact of notorious smuggler Li-Suun Niik, and not long after his new posting he overlooked one of Niik's men attempting to pass through the scanner with illegal gold bonded to two of his ribs. Sergeant-at-Arms Rolex informed Smalyun of his "error," and Smalyun responded by killing the man without hesitation. Smalyun first appeared in Star Wars (1977) 48, written by Larry Hama and released in 1981.
