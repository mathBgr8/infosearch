Era of Rebellion Sourcebook
448

The Era of Rebellion Sourcebook was to be an RPG sourcebook, written by Pablo Hidalgo for West End Games and set to cover events of the Star Wars (1977) comics. It was canceled, although Hidalgo reused much of it for his The Starhoppers of Aduba-3 article in Star Wars Gamer 4. Category:Canceled West End Games sourcebooks
