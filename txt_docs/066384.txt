Spitting Rawl Technique
1257

thumb|A poster showing the Spitting Rawl Technique The Spitting Rawl Technique was a Teräs Käsi attack named after the creature of the same name. It was portrayed in the Spitting Rawl poster. The Spitting Rawl Technique was portrayed in the Spitting Rawl painting in the 2003 massively multiplayer online roleplaying game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, prior to the game's closure on December 15, 2011.
