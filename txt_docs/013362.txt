It was located in the Pii system inside the Arkanis sector of the galaxy. It was one of "Teeda's Eyes." It averaged 105 centimeters of precipitation annually. At one point, Pii III was the site of a Duros colony, which was later abandoned. The Greel Wood Logging Corporation had a significant presence on the world.
