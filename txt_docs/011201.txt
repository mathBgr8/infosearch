Bobby
3360

Bobby was a male Human Jedi Initiate studying with the Jedi Order during the final decade of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive Human, Bobby received his training in the ways of the Force from the Jedi Order at their Coruscant Temple. Placed into the Mighty Bear Clan, a clan of Initiates noted for their bravery, Bobby received direct training from the clan's minder, Grand Master Yoda. Bobby first appeared in the junior novelization of Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones. He later appeared in the non-canon TV special LEGO Star Wars: The Padawan Menace.
