Shrike (Alpha-52 starfighter)
2215

The Shrike was a modified Incom Corporation Alpha-52 starfighter prototype owned by Talon Karrde that was never developed for the open market. Karrde permanently loaned it to Mara Jade for her use around the time of Operation Shadow Hand. In 10 ABY it was impounded while in Katraasii Spaceport, but was soon retrieved by Ka'Pa the Hutt after Jade was captured by the crime lord Takara.
