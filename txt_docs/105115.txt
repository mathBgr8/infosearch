The Journal of Energy Physics was a scientific magazine where Nasdra Magrody published articles about his study of directed thought waves. He believed that it was through these articles that Palpatine came to believe he was Force-sensitive. *Children of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Scientific and medical publications
