Ni'Korish
2524

Ni'Korish was a Hapan Queen Mother whose reign coincided with the early part of Palpatine's rule in the wider Galaxy. She was known for her extreme anti-Jedi sentiments,The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse'The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey regarding them as a closed elite who monopolised power through their magic, and her grandson Isolder believed that if any Jedi had tried to hide in the Hapes Cluster during the Jedi Purge, she would have enjoyed destroying them. Ni'Korish had several daughters, including Ta'a Chume, who succeeded her as ruler, and Secciah. Because of her opposition to the Jedi, she gave her name to a clandestine faction which rose to prominence on Hapes during the Yuuzhan Vong War, opposed to renewed Jedi influence, and specifically to the succession of Ta'a Chume's Force-sensitive granddaughter Tenel Ka. *The Courtship of Princess Leia *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey *Galaxy of Intrigue Category:Females Category:Hapan Queen Mothers
