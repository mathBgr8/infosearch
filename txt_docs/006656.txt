|death=February 18, 2022 |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Author |companies=Dark Horse Comics |website= }} Tom Veitch (September 26, 1941 &ndash; February 18, 2022) was a comic book writer. He worked on several titles for Dark Horse Comics, including Tales of the Jedi and Star Wars: Dark Empire. His brother was Rick Veitch. In 2019, Veitch was writing a book about his work within the Star Wars franchise, and planned to release it that same year. However, at the time of his death, no such book has been released.
