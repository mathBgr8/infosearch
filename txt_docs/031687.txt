|eligibility= |recipients= |prerequisites= |upgrades= |first awarded= |last awarded= }} }} The Alliance Medal of Honor was a Rebel Alliance military decoration awarded to those who performed above and beyond the call of duty while serving in, or with, the Alliance Military. The award was a silver badge emblazoned with the red phoenix of the Alliance beneath an etching of an X-wing starfighter. It was attached to a red ribbon encrusted with various gems. The Galactic Empire had a similar award named Imperial Medal of Honor. By the time of the Battle of Yavin, an Imperial pilot shot down a Rebel pilot above Dantooine and stole his Alliance Medal of Honor. In 1 ABY, one such medal was awarded to a spacer alongside several Rebel supporters who assassinated an Imperial officer aboard a Rebel Corellian corvette captured by the Empire before he could used the ship against civilian targets.
