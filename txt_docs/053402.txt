Pohtarza
1119

Pohtarza was a male t'landa Til who was the Head Sacredot of Colony Five on the Hutt-controlled world of Ylesia. While he was officiating over a ceremony of Exultation, Pohtarza was assassinated by an Aar'aa on the payroll of Jabba the Hutt just prior to the Battle of Ylesia. *Rebel Dawn *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Sacredots Category:T'landa Til
