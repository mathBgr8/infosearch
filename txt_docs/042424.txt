Urrdorf City
1333

Urrdorf City was the smallest of the twenty orbital cities that orbited Duro. Disc-shaped, it was the lone survivor of the Battle of Duro during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Although lacking the hyperdrive of the larger cities, it was equipped with shield generators and powerful sublight engines, and escaped into interstellar space. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Duro city-ships
