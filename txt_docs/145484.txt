Ferret Baudoin
647

Ferret Baudoin was a writer and a video game designer, and was part of the design team of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords. He worked for Obsidian Entertainment. He eventually left the company and went to work for BioWare and then left them for Bethesda Softworks. {| !
