Core Post

Core Post was one of the New Republic Scout Service's two headquarters, in charge of NRSS' recordkeeping and continued funding. Located on Coruscant, it employed roughly 2,000 people and housed the Nova Core, a club for outstanding scouts. *Galaxy Guide 8: Scouts Category:Bureau of Scouting and Exploration Services Category:Coruscant government buildings
