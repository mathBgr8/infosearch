This time period corresponds to the year 0 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. |num books= |preceded by= |followed by=Star Wars: Episode I Adventures }} Star Wars Missions was a young-readers series and roleplaying game published by Scholastic beginning in September 1997. The series, developed by David Levithan, lasted for twenty adventure books, which were authored by Ryder Windham and Dave Wolverton. Scholastic introduced the Star Wars: Episode I Adventures game in September 1999 as a follow-up to Star Wars Missions. Star Wars Missions originally shipped to subscribers in a black cardboard box featuring illustrations of the Rebel Alliance X-wing starfighter and the Imperial TIE fighter.
