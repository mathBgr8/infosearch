Of Lish's fifteen moons, L-5 was the only one with a breathable atmosphere. Lish V was taken over by the Lant Mining Corporation, the last of ten moons in the system on which mining facilities were established by the company. The moon suffered many earthquakes due to its close proximity to the moons Lish IV and Lish VI, which created tidal pulls on L-5. To ensure the safety of its workers, the Lant Corporation populated the moon with repulsorlift-equipped cities, such as the capital, Gadde. *Flashpoint!
