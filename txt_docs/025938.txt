|imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Muugrah was a planet that was closer to the fringe of the galaxy than it was to the Galactic Core. Its capital was Slaibo, which was close to Squarn's Junkyard. It was a planet of open grasslands inhabited by prospectors and ranchers. Category:Terrestrial planets
