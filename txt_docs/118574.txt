George Buza
1950

George Buza (born January 7, 1949) is an actor whose first role was playing Gregg in one episode of Kung Fu. He later appeared on other live-action films and series like Highpoint, Seeing Things and The Amateur. Meanwhile he started voicing his first character, Chief Chirpa, in the first Ewoks season. A decade later he was a frequent guest star on Fox Kids' X-Men and appeared on four episodes of Mythic Warriors. His other appearances in cartoon animation were Spider-Man and Beyblade. Born in Cleveland, Ohio, Buza grew up in Canada. After his grandfather took him to the movies, he decided when still a boy that he wanted to be an actor.
