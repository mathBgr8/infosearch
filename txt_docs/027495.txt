Warshack Rojo
1664

Warshack Rojo was a Corellian officer in the New Republic Defense Force. He was an intense but confident man with a shaven scalp and a diamond earring in one ear, and in 25 ABY he commanded the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Rejuvenator and led a task force based out of Ord Mantell. He was killed in the Battle of Helska IV during the Yuuzhan Vong War, when the Rejuvenator was shot down by a massive fleet of coralskippers. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corellians Category:Humans Category:Males Category:New Republic Defense Fleet officers
