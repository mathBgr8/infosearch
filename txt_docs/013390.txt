The third planet in the system, Kulthis became a target of the Sith Lord Darth Vader around 3 ABY, who began to amp up the production of Imperial AT-AT walkers on nearby Belderone in preparation for an assault on the world. However, the Rebel base on Kulthis launched several fighter squadrons in a pre-emptive strike on Belderone, destroying the Imperial threat. During the era of the New Republic, Kulthis and Belderone became the site of Stenax settlements, although New Republic Intelligence was unaware of what interest the Stenaxes could possibly have had in the worlds.
