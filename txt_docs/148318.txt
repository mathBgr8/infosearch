Adana Vermor
1357

Adana Vermor was an Imperial Economic Advisor who monitored economic activities of the Empire. She was unknowingly Force sensitive, which helped her in managing the Empire's resources. Shortly before the Battle of Endor, the Imperial Security Bureau assigned Trevas Jotane to observe Vermor, who was suspected of leaking data to the Alliance to Restore the Republic and certain Noble Houses of the Tapani sector.
