Gonar
1665

Gonar was a pale, redheaded Human in Jabba's court who took an interest in the rancor. Gonar attempted to gain the position of rancor keeper by blackmailing Malakili, the current rancor keeper, with evidence of his secret dealings with Lady Valarian. However Malakili beat him to death and fed his corpse to the rancor.
