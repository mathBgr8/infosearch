VernanGroup
1341

VernanGroup, or The Vernan Group, was a successful industrial business in the final years of the Galactic Republic. The business also owned a large portion of the Minos-Mestra Corporation. Unfortunately, with the rise of the Empire, businesses came under closer scrutiny and the Minos-Mestra Co. suffered.
