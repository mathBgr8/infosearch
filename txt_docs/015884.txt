Cor Lane
697

The Cor Lane, or Cor Run, was a hyperlane located in the Tapani sector. The route began at the Barnaba system and passed through the Crella system, Cor I system, and Cor II system before reaching its terminus at the Cor III system in the Barnaba Province. The route was devoid of debris for the most part, allowing for quick travel between systems. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector hyperlanes
