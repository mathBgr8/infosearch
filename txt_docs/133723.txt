|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=144 |isbn=1593078021 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series=Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection |issues=5 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Volume 5: Light and Dark is the fifth of the Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection TBPs, released July 4, 2007. It is a hardcover version of Star Wars: Clone Wars Volume 4: Light and Dark. The Clone Wars have begun, and what it means to be a Jedi will be changed forever! Taking on new roles as generals, warriors, and even spies, the Jedi face difficult choices about their ideals, and what they are willing to sacrifice for the Republic they are sworn to protect. Jedi Master Quinlan Vos has had more than his share of brushes with the dark side of the Force, which is exactly why he is chosen to pierce the inner circle of Count Dooku, the ex-Jedi Separatist leader.
