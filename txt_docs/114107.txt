Zeven Mallat
1335

Zeven Mallat was an Imperial Security Bureau agent still serving the New Order in 16 ABY. In that year, he responded to a request from Grand Moff Gann of Sector 5 for information on the Yevetha by forwarding a report assembled by Obo Rin, author of the Catalog of Intelligent Life in the Galaxy.
