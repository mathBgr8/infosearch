|pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=17 }} Star Wars Tales 17 is the seventeenth issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. It was published on October 1, 2003 by Dark Horse Comics. Welcome to the dark side! In this issue of Star Wars Tales we turn our attention to all that is dark, creepy, and evil, bringing you tales sure to chill you to your core! Watch as Chewbacca comes face to face with undead specters on Hoth! Scream as Han Solo tries desperately to overcome a zombie hoard! And gasp in shock as Luke Skywalker battles a ghostly Darth Maul! This issue of Tales is sure to leave you SCREAMING for more! *"Planet of the Dead" *"All for You" *"Phantom Menaces" *"Ghosts of Hoth" *"The Apprentice" *"Dark Journey" The story "Ghosts of Hoth" was pitched by Rob Williams to Dark Horse Comics assistant editor Jeremy Barlow, as he was in charge of the Star Wars Tales issues. Williams did the story as an eight pager that impressed Barlow, leading him to request Williams to write more stories for the series and eventually the Star Wars: Rebellion series.
