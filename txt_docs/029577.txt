Boldheart
1098

Boldheart was a Corona-class frigate of the New Republic Defense Fleet. While on patrol during Admiral Daala's campaign in 11 ABY, Boldheart came across the Teljkon Vagabond&mdash;the New Republic's first encounter with the vessel. When Boldheart attempted to halt the vessel with a shot across its bow, the Vagabond disabled the frigate's engines with a single shot and fled into hyperspace.
