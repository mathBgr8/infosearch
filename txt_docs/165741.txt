Cluster grenade
780

thumb|A cluster grenade A cluster grenade was a type of grenade which was used by, among others, Renegade Squadron. Once thrown, the cluster grenade multiplied damage potential by exploding a charge in midair and dropping four smaller charges in a several meter radius. The charges exploded upon impact with heavy damage within the radius of each. *Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith video game *Star Wars Battlefront: Elite Squadron *Star Wars Battlefront: Renegade Squadron Category:Grenades
