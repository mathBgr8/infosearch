Whitecloak fighter
1966

Whitecloak fighters were modified CloakShape fighters employed by the Jedi Order prior to the Clone Wars. They were painted white by their pilots, lending to their nickname. The Whitecloaks were mostly phased out as the Jedi Order received dedicated starfighters like the Delta 7. *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook *Starships of the Galaxy, Saga Edition Category:CloakShape fighters Category:Starship classes of the Jedi Order
