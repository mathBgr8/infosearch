Jinkins' personal starfighter, the Freefall, was a modified version of this craft. Measuring 30 meters in length, the Freefall-class was named for the fact that it was designed to simply drop unguided bombs on a target. The craft's central fuselage could accommodate up to 20 metric tons of cargo, and was attached to two huge, wing-mounted sublight engines.
