|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: Dark Force Rising |issue=4 }} Dark Force Rising 4 was the fourth issue in the Star Wars: Dark Force Rising comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel Dark Force Rising. It was released August 27, 1997. Luke's new mentor, Jedi Master C'Baoth, is teaching him new ways to use the Force, ways that seem . . . inappropriate. But ways that may become necessary in light of the fact that Grand Admiral Thrawn has taken command of the remnants of the Imperial fleet.
