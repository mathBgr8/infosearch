Burnell Tucker
2124

Burnell Tucker is a Canadian actor who appeared in the first two films of the Star Wars original trilogy. His role in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope was uncredited, while Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back credited him as one of several "Other Officers" in the Rebel Forces. In Star Wars Legends, Tucker's character in A New Hope is identified as Del Goren, a Rebel technician during the Battle of Yavin. For The Empire Strikes Back, Tucker plays two different Echo Base controllers, Romas Navander and Wyron Serper.
