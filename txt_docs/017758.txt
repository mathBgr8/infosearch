|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=3 BBYThe date of this story is established by in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire |issue=2 }} Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire 2 was the second issue in the Star Wars: Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire series of comics. It was released February 24, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. As Boba Fett hones in [sic] on his quarry, he is followed by Darth Vader's hired goons, but the Dark Lord trusts no one in this particular endeavor, nor does he expect any of them to walk away once the prize is his. But Boba Fett knows the craft of tracking, and he also knows that if Vader wants something this badly, it must be very precious indeed!
