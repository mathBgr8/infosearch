Algrann
1769

Algrann was a male senior watch and technical officer on Outbound Flight during the time of the Separatist Crisis. In 27 BBY, Algrann worked for the Outbound Flight Project. As a technician, he ensured the smooth running of the project. During a shift in which he was supposed to work with Sivv in the monitor room, the Jedi Master Justyn Ma'Ning claimed the space for himself and left the technicians outside the door where they met Chas Uliar. They explained that Ma'Ning wanted to bring some students closer to the control room.
