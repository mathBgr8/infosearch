|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=*30 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion *44 BBY Star Wars: The Stark Hyperspace War trade paperback |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=37 }} Star Wars 37 is the thirty-seventh issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on December 19, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. Iaco Stark has cut off the hyperspace lanes to Republic ships and the remnants of the Republic Army and Navy are stranded with the Jedi Tyvokka, Plo Koon, Adi Gallia, Qui-Gon Jinn, and Obi-Wan Kenobi on Troiken. Meanwhile, Tholme and his Padawan discover the truth about the scarcity of bacta. The worst is yet to come in this installment of the Stark Hyperspace War.
