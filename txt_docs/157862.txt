|class=Insect |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor=Red |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Yavin 4 |habitat=Forests |diet=Carrion |language= }} Tanc mites were insects native to the jungle moon Yavin 4. These creatures had six legs, two arms that ended in claws and two antennae. Tanc mite hives were ruled by a queen. Tanc mites wre scavengers eating carrion. They were covered with a rock-hard shell that protects against electricity, fire, and most physical damage. In 1 ABY, a small population of tanc mites populated the atrium of Tansarii Point Station.
