Cilia Dil
1337

Cilia Dil was a Human female citizen who lived on the planet Junction V with her husband Jaren Dil during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. Cilia Dil was a political dissident who aided in a rebellion against the Guardians of her homeworld. After being imprisoned by the Guardians, a force set up by Jedi Master Dooku's old rival, Lorian Nod she escaped with the help of Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi. Cilia eventually ended the Annihilator conspiracy set up by Lorian and was proclaimed as a hero. *Legacy of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans
