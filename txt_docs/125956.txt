Cafarel
1934

Cafarel, also known as criblez, were Zeltron courtesans known for fulfilling any sexual desire a being might have, without limits. They were sought out by many crime lords, especially Hutts.The Essential Guide to Alien Species Female cafarel outnumbered their male counterparts by a factor of twenty to one. At the time of the Galactic Empire, Zeltron cafarel were often seen sharing Grand Admiral Miltin Takel's private quarters aboard his personal Imperial-class Star Destroyer Magic Dragon. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Service occupations Category:Zeltron culture
