Eela Valotta
1393

Eela Valotta was a female Twi'lek who was an agent of the so-called "Freedom Convoy" during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Her associate was Tray'la Sheek. She was wanted by Jabba Desilijic Tiure for kidnapping several Twi'leks from the Hutt's personal harem.
