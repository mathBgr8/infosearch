Feldspar
1192

Feldspar was a mineral. Along with diorite, it was one of the primary components of the mantle of Endor. The planet Guiteica held extensive underground reserves of this mineral. Many different pro-Imperial mining companies had tried to convince native Bitthævrians to perform mining activity on the planet. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galaxy at War Category:Minerals
