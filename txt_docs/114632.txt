Pure Sabacc (Marauder-class)
1722

Pure Sabacc was a Marauder-class corvette in private service. The corvette was used as a transport vessel by its captain, Vortia Kwis. Kwis agreed to ferry refugees from Dubrillion to Dantooine shortly after the Battle of Dubrillion. After fighting their way out of a Yuuzhan Vong sneak attack at Dantooine, Kwis lost faith in the New Republic and joined the Peace Brigade, adapting Pure Sabacc into a traveling recruitment center. *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Marauder-class Assault Corvettes Category:Starships of the Peace Brigade
