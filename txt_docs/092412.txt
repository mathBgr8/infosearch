Delta Unit
555

Delta Unit was a military unit of the Alliance Special Forces. It was a part of infiltrator Scandium Team, and the most notorious of its units, being highly respected by other Rebels. In particular, SpecForce Delta Team was named Delta to honor Delta Unit.
