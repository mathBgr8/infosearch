XP-2000 prototype speeder bike
1328

The Ikas-Adno XP-2000 prototype speeder bike was a racing vehicle as fast as a swoop. Four meters long, the prototype had a flight ceiling of 50 meters and could achieve 1,300 kilometers per hour. However, usage placed the rider under unhealthy G-forces. As a result, the vehicle was never put into production.
