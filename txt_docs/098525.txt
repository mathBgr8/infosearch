Code 497.8
894

Code 497.8 was a law of the Galactic Republic, the ruling government of the galaxy at the time, mandating that a representative of the Republic would have to leave a planet if ordered to do so by that planet's leadership. This law made an only appearance in the 2004 novel, The Cestus Deception, by Steven Barnes. *The Cestus Deception Category:Codes and regulations
