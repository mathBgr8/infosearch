|publisher=Decipher |release date=November 1998 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Special Edition Limited |followed by=Endor Limited }} Enhanced Premiere was a boutique product created and produced in November of 1998 for the Star Wars Customizable Card Game. The package came with one new character card and several packs of Premiere. It was mainly used as a way for Decipher to get rid of excess product (the Premier packs) as well as create a new, easy to obtain, easy to use, (also basically a "must-have" due to its game-text and relevance to the game), and a good starting point for newer players. Decipher would later produce two more "Enhanced" versions: Enhanced Cloud City (used to get rid of excess Cloud City packs), and Enhanced Jabba's Palace (used to get rid of excess Jabba's Palace packs). Six new cards (one of the following per package): *Luke with Lightsaber *Obi-Wan with Lightsaber *Darth Vader with Lightsaber *Han with Blaster *Leia with Blaster Rifle *Boba Fett with Blaster Rifle Category:Star Wars Customizable Card Game
