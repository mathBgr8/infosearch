Shevre'starn
539

Shevre'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the midlands of the planet's eastern continent. Mesdri'starn was located to the west, Rhan'starn to the southwest, and Sal Vhe'starn with Fevye'starn to the southeast. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
