Miss Vix
967

Miss Vix was a female Vjun fox native to Vjun. She was a Force familiar of Viscountess Whirry Malreaux, who she constantly followed. She lived with her master in the Château Malreaux. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Named pets
