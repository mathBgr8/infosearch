It was taken over by the Galactic Empire for its nova crystals, and the native Kluuzot were enslaved. Following the Battle of Endor, the sector governor seceded and established a neutral terrority. The Kluuzot were freed, and various commercial investors moved to the planet. The planet Krann was introduced in the 1993 West End Games sourcebook Twin Stars of Kira, which placed it in the Opiteihr system.
