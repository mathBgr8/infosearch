|companies= |website= }} Tim Dry is a mime artist who portrayed J'Quille and a Mon Calamari officer in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. In his book entitled Continuum -The Star Wars Phenomenon As Experienced From The Inside, he recounts being in one of the biggest-grossing movies of all time.
