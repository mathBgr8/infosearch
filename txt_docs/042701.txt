Sembric Skamini
1366

Sembric Skamini was a Human male who served as the proprietor of an ore prospecting launch base on Mon Gazza, along with his wife Ashielle Skamini and was the father of Warpoc Skamini, who became Padawan Zett Jukassa. He and his wife were murdered by Black Sun operatives seven years after the birth of Zett Jukassa. Zett sensed the murder through the Force, and was involved in the investigation with his master, Mierme Unill.
