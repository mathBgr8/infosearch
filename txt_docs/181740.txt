Vevut Squad
3188

Vevut Squad was a clone commando unit that was part of the Special Operations Brigade in the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. With all of its members created on the planet Kamino, Vevut Squad was trained by Mandalorian sergeant Rav Bralor. In 21 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Republic Commando: True Colors to 21 BBY. Vevut Squad was one of the many commando units commanded by Jedi General Bardan Jusik. In 19 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Order 66: A Republic Commando Novel to 21 BBY. Vevut Squad&mdash;now under command of Jedi General Etain Tur-Mukan&mdash;went to the Republic Station Nerrif to be reassigned. Dec, the sergeant of the squad, led Tur-Mukan to the mess and encouraged her to eat something, after exiting their shuttle. Tur-Mukan noted that the squad behaved like sons trying to please their mother. Vevut Squad was first mentioned in Republic Commando: True Colors, a 2007 novel written by Karen Traviss.
