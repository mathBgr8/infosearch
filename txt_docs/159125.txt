Whirlwind (Immobilizer 418 cruiser)
1928

Whirlwind was an Immobilizer 418 cruiser used by the Galactic Empire. It was the lead ship of Task Force Whirlwind that was supposed to arrive at a convoy that was being ambushed by Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's forces to trap them. Unfortunately Zaarin sent his own Immobilizer 418 cruiser called Maelstrom to destroy the Whirlwind.
