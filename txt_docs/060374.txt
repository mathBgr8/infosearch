Pizzazz 5
1205

Pizzazz 5 is the fifth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated February 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. The cover illustration features C-3PO as a drummer playing in a band led by singer Linda Ronstadt, and including Marvel characters Doctor Strange and Captain America playing guitar. 05
