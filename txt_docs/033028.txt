Duan
1741

Duan was a female Human officer who served Darth Malak's Sith Empire during the Jedi Civil War. In 3956 BBY, she was stationed on Manaan. During her stay, she was known to be a heavy drinker. Duan will only have a conversation if Revan is a male.
