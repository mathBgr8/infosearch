New Plympto resistance (Yuuzhan Vong War)
1753

The New Plympto resistance was the Nosaurian resistance movement on the planet New Plympto against the Yuuzhan Vong during their invasion of the galaxy. It was led by Jedi Numa and Alema Rar; the Twi'lek sisters only went into public one at a time, with their lekku hidden, causing the Yuuzhan Vong to believe they were a single Human woman. Kyp's Dozen, a starfighter squadron ran supplies for them. In 27 ABY, the Yuuzhan Vong tired of the resistance and unleashed a virus onto the world, instantly killing seven million of the planet's fifteen million inhabitants.
