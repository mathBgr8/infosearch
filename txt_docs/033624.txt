Lumus
1458

Lumus was a former Imperial assassin condemned to death for his part in a brutal massacre. However, his sentence was commuted by Darth Vader along with those of Nevo, Fraal, and Gann so that they could follow Boba Fett along his hunt of Abal Karda.
