Munch-fungus bread
1052

Munch-fungus bread was bread cooked with munch-fungus. At Mal'aa's Kitchens, a Kala'uun restaurant, it was served as a side dish to rycrit stew. Munch-fungus bread was first introduced to the Star Wars universe in the Star Wars roleplaying game book Platt's Starport Guide, written by Peter M. Schweighofer and published by West End Games in February 1995. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Breads Category:Twi'lek food
