Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 6

Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 6 is the sixth issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in winter 1996. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 6, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with a Topps Finest chromium Boba Fett promotional trading card and a 1996 Topps Star Wars: Return of the Jedi Widevision promotional card.
