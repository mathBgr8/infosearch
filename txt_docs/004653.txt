Project Ambition
1737

Project Ambition was the codename of Director of Imperial Intelligence Ysanne Isard's plan to liquidate the Imperial Ruling Council and dispose of Interim Galactic Emperor Sate Pestage, which would give her complete control of the Galactic Empire, following the Battle of Endor.
