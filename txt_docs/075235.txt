Pesktda
1751

}} Pesktda was the capital city of Garqi, a graceful city of broad avenues lined with bimiza trees and dotted with open-air plazas. It was the only consolidated population center on the planet and contained one-quarter of the world population. It contained the Garqi Agricultural University, the Pesktda Xenobotanical Garden, and the academic Wlesc neighborhood.
