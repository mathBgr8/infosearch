Lauhu
1963

Lauhu was a CR90 corvette which served with the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. It once supplied the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Anvil which was carrying the survivors of the destroyed Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Maximus. During that operation, Anvil sustained two attacks from the Imperial escort frigate Firelance, which launched squadrons of TIEs.
