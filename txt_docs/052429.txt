Castaan Armory
901

Castaan Armory was a specialized body armor manufacturing company. They customized each suit to meet the needs of different alien species. One of these was the Staad armor which was a form of protective armor designed for Twi'leks. *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Personal gear companies
