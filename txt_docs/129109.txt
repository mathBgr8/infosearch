|description= |animals= |plants= |other= |edible=Ortolans |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Kiwip grass was a grass edible by Ortolans. Around 4 ABY,Kiwip grass's mention in "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale" takes place a few months before the part of the story that is concurrent with , the events of which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. the Ortolan musician Max Rebo was excited at the prospect of eating kiwip grass at an upcoming dinner, but he ultimately received neither the dinner nor the kiwip grass. Kiwip grass was mentioned in "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale," a short story written by John Gregory Betancourt and published in the Tales from Jabba's Palace story collection in 1995.
