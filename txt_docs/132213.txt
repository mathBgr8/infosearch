Tensiger

**Imperial Army |masters= |apprentices= }} Tensiger was an officer in the Imperial Army. During the Galactic Civil War, Tensiger commanded the 6th regiment during the Raid on Picutorion. During the battle, elements of Tensiger's armor battalion penetrated a tracking station occupied by the Alliance to Restore the Republic.
