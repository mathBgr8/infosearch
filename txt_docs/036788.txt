Furellas
1349

Furellas was a Force-sensitive female Wookiee with light brown fur and shaved shoulders. She was an associate of the Ithorian Kikow and a lieutenant in the Kalmec, a Wookiee criminal organization with rumored ties to the Separatists. Furellas helped smuggle a Ralltiir tiger kitten out of Ralltiir for Vareesa Enan in the years leading up to the fall of the Republic. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Females Category:Force-sensitives Category:Wookiees
