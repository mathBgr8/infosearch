The Characters of Star Wars was a featurette in the 2004 release of the Star Wars Original Trilogy, focusing on the following characters: *Luke Skywalker *Anakin Skywalker *Han Solo *Chewbacca *Leia Organa *C-3PO *R2-D2 *Owen Lars *Beru Whitesun Lars *Wilhuff Tarkin *Obi-Wan Kenobi *Lando Calrissian Category:Home video featurettes
