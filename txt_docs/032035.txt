Cloud City Casino
691

Cloud City Casino was a style of sabacc play that was presumably developed by the Yarith Bespin casino, located on Cloud City. The notable variant in this rule set is that players have the option to play toward a score closest to 0, instead of just 23 or -23. Played with four or five cards, zero is statistically harder to achieve than the two forms of sabacc from the traditional set. *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire *Dark Apprentice Category:Sabacc Category:Sabacc variants
