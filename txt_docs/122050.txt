Power rod
1537

thumb|250px Power rods were mysterious crystalline formations that grew in the cavern of Haven Base on Arbra. They perpetuated huge amounts of energy and released harmless bolts of lightning. They were the main source of energy on which the Hoojibs fed. They also served as the power supply for the Rebels in Haven Base. Unfortunately, the heat they generated also attracted a slivilith. Luke Skywalker believed they were powered by the Force.
