Cult of the Power Droids
768

The Cult of the Power Droids was a religious sect solicited by rogue GNK power droids. Apparently, a duo of the droids would come to one's door and request funding for this religious fringe group. Advice from renowned linguist Ebenn Q3 Baobab was that one should merely utter the phrase "Gonk. Gonk. Gonk ko kyenga see," a highly controversial statement that was not allowed to be legally translated (see Baobab Security Directive 51-C). *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Religious organizations
