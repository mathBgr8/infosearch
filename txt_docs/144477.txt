Baobab Merchant Fleet Complex
557

The Baobab Merchant Fleet Complex was a docking complex on the planet Manda. In 23 BBY, it suffered an explosion from a freighter accident that killed over 2,000 employees and grounded 125 Baobab Merchant vessels. It was later determined that Lasro Javeq, the freighter captain, was behind the explosion. Category:Manda locations
