Transit Galactic Shipping
710

Transit Galactic Shipping was a company that specialized in hauling cargo across interstellar distances. The company was based out of the world of Cybloc XII, where it operated several massive warehouses. In order to investigate the dealings of Dzym, Seti Ashgad, and their dealings with Loronar Corporation, the Chadra-Fan Yarbolk Yemm shipped the droids C-3PO and R2-D2 through the network serviced by Transit Galactic Shipping. *Planet of Twilight Category:Transport companies
