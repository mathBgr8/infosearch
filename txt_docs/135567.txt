The cadet served the Empire of the Hand. In 19 ABY,The events of Red Sky, Blue Flame take place in 19 ABY according to The Essential Reader's Companion. when the academy was attacked by pirates, cadet commander Shawnkyr Nuruodo commanded Fenlish and the cadet Khana to gather eight charric weapons and energy packs. The cadets ran to a nearby weapons cache, which they could not open, asking Nuruodo to open it; however, she was unable to do so. Human cadet Jagged Fel then opened the cache with a blaster, giving its contents to the cadet commander. Shortly after, under Nuruodo's directive, Fenlish and the other cadets fled to the academy's forests. The battle ended with the pirates' defeat. Fenlish appeared in the short story "Red Sky, Blue Flame," which was written by Elaine Cunningham and published in the seventh issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine on October 16, 2001.
