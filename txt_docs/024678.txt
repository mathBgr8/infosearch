SecuriTech
1129

SecuriTech was an influential vehicle manufacturing company within the Corporate Sector. SecuriTech supplied the Corporate Sector Authority with patrol vehicles which included the Espo Walker 91 and the Espo Walker 101. It also produced Gladiator walkers for civilians. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Arms & Equipment Guide Category:Vehicle manufacturing companies
