Slork
1442

Slorks were solitary, omnivorous ungulates native to the swamps of Kidron, but also found on Corellia. They vaguely resembled swine, and were fiercely territorial. While they seldom engaged in predation, they scavenged on the remains of quamin kills. They also ate industrial waste and garbage, and were themselves immune to predation due to their own stench.
