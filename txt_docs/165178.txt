Batattis
596

Batattis was a modified CorelliSpace Gymsnor-3 light freighter owned by the Ho'Din terrorist Ort Hoogra-D'En. He had the ship hidden in his base on Pallaxides. He planned to use it to escape the planet in the event of an attack. His escape would involve launching two decoy freighters before he escaped.
