During the last few years of the Galactic Republic, she frequented the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall located in the Uscru Entertainment District of her homeworld. During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Gosan descended down and exited a turboliftStar Wars: Complete Locations in the Outlander Club, just as Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered, searching for bounty hunter Zam Wesell. As Skywalker walked through the club, she stood at a table with a Kajain'sa'Nikto and two other Humans. Gosan had light skin, blonde hair, and a feminine first name of Human origin.Galactic Campaign Guide She wore a red dress with black boots while at the club.
