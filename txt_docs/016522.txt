This mountainous world was a small mining colony maintained by the Galactic Empire. The planet held important ruins from a vanished civilization, including the Heffrin Aqueducts, which spanned Heffrin's continents; research during the Cold War between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire identified the Aqueducts' builders as early Ithorian explorers.
