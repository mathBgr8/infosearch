Skar'kla
2897

|diet=Carnivore |language= }} The skar'kla, skar-kla, or glitterclaw, was a semiaquatic, carnivorous reptile, lethal predator native to Bothawui. Skar'klas had a thick fur that protected them from the freezing cold of the high elevations. Its almost-white color provided camouflage in the mountainous and snowy terrains they thrived, and was the origin of the animal's name. They existed in prides of three to five mated pairs with their kittens (each litter produced eight or nine young). They hunted in packs of three or four. Stealthy hunters that relied on quick, sudden bursts of activity; lacking the fierce strength of the krak'jya, they relied on speed, able to neutralize their prey in a single attack. Skar'kla were often found in Bothawui's mountains and shy of civilized areas, although they would attack lone travelers.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Distantly related to the feline krak'jya, the skar'kla had evolutionarily diverged from its relative to adapt more for speed rather than power.
