|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 8 |reprinted in= |pages=18 |timeline= |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Versus" is a comic story in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 8. Grandly surveying the audience of his arena, Mondo-Mod the Hutt announced the contenders for the evening: one Jedi Master, Luminara Unduli, against an assortment of beasts, with no lightsaber and no Force. For the first fight, Mondo-Mod unveils a wampa, who Unduli quickly dispatches by jumping up, grabbing him by his horns, and flipping him, using simple physics. Second, the Hutt announces a durkii, who the Jedi defeats by darting underneath his legs and back, causing the creature to flip onto its back. Finally, quite displeased, Mondo-Mod declares the final contender: a heavily outfitted droid, who is quickly disabled when Unduli flicks a rock into its visor. Winning their bet, Unduli returns to Mondo-Mod, who obliges their deal by handing her the coordinates of a Separatist droid factory on Diorda. Afterwards, the Master meets with her Padawan, Barriss Offee, who, disguised as a masked gambler, managed to win 10,000,000 credits, forcing the Hutt to go out of business.
