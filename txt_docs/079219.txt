Droid bio-assault spray
706

Bio-assault spray was an illegal weapon in many star systems and employed by combat droids during the Great Sith War. These sprays would fire a chemical that broke down a target on a molecular level, leaving nothing but a gray slime behind. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Chemical weaponry Category:Droid weaponry
