Quarren Command Center 12
898

Quarren Command Center 12 was one of the Quarren Isolation League's command centers on Mon Calamari. During the Battle of Mon Calamari, it held out after the League's leadership was captured by Kit Fisto, now receiving orders from the Moappa.
