thumb|right|250px|Comparison between [[Duros/Legends|Duros and Neimoidians]] Near-Duros were species that had evolved in slightly different directions to base-line Duros due to living in isolation from other Duros for many thousands of years. This was often as a result of adapting to different environments. The most well-known of the Near-Duros species were the Neimoidians. thumb|150px|A possible yellow near-Duros.
