Juno II
1843

The Juno II was a Republic Sienar Systems Marauder-class corvette in the Kathol Republic Navy. In 8 ABY, the Juno II responded to a distress call from the Bravado III, alongside her sister ships Mirandine, Quillion, Siroma, Solidarity, and Triumph. Together, they intercepted the modified New Republic corvette FarStar and impounded it on Dayark. *The Kathol Outback *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Marauder-class Assault Corvettes Category:Starships of the Kathol Republic Navy
