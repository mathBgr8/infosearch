Pachydermoid
2961

A pachydermoid, also known as pachyderm,The New Essential Guide to Alien Species was a type of species, unrelated and from separate planets, members of which shared certain physical characteristics. Pachydermoids typically had either trunks or elongated mouths, thick skin, and hoof-like feet. Another typical&mdash;but not all-encompassing&mdash;trait was the possession of tusks or tusk-like teeth.
