|capital=Caucus Chambers |language= |currency= |religious= |holiday= |anthem= |formed= |established= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Confederacy of Independent Systems }} The Jabiim Congress, also known as the Jabiim Council,Galaxy at War was the primary governmental body of the planet Jabiim that supported the Galactic Republic. At some point, a Jabiimite Spokesbeing Alto Stratus executed almost all of the Congressional Jabiimi Loyalists. Orliss Gillmunn was one of the few to escape. Later, Stratus made a speech to the Jabiim Congress that rebuffed appeals from the Galactic Republic in the Caucus Chambers. The speech was later recorded in a HoloNet News Core Edition report on the situation on Handooine and Jabiim.
