New Republic War College
999

The New Republic War College was an educational institution responsible for the preservation of and education on topics of warfare. Established on Woostri shortly after the creation of the New Republic, the War College maintained an extensive library of materials covering the tactics, strategies, weapons, history and general methodologies of warfare and militaries across galactic space and time.
