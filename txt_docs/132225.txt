Kanda
1270

Kanda was a male Human Jedi Master stationed on Ilum in 3640 BBY, when he was killed. Kanda appears in the video game Star Wars: The Old Republic as a Champion boss on Ilum. He is not directly involved in any quests, but can be killed for loot and as part of the achievement.
