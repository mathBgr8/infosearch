Tarrant Snil
1441

Tarrant Snil was an Imperial customs officer who worked in Lay Pa-Sidian on G'wenee. As part of his plan to ingratiate himself with Grappa the Hutt, Kir Kanos captured Snil under the guise of bounty hunter Kenix Kil.
