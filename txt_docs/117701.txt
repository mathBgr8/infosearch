Hydrocarbon
1298

Hydrocarbons were organic compounds that were composed of a combination of the following elements: carbon, nitrogen, oxygen, and hydrogen. The planet Shaymore contained hydrocarbon-bearing fossil fuels that were mined by the Galactic Empire.
