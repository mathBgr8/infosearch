Vima-Da-Boda's Master
2493

A male Jedi Master served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic during the Golden Age of the Old Republic and was responsible for the training of Jedi Vima-Da-Boda. A Force-sensitive male was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order during the time of peace in the centuries following the Ruusan Reformations of the Galactic Senate. Raised at the Coruscant Jedi Temple as a Initiate in the Temple academy, the young man was selected for training as a Padawan by a wise Master. During his training with his Master, she presented him with an ancient lightsaber, leaving it a mystery as to where it came from. After traveling the galaxy together, the man ascended to the rank of Jedi Master and between 200 and 100 BBY, selected the young Initiate Vima-Da-Boda as his own Padawan.
