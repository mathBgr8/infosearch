Inamo
2332

Inamo was a Rebel Alliance EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in service after the Battle of Hoth. It was an early casualty of TIE Avenger starfighters, who destroyed it, along with X-wings and transport Mule 1 with a rocket attack during a resupply operation. The MC80 Star Cruiser Ardent arrived too late to save it, and itself escaped with a badly damaged hull.
