Tal'cara
2757

Tal'cara was the capital city of the planet Kothlis. It contained a Spynet facility. During the Galactic Civil War, the Havocs swoop gang sacked the city. Maxeena Sov'lya was a local crime boss who ran protection rackets and smuggling in the city.Platt's Smugglers Guide The Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide includes a city map of Tal'cara that is inexplicably titled "Kla'taal." However, the name Kla'taal is not mentioned anywhere else within the text or in any other source material.
