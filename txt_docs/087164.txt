D'zor
1826

D'zor was a Kajain'sa'Nikto male who was a resident outlaw of Mos Espa in 32 BBY. He and his thugs camped out in the back of Barbo's store, keeping a repeating blaster for protection. When the Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn sought to trade parts with Barbo, the Gran told him talk to D'zor. When Jinn entered the backyard, D'zor and his posse opened fire on the Jedi, who was forced to kill them in self-defense.
