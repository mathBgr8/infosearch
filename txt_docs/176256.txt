In 1 ABY, he was the warehouse manager of Tosche Station. At some point, he bought some stuff to the Jawa traders of Sand Crawler 345. During a transfer mission to get power converters, his speeder bike broke down in the desert. His boss Cuan Talon hired a spacer to rescue him but Janus was killed by a group of Desert Demons. Janus was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
