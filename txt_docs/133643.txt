Umgullian blob massage therapy
547

Umgullian blob massage therapy was used to cure people under stress. Blobs would slide themselves over someone to help relax their muscles. However, the therapy had its drawbacks. Rith Murisson, a native of Krnay, got disfiguring marks on her back after receiving blob therapy. *Jedi Search *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Medical technology Category:Society and culture
