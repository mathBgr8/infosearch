**Galactic Senate *Confederacy of Independent Systems |masters= |apprentices= }} Corlissi Ludar was a Sluissi male senator who represented Sluis Van and the Sluis sector in the Galactic Senate, succeeding Senator Bor Gracus. During the outbreak of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY, Corlissi Ludar left his seat and announced his withdrawal from the Senate since he found how corrupt the Republic was. He was one of the representatives from the Rimma Trade Route that left the Republic.
