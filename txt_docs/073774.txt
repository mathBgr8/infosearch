Ooi Say Hien
357

Ooi Say Hien is a Star Wars fan who designed the Critokian in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was picked as the overall winner in the under 16 years old category. He was 13 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
