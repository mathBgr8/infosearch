Pad 642 was a landing pad at Erwithat Spaceport on Pembric II. In 8 ABY, an Aegis-class combat shuttle assigned to the New Republic CR90 corvette FarStar utilized this pad when visiting Pembric II. Category:Docking bays Category:Pembric II locations
