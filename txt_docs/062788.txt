Mundi family
1817

The Mundi family was a Cerean family from Cerea. Jedi Master Ki-Adi-Mundi was the most famous member of the family. Due to the low Cerean birthrate, he was allowed by the other Jedi to follow the Cerean custom of polygamous marriage&mdash;he had four honor wives and seven daughters.
