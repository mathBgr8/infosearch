Thousands of years before the Galactic Republic discovered the Anx on Gravlex Med, the Anx developed interstellar travel and colonized their neighboring systems in what would come to be known as Anx Space. Unfortunately for them, the Anx quickly encountered the warlike Shusugaunt who viewed them as enemies from without. The Shusugaunt then conquered the worlds of Anx Space and enslaved the Anx, driving them from their colony worlds back to Gravlex Med.
