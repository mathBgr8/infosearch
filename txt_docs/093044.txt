|intl release= |pages= |issue=9 |featured= |series=The Official Star Wars Fact File }} The Official Star Wars Fact File 9 was the ninth issue of The Official Star Wars Fact File, released on February 27, 2002. The issue was authored by Darren Allanson, Andrew Darling, Chris Gardner, and Iain Lowson.
