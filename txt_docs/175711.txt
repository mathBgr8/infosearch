Kalif
900

Kalif was a Xiytiar-class heavy transport belonging to the Imperials. Kalif was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupters vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor to the Corrupter in the Mev system. Kalif cargo was "probe droids". The ship was then attacked by Rebel raiders and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire Category:Xiytiar-class heavy transports
