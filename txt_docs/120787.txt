EPT-212
1482

thumb|right|250px|The EPT-212 at Chalmun's Cantina The EPT-212 was a security device manufactured by Fabritech. The device scanned for frequencies specific to droid wireless systems and droid power cell oscillations. Chalmun installed a refurbished EPT-212 in his Mos Eisley Cantina to discourage droids from entering the premises.
