Jarg (Mandalorian)
1774

Jarg was a Human male Mandalorian who joined the Neo-Crusaders during the Mandalorian Wars. Following the end of the war, he joined Sherruk's group of bandits, along with Reeza. He was a pilot of XT-model bike. They eventually came to Dantooine, where they terrorized and extorted the local populace.
