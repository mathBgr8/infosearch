Wolgiyn tree
1543

The wolgiyn tree was a tree that grew on the Mid Rim &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Ansion system planet Ansion. When the Jedi Anakin Skywalker and Barriss Offee and their companion Bulgan were threatened by a flock of dangerous kyren birds, Skywalker noted that the only nearby shelter was a grove of three wolgiyn trees.
