Firewind
734

Firewind was a Victory-class Star Destroyer that served in the Death Squadron. During the Battle of Hoth, it was not in its assigned position when the assault began. This was due to Admiral Ozzel ordering the fleet to drop out of hyperspace close to Hoth rather than a farther distance away.
