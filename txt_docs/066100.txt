Deployed in 22 BBY during the First Battle of Geonosis by the Grand Army of the Republic against the secessionist Separatists,The New Essential Chronology the walker was one of numerous weapons and vehicles acquired by Jedi Master Yoda, who had delayed his arrival to the planet Geonosis to retrieve these military supplies. Like all AT-TE walkers, 23X was 13.2 meters in length, 5.32 meters wide and 5.02 meters tall. It could achieve a maximum speed of 60 km/h and had a cargo capacity of 10 metric tons. Armed with six anti-personnel laser cannons and a single mass-driver cannon, the walker was manned by at least four crew members&mdash;a pilot, a spotter and at least two gunners/technicians.
