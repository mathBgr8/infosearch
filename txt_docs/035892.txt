|lifespan= |origin=Julsujod III |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Stribers were an aquatic species originally native to Julsujod III. The Stribers relocated to the water-covered planet Iskalon to escape a cataclysm that struck their homeworld around five millennia before the Galactic Civil War, states that the species constituting the Iskalonian cultural group were brought to Iskalon approximately five millennia before the Galactic Civil War. Since "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series" establishes that the relocation of the Iskalonian species was prefaced by the cataclysms on the species' original homeworlds, the cataclysms must also have occurred around that time. and became a part of the Iskalonian School, a cultural group of several aquatic species that had traveled to Iskalon with the aid of spacefaring benefactors. Blue-skinned and telepathic, Stribers were known as very slow to react when addressed, as they preferred to carefully examine all aspects of a situation before acting.
