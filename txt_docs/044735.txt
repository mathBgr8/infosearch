Balosur
1282

Balosur or Balosar was the native tongue of the Balosar species from the planet Balosar. It was a crudely-written language made of throaty rasps and deep vocalizations. Written Balosur seemed less complex than written Basic due to its crude aspect, but in fact was considered to be no less complex than Basic.
