Berubian
729

A precious mineral, berubian was a frequent tool of con-artists who would "salt" a mine, giving it the impression of having found a valuable cache of berubian. Lando Calrissian was fooled by such a ruse in the Borgo asteroid belt. *Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures *Rebel Dawn *The Essential Chronology *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Minerals Category:Ores
