|pages= |upc= |timeline=0 ABYThe Essential Guide to Characters places the Thirteenth Imperial Diplomatic Conclave shortly after the Battle of Yavin. |series=Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures |issue=5 }} Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 5 was the fifth issue of the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Russ Manning. Princess Leia narrowly escapes an Imperial blockade and is forced to crash-land on a nearby planet... a planet that happens to be an Imperial labor colony! There she discovers that the colony is one of the Empire's main sources of volatile megonite moss, a central ingredient in Imperial ammunition. What's worse, Leia is pressed into service by Lady Tarkin, the angry, bereaved wife of Grand Moff Tarkin, who died aboard the Death Star.
