The Lucky Star
605

The Lucky Star was a rundown casino that was located in the Corellian sector on Nar Shaddaa. In 5 BBY Han Solo visited the place when he searched for his old friend Mako Spince on the Smugglers' Moon. *The Hutt Gambit *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Casinos Category:Corellian Sector locations
