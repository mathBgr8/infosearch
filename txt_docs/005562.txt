High Admiral
3322

The title of High Admiral was used by the commander of Imperial Navy forces within an Imperial Sector Group. The title was usually granted to the Moff who led the sector. If the sector was involved in continuous naval actions, the Sector Group's high admiral was a distinct individual from the Moff, so that the Moff did not have to constantly devote their time to the conflict.
