Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience
2038

Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience is a compilation release of the massively-multiplayer online role-playing video game Star Wars Galaxies. It was released to coincide with the Combat Upgrade and the release of Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. It was released on May 24th, 2005. It includes the base game, An Empire Divided, along with the first two expansions, Jump to Lightspeed and Rage of the Wookiees. Players who bought the compilation were also rewarded with a BARC speeder, as seen in Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith, as a usable in-game vehicle.
