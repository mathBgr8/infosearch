Taking part in the Outer Rim Sieges, they led units of clone troopers into battle alongside fellow Jedi Ki-Adi-Mundi, Rivi-Anu, and Eon. A Force-sensitive trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, this Human Jedi fought alongside the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. During battle on an unknown planet between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, they were assigned to a battlefront under the leadership of Councilor Ki-Adi-Mundi. Joined by recently knighted Rivi-Anu and Eon, the group fought valiantly to repel the Separatist Droid Army. When a Victory-class Star Destroyer plummeted toward the battlefield, they all believed they would die, until Rivi-Anu held back the warship with an astounding display of the Force. Rivi-Anu waited until all Jedi and clones had fled the field, including this Jedi, and then allowed the ship to crash down upon herself.
