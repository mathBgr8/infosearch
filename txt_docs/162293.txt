The Spiral
902

The Spiral was a cavern section in Eskaron which at least one race course went through. As it name implied, it was a long spiral-like section of tunnel about forty meters in diameter much of the way. At the end of the spiral, the tunnel unexpectedly turned upward at an angle of close to 90 degrees. In 32 BBY, Blood Raptors member Gael Kinner's G-59 Cannibalizer and Rodian intimidated entrepreneur Teeloo's YT-1210 Neeva-Beelo raced through Eskaron, passing through this area.
