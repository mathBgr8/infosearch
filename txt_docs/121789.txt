Lightning (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
2071

Lightning was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in the service of the Galactic Empire. It served as a command ship on multiple simulation missions that Maarek Stele went through. The Lightning attacked and destroyed number of small Rebel footholds in various parts of space. The missions involved sending a single TIE Interceptor to clear a minefield and steal equipment from a Class-A cargo container Koss protected by a minefield, destroying a Rebel depot which consisted of shuttles Revt transports Bultz, Container transport Moth and containers with starfighters for defense. Finally, a Rebel starfighter base consisting of 3 containers Retuna, Reyve and Garble and was filled with multiple starfighters, a single transport Trole and some tugs Toone and finally two cargo ferries Juble.
