By the year 4.3 ABY, he was employed by the Star Tours travel agency at their Tomorrowland Starport. Along with F-23-DKMx, F-24 performed the final assembly of StarSpeeder 3000 and serviced their ion engines, and was stationed in front of a vertical moving lift.Complete Guide to Star Tours F-24-AMTx was created for Tokyo Disneyland's simulator ride theme park attraction Star Tours, which opened on July 12, 1989. In Star Tours: The Adventures Continue, the ride's sequel, a what appears to be a modified F-24 has now been moved further inside the queue, though his name is no longer visible.
