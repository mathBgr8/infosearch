Krevi
1015

Krevi was a Bothan concept of loyalty. Nek Bwua'tu swore an oath of krevi to the Galactic Alliance after becoming a Fleet Admiral. This made his loyalty to the Alliance even stronger than his loyalty to Bothawui or his people.
