Sarken Rimk
1320

Sarken Rimk was a Gungan who ran a dive instruction business out of his home on Whitesand Island on Dorumaa. He was an impatient teacher, and would irritably flip his ears when somebody grated on him. He would also mutter insults under his breath.Living Force Campaign Guide Secretly, Sarken was slightly Force-sensitive.
