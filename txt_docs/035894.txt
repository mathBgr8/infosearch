|habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Frid were an aquatic sentient species native to the planet Mackar. The Frid relocated to the water-covered planet Iskalon to escape a cataclysm that struck Mackar around five millennia before the Galactic Civil War, states that the species constituting the Iskalonian cultural group were brought to Iskalon approximately five millennia before the Galactic Civil War. Since "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series" establishes that the relocation of the Iskalonian species was prefaced by the cataclysms on the species' original homeworlds, the cataclysms must also have occurred around that time. and became a part of the Iskalonian School, a cultural group of several aquatic species that had traveled to Iskalon with the aid of spacefaring benefactors. A fishlike species, Frid were characterized by their bulbous violet eyes, long mouth-flaps and green, scaly skin. Frid were sexually dimorphic, as males had fins on their heads which females lacked; they were also incapable of physical speech, instead communicating solely through telepathy.
