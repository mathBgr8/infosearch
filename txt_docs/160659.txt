Fervse
1430

Fervse, also known as Bothan Glitterstone, were a kind of gem created in the formation of the planet Bothawui.The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons Although ubiquitous on the planet, it was precious; important buildings, specifically clan homes and great halls contained much glitterstone, and the city of Drev'starn sparkled from glitterstone buildings. Glitterstone was used for jewelry and pieces of art. Blue or white flawless variants, with silvery specks, were also crafted for medallions, typically used for symbols of office or academia. Glitterstone and artifacts made of it, was much sought aroung the galaxy, forming a flourishing industry for Bothan artisans, and a consistent export for the planet. *The Hutt Gambit *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *Pirates & Privateers *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gemstones
