Tendrando Arms
2412

Tendrando Arms was the corporation founded by Lando Calrissian and his wife, Tendra Risant Calrissian to produce modern battle droids during the war with the Yuuzhan Vong. Their first and stunningly successful product was the fearsome YVH 1 droid. A later, less frightening, but by no means less deadly version called Nanna was used by Luke Skywalker as a nanny/bodyguard for his son, Ben Skywalker.
