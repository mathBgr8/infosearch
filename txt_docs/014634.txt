Xeron
2548

Xeron was a planet located within the Xeron system, a part of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Inner Rim. Xeron was the site of a battle between the Rebel Alliance and Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War circa 3 ABY, where the Imperial Army attacked Rebel ground troops with a new warbot to devastating effect. Xeron was first mentioned in Star Wars (1977) 47, written by Archie Goodwin and released in 1981.
