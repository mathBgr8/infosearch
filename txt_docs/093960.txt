Trace-breather cartridge
1223

The trace-breather cartridge was a life-support device that slowly delivered limited amounts of breathable gases for a long period of time. It could not provide full atmosphere for the user like an air tank, but was useful in environments that were beginning to collapse. *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Life support systems
