Oradin
1772

Oradin was a city on Brentaal IV, located to the west of the city of Vuultin. Oradin contained a large spaceport, Imperial-class. Parts of the city were destroyed during the Battle of Brentaal IV between the New Republic and the Galactic Empire. A gunship was shot down and smashed into a school during this battle.
