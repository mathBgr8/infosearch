Hau polyp
850

Hau polyps were an organic material used by the Yuuzhan Vong as seats and mobile cushions. Constantly pulsing, they were made of fiberous red material. Supreme Overlord Shimrra had a huge throne in his Citadel made of hau polyps, and a polyp would occasionally burst due to his massive size. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: Ylesia *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
