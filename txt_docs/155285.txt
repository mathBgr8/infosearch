Keeramak Entourage
1060

The Keeramak Entourage was the Ssi-ruuvi fleet that accompanied the Keeramak on its mission to Bakura in 28 ABY. The formation consisted of two Sh'ner-class planetary assault carriers (Firrinree and Errinung'ka), several Fw'Sen-class picket ships, seven D'kee-class landing ships, and a number of smaller battle droids and support ships. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee Category:Fleets Category:Ssi-ruuvi Imperium
