|series=Star Wars (1977) |issue=104 }} Star Wars 104 is the one-hundred-fourth issue in the Marvel Star Wars series of comics. Luke, Leia, Han and their friends fly to Zeltros to enlist their aid in the war against the Nagai, only to have the war follow them! "Nagais and Dolls" is written by Jo Duffy, penciled by Cynthia Martin and inked by Art Nichols.
