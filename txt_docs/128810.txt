SM-2 was a sentient controlled droid onboard the New Republic ship Steadfast, and was used to search destroyed vessels for traps before being salvaged. In 16 ABY SM-2 was one of the droids involved in the finding of a operational datacore on the Star Destroyer Gnisnal that allowed for the discovery of the Black Fleet.
