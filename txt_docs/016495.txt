Discovered sometime after the Ruusan Reformation of the Galactic Republic, Anemcoro was colonized by the Ho'Din from Moltok. After the rise of the Galactic Empire, the world suffered a war in 17 BBY with fighting erupting between two races from neighboring planets. A supposedly accidental Imperial turbolaser attack destroyed many cities and caused a vast firestorm which desolated the planet's terra-scaped surface. Few Ho'Din escaped, but one escapee was Ort Hoogra-D'En, whose rare plants he had tended on the planet were destroyed. After the devastation of Anemcoro, the world was annexed as part of Oversector Outer and, following the death of Emperor Palpatine, eventually formed into the Pentastar Alignment. Admiral Gilad Pellaeon brought the world into his Imperial Remnant. The planet Anemcoro was created for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game sourcebook No Disintegrations, published in 1997.
