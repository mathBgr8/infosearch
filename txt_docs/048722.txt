He piloted the Walker Blizzard Scout 1 during the Battle of Hoth, and later Tempest Scout 1 in Battle of Endor. Arnet was a male Human veteran AT-ST pilot, who commanded the Imperial walker Blizzard Scout 1 in General Veers' Blizzard Force during the Battle of Hoth in the year 3 ABY. During the battle, Much of Blizzard Force was destroyed by Alliance snowspeeders, including the AT-AT Walkers Blizzard 2 and Blizzard 4. Ultimately, the battle would end in a tactical victory, though strategic failure for the Imperials. Earning the rank of lieutenant by the year 4 ABYThe New Essential Chronology dates the events of Return of the Jedi to the year 4 ABY., Arnet piloted the enclosed AT-ST walker Tempest Scout 1. After the ambush of the Endor shield generator bunker, which was protecting the under construction Imperial battlestation, the Death Star II, Tempest Scout 1 was present near the bunker. Arnet was assigned to coordinate the surprise counterattack in the Battle of Endor. However, the battle would become a victory for the Alliance. Arnet was a human male with light skin. As an AT-ST driver, Arnet wore a gray jumpsuit, and a dark green and black helmet with a chinstrap and goggles. Arnet's name was first established by his character card in the 1999 Endor Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game, with the name's simultaneous mention in the text for a card for his AT-ST, Tempest Scout 1, released in the same set. Although the Tempest Scout 1 card identifies the walker as one of the AT-STs seen during the onset of the Battle of Endor of the 1983 film Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, it is difficult to tell if the walker's onscreen occupant is the same actor used for the "Lieutenant Arnet" CCG card.
