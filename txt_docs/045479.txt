K'yne Harmion
1346

K'yne Harmion was an executive who worked for Gastess' Finance, Inc. Like his brothers Halleb and Sarell, his specialty was taking over the operations of corporations which the ruthless financier Sarlim Gastess had acquired. He enjoyed taking a well-run company, stripping it for short-term credits, and leaving it behind once Gastess' Finance was finished with it.
