The Garhoon were a species of vampiric sentients. Bail Organa presided over a court case between the Garhoons and their prey. At the end of the case, the prey returned to the Garhoons, saddening Leia Organa. *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
