Duro Squadron
1556

Duro Squadron was an all-Duros starfighter squadron that fought for the New Republic and Galactic Alliance during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Formed after the fall of Duro, they were devoted to reclaiming their homeworld from the extra-galactic invaders. The pilots of the squadron were a mixed group, ranging from seasoned combat veterans to those who had never before served in a military unit. In 29 ABY, they fought to retake Duro under Commander Yurf Col.
