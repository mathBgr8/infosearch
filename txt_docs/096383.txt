T'rinn
1540

The T'rinn were a sentient species with bright plumage and claws, and a body plan which allowed them to wield a shoulder-mounted missile launcher. In 2 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion one female T'rinn worked as a mining technician within the criminal mining operation of the Human J'uoch on the planet Dellalt. She was killed when the operation was attacked by the Guardian Corps, a brigade of war-robots under the control of a group known as the Survivors, who feared that J'uoch was a threat to them.
