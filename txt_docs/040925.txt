Mon Adapyne
2042

Mon Adapyne was an MC80B Star Cruiser that saw service with the New Republic Defense Fleet and later the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances during the Yuuzhan Vong War. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, Mon Adapyne served as the flagship of General Keyan Farlander The vessel participated in many battles, including the Second Battle of Obroa-skai, the Battle of Ebaq, and the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flagships Category:MC80B Star Cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance Category:Starships of the New Republic
