Sahrunba
1270

Sahrunba was a male Dug who served under Sebolto's employ during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted for being the oldest one of the three Dug brothers, the others being Jahrunba and Nahrunba, who smuggled numerous death sticks to the Gran Youth Council of Pixelito. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Dugs Category:Males
