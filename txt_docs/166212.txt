Storrd Township
868

The Storrd Township was a city on the world of Seregar. It was the closest thing to a starport that could be found on the entire planet, and visitors could stay in the Callamvor Hotel. The township had once been a prosperous mining town until the mines ran dry. Subsequent to this, most of the outskirts of the city were abandoned. The city was still frequented by smugglers and other types of criminals. Category:Cities Category:Seregar locations
