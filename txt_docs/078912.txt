Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds
1891

thumb|250px|Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 1 cover. Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds is a two-part comic collection that is part of the Classic Star Wars series of comics and re-publishes several of the more obscure Star Wars tales by Alan Moore, Steve Moore, and Steve Parkhouse from Marvel UK Star Wars . The artwork has been colored, reformatted, and retouched by Dark Horse Comics. It is notable for featuring stories of ambiguous canonicity with several of the tales only much later being referenced and brought into the official continuity.Characters and events from Dark Lord's Conscience, Blind Fury!, The Pandora Effect and Tilotny Throws a Shape have all been referenced in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia, elevating the stories to C-Canon.
