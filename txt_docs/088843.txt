Battle of unidentified planet (Yoda's charge)
2409

A battle occurred between the Confederacy of Independent Systems and the Galactic Republic on a planet during the Clone Wars. The battle can be seen at the very beginning of the first episode of Star Wars: Clone Wars. At this point, however, the battle, and even the planet the battle takes place on, are yet to be expanded on. *Star Wars: Clone Wars Cine-Manga Category:Battles of the Clone Wars
