Qimtiq
1386

Qimtiq was a male Quarren and proprietor of the cantina in Iziz on Onderon during the Onderon Civil War. He was also the arranger of swoop races. He provided information to Meetra Surik about the murder of Captain Sullio. She talked to the soldiers in charge of the investigation, and four of them were Bahima, Nikko, Panar, and Kiph. He was in a good mood because the Civil War was a boon to his business.
