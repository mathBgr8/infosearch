Nella 342 light freighter
1751

The Nella 342 light freighter was produced by SoroSuub Corporation. An older model of light freighter known for its durability, reliability, and rugged construction - the ship was nonetheless discontinued after the market standards for freighters were redefined by the introduction of the YT-1300. *Pirates & Privateers Category:Freighter classes Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
