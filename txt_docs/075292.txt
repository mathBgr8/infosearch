Pod Trooper

Pod Trooper was a name used by the Yuuzhan Vong to describe troopers inserted to planetary locations through Yorik coral pods that contained minimum life support, just enough for the warriors to reach the planet. *The New Jedi Order: Traitor *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Soldiers Category:Yuuzhan Vong military units
