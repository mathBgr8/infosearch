Lee |illustrator= |editor= |publisher=Boulevard |first date=October 1995The Essential Reader's Companion |last date=September 1997 |media type=Hardcover, Paperback |timeline=22 ABY |num books=6 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Junior Jedi Knights was a series of six young reader novels written between 1995 and 1997 by Nancy Richardson and Rebecca Moesta. The novels are set in 22 ABY and detail the adventures of Anakin Solo, Tahiri Veila, the Kushiban Jedi Master Ikrit, and Uldir Lochett. The series was published by both Berkley Boulevard and Berkley Jam. Published in the mid-1990s, the Junior Jedi Knights and Young Jedi Knights series never use the term Padawan for the apprentices studying at the academy, as the word did not become common in lore until after Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace (1999).
