Wildwater City
1549

Wildwater City was one of the newer floating cities on Mon Calamari&mdash;it was less than one century old by the time of the Galactic Civil War. Wildwater City was built above Rako's Rift, less than a century before the Galactic Civil War. True to its name and its youthful population, it had a reputation as a less refined community than most Mon Calamari settlements. It attracted many artists, eccentrics, and offworlders.
