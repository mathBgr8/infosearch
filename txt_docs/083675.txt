Regga
3522

Regga was an Ithorian podracer who competed in a race on Tatooine around 32 BBY. Racing through Beggar's Canyon, he stayed too high and brushed the cliffs, dying instantly.Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace novelization After his death, pit droids searched for the parts from his podracer for a month. Prior to the Boonta Eve Classic, Toydarian shopkeeper Watto commanded Anakin Skywalker to visit the Jawas at Mochat Steep to acquire parts from Regga's podracer, specifically the thrusters. Skywalker repaired the Jawas' vaporator for their sandcrawler and left with the parts, which he added to his own podracer. Regga was first mentioned in the novelization of the 1999 film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The novel was written by Terry Brooks. In the 1999 Jedi Readers book Anakin's Fate, Regga is stated to have survived a crash with Sebulba. He left Tatooine after the race and vowed to never race against the Dug. For the purposes of this article, this is assumed to be an inconsistency, as this is the only source to record the events differently.
