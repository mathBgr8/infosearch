Fern bicker
1467

|language= }} Fern bickers were bipedal simian creatures that lived on Kashyyyk. For the most part, the fern bickers were solitary creatures. They had gray skin, green hair and four teats. They also featured three long, sickle shaped claws on each hand and foot. Fern bickers were so similar to the Kashyyyk ro-roo that it may have been a different breed or close cousin.
