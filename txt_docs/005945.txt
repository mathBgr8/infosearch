It was also within the region known as the Slice. It was situated at the junction of four major hyperlanes: the Bothan Run, the Kaaga Run, the Reena Trade Route, and the Manda Merchant Route. Containing a number of planets, including Bothawui and Golm, the unstable third planet broke, leaving in its place an asteroid field. The cataclysm affected the axial plain of Bothawui (which became the third planet).Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Pre-Republic explorers first discovered the farthest planets of the system, Hoppawui and Jentawui.
