Disruption bubble generator
1985

Disruption bubble generators were small devices used to disrupt communications monitors by creating a sonic shell. They were used to assure privacy, creating a two-meter zone of silence through which normal sound could not pass. Disruption bubble generators were marketed to music-lovers as a way to enjoy music without disturbing anyone else around them. However, due to its usefulness, disruption bubble generators were favored by thieves and Alliance SpecForce Infiltrators.
