Starweb
606

The Starweb was a disk-shaped freighter owned and operated by Asyr Sei'lar after she resigned from duty with the New Republic. It had been loaned to her by Booster Terrik. Under a new identity, Sei'lar used the Starweb to reach her homeworld of Bothawui, where she hoped to influence change in Bothan society. She left the ship at Commenor for Terrik to retrieve. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge Category:Freighters
