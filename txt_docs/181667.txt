Twilight lizard
1089

Twilight lizards were small reptiles native to Yavin 13. With sizes ranging from 0.6 to 1.0 meters long, the lizards had four clawed legs, and a long tail. They emerged only during twilight hours, seeking out small bugs to eat, and retained virtually their entire weight in liquid; on the desert world, this made them valuable prey for larger creatures.
