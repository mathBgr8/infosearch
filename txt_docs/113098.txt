S-EP1 security droid
2280

S-EP1 security droids were a unique droid model designed by Ulban Arms. The S-EP1 was designed as a combination of bodyguard and law enforcement. The electronic brain in the S-EP1 was based on a Arjan IV logic computer and was programmed with all available data to excel at its duties. They cost over 200,000 credits but were generally considered worth it, as they were top-notch. Their body was resistant to minor explosions. The arms of the droid were heavy and piston-driven, giving them immense strength, while the hands had twin opposable thumbs for increased dexterity. The droids were programmed for non-lethal combat but could resort to lethal force if it was the only way to save their protectee. They were smart and skilled in battle tactics, criminal profiles, medical care, and vehicle operation.
