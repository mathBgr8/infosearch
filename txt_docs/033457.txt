Gyr Keela
2483

Gyr Keela was a male Mrlssi who taught at the Mrlsst Trade and Science Academy during the Galactic Civil War. He was known for his beautiful yellow and red facial plumage. Keela became the president of the Mrlsst Trade and Science Academy, a position to which he was promoted after Rorax Falken suffered a nervous breakdown. He tried to extort money from the Empire by pretending to have a cloaking device. When the fledgling New Republic also showed interest in the fictitious device, Keela opened negotiations between the New Republic and the remnants of the Empire. During the meeting, Wedge Antilles represented the Republic, while his old nemesis Loka Hask represented the Empire. An Imperial plot, involving faked video of Tycho Celchu and the imprisonment of Wedge during the negotiations, virtually assured Imperial control of the project. In the end, Keela admitted that the project was a fake.
