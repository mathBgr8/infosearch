Dona sheltered former Jedi Ferus Olin when he was on the run from the Empire. She escaped during an attack from the bounty hunters Boba Fett and D'harhan. In 18 BBY, Telamark was arrested along with Roan Lands by the Empire on charges of conspiracy against the government of Bellassa and plotting to assassinate the Bellassan Imperial advisor. The two prisoners were held aboard the True Justice in an effort to coerce Olin to work for the Empire.
