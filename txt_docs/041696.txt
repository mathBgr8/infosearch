Sila was friends with the Humans Dixon Just and Zey Nep, and an associate of the archaeologist Artuo Pratuhr. Dixon and Nep were often found in Sila's company. During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Sila, alongside Just, Nep, and Prathur, visited the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall located in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary Coruscant, as Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered, searching for the bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Sila approached Just near the entrance of the club, and the two talked. Soon after, Sila talked to Just, Nep, and Pratuhr near the club's bar. As Skywalker searched the club for Wesell, Sila debated with Just. Sila later watched as Wesell was disarmed by Kenobi and taken out of the club. As Kenobi did so, Skywalker told the clubgoers to go back to their drinks. Sila wore dark clothing at the club. Civ Sila first appeared in the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002.Star Wars Year By Year: A Visual History, New Edition The character was portrayed by Trevor Tighe, a member of the film's Droid Unit crew.
