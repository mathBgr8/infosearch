The Times and Trepidations of Majnar Roak
715

"The Times and Trepidations of Majnar Roak" was an animated holostrip syndicated by Sektor 242 NewsLine. It was one of the ten most popular animated holostrips in the Outer Rim. "Roak" concerned itself with the problems of a retiring finance clerk living on a backwater world, though it was also at times a satire of the Sarin sector. At any given time could be difficult to tell which it was, and the strip was described as "bewildering." *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Entertainment
