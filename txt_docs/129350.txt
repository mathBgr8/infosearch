8-2A medical bundle
1480

The 8-2A medical bundle was a hand-held medical kit carried as part of a Jedi's field kit during the time of the Galactic Republic. This medical bundle featured multiple reservoirs of bacta, allowing a Jedi to temporarily treat potentially mortal wounds during missions or on the battlefield. *Jedi Academy Training Manual Category:Medkits
