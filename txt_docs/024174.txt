Muur (food)
752

Muur was a yeast-based food that was a staple of the Yuuzhan Vong. Even though it was widely used, it had an unenjoyable taste. Prior to the Yuuzhan Vong War, Yuuzhan Vong scouts planted large quanities of muur and lambents on the planet Shramar, to support the upcoming invasion. *Invasion: Revelations 4 *Invasion: Revelations 5 *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Yuuzhan Vong food Category:Yuuzhan Vong plants
