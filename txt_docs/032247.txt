Gorzima
1550

Gorzima was a male Quarren gambler and crime lord living on Nar Shaddaa circa 31 BBY. When the Devaronian smuggler Vilmarh Grahrk bet his starship, the Inferno, and lost, Gorzima was the one who collected it. Villie and Jedi Knight Quinlan Vos made a deal with Gorzima to let them take the ship off Nar Shaddaa, allowing Grahrk to collect his winnings from betting that Vos would get off the moon alive, and to buy the ship back from Gorzima.
