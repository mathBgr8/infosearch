Alpiett Gredge
1739

Alpiett Gredge was a male Human from Brentaal IV, and an officer in the Imperial Navy during the height of the Galactic Civil War. A Vice Admiral during the time of the Rebellion, Gredge was the commanding officer of the Victory-class Star Destroyer Scourge as of 0 ABY, which patrolled space around the planet of Ralltiir in the Galactic Core.
