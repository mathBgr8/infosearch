Bet's Off
633

The Bet's Off was a somewhat elegant tapcafe that was located on the Jubilee Wheel, which contained a number of sabacc tables. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, Han Solo visited the place while on the Wheel, where he had a rather unpleasant encounter with Bossk. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tapcafes
