Ghaz Kul IV
1032

Ghaz Kul IV was a mining town on the planet Pitann. It was connected to one of Pitann's limited-service starports via repulsorlift barge. The town was grimy and populated with industrial buildings. It was surrounded by slag heaps and open pit mines. In 7 ABY, the droid gladiatorial contests began in the town, prompting the construction of a specially built arena to hold the games. *The Kathol Outback Category:Cities Category:Pitann locations
