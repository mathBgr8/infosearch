Coruscant Fashions
1033

Coruscant Fashions was a high-end retailer located within the Core District of the city of Cinnagar. Like many of the attractions within the Core District, the store's architecture represented a specific planet within the Core Worlds region of the galaxy; specifically Coruscant.
