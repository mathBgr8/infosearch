GS-138
2074

The GS-138 was a gunship in the service of the Alliance Fleet. Its twelve-being crew were members of the Alliance Intelligence and/or Alliance Special Operations; they were tasked with a top-secret mission in 5 ABY. During the raid, it was ambushed and destroyed. Some survivors, including a male Mon Calamari, a male and a female human, managed to reach the life pods and floated among the ship's debris until they were rescued. They were taken to the Mercy above Shackel. Three replacement bacta tanks were pressed into service in order to accommodate them, and placed on a corridor outside ward 114.
