General Purpose Attack Fighter
714

The General Purpose Attack Fighter, commonly known as the GPA fighter, was a starfighter developed by the Bakurans. GPA snubfighters were the core of the starfighter wings of the Bakuran Defense Fleet in 18 ABY when the fleet was sent to aid the New Republic against the Sacorrian Triad in the Corellian Crisis. *Showdown at Centerpoint *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bakuran starship classes Category:Starfighter classes
