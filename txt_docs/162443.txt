The Shelter
739

The Shelter was home to the 25 descendants of a survey team to Dagobah. A small island of dry ground, it held between 20 and 30 huts. The huts were made of dried mud, with roofs formed from gnarlwood branches coated with slime. In the center of the village was an open shelter without any walls. Inside it, the Children kept the broken log of the original survey team. *Galaxy of Fear: The Hunger Category:Dagobah locations
