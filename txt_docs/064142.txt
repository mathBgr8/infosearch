Spaceport Southeast II
1925

The Spaceport Southeast II was the largest of ten spaceports on Bonadan and one of the most important in the Corporate Sector. The spaceport covered a larger area than many cities, although little of it extended above or below the planet's surface. There were numerous shipbuilding and refitting yards, dock facilities for barges and bulk freighters, an Espo command center, an Authority Merchant Marine academy, and the postmaster's headquarters.
