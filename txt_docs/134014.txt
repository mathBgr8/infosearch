Norello
876

Norello was the captain of the freighter Happer's Way, out of Chandrila. He was contracted by the Imperial Army to bring fifty AT-ST walkers to the garrison on Llorkan, but was interrupted in his delivery by an attack from the pirate ship Cavalcade.
