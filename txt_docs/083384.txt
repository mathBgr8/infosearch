Ongun-nur
1274

The ongun-nur were large bird creatures native to the planet of Ansion. Despite their great, intimidating size, the ongun-nur were generally timid creatures. They usually traveled in large flocks. They had yellow eyes, pointed beaks, and their bodies were large, but mostly filled with air. The ongun-nur had very thin wings, and were unable to fly against the wind.
