Frazier
1988

Frazier was a CR90 corvette in the Rebel Alliance Fleet during the Galactic Civil War. Shortly before the Battle of Yavin, the Imperial BFF-1 bulk freighter Sidral II was hijacked by privateers who scheduled a rendezvous to transfer its cargo of military equipment belonging to the Death Star to the Rebels. The Frazier was sent to conduct the pick-up, under escort from two R-22 Spearheads from Red Squadron, led by Keyan Farlander. The Rebels arrived to discover that the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Immortal had already arrived and begun launching TIE/LN starfighters and TIE/sa bombers, though the Star Destroyer itself was some distance away. The R-22s were able to hold off the TIEs while the Frazier conducted its boarding operation, retrieving the components, and both ships hyperspaced to safety.
