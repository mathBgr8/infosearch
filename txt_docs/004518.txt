The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook
34151

The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook was a roleplaying guide published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. It was written by Kathy Tyers and Eric S. Trautmann, and was first published in February of 1996. The book summarizes and details the events of the novel The Truce at Bakura. It also includes a great deal of new information about the Battle of Endor, as well as several new short fiction contributions by Tyers. The Official Guide to Kathy Tyers' Novel The Truce at Bakura Cold-blooded invaders from beyond known space; a desperate alliance with tattered Imperial forces; a fight for the survival of the Rebel Alliance itself! Kathy Tyers' exciting Star Wars novel comes to life in this hardcover sourcebook.
