High Cabinet
788

The High Cabinet was the executive branch of the Five Worlds after the assassination of Thrackan Sal-Solo. Led by the Five World Prime Minister and the President of Five Worlds, the Minister of Intelligence, and Minister of Defense were also involved. President Dur Gejjen, along with Ministers Gavele Lemora and Rorf Willems plotted the assassination of Queen Mother Tenel Ka Djo of the Hapes Consortium during the Second Galactic Civil War. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corellian organizations
