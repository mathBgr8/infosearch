Task Force Apex
1517

}} Task Force Apex was one of five task forces of the New Republic Defense Fleet's Fourth Fleet. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Apex was placed under the temporary command of the Fifth Battle Group and led by Commodore Farley Carson from Yakez. It served in General Etahn A'baht's plan that culminated in Operation Strong Hand.
