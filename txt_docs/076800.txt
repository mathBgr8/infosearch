Orma Hundeen
1138

Orma Hundeen was a female Human. Along with Zeela Nall, Nuri Pravada, Sheyne Tyndos, and Leia Organa, she went on a mission on Elerion to recover the holocube map containing the location of Rebel bases from Nescan Tal'yo, Hermos, and Gogol.
