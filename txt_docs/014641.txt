|water= |interest=Silan cave |flora= |fauna=*Silan *Squollyhawlk |otherlife= |species=*Moggonites *Blagwahrs |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation=Ancient Sith EmpireBook of Sith: Secrets from the Dark Side }} Arorua was a jungle planet home to fierce monsters spawned from Sith sorcery, such as the squollyhawlk and the silan. After an invasion by the Infinite Empire of the Rakata species and a civil war on their homeworld Korriban around 27,700 BBY,The Essential Atlas surviving members of the Sith species colonized Arorua with Rakatan vessels, integrating the planet into the ancient Sith Empire. The Sith created monsters via the means of Sith sorcery, such as the silan and the squollyhawlk. The Jen'jidai Sorzus Syn later wrote of the planet in her chronicle, which was later collected by Darth Sidious into his Book of Sith. Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi visited Arorua in 44 BBY, where Obi-Wan was nearly killed by a native squollyhawlk. They also met a Moggonite named Mosko Bolpa, and destroyed an ancient silan. The text "Arorua" appearing in the comic can easily be misinterpreted as "Arorlia" due to the chosen font making the "U" look like "LI."
