Scum and Villainy (Jedi Knights TCG)
6574

|figures= |cards=140 |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Premiere |followed by=Masters of the Force }} Scum and Villainy is the first expansion set of the Jedi Knights Trading Card Game and was published on July 11, 2001. This expansion pack contained new themes of Leia Organa (purple), Obi-Wan Kenobi (blue), Boba Fett (yellow) and Jabba the Hutt (orange). A new force card color (orange) was also added. This expansion pack was focused on Independent characters, mainly bounty hunters, and added the possibility of creating an Independent-themed deck. Category:Jedi Knights Trading Card Game
