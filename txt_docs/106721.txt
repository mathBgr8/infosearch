Mauler
2237

|retired= |battles=Battle of Kuat |affiliation=Galactic Empire |navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Mauler was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer that was part of Kuat's planetary defenses. It was present at the Battle of Kuat. After its TIE squadrons had been overwhelmed to only a few remaining fighters by the Hawk-bats and Vibroaxe Prime, Mauler, to escape being defenseless to the snub fighters, bypassed the engagement zone by a significant distance, only to find itself within the range of Zsinj's Super Star Destroyer, Iron Fist. After a futile attempt of contending with the opposing ship, Mauler found herself spinning inflamed from half a dozen spots along her hull.
