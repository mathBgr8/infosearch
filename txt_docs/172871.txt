Nobu
898

Nobu was a group of 4 Cargo ferries belonging to the Rebel Alliance in the Airam sector during the operations of Task Force Vengeance. When the decision was made to construct a shipyard in Airam space, Nobu, were part of a Rebel convoy charged with bringing the needed supplies and personnel. Nobu group contained construction equipment. Unfortunately for the Rebels, the Galactic Empire had learned of them and readied a trap in the Rocrin system. Nobu were destroyed along with all the others. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups
