Lianna Guard
707

The Lianna Guard was a Guardian-class light cruiser commanded by Captain Lodrel. It was part of the Imperial squadron present at the Battle of Nar Shaddaa in 4 BBY. The ship attempted to engage Salla Zend's Rimrunner, but was quickly destroyed by the freighter's rear-firing concussion missile launchers. *The Hutt Gambit Category:Guardian-class light cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
