Longshot (Dreadnaught-class)
1982

Longshot was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser owned and operated by casino-owner and Rebel ally Dunari. Longshot arrived midway through the Rebel assault on the Hurrim Base, and aided the Alliance in defeating the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter. It deployed squadrons of Cloakshape fighters to aid the Rebels during the battle.
