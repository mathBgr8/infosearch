|listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Writer, Editor |companies= |website= }} Brian Augustyn (d. February 1, 2022) is a comic book editor and writer who wrote the comic "Lucky Stars." During the late 1980s and early 1990s he was an editor for DC Comics where he edited The Flash, Justice League and the !mpact Comics line of titles. He discovered his love of comics with DC's Showcase #4, which starred The Flash. As a writer he has worked on titles such as Batman: Gotham by Gaslight and it's sequel Batman: Master of the Future, The Flash, Imperial Guard, Out There, Crimson, and Mega Man. He has been recognized for his work in the industry with the Wizard Fan Award for Favorite Editor in 1994. Currently, he is the Managing Editor for Visionary Comics Studio. Category:Comic authors
