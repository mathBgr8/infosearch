Rebel Star
3425

The Rebel Star was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in service to the Alliance to Restore the Republic, and then utilized by the New Republic till 10 ABY. Captain Huoba Neva was given command for it due to her actions in the Illoud system.Dark Empire Sourcebook After Luke Skywalker, Lando Calrissian, Wedge Antilles, and other Rebels aboard the Star Destroyer Liberator crash-landed on Coruscant, Rebel Star was sent along with the Antares Six and the Millennium Falcon to rescue them.
