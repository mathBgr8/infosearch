Umbaran Shadow Assassin
2227

thumb|right|300px|An Umbaran Shadow Assassin during the [[Crisis on Umbara.]] Umbaran Shadow Assassins were Umbaran warriors in existence at least since the time of the rebellion against the Eternal Empire, taking part in some galactic events. They eventually became Sith spies employed by the Brotherhood of Darkness during the New Sith Wars. They trained in the Umbaran Sith Academy. Their preferred weapon of choice was the Force pike Shadow Assassins were able to mask their presence, not just in the Force but also from physical senses such as sight and sound.Darth Bane: Path of Destruction It was also hinted that they had the ability to kill using the dark side with minimal effort. They were not as skilled at combat if their primary advantages, surprise and secrecy, were neutralized.Darth Bane: Rule of Two At least eight Umbaran Shadow Assassins survived the Thought bomb that killed virtually all the Sith at the Seventh Battle of Ruusan. They were recruited by a Sith-following Force-sensitive known as Hetton for his own personal use.
