Glop bomb
546

Glop bombs were a type of armament used by Jedi StealthX fighters. They were essentially a bomb that spread a quick-hardening foam over an area. A group of rogue Jedi, led by Jacen Solo, used them during the Raid on Supply Depot Thrago.
