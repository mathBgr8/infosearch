Krai was one of many scouts for the Nagai sent from the Unknown Regions into wider galaxy, to which the Nagai called "Skyriver", in order to find a new home losing their own to the Tof. Instead of searching and observing the galaxy, Krai settled down on Corellia, where she fell in love with a human male and gave birth to a child, Bey. When it was discovered by Nagai commanders that Krai was not doing her job, she was ordered to leave her family and continue her mission elsewhere. She left her family with little explanation.
