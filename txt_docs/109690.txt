Several ship types were referred to as Republic battleships: *An alternate term for the Inexpugnable-class tactical command ship, which fought during the Mandalorian Wars. *The Republic battleships, which fought during the Great Sith War. *An alternate term for the Venator-class Star Destroyer, which was a fast, medium-sized class that escorted the aforementioned Clone Wars-era battleships.
