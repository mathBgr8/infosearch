Cseria
754

Cseria was the title given to the leaders of Elom community groups analogous to the role of a village mayor. The communities, which lived in burrows known as sevriinas,Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies were also known as cseria. The cseria met every year to resolve problems until the arrival of the Galactic Empire. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Elom culture Category:Titles
