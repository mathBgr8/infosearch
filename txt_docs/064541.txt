Wizards.com
1109

Wizards.com is the official website of Wizards of the Coast, a subsidiary of Hasbro Inc.. Beginning with the introduction of Wizards' Star Wars Roleplaying Game in 2000, the website included various supplements and information for the roleplaying game, Star Wars Miniatures, and the Star Wars Trading Card Game. On May 28, 2010, with the conclusion of Wizards' license with Lucasfilm, all Star Wars material was removed from the website. Category:Roleplaying websites
