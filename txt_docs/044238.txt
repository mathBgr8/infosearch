Olivier Vatine
457

Olivier Vatine is a French artist, best known to Star Wars audiences as the penciler of the Dark Horse Comics adaptation of Timothy Zahn's Heir to the Empire and as the cover artist of the Dark Horse Books trade paperback Star Wars Rebels. Category:Comic book inkers Category:Comic book pencillers
