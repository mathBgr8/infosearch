Triumvirate (Corellian)
896

The triumvirate was a group of three Democratic Alliance officials who assumed power over the Five Worlds following the assassination of Dur Gejjen. The reason they assumed power was that, after the deaths of so many Five World Prime Ministers in such a short span of time, no one wished to assume the post. However, soon afterward Sadras Koyan was elected as Five Worlds Prime Minister, apparently replacing the Triumvirate. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Corellian organizations
