Sadie Edon
1619

Sadie Edon, sometimes credited as Sadie Eddon, or Sadie Eden was a regular stunt woman on the James Bond films before her role in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. She played Garindan and Reegesk. On the day of shooting, none of the regular background artists wanted to wear the Ranat suit, so Edon braved the extremely hot costume.
