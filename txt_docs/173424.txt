FGA-583 nerve agent
970

FGA-583 nerve agent was a highly effective nerve toxin that was commonly loaded into Type-12B Hollow-Tip Rockets. When used, the toxin could induce unconsciousness in a target in less than six seconds. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *Arms & Equipment Guide Category:Neurotoxins and nerve agents Category:Weaponized gases
