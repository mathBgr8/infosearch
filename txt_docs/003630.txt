Like the Coway, Mimbanites were humanoids with short fur, but they were smaller and less robust. Their fur was green rather than red, hence the derogatory nickname Greenies. (This derogatory term was also applied toward Rodians.)Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic The term "Mimbanite" also refers to a race of furry quadrupeds which inhabited the planet. It is unclear if the quadrupeds were sentient, non-sentient, or semi-sentient. Unwilling or unable to retreat to caves deep in the swamplands like their cousins, the Coway, when the Galactic Empire set up their mining operations, Mimbanites were reduced to scavenging garbage heaps and begging for alcohol.
