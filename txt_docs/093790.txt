Silver Forest of Dreams
2405

The Silver Forest of Dreams was a forest located on the planet Kubindi that was made up of kilometer-tall silver trees that had 20-meter-wide leaves. During the fall, the leaves would fall to the ground and looked like giant rafts. A mist would shroud the forest which would reduce visibility. While difficult to navigate, the area was said to be beautiful from above, where it had the appearance of a huge field of glass shards caressed by a soft, diffusing mist. The forest was home to many ghost spiders, which made traveling through it dangerous because of the many webs woven between the trees.
