Barleos
1264

Barleos was a minor Lord of House Melantha, a saber rake and protégé of Lord Vaskel Savill. A vain man he was said to be good looking but with an effeminate appearance and had numerous lady admirers. Barleos was emotionally unstable, hot-headed and easily insulted into a duel. The fact that he was a talented warrior meant that such a challenge could be deadly.
