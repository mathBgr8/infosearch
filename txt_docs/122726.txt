Magnopium ferrocongregate
648

Magnopium ferrocongregate was a material used in the assembly of core bombs. Qui-Gon Jinn ordered a helmsman aboard the Aurorient Express to set the ship's vapor samplers to search for both magnopium ferrocongregate and accellerite, in order to locate a core bomb planted onboard. *Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: The Aurorient Express 2 Category:Substances
