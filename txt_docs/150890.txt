Venithon Bandit
1639

The Venithon Bandit was the Ghtroc Industries 580 freighter used by the pirate organization Venithon Twins. Originally owned by an Elomin smuggler who had stolen it, the Venithon bought the ship. With the original documents conveniently "lost", they then registered the Venithon Bandit under both of their names, and during the following years, they refitted it with parts stolen to other, newer Ghtrocs; only the original hull was left, but it was the only ship the Venithon Twins ever had. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:580 freighters Category:Pirate starships
