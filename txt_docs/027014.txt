CIS Shadowfeed (Insider)
1517

CIS Shadowfeed, featured in Star Wars Insider magazine, was a series of in-universe news articles from a Separatist perspective, detailing galactic events during the Clone Wars. The articles are written by Pablo Hidalgo. CIS Shadowfeed articles were published in Star Wars Insider issues #65 to #76, after the release of Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones.
