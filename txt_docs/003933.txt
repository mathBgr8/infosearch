It was one of four gas giants in that star system, along with Zauhu, Urazuun, and Faargau. It was the outermost of them and had the fewest natural satellites – thirteen in total. Laazrau was first mentioned briefly in "Three Simple Laws," the first part of the Planet Hoppers article series Aargau: For All Your Banking Needs, which was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on the Wizards.com website in 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Zug system, thereby including Laazrau, in grid square L-10.
