Pestage family
812

The Pestage family was the family of Sate Pestage. Residing in the Ciutric Hegemony, they numbered over one hundred. When Admiral Delak Krennel seized control of the Hegemony from Pestage after murdering him, his next move was to coldly round up and execute all of Pestage's family members.
