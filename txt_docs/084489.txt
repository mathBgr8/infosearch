Unidentified Zeltron Sith general
1611

A Zeltron general was a member of the Black Knights under the command of the Sith Lord known as the Dark Underlord; he faced Mandalorian mercenaries who intruded upon the Underlord's fortress on Malrev IV. The Jedi Master Murrtaggh had cut a deal with them to stage a diversionary attack on the Underlord's forces, and while the battle raged on, Murrtaggh assassinated the Sith Lord but died in the process. Until the release of Evil Never Dies: The Sith Dynasties, this character was frequently identified by fans as the Dark Underlord himself.
