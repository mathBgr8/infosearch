Darth Vader's secretary
1979

Darth Vader's secretary was in reality a shapeshifting assassin who accompanied Darth Vader to Aargau, along with two other assassins, to kill Viscount Tardi. While the Viscount was visiting the Garden of Butterflies, the shapeshifter changed into a rare butterfly with a poisonous sting and set off to attack Tardi. However, Princess Leia Organa quickly observed that the butterfly was approaching Tardi, and she ordered R2-D2 to spray it with fire retardant.
