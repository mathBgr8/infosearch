Scythe (Strike-class)
2172

A Loronar Strike-class medium cruiser serving in the Galactic Empire's Imperial Starfleet, the Scythe had been assigned as a convoy escort ship during the Battle of Hoth. The convoy it was assigned to protect en route to the Hoth system was destroyed by then-rookie pilot Ace Azzameen.
