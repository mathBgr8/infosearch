Making Episode III Webdocs
3319

Making Episode III Webdocs was a feature on the Star Wars Hyperspace fan club website. It showcased eighteen web-based documentaries covering the production of Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. The movies were available in two QuickTime sizes: 480 x 270 for Hyperspace subscribers and 320 x 180 for non-members. The webdocs were later included on the Episode III second DVD. † Category:Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club features Category:Web documentaries
