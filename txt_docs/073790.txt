Brian Fraim is a Star Wars fan who designed the Skels in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the 16 years and older category. He was 22 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
