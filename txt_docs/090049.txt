|designer= |homeworld= |line= |model= |class=Assault droid |degree=Class four droidAccording to The New Essential Guide to Droids, battle droids are classified as class four droids. |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |sensor=Black |plating=Bronze |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=Arakyd Industries |firstmade= |retired= }} The AAD-4 was a line of assault droids manufactured by Arakyd Industries. Hulking, humanoid class four droids strong enough to lift a Vulptereen off the ground by their neck, they had bronze plating and were equipped with a pair of black photoreceptors. Shortly before the outbreak of the Clone Wars in the year 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology Arakyd sent sixty AAD-4 assault droids into Viper Tower, headquarters of Viper Sensor Intelligence Systems, during its hostile corporate takeover of VSIS, which quickly eliminated its ground floor security units. A garrison remained in order to assist with the transition. The AAD-4 assault droid first appeared within the HoloNet News website in the in-universe issue HoloNet News Vol. 531 50, published April 4, 2002. and written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
