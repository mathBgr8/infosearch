Hakma Kinto
1434

Hakma Kinto was a male Weequay enforcer who worked for Gardulla the Hutt during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was also wanted by the InterGalactic Banking Clan for hijacking a cash transport on Sriluur.
