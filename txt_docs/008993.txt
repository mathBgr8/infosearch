Star Wars Galaxy Collector
2763

}} Star Wars Galaxy Collector, abbreviated as SWGC, was a magazine published quarterly by Topps that focused on Star Wars collectibles. The successor to Topps' previous Star Wars publication, Star Wars Galaxy Magazine, Star Wars Galaxy Collector ran for eight issues, from February 1998 to December 1999. Subscriber copies of Star Wars Galaxy Collector, dubbed Deluxe Collectors' Editions, came polybagged with exclusive Star Wars collectibles, such as Topps trading cards. The Deluxe Collectors' Editions were offered only to subscribers and comic stores, and were not available on newsstands.
