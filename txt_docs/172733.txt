Rateb

Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Rateb was an Airam starship. Present at Goff Platform during the Imperial attack there, it was part of the initial Rebel/Airam negotiations. When the Rebels' frigate Colada arrived to help rescue its own emmissary craft, Omicron 2, Rateb launched from Goff Platform with an Airam leader aboard.
