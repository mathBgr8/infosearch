Stim tea was a type of tea that came in a variety of flavors, including scry-mint and Roonan lemon. It could be served with flavorings such as Zingbee honey and Christophsian sugar. Stim tea could be served cool and in a glass,Darksaber or served hot in cups.Rebel Dawn Dex's Diner sold a number of stim tea flavors from across the galaxy. The tea cost one and a half credits, and Dex offered complimentary refills on all beverages.
