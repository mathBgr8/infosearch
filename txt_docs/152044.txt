Wila Prammi
2073

Wila Prammi was a female Human who became the first democratically elected Monarch of Gala, after Queen Veda decided to abdicate the throne. Prammi had more political experience than her candidates, Deca Brun and Prince Beju, having served in the government of the now deceased King Cana. It was thought by many that her position within the monarchy seemed to distance her from the majority of the people.
