Triple Nova Casino
545

Located in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae, the Triple Nova Casino was a noisy casino owned by Friizt, friend of Governor A'jidre Skrigatov, and was known for its extremely gaudy color schemes. Category:Abregado-rae locations Category:Casinos
