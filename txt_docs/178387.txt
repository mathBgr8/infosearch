Nast Hoberd
1909

Nast Hoberd was a male Human captain in the Imperial Navy. Prior to the Battle of Yavin, he served as commanding officer of the Port Heavy Blaster Station on the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Steel Talon. He was awarded the Silver Valor medal sometime during his Naval service and it was a personal quirk of his to constantly move its display location in his office so that no one ever saw it in that same place twice.
