Davin Kotras
1397

Davin Kotras was a smuggler and pilot before and during the time of the Jedi Civil War. He was a good friend of Jolee Bindo, Sunry, and Elora, and had shared many adventures with Bindo during his adventuring days. During the Jedi Civil War, Davin came across Jolee once again and told him of the predicament of another friend of theirs, a war veteran named Sunry, who had been arrested for murder on Manaan. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Pilots Category:Smugglers
