Obroa-skai information raid
2222

In 9 ABY, Imperial scout ships, directed by Grand Admiral Thrawn, conducted an information raid on Obroa-skai's extensive library, in order to uncover the location of the planet Wayland. An Elomin task force, patrolling the system as part of the New Republic's diplomatic efforts to bring Obroa-skai into the Republic, gave chase and followed the scouts into hyperspace. *Heir to the Empire *Heir to the Empire Sourcebook *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *The Essential Atlas *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Battles of Obroa-skai Category:Battles of the Thrawn campaign
