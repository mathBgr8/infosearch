Lake Umberbool
1357

Lake Umberbool was a lake on Naboo, and the site of an arena for the Gungan annual Festival of Warriors. Shortly before the Battle of Naboo in 32 BBY a new arena was built at the bottom of the lake.Episode I Adventures 10: Festival of Warriors Lake Umberbool first appeared in Ryder Windham's 2000 children's book, Episode I Adventures 9: Rescue in the Core.Episode I Adventures 9: Rescue in the Core It shared a name with Umberbool City, a location mentioned in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds *Episode I Adventures 9: Rescue in the Core *Episode I Adventures 10: Festival of Warriors *Episode I Adventures 11: Pirates from Beyond the Sea *Episode I Adventures 12: The Bongo Rally *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Umberbool Umberbool
