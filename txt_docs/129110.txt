|description= |animals= |plants= |other= |edible= |inedible= |potable=Ortolans |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Gannesa juice was a juice that was potable by Ortolans. Around 4 ABY,Gannesa juice's mention in "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale" takes place a few months before the part of the story that is concurrent with , the events of which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. the Ortolan musician Max Rebo excitedly anticipated a dinner at which gannesa juice would be served. However, he ultimately received neither the dinner nor the juice. Gannesa juice was mentioned in "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale," a short story written by John Gregory Betancourt and published in the Tales from Jabba's Palace story collection in 1995.
