Hurom
816

thumb|250px|Hurom Hurom was the capital city of the planet Thule. Located in the center of the city was an ancient Sith Temple which continued to attract devotees for thousands of years following the Great Sith War. Much of the population spoke some dialect of the ancient Sith. It was believed that many of the buildings in Hurom were older than some other civilizations, having been built to military standards. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Capital cities Category:Thule locations
