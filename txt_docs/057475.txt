Baobab School of Speed-Learning
641

The Baobab School of Speed-Learning was a division of the Baobab Archives. Devoted to the education of beings in short amounts of time, the school specialized in the teaching of languages. This is not surprising, due to the Archives' penchant for philology, and the Huttese program at this institution was particularly popular. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Schools
