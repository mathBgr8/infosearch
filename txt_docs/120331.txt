Valiant (MC40a light cruiser)
2004

Valiant was a light Calamari cruiser in the service of the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. At the time of the Battle of Hoth and immediately after, the vessel was part of the Defiance Task Force and escaped the Anoat sector along with the rest of the task force's ships after a brief campaign against Admiral Garreth Holtz.
