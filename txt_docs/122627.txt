Kotiate
2020

The Kotiate was an Imperial Star Destroyer during the Galactic Civil War. During one supply run, the Rebels attacked with a strike force consisting of X-wings, B-wings, and A-wings. With his ship's systems failing, the captain of the Star Destroyer tried to retreat in a shuttle, but it was also destroyed.
