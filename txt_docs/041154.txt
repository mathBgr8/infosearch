She reported to the mayor any useful information their guests foolishly divulged. She was at the Outlander Club on Coruscant shortly before the outbreak of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY. Mya Nalle was portrayed by Amy Allen in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones.
