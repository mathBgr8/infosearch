The planet orbited the sun between Pii IV and the system's second asteroid field. It was located in the Arkanis sector in the Slice portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Like the rest of the Pii system, the planet was a seven-day hyperspace journey away from the Perlemian Trade Route and along the Old Corellian Run and the Trans Gulf Route hyperlanes. The Pii system's fifth planet was mentioned in "The Greel Wood Haven," an article written by C. Robert Carey and featured in the sixth issue of Star Wars Adventure Journal, a roleplaying magazine that was published by West End Games in May of 1995.
