Real Deal
1855

*Emala, Grees, and Sligh |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Real Deal was a Mon Calamari Sailfish originally provided by Lando Calrissian to Jae Juun and Tarfang. Juun sold the vessel to the Squib war profiteers Emala, Grees, and Sligh who used it to supply material to the Killiks during their border conflict with the Chiss. The Real Deal featured a sensor package enhanced with a LongEye booster that was a hallmark of Calrissian's ship modifications. In sublight pursuit, it could outrun Han Solo's Dray-class transport, the Swiff. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War Category:Criminal starships Category:Sailfish Category:Squib starships
