|commission= |destroyed=3963 BBY, Serroco |retired= |battles=Battle of Serroco |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Republic Navy |navy=Battle Group Serroco |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} CX-03 was a cargolift vessel in the Republic Navy during the Mandalorian Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Mandalorian warrior clans. In 3963 BBY, as the Mandalorians threatened the planet Serroco, CX-03 was attached to Admiral Saul Karath's Battle Group Serroco intended to protect the world. When the Mandalorians arrived, they devastated Serroco with nuclear missiles,Knights of the Old Republic 15 and CX-03 was lost. The ship's destruction was later noted in an edition of The Admiral's List, an official publication issued by the Republic Navy, along with two other cargolift vessels: CX-09 and CX-14. CX-03 was first mentioned in The Admiral's List: Remember Serroco!
