Julie Eccles
1175

Julie Eccles is an actress who provided the voice of Jan Ors in Star Wars: Dark Forces and played Ru Murleen in the live-action cutscenes of Star Wars: Rebel Assault II: The Hidden Empire. Years earlier, she had a minor role in Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade as Indiana Jones's secretary, Irene. *Dark Forces Official Player's Guide Category:Star Wars: Dark Forces voice actors Category:Star Wars: Rebel Assault II: The Hidden Empire actors
