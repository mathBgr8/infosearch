Toat
1312

Toat was a male Neimoidian who served as a top-ranking official in the Trade Federation on Maramere during the Separatist Crisis. In 24 BBY, Lord Toat was killed by Sol Sixxa's pirates aboard the pleasure liner Syren, before the pirates scuttled the ship.
