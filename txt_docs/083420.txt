|release date=November, 2003 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Jedi Guardians |followed by=Rogues and Scoundrels }} The Empire Strikes Back was the sixth expansion card pack made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game produced by Wizards of the Coast, and based on Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. The game was introduced on November, 2003. The expansion symbol was that of the Empire. Expansion contains 210 cards including 72 rare, 69 uncommon and 69 common cards.
