Maslak plant
613

Maslak plants was a type of an aquatic plants that grew on the planet Somov Rit. They had a thick green stalk growing out of a lily, ending in a bulbous pod that contained winged seeds. The seeds would be released when the pod burst. *Jedi vs. Sith 1 Category:Aquatic plants
