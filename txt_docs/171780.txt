Verack group
1030

thumb|right|250px Two Cargo Ferries from Verack group brought Experimental technology to Grand Admiral Zaarin during the Galactic Civil War. During the Conflict at Mylok IV, Habeen Ferries Verack docked with Zaarin's Nebulon-B2 frigate Shamus as part of an arranged deal for Admiral Zaarin to inspect prototypes for new hyperdrive systems suitable for the TIE Avenger. Verack 1 & 2 withstood heavy fire from Nharwaak dissidents during the transfer, yet all cargo arrived into Imperial hands unharmed. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Habeen starships
