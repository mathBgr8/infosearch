Candle of Pure Light
1393

The Candle of Pure Light was a sacred Ewok item bestowed to Cindel Towani by the Ewok shaman Logray before the expedition to save her parents from the Gorax around 3 ABY. It could be used to summon Queen Izrina, of the Wisties, when she needed help. *Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure Category:Sacred Ewok items
