Star Wars Science Adventures: Journey Across Planet X
8419

Star Wars Science Adventures: Journey Across Planet X is a Legends junior novel written by Jude Watson and K.D. Burkett, with interior art by Frank Boyd Forney. It is the second installment of the book series Star Wars Science Adventures, and it was published in February 1999 by Scholastic. The book is set in 0 ABY, and it follows young Rebel Stuart Zissu and the droids C-3PO, 4B-X, and R2-D2 as they journey across a strange planet, using science to find rescue. Journey Across Planet X did not receive a sequel, and its story was left unresolved. ONLY SCIENCE CAN SAVE THEM! Trapped on a mysterious planet, Artoo-Detoo, See-Threepio, Forbee-X, and a young Rebel named Stuart Zissu must summon all their courage &mdash; and knowledge &mdash; in order to survive. Strange creatures, weird weather, and killer twisters stand in their way. There's only one chance at rescue.
