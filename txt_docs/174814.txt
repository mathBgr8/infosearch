Indiko
2020

Indiko was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer in the service of the Galactic Empire during 4 ABY. The Indiko was active on the frontlines of the war mere weeks before the Battle of Endor. The only thing known about it is the fact that it, alongside the Star Destroyer Darkk, were sent to collect Tan Maarek Stele after he defended Emperor Palpatine's personal yacht from a failed assault launched by Imperial traitor Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial-class Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
