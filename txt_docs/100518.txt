Puzzleflower
616

The puzzleflower was a flowering plant from the planet Nimban. The flower was eaten by the Nimbanese people. In order to get to the edible part, the flower's petals had to be unlocked. This forced the Nimbanels to develop advanced puzzle-solving skills early in their history. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Fruits and vegetables Category:Plants of Nimban
