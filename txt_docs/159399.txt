Michael Stern
1239

Michael G. Stern is an author who wrote many of the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game supplements for West End Games. He is also credited, along with Douglas Kaufman and Greg Gorden, with developing the official sabacc rules that were included in West End Games' Crisis on Cloud City supplement. Stern is credited as a writer on children's TV shows Imagination Movers, Sofia the First, and Doc McStuffins.
