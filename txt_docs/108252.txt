Frang Colgter
1357

Frang Colgter was the husband of Condren Foreck who was a wealthy farmer's daughter on the agriworld of Azbrian. Frang's marriage to Condren happened to coincide with Lando Calrissian's arrival, in which he hoped to make a proposal to Condren. The unfortunate chain of events led Lando, in his subsequent nuptial visits, to call first rather than arrive unannounced. *Ambush at Corellia Category:Humans Category:Males
