thumb|right|150px|Full-length view of a warbot Warbots were assassin battle droids used by mercenaries during the days of the Galactic Republic.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords The Jedi Exile encountered two warbots on Telos IV. A wily Twi'lek female mercenary commander attempted to trap her by stationing herself and two of her warbots, as well as a minefield with around 6 minor fragmentation mines, in front of the Jedi Exile as the Jedi charged towards the abandoned Czerka base in RZ-0031. As with the other unfortunate mercenaries, the Twi'lek was slain, and her two warbots were destroyed. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Battle droid models
