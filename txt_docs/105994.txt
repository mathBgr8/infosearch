Hamadryas
3070

The Hamadryas were a simian humanoid species with green skin and ape-like snouts. They seemed to not possess the need to blink, and the species lacked a true carotid artery.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia The holoperformer Cebann Veekan was a notable member of this species. Senator Haydel Goravvus of Taris was a Human/Hamadryas hybrid. The name of this species was created by Bob Vitas as part of the What's The Story? campaign on StarWars.com. The species was named after the Hamadryas baboon. As revealed in his Star Wars Blog, Bob Vitas intended that the Hamadryas species to be from a planet called "Papio". *Star Wars: The Force Unleashed novelization *The Star Wars Holiday Special *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Star Wars: Yoda Stories *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Primate sentient species
