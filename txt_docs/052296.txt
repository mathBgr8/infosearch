Pelagia's Moon
2749

Pelagia's Moon was a space station that had been converted from the bruised Pelagia Star that orbited the planet of Pelagon. The Pelagia Star was the flagship of the Pelagian navy until the day the Galactic Empire attacked Pelagon. It was a massive ship of an unknown and ancient design; during the attack even three Imperial-class Star Destroyers couldn't bring the vessel down. Ultimately, what was left of the massive vessel was converted into a space station and starport, having been renamed Pelagia's Moon.
