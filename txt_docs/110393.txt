thumb|right|250px|Mo-die among [[Tulon's task force.]] Mo-die was the callsign of an All Terrain Scout Transport assigned to a detachment commanded by Colonel Beri Tulon. This detachment was intended to join with the forces of General Brenn Tantor for the Massacre on Abridon, but was instead completely destroyed when Tulon turned the guns of his AT-AT, Annihilator 1, on his squad in an attempt to defect to the Rebel Alliance. *Star Wars: Force Commander Category:All Terrain Scout Transports
