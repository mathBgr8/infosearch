Aama
1169

Aama was a female Major with Alpha Blue, a group within the New Republic Intelligence Service. In 17 ABY, Aama was one of Hiram Drayson's senior facilitators who informed Drayson the location of the Millennium Falcon. Drayson passed the information to Formayj, who then gave it to Chewbacca in order to save Han Solo's life. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:New Republic Intelligence personnel
