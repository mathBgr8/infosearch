Gungan shield generator
2104

The Gungan shield generator was a two-part device used to generate protective shields around a large segment of a battlefield. Presumably, like the Gungan personal energy shield, it had the same basis as other Gungan plasma technology. The force field created by this device was capable of deflecting energy weapons, including shots from AATs, projectiles, and other small objects, but could be breached by infantry. These generators were usually brought to battle by large war beasts such as fambaas.
