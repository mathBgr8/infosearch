Hydration tablet
1442

Hydration tablets were tablets designed to keep beings hydrated in situations with no access to fresh water for long periods of time. In 1000 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion during the time of the New Sith Wars, Bane, a student at the planet Korriban's Sith Academy, used hydration tablets to survive a trip to the Valley of the Dark Lords, an ancient Sith burial ground. Hydration tablets appeared in the 2006 novel Darth Bane: Path of Destruction, written by Drew Karpyshyn. *Darth Bane: Path of Destruction Category:Drugs and medicine
