A Dantooine flapjack was foodstuff from Dantooine. The Rebel crew of the shuttle Bonderium mentioned this dish in an analogy to help the chef droid L9-G8 fly the shuttle in combat against Teehl's pirate ship Grasp. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Food
