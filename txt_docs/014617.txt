**Suolriep sector ***Tefau system |terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} Tefau was a location in the Tefau system of the Outer Rim Territories' Suolriep sector. Soth Petikkin, a Tefaun who worked as a recruiter for the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War, was from Tefau. Tefau was created in 1998 for Soth Petikkin's card in the Special Edition Limited set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Tefau system, and thus Tefau itself, in grid square S-9.The Essential Atlas Category:Suolriep sector locations
