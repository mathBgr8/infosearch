The New Osler Flejj Beasts were a professional Pugil team on the planet Lannik. In 22 BBY, its members included Rolsat Noviee and Kova Naan. Rolsat, nicknamed "Rolstone", was the highest-scoring and highest-paid Pugil player in Lannik history, and was later revealed to be a Clawdite changeling disguised as a Lannik. New Osler Flejj Beasts
