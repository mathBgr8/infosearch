The Alazhixazha, Vratix Occupation or Thriving Season was a time period marked by the Vratix dominance of the bacta market.Strongholds of Resistance The natives of Thyferra under the Ashern forced the Bacta Cartel to compete with local companies and alien business interests, including the Hutts.
