"Tears of Aquanna" was a song performed by Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes. It was considered a favorite by band member Ickabel G'ont because it allowed him the opportunity for a long, elaborate Fanfar solo. *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Songs by Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes
