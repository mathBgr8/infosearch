Queen's Victory
1188

thumb|right|Queen's Victory in flight. Queen's Victory was a modified bulk freighter owned by Hawker Bryce-Kelly and his copilot Rypka. They used it to race a group of Rebel wannabe's flying in the Limping Lady to the planet Zoma V. Sometime after this, they landed the ship on the planet Reginard when they were trying to flee from Imperial Customs Frigate 517. When they arrived there, however, they were not pleased when they discovered that there was not a Stellar Class spaceport, as indicated by the homing beacon.
