T-81 airspeeder
1355

The T-81 airspeeder was developed by Incom Corporation, sometime after the death of Grand Admiral Thrawn. They could carry several passengers. Lando Calrissian used one of the airspeeders at the time of the Caamas Document Crisis. The T-81 appears in Timothy Zahn's novel Specter of the Past. *Specter of the Past Category:Airspeeder models Category:Incom Corporation products
