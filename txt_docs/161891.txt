El'Tar Miskin
1306

El'Tar Miskin was the concierge of the Greentree Pointe resort on Dorumaa.Living Force Campaign Guide Miskin had previously worked as a shipping clerk on Ipsus for Naescorcom, but was fired for negligence, having mixed up a pair of shipments. Apparently, a group of Outer Rim pirates received textiles from Line One, while a Coruscant orphanage was shipped several crates of thermal detonators from Line Two. Miskin could have been executed for the failure, if not for his personal charm. Miskin enjoyed work that had him interacting with people, especially rich and influential people.
