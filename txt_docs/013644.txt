Betshish
2198

Betshish was a planet located in the Gordian Reach along the Pinooran Spur between Mogoshyn and Kli'aar. It was the homeworld of a sentient species. Betshish was discovered by a scout, Lile Gorsek, but his claim to the world had to be abandoned when the sentient species was discovered on landing.
