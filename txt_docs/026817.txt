Dankko Verrimuch
2682

Dankko Verrimuch was an Anx Podracer pilot who competed in the final race of the Vinta Harvest Classic on the planet Malastare around 32 BBY.Star Wars Omnibus: Emissaries and Assassins establishes that the events of Star Wars (1998) 15 take place approximately 32 years before the Battle of Yavin, which corresponds to around 32 BBY per The New Essential Chronology. Verrimuch's presence at the Classic's starting point in Malastare's methane wastes was recorded by cam droids and announced by race commentator Fodesinbeed Annodue, who labeled the group as experienced professionals and mocked the Anx's name for its similarity to the phrase "thank you very much." A green-skinned Anx, Verrimuch wore green goggles with blue lenses, a purple jacket, and a gold-colored necklace during the Vinta Harvest Classic's deciding race.
