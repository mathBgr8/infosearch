Ugly Truth
966

Ugly Truth was a spacecraft located on Coruscant during the time of the Yuuzhan Vong War. The spacecraft, formerly owned by Hasville and Adray Terson, was disguised as a covered landspeeder. Viqi Shesh attempted to escape the planet on it, but was stopped by members of Wraith Squadron, who escaped on the ship instead, traveling in it to Borleias with the Jedi. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order Category:Starships of the New Republic
