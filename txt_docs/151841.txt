Katraasii Droid Ballet Studio
1739

The Katraasii Droid Ballet Studio was located in the Katraasii Spaceport on the planet Katraasii. This building was located in the spaceport on the planet Katraasii. Catering solely to droids, the studio's doors remained locked to organics, opening only at the approach of a member droid. It also had a second story, with a fair view of the streets outside. In 10 ABY Mara Jade visited the studio, and glimpsed several protocol and GNK droids executing a well-timed, tightly choreographed dance. This building appears in the game Mysteries of the Sith. It is only enterable if the player takes the time to follow a particularly slow-moving droid through the streets of Katraasii. Reaching the room will not affect or advance the scenario, but it does contain some very large health and shield replenishes, as well as a small ammo deposit. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith Category:Buildings Category:Easter Egg articles
