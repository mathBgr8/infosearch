New Galactic Empire (disambiguation)
2554

The terms New Empire or New Galactic Empire may refer to the following organizations: *The First Order, the successor state to the Galactic Empire after the former regime fell during the Battle of Jakku. *The Final Order, also known as the New Empire and the Sith Empire, which was led by the reborn Darth Sidious but fell during the Battle of Exegol. *The New Empire, a Sith Empire founded by Darth Malgus to replace the reconstituted Sith Empire formed by Darth Vitiate. *The New Sith, also known as the New Sith Empire, a Sith Empire created by Darth Ruin that lasted throughout the millennium-long New Sith Wars. *The Imperial Remnant as well as various splinter factions and smaller empires created after the fracturing of the Galactic Empire at the Battle of Endor, such as the Dark Empire led by the reborn Darth Sidious, the Restored Empire of Ennix Devian, or Trioculus' Empire. *The New Order of Darth Caedus, which called for the reorganization of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances into a successor of the Galactic Empire known as the New Empire. *The Fel Empire, also known as the New Galactic Empire, which was the restored and ethically reformed version of the Galactic Empire created from the Imperial Remnant, led by the Fel dynasty. *Darth Krayt's Galactic Empire, also known as the New Galactic Empire and the Darth Krayt's Sith Empire, which was a revival of Darth Sidious' Galactic Empire after Darth Krayt overthrew the Fel dynasty. *The non-canonical Galactic Empire, also known as the New Galactic Empire, a dictatorship featured in The Star Wars. *Am and Karre's Galactic Empire, also known as the T-Empire, an Imperial faction which emerged following the Battle of Exegol that appeared in the Star Wars: Visions episode "THE TWINS". *In the Original draft for The Force Awakens, the first order was to be called The Neo-Empire
