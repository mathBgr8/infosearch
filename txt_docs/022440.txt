Richard McGonagle
1310

Richard McGonagle born October 22, 1946) is an American voice actor who provided the voices of General Grievous, Kit Fisto, Orvos, and Harvos in Season Three of the Star Wars: Clone Wars cartoon. He also provided the voice of a Rebel soldier for the computer game Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader. He also had a bit part in the Lucasfilm cult/camp film, Howard the Duck.
