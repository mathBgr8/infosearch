Patriotic Art Competition
596

The Patriotic Art Competition was an Imperial competition arranged by the Arts Division of COMPNOR. In 1 ABY the competition was won by an artist with a painting entitled "Lord Vader: Protector of the Empire's Youth". He received an all-expense-paid vacation to Coruscant.
