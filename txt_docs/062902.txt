Gold Squadron was the name of several starfighter squadrons in operation throughout galactic history: *Gold Squadron, a Rebel Alliance and New Republic starfighter squadron that participated at the Battle of Yavin and the Battle of Endor *Gold Squadron, Galactic Republic starfighter squadron during the Clone Wars *Gold Squadron, Galactic Republic squadron that participated in the Battle of Muunilinst and participated in the Battle of Boz Pity *Gold Squadron, the Nharwaak squadron which fought against the Galactic Empire *Gold Squadron, a Rebel Alliance B-wing starfighter squadron during the Galactic Civil War *Gold Squadron, a Rebel Alliance squadron that participated in the Battle of Yavin, the Battle of Endor, and the Battle of Saijo *Gold Squadron, New Republic squadron that participated in the Battle of Folor and the Battle for the Katana fleet *Gold Squadron, New Jedi Order squadron that participated in the Battle on Nam Chorios
