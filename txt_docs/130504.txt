Core World Critics Association
1296

The Core World Critics Association was an association of holodrama critics that was composed of the some of the most influential media critics of the Core Worlds. They gathered annually to discuss the year's holofilms. During their gathering in 22 BBY,The date 13:3:14 corresponds to 22 BBY per the reasoning here. the president was Jaysa Namoor. That same year, they criticized the writing quality standards of the films of the upcoming summer season. The films they preemptively panned included Another Idiot's Array 2, Cataclysm Prism 4, Crimson Empire III, Whelping Day, Split Infinitive, Lightspeed Lightweight, Tenta-kill 5, and A Google of Gornts.
