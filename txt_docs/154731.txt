Battle over Far Thunder
2133

After the Second Battle of Obroa-skai, the damaged New Republic ship Far Thunder was allowed to limp back to the staging area at Kashyyyk with minimal assistance. En route, it was intercepted by Yuuzhan Vong fighters and attacked. A small relief force of New Republic starfighters drove them off, although the ship had to be scuttled. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way Category:Battles of the Yuuzhan Vong War
