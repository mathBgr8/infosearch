Resolution (Imperial-class)
2197

Resolution was an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer that served under the overall command of then-Admiral Thrawn during Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's attempted coup against Emperor Palpatine shortly before the Battle of Endor. It was tasked with accompanying the Star Destroyer Grey Wolf and the Strike-class Star Cruisers Indomitable and Triumph in protecting an Imperial convoy carrying Missile Boats.
