Security Act amendment
1482

The amendment to the Security Act was a piece of legislation passed by the Galactic Senate as an amendment to the Galactic Constitution that was voted by a wide margin the day after the Battle of Coruscant. It nominally transferred command of the Jedi High Council from the Senate directly to the Supreme Chancellor, thus providing him with the powers from the Galactic Constitution to disband the Jedi Order. It also removed Jedi and the Galactic Senate oversight of the Republic Military, placing the chancellor in direct command as Supreme Commander. It was written by Palpatine but given to a loyal senator to introduce, in order to maintain the façade of the Senate forcing power on him against his will. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization Category:Clone Wars laws and acts Category:Amendments to the Galactic Constitution
