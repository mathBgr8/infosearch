Combat Attack Transport
1789

The Combat Attack Transport (C.A.T.) was a model of combat vehicle deployed at the Battle of Hoth. It was one of the few vehicles piloted by stormtroopers; in the case of the Battle of Hoth, it was piloted by snowtroopers.
