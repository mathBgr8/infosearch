Telkadis hiding tree spider
1211

The Telkadis hiding tree spider was a mostly static predator from Telkadis. Spider's eggs hatched inside a taku tree and hatchlings burrowed deeper inside the trunk while they grew. They hunted small rodents like voletes, preying by the presence of heat in the surroundings.
