The Dhashaan Shield was a group of mercenaries formerly run by Dharus. They were hired by the Nhoras to fight the Clatear in 19 ABY for 30,000 credits a day, as was their former leader who came out of retirement in order to lead them. *Specter of the Past Category:Mercenary units
