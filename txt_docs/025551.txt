Fidelity
2734

Fidelity was a Thranta-class War Cruiser and the sister ship of Valiant and Courage. Manned by droid crews, the three ships served as the escort of the Alderaanian War Frigate Another Chance, which was carrying all of Alderaan's leftover weapons after the world disarmed in the wake of the Clone Wars. When the Another Chance was located by Rebel sympathizers and its weapons given to the struggling Alliance in the wake of the Battle of Derra IV, Fidelity was left to wander the Alderaan system. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: The Official Starships & Vehicles Collection 29 Category:Thranta-class War Cruisers
