Bail Organa (Star Destroyer)
2260

The Bail Organa was a Star Destroyer (with gravity well projectors added), a sister ship of the Mon Mothma and the Elegos A'Kla. The vessel served as flagship of Fleet Group Two, one of three major battle groups organized to defend Coruscant in 27 ABY. Under the command of Garm Bel Iblis, the Bail Organa and the ships it commanded went head-on into a massive Yuuzhan Vong fleet, losing virtually all of their ships in the battle.
