Den-mother (Squidge)
481

Den-mother was a term used for the guardian of Squidge children. They also had a level of psychic ability, in which Otalp and Remoh's Den-mother displayed when she said that they would find a storyteller. This turned out to be none other than C-3PO, who was shot by the Vindar moments later. Category:Society and culture
