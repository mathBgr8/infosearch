Mullanite lattice-sculpture
573

The Mullanite lattice-sculpture was an artform that used specially constructed lattices, on which were grown creeping vines of Mullanite. The University of Rudrig was home to at least one of these sculptures, which was situated near the headquarters of the university's Curriculum Committee, until Chewbacca drove a groundlimo through it. *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Art
