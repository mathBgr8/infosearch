The ship had a Class 2 hyperdrive, and a Class 20 backup. 850 kilometers per hour could be achieved in atmosphere. Deflector shields were supplemented by 2 fire-linked forward laser cannons. It was produced in two models. The Model 1 had more seats and was designed to carry 24 passengers. Although the Model 2 carried fewer passengers than the original variety, it had four enclosed sleeping cabins, and boasted a miniature galley.The Unknown Regions The Radell Mining Corporation, the Galactic Empire, and the New Republic all employed the Mu-2.
