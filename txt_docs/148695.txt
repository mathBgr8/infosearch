Jennie Lee Taggar
1377

Jennie Lee Taggar was a starfighter pilot with the Rebel Alliance. She flew a Y-wing at the Battle of Endor. She died while attacking a Star Destroyer. She had a son named Rone Taggar, who became a pilot with the New Republic, and would later name his Recon-X starfighter after her, the Jennie Lee.
