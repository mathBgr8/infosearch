Urartu
1660

Urartu was a Wookiee bouncer who worked at the Contumely Club, enforcing the Club's policies of allowing no Humans or droids on the premises. Urartu made use of a small creature who translated for the Wookiee. Urartu stopped Roark Garnet from entering the Club at some point during the Galactic Civil War, despite Garnet having business with a person inside the establishment. In real life, Urartu was a kingdom in eastern Anatolia (present-day Turkey), existing from before 1000 BC until 585 BC.
