Battle of M2398-3
1938

The Battle of M2398-3 was a small battle between Wraith Squadron and a band of pirates which used the moon Blood Nest and a local asteroid belt as their headquarters. The battle was won by Wraith Squadron, but Jesmin Ackbar was killed in a dogfight over the moon. *X-Wing: Wraith Squadron *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles in the Halori sector Category:Battles of the Hunt for Zsinj
