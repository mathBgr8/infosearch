Lol Miilarta
977

Lol Miilarta was a female Hapan from Terephon. She was that world's delegate in the Hapes Consortium, and voted to ally with the New Republic in the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Hapans Category:Legislators and councilors
