Cerrabore
1091

The cerrabore was a hulking creature indigenous to the swamps of Naboo. Cerrabores were captured and partially domesticated by the Gungans to serve as living battering rams to smash enemy fortifications and military installations. With their thick skulls and natural aggressiveness, they could tear through reinforced metal walls through sheer brute force.
