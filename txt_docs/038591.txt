Xeno
1100

thumb|280px|The Xeno furniture line Xeno was the name of a furniture line designed and manufactured by the Affodies Crafthouse of Pure Neimoidia. The line included a couch, a recliner, a table, a desk, a desk lamp and a rug. In 1 ABY Jag Silon gave out pieces of this furniture line to members of the Galactic Vacant Building Demolishing Movement. These pieces of furniture were rewards for players of Star Wars Galaxies that participated in the house packup events in 2007 and 2009.
