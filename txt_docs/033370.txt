Tullius Valorum
1795

The politician Tullius Valorum was a member of House Valorum, a renowned political dynasty that began over 1000 years prior to the Battle of Yavin. Alongside Tullius, the Valorum lineage included figures such as Tarsus and Laeca Valorum. When read together, their names sounded like an induction list for the Hall of Heroes, a structure on the planet Coruscant in the time of the Galactic Empire that honored notable figures.
