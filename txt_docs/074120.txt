Astin
1002

The Astin was an Imperial CR90 corvette that sent a TIE Fighter to destroy a Rebel probe called PR-V74 near Klaymor 4-2 which was 15 Klicks from its location. After the fighter was destroyed the probe the ship returned to pick up the fighter.
