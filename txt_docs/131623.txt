Rizaron
944

Rizaron was an Aramadia-class thrustship of the Duskhan League fleet. Rizaron was patrolling ILC-905 with Tholos, when it detected a New Republic Defense Fleet patrol. The two thrust ships ambushed the New Republic forces and succeeded in destroying Vanguard.
