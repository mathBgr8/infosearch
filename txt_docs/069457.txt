Autohopper
1037

An autohopper was a type of automated self-propelled transport vehicle which traveled on preprogramed courses. They were routed by a central instruction processor (CIP). Autohoppers were used on Danuta, among other places, and an autohopper with governmental markings was parked in the Trid spaceport, next to the Moldy Crow. Until 44 BBY, they were used by the dictatorial government of the planet of Kegan as listening and recording devices as a means of effectively spying on the planet's population. *Jedi Apprentice: The Fight for Truth *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Vehicle models and classes
