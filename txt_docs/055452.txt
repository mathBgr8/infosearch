Tarsh maxer
1791

Tarsh maxers were a type of food enjoyed by Human Jedi Padawan Anakin Skywalker. They could be prepared by roasting, which made the food give off a distinctive aroma. Tarsh maxers were the headliners on the menu of the cantina Panky's on the planet Barlok. Skywalker and his master Obi-Wan Kenobi ate tarsh maxers alongside Padawan Lorana Jinzler at Panky's in 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. Tarsh maxers appeared in Timothy Zahn's 2006 novel Outbound Flight. *Outbound Flight Category:Barlok food
