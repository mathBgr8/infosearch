Bondar
2596

thumb|right|150px|A bondar crystal Bondar was a type of lightsaber crystal. Bondar crystals converted the energy of a lightsaber blade into a different form of energy. A lightsaber constructed with a bondar crystal would stun rather than cut, and so during the Old Republic era were used in training sabers. An asteroid in far orbit around the Alderaan system was the only known source of bondar. This crystal was capable of briefly stunning an opponent if the lightsaber scraped their flesh. The portrayal of this crystal is slightly different between sources. In the video game, Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic this crystal is presented as a lightsaber upgrade. It has a 25% chance to stun an opponent on hit for one round. In the later Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide for the Saga Edition Roleplaying Game, player created Jedi may use a Bondar crystal instead of an Ilum crystal when building a lightsaber. If they do so, the saber becomes a nonlethal stunning weapon.
