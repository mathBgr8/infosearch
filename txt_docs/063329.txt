Oradin Diamond
1043

The Oradin Diamond was a garish medal designed by Admiral Lon Isoto to commemorate the 181st Imperial Fighter Wing's role in protecting Oradin during the Battle of Brentaal IV. It was only awarded once, to all members of the 181st&mdash;including Isoto himself&mdash;except for Turr Phennir and Soontir Fel who were instead given the Vuultin Starburst and declined any reward respectively. The award was a dubious honor given its creator's infamy. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 23 Category:Galactic Empire awards
