Balmorra flu

Balmorra Flu was a mildly contagious form of the flu causing intense sinus pain and swelling, accompanied by the excessive production of mucus. Han Solo once caught Balmorra Flu while on the planet Dantooine. *Rebel Force: Uprising *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Flu
