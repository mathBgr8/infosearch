Balana thranta
1722

The Balana thranta was one of the many species of thranta that were native to the planet Alderaan and became virtually extinct upon the planet's destruction. They escaped extinction, having been exported to Bespin long ago, along with the common thranta and its cousin, the giant thranta. The Balana subspecies was distinguished from the others by its larger body that had a brownish coloration. Furthermore, the wings themselves seemed more hook-like at the front giving an appearance of hands. The tail itself was also different with the horizontal lobes being larger than the vertical ones.
