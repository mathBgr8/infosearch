Ssi-ruuvi
1462

Ssi-ruuvi was the language used by the Ssi-ruuk from the planet Lwhekk. It could not be reproduced by Humans, who did not possess the organs needed to do so. It was formed by melodic clicks and whistles. When spoken it was considered similar to the sound of a flute, which caused the Ssi-ruuk to be nicknamed "Fluties". It was rare for a Ssi-ruuk to learn other languages, preferring to use translators.
