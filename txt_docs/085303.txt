Fohlg
1277

Fohlg was a Quarren male. Fohlg was the caretaker for the Cambriele Exploration Auditorium, following the years after the Invasion of Naboo. He was also known to many others as an access point to the museum's archives. Fohlg could be bribed and would allow people access to the museum's galactic database.
