Pendarran's Revenge
1842

Pendarran's Revenge was an armed organization formed by Kolor Delan that fought against the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|150px|[[Kolor Delan, founder and leader of Pendarran's Revenge.]] The group was based on Shalm and named after the Pendarran Warriors, who were led by Kolor's father, Gendal Delan. The Penderran Warriors fought with the Jedi Knights and the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars, but were exterminated in the Great Jedi Purge. The Pendarran's Revenge group occasionally aided the Rebel Alliance, however, after the Empire's defeat at Endor, Kolor became hostile to the New Republic.
