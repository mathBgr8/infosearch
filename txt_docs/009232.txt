Cloud-Mother
1245

Cloud-Mother was the name of a floating herd city above the planet of Ithor. It was best known for its hospitals, glass manufacturing, guest centers, and large shuttleport. In the 12 ABY Herd Meet, it was where the New Republic delegation was housed, in the Presidential Guest House. *Children of the Jedi *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Herdships
