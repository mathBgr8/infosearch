|subscribers= |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=57 |series=Star Wars: The Official Magazine }} Star Wars: The Official Magazine 57, also styled as Star Wars Magazine 57, was the 57th issue of the Star Wars: The Official Magazine. While labeled as an issue of the magazine, it is actually a reprint of the Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: Official Episode III Souvenir Movie Guide that was published in April 2005. 57
