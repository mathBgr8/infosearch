Doimo
1208

Doimo was a female Duros who became famous for their role as a military commander. Due to their fame as an officer, there were female members of Doimo's species named after them. Doimo was mentioned in the Galactic Campaign Guide, a 2003 sourcebook for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game written by Peter Schweighofer and J.D.
