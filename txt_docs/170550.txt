Dalls Petothel
1632

Dalls Petothel was a male Human who served as an officer within Imperial Intelligence during the first years of the New Order. He was the father of Gara Petothel and husband of Edallia Monotheer. He worked as an Imperial Intelligence agent for Director of Imperial Intelligence Armand Isard.
