D'Arc Vaetes
1653

Colonel D'Arc Vaetes was a Human male surgeon who served the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars on Drongar. In 20 BBY, he was the maximum military authority in the RMSU-7. He survived the attack of the Separatist forces against the medical center, and the RMSU was moved to a new location.
