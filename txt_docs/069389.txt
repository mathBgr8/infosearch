Anaxsi
1850

The Anaxsi were the Human residents of Anaxes. They were fervently loyal to the Galactic Republic and later the Galactic Empire, and the planet was one large Imperial Navy training and staging ground. The Anaxsi were members of the Azure Imperium and Anaxes was the chief shipyard and fortress. During the Galactic Civil War, the Anaxsi showed such loyalty to the Empire that hostility existed towards the Rebel Alliance.
