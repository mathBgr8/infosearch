Coalition of Automaton Rights Activists
1771

The Coalition of Automaton Rights Activists was an organization set up to promote droid rights in the century before the onset of the Clone Wars. The Coalition was founded at a time when anti-droid sentiment was rife in the Galactic Republic. Automaton Rights Activists believed droids should be afforded the same rights as sentient beings, although their methods of protest varied greatly. While most Coalition members held peaceful demonstrations, there were known to have been several outright attacks on droid salvage yards. The Iron Knights attempted to gain the support of groups such as the Coalition of Automaton Rights Activists during the Clone Wars, but the politically-motivated actions of the Supreme Chancellor left the Knights with few allies.
