Glory Room
564

The Glory Room was located in the heart of the Sunulok, flagship of Warmaster Tsavong Lah. Over thirty meters wide, its center contained a cognition throne for Lah to coordinate his forces, and was filled with blaze bugs, which were organized to form a diorama of the various battlefields during the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starship rooms
