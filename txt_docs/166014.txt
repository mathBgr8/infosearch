The Force Is With Them: The Legacy of Star Wars
481

The Force Is With Them: The Legacy of Star Wars is a featurette produced by Gary Leva that was included in the 2004 DVD box set of the Star Wars Original trilogy. It explained the cultural impact Star Wars had on the world and how it changed the lives of the actors involved. Category:Home video featurettes
