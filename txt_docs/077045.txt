|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=c. 5.5 BBYStar Wars Omnibus: Droids |series=Star Wars: Droids (1994) |issue=5 }} Droids 5 is the fifth issue of Star Wars: Droids (1994), a Dark Horse comic book series starring R2-D2 and C-3PO. It was published on August 16, 1994 by Dark Horse Comics. In the droid trade the only use for old units is as spare parts and scrap. So even See-Threepio is impressed when he and Artoo meet veritable antiques Q-E and 2-E, two diminutive Model Es who have operated a nursery for several generations of Kalarbans. But despite their longevity, Q-E and 2-E carry a horrific secret that could spell the end for all droids!
