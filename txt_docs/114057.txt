Weblin
1544

Weblin was a Regional Governor serving the Galactic Republic and later the Galactic Empire as a Grand Moff. When Emperor Palpatine declared his Galactic Empire in 19 BBY, Weblin became Grand Moff of Sector 6 and assumed the role of commander of the 6th Sector Army, better known as Black Sword Command.
