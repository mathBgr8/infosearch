Gerhalt III was situated in the Gerhalt system, which was a part of the Galactic Republic by 17,000 BBY. From that yearThe Essential Guide to Warfare till 3996 BBY, a hyperlane connected the system to the Surron and Tarmidia systems; however, the hyperspace route had ceased to exist by 3960 BBY. At some point during the history of the Republic, Duros spacers helped avert the Vinth Conflict&mdash;the namesake of the Vinth system in the Altier sector's neighboring Venzeiia sector&mdash;on Gerhalt III. Gerhalt III was mentioned in "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Duros," an article authored by Bart Armstrong and Steve Miller with Cory Herndon and published in Star Wars Gamer 2 in February 2001. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Gerhalt system, and therefore Gerhalt III, in grid square O-13.
