Jerry Hardin
542

Jerry Hardin (born November 20, 1929) is a voice actor. He provided various voices for the Star Wars radio drama. Hardin has appeared in several feature films and television shows since the 1970s. He is perhaps best known for playing the mysterious FBI agent known only as "Deep Throat" on The X-Files Category:Star Wars radio drama voice actors
