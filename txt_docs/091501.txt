The Chamber
602

The Chamber was a secret organization which was made up of some of the Tapani sector's most powerful business and political leaders. The goal of the Chamber was to ensure that the Freeworlds Territory seceded from the sector which would have allowed the Chamber to place its members into key positions of power within the new sector. *Emily Janse *Lords of the Expanse Chamber, The Category:Tapani sector culture
