Smugglers of the Outer Rim
6399

"Smugglers of the Outer Rim" is an article by Doug Shuler which introduces the characters Jin-Jin, Bora Boru, Jericho Donovan, Josephine Donovan and SCr-114 as well as their respective starships. Also of note, it introduces many aspects of the Bosph culture.
