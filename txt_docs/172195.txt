thumb|right|250px|Lambda leaving [[Victory-class Star Destroyer/Legends|Victory-class Star Destroyer Protector.]] The Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Lambda served aboard Admiral Harkov's Victory-class Star Destroyer Protector during the Battle for Honor, during the Galactic Civil War. Shuttle Lambda ferried one of Harkov's officers to the Rebel Alliance Calamari Cruiser Margaret.
