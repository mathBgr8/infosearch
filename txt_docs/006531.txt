Bendu symbol
5646

thumb|right|200px|Bendu Symbol The Bendu symbol was the symbol of the Order of Dai Bendu. It was a circle connected by eight even spokes, which represented the unification of the galaxy by the Force. Due to the Order of Dai Bendu study of numerology, the number eight created by this symbol had great meaning.Star Wars: Complete Cross-Sections The reconstituted Sith Empire used a six-spoked crest similar to the Bendu symbol.Star Wars: The Old Republic By the Clone Wars, the Galactic Republic had adopted the symbol as the Republic crestKeeping the Peace to represent their own role in galactic unification. A six-spoked form of the symbol was also used by the Republic during the Battle of Coruscant immediately before the rise of the Galactic Empire. The symbol was later the basis for the Imperial crest.
