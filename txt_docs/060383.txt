Pizzazz 9
1700

Pizzazz 9 is the ninth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated June 1978 and featured the conclusion of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. The issue also features the article "Space Heroes, Monsters and Far-Far Out Worlds," discussing science fiction films including Star Wars. An ad and order form to join the Official Star Wars Fan Club can be found in the issue as well. This issue was advertised in a television commercial for Pizzazz, which showed the first page of The Final Fury!, the issue's segment of The Keeper's World. 09
