Hutar Zash
2435

Hutar Zash was royal advisor to the Lannik High Court under Prince R'cardo Sooflie IX. Zash had been in his post as military advisor for many years, serving also under Sooflie's father, the former King. Though he was an old friend of Jedi Master Even Piell, during the Red Iaro peace-talks on Malastare, Zash participated in an Iaro-led assassination attempt on the lives of their Jedi councilors. During a night-time air taxi ride, he attempted to kill a number of Jedi Masters, including Mace Windu, Adi Gallia and Even Piell, with thermite bombs strapped to his chest. The plot failed when Piell sliced the air taxi in two with his lightsaber, separating the Jedi from the terrorists moments before the bombs exploded, killing Zash and his accomplice.
