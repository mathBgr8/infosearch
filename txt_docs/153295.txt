Jhemiti
1467

Lieutenant Jhemiti was a male Mon Calamari officer who served in the New Republic Defense Force. In 6 ABY he served on the Corusca Rainbow, an Immobilizer 418 interdictor cruiser under the command of Uwlla Iillor, in preparation for the assault on Coruscant.
