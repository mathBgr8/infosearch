Nethius
1939

Sergeant Nethius was Darknon Station's customs official and security guard. thumb|left|200px|Nethius was [[Darknon Station's only security guard.]] Nethius was also an Imperial spy sent to Darknon Station to monitor spacer traffic and alert Imperial authorities of any suspicious visitors or activity. He was trained as a soldier and special operative but hid his true intentions well. Nethius protected the location of a secret Imperial research station deep within the Itani Nebula. Imperial patrol starships occasionally dropped supplies to the Darknon Station and proceeded to the Itani Nebula. Nethius had a friendly demeanor which he used to make casual queries to spacers.
