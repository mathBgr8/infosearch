Lisa Fuson
1798

Lisa Fuson is a voice actress who has played several roles in Star Wars video games. Her roles were mainly Princess Leia and Vana Sage, but she also acted in the Nickelodeon cartoon series, Hey Arnold!, in several minor roles.
