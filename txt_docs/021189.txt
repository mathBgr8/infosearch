|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood |issue=4 }} Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 4 was the fourth issue from the Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood series of comics, and was released on February 10, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. The pieces of the mystery are slowly coming together as Kir Kanos races towards the Zanibar homeworld, Xo, in an attempt to save Mirith Sinn from a fate worse than death. But will he be in time? Will personal animosities between the two be too great? Meanwhile, Grappa the Hutt finds himself in an awkward situation, with the heat of the Black Sun starting to make him sweat.
