Wert Jopi
1146

}} Wert Jopi was a Gungan on Naboo who was believed to be Force-sensitive. In 1 ABY, Ebenn Q3 Baobab hired a guide under the pretense of escorting "that Jedi Knight, what's-his-name" into Moenia for dinner. The real reason for bringing Jopi into Moenia was unknown.
