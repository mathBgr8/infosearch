Tun Wala
1037

Tun Wala was a manufacturing center located on the planet Arda II. The T-6 diodem was manufactured here during the Galactic Civil War. Its massive trade shows were visited by S'kytri who performed dramatizations of Initiation Hunts and sold trinkets from Skye. *"As Long As We Live..." Category:Arda II locations Category:Cities
