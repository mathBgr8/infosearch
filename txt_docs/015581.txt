Eric Cagle
2003

Eric Cagle is a freelance game designer and writer who has done work for Wizards of the Coast on the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. His contributions include co-writing several books, adventures, and scenarios for both the d20 Star Wars Roleplaying Game and the Star Wars Saga Edition Roleplaying Game. *Ultimate Alien Anthology - April 2003 *Ultimate Adversaries - June 2004 *Threats of the Galaxy - May 2008 *Galaxy at War - September 2009 *Galaxy of Intrigue - December 2010 *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook - July 2013 (2005) (2005) (2004) (2004) *Cat and Mouse (2004) *Only the Force (2004) Category:Role-playing game authors
