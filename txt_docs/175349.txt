Unidentified Underworld species
1317

An unidentified Underworld species dwelled in the sewers of the Coruscant underworld around 0 ABY. They were a small, winged species with green fangs, which lived in the ruins of ancient buildings. They revered Wookiees for unknown reasons. It was customary of the species to exchange gifts upon meeting new people. When Chewbacca and his entourage encountered the Underworld avians circa 0 ABY, they swapped weapons for glowworms to assure the creatures they harbored no ill will towards them. *Star Wars Missions 15: Voyage to the Underworld Category:Avian and airborne sentient species Category:Core Worlds sentient species
