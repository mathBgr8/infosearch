First Sun Mobile Regiment
1823

The First Sun Mobile Regiment was a mercenary outfit that worked with the Galactic Empire during the height of the Galactic Civil War. The First Sun Mobile Regiment specialty was Search, Locate and Annihilate operations. As such, they would be supported by infantry vehicles and would contract out to the Imperial Army whenever the latter had need of their talents. Their relationship turned sour when Moff Nile Owen executed an entire company of the First Sun Regiment, after which the Regiment distanced itself from the Empire.
