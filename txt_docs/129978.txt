Amanda's Toy II
1229

Amanda's Toy II was a ship owned by Unlimited Horizons Inc.. It was registered on Kalla VII with registration number RN18-950319. In 16 ABY, Luke Skywalker tracked this ship as it passed the inner flight control buoys of the planet Teyr. *Shield of Lies Category: Personal starships Category:Starships
