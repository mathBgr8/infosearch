Under the command of General Grievous, and armed with new and precise maps of local hyperroutes with the goal of preparing for an invasion of the Core Worlds. During the offensive, the First Fleet and Third Fleet of the Confederate Navy was launched from Yag'Dhul. The fleets were provisioned by supply lines from Sullust and Thyferra. The battles during the campaign were considered to be General Grievous's greatest successes. Weeks into the Offensive, at 0221 local time, the Separatist forces broke through the outer system defenses of Duro, making it another major world to be targeted by Operation Durge's Lance. After the fall of Jyvus Space City, one of the largest space cities of Duro, the Separatists lowered the planetary shield and began a orbital bombardment of the planet's industrial factories.
