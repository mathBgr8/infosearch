Docking Bay 86
3112

Docking Bay 86 was a starship hangar area located in the port city of Mos Eisley on the planet Tatooine. In 0 BBY, the bounty hunter, Spurch "Warhog" Goa docked his cruiser, the Nova Viper, here while searching for work from the planet's criminal kingpin, Jabba Desilijic Tiure. Later, in 0 ABY, the smuggler Platt Okeefe used this docking bay for her starship, the Pok's Demise on a layover on Tatooine. She was forced to flee the bay in her ship when chased by bounty hunters. It was also the place where the Pulsar Skate was berthed while Corran Horn, Mirax Terrik and Gavin Darklighter visited Huff Darklighter to buy arms and armor for Rogue Squadron, during the Bacta War.
