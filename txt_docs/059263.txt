Ox (BFF-1 bulk freighter)
1167

Ox was a BFF-1 bulk freighter that served with the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War . The vessel was part of a Rebel convoy, escorted by the CR90 corvettes Wolf, which contained supplies that were intended to aid in the establishing of a new base. However, the ships were attacked by the Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Red Wind.
