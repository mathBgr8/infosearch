"Defender" deflector shield generator
2172

The "Defender" deflector shield generator, also known as the "Defender" Deflector Screen Projector, and "Defender" line projector, was a model of deflector shield generator manufactured by Chempat Engineered Defenses. The system was used by Incom Corporation in their T-65 X-wing starfighter. Projectors were positioned along the starfighter's wings and tail for full coverage.
