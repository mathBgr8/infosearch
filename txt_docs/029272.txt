Borneck
2672

Bornecks were Near-Humans native to the planet Vellity. Borneck skin tones varied from pale yellow to orange-brown. They were patient, sensible pacifists, and farmers who enjoyed hard work and holidays. They lived to an average of one hundred twenty standard years. The Borneck were a peaceful species, with a intense work ethic and patient demeanor. They valued hard work and had a very close-knit society. Borneck life revolved around family and community. Young Borneck were expected to get an education, and job, and then raise a family. While primarily employed in manual labor jobs, Bornecks were also skilled pilots and often found work on shuttles and tramp freighters. The Bornecks originated on the temperate planet of Vellity in the system of the same name, located in the Trax sector of the Mid Rim.
