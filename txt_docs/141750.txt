Rethin Sea
1218

The Rethin Sea was the name of the liquid rethin (also known as "rethen") metal core of the gas giant BespinThe Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia. During the Trioculus affair, the Empire constructed a factory barge above the sea to harvest metals and tibanna and produce material.
