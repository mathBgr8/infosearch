Balti
805

The Balti were a species of furry bipeds with prominent tusks. The smuggler Bingo Mehndra was a Balti. Rather than a birthday or a hatching day, Balti celebrated their "Spawning Day." Balti strongly resembles the Khormai species. Category:Mammalian sentient species
