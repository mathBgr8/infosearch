Primarin Starshipwrights Union
1801

The Primarin Starshipwrights Union was a union of starshipwrights that shared its name with the Primarin system of the Inner Rim. It produced a sensor array that came to serve as the primary of such arrays in the unique, cannibalized freighter Cannibal of Barosa Warren, the head of the Galactic Outdoor Survival School. By 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Primarin Starshipwrights Union had ceased to exist. The Primarin Starshipwrights Union was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Shipwrights
