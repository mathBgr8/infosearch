Shadowcloak (clothing)
1915

A Shadowcloak was a traditional garment of the Umbaran people of the Ghost Nebula. They were made up of numerous strands of a light blue material. When seen in the ultraviolet spectrum, it appeared to have numerous colors that had no name in most sentients' tongues. To species who could see into the ultraviolet, these cloaks appeared to be a riot of colors, including berl, crynor, nusp, and onsible. They were used for spying and assassinations, for they would be virtually undetectable to other Umbarans.
