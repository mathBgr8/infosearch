Milo (disambiguation)

Milo was a common first name shared by the following individuals: *Milo Fourstar, the Human Rebel Alliance operative on Arbra; *Milo Mensix, the Mustafarian founder of Mensix Corp; *Milo Phineas, the Imperial Navy Captain; *Milo Strander, Stormtrooper; *Milo Thirk, the Bureau Chief for Alliance Intelligence in the Bespin system.
