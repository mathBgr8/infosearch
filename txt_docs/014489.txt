Pouffra Circuit
1623

}} The Pouffra circuit was an amateur podracing circuit in the Pouffra system. It was notorious for being one of the safest (and, therefore, wimpiest) tracks in the galactic podracing circuit, and consequently, serious professionals competitors looked down on the Pouffra's racers.
