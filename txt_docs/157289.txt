Quarren Tower
950

Quarren Tower was a building in Coral City, Mon Calamari. Its uppermost room was a large, circular area that had a 360-degree view of the surrounding area. Near the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War, it was used as a place for meetings of the New Jedi Order leadership. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Buildings Category:Mon Cala city locations Category:New Jedi Order locations
