Entukan Yesosko
2046

Entukan Yesosko was a flamboyant elite Rodian actress of Rodian Theater. A member of the Chattza clan, she was an avid supporter of the Grand Protector Navik the Red, with the ambition of writing a play based on his life.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide She was appointed as the leader of the Rodian Galactic Drama Troupe, the only Rodian theatrical corps allowing to leave the planet. She was the Troupe's most famous member and front woman, and played lead roles in Ghest plays (classic tragedies where Rodian villages were devastated by a ghest). She also acted as Navik's agent, the only member of the Troupe knowing its secondary use to target Chattza enemies; she agreed to inform the bounty hunter Inntak of their schedules, and whenever bounties attended their performances.
