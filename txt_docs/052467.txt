They were mined by Republic colonists. In 27 BBY, a dispute broke out between the colonists and the inhabitants of the planet of Roxuli itself. Palpatine tricked Obi-Wan Kenobi into abandoning the Outbound Flight (thereby sparing the life of Anakin Skywalker) to negotiate between the two parties. Roxuli and its asteroid field provided ore to half the sector.
