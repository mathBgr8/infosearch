|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Insider is an article written by Morrie Mullins as a tie-in to the Living Force campaign. It was published on the Wizards of the Coast website on May 26, 2005 and took the form of a HoloNet poster warning that Sot-sirc was back in the Cularin system. It was shortly after Sot-sirc was last in the system that Len Markus discovered the Darkstaff and the system vanished from the galaxy for 10 years.
