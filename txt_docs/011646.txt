Andrel Lacmar
1334

Andrel Lacmar was a Human military officer during the Galactic Civil War. As the ranking Imperial Army officer in the Brak sector, General Lacmar reported to Moff Lesan Ramier and worked together with Admiral Trier of the Imperial Navy. Trier and Lacmar clashed heavily over battle tactics. Lacmar was relatively inexperienced when he was promoted to command rank and Trier preferred to use unconventional tactics, a combination that produced a series of ineffective battles. *Flashpoint!
