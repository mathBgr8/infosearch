|location= |start date=May 23&ndash;25, 1987 |end date= |type= |series= |preceded by= |followed by=Celebration (1999) }} Starlog Salutes Star Wars also known as Star Wars 10th anniversary convention was the first officially sponsored Star Wars convention. It was "A 10th Anniversary Tribute to George Lucas and the Galaxy Far, Far Away Which He Created," held at the Stouffer Concourse Hotel in Los Angeles, California from May 23 to May 25, 1987. It was organized by Starlog Magazine and Creation Conventions.The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 1 George Lucas attended. Gene Roddenberry, the creator of Star Trek, was a surprise guest; this resulted in the only known photograph of him with Lucas being taken by Dan Madsen. thumb|250px|Gene Roddenberry and George Lucas at the convention Category:Official Star Wars conventions
