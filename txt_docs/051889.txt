Brodan
1130

Brodan was a droid parts manufactuer that produced the RBY Data Collection Scanning Head and Triple-Opposing Midweight Pincher. Brodan was first mentioned in Star Wars: DroidWorks, a 1998 Star Wars Legends educational video game developed and published by Lucas Learning for PC and Mac. *Star Wars: DroidWorks Category:Droid technology companies
