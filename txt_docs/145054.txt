Jenth-44
1425

Jenth-44 was one of Grand Admiral Thrawn's clone sleeper cells. Jenth-44 was located on Pakrik Minor by Thrawn, only to later swear allegiance to the New Republic on the grounds that they just wanted to be left alone.
