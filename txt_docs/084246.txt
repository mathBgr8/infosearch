Epoh Bahb
2181

Epoh Bahb was a famed Bith musician who performed with Jasod Revoc and his Galactic Revue throughout the Clone Wars at the end of the Great Peace of the Republic. A Bith, Epoh Bahb was a musician and a good friend of Epoh Trebor. By the time of the Clone Wars, Bahb and Trebor had partnered with up-and-coming holo-actor Jasod Revoc to participate in a tour produced by HoloNet Entertainment under the banner of Jasod Revoc and his Galactic Revue. As they toured the galaxy, the performers would hold concerts for the troops of the Grand Army of the Republic.
