Polosich
1861

The Polosich were a sentient species with velvety skin of yellow pigmentation. Tyew Fordrod was a Polosich living during the Galactic Civil War. Fordrod was traveling aboard the Kuari Princess, a giant luxury liner, on a cruise up the Relgim Run when it was attacked by pirates while passing through the Maelstrom. Fordrod had booked accommodation on the Lido Deck and considered himself superior to anyone who traveled on less expensive decks. The Polosich were created by Ray Winninger for the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game adventure, Riders of the Maelstrom, published by West End Games in 1989. *Riders of the Maelstrom Category:Sentient species
