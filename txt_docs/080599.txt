CNS strength enhancer
1061

The CNS Strength Enhancer was an experimental system that amplified power signals along the lengths of the central nervous system. This generator, attached to a belt, provided greater impulses to all muscles, as well as a resistance to all sorts of perturbations of the user's system. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Belts
