Krall Praget
1393

Krall Praget was a male senator of the New Republic who represented Edatha circa 16 ABY. Praget chaired the Council on Security and Intelligence during the Black Fleet Crisis, and was still on the council advising the New Republic Senate during the Yuuzhan Vong War.
