Polarizer
886

thumb|right|[[Jeigh Esse holding her polarizer.]] The polarizer was a unique weapon created by Jeigh Esse out of a BlasTech EG-7 blaster. It was able to emit high-energy charges for several meters when fired. This weapon gave Jeigh great job satisfaction when she used it properly. The polarizer was created by Chris Cowdrill, a Star Wars fan, for the backstory of their Jeigh Esse entry in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. *Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 7 Category:BlasTech Industries products Category:Electrical weaponry Category:Rifles
