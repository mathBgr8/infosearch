Boash'ua
1993

Boash'ua was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate captained by the Bothan Kursk Mal'ia during the Galactic Civil War. It most famously took place in the Battle of Kathol System, in which it assisted in the defeat of Moff Kentor Sarne and the liberation of Kathol. The Boash'ua served as Captain Kursk Mal'ia's flagship. Category:Bothan starships Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the New Republic
