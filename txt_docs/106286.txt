Synthtech Medtech Corporation
974

Synthtech Medtech Corporation was a company that specialized in the production of medical equipment. Medical scanners such as the MDS-50 and irradiation boxes were among the equipment produced by Synthtech Medtech. The equipment was specialized for use in military operations. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Platt's Smugglers Guide *The Far Orbit Project Category:Medical companies
