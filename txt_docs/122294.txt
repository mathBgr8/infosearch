Voralla Morbo
1723

Voralla Morbo was female Human who owned the Thyferran cantina Morbo's Place. She was sympathetic to the Rebellion, helping transport bacta and keeping a docking bay clear for them. She had a history with Han Solo after a shipment of Norvanian grog went undelivered, and slugged him at the end of the mission to Thyferra. She passed on information about an Imperial plot using contaminated bacta on Delrakkin.
