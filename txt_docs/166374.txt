Forbidden Systems
702

The Forbidden Systems was a region of the galaxy that was cut off from Aquilae by an asteroid belt, en route to Ophuchi. It contained the planet Yavin. The Forbidden Systems originally appears in the rough draft to A New Hope. The first draft eliminates the term, assimilating it into the Great Rift. *The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film Category:Star system groups
