Reinforced fiber armor
1692

Reinforced fiber armor was a type of light armor that was crafted by armorers on worlds where metals were in short supply. Its makeup consisted of the fibers of the jung-ju tree bound together with synthetic materials, thus offering good, flexible protection. Fiber armor was especially designed to channel energy-weapon attacks away from the body. The fibers woven into its exterior caused it to absorb and harmlessly disperse energy damage.
