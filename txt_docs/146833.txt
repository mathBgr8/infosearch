Rainbow berry
1986

thumb|250px|[[Nippet/Legends|Nippet, Kneesaa, Wicket, and Wiley carry home baskets full of rainbow berries.]] thumb|right|250px|A [[rainbow berry bush.]] }} Rainbow berries were a multicolored fruits from a bush species that grew on the forest moon of Endor. They were used to make rainbow berry pies and Wistie Fest pies. One year in particular, the Ewoks of Bright Tree Village had a shortage of rainbow berries, but woklings Nippet and Wiley discovered a plethora of berries out in the forest just in time for the Harvest Moon Feast. Rainbow berries were created by author Melinda Luke and illustrator Pat Paris.
