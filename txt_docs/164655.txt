Sound dampening belt
811

The Aratech sound dampening belt, or SD belt for short, was a sound dampening stealth unit manufactured by the Aratech Repulsor Company in the period following the Jedi Civil War. It reduced all sound that the user emitted, and even helped to protect against sonic weapons. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Aratech Repulsor Company products Category:Belts Category:Stealth field generators
