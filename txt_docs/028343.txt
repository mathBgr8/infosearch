Olin and Lands
1473

Olin/Lands was a business organized by Ferus Olin and Roan Lands in the few years prior to the Clone Wars, with their offices on Bellassa. It was established to protect victims of corrupt businesses by aiding them in creating new identities and finding new homes. It was a thriving business until the end of the Clone Wars and the rise of the Galactic Empire. The business was shut down when the Imperials announced the warrants for the arrests of the two entrepreneurs, since Olin was a former Jedi Knight. The office of Olin/Lands was located on a quiet street in the Cloud Lake District in the city of Ussa.
