Garnu Hral Eschen
886

Garu Hral Eschen was a male Drovian. He was killed, along with his whole family by Gopso'o warriors. Though his manner of death is unknown, he was apparently disemboweled. His descendant Knezex Hral Piksoar would later work for the New Republic. *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Drovians Category:Males
