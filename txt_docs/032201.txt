Escape Repulsorchute
644

thumb Escape Repulsorchutes were carried aboard Drone ore shuttles. An archaic parachute, repulsorlift engines were attached to the wings that acted as emergency escape equipment in atmospheric bailouts. They were replaced with escape pods after the Great Sith War. *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Repulsorcraft models
