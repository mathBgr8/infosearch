Mecho-organic droid
1215

The mecho-organic droid was a drone created by Ship to wield a lightsaber in combat against Luke Skywalker. At first, Ship thought Luke was merely another simulated opponent, part of the games he played with the ship's computer. When Luke managed to overcome the mecho-organic droid, however, Ship realized that Luke was real. *Star Wars (1977) 38 Category:Battle droids
