Constancian
1797

Constancians were short, telepathic mammalian humanoids indigenous to Constancia. The Constancians were telepathic humanoids with flaming orange-colored skin, orange hair, and yellowish-gold eyes. They had child-like features. The Constancians evolved on the Deep Core planet of Constancia. The area around their homeworld was explored sometime between 1000 BBY and 25 BBY.
