Columus Tourism Board
621

The Columus Tourism Board was a Columi government organization based out of Bailee, Columus that was charged with attracting tourists to the planet. Shortly before the Clone Wars began, it came under fire from physicians like Gidoo Zehow for claiming that Columi's low-gravity environment had therapeutic effects for the overweight.
