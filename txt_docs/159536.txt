Crab Submarine
1848

The crab submarine was a vehicle used by the Trade Federation's aquatic navy. One such model was commandeered by Harro Ruuk after his sensor station on Maramere was destroyed by the Lok Revenants. Ruuk escaped the explosion in his vessel, and launched a desperate assault on Nym and his colleagues. Ruuk was defeated, and his submarine was destroyed, though Ruuk himself escaped in an escape pod.
