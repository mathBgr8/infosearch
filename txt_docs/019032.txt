|pages=22 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope |issue=1 }} Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope 1 is the first issue in the Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope series of comics. It was published on May 2, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. A long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away &hellip; The events and players are very familiar, but something isn't quite right. Luke Skywalker valiantly attacks the Death Star, fires his torpedoes and&hellip; they miss their mark! Welcome to a Star Wars you never imagined, a Star Wars shattered, where the possibilities are ENDLESS! The first in a series of non-continuity Star Wars stories which are sure to delight fans of all ages. Taking off from the end of events in the movie, readers will follow Luke, Han, Leia, C-3PO, and R2-D2 on a journey every bit as action-packed and fantastic as the original trilogy. Make sure you don't miss out on the Star Wars event of the year!
