As such it can be established that the events of the short story take place in 19 BBY. |series=Living Force |preceded by=Living Force: One Minute to Midnight |followed by= }} "Living Force: Last Stand" is the final short story in the Living Force campaign. It was written by August and Cynthia Hahn and was published on the Wizards of the Coast website on February 1, 2007. It continued the saga of Darrus Jeht, following on from Living Force: One Minute to Midnight, and concluding the story arc. Darrus Jeht returns to the Cularin system shortly after Order 66. Having disabled his ship's communications to prevent his clone troopers receiving their orders, they ambush two Republic vessels that are attacking starships containing fleeing Jedi from Almas. After successfully defeating them, Jeht detects the Shadowblade, a Confederate superweapon thought destroyed in a prior battle.
