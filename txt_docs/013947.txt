Selcaron was a moon located within the Selaggis system of the Quelii sector, a part of the New Territories portion of the Outer Rim Territories. It lay on the hyperlane known as the Braxant Run, which connected it to the Bandomeer and Vahaba systems. Selcaron was one of the moons of Selaggis VI, a gas giant that orbited the yellow star Selaggis. Its surface featured gray seas and brown soil. The moon had a favorable climate that attracted colonists.Star Wars: Rebellion In 7 ABY, Selcaron was the location of a New Republic colony that was attacked by Warlord Zsinj, who used his Super Star Destroyer, the Iron Fist, to obliterate the colony. Selcaron was introduced in the 1994 novel The Courtship of Princess Leia, written by Dave Wolverton, which mentioned Selaggis as the location of a colony that was obliterated by Warlord Zsinj's Super Star Destroyer Iron Fist. Selaggis then appeared in the 1998 PC video game Star Wars: Rebellion, which described it as a planet with a "favorable climate that has attracted colonists."
