SoroSuub Business Academy
1031

The SoroSuub Business Academy was located on the planet Sullust. It was here that potential SoroSuub Corporation employees would be trained before being allowed into the company. Though most of the students were Sullustans, there were rare occasions in which they would allow beings of other species to study here. The classes were split into semesters, and at the end of the course there was a final graduation exam.
