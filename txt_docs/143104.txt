Night Havok
1674

Night Havok was a modified YT-1300 light freighter owned and operated by Kara Saffch and her brother Willi Saffch. The ship was often used by them in their scavenging operations and in making cargo hauls in the area of Korbin. The Night Havok was prone to frequent mechanical trouble. At one time the Night Havok was used by the Saffchs to transport black market medical supplies for the New Republic. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:YT-1300 light freighters
