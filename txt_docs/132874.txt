Holt family
1405

The Holt family was a military family that hailed from Anaxes and kept an estate in the Sirpar Hills. Holts had served in the Republic and Imperial Navy continuously for seven centuries and the family was counted among the Generationals. The Holt Cross was named for them. Bannidge Holt and Fleet Admiral Holt were members of the family. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Essential Atlas Category:Families
