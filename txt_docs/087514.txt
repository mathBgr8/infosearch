**Imperial Navy |masters= |apprentices= }} DS-55-2 was the call sign of an Imperial Navy TIE pilot who served by 0 BBY. That year, during the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic, the Alliance attempted to destroy the Imperial DS-1 Orbital Battle Station superweapon during the Battle of Yavin.The New Essential Chronology DS-55-2 fought in the battle, alongside numerous other TIE/LN starfighters that attempted to defend the battle station from the attacking Alliance starfighters. Ultimately, the TIE fighters were unsuccessful in defending the station, which was destroyed in the subsequent Alliance victory over the Empire. thumb|right|250px|The image used by the Battle of Yavin set of the Star Wars Trading Card Game to represent DS-55-2 was of a TIE fighter that was previously identified as Black 4, piloted by DS-61-4. The "DS-55-2" name was coined by the 2003 Battle of Yavin expansion of Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Trading Card Game. Identified as the designation of a TIE fighter in the card game, 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia would later establish that name as the call sign of the corresponding TIE pilot. This article assumes the latter source is correct. Furthermore, the Star Wars Trading Card Game uses a film still of a TIE fighter that was previously identified as Black 4, piloted by DS-61-4, in the 1996 A New Hope Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
