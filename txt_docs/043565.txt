Henry Gilroy
6285

Henry Alan Gilroy developed the Star Wars: The Clone Wars movie and animated series, working at the Skywalker and Big Rock Ranch facilities with George Lucas. He served as head writer for Season One, penning several episodes. He is also the author of several Star Wars comic books.
