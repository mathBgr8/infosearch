Ambris Selit
2134

Grand Moff Ambris Selit was the regional governor of the Greater Maldrood Sector in the Galactic Empire. Faced with the task of suppressing well-established Alliance to Restore the Republic forces on a number of planets in his territory, Grand Moff Ambris Selit asked Army Command for advice on which surface craft were best deployed in such circumstances. The military report was acquired by the Rebels, and became the basis of their own standard guide to Imperial armor.
