Janelle Harcourt
1605

Janelle Harcourt was a female Human and the wife of Imperial Navy captain Aron Harcourt. After Aron was demoted to a desk job after being scapegoated for the destruction of the Imperial Star Destroyer Anya Karu, she and her husband settled on Coruscant, where Aron took a desk job. In 0 ABY, more than three years after Janelle's death, Aron hired bounty hunter Boba Fett to retrieve a holorecording of his late wife from the wreckage of Anya Karu.
