thumb|An advertisement for Sholto's Shuttles!. Sholto's Shuttles! was a travel company, presumably owned by Sholto, which rented out Lambda-class T-4a shuttles for either day excursions or party bookings. They were known for running a service to an asteroid belt. Category:Tourism companies
