}} Bitter Winter was the Basic name for a disease also known as Brekken Vinthern on Corellia. This affliction struck the elderly and created holes in their memories. Toob Ancher, a Corellian smuggler, caught this disease during retirement and slowly lost his mind. It had similarities to melanncho. Category:Mental conditions
