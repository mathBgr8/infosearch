Jengardin double-bladed vibroblade
2257

The Jengardin double-bladed vibroblade was a type of vibroblade manufactured by Yalandross PowerMasters. It was a unique and dangerous weapon designed by the Jengardin Millennial Warriors. Its cleaved edges enabled the user to literally disembowel their enemies. The size, shape and most likely weight of this weapon would make it difficult to utilize by an untrained individual, making it an iconic weapon for the Jengardin Millennial Warriors.
