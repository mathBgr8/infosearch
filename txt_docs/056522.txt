R2-D1
1926

R2-D1 was an R2-series astromech droid. Queen Amidala's royal starship held a complement of droids, one of which was R2-D1. Various sources indicate the six droids onboard the Royal Starship in were R2-D2, R2-B1, R2-R9, R2-N3, R2-M5, and G8-R3.Star Wars: The Phantom Menace: The Expanded Visual Dictionary A single source, a "Set Piece" article in Star Wars Insider 94, replaces the name of R2 unit R2-M5 with R2-D1. This is likely a contradicted alternate name for the same droid. Category:R2-series astromech droids
