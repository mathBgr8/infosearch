Eel (Strike-class)
1966

Eel was a Strike-class medium cruiser used by the Galactic Empire. Eel and Asp were to go up against MC40a light cruisers Ta and Re. Both ships had TIE Avengers from Avenger Squadron and Assault Gunboats from Talon Squadron.
