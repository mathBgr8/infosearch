Darth Nihl's predecessor
3625

A Sith was a member of the One Sith that served as the predecessor of the Sith LordLegacy (2006) 1 Darth Nihl in the position of the Hand of the Dark Lord of the Sith and leader of the One Sith Darth Krayt. They participated in a massacre on the planetLegacy Era Campaign Guide Ossus around 130 ABY, during the events of which they perished. In the wake of the massacre, Nihl was named the new Hand as Darth Talon&mdash;the intended successor&mdash;was not yet ready. Seven years later, Sith Lady Darth Maladi pointed out to Nihl that&mdash;unlike the Sith Lord&mdash;Darth Talon had been a Sith since birth when explaining Krayt's obvious preference for the latter. The Sith Lady also added that Nihl only became a Hand following the predecessor's premature death because of Talon's unreadiness. The predecessor was mentioned in the fifteenth issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series, written by John Ostrander and Jan Duursema and published by Dark Horse Comics on August 8, 2007.
