Juzzian
504

The Juzzians were an alien race from the Juzzia system with cone-shaped bodies and many teeth. There were both sedentary and mountain-hopping Juzzians. They made colony markers and presumably developed the Juzzian armlock. *The Cestus Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Inner Rim sentient species
