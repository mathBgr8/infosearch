|gender=Female |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Green |skin=Blue |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Jedi Order *Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} This individual was a Rutian Twi'lek Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. This female Twi'lek Jedi fought alongside the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. During battle on an unknown planet between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, she was assigned to a battlefront under the leadership of Councilor Ki-Adi-Mundi. Joined by recently knighted Rivi-Anu and Eon, the group fought valiantly to repel the Separatist Droid Army. When a Victory-class Star Destroyer plummeted toward the battlefield, they all believed they would die, until Rivi-Anu held back the warship with an astounding display of the Force. Rivi-Anu waited until all Jedi and clones had fled the field, including this Twi'lek Jedi, and then allowed the ship to crash down upon herself.
