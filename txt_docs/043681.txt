Rachuk roseola
926

Rachuk roseola was an irritating skin inflammation, which was caused by a virus that was found in the Rachuk system, specifically on the world of Vladet. This form of roseola infected nearly every visitor to the planet, unless they were properly vaccinated prior to their arrival.
