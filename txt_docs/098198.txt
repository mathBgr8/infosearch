Nexu (starship)
2462

Nexu was a Acclamator-class assault ship that served as a troop transport in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. The warship was placed under the command of Admiral Arikakon Baraka. CT-9/85 served as its communications technician. The ship was used during training exercises on Vandor-3 in 21 BBY, then sent to Ord Cestus to threaten the planet's leaders. During this mission, the ship targeted the bunker where the leaders of the Five Families were hiding and destroyed it.
