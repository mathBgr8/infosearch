Bilbringi (CR90 corvette)
724

The Bilbringi was a CR90 corvette used by the Habassa. The Imperial frigate Mayhem attempted to attack the Filve and the Bilbringi believing it to be a secret meeting between the Rebels and the Habassa. However, the Rebels leaked the information intending to capture the Mayhem.
