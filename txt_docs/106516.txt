Trif (Human)
1671

Trif was a former Imperial who defected and joined the Alliance of Free Planets. Trif served both as a staff pilot under Fenn Shysa and as a soldier during the Nagai–Tof War. During that conflict, shortly after the Battle of Endor, he joined a fleet that gathered at the planet Godo, where he, along with fellow pilots Wedge Antilles, Fenn Shysa, Nien Nunb, and Maggie, sided with Han Solo in the standoff between the Alliance fleet and the Godoans.
