Twi'lek Dancer (Interceptor IV-class frigate)
1073

The Twi'lek Dancer was an Interceptor IV-class Frigate in the service of Tyber Zann and the Zann Consortium. Unlike most ships in its class, the Twi'lek Dancer made use of a cloaking device to give it an added level of defense against detection.
