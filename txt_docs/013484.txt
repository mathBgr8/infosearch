The planet had at least two moons, and was subject to frequent yet brief periods of torrential downpour. It was an industrialized world with a small shipyard, and was located at a hub of trade routes. By 980 BBY, the capital city of Ciutric IV was Daplona. Near Daplona was the estate of Darth Bane, Dark Lord of the Sith. Bane made Ciutric IV his residence after losing the orbalisk armor which prevented him from actively socializing with other sentient beings, and he and his apprentice, Darth Zannah, lived there for several years. Ciutric IV was a member of the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars. thumb|left|200px|The Battle of Ciutric By the time of the Galactic Civil War, Ciutric IV was twenty parsecs from major Imperial garrisons. It was controlled by Imperial Governor Brothic, but was later controlled by Prince-Admiral Delak Krennel. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, Ciutric IV was conquered by the invaders at the beginning of the conflict.
