|intl release= |runtime=3 minutes |director=Genndy Tartakovsky |writer= |production company= |guests= |timeline=22 BBYThe Essential Reader's Companion |prev="Chapter 9" |next="Chapter 11" }} }} "Chapter 10" is the tenth episode of the Star Wars: Clone Wars animated television series, and the tenth episode in Season One and Volume One. Directed by Genndy Tartakovsky, the episode originally aired on Cartoon Network on November 20, 2003. A massive space battle rages above Muunilinst, with Commander Anakin Skywalker leading V-19 Torrent starfighters in his customized Jedi starfighter, Azure Angel. A huge number of Geonosian starfighters join the battle, and Skywalker orders "Red Leader" to fire his missiles over the bow of a Republic assault ship. The baffled clone pilot and his fellows obey and Skywalker leads the cloud of enemy fighters around the ship into the missiles. With the remaining missiles still chasing him, Skywalker leads them into the docking bay of a Muunilinst gun platform, destroying it. A mysterious starfighter appears out of hyperspace, firing into the Republic fleet and obliterating Blue Squadron. Skywalker warns the clone pilots of the new arrival: "This one's mine."
