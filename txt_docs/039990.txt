GamorrCare

|products=TuskBlaster |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} GamorrCare was the name of a company that specialized in providing medicinal hygenic products to Gamorrean clients. One of its more popular brand-name products was TuskBlaster, a polish used to eliminate yellow stains from a Gamorrean's tusks, which it put an advertisement for on HoloNet News. thumb|left|350px|An advertisement for TuskBlaster from GamorrCare. GamorrCare first appeared in HoloNet News, a website published prior to the release of . Advertisements for TuskBlaster, a product made by GamorrCare, appeared in many issues of HoloNet News.
