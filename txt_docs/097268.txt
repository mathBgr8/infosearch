Aubendo
622

Aubendo was one of the Twin Cities of the planet Radnor, the other being Tacto. When Radnor faced a bioplague in the years leading up to the Clone Wars, Aubendo was in one of the many areas affected by it. Aubendo was in the Isolation Sector, divided from its sister city's Clear Sector by an energy gate. *Jedi Quest: The Way of the Apprentice Category:Cities Category:Radnor locations
