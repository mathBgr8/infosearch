Serving as a Galactic Republic Ordnance/Regional Depot outpost during the Pius Dea Era, millennia later it loosened its immigration restrictions for people displaced by the Separatist Crisis. Eventually the planet became a part of the New Republic. Ord Varee was a planet situated in the Ord Varee system, which was a part of the Belshar sector in the New Territories portion of the Mid Rim. It lay between the Belshar Othacuu and Kalaan systems on the Namadii Corridor hyperlane. During the Pius Dea Era, a period of time that lasted between approximately 12,000 BBY and 11,000 BBY, Ord Varee was an Ordnance/Regional Depot outpost of the Galactic Republic. It was located outside the Republic's borders, within what was at the time considered Wild Space. The Refugee Resettlement Coalition, a group of planets willing to resettle on their surface galactic citizens displaced by the then-ongoing Separatist Crisis, was formed around 13:3. Ord Varee became a member of the coalition and was one of the group's ten Mid Rim worlds to loosen their immigration restrictions for said people, with that fact being noted in the 13:4:18 issue of the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. Ord Varee was a part of the New Republic between 12 ABY and 13 ABY. According to official census records, in 22 BBY is set on the date "13:4:18," which corresponds to 22 BBY per the reasoning here. Ord Varee's population numbered 3.1 billion. Ord Varee was introduced in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #52, an issue of the representation of the in-universe HoloNet News that was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published by April 18, 2002.
