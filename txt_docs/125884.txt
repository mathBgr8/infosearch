Tempest Scout 5
1698

Tempest Scout 5 was an All Terrain Scout Transport in Tempest Force. It used experimental command and control software to coordinate combat data. It participated in the Battle of Endor, where it was destroyed along with the rest of the AT-ST's, apart from Tempest Scout 2. Category:All Terrain Scout Transports
