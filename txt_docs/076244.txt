Angela Phillips
842

Angela Phillips, also known as Angela P. Wade, is a writer, costume maker and doll artist. Angela lives in Newport News, Virginia. Angela married John Wade on July 24, 1999. She gave birth to Samuel J. Wade on August 18, 2003 and Elizabeth B. Wade on February 14, 2006. *Cloak of Obscurity *Queen Isabeau's Book *The Mermaid and The Monster Category:Short story authors
