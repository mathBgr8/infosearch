Sometime between the battle of Bal'demnic and the invasion of Orto, the Separatists attacked the planet with a fleet of Providence-class carrier/destroyers. The Separatists then invaded the planet with B2 super battle droids and OG-9 homing spider droids. The battle ended when countless droids surrounded just a handful of clone troopers, forcing them to surrender. This battle first appeared in the twenty-second episode of the animated television series Star Wars: Clone Wars, which originally aired on Cartoon Network on March 22, 2005.
