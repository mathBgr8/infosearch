Memory wall
1002

The memory wall was a huge wall located near the New Republic naval base on the capital world of Coruscant. This wall bore the names, etched in stone, of the brave individuals of all services who died during the Galactic Civil War. This edifice served as a constant reminder to civilians and members of the New Republic Defense Force alike of what those who came before them gave up in order to achieve the freedom the galaxy possessed after the fall of the Galactic Empire. The description of the wall is reminiscent of the real-world Vietnam Veterans Memorial located in Washington, D.C. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant monuments
