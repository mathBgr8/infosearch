Skyflipper
709

The Skyflipper was a small transport craft that was produced by the Sandtek Corporation, based on Naboo. It was designed to carry a pilot and one passenger across short distances. Ric Olié once flew a Skyflipper during his youth, and crashed with it. He was not discouraged by a near-fatal incident&mdash;which would be normal for most pilot novices&mdash;but instead built a new Skyflipper and continued his career. This accident contributed to other pilots nicknaming him "Reckless Ric." *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Atmospheric repulsorcraft models
