He was later based out of the city of Narmle on Naboo's moon of Rori. Captain Thrawn once sent a spacer to deliver some holodisks taken from the black box of a downed shuttle to Winthazen. Geg Winthazen was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Geg Winthazen was originally part of the initial version of the Imperial themepark in which Thrawn sent players to locate Winthazen near the Emperor's Retreat on Naboo. However, in the revamped "Imperial Theme Park, released with the "Chapter 6.12" update, on September 13, 2007, Winthazen was placed in Narmle on Rori.
