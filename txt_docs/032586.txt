Nosaurian flash
907

thumb|right|Bomo Greenbark emits a flash A flash was a physical ability of the Nosaurians, a species native to New Plympto. The flash was a brilliantly bright emission of phosphorescent light from Nosaurians' mouths. The ability was used as a method of communication and signalling among Nosaurian warriors. Bomo Greenbark used his flash ability to contact his comrades on New Plympto soon after encountering Jedi Master Dass Jennir. *Republic 79 Category:Biological phenomena Category:Nosaurian culture
