Verpine fiber mesh
1223

thumb|150px|Verpine Fiber Mesh in use Verpine fiber mesh was a type of armor made of Verpine fiber by the Verpine species around the time of the Jedi Civil War. During the war, the interstellar pirate Raxsus Nuli was tracked down and captured by the Jedi, who seized his many holdings.
