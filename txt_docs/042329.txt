Wumdi
2686

Wumdi was an Etti Vigo of Black Sun during Prince Xizor's reign. In the past the Pike Sisters worked for Wumdi as assassins, until they changed their name and became freelancer mercenaries.Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 3 Wumdi was present in the Black Sun meeting of 3 ABY where Guri killed Green, as a message to any Vigos who would try to sell information.Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel After Xizor's death in the Battle over Coruscant, Wumdi was present in a Vigo council about the future of Black Sun. He and Clezo expressed their distate when Lonay behaved as he was the head of the council, and he refused to take orders from a Twi'lek; he seemed to like a mysterious attractive human woman, and reminisced of the Pike sisters. During a later meeting, the Heroes of Yavin showed up after the human female's invitation. Then the room was invaded by thugs, who were quickly slaughtered. The human female identified the bodies as Vekker's soldiers; nobody knew that the mysterious human who was actually Savan in disguise, and the one responsible for the attack to incriminate Vekker.
