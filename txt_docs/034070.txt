Grandfather's Shoulder
955

Grandfather's Shoulder was a white-tipped active volcano on the Korunnal Highland of the planet Haruun Kal, near the capital city of Pelek Baw. Its snowmelt fed the Grandmother's Tears river. The tallest mountain on the Highland, it had dozens of active calderas. *Shatterpoint Category:Haruun Kal locations Category:Volcanoes
