The planet was located in the Betha system in Corva sector, at the midpoint of the Daranc Run. It was originally a more hospitable world with more atmospheric moisture, colonized by the Republic wherein it became a thriving port world. Because the planet dried up, it was abandoned. Betha II was resettled by smugglers millennia later, about 20 BBY. Since its surface was often swept dry by dust storms or sandstorms, the caves and canyons of the planet were expanded and connected with tunnels for subterranean habitation by the smugglers. The planet was discovered by New Republic Intelligence to be infiltrated by the Kaarenth Dissension.
