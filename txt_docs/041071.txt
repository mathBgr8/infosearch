Kalaanite
978

The Kalaanites were native to the planet of Kalaan. Red-skinned humanoids with large red eyes and squarish snouts, they spoke the Kalaanite language. When their lush planet, which had served as a Rebel base, was conquered by the Empire, many Kalaanites joined the Kalaanite resistance movement. *Star Wars: Force Commander Category:Mid Rim sentient species
