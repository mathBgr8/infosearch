Zier
1548

Zier was a member of the Besadii Hutt clan and was one of Durga Besadii Tai's primary rivals for clan leader in the wake of Aruk Besadii Aora's death. He was also Durga's cousin. When Black Sun destroyed all opposition to Durga's leadership, Durga made Zier his lieutenant in an effort to keep his enemies as close as possible.
