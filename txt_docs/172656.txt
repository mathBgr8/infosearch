thumb|right|250px|Hellcat 3 under fire from a [[TIE/d "Defender" Multi-Role Starfighter/Legends|TIE Defender.]] Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttles Hellcat served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin deployed Shuttles Hellcat during Treachery at Ottega. Hellcat flanked Escort Shuttles Haven as Zaarin's forces attempted to kidnap Emperor Palpatine.
