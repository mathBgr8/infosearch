Millennium Falcon'Legacy of the Force: Tempest |species=Noghri |gender=Female |pronouns=new |height=1.5 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Black |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*New Republic *Galactic Federation of Free Alliances |masters= |apprentices= }} Meewalh was one of Leia Organa Solo's personal Noghri bodyguards, one of the few in service to the Princess to survive the Yuuzhan Vong War. She and Cakhmaim worked together from 27 ABY to 40 ABY, successfully protecting the Solo family after the fall of Coruscant during their excursions across the galaxy, as well as at key engagements at Ebaq 9 and Yuuzhan'tar. During the Dark Nest Crisis, Meewalh successfully defended the Millennium Falcon from two Fefze assassins. By this time, she and Cakhmaim were also extremely reliable as gunners for the Falcon's quad laser cannons. Meewalh met her end during the Second Galactic Civil War during the attempted coup of Queen Mother Tenel Ka at Hapes. Believing his parents to be responsible for betraying the Galactic Alliance and for taking part in the assassination plot against Tenel Ka, Colonel Jacen Solo ordered his best gunnery team aboard the Anakin Solo to destroy the Falcon. As the Falcon moved in to rescue Ben Skywalker, Jaina Solo, Zekk, and a Twi'lek Galactic Alliance Guard soldier named Sorzo, it was nearly ripped apart by the turbolaser blasts.
