Zian Finnis
1305

Zian Finnis was a Human male Officer who served the Confederacy of Independent Systems and held Mount Corvast after Sev'rance Tann's capture during the Clone Wars. Echuu Shen-Jon's forces challenged him during the Second Battle of Sarapin, during which he lost the citadel to the Galactic Republic.
