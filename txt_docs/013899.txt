The planet joined the Galactic Republic prior to 45 BBY. Emmerian walker vehicles inspired those built by Kuat Drive Yards, such as the AT-AT. By 4 ABY, the planet had become an Imperial fortress world. Following the Battle of Endor, New Republic-aligned Mon Calamari were able to defeat Imperial forces there. However, Warlord Zsinj later took control of the planet.
