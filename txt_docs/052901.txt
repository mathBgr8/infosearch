Yref Orgege
1682

Yref Orgege was an Imperial Moff who was given control of the Hutt Space region after Moff Sarn Shild committed suicide. Orgege tried to fix the mistakes of his predecessor, namely the failed military invasion of Nar Shaddaa, by keeping his forces in tighter control. The Imperial fleet under his command could never act on its own without first checking in and receiving his okay.
