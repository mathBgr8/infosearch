Igpek Droon
2019

Igpek Droon was a male Human small-time trader raised by Agro-Militants. Droon had a strong dislike of droids or other sentient beings which acted as subordinates when they worked for him. Due to this, he later spent a fortune having the programming of his droids changed so they would call him friend or comrade.
