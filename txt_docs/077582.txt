Mon Calamari heavy carrier
2328

The Mon Calamari heavy carrier was a warship developed by the Mon Calamari for use by the Galactic Alliance Defense Force. One warship of the class, Blue Diver, was attached to the Galactic Alliance Second Fleet. It was part of a task force of Second Fleet warships dispatched to Corellia in 40 ABY during a growing crisis between the Alliance and the Corellian government. It carried several fighter squadrons and was equipped with turbolasers and ion cannons.
