Imperial Plaza Hotel
837

The Imperial Plaza Hotel was located in Montrol City on Gandle Ott. In addition to its rooms, bars, and restaurants, the hotel also contained a casino. It played host to the New Republic delegation from the FarStar, who stayed there at the government's expense. During their stay, several droids working in the hotel had their programming modified by Cuthbert, causing them to slip drugs into the food and drink served to the crew of the FarStar. *The DarkStryder Campaign Category:Gandle Ott locations Category:Hotels
