Snivvian (language)
3707

Snivvian was the native language of the Snivvian species from Cadomai Prime. Most Snivvians were fluent in both Snivvian and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language, and they had a nasal accent when speaking Basic.Ultimate Alien Anthology, see Snivvian entry Snivvians were fascinated by galactic culture, and thus were happy to speak Basic with non-Snivvians. However, there were some Snivvians who refused to use Basic because of their personal philosophy.
