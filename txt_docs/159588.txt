Pellaeon Gardens
2800

The Pellaeon Gardens were the personal gardens of Grand Admiral Gilad Pellaeon, located in the Imperial Headquarters on Bastion. He enjoyed trimming the plants and keeping them orderly, using them as a euphemism for the New Order. However, following the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War, he decided to return to his garden and allow a small measure of anarchy to enter into it.The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force The Pellaeon Gardens were officially dedicated in 42 ABY, on what would have been Pellaeon's ninety-third birthday. Natasi Daala gave a speech at the ceremony. By 137 ABY, the Gardens still existed, and were taken care of in honor of Pellaeon. They were visited by the deposed Emperor Roan Fel, where he would meditate.
