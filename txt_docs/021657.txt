Team Tralus
1990

|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Cup of Limmie }} Team Tralus was a limmie team that played for the planetThe Essential Guide to Planets and Moons of Tralus in the Galactic Cup of Limmie. In 13:3, during the Separatist Crisis, they were defeated in an upset by the planet of Ralltiir's team, with a single score in the Cup's opening round. The result was then reported in an article in the CoCo District edition of the HoloNet News. Team Tralus was mentioned in Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
