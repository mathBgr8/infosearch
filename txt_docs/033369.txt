Laeca Valorum
1762

Laeca Valorum was a politician and member of House Valorum. The Valorums were a highly regarded political dynasty that stretched back to over 1000 years before the Battle of Yavin. Lists of the Valorum lineage that included Tarsus, Tullius, and Laeca Valorum sounded like an induction list for the Hall of Heroes, a structure on the planet Coruscant that honored heroes of the Galactic Empire.
