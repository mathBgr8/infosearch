Galactic Criminals Act
594

The Galactic Criminals Act was an act enacted by the government of Telos IV. The law enabled Telosian security forces to apprehend suspected criminals and hold them to a trial. Xanatos utilized the legislature to arrest his former Master, the Jedi Qui-Gon Jinn, and his Padawan, Obi-Wan Kenobi. *Jedi Apprentice: The Day of Reckoning *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Laws
