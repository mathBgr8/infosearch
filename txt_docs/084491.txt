Nevil Tritum
1565

Sir Nevil Tritum was a Human male knight of the Tapani sector and the most skilled agent of the Mecrosa Order. During the Great Jedi Purge, Tritum was responsible for infiltrating House Pelagia's holocron library and bringing the Holocron of King Adas back into Sith hands.
