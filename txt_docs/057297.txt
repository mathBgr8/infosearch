Gordoon
715

Gordoon was a group of three Imperial BFF-1 bulk freighters. Two of them carried ore, the third carried gold and ore. They were present in the asteroid field during the raid of the Imperial convoy that involved the Stormtrooper transport Raider that was attempting to steal food from the convoy and were likely destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit Category:Imperial Navy units
