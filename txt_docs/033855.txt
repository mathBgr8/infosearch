Jedi Cruiser
1831

Jedi Cruisers were transports used by the Jedi Order during the Freedon Nadd Uprising. Two Jedi Cruisers were sent from Ossus to assist Ulic Qel-Droma and the Galactic Republic on Onderon during the Uprising. They landed under heavy fire on the planet and successfully unloaded their passengers.
