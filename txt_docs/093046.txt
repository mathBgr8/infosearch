|intl release= |pages= |issue=11 |featured= |series=The Official Star Wars Fact File }} The Official Star Wars Fact File 11 was the eleventh issue of The Official Star Wars Fact File, released on March 13, 2002. The issue was authored by Darren Allanson, Andrew Darling, Jim Swallow, Chris Gardner, and Iain Lowson. It also featured original artwork by Carl Lyons. *The Battle of Endor **The Emperor's trap on the Forest Moon was carefully prepared. *Yoda **The wise Jedi Master who sat on the Council. *Garindan **The mysterious Kubaz informer who haunted the streets of Mos Eisley *Planets of the Inner Rim **The planets close to the Core were once at the edge of known space. *R3 & R4 Astromech Droids **Two of the important series of droids used by most starships. *Gungan Weapons **Organic technology created the weapons used by the Gungans of Naboo. *Imperial Star Destroyer **Mighty vessel of war that symbolized the Empire's Dominance of space.
