Tarkazza
1636

Tarkazza (meaning "mist ghost" in ShyriiwookGalactic Campaign Guide) was a male Wookiee, with a silver stripe down his back, who was the father of Katarra. He once took Han Solo beneath Rwookrrorro by placing him in a quulaar on his back. Tarkazza was one of the Wookiees who spearheaded the search for the Gran thief Kea Ra-Lan after she infiltrated Kashyyyk and stole ancient texts from the vaults of Neocomora. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds *Rebel Dawn *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:Kashyyyk residents Category:Males Category:New Republic individuals Category:Wookiees
