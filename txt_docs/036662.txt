Harmzuay
2471

The Harmzuay was a modified Kaloth-style battlecruiser that saw extensive service with the Thalassian Slavers. The battlecruiser was modified enough that it could fight any EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate to a standstill. Modifications included adding turbolasers and deck fighters, and increasing the armor thickness. The ship and its efficient crew made a strong argument to their future victims against resisting a Thalassian raid. The ship could also support at least two squadrons of Z-95 Headhunters. In 11 ABY during an attempt by the Thalassians to raid the planet of Kerilt, the Harmzuay was accosted by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Invidious.
