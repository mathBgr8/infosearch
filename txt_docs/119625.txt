Trynic
2259

Trynic was the Devaronian male pilot and owner of the modified YT-1300 light freighter Solar Flare. Hailing from the planet Devaron, Trynic was widely regarded as the best tramp freighter captain in the Minos Cluster. Known as a shrewd bargainer with a quick mind, Trynic usually came out ahead on any deal he made. He has managed to turn a tidy profit with the Solar Flare, reinvesting his profits to earn even more.
