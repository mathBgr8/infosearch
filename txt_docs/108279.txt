Edge-9
1181

Edge-9 was an anarchist organization that existed on Coruscant. It called for the dismantlement of Coruscant's government and the abolition of the gentry. It was known for its violent acts of political protest, though not all members explicitly supported violence. Edge-9's acts included bombings of police and utility stations. Anarchists from Edge-9 at one point plotted to destroy the Senate Building, sneaking explosives through the pipeline from the polar caps, but the plot was foiled. Reina March was a head lieutenant in the organization who planned and participated in violent acts.
