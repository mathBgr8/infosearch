Reytha Minor
1576

*Galactic Empire }} Reytha Minor was a city on the agricultural planet of Reytha. In 0 ABY, it was captured by the Rebel Alliance, which established a stronghold inside the city walls. Darth Vader subsequently led an Imperial strike force during the Reytha campaign to retake the city and raze the Rebel base. Reytha Minor appears during the Darth Vader campaign in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
