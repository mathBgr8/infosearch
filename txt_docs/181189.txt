Deck 92

Deck 92 was the 92nd level of the first Death Star. It contained storage areas and a recycling station at Sector N-One. Celot Ratua Dil frequented this area when he first got on board the station. It was destroyed along with the rest of the Death Star during the Battle of Yavin in 0 BBY. *Death Star Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station decks
