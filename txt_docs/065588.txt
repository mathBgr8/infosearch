Tatooni Booka
832

Tatooni Booka was a shop in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. Among their wares were new and used data storage units, recorded music, and viewplates. It also had an indoor reading area and café. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Dining establishments Category:Mos Eisley locations Category:Vendors and sellers
