Ben Hu'al
1689

Ben Hu'al was a male Sith student at the Sith Academy on Korriban during the Jedi Civil War. During his training at the academy, Ben was accepted into the academy and learn the arts of the dark side, however, he was considered weak and an idiot by his fellow students. Hu'al believed he wouldn't be talented as a true Sith until Master Uthar Wynn instructed him and a group of acolytes to recapture a Mark VII experimental prototype assassin droid, which had holed itself up in the tomb of Marka Ragnos in the Valley of the Dark Lords.
