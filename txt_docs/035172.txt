|published in=Del Rey website |reprinted in= |pages=1 |timeline=21 ABY |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Judge's Call" is a short story written by Timothy Zahn for Valentine's Day of 2004 and was made available for free online at the Del Rey website. It is set 21 ABY and details the second honeymoon of Luke Skywalker and Mara Jade. Luke and his wife Mara are on a planet, where Luke is helping to arbitrate many disputes among the natives, who revere Jedi wisdom. Mara grows increasingly frustrated at the amount of Luke's time being taken by settling these disputes.
