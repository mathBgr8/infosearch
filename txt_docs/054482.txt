Attack on a Tatooine Imperial base
2549

With the Imperial outpost in the Vergesso Asteroids fallen, the attack on a Tatooine Imperial base started when the Alliance fleet moved from the Vergesso asteroids to Tatooine. Alliance Commandos infiltrated an Imperial base, took down the shields, and allowed bombing runs to take place, but suffered several casualties at the hands of Boba Fett, who was hired by the Empire to guard the base at the time. After the shields were brought down, Y-Wings were used to destroy the Imperial base. Then GR-75 transports landed near by and dispatched Rebel soldiers who then moved into the area to clean up any heavy resistance. Boba Fett escaped however. Many stormtroopers were taken captive and their base destroyed. *Star Wars: Empire at War Category:Battles of Tatooine Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
