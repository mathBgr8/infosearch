Supply lift

thumb|right|200px|A supply lift being inspected by [[Chief/Legends|Chief Chirpa and Shaman Logray]] }} A supply lift was a device developed by the Shaman Logray on the Forest Moon of Endor to lift supplies of food from the forest floor up to the heights of Bright Tree Village. The lift was essentially a platform with a pulley attached. The device first came into use during the stranglethorn crisis where it was destroyed by a rampaging stranglethorn. *LEGO Star Wars: The Video Game Category:Lifts
