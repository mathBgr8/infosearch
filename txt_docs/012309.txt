He discovered the infant Shistavanen Voolvif Monn abandoned on a world near the Arah asteroid belt. Rahdool took Monn as his Padawan and trained him to hone his tracking abilities and hunter instincts with his innate Force skills to become a fearsome opponent in combat. By the time of the Clone Wars, Monn also became a Jedi Master.
