AQ-1 Submersi-speeder
1213

The SoroSuub AQ-1 Submersi-speeder was an amphibious speeder, both an elegant watercraft and stylish downtown landspeeder. The model was especially popular on Muunilinst, where frequent travel was required to underwater facilities, such as vault-spires. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Aquatic repulsorcraft models Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
