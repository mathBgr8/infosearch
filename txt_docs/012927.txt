Bonnie Burton
4884

Bonnie Burton is a former Content Developer for Lucas Online, StarWars.com Senior Editor, staff writer for Star Wars Insider magazine, and lead writer for The Official Star Wars Blog. Outside of the Star Wars world, Burton is the founder of Grrl.com and has written for such magazines as AuctionRover, BUST, Digit, Geek Monthly, Organic Gardening, Wired, Yahoo! Internet Life, and a myriad of zines. Bonnie Burton is a former writer for The Official Star Wars Blog. She used the handle "bonniegrrl" on those and other sites. At StarWars.com, Burton maintained the kids section that includes drawing tutorials, original crafts and fun educational articles, and she created the Star Wars Rocks section where she interviewed bands, actors, authors, and celebrities about their love for the films. On May 7, 2012, Burton tweeted that Lucasfilm had laid her off. Burton's "marriage" to R2-D2 at Celebration V earned considerable attention and was featured in a video on the Official Site. In a ceremony at the Celebration Chapel, Darth Maul presided, Darth Vader was R2-D2's best man, model and Star Wars fan Adrienne Curry was a bridesmaid, and Lucasfilm's Stephen J. Sansweet gave Burton away. The two have an "open-source" marriage, and according to Burton, "Exchanging restraining bolts is much more romantic than exchanging rings."
