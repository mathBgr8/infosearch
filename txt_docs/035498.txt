Aarwynn

Aarwynn was a huge humbaba and was known as the Kashoonara legend as the steed of the deity Hurrungat. The legend said that Aarwynn was "six Kashoonara high" at the shoulder, which was much larger than the real humbaba.Creatures of the Galaxy *Creatures of the Galaxy Category:Mythological creatures
