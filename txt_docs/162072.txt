Nest Leader
1125

Nest Leader was a position of leadership among the ArconaGalaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition and the Geelan. On Cona, the elected leader of a Grand Nest held the title of Nest Leader. The Nest Leader conducted meetings and handled city affairs, much like a mayor.
