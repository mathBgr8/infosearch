Vortia Kwis
1188

Vortia Kwis was the captain of the Pure Sabacc. Kwis agreed to ferry refugees from Dubrillion to Dantooine shortly after the Battle of Dubrillion. After fighting their way out of a Yuuzhan Vong sneak attack at Dantooine, Kwis lost faith in the New Republic and joined the Peace Brigade, adapting Pure Sabacc into a traveling recruitment center. *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Peace Brigaders Category:Spacers
