Fivvic
1315

Fivvic was a Barabel officer with the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars and the Task Force Commander for the survey team assigned to scout the planet Dagro. Like the rest of his species, Fivvic was highly respectful of Jedi Knights. That respect, however, didn't transfer as much to their Padawans. Fivvic later participated in the rescue of Obi-Wan Kenobi on Dagro. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Barabel Category:Galactic Republic military officers Category:Males
