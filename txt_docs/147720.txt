|published in=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 10 |reprinted in= |pages=4 |timeline=0 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Sandbound on Tatooine" was a short story written for Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 10. It was written by Peter M. Schweighofer and illustrated by Jeff Rebner. Platt Okeefe, the infamous smuggler, had crashed on Tatooine, and encountered a number of local wildlife while escaping from the Dune Sea. Having escaped bounty hunters, smuggler Platt Okeefe crashes her starship, Pok's Demise, into the Dune Sea on Tatooine.
