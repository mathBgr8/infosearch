Galney fleet

The Galney fleet was a fleet of Hapan Battle Dragons and Nova-class battle cruisers controlled by the Galney family. In 40 ABY, it was sent to reinforce the Heritage Fleet at the Battle of Hapes, but was destroyed by Galactic Alliance reinforcements under the command of Admiral Nek Bwua'tu. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest Category:Confederation fleets Category:Hapan fleets
