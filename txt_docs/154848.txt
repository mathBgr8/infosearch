Ravelev
1676

The Ravelev was a tramp freighter captained by Kassler, that plied the Anarid Cluster. Among its crew was Platt Okeefe who served as a technician for two years. She left the crew on Boztrok in order to join the Klatooinian Trade Guild, and earn enough to buy her own freighter.
