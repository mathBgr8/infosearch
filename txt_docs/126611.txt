Grk'Urr'Akk
1432

Grk'Urr'Akk was a Tusken Raider who was active during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he commenced multiple raids on Gardulla Besadii the Elder's outposts. For this, the Hutt placed a bounty on his head of 3,000 credits alive and 1,500 dead. Jango Fett, a bounty hunter, claimed this bounty.
