Toby Longworth
1818

|companies= |website= }} Toby Longworth is a British actor who worked on Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. Longworth voiced two characters for the film: Senator Lott Dod (body-doubled by Silas Carson) and the Swokes Swokes gorgmonger Gragra. Although Senator Dod reappeared in further Star Wars media, Toby Longworth was not re-cast.Queen's Shadow audiobook Category:Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace voice actors
