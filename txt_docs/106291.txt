Plasto-Cast
1095

Plasto-Cast was a medical product created by SyntheCure. It was a quick drying gel that helped immobilize broken limbs. The plasti-coating was usually applied by a spray-on canister. After the gel was sprayed onto the affected area it could be molded into a workable cast. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Far Orbit Project Category:Splints and casts Category:SyntheCure products
