Droo (starship)
1066

The Droo was a CR90 corvette in service to the Rebel Alliance. It was assigned to the Airam sector. When the decision was made to construct a Rebel shipyard in Airam space, the Droo was assigned to the convoy which would bring the needed supplies and personnel. The Droo was transporting troops to be used as station security and orbital defense. Unfortunately for the Alliance, the Galactic Empire learned of the planned Rebel shipyard, and it ambushed the construction convoy in the Rocrin system.
