An area of the planet was mountainous. thumb|right|250px|Darryn Thyte fought at Vaenrood, where he suffered injuries in battle. During the Galactic Civil War, several months before their campaign to oust warlord Moff Kentor Sarne from his capital on the planet Kal'Shebbol in the Kathol sector, the New Republic battled Imperial forces on Vaenrood. New Republic ground forces and T-65 X-wing starfighters were deployed to the planet; one of the X-wings was flown by Lieutenant Darryn Thyte. During the battle, New Republic ground troops covered by X-wing air support engaged Imperial units supported by artillery in a mountainous area. As the engagement unfolded, Lieutenant Thyte's X-wing was clipped by artillery fire, causing the starfighter to crash. Thyte survived the impact, but the canopy of his X-wing buckled and snapped, shearing off most of his right arm above the elbow. Medics replaced the arm with a clunky cybernetic prosthesis, which was the best available at the time. Following the crash, Thyte was removed from active flight duty. Vaenrood was first mentioned in the short story "The Saga Begins" by Timothy Zahn, published in The DarkStryder Campaign by West End Games in 1995.
