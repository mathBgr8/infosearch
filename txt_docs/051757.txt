|feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} "One-Arm" was the appellation given to the female wampa that Luke Skywalker encountered in the ice cave on Hoth in 3 ABY. A female wampa resided in the ice cave on her native world Hoth in which Luke Skywalker was taken to after being attacked whilst mounted atop a tauntaun by a male wampa. When Skywalker awoke, he was dangling from the ceiling of the cave. Attempting to gain his bearings, Luke surveyed the cave and noticed the wampa was eating the remains of a tauntaun. Luke used the Force to pull his lightsaber from the ice and cut himself free, and escaped with a facial scar and bruises. In the process, the wampa lost her arm when Skywalker used his weapon to fend it off. The wampa later became head of a band of other wampas, organizing them into a group capable of coordinating attacks against the hunters who arrived on the world. She directed the attacks from an elevated position and howled orders to target the strongest of the hunters first. They even sabotaged their ship by ripping out vital components. When Skywalker returned to Hoth in 12 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Darksaber to 12 ABY. with Callista Ming, they sealed themselves in Echo Base with the surviving hunters. The wampas struck the door with rocks to distract them and mask the sound of other wampas tunneling into the base. When Luke and Callista ran outside after the survivors were massacred, they ran into One-Arm and she recognized him as her nemesis. Instead of attacking Luke, One-Arm locked eyes with him and lunged for Callista. Before she could maul Luke's mate, Luke cut the wampa in half with his lightsaber.
