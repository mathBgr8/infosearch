Ancients
1565

The Ancients were worshiped as deities by the age-revering Klatooinian people. From the earliest period in their recorded history, the Klatooinians believed in the idea of strength through age, as exemplified by the Fountain of Ancients. This spectacular formation was considered a gift of the Ancients, inspiring them to tenacity, patience, and respect for tradition.Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies After the Hutts made contact with the Klatooinians and signed them into servitude, the long-lived gastropods were identified with the Ancients.
