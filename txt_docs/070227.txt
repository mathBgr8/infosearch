Rodian bouncers
1012

thumb|right|250px|A pair of tough brutes, the [[Rodian/Legends|Rodian bouncers were.]] The Rodian bouncers worked in Baruk's Bar. When Kyle Katarn entered the bar, one of the Rodians told him to hand over his weapons, promising to give them back when he would leave. However, they, along with everyone else in the bar, attacked Katarn as part of a trap their conspecific boss, Reelo Baruk, had laid for him. Along with everyone else in the bar&mdash;apart from the bartender&mdash;they wound up dead. The Rodian bouncers are canonical, appearing in Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast, in which they have some dialogue with the player. However, they have yet to be named. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast Category:Criminals
