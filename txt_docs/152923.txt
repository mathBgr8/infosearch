Anniha Nega
2992

Anniha Nega was a Human male who became one Coruscant's premier bounty hunters by his early twenties. Active during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, Anniha Nega was a Human male soldier in his early twenties. He was also one of the top bounty hunters and assassins operating out of the planet Coruscant. Though he built a top-notch reputation in spite of his relative youth, Nega did not do many of the tasks on his falsified Bounty Hunters' Guild kill list, a fact concealed from his employers. Even so, he found no shortage of work, being used by crime lords Volven Roxe and Quetemoor the Sub-Elder, and politicians in Coruscant's Senate District.Coruscant and the Core Worlds Though his kill list claimed things he did not do, Nega believed that his legitimate kills would soon outnumber his fake ones. Nega's talent for tracking was almost primal. A bounty hunter, he was proficient in the use of blaster pistols and rifles, and heavy, simple and vibro weapons. The bounty hunter wore combat gloves and Ithullian war armor under a woven desert poncho to preserve his mysterious identity. Weapons used by Nega included an ion pistol, a blaster carbine, and a force pike.
