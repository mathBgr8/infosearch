|narrator= |producer=Jymn Magon |director= |cover artist= |illustrator= |publisher=Buena Vista Records |release date=1983 |rereleased= |media type=*Vinyl LP record (2104) *Cassette tape (2104B) |run time=30 minutes |discs= |cassettes=1 |pages= |isbn=none |timeline=3 ABY |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Rebel Mission to Ord Mantell, subtitled A Story from The Star Wars Saga, is an audio drama taking place just before Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. The script was written by Brian Daley, the author of the Star Wars radio plays; it was released by Buena Vista Records with the producer and cast from the Read-Along Adventures. The drama was released in 1983 on both record album and cassette tape. The running time is approximately thirty minutes. The story concerns a mission to Ord Mantell, where Han Solo, Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia Organa and their companions have a run-in with a bounty hunter. A STORY FROM THE STAR WARS SAGA REBEL MISSION TO ORD MANTELL The Rebel Alliance has just destroyed the dreaded Death Star&mdash;but their fight against the evil Galactic Empire is far from over. Forced to set up a new base on the inhospitable ice world of Hoth, the Rebels must divert the Empire's attention from the move. Han Solo and Luke Skywalker are sent out as decoys against what they think is a lone Imperial Star Destroyer. Unfortunately, the Rebel attack analysts haven't given our heroes all the facts! And without money, the Rebellion cannot continue its fight for freedom. Princess Leia and her Rebel team attempt a daring heist of Imperial funds&mdash;unaware of the treachery that awaits them. It is a time for daring, clever droids and a lightning fast trigger finger. Join Han, Luke, Leia, Chewbacca, R2-D2 and C-3PO on their REBEL MISSION TO ORD MANTELL! }} Near the beginning of The Empire Strikes Back, Han Solo mentions an encounter with a bounty hunter on Ord Mantell. The rebel mission in this audio drama, in which Solo and Organa are nearly captured by the bounty hunter Cypher Bos, is meant to fill in the details of that incident. Other Legends stories have Solo running into different bounty hunters on Ord Mantell during the same time period. "The Bounty Hunter of Ord Mantell," by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson, had already been published in 1981 as a newspaper comic and featured the bounty hunter Skorr. In Scoundrel's Luck, a solo adventure by West End Games published in 1990, Han encounters Alfreda Goot instead.
