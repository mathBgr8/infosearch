Feeven
1573

Feeven was a male Rodian passenger aboard Outbound Flight. His quarters were situated on the titanic ship's Dreadnaught-Two. Feeven greatly disliked the Jedi, who, by all appearances, were becoming increasingly more controlling as the Unknown Regions expedition continued. Feeven specifically loathed the fact that, in the middle of the 'night,' the Jedi had come to seize from a family's care their Force-sensitive child.
