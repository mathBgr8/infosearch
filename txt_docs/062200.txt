The planet was orbited by at least two moons. The second moon was home to a Rebel Alliance outpost during the Galactic Civil War, a large conflict between the Alliance and the Galactic Empire. At some point during the conflict, the base personnel became infected with the Direllian Plague, a fatal disease that could only be cured with the administration of Shiarha Root, a rare plant found on the world of Kirtania. Tiragga was first mentioned in the roleplaying game adventure The Quality of Mercy, written by Nicky Rea and published in February 1994 within the first issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal. In the adventure, players journey to Kirtania in an attempt to acquire a cure for the Direllian Plague that is killing the Rebel base's personnel.
