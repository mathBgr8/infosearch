Mark IV fuel cell
659

The Mark IV fuel cell was a power cell used in Imperial and commercial craft. 75 Mark IV fuel cells were installed in standard models of the Starfeld ZH-25 Questor giving it the ability to last for 2.9 standard months between refueling. This suggests that about 26 Mark IV fuel cells would be required to keep a ship like the ZH-25 Questor going for one standard month. *Stock Ships Mark IV fuel cell
