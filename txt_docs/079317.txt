Chromostring
1443

Chromostring was a drug, patented by BioTech Industries,Cracken's Rebel Field Guide that allowed for deeper penetration of medicines into a patient's body without causing nerve damage.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Dusque Mistflier used chromostring to treat a wound that Finn Darktrin had suffered.
