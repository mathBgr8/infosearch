Alongside its outer neighbor Brenu, Huip was classified as a sungrazer planetoid and had no moons. Huip was first mentioned in "Return to Prosperity," the first entry in the Planet Hoppers article series Beheboth: Blood and Water. The article was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on June 3, 2004. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Beheboth system, and therefore Huip, in grid square O-17.
