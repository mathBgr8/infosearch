thumb|right|150px|Artwork by Dave Gibbons Dave Gibbons provided the cover art and all illustrations for the Vader's Quest series of comics as well as Chewbacca (2000) 2. He also provided artwork for Star Wars Galaxy magazine. He is most well known for having illustrated Watchmen (written by Alan Moore).
