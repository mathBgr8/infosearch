Old District
839

The Old District was more commonly known as the "depths of Coruscant" or "the underworld". It was a haven for the homeless and fugitives on Coruscant. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, it was also a haven to the Shamed Ones of the Yuuzhan Vong.
