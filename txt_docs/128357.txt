Robber Baron
1728

The Robber Baron was a Star Galleon-class frigate owned by the infamous Poss'Nomin pirate, Reginald Barkbone. Barkbone stole the ship from its previous owner and equipped it with Incom corsairs. The ship was pursued by Grand Admiral Rufaan Tigellinus from the Outer Rim to the Colonies Region. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Pirate starships Category:Star Galleon-class frigates
