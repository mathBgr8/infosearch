Mazie
1775

Mazie was a female Twi'lek from Ryloth living in the final years of the Galactic Republic. Mazie and her daughter, Berri, were enslaved under the crime-lord Krayn and forced to labor in the spice-processing facilities on Nar Shaddaa. She befriended Anakin Skywalker and made it her business to relieve the discomforts of (and dangers to) her fellow slaves.
