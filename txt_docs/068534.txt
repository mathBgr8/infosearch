Iron Will
666

Iron Will was the official feed of the Commission for the Preservation of the New Order. All COMPNOR members were given a free subscription and exclusive access to breaking news reports. New members received a special welcome message from Palpatine himself. *Evasive Action: Recruitment *Imperial Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:News networks and companies
