Imperial Prefect
4030

Imperial Prefects were officials in the Galactic Empire that served as a liaison to planetary settlements. Prefect Eugene Talmont wore the insignia of an admiral. During 5 ABY, Mosh Barris worked on Garqi as an Imperial Prefect.
