Frecker
1996

Freckers were a species of small sentient rodents. Though wild and brutal, they were capable of intelligent speech. Freckers also wore clothes, denoting some manner of culture. Five years before the Battle of Yavin, Gru'um Ropple and Miramba transported a horde of Freckers to Jabba Desilijic Tiure's palace, planning to assassinate the Hutt crime lord by unleashing the Freckers to kill him during his sleep. As it happened, Bib Fortuna, Jabba's majordomo, had plans of his own; he and six co-conspirators hoped to capture Jabba alive so that they could pry out as many secrets as possible before killing him. When the Freckers were unleashed upon the court, instead of carrying out his own plan, Fortuna ordered his men to kill the Freckers. The rodents in turn killed all of Fortuna's men save for Nivek, who had been blinded by the Freckers' bites and began yelling about "the plan".
