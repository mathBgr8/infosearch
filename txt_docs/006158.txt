1249th Mobile Corps
621

The 1249th Mobile Corps was a mobile corps of the Imperial Army that operated within the Outer Rim during the Galactic Civil War. During the battle for control of the Kalinda system several years after the Battle of Endor, the 1249th supported the 2391st Battle Squadron. Category:Imperial Army corps
