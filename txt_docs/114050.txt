Harramin
2179

The Harramin was an Imperial capital ship. It was seized by the Yevetha when they captured the Black-15 shipyard and other Black Sword Command military assets, during their successful revolt against Imperial rule, eight months after the Battle of Endor. Twelve years later, she served as one of the control ships from which the slave circuit system devised by Major Sil Sorannan and his escape committee was initiated, recapturing the Black Fleet for the Empire. The Harramin has sometimes been taken to be a misprint of the name of Harridan, mentioned in Before the Storm as a Victory-class Star Destroyer assigned to Black Sword Command.
