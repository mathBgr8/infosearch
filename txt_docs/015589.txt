Moridebo district
896

thumb|right|200px The Moridebo District was a poor area of Metellos, home to the Hellwell. The district was covered with tiny shacks with dirty rooftops that stood right outside of Stratablock 7. The architecture stayed the same except every once in a while the rooftops were broken by factories, stadiums, and smoking ash-dumps.
