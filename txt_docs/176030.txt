The Hunt for Han Solo additionally denotes a January 1998 release date. |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-12792-6 |issn= |genre= |issue=5 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 5 is the fifth issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in January 1998. Issue 5 included part five of the serial comic The Rebel Thief, written by Ryder Windham. Star Wars Kids 5 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book.
