|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=12 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Jedi Academy: Leviathan |issue=3 }} Jedi Academy: Leviathan 3 was the third issue in the Star Wars: Jedi Academy: Leviathan 4 part series of comics; it was published on December 16, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. Kyp Durron arrives on the mining world of Corbos only to find the entire colony destroyed. There are no signs of life but Kyp can still hear the psychic outcries of the colonists. He is drawn toward the Leviathan of Corbos, a beast that he finds has devoured the colonists&mdash;and yet trapped their minds and spirits.
