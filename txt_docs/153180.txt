Guire Sleazebaggano
907

}} Guire Sleazebaggano was the agent of hi-lo ball player Garcy Flugg. After Flugg missed fifteen matches in two years due to chronic elbow injuries, Sleazebaggano informed the HoloNet News of Flugg's decision to replace his injured arm with a mechanical one.
