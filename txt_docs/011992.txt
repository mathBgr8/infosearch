Noa-Jek Darillian
1895

Noa-Jek Darillian was a male Human Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order during the waring days of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive, Noa-Jek Darillian a Human who was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order during the Galactic Republic's final decades. Ascending to the rank of Jedi Master during his time with the Order, he dutifully reported to Coruscant when the Republic's conflict with the Confederacy of Independent Systems escalated into the Clone Wars.
