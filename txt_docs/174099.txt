Lafarn Residential District
821

thumb|Lafarn Residential District on a map The Lafarn Residential District was one of the areas of Corint City, the planetary capital of Pirik. It was the residential area of the city. It contained several non-Human communities, showcasing the melting pot of Pirik. There were also several restaurants and businesses located in the Lafarn area. It was located 14 kilometers from Uloitir Sea. Category:Districts Category:Pirik locations
