Palpatine's private viewing box
1728

thumb|250px|[[Palpatine/Legends|Palpatine's private box.]] Palpatine's private viewing box in the Galaxies Opera House on Coruscant was adjacent to Romeo Treblanc's. The viewing box was reserved for the Supreme Chancellor of the Galactic Republic and other politicians of the government, such as the Senior Administrative Aide Sly Moore and the Vice Chair Mas Amedda. Palpatine could also invite other people to sit in the viewing box, such as Senator of Sembla Sweitt Concorkill and Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker. It was there that Palpatine told Skywalker the story of Darth Plagueis, a story that would contribute to Anakin's fall to the dark side of the Force. *Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith junior novelization *The Rise and Fall of Darth Vader *Tag & Bink: Revenge of the Clone Menace *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant rooms
