Squib Polyanarchy
1485

thumb|right|The Polyanarchy was nominally headed by a king. The Squib Polyanarchy was the main governing body of Skor II and of the Squib species. It was made up of several independent communities under the nominal rule of a king.. The Squib king was Ebareebaveebeedee during the end of the Old Republic era and the beginning of the Imperial Period.Scavenger Hunt. The Squib Polyanarchy's relationship with the Squib Merchandising Consortium, a Squib-wide corporation, is not known.
