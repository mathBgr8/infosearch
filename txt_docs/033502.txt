Lightning Battalion
1394

Lightning Battalion was a special forces team of the Imperial Army fighting against the Icarii on Vestar. They wore nonstandard blue uniforms with a lightning bolt insignia. The battalion was responsible for the capture of Icarii sorceress Selestrine.
