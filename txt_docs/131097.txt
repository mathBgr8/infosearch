|subscribers=August 2007 |pages=84 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=96 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 96 is the ninety-sixth issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine and the fourth issue to be published by Titan Magazines. It was released September 4, 2007 in the US and September 6, 2007 in the UK. It is a period of civil war. Rebel spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the evil Galactic Empire.' We take an in-depth look at the rise of the Empire, featuring everything you wanted to know about the Empire but were afraid to ask!
