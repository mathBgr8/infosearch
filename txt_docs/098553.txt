Jango Fett's mother
2053

The mother of Jango Fett was the wife of the Journeyman Protector of Concord Dawn, Fett. They had two children: a daughter, Arla, and a son, Jango. The mother, along with her husband, was killed by the Death Watch. Before being killed, she saved her son's life by shooting a Death Watch soldier in the head, giving him a chance to escape.
