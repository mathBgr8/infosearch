Z-Caplure Group
934

Caplure was a group of three Imperial CR90 corvettes used by Grand Admiral Zaarin. Caplure group were assigned to destroy the Deep Space Manufacturing Facility ZA-13. However, they were destroyed by Tan Maarek Stele. The corvettes were called Z-Caplure to remind the player that the corvettes were under the command of Zaarin. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter: Defender of the Empire *TIE Fighter: Defender of the Empire: Official Secrets & Solutions *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Starships of Zaarin's forces
