Keschel
1211

Keschel was a rare mineral found on the planet Tyne's Horky. A majority of the planet's financial stability was based upon the success of Keschel miners such as Kleb Zellock, Jann Tosh and his uncle, Gundy. The value of Keschel ore was represented in the form of currency known as a Keshel. Category:Ores
