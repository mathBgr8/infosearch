Illuminated One
625

The Illuminated One was the leader of the Charon Cult of Light sometime before the Battle of Endor. The Illuminated One preached that he and his kindred needed to escape the known galaxy to escape the grasp of their doom-bringing brethren in the Charon Death Cult. The Illuminated One formulated a plan to ensure the survival of his kind after the Cult of Light discovered hyperdrive technology. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Charon culture Category:Religious titles
