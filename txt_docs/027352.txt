Cassandran choholl
1837

Cassandran choholl was an alcoholic beverage. It was a fine beverage, known for its fruity and fragrant flavor, perhaps even better regarded than Cassandran brandy. Grada brand Cassandran Choholl was considered one of the finest types of liquors in the galaxy, and sold for 500 credits a bottle or more. It was created from the leaves and fruits of the hafa vine, which was native to Garqi. Prefect Mosh Barris on the planet Garqi was partial to Cassandran choholl, and the drink was enjoyed by Cryle Cavv, Sienn Sconn, and Shandria L'hnnar after the successful capture of the Executor-class Star Dreadnought Guardian. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Alcoholic beverages
