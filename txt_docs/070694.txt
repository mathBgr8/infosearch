|release date=October 6, 2004 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=The Phantom Menace |followed by=Revenge of the Sith }} Return of the Jedi was the ninth expansion card pack made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game produced by Wizards of the Coast, based on Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. This expansion also contains cards based on elements from Attack of the Clones, A New Hope and The Empire Strikes Back. The set was released on October 6, 2004. This expansion contains 110 cards.
