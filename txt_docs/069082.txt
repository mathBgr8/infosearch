Len Carlson
982

Len Carlson was a voice actor born in Canada. In Star Wars Droids, he played a number of characters. Carlson appeared on many shows for Nelvana like Rolie Polie Olie, Cyberchase, Pecola, Donkey Kong Country, and Beyblade. He died in his sleep of a heart attack at the age of 68 in 2006. The Cyberchase episode "Eco-Haven Ooze" was dedicated to his memory, as was the Atomic Betty episode "Takes One to Know One." Category:Star Wars: Droids voice actors
