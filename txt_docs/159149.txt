Md-12 missile defense satellite platform
1689

thumb|right|200px|An Md-12 Missile Defense Satellite. The Md-12 missile defense satellite was an armed space platform developed by the Corporate Sector Authority for the defense of orbital installations and space stations. The Md-12 was equipped with twin missile pods and internal magazines that fired large volleys of concussion missiles at a target. The missiles were moderately powerful and well-suited for attacking frigates or fighters due to the fact that they could easily penetrate energy shields.
