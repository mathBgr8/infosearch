|commission= |destroyed=3963 BBY, Serroco |retired= |battles=Battle of Serroco |affiliation= |navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Queen of Taris was a civilian spaceliner that was active during the era of the Mandalorian Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Mandalorian warrior clans. In 3963 BBY, Queen of Taris was present on the planet Serroco when it came under attack by Mandalorians&mdash;the invaders devastated the world with nuclear missiles, and Queen of Taris was lost in the fighting. After the battle, Queen of Taris destruction was noted in an edition of The Admiral's List, an official publication issued by the Republic Navy. Queen of Taris was first mentioned in The Admiral's List: Remember Serroco!
