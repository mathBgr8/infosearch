Katarin Pressor
1946

Katarin Pressor was the Human female daughter of Dillian Pressor and his wife and the sister of Jorad and Rosemari Pressor. She was one of the passengers on Outbound Flight. In the novel Survivor's Quest, the name of Jorad's sister was given as Rosemari. Whether Rosemari is the same person as Katarin or is another sister born after the crash is unknown.
