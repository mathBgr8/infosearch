Adrell
3013

Adrell was an individual who served as a Moff in the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War between the Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic. The Moff operated out of the planet Manwess III in the Kilbanis sector, which was located in the Colonies region of the galaxy. Sometime between 0 BBY and 4 ABY,Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters, which includes Malindin Gevarak's bounty posting, focuses on bounty hunting during the time period between the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope and Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which The Essential Chronology places in 0 BBY and 4 ABY respectively. Adrell posted a bounty of 10,000 credits on the Human counterfeiter Malindin Gevarak, who had created counterfeit Imperial currency for the Rebel Alliance and then fled Imperial authorities. An additional 2,000 credits was offered if Gevarak was captured alive with minimal injury. Adrell was mentioned in the bounty posting for Malindin Gevarak in Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters, a supplement released by West End Games in 1994 for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The book was written by Rick D.
