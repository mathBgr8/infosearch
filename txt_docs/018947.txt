Zac'ryah Vos
1679

Zac'ryah Vos was a male Kiffar General of the Galactic Republic who represented Kiffu and Kiffex at sector security conferences during the Clone Wars. He was heavily scarred, with severe burns across his face, and his left eye replaced by a cybernetic implant.
