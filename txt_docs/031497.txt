Arrak snake
2014

The arrak snake was an avian reptile native to the planet Ithor. They fed on shamarok flitters. The arrak snake had green eyes and brightly-colored feathered scales. They had small wings, which allowed them to live among the Bafforr trees in the canopies of Ithor's jungles. Arrak snakes communicated through a complex, musical hooting call.
