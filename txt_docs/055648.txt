Todrin Rossik
2223

Captain Todrin Rossik was a Human male from the planet Coruscant who served in the forces of Warlord Zsinj. He was a rail-thin and blond-haired. Rossik was assigned to accompany Tavin Notsil when he met with Gara Petothel on Aldivy. During their meeting, Rossik discovered that Petothel's escort, Myn Donos, was lurking outside the house where the meeting took place. He was fairly certain that the New Republic had holos of him in their records, and decided to kill Donos and take Petothel back to Iron Fist. Instead, Petothel signaled her partner to indicate trouble.
