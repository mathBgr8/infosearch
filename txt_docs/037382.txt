Municipal patrol droid Mark I
1652

Municipal patrol droid Mark I was a droid designed by Automata Galactica as a law enforcement assistant for urban environments. It was also a popular way to boost firepower without adding personnel among private security companies and wealthy individuals. Even though small, Municipal Patrol Droid Mark I included more firepower than the average patrol droid. During the Jedi Civil War, these droids were massively produced by the Star Forge and used by the Sith Empire to reinforce the Sith troopers on the conquered worlds.
