Ghrag Mercenaries
2343

The Ghrag Mercenaries were one of the many enemies of the Civilian Protection Guild, particularly of Eyma, one of its members, on the Wookiee homeworld of Kashyyyk. They had a base in the Tyyyn Nebula. In 1 ABY, the Ghrag stole a CR90 corvette from the Civilian Protection Agency. It was soon recaptured from them by a group of spacers that had been sent by Eyma. In space battles, Ghrag pilots flew Kihraxz assault fighters, Ixiyen-class fast attack crafts, Rihkxyrk assault fighters and X4 Gunships.
