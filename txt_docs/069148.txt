Haggleday
2183

|significance= |celebrations=Widespread haggling |observances= |place= |begin= |end= |date=Yearly }} Haggleday was a yearly festival of the Squib species. The holiday was observed with widespread revelry and bargaining. On the morning of Haggleday, Squib children, known as fuzzlings, became extremely excited. When the Squib merchant Macemillian-winduarté entered a hidden compartment on a Jawa sandcrawler in 0 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of "Spare Parts" to 0 BBY. the variety of droids inside made him feel like a fuzzling on Haggleday again, despite the machines' various states of disrepair. Haggleday was first mentioned in the short story "Spare Parts," by Pablo Hidalgo. The story was published in the eleventh issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine in November 1996. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cultural events Category:Holidays Category:Squib culture
