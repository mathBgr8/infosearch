Famulus
1531

The Famulus was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruise in the service of House Vandron around the time of the Battle of Naboo. During the Jedi Mission to Senex, the Famulus was tasked with relaying images back to Supreme Chancellor Finis Valorum, which allowed the Chancellor to see the destruction of the Ecliptic in a minefield near Asmeru. *Cloak of Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dreadnaught-class heavy cruisers
