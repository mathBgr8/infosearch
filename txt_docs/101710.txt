Star Wars Adventures 9: Capture Arawynne
1743

Star Wars Adventures 9: Capture Arawynne was the ninth installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. This book is a reprint of Episode I Adventures 7: Capture Arawynne. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 9 in June 2003.
