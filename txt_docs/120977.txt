D'rag
2339

D'rag was a starshipwright and the co-owner of Starshipwrights and Aerospace Engineers Incorporated along with their partner Vinda on the planet Oslumpex V. In 2 BBY, Corellian smuggler Han Solo owed them 2,500 credits for their work on his starship, the Millennium Falcon. Taking advantage of this knowledge, the Corporate Sector Authority executive Odumin&mdash;posing as a skip tracer called "Spray," looking to repossess the vessel for D'rag and Vinda&mdash;got close to Solo and his Wookiee partner Chewbacca in his investigation of a slaving ring in the Corporate Sector.
