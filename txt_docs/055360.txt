Skeeter
1087

Skeeter was a male of an unknown flying species who served as an assistant to Princess Lourdes. He had six limbs and resembled a giant fly. During the duel between the Princess and Lord Gar-Oth, Skeeter held guns to the heads of Gar-Oth's guards to prevent them from interfering.
