|location=Quantill City, Ando |terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} The Raquish Thoroughfare was a busy roadway featuring several streets in Quantill City, the largest city and capital of Ando,Dangerous Covenants the Mid Rim homeworld of the sentient Aqualish species. It shared its name with the Raquish system and the Battle of Raquish, which was fought in 39 BBY and pitted the Spiverelda, the group of worlds colonized by the Aqualish and being under the influence of Ando, againstThe Essential Guide to Warfare the fifteen Aqualish-settled planets known as the Andoan Free Colonies. In 13:2:28, less than two standard hours after Ando officially seceded from the Galactic Republic, the streets of the Raquish Thoroughfare were filled with jubilant Aqualish of various backgrounds and from different areas of their homeworld who protested against the galactic government. The protesters subsequently converged in the city square of Quantill City. The event was later noted by an issue of the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. The Raquish Thoroughfare was introduced in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #45, an issue of the representation of the in-universe HoloNet News that was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published by March 10, 2002.
