Illustrious Chieftain of Junkyards
1072

The Illustrious Chieftain of Junkyards was the leader of the Squib Merchandising Consortium and thus of the entire Squib species. In the final days of the Old Republic and the early years of the Galactic Civil War, Ebareebaveebeedee held the post. The Illustrious Chieftain of Junkyards divided his time between Metrobig City on Skor II and the Momship of the Squib Reclamation Fleet.
