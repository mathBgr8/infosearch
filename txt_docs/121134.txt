Around 5.5 BBY, the station was used as a meeting ground for diplomatic discussions between Troobs, Hobors, and the Tion Hegemony. It was from the station that Nimondro first demonstrated the power of the sky cannon. After the demonstration, the delegates agreed to leave the station and head to the surface aboard a Troob shuttle. Level Seven of the space station was known as the game room.
