Trenn Gant
1426

Trenn Gant was an officer of New Republic Intelligence. Following the ambush of Tampion and the capture of Han Solo, Colonel Gant, then attached to the Fifth Battle Group, oversaw the debriefing of the pilots sent to escort Tampion.
