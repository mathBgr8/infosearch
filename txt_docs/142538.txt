Wastril bread
1686

}} Wastril bread was a favorite food of the young Han Solo, often prepared by Dewlannamapia on board the Trader's Luck. It may have been a traditional Wookiee food, yet it was known to be quite common on Corellia. It was described as a "crumbly" bread with fruit flavorings. In addition to fruit, the bread also contained cereal ingredients. In the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies, wastril bread used to be craftable by the chef profession, but the schematic was rendered unusable during the game's Publish 6 in 2004.
