Vader's Castle
5034

Vader's Castle was a battlefield within the Grand Arena on Vespaara. It was based on Fortress Vader on Mustafar. }} Vader's Castle was first introduced in the canon video game Star Wars: Hunters as one of its playable maps, which was developed by NaturalMotion's BossAlien and initially published by Zynga on November 16, 2021 as a soft launch before the global release on June 4, 2024. The map was added as part of the game's second season "Empire Resurgent" on August 1, 2024. Prior to its proper introduction, the map was briefly shown in the game's launch date reveal trailer. The battlefield was also first identified as Vader's Castle in the official patch notes on July 30 of the same year.Artist Clint Young was responsible for the location's early concept visualizations. *Star Wars: Hunters Category:Grand Arena battlefields
