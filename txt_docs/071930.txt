Kuratcha
2125

The Kuratcha was the mothership of the Hiromi Empire. It was involved in the attempt to incite war among the Alliance members, the Ewoks and the Lahsbee, and took part in the Battle of Zeltros during Operation: Glorious Destiny in 4 ABY. Its crew included Captain Hookyr, First Officer Hirog, Ensign Phoebe, and Chief Navigator Tahkay.
