Zolo
804

Zolo was the name of a squadron of CloakShape starfighters that were operated by the Loose Cannon pirates. Zolo 10 and Zolo 11 were involved in a skirmish between the Loose Cannons and the Azzameen family shortly after the Battle of Hoth. However, both were destroyed after the Azzameens proved to be too tough to contain. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Pirate squadrons
