Tof Soren Slugthrower Pistol
1054

The Tof Soren Slugthrower Pistol, also known as the "Cracker" due to their distinctive report, was a slugthrower produced in Tof Soren on Ord Vaxal. The Tof Soren Slugthrower Pistol could fire 7 shots before having to be reloaded. It used a 7-shot top-loading internal magazine, with bullets inserted individually one at a time. The weapon typically used bullets with an average diameter of between 10mm and 11mm. Tof Soren Slugthrower Pistol
