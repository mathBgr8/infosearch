Electrical Capacitance Shield
941

The Electrical Capacitance Shield, worn around the waist, absorbed and stored electrical energy directed at the user, which was then released slowly over a period of time, dissipating harmlessly. The manner of release generated vibrations along the inside edge of the shield, giving it the less than complimentary nickname, "The Tingler." *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Belts Category:Personal energy shields
