Pokkta was a kind of plant originating from the planet Bakura. During the Invasion of Bakura in 4 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion senator Gaeriel Captison had watched rain fall on pokkta leaves in the morning before an emergency evening call with Governor Wilek Nereus. *The Truce at Bakura Category:Plants of Bakura
