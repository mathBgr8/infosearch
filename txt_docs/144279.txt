In 1 ABY, he was present at Tansarii Point Station, a space station located near the planet Ord Mantell, in the Bright Jewel system. During that time, he expressed frustration at a broken clone terminal. Idau appears as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Idau was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
