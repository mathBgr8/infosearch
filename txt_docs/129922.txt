Taisden
1162

Taisden was a New Republic officer onboard the Glorious. When Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt volunteered to search for Lando Calrissian, Lobot, C-3PO and R2-D2; Taisden, along with Pleck and Bijo Hammax, volunteered as well.
