Tirual Construction Holdings
1600

Tirual Construction Holdings was a publicly traded company in existence prior to the Second Galactic Civil War, that backed the establishment of the Tekshar Falls Casino in Kuat City on Kuat. By the year 40 ABY, the reigning Mand'alor Boba Fett had invested in Tirual Construction Holdings and held subsequent shares in the Tekshar Falls. While visiting the Tekshar Falls Casino in pursuit of Jaing Skirata, Fett reviewed his portfolio and investments in Tirual Holdings, making a personal note to minimize any potential damage to the casino due to his financial interests. Tirual Construction Holdings was first introduced in the novel Sacrifice, the fifth entry in the multi-author Star Wars: Legacy of the Force series, written by Karen Traviss and published May 29, 2007. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Construction companies
