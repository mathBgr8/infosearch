The Chorran shipjackers were a criminal band of starship thieves from the Chorra system that operated in Bamula sector in the Inner Rim. They were routed on Donadus by the bounty hunter Beilert Valance. *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook Category:Pirate organizations
