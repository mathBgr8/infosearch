|feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Klatooinian Trade Guild |masters= |apprentices= }} Nazrita Villache was a Human woman and chief mechanic for the Klatooinian Trade Guild syndicate on Boztrok. She gave Platt Okeefe a tour of her first ship, the Brentaal Princess. Villache had maintained the ship for the Guild and had installed a proton torpedo tube in the ship for extra firepower. She reported to Pok Nar-Ten and would often install modification at his direction on unsuspecting smuggler's ships. She charged standard rates for repairs, although she was only supposed to charge 75% due to her use of the Klatooinian Trade Guild facilities.
