They were one of the few transports that Gallofree created for military application. They were capable of carrying light numbers of infantry within their hold and use their heavy repulsor engines to lift from one location to another, hovering above the battlefield. They were armed with a rapid fire laser cannon suited to taking out infantry and protecting embarking and disembarking troops.
