Viran Qol
1910

}} Viran Qol was an Alliance SpecForce Major who was considered an expert on Imperial fortifications. He lectured on Imperial garrison design and tactics at the hidden Alliance Officer's Candidate School. Qol graduated from a civil engineering program the day before his homeworld was subjugated by the Empire. Days later, he joined the local resistance, which later became part of the Alliance to Restore the Republic.
