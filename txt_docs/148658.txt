Kolor Delan
2117

Kolor Delan was the founder and leader of Pendarran's Revenge, an armed organization that fought against the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Kolor's father, Gendal Delan was the leader of the Pendarran Warriors, who fought with the Jedi Knights and the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. However, the Pendarran Warriors were exterminated in the Great Jedi Purge. This led Kolor to hate the Empire and he formed his own group, which was named after his father's. Pendarran's Revenge occasionally aided the Alliance to Restore the Republic, however, after the Empire's defeat at Endor, Kolor became hostile to the New Republic.
