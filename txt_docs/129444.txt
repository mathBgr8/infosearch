Holographic Ringmaster
868

The Ringmaster was a male humanoid who possessed a long tail that he used as a whip to perform with. He was the central figure of a holographic circus, seen on Lumpy's holographic table in his home on Kashyyyk. The ringmaster performed along with the Great Zorbak, a gymnast, and the Reeko Brothers, a pair of jugglers, and some holographic tumblers. The Ringmaster was petite in stature and had black hair.
