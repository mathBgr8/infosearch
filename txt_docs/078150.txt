High Moff
1532

thumb|right|200px|[[Morlish Veed, a High Moff of the Galactic Empire.]] High Moff was originally a self-aggrandizing title taken by Imperial warlords during the chaos following the Battle of Endor. By 130 ABY, it was used as a ceremonial title to designate the Moffs of the Moff High Council of the Fel and later Sith Empires. Morlish Veed, who also held the rank of Grand Moff at the time, was given the title following his victory at the Battle of Caamas.
