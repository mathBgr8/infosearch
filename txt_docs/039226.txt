Katarn's Hill
904

Katarn's Hill was a hill on Ruusan located near Fort Nowhere. It gained its name from the Sulonese settlements established there in 1 BBY by Morgan Katarn. Nearby was a ravine that the settlers used for waste disposal.Dark Forces: Jedi Knight During the battle of Fort Nowhere, Dinko managed to shoot an attacking TIE fighter, which crashed on the Hill. *Dark Forces: Jedi Knight Category:Mountains Category:Ruusan locations
