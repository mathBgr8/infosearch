Nathan O'Keefe
1448

Nathan O'Keefe is the author of "Tinker, Tailor, Soldier, Praji," a Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club exclusive source article. Through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace O'Keefe previously wrote the backstories of Yavid and Teyora Rekab, co-wrote the Diva Shaliqua backstory, and supplied the name for the Ottegru Grey backstory, all appearing in the Databank. His alias is Valin Kenobi on StarWars.com and several other forums, and also Valin Kenobi on Wookieepedia.
