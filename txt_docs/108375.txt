Nelhal Industries
883

Nelhal Industries was a company that produced the RGB photoreceptor lens used by Industrial Automaton in the design of their R2-series astromech droids. One of that series' most famous units, R2-D2, was fitted with these lenses, in conjunction with Stears Data multifunctional optical readers. *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid technology companies
