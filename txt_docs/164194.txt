Spang's
1129

Spang's was located on Tanquilla Beach, near the bottom of the installation. Run by Spang, it occupied the largest repair space on the station. Spang's work was legendary, and the repair shop usually had a long list of ships waiting to be worked on. Spang's also had a portable drydock available to it to work on capital ships in parking orbit. Spang's granddaughter, Fia, also worked in the shop.
