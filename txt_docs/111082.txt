Tinmolok
2381

The Tinmolok was a Marl-class heavy freighter manufactured by Kuat Drive Yards. Captained by Swori Mdimu and his Etti crew, it was part of the Peace Brigade fleet during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The ship was used to carry supplies and munitions for the Yuuzhan Vong, and carried two Yuuzhan Vong warriors onboard. The ship was hijacked by Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo one jump from the Cha Raaba system, not far from Tynna. They were working on creating the Great River, a string of safehouses to transport Jedi across the galaxy. Among the cargo confiscated were three E-wing escort starfighters, 200 kilos of lommite, and a large supply of rations. All this would be delivered to Shelter base in the Maw, as the start of a privateering effort by Solo. The hold also contained amphistaffs grown on Wayland.
