Nihema
1153

Nihema was an Imperial Navy Assassin-class corvette that fought in the conflict at Mylok IV. When the Habeen team sent two cargo ferry groups, Verack and Stimner, to rendezvous with, then Admiral Zaarin aboard the Nebulon-B2 frigate Shamus, they deployed three flight groups of TIE/LN starfighters, Alpha, Beta, and Gamma.
