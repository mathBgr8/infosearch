Mistryl battle language
1015

}} Mistryl battle language was a special language that was spoken by the Mistryl Shadow Guard when they were engaged in battle. Its main purpose, as well as its major advantage, was that it provided a means of swift, on-the-field communication that maintained the greater degree of security which came with such cryptic speech. *Specter of the Past *Vision of the Future Category:Languages
