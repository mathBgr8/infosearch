Polearm Squadron
1691

Polearm Squadron was an A-wing squadron in Group One of Solo Fleet. Polearm Squadron formed a part of Mon Remonda fighter complement, and flew in several sorties, including the attack on the stolen Executor-class Star Dreadnought Razor's Kiss, and later campaigns against Warlord Zsinj. Todra Mayn, flying in Polearm One, was the first pilot to find Zsinj's flagship Iron Fist as it hid in the Selaggis VI asteroid belt.
