Battle of Goff (disambiguation)
479

The Battle of Goff may refer to any one of three conflicts, all of which took place during the Airam campaign of the Galactic Civil War: *An Imperial attack on Goff *A Rebel attack on the Compellor in the Goff system *A Rebel ambush in the Goff system
