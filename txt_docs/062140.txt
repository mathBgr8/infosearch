Starsider
2484

The Starsider was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser in service to the New Republic and later the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances. During the Yuuzhan Vong War the aging Dreadnaught had been rebuilt as a supply and escort ship. While stationed over Kashyyyk, it was the home base of Twin Suns Squadron.The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way It was present in numerous major engagements during the latter portions of the Yuuzhan Vong War, including the Battle of Ylesia and the Defense of Mon Calamari.
