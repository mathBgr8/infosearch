Reprisal (Dreadnaught-class)
2433

The Reprisal was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser of the Imperial Navy. The Reprisal had a long service life typical of the class, and had visited Kessel at some point prior to the Runaway Droid Ride. By 8 ABY, the Reprisal was part of Warlord Zsinj's fleet and was deployed at Comkin V in the failed attempt to capture the Millennium Falsehood. Her bow shields were softened by an explosive package disguised as debris launched by the Millennium Falsehood.
