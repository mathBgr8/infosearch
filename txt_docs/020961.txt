In 22 BBY, the news story "Yet Another Greenputt League" in the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News reported the formation of the Non-Sentient Professional Greenputt Tour, which joined the existing professional greenputt leagues. The Asexual Professional Greenputt Tour was first mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News published on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
