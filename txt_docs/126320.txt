Sandro
1213

Sandro the Hook was a criminal who bought stolen goods. He was a junk dealer living on Celanon who had past dealings with Captain Bortrek. Occasionally, he would sell them for high prices to willing buyers with a large amount of money. If the person who wished to purchase something from Sandro pulled out of a deal while a transaction was under way, he would hire a bounty hunter to finish the job.
