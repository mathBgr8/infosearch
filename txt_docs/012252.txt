Sen Udo-Mal
1835

Sen Udo-Mal was a male Cerean Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as a Consular. A Force-sensitive Cerean, Sen Udo-Mal received training in the ways of the Force from the Jedi Order.
