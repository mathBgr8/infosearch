Meri
1804

The Meris were a tall humanoid race with dark blue skin, similar to the Teltiors, with whom they shared the planet Merisee. Distinguishing features included a strongly-pronounced eyebrow ridge, a slightly conical ridge on the top of their head, webbed hands with an opposable thumb and finger, inward-spiraling cartilage in the ear, and several thick folds of skin around the neck.
