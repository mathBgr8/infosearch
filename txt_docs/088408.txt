Pillik
1272

Pillik was a young male Omwati who was taken by the Empire to be trained (along with several other young Omwati children, including Qwi Xux) to build superweapons for the Empire. During a test, he had a seizure and was removed by stormtroopers, screaming that he wanted to finish the test.
