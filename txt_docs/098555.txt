The Singularity was an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer manufactured by Kuat Drive Yards. Coated with a rare stygian-triprismatic polymer, the Singularity was almost impossible to detect at sensor range and nearly invisible to the naked eye in deep space. The vessel also possessed a gravity well generator allowing it to interdict hyperspace travel. The ship served as Imperial Intelligence Director Blackhole's main base of operations after he fled Coruscant while being possessed with an irrational fear of being in danger. The Singularity, its attached TIE fighter wing the 123rd Nightstalker Group, and Blackhole's own special stormtrooper division were all cast in obsidian to match his eccentricities. The Singularity was first mentioned in The Emperor's Pawns, an article written by Abel G. Peña and Pablo Hidalgo and published in Star Wars Gamer 5 on July 24, 2001.
