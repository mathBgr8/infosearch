Cartann
1410

Cartann was the large country on Adumar, controlling over half of the planet landmass. Its Perator was Pekaelic ke Teldan until 13 ABY, when his eldest son Balass ke Teldan took the throne. It also possessed a large air force consisting mainly of modern Blade-32 superiority fighters. Its capital was Cartann City. It was overthrown by the Yedagon Confederacy.
