|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=137 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=*Star Wars: Legacy *Star Wars: Vector |issue=29 }} Legacy 29 is the twenty-ninth issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. It is the second issue of Legacy to feature the Star Wars: Vector cross-over arc and is the tenth issue in the over-all Vector story arc. With his bounty-hunter pals and a team of Imperial Knights, Cade Skywalker plans to assassinate the Sith Emperor Darth Krayt. The first step in their scheme requires a risky attack on a remote Imperial outpost in hopes of drawing Krayt away from Coruscant. But even if that works, members of the team are divided in their feelings about their "secret weapon"&mdash;Celeste Morne, an ancient Jedi Knight of the Old Republic who is in possession of&mdash;and possibly possessed by&mdash;the powerful Muur Talisman. As we plunge into the final chapters of the yearlong "Vector" crossover, the power of the Talisman threatens to shake the galaxy to its foundations!
