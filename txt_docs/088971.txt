Romold
1607

Romold was a cargo ferry used by Mugaari pirates. The Cargo ferry was part of an illicit weapons deal between the pirates and the Rebel Alliance. However, the Imperials attacked the the cargo loading area and the cargo ferry, along with all the other Mugaari and rebel ships were destroyed in the Battle of the Tungra sector. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Cargo ferries Category:Mugaari starships
