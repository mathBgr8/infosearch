Happy Spacer

The Happy Spacer was an intoxicant retailer owned by the Teltior Loeerna located in The Life section of the Vergesso Base. It sold various forms of intoxicating substances, including simple salt, lum, and spice liquor. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Vendors and sellers Category:Vergesso Base locations
