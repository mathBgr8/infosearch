Arrowhead
4389

Arrowhead was an area of the Core located at the tip of the Slice and was also known as the Inner Core. Inclusive of the Core Worlds between the Perlemian Trade Route and the Corellian Run, it ranged from Vento to Corellia. The ancient area known as the Tetrahedron was found in this area as the first inklings of modern civilization. During the Imperial Period, Arrowhead Command was the Imperial fleet assigned to the defense of the Arrowhead. It was commanded by Admiral Blitzer Harrsk from the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Whirlwind. The Essential Atlas clearly identified the Inner Core as the Arrowhead.
