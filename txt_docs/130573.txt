Miko Beski
1555

Miko Beski was a commando who participated in various missions in the service of Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Born on a space station over Duros, Miko spent his entire childhood in space. After the Empire subjugated the Duros system, Miko enlisted in the Rebel Alliance and eventually became part of the SpecForces. Upon promotion to Lieutenant, he became the leader of Team 19, reporting to Captain Qarl.
