Ib-Dunn
2471

Ib-Dunn was a blue-green Smotl male who worked for Soergg the Hutt in Cuipernam, the capital of the planet of Ansion. He worked as a communications expert in 22 BBYThe Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The Approaching Storm to 22 BBY. during the Separatist Crisis, when many worlds left the Galactic Republic to form the Confederacy of Independent Systems. When Ogomoor, Soergg's Ansionian majordomo, was on his way to report to Soergg that an ambush intended to kill Jedi Master Luminara Unduli and her Padawan Barriss Offee had failed, Ib-Dunn stared at him with a look of pity.
