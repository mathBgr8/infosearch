Carvanium
985

Carvanium was a metal used in alloys, including durasteel. Null ARC Null-5 sabotaged droid factories by adding 5% extra carvanium to the droid recipes. This resulted in crumbling droids which could easily be destroyed by a single shot of any weapon. *Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures *The Life and Legend of Obi-Wan Kenobi *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Compounds
