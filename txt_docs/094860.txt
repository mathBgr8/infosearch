Sonic Screwdriver
2553

The Sonic Screwdriver was an alcoholic cocktail composed of fruit juice and liquor that was widely available across the galaxy. In 44 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Fate of the Jedi: Conviction to 44 ABY. the drink's textbook recipe was replicated precisely by a protocol droid when the Human Moff Drikl Lecersen ordered a Sonic Screwdriver while in Obridagar's Simulator Palace, a pilot training facility on the planet of Coruscant. }} The Sonic Screwdriver appeared in Fate of the Jedi: Conviction, a 2011 novel by Aaron Allston. Allston borrowed the name "sonic screwdriver" from the all-purpose tool used by the Doctor in Doctor Who. *Fate of the Jedi: Conviction Category:Cocktails
