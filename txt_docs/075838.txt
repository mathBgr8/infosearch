House Harbright
871

thumb|right|200px|Lady Selnia Harbright House Harbright was a wealthy family influential in the Salliche Ag Corporation. In 25 ABY its leader was Count Borert Harbright. Other members were Lady Selnia Harbright and Dees Harbright.
