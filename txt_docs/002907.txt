Captain Antilles (disambiguation)
1051

The title Captain Antilles was held, at some point, by more than one person: *Raymus Antilles, was the captain of Tantive IV from the time of the Clone Wars until his death, and also according to Episode IV: A New Hope, the former owner of C-3PO and R2-D2. *Wedge Antilles, who was not related to Raymus, held the rank of captain in the New Republic Defense Fleet during the last stages of the Galactic Civil War. *Raymus Antilles, referred to in some sources as Colton Antilles, was the captain of Tantive IV from the time of the Clone Wars until his death in 0 BBY *Wedge Antilles, who was not related to Raymus, was a captain in the Rebel Alliance from 4 ABY to 6.5 ABY
