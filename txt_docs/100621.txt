|death=April 3, 2016 |listedpronouns= |nationality=Canadian |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Don Francks (February 28, 1932 &ndash; April 3, 2016) was a Canadian actor who voiced Jann Tosh and Gir Kybo Ren-Cha on Droids and an X-marked Dulok, Umwak and an O-marked Dulok on Ewoks. Francks was the father of Cree Summer, who voiced Princess Kneesaa in Ewoks and other animated Star Wars characters. He performed on other shows like Inspector Gadget, Mister Rogers' Neighborhood, X-Men, and The Neverending Story, and in films like Heavy Metal and Johnny Mnemonic.
