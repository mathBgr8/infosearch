Wedding of Soontir Fel and Wynssa Starflare
2020

The wedding of Soontir Fel and Wynssa Starflare was a high profile event which took place one year after the Battle of Yavin. While members of Fel's family attended the wedding, Starflare's brother, Wedge Antilles, was notably absent. The famous couple followed up the wedding with a two-week honeymoon planned by Starflare on an exotic world, the name of which Fel never learned. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 25 *Star Wars Handbook 1: X-Wing Rogue Squadron *The New Essential Guide to Characters }} Category:Events of the Galactic Civil War Category:Weddings
