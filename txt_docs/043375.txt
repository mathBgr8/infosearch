Fingers
1085

"Fingers" was the nick-name attributed to a female Zabrak animal herder. She specialized in training Borgle bats for arena pit-fighting competition. She hunted the dangerous creatures in their native jungle environment on the Naboo moon of Rori. On one occasion, a Borgle bat bit off two of the fingers on her right hand, earning the Zabrak the uncomplimentary nickname, Fingers. *Star Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine Category:Females Category:Zabrak
