Indenture
2490

The Indenture was a yellow and white Firespray-31-class patrol and attack ship that was piloted by Krassis Trelix as part of the Karazak Slavers Cooperative. The ship was notorious for traveling under multiple phony aliases and transponder IDs, including the Salvation. In 8 ABY, the Indenture was destroyed when it tried to capture a cargo of Twi'lek dancing-girls from a Mistryl Shadow Guard freighter, The Fury, which was being flown by Shada D'ukal.
