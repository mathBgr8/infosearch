A Hydra craft was a repulsorcraft used for transportation over water. This was the primary mode of transportation on Drexel. thumb|left|180px|A hydra-skimmer version. The hydra craft was a small aquatic repulsorlift gunship. The craft was typically equipped with laser cannons and magnetic grapples. An armored type of Hydra craft was called a hydra-skimmer. They were used by the ship-dwellers to salvage wrecked ships and fight Dragon Lords.
