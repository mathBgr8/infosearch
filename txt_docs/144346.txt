Pickaxe
621

Pickaxe was a Naboo Moon Mining Union kassoti spice transport that ran between Naboo and Rori, captained by Calquad Dominé. In 22 BBY, it prevented the Sluissi Wanderlust from landing in Kwilaan Starport. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Transports
