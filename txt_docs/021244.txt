|published in=Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=0 BBY |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Swap Meet: The Jawa's Tale" is a short story written by Kevin J. Anderson for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina, which was published by Bantam Spectra in August 1995. The story, which takes place in 0 BBY, is about Het Nkik. Het Nkik, unlike most Jawas, is not content to run away and hide when threatened. When his clan-mate and best friend Jek Nkik is killed by stormtroopers looking for R2-D2 and C-3PO he buys an illegal DL-44 blaster pistol at a Jawa annual swap-meet, sets off to Mos Eisley for vengeance and to show other Jawas they're not powerless. He's encouraged in this by Obi-Wan Kenobi when they meet at the burnt-out wreckage of Jek's sandcrawler.
