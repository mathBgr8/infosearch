thumb|right|250px|The Condemnation approaches [[Viraxo Industries (platform)|Viraxo Industries.]] Condemnation was a Victory I-class Star Destroyer belonging to the Galactic Empire. It responded to an attack at Viraxo Industries caused by Ace and Emon Azzameen, who were attempting to rescue their uncle Antan.
