Chitlik
2104

Chitliks were burrowing marsupials found on Ord Cestus. They lived in caves and had orange stripes. The females produced a salubrious milk called kista. Cestians would set traps for chitliks in order to harvest the kista from their mammary glands. The kista would be milked and then the chitliks would be released. One notable chitlik was Pocket, the pet of Chief of State Natasi Daala's aide, Wynn Dorvan.
