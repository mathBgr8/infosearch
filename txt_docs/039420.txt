Military Correction Facility 63
1633

Military Correction Facility 63 or MCF 63 was a prison that was part of the base established on the planet Milagro by the New Republic in 5 ABY, three months before the battle in the Milagro system.Dark Forces: Jedi Knight It was maintained by detainment droids. Thanks to the planet's thin atmosphere and the prisoners's inability to escape, they were allowed to work without leg irons. Although the prisoners were always ordered and expected to hurry up, eventually they always had to wait for the officers. Alfonso Luiz Obota was interred there for mutiny. Obota and other prisoners worked for the installation of a communication's mast on the level fused glass, the remnants of the buildings after Galactic Empire bombardment. Its headquarters were in building twenty-three. A prefab building like the rest of the base, the headquarters had interior structures, corridors, doors of fiberboard. At the first hall a read board scroll a list of prohibitions which extended to the floor. The deck consisted of fiber foam. The interior had a green color. It was there where Obota was summoned and interviewed by Jan Ors and Kyle Katarn and recruited for the Mission to Ruusan. *Dark Forces: Jedi Knight Category:New Republic locations Category:Prisons
