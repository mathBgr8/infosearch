Slave Lord Boulevard
624

Slavelord Boulevard was the name of a street found in the capital city of Kessendra on the planet, Kessel. Its original designation is unknown, but the name was changed to Slavelord Boulevard at some point during the reign of Emperor Palpatine. The street intersects with Spice Mines Avenue. C-3PO and R2-D2 once got lost on Slavelord Boulevard after returning from a spy mission at the Kessendra Stadium. *The Glove of Darth Vader Category:Kessel locations Category:Roadways
