He was one of the key commanders at the Battle of Restuss on Rori. After Restuss's destruction, Grot was killed along with the Dark troopers that were guarding him by Rebel commandos dispatched by Alliance Captain Voldez. Grot appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Admiral Grot was added to the game along with the "Battle of Restuss" PvP battlefield, introduced in the "Chapter 1.1" released on June 27, 2006.
