Aubree Miller
1909

Aubree Miller portrayed five-year old Cindel Towani in the two Ewok films: Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure (1984) and Ewoks: The Battle for Endor (1985).Ewoks: The Battle for Endor These were her only two major roles in acting. She currently works and resides in Chico, California. She appeared at the Dark Xmas Convention in Cleveland, Ohio in 2005 alongside Daniel Frishman (Deej Warrick) and Eric Walker (Mace Towani).
