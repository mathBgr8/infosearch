Unidentified Admiral of the Fleet
1192

An unknown male was the Galactic Republic's Admiral of the Fleet during the Mandalorian Wars. He was based on Coruscant. Following the Battle of Serroco, he made an official protest with the Galactic Republic government over the positioning of the Republic Army near the Stereb cities.
