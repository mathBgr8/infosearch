They were based inside the Death Watch bunker on the forest moon of Endor, virtually hidden from the rest of the galaxy. This Death Watch Overlord was in command of Death Watch Wraiths, Ghosts, Bloodguards, and Black Sun mercenaries dispatched within the bunker. Sometime in 1 ABY, he was killed by a group of individuals working for Commander D'krn, who wished to obtain a vial of his blood for possible usage in cloning a new batch of stormtroopers. This Death Watch Overlord appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
