Algarine
1125

Algarine was the capital city of the planet of Algara II. It was located on the continent of Kreesis. Several corporations had branches in Algarine, including Algar Mining, Feduch Importers, Prosperine Entertainment and Distilleries, and Kexeerian Blasters Technology. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Capital cities Category:Cities on Algara II
