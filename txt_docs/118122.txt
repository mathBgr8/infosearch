Jungle Flutes
1037

Jungle Flutes was an Imperial propaganda holodrama that starred the young holofilm celebrity, Garik Loran. It was badly received by many Lorrdian and Argazdan viewers at the Qatamer Performance Square due to its blatantly pro-Imperial stance.
