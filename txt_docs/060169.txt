Model Com C-38
1717

The Model Com C-38 was a command center model designed by Delvin Constructs, that was used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. It was commonly used as a base of operations which Imperial officers and staff used to direct all base and ground force operations on a planet, unless more advanced structures were available. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: Empire at War: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Buildings Category:Delvin Constructs products
