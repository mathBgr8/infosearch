Riva's building

Riva's building was a structure in the capital city of Thani on Telos IV. The building was maintained by Riva, and rented out by Denetrus. The building's exterior was painted mismatching colors by Denetrus at the command of Riva, who could not decide on a color she liked. *Jedi Apprentice: The Day of Reckoning Category:Outer Rim Territories structures Category:Telos IV locations
