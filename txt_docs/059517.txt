Protectors (law enforcement)
711

The Protectors were a law enforcement authority operating on the planet Essowyn. They enforced the will of the Council of Elders, with complete authority in given cities. They were a combination of police officer, judge, and soldier, and were generally staffed by aggressive bullies. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One *The Star Wars Planets Collection *Alien Encounters Category:Law enforcement agencies Category:Saurton culture
