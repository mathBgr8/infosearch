|pages= |upc= |timeline=c. 5.5 BBYStar Wars Omnibus: Droids |series=Star Wars: Droids (1995) |issue=2 }} Droids 2 is the second issue of Star Wars: Droids (1995), a Dark Horse comic book series starring R2-D2 and C-3PO. It was published on May 16, 1995 by Dark Horse Comics. Artoo-Detoo and See-Threepio may have bitten off more than they can chew! Their boss has been blown to bits, and they're marooned in perhaps the most dangerous place in the universe: Nar Shaddaa! The beautiful and deadly Jace Forno has somehow wormed her mercenary self into the spaceport's top security position, and it seems like she's got an agenda for taking care of all kinds of meddlesome droids!
