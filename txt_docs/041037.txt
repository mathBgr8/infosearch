Bik
1476

Bik was a male Twi'lek who lived on Nar Shaddaa. During Meetra Surik's visit to the Smugglers' Moon she visited the swoop racing establishment there. Upon her arrival Bik had become severely drunk, passing out on the floor just as she and her companions walked in, much to the irritation of the proprietor, fellow Twi'lek Lupo Shar.
