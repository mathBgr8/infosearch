Snagel
1701

Snagel was a member of the Imperial Select Committee in 0 ABY. Snagel was sent a code-message by Ranulf Trommer, who was investigating the suspicious activities Governor Grigor on the planet M'haeli some months after the Battle of Yavin. The message contained enough information to "put Grigor in chains". Snagel appears to have passed this information to Grigor's superior, as Grand Moff Lynch Hauser arrived with an assault force to take control of the planet. Ranulf's reaction to the invasion force implies that he regarded Snagel as having some sort of relationship with the Imperial High Command, as he commented to Mora: "I sent a coded message to the High Command, describing Grigor's illegal mining operation.
