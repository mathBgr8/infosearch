|pages=25 |upc= |timeline=0.5 ABY |series=Star Wars: River of Chaos |issue=2 }} River of Chaos 2 is the second issue of the Star Wars Legends comic book miniseries Star Wars: River of Chaos. It was written by Louise Simonson, illustrated by June Brigman and Roy Richardson, and first published by Dark Horse Comics on July 1, 1995. It features the story "The Path to Honor," in which Leia Organa continues her mission on the planet M'haeli. The spy trade is a dangerous game, and if you lose you die. But there are greater dangers than death to those who cast false identities. There is the danger of forgetting who you are. In this second issue of Star Wars: River of Chaos, Leia is behind enemy lines with only a handful of Rebel spies at her command.
