Agorffi
1494

The Agorffi were a sentient species. They were known for their strength and brawn, and were often bracketed with other strong species such as Gamorreans and Wookiees; their resilience, however, made them a prime target for the Slaver Syndicate. The Slaver Syndicate was a large bounty hunter syndicate operating during the Galactic Civil War. They captured bounties of low value, then bought the ownership rights to their captives and sold them as enslaved individuals. The Agorffi were created for Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim, a West End Games sourcebook written by Simon Smith and Eric Trautmann and published in 1993. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Sentient species
