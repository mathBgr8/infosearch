Rorand Zuzz
1785

Rorand Zuzz was a male Ugnaught who was a tech specialist skilled in working with medical equipment. In 20 BBY, he was stationed on Drongar during the Clone Wars and survived the attack of the Separatist forces made against Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7. Afterward, he would take up his business in a new location.
