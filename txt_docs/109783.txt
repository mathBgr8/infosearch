Liberation of Spinnaker
2754

The Liberation of Spinnaker was a retaliatory strike to recapture Spinnaker from the Trade Federation during the Invasion of Naboo. Forces led by Boss Rugor Nass and aided by the Gungan village of Lors attacked Camp 14 at Spinnaker, destroying a Trade Federation monument there and replacing it with a Gungan one, to serve as a rallying point for the Gungan forces. The Second Battle of Spinnaker appears during the Gungan campaign in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
