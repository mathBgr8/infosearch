Gwen Habbert
1413

Gwen Habbert was a starfighter pilot who served with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. She participated in the defense of the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Liberty against a wave of TIE Boosters and other TIE Experimentals that had been dispatched as part of the TIE Experimental Project.
