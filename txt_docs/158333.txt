Chuundar's bowcaster
1820

Chuundar's bowcaster was an enhanced Wookiee bowcaster owned by Chuundar. It was specially modified by Czerka Corporation to use special upgrades, as a result of Chuundar's alliance with Czerka slavers. Revan took it when he killed Chuundar with the help of Zaalbar, Chuundar's brother and Freyyr, Chuundar's father, during the Rebellion on Kashyyyk.
