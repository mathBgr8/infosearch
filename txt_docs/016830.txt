The planet was orbited by nine moons and occupied the sixth orbital position around the star Beshqek. Kissarm was first mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 1: Byss," the first entry in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 14, 2005.
