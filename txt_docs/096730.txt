Wrodian carpet
1295

A Wrodian carpet was an opulent style of carpet that contained rich colors arranged in a distinctive weave. Any single Wrodian carpet would be woven over the course of generations before it was finally complete. Klaus Vandangante decorated the office of his mobile base with Wrodian carpets. The unlicensed roleplaying magazine article "Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star, How I Wonder Where We Are", which was published without Lucas Licensing approval and thus is considered non-canon, attributes the "Wrode system" to Brian Daley's Han Solo at Stars' End which was the first source to mention "Wrodian carpet",Han Solo at Stars' End and places the system in Zebitrope sector. *Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil *Han Solo at Stars' End Category:Art Category:Rugs and carpets
