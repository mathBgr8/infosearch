|birth= |death= |species= |gender= |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Team Coruscant |masters= |apprentices= }} Jipoly Numifolis was the captain limmie player for the planet Coruscant's limmie team. The issue of HoloNet News' CoCo District Edition on the date 13:3:14 reported that the inexperienced Team Coruscant was soundly defeated by the planet Fondor's team in the opening round of the Galactic Cup of Limmie; the score was three to one at Coruscant's own stadium. Although the loss disappointed them, Numifolis told HoloNet News reporters that they had high hopes for the future of limmie on Coruscant, a planet that did not care for the sport on the whole. The captain theorized that because the next generation of Coruscanti had taken to the sport, by twenty years' time, their team would have a much better pool of players, making it a force to be reckoned with. Jipoly Numifolis was mentioned in an issue of the HoloNet News feed, Volume 531, Issue 47. The issue was written in an in-universe perspective, authored by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens, and released on March 14, 2002.
