The Ordeal of Boba Fett

|published in=Dark Empire Sourcebook |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Ordeal of Boba Fett" is a short story written by Michael Allen Horne, originally published in the Dark Empire Sourcebook and later archived on Hyperspace. Just after the destruction of Jabba the Hutt's sail barge, bounty hunter Dengar stumbles upon a badly wounded Boba Fett, who has just escaped from the Great Pit of Carkoon, and transports him to a medical facility for treatment. As Fett lies in a coma, Dengar ponders over his injuries, showing disgust at the damage the Sarlacc had inflicted.
