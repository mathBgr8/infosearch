Shrike (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
1104

Shrike was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate which served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The frigate was attacked and destroyed by three B-wing starfighters, one of them piloted by Keyan Farlander, and four RZ-1 A-wing interceptors.
