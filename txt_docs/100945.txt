As with all Z-95t Headhunters, Tala 2 had a class 3 hyperdrive, which individually allowed Tala 2 long-range reconnaissance. During the Galactic Civil War fought between the Alliance and the Galactic Empire, Tala 2 served in Tala Squadron and was piloted by Lieutenant Blount by 4 ABY. is set around the time of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, the events of which are dated to 4 ABY by The New Essential Chronology. Blount worked for Alliance Intelligence and flew as the wingman of Colonel Airen Cracken, who flew the Z-95t Headhunter Tala 1 and was operationally assigned to support Alliance Special Forces commandos led by Crix Madine, an Alliance general. Tala 2 was equipped with a sophisticated sensor suite to monitor Imperial activity during the Alliance commandos' operations.
