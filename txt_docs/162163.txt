Adi Granov
5008

Adi Granov is a Bosnian artist who illustrated some of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game supplements for Wizards of the Coast, as well as A Campaign Guide to the Centrality, an article from Star Wars Gamer 5 written by Michael Kogge. An Iron Man comics and film veteran, he is providing covers for Marvel Comics' Darth Vader comic series. He has also drawn the covers for the remastered editions of A New Hope, The Empire Strikes Back, and Return of the Jedi comic adaptations, as well as the Emerald City Comicon variant cover for Princess Leia 1, and the Forbidden Planet variant cover for the first issue of Marvel's Star Wars 2015 series, which is based on one of his Iron Man pieces.
