Courage of Sullust
1922

The Courage of Sullust was a modified GR-75 medium transport which was used to carry ten X-wings of the Rogue Squadron during a diplomatic mission to Ryloth. In that occasion, the Courage of Sullust was accompanied by the Pulsar Skate, carrying Wedge Antilles and his X-wing fighter.
