Jeapird ke Fanax
1669

Jeapird ke Fanax was a male Human Adumari starfighter pilot and blastsword duelist from the planet Adumar. He fought in the finals of the Cartann Ground Championship against Cheriss ke Hanadi to whom he ultimately lost. Jeapird died of his injuries from the said match, which prompted his brother Depird ke Fanax to avenge him.
