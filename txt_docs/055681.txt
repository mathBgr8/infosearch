Mosh
1673

Mosh was a shuttle pilot who served aboard Outbound Flight. After a movement against Jedi Master Jorus C'baoth had arisen on board and the passengers hoped to be able to get Thrawn to free them from C'baoth, Mosh brought a group of the dissatisfied to D-Eins with a shuttle, and Dillian Pressor was the initiator.
