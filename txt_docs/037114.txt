P-60
1878

P-60, and its superior, P-59, were the two droidekas summoned by Trade Federation Nute Gunray to keep Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi from penetrating the bridge of the Saak'ak. The droidekas ended up forcing the pair of Jedi Knights to escape into a vent as a result of their firepower. *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace novelization *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace junior novelization *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droidekas Category:Droids of the Trade Federation Category:Droids with no gender programming
