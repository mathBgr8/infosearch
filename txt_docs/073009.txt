Torm
1195

Torm was one of four continents on the planet of Lazerian IV. It was located on the planet's western hemisphere and contained the city of Tormaine. The terrain was mostly made up of plains, with forests covering the middle part, while the southern end had large mountains. Together with Elgar and Laz, it made up the Kingdom of Devit. *Twin Stars of Kira Category:Continents of Lazerian IV
