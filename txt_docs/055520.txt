Katie Purvis
1593

Katie Purvis (born 1966) is an actress who played the "Maternal Ewok" in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. She is also the daughter of Star Wars actor Jack Purvis, and is good friends with Maureen Charlton. Katie became a part of the Star Wars saga with her role in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, where she portrayed the Maternal Ewok who held a wokling in her arms. Her father and many other actors of short stature were featured in that scene. Category:Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi actors
