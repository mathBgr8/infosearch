Ylanda Captison
1802

Ylanda Captison was a female Human who was the daughter of Senator Dol Captison and Marga Captison and the sister of Gaeriel Captison. Like the rest of her family, she was a follower of the Religion of the Cosmic Balance that dictated for every good, there had to be an equal bad (or vice-versa), lest the universe fall out of alignment. As such, when in 2 BBY Gaeriel was chosen to attend the Bakur Senatorial Academy and eventually join the Bakuran Senate, Dol and Marga gave Ylanda a golden alms bowl&mdash;since her sister would become a senator, to preserve the Cosmic Balance, Ylanda was to become a beggar.
