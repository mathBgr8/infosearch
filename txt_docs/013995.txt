It was close to Begeren and Ashas Ree and was located on the Kamat Aegit. The Sith employed huge alchemically mutated silooths to wreak havoc on the world before warriors came to finish the battle. Afterward, the Sith left a horde of silooths on Kalsunor, which continued to prey upon anything they could, and even larger and angrier silooths than those encountered abroad were rumored to exist in Kalsunor's deep canyons. The Battle of Kalsunor was the only known instance of more than a token use of these beasts, whose ferocity may have been too much even for the ancient Sith.
