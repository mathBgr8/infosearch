Hive Palace
1651

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Hive Palace was a large fortress on the planet Cheelit that was owned by the J'feh Lady Dhol during the era of the Galactic Civil War. It contained a chamber specially designed for the playing of the strategy game firepath. The Hive Palace once hosted the Sith Lord Darth Vader after a firepath challenge from Lady Dhol, only for Dhol to hire a Guild of Vindicators assassin named Clat the Shamer to kill Vader. Clat left a trail of stormtrooper corpses across the grounds of the Hive Palace, but Vader anticipated Dhol's treachery and killed them both in the palace's firepath chamber. The Hive Palace appeared in Dark Lord's Conscience, a comic story written by Alan Moore and illustrated by John Stokes, and originally released in 1982's The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 155.
