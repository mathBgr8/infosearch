Doom Mission
5183

"Doom Mission" is a comic strip written by Archie Goodwin and illustrated by Al Williamson, originally published from January to April 1983 by the Los Angeles Times Syndicate. It was republished by Dark Horse Comics in Classic Star Wars 14 and Classic Star Wars 15.
