Three Ellas Run
2506

The Three Ellas Run was a hyperlane located in the Tapani sector. This route connected the planets Sorella, Dorella, and Pernella, which were all ore mining worlds located in the Freeworlds Territory. The route was connected to the Procopian Shipping Lane at the Tavya system. A minor hyperlane branched off of the run at Dorella, connecting it to the Doris system. Like all hyperlanes in the Tapani sector, the hyperlane was difficult to navigate due to space debris which drifted into it. The Three Ellas Run was first mentioned in Lords of the Expanse, a supplement written by Paul Sudlow and Chris Doyle and released by West End Games in 1997 for the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The route was also mentioned in the Player's Guide to Tapani, which was released at the same time by West End Games, and was also written by Sudlow, this time with Eric S.
