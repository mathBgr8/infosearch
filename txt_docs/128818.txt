Marty
1450

Marty was the deck officer aboard the New Republic Defense Force Fifth Fleet flagship Intrepid as staff officer to General Etahn A'baht for the duration of the fleet's deployment in the Farlax sector during the Black Fleet Crisis. He greeted General Han Solo onboard upon his arrival prior to the Fifth Fleet's departure from Coruscant and also greeted General A'Baht upon his return warning him of General Solo's presence onboard. *Before the Storm *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:New Republic Defense Fleet officers
