Battle of Sullust (Delvardus campaign)
2174

A battle took place in 5.5 ABY, when a desperate Sander Delvardus, leader of the Eriadu Authority, tried to capture the planet Sullust to stop the New Republic expansion in the galaxy. Delvardus was roundly defeated by the Sullustan captain Sien Sovv who launched a counterattack campaign for the control of territories of the Eriadu Authority. *The Essential Atlas *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Battles of Sullust Category:Battles of the Delvardus campaign
