150px|thumb|[[Vetter Piin, a Rov.]] Rov was a religious title employed on the planet Ventooine. It was used to denote the highest ranking members of the church on Ventooine. The only known Rov was Vetter Piin who helped end the rule of the Satabs. During the height of the Galactic Civil War, Rov Piin took to caring for a shrine built in honor of Han Solo who helped kill the last of the Satabs. *Star Wars Annual (1977) 2 Category:Religious titles
