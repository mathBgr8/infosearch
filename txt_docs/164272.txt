Hapes refugee camp
464

The Hapes refugee camp held the refugees from Coruscant during the takeover by the Yuuzhan Vong. It consisted of countless rows of durasilk tents that housed a multitude of species. The camp stood out as a likely target to the Yuuzhan Vong. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey Category:Hapes locations Category:Refugee camps
