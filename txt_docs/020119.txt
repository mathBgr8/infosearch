Following an attack on the Republic world Bakura, Separatist forces advanced with the intent to take control of the Republic-aligned world. While also discussing battles at Bespin and Rhen Var, Jedi Master Mace Windu of the Jedi High Council stated to fellow Master Yoda that Ossus would fall within days. Ossus did eventually fall into Separatist control. The Galactic Federation of Free Alliances historian Voren Na'al mentioned the Separatist taking of Ossus in his historical archive, The New Essential Chronology, published in 36 ABY.
