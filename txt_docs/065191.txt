Baronis
538

The Baronis were a family that lived on the planet of Peekoine. The group consisted of the main family, as well as Madame Baronis and an especially paranoid old man. The main family was vacationing when a group of Rebels were visiting Peekoine searching for two Jedi Knights. Category:Families
