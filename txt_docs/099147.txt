Makin
1679

Makin was a Mon Calamarian admiral serving the Galactic Alliance during the Second Galactic Civil War. Makin was the commanding officer of the Third Fleet. Like Admiral Cha Niathal, the Supreme Commander of the Galactic Alliance Defense Force, Makin was known to be an aggressive warrior.
