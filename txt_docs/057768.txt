Galladinium Galactic Exports
2471

Galladinium Galactic Exports was a major technology supply company. Led by the Duros Galladinium at some point from 50 BBY to 47 BBY on the planet Lenthalis, Galladinium Galactic Exports worked itself into one of the galaxy's premier corporations. Their inventory was made known throughout the galaxy via the yearly publication, Galladinium's Galactic Datalog of Fantastic Technology.
