Staraker
2365

}} The Staraker was an experimental, cutting-edge starship designed by the Galactic Empire and created on the planet Foundry. It was stolen by Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa and Mici Shabandar of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. It crashed after being shot down over Alashan by a Death Star-like beam of destructive energy. After teaming up with a stranded Imperial team and dispatching the creature responsible for them both being there, Luke and the rest got the ship up and running again and left the planet, taking the ship back to the Rebel Alliance as originally intended.
