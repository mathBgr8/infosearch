Blue Brubb
833

The Blue Brubb was a cantina in Mos Espa on Tatooine. It was known to serve the drink, Ruby bliel. It was located in a part of town controlled by Jabba Desilijic Tiure. *Episode I: Anakin Skywalker *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cantinas Category:Mos Espa locations
