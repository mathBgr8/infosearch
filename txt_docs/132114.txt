Vor'corkh
1902

Vor'corkh was a Noghri dynast who encountered Leia Organa Solo, the Mal'ary'ush, in 9 ABY. When the maitrakh of clan Kihm'bar brought a convocate of Noghri people to the Grand Dukha in Nystao, Vor'corkh spat at Leia and said that she spoke only words of discord and poison.
