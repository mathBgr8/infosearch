The planet was located in the Arkanis sector in the Slice portion of the Outer Rim Territories. The planet was a seven-day hyperspace journey away from the Perlemian Trade Route, like the rest of the Pii system, and along the Old Corellian Run and the Trans Gulf Route hyperlanes. The planet, along with the rest of the Pii system, was originally located on the super-hyperroute known as the Corellian Run. When that hyperspace route changed its course at some point between 11,000 BBY and 4000 BBY and as a result no longer ran through the Pii system, the planet remained on the Old Corellian Run. The Pii system's first planet was first mentioned in "The Greel Wood Haven," an article written by C. Robert Carey and featured in the sixth issue of Star Wars Adventure Journal, a roleplaying magazine that was published by West End Games in May of 1995 for their Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
