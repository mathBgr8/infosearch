Mara (M4-78)
1140

Mara was a Jedi Master who was killed by droids on M4-78. She was the Master of the Twi'lek Jedi Tourath. Mara was one of the original characters of M4-78 before the planet was cut from Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords.
