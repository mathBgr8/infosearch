|type=Miniature Hardback |pages=128 |dimensions=7 x 8.5 x 1.5 cm |weight= |isbn=0762403217 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Who's Who: A Pocket Guide to the Characters of the Star Wars Trilogy is a miniature-sized reference book released in October 1998. It is a brief character guide written by Ryder Windham and contains pictures and descriptions of the characters from the Star Wars original trilogy. A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away, Luke Skywalker and a heroic group of Rebels battled Darth Vader and the evil forces of the Galactic Empire . . . Now the unforgettable characters of the Star Wars trilogy come to life again inside this pocket guide! Learn all about Han Solo, Chewbacca, Princess Leia, Yoda, and many others—from Admiral Ackbar to Zuckuss.
