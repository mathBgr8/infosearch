Damarind Corporation
1445

}} Damarind Corporation was a jewel merchant conglomerate. They had an exclusive contract with the Galactic Empire to harvest Corusca gems from the Yavin system. They eventually went bankrupt when their gem collection station, the Damarind Fishing Station, failed to collect enough gems. For some time, the company's biggest competitor was Spagga Core, Incorporated.Star Wars Adventure Journal 10 *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Conglomerates
