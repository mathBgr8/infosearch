Victory Forum
1412

The Victory Forum was an arena building in the town of Death, then known as Dying Slowly, on Jubilar around 12 BBY. It was named by the winning side of one the planet's numerous wars. It was brightly lit and had hundreds of rows of seats which allowed for up to 20,000 to view barbaric sporting events.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia It hosted the Regional Sector Number Four's All-Human Free-For-All venue. By 19 ABY the area surrounding the structure had become derelict slums, while the Forum itself was an open mass of rubble. *Star Wars Encyclopedia *Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Buildings
