Bhu Cranna
952

Bhu Cranna was a young Sorrusian, son of the chief of the tribe, Goq Cranna. He and his father showed Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi and Astri Oddo location of the hiding place of the bounty hunter Ona Nobis. *Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment *Jedi Apprentice: The Dangerous Rescue *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Sorrusians
