FE-3PO was a 3PO-series protocol droid active during the Galactic Civil War. Programmed for Human-Cyborg relations, FE-3PO was also an expert on the other prominent individual droids and droid models in the galaxy at the time. *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:3PO-series protocol droids
