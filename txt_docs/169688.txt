Alliance Search & Rescue Teams
544

Alliance Search & Rescue Teams were groups of Rebel operatives dedicated to locating and liberating prisoners of the Galactic Empire. The teams were responsible for the discovery of prisoners from Echo Base such as Kupalo and their subsequent rescue from VTR-LX, where they were being held. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic military units
