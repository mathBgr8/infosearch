Liah'starn
484

Liah'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the southeastern part of the planet's eastern continent. Mesdri'starn was to be found to the northwest, and Rhan'starn to the east-southeast. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
