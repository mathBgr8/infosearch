G-12 power blaster
791

The G-12 Power blaster was a heavy blaster designed by the Galactic Alliance to combat the Killiks of the Colony. They were used by Jedi Masters Mara Jade and Luke Skywalker during Battle of Sarm in the Swarm War. Unlike a typical blaster, the G-12 required a separate, dedicated power pack. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Blaster weaponry Category:Personal heavy weaponry
