Vaynai Archipelago
709

thumb|right|200px|The cutaway diagram of a typical Vaynai Archipelago island. The Vaynai Archipelago was the main landmass on the Outer Rim resort world of Vaynai. Made up of a series of large mesas, the island chain included Streysel Island, among others. While the rich businesses and resorts of that world were built on top of the mesa-islands themselves, the poor were relegated to a series of artificially-created caverns that were carved throughout the high outcroppings.
