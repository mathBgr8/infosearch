Lanthe Artifice

Lanthe Artifice was a company that produced droids for the Noble Houses of the Tapani sector. Its sister company, Danthe Artifice, Ltd., and itself were once based on the planet Lanthe until the local Moff started nationalizing corporations. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Droid manufacturers
