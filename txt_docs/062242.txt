Belinda Vog
1958

Belinda Vog was the owner and operator of Vog Salvage, Inc. She led a team of salvagers to the Graveyard to follow a rumor that the Aldera Royal Palace had somehow survived the Destruction of Alderaan and that the wealth of the building was contained in one of the asteroids in the Graveyard. However, when Vog's team arrived at the asteroid field, they discovered that the rumor was unfounded. *Graveyard of Alderaan Category:Business owners and executives Category:Females Category:Humans
