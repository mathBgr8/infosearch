Antilles Four-Step Instant Speech
1998

The Antilles Four-Step Instant Speech was devised by Wedge Antilles after his promotion to the rank of general, and the host of diplomatic functions the grade came with. Forced to take the podium and address people much more often than he'd like, he designed a rough schematic to improvise a speech in a matter of seconds. Wedge made use of it many times, including his arrival on Adumar, where he confided his 'trick' to his close friends Tycho Celchu, Wes Janson and Derek Klivian. As the name implies, the "Antilles Four-Step Instant Speech" is a simple collection of four reminders, useful to improvise a successful speech. &ndash; Part One: remind the audience who the speaker is in case they had forgotten. &ndash; Part Two: remind the reason behind the speaker's appearance. &ndash; Part Three: say something personal, to affirm that the speaker had been paying attention to his audience and make a good impression. &ndash; Part Four: finish the speech while the good impression from point Three lasts and before the speaker makes a fool of himself. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Speeches
