Subjugator (Imperial II-class)
2391

Subjugator was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer in the service of the Galactic Empire. She attempted to ambush the Rebel frigate Redemption, which had the Imperial defecter Commander Zaletta onboard, together with the Immobilizer 418 cruiser Restrainer. Unfortunately, the ambush failed thanks to Rebel pilot Ace Azzameen, with assistance from the Alliance cruiser Liberty.
