Star Wars Weekly 106 is the one hundred sixth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on March 6, 1980. It included the original story "My Enemies Surround Me!". *"My Enemies Surround Me!" *"Tales of the Watcher: Heroes of the galaxy Part 1" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 16: Reflections in a crimson eye!" *"Tales of the Watcher: The end of the universe!" *Star Profile: Peter Cushing A6
