Jori Daragon's armed courier
1885

Jori Daragon stole an armed courier starship from Cinnagar to pursue her brother Gav Daragon and Naga Sadow to Primus Goluud. Gav managed to warn her of the pending destruction of the star by Sadow, and Jori escaped in the ship, along with the Tetan fleet, and penetrated the Sith Empire. Using the ship, Jori helped destroy the Sith fleet. *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 4 Category:Tetan starships
