F-Web Repeating Blaster
1625

The BlasTech F-Web repeating blaster was the successor of the famous E-Web heavy repeating blaster. As such, it possessed increased power and was outfitted with a shield that could be operated by a third person. Like the E-Web, it served an anti-infantry role, as well as being collapsible, allowing for quick deployment.
