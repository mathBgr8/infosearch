Iron Tether
904

The Iron Tether was a Galactic Republic Space Rescue Corps patrol cutter. In 22 BBY near the end of the Separatist Crisis, it was attacked by Separatist C-73 Tracker starfighters while patrolling the Cowl Crucible near the edge of the Lahara sector. The captain of the Iron Tether took the ship into the Crucible when their shielding was hurt; although the crew had to receive anti-radiation treatment, they all survived. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Patrol craft Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
