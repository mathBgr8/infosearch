Cat Staggs
1341

Cat Staggs is an artist who worked on over 130 Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith sketch cards for Topps, as well as the Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Trading Card Set. She has also worked on a tutorial and activity for StarWars.com, in addition to illustrating the short story "Fists of Ion." She began her Star Wars work after meeting Stephen J. Sansweet, who is Head of Fan Relations at Lucasfilm, at a convention in Chicago. Category:Card game artists
