Brixus Aldine
1437

Brixus Aldine was a male Human who served as a Guard Captain for House Melantha. The soldiers under his command were known as the Aldine Brigade. Although not a noble, Brixus' family had long served House Melantha and he was a great supporter of the nobles that he protected. He had substantial combat experience having seen service in several little-known border skirmishes. He was known as cunning and merciless adversary.
