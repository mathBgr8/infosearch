Tal-Yannar Najjan
796

Tal-Yannar Najjan was a New Republic medic assigned to Delta Base. Najjan treated the injuries of New Republic operatives Kichiir the Wookiee, Oollj, and Cith Sninif following their attempt to stop the theft of Meleenium from the Qua'Tahc mines on Af'El.
