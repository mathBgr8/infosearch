Enton Adelmaa'j
1420

Enton Adelmaa'j was a male Chiss pilot designated as Twin Eight in Twin Suns Squadron during the Battle of Esfandia. During the battle he helped Jagged Fel with his "surprise" for Grand Admiral Pellaeon to secure the northern flank. He was then part of the strike team sent to the surface of Esfandia for the double-cross masterminded by Jaina Solo and Tahiri Veila. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Chiss Category:Males Category:New Republic Starfighter Corps personnel
