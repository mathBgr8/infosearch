|birth=c. 32 BBY |death= |species=Rodian |gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Legislative Youth Program |masters= |apprentices= }} Needo Farr was a male Rodian from Rodia who served as a member of the Legislative Youth Program during the Separatist Crisis. In 22 BBY, Farr and his fellow trainees traveled to Chandrila's capital, Hanna City, for a simulation of the vote on the Military Creation Act, a bill that authorized the creation of a Republic army. Unlike Naboo delegate Dashé Borreno, he voted in favor of the act. The "Yes" side won by a margin of 52 to 48 percent. Needo Farr made his first and only appearance in the HoloNet News Vol. 531 50 article Legislative Youth Program Role-Plays Vote. It was published in 2002 on the HoloNet News website.
