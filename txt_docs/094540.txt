Narcotic incense
1965

Narcotic incense was a substance that when burned produced an aromatic smoke that was capable of stimulating or depressing the nervous systems of beings who inhaled it. The various states of euphoria that a user experienced during this process gave narcotic incense a highly addictive quality. However, one notable drawback of the use of narcotic incense was that death was a symptom of prolonged exposure to it. Narcotic incense made its only appearance as one the various airborne substances that Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa inhale when they enter a tavern in the novel Splinter of the Mind's Eye. Though the novel did not expound upon just what it was, the entry for narcotic incense in the first edition of A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, which reached two sentences in length, considerably increased the depth of knowledge regarding the topic. Narcotic incense was later given a profile in a sidebar regarding a number of illicit drugs in the article "Underworld: A Galaxy of Scum and Villainy."
