He had served in the New Republic Defense Fleet prior to Ben Skywalker's birth, and later in the Galactic Alliance Defense Fleet before joining the Galactic Alliance Guard as part of the crew of the Anakin Solo. Their mission was to find out what had become of Jaina Solo and Zekk after the Jedi Knights' mission to Terephon.
