United Tribes of Kariek
1958

The United Tribes of Kariek was a planetary state on Kariek located in Wild Space near the Unknown Regions, formed by the United Tribes Agreement which ended the fighting between numerous Eickarie tribes, and was governed by the InterTribal Council. Around 22 ABY, the United Tribes Command thwarted the Warlord with military support from the Empire of the Hand.
