The Doobage
891

The Doobage was a YT-1300 which was owned by HoloNet News presenter Rogan B.B. Cham. The ship itself was painted in a pearlescent white color with fluorescent pink and green headlights. The inside of the ship was retrofitted for Cham's job as a HoloNet News presenter with a camera set on the top of the cockpit and an editing suite in the cargo area. The ship was used by Cham to transport a group of Rebels into the Cynestra Space Craft Show by hiding them inside cargo, which was unloaded on the station.
