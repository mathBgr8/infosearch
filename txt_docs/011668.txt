During the ensuing 1750 BBY battle on the Outer Rim Territories planet Malrev IV, Murrtaggh sneaked into the Dark Underlord's territory and assassinated the Sith, although in the process he himself fell to the dark side of the Force. By the time of the reign of the Galactic Empire more than a millennium later, the history of Murrtaggh and the Dark Underlord had become immortalized in a tale told by a Bimm bard whose repertoire of stories and songs focused on the exploits of the Jedi.
