|intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Hardcover |pages=32 |isbn=9780756611576 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline= |series=DK Readers |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: What is a Wookiee? is a Star Wars book by Laura Buller and Kate Simkins that was published by Dorling Kindersley at reading level 1. It is hosted by C-3PO, who describes the Star Wars universe in a form accessible to small children. A canon reissue of the title was released on January 19, 2015 with new cover artwork and additional cover spreads throughout the book, adding 16 pages of content not found in the previous edition. Star Wars DK Readers help kids learn to read while enjoying the stories and characters from the Star Wars universe. Find out all about furry, friendly Wookiees, helpful droids and other aliens with this Level 1 Star Wars DK Reader What is a Wookiee? Level 1 Star Wars DK Readers are read-together stories for children just beginning to learn to read. These stories use short simple sentences, simple vocabulary, word repetition and large type to facilitate learning.
