Veltraa
2156

The Veltraa was a Republic Hammerhead-class cruiser under the command of Dallan Morvis in service during the Mandalorian Wars. It was named to honor the service of Admiral Jimas Veltraa to the Galactic Republic. Under Dallan Morvis' command, which was his first, the Veltraa participated in a defense formation around Coruscant aimed at preventing Zayne Carrick from landing on the world.
