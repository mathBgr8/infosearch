Gwiezdne wojny Komiks
3673

Gwiezdne wojny Komiks (Star Wars - Comic in Polish) was a magazine published in Poland by Egmont from August 1999 to the middle of 2000. The magazine consisted of two or three Star Wars-related comic issues, along with several short articles. Eleven issues were published in total, before the magazine's run was cancelled. Although Egmont stopped publishing Star Wars comics in Poland altogether in 2002, it is currently planning to begin Star Wars-related publications once again, starting with a September 20th issue of the new magazine series called Gwiezdne wojny Komiks. Despite the change in title, most of the internal features will remain the same, except for a lack of comics.
