Thebeon 8
1621

Thebeon 8 was a planet located within the Thebeon system of the Core Worlds with a pro-New Order government. Its ruler was styled "Most High Proctor." Thebeon 8 was explored sometime between 15,000 BBY and 8000 BBY. Thebeon 8 boasted a population of 1 million to 10 million sentients. *"Princess Leia, Imperial Servant" *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in the Core Worlds Category:Planets in the Southern Core
