Volume 6 collects Star Wars: Republic issues 65-71. It was published by Dark Horse Comics on July 20, 2005. From the space station headquarters of the Bounty Hunters' Guild, to untamed worlds on the edge of the galaxy, the Jedi must go where the Clone Wars take them.
