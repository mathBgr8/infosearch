Mallett appeared in Tales from Jabba's Palace which was published December 1995 by Bantam Spectra and is about Bubo the frog-dog, who ended up as a B'omarr Monk. Buboicullaar is yet another of the dregs within Jabba's palace plotting the gangster's demise. Bubo thinks he has an advantage over his adversaries in that many assume him to be an unintelligent creature – but his disadvantage is that he is in fact not very bright. After Jabba's death, the B'omarr monks disembrain Bubo.
