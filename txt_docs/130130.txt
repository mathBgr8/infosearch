Brannij
1667

Captain Brannij commanded the Imperial II-class class Star Destroyer Bastion. During the Battle of Kathol System, Brannij arrived to protect Kathol from the New Republic attack, led by Bothan commander Kursk Mal'ia. However, when the battle began to turn against the Imperial forces, and Brannij realized that the commanding Moff, Kentor Sarne, had no interest in saving anyone but himself, Brannij created a diversion and took the Bastion and the remaining Imperial ships and fled into hyperspace. *The Kathol Rift *Endgame Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy captains of the Galactic Empire Category:Males Category:Personnel of Moff Sarne's forces
