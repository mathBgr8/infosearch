It was the origin point of the group known as the Poraskors, who reportedly wove neural-web reflex threads by hand. Betal was mentioned in Cracken's Rebel Field Guide, a datafile authored by General Airen Cracken that documented various pieces of equipment utilized by agents of the Rebel Alliance. Betal was first mentioned in the 1991 sourcebook Cracken's Rebel Field Guide, which was written by Christopher Kubasik for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
