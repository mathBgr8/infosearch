Star Wars Missions 4: Destroy the Liquidator
7330

Star Wars Missions 4: Destroy the Liquidator is the fourth adventure book in the Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game. It was published by Scholastic in December 1997. The Imperial Star Destroyer Liquidator is quickly approaching the Rebel base on Yavin Four. It is part of an Imperial plan to control hyperspace—and crush the Rebellion. To prevent the Empire from carrying out this evil deed, the Rebels must locate the Imperial forces without being discovered. Your mission: to stop the Liquidator&hellip;and get out of Yavin Four alive! May the Force be with you. *Mission Type: Rebel The Liquidator arrives in the Yavin system, and begins scouting the habitable moons in the area, starting with Yavin 8. Meanwhile, the Millennium Falcon enters the area and prepares to destroy an Imperial probe sent to Yavin 4. It does so, and Captain Han Solo transmits a fake message to the Liquidator, claiming that Yavin 4 is clear. Meanwhile, Captain Skeezer escapes from his cell in the Massassi temple, and hijacks his Carrack Cruiser. As he leaves, he fires on the Millennium Falcon, forcing it to land. The Rebels realize that they must stop Skeezer before he reaches the Liquidator. Along with Q-7N, a Rebel sets off after Skeezer in a Rebel starfighter. Skeezer is forced to land on Yavin 8, where he attempts to contact the recently established Imperial garrison there. The Rebel, however, destroys the garrison, but is captured by the Liquidator. On board the Imperial Star Destroyer, the Rebel escapes with the aid of Sevenen, and destroys the ship. Skeezer is killed in the process, but Termo and Tix escape.
