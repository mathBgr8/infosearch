Slaag Lado
1177

Slaag Lado was a male Draag wanted by the Mon Calamari Civil Authority for creating a seismic shock pulse. In 32 BBY, this created a rift in the Salinrerian Sea, causing large amounts of environmental damage and perpetual rain.
