Mharsup
2391

Mharsup was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by the Galactic Empire. In 3.5 ABY,The New Essential Chronology it had a mission to evacuate a TIE Defender research and development station, since the station's defenses were crippled. Tan Maarek Stele in a TIE Defender protected the bulk freighter from attack by Zaarin's forces, who were attempting to destroy the facility, and the TIE Defender prototypes, which were loaded into the freighter by technicians from the shuttle G'nabgib. The freighter then made it into hyperspace.
