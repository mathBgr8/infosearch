Pelican
1818

Pelican was a Muurian transport owned by scavengers. Its task force once attempted to capture an Imperial malfunctioning Viper probe droid that was stranded in the Saila Na system. However Ace Azzameen on Otana also attempted to capture it on Aeron's behalf and the two forces had a skirmish in the vicinity.
