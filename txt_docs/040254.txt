Gopso'o
1563

The Gopso'o were a tribe of Drovians from Nim Drovis. Though virtually identical to their rival tribe, the Drovians, the two were at war for longer than either side could remember. If a Drovian that Ism Oolos interviewed was to be believed, Gopso'o seemed to enjoy killing innocent family members of their Drovian enemies.
