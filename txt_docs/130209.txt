Magnepulse bomb
806

Magnepulse bombs were a piece of equipment carried aboard the inquisition module of a modular taskforce cruiser.Dark Empire Sourcebook It was a clean weapon that prevented the enemy from retaliating with modern weaponry. The bombs were the same size as a standard TIE Series starfighter, and any starship capable of carrying fighters could transport magnepulse bombs. It was a one-shot weapon, meaning it was mission expensive due to the sophisticated technology. *Imperial Sourcebook *Dark Empire Sourcebook Category:Bombs Category:Electrical weaponry
