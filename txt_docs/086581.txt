Meenon
1239

Meenon was the King of Senali in 44 BBY, and the father of Yaana. The laws of Rutan and Senali made the kings of the planet and the moon exchange their firstborns when they were seven years old, in order to avoid a war. When Leed, King Frane's son, was kidnapped by some accomplices of Taroon, Frane blamed the Senali, and the two kingdoms were near a war. Finally, King Frane accepted Leed's wish to stay in Senali and agreed with Taroon to be his successor and the future King of Rutan. *Jedi Apprentice: The Shattered Peace *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Kings Category:Planetary leaders Category:Senali
