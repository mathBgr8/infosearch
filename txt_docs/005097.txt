Shobquix Yards
1790

Shobquix Yards produced the Toscan 8-Q starfighter, one of the precursors to Koensayr's Y-wing starfighter. The fighter was so notable that the company was sometimes referred to as the Toscan corporation. Secrets of the Sisar Run names the makers of the "Tocsan Q-8" as "Tocsan" corporation. The fighter's name was, however, changed in later sources to "Toscan", probably owing to a typo. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Star Wars Sourcebook, Second Edition *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Star Wars Gamemaster Screen, Revised *Pirates & Privateers *Secrets of the Sisar Run *The Force Unleashed Campaign Guide Category:Shipwrights
