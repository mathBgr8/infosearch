Security and Intelligence Council
1735

The Security and Intelligence Council, also known as Palpatine's War Council, or the Military Advisory Council, was a committee of the Galactic Senate during the Clone Wars. It consisted of Supreme Chancellor Palpatine's most trusted advisers, though membership changed often. Senator Jannie Ha'Nook was a ranking member, along with Vice Chair Mas Amedda, Sate Pestage, Armand Isard and Kohl Seerdon. *Jedi Trial *Star Wars Annual 2009 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Senate organizations
