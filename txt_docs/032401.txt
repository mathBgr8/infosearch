Eport
813

thumb|250px|Inside the Eport auction house Eport was the galaxy's largest auction house, found in the wondrous city of Bidamount, where beings from across the galaxy could bid on obscure and often suddenly valuable merchandise. Eport claimed a large percentage of the final price as well as the opening bid, and many believed that additional funding came from secret and sinister sources. Sholh Dorr sold 3B6-RA-7 at Eport for over a thousand times what he had originally paid. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Structures
