Rrann Hhoss
610

Rrann Hhoss was a gaming event in Togoria centered in the use of sc'rath scimitars in combat and hunting.Alliance Intelligence Reports Young hunter Mlatar Thon Gra won a Rran Hhoss when his tribe was invited to travel with the Margrave's camp. When he won, he attracted the attention of smith Elotis, who offered him a job as his apprentice. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Sporting events
