Flames of Umate
815

The Flames of Umate were a religious cult on Coruscant. They were centered on Umate, the highest peak of the Manarai Mountains and the only outcropping of natural stone on the planet's surface. Their monks meditated while touching the rock in an effort to contact Coruscant's 'inner spirit'. They also patrolled the peak, preventing tourists from chipping off souvenirs from the stone. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Religious organizations
