|subscribers= |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=3 |series=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine }} Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 3 is the third issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in spring 1995. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 3, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with 1995 Topps Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back Widevision promotional card #0, a Topps Finest chromium AT-AT trading card, and a Star Wars milk cap featuring either C-3PO or Darth Vader. This issue also included the fold-out poster article The Essential Guide to Star Wars Aliens. *Rebel Report *Communiqués *Around the Galaxy *Design An Alien Contest *ComicScan: Marvelously Silly Aliens *Game Room: Where Aliens Are Fair Game *Star FX: Creature Creations *The Galactic Bazaar: Strange Tales of Collecting Aliens *Cyber Notes *Use the Force: All-Alien Trivia Quiz *Jae Lee *Lumiya: Dark Star of the Empire *A Book Excerpt: Children of the Jedi *All-Alien Poster *Star Wars: Tales from Mos Eisley 03
