Ewoks (1985) 10
3970

Ewoks 10 is the tenth issue of Marvel's Ewoks series of comic books. It was published under the Marvel Comics Star Comics imprint on July 29, 1986 with a cover date of November 1986. This book is the second part of a story that began in Droids (1986) 4. It contains the chapters "The Demons of Endor" and "A Race in Time." Surrounding the main cover image&mdash;a portrait of Wicket&mdash;the cover of this issue features a frame of several Marvel Comics characters including Spider-Man, the Avengers, the X-Men, X-Factor, the Fantastic Four and more as they existed at that time. This cover frame celebrated the 25th anniversary of the creation of the modern Marvel Universe with Fantastic Four #1.
