Grahrk (disambiguation)
561

Grahrk was a surname used by several Devaronians. *Vilmarh Grahrk, a notorious mercenary, smuggler, and gambler, who worked with Quinlan Vos upon occasion. *Holmar Grahrk, a mercenary who often worked with his cousin Vilmarh. *Olmar Grahrk, Holmar's son, who worked with his relatives during the Yinchorri crisis. *Dvakvar Grahrk, a Jedi Knight during the Jedi Civil War.
