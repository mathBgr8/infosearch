Warrior (Star Destroyer)
2170

The Warrior was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer set to rendezvous with an Imperial convoy carrying supplies and slaves by Mon Calamari at about 2 BBY. The Warrior arrived early only to discover a Rebel force attempting to liberate the slaves. In responce, TIE/LN starfighters were launched against the Rebels.
