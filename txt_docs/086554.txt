Outland Regions Security Force
2783

The Outland Regions Security Force, or Republic Outland Security Force, abbreviated ORSF, was a paramilitary force established by the planet Eriadu and the Greater Seswenna for the protection of the Seswenna sector. The organization was established as a response to the inaction of the Galactic Republic's Judicial Forces in dealing with pirates and privateers in the sector. The Security Force was founded using off-world loans, and were armed with ships built on Eriadu and Sluis Van.
