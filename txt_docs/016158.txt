Centered on the star Pyria, it included the planets Borleias and Pyria VI, the Blackmoon, and the Versieds asteroids. It was located only hours from the Mirit and Venjagga systems. The New Republic code-named it the Blackmoon system and Phenaru system. In 27 BBY, the Outbound Flight expedition traveled through the system on its voyage from Yaga Minor to the Unknown Regions. The system's fourth planet and only inhabited world was Borleias. During the Galactic Civil War, it was the base of operations for Imperial General Derricote. After the New Republic routed the Imperials, it fortified Borleias as a forward base even as ships sent by Warlord Zsinj began making reconnaissance raids into the system. In 27.5 ABY, during the Yuuzhan Vong War, Borleias served as a New Republic fortress. Its loss led to the Battle of Coruscant. The system was later the site of a significant battle, which saw the annihilation of a large Yuuzhan Vong contingent including the Domain Hul worldship and consequently a morally important New Republic victory.
