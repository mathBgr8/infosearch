Ballax
1727

"Shank" Ballax was a male Human guard who worked at the prison Desolation Alley during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He was the leader of Helmet Squad. He was wanted by the Hutts because his activities stymied their flow of money. His bounty was worth 5,000 credits, dead or alive. It was claimed by Jango Fett during the bounty hunter's search for Bendix Fust, a Mordageen wanted by Dug crime lord Sebolto.
