Ina'ganet'nuruodo
2685

Ina'ganet'nuruodo, also known by her core name Ganet, was a Chiss military officer and a pilot who served as a commander of the syndic's phalanx. Along with Chief Navigator Peita Aabe, she was one of the leading figures in an attempt to assassinate Assistant Syndic Soontir Fel in 28 ABY, but she was taken into custody when the attempt failed. Ganet was obviously opposed to Fel, and prepared to undertake extreme action against him. Fel identified her to his Jedi companions at the time as the "phalanx commander for a rival Syndic", implying that she led either a Household Phalanx under the authority of one of the Ruling Families, or else a Colonial Phalanx, the militia of one of the Chiss Ascendancy's twenty-eight colony worlds; but she claimed to speak on behalf of the front-line troops of the Chiss Expansionary Defense Force. Moreover, although the ostensible motive for her attack was an objection to the CEDF's own decision to aid the Jedi in their search for Zonama Sekot, the Chiss diplomat Aristocra Chaf'orm'bintrano later implied that more complex factors were at work.
