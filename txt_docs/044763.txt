|species=Human |gender=Female |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Colders Watch *Alliance to Restore the Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Chessa Dohhenty was a Human female who served as First Mate aboard the bulk freighter Colders Watch. She was in a relationship with the vessel's Chief Engineer, Dirk Harkness, and was also a member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. During a stop at Kelada starport to smuggle blasters to her Alliance contacts, General Halomar Corros and Captain Tony Franco, she was discovered by a squad of stormtroopers and killed in front of Harkness.
