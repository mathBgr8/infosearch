Yinchorri (language)
781

Yinchorri was the native language of the Yinchorri species from Yinchorr. Most Yinchorri were fluent in both Yinchorri language and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. "Uhanayih" meant "Within World" in the language; "rolk-mangir" meant "horned death." *Jedi Council: Acts of War 4 *Alien Anthology *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages Category:Yinchorri culture
