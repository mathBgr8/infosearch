Tony DeZuniga
1616

Tony DeZuniga was a comic-book artist from the Philippines. He drew a few of Marvel's Star Wars covers and worked with writer Roy Thomas on a serialized story arc, titled The Keeper's World, for the Marvel Comics teen magazine Pizzazz.
