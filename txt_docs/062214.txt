|haircolor=Gold |feathers= |eyecolor=Red |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Bog moon of Bogden |habitat=Swamps |diet= |language= }} Sungwas were massive, doglike pack scavengers native to the bog moon of Bogden. Resembling a nightmarishA Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded cross of wolf and weasel, they were quadrupedal creatures with yellow skin covered in golden fur and a pair of red eyes. A pack of sungwas inhabited an abandoned freighter marooned in one of the moon's swamps that became a base of the pirate Gir Kybo Ren-Cha. After imprisoning Gerin Toda, the daughter of Lord Malameu Toda, aboard the starship, Ren-Cha attempted to use the pack to dissuade her from escape her cell. Despite this, Toda was able to fight off the sungwas with the help of Jann Tosh and C-3PO, who, along with Jessica Meade and R2-D2, were sent by King Mon Julpa of Tammuz-an to liberate Toda, while R2 led away the pack and manipulated them into attacking Ren-Cha, who was pursuing the trespassers through the moldering ship's bowels.
