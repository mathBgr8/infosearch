Vibroblade (starship)
1931

Vibroblade was a Skipray Blastboat that operated as part of the Khuiumin Survivors' pirate forces. It was used to board luxury liners and other larger vessels that had been targeted for plunder. In 11 ABY, the Vibroblade, along with Rock Squadron, participated in a raid on the CR90 corvette Galaxy Chance, the objective of which was to capture the former Imperial Navy captain Zlece Oonaar, who had commanded one of the two Victory-class Star Destroyers that had carried out the original Bastiv massacre.
