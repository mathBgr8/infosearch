Trucemaker
1993

Trucemaker was a ship in the Galactic Alliance Fourth Fleet as of 40 ABY. Trucemaker was the flagship of Rear Admiral Gavin Darklighter, and when Admiral Ratobo was killed and his flagship Peacebringer destroyed at the Second Battle of Balmorra, it became the flagship of the Fourth when Darklighter assumed command. It was presumably also present when Darklighter and the Fourth again faced the Confederation Fleet at Kuat shortly thereafter. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flagships Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
