M-68 planetary magnapulse cannon
2369

The m-68 planetary magnapulse cannon was a planetary weapon emplacement designed by Kuat Drive Yards and used by Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. The m-68 planetary magnapulse cannon was built with a large underground generator and a large rotating plasma cannon above the surface. The cannon fired plasma balls at the enemy vehicles, causing massive electrical disturbances that disabled the targets for a time. Originally designed as a surface-to-space weapon, m-68 turned out to be more effective at surface-to-surface combat. The need for a surface-to-space weapon resulted in creation of the w-165 planetary turbolaser.
