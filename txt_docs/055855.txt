Dorr Farm
1759

The Dorr Farm was a moisture farm located on the planet Tatooine and owned by Sholh Dorr. Before the Clone Wars, bantha poodoo was found covering perimeter sensors on the Dorr Farm. Shmi Skywalker Lars wrote in her holojournal that with the growing numbers of Tusken Raiders getting closer to the land near the Lars homestead, Cliegg Lars was going to start to organize with other farmers at the Dorr Farm.Tatooine Ghost When Cliegg left with his son Owen in the morning on their way to the Dorr's farm, they found Shmi's footprints among those of Tuskens, as she had been captured collecting mushrooms. *Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones novelization *Tatooine Ghost Category:Moisture farms Category:Tatooine locations
