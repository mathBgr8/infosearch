Arcona Mineral Harvest Corporation
1582

|associations= |parent= |subsid= |headquarters= |locations= |role=Mining |products=Azurite |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=Commerce Guild }} Arcona Mineral Harvest Corporation was a mining company. Unlike their competitor, Offworld Mining Corporation, they respected their employees, who shared in the net profits. Around 45 BBY, the new chief operations manager, Clat'Ha, tried to get some of Offworld's workers by buying their contracts. The plan led to bitter infighting between the two companies.
