Pizzazz 16
1039

Pizzazz 16 is the sixteenth and final issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated January 1979 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. The issue includes the article "The Special Effects: How They Do It," discussing special effects in science fiction films, including Star Wars. 16
