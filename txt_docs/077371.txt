Machines of War
4056

Machines of War was the fourth Hasbro/Toys "R" Us&ndash;exclusive comic book published in 2002. During the opening stages of the First Battle of Geonosis, Jedi Master Yoda jumps on board a Republic Gunship. When the crew comes under attack by droid starfighters, the clone-trooper crew uses a grappling hook to grab one and slam it against another. They then continue the fight, but Yoda is afraid for the future.
