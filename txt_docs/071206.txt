**Galactic Senate *Confederacy of Independent Systems |masters= |apprentices= }} Moje was a Sephi male who served as an Associate Planetary Representative to Senator Navi during the Clone Wars. When King Alaric and his people joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the galactic conflict, Moje and Navi planned to kill Grand Master Yoda of the Jedi Order and Council during the Battle of Thustra. After Navi was arrested for his attempt to have Yoda killed, Moje escaped and was placed onto the "wanted" list. *Jedi: Yoda *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems individuals Category:Galactic Republic bureaucrats Category:Males Category:Sephi
