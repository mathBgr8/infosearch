Bravo Three was the callsign of a pilot in Bravo Flight during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, Arven Wendik served as Bravo Three on the oldest N-1 starfighter in the Naboo corps around the time of the Battle of Naboo, and survived that conflict. thumb|220x220px Bravo 03
