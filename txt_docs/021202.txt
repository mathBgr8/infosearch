Free-floating fungal fondu
560

Free-floating fungal fondu was a food delicacy enjoyed by Ugors. The chef droid L9-G8 instructed the rebel crew of the Bonderium to cook this dish for an Ugorian feast, in order to bribe ArrGack and GrrKack for admission to the Paradise system. "Fondu" may be similar to fondue, a traditional treat involving melted cheese. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Ugorian food
