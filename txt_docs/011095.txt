Pulsar Supertanker
879

Pulsar Supertanker was a company allied with the Trade Federation. It was not as consumed with the bottom line as the rest of the Federation, which led to its expulsion from the organization. It was a young Nute Gunray's testimony to the Trade Federation Directorate against the company that first brought him to the attention of Darth Sidious. *Cloak of Deception *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: The Official Starships & Vehicles Collection 33 *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Companies
