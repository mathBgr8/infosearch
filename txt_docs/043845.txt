Doman Beruss was the name of two related Senators: *The male Senator Doman Beruss of Illodia, see Doman Beruss (Illodian). *The female Senator Doman Beruss of Corellia, see Doman Beruss (Corellian). *Both of them were named after Doman Beruss, their ancestor.
