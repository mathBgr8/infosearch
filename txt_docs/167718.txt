First Bodyguard
915

The First Bodyguard was the title of the Jedi leader of the Imperial bodyguard. Before the Jedi Rebellion and the replacement of the Jedi by the Knights of the Sith, the First Bodyguard was Kane Starkiller. In the first draft, Kane is renamed Akira Valor. He remains the same character, however, in all other respects.
