|companies= |website= }} }} Sean Stewart is an American-Canadian author. Stewart made his first entry into the Star Wars universe with the Clone Wars novel Yoda: Dark Rendezvous. According to Stewart, he had been interested in writing a Star Wars story that started with the phrase "the Force is weak in this one" since he was twelve years old. As he was finishing Yoda: Dark Rendezvous, specifically two days after he turned it in according to his later recollection, he moved onto the Halo project I Love Bees, which lead to Stewart drawing links between the Jedi Order and Halo Spartans, which affected how he wrote I Love Bees. He was surprised that he was able to find this connection, pointing to the origin story of both groups in that both Jedi and Spartians began their new life as children. {| ! Year !! Title !!
