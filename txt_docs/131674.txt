Diplomatic Services
1712

Diplomatic Services was a branch of Imperial Intelligence's Bureau of Operations. A catch-all branch, it provided personnel for trade and diplomatic missions assigned to Imperial garrisons, and advisory and political experts for governments and Moffs.
