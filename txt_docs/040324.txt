Dorthus Tal Prison
1777

The Dorthus Tal Prison was a prison on the Core World of Sacorria. It was located on Dorthus Tal Island, the same island as Sacorria's capital Dorthus Tal City and the warden was Kedar Blackgate.Coruscant and the Core Worlds, pages 143-144 It was powered by geothermal energy from a volcano. It had the reputation of being "escape proof."
