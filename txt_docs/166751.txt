It was released on January 8, 2014. After a defeat by Rebel forces and the escape of prisoner Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader decides to clean house. From one end of the galaxy to the other, Vader's "new broom"&mdash;a squad of elite stormtroopers backed by the Dark Lord's own lightsaber&mdash;is sweeping the ranks of the Empire!
