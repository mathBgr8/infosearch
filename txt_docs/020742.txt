In 32 BBY,Dark Force Rising Sourcebook, which is set during the events of Dark Force Rising, states that Anselm is 41 years old. The Essential Reader's Companion dates the latter work to 9 ABY, so Anselm must have been born in 32 BBY. the Human male Anselm was born at Baffop. At some point before the Battle of Yavin, the forces of the Galactic Empire ravaged Baffop, leading to extensive civilian casualties. After witnessing the devastation of his homeworld, Anselm became a member of the Rebel Alliance, which opposed the Empire. By the time of Imperial Grand Admiral Thrawn's campaign against the New Republic in 9 ABY, Baffop was situated in space either conquered by or allied with Thrawn's confederation. Baffop was first mentioned in Dark Force Rising Sourcebook, a 1992 supplement authored by Bill Slavicsek for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
