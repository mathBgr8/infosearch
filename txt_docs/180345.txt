Gall City
2048

Gall City, also known as Gall Spaceport, was an Imperial-class spaceport catering to civilian, corporate, and Imperial Military needs on the Outer Rim Territories moon of Gall. The nearby Cadavine Sector Fleet Imperial Enclave protected the starport. Boba Fett utilized the area while being pursued by Dash Rendar. Other mercenaries, particularly IG-88D, were also involved in the chase.
