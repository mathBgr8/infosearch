Eremay Ies
1551

Eremay Ies was the daughter of Daxar and Beda Ies. When Eremay and her mother returned home one day, they found Mara Jade, who at that time was serving as Emperor Palpatine's Hand, searching through Dexar's desk for a list of bank accounts which he had placed two billion credits of the Emperor's personal funds into. Mara told Beda and Eremay to disappear forever, and they left for the Unknown Regions and became Joiners. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Joiners
