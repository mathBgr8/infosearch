Bothan Friendship Award
509

The Bothan Friendship Award was an award given by Bothans to non-Bothans who exhibited stealth and other Bothan activities. Koth Melan and members of the Bothan Spynet honored Ace Azzameen with this award after installing a listening device to an Imperial Communications satellite. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Awards F
