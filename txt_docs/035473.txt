Homanan
1174

The Homanans were a mysterious race of shape-changing sentients from the Homana system. They could change their size from anything the size of a Wookiee to that of an Ewok and were also slightly telepathic, as well as altering the pigment of cells within their flesh. Mimicking clothes, or anything skin tight, was an easy feat. Nana was a member of the species.
