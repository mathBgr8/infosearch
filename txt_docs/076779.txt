Jason Marsden
2110

Jason Marsden (born January 3, 1975) is an American voice actor who voiced Rosh Penin in Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy and numerous characters in the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series such as Dustil Onasi and Brejik. Marsden is most recognized as the voice of Max Goof, son of classic Disney character Goofy, in the 1995 animated film A Goofy Movie in addition to the majority of post-1995 appearances of the Max Goof character, as well as the voice of Haku in the English-dubbed version of the 2001 Japanese animated film Spirited Away.
