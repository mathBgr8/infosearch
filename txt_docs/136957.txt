Lucasfilm Animation Singapore
1439

right|150px Lucasfilm Animation Singapore, a division of Lucasfilm that opened on October 27, 2005. It is a digital animation studio that produces Lucasfilm's digital animated content for films, games and television. Lucasfilm Animation Singapore is responsible for the creation of the animated series Star Wars: The Clone Wars in conjunction with Lucasfilm Animation.
