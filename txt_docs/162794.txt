Empress Teta's Crown Jewels
1796

thumb|right|150px|[[Empress/Legends|Empress Teta in her Crown Jewels.]] The Crown Jewels of Empress Teta were a collection of weapons, armor and jewelry worn by Teta during the Unification Wars and Hyperspace War of 5000 BBY. thumb|left|100px|Symbol worn on Empress Teta's [[Armor/Legends|armor and on the hull of her fleet ships]] The empress wore a set of two fighting knives and battle armor even when not in battle because of the constant threat of assassination. During the coup which brought the Krath into power, a loyalist of the old dynasty stole the artifacts and hid them in the system. Only a few of the Jewels were recovered by the Krath, but over the ensuing millennia, most of them had been discovered. As of 11 ABY, the Crown Jewels were considered a Wonder of the Galaxy, and the only major missing pieces were Teta's knives. The Tetan Royal Family offered a reward of 1 million credits to anyone who could return the knives to them. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Gemstones Category:Jewelry artifacts Category:Wonders of the Galaxy
