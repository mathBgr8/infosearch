Bruce Jones
474

Bruce Jones has been a comic-book writer for over 30 years, best known for his horror comics published by Marvel Comics in the 1970s. He scripted the four-issue Dark Horse Comics adaptation of Star Wars: A New Hope Special Edition in 1997. He is also credited as writer for Tales from Mos Eisley: Heggs' Tale. Category:Comic authors
