Doublefield generator
516

The Doublefield generator was a specialized type of shield generator that was produced by the Prexton company. It was employed aboard Loronar transport ships as a type of cargo container. The generator creates two opposing spherical forcefields that serve as a transport vessel for cargo. *Imperial Sourcebook Category:Prexton products Category:Shield generators
