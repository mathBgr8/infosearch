Embassy Mall
559

Embassy Mall was a shopping center on Coruscant. It was located midway up the dome of the Nicandra Counterrevolutionary Signalmen's Memorial Building, and was open to the sky. During the Battle of Coruscant, the owners refused to allow in any civilian refugees fleeing the fighting. *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Coruscant commercial buildings
