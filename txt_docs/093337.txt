Assault of the Aturi Cluster
2438

The assault of the Aturi Cluster was a battle fought during the Galactic Civil War resulting in a defeat for the Rebel forces by the Scimitar assault wing. The Aturi Cluster had possessed a notable Rebel presence, large enough that it warranted a full-scale assault by the Imperial Navy. The Rebels had set up powerful system of anti-atmospheric defenses however, which forced the Scimitar assault wing to come up with and utilize a new tactic developed by Captain Tomax Bren. Dubbed the Vertical Swoop, this maneuver proved very useful against the network of ground-based defenses. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles of the Hook Nebula campaign
