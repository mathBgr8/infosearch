Zeboron Delta

Zeboron Delta was a building located on the planet Shador, in the burrough of Fink's Hole. It provided a back-entrance into Zeboron Gamma, through a doorway and up a staircase. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, Zeboron Delta was a pile of rubble. *Scoundrel's Luck Category:Buildings Category:Shador locations
