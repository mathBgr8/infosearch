Casus Belli (magazine)
12583

Casus Belli (from Latin casus belli 'occasion for war')casus belli is a French roleplaying magazine published since 1980. The magazine, initially published by Excelsior Publications, has articles written by numerous authors who also produced work released by Jeux Descartes, with the earliest archived website for Jeux Descartes attached to Excelsior Publications.
