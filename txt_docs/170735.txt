|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Shard of Alderaan" was a live-action roleplaying adventure from Live-Action Adventures. The game was designed for ten participants, and lasts three to four hours. The Shard of Alderaan - a slicer program able to decode Galactic Empire scandocs has been stolen and taken to Tatooine. The Empire, the Rebel Alliance, and various third parties must meet aboard the Dune Princess.
