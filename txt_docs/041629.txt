Kudana
2016

Native to the planet Rutan, the Kudana were herd animals. Their pelts were prized for the metallic colored sheen of their skin, and the silky mane that ran down their shoulders. Their skin was extremely tough and could be used to make body armor, clothes, and furniture. Several efforts to breed them on other planets failed. They stood almost two full meters tall, and were built with powerful muscles. Their mouths were massive, with flat teeth used to crush wood and grass to pulp. Kudana had incredible strength, and sharp hooves used for defense and for digging for roots. A very select group of hunters are allowed to hunt them, but only at a great fine.
