Mandalorian flashpistol
581

A Mandalorian flashpistol was a crude blaster weapon used by the Mandalorians. It was a precursor to the blaster pistol. The Mandalorians used them along with swords and axes and would carry these on their Basilisk war droids. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:Blaster pistols Category:Mandalorian weapons
