The humanoid male Ronet Coorr was a senator from the high gravity Inner Rim planet of Iseno. Coorr represented his homeworld in the Galactic Senate. He was close to the office of Supreme Chancellor Palpatine and was a supporter of the proposed Republic Military initiative. Ronet Coorr was created for the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002.Star Wars Year by Year: A Visual Chronicle Early production notes for the film referred to Coorr as "Male Senator #2." Coorr was first mentioned in the online article "Palpatine forms Loyalist Committee," published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #49, part of the HoloNet News website. The news issue, written by Paul Ens and Pablo Hidalgo, was released on March 28, 2002. Coorr was first identified as the character appearing in Episode II on May 17, 2002 with the character's StarWars.com Databank entry. A story involving Coorr and Onaconda Farr dealing with their resignation from the Senate was later overwritten by the events of the Star Wars: The Clone Wars television series, which saw Farr killed off while still a member of the Senate. In 21 BBY, during Operation Durge's Lance, Coorr was bribed by fellow Committee senator Onaconda Farr to redirect the Naval fleet at Iseno to Farr's native Rodia. As the Iseno fleet was meant to reinforce the planet Duro, the fleet transfer served to quicken the fall of that planet to the Confederacy.
