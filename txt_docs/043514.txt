right|thumb|An EWHB-12 with an optional seat attachment The BlasTech EWHB-12 was a model of heavy repeating blaster that was utilized during the Clone Wars. The weapon could be disassembled into sections for ease of transportation and setup. The EWHB-12 had an extremely high rate of fire, while possessing average range and accuracy.
