During the new Separatist Crisis that engulfed the galaxy in 41 ABY, Las Lagon was represented in the Galactic Alliance Senate. However, with the outbreak of open war against the rebellious factions, world Las Lagon along with the planet Beris seceded from the GA and joined the growing Corellian Confederation. Both worlds had the same reasons for seceding; they were unwilling to commit troops to the conflict. Nevertheless, according to Supreme Commander Cha Niathal, these worlds had at most twenty thousand troops, and their militaries were badly trained and poorly equipped. Therefore, the Galactic Alliance could afford to refuse the assistance of their soldiers. On the other hand, the Chief of State Cal Omas believed that granting a waiver to the armed forces of both worlds could be seen by the Confederation as a sign of weakness in the principle of the Alliance’s pooled defensive capability. Instead, Omas arranged a meeting with the ambassadors from Las Lagon and Beris, together with the diplomatic corps, to prevent further spread of separatism.
