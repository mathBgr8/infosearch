Thila Command
3170

Thila Command was the official designation applied to Alliance High Command's temporary base of operations on the Outer Rim Territories planet of Thila. After the Alliance to Restore the Republic evacuated the Great Temple on Yavin 4 six months after the Battle of Yavin, High Command, in search of a suitable site to permanently relocate to, established itself within the catacombs of the planet Thila, already an established Rebel base.Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back The newly formed Rogue Group, under the command of Luke Skywalker, permanently assigned to the High Command group, likewise transferred with High Command to the Thila base. Renegade Flight, under the leadership of Commander Arhul Narra, were similarly assigned to Thila. The Alliance employed electrotelescopes as an early-warning system at Thila Command. The passive sensors detected incoming light and energy emissions, making them capable of being used without fear that the outpost would be discovered.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology High Command only operated out of Thila for a period of months before being forced to evacuate with the threat of its secrecy potentially compromised. Rogue Group performed admirably during several skirmishes during the Thila campaign, assisting High Command in their evacuation efforts, proving their effectiveness. When Imperial forces descended on the Thila outpost, they were ambushed by previously placed explosives.Star Wars: Empire: The Wrong Side of the War After Skywalker recommended the remote ice world of Hoth as a possible relocation site, High Command agreed to set up operations there.
