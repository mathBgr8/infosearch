After stealing Saesee Tiin's lightsaber, he paid her for its return. She was later captured by Confederate forces, who believed her to be a Republic spy along with Tiin. She helped him recover his lightsaber from them, and the two managed to escape. Na-Jia first appeared in the short story It Takes a Thief, which was one of four stories in the sixth volume of the Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures trade paperbacks. She was created and penciled by Matthew and Shawn Fillbach.
