|type=Paperback strategy guide |pages=496 |dimensions=22,8 x 18,8 x 2,1 cm |weight=1.84 pounds |isbn=0-7615-4219-1 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided: Prima's Official Strategy Guide is a strategy guide for Sony Online Entertainment's MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided. It was published by Prima Games in July 2003, simultaneously with the original release of the game. Due to continuous updates and changes, two expansions and one complete overhaul of the game (the so called "New Game Enhancements"), many parts of this strategy guide eventually became outdated and described features that were no longer in the game.
