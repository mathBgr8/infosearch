Hoguss
1110

Hogusses were sentient beings with heavy bodies. During the Mission to Ansion, sanctioned by the Jedi High Council and conducted by Jedi Master Luminara Unduli, Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Padawans Barriss Offee and Anakin Skywalker, a Hoguss wielding a killing ax attacked the two Mirialan Jedi at Cuipernam. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
