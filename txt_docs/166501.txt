The Ellstree Bar
1005

}} The Ellstree Bar was a cantina located on the main street of downtown New Hope Settlement on Ladarra. Drinks included cold lum, and droids were welcome as proclaimed on its repliwood entrance sign. Despite its appearance of being deserted, the bar was home to the last generation of the planet's failed colony of mostly Humans. Rivo Xarran became known as "The Storyteller," as he would gather the children round and tell tales from the past, his own included, when he retired to the world in an attempt to flee Boba Fett.
