Chedaki
2258

Captain Chedaki was a Human male who commanded a Rebel blockade runner during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, Chedaki carried Rebel leader Leia Organa on board his ship on a mission to the remote Shiva system, investigating a strange amount of Imperial interest in the obscure region. Upon arrival, Chedaki and Organa attempted to do short-range scans of the inhabited Shiva IV in the blockade runner's shuttle, only for their vessel to be immediately crippled by unseen anti-matter bombs. Chedaki was killed in the blast, although Organa survived the crash to the planet below.
