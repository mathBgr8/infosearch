Fuce
1049

Commander Fuce served in the Galactic Empire. He commanded a small garrison of stormtroopers on Dantooine around 1 ABY. He served the Empire as the base Commander at the garrison established on Dantooine, during the years following the Battle of Yavin. *Star Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Stormtrooper officers of the Galactic Empire
