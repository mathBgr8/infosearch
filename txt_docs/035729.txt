Heedon
1583

|masters= |apprentices= }} Heedon was a Human male who hated his own first name enough to never use it. Heedon owned and operated the Starcrossed, a luxury liner during the Galactic Civil War, but as few could afford to book passage due to Imperial oppression, Heedon joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic.
