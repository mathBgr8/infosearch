Vindar
2356

The Vindar were a species of white insectoids with four arms and a tough . They possessed large black beady eyes, and they were able to fire powerful purple energy blasts from their hand. Long after the death of Luke Skywalker, they oppressed a species they referred to derogatorily as the Squidge. The Vindar considered all things not of themselves to be inconsequential, and worked to destroy the culture and heritage of those they oppressed. They were also responsible for the "death" of C-3PO after they blasted his remaining parts to oblivion. The Vindar were an insectoid species with a white exoskeleton and large, black beady eyes. They had six limbs, four arms and two legs. Their limbs were quite thin. The Vindar possessed substantial strength, being able to throw a full grown humanoid with relative ease. Individuals usually had 3 fingers, and were capable of firing powerful blasts of purple energy from their hands, said blasts being able to blacken and kill a humanoid with one blast.
