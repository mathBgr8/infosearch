|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} B-2 was a landing platform situated in the city Joventusek on the planet Caarimon. Circa 21 BBY,The Living Force Campaign Guide states that the Living Force campaign is set one year after the Invasion of Naboo, an event that is dated to 32 BBY by The New Essential Chronology. As the Living Force adventure Philology moved the campaign forward ten years to the time of the Clone Wars, Belly of the Beaast can therefore be dated to circa 21 BBY. the Believers Sith cult docked a transport laden with an anti-Caarite virus in the bay.
