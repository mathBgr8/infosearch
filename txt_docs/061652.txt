LV Labs
1314

LV Labs was a pharmaceutical company that offered a laser-burn medication ointment that was designed to relieve the aches and pains caused by Imperial blasterfire. The salve was also helpful to those recovering from carbonite encasement. One of LV Labs' employees was Farsa Zirl. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Force and Destiny Core Rulebook Category:Medical companies
