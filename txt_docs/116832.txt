Evilo Nailati
1596

Evilo Nailati was a B'omarr Monk who lived in Jabba's Palace. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, Evilo had become "enlightened", and his brain was removed from his body per common B'omarr practice. Unlike many of his fellow monks, Evilo did not seek concepts like "truth" or "enlightenment", and continued to communicate with the outside world in whole sentences rather than images. He instead spent his days amassing more information on the universe, something he could not do in a normal lifetime.
