EVS-6962
1489

EVS-6962 was an EVS Construction Droid that was active on Coruscant shortly after Rogue Squadron helped bring the planet under New Republic rule. Under the supervision of Wedge Antilles, EVS-6962 uncovered a secret Imperial Interrogation Center during its routine tear-down assignment.The Essential Guide to Droids This was later discovered to have been built by Emperor Palpatine to test prisoners for Force-sensitivity, as it contained several Force detectors that Luke Skywalker used during his search for new Jedi.Jedi Search If the identification number for EVS-6962 was entered into a touch-tone phone in the Brooklyn area, it would dial the They Might Be Giants , which was a purposeful insert by Dan Wallace into The Essential Guide to Droids. *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:EVS Construction Droids
