Knights
2431

Knights was an MC80 Star Cruiser belonging to the Rebel Alliance. The cruiser along with a Bothan Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser called Doomer were attempting a secret meeting. However the Imperials had sent a strike force consisting of Missile Boats, one of the starfighters piloted by Tan Maarek Stele, TIE Avengers and Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports to capture the Rebel Cruiser and destroy the Bothan Dreadnaught.
