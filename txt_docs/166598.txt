Fortuna (disambiguation)
644

Fortuna could refer to: *Bib Fortuna, a male Twi'lek who served as Jabba the Hutt's majordomo. *Beezer Fortuna, a Twi'lek who was active around the time of the theft of the Death Star plans. *Fortuna City, the capital city of Edan II. *Bib Fortuna, a male Twi'lek from Ryloth that served as a majordomo to Jabba Desilijic Tiure. *Komad Fortuna, a Twi'lek hunter that was on Tatooine during the Jedi Civil War. *Ob Fortuna, a male Twi'lek from Ryloth who was a distant relative of Bib Fortuna.
