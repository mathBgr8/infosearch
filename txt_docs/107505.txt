Galactic News Network
1365

Galactic News Network (GNN) was a large media corporation that reported on the events taking place in the Outer Rim during the New Republic eraThe Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia. GNN's smaller bureaus would take local news stories and transmit them via courier newsdroids to the rest of the galaxy.
