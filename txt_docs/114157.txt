Mirrix
1611

Mirrix was one of the wives of King Sha-Mar Ma-dred of Kashir. Before the clone wars Anakin Skywalker and his master Obi-Wan Kenobi were sent to a meeting with King Sha-Mar Ma-dred. As the jedi approached the king, Mirrix and the other wives (Tiarri, Kheann and Ashala) were present. The wives left before the meeting to do as they wished. That night she heard as Tiarri screamed "An Intruder". Kheann and Mirrix pushed Anakin to the ground and took out they're knives. Anakin explained that someone stole his lightsaber.
