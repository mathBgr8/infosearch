Blue Light
840

The Blue Light was a tavern that only served liquor in the Corellian Sector of Nar Shaddaa. The establishment was owned by Mich Flenn, a former smuggler. The interior was kept dark, and the walls were decorated with holo-posters of Corellian cities and natural wonders. Han Solo frequented the Blue Light and intially met former girlfriend Salla Zend there. *The Hutt Gambit *Rebel Dawn *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bars and clubs Category:Corellian Sector locations
