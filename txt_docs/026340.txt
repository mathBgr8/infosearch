Zho Krazhmir
1207

Zho Krazhmir was Supreme Commander of the Yuuzhan Vong forces in the First Battle of Zonama Sekot, and the one who agreed to take Vergere back to the rest of the fleet in return for calling off the attack. For his discovery of Zonama Sekot, he was granted a new leg implant. However, it never developed properly, and he died in his sleep from it several years later, long before the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Males Category:Yuuzhan Vong senior officers
