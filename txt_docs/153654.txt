Wynt Kepporra
1630

Wynt Kepporra was a Human male from Alderaan, a classmate of Delak Krennel at the Prefsbelt IV Naval Academy. Kepporra participated in the Thespian Union, and later became a captain in the Imperial Navy. An engineer, he was home visiting his family and died when the Empire destroyed Alderaan with the first Death Star.
