|pages=13 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYStar Wars Omnibus: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 1 |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issue= |next=X-Wing Rogue Squadron 1 }} X-Wing Rogue Squadron Special is a one-shot issue of the Star Wars Legends comic book series Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron. It was written by Ryder Windham, illustrated by John Nadeau, and first published on August 1, 1995 by Dark Horse Comics. The issue was available exclusively as a marketing premium for Apple Jacks cereal, and it was distributed through The Kellogg Company. A digital edition was published by Marvel Comics in 2015.
