Every Chiss citizen, along with the four or more Ruling Families&mdash;Csapla, Nuruodo, Inrokini, and Sabosen&mdash;were also represented in the Senate. The Chiss Senate was first mentioned as "Parliament" in the article "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Chiss," which was written by Juan Schwartz and published by Wizards of the Coast in the fifth issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine on July 24, 2001.
