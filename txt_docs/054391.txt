ReliaCharger power calibrator
2622

The ReliaCharger power calibrator was manufactured by Udrane Galactic Electronics and allowed a user to analyze and repair electronics. It could also be used as a portable source of power, capable of energizing smaller equipment for days at time, or larger devices for shorter durations. The ReliaCharger was a model of power calibrator manufactured by Udrane Galactic Electronics. A common tool, it was used to evaluate the performance of equipment such as droids and power generators,The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology and was capable of analyzing and repairing electronic circuitry. The ReliaCharger incorporated a circuit tester, connector rods, and a control computer, and was employed to maintain droids, vehicles, and starship computers. The ReliaCharger could also be used to provide emergency power to a range of common technological devices. It was capable of powering devices such as datapads and holoprojectors for several days. The Reliacharger was also capable of power larger technology, but for more limited periods of time.
