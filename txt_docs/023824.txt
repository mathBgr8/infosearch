It served as an Ordnance/Regional Depot outpost for the Galactic Republic during the Pius Dea Era, a period of time that lasted between 12,000 BBY and 11,000 BBY. At some point before the death of the Imperial Governor Wilek Nereus of theThe New Essential Chronology Wild Space &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Bakura system planet Bakura in 4 ABY, the smuggler starship Belle docked at Ord Zat. During the vessel's stay there, which its captain described as overly long, it became infested with the starship pests known as rat roaches. The ship's crew discovered the infestation after leaving the star system, and Nereus later recorded the captain's brief account of the visit at Ord Zat after the latter had been captured and interrogated. Ord Zat was first mentioned in The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook, a 1996 sourcebook published for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
