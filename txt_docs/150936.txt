Entana Run
860

The Entana Run was a hyperlane that was used to travel through the Lesser Plooriod Cluster, located in the Mid Rim. This route and the Burna Trade Route were used to mark the boundary of Urce Space. High Inquisitor Mox Slosin patrolled through this route, looking for Rebel agents in his area. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Mid Rim hyperlanes Category:New Territories hyperlanes
