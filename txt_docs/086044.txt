Tola (Swarm War)
1208

Tola was a Mon Calamari officer with the Galactic Alliance Defense Force onboard Admiral Ackbar during the Dark Nest Crisis. Tola protected Admiral Nek Bwua'tu when Leia Organa Solo used the Force to dispose of a sabotaged bust of the Admiral.
