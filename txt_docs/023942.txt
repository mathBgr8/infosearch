Wild Knights
3740

The Wild Knights was an ad hoc starfighter squadron, made up of Force-sensitive pilots, that was founded and trained by the Barabel Jedi Knight Saba Sebatyne. The members of the squadron were all trained in the use of the Force by either Saba or Eelysa.
