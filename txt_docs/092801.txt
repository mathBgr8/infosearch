Armored flight suit
1412

thumb|An armored flight suit The armored flight suit was a combat-ready light armor employed by space-faring types ranging from pilots to pirates and mercenaries. The suit provided protection against the vacuum of space for a limited time and came in various models including the Corellian TX-3 and Imperial TIE Suit. The armor cost 4,000 credits and weighed roughly 20 kilograms. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide *Stay on Target Category:Light armor
