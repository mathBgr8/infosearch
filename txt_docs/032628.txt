Erased
3817

The Erased were a group of people who did not want to be executed by the Galactic Empire for treason, so they erased all memory of their existence. They took refuge with Dexter Jettster, who was considered an unofficial leader, in the Orange District on Coruscant, one of the safehouses of the Erased was in Thugger's Alley and was headed by Dexter.The Last of the Jedi: Death on Naboo Many Erased members died when Duro exposed the location of Solace. However, Oryon and Trever Flume were able to escape along with Solace and Keets Freely, eventually learning that Curran Caladian had also escaped the devastation. One of the safe houses of the Erased that was locate don Thugger's Alley was eventually attacked by the Empire, who vaporized the alley. Luckily, Keets, Curran and Dex were able to escape before it was vaporized.The Last of the Jedi: Master of Deception However, they decided to lay low afterwards and find a new home, deciding to back out of Moonstrike, as they believed the Empire knew what they were hitting with that attackThe Last of the Jedi: Reckoning as they were communicating with resistance leaders to organize the first Moonstrike meeting. However, after Ferus Olin realised that Flame was an Imperial agent, he assumed the Empire's attack on Thugger's Alley was because of Flame reporting where the safe house was. Keets, Curran and Dex had found another colony of Erased a hundred levels below the Orange District in the old water reservoir's of Coruscant and went to join them, living on a house raft.
