Nov Gejjen
1153

Nov Gejjen was a Corellian politician and the father of Dur Gejjen. During the First Corellian Insurrection he opposed the Human League and Thrackan Sal-Solo. Nov befriended Chief of State Leia Organa Solo as Leia recalled nearly twenty-two years later.
