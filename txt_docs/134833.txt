Sez
1946

A fish species known as sez was native to the oceans of the Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Bothawui. The species' name was used as a family name by the Bothans, a sentient species native to Bothawui.Ultimate Alien Anthology Mak Sezala, a Bothan senator serving the New Republic during the invasion of the extra-galactic Yuuzhan Vong, had "sez" as part of his surname,The New Jedi Order: Star by Star along with the clan suffix "ala." The species known as sez was first mentioned in the Galactic Campaign Guide, a 2003 sourcebook written by Peter Schweighofer and J.D. Wiker for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game.
