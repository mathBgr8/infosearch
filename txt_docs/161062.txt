|last date=December 8, 2009 |media type=Hardcover |timeline=1006 BBY&mdash;980 BBYThe Essential Reader's Companion |num books=3 |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Star Wars: Darth Bane Trilogy is a Star Wars Legends novel trilogy from author Drew Karpyshyn and publisher Del Rey focusing on the life of the Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Bane. The stories include Darth Bane: Path of Destruction (2006), Darth Bane: Rule of Two (2007), and Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil (2009).
