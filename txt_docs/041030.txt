Spice Mines Avenue
786

Spice Mines Avenue was the name of a street found within the capital city of Kessendra on the planet of Kessel. Its original designation was unknown, but the name was changed to Spice Mines Avenue at some point during the reign of Emperor Palpatine. At one point along the route, it intersected with Slave Lord Boulevard. C-3PO and R2-D2 once passed Spice Mines Avenue on their way to rendezvous with Luke Skywalker after an espionage mission at the Kessendra Stadium. *The Glove of Darth Vader Category:Kessel locations Category:Roadways
