Beard-moss
746

Beard-moss was a moss species indigenous to Tenupe. It was known to grow in curtains on mogo trees. During the Swarm War Alema Rar used a curtain of beard-moss to conceal her StealthX in the upper reaches of a mogo tree during the Battle of Tenupe.Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War Category:Mosses
