Rob Williams
2150

Rob Williams is a Welsh author who wrote numerous Star Wars comics for Dark Horse in the 2000s. After penning several stories in Star Wars Tales from 2003 to 2005, he went on to write twelve of the sixteen total issues of Rebellion from 2006 to 2008.
