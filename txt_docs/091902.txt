Meter
27762

}} thumb|right|The average Ewok was one meter tall. The meter was an established unit of measurement of length. Starfighter pilots and their targeting computers used meters. Species like the Ewoks, the Lurmen, and the Jawas all had an average height of one meter, while the armored Sith Lord Darth Vader was slightly taller than two meters. Grav-ball was played on a 64-meter grid that was divided into eight octets. Meters were also divided into centimeters and millimeters. For very large scale measurements, 1,000 meters were put together to make one kilometer.
