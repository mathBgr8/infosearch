Kena Shont
1253

Kena Shont was a Rebel undercover agent working as a medical technician at BioTech Industries. Shont was assigned by fellow Rebel Saren Llalik to download secret data from Llalik's 2-1B droid to her personal datapad, and then give the information to the Rebel command.
