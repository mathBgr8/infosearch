|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=2 BBYAccording to the entry for Soontir Fel in The New Essential Guide to Characters, this event occurs two years after the Battle of Nar Shaddaa. However, later sources shifted the battle’s date to 4 BBY. Works published around the same time as Characters, such as The New Essential Chronology, instead placed the battle in 3 BBY, which aligns with the timeline presented in Star Wars: Empire issue 8, depicting the mutiny shortly before the Battle of Yavin. More recent references, including The Essential Reader's Companion, ultimately standardized the Battle of Nar Shaddaa as occurring in 4 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Empire |issue=9 }} Empire 9 is the ninth issue in the Star Wars: Empire series of comics. It was published on July 2, 2003 by Dark Horse Comics. As a newly commissioned officer in the Empire, Biggs Darklighter is assigned to the frigate Rand Ecliptic. In his new post he learns that expediency is preferable to honesty, and that the deaths of innocents is an acceptable price to pay for covering one's mistakes. Horrified by what he has seen of the Empire in action, Biggs and a circle of his close companions plot to desert their posts and join the Rebellion. But when their plan is uncovered by another officer, desertion becomes a fight for survival! Don't miss this stunning Classic Era story of how Biggs Darklighter and his boyhood friend, Luke Skywalker, came to fight side-by-side for the Rebellion!
