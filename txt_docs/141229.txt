|birth= |death= |species=Twi'lek |gender=Male |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Chabak's |masters= |apprentices= }} Quink was a Twi'lek who resided at Zirtran's Anchor. He was co-owner of Chabak's with his cousin, Padda. The two of them, who were relatives of Chabak's original owner, took over when he disappeared mysteriously. The older of the Chabak cousins, he had become very prosperous and had the reputation that his younger cousin desired. Forced into taking care of Padda, he wanted nothing more than to send his younger cousin back to Ryloth. Time and again, Quink had to bail Padda out of trouble. His best friend was Kara Reenogga, with whom he had several duels of colorful insults. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Twi'leks Category:Zirtran's Anchor residents
