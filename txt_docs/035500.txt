Blue-ringed sea crab
1045

The blue-ringed sea crab, a species of crustacean from the oceans of Tinallis, was the favored food of the large avians known as treshers. Since sea crabs reproduced so quickly, they had the potential to choke out the other creatures in the Tinallian seas without predation from the treshers. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, pollution had taken a toll on the tresher population.
