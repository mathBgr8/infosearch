Alliance War Museum
1062

The Alliance War Museum was an institution dedicated to the exhibition and preservation of artifacts and information related to the Rebel Alliance's military efforts during the Galactic Civil War. Located on Coruscant, the majority of its items were either donated by former Alliance members or recovered by Operation Flotsam under the Historical Battle Site Preservation Act, which also funded the museum.
