Corellian Horticultural Digest
1441

The Corellian Horticultural Digest was a gardening journal on Corellia, published on datacards.I, Jedi Its first issue was published nearly three centuries before the Battle of Yavin. &mdash; Terminal Mission "Horticultural Researcher Under Siege" The journal's articles covered horticulture, but its editorial staff also sent researchers all over Corellia's landscape to report on the planet's wild plant life. Rostek Horn wrote several columns for this publication, encrypting messages to Jedi in the genetic codes. In 1 ABY one of the journal's researchers was following a lead regarding trioecious cycad carpophylls when he stumbled into a lair of dangerous beasts. He hid in a safe place and called the journal's staff before his comlink was eaten.
