Milagro campaign
3552

A lengthy campaign occurred over the course of three months in 5 ABY on the planet Milagro. Milagro was a planet located at an important hyperroute junction and contained large-scale manufacturing facilities. The New Republic Army fought on the world for three months before the Imperial forces withdrew, initiating a Base Delta Zero operation to destroy anything of worth on the planet.
