Luxury-class shuttle
1409

Luxury-class shuttles were civilian passenger shuttles derived from the military Lambda-class T-4a shuttle. Many such shuttles were employed by Tinta Lines. They were equipped with the Astronav P127 Course Plotter and Chepat Supreme Defender deflector shields. Tinta Blue Seven was one such shuttle. *I, Jedi Category:Shuttle classes
