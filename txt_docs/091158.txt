Hokkor Long
1343

Hokkor Long was the Secretary in Charge of Scheduling for the Imperial Entertainers' Guild. In a communication to Viceprex Hirken on Stars' End, Hokkor Long apologized for rescheduling a visiting troupe of entertainers the Vice-President had requested.
