Unni Yerudi
1441

Unni Yerudi was the name taken by a claqa, or group mind, of Bilars. Like other Bilar claqas, Unni Yerudi was made up of several Bilars which, while capable of only animal intelligence on their own, formed a sentient collective intelligence when together. Unlike other claqas, which typically had four to seven Bilars, Yerudi was made up of ten Bilars. Due to the size of its claqa, Yerudi's intelligence went beyond what other species would class as genius level. It used this intelligence as an interplanetary con artist.
