Nubian Palace
904

The Nubian Palace was the most well-known resort on the planet Nubia. Inside the resort was twelve different casinos, four holovid theaters, a virtual reality suite, and the Solar Gardens. It was very luxurious and large, but unbeknownst to most of its customers, the Nubian Palace was actually the base of operations for Prall the Hutt. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Suns of Fortune Category:Hutt locations Category:Nubia locations Category:Palaces
