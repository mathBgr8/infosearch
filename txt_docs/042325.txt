Kreet'ah
2586

Kreet'ah was a Kian'thar Vigo of Black Sun during Prince Xizor's time. Kreet'ah had been trained from birth for criminal leadership and inherited his position as Vigo from his mother, just as his daughter was in line to inherit his position from him. Before assuming his position as a Vigo, Kreet'ah spent time on his father's derlac farm on his homeworld of Shaum Hil, corralling the cattle with a whistle as well as selling them for export. Kreet'ah used a vast network of spies to infiltrate the megacorporations that worked for the Galactic Empire.
