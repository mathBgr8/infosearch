Tyrimm Wyln
1322

Tyrimm Wyln was a female Togorian farmer. Along with her sisters, Tyrimm Wyln managed a farm. Wyln met Togorian master smith Mlatar Thon Gra and began a romantic relationship with him. Tyrimm embarked on a tour, visiting worlds such as Ord Klina and Zuliria. On Zuliria, Tyrimm was killed in the crossfire of an Imperial attack on a Rebel cell. Eventually, Mlatar left Togoria to search for Wyln.
