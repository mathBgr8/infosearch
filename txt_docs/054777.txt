Laureate of the EmpireGalactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide (or Imperial Laureate) was a great literary honor, bestowed by the Galactic Empire upon only the finest scholarly minds in the galaxy. The author Ebenn Q3 Baobab received the title of Laureate of the Empire on two separate occasions. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Empire awards
