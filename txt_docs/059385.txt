Icaris Tool and Drive
548

Icaris Tool and Drive sold parts and industrial intelligence to the Subpro Corporation. Rumors circulated that Icaris had attempted to steal the Onyx Star in order to steal the design, but no evidence ever appeared. They later went bankrupt just three years before the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Technology companies
