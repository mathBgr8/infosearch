Cardio-Muscular Package
2415

The Neuro-Saav Corporations Cardio-Muscular Package was a cybernetic package that included a Q-2 artificial heart and Ex-Musc enhancement. Designed to augment a recipient's heart, lungs, and musculature, the cardio-muscular package combined an artificial heart with Rybcoarse-based enhancements that allowed the muscles of the body to work harder with less effort. The end result granted enhanced strength, greater endurance, faster running speed, and the ability to withstand more damage. The package often came with a significant drawback, though; often a recipient would feel invincible, and would become brash and prone to self-destructive behavior in an effort to push themselves to their absolute limits.
