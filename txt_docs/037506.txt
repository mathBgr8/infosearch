Pleasant Meadow
1609

Pleasant Meadow was a marshland region of liquefied gases located at the core of the planet, Bespin near the Ugnorgrad Swampway. The only inhabitants of this region were the colonies of Ugnaughts that fled from Cloud City during the Imperial occupation and Ugnaught rebellion. Three years after the Battle of Yavin, Pleasant Meadow became the location of a minor skirmish between Imperial stormtroopers and Rebel Alliance forces. Lando Calrissian boarded one of the floatboat ships operated by the holojournalist news crew for Action Tidings and utilized the craft's belt drive as a makeshift weapon with which to repel the Imperial forces.
