Loo Raelo
2456

Loo Raelo was a renowned male Quermian Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order in the final days of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive male Quermian, Loo Raelo was from his species's homeworld of Quermia. After being inducted into the ranks of the Jedi Order, Raelo was trained in the ways of the Force, and over time attained the rank of Jedi Knight. A noted telepath among the ranks of the Jedi, Raelo's influence among the Jedi was dwarfed by fellow Quermian and Jedi High Councilor, Master Yarael Poof. Poof served on the Council during the Separatist Crisis, which caused strife among the Galactic Senate, and in 27 BBY sacrificed his life to end a threat against Coruscant devised by the terrorist Ashaar Khorda.
