Kalla's Stanchion
2541

The Kalla's Stanchion was a starfighter carrier in the Alliance Military during the early Galactic Civil War. The vessel was a heavily modified EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate. The vessel was enlarged to accommodate several hangars in the vessel's midsection and large enough to accommodate vessels as large as a YT-1300 light freighter. The vessel was stationed in orbit of Etti IV in the early parts of the Civil War.
