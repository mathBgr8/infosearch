Scay Danson
1651

Scay Danson was a member of the Imperial Security Bureau. A few months following the Battle of Yavin, Danson filed a report regarding the possible threat of the Bounty Hunters' Guild. Danson went on to become one of the galaxy's biggest industrial saboteurs.
