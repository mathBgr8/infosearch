Piringiisi

discusses Piringiisi being in Strongholds of Resistance |continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Piringiisi, which meant "healing lava", was a subterranean resort city on the planet of Sullust, famous for its hot springs and mud baths. Vacationers from across the galaxy would visit the resort, to relax and bathe themselves in the hot springs and therapeutic green mud. With private bathing pools as well as massage and other relaxation treatments, visitors of the Piringiisi resort could let their fatigues be whisked away&mdash;nearly literally.
