|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Jabba Desilijic Tiure |masters= |apprentices= }} Gela Yeens was an ill-tempered smuggler who earned credits by helping Jabba Desilijic Tiure collect his debts. Well respected for his uncanny anticipation in battle, Yeens was searching for Debnoli on Tatooine. Later, he accompanied Jabba the Hutt, in the company of Boba Fett, Boelo, another human and a trio of Rodians to Docking Bay 94. He and his fellow henchmen stood guard as Jabba confronted Han Solo about his debt. Gela Yeens first appeared in a deleted scene from Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which was released on May 25, 1977. The scene was added into the special edition of the movie, which was released in 1997.
