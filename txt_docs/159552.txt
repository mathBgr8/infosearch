Lightflash Squadron
935

Lightflash Squadron was a starfighter squadron that served with the New Republic Defense Fleet. Equipped with RZ-1 A-wing interceptors, they participated in the Battle of Adumar that pitted a combination of Adumari and New Republic forces against those of the Imperial Remnant.
