Greg Knight
854

Greg Knight is the cover artist who did the art for all the novels in the Republic Commando series and the concept art for Star Wars: Republic Commando and it has been confirmed that he did the cover art for the fourth book. He also created concept paintings and sketches for the canceled video game Star Wars: Imperial Commando and Star Wars: The Force Unleashed. *Republic Commando video game *Republic Commando book series *Rogue Leaders: The Story of LucasArts *The Art and Making of Star Wars: The Force Unleashed Category:Concept artists Category:Cover artists Category:Reference work artists
