Twilight Squadron (Rebel)
1321

Twilight Squadron was a Rebel Alliance SpecForce unit. It was one of the most reputable units of the infamous Eclipse Team. Major Sisquoc was the leader of Twilight Squadron, with Lieutenant Commander Adazian Liebke and Lieutenant Ma'w'shiye serving under him. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic Special Forces units
