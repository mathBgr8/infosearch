Heilan Rotham
1632

Heilan Rotham was a Human professor at the famous Lorrd University around the time of the Second Galactic Civil War. She was an old, wheelchair-bound woman with white hair, pale skin, and blue eyes. Doctor Rotham's field of specialty was tactile writing and recording methods, and she was considered the top scholar in her field in all of the galaxy.
