|published in=Star Wars Galaxies website |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Move It or Lose It is an online document informing Star Wars Galaxies players about the so-called House Pack-Up operation. It consists of a game-related F.A.Q. and a short in-universe narrative, primarily having the form of a conversation between Colonel Braedic Ekirk and an unnamed Imperial admiral who are devising a plan to demolish abandoned buildings around the galaxy.
