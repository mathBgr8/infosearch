The Boneyard
766

The Boneyard was the largest of the many piles of bones on Iego. Lying north of The Scatter, the Boneyard held gigantic skeletons of unknown creatures. Holes in the planet's surface lay beneath massive ribcages, inside of which Demons could be heard in their subterranean dwellings. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Iego locations
