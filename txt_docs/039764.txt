Mon Calamari Regiment
1145

Mon Calamari Regiments were employed by the Rebel Alliance. These Mon Calamari troops were especially trained for planetary defense, thus they were among the best of the Alliance military units when it came to defense against planetary bombardment. They were often assigned to garrison important systems because of this. *Star Wars: Rebellion *Star Wars: Rebellion: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic regiments Regiment
