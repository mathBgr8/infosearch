FS-1 Farshot sporting blaster rifle
1058

The FS-1 Farshot was a long-range sporting blaster rifle that was popular on the frontier. It was less powerful, but had longer range than most other sporting blaster rifles. It was particularly good for situation where the ability to hit the target at longer range was more important than doing great damage at closer ranges. *Arms & Equipment Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Czerka Arms products Category:Sporting blaster rifles
