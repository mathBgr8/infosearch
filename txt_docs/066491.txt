Red Rancor
761

The Red Rancor was a cantina on Coruscant. It had red lighting for the interior and had a bad reputation. In 19 ABY, Han Solo arranged for Luke Skywalker to have his bachelor party there. *Union 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cantinas Category:Coruscant dining establishments
