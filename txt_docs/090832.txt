Klagg tribe
1310

The Klagg tribe, also known as the Klagg clan, was a clan of Gamorreans native to Pzob. In 12 ABY they were captured along with Luke Skywalker, his companions, their rival clan, the Gakfedds and indoctrinated onboard the Imperial battlemoon, Eye of Palpatine. Their indoctrination was almost successful; the Klaggs learned to speak Basic and were turned into relatively good Stormtroopers. They constantly considered the Gakfedd's to be Rebels, and fought each other consistently.
