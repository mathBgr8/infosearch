Yorvin
1116

Lieutenant Yorvin was a female deputy to Siron Tawaler in the Toryaz Station security force as of 40 ABY. After Tawaler died, she took over as head of security and was given orders as to what she should do by Tycho Celchu and Wedge Antilles.
