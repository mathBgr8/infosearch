Buzz-Knuck
2108

}} Buzz-knucks were powered melee weapons. They were used by Imperial Dungeoneers. The buzz-knuck appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The buzz-knuck was a reward for being successful in the player versus player "battlefields" introduced with the release of the "Game Update 10," on June 2, 2009. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Far Orbit Project Category:Edged weaponry Category:Glove and knuckle weaponry
