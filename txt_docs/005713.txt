Pirates & Privateers
40091

Pirates & Privateers is a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Timothy S. O'Brien and published by West End Games in 1997. The book provides information on piracy and privateering in the Star Wars galaxy, in the context of brief tales and anecdotes about the Rebel Alliance privateer Urias Xhaxin. Pirates & Privateers gives players and gamemasters all the information needed to run a privateer campaign, featuring scores of new character templates, equipment, ship modification guideline, gamemaster characters, and a detailed index to pirate organizations, ships, and shadowports.
