Elaginn gang
822

The Elaginn gang was a group of spice-jackers and slavers operating in Hutt Space around 10 ABY. Originally under Elaginn, the group came under the control of Romort Raort after Raort killed Elaginn. The gang would disable their targets with an ion cannon they stole from a Rebel Alliance ship, then board and capture the cargo and crew, the latter of which they would sell into slavery. Under Raort, the gang built up influence with dockmasters on dozens of worlds, and was looking to expand its operations beyond Hutt Space. *Dark Empire Sourcebook Category:Enslaver organizations Category:Pirate organizations
