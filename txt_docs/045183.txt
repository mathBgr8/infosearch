Repulsor ball
1276

Repulsor balls were small, fast, and maneuverable antigravity spheres that were the primary armament of the Fromm Tower Droid which were owned by the crime syndicate Fromm Gang. The spheres could outrun a speeding vehicle at speeds of over two hundred and fifty kilometers per hour.
