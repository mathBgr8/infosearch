T-47I Airspeeder
2077

The T-47I Airspeeder was Incom's successor to the popular T-47 airspeeder. It went out of production around 10 BBY, but remained popular with adolescents and youths due to the ease of maintenance and prevalence of spare parts. The airspeeder was 4.5 meters long and required one pilot. It carried 10 kilograms of cargo but no passengers.
