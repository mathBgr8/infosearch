Furthermore, The New Essential Chronology places the beginning of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY and the creation of the clone army in 32 BBY, meaning that Two-Eight's death on Kamino occurred sometime between those years. |death=By 22 BBY, Kamino |species=Human Republic Commando: Hard Contact |gender=Male |pronouns= |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Niner's squad |masters= |apprentices= }} Two-Eight, also identified by the number RC-8028, was the name of a clone commando created on the Wild Space planetGeonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Kamino at some point during or after 32 BBY. As a male Human clone of the bounty hunter Jango Fett, Two-Eight stood 1.83 meters tall. Two-Eight was trained by Cuy'val Dar sergeant Kal Skirata to serve in the Galactic Republic's Grand Army. At some point before the outbreak of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY, Two-Eight was decapitated in a training exercise with their squad on Kamino as a result of their own mishandling of explosives during a rapid entry procedure. Although Two-Eight was quickly replaced with another commando, the loss of Two-Eight haunted the squad's sergeant, Niner, sometime after Two-Eight's death.Republic Commando: Triple Zero Two-Eight was first mentioned in the 2004 novel Hard Contact, the first novel in the Republic Commando series written by Karen Traviss.
