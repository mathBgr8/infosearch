Azul Squadron
1922

Azul Squadron was a starfighter squadron that served with the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. The unit, equipped with Z-95 Headhunters, was based on the Nebulon-B2 frigate Colada. It fought against the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Rage during the latter's attack on the Airam-owned supply platform in the Goff system. There, realizing that the most serious danger to the craft evacuating the platform came from the interdictor cruiser Compellor, the Z-95s made that vessel their primary target, attacking it with concussion missiles.
