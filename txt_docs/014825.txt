The planet exported Rathalayan firestones. Grand Admiral Grant, as part of his agreement to defect to the New Republic, was allowed to retire to a villa on this world, although Jan Ors kept an eye on him. The planet was also the base of Kaerobani and the Lumini Pirates: in Kaerobani's den, Mara Jade retrieved a stolen Jedi Holocron and escaped. Many tourists visited the wealthy world.
