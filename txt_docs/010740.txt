Kaiburr Crystal
2095

The Kaiburr Crystal was a type of cocktail. It was the rarest and most valuable cocktail in the galaxy. The cocktail was served in a camtono. The Kaiburr Crystal was served at the immersive bar Star Wars: Hyperspace Lounge aboard the cruise ship Disney Wish. The cocktail was served in a camtono. When it opened, it revealed four small silver cups with various cocktails. Outside of the small drinks, the customer also receives Star Wars merchandise, a bottle of "Skywalker Sommita Sparkling Wine," and a certificate that entitles them to a private four-person tour of the Skywalker Ranch and its vineyards. *Star Wars: Hyperspace Lounge Category:Cocktails
