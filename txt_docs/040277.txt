|height= |width= |affiliation=Confederacy of Independent Systems }} Fortress Kh'aris was a fortress built on Kintan by Kh'aris Fenn, sometime before his attempted return to Ryloth in 30 BBY. Count Dooku apparently was involved in its building. Kh'aris equipped his fortress with as many droids as possible, including AX/RX, although by 30 BBY he employed two Morgukai, Tsyr and Bok.
