700-Penetrator
1458

The 700-Penetrator was a military gradeDark Empire II 5 laser cannon developed around the time of Emperor Palpatine's return. The smuggler Lo Khan installed a pair of 700-Penetrators on his Xiytiar-class transport Hyperspace Marauder after years of flying his ship without armaments.
