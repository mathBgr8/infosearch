Night Herald
1285

Night Herald was a Killik title, bestowed upon Killiks and Joiners alike. It was given to Welk when he became a Joiner of the Gorog nest, becoming the guardian of the nest's Unseen Queen, Lomi Plo. Upon Welk's death at the hands of Luke Skywalker, Alema Rar, herself having become recently Joined, took up the mantle of Night Herald until her defeat at the hands of Leia Organa Solo and the destruction of the Dark Nest. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Unknown Regions Category:Killik titles
