Joth Katrane
1479

Joth Katrane was a male Human from Corellia who was present at Desolation Alley on Oovo IV during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by someone who cheated him out of his pension by reporting his 'extra' income to the warden. Eventually, his bounty was claimed by the Mandalorian-turned Bounty hunter named Jango Fett.
