Invitational Podracing Circuit
1382

The Invitational Podracing Circuit was a series of four podracing courses held on the Outer Rim planets of Ando Prime, Ord Ibanna, Oovo IV, and Baroonda featuring longer and more difficult realignments of the courses found in the Galactic Podracing Circuit. #Ando Prime Centrum #Abyss #The Gauntlet #Inferno The tracks in the Invitational Circuit were unlocked by finishing first in all the races of the Amateur, Semipro, and Galactic Podracing Circuits. Winning races in the Invitational Circuit would unlock the playable characters Slide Paramita, Bozzie Baranta, and Ben Quadinaros. *Star Wars: Episode I Racer *Star Wars: Episode I Racer: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Podracing courses
