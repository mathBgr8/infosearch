Tambles

|class= |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor=Beige and green |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor=Blue |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Endor |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Tambles were a group of diminutive sentient beings who lived on the forest moon of Endor. They bounced along the surface of rivers; without water, they would die. Tambles were small beings who required water to survive. When deprived of water, the Tambles became shorter and thinner. However, when they were exposed to water, they inflated and could bounce or float along the water in rivers. Tambles had webbed hands and feet, with three fingers on the hands and three toes on the feet. Additionally, Tambles had big blue eyes, with some possessing eyelashes.
