It shared its name with the Far Xandil sector, which bordered the Lambda sector in 0 ABY. Wanted by Cracken, a datafile composed by New Republic General Airen Cracken as a compilation of reports on various threats to the New Republic and published in 7 ABY,Wanted by Cracken is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor. Since The New Essential Chronology dates that conflict to 4 ABY, the placement of the sourcebook therefore translates to 7 ABY. noted the rumors that the only occasions on which Par Lankin, the Imperial Moff of the Lambda sector, left his governor's palace were hunting expeditions to the Equatorial Reserves of Xandil VII. Xandil VII was mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, which was written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
