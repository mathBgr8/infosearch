Paan trees were an evergreen species of tree native to the planet Hapes. Traded throughout the Hapes Consortium, the paan tree was used as a screen in decorative landscapes. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Plants of Hapes Category:Trees
