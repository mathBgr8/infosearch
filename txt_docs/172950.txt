Tanque tea
2000

Tanque tea was a variety of tea with a murky appearance, which was due to root fragments that were one of its ingredients. Some of the root fragments floated at the surface of the tea and were visible to the unaided eye. In 20 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of MedStar I: Battle Surgeons to 20 BBY. a Human Republic surgeon named Jos Vondar had Tanque tea in the chow hall of Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7, a medical facility located on the planet Drongar. He shared the tea with his nurse assistant, Tolk le Trene, a member of the genetically Human Lorrdian race. Tanque tea appeared in MedStar I: Battle Surgeons, a 2004 Clone Wars novel written by Steve Perry and Michael Reaves as the first part of the MedStar Duology. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons Category:Teas
