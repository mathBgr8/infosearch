Tarik
2589

Tarik (died in 1994) was a baby black bear whose voice was one of the major components of Chewbacca's vocals,Star Wars: Behind the Magic along with that of a cinnamon bear named PoohGalactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide and a walrus named Petulia. Tarik's growls also provided the primary voice of Lumpawarrump in the 1978 TV movie The Star Wars Holiday Special. He lived in San Jose's Happy Hollow Zoo, where he was a popular attraction. According to the Associated Press, Tarik enjoyed "sleeping, eating, catching bear biscuits, and spending hot days on his back in his pool with his paws in the air."
