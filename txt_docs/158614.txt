Biris J. Te Gaf
1517

Biris J. Te Gaf was the senior purchasing agent for the Galactic Alliance Engineering Support network on Coruscant. Te Gaf was responsible for the purchasing of cannon packs, which had been the subject of numerous complaints in the fleet due to frequent misfires. When Jacen Solo inquired about the cannon packs, he was told that the random sampling showed the packs to perform adequately.
