The Birth of the Lightsaber
768

The Birth of the Lightsaber was a featurette in the bonus material of 2004 DVD release of the Star Wars original trilogy. It revealed how the lightsaber was made, from tape to rotoscoping. It was also included in a DVD that was packaged with select Hasbro lightsabers alongside two chapters of Star Wars: Clone Wars. Category:Home video featurettes Category:Web documentaries
