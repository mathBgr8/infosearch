|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Galactic Empire **Imperial Navy |masters= |apprentices= }} Pepin was the navigation officer of one of Emperor Palpatine's Lambda-class shuttles during the time of the Galactic Empire. He, along with Erv Lekauf, helped Darth Vader quell a mutiny of stormtroopers onboard the shuttle, led by the Emperor's Hand Sheyvan. Pepin was able to survive the engagement. Pepin's name was inspired to author Karen Traviss by Quebec Star Wars fan Dany Pépin, former admin of SithClan.Net, creator of the Star Wars En Direct web radio talk-show, and producer/director of the Karen Traviss' Omega Squad: Targets audio drama.
