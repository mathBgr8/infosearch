Skywalker Loop
515

The Skywalker Loop was a starfighter maneuver invented by Anakin Skywalker. A pilot would loop their fighter around and pass through the exact same spot, only traveling in the opposite direction. *Starfighter Battle Book: X-wing vs TIE Interceptor *Starships of the Galaxy, Saga Edition Category:Starfighter tactics
