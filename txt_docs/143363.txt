Feltran
1124

Feltran was a Human male Rebel officer who served Bria Tharen during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, he accompanied Tharen aboard the Queen of Empire which was traveling to the planet Nar Shaddaa to meet up with the Hutts.
