Derenath had no moons. Derenath was mentioned in "Return to Prosperity," the first article in the Planet Hoppers series Beheboth: Blood and Water. The article was published on Wizards of the Coast's website on June 3, 2004, and it was written by Cory J. Herndon. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Beheboth system, and therefore Derenath, in grid square O-17.
