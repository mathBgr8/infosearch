|pages=25 |upc=761568968040 |timeline=2 BBYStar Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook |series=Classic Star Wars: Han Solo at Stars' End |issue=3 |next=last }} Classic Star Wars: Han Solo at Stars' End 3 is the third and final issue of the Star Wars Legends comic book miniseries Classic Star Wars: Han Solo at Stars' End. It collects material originally printed in the 1980-1981 comic strip of the same name, which was written by Archie Goodwin and illustrated by Alfredo Alcala and adapted of the 1979 novel of the same name by Brian Daley. The issue was published by Dark Horse Comics on May 14, 1997. To rescue his friend Chewbacca, Han Solo has infiltrated Stars' End, the secret prison where the Corporate Authority keeps its political prisoners. As tough as it was to breach security, however, getting in was the easy part. Now the Rebels have to make their way out! The front matter of Classic Star Wars: Han Solo at Stars' End 3 dates the events of the issue to 10 BBY&ndash;5 BBY.
