Iesei
1226

Iesei was a Killik hive that once lived on the planet Tatooine. They were encountered by Vector Hyllus and Cipher Nine during the Cold War between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire. During the Swarm War, the Iesei lived on a desert planet that came under Chiss attack. In Star Wars: The Old Republic both Vector Hyllus and Daizanna pronounce the name of the nest as "Lesei", mistaking the uppercase "I" for lowercase "L".
