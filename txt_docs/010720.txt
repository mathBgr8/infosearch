It was very poorly defended, focusing more on speed and attack power. The sniper could reach speeds of up to six hundred kilometers per hour, as a result of the ion afterburner fitted just behind the cockpit. The Sniper airspeeder was based on a concept for the "Imperial Sniper Fighter" vehicle that would have been part of a proposed but ultimately rejected extension of Kenner's Star Wars toy line, The Epic Continues. It is different than the similarly-named toy that Kenner did produce, the Imperial Sniper.
