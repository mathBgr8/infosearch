Wraw
1173

Wraw was a male Bothan Intelligence officer who was a part of Meloque's infiltration of Caluula in order to destroy the planet's yammosk. He was also there to see if Alpha Red would work outside of a laboratory. He was characteristically sarcastic and butted heads with Han Solo continually during the mission. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothans Category:Galactic Alliance Intelligence personnel Category:Males
