Vagrant
1926

Vagrant was a Victory I-class Star Destroyer of the Imperial Starfleet. Along with two Carrack-class light cruisers, the Nexus and the Xerxes, the Vagrant attempted to stop the Defiance Task Force from rendezvousing with the Rebel fleet at Sullust.
