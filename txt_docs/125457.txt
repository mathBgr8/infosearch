Heff's Souvenirs
1654

Heff's Souvenirs was a location in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was generally considered a junk shop. It sold anything that was broken, damaged or completely obsolete. It also had a few souvenirs that were related to Tatooine such as a glazed Womp rat. Heff's Souvenirs was founded by Heff, a Lepi. After he was killed in an incident involving bounty hunters in 0 ABY, the shop passed to his daughter Tebbi. Tebbi then sold Heff's Souvenirs to Moplin Jarron, a Sullustan.
