Quint (species)
711

Quints were tall, furred sentient species hailing from the planet Quint in the Quint system located in the Inner Rim. They were best known for their speed and agility. Quints were covered in delicate fur, and had small heads with triangular eyes. *Jedi Apprentice: The Evil Experiment *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Inner Rim sentient species
