Calamarian minisub
2449

The Calamarian minisub was a small submarine used on the oceanic planet of Calamari. In 5 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The Glove of Darth Vader, which includes the appearance of the Calamarian minisub, to 5 ABY. Luke Skywalker, Gial Ackbar, C-3PO, and R2-D2 used a fish-shaped Calamarian minisub to track and spy upon a Whaladon-hunting submarine. The Calamarian minisub first appeared in the 1992 Star Wars Legends novel The Glove of Darth Vader, which was written by Paul Davids and Hollace Davids, with illustrations by Karl Kesel.
