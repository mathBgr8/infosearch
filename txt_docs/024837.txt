CarsinShare
1133

CarsinShare was one of the many corporations which had its headquarters situated in Stratablock 7 on Metellos. CarsinShare, and its rival, Nonsu, were primary buyers and sellers of business information. They also created much of the material made to create and maintain the floating city of Ektra. CarsinShare and Nonsu also held death matches deep within the bowels of Ektra, pitting their champion gladiators against each other in bloody fights of death. At some period, the chief of CarsinShare was Hynla. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Vendors and sellers
