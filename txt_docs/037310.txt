UrrOr'RuuR was a Tusken Raider sniper and warrior who was part of a small group of at least four other Tusken Raiders, including the leader Orr'UrRuuR'R and the sniper RuuR'Ur, that lined themselves on the Mos Espa Circuit, a podracing course outside the city Mos Espa on the planet Tatooine. While there, they hoped to take out podracers and their vehicles while they raced in the Boonta Eve Classic, an annual event to commemorate the Boonta Eve holiday. The trouble caused by the Tusken Raiders during the race had become a popular attraction and, as a result, anonymous complaints by pilots to race officials did not lead to anything.Star Wars: Complete Locations thumb|right|300px|UrrOr'RuuR and his companions camped out on Canyon Dune Turn. During the Boonta Eve Classic of the year 32 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of , which includes the Boonta Eve Classic, to 32 BBY. UrrOr'Ruur and his group were camped at the cliffs above Canyon Dune Turn in the circuit. During the first lap, race commentator Fodesinbeed Annodue mentioned the Tuskens. The group communicated with each other, before UrrOr'Ruur, laying prone on the turn, took pot shots at the passing podracers. They cheered after all the racers passed. During the second lap, he stayed in his position and continued to shoot at the racers. One of the Tusken Raiders landed a shot on the podracer of racer Teemto Pagalies, destroying it. During the Boonta Eve Classic of 32 BBY, he wore tan robes.
