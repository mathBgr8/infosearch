Sheekla Farrs
1133

Sheekla Farrs was a female Ferroan living on Zonama Sekot in 29 BBY. Her husband was Shappa. An airship pilot, she was tall and muscled. Although she and her husband were injured in the First Battle of Zonama Sekot, they both recovered for the planet's jump to hyperspace. *Rogue Planet *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Ferroans Category:Pilots Category:Sekotans
