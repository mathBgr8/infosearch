Upland Liberation Front
1742

The Upland Liberation Front (or the ULF) was a name created by Depa Billaba during the Clone Wars. She did this to represent the groups of Korunnai raiding the Balawai on Haruun Kal during the Summertime War. It was not really a group, just a name meant to intimidate the Balawai; it had no formal organization, command structure, or even a rank system.
