Justice Systems
615

Justice Systems was a company set up during the rule of the Galactic Alliance that manufactured the EverAlert droid. This droid was similar in many respects to the YVH 1 combat droid produced by Tendrando Arms during the Yuuzhan Vong War. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid manufacturers
