Skydove Freight
1131

Skydove Freight was a shipping (and smuggling) company owned by the Hutt kajidic Anjiliac. It was based on Nar Shaddaa. As with Popara's other operations, it originally did not deal in slaves, merely glitterstim. Skydove can't be assigned an era, as the adventure Tempest Feud does have a predetermined one.
