Clawbird
2148

The clawbird or stana (as natives called it) was a black-feathered avian from Wayland that ate the flesh of dead animals and aliens. The clawbirds were among Wayland's species affected by the sabotaged Ossus Project. One attempted to scavenge the unconscious body of Cade Skywalker.
