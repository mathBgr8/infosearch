BullbaBong
2113

BullbaBong was the manufacturer of the BB-2 Shuttle and, in cooperation with the Theed Palace Space Vessel Engineering Corps, of the G-1 starfighter. BullbaBong was a Naboo-based firm, run by Gungans. It primarily supplied various customers, including the Gungan Grand Army and Royal Naboo Security Forces, with starships and bongos. It also produced weapons. It was created by the Gungan Bullba Bari before the Battle of Naboo. Business became active during the last decades of the Old Republic, after the Gungans re-established relations with the Naboo. Bullba found himself manufacturing bongos for sale throughout the galaxy, and later expanded into the production of weapons and starships.
