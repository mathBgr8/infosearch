Classic Creatures: Return of the Jedi
988

Classic Creatures: Return of the Jedi is a 1983 documentary on the making of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. Behind-the-scenes footage is interspersed with clips and information from classic monster movies. Hosted and narrated by Billy Dee Williams and Carrie Fisher, it was issued as a triple feature with The Making of Star Wars and SPFX: The Empire Strikes Back in 1983. It was also included in the Star Wars: The Complete Saga Blu-ray release. Category:Television documentaries
