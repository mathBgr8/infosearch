When an LAAT/i gunship carrying Jedi Master Mace Windu flew nearby, this shuttle's IG-100 MagnaGuard crew shot it down. However, Mace jumped out of his gunship before it crashed and attempted to stop the shuttle from taking off. General Grievous took out four lightsabers to fight the Jedi, but he used the Force to crush Grievous's chest, causing his already irritated insides to be disrupted and resulting in spasms of uncontrollable coughing that lasted until his duel with Obi-Wan Kenobi on Utapau. The shuttle escaped to Grievous's Providence-class carrier/destroyer flagship Invisible Hand.Labyrinth of Evil The Tri-wing shuttle first appeared in the novel Labyrinth of Evil, written by James Luceno. In the novel, the craft Grievous used to transport Palpatine to Invisible Hand was referred to both as a tri-wing shuttle and tri-winged shuttle. However, in Star Wars: Clone Wars, the craft used by Grievous shows only two (albeit triangular) wings, with the rear possibly accounting for a third "wing." *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Shuttle classes
