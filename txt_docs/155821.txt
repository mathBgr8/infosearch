StarLiners
550

StarLiners was a successful company located in the Brak sector. It provided transport for individuals throughout the sector in the form of several passenger liners. It was founded by Fae Kalena during the reign of the Galactic Empire where it reached the height of its success and became the foremost passenger transport company in the entire sector. *Flashpoint!
