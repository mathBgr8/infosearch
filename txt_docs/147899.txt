Wedding of Wicket Wystri Warrick and Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka
2586

The Wedding of Wicket Wystri Warrick and Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka took place sometime after the Battle of Endor, shortly after the death of Chief Chirpa. The ceremony doubled as Kneesaa's induction as the new chief of Bright Tree Village.
