Akorec
979

The akorec was a vicious, reptilian creature native to the planet of Trammis III. An average akorec stood at around ten meters tall at the shoulder and had a long head with a mouth filled with huge teeth. The akorec was a feared predator, not least because its scales were resistant to blaster-fire. The Scillal Designs 891c Akorec-class strike cruiser was named after this creature.
