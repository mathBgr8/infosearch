Their equipment included red outlined Phase II clone trooper armor, a pauldron, a kama, macrobinoculars, a DC-15A blaster rifle, and two DC-17 repeater hand blasters. Like all clone troopers, Deviss was created on the planet of Kamino based on the genetic template of the human male bounty hunter Jango Fett, stood 1.83 meters and had tan skin. Deviss received training through the ARC programRise of the Separatists establishes that Advanced Recon Commandos receive ARC program training prior to becoming ARC troopers. to prepare them for special missions. Deviss was first mentioned in the new Star Wars canon in the 2015 reference book Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know. Deviss is based on Clone Commander Bly's original color design, which was changed to better match the environment of the world Felucia from Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. While the red clone commander does not appear in the movie, he was pictured, albeit unnamed, in the Star Wars Legends continuity in Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary, a guide book published in 2005 and written by James Luceno,Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary and used as the basis of a "Clone Commander" figure for the film's toy line by Hasbro.
