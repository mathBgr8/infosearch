Squib Trade Language
585

The Squib Trade Language was a parallel language spoken by the Squib species. Although not as widespread as the Squibbian language itself, most Squibs were also fluent in the Squib Trade Language. Squibs only used the Squib Trade Language to trade among themselves, which allowed them to do without alerting other beings of their goals.
