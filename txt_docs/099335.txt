Nar Shaddaa Shipping

|role=Cargo merchant |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Nar Shaddaa Shipping was a shipping corporation based on the Dravian Starport. During the Galactic Civil War, Nar Shaddaa Shipping were looking to contract independent free-traders to deliver fifty tons of datapad circuit boards to the manufacturing planet Ather. The payment for the work was 3,500 credits. Category:Transport companies
