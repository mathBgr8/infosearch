Worxi
534

Worxi was the ancient native language of the Spiner species from Worxer. As Worxer was destroyed centuries before the Galactic Empire, the Spiners decided to replace their language with Basic, which became its adopted native tongue. As of 0 BBY, Worxi was a dead language. *Alien Anthology *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
