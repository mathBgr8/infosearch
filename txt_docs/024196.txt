Ace Entertainment Corporation
1194

Ace Entertainment Corporation was a company which owned a number of upscale gambling establishments and casinos that were scattered across the galaxy. One of Ace Entertainment's most popular establishments was the Ace of Sabres, which was located on the world of Kluistar. The company was used by owner/founder Sibarra the Hutt as a front for his illegal criminal enterprise. *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Entertainment companies Category:Hutt organizations
