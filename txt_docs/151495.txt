Tamaron
2318

}} Tamaron was a male clan leader of the Airam who agreed to join the Alliance to Restore the Republic during their campaign into the Airam sector. It was Tamaron's engineers who helped the Alliance to transfer gravity wells from a stolen Immobilizer 418 cruiser to a Modified Strike Cruiser at the Airam factory facility Gallofree.
