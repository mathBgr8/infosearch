Sarvel Tal-Oldra
747

Sarvel Tal-Oldra was a Corellian who once worked for the Cozzell Corporation. He was one of the designers for the company's advanced Juggernaut 510 Combat Power Armor. When the project was shut down due to the immense cost of integrating the armor's varied components, Tal-Oldra took offense and began producing suits of Juggernaut armor in the Corporate Sector outlaw tech base formerly run by Klaus "Doc" Vandangante. *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Corellians Category:Males Category:Mechanics
