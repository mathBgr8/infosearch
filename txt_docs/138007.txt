Elis
1213

Elis was a leader of a mining village on Anobis. He was one of the leading members in the civil war on that planet. When Anja Gallandro tricked Han, Jaina and Jacen Solo, and the remnants of the farming village into coming to his village for negotiations, Elis captured them. After his younger brother Protas was killed by one of Anja's burrowing detonators by accident, Elis ordered Yuos, the leader of the farming village, before him; he was about to kill him when the Solos convinced him to end the war. *Young Jedi Knights: Return to Ord Mantell *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Miners
