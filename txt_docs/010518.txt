V38 assault fighter
2830

The V38 assault fighter was a starfighter that served as the basis for the TIE/ph phantom. thumb|left|175px|The modified V38 assault fighter, known as the TIE Phantom. Design-wise, it was unique among TIE Series starfighters, as the quadanium steel solar panel wings attached directly to the hull instead of via pylons. The hull/cockpit itself was non-spherical, compared to other TIEs, though it was similar to the Scimitar assault bomber. The V38 appeared to have the wing of the TIE/IN interceptor, and its triple wing design was reminiscent of the TIE/D Defender and the Dagger-class starfighter.
