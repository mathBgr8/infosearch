Silooth (Star Destroyer)
2755

The Silooth was the personal Imperial-class Star Destroyer of Grand Admiral Peccati Syn. He named it after the Silooth, a Sith animal. The Silooth was vaporized by the New Republic forces during the Liberation of Kashyyyk, killing both Syn and Merili.
