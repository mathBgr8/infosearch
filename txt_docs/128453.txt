Mindra Nox was one of the lieutenants of the gang leader Dovor Dowwn until she went rogue after the Battle of Yavin. To seize Dowwn's Corellian trade, Mindra set up covert smuggling depots as power bases. When Dowwn found out about one of her renegade depots, he ordered it destroyed. But before his men could attack the depot, it was destroyed by an agent of the Coronet Mining Corporation that had mining interests near Mindra's depot and had feared collateral damage. In 1 ABY, Nox also had trouble with a rogue Corellian slicer who called himself Emmin Whitehat. When she finally found out where he lived, Nox hired a spacer to take him out. Although Whitehat intercepted the message, he was unable to prevent the spacer from fulfilling his mission. Mindra Nox is involved in several missions in Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided that players can get at Mission Terminals on Corellia.
