DroidMart
1909

DroidMarts were a chain of droid service and retail stores operated by the droid manufacturer Rim Securities. During the Galactic Civil War, a conflict between the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic, stores were found throughout the Outer Rim's Quence, Parmel, and Portmoak sectors. DroidMarts operated on any planet in these sectors with a spaceport rated stellar-class or above. The stores made up the bulk of Rim Securities' sales of security, defense, and exploration droids. DroidMarts were mentioned in Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids, a sourcebook written by Drew Campbell and Eric S.
