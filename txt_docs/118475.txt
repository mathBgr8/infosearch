She was 2.4 meters tall, which was large for her species. She was also very clever and a talented technician. She was patient and diligent, but did have a tendency to whine during a crisis. Nikaede was born of a noble family on Kashyyyk and smuggled from the planet through a selfless sacrifice by her parents.
