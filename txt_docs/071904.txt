|editor= |website=Wizards.com |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=*January 2, 2003 *January 9, 2003 *January 16, 2003 *January 23, 2003 *January 30, 2003 |series=Planet Hoppers |preceded by= |followed by=Arkanian Chill }} A Traveler's Journals of Ralltiir is an online supplement for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game released on the Wizards of the Coast website on January 2, 2003, as part of the site's Planet Hoppers series. It was written by Morrie Mullins. The supplement was split into five separate articles, focusing on the adventures of Ithorian explorer Vareesa Enan on Ralltiir around the time of the Clone Wars. &hellip;our hero, the wide-eyed Ithorian Vareesa Enan, arrives on the world of Ralltiir, describes its architecture, and perhaps falls prey to many pairs of watchful eyes. &hellip;our hero, Vareesa Enan, finds a comrade in a wayward Ithorian named Kikow, a secret smuggler looking to carve out his own piece of Ralltiir's criminal underworld. &hellip;our hero, Vareesa Enan, learns of the astonishing archive of knowledge in the capital city's Exploration Auditorium, and takes note of a meeting between Kikow and a shaven-shouldered Wookiee name Furellas. &hellip;our hero, Vareesa Enan, is delighted to receive a gift from Furellas -- a baby Ralltiir tiger.
