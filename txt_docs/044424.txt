Imperial Military Oversight Commission
1535

The Imperial Military Oversight Commission was a department of the Imperial Military. The department was formed from the Senate Military Oversight Committee and consisted of several Imperial officers and agents. Their task was to review data collected from Imperial excursions and battles and analyze any problems there may be within the Imperial Service. They would make their suggestions to commanding officers and even to the Emperor himself in order to improve the Imperial war machine. *The Star Wars Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook *The Star Wars Sourcebook, Second Edition *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial Military organizations
