|published in=Star Wars Gamer 5 |reprinted in=Hyperspace |pages= |timeline=24 ABY |series= |preceded by=Young Jedi Knights: Crisis at Crystal Reef |followed by=Boba Fett: A Practical Man }} "The Crystal" was a short story written by Elaine Cunningham and published in Star Wars Gamer 5 in 2001 and later on Hyperspace. Jaina Solo, her brothers and friends graduate from the Jedi Praxeum. After graduating from the Academy, Jaina asks her aunt, Mara Jade Skywalker, to be her mentor. Han Solo gives his daughter a heavily modified Z-95 Headhunter as a present and she decides to fly it to Mon Calamari where they are having a vacation. During her inaugural flight, however, she gets held up because a cyborg piloting an X-wing misidentifies her as his enemy, and tries to destroy her. He thinks that the Z-95 is a prototype despite the fact that it was manufactured many years ago. Despite suffering some damage in the dogfight, Jaina destroys the enemy fighter while sparing the pilot's life, proceeding then to Mon Calamari. She decides to call her Z-95 the Crystal.
