*Bill Smith *Miranda Horner *Peter Schweighofer *Ed Stark |publisher=West End Games |release date=January 1995 |type=Paperback |pages=176 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0874312507 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Goroth: Slave of the Empire is a book for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Nigel D. Findley. It was published by West End Games in 1995. It is set between 0 BBY and 4 ABY.In Goroth: Slave of the Empire, Jered Pole is noted as having joined the Rebel Alliance "two years ago." As the Alliance was formed in 2 BBY per The Essential Guide to Warfare and became the Alliance of Free Planets in 4 ABY per The New Essential Chronology, Goroth: Slave of the Empire must be set between 0 BBY and 4 ABY. Four thousand years ago, Goroth was a beautiful trade world. Caught between two warring factions that coveted its resources, Goroth was too difficult to occupy, yet too valuable to remain free. The solution was destruction. Now the native Gorothites are beginning to rebuild their civilization, but they are caught between The Empire and The Rebel Alliance. Will they suffer the same fate again? A Star Wars Supplement.
