Cet Willak
1642

Cet Willak was an Alliance Special Operations Major assigned as an Alliance observer aboard the Far Orbit in 0 ABY. Willak was none too pleased to be the Alliance watchdog amongst those whom he saw as "cutthroats, thieves, murderers, and space-rats." He hoped to be reassigned to another duty, preferably with more "action". Willak unfortunately was not a skilled diplomat, and Captain Vedij had to intervene to keep the peace between the Alliance SpecOps officer and his crew. Willak's arrogance and abrasiveness, however, did not conceal ineptitude.
