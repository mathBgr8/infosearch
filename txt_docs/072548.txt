Mining Guild Sector Headquarters
1043

The Mining Guild Sector Headquarters on Procopia was the headquarters of the Mining Guild in the Tapani sector. It was comprised of a large building constructed from high-grade polished marble and was located in the Commercial District of Estalle Island. Mining Guild lobbyists and negotiators had offices at the Headquarters. Their objectives were to represent the interests of member companies before the Great Council. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Guildhalls Category:Procopia locations
