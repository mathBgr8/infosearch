Yun-Txiin
1654

Yun-Txiin was a male Yuuzhan Vong deity, one half of the Lover Gods, with Yun-Q'aah being the other. Having a relationship outside one's caste was forbidden in Yuuzhan Vong society, and disobeying automatically led to sacrifice to the Lover Gods. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The New Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Txiin
