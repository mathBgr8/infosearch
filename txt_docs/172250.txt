SoroSuub (CNVYR)
1401

thumb|right|250px|[[Modular conveyor|Modular Conveyor SoroSuub 1 firing at attacking Alpha-class Xg-1 Star Wings.]] Modular conveyors SoroSuub served in the Rebel Alliance fleet at the Battle for Honor during the Galactic Civil War. Conveyors SoroSuub headed for XQ1 Platform DS 5 midway through the battle, attempting to resupply the stranded Admiral Harkov and his Victory-class Star Destroyer Protector. Imperial forces had positioned themselves near the jump point where Conveyors SoroSuub and additional transports exited hyperspace.
