Sarrish |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns= |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Grand Army of the Republic ***212th Attack Battalion ****"A" Squad |masters= |apprentices= }} Eight-eight-six-eight was a clone trooper that served as part of the 212 Attack Battalion's "A" Squad during the Battle of Sarrish. Alongside Marshal Commander Cody, Eight-eight-six-seven, and Eight-eight-six-nine, Eight-eight-six-eight attempted to climb a mountain utilizing an ascension cable, only to be killed in the process; of the group's members, only Cody survived. Eight-eight-six-eight was created by writer Randy Stradley and penciler and inker Douglas Wheatley, first appearing in the comic Routine Valor, released on May 6, 2006 in celebration of Free Comic Book Day.
