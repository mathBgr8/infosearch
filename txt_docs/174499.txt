The heavily modified glider was covered in reflec panels that baffled standard sensors, allowing for infiltrating behind enemy lines. Its augmented repulsorlift generator could carry a much heavier load in addition to the pilot, and an added pull-down display visor granted the user night-vision, terrain-following and directional modes. It was also equipped with low power transponders to allow troops to locate each other after landing.
