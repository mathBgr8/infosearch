Skateboard
1346

thumb|right|An unspecified Skywalker holds up a skateboard. A skateboard was a single-being wheeled vehicle. It was light enough to be carried by a young Human, and could be used to perform acrobatic maneuvers. Yoda once confiscated a skateboard from a Jedi named Skywalker, and subsequently used the vehicle himself. He apparently found the experience quite enjoyable. In an interview discussing Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens, Disney Chairman Allan Horn joked, "I've learned to be very careful about Star Wars. If I say, 'There will be no skateboards in Star Wars'... that gets headlines." *Star Wars Kids (1997) 3 Category:Ground vehicle types
