Zygerrian Slavers Guild
3391

The Guild of Zygerrian Slavers was one of the larger slaver guilds in operation during the reign of Emperor Palpatine. It was based on the slave trade world of Zygerria. thumb|left|150px|A Zygerrian slaver The guild began its operations during the time of the Galactic Republic. In the era of the Galactic Empire, their size and structure made it beneficial for the Empire to offer slaver permits at a reduced cost. They trafficked mainly in Twi'leks and native Mandalorians. Terrinald Screed personally vouched for the quality of their Twi'leks. The Guild also occasionally dealt in Wookiees, but were unable to break the monopoly the Trandoshans had in the Kashyyyk system. With the rise of the New Republic, the Zygerrian Slavers Guild became a remnant of its former power as the new galactic government outlawed slavery.
