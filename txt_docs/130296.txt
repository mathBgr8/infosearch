Salibury
1157

Salibury was a city on the planet Ralltiir. In 2 ABY, Imperial Governor Dennix Graeber cracked a Rebel Alliance spy ring with an armory in the center of Salibury's commercial district. Graeber seized three hundred tons of weaponry, and authorities captured thirty-five Rebels on-site, leading to the capture of roughly another hundred through interrogations and examination of documents captured in the raid.
