Hirin
2177

Hirin, also known as OM814, was a barren rock planet with two moons. It occupied the fourth and outermost orbit around the red giant star Thrantin Major in the Thrantin OM81-A system, a part of the Ollonir Boundaries in the Negs portion of the Colonies. Hirin was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
