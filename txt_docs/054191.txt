A-3P0
667

A-3P0 was a 3PO series protocol droid serving as an administrator in the Rebel base on the planet Entooine during the Galactic Civil War. He welcomed the group of Rebels to the base who went on to help the native Askhew stop Jowart Typok from hunting the blue banthas.
