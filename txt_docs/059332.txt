Reliable
770

Reliable was an Imperial cruiser that was a part of the blockade of ThonBoka. It was the fourth cruiser Lando Calrissian visited as a traveling salesman and sabacc player. While playing sabacc with a pair of gunners and the cooks Clive and Merle, he was pointed on to the Courteous, which was the blockade ship nearest the mouth of the nebula. *Lando Calrissian and the Starcave of ThonBoka *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
