Galderian Slave Traders
671

The Galderian Slave Traders were a group of Galderians slavers who hailed from Galdar. They were known to have had a base in the Trebodar system until it was destroyed by a joint Galactic Empire and Rebel Alliance task force. They also somehow acquired at least one Space ARC, probably through enslaving the race who created them, as well as many I-BEAM Star Fighters. Category:Enslaver organizations
