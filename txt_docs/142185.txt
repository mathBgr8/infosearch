|mayor= |celestialbody=Arkania |continent= |location= |climate=Tundra |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Kanti was a city on the planet Arkania during the reign of the Galactic Republic. Established on the planet Arkania in the Perave system of the Colonies region, Kanti saw notoriety in 22 BBY when it was host to the Regional Dejarik Championship. During the quarter-final match between Ohh Bronna and Corwin Kaezeb, Bronna, the sector champion, was suddenly defeated by Kaezeb. Shocked by the defeat Bronna, an Abyssin, attacked the Siniteen challenger which escalated into a six-player brawl. Meanwhile, players Garem Will and D'nisenz ignored the fighting and continued playing, leading to D'nisenz to be named the Colonies champion. Category:Cities on Arkania
