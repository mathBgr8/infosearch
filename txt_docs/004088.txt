|mass= |gender= |sensor=Purple Doctor Aphra (2020) 37 |plating= |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Alliance to Restore the RepublicStar Wars: Uprising **Uprising *Scourge *New Republic *Resistance |firstmade= |retired= }} The GNK-series power droid, also known as the gonk droid, was a well-known type of power droid manufactured by Industrial Automaton. They were often referred to as gonk droids in imitation of their honking sound. They can often be seen around Tatooine working on moisture farms or spaceports like Mos Eisley. Gonk droids can also be used as a table to play board games on or to put other equipment like a payment dispenser. On Jedha, caretakers used GNK droids to transport materials and house data; these droids would sometimes be interred along with their owners.
