Polis
1779

Polis was a BFF-1 bulk freighter the served in the Imperial Navy. It was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupters resupply convoy which was lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor to the Corrupter in the Mev system. The ship was then attacked by Rebel raiders and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
