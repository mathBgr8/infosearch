Starlite Cloud
2744

The Starlite Cloud was a Sullustan cruise liner that visited Brentaal in 6 BBY. During its stopover there, the twelve-year-old Platt Okeefe joined the crew as a cabin steward. She worked on the ship for two years before leaving for another job.Platt's Smugglers Guide Also among its crewmembers was the Mon Calamari Mussat Nasrabi who served as chief engineer.Imperial Double-Cross While on Kuat, the rebel agent Rixen used finding directions to the Starlite Cloud as a cover for her conversation with the information droid, L1V-IN. The Rebel agent Iona Wince also served as crew on the liner.Cracken's Rebel Operatives In 3 ABY, the cruise liner was chartered by the performers of the Kallea Cycle for their tour of the Core and Colonies regions. *Imperial Double-Cross *Cracken's Rebel Operatives *Platt's Starport Guide *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Platt's Smugglers Guide Category:Liners
