The YT-450a was a clunky-looking starship from Corellian Engineering Corporation's YT-series light freighters. It was slow and not very maneuverable in its stock configuration, but it could be modified to improve its power and weaponry. *Star Wars Gamemaster Handbook *Enter the Unknown YT-0450a
