Holocam E
1648

Holocam E-series cam droids were a series of small, friendly holocam droids, programmed with a female personality and seemingly at least basic level mechanical, technical, and scientific knowledge. Produced by Trang Robotics, Holocam E droids cheerfully dispensed advice and helpful hints as they became relevant. One of these droids, known as "Cammy," helped a Rebel agent to find and infiltrate an assassin droid factory on Tatooine. *Star Wars: DroidWorks *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cam droid models Category:Droids with feminine programming Category:Trang Robotics products
