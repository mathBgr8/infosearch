It was written by J.D. Wiker and published on October 23, 2003. It was followed by the miniadventure Nightsaber. Do the ends justify the means? In this Star Wars Roleplaying Game miniadventure, Luke Skywalker himself sends the heroes to head off a rogue Jedi Academy student who plans to end the war with the Empire -- by assassinating the Moffs! Can the heroes catch him? More importantly, will they stop him? "Rebel Jedi" is a Star Wars Roleplaying Game miniadventure for four heroes of 15th level. It's set about 12 years after the Battle of Yavin.
