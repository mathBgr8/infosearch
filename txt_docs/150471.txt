Sling
4624

The sling was a primitive weapon that hurled metal bullets or stones.Star Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook Usually, they were made of leather and cord. The Ewoks were known to use these, and one user was female Ewok warrior Asha. Treek, another Ewok, employed slings to shoot the healing substance fektur at friendlies. A Stereb in Prak City named Herdr'tui also used a sling.
