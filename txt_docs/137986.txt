Force Web
2119

The Force Web, a network of filaments that connected galaxies, was the largest known structure in the universe by 32 BBY.The events of Mace Windu: The Glass Abyss take place the week after the funeral of Qui-Gon Jinn, which Star Wars: Timelines dates to 32 BBY. Shortly before that time, Galactic Republic scientists had announced the discovery of the Force Web, the study of which they hoped would yield information about the formation and evolution of galaxies. In that year, Jedi Master Mace Windu thought about the Force Web after realizing the nature of the mycelial web that connected the planet Metagos through the Force. The Force Web was mentioned in the 2024 novel Mace Windu: The Glass Abyss, written by Steven Barnes. In the real world, the cosmic web is the large-scale structure that connects galaxies, superclusters, and galactic walls. *Mace Windu: The Glass Abyss Category:Extragalactic astronomical objects
