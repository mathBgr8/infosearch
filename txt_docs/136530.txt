|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation=Drall Patriots }} }} The Drall Patriot's Cave was a cave located on Corellia and inhabited by a gang of anti-government Drall known as the Drall Patriots during the Galactic Civil War. Located in the Agrilat Swamp, the cave was inhabited by the Drall Patriots, a group of Drall extremists fighting for the independence of Drall from the unified government of the Corellian system. It was rumored that this vast network of caves were the epicenter of their operations. After the Battle of Yavin, people campaigning across Corellia to support the politician Karin Featherlight discovered the cave.
