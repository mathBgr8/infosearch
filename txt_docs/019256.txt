The clone trooper who would take on the name Inc was created on the planet Kamino from the clone template of bounty hunter Jango Fett to serve the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Part of the Grand Army of the Republic, Inc was a member of the 327th Star Corps, under the command of Clone Commander Bly and Jedi General Aayla Secura. Early in the war, in 22 BBY,The Clone Wars: Headgames is shown to take place after the beginning Clone Wars and is stated to have occurred some time before the Star Wars: The Clone Wars episode "Jedi Crash." Since The New Essential Chronology dates the beginning of the Clone Wars to 22 BBY and The Official Star Wars Fact File Part 82 dates the events of "Jedi Crash" to 22 BBY as well, then The Clone Wars: Headgames must also take place during that time. the 327th troopers Inc, Barr, Galle, and Commander Bly attempted to reap information from the memory banks of a Separatist tactical droid, by setting up a trap in a rocky canyon. They were able to fabricate an information bunker, which attracted the attention of the T-series tactical droid. In turn, the droid ordered its troops to attack the bunker, prompting Inc and the men of the 327th to attack them from the rear. Using jetpacks, they charged the droids and Inc was instructed by Commander Bly to throw droid poppers at them. The clones were able to effectively dispatch the droid's troops, while Jedi General Aayla Secura beheaded the tactical droid. Since the droid had not gone into cyberostasis, they would be able to extract information from its memory banks. thumb|right|200px|Inc is killed by a dwarf spider droid. In the final days of the Clone Wars, in 19 BBY, the 327th fought in the Battle of Felucia, attempting to capture Separatist leader Shu Mai. During this conflict, Inc, now holding the rank of Clone Lieutenant, was assigned to a squad, along with fellow lieutenant Barr, to support Jedi General Barriss Offee on a trek to the city Har Gau. During their hike through the Felucian forest, Offee, Inc, and Barr split off from the rest of the squad to scout ahead. The trio came across a battle droid patrol, which they proceeded to ambush before the droids could report. During the attack, Inc's blaster experienced a carbon flush, rendering it inoperable. With no way of defending himself, a DSD1 dwarf spider droid was able to close the distance to Inc and blast him, killing him instantly. Shook from Inc's death, Barriss Offee ordered Barr to fall back and retrieve the rest of the squad. As a Human male clone created from the genetic template of bounty hunter Jango Fett, Inc had tan skin and stood at 1.83 meters tall. Inc wore Phase I clone trooper armor that featured the yellow markings of the 327th Star Corps. He also carried droid poppers and utilized a jetpack and a DC-15A blaster rifle during the mission to extract information from a tactical droid. Later on in the war, Inc would adopt Phase II clone trooper armor which would continue to be adorned with the markings of the 327th. He was also equipped with a pauldron, a kama, and a DC-15 blaster while on Felucia. Inc first appeared in Reversal of Fortune, a series of daily webstrips published on Hyperspace from October 2004 through June 2005.
