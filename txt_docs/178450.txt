SSP06 hyperdrive generator
962

The SSP06 hyperdrive generator was manufactured by Isu-Sim. The Galactic Empire's Death Star I battle station had 123 of the units for propulsion in hyperspace, all of which were connected to a single navigational matrix, in order to ensure the Death Star was carried beyond the speed of light due to its massive size. The hyperdrive system was linked with the navicomputer to deliver the battle station to strategic areas beyond the hazardous range of planetary or stellar gravitational forces. *Star Wars: Complete Cross-Sections *Death Star Owner's Technical Manual *Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide Category:Hyperdrive units Category:Isu-Sim products
