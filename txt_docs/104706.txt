Cordioline trehansicol
488

Cordioline trehansicol, also known as CorTrehan, was a chemical that killed metalmites. It was one of the only ways to exterminate metalmites, since normal extermination procedures had no effect on them. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Chemicals
