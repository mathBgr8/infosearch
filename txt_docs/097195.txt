Rebel Rock
1580

Rebel Rock was the codename given to a sniper in the resistance movement on Cilpar in 4 ABY. During the battle to liberate the planet from Imperial occupation, Elscol Loro and Wedge Antilles were being pursued in their landspeeder by a pair of TIE fighters.
