Luke Skywalker's starship (disambiguation)
3496

Luke Skywalker (and his legends counterpart) used a number of starships over the years. *Red Five, a T-65B X-wing starfighter Skywalker flew at the Battle of Yavin. *Breha, diplomatic vessel resembling a J-type 327 Nubian starship used by Skywalker and Leia Organa on a mission to Tibrin. *A vessel used by Skywalker around the time period of the Battle of Jakku. *AA-589, a T-65B X-wing starfighter Skywalker flew during the early years of the New Republic and through the Thrawn campaign. *Civilian craft (Fondor), craft used by Skywalker to travel undercover from Yavin 4 to Fondor to spy on the Super Star Destroyer Executor. *Commandeered transport, ship Skywalker and Leia Organa stole to escape the Tarkin shortly before its destruction. *Jedi Explorer, the first starship used by Skywalker during the reborn Emperor Palpatine's campaign *Jedi Explorer II, the replacement for the destroyed first Jedi Explorer *Luke Skywalker's freighter, freighter Skywalker returned to Tatooine aboard when seeking blockade runners for the Rebellion. *Luke Skywalker's scout craft, ship Skywalker and Leia Organa acquired on Centares. *Luke Skywalker's scout ship, ship Skywalker used to scout for a new Rebel base in the Drexel system before crashing there. *Luke Skywalker's shuttle, shuttle Skywalker used to travel to Solay. *Luke Skywalker's starship (Tatooine), vessel Skywalker used on missions to Vorzyd V and Tatooine. *Luke Skywalker's starship (Harix), vessel Skywalker used on his missions to Harix. *Power-gem attack ship, craft used by Skywalker and Vrad Dodonna in an attempt to bring down the shields of the Executor. *Rebel scout ship, vessel Skywalker and Organa used to scout Jarnollen as a possible Rebel base. *Rebel smuggling ship, craft Skywalker used on his mission to Aridus before ultimately being forced down on Hoth. *Leia Organa's Rebel scout craft, craft used as a scout ship by Skywalker and Organa to make contact with the Akuria Rebel Base. *Unidentified space yacht, yacht used by Skywalker and Callista Ming to travel from Coruscant to the Mulako Corporation Primordial Water Quarry comet, Dagobah, and the Hoth system.
