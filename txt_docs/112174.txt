Borthis
1532

Borthis was a Sith Lord in the Brotherhood of Darkness who served as an instructor at the Sith Academy on Korriban alongside Hezzoran, Kas'im, Orilltha, Shenayag, and Qordis. When Kaan had all Sith summoned to Ruusan to destroy the Army of Light, Borthis also went there to join them.
