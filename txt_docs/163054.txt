Voidfight
850

Voidfight was the slang term for a type of starfighter combat that took place in regions of deep space where the ambient light cast by nearby stars was absent due to the battle's remote location. Because of this, visual scanning of targets and hostile fighters was reduced to almost nothing, forcing pilots involved in a voidfight to rely on other forms of instrumentation for the most part.
