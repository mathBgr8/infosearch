Drextar Pym
1569

Drextar Pym was a male Human senator from Exargan of the New Republic who represented Excarga and the Herios sector. Pym continued to own stock in several companies even after becoming a New Republic senator, notably companies still affiliated with the Galactic Empire. Senator Pym also acted as the chief prosecutor for the Senate panel responsible for prosecuting former Imperial officials and military leaders, an affiliation which caused a former storm commando to attempt to kill him.
