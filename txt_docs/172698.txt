|pages= |published in=Star Wars Insider 96 |reprinted in= }} "Inside the 501st" is an article that appeared in Star Wars Insider 96. Written by Kevin Christley and featuring uncredited photography by Bill Blackburn, it is a follow-up to the Star Wars Insider 58 article "The Fighting 501st - A Legion of Fans." The article covers everything you ever wanted to know about joining the Empire with the 501st Legion, but were too afraid to ask. It details a brief history of the fan-run Imperial Costuming organization known as the 501st Legion of Stormtroopers, why fans join the Legion, membership requirements, how to put together an Imperial costume, their involvement with Lucasfilm, and the numerous contributions Legion members make on behalf of children's charities. *Star Wars Insider 96 Category:Star Wars Insider 96 articles
