|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation=Afarathu }} }} The Afarathu Cave was a cave on the planet Corellia, located near the city of Doaba Guerfel. It was inhabited by the Afarathu extremists. }} The cave served as headquarters for the Afarathu, a Selonian terrorist sect which aimed to drive the Dralls and the Humans from the worlds of the Corellian system. It was used for many years prior to the Battle of Yavin by the vicious Afarathu extremists to stage their strikes against Corellian interests. Very few knew the location of the cave; the former Follower of Nyax Lock Vlash was one of them. After the Battle of Yavin, a Selonian diplomat in Doaba Guerfel recruited a spacer to fight the Afarathu Selonians at the cave. In 1 ABY, some Afarathu fought Rebel Alliance forces on Corellia. Imperial Lieutenant Velso later dispatched a spacer to the cave in order to retrieve the ship destruct sequence codes of a Rebel Corellian Corvette possibly stolen by the Afarathu during the skirmish.
