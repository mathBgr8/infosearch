Vago Gejalli Thokka
1814

Vago Gejalli Thokka was a Hutt crime lord. Although born into the Gejalli kajidic, Vago was adopted into the Anjiliac clan by Popara with the eradication of her blood-clan's holdings. Never fully trusted by the Anjiliac Hutts, Vago was used as an intermediary because of her ability to speak Basic.
