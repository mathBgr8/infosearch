Minnow Hawk
579

Minnow Hawk was the name of a model in the Museum of Flight on the planet Harridan. Though it resembled a Rebel smuggler's starship, the description marked it as a primitive glider incapable of flight for more than 20 seconds at a time, only if there was no wind. Category:Air vehicles Category:Art
