|closed= |upc= |engine= |genre=Action adventure |modes=Single player |ratings=ESRB: Everyone (E) |platforms=GBC |series= |basegame= |expansions= |director= |producer= |designer= |programmer= |artist= |writer= |composer= |timeline=32 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |prev= |next= }} Star Wars: Episode I Obi-Wan's Adventures is a video game released in 2000 for the Game Boy Color that retells the story of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace from the perspective of Obi-Wan Kenobi. The plot closely follows the film, but does not include any of the Tatooine scenes. Instead, there are missions detailing the approach to Theed through a series of catacombs and Obi-Wan's mission aboard the Trade Federation landing craft. It is also the only game based on Episode I that allows the player to pilot a bongo, and also allows Obi-Wan switch to using a blaster during gameplay. Category:Legends console and computer games
