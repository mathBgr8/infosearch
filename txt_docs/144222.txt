In 1 ABY, Laetin A'shera oversaw all Car'das operations in the Lucent Ivaya quadrant of the Bright Jewel system. thumb|left|250px|Laetin A'shera in his [[M3-A Scyk interceptor/Legends|M3-A Scyk fighter.]] During the Battle of Tansarii Point Station between Car'das and Black Sun forces, Laetin A'shera dispatched pilots to search for and eliminate Black Sun spies among Dynathian mining transports. Information found on a Black Sun spy was quickly transmitted to Agent Rayar of the Car'das intelligence ring. A'shera then sent his pilots escort Dynath Miners from the Masul'ivis Asteroid Belt back to their mining facility. Later, during a mission to warn the Car'das allies about Black Sun activity, the Car'das M3-A Scyk fighters were infected by the Black Envy, a Black Sun computer virus. Laetin A'shera and his pilots decided to use it to their advantage and finally trapped the Black Sun assassin Talonbane Cobra in the Lurid Aurora Nebula. Laetin A'shera was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Laetin A'shera was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
