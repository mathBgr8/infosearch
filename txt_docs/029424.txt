Kellmer Institute of Galactic History
744

The Kellmer Institute of Galactic History was one of the premier history and government research institutes in the galaxy. Funded primarily by the New Republic as a General Ministry Institute, the Kellmer Institute was responsible for a number of detailed reports on the history of the New Republic and was renown for its government, history and public affairs curricula. It maintained a branch office on Ryloth, which was built in 27 ABY. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *Cracken's Threat Dossier Category:General Ministry Institutes
