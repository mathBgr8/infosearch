Commando Team One
2051

Commando Team One was a SpecForce team for the Rebel Alliance and New Republic. Its leader held the title Commando One. It operated behind enemy lines and was commanded by Kapp Dendo. It was present on Tatooine in 4.5 ABY, at the Galactic Civil War, and at the evacuation of Ciutric. Judder Page apparently served with the team then as well.
