Issori language
711

Issori was the language of the Issoris from the planet Issor. *Vrecje - unsanctioned birth (translates as "stranger") *Whoon-cha - Fertilization of an Issori egg between two members of the nobility of the same nation. *Vuin-cha - Fertilization of an Issori egg between two members of the nobility of different nations. *Whoon-li - Fertilization of an Issori egg between a member of the nobility and a common caste member, within the same nation. *Vuin-li - Fertilization of an Issori egg between a member of the nobility and a common caste member of a different nation *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge Category:Languages
