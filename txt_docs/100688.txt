When Commander Salvo issued Order 66, the spec-one, along with the rest of Ion Team, questioned and disobeyed the order, allowing Jedi Masters Roan Shryne, Bol Chatak, and her Padawan Olee Starstone to escape. The spec-one was later captured along with the rest of his squad and taken to Darth Vader. Ion Team's spec-one appeared in the 2005 novel, Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader.
