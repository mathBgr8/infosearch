Escape Pod Deluxe
1450

The Escape Pod Deluxe was a type of escape pod featured on luxury starships. While a regular escape pod featured thin armor and a moderate shield (rated at 20 SBD), the Deluxe eschewed the shielding for more powerful thrusters and considerably stronger armor. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Escape pod classes
