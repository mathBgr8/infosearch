Hypno-imprinting
1789

Hypno-imprinting, also called hypno-indoctrinationThe Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology and sleep-imprinting, was the use of hypnosis to implant orders, post-hypnotic commands, or to impart tactical information en route to a mission. Hypno-imprinting was also used to brainwash Alliance prisoners while they were incarcerated by the Galactic Empire.
