Bill Hughes
1520

thumb|180px|[[Admiral Ivpikkis, an example of Bill Hughes' talent]] Bill Hughes is an artist who currently works and lives in Houston, Texas with his wife, Heidi, and their two children. Bill began drawing robots and spacecraft at an early age, which led to self-publishing his first comic book while he was still in high school. While attending the University of Houston, he produced his own television programs. After graduating, he worked for several ad agencies and even designed some toys. In 1995, he worked on the Star Wars: Droids series for Dark Horse Comics, and has been drawing for Star Wars children's books. Bill and his wife formed The Big Tex Movin' Picture Company and now writes/produces Indy Films.
