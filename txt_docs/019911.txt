InterGalactic Ore
1278

InterGalactic Ore (InterGal) was a lommite mining company stationed on Dorvalla, and rival of Lommite Limited up until their merger into Dorvalla Mining following the Toom clan sabotages that nearly destroyed both companies. Caba'Zan was its head of security.
