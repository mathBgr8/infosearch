Reactor Core (drink)
1718

A Reactor Core was a popular mixed drink among smugglers. It was said to give partial immunity to certain toxins and dangerous gases. It also produced hallucinations due to the narcotic agents released by combining Spice Liqueur and Blue Tonic.
