Chimbak wine
2565

Chimbak wine was a thick red wine from the planetThe Essential Atlas Alderaan. Potable by Humans, the wine had a mellow and delicious flavor. In 29 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Rogue Planet, which includes the meeting in Wilhuff Tarkin's apartment, to 29 BBY. Republic Outland Regions Security Force Commander Wilhuff Tarkin and Sienar Fleet Systems Chief Executive OfficerThe New Essential Guide to Characters Raith Sienar drank glasses of chimbak wine in the former's private apartment on the planet Coruscant during a meeting with the assassin Ke Daiv. Although Sienar did not normally drink alcohol, he sampled the wine to be polite during the meeting. Sienar found himself enjoying the wine, finished his entire glass, and complimented Tarkin on his good taste.
