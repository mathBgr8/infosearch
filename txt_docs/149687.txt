Nilo Rodis-Jamero
2333

thumb|250px|Nilo Rodis-Jamero. Nilo Rodis-Jamero was the assistant art director of visual effects for Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, and costume designer for Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi.
