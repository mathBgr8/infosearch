Who are the Meatlumps?
9679

|editor= |illustrator= |artist= |performer= |publisher=Corellia Times |release date=1 ABY |media type=Press article |pages= |published in= |genre= }} Who are the Meatlumps? was a multi-part article, written by Corellian journalist Vani Korr of the Corellia Times newspaper. It was an investigation article about the Meatlumps, a group of fanatical anti-technology thugs and terrorists from Corellia. During her investigation she became injured, but with the help of a traveler, she managed to continue her research. The first part of the article, The Story of the Meatlumps, Part 1, revealed who founded the gang and detailed their unusual interest in technological devices. During her investigations, Vani also discovered a mention of a "King of the Meatlumps." She therefore tasked the spacer to investigate further and find the location of the secret Meatlump Hideout. The adventurer managed to infiltrate the Meatlumps and finally encountered the legendary Meatlump King. The information gathered in the hideout allowed Vani Korr to finalize her article. The article Who are the Meatlumps? appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. It was added to the game with the "Publish 21," released on August 4, 2005. The article was a reward item given to players who completed Vani Korr's original pre-NGE quests that formed the "Corellian Times Investigations" story arc. Vani Korr's quests were later widely expanded as part of "Chapter 10: The Search for the Meatlump King," released on June 19, 2008. With this update, a new series of quests was proposed by Vani Korr, leading to the so-called "Meatlumps Theme Park," a series of quests involving the Meatlumps.
