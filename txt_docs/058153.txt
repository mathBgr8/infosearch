Tam (Stoga)
1101

Tam was an old Human male who lived on Stoga during the time of the Galactic Civil War. Tam befriended BoShek when the latter moved onto the planet. Despite that, Tam betrayed him to Garm Bel Iblis Rebel cells when BoShek helped Rasha Bex escape from them. *Empire 23 Category:Humans Category:Males
