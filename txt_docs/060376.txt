Pizzazz 6
1115

Pizzazz 6 is the sixth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated March 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. The issue also features a page of cartoons by Catherine Siracusa parodying then-current famous characters called "Easter with the Stars." It includes R2-D2 wearing rabbit ears with text suggesting he will star in a science fiction version of Peter Cottontail titled Egg Wars. 06
