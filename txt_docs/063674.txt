Detainer
3233

The Detainer was an Immobilizer 418 cruiser in the Imperial Navy. Following the defeat of the Galactic Empire at the Battle of Endor, Lieutenant Lon Donell seized command of the Detainer and used it for piracy against the New Republic shipping lanes. Donell operated the Detainer from the planet Barpine, and expanded his pirate fleet by capturing New Republic vessels.
