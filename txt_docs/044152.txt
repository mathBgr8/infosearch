Gorto Zaga
1629

Gorto Zaga was a male Aqualish, and chief of the Kiffex town of Deadend, who could often be found in that settlement's Black Hole cantina. He was killed in 30 BBY by Vilmarh Grahrk, who, along with Quinlan Vos, Zao, and Tholme, was trying to escape from an army of Anzati led by Aayla Secura, who had attacked Black Hole.
