Portmaster's Pavilion
420

The Portmaster's Pavilion was a building thirty meters wide that once stood in Redhaven's spaceport on Rhommamool. After Nom Anor came to power, it was torn down to create the center of the Square of Hopeful Redemption. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime Category:Buildings
