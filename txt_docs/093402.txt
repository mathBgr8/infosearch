Izbela Saarrj
1655

Izbela Saarrj was a Human female lieutenant in the Imperial Forensic Intelligence who filed a report concerning the crime lord, Tyber Zann, shortly after the Battle of Yavin. This report would eventually be looked into by Maximillian Seerdon for Emperor Palpatine. Ryan Kaufman named her after his daughter. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Imperial Security Bureau officers
