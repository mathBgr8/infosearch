H'Lokk Consortium
785

H'Lokk Consortium was a rival company of Bakur Mining Corporation. They coded a virus into the mining droids before sending them to Bakura, in an effort to sabotage the activities of the Human settlers on the planet, forcing Bakur Corporation to abandon the planet. The plot failed, however, and H'Lokk failed to drive Bakur Corporation off Bakura. Later, however, H'Lokk purchased Bakur Mining Corporation, forming it into one of their subsidiaries. *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mining companies
