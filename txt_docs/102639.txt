Vengeance (YV-260 light freighter)
1897

The Vengeance was a YV-260 light freighter owned by the Rodian Bomu Clan. It was commanded by Heedron Bomu and his brother Ormann who both worked for Zonnos the Hutt. It was armed with twin pairs of ion cannons, a pair of blaster cannons and a turret-mounted tractor beam. The ship was sent to prevent the rescue of Zonnos' brother Mika, however it failed in that mission.
