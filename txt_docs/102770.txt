MSE-1T
2239

MSE-1T is one of a pair of a certain type of MSE-series droid that lived in Star Tours' Tomorrowland Starport. Both droids rode around a track and were said to consume spare metal parts. The second droid was in disrepair at one point.Complete Guide to Star Tours In 2005, Hasbro released MSE-1T as an exclusive Star Tours action figure. The droid and its counterpart found in the Disney's Hollywood Studios and Tokyo Disneyland version of Star Tours. According to the Spot the Droid poster and the Complete Guide to Star Tours booklet for the Tokyo starport, they made their homes in the Droidnostics Center. A map of the ride noted that passengers are often extremely lucky to find one. *Star Tours: The Adventures Continue *Star Tours *Complete Guide to Star Tours *Star Wars: The Ultimate Action Figure Collection Category:MSE-series droids
