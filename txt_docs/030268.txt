Temple of Fire
4933

The Temple of Fire, also known as Tmmn Zyrth in the Massassi language, was a Massassi temple on the moon Yavin 4 used for blood sacrifices. It was built in 5000 BBY under the leadership of the Dark Lord of the Sith Naga Sadow. 200px|thumb|left|Massassi built the temple in 5000 BBY. Designed as a cone-shaped structure, the Temple concealed Sadow's ship, the Derriphan-class battleship Corsair, beneath the floor.Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 5 Sadow was aware of an ancient prophecy about the return of the Dark Lords of the Sith and placed a mighty Sithspawn beast known as a Sith wyrm within the temple as a guardian against the future Dark Lord of the Sith. When Exar Kun arrived on the moon, he was captured by the Massassi and led to that temple to be executed. However, using the dark power of the Sith amulets, he defeated the Sith wyrm that Sadow had created and was worshiped as a god by the Massassi. In 3996 BBY, the Jedi Order created a Wall of light that destroyed the Temple of Fire.The Official Star Wars Fact File 37 says that the Temple of Fire was destroyed in Exar Kun's final battle with the Jedi, during which Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War 6 shows all Jedi coming together to create a wall of light. *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 5 *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 4 *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 5 *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 6 *The Essential Guide to Characters *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Chronology *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Dark Side Sourcebook *The New Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Fire Category:Yavin 4 locations
