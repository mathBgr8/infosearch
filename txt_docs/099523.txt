Cotin
1022

Cotin was a member of the Five Worlds in 40 ABY working as insurgents on Coruscant during the Second Galactic Civil War. He along with other Corellians were working with the bounty hunter Ailyn Habuur though they were arrested after Ben Skywalker had a hunch about explosives in the area they were located. *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corellians Category:Humans Category:Males
