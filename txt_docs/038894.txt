Ghic Dx'ono
1913

Ghic Dx'ono was a male Ishori Senator who represented Isht and the Ishori Confederene in the Senate of the New Republic. In 19 ABY, in the course of Caamas Document Crisis, Senator Ghic Dx'ono and his people aided Leia Organa Solo, a member of the High Council, in meeting with the Caamasi delegation. Soon after his present political opponents, the Diamalia Porolo Miatamia, with a frightening message to the Senate, revealed that the Chiss Thrawn had survived his death in Bilbringi system.
