From Heir to Last Command
1602

"From Heir to Last Command" is an interview published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 in February 1994 in which Timothy Zahn discussed the process of writing the Thrawn Trilogy with Ilene Rosenberg. The article is an interview between Ilene Rosenberg and Timothy Zahn about his conception of the Thrawn Trilogy. Topics covered include Zahn's connection to Star Wars and George Lucas, as well as his approach to recreating the tone of the original trilogy in his novels. Additionally, Zahn shares insights into the development of his original characters. *Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 Category:Interviews Category:Star Wars Adventure Journal articles
