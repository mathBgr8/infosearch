Ab'el Bogen
1486

Ab'el Bogen was a Senator of the New Republic. From as early as 16 ABY and as late as 25 ABY, Bogen represented Ralltiir in the New Republic Senate. As of 25 ABY, he was a member of the Council on Security and Intelligence.
