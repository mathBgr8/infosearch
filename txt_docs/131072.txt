|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=137 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Legacy |issue=14 }} Legacy 14 is the fourteenth issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. Cade Skywalker has done many bad things in his life, but possibly none worse than turning over a captive —Jedi Master Hosk— to the Sith. Now he has come to Coruscant to atone for his sins, and hopefully set Hosk free. But Hosk isn't the only one Cade has crossed, and a chance run-in with a couple of old "friends" may send him on a new path. Take note! The next part of this issue begins a story that will reveal the secret behind Legacy's most mysterious character, the self-proclaimed emperor of the Sith Empire, Darth Krayt! This issue starts off with Cade Skywalker and R2-D2 escaping Skull Squadron in the Coruscant undercities. Mirroring the famous Obi-Wan Kenobi escape, Cade hides the Mynock on the underside of a building. He then finds Rik's Cantina, where he speaks with Queen Jool. He gives her access codes to the Mynock, and tells her to give them to Syn and Deliah when they come in. He also meets Chak and Kee, who are looking for Syn and Blue, who both borrowed th Grinning Liar before the were captured by Rav and the Imperials. He then leaves and finds his way to the Sith Temple, where he makes his way to the chamber that Hosk Trey'lis is being held in. He learns that Hosk has told the Sith everything he knew about Cade. Then, Darth Talon attacks out of nowhere. She and Cade fight, and Cade would have won, were it not for the meddling of Darth Nihl, who Force-chokes Cade into unconsciousness, and is then taken away by Talon and Nihl.
