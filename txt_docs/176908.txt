The outfit and armor featured a dark appearance with glowing red elements, including the visor of the helmet, and cylindrical shoulder-mounted armament housing a golden payload. The Imperial Inquisitor costume was added to the Zynga video game Star Wars: Hunters as a costume in the 0.20 arena pass of the March 28, 2024 update during the game's soft launch. The game soft launched on November 16, 2021, released June 4, 2024, and ultimately shut down on October 1, 2025. *Star Wars: Hunters Category:Imara Vex costumes
