Stranger Squadron, along with Interloper Squadron, was one of two squadrons of TIE/D Defender starfighters that was dispatched by Colonel Broak Vessery, under orders from the "real" Ysanne Isard, to rescue Rogue Squadron from the trap set by Prince-Admiral Delak Krennel at the Battle of Distna. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge Category:Imperial starfighter squadrons
