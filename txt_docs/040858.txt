Episode I Adventures 4: Jedi Emergency
7022

Episode I Adventures 4: Jedi Emergency was the fourth installment of Star Wars Episode I Adventures. A young girl has been found who has the power to become a Jedi. But an evil scientist doesn't want to let her go. And her home is under attack by Bartokk Assassins. Yoda, Mace Windu, and the other Jedi must save her. The future is at stake.
