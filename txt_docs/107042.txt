Gatling gauntlet
1376

thumb|250px|[[Durge/Legends|Durge firing his gatling gauntlet]] A gatling gauntlet was a weapon usually worn by bounty hunters and mercenaries. It consisted of several barrels mounted on a wristband and had the ability to shoot a variety of ammunition types, from small metal projectiles to much larger spikes. The gatling gauntlet was made popular by the bounty hunter Durge during the Clone Wars, and made a comeback with bounty hunters and mercenaries such as Bar-Kooda and Glott during the Galactic Civil War. In the real world, the term "Gatling gun", referring to multi barreled automatic weapons, is named after Richard Jordan Gatling and he invented.
