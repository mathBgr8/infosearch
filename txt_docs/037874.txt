Yavin 3
2556

Yavin 3 was a rocky moon that surrounded the gas giant Yavin Prime in the Yavin system, a star system apart of the Gordian Reach of the Outer Rim Territories. In the comic story Star Terror!, the third moon of Yavin was depicted as the site of Rebel Alliance headquarters.
