Scohar Xenohealth Institute
774

The Scohar Xenohealth Institute was a contagious-diseases research center located in Storinal's capital city, Scohar. Its disease containment vault contained a plasma bomb large enough to incinerate the building and much of the surrounding area if its containment fields were breached.
