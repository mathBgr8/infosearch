Eastgate

|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} Eastgate was one of the largest commercial and warehouse districts of in the Upper City of the planet Taris. In 3964 BBY, local hood Marn Hierogryph had a warehouse in Eastgate filled with landspeeder engines that he claimed were "right off the line." Like most of Taris,Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Eastgate was devastated by the Sith Lord Darth Malak in 3956 BBY.The New Essential Chronology Eastgate was first mentioned in Knights of the Old Republic 1, an issue of the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic comic-book written by John Jackson Miller and published by Dark Horse Comics on January 25, 2006. *Knights of the Old Republic 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Upper City locations
