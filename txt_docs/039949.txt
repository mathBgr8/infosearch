First Church of the Dark Side
812

The first Church of the Dark Side was the first ever church constructed by the Church of the Dark Side dedicated to their false religion. It was opened on Coruscant following the Battle of Endor by the false Prophets of the Dark Side, as a way to control the masses. Category:Coruscant buildings Category:Temples
