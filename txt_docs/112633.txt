CIS Weapons Factory
1985

}} This CIS Weapons Factory was located on the planet Diado, and was used during the Clone Wars. Its defenses comprised of B1-series battle droids and Cold assault battle droids. The factory produced variants on the Galactic Republic's AT-AT and AT-ST walkers, as well as a starfighter which was stolen by Jedi Master Saesee Tiin.
