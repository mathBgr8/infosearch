Its capital, Ranklinge City, had an Incom Corporation plant on its outskirts that produced I-7 Howlrunners at the time of the Battle of Yavin. The main cities, including the capital and Janusar, were on the northern continent; the southern continent had been embroiled in a civil war since around 10 BBY. The South Cont's civil war began in 10 BBY. After two years of war, the Galactic Empire sent a couple of diplomats in 8 BBY to mediate the conflict. They didn't manage to solve the war and all Imperial forces eventually left the planet.
