Dylan Lewis
994

Dylan Lewis is an Australian television and radio personality who appeared in an uncredited performance as a patron in Dex's Diner in Attack of the Clones. He acted as the Human wearing the helmet sitting directly behind Obi-Wan Kenobi, conversing with Honka and Sidewa. Lewis is known as a Star Wars fan, and appeared in The Phandom Menace, a documentary on Australia's obsession with Star Wars. Lewis, Dylan
