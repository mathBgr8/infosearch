|publisher=Decipher |release date=May 11 2001 |figures= |cards=90 cards :45 Light Side :45 Dark Side |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Death Star II Limited |followed by=Coruscant Limited }} The Tatooine Limited set is the ninth full expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game, a card game based on the Star Wars universe. It consists of 90 regular cards (45 Dark Side and 45 Light Side cards) and nine alternate image cards and was introduced in 2001. This set consists of 30 rare, 9 alternate image, 30 uncommon, and 30 common cards. The Tatooine Limited set was the first set to introduce alternate image cards to the game. These cards were identical to their original version but they just had a different image and were rarer thus adding a new element of collectability to the game. Cards that were usually made into alternate image cards were main characters. This set introduced many of the classic characters from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace that were involved with the events occurring on Tatooine. This set also introduced podracing to the game, in which players could relive the excitement of podracing. A complete list of the Tatooine Limited set can be found on the . Because this is an expansion set, it bears an expansion-set symbol, which is located near the top right corner of the card to the right of the lore and below the destiny number. The symbol for this set is Sebulba's podracer. This set was sold in 11-card expansion packs and 30-pack expansion boxes. Each pack included one rare card, three uncommon cards and five common cards. Starter decks were never produced for this set.
