It had a twenty-four issue run and was published quarterly, beginning in 1999 and concluding in 2005. Each issue features several short stories from various creators that are set in multiple eras of Star Wars storytelling. Star Wars Tales was collected in six paperback volumes that each contain four issues. The Free Comic Book Day issue Star Wars: Tales—A Jedi's Weapon was published in May 2002. It is comprised of the story "A Jedi's Weapon,"Free Comic Book Day 2002 Special which was later featured in Star Wars Tales 12.Star Wars Tales 12 Some of the stories featured in Star Wars Tales are non-continuity.
