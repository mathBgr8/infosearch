Green Flight
1403

Green Flight was a group of BTL-S8 K-wing assault starfighters serving within the 24th Bombardment Squadron as part of the New Republic Fifth Fleet. In 17 ABY they participated in the Black Fleet Crisis. At the start of the campaign, Green Flight was one of three component flights within the squadron, consisting of six K-wings, with one pilot and one gunner per ship. Their commander known as was Green Leader. After the debacle at Doornik-319, the squadron was reorganized into four flights of three planes each. All three K-wings in the reorganized Green Flight, along with seven escorting snubfighters, were destroyed in an unsuccessful attack on the Aramadia-class thrustship Rizaron during the Battle of ILC-905.
