Reef Home City
1943

Reef Home City was a floating city on Mon Calamari.The Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Reef Home was named after the city. The city was webbed by pink coral and housed a generator for Dac's planetary shield.Strongholds of Resistance Admiral Daala assaulted the city with turbolaser fire and cracked it into fragments, but it was eventually rebuilt. *Dark Apprentice *Legacy of the Force: Revelation *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Fly Casual *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Cities on Mon Cala
