Techrats
1510

The Techrats was a youth swoop gang on the planet Cerea, circa 33 BBY. The group was made up of pro-tech teenagers who favored joining the Republic and abandoning the old, low-tech ways favored by the Elders. They painted their faces to hide their identities. Unknowingly, the kids became involved in a conspiracy by a corrupt Elder to smuggle in banned technology in exchange for rare Cerean minerals. They were led by Skeel, who met an untimely end in a swoop-race accident in the Outsider Citadel.
