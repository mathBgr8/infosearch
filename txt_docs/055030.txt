Dalliance
2176

The Dalliance was a modified Corellian YT-1210 light freighter owned by the New Jedi Order. It was housed at the Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4. Corran Horn and Ganner Rhysode flew the Dalliance on their mission to Bimmiel in 25 ABY. The two Jedi were investigating the disappearance of a class of xenoarchaeologists from the University of Agamar. It turned out there was a Yuuzhan Vong presence on the world, and in an attempt to rescue some of the students captured by Yuuzhan Vong, Corran Horn was captured.
