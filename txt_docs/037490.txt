Haangok
1586

Haangok was a male Kajain'sa'Nikto thief who was responsible for stealing various transports from the Commerce Guild and later stayed in the Uscru Entertainment District on Coruscant. The Commerce Guild awarded a reward of 4,000 credits on Haangok's capture and 2,000 credits on his death. The Mandalorian Bounty hunter Jango Fett encountered the Nikto while hunting down Jervis Gloom on a viewing platform in the Uscru entertainment district.
