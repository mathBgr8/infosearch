Cordel Cove Winter Carnival
765

The Cordel Cove Winter Carnival was an annual celebration that took place in Cordel Cove, the largest settlement on the planet of Neftali. Centered on celebrating the beauty of the ice world, the carnival's events included swoop races through ice caverns, ice sculptures, and beverages made with Neftalian pure-ice water. The largest festival in the Socorro system, the carnival drew in tourists from across the sector. *The Black Sands of Socorro Category:Holidays
