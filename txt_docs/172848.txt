Ralph
1156

Ralph was a male employee who served for the Azzameen family company Twin Suns Transport Services. He managed to escape from the facility when the Galactic Empire confiscated the business and its assets. Ralph and fellow co-worker Norton are the only two employees mentioned in-game.
