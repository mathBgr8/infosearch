Chorrawl
1839

Chorrawl was a Wookiee hunter who lived during the Jedi Civil War. When he first met an amnesiac Revan, who came to Kashyyyk in search of a Star Map, he misjudged the Jedi as just another Czerka slaver because he was a Human. Later, Revan retrieved the blade of the great Bacca's sword and gave it to ex-chieftain Freyyr, who had been exiled by Chuundar, Freyyr's son. When Freyyr came back to Rwookrrorro with this important artifact to challenge Chuundar, Chorrawl sided with Freyyr.
