Lagan Ismaren
1838

Lagan Ismaren was a Human male from Alderaan. One of the few Jedi younglings to survive the Order 66, Lagan and his younger sister Roganda hid with the Children of the Jedi in Plawal on Belsavis, where Lagan was a childhood companion of Nichos Marr. After being forced to flee Belsavis, Lagan and Roganda were separated from their parents and brought before Inquisitor Ameesa Darys.
