Scrivinir
1552

The Scrivinir was the leader of the Centrality, a remote sector in the Outer Rim Territories. Although opposing a strong, central governments, a loose coalition of trade and defense agreements in the sector as the Centrality came to exist and was overseen by a position known as the Scrivinir, which meant "head writer" in High Trammic.
