Spira Regatta Open
846

The Spira Regatta was an annual yacht race around the planet Spira. Every year, galactic celebrities, diplomats, wealthy citizens, and the media would go to Ataria City in anticipation of the event. Aneash Maglea was the regatta's reigning champion. Several known attendees of the regatta included Castion Vogol, Tovir Daglear, Ganth, and Darby Willers. *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Sporting events
