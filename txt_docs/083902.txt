Lanyss Gutierrace
1240

Lanyss Gutierrace was a female Human member of the Judicial Department. She was Personnel Director of the Department prior to the Clone Wars, until a complaint from an Ishi Tib whom she rejected after he applied for a clerical job. After a four-month investigation turned up 36 similar cases, Gutierrace was suspended on charges of Humanocentrism and replaced with Tanu Aphidale.
