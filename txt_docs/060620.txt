|pages= |upc= |timeline=1 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures |issue=7 }} Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 7 was the seventh issue of the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Russ Manning. While delivering a cache of pre-Empire textbooks to an outlying system, Luke Skywalker encounters Imperial forces as they take an entire school hostage!
