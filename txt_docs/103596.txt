|published in=A Decade of Dark Horse 2 |reprinted in= |pages=10 |timeline=c. 2 BBYThis story is set not longer after Han Solo acquires the Millennium Falcon, which occurs in 2 BBY per The Essential Reader's Companion |series=A Decade of Dark Horse |preceded by= |followed by= }} "This Crumb for Hire" is a comic story written by Ryder Windham and drawn by Allen Nunis. It was published in August 1996 in A Decade of Dark Horse 2, the second issue of Dark Horse's comic anthology A Decade of Dark Horse. Star Wars, Nexus, and Ghost&mdash;three all-new stories in one great comic! Dark Horse continues its tenth-anniversary celebration in the second issue of A Decade of Dark Horse, featuring Jabba the Hutt's first meeting with Salacious Crumb; Ursula's not-so-subtle mind games with Nexus; and a startling physical transformation for Ghost! Such an incredible line-up of stories by such an incredible line-up of writers and artists! Who could not celebrate?!
