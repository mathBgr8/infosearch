Zochaeb
1143

Zochaeb was a Hutt leader who negotiated with the Nimbanese Krovalis clan. The Krovalis offered Zochaeb and the Hutts the allegiance of the Nimbanese people and free access to information in Nimbanese databanks. In return, the Hutts would allow the Nimbanels to enjoy their freedom. Zochaeb agreed.
