Raich's cave
1308

The Raich's cave was a cavern located within the Dead Forest on the Forest Moon of Endor. The location served as the base of operations for the Raich, who housed a number of animals there. The animals were tied in place and fed food until the Raich decided to devour them. The Raich was first defeated outside his cave by Ewoks of Bright Tree Village. The Ewok warriors were able to track the beast to his lair and eventually trapped him in the form of a tree using a magic cap. The bordok Baga was once trapped within the Raich's cave, during the temporary return of the Raich, but he was eventually freed by the Ewoks Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka and Wicket Wystri Warrick. After the eventual defeat of the Raich, the animals were freed from the cave. Category:Caves Category:Endor locations
