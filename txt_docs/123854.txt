Kiratta-sarn
842

A kiratta-sarn was an object, lifeform, or art style from the Infinite Empire. A Rakata warrior trapped in a Rakatan prison stated that it was specific to his culture, and therefore unusable in a riddle contest he had posed against Revan for one of them to escape the Rakatan prison. He did mention that it was beautiful to look at. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Rakata culture
