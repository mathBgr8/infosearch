thumb|Tokyopop's Star Wars Manga. Star Wars Manga was a series of Legends manga style comics adapting films from the Star Wars saga. The original trilogy and The Phantom Menace were adapted. Tokyopop also released two new manga books in the vein of Star Wars Tales.
