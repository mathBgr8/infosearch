Tinta Lines
827

Tinta Lines was a Corellian shipping corporation. It was owned by the Tinta family. The owner of Tinta Lines hired Corran Horn to guard his daughter, Siolle Tinta, aboard the luxury cruiser Tinta Rainbow.
