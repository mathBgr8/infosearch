1807

|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Yuuzhan Vong Advance" is a scenario for the Star Wars Miniatures game, written by Sterling Hershey in 2006. It depicts a battle between Yuuzhan Vong warriors and New Republic forces. During the bleakest part of the Yuuzhan Vong War, a small force of New Republic stragglers faces a Yuuzhan Vong recon force that is sweeping the area for enemies. Another group of New Republic forces lay in wait to join at the battle. Unfortunately for them, the invaders also have more troops at their disposal and can call in a backup force. The planet's defenders must defeat the Yuuzhan Vong or risk enslavement and death. Category:Miniatures Scenarios
