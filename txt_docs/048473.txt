Carpagian
1069

Carpagian was a Saryabo IV Pinnace Scoutcraft owned by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. It was given to the group of Rebels who attempted to meet with a defecting Imperial, Admiral Joser on the planet Dandrian. When it got there, the Rebels were was ordered to land in Docking Bay 107 in Lon Drasna, which is where the ship stayed while the Rebels learned that Joser was held captive in Alpha-Omega Prison on the orbiting asteroid Alpha-Omega. The Rebels landed the ship on the asteroid close to the prison and proceeded to rescue Joser from Imperial control before Darth Vader arrived.
