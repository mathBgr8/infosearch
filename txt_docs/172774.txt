Cro-Sal-Trei
452

Cro-Sal-Trei was a tribe of Eickaries that around 378 BBY, was defeated by the rival tribe Hu-shi-crive. The Chief Commander and his family were locked in a watchtower by their enemies and left to die. Later that year, Hu-shi-crive lost the watchtower, and would always keep the secret of the watchtower. *Fool's Bargain Category:Eickaries Category:Tribes
