Shoengen Coast
438

Shoengen Coast was a location on Brosi. It was to the east of Shoengen Delta. Shoengen Coast was in the influence area of the Brosin Underground around 2 ABY. The smallest team of the Underground, thirty warriors personally led by Randle Clanse, patrolled the Shoengen Coast. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Brosi locations
