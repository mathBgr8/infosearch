X-26 StarHaul
2401

The X-26 StarHaul was a freighter developed by Incom Corporation and utilized most by the Galactic Empire. A Starhaul was 55 meters long. It required a crew of two, plus a droid. A Starhaul had space for 5,000 metric tons of cargo and a single week's worth of consumables. In atmosphere, this vessel could only achieve 200 kilometers per hour. It also was equipped with a Class 4 hyperdrive.
