It was a part of the Expansion Region's Lostar sector, being located both in the Outer Expansion Zone sub-region and the Northern Dependencies portion of that region. By some point between 0 BBY and 4 ABY, is set in the Rebellion era and during the existence of the Rebel Alliance, the former of which dates to between 0 BBY and 5 ABY and the latter of which was transformed into the Alliance of Free Planets in 4 ABY per The New Essential Chronology. the Human noble Tan Dicium was a princeling from Sifuchi. At one point, he and his four Yuzzem bodyguards traveled aboard his yacht Ryntail to the Inner RimThe Essential Atlas trade world Tirahnn. Between 12 ABY and 13 ABY, Sifuchi was situated within the borders of the New Republic. Sifuchi was introduced in "Triplet Threat," a roleplaying mini-adventure written by Jason Fry for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game and published on Wizards.com on January 22, 2004.
