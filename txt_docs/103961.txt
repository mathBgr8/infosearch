SP Eighty cleaner droid
2330

SP Eighty cleaner droids, also called SP-80 cleaners, were a model of droids from the Single-Purpose series that were programmed with the single purpose of cleaning. When presented with something they considered a mess, they would attempt to clean it even if their limitations rendered them unable to do so. Though limited, the protocol droid C-3PO considered SP-80s and others of their series the core of droid operations. SP-80 cleaners were blocky droids with bronze colored plating, short legs, a single cleaner arm and a vacuum absorption pad, and a rotating upper body that was roughly cube-shaped and equipped with sensor squares that glowed blue when active. They were capable of identifying dirt and clutter visually, but also had a powerful ability to detect odors&mdash;said to be sensitive enough to scent a light stain measuring a mere fraction of a square centimeter at a range of over one hundred meters. A flip-open coverplate in the side concealed a comm cable input that allowed them to be easily reprogrammed.
