Sureshot
1915

The Sureshot was a CEC YT-1300 light freighter. The Sureshot was owned by the Arcona Jedi Izal Waz and was also a part of the Wild Knights squadron. In 26 ABY it was destroyed over Coruscant by assassins working for Senator Viqi Shesh who thought the ship to be the Millennium Falcon. *The New Jedi Order: Recovery *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order Category:YT-1300 light freighters
