*Socorro |birth= |death= |species=Twi'lek |gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Pink |skin=Jet-black |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Ethra Brewery |masters= |apprentices= }} }} Abdi-Badawzi was a black-skinned Twi'lek gangster boss based on Socorro. His base of operation was hidden in the Rym Mountains. He dealt primarily in arms and drug smuggling, and was one of the three founding members of the Ethra Brewery. thumb|left|Abdi-Badawzi with Tra'Parr'Sratt and Secles Uslopos Abdi-Badawzi sported the impression of a kindly monarch who ruled his subjects with an iron grip. This meant that he doted on his lackeys and rewarded them when they accomplished their missions, but he tortured and murdered them if they failed him. He employed a range of talented freighter pilots and presided over a small army of highly trained henchmen and mercenaries. Among his more notable employees were Kaine Paulsen, Elias Halbert, Tra'Parr'Sratt, Durmag, and Secles Uslopos.
