Pellna Gril
1014

Pellna Gril was a lieutenant in New Republic Intelligence Service, specifically Special Threats Division. As an Intelligence agent, one of Gril's assignments was to review the Black Fleet Crisis. He was a primary source on data regarding the Fallanassi and the White Current. *Cracken's Threat Dossier *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Gril, Pellna
