**Galactic Senate |masters= |apprentices= }} Tashrikam was a senator who represented the planetThe New Essential Chronology Grizmallt in the Galactic Senate of the Republic. In 22 BBY,HoloNet News Vol. 531 #49 was published on 13:3:28, which corresponds to 22 BBY per the reasoning here. Supreme Chancellor Palpatine announced the formation of the Loyalist Committee, a group of politicians united to diplomatically address the growing Separatist Crisis. In a statement recorded in the 13:3:28 issue of the HoloNet News CoCo District Edition, Tashrikam considered the committee's creation a maneuver made by the chancellor to save face rather than a chance to hunt conspiracies. Tashrikam compared the committee's formation to the chancellor's HoloNet plea made in the previous week for negotiations with Separatist Head of State Count Dooku, praising Palpatine's new direction in turning to proactive members of the senate for assistance. Tashikram was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #49, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet News, which was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published the HoloNet News website on March 29, 2002.
