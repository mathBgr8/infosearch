During the Invasion of Naboo, Queen Padmé Amidala and Captain Panaka used Flash speeders and Gian heavy patrol speeders in their mission to liberate the capital city of Theed from the occupation of the Trade Federation. During the Clone Wars, Flash speeders were used by a group of bounty hunters hired by Count Dooku during an attempt to kidnap Chancellor Palpatine at the Festival of Light. In 2 BBY, Phoenix Cell used Flash speeders at their base on Atollon. The Lasat rebel Zeb and the droid Chopper drove a Flash speeder to investigate a damaged sensor beacon in Sector 6, where they discovered a droid that they were unaware was an undercover Imperial probe droid. Once they discovered its true nature, the pair, along with AP-5, managed to disable it and used the Flash speeder to follow the infiltrator droid back to its X3-13 hyperdrive pod and disabled its long-range transmitter, forcing the droid to fly back with its proton warhead still active. *Star Wars: Galactic Defense *Most Wanted *Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes *Star Wars Complete Vehicles, New Edition *Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga – The Official Collector's Edition *LEGO Star Wars Yoda's Galaxy Atlas Category:Landspeeder models Category:Naboo vehicle classes Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
