Stinger 627 S
1957

The Stinger 627 S was a sleek, swift Podracer designed by Pizer-Errol and used by Slide Paramita. It was a small and fragile craft, but was very responsive on turns. Paramita's racer was also known for a very good repair and cooling system. *Star Wars: Episode I Racer *Star Wars: Episode I Racer: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Episode I Racer Official Nintendo Player's Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Pizer-Errol products Category:Podracer models
