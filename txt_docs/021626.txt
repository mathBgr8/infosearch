|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: The Last Command |issue=2 }} The Last Command 2 was the second issue in the Star Wars: The Last Command comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel The Last Command. It was released December 24, 1997. When Imperials, disguised as security personnel, break into the palace and try to snatch Jacen and Jaina Solo, they're forced to deal with the likes of Mara and Han; but who's side is Mara really on? The epic continues and the intrigue thickens in the second installment of this adaptation of the best-selling novel!
