thumb|A Huj mat rug A Huj mat was a type of hand-woven rug that, at first glance, appeared to be a jumble of colors and shapes with no definitive pattern. At close inspection, however, a subtle pattern was visible. This led to the common expression "see the pattern on the Huj mat". *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest Category:Rugs and carpets
