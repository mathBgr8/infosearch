27th Hour Social Club
993

The 27th Hour Social Club was a five-star establishment on Elshandruu Pica owned and operated by Kina Margath. It was part of a complex of bars, casinos, and restaurants collectively known as Margath's on Elshandruu Pica. The Imperial captain Sair Yonka kept a suite on the 26th floor of this building. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *I, Jedi *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dining establishments
