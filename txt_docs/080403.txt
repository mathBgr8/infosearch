Treaty of Anaxes
1877

The Treaty of Anaxes was invoked by the Moff Council to initiate the Sith–Imperial War. Initially, the treaty was to have the Galactic Alliance and the Fel Empire defend each other in case of attacks such as by the Yuuzhan Vong.
