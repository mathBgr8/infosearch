Merchant Row (Outland Station)
1713

Merchant Row was an area located aboard Outland Transit Station, a large space station in the Baxel sector of the galaxy's Outer Rim.The Essential Atlas Made up of a number of crowded streets and alleyways, Merchant Row was home to various salespeople attempting to sell their wares to potential customers. Outland Transit Station's Merchant Row was first introduced to the Star Wars universe in the 2002 Star Wars: Bounty Hunter video game, developed and published by LucasArts.
