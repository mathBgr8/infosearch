Sencil Corporation
1271

|locations= |role=Droid part manufacturer |products= |founding= |dissolved=0 ABY (absorbed into Imperial Droid Corporation)Pirates & Privateers |affiliation= }} Sencil Corporation, also known as the Cencil Corporation, was headquartered on the planet Churba. It manufactured matrixes and mission specific components for the assassin droid black market. Alongside Bansche Tech, SGI Systems, Inc. and Reiber Manufacturing, Sencil Corporation was reformed into the Imperial Droid Corporation. *Pirates & Privateers *The Far Orbit Project *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid manufacturers
