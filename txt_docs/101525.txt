Tan Clan
1037

}} The Tan Clan was a Rodian clan on the planet Rodia during the Old Republic period. In the aftermath of a civil war, an additional "wa" syllable was added to form a new clan, the Tanwa. The most notable member of this clan was Suvam Tan, operator of Yavin Station in the Yavin system. Category:Rodian clans
