thumb|right|200px|Eidos Interactive logo Eidos Interactive is a video game publisher that published LEGO Star Wars: The Video Game. However, Eidos did not produce the sequel, LEGO Star Wars II: The Original Trilogy, as LucasArts is the publisher. Starting April 22nd, 2009, Eidos became a subsidiary of Japanese video game developer and publisher Square Enix Category:Computer and video game companies
