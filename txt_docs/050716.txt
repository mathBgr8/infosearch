Trehla Keelo resided in Mos Eisley after the Battle of Yavin. During that time, she used to spend a lot of time near the spaceport from where she tried to help spacers and newcomers in Mos Eisley to be more familiar with the city that she considered as a hive of scum and villainy. Thanks to her numerous and varied acquaintances, including Mayor Mikdanyell Guh'rantt, the entertainer Allura, the medic Kazen Harn, and the Quarren Gendra, she also helped these spacers to find a respectable profession. In 1 ABY, Trehla Keelo started to work with a spacer who just arrived on Tatooine after escaping an Imperial space station with the help of one of her acquaintances Darex Rath. With the assistance of the spacer, she discovered that Tusken Raiders were gathering near Mos Eisley in order to attack the city. After a skirmish with the Tuskens in Junktown, she dispatched a spacer to travel to Mos Espa to inform the journalist Trace Lyson about the situation. It was later discovered that the crime lord Jabba the Hutt was inciting the Tusken Raiders to attack Mos Eisley in order to pressurize the mayor of Mos Eisley and eventually take control of the city. Trehla Keelo was also acquainted with Vourk ver'Zremp, who was working in Jabba Desilijic Tiure's criminal empire. Although she despised the Hutt crime lord, she tried to assist anyway individuals forced to work for the Hutt. Vourk ver'Zremp therefore often referred new Jabba henchmen to her. In 1 ABY, she also helped a spacer who arrived on Tatooine with Han Solo from Tansarii Point Station. Trehla Keelo was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Trehla Keelo was added to the game with the release of the "Publish 17," on May 26, 2005. She provided several introductory quests to help new players who spawned on Tatooine right after the completion of the "New Player Tutorial" to chose their "profession."
