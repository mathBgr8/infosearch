Dejock
1341

Ensign Dejock was a male technician in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. During the Battle of Praesitlyn, he was onboard the Ranger, under the command of Jedi Master Nejaa Halcyon. When the ship was under attack by the Confederacy of Independent Systems, he helped Master Halcyon and several soldiers to hold off the droids and giving enough time for the rest of the crew to evacuate. *Jedi Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Republic Navy officers Category:Technicians
