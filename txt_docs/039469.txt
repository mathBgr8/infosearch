The Forbidden City
1712

The Forbidden City was a city on the Mid Rim planet Lahsbane. It was the home of the mature Huhks, known for their tendency towards violence, and as such, off-limits to offworlders for their own protection. The city was built on a scale appropriate to the large Huhks, and as such had huge doors and windows. This was surprising to any offworlders that did enter the city as it seemed completely out of proportion to the diminutive Lahsbees, the Huhks' more docile, immature form who were more frequently encountered by offworlders. The Forbidden City contained a treasure room that once contained tapes recorded by crashed Rebel pilot Yom Argo. Rebel agents Leia Organa and Dani retrieved the tapes after Argo's death, with Dani also stealing some treasure on the side. *Star Wars (1977) 73 Forbidden City, The
