Scissors
1394

thumb|right|250px|[[C-3PO/Legends|C-3PO uses scissors and a scalpel to bandage Nak Pitareeze's leg.]] Scissors were a hand-held tool made of two blades used to cut thin materials, such as hair. Pruning scissors were used to trim or sculpt plants and bushes.
