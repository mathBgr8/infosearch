Villip Mistress
577

Villip Mistresses were the communications aides who served the Yuuzhan Vong military as well as high-ranking officials. Usually a member of the shaper or intendant castes, a villip mistress would coordinate villip choirs during battles, allowing for quick communication between various unit leaders.
