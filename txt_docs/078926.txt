Melaan Nar
1099

Melaan Nar was a male Yuuzhan Vong who was a member of the Intendant caste. A Consul of Domain Nar, he developed a rivalry with Consul Von of Domain Shul. Supreme Overlord Shimrra, however, would have none of this and ordered the two to put aside their differences and concentrate their attentions on defeating the Galactic Alliance. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Nar, Melaan Nar, Melaan
