Wurger
1172

thumb|right|250px|Wurger 2 under fire. Wurger was group of Assassin-class corvettes used by the Rebel Alliance, that contained troops for Admiral Harkov's fleet. They were eventually destroyed by Imperial pilot Maarek Stele. Wurger goes as a group of 2 corvettes which appear in 2 missions; firstly, before the Protector departure of DS 5; and subsequently when DS 5 is captured. However, as a secret objective both corvettes in both missions have to be destroyed.
