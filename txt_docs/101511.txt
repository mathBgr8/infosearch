Adebsu clan
502

The Adebsu Clan was a Rodian clan on the planet of Rodia. Based on the Betu continent, it owned the small Adebsu Enclave. It owed its loyalties to the Chekkoo clan. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Rodian clans
