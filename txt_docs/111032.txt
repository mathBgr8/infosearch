Cybernetic Experience Chip
766

framed|right Cybernetic Experience Chips were cybernetic devices that allowed their users to increase the experience they gained from their involvement in and exposure to certain actions. This chip was an in-game bonus item in Sony Online Entertainment's MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided. It was awarded to new players that had played the 14 day Star Wars Galaxies trial of October 2006 and later chose to upgrade their trial-account to a full Star Wars Galaxies account. The chip gave players a 20% experience bonus on each of its 50 uses. Category:Cybernetics
