Otoh Gunga Zoological Research Facility
2784

The Otoh Gunga Zoological Research Facility, also known as Otoh Gunga Municipal Zoo or Otoh Gunga Zoo, was a zoo and laboratory structure located in the Gungan city of Otoh Gunga. Built inside of a hydrostatic bubble linked with the other structures of the underwater city of Otoh Gunga, the Zoological Research Facility, or ZRF, was a place where wild animals could be seen and studied. Primarily featuring marine animals that inhabited the swamps and undersea trenches of Naboo, the zoo also kept terrestrial beasts and avian species. Each species of creature was kept in a custom habitat where a small holodisplay presented information on the animal so that visitors might learn and visualize how the animal behaved in the wild. Maintained by highly trained keepers, such as Yr'Apaly Omin, the ZRF was a secure area, with its own security. Containing a research institute that investigated all manner of scientific inquiry, marine scientists, biologists, and researchers milled about the ZRF on a daily basis. Sometime before the Invasion of Naboo, Jar Jar Binks accidentally deactivated half of the bubbles containing the wildlife.
