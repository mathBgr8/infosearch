Tendo
1999

Tendo was a MB-C1 medium transport in the service of the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. The vessel was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupters resupply convoy, which was lead by the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Monitor to where the Corrupter lay damaged in the Mev system. The ship, which bore a cargo of engineering droids, was then attacked by Rebel Alliance raiders and was destroyed along with the rest of the convoy. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:MB-C1 medium transports Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
