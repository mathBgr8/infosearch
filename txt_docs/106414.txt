MSE-9 General Purpose Droid
571

MSE-9 General Purpose Droids were MSE-series droids that often served as security guards. They were equipped with an electro photo receptor, audio sensor, holocamera, a retractable heavy manipulator, and a retractable fine manipulator. *Choices of One MSE-09
