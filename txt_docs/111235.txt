HighBridge Audio
3103

thumb|right|150px|HighBridge Audio logo HighBridge Audio is the publisher of the original Star Wars audio dramas, as well as several additional audio dramatizations. For Return of the Jedi, HighBridge undertook the drama's production, in addition to publishing the recording. HighBridge publishes audiobooks, audio/radio dramatizations, and spoken word audio on CD and digital downloadable files. It was founded by Minnesota Public Radio and incorporated in 1989. From 1991 to 2007, the Penguin Group USA Inc distributed HighBridge's products. From 2007 to 2014, HighBridge products were distributed by Workman Publishing.
