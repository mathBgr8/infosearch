Galactic Chirgotta Awareness Council
689

The Galactic Chirgotta Awareness Council was formed to heighten the public awareness of the intestinal parasite Chirgotta and to encourage good intestinal hygiene. It frequently held public events with entertainer performances to inform the public about the parasite. The council is mentioned in the mission "Chirgotta Awareness Council Grant" which is available at Entertainer Mission Terminals in Star Wars Galaxies. A booking agent hires the player to perform at a certain location to promote the council's goals. Category:Imperial organizations
