It had a number of prairies, mountains, and jungles located along its surface. At some point before the Battle of Yavin, the 1st Tapani Assault Battlegroup claimed an Imperial victory in the Rout of Spuma, which High General Cassio Tagge later commemorated in the Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide.Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide Later, it was on Spuma that New Republic Intelligence Service first discovered increased trooper recruitment by the Imperial warlord Grand Admiral Harrsk's fleet nearly eight years after the Battle of Endor.
