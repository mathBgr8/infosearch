Hex Bomber
2664

The Hex Bomber, also known as a Sabaoth Hex Bomber or a Sabaoth Bomber, was a bomber starfighter used by Sabaoth Squadron of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Hex Bombers were slow and not very agile compared with Sabaoth starfighters, but capable of carrying several kind of warheads including concussion missiles or missiles and bombs modified to include deadly Hex warheads; weapons crafted on Nod Kartha loaded with the chemical Trihexalon. Hex Bombers were often sent into combat alongside larger Hex Deployers, both vessels being escorted by Sabaoth fighters. Several Hex Bomber squadrons were sent by Captain Cavik Toth to destroy an island settlement on Maramere, following Toth's pact with Count Dooku a decade after the Invasion of Naboo. The Bombers were destroyed by Jedi Master Adi Gallia, with help from Reti and Sol Sixxa. Hex Bombers were also deployed during the First Battle of Geonosis, when Toth's Hex fleet launched an assault against the Jedi Knights fighting on the planet's surface.
