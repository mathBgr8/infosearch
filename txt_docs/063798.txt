Galaxy Chance
1165

The Galaxy Chance, a CR90 corvette, was a mobile smuggling base and casino, similar to Booster Terrik's Star Destroyer Errant Venture, but on a much smaller scale. It was owned and operated by a rival of Terrik, who, as a taunt to him, had painted the Galaxy Chance in the same bright red that Terrik desired for the Errant Venture. In 11 ABY, the Khuiumin Survivors raided the Galaxy Chance to abduct one of its passengers, Zlece Oonaar, who had commanded the Victory-class Star Destroyer Crusader during the Battle of Khuiumin.
