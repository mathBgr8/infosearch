Night Blades
1957

The Night Blades were a starfighter squadron made up of Jedi pilots flying StealthX fighters, led by Jedi Master Saba Sebatyne. This was one of the six squadrons which comprised the StealthX wing led by Grand Master Luke Skywalker.Legacy of the Force: Inferno During the Battle of Kashyyyk, the Night Blades lost three pilots in an unsuccessful attack on the Victory-class Star Destroyer Vulnerator, representing around one-quarter of their combat strength. They had already lost one other pilot, Tahiri Veila, when she deserted the Jedi before the battle.
