Lup's Wares and Supplies
2586

Lup's Wares and Supplies, commonly known as Lup's General Store, was a location in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. The store was was owned by two Shistavanen, Kal Lup and Tar Lup and located near Outer Curved Street, near Spaceport Speeders. The store was stocked with provisions, supplies, and machinery for sale. Customers in need of weapons and landspeeders were directed to stores that specialized in that kind of equipment.Tatooine Manhunt The Lups sold everything from provisions to supplies and machinery. In 0 BBY at least one Mobquet A-1 Deluxe Floater was available for sale at the store. Many items could be viewed through touch-screens where the items were promoted. The less legal activities took place in a bathroom and the store's offices. The owners had some problems with Jabba Desilijic Tiure, because even when sales were low, Jabba kept asking the same amount of protection money. 250px|thumb|Lup's on a map of Mos Eisley Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy changed the location of the store.
