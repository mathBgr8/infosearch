Resolute (Imperial-class)
2128

The Resolute was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer. After the Battle of Hoth, it responded to a Rebel attack on a convoy consisting of BFF-1 bulk freighters M-S and Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports Epsilon, but it arrived too late, as over half of the convoy had already been destroyed. B-wings that had attacked the convoy turned on this starship afterwards.
