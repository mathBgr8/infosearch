Action-Keynne XII transport
1840

The Action-Keynne XII transport was a bulk transport built by the Corellian Engineering Corporation. It was noted for its luxurious crew accommodations and, in a radical departure from previous Action series transports, it mounted armament on par with a small capital ship. The Action-Keynne XII was an expensive ship, used primarily by the wealthy Sarkans to ship only their most valuable cargo. When Luke Skywalker and Han Solo went to the Iphigini homeworld, pirates called a customs alert on an Action-Keyenne transport to distract Iphigini security whilst they slipped in. *Specter of the Past Category:Action series models
