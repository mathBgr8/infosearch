Ixnoltah
1367

Ixnoltah was a male Dug who worked for Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Around 32 BBY, he earned a bounty on his head by the Pixelito Grand Council for dealing death sticks to the Gran youth of Pixelito. His bounty was claimed by Jango Fett. Ixnoltah's bounty was worth 3,000 credits alive and 1,500 dead. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Dugs Category:Males
