Annual Mid-Rim Domesticated Sub-Sentient Show
877

The Annual Mid-Rim Domesticated Sub-Sentient Show was a show for the best sub-sentient creatures in the Mid Rim. It was held annually in Munto, Commenor. In one unusual year, a Sullustan wilwog named Itipiniwi was awarded first prize of Best in Show. The owner got excited, exciting Itipiniwi, which also annoyed the runner-up's creature, a Kashyyyk bolstyngar named Boshuda. Boshuda ate Itipiniwi and because rules never dealt with the consumption of the winner by the runner-up, the runner-up was declared the winner due to forfeit. Category:Exhibitions
