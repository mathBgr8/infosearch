Battle of Denab
3672

The Battle of Denab was an engagement fought early during the Galactic Civil War, prior to the Battle of Yavin, between the Alliance to Restore the Republic and the Galactic Empire's Imperial Fourth Attack Squadron at Denab. The battle was part of General Jan Dodonna's campaign to prove that starfighters were the most versatile military asset possessed by the Alliance, and that their tactical use would bring victories and undermine the Imperial control of space. The battle began when BTL Y-wing starfighters, under the command of the RodianSecrets of the Sisar Run Grisserno, attacked the Victory-class Star Destroyers of the Imperial Fourth Attack Squadron. The squadron was arrayed in two wings, and the Alliance ships methodically concentrated their fire on each one in turn, defeating each one at a time.
