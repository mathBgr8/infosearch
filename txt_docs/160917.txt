|type=Paperback |pages=160 |dimensions=10.8 x 8.3 x 0.4 inches |weight=1 pounds |isbn=0761547096 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide was a strategy guide published by Prima Games for Star Wars: Battlefront. The game guide explains and describes the movement, the combat basics, the gameplay concepts, the various modes of the game, the units and classes, the weapons and equipment, vehicles and turrets, and all the battlefields and locations included in Star Wars: Battlefront. Fight the classic Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide battles any way you want to. *Tactics from the testers for all the battles. *Gameplay tips and stats for units, weapons, NPCs, and items. *Team strategies for all 16 maps. *Exclusive background information for all vehicles and weapons. Maps detailing command posts, vehicle spawn points, and defensive features. Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide
