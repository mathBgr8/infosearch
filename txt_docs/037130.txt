ASP-704
2125

ASP-704 was a masculine ASP-7 labor droid imported by Eugene Talmont, the Imperial prefect of Mos Eisley spaceport on the planet Tatooine, shortly before the Battle of Yavin in the year 0 BBY. It was tasked with loading cargo aboard a GR-45 medium transport.
