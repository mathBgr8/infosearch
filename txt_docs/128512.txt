Interloper Squadron
792

Interloper Squadron, along with Stranger Squadron, was one of two squadrons of TIE/D Defender starfighters that was dispatched by Colonel Broak Vessery, under orders from the "real" Ysanne Isard, to rescue Rogue Squadron from the trap set by Prince-Admiral Delak Krennel at the Battle of Distna. The squadron included Interloper Eight and Interloper Twelve. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial starfighter squadrons
