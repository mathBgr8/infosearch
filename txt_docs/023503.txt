Daggibus Scoritoles
1476

Daggibus Scoritoles was a male Givin senator who represented the planet Yag'Dhul in the Galactic Senate. In 22 BBY, just before the start of the Clone Wars, he and his people joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
