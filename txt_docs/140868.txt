Relic (article)

"Relic" was an article for the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. It was published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 6, and was reprinted in Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal. The article detailed the quest for a relic of the Kashi Mer Dynasty, by a group of New Republic agents who were competing with the dark Jedi, Durrei. *Star Wars Adventure Journal 6 *Classic
