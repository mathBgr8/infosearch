Trandoshan holding cell
423

A Trandoshan holding cell was any type of durasteel holding cell made by the Trandoshans. They were commonly used to hold captive slaves. They had to be able to sustain massive damage, as Wookiees were meant to be held in them. *Star Wars: Republic Commando Category:Holding cells
