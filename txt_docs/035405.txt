Gron Marrab
2197

Gron Marrab was a male Mon Calamari senator. He represented Calamari sector in the New Republic Senate and its Galactic Alliance successor. He served on the Council on Security and Intelligence. Following the Battle of Coruscant, Marrab was made leader of the commission to maintain and restore the New Republic's communications networks.
