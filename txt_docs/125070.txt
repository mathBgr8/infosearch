Silver Bantha
498

The Silver Bantha was an award given to droid models for "excellence in industrial design". The GH-7 medical droid won this award four times, according to a Chiewab Amalgamated Pharmaceuticals Company sales manual. It was named after the banthas of Tatooine. *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Awards
