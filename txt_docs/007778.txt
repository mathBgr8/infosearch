Reeve Dallows
2386

Reeve Dallows was a Human male pilot and captain of the freighter Charm's Way and the father of Rhys Dallows of Bravo Squadron Reeve was the pilot of the freighter Charm's Way. He married a schoolteacher and around 54 BBYStar Wars: Starfighter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide they welcomed their son, Rhys. However their happiness did not last for long. Reeve and his ship disappeared under mysterious circumstances soon after, and was widely believed to have been lost in a pirate attack. For the couple of the following decades, he was a slave serving a pirate base. As Rhys grew up, he inherited his father's piloting talent, and decided to become a pilot and protect others from his father's fate.
