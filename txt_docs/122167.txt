Mynock Puffing
395

Mynock Puffing was a game which was engaged in by several sport hunters. It involved attaching helium-based grenades to a mynock, and, due to their allergic reaction to helium, they would swell up and explode. *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Blood sports
