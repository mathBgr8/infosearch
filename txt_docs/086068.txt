Hyperspace transponder
2969

A hyperspace transponder was a device that allowed messages to be sent through hyperspace. Hyperspace transponders varied in size, with at least some transponders being small enough that they became handheld devices. Additionally, hyperspace transponders varied in reliability and quality, with standard transponders not always being reliable in sending messages.
