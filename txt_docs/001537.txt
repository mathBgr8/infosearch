}} The BT-16 was a piece of technology used in the Galactic Empire. It was considered new as of 1 BBY.Star Wars: Galactic Atlas: Updated Edition When the Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi deactivated the first Death Star's tractor beam to allow the Millennium Falcon to leave Docking Bay 327, he overheard two stormtroopers conversing about the BT-16. The subtitles on Disney+ identifies it as VT-16. Voice actor Sam Witwer added lines spoken by stormtroopers in other Star Wars films that referenced the scene which mentioned the BT-16 by mentioning the T-15, T-16, T-17 and T-18. In Star Wars Legends, the BT-16 was a perimeter droid model.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Vol. 1 *LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga Category:Technology
