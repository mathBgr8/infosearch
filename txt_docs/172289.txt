Hyperbaric recharge globe
560

Hyperbaric recharge globes were medical devices of unknown purposes. Wat Tambor used one to recover from his ordeal at the hands of the Republic during his captivity. "Hyperbaric" refers to above-average pressure, suggesting that the hyperbaric recharge globe has to do with the Skakoans' need for high air pressure. Category:Medical technology
