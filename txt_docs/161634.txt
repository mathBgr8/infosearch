A smuggler's reverse was a tactic where an individual in a small spacecraft would use their maneuvering thrusters to make an 180° turn with the engines still on, in order to quickly change directions without turning in an arc. *Vision of the Future *Fate of the Jedi: Backlash Category:Tactics
