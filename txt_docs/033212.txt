"Mouse" Temple
1169

"Mouse" Temple was a small, nervous con artist who was politician Mon Mothma's contact on the planet of Renor, and was supposed to arrange her transport to the Alliance to Restore the Republic in the Outer Rim shortly after the formation of the Galactic Empire. Mothma's entourage was captured by Imperial Security Bureau agents, and Mothma herself was taken by Imperial Senator Pel Talon's personal guard. "Mouse" still boarded the ship that was planned to get Mothma to the Outer Rim, the Blackjack, captained by Jax Keyn.
