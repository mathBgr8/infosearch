Shelltooth
1838

The Shelltooth was a modified CorelliSpace Gymsnor-3 light freighter owned by the Quor'sav trader Kal'Falnl C'ndros. It possessed a Class 1 hyperdrive. It was customized to have corridors that accommodated her 3.5 meter height, and to have a protected chamber where her clutch of eggs was kept for the year necessary for them to hatch. *Secrets of the Sisar Run Category:Gymsnor-3 light freighters
