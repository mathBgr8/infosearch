In the later stages of the Galactic Civil War, she served under the command of Grand Moff Dureya, with Lieutenant Davith Sconn as her first officer. In a notorious incident in around 4 ABY,The date of the bombardment of Gra Ploven can be calculated from the fact that Sconn had spent twelve years in jail on Jagg Island as of 16 ABY. Governor Dureya ordered Forger to subject the planet Gra Ploven to punitive bombardment, creating boiling clouds that killed 200,000 of the native Ploven.
