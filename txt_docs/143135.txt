Darson Kinderlot
1857

Darson Kinderlot was a Frell spacer active by the time of the Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon in 4 ABY.The New Essential Chronology Following that event, Kinderlot was in Mos Eisley, a city on the planet Tatooine.Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition The Klatooinian Umpass-stay went to the city and met Kinderlot, who let him work in exchange for transporting the Klatooinian to Hutt Space. Umpass-stay ultimately would find work for the Hutt Jocoro Desilijic Tiure on the planet Du Hutta. Darson Kinderlot was first mentioned in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003.
