Halurian ice-boar
2472

|lifespan= |origin=Located in the Haluria systemThe Essential Atlas |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Halurian ice-boars were large, porcine creatures native to a location in the Haluria system of the Mid Rim. The creatures had thick hide plates that covered and protected their bodies. The plates were considered to be nearly impervious to such bladed tools as knives. When the Dark Lord of the Sith Skere Kaan attempted to dominate the mind of his fellow Sith Lord, Darth Bane, shortly beforeDarth Bane: Path of Destruction the final battle of the New Sith Wars on the planet of Ruusan in 1000 BBY, Bane compared Kaan's efforts with a knife scraping against an ice-boar's hide plates.
