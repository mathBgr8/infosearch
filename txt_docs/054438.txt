Aggressor (Acclamator-class)
3230

Aggressor was an Acclamator-class assault ship serving as a frigate in the navy of the Galactic Empire, in service during the Galactic Civil War around the time of the Battle of Yavin. Equipped with a shield generator, engines, and a hyperdrive, it was armed with a proton torpedo launcher, concussion missile launcher, and turbolasers, as well as laser cannons. It carried several squadrons of TIE fighters and TIE bombers in its hangar.
