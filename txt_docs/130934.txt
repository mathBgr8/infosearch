Hall of Masters
499

The Hall of Masters was a long, ornate hall in the Fountain Palace on Hapes. It had red qashmel carpets, fine artwork, arched corridors, and white alabas stairways at either end. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hapes locations Category:Museums Category:Rooms
