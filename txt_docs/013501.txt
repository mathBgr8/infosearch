The Tipsy Mynock
501

The Tipsy Mynock was a cantina on Karfeddion. Its owners and patrons were great fans of the local smashball team, the Skull Crackers. Lope often visited the Tipsy Mynock when he was on-planet. *Cloak of Deception Category:Cantinas Category:Karfeddion locations
