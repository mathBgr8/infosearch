Chalactan Enlightenment
432

The Chalactan Enlightenment was the highest spirutual attainment of the Chalactan Adepts. Those who achieved it wore the Greater Mark of Illumination. The Enlightenment was symbolized by the Uncloseable Eye. *Shatterpoint Category:Religions
