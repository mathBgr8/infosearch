Xerxes (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
1988

The EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Xerxes served in the Rebel Alliance Navy at the Battle of Mylok IV during the Galactic Civil War. Imperial forces, led by Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin, destroyed Frigate Xerxes as Rebel leaders attempted to receive new hyperdrive technology from dissident Nharwaak researchers.
