The Star Hummer was a ship owned by Joa Pqis. The ship was registered on the planet Vobos with registration number RN80-440330. In 16 ABY, Luke Skywalker tracked this ship as it passed the inner flight control buoys of the planet Teyr. *Shield of Lies Category:Starships
