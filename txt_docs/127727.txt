Dargon
2994

The Dargon was an Imperial Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser that served the Empire as a prison ship. Its escort typically included TIE Avengers and Assault Gunboats. Carrying Bothan and Rebel prisoners, it came under attack from Rebel forces bent on rescuing prisoners, at least twice. In Star Wars: TIE Fighter, the player (acting as Maarek Stele) prevents the Dargon from being disabled, ensuring that the prisoners can be brought to Lord Vader and interrogated by him. The Rebels sent three waves of starfighters then two CR90 corvettes called Keeper and an Assassin-class corvette called Hut'to. In another mission involving the Dargon, its engines shut down, possibly through sabotage. The Rebels then attack again with a Nebulon-B2 frigate called Bay Point with Y-wings and A-wings. In Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter, the Rebels attempt to disable the Dargon with starfighters and the MC80 cruiser Columbia.
