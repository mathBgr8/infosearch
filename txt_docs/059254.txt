Kiilimaar was the site of a battle between the Galactic Empire and the Rneekii pirates shortly prior to the Battle of Endor. It began with a dispute over an abducted TIE Defender R&D scientist, in which the Rneekii demanded a ransom for the scientist's safe return. The Empire tricked the Rneekii by handing over the ransom, retrieving the scientist, then sending their code to attack the Rneekii "full speed to Kiilimaar" and take back the ransom into Imperial hands.
