Battle of Bompreil
2214

Sometime before the Destruction of Alderaan, a stormtrooper unit attempted to root out a Rebel cell on the planet Bompreil. In the skirmish Imperial forces, including the stormtrooper Daric LaRone, destroyed an Alliance resistance cell in an operation that resulted in many civilian casualties. *Allegiance *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles in the Demetras sector Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
