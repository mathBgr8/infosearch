Star Wars: Young Jedi Knights
5885

Star Wars: Young Jedi Knights is a Legends young-adult novel series from authors Kevin J. Anderson and Rebecca Moesta. The series is set in 23 ABY and follows the adventures of Jedi twins Jacen and Jaina Solo. Star Wars: Young Jedi Knights comprises fourteen volumes that were published between 1995 and 1998 by Berkley Boulevard. A companion series titled Star Wars: Junior Jedi Knights was published concurrently. The Star Wars: Young Jedi Knights novels were republished in several Science Fiction Book Club titles: The Rise of the Shadow Academy (1996), The Fall of the Diversity Alliance (1998), Under Black Sun (1999), Jedi Shadow (2002), and Jedi Sunrise (2003). Young Jedi Knights was a very popular series of novels geared to teens and was instrumental in developing the characters of Jacen and Jaina. Jaina truly takes after her father, being more brash and mechanically inclined, while Jacen is more contemplative, attuned to nature, and has a corny sense of humor. The series would last fourteen books and introduce a supporting cast of young Jedi characters – Tenel Ka, Lowbacca, Zekk, Raynar Thul – who would play important roles later in the timeline, starting with the New Jedi order novels. Qorl's pilot designation of CE3K-1977 is a nod to Steven Spielberg's Close Encounters of the Third Kind, a 1977 motion picture contemporary to Star Wars.
