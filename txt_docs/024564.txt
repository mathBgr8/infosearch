General Spacetronics
1075

General Spacetronics was a starship manufacturer who produced the MaKing-class transport. They offered licenses to local interests to build some of their craft, such as the "Janako" model of the MaKing-class. *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game Category:Shipwrights
