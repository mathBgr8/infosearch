Unidentified Wookiee Rebel
1448

This unidentified Wookiee rebel was a male Wookiee who was recruited by Commander Col Serra to join Rebel Alliances' new elite fighting unit, Renegade Squadron. During the Second Battle of Sullust he was killed alone with a Rebel Vanguard when IG-88 jumped out of a Sentinel-class landing craft and landed nearby.
