Unidentified Crusader-class corvette
2186

A new Crusader-class corvette was illegally acquired by the Zann Consortium shortly after 0 ABY. Pirates working for Tyber Zann had been attacking starships near Mandalore, soon Mandal Hypernautics agreed to supply Crusader-class corvettes to Zann's forces in exchange for a cease-fire. Also Tyber Zann received a free Crusader-class corvette built on Mandalore, this vessel. Urai Fen, Zann's bodyguard, found it curious that the pirate captain send the ship to Tyber Zann instead of keeping it for himself.
