Destabilization
3274

Destabilization, also known as Destab, was a branch of Imperial Intelligence's Bureau of Operations. Their operations were kept quiet, as details of their activities&mdash;which ranged from legitimate Intelligence operations to full scale atrocities&mdash;were enough to make most agents sick. Officially, their mandate was to unravel the fabric of society. During the Galactic Civil War, a team of Destabilization Branch infiltrators traveled to the city Morjanssik on the planet Mon Calamari and enacted the Calamari Project, a covert operation that aimed to spark a war between the Mon Calamari and Quarren species, to prevent the two species from supplying starships to the Rebel Alliance.
