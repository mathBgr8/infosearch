Under Secretary of Scout Services
613

The Under Secretary of Scout Services, also referred to as the Under Minister of Scout Services, was the highest-ranking bureaucrat in the New Republic Scout Service. The Under Secretary answered directly to the New Republic's Minister of State, and had oversight of all the divisions of the NRSS. *Galaxy Guide 8: Scouts Category:Bureau of Scouting and Exploration Services Category:Political titles
