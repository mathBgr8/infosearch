Old Janx Spirit
1367

Old Janx Spirit was a strong, distilled liquor. It&mdash;along with Sullustan gin and spicebrew&mdash;could be used to make the cocktail Sonic Servodriver.MedStar II: Jedi Healer Juno Eclipse had a bottle in her safe aboard the Rebel frigate Salvation, which she planned to open when Emperor Palpatine was dead.Star Wars: The Force Unleashed II novelization "Old Janx Spirit" is also the name of an alcoholic beverage mentioned in the radio play and novel versions of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.The Essential Reader's Companion *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *Star Wars: The Force Unleashed II novelization Category:Liquors
