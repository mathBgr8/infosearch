Gizer L-6 freighter
1355

The Gizer L-6 freighter was a class of freighter developed on Gizer that was used predominantly during the Clone Wars as a civilian transport craft. Omega Squad was tasked to interdict a Separatist Gizer L-6 freighter in 21 BBY. The mission was a near disaster. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero Category:Freighter classes
