Tribune
1284

thumb|right|A meeting of the Tribunal, left to right: [[Plumba, General Paltr Carvin and Challer]] Tribune was the title given to certain high-ranking members of the Imperial Ruling Council between the removal of Sate Pestage from the Imperial throne and the rise of Ysanne Isard.
