The Grom 1 was an Airam Assassin-class corvette which together with its sister ship, the Grom 2, accompanied the Anat on its ill-fated final trip near the Blair Cluster. Upon being pulled out of hyperspace by the Star Destroyer Rage and Interdictor Compellor, the Grom 1 was ordered to engage.
