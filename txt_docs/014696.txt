Zekulae
2552

Zekulae was a planet located at the heart of the Zeemacht Cluster within the Zekulae system of the Inner Rim. It was a barren world, noted for its dark, rich mineral soil, which was studded with blue crystals. Its soil was extremely fine and so was everpresent on the planet. Its inhabitants were known as the Zeku.
