Taxi speeder
2326

thumb A taxi speeder, also known as a hovertaxi, hover cab, grav-cab, repulsorcab, or repulsortaxiAlliance Intelligence Reports was the name applied to landspeeders that functioned as taxis. Unlike their airspeeder counterparts, air taxis, taxi speeders operated close to the ground.
