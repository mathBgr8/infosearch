Norulac Freebooters
2168

The Norulac Freebooters, also known as the Norulac Pirates, were a group of pirates originating on the Inner Rim planet of Norulac, and known to be active in that area beginning a few years prior to the Battle of Yavin, if not earlier. They were infamous for their regular raids on the agricultural planet of Taanab, lasting until 0 ABY, when Lando Calrissian happened to be in the system at the time. With the gambler at their helm, the local defense fleet decisively won the Battle of Taanab, capturing or destroying nearly all the Freebooters.
