Razor-class starfighter
3202

The Razor-class starfighter was a product of Starypan/SunHui Spaceworks, manufactured shortly before Alderaan's destruction. It was meant to succeed the R-41 Starchaser and was used by many corporate security forces, including the Viraxo family, as it was cheap and reliable. The craft had shields, a hyperdrive, wingtip dual lasers, dual ion cannon on the nose, and two launchers which carried four concussion missiles each. It was average in combat, as the shielding was fairly poor for a craft of its size.
