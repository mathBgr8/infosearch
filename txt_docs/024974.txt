Niktha
1299

Niktha was a Jawa beggar who lived during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted dead for 200 credits by the owner of the Dust Bowl saloon in Mos Gamos for turning away potential patrons with his aggressive begging.
