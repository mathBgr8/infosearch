|conc=Galactic Civil War |next=Yuuzhan Vong WarThe New Essential Guide to Alien Species |image= |name=Zolan Civil War |begin= |end=c. 25 ABY |date= |place=Zolan |result=Clawdite overthrow and dominate Zolanders |battles= |side1=Clawdite |side2=Zolanders |side3= |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= }} The Zolan Civil War was a war between the Clawdite and Zolanders of Zolan. As the New Republic replaced the Galactic Empire and ended the Imperial Blockade of Zolan, the already existing tensions between the two species reached its limits. The New Republic tried to intercede to establish a peace between the two species. However, the assassination of a New Republic diplomat resulted eventually in a full-scale war. The Clawdite claimed three quarters of the planet, and dominated the Zolanders.
