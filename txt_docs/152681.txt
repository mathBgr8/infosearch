|commission= |destroyed=3963 BBY, over Serroco |retired= |battles=Battle of Serroco |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Republic Navy |navy=Battle Group Serroco |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Perspicacity was a cruiser in the Republic Navy during the Mandalorian Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Mandalorian warrior clans. In 3963 BBY, as the Mandalorians threatened the planet Serroco, Perspicacity was part of Admiral Saul Karath's Battle Group Serroco intended to protect the world. In the ensuing attack, the Mandalorians devastated Serroco with nuclear missiles,Knights of the Old Republic 15 and Perspicacity was lost over the world. The vessel's destruction was later listed in an edition of The Admiral's List, an official publication issued by the Republic Navy. Perspicacity was first mentioned in The Admiral's List: Remember Serroco!
