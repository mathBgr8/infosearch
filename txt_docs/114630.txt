Mirandine
1784

The Mirandine was a Republic Sienar Systems Marauder-class corvette in the Kathol Republic Navy. In 8 ABY the Mirandine responded to a distress call from the Bravado III, alongside her sister ships Juno II, Quillion, Siroma, Solidarity, and Triumph.
