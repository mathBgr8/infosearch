Arena Champion
859

thumb|right|250px|The Entrance to the Kerritamba Village Arena. Arena Champion was a title granted to individuals who defeated all challenges of the Kerritamba Village Arena, including the reigning Arena Champion. The Arena itself was located near the Kerritamba Village, in the Kkowir Forest on Kashyyyk. Kerritamba highly respected its Arena Champions. Several rewards existed for Arena Champions, ranging from general respect to a badge of accomplishment awarded by the village chief. Two recorded Arena Champions were Wirartu and an unidentified individual who helped Chief Kerritamba complete certain tasks. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees Category:Titles
