Steady Lady

The Steady Lady was a sleek, 500-meter-long fast-haul bulk cruiser that could carry 1000 passengers. It was above Coruscant in 27 ABY and helped the Coruscant Planetary Defense Force evacuate refugees from the Sweet Surprise. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:Bulk cruisers Category:Starships of the New Republic
