Battle of Mantessa
1492

The Battle of Mantessa was fought during the Yuuzhan Vong War. A Yuuzhan Vong force, including Fire Breathers, tried to invade the planet Mantessa but was defeated by the Orange Panthacs&mdash;a platoon of B2 super battle droids. Later, the Orange Panthacs received a special commendation from Chief of State Cal Omas for their defense of the planet. *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles in the Orus sector Category:Battles of the Yuuzhan Vong War
