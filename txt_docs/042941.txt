Archa Sabis
2020

Archa Sabis was a Human male who, due to illness, received cybernetic implants. After creating a fortune on energy shields, Archa Sabis suffered under a sickness known as Knowt's Disease, severely destroying both his social standing and his physical health to the point of needing cybernetic implants. He lived aboard his luxury skyhook in Coruscant, driven mad by isolation and a virus in his cybernetic brain.
