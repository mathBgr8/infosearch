Annihilator (starship)
2175

|commission= |destroyed= |retired= |battles= |affiliation= |navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains=Atha Prime |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Annihilator was the mobile command base of Atha Prime. It resembled two Star Destroyers joined together and was used to launch his Clone Warriors. Prime's personal starfighter, Apex Invader, was able to attach atop the Annihilator which presumably gave him the ultimate command vantage point for battles. The Annihilator was a concept for a vehicle that would have been part of a proposed but ultimately rejected extension of Kenner's Star Wars toy line, The Epic Continues. *The Epic Continues Category:Star Destroyers
