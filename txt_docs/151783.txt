Clive
1108

Clive was the older of two cooks serving on the cruiser Reliable, part of an Imperial fleet blockading the ThonBoka nebula in 3 BBY. He attempted to cheat gambler Lando Calrissian, who had gained entrance to the Reliable posing as a trader, in a game of sabacc. His dealing from the bottom of the deck was clumsy, and Calrissian spotted it right away; it was also ineffective, as the cards he dealt gave the visitor a natural two-card twenty-three.
