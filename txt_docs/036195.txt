thumb|right|200px|A [[Sith/Legends|Sith Minion.]] Sith Minion was a rank of the Sith Order before Darth Bane's reform. They were the lowliest members of the Sith Order. They were often not even Force-sensitive, and sometimes worshiped the Sith Lords as gods. Sith Cultists can be considered examples of Sith Minions. *Knight Errant *The Dark Side Sourcebook Minion
