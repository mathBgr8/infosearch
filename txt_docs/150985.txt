Storm Squadron (Rebel)
1170

Storm Squadron was a SpecForce unit of the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. It was part of Eclipse Team. Around 2 ABY, the Togorian Tynan Ryln Mei took command of the unit. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic Special Forces units
