Togorian Trap additionally denotes an April 1998 release date. |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-02337-3 |issn= |genre= |issue=8 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 8 is the eighth issue Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in April 1998. Issue 8 included part three of the serial comic X-Wing Marks the Spot, written by Mike W. Barr. Star Wars Kids 8 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book.
