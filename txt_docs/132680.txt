|cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |pronouns=new |sensor= |plating= |armament= |equipment=*Crane-like manipulator *Disinfectant spray |affiliation=Star Tours }} P-6 was a treaded droid in service to the Star Tours travel agency as a quarantine inspector. He cleaned the baggage claim area and also inspected any incoming astrocultural freights with his associate, S-4. He was able to do this by disinfecting luggage with a disinfectant spray. Both were supervised by a blue R2 unit. P-6 at some point also performed repairs on StarSpeeder 3000s. The background for this droid comes from a "Spot the Droid" poster displayed at the Tokyo Disneyland Star Tours, which was translated at the fan site, as well as the Complete Guide to Star Tours booklet. The droid was present during the opening day of the first ride, but he and his counterpart, S-4, were eventually removed.
