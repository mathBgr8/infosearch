It was destroyed when its sun went supernova around 198 BBY. The Galactic Republic only managed to evacuate a few of the native Spiners, and by the time of the Galactic Civil War, most people believed the species extinct, a mistake not far from the truth. One remaining Spiner, by the name of Hedji, ws part of Han Solo's freelance group the 'Star-hoppers of Aduba 5', he spoke little of his past due to its suggested horror and distress.
