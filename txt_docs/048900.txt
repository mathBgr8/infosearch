Krish (language)
1062

Krish was the native language of the Krish species from Sanza. Most Krish were fluent in both Krish and Basic, although only literate in their own language. Krish was formed of grunts, bellows and crude vocalizations. The language also had a written form. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
