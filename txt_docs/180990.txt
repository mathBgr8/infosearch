The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 156
1114

The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 156 is the one hundred fifty-sixth issue of the The Empire Strikes Back Monthly series of comic reprints, published in May 1982. This issue includes one of the few original comic stories in the series, "Rust Never Sleeps".
