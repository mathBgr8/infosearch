Fi (High Galactic)
794

"Fi" was a High Galactic expression used by clergymen following the Sacred Way as a term of address directed at non-clergymen. It meant "male child" and was used specifically with male sentients. Grand Admiral Peccati Syn later named his Star Destroyer after the word. *Star Wars (1977) 7 Category:Phrases
