JAKe

thumb|right|250px|"Wookiee Season" JAKe is a UK graphic artist and designer. Although his work has appeared on record sleeves, posters, as toys, and in animated form, his work for Lucasfilm has mainly been for merchandise, for both the Star Wars and Indiana Jones franchises. He has three Star Wars books published by Chronicle - How to Speak Wookiee: A Manual for Intergalactic Communication, How to Speak Droid with R2-D2: A Communication Manual and Star Wars: How to Speak Astromech with BB-8. JAKe is also the creator and director of Geekboy, a series of animated shorts which appeared on the Sci Fi channel, in which the title character meets notable science fiction icons including Darth Vader. JAKe appeared at the Celebration IV and Celebration Europe art shows. Category:Star Wars artists
