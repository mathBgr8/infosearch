Lan Barell (article)
4593

"Lan Barell" is an article written by Paul Sudlow with illustrations by Tim Eldred. It was published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 in February 1994 by West End Games. This article serves as a supplementary piece for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, focusing on the Outer Rim planet Lan Barell, located at the end of the Enarc Run. It explores the Barellian society, its economic history and the political system.
