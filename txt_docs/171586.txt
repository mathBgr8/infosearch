Horvus
1977

Horvus was a group of three Assassin-class corvettes used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Carrying equipment, the corvettes arrived in the Pakuuni system along with Victory-class Star Destroyer Stalwart just as Imperial starfighters were finishing off the last of a Rebel counterattack on the construction site of the proposed XQ1 Platform NL-1 during the Battle on the Frontier. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:CR92a Assassin-class corvettes Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
