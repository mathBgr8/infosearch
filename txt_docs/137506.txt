MD-7 medical droid
1482

The MD-7 medical droid was a model of medical droid and a part of the MD-series that was manufactured by Industrial Automaton. MD-7s were experts in emergency trauma. The line was a prototype model used only in limited sectors, like the MD-8 and MD-9 units.
