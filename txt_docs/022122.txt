Pets' "N" Pieces
1356

}} Pets' "N" Pieces billed itself as the "CoCo District's premier pet store!" A sampling of the pets it sold included anoobas, narglatch, veermok, bantha, eopies, womp rats, nuna, dwarf nuna, dinkos, tookes, and sarlaccs. It specialized in dangerous and rare animals, and was located at 250 Pom Plaza on Coruscant.
