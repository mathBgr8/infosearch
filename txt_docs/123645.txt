Red Tooth
769

Red Tooth was a group of two CR90 corvettes under the service of the Galactic Empire. The corvettes were trying to destroy a B-wing assembly with the help of Assault Gunboats and another corvette group, Purple Claw, which was witnessed by the Habassa.
