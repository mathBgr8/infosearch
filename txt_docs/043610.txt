Drunk Dancer
2951

The Drunk Dancer was a freighter manufactured by the Corellian Engineering Corporation in 39 BBY. She was approximately 150 meters long, and was similar in appearance to a CR70 corvette, but with a circular bridge module. She was equipped with turbolaser and ion cannon emplacements, and a starboard docking bay that housed the crew's dropship and was capable of also holding craft as large as an SX troop shuttle.
