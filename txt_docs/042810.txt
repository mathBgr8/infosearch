It could carry up to 50 troops and 500 kilograms of equipment, with a crew requirement of 3 people. thumb|left|200px|A side view The enhanced structure of the shuttle allowed for the ramming of enemy vessels. Plasma torches mounted around the airlock could then open up a hole, which the airlock would seal itself around. In less than two minutes, boarding troops would be able to deploy into the target vessel. The Katarn-class' only armament was one double laser cannon mounted at the front. Katarn-class shuttles were often stationed aboard Nebulon-B frigates when in the Imperial forces.
