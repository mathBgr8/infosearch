Panda (transports)
1713

Panda group was a pair of Lambda-class T-4a shuttles used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. When the Alliance succeeded in stranding the Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Priam near Kalla VII, General Jan Dodonna gave the order for a "board and capture" operation against the Priam. A strike by Gold Squadron's Y-wings disabled the ship and Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports from Assault group delivered commandos from Alliance Special Forces to capture the vessel. Once captured, Panda 1 delivered officers and Panda 2 brought an Alliance crew to take control of the ship while transport group Jordi delivered supplies.
