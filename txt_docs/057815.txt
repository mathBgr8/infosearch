Kulltu Kutta rode a sowsquatch during battles in the Cauldron on Rattatak. Sowsquatch first appeared as an unidentified creature in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003. The creature was later given a name in Ultimate Sticker Book: Star Wars: Clone Wars, a sticker book written by Simon Beecroft and released in 2004.
