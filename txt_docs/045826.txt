Space mine
2657

*Resistance *First OrderPoe Dameron Annual 1 }} Space mines, also known as orbital mines or simply mines, were a type of mine used in space warfare. An Imperial TIE/sa light bomber could carry up to six such mines in its main ordnance bay. Space mines could range in size, from large explosives that could devastate the area around it, to smaller mines that could be attached to asteroids.
