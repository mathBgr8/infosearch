thumb|right|200px|A GCT device A Global Communications Transponder (GCT) was an illegal device used for transponding communications. Takara possessed one, and was able to hear Ka'Pa's communications. Ka'Pa himself loathed it and ordered Mara Jade to steal it from Takara in order to aid the New Republic. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith Category:Transponders
