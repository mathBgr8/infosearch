Talar
1225

Talar was a material that could be used to tip arrows used with bows.Alliance Intelligence Reports By 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Kiran Tatch, a Tunroth member of the Tunroth Hunters cultural group and the bounty hunter group known as the Granse Confederacy who preferred to use low-technology weaponry, used talar-tipped arrows with his klirun bow for stunning his targets. Talar was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Materials and textiles
