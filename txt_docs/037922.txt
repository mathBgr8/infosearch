The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 19 is the nineteenth issue of The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine, later rebranded as the Star Wars Insider. Issue 19 was published in Summer 1993. *"Listening to Star Wars: The Original Radio Drama" *"Walking in the Footsteps of a Wookie: Peter Mayhew" *"Welcome to the George Lucas Exhibition" *"David Prowse: The Man Behind the Mask" 019
