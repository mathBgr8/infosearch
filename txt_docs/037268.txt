Onicrop K'Cin
1765

Onicrop K'Cin was a male Ugnaught engineer and criminal affiliated with the Galactic Podracing Mechanics' Union. He had a bounty on his head placed by the Trade Federation Recruitment Center for stealing sensitive Trade Federation encryption codes. In 32 BBY, they learned that he was hiding within Outland Transit Station and using his expertise at forging identities to earn a living. Jango Fett claimed K'Cin's bounty sometime after the Battle of Naboo. Onicrop is located in the second level of Chapter One, and is the second bounty the player encounters. He is worth 1,500 credits alive and 750 dead.
