Kadril
2088

Kadril was an inhospitable, irregularly-shaped world colonized by the reptilian Kadrillians in 6 BBY. It was the source of kunda stones. It was here that Darth Vader developed pacifog. The reptilian Kadrillians were the only inhabitants of the planet to survive the planet's bombardment during the Stenax Massacres of 4 ABY. *"Planet of Kadril" *The Rise and Fall of Darth Vader *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Devastated planets Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories Category:Thanium sector locations
