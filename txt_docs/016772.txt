It was located within the Kira sector, in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Expansion Region. The planet was a gas giant with ten moons, and orbited the yellow star known as Lazerian. Lazerian VII was found along the trade routes known as the Harrin Trade Corridor and Kira Run. It was once surveyed by the Lazerians of Lazerian IV, another planet in the Lazerian system. Lazerian VII was created by John Terra and introduced in Twin Stars of Kira, a 1993 supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
