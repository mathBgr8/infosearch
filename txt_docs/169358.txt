Ring Lake
1013

Ring Lake,Dark Force Rising also referred to as Rim Lake,Dark Force Rising Sourcebook was a lake on Jomark. High Castle was located on an island formed by an extinct cinder cone in the middle of the Ring Lake. It was formed by water filling a collapsed caldera of a volcano. *Dark Force Rising *Dark Force Rising 1 *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Jomark locations Category:Lakes
