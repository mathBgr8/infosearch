Grayweave
1108

Grayweave was a low-cost food created by single-cell organisms inside a grayweave generator. Developed to feed the poor, it resembled thick gray felt and did not taste appetizing, but the food did provide enough nutritional value to sustain a Human.The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand When Coruscant fell to the Yuuzhan Vong, the Coruscanti who were left behind were forced to subsist on grayweave for their continued sustenance.The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Many offworld citizens felt it was a horrible fate for the residents of the former galactic capital. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Food
