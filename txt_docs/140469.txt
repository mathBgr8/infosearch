Vnas
969

Vnas was a location at Duro. The Battle of Vnas was fought there in 1 BBY. During this battle, the BTL Y-wing starfighters proved their value against the Empire. Vnas has never been exactly identified. The Battle of Vnas was only described as the "Battle of Vnas at Duro". Vnas is not another planet in the Duro system and Duro has no moon thus Vnas was probably one of the space city orbiting Duro. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Duro locations
