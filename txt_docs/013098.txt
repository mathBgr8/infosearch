Silvestri Trace
1747

The Silvestri Trace was originally a small, lightly traveled, and all together insignificant hyperspace route, originating from Sullust. When the Galactic Empire chose the Forest Moon of Endor as the site of construction for its second Death Star, the Silvestri Trace was expanded and lengthened to reach the moon.
