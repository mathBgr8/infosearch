Inntak
2740

Inntak was a male Rodian bounty hunter. A member of the dominant Chattza Clan—though with little clan loyalty—he only took bounties for rogue Rodians and those that had angered Navik the Red.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Leading a group of five other hunters, Inntak was tasked to follow secretly the Rodian Galactic Drama Troupe as they performed off-world; actress Entukan Yesosko, leader of the Rodian Galactic Drama Troupe, secretly informed him of the Troupe's schedule and whenever wanted Rodians attended their performances. Inntak and his hunters occasionally scouted the crowd. Some members of the Troupe were aware of this behind-the-scenes operation, and tried to sabotage it. Sometimes Inntak hired non-Rodian hunters in case the bounty could have been suspicious or warned; or if his team were busy with other business. Indeed, one time when the Troupe was about to give their final performance of a tour in Iskaayuma, they sold two thousand tickets to select beings, and Inntak knew that they would include some hidden survivors of the Roolek clan; however Inntak was busy with another hunt and let word in several worlds that he saught hunters for hire. He received candidates and presented the job as a routine bounty, without mentioning the wider politics. Before leaving, he instructed them not to disrupt the play, and collect their bounty only away from the arena, and gave them a comm number.
