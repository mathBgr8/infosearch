Haven's Water
1071

Haven's Water was a tavern on Port Haven operated by the Mon Calamari Mister Mxil. It was a warmly lit building made of stone, and served beverages made with natural ingredients he found in the nearby jungle. Over the entrance, there was an empty water skin hanging to show that it was a place for thirsty customers.Platt's Starport Guide *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Dining establishments
