Dark Eyes, Warm Thoughts
606

"Dark Eyes, Warm Thoughts" was a dusk song written by Erik Lauderslag, performed by Far Cry on Tuttin IV during the Galactic Civil War. Beneath the bassline was a message embedded by Ryley Ancum to the Alliance to Restore the Republic concerning an Imperial shipment of metals for a new warship. Category:Songs
