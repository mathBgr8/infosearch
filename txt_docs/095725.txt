Rog
1086

Rog was a Gamorrean male who was married to Gundruk. He was the Warlord of the Nudskutch holding of the Bolgoink clan and brother of the Clan Warlord Vrokk. He was a huge and powerful boar, but had a relatively small force under his command and he coveted the larger clan army of his brother.
