Henry Buntwhistle
746

Henry Buntwhistle was the alias taken by a Rebel smuggler when he booked into the Lullaby Motel. He was informed that, if he wanted to get in contact with Egot Pai-4-Yem, he was to book into this motel under this assumed name, with a false wife also booking in under the name Martha Buntwhistle.
