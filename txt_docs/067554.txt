Ian Tapley
1054

Ian Tapley co-wrote the backstory of Olana Chion, authored that of Antidar Williams', and provided the first name for Tian Chyler in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. His alias was the fragrant wookiee on StarWars.com. (co-written by Master Starkeiller and Billy Buehler) - February 2006 Category:What's The Story? authors
