Kodir of Kuhlvult
1883

Kodir of Kuhlvult was named Kuat Drive Yards' head of security after she revealed the last head of security, Fenald, to be a spy. Although she revealed the man to be a spy, she herself was no better as she had deposed the last head only to further her own agenda. However, not long after she took the post, her sister, Kateel of Kuhlvult, reappeared, saying she remembered everything. Including the fact that Kodir had been the one who had betrayed her and ordered her mind to be brutally wiped of all of its memories.
