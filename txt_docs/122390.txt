Jaamn
1963

The Jaamn was a Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport that was apparently captured by the Rebel Alliance at some point. The ship was sent to repair the BFF-1 bulk freighter Op Mus which had been stranded in space after an equipment malfunction.
