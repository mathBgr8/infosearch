Lastdark Club
700

The Lastdark Club was an exclusive club in Coronet City on Corellia. It was in the top floor of the world's tallest building, which was where it got its name: when the sun set, it was the last location to be in sunlight. Rostek Horn became a member in 8 ABY and supplied half the club's plant arrangements.
