Ketrann
1360

The Ketrann was a dangerous carnivore native to the Outer Rim Territories planet Alk'Lellish III. Described as "huge and snarling," the Ketrann had four long, white fangs. It fed on migrating herds of metarshi, which it killed by leaping onto the metarshi's back and biting it once through the neck.
