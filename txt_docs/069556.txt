Joaa'n
1547

Private Joaa'n was a muscular, stocky, Human female explosives expert who played a key role in the raiding of the slave ship Helot's Shackle during the Galactic Civil War. She was one of the few members of Red Hand Squadron to live long enough to fight in their last stand at Toprawa&mdash;giving Princess Leia enough time to receive the Death Star plans&mdash;where she was killed. *Rebel Dawn *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alliance Army enlisted personnel Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Red Hand Squadron personnel Category:Technicians
