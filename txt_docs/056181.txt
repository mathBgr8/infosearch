Ona Shai
1389

Ona Shai was the Prefect of the Baanu Miir. She was a Yuuzhan Vong. Ona, a member of Domain Shai, was born with a slight stoop in her spine. Though Yuuzhan Vong born physically deformed were usually killed at birth, Shai was allowed to live, as it did not impede her abilities. Because of the problems facing Baanu Miir, she was quite furious that they were not receiving any help.
