H'merrig
496

H'merrigs were biological constructs created by the Yuuzhan Vong. They were used to dispense food from a few raw materials, and were vital for any Yuuzhan Vong distanced from the rest of their society (whether by choice or by necessity). *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
