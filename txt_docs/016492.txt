|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=19 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The New Essential Chronology dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=79 }} Republic 79 is the seventy-ninth issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on November 9, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics. In the aftermath of the events in Episode III, Jedi on opposite ends of the galaxy try to come to terms with how their world has changed. On the run, with enemies on every side, it seems that their only hope may be to locate others of their kind and band together for mutual survival. But what may seem like wisdom could lead to unsuspected consequences, and the path to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant leads only to danger! Republic embarks on the first step into the "dark times" after Revenge of the Sith! Although in Revenge of the Sith Plo Koon was flying above the cities of Cato Neimoidia, Into the Unknown shows his death at the orbit. Stass Allie shown being killed in a rocky desert environment, while the film shows her in a much wetter environment on Saleucami. Also, unlike in the movie, there are three speeders behind Stass Allie, as opposed to two; she herself rides a BARC speeder, instead of a 74-Z speeder bike. The clones on the speeders use blaster pistols instead of the blasters on their speeders.
