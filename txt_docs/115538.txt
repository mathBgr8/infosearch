San Hill (Human)
2456

|masters= |apprentices= }} San Hill was a Human male and the Chairman of the InterGalactic Banking Clan during the Rebellion era. He was kidnapped on the planet of Muunilinst by bounty hunter IG-88. After his capture, he provided the Zann Consortium with access codes to the IBC's computer systems. Curiously, San Hill bears the same position (Chairman of the InterGalactic Banking Clan) and name as San Hill, and both also reside on Muunilinst. Though it seems that both San Hills were meant to be one and the same, this appearance is likely due to simplification on the part of the game developers, as the Muuns in the information screen for the planet are likewise depicted as human. The original San Hill was assassinated by Vader on Mustafar in 19 BBY, which would cause a contradiction in lore should this assumption be taken.
