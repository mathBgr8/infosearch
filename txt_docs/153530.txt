Micro blaster
922

A micro blaster or mini-blaster was a very small blaster that could easily be concealed in one's garments. It had tiny power packs and was typically only capable of firing two or three shots. However, it did give the user an increased chance to escape certain sticky situations, and it was also so small that even weapon detectors often failed to detect it. One such model was the Gee-Tech 12 Defender MicroBlaster. *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *Far Horizons Category:Hold-out blasters
