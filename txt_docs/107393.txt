thumb|200px|The NBC logo. NBC, or the National Broadcasting Company is one of the major television networks in the United States. NBC is notable in Star Wars history for the network television premieres of Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back in 1987 and Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi in 1989.
