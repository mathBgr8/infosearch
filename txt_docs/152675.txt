|commission= |destroyed=3963 BBY, Serroco |retired= |battles=Battle of Serroco |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Republic Navy |navy=Battle Group Serroco |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} CX-09 was a cargolift vessel in the Republic Navy during the Mandalorian Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Mandalorian warrior clans. When the Mandalorians threatened the planet Serroco in 3963 BBY, CX-09 was part of Battle Group Serroco, a task force put together by Admiral Saul Karath and intended to protect the world. Serroco was devastated with nuclear missiles once the Mandalorians arrived,Knights of the Old Republic 15 and CX-09 was lost. The ship's destruction was later noted in an edition of The Admiral's List, an official publication issued by the Republic Navy, along with two other cargolift vessels: CX-03 and CX-14. CX-09 was first mentioned in The Admiral's List: Remember Serroco!
