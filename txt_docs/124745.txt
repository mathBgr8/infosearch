Idrish
1062

Idrish was a follower of the Jeedai heresy as a subordinate acolyte and rival of Yu'shaa. A Shamed One, he lived in the bowels of the newly crafted Yuuzhan'tar where he flocked to hear Yu'shaa speak. Kunra would be the individual to discover Idrish's plans to overthrow Yu'shaa and take his place, a plan which never came to fruition. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Jeedai Heretics Category:Shamed Ones
