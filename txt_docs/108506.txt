Bubble-and-squirt
494

Bubble-and-squirt was a term used by Whie Malreaux to describe the strange food served onboard the Reasonable Doubt. It came in multiple sizes, including 'large'. Bubble-and-squirt may be a reference to the traditional English dish of Bubble and squeak. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous Category:Food
