Narmox
756

Narmox was a small company that specialized in the production of control systems for various models of starfighters, such as the B-wing starfighter or the Z-95 Headhunter. The company reached the height of its success during the Galactic Civil War. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starship technology companies
