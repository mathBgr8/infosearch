Michael Jan Friedman
2137

Michael Jan Friedman (born March 7, 1955) is an American author. He has written written science fiction, fantasy, paranormal, biographies, and comic books for both DC and Marvel. Friedman is the author of a large number of media tie-ins, including over forty Star Trek novels. He was hired to write for Star Wars publishing and was slated to pen the Knightfall Trilogy for The New Jedi Order, but the project was eventually canceled and replaced with Greg Keyes's Edge of Victory Duology. {|class="sortable" |- bgcolor="#CCCCCC" !
