|published in=Star Wars Tales 5 |reprinted in= |pages=12 |timeline=33 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |series= |preceded by="What They Called Me" |followed by="Hoth" }} "A Summer's Dream" was a short comic featured in Star Wars Tales 5 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 2 and Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: Rise of the Sith Volume 2. On Naboo in the city of Theed, the son of the counselor to King Veruna, Ian Lago, arranges to meet with Princess Padmé Amidala. After getting to know her better, as opposed to her public figure, he falls in love with her. But when his father finds out that his son is hanging out with the political competition, he tries to put a stop to the relationship. At the same time, King Veruna abdicates and Amidala is elected Queen of Naboo. She has to devote her time to her people now, and has no time for a boy such as Ian. Heartbroken, and with his father out of a job, he leaves Naboo and never returns. Ian vanishes into the galaxy, never seen nor heard from again.
