Brooks Propulsion Devices
905

Brooks Propulsion Devices (BPD) was a company that specialized in propulsion systems for astromech droids, notably anti-grav booster components for Industrial Automaton R-series droids. Brooks Propulsion Devices was shut down by the Galactic Empire following the Clone Wars. *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: The Secret Life of Droids Category:Droid technology companies
