|editor=Jane Mason |publisher=DK Publishing, Inc. |release date=October 5, 1998 |type=Hardcover |pages=64 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=9780789434814 |series=Star Wars Visual Dictionaries |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: The Visual Dictionary is a reference book written by David West Reynolds and released by DK Publishing on September 19, 1998. It is the first book in the Star Wars Visual Dictionaries series of Star Wars reference books, covering the original trilogy of Star Wars movies&mdash;Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back and Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi&mdash;with photographs and descriptions of elements from those movies. The Visual Dictionary was also included in the 1999 Star Wars Classic Limited Edition Gift Pack.
