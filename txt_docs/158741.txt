Firestorm-1's basic package allowed for an internal magazine of three dumb fired explosive missiles. The Fireball missile package upgraded the missiles to have guidance systems and were capable of locking onto targets, with an effective kill zone of 12 meters, an effective range of 40-120 meters, and a maximum range of 400 meters.
