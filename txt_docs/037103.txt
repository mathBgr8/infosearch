DFS-1308
1715

DFS-1308 was one of the Vulture-class starfighters assigned to Saak'ak during the Blockade of Naboo. It analyzed but was unable to calculate the "Human element" inside the N-1 starfighter. The image used to represent DFS-1308 is a frame captured from The Phantom Menace which was slightly modified by artists at Decipher The same frame was also used to represent DFS-1104.
