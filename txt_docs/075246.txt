Giza
1116

Giza was a droid who fought alongside General Lando Calrissian and Tiatkin on the world of Radix during their campaign against the Eiattu pirates. He and Tiatkin flew one of the transports to Radix. He was a large and powerful war droid of unknown type, with a variety of weapons built into his frame. He had a great deal of affection for Tia, and was overcome by grief and rage when she was killed. Category:Battle droids Category:Droids of the New Republic Category:Droids with masculine programming
