Mark I Bulk Transport
2658

The Mark I Bulk Transport was manufactured by Sienar Ships. A common long-range bulk transport, the Mark I possessed two decks and a dorsal laser cannon turret, though due to the ship's lack of shields it was often considered an easy target for pirates. Common throughout the galaxy, though especially among civilized regions, the craft was sold second-hand at a high resale price.The Far Orbit Project Able to be modified, the Mark I could be made battle worthy through reinforcement of the ship's already robust hull, with shield generators and additional laser cannons having little impact on the ships overall performance.
