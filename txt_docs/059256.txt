Bhoggihalysahonues
1984

Bhoggihalysahonues was a female Oswaft who served as one of three Elders of her massive, vacuum-breathing species during the Imperial Period. In 3 BBY, Bhoggihalysahonues led a delegation of 1,000 Oswaft to negotiate with an Imperial fleet that had arrived in the Oswaft's native ThonBoka, despite significant opposition from other Elders who distrusted the Empire's intentions.
