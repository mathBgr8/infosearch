|owners= |staff=*Sy Snootles *Greeata Jendowanian |goods= |services= |hours= |affiliation= }} The planetThe Essential Atlas Tothis had a nightclub called the Green Planet, which was also a cantina. The musicians Sy Snootles and Greeata Jendowanian worked there for a time and were the only employees not addicted to spice. In the final years of the Clone Wars, a conflict that lasted from 22 to 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology musician Evar Orbus visited the Green Planet while looking for members to join his band, ultimately hiring both Jendowanian and Snootles after Orbus's bandmate Max Rebo noticed the pair and was taken in by Snootles' singing.Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition The Green Planet was first indirectly mentioned in 1997's Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition, a supplement for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The cantina was first named in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003. *Star Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition *Star Wars Character Encyclopedia Category:Bars and clubs Category:Tothis locations
