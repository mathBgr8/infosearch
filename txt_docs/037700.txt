Aggressor (Victory-class)
1727

The Aggressor was a Victory-class Star Destroyer of the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. During one battle of the war, the Aggressor was to make an attempt to destroy the CR90 corvette group Athega had the primary strike force failed.
