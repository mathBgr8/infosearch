Ferra
1967

Ferra was a female Dathomiri Witch of the Singing Mountain Clan. During the Battle of Dathomir, she was killed when her neck was broken by a Nightsister causing Teneniel Djo to lash out in anger and use the Force to kill the Nightsisters. *The Courtship of Princess Leia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Individuals with she/her pronouns Category:Singing Mountain Clan members
