Trayos Toreggen
1607

Trayos Toreggen was an ancient Jedi Master who wrote the text Becoming One with the Force. Within this tome he cautioned Jedi from seeking an easier path to power; recognizing the Jedi Path was a difficult one to walk, he knew the dark side of the Force to offer quicker, more dangerous avenues to power. *Tales of the Jedi Companion Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Jedi Masters of the Jedi Order Category:Males
