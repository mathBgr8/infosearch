Ardent IV
1468

Ardent IV was a cruiser in the service of the Galactic Republic's Judicial Forces, in which participated in the Stark Hyperspace War. Jan Dodonna, still a member of the Navy at the time, served as lieutenant on the craft during the conflict. *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
