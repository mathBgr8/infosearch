Bounty Hunters (Star Wars Miniatures)
6324

|figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Bounty Hunters is the sixth expansion as well as the second huge expansion to Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Miniatures game. It was released on September 23, 2006, following the Champions of the Force expansion. As the name implies, it comprises mainly figures of bounty hunters. This expansion set also introduced Mandalorian as a playable faction. The box artwork is by Chris Trevas. Rarity denoted as follows: Common (C), Uncommon (U), Rare (R), Very Rare (VR).
