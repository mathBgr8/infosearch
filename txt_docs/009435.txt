Randil Sal
1400

Randil Sal was the husband of Tiion Sal-Solo and father of Thrackan Sal-Solo. Thrackan met Han Solo around 18 BBY and told him his dad died when he was only one year old. Randil was a wealthy man, and left Tiion in his will before dying. Tiion never recovered, and constantly wept.
