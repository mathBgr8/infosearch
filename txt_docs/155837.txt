Enser Flay 254
1417

The Enser Flay 254 was a formidable podracer that was manufactured sometime near the end of the Galactic Republic. The pod was unique in that the cockpit was a contained bubble that maintained water so that a marine species could pilot the craft. The only known pilot of an Enser Flay 254 was the Neelabi podracer, Occo Ninebar. *Star Wars: Racer Revenge *Star Wars: Racer Revenge: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Podracer models
