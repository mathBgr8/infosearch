Nightcast
2332

Nightcast was a Stalwart-class freighter originally owned by Karu Nelbin. Nelbin was killed by professional assassin Callandri, who decided to keep Nelbin's ship for herself. She modified the ship, replacing many of the components with Imperial military designs. She usually kept her ground vehicle, a Jadai Q-6100, in the ship's hold.
