Kar Plaushe was a veil-wearing Human female socialite native to the planetCreating the Worlds of Star Wars: 365 Days Coruscant. She frequented the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall located in the Uscru Entertainment District of Coruscant, prior to the start of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY. Plaushe was a mysterious woman who had light skin and green eyes. During her two visits to the Outlander, the socialite wore a black top, a jacket, a black skirt, silver heels, and a veil. Kar Plaushe was created for the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002.Star Wars Year by Year: A Visual Chronicle The Outlander Club exterior scenes, which included Plaushe, were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney. The interior scenes were also shot there on July 17, 2000. Prior to the film's release, Plaushe was first pictured in "Casting Director Robin Gurland", an article published in the fifty-second issue of Star Wars Insider magazine on February 27, 2001. The character then first appeared in the young readers book Jango Fett: Bounty Hunter, written by Eric Arnold and illustrated by Valerie Reckert and released on April 23, 2002.
