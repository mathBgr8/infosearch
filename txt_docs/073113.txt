Naboo whale
1064

The Naboo whale was an aquatic creature native to the southernmost oceans of Naboo. They were hunted by the Gungan company Binks & Son Whaling Company for hundreds of years before Jar Jar Binks was born. On a whaling trip when Jar Jar was hunting with his father, George R. Binks, they came across a whale and harpooned it before Jar Jar's poor steering caused their ship to crash into it. Category:Creatures of Naboo Category:Whales
