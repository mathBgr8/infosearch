Fras
716

The Fras were a sentient species of humanoid tigers who were aggressive but luxury-loving. They generally spent most of their time asleep or lazing about, only being active for about three or four hours a day although they generally spent this time being very active. In fights, they were very aggressive and were liable to eat fallen foes. Category:Feline sentient species
