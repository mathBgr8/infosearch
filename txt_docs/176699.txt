Wuitho Trifalls
1336

Wuitho Trifalls was a river on the planet Alderaan which fell in three spectacular waterfalls. Rogue Squadron pilot Tycho Celchu and his family visited there a week before he went to the Imperial Academy. Corran Horn had a hologram of the falls in his quarters on Borleias. *X-Wing: Wedge's Gamble *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alderaan locations Category:Rivers
