|companies=*Sony Online Entertainment *BioWare |website= }} Arnie Jorgensen is an American comic book artist and video game concept artist. Jorgenson worked for DC Comics, Malibu Comics and Marvel Comics. He later became a concept artist in the video game industry, working for companies like Ion Storm and Retro Studios before he became Senior Concept Artist of Sony Online Entertainment's MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided and its three expansions. *Star Wars Galaxies *Star Wars: The Old Republic *From Pencil to Pixel: The Art of Star Wars Galaxies *The Art and Making of Star Wars: The Old Republic Category:Concept artists Category:Star Wars game developers
