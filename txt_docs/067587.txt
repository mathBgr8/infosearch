The Soundmound
1530

The Soundmound was an establishment on the planet Mrlsst that served drinks and food and was considered the best place to listen to music. This local was so well equipped, that it even had twelve different brands of ice. The band Ghost Jedi performed there in holo. Koyi Komad was serving drinks in Soundmound, before she joined Rogue Squadron, and it was there that she met Squadron's members for the first time. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 5 *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 6 Soundmound Soundmound
