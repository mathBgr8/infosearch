Geor-Dan-Thi
1611

Geor-Dan-Thi was a male Tiss'shar who served as the President of the Tiss'sharl League in 0 ABY, replacing Si-Di-Ri in the office. Geor-Dan-Thi made a quick career by assassinating his opponents, namely Mayor Antha-Kres, Secretary Lai-O-Sid and Vice President Astre-De-Kay. Next in line to be killed was President Si-Di-Ri himself, an act that would make Geor-Dan-Thi President.
