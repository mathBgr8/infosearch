Chipr
1161

Chipr was the name given to a group of two Lambda-class T-4a shuttles owned by the Alliance to Restore the Republic that carried troops away from Yavin 4 following the destruction of the first Death Star. Red Squadron oversaw their exit from the Imperial blockade. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic shuttle groups
