thumb|225px|Sass [[Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic|KotOR concept art.]] Mike Sass is an artist that works primarily within the video game industry. He has a long history of working with BioWare to produce conceptual and cover art for their numerous RPG titles. He painted the popular cover art for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic as well as numerous other promotional pieces. *Beyond the Rim *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook *Star Wars: The Complete Vader *No Disintegrations Category:Concept artists
