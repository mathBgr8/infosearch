Spirit of Jabba
1952

The Spirit of Jabba was a starship hidden by Jabba Desilijic Tiure inside Glass Mountain and watched over by Onoh. It contained many treasures, including eight Mendacian funeral urns. It was discovered by Gorga Desilijic Aarrpo after Jabba's death, but was hijacked by Big Gizz and Spiker. They soon discovered the Jabba Tape, an automated computer defense program that nearly killed them before Onoh entered the proper authorization code. The ship was shot down by the Nemphas in the desert beyond Mos Eisley. *"The Jabba Tape" *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Hutt Cartel
