Zann Consortium Vehicle Factory
1609

The Zann Consortium Vehicle Factory was a modular structure within Tyber Zann's underworld empire. They were built with to be created on a planet relatively quickly, however, with defenses in mind which included built-in armor and a shield generator. They were similar to the Zann Consortium Droid Works in that they were well shielded from most types of weaponry.
