Tahu'ip
430

The Tahu'ip were an ancient subset of Twi'lek culture on the Twi'lek homeworld Ryloth. Its writing technique died out around 460 BBY. By 40 ABY, only three scholars could understand it, including For'ali. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Twi'lek culture
