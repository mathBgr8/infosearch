Monsoon Mesa
631

Monsoon Mesa was the location of a Republic base during the Battle of Jabiim. It was just south of Cobalt Station. At the end of the battle, the last Republic forces on the planet were evacuated from there. *Republic 57 *Republic 58 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Jabiim locations Category:Mesas
