Team Purella
1478

Team Purella was one of the five Jedi strike-teams sent to Corellia as part of Operation Roundabout. Consisting of Jaina Solo, Zekk, the Falleen Jedi Thann Mithric and Bothan Kolir Hu'lya, their mission was to arrest Five World Prime Minister Aidel Saxan. The Corellians had anticipated the strike, however, and the Prime Minister's motorcade was actually an ambush packed with YVH 1 battle droids. Team Purella was able to withdraw from the trap, however, and joined up with Tahiri Veila's Team Tauntaun to improvise an escape by "borrowing" Kirney Slane and Kolot's shuttle from Donoslane Excursions at Behareh Spaceport. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance military units Category:New Jedi Order organizations Category:Strike teams
