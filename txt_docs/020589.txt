Verpine prototype shield
1251

thumb|right|200px|[[Canderous Ordo protected by a Verpine prototype shield]] A Verpine prototype shield was an experimental forearm-mounted personal shield generator in use around the Jedi Civil War. Though the Verpine are often credited with the design the unit was, in essence, nothing more than an upgraded Arkanian shield. Like its technological cousin, the Verpine shield protected its wearer from temperature extremes as well as sonic- and energy-based weapons. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Personal energy shields
