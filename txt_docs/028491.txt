Arboga

**New Republic Fleet |masters= |apprentices= }} Arboga was a gravelly-voiced captain of the New Republic Defense Fleet. In the chaos that followed the Imperial retaking of Coruscant after Grand Admiral Thrawn's death, Arboga served as the commanding officer of the MC80 Liberty type Star Cruiser Hope.
