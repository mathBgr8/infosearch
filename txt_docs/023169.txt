Mesdri'starn
521

Mesdri'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the midlands of the planet's eastern continent, among other significant cities; Liah'starn was located to the southwest, Rhan'starn to the south, and Shevre'starn to the east. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
