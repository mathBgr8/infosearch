Ralltiiri High Council
2209

The Ralltiiri High Council was the primary government of Ralltiir until being disbanded by the Galactic Empire and Lord Tion during the early days of the Galactic Civil War. All members of the council were slaughtered by Tion's men after he discovered sympathizers to the Rebel Alliance among its members.
