Rejuvenator
2953

The Rejuvenator was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer that served with the New Republic Defense Force. In 25 ABY, while under the command of Commander Warshack Rojo, the vessel was serving as the centerpiece of a task force based at Ord Mantell, and was the nearest major warship to Dubrillion in the Outer Rim&mdash;though even then, she was three days away at flank hyperspace speeds from the world. When the Star Destroyer was called upon to assist Lando Calrissian's colony, the Rejuvenator arrived with her task force, consisting of a dozen of warships, including a number of smaller cruisers, six Ranger-class gunships, cargo support, and even a crew transport.
