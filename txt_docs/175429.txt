Department of Punishment
1182

The Department of Punishment was an Imperial subdivision that was tasked with the torture of captured criminals. The Master Torturer was the head of the division; Sir Vengnar Heiff held this position in 0 ABY. Han Solo was forced to sign a contract (via a droid) that gave Lord Vader direct permission to torture him to death.Star Wars Missions 16: Imperial Jailbreak The President of the subdivision was murdered by New Republic partisans during the liberation of Coruscant in 7 ABY.Children of the Jedi *Star Wars Missions 16: Imperial Jailbreak *Children of the Jedi Category:Imperial Departments
