Montrol City
1726

250px|right Montrol City was the capital of Gandle Ott. Located on the edge of the Sanche Ocean, Montrol City was a modern city with many cultural points of interest. Art museums were located downtown, and the city boasted industrial harbors and tourist areas. It was also the location of The Bank, the city courts, and the city computer center. Many of the planet's officials had residences in the city, and groves of cattarash mushrooms were cultivated all over. During the early years of the New Republic, the city's mayor was Stanfeld Duffit.
