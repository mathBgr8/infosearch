Red Feathers
1213

Red Feathers was a crippled Kuat Drive Yards Super Transport VI, which had crashed on Blood Nest, orbiting the gas giant M2398-3. This ship was used as the headquarters for a band of pirates under name Blood Nest until their defeat in 7 ABY by Wraith Squadron. It was then used by the New Republic in the Battle of Ession to deliver three starfighter squadrons and the Page's commandos.
