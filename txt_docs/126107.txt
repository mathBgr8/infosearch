By the time of the Galactic Empire, TransGalMeg Industries, Incorporated (TGM) maintained corporate headquarters on Pako Ramoon. At one point during the Galactic Civil War, the Human Rebel Alliance operative Basal Moor was apprehended on Pako Ramoon, and the Xi'Dec agents K'lial and Diskio Khzrry were sent to extract him. While TGM guards were escorting Moor through the lobby of the company's corporate headquarters, the Xi'Dec pair&mdash;who were posing as recruiters seeking individuals of new sexes to add to their family unit via marriage&mdash;distracted the guards and then used a remotely operated ground speeder that crashed through the lobby's windows to escape together with Moor. The Rebel agent Kara Via subsequently wrote a report on the incident on Pako Ramoon, which, at some point by the time shortly after the Battle of Yavin, Alliance General Airen Cracken included in an entry dedicated to the Khzrrys that was part of a datafile detailing various Rebel operatives. Pako Ramoon was created by Chuck Truett and was introduced in Cracken's Rebel Operatives, a 1994 West End Games sourcebook published for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
