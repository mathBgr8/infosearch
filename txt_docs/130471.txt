Falaricae
2174

Falaricae was a MC40a light cruiser used by the Dimoks. It had a squadron of Z-95 Headhunters and arrived as part of the attack against the Ripoblus convoy. Tan Maarek Stele inspected the cruiser to find it had Dimoks.
