HSB-200
2012

The BlasTech Industries HSB-200 was a small blaster that was barely considered a hold-out blaster. Design decisions by BlasTech made it almost a blaster pistol instead of a holdout. It was not particularly effective, and was considered more for show than actual combat. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Arms & Equipment Guide *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Force and Destiny Core Rulebook Category:BlasTech Industries products Category:Blaster pistols
