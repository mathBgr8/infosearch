Bent Gavler
868

Bent Gavler was a resident of Cloud City on Bespin, and a friend of the family of eleven-year-old Allania Jakien. He ran a trading post. After Jakien had a harrowing experience avoiding stormtroopers and witnessing Darth Vader during the evacuation of Cloud City, she ran into Gavler in the upper plaza. Gavler took her in his arms and consoled her panicked crying. He reunited her with her family, and they all departed Bespin on a crowded transport.
