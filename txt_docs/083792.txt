Arbalest
1941

The arbalest was a projectile weapon typically carried by the militiagungs of the Gungan Grand ArmyThe Essential Guide to Warfare on the planet Naboo. The Gungan General Tobler Ceel established a training center in the Scrumma Jawbone, an arm of Lake Paonga, where militiagungs could practice with arbalests alongside other weapons such as the cesta and atlatl. Arbalests were first mentioned in The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology, a reference book by W.
