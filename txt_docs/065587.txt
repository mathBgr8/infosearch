Gardulla Oola
787

Gardulla Oola was a cheap hotel and casino in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. The establishment featured both Dame Needa, a dancer who impersonated the ill-fated Twi'lek Oola, and the Adukis who performed the Whirling Kavadango Dance. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Hotels Category:Mos Eisley locations
