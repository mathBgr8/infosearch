Morath
1159

The Morath were a sentient species found near the Koornacht Cluster: their name was probably connected to that of the Morath Nebula. Under the New Order, they established two mining colonies a little way inside the Koornacht Cluster, on the planets of Elcorth and Kojash. Both colonies were wiped out during the Great Purge by the Yevetha. A few survivors were apparently taken as hostages, such as Jara ba Nylra and Liekas Tendo.
