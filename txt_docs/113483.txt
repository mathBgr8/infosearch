Keesa
1594

Keesa was the Pa'lowick assistant of Dama Whitesun Brunk at the Sidi Driss Inn, a hotel in Anchorhead. In 8 ABY, when Imperial remnant troops raided the town in search of the famous moss-painting, Killik Twilight, who assaulted Keesa.
