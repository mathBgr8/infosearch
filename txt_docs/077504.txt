Slicksuit
536

Slicksuits were underwater survival suits made by the Mon Calamari and used for exploring Dac's oceans. They were thin, skintight membranes made out of a glistening fabric. The suits provided temperature control, and a membrane at the back of the neck filtered oxygen molecules out of the ocean for breathing. *Young Jedi Knights: Crisis at Crystal Reef *Legacy of the Force: Fury Category:Armor
