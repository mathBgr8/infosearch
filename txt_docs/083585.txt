|published in=Star Wars Tales 11 |reprinted in= |pages=22 |timeline=c. 5 BBY |series= |preceded by="Free Memory" |followed by="In the Beginning" }} "Prey" was a comic in Star Wars Tales 11 and was collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 3. The story was written by Kia Asamiya, colors by Dave Stewart and letters by Steve Dutro. Wilhuff Tarkin hires Boba Fett to apprehend Han Solo after he left the Imperial Army. Darth Vader disagrees with the decision of using the bounty hunter and Tarkin decides that Vader should prove his superiority by completing the task himself. Fett and Vader both track down Solo until they're both in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. They fight over him, then Fett draws a lightsaber that he apparently took from a dead Jedi. As the two continue to fight, Han Solo flees the battle and gets off planet in a short-range vessel.
