Stormwall Defense Fleet
3633

The Stormwall Defense Fleet, also known as the Republic Fleet, was established during the High Republic Era by the Galactic Republic Supreme Chancellor Lina Soh to defend Republic space from the Nihil pirates and send messages into the Occlusion Zone, a region of space that was controlled by the Nihil, in order to inform the citizens trapped there that they were not alone and they would be protected.
