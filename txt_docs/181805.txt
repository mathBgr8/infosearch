It and the Nexus Ortai system were both located near the intersection of the hyperlanes known as the Askar TraceStay on Target&mdash;which linked them to the Leritor, Lanos, and Bresnia system&mdash;and the Reena Trade Route, which connected them to the Masterra and Spirador systems. In 18 BBY, the Noolian Crisis began when Aqualish members of the holdouts of the recently defeated Confederacy of Independent Systems seized Galboron and other nearby worlds. The conflict was resolved when the Aqualish were defeated by a Galactic Empire task force at the famous Battle of Galboron. Galboron was first introduced as part of the name of the Battle of Galboron in Galladinium's Fantastic Technology, a 1995 sourcebook authored by Rick D. Stuart for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. In 2012, the reference book The Essential Guide to Warfare, authored by Jason Fry and Paul R. Urquhart, established Galboron as a distinct astronomical object.
