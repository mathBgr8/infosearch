Imperial records library
1846

The Imperial Records Library was a computerized system holding the government records of the Imperial Remnant. It was moved when the Imperial Remnant relocated its capital. It contained Emperor Palpatine's personal files, including a list of Royal Guardsmen; it was through it that Vilim Disra discovered Grodin Tierce's former status as one.
