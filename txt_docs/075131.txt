Charging Wampa
749

Charging Wampa was a Teräs Käsi move, named after the Wampa of Hoth. The move involved charging at one's opponent, attempting to hit them in the jaw, followed by a simultaneous hit of both arms on the opponent's neck. *Darth Maul: Shadow Hunter *Star Wars: Tiny Death Star *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook Category:Teräs Käsi
