Team Meloque
1136

Team Meloque was the team organized by Galactic Alliance Intelligence to infiltrate Yuuzhan Vong-occupied Caluula in 29 ABY, following the battle there. The goal was to sabotage the yammosk housed there prior to the Yuuzhan Vong attack on Mon Calamari. To that end, they posed as scientists arriving to study the Nocturne of the Winged-Stars.
