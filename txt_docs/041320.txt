Wilst Molan was a Human female native to the planet Coruscant. She was a participant in Coruscant's party scene and a frequent patron of the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall located in the Uscru Entertainment District of Coruscant. Molan and her acquaintances Nyrat Agira and Di Mantid formed a regular group that visited the club in hopes of finding a rich and powerful mate. Molan often danced among the crowds of the club, which led to the assumption that she was a hired dancer for the club. thumb|300px|right|Molan frequented the Outlander Club (pictured). One night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Molan was inside the club alongside Agira and Mantid and stood near the bar as Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered the club in search of bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Later, she stood near Skywalker as he looked for Wesell. She vied for the title of best dressed between Agira and Mantid at the club. However, her dress was not as charismatic as Agira's outfit. Molan was an intriguing, statuesque woman who was acquainted with Nyrat Agira and Di Mantid. While the three appeared to be friends, they were in fierce competition with each other for the unofficial title of "best dressed Outlander patron," a title Agira often won. She had light skin, black hair, and brown eyes. At the Outlander Club, her hair was worn up in two conelike shapes that were reminiscent of the Gotal species. While in the Outlander Club, Molan wore a sleek and shimmering blue dress, a medallion on her forehead, and silver height-enhancing shoes for hired dancers. She also wore thoadeye-style makeup around her eyes. The outfit did not match the charisma of what Agira was wearing at the time. In an image in the web article "Cantina Roll-Call: Shedding Light on Some Alien Aliases," published on StarWars.com on May 2, 2003, Molan was identified as Nogo Molana, a name which has not appeared in any other source.
