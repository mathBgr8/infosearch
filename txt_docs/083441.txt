Essence Stealer
1388

The Essence Stealer was a device invented by Borborygmus Gog as part of Project Starscream. It was designed to rip the Force-essence out of a Jedi's body and contain it. Its victims' bodies were stored in stasis pods while their essence was collectively held in a crystal globe. Gog used the technology from a Ssi-Ruuvi Swarm-class battle droid that he had purchased from Raith Sienar. He placed it in the Jedi library on the Nespis VIII space station. This experiment was based on a theory by Jenna Zan Arbor. The Force-sensitive Human, Tash Arranda, managed to destroy it with the help of her uncle, Mammon Hoole.
