Imperial Board of Foodstuffs and Consumables
990

}} The Imperial Board of Foodstuffs and Consumables was an organization of the Galactic Empire that monitored the usage, quality, and safety of various foods, medicines, and other such substances. In blind sample tests, Board judges chose andris as a superior food additive ten times out of twelve of the time. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Board of Foodstuffs and Consumables
