Paul Celer
582

Paul Celer is an English journalist who wrote the backstory of Grizz Frix appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. His alias is Kast Morben on StarWars.com, and KastMorben on Wookieepedia. - September 2005 Category:What's The Story? authors
