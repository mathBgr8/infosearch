Sh'rip Sh'pa
2800

The Sh'rip Sh'pa, translated into Basic as seedship, was a Yuuzhan Vong freighter that carried supplies and equipment needed for Vongforming captured worlds. Each vessel was built like an enormous gemstone, and its facets were a dozen meters in size.
