Following Tobias Beckett's raid on the planet Kessel, a Crimson Dawn enforcer claimed that the Corellian Han Solo was Suul's grandson. He alleged that the young man had actually joined the Galactic Empire under his real name of "Han Suul," but the intake officer was not listening carefully and wrote "Solo" instead of "Suul". Despite the rumor, Han had told the recruiter that he did not have a family.
