Healing Star
1599

The Healing Star was a hospital ship in the Galactic Alliance Defense Force during the Swarm War. Accompanying the Megador to Tenupe at the end of the war, it was staffed by only droids, all part of a ruse by Grand Master Luke Skywalker to eliminate Lomi Plo. When she went after the Healing Star, upon which she thought Mara Jade Skywalker and Jacen Solo were aboard, Luke Skywalker was able to cut her down with his lightsaber. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Medical starships Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
