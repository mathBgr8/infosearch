Collapsed dwarf star
1156

A dwarf star was artificially collapsed by Doctor Arakkus of the Galactic Empire in 0 ABY. He achieved this by utilizing a negatron impact charge on the dwarf star. When the star began to collapse, its behavior become similar to that of a black hole, sucking in all surrounding space. *"Deadly Reunion" *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Black holes Category:Mayagil sector locations Category:Stars in the Outer Rim Territories
