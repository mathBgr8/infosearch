Darak Lyll
1079

Darak Lyll was a tall, long-haired and bearded Human criminal who lived on Alderaan. To earn money, Han Solo, on his visit to Alderaan in the year 10 BBY, tried to sell Darak Lyll the glitterstorm charge of the Ylesian Dream.
