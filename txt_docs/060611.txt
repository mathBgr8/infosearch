|pages= |upc= |timeline=1 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures |issue=2 }} Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 2 was the second issue of the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Russ Manning. Artoo-Detoo and See-Threepio continue their search for the kidnapped Luke and Leia on Gambler's World. The two droids come up with some innovative plans to rescue their masters, but will they work against the scheming of Darth Vader and the mysterious Blackhole? These strips, which ran in newspapers from 1979 to 1980, have been reformatted and expertly retouched by Rick Hoberg, who worked with Russ Manning on the original newspaper strips.
