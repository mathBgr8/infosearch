Shard-class capital ship
1754

The Shard-class capital ship was a class of capital ship that was designed and built by the Killiks for the Battle of Tenupe. The warships were roughly cone-shaped, and ranged from one and a half to ten kilometers in length. Their name was derived from the fact that they appeared to have been constructed by shattering a moon or asteroid into shards and then hollowing them out in a hive-like fashion. This method of construction provided these ships with extensive defenses, as each layer of the hive could be considered an extra set of armor plating. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Unknown Regions Category:Capital ship classes Category:Killik culture
