Guardian (rank)
678

Guardian was an elite rank in the Guiteica Militia, given to the personal bodyguards of militia leaders. Guardian Aul Tarrus Vishav saved the life of m'Yalfor'ac Order leader Quaal Tavier Catharius during a duel against Major Jael Ganton Farvash.
