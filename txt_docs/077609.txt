Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide |dateengineered= |numberinfected= |numberkilled= |susceptiblespecies= |transmissiontype=Viral infection |incubationperiod= |symptoms=Death |treatments= }} The Great Pandemic of Deersheba was an outbreak of a deadly viral infection disease that originated in the Mid Rim's Deersheba system. The outbreak was caused by members of the Neimoidian species, who served as virus carriers for the disease, although Neimoidians themselves denied their responsibility for the event. The Great Pandemic of Deersheba was eventually mentioned by the author Ebenn Q3 Baobab in the 6 ABY edition of his book the Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide.
