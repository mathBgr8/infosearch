Skeck Draggle
1962

Skeck Draggle was the first mate of the Drunk Dancer. He belonged to the same six-fingered, lanky, severe-faced humanoid species as Archyr Beil. The Drunk Dancer crew, which included captain Jula Shryne, former Jedi Master Roan Shryne, security-chief Archyr Beil and himself, were contacted by Cash Garrulan to smuggle former senator Fang Zar of Alderaan, to his homeplanet Sern Prime. *Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Individuals with he/him pronouns Category:Males Category:Spacers
