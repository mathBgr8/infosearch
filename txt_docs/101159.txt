Card's Tapcaf
686

Card's Tapcaf was a small tapcafe on Corellia. It was still in existence at the time of the Second Galactic Civil War and was where Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo discussed with Dur Gejjen about the possibility of assassinating Thrackan Sal-Solo. Card's Tapcaf was located on Blue Sky Boulevard in the capital city of Coronet. *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines Category:Coronet City locations Category:Tapcafes
