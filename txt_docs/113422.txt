Eventide smasher
1164

The Eventide smasher was a heavy sword of incredible strength. Only the strongest of warriors were able to wield them, and only the most extremely skilled fencers were able to use them effectively in battle. The Eventide smasher appears as a quest reward in Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees. Although its description states that it is a sword, its in-game model is that of a mace, a blunt weapon. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees *Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Swords
