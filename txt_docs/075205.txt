Dubrillion Aquarium
708

The Dubrillion Aquarium was an aquarium in Dubrillion's capital city. It contained transparisteel tanks filled with fish from Dubrillion and other worlds, dominated by a central cylindrical tank filled with big emerald sharks and rainbow fish. Shedao Shai made it his headquarters during the early stages of the Yuuzhan Vong War, after the evacuation of Dubrillion. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin Category:Aquariums Category:Dubrillion locations
