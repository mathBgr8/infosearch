Mayli Weng
3825

Mayli Weng was a Human female representing the Exotic Entertainers' Union on the planet Coruscant around 3.5 ABY. She dealt with Prince Xizor and was always extremely polite to him. He was always polite in return, to the point of never using his pheromones to control her. Mayli Weng began her professional journey as an exotic entertainer in the Core Worlds. Known for her charm, poise, and commanding presence, she quickly ascended within the ranks of the industry. Her natural leadership and diplomatic acumen caught the attention of the Exotic Entertainers' Union, eventually leading to her appointment as one of its senior representatives. By 3.5 ABY, Weng was serving as a senior union representative based on Coruscant. Representing the interests of over 20,000 workers across the Core Worlds, Weng was tasked with lobbying politicians, negotiating on behalf of entertainers, and maintaining relationships with powerful figures such as Prince Xizor. Despite his infamous use of pheromones, Xizor never attempted to manipulate Mayli, reportedly out of professional respect. Weng was deeply involved in political maneuverings related to entertainers being detained under accusations of Rebel sympathies. She was known for her impassioned defense of her colleagues and firm opposition to government overreach, especially from planetary leaders like Governor Snopps of Corulag and the Esselian government. During a high-profile reception on Coruscant, Mayli encountered an old romantic acquaintance, Wendell Wright-Sims, a spice merchant and socialite. Their exchange was tinged with flirtation, unresolved feelings, and professional tension, as Wendell attempted to rekindle their former relationship while she remained focused on union business. Weng brushed off his advances, demonstrating her commitment to her responsibilities. Mayli Weng was elegant, charismatic, and highly intelligent. Her conversational dexterity and seductive charm masked a deeply capable mind skilled in political negotiation. In private, however, she reportedly struggled with isolation and found her life on Coruscant hollow despite its glamor. Mayli was a skilled bargainer and negotiator, able to manipulate conversations to her favor even among powerful Imperial and underworld figures. She was also trained in subterfuge and could detect hidden motives and evade surveillance or manipulation with ease. She was often seen with a business data pad and wearing a exotic dresses.
