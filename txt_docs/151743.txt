Yarith Figg
2326

Yarith Figg was a lesser noble female from Alderaan and the wife of explorer Ecclessis Figg. Yarith met her husband on a luxury liner running along the Corellian Trade Spine, where Ecclessis ended up saving her life. The two eventually married, and Yarith poured her Alderaanian fortune into Figg's business venture, the Outer Javin Company. As a result of the success from Figg's hyperspace explorations with the new company, the couple became extremely wealthy, eventually founding Figg & Associates, a privately-held cousin company to the Outer Javin Company.
