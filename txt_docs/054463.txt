Fireclaw
1754

The Fireclaw was an Immobilizer 418 cruiser that saw service with the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. While patrolling the Lifh sector, the Fireclaw was hacked by R2z-DL and Koedi Raef, who redirected it away from Vilosoria so the Rebel Alliance could overthrow the Imperial garrison there. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Cracken's Rebel Operatives *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Immobilizer 418 cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
