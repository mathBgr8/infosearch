Unidentified Rebel Vanguard (Battle of Hoth)
1577

This unidentified Rebel Vanguard was a male Human who was recruited by Commander Col Serra to join Renegade Squadron, the Rebel Alliances' new elite fighting unit. During his career as a Rebel he was trained and became a member of the Rebel vanguard division.
