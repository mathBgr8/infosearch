Mobvekhar
2661

Mobvekhar was a member of Clan Hakh'khar who was one of the members of the honor guard of the Mal'ary'ush. thumb|left|220px|Mobvekhar is alerted to Councilor Fey'lya's presence Mobvekhar first arrived on Coruscant as the second-in-command to Cakhmaim and leader of the second watch. While guarding Leia, he captured Councilor Borsk Fey'lya listening in on a conversation between Leia and smuggler chief Talon Karrde. He later accompanied Leia and Talon to Wayland in the Wild Karrde in order to aid Han Solo and Luke Skywalker in destroying Grand Admiral Thrawn's cloning facility in Mount Tantiss. While infiltrating the mountain, Mobvekhar and another Noghri held off a squad of stormtroopers, allowing Leia and Talon the chance to reach the Emperor's throne room.
