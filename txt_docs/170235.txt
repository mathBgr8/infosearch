Missile Destroyer (Confederacy)
1416

Confederate Missile destroyers were aquatic anti-air repulsorcraft used to patrol waterways around Confederate installations. These craft were very effective at destroying enemy aircraft such as the LAAT, however lacking any ground based defensive weaponry, these craft would require escort from Confederate Scouts. The Missile Destroyer was later succeeded by the Heavy Missile Destroyer. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns Category:Aquatic repulsorcraft models Category:Vehicle classes of the Confederacy of Independent Systems
