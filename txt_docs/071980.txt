Yinchorri religion
483

The Yinchorri religion focused largely on legends of a spirit realm and of ancient myths. One such myth was that there was a spirit world that existed inside the real world; it was termed "Within World." Given the Yinchorri attitudes their religion presumably encouraged discovery, expansion, and aggression. *Jedi Council: Acts of War 4 Category:Religions
