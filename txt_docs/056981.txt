Special Ops: The Art of Infiltration
1867

"Special Ops: The Art of Infiltration" was a short story originally slated to be printed in Star Wars Adventure Journal 17, written by John Beyer and Kathy Burdette. Despite rumors, "Special Ops: The Art of Infiltration" was not published in Star Wars Gamer 9, though it has been mistaken for the unrelated Topside Infiltration published in Star Wars Gamer 8. An Alliance Intelligence team (including Morgan Q. Raventhorn, Maglenna Pendower, and Dren Nord) fight against Imperial Security Bureau Captain Edmond Mylesgood. The group completes its objective and destroys a skiff full of Imperial troops. *Star Wars Gamer 9 *Star Wars Adventure Journal 17 Category:Star Wars Adventure Journal fiction
