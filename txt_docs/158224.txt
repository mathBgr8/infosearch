Thiss
623

Thiss was an enormous fire-blackened stump of a wroshyr tree on Kashyyyk that acted as a landing field for nearby Rwookrrorro. A monument to Chewbacca was created here that was carved from the branch of a huge wroshyr tree and held a portrait of the Wookiee to honor his life as well as the contributions he made to the galaxy. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Kashyyyk locations
