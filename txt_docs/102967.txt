Gnosos
1834

Gnosos was a male Sunesi who worked as a CorDuro Shipping steward on Bburru Station in 26 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Balance Point to 26 ABY. He did not support the company when they decided to turn Duro over to the Yuuzhan Vong. He helped Jacen Solo escape imprisonment by Durgard Brarun by providing him with a datacard with clearance to access Port Duggan. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Servants Category:Sunesi
