Neimoidian bird cage
2105

250px|thumb|A Neimoidian bird cage containing a pylat bird Neimoidian bird cages were small devices used for holding avians such as the pylat bird. These cages were fitted with force field generators built in to confine the bird.Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary A thin perch rose from a pole in the center of the cage's base. The Neimoidian Nute Gunray received a red-spotted pylat bird as an anonymous gift, and he kept it on display in a Neimoidian bird cage in his office, with it quickly becoming the envy of his peers. Darth Sidious revealed that he sent Gunray the bird and threated to Force choke it in its cage after he unexpectedly contacted Gunray for the first time, forcing Gunray to enter into a reluctant alliance with the Sith Lord.Darth Plagueis These cages were also found aboard Lucrehulk-class battleships such as Saak'ak during the Invasion of Naboo. *Darth Plagueis *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace novelization *Star Wars: Episode I: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided Quick Reference Guide *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary Category:Furnishings
