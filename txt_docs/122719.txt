Dye grenade

Dye grenades were a type of grenade that released billowing clouds when detonated.The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Similar in use to smoke grenades, dye grenades were used to mark landing zones for incoming vessels, as well as obscuring the vision of enemy forces. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Gas grenades
