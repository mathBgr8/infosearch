Duality
657

The Duality, sometimes called the Creed, was a popular and benign Tarrack religious cult on the planet Utharis. It was founded on the twin principles of joy and service. Followers could be identified by the tattoos on their foreheads. *Tyrant's Test *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Core Worlds organizations Category:Religious organizations
