Limm
1623

Limm was a Human female soldier who served in the Galactic Alliance Guard during the Second Galactic Civil War. In the year 40 ABY, Limm and another GAG trooper, Sergeant Wirut of the 967 Commando, arrested Chief of State Cal Omas for treason by order of Colonel Jacen Solo.
