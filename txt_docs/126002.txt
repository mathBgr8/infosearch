The station was built near the navigational beacon known as Darknon Beacon. Over the years, the trade routes shifted and it lost significance, decaying into a half-abandoned ghostly shell of its former self. It featured eighteen docking bays, only six of which were still functional by the time of the Galactic Civil War. Darknon's other amenities included the Stormview Lounge, Galaxy Shop, and the abandoned Van Serai Hotel.
