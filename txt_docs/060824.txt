Hunting the Hunters
4179

"Hunting the Hunters" is a short story published in Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 6, and later on Hyperspace. It was written by Bill Smith and illustrated by Omaha Pérez. Nariss Siv Loqesh apprehends a Bothan quarry when his expeditor, a Bimm named Crote, receives word that the Empire has invited Loqesh to join the bounty hunters hired to track down the Millennium Falcon. Loqesh declines – he doesn’t do work for the Empire – and goes into hiding to avoid Imperial reprisal.
