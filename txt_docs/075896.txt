Jode Tee
1310

Jode Tee was a Human male who was an agent serving in New Republic Intelligence. As one of the guards covertly stationed aboard the passenger liner Queen of Empire, he was tasked with watching over the defecting Yuuzhan Vong priestess Elan and her familiar Vergere to oversee their transfer from Myrkr to Coruscant.
