The Assassin's Guild,Darth Vader (2020) 20 also known as the Assassins Guild, the Assassin Guild,Bounty Hunters 42 or as the league,Darth Vader (2020) 17 was an organization active during the Imperial Era, the members of which were freelance assassins. Prior to entering the service of Emperor Sheev Palpatine, Ochi of Bestoon was a Master Assassin of the league. Sometime later, Chilla Zin, Gee-Ninety, and Loriach, three members of the Guild, were hired by Ochi, now a Sith assassin and a servant of the Sith Lord Darth Vader, to assassinate a pontiff allied with the Crimson Dawn criminal syndicate on Vincorba.
