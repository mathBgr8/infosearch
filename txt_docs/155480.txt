Capital Season
1418

Capital Season, also known as the Procopian Season, was a holiday celebrated by the nobility of the Tapani sector. Every year between Expansion Week and Shelova Week, the upper class would gather on Procopia to mingle and spy on each other. If a noble was to not be in attendance, their economic, social, and political status would suffer. *Tapani Sector Instant Adventures *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector holidays
