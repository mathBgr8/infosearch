Riij Winward
3766

}} Riij Winward was a male Human who served with the Rebel Alliance as a procurement specialist. He was stationed on Tatooine around the time of the Battle of Yavin. Winward was born in Calius saj Leeloo on Berchest. After his older brother was pressed into Imperial services, he stowed away on a freighter to get out of the Empire's reach. When the freighter rendezvoused with the Alliance Fleet, Winward was discovered and joined the Rebel Alliance. In 0 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion Winward helped Shada D'ukal and Karoly D'ulin, who were disguised as the Tonnika sisters at that time, to escape from a local prison in Mos Eisley. He intended to get some information about the Hammertong, a prototypical part of the Death Star II laser. Eventually, the Mistryl Shadow Guards wouldn't allow him to take the part of the weapon with him, but Winward stole their astromech D4, along with a technical readout of the device&mdash;promising politely to return it once the Alliance had acquired the information. In 2 ABY, he and Rathe Palror were ordered to look after a load of blasters smuggled to Derra IV by Haber Trell and Maranne Darmic. On their way, they were captured by the Imperial Star Destroyer Admonitor. Captain Dagon Niriz instructed them to deliver an unknown load to Coronet, Corellia.
