Harem Cave
538

The Harem Cave was a cave, usually, where Killiks would go to mate after participating in the Dawn Rumble. Joiners who had participated in the dance would often go there as well, presumably for the same purpose, though whether or not they mingled with the insects or occupied different areas is unknown. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War Category:Caves Category:Ceremonial locations Category:Killik culture
