|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=1 }} Classic Star Wars 12 is the twelfth issue of the Classic Star Wars series, published by Dark Horse Comics. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson from the early 1980s. Williamson supplied new panels and art to flow the stories for traditional comic format. Out of the frying pan... just as Han Solo and Chewbacca manage to escape the wiles of Mystra and the arena of Raskar with the last of the legendary power gems... Luke and Threepio turn up missing.
