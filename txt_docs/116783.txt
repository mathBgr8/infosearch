Null-burst projector
767

The null-burst projector was a ship-based weapon used by the Aing-Tii on their Sanhedrim ships. The projector created a pocket of negative energy which did little physical damage to its target. However, the target's victims were incapacitated for several hours after the blast, during which time the user could board the ship without resistance. *Vision of the Future *The Kathol Outback Category:Energy weaponry Category:Starship weaponry
