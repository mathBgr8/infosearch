Mimban Cloudrider
886

Mimban Cloudrider was one of the Bacta Tankers captured by Rogue Squadron during the Bacta War. It was later used to insert covertly Corran Horn, Elscol Loro, Sixtus Quin, Mirax Terrik, and Iella Wessiri in Thyferra.
