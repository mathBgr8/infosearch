Model 22T4 ISB Special
3136

The Model 22T4 ISB Special was a model of hold-out blaster that was specifically manufactured and produced for the Imperial Security Bureau. Agents were assigned a 22T4 after being accepted into the Bureau as a personal sidearm. Selling of this blaster to civilians was strictly prohibited by the Galactic Empire, and they strictly monitored the production of the weapon by placing a specially-coded serial number on all models under the actuating blaster module.The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology This was also done to discourage tampering with the weapon.
