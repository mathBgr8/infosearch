Xucphra Alazhi
812

Xucphra Alazhi was one of three Bacta Tankers hijacked by Rogue Squadron from a small convoy escorted by the Victory II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter during the Ambush of the bacta tankers. It surrendered only after having lost its turbolasers, but was destroyed by the Corrupter while running on its exit vector from the system.
