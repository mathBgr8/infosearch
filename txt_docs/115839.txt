|type=JuiceStar Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |description= |animals= |plants= |other= |edible= |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects=Intoxicating |dishes= |affiliation= }} Ondaran willek juice was an intoxicating alcoholic juice that originated in the Ondara system, a part of the Lantillian sector in the Kashyyyk Region ofThe Essential Atlas the Mid Rim. Jolee Bindo, a Human former Jedi Padawan and smuggler who became a hermit after crashlanding on the Mytaranor sector planet Kashyyyk around 3976 BBY,The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, which include Jolee Bindo ending his twenty-year hermitage on Kashyyyk, to 3956 BBY. was familiar with the beverage. Ondaran willek juice was introduced in the 2003 BioWare video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. In the game, the beverage can be mentioned in one version of an optional conversation the player character, Revan, can have with Jolee Bindo. In the dialogue, Revan can question Bindo's perceived elusivity in talking about his motives for joining the former's travels through the galaxy. In response, the amused Bindo states that Revan's choice of words implies that they "obviously" have not at any point consumed an excessive amount of Ondaran willek juice and subsequently attempted to grab a Twi'lek dancing girl. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Alcoholic beverages Category:Juices
