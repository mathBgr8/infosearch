Drac Gerrat
1055

Drac Gerrat was a fisherman. In 31 BBY, he owned and operated a fishing vessel, the Icon, on Dorumaa. When not at work, he could usually be found in one of Dorumaa's cantinas. He dreamed of killing a Nus Whale, and marketing polished bits of whale bone as good luck charms.
