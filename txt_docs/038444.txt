Webweaver
1947

Webweavers were a large species of spiders indigenous to the planet Kashyyyk. They were capable of devouring humans. Wookiees hunted and ate the spiders. Infant webweavers were larger than a human hand and lived in nests with numerous individuals.Legends of the Alliance During the liberation of Kashyyyk in 5 ABY,Star Wars: Timelines dates the events of Aftermath: Life Debt to 5 ABY. Han Solo, Chewbacca, and their allies released a swarm of webweaver spiders aboard the Imperial Star Destroyer Dominion. The spiders proceeded to kill Rear Admiral Urian Orlan and several stormtroopers.
