Battle of Excarga
2719

Less than a month after the Battle of Geonosis, the Confederacy of Independent Systems invaded the planet Excarga after discovering the valuable ore. Garr's parents were separated from him in the battle, and the Separatists took control of the planet's ore-processing facilities and took everybody on the planet prisoner.
