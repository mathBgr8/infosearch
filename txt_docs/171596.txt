Gaarni
966

Gaarni was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in service of the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. As the Battle on the Frontier drew to a close, Frigate Gaarni replaced the battle weary Nebulon-B2 frigate Ludwick. The Gaarni arrived as a part of a larger task force responsible for relieving Grand Admiral Thrawn's initial invasion force sent to establish Space Station NL-1 in the Pakuuni system. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
