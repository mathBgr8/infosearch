Madra Teene
728

Madra Teene was a classic opera that told of a clash between a colonial world and a droid uprising against their controllers. The Theatre Danske performed the play during a visit of the New Republic ship, the FarStar. An assassin droid, sent to eliminate members of the FarStar crew, used the play as cover to approach them during a performance. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Operas
