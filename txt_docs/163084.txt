Garfield Reeves-Stevens
1456

Garfield Reeves-Stevens is an author who, along with his wife and co-author Judith Reeves-Stevens, penned two Star Wars short stories in the 1990s. They wrote "One Last Night in the Mos Eisley Cantina: The Tale of the Wolfman and the Lamproid" for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina and "A Bad Feeling: The Tale of EV-9D9" for Tales from Jabba's Palace. Outside of Star Wars, they are well-known for authoring a bevy of books in the Star Trek franchise, including a series co-written by William Shatner. Category:Short story authors
