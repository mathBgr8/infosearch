Caretaker virus
1224

thumb|300px|Droid [[CD601, infected by the virus, saves a Smiler.]] The Caretaker virus was a computer virus developed and unleashed by the Ithorian botanist and roboticist Zorneth, in an effort to save the Smilers from exploitation. The virus was targeted to infect droids. When the droids encountered a Smiler in trouble, the virus took control of their systems and forced the droids to rescue the Smiler, no matter what the cost. The virus helped Zorneth keep the secret of savorium herb, but it also was planned to help him infect the galaxy with savorium.
