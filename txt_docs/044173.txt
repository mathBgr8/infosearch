Haum Do
1430

Haum Do was a female Kaminoan journalist with a bubbly personality. An orphan on Kamino, Do grew up in the information-rich Datum District and became a reporter. She was quite curious for a Kaminoan and had the uncanny knack of appearing wherever and whenever important stories broke, whether she was invited or not. She began working as Mon Calamari correspondent for TriNebulon News, usually based on Foamwander City. Natives dealing with her were unsure about what species she was.
