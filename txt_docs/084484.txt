Marquee droid
636

Brightly-lit marquee droids were used in public areas by businesses and other organizations to advertise their locations or draw the attention of a passerby to something in the immediate area. The droids were equipped with repulsorlifts so that they and their messages could draw as much attention as possible. Depending on where they were ordered to go, they could stay in a single position, float around within a specified area, or follow a specified object or person. *Young Jedi Knights: The Lost Ones Category:Class five droid models
