Shockwave generator rod
1077

Shockwave generator rods were weapons mounted on Basilisk war droids. A cluster of rods were mounted in the hose of the war droid. When activated, the rods combined their charges to release a plasma burst capable of puncturing a starship's hull. *The Essential Guide to Droids *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Mandalorian weapons Category:Plasma weaponry
