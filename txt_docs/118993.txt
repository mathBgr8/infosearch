Barsoulentinial Enkhet
1416

Barsoulentinial Enkhet was a Human male pilot who served as a member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. He was a member of Koris Haslam's commando team and served in several missions, including the rescue of Gebnerret Vibrion from the Imperial prison on Selnesh, where he disguised himself as a stormtrooper to gain entry, and flew the team's transport during its escape from Imperial ships.
