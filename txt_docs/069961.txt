Mellona Gantrolo
1551

Baroness Mellona Gantrolo was a lesser noble who was married to a Pelagian Baron. She was a House Mecetti sleeper agent who planned to maneuver herself, her husband or eventually her children into House Pelagia's Privy council. Prior to the Battle of Endor Mellona was a beautiful woman in her early twenties, a dedicated socialite who treated Procopia as her personal playground. She was totally dedicated to her true house and was willing to sacrifice everything to achieve her objectives. Baroness Gantrolo was particularly attuned at detecting the political sympathies of others and was an expert at echoing the sentiments back.
