Eye of Deth
663

The Eye of Deth was the name of a great green gemstone that belonged to the mercantile warlord Deth Korakill. When Korakill was murdered by the Yevetha during their purge of the Koornacht Cluster, the gem was taken by the Yevethans and later placed in a trophy room onboard the Aramadia.
