Justin Chung
2267

Justin Chung is an artist whose work for Star Wars includes illustrating sketch cards for the Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith, Star Wars: 30th Anniversary, Star Wars: The Clone Wars (film), Star Wars Galaxy Series Four, The Clone Wars: Season One, Star Wars Galaxy Series Five, Clone Wars: Rise of the Bounty Hunters, Star Wars Galaxy Series Six, and Star Wars Galactic Files trading card sets for Topps. Justin was invited by Lucasfilm to be a guest at the Celebration IV Art Show for which he illustrated a limited edition Star Wars print. The Jedi Knight Kai Justiss, created and frequently illustrated by Joe Corroney, is named after Justin and based on his likeness. Kai Justiss has also been illustrated by Jan Duursema and Chris Trevas. Authors that have contributed stories with Kai Justiss include Pablo Hidalgo, Abel G. Peña, Daniel Wallace, and John Ostrander.
