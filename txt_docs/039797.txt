C4-CZN ion field gun
1108

The C4-CZN ion field gun was tripod-mountable ion cannon, ideal for providing infantry with anti-starship or anti-vehicle firepower. Operatives working for Rebel SpecForce used a C4-CZN to repel an attack from two Imperial Gamma-class assault shuttles on Level 88 on Nar Shaddaa in 1 BBY. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ground-based ion cannons
