Imperial Droid Corporation
1039

The Imperial Droid Corporation was the primary manufacturer of assassin droids and combat droids of the Galactic Empire. It was created when the chief droid developers - Banche Tech, Sencil Corporation, Reiber Manufacturing and SFI Systems - were organized together by the Empire to streamline their productions to the Galactic Emperor's desires. *Pirates & Privateers *The Far Orbit Project *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid manufacturers
