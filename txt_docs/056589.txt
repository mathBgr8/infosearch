LN-26
1256

LN-26 was Adar Tallon's droid majordomo at his home on Tatooine. She served Tallon with his wife Kay, working alongside the two servants Morr and Cala Wenn. LN-26 had been programmed with a feminine personality and mannerisms. It was easily scared, as shown when the crew of the Alabak's Gold met it. During the Galactic Civil War, Adar Tallon decided to leave Tatooine and took Kay and LN-26 with him. *Tatooine Manhunt Category:Droids with feminine programming Category:Service droids
