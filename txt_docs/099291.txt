Eelak also managed to become a member in the prestigious Goa-Ato and one of the few bounty hunters allowed to leave the planet, sometime after the Battle over Coruscant. Goa-Ato official Nilesk and her assistant Opdoa were surprised to see an An'yettu making it into the Guild; considering the clan's unpopularity with the Grand Protector Navik the Red, who pushed them in isolation, they considered to assign one of the Most Dangerous (and low-paying) bounties.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Eelak was given the task of hunting down the Wookiee Gerraba for Jabba the Hutt; Nilesk didn't considered worthy to notify Jabba about the assignment as she doubted that Fask would survive it. Opdoa (who mistaked Eelak for a male) pointed out that in case she could bring the bounty in would result in Eelak earning an Atiang award; however the nefarious duo knew that "sometimes" the verification process could be stuck indeterminately.
