Soo Rcharrz was a humanoid member of the Rebel Four, a team of freedom fighters affiliated with the Alliance to Restore the Republic. The team was led by the IG-series droid F4-MF, and also included members Falaem Onn and Grimgrim. By 0 BBY, during the Galactic Civil War between the Alliance and the Galactic Empire, an informant told the Rebel Four that the Empire was establishing a base within the stone ruins of the Outer Rim planet Vatleria. The team traveled to the ruins, where F4-MF verified the information after sighting Imperial stormtroopers. Rcharrz asked the droid what their plan was, and F4-MF huddled with the team and announced that they would stop the Imperials, confident that they would be victorious. The Rebel Four charged into battle as F4-MF loudly declared that it was "Rebellin' time." In combat, Rcharrz fired at the stormtroopers with a blaster rifle alongside F4-MF. The team's attack was interrupted by the Sith Lord Darth Vader, who activated ropes that pulled the operatives into death chambers that killed each of them in unique ways. Rcharrz was obliterated into nothingness. Vader afterward lamented at how easily the Rebel Four had been defeated. Rcharrz was a brave freedom fighter and answered to F4-MF during the mission to Vatleria. The operative had white skin, black eyes, and short blond hair that was parted over to their right side. Rcharrz wielded a blaster rifle in combat. Their outfit consisted of a light blue shirt, a black vest, blue pants, and a black belt and boots. Soo Rcharrz appeared in "The Rebel Four," a non-canon comic written and illustrated by Jay Stephens and published in the ninth issue of the anthology series Star Wars Tales by Dark Horse Comics on October 10, 2001. The character's name and fate resemble the Marvel Comics superhero Sue Richards, the Invisible Woman.
