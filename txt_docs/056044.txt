In his youth, the Human Bix Arden-Govia left behind his pampered life on the nearby planet Bakura and signed on as a ship's boy aboard a freighter out of Gymelo. By 4 ABY,According to Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds, Bix Arden-Govia had returned from his travels to Bakura by the time the planet was invaded by the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. Arden-Govia, who had learned much about the worlds of the Shiritoku Spur region during his travels, had returned home to Bakura. Gymelo was first mentioned in the Star Wars Roleplaying Game sourcebook Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds, which was published by Wizards of the Coast in 2004.
