Fathead
820

Fathead produces giant wall graphic appliqués and is officially licensed to produce high resolution Star Wars graphics. They have produced appliqués from the Original trilogy, the Prequel trilogy, Star Wars Rebels, the Star Wars Anthology Series and the Sequel trilogy. They also produce sports, art, and entertainment graphics. As of early 2025, Fathead no longer carries any Star Wars products on their website. * *
