Bley-San
1355

Bley-San was a female living on Tatooine in 22 BBY. She owned a cantina in Mos Espa. When Boba Fett killed Gilramos Libkath to receive a bounty from Jabba the Hutt, Ygabba Hise said that Bley-San would help the kids find their families and relatives back since she still owed her a favor for unnamed reasons. *Boba Fett: Hunted *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Business owners and executives Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Tatooinians
