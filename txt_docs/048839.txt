First Administrator
980

The First Administrator was the head of the New Republic General Ministry. The First Administrator was charged with setting down the protocol of the Ministry, and funneled information from the General Ministry to the Chief of Sate or Minister of State, as well as the Ruling Council and the New Republic Senate.
