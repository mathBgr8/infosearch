Raymond Jafelice
889

Raymond Jafelice is a writer, director, artist, and actor. He served as director and storyboard artist for many episodes of Ewoks. He is also known for his work in television shows such as Babar, Little Bear, Care Bears, Strawberry Shortcake, Redwall, and more.
