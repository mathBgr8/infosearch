These starfighters were designed to function solely in the Naboo star system and as such they possessed limited hyperdrives and instead focused the remaining systems on weapons and shields. Visually, they resembled the N-1 starfighter, but were painted forest green. They were designed to maintain operations for long periods at a time and were quite independent.
