Arali Dil
1085

Arali Dil was a female Twi'lek student at Garqi Agricultural University and friend of Dynba Tesc. She was one of many students who were disgruntled with the Galactic Empire and began slicing into their mainframe on Garqi. She, along with Dynba and Sihha, escaped Garqi onboard the Star's Delight thanks to the help of Corran Horn. Category:Females Category:Slicers Category:Twi'leks
