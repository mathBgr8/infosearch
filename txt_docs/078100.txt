Fortress Baarlos
1172

Fortress Baarlos was an ancient citadel that had been built on the world of Pochi some ten thousand years prior to the Battle of Yavin. Faarl the Conqueror renovated this fortress and used it as his base, placing it under the protection of his Tulvaree guards. The fortress was all but impenetrable; until Boba Fett found his way inside. The way he found his way inside is known only to Boba Fett .
