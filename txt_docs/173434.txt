Goldhawk
1833

The Goldhawk was a YT-1300 light freighter in service of the Rebel Alliance. It served in the Airam sector during the Rebellion's operations there. Assigned to the new-built starship component factory Soren, Goldhawk participated in the Imperial attack there. With Soren losing shield strength, it sought to escape with select personnel aboard.
