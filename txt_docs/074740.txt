Anti-Vehicle Turret (disambiguation)
533

Anti-Vehicle Turrets were gun emplacements designed to combat enemy vehicles. There were a number of models of turrets designed by manufacturers that were suitable for the role. *Bp.4 anti-vehicle turret, used by the Imperial Army during the Galactic Civil War. *1.4 FD P-Tower, notably used by the Rebels in the Battle of Hoth.
