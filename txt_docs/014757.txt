|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=19 BBY (36 months ABG)The New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=77 }} Republic 77 is the seventy-seventh issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on July 20, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics. The losses for the Republic&mdash;and the Jedi&mdash;in the Clone Wars have been staggering. And few battles have been as hard fought as the one on Saleucami. Here, the greatest military threat mounted by the Separatists has been encountered, the Republic's greatest heroes have faltered, and the heart, mind, and motives of Jedi Master Quinlan Vos have been laid bare. His secret dealings with Count Dooku exposed, and the depth to which he was manipulated revealed, Vos stands on a knife's edge between the dark side and redemption! This is the final issue of Republic before it is overtaken by the events in Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith. Not to be missed!
