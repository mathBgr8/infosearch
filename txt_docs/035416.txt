Hyperdrive sled
1903

A hyperdrive sled was a device that, in much the same way that the hyperdrive rings were used by Jedi starfighters, allowed a vehicle without a hyperdrive to enter hyperspace. The sleds were most commonly used with CloakShape fighters. Such sleds used R1-series astromech droids, and by 10 ABY cost 15,000 credits. Militia Leader Klyn Shanga used a beat-up, cobbled-together Dreadnaught-class engine as a makeshift hyperdrive sled when he and his fellow pilots hunted Lando Calrissian.
