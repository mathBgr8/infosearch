DL-22 blaster pistol
2116

The BlasTech Industries DL-22 Blaster Pistol was designed as a response to the MerrSonn DD6 weapon. Luke Skywalker had one of these weapons. Reen Irana also used one of these blasters.Scourge The gun's barrel used a higher-grade focusing crystal to increase the damage dealt by the weapon's blasts, at the expense of the gun's stun setting. The DL-22 was held in high regard by those who prefer to customize their weapons.
