Pizzazz 8
921

Pizzazz 8 is the eighth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated May 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. The issue also features an ad and order form to join the Official Star Wars Fan Club. 08
