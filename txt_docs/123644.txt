Purple Claw
771

Purple Claw was a group of two CR90 corvettes under the service of the Galactic Empire. The corvettes were trying to destroy a B-wing Assembly with the help of Assault Gunboats and another corvette group, Red Tooth, which was witnessed by the Habassa. Both of the corvette groups and all of the Assault Gunboats were destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Galactic Empire starship groups
