Max-W 100
2039

max-W 100, nicknamed "Max, the Janitor Droid", was a cleaning droid that worked in the Droidnostics Center at the Star Tours travel agency. Max was a hard-working droid, although he sometimes blew out some carbon dioxide to relax. It formerly worked at Matsushita Electric before being transferred over to Star Tours. Its nickname, "PanaRobo," was a reminder of its former career. The background for this droid comes from a "Spot the Droid" poster displayed at the Tokyo Disneyland Star Tours, which was translated at the EndorExpress.net fan site.
