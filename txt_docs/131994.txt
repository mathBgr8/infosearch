Honoghr Rakatan temple
2005

The Rakatan temple was a structure built on the planet Honoghr by the Rakata of the Infinite Empire. It was similar in appearance to other Rakatan temples, such as the Temple of the Ancients on Lehon. The native Noghri were said to remember the Rakata only as gods, and protected the temple with their lives. It served as neutral ground for the planet's various clans. During the Clone Wars, the Noghri used the temple to store the Scientific Instrument Package they salvaged from the wreckage of the Gahenna. They stashed the SIP in a chamber protected by poison hyperdarts. Jedi General Rii'ke En attempted to infiltrate the temple, but he and his clone trooper were killed by its protectors.
