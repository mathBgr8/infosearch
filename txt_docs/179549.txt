Cophrigin horned goat
1499

This horned goat was a herbivorous quadruped from the Outer Rim world of Cophrigin V. It was distinguished by three cranial horns. Two horns curved downwards on either side of the head and one extended from the top of the head, curving back towards the tail, covering the ridged neck. Some of these creatures could be seen grazing as Darth Vader's Lambda-class shuttle flew overhead when he arrived on Cophrigin 5 to eliminate Jedi Master An'ya Kuro in 1 BBY. As no official name has been given to these creatures, they are conjecturally named for their resemblance to a goat.
