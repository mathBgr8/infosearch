Darth Desolous entry |conc=Draggulch PeriodThe Essential Atlas |next=New Sith Wars |image= |name=Fourth Great Schism |conflict= |date=2000 BBY |place= |outcome=*Emergence of the New Sith *Beginning of the New Sith Wars |keyparties=*Jedi Order *Sith |participants=*Darth Ruin *At least fifty fallen Jedi }} The Fourth Great Schism was a pivotal split among the ranks of the Jedi Order in 2000 BBY. Around this time, a Jedi Master known as Phanius had abandoned the ways of the Jedi in pursuit of alternate teachings, leading him on a quest to revive the dark-side-worshipping Sith Order. By this point, the Sith were commonly believed to have been destroyed for centuries. The fallen Jedi Master proved otherwise, however, after seeking out a number of existing Sith clans throughout the galaxy.Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force Assuming leadership over these clans, Phanius declared himself a Dark Lord of the Sith under the identity of Darth Ruin. Prompted by the Dark Lord's ascension, fifty additional Jedi followed in Ruin's footsteps, leaving the Jedi Order in favor of his growing Sith empire.
