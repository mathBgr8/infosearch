Ken Wheat
963

Ken Wheat (born 1950) is a filmmaker. Along with his brother Jim Wheat, he wrote and directed Ewoks: The Battle for Endor, from a story by George Lucas. Their other credits include the 1999 telefilm Free Fall, which starred Hayden Christensen. Category:Directors Category:TV screenwriters
