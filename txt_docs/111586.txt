Borderlands
2110

The Borderlands or Borderland was the name given to the stretch of space between the territory of the New Republic and the First Order.Before the Awakening The Cassander sector was located in the Borderlands, and during the Cold War between the First Order and the Resistance, the First Order attempted to annex the sector. Star Wars: Battles that Changed the Galaxy labels the Borderlands as the Trans-Hydian Borderlands;Star Wars: Battles that Changed the Galaxy however, Star Wars: The Galactic Explorer's Guide instead labels that region as the New Territories,Star Wars: The Galactic Explorer's Guide and Star Wars: Build the Millennium Falcon 21 places Felucia and the Thanium sector in the Trans-Hydian, consistent with the Trans-Hydian's original definition in The Essential Atlas.The Essential Atlas *Before the Awakening *Poe Dameron: Flight Log *The Last Jedi: Bomber Command *Star Wars: Battles that Changed the Galaxy Category:Regions in the galaxy
