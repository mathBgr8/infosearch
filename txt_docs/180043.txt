Torvin Xyn
1100

Torvin Xyn was a Yuuzhan Vong spy. Even in his Human disguise, he had shoulders as wide as a Wookiee's. He was killed by Jacen Solo with two other spies onboard a conference room in the Right to Rule where a Yuuzhan Vong collaborators were summoned when he removed his ooglith masquer and attacked the young Jedi. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Spies Category:Yuuzhan Vong warriors
