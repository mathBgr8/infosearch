Sentient Property Crime Bureau
499

The Sentient Property Crime Bureau was a special division of the police force on Phindar. The task of the organization was that of stopping the theft and resale of "personality-bearing artifacts", also known as droids. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous Category:Law enforcement agencies
