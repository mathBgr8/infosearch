Peter Sauder
1280

Peter Sauder is a Canadian screenwriter who is known for his Star Wars contributions to Star Wars Droids (Nelvana) the animated television series (1985-1986). Sauder has also done a number of non Star Wars films and television series such as the Care Bears movies (whose three movies he wrote), Babar and Strawberry Shortcake. Sauder was also the head writer for the very first season (1983-84) of Inspector Gadget, as well as the Disney Channels live action series The Edison Twins. In the late 1990s, he was story editor for the computer-animated preschool show Rolie Polie Olie. His latest project (as of 2006) is the CBC science-fiction children's show, Lunar Jim.
