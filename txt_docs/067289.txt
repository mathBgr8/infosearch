Come-up flector
1330

Come-up flector was a term, possibly originating on Caprioril, for a person who worked starports or other points of debarkation, searching for potential buyers of products or services the buyer did not really need. Mara Jade posed as such a person under the alias 'Merellis'. Fleck, a Human male, was the chief come-up flector working for the Krish Gamgalon on Varonat. *Dark Force Rising *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Essential Guide to Characters Category:Sales occupations
