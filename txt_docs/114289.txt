Technology Center
997

The Technology Center was a building on Corellia, in its capital city, Coronet. During the battle at Corellia, Crix Madine was in the building when the Imperial attack commenced, and they surrounded the building with walkers and performed bombing runs on it. The Tech Center was saved by Rogue Squadron. *Star Wars: Rogue Squadron *Star Wars: Rogue Squadron: The Official Nintendo Player's Guide Category:Coronet City locations
