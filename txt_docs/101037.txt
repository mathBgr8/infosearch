Miradyne Limited
2615

Miradyne Limited, also known as Miradyne Computer Systems, was a small-time manufacturer of starship avionics packages that went out of business during the height of the Galactic Empire. Miradyne supplied components for the Z-10 Seeker scout ship and when the company went out of business, this made it difficult to find replacement parts for the Z-10. *Star Wars: X-Wing *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *Stock Ships *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook Category:Starship technology companies
