Domain Kwaad
1743

Domain Kwaad was a Domain of the Yuuzhan Vong Shaper caste. This Domain was one of the most skilled Domains of the Shaper caste. However, the Domain was cursed with several heretics. It also appeared to control Domain Yim. Another particular note about the Domain was that an individual was not allowed to touch a member of Domain's Kwaad's head. The phrase "Veka, Kwaad" was used as a code to gain access to the shaping facilities used by the domain. Domain Kwaad's most infamous member was Mezhan Kwaad, who had an affair with a member of the Warrior caste, violated Shaping protocols when she tried to reshape Tahiri Veila into a Yuuzhan Vong, and openly declared that the Gods did not exist. Riina Kwaad was the personality shaped into Tahiri Veila. Another member of the Domain was Qelah Kwaad, who was placed in charge of the Vongforming of Yuuzhan'tar after Nen Yim (Mezhan's apprentice) disappeared.
