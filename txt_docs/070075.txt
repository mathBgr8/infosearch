Alderaan (yacht)
1596

Alderaan was a space yacht owned by Chief of State Leia Organa Solo as of 14 ABY. It was named for her destroyed homeworld. It saw heavy use from Organa Solo after her children were kidnapped by Hethrir and she was forced to go incognito.
