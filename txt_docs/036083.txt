Superluminal Transit
568

Superluminal transit was the Yuuzhan Vong method of faster-than-light travel. The Vong used organically grown creatures, such as dovin basals, to propel Koros-Strohna and other Vong craft. As of this writing, it is unknown if the term Superluminal transit is simply the Yuuzhan Vong name for hyperspace, or another form of faster than light transportation entirely. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
