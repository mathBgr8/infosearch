Paaloc incense
950

Paaloc incense was a type of incense that was reserved for only the absolute highest-ranked members of the Yuuzhan Vong caste system. Its scent was said to evoke memories of Yuuzhan'tar, the Yuuzhan Vong's ancestral homeworld.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Lord Shimrra burned the incense within his citadel. Because of its rarity, paaloc was outlawed amongst the lower castes; the penalty of execution awaited any Yuuzhan Vong of insufficient status caught possessing the substance. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Incense Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
