Hestiv
2980

Hestiv was a High General of the Imperial Remnant, in command of the Imperial forces of Yaga Minor at the time of the Caamas Document Crisis and the attempted coup of the Imperial Remnant by Moff Disra, Royal Guardsman Grodin Tierce, and the con-artist Flim in 19 ABY. Loyal to then-Fleet Admiral Gilad Pellaeon, he reluctantly agreed that the only possible way the Empire could survive would be to sign a treaty of peace with the New Republic. In the last days of the Caamas Document Crisis and the Galactic Civil War, he agreed to allow the New Republic's expert slicer Zakarisz Ghent search the databank files of the Ubiqtorate base at Yaga Minor for an original copy of the Caamas Document. Hestiv was a competent and loyal Imperial officer. On relative good terms with the native Yagai, he disagreed with Palpatine's brutal and intolerent politics.
