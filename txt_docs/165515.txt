A Calamari blessing pot was a pot where Mon Calamari offered their blessings. Gial Ackbar gave Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo one at their wedding. Leia considered it to be ugly and wanted to smash it. *Before the Storm *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Mon Calamari culture
