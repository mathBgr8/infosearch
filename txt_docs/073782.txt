TDL-3.5
1013

TDL-3.5 was a TDL nanny droid that was purchased by Anakin Solo. It tried to replace C-3PO as caretaker of the Solo children under a code invented by Anakin, "Bantha four five six." Winter Celchu, who was taking care of the children with C-3PO, recognized that "Bantha four five six" was not the Solo family recognition code for their droids. *The New Rebellion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:TDL nanny droids
