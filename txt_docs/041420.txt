Black Nebula (organization)
2579

Black Nebula was a short-lived offshoot of Black Sun, formed after Xizor's death. It was headed by Dequc, a low-level Jeodu operative of Black Sun who styled himself "Prince" after Xizor's death. Black Nebula's headquarters were in the Bubble Cliffs on the planet Qiaxx, and many Jeodu joined the organization. Black Nebula also drew in several remnant cells of Black Sun.
