Tra's M'ins
2392

Tra's M'ins was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive, Tra's M'ins was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. Master M'ins, through an extended period of individual study, became a noted diplomat for the Galactic Republic. Subject to receiving missions from the Council of Reconciliation, Master M'ins was dispatched to mediate the Duinuogwuin-Gotal conflict, a war sometime from 35 BBY to 33 BBY. Accompanied by fellow Jedi Master Jorus C'baoth, M'ins' negotiation attempts were hijacked by the lower ranked C'Baoth.
