Heritage Fleet
1602

The Heritage Fleet was the military force of the Heritage Council of Hapes during the Second Galactic Civil War. Their flagship was the Kendall and included around seventy capital ships, including Nova-class battle cruisers, the dozen House AlGray Battle Dragons, and the Galney fleet. Its starfighters included Z-95 Headhunters and Miy'til starfighters. Although bolstered by a Five Worlds fleet, it was defeated at the Battle of Hapes after Jedi Jaina Solo and Zekk discovered the plot while investigating Ducha Lalu Morwan.
