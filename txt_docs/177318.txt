Aeran
1417

Aeran was a female Human weapons expert who lived on Junction V and was a close friend to Cilia Dil during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. In 44 BBY, she joined a select group, along with her friend Cilia, Stephin, Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn, and Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi, flew to Delaluna to steal plans on the super-weapon, Annihilator. However, these weapons did not exist and it was just a rumor that Lorian Nod was using on Junction V.
