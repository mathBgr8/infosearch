Seeker Transport
1171

The Seeker Transport, also known as the message droid hyperspace pod was designed by Arakyd Industries to provide their Seeker AS-M12 droids with the capacity to travel between star systems. The design was based on their Viper probe droid hyperspace pod, but modified for civilian purposes. Classed as a starfighter, the droid was 2 meters long, and was fully automated, although only capable of storing one jump in its nav computer. It was capable of speeds up to 950 km/h in atmosphere.
