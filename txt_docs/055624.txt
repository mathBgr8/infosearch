Sor-Sull II
1226

Sor-Sull II was a Sullustan EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that carried some of Sullustan delegation to a diplomatic meeting between the Sullustans and the Alliance to Restore the Republic near Sullust during Operation Strike Fear. The meeting was interrupted by the arrival of the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Invincible.
