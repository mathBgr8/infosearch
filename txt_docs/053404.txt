Cirrus Colonies Incorporated
640

Cirrus Colonies Incorporated specialized in prefabricated buildings and construction materials for settlers and explorers, likely for the use on recently colonized worlds. Many of the prefabricated buildings came equipped with their own particle shields to aid in hostile environments. In addition, the buildings, and the construction materials, were always extremely durable and relatively inexpensive, thus making them very effective. *Secrets of the Sisar Run Category:Construction companies
