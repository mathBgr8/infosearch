Tolok Naap
1793

Tolok Naap was a member of the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Tens of cycles before the Invasion of Yavin 4, Naap was part of the war party of Vua Rapuung. When Rapuung resurfaced on Yavin 4 with Anakin Solo, Naap was part of a group of warriors who attempted to stop them from reaching the shaper Mezhan Kwaad. He challenged Rapuung to personal combat, commanding him to take up an amphistaff. Rapuung refused, deigning to fight him with his bare hands. Eighty seconds later, Naap and his group had all been killed. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Yuuzhan Vong warriors
