Cosmic Chance
1064

thumb|250px|[[Luke Skywalker/Legends|Luke Skywalker plays Cosmic Chance.]] Cosmic Chance was a game of chance. Players would use their "luck" to influence cosmic rays that flashed across the galaxy, in hopes of hitting targets. Other gamblers would bet on whether they would score or not. Scoring was very rare, and nobody had ever scored more than twice. Luke Skywalker was very good at Cosmic Chance. *"Gambler's World" Category:Games
