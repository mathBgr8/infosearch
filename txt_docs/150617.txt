Abil Vilorin
2499

Abil Vilorin was an agent with the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Vilorin was the leader of a ten-person cell located on the planet Brosi. Vilorin's cell performed surveillance on an area under the influence of the local resistance group known as the Brosin Underground. Although the Brosin Underground was opposed to the oppressive Corporate Sector Authority-controlled government, their methods made the CSA nervous. Vilorin and their commanders, including General Carlist Rieekan, feared the CSA would increase security and find Vilorin's cell&mdash;as well as three underground Rebel operatives working as false Zinsian spin-sealer technicians. Vilorin repeatedly attempted to contact one of the leaders of the Brosin Underground, Vuraj Marn, to discuss this issue, and convince him to change the Underground's plans. However, as of 2 ABY, Vilorin doubted they would succeed due to the different goals of both resistance groups. Furthermore, Marn proved difficult to find. Abil's surname is alternately spelled "Filorin" or "Vilorin" throughout Alliance Intelligence Reports.
