Nas Choka's tactician
1709

A Yuuzhan Vong warrior served as the chief battle tactician to Warmaster Nas Choka in the prelude to the Defense of Mon Calamari. He was concerned about their campaigns in the Imperial Remnant and the Farlax sector, believing they could have used the Imperials and the Yevetha to good use. When Zonama Sekot arrived at the Coruscant system, the tactician expressed doubt over whether or not it was the Living World of Yuuzhan Vong legend, believing it to be some sort of fabrication by the Jedi and the Galactic Alliance.
