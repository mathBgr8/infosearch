Around the year 0 BBY,The events of Empire Blues occur shortly before the Battle of Yavin, which took part in 0 BBY according to The New Essential Chronology. Jubal and Oxbel were the only other Devaronians that Kardue'sai'Malloc, a Devaronian who went under the assumed name of Labria, knew to live on Tatooine. Jubal was first mentioned in "Empire Blues: The Devaronian's Tale," a short story written by Daniel Keys Moran that was included in Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina, which was released by Bantam Spectra in 1995.
