Rafael Ferrer
1978

Rafael Ferrer (born March 23, 1960) is an American actor and voice actor who voiced Darth Malak in the video games Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic and Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords (the latter as part of a Force vision experienced by the player's character on Korriban) Ferrer is the son of José Ferrer and Rosemary Clooney, brother of Miguel Ferrer, and cousin of George Clooney. He has split his life between New York and Los Angeles. Ferrer began primarily as a theater actor, but later switched to focusing on voice acting and narration. Ferrer has narrated commercials for such high profile clients as Subaru, Ford, Miller Beer and Smith Barney.
