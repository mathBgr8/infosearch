Ho'Din (language)
1126

Ho'Din was the language of the Ho'Dins from the planet Moltok. Its spoken form included an amount of croaks and hisses. The written form of Ho'Din included characters derived from the shapes of Moltok flora. Ho'Din lexicon includes a great amount of plant-based idioms and metaphors. The name "Ho'Din" meant 'walking flower' in the language. *The Last Command *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
