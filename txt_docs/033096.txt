Warren Fu

}} Warren J. Fu was a visual effects art director at Industrial Light & Magic who has worked on all three movies of the prequel trilogy. Probably his most notable contribution was in the design of General Grievous, along with Iain McCaig, Ryan Church, and Robert E. Barnes, though he admits that he prefers his work on the MagnaGuards over Grievous. thumb|left|180px|[[Sheltay Retrac's costume was a design concept by Warren Fu.]] During his childhood, he drew Star Wars art, including an image of the Battle of Hoth. }} thumb|right|230px|Concept art of [[Grievous|General Grievous]] His first name rendered in Aurebesh appears as an easter egg in Episode II, printed on the side of the building Jango Fett shot Zam Wesell from.
