The Aviary (Centares)
989

The Aviary was a place on Centares near the Museum of the Old Republic. Ban Papeega tried to hide in there from Darth Vader. The Sith Lord found Papeega and killed him. Afterwards he burned the building down and killed the songbirds, to prevent them from repeating the name "Skywalker." *Vader's Quest 1 Aviary
