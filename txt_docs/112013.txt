Star's Delight
2034

Star's Delight was a Gymsnor-3 light freighter owned by the Duros weapon trader Lai Nootka. In addition to Nootka, the crew comprised the Devaronian Saricia and a Sullustan pilot. In 6 ABY, the crew were captured by Prefect Mosh Barris for attempting to sell X-wing spare parts and proton torpedoes, keeping the Star's Delight grounded on Garqi.
