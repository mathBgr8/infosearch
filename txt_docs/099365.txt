N'Gant-Zarvel
1201

N'Gant-Zarvel was a localized company on the planet Rodia. It specialized in weapons productions, and used the Hutts to distribute the weapons. This led to N'gant-Zarvel weapons falling into the hands of mercenaries and criminals.
