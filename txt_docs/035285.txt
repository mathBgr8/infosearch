Blue Moon
2921

The Blue Moon was a strip club and bar located in the city of Trid on Danuta. A mirror ran the wall behind, specked by insects, before which were several bottles and liquid vessels. Ten booths lined the opposite wall. The tables around the salon were too small, although groups of customers tried to fit themselves around them. According to policy, only groups of people were allowed to occupy a booth after 8:00 pm.Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire A bored-looking dancer tried to entertain some customers up toward the front, during the duration of her two-hour shift. The Max Rebo Band also played there. According to Meck Odom, the food was like Gamorrean garbage, but he frequented it to enjoy Max Rebo. The restrooms led to a backdoor, exiting to the site of the building into an alley with poorly tended shrubbery and pools of vomit. Locals, colonists and farmers came there but most of its customers were spacers and aliens who had no other place to go. The proprietor wore usually a dingy apron, and was obsessed in wiping the same section of the bar. In 1 BBY, Rebel agent Kyle Katarn met his old Imperial Academy roommate, Meck Odom, in the Blue Moon. Jan Ors who was shadowing Katarn, entered the club, and the doorman asked her if she'd be interested to work there. Katarn needed Odom's assistance to gain access into the Imperial research facility in Trid.
