|type=Paperback |pages=128 pages |dimensions=10.5 x 8.2 x 0.2 inches |weight=318 g |isbn=0761539859 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter: Prima's Official Game Guide was a strategy book for Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter by Prima Games. It was released on March 19, 2002. The guide detailed coverage of both the PlayStation 2 and Xbox versions of the game. It contained a detailed walkthrough out the game. Also included were comprehensive level descriptions and combat tips for helping to beat the more difficult foes of the game. Jedi Starfighter
