Eye of the Albino Cyclops
1025

The Eye of the Albino Cyclops was a mythical artifact of the Skakoan religion. The Eye was said to be hidden in the Gates of Grontessiant. It was guarded by the Four See Seers of the Cyclops and the Lord Being of the Swirblies. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Artifacts Category:Mythology Category:Skakoan religion
