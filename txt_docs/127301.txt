Ladybabies
573

Ladybabies were a food, often covered in chocolate. Porcellus once served chocolate-covered ladybabies to Jabba the Hutt. He also occasionally used his chocolate ladybabies along with Beignets as bribes to the Gamorrean guards to take food to Jabba’s prisoners such as Han Solo/Legends, and Chewbacca/Legends.
