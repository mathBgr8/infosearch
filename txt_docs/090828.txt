Pad 9
1819

Pad 9 was a landing pad on the Outer Rim TerritoriesLegacy Era Campaign Guide planet Lok located within an hour of travel from the establishment Brogar's Safe House and Cantina. Around 137 ABY, a M'shento'su'Nikto who owed the fugitive Bothan Jedi Knight Hosk Trey'lis a favor arranged escape off of Lok for Trey'lis. The Nikto met with the Jedi at Brogar's and told him to be at Pad 9 in one hour. Trey'lis never made it to Pad 9, however, as he was captured by a trio of bounty hunters. Pad 9 was mentioned in the second issue of the comic series Star Wars: Legacy.
