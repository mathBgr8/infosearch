Tentator
1868

Tentator was a planet located within the Tentator system of the Colonies, used as a prison by the Galactic Republic. The Slam gang escaped from the Greylands Security Complex on-planet, but were recaptured and returned to the poorly-maintained high-security prison, along with their ship, an Ubrikkian star yacht.
