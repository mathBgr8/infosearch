Saychel
1311

Doctor Saychel was a Human male who served as an officer within the New Republic Intelligence Service. He once worked with a Coruscant safe house located in the Government District alongside Showolter, and was in contact with Han Solo, Luke Skywalker, and Lando Calrissian.
