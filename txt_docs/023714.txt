He operated out of a base on the planet Hoth. On one occasion, Jabba took advantage of Mehndra's love of jizz music to concoct an elaborate assassination plot. The Hutt sent his retainers, the Max Rebo Band, to Hoth to perform at Mehndra's Spawning Day party. Unbeknownst to Mehndra, the band's sound equipment was rigged to explode at the end of the concert. The explosion destroyed Mehndra's base, presumably killing him and most of his gang.
