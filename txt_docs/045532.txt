*Nar Shaddaa |locations=*Cato Neimoidia *Kwenn Space Station *Sleheyron |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Clans of the Ancients *Hutt Cartel }} Jiramma was a Hutt clan that operated from Nar Shaddaa. Darga Jiramma Mionne was a member of it. Jiramma was one of the Clans of the Ancients, the elite Hutt kajidics that could trace their ancestry back to the Hutt homeworld Varl, and was accorded the right to a seat on the Hutt Grand Council. The clan's throneworld was Kor Jiramma within the Bootana Hutta region of Hutt Space, while the moon Nar Shaddaa served as its primary base of operations. The clan also had spheres of influence on the planets Cato Neimoidia and Sleheyron, and also on Kwenn Space Station. During the time of the Galactic Civil War, Jiramma had close ties to Galactic Empire.
