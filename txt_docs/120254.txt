Galactic Loyalty Act
743

The Galactic Loyalty Act was a law passed by the Galactic Alliance Senate in 40 ABY. Detailing how member worlds were supposed to behave, it included the Anti-Sedition Provision, Law Enforcement Tools Provision, and the Suspicious Silence Provision.
