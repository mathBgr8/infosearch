Seventh-stage bank-courier repulsor-truck engine

thumb|right|150px|The engine, as used in the [[XJ-6 airspeeder/Legends|XJ-6 airspeeder as a dual set.]] This engine was a type of engine used in seventh-stage bank-courier repulsor-trucks, which were common on the world of Aargau. The XJ-6 utilized this type of engine, which, due to its use in larger cargo-carrying airspeeders, could produce a great deal more speed and power in a smaller, sportier type of craft. *Star Wars: Attack of the Clones Incredible Cross-Sections Category:Air vehicle engines
