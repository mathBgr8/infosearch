Tee-ubo Doole
1601

Tee-ubo Doole was a female Twi'lek scientist at ExGal-4 on Belkadan in 25 ABY. She was jealous of the attention the men at the station gave to Danni Quee, and enjoyed ryll. When she, along with Luther De'Ono, Bendodi Ballow-Reese, and Jerem Cadmir were sent out to investigate an incoming storm, Tee-ubo sacrificed her oxygen pack and her life to Cadmir, shooting herself before the insurmountable noxious fumes being emitted by dweebits brought to the planet by the Yuuzhan Vong warrior Yomin Carr killed her. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Astronomers Category:Extragalactic Society members Category:Females Category:Twi'leks
