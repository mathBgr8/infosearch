|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes=Yellow |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Alliance to Restore the Republic **Alliance Intelligence |masters= |apprentices= }} Rund Jesela was a male Bothan who lived during the Galactic Civil War. Following the Battle of Yavin, he reside onboard Tansarii Point Station, a space station located above Ord Mantell, in the Bright Jewel system. During that time, he was the director of the station's cloning facility. Furthermore, he was a Rebel Alliance operative, collecting information regarding Imperial activity for Agent Garek. In 1 ABY, Garek encountered a spacer who was seeking to help the Rebellion. To evaluate this new recruit, the Rebel agent tasked the spacer to retrieve datadisks with sensitive information from Rund Jesela and Adery Shrial, another Rebel spy. Although the datadisks only contained Grandma Drake's soup recipes, the spacer achieved the objective successfully and was later assigned to a real mission. Rund Jesela appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Rund Jesela was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
