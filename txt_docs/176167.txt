Broadcap bomb

Broadcap Bombs, or more commonly known as Punch-and-Pops were used by the Adumari for their aerial bombers. They were able to penetrate dozens of meters upon hitting the ground before detonating, which was ideal for taking out underground chambers and Turumme-warrens.
