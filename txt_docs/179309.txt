|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=*Producer *Director |companies= |website= }} Kevin Thomsen is a producer and director who has produced and directed numerous Star Wars audio dramas and audiobooks. Thomsen's first work for the Star Wars franchise and second job working with audiobooks was directing the 1995 audio drama adaptation of the comics Tales of the Jedi: Knights of the Old Republic and Tales of the Jedi: The Freedon Nadd Uprising by Tom Veitch.Tales of the Jedi audio drama Thomsen went on to produce and direct the abridged audiobooks for the novelizations of the original trilogy which despite being recorded after, they were released before the Tales of the Jedi adaptions. Thomsen later began working with Random House where they worked on over 150 Star Wars audiobooks. {| ! Year !! Title !!
