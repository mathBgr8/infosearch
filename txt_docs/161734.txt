Galliwig's Cantina
1705

|location= |interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=Galliwig }} Galliwig's Cantina was owned and run by the Human male known as Galliwig. The cantina included corner booths, and was known for playing music that offended the ears of some of the clientèle. During the Galactic Civil War, the Twi'lek free-trader Loh'khar the Finder used the cantina to conduct business transactions in, sitting in the same booth so that his regular customers could find him easily. In the cantina, Loh'khar acquired three Turazza eggs during a business deal; the eggs hatched into three sisters whom the Twi'lek named Rizzal, Vizzal, and Nizzal.The DarkStryder Campaign Galliwig's cantina appeared in the short text story Turazza Eggs by Peter M.
