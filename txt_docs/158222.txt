Crossings
606

"Crossings" were the term given by Zonama Sekot and the planet's inhabitants to their often-traumatic jumps through hyperspace. The earlier Crossings were extremely hazardous and only improved as Sekot adapted to the trials of lightspeed travel. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Phrases
