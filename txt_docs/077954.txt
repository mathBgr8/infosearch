19,016th Imperial Line Infantry (Training) Company
968

The 19,016th Imperial Line Infantry (Training) Company was the Imperial training company stationed at Training Camp IMIIF-138 on Sirpar. Nearly the size of a battalion, the company consisted of six infantry platoons and two heavy-weapons platoons. Each platoon consisted of eight squads, each of which had eight trainees who were commanded by a training sergeant. This meant that the line company and each individual platoon was augmented.Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Imperial Army companies
