Nano missile
1032

Nano missiles,Star Wars: Starfighter also known as nano-missles, were small projectile weapons, which were nearly indistinguishable from blaster bolts. Coupled with tracking technology, such as an ion encumbrance system, they were able to home in on enemy craft. They were utilized by several types of starfighter used by the Republic. Vana Sage's Guardian Mantis was equipped with nano missiles, which were coupled with a shield disabling tracker for maximum effect. *Star Wars: Starfighter *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Missiles
