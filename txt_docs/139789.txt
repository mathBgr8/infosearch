Angrix
1515

The Angrix was an Imperial extermination ship used by the Galactic Empire. Along with the Apocahk and Azgoghk, it was used by Grand Admiral Danetta Pitta to exterminate and experiment on aliens in the Outer Rim Territories prior to the Battle of Endor. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial extermination ships Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
