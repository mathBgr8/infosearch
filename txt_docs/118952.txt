Zorp House
1073

Zorp House was an apartment building on Coruscant. In 40 ABY, Dark Lady of the Sith Lumiya had an apartment on the three hundredth floor. It was later found by Luke and Mara Jade Skywalker after Lumiya participated in the death of Tresina Lobi. It had a Neimoidian building manager and contained 25,000 apartments.
