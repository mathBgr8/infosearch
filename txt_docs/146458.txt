|pages=40 |upc= |timeline=137 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Legacy |issue=19 }} Legacy 19 is the nineteenth issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series. This depicts the ultimate decision of Cade Skywalker to where his path leads him to. The galaxy-shaking storyline "Claws of the Dragon" reaches its unbelievable conclusion as the Fel Empire's enemies and allies all make their move for one man&mdash;Cade Skywalker! Cade stands face-to-face with the Emperor of the galaxy and leader of the Sith legions, Darth Krayt. Has the last Skywalker reached the point of no return on the path to darkness? Meanwhile, Deliah Blue and Jariah Syn execute a rescue plan for Cade that revolves around a single, significant word: "Boom." Cade Skywalker versus Darth Krayt!
