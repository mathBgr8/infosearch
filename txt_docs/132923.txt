|commission= |destroyed=3964 BBY, Taris |retired= |battles=Taris Siege |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Republic Navy |navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains=Admiral Jimas Veltraa |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} The Reliance was a Galactic Republic warship that served as the flagship for Admiral Jimas Veltraa during the Mandalorian Wars. During a Mandalorian advance that threatened the planet Taris in 3964 BBY, Veltraa reformed what remained of the shattered Jebble-Vanquo-Tarnith line into a makeshift force to defend Taris from a Mandalorian siege. Although Veltraa and the Reliance held out to the very end, the ship was eventually destroyed with all hands lost. The Reliance was first mentioned in The Admiral's List: Jimas Veltraa Memorial Edition, an in-universe news report located at the end of Knights of the Old Republic 13, a comic written by John Jackson Miller and released in 2007.
