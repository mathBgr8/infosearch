Popular Automaton
641

Popular Automaton was a successful holomag that reviewed droid models. It had an annual ranking of the droid industry, decided by subscribers' votes; in one such article, TT-8L droids were named the "Most Annoying" models on the market. *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Publications and journals
