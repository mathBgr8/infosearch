A Force-sensitive male, Hyris-Well Madorin was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. As conflicts, disputes, and a general crisis threatened the safety of the Republic, Jedi resources were spread thin as many Jedi Knights were called out into the galaxy to negotiate and discuss security. The Jedi, which included Madorin, eventually developed unique starfighters, created specifically for use by pilots. In 22 BBY, as the schismatic conflict went on, he was stationed at the Coruscant Jedi Temple when the Jedi Council assigned him to travel to a starfighter base at Phelar Port on the planet Eriadu. From the Eriadu port, Jedi Master Hyris-Well Madorin met with Brigadier Gideon Tarkin to discuss the movement of the Delta-7 Jedi interceptors and the increased security of the vital Rimma Trade Route. He stated to Madorin that his planet remained loyal to the Republic, as well as the Seswenna sector.
