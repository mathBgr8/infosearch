Mujmai Iinan
1245

Mujmai Iinan was a member of the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. A direct subordinate of Warmaster Tsavong Lah, he told the Warmaster that Kubindi could be taken with half the number of ships originally expected to conquer the planet. While the Battle of Kubindi was a success, Jedi Master Kyp Durron was able to evacuate a larger number of Kubaz from the planet then originally expected.
