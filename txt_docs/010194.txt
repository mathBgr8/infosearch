|subscribers= |pages=64 |isbn= |issn=1081-1362 |genre= |issue=13 |series=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine |next=last }} Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 13 is the thirteenth and final issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine before it was converted into Star Wars Galaxy Collector. Issue 13 was published by Topps in October 1997. The issue's theme focused on Jedi Knights, particularly those of the Old Republic. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 13, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with a Topps 1997 Topps Star Wars Vehicles promotional trading card and a Star Wars UK Foil Pack containing five trading cards.
