|continent= |location= |climate= |interest="Downside" |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Fabrillan was the capital city of the planet Malthor. It was a hotbed of activity during the Galactic Civil War. Fabrillan also had a large population of Reigats. It had a slum area, known as "Downside", where the Reigat Bakku lived.
