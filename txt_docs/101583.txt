|location=Oplovis sector |commander=*Unidentified Imperial admiral *Admiral Gaen Drommel |officers= |crew= |flagship=Guardian |capital= |starfighters= |ground= |naval= |campaigns=Galactic Civil War |battles=*Battle in the Yuvern system *Battle of the Generis system |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} The Oplovis Sector Fleet, also known simply as Oplovis Fleet, was an Imperial Sector Fleet, assigned to the defense of Oplovis sector during the Galactic Civil War. The fleet was stationed at Harrod's Planet. The Oplovis Fleet fought during the Battle in the Yuvern system while assigned to the Yuvern system, where they sustained damage and were forced to retreat. Following the battle, its admiral was removed and a new admiral was personally appointed by Darth Vader. Eight months later, the fleet was ambushed and defeated by a Alliance to Restore the Republic fleet in the Generis system. *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, Second Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial Sector Fleets
