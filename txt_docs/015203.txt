Gluss'sa'Nikto
2432

Gluss'sa'Nikto, or Pale Nikto, were a Nikto subspecies. They came from the Gluss'elta Islands of Kintan, causing them to be natural sailors. They had white-gray skin, eye-horns like the Kadas'sa'Nikto, and fins similar to those of the Esral'sa'Nikto, but smaller.
