StarAnvil heavy blaster rifle
1898

The StarAnvil was designed as the ultimate blaster rifle. It took the scope technology of a sporting blaster and combined it with the power of a long blaster to create a nasty and powerful heavy blaster rifle. It was made on the factory-moon of Cuthbern. The factory was annexed by the Empire and was later destroyed by a Rebel attack.
