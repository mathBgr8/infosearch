Financial Appropriations Committee
1662

The Financial Appropriations Committee of the Galactic Senate supervised, among other things, monetary grants issued by the Galactic Republic to various organizations and charities. It had a reputation for being stiff-necked. Orn Free Taa served on the Committee as of 33 BBY.
