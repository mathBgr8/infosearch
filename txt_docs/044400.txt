|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=247 |isbn=1593072864 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series=Star Wars Tales |issues=4 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Tales Volume 5 is the fifth trade paperback collection of the Star Wars Tales comic book series. Volume 5 collects Star Wars Tales issues 17-20. It was released on February 23, 2005. Star Wars Tales Volume 5 features an astonishing variety of stories featuring Boba Fett, Han Solo, Luke Skywalker, Darth Maul, and more! This substantial volume includes a Chewbacca story gorgeously illustrated by Cary Nord (Conan), Haden Blackman's unbelievable Han Solo tale, Scott Kurtz's parody of The Breakfast Club, and the "all-indie issue"-featuring many of the best independent comics creators in the business!
