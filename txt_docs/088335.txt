|wingspan=10 meters |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Orellon II |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The crier was a type of avian predator native to Orellon II. It had a wingspan of 10 meters, short feathers, and sharp talons. Criers hunted in groups on the open plain, using their pointy beaks to impale their prey. They dwelt in nests on the peaks of the mountain of Orellon and were able to cling to the mountain sides using their talons. They were also able to fly noiselessly and execute stunning aerial acrobatics.
