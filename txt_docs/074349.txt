Centax-class heavy frigate
2083

Centax-class heavy frigates were frigates used by the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. The Centax-class was armed with ten batteries of laser cannons and at least one vessel, the Ranger, was also furnished with two batteries of MG1-A proton torpedo launchers. This class of starship was known to be exceptionally fast considering its size.
