Short-term memory enhancement
2870

Short-term memory enhancement was a Force ability that allowed an individual to 'replay' a recent memory with hyperaccuracy, recalling a particular detail that was previously observed but not consciously remembered. Luke Skywalker was taught this power by Jedi Master Yoda. The ancient Jedi-turned Sith Lord Freedon Nadd also possessed this ability.
