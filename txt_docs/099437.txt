Sky mine
869

Sky-mines were homing weapons used by the Judicial Forces in 29 BBY, deployed from Sky-mine delivery ships. They were shaped like flattened spheres, with quick maneuverability and tracking abilities and high explosive power. They resembled orange blossoms when being deployed. *Rogue Planet *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Space mines
