Galliot
2277

The Galliot was a Corellian Engineering Corporation CR92a Assassin-class corvette operated by the Ripoblus. Along with its sister ship, the Selander, the Galliot attacked the Dimok XQ1 Platform Science Station Youst, during a battle in the Sepan Civil War. A warhead attack, carried out by Maarek Stele in a TIE/sa bomber, in defense of the Dimok platform, drove off the Ripoblus reprisal. Imperial Admiral Harkov's flagship, the Victory I-class Star Destroyer Protector, was able to re-establish order around the station.
