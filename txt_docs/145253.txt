Ministry of Intelligence (Five Worlds)
895

The Ministry of Intelligence was responsible for civilian management of all intelligence in the Five Worlds, which presumably gave it at least partial oversight of CorSec Intelligence. It was headed by the Minister of Intelligence, who was a member of the High Cabinet. Gavele Lemora was Minister during the Second Galactic Civil War. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Exile Intelligence Category:Intelligence
