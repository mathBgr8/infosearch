Executive Level
647

The Executive Level was a level of the first Death Star. It contained exercise suites with Heavy Gravity Rooms. Conan Antonio Motti worked out in one Heavy Gravity Room where he would set it at 3Gs. Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin's quarters were located on the Executive Level where he boasted hot water showers versus conventional sonic showers. *Death Star Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station decks
