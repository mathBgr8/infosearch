Transport Ship (Rebel Alliance)
2169

Transport Ships were aquatic repulsorcrafts used by the Rebel Alliance and later the New Republic to transport troops or personnel across bodies of water. In 0 ABY, they were used to transport Echuu Shen-Jon, Leia Organa, C-3PO, and R2-D2 to see the Bothan spy, Utric Sandov on the Krantian island of Le'Roche. Another one was used years later to carry R2-D5 labor droids during the Battle of New Geddes.
