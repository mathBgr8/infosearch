Ajax Wennel
1668

}} Sir Ajax Wennel was a Knight of House Cadriaan of the Tapani sector. He was a dynamic noble and a saber rake with an anti-Galactic Empire attitude. Although not a supporter of the Rebel Alliance, especially with JAN activities within the sector, Ajax saw the behavior of the Empire as a betrayal of honor.
