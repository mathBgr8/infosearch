|illustrator=Joe Corroney |editor= |publisher= |release date=July 24, 2001 |pages= |published in=Star Wars Gamer 5 |reprinted in= }} "The Emperor's Pawns" was an article appearing in Star Wars Gamer 5, written by Abel G. Peña and Pablo Hidalgo, under the name Juan Schwartz, and illustrated by Joe Corroney. The article provided background information on the Emperor's Hands who served Emperor Palpatine, such as Sarcev Quest and Jeng Droga. It was Peña's first published Star Wars article. A number of adventure seeds were included with the article that contained elements that have worked their way into Legends since the original release by Abel G.
