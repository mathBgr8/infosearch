|wingspan=2.4&ndash;3.2 meters |mass=20 kilograms |skincolor= |haircolor=Gray-white |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Toola |habitat= |diet=Mastmot |language= }} Snow demons were predatory creatures native to the planet Toola. They hunted mastmots and were in turn hunted by Whiphids. They had white claws, large teeth, hairy wings, and long purple tongues. Snow demons flew via a series of tubular bladders that were filled with methane gas to provide lift.
