Warden 10-24 security droid
1974

The Warden 10-24 security droid was an early model of security droid manufactured by Ulban Arms. It was marketed as a gladiator droid despite its manufacturers knowing it would be used for security, of which these droids were were notable for their efficiency. Their fingers were gun barrels and their chest plates would open up to reveal some of the most powerful laser cannons to be mounted on, or in, a Human sized droid. A unit of Warden 10-24s, known as the "Red Terror" was used as the only form of security on Telti, a major droid manufacturing moon.
