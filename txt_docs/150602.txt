Nihun River
680

Nihun River was a river on the planet Brosi in the Corporate Sector. It flowed into Nomers Ocean through Shoengen Delta. Nihun River also crossed Delta 1, Delta 2 and Forest Valley, going near Nihun Valley.Alliance Intelligence Reports Nihun River was within the sphere of influence of the Brosin Underground around 2 ABY. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Brosi locations Category:Rivers
