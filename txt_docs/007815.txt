Kendalina
2366

*Galactic Empire |masters= |apprentices= }} Kendalina was a Human female Jedi Princess who served as a nurse in an Imperial insane asylum on Kessel in 7 BBY. The Jedi Princess Kendalina fell in love with an inmate named Triclops on Kessel and conceived a child with him. After that she was forced to work as a nurse in an Imperial insane asylum on Kessel. When Kendalina was killed by the Galactic Empire, the child, named Ken, was hidden in the Lost City of the Jedi.
