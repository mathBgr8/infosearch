Curator of Records
1584

|datereestablished= |dateabolished= |government= |organization= |type= |term= |powers= |duties=Maintain the records of the Jedi Order's membership }} The Curator of Records was responsible for overseeing the combined records of the Jedi Order, serving at the Coruscant Jedi Temple. During the final decades of the Galactic Republic, the final Curator served throughout the Clone Wars and managed to escape the Temple when Emperor Palpatine ordered the Grand Army of the Republic to destroy the Jedi. Fleeing to the planet Toola, the Curator hid in a cave with the remainder of his records until he was finally hunted down and killed by Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Vader. *The Courtship of Princess Leia Category:Jedi ranks
