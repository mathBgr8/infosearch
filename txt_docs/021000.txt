|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "High Alert!" is a roleplaying mini-adventure published by Wizards of the Coast. It was written by J.D. Wiker and published on January 23, 2003. It is only available on the Wizards of the Coast website. High Alert! is a Star Wars Roleplaying Game mini-adventure for four player characters of any level, and it is usable in any era. Really more of a sub-adventure meant to be played as part of a larger adventure, High Alert! explores what happens when something goes wrong with a covert mission. The heroes have to use their wits and cunning to escape an enemy-controlled starport without attracting attention.
