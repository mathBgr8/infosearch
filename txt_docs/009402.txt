Munto
785

Munto was a large city on Commenor located near Kliffen. It was built primarily in the midst of a valley with houses built on the terraces; the city played host to moderate airspeeder and landspeeder traffic. It was home to the Annual Mid-Rim Domesticated Sub-Sentient Show and was near the Xenovet site. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge Category:Cities on Commenor
