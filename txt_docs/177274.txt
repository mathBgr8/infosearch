In 1 ABY, attempted to discredit Eugene Talmont by providing an ISB officer with details on his operations. She was killed by an Imperial spacer who was sent by Talmont to eliminate her. Nwilla Thar'lyat appeared as a non-player character in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
