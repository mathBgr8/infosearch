Imperial Meats and Produce
1356

|role=Foodstuff manufacturer |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Imperial Meats and Produce formerly Republic Meats and Produce, was a small business located in the clouds of Tyed Kant. It was initially funded by grants from the Galactic Republic. When the Galactic Empire became the new galactic government, the company's name was changed. Imperial Meats and Produce was one of the companies that Rebel privateers were commissioned to raid and ransack from. *Pirates & Privateers *The Far Orbit Project *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Agricultural companies Category:Foodstuff manufacturers
