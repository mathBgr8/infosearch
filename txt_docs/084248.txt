Jasod Revoc
1570

Jasod Revoc was a famous HoloNet actor who was very popular during the Clone Wars. In 20 BBY, he was given top billing by HoloNet Entertainment at shows set up to entertain Republic troops and personnel, due to the fact that he was younger than fellow HoloNet star Epoh Trebor.
