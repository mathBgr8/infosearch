Khorma
1150

thumb|right|250px|Khorma, mistakenly spelled Khoma on a map of Tof Soren Khorma was the largest of the three neighborhoods in Tof Soren on Ord Vaxal. Khorma peacekeepers were better equipped than other neighborhood peacekeepers and pinned a small black disk to their chests as a way to identify each other. Khorma was the location of one of Ord Vaxal's most famous weapon emporiums. The Khorma Weapon Market primarily carried slugthrowers built by artisans on Ord Vaxal with a very limited number of blaster pistols which were left over from the Ord Vaxal jailbreak twenty years before the Battle of Yavin. Khorma is mistakenly listed as 'Khoma' on the Tof Soren map, but is correctly referred to as 'Khorma' throughout the article. Category:Ord Vaxal locations
