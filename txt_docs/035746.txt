Preserve Our Wild Endangered Resources Party
830

The POWER Party, short for Preserve Our Wild Endangered Resources Party, was a political organization on the planet Telos IV. Created in 44 BBY, it was founded, led, and consisted solely of Thani resident, Andra. It opposed the Telosian government's plan to give the UniFy Corporation control over Telos's National parks and Sacred places, for which the organization was outlawed. *Jedi Apprentice: The Day of Reckoning *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Conservation groups Category:Organizations of the Telos system Category:Political factions
