University of Kalla
1809

The University of Kalla on Kalla VII was a prestigious college in the Corporate Sector run by the Authority. It was a place of higher education for the children of influential Authority families, primarily. The institution was rooted in technical education, commerce, and administration, with minimal stress on the humanities. Rekkon was a teacher at the University.
