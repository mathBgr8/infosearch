Grree L'Tharr
841

Grree L'Tharr was a charming and infamous felinoid Jediboh Opera actress in the Tantrellius Theatre Groupe. When a group of Rebels came to the Two Jedi Men From Baronis play looking for a Jedi Knight, they met her backstage. She quickly became fixated on a male member of the group and soon emitted pheromones as all members of her race could do to attract him to her.
