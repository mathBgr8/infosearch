Kot Murno
1452

Kot Murno was an officer of the Peace Brigade force commanded by Captain Imsatad which attacked Yavin 4 in 26 ABY. Claiming to hold the rank of Lieutenant, he commanded the group of five or six starships sent down to attack the Jedi academy directly. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Peace Brigaders
