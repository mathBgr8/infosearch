Scalphunter rifle
1651

The Merr-Sonn Scalphunter rifle was an experimental blaster designed and created by the Trade Federation. It was fully automatic with collapsible stock, cortosis alloy barrel and long-range spectrum microscope. A number of the weapons were stolen by the pirate Nym before the Invasion of Naboo. In The Bounty Hunter Code, Boba Fett mentioned that, along with the EE-3 Carbine rifle and Firestar 10-k, the scalphunter rifle had a good balance of firepower, accuracy and weight. *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Blaster rifles Category:Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. products
