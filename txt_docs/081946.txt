Imperial Navy Intelligence
1819

}} Imperial Navy Intelligence, also called Fleet Intelligence or Naval Intelligence, was the military intelligence branch of the Imperial Starfleet. Spurch Goa once worked as an investigator for it. Navy Intelligence also had interrogators, although they were implied to be less ruthless than their counterparts in Imperial Intelligence.
