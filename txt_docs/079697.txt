Saber-class assault tank
2249

The Saber-class assault tank line was a series of medium, ground-based combat fighter tanks produced by Rothana Heavy Engineering during the Clone Wars and the Galactic Civil War. The Saber-class was first introduced with the TX-130S model early in the Clone Wars. These repulsorlift fighter tanks were piloted by clone troopers and Jedi alike, most notably during the Dark Reaper crisis. During the Imperial era, Rothana Heavy Engineering produced the TX-130T and 2-M variants. The SC2-M Repulsor Tank was an scout variant of the 2-M.
