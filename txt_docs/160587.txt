Wrist vac
690

A wrist vac was a weapon which was worn on the user's wrist. When triggered, the wrist vac released a number of small, deadly blades into a targets vital areas. Han Solo once ran into a Twi'lek bounty hunter who had one. The Wrist Vac bears a striking similarity to the Vac Attack Mk-127, though the book does not implicitly name a model. *The Hutt Gambit *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy Category:Flechette launchers
