Demolinn
1498

**New Republic Defense Fleet |masters= |apprentices= }} Demolinn was part of the security detachment aboard the New Republic Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Liberator when it was downed over Coruscant. General Wedge Antilles assigned Demolinn and Mr. Greybird to escort New Republic journalist Rivoche Tarkin while she explored the ruined cityscape and searched for survivors of the Imperial reconquest of the planet.
