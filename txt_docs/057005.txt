Exiles of the Force
761

Exiles of the Force was a four-part comic miniseries proposed by Jim Baikie to Dark Horse Comics in 1996 that would have Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa Solo dealing with a new Force-related threat. It was apparently going to be a prequel to Heart of the Jedi and Legacy of Doom. Category:Dark Horse Comics comic miniseries
