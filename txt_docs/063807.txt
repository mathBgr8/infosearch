Shadow Squadron (TIE Defender)
1169

Shadow was a TIE Defender squadron associated with Imperial Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's TIE Experimental Project. Fighters from Shadow Squadron participated in both the defense of Bretie Facility and research station Obsidian. However, on each occasion their intervention proved fruitless. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Imperial starfighter squadrons
