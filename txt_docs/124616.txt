It was constructed to ensure that the main facility on Kothlis II was well-defended from attacks by the Galactic Empire. During the Battle of Kothlis, the Rebel Alliance facilities were attacked by the Executor and the Avenger, which were sent by the Galactic Empire to recover a stolen computer that contained the Death Star II plans. Sentinel was the closer target and was bombarded by TIE/sa bombers from the Avenger.
