Queen's Honor Guard
1123

The Queen's Honor Guard was the name given to the group of pilots who escorted the Queen of Naboo offworld. The Honor Guard consisted of members of the Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps division of the Royal Naboo Security Forces. *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace Scrapbook Category:Royal Naboo Security Forces
