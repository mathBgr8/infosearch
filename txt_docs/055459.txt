Corellian noale
1456

Corellian noale was a drink served at Panky's and enjoyed by Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi. In 27 BBY, Kenobi, and Padawans Lorana Jinzler and Anakin Skywalker, Kenobi's Padawan, entered Panky's, there, Kenobi ordered and drank a Corellian noale, which he enjoyed. *Outbound Flight *Outbound Flight abridged audiobook Category:Beverages Noale
