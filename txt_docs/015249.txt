Elerion
3072

|imports= |exports= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Elerion was a notorious gambling planet located in the Outer Rim Territories near Phoebus. During the Cold War between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire, the casino of Elerion collected evidence that one of its richest gamblers was cheating. The establishment consequently hired a slicer to hack the cheater's bank account and transfer the undeserved credits discreetly back. Leia Organa traveled to Elerion on a secret mission to recover stolen Rebel intel. The Lucky Star casino was located in the planet's capital, Kushal Vogh. The Duros M'a Jong once owned a casino on this planet. The planet originally appeared in ambiguously canon Tales story "Lucky Stars."
