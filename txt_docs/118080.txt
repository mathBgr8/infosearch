Durge's pilot droid
1333

Durge's pilot droid flew Durge's ship during the Gen'Dai's final year of life. The droid somewhat resembled Durge in appearance and temperament. The droid was decapitated by Obi-Wan Kenobi, although its head remained functional. Obi-Wan captured the head and used it to track down Dooku and Asajj Ventress to Boz Pity. *Obsession 3 Category:Droids of the Confederacy of Independent Systems Category:Pilot and navigation droids
