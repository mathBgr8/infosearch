Ragoon gave off a blue glow from space and was surrounded by several stars. A younger Qui-Gon Jinn went to Ragoon VI with his friend Tahl. Years later, just after the Stark Hyperspace War, Jinn and his padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi were accompanied to Ragoon VI by Master Tholme's padawan, Quinlan Vos, to complete a survival-training mission. The boys—nearly drowned by a vortex of churning waters—were rescued by Jinn, who called upon the Force to lift them from the whirlpool. thumb|left| Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi on Ragoon VIIn 27 BBY, Jedi pilot Wren Honoran took Kenobi and his padawan Anakin Skywalker to the planet for a tracking exercise (since their last mission to Radnor, Kenobi had grown a beard). Here they met the brother-and-sister bounty hunter team Floria and Dane. Four other bounty hunters were there also—Teleq, Mol Arcasite, Hunti Pereg, and an unknown hunter. It was here that Kenobi and Skywalker first met Void in the Force Granta Omega, with whom they would tangle in the years to come.
