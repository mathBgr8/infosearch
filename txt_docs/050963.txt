T'surrese
486

T'surrese was the native language of the T'surr species from T'surr. Most male T'surrs were fluent in both T'surrese and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. Female T'surrs were usually only fluent in T'surrese. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
