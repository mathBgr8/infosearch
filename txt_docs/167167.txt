Argazdan riot buster
1225

The Argazdan Riot Buster was a powerful type of sonic rifle that was used by the Argazdan during their rule over the Lorrdians as a part of the Kanz Disorders; at this time in history, non-lethal technology was a profitable commodity. This gun knocked its targets over and stunned them; it had a range of twenty-eight meters. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Sonic rifles
