Ryk Osentay
1525

Ryk Osentay was a male reporter for "Eye on Cularin," a Cularin Central Broadcasting program. Ryk acquired a nasty virus when he was beat up by a radiation-scarred Ugnaught on the streets of Tolea Biqua on Genarius. When he first returned to work after his ordeal, he appeared on the program with a shaved head, and puffy face as a result of surgery.
