Vantos Coll
1335

Vantos Coll was a highly obese member of the Supreme Tribunal, in league with Crispin Hoedaack and Darth Vader as they plotted to take control of the New Galactic Empire. He cautioned Hoedaack, however, noting that the plan depended on the success in subduing the Aquilaean system.
