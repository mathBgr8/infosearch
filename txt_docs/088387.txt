Deegan
2928

Deegan was a male lieutenant in the New Republic from the planet Corellia. In 6 ABY, while Wedge Antilles was reforming Rogue Squadron, an elite fighter group, Deegan was chosen as a potential member. However, Antilles felt that having both himself and Corran Horn, both of whom were Corellians, on the squadron was not politically wise and could result in Corellia being overrepresented in the squadron. In place of Deegan, Gavin Darklighter, a farm boy from the planet Tatooine was selected to join. In 11 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion Deegan served underneath General Antilles as part of the reconstruction efforts on Coruscant. When the construction droid Antilles' teams were supervising discovered a shielded vault within a building it was demolishing, Antilles decided to shut the droid down to inspect it, despite the fact the droid would take three days to restart.
