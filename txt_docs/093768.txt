Scarpyen
826

The massive predatory scarpyen were arthropods native to the planet of Kubindi. They were black in appearance with a hard, chitinous shell that allowed them to blend well into the darkness of their homeworld's forests. They would stun or disable prey with their segmented tails, and, once the prey was vulnerable, the scarpyen would then use its claws to grasp the food and inject a powerful venom into it, using its dagger-like stinger. *Star Wars Missions 6: The Search for Grubba the Hutt Category:Arthropods Category:Creatures of Kubindi Category:Jungle and forest creatures Category:Predatory creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
