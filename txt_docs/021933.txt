|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYDate established in narrative text. |series=Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand |issue=3 }} Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 3 was the third issue in the Star Wars: Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand series of comics. It was released on October 7, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics. In the wake of the Emperor's death at the hands of Luke Skywalker and Darth Vader, Mara Jade has been captured! Since her captor is one of only a handful who knows she is the Emperor's Hand, Mara knows she must escape quickly&mdash;or die.
