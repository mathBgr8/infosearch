Thor (Strike-class)
2331

The Thor was a Strike-class medium cruiser in the service of the Rebel Alliance. The ship was part of a convoy along with the Zeus and the Odysseus. The convoy was attacked by Assault Gunboats and TIE Avengers, and the starships had to rely on X-wings from Rogue and Blue squadrons to give the starships enough time to escape into hyperspace.
