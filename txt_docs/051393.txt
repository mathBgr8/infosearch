Three Rings University of Social Sciences
499

The Three Rings University of Social Sciences was an institution where the sentientologist Obo Rin claimed to have studied under the renowned Ditwar Logas. According to Imperial Intelligence's Major Herrit, Three Rings was a compu-order diploma house rather than a genuinely accredited institution of higher learning. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races Category:Universities
