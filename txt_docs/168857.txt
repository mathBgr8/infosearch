Lutdze
897

The Lutdze was an MC80 Star Cruiser belonging to the Rebel Alliance. The Lutdze was part of a Rebel fleet mobilizing at the Delfii system prior to the Battle of Endor. In Star Wars: TIE Fighter the ships cargo says 'Ackbar'.
