At some point, TK-104 was present in a location with an orange sky, alongside the Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Vader and an airborne YT-1300 light freighter, with both being reflected in the visor of TK-104's helmet. A loyal trooper, TK-104 was one of many stormtroopers who tried to stand out in service to Darth Vader in the hopes of receiving the Sith Lord's notice and favor. TK-104 was created by a card for the Scum and Villainy expansion set of Decipher's 2001 Jedi Knights Trading Card Game.
