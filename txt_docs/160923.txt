|type=Paperback |pages=168 |dimensions=10.3 &times; 8.9 &times; 0.4 inches |weight=15.7 ounces |isbn=0761541659 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Prima's Official Strategy Guide was a strategy guide published by Prima Games for Star Wars: The Clone Wars, covering the Xbox, Gamecube, and Playstation 2 consoles. It includes a poster of a TX-130 Saber-class fighter tank. The Strategy Guide contains: *Vital strategies and walkthroughs for all single and multiplayer maps *Comprehensive details on how to master every multiplayer game type *Extensive details on all characters, enemies, and craft, including weapons and takedown tactics *Detailed maps for all levels, complete with waypoints, items, and enemy locations *Comprehensive strategies for piloting all vehicles *Giant technical poster of a Republic fighter tank *A team interview The Clone Wars: Prima's Official Strategy Guide
