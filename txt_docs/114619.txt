Steve Chadwick
5858

Steve Chadwick was a production assistant for the two Nelvana shows, Ewoks and Star Wars Droids. He joined Nelvana as a production supervisor for Rolie Polie Olie, Santa Claus Bros, Pecola, Donkey Kong Country, Clone High, Beetlejuice, Madballs, Flying Rhino Junior High, Ned's Newt, Care Bears, Babar, Stickin' Around, The Adventures of Tintin, Dog City, Fievel's American Tails, Little Bear, Eek!
