AV-1C Combat Armor
2489

AV-1C Combat Armor was a type of fully-enclosed armored power suit produced by GTU. The AV-1C, a variant on the standard AV-1 power armor, was designed for front-line infantry units.The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology The AV-1c augmented the wearer's strength, and was equipped with a comprehensive weapon integration and targeting system&mdash;allowing the user to link two weapons to the suit's onboard battle computer. Additionally, the power suit was fitted with repulsorlifts for greater agility and the power of flight. The system provided a full life support system capable of 24 hours of operation, with an internal air supply and extreme environmental conditions including vacuum. An internal comlink gave the wearer the ability to send and receive encrypted messages at a range of up to 50 kilometers. An integrated Powerlamp offered a powerful floodlight that could create a beam of light up to 100 meters long and 50 meters wide.
