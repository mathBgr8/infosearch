Lido Shan
1707

Lido Shan was a female humanoid technician who worked for the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. In 21 BBY, she was stationed on Coruscant for most of the war, but served in the Battle of Ord Cestus. She had short, black hair. Instead of blinking, her eyes would briefly film over with a yellow mucus.
