Zantay Hills
956

The Zantay Hills were a stretch of hills on Ord Cestus. They were east of the Kibo Geothermal Station and Kibo Lake, 180 kilometers east of the Dashta Mountains, and to the south of Yal-Noy. They were near the Zantay Caves. A river fed by Yal-Noy's snowmelt ran through the Hills, which allowed for more plants to grow in the area than most of Cestus' sparse landscape.
