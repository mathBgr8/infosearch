|last date=August 10, [ |media type=Limited Collector's Edition Paperback |timeline= |num books=3 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure is a trilogy of limited collector's edition paperback novels written by Christopher Golden and published by Bantam Skylark in 1998. The three young reader books follow the events occurring in the Original trilogy, but feature a number of points in which the reader decides which page to turn to in order to continue the story, allowing readers to choose the outcome of events. They are written from the second person point of view with the reader taking on the role of Luke Skywalker's best friend and becoming part of his journey to join the Rebel Alliance, become a Jedi Knight, and save the galaxy from the Galactic Empire. Join Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, and Han Solo to fight against the evil Galactic Empire—only this time you control the twists, turns, and outcomes of the most exciting adventure in the galaxy. Will you help lead the Rebellion to victory against the Empire? Or side with Darth Vader and betray your friends? The fate of the galaxy is in your hands—May the Force be with you. *Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: A New Hope *Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: The Empire Strikes Back *Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: Return of the Jedi
