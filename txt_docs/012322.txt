Adversary (Star Destroyer)
1553

The Adversary was a Star Destroyer in service to the Imperial Navy, operational during the Galactic Civil War. Most of its operational history is unknown, save for its presence at the drydock station at Heriston at one point during the time between the Battle of Yavin and the Battle of Hoth, where it underwent routine maintenance and repairs. *Rebellion Era Sourcebook Category:Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
