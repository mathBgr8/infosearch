Griv
1815

Griv was a Rodian smuggler who worked for Mazzic. He was the pilot of Raptor and one of Mazzic's guards. Along with Amber, Griv was assigned to attack Niles Ferrier as he escaped from Hijarna. When Griv later joined Talon Karrde's organization, he became a gunner aboard Wild Karrde. Griv was a former law enforcement officer on a Rodian colony world.
