C-6 protocol droid
546

The C-6 protocol droid was a household droid programmed with many functions and different forms of communication. It was manufactured by Cybot Galactica on the factory moon of Telti in the time of the New Republic. It was the predecessor of the C-7 protocol droid and the successor of the C-5 protocol droid *The New Rebellion Category:C-series protocol droid models
