Freedom of Sullust
875

Freedom of Sullust was a CR90 corvette of the New Republic Defense Fleet. In 11 ABY, Freedom of Sullust was part of a task force sent to the Kesselian space surrounding the Maw to engage a pirate raid on a spice transport. The Invid forces under the command of Shala the Hutt began taking losses, but were saved by the timely arrival of Leonia Tavira and Invidious.
