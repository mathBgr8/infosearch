It was the home of the Karnak Alphans. Because the Karnak Alphans were very fond of strange and exotic animals; the planet was home to a number of large alternate environment zoos. In 23 ABY Karnak Alpha was a world of strategic importance due to its proximity to the Deep Core and by extension a number of Imperial strongholds.
