Rian-327 airspeeder
2384

The Rian-327 airspeeder, sometimes referred to as a classic speeder, was an aerodynamic, classic-style airspeeder that saw use during the final years of the Galactic Republic on worlds such as Coruscant. Padmé Amidala owned a Rian-327 airspeeder when she served as Senator of Naboo during the Clone Wars. The silver-and-red speeder featured tractor field seats, a rear anti-tracking device and a secret compartment ideal for hiding a blaster pistol. The speeder's engine was modified by her husband, Anakin Skywalker. Senator Amidala's personal airspeeder was often piloted by Captain Typho.
