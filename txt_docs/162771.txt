Koved City
953

Koved City was one of the main cities on Siluria III. Located half an hour from the planet's main spaceport, Koved City was home to Imperial Governor Quannith's mansion. Following the Imperial occupation of the planet, many houses on the edge of the city were abandoned by their owners. However, the mansion Dovecote, owned by Kaiya Adrimetrum, remained in use, primarily as a base for Adrimetrum's resistance fighters. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Cities Category:Galactic Empire cities
