Thrella well
1307

Thrella wells were age-old vertical shafts created by the Thrella on Mimban. Many years after the Thrella's extinction, Coway tribes connected a number of their own tunnels to the Thrella wells in order to access the surface of the planet from their subterranean civilization. *Splinter of the Mind's Eye *Splinter of the Mind's Eye 3 *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Buildings Category:Mimban locations
