Astor
1553

Astor was a male Human rebel against the Imperial-aligned government of the planet Ahakista. Although he was a trusted lieutenant to Dunlan, Astor betrayed him and disclosed the location of one of the rebels' safehouses to Darth Vader in exchange for money. Shortly thereafter, stormtroopers under Vader's command raided the safe house when Dunlan was present.
