Pelagia Embassy
831

The Pelagia Embassy on Procopia, in the Tapani sector, was located on Estalle Island. Despite the Mecetti sponsored purge of House Pelagia they retained the possession of their large embassy building on Estalle Island. It had the largest gardens than any of the other House embassies. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Embassies Category:Procopia locations
