Archais
2391

Archais was a planet in the Interior Region of the Hapes Cluster. Sometime before 2 ABY, Imperial lieutenant colonel Freja Covell and the Assassin's Guild fought three Rebel SpecForces units in the oriental seas of Archais during the battle of Archais.Alliance Intelligence Reports It was believed to be the site of mythical mountain peaks that were very high.
