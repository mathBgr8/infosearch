Kibla greens
1568

200px|thumb|Kibla greens Kibla greens were nutritious vegetables regarded as flavorful by many in the galaxy. They could be found on the planet Dantooine. Klaus Vandangante found kibla greens to be rather unpalatable as a seven-year-old child, and he reprogrammed the droid nanny to discard them in a vase in the family living room, along with flangth and stewed gwouch.Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Kibla greens were created for an offhand mention in the entry on Klaus Vandangante in the Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook, authored by Michael Allen Horne and published in November 1993 by West End Games.
