Kaminoan droid
2164

The Kaminoan droid was a model of labor droid used by the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. The Kaminoan droids resembled the waitress droid FLO, a WA-7 Service Unit. thumb|right|A Kaminoan droid Forager. This model utilized a long pole for foraging plants. This model utilized a two-winged pole in front that acted as a scanner to detect traces of carbon in the ground or nearby. This model utilized a mobile scanner unit in front of it to detect any nova or ore deposits for collection. This model possessed a fishing pole for the purpose of catching aquatic life near the shore. thumb|right|A Kaminoan droid builder/repairer. This model had its arms modified to accompany tools for the purpose of construction and/or repairing various buildings owned by the GAR. This model had a basket grafted onto it for the purpose of harvesting farming plants. This model has a sensor on its right arm. That guided and herded animals towards it. This model has a two-pronged gun on its right arm that allowed it to kill animals that it was hunting for food for the GAR. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns Category:Droid models of the Galactic Republic Category:Specialized labor droid models
