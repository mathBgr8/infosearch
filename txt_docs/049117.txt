Dobo Brothers' Emporium
1243

The Dobo Brothers' Emporium was a store based on Telos IV's Citadel Station after the Jedi Civil War. It was divided in half, and customers could choose to buy items from Dendis, who was an honest merchant, or Samhan, who was a swindler and smuggler. The Duros brothers had a rivalry with each other and often competed for customers. During their stay on the station, the Jedi Exile and her companions may have purchased items from the emporium.
