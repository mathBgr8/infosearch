Constant
4514

Constant was a colony ship that, along with Mother Vima, served as a support starship for the colony ship Beneficent Tasia. In approximately 3900 BBY, the three ships departed the planet Grizmallt in an expedition to the dangerous southern quadrant of the galaxy. The ships carried refugees fleeing a brutal civil warNexus of Power who sought fortune and glory. The expedition, led by the scout Kwilaan,Secrets of Naboo was sponsored personally by Grizmallt's Queen Elsinoré den Tasia and was blessed by a famous Jedi Master. After losing contact with Beneficent Tasia, the inhabitants of Grizmallt assumed that the expedition had failed. However, much later, they learned that the damaged ships had landed on the planet Naboo in Wild Space. The Human colonists, including a member of the Korden family,Galaxy of Intrigue established themselves on Naboo and developed a thriving society after clashing with the planet's native Gungans. Constant was later mentioned in a Galactic Alliance historical document published by the Historical Council in 36 ABY. Constant was first mentioned in the DataBank entry for the Royal Naboo in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, a 2001 LucasArts video game.
