Anti-security blade
1770

Anti-security blades were sophisticated devices that could defeat fence fields and tune out security cameras and other security systems. They functioned by emitting intense harmonic interference waves that caused devices and systems to malfunction. When they were set on a higher level, anti-security blades could erase magnetic locks and allow the operator to gain access to nearly any door.Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary When set to the proper frequency they could also be used to jam comlinks. The bounty hunter Boba Fett kept anti-security blades in his shin pockets. Jango Fett was also known to have carried them. *Star Wars: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary Category:Security countermeasures
