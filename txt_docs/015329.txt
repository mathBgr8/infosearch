Caaraz
2012

Caaraz was a tide-locked, tectonically active planet in the Outer Rim. Its night-side was frozen with glaciers. Its day-side was scorching. In between was a thin, habitable region in which the Ranth race evolved. Both sides of the planet were tectonically active as well, allowing subterranean gases, lava, and steam to escape into the atmosphere. The Galactic Empire discovered the world while looking for Rebel bases, and later mined the planet.
