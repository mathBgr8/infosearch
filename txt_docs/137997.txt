Sutta Chwituskak
1772

Sutta Chwituskak (literally "Flung Spears" in Sith) was the proper name of the Bolt of Hatred, an ancient Sith technique conjured through magic. The Force user would create a radiant sphere of pure hatred which they could hurl at any target in their direct line of sight.
