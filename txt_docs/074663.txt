YT-5100 Shriek-class bomber
2852

The YT-5100 Shriek-class bomber was a Corellian craft used in the Second Galactic Civil War. They were shaped like smaller YT-1300 light freighters. Their armaments included frontal torpedo tubes, concussion missiles, a top-mounted quad laser cannon, and a bottom-mounted bomb/spotter droid drop pod. They had three engines and were painted black. Their pilots wore green and black/white suits. At the time of their first combat operation, the Shriek-class vessel was so new that Galactic Alliance sensors were unable to recognize it as anything other than a YT-series variant. As of 40 ABY, only ten late-model prototypes and several earlier models existed. The Galactic Alliance lacked intelligence on these bombers due to their novelty and the Corellians used that to their advantage.
