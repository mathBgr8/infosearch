|subscribers= |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=17 |series=Star Wars Official Poster Monthly }} Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 17 was published in February 1979 by Paradise Press. *"Escape Through Hyperspace," by John Chesterman: a vignette describing the Millennium Falcon escape from Tatooine. *"Near Encounter With an Alien Body", by Dr. M. F. Marten: an article on Voyager 1's recent encounter with Jupiter. *"Introducing the Intergalactic Inker...Russ Manning," by John May: an interview with the artist producing numerous Star Wars black-and-white newspaper comic strips (many of which were collected much later in Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures.) 17
