Bosph wanderer
580

Bosph wanderers were Bosph who traveled the galaxy in the pursuit of knowledge thousands of years prior to the decline of the Galactic Republic. Their legacy, the way of the traveler, was embraced by the few Bosph that remained during the rise of the Empire and the time of the Galactic Civil War. Category:Bosph culture
