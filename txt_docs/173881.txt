Star Wars Weekly 112 is the one hundred twelfth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 16, 1980. It included the original story "A Creature in the Stars!". *"A Creature in the Stars!" *"Heroes of the galaxy: Monark Starstalker Part 2: In pursuit of Kurt Hammer!" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 22: And once removed never" *"Tales of the Watcher: The secret of the hidden planet!"
