Ace (nickname)
1220

Ace was the nickname Han Solo gave to a Human contender in The Big Game. Ace was not a Corellian. He had long, brown hair, pale skin, and wore a silver bodysuit. Ace mocked Solo for missing him with a needle ray, but Solo was actually aiming for an explosive planetoid, which detonated and killed Ace. *Star Wars (1977) 22 Category:Humans Category:Males
