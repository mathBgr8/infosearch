Galladinium's Galactic Datalog of Fantastic Technology
1421

Galladinium's Galactic Datalog of Fantastic Technology, often referred to simply as Galladinium's Datalog, was a datalog purveying fine merchandise to an upscale clientele. Its 50th-anniversary issue was released during the Galactic Civil War. As many items in the datalog could be used for combat purposes, it was banned by Moff Shinda of Spirva Sector. As a result, a lucrative smuggling opportunity colloquially known as the "Spirva Run" developed to deliver items from Galladinium's Datalog into the sector.
