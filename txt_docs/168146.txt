The Bomerwrights of Sullest were a group which constructed laser swords. Luke Starkiller gave Han Solo a chrome handled sword made by them following their arrival at Yavin 4. *Adventures of the Starkiller, Episode I: The Star Wars Category:Arms manufacturing companies Category:Sullustan culture
