Excelsior
3208

Excelsior was an MC80 Star Cruiser used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. During the Galactic Civil War, Imperial reconnaissance patrols located the Excelsior, and the Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Blue Fox launched an attack against her with TIE/sa starfighters armed with proton torpedoes and concussion missiles. They also sent Alpha-class Xg-1 Star Wings and three CR90 corvettes to try to destroy the Excelsior. However, Alliance RZ-1 A-wing interceptors managed to hold off the attack long enough for the Excelsior to reach hyperspace.
