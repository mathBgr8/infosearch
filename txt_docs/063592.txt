Chidee Na Maak
2230

Chidee Na Maak was a male Duros mechanic, starshipwright, and proprietor of the StarForge Shipyard that he inherited after his parents had been killed in an accidental fuel explosion. He was a friend of Han Solo's and was always trying to acquire Chewbacca as an employee. According to Solo, Maak was the best at fixing YT-1210 light freighters. Chidee Na Maak started his career by working as an engineer on various starships. He returned to StarForge after his parents died and took over operating the shipyard. He managed to resist efforts to take over his business and proceeded to make the shipyard the most important business on StarForge Station. He was in a relationship with Kuuda De Nall. Chidee Na Maak was gregarious and outgoing.
