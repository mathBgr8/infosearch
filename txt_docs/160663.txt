Lady Greelanx
889

Lady Greelanx was the wife of Imperial Admiral Winstel Greelanx. She was disliked by the admiral, who had two mistresses. The two of them had two grown children, a son and a daughter and had a grandchild on the way in 4 BBY. She was widowed when Winstel died at the hands of Darth Vader. *The Hutt Gambit Category:Females Category:Humans
