The base of the sword was cut into jagged triangles, likely to allow the wielder to better damage their enemy in close quarter fighting. The blade itself was not very lengthy. The Mustafar bandit sword appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
