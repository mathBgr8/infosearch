|subgroup= |headquarters= |locations=Bright Jewel system |formed= |founded= |fragmented=1 ABY |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Genesis Red was a criminal organization led by a crime lord by the name of Genesis Red during the Galactic Civil War. Following the Battle of Yavin, Genesis Red were among the many criminal gangs that had infested Tansarii Point Station, a space station located above Ord Mantell, in the Bright Jewel system. Roaming the lower levels of the station, the Genesis Red were one of the major problems for the authorities of Tansarii Point Station. In 1 ABY, the criminals were planning to sabotage Tansarii Point Station. They managed to break through the outer hull of the station, creating a very dangerous situation for its inhabitants. Fortunately, spacers working for Rydel Delan, Tansarii Point's brawling master, discovered the plot. The spacers therefore headed to the lower level of Tansarii Point Station where they fought the saboteurs and prevented them to achieve their insane project. The Genesis Red were finally repelled when their leader and a large portion of their number were killed by a group of freelancer hired by the authorities. The Genesis Red appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
