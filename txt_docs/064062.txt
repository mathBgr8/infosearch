Omicron (transport group)
811

Omicron 1 & 2 were a pair of Rebel Alliance Beta-class ETR-3 escort transports colluding with the Airam pirates at a Goff Platform in the Goff system. Omicron 1 was crewed by regular Alliance personnel, while Omicron 2 carried officers. During the attack by the Imperial forces of Task Force Vengeance, the ETRs of Omicron group concentrated their counterattack on the Interdictor Compellor. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
