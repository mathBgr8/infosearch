Dooje Brolo
1048

Prefect Dooje Brolo was in command of the third worldship in the first invasion force of the Yuuzhan Vong War. He also commanded a thousand coralskippers. Brolo was killed as Jedi used shieldships to melt the Yuuzhan Vong base planet of Helska IV. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Yuuzhan Vong intendants
