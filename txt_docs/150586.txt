Tylerin Embankment
1345

Tylerin Embankment was a location near the eastern seas of the planet Archais During the Battle of Archais, Rebel lieutenant Akul Witig's unit (probably Midnight Squadron or Storm Squadron) was trapped in the Tylerin Enbankment when Rebel officer Mosara Thiirn surrender her ship and herself to the Imperial forces. Due to this, Witig's unit was on increased danger and they only survived thanks to the patchwork of fellow Rebel Shaparo and some maneuvers of Norrion Glidamir &mdash; but they still held a great grudge towards Thiirn, because due to her many good men died that day. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Planetary regions and territories
