It was the homeworld of the Zanibar species and the site of their sacrificial rituals. An Outer Rim planet in a system of the same name, Xo was located in the Spinward sector and possessed a breathable atmosphere and red-colored sky. The planet's surface featured rocky, mountainous terrain, and thick jungle, home to spiked, carnivorous plants, and reptilian creatures. Prior to its collapse in 25,200 BBY, Xo was a remote holding of the Rakatan Infinite Empire. The Sith pureblood Kyyrah, a warrior who was held captive by the Rakata in the Eternity Vault on Belsavis, was known as the "Jewel of Xo." Pre-Republic era Humans constructed an elevated temple in the planet's jungle. The site of a great battle, the temple remained unconquered, but was abandoned, and had fallen to ruin by the New Republic era. In 11 ABY,The Essential Chronology dates the events of Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood to 11 ABY. New Republic operative Mirith Sinn and her traitorous former subordinate Massimo were brought to Xo as captives of the Zanibar. Sinn was rescued from a Zanibar village's sacrificial ceremony by renegade Imperial Guard Kir Kanos.
