Blade of Sacrifice was a Yuuzhan Vong warship. Following the fall of Coruscant, Blade of Sacrifice was recalled to Coruscant to join the massing Yuuzhan Vong fleet preparing to assault the Galactic Alliance force at Mon Calamari. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Yuuzhan Vong starships
