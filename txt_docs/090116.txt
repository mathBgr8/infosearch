|published in=Star Wars Tales 15 |reprinted in= |pages=10 |timeline=c. 4-3 BBYFalling Star depicts Luke Skywalker before owning his first landspeeder, although Biggs Darklighter is shown piloting a Swoop. The New Essential Chronology dates Luke's birth year at 19 BBY and The Essential Guide to Characters dates Biggs birth year at 24 BBY. Falling Stars timeline is further approximated from The Lost City of Tatooine which internally dated Luke to be 16 years old when he acquired his first landspeeder. Lost City is internally dated to 3 BBY, relative to Luke's age and birth year. Hence, Falling Star is set no earlier than 4 BBY when Luke is around 15 years old and prior to Lost City, but before Biggs joins the Imperial Academy or the Rebel Alliance. |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Falling Star is a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 15 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4 and Star Wars: Luke Skywalker, Last Hope for the Galaxy. A young Luke Skywalker and Biggs Darklighter, eager to get off Tatooine for no matter how short a time, have purchased tickets to go to one of the planet's moons on a GR-75 transport. Taking off from Mos Eisley, they are at first ecstatic, but then a near-disaster occurs as the engines malfunction, and the transport starts plummeting back to the surface. Luke manages to fix the engines and is acclaimed a hero, but the damaged shuttle must go back to the surface. Dejected that his dream of spaceflight has been ruined, Luke returns home. According to a post by the writer, Jim Beard, on the old Dark Horse Comics message boards, the hooded figure that can be seen following Luke in several panels is Obi-Wan Kenobi.
