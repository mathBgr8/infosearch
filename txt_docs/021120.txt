It was the homeworld of the Human male Par Lankin, who was born in 50 BBY.Wanted by Cracken is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor. Since The New Essential Chronology dates that conflict to 4 ABY, the placement of the sourcebook therefore translates to 7 ABY, and as the former work establishes Par Lankin to be age 57, he must have been born in 50 BBY. Lankin's homeworld was noted in Wanted by Cracken, a datafile that served as a compilation of reports on various threats to the New Republic and was published in 7 ABY. Desstious was mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, which was written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
