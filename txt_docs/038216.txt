Clan R'lyek
852

Clan R'lyek was a Twi'lek clan on Ryloth that had a multi-generational feud with Clan Doneeta. The feud finally ended in 3986 BBY with a truce negotiated with Tott Doneeta, resulting in the two clan leaders going out to die in the Bright Lands, then being replaced with younger leaders who would be more willing to support the peace. *Tales of the Jedi – Redemption 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia R'lyek
