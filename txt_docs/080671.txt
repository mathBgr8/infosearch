Sla Tsun
1044

Sla Tsun was a member of Domain Tsun and one of six Yuuzhan Vong Supreme Commanders that commanded the flotilla sent to attack Zonama Sekot during the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar. Warmaster Nas Choka ordered him and Supreme Commander Tivvik to drive their mataloks into Zonama Sekot. Whether Sla Tsun survived the battle was unknown. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Yuuzhan Vong senior officers
