Directional landing beacon
1674

A directional landing beacon, also known as a directional guidance beaconThe Essential Guide to Droids and an automated beacon, was a device that transmitted fixed signals that starships used to orient themselves in space. Drone barges were equipped with sensors that allowed them to home in on the beacons while in port. *Han Solo's Revenge *Splinter of the Mind's Eye *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Platt's Starport Guide *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition *The Black Sands of Socorro *Secrets of the Sisar Run *The Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Beacon types
