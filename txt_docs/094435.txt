|type=SwordAlliance Intelligence Reports |culture=Nagai |creators= |created= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners= |locations= |cost= |value= |shape= |size= |material= |length= |width= |diam= |height= |weight= |protection= |crew= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose=Used in Nagai fighting exercises |heritage= |affiliation=Nagai }} A Swiftcut foil was a foil&mdash;a type of sword&mdash;that was used by the Nagai, a warfaring species whose members favored the use of bladed weapons in combat. Along with Tehk'la blades and longswords, Swiftcut foils were employed in a number of fighting exercises that were daily customs in Nagai society. These exercises were both a form of meditation and a sign of social status; success in them could allow the victor to ascend societal ranks. Swiftcut foils received a single mention in The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series, an article that appeared in the first issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine.
