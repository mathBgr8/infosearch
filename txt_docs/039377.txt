Star Wars: The Official Magazine 47
2408

Star Wars: The Official Magazine 47, also styled as Star Wars Magazine 47, was the 47th issue of the Star Wars: The Official Magazine. It primarily reprinted content from Star Wars Insider 67. *Cover Story - Jedi's Lost Scenes *"The Ultimate Return of the Jedi Insider's Guide" *"FX Karma: Pickle Ships, Tennis Shoes, and Other Untold Tales of the Return of the Jedi Space Battle" *"Beyond the Valley of the Ewoks" *"Seeking Revenge: A Collector's History of Revenge of the Jedi" *"The Trouble with Squibs" *"Insider Interview: Lama Su" *Behind the Magic &mdash; A Day At The Factory *Com-Scan *Prequel Update *Best of Hyperspace *New Hopes *Jawa's Corner **Collecting - Toys **Gamescape **Books **Comics Link *Set Piece &mdash; Jabba's Sail Barge *Star Wars Q&A *Comlink 47
