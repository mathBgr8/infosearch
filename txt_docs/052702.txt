Nharpira
1168

Nharpiras were small, mostly aquatic, carnivorous amphibian species that lived on Dagobah. These carnivorous creatures relied on their speed and rapid jaw movement to decimate prey before they even realized what was happening. Because of their small size, these swamp creatures could escape detection by larger predators. They were preyed upon by scranges.
