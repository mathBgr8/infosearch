Employment Center DA4176 was a structure on Coruscant during the Imperial Period. It exclusively hired aliens as construction workers to build the Emerald Splendor Estates on Byss for aristocrat housing. In reality, the Employment Center was funneling aliens to Byss to work as slaves while the Emperor drained their life force. *Evasive Action: Recruitment Category:Coruscant buildings
