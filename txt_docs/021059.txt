Black Hole-class Salvage Dreadnought
2764

The Black Hole-class Salvage Dreadnought, also known as an Ugor battle-wagon, was a heavily armed and armored Ugor starship capable of aggressive scavenging operations in space. The saucer-shaped vessel was 150 meters in diameter, and was flown by a crew of 48. Its antiquated weapons included auto-blasters, pseudosonic beamers, G-type rotolasers and missile weapons similar to proton torpedoes. An array of tractor beams drew space refuse into a central hole.
