Loreza Plirr
1058

Loreza Plirr was a female Human with extremely fine blue-black hair. She was a cartographer mapping Centerpoint Station, and was one of the pro-Corellian scientists allowed in after the Corellian government put restrictions up near the start of the Second Galactic Civil War.
