It was home to the Mon Calamari Quarrie, who designed a prototype for the B-wing starfighter. During the blockade of Ibaar, Hera Syndulla, Sabine Wren, and Garazeb Orrelios traveled to Shantipole to get the prototype B-wing to break Ibaar's blockade. A remote worldStar Wars Rebels: Visual Guide: Epic Battles located in the Shantipole system in the Roche sector of the Mid Rim Territories along the Perlemian Trade Route Shantipole was orbited by at least one moon and had a violent atmosphere of gas and lightning, and thus was considered practically desolate. The planet's surface was dominated by mountain spires covered with vibrantly colored trees. Large, winged creatures known as dactillions could be found in Shantipole's skies. thumb|right|300px|Hera Syndulla tested the Prototype B6 in the volatile atmosphere of Shantipole. Shantipole was once a highly prospected source of rare gases similar to tibanna, though investors abandoned Shantipole due to its unpredictable wind patterns. Because of that violent atmosphere, most pilots typically avoided Shantipole, with one regarding it as a "one-way trip." This attribute, however, made it the perfect workplace for Quarrie, a Mon Calamari master engineer and starfighter designer, as it allowed him to toil on his creations far from the watchful eye of the Galactic Empire. He established a secret starfighter laboratory and, after years of work, he designed and built Prototype B6, an advanced starfighter armed with experimental weapons, with the assistance of his astromech droid, BG-81. Shantipole made its first canonical appearance in the Star Wars Rebels Season Two episode "Wings of the Master," which premiered on Disney XD on November 11, 2015. Research Station Shantipole had previously appeared located on an asteroid in the 1988Star Wars Year By Year: A Visual History, New Edition West End Games adventure book Strike Force: Shantipole, which is part of Star Wars Legends.Strike Force: Shantipole Shantipole subsequently appeared in the non-canon 2017 LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures Season Two episodes "Free Fall" and "Return of the Return of the Jedi." In those episodes, the Freemakers and Quarrie lure the Imperial hunter droid M-OC into a trap on Shantipole, resulting in the destruction of the Arrowhead and the StarScavenger.
