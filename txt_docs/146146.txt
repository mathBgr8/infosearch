Kesselian Dragoon
1295

The Kesselian Dragoons were an Outland military group who were led by Grand Mouff Tarkin. They were present at the Massassi outpost on Yavin 4 during the attack of the first Death Star. They were in the second draft and third draft of the script for A New Hope.
