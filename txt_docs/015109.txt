Viraanntesse
2014

Viraanntesses were ten-legged crustacean-like aliens from the Viraanntesse system. Jason Fry, co-author of The Essential Atlas, stated his intention to create homeworlds for numerous species based on context implied from their names. Viraanntesse appears to be one such case.
