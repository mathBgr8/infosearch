Creed Mitsun
1236

Creed Mitsun was the foreman of Orbital Shipyard 1321. His crew was working on the Amerce, a new Star Destroyer built to combat the Yuuzhan Vong. He had been dating an exotic dancer from Sullust for two years in Fondor City at the time of the Battle of Fondor.
