|birth= |death= |species=Tusken Raider |gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=Ur's tribe |parents= |partners= |siblings=Urur |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Ur was a male Tusken Raider who lived on the desert planet of Tatooine during the Galactic Civil War.The Premiere set of the Jedi Knights Trading Card Game is centered around the original Star Wars trilogy. He was famous for slaying two Gamorrean guards during a territorial skirmish with the Hutt crime lord Jabba Desilijic Tiure. Ur had a younger brother named Urur, who was restricted to defending their tribe. If Ur was killed, Urur would take his place within the tribe. Ur wore desert-colored robes with two bandoliers strapped diagonally across both sides of the chest above the belt, and the standard Tusken mask. He wielded a Gaderffii. Ur preferred dewback meat over other Tatooine fare.
