Cyberlock
591

A cyberlock was an advanced security device that was often used to control access to certain restricted areas or containers. They could be released by using coded keys, code cylinders, or a combination entered via a keypad or other means. *Jedi Search *Darksaber *Junior Jedi Knights: Vader's Fortress *Young Jedi Knights: The Lost Ones Category:Locking mechanisms
