Mark Harrison
4287

}} Mark Harrison is a comic artist who had provided cover art for a number of Star Wars books. Harrison graduated from Birmingham Institute of Art and Design|School of Art, Bournville in 1982. He started work at 2000 AD comics in 1994 working on the Durham Red-related titles as well as Glimmer Rats and The Ten-Seconders. Most recently he has been creating book covers for Abaddon Books. Harrison was the cover artist for the following comic books: *X-wing Rogue Squadron: Battleground: Tatooine series *X-wing Rogue Squadron: The Warrior Princess series *Jabba the Hutt: The Art of the Deal *Jabba the Hutt: Betrayal *Jabba the Hutt: The Dynasty Trap *Jabba the Hutt: The Hunger of Princess Nampi Harrison also contributed to the level design and cut scene artwork of the PlayStation Portable release of Star Wars: Battlefront II.Mark Harrison.
