Senate Interspecies Advisory Committee
1043

The Senate Interspecies Advisory Committee was an organization established by the Galactic Senate that aimed to limit inter-species tension, and mediate an end to various inter-species conflicts that often occurred throughout the Old Republic. The committee consisted of Senators, Jedi and other experts.
