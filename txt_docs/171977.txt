Keydon
2010

Keydon was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle used by Dimok forces during the Sepan Civil War. In an attempt to end the war by capturing the leaders of both the Dimoks and the Ripoblus, Imperial forces, under the command of Admiral Harkov boarded shuttle Keydon, along with shuttle Phantele towards the end of the Sepan campaign. Following the boarding, Imperial forces did not discover the leaders of the Dimoks but instead discovered the children of Dimok leaders aboard Keydon. After the ship was boarded the Keydon was destroyed.
