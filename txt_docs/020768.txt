Nyssa
2840

Nyssa was a planet in the Tapani sector's Nyssa system, in the Mecetti Province. The Sith cult of assassins known as the Mecrosa Order arose on the planet thousands of years before the Battle of Yavin, backed by House Mecetti. During the Great Sith War, the Order was styled into a Sith cult of assassins that produced a number of Sith Lords, fighting for Exar Kun. The Mecrosa Order was exterminated by the Jedi and House Pelagia during the Cleansing of the Nine Houses, one of the Old Sith Wars.
