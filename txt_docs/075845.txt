Yald Sutel
1436

Yald Sutel was a Human male officer in the New Republic Defense Force. He had a blocky head and by 25 ABY held the rank of general. That year he was assigned to the Battle of Ord Mantell, placed in command of the Imperial forces formerly under the command of Vice Admiral Ark Poinard.
