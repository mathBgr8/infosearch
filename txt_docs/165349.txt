Abregado-Rae Intrigue
5636

|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Abregado-Rae Intrigue" was an adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, which appeared in the sourcebook No Disintegrations. The adventure opens with the players being accosted by a group of thugs working for the Devaronian crime lord A'jidre Skrigatov, Provincial Governor of Le Yer on Abregado-rae. He requests that they recover his missing fiancée whom he believes has been kidnapped by a political rival. After a great deal of searching, as well as encountering other hunters after the same target for different reasons, the players find the fiancée, Brinaloy N'Vaari, but are at odds with what to do with her, since she is being forced into the marriage.
