Team Kubindi
2056

|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Cup of Limmie }} Team Kubindi was a limmie team that played for the planet of Kubindi. According to an issue of the CoCo District Edition of HoloNet News reported on the date 13:3:14, the planet of Fwillsving's team triumphed over the visiting Team Kubindi; the victory inspired a riot wherein four million joyous celebrants ransacked the city in which they played, Svitalos, by looting, vandalizing, and partying in the streets. For the HoloNet News report, Fwillsving merchant Kerlay Utanks expressed his joy over Kubindi's loss. Team Kubindi was mentioned in "Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round," an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
