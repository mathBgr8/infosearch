Imperial ground-skimmer
1530

Imperial ground-skimmers were amphibious troop transports used by the Galactic Empire around 0 ABY. These repulsorcraft were used to traverse the terrain of harsh swamp worlds like Daluuj, where at least three were used by Commander Orlok at the Imperial Training Center. The Heroes of Yavin encountered three ground-skimmers during a mission on Daluuj to rescue Admiral Gial Ackbar. *"It's a Trap" Category:Repulsorcraft models Category:Vehicle classes of the Galactic Empire
