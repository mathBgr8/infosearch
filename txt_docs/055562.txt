Yargh'un
999

Yargh'uns were creatures native to Yuuzhan'tar. Toothy rodents, they were roughly a meter in length, and were used in garbage disposal not thrown to maw luurs. Yargh'un's were also used as a form of humiliating death. Those accused of heresy or treachery were often thrown into pits filled with yargh'uns.
