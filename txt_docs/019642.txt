Greenputt
1649

Greenputt was a sport played by all kinds of beings in the galaxy. This sport was played on a wide playing field and required each player to hit a tiny ball into a remote hole. The game required a wide range of hitting skills to get the ball in the hole. The major leagues were controlled by the Greenputt Governing Council.
