Terrina Square
939

Terrina Square was a public plaza on the world of Quellor. This square was the location of an anti-Imperial rally during the Galactic Civil War, in which the protesters burned an effigy of Emperor Palpatine. The stormtroopers ordered to enforce crowd control opened fire on the rally members, killing them all - despite having specific orders not to fire upon the peaceful protesters by Moff Toggan. *Rebellion Era Sourcebook Category:Colonies structures
