Zeku
2234

The Zeku were the inhabitants of the planet Zekulae,Jedi Apprentice: The Death of Hope located in the Zeemacht Cluster of the Inner Rim. Their planet was a barren world, noted for its dark, rich mineral soil, studded with blue crystals. Its soil was so fine, that it was everpresent on the planet. The Zeku government took a relaxed approach to laws, and as such, it was not uncommon for disputes to be resolved through physical force or blasters. Visible from Zekulae was a globular cluster of orange and red giants, neutron stars, pulsars, and blue stragglers which the Zeku called Cosm's Well. The Zeku were created by Jude Watson for her young adults novel, Jedi Apprentice: The Death of Hope, published in 2001.
