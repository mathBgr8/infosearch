Keluda
2173

Keluda was a male Dug spacer who served as a known associate to Vilmarh Grahrk. During his lifetime, his brother was one of the workers at the Golden Nyss Shipyards killed when the station was blown up by the Yinchorri. Keluda also owned a speeder bike. He was acquainted with Gar Gastinin, Vletho, a Kubaz, and a Trandoshan, with whom he drank with at the Cluster Cantina on Nar Shaddaa. He was a member of the Assassin's Guild, and later tried to get the hit on Simon Greyshade after Tsian was apprehended and the contract was officially open again. When his speeder was destroyed by Obi-Wan Kenobi, the Jedi suspended him in mid-air to question him about the attempted assassination.
