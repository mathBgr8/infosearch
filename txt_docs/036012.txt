Corellian HoverFreighter
4240

The Corellian HoverFreighter was a repulsorcraft used by Wookiees to transport goods such as nova crystals between spaceports. Corellian HoverFreighters were manufactured at spaceports, costing 75 units of carbon to assemble. As repulsorcrafts the HoverFreighters levitated above ground surfaces and could even traverse over bodies of water. The HoverFreighters were grey with painted strips on the side to indicate their affiliation. They had a rectangular shape with strips that extended below the body of the vehicles which carried orange boxes filled with nova crystals. Corellian HoverFreighters were used to exchange goods between spaceports, typically carrying nova crystals on a return journey. Corellian HoverFreighters were used 39 BBY and 36 BBY,The New Essential Chronology places the Alaris Prime colonial war between 39 BBY and 36 BBY during the war for the moon Alaris Prime fought between Wookiee colonists and the Trade Federation. Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn, acting as an advisor to the Wookiees, instructed the Wookiee Chewbacca to build Corellian HoverFreighters at his camp to faciliate trade between him and Shoran, another Wookiee.
