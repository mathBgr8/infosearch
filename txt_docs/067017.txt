|type=Paperback |pages=6 |dimensions=0.1 x 9.0 x 12.0 inches |weight=4.5 ounces |isbn=0375815082 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Star Wars: Attack of the Clones - Padmé Amidala Paper Doll Book contains a paper doll of Senator Amidala that can be dressed in several of her costumes from Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. A similar book, the Star Wars: Episode I - Queen Amidala Paper Doll Book, was published three years earlier. Padmé Amidala, formerly Queen of Naboo, is now a Senator of the Galactic Republic. Her title may have changed, but her exquisite sense of style has not. Punch out her wardrobe and dress Padmé as she appears in the exciting scenes of Star Wars: Attack of the Clones!!
