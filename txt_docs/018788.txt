Bandor (Moff)
1605

Bandor was a Moff who represented the planet of Questal. Bandor championed the Hurlothrumbic Generator project: a device which could stimulate the brain into feeling fear. With his blue eyes and close-cropped black hair, Bandor was considered attractive by Human standards, although a little gruff.
