Tac-Spec Corporation
1123

Tac-Spec Corporation was not actually a real company, but was a front for the GenoHaradan assassins guild. Their only major product was the the FIII Footman droids. Tac-Spec Corporation remained in business for approximately six hundred years (approximately 1000 BBY to 400 BBY). *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Galaxy of Intrigue Category:Droid manufacturers
