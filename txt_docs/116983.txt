Thrackan Sal-Solo (starship)
2054

The Thrackan Sal-Solo was a Corellian Dreadnaught built around Kiris 6 that would later serve as the flagship of the Confederation fleet. Thrackan Sal-Solo was the flagship of the secret assault fleet constructed by Corellia over the course of the decade following the Yuuzhan Vong War. It was named after Chief of State Thrackan Sal-Solo, who was later assassinated in 40 ABY. Though the vessel was named Thrackan Sal-Solo it seems more likely that it was not christened after his death, but when the fleet was first constructed, as Thrackan was largely disliked by his peers, and naming the flagship after him could be seen as honoring him. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corellian Dreadnaughts Category:Flagships Category:Starships of the Confederation Fleet
