The droid had five manipulator arms, a blaster and a communicator. They also featured a self-destruct mechanism, repulsorlift engines, and a sensor suite.Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Character Encyclopedia - Join the Battle! The Rodian Gedeek Obaz designed the Alpha Droid variant for the Viper line during his service to the Empire but came to regret his decision.Star Wars Outlaws After the Fall of the Empire, Gideon's Imperial remnant still used viper probe droids. The droids communicated using Omnisignal unicode. Viper probe droids performed a large range of missions, from searching planets for signs of pirates and criminals. to conducting sensor scans of areas the Empire wanted to investigate. The droids used their sensors to probe wreckage and map terrain. They were equipped with powerful transceivers, which were used to relay their discoveries to Imperial-class Star Destroyers and intelligence outposts for further analysis.
