Star Wars Weekly 6 is the sixth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints. *Star Wars (1977) 3 *"Man-Gods from Beyond the Stars" *"The Sword in the Star!: Stave 1: Alas, the Seeds of Man!" *"Behind the Scenes of Star Wars" *Competition to win a copy of Star Wars on 8mm film from Mailmaster. This version of the film is twenty minutes long, and features color and sound. To enter, readers must count the number of a particular symbol hidden throughout the issue. Notably, this was supposed to be the prize given away in the previous issue. 06
