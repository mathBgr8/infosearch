Quinn Sheffield
1126

Quinn Sheffield was a Baron of House Reena. He represented his house in the Vor-cal on at least seven occasions. He was, however, not a very good hunter and participated because it gave him an opportunity to socialize and escape from the rigors of bureaucratic life.
