Odve'starn

Odve'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located on the western shores of the planet's western continent, facing the Rhyde'vak ocean. Kolme'starn was found to the east. *Vision of the Future *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
