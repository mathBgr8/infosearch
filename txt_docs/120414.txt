Lifeblood cleaver

thumb|250px|right|A Sayormi warrior wielding a Lifeblood Cleaver The lifeblood cleaver was a large battle axe that was used by the more bloodthirsty Sayormi warriors of the Kkowir Forest on the Wookiee homeworld of Kashyyyk.
