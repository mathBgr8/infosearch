Yaeger Doru
875

Yaeger Doru was a young Human with a habit of biting his fingernails. He worked for House Hirskaala In 31 BBY, head of security for Hirskaala Nim'Ri disappeared in order to join the Cularin resistance, along with other seven people. Doru took over Nim'Ri's post.
