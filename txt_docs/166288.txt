Glowing nectar
996

The glowing nectar, also called the glowing liquid, was a nectar produced by sunnydew flowers. Most of it was stolen by Gantu, who used it to light his cave. The absence of the nectar from the flowers killed most of them, making the native Zandor Rockers hate Gantu. *Ewoks (1985) 1 Category:Substances
