Rashtah
2440

Rashtah was a Wookiee slave trader and an associate and bodyguard of the crime lord Krayn. Involvement in such a 'profession' was, of course, unusual for a Wookiee. In 29 BBY, Rashtah accompanied Krayn to Rorak 5 for a business meeting with the Colicoids, who sought total control over the spice operations on Nar Shaddaa. Krayn directed Rashtah to monitor the movements of the slave trader "Bakleeda," an advisor to the Colicoids who was tasked with inspecting the crime lord's factories on the "Smuggler's Moon." After an intense struggle, Rashtah was killed in a turbolift within the slave factories of Nar Shaddaa by "Bakleeda" (Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi in disguise), whom the Wookiee had attacked at the behest of Krayn. Rashtah had a scar that ran from his head to his lip; a battle-damaged eye was covered by an eyepatch. Although Jedi Quest: Path to Truth depicts Rashtah with a scar running through his eye and wearing an eye patch, in the Jedi Quest comic series he is depicted with both eyes intact.
