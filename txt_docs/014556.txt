It was the homeworld of the Equani. In 23 BBY, the Galactic Republic tested a superweapon, a predecessor of the Death Star, on the star Nharl. The resulting intense solar flare accidentally destroyed all life on Equanus, as well as the superweapon and its testers. Only those Equani offworld at the time survived.
