Kubrai-class S4 transport
1487

The Kubrai-class S4 transport was a transport starship produced by Kubrai Transportation. It was 20 meters long and was armed with two blaster cannons. The Kubari S4 was quite expensive&mdash;during the days of Rebellion new ship price was 180,000 credits, mostly because of its great sublight engines and fast Class I hyperdrive.
