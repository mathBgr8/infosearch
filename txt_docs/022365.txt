Touch-not
2799

The touch-not was a shrub native to Yavin 4. It exuded a caustic resin that blistered the skin upon contact. Mon Calamari were particularly susceptible to the resin. They were amongst the many perils faced by the Rebels of Massassi Station.Empire 35 The plant could also be found on Tatooine. The stem of the touch-not is slick and wet-looking even during Yavin 4's dry season. Bright yellow, curly leaves catch even the slightest wind and will twist and spin crazily on their flexible petioles. The plant's flowers feature 10 or 12 yellow petaled rosettes which mature into waxy berries. Because the touch-not's caustic resin dissolves in water, these plants are dangerous to be around in rain or misty weather, as the dripping resin can scar anything in its proximity. The touch-not's resin can cause severe rashes that itch and blister the skin, which must be immediately washed with hot water and detergent to attempt to mitigate its effects. The resin can eat through organic fabrics and fibers, rendering most clothing ineffective against its toxins.
