Senior General
1121

Senior General was a flag officer military rank utilized by the Chiss Ascendancy. It was above the rank of admiral and below Fleet Admiral. The rank of Senior General was first mentioned in the 2020 novel Thrawn Ascendancy: Greater Good, the second installment in the Star Wars: The Ascendancy Trilogy by Timothy Zahn. *Thrawn Ascendancy: Greater Good *Thrawn Ascendancy: Lesser Evil Category:Chiss Ascendancy military ranks
