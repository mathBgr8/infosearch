Crozo Industrial Products
1698

Crozo Industrial Products was the company that manufactured the 2-MAL personal comlink, a communications device commonly used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. During the High Republic Era, an ancient deep-space transmitter from the Jedi Archives which was to be displayed at the Valo Republic Fair was going to be loaned to Crozo for a communications showcase afterwards. *The High Republic: The Rising Storm *Star Wars: Rogue One: The Ultimate Visual Guide *Fully Operational *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, New Edition Category:Manufacturing companies
