Echani Firedancer
567

The Echani Firedancers were martial artists who combined fighting techniques with exotic weapons; the usage of the two together almost seemed like a dance. Echani Firedancers made use of ritual brands that were heated on both ends, which caused burn wounds to enemies struck by the weapon. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Echani culture Category:Martial arts Category:Soldiers
