Sal'kaasa
658

Sal'kaasa was a small settlement on Ryloth. Being on the fringes of the Bright Lands, it was far from Ryloth's main urban zones like Kala'uun. It had a spaceport and housed a small contingent of Rodians. Dindoo's mechanic shop and the business Dindoo Tours, managed by Dindoo's heir Kelko, were located on Sal'kaasa.
