Emmin Whitehat
848

Emmin Whitehat was a rogue Corellian slicer. In 1 ABY, he caused trouble to the Corellian entrepreneur Mindra Nox, until she finally found out where he lived and hired a spacer to take him out. Although Whitehat intercepted the message, he was unable to prevent the spacer from fulfilling his mission. Whitehat is mentioned in the description of a mission called "Criminal Slicer Emmin Whitehat" in Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided that players can get at Mission Terminals on Corellia.
