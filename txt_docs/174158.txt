Tarmul
1164

Tarmul was a kind of plant used as hedges on the Mid RimThe Essential Atlas planet Qiilura. Ghez Hokan, a Mandalorian mercenary hired by the Confederacy of Independent Systems tested Jedi Master Kast Fulier's lightsaber on them. Tarmul appeared in the 2004 novel Hard Contact, the first novel in the Republic Commando series written by Karen Traviss. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Plants of Qiilura
