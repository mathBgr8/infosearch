Homestar
726

Homestar was a cantina located on Treasure Ship Row on Corellia. Its unremarkable exterior hid an expansive interior, complete with dancers and a live band. Jedi Knights Aayla Secura and Ylenic It'kla got into a scuffle with three Gotals here while searching for Ratri Tane. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cantinas Category:Coronet City locations
