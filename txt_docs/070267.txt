Jedi Praxeum protocol droid
2029

The Jedi Praxeum protocol droid was a golden protocol droid deployed in Luke Skywalker's Jedi Praxeum. It once greeted Kyle Katarn in the hangar bay of the Praxeum upon his arrival, informing him that Skywalker was waiting upstairs.Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast By 14 ABY, this droid routinely briefed students on missions when Luke or Kyle were not available, namely the mission to Nar Kreeta, Zonju V and Dosuun. The Jedi Praxeum protocol is canonical, as it appears in Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast, but has not been named. It has some dialogue with the player. Although it resembles C-3PO, it is not him, as its voice is much lower.
