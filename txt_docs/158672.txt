Hayca Mekket
1157

Hayca Mekket was a female Mandalorian veterinarian who treated Mandalore Boba Fett for his terminal illness by injecting him with the bone marrow of Jaing Skirata. All she asked for payment was a thick, red quilt and told Fett he shouldn't have anymore children. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Legacy of the Force: Revelation *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Healers and doctors Category:Humans Category:Mandalorians
