Sah'c Canyon
642

Sah'c Canyon was located in the Sah'c District of Coruscant. It was lined by skyscrapers and a mag-lev train rail, and led to the Chancellor's emergency command bunker from the Senate District. It was used as the escape route for the Supreme Chancellor Palpatine during the Battle of Coruscant. *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Canyons Category:Coruscant landforms
