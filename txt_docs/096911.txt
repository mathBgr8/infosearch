|formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Pelemaxians were a social groupCreatures of the Galaxy that originated in the Colonies' Pelemax system. Generations before the Galactic Civil War, the Pelemaxians tamed the jungle creatures known as the tentacle birds of Pelemax as war mounts, capitalizing on their savage call, which could scare and confuse enemy soldiers. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, the Pelemaxians had begun using the birds as caravan animals as well as a mode of personal transportation. The Pelemaxians also sold the tentacle birds of Pelemax offworld; such exports made up a major part of the local economy. The Pelemaxians were introduced in Creatures of the Galaxy, a 1994 sourcebook published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The respective entry was written by Rick D.
