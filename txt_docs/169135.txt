Model 210 personal armor
2121

Krail 210 personal armor was a type of full-body battle armor complete with helmet; it was the armor of choice worn by the bounty hunter Noval Garaint. Garaint's modified armor was a reinforced set of armor with power suit booster and an Arakyd Whisper jet pack. The suit included a fully integrated on-board sensor pack with stress-reinforced blast helmet, integral dual force blades, integral line slinger with attached magnetic grapple and winch, and a sealed environment and atmosphere filter. A blaster rifle holster, medpac pouch and chest-brackets for grenades rounded out the kit. The more common version was a favorite of private security forces, bounty hunters, and people who wanted custom armor.
