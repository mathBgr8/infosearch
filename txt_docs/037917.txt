Star Wars Insider 38

Star Wars Insider 38 is the thirty-eighth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was published in June/July 1998. This issue is notable as the first issue in which regular writer Scott Chernoff became Managing Editor. *"From the Editor's Desk" *"Rebel Rumblings" *"Prequel Update with Rick McCallum" *"Prequel Profile: Nick Gillard" *"In the Star Wars Universe: Drewe Henley" *"Star News" *"The Wonder Column" *"Straight from the Horse's Mouth" &mdash; The Passing of a Legend: Archive Goodwin *"Scouting the Galaxy" &ndash; Toy Fair, Episode I And the Elusive B'omarr Monk *"Jawa Trader" *"Pulp Jedi: An Interview with Episode I Star Samuel L. Jackson" *:Hollywood hot shot Samuel L. Jackson tells Scott Chernoff how he turned his dream of joining the Star Wars universe into a reality. *"I Bent My Wookiee! Celebrating the Star Wars/Simpsons Connection" *:A complete rundown of every Star Wars reference on our favorite animated sitcom. Scott Chernoff gets the scoop from Simpsons writers and artists on why the Force is with them. *"Among the Clouds: An Audience with Billy Dee Williams" *:Star Wars icon Billy Dee Williams tells Scott Chernoff how he made Lando Calrissian so memorable, and talks about his stature as one of the preeminent African-American stars of cinema. *"Top 20 Star Wars Characters" *:Jon Bradley Snyder reveals the results of the Insider's exhaustive reader poll, complete with your quotes about your favorite characters.
