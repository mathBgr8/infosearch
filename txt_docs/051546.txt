Third Fleet (disambiguation)
898

Third Fleet could refer to several things: *Third Fleet, of the Imperial Navy. *Imperial Third Fleet, of the reconstituted Sith Empire. *Confederate Third Fleet, of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. *New Republic Third Fleet, of the New Republic Defense Fleet. *Galactic Alliance Third Fleet, of the Galactic Alliance Defense Fleet. *Coruscant Third Fleet, of Darth Krayt's Galactic Empire, stationed at Coruscant. *Outer Rim Third Fleet, of Darth Krayt's Galactic Empire, stationed in the Outer Rim.
