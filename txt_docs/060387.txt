Pizzazz 10
992

Pizzazz 10 is the tenth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated July 1978 and featured the beginning of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. The issue features the article "The Monsters," discussing science fiction movie monsters, which includes Chewbacca on the Most Likeable Monster list. 10
