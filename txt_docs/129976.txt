Rode to Ruin

The Rode to Ruin was a ship owned by Fracca. It was registered on Orron III with registration number RN27-382992. In 16 ABY, Luke Skywalker tracked this ship as it passed the inner flight control buoys of the planet Teyr. *Shield of Lies Category:Starships
