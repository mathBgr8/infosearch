Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection (comics)
2591

Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection was a limited series of comics that was released monthly from 2007 to 2008. They consisted of hardcover versions of the best Dark Horse TPBs. They were available only through Direct Market outlets, and each volume was limited to initial orders with no reprints. *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 1 - The Freedon Nadd Uprising *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 2 - Jedi vs. Sith *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 3 - Darth Maul *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 4 - Jango Fett & Zam Wesell *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 5 - Light and Dark *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 6 - Endgame *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 7 - Darklighter *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 8 - Splinter of the Mind's Eye *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 9 - Boba Fett: Death, Lies, and Treachery *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 10 - Crimson Empire *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 11 - Union *Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection Vol. 12 - Legacy
