Indu Council
595

The Indu Council was the governing body of the Indu San system. During a meeting on the planet Indu San to discuss an alliance with the New Republic, chief councillor of the Indu Council, Shek Barayel, was assassinated by Council Authority Officer Kaleb Darme. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Legislatures
