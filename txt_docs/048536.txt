Green Squad 3 was one of eight Rebel Alliance training squads who trained at Alliance Training Center-5/Green Squad on the planet Ksift. They were previously known simply as Green Squad, and helped in the defense of the base when Imperial forces attacked just after they had retrieved the data file Green3 in their training exercises.
