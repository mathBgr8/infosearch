Sale Mardec
1206

Sale Mardec was a Human female. She lived in Jalor City with her parents Riic and Lina, and a sister Fora. Unfortunately, on 2 ABY, the Mardecs (except for Riic) died in a crossfire when the Galactic Empire attacked a Rebel cell.
