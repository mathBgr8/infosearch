It adapts the 1977 film Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, and it was first published in October 2004 by Scholastic. The novel was later collected in Star Wars Trilogy: Collector's Edition (2005), Star Wars Boxed Set: Episodes I–VI (2007), and Star Wars: Classic Trilogy (2014). A revised, canon edition of the novel, Star Wars: A New Hope, was published in 2017. A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away.... A young Jedi is discovered. A rebellion grows.
