Four See Seers of the Cyclops
1003

thumb|250px|The See Seers with their Lord Being The Four See Seers of the Cyclops were four beings of the Skakoan religion. According to myth, the four See Seers guarded the Eye of the Albino Cyclops within the Gates of Grontessiant, which itself was located beyond the Threshold of the Hidden Realms. They were overseen by the Lord Being of the Swirblies, and judged individuals upon their worthiness to behold the Albino Cyclops. They were possibly Swirblies themselves. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Deities Category:Skakoan religion
