Pizzazz 7
1277

Pizzazz 7 is the seventh issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated April 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. The cover is UFO themed, in reference to the issue's featured article, and includes Darth Vader alongside an alien from Close Encounters of the Third Kind and Mr. Spock from Star Trek. The issue also features an ad and order form to join the Official Star Wars Fan Club. 07
