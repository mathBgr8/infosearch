Ishori Confederene

The Ishori Confederene was the region of space containing Isht. It was represented in the New Republic Senate by Ghic Dx'ono. Its neighbors included the sectors which contained Iphigin and the Diamala homeworld. *Specter of the Past *Vision of the Future Category:Core Worlds regions
