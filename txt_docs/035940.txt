Confederacy Cruiser
2046

Confederacy Cruisers were air cruisers employed by the Confederacy of Independent Systems from the earliest months of the Clone Wars. thumb|left|200px|Confederacy Cruisers [[Battle of Asteroid 426|attack Asteroid 426 near Sarapin.]] The Confederacy Cruiser's main role was as an air-to-surface artillery platform. Its cannon was an area-effect weapon, capable of wiping out large numbers of ground-based targets, often decimating clone trooper units. They were also effective at destroying enemy fortifications and weapon emplacements. They proved essential in allowing Sev'rance Tann to negate the orbital defense net of Sarapin.
