Boz Addle
2809

Chief Technician Boz Addle was an engineer working in the hangar bays of the Jedi Temple's mooring tower on Coruscant during the Clone Wars. His duty was to maintain the starships used by the Jedi Order, and he seems to have been a non-Force-sensitive specialist working for the Order, rather than a Jedi Knight himself. For years, Boz Addle had been working as a passionate chief technician during the time of the Clone Wars, a galaxy-wide conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. During the height of the war, Boz worked in the higher levels of the Republic's capital world of Coruscant, for he had been assessing and caring for incoming ships with the necessary enthusiasm. He always took things seriously and he deeply cared for all the ships he was responsible for. Boz particularly loved courier ships. When Jedi Master Jai Maruk returned from a reconnaissance mission in the Hydian trade route, he inspected his battered and battered ship Limit of Vision, a Seltaya-class fast courier. Chief Boz was a skilled and passionate specialist, able to differentiate different types of damage by a quick visual inspection.
