|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=Nightsisters }} The Nightsister Stronghold was located on Dathomir, the homeworld of the Nightsisters. It served as a refugee settlement for the exiled witches. Right outside the walls of the stronghold was the entrance to the Rancor Cave, where many rancors lived.
