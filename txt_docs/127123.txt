Golden Spoon

}} 250px|thumb|Porcellus' Golden Spatula, marked with an [[Galactic Empire/Legends|Imperial emblem.]] The Golden Spoon, also known as the Golden Spatula, was an award given for exceptional cooking. Jabba the Hutt's chef Porcellus was awarded the Golden Spoon three times. In Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, Porcellus called the prize a Golden Spatula, rather than a Golden Spoon. One of the spatulas can be seen hanging in the kitchen. *Secrets of Tatooine Category:Awards
