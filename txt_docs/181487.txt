Guba bird
945

Guba birds were a species of birds. They made their nests on the mountains of the Forest Moon of Endor. The young Ewok Princess Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka once ordered her close friends to help her take jinjang berries to some baby guba birds nested on the top of a mountain.
