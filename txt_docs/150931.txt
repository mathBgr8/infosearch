Hifold Sensory Package
2645

The Hifold Sensory Package, manufactured by Neuro-Saav Corporation, was a cybernetic implant package that enhanced the eyes and ears.Cracken's Rebel Field Guide Using a combination of true-fleck brain implants and artificial hypergleamum glands to increase the brain's capacity to handle extra sensory input and keep it organized, the Hifold sensory package enhanced all aspects of perception by way of superior visual and aural acuity. In addition, the implant also allowed the user to interpret body language much better; with the implant the user became acutely aware of changes in breathing patterns and subtle changes in eye patterns, with a user being able to interpret this data to tremendous advantage. *Cragus 12 *Equa FelensFlashpoint!
