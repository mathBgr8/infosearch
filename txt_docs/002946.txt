Helska IV
4969

Helska IV, also called Helska 4, Helska Four, or simply Helska, was the fourth planet in the Helska system, located in Dalonbian sector in the Outer Rim Territories. Helska IV was essentially a shell of ice around a vast inner ocean, with occasional pockets of air located in the thick icy crust. The planet was generally considered inhospitable to life as the galaxy knew it, and thus had no native life forms. Helska IV was the site of the Yuuzhan Vong known major foothold in the galaxy. It was here that the Praetorite Vong, comprising the first wave of their invasion, set their own yammosk, or War Coordinator, under the frozen surface. Following the discovery of the Vong on Helska IV by the New Republic, Jedi Knight Anakin Solo formulated and executed a plan to use a shieldship to reflect energy back at the planet, thereby stealing the yammosk's energy through evaporation.
