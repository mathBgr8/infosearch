Boma (species)
1622

Bomas were large sentient species, standing at about five feet tall, and covered with brown fur. They did not speak Basic. The Rebels named a starfighter group which attacked the Death Star "Boma" flight. Boma are described as being a "cross between a brown bear and a guinea pig." In the first draft to Episode IV, George Lucas created the name Boma as a possible replacement name for Chewbacca. For the second draft, however, a Boma named Oeeta shared an apartment with Han Solo.Star Wars Insider 95 "Boma beasts" appeared in the Tales of the Jedi comic series and Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords as dangerous non-sentient creatures on Onderon and its moon Dxun.
