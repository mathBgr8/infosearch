Jeswandi
931

Jeswandi was an ancient art of meditation and martial arts among the Bothan people. Girov Dza'tey mastered it, and that ability allowed him to overcome the power of the Sith Temple on Malrev IV. He later used Jeswandi to harness the dark power of the temple in an attempt to destroy Rogue Squadron. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 20 *Star Wars Handbook 1: X-Wing Rogue Squadron *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothan culture Category:Martial arts
