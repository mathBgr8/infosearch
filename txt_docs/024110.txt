DR919a was the ship used by Jae Juun and Tarfang after the destruction of the XR808g. DR919a was a Damorian Ronto-class transport that was sacrificed in order to let the passengers board the Dark Nest hiveship. As with Juun and Tarfang's previous ship, the vessel's name simply originated from its Galactic Alliance registry identification number. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ronto-class transports Category:Smuggler starships
