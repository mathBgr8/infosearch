Nash Edgerton
1756

Nash Edgerton (born January 19, 1973) was, during the filming of the prequel trilogy, a stunt double for Ewan McGregor (Obi-Wan Kenobi). Edgerton is the brother of Joel Edgerton (Owen Lars). By coincidence, Obi-Wan and Owen were referred to as brothers in the Return of the Jedi novelization.
