Sullustan (starship)
825

Sullustan was a Corellian corvette captained by T'nun Bdu. The ship was intercepted and destroyed by Admiral Daala when she found heavy weapons, communications equipment, and prefabricated building material that could be used to aid the New Republic, as well as maps and information on the New Republic in its databanks. *Dark Apprentice *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corvettes Category:Starships of the New Republic
