Tendir Blue
1317

Tendir Blue was a male Separatist agent working for the Techno Union. He was heavyset, with red hair, freckles, and crooked teeth. Tendir was used by Jedi Knights Aayla Secura and Ylenic It'kla to help extract Ratri Tane and his family from Corellia. Blue's first name was misspelled as "Tendril" in the last appearance of his name in the short story Elusion Illusion.
