Tobbert Dalonbian
2698

|masters= |apprentices= }} Tobbert Dalonbian was president of the Extragalactic Society, an institute searching for extragalactic life, in 22 BBY.HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47 is dated to "13:3:14," which is a Great ReSynchronization date corresponding to 22 BBY, per the reasoning here. When scientific funding was restricted by the Galactic Republic's General Ministry across all of its institutes in that year, a clearly disconsolate Dalonbian reported that the Society would have to close most of its offices and rely on droid-based methods of operation.
