Annihilator (Imperial I-class)
1967

The Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Annihilator, was stationed on the borders of Hutt Space, near the Periphery, with the Ruthless around 4 ABY. Governor Newen Streeg of the Periphery had the option of calling on the Annihilator when necessary. There are two Imperial warships called Annihilator at roughly the same time.
