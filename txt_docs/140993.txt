Big Boom
917

The Big Boom was the cantina on Nova Station. It featured Bobolo Baker's All-Bith Band. After escaping from GAG custody, Ben Skywalker stopped on Nova Station to rendezvous with Trista Zel and Taryn Zel, twin sisters in Hapan Security, and to send Lon Shevu's body to his wife, Shula Shevu, on Vaklin. As they departed the station, they were ambushed by SD-XX, who attempted to kill Ben, but was shoved out an airlock after a brief duel.
