Bargainer
499

Bargainer was a title for the leader of a Qom Jha nesting. The title was also used by the Qom Qae who gave the title to the eldest member of the nesting. Eater Of Fire Creepers was a Bargainer for his nesting. *Vision of the Future *Vision of the Future abridged audiobook Category:Titles
