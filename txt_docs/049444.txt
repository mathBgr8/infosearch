Coruscani Pilot Institute
1655

The Coruscani Pilot Institute, abbreviated as CPI, was an academy located on the planet Coruscant, specializing in the training of space pilots. CPI was a three-year course that preceded a one-year enrollment in the Imperial Academy system. At least the top cadets in each year went on to the specialized TIE pilot training course on Carida, and the Institute was prestigious enough to attract recruits from as far away as the Outer Rim. It commonly trained the children of famous military families.Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide In the early stages of the Galactic Civil War, the commandant of the CPI was Mogurk.
