Runyip stew
566

Runyip stew was a dish that contained runyip meat which Kyle Katarn had during his trip aboard Star of Empire. Runyip stew was also on the menu of Luke Skywalker's Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4. Mara Jade ate a bowl there along with a side of mixed green vegetables. *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire *Dark Apprentice Category:Soups and stews
