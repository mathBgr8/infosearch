|continent= |location= |climate= |interest=*Lutris Lake *Tynna Central Government Building |population= |demonym= |affiliation=Galactic Republic }} Lutris was a locale on the Expansion RegionThe Essential Atlas planet of Tynna. Shortly before the Clone Wars in 22 BBY,The Essential Atlas the Tynna Central Government Building, which was sited in Lutris near Lutris Lake, collapsed due to a stone mite infestation. The incident prompted Tynnan planetary leader Razelfiin to secede from the Galactic Republic and join the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
