Deadman's Hand
1621

Deadman's Hand was Dunari's personal Muurian transport. With this, he helped Emon Azzameen escape from Quesna Base. Later, it was used to help the Azzameen siblings escape an ambush set up by their uncle Antan in the Kessel Station.
