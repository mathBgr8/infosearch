Liquidsilver L7 light blaster pistol
1416

The Liquidsilver L7 light blaster pistol was a blaster pistol used among smugglers. It was named for its ability to blend into a starship's cargo hold, allowing its owner to get it unnoticed in dangerous situations. This weapon can be looted from Wookiee NPCs in the Kkowir Forest region on Kashyyyk. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees *Fly Casual Category:Blaster pistols
