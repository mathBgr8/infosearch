Sungrass
2302

The Sungrass was a Xiytiar-class heavy transport used by Wraith Squadron during their time posing as the Hawk-bat Independent Space Force in the Halmad system. Previously belonging to an Imperial shipping corporation, it was stationed at a facility which was attacked by New Republic Intelligence and listed by the Empire as destroyed. In reality it was repaired and given a new name and history. As part of the Hawk-bat ruse, the Sungrass primary role was to act as a transport for the unit's TIEs, which were otherwise incapable of hyperspace travel.
