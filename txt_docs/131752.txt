thumb|250px|The iconic bright blue swirls of a simu-tunnel A simu-tunnel,Solo: A Star Wars Story The Official Guide also known as a hyperspace tunnel,The High Republic: Beware the Nameless or as a hyper tunnel,Star Wars: Creatures Big & Small was a tunnel-like pocket of realspace allowing safe travel through the alternate dimension known as hyperspace. Although most beings needed hyperdrive technology to produce such tunnels, the purrgil created them naturally. From the traveler's point of view, a simu-tunnel looked like a spiral of blues.
