Uitgeverij M
2117

Uitgeverij M is the science-fiction, fantasy, and thriller imprint of De Boekerij. It currently is responsible for the translation and distribution of Star Wars novels in the Netherlands. Uitgeverij M was formed when the crime, science-fiction, and fantasy sections of J. M.
