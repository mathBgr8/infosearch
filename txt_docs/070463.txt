Gorgano IV Museum
926

The Gorgano IV Museum was located in the Historical District of Estalle Island on Procopia. It was of the Tapani sector's most prestigious art museums and regularly sponsored exhibitions of established and upcoming artists. It was named after a former Emperor, Gorgano IV. The museum's collection of art was extremely valuable and the institution had an excellent security system to deter and prevent would-be thieves. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Museums Category:Procopia locations
