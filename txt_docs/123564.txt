Newoongall
1193

Newoongalls were multi-legged arachnoid creatures native to Rodia. The creatures were often spotted with dripping pincers. Able to climb and jump, they clung to cavern ceilings and dropped down on unsuspecting prey in surprise attacks. During the Galactic Civil War, Teeko Soonca, Ruku Kooff and Mannee Swaano, with some off-world visitors, encountered a trio of newoongalls while they traversed a cave in order to find their way to their hunting lodge. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Creatures of Rodia Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Species of undefined sentience
