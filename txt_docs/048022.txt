Hamogoni tree
1156

Hamogoni trees, native to the planet Woteba, were gigantic, often larger than skyscrapers. They had knee-roots around the size of a dewback, and enormous boughs like great green balconies. They were used as lumber by the Saras nest of Killiks when they occupied the planet.
