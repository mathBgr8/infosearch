New Year's Day was a Corellian holiday. On one occasion, eight years after they were taken down from public display, the Coronet City Museum of Fine Art gave a private showing of Chassu's Selonian nude studies for four hours, 10,000 credits per person. *"Side Trip" Category:Corellian holidays
