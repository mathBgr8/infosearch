|population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Eckless was a planet in Wild Space, considered to be an almost mythical world containing a great wealth of gems and metals. Eckless was a primitive world located in unexplored space, rich in ores, minerals, gems, and metals. It was home to a lost colony of humans. Eckless and its lost colonial civilization, which had no knowledge of the wider galactic community, were overseen by the planetary Council of Eckless, led by a President. Its people believed the Black Sphere was key to their existence. In time, the planet became fabled for its mineral wealth, reaching almost mythical status on other worlds. Hahz Fallone invaded Eckless, enslaving its people to mine their lands for minerals, using a fleet supplied by Jorga Sune. Fearing the destruction of their world, the natives stole weapons and forced Fallone to depart through strength of numbers, though he raided the planet's treasury and stole the Black Sphere before leaving. Famine lingered for at least twenty seasons after his departure. During the period of the Galactic Civil War, some twenty years after Fallone's devastation, Rusha served as diplomatic ambassador of Eckless.
