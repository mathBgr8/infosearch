Dindoo's Glory
1395

Dindoo's Glory was the personal starship of the Rodian entrepreneur Kelko. Once Kelko became rich, he named his ship as an homage to his long-dead stepfather and mentor, Dindoo, the one who had taught Kelko everything he knew. Kelko, a talented pilot, would take the Dindoo's Glory for short excursions with only Weedo, his old and trusty droid, as company, while leaving his wife, Shinba, and their children behind.
