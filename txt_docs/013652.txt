Torque
2472

Torque ("Torqua-na" in Huttese) was a planet located within the Torque system of the Outer Rim Territories and the sector capital of the Gordian Reach. A lackluster industrial world, it was the headquarters of Bwahl the Hutt. The planet served as an important commercial hub in the Gordian Reach, while its cities were known as one of the most busiest spaceports on the Junction-Tierell Loop hyperlane.
