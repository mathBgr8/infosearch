Krakana Current
863

The Krakana Current was the personal starship of Quarren Senator Tikkes, presumably a Mon Calamari model, during the Clone Wars. It was named after the fearsome krakana, a large, dangerous, carnivorous fish from Tikkes' homeworld of Mon Calamari. This may be the name of Tikkes's Mon Calamari ceremonial ship.
