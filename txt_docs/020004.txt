Shwock Dubllon
952

"Shwock Dubllon", known as "Crested Wake" in Basic, was a piece of classical music by Mon Calamari composer Dembaline. It was considered one of Dembaline's best performances, and was later covered by Gaeriel Capens using a TribBot mimic series droid in 22 BBY. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime Category:Mon Calamari culture Category:Musical compositions
