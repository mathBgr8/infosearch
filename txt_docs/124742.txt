Collaborator
1638

The Collaborator, originally the Hrosha-Gul (Price of Pain in the Yuuzhan Vong) was a Yuuzhan Vong Yorik-stronha picket ship analog. It was rechristened the Collaborator by Jaina Solo when it was captured for the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances. Solo, Tahiri Veila, Jagged Fel, and Arth Gxin made use of it during the Battle of Esfandia. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance Category:Yorik-stronha
