Boltthrower
960

The Boltthrower was an Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate. It was responding to an attack on a Cargo Transfer Area which the BFF-1 bulk freighter Libom, which carried new surveillance probes for the Relentless, was disabled. Despite assistance from the CR90 corvette Scorpion and the frigate's own TIE/IN interceptors and TIE/sa bombers, the freighter was captured and the Scorpion was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
