Nim Abek
1777

Nim Abek, whose real name was Nim'abek'shy-wedell, was a male Kajain'sa'Nikto pirate who worked for the Black Sun Vigo Sprax. Nim Abek began as a shipjacker in the Sisar Run until he stole a ship from Sprax's personal fleet in a fast, professional, and flawless way. Sprax appreciated the non-destructive method of stealing starships and arranged for Nim Abek to be captured just so he could offer him a job.
