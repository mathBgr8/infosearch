Yitheeth
2759

Yitheeth was an oceanic planet of the Yinchorri system. The Yinchorri built at least one city on its surface. In 33 BBY during the Yinchorri Uprising, it was the site of the Ambush on Yitheeth. Shallow seas covered 93% of its surface.
