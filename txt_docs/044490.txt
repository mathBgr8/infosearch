MetaCannon
960

The MetaCannon was a type of Chiss artillery weapon that was capable of firing a variety of projectiles into both orbit and onto the ground. The MetaCannon was one of the largest field artillery pieces that could be drop-deployed aboard the landing craft used by Chiss forces in the Swarm War. It was designed to fire charric maser beams, blaster bolts, or old-fashioned shell projectiles, perhaps including delivery systems for chemical and biological agents.
