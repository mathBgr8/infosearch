Ratambo Gale
1289

Ratambo Gale was a member of the Malkite Poisoners, which were adept assassins on the world of Chandrila. A skilled poisoner for more than four decades, Ratambo had never been caught, but some of his poisons were known to not have worked. He was hired by an unknown force to harass the governor of Hanna City, Jovive Centi.
