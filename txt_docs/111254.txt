Hallolar Voors
1483

Hallolar Voors was an entrepreneur on Jubilar. The son of a wealthy local businessman, Voors trafficked in spice, and was apparently able to find a source of the substance independent of the Hutts, who controlled the spice trade in the local sector. In 12 BBY, Jabba the Hutt put out a bounty on him to remove this competition; Boba Fett accepted the contract and killed Voors with a deadly neural toxin under the guise of being a buyer. *Star Wars: Boba Fett *Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Criminals Category:Humans Category:Males
