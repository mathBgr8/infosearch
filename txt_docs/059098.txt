Quality Electronics of Torina
797

Quality Electronics of Torina was a small company on the planet Torina notorious for its cheap knockoffs of normally expensive technological goods. They had offices and major retail outlets on Coyn, Lanthrym, Torina, and Elrood proper and their products were available through independent merchants throughout the Elrood sector. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Personal gear companies
