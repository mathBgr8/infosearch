Dunsenn FlopCarrier
1223

The Dunsenn FlopCarrier was a troop carrier used by the Gungan military to transport troops or evacuate civilians by air. It consisted of a seating block attached to the underside of a massive dunsenn, a fish-like, lighter-than-air creature. The passengers' compartment was covered by bubble-like canopies, actually hydrostatic fields, similar to those used on the bongo. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Air vehicle models Category:Gungan vehicle classes
