|feathers= |eyes=Black |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Bright Tree Village tribe |masters= |apprentices= }} Neddoo was an Ewok who lived on the Forest Moon of Endor. In 3 ABY, the Ewok Teebo, who was being affected by a loonee bird, threw a bundle of reeds at Neddoo's hut as the former was attempting to repair it, destroying the structure. Neddoo was angered by the move and went to Chief Chirpa to complain. Neddoo was an Ewok member of the Ewok tribe in Bright Tree Village on the Forest Moon of Endor. On at least one occasion, Neddoo's hut had needed to be repaired. In 3 ABY, dates the events of the Ewoks comic series, which include the events of Ewoks 7, to three years after the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which corresponds to 3 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology. Neddoo, who again needed to once again repair the hut, was approached by the young Ewok scout Teebo, who was under the influence of a loonee bird, which caused him to find everything around him hilarious and to play pranks. Neddoo was strengthening the home's support rods, explaining such to Teebo, and asked Teebo to help by passing a bundle of reeds. Instead of doing so, however, Teebo used a wagon to launch the bundle at a startled Neddoo, which caused Neddoo to crash into the house, destroying it. thumb|left|300px|Teebo laughs at Neddoo. Teebo laughed at Neddoo, who was shocked that the Ewok scout would do such a thing. It was then that Neddoo caught the effect of the loonee bird, and also began laughing at the plight, with Teebo encouraging such mirth. However, once Teebo left, the effect wore off, and Neddoo questioned laughing about it. Neddoo later showed up at the hut of Chief Chirpa along with several other victims of Teebo's pranks in order to complain. By that time, Neddoo's arm was in a sling, while explaining to Chirpa what happened to the hut. Shortly after, the Ewok medicine man Logray told everyone that Teebo was had been behaving strangely due to the loonee bird. Teebo considered Neddoo to be the "biggest sourpuss" in Bright Tree Village. Neddoo was angry at Teebo after the hut was destroyed, and questioned why he had pulled such a prank. After the effect of Teebo's laughter wore off, Neddoo could not understand what was funny. Neddoo then felt the need to complain about what Teebo had done to Chief Chirpa. Neddoo's fur was gray and blue, and the Ewok had black eyes. Neddoo also sported a white beard. Neddoo wore green clothing with a hood.
