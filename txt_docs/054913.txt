Quenfis
3982

The Quenfis was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that served with the New Republic Defense Fleet. thumb|left|200px|The Quenfis arrives at the location of the Katana fleet During the series of battles against Imperial Grand Admiral Thrawn that would become known as the Thrawn campaign, the Quenfis was assigned to the Home Guard Fleet. The vessel was placed under the command of Captain Sarin Virgilio by Borsk Fey'lya prior to its excursion to the Katana fleet, carrying Leia Organa Solo and Talon Karrde as well as Fey'lya himself. The frigate participated in the Battle for the Katana fleet that resulted when it arrived at the fleet's location just as an Imperial task force, including the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Judicator, arrived to reclaim the rest of the Dreadnaught-class heavy cruisers of the derelict force. *Dark Force Rising *Dark Force Rising 6 *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Essential Guide to Characters *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigates Category:Starships of the New Republic
