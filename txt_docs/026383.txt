Feynir
1616

Feynir was a male Draedan dictator who ruled one of the planet Sesid's major nations. A dictator of a major nation on the planet Sesid, the Draedan named Feynir was a proponent of the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Due to his close ties with Imperial Governor Arvel Trace, Feynir was able to arm his military with Imperial-grade weaponry and, after combining forces with neighboring dictator Maeril, the pair laid siege to surrounding nations.
