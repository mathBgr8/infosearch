Ammuud Swooper
2131

The Ammuud Swooper was a skyblue light Corellian Engineering Corporation YT-2400 light freighter which helped at the Battle of Borleias to evacuate the planet. It was heavily damaged and was repaired in the jungle of Borleias, while the Battle was going on. They could only escape with the help of Wedge Antilles, who was the last to leave the base. He defended the Ammuud Swooper with the heavily damaged T-65 X-wing starfighter Blackmoon 11, which was the only possibilty to escape.
