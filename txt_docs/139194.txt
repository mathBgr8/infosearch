The Insurrection of Iliabath was an insurrection in the vicinity of the Iliabath system sometime before 40 ABY. The victors of the conflict gave the Amulet of Kalara as a gift to Stonias Leem on Drewwa. *Legacy of the Force: Exile Category:Battles during the post-Imperial era Category:Rebellions and uprisings
