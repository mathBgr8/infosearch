Revenue
3172

The Revenue was a Lucrehulk-class LH-3210 cargo freighter commanded by Daultay Dofine. The rest of the crew included a Neimoidian navigator, Sullustan communications officer, Gran computer controller, and Ishi Tib treasurer. The ship's complement of security droids included OLR-4. The freighter was defended by equatorially-mounted turbolaser batteries and deflector shields. It was loading tons of Lommite ore at Dorvalla when it was attacked by the mercenary Arwen Cohl in name of the Nebula Front, a terrorist group fighting against the hold of the Trade Federation on Outer Rim worlds.
