Neuro-shock hand
1482

The neuro-shock hand, manufactured by BioTech Industries, was a cybernetic prosthetic limb. It was equipped with a neuro-repulsor field generator which delivered a neuro-shock to any organic being it touched. The hand functioned similarly to a standard prosthetic, and had full sensory response, meaning the user could feel sensations and pain through the hand.
