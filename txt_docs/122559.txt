The Adjudicator
938

The Adjudicator, or The Adjudicator Holofeed, was a trade publication for bounty hunters during the time of the Mandalorian Wars. It was edited by Krav Noblis. The Adjudicator is one of a number of "news organs" based on the Taris Holofeed that were included as extras in monthly issues of Knights of the Old Republic starting with issue 13. Adjudicator
