101378

This a timeline for short stories considered canon in the Legends continuity that have been published in the Star Wars Adventure Journal, the Star Wars Insider and other magazines as well as the internet. This list does not include documents, adventure/scenarios, or roleplaying-game (RPG) articles. This timeline is organized by chronology, and separated by era, as defined by Lucas Licensing.
