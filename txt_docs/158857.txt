Maria Brill
2074

|companies=Industrial Light & Magic |website= }} Maria Brill worked in the Visual Effects department and her likeness was used as the holographic image of Shaak Ti, which was the only canonical appearance of the character in Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. Brill is currently the Director of Virtual Production Operations at Industrial Light & Magic. She manages StageCraft operations worldwide.
