Brian Ching
5176

Brian Ching is an artist for the Dark Horse Comics. His work includes the Star Wars: Obsession series. After illustrating the launching issues of Knights of the Old Republic comic and then a select few afterward, began work on The Force Unleashed graphic novel. He was supposed to move on to the Rebellion comics though the series was canceled after being put on hold.
