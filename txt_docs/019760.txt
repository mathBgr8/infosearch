Bralor
2221

Bralor was a Human male Mandalorian Field Marshal and the champion of the Battle Circle at the Mandalorian base on Dxun following the Jedi Civil War. Although Bralor was an excellent fighter and champion of the Battle Circle, he was defeated in a duel with Meetra Surik. However, he considered this an honor, having seen the Jedi's prowess during the Mandalorian Wars, especially their victory at the Battle of Malachor V. At the battle, he had boarded a Republic cruiser and personally dueled a Jedi and although he lost, he was spared for his courage.
