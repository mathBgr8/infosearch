He was an assault specialist with training in the use of many heavy weapons, including the E-Web heavy repeating blaster. In 3 ABY, after the Empire discovered a hidden Rebel base on the planet Hoth,The New Essential Chronology RGA-972 participated in the subsequent ground assault against the Rebel Alliance and invaded the Rebel base. The battle ended with an Imperial victory and the Alliance evacuating Hoth. RGA-972 wore the standard suit of snowtrooper armor and wielded an E-11 blaster rifle.
