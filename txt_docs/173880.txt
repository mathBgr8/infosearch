Star Wars Weekly 111 is the one hundred eleventh issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 9, 1980. It included the original story "Unholy Alliance!". *"Unholy Alliance!" *"Heroes of the galaxy: Monark Starstalker Part 1" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 21: The Omni-Computer!" *"Tales of the Watcher: We search the stars!"
