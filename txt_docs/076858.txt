The Comestibles and Curatives Administration, or CCA, was a New Republic organization that routinely inspected food production companies and areas, to ensure that they met the proper standards. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:New Republic organizations
