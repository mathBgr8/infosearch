Those battles are dated to 0 BBY and 4 ABY, respectively, by The New Essential Chronology, which also establishes the Battle of Yavin as the final event before the beginning of 0 ABY. Composed in the Core Drive genre, the song was banned by the Imperial Board of Culture, who considered the track to be anti-Imperial. "Blow 'em All Away" was mentioned in the 1993 book Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim, a supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Simon Smith and Eric Trautmann. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Songs by Boba Fett and the Assassin Droids
