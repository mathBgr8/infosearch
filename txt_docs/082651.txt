By that time, Bobissia had become a regular at the eatery Dex's Diner,. and one day outside of the diner, the Triffian chatted with a Nuknog named Slyther Bushforb. Ony Bobissia was created for the 2002 film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, where the character appears very briefly. Bobissia's digital model was a retooling of the digital model used for the character Ebe E.
