Shadow (Muurian transport)
925

The Shadow was a Muurian transport used by loyal Imperial forces to install and activate CR90 corvette Vorknkx hyperdrive, as well as evacuate the research staff from platforms V1 and V2. The research facility was under heavy assault by rogue Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's traitorous forces, attempting to steal the new technology.
