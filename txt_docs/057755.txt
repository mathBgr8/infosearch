Galacticon Insurance
922

Galacticon Insurance was a company that sold insurance. Galacticon Insurance was removed when Riders of the Maelstrom was reprinted in Classic Adventures: Volume Three. The reference was replaced with a mention of generic insurance payments, rather than identifying a specific company. *Riders of the Maelstrom Category:Insurance companies
