Baintorf
1948

Baintorf was a New Republic Defense Force officer. During 16 ABY, Baintorf held rank of General, commanding the units responsible for Coruscant's planetary shield. When the Yevethan thrustship Aramadia broke off negotiations with the New Republic and made an unauthorized launch to return to N'zoth, Admiral Gial Ackbar ordered Baintorf to raise the planetary shield to prevent the Aramadia passage and for the Nebula-class Star Destroyer Brilliant to shadow the Aramadia while it held position underneath the shield.
