Sulituan
1218

Sulituans were a species with nautilus-like heads and shells with long tentacles that served as arms and legs. They lived on Archae Teuthis. The Podracer Tzidik Wrantojo was a Sulituan. "Sulituan" is "nautilus" spelled backward, letter for letter. "Architeuthis" is the genus name of the Giant Squid. *Star Wars: Racer Revenge *Star Wars: Racer Revenge: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Cephalopods Category:Expansion Region sentient species
