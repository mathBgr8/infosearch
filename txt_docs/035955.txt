Clone Scout Walker
1661

The Clone Scout Walker was a quadrupedal war walker used by clone troopers in the Clone Wars. There was also a heavier version of the scout walker. The LEGO company created an AT-RT LEGO set for the release of Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith.
