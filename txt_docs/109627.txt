Vehicle Maintenance Energizer
1632

thumb|right|250px|Vehicle Maintenance Energizer The Vehicle Maintenance Energizer was a device used to maintain starships and vehicles. It also provided ships with energy. The Rebel Alliance used such devices on Yavin 4. This device still goes by the name given to it by Kenner when they released it as an accessory in their Vintage line in 1982. It came with 8 Hydrospanners and 2 energy hoses.
