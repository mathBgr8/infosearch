Adamant (bulk cruiser)
2428

The Adamant was a New Republic bulk cruiser under the command of Admiral Gial Ackbar. In 23 ABY, she fell under attack by an Imperial insurgency group known as the Second Imperium in the Coruscant system while transporting hyperdrive cores and turbolaser emplacements to Kuat Drive Yards. This raid was lead by ex-TIE pilot Qorl under the command of the fallen Jedi Brakiss who operated the Shadow Academy, an ally of the Imperium.
