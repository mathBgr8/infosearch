Unidentified Korriban ground crewman
886

This unnamed Human was a member of the Korriban spaceport's ground crew around 40 ABY. Along with the Bothan Yas'tua and the Barabel Rak'k, he met Alema Rar when she landed at the spaceport in search of the new Sith Order. When he refused to reveal their location, fearing for his life, Alema crushed his jaw using the Force and sent him stumbling into the port-master's office. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno Category:Humans Category:Males
