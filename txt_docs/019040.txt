|pages=22 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Infinities |issue=3 }} Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope 3 is the third issue in the Star Wars Infinities: A New Hope series of comics. It was published on September 19, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. In an altered Star Wars timeline, the Death Star is never destroyed and the Rebellion is seemingly crushed. The Empire's strong presence throughout the galaxy leads to the formation of a new Senate, filled with Imperial puppets. Han Solo and Chewbacca watch in amazement as the newest Senator from the planet Coruscant, the Imperial Throneworld, is announced - Leia Organa! Hope springs on Dagobah, as Luke Skywalker nears the completion of his training with Jedi Master Yoda. But can any amount of training prepare him for the combined dark forces of the Emperor, Darth Vader, and Leia, Vader's new apprentice?
