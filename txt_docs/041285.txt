|gender= |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Black |skin=Brown |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Ror Ithh was an unsavory Ithorian cantina crawler and carouser who frequented the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars: Complete Locations Coruscant, in the years surrounding the First Battle of Geonosis of 22 BBY. During a night in that year,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Ithh was visiting the club, just as bounty hunter Zam Wesell ran into the club to hide after her assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala. Ithh stood next to the Gran gambler Nic Whoma as Jedi Padawan Anakin Skywalker searched for Wesell. When Skywalker surveyed the crowd, Ithh interacted with two Rodians. Ithh wore dark robes while at the club and had brown skin and black eyes. Ithh had a male first name that meant "strong, mighty." The carouser's surname was an Ithorian family name that meant "soil," specifically rich and growing soil.Galactic Campaign Guide }} Ror Ithh first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The Outlander Club scenes, in which the character was played by an uncredited extra, were filmed at Fox Studios, Sydney, on July 17, 2001.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones The name Ror Ithh first appeared in the May 2, 2003, web article "Cantina Roll-Call: Shedding Light on Some Alien Aliases," where it was applied to the Ithorian Momaw Nadon. The name was first applied to the Ithorian appearing in Episode II through the StarWars.com Image Attack feature on November 6, 2003.
