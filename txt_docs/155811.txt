Hidu Rezodar
910

Hidu Rezodar was a gangster from Phaeda who imprisoned bounty hunter Sintas Vel in carbonite. Rezodar was killed shortly after Sintas' imprisonment, and the slab of carbonite containing Sintas Vel was held in storage by Phaedan authorities for almost forty years. *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Legacy of the Force: Revelation *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals
