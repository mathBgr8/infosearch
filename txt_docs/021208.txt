De-Purteen
1351

De-Purteen was the capital city of Ord Cantrell and hosted many famous resorts. Following the final death of Emperor Palpatine in 11 ABY, De-Purteen served as the capital of the Crimson Empire, which was controlled at that time by the Imperial Ruling Council. *Star Tours: The Adventures Continue *Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 1 *Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 2 *Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital cities Category:Galactic Empire cities
