|subscribers= |pages=64 |isbn= |issn=1081-1362 |genre= |issue=12 |series=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine }} Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 12 is the twelfth issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in August 1997. This issue's theme focused on Princess Leia. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 12, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with a Topps Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition Widevision promotional trading card.
