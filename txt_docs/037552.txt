Enemy Eradication Order of Coruscant
1026

The Enemy Eradication Order of Coruscant was an decree issued by Emperor Palpatine not long after the formation of the Galactic Empire. The order specifically targeted those who served the Galactic Republic. It was originally a supervision tactic, to keep track of enemies. Those who fell into the category of being watched had to report to an Imperial officer every week. Before long, however, the order allowed targets to be arrested or sentenced to execution. There were those who were able to make themselves "disappear" from Imperial files, by erasing their identification documents.
