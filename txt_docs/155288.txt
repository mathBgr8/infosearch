Mount Ste'vshuulsz
838

Mount Ste'vshuulsz was a jagged mountain located on the dark, murky planet Barab I in the Outer Rim Territories. Sheer slopes of shale rock, the mountain was home to the wild skotcarps. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Barab I locations Ste'vshuulsz
