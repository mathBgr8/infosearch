Itani Run
1844

The Itani Run was a hyperlane that bypassed the Itani Nebula. This Itani Run was blazed by Freia Kallea in 3703 and 3702 BBY. It was the first line which crossed the Itani Nebula. The route was first integrated into the Hydian Way but became an independent route after a new segment was discovered in the nebula.
