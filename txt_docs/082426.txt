*Timmser |subgroup= |headquarters= |locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Khuiumin Survivors *Invids }} Rock Squadron was the lowest-ranked of the five Khuiumin Survivors starfighter squadrons. Its commander was originally Nakk Kech, but upon his death command was turned over to Corran Horn who was posing as Jenos Idanian at the time. Upon Horn's transfer to Bolt command of Rock Squadron was given to Timmser. Other members included Caet Shrovl, Blook, and Yander.
