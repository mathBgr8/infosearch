Brendan Holmes
654

Brendan Holmes voiced the Mobile artillery officer in Star Wars: Force Commander and the Droid Eye, Stormtrooper and Intercom in Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith. Holmes is also credited as an additional programmer for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.
