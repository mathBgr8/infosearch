Pordimer
2530

}} Pordimer, often referred to by the name Pordy, was a thin, brown-haired Ugnaught that lived in the artificial city known as Ugnorgrad, one kilometer below Cloud City on the planet Bespin. Pordimer was employed as a cameraman working for Ars Fivvle on the news media program known as Action Tidings. Three years after the Battle of Yavin, Pordy was present when former Cloud City Administrator Lando Calrissian returned to Bespin to destabilize the Imperial presence in Cloud City. Pordimer and the rest of the Action Tidings news crew went to Cloud City and witnessed Rebel agents Luke Skywalker, Shira Brie, Calrissian and Lobot take control of the city away from Imperial Captain Hugo Treece. *Star Wars (1977) 57 *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Journalists Category:Males Category:Ugnaughts
