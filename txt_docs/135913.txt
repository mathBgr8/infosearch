Terkuss
1890

Terkuss was a corporal in the Imperial Army under General Freja Covell. He originally resisted being drafted, only acquiescing when the Imperials threatened to raze his home. However, Terkuss was a bully at heart, and soon grew to enjoy serving as an Imperial soldier.
