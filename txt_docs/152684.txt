J12 twin-pod airspeeder
1494

The J12 twin-pod airspeeder was used, and first produced, on Cloud City. City security used it for minor law enforcement, such as dealing with landing regulations. The J12 was also popular during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, especially on Coruscant.
