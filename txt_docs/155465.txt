Corps Commander
1230

The Corps Commander was a variant of Xim's war-robots. The Corps Commanders were designed with greater independence than their counterparts and could be distinguished by a golden death-head emblem on their breastplates in contrast to the white born by standard models. They saw action during the Xim Wars. Despite being outdated by modern era standards, they were still deadly opponents in combat.
