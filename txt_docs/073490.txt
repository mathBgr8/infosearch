DFS-1VR first appeared in , the first film in the Prequel trilogy of the Star Wars film franchise. The droid starfighter was subsequently identified in the Sith Rising expansion for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Trading Card Game, receiving its own card in the 2002 expansion.
