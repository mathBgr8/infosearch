Hoggon's starship
1465

Hoggon's starship was an ungainly looking transport owned by spacer Hoggon. He used it to transport Ulic Qel-Droma to first Yavin 4 and then Rhen Var. Hoggon then flew in it to Exis Station, where Vima Sunrider stowed away on the ship. Upon discovering her, Hoggon took her to Rhen Var. Hoggon later used the ship to take Sylvar to Rhen Var. *Tales of the Jedi – Redemption 1 *Tales of the Jedi – Redemption 5 Category:Transports
