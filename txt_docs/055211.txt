Domain Karsh
928

Domain Karsh was a Domain among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Notable members of Domain Karsh included Supreme Commander Komm Karsh and Commander Hreven Karsh. During the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar, Domain Karsh remained loyal to Supreme Overlord Shimrra, and their ships were part of the flotilla that was sent to attack Zonama Sekot. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Karsh
