Umber Banks
844

|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} The Umber Banks were located near some plains of planet Bresnan, on Wellte-ir system. The Umber Banks were the battlefield of the Wellte-ir Massacre, where fifteen of the Rebel Scandium Team members were killed. Scandium member Aven Cholus rescued the other five survivors.
