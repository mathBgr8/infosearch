Poesy
2740

The Poesy was a Mon Calamari light cruiser. Its armament consisted of 14 turbolasers, 18 ion cannons, 6 heavy tractor beam projectors and powerful shields. It also was equipped with four starfighter squadrons. The Poesy was under the command of Mon Calamari Commodore Mabettye and served as the flagship of the New Republic/Duros fleet at the Battle of Duro during the Yuuzhan Vong War in 26 ABY.
