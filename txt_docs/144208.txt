The Tanglewoods
506

The Tanglewoods were a forest in Bellassa. It was south of Ussa and the Empire had trouble maintaining patrols there. Because of that, the Eleven kept a secret route functional through the wood to Ussa, using holographic displays to hide it. *The Last of the Jedi: Secret Weapon *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Forests and woods
