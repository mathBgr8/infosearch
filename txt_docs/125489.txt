Fillin Ta
1486

Fillin Ta was the male Whiphid co-owner of Gep's Grill in Mos Eisley. He met Norun Gep, his partner, in jail when they were street urchins. They remained friends even since. Usually the two Whiphids got the meat for their business from hunters, but when the hunts went bad, the local stables were known to get a visit from two Whiphids.
