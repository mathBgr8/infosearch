|published in=Star Wars Tales 10 |reprinted in= |pages=10 |timeline=4 ABY |series= |preceded by="Nameless" |followed by="Free Memory" }} "A Wookiee Scorned!" is a comic story that appeared in Star Wars Tales 10 and was collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 3 and Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: The New Republic Vol. 1.. The story was written by Jason Hall, drawn by Christina Chen, colored by Dan Jackson, lettered by Steve Dutro and edited by Scott Allie. Chewbacca feels that he isn't being very much appreciated by Han who is in love with Leia.
