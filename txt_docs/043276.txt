Sena Leikvold Midanyl
3346

Sena Leikvold Midanyl was a Human female from Corellia. During the time of the Galactic Republic, Midanyl became chief aide of Senator Garm Bel Iblis of Corellia, and continued in this role after he joined the Rebel Alliance. After Bel Iblis' split from Mon Mothma, she helped Bel Iblis and Irenez plan their attacks against Imperial forces. Although Midanyl harbored no love for Mothma, she eventually realized the Chandrilan woman did not desire to be a new Palpatine, and was the one who suggested that Han Solo request Bel Iblis to rejoin his old counterpart, knowing that he would never go back on his own. At some point in her life, she bore two sons, Peter and Dayvid.
