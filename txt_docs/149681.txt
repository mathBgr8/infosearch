Ty Matthews
1595

Ty Matthews was a young male Human Lieutenant who served in the Alliance to Restore the Republic around the time of the Bakura Truce in 4 ABY. He joined the Rebellion on his fifteenth birthday. His father was Colonel Tev Matthews. Ty Matthews served as chief aide in Admiral Ackbar's war room aboard Home One.
