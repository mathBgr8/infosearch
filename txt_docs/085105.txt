In 40 ABY, Haxan was taken hostage by his colleague, Professor Movac Arisster. Taking Haxan atop the aquarium of the Lorrd Academy for Aquatic Studies, Arisster strapped Haxan to his back, threatening to blow the aquarium up if he did not talk to Nelani Dinn, the Jedi Knight stationed onplanet.
