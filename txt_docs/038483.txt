James Floyd
7369

James Floyd is a freelance writer, and author of Star Wars Crossword Puzzles: And Other Word Games from a Galaxy Far, Far Away. . . .. He has written for Star Wars Insider since 2018 and the official Star Wars website since 2012. He also co-contributed to the backstory of Melee appearing in the Databank, and also provided the first name of Barrow Oicunn, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club.
