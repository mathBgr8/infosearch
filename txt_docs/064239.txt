Bill Black
732

Bill Black was the publisher, editor, Chairman of the Board, and founder of AC Comics (original Americomics). He was an inker for several issues of Dark Horse Comics, including Tales of the Jedi: The Golden Age of the Sith (TPB), Tales of the Jedi: The Fall of the Sith Empire, Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi &mdash; The Sith War, and Jedi Academy: Leviathan.
