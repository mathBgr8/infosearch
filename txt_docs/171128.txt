Poly Pyramid
1389

The Poly Pyramid was one of many inebriation emporiums in Teguta Lusat on Rafa IV. It was visited mostly by miners and once by Lando Calrissian in 4 BBY. Lando's droid companion at the time, Vuffi Raa, had to wait outside because droids were not allowed inside. It had a bar running down the left side of the building with booths lining the opposite wall. On the walls hung paintings and scenes from sporting events from around the galaxy. *Lando Calrissian and the Mindharp of Sharu *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Outer Rim Territories structures
