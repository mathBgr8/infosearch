In 137 ABY, he met with Admiral Gar Stazi at the Wheel for negotiations of an alliance with the Galactic Alliance Remnant. However, a plot by Morrigan Corde and Jor Torlin led to the two factions firing upon each other, and the proposed alliance was dead before it even began. Bovark was attacked by Stazi with a knife, but was saved by Imperial Knights Antares Draco and Ganner Krieg.
