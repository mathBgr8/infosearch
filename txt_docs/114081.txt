Dobbatek
1352

Dobbatek was an Imperial officer from Black Sword Command, taken prisoner by the Yevetha in 4 ABY. Dobbatek managed to escape from the Yevetha, and in 17 ABY, he was one of the senior officers working with Major Sil Sorannan's secret escape plan. He was charged with activating the secret slave circuitry aboard one of the captured Imperial vessels being used as command ships by the Duskhan League, thereby seizing one of the squadrons of Yevethan-controlled Star Destroyers back for the Empire, and taking them to the Deep Core. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy officers of the Galactic Empire Category:Males
