Bosmihi
1520

Lord Superior Bosmihi was the leader of the Kroctari people, and their government, the Unified Factions. Bosmihi was one of the first to make contact with Grand Admiral Thrawn, after what seemed to be his return from the dead. What Bosmihi did not know was that Thrawn was still dead and the return of Thrawn was really a cleverly orchestrated operation lead by Grodin Tierce and Moff Vilim Disra, and played by Flim, who looked remarkably like Thrawn. *Vision of the Future *Vision of the Future abridged audiobook Category:Heads of government and state Category:Kroctari Category:Males
