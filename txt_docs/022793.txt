Luna-weed
1156

Luna-weed was a plant native to Aduba-3. If chewed, it could produce delusional effects in the user. It gave rise to the phrase "chewing the luna-weed" when referring to someone who was acting crazy. *Star Wars (1977) 9 *Star Wars (1977) 10 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hallucinogens Category:Pharmaceutical plants
