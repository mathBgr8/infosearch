Unidentified Jedi Master (Gran)
2146

This individual was a Gran Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as a member of the Jedi High Council during the Clone Wars. A Force-sensitive Gran was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. During the Clone Wars, a conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, the Gran was appointed as a member of the Jedi High Council, the governing body of the Order. During the Gran's time on the Council, the Kaleesh cyborg General Grievous was revealed to be the Supreme Commander of the Droid Armies.
