MCS
1079

MCS was a computer technology company that focused on the manufacturing of mainframe units. While the Galactic War between the reconstituted Sith Empire and the Galactic Republic took place, the firm arranged a competition for slicers. With this contest, MCS tried to figure out which one of the competitors could dismantle their newest and best product the quickest. It was probably a marketing-sketch for MCS to prove that their products are unsliceable. During the early days of the Galactic Empire, it went out of business. *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Shadow of Revan *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Computer and software companies
