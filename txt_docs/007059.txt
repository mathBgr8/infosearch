|pages=22 |upc= |timeline=c. 15 BBY |series=Star Wars: Droids (1986) |issue=3 }} Droids 3 is the third issue of the Star Wars: Droids series of comics. The story is split into two chapters, "The Scarlet Pirate Part 1" and "The Scarlet Pirate Part 2: End of the Game." The issue was published on May 13, 1986 by Marvel Comics. C-3PO and R2-D2 are purchased by Ambassador Zell, who wants the droids to look after his son, Llez, while he's away on business.
