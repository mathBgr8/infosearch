Premier Provisions

|role=Foodstuff manufacturer |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic }} Premier Provisions was a small company that operated out of a orbital ranch on Tyed Kant. In addition, the company owned a protocol droid named J-3PA. Premier Provisions specialized in the production of unique and specialized foods. The company's headquarters was also the location of a fairly advanced Alliance listening post. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Foodstuff manufacturers
