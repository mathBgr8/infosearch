Mawrunner
1579

The Mawrunner was a frigate assigned to the Second Fleet. It was active during the Second Galactic Civil War. The frigate, along with fleet admiral Tarla Limpan and a portion of the Second fleet, were destroyed by a blast from the Centerpoint Station. Tiom Rordan, a starfighter pilot and love interest to Syal Antilles, perished during the battle. *Legacy of the Force: Fury *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
