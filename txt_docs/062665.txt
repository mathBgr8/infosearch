Selonian Fire
1917

Selonian Fire was a New Republic Victory I-class Star Destroyer that participated in the Battle of Ciutric, which occurred in 9 ABY, under the command of General Garm Bel Iblis. Having received a signal from Admiral Ackbar, the Selonian Fire performed the Thrawn Pincer, reverting into real space behind enemy lines, along with the Corusca Fire, another New Republic Victory I-class Star Destroyer. With their combined firepower, the two Star Destroyers managed to destroy the bridge of Prince-Admiral Delak Krennel's flagship, the Reckoning, killing Krennel in the process. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge Category:Starships of the New Republic Category:Victory I-class Star Destroyers
