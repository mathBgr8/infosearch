Vagabond Registration Office
1176

The Vagabond Registration Office was a branch of the Mon Calamari government. From it's name it can be assumed that its purpose was to register immigrants from other planets that did not have a permanent residence. Around 1 BBY, the Vagabond Registration Office was responsible for taking the details of Illyan Webble, a vagrant that had traveled to the planet from Kalkovak. The office recorded when Webble eventually stole a skiff and piloted it out into the middle of one of Mon Calamari's oceans.
