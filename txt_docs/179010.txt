thumb|250px|[[Wicket Wystri Warrick/Legends|Wicket's monmon seeds in a bag on a Mooth's table.]] The monmon seed was a special seed from Endor. When properly collected and polished, they made for a lucrative trade item. Wicket Wystri Warrick prepared such a supply for the annual village expedtion to Mooth's trading post. Mooth was interested enough to offer the young Ewok a "lucky" stone, which he gave to his loved sweetheart, Princess Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka. *Droids\Ewoks Category:Edible nuts and seeds
