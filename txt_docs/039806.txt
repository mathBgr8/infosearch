Dace Bonearm
2671

Dace Bonearm was a male Human bounty hunter active in the Outer Rim Territories during the height of the Galactic Civil War. Dace would frequently associate with the assassin droid IG-72. Bonearm and IG-72 stole the HWK-290 light freighter Moldy Crow from Grappa the Hutt, using it as a decoy in a ploy to claim the Imperial bounty on Rebel commander Winfrid Dagore, before abandoning the Crow on the planet Teth. In 0 BBY, Dace and IG-72 both accepted bounties from Jabba the Hutt in his townhouse in Mos Eisley, meeting with him immediately after Greedo accepted the bounty on Han Solo. *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bounty hunters Category:Humans Category:Males
