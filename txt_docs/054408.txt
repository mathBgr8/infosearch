Tol (gambler)
583

Tol was a gambler who was found on the Ananuru Express when it was auctioning a secret Imperial brainwashing virus. He, along with Jemmy and Chral, knew nothing about the auction and were just there to win money from rich people. He was pleasant and amiable and when a fight broke out he simply stayed under cover.
