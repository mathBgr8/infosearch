In March 2008, it was archived on Hyperspace. It was also included in a Czech-language anthology Setkání v mlze. In the midst of the Clone Wars, a Republic Intelligence agent called Edbit Teeks goes missing on the world of Tarhassan, which has recently declared itself for the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
