|result= |side1=Galactic Republic |side2=Unidentified Anti-Republic force |side3= |side4= |commanders1=Jedi General Roan Shryne |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |forces1=*At least two Jedi |forces2= |forces3= |forces4= |casual1=*Roan Shryne's Padawan killed |casual2= |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} The Battle of Manari was fought in 19.5 BBY as part of the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and an anti-Republic force on the Expansion Region planet Manari. Jedi General Roan Shryne was one of the combatants present, and his second Padawan died during the fighting. As a result of his Padawan's death, Shryne changed, becoming disillusioned about the war and losing hope in the Jedi Council.
