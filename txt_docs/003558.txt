Bnach
2938

}} Bnach was a hot, desolate prison planet that housed an global Imperial penal camp, and was renowned for its deadly rock quarries. Prisoners forced to labor in these quarries were said to survive no more than a year. Aging Corellian smuggler Toob Ancher, while suffering from the deleterious mental effects of Bitter Winter, claimed to Drake Paulsen that he and Drake's father Kaine had a shootout with Sector Police on Bnach, though Drake was quick to point out that Bnach was an Imperial prison where no one went.
