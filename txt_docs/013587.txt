Tangrene was a craggy mountainous world, almost completely devoid of surface vegetation and water. The local day lasted 29 standard hours; the local year 356 standard days. Tangrene indigenous species were peaceful nomads. During the Clone Wars, Tangrene was located deep and secured within the borders Separatist space. The Galactic Empire made Tangrene one of its most heavily defended fortress worlds. It was defended by two Imperial-class Star Destroyers and from ten to fifty other smaller ships, ranging from small Carrack-class cruisers to big Victory-class Star Destroyers. Fifteen Imperial garrisons were scattered throughout the planet. The indigenous species was enslaved by Imperial forces and forced to help construct the Imperial fortifications. They were eventually wiped out by the local Moff to avoid any risk of insurrection. It was the location of an Imperial Ubiqtorate center attacked by Garm Bel Iblis after his split with Mon Mothma. During the last stages of the Thrawn campaign, Admiral Ackbar intended to fool the Imperial forces by sending a fleet at Tangrene while he attacked the Bilbringi Shipyards.
