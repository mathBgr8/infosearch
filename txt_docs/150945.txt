Loran Llalik

Loran Llalik was a Human female scientist and doctor who lived on the planet Plavin 6 with her family. Llalik's family included her husband, Doctor Nash Llalik, and their three children: An oldest son, Doctor Nashiak Llalik, and Rebel agent Saren Llalik. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Scientists
