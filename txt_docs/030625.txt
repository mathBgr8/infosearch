|line= |model=KPR security droid |class= |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |pronouns= |sensor= |plating=Green |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=Jabba's criminal empire }} 2X-7KPR ("Tooex") was a green domed KPR security droid operational during the Galactic Civil War. It was capable of monitoring floodlights and perimeter sensors as well as maintaining security devices. The droid was originally owned by Jabba the Hutt until it was stolen, ending up in the possession of the Jawa tribe led by Nebit on the planet Tatooine. In 0 BBY,The Essential Chronology dates the events of to 0 BBY. Nebit's tribe stopped at the Lars homestead. Though the droid's sensor package still worked at the time, its treads were faulty and needed the Jawas help to move. 2X-7KPR was offered for sale to the moisture farmer Owen Lars.
