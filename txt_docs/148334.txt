B22 Hold-Out Blaster
1805

The B22 Hold-Out Blaster was a hold-out blaster designed by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. for Imperial political agents. Its special handgrip allowed easy concealment and quick draws from a pocket. The B22 Hold-Out Blaster was first mentioned and illustrated in the 1997 roleplaying supplement Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear by West End Games. *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear *Arms & Equipment Guide Category:Hold-out blasters Category:Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. products
