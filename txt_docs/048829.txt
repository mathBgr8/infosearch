The Unseen Planets of Episode II
2568

|pages= |published in=Star Wars Insider 80 |reprinted in= }} "The Unseen Planets of Episode II" was an article written by Daniel Wallace for Star Wars Insider 80, which featured five planets (and moons) that were only in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones through dialogue. Note: One of them, Shadda-Bi-Boran was only inferred to, not heard, and in a deleted scene of the film. These are five worlds were explored in the article. They are accompanied by the quote that mentioned them in the film. *Ansion - "That's possible, he's just returned from a border dispute on Ansion." - Mace Windu *Shadda-Bi-Boran - Inferred "That was when I went with a relief group." - Padmé Amidala *Bogden - "I was recruited by a man called Tyranus on one of the moons of Bogden." - Jango Fett *Subterrel - "I ain't seen one of these since I was prospectin' on Subterrel, beyond the Outer Rim." - Dexter Jettster *Rishi - "I'd say about um, twelve parsecs outside the Rishi Maze." - Dexter Jettster *Star Wars Insider 80 Category:Star Wars Insider 80 articles
