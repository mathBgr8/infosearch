Gizka poison
753

Gizka poison were small, poison pellets that were used for eliminating gizka. These pellets were very toxic and could kill a gizka in a matter of seconds. They would also drive the creatures insane, causing them to attack other gizka in the area, infecting them as well and ultimately killing all of them. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Poisons
