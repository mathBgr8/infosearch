thumb|right|150px|Portrait of Scott Allie Scott Allie has written for Dark Horse comics in the Star Wars: Empire, Star Wars: Republic and Star Wars Tales series. Scott Allie is from Ipswich, Massachusetts and now lives in Portland, Oregon with his wife Myndi and their phantom cat, Shadow. He writes and edits for Dark Horse Comics and Glimmer Train Press. His non&ndash;Star Wars credits include the horror comic The Devil's Footprints, tributes to H.P. Lovecraft, contributions to Planet of the Apes, and a series of self-published horror comics called Sick Smiles, from Aiiie! Comics. {| !Title !! Format !!
