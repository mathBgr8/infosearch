Gra'aton
2771

Gra'aton was a Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order. A Force-sensitive trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, Gra'aton achieved the rank of Jedi Master circa 400 BBY. Gra'aton studied as a Consular, specializing in the field of diplomacy and becoming a well-respected diplomat. On a mission assigned by the Jedi High Council, Gra'aton was part of a relief effort to Dathomir that included fellow Masters Vulatan and Yoda, as well as at least fourteen Padawans. Attempting to salvage the wreck of the lost praxeum ship, the Chu'unthor, the Jedi were met with the Witches of Dathomir, who guarded the wreck from the Jedi.
