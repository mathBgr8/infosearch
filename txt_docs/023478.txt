Steve Dutro
2976

Steve Dutro is a comic-book letterer. He began his career at Marvel Comics in 1991. Besides Star Wars, Dutro's work includes titles such as Spider-Man, Fantastic Four, Superman, and various other books for Marvel, DC Comics, Image, and Dark Horse Comics.
