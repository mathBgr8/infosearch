Meir wine
611

Meir wine was an intoxicant made on Sullust. Renna had just starting importing Meir wine to the Cularin system when a cargo of it was hijacked by pirates, along with speeder parts. When the goods were recovered from the pirates, Renna let the people responsible keep a few bottles of the wine as a reward. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Wines
