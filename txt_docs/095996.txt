C-9969 landing craft
882

The C-9969 landing craft was a modified version of the C-9979 landing craft. The C-9969 had a larger landing pedestal than the C-9979, allowing it to carry 12 MTTs, rather than eleven. It also had widened and elongated wings to fit 120 AATs, rather than 114, and 30 Trade Federation troop carriers, rather than 28.
