He was present at the Battle of Restuss, and was in charge of placing antennas in the ruins of the city. He was eventually killed by a group of Imperial soldiers under order from Captain Okto. Vrinko appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Captain Vrinko was added to the game along with the "Battle of Restuss" PvP battlefield, introduced in the "Chapter 1.1" released on June 27, 2006.
