Sala Natu
1227

Sala Natu was an officer who served with the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. Stationed at Echo Base on Hoth at the time of the Imperial assault on that world, he was a specialist who possessed expertise in survival in cold environments.
