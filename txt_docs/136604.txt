Gara
1827

Gara was a Jedi of the Jedi Order during the Republic Era. Alongside Grand Master Yoda, Gara briefed the Jedi Mace Windu on the creation of coaxium ultra, a new form of coaxium, on the moon Ro Mira and the consequences if it were to fall into the hands of criminal organizations.
