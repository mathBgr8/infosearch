Terbeast
1206

The terbeast was a three headed canine creature that roamed the forests of Ogden Minor, terrorizing the locals. The two Jedi Aayla Secura and Quinlan Vos were sent to kill the creatures. Terbeast were large three headed canine species that were very aggressive in nature. Their bodies resembled that of a bear but their heads were more akin to the look of a canine.
