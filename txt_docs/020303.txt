Summerside Tour
756

The Summerside Tour was the name for a concert tour by jatz musician Fitz Roi, sponsored by fast food chain Flangth-2-Go. The event was preempted shortly before the Battle of Yavin after Roi used his antique slugthrowers to shoot numerous historical artistic artifacts in the Teirra Parko's Crystal Room on Rimma.
