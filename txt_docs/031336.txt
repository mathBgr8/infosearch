Peche
2536

thumb|left|200px|The villages of Theenes and Peche reunited by Leia Organa. Peche was a settlement on Krant. It was situated to the west across a large chasm from the city of Theenes, with the two cities connected by three sturdy bridges. Peche included a Command center, a Research Center, prefab shelters, and farms inside its walls. In 0 ABY, Peche and Theenees were threatened with extermination when the Imperials set up two military camps, Sagol and Kaxol to destroy Rebel operations in Peche and Theenes by breaking the bridges that separated them from the mainland. However, Rebel forces led by Princess Leia Organa and the Jedi Echuu Shen-Jon launched an attack from a nearby Krantian village and destroyed one of the Imperial camps, forcing the other to withdraw. Peche and Theenes subsequently united and agreed to assist the Rebel Alliance. Peche appears in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds during the Leia Organa campaign. The campaign is also included in the game's 2002 expansion pack Clone Campaigns. The player has the option of destroying either Sagol or Kaxol, and as the choice is a matter of game mechanics, this article is neutral to that choice.
