Hrosma tiger
993

|habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Hrosma tiger was a large feline predator with a horn on its forehead. It was native to Baskarn. It was Force-sensitive and thus a very dangerous and ferocious hunter. Hrosma tigers had long life-spans but were rare in numbers as they often fought and killed each other in competition for territory and prey.
