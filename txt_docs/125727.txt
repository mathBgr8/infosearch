Ion double blaster
754

The ion double blaster was a form of ion weapon used by the B1-series battle droid owned by Wittin during Jabba the Hutt's demolition contest. When charged up, it had the capacity to disable anything from a rancor to a T-47 snowspeeder in a single shot, but would have to be charged up again after every shot. *Star Wars: Demolition *Star Wars: Demolition: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Ion weaponry
