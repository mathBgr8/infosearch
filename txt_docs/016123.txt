Executor (Imperial rank)
2275

The Executor was the person appointed by Emperor Palpatine to serve as Commander-in-Chief of the Galactic Empire's forces on behalf of the Emperor. The position was also referred to as Imperial Executor, Military Executor, Darkside Executor, or a number of combinations of the three. When Emperor Palpatine was "reborn" in clone bodies and led a renewed assault against the New Republic in 10&ndash;11 ABY, he named Sedriss as his new Executor.
