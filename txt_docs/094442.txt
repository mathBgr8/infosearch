Drace-2 Alert
505

A Drace-2 Alert was a battle readiness alert to inform the Chiss of a military expedition or to prepare for a rescue mission. This was usually initiated when there was a need to rescue members of the expedition that were trapped either on a damaged ship or a ship that was in danger of exploding. *Survivor's Quest Category:Chiss Ascendancy military codes
