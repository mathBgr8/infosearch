Inadi (captain)
1455

Inadi was a female officer who served in the New Republic Defense Forces during the Galactic Civil War and the Black Fleet Crisis. Having attained the rank of Captain, she was the commander of the Warrior-class gunship Vanguard when the warship participated in the Battle of ILC-905.
