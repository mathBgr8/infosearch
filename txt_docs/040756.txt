CT-5
1190

CT-5, also referred to as the CT 5-series, was a famous batch of clone trooper pilots that were recognized as the best trained to pilot Jadthu-class landing ships. Clone Commander Jai'galaar was a member of this batch who commanded the 127th Gunship Wing and was later demoted to captain after the Retreat at Katraasii. *Star Wars: The Official Starships & Vehicles Collection 35 Category:Grand Army of the Republic units
