Lieutenant Kiefer was a Human who served in the Imperial Army on Bastion, the former throneworld of the Fel Empire. In 130 ABY, Emperor Roan Fel had been overthrown by the Dark Lord of the Sith, Darth Krayt. When Fel arrived on Bastion in 137 ABY, Kiefer greeted him, accompanied by stormtroopers, and demanded that the former emperor surrender to be arrested. However, to Kiefer's chagrin, Fel instead stated that he was there to take control of the planet. Defying Kiefer's orders to do their duty to their true Emperor, Darth Krayt, the stormtroopers instead bowed to Fel. Kiefer drew his blaster, stating that he would execute his orders. Before Kiefer could shoot, however, one of the stormtroopers serving General Oron Jaeger&mdash;who still considered Fel to be the true ruler&mdash;fired at Kiefer, killing him. thumb|right|200px|Kiefer did not believe Fel to be the true Emperor. Kiefer believed that his loyalty was to the throne, not the being who held it, and thus considered Fel a fugitive from justice, as Krayt had called for his arrest. Kiefer did not understand why no one else ordered Fel to surrender, and so when the stormtrooper contingent with him refused to kill the former Emperor, Kiefer attempted to take matters into his own hands. Fel believed that Kiefer was just one of many people who had been brainwashed by Darth Krayt and his new policies. Kiefer had black hair and light skin. Kiefer wore an Imperial officer's uniform and was armed with a blaster pistol. Kiefer's only appearance is in the third issue of the Star Wars: Legacy comic series, Broken, Part 3, published on September 13, 2006. The issue was written by John Ostrander and Jan Duursema, and was also penciled by Duursema.
