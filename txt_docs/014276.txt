Acquisitor (Lucrehulk-class battleship)
3061

The Acquisitor was a Trade Federation Lucrehulk-class battleship, more heavily armed than most battleships of its class at the time. As with other Trade Federation combat ships, the Acquisitor command crew included several Neimoidians, one of whom was Commander Nap Lagard. Following the Incident at Dorvalla, where it rescued the surviving crew and cargo of its sister ship, the Revenue, command passed to Daultay Dofine. After returning to Neimoidia, the Acquisitor hosted a military vehicle showcase by the representatives of Haor Chall Engineering and Baktoid Armor Workshop, where the Trade Federation leadership agreed to purchase those war machines that would comprise the military force at the Invasion of Naboo.
