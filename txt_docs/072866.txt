Spacecaster-class shuttle
1159

thumb|left|100px|A Spacecaster-class shuttle at the ExGal-4 facility The Spacecaster-class shuttle was a class of starship that was considered old and outdated by the standards of 25 ABY. Among its other components, these types of shuttles carried an inertial compensator, ion engines, and a slow hyperdrive unit. ExGal-4's equipment included a Spacecaster; it was used by Danni Quee to repair an orbiting telescope. The Spacecaster at the ExGal-4 facility was about 34 meters in length and about 30 meters in width. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook Category:Shuttle classes
