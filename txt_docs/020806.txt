Procurator of Justice
2179

The Procurator of Justice was the legal representative of prosecution in the Galactic Empire, charged with overseeing the criminal justice system, and the prosecuting political prisoners and administering sentences on entire worlds. After the Declaration of a New Order, the Procurator declared twenty-seven planets as expendable, including New Plympto, allowing their exploitation. Due to the dangerous nature of the office, the Procurator of Justice was normally anonymous.
