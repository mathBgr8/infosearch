Straasa
1933

The Straasa was an Ton-Falk-class escort carrier used by the Galactic Empire. It was scheduled to be repaired at Depot XLS in the Fondor system, but was attacked by Rebel B-wing starfighters intending to destroy all the capital ships while they were at their weakest condition.
