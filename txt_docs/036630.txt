Yeb Yeb Adem'thorn was a Swokes Swokes senator in the Galactic Senate around 32 BBY, who represented the world of Makem Te. He was able to move from his homeworld to Coruscant, where he resided in a lavish and luxurious home. Some cited the move as evidence of Adem'thorn's corruption. He opposed Senate Resolution BR-0371, which called for the taxation of trade routes.The Essential Chronology The resolution was later passed, and the Trade Federation protested the move by blockading the peaceful world of Naboo. thumb|left|200px|Yeb Yeb Adem'thorn and his companions Adem'thorn was joined by two other Swokes Swokes when Supreme Chancellor Finis Valorum called for a special session of the Senate, to discuss the blockade of Naboo. Naboo's representative senator, Palpatine, introduced his planet's ruler, Queen Amidala, who went on to claim that Naboo had been invaded by the Trade Federation. The Federation's representative, Lott Dod, took exception to the accusation, and recommended that a commission be dispatched to Naboo, in order to discover the truth of the situation. Aks Moe of Malastare concurred with Dod. Valorum, after conferring with Vice Chairman Mas Amedda, asked Amidala if she would be willing to allow a commission to be sent, but the queen was not satisfied or pleased in the slightest. She, in response, called for a Vote of No Confidence in Valorum. Soon, calls for an immediate vote rang around the Grand Convocation Chamber, and Adem'thorn, in addition to his two companions, joined in. Adem'thorn later supported the Vote of No Confidence, which resulted in the end of Valorum's career, and Palpatine's succession as Supreme Chancellor. Considered corrupt in some quarters, Yeb Yeb Adem'thorn opposed such resolutions as the taxation of trade routes, but would support motions such as the Queen of Naboo's Vote of No Confidence in Chancellor Valorum. In expressing his desire that the vote to decide Valorum's fate be held immediately, Adem'thorn waved his arms around vigorously. The senator also lived in an expensive residence, drawing ire from others. Adem'thorn had black eyes and pale green skin. Yeb Yeb Adem'thorn first appeared, unnamed, in George Lucas's 1999 film, Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace.
