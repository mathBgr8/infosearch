Drezzel
2088

Drezzel was an Imperial Captain in command of the Imperial outpost on Seoul 5 just after Battle of Endor. An Imperial Governor dispatched Drezzel and a sizable landing force to the formerly lost world, home to an ancient and legendary Human civilization, in order to search for valuable treasures. There they found a cache of extremely valuable Seoularian crystals, and assigned scientists Ualp Xathan and Fem Nu-Ar to study the find. However, they were soon joined by Han Solo and Chewbacca, representatives of the new Alliance of Free Planets, inveterate fortune-seekers who had themselves come searching for plunder.
