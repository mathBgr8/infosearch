KDT Group
1432

thumb|right|280px|A [[BTL Y-wing starfighter/Legends|BTL Y-wing starfighter attacks Sentinel-class landing crafts of KDT Group.]] KDT was the designation of a convoy of Sentinel-class landing craft carrying prisoners and supplies. It was overseen by Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Goliath. Several of them had Bothan slaves who were sympathetic to the Rebel cause. After a mysterious transmission by an unnamed informer, four Y-wings&mdash;one of them piloted by Ace Azzameen&mdash;and three T-65 X-wing starfighters attacked the convoy and disabled the convoy craft with the slaves near the Golan platform Golan I. Then Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports from Tango squadron liberated the slaves while the Rebel fighters provided cover for them. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Imperial Navy units
