Macor was one of the four continents of the planet Essowyn. It was located in the south-eastern hemisphere. The other three continents were Baslar, Steltout, and Sorontill. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Continents of Essowyn
