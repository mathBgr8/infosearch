"After the Old Life" was a song by Starburst, released on the album Only In Your Dreams. It was given a Scarlet rating by the Imperial Board of Culture, meaning it neither protested the Empire nor glorified it. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Songs by Starburst
