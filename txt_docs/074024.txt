Tralkpha
1322

Tralkpha was the Mon Calamari navigator of the ship Jade's Fire. He was described as taciturn. After the resolution of the First Corellian Insurrection, Mara Jade surmised that he was most probably dead, but hoped there was a chance he had only been captured or had hidden on Corellia. *Ambush at Corellia *Showdown at Centerpoint *Cracken's Threat Dossier *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Mon Calamari
