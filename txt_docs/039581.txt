Garn (Rakata)
1863

Garn was a warleader of The One's tribe of Rakata on Lehon. A group of Garn's scouts were killed by Mandalorians who were stranded on Lehon. If players agree to meet with the One upon meeting the Black Rakata for the first time, Garn will be friendly to the player and ask them to bring him the head of the Mandalorian leader who camped in front of the Temple of the Ancients. In reward, he gives the location of an equipment footlocker.
