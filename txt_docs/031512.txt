Creatures of the Galaxy |class= |subspecies= |races= |height=1.5&ndash;2 meters |length= |wingspan= |mass=150 kilograms |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=Four genders *Female *Male *Camale *Shamale |lifespan=50 years |origin=Garban |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Danchaf, also called tree goblins by the Jenets, were predatory pack animals which originated on the planet Garban. Growing to about a couple of meters tall, the humanoid-build Danchaf were climbing creatures that possessed clawed feet and broad shoulders protected by scales with horn-like protrusions. Xenobiologists identified the goblins as non-sentient, although the Jenet argued that the creatures' abilities in problem-solving and reasoning manifested themselves when the offworld observers were not present.
