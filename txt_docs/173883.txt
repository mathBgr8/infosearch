Star Wars Weekly 114 is the one hundred fourteenth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 30, 1980. It included the original story "Betrayal!" *"Betrayal!" *"Heroes of the galaxy: Man-Wolf Part 1: Weird Stone" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 24: It's still alive!" *"Tales of the Watcher: They lurk in space!"
