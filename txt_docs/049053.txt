Myneyrsh (language)
1305

Myneyrsh language was the native language of the Myneyrsh species from Wayland. The other intelligent species from Wayland, the Psadan, also used Myneyrsh as a second language. Similarly, most Myneyrsh were fluent in both Myneyrsh and Psadan languages, although they were only literate in their own language. Myneyrsh usually also know to speak Basic. Myneyrsh was a primitive language of rough vocalizations, snorts and honks.
