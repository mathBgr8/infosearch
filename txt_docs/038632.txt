|intl release= |pages= |issue=3 |featured= |series=The Official Star Wars Fact File }} The Official Star Wars Fact File 3 was the third issue of The Official Star Wars Fact File, released on January 16, 2002. The issue was authored by Darren Allanson, David Bailey, Chris Gardner, Jim Swallow and Iain Lowson.
