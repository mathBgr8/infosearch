Gaddatha In'Kro
1601

Gaddatha In'Kro was a male Caamasi who served as a Republic diplomat during the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, In'Kro was one of several diplomatic envoys that were sent by the Galactic Republic to the planet Aargau when the Banking Clan was heavily involved in the war on the Separatist side.
