Dr'xureretue
2316

Dr'xureretue was the owner and operator of Docking Bay 87 in Mos Eisley, a settlement on the planet Tatooine. A member of the Ishi Tib species, he insisted on being called "Drue," as he knew his name was difficult to pronounce by numerous beings. Dr'xureretue had a cheerful disposition and was known to introduce newcomers to the laws and habits of Mos Eisley. He was a good source of information and gossip since he would also warn newcomers about how business was conducted in the city. Despite his cheerful mannerisms, he would not compromise on matters of business and fees. He owned two labor droids that handled the unloading and loading of cargo from the starships that berthed in the docking bay. The owner of Docking Bay 92, a Human named Dom Antyll, would complete any repairs and maintenance on ships within Dr'xureretue's mooring. The Ishi Tib also kept the docking bay modernized to maintain its desirability with merchants and smugglers.
