Grythsorin
559

Grythsorin was the largest canyon system on the planet of Ebra. It was also the location of lu-ramin mines. During the Imperial rule of Ebra, these mines were penal colonies worked by Ebranite political prisoners. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Canyons
