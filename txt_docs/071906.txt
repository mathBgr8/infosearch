In one episode, which was only broadcast once due to legal threats, Derec discussed his failed attempt to seduce a beautiful women with the Devaronian bartender Jojo. Jojo eventually suggests that Derc use a Zeltron pheromone producing perfume called the Essence of Zeltros. Derc appeared in Zeltros: Pleasure Planet, a Star Wars Roleplaying Game supplement written by Cory J. Herndon and published online by Wizards of the Coast in 2004.
