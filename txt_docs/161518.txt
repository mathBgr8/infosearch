Norge
2980

Norge was a Virella Systems cargo freighter that approached Azzameen Station shortly after the Battle of Hoth in 3 ABY. The cargo freighter pulled three Class-I cargo containers, two Class-J cargo containers, and four Class-D cargo containers, as well as a rear engine block. Shortly after the Battle of Hoth, a pivotal battle between Galactic Empire and Alliance to Restore the Republic forces in 3 ABY,The events portrayed in Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance take place between the conclusion of the Battle of Hoth and the destruction of the second Death Star; the family missions, which serve as a prologue and in which Ace Azzameen makes his first transport run, all take place before the Battle of the Bajic Shipyards, also portrayed in X-Wing Alliance, which The New Essential Chronology places in 3.5 ABY, and the Battle of Hoth is known to have taken place on 38:6:6 according to The Essential Atlas. Therefore, because the prologue missions of Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance take place between the Battle of Hoth and the Battle of the Bajic Shipyards, the Norge visits Azzameen Station some time between these two dates. the Norge exited hyperspace near Azzameen Station as Ace Azzameen and Aeron Azzameen made a delivery to the XQ1 Platform Harlequin Station on Ace's first transport run. "Norge" is the Norwegian word for "Norway." *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Virella Systems cargo freighters
