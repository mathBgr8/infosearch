Battle of Smarck
2620

The Battle of Smarck was a New Republic raid against Black Sun forces in 11 ABY. In retaliation for the joint Black Sun-Grappa the Hutt funded pirate raids on New Republic hyperspace routes during the crisis against the Interim Ruling Council, Red Leader commander Mirith Sinn led a multi-squadron raid on Black Sun's forces on Smarck, destroying their Spaarti cloning cylinders there and helping unravel the connection between the criminal forces and the Ruling Council. During the fighting, Commander Sinn was contacted by the force's base informing her that they had intercepted a transmission from Imperial channels.
