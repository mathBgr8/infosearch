Garthus Hal
1598

Garthus Hal was a Human explosives expert and bomber for hire. His early life included stints as a soldier, a pirate, and a gambler, but he soon became recognized for his skill in demolitions. With his reputation established, he went into business along with a small band of "lads," as he called them, as freelance bombers. Garthus had no scruples and hired himself out equally to the Galactic Empire, the Rebel Alliance, or to various terrorist factions, whoever could afford his services.
