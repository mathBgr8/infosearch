A Force-sensitive, Renxis Dielle was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order during the Separatist Crisis which plagued the Galactic Senate. Just prior to the Clone Wars, Padawan Dielle was dispatched to Kabal with fellow Knights Ixian Rovieda, Plessus Ghon, and Nygreena Clo to end the month-long riots in the capital city of Shoribus. The riots begun due to planet-wide food shortages, and were only ended when the Confederacy of Independent Systems dropped parcels of rations and other food stuff within the city.
