Verpine cardio-regulator
820

The Verpine cardio-regulator was a medical support system that detected whether the body was in "distress". If detected, it provided emergency support. It worked so well that many wondered how much "distress" people were put through when the Verpine were testing the device. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Belts
