Celana Aldrete
1764

Celana Aldrete was a Human female from Alderaan and an aide of Bail Organa during the early days of the Galactic Empire. She may have been related to Agrippa Aldrete, an aide of Senator Bail Antilles during the time of the Invasion of Naboo, and Alya Aldrete, a noblewoman.
