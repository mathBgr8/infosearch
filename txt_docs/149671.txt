Star Wars: Jedi Adventure
1497

Star Wars: Jedi Adventure is a game created for Tiger Electronics' R-Zone handheld gaming system, released in 1997. *Play as Luke Skywalker, Jedi Knight!' *Use your trusty blaster and lightsaber to battle the forces of evil!' *Rescue Han Solo, Princess Leia, and Chewbacca from Jabba the Hutt!'
