Bivoli Tempari
2155

Bivoli Tempari was a very expensive and sweet dish that consisted of toughened meat in a syrupy sauce. thumb|left|100px|Bivoli Tempari's original appearance In Star Wars Galaxies, some food and drink items like Bivoli Tempari got a new appearance after Publish 6, a publish which included a revamp to the Chef profession. The original appearance of Bivoli Tempari was that of a hunk of meat. All Bivoli Temparis made before Publish 6 were retroactively changed to the new appearance. The original appearance could still be found in some locations such as The Ranch on Tatooine.
