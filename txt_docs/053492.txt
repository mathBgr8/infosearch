CoreDataFiles was a news agency. Millennium Entertainments had a shareholder interest in the company. They produced the CFC-100, a corporate watcher computer that tracked information on over 10,000 of the galaxy's most important businesses. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology Category:Media companies
