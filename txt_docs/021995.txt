|pages=64 |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Mission to Lianna" is a paperback supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. It was written by Joanne E. Wyrick and published in April 1992. A weary Rebel unit, after completing a mundane mission, discovers a top-secret Imperial operation intent on producing an effective cloaking device for their starships.
