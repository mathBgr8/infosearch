Hutt battle armor
827

thumb|250px|A [[Hutt/Legends|Hutt clad in Hutt battle armor.]] Hutt battle armor was ancient armor worn by the Hutts of old. It increased their strength, dexterity, speed, and provided optimal protection. Tiny microrepulsors in the armor made it lighter and more maneuverable. It was equipped with a droid translator unit, a datapad, two wrist-mounted blasters, and an E-Web heavy repeating blaster mounted on the shoulder. *Tempest Feud *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Heavy armor battle armor
