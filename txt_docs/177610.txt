Jorus Trass
2003

*Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Jorus Trass was a Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order as the Chief Linguist of the Almas Academy, an experimental Jedi academy located on Almas in the Cularin system. A Force-sensitive, Jorus Trass was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, achieving the rank of Jedi Master. Stationed on Almas in the Cularin system, Master Trass served as the Chief Linguist of the academy, having trained as a researcher and specializing as a linguist for many years. Among Master Trass' contributions to the field, the Jedi was most famed for the endeavors put into the research of the Skintone Language of the Tarasin species. Master Trass compiled all the research findings in a published document entitled, Shades of Meaning.
