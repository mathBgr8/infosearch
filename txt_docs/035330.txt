Great Door of the Senate
1079

thumb|right|200px|The Great Senate Door by the time of the [[Treaty of Coruscant.]] The Great Door of the Senate was the main entrance to the Senate Building on Coruscant. The terminus of the Avenue of the Core Founders, it led into the Atrium of the Senate.
