Boboqueequee
730

Boboqueequee was a location in Hutt mythology, probably associated with the underworld. A common Huttese imprecation was "Rot in Boboqueequee!" "Rot in Boboqueequee!" is one of several intentionally abstruse Huttese imprecations featured in the Jabba the Hutt series and is obviously the Huttese equivalent of "Burn in Hell!" *Jabba the Hutt: The Hunger of Princess Nampi Category:Hutt locations Category:Mythological places Category:Slang
