Rewinda Briil
2459

Rewinda Briil was a smuggler who, along with their twin Lozira, worked as part of an underworld crew led by Roa on Nar Shaddaa during the Imperial Era. In that time the Briils worked alongside fellow smugglers Tregga and Vonzel, and eventually, partners Han Solo and Chewbacca, who went on to great fame as Rebel heroes.The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial However, the Briil twins' careers were cut short some time before 2 BBY, when they were killed in a battle with a patrol cruiser in the Tion Hegemony. Lozira Briil was first mentioned in Han Solo's Revenge, written by Brian Daley and released in 1979.
