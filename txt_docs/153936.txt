Liaq
3067

Liaq was a terrestrial celestial body located in the Core Worlds' Liaq system. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Rebel Alliance officer Beryl Chiffonage encountered a group of mercenaries on Liaq. Later, when, around 2 ABY, the Alliance officer Risiev Credal jumped to hyperspace during a patrol, with all contact with him being lost, he was considered to have potentially defected to the Galactic Empire. Chiffonage, who had mentored Credal, subsequently made a personal addendum to a Task Force on Alliance Security SecuriDex datafile on the latter officer. In the comment, Chiffonage noted that, when Credal returned, he would reprimand him and then tell him about the encounter on Liaq. Liaq was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
