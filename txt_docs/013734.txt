Before the Battle of Naboo, the natives of Delaluna were accused of developing a superweapon known as the Annihilator. Although its existence was denied, the people of Junction V lived in fear. The rumor was Started by One of Count Dooku's old friends who was once a Jedi Padawan. He then created a fear free planet only in fear of his power until Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi proved it as falsified information. The people of Junction V and Delaluna made peace after a war shortly before the Clone Wars and joined with the people of Vicondor and Bezim to build the Station 88 Spaceport.
