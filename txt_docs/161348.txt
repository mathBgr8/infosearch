Executioner's Row
832

thumb|right|250px|Boba Fett and Han Solo face each other in Executioner's Row. Executioner's Row was one of the slums in the city of Dying Slowly on Jubilar. On the edge of the city, it was the home of Hallolar Voors's spice warehouse in 12 BBY. By 19 ABY, the name had apparently fallen into disuse. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Jubilar sector locations
