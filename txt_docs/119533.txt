Wicked

thumb|300px|Signs for Wicked and other shows outside the Galaxies Opera House Wicked was a musical playing at the Galaxies Opera House in 19 BBY. A sign advertising the show was displayed outside of the opera house during a performance of Squid Lake attended by Palpatine and Anakin Skywalker. The sign depicted an Imperial stormtrooper whispering into the ear of a green-skinned Palpatine.Tag & Bink: Revenge of the Clone Menace This is a reference to the musical Wicked.
