Benevolent Guide
895

The Benevolent Guides, V-Tan and O-Vieve, were responsible for creating the isolationist structure on the planet of Kegan. However, their system was eventually rejected by the people. The Benevolent Guides lived within the Central Dwelling in the city of Kegan.
