Ashanti
1823

The Ashanti was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by the Rebel Alliance. It was part of the operation to steal TIE Avengers from a secret Imperial base. Despite the Imperials sending reinforcements consisting of the CR90 corvette group Eagle, Assault Gunboats, and Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports, the freighter managed to steal TIE Avengers so that the Rebels could study them. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
