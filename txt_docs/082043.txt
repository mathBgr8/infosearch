Yusanis's Brand
1367

thumb|right|200px|[[Bastila Shan brandishing Yusanis's Brand]] Yusanis's Brand was the Echani Ritual Brand that the famous Echani general Yusanis used. It was particularly effective against droids. He fought alongside Revan in the Mandalorian Wars, and used it effectively. It proved to be of little help, however, when he challenged the Dark Lord to a duel and was killed. Revan later had the opportunity to buy it, at a hefty price, on the planet Tatooine from the Paaerduag merchant Mic'tunan'jus Orgu.
