B-4R5-GE3
1335

B-4R5-GE3 was a GE3-series protocol droid working for Vogga the Hutt on Nar Shaddaa in 3951 BBY. The droid monitored the transponder codes of all ships leaving the docks, then transmitted departure information for any of Vogga's freighters.
