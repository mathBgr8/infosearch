Dromon
2014

The Dromon was a Ripoblus frigate that attempted to aid its forces at the Battle of Science Station Youst during the Sepan campaign. It was destroyed while attacking the Victory-class Star Destroyer Protector. A dromon was a class of warship in the ancient Byzantine navy.
