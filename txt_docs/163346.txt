Monofilament net
1053

Monofilament nets were virtually invisible traps. They were composed of multiple strands of razor-sharp monofilament wire. These nets shredded virtually anything that came into contact with them. The people of Anobis used monofilament nets during their civil war.Young Jedi Knights: Return to Ord Mantell However, not all monofilament nets were lethal. At least one net was used as a safety net in the remains of the Grand Hall in the Aldera Royal Palace. Individuals falling into the net had their fall broken to stop them from descending into a pit. *Graveyard of Alderaan *Young Jedi Knights: Return to Ord Mantell Category:Nets Category:Safety devices Category:Security weaponry
