Red Gauntlet
2254

Red Gauntlet was an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer under the command of Warlord Zsinj. It participated in the Battle of Vahaba, attacking Admiral Teren Rogriss's Stellar Web along with Zsinj's Victory-class Star Destroyer Serpent's Smile. The attack failed due to mass fire and superior tactics on behalf of the temporarily allied New Republic and Galactic Empire, and Serpent's Smile was destroyed. Red Gauntlet surrendered shortly after, under the guns of General Han Solo's Crynyd and Skyhook, as well as Stellar Web.
