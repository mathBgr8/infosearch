Bri'ahl (species)
940

The Bri'ahls were humanoids indigenous to the forested planet of Bri'ahl. They had pink skin, large eyes that lacked colored irises, squarish noses, large sharp teeth, funnel-shaped ears, and three fingers and a thumb on each hand. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Outer Rim Territories sentient species
