|gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes=Gray |skin=Pale |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Mandalorians *Galactic Republic **Cuy'val Dar |masters= |apprentices= }} B'arin Apma was a Human who was one of the seventy-five Mandalorian warriors who were selected by Jango Fett to help train elite clones on Kamino, as part of the one hundred members of the Cuy'val Dar. B'arin Apma dedicated his life to the principles of brotherhood, stamina, and loyalty, and, believing them to be as essential qualities in a superior soldier as marksmanship and courage, worked to instill them in the clone troopers he trained. Pale-skinned, gray-eyed, and brown-haired, Apma wore his hair short and sported a narrow goatee on his chin. He wore silver-colored Mandalorian armor over a gray jumpsuit, and was equipped with two WESTAR-34 blaster pistols and a Z-6 jetpack. thumb|left|B'arin Apma in full armor B'arin Apma was created for the 2007 Entertainment Earth-exclusive seven-action figure pack "Republic Elite Forces: Mandalorians & Omega Squad," part of Hasbro's Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Collection of action figures.
