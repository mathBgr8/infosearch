YT-2550
2181

The YT-2550 was a medium freighter from Corellian Engineering Corporation's YT-series. YT-2550s were seen in use by smugglers shortly after the Battle of Endor. Like other YT series vessels, they could be easily modified. The YT-2550 has only been found in one West End Games sourcebook and its re-issue in a second edition adventure anthology. It wasn't even identified by name until the later revision.
