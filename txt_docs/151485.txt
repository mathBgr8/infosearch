Tyrant (escort carrier)
1802

The Tyrant was an Ton-Falk-class escort carrier serving with the Imperial Navy. It attempted to capture the Compellor following the Empire's earlier failed attempt. Despite having a large amount of TIE interceptors and some Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports at her disposal, the Rebel fighters assigned to protect the ship managed to destroy its defenses as well as the Tyrant itself. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Starships of Task Force Vengeance Category:Ton-Falk-class escort carriers
