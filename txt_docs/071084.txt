Wolam Tser
2704

Wolam Tser was a noted holodocumentarian and historian of the New Republic. His voice was easily recognized by those who lived in the years following the Battle of Endor. After escaping Coruscant following the Yuuzhan Vong invasion, he brought crucial information on the occupation and Vongforming of the planet to New Republic leaders on Borleias. He was later responsible for narrating the holo Battle of Borleias, filmed by his partner, Tam Elgrin.
