|release date=April 2, 2004 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=The Empire Strikes Back |followed by=The Phantom Menace }} Rogues and Scoundrels was an expansion card pack made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game based on the Star Wars films and Expanded Universe. The set was released on April 2, 2004. Expansion contains 105 cards including 35 rare, 35 uncommon and 35 common cards. It featured the first card to be banned from the game. The card, Ugnaught, allowed a dark side player to win a large percentage of games in the first turn, and was deemed too powerful to be used in tournament play. New Abilities: *Bounty: A reward given to the player when that unit defeats an enemy unit. *Upkeep: A continual payment that must if possible be paid to keep the unit in play.
