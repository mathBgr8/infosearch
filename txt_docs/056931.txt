Biggs Darklighter: Blaze of Glory
836

Biggs Darklighter: Blaze of Glory was a short comic scheduled to appear in an issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine, detailing how Biggs Darklighter left Tatooine for the Imperial Academy, his adventures on the Rand Ecliptic, and his joining the Rebellion. Its story was presumably the basis for the Darklighter story arc of Star Wars: Empire. Category:Star Wars Galaxy Magazine fiction
