Imperial Walker
1997

Imperial Walker was a two-legged walker model used by Sith Empire during the Galactic War in the Revanite crisis and the years afterward. Aegis Squadron and Guard Squadron were composed of the walkers of this type, as was Crimson Talon, a walker used during the War on Iokath. This walker model was introduced in Star Wars: The Old Republic: Shadow of Revan expansion to represent the mobile walkers that can serve as either friendly or hostile NPCs, as opposed to the walker model from the original game that only appears static as part of the environment. *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Shadow of Revan *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Knights of the Fallen Empire *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Knights of the Eternal Throne Category:Vehicle classes of the Sith Empire Category:Walker models
