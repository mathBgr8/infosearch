Easter egg

thumb|right|Easter eggs found inside [[Mount Merakan]] Easter eggs were something found in a gigantic ore pipe on Mount Merakan when Nym flew inside it. The name and titular objects are based on a practice done on Easter Sunday by Christians, which is creating colored eggs in celebration of the resurrection of Jesus Christ. Two types existed: One involved actual eggs that had their shells painted, and others were made of plastic and could be opened to find a treat inside, the latter also being the inspiration for the video-game term. *Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter *Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter: Prima's Official Game Guide Category:Eggs
