People's Liberation Battalion
1620

The People's Liberation Battalion was a revolutionary front on Eiattu 6 aimed at overthrowing the Priamsta. It was led by a man claiming to be Prince Harran. Their goals were to destroy the Priamsta and then redistribute wealth to common people.
