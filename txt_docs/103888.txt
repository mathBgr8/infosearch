R6-S1
886

R6-S1 was the astromech unit of elite Bothan pilot Eelo Begraas. Nicknamed "Shootfirst", the droid helped him fly his customized Z-95 Headhunter Dicer. This droid was twice referred to as R6-S1 and once referred as R6-A1. Category:Astromech droids
