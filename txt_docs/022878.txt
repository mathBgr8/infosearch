Council (disambiguation)
16005

A council is a group of people, staffed by councilors and usually those possessing some form of governing power. *Advisory council&mdash; a council that operated on the planet Troithe alongside remnants of the Galactic Empire's forces. *Andelm IV council&mdash; a council that controlled the law enforcement on the planet Andelm IV. *Archaeological Research Council&mdash; a committee of archaeologists on Kallidah. *Bahk-tov Council&mdash; also known as the Bardotta Council, was the ruling body of Bardotta and comprised of members from the Dagoyan Order. *Black Sun ruling council&mdash; a group of Falleen nobles that controlled the Black Sun until its massacre by Lord Maul and the Shadow Collective. *Bromlarch Council&mdash; the planetary government council of Bromlarch. *Calamari Council&mdash; also known as the Mon Calamari government council, was a representative legislative body of Mon Cala. *Council (Remainders), the governing body of the Remainders of Moonbender Colony *Council of Elders&mdash; a religious body within the Elders of the Path, privy to sacred knowledge. *Council of Elders (Ewok)&mdash; the ruling body of an Ewok tribe on the Forest Moon of Endor. *Council of Elders (Path of the Open Hand)&mdash; the governing body that oversaw the Path of the Open Hand. *Council of First Knowledge&mdash; one of four Jedi Councils within the Jedi Order, and was responsible for procuring advise and ancient wisdom. *Council of Labor Abuses&mdash; a body within the Imperial Senate. *Council of Moffs&mdash; a collective body within the Galactic Empire that was composed of moffs and created following the betrayal of Vice Admiral Dodd Rancit. *Council of Mothers&mdash; the ruling body on the planet Gatalenta. *Council of Neutral Systems&mdash; a political faction within the Galactic Senate that was led by Mandalorian Duchess Satine Kryze, and made up of 1,500 neutral star systems. *Council of Reassignment&mdash; one of four Jedi Councils within the Jedi Order, it managed the Jedi Service Corps as well as Jedi Initiates who were chosen to not become Padawans. *Council of Reconciliation&mdash; one of four Jedi Councils within the Jedi Order, it was charged with settling interplanetary disputes, and worked with the Republic Diplomatic Corps and the Galactic Senate. *Defense Hierarchy Council&mdash; also known as the Hierarchy Defense Council, a military council of the Chiss Ascendancy which included high-ranking individuals, including Supreme Admiral Ja'fosk and Supreme General Ba'kif. *Dug Council&mdash; a ruling group of each Hutt crime family that led the Hutt Clan.
