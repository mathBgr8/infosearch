Star Wars (Marvel UK)
6009

Star Wars was a group of comic book series, specials and annuals published by Marvel Comics' United Kingdom imprint. The comics began with reprints of Marvel's US Star Wars (1977) series and Pizzazz magazine comic stories released under the Star Wars Weekly title, starting on February 8, 1978. The main series went through several name changes throughout the years before its end on June 7, 1986.Return of the Jedi Weekly 155 All titles continued a single numbering system until Return of the Jedi Weekly, which reset the numbering to #1.Return of the Jedi Weekly 1 The main difference between the British series and its American counterpart was that the former followed the then-common tradition of weekly British comics of being an anthology title, with several serialised stories each issue. It would take at least two weekly issues to publish all the content printed in only one issue of the US original. Also, unlike the US title, the interior of the British weekly was monochrome, on matte paper, with only the front and back covers featuring full colour printing, on glossy paper. More than one story would often run concurrently. Many issues also included articles and interviews, as well as additional stories from other Marvel Comics series not directly related to Star Wars. This series did eventually contain original stories that did not appear in either the US Star Wars series or Pizzazz. Reprints of these stories in the US would happen over many years, the first being two Marvel Illustrated Books trade paperback collections titled Star Wars and Star Wars 2: World of Fire in 1981 and 1982. No other UK-exclusive stories were reprinted until Dark Horse Comics' Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 1996 miniseries.Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 1Classic Star Wars: Devilworlds 2 The one remaining story never published after that, "Death Masque," was finally reprinted in 2013 along with the other UK stories in Star Wars Omnibus: Wild Space Volume 1.Star Wars Omnibus: Wild Space Volume 1 After the regular series ended, Marvel UK continued republishing stories from the US Star Wars: Ewoks (1985) and Droids comics up until 1989.
