John DiMaggio
2716

John William DiMaggio provided the voice of General Grievous and Sha'a Gi for Chapter 20 of the Star Wars: Clone Wars cartoon. DiMaggio also voiced the bounty hunter Baash in the 2016&ndash;2017 television LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures.
