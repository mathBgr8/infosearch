Drax Squadron
1729

Drax Squadron was one of three stormtrooper units divided from Desert Sands. In 0 BBY, Drax Squadron was tasked with finding an escape pod on the desert planet Tatooine. At first they were joined by Alvien Squadron in their search for the jettisoned craft, but when Zeta Squadron discovered they were looking for a pair of droids, Drax and Zeta were both ordered to search Mos Eisley door to door to find their quarry. *Star Wars: Force Commander *Star Wars: The National Public Radio Dramatization *The Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Desert stormtrooper units Category:Imperial Army ground squadrons
