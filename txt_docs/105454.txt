|pages=92 |upc= |timeline=0 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars Manga: A New Hope |issue=1 }} Star Wars Manga: A New Hope 1 is the first volume in the Star Wars Manga: A New Hope series. It was illustrated by Hisao Tamaki and was based upon the original story by George Lucas. The cover art is by Adam Warren.
