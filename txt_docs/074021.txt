Valsil Torr
1505

Valsil Torr was an elderly male Twi'lek merchant who controlled operations on Yag-prime. After an encounter with the ex-members of Rogue Squadron, he and his men surrendered and allowed them to remove the bacta being stored onboard the station by Warlord Zsinj. He was killed when a Givin maintenance worker "accidentally" put a blaster bolt-sized hole in an exterior wall of the station manager's office, causing him to suffer from explosive decompression.
