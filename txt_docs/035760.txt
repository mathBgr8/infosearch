Esselian New Order Party
1129

The Esselian New Order Party was a political party on Esseles during the time of the Galactic Empire. Led by Jamson Freller, it advocated the philosophy of Humanocentrism as well as complete allegiance to the Empire. Freller questioned the ability of Hall President Cambira Ralle to lead the planet during a series of nonhuman workers' strikes. The New Order Party was often allied with the Cardean Party, and opposed to the Forad Party within the Esselian Hall. Following the election of 2 ABY, the Esselian New Order Party won the majority.
