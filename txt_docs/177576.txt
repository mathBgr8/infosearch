For'o
1513

For'o was a male Bothan admiral who fought in the Bothan navy at the time of the Second Galactic Civil War. When Bothawui sided with Corellia and the Confederation in 40 ABY against the Galactic Alliance, its navy joined the growing Confederation fleet. Admiral For'o was known to have served at the Battle of Kashyyyk where Confederation forces engaged the Galactic Alliance Fifth Fleet threatening the planet.
