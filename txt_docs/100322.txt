The Valley of the Lizard Warriors was a location on Endor. The valley was home to the Lizard Warrior Joddar, and was also the location where the Ewok, Graak, was sent after his banishment from Bright Tree Village. *Ewoks (1985) 4 Category:Endor locations Lizard Warriors
