Ethra Brewery
538

Ethra Brewery was a beverage distiller on Socorro owned by Karl Ancher, Abdi-Badawzi, and Saadoon-Kauldi. They produced raava, a traditional liquor brewed from zsajhira berries. They owned the rights to mine the hot springs and frozen water on Neftali. *The Black Sands of Socorro Category:Foodstuff manufacturers Category:Socorro locations
