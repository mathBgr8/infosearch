Carbonite Guild
1309

The Carbonite Guild was a powerful carbonite-mining conglomerate that rose to prominence on Empress Teta between the Great Hyperspace and Great Sith Wars. By the time of the latter, it was associated with the Mining Guild and shared rule of the Empress Teta system with the Tetan royalty. It was lead by Chairman Bearus. After Satal and Aleema Keto's Krath rose to power, the Guild's prominence was shattered.
