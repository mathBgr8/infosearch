Battle of Commenor (Yuuzhan Vong War)
2001

A battle at the planet Commenor occurred during the Yuuzhan Vong War. As the Yuuzhan Vong continued to expand their control of the Core Worlds, Commenor suffered severe orbital bombardment by Yuuzhan Vong forces.
