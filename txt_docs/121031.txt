FX-1 medical assistant droid
1044

The FX-1 medical assistant droid was a medical droid manufactured by Medtech Industries, beginning the FX-series of medical assistant droids. Produced during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, it was ultimately succeeded by the FX-2 model. *The New Essential Guide to Droids FX-01
