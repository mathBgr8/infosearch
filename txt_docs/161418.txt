Hid Toqema
1339

Hid Toqema was a gray-skinned Twi'lek who worked for Daedalus Gas Mines in Friz Harammel. The Twi'lek Daedala family, which ran Daedalus, hired nearly one hundred Twi'lek engineers, including Toqema, to work at Friz Harammel as part of a "cultural unity" movement from their homeworld. Toqema was involved with the design of thermal gas registers for many years, but as he grew older and slowed down, he was moved to being a maintenance supervisor for a lower sublevel of the gas mines facility.
