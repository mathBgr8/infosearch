Imperial Spin
2751

"Imperial Spin" is a short story written by Greg Gorden. The story consists of three holomessages by Imperial advisor Ars Dangor. It was first printed in the Imperial Sourcebook and was later archived on Hyperspace. In two holomessages, one to the Imperial citizenry and another to the Grand Moffs, Ars Dangor announced the abolition of the Imperial Senate and the transition of administrative authority to the Grand Moffs.
