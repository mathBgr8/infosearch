The Rebel presence on Pirik's capital Corint City was important in times of the Empire: Shroud Team coordinated Rebel activity on the Divis Arm from there. Sometime before 2 ABY, the Galactic Empire began an invasion on the Pirik system. During the attack, Lieutenant Ma'w'shiye almost lost a leg in jumping through the glass window of Strak Tower's 37th floor to escape the Empire.
