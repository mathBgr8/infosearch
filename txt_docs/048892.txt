Kerestese
1114

Kerestese was the native language of the Kerestian species from the planet Kerest. Most Kerestians were fluent in both Kerestese and Basic, although only literate in their own language. An intricate language in both its oral and written form, Kerestese was rich in rules and exceptions to those rules. *Verre - swear word *Verre d'n Nocka - vehement curse *Secrets of the Sisar Run *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
