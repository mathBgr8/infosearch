The Teachings of Master Odan-Urr
633

The Teachings of Master Odan-Urr were a series of influential writings by Jedi Master Odan-Urr, compiled near the end of his life around 4000 BBY. They consisted of his musings and ideas on the Jedi Code and the proper way a Jedi should act, and were extremely influential in the Jedi Order until its end in 19 BBY. *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook Category:Jedi meditations and reflections
