Bana Besadii Diori
1927

Bana Besadii Diori was a Hutt in Besadii clan. He was a member of the Hutt resistance, and helped supply some funds for Shelter after meeting with Lando Calrissian, Han, Leia and Jacen Solo. He initially asked if the Jedi would fight alongside the Hutts, but eventually settled for the use the Great River to run weapons and supplies for the resistance.
