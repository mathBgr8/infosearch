Intrepid (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
2329

Intrepid was a Nebulon-B frigate of the New Republic Defense Fleet. In 9 ABY, Intrepid was escorting a New Republic supply convoy en route to Liinade III when the fleet was pulled from hyperspace into M2934738 by the Imperial Interdictor cruiser Binder. In the subsequent battle, Intrepid damaged Binder heavily but was destroyed by Reckoning barrages.
