Based upon Anakin Skywalker's alterations to R4-P17, they were modified by Kuat Systems Engineering to feature the dome of an R2-series astromech droid in place of the conical head of an R4 unit, creating what was essentially a hybrid of the two models. As a result, the R4-P could store up to ten sets of hyperspace jump coordinates in its memory,Starships of the Galaxy, Saga Edition rather than the single jump of a standard R4 unit.
