R4-B11
1560

R4-B11 was a R4 astromech droid owned by Slythor the Squib sometime after the Battle of Yavin. He was known to be cranky and destructive. The droid's owner soon got tired of its inability to handle simple tasks and had the droid disassembled and rebuilt as an assassin droid. The unit's familiar appearance as an astromech droid allowed it to get close to its targets. Slythor often included the droid in trades whenever he wanted to recover what he gave away.
