He was born around 49 BBY to parents who were wealthy merchants. Surool's parents set up a trading emporium in the planet Naboo's capital city of Theed, where he attended the Royal House of Learning. At the house, Surool befriended several teenagers, including the Wookiee scout Rorworr, Jedi Padawan Sia-Lan Wezz, and the Royal Naboo Security Force volunteer Galak.
