Mission to Myrkr (web enhancement)
2388

|pages=5 |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Mission to Myrkr" is a web enhancement published by Wizards of the Coast for The New Jedi Order Sourcebook. It was written by J.D. Wiker and published on February 7, 2002. This short adventure for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game is set in The New Jedi Order era, during the events of the novel The New Jedi Order: Star by Star, and it's designed for four to six Jedi heroes of 6th to 8th level (though other heroes are welcome, too). Mission to Myrkr is a free web enhancement for The New Jedi Order Sourcebook in which your heroes will get to spy on the Yuuzhan Vong for none other than Master Luke Skywalker himself!
