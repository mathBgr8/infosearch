Stenness pie
1127

thumb|250px|[[Huba steals a Stenness pie from the Pitareeze family.]] Stenness pie was a baked dessert. The astromech droid R2-D2 was programmed with a recipe for Stenness pie, which he gave to C-3PO during their tenure as employees of the Pitareeze family. According to C-3PO, the pie was a favorite food of the offworlders who took the Kalarba Safari tour.
