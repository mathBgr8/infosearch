Yurf Col
1966

Yurf Col was a male Duros from Duro. Following the Battle of Duro, he became the commander in charge of Duro Squadron, and fought in the battle in the Duro system. At the battle, Col became extremely critical of General Wedge Antilles, due to his unwillingness to share his strategy and inability to reclaim Duro.
