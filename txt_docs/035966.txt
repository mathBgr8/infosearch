Gunship Bomber
1871

The Gunship Bomber was a bomber employed by the Clone Army during the Clone Wars similar in appearance to the LAAT/i. During the Battle of Aargonar, a Gunship Bomber Enhanced had crash-landed during the battle, where it was discovered by Jedi Knights Anakin Skywalker and A'Sharad Hett and later fixed up and used to lure a Gouka dragon to a Separatist hideout via its engines, manipulating the dragon's natural attraction to starship engines.Republic 59 *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns *Jedi: Mace Windu *Republic 59 left|thumb|A Gunship Bomber Enhanced.
