Jynne Celwik was a Human female employed as a waitress at the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary Coruscant. She worked at the club just prior to the start of Clone Wars in 22 BBY. In that year,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Celwik was working at the club the night of the attempted assassination on Senator Padmé Amidala, roaming the floor in search big tippers and thirsty patrons. The assassin, bounty hunter Zam Wesell, ran into the club to hide while being chased by the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker. Just as the Jedi entered the club, she serviced a table. The waitress made rounds through the club as Skywalker surveyed the crowd. She poured a drink for a patron moments before Kenobi disarmed Wesell at the bar of the club with his lightsaber. While patrons of the club had their attention drawn to the altercation, Celwik did not turn to look and walked away from the table. Celwik had blonde hair and light skin. Her look while working at the club was considered attractive, yet unpretentious, unlike the patrons she served. Celwik wore a black dress and thigh-high black boots while working at the Outlander. She carried around a white tray. Jynne Celwik first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones.
