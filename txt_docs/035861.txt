|government=Galactic Republic |electorate=Galactic Senate |result=Palpatine wins reelection to the position of Supreme Chancellor |candidates=Palpatine |otherkeyindividuals= }} An election for the office of Supreme Chancellor of the Galactic Republic was held by the Galactic SenateThe New Essential Guide to Characters in the year 28 BBY (7:10:5 GrS). The sitting Chancellor, Palpatine, was re-elected for his second four-year term. The Senate later modified the Galactic Constitution in 24 BBY, to extend Palpatine's time in office beyond the traditional eight-year term limit. Palpatine's reelection as Supreme Chancellor was first mentioned in the 2002 sourcebook The New Essential Guide to Characters, written by Daniel Wallace. *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Supreme Chancellor elections
