Halmad Prime
914

Halmad Prime was a well-reputed alcoholic beverage from Halmad. It was only available for export, not on-planet. It was most often available through black market deals. The production of luxury goods for export only mirrors the free trade zones in developing nations on Earth such as the Dominican Republic, which produce products like cigars and rum for the world market that are not allowed to be sold locally. *X-Wing: Iron Fist *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alcoholic beverages
