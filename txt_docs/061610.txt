Titi Chronelle
1336

Titi Chronelle was a male Human slave in Mos Espa. Unlike Elza Monimi and Hala, he managed to avoid being captured by Krayn's slave raid in Mos Espa on Tatooine approximately two years before the Battle of Naboo. After the raid, he explained to Amee and Anakin Skywalker what had transpired, as the two children had been having a picnic outside the settlement at the time. *Jedi Quest: Path to Truth *Jedi Quest 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Enslaved individuals Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Tatooinians
