Phi-inverted lateral stabilizer
1276

A phi-inverted lateral stabilizer was a model of stabilizer. They were used on Incom Corporation's T-65 X-wing starfighter to stabilize the power flow to the craft's engines. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron *The Star Wars Sourcebook *Starfighter Battle Book: X-wing vs TIE Interceptor *The Star Wars Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Starship components
