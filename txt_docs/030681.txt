Muttani
1478

Muttani were a species from the planet Kalidorn in Wild Space. The Muttani had a sickly appearance and peculiar body odor. Their bodies were generally hairless, and their mouths were filled with sharp teeth. Perhaps the most infamous of all Muttani was Meeko Ghintee. He was captured and sent to Desolation Alley, one of the most secure prisons in the galaxy, but actually managed to escape.
