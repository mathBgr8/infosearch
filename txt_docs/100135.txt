Ha-ran
1291

Ha-ran was a male Eickarie, of the Family Mish-ra and the Clan Sha-kae. He was a prince of the Tribe Si-shae-ral and a member of the resistance on his homeworld against The Warlord and his Lakran mercenaries. He encountered four members of the elite Stormtrooper Unit Aurek-Seven during the battle to liberate Kariek from the Warlord. He convinced the prisoners of The Warlord to unite, even though they were in different tribes.
