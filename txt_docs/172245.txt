Independence task force
1270

thumb|right|250px|Imperial starships approaching the Independence task force during the [[Battle of Sullust (4 ABY)|Battle of Sullust.]] The Independence task force was a Rebel Alliance unit under the command of the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Independence. The task force was accompanying the Independence before the mustering of Sullust and were attacked by the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Immortal and two Victory II-class Star Destroyers, Protector and Vanguard. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic fleets
