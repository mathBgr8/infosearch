Ready Ration
509

Ready Rations, commonly known as RRs were pre-made meals given to clone soldiers during the Clone Wars. They had a reputation for tasting bad, but were easily portable and provided essential nutrients. There were rumors that the clones had been genetically conditioned to enjoy them. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *Coruscant Nights I: Jedi Twilight Category:Rations
