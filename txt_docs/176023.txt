Star Wars Kids (1997) 1 |subscribers=September 1997 Star Wars Kids 1 was released as part of the Star Wars Missions starter kit package, which also included the adventure book Star Wars Missions 1: Assault on Yavin Four. The StarWars.com Cargo Bay a release date of September 1997 for Assault on Yavin Four. Both Assault on Yavin Four and the starter kit's Star Wars Missions Mission Guide additionally denote September 1997 release dates. |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-12788-8 |issn= |genre= |issue=1 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 1 is the premiere issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in September 1997. Issue 1 included part one of the serial comic The Rebel Thief, written by Ryder Windham. Star Wars Kids 1 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book. Star Wars Kids 1 came packaged with the Star Wars Missions starter kit, which also included the game's first adventure book, Assault on Yavin Four. *"Ask R2-D2 About the Prequels" *"Yoda's Puzzle Pages" *"Meet Luke Skywalker" &mdash; Mark Hamill tells Star Wars Kids what it's like to be Luke! *"A-maze-ing Universe" *"Who Does That?" &mdash; Meet the man who made Chewbacca growl and Luke's lightsaber hum *"Poster Pull-Out" *"On Our Planet" *"Brain Blasters" *"Star Wars Missions" *"Space Science" *"The Rebel Thief" Part 1 01
