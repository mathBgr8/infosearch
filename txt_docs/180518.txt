Fanir
831

Fanir was a group of two cargo ferries belonging to Airam weapon smugglers. They were to meet with Rebel cargo ferry group Rongo to deliver their cargo of Imperial munitions at the Ytha'ac Cluster in the Ytha'ac system. However, the Imperials had sent a strike force consisting of Assault Gunboats and Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports to recover the munitions. The smugglers dropped the cargo for Rongo to pick up and then quickly jumped into hyperspace. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter Category:Airam starships *
