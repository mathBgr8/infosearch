Baobab Security Directive 51-C was a legal restriction set up by Ebenn Q3 Baobab's Baobab HoloNet. It successfully restricted the translation of the Gonkian phrase "Gonk. Gonk. Gonk ko kyenga see." *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Laws
