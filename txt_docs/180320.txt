Dressian Kiolsh Merchant Bank
1029

The Dressian Kiolsh Merchant Bank was located on the planet of Mygeeto in Jygat and operated by the InterGalactic Banking Clan. The facility was constructed below the surface, carved into Mygeeto's massive ice sheets. During the Clone Wars, Sergeant Walon Vau and Delta Squad, a Special Operations unit of clone commandos, infiltrated the vault of Vau's father, the Count of Gesl, absconding with a vast amount of riches, from precious metals and jewels, to antiques and industrial secrets. *Republic Commando: True Colors Category:Banks Category:InterGalactic Banking Clan locations Category:Mygeeto locations
