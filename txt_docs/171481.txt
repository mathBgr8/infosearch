Wampa Clan
1210

Wampa Clan was an initiate training clan of the New Jedi Order that was usually made up of children who were fifteen years old. This clan was named after the Wampa, a vicious creature that was native to Hoth. In 40 ABY, the members of Wampa Clan were taken hostage during the occupation of the Jedi Academy.
