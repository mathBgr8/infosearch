Unification Wars can refer to: *Unification Wars&mdash; a conflict waged on Koros Major by Empress Teta according to the Qel-Droma Epics. *Unification War&mdash; a conflict in the Tapani sector that unified the Tapani Noble Houses. *Unification Wars (Galactic Republic)&mdash; a conflict that led to the formation of the Galactic Republic. *Unification Wars (Koros)&mdash; a conflict in the Koros system.
