Star Wars Weekly 57
1153

Star Wars Weekly 57 is the fifty-seventh issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on March 28, 1979. This issue includes part one of a four-part black & white reprinting of the unfinished story "The Kingdom of Ice", originally published in color in Marvel UK' Pizzazz magazine. The final part of the story was eventually printed in Star Wars Weekly 60. *"The Kingdom of Ice!" *"The Snow Demons!" *"Treachery!" *"The power of Warlock Part 4: Madness monster" *"Micronauts Part 4: Death-Duel at Daytona Beach!" 57
