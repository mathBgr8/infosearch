Norvach
593

Norvach was a pair of Imperial BFF-1 bulk freighter belonging to the Galactic Empire. The freighters carried toxic waste in the cargo transfer area above Bofa II, where new surveillance probes for the Relentless were to be delivered. The freighters were both destroyed by Keyan Farlander in a B-wing. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing Category:Imperial Navy units
