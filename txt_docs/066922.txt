Sandstorm Shipping Concern
941

The Sandstorm Shipping Concern was a shipping and transport agency based in Mos Eisley on Tatooine during the Galactic Civil War. This company was operated by Qes Dollis, from his offices in the spaceport city of Mos Eisley, on behalf of the owner, Jabba Desilijic Tiure. *Rebellion Era Sourcebook Category:Front companies Category:Transport companies
