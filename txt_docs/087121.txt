Ubrikkian Steamworks
2255

|subsid= |headquarters= |locations= |role= |products=*DD-19 labor pool droid *DD-13 medical assistant droid *Ubrikkian Steamworks medical unit |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Ubrikkian Steamworks was a subsidiary of Ubrikkian Industries. Ubrikkian Steamworks manufactured droids, most notably the DD-13 cybernetic surgical droid and an earlier medical unit responsible for making Grievous into a cyborg. Darth Vader disliked the "Galactic Chopper" droid so much that he personally prevented the renewal of Ubrikkian's DD-13 supply contract. Despite a planned redesign of the DD-13 line, Ubrikkian retired the medical droids and focused on the DD-19 labor pool droid.
