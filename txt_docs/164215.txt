Dlock plant
579

The dlock plant was a tall, leafy plant with large, blue leaves. The leaves were said to have been the most fragrant and soothing of all the plants in the Endorian forest. On one Hallowe'en, Wicket Wystri Warrick and other Ewoks used dlock leaves to calm a hanadak and lead it away from Bright Tree Village. Category:Plants of Endor
