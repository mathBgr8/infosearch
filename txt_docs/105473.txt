Star Wars Manga: A New Hope
2203

thumb|Two volume Japanese edition of the manga, featuring cover art by Noriyoshi Ohrai Star Wars Manga: A New Hope is a manga-style adaptation of the film A New Hope. The series was illustrated by Hisao Tamaki and edited by David Land, and was specifically adapted from the screenplay of the movie by George Lucas. It was, of course, based on the original story, though slight differences between the manga and the movie do occur. Due to the fact that was an adaptation of the movie, it was treated in the same vein as the Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope novelization. It enjoyed a level of canon only slightly lower than G-level canon, and took precedence over other titles within the franchise's Expanded Universe, though these titles are now considered part of the continuity of Star Wars Legends and are no longer considered canon except to the degree that they align with the events of the original film. *Star Wars Manga: A New Hope 1 *Star Wars Manga: A New Hope 2 *Star Wars Manga: A New Hope 3 *Star Wars Manga: A New Hope 4 *The Art of Star Wars: A New Hope — The Manga Vol. 1 *The Art of Star Wars: A New Hope — The Manga Vol. 2 Category:Legends manga series
