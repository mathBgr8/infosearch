Brint-wo
2087

Brint-wo was a large ringed planet that was orbitted by the Brint-wo Colony. The system was presumably friendly to the Rebel Alliance, as Rebel pilot Olin Garn was temporarily stationed on the planet in 3 ABY. During this time, Garn rendezvoused with the Azzameen family. The family was delivering bacta to the nearby Rebel Hospital, which treated wounded Rebel personnel after the Battle of Hoth. The system then served as a getaway point for Emon and Ace Azzameen after they fled an Imperial ambush on the hospital.
