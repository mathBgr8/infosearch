Black Level
1031

Black Level was the lowest of the three passenger levels on the Errant Venture, located just above the Mines. It was mostly holds, workshops and stripped passenger cabins. It was ideal for those who wanted safe passage aboard Errant Venture, but couldn't afford better accommodations. All sales are forbidden on those level by Terrik himself with the exception of food and shadow shops that deal with business Booster wasn't willing to do in open.
