Galaxy Guide
1732

The Galaxy Guide were a series of supplements published by West End Games for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. Each one looked at a different aspect of the Star Wars universe. *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back *Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 5: Return of the Jedi *Galaxy Guide 5: Return of the Jedi, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters, First Edition *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley *Galaxy Guide 8: Scouts *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *The Art of Star Wars: The High Republic: Volume II
