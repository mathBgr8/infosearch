Theory of Universal Reference
1045

The Theory of Universal Reference stated that time and space were unified and objects could not travel at the speed of light. It was developed by the Drall scientist Tiran circa 35,000 BBY. The Theory of Universal Reference is equivalent in its fundamental aspects to the theory of relativity.
