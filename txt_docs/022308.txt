Star Wars Campaign Pack
37255

The Star Wars Campaign Pack is a gamemaster accessory for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The supplement includes adventure scenarios featuring the Rebel Alliance starship Long Shot and a Star Warriors combat scenario, as well as full-color floor plans of the Long Shot and a Gamemaster Screen. The Star Wars Campaign Pack was written by Paul Murphy and published by West End Games in 1988.
