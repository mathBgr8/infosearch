L'Quar'ta

l'Quar'ta was one of the sixteen continents in Guiteica. It was to the south-east of the Upper Bal'ta'ran Cluster. It was famous as the base of the m'Yalfor'ac Order. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Continents Category:Guiteica locations
