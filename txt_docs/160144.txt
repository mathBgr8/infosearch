Trailblazer (Reidi Artom)
1240

Trailblazer was the name of Reidi Artom's scout ship. She came to own it after having stowed away on it. It was attacked, and the owner killed. Artom repaired it, flew it to a starport, and registered it under her own name. *Living Force Campaign Guide Category:Scout starships
