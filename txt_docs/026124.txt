Domain Lah
2321

Domain Lah was a domain among the warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Domain Lah was extremely powerful, and possessed its own worldship. It produced some of the most skilled and fanatical members of the warrior caste. Czulkang Lah, a Warmaster, was one of the few to live long enough to pass the title to his son, and later became a teacher of young warriors. Chine-Kal Lah was a carrier ship commander. Maal Lah was one of the architects of the Battle of Coruscant. Tsavong Lah was the Warmaster of the Yuuzhan Vong during their invasion of the galaxy. Members of Domain Lah wore a unique gash across their face that was lengthened with the bearer's honor; at full length, it went from ear to ear.
