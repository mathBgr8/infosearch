Gin Scraf
1036

Gin Scraf was a Human female who was one of the earlier settlers of Nam Chorios. She was the aunt of Arvid Scraf and lived during the Galactic Civil War. Living in Ruby Gulch, she influenced the Newcomers in order to help Luke Skywalker get a job in Hweg Shul. *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Farmers Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Nam Chorios residents
