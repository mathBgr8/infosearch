Vil (student)
779

Vil was a Human male student at the University of Agamar. In 25 ABY, he was one of the members of Dr. Anki Pace's team that made a research trip to Bimmiel. He and Denna were captured by the Yuuzhan Vong and placed in the Embrace of Pain.
