As part of the Galactic Civil War's Hook Nebula campaign waged at some point by the time shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the Qeimet fleet of the Galactic Empire carried out an assault at U'Dray. The Rebel Alliance initially achieved a rare victory over the fleet's Scimitar assault wing, although the Empire nevertheless ultimately prevailed in the engagement. The assault wing's commanding officer, Captain Tomax Bren, mentioned the ongoing assault of U'Dray in a datamemo sent to the wing's squadron commanders. The message was subsequently included in a compilation of Imperial documents made by Rebel historian Major Arhul Hextrophon for Alliance Supreme Commander Mon Mothma and other Rebel officers. thumb|right|220px|U'Dray's name was introduced in the Imperial Sourcebook. U'Dray's name was introduced via a mention of the assault of U'dray in Greg Gorden's 1989 Imperial Sourcebook that was published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.Imperial Sourcebook 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia subsequently provided the first description of U'Dray as a planet. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas then placed U'Dray in grid square O-18.
