Praji grew up in wealth and privilege, just like many of his rivals. After distinguishing himself both academically and as an officer, he became an honored commander in the Officer Corps of the Galactic Empire. In 1 BBY, he was stationed on board the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Devastator as key aide to the Sith Lord Darth Vader when the Star Destroyer pursued the rebel CR90 corvette Tantive IV following the Battle of Scarif. During the battle, the plans to the Imperial Death Star superweapon had been stolen and transferred onto the Tantive IV, and Vader aimed to retrieve them. The Devastator disabled the corvette above the planet Tatooine and had soon captured and successfully boarded the ship. Once on board, Praji was tasked with quickly making his way to the corvette's main computer room to search for the Death Star plans and any other useful information stored in the computer banks before the rebels had the chance to destroy it. To achieve this task he used the modified R4 astromech droid R4-M9, who was escorted through the ship by stormtroopers with fellow Imperial droid R2-Q2.Star Wars: R2-D2 Deluxe Book and Model Set However, the plans had already been removed from the computer by rebel Princess Leia Organa,Bloodline who then had the droid R2-D2 carry them off ship in an escape pod. Once it became clear that the plans were not in the main computer, a report was sent to Vader. Praji later stopped VaderStar Wars: A New Hope junior novelization and Commander Daine Jir in one of the Tantive IV's corridors and reported that the plans were no where to be found on the corvette. He also informed the Sith that no transmissions had been made by the rebels, but that one escape pod with no life forms aboard had been launched and made it to the surface of Tatooine below. Correctly deducing that the plans must have been in the pod, Vader dispatched Praji to Tatooine, ordering that he personally see to it that a detachment of troops was sent to the planet to recover the plans. Praji was a light-skinned human with brown hair and blue eyes. According to fleet logistics liaison Arvira, Praji disliked filling out datawork. Praji was equipped with a black military uniform including a kepi with an officer's disc.
