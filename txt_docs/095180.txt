Ov Taraba
1033

Ov Taraba was a university in the city Iziz on the planet Onderon during the Beast Wars era of the planet. It was an expensive school, where the Minister of State Novar studied the art of diplomacy when he was young. This part of the city was controlled by the Galactic Republic. *Tales of the Jedi Companion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Onderon locations Category:Universities
