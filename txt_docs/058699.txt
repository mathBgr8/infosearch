Conference of Uncommitted Worlds
1680

The Conference of Uncommitted Worlds (0 ABY), held on Kabal just after the Battle of Yavin, was a gathering where Princess Leia Organa secured a commitment by Silver Fyre to aid the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Fyre's deputy commander, Kraaken, secretly alerted the Galactic Empire to the conference, resulting in an assault on Kabal by TIE Bombers. *Star Wars: Rebellion *"Deadly Reunion" *"Traitor's Gambit" *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Events of the Galactic Civil War Category:Meetings
