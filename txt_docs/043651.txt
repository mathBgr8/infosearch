Great Plague
701

The Great Plague was the greatest of all offworld afflictions that the X'Ting race experienced some 100 years before the Clone Wars. Many of the X'Ting survivors believed that the plague was unleashed by the executives of Cestus Cybernetics. *The Cestus Deception *The Hive *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Diseases and maladies
