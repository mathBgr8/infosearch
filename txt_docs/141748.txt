Kibo

thumb|The Kibo. The Kibo was a rare, purple flowering plant native to Yavin 4. Its scent was strong and sweet and the seeds of the Kibo could restore sight. When Trioculus attacked Yavin 4 in 5 ABY, the fires he started brought the Kibo to the brink of extinction. Many years later, Galactic Alliance Chief of State Cal Omas kept a Kibo blossom in bronzium vase on his desk. *The Lost City of the Jedi *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice Category:Flowers Category:Plants of Yavin 4
