Seeking rail charge
436

Seeking rail charges were red-colored, heat-seeking rail charges, designed for use with a Seeker Rail Detonator. They were somewhat rarer than average Rail charges. This variation sought out sources of heat while in flight. In all other aspects, they acted like normal charges. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith Category:Explosive projectiles
