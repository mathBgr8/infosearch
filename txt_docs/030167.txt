Return of the Jedi 3
7452

Return of the Jedi 3 is the third issue of the Star Wars: Return of the Jedi mini-series and is part of the Marvel Star Wars series of comic books. Part three of a four issue comic mini-series adaptation of Return of the Jedi. Unlike the Marvel comic adaptations of A New Hope & The Empire Strikes Back, this was not part of the regular series. There are five pin-up pages in this issue, Scout trooper, Lando Calrissian, Home One, Emperor Palpatine & a TIE Interceptor all by Rick J. Bryant. The Rebels travel to Endor to deactivate the Death Star II's deflector shield, while Luke Skywalker tells Leia Organa of her true heritage. thumb|250px|Luke Skywalker's miscolored lightsaber.
