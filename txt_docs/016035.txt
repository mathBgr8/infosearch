It was an extremely volcanic world covered in sulfur, ash, and lava. From space it had the look of a colorful mosaic of black, tan, yellow. It had an orbital transfer station that was a stop for Haj Shipping Lines liners and ships to and from Carida. Many beings on the station board shuttles bound for larger ships.
