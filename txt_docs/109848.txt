Body-Rigs
2354

|start date=1985 |end date= |toy type=Vehicles |series= }} Body-Rigs was a sub-set of Kenner's 1985 Star Wars: The Power of the Force toy line. Originally known as Single Body Transport Vehicles, the small, one-man vehicles were even smaller than the Mini-Rigs toys. Two of the vehicles appeared in Star Wars: Droids: the Imperial Sniper and the One-Man Sand Skimmer.
