Bahalian Shipyards
882

Bahalian Shipyards was a small, well-respected shipwright company located in the Astal sector. It was a front for the Tenloss Syndicate, and it was at the Bahalian Shipyards that the prototype for the Z-95 Headhunter, the Onyx Star, was produced.
