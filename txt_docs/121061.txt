Gelgelar Outfitters
1465

Gelgelar Outfitters was a general store that was located within the Gelgelar Free Port on the world of Gelgelar. It was maintained by Qulo Ecls, who owned the venue during the Galactic Civil War. The store was very cluttered, and mostly stocked with items that were needed on Gelgelar such as breath masks, water-proof cloaks and hunting equipment. The clerks at the store love to bargain and the prices were marked up so the negotiations would make the customers think they were getting a bargain. The building was two stories, with the upper level used as a domicile for the Ecls family. Qulo's office was in the back of the lower level, near the landing platforms, so he could get first crack at buying incoming cargos. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Businesses Category:Gelgelar Free Port locations
