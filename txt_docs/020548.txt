It was the outermost planet in the Arbran system, which was located in the Bon'nyuw-Luq sector, a part of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Arbran VI was mentioned in "Hole in the Ground," a roleplaying supplement written by Cory J. Herndon for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The article was published on Wizards.com on June 5, 2003, as the first part of the Planet Hoppers series Arbra: Sanctuary In the Storm. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Arbran system, and by extension Arbran VI, in grid square N-17.
