Crime against civilization
1379

In Galactic Republic law, a crime against civilization was the greatest crime of war one could be charged with. In a trial for crimes against civilization, the Galactic Senate would serve as the jury, with the Supreme Chancellor serving as the judge. Ulic Qel-Droma was tried for crimes against civilization for his actions in the Great Sith War. During the Clone Wars, Kar Vastor was charged with crimes against civilization for his role in the Battle of Haruun Kal, but he apparently did not go to trial for reasons unknown.
