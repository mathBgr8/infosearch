Sith Academy (Gamorr)
1762

The Sith academy of Gamorr was established by the Brotherhood of Darkness, and was active near the end of the New Sith Wars. It was used to train Sith Warriors and Marauders. At this academy, these warriors would learn to focus their emotions into a raging battle fury.
