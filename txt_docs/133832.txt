Journal of Personality and Galactic Psychology
1436

The Journal of Personality and Galactic Psychology was a publication that at one point featured research studies on the sentient Balosar species. The studies did not indicate any innate or genetic pathologies unique to the Balosars that would lend support to the stereotyping of members of the species as being spineless, weak-willed, and selfish. Instead, those traits emerged due to the condition of the society on the Balosar's crime- and pollution-riddled homeworld, Balosar.
