The Grand Rim Promenade was a large open market in the Canyonade on Cejansij. It was located two-thirds of the way up the eastern slope, was thirty meters wide, and ran all ten kilometers of the canyon.Specter of the Past *Specter of the Past Category:Cejansij locations
