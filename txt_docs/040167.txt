Roger Guyett
2225

Roger Guyett was the Visual Effects Supervisor for Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith (also had a cameo as Ottegru Grey), Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens (also served as second unit director) and Star Wars: Episode IX The Rise of Skywalker.
