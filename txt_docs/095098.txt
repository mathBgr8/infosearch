thumb|right|210px|The Elathan had first contact with the Jedi of the Chu'unthor starship. The Elathan were a social groupPower of the Jedi Sourcebook that shared its name with the Elatha system, located in the Stensen sector of the Outer Rim Territories. They had their first contact with the wider galaxy between the years 400&ndash;230 BBY, when they were visited by the Chu'unthor, a vast training ship operated by the Jedi Order. This first contact encounter garnered significant media attention across the galaxy. The Elathan were created for the Power of the Jedi Sourcebook, a roleplaying game sourcebook published by Wizards of the Coast in 2002. *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook Category:Undefined social groups
