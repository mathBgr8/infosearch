Maranne Darmic
1678

Maranne Darmic was a Female trader. Born on Coruscant, Darmic was close to her father Dreja Darmic. After he was arrested around 2 BBY, she left the planet and shipped out on the first freighter she could find. Together with Haber Trell, the two smugglers and their ship unknowingly got involved in a devious scheme devised by none other than Grand Admiral Thrawn.
