Invisible Island
615

Invisible Island was an island on Maramere in the Haunted Straits. It was said that Invisible Island was impossible to find on any scanner or map. This was eventually found to be due to the fact that the entire island was crowded with stygium crystals. *Starfighter: Crossbones 2 *Starfighter: Crossbones 3 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Islands Category:Maramere locations
