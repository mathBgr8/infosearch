Keben Park
1151

Keben Park was a park in Coronet on Corellia, overlooking the Presidential residence and other government offices. It contained artificial hills that were used for board-skiing during the snow season. Gliders were known to inhabit this park. Han Solo visited there in 40 ABY, planning to assassinate Thrackan Sal-Solo. While planning, he ate breadsticks and fed the crumbs to nearby gliders. It was here that Solo was ambushed by Boba Fett and Mirta Gev. The three discussed the assassination of Thrackan, and Han revealed to Fett that Ailyn Vel had been captured by Jacen Solo's Galactic Alliance Guard.
