Star Wars: A Musical Journey
4953

Star Wars: A Musical Journey is a DVD collection of music video clips from the Star Wars films, set to selected themes from John Williams's scores. Each clip features an introduction by actor Ian McDiarmid. The program can be watched with or without this introduction. It also contains a preview trailer for the Revenge of the Sith video game. A Musical Journey was originally included as a bonus disc with the 2005 soundtrack album for Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith.
