Tymtogo was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle belonging to the Imperial Navy. It was part of Deep Space Manufacturing Facility Drekker's procedure that, should the station's hull integrity go to at least 25%, the shuttle would launch, carrying officers. The shuttle in this case would make a run for the Victory-class Star Destroyer Inexorable. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter Category:Lambda-class T-4a shuttles Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
