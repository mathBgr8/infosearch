It was the location of the Battle of Shindra's Veil around 7328 BBY between Shey Tapani and the Rogue Houses at the end of the Unification War. Shey's ships won the battle, allowing him to unite Tapani sector. During the Unification Gala, shortly before the Battle of Endor, Ajax Wennel participated in a staged reproduction of the battle.
