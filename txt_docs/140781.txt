Prothium
2644

Prothium was a common gas that when excited, produced an intense packet of light. It was one of half a dozen common gases&mdash;alongside orveth, sig, eleton, tolium, and skevonCracken's Rebel Field Guide&mdash;that was used as a standard blaster gas by weapons manufacturers. The first underground network of rakghouls was discovered when a mining crew drilled into what they thought was a pocket of prothium gas. Over four hundred years before the Galactic Civil War, the Hutt artist Gorgo used super-cooled prothium gas to create decelerated luminescent images. By the time of the Galactic Empire, only a few examples of the work remained.Coruscant Nights I: Jedi Twilight The gas giant Killaniri in the Killaniri system was a major source of prothium. BlasTech Industries maintained a prothium refining station in orbit to process gas mined from the planet using automated gas collectors.
