Forgotten Spires
1320

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Forgotten Spires were three manufactured spires located in a mountainous region of the planet Corellia. The origins of these monuments were lost to the ages, though they bore a striking resemblance to the Forgotten Spire on Dathomir. Packs of vicious Corellian slice hounds were spotted near the Forgotten Spires after the Battle of Yavin. Category:Corellia locations
