Beledeen II
822

Beledeen II was a New Republic warship assigned to General Garm Bel Iblis in 19 ABY, as part of a task force for a data raid on Yaga Minor. The vessel was one of the last warships that were dispatched by Admiral Ackbar in preparation for the major operation. The Beledeen II participated in the Battle of Yaga Minor. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the New Republic
