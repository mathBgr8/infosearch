|length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Dathomir |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Bane Back spider was a subtype of gaping spider, an arachnid creature native to the planet Dathomir. A mutated variant, this creature was larger, stronger and more resistant to average gaping spider. The existence of the Bane Back spider was first reported during the Galactic Civil War. An individual by the name Geeot Nuetia encountered such creature during a hunting trip on Dathomir after the Battle of Yavin. He later claimed in the Corellian newspaper Coronet Gossip that a 50-meter tall Bane Back spider attacked him and pretended that he killed it with a thermal detonator. Later, a journalist went to Dathomir to investigate the creature but only found 2-3 meters tall spiders. Then, in 1 ABY, adventurous spacers traveled to Dathomir in order to hunt Bane Back spiders. The creatures were known to have been seen near the Spider Clan Cave. After killing a spider, one of the spacer later used a carapace as a trophy. Bane Back spiders appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to the game's closure on December 15, 2011.
