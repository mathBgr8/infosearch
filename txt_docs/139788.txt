Apocahk
1503

The Apocahk was an Imperial extermination ship. Along with the Angrix and Azgoghk, it was used by Grand Admiral Danetta Pitta to exterminate and experiment on aliens in the Outer Rim prior to the Battle of Endor. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial extermination ships Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
