Darth Nihilus' holocron
2159

thumb|left|100px|[[Darth Nihilus|Darth Nihilus's, gatekeeper projected from his Sith holocron.]] Darth Nihilus' holocron was a Sith holocron made by Darth Nihilus. During the Cold War, the Sith Vaverone Zare attempted to lay claim to this artifact at the Lightspring on Tatooine whilst the Jedi Nariel Pridence attempted to prevent her from retrieving the relic. By 137 ABY, the holocron had fallen into the hands of Darth Krayt, who used it to contact the gatekeeper in the image of the late Sith Lord on Korriban. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Legacy (2006) 5 Category:Sith holocrons
