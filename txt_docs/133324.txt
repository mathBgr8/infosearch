Craver
494

Cravers were the subject of a common galactic expression. To "throw someone to the cravers" was to abandon them. Han Solo stated to Borsk Fey'lya that "...five minutes ago you were ready to throw Bel Iblis to the cravers if it would buy you enough time to bring in the Katana fleet." *Dark Force Rising Category:Phrases
