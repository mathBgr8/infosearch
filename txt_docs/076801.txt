L'pwacc Den
973

L'pwacc Den was one of Selonia's underground cities. It contained a spaceport. During the early Yuuzhan Vong War it was the site of large-scale rioting after the New Republic Advisory Council voted to redirect a fleet from Corellia to Bothawui. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *Suns of Fortune Category:Cities Category:Selonia locations
