Mantilorrian rat-catcher
1238

Mantilorrian rat-catchers were entities originating in the Mantilorr system. Some of them were employed by Kwenn Space Station. Mantilorrian rat-catchers attempted to capture the Kowakian monkey-lizard Salacious B.
