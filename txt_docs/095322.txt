|habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Hardex were a sentient species native to the Hardex system in the Jubilar sector of the Outer Rim Territories. At some point by 5.5 BBY,Star Wars Omnibus: Droids dates the events of Star Wars: Droids (1995), which includes Droids (1995) 6, to 5.5 BBY. The comic places the Hardex ambassador meeting with Craw to before the story's events. the Hardex sent an ambassador to the Targonnian leader Craw. Craw told a joke involving three Hardex and a bantha to the ambassador. However, when the leader's protocol droid translated the joke for the ambassador, they did not find it amusing. Consequently, Craw had the protocol droid deactivated and repurposed into a water fountain. In 5.5 BBY, the Targonnian captain Steggus reminded Craw of the joke and the ambassador's reaction to it.
