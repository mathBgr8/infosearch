Ivr Drop
1137

Ivr Drop was a Human male who served as the director for the Allied Grain and Roughage corporation at the time of Soontir Fel's brief employment at an agro-combine operated by that company. He offered Fel acceptance to the Academy of Carida in exchange for Fel refusing to testify against Ilir Post, the son of a member of AGR's board.
