Computer Mating Service
722

Computer mating services, or CMS, were used by the Bith in order to conceive offspring, since they could not mate naturally due to a lack of reproductive organs. It matched the DNA of prospective mates that would generate a favored child pattern. The process took a year to complete. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Society and culture
