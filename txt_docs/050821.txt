Hoonta
2036

Hoonta was a Rodian henchman of Jabba Desilijic Tiure. A member of the Chattza clan, he was killed when he, along with a group of other henchmen, attempted to assassinate the Jedi Knight Ki-Adi-Mundi during a skiff ride through the Jundland Wastes. The only one brave enough to face the Jedi, Hoonta boasted that he did not fear his "magic," but he was quickly dispatched by the Cerean Jedi.
