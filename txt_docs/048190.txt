Rhoon
1189

Rhoon was a group of three Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic to carry base equipment during the evacuation of Brigia in Operation Strike Fear. However, all three were destroyed by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Invincible, which arrived during the operation. In the video game Star Wars: X-Wing, it is possible to save the transports.
