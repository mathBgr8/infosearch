Book of Sith: Secrets from the Dark Side (p. 27) establishes that the Valley of Golg stood long before the civilization of Zhell existed on the planet of Coruscant which would place the construction of the valley at some point long before around 200,000 BBY. |destroyed= |type= |location=Korriban |terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest=Dathka Graush's tomb |affiliation=*Adas's Sith Empire }} The Valley of Golg was one of many burial valleys constructed on Korriban by the ancient Sith species at some point long before around 200,000 BBY. The valley was particularly notable for containing the tomb of Sith King Dathka Graush.
