After the foundation of the Galactic Empire, she and her husband hid on Rhamalai to escape the Great Jedi Purge. While on the planet, they had a daughter named Nadra. When Nadra was only a year old, Neth was forced to flee Rhamalai to avoid being caught, leaving his family behind.
