Battle of Distna
2878

The Battle of Distna was a battle in the campaign against Delak Krennel's Ciutric Hegemony. Rogue Squadron, along with a snoopscoot, were scouting the moon Distna, believing it to be where the Pulsar Station was being made. They ran into a large ambush established by Ysanne Isard's clone, and were only rescued by a TIE Defender force of the Xucphra Corporation sent by the real Ysanne Isard. The Rogues were believed dead by the outside galaxy, and used this to infiltrate the Hegemony undercover.
