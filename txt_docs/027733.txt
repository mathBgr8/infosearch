The Galactic Empire maintained the camp known as Troopers' Rest on Sochi, where Imperial stormtroopers who reached retirement age were eventually sent. Sochi was created by Anthony Fredrickson for "Soldiers of the Empire!," an article included in the fourth issue of Paradise Press's Star Wars Official Poster Monthly magazine, published in January 1978.
