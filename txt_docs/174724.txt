Streysel Island
870

thumb|right|200px|A map of a cutaway section of Streysel Island Streysel Island was an island that was part of the Vaynai Archipelago, which was the main area of habitable surface land on the Outer Rim resort world of Vaynai. The island was owned by the crime lord, Gallo Memm, and was also home to the Bothan Oneron Fil'vye, who owned and operated the shipping company Fil'vye Transport. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Islands Category:Vaynai locations
