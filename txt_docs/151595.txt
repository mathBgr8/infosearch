Bombing run
1599

thumb|[[TIE/sa tactical bomber/Legends|TIE/sa bombers destroy a town of Rebel sympathizers.]] A bombing run was a tactic used by many factions throughout history, wherein a bomber craft&mdash;such as a TIE/sa bomber, BTL Y-wing starfighter, or GAT-12 Skipray Blastboat&mdash;would lay a load of bombs across a large surface area.
