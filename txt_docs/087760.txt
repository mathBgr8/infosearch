Mol Arcasite
1547

Mol Arcasite was a bounty hunter who was among the five bounty hunters that were hired by Granta Omega to capture Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker for a large sum of money. She first attacked them with a cruiser on Ragoon-6, but the Jedi destroyed her cruiser and Mol was sent packing hastily on her swoop bike. She did not attack again, however, for Mol knew not to mess with her prey if her first attack failed.
