thumb|right|250px|Lackaff's winning Design an Alien entry Jess Lackaff is a Star Wars fan who designed Hepmaga MiOrd and the Remplish in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the 16 years and older category. She was 27 at the time her entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
