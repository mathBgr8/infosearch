Several years before 400 BBYGeonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds its upper atmosphere housed a Bosken & Bosken mining center which attempted to harvest the planet's gases for profit, but it proved to be an unsustainable business due to the high cost of maintaining a floating mining center, leading to the abandonment of the outpost less then a decade after it was built.Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin Admiral Gilad Pellaeon attempted to meet with General Garm Bel Iblis in orbit of Pesitiin to sign a peace treaty between the Imperial Remnant and the New Republic.
