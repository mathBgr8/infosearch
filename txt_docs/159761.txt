High Seat
893

The High Seat was a throne-like chair that was sat in the dukha that served as the center of a Noghri clan. It would be the only seat in the room, and would be where the clan leader sat. During the Galactic Empire's reign, all Imperial governors were permitted to sit upon the throne, in reverence to Darth Vader. *Dark Force Rising *Specter of the Past *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Noghri culture Category:Thrones
