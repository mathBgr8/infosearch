Throughout galactic history, many individuals have been referred to as "The Emperor" despite knowledge of their given names. *Darth Sidious, publicly known as Sheev Palpatine, ruler of the Galactic Empire *Unidentified Emperor of the Empire (feudal) *Lord Keto, ruler of the Empress Teta system *Satal Keto, Lord Keto's son *The Sith Emperor, the Emperor and Dark Lord of the Sith of the post-Great Hyperspace War Sith Empire *Palpatine, liege of the first Galactic Empire *Roan Fel, leader of the Fel Empire *Darth Krayt, Dread and Dark Lord of the Legacy era Sith Empire
