400100500260026 was an area of space in the Gordian Reach consisting of sixteen planets ruled by G0-T0 droids. In 3946 BBY, a number of G0-T0 droids seceded from the Galactic Republic, cutting off HoloNet communications and blocking all traffic in and out of the sector.
