VoxBox
1661

}} VoxBox, sometimes written Vox-Box, was an expensive electronic translator that allowed real-time translation of speech. It facilitated Gamorreans to converse with other species in Basic. Cheaper versions were also available, pre-programmed with 12 common phrases in Basic. It was designed for use by species that could understand Basic, but couldn't speak it. *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Star Wars Insider 62 *Arms & Equipment Guide *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Saga Edition Core Rulebook Category:Translation devices
