Giryulan Findings
1859

The Giryulan Findings were an infamous event that took place at some point by the time of the reign of the Galactic Empire and shared their name withAlliance Intelligence Reports the Giryulan system of Wild Space. At one point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. an ambitious officer of the Imperial Survey Corps framed the Corps' loyal scout Stren Grier&mdash;who they were a commanding officer of&mdash;of falsifying several reports regarding the Giryulan Findings. As a result, Grier was forced to flee Imperial-controlled space and subsequently became a staffmember of the Galactic Outdoor Survival School. The Giryulan Findings were mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Events
