Blaster artillery
2177

Blaster artillery was the designation assigned to any mobile platform that was used to support high-yield blaster cannons. Blaster artillery were terrestrial siege weapons, designed to launch attacks on stationary targets such as military bases, factories, and spaceports, with the intent to destroy them.
