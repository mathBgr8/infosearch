Mecetti Province
2502

Mecetti Province was a section of the Tapani sector ruled by House Mecetti. The Procopian Shipping Lane and the Leozi Route ran through a large portion of the province. Mecetti Province was bordered by Melantha Province, Calipsa Province, and Reena Province. *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:Tapani sector regions
