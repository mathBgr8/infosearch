|gender=Female |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber=Prosthetic right eyeGalactic Campaign Guide |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Yuuzhan Vong |masters= |apprentices=Daye Azur-Jamin }} Allegedly, a female Force-sensitive Yuuzhan Vong was to have been seduced and trained in the dark side of the Force in the aftermath of the Yuuzhan Vong War. Her existence and nature were described by her victim, Daye. Most of the activities of this reported Dark Jedi were unknown, but it was said that she corrupted the Jedi Knight Daye Azur-Jamin. Hiding in the Cloak of the Sith region, she then managed to capture Kyle Katarn who was investigating rumors of a reemergence of the Sith. Kyle Katarn was rescued by a team put together by Jan Ors, Mara Jade Skywalker and Jaden Korr. The cyborg Daye revealed not only this alleged Force-sensitive Yuuzhan Vong, but also that she had a master. This alleged Force-using Vong was mentioned in an adventure seed in The Dark Forces Saga. She has no official name but was dubbed Vongerella on the Jedi Council Forums. Abel G. Peña later used the name informally. According to Peña, the Vader's Legacy article was going to include a backstory and name for her; the article, however, was canceled by Lucasfilm. She would have been the focus of a tie-in Hyperspace short story that was cancelled alongside Vader's Legacy in 2009.
