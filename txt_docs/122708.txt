Luma grenade
1944

Luma grenades were a model of grenade that released an intense blast of light. They were used primarily as flares or to illuminate target areas, but were also effective at blinding opponents when detonated at close range. Particularly effective at night, the impact of the weapon, which produced a luma blast, could be doubled if an enemy was wearing night-vision goggles, blinding the enemy for at least an hour.Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise The effect of a Luma grenade could also be achieved by a Lumablast Rocket. *Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise *Coruscant Nights I: Jedi Twilight *Star Wars Miniatures Battles, First Edition *Star Wars Miniatures Battles, Second Edition *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Flash-bang grenades
