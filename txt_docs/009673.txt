Gandeal-Fondor Hyperlane
3027

}} The Gandeal-Fondor Hyperlane was a hyperspace route blazed by the Galactic Empire with S-thread boosters for use in efficiently ferrying supplies and starships between Fondor and Gandeal. The hyperlane was also an efficient means of traveling between other core world, for example a trip from Ruan to Fondor could be cut by three hyperspace jumps through the use of the hyperlane.The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse Despite its efficiency, the route was a tightly held secret by the Empire. It was in fact due to the presence of the secret Scarl shipyard on this route.
