Chemical load grenade
1566

Highly versatile, chemical load grenades could be used for many different purposes. Each carrying a specific type of chemical within, their contents ranged from lethal Fex-M3 nerve gas to plank gas, an airborne chemical which broke down mechanical objects. Some types were reusable and could carry multiple payloads. *Arms & Equipment Guide *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Chemical weaponry Category:Czerka Arms products Category:Gas grenades
