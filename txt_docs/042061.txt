Ellor
3012

Ellor was a Duros smuggler who worked for Billey. Ellor met Talon Karrde shortly after graduating from a university on Duros. He joined with Karrde on a smuggling job that nearly ended in disaster when they were confronted by an Imperial Interdictor shortly after picking up a load of Gy'lan spice. Karrde managed to get them to safety, but Ellor became enamored with the smuggling life. He joined Talon Karrde's Smugglers' Alliance to fight against Grand Admiral Thrawn after Imperial troops raided a smuggler meet on Trogan. Ellor had ties to some Duros repair yards which were employed by the New Republic, and on one occasion "borrowed" a pair of Republic DP-20 gunships for use in a raid against Thrawn's shipyard at Bilbringi that he helped Mazzic orchestrate.The Last Command Ellor's group later participated in the Battle of Bilbringi along with other members of the Smugglers' Alliance in an effort to procure a crystal gravfield trap to sell to the New Republic. Though their plans were disrupted by the arrival of New Republic and Imperial fleets, the smugglers were able to help the New Republic by launching a diversionary assault on a Golan II battle station.
