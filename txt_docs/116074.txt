Growing powder
764

250px|thumb|Wicket and Teebo using growing powder Growing powder was a substance used by the Ewok shaman Logray to increase the size of shrunken Ewoks. It proved particularly useful when Princess Kneesaa was abducted by the Fleebogs, enabling her and her rescuers Teebo and Wicket to return to their normal sizes. Kneesaa also used the powder to make the ra-lugg larger, eliminating it as a threat to the Fleebogs. *Ewoks (1985) 11 Category:Powders Category:Sacred Ewok items
