Endor Day
1031

Endor Day was a memorial day observed by the New Republic which was held on the anniversary of the Battle of Endor in 4 ABY and commemorate the victory of the Alliance to Restore the Republic over the Galactic Empire. Star Wars Battlefront: Renegade Squadron includes special awards for topping score tables on real-life holidays as an easter egg, casting Wookiee Life Day as Christmas and Endor Day as the Fourth of July. *Star Wars Battlefront: Renegade Squadron *Specter of the Past Category:Culture of Endor Category:New Republic holidays
