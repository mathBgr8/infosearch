Accuser (720 light freighter)
2391

Accuser was a modified 720 light freighter owned by the Tiss'shar bounty hunter and assassin Kal-tan-shi. He acquired it from one of his targets in the Lesser Plooriod Cluster. It was modified by his former employers, House Paramexor, but he left their employ to pursue a solo career before the modifications were completed. Kal-tan-shi himself was not a skilled mechanic, so he tried avoid damaging his ship while pursuing his prey, and did not finish the modifications himself.
