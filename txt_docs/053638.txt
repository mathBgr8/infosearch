Coruscani Publishing
973

Coruscani Publishing was a publishing house located on Coruscant. They were famous for their non-fiction literature. It published the anthology "Lyrical Metaphysics: Ode on an Odon" by the Ithorian Soshu Londahl. Category:Coruscant commercial buildings Category:Publishing companies
