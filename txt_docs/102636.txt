Narnee Bomu
1526

Narnee Bomu was a male Rodian soldier and a member of the Bomu Clan. He worked for Vago the Hutt and the Anjiliac kajidic along with a number of his clan during the reign of the Galactic Empire. Narnee and another Bomu soldier, Seedar Bomu, was hired by Vago to find a treacherous partner of Popara who had stolen one hundred thousand credits in spice. Vago, not completely trusting the Bomus, put tracker devices in their ship.
