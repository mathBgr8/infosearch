Scourge One
2482

Scourge One was an Imperial hunter, a model of long-range intercept starship developed for use by the Dark Empire. It was piloted by the Dark Jedi Vill Goir, a member of the Dark Side Elite. During Operation Shadow Hand, it transported Goir and fellow Dark Side Adept Executor Sedriss QL, along with several squads of darktroopers, to the planet Ossus in the year 10 ABY. *Dark Empire II audio drama *Dark Empire II 3 Category:Imperial hunters Category:Starships of the Dark Empire
