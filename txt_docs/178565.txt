|published in=Wizards.com |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Disapproval" is a narrative written by August Hahn and Cynthia Hahn as a tie-in to the Living Force campaign. It was published on the Wizards of the Coast website on December 23, 2004 and described an encounter between Darrus Jeht and a drunk Jedi Master at the Crosstown cantina on Cularin. The encounter was related by a member of the Cularin Militia.
