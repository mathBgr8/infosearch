Stroth
2070

Stroth was a Human male member of the Bounty Hunters' Guild. He was the leader of Crimson Nova for a long time before turning it over to Mika. thumb|left|Stroth with Mika Although Stroth lost his right arm in a run-in with a Jedi Knight, he held no ill will against them; rather he had a great respect for the Jedi. In the Clone Wars, Stroth watched as Mika accepted bounties on Jedi. Stroth cautioned against it, but Mika didn't listen. Stroth stunned Mika and turned her over to the Jedi so that she would not die battling them. Stroth became the leader of Crimson Nova once again, and agreed to terminate his contract for accepting bounties against Jedi.
