Deltron spice wine
2252

thumb|250px|Deltron spice wine and its microwave fermenter. Deltron spice wine was a type of spiced wine from Deltron. It was fermented by a portable microwave machine and then served instantly.Star Wars (1977) 24 Aboard Captain Quasar's pleasure cruiser, Augustus Tryll offered a glass to Obi-Wan Kenobi. Unfortunately, the signals emitted by the microwave machine attracted the attention of Merson slavers. Lando Calrissian fell victim to the wine's potency while attempting to con Lemo and Sanda on Arcan IV - after a pitcher or two too many, he inadvertently let slip his companion Chewbacca's true identity.Star Wars (1977) 79 It was also used as an ingredient in shaak pot roast served in Dex's Diner on the planet of Coruscant.
