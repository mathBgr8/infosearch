Zack Bossan
933

Zack Bossan is an author who wrote the backstory of Tannon Praji and co-wrote Ottegru Grey appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of StarWars.com Hyperspace. Also a frequent poster on the Star Wars message boards. His aliases are "Nex: The T is Hott!!" on StarWars.com, on TheForce.net and Jerrycurl on Wookieepedia. - August 2005 (co-written by magicofmyth2 and named by Nathan O'Keefe) - April 2006 Category:What's The Story? authors
