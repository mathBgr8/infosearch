Romeo Treblanc's private viewing box
1250

thumb|right|250px|Romeo Treblanc's private viewing box. Romeo Treblanc's private viewing box in Coruscant's Galaxies Opera House was adjacent to that of Supreme Chancellor Palpatine. At the time of the Squid Lake performance just prior to the end of the Clone Wars, Romeo Treblanc, Tannon Praji, Ottegru Grey, Onara Kuat, used this viewing box. Romeo himself was flanked by two women. This viewing box is full of important Industrial Light & Magic employees. The What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace has identified some of their characters.
