Jmin Survival Academy
727

The Jmin Survival Academy was a paramilitary institution operating during the Galactic Civil War. It was founded by Lunkar An, a graduate student from prestigious Summit Class at Galactic Outdoor Survival School. An graduated with a new record around 17 BBY and he decided to create his own school. Seven years after An's graduation, a new class broke his record. An decided to go back to GOSS as a student, wishing to "redeem" himself. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Academies
