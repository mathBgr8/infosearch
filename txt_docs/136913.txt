Sinthia Pulchatt
1361

Sinthia Pulchatt was a female Kibnon spice merchant, who worked as a liaison between her own boss and the Hutt crime lord, Jabba Desilijic Tiure. Groomed for the position from an early age, she worked in Mos Eisley and was known to frequent Chalmun's Cantina. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Category:Females Category:Kibnon Category:Merchants
