Aiaks Fwa
1050

Aiaks Fwa was a male Whiphid mercenary who served Jabba the Hutt during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, Aiaks Fwa served as a suicide bomber who carried out a job for Jabba. His mission was to infiltrate the Ylesian slave trade and kill the t'landa Til priest that led it. *Rebel Dawn Category:Males Category:Mercenaries Category:Whiphids
