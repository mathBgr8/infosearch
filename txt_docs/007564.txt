|penciller=Rick Leonardi |inker=Mark Pennington |letterer=Dave Lanphear |colorist=Lucas Marangon |cover artist=Rick Leonardi |editor= |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |start date=March 16, 2005 |end date=August 10, 2005 |schedule= |format=Miniseries |issues=4 |timeline=20 BBYThe New Essential Chronology, p. 75 (15:5 GrS)The New Essential Chronology, p. 75 places the events of the comic two years after the First Battle of Geonosis, which is dated to 13:5 GrS by The Essential Atlas, p. 149. |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: General Grievous is a four-part comic miniseries published by Dark Horse Comics and released from March 16, 2005 through August 10, 2005. This tale of treachery and genocide leads up to the events of the record-breaking blockbuster Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith. Before his all-out battle with Obi-Wan Kenobi, the latest Star Wars supervillain took on an entire alien species! On a rescue mission to Vandos, Padawan Flynn Kybo and his master T'chooka D'oon come face-to-face with General Grievous, one of the leaders of the Separatist army. After Grievous kills Master D'oon, Kybo goes to the Jedi Council with a plan to take the depraved general out immediately. But when the council rebukes his plans for vengeance, Kybo decides to take matters in his own hands. Seeking out others who feel the same as himself, he sets out to stop the Separatist killing machine before more Jedi lives are lost. Meanwhile, Grievous hijacks a transport ship, and its precious cargo may give him a horrible advantage over the Jedi that they would never expect.
