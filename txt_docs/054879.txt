Gujdim Wiphshun
1363

Gujdim Wiphshun was a male Gran who served as governor of the Gran Protectorate on Malastare. In 32 BBY, Gujdim Wiphshun hosted peace-talks between the Lannik High Court and the Red Iaro terrorists. The peace talks were held in the presence of the Gran Protectorate.
