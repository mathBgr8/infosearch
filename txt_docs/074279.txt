Slith Skael
1781

Slith Skael was a Sluissi male from Sluis Van, who served as a chief communications technician of the Intergalactic Communications Center and assistant to Reija Momen. He was devoted to his superiors, but when the Confederacy of Independent Systems invaded the planet, he along with Rejia and the defenders were killed. The novel Jedi Trial incorrectly portrays the Sluissi as allies of the Republic, when in reality they were Separatists.
