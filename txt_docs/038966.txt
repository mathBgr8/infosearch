Terese Nielsen
3674

Terese Nielsen (born March 11, 1966) is a California-based freelance fantasy artist, who created the cover art for several Star Wars novels and comics. She graduated from the Art Center College of Design in April 1991. She has gained a fan following mainly because of her illustrations for the trading-card game Magic: The Gathering. Nielsen has produced art for both Magic and Wizards of the Coast's other products since 1996, in addition to her work as an illustrator of book covers and movie posters.
