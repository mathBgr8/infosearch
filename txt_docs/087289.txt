R2-K7
1338

R2-K7 was an R2-series astromech droid. During the Rebellion era, R2-K7 was seen as a member of the Mytaranor Slaving Council, sheepherding prisoners from their cells to auction blocks, and sharing maintenance tasks with Verpine technicians. During this time, R2-K7 showed an amount of cruelty that could have been due to a design failure or a provoked alteration, but this wickedness made R2-K7 a valued member of the Council. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Enslavers Category:R2-series astromech droids
