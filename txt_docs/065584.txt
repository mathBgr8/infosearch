Hutt Chuba's
737

Hutt Chuba's, or "Hutt Chuba'z" in Huttese, was a restaurant in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was known for its Bantha blasters and the adjoining Foamwander Spa, where Mon Calamari on Tatooine often congregated. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Dining establishments Category:Mos Eisley locations
