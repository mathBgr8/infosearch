|owners=Fiz Cor'gril |staff=*Garginoolaara *Shanna Kinn *R5-M1 |goods=Used starships |services=Repairs and upgrades to starships |hours= |affiliation= }} Fizzi's Slightly Used Starships, also known as Fizzi's Slightly Used Ships,Stock Ships was a used-starship dealership that was located on Trevi IV and owned by a Bith named Fizzi. Cor'gril opened the business after deciding to settle down. It consisted of a large warehouse on the edge of Trevi City's spaceport district capable of holding six freighters and a large lot with room for another six freighters. To the side of the showroom, there was a maintenance bay able to service two ships. The building was kept cool to both prevent wear on the ships and for client comfort. The lighting was subdued to provide a calming atmosphere for customer with the added advantage of hiding possible flaws on the merchandise. Cor'gril also owned a large warehouse on the outskirts of the city were he stored other ships he had for sale.
