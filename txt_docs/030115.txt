Mos Gamos
1393

Mos Gamos was a remote settlement on Tatooine. It was a known haven for criminals. Mos Gamos served as the headquarters of the Clantaani gunslinger Longo Two-Guns and his gang. The bounty hunter Jango Fett confronted the Two-Guns in this township in 32 BBY, intending to claim the bounty Jabba the Hutt had placed on his gang's heads, and wiped out the Clantaani and his associates. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities Category:Tatooine locations
