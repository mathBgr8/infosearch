Long before the Galactic Civil War, Delderaan's northern regions were home to several tribes that had primitive art. The gas giant&ndash;type planet Yavin Prime, due to its multi-colored bands of atmospheric gas, resembled an intricate ball of yarns woven by artists of the Delderaan tribes and thrown into the night sky.
