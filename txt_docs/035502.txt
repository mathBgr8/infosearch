Shantee Ree was a famous holovid star. She was perhaps best known for her "Save the Stohl" campaign. Ree owned a specially-trained attack stohl named Needla, which bit an overzealous fan during an autograph session. The media portrayed the attack as an assault. Shantee responded that the attack stohl had been trained to protect her.
