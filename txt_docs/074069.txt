Tetan shock trooper
890

thumb|right|250px|Tetan Shock Trooper Tetan shock trooper was the name used to identify the elite warriors of the Krath cult. They were subtly manipulated by the Tetan Elite Aleema and Satal Keto, and were used to subdue the loyalist forces of the Empress Teta system during their coup. They wore ceremonial armor into battle and were completely loyal to their masters. Their primary weapon was a war spear with a Blaster pistol as a sidearm. Some of these warriors would be further brainwashed to become Chaos troopers who served as pilots. *Tales of the Jedi – Dark Lords of the Sith 2 Category:Krath culture
