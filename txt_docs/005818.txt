Her family managed an underground hydroponic garden on Tatooine, and bought most of their water from Owen Lars's farm. She hung out at Tosche Station with Janek "Tank" Sunber, Laze "Fixer" Loneozner, Biggs Darklighter, and Skywalker. thumb|left|180px|Camie A young woman who lived in Anchorhead on Tatooine, Camie Loneozner's family grew hydroponics gardens underground, buying the water from Owen Lars. She owned a furred pet.Empire 8 In 0 BBYThe New Essential Chronology Camie was at Tosche Station when Luke Skywalker arrived excitedly, telling the others about a battle above their planet. She, alongside Biggs Darklighter and Fixer, followed Luke outside to see the battle with Luke's macrobinoculars. The battle appeared to have ended and Camie ridiculed him for making it all up.Star Wars: A New Hope novelization After the deaths of Owen and Beru Lars, some of the locals believed that their nephew had murdered them. Camie Loneozner, having spent much time with him, didn't believe those rumors at all. After the Battle of Yavin, she told this to historian Voren Na'al in an interview.Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope Camie was involved with Laze Loneozner, whom she eventually married at some point between Luke Skywalker and Biggs Darklighter's departures from Tatooine and the Battle of Hoth. Laze Loneozner built an illegal podracer to sell in order to raise enough funds for a lavish wedding party for her.Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy When TaggeCo decided to test their Omega Frost technology on Tatooine, they bought up a number of the local moisture farms. The Loneozners took over the operations of the old Darklighter farm and were hired by the Tagges to take care of all of the local properties.Star Wars (1977) 31 The two Loneozners ran into Luke Skywalker when he returned to Tatooine on a mission for the Rebellion. They feared that Skywalker might cause trouble and ruin their relationship with the Tagges, and so they reported him to the Imperials. However, Fixer Loneozner had second thoughts about turning in his old friend and decided to warn Skywalker about the Imperials, allowing the young Rebel to escape capture. thumb|right|200px|Camie Camie was portrayed by Koo Stark in A New Hope, though her scenes were not used in the final film release.
