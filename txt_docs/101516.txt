Neetakka clan
1072

The Neetakka clan was a Rodian clan on the planet Rodia that emerged around the time of the Galactic Civil War. The name "Neetakka" meant "Ultimate Hunters" in Rodese, following the trend of younger clans to take grandiose names. Despite rising among modern urban environment, the members of the clan dedicated themselves only to their species's main ancestral activity of hunting and refusing to pursue other careers. As such, they spent all their time honing its hunting skills to live up to its name. A few other clans considered the clan to be bloodthirsty goons, despite grudgingly acknowledging their excellence in hunting. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Rodian clans
