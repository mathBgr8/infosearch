There are five known ships with the name Ardent: *Ardent, an MC40a light cruiser in the Alliance Fleet. *Ardent, an MC80 Liberty type Star Cruiser in the Alliance Fleet. *Ardent-class fast frigate, a warship in Fel's and Krayt's Empires. *Ardent IV, a Republic cruiser in the Stark Hyperspace War.
