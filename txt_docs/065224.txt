Dugo Bagy
1573

The Sullustan con artist, Dugo Bagy was solicited for information about Lando Calrissian's operations by Han Solo and Chewbacca while at Riebold's Foam and Sizzle. Bagy stated that Calrissian was mining platinum on Kerane's Folly, an asteroid in the Hoth system. This was largely a cover, however. It was primarily being used as a training ground of sorts for smugglers trying to evade Kyp Durron's Dozen-and-Two Avengers. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Con artists Category:Males Category:Sullustans
