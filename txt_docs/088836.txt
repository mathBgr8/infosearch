TelBrinTel Corporation
1426

TelBrinTel Corporation was a droid manufacturer based in the Core Worlds known for its specialist and highly accurate science droids. Its products were commonly found in universities and military research laboratories. The company expanded its product line with paramedic and information-collating units. *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids *The Essential Guide to Droids *C-3PO: Tales of the Golden Droid *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid manufacturers
