Reciprocal apprenticeship
2273

thumb|Kyle Katarn and Mara Jade training in [[Lightsaber combat/Legends|lightsaber combat as part of their reciprocal apprenticeship.]] Reciprocal apprenticeship was a form of mutual apprenticeship between Jedi. A facet of the Concordance of Fealty, a ceremonial expression of trust by the exchange of the lightsabers of two Jedi,SkyeWalkers: A Clone Wars Story it had become rarely-practiced following the Cleansing of the Nine Houses in the Old Sith Wars conflicts. To complete her training in the Force after abandoning its dark side, the former Emperor's Hand Mara Jade sought out Jedi Knight Kyle Katarn and undertook a reciprocal apprenticeship with him, creating a bond between the two that allowed Jade to redeem Katarn from the dark side of the Force in 10 ABY.The events of Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith, including the mission to Dromund Kaas, take place five years after those of Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II, which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 5 ABY, five years before 10 ABY. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Jedi training
