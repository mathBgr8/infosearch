Pat Paris
1650

thumb|right|250px|Illustration from [[The Baby Ewoks' Picnic Surprise]] Pat Paris is an artist who illustrated several children's books and other works featuring Ewoks. Paris was responsible for much of Lucasfilm's "cute Ewok" art found in books and other materials in 1983 and 1984, before later works began to be more directly inspired by the Ewoks animated series. *The Baby Ewoks' Picnic Surprise *Three Cheers for Kneesaa!: An Ewok Adventure *The Ewoks' Hang-Gliding Adventure *Wicket Finds a Way: An Ewok Adventure *The 1984 Ewok Calendar *"The Electric Company Guide to Endor" Category:Cover artists
