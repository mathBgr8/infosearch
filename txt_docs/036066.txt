74-Y
1498

The 74-Y was a popular model of civilian speeder bike manufactured by the Aratech Repulsor Company. It was built for speed rather than survivability. During the Clone Wars, the company began producing a militarized version of the bike known as the 74-Z speeder bike. Rejecting the BARC speeders of the Grand Army of the Republic, the Galactic Empire used the older, 74-Z model.
