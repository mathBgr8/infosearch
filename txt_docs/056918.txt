Light and Shadow was a story written for the Star Wars Adventure Journal by Paul Danner. It was never published, but was later released on the Internet. It was set in the time of the Great Sith War and dealt with a Force-sensitive young girl helping a fallen Jedi Knight come to terms with himself. |vehicles= }} Category:Star Wars Adventure Journal fiction
