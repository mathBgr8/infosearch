Adept (hilt)
1066

thumb|250px|The Adept hilt. The Adept was a type of lightsaber hilt that was commonly used by Jedi initiates and Knights who served with the New Jedi Order. It was designed to be used by beings with multiple fingers and an opposable thumb on the operating hand. Extra protection to the user was given by a guard below the emitter.
