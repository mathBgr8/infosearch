Keezo Stoolee
1364

Keezo Stoolee was a male Weequay and a prisoner at the penal colony on Oovo IV. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by the governor Kuchcel of an unknown planet for destroying their millennium celebration. This bounty was claimed by the Mandalorian-turned Bounty hunter Jango Fett.
