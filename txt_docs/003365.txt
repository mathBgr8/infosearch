Greel tree
1624

thumb|right|250px|Greel tree forest A greel tree was a type of tree native to Pii III and Pii IV, the only planets with the proper ecosystem to support the fastidious trees. The Greel Wood Logging Corporation, based in the Pii system, maintained a monopoly on harvesting them. The crimson timber&mdash;greel wood&mdash;was in great demand as a luxury item. Industrial Automaton designed a logging droid to specifications for the Greel Wood Logging Corporation, which used them on the rolling hillsides of Pii III.
