Pride of the Senate
2331

The Pride of the Senate was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser, commanded by Captain Soontir Fel during his third tour of duty in the Imperial Navy. It was one of three Dreadnaught-class cruisers present at the Battle of Nar Shaddaa. The Pride of the Senate took fairly minimal damage in the battle.
