Polyhedron 170
1310

Polyhedron 170 is an issue of Polyhedron magazine. It appeared as part of Dungeon Magazine 111. It featured a source article about the first story arc of Star Wars Droids written by Abel G. Peña and Rich Handley. The article was called Star Wars Droids: The Adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO, and was illustrated by Jeffery Carlisle.
