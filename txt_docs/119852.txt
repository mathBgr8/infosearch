The droids were used by the Imperial military. Designed for infantry support, the ZQ was propelled by a repulsorlift engine and came standard with two blaster cannons, each more than capable of dispatching armored troops, and a miniature concussion missile launcher. The ZQ also came with four additional hardpoints for mounting extra weapons, allowing for mission flexibility. Widely deployed in the early days of the Empire, the Imperials would later claim that the droid series had been eventually phased out. However, rumors of their continued use still persisted.
