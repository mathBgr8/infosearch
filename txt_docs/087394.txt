Huxlings
1642

The Huxlings were a group smugglers who worked for Jerf Huxley as of 22 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion By that year the group had worked exclusively with Talon Karrde for the last decade, but Karrde ended his working relationship with them when he chose to become a more legitimate businessman. When Mara Jade Skywalker delivered this news to Huxley, he and twenty Huxlings&mdash;including Bats, Fisk and Sinker&mdash;confronted Jade Skywalker in an attempt to extract a large severance payment from Karrde.
