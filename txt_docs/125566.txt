Ranrt crystal
1105

thumb|right A Ranrt crystal was a type of crystal used by the Wookiees for medicinal purposes. At one point in 1 ABY, an individual from outside the Kerritamba Village was ordered by Chief Kerritamba to gather a Ranrt crystal for an ingredient in a remedy to cure the Great Tree in the Kkowir Forest. The only known way to get one was to recover it from a sayormi witch, who were known to carry them.
