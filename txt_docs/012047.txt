Swamp wampa
2288

Swamp wampas were humanoid, semi-sentient predators, similar to the wampas of Hoth. The only major differences between the two wampa species was that the wampas of Hoth had adapted to their surroundings with heavy fur coats to survive the harsh climate of the ice world; the swamp wampas, in comparison, had lighter fur coats due to their native warm climate and were found in the swamps and marshes of Dromund Kaas.
