Barri
1333

The Barri were semi-sentient beings native to an unnamed planet in Wild Space. They were enigmatic space-faring creatures that could hitch rides on asteroids and derelict spacecraft for thousands of years in space. Their acid secretions broke down and helped them digest the asteroid they clung to.
