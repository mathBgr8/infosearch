Tela (transport)
2142

Imperial Tela was a Rebel Alliance Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport, it served aboard Nebulon-B frigate Xerxes at the Mylok IV Conflict during the Galactic Civil War. Tela fled Frigate Xerxes, carrying Rebel Officers, as Imperial starfighter pilots dealt the final death blows.
