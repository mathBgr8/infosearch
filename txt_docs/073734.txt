Liberation Day (Coruscant)
890

Liberation Day was a holiday in the New Republic that celebrated the liberation of Coruscant during the Galactic Civil War. It was celebrated with massive fireworks on Coruscant. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:New Republic holidays
