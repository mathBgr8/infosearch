Polyhedron 126
1023

Polyhedron 126 was a roleplaying magazine from October of 1997. It contained many roleplaying articles, including advice for a Star Wars LARP. The article was called "Vignette Characters & Scenes for Star Wars: Live Action Adventures" and was written by Paul Sudlow. Like all supplements printed in Polyhedron prior to 2002, the article is considered ambiguously canon. Category:Polyhedron
