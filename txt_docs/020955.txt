Dianoga pie
3753

Dianoga pie was the most popular of the dishes made of dianoga meat.The New Essential Guide to Alien Species The savory pie had a beige crust and was stuffed with a filling, and the Houk species had a particular fondness for it among other meat products. At some point, a field guide for officers of the Galactic Empire noted that Imperial chefs could use dianogas to make a tasty pie, although troops were unlikely to come back for second servings. The pies, when served with krayt milk, were the house special at the Meltdown Café on the moon Nar Shaddaa. In 10 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of the Star Wars: Dark Empire II comic book series, which include the appearance of dianoga pie in the series' second issue, to 10 ABY. the Wookiee Chewbacca visited the café. The bartender there asked him what he wanted to eat, but as he did not understand the Wookiee language, he simply gave a slice of dianoga pie and a cup filled with krayt milk to Chewbacca, noting that the pie would be tasty. Dianoga pie first appeared in the second issue of the Star Wars: Dark Empire II comic-book series, which was written by Tom Veitch, illustrated by Cam Kennedy, and published by Dark Horse Comics on January 24, 1995.
