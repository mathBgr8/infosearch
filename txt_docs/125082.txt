New Republic High Council
1691

The High Council was a body in the New Republic. Among its many functions, it received reports from the New Republic Observers, a governmental group that investigated potentially improper governmental activities. It also had a large say in matters of diplomacy and military deployment. The High Council was formed of the leading members of the New Republic Senate, including the Chief of State, and sat in curved, elevated dias at the end of the Senate. During the Caamas Document Crisis, members of the High Council included Ponc Gavrisom, Leia Organa Solo, Borsk Fey'lya, and Sien Siev. During the crisis, a majority of the High Council opposed the continued relationship with the Smugglers' Alliance, and at least three councilors took an anti-Bothan stance.
