Daniele F.
833

Daniele F. (surname withheld) is the Hyperspace member who developed the entry of Lieutenant Barrow Oicunn through the What's The Story? feature. His alias on StarWars.com is Jedi Master Daniele F, and is known as Jedi Master Daniele on Wookieepedia. A StarWars.com member since August 2002, Daniele has been a Star Wars fan since his childhood and claims to be an expert on the Saga. He is an Italian Law Student. - November 2005 Category:What's The Story? authors
