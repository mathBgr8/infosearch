Olinor
1784

Olinor was a Nebulon-B escort frigate in the service to the Rebel Alliance. She arrived at space station DS 5, presumably to stop Admiral Harkov's capture during the Battle for Honor. Instead she withdrew, losing a few Z-95 Headhunters to Imperial Ace Maarek Stele in the process.
