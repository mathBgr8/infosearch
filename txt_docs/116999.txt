Wintrium
1556

Wintrium was a liquid that spouted from the Fountain of Ancients in the Derelkoos Desert on Klatooine. As it reached the surface, it reacted with the dry desert air to create a glass-like material, appearing to be frozen in time. Wintrium became harder as it aged, and given enough time it could become nearly indestructible. During the Starstalker mission to retrieve wintrium from the Fountain, the Sith Sabers Anyul and Marjaak used their lightsabers to cut wintrium from the sculpture, although even with their lightsabers it was nearly impossible to cut.
