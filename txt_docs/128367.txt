Ibarsi knife
665

An Ibarsi knife was a long blade favored by the Rabaanites. Almost as long as a lightsaber, it was used primarily in close combat, but could also be thrown to lethal effect. Mika Streev was known to use an Ibarsi knife, and severed Modigal Glave's arm using it during an Imperial ambush on the Combat Moon. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Knives
