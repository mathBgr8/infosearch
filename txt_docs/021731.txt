Maze-stalk
1098

thumb|Maze-stalk Maze-stalk was the primary crop grown on Aduba-3. It was a tall grass which annually bore a red melon-like fruit. The husks could be used to make cloth. It was brought to the planet by Sacred Way colonists. Since it did not have the protective thorns that graced plants native to Aduba-3, it was highly susceptible to being eaten by animals, including high-hounds. *Star Wars (1977) 9 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Edible nuts and seeds Category:Plants of Aduba-3
