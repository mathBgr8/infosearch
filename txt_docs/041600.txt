Tregald
2483

|affiliation= }} Tregald was a game of chance played in booths. The Tralla Room casino aboard the Coral Vanda offered tregald to its patrons,Dark Force Rising as did the Casino Kuari on board the Kuari Princess. The Illuminated Aquean on Vaynai also included tregald among its numerous games of skill, luck, and chance. While trying to track down the Black Nebula crime lord Dequc on Qiaxx, Mara Jade briefly considered playing tregald as part of her cover while posing as a baroness. She discarded the idea since her Force abilities had diminished in the wake of the death of Emperor Palpatine and she could only pick up surface thoughts.Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 5 Tomrus, a New Republic technician, was adept at playing tregald. *Riders of the Maelstrom *Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 5 *Dark Force Rising *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Games
