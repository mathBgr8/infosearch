Tof Soren Hideout Pistol
1428

The Tof Soren Hideout Pistol, also known as the "Sleeve Gun," was a slugthrower produced in Tof Soren on Ord Vaxal. The Hideout pistol could fire two shots before having to be reloaded. It used a 2-shot internal magazine, with bullets inserted individually one at a time. The weapon typically used 8mm-diameter bullets, though this varied depending on the manufacturer.
