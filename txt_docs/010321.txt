Kallabow
1710

Kallabow (meaning "wild worker" in Shyriiwook) was a female Wookiee who was the daughter of Attichitcuk, the sister of Chewbacca, the wife of Mahraccor, and the mother of Lowbacca and Sirrakuk. She had blue eyes and dark-reddish brown fur.
