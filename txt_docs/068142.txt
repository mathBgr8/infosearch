Gargan family
993

The Gargan family was an Askajian family from Askaj. Members of this family had the middle name, d'al'. The family was captured and sold to Jabba the Hutt. Members of this family were Nautag and Yarna d'al' Gargan and their cubs, Luka, Leia and Nautag (II). *The Essential Guide to Alien Species *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Askajians Category:Families
