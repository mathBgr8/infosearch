Crèche (clustership)
1177

The Crèche was a Yuuzhan Vong clustership under the command of Chine-kal. Following the Battle of Gyndine, a number of prisoners of war (including Wurth Skidder, Randa Besadii Diori, and Roa) were taken aboard the Crèche to serve the developing yammosks. The vessel was destroyed during the Battle of Fondor, when Kyp Durron and Ganner Rhysode boarded the Crèche to free the prisoners held aboard.
