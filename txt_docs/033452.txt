Gallant (Victory I-class)
2779

Gallant was a Victory I-class Star Destroyer that saw service with the Republic Navy and later in the Imperial Navy. The ship first saw action at the end of the Clone Wars as a part of Jedi Master Roan Shryne's attack force during the Battle of Murkhana.
