Darktrooper
2812

Darktroopers were a group of elite stormtroopersStar Wars: Rebellion empowered with the dark side of the Force used by the Dark Empire during Operation Shadow Hand. The best troopers in the legions received limited Force training that made them far deadlier than the average stormtrooper. In the year 10 ABY,Star Wars: The Comics Companion they served under Zasm Katth and Baddon Fass on Nar Shaddaa and later on Ossus under the command of Sedriss QL.
