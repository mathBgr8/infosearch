Tyu
630

Tyu was a crime lord whose base of operations was on Kazlin 5. For a time, Tyu employed the Rodian Dheendo as one of his bounty hunters, but Dheendo decided that bounty hunting was not for him, and left Tyu's employment to join the Rebel Alliance.
