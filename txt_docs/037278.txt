Bobot Beka
2609

Bobot Beka was a Klatooinian male who lived during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. A lowly thug, Beka initially worked for an individual named Petson Lizze for some time but by 32 BBY was no longer in her employment. A bounty was subsequently placed on his head by the Toydarian administrator of Outland Transit Station, Rozatta, for vandalizing her transport skiffs with rebel graffiti. She offered 2,000 credits for his capture alive, but only 1,000 credits if he was brought in dead. The Mandalorian bounty hunter Jango Fett ran into Beka on Merchant Row in 32 BBY, where the criminal recognized and attacked him with a blaster pistol along with several other thugs. Fett, who was on the station chasing Beka's new employer, Muttani criminal Meeko Ghintee, had also been informed by Rozatta herself of Beka's bounty and claimed it, capturing the vandal alive for 2,000 credits. Bobot Beka first appeared in the video game Star Wars: Bounty Hunter, which was released by LucasArts in 2002. Beka was one of several secondary bounties that players could optionally capture on the second level of Chapter One.
