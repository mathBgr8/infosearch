Rodian razor-stick
763

thumb|right|250px|A Rodian Razor-Stick Rodian Razor-Sticks were melee weapons used by Rodian bounty hunters. They were double-bladed, each blade forming an elongated "S". The handles were attached perpendicularly to the blades. Razor-Sticks were capable of causing extreme blood loss when used. The Rodian Razor-Stick bears a resemblance to the ancient Indian dagger, the haladie. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Edged weaponry Category:Rodian weapons
