Nonak
1209

Nonak was a Cathar poacher and hunter. Along with his brother Nodon and a stormtrooper deserter named Burrk, they tried to hunt wampas on Hoth (a highly illegal act) in 12 ABY. Nonak cared about his brother greatly.
