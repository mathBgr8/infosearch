|races= |height=2 meters |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=Force-sensitiveJedi Academy Training Manual |lifespan= |origin=AmbriaThe Essential Guide to Planets and Moons |habitat=*Caves *Swamps, marshes, and wetlands |diet=Carnivorous |language= }} Hssiss, also called dark side dragons, were a lizard species with the ability to influence Force-sensitive beings around them. If bitten by a Hssiss, one would succumb to the effects of a dark side poison. Hssiss also had the ability to conceal themselves and become invisible, possibly doing so through the Force. thumb|251x251px|[[Ktriss, the pet hssiss of Great Bogga.]] In appearance, Hssiss looked like nothing more than particularly vicious reptiles, about three meters in length, with a green, scaly hide. In addition to those three meters, they had a meter long tail, covered in rows of spikes which stretched up past their backs and onto their necks. When in the lakes on Ambria, they hunted in pairs, grasping their prey with their strong jaws and sinking dozens of teeth into the unfortunate animal's body, while the other Hssiss sunk its jagged claws into it. Hssiss inhabited caves, swamps, marshes, and wetlands and were usually at the top of the local food chain. They were drawn to the dark side locations that spawned them and clustered in impressive numbers. Dark Lizards were a species of hssiss. thumb|right|217x217px|A Hssiss As creatures of the Force, they had a limited ability to mask their presence. Hssiss were drawn to suffering and carnage. After the Jedi Civil War and the devastation of the planet Korriban, they fed upon all the corpses left behind from the war, while the angry phantoms of the Sith&mdash;too weak to influence the sentient&mdash;took these Hssiss as thralls to their will, fending off all intruders who landed on the planet. thumb|A Hssiss Hssiss originated as savage reptilian predators on Ambria, and were one of the few creatures to survive the planet's destruction. They lived in and near Lake Natth, where Jedi Master Thon had imprisoned the dark side energies that had plagued the planet for millennia. The dark spirits then began to possess the creatures, turning them into something much fouler&mdash;it was said that these "dark side dragons" aided those who used the dark side of the Force. Eventually, Great Bogga began to use them as body guards and pitted them against those who had failed him. When he realized how effective and vicious they were, he had them transported off-world and sold in bulk. Soon, Hssiss could be found all over the galaxy, especially on worlds where the dark side was strong, including Korriban, MimbanThe Temple Guardian from Splinter of the Mind's Eye was identified as a hssiss in The Official Star Wars Fact File. and Vjun. They would eventually be used by the Sith as warbeasts.Book of Sith: Secrets from the Dark Side They were found on many planets throughout the galaxy, including Korriban, Oricon, Ambria, Mimban and Vjun. Their prevalence was probably the result of being brought with Sith armies in their many wars. The Jedi philosopher Tionne Solusar once met with a Hssiss on the planet Ambria. She killed it by removing its head with her lightsaber, but did not escape unscathed. The Hssiss had slashed her with its claws, and only a Jedi healing trance proved able to remove the venom. This led her to believe that the poison was spawned from the dark side of the Force. In Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, Ludo Kressh's tomb on Korriban contains an infinite hssiss spawn next to a dead Jedi. By looting the body, two Hssiss spawn. This is the basic level up glitch.
