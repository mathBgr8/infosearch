Phirk
1720

Phirk was a male Quara Aqualish and a swoop pilot for the Hidden Beks on Taris during 3956 BBY. Phirk's track record was easily beaten by Revan, who raced with a prototype accelerator that gave him an advantage over veteran swoop racers. He was present when Revan was declared victor of the competition and witnessed how he was betrayed.
