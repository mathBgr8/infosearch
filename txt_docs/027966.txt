Ando Project
1115

The Ando Project was an Galactic Empire operation to harvest Andoan mineral-fish to provide a cheap method of extracting minerals essential to the production of laser weapons. The Bountiful collected the fish, which were then transferred to the freighter Eclipse where they were processed and shipped to Uridia. Once there, the fish were placed in special tanks and adapted to process uridium from the Uridian soil. Captain Amarith of the Rebel Alliance was tipped off to the Imperial operation on Ando, and led a team of Alliance agents from Chiron Base to disrupt the project.
