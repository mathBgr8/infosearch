Aqualis Base
1391

Aqualis Base or Aqualis Baralou Algae Processing Plant #T-18 was a bestrum algae harvesting and food processing plant on the planet Baralou. Officially it was owned by the Aqualis Food Conglomerate. Unofficially, Aqualis Base was an Alliance to Restore the Republic food production plant. The base was managed by Fez Devvol and staffed by thirty workers who harvested the algae using aquapods. Over one hundred droids were responsible for the maintenance of the base.
