Equa Felens
1416

Equa Felens was a Human male who served in the Rebel Alliance. He was a Major in the Rebel Alliance and served in the Brak sector. Prior to joining the Rebellion, he attended the Bacrana Educational Institute where he met Dara Gion. The two fell in love and later participated in an anti-Empire protest on Bacrana.
