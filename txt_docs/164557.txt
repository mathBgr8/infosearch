Unidentified Jedi Master (Humanoid)
2398

A male Jedi Master served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as a member of the Jedi High Council during the Clone Wars. A Force-sensitive was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. During the Clone Wars, a conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, he was appointed as a member of the Jedi High Council, the governing body of the Order. During his time on the Council, the Kaleesh cyborg General Grievous was revealed to be the Supreme Commander of the Droid Armies. Believing Grievous to be a true threat, he supported Padawan Flynn Kybo's assertion that the cyborg should be targeted by the Order for assassination. Ultimately, the High Council decided against Kybo's plan.
