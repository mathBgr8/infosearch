Kalonterium
601

Kalonterium was an ore mined on Inysh. The Galactic Empire strip-mined kalonterium during their rule of the planet. It was considered a low-grade ore and was used in the production of weapons and construction materials before the advent of doonium and meleenium. *Alien Encounters Category:Ores
