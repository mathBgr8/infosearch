It was a gas giant orbited by thirty-two moons, itself circling the star Muunilinst. The entirety of the Muunilinst system was located along the hyperlane known as the Braxant Run. Havath Prime was first mentioned in the 2004 Star Wars Roleplaying Game sourcebook Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds.
