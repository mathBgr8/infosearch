That year, Team Yag'Dhul triumphed over the planet Kinyen's team in the opening round of the Galactic Cup of Limmie with two unanswered points from Richblum. He tied the league record for number of goals scored by a player in a single game with this feat. The sporting event was reported on HoloNet News on 13:3:14 GrS. Onila Richblum was mentioned in an issue of the HoloNet News feed, Volume 531, Issue 47, which was written in an in-universe perspective and published on March 14, 2002.
