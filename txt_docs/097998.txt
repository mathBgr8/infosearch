Dinah L. Moché
726

Dr. Dinah L. Moché (October 24, 1936 &ndash; May 22, 2018) was Professor of Physics and Astronomy at the City University of New York and writer of many books about astronomy. She has sold over 10 million copies in 7 languages worldwide. Dr. Moché wrote The Star Wars Question and Answer Book About Space, published by Random House in 1979. Category:Non-fiction authors
