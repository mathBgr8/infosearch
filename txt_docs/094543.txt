Gallofree
2754

The Gallofree was an Airam-owned X7 Factory located in the Mobetta system. In approximately 3 ABY, the Airam Clans joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic and allowed them to transfer the Interdictor Compellor gravity well projectors to the modified Strike-class cruiser Peregrine. During this procedure, the factory came under attack twice by the Galactic Empire, first a comparatively small scout group, then another, larger one.
