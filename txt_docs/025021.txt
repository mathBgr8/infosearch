Loowil Galt
3870

Loowil Galt was a Human male, originally from the planet Naboo, who served as a police officer of the Coruscant Police Force in the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Loowil Galt was a Human male who originated from the planet Naboo. He later moved to the Galactic Republic capital of Coruscant and became a police officer with the Coruscant Police Force. During his career, Galt arrested a negotiator from a defense contractor resulting in the collapse of a business deal. The Techno Union, a hopeful participant in the collapsed deal, issued a bounty on Galt for 2,000 credits to paid for Galt's death. In 32 BBY, Mandalorian bounty hunter Jango Fett found Galt in Coruscant's Financial District. Fett managed to shoot and kill Galt and collected on the bounty. Galt was a Human male with dark eyes and light skin. When on duty, he wore his CSF uniform and carried a blaster. Loowil Galt first appeared in Star Wars: Bounty Hunter a single player game released on December 5, 2002. The player can choose to pursue Loowil Galt's bounty though it is not required to move to the next level. Galt had an entry in Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide which was published on November 26, 2002.
