Kono Nolan
2936

Kono Nolan was a Human male scientist who worked for the Galactic Republic on Manaan during the time of the Jedi Civil War. During his work for the Galactic Republic on Manaan, Nolan served as the chief scientist at the secret Hrakert Station on Manaan's ocean floor as of 3956 BBY. When it was overrun by insane Selkath he, along with Sami, was one of the few survivors that were saved by the amnesiac Revan during his search for the Star Map. Despite his position as a scientist for the Republic, Nolan was amoral, against believing the ethical consequences of his work. He advocated using an experimental poison to kill the Progenitor, and was furious when Revan, following Sami's advice, chose to destroy the facility instead. If the player takes the dark side path, he can have Revan poison the Progenitor and/or kill either or both of the scientists.
