ST 007
1345

ST 007 was a Lambda-class shuttle used by the Imperial Navy. The "ST" in the shuttle's designation stood for "space transport". White Imperial logos adorned the upper parts of the port and starboard wings, while a black "007" was painted on both sides of the dorsal wing. Although the accuracy of its weapons were below the norm for most vessels of its class, ST 007 more than made up for it with the increased explosive power of its weapons, not to mention that it carried proton torpedo launchers normally installed on Imperial patrol vehicles. Since power was diverted from the shields to its ordnance, electronic countermeasures were installed to boost the shuttle's defensive systems.
