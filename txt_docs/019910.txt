Lommite Limited
1413

Lommite Limited (LL) was a Dorvallan lommite mining company who was a rival of fellow ore mining company, InterGalactic Ore. When Wilhuff Tarkin was governor of Eriadu, which had a shipbuilding lommite economy, he arranged a competition with InterGalactic Ore for Lommite Limited. The competition was a race to deliver ore to Eriadu. Both competed well, but the barges were sabotaged by the Toom clan and crashed into each other. The Trade Federation quickly took over both companies and merged them into Dorvalla Mining in 33 BBY.
