Thyfonian
927

The Thyfonian was a modified Lambda-class T-4a shuttle. It had high capacity shield generators and upgraded laser cannons. It was to be Fliry Vorru's private escape shuttle, however Ysanne Isard commandeered it to be her private escape shuttle during the final stages of the Battle of Thyferra.
