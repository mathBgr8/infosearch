Outland Mining
672

Outland Mining was a mining company that existed during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. It had a private large fleet, however it was eventually dissolved by the Galactic Senate following the Battle of Naboo due to the threat posed by organizations with cooperate militaries like the Trade Federation. *Rogue Planet *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mining companies
