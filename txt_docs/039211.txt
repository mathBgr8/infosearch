Pappfak
1153

A Pappfak was the name of an alien species known for possessing long, blue-colored tentacles. Pappfaks could regularly be found inhabiting the marketplaces at Mos Eisley on the planet, Tatooine. They may have had three sexes: at the wedding of Lady Valarian, musician Doikk Na'ts of the Modal Nodes observed three of them in what he described as a "prenuptial embrace." *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
