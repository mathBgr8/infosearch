Koruu
1743

Koruu was a furred pirate who worked as a member of thugs Lemo and Sanda's gang on Arcan IV. In 3 ABY, Koruu was with the gang at a cantina when they were approached by disguised Rebel agents Lando Calrissian and Chewbacca, who were searching for information on the whereabouts of their friend Han Solo.
