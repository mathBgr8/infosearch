|water= |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species=A one-eyed species |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government=Rivalled clans |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Gryphon was a mountainous planet located in the Inner Rim, where war among its clans had long been a way of life. During the rise of the Galactic Empire, the native Brock Lar had been the highest-ranking warrior in his clan, but after the death of his youngest son, he realized that there was no hope for peace. Around 0 BBY, he chose to escape the clutches of death and seek out a peaceful world where his family could begin a new life. The people of Alderaan listened to Brock’s story and welcomed his family with open arms.
