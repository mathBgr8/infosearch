Emerald shark
1917

The emerald shark was a huge species of shark. Multiple emerald sharks were part of the rainbow-colored school of fish that dwelt within a central column of water in an aquarium located on the Outer Rim TerritoriesThe Essential Atlas &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Dubrillion system planet Dubrillion in 25 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin, during which emerald sharks were present in the Dubrillion Aquarium, to 25 ABY. Emerald sharks were mentioned in Michael A.
