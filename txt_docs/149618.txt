Dromath
3102

Captain Dromath was a Rebel and New Republic officer who met Dynba Tesc shortly after the Battle of Endor when she was rescued from Garqi along with Lai Nootka during Corran Horn's Great Evacuation operation. He later married Tesc and fathered a son with her, Rade Dromath before his death during the Thrawn campaign. }} Captain Dromath was an officer who served the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Shortly after the Battle of Endor. Dromath witnessed Corran Horn's actions during his Great Evacuation operation on Garqi that led to Dynba Tesc's escape from the planet. Impressed with Horn's piloting skills, Dromath encouraged the Corellian to look into joining Rogue Squadron which was being reformed and in need of good pilots. He and Horn separated, but Dromath and Tesc became closer. They fell in love, married and had a child together, Rade Dromath.
