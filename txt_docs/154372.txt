After Artom
596

"After Artom" (A.A) was a system of time calculation used in the Cularin system to measure years. Cularin and Almas both used this method to calculate the passage of time. The calculations began with the rediscovery of the Cularin system by Reidi Artom in 232 BBY. *Living Force Campaign Guide Category:Calendars
