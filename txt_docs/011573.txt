Gullipud
2131

Gullipuds were bulky, loose-skinned, self-inflating amphibians native to the swamps of Naboo. As they were small and easy prey for other animals, like pikobis, gullipuds would extend their spine to make themselves harder to catch and swallow. When inflated, gullipuds actually bounced.
