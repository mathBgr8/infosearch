Jolee
1311

Jolee was the daughter of the Jedi Master Dorin Se'ol and his wife Vorena, and was born on the planet Volik during the early years of the Galactic Empire. The waterfall Jolee Falls was named after her. Jolee once boarded a merchant ship that stopped briefly on Volik, so Master Se'ol sent a group of his students to find her and bring her back home.
