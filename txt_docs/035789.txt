Imperial tanker
2270

Imperial tankers were long, tube-shaped transports in use by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. One of their uses was transporting tibanna gas. At least one participated in the raid on Bespin.Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader Imperial Tankers appear in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader.
