Easal Mar
1346

Easal Mar was a famous Jedi Knight who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic during the time proceeding the Ruusan Reformation and the sweeping changes within the Order. Unlike the bulk of the Order's members, Mar was trained aboard the mobile training academy called the Chu'unthor and attained knighthood aboard the vessel.
