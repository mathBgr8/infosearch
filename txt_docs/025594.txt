During the early years of the reign of the Galactic Empire, which began in 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology it served as the Tagge Company's primary base for operations in the Greater Javin region. By the time not long after the Battle of Hoth of 3 ABY, Planet Dreams, Incorporated had custom-developed and sold to the House of Tagge a compound at Doaskin, with the company's primary saleswoman Treva Horme being credited with seeing the deal through. The name "Doaskin" was introduced in reference to House of Tagge's compound mentioned in Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game supplement "Rough and Tundra," which was published on Wizards.com on March 25, 2004. 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia subsequently established it as a planet.
