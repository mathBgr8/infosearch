The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Yavin and Evacuation of Yavin to 0 ABY and 0.5 ABY respectively. |series=Pizzazz |preceded by= |followed by=The Kingdom of Ice }} "The Keeper's World" is a serialized comic story published through nine issues of Marvel Comics' magazine Pizzazz from October 1977 to June 1978. The story was reprinted in the UK in Star Wars Weekly 47–50 in 1978&ndash;79, and in the 1999 limited-release comic Star Wars 0. Sometime after the Battle of Yavin, Luke and Leia travel toward the Rebel base in the Akuria system to insure against a sudden Imperial counterattack on Yavin 4. En route to the planet, a mistake by R2-D2, who appears to not have been properly repaired after the Battle of Yavin, causes the ship to fly wildly off course.
