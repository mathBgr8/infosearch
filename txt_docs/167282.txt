Independent Systems
923

The Independent Systems were star systems as yet unincorporated into the Galactic Empire. Aquilae was one of the last remaining. *The term "Independent Systems" appears in one of the earliest versions of the saga, the Journal of the Whills, Part I in the Alliance of Independent Systems. *The "Independent Systems" phrase was resurrected for Attack of the Clones in the name of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. *The Star Wars rough draft *The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film Category:Star system groups
