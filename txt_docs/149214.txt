Snow-flier
829

The snow-flier was a type of modified airspeeder developed by the Chiss Ascendancy which was adapted for use in the cold of the Chiss homeworld of Csilla. They appeared as floating vertical disk which possessed four spindly legs which were used for landing operations. They were equipped with blaster cannons which were used for both military and police duties. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee Category:Airspeeder models Category:Vehicle classes of the Chiss Ascendancy
