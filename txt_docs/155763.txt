Omniprobe
693

Omniprobes were special devices that were designed to counteract probe droids by tracking them down and neutralizing them. Ken deviced a particularly efficient omniprobe as a homework assignment under the tutelage of DJ-88. The Alliance later employed this omniprobe on their Yavin 4 base to reduce Imperial incursions. *The Lost City of the Jedi *Prophets of the Dark Side *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Probe droid models
