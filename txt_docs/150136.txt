Community Heap
650

A Community Heap was a pile of scavenged items gathered by a community of Jenet. Each Jenet in the community would contribute things it scavenged. Community Heaps were administered by the community's government and often included discarded food, broken tools, and household goods. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Jenet culture
