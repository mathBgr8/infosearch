|gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Fair |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire **Imperial Intelligence |masters= |apprentices= }} Evax was a fair-skinned Human with brown hair who served as an Imperial officer during the Galactic Civil War fought between the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic. By the year 0 BBY, he became a leader in Imperial Intelligence, having a proven track record for predicting Alliance Fleet movements, and his coordination of starship maneuvers saved many vulnerable bases. In 0 BBY, Evax was aboard the Imperial Death Star I battle station during the Battle of Yavin, where Alliance starfighters attacked the Death Star over the Yavin system. He was killed in the station's subsequent destruction. Evax wore the standard white uniform for Imperial Intelligence members, with a rank insignia plaque adorned with three blue and three amber tiles.
