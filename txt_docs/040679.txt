, which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars Missions |preceded by=Star Wars Missions 7: Ithorian Invasion |followed by=Star Wars Missions 9: Revolt of the Battle Droids }} Star Wars Missions 8: Togorian Trap is the eighth adventure book in the Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game. It was published by Scholastic in April 1998. You are a bounty hunter. The Galactic Empire has sent you to Togoria to trap the Rebels Han Solo, Princess Leia, and Luke Skywalker. To find them, you must travel through the wilds of Togoria, where fierce creatures stalk their prey. A great reward awaits you &ndash; if you can survive. Your mission: to ensnare the Rebels and bring glory to the Empire. Good luck. As the Punishing One enters the Thanos system, Han Solo, in the Millennium Falcon, disables it, rendering it useless. The occupants, Dengar, Udin, and Eron Stonefield, planned to capture Solo and claim the large bounty on his head, but find themselves outsmarted. Forced to abandon the Punishing One, they use an escape pod to reach Togoria. The Falcon approaches the city of Caross with its passengers: Chewbacca, Leia Organa, Luke Skywalker, R2-D2, and C-3PO. The Margrave-sister, Ffaseer effet Ihsss, meets with Leia, and invites her and her companions to a royal reception. Meanwhile, the bounty hunters land on Togoria, bruised and shaken. While tracking through the forest, the bounty hunters are set upon by Fassool's hunting band, who agree to help him find and capture Solo. One of the bounty hunters joins Fassool's band, and makes their way to Caross. Upon reaching Caross, the hunter subdues Solo's allies, and grabs Grubba the Hutt. Solo gets the upper hand for a short while, but Grubba saves the hunter. The Rebels are then taken to the Star Destroyer Valiant. The Valiant, however, has been overrun by Rebel forces. The bounty hunters unwittingly deliver the Rebels into the hands of other Rebels masquerading as Imperials, collecting counterfeit credits.
