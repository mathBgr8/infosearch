It was the base of a division of the InterGalactic Banking Clan, and was the site of the Battle of Muunilinst during the Clone Wars, where the Galactic Republic took the planet. Muunilinst was first mentioned in Star Wars canon in the Databank entry for Clu Lesser on StarWars.com. Muunilinst first appeared in Specter of the Past, a Star Wars Legends novel written by Timothy Zahn as the first entry in Star Wars: The Hand of Thrawn Duology'Specter of the Past that was released on November 3, 1997.
