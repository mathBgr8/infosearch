Sprig Cheever
1169

Sprig Cheever (nicknamed Cheeve) was a bearded Human male KeyBed player of a band at Happy's Landing on Druckenwell. He was the partner of the band's singer, Twilit Hearth. The couple helped Tinian I'att Azur-Jamin hide from Imperial detection when I'att took to the stage in Hearth's place. Category:Druckenwell residents Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Musicians
