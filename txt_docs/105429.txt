Ossus dueling lens
668

An Ossus dueling lens was a type of lightsaber focusing lens that was widely used at the Ossus Jedi Enclave. It was intended to create a highly focused blade for providing fine-tuned control over one's lightsaber. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Lightsaber lenses
