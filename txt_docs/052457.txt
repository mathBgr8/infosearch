Lonnaw was a planet located in the Lonnaw system, within the Droma sector of the Mid Rim and New Territories regions. The Entralla Route hyperlane linked the planet to the Orinda and Obredaan systems, with at least five more systems being located between Lonnaw and the world of Orinda. thumb|left|200px|Lonnaw was captured by the Imperial Remnant in 12 ABY. As the first scheduled stop in a three-week-long shakedown cruise, the Outbound Flight visited Lonnaw for a routine navigational check, leaving afterward for the Colonies' Argai Minor system. Lonnaw fell within the borders of the New Republic by 12 ABY. It was seized by the Imperial Remnant that year, however, as part of a series of conquests of Republic systems. Lonnaw was the most Coreward of the Remnant's captured holdings from Orinda. The Imperial forces entrenched themselves at Lonnaw but eventually fell back to Orinda after a number of skirmishes against the forces of the New Republic. Lonnaw was first mentioned in the 2006 novel Outbound Flight, written by Timothy Zahn.
