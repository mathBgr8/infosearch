When she was only a year old, her father was forced to flee Rhamalai to avoid being caught by Imperials, leaving his family behind. Nadra grew up believing her father was dead. She wanted to train as a medic and healer at the Bellorin Medical Academy on Seitia Prime, but stayed with her ill mother.
