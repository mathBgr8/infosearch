Santherian tenho-root
869

Santherian tenho-root was a plant whose extract was also a potent drug. It was often used by abusers who desired a sense of escapism. When taking the drug, users often described it's effects as similar to that of watching a sunset. The extract was immensely effective on Falleen, who would experience intense psychotropic attacks after taking it. *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *Planet of Twilight Category:Pharmaceutical plants
