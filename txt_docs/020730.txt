Russ Manning
1677

Russ Manning (January 5, 1929 &ndash; December 1, 1981) was the writer and penciller of numerous Star Wars black-and-white newspaper comic strips published in 1979 and 1980. His works were later colorized and re-released by Dark Horse Comics in the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series.
