Plee'h
1134

Plee'h was a group of BFF-1 bulk freighters. During the supply shortage following the sortie from Yavin 4, they were about to receive grain from seemingly defected Ghorin, near the edge of his territory. However the Imperials had already mined the area around the Hyperspace Navigation Buoy of their arrival. When that was learned, it was too late since the freighters were already in hyperspace.
