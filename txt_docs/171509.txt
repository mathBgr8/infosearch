*New Republic Defense Fleet **32nd Cruiser Squadron |masters= |apprentices= }} S'lixike was a New Republic Captain who served as the commander of the 32nd Cruiser Squadron following the Battle of Endor. Captain S'lixike was part of the briefing to determine the New Republic's response to the Corporate Sector Authority and Galactic Empire's attempt to seize the Kalinda system.
