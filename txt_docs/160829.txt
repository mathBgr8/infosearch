Mikish
1250

Mikish was the most infamous platform city on the planet Cularin. It was also the seat of Restimar Mining Corporation, from which its mining operations on Tilnes were run.Living Force Campaign Guide Many of Mikish's police officers were corrupt, and they all had outside jobs of one kind or another. The city also had looser weapons ordinances than the other platform cities. Mikish was the center of Nirama's criminal enterprises on the planet. Riboga the Hutt used to have a mansion there, and Nirama operated from that mansion when he was on Cularin, as when he met with the people responsible of the death of one of his pilots in 32 BBY. *Living Force Campaign Guide Category:Cities on Cularin
