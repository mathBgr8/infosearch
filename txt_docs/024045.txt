Starskiff
785

The Starskiff was a personal transport vessel produced by the Koensayr Corporation prior to the Dark Nest Crisis. The Starskiff was a sleek starship, with a cockpit large enough for a pilot and a co-pilot. Jacen Solo owned a Starskiff during the lead-up to the Swarm War, using it to travel between meetings with Tenel Ka on Hapes and his family and friends in the Jedi order.
