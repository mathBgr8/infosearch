ScreenThemes
1009

ScreenThemes was a desktop wallpaper and screensaver program for personal computers running Microsoft Windows. ScreenThemes provided still images in resolutions up to 1600x1200 (with some images able to be viewed in resolutions higher than advertised), allowing scenes from movies and other media to be seen in clearer detail than many other sources could allow. Star Wars ScreenThemes were available beginning in 2002, with packages available from all six original theatrical films and the Clone Wars micro-series. Category:Star Wars media
