Sol Mon
1903

Sol Mon was a Human male and the leader of a pirate gang that rose to power during the Galactic Empire's fragmentation following the Battle of Endor. He engaged in several trips across the galaxy, acquiring a fleet of small, black-market-modified starfighters and the pirates to crew them. He was hired by Grappa the Hutt to raid various cargo transports, both those of the New Republic and the Empire.
