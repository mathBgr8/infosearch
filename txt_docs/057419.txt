Clan-Vorsazg
715

Clan-Vorsazg was an Ubese clan from the Southern Wastes of Uba IV. Circa 3 ABY, they were led by Savax, who styled himself War-Master of the Southern Wastes. They were part of the ill-fated alliance that warlord put together to attack the "yrak pootzck" Ubese of Ubertica. Their forces were utterly defeated by the Galactic Empire during the strike against Ubertica. *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook Vorsazg
