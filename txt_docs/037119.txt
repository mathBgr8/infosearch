He was later transferred to the Droid Control Ship Vuutun Palaa, dying when it was destroyed during the Battle of Naboo. thumb|left|250px|Sil Unch, [[Tey How/Legends|Tey How, and Jull Dremon seated on the Saak'ak bridge]] By the year 32 BBY, Lieutenant Sil Unch served as a comm officer aboard the Trade Federation flagship, the modified Lucrehulk-class LH-3210 cargo freighter Saak'ak. During the Invasion of Naboo, he was assigned by Gunray to the Droid Control Ship Vuutun Palaa when the Saak'ak left the Naboo system, where he was killed when it was destroyed by Anakin Skywalker during the Battle of Naboo. Specialized in battle droid control programming and interfaces, Sil Unch did not enjoy being commanded by Captain Daultay Dofine. A Neimoidian, he had mottled gray skin and red eyes. Originally created for the 1999 film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, Sil Unch was first identified in the 2001 Boonta Eve Podrace expansion set for the Star Wars Legends Young Jedi Collectible Card Game. However, Decipher claimed he was the Neimoidian sitting in the middle of the Vuutun Palaa bridge, erroneously depicting him using an image of Tey How, an error repeated in its Theed Palace Limited expansion set for the Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
