|masters= |apprentices= }} B'inka Fibuna was a Twi'lek female native to the planet Ryloth. She was enslaved by the Hutt crime lord Ka'Pa some point prior to the Battle of Naboo in 32 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of , which includes the Boonta Eve Classic, to 32 BBY. While enslaved, she worked as a dancer on Ka'Pa's estate on the planet Nal Hutta.
