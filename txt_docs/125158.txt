Fighter Command Liaison
686

Fighter Command Liaison was the position taken by Wedge Antilles after being promoted to general following the Battle of Bilbringi. Antilles accepted the position only after a compromise with Gial Ackbar allowed him to retain the right to personally lead Rogue Wing into combat. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook Category:Military ranks
