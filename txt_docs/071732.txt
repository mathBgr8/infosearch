Dark Trooper hyperspace capsule
1791

250px|thumb|Launch pods approaching [[Talay/Legends|Talay.]] Dark troopers used hyperspace capsule vehicles when serving the Galactic Empire, which were were launched from the Arc Hammer. One devastating feature of the launch pods was the ability to explode on contact with a Rebel planet, which softened the Rebel forces just prior to the attack on Tak Base. As their name indicated, they had the ability to travel through hyperspace. Clint Bajakian created the sound effect for the capsule launch, as heard in Dark Forces. His sources were the launch sound effect of the Viper probe droid, combined with the sound of a Snowspeeder (both from Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back) and his own voice with tweaked frequency, for "a semihuman personality".Dark Forces Official Player's Guide *Star Wars: Dark Forces *Star Wars: Dark Forces Remaster *Dark Forces Official Player's Guide Category:Starship classes Category:Starship classes of the Galactic Empire
