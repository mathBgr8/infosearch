He was part of the Imperial Shadow wing and flew under the call sign Shadow Two. When not tweaking the control systems of his TIE/LN starfighter, PS-29-2 studied Rebel starfighter schematics. His fighter was used to enforce blockades, and had exquisite handling for slashing attacks on unescorted transports and freighters. PS-29-2 wore a black TIE pilot helmet and jumpsuit.
