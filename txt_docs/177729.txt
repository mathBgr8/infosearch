Republic Bureau of Xenology
546

The Republic Bureau of Xenology was a Galactic Republic-funded organization which studied and investigated new species. Having very little information on Jedi Master Thon's species, the Tchuukthai, the Bureau gave the race the arbitrary name "Wharl." *Tales of the Jedi Companion *Far Horizons Category:Galactic Republic ministries and bureaus
