Oozhith
1215

The oozhith was a Yuuzhan Vong creature that was worn by members of the Shaper caste. It had cilia on the outside as well as the inside, which sought out and fed on microorganisms in the air, creating a subtle shimmer to the surface as they moved. The full-body oozhith was only worn by Master Shapers, however, with Shaper Adepts wearing a sleeveless version that ended above the knees. The oozhith had a small pouch for equipment. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
