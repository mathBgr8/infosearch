Wookiee-wango
1867

Wookiee-wango was an alcoholic drink made with Sullustan gin. It was considered to be stronger than Gizer ale and lum, and was commonly shaken rather than stirred. In 4 ABY, establishes the events of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, including the Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon, as set in the year 4 ABY. the Gran criminal Ree-Yees accepted a Wookiee-wango from R2-D2 while aboard Jabba Desilijic Tiure's sail barge, the Khetanna, shortly before its destruction. By 27 ABY, it was a popular drink on Coruscant. *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cocktails
