Jovan III
2706

Jovan III, often called simply Jovan,Yoda: Dark Rendezvous was a planet located in the Jovan system and was noted for the Jovan Station, built in orbit around the planet. Jovan III was a fertile planet and one of the premier agriworld of the Gordian Reach. It was crisscrossed by robo-harvesters and dotted with agricultural research labs. After the Battle of Yavin, the Galactic Empire converted the Jovan Station as the headquarters of the Blockade of Yavin.
