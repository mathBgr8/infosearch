|height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Black |skin=Green |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Luugro was a Rodian assassin present on the Outer Rim planet Taris during the Jedi Civil War between the Galactic Republic and the Sith Empire. He had black eyes, green skin, wore a red, white, and brown flight suit with a black harness and boots, and carried a blaster pistol. Luugro understood Basic and spoke Huttese. In 3956 BBY, Luugro and his Rodian partner had bounties put out for them. While the Rodians were in a Lower City apartment complex on Taris, they were followed and confronted by the famed bounty hunter Calo Nord, seeking to claim the bounties. Luugro's partner recognized Nord and warned him, while Nord informed them of the bounties. Luugro stated that Nord would have to collect it over their dead bodies. Nord retorted that it was his plan, pulled out two blaster pistols, and shot both Rodians, killing them. Revan, a former Dark Lord of the Sith with the memories of a Republic soldier, witnessed the confrontation. Luugro first appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, developed by BioWare. He appears in an unavoidable cutscene when the player enters one of the apartment rings in the Taris's Lower City. The player cannot directly interact with Luugro, and no actions regarding him will change alignment or progress plots or quests.
