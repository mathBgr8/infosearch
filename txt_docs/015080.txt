Jesra Loture
2107

}} Jesra Loture was a Human female who served as Captain of the Guard (and commander of the Senate Guard) of the Galactic Republic during the Separatist Crisis and the start of the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, Jesra was among the Senate Guards who prevent a crowd of protesters from moving into the Senate Building during the establishment of creating a Grand Army of the Republic. In order to ensure the safety of the delegates and senators, Jesra decided to proceed with force against the demonstrators. Category:Coruscanti Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Senate Guards
