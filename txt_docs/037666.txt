In the year 32 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion she was present in Dod's repulsorpod alongside him and his aide, Lufa Danak, when Senator Palpatine and Queen Padmé Amidala of Naboo accused the Trade Federation of invading their homeworld. As a mathematician, Mik Regrap would always find the most profitable way to deal with parliamentary motions. A Neimoidian, she had green skin and red eyes. Regrap wore a light gray financial officer's collar over her dark blue and light gray robe, as well as a dark gray hat. Originally created for the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, where the character's mask was sculpted by Gary Pollard, Mik Regrap first appeared in its Star Wars Legends novelization by Terry Brooks, where she was referred to simply as a "trade baron."
