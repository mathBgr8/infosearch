|language=Tionese |currency= |religious= |holiday= |anthem= |formed=Xim's empire |established=Between 25,100 BBY and 25,000 BBY |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved=23,900 BBY |restored= |affiliation= }} The Honorable Union of Desevro and Tion was a political union in the Tion Cluster. Its capital was on the planet Desevro. It was one of the feuding states that rose from the ashes of Xim the Despot's empire in the century after his death. The union was destroyed in 23,900 BBY following the Tionese War, fought by the union against the Galactic Republic and the Hutt Empire.
