EV-2-E
3055

EV-2-E was an EV-series supervisor droid manufactured by MerenData. Sometime between the years 2 BBY and 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the formation of the Alliance to Restore the Republic and its subsequent reorganization into the Alliance of Free Planets to the years 2 BBY and 4 ABY, respectively. As such, it can be determined that EV-2-E served in the Alliance Fleet's 14th Roving Line sometime between these two events. it served in the Alliance Fleet's 14th Roving Line as a droid overseer onboard the line's flagship, the Mantan Wanderer, during the Galactic Civil War. EV-2-E was first mentioned in Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook, a 1997 Star Wars Legends supplement written by Timothy S.
