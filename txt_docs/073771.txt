Chuck Hamilton
494

Chuck Hamilton is a Star Wars fan who designed N'Kata Del Gormo and the Hysalrian in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was given an honorable mention in the sixteen years and older category. He was twenty-three at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
