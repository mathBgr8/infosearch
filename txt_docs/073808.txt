|height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= *Galactic Empire **Obsidian Squadron |masters= |apprentices= }} OS-72-10 was a TIE pilot in the Imperial Navy who had at certain points served aboard the Imperial-class Star Destroyers Conquest and Thunderflare, as well as the Super Star Destroyer Executor. By 4 ABY,The Death Star II Limited set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game is set around the time of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. OS-72-10 had joined the Imperial Obsidian Squadron, flying under the call sign of Obsidian 10. By that year, Obsidian 10, stationed aboard the Thunderflare, was recalled to defend the Empire's Death Star II during its construction in the Endor system. OS-72-10 wore the standard uniform for TIE pilots. He also had no interest in advancing in rank, as it would remove him from the pilot's seat.
