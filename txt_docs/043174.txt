Brownlung
488

Brownlung was a disease found on the planet of Adumar and was similar to lung cancer or black lung. It was more common in the working classes than in the nobility, mostly because the working classes were forced to live in underground turumme-warrens. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Diseases and maladies
