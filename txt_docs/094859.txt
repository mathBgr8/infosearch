Crude phobium emitter
800

A crude phobium emitter was a lightsaber emitter matrix, crafted from Phobium and designed specifically for more aggressive Jedi and Sith (likely making it well-suited for practitioners of the fourth form of lightsaber combat). A phobium emitter is heavier and more unwieldy than most disrupting emitters, but arguably far more effective. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Lightsaber emitters
