Kagles
1269

The Kagles were a trio of sentient, brown-furred brothers who lived on the Forest Moon of Endor. They were distinguished by their differently colored shirts: one in blue, one in green, and one in yellow. They lived near Bright Tree Village and the home of Larry the Shriek. The Kagles enjoyed gambling. It is not known whether Kagle is the name of their species or their family.
