|officers= |crew=Kylan DuPre |flagship= |capital=Stormstrike |starfighters= |ground= |naval= |campaigns= |battles= |affiliation=*Chandrilan Security Force *Galactic Republic }} The Chandrila Defense Fleet was the Defense Fleet of the planet Chandrila. Admiral Hiram Drayson led the Fleet for many years during the last decades of the Republic, until he was replaced by Palpatine's military council with Kohl Seerdon during the Clone Wars. It was requisitioned by the emerging Galactic Empire and merged into the Imperial Navy under his command.
