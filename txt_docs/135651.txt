thumb|right|250px|A map of Tunisia annotated with Star Wars filming locations Tunisia is a country in North Africa. Locations in Tunisia were used to film scenes for the first six Star Wars movies except Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. The main locations are: *Chott el Jerid &mdash; exterior of the Lars homestead. *Djerba (Sidi Jemour and Ajim)&mdash; Ben Kenobi's home, Mos Eisley and the deleted scenes set in Anchorhead (Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope). *Grand Dune &mdash; the Dune Sea where R2-D2 and C-3PO crash in Episode IV. Nefta was also in this area. *Hôtel Sidi Driss &mdash; the interior of the Lars homestead. *Ksar Hadada, Ksar Medenine and Ksar Ouled Soltane &mdash; the Slave Quarters Row. *Oung Jmel &mdash; a rock seen in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, especially for Mos Espa. *Sidi Bouhlel &mdash; the canyon where Luke Skywalker meets Ben Kenobi in Episode IV.
