M'truli
1289

M'truli was a Kajain'sa'Nikto terrorist assisting in the supply of a Separatist terror net on Coruscant. He was captured by Omega Squad during a TIOPS mission. He was later unsuccessfully interrogated by Walon Vau and executed along with his comrades, Farr Orjul and Gysk. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems individuals Category:Kajain'sa'Nikto Category:Males Category:Terrorists
