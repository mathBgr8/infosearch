Ubrikkian Frigate
1576

The Ubrikkian Frigate was a patrol cruiser manufactured by Ubrikkian Industries at the Ubrikkian Yards. It required a skeleton crew of eighty-one and could hold 300 metric tons of cargo. To protect the ship, the frigate employed twenty Escort quad laser cannons. It was a popular Hutt ship and was used primarily to protect and aid Hutt interests. *Tempest Feud Category:Frigate classes Category:Hutt starship classes Category:Ubrikkian Industries products
