Reybn
1311

Sergeant Reybn was the commanding officer on an Imperial communications outpost on the planet Vaal. Stationed on Vaal for several months, he and his crew grew lazy, and preferred to sunbathe instead of writing reports. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, he saw Darth Vader entering the outpost with a pack of aggressive hyenax and silently taking the Lambda-class shuttle. *Empire 14 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Enlisted personnel of the Galactic Empire Category:Humans Category:Males
