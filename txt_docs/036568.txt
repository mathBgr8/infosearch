Code Green
1326

Code Green was a term used by Rebel Alliance intelligence. When used in a transmission, it meant that the agent was in trouble and needed immediate extraction. Alliance operatives responded to a Code Green from Agent Dana, who was undercover aboard the Star Destroyer Relentless. They arrived too late to save her from Imperial execution, but she managed to pass on information to the Alliance that Adar Tallon was alive and in hiding on the planet Tatooine. *Tatooine Manhunt Green Category:Intelligence
