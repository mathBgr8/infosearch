Rob Paulsen
2225

Rob Paulsen (born March 11, 1956) is an American voice actor who provided voices for several Star Wars video games. Paulsen is most famous as the voice of Raphael in the original Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Yakko Warner and Pinky in Animaniacs, Arthur in the second and third seasons of The Tick, Spike, Chomper, Strut, Rinkus and Thud in The Land Before Time Series, Banzai in Timon and Pumbaa, Bird Brain in T.U.F.F. Puppy and Broocoli Overlord in The High Fructose of Annoying Orange. Paulsen returned to TMNT for the 2012 relaunch series, this time in the role of Donatello.
