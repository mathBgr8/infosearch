Captain Yossarian
1175

Benjamin Christopher Norton Dawe wrote the backstories for Kalyn Farnmir, Myhr Rho, Willrow Hood and Seib Nod as well as contributing the name and other elements to the story of Romeo Treblanc appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club. He was also a StarWars.com Message Boards forum moderator on StarWars.com under the alias Captain Yossarian. - September 2005 (co-written by Aaron Sinner) - September 2005 - September 2006 - September 2007 - March 2008 Category:What's The Story? authors
