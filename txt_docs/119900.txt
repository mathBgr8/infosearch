Unidentified female Jedi Master
1671

A female Jedi Master lived sometime in the years between 800 and 200 BBY. A Force-sensitive, woman was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order sometime circa 800 BBY. Attaining the rank of Jedi Master, she eventually came to possess a lightsaber uncovered on the abandoned world of Ossus.
