Smuggler's Canyon
1329

250px|thumb|Smuggler's Canyon Smuggler's Canyon, also known as Smuggler's Gorge, was a canyon on the moon Gall, located within the Grand Trench canyons. The canyon consisted of steep slopes and high cliffs and walls. An Imperial Enclave was stationed at this barren location. Despite the scarcity of vegetation and life, a sub-species of wampa, called the cliff wampa, lived there. Dash Rendar visited Smuggler's Canyon on his quest to find Boba Fett and his cargo of Han Solo frozen in carbonite.
