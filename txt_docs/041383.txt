Orsaa
1832

Orsaa was a Rakata of Lehon who was a member of the Elders, a group of Rakata who refused to revert to cannibalism and barbarism like the tribes led by The One. He was the Keeper of History, maintaining the knowledge and history of the Infinite Empire, the empire that had once ruled and enslaved the galaxy under the rule of the Rakata.
