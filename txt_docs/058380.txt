Rego Mineral Company
1054

Rego Mineral Company was a mineral mining/prospecting corporation that existed towards the end of the Rise of the Empire era. They were perhaps best known as the discoverers of Altor 14, in the year 15 BBY. In return for a trade agreement with the native Nuiwit, the Rego Mineral Company gained rights to the planet's mineral deposits. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition Category:Mining companies
