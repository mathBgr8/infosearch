Miser-plant
638

Miser-plants were plants native to the arid planet of Kamar. Their survival in the dry climate of the Badlands was due in large part to their ability to extract moisture from the air. Any moisture that a miser-plant trapped was secreted in small organs that resided in its roots. This feature of the miser-plant could be exploited by individuals trying to survive while stranded in the Badlands. *Han Solo's Revenge *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Category:Plants of Kamar
