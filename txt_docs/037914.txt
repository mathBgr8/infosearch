The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 15
1744

The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 15 is the fifteenth issue of The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine, later rebranded as the Star Wars Insider. Issue 15 was published in Winter 1992. *"The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles: Fan Club Exclusive" *"Defenders of Dynatron City: Sneak Preview" by John S. Davis *"Learning with Lucasfilm" by Adam Schultz *"The ILM FX Team" *"Inside Lucasfilm Games" by John S. Davis and Mark Streed *"Who's Scruffy Looking?" cartoon by Lisa E. Cowan and R. Tomasic 015
