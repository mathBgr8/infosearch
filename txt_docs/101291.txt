Bogga's palace
1161

Bogga's palace was on Vo Dasha, a moon orbiting the planet Taboon. Bogga often stayed there with members of his court. It was the location of executions for the purposes of entertainment and socializing, and a hub of criminal activity in the years before the Great Sith War. *Tales of the Jedi audio drama *Tales of the Jedi 4 *Tales of the Jedi 5 *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Hutt locations Category:Palaces
