A human clone, Muzzle was created on the planet Kamino from the genetic template of bounty hunter Jango Fett, and as such had tan skin, a height of 1.83 meters (6 ft), and a weight of 80 kilograms. They were also implanted with a behavioral modification biochip by the Kaminoan scientists to ensure that they would not resist certain orders. Muzzle eventually deserted the Republic and led the mercenary organization Aurodium Sword, which typically favored jobs for providing security to Galactic Republic corporate personnel, such as Raith Sienar. thumb|left|150px|Muzzle's Star Wars Legends depiction Muzzle was mentioned in the current Star Wars canon in Collapse of the Republic, a 2019 supplement to Fantasy Flight Games' series of Star Wars Roleplaying products.
