Caal Jordan
1386

Caal Jordan was a Human male scientist who worked for the Galactic Republic during the Jedi Civil War. In 3956 BBY, he was assigned to Hrakert Station on the planet Manaan where he developed a sonic weapon for use against Firaxan sharks.
