Death Star Designer
3440

Death Star Designer was an online promotional game for Star Wars: Lethal Alliance. It allowed the player to create their own version of the first Death Star, with customizable parts including hull size, weaponry, color and so on. While some of the customizable parts were included in the game for fun (such as ferris wheels or saunas), and some for added power such as the Galaxy Gun, all the new information on the site was potential continuity, and as such was included in the Holocron continuity database.
