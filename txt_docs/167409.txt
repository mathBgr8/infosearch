It had a spherical gray shell, with small ridges along its surface. At one point, a V-1 Thermal Detonator was submerged underwater, in a location surrounded by algae. The V-1 Thermal Detonator was first mentioned in the new Star Wars canon in a weapon upgrade card of the same name, created for the 2018 Way of the Force booster pack set. The set was created for the Star Wars: Destiny card and dice game, published by Fantasy Flight Games in 2016. The card was illustrated by Lukasz Jaskolski. The V-1 Thermal Detonator originated in the Star Wars Legends continuity, and first appeared, albeit unnamed, in Pandemic Studios' Star Wars: Battlefront video game,Star Wars: Battlefront published by LucasArts on September 21, 2004.Star Wars Year By Year: A Visual History, New Edition It was later identified in the Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide,Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide a strategy guide published by Prima Games on September 28, 2004.
