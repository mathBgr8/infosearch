Incom Factory
770

The Incom Factory was located on an unknown planet, presumably in the Outer Rim Territories. Vors Voorhorian, the designer of the T-65 X-wing starfighter, used it as a place to house false shells of ships, to mislead the Empire. Category:Incom Corporation Category:Industrial facilities
