Paul Ens
1256

Originally a co-founder of TheForce.net, Paul Ens became the director of Lucas Online, responsible for the official sites like StarWars.com and leading team members including Pablo Hidalgo and Bonnie Burton. In August 2006, he left Lucasfilm to become founder of a new comic book company called Red 5 Comics and join visual effects / animation company Satellite Studios. He is also the author of the online Evasive Action webstrips, the mock website HoloNetNews.com, and co-writer of R2-D2: Beneath the Dome. He posts at the official site discussion boards under the name Ghent. Category:Webmasters
