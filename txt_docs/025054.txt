Ukon Eyste
1401

Ukon Eyste was a male Gran hitman and convicted killer who lived during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Having a death sentence on six star systems, the Republic Security Force placed a bounty worth 3,000 credits alive and 1,500 dead. The bounty was claimed by Jango Fett. Despite being labeled as armed and dangerous, Ukon Eyste is a coward.
