Jhyarra
2017

Jhyarra was a very popular dancer who performed at the GlitterGlow Nightclub and the Ace of Sabres casino. Jhyarra was one of the most popular entertainers at the casino and would put on a show for the casino patrons. She was good enough that she was one of the select few that was able to perform for the Sabre Club, the Ace's VIP group. She learned of some of the criminal activities that were occurring behind the scenes of the casino, including the slavery ring that was kidnapping patrons, and hired a group of patrons to help her escape the casino. However, her snooping had tipped off both the casino's head of security, Dunan Par'Ell, and Sibarra the Hutt's local assassin Cha-Val Sha-Vak. Both of these dangerous individuals attempted to track her down and remove her as a threat to the casino. Jhyarra was designed by Paul Danner and Bill Smith for Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy, a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game that was published by West End Games in 1997.
