Rodolfo Damaggio
706

Rodolfo Damaggio (born April 11, 1965) is a comic book and concept artist who penciled the Dark Horse Comics adaptation of Star Wars Episode I — The Phantom Menace. He later served as a concept artist for the films The Last Jedi and The Rise of Skywalker. *The Art of Star Wars: The Last Jedi *The Art of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker Category:Comic book pencillers Category:Concept artists
