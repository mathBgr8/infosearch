Grania (Brentaali)
2080

Grania was a young platinum blonde Human female from Brentaal IV who lived during the Galactic Civil War. Grania was one of Imperial Admiral Lon Isoto's concubines. On one occasion shortly before the Battle of Brentaal IV, he offered her to Baron Soontir Fel, who politely refused. In reality, she was one of Ysanne Isard's spies, and killed Isoto on her orders as part of Project Ambition following the Battle of Brentaal IV.
