Cam Kennedy
1537

Cam Kennedy is a Scottish comics artist. He was the illustrator of the Star Wars: Dark Empire, Star Wars: Dark Empire II and most of the Star Wars: Boba Fett comics series. He also illustrated Empire 7 and the covers for Classic Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back 2, Jabba the Hutt: The Gaar Suppoon Hit and Star Wars Tales 18.
