Gimer bush
922

thumb|A Gimer bush. A gimer bush was a plant that grew in the mountains on Genesia. A cane used by Jedi Master Yoda was constructed from gimer. He often chewed on the stick to consume its helpful nutrients, which also aided him in meditation. The bush also grew on Ohma-D'un and was known for its sap which could be made into a juice. The bush was also found on Kashyyyk and Dagobah. *Star Wars Episode I: The Gungan Frontier Category:Bushes Category:Pharmaceutical plants
