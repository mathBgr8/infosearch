"The Ballad of Master Vodo-Siosk Baas" was a ballad written by Tionne Solusar about the eponymous ancient Jedi Master. It recounted the betrayal and murder of Vodo-Siosk Baas by his former pupil, Exar Kun. *Young Jedi Knights: Heirs of the Force Category:Ballads
