The starfighter was flown by TIE pilot "Mauler" Mithel, and the craft was stationed aboard the first Death Star. The cockpit had twenty-seven flame emblems on it, each one representing a Rebel kill. Mithel placed a holo of his young son, Rejlii on the fighter's control yoke.
