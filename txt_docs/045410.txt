Ektra
1304

Ektra, which was also known as Ektra City, was one of many cities of the planet Metellos, and was one of the many floating cities found just above the surface of the planet. The CarsinShare and Nonsu corporations had offices there and they controlled a gladiatorial arena in the bowels of the city where gladiators such as Lowal Pulse would fight. The losers of the fight may be kicked out of the bottom of the floating city through a hole in the floor.Coruscant and the Core Worlds It was located above the Moridebo District.
