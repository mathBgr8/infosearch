Unidentified Jedi (Talasea)
2580

This individual was a Jedi who served the Jedi Order during the final years of the Galactic Republic. After surviving the initial stages of the Great Jedi Purge, this individual went into hiding on the planet Talasea. A Force-sensitive, this individual had received instruction in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, and had been a member of the Order during the Clone Wars. While their position in the Order and their rank or degree of involvement in the war is lost to history, it is known that they escaped the horrors of Contingency Order 66, a treacherous order issued by the twisted Sith Lord Darth Sidious' alter ego, Supreme Chancellor of the Republic Palpatine to wipe out the Jedi. With the Order disbanded and the Republic in ashes as the anti-Jedi Galactic Empire emerged, this individual fled to the planet Talasea and took up residence on a sparsely settle continent. Living in a small farming village, this individual's presence was discovered by Sith Lord Darth Vader.
