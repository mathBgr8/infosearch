Star map
9641

A star map,Tarkin also called a star chart,Nexus of Power was a map used to show the locations of celestial objects within a galaxy. The maps could be represented in a two-dimensional form or as a three-dimensional hologram and were used in astrogation, naval operations, and governmental logistics. The Jedi Order maintained many star-charts which showed the location of legendary Force vergences, though by the time of the Galactic Empire, many of the known vergence star-charts were clever fakes. Authentic star-charts that were recovered during the era were thousands of years old, rendering them nearly impossible to interpret.
