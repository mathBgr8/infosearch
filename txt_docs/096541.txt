thumb|right|150px|[[Tholme/Legends|Tholme arrives at the Magma Caverns.]] The Magma Caverns were a labyrinth of tunnels, created by the lava rivers from the several volcanoes of Saleucami. It was placed under the capital city. During the Clone Wars, the Dark Jedi Sora Bulq installed a Cloning Complex in order to create an army of Morgukai clones.
