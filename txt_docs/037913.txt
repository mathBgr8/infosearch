The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 14
2404

The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 14 is the fourteenth issue of The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine, later rebranded as the Star Wars Insider. Released in Summer 1991, issue 14 represented a major departure from the magazine's regular quarterly publication schedule. It was the only Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine issue published that year, between the Fall 1990 release of issue 13 and the Winter 1992 release of issue 15. *"The 'Force' Strikes Back In New Nintendo Form!" by Pamela Roller *"Billy Dee Williams: The Life and Times of Lando Calrissian" by Dan Madsen *"Heir to the Empire — The Making of a Novel" by John S.
