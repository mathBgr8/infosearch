Firethorn tree
1459

The firethorn tree was a species of tree found on Abbaji. The trees grew only in a single, small grove of the Irugian Rain Forest, and could reach over a hundred meters in height. Tiny versions were owned by the rich across the galaxy as heirlooms. These were worth a decamillion credits. Xizor had a 600-year-old specimen given to him by a former rival to make amends. It was only trimmed once a year, with one small cut per trim. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Trees
