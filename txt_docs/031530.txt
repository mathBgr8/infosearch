Aarwynn was a notable mythological humbaba. Covered in dense fur, they were natural burrowers, making the soft swamp lands of their homeworlds an ideal habitat. With four known sub-species the humbaba of Kashoon were short-legged, long-trunked creatures while the humbaba of Corellia and Talus were tall and similar in appearance to the falumpaset.
