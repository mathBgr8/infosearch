Duros Cultural Center

The Duros Cultural Center (DCC) was the primary gathering place for those Duros who were living on Coruscant during the decades leading up to the Clone Wars. The DCC was located in the Boribos Prefecture at the time. Category:Coruscant places of learning Category:Duros culture
