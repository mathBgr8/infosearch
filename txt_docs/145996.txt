In 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:5:9, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. during a conflict between the Republic and a militant secessionist faction,The New Essential Chronology the Oligarchy's loyalty lay with the former. In the wake of a pro-Separatist uprising in Cadinth's capital city of Saskapeg that ended with the death of the planet's Jedi Watchman, Bodis-Ker Vitan, the Oligarchy retracted its previous support of the Republic. Fopow later withdrew all statements saying instead that the planet was "firmly undecided" between the two groups. The Cadinth Oligarchy was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #55, a May 9, 2002 issue of the HoloNet News website. The article that references the government, entitled "Cadinth Loyalty Uncertain After Jedi Death," was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
