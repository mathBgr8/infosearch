Dagary Minor
3902

Dagary Minor was a celestial body located in the Dagary Minor system, a part of the Northern Dependencies portion of the Inner Rim. By 3976 BBY, it was situated within the area of the galaxy that had been widely explored by the Galactic Republic and was known as the "civilized galaxy." A battle of the Mandalorian Wars was fought on Dagary Minor in 3963 BBY between the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders and the military forces of the Republic, the latter of which included the soldier Xaart as well as the Jedi Knight eventually known as the Jedi Exile. Years later, when the Jedi Exile encountered Xaart on the planet Onderon, the latter reminded herThe Old Republic: Revan of the battle they had participated in on Dagary Minor. Between 12 ABY and 13 ABY, Dagary Minor fell within the borders of the New Republic. Dagary Minor was introduced in the 2004 Obsidian Entertainment video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords. In the game, the celestial body can be mentioned in an optional conversation the player character, the Jedi Exile, can have with the character Xaart. This article assumes one hundred percent game completion and that the conversation took place as described.
