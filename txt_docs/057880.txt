Jesa Corporation
779

The Jesa Corporation was a mining operation which was established and operated by individuals of high moral character. They helped turn the planet Pergitor into a lucrative and popular world, though despite their good intentions, Jesa's continued mining eventually led to an increase in volcanic activity, causing the world to become uninhabitable. *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters, Second Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mining companies
