Kobola Spice Mine

|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation=Kobola Miners }} }} The Kobola Spice Mine, also known as the Kobola Bunker, was a cave located on Naboo's moon of Rori. During the Galactic Civil War, the cave belonged to the Kobola Miners and was illegally mined for spice. The Kobola Miners were a criminal organization that abducted travelers in the Naboo system and enslaved them yo do the mining work. That spice is then sold to the Hutts. The Kobola Spice Mine was also used as a bunker by the Kobola Miners and was carefully guarded.
