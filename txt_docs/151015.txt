Jar'Kai dueling saber
1739

thumb|right|250px|A pair of sabers owned by Ace Azzameen The Jar'Kai dueling sabers were a set of weapons developed by the Yovshin Swordsmen on Atrisia. Named after a city on Atrisia, the weapons were designed to be dual-wielded, leading to the creation of the lightsaber combat style Jar'Kai. Antan Azzameen possessed a set of Jar'Kai dueling sabers, which was rumored to have been the result of a duel that he won against a prince. He later gave his nephew, Ace Azzameen, his set as thanks for rescuing him from Viraxo Industries.
