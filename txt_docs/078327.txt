Sith wyrm

The Sith wyrm was a massive creature that lived on the fourth moon of Yavin in large subterranean lairs. thumb|left|200px|A Sith wyrm The Sith wyrm was originally an infant exogorth that attached itself to Naga Sadow's battleshipThe Dark Side Sourcebook in 5000 BBY.The New Essential Chronology Failing to tame the creature and seeing no other use for it, Sadow subjected it to Sith alchemy so that it would serve as a guardian for the Temple of Fire. The Massassi worshiped the beast as a god until Exar Kun killed it a millennium later when the Massassi shaman Zythmnr tried to feed Kun to it. Unbeknown to Zythmnr, the spirit of Freedon Nadd engineered this conflict to manipulate Kun; Kun could only defeat it by using the power of a Sith amulet and committing himself more fully to the dark side of the Force.
