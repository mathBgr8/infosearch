Micro Instrument division
746

During the Galactic Civil War, Nordoxicon's Micro Instrument division was responsible for the creation of the bomb sights that were fitted aboard Sienar Fleet Systems' TIE/sa bomber. The sight was reportedly so accurate that bomber pilots were able to place a free-falling thermal detonator through the top hatch of a laser turret from a distance of ten kilometers while flying through a hurricane. *The Star Wars Sourcebook Category:Businesses
