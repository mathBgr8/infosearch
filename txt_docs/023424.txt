Bimmisaari tiga loreng
1916

The tiga loreng was a non-sentient beaked bird native to the planet Bimmisaari from the Mid Rim. The native inhabitants of Bimmisaari banned all weapons of violence and hunting on their homeworld, ensuring that tiga loreng thrived in the wild.
