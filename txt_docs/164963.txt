Load-lifter (model)
1132

The Drendan Load-lifter was a bulky droid designed to move heavy equipment around. Slow and cumbersome, the Load-lifter had little utility. Because of their size, they effectively blocked shipboard corridors, and were usually confined to maintenance or storage bays.
