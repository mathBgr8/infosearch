New Republic Honor Guard
2152

The New Republic Honor Guard was a military force appointed by the Combined Defense Forces of the New Republic to perform ceremonial duties to high ranking New Republic officials and dignitaries. Units were stationed aboard diplomatic ships and locations, including the Imperial Palace and locations visited by the Chief of State. Members included Ssyrmik, Shreel, and Marcopius, and often attended the New Republic Fleet Academy.
