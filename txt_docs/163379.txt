Theatre Danske
759

Located in Montrol City on Gandle Ott, the Theatre Danske was a huge, multi-balconied building with wooden railings and red drapes. During the visit of the crew of the FarStar to Gandle Ott, the theatre staged a performance of Madra Teene. During a performance, an assassin droid attempted to eliminate senior members of the crew of the FarStar who were attending the show at the time. The assassin droid was destroyed in the fighting. *The DarkStryder Campaign Category:Buildings Category:Gandle Ott locations
