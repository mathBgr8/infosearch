thumb|left|150px|Stormtroopers loyal to Walang Grazz. Walang Grazz was an Imperial Moff who became a warlord following the Battle of Endor. After the fall of the Empire, Stormtroopers loyal to the former Moff, denoted by circular insignias on their helmets defended one of the self-styled warlord's bunker complexes. Grazz fashioned a small empire and became one of the many warlords who pestered the New Republic.
