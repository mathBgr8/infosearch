Galactic Alliance Committee on Corporate Oversight
1025

The Galactic Alliance Committee on Corporate Oversight (GCoCO) was a sub-division of the Senate of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances, in operation around the time of the Dark Nest Crisis. The Committee on Corporate Oversight was tasked with keeping checks on the commercial and corporate activities of the Alliance's member species, to ensure compliance with the law, attempting to prevent individuals and companies from benefitting from the upheavals caused by the war. In particular, the committee kept a close eye on the activities of the Koorivar, who had been a powerful presence in the Corporate Alliance prior to and during the Clone Wars. *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance Senate organizations
