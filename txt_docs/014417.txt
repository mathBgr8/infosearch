Among the Humans who called Telfrey home were Tay Vanis and Yom Argo, who later left home to serve the Rebel Alliance as spies. Vanis and Argo later returned to Telfrey to investigate reports of increased Imperial activity in the region, and eventually Telfrey erupted in open rebellion. The Imperial response was swift and brutal, and the ensuing bombardment rendered the planet completely uninhabitable.
