Angry Rodian
1521

}} This angry individual was an aggressive male Rodian who resided in tower 214 on Byblos. The Rodian, who disliked droids, may unwillingly have been part of U-THR's scheme to liberate Crimson from Imperial Customs. It is possible that U-THR provoked the Rodian to chase him into the Imperial Custom's office to create a diversion, providing Crimson with the chance to escape. The reader has a choice of using the angry Rodian to create a diversion as one of the possibilities of the solitaire story Crimson Jailbreak.
