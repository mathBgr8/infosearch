The Bosphon Forever was covered with glyphs of ownership on the exterior and coated with glyphs rendering stories and philosophies associated with Bosph mythology and tradition. The ship functioned almost as a temple to the lost lore of the Bosph and their destroyed homeworld, and Bora Boru rarely allowed anyone to enter the ship unguarded.
