thumb|right|250px|The Jedi library on Coruscant A Jedi library was a database containing information relating to the Jedi Order and its history. Jedi libraries were administered by Jedi librarians (such as Atris, and Jocasta Nu) and accessible only to members of the Jedi Order. Some were even restricted to members of having certain ranks, such as Dorak's library in the Jedi Enclave on Dantooine, which was reserved for Jedi Masters only. Perhaps the greatest Jedi libraries in the history of the Order was Odan-Urr's Great Jedi Library on Ossus, which was founded around 5000 BBY and destroyed around 4000 BBY, the one on Exis Station and the Jedi Archives in the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, administered by Jocasta Nu around the time of the Clone Wars. thumb|120px|A [[Jedi/Legends|Jedi Holocron.]] *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Darth Bane: Rule of Two *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith video game *Coruscant Nights III: Patterns of Force *Galaxy of Fear: Ghost of the Jedi *The Lost City of the Jedi *Zorba the Hutt's Revenge *Mission from Mount Yoda *Queen of the Empire *Prophets of the Dark Side *Junior Jedi Knights: Kenobi's Blade *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Library Category:Libraries
