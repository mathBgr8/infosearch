Galactic Justice Center
3480

The Galactic Justice Center was a massive government building on Coruscant. Located in Quadrant A-89 of the Senate District, the main building of the complex was shaped like a giant cylinder plated with reflective transparisteel. It contained several courthouses and other judicial and law enforcement facilities. The Justice Center was constructed sometime before the Cold War that started in 3653 BBY. It was destroyed during the Battle of Coruscant and rebuilt under the Galactic Alliance after the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War. During the Second Galactic Civil War, the Justice Center housed the headquarters of the Galactic Alliance Guard. This incarnation of the Galactic Justice Center was destroyed in 44 ABY, during the Liberation of Coruscant. The dark side entity Abeloth, who had been masquerading as Chief of State Rokari Kem and was leading the Lost Tribe of Sith forces in the Jedi Temple, caused widespread seismic and volcanic activity across the planet to distract the Jedi, enabling her to escape with Ben Skywalker and Vestara Khai.
