Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 9
2466

Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 9 is the ninth issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in October 1996. This issue's theme focused on the Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire multimedia series. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 9, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with two Topps promotional trading cards, one for the 1996 Topps Star Wars 3Di Widevision set and another for the 1996 Topps Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire set.
