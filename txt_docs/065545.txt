Isorpian Raiders
491

Isorpian Raiders were a group of beings who attacked a small village in both the Jedi Men From Baronis and Two Jedi Men From Baronis Jediboh Opera plays. They were driven back by two bantha salesmen who were mistakenly identified as Jedi Knights. Category:Fictional organizations
