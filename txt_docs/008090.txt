Terter
1389

Terter was a podracer in the Boonta Eve Classic. During the second lap, they closed in on Sebulba. Sebulba broke a small piece of his podracer and it flew into the engine of Terter's engine. Terter swung towards Anakin Skywalker's podracer, damaging a strap connecting the pod to the engines.
