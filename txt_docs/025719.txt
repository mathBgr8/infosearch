Heorem Skytunnel
559

The Heorem Skytunnel was a kilometer-long tunnel that led through the Heorem Complex in the Senate District on Coruscant. It provided transit for both mag-levs and sky traffic, leading from the Senate District to Sah'c Canyon in the Sah'c District. *Labyrinth of Evil *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant infrastructure
