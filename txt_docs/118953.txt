General Crix Madine Military Reserve
1175

The General Crix Madine Military Reserve, informally called Crix Base, was a military reserve comprising a number of orbital hangars over Coruscant. It was constructed during the first wave of Galactic Alliance Navy restructuring following the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War. It was dedicated to its namesake, Crix Madine. It was home to the Third, Eighth, and the mysterious Ninth Fleets. It also housed two elite units, Gamma Corps and the Space Rangers.
