Mount Meru (Drogheda)
1385

Mount Meru was a mountain on the planet Drogheda. It was the location of the Mount Meru Mine and was the temporary headquarters of a revolutionary group that waged war with the reigning monarchy in 4 ABY. Working on behalf of the reigning Queen Sarna, Rebel Alliance hero Lando Calrissian flushed the revolutionaries out of the mine by destroying several water tanks within. *Star Wars (1977) 83 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Drogheda locations Meru
