SBP-200 Blaster Pistol
1998

The small Elmainin Armaments group attempted to undercut BlasTech and Merr-Sonn with their SBP-200 Blaster Pistol, which turned out to be an ineffective imitation of the more popular models. The SBP-200 was notoriously fragile, and used low grade focusing crystals, meaning the gun packed little punch.
