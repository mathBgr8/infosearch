Bal'ta'ran crystal
860

Bal'ta'ran crystals were mineral formations on Guiteica.Alliance Intelligence Reports Planet Guiteica held extensive underground reserves of this mineral. Slave-operated mines on Upper Bal'ta'ran Cluster obtained these crystals. Many different pro-Imperial mining companies had tried to convince native Bitthævrians to perform mining activity on the planet. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Crystals
