Stealth field enhancer
1542

thumb|right|200px|[[Bao-Dur wearing a stealth field enhancer]] A stealth field enhancer was a type of visor that was capable of increasing the wearer's perception while utilizing a stealth field generator, enhancing the power of such units by allowing for increased monitoring of the status of the field in relation to the wearer's surroundings. Such items were rare and expensive. The stealth field enhancer was intended to appear in the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic but it was removed from the final version of the game.
