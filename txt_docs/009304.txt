Griann
1103

Griann was an agricultural town on the planet of Teyr, located in the Greenbelt. It was laid out in a perfect checkerboard grid pattern and catered to the working-class. In 17 ABY Jedi Master Luke Skywalker and his companion Akanah traveled to the quiet town of Griann via Flyway 120 seeking traces of the Fallanassi. *Shield of Lies *Tyrant's Test *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *The Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities on Teyr
