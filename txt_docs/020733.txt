Barenth was a terrestrial world located in the Barenth system. It was a part of the Expansion Region's Drannik sector, being located both in the Outer Expansion Zone sub-region and the Northern Dependencies portion of that region. During the Waymancy Storm of 7811 BBY, the Galactic Republic mounted a defense of Barenth against its enemy, the Signatories of Waymancy. During the engagement, almost the entire civilian population of the world was evacuated, although the effort cost the Republic an entire battle group. At a speech delivered on the 25th anniversary of Victory in the Waymancy Storm in 7786 BBY, the Republic's Supreme Chancellor Nagratha noted the defense of Barenth as one of the bloodiest events of the Waymancy Storm. thumb|right|200px|The Ho'Din Baji resolved to take up permanent residence on Barenth. Barenth fell within the borders of the New Republic between 12 ABY and 13 ABY. By the time of the reign of that government, an ancient laboratory consisting of multiple buildings and containing unique specimens of plants and seeds was located on Barenth. During that time, the Ho'Din healer Baji visited the laboratory with the intent of researching healing remedies. Baji's visit to the Barenth facility coincided with an ecological disaster on the world, with a groundquake damaging the laboratory. During the quake, Baji quickly obtained the plants and seeds necessary for his research and left the facility. Upon his return to investigate the laboratory, the Ho'Din discovered that the facility grounds and plants located there had been tainted by a radiant contamination. Due to his sympathy for the damaged plants&mdash;and the species-wide veneration the Ho'Din exhibited for plants&mdash;Baji resolved to inhabit the Barenth laboratory for the rest of his life in order to attempt to heal the plants therein. Baji's visit to Barenth was later noted in the book The Essential Guide to Alien Species, which was based on the notes of Senior Anthropologist Mammon Hoole. Barenth was home to a civilian population aligned with the Galactic Republic. During the Waymancy Storm, the Republic largely evacuated the civilians from the world. When the Ho'Din Baji visited Barenth by the time of the reign of the New Republic, he resolved to take up permanent residence there. Barenth was introduced in the 2001 reference book The Essential Guide to Alien Species by Ann Margaret Lewis.
