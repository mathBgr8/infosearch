Mrp-Mrp Poo
1991

Mrp-Mrp Poo was considered the Lothario Smerg of the Quenk jazz scene. She hosted annual Vector Day parties at the Boulderdash terrarium. Several hundred of Poo's close friends and investors attended the event, including Dyslogia Twang, Rzsco Snowwt, Tireis Blackandwhite, Eban, Gall Supreme, Bucrkam Inney, Massie Herhis, and Quocrag Mogra. Mrp-Mrp Poo was mentioned in Bob Carrau's Monsters and Aliens from George Lucas, a book published in 1993.
