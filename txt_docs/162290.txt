Energy harvester
517

Energy harvesters were used on Genarius. They collected the planet's atmosphere to extract the energy stored within. A number worked around the abandoned city of Nub Saar. "Energy harvester" seemed to be a generic term to refer to the ships used to mine Genarius, such as the AR-25 Bubbleship transport. Category:Mining technology
