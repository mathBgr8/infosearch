VCD987
1948

The VCD987 was an Acclamator-class assault ship serving in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, it was ambushed while bringing Republic reinforcements to the Battle of Drongar, leading to the temporary capture of Jedi Kai Justiss, Tsui Choi, and Sian Jeisel by Count Dooku, although they were quickly released by the Count.
