Eliall-class Cargo Barge
1355

The Eliall-class Cargo Barge was a cargo hauler used by many shipping companies in the Outer Rim. The freighter measured 100 meters in length and required one pilot and four other crew. It could carry up to 300 metric tons of cargo as well as five passengers. Category:Freighter classes
