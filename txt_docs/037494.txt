Regg Kuuga
1282

Regg Kuuga was a male Draag who was wanted by the Coruscant Police for stealing medical frigates, scrapping parts, and transforming them into tourist-attacking melee ships. In 32 BBY, his bounty was claimed by Jango Fett.
