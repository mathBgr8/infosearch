To its south ran the hyperlane known as the Authority Guardian Corridor, while the Shaltin Tunnels hyperspace route passed through the Thandon Cluster's western section. To the north of the area, a third hyperlane passed through its fringes, terminating at the Thandon Cluster's Dostra system. The Dostra system contained the planet Brosi, which was the only inhabited world in the Thandon Cluster, being home to a population of near-Humans known as the Brosins. The Thandon Cluster eventually came under the control of the Corporate Sector Authority, which struggled with developing the remote area. By 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Corporate Sector Authority had established mining operations on Brosi, with the planet's population by that time also including Humans and numbering nineteen million. The Thandon Cluster was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. In the book, the Thandon Cluster planet Brosi was illustrated by Mike Vilardi.
