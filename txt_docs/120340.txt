Visage
2019

The Visage was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer in service with the Imperial Navy. In 4 ABY, the Visage was present at the Battle of Endor, but was usually outfitted to explore and chart unknown expanses of the Outer Rim Territories. Staff aboard the ship included a galactic cartography contingent; most lower-ranked Imperial officers considered being assigned to the Visage a joke duty post. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Category:Imperial II-class Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
