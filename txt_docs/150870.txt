Soknar
631

thumb|Cene wielding her Soknar The Soknar was Cene Gilvent's customized weapon, built by her partner, master smith Mlatar Thon Gra. It combined a Somreth tri-blade of the Drevun Six, the Barnax "Twenchok" foil and the Coynite sat'skar. The name soknar referred to the Taloron word for "several". The weapon, being heterodox, was customized to adapt to Gilvent's own heterodox fighting skill. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Edged weaponry
