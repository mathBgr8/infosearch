Unidentified Tatooine moisture farmer
1965

While in a local bar, a moisture farmer who lived on the planet Tatooine in 14 ABYAccording to Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy, the events in the game take place ten years after the Battle of Endor, which according to the The Essential Reader's Companion is placed in 4 ABY. overheard a conversation about the Disciples of Ragnos, a cult of Dark Jedi who were attempting to return the dead Sith Lord Marka Ragnos to life. The moisture farmer told R5-D2, their R5-series astromech droid, to record the conversation. However, the farmer became fearful of reprisals from the cult and sold the droid to a clan of Jawas before moving away.
