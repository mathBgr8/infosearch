Espaa Valorum
2957

His Eminence Prince Espaa Valorum was the Master of the Sith, a Bogan Master. His second in command was Darth Vader. The name Valorum first appeared in the rough draft as a Knight of Sith. After being demoted to stormtrooper after failing to capture the Rebels aboard a Baltarian freighter and witnessing the torture of Annikin Starkiller aboard the Space Fortress, Valorum became disgusted with the Empire, and joins the heroes in escaping the space station as it is destroyed. In the first draft he was renamed Dodana, but this name was reused in the next version of the script for a Rebel General, a character we know as Jan Dodonna. Valorum, now given a first name, is not present in any scenes, and fills a position analogous to the Emperor in A New Hope. He isn't mentioned in later drafts. Espaa Valorum shares a surname with Finis Valorum, penultimate Supreme Chancellor of the Galactic Republic. The two share few similarities, however. The notion of a Sith rejecting the dark side could have informed Darth Vader's redemption in Return of the Jedi. Espaa's first name may be the origin of Mos Espa.
