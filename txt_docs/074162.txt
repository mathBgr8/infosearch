Stun pistol
2660

A stun pistol was a type of stun blaster.Young Jedi Knights: Shadow Academy Designed exclusively for nonlethal crowd control, stun-pistols only fired stun bolts, and emitted a glowing blue arc that could immobilize an organic target, but caused no damage to inanimate objects. Since they were designed for stunning only, they had a longer range than most blaster's stun settings, with a typical stun pistol getting fifty shots out of a single power pack.The Unknown Regions Additionally, the bolts were heavy enough that they could pierce medium armors like stormtrooper armor.Young Jedi Knights: Shadow Academy *The Bounty Hunter *Young Jedi Knights: Shadow Academy *The Star Wars Sourcebook *Star Wars Campaign Pack *Star Wars Gamemaster Kit *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Star Wars Sourcebook, Second Edition *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Star Wars Gamemaster Screen, Revised *Heroes & Rogues *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy *Lords of the Expanse *The Far Orbit Project *The Unknown Regions *Strongholds of Resistance *Keeping the Peace Category:Energy pistols Category:Stun weaponry
