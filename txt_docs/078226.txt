Fusion generator supply tank
2039

thumb|right|250px|A pair of fusion generator supply tanks at the Lars homestead A fusion generator supply tank was a device for power generation and storage. Using standard fusion technology, it generally provided starships with energy for hyperspace travel. It was often used on harsh worlds like Tatooine. The Lars family had a number of these at their homestead. The Empire also had various generators installed at many of their facilities to provide energy for the Imperial Starfleet to allow their vessels the ability to do sub-light and hyperspace jumps. *Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope junior novelization |cards= }} *Secrets of the Sisar Run *Star Wars: Behind the Magic *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Reactors and power generators
