Borna Lys
3656

Borna Lys was a Twi'lek female swoop racer who visited the planet Nar Shaddaa. A Twi'lek female who was the child of a father, Borna Lys was a swoop racer who hung out on the planet Nar Shaddaa following the end of the Jedi Civil War in 3956 BBY.The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, which includes the end of the Jedi Civil War at the Battle of Rakata Prime, to 3956 BBY. Lys had red, wrinkled skin and blue eyes. She grew frustrated when C9-T9-GE3, who was nearly unbeatable, began appearing at Lupo Shar's swoop-racing track. Lys wore a silver headdress, tan-colored robes, a brown undershirt, a brown belt, tan pants and brown boots. Borna Lys first appeared in the 2004 Obsidian Entertainment video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords.
