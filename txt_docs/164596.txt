Spider (corvette group)
827

Spider was the designation of a pair of CR90 corvettes belonging to the Galactic Empire. They responded to a Rebel attack at a cargo transfer area where new surveillance probes were to be delivered by the BFF-1 bulk freighter Libom. Both corvettes were disabled and destroyed by Keyan Farlander in a B-wing starfighter. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Galactic Empire starship groups
