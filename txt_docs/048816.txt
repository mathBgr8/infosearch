|model=E-11b |type=Blaster rifle |culture= |creators= |created= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners= |locations= |cost= |value= |shape= |size= |material= |length=0.438 Meters (with stock folded) |width= |diam= |height= |weight=2.6 kilograms |protection= |crew= |capacity=500 shots |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Alliance to Restore the Republic }} The BlasTech E-11b blaster rifle was an expertly and expensively modified version of the standard issue E-11 blaster rifle used by the Imperial stormtroopers during the Galactic Civil War, which were modeled after the DC-15 series used in the Clone Wars. These weapons were often found in the hands of Rebel troops, namely those in the Battle of Endor. The original BlasTech E-11 was powerful and light, and nigh innumerable due to its use by the Imperial forces. However, its inaccuracy and overheating were listed among the item's drawbacks. Modified by skilled BlasTech and SoroSuub armorers who defected to the Rebel Alliance, the BlasTech E-11b's main changes were the addition of expensive cooling units, making these blaster rifles less likely to overheat at higher fire rates. The basic E-11 and modified E-11b were 438mm long with the folding stock folded, though the E-11b was usually seen extended. Unmodified, the E-11 weighed 2.6 kilograms. The power cell housing opened on the left side of the action housing, above the trigger, giving it a low profile.
