The Deposition of General Solo
3067

"The Deposition of General Solo," originally titled "Deposition 9483749/12982 {Verbal}," is a short story in dialogue form, featured in The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook. It was written by Kathy Tyers and Eric S. Trautmann. The story was later made available for reading on Star Wars Hyperspace under the revised title.
