Genna Delpin
2101

Genna Delpin was a female Human who served as an admiral in the Corellian Defense Force, where she was considered a rising star in the Corellian military. She led the assault fleet at the Battle of Hapes. She took the post of Supreme Commander of the Corellian military when Dur Gejjen forcibly removed Wedge Antilles from the post. She was later in charge of firing Centerpoint Station when it destroyed the Alliance Second Fleet and would later form an alliance with Denjax Teppler following the death of Sadras Koyan.
