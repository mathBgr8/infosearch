Solar Gardens
761

The Solar Gardens was a beautiful park located within the Nubian Palace resort. It was a popular tourist destination with locations for picnics or meeting places. A large transparisteel wall reflected sunlight into the Gardens and illuminated the entire park. This effect also provided a beautiful landscape for two restaurants that bordered the Solar Gardens.
