Verinia Paddox
959

Verinia Paddox was the sister of Theus Paddox, High Lord of House Pelagia. Verinia (aged 10) and her brother Trad Paddox (aged 8) disappeared during the House Mecetti purge of their House. They were discovered 20 year later, frozen in stasis, onboard the Regal Destiny by the crew of the Bright Seeker. The children's governess was Mistress Winslo.
