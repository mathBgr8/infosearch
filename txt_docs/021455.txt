|other date=January 4, 2012 |media type=Trade paperback |pages=122 |isbn=1569714436 |isbn2= |timeline=32 BBY |series= |issues= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Episode I Adventures is a trade paperback collection of the five Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace one-shot comic stories published by Dark Horse Comics. It was released in March 2000. The trade paperback was republished on January 4, 2012, in digest size and with a new cover to coincide with the theatrical 3-D re-release of The Phantom Menace. Showcasing key characters from Star Wars: Episode I - The Phantom Menace, this exciting collection expands the story from the international film blockbuster, exposing key events from the epic tale not shown on screen. Join Queen Amidala, Qui-Gon Jinn, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Anakin Skywalker, along with a top-flight creative cast in this essential piece of the Episode I mythos.
