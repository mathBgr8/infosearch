Sniquux
1460

Sniquux was a Rodian in the employ of Jabba the Hutt who was tasked with the mission of killing all the t'landa Til priests that resided in Colony Nine of Ylesia. He accomplished this by stringing monofilament wire around the courtyard where several priests and their mates were taking a stroll, then triggering an ancient recording of a thota, a predatory creature from the t'landa Til's homeworld of Varl. The priests panicked and stampeded right into the trap where they were dismembered by the almost invisible monofilament wires. Among the priests killed was the head Sacredot Tarrz.
