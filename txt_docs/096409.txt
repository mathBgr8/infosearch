Here's How
1041

Here's How is an unproduced Star Wars-themed educational television show and comic strip for children. Developed by Lucasfilm Special Project Manager Jok Church in the mid-1980s, Here's How would have featured C-3PO teaching a foreign language and R2-D2 explaining the physical science of real-world objects. Despite initial interest from Lucasfilm, the project was ultimately abandoned. Church went on to create You Can With Beakman and Jax, a weekly syndicated newspaper strip (later adapted for television) that, similar to the premise for Here's How, taught children about science in an entertaining fashion.
