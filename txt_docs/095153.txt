Freda (species)
780

Freda were a species from Freda. They were locked in an arms race against the nearby Jante until the Freda attacked Jante industrial plants on Rett II. The Galactic Empire, led by Moff Haveland ordered a ceasefire, which the Freda eventually withdrew from. Category:Mid Rim sentient species
