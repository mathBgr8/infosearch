Filordian
807

Filordian was the native language of the Filordi species from Filordis. Filordian lacked some of the consonant sounds in Basic, so when Filordi spoke Basic, they usually ignored these sounds. They also had a gravely voice when speaking Basic. *Living Force Campaign Guide *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
