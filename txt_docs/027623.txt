She protected Lo's interests on the planet Coruscant and kept watch over his consort, Ayy Vida. A native of the planetUltimate Alien Anthology Sriluur, the Weequay female Tas Kee was one of multiple protectors for Hat Lo, a crime lord based on the planetStar Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary Coruscant. Hired in the years before the Clone Wars in 22 BBYThe New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, she protected the crime lord's interests in Coruscant's gambling and extortion rackets. Kee was under instruction to keep an eye on Ayy Vida, Lo's Twi'lek consort. The Weequay was also a frequent patron of Dex's Diner in Coruscant's CoCo Town district. The ruthlessStar Wars: Attack of the Clones Scrapbook Tas Kee was the only thing that kept Ayy Vida from fleeing from Hat Lo, and the Twi'lek lived in fear of the enforcer. Kee was an acquaintance of Lowle Ch'red, a Coruscanti woman who frequented Dex's Diner as she did. The Weequay had frizzy, braided brown hair, green eyes, and leathery brown skin. Kee was an adept at hiding weapons on her body, making sure Lo was protected by avoiding the few scanners and sensors in the Coruscant undercity. The enforcer wore discarded dark clothing and a maroon hat. She was always armed, with the several weapons on her person crafted to evade Coruscant's undercity sensors and scanners, and designed to evade standard sensors. Even though they were made in this way, Kee still arranged them in a way to trick security scanners. The Weequay also carried concealed knockout drops near her hips.
