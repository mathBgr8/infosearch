|death=July 13, 2016Gulf Breeze, Florida |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Voice artist Star Wars radio dramas |companies= |website= }} Ken Hiller (October 18, 1938 &mdash; July 13, 2016) was a voice artist for radio and television. He narrated the opening crawl, outro, and end credits for all episodes of the Star Wars radio dramas: Star Wars, The Empire Strikes Back, and Return of the Jedi - 29 episodes in all. He also was the narrator of the audio dramas Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire, Dark Forces: Rebel Agent and Dark Forces: Jedi Knight.Dark Forces audio dramas Hiller first entered broadcasting in Hawaii but spent most of his career in St Paul, Minnesota, an important center for public radio in the United States.
