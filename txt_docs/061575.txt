Nor Fik
1667

Nor Fik was the lead negotiator for the Colicoids who sought total control of the spice-processing operations on Nar Shaddaa prior the Clone Wars. Fik journeyed to Rorak 5 to meet with the slave crime-lord Krayn, who ran the spice factories, in order to complete final negotiations. The Colicoids brought with them the slave trader and advisor "Bakleeda" (Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi in disguise), who would inspect Krayn's slave-operated factories.
