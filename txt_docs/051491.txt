Bank of Aargau Security, Ltd.
1394

The Bank of Aargau Security, Ltd. was a subsidiary of the Bank of Aargau that managed every aspect of planetary defense on the planet Aargau. By the height of the New Order, the Bank of Aargau Security had one of the largest private armies in the galaxy. The foremost mission of the company was to uphold the Three Statutes of Aargau, and while many Aargauuns believed the private army helped secure their neutrality during the Galactic Civil War, the private army was a necessity influenced by Imperial dignitaries, who maintained many bank accounts with the Bank of Aargau, and did not want the planet to be fully subjugated.
