Cladus Glynn
1558

Cladus Glynn was an officer in the Imperial Security Bureau, and was stationed on Kothlis during the height of the Galactic Civil War. His role was to locate any Rebel Alliance personnel moving through the Kothlis system, specifically at the Kothlis shadowport. To help him gather information, Glynn assumed the guise of Gart, the drunken spacer who claimed to know the location of a ganjuko graveyard. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Security Bureau officers Category:Males
