Amel Bakli
2808

|masters= |apprentices= }} Amel Bakli was an Imperial governor who ruled over the Core World of Caprioril, a normally tranquil planet. The governor was targeted by an assassin droid, who reasoned that the best way to ensure its target's death was to kill all 20,000 spectators at a swoop race Bakli was attending.
