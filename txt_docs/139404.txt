New Republic Reference Service
619

The New Republic Reference Service was an information bureau present within the New Republic where data was stored on a wide variety of subjects. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Luke Skywalker performed a search with the reference service on the terms "Fallanassi" and "White Current" and turned up no results. *Shield of Lies *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Reference Service
