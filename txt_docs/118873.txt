Force-flash
2945

The Force-flash, also spelled Force flash, was a Force power that prevented the user from being detected by others. The user could temporarily "fuzz"&mdash;disable&mdash;a security holocam through the Force, causing a brief period of static, allowing the user to pass while only alerting security to a minor holocam malfunction.Fate of the Jedi: Outcast Another usage of the Force under the same name allowed the Force-user to produce an unnatural flash of bright light, temporary blinding their opponents. Seff Hellin used Force flash on the Armand Isard Correctional Facility's holocams during his raid on it. Abeloth was capable of producing a flash powerful enough to affect the visuals of ships across the entire Exodo system.
