Kilo Squad
973

Kilo Squad was a clone commando squad in the Grand Army of the Republic. The clones of Kilo Squad were among the first to receive commando training prior to the start of the Clone Wars, and were one of the first commando squads decanted on Kamino. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Republic commando squads
