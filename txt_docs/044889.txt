Caamasi (language)
1688

Caamasi was the official language of Caamas and spoken by the native Caamasi in addition to Galactic Basic Standard. After the devastation of the planet, the surviving Caamasi were able to keep the language alive through the continued use and sharing of memnis from generation to generation. *Vision of the Future *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *Star Wars Gamer 9 *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Ultimate Adversaries *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide *Desperate Allies Category:Languages
