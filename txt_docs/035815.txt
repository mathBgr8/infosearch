The husband of Queen Denira, Lorac fathered two sons, the crown prince Denid and his younger brother Anod. In 17 BBY, the Empire hatched a coup against Lorac after finding that the king held rebel sympathies, fomenting a mob to storm the palace and kill the king and his wife. Denid was forced to flee into exile, while Anod remained behind as the new, Imperial-aligned crown prince. Lorac was first mentioned in Star Wars (1977) 49, written by Mike W.
