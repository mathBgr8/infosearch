Tayana
727

Tayana was a meteor crater on Duro. It contained ancient ruins of a former surface city which reached its height in 220 BBY, before the Duros built their space cities. Gateway was built within Tayana. It itself was located in the Tayana district. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *Suns of Fortune Category:Cities on Duro
