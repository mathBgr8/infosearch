Vekker (Quarren)
2474

Vekker was a male Quarren Vigo of Black Sun during Prince Xizor's reign. Known by Xizor as being content with the status quo, he had no ambition to rise any higher within the organization. He was present in the meeting of Vigo's where Guri publicly executed Vigo Green for spying for an outside entity.Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel After Xizor's death in the Battle over Coruscant, the Black Sun was in the verge of an internecine crime war. The Vigos assembled in a council where Vekker was seen arguing loudly with Sprax.Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 3 Savan staged a fake assault against the Vigos and accused Vekker, instigating a feud. Soon after Vekker was hunted and killed in 4 ABY.Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 4 Vekker was named after a predatory species native to Dac. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 3 *Shadows of the Empire: Evolution 4 *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook Category:Males Category:Quarren Category:Vigos
