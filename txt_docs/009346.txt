Orko SkyMine Asteroid Processing Corporation
1180

Orko SkyMine Asteroid Processing Corporation was a fake corporation set up by Durga the Hutt. It was made to hide a huge amount of credits; the company was supposed to be a commercial venture to exploit the riches of the Hoth asteroid field, but in fact the money was being diverted to the top secret Darksaber Project. *Darksaber *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Black Sun companies Category:Front companies Category:Hutt Clan organizations Category:Mining companies
