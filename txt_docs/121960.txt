Kylantha's Whim
1533

Located in the Naboo system, Kylantha's Whim was a mostly deserted quadrant of space, usually safe for travel and hyperspace jumps. The Galactic Empire patrolled the region frequently. It was named after Queen Kylantha, the successor to Queen Apailana. At one point, a young and wealthy suitor, in an attempt to win her heart and hand in marriage, purchased the quadrant for her; however, Kylantha politely refused his offer, saying that she had "married" her people and wished to serve them with all of her time.
