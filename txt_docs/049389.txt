Yount
2627

|masters= |apprentices= }} Yount was an Imperial Governor posted on the planet Wakeelmui, whose duties included being in charge of a stormtrooper garrison on the world. At some point prior to the Battle of Endor in the year 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Endor to 4 ABY. Yount recommended stormtrooper Sergeant Wallen and his unit to Commander Igar, the Imperial officer in charge of Tempest Force, the military legion stationed on the Forest Moon of Endor. Yount was briefly mentioned on Wallen's character card for the 1999 Endor Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game. As the name "Wakeelmui" is an anagram of the United States city of Milwaukee, Yount's surname is shared with that of Milwaukee Brewers Hall of Famer Robin Yount.
