Kosh (tea)
1778

Kosh was a type of tea available in the galaxy by 0 BBY.The Death Star novel, which mentions Kosh, is dated to 0 BBY by The Essential Reader's Companion. Some Kosh was among the kitchen stores aboard the Death Star battle station. On one occasion, librarian droid P-RC3 inquired if Human Chief Librarian Atour Riten wanted some tea, offering him a choice of Kosh, Manellan Jasper, and Bluefruit Kintle. Kosh was mentioned in Death Star, a 2007 novel by Michael Reaves and Steve Perry. *Death Star Category:Teas
