The Star Wars Trilogy Scrapbook: The Rebel Alliance
9265

The Star Wars Trilogy Scrapbook: The Rebel Alliance is a guide to the Rebel Alliance and its battles against the Galactic Empire. It was written by Mark Cotta Vaz as part of Scholastic's Star Wars Trilogy Scrapbook series.
