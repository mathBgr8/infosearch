Mission to Ord Mantell (Disciples of Ragnos crisis)
2488

The Mission to Ord Mantell was carried out by Jedi Knight Jaden Korr for the New Jedi Order. After learning of reports about a large weapons stockpile in the wastes of Ord Mantell, Korr was sent to destroy it, to prevent it from falling into the wrong hands. thumb|left|200px|A map of the weapons depot Korr arrived on Ord Mantell and destroyed one of the weapons caches. However, before he could proceed, he encountered Boba Fett. Obviously intent on taking the weapons for his own use, Fett told the young Jedi Knight to leave. However, Korr refused and evaded the Mandalorian bounty hunter as he went on to destroy the rest of the stockpile. However, Korr was unable to leave, as Fett prevented him from entering his Z-95 Headhunter Far Wanderer. Korr faced off with Fett and, after he proved to be too much of a challenge, the bounty hunter fled. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy Category:Battles of Ord Mantell Category:Battles of the Disciples of Ragnos crisis
