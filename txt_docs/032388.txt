Service Special
890

The Service Special was a small blaster used by Imperial law enforcement agency officers. Developed around 4 BBY, the Service Special was only half the size of most blasters but possessed six times the power, and was a secret even from some elements of the Imperial military. Its power was roughly equal to one of the four cannons in the Millennium Falcon's quad-guns.
