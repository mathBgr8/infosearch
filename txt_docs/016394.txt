UR41-284 was a planet located within the UR41-284 system of the Unknown Regions. It was the homeworld of a sentient species that attempted to resist Grand Admiral Thrawn. Thrawn ordered Captain Ferob of the Victory-class Star Destroyer Iron Fist to destroy everything within 70km of the coordinates of the planet's museum district. *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in the Unknown Regions
