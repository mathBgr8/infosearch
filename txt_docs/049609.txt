Sakiyan (language)
583

Sakiyan language was the native language of the Sakiyan species from Sakiya. Most Sakiyan were also fluent in either Huttese or Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Alien Anthology *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Legacy Era Campaign Guide *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Languages
