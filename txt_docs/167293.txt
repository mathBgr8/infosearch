Mina (handmaiden)
972

Mina was a female Human who served as one of the handmaidens, with Alana, of Princess Leia during the Galactic Civil War. She remained at the Academy of Chatos at Yuell, after the Galactic Empire invaded the Aquilaean system, impersonating her mistress. Mina physically resembled Leia and had long dark hair.
