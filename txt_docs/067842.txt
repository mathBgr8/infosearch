Yoh Podd
657

Yoh Podd was a Jediboh Opera actor, part of Master Thespian Damien Tantrellius' Tantrellius Theatre Groupe, and played the role of Mouchus in Two Jedi Men From Baronis. He also had a thing for Damien's daughter Plum Tantrellius and, when she was kidnapped after a show, he insisted on joining the group of Rebels who were trying to rescue her.
