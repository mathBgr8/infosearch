Supreme Prophet
1948

thumb|254px|[[Kadann, Supreme Prophet of the Galactic Empire.]] Supreme Prophet of the dark side was the title of the highest ranking member of the Dark Force clergy. The founder of the religion, Darth Millennial, bestowed this title upon himself. Under the New Order, an alternate title was Supreme Prophet of the Galactic Empire.
