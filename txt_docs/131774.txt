Corporate Sector System Development Programs
795

The Corporate Sector System Development Programs, also known as D-Progs, was a joint development project coordinated by the Galactic Empire and Corporate Sector Authority. Overseen by the Coalition for Progress, it gave economic aid to frontier worlds that had suffered during the end of the Galactic Republic, and was considered a success. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Corporate Sector Authority projects and operations Category:Galactic Empire projects and operations
