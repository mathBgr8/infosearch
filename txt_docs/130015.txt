Queen of the Valkyries
962

Queen of the Valkyries was a Yevethan Aramadia-class thrustship. As part of his deal to aid Nil Spaar's efforts against the New Republic, Belezaboth Ourn allowed the Paqwe diplomatic vessel Mother's Valkyrie to be damaged by the departing Aramadia. In exchange, Ourn was promised Queen of the Valkyries, likely known by a Yevethan name at the time. When the New Republic Fifth Battle Group entered the Farlax sector, Nil Spaar rescinded his offer and kept Queen in the Koornacht Cluster for defense, with the added benefit of stranding Ourn on Coruscant to act as his spy. *Shield of Lies Category:Aramadia-class thrustships
