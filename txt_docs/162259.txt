Galma (fruit)
594

Galma was a blood-red fruit that Rodians ate as a breakfast food. It smelled faintly like a sweaty bantha, and was not appetizing to most other species. Rodian merchant Teeloo cooked it for breakfast for him and his friend before racing through Eskaron in 33 BBY. *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Onslaught Category:Breakfast foods Category:Fruits and vegetables Category:Rodian food
