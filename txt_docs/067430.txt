Time of Renewal
816

The Time of Renewal was a sacred celebration for the Gupins of the Forest Moon of Endor. During the ceremony, the Gupins unlocked the Juniper Chest, and young Gupins received their wings and the ability to fly. The ceremony also rejuvenated the Gupins' ability to change shapes.
