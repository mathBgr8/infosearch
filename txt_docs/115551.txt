Sith Academy (Iridonia)
1460

The Sith academy of Iridonia was established by the Brotherhood of Darkness near the end of the New Sith Wars. They used it to train Sith Acolytes. Trainees here were apprenticed to Sith Masters. The skills learned here equipped adepts with abilities on par with Jedi Knights.
