It was the homeworld of the Human thief Merc Sunlet. Tirac Munda was first mentioned on a card for Merc Sunlet from the 1996 A New Hope Limited expansion of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Tirac Munda system, and therefore Tirac Munda itself, in grid square P-15.
