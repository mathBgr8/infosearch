Babb
1409

The Babbs were a sentient species that were small in stature. Consequently, they were occasionally thought of in contrast to Wookiees. In 27 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games to 27 BBY. a number of Babbs were present on the planet Euceron, which was hosting the Galactic Games, and they were seen by the Jedi Padawan Anakin Skywalker on a landing pad. The Babbs appeared in Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games, a 2002 young readers novel written by Jude Watson. *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games Category:Sentient species
