Stigrit Krax
1003

Stigrit Krax was a female Troob, the wife of General Krax and the mother of Ket Krax. She tried to sabotage Counselor Harthan's attempts to mediate a truce between the Troobs and Hobors by deliberately misinterpreting what the Hobor delegate said, and then by buying C-3PO from Harthan once the droid was called in to negotiate.
