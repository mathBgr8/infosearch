Zuud was one of the Empire's most effective interrogators. She loved to torture prisoners to get the information that she wanted, and was known for her ability to instantly determine a prisoner's limits. In 0 ABY, Zuud was assigned to the Imperial base on Kalist VI. Charged with the interrogation and torture of Rebel agent Jorin Sol, Lieutenant Zuud used a scan grid to interrogate Sol. Lieutenant Zuud performed the interrogation with such enthusiasm and zeal that would plague the Rebellion far more than what an ordinary breaking would have done.
