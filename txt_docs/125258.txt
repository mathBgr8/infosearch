Ivo Laibach
1424

Ivo Laibach was an Imperial Security Bureau-trained CompForce battalion commander. A few months before the Battle of Endor, Zevulon Veers was assigned to be Laibach's bodyguard while he interrogated Rebels captured in a standard police operation. While interrogating an old librarian by torturing and beating him, the Rebels staged a rescue.
