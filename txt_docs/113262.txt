Mutandan porf
1069

Mutandan porf was a blend of grain, meat, and spices from Mutanda. It was eaten with the hands and generally served with flat bread. One of the ingredients contained a mild stimulant. It was very filling but considered an acquired taste and was popular among the pilots serving onboard the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Independence. *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Edible nuts and seeds Category:Meat
