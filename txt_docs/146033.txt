Nura Tinall
1813

}} Nura Tinall was a Rebel who operated near Moenia, Naboo. Ebenn Q3 Baobab once stated that Tinall was a young female poet who had sent him her original poetry, requesting that he would pass it on to his publishers. Baobab refused to do this, and hired a person to return the poetry to its author. This "poetry" was in fact the plans to the nearby Emperor's Retreat. The plans were delivered to Nura Tinall, who in turn hinted that they would be sent to Leia Organa.
