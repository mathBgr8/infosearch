Prior to the Bounty Hunters' Guild Guildmaster Cradossk's death in 1 ABY,The Mandalorian Armor establishes Cradossk's death in the Battle of Circumtore, dated to four years before 5 ABY in The Essential Guide to Warfare. the guild owned stock in Lerrimore Droids, which sold KPR servant droids at a discount to Guild members. Arack Dostryt, who was the guild's director of recruiting, noted in a chapter of the Bounty Hunters Guild Handbook that Lerrimore Droids was one of the galaxy's cornerstone corporations and that the organization's executive leadership would be eager to respond to Cradossk hypothetically demanding to see them. Bossk, Cradossk's son and himself an eventual bounty hunter, later went on to annotate a Bounty Hunters Guild Handbook copy, asking why his father would want to meet organizations such as droid manufacturers. Lerrimore Droids shared its name with Lerrimore Contracting Company, a household goods manufacturer.
