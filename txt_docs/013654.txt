Kamiel's execution, however, saw a two-month delay as each of the fifty-four star systems in which he had been given the death penalty wished to have him transported to their capital for the carrying out of the sentence. Ultimately, Imperial Moff Gandril ordered the execution to take place on Haldeen itself. Haldeen was created by Paul Sudlow and first mentioned in the "Galaxywide NewsNets" feature of the eighth issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal, which was published in November 1995.
