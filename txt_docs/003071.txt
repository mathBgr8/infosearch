|intl release= |rereleased= |media type=*Paperback *eBook |pages=416 |isbn=9780553564686 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline=4 BBY&ndash;4 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Tales book series |preceded by= |followed by=Tales from Jabba's Palace }} Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina is a Star Wars Legends anthology book featuring sixteen short stories set in and around Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. It was edited by Kevin J. Anderson, and it was first published in July 1995 by Bantam Spectra. The book was followed by Tales from Jabba's Palace and reprinted in the 1997 compilation book Star Wars: Tales omnibus. In a far corner of the universe, on the small desert planet of Tatooine, there is a dark, nic-i-tain&ndash;filled cantina where you can down your favorite intoxicant while listening to the best jizz riffs in the universe. But beware your fellow denizens of this pangalactic watering hole, for they are cutthroats and cutpurses, assassins and troopers, humans and aliens, gangsters and thieves.... {| ! Title !!
