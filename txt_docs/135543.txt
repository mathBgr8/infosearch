Ktlin
901

Ktlin was the name given to a domesticated mosgoth owned by Togorian Mlatar Thon Gra. Mlatar used Ktlin as a flying transport in the cities of Zuliria as an alternative to the more common ruori. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Mosgoths Category:Named creatures
