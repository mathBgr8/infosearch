Kerritamba Village Arena
1297

thumb|right|300px|The entrance to the Village Arena. The Kerritamba Village Arena was an arena just across a bridge from the Kerritamba Village on Kashyyyk. One had to receive permission from Chief Kerritamba to get into the arena. If the person who challenged the Arena Champion were to defeat all the arena challenges, including the champion, that individual would become the new Arena Champion. In the arena, one would face many types of non-sentient species, and if they defeated them all, only then could one move on to face the Arena Champion. The Kerritamba Village Arena appeared in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees Category:Kashyyyk locations
