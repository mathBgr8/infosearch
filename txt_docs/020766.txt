It was the capital of Mecetti Province and throneworld of House Mecetti. It was in perpetual twilight due to its dim red star. Its orbital shipyards were smaller than those of Tallaan but nonetheless successful. Most of the planet was covered in cities, ash-colored rock, and shallow seas.
