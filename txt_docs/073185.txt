Quadrijet 4-Barrel 904E engine
2372

The Bin Gassi Racing Engines Quadrijet 4-Barrel 904E engine was a Podracer engine. With these engines, Boles Roor went on to win the Boonta Eve Classic twice. The engines were visible in Watto's junkyard. The Quadrijet 4-Barrel 904E podracer was known for it's unique design and high top speed, though it was rather average in most other categories and was difficult to control if the pilot wasn't familiar with the craft. It did however boast an exceptional boost package, allowing it to reach very high speeds. The only downside to this was an underwhelming cooling system that made boosting infrequent.
