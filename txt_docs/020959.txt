}} The Professional Greenputt Tour, abbreviated PGT, was one of the many professional greenputt leagues in 22 BBY. is set on the date 13:3:14, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. It was overseen by the Greenputt Governing Council. The Professional Greenputt Tour was first mentioned in the in-universe news story "Yet Another Greenputt League," which was published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, a March 14, 2002 issue of the HoloNet News website.
