Spydr
1347

The spydr was an often domesticated insectoid creature, native to the irradiated forest planet Kubindi. Udin's hive grew them within their holdings. They were found in the Silver Forest of Dreams. The meat of the spydr was thought to be a delicacy in some cultures. The ghost spider was one spydr species.
