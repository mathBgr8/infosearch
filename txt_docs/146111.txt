Ruto
2370

Ruto was an individual who resided on the Outer Rim jungle planet Akiva during the reign of the Galactic Empire.Star Wars Outlaws By 3 ABY, Ruto had been sent a message by a fellow Akivan who had learned that the Empire had established an excavation site in the Great Basin region of Akiva to search for artifacts for cultural importance. In the message, the Akivan expressed their belief that the Empire's operation would result in the theft of Akivan history and went on to share their plan to sabotage the site. They concluded the message by asking Ruto to join them as well as reminding Ruto to delete the message. The communication ultimately end up in possession of the Empire, a copy of it was stored on a datapad at the excavation site in 3 ABY. That year, the thief Kay Vess found and read the datapad while at the excavation site. Ruto was mentioned in the 2024 video game, Star Wars Outlaws developed by Massive Entertainment and published by Ubisoft.
