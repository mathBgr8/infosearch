Droidworks (disambiguation)
1307

Droidworks or DroidWorks may refer to the following items: *TransTech Droidworks, the manufacturer of the S-series survey droid and the SR-series scout/observation droid *Farrfin Droidworks, the manufacturer of the Aggressor-series battle droid with which they hoped to counter the Sith. *Imperial DroidWorks, the manufacturer of the Seneschal-series factotum droid. *Rossum Droidworks, a droid manufacturer and trader owned by Iaco Stark that worked on the Iron Eclipse project. *TransTech Droidworks, a Trade Federation corporation that produced battle droids on Loken III. *Star Wars: DroidWorks, a 1998 PC game released by Lucas Learning. *Droidworks Assault, a three-part set of scenarios published by Wizards of the Coast for the Star Wars Miniatures game.
