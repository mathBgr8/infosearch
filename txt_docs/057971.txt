Rutin Cobral
2016

Rutin Cobral was the husband of Lena Cobral and the son of Zanita Cobral. He grew sick of the Cobral family's plundering on the planet of Frego and compiled a case against them. However, his siblings and mother caught on and executed him. Rutin was avenged by his wife, Lena, who found the evidence and testified against the family in front of the Galactic Senate.
