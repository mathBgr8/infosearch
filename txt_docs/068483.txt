Cartann Ground Champion
857

The Cartann Ground Champion was the officially recognized top blastsword duelist in the Adumari nation of Cartann. The title was earned by winning the annual Cartann Ground Championship or by defeating the current champion in a duel. Becoming Ground Champion was the only way for a non-pilot to attain wealth and status in Cartann. In 13 ABY, the Cartann Ground Champion was Cheriss ke Hanadi.
