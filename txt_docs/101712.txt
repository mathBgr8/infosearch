Star Wars Adventures 11: Danger on Naboo
1843

Star Wars Adventures 11: Danger on Naboo was the ninth installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. This book is a reprint of Episode I Adventures 13: Danger on Naboo. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 11 in August 2003.
