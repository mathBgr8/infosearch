Sirromco
1242

}} Sirromco was a corporation based on Coruscant that sold office furniture during the late years of the Galactic Republic. Its taglines were Intimidate your business rivals! and Office furniture on a grand scale. In its advertisement it shows the table used in Count Dooku's conference at Geonosis, and later for the Death Star's conference room. Towards the end of the Separatist Crisis, Sirromco held a sale and were accepting all currencies for payment. Category:Vendors and sellers
