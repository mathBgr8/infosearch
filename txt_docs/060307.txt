Automated repair droid
1747

The automated repair droid, also known as the vehicle repair station, was an on-the-spot repair facility for combat situations produced by Loratus Manufacturing. The droid was equipped with a variety of tools to allow repairs of nearly any vehicle. When placed strategically on the battlefield, it provided patch jobs for vehicles that only had time for a quick fix.
