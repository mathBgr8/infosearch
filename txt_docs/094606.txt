Titan (Imperial-class)
2472

The Titan was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer active in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, it was part of Task Force Vengeance when it was tasked with retrieving the Interdictor Compellor together with the ISD Juggernaut.
