Big Nasty Free-For-All
809

The Big Nasty Free-For-All was a competition held by the Gungans as a test of skill for warriors. Events included the gulli-brawl, gulli-throw, and a race through the ocean. Rugor Nass won this competition a record three times in a row. *Episode I Adventures 10: Festival of Warriors *Episode I Adventures 11: Pirates from Beyond the Sea *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gungan culture Category:Sporting events
