Candorian colonies
1599

}} The Candorian colonies were colonies of the planet Candoria.The Essential Atlas In 46 BBY, Candoria and its colonies were depopulated by an outbreak of the Blue Shadow Virus. The disease was later known as the Candorian plague. The plague seemed to end then, but was introduced to Dentaal by the Empire forty-six years later. The Candorian colonies were first mentioned in the 1995 magazine Star Wars Adventure Journal 5 as the "Bandorian colonies". As they are related to the Candorian plague, "Bandorian" was considered a misspelling of "Candorian". The correct spelling was then confirmed by the 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas. *The Essential Atlas Category:Colonies
