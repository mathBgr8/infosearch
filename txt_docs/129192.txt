Most Perfect Order of K'vin
803

The Most Perfect Order of K'vin was a sect of the B'omarr Order, and were a group of disembodied brains who worshiped their Force-sensitive leader, K'vin. They were situated on Tatooine. The only definite distinguishing factor that separated the sect from the B'omarr was that green nutrient fluid was used to maintain the disembodied brains while the B'omarr used a red fluid. *Star Wars Missions 5: The Hunt for Han Solo *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Religious organizations
