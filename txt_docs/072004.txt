Raid on the Golden Nyss Shipyards
1968

Yinchorri raided the Golden Nyss Shipyards in 33 BBY. The goal was to capture a number of attack ships and Golden Nyss fighters from the Golden Nyss Shipyards. Yinchorri troops were smuggled in by Vilmarh Grahrk, who fooled the shipyard master, Gzin.
