Dan Android
2639

Dan Android was a masculine PR droid with blue photoreceptors. By the year 4.3 ABY, he was in charge of public relations for the Star Tours travel agency, introducing other tours on Tourscan. Dan Android was created for Tokyo Disneyland's simulator ride theme park attraction Star Tours, which opened on July 12, 1989. He was named after actor Dan Aykroyd, which was jokingly referenced by a Spot the Droid poster.
