Vet droid
1041

Vet droid was the name given to any medical droid designed for or specializing in treatment of animals. They were known to be active as of the time of the Galactic Empire, though it was apparently hard to find vet droids in small townships such as Anchorhead on the planet Tatooine. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Droid classifications *
