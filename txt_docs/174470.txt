Timoth 4
1058

Timoth 4 was an CR92a Assassin-class corvette belonging to the Bothans. Timoth 4 was carrying specialized decoding computers which could decipher the secret data onboard the Suprosa, a Imperial freighter that was stolen by the Rebels. Tan Maarek Stele inspected this corvette under orders of the secret order before allowing the ship to enter hyperspace.
