Vulnerator
1790

The Vulnerator was a Victory-class Star Destroyer assigned to the Galactic Alliance Fifth Fleet. Jedi Knight Jaina Solo commented that it had been in service as long as her parents. The Vulnerator and its two escorting frigates participated in the Battle of Kashyyyk. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance Category:Victory-class Star Destroyers
