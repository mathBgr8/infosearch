Star Wars: The Special Edition Box Set
1985

|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages= |isbn=1569712573 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series= |issues= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: The Special Edition Box Set was a box set collecting the Star Wars Special Edition Trilogy Trade paperbacks. Featuring a myriad of talent, including Al Williamson, Bruce Jones, Archie Goodwin, Eduardo Barreto, and the Brothers Hildebrandt, all three of the Star Wars Special Edition trade paperbacks are collected in this slipcased set! This boxed set is perfect for the completist Star Wars collector! *Star Wars: A New Hope - The Special Edition (TPB) *Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back - The Special Edition *Star Wars: Return of the Jedi - The Special Edition Original Trilogy Special Edition Special Edition
