Releqy A'Kla
3382

Releqy A'Kla was a female Caamasi who served the New Republic and the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances during the Yuuzhan Vong War. She was the daughter of Elegos A'Kla. In 28 ABY, she was present on Mon Calamari with the majority of the Senate that had survived the Fall of Coruscant, and supported Cal Omas in his bid to become Chief of State. After the Alderaanian won the election, Omas appointed Releqy Minister of State as well as appointing her to the High Council, where she voted against the use of the biological weapon Alpha Red.
