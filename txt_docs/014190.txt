Gotida
2609

Gotida was a celestial body located in the Goti system within the Slice portion of the Inner Rim. It was the site of the settlement known as Jalor City as well as its suburbs. Gotida was the homeworld of the Human Riic Mardec, who worked as a repulsortaxi driver since his teenage years and spent nearly fourteen years saving money to move his family out of Jalor City. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. however, the Galactic Empire carried out an attack on a Rebel Alliance cell in the city that resulted in Mardec's family being killed in cross-fire. Gotida was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
