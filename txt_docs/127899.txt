Imperial Remandation List
1779

}} The Imperial Remandation List was a list of individuals wanted by the Galactic Empire. Individuals identified as leaders of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, such as senators Mon Mothma and Leia Organa, were sentenced to death and placed on the list. The Imperial Remandation List was mentioned in an Imperial HoloVision report published in 0 ABY.The Imperial HoloVision report in states that it takes place on 35:9:1 of the Great ReSynchronization calendar, which corresponds to 0 ABY per the reasoning here. The Imperial Remandation List was mentioned in the Galaxywide NewsNets section written by Paul Sudlow and published in the the fourth issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine, which was published by West End Games in November of 1994.
