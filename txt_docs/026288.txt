By 7 ABY,Wanted by Cracken is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. Therefore, the events of Wanted by Cracken translate to 7 ABY. the Galactic Empire informant Rodin Baem was sighted on many worlds, most of which were controlled by the New Republic. Those worlds included Farsellah, and in a personal addendum to an entry on Baem included in the datafile Wanted by Cracken, New Republic General Airen Cracken noted that Baem's appearance on those worlds potentially signaled that the Empire would soon attack them and that the respective star systems should make appropriate preparations. Farsellah was mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, which was written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
