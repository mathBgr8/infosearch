Unidentified Bando Gora captain
2006

This Bando Gora Captain was a prominent figure among the Bando Gora. The alien cultist was Komari Vosa's chief lieutenant and relayed information about the outside world to her. Unlike lesser cultists, the captain was still capable of speaking. The game manual and credits list the character as "Bando Gora Captain" voiced by Nick Jameson. This character is labeled in an out-of-universe capacity as "Mysterious Scene Stealer" in the outtakes of the Star Wars: Bounty Hunter video game, having barged in on the recruitment of Jango Fett by Darth Tyranus.
