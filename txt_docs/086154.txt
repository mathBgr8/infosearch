Trade Federation Patrol Boat
1901

Trade Federation Patrol Boats were boats used by the Trade Federation in the many lakes, rivers, and seas of Naboo during the Invasion of Naboo in 32 BBY. They had a crew of two B1-series battle droids and were armed with one laser cannon. A flotilla were used to patrol a prison camp on the Solleu River; they failed to interdict three Naboo Resistance rescue ships from escaping with the prisoners.
