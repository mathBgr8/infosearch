Karrison Lee
1991

Karrison Lee was a smuggler who was fond of gambling even though he was not particularly good at it. He was a patron at the Ace of Sabres on the planet Kluistar. He had been imprisoned by the Empire at least three times, but always managed to escape. He also had been double-crossed and sold out by friends and partners many times, so he was very reluctant to trust others. Karrin was a typical brash smuggler who also enjoyed almost every form of gambling although he rarely won. He was personable and easy-going although because of his occupation and history he was reluctant to move beyond small talk. He was willing to transport almost anything anywhere anytime as long as the price was right. He owned a modified YT-2000 light freighter called the Distant Sunrise. He also carried around a heavy blaster pistol.
