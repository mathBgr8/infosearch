Shuttle 37
2028

Shuttle 37 was an Imperial shuttle originally designed to supply the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Chimaera in the Wistril system. During the Thrawn campaign, Mara Jade and Luke Skywalker stole the shuttle on Wistril and used it to gain entry to the Chimaera and rescue Talon Karrde. *Dark Force Rising *Dark Force Rising 5 *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Captured starships Category:Lambda-class T-4a shuttles Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
