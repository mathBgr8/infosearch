The Emperor's Trophy
4013

"The Emperor's Trophy" was a short story and role-playing game written by Peter Schweighofer, originally published in Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 11 and later reprinted on Hyperspace. After the duel on Cloud City, Lord Vader arrives at the Mount Tantiss storehouse. The Sith meets with his master the Emperor. He brings a trophy: his lightsaber — and the remains of Luke Skywalker's severed hand. Palpatine reveals that he has foreseen Skywalker's fate, and returns to Coruscant.
