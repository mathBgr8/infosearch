Arthézia
623

Arthézia was a planet and the homeworld of Professor Adazian Loh'Khar. His family lived on the planet while he worked for the Flight Unlimited Group, an Imperial-aligned corporation. The corporation threatened to injure Loh'Khar's family if he stopped working for them.' This planet is not mentioned in the The Essential Atlas. *
