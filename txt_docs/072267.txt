Squad Four (Galactic Republic)
1232

Squad Four was a squad of Alpha-3 Nimbus-class V-wing starfighters tasked with protecting Coruscant during the Clone Wars. The squad's fighters were painted gray with maroon markings, including a vertical jagged stripe visible when the wings were in flight position. When Supreme Chancellor Palpatine was kidnapped in 19 BBY, Squad Four and Clone Fight Squad Seven attacked the Invisible Hand in an attempt to help Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi rescue him. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization Category:Galactic Republic starfighter squadrons
