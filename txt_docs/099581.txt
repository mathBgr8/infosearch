Nubia Star Drives, Incorporated
2221

*T-14 hyperdrive generator |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Nubia Star Drives, Incorporated,Unlimited Power shorted to Nubia Star Drives, Inc., or referred to as Nubian Design Collective, was a manufacturing company. It notably produced the J-type 327 Nubian starship, the T-14 hyperdrive generator and certain sublight engines.
