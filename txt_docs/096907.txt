Paonnid
1587

The Paonnid were a sentient species from the Paonid system. In 9 ABY, while wandering on a marketplace on the planet of Bimmisaari, the New Republic hero Han Solo spotted a being who looked like a Paonnid among the crowd. They produced extrassa art similar to the Saffa paintings and the pre-Empire Vaathkree flatsculp. The Paonnid, the Paonid system and the Paonidd extrassa art were first mentioned in the novel Heir to the Empire, which used instinctively the spellings Paonnid and Paonidd.
