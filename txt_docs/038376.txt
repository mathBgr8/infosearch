Rogue Squadron Headquarters
1022

thumb|250px|Starfighter hangar at Rogue Squadron Headquarters Rogue Squadron Headquarters was the headquarters for the New Republic fighter unit Rogue Squadron. Located on Coruscant, it contained hangars for the squadron's X-wings, as well as living quarters and offices.
