Synthdroid
1751

Synthdroids were a type of droid created by Loronar Corporation and launched in 13 ABY.The Essential Guide to Droids They used sculpted synthskin over metal armatures to resemble Humans or other lifeforms. In this respect they were similar to Human replica droids. However, unlike Human replica droids, synthdroids used CCIR technology, allowing them to be controlled from a central location and to contain minimal internal computers. Loronar stressed the benefits of synthdroids including aesthetic quality, reliability, high performance standards and immunity to viruses. However, the CCIR technology made them expensive. Synthdroids could be found in pleasure domes on Hesperidium and Carosi XII, where they were quite popular. *Planet of Twilight *Fate of the Jedi: Conviction *The Essential Guide to Droids *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Droid classifications Category:Loronar Corporation products
