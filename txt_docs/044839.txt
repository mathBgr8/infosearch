Ibian
1574

The Ibian is a small amphibious creature native to Tatooine, the ibian fed on microscopic dust mites and insects. It was in turn preyed upon by the worrt. These small lifeforms could be recognized by their elongated orange and blue bodies, as well as their distinctive eye stalks. It is assumed that, like the amphibious gorg, ibians retained moisture by inhabiting abandoned profogg burrows, as such accounts did exist. *The Art of Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace *The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: What Makes a Monster?
