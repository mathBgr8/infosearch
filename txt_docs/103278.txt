The Mneffe-class Superluminal was comprised of a long, central fuselage slung beneath a semi-circular wing. Capable of carrying up to six passengers and twenty metric tons of cargo, the Mneffe-class required a pilot and copilot to operate, though could be flown with a skeleton crew of one. For defense, the vessel was armed with a turret-mounted double laser cannon. The Mneffe-class was built by DuroTech.
