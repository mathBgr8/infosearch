The Baby Ewoks' Picnic Surprise
2559

The Baby Ewoks' Picnic Surprise is a 1984 children's book written by Melinda Luke and illustrated by Pat Paris. It was published by Random House. Wicket and Kneesaa babysit Nippet and Wiley and decide to take them fishing and have a picnic.
