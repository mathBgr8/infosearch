Sardoran,Alliance Intelligence Reports at one point known as Ord Sardoran, was a world states that the term "Ordnance/Regional Depot" and the abbreviation "Ord" applied to certain worlds settled by the Galactic Republic. located in the Sardoran system, a part of the Arrowhead, or the Slice portion of the Core Worlds. It served as an Ordnance/Regional Depot outpost for the Galactic Republic during the Pius Dea Era, a period of time that lasted between approximately 12,000 BBY and 11,000 BBY. The Human Casti Tholon, who was active by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. was native to Sardoran, although she grew up in the Core Worlds' Duro system. Sardoran was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas subsequently placed the Sardoran system, and therefore the world itself, in grid square M-11, while the 2012 reference book The Essential Guide to Warfare by Jason Fry and Paul R.
