Hayzo Trebors
1034

Hayzo Trebors was an entrepreneur who decided to build an amusement park on Aralia, thinking the planet unpopulated. As construction was started, it was discovered that the Ranats, a large species of omnivorous rodents, lived on the planet. The Ranats attacked the workers, the crew was defeated, and the project ended in disaster.
