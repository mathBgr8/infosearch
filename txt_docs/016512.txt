At some point by the time of the Galactic Civil War, the interstellar smuggler and pirate organization Black Bha'lir had established a facility on Duunir that was the largest of several isolated refuges for the creature also known as the Black Bha'lir. The latter, a forest predator from the world of Iyred, had been adopted as a mascot of the criminal group, and consequently the Black Bha'lir founded the Duunir refuge as a charity. At that and other, similar facilities, small numbers of Black Bha'lir were raised despite the creature being officially listed as extinct in the wake of environmental degradation on Iyred. Duunir was mentioned in The Black Sands of Socorro, a 1997 sourcebook authored by Patricia A. Jackson for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
