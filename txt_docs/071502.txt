Bakuran power station
1305

}} The Bakuran power station was located atop a dormant volcano in the mountains of Bakura. In 14 ABY, the power station was occupied by Imperial Remnant who sought to sabotage it by setting off explosive charges. The Imperials damaged the power plant during the initial takeover and also set up defensive autoturrets and E-Webs.
