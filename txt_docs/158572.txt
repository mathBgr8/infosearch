Imperial Defense Daily
1057

thumb|250px||right The Imperial Defense Daily, or IDD, was a daily newsfeed service for those interested in the Imperial Military. It profiled spacecraft and weapons systems as they became declassified, and was thus very popular among weapons enthusiasts. An example of this was when the IDD were the first to provide coverage of the Storm Commando, describing their equipment, training, and future missions. *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:News networks and companies
