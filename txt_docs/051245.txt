Yarkora (language)
716

Yarkora was the native language of the Yarkora species. It was never spoken in the presence of non-Yarkora. All Yarkora found on the galaxy were fluent in both Yarkora and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
