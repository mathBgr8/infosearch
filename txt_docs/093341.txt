Retribution (Imperial-class)
2859

|captains= |crewmembers=Captain Tomax Bren |registry= |aliases= }} Retribution was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer. The vessel was attached to the Qeimet fleet and functioned as home base for the TIE bombers of Scimitar assault wing. During the Hook Nebula campaign, the Retribution was essentially relegated to a support role for the TIE bombers so they could carry out the primary mission. Retribution was later found to have been appropriated by Moff Lobax Resuun of the Tamarin sector, some time after the Battle of Endor. It was part of his personal fleet and served as Resuun's base of operations.
