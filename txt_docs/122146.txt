NovaFlare

The NovaFlare was a Corellian corvette in service to the Rebel Alliance Fleet during the Galactic Civil War. Tresk Ortola, after spending several years as first officer of a rebel gunship, was promoted to Captain and given command of the NovaFlare prior to the Battle of Endor in 4 ABY. *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
