Arthur Papadam
2220

thumb|200px|Arthur Papadam in his own Jedi costume. Arthur (Arfy) Papadam wrote the backstory of Bal'demnic, and co-wrote the backstory of Leektar appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. In 2018 Arfy wrote and directed a fan film / mockumentary called The Dackwaller Files, which he submitted in the 2018 Star Wars Fan Awards, Long Video category. It was selected as a finalist for the Audience Choice award. He played for the 2014 national Australian quidditch team, and invented wheelchair quidditch in 2011. Arthur is a graphic designer, and redesigned the website of Abel G. Peña in 2009. Other fan projects include the creation of Star Wars Outsider magazine in 2007, being a former administrator at Wookieepedia (under alias ), and costume making (see Jedi costume pictured right). His alias is "Arf Maul" on StarWars.com, and on TheForce.net. (with Andrew Gilbertson) - November 2005 - February 2006 Category:What's The Story? authors
