Silencer-7 was the lead World Devastator that attacked the water world of Mon Calamari when Emperor Palpatine returned in a clone body. During the battle, where it destroyed Mon Remonda and Emancipator, it was captained by Commander Titus Klev. Silencer-7 was the largest and most powerful of the World Devastators, reaching a tonnage that put it in dreadnought class within the Anaxes War College System of starship classifications.
