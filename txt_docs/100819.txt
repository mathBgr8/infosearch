Kut-Rate Kruise Lines
799

Kut-Rate Kruise Lines, also known as Kut-Rate Kruises, was a transport company during the Clone Wars which provided cheap conveyance to a number of planets in the galaxy. Although their ships were poorly kept, they had a good record of reaching their destinations with little to no problems. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous Category:Transport companies
