Ed Hannigan
1112

Ed Hannigan (born August 6, 1951) is a comic book illustrator who worked on a few issues in the Marvel Star Wars comic series. Hannigan began in the comic industry in the 1970s pencilling several titles at Marvel and DC Comics including Defenders and Green Arrow.
