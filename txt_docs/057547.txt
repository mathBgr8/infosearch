It was stationed in Sector 2 of the Tomorrowland Starport. DL-X2 was a droid created as part of the Star Tours ride for Disneyland in California. It was a modified version of an Imagineer-developed prop for the robot TK4600 from the NBC police drama series Hill Street Blues.
