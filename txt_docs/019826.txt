|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=3.5 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire |issue=6 |next=last }} Shadows of the Empire 6 was the sixth and final issue in the six-part Shadows of the Empire series of comics, and was released on October 1, 1996 by Dark Horse Comics. Leia has been captured by Prince Xizor, underlord of the galaxy's most ruthless criminal organization, and Han Solo has been encased in carbonite by Darth Vader, a package Boba Fett intends to deliver to Jabba the Hutt. As the Rebels approach their most deperate[sic] hour, it appears as though their long battle may be at an end. But looks can be deceiving in the Shadows of the Empire!
