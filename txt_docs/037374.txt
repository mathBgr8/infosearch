Automated fire extinguisher
739

thumb|right|An automated fire extinguisher. Automated fire extinguishers were simple droids used around 3951 BBY. Mounted on turrets, they used carbonite freeze rays to put out fires, or, if their programming was altered as happened at the Peragus Mining Facility, to freeze living beings where they stood. In the case of Peragus, HK-50 was responsible for the alteration of their programming to prevent the miners from capturing the Jedi Exile. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Firefighter droid models
