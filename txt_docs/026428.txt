Kenobi Medallion
2373

The Kenobi Medallion was a bronze medal that was awarded to Alliance Army personnel for displaying extraordinary acts of bravery in combat. It was a sixth embellishment to the Kalidor Crescent enacted shortly after the Battle of Yavin, initially as a means to posthumously honor those who fell in combat during said battle.
