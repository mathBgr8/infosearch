Tosi-karu
1360

Tosi-karu was a Sernpidalian goddess.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia When the Praetorite Vong used the Yo'gand's Core tactic to pull one of Sernpidal's moons, Dobido, into the planet in 25 ABY, the Sernpidalians believed this to be the arrival of Tosi-karu, and panicked accordingly and/or prayed in the streets instead of fleeing from the falling moon. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Deities
