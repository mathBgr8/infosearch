Moraj Memorial

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Moraj Memorial was a memorial near the city of Bestine on Tatooine. It was erected by the first settlers and founders of the Bestine Township—"in memory of brave Captain Moraj". This memorial was placed by the developers in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies to honor Nathan "Moraj_Markinnison" Larkins, one of the first beta testers, who had died shortly after the beta test launched due to a car crash. It was placed in the exact location where the first beta testers entered the game. It is commonly called "Moraj Memorial" by players and fansites, although the in-game description of the memorial flag just calls it "A historical marker". The developers also added a special guild hall with a Tatooine Skin that could be placed on any planet. It was noted in the Guinness Book of Video Game Records to be the first MMORPG to have a virtual memorial for a deceased, real life individual.
