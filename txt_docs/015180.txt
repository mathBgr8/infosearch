Kalkovak
2141

|imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Kalkovak was a remote planet populated by various species including a large sector populated by Quarren. It was a port world for travelers going to the Outer Rim Territories. Illyan Webble was left on the planet after he irritated the crew he was traveling with.
