9j ion power cell
597

The 9j ion power cell was the backup power source in the Cygnus Spaceworks Assault Gunboat, and was manufactured by Iotek. Eventually Iotek found an alternate backup power source that was more sustainable, which they converted most of their ships to use. Category:Energy cells and power cells
