Tarneel
1215

Tarneel was a Human male who headed the Peace Brigade on Ord Sedra. During the Battle of Ord Sedra, he was Force-pushed by Kyle Katarn and taken into custody. It is unknown what happened to him during the Yuuzhan Vong prison breakout. Although not named in the comic, Tarneel was named in the script. Author Nathan Butler named the character after the Tar Heels. (49:08) Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Peace Brigaders
