Mazanga
1284

Mazanga was a male Dug who served under Sebolto's employ who, along with Ghazdik Jah, was wanted by Senators Ask Aak and Aks Moe for the murder of special assistants to the Senators of Malastare. In 32 BBY, his bounty worth 3,500 credits alive and 3,000 credits dead.
