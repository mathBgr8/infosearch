Reina Faleur
2793

Reina Faleur was a female Human from Chandrila who held the rank of lieutenant under the New Republic Defense Fleet's Quartermaster Corps. Several months after the Battle of Endor, Faleur was tasked with supplying Rogue Squadron the equipment it needed. She briefly had a romantic liaison with Wedge Antilles. Reina Faleur first appeared in the thirty-second issue of the Dark Horse comic book series Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron, which was written by Michael A.
