Darjeelai Swan
1529

Darjeelai Swan was a Human female from Corellia who lived during the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 28 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion she joined Thrackan Sal-Solo on his journey to Yuuzhan'tar in order to have the Centerpoint Party and Corellia sign a peace accord with the Yuuzhan Vong.
