Core Rulebook can refer to any of three basic rulebooks released by Wizards of the Coast for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game or any of the three rulebooks published by Fantasy Flight Games for their Star Wars Roleplaying game. *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Revised Core Rulebook *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Saga Edition Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Age of Rebellion Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook *Star Wars: Force and Destiny Core Rulebook
