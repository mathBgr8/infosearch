Rainbow-Eyed Enemy
524

The Rainbow-Eyed Enemy, or Rainbow-Eyed One, was the name given to Shimrra Jamaane by the Jeedai heretics due to his mqaaq'it implants. It was said among the Shamed Ones who practiced the heresy that it was his evil that caused the Yun'o to call down their holy warriors, the Jeedai, to destroy him. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee Category:Yuuzhan Vong deities
