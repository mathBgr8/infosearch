*1996 Star Wars Technical Journal of the Rebel Forces, Special Edition, pg. 11, notes that the magazine's three Special Edition volumes were published prior to September 15, 1996. |subscribers= |pages=83 |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=3 |series=The Official Star Wars Technical Journal }} Star Wars Technical Journal of the Rebel Forces, also styled as "Star Wars Technical Journal Vol. 3," was the final volume in The Official Star Wars Technical Journal magazine series from Starlog Communications International. It was published in October 1994. The three issues were later compiled into the compendium title Star Wars Technical Journal. In 1996, to celebrate the upcoming release of The Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition, Starlog reprinted all three volumes of the Technical Journal as "Special Edition" versions. Each Special Edition volume came in a sealed poly bag along with bonus content in the form of an eight-page gatefold supplement.
