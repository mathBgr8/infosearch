Tonkoss Rathba
1714

Tonkoss Rathba was a being who was dictator of Daluba. Leia Organa Solo had been involved in diplomacy with Rathba some time prior to the Osarian-Rhommamool conflict in 25 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime to 25 ABY. and she considered Rathba to be something of a tyrant.
