Bacta Tanker
1038

A Bacta Tanker was a type of cargo vessel used by the government of Thyferra. This type of ship was 300 meters long from prow bridge to stern hyperdrive, having an almost insectoid feel about it. The ship's central section had two parts, each of which held six cargo cylinders. At the beginning of the journey, those cylinders were filled of bacta and, once at destination, they were swapped with others similar cylinders with the help of smaller ships. The returned cylinders might be empty or they could carry native goods to be imported on Thyferra or traded later during the rest of the journey. For armament, these ships were armed with two dorsal turbolasers. *X-Wing: The Bacta War Category:Tanker classes
