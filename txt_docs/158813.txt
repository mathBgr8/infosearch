Coruscant Financial Exchange
1208

The Coruscant Financial Exchange was an agency of the Galactic Republic, established by the Coruscant Financial Exchange Establishment Act in the year 3985 BBY.Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide It regulated corporate finances throughout the Republic.
