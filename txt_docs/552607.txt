Rendili was an ancient planet located in the Rendili system within the Core Worlds, positioned at the coordinates M-11 on the Standard Galactic Grid. The planet was the headquarters of the shipbuilding corporation Rendili StarDrive, as well as the company's predecessor, Rendili Hyperworks.Dawn of Rebellion After the First Battle of Geonosis, during which the Acclamator-class assault ships were introduced, the Arch-Provost ordered industrial spies and starship designers to reduce Kuat's lead for new contracts handed out by the Galactic Republic. The planet's extensive system of smaller shipyards scattered across the Mid Rim and Outer Rim Territories gave Rendili a temporary ascendancy. During the Imperial Era, Rendili supported the Galactic Emperor Sheev Palpatine. In 5 ABY, the New Republic General Hera Syndulla noted the corvettes that were manufactured on Rendili and Corellia while the fleet assembled near the planet Jakku. These ships participated in the Battle of Jakku,Victory's Price: An Alphabet Squadron Novel which marked the end the Galactic Civil War with the subsequent signing of the Galactic Concordance.Star Wars: The Force Awakens: The Visual Dictionary After the signing of the Galactic Concordance, which conditionally allowed the remnants of the Empire to peacefully co-exist with the New Republic, Rendili came under control of the Inner systems' Imperial remnant.
