R-65 heavy blaster
809

thumb|right|250px|A R-65 heavy blaster. R-65 heavy blaster was used by the Naboo Security Guards. The blaster shot twin energy bolts that could bounce off walls and other solid objects. The energy bolts could pierce through armor, whereas regular blaster shots couldn't. The R-65 was Captain Quarsh Panaka's personal blaster. His R-65 was a twin-bolt model of the standard guard CR-2 heavy blaster pistol. *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace video game Category:Heavy blaster pistols
