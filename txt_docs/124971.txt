Osprey
2014

The Osprey was a Nebulon-B2 frigate used by the Empire, more specifically the Prophets of the Dark Side. It was first used when Maarek Stele was set up by Admiral Harkov and had called for backup. Its next sighting was when the Immobilizer 418 cruiser Harpago, under the command of Grand Admiral Zaarin, tried to trap a TIE/D Defender squadron which had Darth Vader as one of the pilots.
