Sector Delta
963

Sector Delta was a district of the mysterious space station known as Zirtran's Anchor. It was the location of a number of various different businesses and services and also served as residence of a number of famous and infamous individuals. The most well lnown locations in Sector Delta were the Death Mark, Reenogga Personal Services, Cubed, the Rusted Cutlass II, and the Laughing Sullustan. Delta, Sector
