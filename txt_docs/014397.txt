Dolis 3 was a planet situated in the Dolis system of the Obtrexta sector in the Outer Rim Territories, located at grid square J-3. The world featured an urban environment that experienced heavy snowfall, with flying vehicles passing between towering buildings. Several structures featured snow-covered statues, and slum areas were found on ground level. Chasa owned operated a cantinaThe Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia on Dolis 3. Banner Sumptor's team operated for the Imperial Remnant on the planet, and became stranded after the peace accord with the New Republic. During the announcement of the wedding of Luke Skywalker and Mara Jade, Sumptor ventured to Chasa's establishment, offering credits to one of the unemployed peoples begging on the snowy streets.
