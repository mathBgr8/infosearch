|editor= |website=Wizards.com |publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=*April 1, 2004 *April 8, 2004 *April 15, 2004 *April 22, 2004 *April 29, 2004 |series=Planet Hoppers |preceded by=Vandelhelm: Enemies and Alloys |followed by=Hapes: Ladies First }} Adumar: Pilots Wanted was a five-part Planet Hoppers article series from April 2004, covering the planet Adumar. The articles were written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards.com by Wizards of the Coast as a supplement for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. &hellip;an anonymous New Republic Intelligence agent offers a field report on the culture of Adumar. &hellip;Derek Klivian provides an X-wing pilot's perspective on Adumar's Blade fighters. &hellip;Wes Janson offers a brawler's look at ubiquitous Adumari honor duels and blastswords. &hellip;Tycho Celchu describes Adumari native Cheriss ke Hanadi's contributions to the planet's history. &hellip;Wedge Antilles offers a few words of wisdom for Adumar and recalls the turning point in his diplomatic mission.
