Le Yer
1941

Located on the planet Abregado-rae, Le Yer was a beautiful, manicured coastal region and was known as resort city. It was home to the Triple Nova Casino. A subdivision of Le Yer was Phyrstal Island, reachable primarily by hydrobike, which had attractions like the Lost Loves Casino, the Starlight Theater, and Njinska's Tavern.
