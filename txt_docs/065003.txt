Gromas Mines
2101

The Gromas Mines were an Imperial mining facility located on the moon Gromas 16. It was built when the Empire cleaned the Gromas system, in order to be a part of Rom Mohc's Dark Trooper Project and supply it with phrikite. Kyle Katarn, having discovered a sample on Fest, was led to infiltrate the facility, detonating the reactor to destroy it.
