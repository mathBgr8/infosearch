Star Wars Reunion II
1499

thumb|right|250px|Star Wars Reunion II logo Star Wars Reunion II was held at Le Grand Rex, the famous theatre of Paris, October 27 through October 28, 2007, almost thirty years after the first French release of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. On May 3, 2007, StarWars.com posted regarding Star Wars Reunion II. The six films of Star Wars where screened in a two-day marathon, all in French. *Rick McCallum *Anthony Daniels *Robert Watts *Lorne Peterson *Stephen J. Sansweet *Garrick Hagon *Richard LeParmentier *Orli Shoshan *Dan Madsen *Doug Wheatley *Roger Carel - French voice of C-3PO *Bruno Choël - French voice of Obi-Wan Kenobi *Bernard Dhéran - French voice of Count Dooku *Francis Lax - French voice of Han Solo *Jean Lescot - French voice of Yoda Category:Official Star Wars conventions
