E-series protocol droid
886

The E-series protocol droid was a line of protocol droids. Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt of the New Republic Intelligence considered them superior to the most well known 3PO-series protocol droids. Five of theses droids served onboard the Belarus-class medium cruiser Glorious during the hunt for the Teljkon Vagabond. *Before the Storm Category:Protocol droid models
