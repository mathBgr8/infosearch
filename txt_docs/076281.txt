Star Lady
1807

The Star Lady was a modified YT-2400 light freighter and the ship of the con-artist duo Fenig Nabon and Ghitsa Dogder. It was in the drydocks of Nal Hutta when Fen and Ghits teamed with Shada D'ukal to deliver Clan Shak females to Durga the Hutt. thumb|left|Nabon and Dogder standing on the Star Lady boarding ramp.
