Terra Sool militia fighter

thumb Terra Sool militia fighters were operated by the militia of Terra Sool. They fought under the banner of the Galactic Republic. The fighters were single-seater ships shaped like a rounded flying wing, similar in shape to the DC0052 speeder. They fared poorly against CIS Floating Battle Platforms during the battle.
