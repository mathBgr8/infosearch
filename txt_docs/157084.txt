Battle of Selvaris
3017

A battle at Selvaris took place in 29 ABY, near the end of the Yuuzhan Vong War. The Yuuzhan Vong were preparing to move a number of ranking Galactic Alliance prisoners from Selvaris to Coruscant in preparation for a major sacrifice. The prisoners included a number of celebrated heroes and resistance leaders, including Pash Cracken and Judder Page, whose continuing operations were deemed needed to continue the war effort.
