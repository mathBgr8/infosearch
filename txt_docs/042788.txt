Mendacian funeral urn
739

thumb|Big Gizz opens one of the urns. Jabba Desilijic Tiure had a colletion of eight Mendacian funeral urns kept in the Spirit of Jabba hidden inside Glass Mountain and protected by Onoh and the Jabba Tape. The urns were from the young Krung dynasty and were considered priceless. Much to the horror of Gorga Desilijic Aarrpo, the urns were destroyed by Big Gizz and Spiker. *"The Jabba Tape" *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Artifacts
