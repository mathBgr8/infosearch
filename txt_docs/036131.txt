Mageye
1550

Mageye was a Hutt crime lord from the planet of Taboon. In 1 BBY, he was caught in a crossfire between the Thig Brothers and Jodo Kast. Mageye was dropped by the Weequays who'd been carrying him and rolled on top of bounty hunter Zardra, prompting her to stick a thermal detonator in his mouth rather than be suffocated.
