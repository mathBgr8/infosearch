Ferryboat liner
2068

The Ferryboat Liner was a civilian light vessel in use during the Galactic Civil War, manufactured by the SoroSuub Corporation. Built for tourists and business travelers, it was equipped with drinking and dining establishments, nightclubs, and casinos. A Ferryboat Liner could carry 500 passengers. They often hosted large banquets and parties.
