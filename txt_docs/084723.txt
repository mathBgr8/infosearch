WW-47 CryoBan grenade
1123

The WW-47 was a CryoBan grenade produced by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc.. The WW-47 resembled a short stick, with an activation trigger on the side and a timer dial on the pin. This grenade used the CryoBan chemical agent which absorbed all heat on activation, creating a field of freezing cold. *Fate of the Jedi: Allies *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:CryoBan grenades
