Kattadan Guard
1158

Kattadan Guards were the planet Kattada's primary defense force. They wore blue body armor and were armed with blasters. They were lightly armed since Kattada was a peaceful planet. Members of the organization served Kattada's ruler Madam Mia Ikova as her personal body guards in 1 BBY.
