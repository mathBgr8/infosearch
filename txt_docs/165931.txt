Corphelion Comets
1047

The Corphelion Comets was a celestial phenomenon over Corphelion. The silver light and movement from the comets' display were a popular tourist attraction and mood setter for romantic getaways. As the comets were a natural phenomenon instead of being created by intelligent beings, they were not included in the list of the Twenty Wonders of the Galaxy by the historian Vicendi in in his 10,000 BBY publication Arturum Galactinum.The Essential Atlas Han and Leia Solo stopped on Corphelion to see the light show in the early stages of their honeymoon. *Crucible *The Essential Atlas Category:Comets Category:Corpheli sector locations
