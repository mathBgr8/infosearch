Rubogean Gambit
897

The Rubogean Gambit was a political maneuver in which one party made a provocative statement to an opponent, in an effort to distract the opponent and make them so upset they were unable to discern the other's true motives.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Luke Skywalker used this move at a Jedi Convocation on Ossus during the Dark Nest Crisis in 36 ABY, asking every Jedi to rethink their commitment to the Order. This was to distract them so they would have trouble noticing Luke's power play in becoming Jedi Grand Master. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tactics
