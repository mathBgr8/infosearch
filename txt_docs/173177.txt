Agromech droid
935

Agromech droids were the planetary, agrarian version of astromech droids. Industrial Automaton produced several types of agromech in the years leading up to the formation of the Galactic Empire, including the R2-AG and R4-series droids of the popular R-series of astromech droids. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley *Star Wars Technical Journal of the Planet Tatooine *The New Essential Guide to Droids *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary 2
