People's Inquest
2019

The People's Inquest was a grassroots populist Jedi-accountability watch-group active before and during the Clone Wars. They supported Jonava Billane in the Case of "Baby Ludi". Thrynka Padaunete was president of the People's Inquest. They were known for their multiple petitions and marches on the Jedi Temple. In one particular march, several Padawans were overwhelmed and were unable to quell the angry group. Jedi Master Plo Koon easily dispatched the disruptive mob using a Jedi mind trick. In the wake of Order 66, the People's Inquest aided the Galactic Empire by compiling a list of all known Jedi that were still at large, along with information on other Force-using sects. *Imperial Commando: 501st *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Organizations
