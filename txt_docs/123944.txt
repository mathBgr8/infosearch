thumb|right|200px|An example of Mark's work from the [[Galactic Campaign Guide]] Mark Tedin is an artist who first began working for Wizards of the Coast over eighteen years ago. His most notable work is for the collectible card game Magic, but he has also illustrated for a number of their Star Wars products.
