Mad Mrelf
537

Mad Mrelf was a Corellian liquor renowned for its potency. Reportedly, it was strong enough that many spacers who had imbibed the liquor awoke in Imperial labor camps or other unpleasant places after a drinking spree. Salla Zend was known to drink Mad Mrelf. *The Hutt Gambit *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corellian alcohols
