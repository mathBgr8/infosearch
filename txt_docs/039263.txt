Subjugation of the Yatir
1981

The subjugation of the Yatir on the planet Absit was a battle that took place during the Galactic Civil War, on the Yatir homeworld. The planet was overrun by the Imperial Army's Imperial Hammers Elite Armor Unit, also known as the Hell's Hammers; the conflict resulted in the subjugation of the Yatir. *Imperial Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Battles in the Tunka sector Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War Category:Conquests and occupations
