Sabiador Slavers
856

The Sabiador Slavers were one of the largest slaving operators during the Galactic Civil War, operating in and around the Kathol sector. They were destroyed during the Battle of Kathol System between Moff Kentor Sarne and the crew of the FarStar. One-time members of the Slavers were the Human Uta T'Cha and the Rodian Gorak Khzam. *The DarkStryder Campaign *The Kathol Outback Category:Enslaver organizations
