Kashyyyk blue primate
1296

The blue primates of Kashyyyk were arboreal monkeys. Each of their hands and feet had five digits, one of which was opposable. They had long tails and wide, flat faces. Their fur and skin were blue, but their eyes were yellow. They could be found in the vicinity of Palsaang.
