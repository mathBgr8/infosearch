Red Hand
983

The Red Hand was a criminal organization on Coruscant that undertook a series of political assassinations during the Clone Wars. They made their way around the world-city using the underground sewer network and followed the Code of the Streets. Ayo Morota was its leader, and Yoda assigned Quinlan Vos to infiltrate it. He killed every individual of their organization who confronted him. Category:Criminal organizations
