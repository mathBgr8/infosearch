Gauntlet starfighter
3375

The Gauntlet starfighter was manufactured by Shobquix Yards. Measuring fourteen meters in length, it required a pilot and a gunner/co-pilot. It carried consumables for one or two days and 85 kilograms of cargo. The craft could achieve 850 kilometers per hour, and it did not have a hyperdrive. For combat it boasted two forward fire-linked laser cannons, two turreted fire-linked laser cannons, one proton torpedo launcher, and modest shields. It was widely used by criminals, including the Tenloss Syndicate and Black Sun.
