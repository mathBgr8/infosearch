The following events took place in 206 BBY. *The Krozurbian war begins around this year.According to Star Wars Omnibus: Droids, the story featured in Star Wars Droids: Season of Revolt is set five and a half years before the Battle of Yavin, which puts it in 6 BBY. Between the first and fourth issue, 200 years of Krozurbian war were brought to an end, placing the war's start in or around 206 BBY. *Oppo Rancisis on ThisspiasThe New Essential Guide to Characters The New Essential Guide to Characters states that Oppo Rancisis is 174 years old as of the Battle of Naboo in 32 BBY.
