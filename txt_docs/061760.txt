Outer Javin Company
1597

The Outer Javin Company (OJC) was founded by Ecclessis Figg and his wife Yarith as the first of the soon-to-be Figg conglomerate. Headquartered on the planet Gerrenthum, its main purpose was to chart the Greater Javin area of the Outer Rim Territories. Outer Javin was a highly profitable company, and managed to stay in existence through the rule of the Galactic Empire. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Scouting companies
