Langoona was the homeworld of the Langoonans. The species specialized in the making of deathmasks. The Langoonans were enslaved by the Hutt Empire and gradually became a favored slave species. However, they rebelled against the Hutts but were crushed and large numbers of Langoonans were slaughtered. In retaliation, the Hutts stripped the world of all technology and reduced the surviving Langoonans to a primeval existence.
