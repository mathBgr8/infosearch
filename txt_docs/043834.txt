Steve Chorney
3592

Steve Chorney is an illustrator. He was the cover artist for the junior book series Star Wars: Galaxy of Fear and Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure. He also illustrated nine cards in the 2021 Topps Chrome Star Wars Galaxy set. Chorney has created book covers, trading cards, movie posters, and other printed media from the 1970s to the 2020s.
