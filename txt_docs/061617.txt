Alliance Civilian Battle Award
1101

The Alliance Civilian Battle Award was an honor presented by the Rebel Alliance High Command to civilians who assisted in the fight against the Galactic Empire. Ace and Aeron Azzameen were among the recipients of this award for their independent actions in acquiring arms for the Rebel Alliance.
