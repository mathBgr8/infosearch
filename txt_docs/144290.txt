Aleas Rans'ery was a well-trained pilot for the Car'das based on Tansarii Point Station, along with Serissu, Laetin A'shera and Dartas Pytin. In 1 ABY, she oversaw all Car'das operations in the Bright Jewel Ribbon quadrant of the Bright Jewel system. During the Battle of Tansarii Point Station between Car'das and Black Sun forces, Aleas Rans'ery dispatched pilots to attacks Black Sun ships in the area. During the battle, Rans'ery also tasked the Kallor brothers Sulale and Waris to scout enemy territory and find weaknesses in the Salient Trinity bases. The brothers succeed in their mission but were attacked by Black Sun starfighters in the Bright Jewel Ribbon. Car'das starfighters dispatched by Rans'ery finally rescued the brothers. Rans'ery's pilots later rescued First Officer Atris of the Dynath Miners from Black Sun attacks. Aleas Rans'ery eventually tasked her pilots to eliminate the ace and Black Sun hero Arlem Demonhunter. Aleas Rans'ery appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Aleas Rans'ery was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
