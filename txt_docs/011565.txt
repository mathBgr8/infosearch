Jinzler family
2191

In the years of peace before the Clone Wars, the Jinzler family lived on Coruscant, in the shadow of the Jedi Temple. The Jinzler parents worked at the Temple itself, as maintenance engineers on the electrical equipment. They adored and honored the Jedi and took each of their children in to be tested for potential Force sensitivity. The third Jinzler child, Lorana, was accepted for Jedi training at the age of 10 months.
