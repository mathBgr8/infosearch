X-31 landspeeder
1926

The SoroSuub X-31 landspeeder was the predecessor of the X-34 landspeeder. First produced prior to the Clone Wars, it was still used after the Battle of Yavin. During the Galactic Civil War, SoroSuub provided these vehicles to planetary governments, meant to be supplied to municipal employees, maintenance workers and donated to farmers, pioneers and other citizens for support. *Kenobi *Rebel Force: Hostage Category:Landspeeder models Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
