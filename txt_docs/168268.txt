Friij News Services

Friij News Services was a front for the Tenloss Syndicate. It was a Hutt-sponsored news network that operated as a clearing house for information and illegal data acquisition and replication. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations Category:News networks and companies Category:Tenloss Syndicate companies
