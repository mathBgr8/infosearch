Mookiee
4059

Mookiee, a female wokling, lived on the forest moon of Endor around the time of the Battle of Endor. She came of age and received her hood&mdash;together with Malani and Gwig&mdash;at the Festival of Hoods. She could often be seen playing with a wheeled animal toy. She was named for a legendary huntress in Ewok mythology.Galactic Campaign Guide thumb|left|Mookiee plush toy Mookiee first appeared as one of six 8-inch plush woklings made by Kenner as part of their Return of the Jedi toy line, then as a figure in their Kenner Preschool division's Wicket the Ewok toy line. She is part of the Endor Woodland Wagon set with Paploo and Latara. Though Mookiee has yet to be identified in the Ewoks animated television show, two different Nelvana animation model sheets feature variations on a female character named "Mookie," with and without a hood, which matches characters appearing in some episodes.
