Rrakktorr
508

The rrakktorr was the inner strength and fire of male Wookiees. To unlock it, young Wookiees would go through the hrrtayyk ceremony at their age of ascension. *A Forest Apart *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Category:Wookiee culture
