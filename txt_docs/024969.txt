Guanolta
1316

Guanolta was a male Dug who worked for the crime lord Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, Guanolta was wanted by the Malastarian Art Union for the mixing of several sets of rare paint in a public display of Malastarian art and the sale of death sticks to the young Pixelito.
