Kath Talor
1497

Kath Talor was a female Human who was the wife of Rand Talor. She was found on Sarapin while Rebel forces infiltrated the planet around the Battle of Yavin. A decrease in production alerted the Galactic Empire to that, and in a few days, the planet was back in Imperial hands. Boba Fett helped the Empire capture Kath and she was held as a captive of Tal Ashen. Her husband, Rand, participated in the Liberation of Coruscant.
