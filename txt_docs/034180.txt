Polyhedron 103
1018

Polyhedron 103 was a roleplaying magazine from October of 1994. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "Prophets of the Dark Side: Villains for the Star Wars: New Republic Campaign, Part One" and was written by Bill Slavicsek and Michele Carter. Category:Polyhedron
