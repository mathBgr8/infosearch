Guril
844

Guril was a group of four BFF-1 bulk freighters which were used by the Rebel Alliance in the Airam sector. When the decision was made to construct a shipyard in Airam space, Guril, were part of a Rebel convoy charged with bringing the needed supplies and personnel. Guril bore generic supplies. Unfortunately for the Rebels, the Galactic Empire had learned of them and readied a trap in the Rocrin system.
