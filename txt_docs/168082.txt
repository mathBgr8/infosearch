It was the original design of the Purgatory-class prison ship. The Porter-class transport was originally designed as a troop conveyance during the Clone Wars, but its cramped and uncomfortable accommodations caused the soldiers it transported in the initial trials to hate it. Due to the model's unpopularity, the Porters were later converted into prison ships and renamed the Purgatory-class.
