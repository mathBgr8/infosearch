Well of the Dead
1035

The Well of the Dead was located on Kashyyyk in the Shadowlands. It was connected to Rwookrrorro by the Rryatt Trail. Young Wookiees would go there to harvest syren plants or hunt katarns for their initiation rites. *A Forest Apart *Tyrant's Test *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Kashyyyk locations
