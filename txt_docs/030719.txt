2-124
1745

2-124 was an Uulshos justice droid programmed as an Imperial assassin. Captured in a Rebel Alliance operation, 2-124 was probed to locate any useful intelligence he might contain. Before deep analysis of his memory banks could begin, the droid killed dozens of Alliance soldiers and escaped. 2-124 vanished from Alliance Intelligence's view, only surfacing on a few occasions to kill select Alliance leaders. As the Alliance transitioned into the New Republic, General Airen Cracken included 2-124 in his confidential New Republic Intelligence brief that listed top fifty most dangerous beings to the New Republic, assuming that the droid's programming was sophisticated enough for it to understand the governmental shift. *Wanted by Cracken Category:Assassin droids Category:Droids of the Galactic Empire Category:Droids with masculine programming
