thumb|right|250px|A tribute image by Dusty Abell and Jim Royal dedicated to Edvin and included in the comic Chewbacca 4 Edvin Biuković (June 22, 1969 &ndash; December 5, 1999) was the penciller of Dark Horse Comics' Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron: The Phantom Affair and the Star Wars: The Last Command comic adaptation. He died in 1999 from complications relating to a brain tumor, which had been diagnosed a few weeks prior to his death. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 6 *Star Wars Handbook 1: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Category:Comic book inkers Category:Comic book pencillers
