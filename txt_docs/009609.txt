Karreio
1407

Karreio was the daughter of an important Imperial diplomat and the fiancée of Crix Madine. She was a firm believer in the New Order. Madine defected to the Rebel Alliance without telling Karreio, fearing that any knowledge she possessed could put her life in danger.
