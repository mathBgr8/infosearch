Two Jedi Men From Baronis
997

Two Jedi Men From Baronis was the followup Jediboh Opera play to Master Thespian Damien Tantrellius' first play which brought him fame on Bespin, Jedi Men From Baronis. Though this had the same general story, it differed in that it starred Tantrellius' daughter, Plum Tantrellius, who appeared in the role of Leia. It also featured other members of Tantrellius' troupe, including Yoh Podd as Mouchus, and the great dame Annah Dublavviane as Leia's mother. It was claimed to be unmissable, although it contained some violence. This play's title evokes the real-world William Shakespeare play The Two Gentlemen of Verona.
