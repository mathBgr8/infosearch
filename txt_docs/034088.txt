Challenge 73
973

Challenge 73 was a roleplaying magazine from June of 1993. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The article was called "New Character Templates: A Supplement to Star Wars' Mos Eisley Galaxy Guide" and was written by Martin Wixted. Category:Challenge
