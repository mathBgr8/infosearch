Takital
1734

|side3= |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= }} Takitals were ritual battles conducted by various Amanin tribes on Maridun, organized in areas sacred to the Amanin. Their goal was to create a peace between the tribes. Imperial General Ziering and his army unknowingly became involved in a Takital when they trespassed into such an area.
