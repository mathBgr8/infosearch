|pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Jedi Order *Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Jojighar was a male Jedi Master famed for his service to the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic, alive sometime between the Ruusan Reformation and the Separatist Crisis. He was commemorated by the Biscuit Baron food chain by way of a holocube dispensed with certain food orders. A Force-sensitive, Jojighar was trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. During his lifetime, Master Jojighar served with distinction, remembered after his death by the Biscuit Baron food chain, attempting to promote the Loyalist cause by offering commemorative holocubes of "Heroes of the Republic" with their QuickSnack and QuickSnackLite meals.
