Kroeskin Fabrications
1575

}} Kroeskin Fabrications was the manufacturer of ceramisteel hulls for a variety of vehicles and starships. It was a one of the original contributing sponsors for the Corporate Sector Authority, and was upgraded to voting sponsor. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, R1-G4, an obsolete R1-series astromech droid, bore the old Kroeskin corporate logo on its shell. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Category:Corporate Sector Authority voting sponsors Category:Manufacturing companies
