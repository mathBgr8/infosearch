It was written by Sterling Hershey for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. Like all supplements printed in Polyhedron prior to 2002, the adventure is considered non-canon. "Milk Run" was originally run as an RPGA&trade; Network-sanctioned tournament scenario by the RPGA at the Gen Con '91 convention, two years prior to its publication in Polyhedron.
