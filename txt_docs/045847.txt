Starfight
1800

Starfight was a coin-operated arcade game that consisted of two banks of monitors and controls. Between them was a holotank with star charts. Each player sent their ships to battle one another in virtual space. The holotank was rigged with sensory feedback, so that the players experienced the sensations of acceleration and battle damage.
