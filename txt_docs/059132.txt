Armored rat
1664

The armored rat, also called ratidillo, rat monster or kragget rat, was a large, hard-plated rodent native to the lower and bedrock levels of Coruscant. A row of lethal spikes ran down its back and tail, and two large tusks jutted from its mouth. They scavenged for whatever little food they could find. It had a strong, painful bite and was known to bite unwary beings without provocation. There was a soft spot at the back of its neck, just between the head and body carapace. It may have been related to the borrat.
