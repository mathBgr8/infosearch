427
1381

427 was the designation of a group of three CR90 corvettes that served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. They were part of the task force, led by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Invincible, during Operation Strike Fear where they served as support for the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Warspite.
