Barrckli
1155

The Barrckli were a social group with one member present on the planet Darkknell in 0 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of "Interlude at Darkknell" to 0 BBY. While at the Continuum Void cantina, the Barrckli was in a drunken state and the professional con artist Moranda Savich had to dodge past the individual while exiting the cantina's refresher. A Barrckli appeared in Timothy Zahn's Part I of the short story "Interlude at Darkknell," included in the 1999 Tales from the New Republic anthology. Category:Undefined social groups
