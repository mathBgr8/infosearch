Lucky Day

|navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} Lucky Day was a YT-1300 captured by Imperial forces during the Galactic Civil War. Following information from Imperial Intelligence to the Galactic Electronics Corporation XQ2 Platform Pondut, Imperial forces stripped the facility of all technology, to include the Mag-pulse Torpedo. Imperial starfighters inspected Corellian Transport Lucky Day as it fled the research facility carrying an arms dealer.
