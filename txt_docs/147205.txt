Happy's Landing
736

Happy's Landing was an ale house on Druckenwell. Situated in Il Avali's bad quarter, the establishment was dimly lit and quiet on afternoons, but provided music and spicy foods along with a drink selection including Elba water and TrooperBreath. Tinian I'att and Daye Azur-Jamin frequented this tavern during the Galactic Civil War. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Happy's Landing
