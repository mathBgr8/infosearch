Govian
984

Govians were a sentient species from the Govia system. A female member of this species was a receptionist at one of Dr. Cornelius Evazan's "clinics" who was stunned unconscious by Jodo Kast as he pursued the bounty on Evazan's head. *Suns of Fortune Category:Core Worlds sentient species
