Domain Choka
1053

Domain Choka was a Domain among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong, often noted for its many skilled and cunning warriors. The most famous member of Domain Choka was Nas Choka, Supreme Commander of the Yuuzhan Vong fleet and the final Warmaster of the Yuuzhan Vong.
