Tantive III (Rebel Alliance)
2523

Tantive III was a CR90 corvette used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. When Biggs Darklighter's X-wing broke down while carrying the recently intercepted schematics for the Death Star, the Tantive III, along with the Tantive I and the Tantive II, was sent to escort Lambda-class T-4a shuttles which would restore the X-wing's power generator. It arrived from hyperspace to find Rebel R-22 Spearheads fighting Imperial Assault Gunboats which were attacking the X-wing.
