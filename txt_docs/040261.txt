In the year 15 BBY, dates the events of Star Wars: Droids: The Adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO to fifteen years before . As The New Essential Chronology dates the events of A New Hope, including the Battle of Yavin, to the year 0 BBY, the events of "The Lost Prince" can therefore be dated to 15 BBY. it was requested over the tent's public address system that it report to the emergency sick bay. 318 was first mentioned in "The Lost Prince," the fifth episode of the Star Wars Legends animated series Star Wars: Droids: The Adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO, which originally aired on October 5, 1985.
