Halifax
1297

Halifax was an Imperial CR90 corvette owned by Overlord Ghorin, that supplied Emperor Palpatine with food. It was usually escorted by 6 BTL Y-wing starfighters. When Ghorin's doublecrossing of the Rebel Alliance was uncovered, Red Squadron aided in the revenge. Halifax was disabled above Plooriod IV and she was boarded by the transport Storm, which stole her cargo while the shuttle groups Typhoon, Thunder, and Lightning captured her escorts.
