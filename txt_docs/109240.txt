|pages= |upc= |timeline=33 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=0 |prev=first |next=Star Wars 1 }} Prelude to Rebellion 0 was an online preview issue to Dark Horse Comics' comic series Star Wars: Republic. It was written by Peet Janes and published exclusively on the Official Star Wars website in Shockwave format. It was taken offline later because the file was broken and could not be restored. It was not reprinted in the Star Wars: Prelude to Rebellion trade paperback, and Dark Horse Comics editor Randy Stradley has said that the comic will never be printed.
