Saber Enterprises
1665

Saber Enterprises appeared to be a legitimate corporation headed by Renik during the Galactic Civil War, but in fact it was a front for much of the Empire's counter-espionage operations. Guri, following orders by Xizor, paid 10,000 credits to a Rebel chief mechanic on the moon of Kile in order to program Wes Janson's R2 droid to shoot Luke Skywalker.
