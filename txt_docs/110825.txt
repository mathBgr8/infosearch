Tiatha stole his brother's ion blaster, a family heirloom, to use as his personal weapon. After four seasons, the blaster earned Tiatha enough wealth to retire. Tiatha was a legendary scavenger of rare quality. Very few of his fellow scavengers could match his talent for turning an old starship's husk into a "veritable palace". Tiatha had yellow eyes. His equipment included a bandolier strapped diagonally across the chest of his robes, above another bandolier worn as a belt.
