|editor= |illustrator= |artist= |performer=Great Municipal Band |publisher= |release date=Before 32 BBY states that Beezar Pert was known for having composed Symponika Crunchen-Grand before Augara Jowil succeeded him as leader of the Great Municipal Band. It also states that she was the leader of the band when Padmé Amidala of the Naboo approached Boss Nass, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 32 BBY. Therefore, Pert must have composed his symponik prior to this year. |media type= |pages= |published in= |genre=Symponik }} "Symponika Crunchen-Grand" was a symponik&mdash;a historical work of music&mdash;that recounted the formation of the Gungan Grand Army. It was composed sometime prior to 32 BBY by the famous Gungan Beezar Pert, conductor of the Great Municipal Band, the group of musikagungs that performed the piece. Pert's apprentice, Augara Jowil, was one of the first to hear the composition performed. "Symponika Crunchen-Grand" was first mentioned in the Databank entry "Augara 'Augie' Jowil," which was written by Hyperspace member Chad Finke on StarWars.com via the website's feature "What's The Story?"
