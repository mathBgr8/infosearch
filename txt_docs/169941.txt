Light Corvette (air cruiser)
1611

The Light Corvette was a vessel utilized by the Rebel Alliance. The Light Corvette was also known generically as an "air cruiser". Its weaponry consisted of a single main cannon, most effective against landbased targets such as fortifications, infantry, and mechanized units. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds thumb|left|200px|Detail view.
