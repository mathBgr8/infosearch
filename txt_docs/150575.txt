An inhospitable world, Frewwil was explored by the Republic Survey Corps scout Barosa Warren. More than fifty years later, by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Frewwil was controlled by the Galactic Empire and was considered to be one of the most dangerous planets under the Empire's jurisdiction. Frewwil was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
