Vagnerian canapé
1797

Vagnerian canapés were sweet, tasty pastries that were often served at formal dinners. They were occasionally served in the New Republic Senate chambers and were a favorite of Leia Organa Solo. In The New Rebellion, Vagnerian is lower-cased.
