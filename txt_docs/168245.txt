F-s3.2 flight avionics system
876

The F-s3.2 flight avionics system was the primary flight control system for both the TIE/LN starfighter and the TIE/sa bomber. The system was developed and manufactured by Sienar Fleet Systems, who also built the starfighters themselves. *Star Wars Screen Entertainment Category:Flight control systems Category:Sienar Fleet Systems products
