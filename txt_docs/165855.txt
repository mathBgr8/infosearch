Honest Gjon Starship Service
474

Honest Gjon Starship Service was a landing pad and ship repair facility located on Bogg 4. The facility was run by "Honest" Gjon, a scam artist. Gjon would overcharge for repairs, then steal the ship and fly it to another moon of Bogden, where he would take it apart and use it for spare parts. *Boba Fett: The Fight to Survive Category:Planetary shipyards
