Salishh Tilloman
1025

Salishh Tilloman was a Devaronian con-artist during the Galactic Civil War. Tilloman grew up as an orphan on Devaron, but developed expensive tastes. In his adolescence, he left his homeworld and became a drifter. He scammed people on many different worlds and eventually took on several apprentices. *Heroes & Rogues Category:Criminals Category:Devaronians Category:Males
