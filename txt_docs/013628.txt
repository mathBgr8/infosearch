Site of the famous University of Charmath, Alliance historian Arhul Hextrophon searched through old, classified data files at the University in order to find the hidden location of Yoda for his work, Official History of the Rebellion, Volume One. After meeting with Yoda on Dagobah, Hextrophon wiped out all records of Yoda's existence from the University's computer in order to preserve his anonymity and keep the hope for the Alliance alive.
