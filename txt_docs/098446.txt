Tolmeatek
846

Tolmeatek was the highest mountain in the Tolmea mountain range on Ord Cestus. "Tolmeatek" meant "untravelable" in X'Tingian. Its peak was covered in snow and it was around 32 kilometers tall. The first people to reach the summit without oxygen supplies did so around 120 BBY. *The Cestus Deception *The Cestus Deception abridged audiobook Category:Mountains of Ord Cestus
