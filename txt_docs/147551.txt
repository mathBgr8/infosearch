Zoochberry dumpling
2271

Zoochberry dumplings were a type of food that was made from zoochberries, a type of fruit,The Glove of Darth Vader and was edible for members of the Hutt species. They were served by the Prophets of the Dark Side, a dark side cult, at a banquet held to celebrate a visit from the Hutt crime lord Zorba Desilijic Tiure in 5 ABY. establishes that the events of Prophets of the Dark Side, including the appearance of zoochberry dumplings, take place five years after the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which corresponds to 5 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. While eating the dumplings at the banquet, Zorba spilled some on the robes of the High Prophet Jedgar, which left stains on his clothes.
