Stockade
1968

The Stockade was a Trade Federation Lucrehulk-class battleship. The Feeorin pirate Nym was held prisoner aboard the Stockade after being captured by Vana Sage. During the Clone Wars, it was upgraded with additional weapons systems for service in the Navy of the Confederacy.
