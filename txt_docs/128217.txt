R2-R-series astromech droid
1353

The R2-R-series astromech droid was an astromech droid model produced by Industrial Automaton. It was used primarily with X-wing and Y-wing fighters that were on reconnaissance missions. The R2-R could also download recon data. An R2 unit cost anywhere from 4,000 to 10,000 credits based on the R2 unit. *Shield of Lies *Threats of the Galaxy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Sub-R2-R
