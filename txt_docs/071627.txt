A-77
2161

A-77 was a CIS assassin droid under the command of Asajj Ventress, while trying to hunt down Grand Master Yoda. Yoda had a rendezvous with Count Dooku on Vjun, but he preferred traveling incognito. When Jedi Masters Jai Maruk and Maks Leem, along with their Padawans, Tallisibeth Enwandung-Esterhazy and Whie Malreaux, and the unrecognizable Master Yoda arrived at Phindar, Asajj Ventress and her droid battalion were waiting for them. She had discovered that Yoda used a lure, the actor Palleus Chuff, and Ventress threatened to kill him.
