Galaxy News Service
1606

Galaxy News Service was a media company and one of the original signatory sponsors of the Corporate Sector Authority. It was second only to Imperial HoloVision in popularity among readers. Galaxy's major shareholder was Millennium Entertainments. *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Heroes & Rogues *The Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:News networks and companies
