Civilian Protection Guild
1407

The Civilian Protection Guild was a small group of sentients on Kashyyyk who dedicated their time to the protection of the citizens of that planet. The Civilian Protection Guild appeared in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees *Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Guide: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Organizations of Kashyyyk
