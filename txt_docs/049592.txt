thumb|right|300px|Photo ©1995 Anthony Taylor Dave Cockrum was a comic-book artist who worked for both Marvel and DC Comics. Cockrum's first work in the comic industry after leaving the United States Navy was for Warren Publishing, and he was later hired by DC Comics to draw Legion of Superheroes stories.
