Kayala Ravein
1480

Kayala Ravein was a female Human from Alderaan. She was the daughter of an influential politician and lived a pampered life. However, her world was shattered when her mother was killed by a fleeing smuggler. This led her to take the law into her own hands. She became a free-range bounty hunter operating under the name Ra Katana.
