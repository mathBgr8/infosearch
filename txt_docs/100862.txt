Medical Diagnostic Systems, Inc., also known as MeDiS, was a medical technology manufacturer located in the city of Tal'cara on the planet Kothlis. During its heyday, its specialized medical hardware and software made it one of the top three corporations on Kothlis, but the company lost its footing when other operations created more advanced medical droids. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Medical companies Category:Tal'cara locations
