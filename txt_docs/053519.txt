Anoat malted was a beverage from the planet Anoat. Anoat malted was a rich, sweet beverage known for its deep, smoky flavor. It cost roughly one half-credit, and were sometimes used to dunk Gamorrean snack-crackers. *Scavenger Hunt *Fate of the Jedi: Ascension Category:Beverages
