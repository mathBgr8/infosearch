Kalindan System Defense Force
695

The Kalindan System Defense Force was part of the military commanded by the monarchs of the Kalinda system. The Kalindan System Defense Force fought alongside the Kalindan Deep Space Fleet when faced with an Imperial invasion of the Kalinda system. Their attempts to control the system failed, as the Kalindan forces were victorious. Category:Military units
