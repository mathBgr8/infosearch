FX-5 medical assistant droid
1052

The FX-5 medical assistant droid was a medical droid manufactured by Medtech Industries as part of the FX-series of medical assistant droids. Produced during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, it was ultimately succeeded by the FX-6 model. *The New Essential Guide to Droids FX-05
