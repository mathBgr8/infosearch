Charenthoth
2689

Charenthoth was a celestial body located in the Charenthoth system, a part of the Slice portion of the Colonies. During the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, the Battle Chant of the Alliance's Legion of Alderaan military unit mentioned the Rebels fighting the Imperial forces "in the night of Charenthoth," among other locales. Charenthoth was first mentioned in Paul Murphy's 1990 The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, which was published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Charenthoth system, and therefore the celestial body itself, in grid square N-9. *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Astronomical objects in the Colonies Category:Astronomical objects in the Slice
