Ragithian Human
1884

Ragithian Humans were Near-Humans who inhabited the high-gravity world of Ragith III. The descendants of Human colonists, Ragithian Humans were the product of generations of selective breeding and genetic engineering to adapt to the high gravity of Ragith III. Because of this, Ragithians typically dwarfed baseline Humans, standing at two meters tall and weighing a hundred and ten kilos of adapted muscle. In standard gravity, Ragithians possessed impressive strength; a Ragithian was capable of lifting a fully grown Human practically effortlessly with a single hand, and were more than strong enough to lift and flip landspeeders.
