Clear Sector
558

The Clear Sector was the area of the planet Radnor that was not affected by the planet's deadly bioplague. The city-state of Tacto was located in the Clear Sector. The city-state of Aubendo was located in the Isolation Sector, where the bioplague first struck. *Jedi Quest: The Way of the Apprentice Category:Planetary regions and territories Category:Radnor locations
