Grrrwahrr
3107

Grrrwahrr was a male Wookiee hunter living in Rwookrrorro in 3956 BBY. During Revan's search for the Star Map on Kashyyyk, Grrrwahrr's hunting party was ambushed in the Shadowlands by cloaked Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders, and only he survived, due to the timely intervention of Revan and Jolee Bindo, who slew his attackers. Grrrwahrr was healed of his injuries by Revan, who then agreed to slay the remaining Mandalorians and their leader. After Revan killed the leader of the Mandalorians, he presented the helmet of their leader to Grrrwahrr, who in turn awarded Revan with an item. If Canderous Ordo is in the party with Revan, he will say that these Mandalorians who killed Grrrwharr's hunting party were the first to flee when the Mandalorian Wars turned against them and called them "bloody cowards."
