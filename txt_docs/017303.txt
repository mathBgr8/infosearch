It was the outermost planet in the Velm system, which was located in the Halori sector in the Trans-Hydian portion of the Mid Rim. Veladine's surface consisted of ice, and it was orbited by three asteroid moonlets. Veladine was mentioned in the source article Velmor: Royalty and Rebellion, a roleplaying supplement written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards.com on March 6, 2003 for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Velm system, and by extension Veladine, in grid square P-7.
