G'Mai Duris
1866

G'Mai Duris was a female X'Ting Regent of Ord Cestus who lived during the Clone Wars. She was related to the original hive queen, but only tangentially. Around 22 BBY, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Kit Fisto encountered her during their mission to undermine the partnership between Cestus Cybernetics and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. G'Mai Duris was later a victim of several attempts by her cousin Caiza Quill who was able to dethrone her from the position of regent.
