Battle of Antar 4 (Clone Wars)
2740

The Battle of Antar 4 was a skirmish between the Antarian Rangers and the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars. thumb|left|250px|Count Dooku (pictured) had Antar 4 bombarded. Separatist loyalists clashed with the Antarian Rangers, who held order on the planet after the Battle of Antar 4 of the Separatist Crisis.
