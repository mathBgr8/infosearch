Nightshrike
2509

Nightshrikes were an imposing avian predator native to the rainforests of the planet Monastery. Large and bat-like, nightshrikes were nocturnal and mostly preyed on the other beasts of the forests. As such, they were rarely seen during the day&mdash;however, in 0 ABY, Rebel leader Luke Skywalker and Order of the Sacred Circle priestess Domina Tagge faced a rare daytime attack from a nightshrike, with Skywalker slaying the beast with his lightsaber. Nightshrikes first appeared in Star Wars (1977) 35, a Marvel Star Wars comic book issue written by Archie Goodwin and released in 1980. In the comic, the beast was illustrated by Carmine Infantino.
