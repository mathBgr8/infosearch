Algic current
603

The Algic current was an underwater current that ranged from the planet Chadra's equator to its arctic circle. Callista Ming's deep-water ranch followed this current. Tsaelkes migrated with the current annually. *Children of the Jedi *The Essential Guide to Characters *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Waterfalls
