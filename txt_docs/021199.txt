Single-celery soda was a drink enjoyed by Ugors. The chef droid L9-G8 instructed the rebel crew of the Bonderium to prepare this soda, in order to bribe ArrGack and GrrKack for admission to the Paradise system. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Effervescent beverages Category:Ugorian food
