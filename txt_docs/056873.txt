Gamma Squad (Galactic Republic)
1567

Gamma Squad was a clone commando squad of the Grand Army of the Republic. The commandos of Gamma Squad suffered heavy losses at the Battle of Geonosis, with only Stoker surviving. Later reformed, they were dispatched to the planet Cularin where they infiltrated a Separatist base, replacing command codes with those of the Galactic Republic. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Republic commando squads
