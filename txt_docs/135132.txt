In 1 ABY, the Bez'ox Mentzz was traveling through the rich shipping lanes of the Corellian system, transporting atmospheric inhibitors. It was escorted by mercenaries piloting Y-wing starfighters. Just as the crew was preparing to jump into hyperspace, the freighter was intercepted and their escort was shot down by a spacer in a heavily armed ship. The spacer had been hired by the the station master of the Dantooine Space Station to secure the cargo for him. Threatened by the firepower of the spacer's ship, the freighter's crew surrendered and followed him to a rendezvous point where their cargo was transferred. Three Z-95 Headhunters of the Corellian Security Force, possibly alerted by the freighter's crew, tried to intercept the pirate's ship shortly thereafter, but were shot down after a short dogfight. The Bez'ox Mentzz appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The Bez'ox Mentzz was added to the game with the release of the expansion pack Jump to Lightspeed on October 27, 2004. It was the second target of a mission that was available to players when communicating with the Dantooine Space Station.
