Unidentified flying flower species
904

Members of a species of flying flower creatures lived on the Floating Mountain on the Forest Moon of Endor. While at first they could be mistaken for simple plants, the creatures were actually capable of communicating with other beings. They had a somewhat lizard-like build and petal-wings that rotated, allowing them to fly. Category:Sentient species of Endor
