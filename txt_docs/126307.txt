Umolly Darm
1425

Umolly Darm was one of the Newcomers on Nam Chorios. She was one of the people that found Tsils to sell to Loronar Corporation. When she learned of their sentience, she gladly gave up searching for them and joined the Theran Listener Be in search of Rock ivory and other minerals to sell in small quantities offworld.
