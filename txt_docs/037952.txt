|subscribers=Early June 2006 |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue= |series= }} Star Wars Insider 88 is the 88th issue of Star Wars Insider, released in mid-June 2006. This issue centers around the Sith and the dark side of the Force. It came in four different "Special Sith Issue" covers by Aaron McBride and Christian Alzmann, each featuring a different Sith Lord.
