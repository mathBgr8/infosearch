After the Battle of Yavin, he worked for the Corellian Port Authority at the Tyrena Starport, in the city of Tyrena. In 1 ABY, a group of spacers investigated the disappearance of a shuttle transporting a man named Valance Serth while traveling from Corellia to Naboo. The spacers discussed with a woman by the name Selan Ellison, an acquaintance of Amarent Loren, in the Moenia Starport on Naboo and, following her advice, they then went to visit Loren in Tyrena. Amarent Loren was not eager to disclose any information as the Imperial Transit Authority tightly controlled the activities of the Corellian Port Authority but he finally revealed that Valance Serth's shuttle crashed on the planet Talus. 250px|thumb|Every possible depiction of Amarent Loren Amarent Loren was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Valance Serth was added to the game with the release of the "Publish 21," on August 4, 2005.
