Cala Wenn
1076

Cala Wenn was Adar Tallon's servant. Wenn performed household tasks on Tallon's home while he was living on Tatooine with his wife Kay. Morr Wenn worked at the same home. Years later, Adar Tallon decided to leave Tatooine with his wife and droid LN-26.
