Kelliadu
2899

Kelliadu was a terrestrial planet in the Eriadu system, located in the Seswenna sector of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Along with the rest of its star system, the planet was located on the hyperspace routes known as the Hydian Way, the Lipsec Run, the Nothoiin Corridor, the Rimma Trade Route, the Senex Trace, and the Yankirk Route. Kelliadu had no moons and was the sixth planet that orbited the star Eriadu. The world featured an icy surface and a frigid climate.
