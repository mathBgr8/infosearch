Unidentified third moon of Storm
2743

The third moon of the gas giant&ndash;type planet Storm was located in the Naboo system, a part of the Chommell sector in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Mid Rim. Along with the rest of its star system, it was situated along the hyperlane known as the Enarc Run as well as the Old Trade Federation Route. The moon housed a penal colony used by the inhabitants of the planet Naboo. The moon was first mentioned by Secrets of Naboo, a 2001 sourcebook for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game.
