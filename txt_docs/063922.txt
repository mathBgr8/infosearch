Black Sun gladiator droid
1852

The Black Sun gladiator droid was a gargatuan droid model used by Xizor as a sentry droid in his palace. The droid was divided into three parts: lower body, torso, and head. It could not be destroyed easily, as all the sections were capable of operating independently. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire video game *The Secrets of Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire Limited Collector's Edition Category:Black Sun Category:Gladiator droid models
