Ahto City Provisional Company
626

The Ahto City Provisional Company was a Selkath-run vendor based on Ahto City on Manaan. It operated prior to and during the Jedi Civil War, offering supplies to both offworlders and the native Selkath. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Organizations of Manaan Category:Vendors and sellers
