School of Conceptual Design
1414

The School of Conceptual Design was a branch of the Lorrd University. Below it there existed steam tunnels, were once a daughter of a prominent buisiness women had been kidnapped. After the Lorrd Security Force scanned the plans of the tunnels, it became clear they did not have an approach to the child, without getting the girl killed. So, Jedi Knights Nelani Dinn, Jacen Solo and Ben Skywalker were called. Solo noticed one of the steam pipes was sufficiently large for an average-sized adolescent to slither on through. Skywalker crawled down the pipe and dropped into the kidnapped girl’s chamber, defending it, while Solo, Dinn, and the security forces stormed and secured the hideout. The kidnapping was conducted by the Lorrd Logistician Liberation League. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Lorrd locations Category:Schools
