Anti-aircraft flak pod
1311

thumb|right|Anti-aircraft flak pod Anti-aircraft flak pods were a kind of homing explosive flak pod that were used by the Imperial Army of the Galactic Empire as ammunition for AT-AAs. Launching dozens of flak missiles in a scattered pattern to home in on enemy starships, they proved very effective against aerial vehicles such as the T-47 airspeeder, but were nearly useless against ground targets such as infantry, T2-B repulsor tanks, and other units. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: Force Commander *Star Wars: Empire at War: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide *Rebellion Era Campaign Guide Category:Explosive projectiles
