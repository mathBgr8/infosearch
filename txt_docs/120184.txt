Stan Sakai
992

Stan Sakai (born May 25, 1953) is a Japanese-American comic-book writer and artist best known for the long-running series Usagi Yojimbo. Sakai wrote and pencilled the Star Wars Tales story "Urchins," and was the letterer for the Tales story "Spare Parts."
