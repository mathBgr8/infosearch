Nosstrick
1371

Nosstrick was a winged humanoid pirate. Nosstrick worked for Jerresk and attacked several New Republic supply shipments, earning very large bounties, moving him from the Hundred Club to the list of "Most Wanted". Jodo Kast tracked Nosstrick to Fluwhaka and shot him with frinka venom to ensure a live bounty. Dengar also tracked Nosstrick to Fluwhaka, only to discover that Kast had nearly fallen for one of Nosstrick's traps. Initially believing Kast to be Boba Fett, Dengar was very surprised, but only until he realized he wasn't dealing with Fett.
