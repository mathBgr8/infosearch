It was home to large trees. During the early years of the Galactic Civil War, which began in 2 BBY,The New Essential Chronology Hintivan II was controlled by the Galactic Empire. An Imperial-sanctioned company that utilized enslaved labor carried out logging operations there, handing out early-model suits of Imperial snowtrooper armor to its workers to protect them from the low temperatures and hazardous conditions of their work. At some point by 2 BBY,Alliance Intelligence Reports, which establishes that Talas Piran bought his freedom from enslavement at least two years prior to its setting and became enslaved at least two years prior to that, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. enslavers sold the Locan Talas Piran from the Wild Space &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Loce system celestial body Loce to the company on Hintivan II. There, Piran and his work gang were forced to cut down trees utilizing only vibro-axes. After long years of work, at one point by 0 ABY, the Locan managed to buy his freedom and ultimately became an off-world enslaver himself. Hintivan II was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
