C9-T9-GE3
1624

C9-T9-GE3 was a GE3-series protocol droid that was modified to become a swoop racing droid. Due to the installed protocols, it was unbeatable by any other racer. After racing and losing, Meetra Surik studied the mechanics of the droid and modified its protocols, making the droid think it was losing and causing a self-destruction. In this manner Surik was able to beat the best time and to retrieve the prize.
