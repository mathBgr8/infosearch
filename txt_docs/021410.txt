It was released September 10, 1997. Emperor Palpatine has a new weapon&mdash;one that can annihilate a planet. But he's not targeting a planet&mdash;he's targeting the future of the Jedi, Leia's children. It looks like a hopeless situation for Luke Skywalker, who has the critical task of protecting the children. But it's not the first time Luke has found hope where none existed.
