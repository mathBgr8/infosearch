Delat Personal Electronics
541

Delat Personal Electronics was a consumer goods manufacturing corporation. The company specialized in datapads and holoprojectors. It was headquartered on Elrood in the Elrood sector and had offices located in the Elrood Bazaar. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Technology companies
