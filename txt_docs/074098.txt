Freedom Warriors
1820

The Freedom Warriors were an elite group of specially trained soldiers who aided the Jedi Knights of the Old Republic during resource-intensive operationsRules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook and dealt with responsibilities too politically inconvenient for the Republic's government to directly address. Membership was limited to non-Force-sensitives, though despite this limitation, they were able to take on Sith-tainted soldiers and sithspawned monsters. By the era leading up to the Great Sith War, the Freedom Warriors had been in existence for at least three generations. A typical Freedom Warrior wore flex-armor and carried a modified pulse-wave blaster pistol and a PTP link communications device. *Tales of the Jedi Companion *Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook Category:Galactic Republic Special Forces units
