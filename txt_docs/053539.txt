Ammonia bomb
1518

Typically used by the Gand, ammonia bombs were explosives that, when thrown or detonated,The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology distributed a large amount of ammonia into the atmosphere of oxygen-rich worlds. They were thus lethal to most oxygen-breathers and many species would not utilize these weapons. However, since many Gand races were ammonia-breathers, Gand bounty hunters would use ammonia bombs if necessary. Zuckuss, one such Gand bounty hunter, was notable for his usage of ammonia bombs, which he would often hide up his sleeves. *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gas demolitions
