Beach trooper
3194

Beach Troopers were a specialized branch of stormtroopers who wore a stripped down variant of the usual stormtrooper armor, which included the regular helmet and a swimsuit. Beach troopers were often found lounging in baths and spas, seemingly off-duty. If an intruder or enemy were to come upon them, however, they would jump into action and engage the opposition.
