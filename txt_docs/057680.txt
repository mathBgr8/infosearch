He was the son of Lumat and Zephee and the brother of Latara and Nippet. Wiley was named after a legendary hunter in Ewok mythology.Galactic Campaign Guide Wiley was one of six 8-inch plush woklings made by Kenner based upon the six woklings seen in Return of the Jedi.
