|pages=12 |timeline=8 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |campaign=The DarkStryder Campaign |preceded by=Omens |followed by=Death Is Remotely Possible }} "Artifact of Aaris" is an adventure in The DarkStryder Campaign. It is set in 8 ABY. The crew of the FarStar receive a distress call from Imperial science team MS-133 on the planet Aaris III. Arriving at the planet, they rescue the last two surviving members of the team, who claim to have been attacked by hostiles. An investigation turns up no hostiles, just the dead bodies of the science team. The crew then begin to feel paranoid and hostile towards each other, and the effect is traced back to an artifact, the Plaque of Victory, that the science team found while excavating ruins.
