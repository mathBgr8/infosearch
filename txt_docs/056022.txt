DuroDelve Industries
791

DuroDelve Industries was a Duros mining company. They bought the rights to the majority of Glydus Mining Corporation's equipment and facilities after that company went bankrupt before the Clone Wars. DuroDelve was later nationalized by the Galactic Empire, and they lost control over most of their property, with the exception of the Glydus Mining Platform.
