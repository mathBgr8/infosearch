JU-9 Juggernaut War Droid
601

JU-9 Juggernaut War Droid were types of Juggernaut war droids used by Governor Vrox. One such droid was used to attempt to slow down the forces of the Republican Army so that Vrox could escape the Battle of Mandalore. Category:Battle droid models
