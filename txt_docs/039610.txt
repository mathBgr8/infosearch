Galaxy of Fear: The Doomsday Ship
7345

|series=Star Wars: Galaxy of Fear |preceded by=Spore |followed by=Clones }} Galaxy of Fear: The Doomsday Ship is the tenth book in the Star Wars: Galaxy of Fear series of young reader novels. Bug swarms, space slugs, brains on legs, mad Imperial scientists—Zak has had enough! He's glad to be on board the luxury space yacht, Star of Empire, where at last, there's peace and quiet. Until—"Abandon ship! Critical meltdown!" As a blaring siren sounds, panic-stricken passengers rush to get off the ship. Everyone evacuates&hellip;except Zak and Tash. But to their relief, nothing happens. There's no meltdown, no explosion. Everything is fine. Except that the ship's exits are sealed and all communications have been shut down. Zak and Tash are trapped. And they are not alone. Mammon Hoole decides that the best way to get to Dantooine would be through the luxury cruise ship Star of Empire. Whilst on board they meet Dash Rendar and Zak meets a computer called SIM. An emergency warning sounds and everyone escapes the ship except Zak, Tash, Dash Rendar, Captain Hajj and some of his crew. The alarms then stop but there is no explosion. The Captain and all his crewmen get killed during the investigation. SIM makes Zak think it is Malik, the person who made him, whereas it is SIM, programmed to subvert, and survivors manage to escape the ship after thwarting the program.
