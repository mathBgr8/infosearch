|death=July 27, 2006San Francisco, California |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Johnny Weissmuller Jr. (September 23, 1940 &ndash; July 27, 2006) was an American actor credited with portraying Card Player #2 in the 1985 made-for-TV film Ewoks: The Battle for Endor. Weissmuller also worked with Lucas when he played a Chrome Robot in the 1971 film THX 1138 and was credited as "Badass #1" in the 1973 film American Graffiti. His father, Johnny Weissmuller Sr., is best known for his portrayal of Tarzan in twelve films made between 1932 and 1948. The senior Weissmuller's performance originated the Tarzan yell, which Chewbacca gave in Return of the Jedi. Category:Deceased actors Category:Ewoks: The Battle for Endor actors
