Unit B-6
838

Unit B-6 was the name of a droid-operated medical center located in the capital city on the planet Tammuz-an. Freighter pilot Jessica Meade was once brought here after suffering several cracked ribs following an altercation with the space pirate, Captain Gir Kybo Ren-Cha.
