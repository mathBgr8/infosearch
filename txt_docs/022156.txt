Havidald Guffin
2821

|masters= |apprentices= }} Havidald Guffin was the principal of the public subadult school PSAS-5128, located in the Jrade District of the Core World planet Coruscant, in 22 BBY. takes place on the date 13:4:25, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. That year, the Jrade School Board discovered several students in the school who possessed death sticks. Guffin, along with the rest of PSAS-5128, resolved to get to the bottom of the matter. Guffin's statement was included in an issue of the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News. Havidald Guffin appeared in an article in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #53, an April 25, 2002 issue of the in-universe HoloNet News website, which was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens.
