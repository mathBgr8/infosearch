Vog Salvage, Inc.
688

Vog Salvage, Inc. was a small salvaging company that was owned and operated by Belinda Vog. The company reached the height of its success during the rule of the Empire. Vog Salvage was one of the many salvaging companies that tried to reap a profit by sifting through the destroyed remains of Alderaan *Graveyard of Alderaan Category:Salvage companies
