Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 2
2762

Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 2 is the second issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in Winter 1995. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 2, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with the comic book, a prototype Topps Star Wars MasterVisions card, and a Topps Finest chromium trading card.
