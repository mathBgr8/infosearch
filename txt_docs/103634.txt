Vine-coffee
1381

Vine-coffee was a variety of coffee that was notoriously hard to grow. Emperor Palpatine owned farms on various planets that provided the beans to produce high quality coffee and vine-coffee, solely for the use of his Court. On Belsavis, the vine-coffee plant was grown on adjustable platforms built under the artificial dome of a volcanic rift valley - a plan conceived by Jedi Master Plett. Like regular coffee, it could be prepared and dispensed from a coffee machine, and was usually served in a coffee cup. It also had a much richer and stronger taste compared to a high quality coffee.
