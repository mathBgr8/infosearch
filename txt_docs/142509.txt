Fun World Founder's Day
2449

Fun World Founder's Day was a holiday on the amusement park&ndash;space station Hologram Fun World. Government offices closed early in observance of the holiday. In 5 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Queen of the Empire to 5 ABY. the Humans Leia Organa and Han Solo tried to get married on Hologram Fun World. When their host, Lando Calrissian, informed the couple that they needed their birth certificates for the marriage to be legal, Organa and Solo admitted that they did not have the documents with them. Calrissian promised to print up duplicates in the station's Document Bureau, but due to the observance of Fun World Founder's Day, the office was closed and the printing had to wait until the following day. Fun World Founder's Day is mentioned in the 1993 young-adult novel Queen of the Empire, written by Paul Davids and Hollace Davids. *Queen of the Empire Category:Holidays
