Isshaddik performed smuggling raids on behalf of Trader's Luck captain, Garris Shrike, and was killed in 19 BBY during one such raid to the planet Nar Hekka. Isshaddik was a male Wookiee, the mate of Dewlannamapia, and the father of several cubs. When translated from the Wookiee language of Shyriiwook to Galactic Basic Standard, Isshaddik's name could be roughly interpreted as "dark sentinel."Galactic Campaign Guide Around 20 BBY,The Paradise Snare places Isshaddik's exile ten years before the events of the novel, which is approximately dated to 10 BBY by The Essential Reader's Companion. Isshaddik was exiled from his homeworld of Kashyyyk for a crime he had committed. Although she was not required to do so, Dewlannamapia followed Isshaddik into exile, leaving behind her home and their cubs. The two joined the crew of Trader's Luck, a starship crewed with criminals and captained by a Human named Garris Shrike. Isshaddik started participating in Shrike's criminal activities, working for him as a smuggler. Only a year later, however, he was killed during a smuggling run to the planet Nar Hekka in Hutt Space. After his death, Dewlannamapia remained aboard Trader's Luck and befriended a young Corellian boy named Han Solo, who was working for Shrike. Around 10 BBY,The Paradise Snare, which features Dewlannamapia's death, is approximately dated to 10 BBY by The Essential Reader's Companion Dewlannamapia sacrificed her life while helping Solo escape from Shrike and pursue his dreams. Isshaddik was first mentioned in The Paradise Snare, a 1997 novel written by A. C. Crispin. Galactic Campaign Guide, a Star Wars Roleplaying Game supplement published by Wizards of the Coast in August of 2003, included a translation of some common Wookiee name prefixes. Due to the complexity of Shyriiwook, each part of a Wookiee's name is translated separately.
