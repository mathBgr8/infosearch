Fel's Day

Fel's Day was a holiday observed every year on the planet Brentaal IV, to mark the efforts of Baron Soontir Fel in defending the planet from attack by the New Republic. This holiday was first commemorated by Lon Isoto. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 23 Category:Brentaal IV holidays
