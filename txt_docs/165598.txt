Jetboarding
846

Jetboarding was a leisure activity practiced on Jerrilek. Jetboarding first appeared in "Loyalties," a roleplaying adventure written by James L. Cambias for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game and published in the November 1994 fourth issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine. Category:Sports
