Sommen
1333

Sommen was an agent who worked for the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. As a part of an infiltration mission, Sommen worked as a medical engineer for Neuro-Saav Corporation on Tauber. He was design-station neighbor to Dr. Cinto Alaras.
