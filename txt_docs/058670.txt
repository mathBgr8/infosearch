Yaawl
1029

Yaawl was a Wookiee and Imperial fugitive who was once found on the planet Hoth. A Corellian smuggler with a YT-1300 freighter took him from Hoth, despite the fact that it meant the Galactic Empire was on their back more. The smuggler paid Yaawl 50 credits per day to help him with his duties. Category:Males Category:Wookiees
