Keeper of the Tower
984

thumb|250px|The Keeper of the Tower The Keeper of the Tower was a title bestowed upon a selected royal official of the palace on Tammuz-an. Their responsibility was to act as guardian to the Tammuz-an royal scepter. One of their functions included presiding over a coronation ritual wherein the heir to the throne of Tammuz-an placed the royal scepter inside a preselected housing within the tower on the first sun of the equinox. The Keeper of the Tower was voiced by Len Carlson in "The New King." *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Titles
