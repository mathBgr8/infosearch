Scavenger hawk
997

Scavenger hawks were winged creatures native to the planet Corellia. These avians had a strong, natural instinct to swoop down from the sky and snatch any object they could carry, be it prey, a loose branch, a fallen comlink, or whatever out-of-place object might catch their attention.
