Caamasi Remnant
2223

The Caamasi Remnant was the term given to an association of individual communities of Caamasi refugees who survived the orbital bombardment of Caamas during the Galactic Civil War. The term "Caamasi Remnant" could also be used to describe the collective remnant communities, although they were only loosely affiliated. Each Remnant community was headed by a Trustant. The largest Remnant community, under Trustant Ylenic It'kla, was established on Alderaan; however it was destroyed along with the planet in 0 BBY. Another was established under Trustant Elegos A'Kla in the city of Morymento on Kerilt. It was later relocated to Susevfi. After A'Kla was murdered during the Yuuzhan Vong War, his daughter, Releqy A'Kla, succeeded him as trustant.
