Star Sea Flower
1848

The Star Sea Flower was the personal YT-1150 cargo transport of the aquatic Priapulin, Charza Kwinn. Using it, Kwinn was able to transport Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker to Zonama Sekot. The interior of the ship was dark and damp; pumps and filters were used to refresh the water. Small wriggling things and larger animals populated the ship and the latter were followed by tiny spotlights, which, along with instruments and thin laser beams, provided some illumination. There were quarters for non-aquatic species, which were used by Kenobi and Skywalker. *Rogue Planet *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:YT-1150 cargo transports
