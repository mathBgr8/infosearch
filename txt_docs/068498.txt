Kuat Explorer
1373

The Kuat Explorer was a Human colony ship. The Explorer was the vessel that colonized the planet of Alsakan in 26,000 BBY. Members of the Hirken family were aboard, making them among the oldest families on Alsakan. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Colony ships Category:Pre-Republic Category:Sleeper ships
