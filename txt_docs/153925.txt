Uvena System Constabulary
681

The Uvena System Constabulary was a government corps on Uvena Prime.Alliance Intelligence Reports Sometime before 11 BBY, the Alpha Premier Korta Sarc suddenly disappeared. The Uvena System Constabulary assigned two Shistavanen scouts, Lak Sivrak and Yurdak Fav, to find Sac. They eventually discovered his murdered body inside the Jenet embassy. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Law enforcement agencies
