Twik'gar
1366

Twik'gar was a Gran thug on Nar Shaddaa who lived on Nar Shaddaa during the Old Sith Wars. In 3951 BBY, he was accompanied by two Trandoshan mercenaries to capture Geredi, who borrowed him a large sum of credits. Twik'gar wanted Meetra Surik to fetch Geredi from the Pazaak Den, but she never did the job. If the player follows dark side story in the game, the player would tell Geredi that either a young female Twi'Lek or his friends were waiting for him outside the Den.
