|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Yellow |skin=Dark blue |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Jabba Desilijic Tiure's criminal empire |masters= |apprentices= }} Vourk ver'Zremp was a male Twi'lek who was on Tatooine during the Galactic Civil War. He worked for Jabba Desilijic Tiure's criminal empire. Vourk ver'Zremp worked in Mos Eisley with Reimos Taligg and Niko Brehe, under the orders of Bib Fortuna. He was a friend of Han Solo and was acquainted with Trehla Keelo. Vourk also acted as intermediary between Jabba the Hutt and the mayor of Mos Eisley, Mikdanyell Guh'rantt. In 1 ABY, a spacer wanted by the Galactic Empire landed in Mos Eisley with Han Solo. Solo advised the spacer to work for Jabba the Hutt and introduced him to Vourk ver'Zremp. Vourk requested the spacer to help Mayor Mikdanyell Guh'rantt, who was indebted to Jabba, with various tasks. He then tasked the spacer to work with Niko Brehe. Vourk ver'Zremp appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Vourk ver'Zremp was added to the game as part of the "Legacy Quest" with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
