Vagabond Trader
972

The Vagabond Trader was an SX troop transport stolen from Dellalt by a group of fugitive Jedi and subsequently docked in the hangar bay of the Drunk Dancer. It was later disguised as a civilian ship and christened the Vagabond Trader in an attempt to fool Kashyyyk Commerce Control and ferry Olee Starstone and her fellow Jedi to Kashyyyk.
