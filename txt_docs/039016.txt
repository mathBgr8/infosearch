The M-Web Repeating Blaster was a heavy repeating blaster produced by BlasTech Industries following the Battle of Endor. It was one of the variations of the E-Web Repeating Blaster. The M-Web, or 'Mobile Weapon Heavy Blaster', was similar to the standard E-Web model, though this version was not a fixed emplacement model. Small repulsorlifts built into the turret and power generator allowed the cannon and tripod to be easily moved around the battlefield. *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:BlasTech Industries products Category:Heavy repeating blasters
