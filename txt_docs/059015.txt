Rage (Imperial-class)
2754

Rage was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer of the Imperial Starfleet. While assigned to Admiral Senn's Task Force Vengeance in the Outer Rim Airam sector, it was the command ship of a small battle group that acted as the home of the elite Avenger Squadron. Paired with Immobilizer 418 cruiser Compellor, the battle group possessed the capability to track down possible Rebel and Airam pirate staging areas and keep the enemy from escaping. During several missions, the enemy targeted the Compellor in order to escape into hyperspace, and it was Avenger Squadron's duty to defend both it and the Rage at all costs.
