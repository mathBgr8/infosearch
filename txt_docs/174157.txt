Kushayan
1928

Kushayan was a non-sentient species of luxury crop plant grown on the planet Qiilura at the start of the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. In 22 BBY, the Neimoidian Separatist Lik Ankkit owned a villa built in Qiilura's capital city of Imbraani which overlooked a Kushayan plantation. During a Republic mission on Qiilura Jedi Padawan Etain Tur-Mukan and clone commando Darman used strips of dried Kushayan, along with other crops, to hide the clones gear while they transported it on a cart to a safe house.
