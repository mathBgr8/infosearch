Appreciated Reminiscences
984

"Appreciated Reminiscences" was the Basic translation of a popular Bith song which became Epoh Trebor's theme song during his tours visiting the clone troopers of the Republic during the Clone Wars. The title of this song is a reference to "Thanks for the Memory," a song which was a trademark of Bob Hope. *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Songs
