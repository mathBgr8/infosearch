Unidentified Bith Jedi (Yinchorri Uprising)
1563

A Bith Jedi was present at the Jedi Temple during the Yinchorri Uprising and played a role in helping the Jedi Council. He was present during the Raid on the Jedi Temple and was one of the Jedi who fought the Yinchorri there. It is unknown what became of him after the resolving of the uprising.
