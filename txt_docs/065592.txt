Jeh Bonegnawer
977

Jeh Bonegnawer was a restaurant in Mos Eisley, Tatooine named after the desert predator of the same name. They offered both live and prepared food, including specialties and imported foods, including a sizeable Corellian menu. Dishes on the menu varied, however, as availability differed. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dining establishments Category:Mos Eisley locations
