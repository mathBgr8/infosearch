It was located in the Rannon system, a part of the Instrop sector in the Slice portion of the Outer Rim Territories. The planet occupied the closest orbit to its star, the yellow-white Rannon. Moll was first mentioned in the 1993 Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game supplement Wanted by Cracken, which was written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games.
