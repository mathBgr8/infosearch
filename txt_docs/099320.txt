Vio
1343

Vio was the flag lieutenant onboard the Ocean in 40 ABY. He operated the Ocean's main comm console during the Blockade of Corellia, where he predicted to Jacen Solo that Jabiim and Rothana would come to the aid of Corellia first. During the Second Battle of Fondor, Vio put Cha Niathal into contact with Shas Vadde after Darth Caedus' bombing of Oridin City.
