Piton (species)
949

The Piton were a species from the planet Piton with pale skin. They were known for being tall and thin, with high foreheads. They also had near-white eyes. Most Pitons lived underground, causing them to have translucent skin and no hair. When traveling off world, they were forced to don eye shields to block any glare from a given planet's sun.
