Miguel A. Fuertes
903

Miguel A. Fuertes is a computer graphics animator and digital artist. He worked on the video games Star Wars: Bounty Hunter and Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II. He was also the lead animator with ILM for Sebulba in The Phantom Menace. Other notable work includes Casper, Jurassic Park, and Happy Feet. He was born in Madrid, Spain on October 30, 1957.
