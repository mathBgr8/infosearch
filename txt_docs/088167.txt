Gorev
1430

Lieutenant Gorev was the gunnery officer of the Super Star Destroyer Lusankya during the Bacta War. A thickset man, he was not afraid to question the orders given by Captain Joak Drysso during the Battle of Thyferra, but Drysso overruled him, and threatened to replace him at his post if he continued to challenge his command. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy officers of Ysanne Isard's Thyferran regime Category:Males
