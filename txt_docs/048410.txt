|end date=June 30, 2004 |schedule= |format= |era= |timeline=0 ABY The date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series= |issues=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Empire: A Little Piece of Home was a 2-issue story arc of Star Wars: Empire published by Dark Horse Comics. It ran from May 26, 2004 to June 30, 2004. Princess Leia of the Rebel Alliance has been visiting planets to find allies in the fight against the Galactic Empire. One visit takes her to the Panteer estate, home of two brothers from Alderaan. She is welcomed warmly by Raal Panteer. The two plan a trek into the jungle. Raal saves Leia's life but in turn loses his when he was bitten by a morp.
