Clandes Industrial
1086

Clandes Industrial was a droid-manufacturing corporation that was located on the world of Ord Cestus, based in the city of Clandes. Its Clandes plant accounted for sixty percent of Ord Cestus' economy, including production of the JK-13 security droids.
