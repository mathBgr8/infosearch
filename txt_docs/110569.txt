Medal of Loyalty
1187

The Medal of Loyalty was an Imperial starfighter pilot medal awarded for successfully hunting down and capturing the traitor Admiral Harkov. The ribbon was primarily blue with a red stripe at the middle. The medal itself was gold colored and wedge-shaped with an opening at the bottom. There were also two lines on each side, and it possessed a blue triangular center with silver linings. Maarek Stele, who received this medal after completing his fifth tour of duty. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:TIE fighter medals
