Homing droid
1208

thumb|right|300px|A [[HMOR homing droid]] }} Homing droids were small, fifth-degree droids used as mobile tracking devices originally developed by spy agencies. Homing droids were primarily vehicles used to plant homing beacons on targets, though some models doubled as a homing device themselves. Commonly fitted with repulsorlifts, homing droids were difficult to track, due to their ability to stay mobile and avoid detection.
