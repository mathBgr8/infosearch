R5-L4
1697

R5-L4, also called Elfour, was an R5-series astromech droid who frequently served on Kyp Durron's X-wing during his time in the Dozen-and-Two Avengers.The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime During the Dozen-and-Two's investigation above Helska IV in 25 ABY, all pilots but Kyp and Miko Reglia, who escaped to the planet's surface, were killed by the Yuuzhan Vong, and R5-L4 was destroyed by a grutchin. Durron was incredibly saddened by the loss of the droid, whom he had come to appreciate as a true friend. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:New Jedi Order members Category:R5-series astromech droids
