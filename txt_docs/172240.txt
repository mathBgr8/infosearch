Stardust (transport)
1526

Stardust was a Muurian transport that carried musicians of the Dunari's Rest when the latter was attacked by Imperial forces. The ship and its crew escaped to hyperspace intact thanks to assistance from the Rebel Alliance. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Muurian transports
