Cobral father
975

The Cobral father was a male Fregan from the planet Frego. He was the husband of Zanita Cobral and father of three sons, Solan, Bard, and Rutin, He died circa 44 BBY of natural causes. The Cobral father is never canonically named, only being referred to as "Rutin's father." *Jedi Apprentice: The Only Witness Category:Cobral family Category:Fregans Category:Males
