Grand Duke

Grand Duke was a title of nobility on Eiattu 6. Gror Pernon, a leading member of the Priamsta, held the title as of 4 ABY. During the New Republic era, Pernon encouraged his grand-niece, Plourr Ilo to return to Eiattu 6 with the Rogue Squadron to take back the throne from the impostor Prince Harran. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 13 *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 14 Category:Titles of nobility
