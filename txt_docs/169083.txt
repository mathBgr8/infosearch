Request For Privacy
577

A Request For Privacy was a document that the New Republic required scouts or other claimants to file in cases where information about a planet or system was to remain unavailable to the public. This usually only occurred when a military research base or similarly sensitive facility was to be built on the planet. *Galaxy Guide 8: Scouts Category:Legal documents
