Dasha Fanron
1266

**Combined Defense Forces of the New Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Lieutenant Commander Dasha Fanron was an officer serving with the New Republic Defense Force who had been given jurisdiction over the Ison Corridor following the Battle of Endor. Fanron was responsible for arresting Slar-dan ti Gardi after Gardi attempted to bribe the officer.
