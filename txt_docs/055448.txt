Brachno-jag
792

Brachno-jags were small carnivores, a hundred of which could strip the flesh off a Human in five or six hours. They were used to perform very painful torture and executions, and were mostly used on backwater and outer-rim worlds. *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Carnivorous creatures Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Species of undefined sentience
