Skrilling (language)
638

Skrilling was the native language of the Skrilling species from Agriworld-2079. Most Skrillings were fluent in both Skrilling language and Basic, although they spoke Basic with a nasal quality due to their breathing tubes, which gave them a whining accent. They were only literate in their own language. *Alien Anthology *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
