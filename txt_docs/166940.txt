Aeron
1270

Aeron was a young male Human who was the son of Ylesian slaves during the Galactic Civil War. He was rescued by Han Solo and Chewbacca after the Rebel raid on Ylesia. He and three other children helped Han load and tie spice that had to be jettisoned in order to evade arrest. Aeron was dropped off at a Corellian spaceport after Imperials escorted the Millennium Falcon to Corellia. *Rebel Dawn Category:Humans Category:Males
