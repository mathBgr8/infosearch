Liz Holliday
1063

Liz Holliday is a British editor and writer of science fiction and mystery, who was the author of the The Official Star Wars Fact File, published 2002 through 2005. She also edited the magazines Odyssey and 3SF, and was fiction editor for Valkyrie magazine for its first thirteen issues. She has written novelizations of British television programs, including Cracker and Soldier Soldier. Holliday's short stories have appeared in numerous anthologies and magazines, including Dragon. Her story "And She Laughed" was adapted for television as an episode of The Hunger in 1999. Category:Reference work authors
