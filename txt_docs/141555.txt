Buccaneer
468

Buccaneer was a rank in the Phosphura Belt Pirates' system of ranks. Buccaneers were second-in-command to the Pirate King or Queen and would often be in direct command of the pirates. They also served as assistants and advisors to the King or Queen. Category:Belt pirates Category:Titles
