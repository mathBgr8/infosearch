Hypermatter reactor
2595

thumb|right|250px|A [[III-a1a primary hypermatter-annihilation reactor|III-a1a model hypermatter reactor.]] A hypermatter reactor, also known as a hypermatter power plant, hypermatter annihilator or hypermatter-annihilator reactor, was a type of starship and battle station fusion reactor that generated power by destroying hypermatter.Star Wars: The Force Awakens: Incredible Cross-Sections The cores of such reactors were usually insulated with exfoliating doonium and dolovite. The hypermatter reactor of the Death Star was an elaboration of the ones equipped on Star Destroyers and other Imperial vessels.
