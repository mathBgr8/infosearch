Enforcer (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
1212

The Enforcer was an Imperial Nebulon-B frigate during the Galactic Civil War. The ship was sent to protect an Imperial commercial staging area near Bonadan but arrived too late to prevent the destruction of valuable supply containers and freighters by two Rebel Y-wings.
