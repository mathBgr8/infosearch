Thunder (CR90 corvette)
1801

The Thunder was an Airam CR90 corvette active during the Galactic Civil War. During the Battle of Nocto, the Airam sent the Corvette, fully-loaded with Proton bombs, to ram the Star Dreadnought Vengeance bridge.
