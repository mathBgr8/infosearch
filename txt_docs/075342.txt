Directive 59826, also known as Imperial directive 59826The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia was an edict invoked by the Imperial Remnant prior to the Battle of Ithor. It stated that if Gilad Pellaeon, who issued the edict, were to be removed from command of the joint taskforce of both the Imperial Remnant and New Republic around Ithor, then all Imperial ships were to immediately return to Bastion.
