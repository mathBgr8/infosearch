Bothan Spynet
3156

}} The Bothan SpynetStar Wars: The Rebel Files was a well-organized intelligence network that was active during the time of the Galactic Civil War. A Kupohan named Sakhet occasionally did contract work for the Bothan spynet, running a small noodle hut as a front of her intelligence services.
