Rak'k
1349

Rak'k was a Barabel living on Korriban around 40 ABY. A member of the ground crew at Korriban's spaceport, he agreed to tell Alema Rar the location of the One Sith, over the objections of his companions, Yas'tua and a Human, in return for Alema's ship in the event she didn't return. Alema's Force powers, however, caused him and his companions to forget about the bargain shortly after she left. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Barabel Category:Males
