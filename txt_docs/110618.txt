The Rancor's Tooth was a Corellian Engineering Corporation CR90 corvette that was commissioned by Orax Tazaene. During the Galactic Civil War between the Alliance to Restore the Republic and the Galactic Empire, the corvette transferred into service as a privateer ship in the Alliance Fleet.
