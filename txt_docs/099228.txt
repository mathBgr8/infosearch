At least one vessel of this class was employed by the Mandalorians during the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|200px|A Keldabe-class battleship under attack by a flight of [[A/SF-01 B-wing starfighter/Legends|B-wing starfighters.]] Like the Crusader-class corvette, the Keldabe-class battleship was a Mandal Hypernautics design. The ship was named after Mandalore's capital, Keldabe. Like other Mandalorian starships, the battleship design was marked by sharp edges hearkening back to the ancient Mandalorian Kandosii-type dreadnaught. Another significant aspect of the design was the tower atop the main superstructure, which was similar in design to the Galactic Empire's Star Destroyers. thumb|right|150px|Keldabe-class battleships within a Zann Consortium fleet. Keldabes were able to compete with the Galactic Empire's Imperial-class Star Destroyers, boasting an armament of turbolasers, ion cannons, and a group of mass driver weapons. The mass drivers were located in batteries between the two engine systems and at the front of the ship. Overall, the battleship was approximately a third more powerful than the Consortium's Aggressor-class destroyer, but this also led each battleship to be much more expensive. Through technology acquired from the Alliance to Restore the Republic via the black market, a capability of the starship's design was the ability to leech energy from enemy vessels via its shields in order to supply power for its own weapon systems. thumb|right|150px|Rear view of a Keldabe-class [[Battleship/Legends|battleship.]] The Zann Consortium obtained the Keldabe-class battleship after its pirates extorted plans from Mandal Hypernautics. Tyber Zann, although annoyed by the pirate's disobeying of his orders to leave the shipyards alone, nonetheless added the Keldabe-class to his fleet, after executing the offending ship captain.
