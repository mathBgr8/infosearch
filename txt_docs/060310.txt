Orbital long range scanner
1696

The Orbital long range scanner (ORL Scanner) was a scanner made by Loronar Corporation. Used by both the Alliance to Restore the Republic and the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War, it detected ships in hyperspace over many light years' distance, providing advance warning of approaching enemy fleets. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: Empire at War: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Emplacement sensors Category:Loronar Corporation products
