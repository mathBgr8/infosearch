Spinnerfish
1112

Spinnerfish was a Galactic Alliance armed shuttle and part of the Galactic Alliance Second Fleet patrolling the Corellian Exclusion Zone. Officers from the Spinnerfish boarded the Love Commander in an attempt to search the vessel. Leia Organa Solo in disguise then tricked lieutenant officer to give the Intelligence access codes to enter the Corellian system. *Legacy of the Force: Exile *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shuttles Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
