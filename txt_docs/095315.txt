Gorum
853

The Gorum were a species of humanoids with prehensile tails. They used these tails to hang from trees when they went to sleep. They had large red eyes and forked tongues which could suck fluids from food. Tormey was a member of this species. *Jedi's Honor *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
