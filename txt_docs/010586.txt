Keira Wingate
1990

}} Keira Jane Wingate is an Australian former child actress who played Ryoo Naberrie in Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. She also filmed a scene as Ryoo Naberrie in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, though it was eventually cut from the final release. It can be found in the "Deleted Scenes" section of the Attack of the Clones DVDs. Wingate also appeared in the 2001 film Lantana as Hannah Daniels, and in the 2004 TV movie The Mystery of Natalie Wood as a very young Natalie Wood.
