|published in=Star Wars Tales 20 |reprinted in= |pages=1 |timeline=22 BBY (13:5 GrS) |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Nobody's Perfect" was a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 20. Before their wedding. Anakin tells Padmé some of the other sins he has done besides killing a Tusken Raider tribe. Those sins include: Killing a colony of Desert Gnomes for giving him bad directions; Beating up a homeless Wookiee for bumping into him; Talking back to Obi-Wan and letting Obi-Wan take the blame for his mistakes; Setting the Jedi Temple on fire, this killing at least six Jedi. After telling Padmé his mistakes he says he would understand if she cancels the wedding.
