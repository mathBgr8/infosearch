Alderaanian Oversea
988

thumb|250px|Tipoca City on Kamino was inspired by the Alderaanian Oversea architecture. Alderaanian Oversea was a form of city construction that originated from Alderaan wherein cities were built on stilts in the shallow saline seas. A manifestation of Alderaanian desire to build their cities as a part of the environment, the concept was used in Tipoca City and many other stilt-cities on Kamino, although their stilts were significantly taller due to the much-deeper oceans on that world. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Architecture Category:Culture of Alderaan
