Anuti
1833

The Anuti was a GR-75 medium transport that was part of the Rebel Alliance. It saw service in the Airam sector. When the decision was made to construct a Rebel shipyard in Airam space, Anuti and its sister-ship Anuto were part of a convoy sent with the needed supplies and personnel. Anuti itself bore the construction workers who would assemble the shipyard.
