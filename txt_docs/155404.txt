Shasstariss Whisperers Corporation
949

|products=Encryption codes |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Shasstariss Whisperers Corporation was a company that was family-owned and relatively unknown. They were contracted by the Galactic Empire to develop military encryption codes. The encryption codes created by Shasstariss Whisperers Corporation were used to protect the control nodes on Imperial-class Star Destroyers. Category:Communication companies
