Michael W. Royer
1326

Michael W. Royer (born June 28, 1941) is a comic book letterer who lettered a few issues of the Marvel Star Wars series. He often collaborated with Jack Kirby on The Demon, Mister Miracle, New Gods, The Eternals, Captain America & Kamandi, The Last Boy on Earth. *Star Wars (1977) 6 *The Marvel Comics Illustrated Version of Star Wars *Marvel Special Edition Featuring Star Wars 2 *Marvel Special Edition Featuring Star Wars 3 *Marvel Movie Showcase 2 *Classic Star Wars: A New Hope 2 *Classic Star Wars: A New Hope *Classic Star Wars Box Set *Star Wars: A Long Time Ago...
