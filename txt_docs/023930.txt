Morymento
1063

Morymento was the capital city of the planet Kerilt. It was a Caamasi Remnant community under Trustant Elegos A'Kla, and included a spaceport and a warehouse district. It received shipments of supplies semi-annually, making it a target for pirates. In 11 ABY it was hit by both the Thalassian Slavers and the Invids.
