Transland Day
2032

Transland Day was a day during which presents were gifted and souvenirs were bought.Outbound Flight In 27 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. the smuggler Dubrak Qennto stated that the Corporate Sector was purchasing Dreadnaught-class heavy cruisers as if they were Transland Day souvenirs. In 0 ABY,The events of Choices of One take place in 0 ABY according to The Essential Reader's Companion. the Hand of Judgment, a group of five former Galactic Empire stormtroopers acquired a Troukree knife from the Troukree Vaantaar on the planet Poln Major. The Hand of Judgment's leader, Daric LaRone, then gave Korlo Brightwater the knife as an early Transland Day gift. Transland Day was first mentioned in the 2006 novel Outbound Flight, which was written by Timothy Zahn. *Outbound Flight *Choices of One Category:Holidays
