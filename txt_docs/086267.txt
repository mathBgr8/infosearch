Derra'makin
1375

Derra'makin was the Grand Daern of the Marasan people. He headed the Marasan government, called the Mindnet, making decisions based on information from the lesser Marasan elected officials (the Electnodes.) Like almost all Marasans, he was a cyborg, with a sophisticated computer implant used for communication and rapid computation. When the Galactic Empire conquered the Marasan people on Marasai and its colonies in the Marasa Nebula, Derra'makin was forced to flee.
