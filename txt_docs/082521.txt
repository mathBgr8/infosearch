Remoh (Squidge)
1879

Remoh was a male Squidge and the den-brother of Otalp who lived long after the death of Luke Skywalker. His species was being oppressed by the Vindar. When Otalp heard his den-mother speak of a vision she had, he set out in search of the storyteller. Remoh, though skeptical of the tale's veracity, followed, believing that even some hope was better than none. After a long journey, they found the storyteller, broken down and inactive, in a cave. After they reactivated him, the droid told them a tale of a hero who led the fight against a dark oppressor and brought hope to the galaxy. When Vindar discovered the cave, Otalp told Remoh to hide and was killed by the Vindar, who also shot C-3PO. In the droid's wreckage, Remoh found a lightsaber, and determined to use it to bring freedom to his people. The names of Otalp and his brother Remoh are ananyms of Plato and Homer respectively.
