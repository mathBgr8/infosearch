Gnullith
2492

thumb|A [[Yuuzhan Vong with a gnullith attached.]] Gnulliths were a branch of biotechnological breathing apparatus worn by the Yuuzhan Vong. Like all Yuuzhan Vong equipment and devices, these were living organisms: in effect, they functioned as an atmospheric filter. thumb|left|A Yuuzhan Vong warrior wearing a gnullith on [[Dibrook.]] The gnullith was a form of biological breathing mask used by the Yuuzhan Vong that allowed the wearer to breathe in any non-corrosive or fluidic environment containing oxygen. Resembling fleshy, starfish-like creatures, the gnullith sealed itself over the mouth of the wearer and inserted a long proboscis down the throat in order to provide the host with a supply of breathable air. While uncomfortable to use, unlike most Yuuzhan Vong biots, gnulliths did not inflict pain on the wearer. Unlike breath masks, gnulliths could be used indefinitely as they did not use air canisters or require replacement filters. The Yuuzhan Vong created a hybrid gnullith-villip hybrid combining the function of both a communication device and breathing apparatus.
