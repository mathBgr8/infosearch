Lhira
1309

Lhira was a Twi'lek female who joined the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. During the Galactic Civil War, she was a member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic loyal to Garm Bel Iblis. She helped Karn in running Iblis' six Rebel cells on the planet Stoga.
