|commander=Clone Captain Tyto |second= |staff= |subunit=2nd Platoon |strength=*4 platoons, 144 clone troopers *Support personnel |vehicles= |headquarters= |location= |battles= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Hawkbat Battalion }} Bacta Company was a company within the Grand Army of the Republic that was active during the Clone Wars. The company was under the command of Clone Captain Tyto. It was one of four companies, alongside K Company, that made up Clone Major Twelve's Hawkbat Battalion. Bacta Company consisted of 144 clone troopers that were organized into four platoons, including 2nd Platoon, along with support personnel. It shared its name with bacta, a healing fluid used to treat wounds. Bacta Company was mentioned in the eighty-fourth issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine in the "Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic" feature, which was written by Karen Traviss and Ryan Kaufman.
