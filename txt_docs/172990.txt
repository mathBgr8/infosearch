Christopher Golden
1169

Christopher Golden is the author of the Shadows of the Empire junior novelization based on the original novel by Steve Perry. Golden also wrote the "Choose Your Own Adventure" adaptations of the films of the original Star Wars trilogy. *Shadows of the Empire junior novelization *Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: A New Hope *Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: The Empire Strikes Back *Choose Your Own Star Wars Adventure: Return of the Jedi Category:Young Reader authors
