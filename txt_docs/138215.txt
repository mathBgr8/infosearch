Cracken's Rebel Operatives (article)
16796

"Cracken's Rebel Operatives" was a series of articles presented in the Star Wars Adventure Journal. Following roughly the same format as the book of the same name, it profiled Rebels under the command of Alliance Intelligence chief Airen Cracken.
