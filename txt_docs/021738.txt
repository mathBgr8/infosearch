The Galactic Republic established a colony on the planet which was wiped out by clodhoppers which arrived in a grain shipment from Naboo. The clodhoppers ate all the vegetation on Corva as well as 87% of the colonists. The name Corva in Secrets of Naboo is ambiguously applied to this world, since the first paragraph in which it is mentioned simply calls it "a failed Republic colony in the Galaan System, Corva sector". The next paragraph reads "[t]he incident at Corva...". As such it is unclear whether the sector or the world (with the same name) is being referred to.
