Balmorran defense force
1048

The Balmorran defense force was the local militia of the planet Balmorra. During the reign of the reborn Emperor Palpatine, the Balmorran militia was made up of SD-series battle droids, X-1 Viper Automadon droid walkers, and sentient soldiers serving as commanders for advancing battledroids.
