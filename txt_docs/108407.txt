RZ-52 Dekard transport
2176

The RZ-52 Dekard transport, otherwise known as the Consortium Light Transport, was a starship used by the Zann Consortium during the Galactic Civil War. It was used to transport infantry and droidekas between planets, as well as board enemy starships. A Dekard transport was used during the Battle for the Eclipse to board the Eclipse so the Consortium could take control of it. *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Troop transport starship classes
