Essence
1461

thumb|250px|[[Laslo Dorits speaking to AndroosinLiann on Essence.]] Essence was a talk show based on Eriadu. Its host was AndroosinLiann, and it was produced by Garox Tronten. The guest on one of its most famous episodes was Jonava Billane, mother of "Baby Ludi." Her particular episode was seen by over 25 billion sentient viewers and gained her much support. During the show she broke into tears four times, gaining even more sympathy galaxywide than before. She thanked all her supporters and also revealed that she had authorized a full-length holo feature based on her story. Other guests who appeared on the show at one point were Laslo Dorits and Gideon Tarkin. It aired on Feed EHN213 at 2100. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:News and entertainment programs
