Explosive Developments
3576

"Explosive Developments" was a short story written by Peter M. Schweighofer with illustrations by John Paul Lona. It was published in the Star Wars Adventure Journal 3 by West End Games in August 1994. Jai Raventhorn attempts to kill Imperial Moff Antoll Jellrek at his estate on Romar.
