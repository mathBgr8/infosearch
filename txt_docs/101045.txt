Millennium Entertainments
1746

}} Millennium Entertainments was a huge entertainment and information combine, one of the largest in the galaxy. Millennium was the largest majority shareholder in Galaxy News Service, and also had interests in other, smaller new companies such as TriNebulon News and CoreDataFiles. They were also Signatory Sponsors of the Corporate Sector Authority and had a division specifically for the production of holos, Millennium Holos, which produced the popular holo-series "Who Are You To Accuse Me?"
