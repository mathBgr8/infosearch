Kruffy pot pie
2063

Kruffy pot pie, also spelled kruffy potpie, was a savory dish edible by Humans. The pie's thick pastry shell was stuffed with fowl meat, vegetables, and gravy. In 40 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Legacy of the Force: Exile, which include the mention of kruffy pot pie, to 40 ABY. the Human Jedi Ben Skywalker ordered kruffy pot pie along with caf when he visited a sidewalk café while on Drewwa, the moon of the planet Almania. After eating his kruffy pot pie, he felt well fed. Kruffy pot pie was mentioned in the 2007 novel Legacy of the Force: Exile, written by Aaron Allston as the fourth installment of the Star Wars: Legacy of the Force series. *Legacy of the Force: Exile *Legacy of the Force: Exile abridged audiobook Category:Savory pies
