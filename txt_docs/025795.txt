United States |death= |listedpronouns=He/him |nationality=American |occupation=*Singer *Voice actor |companies= |website= }} Jeff Glenn Bennett (born 1962) is an American voice actor and singer who provided the voices for a variety of characters in the Star Wars franchise. These include protagonists Brenn Tantor and Kyle Katarn from the video games Star Wars: Force Commander and Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast, respectively, Darth Revan in the video game Star Wars: The Old Republic, and Plumestriker from the animated series LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures, among other roles. thumb|left|220px|Bennett played Kyle Katarn in Jedi Outcast and Jedi Academy. Jeff Glenn Bennett is an American singer and voice actor born in 1962 in Burbank, California, United States. He worked as a singer before turning to voice work, and would go on to receive an Annie Award and a Daytime Emmy Award for his work in the industry. For the 2000 video game Star Wars: Force Commander, his first Star Wars role, Bennett provided of the voice of protagonist Brenn Tantor as well as a refugee ofStar Wars: Force Commander the world Abridon. Bennett subsequently voiced an Imperial officer in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader'Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader as well as the clone troopers, Count Dooku, bounty hunter Jango Fett, and a captain forStar Wars: Jedi Starfighter the March 11, 2002 video game Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter. The March 26, 2002Star Wars Year By Year: A Visual History, New Edition video game Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast saw him play protagonist Kyle Katarn and a stormtrooper officer.Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast He also voiced Fett and the clones inStar Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns the May 15, 2002 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns, itself an expansion pack for 2001's Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds. Bennett reprised his role of Katarn and voiced a saboteur for Jedi Outcast sequel, 2003's Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy.Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy He later played Sith Lord Darth Revan in the 2011 video game Star Wars: The Old Republic. thumb|right|220px|Bennett played Plumestriker, a recurring character from LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures. Bennett lent his voice to the animated series LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures, beginning with "The Lost Treasure of Cloud City," the June 24, 2016 fourth episode of the first season, in which he voiced the Sullustan Nien Nunb. In "Peril on Kashyyyk," the June 27, 2016 fifth episode of the first season, he voiced the Twi'lek Ignacio Wortan and the Imperial Lieutenant Plumestriker. He went on to voice Wortan in "The Embersteel Blade," the August 1, 2017 fourth episode of the second season, and Plumestriker in the August 1, 2016 ninth episode of the first season, "The Kyber Saber Crystal Chase," the August 15, 2016 eleventh episode of the first season, "Showdown on Hoth," the May 4, 2017 short "Beware, the Gamorrean Flu!," the August 7, 2017 seventh episode of the second season, "The Lost Crystals of Qalydon," and the August 9, 2017 ninth episode of the second season, "Flight of the Arrowhead." Bennett also voiced a number of incidental characters in multiple episodes; an Imperial officer in "The Embersteel Blade," the Lurmen Biz in "The Lost Crystals of Qalydon," a rebel officer in "Flight of the Arrowhead," a Rodian junk dealer, their associate Grummgar, and a rebel technician in the May 4, 2017 short "Home One," a rebel pilot in one of the shorts collected in the June 17, 2017 first episode of the second season, "A New Home," and a stormtrooper in "Beware, the Gamorrean Flu!." {| !
