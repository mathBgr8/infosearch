Y'tras
1062

The Y'tras was one of the five races of Shatras. The most common race, Y'tras, composed the 87% of the Shatras population. They were technologically advanced and commonly traveled through spacelanes, having colonies in different worlds. The Y'tras knew of the Hy'tras race living in Klypash. After a vast, centuries-long war that made the Hy'tras reject technology, the Y'tras made a deal with them: Leaving the Hy'tras alone in exchange of their riches.
