Ydde
2010

Ydde was a male Ortolan trader who lived in the Outsider Citadel on Cerea, shortly before the Invasion of Naboo. Ydde was good friends with Ki-Adi-Mundi, often helping Ki track people on the planet. He was caught overhearing the plans of Cerean Elder Dorr-Femi-Bonmi and Ephant Mon to import swoops and other technology to Cerea in exchange for quantities of malium. He tried to escape the citadel, but was executed for his complicity by the Elder's henchmen.Star Wars (1998) 2 Ydde was survived by his extended family on Orto.
