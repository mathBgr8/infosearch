Elegaic Fabrications
1026

Elegaic Fabrications was a furniture manufacturing company with a plant on Coruscant during the Yuuzhan Vong War. They were known for very expensive and functional furnishings, including chairs that converted into beds, chairs that carried their owners in the air, and furniture that massaged those who used them. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand Category:Manufacturing companies
