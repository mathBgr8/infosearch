Jamie Glover
1549

Jamie Glover was the voice of General Maximilian Veers in Star Wars: Battlefront II and in Star Wars: Empire at War. He also did voice work in Knights of the Old Republic II as Azkul, Star Wars: The Force Unleashed II as an Imperial Officer, and Star Wars: The Old Republic as Darth Malgus. His father, Julian Glover, played General Maximilian Veers in The Empire Strikes Back.
