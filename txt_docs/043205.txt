Upari
1319

thumb|right|150px|An Upari crystal Upari was a type of lightsaber crystal primarily encountered scattered in the orbit of forest worlds. It was brittle but versatile, and a Jedi Master could get it to produce many effects. A Wookiee clan on Kashyyyk could cut an Upari stone to use as decorative jewelry.
