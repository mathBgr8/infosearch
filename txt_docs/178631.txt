Vemmer
1210

Vemmer was the designation for a group of six Mobquet medium transports in the service of the Alliance to Restore the Republic in 3 ABY. Vemmer group was active within the Rebel Alliance shortly before the Battle of Endor. During the Alliance's battle with Hurrim pirates, all six ships from Vemmer were captured off-screen by the Hurrim and taken to the Hurrim Base. When the Rebel Alliance and Dunari launched an assault against the Base, Rebel ships from Storm Unit boarded and regained control of all six vessels, allowing them to escape to safety as the Hurrim base was destroyed. The capture of Vemmer and their presence at the Hurrim Base is left totally unexplained in the game, making their appearance seem somewhat random. Even when Vemmer is boarded by Rebel craft, at least two ships fail to fly off in an apparent glitch in the game. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
