Shaclor
1093

Shaclor was an adventurous Selonian assigned to be a digger and explorer for the Chanzari Den. She was tasked to explore the outer reaches of Chanzari land, both above and below ground. The territory included an island chain that once belonged to the shamed clan of Selonians who were exiled to Sacorria in the distant past. She was a skilled climber, swimmer, and survivalist, and unusually curious for a Selonian. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Females Category:Scouts Category:Selonians
