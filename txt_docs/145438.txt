Decisive
1873

Decisive was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer which served in the fleet of the Ciutric Hegemony, when the self-proclaimed "Prince-Admiral" Delak Krennel took control of the region. In the Battle of Ciutric, the Decisive surrendered to the New Republic after Krennel was killed and his flagship Reckoning was destroyed.
