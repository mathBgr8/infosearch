FLO was a WA-7 droid that was used in Dex's Diner on Coruscant. On the planet Stobar, a WA-7 named Marwigo was employed by the Plop Dribble's diner. At least one WA-7 droid worked at the Gilroy Plaza Diner.Thrawn 3 One WA-7 served aboard the Rebel Alliance starship Silver Arrow, which fell under attack from the Scourge during the "scourge of the droids." Like the rest of the ship's droid pool, the WA-7 was infected and became a vessel for the Scourge, which murdered every living being aboard.
