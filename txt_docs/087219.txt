The Ministry of Justice Building was a fortress-like building in Pelek Baw, Haruun Kal. It was headquarters for the Balawai militia and the Ministry of Justice, and included a prison and interrogation center in the basement. *Shatterpoint Category:Buildings Category:Haruun Kal locations
