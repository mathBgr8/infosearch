Wode
3115

The Wodes were an octopedal sentient species native to a planet with a gravitational pull twice that of the galactic standard. When traveling to worlds with lesser gravities, the Wodes took very heavy steps, unaccustomed to their lighter weight. During the Clone Wars, in which the Galactic Republic fought against the Confederacy of Independent Systems from 22 BBY to 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology the Wodes were liberated by the Republic's General Solomahal. At some point between 2 BBY and 1 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Star Wars: The Han Solo Adventures, of which Han Solo's Revenge is part of, to between 1 BBY and 2 BBY. a number of Wodes were present on the planet Bonadan at the same time that the smuggler Han Solo was there investigating an enslavement ring. The Wodes first appeared in Han Solo's Revenge, a 1979 novel written by Brian Daley as the second volume of the Star Wars: The Han Solo Adventures trilogy. "Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star, How I Wonder Where We Are," a 1990 roleplaying game source article published in the thirteenth issue of the Voyages SF magazine, introduced the Mahrusha sector's Wode system, which shared its name with the species. Since that article was released outside of the Lucas Licensing process, it is considered non-canon with respect to the Star Wars Legends continuity. *Han Solo's Revenge Category:Sentient species
