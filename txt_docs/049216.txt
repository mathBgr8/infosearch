Neas Nyl
1303

Neas Nyl was a Human male and the son of Darca and Teril Nyl. When a ship on a mission to return the deadly murderer Lycan to Coruscant crashed on their lands, his father went to investigate the shipwreck and ordered Neas to wait in the house. Lycan found his way to their house when Darca Nyl was still at the crash site and mercilessly killed Neas.
