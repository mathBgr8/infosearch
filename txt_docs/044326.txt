Swamp demon

The swamp demon was a mythological demon on Somov Rit. According to legend, he would swallow anyone who said their real name aloud. This led to the Somovian practice of everyone using nicknames. *Jedi vs. Sith 1 Category:Deities Category:Mythological creatures
