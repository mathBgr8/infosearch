Havoc Squadron
2081

Havoc Squadron was one of a number of the starfighter squadrons that served with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The squadron was commanded by the Twi'lek Warvog Arkon, who was a veteran pilot of the Alliance as of 1 ABY. The unit was known to have many recruiters, among them included Captain Kreezo who was stationed at Tyrena on Corellia, Lady Viopa, who operated out of Nym's Base on Lok, Lieutenant Colonel Aqzow, stationed at Labor Outpost on Yavin 4, as well as Arkon himself, who based the unit out of Nym's Stronghold.
