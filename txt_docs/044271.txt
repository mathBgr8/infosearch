Dbergo tree
1440

Dbergo trees were jungle plants indigenous to Ossus known for their large red fronds. While speaking with Jedi Masters Luke and Mara Jade Skywalker before a meeting with nearly all Jedi on their way from the meditation pavilion to the lecture hall at the Jedi Academy, Jacen Solo glanced at the crown of a dbergo tree while deciding how much information to reveal about a Force vision he had had showing the galaxy die after a devastating war with the Killiks. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Plants of Ossus Category:Trees
