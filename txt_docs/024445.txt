Bachs, starring Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi. The series is composed of 3 issues released in December 2000, and in February and March 2001. When a valuable cargo freighter fails to reach its destination on the planet Coruscant, Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan are sent into deep space to search for the missing ship.
