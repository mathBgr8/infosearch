Mercenary outfitter
1014

thumb|right|200px|A mercenary outfitter. The mercenary outfitter was a name given to small, independent businesses that sprung up throughout the galaxy during the Galactic Civil War in an effort to provide and profit from the sale of the latest military equipment produced by a variety of corporations to both sides of conflict.
