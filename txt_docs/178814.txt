Quad-pulse laser
772

The quad-pulse laser was a weapon fitted to two prototype Delta-7 Aethersprite-class light interceptors by Jedi Master Saesee Tiin. Quad-pulse lasers were top-of-the-line armaments as of 22 BBY, and their high rate of fire compensated for their relative weak power against conventional laser cannons.
