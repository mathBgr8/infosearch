David Tomblin (October 18, 1930 - July 20, 2005) was the first assistant director on Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back and Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. thumb|left|196x196px|Carrie Fisher and David Tomblin He also wrote and directed Warwick Davis' short film Return of the Ewok during production on Return of the Jedi in 1982.
