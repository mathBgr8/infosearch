Mobile Missile Launcher
1754

The Mobile Missile Launcher was a mobile anti-air vehicle used by the Royal Naboo Security Forces. It was in essence an armored speeder fitted with a missile launcher, which was able to spin 360 degrees to target incoming aerial units. The Heavy Mobile Missile Launcher was an upgraded version with two launchers.
