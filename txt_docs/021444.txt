|pages=24 |upc=761568983265 |timeline=32 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series= |issue=1 }} Episode I: Obi-Wan Kenobi is a one-shot adaptation of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace told from the perspective of Obi-Wan Kenobi. The comic was written by Henry Gilroy, illustrated by Martin Egeland, and published by Dark Horse Comics on June 2, 1999. In 2015, Episode I: Obi-Wan Kenobi was digitally republished by Marvel Comics under the Star Wars Legends banner. Though all eyes are on the boy Anakin Skywalker, there is another young man making his rite of passage.
