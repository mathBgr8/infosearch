Sand Crab Nebula
1056

The Sand Crab Nebula was a made-up nebula devised by Han Solo. Jason Fry, co-author of The Essential Atlas, indicated in June 2015 that the Sand Crab Nebula was excluded from The Essential Atlas Online Companion because it was an imaginary location used as an example. *Assault at Selonia Category:Fictional locations
