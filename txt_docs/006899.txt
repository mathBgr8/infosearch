|pages=22 |upc= |timeline=*33 BBYThe New Essential Chronology *88 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Republic - Prelude to Rebellion (4 of 6) & Vow of Justice (1 of 3) |issue=4 }} Star Wars 4 is the fourth issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on March 17, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. Sylvn and the fugitives have taken flight from Cerea, but their starship's cargo hold is fit to burst with a load of unstoppable alien beasts. Action was what the youngsters hoped to find in the space lanes beyond their provincial homeworld, but now they have more than they bargained for, and a trial back home to face the consequences of their actions is beginning to sound downright cozy! After escaping Cerea, Ephant Mon and the Cerean children are quarantined above Tatooine, because of the ravenous Divviks loose on board their ship. Meanwhile, pro-Republic sentiment grows on Cerea in the wake of the investigation. Because of the severity of the situation, Ki-Adi-Mundi is brought out of Bacta healing early and sent to Tatooine to recover the fugitives, with a crew of hastily cobbled together droids.
