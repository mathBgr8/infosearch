Maraba Tev
2517

Captain Maraba Tev was a male Human pilot and spy for the Rebel Alliance. In 3 ABY, Tev accepted a high-risk intelligence-gathering mission in the Patriim system, and uncovered information that the Galactic Empire was developing a new superweapon known as the Tarkin.
