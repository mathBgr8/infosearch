Nut-beetle
713

A nut-beetle was a small insect often eaten as a snack. Its shiny outer shell could be split to reach the soft, juicy meat inside. Nut-beetles were eaten alive, with the inedible portions usually spat out into a wastebasket. Imperial Supervisor Gurdun was known to enjoy nut-beetles. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Beetles Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Food creatures Category:Snack foods Category:Species of undefined sentience
