Guarja Shipyards
1107

The Guarja Shipyards were a shipyard on Nal Hutta. The Shipyards produced Dor'bulla-class warships and a number of knock-off starfighters. They covered approximately 20 square kilometers. A Klatooinian named Odnalor Chull stole ships from Kukul the Hutt, so he put a bounty on his head.
