L-hand 980
1728

thumb|right|250px|Luke Skywalker tests his prosthetic hand. The L-hand 980 was a prosthetic hand model produced by Antilles BioGen (ABG). The model was used by medical droid 2-1B to replace Luke Skywalker's hand after it was lost on Bespin when fighting against his father, Darth Vader. ABG later discontinued the arm that went with the hand, but 2-1B suggested the J2J model prosthetic arm from Organa Organics.
