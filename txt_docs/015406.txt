Dantooine's Second Star
2736

Dantooine's Second Star was a planetoid nestled within the Dantooine system's Ferionic Nebula. Somewhat round in appearance, the iron asteroid was actually designated a planetoid, and was under the control of a little known, small band of thugs called the Velocity Mercenaries. The mercenaries mostly kept to themselves, until the Galactic Empire moved into the area.
