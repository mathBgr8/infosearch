1993 |death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Jett Lucas (born February 17, 1993) is the adopted son of George Lucas. He has cameos in both Attack of the Clones and Revenge of the Sith as a young Jedi Padawan named Zett Jukassa. As a young boy, Jett inspired the name of the Gungan species. Jett is credited as an intern in the video game Star Wars: The Force Unleashed. In the Star Wars: The Clone Wars television series, the character Ion Papanoida is based on him.
