Terno
1749

Terno was a Kitonak musician who lived during the Galactic Civil War. A vital member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic in the Minos Cluster, Terno led a Kitonak band in a traveling circus that was actually a front for the Alliance. Terno was the leader of the Alliance team, although publicly, Terno was merely the director of the band.
