Geonosian executioner cart
2282

The Geonosian executioner cart was a small cart that was pulled along by an animal, commonly used by the Geonosians that took victims to the Petranaki arena for execution. At least one of these carts was used in 22 BBY, during the attempted execution of Senator Padmé Amidala, Jedi Padawan Anakin Skywalker and Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi.
