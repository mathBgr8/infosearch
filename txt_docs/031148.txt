Vyn Narcassan
3291

*Shalla Nelprin |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Senate Bureau of Intelligence |masters= |apprentices= }} Vyn Narcassan was a high-ranking Senate Bureau of Intelligence official during the time of the Separatist Crisis. Just before the Clone Wars, he claimed to have found an immensely secure communications channel linking Separatist factions with someone within the Galactic Republic government. However, he was not taken seriously. In his 20-year career he performed over 100 covert missions. When Palpatine became Galactic Emperor, he successfully engineered his disappearance before Imperial Intelligence could eliminate him. He fathered two daughters, Vula and Shalla Nelprin. In 8 ABY, members of Wraith Squadron visited an exhibition on the history of Imperial Intelligence at the Galactic Museum on Coruscant.
