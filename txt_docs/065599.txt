Spiz's gang
1088

Spiz's gang were a gang of young miscreants who terrorized the slums of Coruscant prior to the Clone Wars. They were lead by Spiz and included a H'nemthe, a Kubaz and a number of other aliens. One of their frequent victims was a child whom they beat up for fun. On one such occasion, in 27 BBY, they were scared off by a Force illusion of a rancor created by Jedi Master Yarael Poof. *Star Wars: Zam Wesell Category:Criminal organizations
