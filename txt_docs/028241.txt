Grejic
1444

Grejic was a Hutt who was the Executive Secretary of the Hutt Grand Council a few years before the Battle of Yavin. Grejic apparently succeeded Mardoc. He decreed that the Besadii should suffer for raising the price of spice after the Battle of Nar Shaddaa.
