Permission Denied
2100

"Permission Denied" was a song by the band Red Shift Limit. The track was featured on the compilation album Thoughts from the Core, preceded in the track list by a song titled "Permission to Think." The album that the song appeared on was in circulation in the galaxy by the period of time between the years 0 ABY and 4 ABY.Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim is set in the time period between the Battle of Yavin and the Battle of Endor. Those battles are dated to 0 BBY and 4 ABY, respectively, by The New Essential Chronology, which also establishes the Battle of Yavin as the final event before the beginning of 0 ABY. Due to Red Shift Limit being an anti-Imperial musical group, the song was banned by the Imperial Board of Culture. "Permission Denied" was mentioned in the 1993 book Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim, a supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Simon Smith and Eric Trautmann. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Songs by Red Shift Limit
