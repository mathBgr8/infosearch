Eneboros ke Shalapan
1844

Eneboros ke Shalapan was a male Human Adumari starfighter pilot and blastsword duelist from the planet Adumar. ke Shalapan challenged Cartann Ground Champion Cheriss ke Hanadi for her title before the perator's announcement of world government for Adumar. The duel did not last long as ke Hanadi evaded ke Shalapan's initial thrust, and caught him in the ribcage with her blastsword. ke Hanadi gave the choice of his fate to the perator, who decided that he should die. ke Shalapan was spared when ke Hanadi decided to embarrass the perator and deliberately disobeyed his order. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Adumari Category:Duelists Category:Humans Category:Individuals with he/him pronouns Category:Males Category:Pilots
