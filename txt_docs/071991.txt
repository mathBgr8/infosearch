|death=April 9, 2020Blackpool, Lancashire, England, United Kingdom |listedpronouns= |nationality=English |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Malcolm Watson Dixon (1934 &ndash; April 9, 2020) was an actor who played an Ewok warrior in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. Stock footage of his Ewok character from the film was later used in the video game Star Wars: Battlefront. He later worked with George Lucas on the 1988 film Willow, in which he played a Nelwyn band member.
