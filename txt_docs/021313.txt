|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=10 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Dark Empire |issue=3 }} Dark Empire 3 was the third issue of the six-part Star Wars: Dark Empire series of comics. It was released April 21, 1992 by Dark Horse Comics and reprinted on August 18, 1992. A war for the planet Calamari erupts over that planet's surface, already threatened by the massive World Destroyers. While Lando and Wedge take on the Imperial stormtroopers, Leia is tormented by visions of Luke being sucked into the darkness. Meanwhile, it seems that Han has run into some "old friends"! The events of The Battle for Calamari were adapted in the Dark Empire (audio) radio drama, which featured the music of John Williams' score from the films, as well as a voice cast that included Billy Dee Williams reprising his role as Lando Calrissian.
