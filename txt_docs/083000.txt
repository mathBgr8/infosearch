Realm of Death
3987

Realm of Death was a Yuuzhan Vong warship that was part of the armada set to be dispatched to the Calamari system in 29 ABY to battle the forces of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances. Before leaving with the fleet, Realm of Death participated in the Yuuzhan Vong sendoff for the armada in orbit of the planet Yuuzhan'tar. Realm of Death was a hyperdrive-equipped warship that featured mica viewports. The warship's decks were made of yorik coral. thumb|left|200px|The Realm of Death was present in Yuuzhan'tar's orbit for a fleet sendoff in 29 ABY. Realm of Death operated as part of the Yuuzhan Vong armada in 29 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force to 29 ABY. during the Yuuzhan Vong War, in which the extra-galactic Yuuzhan Vong species invaded the galaxy. That year, in preparation for the armada's departure to the Calamari system and planned attack on the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances at the world of Mon Calamari, Realm of Death and the fleet were present in orbit of the planet Yuuzhan'tar for a sendoff. For the ceremony, Realm of Death was branded with the emblem of its domain and adorned with the blood from sacrificed Galactic Alliance captives like the other warships of the fleet. The vessel's officers knelt in subservience with their right fists pressed to the deck while Supreme Overlord Shimrra Jamaane recited a prayer for victory from his holy yacht. When the sendoff was complete, the warship left Yuuzhan'tar's orbit and entered hyperspace with the rest of the armada as it began its journey to the targeted Calamari system.
