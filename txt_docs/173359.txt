Star Wars 0
1611

Star Wars 0 was a comic book published by Dark Horse Comics, with two versions released exclusively for two companies: American Entertainment and AnotherUniverse.com. The comic collected the story The Keeper's World, originally serialized in Pizzazz from Marvel Comics. The two versions had different covers: one using Brian Stelfreeze's Princess Leia art from the Star Wars Galaxy trading card set, and the other by Dave Dorman.
