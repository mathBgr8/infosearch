Unidentified henchman (Tatooine)
3680

A Corellian pirate was part of the group that escorted Jabba the Hutt to Docking Bay 94 in Mos Eisley to confront Han Solo. Minutes prior he had been drinking in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina, along with Boelo and Gela Yeens. One of three "Corellian pirates",Star Wars Chronicles (p. 86) this character, played by Ron Tarr, appeared in a scene which was originally deleted from Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope but was included in the Special Edition release of the film.
