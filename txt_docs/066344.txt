Yunabana
1178

Yunabana was a male Gungan pilot who lived during the time when the Galactic Republic was reorganized into the Galactic Empire. In 18 BBY, he rescued Ferus Olin, Trever Flume, and Solace after their encounter with Inquisitor Malorum at Ryoo Thule's home.
