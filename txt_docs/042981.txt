Spaceport City
748

Spaceport City was the capital of the planet Harridan. Its many twisted streets were almost always full with traffic, as the population of the entire planet was extremely high. The traffic consisted of anything ranging from simple pedestrians to foot-drawn taxis to expensive repulsorlift vehicles. As its name suggested, it was home to the planets spaceport, and just outside the bulk cargo gates you could find the Lullaby Motel and, on Nelson Boulevard, the Museum of Flight could be found.
