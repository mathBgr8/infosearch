Don Austen
981

Donald Austen is an English puppeteer who worked on Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace During 1999, Austen was hand-picked by master puppeteer Frank Oz to help him portray Yoda in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. For this complex puppet, Oz would need three helpers, including Austen, David Greenaway and Kathy Smee. *Star Wars: The Making of Episode I The Phantom Menace Category:Puppeteers Category:Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace production staff
