Raven Base
1508

Raven Base was a Rebel base located on Yavin 4. It was destroyed by Darth Vader and his forces during the Evacuation of Yavin in 0 ABY. It was located close to Falcon Base. It contained three troop centers, a power core, a mech factory, and two Prefab shelters.
