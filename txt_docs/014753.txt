The Human Kant Aryon, who was the son of a baron and a baroness and was age thirty-two by the time of the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, hailed from Bethars. Bethars was mentioned in Martin Wixted's 1993 West End Games Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game sourcebook Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley.
