Um'Tal
482

The Um'Tal was an ancient artifact of a lost civilization believed to be invested with mystical powers. Also known as the "Sky Stone", the Um'Tal was believed to have been located on the planet Teh'Jar II. The search for the Um'Tal was undertaken by Doctor Angrail Ryhl and his nemesis, Nardo Sau. *Heroes & Rogues Category:Artifacts
