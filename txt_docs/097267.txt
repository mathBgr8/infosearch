Geonosian electro-staff
1051

thumb|250px|A [[Human/Legends|Human warrior wielding an electro-staff.]] The Geonosian electro-staff was a melee weapon that was created and used by the insect-like Geonosians. It was a large, two-handed staff which inflicted powerful electric shocks on its target. Because of the xenophobic nature of the Geonosians, the electro-staff was seldom seen outside of Geonosis.
