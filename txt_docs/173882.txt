Star Wars Weekly 113 is the one hundred thirteenth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 23, 1980. It included the original story "The Guardian of Forever!". *"The Guardian of Forever!" *"Heroes of the galaxy: Monark Starstalker Part 3: Confrontation in the snow!" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 23: Out of my mind!" *"Tales of the Watcher: The search for Shanng!"
