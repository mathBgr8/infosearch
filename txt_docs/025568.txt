Shortly before the Battle of Yavin in 1 BBY, the Camblian Order from the Outer Rim world of Eedoq sent 309 prospective students to be enrolled at the Clarion Scholars Academy on the planet Daemen. However, their transport ship, the Lark, was destroyed during the journey by the Imperial pilot Tars Nandy, resulting in the deaths of all 309 children.
