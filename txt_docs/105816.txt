Port
1697

Port was a male Vorzydiak who was the Chairman of Vorzyd IV, in 39 BBY. He was the husband of Bryn and father of Grath. During that year, Vorzyd IV suffered attacks on its factories. Chairman Port accused the people of Vorzyd V, which emerged to be a competitive planet. He told his problem to the Jedi Council and Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi were sent there to solve the problem. They finally discovered that was not Vorzyd V's fault, but the Freelies'. These actions got Vorzyd IV and Vorzyd V close to war, but finally Port met with Felana, leader of Vorzyd V, to close down the misunderstanding.
