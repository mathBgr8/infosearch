thumb|250px|Palencar's cover for Path of Destruction John Jude Palencar is a worldly renowned illustrator, usually working on fantasy, science fiction and horror book covers. He did the cover art for Darth Bane: Path of Destruction and Darth Bane: Dynasty of Evil, the first and third book in the Darth Bane Trilogy. Category:Cover artists
