Rikyam
1306

Rikyams were the central brains of the worldships of the Yuuzhan Vong. Relatives of the yammosk and dhuryam (but with less religious significance), they controlled everything onboard the worldship, monitoring the environmental controls, controlling the breeding of creatures, and monitoring ship functions.
