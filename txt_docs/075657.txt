Familiar
1241

To the Yuuzhan Vong, a familiar or mascot was one who was a trusted aide, a close companion, and if need be, tutor. A familiar had additional meanings and duties which were unknown. Familiars and masters were connected to each other until one died. Vergere was a familiar to Falung and Elan. Vergere's relationship with the two priestesses was a form of mentorship. Onimi was also the familiar of Supreme Overlord Shimrra.
