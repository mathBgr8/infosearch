thumb|right|250px|Walking dead in the Bare Forest. The Bare Forest was a mountain valley located on Zelos II. A rocky valley studded with cusps of conifer trees, it was located in the northern part of the planet's main continent at some 1,200 meters above sea level. It was home to a tiny worm-like creature whose enzymes could sometimes animate corpses, hence it was often called the Forest of the Walking Dead. Category:Forests and woods Category:Zelos II locations
