He became one of the top commanders of Naga Sadow, the leader of the Sith Empire, and Sadow used dark side alchemy to transform Ra-Zyrth into a mutated monstrosity. The Massassi subsequently entered a deep sleep inside a suspended animation capsule, which was fired onto the planet Coruscant during a Sith attack on the world in 5000 BBY. Ra-Zyrth was a male Massassi who was born during the time leading up to the Great Hyperspace War. He received a violent upbringing, and, like all Massassi, he was trained from birth to become a soldier for the Sith Empire, a powerful state located within the Stygian Caldera.The Essential Atlas Ra-Zyrth eventually became one of the top commanders of Naga Sadow, the leader of the Sith Empire, and, shortly prior to Sadow's invasion of the Galactic Republic in 5000 BBY,The New Essential Chronology the Sith Lord summoned together Ra-Zyrth and a number of Sadow's other commanders. Using dark side alchemy, Sadow transformed Ra-Zyrth and the other commanders into mutated monstrosities. Ra-Zyrth and the commanders subsequently entered suspended animation capsules, and the Massassi went into a deep sleep. Shortly afterward, Sadow's forces attacked the planet Coruscant, the capital of the Republic, and during the battle the capsules containing Ra-Zyrth and the other slumbering commanders were fired into the Coruscant cityscape. A number of vague and conflicting accounts existed regarding Ra-Zyrth's subsequent fate, and some individuals claimed that following the deployment of his capsule, the Massassi immediately awoke and died while fighting in combat against Coruscant's Jedi defenders. Others claimed that his capsule came to lie in a forgotten region of the Coruscant Undercity, where Ra-Zyrth remained in his sleep for many years, waiting for his capsule to be activated by accident, so that he could be reawakened. From a young age, Ra-Zyrth was trained to be a ruthless, efficient soldier, who strove to achieve glory for the Sith Empire. After his exposure to Naga Sadow's alchemical manipulations, he became very muscular and gained an armored hide. Ra-Zyrth could speak in the Sith language. The tough and athletic Massassi could wear light armor and was proficient in the usage of simple weapons and the lanvarok, a wrist-mounted weapon that fired small discs.Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide He was equipped with a Massassi insignia of rank, a sword, and a Sith lanvarok.
