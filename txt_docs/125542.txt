Dewback Stables
1593

The Dewback Stables was a building in Mos Eisley on Tatooine, situated adjacent to the Tatooine Militia and the Regional Government Offices. It was built soon after the founding of the city, and originally was a stable for Dewbacks. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, Dewback Stables no longer housed animals, but was owned by the Tatooine militia. The building now serves as a garage for the militia's speeders.
