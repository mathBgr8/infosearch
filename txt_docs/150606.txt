Shoengen Shores
412

Shoengen Shores was a location on Brosi. It was to the west of Shoengen Delta. It was said that Randle Clanse was born in the rural areas north to the Shoengen Shores. Shoengen Shores was in the influence area of the Brosin Underground around 2 ABY. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Brosi locations
