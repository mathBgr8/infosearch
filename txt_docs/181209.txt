L8O protocol droid
943

The L8O protocol droid was a model of protocol droid used toward the end of the Galactic Republic. Riboga the Hutt used a number of them, such as L80-RC, for his operations in the Cularin system before he left in 53 BBY. By 32 BBY, some described the L80 as an "old model."
