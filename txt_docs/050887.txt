They were often paired with 2-1B-series medical droids. The FX-series had a similar name to the FX-2 droidThe High Republic: Cataclysm and FX-6 droid,The Vow of Silver Dawn both of which had been seen in the High Republic Era. They were designed with multiple arms and to look after patients, perform tests, operate equipment and recommend procedures.
