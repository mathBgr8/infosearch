Gar-Ul
1466

Gar-Ul was a male Weequay criminal active on Qiilura. He and his brother Guta-Nay were mercenaries employed by Ghez Hokan. It was said about Gar-Ul that he had more muscle than brains. It was Gar-Ul who discovered Jedi Master Kast Fulier and his Padawan Etain Tur-Mukan after a group of farmers tipped him. Kast Fulier was captured, but Etain escaped and Gar-Ul was unable to keep up with the Jedi Padawan.
