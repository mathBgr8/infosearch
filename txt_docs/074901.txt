Star Wars: Epic Duels
3298

|upc= }} Star Wars: Epic Duels was a board game published by Hasbro in 2002. It is now out of print. The game features 4 maps and 31 miniature plastic figurines. There are 12 decks of 31 cards each featuring a major character and 1 or 2 minor characters. Players choose character sets and duel each other by rolling a die, moving their figures around the board and drawing and playing cards constituting "actions" each turn.
