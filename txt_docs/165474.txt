|model= |type=Pulse laser cannon |culture= |creators= |created= |modifier= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners= |locations= |cost= |value= |structure= |size= |material= |length= |width= |height= |weight= |protection= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation= }} The Prm-3 pulse laser cannon was a pulse laser cannon built by BlasTech, a company which normally produced personal blaster weapons. While designing the Prm-3, BlasTech cut corners to make it more affordable. This resulted in a substandard weapon with a limited maximum of 3.1 GP. This relatively low damage capability hindered the ships it was installed in, such as the standard model of the Starfeld Z-10 Seeker. Another disadvantage was that the Prm-3 did not have its own targeting system.
