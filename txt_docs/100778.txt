Kala Kevv
1537

Kala Kevv, code-named Prodigal-One, was a Sullustan male pilot during the Galactic Civil War. While flying for the Alliance to Restore the Republic, Kevv's Y-wing starfighter crashed on NCW-781. The resulting rescue operation retrieved both him and his datacard. Kala Kevv appears in the 1996 roleplaying game sourcebook Instant Adventures.
