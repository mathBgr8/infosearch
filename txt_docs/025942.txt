|imports= |exports= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Alliance to Restore the Republic }} Ksift was an Imperial rainforest world in the Outer Rim Territories. Its capital was Rakati. It was also the location of ATC-5/GS and a Rebel security outpost. Nik betrayed the local Rebels, but was brought to justice by Bret Hanson.
