Saleucami Cloning Complex
1477

thumb|left|150px|[[Tholme/Legends|Tholme is prepared to destroy the Cloning Complex.]] The Saleucami Cloning Complex was an installation, overseen by the Dark Jedi Sora Bulq, built in the Magma Caverns of Saleucami. There, the Separatists created an army of Morgukai clones. Jedi Master Tholme damaged part of the installations during the Clone Wars.
