Unidentified Amani chief
1224

An Amanin chief was present during the Battle of Maridun. After a vicious battle with Imperial forces led by General Ziering, he commended Janek Sunber on his valor. He forged a truce that would prove profitable for the Empire: Any captives taken by his tribe during ritual warfare would be given as slaves to the Empire. *Empire 18 *Star Wars: The Comics Companion *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Amanin Category:Chiefs Category:Males
