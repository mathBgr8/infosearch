Voren, Vo'ren, and Vor'en were similar first names used by human males. People with these names included: *Voren Na'al, Rebel historian *Voren Na'al, Rebel historian *Darth Voren, AKA Voren Renstaal, Sith apprentice to Revan *Vo'ren Faalo, Jedi Master *Vor'en Kurn, mercenary
