Narik
3603

Narik was the senator of Rodia during the Yuuzhan Vong War. "Narik" was Rodese for "light".Galactic Campaign Guide Narik was the senator of Rodia in the New Republic Senate in 25 ABY. He was appointed to Borsk Fey'lya's Advisory Council, and voted for defending Bothawui over Corellia, due to Rodia's proximity to Bothawui.The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse In 26 ABY, during a meeting concerning the Yuuzhan Vong invasion, he was outraged at the loss of the Mid Rim worlds of Kalarba, Druckenwell and Falleen to the enemy, citing that the New Republic favored the Core Worlds and Chief of State Borsk Fey'lya's homeworld of Bothawui. However, Narik showed his own opinions when he blatantly told Jedi Master Luke Skywalker that Tatooine, which was looking at potential invasion, was not his concern. Narik first appeared in The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse, where he's identified as "Navik". A Hunter's Fate: Greedo's Tale had previously identified "Navik the Red" as the name of a warlord of Rodia. He was first identified as "Narik" in The New Jedi Order: Balance Point. Galactic Campaign Guide says that "Navik" and "Narik" are both Rodese names, with "Navik" meaning "dark" and "Narik" meaning "light".
