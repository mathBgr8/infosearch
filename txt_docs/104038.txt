Nicole Fantl
1022

Nicole Fantl is the actress who played Senator Lexi Dio in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones (2002). Before appearing in Attack of the Clones, she appeared in a 2000 episode of Water Rats, as well as the films Alone Together (1998, Eric Eason), Moulin Rouge! (2001, Baz Luhrmann, featuring Ewan McGregor) and Queen of the Damned (2002, Michael Rymer). Fantl was also the English voice of Fran in the 2006 game Final Fantasy XII.
