Imperial Engineers Academy
2079

The Imperial Engineers Academy,Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim also known as the Imperial Engineering Academy, was a prestigious Imperial engineering school. The Empire required that all graduates of the Academy receive biannual certification from the prestigious Institute of Starship Engineers. Sometime between the formation of the Galactic Empire in 19 BBY and the occupation of the planet Biitu in 15 BBY, a team of brilliant scientists from the Imperial Engineers Academy, at Terrinald Screed's order, was directly responsible for the revival of the Abominor known as the Great Heep at the Braltzamir Museum of Antiquated Machinery on Coruscant. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Imperial Academies
