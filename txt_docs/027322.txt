Nectarwine
1047

Nectarwine was a fermented alcoholic drink from Nepoy, in the Senex sector. Seven hundred barrels of this beverage were served at the wedding feast of Gorga the Hutt and Anachro the H'uun. Nectarwine clotted and curdled if subjected to rough treatment, which gave it an appalling taste.
