Happy-patty
822

The happy-patty was a type of food available around the year 13 ABY.The New Essential Chronology places the events of Planet of Twilight in 13 ABY. A favorite among children, the dish could be prepared quickly. Happy-patties were first mentioned in Planet of Twilight, a 1997 novel by Barbara Hambly.Planet of Twilight *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Food
