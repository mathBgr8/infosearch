Aqualis Food Conglomerate
1105

The Aqualis Food Conglomerate was a corporation on Baralou that generated foodstuffs from the life in the seas on the planet. It was also a front for the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War, and was the official owner of Aqualis Base *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Conglomerates Category:Foodstuff manufacturers Category:Front companies
