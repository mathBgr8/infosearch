Military Mobility Shield
1293

right|250px The Merr-Sonn Munitions Military Mobility Shield was belt-mounted deflector shield generator designed to be used on the battlefield. As such, it could absorb about fifty heavy blaster shots before it failed. It also featured self recharging energy cells, capable of recharging the shield within a minute after depletion. As with most other personal energy shields, it was only useful against energy weapons, such as blasters. The Military Mobility Shield was small enough to be worn on a belt. Its components included a shield projector, cooling vents, activation panel, power indicator, power cell housing, and side clasp. *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Belts Category:Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. products Category:Personal energy shields
