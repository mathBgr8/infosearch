Sarsuma
1730

Sarsuma was a Rebel BFF-1 bulk freighter which attempted to steal Imperial supplies from a depot, during the food shortage following the evacuation from Yavin 4. Shortly after its arrival, it was destroyed by the turbolasers of the Star Destroyer Badi Dea, which had also arrived at that time.
