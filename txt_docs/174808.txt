It was said that the Sharpshooter combined the best Vershyi had to offer in scope, power output and barrels. Few rifles could compare with this model. The Vershyi Sharpshooter appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
