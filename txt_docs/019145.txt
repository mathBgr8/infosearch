GFFA (disambiguation)
369

GFFA may refer to either: *The Galactic Federation of Free Alliances, the successor state of the New Republic which emerged during the Yuuzhan Vong War. *The "Galaxy Far, Far Away," a term among the Star Wars fandom that refers to the Star Wars galaxy as a whole.
