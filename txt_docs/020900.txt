University of Garos
994

The University of Garos was located in Ariana, on Garos IV. It contained a large number of Imperial records that survived the data purges initiated by Ysanne Isard. As such, after Garos joined the New Republic, following the Thrawn campaign, New Republic historians and scientists swarmed to the University's databanks. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook Category:Garos IV locations Garos
