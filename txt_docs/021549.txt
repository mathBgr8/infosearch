|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: Heir to the Empire |issue=4 }} Heir to the Empire 4 was the fourth issue in the Star Wars: Heir to the Empire comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel Heir to the Empire. It was released January 2, 1996. Luke Skywalker's Jedi senses have been struck blind! To make matters worse, Grand Admiral Thrawn has just arranged to receive a shipment of creatures capable of pushing back the power of the Force. Thrawns's [sic] purpose: to ambush Luke and turn him over to the dark Jedi Master, Joruus C'baoth!
