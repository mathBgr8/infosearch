Type-12B Hollow-Tip Rocket
2877

The Type-12B Hollow-Tip Rocket, also known as Type-12B capsules and Type-12B rockets, was an ammunition type manufactured by Locris Syndicates and Locris Syndicated Securities.A hollow-tip rocket, Type-12B rockets were typically loaded with FGA-583 nerve agent, although other compounds could be used such as Accudrop Stun Gas. Some bounty hunters were known to "hot load" their rockets with exotic poisons. The Type-12B was designed for use with Locris Syndicates' Model RDP-12 Rocket Launcher.
