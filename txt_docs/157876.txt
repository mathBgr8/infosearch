Governor of Corulag
1668

The Governor of Corulag was the New Republic governor of Corulag during the Yuuzhan Vong War. After the Battle of Coruscant, the governor surrendered to the Yuuzhan Vong and ruled as a collaborationist. After Corulag was liberated, the governor was hanged in the streets of Curamelle by a lynch mob which then desecrated the corpse.
