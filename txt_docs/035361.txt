Planet Hoppers
5313

}} Planet Hoppers was a web feature on the Wizards of the Coast website published weekly (skipping a few weeks) from January 2003 to October 2004, with an additional chapter in April 2005 and a few more in 2008. Each story is four to five chapters set on a single planet, along with roleplaying-game stats and info about the characters, technology, creatures, and locations in the story.
