Whistler's Whirlpool Tapcafe
1618

The Whistler's Whirlpool was a tapcafe in the Drinking Cup on Trogan, near the city of Kergara. It was designed and built by an Ortolan known as Whistler. During the time of the Old Republic, the cafe was very popular, but it eventually fell on hard times. The geyser made it cold and damp, and the noise drowned out conversation.
