Solo apartment
1949

The Solo apartment referred to the Solo family's home on Coruscant after they moved out of the Imperial Palace, located in Eastport's administrative district. It was situated some three hundred meters from the top of its building, making it nearly a penthouse suite by the standards of that world. The apartment was abandoned when Han and Leia were forced to evacuate the capital in the face of the Yuuzhan Vong's conquest of Coruscant. Some time later Jacen Solo, who was wandering the Vongformed planet after having escaped from Vong captivity with the help of Vergere, stumbled upon the remains of the apartment.
