Stinna Draesinge Sha
1696

Stinna Draesinge Sha was a programmer and teacher at the Magrody Institute of Programmable Intelligence, and a brilliant AI programming theoretician. Sha was taught by Professor Nasdra Magrody himself and she in turn instructed future Jedi, Cray Mingla. Sha studied Ssi-ruuvi droid starfighters and the entechment process, trying to discover how the aliens were able to capture a person's life energy.
