Lungworm
941

A Lungworm was a biotechnological creature shaped by the Yuuzhan Vong to supply air to to the gnullith worn with a vacuum ooglith, their equivalent of a spacesuit. Worn coiled on the wearer's back, it reached round over the shoulder to connect its mouth to the gnullith, and it could supply air for up to two hours&mdash;at which point, having run out of air itself, it would expire. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Species of undefined sentience Category:Yuuzhan Vong creatures
