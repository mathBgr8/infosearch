Quyste
1225

Qusyte was a planet in the Quyste system within the Thesme sector of the Outer Rim Territories on which Talon Karrde had a training center. The smuggler sent H'sishi there before 18 ABY to study starship operations. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories Category:Thesme sector locations
