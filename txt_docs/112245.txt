Vibroscalpel
3650

Vibroscalpels were a specialized type of vibroblade that had numerous medical applications due to its ability to cut precisely and effortlessly. Vibroscalpels were medical tools which allowed for precise and effortless incisions.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Miniaturized vibroscalpels were standard equipment fitted to MD-4 medical droids to assist them in performing delicate surgery. The medic at the ORO mining facility on Apatros used a vibroscalpel and synthflesh to reattach Gerd's thumb on a miner.Darth Bane: Path of Destruction Vibroscalpels were used for a variety of surgical procedures at Rimsoo units during the Clone Wars.MedStar I: Battle Surgeons The thief Bink Kitik used a vibroscalpel to cut through transparisteel.
