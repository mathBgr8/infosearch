"A'den" was a word in the Mando'a language that meant "rage" or "wrath." As a clone trooper, A'den was created on the planet Kamino from the clone template of the Human Jango Fett. The male clone trooper shared the template's tan skin and height of 1.83 meters. A'den was first mentioned in the "Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic," an reference article in the eighty-fourth issue of Star Wars Insider, released on September 26, 2005. Named by author Karen Traviss, it was the same name given to Null-class Advanced Recon Commando Sergeant N-12.
