Kuat Drive Yards weapons platform
2056

The Kuat Drive Yards weapons platform was a class of military space station produced for the Imperial Navy at the Kuat Drive Yards. It required sophisticated shipyards for its production, beyond the capabilities of facilities like those near Coruscant which could produce Imperial I-class Star Destroyers. *The Illustrated Star Wars Universe Category:Defense platform classes Category:Kuat Drive Yards products
