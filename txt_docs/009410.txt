Jyvus Space City
1510

thumb|250x|Jyvus surrenders to the Confederacy. Jyvus Space City was one of the largest space cities surrounding the planet Duro. During the Battle of Duro in 21 BBY, it was one of the first targets captured by the Confederate Navy. It was made the headquarters for General Grievous, and its capture allowed the Separatists to lower Duro's planetary shield, thus facilitating the planet's surrender within the week.
