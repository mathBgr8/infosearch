Random sabacc
888

Random sabacc was a type of sabacc in which the rules of play changed at random intervals. This particular type of sabacc was played by Han Solo and Lando Calrissian over ownership of the Millennium Falcon in 11 ABY, during which C-3PO served as the randomizer. Because of the wildly varying rules and procedures used during Random sabacc, a scoring system is often used to keep track of progress. During the game between Han and Lando, the two players played to one hundred points, and kept score by giving the winning player an amount of points equal to the difference between the winning and losing hands. *Dark Apprentice Category:Sabacc variants
