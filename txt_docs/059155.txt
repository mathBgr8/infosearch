Radley
1191

Radley was a Rebel BFF-1 bulk freighter which carried B-wing prototypes and delivered them to the Cathleen. While a Blue Squadron A-wing guarded it, its shield system malfunctioned and had to be relieved by Keyan Farlander. During that time, the Nebulon-B frigate Interceptor approached and launched TIEs that attempted to destroy the freighter.
