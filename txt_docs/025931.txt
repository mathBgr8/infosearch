It was the only inhabited celestial body in the system and had an extreme electromagnetic field. The moon was a frigid world with ice crevasses, polar ice caves, lichen, shrubs, indigenous quohr, and two sapient species: the tribal Askhew and the blue banthas. The world joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic and served as the location of a Rebel strike base with easy access to a nearby Imperial shipping lane.
