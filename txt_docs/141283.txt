Sector Epsilon
1052

Sector Epsilon was the newest sector of the strange space station called Zirtran's Anchor. It was reserved for newcomers to the station and offered several good spots for thos who wished to settle there. There was a small connection charge to be paid, though, and a quarterly payment was also required for maintenance, communications, power and life support. Among the more mysterious settlers in this area were the Kalai who resided in the Destiny. While no pure Kalai were seen roaming the station, their offspring, called the lethagoes would venture into the station from time to time. Epsilon, Sector
