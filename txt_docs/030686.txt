Task Force Gemstone
1457

}} Task Force Gemstone was one of five task forces of the New Republic Defense Fleet's Third Battle Group. In 16 ABY, Gemstone was led by Commodore Poqua. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Gemstone was placed under the temporary command of the Fifth Battle Group and paired with Task Force Copperleaf to form a combined task force under Commodore Mirx.
