Codruese
1155

Codruese was the official language of Munto Codru and was spoken by the native Codru-Ji species. The younger Codru-Ji, under the shape of Wyrwulves, could not speak this language, but they could understand it. Codruese used snarls, whines and throaty barks, as well as some other canine noises. Codruese also had a complex lexicon and many idioms, making it a difficult language. *The Crystal Star *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Legacy Era Campaign Guide Category:Codru-Ji culture Category:Languages
