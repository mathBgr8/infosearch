Tabbins Vee
1959

Tabbins Vee was an Imperial stormtrooper who, in 3 ABY, served in Lieutenant Mils Giel's expedition to investigate the formerly Rebel-held Golrath Station. Vee was a graduate of the Imperial Academy, where he was eternally in the shadow of his roommate Bens Figg&mdash;while Figg was considered a rising star and future officer material from the moment he arrived, Vee was regarded as second-best in all respects. Vee and Figg were assigned to Golrath Station together after leaving the Academy, but their first posting didn't last long.
