Festival of the Twin Gods
790

The Festival of the Twin Gods was a holiday among the Yuuzhan Vong. It was celebrated in honor of Yun-Yammka, the Slayer, and Yun-Harla, the Trickster. Rather whimsical for the normally serious Yuuzhan Vong, the holiday was marked by a dropping of rules among the Yuuzhan Vong. For example, crèchlings would play tricks on their elders, without any fear of reprisal, while adult Yuuzhan Vong would often trade secrets with each other, even among Domain rivals. *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Holidays Category:True Way
