Mousul
2114

Mousul was a male Ansionian Senator of the Galactic Republic who represented Ansion during the Separatist Crisis before the Clone Wars. Throughout the Jedi Mission to Ansion, Mousul conspired with Shu Mai, Presidente of the Commerce Guild, and others to secretly compel Ansion to secede from the Republic—to cause not only a chain reaction that would ultimately result in many other planets seceding from the Republic, but also to further the goals of the secessionist movement, also known as the Confederacy of Independent Systems. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns *Star Wars Adventures Magazine 9 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ansionians Category:Confederacy of Independent Systems individuals Category:Galactic Republic senators Category:Males
