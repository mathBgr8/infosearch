Majestic (Acclamator-class)
3837

The Majestic was an Acclamator-class assault ship used by the Republic during the Clone Wars and later by the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. It was refitted with heavy armor plating to enhance its defensive capabilities. The Majestic saw action during the Battle of Geonosis.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Three months after the Battle of Geonosis, the Majestic was deployed to Qiilura to extract Omega Squad from the planet, and was eventually joined by the cruiser Vengeance. The Majestic provided gunnery support to Omega and heavily damaged a Techno Union vessel in orbit of Qiilura before extracting Omega Squad.Republic Commando: Hard Contact 367 days, almost a year, after Geonosis, the Majestic responded to a Red Zero signal from Omega Squad. However, Omega was rescued by Delta Squad before Majestic could reach their position, so the Majestic was diverted to find their TIV pilot, Sicko.
