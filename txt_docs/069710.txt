Drub McKumb
2215

Drub McKumb was a blue-eyed smuggler and spacer whom Han Solo and Mara Jade each knew from their smuggling days. Drub was a plump Human male. McKumb had last seen Han prior to the Battle of Hoth, when he warned Han on Ord Mantell about the price Jabba Desilijic Tiure had on his head. Years later, in 12 ABY, when Han went to Ithor to see the Time of Meeting with Leia Organa Solo, a crazed Drub attempted to warn Han of the looming threat of Eye of Palpatine.
