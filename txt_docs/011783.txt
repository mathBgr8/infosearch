Hrush and was, for a time, the second best-selling book according to the CoCo Town Edition of the HoloNet News. A ranking of the five best-selling books, including the number two ranked The Lost Twenty, appeared in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #45, an in-universe website issue that was published on February 28, 2002.Per , HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47 was released on March 14, 2002. As HoloNet News segments were published weekly, and was published two issues prior, it must have been released on February 28 of the same year. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content.
