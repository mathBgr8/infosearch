Palpatine's meditation chamber
1159

In the later stages of the Clone Wars, the Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Sidious, publicly known as Supreme Chancellor Palpatine of the Galactic Republic, maintained a meditation chamber inside the Grand Republic Medical Facility on Coruscant. The meditation chamber notably contained a semicircle of padded couches, resembling Palpatine's most private office in the Senate Office Building. *Star Wars: Complete Locations Category:Coruscant rooms
