Battle of Vactooine
2818

The Battle of Vactooine was an engagement between the Alliance to Restore the Republic and 12-4C-41's Droid Army that took place on the planet Vactooine in 0 ABY. After the Alliance ceased receiving shipments of metal from the planet, the Rebels Luke Skywalker, Han Solo, Leia Organa, Chewbacca, C-3PO, R2-D2 and Q-7N investigated and discovered that Olag Greck had influenced droids to seize control of the outpost.
