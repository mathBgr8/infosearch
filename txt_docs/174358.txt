Star Wars Weekly 59 is the fifty-ninth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 11, 1979. This issue includes part three of a black & white four-part reprinting of the unfinished story "The Kingdom of Ice", originally published in color in Marvel UK' Pizzazz magazine. The final part of the story was eventually printed in Star Wars Weekly 60. *"The Ice Worm Cometh!" *"Showdown" *"The power of Warlock Part 6: In-Betweener!" *"Micronauts Part 4: Death-Duel at Daytona Beach!" (Continuation) *"Tales of the Watcher: Earth is off-limits!" 59
