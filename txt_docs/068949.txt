JAN Camp
1510

The JAN Camp was located on Mrlsst and was one of the primary camps for the Justice Action Network in the Tapani sector. The camp was located approximately 400 kilometers north of the Mrlsst Trade and Science Academy along a swampy sea coast. It was positioned amongst the tall greenstalk trees which provide cover for the encampment. The JAN used camouflage nets to enhance the protective effect of the greenstalks and set up sensor pods in the surrounding trees to provide early warning against intruders.
