Lighter
2750

A lighter was a class of small starship that was commonly used in various navies throughout the galaxy, including those of the Galactic Republic and the Galactic Empire. Essentially a militarized yacht designed for short trips, lighters were put to a variety of uses, especially as personal vehicles for use by higher-ranking officers. Generally capable of carrying only a few passengers and small amounts of cargo, they were named for ancient water-faring vessels that were used to lighten the burden of larger such surface ships.MedStar I: Battle Surgeons Included among such classes put to this use was the Conqueror-class assault ship; one such vessel was used by Republic Navy Admiral Tarnese Bleyd during the Clone Wars. While he oversaw the construction of the first Death Star, Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin made use of a lighter as his personal transport, as did Admiral Conan Antonio Motti.Death Star In some cases, such as with the Etti Lighter used by the Corporate Sector Authority, they were pressed into service as frontline combat vessels, but in common parlance they were still classified as lighters.Han Solo at Stars' End Kyle Katarn's Moldy Crow, an HWK-290 light freighter, was sometimes described as a lighter.Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *Death Star *Han Solo at Stars' End *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire *Dark Forces: Jedi Knight *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back, Second Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starship types
