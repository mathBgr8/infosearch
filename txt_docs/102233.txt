Phenaru Prime
1338

Phenaru Prime was a fictitious planet created by New Republic to disguise attack on Borleias and intended to throw off any imperial interceptions. It was created from Borleias, but added an asteroid belt and different configuration of landmass and seas. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Fictional locations
