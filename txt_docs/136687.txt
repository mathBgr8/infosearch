thumb|250px|[[Alderaan mountains]] Grindelwald is a municipality in the district of Interlaken in the canton of Bern in Switzerland. Although no actual filming took place there, its mountains were scouted for Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith to capture plate photography for backdrops on the planet Alderaan.
