Domain Cha
841

Domain Cha was a Domain among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong and great rivals of Domain Kraal. Notable members of this domain included Wyrpuuk Cha, as well as his aide, Kadlah Cha. No members of Domain Cha were present when the Lusankya destroyed Domain Cha's military fleet. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines I: Rebel Dream *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Cha
