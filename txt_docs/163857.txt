thumb|right|250px|Erdelac and R2-D2 Edward M. Erdelac (b. September 28, 1975) is the author of "Hammer," a Star Wars Insider short story, and "Fists of Ion," a StarWars.com Hyperspace-exclusive short story. He also wrote the backstory of the Mini-Huvicko/Yuzabi Dowser binary hydromech droid, Yoxgit, and Bane Malar appearing in the original Databank, through the What's The Story? feature. His alias is caledre on StarWars.com, where he maintains a blog (If There's A Bright Center To The Universe...) as well as on TheForce.net's Jedi Council Forums. He goes by the name of on Wookieepedia. In addition to his Star Wars work, he is the author of The Merkabah Rider weird western series as well as various other horror and fantasy works.
