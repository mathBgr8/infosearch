Vortex pool

A vortex pool was a chamber filled with water that shot in currents, where beings could relax or play. Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo had one in their garden during the Black Fleet Crisis. This appears to be similar to a hot tub or jacuzzi. *Before the Storm Category:Entertainment Category:Hygiene
