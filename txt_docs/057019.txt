The New Jedi Order: Knightfall III: Jedi Blood
1168

The New Jedi Order: Knightfall III: Jedi Blood was the third book in the canceled Knightfall Trilogy of the New Jedi Order series. It was to be written by Michael Jan Friedman, with the cover art by Terese Nielsen. The plot would have dealt with Danni Quee and Jorallen fighting a Yuuzhan Vong High Priestess to stop the Vong from mass-murdering Jedi. C4
