Lolnar
2379

Lolnar was a planet located in the Lolnar system, in the Slice portion of the Core Worlds that was known as the Arrowhead. It was situated on the Corellian Run super-hyperroute, between the Perma and Rehemsa systems. The Human Lesan Ramier, who was a Moff of the Galactic Empire, hailed from Lolnar. Lolnar was first mentioned in Flashpoint! Brak Sector, a 1995 supplement written by Sterling Hershey for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The Essential Atlas, a reference book written by Daniel Wallace and Jason Fry and released in 2009, placed the Lolnar system, and therefore Lolnar itself, in grid square M-11. *Flashpoint!
