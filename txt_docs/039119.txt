Rimmer's Alley
1165

Rimmer's Alley was a street in the Low Town neighborhood of the Sulonese city of Barons Hed. It was part of the side street of the city and led towards the hill of Barons Knoll. The shops, cantinas and taverns had their doors permanently open. It was crowded by street vendors, beggars, bounty hunters, informers, prostitutes with glowing makeup and customers of the cantinas.
