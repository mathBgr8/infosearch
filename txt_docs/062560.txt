Tragg Brathis
1323

|masters= |apprentices= }} Tragg Brathis was a human male who was an alleged Moff that led the Grand Imperial Union. The only known appearance of Brathis was during a pirate HoloNet transmission over Channel 81 during the Black Fleet Crisis. In the transmission, Brathis displayed his Imperial fleet and announced his support of the Dushkan League. The broadcast dismayed some New Republic leaders, fearing that the forces of Nil Spaar were stronger than anticipated, but the existence of Brathis and his forces were never substantiated and was disregard as Yevethan propaganda. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males
