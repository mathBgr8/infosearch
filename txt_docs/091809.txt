Blue Sky Boulevard

Blue Sky Boulevard was a road on Corellia. It led from downtown Coronet City into the Blue Sector, and gave the sector its name. Card's Tapcaf was located on this road. *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Coronet roadways
