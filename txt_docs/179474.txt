Rinn Renado
1556

Rinn Renado was the Human male leader of a corporate resistance movement on Metellos. He specialized in guerrilla tactics and concentrated his efforts against CarsinShare and Nonsu targets. He saw Metellos as irredeemable and thus had no long-term goals, content with stirring up anarchy. He sometimes recruited from the gladiators who fought in Ektra. Losers in the gladiatorial contests were dropped 1,200 meters from a repulsorlift spire, and Renado would occasionally position an aircar underneath to catch them. They were offered an opportunity to join the anarchists or continue their deadly fall.
