Gavin Zmitt
1588

**Imperial Security Bureau |masters= |apprentices= }} Gavin Zmitt was a member of the Imperial Security Bureau who worked in the office of Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin. A few months following the Battle of Yavin, Zmitt filed a report regarding the Smuggler's Run. Ryan Kaufman named Gavin Zmitt after his friend, Kevin Schmitt, who had been the lead level designer on Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith.
