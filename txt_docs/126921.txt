Adrian Zmed
1013

Adrian Zmed voiced Cavik Toth in Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter. He is perhaps best known for portraying 1961 T-Bird leader Johnny Nogerelli in the 1982 musical comedy sequel, Grease 2. He also had a role the same year as LCPD Officer Vince Romano in the TV series T.J. Hooker, which starred Star Trek alumni, William Shatner. Category:Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter voice actors
