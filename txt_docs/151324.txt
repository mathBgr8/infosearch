Galactic Vacant Building Demolishing Movement
1738

}} thumb|left|175px|A G.V.B.D.M. pilot The Galactic Vacant Building Demolishing Movement, or G.V.B.D.M. for short, was a civil movement conceived by Colonel Braedic Ekirk. They would spot abandoned buildings and report their location to the Galactic Empire so they could be demolished. The G.V.B.D.M. employed pilots from the Hidden Daggers to demolish the abandoned homes. These pilots wore red R.I.S. armor helmets and flew VAAT/e's. The Galactic Vacant Building Demolishing Movement is mentioned in Move It or Lose It, a small in-universe introduction to the House Pack-Up operation in Star Wars Galaxies.
