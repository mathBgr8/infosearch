Droe was born on the Mid Rim planet of Cerea sometime before 95 BBY to a mother and father. She had several sisters and one younger brother. She was present when An'ya Kuro arrived on Cerea intending to take her younger brother to the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. When Kuro's starship landed on the Mundi family's farm, Droe and her sisters fled in fear of the strange machinery. Later that day, her brother sensed a group of raiders, led by Bin-Garda-Zon, approaching the farm. Droe stayed with Ki-Adi in a hidden cellar underneath the house. When their father was struck by Zon, Droe, though worried herself, managed to cover her brother's mouth with her hand, preventing him from making any sound and giving away their location to the raiders. Soon after, the door to the cellar opened, and the two were allowed out by their mother.
