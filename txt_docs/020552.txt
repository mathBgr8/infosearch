It was the innermost planet in the Arbran system, which was located in the Bon'nyuw-Luq sector, a part of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Arbran I was mentioned in "Hole in the Ground," the first article in Arbra: Sanctuary In the Storm, an online roleplaying supplement written by Cory J. Herndon for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The article was published on June 5, 2003, as part of the Planet Hoppers series on Wizards.com. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Arbran system, and therefore Arbran I, in grid square N-17.
