Boonta Training Course
1406

The Boonta Training Course was a podracing circuit on Tatooine. Like the Boonta Eve Classic podrace, it began at the Mos Espa Grand Arena and took a route through the desert, albeit a drastically less arduous one than Boonta Eve. This course was the first race in the Amateur Podracing Circuit tournament.
