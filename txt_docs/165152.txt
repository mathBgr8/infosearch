He travelled to the Black Dust Tavern with two Gamorreans to invite Drake Paulsen and his father Kaine Paulsen to a meeting with Abdi-Badawzi. Drake refused, but was stunned and kidnapped by Abdi-Badawzi as the smuggler left the tavern. He took him to the crime lord's base.
