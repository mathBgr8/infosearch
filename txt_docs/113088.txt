X10-D draft droid
2469

The X10-D draft droid was a droid developed by the Trandoshan species for labor. They were able to pick up things that Trandoshans&mdash;due to their claws&mdash;could not. Only those deemed worthy by the Scorekeeper&mdash;those who had achieved a significant number of Wookiee kills&mdash;could own a draft droid. The X10-D was over 3 meters tall, with extendable arms that could reach a distance of 3 meters. They moved via treaded feet. The droids were not equipped with internal cognitive modules or behavioral matrices which required them to be controlled by a central computer. The bounty hunter Bossk once owned a red and bronze X10-D, which he used aboard his ship, the Hound's Tooth. The droid's mind was replaced with that of Flirt.
