Bryar rifle
2553

The Bryar rifle was a type of blaster rifle that could fire small and fast shots, although it could also fire a 'charge,' one large and stronger shot. The Bryar rifle was heavy, and less accurate than other types of blaster rifles. Despite its weight and accuracy it was popular during the waning days of the Galactic Republic, and could be modified to make a much more accurate Bryar pistol. In later times, Bryar rifles became a much sought-after collector's item.
