MG3 mini-missile
788

The MG3 was a Krupx mini-missile tube developed for use by groundships and speeders. Turbostorm-class gunships were equipped with two MG3 tubes, for the fore and aft. Each MG3 tube carried two launchers, each which could fire one missile a second and had a magazine of four missiles. *Shatterpoint *Shatterpoint abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Krupx Munitions products Category:Surface-to-air missiles
