Intermatic, R.C.
463

Intermatic, R.C. was a shipping company that was in actuality a front for the New Republic's Alpha Blue team. The "business" owned two private hangars located in Section 41 of Newport. The hangars served primarily as a location where the Alpha Blue Technical services could receive important shipments that needed to be kept confidential. *Tyrant's Test Category:Transport companies
