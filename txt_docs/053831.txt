The interceptor was part of the Imperial 181st Imperial Fighter Wing's Saber Squadron by 4 ABY,The Death Star II Limited expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game is set around the time of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. and piloted by DS-181-4, who served as the wingman of Major Turr Phennir, also from Saber Squadron. Saber 4 was often assigned to fly in a reserve position in battle, and bore a bloodstripe representing at least 10 kills made by DS-181-4. In 4 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Endor to 4 ABY. DS-181-4 fought in the Battle of Endor, where Alliance to Restore the Republic starfighters attempted to destroy the Empire's Death Star II battle station with TIE pilots defending the Death Star from the Alliance starfighters. The battle ended in an Imperial defeat and the Death Star's destruction.
