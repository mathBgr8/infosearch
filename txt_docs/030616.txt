It was published by Marvel Comics in May 1980, and the story was later serialized in issues 39&ndash;44 the comic book series Star Wars (1977). The cover for this comic was also used for Marvel Special Edition 2: The Empire Strikes Back and Marvel Super Special 16, though this book has a unique back cover design.
