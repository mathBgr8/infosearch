Xibo was a group of three Modular Conveyors belonging to the Imperials. The group, under escort from the Nebulon-B frigate Tegret, were delivering R'alla H2O in the Atterol system when they were attacked by Rebels intending to capture the ships. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter Category:Imperial Navy units
