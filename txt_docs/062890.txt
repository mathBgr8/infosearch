Mallakin
963

The mallakin was an avian native to the planet Kashyyyk. The creature's natural habitat was within the leaves of the world's giant wroshyr trees, where it would hide and await its prey, commonly small insects. *A Forest Apart *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Birds Category:Creatures of Kashyyyk Category:Insectivorous creatures Category:Jungle and forest creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
