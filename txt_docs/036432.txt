KS-500 Spaceways Transport light freighter
1560

The KS-500 Spaceways Transport light freighter was a standard light freighter produced by the Corellian Engineering Corporation. They were high performance vehicles which could be easily modified, as was evidenced with The Fatal Vision. Category:Corellian Engineering Corporation products Category:Freighter classes
