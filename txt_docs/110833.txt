Ururur was the leader of a Tusken tribe. Ururur wore desert-colored robes with two bandoliers strapped diagonally across both sides of the chest above another worn as a belt, the standard Tusken mask, and wielded a Gaderffii. As a tribe leader, Ururur made harsh laws with severe penalties as it was the only way to maintain control. Ururur was first mentioned on a card in the 2001 Masters of the Force expansion set of Decipher's Jedi Knights Trading Card Game.
