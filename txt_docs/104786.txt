Garonnin
1357

Garonnin was the Human male leader of House Garonnin and a Senex Lord. In 12 ABY he was an ally of Roganda Ismaren. He was killed when Irek Ismaren cleaved him in half with a lightsaber after discovering they were betraying the Senex Lords by abandoning them to the Eye of Palpatine. *Children of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Nobility
