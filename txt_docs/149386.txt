|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Adony Station was a small Imperial outpost on the planet Jerne, close to ancient ruins of the Jerni. Due to the machinations of Darth Vader, during the Galactic Civil War, Rebel leaders Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa were led to believe that a powerful artifact known as the Eternity Crystal was hidden near Adong Station. As a result, Skywalker and Organa, supported by the pirate Meeka Reen and her guerrillas, assaulted the station and killed its contingent of Imperial troops. Category:Imperial military bases
