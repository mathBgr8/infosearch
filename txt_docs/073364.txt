During the reign of the Galactic Empire, a TIE staging area was located near the astronomical object. It was used to resupply battle-depleted Imperial forces and nearby capital ships. In 1 ABY,The attack on a TIE staging area is featured in Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing, which is set after Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit. The New Essential Chronology establishes that Operation Ram's Head (in Imperial Pursuit) took place approximately one year after the Battle of Yavin, which the book places in 0 BBY. Thus, the attack on a TIE staging area must take place as early as 1 ABY. The attack on a TIE staging area is also mentioned in Star Wars Galaxies: Jump to Lightspeed. Star Wars Galaxies is also set in 1 ABY (per the reasoning here) so the attack on a TIE staging area must take place as late as 1 ABY. Lieutenant Keyan Farlander, a rebel pilot, was dispatched to the staging area in a B-wing starfighter in order to destroy the cargo containers and parked TIE starfighters there. Farlander destroyed all of his targets before jumping into hyperspace. Yunkor IX was first mentioned in the Tour of Duty: B-Wing expansion to Totally Games' video game Star Wars: X-Wing.Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing X-Wing was released in February 1993, while the expansion released in the fall of that year. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Yunkor system, and therefore Yunkor IX, in grid square K-19.
