Battle of Althir (second)
2074

The Battle of Althir&mdash;the second battle to bear that name&mdash;was one of the last battles of the Mandalorian Wars. Occurring sometime between 3962 BBY and 3960 BBY, the battle saw the Galactic Republic's forces under the command of the Jedi Knight Revan annihilate most of the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders's ground forces. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *The New Essential Chronology *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles of Althir III Category:Battles of the Mandalorian Wars
