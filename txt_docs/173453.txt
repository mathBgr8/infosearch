Plasticene thermite
1927

Plasticene thermite was a type of substance that could be shipped and was considered to be contraband during the Imperial Era. It shared its nameStar Wars Outlaws with Plasticene, a substance used in starships,Star Wars: The Force Awakens novelization and thermite, a substance used to make the shell casing. In 3 ABY, the scoundrel Kay Vess accepted an underworld contract for the Pyke Syndicate to smuggle a shipment of plasticene thermite from Myrra Spaceport on the jungle planet Akiva to the Pyke's crystal mine in the Stormhollow region of Akiva. Plasticene thermite was mentioned in the current Star Wars canon continuity as part of the optional contract "Deliver Pyke contraband" for the 2024 video game Star Wars Outlaws, which was developed by Massive Entertainment and published by Ubisoft. Plasticene thermite gel was originally introduced in the Star Wars Legends continuity; first being mentioned in Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations a supplimentry book for the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
