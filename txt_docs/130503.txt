A Google of Gornts
768

A Google of Gornts was one of many holofilms denounced by the Core World Critics Association President Jaysa Namoor in 22 BBY. The second word of the title seeming to call for a quantity, "Google" is perhaps an erroneous substitution for the homophone "Googol," a number equal to 10100. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Holofilms
