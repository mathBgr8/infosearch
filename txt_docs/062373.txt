Abyss (Ord Ibanna)
1746

The Abyss was a podracing course on Ord Ibanna and the second race of the Invitational Podracing Circuit. This twisting and infamous course required the racers to be fully alert. When the path spits into an upper path and a lower one, the bottom path loops around the main arena, making it much longer and throws you back a couple places if you fall from the top. This course was the second race in the Invitational Podracing Circuit tournament.
