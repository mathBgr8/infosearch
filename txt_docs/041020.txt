Yima
1369

Lieutenant Yima was a female officer in the Telos Security Force following the Jedi Civil War. Dol Grenn assigned her to investigate how the Batu Rem impostor infiltrated the TSF. In cut content of Knights of the Old Republic II, Lieutenant Yima was supposed to be able to meet the Jedi Exile. Yima would give the Exile a quest to expose Lieutenant Atallo, a corrupt TSF officer who oversaw the Czerka Corporation hangar bay.
