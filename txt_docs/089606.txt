Pittin
2050

Pittins were small creatures commonly domesticated and used as pest-catching predators in households where droid pest-removal units were impractical. They were known for their purring,The Essential Guide to Droids and they came in a variety of colors. The original homeworld of the pittin was unknown, though they were tamed on many worlds, including Alderaan. Tia Organa kept pittins as pets, four in all; they were named Taffy, Winkie, Fluffy, and "All-Terrain Attack Vehicle," the last named by Leia Organa. These pittins were presumed dead in the destruction of Alderaan.
