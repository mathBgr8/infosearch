Kalree

thumb|right|250px|Kalree fleeing [[XQ2 Platform Pondut under heavy fire.]] Galactic Electronics Corporation used Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Kalree during the Galactic Civil War. Shuttle Kalree attempted to flee XQ2 Platform Pondut carrying G.E.C. VIP's as an Imperial assault force arrived during the "Arms Race." Imperial Intelligence had discovered the illegal arms deal between Galactic Electronics and the Rebel Alliance, and thus dispatched the assault force to strip the research facility of all technology.
