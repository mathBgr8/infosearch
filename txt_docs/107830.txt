Power pulsator
810

A Power pulsator was an upgrade component intended for use in blaster weapons, in order to inflict greater damage by adding additional plasma energy to the blaster bolt. This type of technology typically came in Mark I, II, III, IV & V types respectively, the largest of which could turn a small, holdout-style blaster pistol into a weapon possessing power equivalent to contemporary rifles. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Weapon attachments
