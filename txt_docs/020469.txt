Vissëncant Variations
1293

The "Vissëncant Variations" was the name given to a popular classical piece consisting of fifteen variations,MedStar I: Battle Surgeons composed by famed musician Bann Shoosha to celebrate the Shoosha family's escape from Brentaal. It was the favorite musical composition of Zabrak musician and surgeon Zan Yant. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar II: Jedi Healer Category:Musical compositions
