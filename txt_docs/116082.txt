Lucos Dannt
1997

Lucos Dannt was originally a headmaster at the Naboo Royal House of Learning. However, with the Invasion of Naboo in 32 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion he became a leader of a resistance group referred to as the Naboo Underground. He was well-informed about the development of the situation and had many contacts, including members of the Royal Naboo Security Forces.
