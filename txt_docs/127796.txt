The Skids
1046

The Skids was a neighborhood in the city of Tyrena on the planet of Corellia that was known for its somewhat rough appearance and even rougher residents. For a few months, Corellians recruited into the Corellian Engineering Corporation disappeared in this region, due to the recruiting agents of Kuat Drive Yards, who used a mix of persuasion and intimidation to get them to leave CEC. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Tyrena locations
