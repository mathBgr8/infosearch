Wheel Security Force
1998

The Wheel Security Force or more simply Wheel Security were the local police of the space station known as The Wheel. They answered to Administrator Simon Greyshade and their job was to maintain order. They also handled situations beyond the abilities of casino bouncers. Officers were usually Human and wore a green and yellow uniform with enclosed helmets.
