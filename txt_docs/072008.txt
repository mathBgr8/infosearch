Wint
1455

"Wrong-Way" Wint was a male Clantaani who belonged to a gang of criminals in Mos Espa led by Longo Two-Guns during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Jabba the Hutt placed a bounty on Wint for skimming profits from a "legitimate" protection agency on Mos Espa.
