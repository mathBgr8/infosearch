Second Battle of Ruusan
1346

The Second Battle of Ruusan was one of the final battles of the New Sith Wars. Following the previous engagement over Ruusan, the Republic tried to retake the planet. However, their fleet was defeated by the larger Sith fleet orbiting the world. *Darth Bane: Path of Destruction *The Dark Side Sourcebook Ruusan 2
