Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 4
2521

Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 4 is the fourth issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in summer 1995. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 4, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with a 1995 Topps Star Wars Galaxy Series 3 promotional trading card, a Topps Finest chromium Dagobah trading card, and a Jumbo "Quad" card featuring the cover art from the first four issues of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine.
