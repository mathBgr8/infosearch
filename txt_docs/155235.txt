Habba Kee
1942

Habba Kee was a male podracer who raced before 32 BBY, and he was considered one of the best in the Outer Rim Territories. Kee hit part of Obitoki's destroyed pod, wrecking his own racer as well. This character only appears in the novelization of The Phantom Menace, and no specific descriptions or references to him are made in the book. He is also seen in the script for the film.
