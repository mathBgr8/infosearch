A native of the planet Caprioril, Shren Whist was the planet's Proconsul during the Separatist Crisis, a growing movement which plagued the Galactic Republic Senate. During this time the Ratts Tyerell Foundation were successful in their lobbying efforts to ban podracing on the planet; Whist personally addressed the populace and announced the closing of the Doolis Podrace Arena.
