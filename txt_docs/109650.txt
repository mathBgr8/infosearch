Vol Hamame
1458

Vol Hamame was a Human male who made his living as a thug and was once a member of Big Gizz's swoop gang before seeking greater profits with Phedroi during the Galactic Civil War. After intercepting a message about the survival of Boba Fett, the pair followed Dengar&mdash;who at the time was overseeing the Mandalorian's recuperation&mdash;out across the Dune Sea in hopes of eliminating the weakened Fett.
