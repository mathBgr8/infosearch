John Nadeau
5279

}} John Nadeau is a comic book artist who did pencils and covers for Dark Horse Comics from 1996 to 2000. He drew interiors and covers for a number of issues of Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron, as well as for miscellaneous other Star Wars comics. Boba Fett: Twin Engines of Destruction, which he worked on, was voted "Best Original Star Wars Comic" by readers of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. Nadeau landed his first professional gig while still a senior in High School, drawing for Americomics' Best of the West. He has also worked for Marvel and DC, and he has a degree in Film Production Technology from the University of Central Florida. Recently, Nadeau has continued drawing comics while also working as a commercial artist and architectural renderer.
