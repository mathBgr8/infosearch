Fel II
1861

Fel II was the second Galactic Emperor of the galaxy under the Fel dynasty. Galactic Emperor Fel II was the son of Jagged Fel, the father of Roan Fel, the grandfather of Marasiah Fel and the uncle of Mohrgan Fel.
