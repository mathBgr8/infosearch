Tralla Room
1257

thumb|right|200px|The Tralla Room The Tralla Room was one of eight identically impressive gambling rooms on the Coral Vanda, named for the Tralla continent found on the planet Pantolomin. Its three half-levels contained over a dozen sabacc tables, along with numerous other locations to play lugjack, tregald, holochess and crinbid. A long bar ran down the middle of the room so that alcohol was readily available to those who wished to celebrate or forget their time in the lavish casino. Han Solo and Lando Calrissian went to gamble in this room during the Thrawn campaign in order to find Captain Hoffner. *Dark Force Rising *Dark Force Rising 6 *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Casinos Category:Starship rooms
