Lullaby Motel
539

The Lullaby Motel was a sleazy establishment on the planet Harridan, just outside Spaceport City's spaceport's bulk cargo gate. Two Rebels posing as newlyweds Henry Buntwhistle and Martha Buntwhistle once signed into this motel in order to get in contact with Egot Pai-4-Yem. Category:Hotels
