It might have been ruled by the trade monarch. It had close ties with the Trade Federation, whose Commanding Viceroy, if a Neimoidian, served as the leader of the Inner Circle. The Inner Circle was supposedly subordinate to the trade monarch. Nute Gunray was the only known leader of the Inner Circle, though presuming it was still in operation at the time, after the end on the clone wars, and Nute Gunray's murder at the hands of Darth Vader the inner circle was most likely led by Acting Viceroy, Sentepeth Findos. *Cloak of Deception *The New Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Legislatures Inner Circle Category:Trade Federation organizations
