Silentium's creators
2496

thumb|right|250px|[[Vuffi Raa resembled the form of the organic progenitors of the Silentium.]] The Silentium's creators were an extra-galactic species of sentient star-shaped organics who created the sentient droid species known as the Silentium. They were possibly one of the native species of the Yuuzhan Vong galaxy. Their name, homeworld and history have been long lost, with their creations being their only legacy. At an unknown time, the species was wiped out by a radiation storm from a nearby supernova, though their droid creations "lived" on. The species were known to have been advanced. Their droid creations resembled their star-shaped form, in much the same way that humanoid sentients created bipedal droids. Thus there is implication that Vuffi Raa's progenitors may have been the makers of the Sharu.
