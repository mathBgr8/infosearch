|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=1 BBY |series=Star Wars: Empire |issue=3 }} Empire 3 is the third issue in the Star Wars: Empire series of comics. It contains the story "Betrayal, Part 3," and it was published on November 13, 2002 by Dark Horse Comics. In part three of "Betrayal," Darth Vader has landed on a distant world to track down a mysterious Jedi. But, instead of a Jedi Knight, he is confronted by a cadre of bounty hunters who have come to the conclusion that ganging up on&mdash;and defeating&mdash;the Sith Lord will bring them fame and fortune. Boy, are they in for a wake-up call! Meanwhile, on Coruscant, the ranks of those plotting to kill Emperor Palpatine are thinned as the co-conspirators begin targeting each other.
