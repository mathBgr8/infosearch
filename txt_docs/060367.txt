Pizzazz 2
1059

Pizzazz 2 is the second issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated November 1977 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Keeper's World. This issue included an advertisement and order form for Marvel Special Edition Featuring Star Wars 1 and Marvel Special Edition Featuring Star Wars 2. 02
