Docking Bay 87 (Mos Eisley)
1605

Docking Bay 87 was a docking bay in the city of Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was situated across from Docking Bay 86. It was the same size as most bays, but had been modernized and thus became one of the favorite docking bays in Mos Eisley. The standard fee was thirty credits a day.
