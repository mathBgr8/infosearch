Friends of Paran
824

The Friends of Paran were a resistance group on Derilyn during the Galactic Civil War. The protocol droid N2-3PO was a member. After contact with the Alliance to Restore the Republic via the smuggler Shondra Del, the Friends of Paran took into consideration aligning with the Alliance. *Operation: Elrood *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection *Ultimate Adversaries Category:Resistance groups
