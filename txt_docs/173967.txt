By the time of the Clone Wars, it was the site of several urban centers. A Separatist group was believed to be hiding in one of the cities during that conflict. As a result, during the first year of the war, Ordo, a Null-class Advanced Recon Commando of the Grand Army of the Galactic Republic, was sent to the planet to assault the city. The ensuing battle was highly intense, with Ordo almost completely destroying the city single-handedly in order to achieve his mission objectives. During a counterterrorism operation on the Core Worlds planetThe Essential Atlas Coruscant in 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Republic Commando: Triple Zero, which include the counterterrorism operation on Coruscant, to 22 BBY. Ordo discussed his mission to N'dian with the clone commando Sev. N'dian was first mentioned in the 2006 novel Republic Commando: Triple Zero, written by Karen Traviss.
