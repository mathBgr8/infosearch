Hes Jor-Sharu
1696

Hes Jor-Sharu was a Human male Jedi Knight of the New Jedi Order. He discovered information about Phantom Squadron in the Jedi Archives on Coruscant. Jor-Sharu later tracked down Col Serra, the squadron's commander, and asked him about Phantom Squadron. Serra told him about the squadron's exploits, including the Evacuation of Yavin. Hes Jor-Sharu was originally intended to appear in the video game Star Wars Battlefront: Renegade Squadron.
