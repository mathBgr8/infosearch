Ithorian Supremacy League

The Ithorian Supremacy League was mentioned by Lassavou on Nar Shaddaa, in the Docks sector. His accidental mention of it, and his subsequent attempt to cover it up, leads to speculation that it was a secret organization formed to attack perceived views of Ithorian weakness. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Supremacy League Category:Political factions
