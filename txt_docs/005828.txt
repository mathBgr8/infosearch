Detonation pack
1538

thumb|right|200px|Detonation packs were useful for destroying equipment and breaking through doors. A detonation pack (also called det pack or a detpack) was a small explosive with a remote activator that could be triggered by the user. The pack was set in place or thrown, and then detonated whenever the user desired. Typically, this was used to ambush enemies or blow open sealed doors. Some detonation packs were equipped with a three-second timer device. Detonation packs were employed by the clone commandos during the Clone Wars to blow up heavy droid installations during raiding and boarding missions.
