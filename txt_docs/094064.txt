Fungus ale
1105

Fungus ale was a common alcoholic beverage brewed on the planet Ryloth. It was often served as an accompanyment to rycrit stew and munch-fungus-based dishes. Fungus ale was available at Mal'aa's Kitchens in Kala'uun Starport. Fungus ale was first introduced to the Star Wars universe in the Star Wars roleplaying game book Platt's Starport Guide, written by Peter M. Schweighofer and published by West End Games in February 1995. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Ales Category:Twi'lek food
