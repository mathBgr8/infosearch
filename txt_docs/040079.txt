Bahima
1548

Bahima was a male Bith bartender in the Iziz cantina during the Onderon Civil War. In 3951 BBY, he owned S-0D3-GE3, and assisted Meetra Surik during her investigation of Captain Sullio's murder. According to some of his patrons, Bahima had an ever-growing problem with keeping his droids activated and on his property.
