Toklar (Mon Calamari)
1550

Toklar was a famous Mon Calamari philosopher. He was active seven centuries before the Yuuzhan Vong War. Admiral Gial Ackbar took Toklar's philosophies to heart, and found the following particularly inspirational: :"Greed has no limits, envy no boundaries, in the heart of a petty man." :"One sting is remembered longer than a thousand caresses." :"The tide will slacken, the whirlpool will shrink, the krakana will cower.
