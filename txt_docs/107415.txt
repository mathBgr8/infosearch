Organoform circuitry
613

Organoform circuitry was circuitry used in Sekotan starships to interface between the living ship and its mechanical parts. For hundreds of years before 29 BBY, due to its rarity, this technology was only rumored to exist. The circuitry used in the Jabitha, Anakin Skywalker's ship, was a delicate crystalline structure. *Rogue Planet *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Biotechnology
