Bracha e'Naso
961

Bracha e'Naso once ran Esau's Ridge. After his boss, Plothis, was shot and killed by a customer, he took over Plothis' business. During the Black Fleet Crisis he tried to cheat Chewbacca in a business deal, though he lost and allowed Chewbacca to name his price. *Tyrant's Test *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Smugglers
