Otis Frampton
3491

Otis Frampton is a comic book writer and illustrator who has contributed 406 sketch cards to the Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith trading card collection and created content for StarWars.com, including two Star Wars Kids: Learn To Draw tutorials (Yoda and Salacious Crumb) and an Emperor Palpatine Halloween mask. He is also an artist on the YouTube series and has worked on several of the Star Wars parodies that the series has produced.
