Kee-Piru
1236

Kee-Piru was a floating city on Mon Calamari, located near the Mester Reef in the southern territorial zone. Along with Heurkea, it took much of the World Devastator damage during the Battle of Calamari, resulting in a minimum of tens of thousands of deaths.
