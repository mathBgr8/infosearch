*Terrorist organizationThe New Essential Chronology *Separatist movement |founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters=Afarathu Cave |locations=Corellian system |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved=380 BBY |restored=By 1 ABY |affiliation= }} Afarathu was a sect of Selonians extremists that terrorized the Corellian system some four centuries before the Battle of Endor. }} The Selonians of the Afarathu terrorist sect marauded the Corellian system four centuries before the Battle of Yavin. Their objective was to drive the Dralls and the Humans from the worlds of the Corellian system because they considered them to be their home worlds. The sect was finally destroyed by the Corellian Jedi Keiran Halcyon, in 380 BBY. In the next centuries, the Afarathu sect was all but forgotten until the Galactic Empire used its specter to incite xenophobic reactions among the Human population of the Corellian system. However, despite their destruction, the Afarathu cult rose from the ashes prior to the Galactic Civil War and was particularly active on Corellia after the Battle of Yavin. In 1 ABY, a Selonian diplomat traveled to the Corellian city of Doaba Guerfel in order to recruit a spacer to fight the Afarathu extremists at their cave nearby.
