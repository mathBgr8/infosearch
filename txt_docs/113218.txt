Firestar 10-K
1325

The Firestar 10-K was a carbine weapon. Oddly, it was once found in the stomach of a creature known as SteelClaw in the Dead Forest on Kashyyyk. After the Firestar 10-K was found, they went through mass production. According to Boba Fett, the weapon has a good balance of firepower, accuracy, and weight.
