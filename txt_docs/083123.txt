Gobindi (species)
1004

The Gobindi were the native people of the planet Gobindi. They were master architects, responsible for the huge ziggurats found in the deep jungles of the planet. These ziggurats were built to contain a deadly virus the Gobindi had discovered in the jungle. Despite this, the Gobindi were completely wiped out by the Gobindi virus. They managed to create an antidote, but it was too late save them.
