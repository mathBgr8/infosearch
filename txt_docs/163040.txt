Phrusaani
963

Phrusaani was a Chevin who operated the Docking Bay bar on the Life Level on Vergesso Base. A fan of Bith musicians, he provided Modal Nodes holos in his cantina. He tried to stay out of the business of his customers and the deals that took place in the booths of his place. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Bartenders Category:Chevin Category:Males
