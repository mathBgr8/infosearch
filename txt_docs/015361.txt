In the world's lush jungle lived a rare subsentient species of insectoids that shared their name with their homeworld. Shikitari were born in clusters of eggs suspended in a poisonous protective gel that warded off predators. As the eggs hatched, the developing pupae fused together, growing into a single combined lifeform. The pupae developed a body structure that mimicked their guardian. However, this stage of their life cycle was extremely short, and the unified organism later became encased in a hardened chrysalis, from which emerged a dozen harmless creatures. The 2006 reference book The New Essential Guide to Droids mentioned a «Shikitari insectoid»; nevertheless, it did not confirm which specific character was being referred to. Shortly later, the online supplement to the reference book The Essential Atlas introduced the Shikitari system, located in the Unknown Regions. The planet Shikitari, in particular, was first and last mentioned in the Databank entry for the character Carl on the official Star Wars website, StarWars.com, in 2011.
