AAP-II blaster box
956

thumb|right|250px|[[BL-17 using a AAP-II blaster box.]] The AAP-II blaster box was a model of AAP blaster box manufactured by Briletto Company and utilized by BL-series Battle Legionnaires. It was a rectangular weapon specifically created for use by droids, mounted on the Battle Legionnaire's chest or held in front of the body. It was a modestly powerful weapon much smaller than its successor, the AAP-IV blaster box. *A Race to the Finish picture book *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:AAP blaster box models
