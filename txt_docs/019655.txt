|schedule= |format= |era= |timeline=31 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Republic |issues= |preceded by=Emissaries to Malastare |followed by=Infinity's End }} Star Wars: Twilight was a story arc in the comic book series Star Wars: Republic. It consisted of issues 19-22. Jedi Knight Quinlan Vos has lost his memory. Not only must he fight to rediscover his past, he must also track down his Padawan, Aayla Secura, who has mysteriously disappeared. With only his lightsaber and a scheming Devaronian named Villie to help him, Quin must face off against deadly gamblers, false Jedi, corrupt officials, and the pull of the dark side if he is to survive!
