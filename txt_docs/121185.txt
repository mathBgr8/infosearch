Talmog
596

A Talmog was an alcoholic drink common during the Imperial era. It consisted of one part spiced ethanol and one part Lyme's rose juice. It was reportedly popular in a sunless, centerless system and was known for creating a burning feeling when consumed. Lando Calrissian hated them, but would order one while playing sabacc so he would not overdrink and muddle his wits. *Lando Calrissian and the Mindharp of Sharu Category:Cocktails
