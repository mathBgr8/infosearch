Vrassh Slaughter
1670

The Vrassh Slaughter was a large-scale slaughter of the Vrassh by the Pas'sic. By 19 ABY, neither the New Republic nor the Pas'sic government had compensated the survivors. *Specter of the Past Category:Battles in the Oricho sector Category:Genocides and massacres
