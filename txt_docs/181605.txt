Satna-chakka
590

The Satna-chakka was a Myneyrsh bond of peace used during meetings between two groups who in other situations would be violent to one another. The Myneyrsh used this when Luke Skywalker and Han Solo were traveling the forests of Wayland trying to destroy Mount Tantiss. The bond of peace consisted of a killed bird. *The Last Command Category:Myneyrsh culture
