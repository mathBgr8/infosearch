Kim Yeasson
1518

Kim Yeasson was the Junior Technician for the Rebel Alliance's Reginard Base during the Galactic Civil War. She supervised the X-wing technical crews and was a capable X-wing technician as well, often being found on the flight line repairing a starfighter. She had roughly cut blonde hair and a greasy technician's work suit which gave her a rugged look. She was known to have had a relationship with Chief Technician Adonar Dellox which ended a month before his death. When his body was found, she had been repairing X-wing's on the flight line, though she soon arrived at the body. When Doctor Akimbo informed everyone to whom the body belonged, she rushed out of the room crying.
