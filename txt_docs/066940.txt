Kenn Nitram
3465

}} Kenn Nitram was a young X-wing pilot recruited by Wedge Antilles. Nitram was a fan of swoop racing, and attended a race at the Imperial Arena on Coruscant with Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo. He believed that the swoop pilot Fargus Deel was unbeatable, even though Solo had beat Deel consistently in his youth, and strongly believed he could do so again. Nitram flew as Rogue Three in the famed New Republic squadron Rogue Squadron, participating in the Battle for the Katana fleetDark Force Rising in the year 9 ABY.The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle for the Katana fleet to 9 ABY. Kenn Nitram created by Bill Slavicsek for the short story "A Day at the Races," which first appeared in 1992's Dark Force Rising Sourcebook. The story simply named him as an X-wing pilot and "one of Wedge Antilles's new recruits," but 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia (misspelling his name as Kenn Tiram) established that he was Rogue Three.
