Thoadeye-style makeup
5382

thumb|right|200px|Wilst Molan wore Thoadeye-style makeup. Thoadeye-style makeup was a style of makeupStar Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary that originated from the Thoadeye system in the Deep Core.The Essential Atlas It consisted of dark lines and cosmetic beadwork surrounding one of the wearer's eyes. During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. the Human woman Wilst Molan" wore the makeup style to the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planet Coruscant. While the makeup and her outfit were striking, it did not match the charisma of the outfit of her acquaintance Nyrat Agira, a woman who Molan vied with for the unofficial title of "best-dressed Outlander patron." thumb|right|200px|Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary provided a name for thoadeye-style makeup. Thoadeye-style makeup first appeared in the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002.Star Wars Year by Year: A Visual Chronicle The makeup was first pictured before the film's release in the documentary "A Twinkle Beyond Pluto," released on July 31, 2001, and first identified in the reference book Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary, written by David West Reynolds and released on April 23, 2002. The ninety-sixth issue of The Official Star Wars Fact File magazine, written by Iain Lowson and released on October 29, 2003, provided the capitalization of the makeup, but spelled it as "thoadeye-style make-up." In the article "Who's Who in the Outlander Club: It's Ladies' Night," written by Daniel Wallace and published in the seventy-fifth issue of Star Wars Insider on April 6, 2004, the makeup is incorrectly spelled as "thodeye style." *Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars Chronicles: The Prequels *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Ultimate Illustrated Guide Category:Fashion styles
