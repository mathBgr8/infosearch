Vulkar shock stick
1028

The Vulkar shock stick was a basic stun baton, charged at a higher frequency to make it cause decent physical damage. It was carried primarily by members of the Black Vulkars on Taris around the time of the Jedi Civil War. It focused electrical pulses around the baton, thereby causing intense wounds. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Stun batons
