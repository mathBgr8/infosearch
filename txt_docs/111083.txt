Marl-class heavy freighter
1646

The Marl-class heavy freighter was created by Kuat Drive Yards. The design was a blocky drive connected to a long series of detachable storage pods. The ship was headed up by a narrow habitation compartment. Tinmolok was a Marl-class heavy freighter used in the Yuuzhan Vong War first by the Peace Brigade and later by the New Jedi Order. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook Category:Freighter classes Category:Kuat Drive Yards products
