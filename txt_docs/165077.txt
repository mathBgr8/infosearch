Dark Jedi Master robe
1246

thumb|right|250px|A style of Dark Jedi Master robes common during the [[Sith Civil War.]] Dark Jedi Master robes were worn by those who had ascended the ranks of the Dark Jedi, those whose will and authority went unopposed in the era surrounding the Jedi and Sith Civil Wars. These black robes provided excellent protection while still offering the user full freedom of movement. These robes look very similar to its lower counterpart, the Dark Jedi Knight Robe, but did have different mechanics like the Master robe offered a little more defense to the wearer than the Knight Robe did. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Robes and cloaks
