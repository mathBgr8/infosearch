thumb|right|200px|[[Stagorr gazes into his ice mirror.]] The ice mirror was a Force-imbued object used by the Dark Wizard Stagorr on the Forest Moon of Endor. It was probably formed by Stagorr following his imprisonment in Ice Mountain. He used the mirror as his sole portal to the outside world. The special abilities of the object allowed Stagorr to witness events all over the moon, such as things happening in Bright Tree Village. After Stagorr's death, the ice mirror remained in Ice Mountain without an owner. *Ewoks (1985) 6 Category:Force-sensitive objects
