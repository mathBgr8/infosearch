Kuati Security Forces
2047

thumb|250px|A Kuati Security team. The Kuati Security Forces was the police force of the planet of Kuat. Their organizational purpose was to stop crime on Kuat and maintain security in the Kuat system.Coruscant and the Core Worlds They patrolled the various orbiting shipyards and outlying spaceport stations. Some were implicated in dealings with Neimoidian agents shortly before the Battle of Geonosis. Upon the creation of the Galactic Empire, Kuat Drive Yards found themselves busy with the construction fleets of Imperial Warships, and the Kuati Security Forces were augmented and Imperialized to increase security and ensure the warship-production.
