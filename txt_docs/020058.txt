*Max Rebo Band |publisher= |release date= |media type=Song |pages= |published in= |genre=Jizz }} "Galactic Dance Blast" was a jizz song recorded by Evar Orbus and His Galactic Jizz-Wailers. Following the death of bandleader Evar Orbus, many of the surviving band members formed the Max Rebo Band and continued to perform it. It is notable for never getting an official release due to its master tape being lost after its usage on the documentary From Star Wars to Jedi: The Making of a Saga. Some unfinished versions of the song are on YouTube. Because the original music is lost, it is not known if these two snippets are from the same song, or if there are two forms of music with the same title. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Songs by the Max Rebo Band
