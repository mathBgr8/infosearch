Dual Cannon
789

The Dual Cannon was a weapon installed in the Arakyd Industries security droid Zeeo. It fired large waves of energy and was capable of killing multiple enemies, consuming large amounts of Zeeo's energy in the process. thumb|right|Rianna Saren uses the dual cannon The Dual Cannon appears in the Nintendo DS version of the game Star Wars: Lethal Alliance. To use it, players must first achieve the Maximum Alliance status by using Zeeo to help Rianna defeat her foes. *Star Wars: Lethal Alliance Category:Arakyd Industries products Category:Blaster cannons
