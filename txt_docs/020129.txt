Corellian heavy lifter
1559

The Corellian heavy lifter was a transport vessel designed by the Rebel Alliance to haul cargo. It was roughly based around the shape of a CR90 corvette, which had its central section modified to make room for two heavy-duty clamps, which could grab and hold large cargo-modules for transport to battlefields. *Star Wars: Force Commander Category:Corellian Engineering Corporation products Category:Freighter classes Category:Starship classes of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
