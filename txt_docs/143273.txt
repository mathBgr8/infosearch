Luxe Flightwing
1564

The Luxe Flightwing was a limited-production, swift, gleaming white starship. The Flightwing consisted of a curved nose and swept-back wings, having such a unique profile that it was nearly unforgettable to those who saw it. Its exterior surface was made of a rare, white ore. In 24 BBY, dangerous convict Jenna Zan Arbor, recently escaped from the planet Vanqor,Zan Arbor escaped at the conclusion of the Jedi Mission to Typha-Dor. purchased a Luxe Flightwing, which enabled Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker to track down the deranged scientist through her singularly uncommon ship-purchase to the planet Romin. *Jedi Quest: The Changing of the Guard Category:Starship classes
