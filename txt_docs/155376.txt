Unidentified Ortolan merchant
1390

An Ortolan merchant was a salesman on the planet Dantooine. After the Rebellion relocated to Hoth following their evacuation of Yavin 4, Princess Leia Organa and Luke Skywalker arrived on Dantooine to secure supplies for the base. The Ortolan Merchant was under strict orders to only sell to those who told him a specific codeword.
