Pit racing
1683

thumb|right|250px|Pit racing prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars. Pit racing was a popular&mdash;yet illegal&mdash;sport practiced in the garbage pits of Coruscant. Contestants competed in unique, individually-made pit racers, gliders in which they descended the garbage pits to the lowest levels, where they collected scales from the backs of garbage worms. The sport was highly dangerous and subject to Judicial raids, since garbage canisters, regularly ejected from the pits into orbit, could kill the competitors. Pit racing had been practiced since approximately 129 BBY.Rogue Planet mentions that the sport was invented "perhaps a century before" 29 BBY. Successful pit racers included Reymet Autem and Anakin Skywalker, who frequented the Wicko District garbage pits.
