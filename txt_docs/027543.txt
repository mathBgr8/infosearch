Assigned to Rogue Squadron, the vehicle was stationed at the Alliance's Echo Base on the ice planet Hoth. Rogue 4 was assigned to be piloted by Derek "Hobbie" Klivian alongside gunner Kesin Ommis. The laser cannons on Rogue 4 were modified, giving the weapon an improved recharge rate. During the Battle of Hoth, Rogue 4 was part of the Alliance's snowspeeder counterattack against the Galactic Empire's force of All Terrain Armored Transports. Rogue 4 flew as Rogue Leader's wingman during the battle. Category:Snowspeeders
