SLD-14 shield generator
1926

The SLD-14 shield generator was a kind of shield generator, created by CoMar Combat Systems, that was used by both the Alliance to Restore the Republic and the Galactic Empire. It produced a blue energy shield around a base that protected it from energy blasts and orbital bombardments. It did not however, prevent solid objects such as infantry, vehicles, or missiles, from entering the barrier, however it could be upgraded to stop missiles. Like all other shield generators, it required a power generator to stay operational.
