Scrumma Jawbone
1764

The Scrumma Jawbone was a shallow arm of Lake Paonga on the planet Naboo. General Tobler Ceel of the Gungan Grand Army established a training center for his militiagungs in it, where they could practice using projectile weapons such as the arbalest, atlatl, and booma. The targeting system at the training consisted of methane-filled gasbags and floating bull's eyes that could dive in and out of the water on command. The creatures in the surrounding swamps of the Scrumma Jawbone knew to avoid the training center when militiagungs were practicing. The Scrumma Jawbone was mentioned in The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology, a 2004 reference book by W.
