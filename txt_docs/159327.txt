Drake Lo'gaan's airspeeder
1378

This airspeeder was used by Drake Lo'gaan and Ekria on Coruscant following the Battle of Felucia. It was fitted with a holoprojector. Lo'gaan piloted this speeder to landing platform NL1922 where he confronted Darth Vader.
