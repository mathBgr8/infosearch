The Authority Guardian Corridor hyperlane connected Tothis to the Atchorb and Kir systems. Musicians Greeata Jendowanian and Sy Snootles worked for a time at the nightclub Green Planet on Tothis. They were the only employees there who were not addicted to spice, and in the final years of the Clone Wars, a conflict that lasted from 22 BBY to 19 BBY, musician Evar Orbus visited the Green Planet while looking for members to join his band, ultimately hiring both Jendowanian and Snootles. Tothis was first mentioned in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003.
