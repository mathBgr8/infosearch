Trux Zissu
1439

Trux Zissu was to be instated as governor on Delantine by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. He was accompanied by his son Stuart Zissu, 4B-X, R2-D2, and C-3PO from Yavin 4 in the Timespan. While traveling, the Timespan was ambushed and boarded by stormtroopers. Trux shot the first two stormtroopers and surrendered in order to give the droids and Stuart time to escape in escape pod 4.
