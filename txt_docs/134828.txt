Kothtri
1496

Kothtri was a traditional type of spiced fruit juice that originated on Bothawui, a Mid Rim planet that was the homeworld of the Bothan species.Ultimate Alien Anthology The Bothans, who found the beverage enjoyable, used "Kothtri" as a first name for some female members. Kothtri was first mentioned in the Galactic Campaign Guide, a 2003 sourcebook for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game, written by Peter Schweighofer and J.D. Wiker. The fruit juice was only described in a table of Bothan female names. *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Bothan food Category:Juices
