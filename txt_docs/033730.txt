|feathers= |eyes=Black |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Planet Dreams, Incorporated |masters= |apprentices= }} Wiorkettle was a Snivvian on Cloud City during Lando Calrissian's administration. He worked for Planet Dreams, Incorporated, a company that terraformed asteroids and inhospitable planets and used them to create habitable atmospheres. Wiorkettle was Planet Dreams' chief artist, responsible for the aesthetics of the environments they created. He was an equal partner in the company along with the Lutrillian Treva Horme and Ozz the Ugnaught. In approximately 2 ABY, the partners relocated their head offices to Bespin's Cloud City. The three Planet Dreams executives were considering selling private hovering platforms in Bespin's Life Zone when Ozz revealed that someone was embezzling money and terraforming equipment from the company. Ozz vowed to investigate but was interrupted in 3 ABY when the Galactic Empire arrived and took control of the city. The partners decided to download all of their files and evacuate, but while Horme and Wiorkettle followed the plan, Ozz disappeared.
