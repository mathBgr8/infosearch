Buena Vista Records
2143

Buena Vista Records, was the publisher of various book-and-record and book-and-tape sets and audio dramatizations, including many Star Wars film adaptations and other titles. The record label, headquartered in Burbank, California, fell under the collective label of the Disney Music Group. *The Story of Star Wars *The Story of Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back *The Story of Star Wars: Return of the Jedi *Star Wars *The Empire Strikes Back *The Further Adventures: Droid World *The Further Adventures: Planet of the Hoojibs *Return of the Jedi *The Ewoks Join the Fight *The Ewok Adventure *Ewoks: The Battle for Endor *Rebel Mission to Ord Mantell *Star Wars Adventures in Colors and Shapes *Star Wars Adventures in ABC Category:Subsidiaries of the Walt Disney Company
