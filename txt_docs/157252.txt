Z-Oz Group
817

Oz was a pair of Assassin-class corvettes sent to loot ZA-13 by Grand Admiral Zaarin. They carried hyperdrive units that could be placed into TIE Avengers. However they were disabled by Tan Maarek Stele, then boarded by Imperial boarding parties to recover the cargo, before being destroyed. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter: Defender of the Empire *TIE Fighter: Defender of the Empire: Official Secrets & Solutions *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Zaarin military units
