Rainbow qaana
1395

Rainbow qaana were a species of insects that were bred and used by the Yuuzhan Vong. They were kept for the ethereal music that they produced when clicking their mandibles together and were only used in special ceremonies, or in the presence of the Supreme Overlord. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Insects Category:Musical instruments Category:Species of undefined sentience Category:Yuuzhan Vong creatures
