It was the site of a former base for pirates operating in the sector. Once, the entire planet was single-handedly taken over by the gunman Gallandro during one of his early exploits. After the death of King Alaric the whole sector including this planet, left the Republic and joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Above the planet, Red Squadron and Blue Squadron manning Ghorin's stolen Y-wings, destroyed a convoy consisting of seven CR90 corvettes from Geedon group that were to supply the Empire with food, according to Operation Grain Snatch.
