Malio Preserve Units
560

The Malio Preserve Units were facilities that contain alpha, beta, secondary, and tertiary copies of every creature or being the Kaminoans ever cloned, bred, intended to clone, or examined. The Preserve Units were attached to Tipoca City, but could be separated and isolated in case of a breakout and to protect the samples from each other. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Tipoca City locations
