Upon becoming trapped in the planet's gravity, Sivvi was often referred to simply as the Thule Moon. Approximately 4,000 years before the Battle of Yavin, the Sith established a military training facility on the moon, using Sivvi's natural caves and tunnels to practice zero-gravity maneuvers. During the Clone Wars, Sivvi housed a planetary shield generator to protect a base established by the Confederacy of Independent Systems on Thule.
