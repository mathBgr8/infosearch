Star Wars: The Old Republic Encyclopedia |owners=*Tulak Hord *Darth Revan |locations=Tomb of Tulak Hord, Korriban |cost= |value= |structure= |size= |color= |material= |length= |width= |height= |weight= |protection= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose=Allows the wearer to move in secrecy |heritage=Sith |affiliation=Old Sith Empire }} Tulak Hord's mask was a Sith artifact that belonged to the Sith Lord Tulak Hord. Gold-colored, the mask consisted of two pieces on either side of the wearer's face that ran from the ear to the chin, and it allowed those who were steeped in the dark side of the Force to move in secrecy. The mask was discovered by the former Sith Lord Darth Revan between 3960&ndash;3959 BBY when he pillaged the tomb of Tulak Hord on the planet Korriban.
