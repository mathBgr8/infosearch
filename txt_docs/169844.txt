Red Nebula comet field

The Red Nebula comet field was a dangerous barrier that ringed the Red Nebula. It was formed when the Nebula was separated from the rest of the galaxy. Its presence lead to the devastation of the Planet of the Red Nebula. *Star Wars (1977) 50 Category:Comets Category:Red Nebula locations
