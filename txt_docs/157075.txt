Oqa membrane
769

thumb|right|200px|People from the [[Heart of Artorias enter the Dibrook Space Station through an oqa membrane.]] Oqa membranes were tubes used by the Yuuzhan Vong to connect two ships in space, for transfer between them. They were the equivalent of cofferdams and were made from microbial mats.
