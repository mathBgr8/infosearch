Twomi Skyfire
426

The Twomi Skyfire was a kind of ship that had the engines of a racer, the lines of a starfighter and designed to make the occasional pilot comfortable at the controls. They came with automatic landing, navigation, crash and collision avoidance overrides and voice assisted preflight. The Jump For Joy was a Twomi Skyfire. *Tyrant's Test Category:Starship classes
