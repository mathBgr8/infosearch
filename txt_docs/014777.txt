After the Great Galactic War, Erigorm was one of the planets that threatened secession from the Republic, as part of the Rift Alliance. By that time, it had recorded and administrated the Republic's finances for centuries. The planet's representative in the Alliance council was Augin Blaesus, secretly a Child of the Emperor.
