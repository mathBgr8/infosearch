Zoronhed
1192

Zoronhed was one of the largest settlements established on the planet Zonju V. During the Disciples of Ragnos crisis, a spaceport worker on the planet had information pertaining to the Disciples of Ragnos and its connection with the Imperial Remnant. The worker was hiding at an abandoned outpost three kilometers outside of Zoronhed. Jaden Korr traveled to meet up with the worker and find out what he had to say, but was hindered by swoop mercenaries in the process, resulting in the assassination of the associate before he could divulge anything of use to the Jedi. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy Category:Cities
