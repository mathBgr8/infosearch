R7 droid brain matrix
523

The R7 droid brain matrix was a component of the R7-series astromech droid. Pirated versions were used in Imperial XR-85 tank droids; however, this modified sentient version of the droid brain proved very costly for mass production. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid brains
