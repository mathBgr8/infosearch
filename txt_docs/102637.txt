Seedar Bomu
1595

Seedar Bomu was a male Rodian soldier and a member of the Bomu Clan. Seedar worked for Vago the Hutt and the Anjiliac kajidic, along with a number of his clan during the reign of the Galactic Empire. He and another Bomu soldier, Narnee Bomu, was hired by Vago to find a treacherous partner of Popara who had stolen one hundred thousand credits in spice. Vago, not completely trusting the Bomus, put tracker devices in their ship.
