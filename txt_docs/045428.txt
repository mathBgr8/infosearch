Nutmeat
600

Nutmeat was a food paste made from crushed ettel nuts. It was commonly served as a separate course during meals. Nutmeat was sweet, nutritious and high in calories. Because of this last property, it was occasionally part of a soldier's survival rations. *Tales of the Jedi 1 *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Category:Edible nuts and seeds
