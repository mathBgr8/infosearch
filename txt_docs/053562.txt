Domain Hul
719

Domain Hul was a domain among the warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Domain Hul was a very powerful Domain, so much that it had its own worldship. Many warriors of Domain Hul took part in the Battle of Borleias, where their worldship was lost. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines I: Rebel Dream *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Hul
