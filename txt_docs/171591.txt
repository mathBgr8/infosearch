Electra
2459

Electra was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle that saw service with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Electra entered the Pakuuni system near XQ1 Platform NL-1 in the final Rebel engagement against the Galactic Empire during the Battle on the Frontier. Heavy fighting had erupted between, then Vice Admiral, Thrawn's task force consisting of Nebulon-B2 frigate Ludwick, Platform NL-1, IPV-1 Patrol Craft Ranger, and various starfighters. Maarek Stele was among Imperial pilots fighting for the Empire during the engagement, breaking off to inspect Electra, revealing the Rebel Advisors onboard.
