Laerdocian
1538

The Laerdocians were a species who spoke Basic with an unusual accent (pronouncing "nothing" as "nothjing", for example.) A Laerdocian named Bodwae served under the smuggler Booster Terrik as a technician on the Errant Venture circa 19 ABY. Star Wars: The Essential Atlas Online Companion, a supplement to The Essential Atlas, mentions a Laerdocia system.
