Kidriff 5
2486

|imports= |exports= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Zsinj's Empire }} Kidriff 5, or Kidriff Five, was the most coreward world in Zsinj's space. It was a wealthy trade depot along several hyperroutes. It developed heavy alloys used to improve TIE hulls. Its gleaming cityscapes were similar to those of Coruscant. It had blue seas and rusty red forests.
