Rules of Engagement
1104

The Rules of Engagement were the Jedi's rules for engaging an enemy in combat. A Padawan was required to learn and exercise these rules before they were allowed to obtain the rank of Jedi Knight. Qui-Gon Jinn tested Obi-Wan Kenobi on these rules during the Dorvalla Incident. Known Rules of Engagement are listed below: *A Jedi uses the Force and the lightsaber only for defense, never for Attack. *Understand the dark and light in all things. *Learn to see accurately. *Open your eyes to what is not evident. *Exercise caution, even in trivial manners. *Cloak of Deception *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Doctrines Category:Jedi manuals and instruction guides
