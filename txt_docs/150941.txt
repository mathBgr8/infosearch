Shanda Dok
1442

Shanda Dok was a colonel in the Rebel Alliance. During the subjugation of Fitca Prime by Imperial Intelligence interrogator Mox Slosin, Dok took part in the first Alliance support wave on Fitca Prime. Unfortunately, she was captured and interrogated by Slosin. She refused to betray the Alliance, and thus the torture continued until her death.
