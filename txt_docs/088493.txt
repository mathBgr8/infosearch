Jedi Monument (disambiguation)
520

*Jedi Monument (New Holstice), was a monument that commemorated each Jedi that had died since the birth of the Old Republic using Memory moths. *Jedi monument (Ohma-D'un), was a bubble building that was constructed to immortalize the efforts Obi-Wan Kenobi put into colonizing New Otoh Gunga. *Jedi monument (Rhen Var), was an ancient Jedi temple in which Ulic Qel-Droma was buried.
