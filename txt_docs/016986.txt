Zhat Lah
1611

Zhat Lah was a relative of Tsavong Lah and a Yuuzhan Vong commander. He retreated his forces from the battle at Fondor in the face of an attack launched by overwhelming Galactic Alliance forces, an unusual action for Yuuzhan Vong warriors, who typically fought to the death.
