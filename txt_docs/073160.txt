Mission to the Executor
3203

The Mission to the Executor was a minor conflict during the Galactic Civil War. After boarding the Executor with a smuggler ship stolen from the Imperial Fuel Station Ergo, Kyle Katarn made his way through inner defenses of the Star Destroyer. After traversing two arenas housing three Phase II dark troopers, Katarn made his way to the shuttle bay. He operated the controls to guide a shipping container to the launch bay, which then he boarded through the airlock. The container then left to dock to the Arc Hammer.
