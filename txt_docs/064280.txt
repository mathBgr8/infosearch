Redheart
1218

The Redheart was a Duros-crewed warship operated by the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances during the later stages of the Yuuzhan Vong War. Redheart was part of a task force commanded by General Wedge Antilles during an operation over Duro in 29 ABY. It was one of several ships lost when a portion of Antilles' fleet and some local Duros resistance fighters refused an order to fall back. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
