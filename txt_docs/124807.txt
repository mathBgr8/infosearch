Triumph (corvette group)
1841

Triumph was a group of two CR90 corvettes belonging to the Rebel Alliance. They were sent to try and destroy the Immobilizer 418 cruiser Harpax along with three B-wing starfighters and two Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttles.
