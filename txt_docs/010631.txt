Liberation Lake
876

Liberation Lake was a lake in Unity Green on the planet Coruscant, five years after the end of the Yuuzhan Vong war. Its rolling whitecaps were home to yammal-jells and coufee eels; favored prey of the native hawk-bat. The lake also contained y'luubi, which Mara Jade Skywalker enjoyed at a dinner meeting held by Aryn Thul at Yuza Bre in 36 ABY.
