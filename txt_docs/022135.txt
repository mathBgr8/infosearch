Devaronian Army
1158

The Devaronian Army defended the Devaronian homeworld of Devaron. Kardue'sai'Malloc, later known as the drunkard Labria in Mos Eisley, was a captain in the Devaronian Army, following sixteen generations of family tradition. The Devaronian Army suppressed a rebellion on Devaron early in the reign of Emperor Palpatine. It was an extremely large army with advanced weaponry and droids, and possessed a strong confidence in the face of battle. Category:Military units
