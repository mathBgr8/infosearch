I'fii
1389

I'fii was a foodstuff eaten by the extragalactic Yuuzhan Vong species. At one time, future Yuuzhan Vong shaper Nen Yim and her crèche-mates P'loh and Zhul stole a korsk and released it into a communal food area, whereupon the cleaning creature consumed all of the i'fii there.The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy I'fii was first mentioned in Greg Keyes' 2003 novel The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy Category:Yuuzhan Vong food
