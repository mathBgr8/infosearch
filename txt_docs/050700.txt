Sith Shadows
3697

The Sith Shadows were a group of thugs who were known to attack Force-sensitives during the time of the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|180px|An armored Sith Shadow They were generally seen on the planet Dathomir, near the village of Aurilia, though were reportedly seen on other planets also. Though many species were Sith Shadows, Wookiees were never seen. The Dark Jedi Mellichae led the Shadows until imprisoned by the citizens of Aurilia. The Sith Shadows may have engaged in regular thefts in the village of Aurilia: they were often found carrying valuable resources only used there. Those resources were often returned by spacers and used for much-needed repairs.
