Loader Droid
1391

Loader Droids were used in Trade Federation ships. During the Invasion of Naboo in 32 BBY, the two Jedi Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi fought a Loader Droid while trying to escape the Saak'ak. A Loader Droid appears in the 2000 video game Star Wars: Episode I Jedi Power Battles.
