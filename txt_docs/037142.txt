Due to the imprisonment of Princess Leia Organa, Darth Vader ordered more guards to Detention Block AA-23, where she was being held. Cant thus augmented the security, personally guarding her. When Han Solo, Luke Skywalker and Chewbacca stormed the detention block to free the princess, Cant was the first victim of the battle. Cant was punched by Chewbacca and later shot by Solo during the ensuing firefight.
