Yarlubb river
867

The Yarlubb river was on the forest moon of Endor, flowing somewhere between Bright Tree Village and Red Bush Grove. Leektar once followed this river during his search for another village after his own was destroyed. Yarlubb river was a location developed by Arthur Papadam as part of Leektar's What's The Story? entry. Category:Endor locations Category:Rivers
