Brauken
1263

Brauken was a male individual who served as the mayor of the trading outpost Harlequin Station during the Galactic Civil War. After Ace Azzameen helped fend off an attack on the station, Brauken sent him a Key to the Station, and expressed his gratitude in an e-mail message to the Rebel pilot. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Mayors
