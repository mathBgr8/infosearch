Adminidome
742

The Adminidome was a location on Hologram Fun World where the planet's central operations were located; from here workers could oversee and hold control over the worldwide amusement park's many facilities. It was a major city in its own right, due to its sheer size and the fact that it was the base of HFW operations. Category:Cities
