Half-Axe Pass
1070

Half-Axe Pass was an area of New Plympto located close to Cadgel Meadows. In 19 BBY, it was the site of the Battle of Half-Axe Pass, where an army of Nosaurian Separatists were slaughtered, one month after the official end of the Clone Wars. Half-Axe Pass was ruined when a Yuuzhan Vong bioweapon destroyed New Plympto in 27 ABY. *Republic 80 *Dark Times 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Suns of Fortune Category:New Plympto locations
