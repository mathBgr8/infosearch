Solohan the Deceiver
579

Solohan the Deceiver was a deity of the Cult of Varn based on the smuggler Han Solo. He was a messenger of the gods who turned to evil, and was taken back to the heavens on his great flying chariot as punishment for his blasphemy. He was aided by a sinister, furry demon. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Category:Deities
