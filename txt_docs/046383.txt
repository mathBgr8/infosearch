Bo-Pe Sommo
1289

thumb|left|Bo-Pe Sommo's starship is shot down. Bo-Pe Sommo was a hapless space trader with business on the Byss Run. He bought a clearance code from fellow spacer Shug Ninx and made the run along with Ninx; however, unbeknownst to him, the code was illegal. While Shug Ninx was able to get through Byss Security with his illegal code, Sommo did not, and his ship was destroyed by one of Byss's Gauntlet Scanner Stations. Sommo's existence is only depicted in a single panel of the third issue of Dark Empire II.
