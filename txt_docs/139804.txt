|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Taris Chamber of Commerce was an institution located on the Outer Rim world of Taris at the time of the Mandalorian Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders. The Taris Chamber of Commerce guaranteed the largest of nine bounties placed on the head of Zayne Carrick, a former Jedi Padawan accused of murdering his classmates. However, by 3963 BBY, Taris was under siege by the Mandalorian warrior clans, the Chamber of Commerce was in danger of attack, and the status of their guarantee was uncertain. This development was reported on by editor Krav Noblis in the Holofeed The Adjudicator. The Taris Chamber of Commerce was first mentioned in The Adjudicator Special Report: The Outer Rim, an in-universe newsletter appearing on the last page of the fourteenth issue of the Knights of the Old Republic comic-book series.
