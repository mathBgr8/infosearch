Potin
1011

Lieutenant Potin was an officer in the Imperial Starfleet. In 6.5 ABY he was stationed aboard Black Asp. In a shuttle, he fled the battle in the Chorax system, causing his commander, Uwlla Iillor, to write him up for fleeing when not threatened. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial Navy officers of the Galactic Empire
