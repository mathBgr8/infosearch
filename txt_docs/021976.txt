It was released July 19, 2000. In the eight years since the fall of the Empire, Luke Skywalker has trained a new group of Jedi Knights, among them the Dathomir Witch Kirana Ti, the sky-hermit Streen, the scholar and lore-master Tionne, and Luke's greatest student, the brash Kyp Durron. Once trained, a Jedi must go where he is needed, to defend the New Republic and use the Force to help those in need.
