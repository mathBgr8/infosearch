Caf dispenser
699

A caf dispenser was a small machine that produced and dispensed ready-to-drink caf. A caf dispenser was a common sight in starships because they could keep the pilot or crew awake on long voyages. *Knight Errant *Legacy of the Force: Tempest Category:Consumables storage and processing Category:Dispensers
