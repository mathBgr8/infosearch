Stygium crystal
1227

Stygium crystals were a type of rare crystals composed of stygium that were used to power stealth systems for starships. The cloaking technology of the Carrion Spike, the personal corvette of Moff Wilhuff Tarkin, ran on stygium crystals. Darth Maul's Sith Infiltrator, the Scimitar, also utilized them.Star Wars Complete Vehicles, New Edition *Tarkin *TIE Fighter Owners' Workshop Manual *Star Wars Complete Vehicles, New Edition Category:Crystals
