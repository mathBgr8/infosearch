Tibor
1743

Tibor was a Barabel bounty hunter who assisted Zorba Desilijic Tiure during his trip to Jabba Desilijic Tiure's palace on Tatooine. He later accompanied Zorba to Cloud City to help capture Princess Leia Organa. He also supplied Zorba with information about various subjects and the Jedi Prince, which he interrogated using a variety of spices.
