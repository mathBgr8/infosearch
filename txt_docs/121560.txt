Azurite
2327

Azurite was a mineral found on Bandomeer. Less valuable than ionite and mined by several companies including Offworld Mining Corporation, it was blue in color, and used mainly for ornamentation. For example, the Singing Mountain Clan on Dathomir possessed decorations which Han Solo believed to be made of azurite.
