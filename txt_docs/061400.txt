Flash grenade
3120

A flash grenade, also known as a flash bomb was a type of non-lethal grenade which, when activated, created a bright energy flash and loud noise to disorient opponents. It also gave a mild impact although it was not as destructive as thermal detonators, and was capable of disabling droids for a short period of time.
