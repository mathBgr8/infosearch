Hurrikaine crystal
2150

thumb|right|250px|A [[Unidentified Hurikane species|Hurikane native hands Mace Windu some Hurrikaine crystals]] Hurrikaine crystals were deep blue to violet lightsaber crystals from the planet Hurikane admired for their unparalleled beauty. When used in lightsabers, they created blades that were very good at penetrating defenses. thumb|left|150px|[[Mace Windu/Legends|Mace Windu wielded a lightsaber made with Hurrikaine crystals.]] In 58 BBY, a young Mace Windu was sent to Hurikane on a mission as a Padawan. During the mission he harmed a local with force repulse. Upon realizing this, Mace used force heal to help the local. In exchange for helping the stone-encrusted native species, Windu was given a handful of the rare amethyst Hurrikane crystals, which he used to create his unique electrum lightsaber. The Windu's Guile crystal was later named in honor of the Jedi Master. "Hurrikaine" crystals appear in Knights of the Old Republic II, and possess many of the attributes associated with Mace Windu's lightsaber, including its amethyst color. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Onslaught *Star Wars: The Force Unleashed *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *Jedi Academy Training Manual *Star Wars: Lightsabers: A Guide to Weapons of the Force Category:Lightsaber crystals
