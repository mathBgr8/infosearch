Kraven
1870

Kraven was a Human male who served as commander of the CR90 corvette Badlands during the Galactic Civil War. In 4 ABY, he was assigned to Corrupter Task ForceStar Wars: X-Wing Alliance and later became an admiral. Following the Emperor Palpatine's death at the Battle of Endor, the now Admiral Kraven became the self-styled Imperial Warlord of a Mid Rim stellar cluster.
