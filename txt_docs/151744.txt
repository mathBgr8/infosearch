Borleias (transport)
1586

The Borleias was a New Republic troop transport based on Folor. When Admiral Apwar Trigit attacked the base, the transport was delayed in evacuating by an engine malfunction. Luckily for the crew, the newly-formed Wraith Squadron was able to buy time for them to escape.
