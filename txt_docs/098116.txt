Viurre
1440

Viurre was a Human female university student taking courses in nonhuman ethnology at the University of Rudrig. In 2 BBY, Viurre and her friend Kiili encountered a Wookiee named Chewbacca while walking their campus&mdash;fascinated by the massive alien, the two students accompanied Chewbacca and his friend Han Solo, who found the pair quite attractive, for a time.
