Imperial Surgical Corps
1889

The Imperial Surgical Corps was a medical branch of the Imperial Military during the Galactic Civil War. The Surgical Corps operated in surgical theaters and aboard naval warships near planetary battlefields. The branch also were key to developing new methods of biological warfare due to the members' knowledge in medicine. Army surgeons wore turquoise blue uniforms and helmets with breathing filters that partially covered their faces. They utilized a wide variety of operating gear and a small network of medical computers that aided in monitoring the patient's condition.
