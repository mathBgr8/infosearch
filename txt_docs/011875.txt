Beruga
1194

Beruga was a female Wookiee slave until she was inadvertently saved by Nim Abek, whom she repaid with a life debt by becoming his pilot for life. Her room on Abek's Station was adorned with starfighter memorabilia as well as a poster of the famous starfighter tactician Adar Tallon.
