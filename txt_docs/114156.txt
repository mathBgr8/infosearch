Kheann
1554

Kheann was one of the wives of King Sha-Mar Ma-dred of Kashir. Before the clone wars Anakin Skywalker and his master Obi-Wan Kenobi were sent to a meeting with King Sha-Mar Ma-dred. As the jedi approached the king, Kheann and the other wives (Tiarri, Mirrix and Ashala) were present. The wives left before the meeting to do as they wished. That night she heard as Tiarri screamed "An Intruder". Kheann and Mirrix pushed Anakin to the ground and took out their knives. Anakin explained that his lightsaber was stolen by someone.
