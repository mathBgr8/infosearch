Granita Clusta
718

The Granita Clusta contained the planet Hoth. According to Star Wars: The Annotated Screenplays, the first draft of The Empire Strikes Back placed Hoth in the Granita Clusta. *Star Wars: The Annotated Screenplays Category:Locations of unspecified type
