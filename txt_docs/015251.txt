It was a lightly populated world, with almost of all of its people engaged in farming to provide food for neighboring sectors. An Imperial Prefect oversaw its starport during the Galactic Civil War. Hills and fields covered most of its surface. In 0 ABY a Rebel contact and farmer named Idu Taanfaar was discovered and killed by stormtroopers under the command of Falto Dragen.
