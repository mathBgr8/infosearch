Kshyy vine
1803

thumb|right|150px|A kshyy vine. Kshyy vines were plants which grew on the wroshyr trees of Kashyyyk. The Wookiees hung sacrifices from one such vine to feed a terentatek. These vines were self-repairing vines, which could not be damaged even by blasters. Liftcars on Kashyyyk were held in place and moved vertically by kshyy vines.
