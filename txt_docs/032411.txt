Yeere
1538

Yeere was the largest city found on the planet Reytha and the planet's capital. There was an Imperial base there. By 0 ABY, the base was abandoned. During the Reytha campaign of that year, Darth Vader led an attack force against Rebel Alliance forces from Yeere. Buildings within Yeere included a mech factory, a command center, a troop center, and an animal nursery. Yeere appears in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds during the Darth Vader campaign.
