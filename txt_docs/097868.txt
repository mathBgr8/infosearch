Aerial Plankton Uprising
1604

The Aerial Plankton Uprising was a conflict in which Captain Gordun and the Human scholar Ebenn Q3 Baobab fought together against the Kinkees. The uprising was mentioned in the "About the Author" section of Ebenn's book, Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide. The Aerial Plankton Uprising was first mentioned in Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide, which was written by Ben Burtt and released in 2001. The book did not give a time frame for the uprising. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Rebellions and uprisings
