Trask (sergeant)
2349

Trask was a male sergeant who served under Lieutenant Bella in Sabaoth Squadron prior to the onset of the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, Sergeant Trask was ordered by Bella to deploy Hex weapons against Point Modie on Maramere, as a test of the weapon's potency. Waves of Hex missiles were launched, though they were intercepted and destroyed by Jedi Master Adi Gallia, Reti and Sol Sixxa. Nevertheless, Trask felt the operation had been successful, though Bella ordered him to remain in the Karthakk system and destroy their newfound enemies. Flying a Sabaoth starfighter, he took a small convoy of Hex Deployers and bombers to the planet's surface. Trask attempted to personally eliminate the adversaries, but was shot down and killed by Gallia. Trask's name is revealed in dialogue during Mission 5: Poisoned Skies: "The test was a success, wouldn't you say, Trask?"
