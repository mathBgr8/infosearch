|gender=Male |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes=Blue |skin=Beige |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners=Norky's mother |siblings= |children=Norky |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} The father of the child Norky was a male persta, the same species as his son. Norky's father lived with their son's mother and Norky on the Forest Moon of Endor. In 3 ABY, they accompanied the mother and son to deliver the latter to the Ewok Shodu Warrick in Bright Tree Village, where he would spend the day and night until his parents returned. Before they left, the father reminded Norky that they would be back the next day and to mind his manners. Indeed, the father returned the day after to pick up Norky. They had brown fur, blue eyes, and beige skin, and they wore an article of brown clothing around their neck. Norky's father appeared in "Bringing Up Norky," the nineteenth episode in the second season of the animated television series Ewoks.
