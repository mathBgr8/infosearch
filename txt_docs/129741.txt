Polearm One
858

Polearm One was the call sign used by Todra Mayn, a Human female from Commenor who was the commanding officer of Polearm Squadron, a unit of New Republic Starfighter Command that flew RZ-1 A-wing interceptors. She used this call sign while participating in General Han Solo's campaign against Warlord Zsinj and his Executor-class Star Dreadnought Iron Fist. *X-Wing: Solo Command Polearm 01
