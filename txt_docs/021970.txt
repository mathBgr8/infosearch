|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=12 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Jedi Academy: Leviathan |issue=4 |next=last }} Jedi Academy: Leviathan 4 was the fourth and final issue in the Star Wars: Jedi Academy: Leviathan 4 part series of comics. It was published on January 27, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. Kyp Durron thought he had destroyed the Leviathan that laid waste to the mining colony of Corbos. But he was wrong. He killed its offspring and now the mother of the Leviathan is looking to take revenge on the Jedi. Now Kyp and the rest of the Jedi Academy must face a threat even larger than they had first imagined.
