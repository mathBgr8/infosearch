B'ay tribe
943

thumb|250px|The B'ay tribe's sandcrawlers The B'ay tribe was a tribe of scavenging Jawas on Tatooine. The clan owned three sandcrawlers. Around 4 ABY, the clan was wiped out by a surviving dark trooper on a Rebel ship they were scavenging. The sandcrawler in use went hurling out of control and was destroyed when it went off into the Mos Espa Arena, nearly killing the mercenaries Spiker, Big Gizz, and Onoh who had an encampment near by. Only one member, Klepti, survived, swearing a life debt to the three mercenaries. Category:Jawa clans
