Milli Bufflin
919

Milli Bufflin was the wife of Huberdink Bufflin III. Both natives of Corulag, the couple were vacationing on the planet Amfar during the growing Separatist Crisis. Due to the steep drop in tourism, the Bufflins found that they had many of the resorts facilities to themselves. Category:Corulag residents Category:Females
