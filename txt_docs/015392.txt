Rebecca Moesta
2757

Rebecca Moesta is an author who made a number of contributions to Star Wars fiction in the 1990s. She is the co-author of the Young Jedi Knights young readers series with her husband, Kevin J. Anderson, and she also wrote three books in the Star Wars: Junior Jedi Knights series. Moesta additionally penned the short story "Trade Wins: The Ranat's Tale" in the anthology book Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina, and co-authored two pop-up books with Anderson. (With Kevin J.
