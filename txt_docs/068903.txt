The Domain Lah worldship was the Yuuzhan Vong worldship possessed by Domain Lah. After the Fall of Coruscant and the death of the Baanu Rass during the Mission to Myrkr, the Domain Lah worldship was stationed in orbit around Myrkr, and was used as a teaching place for members of the Warrior caste. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines I: Rebel Dream Category:Koros-Strohna
