Tempest Scout 4
1777

Tempest Scout 4 was an All Terrain Scout Transport in Tempest Force. It was equipped with a prototype targeting computer, and it maintained accuracy even in dense foliage. In addition, energy could be diverted from propulsion to weapons systems. It participated in the Battle of Endor, and was destroyed in the battle. Category:All Terrain Scout Transports
