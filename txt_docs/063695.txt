The Smugglers of Naboo
2628

|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Smugglers of Naboo" is a free Role Playing Game Supplement released by Wizards of the Coast designed to promote their Invasion of Theed game, which is a simplified introduction to the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. In it, the players each take one of four pre-made characters, either Rann I-Kanu, Galak, Arani Korden or Rorworr. In game, Rann's master Ali-Vor has sent Rann and his companions to Naboo to investigate rumors of smugglers poaching animals on the planet, thinking that though the rumors are likely false, it would be good experience for the Padawan. Thus the adventure begins. The Smugglers of Naboo Fast-Play Game is a reprinted version of The Predators, an adventure from issue 2 of Star Wars Gamer magazine. The only alteration is a change to the spelling of Professor Tasrah Boh's first name.
