Madame Baronis
705

Madame Baronis was a "harmless" old lady living on the planet Peekoine during the Galactic Civil War. When a group of Rebels came to her looking for two Jedi Knights, she had not had any company in several years and so tried to help them as best she could. Although she kept them in her house for over four hours, she yielded them no useful information. Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species
