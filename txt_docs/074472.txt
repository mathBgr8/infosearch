Durastar
1269

The Durastar was a starship owned by the New Jedi Order, and used by Daeshara'cor in the early months of the Yuuzhan Vong War. It suffered a hyperdrive failure near Corellia, forcing Daeshara'cor to abandon it. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order
