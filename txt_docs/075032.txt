Month of Plenty
564

The Month of Plenty was a Weequay holy season that honored the moon god Quay. Temples on the planet Sriluur which were dedicated to Quay had an open skylight in their roof which allowed Weequay priests to track the moon's progress during the month. *Secrets of the Sisar Run *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Holidays Category:Weequay culture
