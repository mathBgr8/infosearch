Freebird (cargo hauler)
819

Freebird was a cargo hauler owned by Captain Stanz. He used Freebird to transport the settlers of New Brigia from Brigia, and then to haul the chromite they mined to the Core. In Death Troopers, there is a Sentinel-class landing craft which is named Freebird that might or might not be the same ship. *Before the Storm *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Freighters
