Dunsenn
1452

The dunsenn was a species of fish-like animal of vast size, indigenous to Naboo. With troop containers strapped to their undersides, such creatures were used as aerial troop transports called FlopCarriers by Gungans. thumb|left|180px|Dunsenn. The body of the dunsenn was essentially an enormous balloon, enabling it to float in the air. It ingested water through its gills and drove itself forward by expelling air out through specialized funnels. Once airborne, it propelled itself with its strong rudder tail and navigated with a number of large, feather-like fins.
