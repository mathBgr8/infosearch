Etherhawk
1591

Etherhawk was a Marauder-class corvette of the New Republic Defense Force. An old ship by 7.5 ABY, Etherhawk was assigned to Group One of Solo Fleet in the hunt for Warlord Zsinj. Ships of this class had the capacity to carry one squadron of starfighters, though Etherhawk was not known to carry any. *X-Wing: Solo Command *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Marauder-class Assault Corvettes Category:Starships of the New Republic
