Machar Heng
955

Machar Heng was a member of the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. A Subaltern, he was charged by Warmaster Tsavong Lah to coordinate the Yuuzhan Vong's efforts with the Peace Brigade to locate the Jedi.
