Trimpian
1438

Trimpian was a tangy finger food capable of getting stuck in close-set teeth. It could be served with miasra sauce. The odd taste was believed by some to be a sign that the meal made the eater's skin slightly flame retardant. Trimpian appeared in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. *Vision of the Future *Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Guide: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Snack foods
