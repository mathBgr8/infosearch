School of Hidden Wisdom
956

The School of Hidden Wisdom was the only dueling academy to survive the Clone Wars and the rise of Emperor Palpatine. Established on Baltimn by Ephaan Kenzon, it was the refuge of several surviving Jedi after the Great Jedi Purge. The school was closed down when a number of Ephaan's students were accepted into Luke Skywalker's Jedi Praxeum. *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook Category:Jedi academies
