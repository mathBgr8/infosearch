The Art of Star Wars Galaxy: Volume Two
1474

The Art of Star Wars Galaxy: Volume Two is a second book showcasing more artwork from Topps' Star Wars Galaxy trading card set by various artists. It was published in 1994 following the release of the first volume a year earlier. The book includes a foreword by original trilogy concept artist Ralph McQuarrie.
