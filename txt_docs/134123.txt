Kumauri Empire
1995

The Kumauri Empire was a realm of several dozen star systems established by the warlord Vall Kumauri around 10,000 BBY, on what was then the rimward frontier of the Galactic Republic. The Kumauri Empire was short-lived, quickly absorbed by the Galactic Republic, but its legacy survived in a series of massive capital ships capable of planetary bombardment, known as the Kumauri Battleship. The last line in the series was the three kilometer long Cal-class battleship.
