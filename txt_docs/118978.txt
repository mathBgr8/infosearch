Fogblaster
817

Fogblaster was a type of alcoholic beverage. Jedi Knight Leia Organa Solo hated fogblasters, and only ordered them when she wanted a drink to nurse for some time. Ben Skywalker was instructed to order a Sapphire Fogblaster at the Big Boom cantina on Nova Station after breaking out of a GAG prison. Shortly after ordering one, the twin Hapan Security agents Trista and Taryn Zel recognized the fogblaster and extracted Ben from Nova Station. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Invincible *Crucible Category:Alcoholic beverages
