Sea Sprite 1
1425

Sea Sprite 1 was a C-3 passenger liner that carried tourists to and from various locations across the galaxy. It was present at the Destreg Spaceport near Destreg II when Emon and Ace Azzameen attempted to assassinate K'Armyn Viraxo by destroying his personal yacht, the Highroller. Sea Sprite 1 was not harmed in the battle. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:C-3 passenger liners
