|end date=July 25, 2007 |schedule=MonthlyStar Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Nights of Anger |format=3 issues |era=Old Republic era |timeline=3963 BBY |series=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |issues=16-18 |preceded by=Days of Fear |followed by=Daze of Hate }} Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Nights of Anger is the fifth story-arc of the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series of comics. On Arkania, whose society is in a state of segregation between pure Arkanians and offshoots, Jarael disguises herself as a pure specimen to get an analysis of Camper's blood. However, this move attracts the attention of Adascorp, which recognizes Camper as one of its former employees; as such, the Last Resort is captured by the Adascorp flagship, the Arkanian Legacy, and Jarael and Camper are brought before Arkoh, Lord Adasca. After Rohlan's unsuccessful attempt to free Jarael, she is kept close to Adasca to ensure that Camper finishes the secret project he used to work on before fleeing and hiding on Taris. It is aboard the Arkanian Legacy that Jarael learns about the past life of "Camper", actually named Gorman Vandrayk. His past research has attracted Adascorp's attention to giant space slugs, or exogorths, which it intended to use as weapons of planetary destruction.
