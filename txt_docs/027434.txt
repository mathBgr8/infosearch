Friesner's short story "That's Entertainment: The Tale of Salacious Crumb" appears in Tales from Jabba's Palace, which was published December 1995 by Bantam Spectra. It provides the backstory for the Kowakian monkey-lizard Salacious B. Crumb and Melvosh Bloor, the Kalkal professor. An investigative politico-sociology Kalkal professor, Melvosh Bloor, is intent on studying the criminal underworld of Jabba's palace by attempting to interview the notorious crime lord. Salacious B. Crumb appears and pretends to help Bloor. Crumb tricks Bloor into saying that Jabba "lies like a Gran" along the way to Jabba's Palace. When the duo reach Jabba, Crumb explains that Bloor had said that the Hutt "lied like a Gran." Jabba is tired of Crumb's idiotic jokes, and takes the interview Bloor set up. Once the professor asks how he can repay the crime lord, he tells Bloor to make him laugh.
