Anxarta
882

Anxarta was a city on the northern coast of Anai, on the planet Lianna. It was the location of Anxarta Spaceport, which was mainly used by transport and private starships supplying foodstuffs to the local tourist resorts. Sienar Labs, under the control of Sienar Fleet Systems, was located at the Spaceport. *Mission to Lianna Category:Cities on Lianna
