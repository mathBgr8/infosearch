Cretacian
2094

Cretacian was Leia Organa's Gallofree transport ship in the early days of the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|250px|The Cretacian crashed on Krant. While on a mission to Krant in 0 ABY in order to meet Utric Sandov, the ship had a fight with TIE/LN starfighters and crashed on the planet. Organa, the protocol droid C-3PO and eight Rebel troopers survived the crash. Cretacian appears in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:GR-75 medium transports Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
