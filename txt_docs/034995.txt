Clone trooper captain Deviss led the Republic forces alongside a Jedi General on the Outer Rim Territories &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Altyr system planetStar Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith Altyr V. However, the Jedi General was vaporized by Separatist fire, leaving Deviss to improvise a plan of attack. Deviss rallied the remaining companies of clone troopers under his leadership and destroyed the Separatist ion cannon, opening the battlefield to aerial bombardment and winning the battle for the Republic. For his act of bravery and initiative, Clone Commander Bly promoted Deviss to the rank of commander. The battle was mentioned in the entry for Clone Commander Deviss in the article "Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic," which written by Karen Traviss and Ryan Kaufman and published in Star Wars Insider 84, released on September 13, 2005.
