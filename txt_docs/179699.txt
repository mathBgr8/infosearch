Star Wars: Return of the Jedi Cine-Manga |intl release=April 23, 2008 |rereleased= |media type= |pages=96 |isbn=9781593079130 |timeline=4 ABYThe New Essential Chronology |series= |preceded by=The Empire Strikes Back Cine-Manga |followed by= }} Star Wars: Return of the Jedi Cine-Manga is the third of the Cine-Manga series, published by Tokyopop in Europe in 2005 and later republished in the US by Dark Horse Comics in 2008. High above the forest moon of Endor, the Empire is building another Death Star. If the Rebel Alliance hopes to bring its vision of peace to the galaxy, Emperor Palpatine and his unflinching enforcer Darth Vader must be stopped! Experience the climax of one of the most beloved sagas in history as Luke Skywalker, Princess Leia, Han Solo, and all the rest reach their ultimate destinies in this photo comic that fits perfectly into your pocket! *This manga-sized volume adapts Star Wars: Return of the Jedi into a comic made up entirely of images from the film!
