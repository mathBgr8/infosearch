Cutting Edge Clinic
1557

The Cutting Edge Clinic was a location at Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was a horrible place run by the infamous "Doctor" Cornelius. It had four rooms and specialized in cyborg operations which were seldom successfully performed. It was in this clinic that Evazan continued his research for eternal life. Evazan was aided by a Devaronian clerk named Jubel.
