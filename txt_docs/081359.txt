Garray
1248

Garray was a portly male Human. He served as a Major in the Galactic Alliance and the commander of Caluula Station during its siege by the Yuuzhan Vong. After Operation Trinity, the Vong began attacking the station, sending a ychna to which Garray was helpless to defend against.
