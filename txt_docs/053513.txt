Alderaanian ale
922

Alderaanian ale was an extremely rare, fermented alcoholic ale, brewed from fragrant grains once native to Alderaan. It had a smooth, rich flavor. Also, it was both Han Solo's and Garris Shrike's favorite drink. *Cloak of Deception *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *The Paradise Snare *The Hutt Gambit *Rebel Dawn Ale Category:Ales
