Brilliant missile
1677

thumb|right|250px|[[Saesee Tiin/Legends|Saesee Tiin stops a brilliant missile in mid-flight.]] The brilliant missile was a variety of homing missile capable of tracking its targets with such precision that it could be considered semi-sentient. The Vindicator XM-15 was one type of brilliant missile produced by Arakyd Industries. The Coalition of Automaton Rights Activists called the XM-15 an abomination, and the Mechanical Liberation Front tried to free a shipment of XM-15, but blew up a 25-kilometer radius of Havridam City on the planet New Bakstre, killing 14 activists.
