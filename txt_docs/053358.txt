|subsid= |headquarters= |locations= |role=Transport corporation |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Santhe Passenger and Freight was a subsidiary of Santhe/Sienar Technologies. It was a large transport corporation. Almost 15% of the galaxy's passenger liners hailed from the Allied Tion, home sector of Santhe Passenger and Freight, during the Galactic Civil War.
