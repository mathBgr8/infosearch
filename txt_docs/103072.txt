WQ 445

The WQ 445 was a class of box-shaped transport vessel that was manufactured by TaggeCo. They were considered to be outdated as of 26 ABY. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, this type of freighter was piloted by Han Solo's Ryn partner Droma.
