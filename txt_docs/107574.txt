When Kella Rand discovered evidence that would incriminate Kaleb Darme in the assassination of Shek Barayel and attempted framing of the New Republic, Juloff turned on Rand and notified Darme. He further proved his sympathy to the Galactic Empire by taking several potshots at Rand with a blaster.
