Horville's Hut of Horrors
984

Horville's Hut of Horrors was a business run by Horville on the Forest Moon of Endor during the Galactic Civil War. The establishment featured various frightening spectacles designed to thrill those who attended. Horville's Hut of Horrors was popular with Ewoks and Duloks, particularly during festivals and celebrations. Category:Endor locations Category:Entertainment companies
