Diamond Level
746

Diamond Level was the top of the three passenger levels on the Errant Venture. Its clientele was relative high class, as the level was reserved for Navy officers, wealthy travelers, valued guests, friends and family. It contained dining halls, conference rooms and recreational facilities. Some extremely rich civilians paid a hefty rent to live there full-time.
