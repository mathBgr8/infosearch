Surviving the rise of the Galactic Empire with his Padawan, Drakka Judarrl, this Master was eventually found and executed by Sith Lord Darth Vader. A Force-sensitive male, this individual was trained by the Jedi Order in the ways of the Force, attaining the rank of Jedi Master. Selecting Initiate Drakka Judarrl as his Padawan, the pair served the Order in some capacity during the Clone Wars. Surviving the war, the Master-Padawan pair went to ground as the Galactic Empire emerged and successfully initiated a Great Jedi Purge. Helpless to assist as hundreds of their peers were hunted down and slaughtered by the Empire, which was controlled by a Dark Lord of the Sith, the pair assisted many members of the Galactic Senate escape persecution; an act which drew the attention of Darth Vader. Eventually hunted down, Vader slew the Jedi Master but was unable to catch Judarrl. The young Zabrak escaped Vader with the assistance of a smuggler who had been aiding her master; the pair fled to Dantooine where Judarrl would take up residence in the basements of a destroyed Jedi academy.
