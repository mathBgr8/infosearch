Movo Brattakin
1850

Movo Brattakin was an acquaintance of Olag Greck. After Greck nearly murdered him, Movo merged his mind with the metallic body of his aide B-9D7. He then reprogrammed C-3PO to lead a droid rebellion to topple Boonda the Hutt, allying himself with R2-D2 in order to join the rebellion and gain access to Boonda's facilities.
