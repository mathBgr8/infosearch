Bazurkah
1493

Bazurkah was a male Dug who was leader of the Bazurkah Clan on Malastare during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was insulted by the family of Bog'Ruhx, then vengefully set out to kill that of Bog'Ruhx through posted bounties, beginning with a bounty of 2000 credits for Bog'Ruhx wanted dead on him. Jango Fett secured the bounty while in Malastare's death stick factory.
