Lokk Gimble
1815

Lokk Gimble was a male Ugnaught who worked for Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He earned a bounty on his head by the Corporate Alliance for stealing a chemical process and adapted this technology for the production of death sticks laced with the deadly neurotoxin. The bounty was claimed by Jango Fett who caught Gimble and his accomplice "Rachet" Gramzee on Malastare.
