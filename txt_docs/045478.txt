Halleb Harmion
1246

Halleb Harmion was an executive with Gastess' Finance, Inc. His specialty was taking over the operations of corporations which the ruthless financier Sarlim Gastess had acquired. Like his brothers K'yne and Sarell, he enjoyed taking a successful company and running it into the ground for short-term credits.
