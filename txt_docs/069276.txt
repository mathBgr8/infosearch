Selander
2213

The Selander was a Corellian Engineering Corporation Assassin-class corvette operated by the Ripoblus. During a battle in the Sepan Civil War, the Selander, along with its sister ship, the Galliot, attacked the Dimok XQ1 Platform Science Station Youst.
