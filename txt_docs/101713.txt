Star Wars Adventures 12: Podrace to Freedom
1782

Star Wars Adventures 12: Podrace to Freedom was the ninth installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. This book is a reprint of Episode I Adventures 14: Podrace to Freedom. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 12 in September 2003.
