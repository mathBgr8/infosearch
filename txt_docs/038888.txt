Howlrunner formation
899

The Howlrunner formation was a military maneuver developed by Jedi Master Oppo Rancisis. It divided a ground force into two groups. The first flanked the enemy while the second drew their fire. This way, the enemy could be attacked from the side or from behind. This tactic was used extensively by Sharad Hett, even managing to incorporate it on ground based assaults made by the Tusken Raiders. *Star Wars (1998) 12 *Starships of the Galaxy, Saga Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ground vehicle tactics Category:Infantry tactics
