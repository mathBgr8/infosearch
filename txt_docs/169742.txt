Jawajawa clan
1499

thumb|The clan with their [[Unidentified Jawa Hero|hero in front.]] This Jawa clan managed to salvage a downed X-wing starfighter, and would later steal a TIE advanced, believing it to be Darth Vader's personal fighter, from a trio of salvagers. Among the clan's members were the TIE/Ad thief, the scarred warrior, and a knowledgable aging Jawa who had insights into the Galactic Civil War. This clan only appears in the story Oh!! Jawajawa, published in Star Wars Manga: Silver issue of Tokyopop's Star Wars Manga. Originally published in Japan, those stories were considered to be of "fuzzy" continuity by Lucasfilm. Since then, they have been republished in English in the United Kingdom under Infinities label, which established the material in them as non-canonical. Category:Jawa clans
