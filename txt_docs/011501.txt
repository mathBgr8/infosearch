High Prophet

thumb|right|200px|High Prophet [[Jedgar]] High Prophet of the dark side was the title of the Supreme Prophet's chief lieutenant, in the cult the Prophets of the Dark Side. Under the leadership of Kadann, Jedgar served as High Prophet.
