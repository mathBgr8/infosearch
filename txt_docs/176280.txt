Dhuna
695

Dhunas were domestic animals native to Bellassa. Ussan priests took the bodies of the dead to burial in flower-covered, white carts pulled by dhunas. The priests coaxed the dhunas along by making sounds similar to the chants the priests would sing in the streets.
