14th Roving Line
2117

|capital= |starfighters= |ground= |naval= |campaigns= |battles= |affiliation=*Alliance to Restore the Republic **Alliance Fleet }} The 14th Roving Line was a battle line of the Alliance Fleet, assigned to attack Imperial bases on the border between the Inner Rim and Outer Rim. The squadron was led by Captain Qarl aboard the command cruiser Mantan Wanderer. Other capital ships included an assault frigate, an escort frigate, and a Corellian corvette. The 14th Roving Line was also assigned a single company of Alliance Special Forces troopers, including the elite platoon-sized task force known as Team 19. *Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic fleets
