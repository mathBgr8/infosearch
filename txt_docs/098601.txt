Nor Wedd
1795

Nor Wedd was a Quarren official who represented Mon Calamari in the Senate after former Senator Tikkes joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems. In 22 BBY, Wedd served as one of the aides to Senator Tundra Dowmeia, along with Zil Topur. When Supreme Chancellor Palpatine announced the proposal of forming the Grand Army to defend against the Separatists, Nor Wedd, along with the rest of the delegation agreed to the Chancellor's proposal.
