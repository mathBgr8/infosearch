Blade series
1091

The Blade series was a series of rugged but somewhat primitive atmospheric fighters and starfighters produced by, among others, Tarrvin-on-Kallik on Adumar. Blade series fighters were distinguished by mounting both forward- and aft-firing laser cannons. Most were designed for atmospheric combat, but could be modified relatively easily for space use. Some models even featured hyperdrives.
