Seeker-torpedo
2214

A seeker-torpedo was a type of ground-to-air missile, used by the Beast Riders of Onderon. Seeker-torpedoes launched from a six-barreled, swiveling emplacement. Missiles were fired singularly, and were capable of taking out Jedi Cruisers and other starships as well. Thus, they offered protection from the occasional invasion attempt by the city-dwellers of Iziz. When fired, the missile capable of reaching at least a magnitude of six over the speed of sound.
