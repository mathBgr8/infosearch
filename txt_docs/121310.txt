Krax's castle
995

Krax's castle was the home of the Krax family on the planet Tahlboor. It was located near Mount Yeroc. Many Hobor guards patrolled the expansive grounds. Around 5.5 BBY, the castle served as the staging ground for Troob forces prior to the near-siege of the Hobors and the sky cannon within Mount Yeroc. During this time, C-3PO sent several guards off to the apparently nonexistent Narthex Antechamber, in a ruse to steal a speeder bike and rescue R2-D2 and Larka Nimondro. *The Protocol Offensive Category:Castles Category:Tahlboor locations
