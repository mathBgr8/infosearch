M'shento'su'Nikto
2514

M'shento'su'Nikto, or Southern Nikto, were a Nikto subspecies with no horns, a covered nose, and several pairs of breathing tubes. These tubes were larger than those of the Kajain'sa'Nikto, and also acted as primitive ultrasonic sensory organs.
