Miat Temm
1377

Miat Temm was a human female who served as one of Grand Admiral Gilad Pellaeon's civilian aides in the Imperial Remnant during the early period of the Yuuzhan Vong War. She was Force-sensitive, and used this ability to shield her thoughts from other users of the Force.
