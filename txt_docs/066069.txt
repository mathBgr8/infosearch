Grappa's yacht
1362

Grappa's yacht was the personal starship of Grappa the Hutt, a crime lord allied with Black Sun. When Grappa killed Macus Kayniph, a high-ranking member of Black Sun, in a fit of rage in 11 ABY, he reluctantly arranged the destruction of his yacht in order to cover up the murder. *Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 2 Category:Starships of Black Sun Category:Yachts
