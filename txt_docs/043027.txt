Danthe Artifice, Ltd.

|products=Personality matrixes |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Danthe Artifice, Ltd. was a designed of droid personality matrixes based on the Core World of Corulag. It was a major subcontractor for Arakyd Industries and developed most of the company's droid interface systems.
