|navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains= |crewmembers=*Wedge Antilles *Ooryl Qrygg |registry= |aliases= }} The Eclipse Rider was a modified Corellian Engineering Corporation YT-1300 light freighter that pilot Wedge Antilles rented in 7 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion places X-Wing: The Bacta War, in which Wedge Antilles rents the Eclipse Rider, in 7 ABY. during the preparations for the Bacta War, a conflict between Antilles' independent starfighter unit Rogue Squadron and the pro-Imperial government of the Inner RimThe Essential Atlas planet Thyferra. It was used by Antilles and fellow Rogue Squadron pilot Ooryl Qrygg for a trip from the Core Worlds planet Coruscant to the Outer Rim planet Rishi to purchase a cache of X-wing starfighter parts. The trip out from Coruscant went well, but upon landing on Rishi, the Eclipse Rider lost a repulsorlift coil. While Antilles purchased the necessary X-wing parts, Qrygg worked on replacing the missing repuslorlift coil. Local regulations on the use of repulsorlift vehicles ultimately delayed the departure from Rishi by twelve hours. The Eclipse Rider was mentioned in the novel X-Wing: The Bacta War, written by Michael A. Stackpole and published in 1997. *X-Wing: The Bacta War Category:YT-1300 light freighters
