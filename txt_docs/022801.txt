Rin Assid
1652

Rin Assid, also known as "Assid the Lucky", was a near-mythical merchant who lived during the fourth millennium of the Old Republic. He was a good-natured man with a love of good food and good drink. He regularly won vast fortunes, only to in turn lose his newfound wealth helping others in need. One of his partners was Arlo Hirken. He was credited with helping to spread the usage of the Aurebesh throughout the galaxy.
