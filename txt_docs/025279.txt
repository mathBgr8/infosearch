T'laalak-s'lalak-t'th'ak
2353

T'laalak-s'lalak-t'th'ak was the Xi Charrian who designed the holographic-projector chair that Trade Federation of Planets Viceroy Nute Gunray used to communicate with Darth Sidious. During the hunt for Darth Sidious, Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi and Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker tracked him down on Charros IV in order to learn more about the mechno-chair they had discovered in Gunray's palace on Cato Neimoidia. They were forced to chase him through a large warehouse before they could catch him and ask questions, which he reluctantly answered. His answers furthered the Jedi's hunt for Sidious.
