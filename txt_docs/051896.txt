Bunkurd Corporation
943

The Bunkurd Corporation was a corporation that engineered a bacterium that helped break down the huge amount of waste that was produced by Imperial City on Coruscant daily. The waste was converted to a more biodegradable by-product. Even though the bacterium improved Coruscant's sewage problems by twenty-precent, if the bacterium was consumed by Humans, they would suffer from what would later be known as Bunkurd Sewer Disorder. Ead Vionor was a major shareholder in it, until he was assassinated by Kylaena in 1 ABY. *X-Wing: Wraith Squadron Category:Scientific companies
