Jungle folk
939

|class=Kentra |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin=Orellon II |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The jungle folk were a Kentra subspecies with black fur. They were a respected minority who dwelled deep in the Orellan jungles. The jungle folk were hunters, relying on their great physical strength and cunning to outwit both game and enemies. They were confident and knowledgeable of the jungle and used it as protection. Farmers among the jungle folk were extremely rare. Category:Kentra Category:Kentra culture
