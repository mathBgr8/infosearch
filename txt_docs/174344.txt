Star Wars Weekly 50
1229

Star Wars Weekly 50 is the fiftieth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on January 17, 1979. Edited by Dez Skinn, the title was revamped as part of his "Marvel Revolution" to more directly appeal to fans of the film by including photo-covers and features in addition to the existing comic strips. This issue includes part four of a four-part black & white reprinting of the story The Keeper's World, originally published in color in Marvel UK' Pizzazz magazine. *"The Keeper's Secret!" *"The Final Fury!" *"Tales of the galaxy: Visitation Part 3: Demonspawn!" *"The power of Warlock Part 6: Warlock!" 50
