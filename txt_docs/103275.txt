Naroon Cuthus
3010

Naroon Cuthus was Jabba Desilijic Tiure's majordomo for decades. When he finally retired, the Hutt crime lord ordered Cuthus's assassination, lest he spill secrets to Jabba's enemies. Prior to his demise, Cuthus memorably persuaded the Ortolan musician Max Rebo to accept a lifetime contract under the terms of which his band would perform exclusively for Jabba in return for free food.
