Noble (starship)
1682

Noble was a Invincible-class Dreadnaught of the Republic Navy. This vessel was significant in that its Human crew all had natural green hair. Noble was lost centuries before the time of the Empire. Some people believed that Diran Miciluz, who had green hair, was a descendant of its crew. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Invincible-class Dreadnaught Heavy Cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
