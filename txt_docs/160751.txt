The book was released May, 2005 by Wizards of the Coast as a paperback. Third in a series of titles that accompany each Star Wars miniatures expansion, Ultimate Missions: Revenge of the Sith provides extended information and gameplay suggestions for anyone looking to maximize their Star Wars Miniatures experience.
