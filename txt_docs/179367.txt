Trade Federation Troop Transport
2126

thumb|right|250px|A Troop Transport in flight The Trade Federation Troop Transport was a battle droid transport craft employed by the Trade Federation around the time of the Occupation of Karthakk. The Troop Transport bore a resemblance to the Multi-Troop Transport used by the Federation, though with the addition of two fin-like wing protrusions on either side of the vessel's bow. It was used as a space-borne equivalent of the MTT. Troop Transports were often escorted by Scarab-class starfighters. Trade Federation Troop Transports saw action prior to and likely during the Clone Wars.
