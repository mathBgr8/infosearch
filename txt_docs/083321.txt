|published in=Star Wars Tales 11 |reprinted in= |pages=8 |timeline= |series= |preceded by="Tall Tales" |followed by="A Day in the Life" }} "Ghost" was a story found in Star Wars Tales 11, written and illustrated by Jan Duursema. It involves a young Han Solo attempting to find a lost treasure and encountering the old Jedi Quinlan Vos, who is on the run from bounty hunters. Solo later finds out that the treasure is Quinlan's lightsaber. A reader asked in the Q&A section of Star Wars Insider 99 why Han didn't believe in the Force.
