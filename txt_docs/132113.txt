Bentro
1710

Bentro was a Fleet Admiral in the Imperial Navy. In 0 ABY, Bentro was the captain of Darth Vader's command ship, Executor. Bentro was in command of the Executor when Vader brought his command ship to Shelkonwa in an effort to capture Princess Leia. Bentro was eventually replaced as the commander of the Executor by Kendal Ozzel, at the behest of Mara Jade, for the sake of "keeping an eye on him". *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Fleet admirals Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy admirals of the Galactic Empire Category:Males
