Star Wars Infinities
2733

thumb|250px|"The world of endless Star Wars possiblities" Star Wars Infinities is a group of four-part comic book series published by Dark Horse Comics. The comics tell the stories of alternate histories to the original trilogy, therefore making them non-canonical, unlike most comics, which are considered canon. Each comic tells the story of what would have happened if certain key events in their respective canon sources had played out differently.
