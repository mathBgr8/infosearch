Rights of Sentience League
1736

The Rights of Sentience League was an organization affiliated with the Galactic Republic. In 32.5 BBY,The New Essential Chronology the organization participated in the Eriadu Trade Summit, an event held on the planet Eriadu as a forum for the Galactic Republic and the Trade Federation to discuss the taxation of outlying trade zones. During the summit, a mixed-species group representing the League waited to be admitted at the east entrance of the event's location at Seswenna Hall civic center in Eriadu City, the planet's capital.
