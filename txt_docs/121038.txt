FX-10 medical assistant droid
1078

The FX-10 medical assistant droid was a model of medical droid that was manufactured by Medtech Industries, as part of the FX-series of medical assistant droids. It was the successor of the FX-9 model. FX-10s were used in the Aurora Medical Facility on Obroa-skai. *Millennium Falcon FX-10
