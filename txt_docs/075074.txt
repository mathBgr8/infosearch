Sneaky
1565

Sneaky was Jaina Solo's R9 unit assigned to her new StealthX starfighter at the time of the Swarm War. Sneaky was destroyed when Jagged Fel shot down Jaina during the Battle of Tenupe. He was later replaced by Jaina's new R9 unit, Sneaker. Apparently, there was a second droid named Sneaky that was used by Jaina during the Battle of Uroro Station.
