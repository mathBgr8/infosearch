The Star Wars: The Essential Atlas Online Companion establishes that Gelgelar is located in the Elrood sector, meaning that Nebula Consumables also operates within the Elrood sector. *Noonian system |role=Food production |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Nebula Consumables was a food producer and supplier headquartered on the planet Tyed Kant. They were the largest food producer on Tyed Kant. During the Galactic Civil War the company sold most of its product to planetary governments and to the Imperial Military.
