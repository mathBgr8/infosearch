It occupied the second orbital position around its star, Vulpter. The planet featured a toxic environment and a rocky surface, and it had one moon. Kins was mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 4: Vulpter," the fourth entry in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 28, 2005. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Vulpter system, and therefore Kins, in grid square L-10.
