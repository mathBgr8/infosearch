Galactic Access, Inc.
812

Galactic Access, Inc. was a company that operated Estaria Central Starport. Galactic Access's business model meant they operated the facilities for profit first, legality second. Because of this, the company drew the attention of Division Three, the anti-contraband department of the Imperial Taxation Bureau. *Hideouts & Strongholds Category:Spaceport companies
