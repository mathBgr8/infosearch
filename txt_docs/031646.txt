Arch of Triumph
1024

The Arch of Triumph was a landmark erected on Coruscant by the Galactic Empire, shortly after Emperor Palpatine instituted the New Order. The Arch was erected in Palpatine's honor. Han Solo, during one of his Academy training exercises, obtained the fastest time for an assigned run when he went through, rather than over, the Arch. *The Paradise Snare *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Core Worlds structures Category:Coruscant monuments
