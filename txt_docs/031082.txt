Marvel Movie Showcase 1
1433

Marvel Movie Showcase 1 collects the first three issues from the Star Wars (1977) series of comics; it has a publishing date of November 1982 and a $1.25 cover price. This issue reprints the cover to Star Wars (1977) 1, but with different text. This is a comics adaptation of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. *Star Wars (1977) 1 *Star Wars (1977) 2 *Star Wars (1977) 3 01
