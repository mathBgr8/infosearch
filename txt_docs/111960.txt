Jev Nerran
1596

Jev Nerran was an individual who lived in the city Theed on the planet Naboo. Nerran was a neighbor and family friend of Jobal and Ruwee Naberrie of House Naberrie, who lived in the same district in Theed as Nerran. The Naberrie's eldest daughter, Sola Naberrie and her children, Ryoo and Pooja Naberrie, regularly visited Jobal and Ruwee. During their trips, they would also visit Nerran, spending a great deal of time with them and other family friends. Jev Nerran was first mentioned in the StarWars.com Databank entries for Ryoo and Pooja Naberrie, which were published in 2002.
