Root
1211

Root was the nickname of a Human male with black hair living on Somov Rit as of 1000 BBY. He was the cousin and guardian of Zannah, Darovit, and Hardin. He was an ally of the Jedi Order, a friend of Torr Snapit, and apparently a Force-sensitive. *Jedi vs. Sith 1 *Darth Bane: Rule of Two *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Force-sensitives Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Somov Rit residents
