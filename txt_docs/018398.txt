|pages= |upc= |timeline=c. 19 BBYThe New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Obsession |issue=3 }} Obsession 3 is the third issue of the Star Wars: Obsession comic series, released February 2, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics. Lured into a trap aboard a drifting space wreck, Obi-Wan and Anakin become separated, and Anakin is forced to face the seemingly unkillable bounty hunter Durge. But Durge has not reckoned with Skywalker's innate power, or the young man's growing ties to the dark side. Without Obi-Wan's moderating influence, Anakin goes off on Durge! A battle not to be missed! *Five must read issues before you see Episode III!'
