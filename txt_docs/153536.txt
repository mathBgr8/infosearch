Red Threxa's Boarding House
1213

Red Threxa's Boarding House was a boarding house in Gelgelar Free Port on the planet Gelgelar. It was the only place to rent a room at the port. It was run by the Suiraon Red Threxa. Situated in the middle of Gelgelar Free Port next to the Shrine of Kooroo and across from the Slippery Gelgelar Eel, the boarding house was made of stolen prefab buildings and had a central common room built around a large heating unit, with various rooms and halls off to the side. The boarding house was a cheap and dry place to stay, for a fee of fifteen credits per night. It was used mainly by ship crews on down time or pilgrims for the Shrine of Kooroo. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Businesses Category:Gelgelar Free Port locations
