|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 3 |reprinted in= |pages=14 |timeline=22 BBY (6 months ABG)Date established in narrative text. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "One Battle" was a short comic story in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 3 featuring Plo Koon in a battle. On the planet of Aridka,Star Wars: The Comics Companion nine clone troopers are the remainder of their force. A weakening force field is all that protects them from an army of thousands of battle droids. As the force field breaks, the droid army immediately marches in and demands surrender. The clones choose to go out fighting, and their resilience pays off when a smoking Republic gunship drops out of the air. Plo Koon jumps out, and although the clones cannot understand him, they comprehend enough to shoot a rope at a massive Separatist weapon, providing a small bridge for the Jedi Master. Koon, effortlessly wielding his lightsaber, quickly destroys the machine, and the remainder of the Separatist forces. As he leaves, he drops one last comment, which the clones understand: "One battle, one Jedi."
