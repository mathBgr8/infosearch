Senate-class transport

Senate-class transports was a multi-purpose transport used by senators of the Galactic Republic shortly before the Clone Wars. One such transport was also used to carry Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker to a transfer dock in the orbit of Coruscant, where they boarded the starship Star Sea Flower. *Rogue Planet Category:Starship classes of the Galactic Republic Category:Transport classes
