Forgotten Spire
1280

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Forgotten Spire was a manufactured spire that rose from the rocky plains of the planet Dathomir. The origins of this monument were lost to the ages, though it bore a striking resemblance to the Forgotten Spires on Corellia. Rancor pygmies were spotted near the Forgotten Spire after the Battle of Yavin. Category:Dathomir locations
