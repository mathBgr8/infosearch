Quentin Pierre
1362

Quentin Pierre, sometimes credited as Quentin A. Pierre, played Sergeant Isdam Edian in Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. He is also listed on a callsheet as a stand-in Y-wing pilot for Return of the Jedi.
