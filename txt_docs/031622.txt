|birth= |death= |species=Human |gender= |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=Sylo family |parents= |partners=Griggen Sylo |siblings= |children=*Paxi Sylo *Six children |affiliation=Sylo farm |masters= |apprentices= }} Tashna Sylo was a Human and the spouse of Griggen Sylo. The two owned a large farm in the outback of the Outer Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Dantooine in the waning years of the Galactic Republic, a farm that had been in the Sylo family for ten generations. In order to supply a larder to feed their family of seven children and to sell offworld, the couple worked hard to harvest tritacale and yot beans. Between the years 30 BBY and 31 BBY,Paxi Sylo's StarWars.com Databank entry states that Paxi was nine years old when he witnessed the battle on Dantooine, which The New Essential Chronology places in 21.66 BBY. Therefore, this article assumes Paxi's year of birth to be either 30 BBY or 31 BBY, nine years earlier depending on which month he was born. the youngest of the Sylo children, Paxi, was born. By the time of the Galactic Empire, which formed in 19 BBY, the Sylo farm had been abandoned. Tashna Sylo was mentioned as one of the parents of Paxi Sylo in the latter's original Databank entry on StarWars.com, first posted in 2004.
