Security spike
3434

A security spike or slicing spike was an electronic device used to bypass electronic security measures, usually to open the electronic lock of a door or container. It was similar to the more advanced computer spike. Many security spikes could only be used once. Security spikes first appeared in BioWare's 2003 video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, where they were intended to be single-use items that would give a +5 bonus to the player character's roll when attempting to unlock doors or containers. Due to a bug in the code, security spikes did not function correctly in the PC release of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.
