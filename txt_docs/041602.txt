Spinner-pit
983

Spinner-pit was a gambling game that involved a rotating circular tabletop and several balls. The balls would land on a number (such as 21), a symbol (such as a flask) and a color (such as green). Participants had to guess all three. Mara Jade, thanks to her use of the Force, was skilled at spinner-pit. *Star Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine *Mara Jade – By the Emperor's Hand 5 Category:Wheel games
