Hanson Mining Consolidated
798

Hanson Mining Consolidated was a mining consortium based on Neona in the Tapani sector. They owned the planet, and mined ores from the planet's sea floor. HMC later went into partnership with Aquatic Ecosystems, Incorporated to exploit the planet's fish species as food sources. *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mining companies
