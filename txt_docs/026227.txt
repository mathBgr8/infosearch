Battle of Virujansi
3598

The Battle of Virujansi occurred when the Republic launched a siege against the Separatist-held planet of Virujansi. The Separatists were using the planet as a staging-grounds for a significant number of battle droids. Obi-Wan Kenobi led the ground force, while Anakin Skywalker led the aerial units, which included the Rarefied Air Cavalry, a military unit which Garven "Dave" Dreis served in. The result was a significant Republic victory.
