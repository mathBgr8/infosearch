Agro-combine
1537

An agro-combine was a place where livestock and crops were raised and harvested. The world of Corellia boasted a number of agro-combines, as did other worlds noted for producing large quantities of foodstuffs. Soontir Fel worked for an agro-combine for a time before entering the Imperial Academy. As a child Delak Krennel was involved in the destruction of farmland owned by aliens on Corellia, which would later be used for agro-combines.X-Wing: Isard's Revenge The Allied Grain and Roughage agro-combines of Corellia held annual Harvest Festivals to celebrate the harvest.
