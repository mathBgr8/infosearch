Janson (CR90 corvette)
1881

Janson was a CR90 corvette that the Nharwaak used to escort the corvette Glazer as it made a technology delivery to the Rebel Alliance Nebulon-B frigate Xerxes. As with the other Nharwaak craft, near Mylok IV the Janson came under attack by Imperial forces under Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Nharwaak starships
