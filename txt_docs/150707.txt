YVH 1-25A was a YVH 1 combat droid developed by Tendrando Arms. It accompanied YVH 1-1A onto the vessel Byrt during the evacuation of Coruscant to rescue C-3PO and Ben Skywalker and was destroyed in the attempt by a singularity mine. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:YVH 1 units
