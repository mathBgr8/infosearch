|other date= |media type= |pages=50 |isbn=none |timeline=3 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back |issues=1 |preceded by= |followed by=The Empire Strikes Back 2 }} Classic Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back 1 is a collection containing the first three issues of Marvel Comics' adaptation of The Empire Strikes Back. The best Star Wars comics creative team to ever put pen to paper, Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson, unite once again to adapt the second Star Wars movie to comic form. This first of two volumes brings the Marvel Comics adaptation of The Empire Strikes Back back into print for the first time since 1980.
