Despite its reputation as a peaceful world of museums, Porus Vida saw conflict throughout its history, including several sieges during the Alsakan Conflicts, and was devastated by the United Warlord Fleets in 12 ABY. Populated by Humans, Porus Vida was a world with beautiful museums of its culture and manicured gardens. Porus Vida joined the Galactic Republic prior to the Indecta Era. During the Alsakan Conflicts, it was located in the Exploitation Region. The planet was besieged during the First Alsakan Conflict and again during the Second Alsakan Conflict. Around the era of the Sith Wars between the Republic and various Sith factions, Porus Vida was subjugated by a mercenary fleet hired by the nearby world of Norulac. Led by the psychotic mass-murderer Lunatta, the mercenaries successfully conquered the peaceful world, and Lunatta was permitted to loot an art museum on Porus Vida as her payment.Star Wars: The Old Republic Encyclopedia Porus Vida eventually affiliated with the New Republic and it was one of the first planets attacked by the Imperial forces after the Imperial Reunification in 12 ABY.
