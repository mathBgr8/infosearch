Pakrik Major
1491

Pakrik Major was the sector capital of Kanchen sector. It was a Planet in the Pakrik system and was located in the Core Worlds region. It was more densely populated than its twin world, Pakrik Minor, and hosted the annual Kanchen Sector Conference.
