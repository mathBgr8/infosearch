Zurric Que'stiil
1851

}} Zurric Que'stiil was a male explosives expert who worked for the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. He was tall and pale with dark eyes. He preferred clothes of white and dark blue. He enjoyed using his skills to get revenge against the Empire. Zurric was an industrial engineer on a remote deep space facility and cared little for galactic politics. His life changed when the Empire seized the facility and killed his wife. He escaped and joined the Rebellion. He once visited Cather Taan's Gun Shop in Storrd Township on Seregar to stock up on detonite before a mission.
