Glazion energy torch
379

Glazion energy torches were powerful cutting tools. The walls of the bunker used by the Five Families of Cestus Cybernetics were reputed to be so thick that it would take one standard day for a glazion torch to penetrate them. *The Cestus Deception Category:Cutting tools
