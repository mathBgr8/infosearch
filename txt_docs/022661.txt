Young Jedi Knights: Trouble on Cloud City
11993

Young Jedi Knights: Trouble on Cloud City is a Star Wars Legends young-adult novel written by Kevin J. Anderson and Rebecca Moesta. It is the thirteenth book in the Star Wars: Young Jedi Knights series, and it was first published by Berkley Boulevard in August 1998. It also includes a seven-page preview of the next book in the series, Crisis at Crystal Reef, and a fourteen-page preview of Lucasfilm's Alien Chronicles: The Golden One. The novel was reprinted in Young Jedi Knights: Under Black Sun (1999). Jacen and Jaina When the Empire died, they were born—a new hope for the New Republic. The young twins of Han Solo and Princess Leia have grown strong with the Force, thwarting the evil Nolaa Tarkona and destroying the Emperor's Plague. Now, with their friends Lowbacca the Wookiee and Tenel Ka, the future heroes of an already legendary saga continue their training. Lando Calrissian offers the young Jedi Knights a rare opportunity—a vacation on Cloud City. They jump at the chance—as long as their new friend, the mysterious Anja Gallandro, can come along. Anja is happy to be included. But little do the twins know that they are actually helping Anja hatch her sinister plan. A plan that goes far deeper than simple revenge&hellip; At the invitation of the Solos, Anja Gallandro studies at the Jedi Academy, but she's too short-tempered and distracted to make much progress. Lando Calrissian invites the kids to visit the newest business venture: the SkyCenter Galleria amusement park on Cloud City. There, the Jedi investigate the strange death of Lando's business partner, Cojahn, who was secretly murdered by Czethros of the Black Sun. Lando, Jaina, and Zekk follow leads to Clak'dor VII, while Tenel Ka, Lowie, Jacen, and Anja stay on Bespin. Anja warns her benefactor, and Czethros dispatches Black Sun assassins to kill the Jedi. With Czethros’ involvement discovered, and all traitorous Galleria employees exposed, the threat seems abated. But Anja frets that since she is now cut off from Czethros, she is also cut off from her badly needed andris spice.
