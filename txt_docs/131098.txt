|subscribers= |pages=84 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=95 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 95 is the ninety-fifth issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine and the third to be published by Titan Magazines. There are two cover variants for this issue. The first is the newsstand edition. The second was shipped exclusive to comic book stores through Diamond Distributors on July 24, 2007.
