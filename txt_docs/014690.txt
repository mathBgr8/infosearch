Piton
2402

Piton was a terrestrial planet located in the Piton system of the Core Worlds. It was the homeworld of the sentient Piton species, which lived underground. Miro Daroon, who had become a member of the off-world Jedi Order at some point by approximately 53 BBY,Jedi Apprentice: The Captive Temple establishes that Miro Daroon was a member of the Jedi Order at the same time as Xanatos, who ceased to be a Jedi around 53 BBY per Jedi — The Dark Side 5. was a Piton from the planet Piton. Piton was first mentioned in The Captive Temple, Jude Watson's 2000 seventh installment of the Star Wars: Jedi Apprentice young-reader novel series.
