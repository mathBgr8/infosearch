Shoaneb Culu's starship
1435

Shoaneb Culu's starship was the starship given to Shoaneb Culu at Ossus which she used to pursue Aleema Keto and Crado into the Cron Cluster. When Aleema detonated the Cluster, Shoaneb's vessel was destroyed in a following supernova in The Cron Drift, Shoaneb got killed with it. *Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War 4 Category:Starships of the Jedi Order
