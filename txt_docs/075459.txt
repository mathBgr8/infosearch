Jaddyyk moss
928

Jaddyyk moss was a dangerous, parasitic plant that was native to the Wookiee homeworld Kashyyyk. It grew in stalks and mounds on the floor of the Well of the Dead, giving an appearance of a long-furred Wookiee. *Tyrant's Test *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mosses Category:Plants of Kashyyyk
