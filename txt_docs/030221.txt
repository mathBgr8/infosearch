General Nilo was a member of this species that was kidnapped from his homeplanet by a Zygerrian slavers. He eventually ended up in the gladiatorial arena of Rattatak around 22 BBY.The New Essential Chronology Nilo the Rodisar first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003. The character was later given a name and short backstory in the personal Databank entry on the official Star Wars website, StarWars.com, in 2011. Nilo's appearance was based on unused concept drawings&mdash;done by the artist Nilo Rodis-Jamero&mdash;of the Gamorrean guards during the production of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. His name Nilo, and his species Rodisar, were an homage to the original artist. Category:Outer Rim Territories sentient species Category:Reptilian sentient species
