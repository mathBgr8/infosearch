Dora-mu
964

The dora-mu was a giant, marine creature from the Yuuzhan Vong's home galaxy. It was covered by hundreds of long, chitinous green scales which were able to withstand blaster shots and could heal over time. Its scales were used by Shapers for the armor of an aquatic vehicle called the Vangaak.
