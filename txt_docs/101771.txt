Warrl Surprise
882

Warrl Surprise was a beverage on the market during the Galactic Civil War. This sweet drink was prized by the Myyydril people. It was made by leaves of the Warrl plant, only native to the Myyydril Caverns. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees Category:Beverages
