When Order 66 was issued by Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, Galle opened fire on Secura alongside the other clones present, killing her. thumb|left|220px|Lieutenant Galle on Felucia Galle was the taken name of a clone of the human male bounty hunter Jango Fett created on the planet Kamino to fight for the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. In 19 BBY,Star Wars: Galactic Atlas dates the events of to 19 BBY. during the Clone Wars they held the rank of lieutnenant and served in the Grand Army of the Republic's 327th Star Corps under the command of Clone Commander Bly and Jedi General Aayla Secura. Along with the rest of the corps, Galle was deployed to the planet Felucia to battle the Separatist forces there. Galle and Bly were amongst a group of clone troopers who stood with Secura at the head of a column of Republic vehicles as they prepared to engage approaching Separatist forces when Supreme Chancellor Palpatine issued Order 66 to all clones, decreeing all members of the Jedi Order traitors to the Republic. Galle and the other troopers then opened fire on Secura, shooting her in the back and killing her. thumb|right|250px|Galle wearing Phase II armor in the colors of the 327th Star Corps. Like all clones of Fett, Galle stood 1.83 meters tall. While on Felucia, Galle wore Phase II clone trooper armor with a pauldron and a kama, all of which were decorated in the yellow coloring of the 327th Star Corps. Galle was equipped with a DC-15A blaster carbine and two DC-17 hand blasters, which they carried in holsters on a utility belt. Galle first appeared in the 2005 prequel trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. In the current Star Wars canon, Galle was first identified on a card released through the Star Wars: Card Trader app. The character originated in Star Wars Legends, where he was first created for Paul Ens and Tom Hodges' webstrip, Reversal of Fortune, which was published starting in 2004.Reversal of Fortune In the 2018 reference book Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, New Edition, an image of Galle is mistakenly labeled as being Clone Commander Bly.Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, New Edition *Star Wars Encyclopedia of Starfighters and Other Vehicles *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, New Edition Category:Clone trooper lieutenants Category:Individuals of unspecified gender Category:Individuals with unspecified pronouns Category:Members of the 327th Star Corps
