True Victory Party
1056

The True Victory Party was a political party of Bothans who tried to continue the ar'krai against the Yuuzhan Vong. The party was led by Reh'mwa around 40 ABY. Jacen Solo arranged for the leaders of the party's Coruscant branch to be killed by Lumiya and Alema Rar, causing the Bothans to side with the Corellians in the Second Galactic Civil War. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bothan organizations Category:Political factions
