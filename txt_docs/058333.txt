Operation Blunt Stick
2322

After the destruction of Relentless, Operation Blunt Stick was a brief operation led by Keyan Farlander to destroy an Imperial listening probe in a minefield, in order to allow the Rebel fleet to migrate to Hoth unnoticed. On that operation, Imperial Corvette groups TIE Fire and Striker were destroyed, along with the three BFF-1 bulk freighters, which were carrying mines, Kan, Kant and Wunt. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
