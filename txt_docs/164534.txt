*Galactic EmpireServants of the Empire: Edge of the Galaxy |organization=*Imperial Academies *Imperial RemnantsAftermath: Life Debt **Imperial territory G5-623 **Imperial Remnants Shadow Council |type= |term= |powers= |duties=Oversee certain military commands or locations }} Commandant was a title given to overseers and commanding officers of particular military forces or installations, such as prison camps, Imperial Academies, or garrisons. During the Clone Wars, Osi Sobeck was a commandant in the Confederacy of Independent Systems who served as the warden of the Citadel, a major prison located on the planet Lola Sayu. Toward the end of the Galactic Civil War, Camp Sardo's labor facilities in Kashyyyk's Imperial territory G5-623 were run by Commandant Theodane Sardo. Imperial Commadandant Stradd was captured by Norra Wexley's Imperial hunters around the same time. In 5 BBY, Jayhold Beehaz, an officer of the Imperial Army, was stationed on Aldhani and acted as the Commandant of the Aldhani Garrison, overseeing both the Aldhani dam and the Alkenzi Air Base. The president of the Royal Imperial Academy on Coruscant in the Galactic Empire's early years was Commandant Deenlark.Lost Stars Cumberlayne Aresko carried the title in his duties as an officer at the Imperial Academy on Lothal, the Academy for Young Imperials.
