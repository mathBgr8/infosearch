Sundancer Squadron
1497

Sundancer Squadron was a Rebel Alliance starfighter squadron composed of Z-95 Headhunters. It saw service in the Airam sector where it was assigned to the MC80 cruiser Crucible. When the Crucible was sent to rescue factory personnel in the Mobetta system from an Imperial attack, Sundancer Squadron went with it. Upon arrival, the squadron was given the dangerous task of suppressing Imperial starfighters and escorting Blood Squadron in its mission to torpedo the Imperial command ship, Star Destroyer Rage.
