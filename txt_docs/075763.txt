Warton
1894

Warton was a male Human former colonist from the volcanic planet of Eol Sha. He led Jedi Master Luke Skywalker to Gantoris, a Force-sensitive, for his new Jedi Academy. Warton, along with the other colonists, were relocated to the planet Dantooine in 11 ABY. He, his wife, and his fellow evacuees were killed when Admiral Daala razed the fledgling group.
