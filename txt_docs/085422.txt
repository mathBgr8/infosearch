Paluruvu
837

Paluruvu was an expensive violet-colored perfume from the Dzavak Lakes district of the planet Ansion that was often sold by the Qulun. A large flagon of the perfume was created by diluting a few drops of the pure essence with clear water. Paluruvu not only excited the sense of smell, but it could also have a soporific effect on most humanoid species. *The Approaching Storm *The Approaching Storm abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ansionian culture Category:Perfumes Category:Sedatives
