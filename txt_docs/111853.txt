Tax Levy Bill
590

The Tax Levy Bill was a bill debated by the Galactic Senate sometime between the Invasion of Naboo and the Clone Wars. It proposed raising taxes to help deal with pirates raiding hyperroutes. However, some feared that the money would be used for less important measures. Jar Jar Binks voted against it. Category:Galactic Republic laws and acts
