Wuxod (senator)
1725

Wuxod was a male senator of the Galactic Empire who represented Derilyn and the Elrood sector during the early years of the Empire. Born in the city of Paran, he was an open critic of Emperor Palpatine and disappeared after stating Derilyn's opposition to Palpatine's power grab.
