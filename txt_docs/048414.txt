|end date=March 5, 2003 |schedule= |format= |era= |timeline=0 BBY (35:2 GrS) |series= |issues=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Empire: Princess... Warrior was a 2-issue story arc of Star Wars: Empire published by Dark Horse Comics. It ran from February 5, 2003 to March 5, 2003. The story takes place 3 weeks before Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope.
