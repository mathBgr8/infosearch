Richard Bonehill was an actor who played character extras in The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi, the second and third films of the Star Wars original trilogy. Actor Richard Bonehill appeared in the final two films of the Star Wars original trilogy, primarily playing masked background extras. See also . Bonehill worked on Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back for three weeks and Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi for six weeks.
