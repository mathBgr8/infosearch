|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters= |locations= |formed= |founded=By 13:3:14 |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Greenputt Governing Council }} The Female Professional Greenputt Tour, abbreviated FPGT, was one of several major professional leagues for the ball sport greenputt. Founded by the date 13:3:14, the league was overseen by the Greenputt Governing Council. That day, the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News published a news story titled "Yet Another Greenputt League" which reported on the formation of the Non-Sentient Professional Greenputt Tour, which joined the existing professional greenputt leagues. The Female Professional Greenputt Tour was first mentioned in the in-universe issue HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, published on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002. The content of the site, including this news article, was co-written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. Category:Greenputt leagues
