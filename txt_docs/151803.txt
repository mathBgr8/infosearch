Edgefields-on-Varonat was a city on the planet Varonat. Founded alongside Tropis-on-Varonat circa 250 BBY"Galactic Gazetteer: Hoth and the Greater Javin" claims the city was settled 250 years before the Galactic Civil War. by Humans from Salliche, the city's population eventually grew to around several thousand individuals.
