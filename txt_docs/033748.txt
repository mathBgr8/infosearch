B'olba
2078

B'olba was a male Quarren who served as an aide to Senator Tikkes during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. Two years after the Invasion of Naboo, B'olba escorted Senator Tikkes to a remote planet that was for sale and had attracted the corrupt senator's attention. On their way, they discovered an escape pod in the planet's orbit and found that there were three Jedi aboard: Ki-Adi-Mundi, Adi Gallia, and A'Sharad Hett. The Jedi's cruiser had just been attacked by the bounty hunter Aurra Sing, a former Jedi apprentice of An'ya Kuro. When the group reached the planet's surface, B'olba counted among those who left the ship to look at the new world.
