The Hub (computer)
867

thumb|250px The Hub was a massive computer complex built by the Galactic Empire under Palpatine's orders to coordinate the Imperial Navy. It was decided it was too risky to be held on Coruscant, and so was constructed in Pyramid One on Ahakista. In 0 ABY Raze assembled a team under Wyl Tarson to destroy it; however the plot was foiled. *Rebellion 8 *Rebellion 9 *Rebellion 10 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Computer technology
