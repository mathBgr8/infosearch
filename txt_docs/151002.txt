Trader's Quarter
450

Trader's Quarter was a city on Ord Mantell. The Brawl and Grill establishment was there. Callandri found Alton Lochner on Trader's Quarter and attempted to kill him. However, Lochner was saved by his friends Adazian Liebke and Tirranna. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Cities on Ord Mantell
