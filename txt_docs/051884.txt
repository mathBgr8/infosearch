Brila Industrial Supplies
995

Brila Industrial Supplies was an industrial supply company owned by Tanass Brila. It traded in supplies of standard parts for numerous businesses and industrial system. The company was a front for the Rebel Alliance and secretly concealed shipments of arms and supplies to the Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. *Hideouts & Strongholds Category:Vendors and sellers
