Kaggle
1519

Kaggle was a prominent Force-sensitive crime lord who operated on the planet Lanthrym and was native to Elrood sector. Like all Dazouri, Boss Kaggle had two forms: rest and rage. His career in crime began with petty larceny, but Kaggle rose to eventually command thugs of his own, thanks to his shrewd intellect in rest form and his unstoppable violence in rage form.
