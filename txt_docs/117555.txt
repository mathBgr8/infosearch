Jugsmuk Station
1149

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=Jugsmuk clan }} Jugsmuk Station was a spaceport located adjacent to the settlement of Jugsmuk on Gamorr. It was constructed of prefabricated buildings. During the Galactic Civil War, Jugsmuk Station included the Irrational Number cantina, Sheebareevadee's Emporium of Interstellar Goods, and Momma Reseros' Diner. At Jugsmuk Station, offworlders could live out of the way of the native Gamorreans. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Gamorr locations Category:Spaceports
