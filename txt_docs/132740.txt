|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} The Lagulla ice cave was an underwater passage in the core of the Mid RimThe Essential Atlas planet Naboo. In 32 BBY, Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn and his Padawan, Obi-Wan Kenobi, along with their navigator, Jar Jar Binks, traveled through this ice cave in a tribubble bongo on their journey from Otoh Gunga to Theed during the Invasion of Naboo.
