Swoop analog
650

Swoop analogs were the Yuuzhan Vong's equivalent of the swoop bikes used by the Galaxy. These open-air fliers could a seat a crew of two. Swoop analogs were equipped with a dovin basal propulsion unit which they used to drain the shields of enemy vehicles, overloading them and causing them to break down.
