Boranall
2056

Boranall was a temperate world in the Mid Rim. In 3640 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion Boranall was attacked by the Sith Empire warship Ascendant Spear in order to suppress rebellion in the Boranall system. Many centuries before the Invasion of Naboo, Shang-Troy Thanabo was directed to this planet by the Force. He discovered a pregnant woman, whose twin sons he helped deliver.
