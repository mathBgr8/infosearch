Skyroute D25D
838

Skyroute D25D was an autonav skylane on Coruscant. It connected Boribos Prefecture, Hirkenglade Prefecture, and CoCo Town. In 22 BBY, a garbage collection crisis resulted in huge piles of trash being deposited in city streets and outside major buildings in CoCo Town. This forced Skyroute D25D to be detoured between CoCo Town's Kansen Entrance and Trotus Exit. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:CoCo Town locations Category:Coruscant roadways
