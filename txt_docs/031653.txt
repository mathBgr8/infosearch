Commerce Way (Coruscant)
445

Commerce Way was the name of an avenue on Coruscant, after the New Republic re-established the planet as the central world of the Republic. It was located in the Senate District, near the New Republic Obelisk. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse Category:Coruscant roadways
