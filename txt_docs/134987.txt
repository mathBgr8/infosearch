Rakikta
966

Rakikta was a Wookiee security controller in the city of Cjaalysce'I on Socorro. He was known as a perfectionist and was never lax on the rules when dealing with spacers. Rakikta, whose name meant "strongarm," had six brothers, the eldest of which was Kuykenda. *The Black Sands of Socorro Category:Males Category:Socorrans Category:Wookiees
