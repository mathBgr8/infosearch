Cinnagar plate armor
1379

Meant primarily for the personal guards of dignitaries, Cinnagar plate armor was designed with appearances in mind. Due to its role, however, the armor was still effective at protecting against a wide range of attacks including sonic assaults. The Cinnagar plate armor was intended to appear in the video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic but it was removed from the final version of the game. As the armor was still present in the game files, it could be obtained using cheat codes. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Heavy armor
