Spence
1680

Spence was a Human male who joined the Erased along with his twin brother, Gilly, after the Galactic Republic was reorganized into the Galactic Empire by Emperor Palpatine. Spence and his brother never talked much and kept things to themselves after they joined the resistance group. When stormtroopers attacked one of the bases, Spence fought alongside his brother to defend the base, but they had no chance and were both shot down by concentrated fire. *The Last of the Jedi: Underworld *The Last of the Jedi: Death on Naboo *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Resistance fighters
