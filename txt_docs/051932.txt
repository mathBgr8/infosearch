Marsh Limoth
1399

Marsh Limoth was a Human male Imperial Governor of Goroth Prime installed in 17 BBY. He turned against his father when he withdrew his support of Palpatine, and was rewarded for his loyalty by being assigned the "Personal Assistant" of the Moff of the Trans-Nebular Sector, Nebin Cray.
