Lonely Five Range
2139

The Lonely Five Range, best known simply as the Lonely Five, was a mountain range on the planet Ryloth. The subterranean city of Kala'uun was located within a basaltic peak of the Lonely Five. Clan Fenn had a fortress in the mountains.
