Minds and Perceptions: A Comparative Study of Persuasive Techniques During the Galactic Civil War
834

Minds and Perceptions: A Comparative Study of Persuasive Techniques During the Galactic Civil War was a work of non-fiction written by Ulm-Aaa-Janzikek and published by TriPlanetary Press. The work covered in great detail the methods of interrogation, information extraction, and, in some cases, torture used by the Galactic Empire, Corporate Sector Authority, and Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Articles and academic papers
