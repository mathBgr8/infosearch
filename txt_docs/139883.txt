Latika Garboren
858

Latika Garboren was a woman who lived in the Taung Heights in Coruscant. She was a self-professed "history nut", having a great interest in history. In 22 BBY, she noticed that a dollop of pastebread she was preparing resembled the emblem of a Sith Acolyte. She first tried to contact the Jedi, but failing that, contacted local security instead. Constabulary detective Dherik Rule investigated it for her, and found that there was no case to pursue. Category:Coruscanti Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species
