|stellar= |endpoints= *Hyabb *Twith |transitpoints= |otherobjects=Hyabb - Voon - Twith |junctions=Namadii Corridor }} The Hyabb-Twith Corridor was an ancient hyperspace trade route that was located on the spinward arc of the Galactic Core. The route connected the planets Hyabb and Twith, passing through Voon in the process. In the decades prior to the Old Sith Wars this route was in contended territory, and there was a constant threat attacks from the warring factions.
