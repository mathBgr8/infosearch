Veda (Youngling)
2118

Veda was a Force-sensitive Jedi Initiate studying within the Jedi Order during the Clone Wars. At a young age, Veda was identified and inducted into the Jedi Order and began her studies in the ways of the Force at the Jedi Temple on the Republic's capital world, Coruscant. In 19 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Boba Fett: Pursuit to 19 BBY. Veda and her clan were passing down a corridor at the temple, when she recognized Jedi Master Mace Windu passing by and went to him to ask the senior Jedi a "very important question," much to the distress of her instructor.
