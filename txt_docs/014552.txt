Hawk-Bat (gunship)
2014

The Hawk-Bat was a jet-black Tempest-class gunship famous for being the personal ship of Arwen Cohl, and recognized as such on sight by many lowlifes throughout the Outer Rim Territories. It led the attack on the Revenue over Dorvalla in 33 BBY. It was later commandeered by Qui-Gon Jinn on Karfeddion, where he used it to travel to Eriadu. *Cloak of Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Personal starships Category:Starships of the Jedi Order Category:Tempest-class gunships
