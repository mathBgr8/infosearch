Natas Kyber
1253

King Natas Kyber was the ruler of the Imperial backwater world of Troska. As head of the Kyber Royal Family, Natas often clashed with the local Imperial Garrison. Eventually, Imperial Lieutenant Treyz Manech hired an outside contracter, one Boba Fett to depose the local monarchy.
