Vortext 9
1102

Vortext 9 was a roleplaying magazine from Summer of 1993. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an article for the West End Games Star Wars Miniatures system. The article was called "Shootout at Landing Pad 94" and was written by Ted Arlauskas with help from Chuck Turnitsa. Category:Vortext
