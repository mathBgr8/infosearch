Das Depu Epu Sea
885

Das Depu Epu Sea, or Deep Dark Water, was an epic poem belonging to the Gungan culture of Naboo. Written in both Gungan Basic and Old Gungan, this ode to Da Beauty Al'Round would make a great impression on a Gungan family if used during the course of a visit. An excerpt of the poem is as follows (courtesy of the Baobab Archives): Oomesa pa Guna mer muda. J'ai bongo do deepu sea. Mesa hearta go tippy-tap. J'ai bongo do deepu sea. All dha Gunga wail an gnash, No bomba, no crashen... J'ai bongo do deepu sea ... Tank yu. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Gungan culture Category:Poems
