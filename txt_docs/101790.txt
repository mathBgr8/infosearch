The Anchorhead Municipal Council was the city government of Anchorhead on Tatooine. At the insistence of Huff Darklighter, they passed an alien landowner tax sometime after the Battle of Yavin, in order to persuade Throgg to sell the Lars homestead to Huff. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron Category:Legislatures Category:Tatooine organizations
