Caldrahlsen Mechanicals
1745

Caldrahlsen Mechanicals was a droid manufacturing company specializing in class three droidsThe Far Orbit Project based on the Core World of Esseles. The company produced interrogation droids for the Galactic Empire and was the first to design and market droids specifically for the legal profession. Initially, these droids were met with a degree of skepticism but soon became commonplace within the Empire's legal system. Their most notable product was the C4LR litigation droid.
