Sure-Sight
2712

The Sure-Sight was a weapon scope manufactured by Neuro-Saav Technologies and used by the New Republic during the Galactic Civil War. The Sure-Sight utilized holo-imaging technology, computerized light amplification, heat bafflers, image sifters, and radiation signature micro-sensors to positively identify targets over 500 meters away in the most difficult conditions such as near darkness, low-light conditions, and fog. The crosshairs on the scope allowed for instanteous targeting, with readouts that automatically displayed the range to a target. The scope also came with sensor correction software, which meant it could be run by most second-degree droids meeting GalactiDroid Architecture Class 2-B standards. There was also a special macrobinocular mini-comp attachment available. The Sure-Sight required a buyer to possess a New Republic Weapon Class III permit.
