Dalea Trovin
1061

Dalea Trovin was one of the few female Imperial Customs officers. She worked in Votrad, a city on the planet Brentaal during the Galactic Civil War. She was responsible for seizing a huge warehouse full of raw materials, belonging to smugglers. Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Imperial Customs officers
