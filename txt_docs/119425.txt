Nerfloaf was a dish made from the meat of a nerf. A nerfloaf prepared by Jedi Master Kenth Hamner caused his Padawan, Izal Waz, to go into a salt binge that temporarily threw him off his path to Jedi Knighthood. *The New Jedi Order: Recovery *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Fate of the Jedi: Outcast *Fate of the Jedi: Conviction Category:Meat loaf
