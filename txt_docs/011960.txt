Pirt Neer
2051

Pirt Neer was a female Jedi Initiate studying at the Jedi Order's Coruscant Temple academy during the Clone Wars. A Force-sensitive, Pirt Neer was inducted into the Jedi Order and received her training in the ways of the Force at the Coruscant Jedi Temple. When the Clone Wars erupted between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Neer remained at the Temple and participated in the 19 BBY Jedi Temple Apprentice Tournament. Administrated by Master Ilena Xan, Neer faced off against fellow Initiate Enver Hoxha and defeated him during the Round of Eight.
