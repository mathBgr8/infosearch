Star Wars Weekly 115 is the one hundred fifteenth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on May 7, 1980. It included the original story "The Monster's Secret!". *"The Monster's Secret!" *"Heroes of the galaxy: Man-Wolf Part 2: Who are Man-Wolf's mystery assailants?
