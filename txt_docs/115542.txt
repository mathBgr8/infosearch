Sith Academy (Gentes)
1440

The Sith academy of Gentes was established by the Brotherhood of Darkness, and was active near the end of the New Sith Wars. The Sith used the academy to train Sith Warriors and Marauders. Here these warriors would learn to focus their emotions into a raging battle fury and become impressive forces of destruction. Sorcerers of the dark side would mold these warriors into ravenous beasts, used for the sole purpose of destroying enemies of the Brotherhood of Darkness. *Darth Bane: Path of Destruction *Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force *The Essential Atlas Gentes
