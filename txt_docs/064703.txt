Breeda
1638

The town of Breeda was located west of Yeere, the capital of Reytha. In 0 ABY, it and its food storage facilities were taken by the Rebel Alliance. It was subsequently was retaken by an Imperial strike force led by Darth Vader. The Imperials razed the troop center the Rebels had built and eliminated the small garrison. Breeda appears during the Darth Vader campaign in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
