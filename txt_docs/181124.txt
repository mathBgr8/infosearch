*17 |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Regina Cayli" is a solitaire adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Ken Rolston. It was originally published in Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game in October 1987 and later reprinted in Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 in February 1994, both by West End Games. In the adventure, the player assumes the role of the smuggler Roark Garnet, who is in the process of trying to spring his co-pilot from confinement aboard the Imperial vessel Regina Cayli, from which the adventure gets its name. Smugglers Roark Garnet and Hawk Carrow are forced down on New Bakstre by Imperial Customs vessels. Their ship, the Dorion Discus crashed in the forest and an Imperial team wounds and captures Carrow.
