Hodidiji
1677

Hodidiji was a New Republic Senator during the Black Fleet Crisis. During the visit of Nil Spaar to Coruscant in 16 ABY, Hodidiji was convinced by Senators Tig Peramis and Cian Marook to support the Yevethan Viceroy. When talks with Chief of State Leia Organa Solo failed and Nil Spaar fled the planet, Hodidiji yielded his time on the Senator floor to both Peramis and Nil Spaar.
