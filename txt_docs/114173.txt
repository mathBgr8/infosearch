Fem Nu-Ar
2185

Fem Nu-Ar was an archaeologist who worked alongside Dr. Ualp Xathan during the era of the Galactic Civil War. Nu-Ar and Xathan were pressed into service by Imperial forces led by Captain Drezzel in 4 ABY, who tasked them with studying the ruins of an ancient Human civilization on Seoul 5. Nu-Ar and Xathan uncovered a large cache of powerful Seoularian crystals within the city, leading to their imprisonment as the Empire seized the crystals for itself for weapons development.
