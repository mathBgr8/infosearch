Knowt's Disease
953

Knowt's Disease was a degenerative affliction that destroyed body tissue. Pter Thanas died of it less than five years after being diagnosed.Assault at Selonia The wealthy Coruscanti businessman, Archa Sabis was afflicted with a non-lethal form of the disease.
