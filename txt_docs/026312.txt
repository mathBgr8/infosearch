Talnar's Tatooine Traitor
2522

|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Talnar's Tatooine Traitor" is a scenario for the Star Wars Miniatures game, written by Sterling Hershey in 2004. It depicts a skirmish on Tatooine between Rebels led by Lieutenant Talnar, and a squad of stormtroopers. LT Talnar, one of the Rebel Alliance's best procurement agents, is pursuing a deal in Mos Eisley with Orex and his gang of tech runners. Their relationship is fraught with tension. During a negotiating session, Orex discovers that his private data stores had been accessed and the information relating to his deals with the Rebels have been stolen. The Empire then launches a raid based on the traitor's info.
