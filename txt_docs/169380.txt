The battle costed the Imperials an AT-AT, an AT-ST, and one TIE bomber. When the TIE bomber was shot down by Rebels, the lucky pilot who piloted the vessel was able to safely crash-land near the battlefield instead of blowing up. The pilot contacted Imperial Officer Ceth Laike to request evacuation but he was assassinated on the spot by a group of Binayre scalawags looking for ship parts to salvage. thumb|right|200px|The downed AT-AT, moments after collapse. The Imperial vs. Rebel Battle was a point of interest in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
