Mondrak was an exotic-looking, humanoid Sith magician who served as Gatekeeper to Krova the Hutt's Sith holocron. Although she claimed to be the "Judge of the Jedi", she was unable to break Krova's mind. Category:Females Category:Sith acolytes and apprentices
