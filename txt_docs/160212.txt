Nici (criminal)
1129

Nici "The Specialist" was an expert counterfeit ID maker who had contacts inside the Imperial offices on Coruscant. His counterfeits were so good that the Imperials were unable to recognize them as fakes. When Han Solo tried to gain entrance into the Imperial Academy, Nici made him a clean ID for Han Solo and even changed his retinal patterns. Nici owned a "clinic" as well as medical droids. *The Paradise Snare *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Males
