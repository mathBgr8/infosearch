Carosi Alpha Hospital
684

The Carosi Alpha Hospital was the largest hospital on Carosi XII. It received a number of patients from the hospital ship Sudden Restoration. On one occasion it received a wounded Imperial officer who was being hunted by a bounty hunter. The hunter infiltrated the facility, but was prevented from carrying out their mission. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Carosi XII locations Category:Medical facilities
