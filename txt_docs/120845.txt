Twenty-Five Personifications of Virtue
300

The Twenty-Five Personifications of Virtue were a religious belief of the Gopso'o tribe of Drovians. They were often shouted out during battle as a type of war chant. The virtues included Truth and Beauty. *Planet of Twilight Category:Religions
