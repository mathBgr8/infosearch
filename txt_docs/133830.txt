Lorekeeper
938

thumb|right|200px|A Nightsister lorekeeper "Lorekeeper" was the term used by the Amani to describe their tribal leaders, or shamans. It was these individuals in their society that kept tales of historical struggles to relate at special events. The title was also applied to other, non-Amani beings that the species deemed wise. The Nightsisters of Dathomir also had a lorekeeper who kept all the secrets of past generations. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Amani culture Category:Dathomirian titles
