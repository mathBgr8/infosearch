Thulpin
560

Thulpin was the name of the summer growing season on the planet Draenell's Point. Due to the local year on Draenell's Point lasting for 4.2 standard years, Thulpin lasted for fourteen standard months, accounting for a quarter of Draenell's Point's orbital cycle. *Star Wars Gamemaster Kit *Classic Campaigns Category:Seasons of Draenell's Point
