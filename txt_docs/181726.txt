Ellu Viss
1325

Ellu Viss was a male Jedi Padawan in 130 ABY. Viss survived the Battle of Ossus, where the Padawan managed to recover Tionne Solusar's records, recorded nearly a century prior. Viss, using Solusar's records, recorded the Jedi Order's plight against the One Sith. *Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Jedi trainees of the New Jedi Order Category:Males
