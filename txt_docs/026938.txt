Pentastar Alignment Treaty
1076

The Pentastar Alignment Treaty was the founding document of the Pentastar Alignment. It was signed on the Reaper following the end of the Pentastar Talks by Moff Ardus Kaine and representatives of the Velcar Free Commerce Zone. Category:Pentastar Alignment laws and acts Category:Treaties
