Stuart Zissu
1857

Stuart Zissu was a Human male Rebel pilot and the son of the governor of Delantine, Trux Zissu. His mother died before he was twelve. Zissu was a bit of a rebel and would play space poker with the X-wing pilots while on Yavin 4. In addition, he pulled pranks such as dumping Bassel sea salt into the food, causing the pilots to use up their water rations.
