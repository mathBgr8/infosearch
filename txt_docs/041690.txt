He frequented the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall located in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary Coruscant, prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY.The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. During a night in that year, he was present in the Outlander Club as Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered, searching for bounty hunter Zam Wesell. As Skywalker walked around the club, he lurked behind the Twi'lek Lunae Minx and the criminal Achk Med-Beq. Being a Quarren, Ren-Quarr's species added to the interplanetary feel of the club on that night. He wore dark colored clothing while at the club. Ren-Quarr first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The Outlander Club scenes including Ren-Quarr were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney on July 17, 2000.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones Prior to the film's release, the character was first pictured in the documentary "A Twinkle Beyond Pluto," released on July 31, 2001. The character's name first appeared in the StarWars.com web article "Cantina Roll-Call: Shedding Light on Some Alien Aliases," published on May 2, 2003, where it was applied to a picture of the Quarren Senator Tikkes. The name would first be applied to the character seen in Episode II in the article "Who's Who in the Outlander Club: It's Ladies' Night," published in the seventy-fifth issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine on April 6, 2004.
