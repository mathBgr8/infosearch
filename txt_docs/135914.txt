Plesstil
3193

Plesstil was an astronomical objectDark Force Rising Sourcebook located in the Plesstil system, a part of the Outer Rim Territories' Veragi sector. A hyperlane linked Plesstil to the Veragi system as well as the northern reaches of the galaxy. Plesstil was the homeworld of the Human male Terkuss, who was born in 14 BBY. At some point by 7 ABY,Dark Force Rising Sourcebook, which is set during the events of Dark Force Rising, states that Terkuss is 23 years old and has served with the Imperial Army for at least two years. The Essential Reader's Companion dates the latter work to 9 ABY, so Terkuss must have been born in 14 BBY and must have joined the Imperial Military at some point by 7 ABY. Terkuss was conscripted into the military forces of the Galactic Empire. Although the new recruit attempted to refuse to serve at first, the Imperials forced Terkuss to cooperate by threatening to destroy Plesstil. Plesstil was first mentioned in Dark Force Rising Sourcebook, a 1992 supplement authored by Bill Slavicsek for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
