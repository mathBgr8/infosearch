Pink Lizard Thunderbolt
816

The Pink Lizard Thunderbolt was a more powerful variation on the Elshandruu Pica Thundercloud. It was named in honor of the reptiloid inventor who concocted it in Margath's on Elshandruu Pica, who, once having invented it, would drink nothing else. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Cocktails
