It was heavily defended, at one point in 4 ABY its defenses included a squadron of TIE/LN starfighters, two squadrons of TIE/IN interceptors and a squadron of TIE/sa bombers, as well as the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Inexorable. However, it was unclear whether these were the actual station's defenses, or Judicator's own complement of starfighters. thumb|left|The interior of the Outpost.
