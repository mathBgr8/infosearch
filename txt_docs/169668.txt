Duinuogwuin (language)
673

Duinuogwuin was the native language of the Star Dragons. It was a series of hissing sibilants and guttural growls. Most other species could not reproduce the language with their vocal cords. the language had a written form. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Languages
