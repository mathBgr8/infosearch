Escape from Rattatak
3374

The Escape from Rattatak was the escape of Obi-Wan Kenobi and the ARC clone trooper Alpha-17 from Asajj Ventress's citadel of Rattatak. Ventress tormented Obi-Wan with muscle maggots and a Sith torture mask. She did this to prove him unworthy of being a potential apprentice to Dooku. Obi-Wan and Alpha escaped from their cells and killed Asajj's guards and tried to escape on the landing pad. Asajj intercepted them, but couldn't prevent them from escaping. They were pursued by Separatist fighters, but were destroyed by bounty hunters trying to get Jedi bounties.
