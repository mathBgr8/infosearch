B-7 light freighter
3411

The Loronar B-7 light freighter was a starship used throughout the Galaxy. It was 19 meters long, it carried 1 pilot, 1 gunner, 8 passengers, and 1 metric ton of cargo. It boasted one light laser cannon. Its cockpit was inset underneath an overhanging element of the mail hull, and sat above the large downward folding loading platform. It also had a radar dish. The B-7 was manufactured decades before the Clone Wars. The bounty hunter Cradossk used a B-7 leased from the Guild early in his career.The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett During the last months of the Clone Wars, Grand Master Yoda purchased a B-7 freighter to take Jedi Padawans Whie Malreaux and Scout, Tac-Spec Footman droid Fidelis and himself from Jovan Station to Vjun to meet Count Dooku. In 3 BBY, the B-7 Starhaven belonged to the spy Aveca Dunn.
