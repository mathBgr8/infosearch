|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=37 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 32 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: Last Stand on Ord Mantell |issue=2 }} Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: Last Stand on Ord Mantell 2 was the second issue in the Star Wars: Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan: Last Stand on Ord Mantell series of comics. It was released February 21, 2001. Investigating the deaths of a cargo freighter's crew, Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi travel to a moisture plant on Ord Mantell. There, they they learn that a ruthless land baron named Taxer Sundown may have had ties with the ill-fated freighter. But when they discover that most of the Ord Mantell colonists are convinced that Taxer Sundown is a Jedi, Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan realize that they might be up against more than they can handle!
