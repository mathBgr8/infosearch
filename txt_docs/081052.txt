Halbara One
786

Halbara One was the capital of Halbara. All operations of the Halbara's mines were run from Halbara One. Its facilities included a spaceport, an ore loading facility, a small Radell Mining Corporation office building, and an apartment complex for the miners. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Capital cities
