Suspicious Silence Provision
614

The Suspicious Silence Provision was a section of the Galactic Loyalty Act that made silence automatic suspicion in criminal investigations and would automatically allow for an interrogation warrant. It was passed by a group of senators at 0320 in the morning, using the eliminated quorum function of the Law Enforcement Tools Provision. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance laws and acts Category:Second Galactic Civil War laws and acts
