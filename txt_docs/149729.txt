Battle of Eclipse
2483

A battle took place in 27 ABY during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Following the Yuuzhan Vong capture of Reecee, a small number of surviving New Republic ships accompanied the Errant Venture to the secret Eclipse Station, a Jedi base in the Deep Core. A Yuuzhan Vong fleet followed, but it was destroyed by Booster Terrik's surprise tactics and the forces of the Jedi base, which was kept secret. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Battles in the Deep Core Category:Battles of the Yuuzhan Vong War
