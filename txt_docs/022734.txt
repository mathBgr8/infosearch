Jerem Cadmir
1783

Jerem Cadmir, born on Corellia, was a Human male scientist of ExGal-4 on Belkadan in 25 ABY. When he, Tee-ubo Doole, Bendodi Ballow-Reese, and Luther De'Ono were sent out to investigate an incoming storm, all but Jerem succumbed to noxious fumes being emitted by dweebits planted there by the Yuuzhan Vong Yomin Carr as part of the preparation for his species' invasion. Cadmir, on what little oxygen he had left, attempted to get back to the ExGal-4 compound, but was confronted and killed by Yomin Carr, who broke his neck and felt he had shown a great amount of respect by killing him honorably. *The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Astronomers Category:Corellians Category:Extragalactic Society members Category:Humans Category:Males
