Mike Deodato, Jr.
4843

}} Mike Deodato, Jr. (born Deodato Taumaturgo Borges Filho on May 23, 1963) is a Brazilian comic book artist who penciled the comic story "Outbid But Never Outgunned" published by Dark Horse Comics. Deodato has drawn the first, third, and fifth issues of Marvel Comics' crossover event Star Wars: Vader Down. He provided the main cover to four issues from The Last Flight of the Harbinger story arc and variant covers for the first issue and all five issues of Yoda's Secret War from the Star Wars series. He also drew the cover for The Force Awakens Adaptation 3, as well as variant covers for various other issues, such as the first issues of Poe Dameron and Shattered Empire. He has also worked on many other Marvel series', with lengthy runs on titles such as The Amazing Spider-Man, Dark Avengers and New Avengers.
