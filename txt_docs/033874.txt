|habitat= |diet= |language= }} }} Adegan eels were snake-like creatures indigenous to Adega. They were also eaten by the royalty of Empress Teta. The leaders of the Krath used the eels in the takeover of the Empress Teta system. When trying to buy spice from Nem'ro the Hutt during the Cold War, Nokril offered him Adegan eels.
