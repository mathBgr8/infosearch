Constrictor
2719

The Constrictor was a CR90 corvette in the service of Warlord Zsinj. Like the Night Caller, its enlarged forward hold was modified to carry four TIE/LN starfighters. Along with the Nebulon-B frigate Provocateur and Night Caller, Constrictor was assigned to support Admiral Apwar Trigit's Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Implacable at the Battle of Talasea.
