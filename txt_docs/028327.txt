Bok (Mon Calamari)
1709

Bok was a male Mon Calamari Rebel who commanded the Grey Damsel as its Captain during the Galactic Civil War. Bok's ship was present at the attack on Tak Base during the dark trooper demonstration assault, and temporarily escaped the following devastation. Admiral Toka instructed Bok to take the Grey Damsel to warn allies of the new dark trooper threat. Bok ordered his crew to make a jump to hyperspace, before he realized that a dark trooper had snuck aboard. Bok and his entire crew were killed, and the ship crash-landed on the surface of Tatooine. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alliance Fleet captains Category:Males Category:Mon Calamari
