Dreebo was a starfighter pilot who worked as an enforcer for the Stenness Node&ndash;based crime lord Bogga the Hutt in 3999 BBY. Although Dreebo was not one of Bogga's best, he was assigned to protect ore haulers in the Stenness system from pirate attacks. That year, Dreebo was sent to defend a Nessie Ithullan ore hauler in the Stenness system that was captained by Bogga's client Gruna. Dreebo, flying escort for the ore hauler in his light tactical fighter, came across two pirate ships that were trying to siphon the hauler's load of mutonium. Maneuvering his starfighter around to attack the pirates, Dreebo drew the attention of the main ship, the Marauder Starjacker, captained by pirate leader Finhead Stonebone. However, Dreebo's gunning skills left much to be desired, and every shot he fired missed its target. As Dreebo continued to strafe the pirate ships, C'borp, chief gunner on the Starjacker, aimed his turret guns at Dreebo. C'borp quickly destroyed Dreebo's fighter, killing him. Feeling merciful after Dreebo's death, Bogga decided to withhold Dreebo's service bonus and not charge Dreebo's home clan for the destroyed starfighter's replacement.The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Dreebo had violet skin, white eyes, three-fingered hands, and face tendrils. He was confident in his shooting and flying abilities and would go after his targets eagerly and without hesitation. Dreebo was a capable starfighter pilot, although he was not one of Bogga's best enforcers as he was a poor shot in his starfighter. However, Dreebo was still trusted enough to be tasked with defending the ore hauler, managing to miss every shot that he fired at the pirates. Dreebo flew a light tactical starfighter. He wore a brown tunic with brown pauldrons, brown gloves, an orange belt with pouches, brown elbow pads, and an orange arm band. Dreebo appeared in the fourth issue of the Tales of the Jedi comic book series.
