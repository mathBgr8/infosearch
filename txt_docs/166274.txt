Imperial Academy of Science and Methodology
894

The Imperial Academy of Science and Methodology was an academy which taught science to Imperial citizens. Among the researchers at the academy were the Issori scholar Arner Figgis, who held a prestigious tenure at the academy before leaving for unknown reasons, and Chief Scientist K'ar Zah. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial Academies
