Devonian
1167

The Devonian was an assault shuttle used in the First Battle of Borleias. Judder Page was on the ship, which was saved from TIE interceptors by Rogue Squadron X-wings under Corran Horn. It was also part of the New Republic fleet during the Liberation of Coruscant.
