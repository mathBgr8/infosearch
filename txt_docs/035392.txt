Jordo (Republic Military)
1938

Jordo was a male Human from Telos IV who served as a soldier with the Republic Military, serving alongside Carth Onasi for a time. However, while Carth stayed on with the fleet after the devastation of Telos, Jordo left, taking a job with Czerka Corporation. Some years later, he informed Carth of the fate of his son, Dustil, stating that he was alive and had joined the Sith Empire as a student at the Sith Academy on Korriban.
