Sabaoth Squadron spy ship
1583

thumb|right|240px This spy ship was a scout vessel employed by Captain Cavik Toth's Sabaoth Squadron around the time of the Occupation of Karthakk. Its design resembled the U-shape of a Sabaoth Frigate, but was the size of a starfighter and unarmed. Jedi Master Adi Gallia encountered a spy ship during a meeting with Reti in the Ruby Nebula, moments after a C-9979 landing craft arrived in the vicinity. Registering as an unknown craft, the spy ship was presumably tapping into communications between Gallia and Reti. The Jedi Master destroyed the spy ship from her Delta-7 Aethersprite-class light interceptor, though she would later come into conflict with the entire Sabaoth Squadron.
