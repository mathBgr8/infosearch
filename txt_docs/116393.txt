ATK 253
1972

ATK 253 was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. When Biggs Darklighter's X-wing, carrying schematics for the Death Star, broke down, ATK 253 was one of three shuttles sent, under Assault Gunboat escort, to capture the fighter.
