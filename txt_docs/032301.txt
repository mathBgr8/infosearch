September 11, 2001 |pages=100 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=56 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 56 is the fifty-sixth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was released to newsstands on October 2, 2001. Issue 56 was the last produced by Christopher Perkins, who had taken over as Star Wars Insider chief editor with issue 55. This issue featured a reverse flip cover on the back of the magazine with related content focusing on filmmaker Kevin Smith, known for his role in the character duo Jay and Silent Bob. *"From the Editor's Desk" *"Rebel Rumblings" *"Episode II Prequel Update with Rick McCallum" *"Star News" *"Bookshelf" &mdash; Troy and Troy Again *"Gamescape" &mdash; Take Your Seat on the Senate *"Set Piece: The Palace of Jabba the Hutt" *"Scouting the Galaxy" &mdash; Help Celebrate a Scouting Anniversary! *"Dear 2-1B" *"The Last Page" *"Behind the Episode I: The Phantom Menace DVD" *:New scenes.
