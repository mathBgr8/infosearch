Connected to the neighboring planets of Shintel and Peirs by hyperlanes, Ivatch supported a small colony whose economy was based around supplying ore and natural resources to Gandle Ott. Ivatch was featured in The DarkStryder Campaign, a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game by West End Games. Ivatch was not featured directly in any adventure, but was included to give background information on worlds within the Kathol sector.
