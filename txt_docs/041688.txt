In 22 BBY, she was a frequent vistor of the Outlander Club on Coruscant. She was often found with her friends Dixon Just and Civ Sila. After Pratuhr was robbed of his money by a double-crossing ex-member of Sisterhood of the Beatific Countenance, Seib Nod, Nep helped to reclaim the money by training her blaster upon Nod's back when Pratuhr engaged the thief in a dock from which she tried to leave Coruscant. As a result, Pratuhr got the money back and the stolen Sisterhood artifacts which Nod used to lure the archaeologist were sent back to the planet Lorrd.
