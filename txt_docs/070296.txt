UH-478
2271

UH-478 was the designation of a Rothana Heavy Engineering Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry in service with the Grand Army of the Republic. UH-478 saw its first combat operation at the Battle of Geonosis during the rescue of Anakin Skywalker, Senator Padmé Amidala, and Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi Category:Low Altitude Assault Transports
