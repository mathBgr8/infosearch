Fine-point laser cutter
592

A fine-point laser cutter was a tool used for precise cutting work. Darth Vader used one while repairing his prosthetic limbs in the early days of the Galactic Empire. It is possible that this is merely another name for the laser scalpel. *Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader Category:Cutting tools
