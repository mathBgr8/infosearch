Objurium
760

Objurium was the shuttle that brought Kirtan Loor to Coruscant from the Aggressor in 6.5 ABY. It also transported the Sith Lord Darth Vader to the Emperor after the Battle of Yavin, docking at Tower 78, level 443, bay 2 of the Imperial Palace. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shuttles Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
