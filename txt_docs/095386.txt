Coldtime
724

Coldtime was a season on Gamorr that was the equivalent of winter. It was a harsh period of freezing temperatures and blowing winds. Most Gamorreans remained indoors at all times, forcing the warlike males to soften a bit, and resulting in much courtship and romance between the sexes. Category:Seasons of Gamorr
