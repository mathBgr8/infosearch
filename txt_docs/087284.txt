Kneeb
1432

Kneebs were small, rodentlike creatures with yellow fur. The Hutts considered them a delicacy and used the cured hides to cover luxury items.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia They were apparently named for the cries they made. Transporting crates of kneebs was part of a lucrative shipping business.
