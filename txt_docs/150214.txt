REPulsor-equipped SUBmersible carrier
1299

The REPulsor-equipped SUBmersible carrier (REPSUB) was a submersible vehicle produced by Aratech Repulsor Company. It was designed to move up to twenty troops into water-filled areas. It required a pilot and co-pilot to operate, and was armed with a light laser cannon. Category:Aratech Repulsor Company products Category:Submarine models
