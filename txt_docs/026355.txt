It was the leader of the task force that conducted Operation Strike Fear before the Battle of Yavin. The Alliance Intelligence learned about the ship while mustering in the Dellalt system and was identified by a Rebel reconnaissance mission flown by Keyan Farlander in a lone R-22 Spearhead. The Invincible was initially charged with the destruction of the Rebel base on Brigia. It arrived to find the Rebels in the process of evacuating but was able to inflict heavy losses on Rebel forces although the Alliance High Command escaped. Later, the Invincible was awaiting reinforcements and resupplies, ahead of an attack on Rudrig, when a Rebel force arrived and attacked the incoming convoy. The Invincible launched TIEs but they were unable to reach the Rebels in time to prevent the destruction of the convoy. The loss of the supplies disrupted the operation for several weeks. thumb|left|Rebel agents deliver the bomb to the Invincible. When the Alliance held a diplomatic meeting with the Sullustans near Sullust, the Invincible disrupted the meeting and launched fighters. Hamo Blastwell and Keyan Farlander, flying R-22 Spearheads, held off the Imperial fighters until the meeting was concluded and all ships fled into hyperspace. The Invincible attack, however, had been merely a diversion. While the meeting was taking place, stormtroopers kidnapped the Sullustan leader Sian Tevv. Farlander and Blastwell were called into action again to disable the shuttle that bore him to the Invincible and hold off Imperial fighters until a Rebel shuttle conducted a boarding operation and escaped into hyperspace. Eventually, the Sullustans set up a plan with the Alliance; with captive Cygnus Spaceworks engineers, they modified a shuttle to appear to be an Imperial vessel. Rebel agents with the shuttle were able to get aboard the Invincible, disguised as suppliers delivering cargo to the ship. The cargo however was armed with an Imperial nuclear warhead, stolen earlier by Rebel pilots from the freighter Gafra.
