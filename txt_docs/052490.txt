thumb|250px|A Gall jetpack used by [[Dash Rendar/Legends|Dash Rendar]] The Gall Enclave jetpacks were a line of jetpacks used by the forces of the Imperial enclave on Gall. Dash Rendar stole one during the Battle of Gall, using it to negotiate the treacherous terrain of the planet's canyons and integrating it into his normal equipment for use in later missions. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire video game Category:Jetpacks
