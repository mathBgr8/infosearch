|terrain= |fauna=Squills |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} }} The Squill Cave was a cavern located somewhere in the deserts of Tatooine and was home to many squill beasts. }} During the Galactic Civil War, a Rodian hermit lived deep within the caves and recruited a spacer to help him save Tatoonine. This spacer would eventually become the Hero of Tatooine. After the Battle of Yavin, a commoner in Anchorhead attempted to lure spacers into going in the cave to kill some squills and collect squill hide.
