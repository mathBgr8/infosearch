Gar (lieutenant)
1157

Lieutenant Gar was an Imperial officer. As lieutenant in the Empire, Gar served under Sil Sorannan within the ranks of the Black Sword Command. He was a prisoner who was held by the Yevetha at Camp Pa'aal on N'zoth after being captured by the invaders.
