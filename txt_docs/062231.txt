Hoopster's Prank
920

The Hoopster's Prank was a freighter owned by Booster Terrik and used in his smuggling operations. It was often code-named Sycophant Jolly. During the Caamas Document Crisis of 19 ABY, a New Republic task force under the command of General Garm Bel Iblis used searching the Hoopster's Prank as a pretext for breaking a Frezhlix blockade of Sif'kric. *Specter of the Past *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Freighters Category:Smuggler starships Category:Starships of the New Republic
