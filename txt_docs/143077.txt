He wore armor with red markings, a pauldron, and a jetpack. During the Battle of Coruscant in the final days of the Clone Wars, he led clone troopers along with Saesee Tiin in a space jump to capture the Prosperous after the Impavid was scuttled. His forces raided the bridge and took over the turbolaser controls. His armor resembled that of ARC trooper Fordo's armor due to the red markings and pauldron. He also utilized a jetpack.
