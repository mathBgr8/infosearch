|formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized=c. 19 BBY Imperial Sourcebook |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Galactic Senate }} The Republic Security Organization (RSO), also called the Republican Security Organization, was a security agency of the Galactic Republic that was responsible for the overall safety and security of the institution of the Galactic Senate. The Republic Security Organization responsibility included safeguarding the Senate Building itself, protecting the Supreme Chancellor, and recruiting and overseeing the Senate Guard. The RSO drew from a pool of applicants that were from across the galaxy to fill the ranks of the Senate Guard. However, they preferred those that were humans and from the Core Worlds which proved to be controversial. The RSO came under investigation around 32 BBY and when Palpatine became Supreme Chancellor, he made radical changes to the membership of the RSO upon the advice of the Loyalist Committee and the Director of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence, Armand Isard. Later, Isard was named to head an intelligence reform of the four largest of the Republic's security services, of which the RSO was one.
