Vice-Chair of Communications was the title held by Lo Vapeet in the InterGalactic Banking Clan. He was also known as Communications Officer, and liased with HoloNet News and CIS Shadowfeed networks prior to and during the Clone Wars. Category:InterGalactic Banking Clan Category:Titles
