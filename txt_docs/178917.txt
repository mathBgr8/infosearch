Deluxe Boarding Armor
1169

The Deluxe Boarding Armor was a type of armored space suit manufactured and sold by Wrokix Works. Sometimes called spacetrooper armor, models typically came equipped with self-healing patch systems, multiple respiration systems, comlinks, and armor plating to prevent possible breaches. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Heavy armor Category:Wrokix Works products
