Luxury 2400 yacht
731

The 2400 yacht was a model of luxury yacht that was manufactured and sold by SoroSuub Corporation as a luxury model. Resembling the later Personal Luxury Yacht 3000, it was fifty meters long, with a thin hull and two outrigger propulsion pods, one on each side.
