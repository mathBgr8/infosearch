The Y-8 was designed as the ultimate in mining technology, equipped with a full array of lasers and tractor beams for mining lucrative asteroid belts. It was equipped with heavy shielding for protection from pirates and meteors. The core of CEC operations near Kashyyyk, the vessels were highly prized and protected. Some mercenary groups that acquired Y-8's stripped them of their mining lasers and equipped them with more lethal laser cannons for use as capital ships. thumb|right|200px|Concept art for [[Star Wars Galaxies.]] The Y-8 was created for the Rage of the Wookiees expansion for Star Wars Galaxies.
