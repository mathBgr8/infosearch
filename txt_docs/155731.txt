D'larah palm
1140

|origin= |found=Demophon |habitat= |lifespan= |description= |edible= |inedible= |nutrition= |effects= |affiliation= }} The D'larah palm was a palm tree native to the planet Demophon and widely seen in the city of D'larah. The plant had been genetically designed to be able to grow inside the city, as it was covered by a large dome. The D'larah palm was noticeable for its bright red colored leaves. The plant was also well known for producing a deep red fruit that contained a very sweet milk. Category:Palm trees Category:Plants of the Core Worlds
