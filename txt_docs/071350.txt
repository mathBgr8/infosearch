Under the command of General Davits Draven, in 1 BBY, the Bravo Team took a successful mission to break out Jyn Erso&ndash;a human female criminal who would later become a key figure in the theft of the Death Star plans&ndash;from a labor camp that was used by the Galactic Empire on the planet Wobani. After a successful extraction on the planet surface, Bravo One encountered a minor technical setback and was unable to jump to hyperspace.
