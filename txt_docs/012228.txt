Asli Krimsan
2498

Asli Krimsan was a plump Humanoid female Jedi Master with black hair who was fond of wearing a burgundy cloak. She lived thousands of years before the Battle of Yavin and trained Jedi for over 200 years.Junior Jedi Knights: Kenobi's Blade Krimsan later created a holocron. A lecture by Dooku was recorded in this holocron before the Clone Wars. Darth Vader kept Asli Krimsan's holocron in Bast Castle on Vjun following the Great Jedi Purge.
