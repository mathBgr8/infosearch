Lunasa
1440

Lunasa was a female Human bounty hunter, who operated in the years prior to the Invasion of Naboo. The body armor she wore appeared to be Mandalorian in origin. Lunasa was one of the group of bounty hunters assembled by Magus to hunt down a young boy named Talesan Fry, who had discovered their plans for the assassination of twenty galactic leaders at a conference on Rondai-2. She was able to infiltrate the Ulta Center by disguising herself. The bounty hunters found themselves pursued by a group of Jedi Knights.
