|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY (35:10 GrS)The date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Empire |issue=27 }} Empire 27 is the twenty-seventh issue in the Star Wars: Empire series of comics. It was published on December 15, 2004 by Dark Horse Comics. Luke Skywalker is a pilot for the Rebel Alliance and a Jedi-in-training, but when a Rebel intel team gets pinned down by an unexpected Imperial patrol, Luke receives an unexpected promotion &ndash; to General! It's all part of Commander Narra's plan to enlist the aid of an unexpected ally that could turn the tables on the Imperials.
