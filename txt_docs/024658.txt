Pantolomin Shipwrights
1108

Pantolomin Shipwrights was a small, family-owned manufacturer based in the space station Panto Prime. It produced sea and land bearing luxury cruisers, which were custom-built. The vessels were built with exquisite craftsmanship. Less than a dozen craft were produced every standard year. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Shipwrights
