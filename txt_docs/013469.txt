Lando Calrissian and the Mindharp of Sharu |terrain=Ice |water= |interest=Teguta Lusat |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species=Sharu |otherspecies=Humans |socialgroup= |language=Old High Trammic; formerly: Basic |government= |population= |demonym= |cities=Teguta Lusat |imports= |exports= |affiliation=*Centrality *Galactic Empire }} Rafa IV was a temperate planet in the Rafa system of the Centrality, located in the Outer Rim Territories. It was a colony world of the Sharu species. Before the Sharu uprising in 4 BBY, its main city Teguta Lusat was the capital of the Rafa system. Rafa IV was governed by Duttes Mer, colonial governor of the Rafa.
