Emergency Strategic Option Six
740

Emergency Strategic Option Six was an Imperial computer-derived logic response to a combat situation involving excessively uneven odds. The option called for the immediate surrender of affected forces to opposing adversaries. The ranking officer in charge of the Imperial outpost at Spindrift enacted Emergency Strategic Option Six when he found himself outmaneuvered by Rebel Alliance forces in 3 ABY. *Star Wars (1977) 60 Category:Combat tactics Category:Galactic Empire codes
