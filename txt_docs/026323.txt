Sterling Hershey
3595

A full-time architect and game designer, Sterling Hershey worked on every incarnation of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game for West End Games, Wizards of the Coast and Fantasy Flight Games. During this time he was also a participant in the FidoNet Star Wars Echo. His screen name is SilverForce on both StarWars.com and the . At GenCon in 2012, it was announced that he was one of the designers of Fantasy Flight Games' new Star Wars&ndash;themed roleplaying game, Star Wars: Edge of the Empire, and revealed on his blog the names of two sequels: Age of Rebellion in 2014 and Force & Destiny in 2015. However, on June 6, 2013, he announced on his website the completion and upcoming release of the first direct expansion of the game, Beyond the Rim, Edge of the Empire first full-length adventure, scheduled for release in the third quarter of 2013. *Flashpoint!
