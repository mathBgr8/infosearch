Pizzazz 12
1132

Pizzazz 12 is the twelfth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated September 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. The issue also includes the article "The Villains," discussing science fiction movie heroes and featuring Darth Vader and Grand Moff Tarkin. An ad and order form for the album The Story of Star Wars is included in the issue as well. 12
