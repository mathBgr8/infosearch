Wwebyls

Wwebyls was a humanoid male senator of the New Republic who hailed from Yn. In 17 ABY, he was newly elected to the Inner Council. He was tiny in stature. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humanoids Category:Males Category:New Republic Senators
