Nanth'ri Pirates
717

The Nanth'ri Pirates were a group of space pirates who plundered the Nanth'ri Route under Celis Mott. After Mott's disappearance, the group evaporated. In the "Rebel Privateers!" article in Star Wars Adventure Journal 2, Nanth'ri is spelled Nanthri (without the apostrophe). *Otherspace *Pirates & Privateers Category:Pirate organizations
