Jamoh Hogra's battle armor
1494

Jamoh Hogra spent a fortune on his own personal battle armor, fearing the consequences of a raid on a Sith dreadnought, only to be, ironically enough, killed in the bath. This armor increased the strength of the wearer and made them immune to critical hits.
