Interplanetary Ion Cannon
1737

The Interplanetary Ion Cannon was a much larger version of Kuat Drive Yards' v-150 anti-orbital ion cannon. The Interplanetary Ion Cannon was able to target planets at interstellar ranges, and its ion beams could vaporize a terrestrial planet's atmosphere.
