Merr-Sonn stun baton
2790

The Merr-Sonn stun baton was a stun baton designed for crowd control. The weapon was manufactured by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. and used by the Corporate Sector Authority. Merr-Sonn's stun baton was a simple weighted club that was installed with a shock charge generator. The baton had a weight inside that could be slid towards one end of the weapon or the other that altered the amount of physical damage that could be dealt out with each blow. The stun baton had ridges that were fitted with miniature shock projectors along its length that were capable of delivering a painful stun charge. The intensity of the stun setting could be changed by rotating a small dial above the stun baton's recharge socket. Individuals stuck with the stun baton were paralyzed for several minutes, with most victims being rendered unconscious after a single blow. A rechargeable power cell gave the weapon three hours of continuous use. The stun baton was effective against unarmed protestors, and was used extensively by the Security Police of the Corporate Sector Authority.
