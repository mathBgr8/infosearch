Sakai
2336

Sakai was an Imperial Moff who governed Thuris sector during the Galactic Civil War. Moff Sakai was the Imperial ruler of Thuris sector who was critical of the way Moff Abran Balfour controlled the neighboring Parmic sector. Soon after the Battle of Yavin, an elite Rebel SpecForce team known as Reekeene's Roughnecks began opposing Sakai. To do so, they used the CEC customized HT-2200 medium freighter Reekeene's Retribution in 1,200 armed attacks against their forces, as well as other resources. The Roughnecks were credited with the destruction of 872,000,000 tons of Imperial equipment including an orbital starship yard, which might refer only to their actions against Sakai.Stock Ships Sakai managed to discover the identities of several members of the Roughnecks and contacted with the special forces of the Imperial Navy. They assigned a special unit, codenamed Venom Guard, to track and execute the Roughnecks.
