Odupiendo racing
1760

thumb|280px|Odupiendo racing during the [[Clone Wars/Legends|Clone Wars]] Odupiendo racing was a popular sport during the last decades of the Old Republic. In this fast-moving sport, flightless Odupiendos raced against each other in circular tracks. The planet Naboo was famous for its Odupiendo racing tracks. The race was a favorite betting sport in establishments such as the Outlander Club on Coruscant. On one of the viewscreens in the Outlander Club, an Odupiendo race could be witnessed.
