Trebodar system
780

The Trebodar system had only one habitable planet, Danton VI. It also contained the ice planet Teshi, which had one moon, the gas giant Narn IV, with no moons, a barren rock called Pelicd with one moon, and a second gas giant Chanj, which had 3 moons. Category:Star systems/Unlicensed
