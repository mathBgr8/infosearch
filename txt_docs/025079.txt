Dodra F'ass

**Galactic Senate *Confederacy of Independent Systems |masters= |apprentices= }} Dodra F'ass was a male Bith who represented Clak'dor VII and the Mayagil sector in the Galactic Senate of the Republic until that planet seceded to join the Confederacy of Independent Systems just before the start of the Clone Wars.
