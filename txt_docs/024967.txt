Dewanga
1513

Dewanga was a male Dug slaver who worked for Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Gorga the Hutt for stealing several of Gorga's personal Twi'lek dancers. He was captured by Jango Fett who claimed the bounty. Dewanga is located in a settlement which has a balcony which has a Dug sniper guarding the door. The settlement contains pictures of Sebulba as well as a podracer bed.
