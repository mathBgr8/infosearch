X46-7-Class Ore Carrier
1601

The X46-7-Class Ore Carrier was a transport produced by Damorian Manufacturing Corporation. One such ship was the Galax Titan. The X46-7 was designed to transport raw ore to refineries. Measuring 175 meters in length, it could carry up to eight passengers and 5,000 metric tons of cargo. It was manned by a crew of twenty.
