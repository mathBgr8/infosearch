H'Kak bean
702

H'Kak beans grew on Tatooine. They were gathered from H'Kak bushes and were easily found due to their intense orange color. Harvested beans were used to create Tatooine H'Kak bean tea. *The Cestus Deception *The Cestus Deception abridged audiobook *Jedi Academy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Beans Category:Plants of Tatooine Category:Tatooinian food
