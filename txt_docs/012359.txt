Clone trooper grenadier
2154

thumb|right|250px|A [[Clone trooper/Legends|clone trooper grenadier.]] Clone trooper grenadiers were clone troopers developed to offer concentrated firepower to the Grand Army of the Republic using grenades. Another favored weapon of the grenadiers were grenade launchers.Star Wars: Battlefront In typical patrols, the ratio of regular clone troopers to clone trooper grenadiers was roughly three to one. Once deployed clone grenadiers would devastate close combat battles. They often used thermal detonators to destroy vehicles and enemy outposts.Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns *Thermal Detonator Trooper: These troopers used mortars to shoot thermal detonators. They fought alongside General Echuu Shen-Jon during the Decimator campaign. *Clone jet trooper: These aerial clone troopers were known to often use EMP grenade launchers. *Star Wars: Battlefront *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Grenadier
