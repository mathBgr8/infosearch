Northstar
1775

The Northstar was a Hajen-class fleet tender that served with the New Republic Defense Force's Fifth Battle Group during the Black Fleet Crisis. During the transit to the Koornacht Cluster, the Northstar became lost along the way and had to reunite with the rest of the battle group at a later point in time. *Shield of Lies *Shield of Lies abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hajen-class fleet tenders Category:New Republic Fifth Fleet elements Category:Starships of the New Republic
