Darth Vader's porter
2163

Darth Vader's porter was in reality an assassin who together with two others accompanied Darth Vader to Aargau on a mission to kill Viscount Tardi, the Minister of Finance of the Rebel Alliance. While Princess Leia Organa and Tardi were traveling along a highway in New Escrow, the Rigellian porter jumped in front of their vehicle from an overpass, causing them to stop, and proceeded to rip their vehicle apart. He threatened to kill the Viscount when Leia produced a gravitational field disruptor and activated it on the porter, causing his weight to increase to the seventh power.
