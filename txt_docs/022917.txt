Valaqil
1447

Valaqil was a male Gurlanin from the planet Qiilura, and was an ally to the Galactic Republic, and Jinart's mate. Valaqil was present at the Omega Squad mission briefing on Ord Mantell before their departure to Qiilura. While the clones tried to remove the colonists from Qiilura, their Jedi General Etain Tur-Mukan blindly led them into a minefield trap laid by the colonists, who had faked their surrender.
