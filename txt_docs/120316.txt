Veda (Galacian)
2048

Veda was the queen of Gala, and the mother of Prince Beju. Veda had been married to King Cana who had died, and she was the only ruler of Galu and the rest of Gala. Veda was being poisoned by Jono Dunn, Beju, and Giba, who served the family in the palace. Obi-Wan Kenobi told the queen that she was being poisoned, and she understood because she had been dying. Her night-time tea was being poisoned. When Beju learned that Elan had the Mark of the Crown, and not him, he stepped out of the election.
