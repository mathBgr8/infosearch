}} Dekker was a courier working for Borvo the Hutt on Naboo. In 1 ABY Dekker was delivering a sample of the Berserker Virus, a bioweapon developed by Borvo's organization, to some Corellian buyers when he was intercepted by an agent of local criminal Kritus Morven. After a short exchange of blaster fire, Dekker was killed and the bioweapon sample fell into Morven's hands. 250px|thumb|Every possible depiction of Dekker Dekker was a non-player character (NPC) in Star Wars Galaxies that was spawned somewhere outside of Keren when the player accepts Kritus Morven's fourth mission.
