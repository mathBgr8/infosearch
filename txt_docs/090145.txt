Gogol (owner)
1168

Gogol owned the Lucky Star, which was one of the largest casinos in the Kushal Vogh on Elerion. He planned to help Nescan Tal'yo give his Rebel holocube to the Galactic Empire, but his plan was foiled by the Quetzal Sisters and Leia Organa, who destroyed the cube. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males Category:Business owners and executives
