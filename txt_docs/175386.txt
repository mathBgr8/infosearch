Battle of Bright Tree Village (disambiguation)
1015

Battle of Bright Tree Village was the name of a number of conflicts which took place in Bright Tree Village on the forest moon of Endor: Battles: *Battle of Bright Tree Village (Zorbian Space Pirate crisis): The Ewoks are attacked by the Zorbian space pirates, led by Captain Krag. *Battle of Bright Tree Village (Kradak crisis): The village comes together to fight off a monstrous Kradak. *Battle of Bright Tree Village (Stranglethorn crisis): The village is attacked by a rampaging stranglethorn. *Battle of Bright Tree Village (Stranger War): The village is attacked by the Duloks, under the leadership of the Stranger. *Battle of Bright Tree Village (Imperial-Ewok War): The village is attacked by Raygar, an Imperial scientist. *
