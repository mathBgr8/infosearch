Nixor (species)
581

The Nixors were a race from the planet Nixor in the Nixor system. They were in a longstanding feud with the rest of the system during the early years of the Galactic Empire. The Nixors operated the decrepit Nixor Spaceport. *The Last of the Jedi: Dark Warning *The Essential Atlas *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Mid Rim sentient species
