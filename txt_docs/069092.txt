Fuzzynettle
789

thumb|250px|Fuzzynettles. The fuzzynettle was a plant native to the forest moon of Endor. It was known to the Ewoks of Bright Tree Village to have medicinal properties. At one time, Wicket Wystri Warrick went out to retrieve a sample of the plant for the medicine man Logray, who used it to create a medicine which helped cure Latara of a disease. *Wicket Finds a Way: An Ewok Adventure *The Essential Guide to Characters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Plants of Endor
