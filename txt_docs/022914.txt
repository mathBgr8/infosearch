Love is Waiting
1550

Love is Waiting was a holographic drama Han Solo purchased from his fellow smuggler Sonniod to replace the dull documentary Varn, World of Water at his makeshift holotheater on Kamar. Due to communication difficulties, Solo failed to realize that the desert dwelling Kamarian Badlanders revered water and had set up a religion around his depiction of a water planet. The alternate film caused the Cult of Varn to riot, forcing Solo and his companions to flee in the Millennium Falcon. *Han Solo's Revenge *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Holofilms
