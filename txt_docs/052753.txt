|published in=Star Wars Tales 7 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by="Single Cell" |followed by="Jedi Chef" }} "Nerf Herder" is a comic that was published in Star Wars Tales 7. The main character, Domo Jones, a young nerf herder from Tatooine, decides to help the Alliance to Restore the Republic by sabotaging an Imperial purchase of proton torpedoes. He enlists the help of the Twi'lek waitress JillJoo Jab, the protocol droid UR-1, and the Bith musician Blerx.
