Destiny (Imperial starship)
1336

The Destiny was an Imperial craft under the command of General Wessel and Imperial Sovereign Protector Carnor Jax. It was used in the hunt for Kir Kanos. Most of the crew were killed on Zaddja by a trap set by Kanos. *Crimson Empire 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital ships Category:Starships of the Crimson Empire
