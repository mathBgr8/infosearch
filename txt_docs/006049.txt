Hebeth's surface consisted of molten rock. Hebeth was first mentioned in "Return to Prosperity," the first Planet Hoppers article in the series Beheboth: Blood and Water. The article was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on June 3, 2004.
