Instant camera
1102

thumb|right|A [[Wookiee/Legends|Wookiee snapping a picture with an instant camera.]] An instant camera was a type of recording device which captured a still image of an event and then printed it on a small page of paper. This piece of paper would then be ejected from the camera. A flash of light was also released simultaneously with the recording, presumably to provide additional brightness to the captured image. A device of this type was used by an unknown Wookiee in an unknown cantina, to take a picture of Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi and fellow Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker. However, the printed image was not that of the two friends; instead, the printout showed a picture of an aged Obi-Wan dueling Darth Vader. *LEGO Star Wars: Revenge of the Brick Category:Cameras
