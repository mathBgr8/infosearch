*8,000 credits |value= |structure= |size= |color= |material=Metal with leather straps |length= |width= |height= |weight=100 kilograms |protection= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation= }} M'uhk'gfa was a type of heavy battle armor that was utilized by many Gamorrean warriors. This type of battle plate was traditionally welded by the warriors that wore them, welding pieces of metal that had been scavenged from the battlefields of their victories, giving each individual's armor a distinct appearance. Ironically in Knights of the Old Republic II, only Humans can equip this armor despite it being made for Gamorreans.
