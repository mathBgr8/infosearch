|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=2 BBYAccording to the entry for Soontir Fel in The New Essential Guide to Characters, this event occurs two years after the Battle of Nar Shaddaa. However, later sources shifted the battle’s date to 4 BBY. Works published around the same time as Characters, such as The New Essential Chronology, instead placed the battle in 3 BBY, which aligns with the timeline presented in Star Wars: Empire issue 8, depicting the mutiny shortly before the Battle of Yavin. More recent references, including The Essential Reader's Companion, ultimately standardized the Battle of Nar Shaddaa as occurring in 4 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Empire |issue=10 }} Empire 10 is the tenth issue in the Star Wars: Empire series of comics. It was published on July 23, 2003 by Dark Horse Comics. General Dodonna was one of the Old Republic's most brilliant strategists. Now he must use all of his knowledge and all of his tricks to outwit the commanders of the Imperial fleet--many of whom were trained with his own tactics! To give the fledgling Rebel Alliance time to consolidate its forces, Dodonna chooses as a base an ancient, deserted temple on a moon orbiting a gaseous planet. But setting up Yavin Base is complicated by the need to raid Imperial convoys, internal squabbles among the Rebel leaders, and by the monstrous creatures who have also laid claim to the temple! This two-issue story reveals little-known secrets about the early Rebellion.
