Trautmann |publisher=West End Games |release date=May 1996Star Wars Adventure Journal 10 |type=Paperback |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0874312736 |series=The DarkStryder Campaign series |preceded by=The Kathol Outback |followed by=Endgame }} The Kathol Rift is the third installment in The DarkStryder Campaign series, a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. It was published in May 1996. This adventure is set in 8 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion The complete campaign consists of: *The DarkStryder Campaign *The Kathol Outback *The Kathol Rift *Endgame The crew of the FarStar returns in the second supplement for the DarkStryder Campaign! The New Republic vessel FarStar has relentlessly pursued rogue Imperial Moff Sarn through the uncharted reaches of the Kathol Outback and has arrived at the mysterious Kathol Rift, an ancient region of space with a dark, terrible history. The Rift - a monstrous cloud of charged particles, radioactive storms and energy discharges - is a navigator's nightmare. Stories abound about the effect the Rift has on sane beings, stories of madness, despair and terror. Superstitious spaces avoid this segment of space as if it were cursed, telling of terrifying hallucinations, random navigational anomalies and other inexplicable phenomena. But somewhere inside this "seething cauldron of galactic power" lie the clues that will ultimately lead the New Republic crew to its final showdown with Sarne and his mysterious benefactor ... DarkStryder. The Kathol Rift features source material on this fearsome region of space, tips on integrating new sub-plots into the multi-character DarkStryder Campaign, and six new adventures that lead the New Republic crew closer to their final battle with the forces of the Empire. *Harm's Way *Rogue Elements *Waystation *Home *Showdown *Campaign Update *Introduction *The Kathol Rift *Gamemaster Crew Notes
