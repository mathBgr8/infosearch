Lucinta Cal-Meg
1870

*Academy of CaridaThe Hutt Gambit |masters= |apprentices= }} Lucinta Cal-Meg was the Physics Professor and Student Adviser at the Academy of Carida when Han Solo and Mako Spince were cadets training to become officers. Cal-Meg noted Cadet Solo's innate intelligence and the fact that he had attempted to improve his grades.The Essential Guide to Warfare Cal-Meg was present at Spince's expulsion proceedings after the cadet used antimatter stolen from the professor's own physics laboratory to blow up the Academy's Mascot Moon.
