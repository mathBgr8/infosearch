Jedi Counseling
4408

|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Jedi Counseling was a regular feature on the Wizards of the Coast website which provided rules clarifications for players of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game, the later Saga Edition roleplaying game, and Star Wars Miniatures. It ultimately had 115 issues posted. It was written by Gary M. Sarli for its latter run, but was written for the first 50 issues by J.D. Wiker. Issues 1 to 99 dealt with the Star Wars Roleplaying Game, while issues 100 to 115 dealt with the Saga Edition game system. The Jedi Counseling articles dealt with various themes.
