Sela (cargo ferry group)
1393

thumb|Group Sela picking up [[Cargo container/Legends|Cargo containers with TIE Defender prototypes.]] Imperial Grand Admiral Zaarin utilized three Cargo Ferries named Sela as part of the "Arms Race" during the Galactic Civil War. Cargo Ferries Sela transported TIE Defender prototypes to rendezvous with Escort Carrier Hininbirg, headed to Coruscant for review by Emperor Palpatine.
