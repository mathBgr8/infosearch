It was the site of a battle during the Clone Wars. Clone Commander Jai'galaar embarrassed himself during the retreat from Katraasii, and was demoted to captain for his actions. After the fall of the Galactic Empire, Katraasii was still held by the Imperial Remnant for several years. However, by 40 ABY it was a member world of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances. During the Second Galactic Civil War, representatives from Katraasii condemned the raids on Corellian citizens by the Galactic Alliance Guard.
