Shelkonwa was the capital planet of Shelsha sector. Its capital was Makrin City that contained Shelkonwa governor's palace. The planet had three spaceports, including Makrin Main Spaceport and Greencliff Regional Spaceport. Shelkonwa joined the Galactic Republic prior to the Indecta Era. During the Alsakan Conflicts, it supported Alsakan. In 0.5 ABY, Princess Leia Organa traveled to Makrin City in order to negotiate the support of the Rebel Alliance in Imperial Governor Barshnis Choard's plot to declare the independence of Shelsha sector. Choard was eventually betrayed by his Chief Administrator, Vilim Disra who asked the assistance of Darth Vader.
