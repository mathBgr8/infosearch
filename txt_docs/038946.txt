Lag Mettier
1460

Lag Mettier was a Rebel Alliance ground crew officer stationed at Echo Base around the time of the Battle of Hoth and was captured during the battle. After his capture, he was transferred to Lusankya, where he spent several years before being sent, along with several other prisoners, to the Xenovet facility on Commenor to be disposed of. He, along with most of the other prisoners, were rescued in a joint operation between Rogue Squadron and the Katarn Commandos.
