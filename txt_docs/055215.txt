Delban Faxicorp
1652

|founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Delban Faxicorp was a large droid manufacturer that was considered troubled after the Clone Wars and collapse of the Trade Federation. Taking advantage of the economic chaos, Nimbanel businessmen and investors purchased a controlling interest in the company. They imposed Nimbanel efficiency on Delban Faxicorp, which became based on the planet Nimban, homeworld of the Nimbanel species. It manufactured the DBX model, which followed the trend of bigger, more imposing droid models that existed before Industrial Automaton brought the R2-series astromech droid to market. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Droid manufacturers
