Marvel Super Special 27
1929

Marvel Super Special 27: Return of the Jedi collects all four issues of the Star Wars: Return of the Jedi series. It is a complete comic book adaptation of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. It was misspelled as "Super Special 26" in the second issue of Marvel Age, which also provided its release date in the Coming Attractions section. Easily the most eagerly-awaited movie adaptation ever done.
