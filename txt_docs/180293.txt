Pilum
1625

The Pilum was a Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transport in the service of the Ripoblus government. It served in the Sepan Civil War during the year 3 ABY. It was destroyed by Maarek Stele of the Galactic Empire during the Battle of Science Station Youst.
