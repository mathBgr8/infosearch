Fractal Heresy
530

The Fractal Heresy was an event that took place sometime around the formation of the Galactic Empire. It proved many of the Givin beliefs false, and thus, many Givin priests left their religious orders, including Chrag'mak'al, who became a pirate in the Outer Rim. *Pirates & Privateers Category:Events Category:Givin culture
