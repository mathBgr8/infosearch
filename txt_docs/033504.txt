Boba Fett pursued the Imperial deserter Abal Karda to the station while on assignment for Darth Vader. After killing General Nim of the Imperial Lightning Battalion, Karda bribed the crew of the freighter Port-Esta Queen to secretly drop him off at Stastation 12. Using the pseudonym Mixxim, Karda paid a tatooist named Bojam Rees with Icarii jewels to disguise his face with the tribal marks of the Duhma. He fled the station for Maryx Minor aboard the passenger liner Drramas shortly afterwards.
