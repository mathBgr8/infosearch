Woosha plant
923

Woosha plants grew on Naboo. They were eaten by Muudaboks, and glowed. They were also exported; some of these plants could be found in the Narsacc Habitat on Toryaz Station, in the Kuat system. The woosha plant was used to make photo-imaging devices and to treat sunburns. *Episode I Adventures 9: Rescue in the Core *Star Wars Episode I: The Gungan Frontier *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide Category:Plants of Naboo
