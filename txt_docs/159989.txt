Ray Vallese
1110

Ray Vallese is an author and editor who edited many supplements for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game and Saga Edition published by Wizards of the Coast from 2001 through 2010. He also edited many articles and features that appeared on the game's website during that time, including the Dawn of Defiance super-campaign.
