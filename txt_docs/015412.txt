The planetoid was also considered to be one of the largest asteroids in the star system. The Namadii Corridor hyperlane linked the Bilbringi system to the worlds of Dorin and Palanhi. Bilbringi VII had no atmosphere, yet it was home to the crystal barnacle species. Bilbringi VII was first pictured in the 1998 reference book The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons authored by Daniel Wallace.
