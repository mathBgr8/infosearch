It was the site of the settlement known as Tilam City, and technology that was relatively simpler than high-technology tools available in the wider galaxy was in common use on the celestial body. The female Human Callandri grew up in Tilam City, being involved in crime of all varieties since a very young age. While employed by various criminal organizations on Pendal, she developed assassination skills. At some point by 0 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports, which establishes that Callandri had traveled off Pendal at least several years prior to its setting, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Callandri traveled offworld, eventually becoming an assassin who used relatively simple technology such as a slugthrower submachinegun and a Q-6100 groundspeeder. Pendal was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
