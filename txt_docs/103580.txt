Brahle Logris
3884

Brahle Logris was an Human male assassin who specialized in poisoning his targets. It is believed he started his career with killing his own father in 4 ABY. He was confronted by him in regards to large sums being embezzled from their corporate accounts. Realizing he had been discovered, he coated his father's computer in a slow-acting contact poison that mimicked heart failure. Afterwards, he was a suspect, and tried, on murder charges three times, but was not convicted. In 8 ABY he was convicted for the attempted murder of a Sector Ranger on Gandalla, and sent to prison on Tamazall. He was freed from captivity when Kumac paid Deshic Mall to betray his fellow Tamazall Militia members and assist the assassin. Logris met with a arms dealer to purchase a sporting blaster. Not wanting to pay the 25,000 credits for the weapon, he coated his money with a quick acting contact poison to kill the dealer and left with the blaster. On Bresallis, Logris broke into a hotel room across from Ambassador Walads's penthouse and shot him with a customized blaster/slugthrower rifle that was designed to penetrate the armored windows and deliver a slug coated with poison to ensure the ambassador's death. Logris used delayed reaction poisons in his killing and made sure he was present when they took effect. He was obsessed with personal appearance and prided himself on his immaculate appearance.
