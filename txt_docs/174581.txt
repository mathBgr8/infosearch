Christine Hewett

|listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Actress |companies= |website= }} Christine Hewett was an actress who portrayed the "space girl" in a blue bodysuit in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. Her character was later identified as Brea Tonnika of the Tonnika sisters, also known as Shada D'ukal. The actress was a guest at Celebration III.
