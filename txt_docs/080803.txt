Avoni
978

The Avoni were the indigenous species of Avon. In 27 BBY, the Avoni attempted to conquer Radnor after a bioplague struck the planet, which resulted in death followed by evacuation. They sent Dol Heep, the ambassador of Avon, to conquer the planet when everyone was gone from Radnor. *Jedi Quest: The Way of the Apprentice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mid Rim sentient species
