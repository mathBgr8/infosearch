Radiance
572

The Radiance was a passenger liner that ferried beings down to Coruscant's surface after they arrived in-system. When Han Solo and Bria Tharen visited the planet in 10 BBY, they were shuttled planetside aboard this ship. *The Paradise Snare *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Liners
