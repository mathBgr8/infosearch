Garnoo
2604

Garnoo was an ancient and long-lived Neti Jedi Master. They had an illustrious career among the Jedi, working to defend what the Order believed in and training many Jedi Knights to carry on that tradition, even until the preceding years before the Great Sith War began. One of their most famous apprentices was Oss Wilum, a participant in the infamous Great Sith War, though Oss had just begun his training when Garnoo realized their time was coming to an end. To make sure Oss' training was seen through to the finish, Garnoo asked Jedi Master Thon if he would take Oss on as his student.
