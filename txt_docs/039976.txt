The supplement is written by Abel G. Peña, and is an exclusive Hyperspace publication. This supplement includes information on the origin of N-K Necrosis and his relation to Grievous, the Kaleesh Elite, what happened to his bodyguard droids after the Clone Wars, what Lig swords and
