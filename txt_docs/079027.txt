Compeer
616

The Compeer was the title for the overall head of the galactic crime organization known as the Exchange. Although details of the Compeer's powers are sketchy it can be suggested that, considering the power of the Compeer's under-bosses, such as G0-T0 and Davik Kang, that it was immense. *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Compeer Category:Titles
