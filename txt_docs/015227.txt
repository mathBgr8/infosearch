Dagro was a planet mostly encompassed by plains that were set aside for the growing of various grains, including sargheet. However, several deep rift canyons split the surface in many areas. Two years into the Clone Wars, Obi-Wan Kenobi discovered a secret Separatist base on the planet. With the help of Anakin Skywalker, he was able to infiltrate the base and discover the development of crawl-carriers. They were able to destroy the base, however a crawl-carrier managed to escape.
