Gamorrean gauntlets
657

Gamorrean gauntlets were gloves created by the Gamorrean race. They were heavy and clumsy, yet in unarmed combat allowed for powerful albeit less accurate blows. These gauntlets were believed to be impractical for combat with weapons. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Gamorrean culture Category:Gauntlets
