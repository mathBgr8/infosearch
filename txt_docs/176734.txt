Trandoshan Elites
1608

thumb|right|200px|A Trandoshan elite. Trandoshan Elites were a Trandoshan mercenary group that worked closely with Trandoshan slavers. In battle, Trandoshan Elites typically carried LS-150 Heavy Accelerated Charged Particle Repeater Guns, with a bayonet, which could pierce even clone trooper armor. They also wore a set of extremely durable armor. The clone commando unit Delta Squad encountered several of them on various missions such as the Prosecutor infiltration.
