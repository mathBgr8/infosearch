Grain maggot
1782

Grain maggots were a species of insect which members of the sentient Hutt species would roast and eat. After roasting, most maggots would lie in crispy husks although some would continue to squirm in an attempt to survive, although all would be eaten, dead or alive. In 12 ABY, the Hutt crime lord Durga Besadii Tai had roasted grain maggots served at a meal in his palace on the planet Nal Hutta, which was attended by several of his cousins and the Human diplomats of the New Republic, Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo. Grain maggots were first mentioned in the 1995 novel Darksaber, written by Kevin J.
