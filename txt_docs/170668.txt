|last date=January 27, 1994 |media type= |timeline= |num books=2 |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Lost Jedi is a series of "choose your own adventure" style books published from 1993 to 1994. Written by Paul Cockburn and published by Boxtree, the books were only available in the United Kingdom. The series included two books, Jedi Dawn and The Bounty Hunter, which were both later collected in The Lost Jedi Adventure Game Book.The Lost Jedi Adventure Game Book Lost Jedi
