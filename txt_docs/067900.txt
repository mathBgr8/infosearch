Domenico Neziti
590

Domenico Neziti (born May 9th, 1971) was the colorist for the comics Empire 38 and Empire 39. Born in Toronto, he now lives in Italy and is a comic book artist as well as an illustrator and concept artist. He also works as a colorist for the French comic industry. Category:Comic book colorists
