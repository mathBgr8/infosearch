As Star Wars: Battlefront II places the droid rebellion between these two events, it can be determined that the latter occurred sometime between 18 and 12 BBY. }} The Confederate infantry prototype was a model of battle droid. It was created by Gizor Dellso&mdash;a fugitive Separatist engineer who refused to surrender after the Confederacy of Independent Systems was defeated during the Clone Wars&mdash;and several fellow Geonosians, intending to add them to an army of reactivated battle droids in hopes of resurrecting the Separatist Droid Army. However, both the prototype's schematics and the hidden factory on the planet Mustafar where the droids were built were destroyed by the Galactic Empire's 501st Legion sometime between the years 18 and 12 BBY.
