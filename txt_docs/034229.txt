Game Trade Magazine 10
823

Game Trade Magazine 10, published November of 2000, was the tenth issue of Game Trade Magazine. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the Wizards of the Coast Star Wars d20 system. The article was called "The Nova Crystal" and was written by Bill Slavicsek.
