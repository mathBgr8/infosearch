|species= |gender= |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Jedi Order |masters= |apprentices= }} Yrada Soludisan was a Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. Along with two other Knights, Soludisan was killed aboard the Monitor III during the Separatist Crisis. When the Separatist Crisis broke out between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Soludisan was called before the High Council to accept a mission. The Council informed Soludisan that the Confederacy had been in contact with the Pirates of Iridium in the Atrivis sector and were planning to steal shipments of spice from a host of Republic convoys. Under the leadership of Jedi Masters Kit Fisto and Belsed-Qan Idan, Soludisan and several other Jedi accompanied a task force of Judicial Department agents aboard the Republic cruiser Monitor III. Protected by a number of Jedi-piloted starfighters, the Jedi were ambushed immediately after coming out of hyperspace. Surprised by the pirates' powerful weaponry, augmented by their unique gem technology, the Monitor III sustained heavy damage, killing many Judiciaries and three Jedi Knights. Among the dead were Jedi Aruden Kej, Dovish Hokken, and Soludisan.
