Derris
1565

Derris was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle group that consisted of two shuttles owned by the Rebel Alliance following the Battle of Hoth. These shuttles escaped from the MC40a light cruiser Lulsla just prior to its destruction at the hands of the Galactic Empire. The shuttles were carrying the command cadre from the cruiser, but were intercepted and disabled by Imperial Assault Gunboats and subsequently captured by the Stormtrooper Transport Zack 1. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic shuttle groups
