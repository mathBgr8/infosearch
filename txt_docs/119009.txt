Tropis-on-Varonat
1433

Tropis-on-Varonat was a small city in the jungles of the planet Varonat. Founded alongside Edgefields-on-Varonat circa 250 BBY by Humans from Salliche, the city's population eventually grew to around several thousand individuals. It had a small spaceport facility. The Essential Guide to Characters claimed in the entry for Talon Karrde that "Tropis-on-Varont" was a planet, rather than a city.
