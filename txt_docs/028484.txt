Domain Lian
1058

Domain Lian was a Domain among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. While they were ruthless in battle, Domain Lian's position among the other Domains had declined considerably by the Yuuzhan Vong War, and they held secondary roles during the invasion. Four of the Domain's members were killed at the Battle of Dantooine.
