thumb|200px|[[Amee/Legends|Amee wearing the teeth implement.]] A device was implemented by individuals on their teeth. The device were known to be in use at least around the time of 32 BBY, during which the human Amee from Tatooine was known to have worn. The device first appeared in which was released on May 19, 1999. In her final year in the middle school portion of the Jedi academy at the Jedi Temple on Coruscant, Gaiana, one of the Jedi Padawans training at the school, returned from spring break wearing braces. Though Cronah, a fellow student and school bully, teased her for it, none of the other students made an issue of it.
