Ulicx Vinaq
1480

Ulicx Vinaq was a Devaronian merchant known for his many facial piercings. After losing a large amount of credits to Vinaq, Imperial Governor Desh planted a crate of Imperial weapons on the Devaronian's ship and issued an arrest order for Vinaq.
