Enver Hoxha
1458

Enver Hoxha was a male Jedi Padawan during the Clone Wars, stationed at the Jedi Temple. During the Jedi Temple Apprentice Tournament he was pitted against Pirt Neer in the eighth round. While their duel in the refectory drew a lot of attention, it was cut short when Tallisibeth Enwandung-Esterhazy took his lightsaber.
