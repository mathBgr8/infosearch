Keeper (Lucrehulk-class battleship)
1884

Along with the Darkvenge, the Keeper was one of the two Lucrehulk-class battleships in the Trade Federation Special Task Force One. It was destroyed by Thrawn, along with most of the force, in 27 BBY.
