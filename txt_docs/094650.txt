Final Prophet
562

The Final Prophet was the name and title given to the Charon who led the Charon Death Cult. It was believed that this prophet would lead the Charon's goal in destroying all life and returning to the void. Once there, the void would reveal to the prophet all the secrets of the universe, at which point the Charon would also become one with the void. *Otherspace Category:Charon culture Category:Religious titles
