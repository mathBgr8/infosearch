Cathy Munro
2061

thumb|right|250px|Catherine Munroe Catherine Munroe, known in the credits as Cathy Munro, is an actor who portrayed Zuckuss in Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. Though most notable for her appearance as the Gand bounty hunter, Munro also had a small role as the Snivvian Wiorkettle. Munro is a close friend of actor Chris Parsons, who portrayed the droid 4-LOM. Category:Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back actors
