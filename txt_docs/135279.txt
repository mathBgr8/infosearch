EVO trooper
3472

The EVO Troopers (Environmental) were a specialized variant of stormtroopers that acted as support units, or working in hazardous environments. The very heart of this variant consisted of stormtroopers that expressed hatred towards Non-Humans. They were equipped with FA-3 flechette launchers. thumb|left|210px|Camouflage EVO Troopers [[Second mission to Raxus Prime|fighting Starkiller on Raxus Prime.]] Equipped with heavy enhanced armor with breathing tubes and undergoing special training, EVO troopers were able to survive and operate in the most hazardous of conditions such as extreme heat, acid rivers, and lightning. They were equipped with FA-3 flechette launchers, although some utilized T-21 light repeating blasters and E-11 blaster rifles. The bulk of EVO troopers were selected from groups of stormtroopers who expressed hatred towards Non-Humans, making them more aggressive toward the alien species populace they encountered on harsh worlds.
