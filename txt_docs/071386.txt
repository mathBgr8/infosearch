Modbrek
1787

}} Modbreks were a species of pale-skinned humanoids (or possibly Near-Humans). They had small noses and mouths, sharp facial features and large eyes. They were hairless, save for a mane of blue hair. At least one family of Modbreks lived in Mos Eisley on the desert world of Tatooine. Some Human males found female Modbreks physically attractive, though the Gotal tax collector Feltipern Trevagg found them to be wispy and insubstantial in appearance, and merely semi-sentient in intellect. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
