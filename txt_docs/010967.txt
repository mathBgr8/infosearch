The Silken Asteroids was a field of asteroids in a system governed by the Galactic Empire's Moff Derliff in Wild Space at coordinates O-20. The area containing the Silken Asteroids was governed by Moff Derliff during the first years of the Galactic Civil War. The asteroids were rocky and were without water. Mynocks were found in the field. Rebel General Jan Dodonna established a minor base on an asteroid with a thin atmosphere around 2 BBY,"Early days of the Rebellion" is around 2 BBY, according to the The New Essential Chronology. but it was discovered by Imperial-class Star Destroyers belonging to Moff Derliff. The Rebel base's Z-95 Headhunters held off Derliff's initial attack and Dodonna rallied the base's forces to attack Derliff's base and drove away the Imperials. Silken Asteroids was first mentioned in the strategy guide to the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds. It was the Princess Leia campaign's challenge mission.
