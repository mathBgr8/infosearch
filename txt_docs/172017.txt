Red Terror
1349

The Red Terror was an automated police force that consisted of five hundred Warden 10-24 security droids. When Brakiss took over the droid factory world of Telti, he requested five hundred Warden 10-24s from Ulban Arms to provide security for the moon. Each member of the Red Terror was painted scarlet, and the five hundred-strong force was divided into ten troops of fifty droids each. Each troop had one unit designated as a "lieutenant," and the other droids in the troop were modified on the cognitive level to make them more compliant to orders. *The New Rebellion *The Essential Guide to Droids Category:Law enforcement agencies
