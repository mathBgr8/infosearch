There were at least two Imperial generals in the Tagge family: *Cassio Tagge, famed officer who was part of Death Star I's command triumvirate. **His Canon counterpart, member of the House of Tagge, Joint Chief, Chief of the Imperial Army, and Grand General. *Ulric Tagge, younger brother of Orman Tagge and eventual Baron of the Tagge family.
