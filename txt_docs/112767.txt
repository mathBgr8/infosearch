|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=Kiwi |occupation=Artist |companies= |website= }} }} Colin Wilson is a comic book artist who penciled interior and/or cover art for a number of Star Wars comics published by Dark Horse. Born in Christchurch, New Zealand in 1949 on Halloween Day, he worked in New Zealand as a graphic artist, photographer, and freelance illustrator before moving to London in 1980.
