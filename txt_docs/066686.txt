Kreesis
855

Kreesis was the largest continent located on the planet Algara II. The cities of Algarine, Benis, and Tivin were all located on the land mass. The popular Club Prosperine, the planet's main tourist attraction, was also located there. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Continents of Algara II
