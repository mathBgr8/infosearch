Attack on Delrakkin additionally denotes a November 1997 release date. |subscribers= |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-12790-X |issn= |genre= |issue=3 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 3 is the third issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in November 1997. Issue 3 included part three of the serial comic The Rebel Thief, written by Ryder Windham. Star Wars Kids 3 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book.
