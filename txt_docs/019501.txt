Kurlen Flennic
3386

Kurlen Flennic was the Human male bureaucrat who served as Moff of Yaga Minor. Under Galactic Empire, Flennic was appointed to that position by Emperor Palpatine himself, and was still a Moff as of the signing of the Pellaeon–Gavrisom Treaty in 19 ABY. After the arrest of Grand Moff Disra, Flennic became the main voice of the 'old guard' faction of the Council. In this regard, he was in opposition to Moff Sarreti (Disra's successor) and Supreme Commander Gilad Pellaeon, who advocated for a more progressive stance on foreign and domestic affairs. Flennic retained his post until at least the Yuuzhan Vong War. He was beneficial in driving back the Yuuzhan Vong during the battle of Yaga Minor, through the use of his sector fleet. Flennic held off the Yuuzhan Vong forces long enough until Pellaeon's reinforcements arrived, at which time B'shith Vorrik retreated. During that time, he became so frustrated with Pellaeon's tactics that he came close to assassinating the Supreme Commander with a blaster. However, his aim was spoiled by a Force nudge from Mara Jade Skywalker and the blaster bolt went harmlessly over Pellaeon's shoulder.
