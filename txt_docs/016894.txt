Quenus
2580

Quenus was the fourth planet of the Chandrila system, located in the Bormea sector of the Ringali Shell in the Core Worlds. A gas giant, the planet orbited the star Chandrila and was itself circled by three moons. The entirety of the Chandrila system lay on the Perlemian Trade Route, which served as a dividing line between the Northern Dependencies and the Arrowhead portion of the Slice. Quenus was mentioned in the 2003 Star Wars Roleplaying Game sourcebook Coruscant and the Core Worlds.
