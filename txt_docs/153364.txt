Vuin-li
452

A Vuin-li was a type of egg-laying ceremony of the Issori species. It was a bond between a noble family and a common family from another realm. Vuin-lis were heavily frowned upon as forbidden, and it was believed that they never happened.X-Wing: Isard's Revenge *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge *
