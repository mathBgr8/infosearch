Trade Hall
860

The Trade Hall in Cormond was the center of Brentaal's commercial interests. Meetings and policy making took place within its halls, and the shipping carried on throughout the rest of Brentaal was directed from within its walls. The Trade Hall held several expansive trade floors, thousands of offices, gardens, and shopping centers. It also had a private spaceport, which, while permits to land there cost more, was a mark of prestige for all who did. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Brentaal IV locations Category:Buildings
