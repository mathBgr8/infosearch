Orame
1440

Orame was a civilian worker at the Ossus Jedi Academy during the events of the Dark Nest Crisis. She often worked as a flight controller supervisor at the hangar. When Jaina Solo, Zekk and Jagged Fel came to Ossus searching for Alema Rar, Orame warned them that something was amiss in the Praxeum by telling them to watch out for gokobs after the Praxeum was taken under the control of Serpa and his Galactic Alliance Guard battalion.
