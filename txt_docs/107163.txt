|pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Unknown swoop gang |masters= |apprentices= }} This swoop biker rode a Flare-S swoop in Mos Eisley and startled a ronto and its Jawa riders when Luke Skywalker was about to meet Han Solo in the Mos Eisley Cantina. He was a mercenary and likely part of a Tatooine swoop gang. He wore a tall shock-helmet. Concept art of this rider drawn by TyRuben Ellingson showed a complete detailed view of the character, though he can only be glimpsed briefly in the Special Edition of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. One piece shows two riders instead of one, both wearing similar outfits.
