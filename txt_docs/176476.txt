Bonemer
513

Bonemer was a synthetic substance used to repair, replace, or supplement bone. Ottdefa Osuno Whett extended his limbs with bonemer as part of an attempt to change his appearance to avoid the wrath of the Renatasians. Bonemer was about the same weight as regular bones, but nearly five times harder. *Lando Calrissian and the Starcave of ThonBoka Category:Drugs and medicine
