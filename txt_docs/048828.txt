Jamison Jones
1353

Jamison Jones (born February 15, 1969) is an actor who portrayed Rookie One, the protagonist in the live action cutscenes of Star Wars: Rebel Assault II: The Hidden Empire. He also had an uncredited role as "Pilot" in George Lucas' 1994 film Radioland Murders, and a part in Hollywood Homicide (2003) with Harrison Ford.
