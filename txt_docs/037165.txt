Located on the Descri Wris hyperlane, Korriz was part of the Sith Empire before its collapse in the Great Hyperspace War of 5000 BBY, and around that time, it was the site of a workshop owned by the Sith Lord Tritos Nal. Agents of the Sith Lord known as the Patron went to Korriz in order to steal Garu's Warblade, a Sith sword that Tritos Nal had made for the Sith Lord Garu, and they successfully stole the weapon despite the efforts of Garu's agents to stop them.
