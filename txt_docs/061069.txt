Vendetta was the name of four starships: *a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser under Ambassador Furgan's command, see Vendetta (Dreadnaught-class) *an Imperial-class Star Destroyer of the Imperial Starfleet, see Vendetta (Imperial-class) *Prince Xizor's personal starship of unknown make, see Vendetta (Xizor) *an Imperial-class Star Destroyer in LEGO Star Wars, see Vendetta (Freemarker Adventures)
