Jedi monument (New Holstice)
2052

A Jedi monument was a memorial built by the people of New Holstice to commemorate all the Jedi who had died since the birth of the Galactic Republic. A being needed only to whisper a name and the memory moth's fluttering wings would mimic back the words for eternity. Memory moths were then released into a giant cylinder where thousands more were held. Anakin Skywalker added names of his fallen comrades after the Battle of Jabiim. Mistakenly believing that his master had died during the battle, Anakin mentioned him among them. *Republic 58 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Locations of the Jedi Order Category:Monuments and memorials
