Zanthe Nadia
1181

Zanthe Nadia was the leader of the Rebel Alliance cell called the Pelagia Freedom Force that operated in the Tapani sector of the Colonies. She was a native of the planet Pelagon in the Pelagia Province, and was devastated by the Imperial attack on her homeworld.
