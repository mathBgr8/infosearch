|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Farrimmer Cafe |masters= |apprentices= }} H'nib Statermast was the Filvian proprietor of the Farrimmer Cafe aboard the Mynock 7 Space Station. A retired spacer, he had traded his stock light freighter for ownership of the cafe. He took on Grosteek as his partner in the business, splitting the profits 65/35. After a while, he felt like returning to his spacefaring ways, but didn't want abandon his business. He kept his eyes open for a possible investor to sell his share of the restaurant to. He had visited many planets, including Tatooine. He particularly liked desert worlds.
