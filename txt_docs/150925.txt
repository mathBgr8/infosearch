At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Klatooinian mercenary Beatas spent a short time employed in the Timja system. During his stay there, Beatas was exposed to too much of the Timja Suns' electromagnetic radiation, resulting in the region of his otherwise dark green skin around his eyebrows turning yellow. The Timja Suns were mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
