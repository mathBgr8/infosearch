Troll Magazine 2
1156

Troll Magazine 2 was a roleplaying magazine from 1998. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "Centripetal Drive," and was written by Floyd C. Wesel. Issue #0 of Eclipse Studios' Troll Magazine was released at Gen Con Game Fair and the magazine was announced in a November issue of InQuest Gamer by Tom SlzewskiTroll Magazine 1 however the magazine series was cancelled after issue #3.
