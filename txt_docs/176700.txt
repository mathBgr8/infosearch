Sarlacc Project
1522

The Sarlacc Project, also known as Project Sarlacc, was an attempt by the Galactic Empire to create a Super Star Destroyer prototype shortly after the end of the Clone Wars. It was foiled by an early rebel cell from Alderaan during the Battle of Byss.
