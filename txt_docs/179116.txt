Meelweekian silk
624

Meelweekian silk was a type of valuable silk that was used in fashioning luxurious furnishing and clothing. Celot Ratua Dil haggled with a pirate on Trigalis over some meelweekian silk that had "fallen" from a commercial hovervan when Imperials captured the gang and threw him in with them. *Death Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Silks
