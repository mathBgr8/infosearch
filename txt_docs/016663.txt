Another Hopetown
642

Another Hopetown was a colony on Ennth. It was destroyed when the eight year tradition of groundquakes, tidal waves, and volcanic eruptions shook the planet. A groundquake rumbled through the town, knocking down several of the houses and buildings. Then, only a few seconds later, an enormous groundquake, much bigger than the last, ripped a huge fissure in the city and demolished all the remaining buildings. The second groundquake killed a local woman named Shinnan. *Young Jedi Knights: Shards of Alderaan Category:Cities on Ennth
