Stern Traders
548

Stern Traders was a trading company owned by Roger Stern. It operated mainly from Da'Harrin Spaceport on Greyman's Planet, where it handled the majority of operations in the spaceport so, when Roger Stern was captured for being a Rebel sympathizer, it caused the spaceport to go into chaos.
