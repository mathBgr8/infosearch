Shorty (droid)
1269

Shorty was a nickname given to a astromech droid on Cloud City during the height of the Galactic Civil War. He was secretly a Rebel Alliance agent. The unit helped refugees on Cloud City to reach Rebel safeworlds. Shorty was also the name given to the character Short Round in the Lucas/Spielberg film Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom, who was in turn named after the film's producers William Hycuk and Gloria Katz's dog Short Round.
