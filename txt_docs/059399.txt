Golov Nakhym
1794

Golov Nakhym was a male pirate who was the leader of the notorious Outer Rim group, the Hurrim, during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, he gave the order to open fire on civilian transports in the Nezni system in a raid that was unintentionally aided by the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Nakhym was on the Hurrim Base during a revenge strike by the Alliance and Dunari. He attempted to escape the platform's destruction on the Lambda-class T-4a shuttle, Lendros, only to be disabled.
