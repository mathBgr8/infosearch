On her homeworld, she frequented the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the planet's Uscru Entertainment District, during the last few years of the Galactic Republic. During a night In 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Llom visited the club and chatted with two Humans, one of which was the woman Sne Wo. Anakin Skywalker, a Jedi, passed by a laughing Llom and the Humans as he was searching for the bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Shortly after, Llom, holding a drink, walked with the other Human near the bar of the club. Just moments later, Wesell was disarmed by the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi at the bar. Llom had blue-green skin, brown eyes, and black hair. While at the club, she wore a beaded decoration on her forehead, and a dark hooded top that showed her midriff. Ame Llom first appeared in the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002. The Outlander Club scenes including Llom, who was portrayed by uncredited extra, were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney on July 17, 2000.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones The character was first revealed when the extra playing Llom, in costume, introduced the fourth episode of the Episode II behind-the-scenes video series on location. The episode, titled "The Man Behind C-3PO," was released on August 22, 2000. "Ame Llom" is a backwards rearrangement of Mollema, the maiden name of Lucas Licensing employee Stacy Chergotis. Llom was first identified in the April 6, 2004 article "Who's Who in the Outlander Club: It's Ladies' Night", written by Daniel Wallace and published in the seventy-fifth issue of Star Wars Insider. Wallace reveals in the article that the character is a member of an unidentified species.
