Shadorians was the name of the residents of Shador, whether they were native or not. Han Solo interacted with an Avogwi Shadorian when he visited the planet shortly after the Battle of Yavin. Shadorians were noted as assassins. *Scoundrel's Luck Category:Planetary cultural groups
