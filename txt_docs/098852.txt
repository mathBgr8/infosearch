|species=Clone |gender=Male |pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair=Black |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Galactic Empire **Stormtrooper Corps |masters= |apprentices= }} Dalia was a male who served as a First Sergeant and a GeNode, and for a brief time, stormtrooper. Shortly before the Imperial occupation of Kashyyyk, the sergeant was dispatched to Janzor's platoon. Known for being brave and skilled with a blaster, Sergeant Dalia led the charge on the Wookiee outpost, skillfully dodging blaster fire and advancing on the enemy. However, the Wookiees ambushed the group later in the day, and Dalia died in the subsequent battle.
