Dean Williams
557

Dean Williams is an award-winning visual artist and illustrator. He illustrated William C. Dietz's novella Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire. At the 1996 Association of Illustrators' Los Angeles Show, seven of his pieces were chosen, two among which are features in the novella (one depicting the Katarn household droid Wee Gee and one depicting Morgan Katarn's head on a stake).
