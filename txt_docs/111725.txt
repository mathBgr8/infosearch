Unidentified Muun
1789

A male Muun was captured after the Battle of Muunilinst. He provided a code to the Galactic Republic in order to see holographic transmissions sent to Trade Federation Viceroy Nute Gunray's Neimoidian mechno-chair's holotransceiver.
