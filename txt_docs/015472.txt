Wodanian wood-sprite
1286

Wodanian wood-sprites were tiny beings with which Amaiza Foxtrain was familiar. She claimed that Merri Shen Doshun was only slightly larger than the species. They were from Wodan, a planet located within the Rifle Worlds of the Hapes Cluster.The Essential Atlas Wodanian wood-sprites were created by Roy Thomas and were mentioned in the dialog of Star Wars (1977) 9, published in 1978.
