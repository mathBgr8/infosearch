Sabran
1476

Sabran was a bounty hunter and former Imperial Navy officer during the Galactic Civil War. Sabran was well known within the Imperial Navy for her fluency in over 300 alien languages. However, she was sent to an Imperial prison after being framed for leaking information to the Rebel Alliance. Janq Paramexor, a leading bounty hunter, had heard of her skills and arranged for her to be freed in return for Sabran joining his guild.
