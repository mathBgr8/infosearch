Guild of Glorious Mercenaries
504

The Guild of Glorious Mercenaries was just one of many guilds among the Wroonian population. It served as a bounty hunter guild in the Wroona system. While the guild had no authority beyond Wroona, members normally helped other members that they met outside their home system. Nell Indigo was a member of this guild.
