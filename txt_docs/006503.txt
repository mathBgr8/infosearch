|end date=January 1, 1996 |schedule= |format=Comic book |issues=6 |timeline=3996 BBYTales of the Jedi – Redemption 1The New Essential Chronology |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War is a story arc of the Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi comic book series. It ran from August 1, 1995 to January 1, 1996 and was published by Dark Horse Comics. Ulic Qel-Droma has spent a lifetime learning to control the Force. He wanted to be on the side of the good, a member of the Jedi elite. But it is not the Force one must control to become a Jedi; rather, one must learn to control oneself. Ulic Qel-Droma has tasted power, and now he cannot fight the dark hunger inside him...
