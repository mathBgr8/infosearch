Club Prosperine
978

Club Prosperine was the name of the famous and exclusive resort located on the Kreesis continent on the planet Algara II. The resort was very expensive in nature and prided itself on providing the utmost satisfaction to its customers. It was owned by Dellin Sorth, Chief Executive Officer of Prosperine Entertainment and Distilleries. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Algara II locations Category:Hotels
