Gary Astleford
1492

Gary Astleford is a freelance writer and game designer who has contributed to products for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. Prior to being published, Gary ran the Rebellion, a web site devoted to continued support for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game by West End Games.
