Pride of Selonia (Lancer-class)
2725

Pride of Selonia was a modified Lancer-class frigate under the command of Captain Todra Mayn that served in the Galactic Alliance Defense Force during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The warship accompanied the Millennium Falcon on its mission to reestablish contact with several outlying sectors of Galactic Alliance space that had been cut off in the wake of the Yuuzhan Vong attacks on HoloNet relay stations. Though the vessel still retained her original armament of twenty quad laser cannons, the Pride of Selonia had been modified to enable it to carry a composite squadron of X-wing starfighters and Chiss clawcraft from Twin Suns and Chiss Squadrons, which served under the joint command of Jaina Solo and Jagged Fel. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Lancer-class frigates Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
