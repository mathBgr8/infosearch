Destructive Electromagnetic Pulse 2 ion carbine
1931

The Destructive Electromagnetic Pulse 2 ion carbine, or DEMP 2, fired high power ion bursts capable of disabling electrical systems and stunning organic targets. It had a range of three to 120 meters.
