Star Wars: The Official Magazine 68
2228

Star Wars: The Official Magazine 68, also styled as Star Wars Magazine 68, is the sixty-eighth issue of Star Wars: The Official Magazine, released on February 15, 2007. It contains 100 pages. It is the final issue of the magazine, as it was merged with Star Wars Insider as part of Titan Magazines' acquisition of Insider. *Com-Scan *Best of StarWars.com *Jawa's Corner *Q&A *Refresher Reading &mdash; 20 Things You Didn't Know About The Mos Eisley Cantina *Set Piece &mdash; X-Wing Starfighter *"The Man Behind the Mask" *"Hell Raising: The Models of Revenge of the Sith, Part II" *"Spacebase Wales: The Last Corellian Shipyard" by Jeremy Beckett *"Comic Marvels" *"Guilty By Association: Early Star Wars Bootleg Merchandise" *"Unknown Origins" *"Pirates of the Boards" 68
