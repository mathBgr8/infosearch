Great Library of Manuals
843

The Great Library of Manuals was located on the planet Te Hasa within the Gree Enclave and served as the repository in which all Gree knowledge as well as artifacts or devices were stored. This world was vital to the enclave ever since the Gree's slow decline and, as such, was maintained by twelve to fourteen Textmasters. It was hoped that continued study of the technological relics would one day unlock their secrets to the Gree once again. Category:Libraries Category:Te Hasa locations
