Buzzard (starfighter)
2004

Buzzards were starfighters used by the Brotherhood of Darkness in the last stages of the New Sith Wars. thumb|left|200px|Buzzard fighters chase after [[Torr Snapit's transport.]] They had long cylindrical bodies with two pairs of stubby wings near the rear. They were equipped with hyperdrives. Pilots often painted the noses of their fighters, making them look like predatory animals as an intimidation and demoralizing tactic.
