Rusted Cutlass II
1017

}} The Rusted Cutlass II was a bulbous freighter that belonged to the Phosphura Belt Pirates which was attached to Zirtran's Anchor. While most of their fleet was concealed in the Phosphura Belt Nebula, the pirates' leader at the time of the Galactic Civil War resided at the Rusted Cutlass II.
