Wasp (CR90 corvette)
1150

Wasp was the designation given to a pair of CR90 corvettes that served with the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The ships escorted Cielo as part of a convoy containing supplies for a new base that was attacked by a force of Imperial Navy ships, including Alpha-class Xg-1 Star Wings and the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Red Wind.
