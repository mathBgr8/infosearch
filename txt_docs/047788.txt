The Harbinger was a landing ship that was custom-made by the Geonosians for Count Dooku, leader of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. A large experimental design meant to conquer and hold planets, the ship was equipped with a supercharged light drive that made it ideal for invading unsuspecting star systems. It also had seven power cores on its underside. Brown in color, it had two cargo bays that could be used to deploy Ground Armored Tanks, and it was capable of storing several squadrons of tanks and walkers. Thanks to its compartmental efficiency, the craft was able to deploy armies within minutes. The ship was armed with twelve large turrets around its exterior, along with multiple ion cannons. It also had a prismatic turbolaser that allowed it to fire sustained beams in several directions simultaneously. In 21.9 BBY, one month after the Battle of Geonosis, which marked the start of the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and Confederacy of Independent Systems, Dooku and the Separatist Droid Army used the Harbinger to invade the moon of Alaris Prime in the Kashyyyk system. Having recently captured the Jedi Anakin Skywalker on Raxus Prime, Dooku brought Skywalker to Alaris to test the Force Harvester, an ancient Sith artifact that drained the Force from living beings and used that energy to power the Dark Reaper superweapon. thumb|right|250px|Anakin Skywalker destroys the Harbinger. However, Skywalker managed to escape and contacted the Republic. The Padawan's Master, Obi-Wan Kenobi, soon arrived on the moon with Republic reinforcements and helped defend a nearby Wookiee village. After defeating the Separatist forces, the two Jedi departed down a canyon in TX-130 Saber-class fighter tanks to locate and destroy the source of the droid forces. While in the canyon, the Jedi encountered the Harbinger, which began attacking them with its prismatic turbolaser. As the Jedi continued their attack on the ship, Ground Assault Tanks deployed from the cargo bays on either side of the Harbinger. Skywalker eventually defeated the ship by using his fighter tank to destroy its main turbolaser, causing the Harbinger to explode.
