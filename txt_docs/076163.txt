Malka'an Eternal
645

The Malka'an Eternal was an enormous pleasure cruiser, which crashed on Edonaaris after its crew was forced to abandon it, leaving behind all its droids. It was later found by a crew of Human scavengers who were killed by the surviving maintenance droid SCr-114, who in turn stole their ship. Category:Luxury liners
