Hydroponics station
2863

|location=Tatooine |interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} Hydroponics stations, also known as agridomes,Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope, Second Edition were used to grow fruits and vegetables. Common on the planet Tatooine, they were often located underground and made efficient use of water to irrigate the crops. Hydroponics stations were often used by moisture farmers to feed their families, with any excess produce being sold at markets. Moisture farmers who sold produce did so as a side business, generating credits to buy supplies and the occasional luxury.
