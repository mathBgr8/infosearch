Hero's Cross
864

The Hero's Cross, also known as the Cross of Heroes, was a military medal awarded in the Galactic Republic military during the time of the Old Sith Wars. Sunry was awarded the Cross for his actions in the Great Sith War. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Republic awards
