An individual served as an admiral in the Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Revan's Sith Empire during the Jedi Civil War,Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide a galactic conflict between the Sith Empire and the Galactic Republic. The admiral served as commander of the entire Sith fleet. At some point during the war, the admiral questioned the orders from Revan's Sith apprentice, Darth Malak, prompting the Sith Lord to remove the admiral from the position in the Sith armada.
