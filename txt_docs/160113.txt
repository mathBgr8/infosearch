Headset
15537

Headsets were a type of communications device worn on the head that allowed hands free communication with others, predominately by pilots or crew among large starships. They were generally made with either a single or double ear-piece, although some were known to cover a large area of the head of the wearer. thumb|left|300px|Obi-Wan Kenobi wears a Jedi headset As seen as far back as the High Republic Era,The High Republic: The Great Jedi Rescue virtually all Jedi used headsets to communicate with other pilots or their astromech droids, as was the case with Obi-Wan Kenobi during his skirmish with Jango Fett in orbit of the planet Geonosis.
