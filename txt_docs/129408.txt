Great Zelosi Sea
633

The Great Zelosi Sea was a large ocean on the planet of Zelos II. It contained Zelosian's Chine, a massive coral outcropping that jutted from its surface. The ocean was inhabited by large numbers of melk, a reptilian scavenger. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Seas and oceans Category:Zelos II locations
