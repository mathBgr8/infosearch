Star Wars Insider 42 is the forty-second issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was published in February 1999. *"THX: The Sound And The Fury" *:Kevin Fitzpatrick offers a preview of the new sound system developed by THX and Dolby to premiere with The Phantom Menace&ndash; Dolby Digital-Surround EX. *"Darth Maul: Evil Has a New Name" *:He's scary, he's tough, and he's a Wu Shu master. Scott Chernoff meets Ray Park, the actor behind the make-up and double-sided lightsaber of Episode I villain Darth Maul. *"George Lucas Down Under" *:Our favorite director talks about Episodes II and III at his press conference Down Under&ndash;and the Insider is there to hear it. *"Frank Oz: Blizzard of Oz" *:The multi-talented director/performer who brought Yoda to life is back for Episode I. Frank Oz talks to Scott Chernoff about directing, the Muppets, and the triumphant return of the Jedi Master. *"From the Editor's Desk" *"Rebel Rumblings" *"Prequel Update" *"Prequel Profile: Nick Dudman" *"Star News" *"The Wonder Column" *"In the Star Wars Universe: Tim Rose" *"Straight from the Horse's Mouth" &ndash; Vader & Fett Blast Off in New Comics *"Scouting the Galaxy" &ndash; Yellow Oola, Gray-Cord Luke and the Burger Chef *"Jawa Trader" *"The Last Page" 042
