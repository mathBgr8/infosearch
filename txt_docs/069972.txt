ZD-9000 luxury liner
1134

The ZD-9000 was a model of luxury liner produced on Corellia during the last years of the Empire. It measured 500 meters in length, and could accommodate 2,200 passengers and 20,000 metric tons of cargo. The ship's crew included 350 officers and deckhands. *Pirates & Privateers Category:Yacht classes
