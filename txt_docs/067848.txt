Krnay
2025

|terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} Krnay was a location in the Krnay system of the Mid Rim's Vilonis sector. The young Rith Murisson, who at one point underwent a Umgullian blob massage therapy session on the planet Umgul, hailed from Krnay. Senior Anthropologist Mammon Hoole later noted Murisson's origin in an account of her experience on Umgul that was eventually included in the book The Essential Guide to Alien Species. Krnay was introduced in the 2001 reference book The Essential Guide to Alien Species by Ann Margaret Lewis. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Vilonis sector locations
