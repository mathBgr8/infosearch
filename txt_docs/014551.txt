The Tempest-class gunship was a type of small attack craft the size of a light freighter. It was in use around the time of the Battle of Naboo, and was used by criminal elements. The Hawk-Bat was perhaps the most famous Tempest.Cloak of Deception *Cloak of Deception Category:Gunship starship classes
