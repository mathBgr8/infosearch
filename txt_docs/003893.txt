thumb|right|200px|Nico Diath, famed nomad Jedi Master The Diath family was a famous Jedi bloodline associated with the planet Tatooine. While the planet was abandoned between the Old Sith Wars and 100 BBY, Tatooine was still the homeworld to members of the Diath family in the decades before the Clone Wars.
