Commerce Council
875

The Commerce Council operated within the government of the New Republic and was part of the Senate Council. The Council was charged with regulating commerce throughout the galaxy within the borders of each respective government. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Dall Thara Dru was Chair of the Commerce Council.
