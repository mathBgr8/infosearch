Eryl Besa
2003

Eryl Besa was a female Human Jedi who, in 27 ABY, participated in the Mission to Myrkr. Eryl's father had been a space racer, and she had been born in the middle of one of his races, in 7 ABY. She later participated in many of his races, learning to use the Force to discover where in the galaxy she was at any given moment. She later joined the New Jedi Order. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, Eryl joined the Myrkr strike team. Due to her ability to know exactly what part of space she was in at any given time, her role in the mission was to let the team know when they were at Myrkr.
