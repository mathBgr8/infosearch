Nyroska
1237

Colonel Nyroska was a male Human officer in the Darkknell Defense Agency with a great dislike for Jedi and their form of justice. He served to coordinate local law enforcement in the city of Xakrea and bumped heads with both Hal Horn of CorSec and Ysanne Isard of Imperial Intelligence. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Police
