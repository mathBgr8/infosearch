Droid bomber
1884

The droid bomber is a model of starfighter utilized by the Trade Federation during their invasion of Naboo. Gavyn Sykes encountered many of these fighters during his mission to repel the occupation force, witnessing them assigned to tasks such as destroying civilian centers like Rohan Wayside's trading village and the town of Ferentina, as well as lesser tasks of performing patrols and seeking the downed Captain Kael. *Star Wars: Episode I: Battle for Naboo Category:Bomber classes Category:Droid starfighter classes
