Bandonia
3266

Bandonia was a terrestrial celestial body located in the Bandonian system. It was a part of the Arrowhead, or the Slice portion of the Core Worlds. Bandonia shared its name with the disease known as the Bandonian Plague.X-Wing: Wraith Squadron The estate of the family of the Galactic Empire Lieutenant Boes Tark was located at Bandonia, and Tark's Human son, Moxin,Wanted by Cracken who was born around 23 BBY,Wanted by Cracken is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. Therefore, the events of Wanted by Cracken translate to 7 ABY. In addition, Wanted by Cracken states Moxin Tark is thirty years old. He must have therefore been born around 23 BBY. grew up there. When he was eighteen years old, however, Moxin Tark fled his family's estate, eventually becoming a bounty hunter. Bandonia was first mentioned in Louis J. Prosperi's 1993 West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game sourcebook Wanted by Cracken.
