Chronicles (disambiguation)
945

Chronicles could refer to the following: *Chronicle :*The Chronicle was a colorful mosaic that decorated the interior of the Prime Chamber in the Killik Colony on the planet Yoggoy. *Chronicles of the Old Republic :*An in-universe historical document from the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords website, covering the Old Sith Wars. *The Stele Chronicles :*A short story booklet packaged with Star Wars: TIE Fighter, expanding upon the character Maarek Stele. *Star Wars Chronicles :*A reference book that covers the Original trilogy, published by Chronicle Books. *Star Wars Chronicles: The Prequels :*A reference book that covers the Prequel trilogy, published by Chronicle Books. *Chronicle Books :*A Star Wars book publishing company.
