Roa's Rules
641

Roa's Rules were a series of honorable maxims and common-sense tenets developed by the smuggler Roa when teaching young smugglers the trade. Han Solo, among many others, learned these rules at his side. *Never ignore a call for help. *Never take from those who are poorer than yourself. *Never play sabacc unless you're prepared to lose. *Always be prepared to make a quick getaway. *Never pilot a ship under the influence. *The Hutt Gambit *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:Doctrines
