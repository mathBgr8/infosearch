Steve Sauers
782

Steve Sauers is an Industrial Light & Magic employee. He was the digital model development and construction artist on Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones and a digital model viewpaint and simulation artist for Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith.
