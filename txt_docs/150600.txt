Bay of Sypon
909

The Bay of Sypon was a body of water on the planet Brosi. The West Reyno River emptied into the Bay, and locations along the coastline included the city of Rann, the Shoengen Coast, and the Shoengen Delta. Around 2 ABY, it was located in the Brosin Underground's area of influence. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Sypon Category:Brosi locations
