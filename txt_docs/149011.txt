Targeting brain
534

Targeting brains were used aboard Yuuzhan Vong starships to target weapons. Around the size of a Human fist, they were contained in a nut-like shell and immersed in a salty nutrient broth that smelled like rotting seaweed. A targeting brain could be 'reprogrammed' by surgically altering its nerve paths. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:Data storage and processing Category:Targeting systems Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
