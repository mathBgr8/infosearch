Ogana Major
3369

Ogana Major, or Ogana, was a huge blue Planet in the Kessil system. It was orbited by five moons, including the Fourth Moon of Ogana. It was the homeworld of The Starkiller, and was destroyed by the Galactic Empire before Luke Starkiller could reach it. It was a desert world, above which Princess Leia was captured by the Empire.The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film, Adventures of Luke Starkiller: Episode I: The Star Wars story synopsis summary "Ogana" replaced Utapau in the first draft of the script for A New Hope. In the second draft, Ogana Major was the name for what would become the planet Alderaan. It became known as "Organa" in the synopsis of the third draft and again stood in for the future Tatooine. In the third draft, Organa Major had the role that Ogana Major had in the second draft and was Leia's homeworld. Additionally, Organa was Leia's surname in the third draft and all subsequent drafts.
