Discovered during the Mandalorian Wars, the planet was taken by the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders and eventually fell to Darth Revan's Sith Empire during the Jedi Civil War. Contested over during the New Sith Wars, the planet remained independent following the Ruusan Reformation, when the ruling tetrarch claimed total control over the system. Remaining neutral during the Clone Wars, an individual from Anarak IV was serving the Republic in the capacity of a chef on Drongar. At the Republic Mobile Surgical Unit's mess hall, this individual served several different species several different dishes to please the varying palates. Black Sun members Kaird, Thula, and Squa Tront commented that if this chef cooked for the tetrarch of his homeworld, he would likely be boiled alive. A member state of the Galactic Empire, the world avoided the atrocities of the Galactic Civil War and Yuuzhan Vong War.
