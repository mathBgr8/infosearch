thumb|right|200px|Dave Seeley Dave Seeley is an artist who has created cover art for a number of Star Wars novels including MedStar I: Battle Surgeons, MedStar II: Jedi Healer, The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines I: Rebel Dream, The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand and Luke Skywalker and the Shadows of Mindor. The last one was originally created as an exclusive piece titled Rogue Leader for Celebration 4.
