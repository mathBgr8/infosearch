Supreme Commander of the Confederation military
3246

thumb|right|200px|[[Turr Phennir, Supreme Commander of the Confederation military during the Second Galactic Civil War]] Supreme Commander of the Confederation military was the title accorded to the leader of the armed forces of the Confederation. The post of Supreme Commander was created in 40 ABY, when the Five Worlds of the Corellian system were joined in their war against the Galactic Alliance by other worlds, including Bothawui and Commenor. The member governments of the new union needed to find a relatively neutral military leader to take command of their combined fleets, and new planets were encouraged to join the Confederacy with the suggestion that one of their commanders might be elected as a compromise candidate. As the Corellian Defense Force was integrated into the Confederation military, the position of the Supreme Commander of the Corellian Defense Force was disbanded to be replaced by the new position of Supreme Commander of the Confederation. The Confederation led the Alliance to believe that a conference to select the new Supreme Commander was being held at Gilatter VIII. In fact, General Turr Phennir had already been appointed to the position, and the "conference" was a ruse to draw the Alliance into the Battle of Gilatter VIII. As Supreme Commander, General Phennir wore a white uniform in a style similar to those worn by officers of the Galactic Empire that he had once served&mdash;reminiscent of, if not actually identical to, the uniform of an Imperial Grand Admiral.
