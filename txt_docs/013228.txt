Corulag-Jatir Hyperroute
1308

The Corulag-Jatir Hyperroute was a hyperspace trade route that connected Corulag, located near the intersection of the Hydian Way and Perlemian Trade Route, to Jatir on the border of Colonies and the Core Worlds. In 22 BBY a Arcon Multinode container ship was involved in a micrometeorite collision, along the route, and reverted back from hyperspace clogging the route with debris, and forcing the closure of the hyperroute until it could be cleared of the debris.
