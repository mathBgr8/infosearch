|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=11 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood |issue=5 }} Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 5 was the fifth issue from the Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood series of comics, and was released on March 10, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. It's revelation time, as the series begins to accelerate toward its thrilling conclusion. A hair-raising rescue attempt on the part of Kir Kanos goes afoul while the Interim Council finally collapses beneath its array of intrigues. And Grappa learns the meaning of "caught between a rock and a hard place."
