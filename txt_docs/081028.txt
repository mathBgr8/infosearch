He was in command of the Acclamator-class assault shipThe New Essential Chronology Halleck when it was assigned to Jedi Master Mace Windu in 22 BBY. During a mission to extract Windu and his Padawan Depa Billaba, the Halleck was attacked by droid starfighters, and the captain was injured. His subordinate, Commander Urhal, was able to withdraw the ship to Republic space.
