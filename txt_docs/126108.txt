At some point by the time of the Galactic Civil War, the crew of a II-xC Maintenance/Broadcast Ship of the Galactic Empire stored into the ship's computer system a flight plan from Salteract to the planet Narg. Salteract was introduced in The Abduction, a 1992 roleplaying adventure authored by Chuck Truett and published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
