Mugruebe stew
933

Mugruebe stew was a special Agamarian meal that was made from the flesh of the mugruebe, although its popularity throughout the rest of the galaxy was limited. It was usually prepared with roots and tree bark. Not surprisingly, mugruebe stew was a favorite of Rebel Alliance starfighter pilot Keyan Farlander, who was a native of that world. *Boba Fett: Hunted *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons Category:Soups and stews
