Jakan
2396

Jakan was the High Priest of the Priest caste and one of the highest members of the Yuuzhan Vong Elite. Even before the invasion began, Jakan was a senior religious advisor to Supreme Overlord Shimrra. The father of Elan, her death hardened his religious orthodoxy, and he consistently urged Shimrra to eliminate the Jeedai heresy before it gained strength (including a recommendation to send wild spinerays into the Coruscant underworld). Though he expected to lead many glorious sacrifices for the Gods on Yuuzhan'tar, the Priest caste was humiliated repeatedly by the sabotages the World Brain caused. During the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar, Jakan, along with High Prefect Drathul and Master Shaper Qelah Kwaad, attempted to sacrifice Han Solo, Leia Organa Solo, and Harrar to the World Brain, but was stopped by a group of heretics and renegade Warriors.
