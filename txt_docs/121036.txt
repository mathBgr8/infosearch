FX-8 medical droid

The FX-8 medical droid was a medical droid manufactured by Medtech Industries as part of the FX-series of medical assistant droids. Produced during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, it was ultimately succeeded by the FX-9 model. *Star Wars: The Force Unleashed novelization *The New Essential Guide to Droids FX-08
