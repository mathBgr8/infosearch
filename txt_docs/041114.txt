Warrior The Human female Mia Ikova served as the leader of Haleoda, the primary city of the planet Kattada, and ostensibly the planet in the years before the Battle of Yavin. She attempted to remain neutral in the conflict between the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic but still provided services to the highest bidder. A year before the Battle of Yavin, Ikova welcomed Princess Leia Organa during her stay at Kattada. While the princess searched refuge she was found by stormtroopers and captured alongside Mia Ikova. thumb|left|200px|Mia Ikova is shot. She was shot by Commander Karg and his stormtroopers and passed away in Leia's starship.
