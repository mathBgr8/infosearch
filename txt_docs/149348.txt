thumb|right|200px|The [[Ewok/Legends|Ewok Teebo holds a vial of dried Wank's eyes.]] A wank was a creature found on the Forest Moon of Endor. It had eyes that could be dried and used in magical concoctions. Gonster used dried wank's eyes in the creation of the magic cap, a magical hat that was used to trap the Raich in the form of a harmless tree.
