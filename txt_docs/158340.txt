Security collar
2856

Security collars were a type of slaving collar that was manufactured by the Thalassian Slavers. The security collar consisted of an adjustable durasteel neck restraint connected to a heavy chain. The brace was secured by a magnolock, and incorporated shock panels into the interior of the ring. The collars were designed to be escape-proof and resistant to tampering; attempting to remove them without inputting the proper circuit key triggered the shock panels to deliver a painful neural shock that were potentially lethal. Some variants of the collar had biometric or voice-activated locks.Lords of Nal Hutta They could be attached to other collars or a pair of stun cuffs. Jabba Desilijic Tiure used these kinds of collars to restrain his enslaved dancers and keep them within easy reach.
