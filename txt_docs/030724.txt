Logirth
1480

Logirth was an officer of the New Republic Defense Force. An inaugural officer of the New Republic Fifth Battle Group, Captain Logirth served as the commanding officer of Venture. When the shuttle Tampion was sent to the Fifth Battle Group bearing Han Solo, Logirth was charged with establishing a starfighter escort. To maintain tight security, Logirth chose and briefed the pilots for the mission only four hours before cockpit call.
