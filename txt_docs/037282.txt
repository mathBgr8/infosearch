Alby Ermad
1305

Alby Ermad was a male Ugnaught armorsmith whose speciality was designing battle armor suits. Alby Ermand was wanted by Sienar Fleet Systems for stealing architectural designs. He was the accomplice of Artzam Hathan, who was wanted for the same crime.
