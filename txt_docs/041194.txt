LF9 ion engine
1550

The LF9 ion engine was a type of sublight drive manufactured by Alderaan Royal Engineers. They were the primary engines of the Victory-class Star Destroyer. However, the engines were not capable of providing the speeds needed for the vessel to match the velocities of other combat starships or starfighters.
