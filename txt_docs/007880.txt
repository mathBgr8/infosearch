Dark Side Daiquiri
630

A Dark Side Daiquiri was a mixed drink served at Republic Mobile Surgical Unit 7's cantina during the Battle of Drongar. The Falleen criminal Thula had consumed this beverage when she reported her success in tricking the Republic quartermaster Nars Dojah into hiring her to Kaird. *MedStar II: Jedi Healer Category:Cocktails
