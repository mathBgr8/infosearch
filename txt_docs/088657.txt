Cross Crosslet
475

The Cross Crosslet was a Rebel SpecForces decoration awarded for outstanding valor that went well beyond the call of duty. As such, it was awarded posthumously 45% of the time. The Cross Crosslet was a gold cross on a platinum background. *Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic awards
