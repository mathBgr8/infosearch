Evocii (language)
739

Evocii was the native language of the Evocii species from Nal Hutta, previously known as Evocar. Evocii lived under the surface of the fifth moon of Nal Hutta, Nar Shaddaa, having little interaction with other species. Hutts believed them extinct long ago. Most Evocii were fluent in both Evocii and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. Some Evocii were also fluent in Huttese.
