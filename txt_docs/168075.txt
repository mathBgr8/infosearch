Tof Soren Hunting Rifle
1252

The Tof Soren Hunting Rifle, also known as the "Hunter," was a slugthrower produced in Tof Soren on Ord Vaxal. The Tof Soren Hunting Rifle could fire five shots before having to be reloaded. It used a 5-shot internal magazine. The weapon was equipped only with iron sights, but could be optionally equipped with simple telescopic sights. It used a powder charge to fire bullets of approximately 6 millimeters in diameter. Maximum effective range was 300 meters. Category:Magnetic acceleration weaponry Tof Soren Hunting Pistol
