Maree Yad
1246

Maree Yad was a male Dug wanted by Pixelito Grand Council for dealing death sticks to the Gran youth of Pixelito. His bounty was eventually claimed by Jango Fett. Maree Yad's bounty is worth 2,400 credits alive and 1,200 dead. According to the Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide, Setongah is supposed to be the third bounty to encounter but in his place instead is Maree Yad.
