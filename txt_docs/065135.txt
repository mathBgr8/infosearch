Corona Limited
2307

The Mobquet Swoops and Speeders Corona Limited luxury groundspeeder was a popular landspeeder model in the Corporate Sector. At ten meters long, it could carry six passengers. It was available in 500 colors, including black as well as many colors undetectable to the Human eye. It had kudana leather seats that could be converted into sleeping berths. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Galaxy of Intrigue Category:Luxury landspeeder models Category:Mobquet Swoops and Speeders products
