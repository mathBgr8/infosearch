|type=Hardcover |pages=224 |dimensions=11.9 &times; 9.3 &times; 0.8 inches |weight=2.8 pounds |isbn=0345431359 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Art of Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith is a collection of various concept art and other works produced during the making of Revenge of the Sith. It unveils never-before-seen images&mdash;storyboards, models, production sketches, and ILM-generated art&mdash;that chronicle the journey from first draft to theater screen. Among the hundreds of pieces are images and anecdotes covering Darth Vader then and now&mdash;the new look of the ultimate Star Wars icon; conceptual looks at the climactic lightsaber duel between Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi; the evolution of General Grievous from initial sketches to ILM animation art; images of costumes, weapons, landscapes, and spacecraft; exclusive interviews with the artists; and excerpts from Episode III's Visual Script, created to help guide production through the film's digital creations. *Part I - Preproduction *Part II - Principal Photography *Part III - Digital Shot Creation *Notes & Index {|class="wikitable sortable" !
