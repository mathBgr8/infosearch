Umwaw Moolis
2146

Umwaw Moolis was an Ithorian Senator of the New Republic who represented Ithor and the Ottega sector circa 12 ABY. She greeted Han and the Chief of State Leia Organa Solo, Luke Skywalker and Winter as they arrived on Ithor for the Time of Meeting only to have the meeting interrupted by Drub McKumb trying to warn Han Solo.
