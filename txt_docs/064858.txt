*69,000 credits |modifier= |sysmods= |length=20.3 meters |width= |height= |mass= |max accel= |mglt= |max speed=1,000 kmh |maneuverability= |engine= |hyperdrive=*Class 1.0 *Backup: Class 12 |hdrange= |hdsystem= |poweroutput= |power= |shield gen= |hull= |sensor=Fabritech ANx-y/2 sensor cluster |target= |navigation= |avionics=Miradyne Limited RCS-1 flight control computer |maincomp= |countermeasures= |armament=1 BlasTech Industries Prm-3 pulse laser cannon |complement= |bays= |escapepods= |crew=1 |skeleton= |passengers=2 |capacity=45 metric tons |cargohandling= |consumables=2 to 3Pirates & Privateers months |lifesupport= |communications= |othersystems= |availability= |role=Transport |firstuse= |retired= |affiliation= }} The Z-10 Seeker-class scout ship was a scout ship manufactured by Starfeld Industries. thumb|left|180px|Z-10 Seeker schematics It was 20.3 meters long, carrying 1 pilot, 2 passengers, 2 months worth of consumables, 5 metric tons within the ship, and 20 optional additional metric tons of cargo per each of two cargo pods attached. The ship could achieve a speed of 1000 kilometers per hour in atmosphere. A Miradyne Limited RCS-1 flight control computer and Novaldex JV-71 ion engine allowed sublight speed, which was slowed down and reduced in maneuverability if the cargo pods were attached. The ship had a Class 1 hyperdrive and a Class 12 backup. Though unshielded, the craft had a Fabritech ANx-y/2 sensor cluster and a forward BlasTech Industries Prm-3 pulse laser cannon. The cannon was under-powered and could only fire in a limited forward-facing arc. Targeting was provided by the Fabritech sensor, but this meant that if it wasn't working, the vehicle was defenseless. The Seeker was created as a replacement to the Vangaard Industries Pathfinder.
