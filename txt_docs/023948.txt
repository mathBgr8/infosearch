Ovolyan
1030

Ovolyan were bantha-sized reptilian quadrupeds native to Laboi II. They were the favorite prey of the Laboi, the fur-covered serpentine sentients who were the planet's dominant species. Interestingly enough, the Laboi were the favorite prey of the Ovolyan&mdash;but only the ones small enough for the toothless ovolyans to swallow whole.
