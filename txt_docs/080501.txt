At one point, the bomber was struck by an asteroid in the Anoat system and underwent repairs. By 4 ABY, Scimitar 2 was stationed on the Galactic Empire's second Death Star battle station. By that time, Scimitar 2 was flown by the TIE pilot Captain Jonus, who often flew as top cover during bombing runs. In 4 ABY, Scimitar Squadron was stationed in the Endor system, assigned with destroying any ground troops or equipment that might land on the Forest Moon of Endor, which contained the shield generator protecting the orbiting Death Star.
