Echani vibroblade
1456

Used in the offhand, the Echani vibroblades possessed not only the cortosis weave that made them capable of blocking a lightsaber strike but, on occasions, were also super cooled, to keep them in alignment, which made strikes by these weapons icy to the touch. Revan, an amnesiac Sith Lord, gained this sword when he helped the injured Wookiee Grrrwahrr avenge his comrades' deaths at the hands of Mandalorian raiders. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Echani weaponry Category:Vibroblades
