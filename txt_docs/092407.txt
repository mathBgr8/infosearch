Ozik Poyiu
890

Ozik Poyiu was a smuggler who lived during the times of the Galactic Empire. Poyiu was the leader of a team known as Kwuennox Smugglers. During Poyiu's leadership, the Kwuennox Smugglers were decimated by the forces of the Galactic Empire. Poyiu and the Kwuennox Smugglers joined the Rebel Alliance, and Poyiu reformed the team to create the Gale Team. Poyiu was eventually replaced by Aven Cholus as commander of the Gale Team.
