C'taunmar
1372

C'taunmar was an A-wing commander who controlled the starfighter base on Di'tai'ni. She fought under General Garm Bel Iblis in the Battle of Yaga Minor. She volunteered to screen the Errant Venture during the attack, but Bel Iblis refused, as New Republic starfighters would ruin the ruse that the Errant Venture was an Imperial ship. *Specter of the Past *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:New Republic Starfighter Corps personnel
