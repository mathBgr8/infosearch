He piloted a LAAT/i gunship during the Battle of Haruun Kal. Under his command were 2nd Lieutenant CL-33/890 and two Auxiliary Heavy-Weapons Specialists, Seven-Four, and Eight-Three. His gunship was assigned to the medium cruiser Halleck and sustained heavy damage during the action at Haruun Kal, though the ship endured and assisted with the post-battle rescue of stranded surviving clones.
