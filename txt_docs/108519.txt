HAVt B5 Juggernaut
2956

}} The HAVt B5 Juggernaut was an armored fighting vehicle used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. Despite having some armor and weaponry, the B5 model was considered more of a transport than an attack craft like the older models. They were known to utilize a special laser defense system that was designed to shoot down enemy missiles before they reached friendly lines, which made them a valuable support element in Imperial armored columns. Unlike previous models, in which the rear housed an additional control center, the rear end of the B5 Juggernaut would open up to load and unload infantry and light vehicles. The B5 was used by the Empire in various battles as a method of transporting troops across a battlefield.
