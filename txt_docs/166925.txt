Nackhar
1373

Nackhar was Wuher's Nartian assistant behind the bar of the Mos Eisley Cantina. He was assigned to clean up the mess from Ponda Baba's lightsaber injury in 0 BBY. He also ran a fast-food stand in the Mos Eisley marketplace. He was incorrectly mentioned in Nightlily: The Lovers' Tale as "Nackhan". In the audio drama adaptation of the story, he is correctly called Nackhar, but he is portrayed as a Jawa. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bartenders Category:Males Category:Nartians Category:Tatooinians
