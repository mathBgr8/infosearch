Wing-Blast rocket pack
2920

right|[[Boba Fett/Legends|Boba Fett's Wing-Blast rocket pack.|thumb|247px]] The Wing-Blast rocket pack was a deadly one-person weapons system utilized by the Mandalorians similar to the Mandalorian battle harness. It had two nearly vertical wings, each packed with miniature concussion missile launchers inside and carrying a heavy rotating double blaster. The pack also included a proton torpedo cannon, mounted over the head of the pack's user. The pack was allegedly designed several hundred years BBY by General Gustav Zenlav, of the MandalMotors corporation, and originally incorporated an experimental photonic beam where the proton torpedo cannon would eventually be mounted; the addition of the weapon caused Zenlav to shelve the design. Hundreds of years later, the blueprints for the pack fell into the possession of Boba Fett. Recognizing the promise of the design, Fett replaced the photonic beam with the proton torpedo system, and used the Wing-Blast rocket pack to storm Fortress Baarlos, defeat the fortress's Tulvaree guardians, and collect the bounty on Faarl the Conqueror. The Wing-Blast rocket pack first appeared in Hasbro's Power of the Force action figure series, released in January 1997.Star Wars: The Action Figure Archive Its history was expanded upon in Abel G.
