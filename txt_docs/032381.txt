XT-6 droid
1682

XT-6 droids were a series of service droids used around 4000 BBY. Their limbs could be adapted to serve as prosthetic replacements for injured Humans. During the attack on Iziz, Cay Qel-Droma lost his left arm and replaced it with that of one such droid. *Tales of the Jedi 2 *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Service droid models
