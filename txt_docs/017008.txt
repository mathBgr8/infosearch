Erinar
2908

Erinar was a volcanic rock planet in the Eriadu system, located in the Seswenna sector of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Erinar was circled by one moon and itself occupied the third orbital position around the star Eriadu. Along with its star system, it was located on the hyperspace routes known as the Hydian Way, the Lipsec Run, the Nothoiin Corridor, the Rimma Trade Route, the Senex Trace, and the Yankirk Route.
