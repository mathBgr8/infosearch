The Life Monitor
581

The Life Monitor was a newsgrid service that was run and managed by Cindel Towani during the early years of the New Republic. Hiram Drayson called in a favor from Cindel, hoping to run a story that would bolster Leia Organa Solo's appearance during the Black Fleet Crisis. *Tyrant's Test Category:News networks and companies
