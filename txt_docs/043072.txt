Mayjeln was considered to be a courageous scout who succeeded in high-speed heroics and daring navigation. He reached a status of legendary; many Sullustan pilots years after him consider him a hero and would like to emulate his deeds. Sullustan trader Rai-Kalei, who lived in the times of the Galactic Empire, named his XS-800 light freighter Mayjeln in honor of his fellow. However, Rai-Kalei was not exactly a scout, but a smuggler.
