They later lived with their older brother, Rehtul Minnau, in the capital city of Theed, where the two girls attended a private school to learn etiquette, hospitality, history, and the arts. When the Trade Federation invaded Naboo in 32 BBY, Nandi went into hiding in the swamps with Teckla and several Royal Naboo Security Forces members, but she was soon separated from her sister by the growing conflict, only to be reunited with her at the Celebration Parade. Nandi and Teckla then finished their schooling and went on to work for Senator Padmé Amidala as servants at Varykino, Amidala's family retreat. thumb|right|260px|Varykino, House Naberrie's lake retreat In 22 BBY, Amidala and her Jedi bodyguard, Anakin Skywalker, used Varykino as a sanctuary after two failed assassination attempts on the Senator's life. Nandi and Teckla served them their meal, including shuura fruit as dessert.Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones novelization At the end of the day, they said goodbye to the young couple and left the two alone. During the Clone Wars, Nandi was separated from Teckla once again when the latter left for Coruscant to act as one of Amidala's handmaidens. Later in the war, Nandi's sister was assassinated by the bounty hunter Embo on the planet Scipio.
