GandleMotors
2229

|role=Repulsorlift vehicle manufacturing |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation=*Kolatill Council *Moff Kentor Sarne's forces }} GandleMotors was a repulsorlift vehicle manufacturing corporation based on the planet Kolatill in the Kathol sector of the Outer Rim Territories. The company also manufactured repulsor parts for Ikas-Adno, a major repulsorlift manufacturer that had factory-complexes on the world. In 0 BBY,"Omens" states that martial law on Kolatill was "declared eight years ago". The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The DarkStryder Campaign to "eight years after the Battle of Yavin," for a date of 0 BBY. the local Imperial official, Governor Tetrum, instituted martial law on the planet under the orders of Moff Kentor Sarne, ruler of the sector. Under the terms of the martial law, all local repulsorlift manufacturing corporations were required to build combat speeders for the Moff's forces. GandleMotors was mentioned in the roleplaying game adventure Omens, which was published in The DarkStryder Campaign by West End Games in 1995. Category:Repulsorcraft manufacturers
