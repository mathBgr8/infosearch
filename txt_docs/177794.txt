*Trailing SectorsThe Essential Atlas &mdash; Client States and Miscellaneous Regions map |sector= |system=Arkam system |coordinates=M-14 |xyz= |orbited= |distance= |routes= |role= |affiliation= |constructed= |destroyed= |retired= |battles= |owners= |namedcrew= |captains= |registry= |aliases= |population= |interest=Holorepository |services= |sectors= |modules= }} Arkam 13 was a wheelworld in the Arkam system that was home to the archive known as the Holorepository. Although the Inner Rim map of The Essential Atlas placed the Arkam system in the galactic grid M-14, the star system appendixes of both the Atlas and its Online Companion placed the Arkam system in N-13. Jason Fry, author of The Essential Atlas and its Online Companion, later moved the Arkam system from N-13 to M-14 in the system appendix of the 2025 Canon Star Systems of the Galaxy.
