Ivak
1495

Ivak was a Human male agent of the Galactic Empire's Imperial Intelligence at the time of the Battle of Endor. He was one of the agents assigned by Ysanne Isard to interrogate the arrested Mara Jade; it was Ivak's stupidity that allowed Jade to escape.
