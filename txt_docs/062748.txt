Grubba Desilijic Aarrpo
1866

Grubba Desilijic Aarrpo, also known as Grubba the Hutt, was the nephew of Jabba of the Desilijic clan. He was kidnapped by the Whiphid "Black Tongue" while in transit to Tatooine. However, the Hutt was rescued by Han Solo, who hoped to have the bounty on his head rescinded. Unfortunately, he was captured and imprisoned instead. Bounty hunter Eron Stonefield later kidnapped Grubba in an attempt to get the bounty on Solo in a trade-off.
