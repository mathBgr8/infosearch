The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale
1274

|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale is a series of articles containing scenarios for use with the Star Wars Miniatures game, written by Jason Fry. It expands upon the history of Beilert Valance, a character from the Marvel Comics series. The articles were published on the Wizards of the Coast website between December 2, 2004 and January 27, 2005. *"The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale: Part 1" *"The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale: Part 2" *"The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale: Part 3" *"The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale: Part 4" *"The Hunt Within: Valance's Tale: Conclusion"
