Tabba
1516

Tabba was a Rebel Alliance fighter pilot who was stationed onboard the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Defiance during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, Tabba was killed in action near the Japai system in the months leading up to the Battle of Endor, along with fellow pilots Kraigg and Lady Blue.
