Steamcrawler
771

Steamcrawlers were large tracked vehicles used by the Balawai on Haruun Kal, especially in jups. They were equipped with turrets and large headlamps. They were purposefully low tech so that they could better resist the metal-eating fungus of Haruun Kal.
