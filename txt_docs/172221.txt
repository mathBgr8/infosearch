Executive Board
1742

The Executive Board was an organization consisting of the highest-ranked officials in the Tagge Corporation, a wealthy and powerful company owned by the Tagge family. The Board was headed by the company's head Lady Domina Tagge and convened for official meetings aboard TaggeCo's flagship Acquisitor. Led by Domina, the Board consisted of seven other members.
