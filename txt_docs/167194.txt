Alok Adasca
2265

Alok Adasca was the seventh Lord Adasca, son of Argaloh Adasca, and the next head of Adascorp sometime prior to the Mandalorian Wars. He was the father and mentor of his successor, Arkoh Adasca. Alok held a wake for his friend Barrison Draay on Arkania as a good-bye from business to "a life of levitating rocks." But he did hope to dissuade Barrison to "save an important investor." Alok later had a son Arkoh, who became friends with Barrison's son, Lucien.
