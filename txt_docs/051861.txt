Bothan Debris, Incorporated
1452

The Bothan Debris, Incorporated was a Bothan salvage operation that was established on Caamas by Kursk Te'tell, charged with recovering any equipment or materials that might have survived the destruction of the planet. However, the corporation also worked to cover up the truth behind Caamas's destruction. It was based in a huge crater on the planet, located about seventy miles south of Refuge City. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Bothan organizations Category:Salvage companies
