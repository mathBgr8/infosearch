|intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Paperback |pages=48 |isbn=0375826114 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith Scrapbook is a Star Wars Legends children's reference book that serves as the "Ultimate Insider's Guide" to the prequel trilogy film . It was written by Ryder Windham and published by Random House on April 2, 2005. Go behind the scenes of the movie we've all been waiting for!'This scrapbook is your guide to Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith.'Discover incredible facts about the characters, aliens, planets, droids, weapons, and ships—including all the newest additions to the Star Wars galaxy.'See over 100 action-packed photos of the spectacular film where Anakin Skywalker dies—and Darth Vader is born. The book makes reference to other Star Wars product lines, including the Star Wars: Clone Wars multimedia project and The Star Wars Holiday Special.
