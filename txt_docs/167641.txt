Vondri Deeq
892

Vondri Deeq was a male Ortolan chef. He was the perfectionist and irritable head chef of the Three Jetties Gourmet Restaurant in Lamaredd, a establisment serving only seafood from Bartyn Gourmet Delicacies, always cooked under the personal supervision of Deeq.
