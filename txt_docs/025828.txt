Ackdool
1738

Ackdool was the Mon Calamari Commander of the New Republic Star Cruiser Mediator in 25 ABY. Shortly before the outbreak of the Yuuzhan Vong War, Ackdool and the Mediator were sent to oversee the New Republic mediation of the Osarian-Rhommamool conflict. The firebrand Nom Anor created a plan that would allow him to escape Rhommamool, and intensify the conflict. He hid an A-wing in a missile that was launched at the Mediator after the nuclear assault had begun.
