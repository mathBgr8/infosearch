|masters= |apprentices= }} Rogar Garret was a New Republic General who served as Sector Commander of the Vilonis sector following the Battle of Endor. General Garret was part of the briefing to determine the New Republic's response to the Corporate Sector Authority and Galactic Empire's attempt to seize the Kalinda system.
