Tike Flume
1543

Tike Flume was the older brother of Trever Flume. He participated in the resistance movement against the Galactic Empire on the planet Bellassa with his father. In an attempt to prevent the seizure of a defense plant on their homeworld, Tike went off to protest inside the plant. When his father arrived to bring his son home, the facility exploded, resulting in both their deaths.
