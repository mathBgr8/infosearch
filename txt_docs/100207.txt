XJ7 X-wing starfighter
2176

The Incom XJ7 X-wing starfighter was in the service of the Galactic Alliance during the Second Galactic Civil War. Sporting laser cannons and proton torpedoes for armament, the XJ7 featured improvements to the weapons and shielding over the XJ6 X-wing starfighter. It was the primary space superiority fighter of the Galactic Alliance. Rogue Squadron was known to be equipped with XJ7 X-wings.
