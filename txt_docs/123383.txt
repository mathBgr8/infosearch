Watumba bat
1001

Watumba bats were flying animals that lived in nests. They ate fire, algae, insects, and rock dust. Glottalphibs considered them to be a great delicacy and liked to keep them, although this was at great risk to themselves as the bats would kill the Glottalphibs to eat their fire if enraged. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Chronology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bats Category:Creatures of Glottal Category:Insectivorous creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
