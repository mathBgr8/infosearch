|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Death in the Undercity" is an adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Michael Nystul. It was published by West End Games in June 1990. It was republished in Classic Adventures: Volume Three. In this adventure, a group of Rebel agents&mdash;the player characters&mdash;are sent to the planet Mon Calamari to investigate sabotage in the Quarren city of Morjanssik. Once they discover the truth behind the so-called "mining accidents", they must work quickly to prevent a civil war between the Quarren and the Mon Calamari.
