Pamela Rambo

Pamela Rambo is a comic book colorist who colored a number of Dark Horse's Star Wars comic books. Outside of Star Wars, she has colored for series such as Preacher and Y: The Last Man, with the former winning an Eisner Award in 1999. }} Category:Comic book colorists
