Outbound Flight Colony
2996

The Outbound Flight Colony was a corporate government on a remote planetoid in the Redoubt, located in the Unknown Regions of the galaxy. It was established from survivors of the Outbound Flight after it was destroyed by the Chiss Ascendancy. It was led by a Director, and the Managing Council, with the governing document being the Charter.
