Elza Monimi
1288

Elza Monimi was a male slave who lived on Tatooine prior to the Invasion of Naboo. He was captured by Krayn. As of 1 ABY he lived in a settlement on Tatooine. When suspicious looking people from outside of the town started sneaking around the neighborhood during the nights, Monimi collected credits from his neighbors to pay a spacer to deal with these strangers. - Tatooine terminal mission "Quieting The Nosy Neighbor" *Jedi Quest: Path to Truth Category:Enslaved individuals Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Tatooinians
