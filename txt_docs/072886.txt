Mhingxin
712

The Mhingxin was a species of rodent-faced sentients with a reputation for high-pitched voices. They tended to have a low opinion of themselves, since their appearance was similar to the sewer rats and such scum that beings all over the galaxy despised, hated, and strived to exterminate. They were easily provoked because of this. Eobbim Figh was a male Mhingxin. *Hard Merchandise *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Rodent sentient species
