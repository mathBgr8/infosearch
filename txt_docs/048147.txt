|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=232 |isbn=1593074476 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series=Star Wars Tales |issues=4 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Tales Volume 6 is the sixth trade paperback collection of the Star Wars Tales comic book series. Volume 6 collects Star Wars Tales issues 21-24. It was released on January 25, 2006. The vastness of the Star Wars galaxy hosts an ever-unfolding mythology, filled with character-driven stories of loss and tragedy, of heroism and redemption. Collected here are ten such tales&mdash;thrilling adventures that traverse and illuminate every era in the Star Wars mythos.
