Ask Lobot
3141

"Ask Lobot" is a regular feature of the Star Wars Insider magazine, where Leland Chee answers Star Wars questions sent in by readers. Ask Lobot succeeded the earlier Star Wars Q&A department in Star Wars Insider 101 as part of the magazine's new look and has featured in every issue since then, spread throughout the Blaster section.
