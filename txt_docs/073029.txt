Battle Group of Yun-Yuuzhan
989

The Battle Group of Yun-Yuuzhan was one of the five battle groups used by the Yuuzhan Vong during the Battle of Ebaq 9. It was under the direct command of Warmaster Tsavong Lah. Initially, the Yun-Yuuzhan battle group, along with the Yun-Harla battle group, were kept in reserve. When the Battle turned against Lah, he took a third of the Battle Group of Yun-Yuuzhan with him on the Jeedai hunt on Ebaq 9, and ordered the rest to retreat. The Battle Group of Yun-Yuuzhan had the most warning about the minefield that the New Republic had placed to hamper their retreat, and took the least amount of loses.
