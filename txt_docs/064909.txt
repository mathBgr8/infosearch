Dimel assault ship
2157

The Ulig Abaha Ltd. Dimel assault ship was a class of warship that was utilized by the Corporate Sector Authority. One of these vessels, the Eye of Shiblizar, was utilized by slavers Magg and Zlarb for their operations. The only statistics available for the vessel come from a specific and possibly modified example.
