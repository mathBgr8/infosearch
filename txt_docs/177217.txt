Keith Carter
772

Keith Carter is an artist who specializes in computer illustrations. His work has appeared in The Washington Post, USA Today and National Geographic. He has also contributed to several Nike campaigns. Keith produced artwork for a number of trading cards in Topps's Star Wars Galaxy Series 3 depicting scenes and characters from LucasArts Entertainment Company games.Star Wars Galaxy Series 3 Category:Card game artists
