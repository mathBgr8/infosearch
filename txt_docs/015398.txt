Iron Planet
1320

thumb|right|250px|The "Iron Planet" The Iron Planet was an iron asteroid in the Tatoo system. The "Iron Planet" was notable in that its material was starkly different than the neighboring asteroids in areas circumventing the Trader's Grotto.
