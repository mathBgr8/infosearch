Mimya
966

Mimya was the aunt of Keyan Farlander who lived with her husband, Trinn on Oorn Tchis. After the Imperial attack on Tondatha, Farlander asked his aunt and uncle to look after his sister, Kitha, who was injured in the attack. *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species
