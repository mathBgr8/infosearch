Iotran Defense Technologies
634

Iotran Defense Technologies was a relatively tiny company located on Iotra. They produced specialized weapons for use by the Iotran Police Force. Many of their weapons were employed by skilled veterans of the Police Force, called the Iotran Braceman Council.
