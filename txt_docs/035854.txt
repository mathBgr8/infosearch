|birth= |death= |species=Jawa |gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Yellow |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Thedit was a Jawa who lived on the planet Tatooine during the Galactic Civil War. He organized perimeter patrols for the Jawa Chief Kalit's territory. Thedit kept a watchful eye for krayt dragons, Tusken Raiders, and bandits working for Kalit's rival, Wittin. Thedit was an inspiring leader. Even in the indigenous Jawas' simple societies, he still managed to become both a leader and an organizer. Thedit had yellow eyes. Thedit was introduced on a card for November 1998's Special Edition Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
