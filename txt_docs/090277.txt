Saber Squadron (Eclipse)
1682

|dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*New Republic *New Jedi Order }} Saber Squadron was a starfighter squadron founded by Jedi Master Luke Skywalker for the Eclipse Station in 27 ABY during the Yuuzhan Vong War. It consisted of Jedi and non-Jedi, and was the personal elite squadron of Luke Skywalker. The original roster included Luke, Mara Jade Skywalker, seven non-Jedi pilots, and six newly trained Jedi pilots, including Tam Azur-Jamin. During the Battle of Coruscant, Sabers lost so many pilots that Luke combined it with Shocker Squadron. Later survivors of both squadrons would become the infamous Twin Suns Squadron.
