Sarma

Sarma was a Dubravan bounty hunter on Dubrava following the Battle of Yavin. The father of Nevana owed a debt to Sarma, but Nevana paid it off with information leading to the location of Luke Skywalker. Sarma betrayed Nevana, and led Imperial stormtroopers to her to find her source&mdash;Jal Te Gniev.
