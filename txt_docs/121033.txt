FX-3 medical assistant droid
1139

The FX-3 medical assistant droid was a medical droid manufactured by Medtech Industries as part of the FX-series of medical assistant droids. Produced during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, it was ultimately succeeded by the FX-4 model. *The Clone Wars: Decide Your Destiny: The Lost Legion *The New Essential Guide to Droids FX-03
