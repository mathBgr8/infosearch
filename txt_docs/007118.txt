Iron Fist (Victory-class)
3858

Iron Fist was a Victory-class Star Destroyer of the Imperial Starfleet during the Galactic Civil War. While under the command of Captain Zsinj, Zsinj developed the tactic of fending off a starfighter attack by flying the ship through an asteroid field&mdash;when the fighters moved in to attack, the ship would target asteroids close to the fighters with its turbolasers, causing them to explode and damage or destroy nearby fighters.X-Wing: Solo Command When Zsinj was promoted to Admiral and received a new flagship, the Executor-class Star Dreadnought Brawl, which he renamed to Iron Fist after this original ship.
