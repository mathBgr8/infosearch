Military Research and Development Division
621

The Military Research and Development Division was the arms branch of the Adasca BioMechanical Corporation of Arkania. It was newly opened in 3963 BBY, in the wake of the Great Sith War. It provided battle droid musculature to the Galactic Republic, a service that provided Adascorp with over half of its military revenue. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Arms manufacturing companies Category:Droid manufacturers
