Fortuna City
618

Fortuna City was the capital city of Edan II. A battle between the Rebel Alliance and Galactic Empire took place there. In the Adventure Book introduction of the Introductory Adventure Game, Fortuna City was referred to as "New East Bay." There is a real Fortuna City located in California. *Introductory Adventure Game Category:Capital cities Category:Edan II locations
