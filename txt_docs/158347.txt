Z-Kopsh
2348

Kopsh was a Strike-class medium cruiser belonging to Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. It was used to attack the Immobilizer 418 cruiser Red Claw, which was stranded in space because of a pirate attack that disabled the hyperdrive. The attack was unsuccessful and the Kopsh was destroyed by two loyalist Strike cruisers Nagan and Shotel, with the assistance of Maarek Stele's Assault Gunboat. The ship was called Z-Kopsh to remind the player that it belonged to Zaarin.
