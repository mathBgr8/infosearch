Trinn Reena
1283

Trinn Reena was the founder of Reena's Interstellar Shipping. The local government of Chekkoo Province of Rodia, suffering from Grand Protector Navik the Red's embargo imposed against rival clans, hired Reena to smuggle illegal technology and weapons; for this, Reena had to find suitable parties for the job, free-traders and smugglers who could evade the Grand Protector's forces. Reena was promised a bonus for each group she could find. Reena approached several individuals she found suitable, and, convinced they were interested for a challenging job, got into details about the political situation with the Rodian Chekkoo clan with a datapad with a local map and a rendezvous point with the officials. She instructed them to begin with smuggling some power fence generators in order to be accepted by the Chekkoo.
