Timi Rotramel
3448

Timi Rotramel was a male Mon Calamari Senator who represented the planet Dac and Calamari sector in the Imperial Senate during the Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|Timi Rotramel is killed by Darth Vader. Senator Rotramel's representation ended before the Senate's dissolution in 0 BBY, when the planet Dac was enslaved by the Galactic Empire. Timi was among those who joined the growing Rebellion against the corrupt Sith-led government. Travelling to Tiss'sharl in secret as an envoy, Senator Rotramel tried to recruit President Si-Di-Ri of the Tiss'sharl League to the Rebel Alliance, using the recent Alliance victory at Yavin to persuade the latter into allowing the establishment of a potential base on the planet.Empire 31 The two were interrupted by the Imperial forces' Supreme Commander, Darth Vader, who had used President Si-Di-Ri as an unwitting pawn in an attempt to lure Princess Leia Organa herself.
