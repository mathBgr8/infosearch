Unidentified High Master
2859

This individual was a male Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as the High Master during the Golden Age of the Old Republic. A Force-sensitive, this man was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, serving as a Jedi Master during the Golden Age of the Old Republic. By 340 BBY, this man served as High Master at the Coruscant Jedi Temple, and debriefed members of the Order as they returned from missions. During his tenure, the High Master received reports from a blue-skinned Jedi who had fought pirates, a yellow-eyed Twi'lek who had prevented the assassination of a diplomat, and Yoda's report on the loss of the Chu'unthor on Dathomir. Several centuries later, holorecords of these debriefings survived the Great Jedi Purge due to the diligence of the last Curator of Records. Hiding them away on Toola, the Curator was eventually hunted down and executed by Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Vader and his stormtroopers.
