During the New Sith Wars, Jedi scout Torr Snapit came to the planet seeking Force-sensitive recruits, but his only luck was with three young children: Rain, Tomcat and Bug. Rain later helped rebuild the Sith Order as Darth Bane's apprentice, Darth Zannah. The natives of the planet went by nicknames due to fear of the legend of the swamp demon. It was not uncommon for someone to live their whole life without their friends and family being aware of their real name.
