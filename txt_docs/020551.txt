It was located in the Arbran system in the Bon'nyuw-Luq sector, a part of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Arbran III's surface consisted of ice, and it was orbited by two moons. Arbran III was mentioned in "Hole in the Ground," the first article in the series Arbra: Sanctuary In the Storm, which was written by Cory J. Herndon for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The article was published on Wizards.com on June 5, 2003, as part of the site's Planet Hoppers roleplaying series. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Arbran system, thereby including Arbran III, in grid square N-17.
