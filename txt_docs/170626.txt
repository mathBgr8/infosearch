Mark Siegel
1499

Mark Siegel, born August 22, 1947, in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA, has worked in the visual effects, special effects, and art departments on many movies, including The Empire Strikes Back Special Edition, Return of the Jedi Special Edition and Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace.
