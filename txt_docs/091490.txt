Gorgon (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
1630

Gorgon was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate which served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Gorgon was dispatched to prevent the defection of the CR90 corvette Bixby during a Rebel attack on an Imperial convoy near Dellalt system.
