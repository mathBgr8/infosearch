Lilla Dade
1923

Lilla Dade was a Human female who served as a member of the Katarn Commandos and was considered one of their best trackers. In preparation for the assault on Kal'Shebbol, she was sent planetside as the primary infiltrator in Moff Kentor Sarne's base. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, she was assigned to set up an intelligence and resistance unit on Ylesia when the New Republic attacked the planet. Judder Page once commented that she was the best Pathfinder in the entire Special Forces.
