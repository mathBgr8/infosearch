Cambriele SolidState
1938

|locations= |role=Technology manufacturer |products=Power cells |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Cambriele SolidState was a manufacturer of power cells. It was headquartered on the Core World of Ralltiir for the sole intention of being able to claim it was a Core-based company. Cambriele SolidState's manufacturing facilities were strategically located throughout the Empire. The company produced power cells for use in multiple products including airspeeders, blasters, comlinks, glowrods, nanny droids, and gamepacks. Cambriele also provided the Imperial Military with power cells.The Far Orbit Project During the Galactic Civil War, Transport A-175 was contracted by Cambreile SolidState. It was carrying a shipment of power cells and power system components from Nopces Prime and was due to join a convoy of vessels at Brentaal IV. *The Far Orbit Project Category:Manufacturing companies
