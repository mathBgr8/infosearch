Null Space
633

The Null Space was a Corellian DP20 gunship. It was commanded by the pirate, Captain Obigon, and his crew including H'Krav and Meekeef. The Null Space was surrendered to Imperial Zero-G assault stormtroopers to avoid a fight. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:DP20 gunships Category:Pirate starships
