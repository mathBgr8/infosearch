Masi Oka
1326

Masi Oka is a former employee of Industrial Light & Magic, where he was a digital and visual effects artist working on the Star Wars prequels. He was responsible for the effects involved in creating Geonosis's rings in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones.
