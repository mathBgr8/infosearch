Curtis Saxton
1930

Curtis Saxton is the author of two books in the Incredible Cross-Sections series. He served as a named consultant on two other Star Wars technical reference books, and provided technical input on others. Dr. Saxton holds a doctorate in astrophysics from the University of Sydney. He is currently conducting postdoctoral research at the Mullard Space Science Laboratory, University College London. He began his Star Wars technical writing career with the unofficial Star Wars Technical Commentaries, a set of detailed documents exploring the nature and limits of the technology of Star Wars.
