Kalon Lenitor
1112

Kalon Lenitor was a Rodian and a legendary ghestslayer. He lived with his family in an island south-east of Equator City. He fathered an only child, his daughter Vadon, and he and the rest of the loving family raised her in their ways. They managed to raise a Rodian bounty hunter without the usual attitude of Rodian bounty hunters. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Hunters Category:Males Category:Rodians
