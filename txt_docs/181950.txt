Star Wars Summer Special 1983
1105

Star Wars Summer Special 1983 was a comic book released in the United Kingdom that reprinted the UK-original stories The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 149, The Pandora Effect, Rust Never Sleeps and The Flight of the Falcon. The stories were originally published in UK in The Empire Strikes Back Monthly 149, 151, 156 and 157. Category:Marvel UK specials and annuals
