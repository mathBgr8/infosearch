Elth Nardah
1905

Elth Nardah was an older Human male, with wizened skin and stooped shoulders. He was known to have dressed well, in the style of the Old Republic. He held the title of doctor and was a member of the Alliance Diplomatic Corps. His primary concern was keeping primitive populations from becoming slaves to the Empire. He spent time on Karra with the native Karran trying to negotiate a treaty between them and the Rebel Alliance.
