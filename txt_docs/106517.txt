Maggie
1878

Maggie was a former Human female Imperial who defected and joined the Alliance of Free Planets as a pilot and officer during the Galactic Civil War. Maggie served as both a starfighter pilot and a soldier during the Nagai–Tof War. Shortly after the Battle of Endor, she traveled to the planet Godo to investigate a strange plague that was affecting the native inhabitants.
