Venture
1359

The Venture was an Endurance-class fleet carrier of the New Republic Defense Fleet. After commissioning shortly before 16 ABY, Venture entered the inaugural New Republic Fifth Fleet under the command of Commodore Tolsk and Captain Logirth as the flagship of Task Force Blackvine. It participated at the Battle of Doornik-319, where it lost fourteen starfighters and took damage along three portside starfighter hangar bays. Afterward, the Venture participated in the Battle of N'zoth.
