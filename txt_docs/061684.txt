Kashyyyk slash-spider
1164

The Kashyyyk slash-spider was a species of arachnid native to the Wookiee homeworld of Kashyyyk. The scissors-like mandibles of the spider provided the name for the slip-jaws, a maneuver that was utilized in starfighter combat.
