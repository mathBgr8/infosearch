Xijulba
1364

Xijulba was a male Dug who was a member of the family of the famous Sebulba and also the Sebolto Clan on Malastare. In 32 BBY, he had a bounty posted on his head by a hateful rival of his podracing relative, Mawhonic. The bounty was eventually collected by the bounty hunter, Jango Fett. Xijulba's bounty was 2,000 credits alive and 1,000 credits dead.
