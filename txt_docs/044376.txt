InterGalactic Bank of Kuat
946

The InterGalactic Bank of Kuat was one of the financial institutions set up on the planet Kuat during the rule of the Galactic Republic. The InterGalactic Bank was perhaps the most prominent institution of its kind on Kuat, and had links to the vast banking world of Aargau. Much of the collateral controlled by the bank was stored on Aargau.
