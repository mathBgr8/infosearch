Imperial crest
24549

thumb|right|200px|The Imperial crest was a modified version of the Republic crest. The Imperial crest,Star Wars Rebels: The Visual Guide also referred to as the Imperial insignia, the Imperial logo,The Star Wars Book or the Imperial Army crest, was the six-spoked symbol of the Galactic Empire. An adaptation of the Galactic Republic's eight-spoked roundel, the Empire's omnipresent sigil adorned many things Imperial, from military uniforms and starships to flags and propaganda posters. The spoked design of the insignia was likened to a cogwheel. The Imperial insignia was designed by John Mollo, who was the costume designer for Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope and Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back.
