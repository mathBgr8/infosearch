Anselm
2645

Anselm was a male Human engineer in the Alliance Fleet. Although he had no military experience or combat training, Anselm joined the Alliance Fleet after the Galactic Empire ravaged his homeworld prior to the Battle of Yavin, and rose to the rank of Lieutenant due to his technical expertise. At that battle, he served as Luke Skywalker's X-wing technician. He often refused to carry a weapon, even in dangerous circumstances, unless directly ordered to do so. He went on to serve in the New Republic Defense Force.
