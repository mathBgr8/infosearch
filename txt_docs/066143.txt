Wermyn
2229

Wermyn was an Aquellan in charge of plant operations and keeping the Maw Installation up-and-running. One-handed, he wore the traditional green-purple tattoos found on all Aquellan men. During the Battle of the Maw in 11 ABY, he sabotaged the Installations power generators, though when he was captured by General Wedge Antilles and Page's Commandos, he offered to repair the damage. *Champions of the Force *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Empire science and engineering personnel Category:Humans Category:Individuals with he/him pronouns Category:Males
