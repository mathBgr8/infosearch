Martha Buntwhistle
844

Martha Buntwhistle was the alias used by a female member of the Rebel Alliance who accompanied a Rebel smuggler to the planet Harridan to pay off a 25,000 credit debt to Egot Pai-4-Yem. "Henry Buntwhistle," the smuggler, was told to book into the Lullaby Motel with a false wife under this name if he wanted to make contact with Egot.
