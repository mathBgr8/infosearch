|creator= |manufacturer= |line=R-series |model=R5-series astromech droid |class=Astromech droid |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |pronouns=new |sensor= |plating=White, red and yellow |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=*Alliance to Restore the Republic **Gold Squadron }} R5-F7 was an astromech droid serving onboard Gold Four Lieutenant Lepira's BTL Y-wing starfighter, flying point for Gold Squadron during the assault on the first Death Star at the Battle of Yavin and fending off Imperial fighters to clear a path for the Rebels to reach the Death Star's vulnerable exhaust port. R5-F7 was destroyed during the battle along with Lepira when their starfighter was shot down. This astromech was first identified when it was released as a Hasbro action figure as part of a 2007 Toys "R" Us exclusive. It was packaged with a BTL Y-wing starfighter that also included Lepira. R5-F7 was made as a LEGO® minifigure in 2012. However, he was mistakenly positioned as Dutch Vander's astromech.
