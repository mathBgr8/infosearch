Raibat
1627

}} Colonel Raibat was a knight of House Reena of the Tapani sector and an Imperial Army officer. He was described by many as a thick-necked bull of a man and was a strong supporter of the New Order. He was a guest at the Unification Gala held prior to the Battle of Endor and hosted by Lady Peta Lactril of House Barnaba.
