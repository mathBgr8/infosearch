Glazed glucose pate
631

Glazed glucose pate was a delicacy enjoyed by Ugors. It was extremely dense and made of sugar. The chef droid L9-G8 instructed the Rebel crew of the Bonderium to cook this dish for an Ugorian feast, in order to bribe ArrGack and GrrKack for admission to the Paradise system. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Ugorian food
