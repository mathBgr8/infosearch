Vjun tunic
812

Vjun tunics were extremely expensive garments handwoven on the planet Vjun. Count Dooku owned such a tunic, he had it in his possession sometime before the Mission to Jabba's Palace. He also wore it during the Battle of Coruscant prior to the end of the Clone Wars. *Star Wars: The Clone Wars film *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Clone Wars: The Visual Guide Category:Shirts and tunics
