Arkanian blaster rifle
1241

The Arkanian blaster rifle was a blaster rifle used during the time of the Mandalorian Wars and its aftermath. The design, created by the Arkanians, could equal that of modern blasters. The one problem faced with these weapons was their lack of upgrade options as they could not accept newer firing chambers and power cells. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Arkanian culture Category:Blaster rifles
