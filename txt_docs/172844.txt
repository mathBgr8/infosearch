Veed
1006

Veed was a CR90 corvette used by the Rebel Alliance which served in the Airam sector. When the decision was made to construct a Rebel shipyard in Airam space, Veed was assigned to the convoy which would bring the needed supplies and personnel. Veed itself held troops which would be used as defense and security. However, the Galactic Empire learned of them. It ambushed the convoy in the Rocrin system. Veed, along with the other Rebel ships, was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
