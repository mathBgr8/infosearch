Rebel Commando
3704

Rebel Commandos, also known as SpecForce soldiers,Forged in Battle were an elite variant of rebel troopers of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. They composed the multiple units of the Rebel Alliance Special Forces including the Marine Corps, the Special Forces Trackers Unit, and more.
