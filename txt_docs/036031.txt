OX9
1812

The OX9 was a model of labor droid used by the Trade Federation. It was essentially humanoid in shape, short, stocky, with large plate-like hand appendages to facilitate carrying large loads. They were also capable of constructing various buildings. They had elongated heads, were the same beige color as B1-series battle droids, and spoke in warbling droidspeak. OX9 droids were produced at command centers. The OX9 first appeared in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds and in its 2002 expansion pack, Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns.
