Priests of the Red Nebula
1494

The Priests of the Red Nebula preached the faith of the Cosmic Destiny, which was based on fate, and predetermined events. Their religion was a major part of Red Nebula society, although they experienced a schism when a small part of the Planet of the Red Nebula went untouched during a great cataclysm.
