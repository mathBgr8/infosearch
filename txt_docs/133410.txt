Alzoc III Base
985

The Alzoc III Base was an Imperial military base on the arctic planet of Alzoc III. During the Galactic Civil War, Kyle Katarn and Jan Ors were assigned to infiltrate the base. Ors deployed Katarn in a supply crate on a landing pad, from which he took the outside defenses by surprise. Most of the base's staff, including a Reborn, were killed. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast demo Category:Imperial military bases
