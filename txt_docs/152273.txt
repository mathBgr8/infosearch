Toccat
1223

The toccat was a species native to the jungle planet Darlyn Boda. The creatures had four hooves, long pink snouts, and black-furred bodies. Haruspexes—fortune tellers on Darlyn Boda—made a living divining the future from the entrails of slain toccats. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Creatures of the Anoat sector Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Jungle and forest creatures Category:Mammals Category:Species of undefined sentience
