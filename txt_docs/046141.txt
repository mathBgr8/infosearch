Flyer
1872

Flyers were a type of vehicle that used two turbojets and could carry multiple individuals. During the scavenger Rey's childhood on the planet Jakku following her arrival there in 16 ABY,Star Wars: Timelines the junk dealer Unkar Plutt purchased a used flyer and made her repair it. Rey unclogged one of the vehcile's turbojets and rebuilt the other using scrap from the front half of an airspeeder. Plutt then sent six men and a hired pilot in the flyer to Carbon Ridge mountain in search of its treasure. The group took two casualties fighting dead-enders at the mountain and found no treasure, returning only with a stack of brittle stormtrooper armor. Rey later mentioned the incident and vehicle in a survival guide that she wrote by 34 ABY,Rey's Survival Guide includes an addendum at the end describing some of the events of , written several days after they take place. The addendum is the final entry in the guide and is included chronologically after the entry mentioning the flyer. Star Wars: Timelines dates the events of the film to 34 ABY. *Rey's Survival Guide Category:Air vehicle types
