2532

Station Gamma |armament=None |equipment= |affiliation=Rak'qua |firstmade= |retired= }} The ESPY E.R. Droid was a type of modified TT-8L gatekeeper droid used during the Galactic Civil War. Droids of this kind was unarmed as it primarily filled a reconnaissance role. ESPY E.R. Droids were used by the Rak'qua on Station Gamma above Ord Mantell following the Battle of Yavin. In 1 ABY, a number of spacers who explored Station Gamma fought and destroyed multiple ESPY E.R. Droids. ESPY E.R. Droids appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. ESPY E.R. Droids were added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005. *Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Guide: Prima Official Game Guide Category:TT-8L gatekeeper droids
