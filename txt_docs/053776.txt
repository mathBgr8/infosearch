Renthal's Fist
2281

The Renthal's Fist was a CR90 corvette that served as the flagship of the pirate Drea Renthal. In addition to the standard armament of a CR90 corvette, the Renthal's Fist was equipped with a pair of capital ship grade proton torpedo launchers. While these weapons greatly enhanced the ship's firepower, the torpedoes were expensive and very difficult to acquire. During the Battle of Nar Shaddaa, Renthal's Fist played an important role, being the largest of the ships in the smugglers' fleet and destroying the bulk cruiser Arrestor.
