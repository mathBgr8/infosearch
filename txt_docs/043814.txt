Mashi Horansi
1387

The Mashi Horansi, nicknamed the "Night Stalkers", were a subspecies of the Horansi native to Mutanda. Mashi stood on average between 1.5 and 2 meters tall. Their fur was jet black and the pelt of a Mashi Horansi was prized by other races. They were a very elusive race and were purely nocturnal. They mated for life and the male raised the young. While able to use technology, they were uncomfortable with it.
