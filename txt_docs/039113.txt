Barons Knoll
1414

Barons Knoll was a hill on Sulon. It gave its name to the city of Barons Hed that was built around it. The summit of Barons Knoll was the location of Government House, built after the Subjugation of Sulon (1 BBY). The residences built near it formed the High Town, belonged to the wealthiest citizens; the buildings falling away gradually near the bottom formed the Low Town and belonged to the poorest strata.Dark Forces: Rebel Agent Barons Knoll appears in Dark Forces: Rebel Agent, the novelization of the earlier video game Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II.
