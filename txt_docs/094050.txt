Forbelean Defense
768

The Forbelean Defense,The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant also called forbelean defense, was a martial arts posture from the Forbelea system created by the Chiss species. The defense required the user to maintain a posture that would allow all attacks to be intercepted and deflected. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Chiss Ascendancy Category:Chiss culture Category:Martial arts
