Duha spear
1425

Duha spears were a type of spear that had a metal point at the end. In a 26 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion speech, Duros anti-Jedi agitator and Yuuzhan Vong Ducilla Brarun used the point of a duha spear as an example of strength when expressing her conviction that the people of Duro could survive the Yuuzhan Vong invasion only by standing apart from the rest of the galaxy and New Republic. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point Category:Spears
