150px|thumb|Spysprites, as seen on [[HoloNet/Legends|HoloNet advertisement.]] The Spysprite mini-holocam by SoroSuub Corporation was sold in 22 BBY. It was sloganed "the ultimate in security". They appeared to be a public version of the Senate hovercam, and were available in multiple colors.
