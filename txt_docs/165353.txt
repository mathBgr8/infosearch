Jared Brojtal
1317

System Sub-Commander Jared Brojtal was a Human male officer serving with the Imperial Navy and assigned to the Outer Rim, specifically the Celanon system. He had a son who attended the Syndaar Military Prep School. When his son was killed in an attack by the Ho'Din terrorist Ort Hoogra-D'En, he used every Imperial ship at his disposal to track down the Ho'Din, including his personal command ship, the Carrack-class light cruiser Marduk.
