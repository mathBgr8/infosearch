Billibango
1837

Billibango was a male Xexto from Troiken who supported the Galactic Republic. Unlike many of the other members of his species, he did not dislike the Jedi, and in fact wished to be one. In 44 BBY, during the Stark Hyperspace War, he helped the Republic force at Mount Avos by providing them with information on the mountain and its challat eaters.
