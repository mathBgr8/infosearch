thumb|150px|The heavy exoskeleton A heavy and durable suit of armor, the heavy exoskeleton was a mechanical exoskeleton that provided much greater strength and endurance, as well as vastly improved armor protection to the wearer, than lighter models. With additional enhancements, its capability could be expanded even further, rivaling some light armored vehicles for protection. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Medium armor
