Laurent Bouzereau
1835

Laurent Bouzereau is a film historian and documentarian. He is the author of Star Wars: The Annotated Screenplays and co-author of The Making of Episode I: The Phantom Menace. In addition to his documentary for George Lucas' American Graffiti, Bouzereau has made documentaries for many of Steven Spielberg's films, including E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, The Terminal, Jaws, Minority Report, and many others. Bozereau directed the documentary Secrets of The Force Awakens: A Cinematic Journey for the home video release of Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens.
