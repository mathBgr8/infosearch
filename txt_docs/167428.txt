Fathoms
1104

Fathoms was a large entertainment establishment located on eight levels of the Glitter Cay tower in Wildwater City on Dac. It was owned by a female Mon Calamari named Odanni. It actually consisted of four separate businesses: *Fathoms was a restaurant with fine dining operated by Odanni *The HyperDive Cantina was a bar run by Baydo Chasdy *Wave Works was a gift shop run by Jund Voon *The Seabed Lodge was a luxurious hotel *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy *Hideouts & Strongholds *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Businesses Category:Mon Cala locations
