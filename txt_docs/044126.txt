|published in=Star Wars Insider 67 |reprinted in=Hyperspace |pages= |timeline=8 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series= |preceded by=Tatooine Ghost |followed by="Mission to Zila" }} "The Trouble with Squibs" was a short story written by Troy Denning, originally published in Star Wars Insider 67 and later archived on Hyperspace. On a trip to Pavo Prime to retrieve Alderaanian boasa statues, Han and Leia Organa Solo run into two old "friends": the Squibs Emala and Sligh, who they met on Tatooine while trying to obtain the moss painting Killik Twilight. However, the third Squib, Grees, was conspicuously absent. It turned out that Grees had been frozen in carbonite and was in the possession of a Jenet casino owner on Pavo Prime.
