Starlight Red
832

The Starlight Red, formerly known as Faceted, was a YT-1300 light freighter owned by the free-trader and smuggler known as Celia Durasha and piloted by her and U-THR. Before Durasha acquired the ship, it belonged to the bounty hunter Thune. Category:Bounty hunter starships Category:Smuggler starships Category:YT-1300 light freighters
