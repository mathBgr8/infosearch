Sith Intelligence and Assassination
1498

Sith Intelligence and Assassination was an agency of the One Sith of Darth Krayt. It was in charge of espionage, political assassination, interrogation and torture. Around 130 ABY, it was headed by Darth Maladi. Jor Torlin was one of its ranking agents.
