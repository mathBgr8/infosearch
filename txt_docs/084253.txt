Mohris
1345

Mohris was a Human male who served as the head bartender of the Rimsoo Seven cantina on Drongar during the Clone Wars. In 20 BBY, Mohris survived the attack of the Separatist forces against the medical center during the Clone Wars, and the RMSU was moved to a new location.
