It adapts the 1980 film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, and it was first published in October 2004 by Scholastic. The novel was later collected in Star Wars Trilogy: Collector's Edition (2005), Star Wars Boxed Set: Episodes I–VI (2007), and Star Wars: Classic Trilogy (2014). A revised, canon edition of the novel, Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back, was published in 2017. A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away.... An Empire grows stronger. A rebellion is threatened.
