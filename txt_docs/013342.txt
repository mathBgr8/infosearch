Commonly called simply Eiattu by its inhabitants, it was ruled by a monarchy. thumb|left|160px|The [[Imperial Citadel (Eiattu 6)|Imperial Citadel]] Red-purple plants dotted the landscape, while the swamps were filled with large feathered-reptilian beasts, such as thuvasaurs and native thaks. Eiattu's capital served as a port on one of the planet's oceans. thumb|left|190px|Royal family: [[Isplourrdacartha Estillo (far left), Kassen (right), Uthorrferrell Cartha (second right) and Harrandatha Estillo (third right)]] The planet was ruled for centuries by the royal house of Eiattu 6. When the Declaration of a New Order transformed the Old Republic into the Galactic Empire, Antbbianplourr III made humiliating concessions to retain his throne and to prevent the deployment of Imperial forces to the planet. He was succeeded by his only son, Uthorrferrell Cartha. After five years of reign, the Empire dispatched Moff Tharil Tavira and a garrison of stormtroopers to Eiattu. The planet's nobles, known as the Priamsta, later rebelled, believing that they could deal better with Tavira on their own terms. They overthrew and exiled the royal family, but a small faction murdered the family. The Priamsta took power on the world and acted as an oligarchy but found itself embroiled in a civil war against the People's Liberation Battalion. Years later, Moff Tharil Tavira was succeeded by his wife, a young native woman named Leonia Tavira. thumb|right|240px|The [[People's Liberation Battalion|PLB fighting stormtroopers in the swamps of Eiattu]] After the Battle of Endor, the Priamsta discovered that an heiress to the royal line had survived, Princess Isplourrdacartha Estillo, also known as Rogue Squadron pilot Plourr Ilo. She returned to rule her people with the support of the nobility which intended to counteract another royal heir, Prince Harrandatha Estillo, who had joined the People's Liberation Battalion. A battle soon erupted in which the PLB and Rogue Squadron defeated the Imperial forces on the planet, forcing Moff Tavira to flee Eiattu. During the battle, Princess Isplourrdacartha revealed Prince Harrandatha as an impostor and an Imperial agent, gaining the recognition of the People's Liberation Battalion. The Priamsta was eventually forced to adjust to a new political situation, with a revitalized monarchy determined to rule in cooperation with the people. Several months later, Rogue Squadron members, Han Solo, Chewbacca, and Winter impersonating Leia Organa went to a reception organized on Eiattu 6 by Princess Estillo in order to cover the true Leia's mission on Axxila.
