Shamarok flitters were small, flying rodents native to the planet Ithor. They could imitate other beings' speech. In 0 BBY, Shamarok flitters became a heavily sought after item amongst the wealthy elite on Lianna. Cynabar advised that anyone looking for a quick credit-turnaround should buy flitters from Ithorian Herdships for around 400 credits, and sell them on Lianna for around 1,200 credits.
