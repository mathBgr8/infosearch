The Human male Bachenkall served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. He graduated from the Imperial Training Academy on the planetThe Truce at Bakura Sourcebook Raithal, a naval school that trained pilots in capital starship helm tactics. By early 3 ABY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back to 3 ABY. Bachenkall had risen to the rank of warrant officer and served with the Imperial Death Squadron aboard the Star Dreadnought Executor, the personal flagship of Lord Darth Vader, as a helmsman under Lieutenant Commander Ardan's supervision. In 3 ABY, Bachenkall was present within the Executor starboard command pit when Vader strode across the bridge's command walkway to discuss the findings of a Viper probe droid in the Hoth system with Fleet Admiral Kendal Ozzel and Captain Firmus Piett. Along with the rest of the crewmen within the command pit, Bachenkall watched in fearful silence as the Dark Lord passed above them. The probe droid's data revealed the location of Echo Base, the Rebel Alliance's headquarters on the planet Hoth, leading to the Executor participation in the subsequent Battle of Hoth. Bachenkall and the other members of the pit crew displayed fearful deference at the presence of Darth Vader as he stalked across the upper command walkway of the Executor bridge to learn of the Viper probe droid's report in the Hoth system. He had fair skin. Bachenkall was typical of graduates of the Empire's Training Academy on Raithal, where he was trained in capital starship helm tactics. Bachenkall wore a light gray technician's uniform and a dark gray cap with a headset comlink. Bachenkall made his first appearance in the film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back as an unidentified extra among Darth Vader's bridge crew. The character's name and elements of his backstory were introduced in the Dagobah Limited expansion set of Decipher's Star Wars Customizable Card Game, released in 1997.
