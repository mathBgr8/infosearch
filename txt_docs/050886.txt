From Star Wars to Indiana Jones: The Best of the Lucasfilm Archives
2075

From Star Wars to Indiana Jones: The Best of the Lucasfilm Archives is a predominantly photo-based reference book that chronologically surveys the Lucasfilm Archives's highlights: props, models, storyboards, backdrops, and sketches, spanning the Star Wars Trilogy as well as the Indiana Jones Trilogy. This book is very similar to Star Wars: Chronicles, also published by the Chronicle Books, but is less indepth.
