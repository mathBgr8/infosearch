Migress
1935

Migress was a male Brolf working under Patriot (Filvian) on the planet Barlok to assassinate Passel Argente in 27 BBY, when negotiations were held on the planet between the Corporate Alliance and the guildmaster of Barlok's mining operations. Migress received burst thrusters from his fellow conspirators Duefgrin and Jhompfi to contribute to the building of a homemade missile. The missile was to be used for the assassination attempt against the Corporate Alliance leader. The plot, masterminded by Darth Sidious and Kinman Doriana, was foiled by Jedi Master Jorus C'baoth, who called on the Force to stop the missile as it entered the negotiations chamber—using a Force grip. *Outbound Flight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Brolfi Category:Males Category:Terrorists
