|editor=*Bill Smith *Paul Sudlow |publisher=West End Games |release date=July 17, 1995 |type=Boxed set |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=087431254X |isbn2= |isbn3= |series=The DarkStryder Campaign series |preceded by= |followed by=The Kathol Outback }} }} The DarkStryder Campaign is the first installment in The DarkStryder Campaign series, a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. Released on July 17, 1995, this boxed set includes a 14-page original story by Timothy Zahn titled The Saga Begins. Set in the early years of the New Republic, the campaign takes place in the remote Kathol sector. The boxed set contains both a campaign book and an adventure book, along with a full-color map of the modified Corellian Corvette FarStar and a set of stat cards. The complete campaign consists of: *The DarkStryder Campaign *The Kathol Outback *The Kathol Rift *Endgame A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away ... Four years after the Battle of Endor, the struggle against the remnants of the Empire continues. A New Republic task force has been dispatched to the distant Kathol sector to topple rogue warlord Moff Sarne. During the battle, the New Republic discovers mysterious artifacts known as "DarkStryder technology." Join the crew of the Corellian Corvette FarStar as they scour the fringes of the Empire in search of Sarne and the source of the DarkStryder technology! *Captain Keleman Ciro&mdash;A member of Page's Commandos, handpicked by Lt.
