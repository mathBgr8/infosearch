Ia
1649

Ia was a Human female police officer on the planet Coruscant who held the rank of lieutenant with the Coruscant Security Force Traffic Division. Ia was a middle-aged Human female with a husky, monotonic voice, white hair, and no-nonsense manner. She took her job very seriously of policing the skylanes in her armored swoop, CS-117, and frequently tossed impolite traffic violators in the police lockup.
