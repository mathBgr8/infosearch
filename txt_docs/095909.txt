Inchichtok
2199

The Inchichtok were a xenophobic sentient species. Around 200 BBY,Star Wars Gamemaster Kit establishes that the Terr'skiar Pass was considered "newly discovered" at the time Arnoth Draenell encountered the Inchichtok. As The Essential Atlas establishes that the Terr'skiar Pass was discovered around 200 BBY, the species must have been discovered around that year. the Inchichtok had their first contact with the Galactic Republic, meeting the scout expedition commander Arnoth Draenell and his crew in an unscouted star system that later became known as the Bissillirus system in the Mid Rim. Though Draenell's crew attempted to continue their expedition, the Inchichtok prevented them from doing so by ending their lives. The Inchichtok were first introduced in the 1991 roleplaying game sourcebook Star Wars Gamemaster Kit, which was written by Bill Smith and Ed Stark and published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Star Wars Gamemaster Kit *Classic Campaigns Category:Mid Rim sentient species
