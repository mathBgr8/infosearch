Hinthra
1696

The Hinthra was Elis Helrot's starship of choice. It was a highly modified freighter. He usually smuggled slaves or spice to and from Kala'uun. It was armed with a pair of double blaster cannons. The crew consisted of two other Givins and a male Human.
