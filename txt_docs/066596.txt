Container hangar
2030

*Rated 76 RU |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Various private, commercial, and pirate interests }} The container hangar was a sizable box-shaped freight container manufactured by Xizor Transport Systems. Designed specifically to contain multiple cargo containers in the Xizor line, the container hangar could be customized with laser turrets for protection. In addition, a number of them served as starfighter hangars for installations that required starfighter defense, but did not have the hangar facilities to support them.
