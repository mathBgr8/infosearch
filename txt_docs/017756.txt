|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=c. 3-0 BBYStar Wars: The Comics Companion places these events as "between three and zero years" before the Battle of Yavin (p. 62). |series=Star Wars: Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire |issue=1 }} Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire 1 was the first issue in the Star Wars: Boba Fett: Enemy of the Empire series of comics. It was released January 27, 1999 by Dark Horse Comics. Before the events of A New Hope, Darth Vader employed the galaxy's greatest bounty hunter on a search-and-retrieval mission: Boba Fett must discover and return to Vader a single small box, the contents of which could change the fate of the galaxy. For such an important mission, Vader cannot rely on a single operative&hellip; nor can he allow that operative to walk away alive! It is noted in the 2005 Star Wars: The Comics Companion (p. 62) that this is the first meeting between an adult Boba and Darth Vader in the Dark Horse Comics Legends timeline. Although Boba (technically) met Anakin when he was a kid on Geonosis after his 'father' Jango helped captured Obi-Wan Kenobi, Padmé, and Anakin (when still a Padawan to Obi-Wan), for the Geonosian's and Count Dooku as leader of the Separatists, this is also the first Legends comic storyline that portrays Boba as an adult.
