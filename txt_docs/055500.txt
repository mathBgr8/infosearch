Jawa Traders
1966

Jawa Traders was a store in Mos Eisley which specialized in starship- and vehicle-repairing droids. Due to this, the store was a source of income in a spaceport that was rich in smugglers and other sorts of unsavory clientèle. The shop was owned and managed by the Jawa Aguilae, who later hired a Squib, Macemillian-winduarté, to help her for she discovered that she had difficulties parting with her own merchandise.
