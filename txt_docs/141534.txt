Omicron 023
1482

Omicron 023 was a Mesens SCT Scout Craft that arrived at the meeting site between Rebels and Dunari to investigate the craft present in the system. Shortly after the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter arrived to capture Rebel and Dunari ships however they managed to flee. Omicron 023 itself was destroyed during the skirmish. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:SCT Scout craft Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
