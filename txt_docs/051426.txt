thumb|right|250px|The MX on Coruscant The MX was a heavy weapon the Galactic Republic was developing in 3996 BBY. It was an emplaced weapon that utilized crystals and was powered by an ion flow. It was stated to be powerful enough to "clean off a quarter-deck in half a millisec." Mandalore the Indomitable captured an MX prototype during the Battle of Coruscant. *Tales of the Jedi – The Sith War 2 *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ion weaponry
