Ewoks 11 is the eleventh issue of Marvel's Ewoks series of comic books. It and was published under the Marvel Comics Star Comics imprint on September 30, 1986 with a cover date of January 1987. Wicket and Teebo must rescue Princess Kneesaa, who has been shrunken by the Fleebogs in order to defeat the ra-lugg.
