To Fight Another Day
9625

"To Fight Another Day" was a short story written by Kathy Tyers. It was originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 6 by West End Games in May 1995 and was later archived online at Hyperspace. Tinian I'att, fugitive from the Empire, arrives at Silver Station to hand over her prototype portable shielding technology to a Rebel Alliance contact, the elderly Una Poot. Tinian meets the Wookiee bounty hunter Chenlambec and asks to be apprenticed to him. She departs with the Wookiee aboard his starship, Wroshyr. Around the same time, Rebels carrying the wounded Daye Azur-Jamin also arrive at Silver Station. His bacta recovery is cut short when he and the Rebels are forced to flee the station as it comes under attack by an Imperial Star Destroyer. This story took place three weeks after Tinian on Trial (1995) and featured the first meeting of Tinian and Chenlambec, who would appear in the short story The Prize Pelt: The Tale of Bossk (1996)
