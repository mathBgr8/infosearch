Pizzazz 14

Pizzazz 14 is the fourteenth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated November 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. The issue also includes the article "The Damsels," discussing women in science fiction movies and featuring Princess Leia. 14
