Zikri

Zikri was a small and playful water spirit in the Bothan speciesUltimate Alien Anthology mythology. "Zikri" was also a given name for Bothan females. Zikri was mentioned in the Galactic Campaign Guide, a 2003 sourcebook written by Peter Schweighofer and J.D.
