Françoise Mouly
1269

Françoise Mouly (born October 24, 1955) is a comic book creator who worked on a few issues in the Marvel Star Wars comic series. In the late 1970s she and her husband Art Spiegelman founded the ground breaking underground comic anthology RAW. She has held the position of art editor at The New Yorker since 1993, and more recently Mouly and Speigelman have launched a Raw Junior division as well as collaborated on a comic anthology for children call Little Lit. *Star Wars (1977) 10 *Star Wars: A Long Time Ago...
