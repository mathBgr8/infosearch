Portha
1943

Portha was a Trandoshan that lived on the planet Coruscant in Invisec during Rogue Squadron's operation there in 6 ABY. Portha assisted the Rogues during their attempt to bring down the planetary shield, though was unable to participate in the final battle after being struck down with the Krytos virus.
