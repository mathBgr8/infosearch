Llewebum (species)
627

The Llewebum were a sentient species with large bumps across their bodies, including a secondary series of bumps below their arms. The senator from Llewebum was severely injured in the explosion caused by Kueller in the Senate Hall on Coruscant. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
