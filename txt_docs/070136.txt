Bothan brandy
1714

Bothan brandy was a type of brandy available in the galaxy. An alcoholic beverage, Bothan brandy was available for sale during the time of the Cold War, where it was served aboard Port Nowhere. Ngani Zho ordered a Bothan brandy and water during a visit to the station. Bothan brandy was still available thousands of years later, where it was served during the annual Vor-cal Feast held at the House Melantha Orbital Hunting Lodge over Vycinyth. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *The Old Republic — The Lost Suns 3 *Lords of the Expanse Category:Brandies
