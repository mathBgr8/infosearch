Wesk Herf
1506

thumb|Veed's tattoo of Wesk Herf The two glyphs Wesk Herf (the letters W. H. in the Aurebesh) represented the name of a spaceship, apparently a capital ship in the Imperial Navy of the Fel Empire. This was the first vessel that future Grand Admiral Morlish Veed served aboard at the start of his spacefaring career. As a result, he wore a tattoo with the two letters on his right bicep, one above the other. Moff Veed's tattoo appears in the preview for issue #16 of the Star Wars: Legacy comic at the Dark Horse Comics website. Legacy artist Jan Duursema subsequently explained the meaning of the glyphs in a discussion thread at TheForce.net. *Legacy (2006) 16 Category:Capital ships Category:Starships of the Fel Empire
