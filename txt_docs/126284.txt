|eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin= |habitat= |diet= |language= }} A cairoka was a type of creature capable of flight and found on the planet Alderaan. They had wings as white as snow. By 1 BBY,Star Wars: Galactic Atlas: Updated Edition dates the Disaster to 1 BBY. As Rel's viewing of the cairoka takes place on Alderaan before its destruction, the events must have transpired by 1 BBY. the Alderaanian youngling Rel watched cairoka fly over some trees on Alderaan. Cairoka appeared in the new Star Wars canon in "The Song of Winter's Heart," a short story written by Cavan Scott and George Mann in the 2021 Life Day Treasury anthology.
