Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide was a strategy guide published by Prima Games for Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, as well as its expansions, Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees and Star Wars Galaxies: Jump to Lightspeed.
