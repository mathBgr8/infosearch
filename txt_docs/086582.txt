Mesan (Senali)
889

Mesan was the wife of Jaret, the nephew of the King Meenon of Senali. She had one child, Tawn. She welcomed Qui-Gon Jinn and Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi when they visited Senali. *Jedi Apprentice: The Shattered Peace *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Senali
