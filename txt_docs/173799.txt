In 1 ABY, Dekven was wounded on Dantooine while searching for a new place to hide in case of discovery by the Empire. He had to recover in a hospital at a pirate outpost. At the outpost, he met a spacer who was willing to check out the Mokk Stronghold for him, to see if it was still occupied by Dantari or could be used by the Rebellion. Daiv Dekven was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Daiv Dekven was added to the game with the "Publish 1" update, released on August 3, 2003. He was one of many "herald NPCs" that were used to guide players to static points of interest and dungeons.
