Arth Gxin
1252

Arth Gxin was a male Human Sergeant in the Imperial Remnant during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Gxin served under Grand Admiral Gilad Pellaeon on the Right to Rule during the invasion. Gxin joined Jaina Solo, Tahiri Veila and Jagged Fel onboard the Collaborator to aid the crew of the Esfandia Bay Communications Base during the Battle of Esfandia. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy officers of the Imperial Remnant Category:Males Category:Pilots
