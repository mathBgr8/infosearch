Boba Fett and X-Wing: The Mandalorian Candidate
945

Boba Fett and X-Wing: The Mandalorian Candidate was a crossover story-arc or miniseries for the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron comics, proposed by Andy Mangels. In it, Boba Fett would have teamed up with Rogue Squadron to discover an enemy agent with Rogue Squadron, and who his employer was. Mangels discussed it with Dark Horse Comics and very briefly with Michael A. Stackpole, but it was never published. Category:Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron story arcs
