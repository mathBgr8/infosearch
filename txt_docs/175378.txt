Nectar pearl
847

thumb|right|200px|The Ketes carry nectar pearls on their backs. Nectar pearls were round, gray containers which were used to carry kete nectar. The Ketes on the forest moon of Endor used nectar pearls to carry the nectar back to their hive. The shape of the pearls allowed the Ketes to conveniently store the pearls within storehouses inside their hive. During the stranglethorn crisis, the two Ewoks, Wicket Wystri Warrick and Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka obtained a nectar pearl from the Kete hive because of the importance of kete nectar in the antidote for plant growth formula.
