It was a predecessor to the AT-RT and AT-ST, the latter of which was used extensively by the Galactic Empire. The AT-XT was equipped with twin laser cannons mounted under its chin and two long-range proton mortar launchers. Also, as a defensive measure, some units were equipped with a plasma shield capable of absorbing enemy fire for a short period of time. In spite of its reasonably slow max speed of seventy-five kilometers per hour, which was only fifteen kilometers per hour faster than the AT-TE, and five kilometers per hour faster than the normal speed of the AT-RT, it was capable of demolishing entire columns of enemies. AT-XTs played a very important role in bringing the Dark Reaper Project to a halt. They fought during the battles of Rhen Var and Thule. thumb|left|AT-XTs deployed with [[Jedi General/Legends|General Kenobi's forces on Jabiim.]] Although it was a highly effective combat vehicle, the AT-XT was never mass-produced as its designers were constantly improving its design and combat capabilities. Even though it didn't see as much combat as the AT-TE or AT-RT, the design of the AT-XT would prove to be valuable to Imperial designers as an inspiration for the AT-ST walker that would be used extensively throughout the Galactic Civil War. In addition to its design-legacy, various AT-XT models were retained in use by the Galactic Empire, mostly for research and development purposes.
