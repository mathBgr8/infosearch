Smuggler's Log
17399

"Smuggler's Log" was a series of RPG articles appearing in several issues of the Star Wars Adventure Journal and introduced several characters in the perspective of Platt Okeefe. The first Smuggler's log segment appeared in the Star Wars Adventure Journal 1.
