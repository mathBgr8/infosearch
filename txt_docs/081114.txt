Hilissa was a city of the planet Essowyn, the Saurton homeworld. It was located in the north-western hemisphere, on the continent of Sorontill. It contained the planet's main spaceport. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Cities on Essowyn
