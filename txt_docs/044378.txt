L2 units were able to perform tasks with up to 100 separate steps. An L2 was equipped with two heavy grasper arms, one retractable fine work grasper arm, two tractor feet, a small circular saw, and auditory and visual recorders. L2 units were used by the Galactic Empire to maintain the prison complexes of Balis-Baurgh. Hardin owned a modified L2.
