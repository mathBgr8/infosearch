Jerome Blake
2271

}} Jerome Blake played Mas Amedda in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and Revenge of the Sith. He also played Oppo Rancisis in Episode I and II, and Rune Haako, Mik Regrap, Horox Ryyder, Graxol Kelvyyn, and Orn Free Taa in Episode I. He also filmed scenes for Solo: A Star Wars Story that were deleted from the final film where he played a new character known as The Mad Uncle. He has played minor roles in many different films and commercials. He is usually under a mask or being the voice of a character, and is therefore rarely recognized in public.
