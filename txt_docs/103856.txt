Brusela
807

Brusela was a Human female who was the grandmother of Bria Tharen. Renn Tharen, wanting to honor his mother and mother-in-law both, yet not wanting his daughter to be stuck with either of their names, combined the first syllables of their names, Brusela and Iaphagena, to make Bria. *Rebel Dawn Category:Corellians Category:Females Category:Humans
