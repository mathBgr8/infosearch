12th Squadron
705

The 12th Squadron was an Imperial starfighter squadron operating some time after the end of the Clone Wars. 12th Squadron served onboard the Assiduous at the behest of Inquisitor Valin Draco. They were one of the first Imperial squadrons to switch from V-wing starfighters to TIE/LN starfighters. The 12th participated in a battle above Bespin. Category:Imperial starfighter squadrons
