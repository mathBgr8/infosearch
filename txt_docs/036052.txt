|gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Blond |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Light |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=House Halliikeenovich |masters= |apprentices= }} Són Halliikeenovich was a Human male of the House Halliikeenovich, a noble house from the planet Dramassia in the Juvex sector. During the last years of the Galactic Republic, Halliikenovich was a regular patron of the Galaxies Opera House on the planet Coruscant. He attended the Squid Lake production at the Galaxies Opera House in 19 BBY. He hoped to gain his House prestige by marrying Terr Taneel and joining their Houses. If she returned his interest, it was not obvious to the public. Són Halliikeenovich is a tuckerization of the name of his portrayer, Jason Hallikainen.
