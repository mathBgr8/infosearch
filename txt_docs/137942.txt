A female Human, Tagdel worked for the Galactic Republic's Ministry of Finance during the last decade of the government's existence. A wealthy woman, Tagdel loved her orphaned nephew, Joram Kithe, and ensured that he attended the best institutes of learning at her expense. Despite her greatest efforts, Tagdel was disappointed with Kithe and his apparent lack of focus and unemployment. Insisting that he find employment, Tagdel used her connections within the government to get him an accounting position within the Department of Cost Accounting.
