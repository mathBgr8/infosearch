Martika
606

Martika was a cargo starship which ran shipping jobs from the planet Alderaan. When Brock Lar came to the planet, he managed to become the first mate of this ship. Martika was created by Matthew Berry and Craig Farr, two Star Wars fans, for the backstory of their Brock Lar entry in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines.
