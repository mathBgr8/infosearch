Alderaan Ruge Liqueur
1607

Alderaan Ruge Liqueur was an alcoholic drink that shared its name with Alderaan, a planet that was destroyed by the Galactic Empire using the first Death Star's superlaser. During the New Republic era, the Ithorian gangster Marg Sonat hired a crew to smuggle a crate from Rantine Space Station to Kwenn Space Station. Among its contents were three bottles of the very rare liqueur that were each worth about 350 credits. However, the liqueur and other luxury items were only decoys to distract Imperial customs inspectors like Sergeant Tenric from noticing the true treasure: a protoype personal energy shield that crimelord Sard Nightbringer intended to sell at an auction on the Stellar Mermaid.
