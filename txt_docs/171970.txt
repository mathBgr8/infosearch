Gamu
1443

Gamu group was a group of two Lambda-class T-4a shuttles used by the Ripoblus during the Sepan Civil War. Imperial forces encountered shuttles Gamu as Dimok starships staged a raid on a Ripolus supply convoy. Shuttles Gamu aided Imperial starfighters in pushing back the Dimok attackers. They were not much of a match for their Dimok opponents, so there may be chance that they were destroyed.
