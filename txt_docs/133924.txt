Christina Wald

Christina Wald is an artist. She illustrated several West End Games Star Wars roleplaying books as well as roleplaying books for other series such as Shadowrun or Mechwarrior. *Instant Adventures *Stock Ships *Star Wars Gamemaster Screen, Revised *Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *Tales of the Jedi Companion *Lords of the Expanse *The Kathol Rift *No Disintegrations *Platt's Smugglers Guide *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Operation: Elrood Category:Roleplaying game artists
