Lords of Dismay Flightknife
1031

Lords of Dismay Flightknife was a unit of Adumari Blade-32 superiority fighters. They were the sixth Adumari unit to challenge Wedge Antilles' Red Flight to a duel during Red Flight's test run on the Blade-32 Fighters. During Red Flight's escape from the nation of Cartann on the planet Adumar, the Lords of Dismay and Strike the Moons Flightknife appeared, seemingly to finish off the badly damaged Red Flight. Suddenly, Strike the Moons fired upon them, justifying their action as refusing to allow "some honor-grubber" prevent Red Flight's escape. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Adumari flightknives
