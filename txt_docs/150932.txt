|result= |battles= |side1= |side2= |side3= |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= }} The Glite-Ven hostilities were a conflict fought in the Glite-Ven system. By the year 2 ABY, the crime lord Ploovo Two-For-One had acquired the modified EV-series supervisor droid EV-4D9 during the hostilities.
