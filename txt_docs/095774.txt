Corbos Mining City
1275

Corbos Mining City was an experimental mine established in a crater on on Corbos in 12 ABY. It generated electricity by using a heat exchanger in a volcanic crater, and it housed 793 miners and their families. All were killed by a Leviathan attack. *Jedi Academy: Leviathan 1 Category:Cities Category:Mining areas
