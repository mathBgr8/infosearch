Moplin Jarron
1505

Moplin Jarron was a male Sullustan who ran Heff's Souvenirs at Mos Eisley. He bought the place when Heff died. Since the shop was basically a junk store and the tourist trade during the Civil War was too low on Tatooine to make a living from the shop, Jarron got his primary income from his ability to forge documents such as IDs and passports. *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope, Second Edition Category:Males Category:Merchants Category:Sullustans Category:Tatooinians
