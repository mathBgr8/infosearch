Bennet Guillory
1344

Bennet Guillory is the actor who played Jedi Master Qu Rahn in the cutscenes of Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II, credited as "Bennett Gilory" in the game's instruction manual. He also played a Medical Big Shot in Star Trek in an episode of Deep Space Nine and is thus one of the actors to have appeared in both Star Wars and Star Trek material.
