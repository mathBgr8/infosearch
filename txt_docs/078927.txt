Von Shul
566

Von Shul was a member of the Intendant caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. A Consul of Domain Shul, he developed a rivalry with Consul Melaan of Domain Nar. Supreme Overlord Shimrra ordered them to put aside their differences, and concentrate on defeating the Galactic Alliance. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Shul, Von
