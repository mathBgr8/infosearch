Benar
820

Benar were a pair of Modular conveyors belonging to the Imperials. Benar group were part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor to the Corrupter in the Mev system. Both of their cargoes were 'provisions'. The ships were then attacked by Rebel raiders and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Imperial Navy units
