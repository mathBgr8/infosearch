AV-21
1446

The AV-21 was a type of vehicle. In 10 BBY,Solo: A Star Wars Story The Official Guide the Corellian Han Solo told Tobias Beckett that he had been stealing AV-21s since he was ten years old. In the Star Wars canon, AV-21s were first identified in the 2018 Anthology film Solo: A Star Wars Story.
