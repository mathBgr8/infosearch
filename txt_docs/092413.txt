Delta Base
1945

Delta Base, also known as the Suolriep Sector HQ, was a remote Alliance to Restore the Republic base on New Kisge, the eighth moon of Chaasch in Suolriep sector. It was established by General Vanden Willard, with Lieutenant Tole Warren as one of his top officers.Alliance Intelligence Reports It was named because of famed Delta Unit, from Scandium Team. Rebels Mors OdrionSecrets of the Sisar Run and Rekara were assigned to Delta Base soon after the Battle of Yavin. Former Scandium Team leader Harovan Toth also went to Delta Base after semi-retiring, to work as patrol pilot.
