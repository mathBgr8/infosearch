Kiili
1428

Kiili was a Human female university student taking courses in nonhuman ethnology at the University of Rudrig. In 2 BBY, Kiili and her friend Viurre encountered a Wookiee named Chewbacca while walking their campus&mdash;fascinated by the massive alien, the two students accompanied Chewbacca and his friend Han Solo, who found the pair quite attractive, for a time.
