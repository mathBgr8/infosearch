Following the foundation of the Rebel Alliance in 2 BBY,The Essential Guide to Warfare the first base of the Alliance High Command, named Chrellis Base, was established on the planetoid under the command of the Human General Jan Dodonna. When the Galactic Empire reallocated its sector fleets, Chrellis Base became uncomfortably close to Imperial Navy activity. The base, which had been designed for continual relocation, was then moved to the Tion Cluster planet Brigia. The fact that Chrellis hosted the first Alliance High Command base was eventually recorded in a 25 ABY galactic history chronicle by the Historical Council of the New RepublicThe Essential Chronology as well as its 36 ABY updated edition by the Council's incarnation under the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances.
