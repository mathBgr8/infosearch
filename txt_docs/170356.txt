Tiger (CR90 corvette)
749

Tiger was a group of two CR90 corvettes belonging to the Galactic Empire. They, along with some Assault Gunboats, attempted to prevent the destruction of an Imperial research facility which was trying to duplicate the shield X that was used in Operation Ram's Head.
