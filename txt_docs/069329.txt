Garbage grabber
1280

thumb|200px|Squib with garbage grabber A garbage grabber, also known as a power grappler, was a tool used to collect hard-to-reach items. The device consisted of a claw attached to a long shaft. The grabber utilized tractor beam technology. When the wielder desired to pick up something out of reach, he or she could capture it with the tractor beam, bring it close, and secure it with the claw. The garbage grabber was of Squib origin, hence the association with garbage.
