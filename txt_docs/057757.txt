Galacticorp Mines
757

Galacticorp Mines was a corporation that was bidding for the rights to mine ore from the Hyggin Mountains on the planet Seltos during the early years of the New Republic. They were negotiating with Sinya Deboora, Seltos' Magister of Trade. Category:Mining companies
