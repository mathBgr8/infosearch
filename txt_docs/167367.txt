Microcase
1140

Microcases, also spelled "micro-cases" and called "micro-packs" were large cases that could be carried on the back of an adult male Human. Luke Skywalker hid Biggs and Windy, Leia's two brothers, in hollowed out microcases, and put them into hibernation using a power pack from Kane Starkiller's chest cavity. The shell of the microcase resisted scanners, making it easier for Luke to get his companions to safety in them. The avoidance of scanners by hiding someone in a microcase eventually became the secret compartments on board the Millennium Falcon, that resisted the the Empire's scanner equipment. *The Star Wars rough draft *The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film Category:Containers
