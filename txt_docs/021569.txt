Tibanna gas platform
4310

thumb|right|250px|A tibanna gas mining platform. Tibanna gas platforms were large floating structures that were used to extract tibanna gas. Tibanna gas stations could be found on different gas giants, like Bespin, Kaer,Star Wars: Legacy Kril'Dor,Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Jedi Academy Vandin and Ord Ibanna. Rogue Squadron raided the Galactic Empire's platforms on Taloraan several months after the Battle of Yavin.Star Wars: Rogue Squadron Following the confrontation at Bespin's Cloud City between Darth Vader, Han Solo and Leia Organa, Imperial forces remained on the planet and had a garrison of starfighters watch over the platforms' mining operations. Rebel troopers fought against stormtroopers for control of a platform during a battle on Bespin.Star Wars: Battlefront The Rebel Alliance also raided the platforms to steal tibanna supplies.Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader Rogue Squadron was assigned to destroy an Imperial research base on Destrillion, but instead found a gas platform guarded by TIE Hunters.Star Wars: Rogue Squadron III: Rebel Strike The Jedi Knight Jaden Korr, along with the New Republic pilot Wedge Antilles, liberated another platform on Kril'Dor from the Empire. Tibanna gas platforms first appeared in Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back.
