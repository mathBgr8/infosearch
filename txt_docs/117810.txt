*Tionne Solusar |origin= |locations= |early= |prominence= |fragmented= |restored= |extinct= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Jedi Order *New Republic *New Jedi Order *Galactic Federation of Free Alliances }} The Solusar family was a Force-sensitive family of Human Jedi Knights. Its known members included Ranik Solusar, Kam Solusar, and his wife Tionne. The original creator of Kam Solusar, Tom Veitch, intended to expand upon the Solusar Family through his unreleased novella, Star Wars: Lightsider.
