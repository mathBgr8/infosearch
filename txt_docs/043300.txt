Battle of Bogo Rai
1562

The Battle of Bogo Rai was a famous battle fought by the Chiss at some point prior to the Swarm War. The Chiss forces deployed during the battle were noted for utilizing a space fleet to perform close ground support. This tactic was again used during the Battle of Tenupe, after the Colony learned of the Battle of Bogo Rai from the Joiner ReyaTaat, former Chiss intelligence officer Daer'ey'ath.
