Wuppa
2196

thumb|right|Ho'Din head tresses resembled wuppa flowers in bloom. The wuppa was a species of plant that grew on Moltok, a rainforest-covered planet that was located in the Atrivis sector of the Outer Rim Territories.Star Wars: Rebellion When in full bloom, the wuppa had bright flowers of red and violet. These flowers were similar in appearance to the head tresses of the sentient Ho'Din species, such that the reptilian species resembled giant wuppa plants. In addition to its blooms, the wuppa took the form of tall grasses. Slender Ho'Din such as the smuggler Par'tah often resembled swaying wuppa grass.Heir to the Empire Sourcebook The wuppa plant was created by Troy Denning for the first edition of the Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, published by West End Games in 1989. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Heir to the Empire Sourcebook *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Plants of the Outer Rim Territories
