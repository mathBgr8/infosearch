How to Get Banned (Have a Mind)
2074

"How to Get Banned (Have a Mind)" was a song featured in the album Advanced Explosives Handbook by Deeply Religious, a rock music group known for their fast, cynical songs that disregarded the laws of the Galactic Empire. The song was released at some point prior to the Battle of Endor between the Empire and the Rebel Alliance in the year 4 ABY. Due to the band's disregard for Imperial law in their music, the Imperial Board of Culture banned the song and its album, although copies of it could still be found on the black market.
