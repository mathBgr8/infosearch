Dedra
895

Dedra was a student interested in the Sith who studied under Doctor Murk Lundi. Following the incident which drove the doctor and his two prized students, Norval and Omal, insane, Dedra took care of the unstable Omal. *Jedi Apprentice Special Edition: The Followers *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females
