During the Galactic Civil War, the Rebel Alliance maintained a depot there. A group of Alliance operatives that used the Simiyiar-class light freighter Waterspire operated in Wilpiet's vicinity. Less than two months prior to some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Kerestian bounty hunter Milacass stole the Waterspire from the Wilpiet depot, as a result becoming wanted for theft and destruction of Alliance property. Wilpiet was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
