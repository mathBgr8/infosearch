Greg Mitchell
1122

Greg Mitchell wrote the backstory of the Dusty Duck, and co-wrote the backstory of Silya Shessaun appearing in the Databank, through the What's The Story? feature of Hyperspace. His alias is "Hedec Ga" on StarWars.com and he is on TheForce.net. (co-written by Twi'LekEntertainer, Evan Burger and Master Starkeiller) - April 2006 - August 2006 - September 2013 - December 2013 Mitchell, Greg
