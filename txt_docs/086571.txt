Garth was the husband of Ganeed, the sister of the King Meenon of Senali. He had five children: Hinen, Jaret, Drenna, Tinta, and Bu. He welcomed Qui-Gon Jinn and Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi when they visited Senali. *Jedi Apprentice: The Shattered Peace Category:Males Category:Senali
