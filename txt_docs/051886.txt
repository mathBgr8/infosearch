Renia Brila
1478

Renia Brila, wife of Tanass Brila, was a doctor and a member of the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. A respected physician, she was recruited into the Alliance along with her husband and operated a safehouse for the local Rebel cell. She had her private practice as well as working at a local medical center. Brila was intelligent and outgoing.
