Nania
895

Nania was a member of the Freelies. She, like all Freelies, sabotaged the factories of Vorzyd IV, to make the people realize that the job was not the main thing in a perfect society that the Vorzydiaks tried to built. She was a supporter and a good friend of Grath. *Jedi Apprentice: The Threat Within *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Freelies Category:Vorzydiaks
