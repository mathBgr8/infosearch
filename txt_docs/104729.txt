Seinar Corporation
1026

The Seinar Corporation was allied with Roganda Ismaren in 12 ABY. Its president and many of its executive board were Sullustans.Children of the Jedi Luke Skywalker used a proton blaster manufactured by Seinar on Nam Chorios in 13 ABY.Planet of Twilight "Seinar" may be a misspelling of "Sienar"; however, there is no known "Sienar Corporation," and all of Sienar's projects were not run by Sullustans.
