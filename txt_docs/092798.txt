Corellian powersuit
2169

The Corellian powersuit was a suit of powered armor of Corellian origin. It employed an energized exoskeleton that included a series of servomotors. These characteristics enhanced the wearer's strength. The armor was favored by professional soldiers, mercenaries, and bounty hunters.
