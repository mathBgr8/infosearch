Wendiri Nightstar
647

The Wendiri Nightstar was a luxury pleasure cruiser that was attacked by Warlord Delurin's flagship, the Galaxy Dragon. Though he managed to knock out the hyperdrive engines, a group of passengers were able to hold back his dragon troopers long enough for them to be repaired, and the ship jumped into lightspeed to escape.
