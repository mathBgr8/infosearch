Invisible Economy
982

The "Invisible Economy" was the term given to the underworld market, free from the restrictions of the Galactic Empire. The invisible economy was composed of: *Major underworld occupations such as crime lords, outlaw techs, spice-jackers, smugglers, pirates, slavers, mercenaries, and spice sifters; *Occupations such as accountants, bodyguards, professional women, and cooks that served the upper echelon *Petty criminals seeking to emulate the upper echelon *Bounty hunters seeking to arrest the upper echelon *Dark Empire Sourcebook Category:Criminal organizations Category:Economic systems Category:Phrases Category:Secret societies
