It was written by James Luceno and illustrated by Hans Jenssen and Richard Chasemore and John Mullaney with Curtis Saxton as consultant and was published by DK on August 5, 2004. *Tatooine *Lars Homestead *Tosche Station / Ben's House *Mos Eisley *The Cantina *The Death Star *The Great Temple *Battle of Hoth *Echo Base *Dagobah *Yoda's House *Cloud City *Processing Vane *Jabba's Palace *Jabba's Throne Room *Battle of Endor *Ewok Village *Death Star II *Emperor's Lair *Executor Command Tower Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy presented a new location for Jabba's Palace, which had historically been placed at the southwestern edge of the Western Dune Sea&mdash;a location far to the southwest of Mos Eisley, Anchorhead, and the Jundland Wastes. Inside the Worlds moved the Palace and the Great Pit of Carkoon to the southwestern edge of the Northern Dune Sea instead, placing it at the northeastern edge of the Great Mesra Plateau and significantly closer to other cities.
