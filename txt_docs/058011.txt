Drach'nam
1365

|habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Drach'nam were a sentient reptilian alien race from La'drach that was known more for size and strength than intelligence. They had three fingers on each hand and three toes on each foot. Drach'nam had pale, pinkish blood. A slave consortium on the planet Torpris was run by a Drach'nam crime lord, Chay Praysh. Praysh also stationed many Drach'nam within his facility. *The New Essential Guide to Characters Category:Reptilian sentient species
