Inad (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
2561

Inad was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in the Rebel Alliance Fleet during the Galactic Civil War. After the discovery of the Death Star project and, in order to learn more about it, Alliance Special Forces managed to sabotage and disable a Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport that was carrying Imperial officers assigned to the Death Star as it traveled through the Corellian system. The Alliance sent the Inad, along with the Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Hunter, to capture the disabled transport. The operation was protected by three X-wings from Blue Squadron and three Y-wings from Red Squadron, flown by Keyan Farlander, Namrhe and Omin-Oreh. Soon after the mission began, several Assault Gunboats arrived. The Rebel fighters moved to intercept at the cost of all three X-wings. The Hunter boarded the transport and captured the officers. However while both vessels were returning to the Inad, the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Immortal arrived and began to launch fighters. An Imperial transport also jumped out of hyperspace and attempted to recapture the fleeing transport. Farlander destroyed the attacking transport, and two more which arrived shortly afterwards. However, both his wingmen were shot down while attempting to keep the Imperial fighters off his tail.
