Vratix (language)
1329

Vratix was the native language of the Vratix species from Thyferra. It was high-pitched and used clicking sounds and even skin color changes for punctuation. Species and droids physically unable to change color and click could understand Vratix language, but not speak it. However, the Vratix were a social species, trading with many other races.
