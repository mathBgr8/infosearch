Star Wars Weekly 110 is the one hundred tenth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on April 2, 1980. It included the original story "The Word for World is Death!". *"The Word for World is Death!" *"Tales of the Watcher: Heroes of the galaxy Part 5" *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 20: Confrontation with Ryker!" *"Tales of the Watcher: Rocket ship!"
