|plating=Orange, black, and white |armament= |equipment= |affiliation= }} GX-8 was a sewing assistant droid that was employed by Arta Kleidun, owner of Black Spire Outfitters, to help keep up with demand at her store. It helped with mending clothing and other minor sewing tasks using its many arms, each one of which was specially designed for mending and sewing garments. In 34 ABY,Star Wars: Timelines dates the events of Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge to 34 ABY. during the First Order occupation of the planet Batuu, GX-8 sat in power-down mode in its stall within Black Spire Outfitters, which was located in Black Spire Outpost on Batuu. Also in that year, the droid was mentioned in Traveler's Guide to Batuu, a book written by historian Eloc Throno. GX-8 had orange, black, and white plating and a black sensor. GX-8 first appeared as a prop within the themed land Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge, which opened to guests at Disneyland Park and Disney's Hollywood Studios in 2019.
