Alfex Cargo Stacks
886

thumb|right|250px|The Alfex Cargo Stacks Alfex Cargo Stacks was a cargo complex on Brentaal. Its business was sorting cargo bound for the same destination into individual shipments, which were then sent to transports to be delivered. Alfex was also a front for Black Sun. Black Sun's local leaders based themselves out of specially outfitted rooms hidden in Alfex's cargo containers, where they coordinated criminal operations on the planet, including smuggling.Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Brentaal IV locations
