|formed= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Coruscant Full Symphony was a famous musical band during the Galactic Civil War. Emperor's Hand Mara Jade enjoyed this band and was often present at its concert during the Imperial era. Shortly after the Battle of Endor, the Offworld Special Events organized a concert in the Chibias concert hall.
