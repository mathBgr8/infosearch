Supreme Commander (Republic)
2999

The Supreme Commander of the Republic Military was the title given to the head of the Republic Military. During the Cold War, it was held by the Duros Stantorrs. With the beginning of the Galactic War, the title passed to Jace Malcom. Millennia later, before the end of the Clone Wars the Senate enacted the Security Act amendment, making Chancellor Palpatine the Supreme Commander. It gave him constitutional authority over the Republic Armed Forces, stripping it away from the Jedi and the Senate.
