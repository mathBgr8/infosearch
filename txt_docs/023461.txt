The Atrisian Iron was a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser that served in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. It was destroyed by Separatist forces during the Battle of Duro. Category:Dreadnaught-class heavy cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
