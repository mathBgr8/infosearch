|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Voice actress |companies= |website= }} }} Vanessa Marshall (born October 19, 1969) is an American voice actress and model, and the daughter of veteran actress Joan Van Ark. She voiced the Rebel Twi'lek pilot character Hera Syndulla as main character on the animated series Star Wars Rebels and as recurring character on the animated series Star Wars: The Bad Batch and later voiced Rook Kast for the seventh season of Star Wars: The Clone Wars. Marshall has provided the voice for Jan Ors in Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast, as well as additional voices for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords.
