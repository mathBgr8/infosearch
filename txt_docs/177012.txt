Ammo canister
1233

An ammo canister was a small container of small arms ammunition. Because of its miniature size, a canister was generally limited to one to two clips of ammo and a few grenades.Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide During the Clone Wars and the Galactic Civil War, engineers and pilots commonly carried such canisters to readily resupply their allies with ammunition. It was often designed with a glowing red line around its cap that blinked on and off along with the words "AMMO SUPPLY" printed in Aurebesh along its side. *Star Wars: Battlefront *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars Battlefront: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Battlefront II: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Weapon accessories
