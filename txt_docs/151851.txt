OmniNode communications set
1829

The OmniNode communications set was a communication device. The unit's signal power was often used as a base level for escape pod emergency homing beacons. Produced by the BCC corporation, the OmniNode communications set was a larger, more powerful version of the simple BCC MultiNode communications link. The set was capable of transmitting to orbiting starships even during harsh weather and could manage twenty different signals at one time.
