Jahrunba
1325

Jahrunba was a male Dug under Sebolto's employ during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted for being one of three Dug brothers, the others being Nahrunba and Sahrunba, who smuggled numerous death sticks to the Gran Youth Council of Pixelito.
