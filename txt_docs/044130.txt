Changing the Odds: The Adventures of Dannen Lifehold
5040

"Changing the Odds: The Adventures of Dannen Lifehold" was a short story written by Dave Marron, originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 3 by West End Games in August 1994 and later archived online at Hyperspace. Though he does not want to get involved with the Rebellion, Dannen Lifehold delivers medical supplies from Alderaan to a Rebel outpost on Rafft. A homing beacon hidden in the cargo alerts the Empire to the Rebel presence.
