|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=48 |isbn=1569713073 |isbn2= |timeline=3998 BBY |series=Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising |issues=TPB |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising trade paperback is a graphic novel collecting both issues in the Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising series of comics. It was published by Dark Horse Comics on December 17, 1997. Determined to uproot Sith magic from Onderon, Jedi Master Arca orders the sarcophagi of Queen Amanoa and Dark Jedi Freedon Nadd removed to the Dxun moon. When the spirit-avatar of Nadd appears and captures Arca, Ulic Qel-Droma faces the task of saving Arca and the planet from being completely swallowed by evil. *Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising 1 *Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising 2 02
