Castaways of Endor
5283

|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Castaways of Endor is an article on Hyperspace that details life on Endor, especially the flora and fauna from the Ewoks television show. Daniel Wallace and Robin Pronovost co-wrote the article with illustrations provided by Pronovost. The Ewoks cartoon was a favorite of both Wallace and Pronovost growing up. Beginning in 2001, the pair began collaborating on the article and drew inspiration from the ongoing feature The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life in the magazine Star Wars Gamer. According to Wallace, the article was unsuccessfully pitched for Star Wars Insider. It was repackaged and pitched for Star Wars Gamer with roleplaying details, but that magazine's cancellation left the article unpublished.
