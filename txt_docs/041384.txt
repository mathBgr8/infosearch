Paul Campion
706

Paul Campion was the cover artist for Jedi Dawn and possibly The Bounty Hunter, as well as The Lost Jedi Adventure Game Book that collected the two books in an omnibus edition. Paul Campion worked as a freelance cover artist for ten years, before he completed a degree in computer animation and went on to work on feature films including The Lord of the Rings trilogy, Constantine and Sin City.
