thumb|250px|A blob attacking Tash Arranda A blob was the nickname for a gelatinous, green creature from Gobindi. These creatures were not native to the jungle as the residents thought. They were actually the result of a virus created by Borborygmus Gog, an Imperial bio-scientist. The virus, unlike most, thrived on heat, such as the natural body heat created from angry feelings as well as Gobindi's climate. Gog would capture beings and inject them with the virus. The area where the shot was injected would swell up and soon spurt green goo. The goo would cover the body and turn them into a blob.
