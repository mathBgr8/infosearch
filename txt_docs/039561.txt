|listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation= |companies= |website= }} Martha Veitch was an author who wrote the short story A Hunter's Fate: Greedo's Tale, in which she fleshed out Greedo's backstory prior to his demise in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. She was also married to late Tales of the Jedi author Tom Veitch. Martha died on September 11, 2022. Category:Short story authors
