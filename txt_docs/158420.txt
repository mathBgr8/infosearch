Keeper (corvette group)
772

Keeper was a pair of CR90 corvettes belonging to the Rebel Alliance. They were fitted with explosives and were attempting to destroy the Dreadnaught Dargon through crashing into the ship. The attempt was foiled by Maarek Stele in a TIE Avenger. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
