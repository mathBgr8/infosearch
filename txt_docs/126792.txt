Redge Dunlak
1442

Redge Dunlak was a male Human who worked as a Podracing official during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted for tampering with Hutt-owned pods prior to last year's Boonta Eve Podrace. He was captured by the bounty hunter Jango Fett who claimed the bounty on his head. Redge Dunlak' bounty is 2,400 credits alive and 1,200 credits dead.
