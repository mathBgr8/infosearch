Rathe Palror
1591

Rathe Palror was a male Tunroth mercenary who served as a member of Churhee's Riflemen during the Galactic Civil War. He also was a crew member of the Hopskip that was sent on a mission by Grand Admiral Thrawn in 2 ABY.
