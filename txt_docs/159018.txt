Hutt Citadel
1046

A Hutt Citadel was a fortress owned by a Hutt. Jabba Desilijic Tiure had such a building on Tatooine in 33 BBY. Unlike the tri-domed structure of his palace, the Hutt Citadel of Jabba's was a series of mostly flat-roofed cylindrical towers, with Ion attractors in the center of each one, that housed craft including many flyers to rout attacking forces and a shuttle. *Star Wars (1998) 5 *Star Wars (1998) 6 Category:Buildings Category:Hutt Clan locations Category:Tatooine locations
