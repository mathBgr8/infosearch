Pydyrian healing stick
680

A Pydyrian healing stick was an object made on the Almanian moon of Pydyr with astounding healing capabilities. When rubbed against a surface, it foamed, leaving a white residue behind. It was in use all through the galaxy before bacta became common, and some thought it was more effective. *The New Rebellion *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Medical tools
