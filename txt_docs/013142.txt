Ebor Taulk
2837

Ebor Taulk was a male Jedi Padawan serving the Jedi Order during the final years of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive male, trained in the ways of the Force, and was selected as the Padawan of Jedi Master Naeshahn. The pair was dispatched by Master of the Order Mace Windu to investigate the disturbance in the Yinchorri system, unknowingly walking into the midst of a Sith plot. Upon their arrival, the Sith-incited violence on Yinchorr overwhelmed Naeshahn and Padawan Taulk and they were killed by the war-like Yinchorri. At the behest of Darth Sidious, the architect of the uprising, Sate Pestage and Kinman Doriana arranged for the bodies of the Jedi to be shipped to the Presidential Tower of the Senate Building on Coruscant.
