Zalorian Separatists
3121

The Zaloriis Separatists were an organization of humans from the planet Zaloriis who wanted to secede from the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War, a conflict which it fought the Alliance to Restore the Republic. In 3 ABY, the separatists secretly allied themselves with the Alliance and captured most of the Imperial forces on Zaloriis. The planet was later visited by the second-in-command of the Empire, Darth Vader, who was searching for the Alliance and had come to Zaloriis to meet with Transe Decar, one of Zaloriis's Ministers and a separatist leader. With several soldiers, Decar ambushed Vader and announced that Zaloriis had seceded, but was killed by him. Vader then questioned one of the soldiers where the Imperial forces were, and then killed all of them. The Empire then freed their captured stormtroopers and destroyed the Separatist headquarters at the planet's capital, Zaloriis City, ending their secession. Zaloriis Separatists were first mentioned in the strategy guide for the 2001 PC video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds,Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Prima's Official Strategy Guide and later appeared in the game and its 2002 expansion.
