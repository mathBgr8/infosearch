Tariga
3426

Tariga was a male Human raised on a planet in the Outer Rim Territories. She spoke the Twi'lek language rather than Basic as her native tongue. She was a student at the Sith Academy on Korriban 3956 BBY. When Revan had found the Star Map he was searching for on Korriban and killed Uthar Wynn, the master of the academy, Tariga turned on Revan, like the other students and guards, when Revan entered the Sith Academy. However, they were all killed by the former Dark Lord. Tariga appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, developed by BioWare. Tamlen, another non-player character refers to Tariga using she/her pronouns in dialogue, which this article assumes was intended.
