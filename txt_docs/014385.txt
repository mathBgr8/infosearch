It was the home planet of the Affytechans, a race of sentient botanicals. In 18 BBY, the Galactic Empire stationed several stormtroopers on the world to be picked up by the Eye of Palpatine. After the ship was temporarily disabled by Callista Ming, its arrival was delayed until 12 ABY, by which point the stormtrooper contingent stationed on the planet was long dead.
