Raw Deal
2089

The Raw Deal was a SoroSuub Personal Luxury Yacht 3000 belonging to Outer Rim entrepreneur and casino owner Dunari. The yacht was the venue of a meeting between Dunari and Rebel Alliance delegates pm Saloon shortly before the Battle of Endor, in order to arrange supplying procedures. During the meeting the yacht came under attack from both pirates and Imperial forces. Fortunately, the covert operations Z-95 Headhunter Bandit Squadron were on hand to defend it. The ship escaped the assault against Dunari's Rest by the Corrupter and Lament, having on board Dunari and Emon Azzameen. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Personal Luxury Yacht 3000s
