Cavern of Teeth
1076

The Cavern of Teeth was a large cavern in Eskaron which at least one race course went through. The cavern was over three kilometers long and around 200 meters high. The cavern got its name from the enormous stalactites and stalagmites littered throughout. A pilot could not fly straight through the cavern, having instead to dodge around the pillars. Sometimes pilot shot the stalactites, to make them fall on their rivals. In 32 BBY, Blood Raptors member Gael Kinner's G-59 Cannibalizer and intimidated Rodian entrepreneur Teeloo's YT-1210 light freighter Neeva-Beelo raced through Eskaron, passing through this area.
