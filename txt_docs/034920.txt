Jenuwaa Sea
1957

|location=Kalee |terrain= |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} crash|}} The Jenuwaa Sea was a small body of water located on Kalee, which was the site of a battle between the Kaleesh and Yam'rii during the Huk War. When Ronderu lij Kummar was killed battling the Yam'rii on the beaches of Kalee, her body was lost to the waters of the Jenuwaa Sea. Later in the war, when the Separatists planted a bomb aboard Grievous' shuttle, the destroyed ship plummeted into the sea.
