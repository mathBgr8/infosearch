Bondo
2002

}} Bondo was the leader of the Travelling Jindas, a nomadic tribe of Jindas on the Forest Moon of Endor. The overweight Jinda wore a red tunic and much jewelry. Bondo was a jovial leader, although he frequently got his tribe lost in the forests of Endor. Bondo was voiced by Don McManus.
