Rebelscum.com
1748

Rebelscum.com is a website that centers around Star Wars collecting. Rebelscum provides information and news relating to licensed merchandise from companies like Hasbro and Gentle Giant. The site is updated frequently, provides coverage of Star Wars conventions, and has several product databases with reference galleries and archives. In 1996, Philip Wise started the website "Philip's Star Wars Toys, Pictures and Thoughts." Eventually, with the help of Chris H. and the release of The Star Wars Trilogy Special Edition, the site grew into Rebelscum, one of the most well-known and heavily trafficked Star Wars collecting news sites. Wise and Holoka still run Rebelscum today with contributors Dustin Roberts, D. Martin Myatt, Jay Shepard, Grahame Wright, and Chris Wyman, and the technical support of Paul Pritchard. The staff of sister site TheForce.net also write for Rebelscum. These writers include Eric Geller and Mike Barrick. *Star Wars: 1,000 Collectibles Category:Unofficial websites
