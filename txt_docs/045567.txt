Lucian Hejaran
1208

Lucian Hejaran was a prominent member of the influential and wealthy Hejaran family of House Mecetti. His family owned the moon of Pella known as Nightsinger's Orb and he lived at the Hejaran Castle until his death at the hands of his cousin Lady Brigta Hejaran. Lucian had learned that Brigta had been corrupted by the dark side and was under the influence of Palpatine's regime. Brigta ended up killing Lucian in order to protect her secret.
