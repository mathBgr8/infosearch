Gillian Libbert
1452

Gillian Libbert was the costume supervisor for Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones and Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. She has a cameo in Attack of the Clones as Lillea Bringbit, a patron in the Outlander Club. The character's name is an anagram of Libbert's name. She also inspired the name of Gilramos Libkath.
