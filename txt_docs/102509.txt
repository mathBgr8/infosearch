|published in=Star Wars Tales 20 |reprinted in= |pages=12 |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Luke Skywalker: Detective" was a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 20. Sometime during his teenage years, Luke Skywalker was challenged to a speeder race by a local braggart, Gorm Maldorf, in The Cage. On the morning of the race, Gorm's decapitated body was found in The Cage.
