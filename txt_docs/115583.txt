Raymond L. Velasco
615

Raymond L. Velasco was the author (credited as the "compiler") of the first edition of A Guide to the Star Wars Universe. The second and later editions of the book were written by Bill Slavicsek. These editions revised and expanded many of the entries in Velasco's edition, while omitting others completely. Category:Reference work authors
