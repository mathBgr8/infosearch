Anthracite

Anthracite was a substance that was used for starship fuel. It could be left in piles in the open. Nilash III was a known source of anthracite. Anthracite is a type of hard, glossy coal that burns without much smoke. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *X-Wing: Mercy Kill Category:Fuels
