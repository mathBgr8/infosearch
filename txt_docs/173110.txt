|pages= |published in=Star Wars Gamer 8 |reprinted in= }} "The Vaynai Archipelago" is a Star Wars Roleplaying Game source article published in Star Wars Gamer 8. It was written by Peter M. Schweighofer and illustrated by Ramón Pérez. The article does not mention any subjects that did not exist in-universe outside of the three eras of play available in the first edition of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game as defined by that edition's core rulebook&mdash;the time surrounding the events of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, the Rebellion era, and the New Jedi Order eraStar Wars Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook&mdash;therefore allowing game-master players to use the settings and adventure hooks described in the article in any of the mentioned time periods. The same lack of any chronological restrictions upon gameplay is also explicitly noted in the article's companion adventure, "Topside Infiltration," which was also published in Star Wars Gamer 8. The 2008 reference book The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia did, however, establish that the tourism industry of the islands on Vaynai, as described in the article, already catered to rich and famous individuals during the reign of the Galactic Empire and that both Preena Jallop and Whuvumm owned and operated their respective businesses around the same time. *Star Wars Gamer 8 *Guzerre *Havaal *Preena Jallop *Gallo Memm *Amira Nasrabi *Nyyv *Whuvumm |organisms= *Cliff-snail *Gnooroop |locations= *Ando *Aquaris *Bestine *Corporate Sector *Hutt Space *Hydian Way *Outer Rim Territories **Mon Calamari **Ryloth **Tion Cluster ***Lianna **Vaynai system ***Vaynai ****Boiling Sea ****Distant Peaks ****Vaynai archipelago *****Vaynai spaceport *****Streysel Island ******Doctor Guzerre's Clinic ******Fil'vye Transport Tower ******Illuminated Aquean ******Jungle Vault ******Ocean Harvester Maintenance Station ******Preena's Repair Bay ******Uncle Nyyv's Floating Eatery *Pantolomin *Perlemian Trade Route |organizations= *Fil'vye Transport *Ocean Harvesters *Smuggler *SoroSuub *Ubrikkian |species= *Bothan *Duros *Human *Ithorian *Klatooinian *Mon Calamari *Sullustan *Twi'lek |vehicles= *AQ21 Aqua-Harvester Factory *Cargo skiff *Herd ship *Load lifter *TIS Zeta 19 starfighter *Transport Barge |technology= *Bacta tank *Blast door *Comlink *Comm *Datapad *Force pike *Glow rod *Heavy blaster pistol *Hold-out blaster *Laser cannon **Quad laser cannon *Luma *Medpac *Quarterstaff *Repulsorlift *Stun baton *Tractor beam projector **Tractor beam *Turbolaser |miscellanea= *Aquyfin *Bacta *Credit *Cripe oil *Dejarik *Double-down *Glaav *Horansi *Ithorian foombah *Laro *Lugjack *Message to Spacers / "METOSP" *Pantol spine venom *Point 5 *Rodian rygg-noodles *Ryll *Sabacc *Scale dust *Seaweed salad *Slick *Sponge powder *Sullustan magwip *Transparisteel *Tregald *Trin sticks }} Category:Star Wars Gamer 8
