Exar Kun's light battle suit
1717

thumb|150px|Exar Kun's light battle suit. Exar Kun was known to wear various types of armored suits, including a customized light battle suit which offered considerable protection despite its weight and maintained good flexibility. Soon after the first Death Star was destroyed, a female Rodian treasure hunter named Suz Tanwa found what she reported to be Exar Kun's light battle suit on Yavin 4, which her ancestor, Suvam Tan had hidden in a storage pod.
