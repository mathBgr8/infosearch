Tranthebar mountain ripper
1230

The tranthebar mountain ripper was a species of bird of prey that possessed a wingspan of over five meters, and was native to the Mid Rim planet of Tranthellix. Captured beasts were trained by local hunters or used in illegal races throughout the Core, with racers strapped beneath them.
