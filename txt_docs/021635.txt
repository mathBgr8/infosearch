|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: The Last Command |issue=6 |next=last }} The Last Command 6 was the sixth and final issue in the Star Wars: The Last Command comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel The Last Command. It was released July 29, 1998. Grand Admiral Thrawn sets his trap for the New Republic as they arrive through hyperspace to the Bilbringi Shipyards. Meanwhile, Luke and Mara Jade confront Jedi Master C'baoth in his hidden prison chambers inside Mount Tantiss. C'baoth has a surprise waiting for Luke, and as Luke faces himself, the true battle of the Force begins.
