PI-series medical assistant droid
1870

The PI-Series medical assistant droid was a medical droid manufactured by Arakyd Industries, that was mainly used by medical researchers throughout the galaxy. It could not perform medical aid on its own, as it was incapable of making critical decisions, and had to be paired with either a living doctor or a 3Z3 medical droid.
