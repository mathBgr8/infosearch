Terson Comfort Carriers was an air taxi company on Coruscant around the time of the Yuuzhan Vong War. It was established by Hasville and his wife, Adray Terson. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Transport companies
