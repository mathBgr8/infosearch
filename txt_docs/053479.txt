Core Foodstuffs
1434

Core Foodstuffs was a major macro-farming corporation that, along with other companies such as the Tagge Restaurant Association and the Imperial Fruit Company, benefited from employing workers impoverished by the Galactic Empire's agri-sector maximization policies. Core Foodstuffs also had heavy investments on Taanab, along with Arcon Multinode Agricorp and the Tagge Restaurant Association. Core Foodstuffs was first mentioned in the Dark Empire Sourcebook, written by Michael Allen Horne, and released in June 1993. Core Foodstuffs was later briefly mentioned in The Essential Atlas, a reference book written by Jason Fry and Daniel Wallace, and released in August 2009. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Essential Atlas Category:Agricultural companies
