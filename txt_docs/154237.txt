Coolsap tree
505

Coolsap trees were densely-leaved trees that were native to the world of Gamorr, the homeworld of the Gamorrean species. Their name came from the fact that their sap was icy cold as was used as coolant in some ships. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way Category:Plants of Gamorr Category:Trees
