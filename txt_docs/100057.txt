Type 2 Navigation Buoy
919

The Type 2 Navigation Buoy was a hyperspace navigation buoy. Its holographic projection generated by a starship's flight computer served as a visual reference marker for the pilot. The Type 2 was commonly used to mark hazards in space. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Holographic technology Category:Hyperspace navigation buoys
