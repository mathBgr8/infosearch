Freighter Type C
2070

The Freighter Type C was a medium-sized cargo freighter manufactured by Yuung, Tygg & Josse Engineering. This common light freighter was found throughout the galaxy, and was YTJ Corporation's first attempt to satisfy the demand for an economical transport. Unarmed and only lightly armored, the Type C was best deployed as part of a convoy for safety, as its top speed was far slower than even the most primitive starfighter. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Freighter classes Category:Yuung, Tygg & Josse Engineering products
