Yinchorri starfighter
2259

A Yinchorri starfighter was a starfighter used by the Yinchorri during their expansion in 33 BBY. Such fighters required one pilot and were armed with a heavy blaster cannon and a medium blaster cannon. These vessels also had two sublight accelerators.
