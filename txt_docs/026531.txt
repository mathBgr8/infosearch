|place=The Rig |result=Shut down of the Crimson Nova |side1=Jedi Order |side2=Crimson Nova |side3= |side4= |commanders1= |commanders2= |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |ppl1=*High General Mace Windu *High General Kit Fisto *High General Saesee Tiin *High General Agen Kolar |ppl2=*Mika *Harll&dagger; |ppl3= |ppl4= |casual1=None |casual2=Harll |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} The Mission to the Rig was a mission undertaken by members of the Jedi High Council during the Clone Wars. In the aftermath of a battle on Null, the Jedi Council learned that a bounty had been posted on the Jedi, and that members of the Crimson Nova bounty hunters guild were attempting to collect. Rather than have his Jedi be targets on and off the battlefield, Mace Windu assembled a team of the most powerful Jedi available&mdash;Jedi Masters Kit Fisto, Saesee Tiin and Agen Kolar, the same Jedi that would later fight with Palpatine&mdash;and infiltrated the Crimson Nova headquarters on The Rig spacestation. Windu's team successfully breached The Rig's security. Mace himself remained fully visible to the bounty hunters, deflecting attention from the other Jedi. Kolar posed as Argent Kole, a bounty hunter looking to collect credits for his "captive", Kit Fisto. While Fisto incited riots among the prisoners, Tiin, in the guise of a smuggler, set off an explosion in the hangar bays. thumb|left|250px|[[Mace Windu/Legends|Mace Windu in battle against several bounty hunters]] Windu had arrived on the Rig and he demanded to know who in the Bounty Hunters' Guild was responsible for the bounties that were posted on the Jedi. The bounty hunters who were present attacked Windu, but were cut down. With The Rig in turmoil, the Jedi executed their show of force, fighting their way into the command center and ensuring the deaths of the guild leaders.
