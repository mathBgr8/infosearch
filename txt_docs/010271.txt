Mat Lucas
2623

Mat Lucas is an American voice actor who voiced Anakin Skywalker in Star Wars: Clone Wars and a few Star Wars video games. He is considered an "audio double" of Hayden Christensen. He also has done editing and other film-related tasks at AFI. Mat has stated that he hopes to do more voice-overs in the future. Despite this, he would be replaced by Matt Lanter, who voiced the character onward. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns *Star Wars: Clone Wars ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** ** *Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith video game *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars: The Clone Wars video game *Star Wars: The Force Unleashed (PSP, PS2 and Wii version only) (Anakin Skywalker) *Mat Lucas was originally going to voice Anakin in the 2008 show Star Wars: The Clone Wars as well, and even recorded his lines for the first 6 episodes. However, he was later replaced by Matt Lanter.
