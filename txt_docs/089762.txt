Quinze
1979

Quinze was serving a term as one of the Peacekeepers in 22 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Survivor's Quest to 22 ABY. As such, Quinze enforced the laws of the Outbound Flight Colony, the government established by those who survived the Galactic Republic's survey ship Outbound Flight after it crashed in a star cluster called the Redoubt. In that year, the Human Trilli mentioned to fellow crewman Dillian Pressor that Quinze was assigned to guard the Quarantine section, where the colony kept Force-sensitive children under surveillance, alongside Perry.
