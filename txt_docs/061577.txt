Kraigg
1399

Kraigg was a Rebel Alliance fighter pilot stationed onboard the Mon Calamari cruiser Defiance. Kraigg was killed in action near the Japai system in the months leading up to the Battle of Endor, along with fellow pilots Tabba and Lady Blue.
