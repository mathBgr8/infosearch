Star Chamber (Star Temple)
1337

The Star Chamber was the main chamber of the ancient Star Temple constructed by the Kwa c. 100,000 BBY. It was in this chamber that the Infinity Gate was contained. Inside the Star Chamber was a massive Realm of Infinity, which functioned similar to a pocket dimension.
