Echo Lake
1016

|terrain= |fauna=Fish |flora= |interest= |affiliation= }} Echo Lake was a lake on the Inner Rim world of Tasariq. It was located in the center of the mining boom-town Roaring Crater. Echo Lake provided oxygen for the crater, and was stocked with fish by the owner of the nearby Lakefresh Restaurant. Fishing was forbidden but licenses could be purchased from the restaurant.
