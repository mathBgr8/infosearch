Haven 3
1810

thumb|right|200px|[[Maarek Stele/Legends|Maarek Stele disabling Escort Shuttle Haven 3]] Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttle Haven 3 served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin deployed Haven 3, a part of flight group Haven during the Treachery at Ottega, attempting to kidnap Emperor Palpatine. Maarek Stele discovered the Emperor aboard Haven 3, swiftly destroying the remaining Escort Shuttles before disabling Haven 3. With Haven 3 disabled, Assassin-class corvette Mescue arrived to liberate the Emperor from his captors, and move him to safety. Haven 3 was presumably destroyed or abandoned afterwards. In Star Wars: TIE Fighter, which of the three Haven shuttles carries the Emperor is randomized.
