*T7-O1 *Kira Carsen *Archiban Frodrick Kimble *Fideltin Rusk *Scourge *Meteor *Theron ShanThe Old Republic: Annihilation *Gnost-Dural *Teff'ith |prerequisites= |upgrades= |first awarded=3956 BBY |last awarded= }} The Cross of Glory was the highest award given by the Old Republic during the time of the Jedi Civil War.The Old Republic: Revan After the Battle of Rakata Prime, Admiral Forn Dodonna bestowed the medal upon Revan and each of his allies for their actions up to and through the battle that saw the destruction of the Star Forge and the breaking of the Sith Empire. Revan's Cross of Glory later became a valuable prize to the forces of the Sith Empire on Corellia. After the Hero of Tython's duel with the Sith Emperor, the Hero of Tython, T7-O1, C2-N2, Kira Carsen, Archiban Frodrick Kimble, Fideltin Rusk and Scourge were awarded the Cross of Glory by Grand Master Satele Shan. In the aftermath of the Battle of Duro the Cross of Glory was awarded to Republic Strategic Information Service Agent Theron Shan, Jedi Master Gnost-Dural, and Teff'ith. If HK-47 or T3-M4 were to free Revan, Carth Onasi and Bastila Shan in the Leviathan prison, Carth makes a comment saying that: "You may be a droid, but when this is all over I'm going to see that the Republic gives you a medal for your part in this." This may or may not relate to the droids actually receiving any decorations. On Star Wars: The Old Republic, if the player is a Republic trooper and captured General Arkos Rakton, the Trooper is awarded the Cross of Glory.
