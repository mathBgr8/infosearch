In 15 BBY, Ambassador Zell of the planet Majoor went on an important peace mission to Armath, accompanied by a convoy of starships. Armath was first mentioned in the third issue of the Star Wars: Droids comic series, which was written by David Manak and published by Marvel Comics on May 13, 1986.
