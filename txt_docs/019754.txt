Battle of Jaga's Cluster
2551

The Battle of Jaga's Cluster was one of the final battles of the Mandalorian Wars. Despite the fact that the Mandalorians would soon lose the war and be almost entirely wiped out, they still managed an impressive victory against the Old Republic, destroying an entire enemy fleet group.
