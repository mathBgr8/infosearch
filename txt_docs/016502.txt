Outer Rim Territories |interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=Prengahl Kreen }} Breshkall was a small hidden base of the Twi'lek arms dealer Prengahl Kreen situated in the Breshkall system of the Outer Rim Territories' Khuiumin sector. It served as a refuge to Kreen, who operated in the Hook Nebula's Empartheca system during the Galactic Civil War between the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, and as home to his family, various servants, friends, and trusted associates. The weapons merchant did not carry out business at Breshkall. Unsolicited visitors were not welcome at the base, which was rumored to be a veritable floating fortress, and the select people who were made privy to the knowledge of the facility's location rarely dared to confirm those rumors. Breshkall was mentioned in the 1995 sourcebook Heroes & Rogues, authored by Rick Stuart and Paul Sudlow and published for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Heroes & Rogues Category:Fortresses Category:Khuiumin sector locations
