Sabrin Ring
1048

The Sabrin Ring was a territorial gang founded by Lady Miletta Sabrin after the Battle of Endor. Their operations were limited to three planets in the Sabrixin system - Sabrix, Elokas and Helisk. Their activities included dealing in large-scale extortion, credit laundering, forced labor and the manufacture of illegal goods. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy Category:Criminal organizations
