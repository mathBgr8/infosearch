The Restful Nights Hotel was reputed to be anything but. Located on Faldos, it was supposed to be a haven for people who did not want to be found, but its employees had a tendency to take up the bounty hunter trade when the mood struck them. *Star Wars Missions 5: The Hunt for Han Solo Category:Hotels
