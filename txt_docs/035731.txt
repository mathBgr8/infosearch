Nullifier-5
1347

The Nullifier-5 was a World Devastator. Nullifier-5 participated in the Battle of Calamari. During the battle, the Nullifier-5 was severely damaged and crashed into Mon Calamari's watery surface near the Algae Sward. The burnt-out hulk sank to the ocean floor, where it became an artificial reef. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Starships of the Dark Empire Category:World Devastators
