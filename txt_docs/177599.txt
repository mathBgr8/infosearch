Géraldine Fleitcher
666

Géraldine Fleitcher was a female born around 22 BBY. She was a scoop-hungry reporter for the Freedom Galactic News with something to prove; namely, her independence from her father, the Coruscant Galactic Media's mogul Graham Fleitcher.
