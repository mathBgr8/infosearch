Hnsi
567

Hnsi were a Human tribe native to Thracior. They objected to their people marrying members of other tribes such as the Tantt. If done, it usually ended in the death of the whole family. Floria and Dane were members of the Hnsi. *Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tribes
