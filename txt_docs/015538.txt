Andrasta
2994

Andrasta was a Firespray-31-class patrol and attack craft owned and operated by Emon Azzameen. Emon used this vessel in every one of his known engagements in space. He flew it during his brother Ace's training missions, the attack on Viraxo 54, and the defense of the Rebel Hospital. When the Azzameen family's home base fell to Imperial authorities, Emon used the Andrasta to defend his exiled family. He continued to fly this craft even after the family officially joined the Rebel Alliance.
