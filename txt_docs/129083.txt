Demigue Terror
1673

Demetras |stellar= |endpoints=*Gascon *Demigue |transitpoints= |otherobjects= |junctions= }} The Demigue Terror was a trade route connecting Gascon in the Mid Rim with the fairly distant world of Demigue in the Outer Rim Territories. It was a treacherous route, plagued by pirates and hijackers. Agents working to recover a ring given by Queen Marilla of Gascon to her paramour, the Prime Minister of Demigue, were forced to take this route in order to recover it in time to prevent the Holy Advisor from outing her as a philanderess at a ball held by her husband, the King of Gascon.
