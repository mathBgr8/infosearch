Bothan Diplomatic Corps
1354

The Bothan Diplomatic Corps was one of the few Bothan government organizations not to be incorporated into the Bothan Council and was, in fact, expanded. The Diplomatic Corp operated as a branch of the Bothan government and held the responsibility of furthering Bothan ideals through diplomacy. Its agents meets with the leaders of other worlds, forged treaties, and represented Bothawui in the New Republic, to ensure that Bothan ideals were met in any alliance.
