R2-0
1779

R2-0, known as Artoo-Oh or ArOh, was the original prototype of the R2-series astromech droid. It was an ancient astromech droid with a brass casing, probably built well before 32 BBY. By 36 ABY, when Industrial Automaton was purchased by Bornaryn Trading, the droid was lying forgotten in a disused storage facility. Aryn Thul tracked down the droid and presented it to Luke Skywalker and Mara Jade as a goodwill gift.
