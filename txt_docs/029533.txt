Behn-kihl-nahm
2063

Behn-Kihl-Nahm was a male senator who served in the Senate of the New Republic during the Black Fleet Crisis. In 16 ABY, Behn-Kihl-Nahm served as the Chair of the Senate as well as the chairbeing of the Defense Council. As a close political ally and personal friend of Chief of State Leia Organa Solo, who nicknamed him "Bennie".
