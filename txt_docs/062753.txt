Wish plant
946

thumb|200px|The [[Leaf Queen's wish plant.]] A wish plant was a kind of plant that would seemingly give someone any object they wished for. Excessive granting of wishes would severely affect the plant's health. However, tears cried onto the plant could bring it back to full verdure. The Leaf Queen owned a wish plant which she once left in the care of Princess Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka. Category:Plants of Endor
