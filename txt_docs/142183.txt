Havridam City
704

Havridam City was one of the largest cities on the planet New Bakstre. The Mechanical Liberation Front partially destroyed the city in an attempt to free a shipment of Vindicator XM-15 missiles. When the attack ensued, the missiles detonated, killing 14 MLF members and destroying a 25-kilometer radius of the city. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities
