Teroch Squad
1166

Teroch Squad (teroch means pitiless in Mando'a, language of the Mandalorians) was a clone commando squad that fought in the First Battle of Geonosis. The only known member was RC-8015, or "Fi", who was the only survivor in Teroch Squad following that battle. He later served in Omega Squad. The squad was trained by Kal Skirata.
