Arden (disambiguation)
844

Arden could refer to several things: *Arden, a planet in the Bakura system. *Arden, a servant to King Rilvvan K'ntarr on Rydonni Prime. *Arden, a captain of the Republic Strategic Information Service during the Cold War. *Arden House, the governor's mansion in the city of Salis D'aar on Bakura. *Arden Industries, a small starship building company. *Arden Lyn, a cyborg Teräs Käsi master. *Deredith Arden, founder and Prime Minister of Bakura around 150 BBY.
