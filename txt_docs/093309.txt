Boarding harpoon
985

A boarding harpoon was a flexiglass weapon with a superheated tip used for boarding enemy vessels. The harpoon was filled with coma gas and fired at the other ship. During flight, the weapon's armored tip was superheated and would puncture the target's hull. Once the harpoon penetrated the ship, the tip would fuse with the hull, making removal impossible; in addition the gas was released, rendering anyone who came in contact with it unconscious.
