Baobab family
2095

The Baobab family was one of the galaxy's oldest and most affluent clans. Hailing from the planet Manda, the Baobabs had become known for both the Baobab Archives and their Baobab Merchant Fleet, one of the finest trading flotillas in the galaxy. Notable members of the Baobab family included Mungo Baobab, "Old Ogger" Baobab, Ebenn Q3 Baobab, Pookie Baobab, and the father of droidspeak, Yperio Baobab. Crix Q5 Baobab was an officer candidate instructor at the Baobab Merchant Marine Academy on Manda.
