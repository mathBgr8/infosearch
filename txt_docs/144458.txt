Sabmin Devist
1786

Sabmin Devist was a clone of Soontir Fel. He was part of a sleeper cell of Fel clones planted by Grand Admiral Thrawn on the planet Pakrik Minor. He informed his "brother" Carib Devist of their reactivation, after which Carib asked to meet all of the other members of their sleeper cell, Dobrow, Tabric and Hovarb.
