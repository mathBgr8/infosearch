Song of the Clouds
1668

The Song of the Clouds was an Ithorian Manollium-class herdship. It was one of the many Ithorian Herdships which could travel between star systems. The Rodian Vop the Usurer had planned to buy a sealed lockbox from the Ithorians on the ship, but was undermined by the actions of his rival, Ritinki. *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Manollium-class herdships
