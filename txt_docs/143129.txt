|pronouns= |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Onici Wen was a female who became mistress to Bib Fortuna, the majordomo of the planet Tatooine's crime lord Jabba the Hutt, by 4 ABY. That year,The New Essential Chronology establishes the Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon and the preceding journey of the Khetanna to the pit occurred in 4 ABY. Fortuna sent his errand-runner, Doda Bodonawieedo, to Tatooine's cityStar Wars Trilogy Sourcebook, Special Edition of Mos Eisley to deliver a love-letter to Wen. A few hours after Bodonawieedo was sent to Mos Eisley, Jabba's luxury sail barge, the Khetanna, left the Hutt's Tatooine palace and was destroyed in the Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon. Due to that coincidental timing, Bodonawieedo was spared from death. Onici Wen was mentioned in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, released on April 15, 2003.
