X10-D droid brain
1340

The X10-D droid brain was a droid brain used by pilots who operated their starships without a crew. The X10-D was capable of controlling shipboard functions. The bounty hunter Bossk had one installed aboard the Hound's Tooth, allowing him to control the ship from either the bridge or his personal quarters. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *Secrets of the Sisar Run Category:Droid brains
