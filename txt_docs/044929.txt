Daywing
2163

Daywings were a species of insect found on the Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Qiilura. Notable for their extremely short lifespan, daywings hatched in the morning and died by the evening. The ruby&ndash; and sapphire&ndash;colored insects were found on the Braan River and other small standing bodies of water on Qiilura in 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, which includes the appearance of daywings, in the year 22 BBY.
