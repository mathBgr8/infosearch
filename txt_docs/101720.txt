Cobak
1361

Cobak was a Bith bounty hunter. Cobak was born on the Bith homeworld Clak'dor VII in the Colu system. Zorba the Hutt, the father of the notorious Jabba the Hutt, wanted Leia Organa to be punished for the death of his son, so he hired Cobak.
