Anzat (language)
799

The Anzat language was the language used by the Anzat species. The language was apparently an archaic one, only known by the Anzati, who also used Basic when dealing with other people in the galaxy. Its written form was so old that there were no known samples anywhere in the galaxy. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Unknown Regions Category:Anzati culture Category:Languages
