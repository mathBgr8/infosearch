Star Wars: Imperial Assault (R-Zone)
1814

Star Wars: Imperial Assault is a game created for Tiger Electronics' R-Zone handheld gaming system, released in 1997. Because of the system's many flaws, it plays like a Game and Watch version of X-Wing game series. *Take control of the Advanced TIE x 1 prototype!' *Dogfight with X-wings, Y-wings — even the Millennium Falcon!' *Crush the Rebellion and restore order to the galaxy!'
