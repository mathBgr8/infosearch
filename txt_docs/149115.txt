Nebin Cray
1249

Nebin Cray was a male Imperial Moff who governed the Trans-Nebular sector and the planet Goroth Prime during the Galactic Civil War. Cray was made Moff of the sector after his predecessor and his government co-conspirators had been executed for treason. It was Cray who developed the plans for mining Goroth Prime, after re-discovering the planet in old documents.
