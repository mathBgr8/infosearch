|continent= |location= |climate= |interest=Sewage complexStar Wars: Dark Forces |population=None |demonym= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Anoat City was a city on the planet of Anoat. It was the planet's only settlement. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, the city itself had long been vacated. Directly underneath the abandoned city was a massive abandoned sewage complex, consisting of rusting sludge-soaked durasteel girder tunnels and passageways that made up the subterranean sewer system. The difficult to navigate, vast sewers were inhabited by dianogas; Moff Rebus chose that hostile place for his hideout and laboratory. He engineered a series switches that controlled an elaborate system of drain gates to keep any intruders away.
