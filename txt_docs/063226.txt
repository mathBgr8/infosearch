Base Delta-Nine
1689

Base Delta-Nine was a secret New Republic starfighter base operational six months after the Battle of Endor. Rogue Squadron and Aggressor Wing were based at the facility for a short while. Base Delta-Nine's location is marked as "classified" in Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron: In the Empire's Service. However, the facility may be located on the planet Recopia, which was the staging area for the New Republic fleet prior to the Battle of Brentaal IV,The New Essential Chronology an engagement that Rogue Squadron, Aggressor Wing and Admiral Ackbar all participated in.
