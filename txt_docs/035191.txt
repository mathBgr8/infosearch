Shesharile
1706

Shesharile was a gas giant world that was located within the Shesharile system, in the Minos Cluster. A number of moons orbited the large planet, two of which were able to support life. Known as the Twin Planets, their societies collapsed when the drug trafficker Shlith-dan introduced a fungus that was addictive to the inhabitants of both worlds. *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters, Second Edition *Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Gas giants Category:Planets in the Outer Rim Territories Category:Shesharile system locations
