Intruder (Namana-class)
2791

The Intruder was a Namana-class light cruiser and the flagship of the Bakuran Defense Fleet. Like the rest of the Fleet, it was loaned to the New Republic in 18 ABY during the Corellian Crisis. In the subsequent Battle of Centerpoint Station, Intruder was critically damaged by two robot ramships. Its commander Admiral Hortel Ossilege, and the former Prime Minister of Bakura Gaeriel Captison, activated the ship's self destruct sequence.
