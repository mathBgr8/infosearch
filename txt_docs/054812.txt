Battle of Kuat (disambiguation)
2016

Battle of Kuat could refer to several things: *Bombing at the Kuat shipyards, a bombing in which Gideon Hask's parents were killed. *Bombing of the Kuat shipyards, a bombing of the Kuat shipyards by the Alliance to Restore the Republic following the Battle of Yavin. *Hijacking of the Executor, in which Cylo attempted to steal the Executor from the Kuat shipyards. *Battle over Kuat, in which the New Republic stole a dreadnought. *Defense of Kuat, in which the New Republic launched a full-scale assault on the Kuat shipyards. *Battle of Kuat *Mission to Kuat, circa 2 BBY–1 BBY, undertaken by General Rahm Kota. *Space Battle at Kuat, during the Fresian campaign. *The Bombing of the Kuat shipyards, during the Battle of Endor. *Battle for the Eclipse, in 4 ABY, launched by the Zann Consortium. *Battle of Kuat, during the Hunt for Zsinj. *Taking of Kuat, during the post–Zsinj campaigns. *Undercover attack on Kuat, undertaken immediately after the New Republic's capture of Kuat. *Attack on Kuat, during the Yuuzhan Vong War. *Battle of Kuat
