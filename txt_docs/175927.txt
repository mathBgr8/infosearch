Bonadan Alien Quarter
764

The Bonadan Alien Quarter was a district in Bonadan's Spaceport Southeast II that contained many businesses and facilities that accommodated the needs and desires of non-Human travelers. The Bonadan Alien Quarter's size and variety of diversions, as well as its reputation as a considerably rough area, resulted in its status as the most well-known alien quarter in the galaxy. *Han Solo's Revenge *Star Wars Missions 19: Bounty on Bonadan *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Category:Bonadan locations
