Maizie's
901

Maizie's was a tapcafe that was located on the world of Mandrine. It was in this bar that Captain Scarlet Bloodhawk met many beings, including Kelly Vermillion, Tyric, Nana, Red, Justice and Truth. While he was talking to Kelly and his gang, a thug known as Kronus entered the bar with his goons and proceeded to cause a brawl. During the fight he was killed in the explosion from a thermal detonator, dropped by one of his dead dupes, of whom all were killed. Maizie's wasn't clean for weeks afterwards.
