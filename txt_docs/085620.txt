Palace of Peace
1100

The Palace of Peace was the main headquarters of the Peace Brigade in Peace City on Ylesia, and was also the meeting place of the Ylesian Senate. The flayed skin of a large Hutt decorated the outside. It was destroyed in the battle of Ylesia. *The New Jedi Order: Ylesia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Palaces Category:Ylesia locations
