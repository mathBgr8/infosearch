Sivaa
1031

Sivaa was an Imperial BFF-1 bulk freighter during the Galactic Civil War. As the Battle on the Frontier drew to a close, Freighter Sivaa escorted EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Gaarni to replace the battle-weary Nebulon-B escort frigate Ludwick. The Gaarni's task force arrived to relieve Grand Admiral Thrawn's initial invasion force sent to establish Space Station NL-1 in the Pakuuni system.
