Ellichon
1798

Ellichon was an Airam Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttle. During the operations of Task Force Vengeance, it was unfortunate enough to cross paths with the Star Destroyer Rage and the Interdictor Cruiser Compellor near the Blair Cluster where the two Imperial vessels were watching for illegal activity.
