Tom Kenny
2175

Tom Kenny is an American actor, voice actor, singer, and stand-up comedian who provided the voice of Biggs Darklighter in Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader and Star Wars: Rogue Squadron III: Rebel Strike and Nute Gunray, Nahdar Vebb, Tan Divo, Greedo, and the Rodian Silood in Star Wars: The Clone Wars television series. He also voiced Nute Gunray in LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga.
