Tapani Imperial Hotel
968

The Tapani Imperial Hotel was located in the Commercial district of Estalle Island on Procopia. It was a very luxurious hotel but was not as exclusive as the Grand Royal Hotel, also located on Estalle Island. Getting a room at the hotel was usually a matter of being able to afford the outrageous prices, although the rooms were normally fully booked during Capital Season. The Tapani Imperial Hotel typically catered for high-ranking Imperials and megacorp executives. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Hotels Category:Procopia locations
