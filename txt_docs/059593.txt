Romany
1411

Romany was the leader of one of two clans of Ryn refugees stranded on the planet Duro during the Yuuzhan Vong War. He was a former biologist and had a bushy mane. "Romany" is another name for the Romani, after whom author James Luceno modeled Romany's species, the Ryn. *The New Jedi Order: Balance Point *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Biologists Category:Males Category:Ryn
