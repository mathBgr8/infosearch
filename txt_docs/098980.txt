LD-1 target blaster rifle
3947

The Merr-Sonn LD-1 target blaster rifle (also called a targeting blaster rifle) was technically a sporting blaster rifle, but in actuality it was nearly as powerful as a military-grade blaster rifle like the E-11. This was intentional - the goal of the project was to create a weapon that approaches the damage of a blaster rifle without the pesky legal restrictions that come with such weapons. The LD-1, with its long barrel, was quite useful as a sniper rifle, and could be disassembled into a carrying case like most such weapons.
