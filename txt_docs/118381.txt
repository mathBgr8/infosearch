Yawari Cliffs
2460

|affiliation= }} The Yawari Cliffs were a geographical feature on the Forest Moon of Endor. The cliffs resembled a plateau, separating the lower more lush and forested Jiberah Plains and Mertuen Forest to the south, and the higher more barren Desert of Salma to the north.
