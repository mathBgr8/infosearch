Juvaini
2196

The Juvaini was an Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate. Whilst being aided by the Metternich, the Imperial frigate attacked the Maria which was receiving B-wing starfighter prototypes. The Juvaini was near Sunaj IV en route to a scheduled meeting with the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Relentless when the Nebulon-B was intercepted by starfighters of the Rebel Alliance.
