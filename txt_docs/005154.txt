Battle of Alaris Prime (disambiguation)
793

There have been multiple battles that took place on Alaris Prime throughout galactic history: *Skirmish on Alaris Prime, a short lived conflict during the New Sith Wars. *Alaris Prime colonial war, between Wookiees and the Trade Federation *Battle of Alaris Prime (Hunt for the Decimator), a battle in the Clone Wars against a Separatist effort to re-obtain stolen activation codes *Battle on Alaris, an effort in the Clone Wars to free multiple loyalist captives.
