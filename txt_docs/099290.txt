Ulig Abaha Ltd.

Ulig Abaha Limited or Ulig Abaha Ltd. was a company that specialized in the production of small starships. The company was headquartered in the Corporate Sector, and its products included the #2 Warpod and the Dimel-class assault ship. *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Pirates & Privateers Category:Shipwrights
