Freyborn
1168

Freyborn was a Human Moff of the Imperial Remnant during the time of the Yuuzhan Vong War. He refused to believe that the Galactic Alliance was more capable of handling the Far Outsiders and their biots, even after the Battle of Borosk. He viewed them as the same "scum" that made up the Rebel Alliance. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Moff Council members Category:Moffs of the Imperial Remnant
