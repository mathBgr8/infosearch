Neva (Alderaanian)
1452

Neva was a female Human Alderaanian who lived during the Cold War and worked as an assistant to Peema Ahuff, the curator of the House Alde Royal Museum. In 3643 BBY she was bribed by the gangster Skavak as part of his scheme and was convinced to fake the genetic test results, identifying the head Skavak brought to Ahuff as belonging to Darth Bandon.
