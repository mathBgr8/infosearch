|model= |class=Astromech droid |degree=Class twoStar Wars: The Visual Encyclopedia |cost=5,000 creditsStar Wars: R2-D2 Deluxe Book and Model Set |length= |width= |height=1.09 meters |mass= |gender= |sensor= |plating= |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=*Royal House of Naboo *Galactic Republic *Jedi Order *Galactic Empire *Alliance to Restore the Republic *New Republic *Plazir-15 government *Resistance |firstmade=During or prior to 132 BBY |retired= }} R2-series astromech droids, also known as R2 units, were a very popular series of astromech droids produced by Industrial Automaton. They were the most popular line of all the R-series.Ultimate Star Wars, New Edition They stood at around 1 meter in height, sported a distinctive domed head, and were programmed to perform a variety of tasks. The R2 series was a major success for Industrial Automaton, and many later models of astromech were based on the R2 series, though none had the same success. R2-D2 was a notable example of this model. Although the R2 series was used over a century prior to the Clone Wars, they were still considered to be fairly new during the Age of the Empire.
