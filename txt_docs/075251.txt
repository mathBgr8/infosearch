Wlesc
1104

Wlesc was a neighborhood in the city of Pesktda on Garqi, located just east of the Pesktda Xenobotanical Garden. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, the Yuuzhan Vong used it as a Thrall training area. Jacen Solo used holovision goggles to gain intel on the training area during the Battle of Garqi. Wlesc first appeared in the 2000 novel The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin, written by Michael A.
