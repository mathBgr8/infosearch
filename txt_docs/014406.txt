Engebo V

|imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Engebo V was a planet in the Outer Rim Territories with constant winds. Engebo V was a planet of nerf ranches that was colonized by Outer Rim Oreworks Company, who mined most of Engebo's mineral resources and later abandoned the world.
