Predecessor of the Acclamator II-class Assault Ship, the assault ship was armed with several missile launchers. Alongside the Acclamator II, the Acclamator I-class served as the primary deployment vessel for the Republic's clone troopers throughout the war's duration. Following the war's beginning on the planetRise of the Separatists Geonosis, the Republic ordered an additional thousand Acclamator I-class vessels. Later, at some point in the war, two Acclamator I-class Assault Ships traveled through space, where one of them fired several missiles against an opponent. The Acclamator I-class Assault Ship was introduced in Galactic Republic Fleet Starter, a 2020 supplement to the Star Wars: Armada miniatures game published by Fantasy Flight Games.
