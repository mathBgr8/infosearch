|origin=Endor |found= |habitat=Forests |lifespan= |description=*Indigo-blue *Bioluminescent |edible= |inedible= |nutrition= |effects= |affiliation= }} }} Aura blossoms were an exquisite species of indigo-blue, bioluminescent flower that grew in abundance on the Forest Moon of Endor. They were a favorite of the native Ewok population. In 3 ABY, dates the events of the television series Ewoks&mdash;which include the mention of aura blossoms in the episode "The Cries of the Trees"&mdash;to three years after the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which corresponds to 3 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. following months of drought on Endor, the young Ewoks Wicket Wystri Warrick, Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka, Latara, and Teebo were tasked by Bozzie to find aura blossoms for decorating the Festival of Hoods. After tumbling down a hill, Warrick found a group of Wisties&mdash;diminutive, bioluminescent beings&mdash;which he mistook for aura blossoms. Teebo pointed out Warrick's error, and the Wisties took flight. Aura blossoms were first mentioned in "The Cries of the Trees," the pilot episode of the animated television series Ewoks.
