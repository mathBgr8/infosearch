Pandemic Studios
1895

Pandemic Studios was a video game developing company based in Los Angeles, California and Brisbane, Australia. It was founded in 1998 by Josh Resnick and Andrew Goldman. Pandemic was responsible for developing several Star Wars video games, including Star Wars: The Clone Wars, Star Wars: Battlefront, and Star Wars: Battlefront II. On November 3, 2005, Pandemic Studios announced a partnership with BioWare, the developers of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. Though they had common ownership and pooled their expertise to develop games, both companies retained their individual staffs and brands. In 2006, Pandemic employed over 250 programmers, artists, and designers.
