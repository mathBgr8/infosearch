UrrOr' Shurk
1521

UrrOr' Shurk was a male Tusken Raider who was active during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Gardulla the Hutt for leading raids on her compound. If captured alive, he was to be fed to the Hutt's pet Krayt dragon. He was caught by Jango Fett in Tusken Canyon; although it was unspecified whether or not the bounty was for a live capture or a death, he was worth more dead (for a 5,000 credit payoff) then he was alive (for only 1,000 credits). *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Criminals Category:Males Category:Tusken Raiders
