It was one of four gas giants in that system, along with Zauhu, Faargau, and Laazrau. Urazuun was first mentioned briefly in "Three Simple Laws," the first part of the Planet Hoppers article series Aargau: For All Your Banking Needs, which was written by Cory J. Herndon and published on the Wizards.com website in 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Zug system, thereby including Urazuun, in grid square L-10.
