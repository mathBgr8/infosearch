Gaitzi
951

Gaitzi was a female Human who lived on Bakura around the time of the Yuuzhan Vong War. She was employed as part of a cleaning crew, known as the Tripod, reporting to Jakaitis. In 28 ABY, Tahiri Veila impersonated her when sneaking into the Bakuran Senate Complex. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bakurans Category:Females
