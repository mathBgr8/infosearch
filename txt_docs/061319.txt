Malindin Gevarak
1425

Malindin Gevarak was a small-time counterfeiter from Ord Mantell who was wanted by the Galactic Empire for forging Imperial credits for use by Rebel Alliance personnel. Gevarak traveled with the Wookiee, Cherioer. Last seen in the Baronos sector, Gevarak's bounty eventually reached 10,000 credits for live capture.
