Pandur
1556

Lieutenant Pandur was the Imperial Liaison to Korfo II. He was also one of the Imperial officials who directed Obo Rin in the production of the Catalog of Intelligent Life in the Galaxy. Imperial Intelligence Major Herrit was instructed by Darth Vader's aide Captain Solistein to handle his involvement in the project, as well as Rin's payment, through Pandur. One of Pandur's duties was transferring funds to Rin's account at the Imperial Galactic Bank of Korfo II to reimburse the sentientologist's considerable expenses.
