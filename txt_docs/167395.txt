Amber (thumper)
931

Amber was the name of a thumper caught by Biggs Darklighter on Yavin 4. He named it after his bodyguard on Aquilae. Amber was killed in the Lars treehouse after a group of stormtroopers discovered the structure and investigated. When Biggs and Threepio hid, Amber attempted to defend them and one of the troopers killed him with his lazersword.
