|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=120 |isbn=9781569714416 |isbn2= |timeline=4 ABY |series=Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire: Evolution |issues= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire: Evolution collects the five-issue Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire: Evolution series of comics, which served as a sequel to Shadows of the Empire. It was released on February 2, 2000. Xizor, the leader of the Black Sun criminal organization, has been destroyed, and Guri, a beautiful Human replica droid and Xizor's personal assassin, is left wondering what her future will hold now that she no longer has anyone to answer to. Throughout the ongoing struggle for underworld supremacy, Guri endeavors to uncover hints of her Human side &ndash; if it even exists. But in her quest to alter her programming and gain Humanity, Guri becomes the quarry of both bounty hunters and Rebels (led by a Jedi-to-be named Luke). What secrets lie buried in her android mind that make her such valuable prey?
