Belnar Sector Force
657

The Belnar Sector Force was a military organization in the Belnar system. They were direct enemies to an opposing military group in the system, the Peltsic League. At some point before 2 ABY, the Belnar Sector Force was led by Khil master tactician, Tarlo Ganar. Thanks to Ganar, the Sector Force eventually defeated the League.Alliance Intelligence Reports *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Military units
