Hidden Leader

Hidden Leader was the title given to the head of the Human League, based on Corellia. Thrackan Sal-Solo acted as the Hidden Leader during the First Corellian Insurrection. *Ambush at Corellia *Cracken's Threat Dossier *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Titles
