Bead of Bravery
1029

thumb|right|250px|[[Shodu Warrick presents Beads of Bravery to the Ewok children.]] }} Beads of Bravery were ceremonial beads presented to Ewoks of Bright Tree Village as an award for bravery in dangerous conflicts. The Ewok children, Wicket Wystri Warrick, Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka, Teebo, and Latara were all awarded Beads of Bravery for their service in the Stranger War. Wicket thereafter wore the bead as a trophy on his new Belt of Honor to replace the one which had been lost during the defeat of the Stranger. Category:Sacred Ewok items
