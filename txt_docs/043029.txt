Mansom Corporation
1008

|locations= |role=Construction contracting |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Mansom Corporation was a company based on Corulag. It was a construction contracting firm that specialized in the design and construction of Imperial-class starports. Mansom spent over a century building a reputation as a provider of exclusive and unique designs, and usually committed to no more than six projects a decade. A Mansom contract was highly sought-after, and most applicants for Mansom designs were turned down. Category:Construction companies
