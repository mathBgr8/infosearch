Stranglethorn
1335

Stranglethorn was a species of vine found on the forest moon of Endor. When it first sprouted, stranglethorn was of little note. However, over time, the stranglethorn grew into a large, carnivorous plant with a bulbous head and sharp teeth. Unsuspecting Ewoks sometimes grew the plant only to learn of its carnivorous tendencies the hard way.
