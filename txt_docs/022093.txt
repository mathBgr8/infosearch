Corellian Bloodstripe
10984

thumb|right|120px|[[Han Solo/Legends|Han Solo's pants, displaying his second-class Bloodstripes.]] The Corellian Bloodstripe was a Corellian military award for conspicuous gallantry. }} It was awarded most typically for courage demonstrated after deliberation; it was said that Corellians believed that anyone can be brave in the heat of the moment, but true heroism came when one knew that doing the right thing would hurt but did it anyway. Though they could be awarded for many reasons, the majority of the prestigious awards were awarded for courage under fire. Usually, the Corellian bloodstripes were awarded by the Corellian governmentThe Hutt Gambit to members of the Corellian Armed Forces.X-Wing: The Bacta War However, the award could also be given to officers of the Corellian Security Force or to non-Corellian soldiers who fought for Corellia. In everyday dress, the stripe marked by colored piping worn down the side of the trousers. There were two classes of Bloodstripes. The second class was represented by yellow piping; the first class was represented by red piping, and were almost always awarded posthumously.Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Though not formally recognized by the Empire, the Imperial Navy allowed its servicemen recipients to don the award with their uniforms. According to some sources a Corellian was entitled to kill without repercussions any other being caught wearing unearned bloodstripes. thumb|right|220px|Han Solo wearing his first-class Bloodstripes. During the Galactic War, first class stripes were awarded to the Galactic Republic heroes who defeated Darth Decimus and recaptured the Legislature Building from the Sith Empire during the Battle of Corellia. Han Solo won both classes of bloodstripes. His second class stripes were rumored to have been earned for the events surrounding his rescue of Chewbacca; his first class stripes were earned during events that have never been disclosed. When Solo was cashiered from Imperial service, all the awards and decorations he had earned were literally and systematically ripped off him. He expected to lose his bloodstripes as well, but was allowed to keep them, apparently because they were awarded by the Corellian military and not the Empire. However, Han Solo wore more often the yellow second class bloodstripes during his career as a smuggler since the first class stripes would draw too much attention to him. Early in the Bacta War, Tycho Celchu suggested that Wedge Antilles paint his X Wing starfighter blue with red stripes in imitation of bloodstripes. Wedge was reluctant to do so as he had never served in the Corellian military and chose a more personal color scheme.
