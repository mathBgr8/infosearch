In 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. she was a patron of the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of Coruscant, during the night of the attempted assassination of Senator Padmé Amidala. After the assassin, bounty hunter Zam Wesell, ran into the club, Sing talked with a Human next to a turbolift when the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered searching for Wesell. The woman later walked past Skywalker when he surveyed the crowd. Sing had dark skin, brown eyes, and dark hair worn up in a conical hairstyle. During her visit to the Outlander, she wore a dark outfit and a beaded silver veil. Necil Sing first appeared in the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which released on May 16, 2002.Star Wars Year by Year: A Visual Chronicle The Outlander Club scenes including Sing, who was portrayed by uncredited extra, were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney on July 17, 2000.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones The extra playing the character, in costume, introduced the ninth episode of the Episode II behind-the-scenes video series on location. The episode, "In The Money," released on September 9, 2000. Sing was first identified in in an Episode II Depth Commentary available on StarWars.com that could be accessed through the DVD-ROM home release of Attack of the Clones on November 12, 2002.
