The New Essential Chronology |date=Between 13 ABY and 17 ABYThe New Essential Guide to Characters establishes that the Battle of Gravlex Med occurred between the destruction of the Super Star Destroyer Reaper and thirteen years after the Battle of Endor. The New Essential Chronology dates the loss of the Reaper to 13 ABY, and the Battle of Endor to 4 ABY, thirteen years before 17 ABY. |place=Gravlex Med |result=*Imperial victory **Chimaera captured |side1=Imperial Remnant |side2=New Republic |side3= |side4= |commanders1=Fleet Admiral Gilad Pellaeon |commanders2=Admiral Gial Ackbar |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |forces1=*Slicers *Imperial commandos |forces2=*New Republic pickets |forces3= |forces4= |casual1= |casual2= |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} The Battle of Gravlex Med was a battle during the late Galactic Civil War. It began when slicers hired by the Imperial Remnant forged a disassembly order for the Imperial Star Destroyer Chimaera, which had been captured by the New Republic during Operation Shadow Hand. This order sent the vessel to the shipyards of Gravlex Med, a wasted planet that was formerly the homeworld of the Anx. Upon the arrival of the Chimaera, its skeleton crew was overcome by Imperial commandos, who blasted their way through New Republic pickets. Admiral Ackbar himself led the forces which attempted to recapture Chimaera from Fleet Admiral Gilad Pellaeon, but Pellaeon was nonetheless able to regain his flagship.
