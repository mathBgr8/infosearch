Phedroi
1562

Phedroi was a male individual who worked in partnership with Vol Hamame to seek profit. A sentient with a mucus-lined nasopharynx and dirty ringlet beard, he was responsible for the death of Codeq Santhananan and wanted part of the reward that could be gained from taking in the corpse of Boba Fett after intercepting the message via a q'nithian mimbrane that the bounty hunter was still alive. He and Hamame followed Dengar's swoop bike to Fett's location and believed they had the Mandalorian holed up before being killed under the weight and heat of Slave I. *The Mandalorian Armor *Hard Merchandise *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males
