Vycinyth
3008

Vycinyth was a forested resort planet in the Barnaba Province of the Tapani sector. Its Tapani Arts and Sports Complex accommodated swoop races, shockboxing, and Cracian thumper races. The planet was orbited by four space casinos and the moon Vilhon. The Rebel Alliance cell, the Pelagia Freedom Force, maintained a number of spies on Vycynith.
