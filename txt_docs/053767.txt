Abesmi
1445

Abesmi was an "island-monolith" located in the Jenuwaa Sea on Kalee. It was believed to be the point from which the native gods ascended to heaven. The native Kaleesh species took it to be a sacred place, and it was an important altar of worship.The New Essential Guide to Alien Species After the death of Ronderu lij Kummar, Qymaen jai Sheelal traveled across the ocean to Abesmi to beg the gods to raise her from the sea that had claimed her body so that he might see her one last time. His prayers were to no avail. *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Atlas Category:Kalee locations
