Tassa
1729

Tassa was a female Codru-Ji and the daughter of a clan leader. Her clan kidnapped Rikkar-Du, a member of a rival clan, and they eventually married and had a son, Jassar. In 137 ABY, when Darth Kruhl, a Sith Lord of the One Sith tried to secure her husband's support for the Sith Empire, he refused and was assassinated, while Tassa was killed by Kassek-Ka, a rival clan leader loyal to the Empire. *Legacy (2006) 13 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Codru-Ji Category:Females Category:Individuals with she/her pronouns
