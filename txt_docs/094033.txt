|class=Repair droid |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender=Masculine programmingStar Tours |pronouns=new |sensor=Green |plating=Silver, with gold trim |armament= |equipment= |affiliation=Star Tours }} F-22-RGKx, also known simply as F-22, was a masculine F series repair droid with green photoreceptors and silver plating, with a gold trim. By the year 4.3 ABY, he was employed by the Star Tours travel agency and performed maintenance on the fans in Tomorrowland Starport's hangar. F-22 possessed a cantankerous attitude, finding various things to complain about, from the factory he used to work at, to how "kid" droids were like currently, even down to his "mother-in-law."Complete Guide to Star Tours When he had finished his job for the day, F-22 often went to the Alien Quarter Cantina. He also enjoyed listening to The Rolling Droids and drinking oil on the rocks. F-22-RGKx was created for Tokyo Disneyland's simulator ride theme park attraction Star Tours, which opened on July 12, 1989. *Star Tours *Complete Guide to Star Tours *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droids with masculine programming Category:F series repair droids Category:Star Tours personnel
