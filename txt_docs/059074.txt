Hightower Conglomerate
830

Hightower Conglomerate was a civil construction firm on the planet Coruscant. In reality, it was a front for the Black Sun criminal syndicate. Underbidding the competition when vying for a contract, a series of cost overruns would then flow into their account. *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Black Sun companies Category:Construction companies
