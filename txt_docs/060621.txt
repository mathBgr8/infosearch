|pages= |upc= |timeline=c. 0.5&ndash;2 ABYThe "Time Line" of The Essential Chronology places Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 1&ndash;9 between the Evacuation of Yavin in 0.5 ABY and the Duel on Mimban in 2 ABY. |series=Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures |issue=9 |next=last }} Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures 9 was the ninth issue of the Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures series. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Russ Manning. Boba Fett makes his first appearance ever in this issue of Classic Star Wars: The Early Adventures!
