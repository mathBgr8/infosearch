*Scott Chernoff |cover artist= |contributors= |publisher=*Wizards of the Coast *Fantastic Media, Inc. |release date=January 2001 |subscribers= |pages=82 |isbn= |issn=1041-5122 |genre= |issue=51 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 51 is the fifty-first issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was the first issue published under the new direction of Wizards of the Coast and the last produced by former Star Wars Fan Club president Dan Madsen, who oversaw the creation of Star Wars Insider in 1987 under the title The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine. It's also the last issue for longtime Editor-in-Chief Jon Bradley Snyder. Managing Editor Scott Chernoff, who had been running the magazine, stayed on for a while but stepped back to Contributing Editor status after this issue.
