Kroohhuhwoor
1343

|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Kroohhuhwoor was a small agricultural community on the planet Kashyyyk located in the galaxy's Mid Rim. The goods produced in Kroohhuhwoor were primarily sold on Kashyyyk rather than being exported. Kroohhuhwoor was mentioned in "Secrets of Kashyyyk," an article written by J.D. Wiker and Craig Robert Carey for the fourth issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine published by Wizards of the Coast on June 19, 2001. Category:Cities on Kashyyyk
