Durkish Corporation
825

Durkish Corporation was a leading manufacturer of aural amplifiers during the Jedi Civil War. Originally a maker of visual detection devices, it switched products in the belief that the respiratory sounds made by most creatures would be more perceptible and less easily disguised. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Personal gear companies
