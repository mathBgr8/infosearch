Tower of Law
1977

The Tower of Law was a governmental building on the planet Bimmisaari. Established at a crossroads circa 200 BBY, the Tower of Law was surrounded by a massive marketplace and frequented by both races of Bimm. A modest building constructed of white stone with horizontal black markings, the Tower was the meeting place of the Law Elders of Bimmisaari, the planet's main governing body. Within the tower the walls were covered by large tapestries, and the top level was home to a small museum which held relics from the middle era of the Old Republic.
