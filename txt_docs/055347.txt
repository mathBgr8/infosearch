Crash n' Burn
919

thumb|200px|Crash n' Burn Crash n' Burn was a form of recreational spice that was created during the height of the New Order. It was popular among smugglers. Crash n' Burn first appeared in Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, as a lootable item found on the Corellian Corvette missions, and also as a craftable spice. Ever since the New Game Enchancements of 2005, the ability to craft this spice had been removed. *The Essential Atlas Category:Spice variants
