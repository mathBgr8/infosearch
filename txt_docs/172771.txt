Meen-tris
701

Meen-tris was an Eickarie family, that made up Sav-ro clan and tribe Hu-shi-crive on the planter Kariek during the New Republic era. For centuries the family guarded the secret of the third watch tower entrance to the Cro-sla-trie fortress.
