Spleen
1130

A spleen was an organ that several organisms possessed, including Humans and dianogas. Spleens could rupture or suffer from spleen-rot. Dianogan tea was made from the spleens of dianogas. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, the Jedi Anakin Solo was critically injured when his spleen was ruptured.
