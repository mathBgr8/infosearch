Kaldani Spires Residential Apartments
1927

The Kaldani Spires Residential Apartments was an exclusive apartment building towering over Coruscant's Monument Plaza. It was owned by the Mygeetan Muun financier and Sith Lord Hego Damask, who maintained a private residence in the building's summit. The building was constructed in the style of Hasennan Period architecture. In the twilight of the Old Republic, the building, while not as tall or grand as 500 Republica, was Galactic City's most desired address outside the Senate District. At the time of his death in 32 BBY, Hath Monchar owned a suite on its 500th floor.
