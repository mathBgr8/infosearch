Sclipune
1078

A sclipune was a beetle-like creature used by the extragalactic Yuuzhan Vong to store sensitive possessions. It had a shell that split in two, and would only open when an individual tapped in the correct sequence to a fringe of sensory tissue on the sclipune's side.
