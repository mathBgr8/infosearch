Jin'ri trade syndicate
1036

The Jin'ri trade syndicate was a group of war profiteers formed after the fall of Duro to meet the demands of the refugees. They sent Opeli Mors as their representative to a Yuuzhan Vong resistance meeting at Shelter station, offering their services in supplying troops and ships for a price. As an act of good faith and investment, a ship was loaned to the resistance with no charge. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory II: Rebirth *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminal organizations
