Van Ling
1771

}} Van Ling is the artist who created the menus for the DVDs of George Lucas' six Star Wars saga films and the DVDs for the Star Wars: Clone Wars animated series. He also gathered behind-the-scenes imagery to produce the DVD included with the Limited Edition of Sculpting a Galaxy: Inside the Star Wars Model Shop. Ling additionally created screen graphics and other material for the preshow queue of "Star Tours: The Adventures Continue" for the Disneyland theme parks around the world. Category:Animators
