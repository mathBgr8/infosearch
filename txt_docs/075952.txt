Disremembering
538

Disremembering was the ultimate insult in Bosph society. Disremembering consisted in ignoring people who had done harmful deeds toward one and not remembering the deed itself either. Many Bosph would disremember the destruction of Bosph by the Empire and go about their businesses without any regard to the Empire at all. Category:Bosph culture
