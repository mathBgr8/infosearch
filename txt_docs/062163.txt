Verekil

Verekil was a company that specialized in the creation of servo-lifters such as the Servo-Lifter V-2z. Their devices were mostly employed to move large crates and other objects in starports. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Platt's Smugglers Guide Category:Technology companies
