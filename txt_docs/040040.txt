Falling Leaf
1298

The Falling Leaf, or Falling Avalanche, was a lightsaber combat technique that involved spinning on one's feet to "slash from the sky".Dark Forces: Jedi Knight Kyle Katarn learned this technique by tapping on the spirit of Tal on Ruusan in 5 ABY. Kyle used it to kill Jerec after the latter tried to kill him, despite Kyle having offered him a chance to live. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Dark Forces II *Dark Forces: Jedi Knight *The Jedi Path: A Manual for Students of the Force Category:Lightsaber combat
