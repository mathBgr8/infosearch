Boaboo fungus
519

Boaboo fungus was grown on Sullust. The fungus was grown in caverns, and had many different breeds. Sullustans such as Bungo Bung's father cultivated farms of the fungus. The fungus was then mashed into Boaboo juice and sold to the general populace. Boaboo fungus was very moist, and was tended mainly by droids. Category:Edible fungi Category:Sullustan food
