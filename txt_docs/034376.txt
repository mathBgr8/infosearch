Junction City
1274

thumb Junction City was the capital city of Feriae Junction. Run down, but still busy with trade, Junction City contained reminders of a better time. The vast Grand Terminal was one such reminder. After the destruction of the Death Star, Junction City was crawling with spies from the Empire, the Alliance, the Black Sun and the Hutts.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars (1977) 27 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital cities Category:Feriae Junction locations
