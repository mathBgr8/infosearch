Imperial Species Preservation Zone
1088

An Imperial Species Preservation Zone was an area designated by the Galactic Empire for the preservation of a certain species. Planets falling under the authorization of this edict were considered restricted to civilian travel. The planet Dar'Or was declared an Imperial Species Preservation Zone shortly after it was conquered by the Empire prior to the Battle of Yavin.Star Wars: Rebellion Creatures under protection of this act included the winged Ri'Dar, the predatorial indola, and the avian elix. *Star Wars: Rebellion *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition Category:Galactic Empire locations
