|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=19 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: Union |issue=1 }} Union 1 1 is the first issue in the Star Wars: Union series of comics. It was released by Dark Horse Comics on November 10, 1999. Though the New Republic is thriving, there are still many who remain true to the spirit and evil designs of Emperor Palpatine. So when Luke Skywalker, the New Republic's greatest hero and sole Jedi master, decides to marry Mara Jade, the woman who was formerly Emperor Palpatine's personal assassin and executor of the Empire's will, you can be sure that somebody is going to raise their hands in protest!
