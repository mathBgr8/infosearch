Lyster Innovations
1197

Lyster Innovations was a firm based on the world of Almania which employed quality specialists and "idea generators" in order to help other companies do their jobs more effectively. Their offices began on the floor directly above those of Tendrando Arms' Almanian office; Ben Skywalker used its proximity to his advantage, sneaking into the place on his quest to find the Amulet of Kalara. The firm produced a patented chameleon cover material which could be manifested into a garden-like state, a "pure" white and silver and undecorated state, and probably others. Their goal was to help companies create and maintain their own cultural identities through environmental design. *Legacy of the Force: Exile *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Technology companies
