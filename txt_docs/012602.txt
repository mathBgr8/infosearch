|type=Softcover |pages=96 |dimensions=11 x 8.6 x 0.3 inches |weight=7.8 ounces |isbn=0874312906 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Secrets of the Sisar Run is a supplement to Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games. It was written by Craig Robert Carey, Shane Hensley, and Pablo Hidalgo, and was first published July 14, 1997. The book is a campaign setting based on elements introduced in Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire.
