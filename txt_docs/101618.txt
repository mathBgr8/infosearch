GRS-600
1089

The GRS-600, known commonly as a replar splint, was a field splint manufactured by Xarnin Medical Technologies. It created a miniature suspension field that immobilized the limb and relieved pressure on a damaged bone. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Far Orbit Project Category:Splints and casts Category:Xarnin Medical Technologies products
