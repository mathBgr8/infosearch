Lurze Kesh
1580

Lurze Kesh was a male Rodian who met Revan in Dreshdae on Korriban, in 3956 BBY. He had been expecting a cargo of spice from Davik Kang, and hoped Revan had it on the Ebon Hawk, Davik's former ship. He also gave Revan a Rakatan prison to deliver to Motta the Hutt on Tatooine. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Males Category:Rodians
