|published in=Star Wars Tales 15 |reprinted in= |pages=14 |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Lucky Stars" is a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 15 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4. Leia Organa arrives on the planet Elerion, helping General Orma Hundeen recover a holocube that was stolen on Farwell Station. It contains the locations of the main Rebel bases, and the thief, Nescan Tal'yo, plans to sell it to the Galactic Empire. Leia is introduced to the Quetzal Sisters, a group of attractive women who will help distract the Imperial party for Leia to accomplish her mission. After being brought up to Tal'Yo's room, Leia ties him up and threatens to torture him if he doesn't reveal the cube's location. Finally finding it, she destroys it, as the Sisters deal with the Imperial party. When their superior arrives, embarrassed to admit being beaten up by three women, the officers claim it was done by a dozen strong men.
