The Jedi Dreamer was a custom designed Scout vessel. It was built out of sturdy alloys to protect it, and had an aerodynamic design for smooth flight in atmosphere. It was equipped with a powerful sensor package. The ship was used on a scouting mission to the Serianan system. Korren Starchaser commanded the ship, and Mowa Gundeeb was the second in command.
