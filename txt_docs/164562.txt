Hyper adrenaline amplifier
656

A Hyper adrenaline amplifier was an advanced model of the adrenaline amplifier belt that monitored adrenaline in the wearer's bloodstream. When the levels elevated in combat, a large additional dose of adrenaline was released. This greatly enhanced reaction time. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Belts
