Samarian
1277

Samarians were a sentient species native to the desert world of Samaria. The Samarians were engaged in the process of setting up a trade agreement between their neighbors the Roshans, however the Galactic Empire intervened. Samarians were most likely Humans; however, they were never positively identified as such in A Tangled Web, Return of the Dark Side and Reckoning. *The Last of the Jedi: A Tangled Web *The Last of the Jedi: Return of the Dark Side *The Last of the Jedi: Reckoning *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Core Worlds sentient species
