Dorak Quinn
1793

Dorak Quinn was a male Human hunter whom Revan met in Anchorhead during the Jedi Civil War. In 3956 BBY, he was one of the newer hunters on Tatooine. Dorak was, at first, rude to Revan when he met him in Fazza's Hunting Lodge.
