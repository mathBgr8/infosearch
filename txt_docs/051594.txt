YT-1150 cargo transport
2042

The YT-1150 cargo transport was a YT-series starship produced by the Corellian Engineering Corporation during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. It resembled an oval split lengthwise into three sections, the middle being the longest. Its hyperdrive was held on a nacelle. Charza Kwinn, a Priapulin pilot who worked for the Jedi Order, had a YT-1150 named Star Sea Flower, which transported Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi to Zonama Sekot in 29 BBY. *Rogue Planet *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:YT-series freighter classes
