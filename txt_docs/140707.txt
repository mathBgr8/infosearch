Dorzo
1145

Dorzo was a male Rodian who served as Jiliac Desilijic Tiron's majordomo. It was he who greeted Vykk Draygo (the alias of Han Solo while employed on Ylesia) and Jalus Nebl when they arrived at Jiliac's palace on Nal Hutta, carrying a message and gift from Zavval.
