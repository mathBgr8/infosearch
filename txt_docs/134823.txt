Krusk
1721

A violent herbivorous species known as krusk was native to the Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Bothawui. The species name was used by the Bothans, a sentient species native to the planet,Ultimate Alien Anthology as a first name given to some members of the male sex. The species known as krusk was first mentioned in the Galactic Campaign Guide, a 2003 sourcebook for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game, written by Peter Schweighofer and J.D. Wiker.
