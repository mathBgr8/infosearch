Ohran Keldor
2303

Doctor Ohran Keldor was an Imperial engineer at the Maw Installation, born in 50 BBY. Keldor was one of the instructors at Wilhuff Tarkin's orbital training academy above Omwat, and he later employed a hundred Mrlssi. Nasdra Magrody instructed him in AI theory. He was present when the first Death Star destroyed Alderaan.
