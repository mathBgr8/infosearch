Karakan gauntlets
1290

thumb Karakan Gauntlets, created by the isolationist Karakan, were almost a complete medical computer in themselves. They constantly monitored and adjusted the nervous impulses, blood pressure, and tension through the wearer's hands. The resulting increase in stability and overall system integrity had many benefits to the wearer.
