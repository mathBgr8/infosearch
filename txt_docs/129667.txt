Jennifer Seiden Schoonover
1531

Jennifer Seiden Sadler (born August 12, 1973) worked at West End Games from 1996 to 1998. She was married and legally changed her last name to Schoonover in 2003, then divorced and remarried again in 2013. In June 2005, Sadler created the independent publishing company FireWater Productions.
