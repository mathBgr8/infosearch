Star Cruiser (Galactic Empire)
2238

The Star Cruiser was a starship employed by the Galactic Empire. It was considered an air cruiser, and was smaller than an Imperial-class Star Destroyer. It had TIE-like wings on each side of the superstructure. The Star Cruiser fired one large laser blast rather than having many smaller cannons. The air cruiser had a similar role to the Lancet Aerial Artillery, as they both played the same role in an atmosphere.
