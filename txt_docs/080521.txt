Military suit
1477

thumb|150px|[[Carth Onasi wearing a military suit]] The military suit was a standard-issue type of medium armor that saw widespread use in the days of the Mandalorian Wars, Jedi Civil War and Dark Wars. While the suit was heavier and more restrictive than some of its counterparts, it was sturdy and afforded solid protection. For this reason, it was a popular choice of armor for many mercenaries, who found the trade-off tolerable.
