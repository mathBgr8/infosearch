Bendak
1477

Bendak was a Human male criminal, who was tracked by the bounty hunter Boba Fett on the planet of Sathiemon. Bendak tried to hide in a deep hole in the ground, but Fett located him. When a group of hired mercenaries cornered Fett and tried to kill him, Fett killed Bendak, switched clothes with him, and launched him out of the hole using the jetpack.
