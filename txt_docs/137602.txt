Barani List
629

The Barani List was created by Neil Barani. The list contained information on the conspiracy pertaining to the Yen-2 mining outpost. Vigo Green provided the false information to Neil Barani and hoped that Barani would sell it to the Empire and bring down Sprax's operation along the Sisar Run. However, Green and Barani failed. The list was recovered by Guri, who later killed Green. *Secrets of the Sisar Run Category:Documents
