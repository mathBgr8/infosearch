|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 4 |reprinted in= |pages=20 |timeline=c. 19 BBYStar Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 4 dates its stories to just before and during the events of , which The Essential Atlas dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Brink" is a comic story from Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 4. While waiting for orders in the Outer Rim Territories, Anakin Skywalker locks on to a weak distress signal. Leaving without his master, Obi-Wan Kenobi's, permission, Anakin arrives at a seemingly abandoned space station, known as the Brink of the Celestial Wake. While investigating inside, Anakin finds that all of the clone troopers that were on the station are dead. Eventually, Anakin meets another Jedi Knight, Serra Keto. She tells him that they need to leave, but Anakin insists that they press on. They are attacked by pink blobs with metal on their backs. Anakin strikes at them with his lightsaber, yet they come back to life and start scurrying to another room. The two Jedi follow the pink blobs and find two huge OG-9 homing spider droids, made completely from the blobs. Thinking fast, Anakin uses the Force to break open a hole in the wall, sucking out the spider droids, but also breaking apart the station. As the vacuum of space tears the station to pieces, Anakin attempts to activate the station's emergency ray shields. Keto quickly convinces Anakin not to, as the only hope for destroying the pink blobs is for the station to destroy itself, and them with it.
