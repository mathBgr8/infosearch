It was written by Rebecca Smith and published by Dorling Kindersley in the United Kingdom on August 4, 2004, and in the United States on August 16, 2004. Rediscover the excitement of the original Star Wars movies and create your own picture book using the characters, vehicles, and spacecraft found in this book.
