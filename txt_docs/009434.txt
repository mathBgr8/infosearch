Jonash Suul
2932

Jonash Suul was a Human male who was the firstborn son of Denn Solo and Tira Gama Solo, the twin brother of Tiion Sal-Solo, and the father of Han Solo. When he was an infant, he and his father were kidnapped by pirates during a raid on Tralus. He escaped and married a woman named Jaina. The two later had a son who he named Han Solo. Jonash Suul and Jaina disappeared early in their son's life, rendering Han an orphan by the age of seven. Jonash Suul was first mentioned in The Courtship of Princess Leia. During the development of Star Wars: The Han Solo Trilogy, author A. C.
