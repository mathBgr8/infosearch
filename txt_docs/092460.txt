Quay novelty toy
1752

}} The Quay novelty toy was a "pre-programmed prognosticator". Comprised of a white plastic sphere twenty-three centimeters in diameter, the toy contained a selection of stored generic answers on a voice chip. When asked a question, a reply was given in a loud voice. The Weequay species believed that the toy was a physical connection to the Moon God Quay, and would consider the answer as though it were of divine origin. Three Quay novelty toys were collected by the Tynnan demolitions expert Dawson on the planet Kabal.
