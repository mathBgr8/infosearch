Fleet Watch
583

Fleet Watch was the newspacket of the Alliance Veteran's Victory Association during the 16-17 ABY period. Veterans, including many that had earned high-ranking offices in the New Republic, read it to keep up-to-date with declassified, non-confidential intelligence on the activities of the New Republic Defense Fleet and related armed forces. *Tyrant's Test Category:News publications
