Cell of Imu
1311

The Cell of Imu was the storehouse of the eccentric Jedi Humat established more than 19,000 years before the Battle of Yavin. Located in the Petrax Quarter of Coruscant, the Cell of Imu was rumored to contain Humat's collection of odd and arcane devices. The centerpiece of this collection was a milky-white talisman known as the soul stealer. To protect his collection, Humat installed a number of deadly traps in his vault and left behind numerous animal skeletons reanimated with dark side magic. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Locations of the Jedi Order
