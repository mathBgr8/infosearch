This model was similar to the Power 5 heavy blaster pistol. The Model 44 was a low-powered blaster, featuring a shorter barrel than the Power 5. This weapon was carried by Imperial officers aboard the first Death Star. The Model 44 was also the pistol carried by Leia Organa during her masquerade as the bounty hunter Boushh. Alliance to Restore the Republic pilot Mazer Rackus carried a Model 44 blaster pistol.Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim The original image of this weapon from the Star Wars Customizable Card Game appears to be based on an image of the same prop that was the basis for the Power 5 heavy blaster pistol, but incomplete or broken. Star Wars: Behind the Magic includes a behind the scenes photo of the incomplete prop captioned "A heavy blaster under construction."Star Wars: Behind the Magic Like almost all the other weapon props used in the Original trilogy, the design of the 44 was based on an actual weapon. In this case, the German "Broomhandle" Mauser pistol was used and modified. The Mauser is the same weapon upon which the DL-44 heavy blaster pistol (popularized by Han Solo) was built. To make the Model 44, various odds and ends were attached to the Mauser, as well as certain pieces (most notably, the Mauser's long barrel) being hacked off. The Power 5 heavy blaster pistol was also based on the German Mauser.
