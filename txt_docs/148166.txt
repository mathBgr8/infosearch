Zal Tuag Th'Trar
1330

Zal Tuag Th'Trar was a Coynite from the planet of Coyn who was sympathetic to the Rebel Alliance. Due to this, he met with many offworlders and regularly reported back to his father on the events of the Galactic Civil War. Fellow Coynite, Zal Afreg Kt'Aya was a rival of his. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Coynites Category:Males
