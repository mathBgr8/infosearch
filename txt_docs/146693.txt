Manticore (Strike-class)
1878

The Manticore was a Strike-class medium cruiser belonging to the Empire. It was scheduled to be repaired at Depot XLS, but was attacked by Rebel B-wing starfighters intending to destroy all the capital ships while they were at their weakest condition.
