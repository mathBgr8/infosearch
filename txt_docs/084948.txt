Terkury Housing Complex
1046

The Terkury Housing Complex was a building in Rellidir, Tralus. It was an upscale housing complex, created over a demolished site, and under construction as of the Battle of Tralus. Its underground garage allowed Panther Flight a route under Rellidir's shield, allowing it to take down the rest of the shield for the other Corellian forces to get through. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Residential buildings Category:Tralus locations
