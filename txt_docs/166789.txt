|pages=45 |upc=759606081134 |timeline=1 ABYStar Wars: Timelines dates the events of Star Wars (2015) 50 to 1 ABY. |series=Star Wars (2015) |issue=50 }} }} Star Wars 50 is a Marvel comic book by Kieron Gillen. Gillen described the issue as a "big story" that his "entire run centers around." It followed the Mutiny at Mon Cala arc, which led into Star Wars 50 in a cataclysmic way. It was published on July 4, 2018. The issue also includes a backup story titled "Shu-Torun Lives." IN THIS ISSUE&hellip;HOPE DIES. The REBEL FLEET is completed. It's magnificent. It's beautiful. It's secret. But no secret is kept for long from DARTH VADER. The most epic story yet done in a STAR WARS comic begins here. Prepare to witness the Empire TRULY strike back. On the Mako-Ta rebel base, Mon Mothma and Leia reflect on the Rebellion's success in freeing the Mon Calamari. Leia excuses herself to check on Queen Trios, who seems to be enjoying her time but confesses she is a pampered monarch with no personal skills. Meanwhile, on the Ring of Kafrene, Han Solo and Chewbacca wrap up a negotiation with help from C-3PO acting as a translator. After leaving, they realize they are being chased by bounty hunters, but Han and Chewbacca lose them by hiding behind an entrance point. Luke arrives at the Mako-Ta base for the celebration. The rebel fleet is celebrating Leia's efforts by putting on a spectacle that includes Luke piloting his X-Wing. General Jan Dodonna tells Luke that the Rebellion's fleet must be used strategically, as they cannot win a war of attrition. During her speech, Mon Mothma calls the rebel fleet a "hidden blade" that will be deployed where it's needed most. The ships try to leave but their engines fail. Leia realizes Queen Trios left during the speech and grows suspicious. Suddenly, several Star Destroyers arrive from hyperspace.Darth Vader orders his boarding parties to wait, explaining that he wants to give the rebels time to feel fear before the attack. The rebels realize their comms, artillery batteries, engines, and hangar bay doors are all disabled. Aboard Vader's Star Destroyer, Queen Trios drinks wine while watching, but in a moment of shame, she throws the drink away. Vader senses the rebels' dramatic change in hope and believes he can now end the Rebellion. The issue places the Ring of Kafrene in the Outer Rim Territories, whereas Star Wars: Rogue One: The Ultimate Visual Guide places it in the Expansion Region.
