Zorbian space pirates
1352

The Zorbian space pirates were a disorganized group of Human pirates from Zorbia II. They were believed to be of Corellian descent, and had proven to be nuisance in the Moddell sector for centuries. One group led by Captain Krag embarked on an expedition to raid Bright Tree Village for its valuable gemwood tree. They used axes and bolo-slings as weapons, and after capturing most of the village's fighting-age males, the group nearly obtained the valuable gemwood tree until thwarted by Princess Kneesaa and the women and children of the village. *Ewoks (1985) 2 *The Essential Atlas Category:Pirate organizations
