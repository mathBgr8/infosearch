Twilight Fountains
1078

The Twilight Fountains were fountains on Talo Square in the city of Sath. The fountains changed color across the spectrum, including aquamarine, gold, orange, and navy blue. In 18 BBY, the Samarian resistance chose the fountains as a new meeting location, as the resistance's old drop spot had been used for over a month. Ferus Olin once met Nek at the Twilight Fountains to discuss resistance matters. Later, Olin dropped off Trever Flume at the fountains in an airspeeder, before Emperor Palpatine called Olin back to his work. The Twilight Fountains are first referred to as the singular "Twilight Fountain" in Return of the Dark Side.
