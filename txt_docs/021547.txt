|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=9 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series=Star Wars: Heir to the Empire |issue=3 }} Heir to the Empire 3 was the third issue in the Star Wars: Heir to the Empire comic adaptation series of comics based on the novel Heir to the Empire. It was released December 12, 1995. When an Imperial assassin puts Leia in his cross-hairs, Chewbacca crosses the galaxy to take Leia to a place of safety. But there are things in the galaxy that are worse than being assassinated, and "safety" is only relative.
