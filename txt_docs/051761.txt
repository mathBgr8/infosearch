The Other
2066

The Other was a prominent member of an extragalactic droid society named Silentium, alongside the One. A fifty-kilometer droidThe Essential Guide to Droids capable of traversing galaxies and possessing an incredibly long lifespan, the Other set out with the rest of his people in 3 BBY to track down the One's "son," Vuffi Raa, a young Silentium created to travel through the galaxy and learn as much as he could about the various cultures and civilizations he found.
