The moon was home to the Oasis mining colony, a thriving settlement where Oxite was mined. Both Rodin Higron's privateers and the Rebels had a base here during the Galactic Civil War. Some time after the Battle of Yavin, the Riders of the Maelstrom attacked the moon and attempted to crash the luxury liner Kuari Princess into Higron's base.
