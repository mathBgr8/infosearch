Ferfer
1200

Ferfer was a male Ryn who was a part of the Ryn Network, as gatherer One-Six-Four out of Balmorra, during the Yuuzhan Vong War. He was a member of Team Meloque, who infiltrated Yuuzhan Vong-occupied Caluula in order to destroy that world's yammosk in the last year of the war. He was killed when he was split open by an amphistaff wielded by a Slayer. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Ryn Category:Spies
