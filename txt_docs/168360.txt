Recon & Report: The Journey to Coruscant
6781

"Recon & Report: The Journey to Coruscant" was an article written by Peter M. Schweighofer originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 2 and reprinted in The Best of the Star Wars Adventure Journal, Issues 1-4. It was later reprinted on StarWars.com's Hyperspace in 2008, simply titled Recon & Report. The article details the status of the several parts of the galaxy (primarily the Core Worlds) six months after the Battle of Endor, as reported by a New Republic-allied mercenary group, the Black
