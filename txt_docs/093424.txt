Jim Swallow
4402

Jim Swallow (also credited as James Swallow) is the New York Times bestselling author of several original and licenced novels, along with numerous scripts for audio drama and videogames. He has written articles for several official Star Wars publications, including Star Wars Insider, Star Wars Gamer, The Official Star Wars Fact File and Star Wars: The Official Magazine. He worked on the Disney Infinity 3.0 videogame, writing the script for the Twilight of the Republic playset and character scripts for the Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Star Wars Rebels figure sets.
