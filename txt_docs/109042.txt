Besum
977

Besum was a four-eyed individual who was employed by Uso Yso. During the Galactic Games, Bog Divinian, a member of the Galactic Games Council in charge of seating the Senators, told Obi-Wan Kenobi that Besum gave him the seating chart for the Senators so he transferred the credits and ordered the seating from Besum. *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males
