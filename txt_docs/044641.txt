thumb|250px|The [[Galactic Senate/Legends|Senate Torture Chamber.]] The Senate Torture Chamber was a chamber in the Republic Executive Building during the Great Jedi Purge. Captured Jedi and other enemies of the Galactic Empire were sent here to be questioned and tortured. One such Jedi tortured here was Dama Montalvo, who was tortured by Darth Vader. *Purge Category:Coruscant rooms
