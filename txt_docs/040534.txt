Fassa (Twi'lek)
2296

Fassa was a gaunt male Rutian Twi'lek who served as dock manager for Vogga the Hutt's docks on the Hutt homeworld Nal Hutta's moon, Nar Shaddaa. When the Jedi exile Meetra Surik came to Nar Shaddaa searching for the missing Jedi Master Zez-Kai Ell, Fassa asked her to help assign three freighters their ID signatures, and later aided her by transporting the refugee Kahranna's family to the planet of Telos and possibly hiring the veteran pilot Odis. Fassa was originally planned to be a Toydarian, there are cut audio files in the game with all of Fassa's lines spoken with a Toydarian voice. It is unknown why the developers chose to replace the original Fassa with a Twi'lek and his voice-over with generic Twi'lek sounds. Fassa appears as a Toydarian in the Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords restored content mod.
