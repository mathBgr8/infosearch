He had light brown hair, brown eyes, and brown skin. After the Battle of Yavin, he resided aboard Tansarii Point Station, a space station located above the planet Ord Mantell, in the Bright Jewel system. There, he was working as a junk dealer, buying droid parts, old weapons and various other things from spacers that passed by. He was one of the two junk dealers working on Tansarii Point Station, along with Anniya, and he could frequently be found on the main level of the station, between the station's medical center and close combat training room where he bought item. Eocade appeared as a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Eocade was added to the game with the release of the "Chapter 1," on June 8, 2006. In the game, he was a junk dealer that the player could sell some, but not all, types of items to.
