Maldor Ring

The Maldor Ring was a group of slavers active near Ryloth. The Gamorrean bounty hunter Grefnakk was once owned by the Maldor Ring, but he was freed after saving the group's second-in-command during an ambush by rival slavers. *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters Category:Enslaver organizations
