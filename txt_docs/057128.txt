Battle of Bothawui (disambiguation)
1146

Battle of Bothawui may refer to: *Battle of Bothawui (22 BBY), a battle near Bothawui during the Clone Wars. *First Battle of Bothawui (3671 BBY), the first battle over Bothawui during the Great Galactic War. *Second battle of Bothawui (3671 BBY), the second attempt at an invasion of Bothawui during the Great Galactic War. *Battle of Bothawui (22 BBY), a battle near Bothawui during the Clone Wars. *Battle of Bothawui (19 ABY), one of the two concurrent final battles of the Galactic Civil War and subsequently the first of the Caamas Document Crisis. *Battle of Bothawui (40 ABY), a battle above Bothawui during the Second Galactic Civil War.
