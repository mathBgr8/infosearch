The planet had a barren rock surface and had one moon. Nur occupied the fourth orbital position around the star Vulpter. Nur was mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 4: Vulpter," the fourth entry in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 28, 2005.
