Ng'rhr
740

A Ng'rhr, in the Wookiee culture, was a Wookiee who was the master of an apprentice in a given trade. The word "Ng'rhr" meant "clan uncle" in Shyriiwook. In 0 ABY, the Wookiee bounty hunter Chenlambec acted as the Ng'rhr to Tinian I'att. She often referred to him as such both as a sign of respect and affection. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Wookiee culture
