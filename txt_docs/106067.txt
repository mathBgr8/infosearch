Sorrus Medical Center

Sorrus Medical Center was the main hospital in Sorrus. Rai Unlu worked at this facility. Once, the Sorrusian bounty hunter Ona Nobis set a trap in an attempt to apprehend Obi-Wan Kenobi and Astri Oddo.
