Jeff Quick
937

Jeff Quick is an Expo award-winning role-playing game designer. He was formerly the Senior Editor of Star Wars Gamer Magazine, as well as the Editor-in-Chief of Star Wars Insider. At Samford University where he attended college for his undergraduate degree, his nickname was "Zippy." *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Quick, Jeff
