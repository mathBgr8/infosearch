Daedalus Corporation
596

The Daedalus Corporation was a mining company. They worked mining ores in the Grilliss asteroid belt in the Atrig system. Around 6 ABY, Daedalus was employing slave labor to dig for radioactive ores without proper protection, and selling them on to the Galactic Empire. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Mining companies
