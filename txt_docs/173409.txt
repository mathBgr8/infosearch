Kann
1703

Kann was a cargo ferry used by Rebel Alliance. The cargo ferry was part of an illicit weapons deal between the Mugaari pirates and the Rebels. However, the Imperials attacked the the cargo loading area and the cargo ferry, along with all the other Mugaari and rebel ships were destroyed in the Battle of the Tungra sector. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Cargo ferries Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
