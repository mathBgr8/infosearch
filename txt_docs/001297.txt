Republic (disambiguation)
2335

A republic is a system of government in which citizens are ruled by elected officials. The concept of a republic, and by extension, democracy due to the heads of state being appointed by the people and having limited powers before a plurality, is generally considered antithetical to an empire largely because the latter concept has an absolute head-of-state ruling over everything with little input by the citizenry. It was founded to restore the Old Republic. *The Ylesian Republic, the Yuuzhan Vong puppet government set up by the Peace Brigade. *The Galactic Federation of Free Alliances, formed from the New Republic after the Yuuzhan Vong took over Coruscant in 27 ABY, also referred to as the Federal Galactic Republic.
