Bloodory
1039

Doctor Bloodory was a scientist on Aquilae. He worked at Medical Station Blue in the Underground Fortress. Bloodory was a slightly overweight middle-aged male Human. He perfected a technique of extracting knowledge from the brains of doctors and scientists called Bloodory's distillation. He prepared distillations of the minds of 33 leading lights of Aquilae. The product was transported in vials on a shock belt. They were intended as payment to gain the support of the Chrome Companies on Ophuchi against the Galactic Empire.
