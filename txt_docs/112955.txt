Misch'an
1755

Misch'an was a pilot who sometimes served in the Rebel Alliance's Red Squadron aboard the MC80 Home One type Star Cruiser Independence during the Galactic Civil War. A popular pilot, Misch'an was often seen with fellow pilots Karka and Dontal aboard the Independence.
