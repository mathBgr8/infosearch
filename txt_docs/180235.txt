Pestage (Victory-class)
2713

The Pestage was a Victory-class Star Destroyer that served in the Imperial Navy during the height of the Galactic Civil War. In response to a number of Rebel attacks in the Outer Rim against various hydrocarbon processing facilities, the Empire decided to subjugate the planet of Kheedar to protect its hydrocarbon resources.
