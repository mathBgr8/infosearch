Tel Bollin
1848

Tel Bollin was the capital and only major city of Endregaad, a planet in the Outer Rim. It was built on a mesa, like all other settlements on Endregaad, to avoid the sandstorms that beset the valleys and canyons of that world. The closest other communities were a day's walk away. Tel Bollin had a geode Miners' Guildhall, a warehouse belonging to Skydove Freight, a cantina called the The Fallen Warrior and a disused sports ground. The city had a City Hall, the center of all local administration, and apparently the highest level authority on the planet.
