WED Treadwells were flexibleStar Wars Builders: Droids class 5 utility and repair droids. Standing at around a meter tall, they came in a range of models, but all had a flat, wide treaded base, a spindly, telescopic stalk, upon which was mounted binocular visual sensors. WEDs also had a cluster of arms tipped with various tools, which could be purchased separately. Treadwells were used to repair ships, machinery, and other droids, but were relativity fragile and required frequent maintenance. Despite this, they had the ability to keep working at optimal capacity until they were destroyed.Star Wars: Force Arena There were four known models: *WED-15 Treadwell droid *WED-15 Septoid Treadwell *WED 15 "Septoid 2" Treadwell toolkit droidStar Wars: Complete Locations *WED-15-XT Treadwell droidStar Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary, New Edition Manufactured by Cybot Galactica, WED Treadwell repair droids were commonly found throughout the galaxy, just as helpful on a moisture farm as they were in a starship hangar.
