Polygamy
1696

Polygamy was the act of having more than one spouse at a time. Because of the low male birthrate among Cereans, polygamy was a generally accepted practice on Cerea. While the Jedi Order's tenets forbade any emotional attachment, Jedi Master Ki-Adi-Mundi was allowed to take multiple wives. He had four wives, two of which were known as Shea and Mawin. Polygamy was referenced in Labyrinth of Evil and Star Wars: Prelude to Rebellion.
