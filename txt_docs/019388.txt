Olys Corellisi
1907

Olys Corellisi, translated to Old Corellian, was an ancient Corellian language. In the Corellian Spike variation of the sabacc game, the two cards with a value of 0 were called sylops, which meant "idiots" in Old Corellian. By the time of the New Republic, a song in Old Corellian was played in a lounge in Adelphi Base, which was a New Republic outpost on Adelphi. *Solo: A Star Wars Story *The Last Jedi: Rose Tico: Resistance Fighter Category:Corellian culture Category:Languages
