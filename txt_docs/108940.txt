Bennie
2415

Bennie was a medical droid that worked at the medcenter of the Moonbender Colony, assisting Doctor Korvik, who felt that the droid needed some reprogramming. Between around 40 BBY and 32 BBY, while the Sith apprentice Darth Maul attacked the colony to kill the Remainders who ran Moonbender, Bennie assisted Korvik in tending to the wounded. After Maul defeated most of the Remainders, he entered the medcenter and fatally wounded Korvik.
