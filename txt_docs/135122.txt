Valkyrie 19
726

Valkyrie 19 was a roleplaying magazine from Spring of 2000. It contained many roleplaying articles, including the first of a two-part adventure written by Phil Sidebotham for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The adventure was called "Flight of the Jedi." Part two of the series continued in Valkyrie 20. Category:Valkyrie
