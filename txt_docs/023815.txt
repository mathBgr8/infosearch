Tusken's Eye
1606

The Tusken's Eye was a G-class star in the Tusken's Eye system, orbited by a cloudy planet in the Utegetu Nebula of Wild Space, and was where the Dark Nest resided following the destruction of Kr. It was here that fifteen nest ships were built: one for Gorog and one for each of the fourteen nests that abandoned the nebula in 36 ABY. With the help from Jae Juun and Tarfang, Luke Skywalker and Han Solo traced the Dark Nest from Woteba to the Eye aboard the DR919a.
