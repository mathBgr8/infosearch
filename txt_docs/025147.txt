Threfal had a Type I breathable atmosphere and was the homeworld of the plant known as the meatvine, which could be used as food. Threfal was mentioned in the article "Endor and the Moddell Sector," which was authored by Craig Robert Carey, Daniel Wallace, and Jason Fry, and was published in the ninth issue of Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Gamer magazine on February 22, 2002. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Threfal system, and therefore the world Threfal, in grid square J-16.
