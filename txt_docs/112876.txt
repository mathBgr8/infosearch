Team Tauntaun
1219

Team Tauntaun was one of the five Jedi strike-teams sent to Corellia as part of Operation Roundabout. Consisting of Tahiri Veila, Doran Sarkin-Tainer, and Tiu Zax, their mission was to detain Thrackan Sal-Solo. The Corellians had anticipated the strike, however, and the ambush found itself ambushed instead.
