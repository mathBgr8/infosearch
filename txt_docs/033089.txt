SHADIS Magazine 14
874

SHADIS Magazine 14 was a roleplaying magazine from July/August of 1994. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "Strange Bedfellows," and was written by John Zinser. Category:Shadis
