Antmuel VI was the site of the Ch'hosk Cave Drawings that were discovered by the Human male xenoarchaeologist Doctor Angrail Ryhl at some point during his career. That discovery was later considered to be a remarkable find. Antmuel VI was first mentioned in the 1995 West End Games' sourcebook Heroes & Rogues, written by Paul Sudlow and Rick D. Stuart.
