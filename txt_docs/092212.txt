Gungan assault cannon

}} }} The Gungan assault cannon was a powerful assault weapon produced by the Gungans of Naboo. The assault cannon was a fusion of organically-grown technology coupled with standard electronic components. After the Battle of Yavin, Imperial scientists at a secret Imperial Research Facility on the planet Lok had received a grant to reverse engineer a Gungan assault cannon.
