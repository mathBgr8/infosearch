Spaceport Row
630

Spaceport Row was a street in Plawal Rift, Belsavis. It was lined with assorted bars and tapcafes, included among them were the Smoking Jets and Madame Lota's House of Flowers. It was presumably named due to its proximity to the local spaceport and thus being often frequented by spacers. *Children of the Jedi *Children of the Jedi abridged audiobook Category:Belsavis locations Category:Roadways
