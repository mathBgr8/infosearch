The New Essential Chronology dates that battle to 0 BBY, while establishing it as the final event before the beginning of 0 ABY. the Human male Graf Yonna and his family lived on Bront. During that time, the Galactic Empire committed atrocities on Bront that led to the planet's destruction as well as the death of Yonna's family. The resultant psychological trauma caused Yonna to lose his sanity, and he assumed the personality of Parsol d'Ahndole, an ancient adventurer and hero. Bront was created by Chuck Truett and mentioned in Cracken's Rebel Operatives, a 1994 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
