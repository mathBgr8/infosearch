Coral Vanda
4508

The Coral Vanda was a well-known luxury submersible cruiser that operated in the oceans of Pantolomin. thumb|left|250px|Coral Vanda [[:Category:Technical drawing images|schematics.]] Constructed locally by the Pantolomin Shipwrights collective, the Coral Vanda was one of the most sumptuous mobile hotels in the galaxy, rivaled only by the giant spaceliners of the Core Worlds. Offering a schedule of excursions lasting three or five standard days, these trips provided a stunning sightseeing tour. Despite this, most of the people traveling aboard the Coral Vanda did so to gamble in one of the eight extravagant casinos, each of which featured its own theme, identity and unique game. The vessel featured, among others, the Tralla Room and the Saffkin Room. thumb|right|250px|The Coral Vanda Prior to the Battle of Yavin, the scoundrels Raal Yorta, Smileredon-Verdont and Sammie Staable attempted a heist on the Coral Vanda. In 9 ABY,The Essential Atlas Generals Han Solo and Lando Calrissian visited the Coral Vanda while searching for information about the fabled Katana fleet. However, due to the duplicity of one of their sources, Solo and Calrissian were almost captured by Grand Admiral Thrawn's forces.
