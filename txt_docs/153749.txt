Along with its star system, the station was situated on a hyperlane that linked it to the Asamin system of the neighboring Strabin sector as well as the Adin system of the Expansion Region.The Essential Guide to Warfare &mdash; Based on corresponding data for the star system containing Triewahl Docking Station At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Rebel Alliance Special Forces operatives half-Human-half-Morellian Lieutenant Alton Lochner and Wookiee Tirranna, along with a number of other Rebel agents, visited Triewahl Docking Station. While there, the Human bounty hunters Callandri and Zayl Braith, members of the anti-Alliance Granse Confederacy group, attacked Lochner and Tirranna, attempting to exact revenge on the pair for repelling a previous attack on them by Callandri. Although the two Special Forces operatives were nearly killed, Tirranna's other companions managed to repulse the two bounty hunters. Triewahl Docking Station was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
