Gift of Anguish
746

Gift of Anguish was a Yuuzhan Vong Ro'ik chuun m'arh-class warship that was involved with the intercepting of the Nebula Chaser near Talfaglio some two years into the Yuuzhan Vong War. When Captain Pollux lied about the presence of Jedi Knights Alema Rar and Numa Rar aboard the Nebula Chaser to the Vong commander, the Gift of Anguish destroyed the vessel, killing all of the refugees aboard. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ro'ik chuun m'arh
