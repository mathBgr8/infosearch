Mallixer
745

The Mallixer was a ship that belonged to Jaxa and Bettle, two smugglers who lived in the time of the early part of the Galactic Civil War. The Mallixer was used for smuggling, and quite often (as is to be expected in such operations) narrowly escaped destruction, or worse, confiscation. *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Smugglers' Alliance
