Relentless (Star Destroyer)
1606

The Relentless was a Star Destroyer serving in the Imperial Navy. It was one of four ships that formed the core forces of Moff Vanko's personal fleet. Jak Durasha, Celia Durasha's brother was stationed aboard the Relentless in 0 BBY. *Riders of the Maelstrom *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
