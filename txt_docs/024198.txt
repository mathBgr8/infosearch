Black Wing Squadron
1708

Black Wing Squadron, was an elite fighter wing serving on board the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Chimaera. It was led by Lieutenant Creb, who based the squadron off of Rogue Squadron, an elite wing of the New Republic. It composed of TIE/LN starfighters. It would notably fight at the Battle of Bilbringi, which saw the death of Grand Admiral Thrawn. *The Last Command *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Imperial fighter wings
