Intestinal Revenge of Bars Barka
1589

|transmissiontype=Neimoidian microbes |incubationperiod= |symptoms=Crippling weight loss |treatments= }} The Intestinal Revenge of Bars Barka was a devastating illness transmitted by microbes normally found in the bodies of members of the Neimoidian species. Several decades before the outbreak of the Clone Wars in the year 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology it was believed to have been carried to the planet Bars Barka by a Neimoidian trade delegation. The global disease infected its Ubese colonists, resulting in crippling weight loss. The Neimoidians denied any part in the onset of the Intestinal Revenge of Bars Barka. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Diseases and maladies
