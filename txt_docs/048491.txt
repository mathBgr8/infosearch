thumb|right|250px|Hangar 9 advertisement that appeared in [[HoloNet News/Legends|HoloNet News.]] Hangar 9 was a secretive hangar on Coruscant. Its owners allowed it to be used for illegal activities, and even put out advertisements in HoloNet News suggesting it could be used so. It was also available for more legitimate uses, such as parking. Category:Coruscant docking facilities
