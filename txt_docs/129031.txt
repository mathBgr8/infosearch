Merman
1519

The Mermen (singular Merman) were extinct ocean-dwelling inhabitants of Mon Calamari. They were green-skinned beings whose legs fused to form a fish-like tail. The Holographic Zoo of Extinct Animals on Coruscant contained a holographic exhibit of a group of Mermen hunting a krabbex while armed with spears made of bone. According to Star Wars (1977) 75, Mermen were apparently present on the planet Iskalon during the Iskalon Effect disaster, and many innocent mermen were killed by the tidal wave.
