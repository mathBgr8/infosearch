Cassus Fett's Battle Armor
1978

Cassus Fett's Battle Armor was a personal suit of Mandalorian battle armor that had been designed for and worn by the Mandalorian warrior and tactician Cassus Fett prior to the Jedi Civil War. Fett presumably wore this armor at the Battle of Jaga's Cluster, when he killed the captain of a Republic frigate—an action that earned him infamy across the galaxy.
