Coruscant wedding hairbow
819

A Coruscant wedding hairbow was a white ribbon tied in a complex way. On Coruscant, it was traditionally worn in the hair of a bride on her wedding.Specter of the Past Shada D'ukal knew how to create a type of wedding bow that could be created in just a few minutes. She created and used such a bow in order to infiltrate the building where Leia Organa Solo and her family lived. *Specter of the Past Category:Apparel for weddings Category:Apparel of Coruscant Category:Hairpins Category:Headbands
