While passing through this system to avoid navigating through an asteroid field, a Rebel convoy consisting of two BFF-1 bulk freighters designated Xroi and three CR90 corvettes designated Quluiy was attacked by pirates. An Imperial responsive force soon followed, attemping to rout both factions before they could escape.
