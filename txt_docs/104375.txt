Codeq Santhananan
1730

Codeq Santhananan was an individual that was in the business, among others, of transmitting messages on the behalf of customers that included Dengar. He died just outside the Mos Eisley Cantina on Tatooine in debt to the establishment, the results of a conflict burned into the doorway as an irremovable stain despite the best efforts of two rehab droids.
