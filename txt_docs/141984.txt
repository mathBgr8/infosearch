Chie (MC80 Star Cruiser)
1978

The Chie was a MC80 Star Cruiser used by the Rebel Alliance. It was attacked by a squadron of Strike-class medium cruisers called Longbow who were assisted by Avenger Squadron in TIE/ad starfighters, and Assault Gunboats. The cruiser had X-wings from Rogue Squadron, B-wings and A-wings.
