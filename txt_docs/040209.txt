B-5D8-GE3
1075

B-5D8-GE3 was a GE3-series protocol droid who worked for Vogga the Hutt as a clerk for his droid-run warehouse on Nar Shaddaa. He unwittingly allowed the warehouse to be infiltrated by T3-M4, who was looking for a way to save Meetra Surik. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide Category:GE3-series protocol droids Category:Nar Shaddaa residents
