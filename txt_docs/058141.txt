New Cov Biomolecule Company
840

The New Cov Biomolecule Company was a leading harvesting and exporting company of naturally occurring biomolecules that were indigenous to New Cov from otherwise toxic plants and animals. These molecules had numerous applications galaxy-wide in medicine, industry, and military projects, and were often the objective of raids.
