Untitled Hodges-Traviss webstrip
1163

After the fourth Evasive Action webstrip, Tom Hodges and Karen Traviss were going to collaborate to create a new webstrip for Star Wars Hyperspace. Hodges implied that it could involve Mandalorians. Hodges himself first announced this webstrip, however no official confirmation has come from Lucas Licensing yet. As of the beginning of 2013, there were no known plans to release this webstrip in the near future. Category:Webstrips
