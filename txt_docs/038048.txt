Karal Kaar
1965

Karal Kaar was a green-skinned male Twi'lek employed by Aratech Droids Inc.. He worked at the Aratech Mercantile on Dantooine in 3956 BBY. The Jedi Master Nemo described him as a bit brusque albeit a good being; the two often shared games of pazaak during the early hours of the morning. When Karal Kaar introduces himself, he says 'Karal Karr.'
