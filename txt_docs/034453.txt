Jess Allashane was a veteran warrior of the Rebel Alliance's cause. Serving at the Rebellion's Echo Base ice facility, Allashane was trained to counter Imperial tactics in cold-weather environments. A Human, Allashane had dark eyes and light skin. Allashane wore the standard cold-weather armor of the Rebel Echo Base field troops while on Hoth, including headgear, anti-glare goggles, scarf, padded vest, thermal flak jacket, gloves, belt, leggings,Star Wars Character Encyclopedia boots, and backpack. The character of Jess Allashane was created for the as part of the November 1996 Hoth Limited expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game. The Hoth Limited set linked Allashane with a production photo of a Hoth Rebel soldier extra from the 1980 film Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. However, it is unclear whether the extra&mdash;and, by extension, Allashane&mdash;appears on-screen in the film. Many in the online Star Wars fan community have credited American actor Treat Williams with Allashane's portrayal. Williams visited England's Elstree Studios during the 1979 filming of The Empire Strikes Back "Ford had befriended Williams at England's Elstree Studios during the 1979 production of The Empire Strikes Back &mdash; where Treat had managed to make his way onto the exclusive set, and was in tan fatigues to portray a rebel officer." and was photographed in-costume on the Echo Base set with actress Carrie Fisher, who played Princess Leia Organa. However, the film's external Hoth scenes,The Making of The Empire Strikes Back which include the Allashane character production photo, were shot in Finse, Norway, concluding on March 11, 1979. Moreover, the extras hired to portray Rebel soldiers during the Battle of Hoth were local Norwegians, including several dozen mountain-rescue skiers. Filming at Elstree Studios did not begin until March 13, once the Norway shots were completed. Therefore, Williams' involvement in the film does not correspond to the Allashane character.
