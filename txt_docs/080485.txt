Urup Fal'co
1395

Urup Fal'co was a Mustafarian of the Mensix Mining Company. He was present in the Mensix Mining Facility on Mustafar at the point where the company was suffering a small labor crisis. Many miners went on strike, and refused to talk to company representatives. Urup thus employed offworlders to convince miners to re-enter negotiations with the company.
