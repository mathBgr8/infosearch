Thranta-class War Cruiser
2802

The Thranta-class War Cruiser was one of the last starship classes manufactured by Alderaan. Most of the Thranta-class vessels were scrapped when Alderaan demilitarized after the Clone Wars. Courage, Fidelity, and Valiant were spared and assigned to escort the Alderaanian War Frigate Another Chance, which had been modified into an armory ship.
