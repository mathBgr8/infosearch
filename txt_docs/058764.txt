Galindas Exports
1949

Galindas Exports was one of the Tenloss Syndicate's mass-market fencing and money laundering operations.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Galindas maintained a large processing network in the Sektor. A number of asteroid storage facilities, near Lucrenn, were maintained by Galindas Exports. It maintained a branch office at the Vergesso Base, which was controlled by Ororo (another Tenloss subordinate). Galindas was a big supplier of materiel and equipment for the Rebel Alliance, either directly, or through Ororo or independent smugglers; Vergesso was a location where hundreds of tons passed to the Alliance each month of the Galactic Civil War. *Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations *Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Tenloss Syndicate companies Category:Transport companies
