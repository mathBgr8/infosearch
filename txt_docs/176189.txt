Irasal ke Voltin
2423

Irasal ke Voltin was a male Human Adumari who had a feud with fellow Adumari Garatty ke Kith. Intending to ambush ke Kith as he returned home, ke Voltin tried to enter his apartment but after learning that it was locked, he then tried finding a hiding spot to wait. It was then when ke Voltin encountered Rogue Squadron pilots Wedge Antilles and Wes Janson, who were waiting for New Republic spy Iella Wessiri, ke Kith's neighbor. After confirming that the pilots were neither hunting him nor his target, ke Voltin stated that they had no quarrel and wished them good fortune before relocating to a different hiding place. When ke Kith finally arrived, ke Voltin attacked and engaged him in a blastsword duel. ke Voltin emerged victorious and departed the apartment with ke Kith slung over his shoulders. Janson believed ke Kith to have been dead. ke Voltin was equipped with an Adumari pistol and a Blastsword.
