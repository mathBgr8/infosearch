Amador Cisneros
418

Amador Cisneros was a letterer who was employed with Dark Horse Comics. He was responsible for the lettering of Union 2, Sergio Aragonés Stomps Star Wars, The Bounty Hunters: Kenix Kil and Luke Skywalker's Walkabout. The latter is reprinted in Star Wars: Luke Skywalker, Last Hope for the Galaxy. Category:Comic book letterers
