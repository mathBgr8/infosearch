Python (corvette group)
1755

Python was a pair of CR90 corvettes that were part of a Rebel convoy that contained B-wing starfighters. The corvettes were attacked, but thanks to the X-wings the entire convoy managed to escape into hyperspace. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
