Ramit
1553

Ramit was a BFF-1 bulk freighter that delivered munitions and repairs to the Imperial Nebulon-B frigate Red Wind. However her arrival was timed to a rebel attack consisting of 9 CR90s and two A-wings, one of them piloted by Keyan Farlander.
