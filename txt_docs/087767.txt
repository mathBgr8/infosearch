Longprow attack cruiser
1655

The Longprow attack cruiser was a cruiser used by the Pirates of Iridium in 22 BBY. The pirates had two in their possession at the time of their demise. The pirates fitted their power gem to the front of the Longprow and used the gem's shield-disrupting abilities to smash through the defenses of unsuspecting ships. The Republic assault was led by Kit Fisto and the Monitor III, an armed Republic Cruiser. One Longprow was destroyed in the battle, but the second was captured intact. *"The Power Gem" Category:Cruiser classes
