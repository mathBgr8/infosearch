|type= |pages= |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=087431190X |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Battle for Endor is a solitaire board game published by West End Games in 1989, designed by Peter Corless. The game includes: *terrain map *62 stand-up playing pieces *Action Cards deck *Event Cards deck *6 special fire dice *rules manual A SOLITAIRE GAME Using the STAR WARS HEROIC BATTLES Game System! THE SITUATION SEEMS HOPELESS! Hundreds of Imperial troops surround the Rebel strike team: stormtroopers, speeder bikes, and AT-ST walkers. The mission has failed. With the energy field still protecting the new Death Star, the Rebel fleet faces imminent destruction. The last hope of the Alliance lies with a handful of captured heroes and an unorganized army of natives &mdash; Ewoks &mdash; whose weaponry is no match for Imperial firepower. Can you, as the Rebel leader, reverse the fortunes of war? An innovative solitaire system controls the Imperial forces. Your enemy has great firepower and the ability to shift tactics. All you have is your skill, your courage &mdash; and the primitive technology of a clever and resourceful ally. May the Force be with you.
