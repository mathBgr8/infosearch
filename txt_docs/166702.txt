|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic *Alliance Special Forces }} The Harrier Infiltration Team was a Rebel Alliance Special Forces unit. It was composed of Fable Astin, Deke Holman, and Arecelis Acosta. All three members were stationed aboard the Prodigal. During a mission to destroy an Imperial communications bunker on Nysza III, Arecelis Acosta was killed by dark Jedi Vialco, though the other two members survived after completing their objective. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic Special Forces units
