250px|thumb|Map of Finse Finse is a locality in Norway, with its railway station located alongside the main line between Oslo and Bergen. At a height of 1,222 meters, it is the highest station in the entire Norwegian railway system. Filming for Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back took place in Finse in 1979 to represent Echo Base on the planet Hoth. *The Making of The Empire Strikes Back *Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga – The Official Collector's Edition Category:Filming locations
