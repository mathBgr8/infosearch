Lost Sultans of Lust
1575

|formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Lost Sultans of Lust were a groupGalactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide hailing from the Lust system who dined with the Human scholar Ebenn Q3 Baobab at some point prior to the year 6 ABY. Baobab wrote about the encounter in his autobiography, Blazing Rockets, and the group was also mentioned in his book Galactic Phrase & Travel Guide. The Lost Sultans of Lust were first mentioned in the Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide, which was written by Ben Burtt and released in 2001. The book did not make clear at what stage of his life Baobab dined with the Sultans. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Undefined social groups
