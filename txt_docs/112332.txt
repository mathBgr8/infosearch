Duplicator
1152

The Duplicator was a device that could recreate any object that was first digitized by a prototyper. It was developed during the final years of the Old Republic in order to meet the needs of the scientific community of the time. Early on during the New Order, duplicators were commonly found on the black market, where they were often used in counterfeiting schemes. *Han Solo and the Lost Legacy *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Manufacturing technology Category:Tools
