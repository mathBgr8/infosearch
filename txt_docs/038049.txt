Crattis Yurkal
2379

Crattis Yurkal was a Twi'lek male who ran the general store Aratech Mercantile inside the Jedi Enclave on Dantooine in 3956 BBY. He was very talkative, and despised the Czerka Corporation. He owned a blade that belonged to Sanasiki.
