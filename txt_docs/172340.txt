thumb|right|250px|[[Lambda-class T-4a shuttle/Legends|Lambda-class T-4a shuttle Pamir 1 under attack from an Imperial TIE/sa bomber.]] Pamir referred to a group of Lambda-class T-4a shuttles that were employed by a group of Nharwaak researchers during the Conflict at Mylok IV. As Imperial forces assaulted the Nharwaak research facility, the shuttles of Pamir group took pilots to their Y-wings in order to defend themselves from the Imperial onslaught.
