Jobe Keely
1865

Jobe Keely was a human male crewman of Outbound Flight in 27 BBY. Among his onboard technician friends were Chas Uliar and Brace Tarkosa. Keely later represented the Survivors on the Managing Council in the Redoubt, where Outbound Flight crash-landed.
