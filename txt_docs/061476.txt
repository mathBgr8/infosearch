Glakka
2109

Glakka was an ice-covered moon in the Outer Rim Territories where Jabba the Hutt had a gun-running operation along with Chevin criminal Ephant Mon. The two tried to steal an Imperial weapons cache, but were ambushed by an Imperial squad.
