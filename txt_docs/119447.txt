|penciller=*Brian Ching *Dustin Weaver *Harvey Tolibao |inker= |letterer=Michael HeislerStar Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 2: Flashpoint |colorist=*Michael Atiyeh *Jay David Ramos |cover artist=Travis Charest |editor=*Jeremy Barlow *Dave Marshall |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=May 16, 2007 |other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=144 |isbn=1593077610 |isbn2= |timeline=3964&ndash;3963 BBY |series=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |issues=7-12 |preceded by=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 1: Commencement |followed by=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 3: Days of Fear, Nights of Anger }} Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 2: Flashpoint is the second trade paperback of the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic comic book series written by John Jackson Miller and published by Dark Horse Comics. Released on May 16, 2007, the paperback collects issues #7-12 of the series: the three-part Flashpoint story arc, the stand-alone issue Homecoming, and the two-part Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Reunion arc. Volume 2 restores the correct ordering of Flashpoint and Homecoming, placing the stand-alone issue in its intended position following Flashpoint. Nearly 4,000 years before the Death Star, fugitive Padawan Zayne Carrick's quest to clear his name for the alleged murder of his fellow Jedi-in-training brings him head to head with the galaxy's most feared fighting force&mdash;the Mandalorians! Along with a petty crook named Gryph and Elbee the grouchy droid, Zayne is a passenger aboard the Last Resort, a renegade ship piloted by the senile genius Camper and his fierce protector Jarael. Together, this motley crew will face kidnappings, hijackings, maniacal scientists, Mandalorian traitors, bumbling bounty-hunting brothers, and a few really big explosions.
