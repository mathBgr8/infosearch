Goure Conor
1171

Goure Conor was a male Ryn who was staying on Bakura. He greeted the Solo family upon their arrival, and became a friend to Tahiri Veila. He was part of a vast Ryn communication network, and directed the Solos to Onadax, where a contact was reportedly awaiting, after the Ssi-ruuk Consecration Ceremony had been ended abruptly. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bakurans Category:Males Category:Ryn
