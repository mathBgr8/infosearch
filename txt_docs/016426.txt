|subgroup= |headquarters=Jedi Academy |locations=Ossus |formed= |founded=c. 30 ABY |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=New Jedi Order }} The Woodoos were an initiate training clan of the New Jedi Order that was usually made up of children between the ages of five and seven depending on species. This clan was named after the Woodoo, a creature native to Tatooine. In 40 ABY the Woodoo Clan, along with the rest of the students, were taken hostage during the raid on the Jedi Academy.
