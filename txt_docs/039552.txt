Imperial troop transport
2220

The Imperial troop transport was an Imperial transport, somewhat similar to the TIE lander, which also served the same purpose. thumb|left|100px|Rear view of a troop [[Transport/Legends|transport.]] While the TIE lander had broad, bent wings, this transport had smaller, straight wings. It also had an insect-like front part containing the cockpit, thus deviating from the standard TIE-cockpit design. Imperial troops operating in Bespin's Cloud City used these kinds of transports to and from the city.
