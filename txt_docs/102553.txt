Feena Shaan
956

Feena Shaan was a native gang leader on Mawan in 25 BBY, when Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi were sent there to make peace. Feena controlled most of the goods and services sold to the majority of citizens harbored beneath the capital city of Naatan. She also knew the tunnels below almost as well as those of the Resistance movement. *Jedi Quest: The Shadow Trap Category:Criminals
