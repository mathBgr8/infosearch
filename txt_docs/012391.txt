Tnani Ikon
2418

Tnani Ikon was a female Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order during the final years of the Galactic Republic. Tnani Ikon was a Force-sensitive female member of the Jedi Order. She eventually passed her Trials of Knighthood and was elevated to the rank of Jedi Knight. Studying the ways of the Jedi Consular, Ikon became a Lore Keeper in the Temple Archives, aiding her fellow Jedi in data searches. During the years preceding the Separatist Crisis, Ikon assisted Master Tahl in her duties until her death, at which point he assumed her duties in full. Continuing his work as a librarian, Ikon's methods noticeably differed from Tahl's, as she had formed lasting working relationships with her peers over her many years of experience, which Ikon had yet to gain.
