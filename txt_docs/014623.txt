Eriscot was a water world located in the Eriscot system, a part of the Haserian sector in the Slice portion of the Mid Rim. thumb|right|185px|Eriscot was the homeworld of the Nejma species (foreground). Eriscot was the homeworld of the Nejma, a species of aquatic sentients. At some point five millennia before the Galactic Civil War, states that the species constituting the Iskalonian cultural group were brought to Iskalon approximately five millennia before the Galactic Civil War. Since "The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series" establishes that the relocation of the Iskalonian species was prefaced by the cataclysms on the species' original homeworlds, the cataclysms must also have occurred around that time. a series of cataclysms occurred on several water worlds throughout the galaxy, including Eriscot. The catastrophes left the native inhabitants helpless until a group of spacefaring benefactors arrived and helped relocate the species of the affected worlds to the Mid Rim &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Iskalon system planet Iskalon, where the latter eventually formed the Iskalonian cultural group. At some point during the reign of the New Republic, the Shi'ido anthropologist Doctor Hoole was studying the ruins of the Iskalonian homeworlds, including Eriscot, to determine the nature of the cataclysms that occurred there and the identity of the Iskalonian benefactors.
