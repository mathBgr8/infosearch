38th Armored Division
4244

}} The 38th Armored Division was a unit of clone troopers in the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. The Combat Engineer Battalion was a subdivision of this unit. thumb|left|300px|[[Clone engineers of the 38th Armored Division.]] The armor of this division was painted with dull mustard markings. Heavy artillery troopers in the Combat Engineer Battalion subdivision of the 38th Armored were marked with similar markings, but with brown coloring instead of the dull mustard of the rest of the 38th. The 38th Armored Division included many heavy artillery units such as HAVw A6 Juggernauts and Self-Propelled Heavy Artillery-Turbolaser (SPHA-T) walkers, All Terrain Tactical Enforcer, and Republic Artillery Guns. The division was also equipped with Nu-class attack shuttles and Low Altitude Assault Transport/infantry gunships. In addition to regular troopers, the unit had a heavily armored trooper variant. It participated in numerous battles of the war, including the Battle of Kashyyyk, where it was led into combat by Jedi Grand Master Yoda. *The Making of Star Wars Revenge of the Sith |cards= }} *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Grand Army of the Republic divisions Category:Members of the 38th Armored Division
