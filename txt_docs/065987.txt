Hort
2778

Hort was a Moff on the Imperial Remnant's Moff Council who was in charge of a sector in 19 ABY. That year,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Specter of the Past to 19 ABY. he attended a meeting with the other Moffs and Supreme Commander Gilad Pellaeon on the Outer Rim TerritoriesThe Essential Atlas Imperial capital planet Bastion. After the supreme commander suggested they should surrender to the New Republic, Hort gathered his datacards and attempted to leave while saying that he had important matters waiting for him at his sector. The council's host, Moff Vilim Disra, told him to sit down, and Hort complied. Shortly after, Hort told fellow council member Sander to stop describing how the Republic would kill them if they gave up.
