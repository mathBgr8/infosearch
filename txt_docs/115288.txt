Magnus (starship)
2145

Magnus was a Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The Magnus delivered supplies to the CR90 corvette Jeffrey in the Cron Drift. An Imperial force consisting of four transports, escorted by Assault Gunboats attacked the rendezvous but were defeated by X-wings flown by Keyan Farlander and Puck Naeco, allowing the transfer to take place. *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
