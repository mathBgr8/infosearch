*Alliance to Restore the Republic **Scandium Team |masters= |apprentices= }} Korgath was a Taloron infiltrator agent for the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Kogath was a former member of the Taloron Hunters when he joined the Alliance, he became a member of the Scandium Team. Korgath was considered the luckiest Scandium, because he survived many high-risk situations. During the Wellte-ir Massacre, Korgath was one of the six survivors of the Scandium Team. After that, the Scandium Team was dismantled, and Korgath continued to do field work for the Alliance. Korgath had a reputation for being hard and cold, although close acquaintances found him to be very warm and personable. He worked at maintaining his reputation of having a bad attitude, often starting bar brawls over minor incidents. He was considered very lucky.
