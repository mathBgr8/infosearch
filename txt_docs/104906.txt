The WED-600 Treadwell is a large armored labor droid designed to work in antimatter furnaces. Their multi-armed, eyestalked bodies were case-hardened and could resist blaster fire and lightsaber blades. *Children of the Jedi Category:Hazardous-service droid models Category:WED Treadwell models
