Domain Dal
668

Domain Dal was a Domain among the Shaper caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Domain Dal was a powerful Domain, as evidenced by the fact that it possessed its own Koros-Strohna. After the Battle of Coruscant and the destruction of the Sunulok, Domain Dal provided its Worldship to Warmaster Tsavong Lah. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines I: Rebel Dream *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Dal
