Eyar Treatment Systems
819

Eyar Treatment Systems was a company contracted to clean and maintain the underground sewers of Eyar City. The sewers were often left unclean, as Eyar Treatment Systems was often underfunded by the government. Eyar Treatment Systems employed a number of technicians and utilized GH Maintenance Droids. *Hideouts & Strongholds Category:Waste companies
