Glie'oleg Kru
1450

Glie'oleg Kru was a Twi'lek serving the New Republic Defense Force during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Holding the rank of Brevet Admiral, he was in command of the Bothan Assault Cruiser, Champion and led New Republic forces during the Battle of Kalarba.
