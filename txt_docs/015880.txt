Nahkee
1336

Nahkee was a Phlog infant (or Phlogling), the son of Zut and Dobah. Nahkee was stolen by Morag. She framed the Ewoks by placing Ewok hoods, bows and arrows at the scene of the abduction so that the infants parents would think that the Ewoks were responsible and seek revenge.
