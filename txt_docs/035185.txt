Senior Jedi General
1382

Jedi Masters during the Clone Wars could attain the rank of Senior Jedi General. Ranking immediately below High Jedi Generals, Senior Jedi Generals commanded the twenty Sector Armies within the Grand Army of the Republic. Jedi Master Luminara Unduli was a Senior Jedi General,Star Wars: The Official Starships & Vehicles Collection 30 as was Master Ry-Gaul, who commanded the Second Army. *Star Wars: The Official Starships & Vehicles Collection 30 Category:Grand Army of the Republic ranks Senior General
