Croig's Fix-It Barn
939

Croig's Fix-It Barn was a repair shop located on the world of Nam Chorios, in the capital city of Hweg Shul. The facility's owner, the Duros Croig Jacamden, took pride in it being the biggest repair shop in Hweg Shul, and consequently on the entire planet, since it encompassed about thirty repair bays. While searching for his love, Callista Ming, in 13 ABY, Luke Skywalker took a temporary job as a mechanic at the Fix-It Barn in order to better conduct his search. *Planet of Twilight *Planet of Twilight abridged audiobook Category:Vendors and sellers
