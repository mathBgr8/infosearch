Dangerous Species Act
676

The Dangerous Species Act was an Imperial legislature which decreed that any species that was dangerous and interfered with Imperial occupation of a planet was subject to eradication by the Empire. The act was only intended to apply to non-sentient lifeforms or wild beasts. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition Category:Galactic Empire laws and acts
