Ril Garn
1688

Ril Garn was an officer in the Imperial Army and held the rank of lieutenant during the Galactic Civil War. As the commander of Imperial Army Unit 23/443 located on the planet of Yetnis, Lieutenant Garn attempted to cleanse that world of Rebel operatives and the cells that controlled them in a campaign dubbed Operation: Rebel Hunt. Garn was fiercely dedicated to the New Order and hoped to advance in the Army. He generally followed regulations, but would bend the rules if necessary.
