*10 credits |value= |shape= |size= |material= |length= |width= |diam= |height= |weight=0.5 kg |protection= |crew= |capacity=4 shots |range=25 |inscription= |markings= |purpose=*Backup weaponThe Clone Wars Campaign Guide *Concealed weapon |heritage= |affiliation= }} The Adjudicator was a small palm-sized hold-out slugthrower pistol manufactured by Czerka Arms. One of the few non-energy weapons commercially available in large quantities all over the galaxy, it was used by civilians as a personal defence weapon.Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook The weapon was sold with a wrist clamp magnetic holstering lock; when triggered, the spring-activated rig would thrust the weapon into the user's hands, allowing for a quick and nasty surprise. The Adjudicator shared many attributes with hold-out blasters, such as its small size and the ability to easily conceal it within a sleeve or pocket. The pistol had a capacity of four shots, but compensated for this with sheer stopping power. However, the Adjudicator was slow to reload, leading to many to use it as a back-up weapon. left|thumb|Alliance Adjudicator A modified version of the Adjudicator was used by the Alliance Special Forces.
