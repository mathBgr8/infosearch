Martial-class shuttle
672

The Martial-class shuttle was a starship used in boarding operations. Produced by Sienar Fleet Systems, the ship was small enough that three could fit into a retrofitted landing bay in a CR90 corvette. They were in use since at least 5 ABY. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shuttle classes Category:Sienar Fleet Systems products
