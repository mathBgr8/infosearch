|gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Blonde |feathers= |eyes=Blue |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Galactic Empire **Stormtrooper Corps |masters= |apprentices= }} Milo Strander was a male GeNode clone who served Galactic Empire as a stormtrooper. During the Imperial Era, he was a corporal in Captain Janzor's platoon and was friends with Wayson Dower. Strander was a foot soldier in the Imperial occupation of Kashyyyk, where he and Dower were the only members of their 200-man unit to survive. For surviving the mission, he was promoted to first sergeant and did not serve in the rest of the campaign. Strander had thick blonde hair, deep blue eyes, and a muscular build. At the time of the occupation of Kashyyyk, his generation of clones made up forty percent of the Empire's stormtroopers.
