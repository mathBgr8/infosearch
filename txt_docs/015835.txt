Larrad Shrike
1299

Larrad Shrike was the brother of Garris Shrike. Shrike was a tall, heavy-set Human male who carried a blaster. He was allowed to be armed aboard Trader's Luck because he was an officer. Shrike was severely injured aboard Trader's Luck by the Wookiee Dewlanna when a young Han Solo made his escape. Dewlanna, in rage, pulled and twisted his arm. Several pops were heard as tendons and ligaments gave way.
