Ettam
1088

Ettam was a city on Ralltiir that contained several farms. Just before the Battle of Yavin, Imperial forces under Commander Akobi assaulted the town, resulting in many unintentional deaths when the Empire thought a medical center was a Rebel outpost. *Empire 13 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities on Ralltiir
