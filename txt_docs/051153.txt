Gauntlet (Imperial-class)
1647

Gauntlet was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer that had been assigned to the Tapani sector fleet of the Imperial Navy. The vessel, under the command of Captain Lin Nunsk, patrolled Lamuir IV and escorted bacta convoys on their runs through the Shapani Bypass. *Lords of the Expanse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flagships Category:Imperial-class Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
