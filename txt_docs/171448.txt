Lohema
755

Lohema was a small mining town that was located on the Twi'lek homeworld of Ryloth. The community became prosperous while digging for ryll and precious metals. The mining operations were run by the Rooshan clan, who consequently became the leaders of the settlement. They enforced a strong work ethic among the miners and inflated their prices.
