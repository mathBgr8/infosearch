Chitter
2484

Chitter was a large, blue-skinned, birdlike being with beady black eyes who trained in the Junior Jedi Academy, a class of the youngest students at the Jedi Praxeum on Yavin 4.Junior Jedi Knights: The Golden Globe Chitter was in the same class at the Praxeum as fellow Jedi trainees Anakin Solo and Tahiri Veila. In 22 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion within the the academy's Grand Audience Chamber, Jedi Master Luke Skywalker began a lesson on how to use the Force to recapture lost memories. Rather than telling the young students how to perform the technique, Skywalker only told the students to believe and they would succeed. Concerned by this, Chitter asked the Jedi Master what would happen if they failed, to which Skywalker replied that there was no try for a Jedi, only do. The students were then instructed to pair up to practice the exercise.
