Having survived Order 66 and the rise of the Galactic Empire Enasteri went into hiding on Rhamalai with his secret family. Having passed the Trials of Knighthood of the Jedi Order, Neth Enasteri served the Order as a Jedi Knight. Enasteri evaded the execution of Contingency Order 66, issued by Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, who was secretly a Dark Lord of the Sith. He then took his wife into hiding on the planet Rhamalai. Living their lives as best they could while evading attention from the Jedi hunting Galactic Empire, Charis Enasteri gave birth to a daughter, Nadra. In order to ensure his wife and daughter's safety, Enasteri fled the world, leaving his loved ones behind forever.
