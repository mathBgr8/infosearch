Chun
1374

Chun was an individual who formulated Chun's Theory of Reductional Infinity. The theory had not been solved by 20 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of MedStar I: Battle Surgeons to 20 BBY. During that year, the underemployed protocol droid I-5YQ considered using the free space of his cognitive module in solving the formula. Chun was first mentioned in MedStar I: Battle Surgeons, the first novel of the MedStar Duology.
