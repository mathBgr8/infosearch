Gonster
1151

Gonster was a member of a sapient, humanoid species with large, pointed ears, and two heads with wide mouths. He lived in the Twin Hills of the forest moon of Endor with his pet hoosha. The two-headed Gonster used dried wank's hide in the creation of the magic cap, a magical hat that was used to trap the Raich in the form of a harmless tree. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Endorians Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males
