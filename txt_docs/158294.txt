Tash Scrambas
1053

Tash Scrambas was the father of Pello Scrambas. Scrambas served as Captain aboard the frigate Qel-Droma. During the Alderaan Ascendancy Contention, he refused to take any sides and cooperated with Jorus C'baoth in negotiating a truce. The day the crisis ended, Scrambas's 15-year-old son, Pello, joined the Alderaanian armed forces. Category:Alderaanians Category:Humans Category:Males
