Dendis Dobo
1724

Dendis Dobo was a Duros merchant encountered by Meetra Surik on Citadel Station over the planet Telos IV. Dendis co-owned the Dobo Brothers' Emporium with his brother, Samhan; the two shared a module but often disagreed over the ethics of their business. Dendis preferred not to sell weapons and was doing only fully legal business.
