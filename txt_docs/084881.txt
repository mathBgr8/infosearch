|expansions= |timeline= }} Star Wars: Shatterpoint is a miniatures skirmish game released by Atomic Mass Games under Asmodee on June 2, 2023 The game features characters and factions of the Clone Wars and Galactic Civil War eras, specifically grouping them as squad leaders, second-in-commands, and troops. The game started with the release of its Core Set.
