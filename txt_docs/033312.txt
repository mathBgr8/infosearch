Warrick spoke out against the show, decrying it as "no place for a warrior to be seen." thumb|left|250px|Three Tumble Bunnies and their trainer The Tumble Bunny trainer was of a humanoid species. They were green in color, though pink at the neck and eyes; they also had a bill. The trainer had a show in which small animals called Tumble Bunnies were made to perform tricks for a live audience. One such stunt involved having the Tumble Bunnies jump through a hoop held up by the Tumble Bunny trainer. In 3 ABY, the Tumble Bunny trainer performed their show at Bright Tree Village on the Forest Moon of Endor, as a part of a festival in the village. While performing, the trainer's show gathered an audience of woklings. These young Ewoks enjoyed the show, and the Tumble Bunny trainer called out to the other Ewoks at the fête, hoping to attract a larger audience. Word of their act reached fellow fête participant Shodu Warrick, who sent her son, Wicket, and daughter, the wokling Winda, off to see the show. Upon arriving at the Tumble Bunny trainer's exhibit, however, Wicket scorned the Tumble Bunny trainer's performance and announced that an aspiring warrior such as himself should not be seen attending such a display. Accordingly, he and his friends loudly decided to seek other forms of entertainment. Later, as punishment for not suitably looking after the woklings under his care, Wicket was forced to emulate the Tumble Bunny trainer's act, even going so far as to wear the same Tumble Bunny-resembling attire while doing so. When performing, the Tumble Bunny trainer displayed enthusiasm, jovially interacting with their audience, from whom they garnered positive responses. For attire while performing, the Tumble Bunny trainer dressed in a yellow suit that featured the three ears of the Tumble Bunnies, imitating, to a degree, the centerpieces of their show. The Tumble Bunny trainer appeared in an episode of the animated series Ewoks entitled "Horville's Hut of Horrors," which was written by Paul Dini.
