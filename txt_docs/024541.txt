Nixor Spaceport
1160

Nixor Spaceport orbited around the Nixor system around the time of the Galactic Empire. It was most commonly described as a disorganized mess due to the longstanding feud between the Nixors and the rest of the system. Due to their stubborn attitude, the Nixors refused to upgrade the spaceport as necessary or even give it the most basic of repairs. Due to its location, it was always crowded, which caused many pilots to avoid it. However, this was to the advantage of Ferus Olin and Roan Lands. The two chose this as one of many places where they could meet each other in secret, and it was here that the two met up once again.
