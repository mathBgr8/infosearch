Trisha Biggar
9121

Trisha Biggar is a costume designer who led the costume design departments for all three Star Wars prequel trilogy films, including the lavish wardrobe of Queen-turned-Senator Padmé Amidala, Jedi robes, Jango Fett's armor, Beru Whitesun and Owen Lars, and the Tusken Raiders for 2002's Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, which Biggar considers her most challenging project.
