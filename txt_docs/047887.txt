Revenant (Mere)
1523

The Revenant was the pirate ship of Sol Sixxa. It was equipped with a prototype cloaking device by Raith Sienar similar to that used on the Scimitar. Nym rammed the ship with the Sunrunner, sinking both vessels and destroying many B1-series battle droids. *Starfighter: Crossbones 2 *Starfighter: Crossbones 3 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Aquatic vehicles
