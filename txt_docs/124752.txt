The Fortune Seeker was a Ryn starship associated with the Ryn Network during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The Ryn used it to move about the galaxy and gather information. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ryn culture Category:Starships
