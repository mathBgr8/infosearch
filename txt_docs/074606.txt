Coronet Capitol Tower
2441

The Coronet Capitol Tower was the building situated in Coronet City that was home to the leaders of the Corellia government. thumb|left|250px|The [[Millennium Falcon/Legends|Millennium Falcon and a Lambda-class shuttle escape the destroyed Capitol Tower.]] During the Battle of Corellia around 6 months ABY, Rebel Alliance General Carlist Rieekan was met inside the Tower. The Galactic Empire attacked the city to prevent Crix Madine's defection, forcing the evacuation of the Tower. A shuttle docked on top, along with the Millennium Falcon. Just after the evacuation was finished, two TIE bombers launched their payload of proton bombs on the structure, leveling it.
