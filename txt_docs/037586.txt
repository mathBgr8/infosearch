Deadly laigrek
1288

The deadly laigrek was a subspecies of laigrek that was native to the grassy world of Dantooine. They were similar in appearance to their insect brethren, however they possessed tougher armor that could endure high temperatures, as well as the ability to breathe short streams of fire.
