Sucharme
2580

Sucharme was a planet affiliated with the Trade Federation prior to the Invasion of Naboo. The Federation's primary garrison, Tallaga Garrison, was located in the city of Tallaga. When the government of Sucharme failed to pay an outstanding debt, the Trade Federation blockaded the planet and launched an invasion on the pretext of forcing the natives to repay their debt. However, the local armed forces, including Major Veril Acamma, opposed the Federation invasion, and the would-be occupation force was ousted from Sucharme.
