X1 carbine
2173

The X1 carbine was a disruptor carbine weapon produced by the Wookiees. A variant of this weapon called the Malevolent bowcaster also existed. Non-Wookiee spacers were known to obtain modified versions of the weapon from the Avatar Orbital Platform. This weapon was used during the Battle of Kashyyyk to defend the tree-city of Kachirho. The X1 was a weapon mass-produced in workshops across Kashyyyk. The ornamental weapon featured an elongated barrel, and a ring-grip was particular to southern hemisphere tree-cities.
