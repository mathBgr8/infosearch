Arraxx
674

Arraxx was a pilot who flew a Z-95 Headhunter in the Eskaron races for Nirama. In 32 BBY, during one race in which Arraxx was winning, Arraxx's path crossed with that of Gael Kinner, who was flying in a separate private race. To avoid a collision, Kinner blasted Arraxx's Z-95 Headhunter into a cloud of debris. This killed Arraxx and earned Kinner Nirama's wrath.
