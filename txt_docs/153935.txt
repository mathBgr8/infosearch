Intentions
2545

Intentions was a section of Alliance Intelligence. It interpreted and analyzed all information gathered by Alliance Intelligence and attempted to predict the intentions and moves of the Galactic Empire. Intentions agents required a keen eye for detail, endless patience, and the ability to see patterns and draw conclusions from incomplete data. Intentions agents were characterized as pale and skinny, with twitches, stooped shoulders, and google-eyes from staring at computer screens in poorly-lit conditions. Commander Zgorth'sth was the Senior Officer of Intentions division around 2 ABY.
