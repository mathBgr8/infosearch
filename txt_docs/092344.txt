|pages=80 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |issue=7 }} Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 7 is the seventh volume in the Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels series. Explosive action, white-hot lightsaber battles, evil schemes, and heroic sacrifices—it's all here in this fast-paced volume jam-packed with four wickedly awesome all-ages adventures set in the most exciting era of Star Wars! The fate of the galaxy hangs in the balance, the Republic and Confederacy taking their fight from the cold reaches of space to exotic alien worlds.
