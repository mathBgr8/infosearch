Boba Fett vs. The Empire
624

Boba Fett vs. The Empire was a proposed comic to be written by Tom Veitch. Its plot would have been centered around Boba Fett being hired to apprehend Umak Leth after the latter betrays the Galactic Empire. Category:Dark Horse Comics one-shot comics
