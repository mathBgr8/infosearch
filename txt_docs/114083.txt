Jaratt
1546

Jarrat was a male Human and an Imperial officer serving with Black Sword Command. He was captured by the Yevetha in 4 ABY. After more than a decade of captivity, however, Jarrat had become one of the leaders of Major Sil Sorannan's plot to liberate the remaining Imperial personnel in the Koornacht Cluster and return the Duskhan League's fleet of captured Star Destroyers to Imperial control.
