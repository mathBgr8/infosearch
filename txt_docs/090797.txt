Alderaan Museum of Fine Arts
1247

The Alderaan Museum of Fine Arts was a museum located within the Core District of the city of Cinnagar. Like many of the attractions within the Core District, the museum's architecture represented a specific planet within the Core Worlds region of the galaxy; specifically Alderaan.
