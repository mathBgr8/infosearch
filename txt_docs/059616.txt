Corsair-class cruiser
3591

The Corsair-class cruiser was an 18-meter heavy assault starfighter built by SoroSuub. It was armed with two turbolaser batteries and a pair of ion cannons. It was used by the Cavrilhu Pirates, Dharus’ Buccaneers, as well as by the pirate Rei'Kas. Five of these starfighters were used by the Cavrilhu Pirates to attack Iphigin just before the Caamas Document crisis struck the New Republic, proving to be rather well-defended in terms of shield and armor.Specter of the Past Seven Corsairs would attempt to ambush the Wild Karrde at Kathol, before being driven off by the modified freighter. Eighteen would later be destroyed at Exocron. The only statistics available for the Corsair-class cruiser come from Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim in reference to the Red Lancer and the Blue Queen, which are both identified as heavily modified.
