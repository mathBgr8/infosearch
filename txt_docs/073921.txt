New Republic Trade Council
536

The New Republic Trade Council was a sub-committee of the New Republic Senate, comprised of businessbeings and New Republic officials. The goal of the New Republic Trade Council was to maintain trade and commerce in the galaxy, and ensure that ventures remained open and free. *Young Jedi Knights: Shards of Alderaan Category:New Republic Senate organizations
