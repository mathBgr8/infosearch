Kresch
1604

The Kresch was a semi-sentient mollusk native to Naboo. It contained information about nearly every aspect of Gungan civilization, and when opened, its blood would form pictures and words on its skin. Because of this, the Gungans used the Kresch as an encyclopedia of sorts.
