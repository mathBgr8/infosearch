Glitterstar
737

Glitterstar was a popular luxury cruise liner. During the aftermath of the Emperor's final defeat, Glitterstar was ambushed by Phan Riizolo and the Invid pirates aboard the Booty Full just off Alakatha. Thanks to a tip-off, Rogue Squadron was present to drive off the pirates, and Glitterstar was escorted back to Coruscant by Home One. *I, Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Luxury liners
