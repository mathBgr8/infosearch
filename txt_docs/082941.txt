Gidoo Zehow
1738

}} Doctor Gidoo Zehow was a male physician who worked at the Rhinnal State Medical Academy shortly before the commencement of the Clone Wars. In an interview with HoloNet News, he attacked the Columi claims that their homeworld's low gravity had health benefits for the overweight. He said that the decreased muscle strain would in fact worsen weight conditions by limiting physical exertion. Instead, he offered, those struggling with weight should visit high-gravity worlds with predatory fauna to encourage exercise.
