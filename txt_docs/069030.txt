Skylight Squadron
1097

Skylight Squadron was a unit of modified, shield-equipped TIE/LN starfighters assigned to the flight complement of the New Republic Star Destroyer Allegiance. This unit fought in the Battle of Adumar twelve years after the Battle of Yavin, and were part of the force that attacked the TIE Defenders of the 181st Imperial Fighter Wing. Unfortunately, the units own TIEs were no match for the Defenders of the Imperial invasion fleet. It took General Wedge Antilles' Red Flight to successfully engage and fight off the Defender unit. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:New Republic starfighter squadrons
