VXI-2c electrotelescope
1194

The VXI-2c electrotelescope was an electrotelescope manufactured by the Neuro-Saav Corporation during the Galactic Civil War. Bolvan and Hija used it to pick up the escape pod that R2-D2 and C-3PO were in. Category:Neuro-Saav Corporation products Category:Scopes Category:Starship sensors
