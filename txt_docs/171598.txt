Stallion

Stallion was an Imperial CR90 corvette during the Galactic Civil War. As the Battle on the Frontier drew to a close, Corvette Stallion escorted Nebulon-B frigate Gaarni to replace the battle-weary Nebulon-B2 frigate Ludwick. The Stallion's task force arrived to relieve Grand Admiral Thrawn's initial invasion force sent to establish Space Station NL-1 in the Pakuuni system. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
