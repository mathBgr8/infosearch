Planore
2193

Planore was an individual who, in 3951 BBY,Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide offered to arrange transport off the moon Nar Shaddaa for the Human Kahranna and her family. He charged Kahranna fifty credits for a referral to his pilot friend, whom he claimed would take the family off world for 500 credits. However, Planore never came through with his offer of assistance. During that time, Kahranna was forced to rely on Meetra SurikThe Old Republic: Revan&mdash;a former Jedi Knight otherwise known as the Jedi Exile&mdash;for help. After hearing the Human's story, Surik expressed that Planore may have scammed her; Kahranna agreed, noting she should not have trusted him.
