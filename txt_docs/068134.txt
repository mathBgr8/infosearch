Kimar Walc
1563

}} Kimar Walc was a male Arcona from Cona. He was Nest Leader of the Grand Nest and had a son named Denn. When Jedi Knights Tassida Judrelle, Quarmall and Stass Allie discovered and penetrated the inner circle of Lojrak Shrag's illegal salt smuggling cartel on the Cona, Kimar Walc publicly expressed his gratitude to the Jedi on the Holonet. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Arcona Category:Males
