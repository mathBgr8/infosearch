Sith Academy (Honoghr)
1487

The Sith academy of Honoghr was established by the Brotherhood of Darkness, and was active near the end of the New Sith Wars. They used it to train Sith Warriors and Marauders. Here these warriors would learn to focus their emotions into a raging battle fury.
