Mark Brooks
6390

Mark Brooks is an artist who contributed sketch cards for the Star Wars: 30th Anniversary Trading Card Set. He currently works as a comic book artist for Marvel Comics, where he provided artwork for the Star Wars: Han Solo miniseries. George Lucas expressed an interest in purchasing Brooks' original art pages for the first and second issues of the series. He also created the cover art for eleven issues of Star Wars: Kanan and all five issues of the Star Wars: Vader Down crossover. Brooks has also drawn various other Marvel series, including Dark Reign: Young Avengers, Ultimate X-Men, and Ultimate Fantastic Four.
