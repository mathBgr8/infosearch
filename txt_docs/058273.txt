Taris Holofeed
557

The Taris Holofeed was one of the primary news sources on the planet Taris during the time of the Mandalorian Wars. It included planetary, as well as galactic news. It was based in a Highpoint office destroyed during the invasion of Taris. Category:HoloNet technology Category:News publications
