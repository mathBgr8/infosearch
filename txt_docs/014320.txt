Rella
1641

Rella was a Human female with short brown hair. She was the right-hand-woman, as well as lover, of Arwen Cohl. She was killed in a skirmish on Eriadu when the Nebula Front, by order of Eru Matalis (who at the time went by the alias Havac), turned against Cohl at the Eriadu Trade Summit in 33 BBY.
