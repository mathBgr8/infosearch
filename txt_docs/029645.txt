Jain Havero
2324

Jain Havero was a male Calian warrior and a comrade of powerful warlord Aron Peacebringer on the remote planet Shiva IV during the era of the Galactic Civil War. A boastful young man, Havero ranked among the best fighters on Shiva IV by 3 ABY, although he had such prowess in battle that he sometimes became careless.The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia In 3 ABY Havero&mdash;while investigating the fate of the city of K'avor with Peacebringer, Kéral Longknife and Ygal Delois&mdash;discovered a group of outcast cutthroats menacing Leia Organa, a Rebel leader who had crash-landed on Shiva IV. Havero and his fellows fought the bandits off and rescued Organa, who amazed Havero, who had seen precious few offworlders in his life.
