They were loners and not particularly aggressive in the wild. Their body was covered by reddish brown, leathery hide and consisted of a lower, arachnid part and a humanoid torso sitting on top of it. The four hind limbs were connected to the lower arachnid part and were used exclusively for locomotion. The front pair of limbs terminated with claws and were used for attacks.
