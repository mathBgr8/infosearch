Sloogarian
1189

Sloogarians were slimy, slug-like sapients from the Sloogaria system. They were known to enjoy eating bantha droppings and other treats that most species usually found disgusting. They were also known to stretch the truth for profit. Squishmael was a member of the species; he told the supposedly true story of Han Solo and the Kessel Run in exchange for a few drinks, and then offered to tell about his "good buddy" Darth Vader's Tauntaun-hunting expedition on Hoth.
