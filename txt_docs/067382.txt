Mawz Dakko
1448

Mawz Dakko was a male Gran from the planet Malastare, living during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he worked for the Dug crimelord Sebolto, and owed the Hutts a large sum of money for hijacking spice shipments. When a bounty was placed on his head, he was killed by Jango Fett who claimed the bounty.
