Kalarba Safari
875

thumb|250px|The [[Tharen Wayfarer, owned and operated by Kalarba Safari.]] Kalarba Safari was a company owned by Jarth and Meg Pitareeze that provided touring services to the Ancestral Lands of Kalarba. The tour, known as "Journey of the Spirit," took place aboard the Tharen Wayfarer, taking visitors to Kalarba past the Great Sea and Three Peaks of Tharen. While on the voyage, droids C-3PO and R2-D2 catered to guests' needs and provided food such as Stenness pie. *Star Wars: Droids Special *Droids (1994) 2 Category:Transport companies
