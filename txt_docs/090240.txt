Mutant acklay
2549

The Mutant Acklay was a variety of the amphibious acklay that had been mutated by the toxins of Golbah's Pit on Geonosis. Many lived under the waters of the Ebon Sea, where they hunted creatures within the sea and surfaced to attack prey on land. Mutant acklays were hardier than standard acklays, and had heavier claws. Their shells were a glossy black.
