Clone trooper learning helmet
2245

thumb|250px Clone trooper learning helmets were special helmets worn by maturing clone troopers grown and trained on Kamino prior to and during the Clone Wars. Learning helmets were designed in two variants: odd-class and even-class, distinguished by alternating patterns of maroon-and-cream, and plating of gold and black respectively. Cadets with even designation number wore helmets with black plating and maroon-cream-maroon pattern while odd numbered cadets wore helmets with gold plating and cream-maroon-cream pattern. These helmets were fitted with mental receptivity enhancers, to help the clones process the massive amounts of data they received daily. Broadcast signal receivers allowed Kaminoan overseers to transmit data directly into the helmets, which were synchronized to the wearer's biorhythm. Side-mounted earphones served to silence external sounds, providing an isolated audio environment in which the clone could concentrate on his learning.
