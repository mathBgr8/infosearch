Nimba Five
1238

Nimba Five was a planet in the Nimba system in Wild Space with which the hunter Sam Heggs was familiar. According to Heggs, Nimba Five had a time warp, with one time tropical and the other frozen in ice. It was located on the same hyperroute as Beta Rhama. *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in Wild Space Category:Terrestrial planets
