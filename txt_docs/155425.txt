Fest Resistance Group
1118

The Fest Resistance Group was a rebel organization which fought against the Imperial forces in the Atrivis sector. The organization's founder was Travia Chan. The organization gained fame during the Galactic Civil War as it became one of the first resistance groups to successfully fight local Imperial forces. The Fest rebels joined the Mantooine Liberators in forming the Atrivis Resistance Group, a military force that joined the Rebel Alliance and changed its name to the Atrivis Sector Force. *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook Category:Resistance groups
