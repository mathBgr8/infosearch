43rd Salagori Lancers
1763

The 43rd Salagori Lancers was a unit of the military of the Galactic Empire that was active during the Galactic Civil War and shared its name with the Inner Rim's Salagor system. The notable unit repeatedly distinguished itself while carrying out special operations. The 43rd Salagori Lancers was mentioned in the 1995 sourcebook Heroes & Rogues, authored by Rick Stuart and Paul Sudlow and published for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The book gives the unit as an example of a special operations unit that player characters in a Galactic Empire military campaign can be assigned to based on their record of renowned deeds, noting that serving with the 43rd Salagori Lancers would lead to the characters experiencing a lot of action. *Heroes & Rogues Category:Imperial military units
