Anywhere Room
868

The Anywhere Room was a popular tourist attraction, and the most popular of Hologram Fun World's features. The small building contained holograms of every known planet and could take visitors to holographic representations of places such as Alderaan, Sittana, and the Emperor's throne room. *Galaxy of Fear: The Nightmare Machine *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Outer Rim Territories structures Category:Rooms
