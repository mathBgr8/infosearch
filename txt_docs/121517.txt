Azurite Society of Lords
824

The Azurite Society of Lords was a secret society in the New Republic era. They were presumably named for the mineral azurite. The Society was watched by Jan Ors, in her capacity as an agent for New Republic Intelligence. They were considered an ambiguous threat to the New Republic, comparable to the Order of the Canted Circle.' Category:Secret societies
