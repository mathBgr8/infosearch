Challenge 38
1938

Challenge 38 was a roleplaying magazine from June/July of 1989. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called Starfighters Down and was written by William W. Connors. {|class="sortable" !Title !! Author(s) !!
