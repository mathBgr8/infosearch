Vedis IV
2439

}} Vedis IV was a planet located in the Vedis system, which was part of the Mayagil sector in the Outer Rim Territories of the galaxy. Around 3 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion the smuggler Toob Ancher was known to have left Soco-Jarel Spaceport with the YT-1300 transport Glory to reach the planet. Vedis IV was also where another smuggler named Lu Esi died when he crashed his freighter into the planet while being chased by sector authorities. Vedis IV was first mentioned in the May 1, 1994's short story Out of the Cradle, written by Patricia A. Jackson and published within Star Wars Adventure Journal 2.
