Dall Thara Dru
1423

Dall Thara Dru was a female Human Senator of the New Republic who represented Raxxa. During the Black Fleet Crisis, she was Chair of the Commerce Council, and was the only female member of the Ruling Council at the time. She supported Chief of State Leia Organa Solo when Doman Beruss submitted a Vote of No Confidence against her, but this was mostly because she believed Luke Skywalker and the New Jedi Order were planning to intervene on the side of the New Republic. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:New Republic Senators
