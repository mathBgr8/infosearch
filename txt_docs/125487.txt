Gep's Grill
2202

Gep's Grill was a location at Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was a stall at the market place where grilled Dewback and Bantha meat was offered at fair prices. It was owned by two Whiphids: Fillin Ta and Norun Gep. The menu was simple, but adequate for the people who visited the place.
