Construction droid Mark II
979

The construction droid Mark II was a droid model used c. 3,951 BBY. It was a huge biped, stronger than the construction droid Mark I or construction droid foreman, but far less dexterous. It was used for heavy lifting that the smaller droids couldn't accomplish. A typical construction team consisted of one foreman, one Mark II, and multiple Mark I droids. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Construction droid models
