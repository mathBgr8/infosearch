B-12 Bacta Transport
3577

The B-12 bacta transport was manufactured by Kuat Drive Yards specifically to transport bacta. thumb|left|150px|B-12 Bacta Transport forward Deck Plan The B-12 was a 238-meter long starship dedicated to bacta transport. Its hulls was a spinoff of the Lancer-class frigate also produced by Kuat Drive Yards. The ship carried 26 crew, 6 passengers, and 600,000 metric tons of bacta in sixteen pressurized spheres with controlled temperature to keep the bacta from spoiling. The B-12 bacta transport was commissioned by the Galactic Empire after the Battle of Yavin to transport safely bacta. It soon became one of the most popular bacta transport class. Sometime before 1 ABY, a B-12 transport, the Suveron, was destroyed in the Endor system, releasing half million tons of bacta in space.
