|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Jimmy Smits (born July 9, 1955) is an actor who portrayed Senator Bail Organa in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones and Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith as well as in the Legends video game Star Wars: The Force Unleashed. He also reprised his role as Senator Organa in the Anthology film Rogue One: A Star Wars Story and in the limited series Star Wars: Obi-Wan Kenobi. Tony Gilroy and the team behind Star Wars: Andor worked hard to get Smits to return, but the actor was busy with another television project and thus, in the end, could not appear.
