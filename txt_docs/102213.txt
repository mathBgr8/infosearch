Corulag (bulk cruiser)
848

Corulag was a bulk cruiser of the New Republic Defense Fleet during the early years of the New Republic. Modified for assault, Corulag participated in the First Battle of Borleias, where, serving as a platform of Defender Wing, it launched the eight Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports that participated in the battle. *X-Wing: Rogue Squadron Category:Bulk cruisers Category:Starships of the New Republic
