Reaper (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
1720

The Reaper was a Nebulon-B frigate that served in the Imperial Starfleet at the height of the Galactic Civil War. The Reaper arrived after frigate Rapier was destroyed as transport group Renhoek picked up a supply of lonsigars from a group of abandoned containers.
