Mount Ganton
872

Mount Ganton was a mountain in the northern continent of the planet Shalm. A steep cliff on its western side was the site of an Imperial garrison that was later abandoned and used as a base of operations by Pendarran's Revenge. It overlooked the valley containing the capital city of Hondan. *Wanted by Cracken Ganton Category:Shalm locations
