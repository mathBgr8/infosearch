|diameter= |atmosphere= |climate= |gravity= |terrain= |water= |interest= |flora=Hemmel |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Delle II was a terrestrial celestial body located in the Colonies' Delle system, which contained massive farm worlds. It was situated between the Neuvia and Yabol Opa systems and the Core Worlds' Ralltiir system on the Perlemian Trade Route super-hyperroute, which served as the dividing line between the Northern Dependencies and the Slice. During the reign of the Galactic Empire, the type of crop known as hemmel was grown on Delle II. At one point, hemmel farming experienced a bad year, a fact that was reported on a NewsNet network and eventually mentioned by the spacer Gabriel to her partner, Danna. Delle II was mentioned in the "Galaxywide NewsNets" feature of the third issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine, which was published in August 1994. The feature was authored by Paul Sudlow. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Delle system, and therefore Delle II, in grid square L-9.
