Transsector Lines Ltd.
812

Transsector Lines Ltd. was an independent company that specialized in transporting cargo between different sectors. It was based on the planet of Elrood where their company's headquarters was located in the city of Dinbar, but they also maintained service locations in a variety of locations including the Perma of the Elrood Bazaar. The people of Elrood depended on the company for transport of goods from the planet of Torina, as the company made regular runs through this route. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Transport companies
