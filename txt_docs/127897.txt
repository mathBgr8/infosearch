Endeavor (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
1031

The Endeavor was a Rebel EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate that challenged the Imperial frigate Malevolent to an evenly matched duel. While the occurrence of the space battle depicted in this mission, as well as the participants, was C-canon within the Legends continuity, the outcome depends on the actions of the player and is therefore indeterminate.
