Swift Dreams
1521

Swift Dreams was one of the six hundred captured refugee ships that the Yuuzhan Vong war fleet used as shields against the defending New Republic forces and as ramships against the capital's planetary shields during the opening stages of the Battle of Coruscant. It was destroyed by warships under the command of General Garm Bel Iblis. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:Captured starships Category:Transports
