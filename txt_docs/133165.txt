Brak Dunell
1452

Brak Dunell was a member of the Imperial Security Bureau. He used the alias Bel Janius when he posed as a Mrlsst Trade and Science Academy graduate and was the anonymous benefactor of the Pan-Tapani Historical Society. He infiltrated the Justice Action Network terrorist group, during the Galactic Civil War.
