Plasma-Jet Mole Miner
6097

Slayn & Korpil Plasma-Jet Mole Miners (also called drill-jets or mole miners) were large mining devices that could be used in vacuum. Lando Calrissian used mole miners when he created Nomad City on Nkllon. The craft were designed as small cones with their points chopped off. They had concentric rings of plasma jets along the bottom of the vehicle. Once in position, the jets would carve out a concave work area. On-board ore sensors would filter out the most valuable material which was stored in storage bins via vacuum shafts and grinders. They could either be operated by an on-board crew or remotely via a integrated slave circuit. Mole miners were specially designed to work in harsh environments like space, asteroids, or planets with no atmosphere. As droid miners became more capable, the use of manned mole miners was diminished. They were used for small mining operations rather than bulk mining due to their lack of storage space. During his campaign against the New Republic, Grand Admiral Thrawn stole a complement of mole miners to be used at the subsequent Battle of Sluis Van, where they were used to attack the New Republic Defense Fleet stationed at the Sluis Van shipyards. Thrawn used these mining vessels as makeshift boarding craft, to drill into New Republic capital ships, and deliver boarding parties of spacetroopers and stormtroopers. As most of the ships were undergoing repairs, they only had skeleton crews, meaning that the small number of troops who could fit into the miners could easily take them over. This combined with the surprise of the unconventional use made the tactic highly effective. However, the plan to steal the ships failed when Han Solo remembered that the mole miners had been remotely controlled and realized that the Imperials were not blocking comms. Han had Lando use his override codes to order the all of the miners to simultaneously activate their drills. This depressurized the ship's bridges, killing most of the standard stormtroopers, while New Republic starfighters killed the spacetroopers. Although this further damaged the ships, it did prevent their loss to the Imperial Remnant.
