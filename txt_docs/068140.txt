Luka d'al' Gargan
2351

|masters= |apprentices= }} Luka d'al' Gargan was the daughter of Nautag and Yarna d'al' Gargan, and sister of Leia d'al' Gargan and Nautag d'al' Gargan II. She was named after Luke Skywalker in honor of the fact that he was the ringleader of the Rebel team that overthrew and killed Jabba. Luka and her family were taken to Tatooine as slaves in about 3 ABY. Her mother and father were bought by Jabba Desilijic Tiure. However, Luka's mother, Yarna, with the help of Doallyn, was able to buy Luka and the other cubs after escaping from Jabba's Palace.
