It was the homeworld of the Human Viran Qol. At some point by 3 ABY, features an in-universe mention of the Battle of Hoth, which is dated to 3 ABY by The New Essential Chronology. Qol was enrolled in a civil engineering program on Niran. The day after his graduation, Niran was invaded by the Galactic Empire. Qol became involved with the Niran Resistance less than a week later and eventually became a major with the Rebel Alliance Special Forces. Niran was first mentioned in "Imperial Garrisons," a 1997 roleplaying game source article written by Timothy S. O'Brien and published in the twelfth issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine.
