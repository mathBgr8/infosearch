Lightstaff
1349

250px|thumb|[[Qrrrl Toq with his lightstaff.]] A lightstaff was a melee weapon used by the Jedi during the Great Sith War and the period leading up to it. It consisted of an armored wooden stave imbued with the Force. A famed Jedi Knight of that age, Qrrrl Toq, wielded a lightstaff.
