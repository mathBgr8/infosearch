Dream flower potion
948

thumb|right|200px|[[Wicket Wystri Warrick/Legends|Wicket Wystri Warrick holds a vial of dream flower potion.]] Dream flower potion was a strong medicine found on the Forest Moon of Endor. It was a powerful sedative that could put even the largest organisms into a deep sleep. Gonster of the Twin Hills was known to possess a batch of this potion, kept alongside its other ingredients. The Ewoks Wicket Wystri Warrick and Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka obtained a vial of Dream flower potion for use on the Raich during his temporary return. Unfortunately for the two Ewoks, Wicket inadvertently alerted the Raich to his presence while trying to place the potion in his water bowl, and the potion was lost in the process. Category:Potions
