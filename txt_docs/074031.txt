Koruun
1891

Koruun, also known as Korunni or Korunnal, was the language of the Korunnai people of the planet Haruun Kal. After Mace Windu was taken from his homeworld of Haruun Kal at a very young age, he returned to the planet as a teenager long enough to learn the language and experience the culture.
