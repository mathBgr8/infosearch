Unidentified Clan
1351

A Jedi Initiate Clan was slaughtered by Darth Vader during his Raid on the Jedi Temple in 19 BBY. As the clone troopers of the 501st Legion stormed the Jedi Temple, they took refuge in the High Council Tower. When Vader found them, they believed that he was attempting to protect them, asking what they should do. The newly-minted Sith Lord, however, killed them all. Sors Bandeam and Jeswi Ele were members.
