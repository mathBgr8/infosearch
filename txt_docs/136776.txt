Sidi Jemour
1106

thumb|250px|Map of Djerba Sidi Jemour (Arabic: سيدى جمور) is a marabout located on the west coast of Djerba. Two scenes from A New Hope were filmed there: a deleted exterior sequence for Anchorhead, and a shot where Luke and Obi-Wan drive into Mos Eisley. * by Mark Dermul * by Gus Lopez and Pamela Gren * by Brady Walters Category:Filming locations
