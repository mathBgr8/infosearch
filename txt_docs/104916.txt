Breath of Heaven
1352

Breath of Heaven was an expensive and exceedingly strong alcoholic beverage. One of its ingredients was rice. Breath of Heaven appeared in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. *Children of the Jedi *Star Wars Galaxies: The Total Experience: Prima Official Game Guide *Star Wars Galaxies: The Complete Guide: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Alcoholic beverages
