Curdik
1681

Curdik was a male Whiphid swordsman. Born mute on Toola, Curdik learned to communicate through his weapons and his datapad. In the years after the formation of the Galactic Empire, Curdik worked as a scout for off-world hunters that visited Toola. Bored with serving tourists, Curdik left his homeworld and traveled the galaxy. Eventually he made his way to Zuliria where the Arcona Ther-das noticed that he was a great swordsbeing and invited him to join the Zulirian Swordmasters.
