It was a major source of meat and animal-based textiles for the Colonies and Core Worlds. Roaming herds of livestock included banthas and nerfs. Ycaqt were used for transportation. Its sparsely-populated rolling prairies were home to bandit hideaways and tourist-oriented ranches, where livestock had to be guarded against indigenous plains panthers.
