In 1 ABY, he was present at Tansarii Point Station, a space station located near the planet Ord Mantell, in the Bright Jewel system. During that time, he frequented the station's cantina with his friend Mot Draken. Draken had fallen deeply in love with O'choa Detera, a dancer performing in the cantina, but the woman instead had a crush on Bun'Del. Bun'Del was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Bun'Del was added to the game as part of the "New Player Tutorial," introduced with the release of the "Publish 25," a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005.
