Coruscant Customs Bureau
1754

The Coruscant Customs Bureau was the customs enforcement service of the planet Coruscant during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. The Customs Bureau would inspect arriving and departing ships for irregularities in manifests and cargo. They would also document arriving and departing persons, the collection of duty fees, issue licenses for imports, and enforce customs trade laws. In 32 BBY Sith Lord Darth Maul entered a Coruscant Customs Bureau building to do a search on Neimoidians who had recently entered Coruscant.
