Thunderer (Imperial-class)
1819

The Thunderer was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer which served under rogue Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin. She helped destroy the Star Destroyer Courageous. Her fate following Zaarin's defeat is unknown.
