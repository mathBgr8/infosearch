A loyal Imperial soldier, Kreigg earned Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin's favor for his cunning during a Rebel attack on the Moff's flagship. Kreigg had brown hair and light-colored skin. Commander Kreigg first appeared on a card for the Premiere set of Decipher's 2001 Jedi Knights Trading Card Game.
