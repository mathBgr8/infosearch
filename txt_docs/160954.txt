Michael Manley
1975

Michael Manley, also known as Mike Manley, is an artist who illustrated two of the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game supplements, as well as Lightsaber Dueling Pack. He also composed the sketches for Tales of the Bounty Hunters; and along with Al Williamson and Aaron McClellan, he provided the sketches for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina and Tales from Jabba's Palace. *Lightsaber Dueling Pack *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope *Imperial Sourcebook *Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley *The Movie Trilogy Sourcebook *Imperial Sourcebook, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition *Galaxy Guide 1: A New Hope, Second Edition *Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina *Star Wars Gamemaster Screen, Revised *Tales from Jabba's Palace *Galaxy Guide 2: Yavin and Bespin, Second Edition *Tales of the Bounty Hunters *Classic Adventures: Volume Three *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids Category:Roleplaying game artists
