Authority Cash Voucher
1162

An Authority Cash Voucher was a debt-receipt used in the Corporate Sector Authority in the years directly preceding the Galactic Civil War. The amount due to a being was magnetically entered into a plastic datacard that was used in lieu of currency within the CSA.
