Lodon Ocean
563

The Lodon Ocean was one of three major oceans on the planet Elrood. It was the northernmost ocean and was located on the edge of Elrood's largest continent. The city of Dinbar was located on the western bank of this body of water. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Elrood locations Category:Seas and oceans
