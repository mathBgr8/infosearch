Herlixx
1313

The Herlixx were omnivorous swamp creatures native to the planet Kasiol 3 who were comfortable on both land and in the water. They were 45 centimeters tall and stood on their rear stubby legs. Their long bodies allowed them to move through the trees and their obesity allowed them to float in the waters of Kasiol 3. Their diet consisted of a variety of fish, small amphibians, leaves and algae.
