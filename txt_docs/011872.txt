Malnic was once asked by a journalist from HoloNet News CoCo District Edition what the Rodian thought of the Jedi Order refusing to return the six-month-old Force-sensitive Human Ludi to her mother, Jonava Billane, in the "Baby Ludi" custody case. Malnic responded that it did not make sense why the media cared so much about the case when the Galactic Republic was falling apart due to the Separatist Crisis. The Rodian was so upset about the situation that Malnic swore while being interviewed. Wuuden had had black eyes and green skin, and on at least one occasion, wore red clothing. Wuuden Malnic first appeared in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #46, an issue of the representation of the in-universe HoloNet News, published on the HoloNet News website on March 7, 2002.Per , HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47 was released on March 14, 2002. As HoloNet News segments were published weekly, and was published in the issue immediately preceding the forty-seventh edition, it must have been released on March 7 of the same year. Malnic was created by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens, and they were pictured in an article entitled "Jedi Refuse to Hand Over Baby Ludi," which was illustrated by artist Joe Corroney.
