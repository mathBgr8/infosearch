Tensh Ly'alu
1559

. |masters= |apprentices= }} Tensh Ly'alu was a male Bothan Jedi Knight who served the New Jedi Order. His first assignment, upon reaching the rank of Jedi Knight, was to monitor the Twi'lek homeworld, Ryloth, both for Force-sensitives and to watch for Jedi interests on planet. He felt he could do it but after discovering that most Twi'leks communicated with their lekku, suddenly was floundering.
