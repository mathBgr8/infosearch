Regional Sector Number Four's All-Human Free-For-All
995

The Regional Sector Number Four's All-Human Free-For-All was a gladiator event held on Jubilar in 12 BBY. Han Solo won by defeating three larger combatants. Han also saw Boba Fett for the first time. The venue had moved on from the Victory Forum where it was held or was defunct by 19 ABY. *Boba Fett: Pursuit *The Paradise Snare *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Chronology *Aurra Sing: Dawn of the Bounty Hunters *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sporting events
