|plants=Cereal |other= |edible= |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Fried crispics, or crispics for short, were fried snack rolls commonly found in cantinas. Ingredients of fried crispic included meat for its meaty core, and cereal for its flaky crust. Crispics appeared in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies. It could be made by traders, a profession that specialized in the creation of various goods, including food.
