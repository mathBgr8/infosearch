Bacta Team
2238

Bacta Team and Aurek Team were military units tasked by Mace Windu with locating Darth Sidious on Coruscant. Windu put together what he considered a dream team that consisted of ARC commander Valiant, Captain Dyne of Republic Intelligence, Jedi Master Shaak Ti, several capable Padawans, and several clone commandos. The commandos reported directly to Valiant. At one point, Mace split up these teams with him taking Aurek, Dyne, and Valiant, and High Jedi General Shaak Ti taking Bacta. The search was focused in Sector 943, also known as "the Works". Bacta team was ultimately unsuccessful in locating Sidious just before the execution of Order 66. *Labyrinth of Evil Category:Galactic Republic intelligence organizations
