Khoan
1012

The Khoan were a species who, though shorter than Humans, weighed twice the average Wookiee. They had yellow skin, a dorsal fin, and spikes on their faces. These spikes fanned around the face, forming a spiked collar around their faces. Khoan imagined themselves as the fairest of all intergalactic races.
