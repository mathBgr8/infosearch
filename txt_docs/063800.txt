Zlece Oonaar
1792

Zlece Oonaar was a male Human who served as an officer in the Imperial Navy. During the Galactic Civil War he was captain of the Victory-class Star Destroyer Crusader, partaking the Battle of Khuiumin. In 11 ABY, Oonaar, having since left Imperial service, was a passenger on the Galaxy Chance when the Khuiumin Survivors raided the corvette to capture him and exact their revenge. After a show trial he was executed by being blown out of a decompression hatch into the vacuum of space.
