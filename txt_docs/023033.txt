Gesco City
788

Gesco City was a city on Bakura. Gesco City was first mentioned in the 1996 The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook, which was authored by Kathy Tyers and Eric S. Trautmann for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook Category:Cities on Bakura
