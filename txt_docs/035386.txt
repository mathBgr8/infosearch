Lucas Licensing
1156

Lucas Licensing is a wholly owned subsidiary of Lucasfilm responsible for licensing and merchandising activities related to Star Wars, Indiana Jones and other Lucasfilm properties. It is unclear if they are still active following The Walt Disney Company's acquisition of Lucasfilm. *The Secrets of Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire Category:Subsidiaries of Lucasfilm
