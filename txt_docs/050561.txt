Too Late Now
1893

Too Late Now was a captured and modified Imperial Customs Frigate that was part of Drea Renthal's pirate fleet that was present at the Battle of Nar Shaddaa. One of its laser cannon turrets was replaced with an ion cannon, allowing it to disable ships for capture. It was instrumental in helping to destroy the Carrack-class light cruiser Vigilance.
