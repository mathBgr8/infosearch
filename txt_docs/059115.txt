Wooter, Rimki, and Vass, Attorneys at Law
507

Wooter, Rimki, and Vass, Attorneys at Law was an unscrupulous law firm on the planet Commenor headed by Mem Wooter, along with Rimki and Vass. It was particularly wealthy and influential, and was chosen by Ysanne Isard to handle the the sale of a Xenovet Corporation facility for use in handling prisoners from the Lusankya. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge Category:Legal companies
