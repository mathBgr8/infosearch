Daya
680

The Daya was the name for the central grand plaza of the large Elrood Bazaar on the planet Elrood. The location served as a place where customers could mingle and move through the shops. The area was surrounded by a ring of permanent buildings known as the Perma. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Elrood locations
