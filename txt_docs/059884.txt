Four-Onebee
1844

Four-Onebee was the medical droid that Captain Ixsthmus used aboard the starship Long Shot. It performed its duties happily and was always eager to please. Its programming was also in very good condition. Its only complaint was that when it was not needed by the crew, it would usually be turned off and stored in a closet. *Star Wars Campaign Pack *Classic Campaigns Category:2-1B surgical droids Category:Reekeene's Roughnecks personnel
