Arhythmic resonance scheme
584

An arhythmic resonance scheme was a technique that involved running a positive flow regulator from a power cable into a negative flow coupler. The resulting explosion was considered quite destructive. Lando Calrissian and Chewbacca used an arhythmic resonance scheme to destroy the Mount Tantiss storehouse on Wayland. *The Last Command Category:Demolition technology
