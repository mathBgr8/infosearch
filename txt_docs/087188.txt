Orange Panthacs
1470

The Orange Panthacs were a privately-owned platoon of B2 super battle droids used during the Yuuzhan Vong War with the intention of slowing down the Yuuzhan Vong's frequent occupation of planets. On the world of Mantessa, the droids were able to fight off a force of Yuuzhan Vong Fire Breathers. Chief of State Cal Omas of the Galactic Alliance later awarded the Orange Panthacs a special commendation. The Orange Panthacs were first mentioned in the reference book The New Essential Guide to Droids, written by Daniel Wallace and released in 2006. *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galactic Alliance military units
