Grappler (starfighter)
1051

The Grappler was an organic ship created by the living planet Zonama Sekot during the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar as part of the "Fight Without Fighting" plan against the Yuuzhan Vong.The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force They resembled a maidenfly insect with green wings and red carapaces.
