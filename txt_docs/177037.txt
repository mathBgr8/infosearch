*Battle of Atraken |next=Mission to Nar Shaddaa |image= |name=Battle of Esseles |conflict=Clone Wars |date=21.6 BBY, 5 months after the Battle of Geonosis |place=Esseles |result=Republic victory |side1=Galactic Republic |side2=Confederacy of Independent Systems |side3= |side4= |commanders1=Republic loyalists |commanders2=Esselian Separatist government supporters |commanders3= |commanders4= |unit1= |unit2= |unit3= |unit4= |forces1= |forces2= |forces3= |forces4= |casual1=Severe |casual2=Severe |casual3= |casual4= |civilian= }} The Battle of Esseles took place five months after the First Battle of Geonosis. When a Separatist-funded government faction declared that the planet had joined the Confederacy of Independent Systems, a Republic loyalist resistance movement was formed. The two sides clashed for five days in a battle described as "bloody." The loyalists eventually won, however, and they forced the Separatist faction out.
