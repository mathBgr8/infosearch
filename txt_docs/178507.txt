Star Wars Kids 18 *February 1999 Star Wars Kids 18 was released packaged with Star Wars Missions 18: Rogue Squadron to the Rescue, for which the StarWars.com Cargo Bay a release date of February 1999. Rogue Squadron to the Rescue additionally denotes a February 1999 release date. |subscribers= |pages=26 |isbn=0-590-51223-4 |issn= |genre= |issue=18 |series=Star Wars Kids (1997) }} Star Wars Kids 18 is the eighteenth issue of Star Wars Kids magazine. It was published by Scholastic and released in February 1999. Issue 18 included part three of the serial comic "Death Star Pirates," written by Henry Gilroy. Star Wars Kids 18 was available exclusively to subscribers of Scholastic's Star Wars Missions young-reader series and roleplaying game, which arrived as monthly continuity packages via direct mail. Each package included a new Star Wars Kids magazine issue and the latest Star Wars Missions adventure book. Star Wars Kids 18 came packaged with the game's eighteenth adventure book, Rogue Squadron to the Rescue. 18
