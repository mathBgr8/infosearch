Yali
2373

Yali was a MC80 Liberty type Mon Calamari Star Cruiser in service to the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. She was one of the last capital ships to leave for the new Rebel base in Hoth, with Maria. The two cruisers were attacked by Imperial frigates Fear, Hate, Fury, and Spite.
