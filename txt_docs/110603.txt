|gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Light |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Bespin Wing Guard |masters= |apprentices= }} Airten was a light-skinned Human sergeant of the Bespin Wing Guard, the main security force of Cloud City on the gas giant Bespin, during the Galactic Civil War. A burly Wing Guard member, his large size prevented him from becoming a pilot; however, Airten's nonstandard build did not matter to Lando Calrissian, the city's Baron Administrator. Airten was a sympathizer of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. He wore the standard uniform for the Bespin Wing Guard. He also wielded a blaster pistol.
