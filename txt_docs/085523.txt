Droekle

Droekle was a male Hapan who was one of Queen Mother Tenel Ka Djo's courtiers and had lost his left hand in a "fencing accident" to the Queen Mother as did most of her courtiers to mirror the loss of her hand to Jacen Solo. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hapans Category:Males
