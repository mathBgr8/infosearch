Navshe'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the eastern part of the planet's western continent, halfway between the cities Covepi'starn (to the northwest) and Gna She'starn (to the southeast). *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
