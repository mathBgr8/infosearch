Combat Multi-view Display
1468

thumb|right|250px|Combat Multi-view Display on a [[T-65 X-wing starfighter/Legends|T-65 X-wing starfighter]] Combat Multi-view Display was the main sensor readout on a starfighter. Although functions appeared to vary depending on the model of CMD used, they generally displayed sensor readouts on targeted ships, including deflector shield strength and hull integrity.
