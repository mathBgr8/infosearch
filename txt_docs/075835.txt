Government House (Worlport)
1295

The Government House was the official residence of the Governor of Ord Mantell. A towering, palatial building located at the heart of Government Circle, the federal district of the planet's capital city of Worlport, Government House was designed with the Corellian classic-revival architectural style in mind. The central government of the planet operated within and around the Government House to ensure the smooth flow of political agendas.
