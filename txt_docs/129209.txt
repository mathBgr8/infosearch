A. L. Singer
2025

A. L. Singer was the author of books 13&ndash;15 in the Episode I Adventures series and 11&ndash;12 in the Star Wars Adventures series. Books 11&ndash;12 were reprints of 13&ndash;14 of the Episode I Adventures. Singer is a pseudonym for the writer Peter Lerangis. "A. L. Singer" is an anagram of "Lerangis". Peter Lerangis was born in Brooklyn, New York in August 1955. He moved to Freeport, New York for school, which made him a bit more creative when it came to writing fiction. The first writing he published was under a pen name for his school's newspaper. Later he majored in biochemistry at Harvard. After singing a cappella with the Harvard Krokodiloes, becoming their musical director, and participating in a few plays at the university, he entered law school. Later he left law school to pursue musical theater. He later got a job as an editor. After marrying his girlfriend Tina deVaron, they had a son called Nick, which made him turn full-time to writing. He mostly focused on writing novels for children and adolescents.
