Paul Hirsch
2069

Paul Hirsch (Born November 14, 1945) is a motion picture editor. In 1978, he, along with Richard Chew and Marcia Lucas, won the Academy Award for Film Editing for his work on Star Wars. He is credited for coining the phrase "It's sound people, or worse!" to chide the sound crew whenever they appeared, paraphrasing Luke Skywalker's line about the Sand People in Star Wars.
