Alec Lamere
1894

Alec Lamere was a Lord of House Barnaba. Lamere was one of the Tapani sector's best known saber rakes during the Imperial era. Although he was not described as an attractive man, he was very popular among young noble ladies who were attracted to dangerous men. Alec was a deadly duelist, whose apparent clumsiness would disappear when he ignited his lightfoil. Even before Alec had reached eighteen years of age, very few who had fought him had walked away unharmed. He did, however, have a cybernetic left arm as a result of a duel.
