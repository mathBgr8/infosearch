Magic Dragon
2188

Magic Dragon was the personal Imperial-class Star Destroyer of Grand Admiral Miltin Takel. Dan Wallace originally named the Magic Dragon in a segment of his Grand Admirals article that was cut, and was therefore unofficial until being mentioned in Barely Tolerable: Alien Henchmen of the Empire web article. "Magic Dragon" was a reference to Takel's drug use and the hidden meanings of the song Puff, the Magic Dragon. However, the rumors of the song relating to drugs are untrue.
