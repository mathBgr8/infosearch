Nance
833

Nance was a patron of Vanster Enan's Sop House during Gadrin's celebrations for the 200th anniversary of the city's founding. He watched the arm-wrestling matches between Oosk and Varoca, and was willing to take bets on the matches, giving 3:1 odds against Oosk. Nance was a small Human who dressed in a gray cloak.
