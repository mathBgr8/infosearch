*Seven other planets |owners=Max Rebo |staff= |goods= |services= |hours= |affiliation= }} Max's Flangth House was a restaurant that was opened by the Ortolan Max Rebo on the planet Coruscant shortly after 4 ABY'sThe New Essential Chronology Battle of Endor. The restaurant was extremely successful and went on to become a chain with franchises established on seven other planets. Rebo became very rich through the flanth-house's success, and he relocated to Coruscant to enjoy a retirement in lavish surroundings and a lot of food. Max's Flangth House made its first appearance, albeit unnamed, in "Epilogue: Whatever Became Of...?," a short story written by Kevin J. Anderson and published in the 1995 Tales from Jabba's Palace anthology. The restaurant was first named "Max's Flanth House" in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003. In 2008, The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia went on to identify the restaurant as "Max's Flangth House," which is the spelling used for this article. Max Rebo's entry in the original StarWars.com Databank and in The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia state the restaurant was opened after the Galactic Civil War. The restaurant's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia entry clarifies that it was opened shortly after the Battle of Endor, which 2005's The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY and before the Galactic Civil War's end.
