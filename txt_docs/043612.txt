Moegid
1877

Moegid was a Verpine smuggler and slicer. In 19 ABY, Moegid accompanied Han Solo, Lando Calrissian, and Lobot on their mission to find Bastion. Using the unique Verpine bio-frequencies, he controlled Lobot from onboard Lando's personal starship, the Lady Luck, using him to slice into Imperial files looking for a copy of the Caamas Document. This plan failed when Imperials realized the nature of the plot, and began scanning Verpine frequencies.
