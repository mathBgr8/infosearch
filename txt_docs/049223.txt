Tsi
1445

Tsi, or Mrlsstese was the native language of the Mrlssi species from Mrlsst. Mrlsstese had high piping sounds that some species found delightful and others exasperating. It was considered by some a difficult language to learn. Most Mrlssi were fluent in both Mrlsstese and Basic, being literate in both languages.
