Sornsen
766

Sornsen was a group of BFF-1 bulk freighters scheduled to deliver a batch of TIE/LN starfighters and TIE/sa bombers under the escort of corvette group Shurik. However despite this amount of protection they were destroyed by an RZ-1 A-wing interceptor and the CR90 corvette Chip and corvette group Yogi. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: B-Wing *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Imperial Navy units
