|terrain=Volcanoes |fauna= |flora= |interest= |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic }} The Hitak mountains were located on Turak IV. The volcanic mountains were used as strategic hiding places by the Rebel Alliance soldiers who fought during the Battle of Turak IV.
