Omwatese
1473

Omwatese was the native language of the Omwati species from Omwat. Omwatese used some odd tonal inflections, but was nevertheless considered an elegant language. The language had a written form There was a written form of Omwatese, which used a flowing script and letters that had to be combined to form words. Most Omwati were fluent in both Omwatese and Basic, being literate in both languages.
