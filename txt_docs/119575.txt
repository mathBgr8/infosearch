Cavern spider
1867

A cavern spider was subterranean race of gaping spider, a giant, aggressive arachnid from the planet Dathomir. Most members of the Spiderclan emulated those quiet, efficient and deadly predators. thumb|right|200px|A cavern spider attacking a prey. The spiders lived in caves like the Nightspider Cult cave and the Rancor Cave on Dathomir. A few cavern spiders were also brought to the Geonosian Bio-Lab on Yavin 4.
