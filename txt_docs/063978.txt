Elcor Station
1749

Elcor Station was a hovertrain station operating in the lower sectors of the planet Coruscant in 32 BBY. Shortly before the Invasion of Naboo, Obi-Wan Kenobi was sent to the sector containing Elcor Station to get information regarding the Black Heth, a street gang. Kenobi managed to track one of the gang's Trandoshan leaders to Elcor Station. The two dueled on top of a hovertrain that was supposed to be leaving, but was blocked by large pieces of metal debris. After facing several Black Heth thugs and defeating their leader, Kenobi cleared the debris using a Force push, boarded the hovertrain, and left the sector.
