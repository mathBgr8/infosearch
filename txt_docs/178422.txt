N-One Medcenter
686

N-One Medcenter was a medcenter located in Sector N-One on the original Death Star. It contained an Operating Theater and at least one Surgical Suite. Notable personnel assigned to this medcenter included Kornell Divini, Zam Stenza, Abu Banu, and Roa. It was destroyed along with the rest of the Death Star during the Battle of Yavin in 0 BBY. *Death Star Category:DS-1 Orbital Battle Station locations Category:Medical facilities
