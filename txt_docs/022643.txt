Three Jetties Gourmet Restaurant
941

The Three Jetties Gourmet Restaurant was a dining establishment in The Three Jetties district of Bartyn's Landing, a city of Lamaredd. The Restaurant officially served only with previous date, although certain well-dressed individuals managed to impress the doorman. The head chef, the Ortolan perfectionist Vondri Deeq, supervised every dish as of 29 BBY. Guther Bartyn, mayor of Bartyn's Landing, owned an 80% of this restaurant; and the Restaurant served only products from the Bartyn family business, Bartyn Gourmet Delicacies. This was not of the liking of working sailors, who would have preferred the Bartyns to stay outside the Three Jetties District. Category:Bartyn's Landing locations Category:Dining establishments
