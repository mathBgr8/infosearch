Rq8.Y flight computer
757

The Rq8.Y flight computer was a flight control system designed by Torplex. It was fully-integrated into the popular X-wing starfighter to translate data from control surfaces to engine performance. *Star Wars Technical Journal of the Rebel Forces *Star Wars Blueprints: Rebel Edition Category:Flight control systems
