Katarn Clan
2288

The Katarn Clan was one of the many clans that prospective young Initiates were placed in when they first began to learn the ways of the Jedi Order. Members of this clan were selected based on their proclivity for stealth. Once a member of the clan, the students would travel together through the academy at the Jedi Temple until the time they graduated. Named after the katarns of Kashyyyk, this clan was stationed at the Jedi Temple following the Ruusan Reformation of the Galactic Republic. Producing many students over the centuries, specifically Jedi Shadows, the clan was dissolved along with the rest of the Order in 19 BBY during the Great Jedi Purge. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Jedi Path: A Manual for Students of the Force Category:Jedi clans
