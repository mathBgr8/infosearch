Exquisite Death
1716

Exquisite Death was a Yuuzhan Vong corvette analog under the command of Commander Duman Yaght during the Yuuzhan Vong War in 27 ABY. It was two decks tall and 122 meters long, with a crew of 98. It had a forward-facing bridge, three aft holds, and four main access corridors. Duman received seventeen Jedi captives from Fitzgibbon Lane, who was in truth Lando Calrissian, in the plan to deliever a team of Jedi Knights to Myrkr for the Mission to Myrkr. Once the vessal had crossed Yuuzhan Vong lines, the Jedi attacked and killed all of the Yuuzhan Vong and voxyn aboard, commandeering the ship for the rest of the journey. During the approach to Myrkr, Jedi Apprentice Ulaha Kore stayed aboard the Death to distract enemy forces so that her friends could sneak onto the worldship Baanu Rass.
