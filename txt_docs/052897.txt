37th Imperial Fighter Wing
1638

The 37th Imperial Fighter Wing was a starfighter fighter wing that served with the Imperial Starfleet. thumb|left|160px|[[Tunroths fight against the Reslian Purge with the aid of the 37th Imperial Fighter Wing.]] Soontir Fel was assigned to its sixth squadron, under Captain Lun Tessra, based aboard the Abrogator. Zelen also flew as a part of the unit.
