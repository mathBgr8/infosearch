Jaren Dil
1032

Jaren Dil was a Human male citizen who lived on the planet Junction V with his wife Cilia Dil during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. He helped Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi find Cilia so that they could get information from her. *Legacy of the Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Males
