It was the homeworld of the senoti tree, a plant that produced medicinal sap and could also be found on the Forest Moon of Endor. A popular theory among xenoarborists as to how the senoti made its way to Endor was that one or both of the Canistel AgriStar freighters that had been lost in the Moddell sector had been carrying seeds of the tree and had crashed. Endor's conditions were not ideal for the senoti; New Canistel's alkaline soil allowed the trees to fare far better.Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds By 3 ABY,Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds establishes that the senoti tree depicted in Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure was among the few such trees left on Endor. dates the events of Caravan of Courage to three years after those of , which corresponds to 3 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology. very few senoti trees remained on Endor.
