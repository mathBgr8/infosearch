Godo
2015

Godo was a planet in the Outer Rim Territories. It was the source of flame gems. The world was saved by Lando Calrissian and Han Solo. The Godoans refused to save Lando from a deadly disease in return until the Alliance of Free Planets threatened to attack Godo. Jabba Desilijic Tiure once stole a valuable statue from the Godoans, the Dancing Goddess, and the Minstrel, both of which were unexplainable connected to the planet's lifeforms.
