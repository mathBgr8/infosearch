|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Abduction" is an adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published in 1992 by West End Games. (The book's spine and all interior pages give the title as The Abduction of Crying Dawn Singer; only the front cover uses the truncated title.) It was reprinted in Classic Adventures, under the full title.
