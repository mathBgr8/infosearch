Rheostat
668

A rheostat was an internal device on droids that regulated internal heat. When C-3PO and R2-D2 were scouting the ice planet of Hoth, Han Solo suggested that they turn up their rheostats to keep from freezing solid. *Star Wars 3-D 2 Category:Droid parts
