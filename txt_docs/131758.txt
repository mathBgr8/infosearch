Sand-casting
1250

Sand-casting was a type of art which was very common on the planet Tatooine. During the Clone Wars, a stolen Tatooine sand casting that depicted a Tusken Raider in battle and was valued at 2,500 credits was taken to a hotel room in the Starlight Lounge on the planet Genarius by the minions of the Rodian slaver Gomalo.
