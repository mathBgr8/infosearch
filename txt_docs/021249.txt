|published in=Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=0 BBY |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "At the Crossroads: The Spacer's Tale" is a short story about BoShek written by Jerry Oltion for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina, which was published in August 1995 by Bantam Spectra. It takes place in 0 BBY. Racing to Tatooine after breaking the Kessel Run record, spacer BoShek briefly meets Obi-Wan Kenobi in the Mos Eisley cantina. As a Force-sensitive, BoShek is particularly touched by this encounter with a former Jedi Knight.
