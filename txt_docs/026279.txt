Vuole
1550

The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Vuoles were small lizards, approximately the size of a Human hand, that could be found throughout the Rafa system, thriving in all kinds of terrain. They possessed two small protrusions below the mouth, and one beside each eye; the ones beside the eyes met above the head and merged into a crest approximately an inch high. Described as "curious," vuoles were one of the culinary staples of the Toka (whether they continued to be so after the Toka's reemergence as the Sharu is unknown). They were usually spit-roasted over an open fire, then peeled off the stick and eaten by hand.
