Eventually, shortly after the outbreak of the Clone Wars,The New Essential Chronology this droid ended up in the Cauldron. It was the largest gladiatorial pit on Rattatak, filled with violent warriors, where war barons and generals sought out the best combatants to win their wars. Therefore, like many other gladiators, Mantoid entered a deadly free-for-all battle,The New Essential Guide to Droids where only a rare combatant survived multiple fights. During one battle, Mantoid encountered the Aqualish Grunda Dolma, who wielded a double-headed staff. Despite its numerous weapons, the droid was slower than Dolma, which allowed the Aqualish to deliver a fatal blow. An unusual combat droid composed of cylindrical black body with an oval head. The skinny, multiarmed Mantoid was armed with a variety of bladed and other weapons. Mantoid first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003.
