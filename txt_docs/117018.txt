Senate Guard analysis droid
789

thumb Senate Guard analysis droids were repulsorlift-equipped units that analyzed evidence gathered by the Senate Guard in investigations. The droids had a Holoprojector. They were programmed to release sensitive information only to individuals with appropriate-level clearance.
