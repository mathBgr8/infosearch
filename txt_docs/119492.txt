Clam Clan
879

Clam Clan was a Jedi youngling clan in the Jedi Temple in the time prior to the Invasion of Naboo. It was named for the clam. Producing many students over the centuries, the clan was dissolved along with the rest of the Order in 19 BBY during the Great Jedi Purge. *Tag & Bink: Revenge of the Clone Menace Category:Jedi clans
