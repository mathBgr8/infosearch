|class= |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=*Humanoid *Wobbly-fleshedHan Solo's Revenge |lifespan= |origin=Courataine homeworld |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Courataines were a sentient species of humanoids from a homeworld with lower than standard gravity. Their bodies were wobbly-fleshed, resulting in them wearing exoskeletal travel suits when they traveled. On their homeworld, they breathed a very thin atmosphere; to simulate this offworld, they used respirators built into their suits for comfort. At some point between 2 BBY and 1 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Star Wars: The Han Solo Adventures, of which Han Solo's Revenge is part of, to between 2 BBY and 1 BBY. a number of Courataines were present on the planet Bonadan at the same time that the smuggler Han Solo was there investigating an enslavement ring. Courataines first appeared in Han Solo's Revenge, a 1979 novel written by Brian Daley as the second volume of the Star Wars: The Han Solo Adventures trilogy. *Han Solo's Revenge *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sentient species
