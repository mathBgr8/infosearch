Hasamadhi warehouse district
1360

The Hasamadhi warehouse district was a sector of Coruscant located near the south pole. It was near the Southern Underground, and served as a base for Lando Calrissian and Luke Skywalker for a time while they were hiding the Millennium Falcon before staging the rescue of Leia Organa from Prince Xizor.
