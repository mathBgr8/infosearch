Morag's lair
1055

Morag's lair was a large castle atop Mount Thunderstone on the forest moon of Endor. It was protected by Yuzzum guards, who were loyal to Morag, the Tulgah witch. Morag used the lair as a base of operations for her many attacks against her sworn enemies, the Ewoks.
