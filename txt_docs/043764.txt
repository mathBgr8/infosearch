Vaa-tumor
1129

The Vaa-tumor was an enhancement specific to Yuuzhan Vong Master Shapers, and was believed to have been a piece of brain matter of the goddess Yun-Ne'Shel. The tumor was implanted into the brain of the aspiring Master, who had to endure a period of personal sacrifice as part of the process. The tumor was structured to alter the Shaper's brain structures, and enhance the mnemonic ability of the host.
