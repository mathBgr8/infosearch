thumb|250px Anti-personnel missiles were used by the Aargau Home Guard. During the Guard's military exercise display, Darth Vader's valet attempted to telekinetically guide such a missile to kill Viscount Tardi. However, R2-D2 projected a hologram of Tardi over the valet, and one of the valet over Tardi, causing the valet to direct the missile to kill himself. *Star Wars (1977) 48 Category:Surface-to-air missiles
