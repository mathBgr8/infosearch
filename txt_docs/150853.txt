Al'ma'den
543

An Al'ma'den was one of the popal priests in the Ni'Shaw-Dak religion. A high rank, the Al'ma'dens were only second to the Third Prophet. There were only twelve Al'ma'den in the religion, housed at the Ministry headquarters.Alliance Intelligence Reports *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Religious occupations
