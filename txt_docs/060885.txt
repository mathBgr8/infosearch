Kubazian skirt
1281

200px|thumb|right|A [[Northern Mustafarian/Legends|Northern Mustafarian waring a Kubazian skirt.]] Kubazian skirts were non-gender specific garments that came from the planet Kubindi. Mustafarians, who looked very similar to the Kubaz, were often seen wearing these skirts while harvesting lava on their planet. The skirts were equipped with a cooling backpack-turbine, which aided in keeping the species from suffering heat exhaustion. *Star Wars Galaxies: Trials of Obi-Wan *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Skirts
