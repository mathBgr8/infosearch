Minister of War (Five Worlds)
1001

Minister of War was a title used by the planets of the Five Worlds to denote their civilian commander-in-chief. At the start of the Second Galactic Civil War, the Head of State of Corellia, Thrackan Sal-Solo, deposed the previous Minister and declared himself acting Minister of War.
