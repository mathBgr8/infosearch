alt=|thumb|250x250px|Two-volume Japanese edition of the manga, featuring cover art by Noriyoshi Ohrai Star Wars Manga: The Empire Strikes Back is a manga-style adaptation of the film The Empire Strikes Back. The series was illustrated by Toshiki Kudo and edited by David Land, and was specifically adapted from the screenplay of the movie by Lawrence Kasdan and George Lucas.
