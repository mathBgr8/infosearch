Crimson Forever
2901

The Crimson Forever was a disease caused by the Great Life Jewels. thumb|left|210px|The first stage of the Crimson Forever When the Great Life Jewels were separated, they produced a negative aura. This caused an exotic ailment, which induced a coma in its victims. The first stage of infection was a reddish color in the eyes of the infected, who stared into space while failing to respond to attempts at reviving them, thus providing the affliction with its name. Advanced stages were signaled by the skin of the victim turning entirely red.
