Ledd Pinot
1823

Ledd Pinot was a male Human who served as a guard in Desolation Alley around the time of the Invasion of Naboo. In 32 BBY, Pinot was on duty during the uprising caused by Zam Wesell when she was on the hunt for Bendix Fust. He was wanted by the Hutts for skimming profits in a smuggling operation. A bounty worth 4,000 credits dead or alive was placed on his head. This bounty was claimed by Jango Fett who also was looking for Bendix Fust. Ledd Pinot is the first, out of five bounties to encounter in the first Oovo IV level.
