Maramu
1174

Maramus were herbivorous furred creatures on the forest moon of Endor. They stood upright on powerful hoofed hindlegs and moved by hopping and jumping. They had forearms which also ended in hooves, prominent horns jutting from the sides of their heads, manes, and long tails.
