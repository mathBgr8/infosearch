Sran Outpost
2160

|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=Rebel Alliance }} The Sran Outpost was an installation of the Rebel Alliance located in the Sran system, a part of the Outer Rim Territories' Ash Worlds sector. At some point prior to the evacuation of the Alliance High Command base on the planetThe Essential Atlas Dantooine during the Galactic Civil War, the outpost came under attack. The Rebel officer Risiev Credal exhibited tactical brilliance during the defense and was subsequently transferred to the Dantooine base to further his tactical education under high-ranking Alliance officers. The Sran Outpost was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic installations Category:Ash Worlds locations
