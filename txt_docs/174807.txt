Vershyi Corporation
1458

The Vershyi Corporation was a weapons manufacturer known for its long-range weapons development teams. They had brought many weapons on the military market, but the Vershyi Sharpshooter was considered one of their best weapon. The Vershyi Corporation was mentioned in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The Vershyi Corporation was only mentioned in the description text of the Vershyi Sharpshooter, a weapon added to the game with the release of the expansion pack Rage of the Wookiees on May 5, 2005. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees Category:Arms manufacturing companies
