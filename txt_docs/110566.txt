Medal of Unity
998

The Medal of Unity was an Imperial starfighter pilot medal awarded for successfully putting an end to the Sepan Civil War and bringing about renewed unity for the Dimok and Ripoblus peoples. The ribbon was primarily blue, with a red upside down triangle on top. The medal itself was primarily silver with gold linings and was molar-shaped. The center of the medal had the Imperial Crest prominently displayed in gold and blue. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:TIE fighter medals
