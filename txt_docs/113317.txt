Chiithii'n
1807

Chiithii'n was a male Sullustan lieutenant commander in the Rebel Alliance and navigator on the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Independence during the Galactic Civil War. Chiithii'n was an acquaintance of pilot Keyan Farlander, who found the Sullustan kindly and mild, but struggled to pronounce his name. After Farlander's rescue of Sullustan tech staff captured by the Galactic Empire, the navigator conveyed to him the rescued prisoners' thanks, and offer of a feast of Drutash grubs and a bath in the hot springs of Piringiisi on the planet Sullust. *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance Fleet officers Category:Males Category:Sullustans
