|origin=Tenupe |found= |habitat= |lifespan= |description=*Broad-limbed *More than 100 meters |edible= |inedible= |nutrition= |effects= |affiliation= }} The Mogo tree was a species of tree native to the planet Tenupe's jungles. The trees were broad-limbed and could grow to more than 100 meters in height, allowing them to form a thick canopy. During the Battle of Tenupe, which marked the end of the Swarm War between the Chiss Ascendancy and Killik colonies, the Ascendancy used defoliants on Tenupe's jungles, killing many mogo trees. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Trees
