Blossom wine

|other= |edible=Humans |inedible= |potable= |nutrition= |effects= |dishes= |affiliation= }} Blossom wine was a rare, fragrant wine created on the planet Naboo by distilling natural essences of different flower blossoms. In 3653 BBY, during the Sacking of Coruscant, Sith Lords Adraas and Darth Angral drank some blossom wine in the office of Supreme Chancellor Berooken, whom Angral had brutally murdered. The two Sith thought that it was an excellent vintage, but "right at the end of its cellar life." During the reigns of Kings Bon Tapalo and Ars Veruna of Naboo, blossom wine was often served. At the time of the Trade Federation's blockade of Naboo, the wine was manufactured at the Summit Farm Blossom Winery, a farm situated in the Gallo Mountains. In 0 ABY, on Void Station, a Bothan information broker named Jib Kopatha offered Darth Vader some "delicacies" in order to calm down the Sith Lord's anger.
