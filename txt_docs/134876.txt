Tekra Point
743

Tekra Point was a colony ship once in the service of the Galactic Empire. When agents of the Rebel Alliance stole the vehicle, they remodeled it to serve as a drop-off point for the Wookiee bounty hunter Chenlambec, who would often rescue high-risk Rebel sympathizers, such as Agapos the Ninth.
