Castle Ventress
1759

Castle Ventress was Asajj Ventress's citadel on the planet Rattatak during the Clone Wars. It was her base of operations and a prison to many Rattataki. She kept the lightsabers of her dead Jedi Master, Ky Narec, housed within the halls of the citadel, along with a statue of him. During the Clone Wars, Jedi Knight Obi-Wan Kenobi and ARC trooper Alpha-17 were held in the castle's prison, where they were tortured by Ventress after the Battle of Jabiim.
