Dnalvec
1658

thumb|250px Dnalvec was the main port city of Sriluur, which housed three of the four main spaceports on the planet. It was a chaotic frontier environment of drifting star pilots, information merchants, and mercenaries. It was home to the Weequay's Dnalvec Militia.Alliance Intelligence Reports The Houk Territories were located less than 30 kilometers to the north, and there was a substantial Houk population in the city. The Boneglass Canyon connected it to the northern edge of the Copper Coast twenty-five kilometers away. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *Secrets of the Sisar Run *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Cities on Sriluur
