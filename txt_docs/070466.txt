Leocald Opera House
1510

The Leocald Opera House was a large, multi-storied opera house located within the Historical District of Estalle, an island city on the planet Procopia. The building was named after a former Emperor of the Tapani sector, Leocald VII, who was considered the patron saint of Tapani opera. Performers working in the building had access to three theatres, a large number of rehearsal halls, a dance academy and apartments for those in residence. During peak time in Capital Season, the House would often have two to three different shows running at the same time, with less on during the rest of the year.
