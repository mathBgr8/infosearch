James Arnold Taylor
27611

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Voice actor |companies= |website= }} James Arnold Taylor (born July 22, 1969) is an American voice actor. Considered an official "audio double" for Ewan McGregor, he has vocally portrayed Obi-Wan Kenobi in both the 2D Clone Wars animated micro-series and the 3D Star Wars: The Clone Wars animated film and TV series,Star Wars: The Clone Wars film as well as in several Star Wars video games. He has reprised his role as Obi-Wan Kenobi in Star Wars Rebels. Taylor is also credited as voicing "group voice: bomber pilot" in the 2012 film Red Tails, executive-produced by George Lucas.
