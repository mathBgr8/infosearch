As a member of the death-worshiping Cult of M'dweshuu, McKnives sought ways to appease his religions' demand for blood sacrifice. During the Clone Wars, he made his way to the violent, nigh-unknown planet of Rattatak. He committed several murders with the intent of being forced into the gladiatorial games as punishment&mdash;a wish which was granted.
