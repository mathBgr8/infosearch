Zonama Sekotan fighter
1121

The Zonama Sekotan fighter was a three-lobed bioship armed with lava cannons and propelled by a dovin basal that Zonama Sekot grew for defense against the Yuuzhan Vong. The Zonama Sekotan fighter lacked a conventional hyperdrive. The vessel was also similar to the Yuuzhan Vong's coralskippers.
