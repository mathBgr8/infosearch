Loli
1628

Loli was a Jedi Knight. In 40 ABY, while still a teenager and just back from the Jedi Trials, Loli was among the Jedi who were present at the Ossus Academy when the Galactic Alliance Guard&mdash;GAG&mdash;attempted to secure it, holding the students hostage.
