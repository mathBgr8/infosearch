Garollian ghost viper
2576

|habitat= |diet= |language= }} Garollian ghost vipers were creatures native to a location within the Garollia system of the Expansion Region's Baroli sector. The ghost vipers had tongues that they could move rapidly. While engaged in a lightsaber duel against the Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi during the Separatist attack on the planet Coruscant in 19 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion Count Dooku mentally compared Kenobi's lightsaber attacks to the quick movement of a ghost viper's tongue.
