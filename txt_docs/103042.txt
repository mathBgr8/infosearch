Battle of Tantive V
3502

}} The Battle of Tantive V was a battle between forces of the New Republic and a Imperial force commanded by Admiral Gaen Drommel. During the battle, two of Drommel's three Imperial-class Star Destroyers were destroyed, while one, the Wolf's Claw, was captured. Drommel's flagship, the Executor-class Star Dreadnought Guardian, took heavy damage, but managed to retreat to Soullex. Admiral Drommel himself survived the battle.
