Council, Nothoiin, and Bavva are all seen to be on the Nothoiin Corridor on the map, but not Polmanar, meaning the Nothoiin Route is the hyperlane connecting Nothoiin and Polmanar. and the Lipsec Run. It was located within the Anoat subsector of the Greater Javin sector. Centuries before the Galactic Civil War, entrepreneur Ecclessis Figg established this trade route between the planets Nothoiin and Isde Naha in the Greater Javin region of the Outer Rim Territories. The route opened up the backwaters of what was then the Javin sector by forging a secondary link between the Corellian Trade Spine and the Nothoiin Corridor. The leg of this route from the Mev system to Isde Naha eventually became a part of the Lipsec Run. *The Essential Atlas Category:Greater Javin hyperlanes
