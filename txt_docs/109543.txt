Liak ke Mattino
1862

Liak ke Mattino was a Human male pilot and captain of the Strike the Moons Flightknife from the planet Adumar. He and his flightknife were the first Adumari pilots to fly against Red Flight, the New Republic diplomatic group sent to Adumar, in a non-live-fire battle. He later helped them escape the gauntlet by engaging the Lords of Dismay Flightknife. During the Adumari Civil War, ke Mattino was shot down and killed.
