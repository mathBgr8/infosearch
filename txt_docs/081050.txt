|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation=New Republic }} Marter An was the capital of Sheris. This city had a spaceport. Marter An was built in the mountains of the planet. The Bhir'khi Pass was one of the only access path to the city. During the Thrawn campaign, the New Republic and Imperial forces fought in the Bhir'khi Pass in order to control the access to the capital. Category:Capital cities
