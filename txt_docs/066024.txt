Barguna Moocha
785

Barguna Moocha was an extremely successful chain of superstores that had locations throughout the galaxy. It often purchased smaller, private enterprises to be absorbed into its organization. One example of this was the Bargoon Tatooni trading post in Mos Eisley on Tatooine, which was bought out by the chain at some point after 6 ABY.
