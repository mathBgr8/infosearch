Tusken battle talisman
1613

A Tusken battle talisman was an artifact of the Sand People carved from a bantha horn that was believed to give the owner the strength of a bantha and the courage to face death. It had the word "luck" engraved in aurebesh on it. Jabba Desilijic Tiure owned one of these as a lucky charm, although he lost it. It was later discovered to have somehow ended up in his pet rancor's body, which Jubnuk retrieved when he unwittingly fell into the chamber while trying to defend Jabba from Luke Skywalker. *Jabba's Palace Pop-up Book *The Essential Guide to Characters Category:Tusken Raider culture
