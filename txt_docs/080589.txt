Frank Welker
4329

Franklin Wendell Welker (born March 12, 1946) is an American voice actor. He is responsible for a broad spectrum of character voices, noises, and other vocal effects that have appeared over the last 40 years in American TV shows and movies. Welker is particularly noted for a wide range of non-verbal vocal effects, i.e., for aliens and (non-talking) animals. Frank Welker portrayed the characters Jorak Uln, Sunry, and Handon Guld in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic. In 1980, he voiced Mr. Cool in the animated series The Fonz and the Happy Days Gang, alongside Ron Howard. As of July 2011, Frank Welker has voiced or appeared in ninety-six motion pictures with a combined gross revenue of over 6.2 billion dollars.
