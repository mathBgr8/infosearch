Nuknog (language)
871

Nuknog was the language spoken by the Nuknog species, consisting of squeaks, barks, and gruff vocalizations. It utilized a simple alphabet. Most Nuknogs were fluent in both Nuknog and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
