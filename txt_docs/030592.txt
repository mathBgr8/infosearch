|figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Jedi Pack was a bonus "gift" given away by Decipher starting in October 1996 as part of their Star Wars Customizable Card Game. It was usually accompanied by the Rebel Leader Pack. It was handed out at tournaments by Decipher advocates to help newer players. For a while it was available for free still on Decipher's website by sending in a SASE and 2$ for shipping and handling. Since the game stopped being printed, Decipher stopped giving them away (as was a term of agreement). The Jedi Pack contained cards of four main characters from Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, with lower statistics than their Premiere Limited counterparts and no game text.
