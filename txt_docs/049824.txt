Rhodi's Silence
1248

The Rhodi's Silence was Nas Ghent's personal freighter which he used for high risk smuggling missions. The Silence was "silenced" when Darth Vader personally took down Ghent to recruit him to train a personal squadron for Vader's use. Category:Freighters Category:Smuggler starships
