|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=144 |isbn=9781593075538 |isbn2= |timeline=19 BBY |series=*Star Wars: Clone Wars trade paperbacks *Star Wars: Republic |issues= |preceded by=Star Wars: Clone Wars Volume 8: The Last Siege, the Final Truth |followed by= }} Star Wars: Clone Wars Volume 9: Endgame is the ninth and final volume in the Star Wars: Clone Wars series of trade paperbacks. Volume 9 collects Star Wars: Republic issues 79-83 and Purge. It was published by Dark Horse Comics on July 26, 2006. Witness the untold stories of the Jedi who took a last stand against Emperor Palpatine in the moments during, and immediately after, the events in Revenge of the Sith! Watch Darth Vader undertake a Jedi "purge" of his own in the hunt for Obi-Wan Kenobi! In the jungles of the Wookiee homeworld Kashyyyk, Quinlan Vos wages a battle of impossible odds against his own troops to protect his loved ones. On the icy Outer Rim world of Toola, Jedi Master Kai Huddora takes a terrified Padawan into his charge after her own master falls to Order 66. Amidst the forests of New Plympto, Dass Jennir finds himself in league with a band of rebels he'd led attacks against only days before.
