Din Tinray
930

Din Tinray was a deaf, female Sullustan. Since deaf Sullustans were not active at all, she went to see a physician for help, Doctor Rachael Tikaris. Tikaris was able to create an ear for her, and after surgery, Din was welcomed back into society. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Deaf and hard of hearing individuals Category:Females Category:Sullustans
