It was orbited by three planets: the molten world Pyr, the water world Manaan, and the gas giant Navlaas. Pyrshak first appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, and was first named in the 2004 Wizards of the Coast article Manaan: Depths of History.
