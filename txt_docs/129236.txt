GSI
1608

GSI was a manufacturing company. It produced the M-L4 Seeker, a model of minelayer sold for 4,000 credits. GSI was first mentioned in the new Star Wars canon in No Disintegrations, a 2017 supplement for the Star Wars: Edge of the Empire roleplaying game published by Fantasy Flight Games. The company originated in the Star Wars Legends continuity, where it first appeared in Star Wars: The Old Republic: Rise of the Hutt Cartel, a 2013 expansion to BioWare's 2011 video game Star Wars: The Old Republic.Star Wars: The Old Republic: Rise of the Hutt Cartel *No Disintegrations Category:Manufacturing companies
