Xenoecoengineering Financial Principles
481

Xenoecoengineering Financial Principles was the name of a course on Muunilinst. Joram Kithe attended it prior to the Clone Wars. Kithe spoke of the course as being located on "Muun." Kithe most likely misspoke, or else used a nickname for the planet. Category:Educational courses and lectures
