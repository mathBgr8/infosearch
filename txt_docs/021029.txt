Yagai spiced dindra
736

Yagai spiced dindra was a dish served at the Farrimmer Cafe. Yagai spiced dindra was mentioned in "The Farrimmer Cafe," a roleplaying source article authored by Jean Rabe and J. Allan Fawcett for use with West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game and published in the November 1996 eleventh issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine. Category:Food
