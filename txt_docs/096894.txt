Ramoan
2543

The Ramoans were a sentient species whose homeworld was ruined by the Yuuzhan Vong. Six years after the Yuuzhan Vong War, they were still being relocated to new worlds. The last stages of their relocation were supervised by Lowbacca, who abandoned the project to join the side of the Killiks. At least one member of this species became a Jedi Knight; Bazel Warv was a contemporary of Valin and Jysella Horn and gave his life while protecting Allana Solo. Ramoans were larger and stronger than average Humans. Their skin was jade green. They had small eyes, a pig-like snout, and tusks jutting out of their mouths. They spoke in a series of grunts, oinks, and squeaks. The shape of their mouths made it very difficult to speak Basic. They may have been related to Gamorreans. Bazel Warv is the only Ramoan to have been given more than a background role.
