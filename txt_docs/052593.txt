Old X'Tingian
530

Old X'Tingian was the original native language of the X'Ting species from Ord Cestus. It was later replaced with X'Tingian. Old X'Tingian was mainly composed by different sounds and smells, that would later evolve to became the "written" form of X'Tingian. Written Old X'Tingian used sets of hieroglyphs and pictograms, that were absolutely ruled out when the language evolved to X'Tingian. *The Hive Category:Languages
