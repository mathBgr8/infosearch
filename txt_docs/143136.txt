Greater Marianas
1608

Greater Marianas was a little known dust-swept planet in the Outer Rim Territories subjugated by the Galactic Empire some time before the Battle of Yavin. It contained at least one settlement of Humans and was also home to the yaggie, a creature hunted for its skin and meat.
