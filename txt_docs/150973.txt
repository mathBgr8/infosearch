Jalor City
674

Jalor City was the capital city of Gotida, and the birthplace and home of Riic Mardec and his family. It also hosted a Rebel cell led by Lieutenant Commander Detta Rotan.Alliance Intelligence Reports In 2 ABY, the Galactic Empire attacked Rotan's cell. Riic Mardec's family died in the crossfire. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Cities
