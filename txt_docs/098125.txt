R'all
1497

R'all was the twin brother of J'uoch and leader of a criminal mining outfit. While searching for a data disk, which contained information on the treasure ship Queen of Ranroon, he, J'uoch, and their criminal band stole the Millennium Falcon from Han Solo on Dellalt.
