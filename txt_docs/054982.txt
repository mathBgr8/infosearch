Warleader
997

Warleader was a title used by the warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. It was an unofficial rank used in two cases&mdash;one was for Yuuzhan Vong Commanders who were in charge of a single theater of war, but not yet escalated to Supreme Commander. Another was for the leaders of simple squadrons and platoons.
