I-10 Probe Droid

Station Gamma |armament=None |equipment= |affiliation=Rak'qua |firstmade= |retired= }} The I-10 probe droid was a type of modified B1-series battle droid used during the Galactic Civil War. Droids of this kind was unarmed as it primarily filled a reconnaissance role. I-10 probe droids were used by the Rak'qua on Station Gamma above Ord Mantell following the Battle of Yavin. In 1 ABY, a number of spacers who explored Station Gamma fought and destroyed multiple I-10 probe droids. I-10 probe droids appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
