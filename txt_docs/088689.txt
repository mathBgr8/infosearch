Peter Burroughs
1441

}} Peter Burroughs (born January 27, 1947) is an actor who played a swinging Ewok in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi and worked on the stunts for the film. Stock footage of his Ewok character in Return of the Jedi was later used in the video game Star Wars: Battlefront. He went on to play a Nelwyn villager in Willow, and worked on the stunts for the film. He has also performed in films such as The Dark Crystal and Labyrinth. Burroughs' daughter, Samantha Davis, is married to actor Warwick Davis. Category:Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi actors
