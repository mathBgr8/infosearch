Relal Tawron
1243

Relal Tawron was the High Priest and leader of the Ithorians of Tafanda Bay as of 25 ABY, having succeeded Momaw Nadon. He gave the New Republic and Imperial Remnant permission to enter the forbidden area of Ithor's surface to prepare defenses against the Yuuzhan Vong.
