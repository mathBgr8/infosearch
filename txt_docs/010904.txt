|gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Fair |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Olander Brit was a fair-skinned Human with brown hair who joined the Alliance to Restore the Republic by 4 ABY, following Major Panno to the Alliance. He was a communications expert and was politically minded. By 4 ABY, Brit was an Alliance major, with staff monitoring the entire spectrum of electromagnetic signals. In 4 ABY, shortly before the Battle of Endor, Brit was aboard the MC80 Home One type Star Cruiser Home One, attending a briefing where the Alliance High Command planned an attack on the Galactic Empire's second Death Star battle station. Brit wore the standard Alliance Fleet officer uniform. Portrayed by an uncredited Peter Roy, Olander Brit first appeared in 1983's Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, the third film of the original trilogy. His scenes were filmed throughout the week of Tuesday, February 16, 1982 at Stage 5 of Elstree Studios.
