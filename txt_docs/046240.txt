Neesada Bari
1011

Neesada Bari was a female Gungan who served on the Gungan High Council on three occasions during her lifetime. She was the mother of Toba, and the wife of Bullba Bari. Category:Females Category:Gungans of unspecified race Category:Legislators and councilors Category:Naboo residents
