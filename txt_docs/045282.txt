Cleansing of Yavin 4
1426

The Cleansing of Yavin 4 was one of the first skirmishes of the Great Hunt. In it, Jedi led by Guun Han Saresh, Shaela Nuur, and Duron Qel-Droma cleansed the moon Yavin 4 of terentateks left there by the Brotherhood of the Sith during the Great Sith War's Krath Holy Crusade.
