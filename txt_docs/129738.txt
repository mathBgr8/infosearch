Ai-Ari Kuldi
1879

*Galactic Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Ai-Ari Kuldi was a Jedi who served the Galactic Republic during the time of the Clone Wars and became the commander of the Republic's Fifth Fleet. During the conflict, the Cularin system seceded from the Republic and became a sovereign star system. Afterward, Kuldi announced the subsequent withdrawal of Republic forces and protection from the Cularin system, expressing their regrets for the situation and wishing them luck. Ai-Ari Kuldi was mentioned in "Cularin Emergency Announcement," a Wizards.com article released on December 8, 2005 and written by August and Cynthia Hahn.
