Gursan Bryes
1391

Gursan Bryes was a male Bothan from Kachirho, Kashyyyk. He was helping the residents of Kachirho defeat the "Enslaver of Kachirho," Lord Cyssc Blackscale. This confrontation came down to the heroes willing to help against the many space forces of Lord Cyssc. In the end, the heroes reigned supreme, and Kachirho was free of Lord Cyssc's wrath. Each was presented with a Kaminoan great staff by Bryes.
