Sector Medical Facility
556

The Sector Medical Facility was a hospital in the city of Bagsho on Nim Drovis. It was once administered by the Ho'Din Ism Oolos. In 25 ABY Elan and Vergere were brought there after their fake defection from the Yuuzhan Vong, and were interrogated by Saychel and Showolter. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:Medical facilities Category:Nim Drovis locations
