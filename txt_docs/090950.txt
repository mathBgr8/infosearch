Guild Reform Committee
1078

The Guild Reform Committee was one of two factions that formed when Boba Fett split the Bounty Hunters' Guild in two. The Committee was formed out of resentment for the elder members of the order who seceded from the Guild and consequently formed their own organization, the True Guild. The Committee was headed by Bossk.
