Reigat
1434

The Reigats were a species of large humanoids with grayish, mottled skin and dark blue hair, eyelids, and lips. They were generally a species of loners whose yearly "breeding drive" was probably all that kept them from becoming extinct.Cracken's Rebel Operatives The Reigats were sometimes very quick to anger.
