Thakitillo
1988

}} Thakitillo was a delicacy which was considered among the finest edible fare in the galaxy. It consisted of tangy curds which dissolved in the eater's mouth. Members of the Myrkr strike team enjoyed it, though it prompted memories of fallen comrades.
