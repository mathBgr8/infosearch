An orbital security station circled Ronika, the eighth planet of the Koros system that was located within the Deep Core's Koros sector. Along with the rest of the system, the space station was situated at the junction of the Byss Run and Koros Trunk Line hyperspace routes, which linked it to the Keeara Major and Jerrilek system, respectively. The Koros system also constituted an endpoint of the Daragon Trail route, which connected it directly to the Horuset system of the Outer Rim Territories. The station was a gray-colored three-part structure, with the two outer parts being connected to the central part. All three of the station's components boasted numerous antennae. The security station had a complement of at least two starfighters that were used to pursue prisoners attempting to escape Ronika. In the year 5000 BBY,According to the Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi comics, Jori Daragon's flight from Ronika occurred shortly before the beginning of the Great Hyperspace War, which is dated by The Essential Atlas to the year 5000 BBY. the orbital station maintained security in space above Ronika, which was at that time used by the Koros Major monarchy as a forced-labor prison world. That year, the Human Jori Daragon, who was imprisoned on Ronika, stole a drone ore shuttle on the planet's surface and attempted to escape the harsh world and return to Koros Major, her homeworld. However, a guard on Ronika's surface notified the security station of the escape attempt and ordered it to intercept Daragon. The security station launched two starfighters, piloted by two individuals who felt certain that no prisoner could escape Ronika, to pursue Daragon; however, due to the fighters' laser cannons being ineffective against the ore shuttle's heavy shielding, Daragon was able to escape to Koros Major. To date, the orbital security station has only appeared in the second issue of The Fall of the Sith Empire story arc of the Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi series of comic books.
