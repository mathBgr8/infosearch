|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Actress |companies= |website= }} Orli Shoshan (born July 23, 1974) is an actress who played Shaak Ti in Attack of the Clones and in two non-canon deleted scenes in Revenge of the Sith. In 2006, Orli joined Jeremy Bulloch, Michael Sheard, and David Prowse in the final part of UK fan film Order of the Sith: Downfall. Orli played the part of a game-show host in a bar on Tatooine. The film was made in aid of Save the Children.
