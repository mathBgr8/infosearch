*Dave Marshall |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=October 31, 2007 |other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=400 |isbn=1593078307 |isbn2= |timeline=5000 BBY&ndash;3999 BBY |series=Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi/Star Wars Omnibus |issues=1 |preceded by=X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 3 |followed by=Tales of the Jedi Volume 2 }} Star Wars Omnibus: Tales of the Jedi Volume 1 is the fourth Star Wars Omnibus by Dark Horse, collecting several issues of the Tales of the Jedi comics. Uncover the ancient history of the Jedi, the Sith, and even hyperspace, in these earliest known stories of the Star Wars galaxy, taking place five thousand years before Luke Skywalker's successful assault on the Death Star. In a galaxy linked together by a newly formed Republic and protected by the Jedi Knights, two fortune-seeking hyperspace explorers unwittingly ignite a war between the previously uncharted Sith Empire and the Republic, the repercussions of which will echo for a thousand years afterward, and change the lives of young Jedi Ulic Qel-Droma and Nomi Sunrider forever! *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 0 *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 1 *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 2 *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 3 *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 4 *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 5 *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 1 *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 2 *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 3 *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 4 *Tales of the Jedi – The Fall of the Sith Empire 5 *Tales of the Jedi 1 *Tales of the Jedi 2 *Tales of the Jedi 3 *Tales of the Jedi 4 *Tales of the Jedi 5 File:SwTOTJomni1.jpg|Cover File:SWOmnibusTOTJ1-art.png|Cover art Tales of the Jedi 1
