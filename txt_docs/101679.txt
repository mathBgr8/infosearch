Star Wars Adventures 8: The Hunt for Anakin Skywalker
2219

Star Wars Adventures 8: The Hunt for Anakin Skywalker was the eighth installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series.This book is a reprint of Episode I Adventures 6: The Hunt for Anakin Skywalker. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 8 in May 2003. Now flashback to the time before Episode I.' Anakin Skywalker and his friends have helped to free a group of Ghostling slaves. Now their owner, Gardulla the Hutt, wants to find out who's responsible. And the Hutt wants revenge. Some of the best trackers on the planet of Tatooine are searching for Anakin. Will he be caught?
