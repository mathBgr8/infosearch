The C-2 protocol droid was a droid manufactured on the moon of Telti by Cybot Galactica. It was programmed with many functions and forms of communication. It was the successor of the C-1 protocol droid and the predecessor of the C-3 protocol droid. *The New Rebellion Category:C-series protocol droid models
