|mayor= |celestialbody=Arkania |continent= |location= |climate=Tundra |interest= |population=est.2,000 |demonym= |affiliation= }} Novania was a major spaceport city on the planet Arkania. Established on the planet Arkania in the decades before the Great Sith War, the city of Novania was built around one of the planet's largest spaceports. Maintaining a population of around 2,000 the city was occupied not only by Arkanians but by many foreign visitors. Control of the spaceport was given to the Security Master who had access to information from across the planet.
