Blood Sacrifice
822

Blood Sacrifice was the personal flagship of Warmaster Tsavong Lah during the Battle of Ebaq. Carrying a yammosk, the vessel was also the flagship of the Battle Group of Yun-Yuuzhan. When the Yuuzhan Vong warmaster ordered an occupation of Ebaq 9, Blood Sacrifice landed on the moon's surface. The vessel was destroyed shortly after leaving the surface when Lah ordered it back into space to rejoin and reinforce the Battle Group of Yun-Yuuzhan. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flagships Category:Yuuzhan Vong starships
