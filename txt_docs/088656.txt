Crescent of Valor
824

The Crescent of Valor was a Rebel SpecForces decoration awarded for brave and valorous conduct under fire. It took the form of a crescent embossed on a field of stars and hung from a blue ribbon. *Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic awards
