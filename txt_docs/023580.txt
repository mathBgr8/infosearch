Roridan Temquill
1060

Roridan Temquill was the CEO of Biscuit Baron during the Separatist Crisis. During the Clone Wars, he devised the restaurant chain's month-long "Heroes of the Republic" promotion in the interests of educating young customers about the history of the Galactic Republic using an array of informative Holocubes.
