H-12 copter
1328

H-12 copters were centuries-old airspeeders used by well-off citizens of Desevro to move around the city of Maslovar. Used during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, surviving into the Galactic Civil War, these ships seated six and were produced by Lorrad Flightworks. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Airspeeder models Category:Lorrad Flightworks products
