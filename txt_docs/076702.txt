Nlora perfume
600

Nlora perfume was a type of perfume. Growing Yammosks smelled like Nlora perfume and garlic rose. James Luceno created nlora perfume for The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse, published in 2000. It is possible this is a misspelling for the "nlorna flower perfume" created by Michael A. Stackpole for X-Wing: Wedge's Gamble, published in 1996. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse Category:Perfumes
