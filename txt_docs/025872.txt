|death=February 15, 2014 |listedpronouns= |nationality=Scottish |occupation=*Actor *Director *Producer |companies= |website= }} Christopher Malcolm (August 19, 1946 - February 15, 2014) was a Scottish actor, director, and producer known for his work in the musical The Rocky Horror Show the 1986 films Highlander and Labyrinth. He played the Rebel pilot Zev Senesca in the 1980 original trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back.
