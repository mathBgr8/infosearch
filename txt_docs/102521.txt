|published in=Star Wars Tales 20 |reprinted in= |pages=4 |timeline=4 ABY |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Problem Solvers" was a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 20. It was reprinted in Star Wars Tales Volume 6 and Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: The New Republic Vol. 1. Months after the Battle of Endor, on an unidentified ecumenopolis within the New Republic, Leia Organa reminds Han Solo of the importance of a new information-technology crew to the fledgling state as many important computers are nonfunctional, and of his responsibility for finding one. He informs her that he instead decided to go to a card game on Bespin that he 'couldn't miss', and that he reassigned the task to C-3PO. A group of rowdy Ewoks, regarded by Organa as the most primitive sentients in the New Republic, barges into the computer room in which the two Rebel heroes stand, and Organa panics as the new 'technicians' destroy the computers with their weapons and anti-AT-ST countermeasures, knocking over C-3PO in the process. A furious Organa tells Solo to evict them from the room, and decides that their first priority should instead be finding a Human Resources director to hire technicians, and asks Solo as to who their droid found to fill that role. He meekly explains that earlier, Organa stated that their new personnel needed to be 'strong and uncompromising - someone everyone would respect'.
