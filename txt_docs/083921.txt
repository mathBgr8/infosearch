Peterson's Guide to Droids of the Republic
768

Peterson's Guide to Droids of the Republic was a comprehensive catalogue of the different types of droids in known space, available in searchable format over the HoloNet in the last days of the Galactic Republic. The entry on the Tac-Spec Footman droid was in the catalog's seventh volume, entitled The Great Corporate Expansion Era. *Yoda: Dark Rendezvous *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Catalogs
