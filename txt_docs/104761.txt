Nine

"Nine" was the designated name given to Mara Jade Skywalker's R9-series astromech droid. The droid was used aboard her StealthX starfighter during the height of the Swarm War. *Dark Nest III: The Swarm War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:R9-series astromech droids
