Winger family
1248

The Winger family was based on Garos IV and consisted of Imperial Governor Tork Winger and his wife Sali Winger. The couple adopted a daughter, Alexandra Winger, who would join the anti-Imperial Garos Resistance group. Alexandra had no recollection of her father and only remembered her mother's name as "Ana". In reality, her parents were named Matt Turhaya and Anii Degarienne.
