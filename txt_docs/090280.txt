Yavin's Caprice
1814

The Yavin's Caprice was a New Republic Gymsnor-3 light freighter. Yavin's Caprice flew in remote areas with escorts. It was flanked with two Y-wing fighters when it was raided by Sol Mon's pirate forces in 11 ABY. During the attack, the pirates destroyed at least one of the two Y-wing fighters serving as escorts.
