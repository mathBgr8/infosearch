Lialla Tane
1201

Lialla Tane was a member of the Naboo Underground resistance group established after the Trade Federation's Invasion of Naboo. Her parents had been captured and held prisoner by the Trade Federation. Her father, Kyu Tane, was the Naboo Minister of Culture.
