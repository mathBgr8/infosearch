Galactic Crystal Creations
1249

Galactic Crystal Creations, also known as Garnib Crystal Corporation, collected and marketed Garnib crystals from the planet Garnib, which the corporation had bought the rights to. Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One refers to the entity as Galactic Crystal Creations, where as the Star Wars Gamemaster Screen refers to the company as Garnib Crystal Corporation. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume One *The Star Wars Planets Collection *Alien Encounters Category:Vendors and sellers
