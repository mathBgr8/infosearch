Crimson Fury
1370

Crimson Fury was a flight of BTL Y-wing starfighters attached to Red Hand Squadron. During the attack on the slave ship Helot's Shackle, they performed a strafing run on the CR90 corvette that allowed the rest of the Red Hands to subdue and board it. *Rebel Dawn Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starfighter squadrons Category:Red Hand Squadron personnel
