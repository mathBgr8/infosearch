Defel Mimicker
668

The Defel Mimicker was an advanced stealth field generator used in the period following the Jedi Civil War. Designed to mimic the appearance of Defels as vague shadows, it was not disrupted as easily as other stealth units. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Stealth field generators
