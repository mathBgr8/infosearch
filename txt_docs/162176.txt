Rose Nebula
643

The Rose Nebula was a massage parlor and spa on Coruscant. It was owned by the Bothan, Rava Teb'lya, who used her spying skills and experience in extravagance to glean information from her influential clients and then sold it to the highest bidder. The Trandoshan, Tsserk, was hired by Teb'lya as a bouncer.
