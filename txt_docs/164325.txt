Feather fern
841

Feather ferns were plants that grew on Yavin 4. Its tall, lacy fronds could reach two to three meters in length. The ferns grew among the exotic (and mostly predatory) flora and fauna of Gardulla the Hutt's pleasure garden in her Tatooine palace, near the Dune Sea. *Episode I Adventures 7: Capture Arawynne *Dark Apprentice *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ferns Category:Plants of Yavin 4
