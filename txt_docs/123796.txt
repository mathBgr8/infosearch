thumb|right|250px|Vop Hui attacking [[Ton-Falk-class escort carrier|Escort Carrier Hininbirg.]] The Vop Hui was a CR90 corvette used by the Rebel Alliance. The Vop Hui along with the Mar Duun attempted to disrupt a meeting involving TIE Defender prototypes being transferred to the Escort Carrier Hininbirg by deploying mines around the transfer point.
