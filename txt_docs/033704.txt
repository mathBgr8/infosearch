The ship was designed to resemble some beast out of the collective nightmares of all humanoid species. It stretched ominously from its vaguely head-shaped bow ending in a multiple-gun tower emplacement that resembles a deadly tail. Hangar bays sat open on either side of the bow like great maws, ready to release a store of 24 TIE/LN starfighters for attack, reconnaissance, and patrol missions. Only one ship of this type was ever known to be produced, the Galaxy Dragon, which was operated by Moff Delurin.
