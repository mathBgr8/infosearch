Legion Condor EX
1043

The Legion Condor EX was a starship model that was in use around the Galactic Civil War. It was capable of hyperspace travel and was popular with starship renters. The process of renting one of these ships was similar, in many ways, as with renting others. The rentor could pay for a fixed number of hyperspace jumps upfront. Upon reaching the last jump, the ship would automatically return to base. However, if a rentor attempted to override this programming or jump-tamper the nav-computer, they would face a hefty fine or be ordered to a weekend flying school with a tough Twi'lek instructor. There was also collision insurance and medical coverage available with the rental. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Starship classes
