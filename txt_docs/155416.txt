Unrik
500

An unrik was a piece of flesh that served as a votive to the Yuuzhan Vong gods. Members of the Priest caste would carve the unrik from their own bodies and keep it alive using their biotechnology, using it as an example of their devotion to the gods they served. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:True Way
