NavStar Transport
595

NavStar Transport was a Rebel front company, which was once used to get a group of rebels on board the Arion Star from Tancon to Greyman's Planet so that they could meet with Roger Stern of Stern Traders. There, they used the company name to gain supplies from him to transport to Karinda Outpost. Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic organizations
