Martz
1728

Martz was a captain in the Republic Navy during the Clone Wars. They commanded the Acclamator-class assault ship Prosecutor, which was commandeered by Trandoshan mercenaries roughly one year into the war. They were presumed dead, most likely killed by the Trandoshan forces. Martz was mentioned in the 2005 video game Star Wars: Republic Commando. In the French and German versions of Star Wars: Republic Commando, Martz is instead referred to as "Stinnet."
