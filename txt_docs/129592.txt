established that a Crisis Branch has not been formed in over 60 years by 22 BBY. *22 BBY (13:4:4 GrS) |fragmented= |reorganized=19 BBY,The Essential Guide to Warfare CrisisImperial Sourcebook |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Senate Bureau of Intelligence }} The Crisis Branch was a division of SBI, formed during emergencies. In 22 BBY, Director of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence Armand Isard formed a Crisis Branch, the first time in over sixty years it had been called into being. Isard's division was composed of Intelligence directors from the Core Worlds, and worked directly with the Jedi High Council and Loyalist Committee. Its first act was to fire local directors from 32 Mid Rim worlds considered corrupt. Category:Galactic Republic intelligence organizations
