Eckla Shoolb
1115

Eckla Shoolb was a female Shamed One and member of Yu'shaa's Jeedai heresy. She was tortured and beaten, but did not break under Supreme Overlord Shimrra's interrogation. She was then sentenced to death. Guards broke her legs, and she was thrown into the yargh'un pit to die of thirst and starvation. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion Category:Females Category:Jeedai Heretics Category:Shamed Ones
