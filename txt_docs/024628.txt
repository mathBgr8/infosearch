Lant Mining Corporation
2141

Lant Mining Corporation was the manufacturer of the Mobile Mining Barge I. It was the dominant economic force of Brak sector, having been chartered by the Republic to develop the entire sector's resources. During the Galactic Civil War, LMC fell on hard times as Brak sector's mines began to play out. LMC was headquartered in the Demar system. During the Galactic Civil War, the Lant Mining Corporation was divided into six different divisions: Mining, Refining, Resource Discovery, Research and Design, Sales, and Security Patrol. Each division was headed by an Executive, with all six reporting to the Chief Executive responsible for running the entire company. LMC had contracts with several notable companies, including Sienar Fleet Systems, Incom Corporation, Kuat Drive Yards, Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc., and Ikas-Adno.
