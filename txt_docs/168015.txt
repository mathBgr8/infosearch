LpM-549 Navigation Sensor
1814

The LpM-549 Navigation Sensor, also known as the LpM-549 computer system and the 549 sensor pallet was a model of navigation sensor manufactured by Microaxial. The unit was a component of Slayn & Korpil's B-wing starfighter. The LpM-549 Navigation Sensor was linked to a navigation computer and was capable of storing two sets of jump coordinates for hyperspace travel. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Microaxial products Category:Navigation computers Category:Starfighter components
