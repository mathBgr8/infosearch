Hy'tras
921

The Hy'tras was one of the five races of Shatras. The second most common race after Y'tras, Hy'tras, composed the 10% of the Shatras population. They were probably technologically advanced once, but a vast, centuries-long war with the Y'tras made them reject technology. Then, the Y'tras made a deal with them: Leaving the Hy'tras alone in exchange of their riches. Both parties respected the agreement. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Alien Encounters Category:Sentient subspecies
