When Supreme Chancellor Palpatine released a broadcast asking the Confederacy of Independent Systems leader Count Dooku to meet for negotiations, Senator Sh'neel told the HoloNet News that they believed the Chancellor had made a cautious move, calling him both intelligent and meek, but warning others to be careful of Palpatine as the senator suspects he had a hidden agenda. R'shinnos Sh'neel first appeared in HoloNet News Vol. 531 48 on the HoloNet News website in 2002 in the article entitled "Palpatine to Separatists: Let's Talk".
