Prosperity (Beheboth)
1910

Prosperity was the largest town on the desert planet Beheboth. Once a hardscrabble settlement mostly populated by moisture farm workers, the town was originally named Garrotine, but was renamed after an economic revival brought about by a rare downpour of rain caused by the Tirrith in 3 ABY. Within five years, Prosperity had grown greatly in size, going from a town with just one small cantina to over a dozen.
