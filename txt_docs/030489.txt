Donadi
383

The Donadi were a species from Donadus. The species was best known for its meditation techniques, in which Donadi lowered themselves into a deep trance where they could see deeper meanings in images. This method was integral to their famed Donadi stain-paintings. *Before the Storm Category:Inner Rim sentient species
