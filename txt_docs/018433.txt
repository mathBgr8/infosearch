Rorworr was the name of two Wookiees: *Rorworr, a Wookiee from Kashyyyk during the Jedi Civil War *Rorworr, a Wookiee raised on Naboo, active during the Battle of Naboo, the Clone Wars, the Imperial Period, and the Post-Imperial era.
