Citadel 1
2305

Citadel 1 was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by the Rebel Alliance. Its mission was to rescue Mon Calamari slaves scheduled for transport to the Imperial Star Destroyer Warrior. Citadel 1 boarded the container Xi 1 containing the slaves and rescued them and, along with Citadel 2, boarded the freighter Wiggins and escaped into hyperspace, while the Rebel pilot, Halley Kadorto, fought of wave after wave of TIE Fighters and TIE Bombers that were attempting to destroy the convoy containing the slaves.X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
