Binarian sabercat
1714

The Binarian sabercat was a predator native to the planet Binaros in the Kathol Outback. A huge feline animal, the sabercat had eight powerful legs tipped with large paws ending in razor-sharp claws. The sabercat was able to bring four of these paws to bear on an opponent at any time. Huge incisors gave the sabercat a vicious bite. The sabercat also had a two-meter tail that ended in a bony tip. While dangerous looking, the sabercat did not use the tail to attack prey. Instead, it was used in the sabercat's mating rituals. Typical sabercats had thick brown-tan fur with green splotches. The coloration of the fur assisted the predator in stalking prey by giving it a natural camouflage.
