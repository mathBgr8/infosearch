Garang spaceport
1274

The Garang spaceport was a small spaceport servicing the city of Garang on Dantooine during the latter part of the Jedi Civil War. Constructed within the city of Garang on Dantooine, this spaceport was home to a small sub-orbital shipping business owned and operated by Rickard Lusoff and Calder Nettic for over 20 years; it was rumored by Handon Guld that either Nettic, Lusoff, or both had dealings with the Hutts. At some point Lusoff had accused Calder of cheating him in a business deal which ended violently. The Jedi Tooka was dispatched from the nearby Jedi Enclave to restore order and end the conflict. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Dantooine locations Category:Spaceports
