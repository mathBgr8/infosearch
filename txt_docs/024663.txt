Publictechnic
2606

Publictechnic was a droid manufacturer, devoted to large industrial-maintenance droids. Though active by 13 GrS, it was still regarded as a recent entry to the galactic droid market during the time of the Galactic Civil War. Its main manufacturing plant was on Sennatt, as was its primary service center and "droid shop." The company primarily sold to municipal and planetary governments.
