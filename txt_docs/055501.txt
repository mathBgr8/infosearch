Two-for-One was a Squib reclamation cruiser, part of the Squib Reclamation Fleet. Macemillian-winduarté was the Assistant-Deputy-Lieutenant-in-Chief-in-charge-of-Bartending on the cruiser, until he got left behind during a stopover on Tatooine. Category:Reclamation cruisers Category:Squib starships
