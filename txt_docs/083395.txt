Behareh Spaceport
704

Behareh Spaceport was a spaceport in Coronet City on Corellia, located several kilometers from the city's center. It was many acres large, but still considered small compared to other spaceports. It was divided into dozens of corporate sections, each with its own offices, hangars, and launch areas. One of those sections was leased to Donoslane Excursions. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coronet City locations Category:Spaceports
