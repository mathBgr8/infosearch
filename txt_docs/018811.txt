Pethros Vos
1368

Pethros Vos was a male Kiffu Guardian. He was married to Quian Vos with whom he had a son, Quinlan. Pethros and Quian were given as a sacrifice to the Anzati by Tinté Vos, in exchange for the Anzati feeding on the Kiffex prisoners. Pethros was remembered by his son, Quinlan, throughout his life.
