|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=French |occupation=*Editor *Visual effects editor |companies=Industrial Light & Magic |website= }} Nicolas Anastassiou (born August 11, 1971) is a visual effects editor at Industrial Light & Magic. He was part of the crews that worked on the films Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. Anastassiou also had a cameo role in Attack of the Clones as the Jedi Knight Nicanas Tassu. He later worked as an associate editor for the TV series Star Wars: The Clone Wars and a lead editor for Star Wars: The Bad Batch. {| ! Year !! Title !! Series !!
