Mich Flenn
1629

Mich Flenn was a Human male who worked as an old bar owner on Nar Shaddaa. In his younger days, he was a smuggler. When he earned enough money, he bought small bar in the Corellian Sector&mdash;the Blue Light. As an old man, Flenn was considered experienced, but rather strange. He was a good bartender and a neverending source of somewhat incredible, but possibly true stories. For example, he spoke of intelligent beings with supernatural powers, which could be a reference to Jedi or Sith and sometimes told stories to Han Solo about Ood Bnar and the Iron Knights.
