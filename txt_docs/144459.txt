Dobrow Devist
1751

*New Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Dobrow Devist was a clone of Soontir Fel. He was part of a sleeper cell of Fel clones planted by Grand Admiral Thrawn on the planet of Pakrik Minor. He later met all of the other members of their sleeper cell, Sabmin, Tabric, Carib and Hovarb.
