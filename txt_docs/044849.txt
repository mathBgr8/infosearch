Bonecrusher (creature)
873

Bonecrushers were Tyrannosaur-like creatures native to the planet Dandrian. They could grow to about the same size as an adult Wookiee and had gaping maws filled with sharp teeth. Sti Groon had a Bonecrusher which he used as his champion in gladiatorial challenges whenever people couldn't afford to pay him for information.'
