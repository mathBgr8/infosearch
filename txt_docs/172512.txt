Riane
1350

Riane was a captain in the Rebel Alliance who operated in the Brak sector during the Galactic Civil War. Captain Riane commanded a force consisting of a CR90 corvette and a Y-wing squadron during the successful capture of Convoy LMC-55c. *Flashpoint!
