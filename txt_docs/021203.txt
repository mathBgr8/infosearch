Mitochondria crunchy surprise

Mitochondria crunchy surprise was a food delicacy enjoyed by Ugors. The chef droid L9-G8 instructed the rebel crew of the Bonderium to cook this dish for an Ugorian feast, in order to bribe ArrGack and GrrKack for admission to the Paradise system. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Ugorian food
