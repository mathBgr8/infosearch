Great Bantha Breakfast Biscuit Run
683

The Great Bantha Breakfast Biscuit Run was established between the planets Sullust and Gastrula by the Sullustan Bungo Bung. For two weeks, Captain Bung transported large amounts of Bantha Breakfast Biscuits from Sullust to Gastrula, making several thousand credits. The run was ended, however, when it was discovered that the biscuits were mutating into Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuits.
