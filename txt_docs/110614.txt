The Alliance valued his connections on the Outer Rim Territories, and Alain served as an Alliance Fleet trooper aboard the CR90 corvette Tantive IV at some point prior to its destruction establishes the Tantive IV was destroyed during the Battle of Tatooine. in 0 BBY.The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Tatooine, during which the Tantive IV was destroyed, to 0 BBY. He was his unit's scavenger. Alain was fair-skinned with dark hair. During his service aboard the Tantive IV, Alain wore the standard Rebel fleet trooper uniform.The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook, Second Edition includes a depiction of the standard Alliance Fleet trooper uniforms, which matches the outfit worn by Alain. Alain was an idealistic Rebel trooper whom the Alliance valued for his Outer Rim connections. He wielded a DH-17 blaster pistol.
