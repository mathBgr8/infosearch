Rintatta City

Rintatta City was a major city of the planet Exocron. Talon Karrde and his group of smugglers landed at Circle 15 in the Rintatta City military landing fields when going to Exocron to visit Jorj Car'das. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities
