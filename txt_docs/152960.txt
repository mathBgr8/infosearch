Elek D'cel
2073

Elek D'cel was a skilled Caamasi male diplomat who had been offworld when Caamas was destroyed. Returning to his homeworld, he became both founder and administrator of Refuge City, an active reclamation project that had been intended to return Caamas to a livable state. Adorned in a shimmersilk noble's outfit and a full-length cloak, D'cel always had a breath mask at hand, as he frequently left Refuge City to take long, reflective strolls in the barrens beyond the compound.
