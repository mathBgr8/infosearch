Belgoa
1615

Emperor Belgoa was a Trunsk male who served as the emperor and nominal ruler of the planet Trunska during the Imperial era. In reality, he was little more than an Imperial puppet. Although his people, the Trunsks, were regarded as a slave species by the Empire, he did little to alleviate their suffering. While he publicly spoke out against the practice, he had a secret agreement with the local Moff allowing Imperials and other slavers to take Trunsks off-world. Belgoa was, however, allowed final say over which Trunsks would be taken. Belgoa ensured that his enemies were the first to be taken away.Alien Encounters Under Belgoa's rule, the Trunsks were forbidden by Imperial law to carry personal weapons or to pilot armed starships.
