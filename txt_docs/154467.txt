Laser cauterizer
2442

A laser cauterizer was a portable device used to disinfect and burn wounds shut by emitting a low-frequency laser beam. Medkits typically came equipped with these. Laser cauterizers were used for emergency field surgery. When activated, a laser cauterizer fired a low-frequency laser that could be used to cauterize wounds and disinfect them.Galladinium's Fantastic Technology Laser cauterizers were capable of resealing major blood vessels.The Old Republic: Fatal Alliance Laser cauterizers were included in medpacs, including Biotech Industries' FastFlesh medpac and Chiewab's GLiS Emergency Medpac. The field cauterizer was a specific type of laser cauterizer manufactured by Nilar Med/Tech Corporation.
