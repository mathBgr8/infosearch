Cortyg brandy
1921

Cortyg brandy was an incredibly hard drink brewed on Kashyyyk. It came in two different variations, the "wilder" side, drunk by Wookiees and the "tamer" Cortyg that tasted smoother. The "tamer" variant was sold to offworlders at high prices.
