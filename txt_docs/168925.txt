Karfeddion Spaceport
642

Karfeddion Spaceport was a spaceport on planet Karfeddion. It was filled with hangars, starship repair businesses and shady cantinas. One of its many docking bays was Docking Bay 331 where mercenary Arwen Cohl and his crew Boiny and Lope rendezvoused with several Nebula Front members in 33 BBY. *Cloak of Deception Category:Karfeddion locations Category:Spaceports
