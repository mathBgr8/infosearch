Newest Coast Town
516

Newest Coast Town was a colony on Ennth. It was destroyed by a massive thousand-meter-high tsunami caused by an underwater groundquake. While 80% of the supplies in the city were saved, a few hundred colonists died, and two starships were lost. *Young Jedi Knights: Shards of Alderaan Category:Cities on Ennth
