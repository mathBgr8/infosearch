QuickSnack
843

QuickSnacks were popular food bundles sold by the Biscuit Baron chain. They were also available in lighter versions. During the Separatist Crisis, Biscuit Baron launched its month-long "Heroes of the Republic" campaign, which consisted of including commemorative holocubes in QuickSnack bundles depicting notable historical figures of the Galactic Republic (including Supreme Chancellors Anwis Eddicus and Vaila Pecivas, and the Jedi Masters S'diawae, Ikrit, and Jojighar).
