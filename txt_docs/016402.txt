A desolate rock world with rich deposits of various ores, it was the first planet discovered by the Human scout Casti Tholon by 2 ABY.Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Tholon was employed by Rim Commercial Mining at the time, which named the planet after her. The Wild Space world proved very profitable for Rim Commercial Mining, establishing it as one of the leading Outer Rim Territories' mining companies, and was considered the most important planet Tholon had ever discovered. Tholon was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
