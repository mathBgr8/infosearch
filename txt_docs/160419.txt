Devaronian blood-poisoning
885

Devaronian blood-poisoning was an affliction, caused by contact with the Devaronian blood-poison gemstone, that would cause great pain to the victim once the absorbed particles reached the bloodstream. Once it reached the bloodstream, it released poison. *The Paradise Snare *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Diseases and maladies
