|mass= |hair=Red |feathers= |eyes=Red |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents=*ZalemStar Wars (1998) 24 *Ros Lai's father |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Nightsisters |masters= |apprentices= }} Ros Lai was a female Human hybrid who was a member of the Nightsisters that lived on the planet Dathomir. Ros Lai was the daughter of Nightsister Queen Zalem and a Jedi Knight. When she was very young, her mother killed her father, beginning a resentment which would last the rest of her life. Quietly plotting her revenge, Ros used the Force to transform her appearance into a displeasing elderly appearance. As a result, the other women of the clan called her "the Rancor," believing Ros Lai was crippled and therefore abused her, with her mother mocking her as well. Later, however, she came out when she was watched by Vonya, Yani and Leela, as she transformed back into her true form. Ros Lai killed Leela when she asked for forgiveness, but she declared that none of them could ever forgive. Yani fled and left Vonya alone with Ros Lai. Ros was not strong enough to face Vonya who almost killed her in a fight. At that time, she discovered the Jedi Quinlan Vos, who was posing as enslaved to investigate a plot by Lai's mother to reawaken the ancient power of the Infinity Gate. left|thumb|220x220px|Ros Lai metamorphsises to her true form. Ros Lai ran toward the star cruiser that belonged to the Givin Yag Shushin. She persuaded him to fly the ship to Quinlan to rescue him. Although the plan was successfully put into action, Quinlan was poisoned during his imprisonment with arterial worms. Therefore, Ros Lai infused him with some of her witch's blood due to having healing properties. Then they flew in the direction of the Infinity Gate, where Zalem was already using the super weapon to destroy the Galactic Republic's capital world of Coruscant. During their journey, they were discovered and shot by Vonya. Yag intercepted the shot that was supposed to kill Ros Lai and Quinlan Vos and died. Subsequently, their ship fired on Vonya and destroyed it, so they could go to Zalem to the stellar control station of the gate. In order to gain Zalem's confidence, Lai stabbed Quinlan Vos with her fire blade, but in reality, it was just an illusion so that Quinlan was not killed. Instead, Lai was successful in killing Zalem, thus fulfilling her quest in avenging her father's death. With Zalem out of the picture, Ros Lai assumed the mantle as leader of the Nightsisters and ordered them to kill Quinlan for good. However, this triggered a mechanism and killed the Nightsisters, only leaving Ros Lai to be stunned. Quinlan reversed the weapon, destroying the Infinity Gate itself. He fled with Ros Lai in Yag's starship to Coruscant where she was taken into custody. His reason to have Ros Lai alive was that he felt that there was good in her.
