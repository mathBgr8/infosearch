Tiree Captison
1479

Tiree Captison was the wife of Yeorg Captison. The sister-in-law of Dol Captison and the aunt/adoptive mother of Gaeriel Captison, Tiree married Yeorg Captison sometime before the latter became a senator in 26 BBY.
