Children's Museum
617

The Children's Museum was a museum on Coruscant, built circa 200 BBY. It chronicled the impact that young people had on the galaxy. Explorer Brin Hesk'l once claimed that he discovered a trigger mechanism on the floor of the museum, which opened a secret room where important documents were stored.
