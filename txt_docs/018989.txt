Holmar Grahrk
1720

Holmar Grahrk was a Devaronian, and, like his cousin Vilmarh was a smuggler, bounty hunter and all-round criminal. Holmar would later team up with Villie to collect the bounty on Quinlan Vos during his search for his Padawan Aayla Secura on Ryloth.
