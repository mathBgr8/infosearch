Magnetic bottle
1632

A magnetic bottle was a starship engine component. The component fed hydrogen to fuel systems. It was vital for ion drives. Many magnetic bottles were shielded by Polydex-9 shielding compounds. Failure of the magnetic bottle during flight could cause a large amount of damage to the starship.
