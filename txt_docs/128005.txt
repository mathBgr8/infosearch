Following the Battle of Endor, the New Republic, Galactic Empire represented by Admiral Uther Kermen, and warlord Utoxx Prentioch clashed over control of the food-rich world Kaal. Diplomats were assigned to reason out a settlement; however, after some time, Kermen grew tired. He used his flagship, Belligerent, to attack Prentioch's flagship, the aptly-named Prentioch. The battle was a stalemate and both ships, too damaged to continue, withdrew.
