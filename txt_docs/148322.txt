Model Kylan-3
2053

The Model Kylan-3 was a heavy blaster pistol manufactured by the Sullustan SoroSuub Corporation. Jokingly referred to as a Sullustan Blast Rifle, it was produced under Imperial contract, though large numbers of them fell into rebel hands. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *Arms & Equipment Guide Category:Heavy blaster pistols Category:SoroSuub Corporation products
