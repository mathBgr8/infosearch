ExcargansThe Essential Reader's Companion were a Near-HumanAlthough Boba Fett: Crossfire uses the word "humanoid" for Garr, it further says Garr is "like Boba [Fett]," who is Human, and compares their hair and skin colors. From this description, it can be reasonably assumed that Excargans are Near-Human. sentient species native to Excarga. During childhood, they were genderless; at around thirteen years old, their bodies would change and their gender would be determined. Many inhabitants of their planet worked for ore-processing corporations. They were attacked by the Separatists during the Clone Wars, which led to Republic intervention, and they sold materials to the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War.
