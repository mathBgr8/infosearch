Moori
1798

Moori was the designation of a group of two Assassin-class corvettes under the service of the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. They were part of the Rebels' second attempt to destroy Space installation NL-1 during the Battle on the Frontier, but they were destroyed by Imperial Ace Maarek Stele. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
