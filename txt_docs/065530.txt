The Slasher Sun Times was a news reporting corporation on the planet Peekoine. Rojene Eborsisk wrote an article on Master Thespian Damien Tantrellius' play, Jedi Men From Baronis, at Imperial Startime 45686.3467 for the Sun Times. Category:News publications
