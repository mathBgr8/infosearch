The cruiser measured 850 meters in length and ran on a Damorian power plant. Weapon systems included ten heavy turbolasers and twenty ion cannons, a light armament for the cruiser's size. Much of the ship's interior was occupied by modular compartments, but most vessels sacrificed those for docking bay facilities.
