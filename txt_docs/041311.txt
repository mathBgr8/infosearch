During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. the woman visited the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars Chronicles: The Prequels Coruscant. During the visit, the bounty hunter Zam Wesell ran into the club to hide from the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker after her assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala. When the Jedi entered, Wo, with a cup in hand, walked with the woman Hayde Gofai near the bar, ending up behind the Jedi as they separated. As Skywalker searched the club, Wo talked with Gofai and the alien Ame Llom. At the club, Wo wore a dark colored top and skirt, a dark fur coat, and heels. The woman had a feminine first name of Human origin.Galactic Campaign Guide Sne Wo first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The Outlander Club scenes including Wo, who was portrayed by an uncredited extra were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney on July 17, 2000.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones The extra playing Sne Wo, in costume, introduced the eighth episode of the Episode II behind-the-scenes video series on location alongside the extra playing Hayde Gofai and another extra, as well as introducing episode ten by themselves. The eighth episode, "George Lucas in Lunch", was released on September 19, 2000. Wo was first named on April 6, 2004, with the release of the Daniel Wallace written "Who's Who in the Outlander Club: It's Ladies' Night", an article released in the seventy-fifth issue of Star Wars Insider.
