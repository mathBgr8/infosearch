S'kytric
713

S'kytric was the native language of the S'kytri species from the planet Skye. S'kytric had been considered a dead language until it became the basis of a folk revival due to its use by musicians and artists. Most S'kytri were fluent in both S'kytric and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Star Wars Annual (1977) 1 *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages Category:S'kytri culture
