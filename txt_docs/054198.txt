Reptor
1252

Reptor was Jowart Typok's personalized repulsorlift transport. He had converted the vehicle's passenger space into a cargo hold which, while he was hunting on Entooine, held dozens of blue bantha horns as well as a single-person survival tent, a sleeping bag, a glowrod, flares, and a comlink.
