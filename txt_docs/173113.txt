|pages=8 |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Topside Infiltration" is a Star Wars Roleplaying Game Adventure published in Star Wars Gamer 8. It was written by Peter M. Schweighofer and illustrated by Carl Critchlow. A group of spacers must infiltrate Gallo Memm's estate on Vaynai. They must sneak past the guards and rob Memm's vault.
