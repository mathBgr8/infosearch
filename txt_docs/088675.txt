Battle of Ossus (Outer Rim Sieges)
2713

A battle, one of the opening engagements of the Outer Rim Sieges, was fought in 20 BBY during the Clone Wars on the planet Ossus. Jedi Master Tholme sent an elite contingent of ARC Troopers to infiltrate the Separatist base.
