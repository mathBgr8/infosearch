Galactic Alliance Intelligence officer
1585

thumb|right|200px|A Galactic Alliance Intelligence officer tips his hat. Galactic Alliance Intelligence officer were officers of the Galactic Alliance Intelligence Service. During the Second Imperial Civil War, three of them accompanied Admiral Gar Stazi of the Galactic Alliance Remnant to The Wheel and served as bodyguards while Stazi negotiated with Captain Mingo Bovark of the Empire-in-exile. They engaged in a brief lightfight with the Imperial Knights Antares Draco and Ganner Krieg after the negotiations were sabotaged by agents of Darth Krayt's Galactic Empire.
