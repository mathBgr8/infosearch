Museum of Fine Art
891

The Museum of Fine Art was an art museum based in Coronet City, Corellia. By the time of the Cold War, a diamond formed from the compressed ashes of celebrated author Custo Ramak was on display in the museum. Thousands of years later, Corran Horn's mother regularly volunteered for it and in a private showing one New Year's Day her husband, Hal, and son were brought in as extra security. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *"Side Trip" *Suns of Fortune Category:Coronet City locations Category:Museums
