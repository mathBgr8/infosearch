Jedi Flow
729

Jedi Flow was a game taught to young Jedi Padawans. It helped hone their senses and familiarize them with the flow of the Force around them. Kit Fisto taught this game to several clone troopers while on Cestus.The Cestus Deception In the afterword to The Cestus Deception Steven Barnes likened body-flow to Jedi practice. *The Cestus Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Jedi training
