Resist Stun
3228

Resist Stun was a Control-based Force power. It allowed Force-users to resist the effects of being stunned by preparing themselves to resist the effects of stun-based weaponry before they were hit. Successful application of the Force power allowed the user to absorb the effects into their body to keep themselves from being temporarily paralyzed or knocked out. *Heir to the Empire *Fate of the Jedi: Abyss Category:Control powers
