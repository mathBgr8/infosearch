Minor Relsted
1193

Minor Relsted was an official of the Galactic Empire. He was initially an assistant to Supervisor Gurdun during which he skimmed funds to create the IG series of assassin droids. When Gurdun left Coruscant to supervise the acquistion and delivery of the Death Star II's computer core, Relsted took Gurdun's old position and office. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Relsted, Minor Relsted, Minor
