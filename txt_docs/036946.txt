Gellefon Freedom Fighters
1191

}} The Gellefon Freedom Fighters were a group of Human settlers from the planet Gellefon who had joined Aran Starfire's bid to drive off the Imperial presence from their planet. They were extremely dedicated and had trained well. Aran demanded his followers learn all they could and conducted many of the training drills for the group himself. A select number of the Freedom Fighters had bonded with the native Gellbeasts and rode them into battle against the Imperials. Category:Resistance groups
