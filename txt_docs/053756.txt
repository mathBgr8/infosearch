Firkrann (Jedi)
1636

Firkrann was an Iron Knight, a Force-sensitive Shard Jedi who utilized an FLTCH-series battle droid body as a host. Firkrann was trained by Jedi Master Aqinos without the approval of the Jedi Council. Like many other Iron Knights, Firkrann took a lightsaber crystal name as his own, reflecting both his connection to the Force and his crystalline body.
