Manufactured by Kuat Drive Yards and measuring 1,600 meters in length, it served as the flagship of Grand Admiral Danetta Pitta, who was killed while commanding a torpedo sphere in the Battle of Tralus of 5 ABY.The Essential Guide to Warfare The Impenetrable was originally created by Daniel Wallace and Abel G. Peña and was to be mentioned in an article on the Galactic Empire's Grand Admirals that was written for an issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine.
