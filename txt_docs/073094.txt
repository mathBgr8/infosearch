|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 5 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=19 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The New Essential Chronology dates to 19 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Order of Outcasts" is a comic story from Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 5. It is written by Matt Jacobs, illustrated by the Fillbach Brothers, and colored by Lee Evandon. Jedi Master Sev and his Padawan, Joc Sah, are stationed on the remote planet of Ragmar V to stage an assault on a Separatist base. Sev, coordinating the attack from his command ship, instructs Sah to secure an outpost. The obedient Padawan, along with clone Sergeant Remy, discovers that the locals are not friendly to the Republic and so must take the outpost by force. Walking into the small town, they are met by a group of townspeople, only a fraction of their force, the rest lying in wait atop the buildings preparing to spring their ambush. Before anything can happen, Remy receives Order 66 and promptly fires upon Sah. Realizing that the Jedi is siding with them, the townspeople spring their ambush upon the clones, obliterating the force. Contacting Sev, the Padawan is shocked to find that there is no answer, something that can only mean that his Master has been killed.
