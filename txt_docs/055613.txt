Attack pattern d'moporai
977

Attack pattern d'moporai was a Chiss Expansionary Defense Force battle tactic that involved starfighters approaching from different vectors and launching shock nets into a concentrated point of enemy fighter strength. This was done to create a hole in the enemy's fighter screen so that capital ships could advance. *Outbound Flight Category:Chiss Ascendancy military tactics Category:Starfighter tactics
