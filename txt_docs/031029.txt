Relentless (disambiguation)
1518

Relentless could refer to several things: *Relentless, an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer commanded by Admiral Kassius Konstantine. *Relentless, a cruiser commanded by Captain Meeba during the Clone Wars. *Relentless, a Star Destroyer commanded by Moff Vanko. *Relentless, an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer commanded by Captain Dorja and used by Moff Vilim Disra as a flagship during the latest point of its history. *Relentless, an Imperial I-class Star Destroyer that attempted to destroy the B-wing project. *Relentless, a Pellaeon-class Star Destroyer commanded by Admiral Dru Valan. *Relentless, a Sith Imperial ship used by Darth Krayt as his royal transport.
