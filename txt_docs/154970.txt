Erla
1175

Erla was a female Kaminoan who resided on the planet Ahakista and was an associate of Sardoth. Erla was likely an employee of Raze, Sardoth's employer, as well. Along with Sardoth, Erla watched the assassination of Councilman Derral and discussed the escalation of Ahakista's civil war. *Rebellion 6 *Rebellion 7 Category:Ahakistans Category:Females Category:Kaminoans
