Zehethbra
603

Zehethbra was the native language of the ZeHethbra species from ZeHeth. It included snarls, growls, chitters, and angry shrieks, among other features. Most ZeHethbra were fluent in both the Zehethbra language and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
