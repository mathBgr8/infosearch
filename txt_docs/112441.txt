Battle of Brentaal IV (New Sith Wars)
2158

The Battle of Brentaal IV was the last battle of Kaan's campaign to capture the Bormea sector for the Brotherhood of Darkness. It was an easy Sith victory. It occurred just after the Third Battle of Ruusan, although Kaan was unaware of the battle there until after Kopecz arrived at Brentaal to inform him. *Darth Bane: Path of Destruction Category:Battles of Brentaal IV Category:Battles of the Light and Darkness War
