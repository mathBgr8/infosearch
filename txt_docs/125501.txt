Ootoowerg
2142

Ootoowergs were vegetables that were often served with a Kommerken steak. When Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa visited Mimban, they ordered such a meal in a restaurant. During the Galactic Civil War, a group of privateers from the Far Orbit were to meet an agent of the Rebel Alliance at Lean Nuuti Bar and Grill on StarForge Station.
