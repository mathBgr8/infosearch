Mixtwirk
687

The Mixtwirk were a small insectoid alien race with sensitive antennae on their heads, which they used for hearing. They communicated in squawks. A Mixtwirtk and a Rodian patron of Stock Lights overheard Cryle Cavv and his nephew Sienn Sconn discussing plans to steal a Imperial Star Destroyer, and were ejected from the premises by Cavv.
