Kevin Schmitt
667

Kevin Schmitt is an employee for LucasArts. He was the Lead Level Designer for Star Wars: Rogue Squadron, Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith and Star Wars: Republic Commando. He also voiced TK8252 in Mysteries of the Sith. He is friends with Ryan Kaufman, who named Gavin Zmitt after him.
