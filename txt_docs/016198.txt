Antar 5
2504

Antar 5 was a moon of Antar colonized by the Gotal as a mining operation. Located in the Inner Rim, the moon was situated in the Prindaar system. Antar 5, along with its sister moon Antar 4, joined the Galactic Republic early in its history, sometime between 25,000 BBY and 22,000 BBY. Settled by the Gotal, the moon was mined for its rich resources, most likely including magnetite.
