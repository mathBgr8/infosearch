Military Police (disambiguation)
1931

Military Police can refer to several different law enforcement agencies in various military forces: *Clone military police, the military police of the Galactic Republic that was part of the Grand Army of the Republic. *Imperial Army Military Police, the military police of the Galactic Empire that was part of the Imperial Army. *Military Police, the military police of the Galactic Empire that was part of the Imperial Navy. *Military Police, the military police of the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. *Shore police, the law enforcement branch of the New Republic Defense Fleet. *Military Police, the military police of the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars *Imperial Military Police, the military police of the Galactic Empire *CompForce, the political military police of the Galactic Empire *Alliance Security, the military police of the Alliance to Restore the Republic *New Republic Military Police, the military police of the New Republic *Galactic Alliance Military Police, the military police of the Galactic Alliance
