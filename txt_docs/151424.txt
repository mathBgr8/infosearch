Indomitable (Imperial I-class)
1807

The Indomitable was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer. It was constructed at the Kuat Drive Yards shipyards on Kuat in 3 ABY. Shannon Voorson was able to slice its name along with several other Star Destroyer names and gave it to her cousin, Deen Voorson, a member of the Rebel Alliance. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Imperial-class Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
