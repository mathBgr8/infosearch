It was formed out of the New Republic First Battle Group. Under the command of Admiral Traest Kre'fey, the First Fleet was stationed at Dac. The First Fleet was a major component of the Alliance Battle of Yuuzhan'tar. A decade later, the First once again found itself on the front lines of conflict.
