Unidentified tenth moon of Storm
2651

The tenth moon of the gas giant planet Storm was home to a Tagge Company mine. The moon was located in the Naboo system, a part of the Chommell sector in the Trailing Sectors portion of the Mid Rim. Along with the rest of its star system, it was situated along the hyperlane known as the Enarc Run, as well as on the Old Trade Federation Route. The moon was first mentioned by Secrets of Naboo, a 2001 sourcebook for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game.
