Southern Wastes
884

The Southern Wastes were an area on the Ubese homeworld, Uba IV. Presumably, they were wastelands formed after the planet was bombarded during the bombardment of the Uba system. Circa 3 ABY, it was home to several Ubese clans, including Clan-Vorsazg, a clan led by War-Master Savax. These clans, possibly with Hutt assistance, manned a flotilla of capital ships to attack the yrak pootzck Ubese of Ubertica. They managed to lay waste to several Ubertican cities before being destroyed by Imperial forces. Category:Planetary regions and territories Category:Uba system locations
