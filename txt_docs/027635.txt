Twi'lek Ruling Council
2221

The Twi'lek Ruling Council was the ruling council of Ryloth's capital city of Lessu. Centralized in the capital city of Lessu, the Ruling Council was formed by five male Twi'leks who represented the Twi'lek clans, and were responsible for community decisions in the city. This system was extremely effective for hundreds of years. When one councilmember died, all the remaining members were to walk the Bright Lands, where they would die for certain.
