Celador Sash
1664

The Celador Sash was a twin-hulled freighter donated to the New Jedi Order. The parents of its original owner were once saved by Obi-Wan Kenobi and in gratitude the ship was gifted to the Academy some time before 12 ABY. Although it was outdated by that time, it was kept in a good state of repairs. It was equipped with a modified navicomputer that included a 3D holomap. Kyp Durron and Dorsk 82 flew the ship on their mission to Corbos. *Jedi Academy: Leviathan 1 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Freighters Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order
