Crin Harson
1154

Crin Harson was an Imperial Moff during the Galactic Civil War. Crin Harson was discovered dead in his room on the resort world of Tralfin, shortly after the Battle of Yavin. A group of resting Alliance personnel were implicated in the crime, but were later cleared of any murder charges. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males Category:Moffs of the Galactic Empire
