Meido
1711

Meido was a male non-Human senator of the New Republic who represented Adin circa 17 ABY. He was one of the senators elected after the New Republic amnesty bill allowed former supporters of the Galactic Empire to run for elected offices within the government that had replaced it and openly accused Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo of causing the Bombing of the Senate Hall. *The New Rebellion *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males Category:New Republic Senators Category:Personnel of the Galactic Empire
