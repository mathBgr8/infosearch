Tavares
1095

Tavares was a Human male and an Admiral who served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. He was part of a group of Imperial officers who were present, along with Darth Vader, at a ceremony near the Kuat system where the Star Dreadnought Executor was officially commissioned.
