Jennifer Kay
530

Jennifer Kay is a Star Wars fan who designed the Zybahhod Yenyoni and the udjudg in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was picked 3rd place in the 16 years and older category. She was 26 at the time her entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
