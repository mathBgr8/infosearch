Magna bolas
593

Magna bolas consisted of three heavy, metallic balls attached by a thin wire. They were twirled and thrown at a target, and if aimed correctly, their natural rotation entangled the target in the wire. The metal balls were then activated, producing a strong magnetic attraction which made the bolas nearly impossible to escape.Coruscant and the Core Worlds *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Bolas Category:Magnetic acceleration weaponry
