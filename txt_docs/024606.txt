New Republic Ruling Council
3382

The New Republic Ruling Council was the senior decision making body of the New Republic and was composed of the Chief of State and and the senators that chaired each of the six sub-committees of the New Republic Senate. If a Vote of No Confidence was brought against the Chief of State, the council would review its legitimacy, then vote on whether or not the motion should be brought before the Senate. The Chief of State could bypass a vote by declaring a leave of absence and nominating one of the council to become caretaker Chief of State. It was the replacement of the New Republic Provisional Council.
