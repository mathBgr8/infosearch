|published in=Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 16 |reprinted in=*The Star Wars Compendium 2: The World of Star Wars *Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club |pages= |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Cantina Communications" is a short story written by John Chesterman, originally published in Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 16, The Star Wars Compendium 2: The World of Star Wars, and later archived on Hyperspace. As Obi-Wan Kenobi searches through the crowd at the Mos Eisley cantina, Luke Skywalker reflects on the various forms of communication used by the different alien species in the room. He recalls his and Obi-Wan's earlier discussion of which species makes the best pilots. This article marks the first and only appearance of several alien species, such as the sentient ocean of Solanus, the giant algae beds of the Cygnus B system, Klytonians, a particular species of olfaxes, and an unidentified species of emotionless multiple-eyed insectoids described as the best pilots in the galaxy. *Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 16 *Elis Helrot *Kardue'sai'Malloc *Obi-Wan Kenobi *M'iiyoom Onith *Luke Skywalker *Solanus's sentient ocean *Han Solo *Feltipern Trevagg |organisms= *Dog *Olfax |droids= |events= |locations= *Cygnus B system *Solanus *Tatooine **Mos Eisley ***Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina |organizations= *Galactic Empire **Stormtrooper |species= *Devaronian *Giant algae-bed *Gas cloud *Givin *Gotal *H'nemthe *Human *Klytonian *Kubaz |vehicles= |technology= |miscellanea= *Universal Encyclopedia }} Category:Legends short stories Category:Star Wars Official Poster Monthly articles
