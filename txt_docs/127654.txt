Jexxel
1121

Jexxels were tundra-dwelling, white-furred, red-eyed felines. They had serrated front claws and a mouthful of razor-sharp fangs. Their eyes glowed in the dark and gave them excellent night-vision. They were vicious predators that were very territorial and aggressive.
