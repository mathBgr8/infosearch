Teles Jalahafi
1489

Teles Jalahafi was a male Human and a Dark Side Adept, whose home planet was Edan II. The brother of Shela Jalahafi, Teles Jalahafi served the Galactic Empire as a Dark Side adept, like his sister. However, Teles lacked his sister's strength in the dark side of the Force, and continually showed that he was less powerful than her. After Shela was lost in an attack in Fortuna City, Teles tracked the Rebel Alliance to the wreckage of the CR90 corvette Last Hope, and perished in the confrontation. *Introductory Adventure Game *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dark side adepts Category:Humans Category:Males
