The home offices of the Ragnar Syndicate, a chapter house of the Bounty Hunters' Guild, were located on Ragnar VIII, leading to the celestial body being considered the homeworld of the house. Ragnar VIII was listed as the Ragnar headquarters in an entry for the house included in the Bounty Hunters Guild Handbook.The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Captain Zgorth'sth of the New Republic eventually compiled available information on the Ragnar Syndicate as part of a threat assessment report focusing on various special units. Zgorth'sth claimed the syndicate would be able to defend Ragnar VIII against New Republic military actions and instead suggested to assorted New Republic military leaders that bounties should be posted on individual Ragnar bounty hunters. Ragnar VIII was first mentioned in the 1994 sourcebook Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters, written by Rick D. Stuart for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
