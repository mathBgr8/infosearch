Watto's landspeeder
1706

This landspeeder was a repulsorlift vehicle owned by Watto on Tatooine. It was a sleek, thin-bodied speeder with a tall tail fin and side-mounted engines. Prior to his fateful meeting with Qui-Gon Jinn and Padmé Amidala, Anakin Skywalker piloted Watto's landspeeder to a Jawa droid sale at Mochot Steep. On the return journey, Anakin encountered and assisted a wounded Tusken Raider. *Episode I Adventures 8: Trouble on Tatooine *Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace novelization *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Landspeeders
