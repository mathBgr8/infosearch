Pliada di am Imperium
952

The Pliada di am Imperium was a plaza at the western base of the Imperial Palace in Imperial City, Coruscant. It formed the eastern nexus of the Glitannai Esplanade, where it terminated at the Palace. It hosted a ceremony that celebrated the reorganization of COMPOR just after the Galactic Empire was established. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide Category:Coruscant public spaces
