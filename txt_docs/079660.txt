Unlimited Horizons Incorporated, also known as Unlimited Horizons Inc. or UHI, was a company on Kalla VII which controlled most of the pallodenite reserves in the Corporate Sector. The company also owned the ship Amanda's Toy II, which was one of many Luke Skywalker tracked near the planet Teyr in 16 ABY. *Shield of Lies *Fate of the Jedi: Apocalypse Category:Companies
