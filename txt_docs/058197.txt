In 15 BBY, establishes that the animated series Star Wars: Droids: The Adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO takes place fifteen years prior to the events of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, which corresponds to 15 BBY according to The New Essential Chronology. the Fromm Gang leader Tig Fromm thought about vacationing on Borga after a plan of his failed, speculating that its swamps were nearly habitable during that time of year. Borga was mentioned in "The White Witch," the first episode of the animated television series Star Wars: Droids, which was written by Peter Sauder and premiered on September 7, 1985. The StarWars.com Online Companion to the 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Borga system, and therefore Borga, in grid square S-14.
