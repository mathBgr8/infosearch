Waw
1023

A waw was a tree that was pale green in color. The tree was known to the Ithorian species, and some Ithorian females had "Owaw" as a given name after the waw's leaves. The waw was mentioned in the 2003 reference book Galactic Campaign Guide, written by Peter M. Schweighofer and J.D. Wiker and published by Wizards of the Coast for use with the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Trees
