Shotgun (CR90 corvette)
999

thumb|right|250px|Corvette Shotgun during the [[Battle of Mylok IV|Conflict at Mylok IV.]] Shotgun was a CR90 corvette. It was used by Nharwaak forces to protect their base of operations at the Mylok IV Conflict during the Galactic Civil War.
