Mark II assault droid
1863

The Mark II assault droid was a battle droid model. Quadrupedal in form, this battle droid was armed for melee combat, featuring two bludgeoning armatures that could deliver brutal blunt-force trauma to unarmored or unprepared opponents. These droids were sometimes outfitted with body-mounted energy deflector shields. It was used by the Galactic Republic and later the Sith Empire prior to and during the Mandalorian Wars and Jedi Civil War. These droids also sometimes fell into the hands of swoop gangs, such as the Hidden Beks and Black Vulkars of Taris.
