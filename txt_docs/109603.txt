Founding Shipwright
854

A "Founding Shipwright" was one of the major starship corporations at the dawn of the Galactic Republic, with many of them lasting throughout most of the Old Republic's history. They included Core Galaxy Systems, Alderaan Royal Engineers, Rendili StarDrive, and possibly the Corellian Engineering Corps. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Vehicle manufacturing companies
