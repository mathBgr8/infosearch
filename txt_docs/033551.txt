Caroline de Souza Correa
1708

Caroline de Souza Correa (born May 19, 1979) is a Brazilian actress and model who portrayed Sheltay Retrac, one of Senator Bail Organa's aides, in Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. Correa was born in Maringá, Paraná, Brazil. She moved to Sydney, Australia at age 20. She occasionally travels back to Brazil to visit. She speaks Brazilian Portuguese and stands 5 feet and 8 inches.
