Omman was an agriworld located in the Tion Cluster along the Desevran Trace. Omman's agricultural production made it a competitor of neighboring Folende, another agriworld. Omman became the site of an Imperial garrison after the Declaration of a New Order. The Empire used the planet as a base from which it could keep watch on the Calamari sector and monitor Rebel activity in the troubled Back Spiral region of the Tion Hegemony. The planet Omman was introduced in the novelWhen the Domino Falls, placed near Socorro. But after another "Omman" was mentioned in the Tion Cluster, the first was identified as Omman Minor by the The Essential Atlas Online Companion.
