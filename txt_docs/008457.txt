Cruiser (Naboo)
1635

thumb|left|250px|Naboo Cruiser (top row); Heavy Naboo Cruiser (bottom row) Naboo Cruisers were aquatic naval vessels used by the Royal Naboo Security Forces. They featured a cannon and could be upgraded with heavier armor and a second cannon. The upgraded vessels were known as Heavy Naboo Cruisers. These vessels appear in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds and its 2003 expansion pack. They are only buildable by playing the Naboo civilization in multiplayer. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Aquatic vehicle models Category:Naboo vehicle classes
