Tibanna tapper

A Tibanna tapper was a person who stole Tibanna gas. Jaina Solo and Zekk stopped some Tibanna tappers on Bespin who were using a tug and three siphoning balloons in 36 ABY. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminal occupations Category:Slang
