Forzi Ghul
2045

Forzi Ghul was a male Jedi Initiate studying at the Jedi Order's Coruscant Temple academy during the Clone Wars. A Force-sensitive male, Forzi Ghul was inducted into the Jedi Order and received his training in the ways of the Force at the Coruscant Jedi Temple. When the Clone Wars erupted between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Ghul remained at the Temple and participated in the 19 BBY Jedi Temple Apprentice Tournament.
