|height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire **Shadow wing |masters= |apprentices= }} PS-29-3, also known as "Hammer," was a TIE pilot in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. PS-29-3 flew under the call sign Shadow Three in the Imperial Shadow wing. He recorded the highest score in academy TIE fighter simulator drills, a fact that caught Admiral Conan Antonio Motti's attention. PS-29-3's starfighter had a command system capable of coordinating the combined power of the entire Shadow wing. He wore a black TIE pilot jumpsuit and helmet, and had designs on being the leading pilot of Shadow wing.
