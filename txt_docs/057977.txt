Raynor Mining Enterprises
912

Raynor Mining Enterprises was a prospecting and mining corporation that existed during the time of the Old Republic. Founded by Trebold Raynor, the company was somewhat notorious for their strategy of buying a planet, harvesting all available resources, (whether they be mineral, ore, or crystal) and then abandoning it. *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Rise of the Hutt Cartel *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Mining companies
