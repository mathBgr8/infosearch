Team Kothlis

|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Cup of Limmie }} Team Kothlis was a limmie team that played for the planetThe Essential Guide to Planets and Moons of Kothlis in the Galactic Cup of Limmie. In 13:3, during the Separatist Crisis, they defeated the planet of Ord Mantell's team with a single point in the Cup's opening round. The CoCo District edition of the HoloNet News covered the result of the match in an article. Team Kothlis was mentioned in Coruscant Eliminated in Galactic Cup Opening Round an article written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. and published in HoloNet News Vol. 531 47 on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002.
