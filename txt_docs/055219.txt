Domain Qel
1503

Domain Qel was a domain of the Yuuzhan Vong shaper caste. During the Yuuzhan Vong War in 26 ABY, Domain Qel was among the smaller minor shaper domains. Yuuzhan Vong from the domain bore forehead marks that distinguished them as members of their domain, such as the young shaper Tsun Qel. Qel helped the Master Shaper Yal Phaath acquire the evidence needed to expose the heretical Master Shaper Mezhan Kwaad by posing as a fellow heretic to Kwaad's apprentice, Nen Yim.
