|release date=March 31, 2003 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=A New Hope |followed by=Jedi Guardians }} Battle of Yavin was the fourth expansion card pack made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game produced by Wizards of the Coast, based on Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope&mdash;in particular, the elements of the Battle of Yavin. The expansion symbol is that of the first Death Star. The expansion was released in March 31, 2003 and contains 105 cards. It was sold in boosters containing 11 (7 common, 3 uncommon, 1 rare) cards. *New Abilities: **Retaliate - If a unit in the same arena as this unit is attacking this unit, this unit does X dice of damage to that unit when the attack ends *Card Types: **Battle: 19 **Character: 39 **Ground: 21 **Mission: 7 **Space: 19 Category:Star Wars Trading Card Game
