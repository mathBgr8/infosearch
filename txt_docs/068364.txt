Frozen Polus Ocean
690

The Frozen Ocean of Polus was the only ocean on the ice planet Polus. Its surface was entirely iced over, but beneath the surface, the bulk of water remained liquid, supporting many species of aquatic life. *Star Wars: Empire at War *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Expansion: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Seas and oceans
