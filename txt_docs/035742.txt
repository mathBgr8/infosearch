Andra (Telos IV)
1407

Andra was a female Human native from Telos IV. She was the founder and sole member of the Preserve Our Wild Endangered Resources Party, and with the aide of the criminal Denetrus, she helped Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi foil Xanatos' plot to bring Offworld Mining Corporation onto Telos.
