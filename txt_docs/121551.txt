Halm (Human)
1376

Halm was a male Human member of Talon Karrde's organization and was assigned to go to Yavin 4 with Karrde, Shada D'ukal, and Ferson to protect the Jedi students. He was shot and killed by a member of the Peace Brigade as they blockaded the Yavin system. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Car'das members Category:Humans Category:Males
