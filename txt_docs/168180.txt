Dedicated energy receptor projector
856

The dedicated energy receptor projector, or DER Projector, was a component of the 4x-Phantom Short-Range Sensor Jammer utilized aboard the RZ-1 A-wing interceptor manufactured by Miradyne Limited. It worked by absorbing and blocking incoming long-range sensor pings and transmissions broadcast by enemy ships, rendering the starfighter invisible to most enemy scanners, targeting computers, and incoming missiles.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Countermeasures
