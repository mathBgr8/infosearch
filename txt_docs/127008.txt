Hyos
1230

Hyos was a female Codru-Ji doctor on Munto Codru while Chief of State Leia Organa Solo made a diplomatic visit there in 14 ABY. When a pressure bomb had gone off, and the Solo children had been kidnapped and Chewbacca was badly injured, she was the doctor who treated him.
