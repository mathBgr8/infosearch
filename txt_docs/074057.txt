Krath blood blade
1597

A Krath blood blade was a weapon of the Krath Sith cult. These weapons were specifically to resistant to an attack from a Jedi's lightsaber and as such the metal was weaved with cortosis ore. These weapons were often used by Krath assassins and many Sith believed that the weapon predated the lightsaber.
