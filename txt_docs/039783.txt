|class= |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions= |lifespan= |origin= |habitat= |diet= |language= }} Fneebs were a sentient species. In 0 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of "A Hunter's Fate: Greedo's Tale" within Chalmun's Cantina to 0 BBY. a number of Fneeb bounty hunters were present on the planet Tatooine within Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in the Mos Eisley spaceport. The Rodian bounty hunter Greedo noticed the Fneebs among the other patrons while he drank Pica Thundercloud with fellow hunters Dyyz Nataz and Spurch Goa. The Fneeb were created by Tom and Martha Veitch for the short story "A Hunter's Fate: Greedo's Tale," published in 1995 as part of the anthology Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina.
