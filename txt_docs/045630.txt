|release date=July 2, 2004 |figures= |cards= |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Rogues and Scoundrels |followed by=Return of the Jedi }} The Phantom Menace was an expansion card pack made for the Star Wars Trading Card Game based on Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace and elements of the Expanded Universe in the Rise of the Empire era. The expansion symbol is that for the Prequel era.
