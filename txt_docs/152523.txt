Storm (CR90 corvette)
1814

Storm was a CR90 corvette used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. Its mission was to capture Ilay, who was on her Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser Kala'din. Despite attempts to destroy it by Assault Gunboats from Talon Squadron and the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Rage, it managed to capture Ilay and escape into hyperspace. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
