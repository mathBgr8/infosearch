Personal rocket packs are small rocket packs, with many made by Zim Systems. Jango Fett owned two rocket packs: a Merr-Sonn JT-12 and a Mitrinomon Z-6, a model later used by his clone son Boba Fett. *Tales of the Jedi Companion Category:Rocket packs
