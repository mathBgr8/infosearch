Tarl (Ord Cestus)
1022

Tarl was a male Human individual who is the son of Yander, a native miner of Ord Cestus, who once was the husband of Sheeka Tull. He had a sister, Tonoté, and a brother, Mithail. Tarl had a burly build. *The Cestus Deception Category:Cestians Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Miners
