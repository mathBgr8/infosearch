Ahto Luxury Resorts
875

Ahto Luxury Resorts was a hotel in East Ahto during the Imperial era. It was run by Olodondo Berzix, and was advertised in the Guide to Beautiful Manaan. However, the descriptions bore little semblance to the reality: bad food, polluted water, cheating sabacc dealers, decrepit facilities, and a large criminal population. Zavol Shan stayed there only once, in Bungalow 17. The hotel was destroyed, and its patrons killed, in the Battle of Ahto. Category:Hotels Category:Manaan locations
