Vector Day
1113

Vector Day was an annual holiday. Mrp-Mrp Poo was known to throw parties for this occasion at the Boulderdash terrarium.Monsters and Aliens from George Lucas Vector Day was mentioned in Bob Carrau's Monsters and Aliens from George Lucas, a book published in 1993. Lucasfilm employee Leland Chee, who maintains the Holocron continuity database, has indicated that information contained in the book is non-canonical. *Monsters and Aliens from George Lucas Category:Holidays
