Kyber Royal Family
904

The Kyber Royal Family was the ruling family of the planet Troska, which directly controlled the planet's fuel refineries. In the time of the Galactic Empire, it only grudgingly supported the Imperial garrison and frequently protested Imperial taxation of its fuel revenues. Members of the family included the King Natas Kyber and his son, Prince Torino. *Boba Fett: Overkill Category:Families Category:Humans
