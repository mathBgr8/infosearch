Corellia Digital
905

Corellia Digital was a large datacard manufacturer at the time of the Galactic Civil War. Secondary product lines included hologram platforms, used to display small decorative holographic images. This company, along with the Bay-Suwe Combine, is mentioned in the description of most hologram rewards in Star Wars Galaxies. *Star Wars Galaxies: Rage of the Wookiees *Star Wars Galaxies: Trials of Obi-Wan *Suns of Fortune Category:Technology companies
