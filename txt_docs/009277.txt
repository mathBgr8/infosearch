LoBue Cantina
2008

Located in the Abregado-rae Spaceport on the planet Abregado-rae, the LoBue Cantina was a bar and casino located near the Old Patch. Despite seeming small on the outside, the inside was the location of not only a bar and dance floor, but also a good number of tables. Notable at the cantina were Kampl and Cyru, the security guards, and a huge, bushy-bearded regular known only as The Reverend. About a year before the Battle of Yavin, Han Solo and Chewbacca visited LoBue. A holochess game between the Wookiee and a Weequay became violent when Chewbacca became angered by a move made by his opponent.
