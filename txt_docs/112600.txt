Cloud City Control
1475

Cloud City Control was the primary law enforcement agency stationed in Cloud City on the planet of Bespin. The Wing Guard comprised the largest segment of Cloud City Control. Cloud City Control officers were tasked with maintaining security in the city, as well as normal duties of traffic control and public safety. They also maintained watch over local space around Bespin to ward off potential attacks to the city, and to dissuade tibanna gas poachers. *Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back junior novelization *The Empire Strikes Back: The National Public Radio Dramatization Category:Cloud City organizations Category:Military units
