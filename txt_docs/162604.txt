Iep Tumu
1366

Iep Tumu was a Corellian merchant who owned the Merasy. Iep Tumu had little talent for trading or for mechanics. Even if he owned the starship Merasy, a Nova-Drive 3-Z light freighter, he performed no maintenance and was unable to stop an acceleration vibration on it. Tumu tried to obtain money through smuggling, but he has little talent for it either. Tumu was arrested in Brentaal IV and his ship was impounded.
