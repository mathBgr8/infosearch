The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 20
1524

The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine 20 is the twentieth issue of The Lucasfilm Fan Club Magazine, later rebranded as the Star Wars Insider. Issue 20 was published in Fall 1993. *"George Lucas' Super Live Adventure: An Action-Packed Spectacle Tours Japan" *"How A Classic Newspaper Strip Became A Comic Book Series: The Story Behind Classic Star Wars" *"Inside New Collectables From Star Wars and Indiana Jones" *"The Special Effects of Jurassic Park" *"The Latest From LucasArts Entertainment Co." 020
