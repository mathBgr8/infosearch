Jenet (language)
931

Jenet was the native language of the Jenet species from Garban. Most Jenets were fluent in both the Jenet language and Basic. The Jenet language was formed by squeaks, squeals and chatters, as well as high-pitched barks. Jenets relied only on oral tradition, and had no written version of their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Jenet culture Category:Languages
