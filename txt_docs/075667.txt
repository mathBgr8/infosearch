Blockade Runners Derby
1237

The Blockade Runners Derby was a competition held by smugglers in the comet cloud around Ord Mantell. It was held annually since 69 BBY. Han Solo won three times and in 24 ABY, during its 93rd anniversary, held the title of Grand Marshal. The same year, Zekk won the derby in his ship, the Lightning Rod.
