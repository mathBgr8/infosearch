|publisher=Decipher |release date=August 8, 2001 |figures= |cards=180 cards :90 Light Side :90 Dark Side |isbn= |upc= |timeline= |preceded by=Tatooine Limited |followed by=Theed Palace Limited }} The Coruscant Limited set was the tenth full expansion set of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game, a card game based on the Star Wars universe. It consists of 180 regular cards (90 Dark Side and 90 Light Side cards) and nine alternate image cards and was introduced in 2001. This set consists of 60 rare, 9 alternate image, 60 uncommon, and 60 common cards. This set introduced many of the classic characters from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace that were involved with the events occurring on Coruscant. This set also introduced the Galactic Senate to the game, in which players could play politics with different senators. A complete list of the Coruscant Limited set can be found on the . Because this is an expansion set, it bears an expansion-set symbol, which is located near the top right corner of the card to the right of the lore and below the destiney number. The symbol for this set is the emblem found on the floor of the Jedi Council Chamber. This set was sold in 11-card expansion packs and 30-pack expansion boxes. Each pack include one rare card, three uncommon cards and five common cards. Starter decks were never produced for this set. Because of the lack of popularity of the Tatooine Limited set, Decipher underproduced this set, which increased prices of single cards and sealed product dramatically.
