Lachton
1503

Lachton was a Human smuggler in Talon Karrde's organization. By 9 ABY he was co-pilot of the Wild Karrde. His duties carried him with Talon Karrde to Bpfassh where he witnessed Grand Admiral Thrawn's hit and fade Star Destroyer attack against the planet.
