Jundland Pox
503

Jundland Pox was a disease that reportedly affected one in a million creatures on Tatooine. In 1 ABY the Bestine Hospital Medical Chief discovered an area of the planet that contained a higher than usual concentration of the disease. The Medical Chief hired hunters to significantly narrow the population of animals in that area to prevent further spread of the disease. Category:Poxes
