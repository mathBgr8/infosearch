Anjavay Rosit
1350

Lieutenant Anjavay Rosit was a male Senate Guard during the Separatist Crisis. One week before the scheduled vote for the Military Creation Act and the establishment of the Grand Army of the Republic, Rosit was one of the Senate Guards assigned to disrupt a protest outside the Senate Building. When he attempted to extricate wounded protesters, he and fellow Guard Troye Mulleshar were clubbed by the crowd and received blunt-force trauma wounds.
