Kur-hashan
1171

The Kur-hashan was the command ship of B'shith Vorrik. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, it participated in the Battle of Bastion, Battle of Borosk, Battle of Yaga Minor, and Battle of Esfandia. It was damaged at Esfandia, and with a Vong defeat ensured, Vorrik attempted to ram the Kur-hashan into the enemy command ship. However, the Imperials destroyed the Kur-hashan before it came close, killing everyone aboard.
