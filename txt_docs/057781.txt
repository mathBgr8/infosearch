Gaminne Group, Inc.
1089

|role=Mining conglomerate |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Gaminne Group, Inc. was a mining conglomerate in the Lan system a millennium before the Galactic Civil War. They had purchased the mining rights to the system from the native Qieg, and established Human settlements throughout the system, including the Human-style Shulell City. The conglomerate itself was no longer in existence by the time of the Galactic Empire, and the mining operations had been taken over by independent mining cooperatives.
