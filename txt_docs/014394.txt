Wynl
1366

Wynl was a male Baragwin Senator who represented the various Baragwin communities in the New Republic Senate twenty-five years after the Battle of Yavin. During the initial stages of the Yuuzhan Vong invasion, Wynl was opposed to Leia Organa Solo, and did not believe that a galactic invasion was imminent. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Baragwin Category:Males Category:New Republic Senators
