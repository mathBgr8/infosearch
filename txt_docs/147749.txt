Rinto Liprus
1395

Rinto Liprus was a male personal assistant of Ebenn Q3 Baobab. He once met up with a Rebel sympathizer from whom he obtained some Imperial political data. Baobab hired an intermediary to retrieve the data from Liprus near Moenia, though he claimed that he was merely sending for his assistant to fix his ear implant. 250px|thumb|Randomly generated depiction of Rinto Liprus Rinto Liprus appeared as a non-player character in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
