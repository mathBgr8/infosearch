The Balance
1468

The Balance was a sense of honor followed by Alema Rar after her near-death experience on Tenupe. She used her twisted sense of morality to justify her dark side actions. The general idea was a form of self-centered karma. Rar felt that the pain she suffered should be suffered by those who caused it; hence, anything that caused pain to the Solos, who were responsible for her mutilation, was good in her opinion. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Exile *Legacy of the Force: Sacrifice *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *Legacy of the Force: Fury *Fate of the Jedi: Vortex *Fate of the Jedi: Apocalypse Category:Society and culture
