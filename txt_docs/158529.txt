Morgath 5
1951

The Morgath 5 was a Container transport used for backup convoy services by Imperial defector Admiral Harkov's Victory-class Star Destroyer, the Protector stationed at XQ1 Platform DS 5. It was intercepted and shot down by Imperial Ace Maarek Stele along with Morgath 1, BFF-1 bulk freighter group Karflo, group SoroSuub, and CR90 corvette group Sundog. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Container transports Category:Starships of the Alliance to Restore the Republic
