Council on Security and Intelligence

|dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=*New Republic **New Republic Senate }} The Council on Security and Intelligence (CSI) was a committee that provided strategic management of intelligence policy and advising on threats to the New Republic. The council comprised seven members, at least five members of the New Republic Senate and three intelligence or military officials, and met in in Room 030 of the Imperial Palace. In 16 ABY, members present during the discussion of the New Republic's response to the Teljkon Vagabond included senators Krall Praget, Cair Tok Noimm, Amamanam and Lillald, as well as General Carlist Rieekan of New Republic Intelligence. In 25 ABY it consisted of six senators (Porolo Miatamia, Krall Praget, Gron Marrab, Tolik Yar, Ab'el Bogen, and Viqi Shesh) as well as at least one intelligence officer, Colonel Belindi Kalenda. It was directly responsible for the secret agency Alpha Blue.
