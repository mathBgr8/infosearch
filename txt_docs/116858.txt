thumb|right|Anson Trask is called a noob by the other stormtroopers of Joker Squad on [[Yinchorr/Legends|Yinchorr.]] A noob, or newbie, was a Gungan word used to describe any newcomers who entered their swamps. The word had since spread across the galaxy, describing anyone new to an area or a job as a "newbie."
