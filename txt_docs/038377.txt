Janwuine-jika
1228

Janwuine-jika was a ceremony of the Gand species. It conferred upon the species' most famous and successful members a sort of folk-hero status, which included the right to refer to themselves in the first-person and to have their mate selected by the race as a whole. The ceremony could also be used to simultaneously grant someone the position of findsman.
