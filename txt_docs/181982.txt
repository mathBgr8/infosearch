Assault gunboat
1219

An assault gunboat was a type of gunboat that was used by the naval forces of the Galactic EmpireDawn of Rebellion and other parties. Imperial I-class Star Destroyers carried five of this type of gunboat and Death Star's fleet was composed of hundreds of starships, that ranged from dreadnoughts to assault gunboats. The SS-54 assault ship was a class of assault gunboat that was mistakenly registered as a light freighter. *Dawn of Rebellion Category:Starship types
