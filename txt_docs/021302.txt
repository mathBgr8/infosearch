|cover artist=John Nadeau |editor=Peet Janes |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=July 1, 1998 |pages=27 |upc=761568973037 |timeline= |series=*Star Wars Handbook *Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issue=1 |next=Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire }} Star Wars Handbook 1: X-Wing Rogue Squadron, titled on the cover as Volume One, is the first issue of the Legends comic book companion series Star Wars Handbook. It was written by Peet Janes, and it covers topics from the comic book series Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron. The issue was first published on July 1, 1998 by Dark Horse Comics, and a digital edition was published by Marvel Comics in 2015. Beginning with the Star Wars X-Wing Rogue Squadron ongoing series, Dark Horse will take a closer look at the various elements that make Star Wars so special: the characters, ships, planets, and aliens.
