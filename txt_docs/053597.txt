Ambush at Thila
2645

The Ambush at Thila was an event during the Galactic Civil War. During the Infiltration of Kalist VI, Rebel agents provided false information regarding a Rebel base on Thila to the Empire. Acting on the information, Base Commander of Kalist VI General Noils, commissioned Captain Kale Roshuir to eradicate the 'rebel base' and its occupants. When the Imperials arrived at the curiously quiet Rebel base, Captain Roshuir sent Lieutenant Clynn and his squad into the catacombs that made up the base to investigate.
