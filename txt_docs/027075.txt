Jih-tan
485

The Jih-tan was an Ebranite thildas, or clan. As late as the Imperial era, the Jih-tan continued their feuding ways. Another clan with a similar outlook was the Rull, who may have been their rivals. Most of the other Ebranite clans had learned to co-operate with one another by that time. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Ebranite culture Category:Families
