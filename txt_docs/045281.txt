Cleansing of Onderon
1483

The Cleansing of Onderon was one of the first skirmishes of the Great Hunt. In it, Jedi led by Guun Han Saresh, Shaela Nuur, and Duron Qel-Droma cleansed the planet Onderon of terentateks left there by the Brotherhood of the Sith during the Great Sith War's Krath Holy Crusade.
