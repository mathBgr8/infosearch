Disruptor field
2469

A disruptor field, also field disruptor, was a type of field projection that interfered with electronic systems and coherent energy fields. The Rakatan Infinite Empire was known to devastate planets with disruptor fields. In 3956 BBY, a disruptor field projected from the Temple of the Ancients on the planet Lehon forced down Revan's ship, the Ebon Hawk, by creating electrical overloads and disruptions. In order to reach the Star Forge, Revan and his companions disabled the disruptor field by manipulating the controls located on the Temple Summit.
