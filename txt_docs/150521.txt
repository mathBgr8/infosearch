Katykam
1466

Katykam was a male albino Wookiee who worked as Crying Dawn Singer's stage manager. He wore loose black pants and a red vest, and smoked a clay pipe. Katykam was killed by Imperial Security Bureau agents from the Interrogation branch after the pirate Yearo Seville kidnapped Crying Dawn Singer. *The Abduction Category:Males Category:Wookiees
