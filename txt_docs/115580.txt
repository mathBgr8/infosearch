Senator of Bilbringi
2372

An unnamed senator represented Bilbringi in the New Republic Senate. In 12 ABY, he requested the New Republic send a communications team to the mining colony of Artus Prime, which had gone silent after reporting the discovery of a large deposit of Artusian crystals. Per the Senator's request, a supply ship was sent there. Unknown to them, the mines had been captured by the Empire Reborn; as apparently did the ship bearing the communications crew, since it disappeared at the Outer Rim.Star Wars: Jedi Knight II: Jedi Outcast In 28 ABY, another unnamed, possibly identical, senator from Bilbringi was bribed by Lando Calrissian during the Chief of State election. This senator sat on the Defense Council. The N.R.I. Reports and The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way were both released in 2002.
