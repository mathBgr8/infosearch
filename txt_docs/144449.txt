Reggi (Soskin Guard)
1038

Reggi was a male Human with whom Lando Calrissian tried to negotiate in order to hire the Soskin Guard to protect his mining shipments from Varn. Reggi requested 50,000 credits a month for the Guard to work for Calrissian, a payment Lando refused. *Specter of the Past Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Mercenaries
