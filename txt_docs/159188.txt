Model A-Fac 333
1919

The Delvin Constructs Model A-Fac 333 was a factory model used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. One of the largest and most expensive building models ever produced by Delvin Constructs, the A-Fac 333 could be tooled to produce almost any common large vehicle design, but the Empire used them almost exclusively for their AT-AT and AT-AA walkers.
