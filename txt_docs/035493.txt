Com Fordox
2212

Com Fordox was the senator of the Corellian sector during the crises of the Eriadu Trade Summit and Blockade of Naboo. After Supreme Chancellor Valorum was attacked, he visited Valorum along with Bail Antilles, Palpatine, and Orn Free Taa.
