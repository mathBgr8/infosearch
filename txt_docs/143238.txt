Mines
998

thumb|The Mines on the Point Nadir's map. The Mines were located in the Point Nadir, on the comet Resh 9376. The sprawling caverns began at the Fissure District and were rumored to contain Salovan Fische' Legacy - a treasure trove full of goods and riches hidden in a remote cave. Though the majority of tunnels were unmapped, they attracted a large number of visitor and residents. Anjiliac clan occasionally send groups of Houk thugs to clear the mines, with little effect. In addition to common mynocks, the mines housed an undocumented spider-like predator, responsible for dozens of deaths. *Scum and Villainy Category:Point Nadir locations
