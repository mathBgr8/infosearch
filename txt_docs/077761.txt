A-10 interceptor
1833

The A-10 interceptor was a starfighter produced by Kuat Drive Yards in operation as of 40 ABY. It was the successor to the A-9 Vigilance interceptor. A-10 interceptors comprised part of the Corellian force deployed against the Galactic Alliance Second Fleet during the Battle of Tralus during the Second Galactic Civil War. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:A-series interceptor models
