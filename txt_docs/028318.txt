Star Wars 32: The Jawa Express is the thirty-second issue in the Marvel Star Wars series of comics. Han Solo, Luke Skywalker, Chewbacca, and the droids are stranded in the Dune Sea of Tatooine after fleeing Imperial stormtroopers from Mos Eisley. They arrange a deal with some Jawas to travel with them in their sandcrawler, and soon discover further evidence of the plans of the House of Tagge working in concert with the Empire to create a long range freezing mechanism, but the purpose of the technology still remains a mystery to the Rebels. The Rebels elude their Imperial pursuers and head back to Mos Eisley.
