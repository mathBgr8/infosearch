Mite (disambiguation)
825

Mites were arachnoid creatures of various sizes that existed on many worlds. Many lived in hives ruled by queens. Known mite species and subspecies included: *Bark mite (Rori, Endor) *Decay mite (Talus) *Drillmite (Haruun Kal) *Dust mite (Tatooine) *Force mite (Balanor) *Itch mite *KyLessian feather mite {KyLessia} *Venom-mite *Ore mite *Ralltiiri glowmite (Ralltiir) *Rockmite (Tatooine) *Shear mite (Dathomir) *Stone mite (various planets) *Tanc mite (Yavin 4) *Garqi vert-mite (Garqi)
