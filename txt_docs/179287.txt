Duergo

The Duergo was Race Kartan's personal starfighter. A battle occurred between Kartan and Jango Fett where many vessels were destroyed, including three Missile Frigates and numerous mercenary starfighters. During the course of the battle, Fett disabled the Duergo to collect the bounty on Kartan. The Duergo appears as the primary target in the Jango Fett bonus mission of Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter.
