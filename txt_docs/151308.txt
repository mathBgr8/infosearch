Consortium palace
1196

thumb|right|200px|A Consortium palace A Zann Consortium palace was a large building used by the Zann Consortium underworld organization. These buildings were often built on worlds with strong Consortium influence, and served as the center for Consortium monetary dealings there. The building was well-defended, making it harder for Rebel or Imperial forces to destroy it. They were also equipped with a shield generator and a communications array.
