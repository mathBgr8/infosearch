|published in=Star Wars Tales 13 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=c. 32 BBY &ndash; 22 BBYAccording to Star Wars Legends Epic Collection: The Menace Revealed Vol. 2, "The Sith in the Shadow" takes place between the events of and , dated to 32 BBY and 22 BBY, respectively, by The Essential Reader's Companion. |series= |preceded by="Stones" |followed by="Children of the Force" }} "The Sith in the Shadow" is a comic that appeared in Star Wars Tales 13 and was collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4. Mace Windu and a Padawan traveled to a planet where the natives worshiped the Sith. The Padawan was as fearful as the natives, and the two almost attacked one another, but Windu defused the situation. Soon after, Windu informed the Padawan that he was to find a nearby Sith Temple. Told that he must overcome an adversary within, the young Padawan ignited his lightsaber and headed inside. Inside, the apprentice found a cloaked figure and attacked it. The adversary was actually Windu, who blocked the student's attack and informed him that he had failed in his training.
