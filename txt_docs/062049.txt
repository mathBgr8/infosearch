Interplanetary Acquisitions
612

Interplanetary Acquisitions was a small business that dealt with the refurbishing and sale of used starships. During the height of the Galactic Empire, Interplanetary Acquisitions was used as a front for the ship smuggler, Pal-Nada. The company served as a means for Pal-Nada to move and sell stolen starships on the black market. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shipwrights
