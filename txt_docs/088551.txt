Mupple
1185

Mupples were small furry creatures that were imprisoned at the new Umboo Lightstation when Mungo Baobab, C-3PO and R2-D2 rescued them. Some considered Mupples a delicacy. The Mupples seem to resemble the Tribble species from the Star Trek original series episode "The Trouble with Tribbles."
