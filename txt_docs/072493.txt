Rebels' Pride
795

Rebels' Pride was a New Republic freighter. It was part of the bacta convoy which was destroyed by Warlord Zsinj in the ambush at Alderaan. Though the Rebels' Pride is only mentioned briefly by Kirtan Loor, with no details given about its design, the context implies that might have been the largest freighter in the convoy, with the Pulsar Skate, the other ship which Loor mentioned, being the smallest. *X-Wing: The Krytos Trap Category:Freighters Category:Starships of the New Republic
