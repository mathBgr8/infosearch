B'dard Tone's courier
963

thumb|B'dard Tone's courier. B'dard Tone's courier was the transport in which B'dard Tone and Codi Ty traveled to Bryndar. The ship had twin pods mounted on short outrigger struts on either side of the central hull which contained the engine. Each pod had a large tail but the starboard pod contained the cockpit while the port-side pod contained a navigation dish. A large circular canopy allowed a 180-degree field of view for the pilot and crew. The ship rested on three articulated landing struts when at rest and which could be retracted into the main hull for flight.General Grievous 1 *General Grievous 1 Category:Starships of the Jedi Order
