}} 2X-NM (Tooexx-ennam) was once a Metropolis Razer assassin droid who was converted to become a cooking droid by Mother for his space diner, Mother's. Although he had his blaster replaced with a spatula and his former programming changed to hold some very tasty recipes, he was known to still be able to wield a mean set of Ginsu Knives when called on to defend the diner.
