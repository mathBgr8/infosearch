Aramadia (Aramadia-class)
1143

The Aramadia was an Aramadia-class thrustship. The lead ship of the Aramadia class, it was chosen by Nil Spaar as his diplomatic vessel. When Aramadia traveled to Coruscant to begin Nil Spaar's diversionary talks with the New Republic, military and intelligence officers spent hours pouring over scans and holos of Aramadia to gain an understanding of Yevethan technology. *Before the Storm *Before the Storm abridged audiobook *Cracken's Threat Dossier *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Aramadia-class thrustships
