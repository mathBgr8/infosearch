Jump For Joy
509

The Jump For Joy was a Twomi Skyfire on Utharis in 17 ABY. Akanah had intentions of stealing it until the captain confronted her outside of his ship, where he would ask her if she had ever had intercourse while in hyperspace. *Tyrant's Test *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships
