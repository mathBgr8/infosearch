Tsillin Wel
1657

Tsillin Wel was a Quarren working with New Republic Intelligence, and specialized in tracking accounts used by Imperial Intelligence to pay their operatives, spies, and other ancillary agents. She was brought in to testify during Tycho Celchu's treason and murder trial in 7 ABY on Coruscant.
