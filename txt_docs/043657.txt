Jurrinex6
772

Jurrinex6 was a hive virus developed by biological terrorists at the height of the New Order. In the past four centuries before the Galactic Civil War, the lethal Jurrinex6 and Jurr-5 hive viruses were responsible for over 100 million deaths. The kalaides of the planet Cols produced masrizeen, the basis for the first effective antidote against these two hive viruses. *Creatures of the Galaxy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Diseases and maladies Category:Weaponized plagues and viruses
