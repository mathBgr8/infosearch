Meyer
1380

"Lips" Meyer was a male Gran criminal and resident of Outland Transit Station wanted by the members of Outland Station Sanitary Service Union 233-34 for informing on them in exchange for credits and immunity for his past crimes. In 32 BBY, the bounties posted on him were 2,000 credits alive and 1,000 dead.
