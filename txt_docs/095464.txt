|death=February 11, 2004 |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Voice artistStar Wars: Rebel Assault |companies= |website= }} Tony Pope (March 22, 1947 &ndash; February 11, 2004) was a voice artist who voiced many characters for The Walt Disney Company including voicing Goofy for eleven years. Pope provided additional voices for the 1993 Star Wars: Rebel Assault video game. Later Pope voiced C-3PO and an A-wing pilot in the 1998 Star Wars Trilogy Arcade coin-operated video game.Star Wars Trilogy Arcade In 1997, Pope lent their voice to portray Grand Moff Wilhuff Tarkin in the A New Hope Read-Along book and cassette tape.
