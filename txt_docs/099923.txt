YVH 2-1S was a YVH S-series war droid developed by Tendrando Arms. 2-1S was part of the Myrkr strike team in the Mission to Myrkr during the Yuuzhan Vong War in 27 ABY. 2-1S was destroyed in action, sacrificing himself to optimize enemy losses and maintain the strike team's cover. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:YVH S-series war droids
