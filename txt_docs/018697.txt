Automixer
921

An automixer or automix machine was a vending device used in cantinas and clubs all over the galaxy. Those who used it would choose their species and the automixer would provide them with an appropriate beverage. This prevented patrons from becoming poisoned due to drinking a beverage that might have been toxic to their species. *Inside the Worlds of Star Wars: Attack of the Clones *Star Wars: Complete Locations *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Consumables storage and processing
