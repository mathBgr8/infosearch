Taureg
1780

Taureg was an Imperial BFF-1 bulk freighter that carried water and left the container Psi 1 carrying grain in an Imperial supplies depot. The Sarsuma attempted to steal the grain during the food shortage following the evacuation from Yavin 4. During the food shortage, following the evacuation from Yavin IV, it was destroyed by the turbolasers of the Star Destroyer Badi Dea which arrived shortly after. "Taureg" is the name of a North African tribe of Bedouins, which also inspired the name of the Volkswagen Touareg. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
