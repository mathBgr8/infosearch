At one point during the Galactic Civil War, the Carosite Rebel Alliance medic Tamo Lan served in a short campaign on Baes Logia. During that same conflict, a trio of Rebel operatives once attempted to lure a general of the Galactic Empire into a trap. The operatives acted as if they had been nearly overcome by Imperial forces; however, as the general was approaching them, Graf Yonna&mdash;a mentally unstable Alliance operative who had partially assumed the personality of the ancient adventurer and hero Parsol d'Ahndole&mdash;also appeared. Yonna then opened weapons fire, killing the general. Not long after the Battle of Yavin, Alliance General Airen Cracken noted the incident on Baes Logia in an entry dedicated to Yonna that was part of a datafile detailing various Rebel operatives. Baes Logia was created by Chuck Truett and was introduced in Cracken's Rebel Operatives, a 1994 West End Games sourcebook published for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
