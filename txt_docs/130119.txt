Law Elder
632

A Law Elder was a member of the ruling planetary council of Bimmisaari. During the early days of the Thrawn campaign, the Elders opened negotiations to join the New Republic. Bimmisaari ultimately did choose to join the New Republic, and other worlds in the Halla sector, such as Danuta, followed suit. *Heir to the Empire *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Political titles
