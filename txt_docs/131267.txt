Sonic scalpel
2148

Sonic scalpels were used by surgeons to cut away dead flesh. Sonic scalpels were medical tools intended and could be used during emergency field surgery. The device was used to cut away dead tissue, a necessary procedure for some wounds to prevent developing a necrotic infection.Legacy of the Force: Tempest Sonic scalpels were included in medpacs, including Biotech Industries' FastFlesh medpac. Ula Vii used a sonic scalpel to clear away the fused flesh and plastoid that had been left behind after Larin Moxla had four fingers severed and cauterized by a Sith lightsaber.The Old Republic: Fatal Alliance Lalu Morwan used a sonic scalpel to treat Han Solo's injured arm aboard the Millennium Falcon. *The Old Republic: Fatal Alliance *Rebel Force: Target *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Cracken's Rebel Field Guide *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *Millennium Falcon Owner's Workshop Manual Category:Scalpels Category:Sonic technology
