Morlish Veed's apartment was the apartment of the Fel Empire's Grand Admiral Morlish Veed. It was located in the high upper levels of the city world of Coruscant. It had state of the art security system to prevent break-ins. Despite of it, Morrigan Corde, agent of Imperial Intelligence, was able to break-in undetected and disable the security systems while Veed was asleep. *Legacy (2006) 16 Category:Coruscant apartments
