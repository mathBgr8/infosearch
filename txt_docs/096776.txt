Morganian
1317

The Morganians were a species of slender, white-skinned Humanoids. They had pointed ears and were renowned for their beauty. Leland Chee, on the StarWars.com Message Boards, stated that his theory is that "all pointy-eared species are either Sephi or closely related to Sephi."
