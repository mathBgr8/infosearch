Arvid Scraf
1697

Arvid Scraf was a male Human who lived on Nam Chorios. He may have been of Alderaanian or Corellian descent and was six or seven years younger than Luke Skywalker. While in the wastelands of Nam Chorios, he found and took Luke back with him to Ruby Gulch to meet his Aunt Gin Scraf.
