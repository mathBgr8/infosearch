GBk-785 hyperdrive

thumb|right|250px|The RZ-1 A-wing was equipped with GBk-785 hyperdrive The Incom GBk-785 hyperdrive was the hyperdrive unit commonly installed in the RZ-1 A-wing interceptor. The class 1.0 GBk-785 was used to accelerate the A-wing to supralight velocities.
