Roshan
1382

Roshans were humanoid beings who lived on the planet of Rosha in the Leemurtoo system. They were a tall species with strong, flexible bodies and light blue or green eyes. Their most defining features were four antennae on their heads that were as delicate as tendrils and sensitive to light, shrinking against their heads during the day and unfurling in darkness or with anxiety. The Samarians also lived in the same system as them.
