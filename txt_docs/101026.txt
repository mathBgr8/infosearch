Morgath Industries
1576

The company Morgath Industries interests included food processing and genetic food additives. They were also a major supplier of dietary supplements and special preservatives to other food manufacturers. Morgath Industries was a contributing sponsor of the Corporate Sector Authority, having provided fifty trillion credits worth of equipment, funds, and services. This investment yielded a proportional share of Corporate Sector profits as well as nominal discounts on bulk goods and raw materials.
