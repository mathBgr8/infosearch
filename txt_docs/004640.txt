|gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes=Blue (according to Asenec of Crakull) |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Inijic kajidic *Hutt Empire |masters= |apprentices= }} General Kossak Inijic Ar'durv, commonly known as Kossak the Hutt or High Exaltedness Kossak, was a famous Hutt known for defeating Xim the Despot at the climax of the Xim Wars in 25,100 BBY, earning the sobriquet Devourer of Despots. Over twenty-five thousand years before the Battle of Yavin, Kossak was the one Hutt leader to put aside internal squabbles and take action against the threat of Xim's burgeoning empire. Proclaiming himself as the commander of the Hutt clans, Kossak united his divided species and declared war on Xim. Using Xim's own underhand tactics against him, Kossak effectively fought the Despot for several years, scoring a major victory at the First Battle of Vontor. After suffering huge losses in a subsequent clash at Vontor, Kossak enlisted the service of the Vodran, Nikto, and Klatooinian species through the Treaty of Vontor, successfully achieved through the efforts of the Hutt representative Dojundo and bolstered by Weequay soldiers. He triumphed over Xim in a final confrontation on the same world. Xim was captured and sent to Kossak's dungeons on Varl.
