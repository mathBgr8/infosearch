Bravo Four
1195

Bravo Four was the call sign given to a pilot in Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps' Bravo Flight during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. Royal Naboo Security Forces' Lieutenant Rya Kirsch served as Bravo Four at the time of the Battle of Naboo, in which he participated and was killed. Bravo 04
