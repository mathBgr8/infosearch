thumb|300px|[[Team Coruscant against Team Fondor playing limmie in the opening round of the Galactic Cup.]] The Galactic Cup of Limmie, sometimes known as just the Galactic Cup, was the national league for the sport of limmie. It had many teams from throughout the galaxy.
