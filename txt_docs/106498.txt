Sea of Jarad
728

The Sea of Jarad was a stormy body of water on Tralus located far from the equator. Human and Selonian settlements on the shores of the Sea of Jarad were quaint throwbacks to earlier times. The sea was a mating ground for styanax, and the hunting of these creatures was an important part of the Tralus' culture.
