Z-95XT Trainer
3446

The Z-95XT Trainer was a training model of the Z-95 Headhunter. It possessed a second seat for a passenger such as a flight trainer, but lacked the standard Z-95's concussion missile system. They were often used by the Rebel Alliance for ferrying personnel in-system or between ships within the same fleet, in addition to their stated use of personnel training. The lasers could be set to only illuminate a target, and therefore not damage it.
