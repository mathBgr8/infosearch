Vis'l
1204

Vis'l was a young Jedi Knight. In 40 ABY, after Vis'l returned from the a final training mission with a Jedi Master, the Jedi was at the Ossus Academy when the Galactic Alliance Guard (GAG) attempted to "secure" it, holding the students hostage.
