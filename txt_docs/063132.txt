Retreat from Coruscant
9846

"Retreat from Coruscant" is a short story written by Laurie Burns. It originally appeared in Star Wars Adventure Journal 7 by West End Games in August 1995 and was later reprinted in the short-story anthology Tales from the Empire, which was published by Bantam Spectra in November 1997. Shortly before the events of Star Wars: Dark Empire in 10 ABY, courier freighter captain Taryn Clancy and her ship, the Messenger, are trapped beneath the planetary shields of Coruscant when the reinvigorated Imperial forces attack the capital. The New Republic signals a full-scale evacuation of the city-planet. General Garm Bel Iblis commandeers Taryn's ship, appointing Colonel Jak Bremen to serve aboard as Taryn must deliver vital information to the New Republic fleet. Avoiding Imperial pursuit and bluffing her way past an Imperial inspection, Taryn succeeds in delivering a secure message regarding a new rendezvous point to the fleet. *Star Wars Adventure Journal 7
