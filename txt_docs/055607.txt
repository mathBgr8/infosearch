Special Task Force One
3295

Special Task Force One was a fleet of Trade Federation ships assembled by Darth Sidious in 27 BBY to destroy Outbound Flight. It was commanded by Vicelord Siv Kav and Commander "Stratis." The ships were operated by skeleton crews to keep secrecy to a maximum. After a confrontation with Commander Mitth'raw'nuruodo (Core name "Thrawn") of the CEDF, only the flagship, Darkvenge, was not destroyed.
