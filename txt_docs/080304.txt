|height= |mass= |hair=Blond |feathers= |eyes=Brown |skin=Light |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Darth Krayt's Galactic Empire **Imperial Navy ***1st Imperial Center Core Defense Squadron |masters= |apprentices= }} Brodie Coburn, a Human male, was a starfighter pilot in the service of Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Krayt's Empire during the Second Imperial Civil War. He flew Skull Three under the call sign of "Cannon" in Captain Gunn Yage's Skull Squadron, a Predator-class fighter unit. In 137 ABY, Skull Squadron was stationed on the galactic capital of Coruscant when bounty hunter and former Jedi Cade Skywalker attempted to rescue the Bothan Jedi Knight Hosk Trey'lis from the Temple of the Sith. Given orders to capture Skywalker alive, Coburn and the Skulls engaged the former Jedi's starship, the Mynock, in the skies of the planet until Skywalker managed to lose his pursuers in the undercity. Coburn flew against the Mynock once again when a group of Skywalker's allies, including his mother, Moff Nyna Calixte&mdash;acting in disguise so she would not be recognized&mdash;piloted the ship as they attempted to rescue Skywalker from the Sith Temple where he was being held captive.
