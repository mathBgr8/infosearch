Kormoran group
806

The Kormoran group was a group of five CR92a Assassin-class corvettes used by Grand Admiral Zaarin. They were sent to destroy a convoy of two container transports, called Neelie. The targets were supposed to be carrying Missile Boats but instead carried munitions. The corvettes were lured into an ambush and destroyed. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Zaarin military units
