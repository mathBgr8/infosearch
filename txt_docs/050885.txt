Obba Babatundé
1010

Obba Babatundé (born December 1, 1951) is an American actor who voices Lando Calrissian in Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, Star Wars: Rogue Squadron II: Rogue Leader, Star Wars: Rogue Squadron III: Rebel Strike and Disney Infinity 3.0.
