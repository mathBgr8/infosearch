The Crash (cantina)
482

The Crash was a cantina in Vlarnya on Courkrus. The pirates of the Khuiumin Survivors hung out there. The interior was decorated with chunks of transparisteel and quadanium steel, from starfighter kills or crashes. *I, Jedi Crash Crash
