Dactyl
858

Dactyl was a yellow-colored ammonia crystal. It was essential to the survival of Arcona when away from the ammonia-rich atmosphere of their homeworld. Arcona who went without dactyl quickly faded from a green to grayish muddy tan, shed scales, and eventually died. Salt was known to dramatically increase an Arconan's need of dactyl. *Jedi Apprentice: The Rising Force *Jedi Apprentice: The Dark Rival *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Crystals Category:Food additives
