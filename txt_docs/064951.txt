thumb|right|250px|The Nashtah Gang. The Nashtah Gang was a gang on Coruscant during the early days of the Empire. They indirectly worked for Xizor and the Black Sun. The gang was sent to assault Kodo Finn but before they could do so Ekria, Zonder and Drake Lo'gaan foiled their plans by protecting Finn.
