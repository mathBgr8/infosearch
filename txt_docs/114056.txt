Moff Weblin
1017

Moff Weblin was a fleet tender in the Imperial Navy named after Grand Moff Weblin. At some point during the years of the New Order, it was assigned to Black Sword Command, with Davith Sconn serving as the bridge officer in charge of the second watch. About three years after the Empire established the Black Fifteen shipyard at N'zoth, the Weblin put in for repairs to a damaged number four power cell.
