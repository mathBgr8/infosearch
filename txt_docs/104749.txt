Unidentified Minister of War
1001

An unknown Corellian served as the Five Worlds' first Minister of War. He had been in office for at least a year before being sacked and replaced by Thrackan Sal-Solo at the start of the Second Galactic Civil War in 40 ABY. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Bureaucrats Category:Corellian nationals
