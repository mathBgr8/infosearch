Sith pincerbug
1316

Sith pincerbugs were arthropods indigenous to the planet Ziost. Their burning venom was used during the initiation of a new Dark Lord of the Sith to burn the Dark Lord's mark on his forehead. While these creatures are canon, they were never actually named. *Tales of the Jedi – The Golden Age of the Sith 4 Category:Arthropods Category:Creatures of the Sith Worlds
