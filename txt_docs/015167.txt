It had an economic breakdown shortly before the Clone Wars. It was represented in the Galactic Senate by Bog Divinian, a wealthy landowner with ties to the Commerce Guild. In the years following the Invasion of Naboo, Senator Divinian's wife, Astri Oddo, set up a relief movement on Nuralee to petition the Galactic Republic for financial assistance.
