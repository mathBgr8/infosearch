Peacekeeper (Outbound Flight Colony)
1156

Peacekeepers were the law enforcement agency of the Outbound Flight Colony. They were led by the Guardian of the People and obeyed the orders of the Director and the Managing Council. Every resident took a term as a Peacekeeper.Survivor's Quest In 22 ABY the Guardian was Jorad Pressor. The ten other Peacekeepers included Trilli, Oliet, Ronson, Bels, Amberson, Perry, and Quinze. Six were killed by the Vagaari in the Battle of the Redoubt. *Survivor's Quest *Survivor's Quest abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Law enforcement agencies
