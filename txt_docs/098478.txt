Cestian
574

Cestian was the term given to the inhabitants of Ord Cestus. Originally, it referred exclusively to the native X'Ting, but over time as the immigrant population boomed and the native population declined, it became used to refer to all of the world's denizens. *The Cestus Deception *The Cestus Deception abridged audiobook Category:Planetary cultural groups
