|origin= |culture=Jedi |creators= |created= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners=Obi-Wan Kenobi |locations= |cost= |value= |shape= |size= |color=Blue |material= |length= |width= |height= |weight= |protection= |capacity= |cont= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose=Lightsaber construction |heritage= |affiliation= }} Kenobi's Legacy was a crystal which could be used in a lightsaber. It had to be properly tuned before it could be inserted into the lightsaber and once inserted meant that the saber could be used by no other. Kenobi's Legacy appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The crystal was made unavailable following the release of the Publish 25, a.k.a. the "New Game Enhancements," on November 15, 2005, that totally revamped the Jedi profession.
