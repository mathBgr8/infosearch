Shanko
1540

Shanko was the insectoid owner and bartender of Shanko's Hive, a drinking establishment located on the inner floor of Borgo Prime. He was a capable bartender and could stay focused on his job despite the constant chaos that erupted inside the walls of Shanko's Hive. Though the majority of his customers were disreputable, he took great pride in the fact that they attracted many more paying customers who were seeking them out.
