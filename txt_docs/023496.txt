thumb|right|250px|Decipher, Inc. logo Decipher were the license holders and publishers of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game between 1995 and 2001. Decipher has been around since the 1980s through various board games. The motto for Decipher has been: "Decipher: The Art of Great Games." Category:Publishing companies (real-world)
