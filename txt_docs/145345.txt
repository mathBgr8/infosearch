Deborah J. Ross
1763

}} Deborah J. Ross, who previously wrote under her married name of Deborah Wheeler, is an American author. She wrote the short story "Goatgrass: The Tale of Ree-Yees" for the 1995 Tales from Jabba's Palace anthology. Outside of Star Wars, Ross has written a number of novels and short stories. She was collaborating with Marion Zimmer Bradley on the Darkover series when Bradley passed away, and Ross continued the series in her honor. She has also served as Secretary for the Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers Association and as Liaison to the World Science Fiction Convention, and she is an advocate for the abolition of the death penalty.
