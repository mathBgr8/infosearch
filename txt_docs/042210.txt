Grub-tea
1283

Grub-tea was a bitter-tasting tea drink. The Human bounty hunter Jango Fett brewed and drank some grub-tea during his stay on the planet Geonosis in 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Boba Fett: The Fight to Survive to 22 BBY. Grub-tea appeared in Boba Fett: The Fight to Survive, a 2002 young readers book written by Terry Bisson as the first book of the Boba Fett series. *Boba Fett: The Fight to Survive Category:Teas
