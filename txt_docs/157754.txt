Its primary task was to shut down an illegal assassin droid factory commissioned by Lannik Racto. Racto sold these assassin droids on the black market to criminal activities. Lannik Racto had been captured by the Jedi Knight Jaden Korr, in a daring mission on Coruscant. Following his arrest, Racto revealed the site of his factory on Nar Shaddaa and a team of NRI agents were sent to Nar Shaddaa to shut it down.
