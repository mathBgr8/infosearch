Battle of Ixtlar (Clone Wars)
2414

A battle at Ixtlar was one of several taking place between the Republic's Victory Fleet and the Separatist's Bulwark Fleet. The battle was inconclusive as the Bulwark Fleet remained a threat, forcing the Victory Fleet to pursue it further. Though the chronology is understood, the precise date of the battle is somewhat unclear, as The New Essential Chronology bases its dates on a 12-month year instead of the Galactic Standard Calendar, describing the Foerost campaign as taking place in "20.1 B.B.Y.
