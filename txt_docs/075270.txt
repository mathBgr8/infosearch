Hammer Group
572

Hammer Group was a squadron of starfighters that was assigned to the warship Ralroost. It aided in the rescue of Corran Horn, Ganner Rhysode, and Jacen Solo from Garqi. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starfighter squadrons
