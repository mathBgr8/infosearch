Fury (corsair battleship)
936

The Fury was a Corsair battleship that saw service in the navy of the Brotherhood of Darkness during the New Sith Wars along with its sister ship, Rage. While its crew was controlled by Lord Kaan's battle meditation, it was still destroyed in the First Battle of Ruusan in 1002 BBY. *Darth Bane: Path of Destruction *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Corsair battleships Category:Starships of the Brotherhood of Darkness
