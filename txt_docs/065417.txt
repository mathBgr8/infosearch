Design An Alien
3614

Design An Alien was a contest held by Star Wars Galaxy Magazine challenging readers to design an original Star Wars alien or creature. The contest was announced in Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 3, with winners revealed in Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 7. Additional honorable mentions were included in issues 8&ndash;10 within the magazine's "Galactic Bazaar" department.
