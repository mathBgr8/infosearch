Return of the Jedi 4
8086

Return of the Jedi 4 is the fourth issue of the Star Wars: Return of the Jedi mini-series, Marvel Star Wars four issue comic adaptation of Return of the Jedi. There are five pin-up pages in this issue, Admiral Gial Ackbar, Logray, B-wing starfighter, Luke Skywalker & Darth Vader by Rick J. Bryant & Yoda, Anakin Skywalker & Obi-Wan Kenobi by Bill Sienkiewicz. If you're one of the two or three people out there who doesn't know the ending to this latest, greatest STAR WARS epic — or even if you do — you'll still enjoy the conclusion to the most sensational space saga of all! The Rebels attempt to destroy the Death Star II while Luke Skywalker duels with Darth Vader. Unlike the Marvel comic adaptations of A New Hope & The Empire Strikes Back, this was not part of the regular series. The Ewok who steals the Imperial speeder bike is incorrectly identified as Wicket Wystri Warrick in the story but it is actually Paploo.
