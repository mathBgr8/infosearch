Classical Ossan
1668

Classical Ossan was a language derived from the language of Ossus from before its destruction during the Great Sith War. One of the Jedi books was written in this language. *Dark Empire II 4 *Empire's End 2 *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force *The Essential Atlas Category:Languages
