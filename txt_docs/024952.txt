In 32 BBY, Orocc attended the Boonta Eve Classic podrace on Tatooine to place various wagers. Orocc was affiliated with Botlat Rej, and became a fugitive sought by the Coruscant Security Force for disrupting a Galactic Senate briefing by smearing Cornesian Wax on the base of several hover platforms, injuring three senators. He had a bounty worth 2,000 Republic credits alive and 1,000 dead. He was last seen by the police at Anchorhead before arriving at Outland Transit Station (where he became a hired gun for Meeko Ghintee) and being captured by Jango Fett for 2,000 credits. Ninopas Orocc is the fourth secondary bounty to be encounter during the second level of Chapter Two in Star Wars: Bounty Hunter.
