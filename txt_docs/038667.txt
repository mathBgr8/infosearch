Kodu Terrafin
2163

}} Kodu Terrafin was a male Arcona who worked for Jabba Desilijic Tiure. He lived on the planet Tatooine and was an employee of Jabba the Hutt shortly before the Battle of Yavin. Kodu operated a landspeeder transport service and shuttled the Jizz band known as the Modal Nodes to the Star Chamber Café at the Lucky Despot Hotel where they were expected to perform at the Whiphid wedding of Lady Valarian and D'Wopp. *The Essential Guide to Characters *Star Wars Encyclopedia *Ultimate Alien Anthology *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Arcona Category:Businesspeople Category:Desilijic servants Category:Males Category:Tatooinians
