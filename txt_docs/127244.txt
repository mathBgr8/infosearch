Battle of Ghorman
3591

The Battle of Ghorman, also known as the Pacification of Ghorman, was a battle that took place on the planet Ghorman during the Galactic Civil War. General Maximilian Veers led the Imperial forces during the battle. When the Rebels retreated to their bunkers, General Veers ordered his troops to use PLX-2M portable missile launchers against them.
