Vocal enhancer
959

The vocal enhancer was designed to enhance or alter the sound or volume of the wearer's voice. Some species used these to make the wearer's voice more understandable. Some vocal enhancers were used for disguising the voice of the wearer. Due to the damage sustained to his respiratory system at Mustafar, Darth Vader used a vocal enhancer to create his deep, ominous voice.
