Odysseus
2948

The Odysseus was an MC80 Star Cruiser belonging to the Rebel Alliance. The Odysseus was part of a convoy along with Thor and Zeus. The convoy was to be attacked by Assault Gunboats and TIE Avengers, and the starships had to rely on X-wings from Rogue and Blue Squadrons to give the starships enough time to escape into hyperspace. The outcome depends on which side the player takes.
