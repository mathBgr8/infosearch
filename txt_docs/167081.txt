Kthysh
988

Kthysh was a type of vine native to the planet Kashyyyk. Specially treated kthysh vines could be used to make strings for that could be used to manually load bowcaster. Many warriors carried treated kthsyh vines in their utility pouches for emergencies.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Chewbacca carried some kthysh vines in his bandolier pouch. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Plants of Kashyyyk
