Spaceport Hotel
1867

The Spaceport Hotel was a hotel established in Mos Eisley on the world of Tatooine; it had a reputation as being somewhat less than mediocre for a standard planet, but above average for Mos Eisley. The hotel boasted forty rooms available, and each room cost approximately fifteen credits for a one-night stay. The rooms were small but adequate and the majority of them had working air conditioning and sonic showers.
