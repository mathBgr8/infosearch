Great Library
1163

The Great Library was a library on Ropagi II. It was located in the outskirts of the city of Ebyl on the continent Forethought.Twin Stars of Kira It was built by the Ropagu to tell the history of their people and held records from as far back as 10,000 BBY. The records were stored in computer data, but there were a few books and scrolls as well. The library was open to everyone, but droids kept an eye on suspicious-looking characters. The library was manned by a few Ropagu, but a most of the business was handled by 3PO-series protocol droids. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Twin Stars of Kira *Alien Encounters Category:Libraries Category:Ropagi II locations
