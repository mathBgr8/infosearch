C-4ME-O, or Fourmio, was a secretary droid stationed on MedStar Four during its deployment to Despayre in 0 BBY. It stood on a single gyroscopically balanced wheel. It was later assigned to aid Dr. Kornell Divini during his stint aboard Death Star I. *Death Star Category:Droids of the Galactic Empire Category:Secretary droids
