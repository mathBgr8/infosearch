Anjet Okeco
1116

Anjet Okeco was a HoloNet News reporter for the Jrade District of Coruscant. In 22 BBY, he wrote an article about the use of death sticks among Coruscant's youths, which included an interview with a fifteen-year-old drug abuser known as Pori Nate. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscanti Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Journalists Category:Males
