4-LB
1809

4-LB was a large, square labor droid who ran the Power Station at Mos Eisley on the planet Tatooine, where locals came to power their speeders and equipment. It cared little about its customers and their whereabouts and was unenthusiastic about its role.Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley During the hunt for Adar Tallon on Tatooine, 4-LB was questioned by operatives of the Alliance to Restore the Republic over Tallon's whereabouts on the world. 4-LB gave them information on an individual fitting Tallon's description but confused him with Bels Lank. The conversation was overheard by the assassin droid IG-72. After the Rebels left, IG-72 trashed 4-LB and the Power Station before leaving for Tallon's supposed location. Tatooine Manhunt describes 4-LB as a labor droid, while Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley refers to the droid as a power droid. *Tatooine Manhunt *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley Category:General labor droids
