Pulsar (DP20 gunship)
1731

The Pulsar was a DP20 gunship that was a part of the New Republic Defense Force attached to General Keyan Farlander's fleet towards the end of 27 ABY. The Pulsar was commanded by an Elomin called Kartha during the Second Battle of Obroa-skai during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The gunship, however, was heavily damaged and was scuttled following the battle. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:DP20 gunships Category:Starships of the New Republic
