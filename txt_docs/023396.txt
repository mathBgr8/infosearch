Tatooine Sunburn
2326

A Tatooine Sunburn, also known as a Tatooine Sunrise, was an intoxicating beverage consisting of two Starshine Surprises in the same glass. It was a rough drink that was said to put the drinker under the table quick to the benefit of nearby thieves. Tatooine Sunburn was served at the Bantha Traxx on the planet Lianna where it cost 7 credits per glassWretched Hives of Scum & Villainy. The Gusha's Luck cantina on Tatooine also sold Tatooine Sunburn for an increased price. Jariah Syn expressed disappointment when he learned a Twi'lek patron only drank that particular drink, as he was low on credits.
