Plu Makor
1582

Plu Makor was a Near-Human male Rebel Alliance operative who ran a ship repair shop on G-Station Seven, a space station that orbited the planet Genesia. He was distinguished from a typical Human by his red eyes and the short barbs which surrounded his eyes and temples. He was also the owner of the Helping Hand, a YT-1300 light freighter. *Flashpoint!
