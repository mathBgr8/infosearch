The first of two gas giants in the Velm system, it was located in the Halori sector in the Trans-Hydian portion of the Mid Rim. Veloc was orbited by thirty-three moons, 281 asteroid moonlets, and a planetary ring. Veloc was mentioned in Velmor: Royalty and Rebellion, a roleplaying source article written by Cory J. Herndon and published on Wizards.com on March 6, 2003 for use with Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Velm system, and thereby Veloc, in grid square P-7.
