Merisee Prime Pharmaceutical
555

Merisee Prime Pharmaceutical was a company which used the planet Meris III's unusual fauna as a basis for most of its drugs. The drug company's headquarters was located in the city of Caronath on the planet Merisee. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Medical companies
