Tol Ado
2304

Tol Ado was a planet located within the Tol Ado system of the Outer Rim Territories. It was an Imperial prison planet where Luke Skywalker was once captured and detained, but escaped. The planet was heavy clouded by a poisonous atmosphere that contained hydrogen fluoride. Its surface temperature was around 477 degrees Celsius, and its atmospheric pressure was ten times higher than the galactic average.
