Korenn
1403

Korenn was a Human male with spiky, sandy hair who looked younger than his experiences foretold. He was one of Crix Madine's commandos. He died in 12 ABY when his A-wing hit an asteroid in the Hoth asteroid field en route to confirm New Republic suspicions on the underhanded schemes of Hutt gangster Durga Besadii Tai.
