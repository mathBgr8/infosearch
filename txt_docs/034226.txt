Gann (assassin)
1373

Gann was a former Imperial assassin condemned to death for his part in a brutal massacre. However, his sentence was commuted by Darth Vader along with those of Nevo, Fraal, and Lumus so that they could follow Boba Fett during his hunt of Abal Karda.
