Retribution (Marauder-class)
2483

The Retribution was a Marauder-class corvette that served the fledgling Corellian Resistance during the early days of the Galactic Civil War. It was purchased from the Corporate Sector Authority by the Corellian underground resistance and commanded by Bria Tharen, serving as the mobile base for Red Hand Squadron. The vessel participated in several major engagements for the Rebels, most notably the capture of the slave-ship Helot's Shackle and the Battle of Ylesia.
