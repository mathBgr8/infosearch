Exozone
1928

thumb|left|260px|Boba Fett, Mako Spince, Dengar, and Exozone pursue Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo on nar Shaddaa. Exozone was an insectoid bounty hunter that sometimes worked with Boba Fett and Dengar. During the events surrounding the return of the resurrected Emperor, he helped the other bounty hunters chase Han Solo and Princess Leia through the streets of Nar Shaddaa. During the pursuit of Han Solo and Princess Leia, Exozone wore a Blast helmet, full body armor, was armed with a Blaster rifle and a long machete-like blade.
