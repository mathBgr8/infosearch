Projectile detonator
578

Projectile detonators were small explosives that were launched from either a wrist-mounted launcher or a slugthrower, that exploded on impact. Leia Organa carried one on a bandolier while wearing Ubese raider armor *Star Wars: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars Character Encyclopedia Category:Explosive projectiles
