Honor
1039

The Honor was one of the capital ships used by the Yevetha of the Duskhan League during the genocidal Great Purge in 16-17 ABY. Along with Liberty and the Star Destroyer command ship Devotion of Yevetha, Honor was one of three ships of the Yevethan task force sent against Polneye at the start of the Purge. Her name suggests that she was one of the Imperial ships from Black Sword Command captured by the Yevetha in their rising in 4 ABY, or at least a Yevethan-built ship of Imperial design, but available evidence does not indicate clearly what class she was. *Before the Storm *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital ships Category:Yevethan starships
