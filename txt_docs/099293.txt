An'yettu clan
1669

The An'yettu clan was a family of Rodians on Rodia. Locked in an endless feud with the Chattza Clan, the ancient tribe hailed from the An'yettu Islands, resolving to remain withdrawn from politics. Grand Protector Navik the Red ensured the continuation of this isolation by preventing them from expanding beyond this archipelago.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Opdoa of the assignment committee of the Goa-Ato, was surprised to hear that a member of that clan, Fask Eelak, made it to become a hunter of the Guild. The An'yettu clan was first mentioned in Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide. In Fantasy Flight Games' roleplaying game sourcebook Dangerous Covenants, there is an adventure seed where the players join the Guardians of the clan in fighting off Chattza Clan raiders.
