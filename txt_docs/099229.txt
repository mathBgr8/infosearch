Throwing spear
1230

Throwing spears (also called javelins) were primitive weapons used by archaic cultures like Ewoks. They were slightly shorter than a normal spear and in the hands of a skilled warrior could be thrown over a long distance with considerable power. The main advantage to these weapons were the fact they were more or less silent, allowing for stealthy kills.
