Mer-9
965

Mer-9 was a protocol droid assigned by the New Republic to observe the actions of the Corporate Sector Authority and the pro-Imperial forces in Verena City. To do this, Mer-9 worked as a translator and assistant to Bren-Aarica Rordan in her shop. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Protocol droids Category:Spy droids
