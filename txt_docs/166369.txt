Hez Kragg
813

The Dread Buccaneer Hez Kragg was an infamous space pirate during the time of the Old Republic. He became so legendary that his adventures were told as a fable to children, until evidence proving his existence surfaced during the Galactic Civil War. His flagship was Kragg's Fury, which he left booby-trapped in order to challenge any profiteers that came across his final resting place. Kraggs' symbol was a crimson fist set upon a circle of gold.
