Raider (Gladius-class)
2554

The Raider was a Gladius-class light freighter owned by Sith Acolyte Jor Torlin, an agent of Sith Intelligence and Assassination. He took it to the Wheel in 137 ABY as part of his mission to capture Cade Skywalker. Morrigan Corde overpowered Torlin, taking them to Ossus where she killed him, before leaving in the Raider.
