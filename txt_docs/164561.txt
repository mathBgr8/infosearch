Inertial inhibitor
625

The inertial inhibitor was a personal inertial compensator worn on a belt in the period surrounding the Mandalorian Wars and the Jedi Civil War. It eased the physical stresses of movement in combat, reducing reaction time for the wearer. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Belts
