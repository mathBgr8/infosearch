The Force Unleashed (Star Wars Miniatures)
5999

thumb|The Force Unleashed set poster The Force Unleashed is a Star Wars Miniatures set that was released on November 16, 2007 covering the Star Wars: The Force Unleashed multimedia project. It consisted of sixty figures, all boosters, including fifteen game characters and ten huge figures.
