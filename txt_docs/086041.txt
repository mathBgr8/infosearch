Grendyl
1351

Grendyl was a female Human member of the Galactic Alliance Navy, stationed onboard the Admiral Ackbar. Part of the starfighter team stationed on the Ackbar, she was present when the ship was ambushed by the Colony's forces during the Qoribu Crisis. Standing between Admiral Nek Bwua'tu and Dark Jedi Alema Rar, she sacrificed herself in order to save Bwua'tu, taking a full volley of Force lightning to the chest. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Galactic Alliance Defense Fleet personnel Category:Humans
