Clariv Shipyards
476

The Clariv Shipyards was a shipyard. The Kerestian bounty hunter Milacass was the victim of an explosion at the shipyards which resulted in searing to both his eyes. Although his vision returned, Milacass's eyes no longer appeared to have pupils. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Shipyards
