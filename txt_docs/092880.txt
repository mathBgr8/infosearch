Cidwen
1391

The cidwen were black, predatory, winged insects native to the planet Keros with dangerous beaks and claws. They were introduced to Atraken when a free-trader left a pair of them behind. By the time of 6 ABY, there were a dozen living on the planet. They could grow up to a half-meter long with a one-meter wingspan. They had grown an appetite for the diehard, an insect that was native to Atraken.
