Corioline Marlee Theatre
680

The Corioline Marlee Theatre was a renowned theater and place of the arts that was located on Alderaan before that world's destruction. Leia Organa Solo visited it in her youth; the rebuilt Grand Convocation Chamber of the New Republic Senate reminded her of the theater. In 0 BBY, it was destroyed along with Alderaan itself. *Specter of the Past Category:Alderaan locations Category:Theatrical venues
