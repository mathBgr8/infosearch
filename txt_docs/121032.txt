FX-2 medical assistant droid
1124

The FX-2 medical assistant droid was a medical droid manufactured by Medtech Industries as part of the FX-series of medical assistant droids. Produced during the last decades of the Galactic Republic, it was ultimately succeeded by the FX-3 model. *Crucible *The New Essential Guide to Droids FX-02
