Le facteur X
2942

"Le facteur X" was a roleplaying adventure in the French gaming magazine Casus Belli. It was written by Philippe Rat and Mehdi Sahmi and illustrated by Stéphane Levallois. The adventure began in the Rebellion era, just prior to the Battle of Endor. A scientist for the Imperial-aligned corporation Flight Unlimited Group got word to the Rebel Alliance of a prototype starship called the Trait Luminique. The Alliance were prepared to send only a small team to rescue the scientist and steal the ship at the same time. However, when escaping in the experimental ship, the Rebels somehow traveled back in time to the Great Sith War. The rest of the adventure is set in this period, with the players having various encounters in this period. *Casus Belli 115 In addition to creating many new locations and characters, the article also made use of the West End Games sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, suggesting various groups that could aid the characters during the Rebellion era segment.
