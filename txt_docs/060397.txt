Pizzazz 13
1266

Pizzazz 13 is the thirteenth issue in the Marvel Comics popular culture monthly magazine. This issue was cover-dated October 1978 and featured the continuation of the serialized Star Wars comic The Kingdom of Ice. The article "Test Driving the Great Spaceships" discusses science fiction movie spaceships, including the Millennium Falcon, Star Destroyers, the Death Star, X-wing fighters and TIE fighters. It is written in the words of a "used spaceship salesman." 13
