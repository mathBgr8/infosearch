Utozz Prime Brewery
1024

thumb|right|250px|Consumers of [[Utoz/Legends|Utoz]] Utozz Prime Brewery was a brewing corporation headquartered in the Elrood sector on Merisee and that had offices located in the Elrood Bazaar. The company brewed Utozz, a brand of the alcoholic drink known as Utoz, during the time of the Galactic Civil War. It shared it's name with the Utozz Prime system in the Outer Rim Territories. *Operation: Elrood *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Three *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Foodstuff manufacturers
