Corusca Fire
4816

Gauntlet, later renamed Corusca Fire, was a Victory I-class Star Destroyer in the service of the Imperial Navy and the New Republic. It participated in the Battle of Ciutric under the command of General Garm Bel Iblis, and later in the Battle of Dantooine during the Yuuzhan Vong War under the command of Captain Rimsen. Having received a signal from Admiral Ackbar, the Corusca Fire performed the Thrawn Pincer, reverting into real space behind enemy lines, along with the Selonian Fire, another New Republic Victory I-class Star Destroyer. With their combined firepower, the two Star Destroyers managed to destroy Delak Krennel's flagship, the Reckoning, killing Krennel in the process. During the Battle of Dantooine, the Corusca Fire, along with the Bothan Assault Cruiser Ralroost, managed to fend off a Yuuzhan Vong cruiser long enough to allow for the evacuation of the remaining soldiers on Dantooine. Gauntlet is one of several names in the class-specific lists from which the video games Star Wars: Empire at War and its expansion Forces of Corruption randomly select names for capital ships.
