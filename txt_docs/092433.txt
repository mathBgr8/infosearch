Snic 2-4-2
1772

The Snic 2-4-2, also known as a Strong Arm Droid, was a fifth-degree labor droid. The 2-4-2 droid was equipped with a repulsorlift drive and two large, primary grasping arms that allowed it to carry cargo. It had two smaller manipulator arms on either side of its body, for fine work. Around 15 BBY some of these droids were owned and operated by Tig Fromm and the Fromm Gang. They worked inside Tig Fromm's base.
