Trampeta's Star Guide was a traveler's guide, first published in 4086 BBY. It was best known for reviewing Taris and giving it the lowest possible recommendation, because the authors' baggage was stolen by T3 droids working at the starport upon their arrival.The New Essential Guide to Droids During the search for Nok Drayen's treasure, Risha Drayen doubted that it gave the local cantinas a rating higher than one. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *The New Essential Guide to Droids Category:Travel guides
