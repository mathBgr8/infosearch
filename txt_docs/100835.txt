Llalik Designs
1001

Llalik Designs was the personal business venture of Nashiak Llalik before he became a devotee of the Galactic Empire. Llalik attempted to use this company for his pet project of Sunder 9 armor, but he eventually abandoned the project and the company after one year to work on the Zero-G assault stormtrooper armor for the Empire. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Gundark's Fantastic Technology: Personal Gear Category:Technology companies
