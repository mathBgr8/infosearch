Sparkle-bop
586

Sparkle-bop was a genre of music that involved catchy or hummable melodies, simplistic three-chord structures, repetitive riffs or "hooks", and lightweight lyrics, that were deceptively simple at best. Sparkle-bop became popular during the time of the New Republic. *Planet of Twilight Category:Music genres
