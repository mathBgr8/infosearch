Blumfruit cooler
574

Blumfruit cooler was an intoxicating drink made from the blumfruit of Endor. At some point, a group of unruly university students visiting the forest moon drank too much of the beverage and caused quite a snaffu by extinguishing a sacred light lantern hanging from the Spirit Tree. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Alcoholic beverages Category:Endorian food
