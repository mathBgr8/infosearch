Patijak Sonn
1299

Patijak Sonn was the author of the book Kholemi Elaru. For a time, it was the fourth best-selling book on a list compiled by HoloNet News's CoCo Town Edition on the date 13:2:28. Patijak Sonn was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #45, a 2002 in-universe website. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content.
