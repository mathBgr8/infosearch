Corvis (smuggler)
1308

Corvis was a male Human smuggler. He was a member of Talon Karrde's organization during the Thrawn campaign, serving as a gunner on Karrde's flagship and mobile base of operations, the Wild Karrde. During 19 ABY, Corvis worked the comm station onboard the Starry Ice.
