It was liberated from the Confederacy of Independent Systems by Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi in 22 BBY, during the Clone Wars. The planet had a militia that opposed the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Its atmosphere blocked sensors and was filled with flying lizards. The planet was covered in farmland and large mountains. In 22 BBY, Terra Sool was under Separatist occupation.
