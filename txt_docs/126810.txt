Gahseelik
1509

Gahseelik was a male Trandoshan who disgraced his clan by leaving Trandosha to work for Gardulla the Hutt during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. For this a bounty was posted on his head by Trossilik. His bounty was claimed by Jango Fett for 3,000 credits, who was on a mission to meet with Gardulla.
