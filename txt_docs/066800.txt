Badlands (CR90 corvette)
1949

Badlands was an Imperial CR90 corvette during the Galactic Civil War. Commanded by Commander Kraven, Badlands served under Garreth Holtz's Corrupter Task Force. The Badlands accompanied the Corrupter during her pursuit of the Defiance Task Force after the Battle of Hoth in 3 ABY.
