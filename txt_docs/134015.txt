Happer's Way
1028

thumb|right|210px|The crashed Happer's Way on [[Shelkonwa.]] Happer's Way was a Rendili StarDrive heavy freighter flown by Captain Norello out of Chandrila. It was contracted by the Imperial Army to bring fifty AT-ST walkers to the garrison on Llorkan, but was interrupted in its delivery by an attack from the pirate ship Cavalcade. The ship and its captain were rescued by Emperor's Hand Mara Jade. *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Heavy freighters Category:Rendili StarDrive products
