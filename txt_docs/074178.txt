Shayl Le'tah
1391

Shayl Le'tah was a female Bothan Jedi Knight who was able to combine her innate abilities offered by the Force and her inherent talent for covert operations that come from being a Bothan. She was known for her skills as a Jedi investigator. Her Force powers included Alter, Battlemind, Control, and Force sense. Shaly Le'tah is described in the Star Wars Galaxies Update article in Star Wars Gamer 3. She is concept art by Arnie Jorgensen for the video game Star Wars Galaxies.
