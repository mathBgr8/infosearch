Wave Works
1165

Wave Works was a watery gift shop owned by the Ishi Tib Jund Voon, which was part of the Fathoms resort in Wildwater City on Dac. It sold a wide variety of water sculptures and other hand-crafted items. Unbeknownst to the general public, all of the items were not actually created by Voon, but by a Wavedancer slave named Kei No Kay. *Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy Category:Businesses Category:Mon Cala locations
