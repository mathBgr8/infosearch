**Kruskob TiltankaThe New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology |model=Kob sonic blaster |type=Sonic blaster |culture= |creators= |created= |destroyed= |discovered= |owners= |locations= |cost= |value= |shape= |size= |material= |length= |width= |diam= |height= |weight= |protection= |crew= |capacity=At least 16 sonic pulses |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=Nagai }} The Kob sonic blaster, also known as the Nagai sonic blaster, was a sonic blaster developed by the Nagai raiders. A weapon that saw widespread use among Nagai raiders, the Kob sonic blaster was the size of a large pistol or small rifle, often including a fore-grip, and was considered an elegant weapon due to its versatility. The Kob sonic blaster utilized a controlled harmonic wave that could be set to a wide variety of power settings including its lowest setting, "light stun", which emitted a sonic pulse that could stun most known organic life, to its highest setting "deteriorate", which emitted a sonic beam capable of tearing through matter at the molecular level. Named after its designer, the cunning General Kruskob Tiltanka of the Nagai, the Kob sonic blaster saw widespread use among the Nagai military, being especially prominent during the invasion of Iskalon. On Iskalon they were tested on captives on the planet, before being deployed against Luke Skywalker and Lando Calrissian as they fought against the Nagai occupation.
