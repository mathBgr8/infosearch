Slar
1637

Slars were predators native to Port Evokk that would not hesitate to attack humanoids. The creatures had long and powerful legs that allowed them to jump long distances. They would use this ability to close the distance on their prey. Their hooves allowed them to get traction on the ground to improve their leaps. They had long muscled tails that allowed them to stabilize themselves. Their hands had three elongated claws with webbing in-between that they used to pin their prey to the ground. Their heads were topped with razored quills which they used to shred their victims. While only needing to feed every several days, slars would sometimes go for longer periods without killing.
