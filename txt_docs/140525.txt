Battle of the white dwarf
2201

A battle took place in 26 ABY during the Yuuzhan Vong War, near a white dwarf between Corellia and Commenor. A Peace Brigade operation to transfer bacta to the Yuuzhan Vong via CorDuro Shipping was foiled by an ambush of the Wild Knights. Han Solo on the Millennium Falcon, evacuating Jedi from Corellia, participated in the battle as well. *The New Jedi Order: Recovery Category:Battles of the Yuuzhan Vong War
