Dalabar Micro-Electronics
1053

Dalabar Micro-Electronics was a company that developed a line of surveillance and detection systems and devices. They had over two decades of experiencing in serving the needs of security-minded customers, and developed a reputation for being discrete and producing quality merchandise. They manufactured the SDS-632 Surveillance Detector and the NI-632 noise inhibitor. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology Category:Security companies
