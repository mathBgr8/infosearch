Vasnish Kay
1067

Vasnish Kay was a Bimm bard who traveled with her merchant family across the galaxy when she was young. While Kay was an adolescent, her parents were killed in a tragic accident. Rather than return to her native planet of Bimmisaari, Kay set out to explore the galaxy, trading stories and information for passage.
