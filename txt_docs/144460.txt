Lacy Devist
1289

Lacy Devist was a female Human who was married to Carib Devist as of 19 ABY. It was unlikely that Lacy knew her husband was in actuality a clone of Baron Soontir Fel, and was also an Imperial Intelligence agent planted on Pakrik Minor by Grand Admiral Thrawn.
