Zoma Gawanga

Zoma Gawanga, literally "Western Ocean" in Huttese, was an ocean on Ylesia. Colony One, Colony Two, and Colony Three were built on its shore. *The Paradise Snare *Rebel Dawn *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Seas and oceans Category:Ylesia locations
