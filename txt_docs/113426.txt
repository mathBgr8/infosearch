Blade of the Betrayer
2339

The Blade of the Betrayer was a single-handed blade owned by the female Near-Human Dark Jedi Vansk, who served as a member of the Force-using organization known as the Blackguard during the Galactic Civil War. Vansk did not often use the blade in combat, preferring instead to use her red-bladed lightsaber, but she carried the blade as a secondary weapon. The blade was taken from Vansk after her death in 1 ABY at the hands of spacers, when a Sullustan archaeologist named Mi Fon Lu hired a group of spacers to kill all the Blackguard in the Southern Jedi Ruins on the planet Mustafar, as he wished to research in the area. The Blade of the Betrayer first appeared in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies as part of the Trials of Obi-Wan expansion released in 2005.
