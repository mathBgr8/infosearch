Blood on the Flowers Flightknife
579

Blood on the Flowers Flightknife was a unit of Adumari Blade-32 starfighters from the planet Adumar, who challenged and lost to the Imperial Remnant flight visiting their world. Only one badly-wounded pilot managed to survive the duel by ejecting from his stricken craft. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Adumari flightknives
