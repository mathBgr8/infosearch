Niman
939

The Niman were the double trinity of gods of the Kashi people. The Legions of Lettow named two lightsaber styles after the double trio: Form VI: Niman and the dual blades technique Jar'Kai. Jar'Kai was called "Niman" because it used two blades, just as there were two trinities. Form VI was called Niman because the two trinities made up a total of six gods. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Deities
