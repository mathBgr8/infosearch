Otoh Raban
2232

Otoh Raban was a Gungan city on Naboo in 3000 BBY. Otoh Raban was a city on the planet Naboo in 3000 BBY during the time of the War of the Gungan tribes. During Boss Gallo's efforts to unite the Gungan villages and defeat Boss Rogoe in the War, Otoh Raban was led by Boss Hoxie. Hoxie was kidnapped by Rogoe and imprisoned in a base guarded by Rogoe's Millitiagungs. Gallo came through the city and after talking with a villager, agreed he would free Hoxie. Gallo and his partner Captain Marsune destroyed Rogoe's stronghold and freed Hoxie, who pledged his support to Gallo's cause.
