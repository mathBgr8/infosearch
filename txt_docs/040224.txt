Elite (disambiguation)
1115

Elite can refer to several things: *Special forces *The Elite, a hotel on Coruscant *The private warriors of General Grievous *The Dark Side Elite, the Dark Jedi that served the reborn Emperor Palpatine. *Elite was a name of an Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate. *The Yuuzhan Vong Elite, the select few that made up the court of the Supreme Overlord of the Yuuzhan Vong. *The Geonosian elites, specialized Geonosian warriors. *Geonosian elite beam weapon, the weapon of the Geonosian elites. *Emperor's Sword, a squadron of skilled TIE pilots in the Imperial Navy who served Emperor Palpatine directly.
