Guile Company
894

Guile Company was a unit of Clawdites during the Yuuzhan Vong War. The shapeshifting Clawdites allied with the New Republic during the war, serving as an intelligence unit behind Yuuzhan Vong lines. Despite a high casualty rate, Guile Company kept receiving new volunteers. However, Clawdites who had children were disallowed into their ranks, though some hid the fact they had children from the unit.
