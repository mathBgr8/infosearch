Esselian Hall
1626

The Esselian Hall, also known as the Esselian parliament and simply as the Hall, was the governing body of the planet Esseles following the Clone Wars.Coruscant and the Core Worlds It was led by a President and they met in the Hall of Esseles in Calamar. During the time of the Galactic Civil War, Cambira Ralle served as president, although much power was lost to Imperial Governor Griff Takel. Political factions of the Hall included the Forad, Cardean, and Esselian New Order Parties. Following the election of 2 ABY, the Forad Party lost its grip on the majority, with the Esselian New Order Party making major inroads. *The Far Orbit Project *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Legislatures Category:Planetary governments
