Brak Sector is a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Sterling Hershey and published by West End Games in 1995. It is a campaign book that gives locations, events and game stats and guidelines to play adventures. The time frame is some weeks after the Battle of Yavin. Flashpoint: Any situation or area with a potential for a sudden outbreak of conflict. Brak sector: A sector of space that serves as a staging area for Imperial Navy missions into the Outer Rim Territories. Rebel forces have made substantial gains in this former mining sector.
