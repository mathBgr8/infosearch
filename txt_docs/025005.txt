|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} A-9 was a landing platform located in the city Joventusek on the planet Caarimon. Circa 21 BBY,The Living Force Campaign Guide states that the Living Force campaign is set one year after the Invasion of Naboo, an event that is dated to 32 BBY by The New Essential Chronology. As the Living Force adventure Philology moved the campaign forward ten years to the time of the Clone Wars, Belly of the Beaast can therefore be dated to circa 21 BBY. the heroes of Cularin, a group of freelance agents from the Cularin system, ventured to Caarimon, seeking to thwart the dispersal on the world of an anti-Caarite virus, and docked their starship on A-9. The agents were greeted on the platform by the Caarite Nuris Blaht of the Caarimon Ministry of Conservation. Category:Caarimon locations Category:Landing platforms
