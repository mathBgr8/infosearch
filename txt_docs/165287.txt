Unidentified laundry service
880

This laundry service in Gadrin was run by an elderly pair of Sullustans. A large portion of its business was the cleaning of uniforms for local companies, such as House Hirskaala and Renna's Transport Service. In 31 BBY, on two days in a row, the laundry service was robbed of the uniforms they had cleaned. These uniforms were then used by the pirates who stole them to help in hijacking goods from the businesses the uniforms belonged to. Category:Cularin locations Category:Service industry companies
