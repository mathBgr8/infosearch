The Galactic Empire laid siege to Dalron Five using techniques that had been developed by Jan Dodonna. The Empire managed to conquer the world after a devastating siege, forcing its people to flee and live as refugees. These refugee-camps were later on discovered by the Imperial scout Lak Sivrak, who agreed to keep their whereabouts a secret. Later on, his superiors used Sivrak's report to piece together their location and the Dalron Five refugees were finally caught.
