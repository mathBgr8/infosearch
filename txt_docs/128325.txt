Bridgeworld Lost
4166

|pages= |published in= |genre= }} Bridgeworld Lost was a painting created after 104 ABY set during the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, which lasted from 22 BBY to 19 BBY.The New Essential Chronology It depicted a group of Skakoans&mdash;a species associated with the Techno Union, a member organization of the ConfederacyThe New Essential Guide to Alien Species&mdash;surrounded by blaster-wielding clone troopers of the Grand Army of the Republic. The background of the painting contained bridge cities and three Munificent-class star frigates. Bridgeworld Lost appeared in "Celestia Galactica Photografica," a collection of in-universe illustrations by Ryan Church collected in Star Wars: Visionaries, a graphic novel edited by Jeremy Barlow and released on April 2, 2005. The concept of a "bridge world" was previously developed by Church in 2002 during production of the prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith, which premiered on May 19, 2005.Star Wars Year By Year: A Visual History, Updated and Expanded Edition It was intended to be the planet Saleucami and to serve as the home of the Techno Union. The designs were instead used for the planet Cato Neimoidia in the finished film. Church additionally created a concept art piece titled "Mygeeto Surrender" on December 6, 2002.
