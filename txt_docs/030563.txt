Administrator Senior
549

Administrator Senior was the title given to the ruler of the Oseon system in the Centrality, seemingly analogous to the title of Governor elsewhere&mdash;with large amounts of power but the ability to be overriden by the ruler of whatever government their system belonged to. Lob Doluff was the Administrator Senior as of 4 BBY. *Lando Calrissian and the Flamewind of Oseon Category:Political titles
