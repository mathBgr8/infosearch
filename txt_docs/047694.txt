Jaw-knee Rise
1405

Jaw-knee Rise, probably not his real name, was a male Gran hitman who worked for the RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. The Grand Council of Pixelito ended up putting a bounty on Rise's head, a bounty later collected by famed Mandalorian bounty hunter Jango Fett. His informant was Meen Burtak.
