Spice Blades
733

The Spice Blades were a small elite force of Sevari serving under Spice Lord Cassius Nolath Rha. They were specially trained in vibroblade and flashrifle combat and had a tendency to shoot first and ask questions later. They wore black and blood-red tunics, trousers and boots, and distinctive respirators pumping humidified black spice into their lungs, which made them totally loyal to their master.
