Baragwin heavy repeating blaster

thumb|right|A Baragwin heavy repeating blaster. The Baragwin heavy repeating blaster was a model of repeating blaster that was manufactured by the Baragwin, who were known as gifted weaponsmiths. Such repeating blasters were highly upgradeable and packed a heavy punch; as such, they were quite pricey.
