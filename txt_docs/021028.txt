DVL-181
1179

DVL-181, nicknamed the Quagmire and the Slime Pit, was a Rebel Alliance starfighter base built on a remote swampy planet. It was formerly known as the Aracara Testing Facility and was used as a corporate weapons and material testing zone. When it proved to be unprofitable, it was abandoned. DVL-181 hosted the starfighters of the Silverblade X-wing squadron. It used a LongView-382.X sensor array. *Hideouts & Strongholds Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic military bases
