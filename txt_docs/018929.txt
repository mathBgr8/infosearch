Self-Regulating Droidmaker
507

Self-Regulating Droidmaker was a fully automated machine for converting raw materials into droids. Based on the same principles as the construction droid, the Droidmaker required no dedicated setup or manufacturing facilities. It was produced by the Loronar Corporation shortly before the Yuuzhan Vong War. Category:Manufacturing technology
