Leinil Francis Yu
5130

Leinil Francis Yu is a Filipino comic book artist who provided covers for Dark Horse Comics' Star Wars Tales 10 and Star Wars Tales 15. He has later drawn variant covers for two issues of the first story arc of Marvel Comics' relaunched Star Wars comic series and for the first issue of the Lando miniseries. Yu has also penciled Darth Vader Annual 1 and the fourth arc of the Star Wars series.
