The Yuuzhan Vong Deception sect had its own language. This was presumably in line with their devotion to the goddess of duplicity, Yun-Harla. The sect's priests could speak the tongue. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:Languages Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
