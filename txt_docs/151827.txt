Eriadu Way
1934

The Eriadu Way, or Eriadu Bypass,Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds was an old hyperspace trade route that ran thousands of light-years to connect the Greater Javin region to the Rimma Trade Route. The Nothoiin Corridor, blazed through the Ivax Nebula by Ecclessis Figg's explorers, connected the Corellian Trade Spine to the Eriadu Way. By the time of the Galactic Civil War, it had become regarded as a part of the Hydian Way after so many traders used it during the final century of the Galactic Republic that some astrographers considered the Hydian Way to have "jumped" from its old course to the bypass through Eriadu. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Outer Rim Territories hyperlanes Category:Segments of the Hydian Way Category:Trailing Sectors hyperlanes
