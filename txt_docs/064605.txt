Grakko
1886

Grakko was a male Quarren mercenary who worked for the Falleen crime lord Zenex on the Wheel space station. Grakko was tasked with killing Khaleen Hentz, whom he had encountered at some point in the past, after she had stolen an important holodisc from Zenex. Grakko caught up with her on the maintenance levels of The Wheel, where they engaged in a brief fight. The fight ended when Khaleen set off a detonator that was still attached to Grakko's utility belt.
