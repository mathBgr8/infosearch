Frankie Ryan Manriquez
2266

Frankie Ryan Manriquez (born March 14, 1994) voiced a young Anakin Skywalker in Star Wars: Clone Wars Chapter 21. Manriquez started working when he was three in print ads. In 2001, he made his acting debut in one episode of the TV series The King of Queens. He appeared in several other TV shows, such as Life with Bonnie, Blue Collar TV, Life on a Stick and That's So Raven. He also voiced several other characters, including Skippy in Megas XLR, Young Ruffshodd in Racing Stripes, Jimmy and Ernie in What's New, Scooby-Doo? and Wayne in Higglytown Heroes. In 2005, Manriquez was nominated for the Best Performance in a TV Series (Comedy or Drama) - Young Actor Age Ten or Younger at the 26th Young Artist Awards for his role in Life with Bonnie.
