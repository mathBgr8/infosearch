Emperor's Plague
1956

thumb|right|300px|Jedi trainees [[Jaina Solo Fel|Jaina and Jacen Solo among several plague canisters in the Emperor's plague storehouse.]] The Emperor's Plague was a mutagenic plague developed by Evir Derricote that only killed Humans. It was more deadly than the Krytos virus which killed millions of aliens on the planet Coruscant following the New Republic capture of Coruscant in 6 ABY. It was said to be so horrible that even Emperor Palpatine feared to deploy it. This plague was stored at an uncharted asteroid known as the Emperor's plague storehouse. In 24 ABY, a sample of it was obtained by an information broker, Fonterrat, who sold it to Nolaa Tarkona.
