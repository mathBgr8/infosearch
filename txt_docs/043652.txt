Hardan plague
1058

The Hardan plague was a virulent disease known to be transported by Bogan's brown nafen. An outbreak which killed 98% of the population on Dagelin Minor was traced back to a shipment of nafen that was imported to control the Dhuru fly population. *Creatures of the Galaxy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Diseases and maladies
