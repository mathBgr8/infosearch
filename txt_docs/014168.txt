Valorum Shipping and Transport
1882

Valorum Shipping and Transport was a business based on Eriadu owned by House Valorum. In addition to shipping and transporting, it also was involved in starship manufacturing and operated shipyards in orbit of Eriadu. Finis Valorum was a member of the company's board of directors; this caused a scandal when it was revealed that the company would benefit by his proposal to tax trade routes, and that aurodium of the same amount lost by the Trade Federation at Dorvalla had been secretly transferred into its bank account. *Darth Plagueis *"Darth Maul: Saboteur" *Cloak of Deception *The New Essential Chronology *The Essential Atlas p.140 Category:Shipwrights Category:Transport companies
