Womwa
1170

Womwa was an Ithorian male who left his homeworld and settled on a remote planet. He liked the planet so much, he decided to remain, and opened a cantina known as The Haven. The cantina was a front, however, for Womwa's lucrative spice smuggling operations. *Ultimate Adversaries Category:Business owners and executives Category:Ithorians Category:Males Category:Smugglers
