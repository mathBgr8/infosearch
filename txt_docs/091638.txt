B4 Cloudbus
1620

The Nubian B4 Cloudbus was a popular model of civilian airspeeder manufactured by Nubian Design Collective and comprising the vast majority of the public transportation on the planet Nubia. Seating 36 passengers, the blue-and-gray, elongated oval-shaped vehicle was equipped with three Nubia Star Drives ion engines.
