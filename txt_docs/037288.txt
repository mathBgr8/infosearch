Natt Bordo
1649

Natt Bordo was a male Human guard who lived in Desolation Alley during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He was wanted by the Hutt Cartel for skimming profits from a smuggling operation. His bounty was collected by the bounty hunter Jango Fett. This character is a bounty target in the game Star Wars: Bounty Hunter, worth 4,000 credits dead or alive.
