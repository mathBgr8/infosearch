Eclipse (hotel)
825

The Eclipse was the most exclusive hotel in Ussa, the capital city of Bellassa, with extraordinary service, catering to wealthy people. The hotel had more than 200 floors with transparisteel elevators. *The Last of the Jedi: A Tangled Web *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bellassa locations Category:Hotels
