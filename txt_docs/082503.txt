At some point in his life, Travgen lost his way and strayed down the path of the dark side of the Force. Surviving the disbanding of the Order and the collapse of the Republic, Travgen fled to the abandoned world of Ossus where he lived out his exile ruling over the Ysanna people. A Force-sensitive, Travgen received instruction in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order and had ascended to the rank of Jedi Knight by the time the Clone Wars erupted between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Travgen's role in the war is unknown; though it is known by historians that the Jedi had fallen under the sway of the dark side of the Force and left the Order before the end of the war. When Supreme Chancellor Palpatine, truly the alter-ego of Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Sidious issued Contingency Order 66 to the Grand Army of the Republic, the Jedi Order was effectively disbanded and most of its members slaughtered. Because he was not directly involved with the Order or the war, Travgen survived. Wandering the galaxy, Travgen discovered the ancient Jedi fortress world Ossus, a planet on which he would make his home. With his superior mastery of the Force, Travgen easily took control of several local tribes of Ysanna, the world's native people. As their ruler, he agreed to protect the ruins of the ancient Library of the Jedi, a looming structure located on the Eocho Mountains. Taking up residence in a dilapidated government building, Travgen occupied the world since around 9 BBY. After the fall of the Galactic Empire, Travgen remained in hiding on Ossus, where he occupied ruins from the planet’s ancient Jedi past and maintained influence over nearby tribes through fear and coercion. In 10 ABY, he sensed the arrival of Luke Skywalker and Kam Solusar, who had traveled to Ossus in search of Jedi records to aid in the reconstruction of the Jedi Order. Although aware of their presence, Travgen chose not to reveal himself or make contact, viewing Skywalker as a potential danger. He allowed the two Jedi to enter the Great Library unopposed and remained concealed throughout their visit. Travgen was relieved when Skywalker departed Ossus, and he took no further action following their departure. Travgen was cautious, self-preserving, and manipulative, having survived the Galactic Empire by remaining hidden rather than confronting its enforcers. After settling on Ossus, he spent decades in isolation and meditation, gradually establishing control over nearby tribes through fear and coercion. He consistently avoided direct confrontation, preferring to act through intimidation or proxies, and relied on enslaved tribes to confront perceived threats on his behalf. When faced with resistance, Travgen was prone to withdraw rather than stand his ground, demonstrating a lack of bravery and decisiveness compared to figures such as Luke Skywalker. Although aware of Skywalker’s presence during his visit to Ossus, Travgen chose not to reveal himself and was relieved when the Jedi departed, viewing such encounters as dangers to be avoided rather than challenges to be faced. His prolonged isolation ultimately reflected a self-serving desire for survival and control, rather than any commitment to wider galactic affairs. According to contemporary accounts, Travgen was a former Jedi Knight and therefore had likely passed the Jedi Trials prior to the fall of the Republic. After settling on Ossus, he spent approximately two decades in isolation, meditation, and the domination of local Ysanna tribes, whose abilities he clearly surpassed through his mastery of the Force. By this period, his overall power was described as being comparable to that of Luke Skywalker around 10 ABY, though he lacked Skywalker’s confidence and willingness to confront powerful adversaries directly.
