Rallan
1157

Rallan was a male Tratlin who served as a Rebel agent and a member of the Sandwind Team on Sriluur. Prior to the formation of the Sandwind Team, Rallan was a solo operative in Meirm City on Sriluur. For years, he fed supplies to the Dnalvec Militia in addition to his "standard" assignments for the Rebellion.
