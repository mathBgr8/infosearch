|pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series=Star Wars Tales |issue=13 }} Star Wars Tales 13 is the thirteenth issue in the Star Wars Tales series of comics. It was published on September 25, 2002 by Dark Horse Comics. He's bald. He's bad. His lightsaber is purple. He's MACE WINDU! Make sure not to miss out on this stunning tribute to the baddest Jedi Council member to ever have his midi-chlorians counted! Featuring "The Secret of Tet-Ami" by Fabian Nicieza and Timothy II, "Children of the Force" by Jason Hall and Lucas Marangon, "The Sith in Shadow" by Bob Harris and Jerome Opena, and MUCH MORE! Featuring a cover by Tim Bradstreet! Introduction: Star Wars Tales editor Dave Land introduces the issue and its focus on Mace Windu, who he calls "the most powerful Jedi of all time," a claim disputed by Yoda. Land counters with the fact that Yoda is merely a puppet, only to discover that he is now a digital creation.
