Garv Tomas
1371

General Garv Tomas was a male Human from Corellia. A colonel and a member of Alpha Blue under Admiral Hiram Drayson, he was later placed in charge of the Imperial Palace security by Chief of State Leia Organa Solo, a position he retained until the Battle of Coruscant.
