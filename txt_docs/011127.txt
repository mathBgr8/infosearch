Alicka
1880

Alicka was a Jedi Knight who served the Jedi Order during the final years of the Galactic Republic. The Knight was known for making blumfruit muffins and Jedi Padawan Anakin Skywalker was instantly able to identify some crumbs as belonging to one of Alicka's muffins while searching for the missing Jedi Wren Honoran on the planet Ragoon VI in 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi to 27 BBY.
