Lortan Fanatics
1790

thumb|right|250px|Lortans are repelled by [[Tunroth.]] The Lortan Fanatics, also called the Kopa Khan cultists in the Galactic Empire's propaganda, were a group of warlike beings from the planet of Lorta. They embarked on a crusade against the Tunroth, nearly exterminating that species until they were stopped by Imperial intervention in 6 BBY.
