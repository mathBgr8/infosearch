Aabatar

Aabatar was a male Mon Calamari smuggler who was once hired by another smuggler on the planet Dac to work on his YT-1300 freighter. Aabatar was paid two hundred credits per day for his services. Category:Males Category:Mon Calamari Category:Smugglers
