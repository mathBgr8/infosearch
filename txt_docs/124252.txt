|cover artist= |illustrator=Boris Vallejo |editor= |publisher=Random House |early release= |release date=April 1999 |intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Paperback |pages=48 |isbn=0375800026 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline=32 BBYThis book adapts part of which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 32 BBY |series=Jedi Readers |preceded by= |followed by= }} Dangers of the Core is a children's picture book inclusive of stickers written by Jim Thomas and illustrated by Boris Vallejo. It is a Jedi Readers Step 3 (Grades 2–3) young readers book that was published by Random House in April 1999. An exciting adventure for Obi-Wan Kenobi, Qui-Gon Jinn, and Jar Jar Binks as they journey through the dangerous core of the planet Naboo. Dangers of the Core is a re-cap of the underwater chase scene from Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace. The summary as described on the back of the book is as follows: "The planet Naboo is being invaded! Qui-Gon and Obi-Wan have to get to Theed and warn the Queen.Jar Jar Binks knows the best way, but it's underwater! Will they make it through the core without being gobbled up by undersea monsters? Read this colorful fully-illustrated beginner reader and find out!"
