|publisher=Wizards of the Coast |release date=September 3, 2004 |figures=60 |cards= |isbn=0-7869-3476-X |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by=Clone Strike }} Rebel Storm was the first release of Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Miniatures game. It featured 60 miniature figures, each with corresponding statistics cards, based on the timeline of the original trilogy. This initial set was released on September 3, 2004 and has been reissued several times since. Miniatures could be purchased with in booster packs of 7 random minis, or a Starter Set, which contained 8 random minis, as well as "Luke Skywalker, Rebel," "Darth Vader, Dark Jedi," a rulebook, and a map based on the Death Star.
