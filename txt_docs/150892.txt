Bandits of Ggy-ynt
1895

The Bandits of Ggy-ynt were a pirate organization that originated in the Ggy-ynt system of the Mid Rim's Esuain sector. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. the Ithorian pirate twins Denuab and Dorin&mdash;known as the Venithon Twins&mdash;had been members of the Bandits of Ggy-ynt. The Bandits of Ggy-ynt were first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. *Alliance Intelligence Reports *Pirates & Privateers Category:Pirate organizations
