Jabba Tape
1013

thumb|250px|The Jabba Tape talking to [[Gizman|Big Gizz, Spiker and Onoh.]] The Jabba Tape was an artificially intelligent holographic recording of Jabba Desilijic Tiure present on the Spirit of Jabba, secretly hidden inside Glass Mountain. Big Gizz and Spiker hijacked the ship and attempted to flee Tatooine but the Jabba Tape took over and threatened to kill them. They were forced back into the atmosphere and were nearly shot down over Mos Eisley by Gorga Desilijic Aarrpo's men aboard the Nemphas. The Jabba Tape refused to grant control without the proper authorization code, which was eventually entered by Onoh.The Jabba Tape *"The Jabba Tape" Category:Holographic technology
