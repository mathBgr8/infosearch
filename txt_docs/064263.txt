Refinery Six-Two-Two
842

Refinery Six-Two-Two was one of the fuel refineries on the planet Troska under control of the Kyber Royal Family. It was overseen by Prince Torino Kyber and was the site of a blockade against the Imperial garrison's raising of taxes. The refinery was later destroyed by Boba Fett. *Boba Fett: Overkill Category:Industrial facilities
