Star Wars: The Force Unleashed (webstrip)
2113

Star Wars: The Force Unleashed was a planned webstrip tie-in to The Force Unleashed series, focusing on Shaak Ti. Along with Rookies: Day Long Remembered, it was originally announced as being available to all on StarWars.com, not just Hyperspace subscribers. It was later revealed that it would only be available to Hyperspace subscribers, after all. Hyperspace was shut down in 2011. However, this webstrip was never published. As all other Star Wars: The Force Unleashed merchandise has long since been released, it is most likely that it was canceled. Category:Star Wars: The Force Unleashed Force Unleashed
