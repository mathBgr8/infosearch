Star Wars Adventures Magazine 3

Star Wars Adventures Magazine 3 was the third issue of the Star Wars Adventures magazine, published in December 2002 as part of the monthly installment of the Star Wars Adventures membership kit. The issue centered around Obi-Wan Kenobi. *"Jedi Through the Ages" *"He's the Wan!" *"Following the Code" *"You Make the Scene" *"Arena Attack!" *Pull-out Poster *"The Way of the Lightsaber" *"The Path of the Jedi" *"Test Your Jedi Knowledge" *"Facing the Followers of the Sith" *"Get Inside a Jedi Starfighter" *"Jedi Jokes" *Answer Key 03
