Bob Wiacek
3318

Bob Wiacek is a comic book inker who worked on the Marvel Star Wars series. He alternated inking chores with Gene Day after Terry Austin left the series in 1978 and continued regularly until the Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back adaptation, and he also co-created the characters of Remel Fud, Dafi and Slssk.
