dates the events of the Star Wars Adventures series to a year before the First Battle of Geonosis, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 22 BBY. |series=Star Wars Adventures (Scholastic) |preceded by=The Hostage Princess |followed by=The Shape-Shifter Strikes }} Star Wars Adventures 4: Jango Fett vs. the Razor Eaters was the fourth installment of Scholastic's Star Wars Adventures series. It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 4 in January 2003. Count Dooku has discovered a conspiracy that he wants stopped. He knows just the bounty hunter to stop it. Jango Fett. May the Force be with you.
