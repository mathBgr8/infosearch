Irith
3264

Irith was a terrestrial planet located in the Irith system, a part of the Hutt Space portion of the Slice. It was a motley stop on the Ootmian Pabol hyperlane between the Du Hutta and Nar Bo Sholla systems. An overcrowded, polluted world, Irith was the homeworld of the criminal Romort Raort, where, during his childhood, he was a bully who shaked down other children for their lunch money. The hyperwave signal interceptors at the Galactic Empire's Oktos Zone Imperial Outpost on the planet Toydaria could monitor lightspeed activity at Irith.Lords of Nal Hutta The Irith system was conquered by the military forces of the extra-galactic Yuuzhan Vong at some point between 26 ABY and 27 ABY during their conquest of the galaxy. Irith was first mentioned in the Dark Empire Sourcebook, Michael Allen Horne's 1993 sourcebook published by West End Games for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
