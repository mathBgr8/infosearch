Starslinger Ion Drive
1354

The Incom Corporation Starslinger Ion Drive was a commercially available ion drive. It cost only 20,000 Imperial Credits, plus a 500 credit fee, for those that would license the engine with the Empire, and weighed 12 metric tons. *Galaxy Guide 6: Tramp Freighters, Second Edition Category:Incom Corporation products Category:Ion engines
