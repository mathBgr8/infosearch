Xobome 6

Xobome 6 was a planet located within the Xobome system of the Colonies, between Commenor and Doldrums. Its high plains were blasted by freezing winds. Wraith Squadron captured the Night Caller over Xobome 6. *The Essential Atlas Category:Planets in the Colonies Category:Planets in the Slice
