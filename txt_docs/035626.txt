S-250 Chela-class starfighter
3653

The S-250 Chela-class starfighter was a craft used by the Republic fleet at many battles, including the Battle of Rakata Prime in 3956 BBY. Though a loser in the "Generation Tomorrow Starfighter" competition, it was still purchased in small numbers by the Republic. thumb|left|250px|A wing of S-250 Chela-class starfighter attacks a [[Centurion-class battlecruiser/Legends|Centurion-class battlecruiser]] These starfighters were armed with twin laser cannons and missile launchers, and flew alongside the more nimble Aurek-class tactical strikefighters in some battles.
