Geroon (species)
4435

The Geroon were a sentient species hailed from the epynomous Unknown Regions planet. They were captured and enslaved by the Vagaari species in 27 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. Around nine Geroons were killed by B1-Series and droideka battle droids under the directive of the Vagaari leader, the Miskara, and many of them imprisoned within zero-g plastic bubbles on the Vagaari fleet's hulls. They were freed by the Chiss species and their government, the Chiss Ascendancy, after the Chiss defeated the Vagaari. Ascendancy Admiral Ar'alani provided foodstuff to the Geroons aboard a transport. Around 18 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Coruscant Nights II: Street of Shadows to around 18 BBY. a Geroon was acquainted with the Cathar Sele,Coruscant Nights II: Street of Shadows and in 22 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Survivor's Quest to 22 ABY. several Vagaari&mdash;including Supreme Commander Estosh, General Bearsh and Purpsh&mdash;posed as Geroons to infiltrate the Chiss expedition to the remains of Outbound Flight colony starship. Many of the expedition's members did not notice the deception, but Ascendancy Aristocra Formbi knew that and let them accompany his party to begin a war against the slavers. The Vagaari were eventually defeated by Jedi Knight Mara Jade and Jedi Master Luke Skywalker.
