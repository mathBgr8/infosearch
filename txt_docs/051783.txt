Bwahl
3042

Bwahl was a one-eyed Hutt salesman/crimelord who operated on Torque. After the Tournament on Serenno, the Box came into the possession of Bwahl, and he made it mobile. Bwahl used the Box to travel to the strongholds of his rival kajidics, where he tortured his enemies for entertainment.The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Sometime before the Battle of Yavin, he had some ancient artifacts he wanted to buy, so he gave the currency to Han Solo and Chewbacca. They traveled to Mimban to deliver and receive the credits and artifacts to the salesman, a male Human named Jake. He was known to use kessum during interrogations.The Essential Guide to Alien Species At one point, Boba Fett's daughter Ailyn Vel took contracts from Bwahl as a bounty hunter. Bwahl owned at least one Mark X Executioner droid.
