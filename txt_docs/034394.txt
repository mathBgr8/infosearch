Destroyer (droid)
1663

The Destroyer was the feared killer droid of Governor Kugg on the planet Dodz. Kugg used the droid as an enforcer to his heavy tax increases on the merchants of the planet Dodz. When the young resident of Dodz, Jost Ellon stood up for the people with the aid of R2-D2 & C-3PO and his outdated Ranger X-1 war droid, the Destroyer was destroyed in a battle with the Ranger X-1. The speech pattern of the Destroyer was basically a snarling growl and nothing more.
