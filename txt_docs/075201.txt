Best Chance (shuttle)
953

Best Chance was a New Republic shuttle in operation during the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 25 ABY, it ferried a commando team of six Noghri and three Jedi: Jacen Solo, Corran Horn, and Ganner Rhysode, hidden within the decoy freighter Lost Hope, from Ralroost to the surface of Garqi. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shuttles Category:Starships of the New Republic
