Ven (Peace Brigade)
2373

Ven was a member of the Peace Brigade, a group of galactic citizens that cooperated with the extra-galactic Yuuzhan Vong invaders during the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 25 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial to 25 ABY. Ven served as Reck Desh's bodyguard alongside a man named Wotson when Desh, the Peace Brigade's leader, traveled to Kuat City on the planet of Kuat. There, the trio entered the Nebula Orchid diner to meet an informant, secretly New Republic Senator Viqi Shesh.The New Essential Guide to Characters While Shesh told Desh of a captured Yuuzhan Vong priestess, Ven and Wotson sat at a nearby table and kept watch. Ven, Desh, and Wotson pursued the informant into the streets after the meeting concluded, but lost sight of the person.
