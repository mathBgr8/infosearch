Bnar VII
3345

Bnar VII was an astronomical object located in the Bnar system, a part of the Braxant sector in the New Territories portion of the Outer Rim Territories. The decades-long career of the Noehon slaver Tir'uh saw her operate within an area of the galaxy spanning from Bnar VII, in the northern section of the galaxy, to Kessel, a planet in the galaxy's eastern portion.The Essential Atlas In 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology. a Rebel Alliance Task Force on Alliance Security SecuriDex datafile on the Mytaranor Slaving Council, of which Tir'uh was a member, noted the possibility that the Noehon was familiar with every location between Bnar VII and Kessel that was related to the slaving business. Bnar VII was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
