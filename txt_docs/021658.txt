|locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Cup of Limmie }} Team Yag'Dhul was a limmie team that played for the planet Yag'Dhul in the Galactic Cup of Limmie. On the date 13:3:14, it was reported on an issue of the CoCo District Edition of HoloNet News that they they humiliated the planet Kinyen's team in the tournament's opening round when they won with two unanswered scores by Onila Richblum. Team Yag'Dhul was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, a March 14, 2002 in-universe issue of the HoloNet News written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published on the HoloNet News website.
