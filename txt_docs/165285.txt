Gerrare household
1032

The Gerrare household was a simple single-story white dwelling owned by Shayne Gerrare. It was located on the west side of Gadrin, with only a hundred meters of grass separating the rear of the house from the Cularin jungles. Small animals would occasionally come out from the jungle to graze on the lawn. The home was an understated building with a very expensive security system. Part of the security system was a booby-trap that, when set, would discharge a powerful electric shock if three people stood in the central hallway. The home had two rooms on either side of the hallway&mdash;a bedroom, a kitchen, a study, and a comm center. A battle was fought on the household's front lawn between members of the Cularin resistance and the Metatheran Cartel, which was trying to capture Gerrare. This left scorch marks on the front door.
