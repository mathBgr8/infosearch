Tree-drum music
2780

Tree-drum music was a form of music. The Wookiee species played tree-drum music to celebrate a holiday called Life Day as early as 1,500,000 BBY. Between 16 ABY and 17 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Before the Storm between 16 ABY and 17 ABY. during the Black Fleet Crisis, Jedi Master Luke Skywalker mentioned to Han Solo, general of the New Republic, that the Wookiee Chewbacca played tree-drum music loudly as part of a metaphor about his duties as a Jedi. Tree-drum music was first mentioned in 1996's Before the Storm, the first novel of Michael P.
