Aurek-Bacta
868

Aurek-Bacta was the designation of a Republic rally point in Murkhana City during the Battle of Murkhana. At this point, Clone Commander Salvo ordered Climber and Ion Team to set an ambush for Roan Shryne at the rally point. Climber, disbelieving Order 66, instead ambushed Salvo and incapacitated him while Shryne escaped.Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader *Dark Lord: The Rise of Darth Vader Category:Murkhana locations
