Calipsa Run
1282

The Calipsa Run was a minor hyperspace route located in the Tapani sector, that because of very few debris in the area made travel on this route very fast. The route connected the Calipsa Province and the Mecetti Province with the Procopian Shipping Lane at the Estaph system. Some of the notable worlds on this route were Calipsa and Grella, as well as the New Javis system, Lorenz system and Nella system. The Calipsa Run was neglected by many bulk cargo shippers until House Cadriaan issued stiff tariffs against House Calipsa. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector hyperlanes
