Llats Ward centered his life around warfare, and committed the campaigns of renowned leaders in Mandalorian military history to memory. Ward drew on these historical events in his teachings as a combat skill instructor to the clone troopers. He wore green armor over a gray jumpsuit, with yellow knee and shoulder pads and a large Mythosaur skull painted across the chest. thumb|left|Llats Ward in full armor Llats Ward was created for an exclusive Entertainment Earth 14 action figure pack.
