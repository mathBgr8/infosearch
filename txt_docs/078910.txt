Scythe 1
1839

Scythe 1 was a TIE/LN starfighter of Scythe Squadron during the Battle of Endor. Flown by Major Mianda, Scythe 1 featured the same upgraded Sienar Fleet Systems P-w702 ion maneuvering jets as the other fighters in its unit, which increased performance in tight quarters. During the battle it pursued Rebel starfighters on Death Star II. Category:TIE/ln starfighters
