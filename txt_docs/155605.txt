Kuhqo
1171

Kuhqo was a Yuuzhan Vong shaper who became a Shamed One. He joined the Jeedai heresy and used a genetic splicer to access an Executor's qahsa, searching for planets that might be the Prophet's living world. His search brought him and a number of other Heretics, including Hul Qat, to Dagobah.
