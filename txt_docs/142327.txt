In the Political Pit
2183

|media type=HoloNet broadcast |pages= |published in= |genre=Politics }} In the Political Pit was a popular HoloNet political debate show which aired in the years prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars. It featured interviews with political figures. On 13:4:11, the show was broadcast on Feed CWN115 at 1935, featuring a panel consisting of RCLU Chairbeing Dotswa Ween, actor Reridan Reksiss, Representative Jar Jar Binks, and a recording artist named Gaeriala Boosin. In the Political Pit was first mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #51, an issue of the HoloNet News feed that was published on April 11, 2002. The section that referenced the show, entitled "What to Scan," was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:News and entertainment programs
