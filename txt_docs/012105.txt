Cal-i-Vaun
2015

Cal-i-Vaun was a male Jedi Knight serving the Jedi Order during the final years of the Galactic Republic. Part of the Temple Security Force, Cal-i-Vaun was stationed at a checkpoint at one of the Coruscant Jedi Temple's many entrances. A male Force-sensitive, Cal-i-Vaun was a member of the Jedi Order. Eventually, Cal-i-Vaun was elevated to the rank of Jedi Knight by the Jedi High Council. In 41 BBY, Cal-i-Vaun was manning a checkpoint in the Temple's docking complex.
