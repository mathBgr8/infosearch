Sage Master
1822

A Sage Master was a variation of Jedi Master or Jedi sage that was established by the Jedi Order. These Jedi were some of the most powerful Force users the Jedi Order had ever seen, and elitists among other Sages. There have only been two known Sage Masters&mdash;statues of whom were placed upon the Processional Way's ceremonial staircase that led to the Jedi Temple, alongside two Warrior Master statues&mdash;but the rank within the Order had been retired until 19 BBY when the Order was destroyed. *The High Republic Adventures – Echoes of Fear 1 *Crimson Reign 2 *Star Wars: Galaxy of Heroes *Star Wars: Complete Locations Category:Jedi Masters Category:Jedi ranks
