|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=0 ABYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars (2013) |issue=10 }} Star Wars 10 is the tenth issue in the Star Wars comic book series. The series takes place between Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope and Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. This issue was released on October 9, 2013. Luke Skywalker and Wedge Antilles have infiltrated an Imperial Destroyer. But now nine thousand stormtroopers are searching for them, and Darth Vader is aware of their presence. Completing their mission and escaping with their lives may be an either/or proposition! Meanwhile, the Rebel fleet prepares for an Imperial assault!
