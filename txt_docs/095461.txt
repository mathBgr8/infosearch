Micah (Cathar)
3910

Micah was the Cathar adoptive child of Nat Skywalker, then going by the alias "Bantha" Rawk, and Droo, and the step-sibling of Ahnah and Skeeto, who lived during the time of the Second Imperial Civil War. Micah had cybernetic legs, built by Nat himself. Micah was once enslaved until he was freed from his plight by "Bantha Rawk", also known as Nat Skywalker. However, Micah's damaged legs could not be saved; Nat's wife Droo healed the stricken Cathar to a certain extent, but his original legs had to be replaced by cybernetic ones. Micah fought alongside his family in defending the Nest from Black Sun invaders. While piloting a speeder, he tricked one swoop into a wall with impressive piloting skills. His family was also assisted by the Mynock, piloted by Cade Skywalker, Jariah Syn and Deliah Blue. Four days later, Micah and his family were enjoying an outdoor fun session. His family later watched the holonews about the Massacre on Mon Calamari, and it was then that Cade revealed that he was a Skywalker. Afterward, Micah and his family, except his father, moved to Kiffu. Jan Duursema once referred to Micah as "Micah Skywalker" on TheForce.net message boards.
