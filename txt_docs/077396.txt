Jake Harthan
1431

Jake Harthan was a male Human from the Tion Hegemony, the son of Counselor Harthan. When they were sent to Tahlboor around 5.5 BBY, he became aware of the sky cannon and decided to seize it by provoking the Troobs and Hobors into a genocidal war through the murder of Ket Krax and the blaming of Larka Nimondro. However, the plot was foiled by C-3PO just before the two peoples were to go to war.
