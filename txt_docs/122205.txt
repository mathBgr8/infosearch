|editor= |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=June 21, 2000 |pages=64 |upc= |timeline= |series= |issue= }} The Dark Horse Presents Annual 2000 is a one-shot comic published by Dark Horse Comics on June 21, 2000. The issue included the story "Aurra's Song," featuring the bounty hunter Aurra Sing. What do a Jedi-hating bounty hunter, a vampire-slaying college student, an Amazon warrior, a teenage spy, and a first-time super-hero have in common? They're all females, and they all kick ass! DHP Annual 2000 celebrates girl power with five exciting new stories featuring some of the most fascinating femme fatales in comics: Star Wars Aurra Sing, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Xena Warrior Princess Gabrielle, SpyBoy Bombshell, and, from Ghost, Silhouette!
