Kirmay clan
2241

The Kirmay clan was a group of individuals who owned a farm near the city of Imbraani on the Mid Rim &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Qiilura system planet Qiilura by 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, which includes the mention of the Kirmay clan, in 22 BBY. The clan lost the property when Ghez Hokan, a Mandalorian serving in the Confederacy of Independent Systems, sold the group to Trandoshan enslavers. The farm was later repurposed as a staging area for fifty to sixty of Hokan's mercenaries, as estimated by a Qiiluran villager. The Kirmay clan was mentioned in the 2004 novel Hard Contact, the first novel in the Republic Commando series written by Karen Traviss. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Clans
