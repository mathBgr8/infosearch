D'skar
2548

D'skar were simple, yet durable daggers constructed by Ekkar Armaments on Coyn and easily the most common weapons to be seen in the possession of a Coynite. The blade was named after D'Skar, the innermost planet of the Coyn system. Every d'skar was uniquely crafted and therefore no two were exactly alike, ranging from as small as thirty centimeters in length to upwards of half a meter. Ekkar Arms was known for their excellent craftsmanship, and as such, when properly cared for, one of these daggers could remain durable and usable for centuries.
