MD-5
2194

MD-5, also called Emdee Five, was an MD-5 medical droid serving the self-appointed Galactic Emperor Trioculus in 5 ABY. MD-5 attended Trioculus at every function and was present during the latter's public address at Kessendra Stadium on Kessel, as well as the whaladon-hunting submarine of Captain Dunwell. Trioculus maintained a close relationship with MD-5, similar to the relationship shared between Luke Skywalker and the droid team of C-3PO and R2-D2.
