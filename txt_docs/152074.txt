Nave Vengaris
1763

Nave Vengaris was a Human female who served as the governess of Aquilaris during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. She welcomed the income and boosted tourism which accompanied podracing and granted the use of Floating City, the capital of the planet, as part of the race course.
