|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Voice actor |companies= |website= }} }} Tom Kane (born April 15, 1962) is a retired American voice actor who had worked on many Star Wars productions, including films, television, and video games. He often served as a voice double for Admiral Ackbar, C-3PO, and Yoda, previously voiced by Erik Bauersfeld, Anthony Daniels, and Frank Oz in the films. Most notably, he had multiple recurring roles in the Star Wars: The Clone Wars television series (2008&ndash;2020), not only voicing Yoda but also as the show's newsreel narrator and Admiral Yularen. On December 30, 2020, Kane's daughter, Sam disclosed that her father had suffered a left-sided stroke in October that left him with right-sided weakness and damage to the speech center of his brain. Kane has been undergoing speech, occupational, and physical therapy as treatment for his injuries.
