Treidum
1717

Treidum was a freighter owner who was a subscriber to, and commenter on, Cynabar's Droid Datalog. In 1 BBY, Treidum ran a load of Roche Siak Protocol Droids to the planet Derilyn in the Elrood sector. There, the smuggler was jumped by pirates, most likely the Khuiumin Survivors who Platt Okeefe later warned were operating in the area. Treidum survived the pirate attack, and on returning to Berchest, posted a warning to other smugglers about the area. The warning was included in the next version of Cynabar's Droid Datalog.Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids Several months later, Treidum commented on the entry for the WED15-D3, stating that they had retrofitted their Treadwell droid with a repair database and six more manipulator arms.
