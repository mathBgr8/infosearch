She worked as a social worker and eventually met New Republic pilot Gavin Darklighter. The two married a few years after the Battle of Bilbringi (around 12 ABY). Sera and Gavin had five children together. First they adopted two boys who were orphans of the campaign against Grand Admiral Thrawn. Sera and Gavin also had three biological children of their own.
