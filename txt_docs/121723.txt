Alpha Squad (Zann)
964

Alpha Squad was a scout squad for the Zann Consortium. Their final mission was to scout the area of Felucia while Tyber Zann attempted to steal a Sith holocron from Jabba the Hutt. They wandered through several Felucian spore plants and died several seconds later. *Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption Category:Military units Category:Zann Consortium organizations
