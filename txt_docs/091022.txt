Glarc Leapfar
1145

Glarc Leapfar was a Nosaurian poacher of Clan Leapfar from New Plympto. He led a gang of poachers who collected rikknit ovum sacs. However, he respected nature and was always careful not to disturb the balance of the ecosystem or harm the rikknit. Leapfar was a skilled and strong tree-fighter, and few tried to compete with him. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Hunters Category:Males Category:Nosaurians
