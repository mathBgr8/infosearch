|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=232 |isbn=1569718369 |isbn2= |timeline=Various |series=Star Wars Tales |issues=4 |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars Tales Volume 3 is the third trade paperback collection of the Star Wars Tales comic book series. Volume 3 collects Star Wars Tales issues 9-12. It was released on February 26, 2003. Collecting issues nine through twelve of the quarterly anthology smash sensation! Star Wars Tales Volume 3 features the thrilling Vader versus Maul story "Resurrection" as well as comics sensation Garth Ennis' take on the life of a stormtrooper in the riviting [sic] "Trooper". Tales has explored every corner of the Star Wars galaxy and kept readers coming back for more!
