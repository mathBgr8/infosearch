Shaala Doneeta
2047

Shaala Doneeta was a female Rutian Twi'lek bureaucrat who was the personal aide of Count Dooku. Doneeta was a personal aide of Count Dooku who wore Sith-like tattoos on her lekku. She accompanied him when he made his Fete Day speech. Shaala Doneeta was based on a Force adept originally created by Bryan K. Borgman for a Star Wars RPG home game in early 2000.
