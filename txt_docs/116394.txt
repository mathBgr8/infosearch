ATK 592
1889

ATK 592 was a Lambda-class T-4a shuttle in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. When Biggs Darklighter's X-wing, carrying schematics for the Death Star, broke down, ATK 592 was one of three shuttles sent, under Assault Gunboat escort, to capture the fighter. The shuttle was destroyed by Rebel R-22 Spearheads, sent to protect the disabled X-wing until a repair team arrived. *Star Wars: X-Wing *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Lambda-class T-4a shuttles Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
