Lexas systems

The Lexas systems were a group of star systemsAlliance Intelligence Reports that shared its name with the Lexas Prime system, a part of the Outer Rim Territories' Kessel sector. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. several petty crimelords operated in the Lexas systems. The Krish smuggler Karalan worked for the crimelords but became progressively more indebted to them. After Karalan lost his starship and was almost killed by a bounty hunter, the Krish instead became a member of the Mytaranor Slaving Council.
