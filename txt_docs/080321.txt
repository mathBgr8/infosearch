Naboo Embassy
2591

}} The Naboo Embassy was the permanent representation of the Royal House of Naboo to the Galactic Republic, and was located on on Coruscant. Occupied by the delegation from Naboo following the Chommell sector's acceptance into the Galactic Republic circa 800 BBY, the Embassy served as a place for all Naboo immigrants living on Coruscant to visit and speak with their representatives. The headquarters of all Naboo activity on Coruscant, the building was located just southwest of the Senate Building and Monument Plaza.
