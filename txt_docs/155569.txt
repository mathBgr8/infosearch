Prophet's Message
547

The Prophet's Message was the gospel of the Jeedai heresy preached by Yu'shaa the Prophet. The Message drew many Yuuzhan Vong, especially in castes other than the Shamed Ones, to the heresy. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Religious codes and documents Category:True Way
