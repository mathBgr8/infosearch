Under the alias Sonopo Bomoor, Suppoon committed many atrocities, such as attacking the homeworld of the man who grew up to become Kosh Kurp, one of the Empire's foremost explosives experts. When Jabba wanted to get rid of Suppoon, he merely revealed to Kurp that Suppoon was Sonopo Bomoor, and Kurp turned on Suppoon.
