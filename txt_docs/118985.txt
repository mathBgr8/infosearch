Roqoo Depot
2261

Roqoo Depot was a refueling station and supply base located in the Hapes Cluster. It was here that Jedi Masters Luke and Mara Jade Skywalker dueled the Sith Lady Lumiya and her apprentice, Alema Rar. Later, it was the place where the Hapans held Ship after they found it abandoned. Alema Rar returned here for the ship, causing much turmoil after a freighter captain had made the mistake of remarking on her deformities.
