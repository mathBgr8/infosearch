Hearing enhancer
476

A hearing enhancer is a small device that can be inserted in the ear to help those who are partially deaf. It is possible to pick up the signal from a hearing enhancer and use it as a long range listening device. This was demonstrated during the Galactic City spaceport hostage siege. Kal Skirata had a hearing enhancer but usually did not use it. Category:Audio sensors
