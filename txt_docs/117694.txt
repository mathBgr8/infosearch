Tarp-Hard-Xenon was a sector in a computer stolen by Bothan spies. It was the location of schematics for the Death Star. Tarp-Hard-Xenon is a reference to THX 1138. Xenon is a real world element. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel Category:Data storage and processing
