Nidôsh
630

A nidôsh was the Korunnal word for "clan-child," which could also be translated as orphan. If a child's parents were killed before their naming day, the child would become a nidôsh, and take the name of his ghôsh as his own. Mace Windu and Nick Rostu were both nidôsh. *Shatterpoint *Shatterpoint abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Korunnai culture
