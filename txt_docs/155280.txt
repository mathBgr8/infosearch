Wizard Entertainment
1686

Wizard Entertainment, previously Gareb Shamus Enterprises, Inc, was the publisher of WIZARD Magazine, InQuest Gamer, ToyFare: The Toy Magazine and the distributor of both X-Wing Rogue Squadron ½ and Episode I: The Phantom Menace ½. Category:Publishing companies (real-world)
