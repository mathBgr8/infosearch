Gamma Base
2708

Gamma Base was the name of a Rebel base on the planet Hoth. The base was populated by a small group of Rebels around the time of the Battle of Tatooine. Rookie One relocated there after the Anchorhead Base was destroyed during that battle in the year 0 BBY. It was discovered by Imperial Viper probe droids and subsequently attacked by Imperial forces which included All Terrain Armored Transports. Rookie One managed to shoot one walker with his snowspeeder and made his way to the hangar and escape along with the rest of the Rebel fleet as the base was overrun by Stormtroopers and Snowtroopers. thumb *Gamma Base commander *Gamma Base communications officer *Rookie One *Ru Murleen (possibly) Gamma Base was created for the 1993 LucasArts video game Star Wars: Rebel Assault.
