The Granicus system included the gas giant Granicus. The Granicus system was mentioned in the Expanded Universe backstory devised by George Lucas in 1977 for C-3PO, but was not mentioned in any licensed products, making its status as yet non canonical. *The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film Category:Star systems/Unlicensed
