Quad-Lo
1961

Quad-Lo was a short, green alien male slaver who frequented the space station Bazarre during the era of the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, Quad-Lo attempted to purchase the Rebel leader Lando Calrissian after he arrived on Bazarre to carry out a weapons deal, assuming him to be someone's property&mdash;however, Orion Ferret, the station's owner, swiftly corrected Quad-Lo and sent him away.
