The knight of Galadoorn disgraced himself by having an illicit love affair with a princess. He later took up a sword to fight Queen Bavmorda alongside his childhood friend Airk Thaughbaer. Madmartigan would vanish a year later when the Cataclysm destroyed Tir Asleen.
