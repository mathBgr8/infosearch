It was designed and built by Hoersch-Kessel Drive, Inc. while the company was owned by Duros. The ship was popularized due to its use by Arca Jeth and his own Delaya-Class courier, known as SunGem. Their fame led to other Jedi acquiring the same class of ship, and eventually for Hoersch-Kessel Drive to create the Vanya-Class Courier based on its design and purpose for use by the Jedi Order. thumb|left|180px|[[SunGem schematics.]] The Delaya-class courier was designed to match a starfighter for speed and maneuverability. Unlike Corellia StarDrive's Coruscant-class Heavy Courier, most of the ship was habitable. The complex automatic systems integrated into the craft allowed it to be operated by a single person. This aerodynamic starship had an insectoid appearance and featured 21 HKD ion engines. These engines were set in blocks of three on servo-controlled mountings to negate the need for maneuvering thrusters. The courier was also lightly armed with only 2 rotating laser cannons and 1 proton torpedo launcher. For further agility, a computer system controlled multiple layers of extendable vanes and aerofoils .
