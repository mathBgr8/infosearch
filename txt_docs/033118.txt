Mooth
1578

Mooth was a trader who lived on the forest moon of Endor. He regularly dealt with the Ewoks of Bright Tree Village for their annual trading expedition. He also had masterful salesmanship skill which was displayed when he sold a rock pendant to Wicket Wystri Warrick for his Monmon seeds, unaware it was actually the missing tooth of the Rock Wizard Mooth was voiced by John Stocker in the first season of Ewoks. *Droids\Ewoks *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded *Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Businesspeople Category:Endorians Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males
