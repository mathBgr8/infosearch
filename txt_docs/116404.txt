Tantive I
2320

The Tantive I was a CR90 corvette used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. When Biggs Darklighter's X-wing broke down while carrying the recently intercepted schematics for the Death Star, the Tantive I, along with the Tantive II and the Tantive III, was sent to escort Lambda-class T-4a shuttles which would restore the X-wing's power generator. It arrived from hyperspace to find Rebel R-22 Spearheads fighting Imperial Assault Gunboats which were attacking the X-wing.
