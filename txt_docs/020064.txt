Pi-Lippa was a kind individual who went out of her way to teach Skywalker technical skills and promised to grant her freedom. Unfortunately for Skywalker, Pi-Lippa died unexpectedly before fulfilling her promise, devastating the Human. The inheritors of Pi-Lippa's estate chose not to honor her commitment to Skywalker's freedom, and the Human was sold to a relative of Pi-Lippa, who did not want to grant Skywalker's freedom.The Rise and Fall of Darth Vader The skills that Pi-Lippa taught Skywalker increased her value to her later enslavers, and she continued to apply them in her daily life on the planetStar Wars: Episode I: The Visual Dictionary Tatooine. Skywalker was eventually freed after being purchased by the moisture farmer Cliegg Lars, who also married her.
