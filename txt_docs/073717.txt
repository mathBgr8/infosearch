Rock-One
902

Rock-One was the code name given to a Yuuzhan Vong miid ro'ik cruiser by Rogue Squadron. Rock-One entered the Dubrillion system shortly after the Battle of Helska IV, where it was used as a base to launch coralskipper probes against the surface of the world. Later Rock-One was targeted by Rogue Squadron during the evacuation of Dubrillion; during the battle, it used interdiction fields to prevent some of the convoy from heading to Agamar, necessitating their rerouting to Dantooine. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught Category:Interdictor starships Category:Miid ro'ik
