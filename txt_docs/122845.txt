Missile droid
1770

Missile droids were flimsy battle droids used in large numbers by the Galactic Empire. They were used in 3 ABY during the Battle of Dubrillion, where they attempted to hinder Rebel Alliance pilot Wedge Antilles. They spewed a seemingly infinite number of concussion missiles. These droids flew or hovered in the air, often at the same level as the cockpit of an enemy vehicle.
