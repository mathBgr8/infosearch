Imperial Guard may refer to: *Emperor's Royal Guard, a guard that protected the Galactic Emperor Sheev Palpatine *Imperial royal guard, a group of clone commandos *Imperial Praetorian Guard, a guard used by Gideon's Imperial remnant *Coruscant Guard, a guard stationed on the planet Coruscant *Coruscant Guard: a clone trooper/stormtrooper police force on Coruscant called the "Imperial Guard" during the Galactic Empire *Emperor's Royal Guard: also called the "Imperial Guard" or "Imperial Royal Guard" they were the elite bodyguards of Emperor Palpatine *Imperial Guard: the elite guard of the Sith Emperor who ruled the resurrected Sith Empire *New Imperial Guard: the elite guard of the short-lived New Empire under Darth Malgus
