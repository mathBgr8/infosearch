AargonarStar Wars: The Comics Companion |species=Kajain'sa'Nikto |gender=Male |pronouns=new |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=*Jedi Order *Galactic Republic |masters=A'Sharad Hett |apprentices= }} Bhat Jul was a male Kajain'sa'Nikto who served the Jedi Order as a Jedi Commander during the Clone Wars. The Nikto was apprenticed to Jedi Knight A'Sharad Hett at the time. Unfortunately, during an early deployment to the planet Aargonar, Bhat Jul spent his last moments in Anakin Skywalker's arms as he suffered from explosion-induced injuries. The young Jedi was left to be covered in the sand to the bidding of his Master, Hett, who was a Tusken Raider who believed the act would honor the fallen Nikto's life. Sensitive to the Force, Bhat Jul was chosen as a Padawan learner by A'Sharad Hett.
