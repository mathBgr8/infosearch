Tatooine H'Kak bean tea
625

H'Kak bean tea was a fragrant orange-colored tea derived from Tatooine H'Kak beans. It was ingested for its relaxing and detoxifying effects. The beverage was known to be served at the Cleft Head wake-up house during the Clone Wars. *The Cestus Deception *The Cestus Deception abridged audiobook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tatooinian food Category:Teas
