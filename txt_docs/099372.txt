Aran (disambiguation)
649

Aran could refer to several individuals: *Aran, a secret informant for the Rebel Alliance. *Aran Cho, a captain in the Imperial Navy. *Aran Nomante, one of many computer-controlled Rebels in the battlefront games. *Aran Starfire, founder of the Gellefon Freedom Fighters.
