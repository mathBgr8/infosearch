|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=19 BBY (36 months ABG)The New Essential Chronology |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=76 }} Republic 76 is the seventy-sixth issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on June 15, 2005 by Dark Horse Comics. Two Jedi heroes have fallen and the Republic forces on Saleucami are on the verge of being overrun by the Separatist's Morgukai clones. As the Sith move toward their final revenge, it seems only one Jedi can help piece together a plan that might yet give the Republic a chance for victory. Unfortunately, that Jedi is Quinlan Vos &mdash; and his plan may be the final betrayal of all the Republic forces on Saleucami!
