O-Bin
1431

O-Bin, also known as Bin,The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia was a Teaching Guide at the Learning Circle on the planet of Kegan during the rule of V-Tan and O-Vieve. She was strict with her students, especially Obi-Wan Kenobi and Siri Tachi. O-Bin habitually wore her hair pulled in a severe style back behind her ears, along with a customary brown tunic and black trousers. *Jedi Apprentice: The Fight for Truth *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Educators Category:Females Category:Keganites
