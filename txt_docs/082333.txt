Hhen
2033

Hhen was a pilot who served in the New Republic and flew during the Yuuzhan Vong War. While attempting to rescue droids from an angry mob on Ando, Hhen was killed when an Aqualish officer blew up the ship in order to trap Jedi Knight Dorsk 82 and appease the Yuuzhan Vong.
