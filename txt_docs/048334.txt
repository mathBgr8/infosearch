Reiber Manufacturing

|locations= |role=Droid manufacturer |products=Assassin droid matrixes and mission specific components |founding= |dissolved=0 ABY (absorbed into Imperial Droid Corporation)Pirates & Privateers |affiliation= }} Reiber Manufacturing was a droid manufacturer based on the planet Jeyell. They also produced black market assassin droid matrixes and mission specific components. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, Reiber Manufacturing was nationalized by the Galactic Empire. Along with Bansche Tech, SGI Systems, Inc., and Sencil Corporation, Reiber Manufacturing was reformed into the Imperial Droid Corporation. *Pirates & Privateers *The Far Orbit Project Category:Droid manufacturers
