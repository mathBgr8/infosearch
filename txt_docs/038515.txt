Booty Full
2536

|navy= |fleet= |owners= |captains=Phan Riizolo |crewmembers= |registry= |aliases= }} The Booty Full was a pirate ship under the command of Phan Riizolo, who worked with the Invids. The Invids were a pirate organization led by Leonia Tavira and in opposition to the New Republic. The Booty Full was a modified bulk cruiser which carried a complement of eighteen Clutches attached to its outer hull. During the Battle of Alakatha in 11 ABY, it was captured by Rogue Squadron above the planet of Alakatha as it was attempting to raid the Glitterstar, a luxury liner. While all of its fighters were destroyed in the battle, the ship itself was captured intact. After its capture, the Booty Full captain provided the New Republic Intelligence Service with intelligence on Leonia Tavira. The Booty Full was created for I, Jedi, which was written by Michael A.
