Aryzah was caught in the gravity well of the Tyus Cluster black holes. Although she was killed, her spirit remained distinct, and later rescued the gambler Kinnin Vo-Shay from the Cluster. Vo-Shay had no interest in becoming a Jedi, but Aryzah continued counseling him. Eventually, the two traveled to Morado, where Vo-Shay met Nyo, a young man who wanted to buy a lightsaber despite the fact that they were illegal under Imperial law. After a series of events, Vo-Shay was able to help Nyo acquire the lightsaber. He then introduced Nyo to Aryzah, who agreed to train him as a Jedi.
