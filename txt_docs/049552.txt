Ancient Houses
1356

The Ancient Houses, also known as the Senex Houses,Cloak of Deception were a narrow group of old-established Human bloodlines controlling planetary fiefs and interstellar lordships. The term could sometimes be extended to include other families of comparable antiquity and authority&mdash;for example, House Organa of Alderaan&mdash;but the original meaning of the term indicated the dominant lineages of the Senex-Juvex sectors, such as House Vandron and House Elegin.Children of the Jedi *Cloak of Deception *Children of the Jedi *Pirates & Privateers *Star Wars Chronicles: The Prequels *The Essential Atlas Category:Noble houses
