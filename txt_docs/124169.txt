|interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Confederacy of Independent Systems **Techno Union *Galactic Empire }} The Capitol, or capitol building, was the main government building in Lessu City, the capital city of Ryloth. Located in the Octagon district, it was the headquarters for the Ryloth Head Clan.Star Wars: Dawn of Rebellion: The Visual Guide When the Separatists occupied Ryloth during the Clone Wars, Emir Wat Tambor used the building as his headquarters. Later, after the formation of the Galactic Empire, the building became the Empire's planetary headquarters.
