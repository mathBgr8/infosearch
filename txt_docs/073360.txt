Estalle Seaport
813

Estalle Seaport was located in the Commercial District of Estalle Island and was the primary supply link with the rest of the planet Procopia in the Tapani sector. The majority of the consumables and commercial products sold on Estalle Island came through Estalle Harbor. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Buildings Category:Procopia locations
