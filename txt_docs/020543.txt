Arda III
2542

Arda III was an industrial world located in the Arda system, a part of the Back Reach area of the Gordian Reach sector within the Trans-Hydian portion of the Outer Rim Territories. The hyperlane known as the Ardan Cross connected it to the Feldwes and Kalishik systems, and another hyperlane linked it to the Capella system.
