B4J4 security droid
2063

The B4J4 security droid was a Blujay-developed security droid developed for the corporate sphere. It was armed with a pair of heavy blasters with a stun setting, a snare pistol or two grapple-cable shooters firing ShrinCord. Its repulsorlift generator enabled it to fly up stairs and navigate turbolift shafts. It had a single eye, and while it had keen hearing, it possessed no olfactory sensors capable of following scents. While capable of following a clear trail of obvious signs such as blaster scorches, they were otherwise not very effective trackers. The B4J4 security droids could be found guarding the headquarters of some of the most prestigious galactic corporations; ten of these droids were used during non-work hours at Santhe/Sienar Headquarters Building to conduct random patrols.
