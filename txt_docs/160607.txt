Comet Casino
620

The Comet Casino was a run-down casino that was located on the Smugglers' Moon, Nar Shaddaa. It could be found within the Corellian sector of the lunar ecumenopolis. In 5 BBY Han Solo visited the place when he searched for his old friend Mako Spince on the Smugglers' Moon. *The Hutt Gambit Category:Casinos Category:Corellian Sector locations
