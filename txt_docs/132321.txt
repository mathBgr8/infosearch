Smekt
919

Smekt was a female Noghri who worked as a clerk for Hyperspeed Express on Wayland. Dal Konur used a Force mind trick on her, and impersonating the Jedi Knight Streen, convinced the Noghri to help him in his goals of acquiring cargo and a lightsaber.
