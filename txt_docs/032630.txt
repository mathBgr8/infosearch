Agridorn
1807

Agridorn was a planet in the Agridorn system of the Colonies region. During the latter days of the Galactic Republic, Agridorn was represented in the Senate by Rhya Taloon, who pledge her world's support to the Republic at the start of the Clone Wars. When the Republic was transformed into the Galactic Empire, Senator Taloon fled into the depth of Coruscant to avoid assassination.
