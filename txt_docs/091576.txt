VTR-LX
2460

VTR-LX was an Imperial Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser that served as a prisoner transport during the Galactic Civil War. In addition to transporting prisoners of war captured during the Battle of Hoth, the ship was used as bait by the Empire in their bid to place an Imperial operative high up in the Rebel Alliance chain of command. She was first seen near Calast by Alliance Y-wings from the Defiance Task Force but soon escaped near an Imperial penal facility, Bundil II.
