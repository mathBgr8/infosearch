|species= |language= |established= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |distinctions= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} The Delephrans were a socio-cultural group that originated in the Delephr system of the Outer Rim Territories' Carrion sector. They maintained the Delephran militia and engaged in commerce shipping that was preyed on for several years by pirates based in the Vexta Belt of the Vexta system. Eventually, at some point no later than 1 BBY,Alliance Intelligence Reports, which is set during Andrephan Stormcaller's service with the Rebel Alliance, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. In addition, establishes that Stormcaller served with the Delephran militia no less than three years before joining the Alliance. the Delephran militia commissioned the decorated Galactic Republic soldier Andrephan Stormcaller to train its commanders, leading to Stormcaller heading the operation known as the Piracy Scouring against the Vexta Belt pirates. At some point after the establishment of the Galactic Empire in 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology the Imperial Military absorbed the Delephran forces, with all personnel of the latter facing mandatory service in the former and Stormcaller going to serve at the Imperial Academy. The Delephr system fell within the borders of the Empire's Pentastar Alignment splinter faction between 4 ABY and 12 ABY and was a part of the Imperial Remnant between 18 ABY and 35 ABY. The Delephrans were introduced in "Blasters for Hire," a Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game source article written by Anthony P.
