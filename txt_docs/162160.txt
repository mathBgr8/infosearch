Christopher Perkins (born February 29, 1968) was the Star Wars Roleplaying Game design manager, who edited some of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game supplements, as well as Star Wars Insider 55 and the preview issue of the Star Wars Gamer magazine. *Star Wars Insider 55 *Zygerrian Takedown *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Tempest Feud *Star Wars Gamer Special Edition *Jedi Academy Training Manual Category:Role-playing game authors
