Campaign against Adumar
3787

A campaign against Adumar took place in 13 ABY during the Galactic Civil War, at around the same time of the Battle of Nam Chorios. Shortly after the Battle of Adumar, Imperial Admiral Gilad Pellaeon launched a campaign in order to conquer the planet Adumar. Thanks to his command ship, the Super Star Destroyer Reaper, Pellaeon was initially victorious and executed orbital bombardments against Adumar.
