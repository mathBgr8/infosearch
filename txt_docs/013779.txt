Ehjenla
1638

Ehjenla was the small homeworld of the Tuhgri species. It was located within the Kathol Outback, which was part of the Kathol sector. Homeworld of the Tuhgri species, the planet began emerging from an ice age during the last centuries of the Galactic Republic.
