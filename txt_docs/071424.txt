Chains of Justice
2435

Chains of Justice was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer, part of Warlord Zsinj's fleet. It often paired with the Victory-class Star Destroyer Venom. It fought at the Battle of Selaggis to help protect Zsinj and his damaged flagship Iron Fist but was forced to retreat when forces under the command of General Han Solo destroyed the Venom. Chains of Justice was introduced in Aaron Allston's X-Wing: Solo Command, the seventh book in the X-wing series.
