Prior to his death in 4 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale," including the death of Evar Orbus, to 4 ABY. the Letaki Evar Orbus, a musician who led a Jizz-Wailers band, could wag a slothar better than anyone. The slothar was introduced in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article written by Rich Handley for the sixty-seventh issue of Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003.
