Rwookawarrump
1175

Rwookawarrump was a Wookiee camp on Alaris Prime. It was one of five Wookiee outposts, along with the Republic army, preventing Sev'rance Tann and the Confederacy of Independent Systems from acquiring the Decimator activation codes. Ultimately, the Wookiee camps and the Republic presence in the area were wiped out, leaving the codes in the hands of the CIS. This was the battle of Alaris Prime during the Clone Wars. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds: Clone Campaigns Category:Alaris Prime locations
