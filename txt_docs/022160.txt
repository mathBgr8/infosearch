Repulsor scout
2340

thumb|left|150px|[[Radiation zone assault trooper|Radtroopers being supported by a repulsor scout.]] Repulsor scouts were used as recon vehicles by the Imperial Army, often in difficult and hazardous terrain. They were armed with a heavy repeating blaster located on the back of the vehicle, which could be accessed via a roof hatch. The gunner would stand behind the cannon on top of the vehicle, surrounded by railing.
