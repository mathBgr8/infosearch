Domain
1460

Domain was what the Yuuzhan Vong called a clan or family. Domains were often restricted to one caste, though an individual might enter a different caste. Rivalry between Domains was often intense, and reached its peak in the Cremlevian War, which rendered the Yuuzhan Vong galaxy uninhabitable.
