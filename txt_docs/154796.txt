*Bill Smith |publisher=West End Games |game=Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game |published in= |reprinted in= |release date=April 1997 |pages=32 |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The "Mos Eisley Adventure Set" was a roleplaying supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game published by West End Games (WEG). The 32-page set was designed to be a guide to the hazards and opportunities player characters could find in Mos Eisley, including adventures, characters, and locations to explore. The set included a variety of material, including West End Games' Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley. It includes five miniatures as well as New Republic era adventure hooks. The full publishing name is the Mos Eisley Miniatures Box Set, since it contained twelve metal miniatures for play with the game and a six-sided die.
