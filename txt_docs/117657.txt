Moonstone Lake
741

Moonstone Lake, located on the outskirts of the city Ussa on the planet Bellassa, was the most distant of the seven lakes within the city boundaries. Compared to the rest of Ussa, the district was grimy: the houses were huddled together and the streets were narrow twined and around one another in a baffling pattern. *The Last of the Jedi: The Desperate Mission *The Last of the Jedi: Against the Empire *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Bellassa locations Category:Lakes
