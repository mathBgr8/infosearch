Zazana Renkel
1667

Zazana Renkel was female Human who served as an agent of the Tarhassan Planetary Security and the Confederacy of Independent Systems during the Clone Wars. Around 22 BBY, Zazana fell in love with a former Republic agent named Edbit Teeks until he was kidnapped by Tarhassan Planetary Security. After serving her support with the Separatists, she was kidnapped by Joram Kithe and a special team from the Galactic Republic and the Republic Intelligence in order to question her about Teek's disappearance.
