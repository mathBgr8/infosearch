Veermok Clan
1196

Veermok Clan was an initiate training clan of the New Jedi Order that was usually made up of children between the ages of 13 to 14 years old. This clan was named after the Veermok, a creature native to Naboo. In 40 ABY the members of the Veermok Clan were taken hostage during a raid on the Jedi Academy. They were later rescued and taken to the Jedi Outpost on Endor's Sanctuary Moon. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Jedi Path: A Manual for Students of the Force Category:Jedi clans Category:New Jedi Order organizations
