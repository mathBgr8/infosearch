|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters=Kobola Spice Mine |locations=*Naboo *Rori |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Hutt Cartel }} }} The Kobola Miners were a dreaded crime organization based on Naboo and its moon Rori during the Galactic Civil War. They mined for spice which they sold to various Hutt organizations. They controlled a spice mine on Rori which they used as a bunker. The Kobola were on the CorSec's Most Wanted list of criminals, as they used slavery as a means of extracting spice. They would abduct travelers to Naboo and Rori and force them into doing the mining work.
