Plett's House
1136

Plett's House or Plett's Citadel was the home Jedi Master Plett built on Belsavis around 88 BBY. He used it as a research lab. Many of the greenhouse caverns that surrounded the site were converted to living quarters when a group of refugee Jedi families joined the Ho'Din there.
