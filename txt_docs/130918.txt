First Queen Mother
2847

A Hapan woman became the first Queen Mother of the Hapes Consortium around 4030 BBY. After the Jedi under Arca Jeth defeated the Lorell Raiders, who controlled Hapes and its large population of female slaves, the women of Hapes vowed that they would never again be ruled by a man. This led to the founding of the Hapan Royal House with the Queen Mother as its head.
