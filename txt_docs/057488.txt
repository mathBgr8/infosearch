Bothan Border
597

The Bothan Border was the edge of Bothan Space. It was near planets such as Dinlo. Republic Commando: Triple Zero indicates that the planet Dinlo is near the Bothan Border. However, more recent sources such as the The Essential Atlas place Dinlo much closer to the Core. The Bothan Border does not appear in any other sources. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero Border Category:Mid Rim regions
