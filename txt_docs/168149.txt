Oeeta
827

Oeeta was a Boma female residing in Mos Eisley, on Utapau. She was affectionate with Han Solo, and served him, Chewbacca, and Luke Skywalker Boma-mush, a form of gruel. As one of the short-lived changes found in the first draft, "Oeta" was considered as a name for a name for the prince named Biggs in the rough draft. *Adventures of the Starkiller, Episode I: The Star Wars *Star Wars: The Annotated Screenplays Category:Chefs Category:Females
