Si
1146

Si was a Gran exile and a commander within the Galactic Alliance Defense Force in 28 ABY. He was based on the planet Esfandia which was home to the Brrbrlpp. During his off-duty hours at the Galactic Alliance base, he studied the Brrbrlpp and studied their language and culture.
