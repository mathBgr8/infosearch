Arkanian jellyfish
2680

The Arkanian jellyfish was a type of aquatic jellyfish native to the frozen planet Arkania. The DZ-70 fugitive tracker droids, used during the rule of the Galactic Empire, had several arms that dangled like the tentacles of an Arkanian jellyfish. In another instance, the Oswaft, a sentient gigantic species from the Centrality, resembled a cross between a Corellian sea ray and an Arkanian jellyfish. The Arkanian jellyfish was first mentioned in the second issue of the Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi — The Freedon Nadd Uprising comic series. A comparison with the Arkanian jellyfish was used in a caption box to describe the debilitated state of the Sith sorcerer Ommin.Tales of the Jedi – The Freedon Nadd Uprising 2 Furthermore, an Arkanian jellyfish was used as a point of comparison in other literary sources, The Essential Guide to Droids and The Essential Chronology.
