Sometimes compared to young krayt dragons, they were often found in lairs made of bones. }} During the Galactic Civil War, Jabba the Hutt's cook, Porcellus, used to cook dragonet flanks for his master. In 1 ABY, Imperial Custodial Officer Pfilbee Jhorn tasked a spacer to eliminate dragonets prowling around the trash compactors in Bestine. Dragonets appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
