MobileMortar-3
1282

The MobileMortar-3 was an artillery platform which combined the MM-s3 grenade launcher and WW-676 repulsorlift sled to create a mobile fire-support weapon. MobileMortar-3s were used by both the Galactic Empire and the Alliance to Restore the Republic, as well as the later New Republic and planetary defense forces.Arms & Equipment Guide These platforms were typically issued as support weapons for army platoons, or operated as four-vehicle light mechanized infantry units.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *Arms & Equipment Guide *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology *Galaxy at War *Star Wars: Imperial Handbook: A Commander's Guide Category:Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. products Category:Vehicle-mounted launchers
