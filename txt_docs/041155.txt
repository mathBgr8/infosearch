Yma Nalle
1976

|masters= |apprentices= }} Yma Nalle was one of the Nalle triplets, a trio of Humans offered by Mayor Acros-Krik as guides to targets he wanted monitored. She reported to the mayor any useful information their guests foolishly divulged. She was in the Outlander Club on Coruscant before the outbreak of the Clone Wars in 22 BBY. Yma Nalle was portrayed by Amy Allen (uncredited) in Attack of the Clones.
