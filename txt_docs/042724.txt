Connected to the neighboring planets of Charis and Gandle Ott by hyperlanes, Mairne was located just off the Trition Trade Route that ran through the sector. By 8 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The DarkStryder Campaign to "eight years after the Battle of Yavin," for a date of 8 ABY. the world supported a small colony whose economy was based around supplying ore and natural resources to Gandle Ott. Mairne was featured in The DarkStryder Campaign, a 1995 supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game by West End Games. Mairne was not featured directly in any adventure, but was included to give background information on worlds within the Kathol sector.
