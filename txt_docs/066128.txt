Reprieve
2046

The Reprieve was an EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate modified as a medical frigate with the New Republic. It was called on to rendezvous with and recover Rogue Squadron after evacuating their base on Talasea, which was attacked by a small Imperial force. Onboard the frigate, Rogue Squadron pilots Corran Horn, Gavin Darklighter and Andoorni Hui were treated for the wounds they received in the fighting on Talasea.
