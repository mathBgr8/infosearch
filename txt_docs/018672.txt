NT 600
2253

NT 600 was an astronavigation and general assistance droid that was hardwired into the Inferno, Vilmarh Grahrk's personal starship. Also known as just NT, she could detach from the Inferno and operate as an independent unit. NT was pre-programmed with ethics circuits, which her Devaronian master found to be quite annoying. She was also quite fond of Jedi Master Quinlan Vos, to whom she had given access to the ship's systems.
