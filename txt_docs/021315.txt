|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=10 ABYStar Wars: The Comics Companion |series=Star Wars: Dark Empire |issue=4 }} Dark Empire 4 was the fourth issue of the 6 part Star Wars: Dark Empire series of comics. It was released June 23, 1992 by Dark Horse Comics. Betrayed by their friends and pursued by everyone's favorite bounty hunter, Han and Leia try to escape from Nar Shaddaa. Meanwhile, the Rebels in combat over the planet Calamari receive aid from an unexpected source, and Luke falls deeper into the grip of the Dark Side! The events of Confrontation on the Smugglers' Moon were adapted in the Dark Empire (audio) radio drama, which featured the music of John Williams' score from the films, as well as a voice cast that included Billy Dee Williams reprising his role as Lando Calrissian.
