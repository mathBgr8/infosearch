|water= |interest=Yorik coral and villip production facilities |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies=Yuuzhan Vong |socialgroup= |language=*Yuuzhan Vong *Huttese |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation=*Hutt Empire *Hutts *Yuuzhan Vong empire *Confederation }} Runaway Prince, called Lordunkee Wanpolla in Huttese, was an uninhabited planet in Hutt Space which the Hutts gave to the Yuuzhan Vong. The origin of its peculiar name was lost in history. When the Hutts first arrived, they squeezed the planet dry of all of its natural resources in a matter of months, making it a bog world like Nal Hutta. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, the planet was one of the many given as a token of goodwill to the extragalactic invaders. The Yuuzhan Vong then terraformed Runaway Prince to suit themselves and turned the planet into a yorik coral production facility.
