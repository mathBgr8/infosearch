Ranth (language)
909

Ranth language was the native language of the Ranth species from Caaraz. It was mainly formed by purrs, growls and rude vocalizations. Ranth had an alphabetic written form. Ranths who were "civilized" spoke Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
