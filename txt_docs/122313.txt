Transport Ship (Wookiee)
1743

Transport Ships were aquatic repulsorcraft used by the Wookiees to transport troops or personnel across bodies of water. Such aquatic transports saw extensive use in the Liberation of Kashyyyk, transporting Han Solo and Chewbacca out of the trap set for them in Okikuti, and also transporting Solo, Chewbacca, Shoran, and their forces from their headquarters in Chenachochan for their raids against Imperial targets.
