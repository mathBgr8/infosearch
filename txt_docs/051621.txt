Espo Walker 101
2503

The Espo Walker 101 was the replacement model for the Espo Walker 91 walker. Like its predecessor, the 101 was intended to turn a single trooper into the equivalent of a combat squad. During the height of the Galactic Civil War, the 101 was still a rare sight in Security Police forces, as fewer than 10% of all stations had been issued the walkers.
