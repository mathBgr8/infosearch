Jaster's Legacy
4913

Jaster's Legacy (JAST07) was the starship of the Mandalorian bounty hunter, Jango Fett, up until shortly after the Invasion of Naboo. thumb|left|180px|Jaster's Legacy. It was named after Fett's mentor Jaster Mereel (who was its original owner). The ship was a Kuat Drive Yards Amphibious Interstellar Assault Transport/infantry. On a mission to capture a bounty on the prison colony of Oovo IV, Jango flew Jaster's Legacy into the system in another ship's shadow and left it on the surface unguarded. When he came back out of the facility during a prison riot, another bounty hunter named Zam Wesell was trying to slice into the ship. While Fett and Wesell argued about the bounty on Bendix Fust, a Firespray-31-class patrol and attack craft appeared and fired upon the ship, destroying it.
