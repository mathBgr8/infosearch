During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Piton walked in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars: Complete Locations Coruscant and turned around to watch the bounty hunter Zam Wesell's Koro-2 all-environment exodrive airspeederStar Wars: Complete Cross-Sections descend through the air. When the speeder crashed, Piton, with the Rodian Nardi Shodu, approached the crash site. Shortly after, Piton traveled on an errand to the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the district with Shodu. They entered the club arm in arm just in front of the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker, who were there to capture Wesell. Shodu and Piton then walked behind a turbolift in the club. During the visit, Piton wore a form-fitting hunters cap and a gray coat.
