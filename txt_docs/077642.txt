Voss (Imperial officer)
1827

Major Voss was a Human male Imperial officer who commanded a Star Destroyer during the Galactic Civil War. In 3 ABY, Voss's Star Destroyer was part of the Admiral Mils Giel's "secret armada" tasked with transporting a rare and unique creature known as the teezl to the Imperial capital. En route, however, it was attacked by a small Rebel squadron disguised in TIE fighters.
