University of Charmath
1672

The University of Charmath, located on the world of Charmath, boasted one of the most complete historical libraries including a medical laboratory with the most detailed medical records throughout the known galaxy. Early in the Galactic Civil War, Major Arhul Hextrophon, a historian for the Rebel Alliance, found evidence there that renowned Jedi Master Yoda was alive on Dagobah.
