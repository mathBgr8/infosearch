|death= |listedpronouns=He/Him |nationality=Canadian |occupation=Artist |companies= |website= }} }} Christopher Chuckry is a Canadian artist. He worked as an inker and/or colorist for a number of Star Wars comics published by Dark Horse, including the comic adaptation of Revenge of the Sith. Raised in small-town Manitoba, Chuckry studied art at the University of Manitoba in the 1980s. During the COVID-19 pandemic, he began drawing political cartoons, completing around 600 over the course of four years.
