Charon battle armor
972

Charon battle armor was an organic defensive armor created through bioengineering by the bioscientists of the Charon species of Otherspace. The battle armor resembled the construction of the Desolate and appeared as an organic stone like construct. It was remarkably adaptive allowing the creator to mold it to the users form. Furthermore, it was capable of being shaped into weapons such as blades and clubs that would protrude from the armor. In addition to that, it was common for a hand held plasma cannon to be attached to the armor.
