Arcon Multinode Agricorp
2532

}} Arcon Multinode Agricorp, commonly referred to simply as Arcon Multinode,Han Solo at Stars' End was a Corellian-owned major macro farming and food processing corporation during the reign of the Galactic Republic and its successor, the Galactic Empire. Arcon was amongst the original group of contributing sponsors who invested in the Corporate Sector Authority, and were upgraded to voting sponsor. This meant that the company received discounted rates on goods that were sourced within the Corporate Sector, although Arcon also had significant holdings in the Outer Rim Territories, where they bought over thirty percent of crops grown by independent farmers.
