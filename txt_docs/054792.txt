Rolling Moisture Cell
596

The Rolling Moisture Cell was an invention created by scientist Burro Flats of the Baobab Archives. By reverse-engineering the hydrostatic field invented by the Gungans of Naboo, Flats was able to create a practical device that could carry water in arid environments. The Rolling Moisture Cell gained immense popularity on Tatooine. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Tools
