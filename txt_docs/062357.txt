Gaupa
1483

Gaupa were a type of creature native to the forest moon Endor. The sentient Ewoks also native to the moon would sometimes ride gaupa. The term gaupa was first used in the Star Wars Legends continuity and was used to refer to a type of horse found on Endor.Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure Gaupa were confirmed to be canon by the sourcebook Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know in 2015, which used the term independently from pony and so suggested the creatures were a separate group. *Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know *Star Wars: Absolutely Everything You Need to Know, Updated and Expanded Category:Creatures of Endor Category:Creatures of unspecified biology Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Domesticated creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
