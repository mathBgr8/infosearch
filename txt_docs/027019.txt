David Stiff
591

David Stiff portrayed Salporin in Revenge of the Sith. Stiff is a former professional basketball player from Melbourne, Australia. He had a long and distinguished basketball career in Australia's National Basketball League (NBL). Stiff, who stands 6'9" tall, won six NBL championships -- a record that has not been surpassed in NBL history. Category:Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith actors
