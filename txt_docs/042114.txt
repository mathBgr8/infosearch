Firith Me
2107

Firith Me was a male Human pazaak addict staying at the Visitor's Hotel in Ahto City on Manaan during the Jedi Civil War. In 3956 BBY, he was a witness in the Sunry case. During the case, he met the former Sith Lord Revan and revealed to him that the Sith Elassa Huros was in fact a Dark Jedi that Sunry killed. He was also a Pazaak Weekly reader. Although Firith Me is a pazaak player, the player can not play pazaak with him. The player can bribe or threaten Firith Me to testify, the latter of which will earn them dark side points.
