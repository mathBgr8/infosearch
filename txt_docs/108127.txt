Vaklu officer
632

Vaklu officer was a rank within General Vaklu's military during the Onderon Civil War. They normally were armored in a light blue battle suit, and carried a one-hand blaster pistol for ranged combat and a vibrosword for melee dueling. These officers were akin to the rank of sergeant in most armies, leading groups of approximately ten men. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Military ranks Category:Onderonians
