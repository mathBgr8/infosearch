Parq
2071

}} Colonel Parq was an Imperial officer who participated in the search of Mos Eisley on Tatooine during the hunt for R2-D2 and C-3PO, just before the Battle of Yavin. Leading his Stormtroopers into Chalmun's Cantina, the sand-blasted and sweating Colonel was delighted to recognize the Tonnika sisters at the bar, and promptly arrested them on behalf of Grand Moff Argon.
