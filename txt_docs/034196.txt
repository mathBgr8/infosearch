Polyhedron 128
962

Polyhedron 128 was a roleplaying magazine from February of 1998. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "It's Technical, Billy: Personal Universal Translator" and was written by Paul Gosselin. Category:Polyhedron
