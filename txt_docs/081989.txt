Tuk Prevoz
1054

thumb|Tuk Prevoz heading to Coruscant The Tuk Prevoz was a modified tug owned by the Independent Ship Owners registered on Coruscant, but under contract to Xizor Transport Systems. In 3.5 ABY, it towed several linked cargo cylinders (each as big as a YT-1300 and containing braking rockets), with total cargo around 800/900 megatons, in to Coruscant. On their way to the planet to rescue Leia Organa Solo from the Black Sun, the Millennium Falcon secretly hitched a ride on the underside of one of its cargo cylinders, to evade Imperial pickets. *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *Shadows of the Empire 6 Category:Tugs
