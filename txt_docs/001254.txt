thumb|right|250px|[[Darth Caedus|Jacen and Jaina on Yavin 4, at the Jedi Praxeum.]] The Solo twins were the humans Jaina Solo and Jacen Solo. They were born in 9 ABY as the first children of Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo. Their birth received much publicity over the HoloNet, resulting in a baby-boom in the New Republic. They were also a source of awe for both the Imperial Remnant, being grandchildren of Anakin Skywalker/Darth Vader, and for the Yuuzhan Vong, as twins were a rare genetic event among them, holding great religious meaning. They also had a younger brother, Anakin Solo. All three Solo children trained with their uncle, Grand Master Luke Skywalker, as Jedi in the ways of the Force. Together, they passed by many adventures, including the Dark Nest Crisis and Swarm War. During the Yuuzhan Vong War, they went to Myrkr on a mission to destroy the Voxyn queen. There, Anakin Solo was killed and Jacen stepped up to lead the team, killing the queen and ending the voxyn threat before he was captured by the Yuuzhan Vong. While Jaina Solo remained a Jedi, her brother Jacen became the feared Darth Caedus in 40 ABY, starting a civil war within the Galactic Alliance.
