Gamma flux boiler
688

Gamma flux boilers were cooking devices. Presumably, they used gamma radiation to heat food. During Princess Leia Organa's captivity by Lady Tarkin, the Lady ordered Leia to monitor her villa's gamma flux boilers during a banquet. *"Princess Leia, Imperial Servant" Category:Consumables storage and processing
