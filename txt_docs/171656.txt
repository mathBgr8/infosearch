Star Wars Weekly 89 is the eighty-ninth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on November 7, 1979. This issue reprints the second of three parts of the story printed in the US as Star Wars (1977) 36. Although it doesn't appear on the non-Star Wars Marvel UK little section of the cover, the Star-Lord comic will be replaced from now on by a Deathlok series. *"Deathlok the Demolisher Part 1: A cold knight's frenzy" *"Guardians of the galaxy!
