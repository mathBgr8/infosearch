Cinnagar weave armor
1302

Cinnagar weave armor was an extremely durable armor suit created during the Great Hyperspace War. A highly complex organic weave allowed the suit to withstand temperature extremes. Similar armor helped the people of Cinnagar defend their city and their planet against the Sith Empire led by Naga Sadow.
