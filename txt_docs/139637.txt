Jedi Enclaves
2568

Jedi Enclaves were small, satellite training and defense facilities of the Jedi Order, as proxies of the Jedi Temple on Coruscant. Enclaves were spread throughout the galaxy during the time of the Old Republic up until sometime before the Clone Wars.
