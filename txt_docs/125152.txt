Rogue Wing
1411

Rogue Wing was a New Republic starfighter wing during the Galactic Civil War. It was renamed "Rogue Wing" by General Wedge Antilles (presumably after his Rogue Squadron), who was placed in command of the wing by Mon Mothma for his actions at the Battle of Bilbringi. The unit fought at the Second Battle of Coruscant, where it suffered only light casualties due to being carried in the Emancipator. *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:New Republic starfighter wings
