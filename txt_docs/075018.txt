Expansion Week
1049

Expansion Week was a holiday in the Tapani sector. It was a five-day celebration that fell between the months of Selona and Telona. Holovids were broadcast featuring special programming commemorating the holiday, and many urban centers hosts cultural festivals. Expansion Week also marked the beginning of the Capital Season, which ran all the way to Shelova Week. *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector holidays
