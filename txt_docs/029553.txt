|continent=Wartaki |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation=Wookiees }} Kamarkiia was a city in the Wartaki continent on Kashyyyk. During the Galactic Civil War it was abandoned by the Wookiees. It was also the location of the sacred vaults of Neocomora, which were thought destroyed during the initial Imperial occupation until they were discovered by the Gran thief Kea Ra-Lan. Kamarkiia was first mentioned in 1996's Star Wars Adventure Journal 10. Category:Cities on Kashyyyk
