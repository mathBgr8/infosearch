Boribos Prefecture
913

Boribos Prefecture was an area of Coruscant. It bordered Hirkenglade Prefecture and CoCo Town, and was connected to them by Skyroute D25D. The Duros Cultural Center was located here. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Coruscant districts
