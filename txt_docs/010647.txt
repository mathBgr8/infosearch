EV-9D9.2
1750

EV-9D9.2 was a feminine MerenData EV-series supervisor droid and the successor to EV-9D9. She was said to be twice as ruthless as EV-9D9, her predecessor. She was head of cyborg operations within the droid factories of Telti. She was also responsible for the torture of Cole Fardreamer, when he was captured and imprisoned on Telti. She was deactivated, along with the other droids of Telti, by R2-D2 and C-3PO.
