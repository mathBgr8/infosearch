Battle of Oovo IV
2745

The Battle of Oovo IV took place during the Galactic Civil War. Imperial forces were led by Admiral Hurkk. The Rebel Alliance forces included new Nebulon-B frigates, ironically designed for use by the Imperial Navy to protect convoys from Rebel starfighter actions. Details of the battle are unavailable, as it is only mentioned within the context of the quote for the Nebulon-B Frigate's entry in The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels. *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Battles of Oovo IV Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
