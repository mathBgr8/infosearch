He was the younger brother of the Tusken Raider Ur. Urur was restricted to defending his tribe. If Ur was killed, Urur would take his older brother's place. Urur wore desert-colored robes, with two bandoliers strapped diagonally across both sides of the chest above the belt, along with the standard Tusken mask. He wielded a Gaderffii.
