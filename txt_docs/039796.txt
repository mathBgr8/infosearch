Starjammer-IZX fast freight hauler
1407

A Starjammer-IZX fast freight hauler was an exceedingly large cargo ship built by Kuat Drive Yards, with sturdy durasteel hull plating. They were routinely found disembarking from various ports of call on the smugglers' moon of Nar Shaddaa. The mechanic Shug Ninx had the chassis of an IZX docked inside of his repair shop in the Corellian Sector of the planet. Category:Kuat Drive Yards products Category:Transport classes
