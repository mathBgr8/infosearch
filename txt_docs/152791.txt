thumb|right|Seclusion Braids Seclusion Braids was the phrase used to describe the braids that a male Weequay wove into their topnots while they were away from their homeworld. Typically a Weequay male twisted one seclusion braid for every year he was away from Sriluur. *Tatooine Ghost *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *Secrets of Tatooine Category:Hairstyles Category:Weequay culture
