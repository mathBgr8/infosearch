The Gradilis sector was a poorly charted sector located in Wild Space, near the boundary with the Unknown Regions. It was classed as a freestanding subsector of the Galactic Empire at the time of the Battle of Yavin. It was poorly explored with insufficiently documentation before the Imperial Era. Yet, the sector was not given a formal status when incorporated into the Empire and was not represented in the Imperial Senate.
