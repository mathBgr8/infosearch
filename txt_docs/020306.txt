The octopedal being Jomo hailed from Yoberra, a fact that was noted in the 13:4:18 issue of the HoloNet News' CoCo District Edition, in an article mentioning Jomo's triumph in the "Mobquet Presents: Fastest Land Beings" racing event. Yoberra was first mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #52, an issue of the representation of the in-universe HoloNet News that was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published on the HoloNet News website on April 18, 2002. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Yoberra system, and therefore Yoberra itself, in grid square M-20.
