The Krayt Dragon Lounge
895

The Krayt Dragon Lounge was a bar in Mos Eisley. Han Solo and Chewbacca visited this bar the day before they met Luke Skywalker and Ben Kenobi in Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina. There they encountered Dash Rendar, and later Kabe. Outside the bar they came across Boba Fett, who delivered the news of Bria Tharen's death. *Rebel Dawn Category:Bars and clubs Category:Mos Eisley locations
