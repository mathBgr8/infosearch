Minstyngar
2994

|language= }} }} Minstyngar were animals that lived in packs on Kashyyyk. A massive simian creature, the minstyngar haunted the lower levels of the Rryatt Trail. The minstyngar had a rudimentary social structure. Although many adult minstyngar could be found roaming the Rryatt Trail, the elders and younglings were often hidden deep in the forest, far from prying eyes. Their skin was either a shade of green or brown.
