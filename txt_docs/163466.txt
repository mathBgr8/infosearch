Darts (game)
753

thumb|right|A [[Rodian/Legends|Rodian playing darts]] The game of darts was a game played by throwing pointed darts at a circular game board, which could be hung on a wall. The darts would be thrown at the bright dart board, and where they would land determined the score the player received. Sometimes, when a dart missed the board, it would embed itself in the surrounding wall. In a cantina, a Rodian could be seen playing darts. The game of darts appeared in LEGO Star Wars: Revenge of the Brick. *LEGO Star Wars: Revenge of the Brick Category:Games
