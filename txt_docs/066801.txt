Malice (CR90 corvette)
1286

thumb|right|250px|The Malice. Malice was an Imperial CR90 corvette that formed part of the Corrupter Task Force in its hunt for the Alliance cruiser Defiance. The Malice was commanded by Commander Linder. The Malice accompanied the Corrupter in its pursuit of the Defiance Task Force in 3 ABY. It was present with her task force when the Rebels raided the fleet during their attempt to launch Probe droids into outer space. The Malice fate afterward is unknown.
