BlasTech light repeating blaster
1785

The BlasTech light repeating blaster was a light repeating blaster produced by BlasTech Industries, and was a smaller version of the T-21 light repeating blaster. Based on a successful riot gun design used by the Corporate Sector Authority, this weapon was known for its high rate of fire in a small package. Due to the weapon's small size, it could be used as a support weapon, or as a soldier's service weapon.
