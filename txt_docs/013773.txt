Bob Bergen
4514

Robert Snelgrove "Bob" Bergen is an American voice actor best known for his work on Looney Tunes cartoons. He has provided voicing for a number of LucasArts Star Wars video games and computer games, and was often used as the voice for Luke Skywalker.
