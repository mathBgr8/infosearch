Zann Remanufacturing
1032

Zann Remanufacturing was a company on Anaxes owned by the parents of Tyber Zann. Affiliated with the Galactic Empire, it specialized in retooling Imperial and Old Republic military hardware for civilian use. Tyber worked there as a youngling.
