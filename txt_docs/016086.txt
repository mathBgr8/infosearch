|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Harazod was the capital city of the planet Gerrard V. In 0 BBY, it was seized by local rebels commanded by General Camon Udeon. An Imperial task force under the command of Admiral Jion Trynn forced the rebels to retreat to the capitol building, where they offered terms of surrender. Udeon refused, and Trynn authorized the orbital bombardment of Harazod's urban center. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities
