Dashadi
1744

Dashadi was the official language of Urkupp. It was used by the planet's native species, the Dashades. Since the destruction of Urkupp in 3996 BBY, apparently there had been only one or a small number of active Dashades at a single time.The Clone Wars: The Fall of Falleen As Dashadi had never been a widely-used language beyond Urkupp, its speakers had increasingly seen the advantages of using Basic to communicate with other species.
