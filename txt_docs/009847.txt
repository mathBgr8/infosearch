Autoblaster
4579

thumb|right|250px|A B-wing autoblaster firing The autoblaster was a type of blaster cannon.The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology They were rapid-fire cannons, fitted with advanced XCiters and specialized actuating modules. The weapons were capable of high cyclical rates of fire with each pull of the trigger or push of the button, releasing hails of blaster bolts at a target.Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook By the time of the Galactic Civil War, autoblasters were considered to be archaic weapons.Scavenger Hunt The Alliance B-wing starfighter was armed with autoblasters mounted under the nose of its cockpit.The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Ugor Black Hole-class Salvage Dreadnought were armed with scavenged autoblasters.
