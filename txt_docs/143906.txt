250px|thumb|Broiled feejay The feejay was a species of small, non-sentient creature that was recognized by its three arms and three head tentacles. Widely considered a delicacy among Hutts, a broiled feejay was once poisoned by the thug Olag Greck, and served to a Hutt crimelord.
