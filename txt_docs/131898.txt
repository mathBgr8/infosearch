Twistler
1035

A twistler was a drink made with Corellian brandy and a tart fruit extract. Senator Garm Bel Iblis enjoyed these regularly, serving them to his aide Sena Leikvold Midanyl, Han Solo, and Lando Calrissian when the latter two visited his base in 9 ABY. While Han enjoyed the beverage, Lando was too preoccupied with unfolding events to appreciate his. *Dark Force Rising Category:Corellian alcohols
