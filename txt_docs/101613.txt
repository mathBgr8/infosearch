General Ministry Institutes
1927

The General Ministry Institutes were various centers of learning and research that were funded by the Galactic Republic General Ministry and then decades later by the New Republic General Ministry. Prior to the Clone Wars, the General Ministry slashed funding for the General Ministry Institutes in some cases by as much as 60 percent. This caused many of the institutes to look towards private donors for funding.
