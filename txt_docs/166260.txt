|intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Paperback |pages=48 |isbn=9780375814631 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline=c. 22 BBYThe events of the book immediately proceed which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 22 BBY |series=Jedi Readers |preceded by= |followed by= }} Anakin: Apprentice is a Star Wars Legends young readers book written by Marc Cerasini and illustrated by Tommy Lee Edwards. The book is part of the Jedi Readers series, and it was published by LucasBooks on April 23, 2002. Set immediately before Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones, Anakin: Apprentice follows Anakin Skywalker as he reflects on his past adventures. Nineteen-year-old Anakin Skywalker, apprentice to Obi-Wan Kenobi, remembers some of his past adventures in which he raced swoop bikes, built androids, and found crystals to complete his lightsaber.
