Department of Cost Accounting
817

The Department of Cost Accounting was a government organization within the Galactic Republic, most likely part of the Ministry of Finance. Joram Kithe worked there as an accountant in a job he got due to the influence of his aunt Tagdel, who had a position in the Ministry of Finance.
