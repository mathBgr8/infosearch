Kikow
1641

Kikow was a pale Ithorian who left his herdship at an early age and eventually settled on Ralltiir. A superb cook with a highly developed sense of smell, he ran a restaurant and had a business trading in culinary herbs and spices. However, this was only a cover for his criminal undertakings. He had a small all-Ithorian organization, the Herd, that smuggled thermal detonators. Kikow worked with the Wookiee Furellas, and modeled his organization on her larger one.
