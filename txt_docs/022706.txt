|destroyed= |type= |location=Criton's Point |interest= |rebuilt= |builder= |height= |width= |affiliation= }} The Library of Xer was a library on the planet Criton's Point. The library was built in 26,000 BBY, by Xer VIII or one of his predecessors. The library was slowly being reopened by a research team during the period of the Galactic Civil War. Inside one vault, they found a tiny tree that was being nourished by the Force. Its Force-presence attracted Force users&mdash;both dark and light. Category:Libraries
