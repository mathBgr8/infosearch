Fargus Deel
1118

Fargus Deel was a famous swoop racer. On several occasions, he performed at the tracks on Coruscant. While still new to the sport, he often raced against Han Solo, who Fargus "constantly" lost to. Fargus was known to wear a red uniform and race a custom SoroSuub racing swoop. He had a great many admiring fans, including Rebel Alliance pilot Kenn Nitram.
