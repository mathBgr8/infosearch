Firrinree
628

The Firrinree was one of the two Sh'ner-class planetary assault carriers in the Keeramak Entourage during the battle of Bakura in 28 ABY. The ship eventually fell into the hands of the P'w'ecks during the battle when they overthrew their Ssi-ruuk overlords. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Sh'ner-class planetary assault carriers Category:Ssi-ruuvi starships
