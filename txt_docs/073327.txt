Agamarian Council
1007

The Agamarian Council was the ruling body of the planet Agamar. The council was headquartered in the Council Center at Calna Muun. In 25 ABY, it was led by a female Speaker who was probably a Muun. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught *The New Jedi Order Sourcebook Category:Legislatures
