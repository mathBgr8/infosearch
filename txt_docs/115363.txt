*Tim Drage |producer= |production company= |writer=*Tony Mines *Tim Drage |starring= |music=Jason Graves |release date=2002 |runtime=3 minutes |budget= |language=English |timeline=3 ABYLEGO Star Wars: The Han Solo Affair is based on Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 3 ABY. |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} LEGO Star Wars: The Han Solo Affair, also known as Star Wars Episode V½, is a short stop-motion animated film using LEGO characters that was released in 2002. It was created by Spite Your Face Productions Ltd and hosted on LEGO.com. It was also shared on Brickfilms.com and later posted to video streaming sites such as YouTube.com by Spite Your Face Productions. From between the scenes of The Empire Strikes Back, it's the Han Solo Affair! Han Solo's friends hatch a plan to rescue him from the clutches of Darth Vader and Boba Fett. Will they succeed?
