Vhek
1554

Vheks were creatures found on the Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Qiilura that were known to be preyed upon by packs of gdans. In 22 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, which includes the mention of vheks, in the year 22 BBY. the Gurlanin Jinart asked the Omega Squad's clone commandos if they had ever dragged an intact vhek home for a meal, indicating that gdans worked together to catch one of the creatures and then dragged their bounty away via large tunnels.
