Temple of the Lovers
748

The Temple of the Lovers was a temple on Coruscant after its Vongforming into Yuuzhan'tar. Built for the disciples of the Undying Flame, the Temple was built for Yun-Txiin and Yun-Q'aah, the Lover Gods of the Yuuzhan Vong. During the Battle of Yuuzhan'tar, the temple was set on fire by war biots acting under the control of the World Brain, who had been tasked by Supreme Overlord Shimrra to render Coruscant uninhabitable. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Lovers Category:Yuuzhan'tar locations
