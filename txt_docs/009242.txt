Meccha
1834

Meccha was a mid-sized town on the planet of Drall located near the Boiling Sea. It was made up of domed buildings connected by skywalks. The dome's temperature was kept at 17° Celsius which was chilly to most species, but comfortable to the Drall. The population consisted mainly of farmers who would work at night in the fields outside of the domes and rest inside during the harsh days. In addition to food, some of the farm products were used for medicinal purposes such as the Vitapill.
