Baladdok
1488

Baladdok was a male Weequay affiliate of Gardulla the Hutt, who was based on Tatooine during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. A bounty, 2,500 credits dead, was posted on him after slaughtering a ronto belonging to a Jawa village in the Jundland Wastes.
