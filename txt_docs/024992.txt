Harribore Onuta
2065

**Coruscant Health Administration |masters= |apprentices= }} Harribore Onuta was the Medxec of the Coruscant Health Administration during the Separatist Crisis which plagued the Galactic Republic. Living on Coruscant, Harribore Onuta was appointed as Medxec of the Coruscant Health Administration before 22 BBY. During the Separatist Crisis, a conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Medxec Onuta oversaw the rise in Brainworm Rot Type A on the galactic capital. During an interview with HoloNet News, Onuta blamed the rising rate of contracting Brainrot on the increased number of different species entering the planet's spaceport's each and everyday. This statement was taken offensively by many non-Humans, believing it to be Republic anti-alien hate speech.
