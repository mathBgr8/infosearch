Lamner
1095

Colonel Lamner was a TIE fighter pilot for the Imperial Navy. Lamner later joined the Invids, where he became the commander of the True Invid fighter wing. He was killed at the Second Battle of Xa Fel after insisting on leading his forces into battle against Rogue Squadron.
