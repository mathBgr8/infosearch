Baragwinian
1060

Baragwinian was the main language used by the Baragwin species, aside from Galactic Basic Standard. The Baragwin themselves, however, were not especially fond of this language. Most of them only learned Basic or the languages used on whatever planet they were born, so Baragwinian was not very common. Baragwinian was still considered useful as a trade language, however.
