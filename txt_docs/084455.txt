Arlo Tyre
2124

Arlo Tyre was a man who joined the Rebellion after an Imperial raid cost him everything he held dear. He became a Master Scout and was partnered with Wadie Firestone. Years after the raid, the pair were sent to investigate the loss of communications with an archeological team on Alashan. Despite being a seasoned veteran, Tyre was killed investigating the ruins, cut down by a mysterious beam of energy.
