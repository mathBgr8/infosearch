Qworlth skaal
1877

A qworlth skaal, also known as dread weapon, was a biologically engineered worm-like organism that extended from the bow of a Yuuzhan Vong warship or worldship. The creature could penetrate the surface of an enemy ship or space station and suck out all beings aboard.
