After the Battle of Endor, it served as headquarters for the false Supreme Prophet of the Dark Side, the pseudo-Kadann. Scardia also functioned as a palacial receiving chamber used to overawe supplicants with displays of the Prophets' vast wealth. Seeking revenge on the true Kadann, and believing the pseudo-Kadann to be the Emperor's Mage whom he imitated, Grand Admiral Afsheen Makati brought every weapon in his fleet to bear on Scardia Station, destroying it and killing the Prophets. After this, High Admiral Treuten Teradoc of the Greater Maldrood plundered the space station of its treasures, adding them to his growing hoard. This chamber was supposedly used by the Church of the Dark Side as a location through which they divined future events.
