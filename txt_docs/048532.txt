Green3
634

Green3 was a datafile that Green Squad had to capture in a training exercise in Alliance Training Center-5/Green Squad before they could become Green Squad 3. The file was stored aboard a simulated Star Destroyer. When the group retrieved the file, the training exercise did not end like it was meant to; instead, an alarm came on, as the base was under attack from the Galactic Empire.
