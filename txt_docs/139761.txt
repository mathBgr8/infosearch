Electro-jabber
2327

An electro-jabber was a hand-held shock device. It was similar to a stun baton, only older and more simplified. They gave the victim a violent and very painful shock. Electro-jabbers were some of the torture devices used by the Death Star's IT-0 interrogation droid on Princess Leia Organa while she was held in captivity.Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi (novelization) During Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi's mission to Romin, during a party, the dictator of the planet used an electro-jabber on a waiter for serving a food item at the wrong time. *Legacy of the Jedi *Jedi Apprentice: The Dark Rival *Jedi Apprentice: The Defenders of the Dead *Jedi Apprentice: The Fight for Truth *Jedi Apprentice: The Dangerous Rescue *Secrets of the Jedi *Star Wars Journal: Darth Maul *Jedi Quest: Path to Truth *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games *Jedi Quest: The Shadow Trap *Jedi Quest: The Changing of the Guard *Jedi Quest: The Final Showdown *The Last of the Jedi: The Desperate Mission *Star Wars: Assault Team *Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi (novelization) *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Electrical weaponry
