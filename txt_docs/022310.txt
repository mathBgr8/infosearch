Cesa
593

Cesa was a game popular among the Gorothite species. Cesa was played by two people and used an ornate checkerboard formed from ten squares on each side. The rules on how to move the pieces across the board were very simple. Among Gorothites the game ranged from being a hobby to an obsession. *Goroth: Slave of the Empire Category:Board games
