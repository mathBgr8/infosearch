Roxi Barl
1511

Roxi Barl was a freelance assassin and thug in the Corellian sector, well known to CorSec. She and her sister, Deli, long dreamed of getting rich, and finally managed it when Deli married CorDuro's Vice-President of Fleets. He was found murdered after he filed for divorce from Roxi's sister. It was this action that led Victor Pomt to hire her.
