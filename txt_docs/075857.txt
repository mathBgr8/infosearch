Nebula Orchid
768

The Nebula Orchid was a popular diner in Kuat City on Kuat during the early years of the New Republic. Reck Desh was at the Nebula Orchid when he learned from an unknown telbun the location of Elan and Vergere. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dining establishments Category:Kuat locations
