Protector (Victory-class)
2421

Protector was a Victory-class Star Destroyer in the New Republic Navy during the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 25 ABY, it brought Leia Organa Solo from Coruscant to Bastion. It was commanded by Admiral Aril Nunb and was equipped with the shuttle Chandrila Moon. In 2008, The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia stated there were three different Victory-class Star Destroyers called Protector in the Expanded Universe.
