Rennt Govia
1269

Senator Rennt Govia was a high-ranking member of the Bakuran Senate at the time of the Bakura Incident. His grandparents were backers of the Bakur Mining Corporation. He attended the Corulag University of Economics and was a self-proclaimed model of Human High Culture.
