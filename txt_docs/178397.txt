A Walk in the Phelopean Forest
779

A Walk in the Phelopean Forest was an alcoholic beverage served in the Hard Heart Cantina on the first Death Star. It consisted of ten different colored layers of liquid, each with their own densities, and required precision on the part of the bartender to prevent any of the layers from bleeding into one another. They were poured into a long cylindrical crystal. This could take up to five minutes to assemble. The drink served four beings and cost fifty credits. *Death Star Category:Cocktails
