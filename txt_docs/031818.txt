|subscribers=November 14 2005 |pages= |isbn= |issn= |genre= |issue=85 |series=Star Wars Insider }} Star Wars Insider 85 is the 85th issue of Star Wars Insider, released November 2005. It started shipping to subscribers the week of November 14, and shipped to newsstands the week of November 28. *"Comlink" *"Skywalking: News from Around the Galaxy" *"Jedi Library" &mdash; From Droids to Darths *"Drawn by the Force" &mdash; For Over a Thousand Generations... *"Technical Readout" &mdash; Invisible Hand: Main Bridge *"Ask the Master" &mdash; Starting With A Clean Slate *"Scouting the Galaxy" &mdash; How To Insure Your Collection... And Still Eat! *"Jabba's Collection" &mdash; Unleash Your Inner Collector *"Best of Hyperspace" &mdash; The Iron Chef Of Filmmaking *Bantha Tracks Vol. 16 *"The Saga is Now Complete" *:The #1 movie of the year is now out! Insider descended upon the serene confines of Skywalker Ranch to get all the details on the Revenge of the Sith DVD as well as get the scoop on the exciting new LucasArts game, Battlefront II! *"The Art of Revenge of the Sith, Part Three" *:Insider concludes its series with a great collection of artistic gems that you positively won't see or read about anywhere else. *"A Two-Edged Sword" *:Darth Vader is one tough customer, but even he needs help to dispatch six fearsome Dark Jedi clones. *"Who's Who in the Delegation of 2000" *:Now that the Revenge of the Sith DVD is out, fans get to see deleted scenes that show the "birth" of the Rebellion. Insider reveals the details on a brave bunch of Senators who dared to try and defy the Chancellor. *"Long Ago, Far Away: Galaxy Fashion" *:In celebration of the "Dressing a Galaxy" exhibit at the FIDM museum in Los Angeles, California, Insider made a visit to check out Trisha Biggar's work firsthand. What we saw can only be described as phenomenal. *"Posters of Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back" *:In part two of a three-part series, poster collector Pete Vilmur provides a once-in-a-lifetime look at rare concepts from the 1980 movie.
