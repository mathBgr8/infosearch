At some point during the Imperial Period, Arani Korden, Deel Surool and Rorworr confronted High Inquisitor Volytar in his private retreat on Skorrupon. They intercepted the Imperial Inquisitor while he was trying to board his private shuttle and escape to his orbiting Star Destroyer. Eventually, they battled Volytar who surrounded himself with eight destroyer droids in a room filled with pop-up grenade traps. Some time after the battle, Korden, Surool and Rorworr became known as the "heroes of Skorrupon" and eventually parted ways. Korden returned to her homeworld of Naboo and became a New Republic senator. Surool used his new celebrity status to become a holovid star, until a major spice scandal forced him to disappear into the criminal underworld where he became a crime lord. With the fall of the Empire at the Battle of Endor, Rorworr passed up a position within the New Republic to become an independent explorer. He took his ship, a modified Svelte-class shuttle, on a grand tour of the galaxy. He then proceeded to explore the galaxy alone, mourning the loss of his friend Sia-Lan Wezz. thumb|right|250px|Sia-Lan Wezz's death on Kessel, as featured on the cover of Star Wars: Purge The Battle of Skorrupon was first mentioned in Galactic Campaign Guide, a Star Wars Roleplaying Game supplement published by Wizards of the Coast in August 2003. Galactic Campaign Guide implied that Sia-Lan Wezz was killed at the Battle of Skorrupon.
