During the height of the Gree civilization, this world's surface was dominated by farms and food production facilities, but after the decline of the Gree, 90% of the planet's surface was given over to game preserves and botanical gardens. The few remaining parcels of land still provided food for the Enclave. An average day on Lonatro was 22 standard hours with a year lasting 340 local days.
