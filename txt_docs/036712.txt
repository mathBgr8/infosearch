Yakez
2406

The Yakez was a Star Destroyer of the New Republic Fourth Battle Group and the New Republic Fifth Fleet. The Yakez served in the Fourth Fleet and was later reassigned to the inaugural New Republic Fifth Fleet because of the dire military threat that the Yevethan Duskhan League posed to the New Republic. Because of its size and armaments, it was the flagship of Task Force Apex during the Black Fleet Crisis, under the command of Commodore Farley Carson.
