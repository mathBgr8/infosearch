Light Brigade Division
1178

Light Brigade Division was a brigade of starfighter corps in the Grand Army of the Republic during the last year of the Clone Wars. The brigade included HOB-147, who flew a V-19 Torrent starfighter. Shortly before the execution of Order 66, Light Brigade engaged Confederate forces in a space battle. During the battle HOB-147's starfighter was badly damaged. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Grand Army of the Republic divisions
