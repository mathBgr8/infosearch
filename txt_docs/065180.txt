Fireater
1547

The Fireater was a DP20 gunship in service to the New Republic in 9 ABY and stationed at Durren. It was part of a small fleet of warships hastily assembled by Han Solo and Lando Calrissian to defend the Meridian sector from invasion, along with the Courane.
