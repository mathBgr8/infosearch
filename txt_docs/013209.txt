Barbara Hambly
1692

Barbara Hambly is an award-winning and prolific American novelist and the author of the Star Wars novels Children of the Jedi and Planet of Twilight published by Bantam Spectra in 1995 and 1997, respectively. She also wrote short stories for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina and Tales from Jabba's Palace, also published by Bantam Spectra in 1995, and for the Star Wars Adventure Journal, which was published by West End Games in 1997. *Children of the Jedi (1995) *Planet of Twilight (1997) (1995) (1995) (1997) Category:Novel authors Category:Short story authors
