Battle on Dagu
3350

A battle took place on Dagu during the Clone Wars. It was between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems during which High General Shaak Ti battled a Dark Acolyte named Artel Darc. Master Shaak Ti was accompanying a large group of clones when they were captured and imprisoned on the swamp world, Dagu. While one of Count Dooku's most cunning Dark Jedi Acolytes, Artel Darc, controlled the Separatist prison, Shaak Ti was able to disguise her identity as a Jedi and laid low amongst her fellow prisoners.
