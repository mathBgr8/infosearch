Winna Di Yuni
2436

Winna Di Yuni was a female Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order during the final decades of the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive female, Winna Di Yuni was educated in the ways of the Force, eventually attaining the rank of Jedi Master. Training independently as a Consular, Master Yuni perfected her Force healing abilities and went on to become one of the Coruscant Jedi Temple's finest diagnosticians. In 43 BBY, Master Di Yuni was stationed in the Temple's Halls of Healing when Master Qui-Gon Jinn brought his friend, Didi Oddo, for treatment after the man had been poisoned by a chemical agent of unknown source.
