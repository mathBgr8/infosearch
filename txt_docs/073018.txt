Gauch
1303

Gauch was a smuggler on Dubrillion in 25 ABY, at the start of the Yuuzhan Vong War; he worked for Lando Calrissian. When the Vong attacked the planet at the start of the war, he was one of the pilots in the rag-tag starfighter fleet who defended the world. He piloted the TIE bomber TB-1.
