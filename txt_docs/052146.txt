After the Battle of Yavin, she was a healer for the Singing Mountain Clan. She once sent an individual to Naboo to meet with Skub, a Gungan holy man who had discovered a form of algae with properties similar to bacta whom she had learned about through a book, to gain a sample of the algae as she thought that it would be well suited to the environs of Dathomir. Vurlene was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Vurlene was one of the quest givers of the original "Singing Mountain Clan Theme Park." This quest series was removed from the game upon the release of the revamped "Witches of Dathomir Theme Park," introduced in the Game Update 19 on December 14, 2010.
