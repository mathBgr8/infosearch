Sat'skar
2057

The sat'skar was a traditional type of sword made by the Coynites. Due to its bulk, this great serrated blade could only be wielded effectively using two hands, to devastating effect. Attempts to wield it single-handedly proved clumsy and ineffective to all but the most skilled warriors.
