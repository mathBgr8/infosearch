Varn, World of Water
1232

Varn, World of Water was a holodocumentary feature about the planet of Varn. Though it was quite dull, it fascinated the Kamarian Badlanders so much when Han Solo exhibited the documentary to them at his makeshift holotheater that they started a religion around it. Solo failed to realize its significance due to communication difficulties and tried to replace it with with the drama Love is Waiting, causing the Cult of Varn to riot.
