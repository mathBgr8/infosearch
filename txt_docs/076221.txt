Tish Eggleston Pahl
528

Tish Pahl co-authored three short stories with Chris Cassidy, which featured the female protagonists Fenig Nabon and Ghitsa Dogder. Two of the stories, "Hutt and Seek" and "Simple Tricks," appeared in the anthology Tales from the New Republic. Category:Short story authors
