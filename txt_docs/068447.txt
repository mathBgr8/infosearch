Blade-30 fighter
1613

The Blade-30 fighter was a combat aircraft built by Tarrvin-on-Kallik on Adumar. The Blade-30 was distinguished primarily by having a two-seat cockpit, with a gunner handling the rear-firing laser cannons. Like the Blade-32, the Blade-30 could be upgraded with a fully pressurized cockpit, allowing it to serve as a starfighter as well as an atmospheric craft. The Blade-30 was less common than both the preceding Blade-28 and the succeeding Blade-32 and was used primarily by the Adumari forces. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Blade series models
