Bank parliament
1130

The Bank parliament was the central legislature of Gandle Ott of the Ott system, located in the Kathol sector of the Outer Rim Territories. Under Imperial control, they served in an advisory capacity to the Imperial Governor. They met with the crew of the FarStar in 8 ABY after the withdrawal of Imperial forces from the planet. However, realizing that they were not high-level officials, they requested a meeting with a genuine New Republic diplomat. *The DarkStryder Campaign Category:Legislatures
