Grejj
1388

Grejj served in the Imperial Army to the garrison on Vryssa as lieutenant. Sent out in command of an AT-AT to prevent Boba Fett from infiltrating the base, Grejj died when the bounty hunter cut communications and hijacked a second AT-AT under the control of Byrga.
