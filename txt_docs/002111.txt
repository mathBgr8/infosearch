Yuuzhan'tar (disambiguation)
535

The name Yuuzhan'tar has been used for two different locations. You may either be looking for: *The Original Yuuzhan'tar which was the homeworld of the Yuuzhan Vong species destroyed during the Cremlevian War. *The Yuuzhan'tar created on a captured Coruscant and terraformed to suit the Yuuzhan Vong conquerors during the Yuuzhan Vong War.
