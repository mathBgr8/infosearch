|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=32 BBYThe date of this story is established by in relation to , which The Essential Reader's Companion dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Republic |issue=14 }} Star Wars 14 is the fourteenth issue in the Star Wars: Republic series of comics. It was published on January 26, 2000 by Dark Horse Comics. Half of the Jedi Council has been sent to the world of Malastare - one of the last worlds to hold Podraces - to negotiate a peace treaty between two warring factions. But the whole affair is being manipulated by a secret, third party, out to assassinate the Jedi! This story arc features many familiar characters from The Phantom Menace. Witness the ongoing adventures that bridge the gap between Episodes I & II! As the Phoebos Memorial Run draws to a close with Sebulba as winner, Senator Aks Moe, the Red Iaro leader and a Ffib priest discuss a conspiracy to assassinate the Jedi leaders. Shortly after the Jedi arrive. On the way to their quarters, they realize they are being watched. Adi Gallia and Even Piell decide to stay behind and are promptly, attacked by the Ffib priest and the terrorist. When more Jedi arrive, the pair flee.
