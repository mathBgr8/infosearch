Korvit Domesa
897

Korvit Domesa was the Drama Editor for HoloNet News shortly before the outbreak of the Clone Wars. Among the plays Korvit reviewed was The Trickery of Vosdia Nooma, which was produced by Senator Onaconda Farr.
