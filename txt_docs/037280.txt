Odnalor Chull
1722

Odnalor Chull was a male Klatooinian thief who lived during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he was wanted by Kukul the Hutt for repeatedly stealing finished starships from Guarja Shipyards on Nal Hutta. He was killed by bounty hunter Jango Fett on Outland Transit Station, where he had fled to from Mos Gamos in the hopes of surviving Kukul's wrath. Desperate for money, he was working as a guard for crime boss Meeko Ghintee at the time of his final encounter with Fett.
