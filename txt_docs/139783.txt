Recondite
1895

Recondite was the personal Imperial-class Star Destroyer of Grand Admiral Nial Declann. It was presumably with him at the Battle of Endor. Dan Wallace originally named the Recondite in a segment of his Grand Admirals article that was cut, and is therefore unofficial. Recondite means hidden, and the ship was named to reflect Declann's moody nature. Category:Imperial-class Star Destroyers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
