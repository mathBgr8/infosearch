Ee
1491

The Ee were a sentient invertebrate species indigenous to Trilos, the moon of Atraken. All members of the Ee were adept in at least one type of mental power, which were divided into three categories; physical skills, environmental skills, and mental skills. One in ten Ee was adept in two categories; one in a hundred Ee were skilled in all three. Each category had a number of different skills.
