|other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=144 |isbn=1569716099 |isbn2= |timeline=22 BBY |series=Star Wars: Episode II — Attack of the Clones |issues= |preceded by=Episode I — The Phantom Menace hardcover |followed by=Episode III — Revenge of the Sith trade paperback }} Star Wars: Episode II — Attack of the Clones collects all four issues from the Star Wars: Episode II — Attack of the Clones comic adaptation series of comics. It was released on April 24, 2002. Dark Horse Comics proudly continues the comics adaptation of the long-awaited second Episode of the Star Wars saga. Adapted for comics by Henry Gilroy from an original screenplay by George Lucas and Jonathan Hales, this series tells of a young Jedi coming of age, forbidden love, and a threat that has been a decade in the planning&hellip; Artfully illustrated by Jan Duursema and Ray Kryssing, this four-issue series is a must for the serious Star Wars collector. The cover for this trade paperback was originally commissioned to be illustrated by artist Tsuneo Sanda before Dark Horse decided on using the movie poster by Drew Struzan.
