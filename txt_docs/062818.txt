Rimrunner (Gymsnor-4)
1254

The Rimrunner, a modified CorelliSpace Gymsnor-4 light freighter, was the personal ship of of the smuggler Salla Zend. The most notable modification was a pair of aft-firing concussion missile launchers, which provided a nasty surprise for any pursuing ship. The Rimrunner played an important role in the Battle of Nar Shaddaa, but was later destroyed during a botched Kessel Run. While racing against Han Solo's Millennium Falcon, Salla skirted too close to one of the Maw's black holes while attempting a micro-jump.
