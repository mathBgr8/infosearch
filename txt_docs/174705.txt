Sitrep
756

Within the Grand Army of the Republic, "Sitrep" (short for 'situation report') was the request for an immediate appraisal of the current situation as faced by the unit from which the report was requested. This term was mainly used by the Republic commandos, however other military forces likely used it as well. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero *Republic Commando: True Colors *Order 66: A Republic Commando Novel *Imperial Commando: 501st Category:Slang
