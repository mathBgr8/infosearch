Force call
1350

Force call was a powerful Force technique utilized by only a few Force-sensitives, due to the amount of energy required to use this power. Utilizing the Force, a Jedi could call other Jedi to his or her position; this amounted to a shout that echoed throughout the Force itself. For those who heard this summons, it would be nearly impossible to resist.
