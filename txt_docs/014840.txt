Loce
2772

Loce was a celestial bodyAlliance Intelligence Reports situated in the Loce system of Wild Space that shared its name with the sentient near-Human Locan species. It was the homeworld of the young Locan Talas Piran. At some point by 2 BBY,Alliance Intelligence Reports, which establishes that Talas Piran became enslaved at least four years prior to its setting, represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Piran was captured by enslavers and taken from Loce, eventually being sold to a Galactic Empire&ndash;sanctioned company for which he was forced to log trees on the world Hintivan II in the Outer Rim Territories' Corva sectors. &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Hintivan system Loce was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas subsequently placed the Loce system, and therefore the celestial body itself, in grid square S-4. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Astronomical objects in Wild Space
