Noble Sacrifice
658

The Noble Sacrifice was a Yuuzhan Vong warship which, when it came near Yuuzhan'tar, was destroyed on suspicion of harboring spies. The vessel's destruction was kept secret, however. In truth, it was ordered to be destroyed by Supreme Overlord Shimrra Jamaane, who believed that its passengers had knowledge of Zonama Sekot, whose existence Shimrra wished to keep secret. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic III: Reunion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Yuuzhan Vong starships
