Grivooga
3212

Grivooga was an oversized hydrospanner owned by the unsociable Ab'Ugartte Jak Sazz. Sazz named the hydrospanner and constantly talked to it, treating the inanimate object as a close friend. He carried Grivooga around with him constantly, with some beings joking and betting Sazz took it into the turboshower with him. Sazz often used Grivooga to batter his food, which he preferred to eat live, and to repair his weathered starship. One of Sazz's former crewmates harassed the Ab'Ugartte about his relationship with Grivooga, finding it humorous. Sazz beat the crewmate to death with Grivooga and cooked the being's remains for a meal, consequently deterring future crewmates from making similar remarks about Sazz's relationship with the hydrospanner. Sazz later claimed that Grivooga had no qualms about being used in such a manner. The Ab'Ugartte was later present with Grivooga at the Byss Bistro on the planet Byss in 10 ABY.Star Wars: The Comics Companion dates the events of Dark Empire 5 to ten years after the events of , which equates to 10 ABY according to The New Essential Chronology. Grivooga first appeared in the fifth issue of the Star Wars: Dark Empire comic book series. The issue was written by Tom Veitch, illustrated by Cam Kennedy, and published by Dark Horse Comics on August 18, 1992.
