It occupied the third orbital position around the binary stars Drup's Star and Drup's Companion in the Drup's Star system, a part of the Outer Rim Territories' Saijo sector. Drup III was mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
