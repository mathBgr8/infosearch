Droideka dispenser
1288

thumb|right|250px|[[Clone commando/Legends|Clone commando Boss encountering a Droideka dispenser.]] A Droideka dispenser was a large roughly cylindrical box made of durasteel that could both transport and dispense droidekas. These units were used by the Confederacy of Independent Systems against Republic forces during the Clone Wars to continually dispense a supply of droidekas. The clone commandos of Delta Squad encountered many of these units. Like the battle droid dispenser, it is unclear whether the units actually had the capacity to produce an unlimited number of droids. *Star Wars: Republic Commando *Star Wars: Republic Commando: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Confederacy military Category:Droid dispensers
