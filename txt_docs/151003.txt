Brawl and Grill
820

Brawl and Grill was an establishment located in Trader's Quarter on Ord Mantell. Callandri found Alton Lochner in Brawl and Grill and attempted to kill him. However, Lochner was saved by his friends Adazian Liebke and Tirranna. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Dining establishments Category:Ord Mantell locations
