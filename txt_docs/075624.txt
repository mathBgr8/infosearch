Universal Conscription Bill

The Universal Conscription Bill was an act passed by New Republic Senate in 25.5 ABY in response to the Battle of Ithor. It provided for widespread mandated drafting into the New Republic Defense Force during the Yuuzhan Vong's invasion of the galaxy. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:New Republic laws and acts
