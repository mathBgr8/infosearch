Coruscant Star of Valor
1040

The Coruscant Star of Valor was one of the first medals that was issued by the New Republic,The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia first struck in 7 ABY. It commemorated the participants of the Seizure of Coruscant and was declared to be the highest honor that could be given for valor by the developing government.
