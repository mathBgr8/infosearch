Desolous
3110

Desolous was a Sith Lord who was active in the galaxy long before the year 35 ABY.Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker: The Visual Dictionary dates the Resistance's discovery of the Sith Eternal and their legions of Sith troopers to one year after the Hosnian Cataclysm. Since the Hosnian Cataclysm is dated to the year 34 ABY by Star Wars: Galactic Atlas, the discovery of the Sith Eternal must therefore take place in 35 ABY. The Sith Eternal cult, which was active during that time, had a custom of naming their Sith trooper detachments after ancient Sith. The Desolous Legion was thus named after them&mdash;a name whose true significance remained a mystery to those outside of the cult. thumb|right|250px|Darth Desolous in the Star Wars Legends continuity Desolous was first mentioned in the current Star Wars canon in Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker: The Visual Dictionary, a reference book published on December 20, 2019 and serving as a companion for the film Star Wars: Episode IX The Rise of Skywalker.
