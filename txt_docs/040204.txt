Kaalah-Nah
2321

Kaalah-Nah was a diminutive yet knowledgeable Chadra-Fan information broker on Nar Shaddaa. She met Meetra Surik near the Pazaak Den, into which she was not permitted. She offered to put a bad word in to the Exchange for Surik, for the right price. Kaalah-Nah eventually helped out T3-M4 by selling him to Vogga claiming she won him in a Pazaak game. Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords: Prima Official Game Guide incorrectly classifies Kaalah-Nah as a Snivvian.
