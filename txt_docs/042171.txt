Ingey
1840

Ingey was a small, white feline animal identified as a Tessellated Arboreal binjinphant. It was owned by a young boy named Prince Coby of Tammuz-an. Ingey had a playful demeanor and once caused a ruckus by scurrying across the shoulders of the protocol droid, C-3PO. The animal's presence caused the droid to topple backwards, upsetting a tray full of food. When Coby was nine years old, he was enrolled in the Imperial Academy. Pilot Jann Tosh escorted the boy to a space port on Chuzalla. A group of brigands known as the Starhunter's Intergalactic Menagerie abducted the rare and valuable Ingey, and brought him to Dandelo.
