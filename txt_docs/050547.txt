Sal Ghator
1081

Sal Ghator was a male Yuuzhan Vong who was living in the mixed class village of La'okio on Zonama Sekot. A former subaltern, Sal was caught by the Extolled Bava, stealing out of the Extolled's crops, who brought this information to Tahiri Veila.
