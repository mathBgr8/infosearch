Randoni
1350

The Randoni were Humans native to the planet Randon,The Essential Atlas known as merchants and traders . One could distinguish Randoni merchants from others by their rough-woven garb and loose hairstyle. Female ward-cousins were greatly highly honored due to their potential inheritance. It is also likely that the Randoni were the origin of Randoni Yellow Plague, a drink. Early in their spacefaring history, the first Randoni merchants and traders would deposit their treasures in hidden vaults. In 22 ABY, Tionne Solusar came across an ancient song which told about that event and where the treasures were hidden.
