Lowtown District

thumb|Lowtown District on a map Lowtown District was one of the areas of Corint City, the planetary capital of Pirik. Lowtown District was the seedier area of the city, including most of the criminal life, cantinas, bars and probably the base of an Alliance to Restore the Republic unit, Shroud Team.
