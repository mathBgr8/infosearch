Kaachtari
1365

Kaachtari was an active volcano on the planet Haariden. In 26 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise to 25 BBY. The novel indicates that the volcano erupted a year before its events, equating to 26 BBY. it experienced a massive eruption, in the midst of a world-wide civil war. *Jedi Quest: The Master of Disguise *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Haariden locations Category:Volcanoes
