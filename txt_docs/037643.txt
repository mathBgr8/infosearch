DFS-1138
1635

DFS-1138 was a Vulture-class starfighter assigned to the Saak'ak. When Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi arrived onboard, DFS-1138 was present in the hangar. Later, when the Jedi's ship was destroyed, DFS-1138 was seen fleeing the hangar with his fellow droid fighters. DFS-1138 is one of many references throughout Star Wars media to George Lucas' first feature film, THX 1138. Category:Vulture-class starfighters
