Draklor
1857

Draklor was a Star Galleon-class frigate serving in the Imperial Navy under Grand Admiral Thrawn. The ship was dispatched by the Grand Admiral as part of the Death's Head to ferry Joruus C'baoth and General Freja Covell from his flagship, the Star Destroyer Chimaera to the Mount Tantiss base on Wayland. *The Last Command *The Last Command Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Star Galleon-class frigates Category:Starships of Thrawn's fleet
