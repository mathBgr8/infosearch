Ta-Ree
1375

thumb|[[Yapi using Ta-Ree]] Ta-Ree refers to a manifestation of the Force that was located exclusively in the Kathol sector. After the Kathol Rift disaster that forced the ancient Kathol to enter the Lifewell in order to survive, the very fabric of space and of the Force itself were twisted in the region. This allowed many beings native to the sector to use this 'spirit energy' and thus have Force powers. However, this form of the Force was only usable within the Kathol sector under most circumstances, and when the Kathol were freed from servitude they made sure that the Ta-Ree energy would never be usable again, but some managed to keep the Ta-Ree energy.
