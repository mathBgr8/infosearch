Isolation Sector
879

The Isolation Sector was the area of the planet Radnor that was affected by the planet's deadly bioplague. The city-state of Aubendo was located in the Isolation Sector. The city-state of Tacto was located in the Clear Sector when the bioplague first struck Aubendo. *Jedi Quest: The Way of the Apprentice Category:Planetary regions and territories Category:Radnor locations
