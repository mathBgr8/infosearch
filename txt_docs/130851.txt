Si-shae-ral
518

Si-shae-ral was an Eickarie tribe. Many of their nobles and elders were taken prisoner by the Warlord in order to keep the tribe and its allies subservient to him, though they would be freed by the 501st Legion and their Prince Ha-ran in the Battle of Kariek, where many would aide in the capture of the Warlord. *Fool's Bargain Category:Eickaries
