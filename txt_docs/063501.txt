Thyferran Home Defense Corps
1815

The Thyferran Home Defense Corps was a defense force founded by the warlord Ysanne Isard during her rule of the planet Thyferra. Built from components from the Imperial Navy and Army, including TIE/IN interceptors, plus volunteers from the Xucphra Corporation, the Home Defense Force was intended to protect Isard's control of the bacta-producing world during the Bacta War, during which the corps was led by Erisi Dlarit.
