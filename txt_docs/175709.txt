Hilo (BFF-1 bulk freighter)
1712

Hilo was a BFF-1 bulk freighter belonging to the Galactic Empire. Hilo was part of the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter's vital resupply convoy lead by the Nebulon-B Frigate Monitor. The ship's cargo was durasteel. Hilo was attacked by Rebel raiders and was destroyed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
