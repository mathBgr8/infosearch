Polyhedron 97
939

Polyhedron 97 was a roleplaying magazine from July of 1994. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called "Adversaries: Characters for the Star Wars: New Republic Campaign" and was written by Bill Slavicsek and Michele Carter. Category:Polyhedron
