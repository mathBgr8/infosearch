Star Wars: Episode I - Queen Amidala Paper Doll Book
1976

|type=Paperback |pages=6 |dimensions=0.2 x 9.2 x 12.0 inches |weight=3.2 ounces |isbn=0375800204 |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Star Wars: Episode I - Queen Amidala Paper Doll Book contains a paper doll of Queen Amidala that can be dressed in several of her costumes from Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. A similar book, the Star Wars: Attack of the Clones - Padmé Amidala Paper Doll Book, was published three years later. Make part of Star Wars: Episode I come alive just for you! Queen Amidala of Naboo is a brave and beautiful ruler whose exotic costumes provide her with an air of regal mystery. Inside this book you'll find a collection of the Queen's most gorgeous gowns, each one more richly detailed than the next. Now you can act as her handmaiden. Punch out her entire wardrobe and dress the Queen as you wish. Then, with an adult's help, remove the staples at the spine of this book and pull out the pages. The inside covers will provide a colorful backdrop of the Queen's Theed palace! *Star Wars: Episode I Insider's Guide Category:Legends Young Readers
