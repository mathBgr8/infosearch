CMC-A14 mining facility
2702

The CMC-A14 mining facility was designed by the Corellia Mining Corporation as a mostly automated platform that would mine minerals, ores and other valuable substances from a variety of common asteroid types. Using large spinning extractors, pumps and holding silos for its mining operations, it also had a large landing pad for collection of processed materials by cargo craft for transport off the facility. A small main habitable area housed crew, but the facility was almost completely automated with minimal need for sentient intervention or control in normal operations. Corellia Mining Corporation sold the CMC-A14 design to many different groups including the Galactic Empire, Rebel Alliance and Zann Consortium under a deal in which CMC would receive a portion of any profits made from their operation.
