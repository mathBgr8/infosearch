It was released by Dark Horse Comics on December 19, 2007. According to Jeremy Barlow, it would be "the last one for a while, at least until we decide how we're going to treat the new CGI animated series." Loved by Star Wars fans of all ages, Clone Wars Adventures returns with four more fantastic tales!
