Lonatran Zoo
676

Lonatran Zoo was a huge biological reserve maintained by the Gree on the planet Lonatro. It covered huge portions of the planet's surface and was home to curators who watched over species that were extinct on other Gree Enclave worlds and many naturally evolved species.
