Cooha
2272

|diet=Noli nestlings |language= }} Coohas were flightless, predatory avians native to Gandeid IV, a temperate, low-gravity planet in the Gandeid system. They were up to a meter and a half tall and covered in feathers. They had a large head in comparison to their lanky body, with a thin neck connecting them; their beaks were filled with small, slashing teeth. Coohas had two thin wings that ended in a long, sharp talon, and two long, thin legs that each ended in a two-toed, taloned foot. The cooha primarily fed on the flightless nestlings of noli, a species that, as adults, had powerful beaks and claws. The Cooha's slashing teeth were used to defend it from adults when it raided noli nests.
