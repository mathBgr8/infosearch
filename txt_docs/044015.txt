Plug-8G 927 Cluster Array engine
1927

The Plug-8G 927 Cluster Array engine was a Podracer engine produced by Vokoff-Strood prior to the Invasion of Naboo. Ark Roose used the Plug-8G 927 engine during the Boonta Eve Classic won by Anakin Skywalker.
