Blackmoon Eleven
748

Blackmoon Eleven was a designation used by a pilot within Blackmoon Squadron. As of 27 ABY, Blackmoon Eleven was Koril Bekam. He was in a bacta tank during the evacuation of Borleias, however, and Wedge Antilles used his fighter to escape the world. Its comm unit was damaged such that it could broadcast its transponder signal, but no voice traffic.
