Xroi
936

Xroi was a group of 2 BFF-1 bulk freighters used by the Rebel Alliance. The two freighters were part of a convoy including 3 CR90 corvettes called Qiluiy in the Mennaalii system which came under attack by pirates aboard the frigate Scorpion.
