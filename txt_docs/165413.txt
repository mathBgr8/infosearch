establishes that the "Wanted by Cracken" segment of the Star Wars Adventure Journal represents an in-universe update to the datafile Wanted by Cracken, the original version of which is presented in the sourcebook Wanted by Cracken. The latter work is dated to three years after the Battle of Endor, and since The New Essential Chronology dates that conflict to 4 ABY, the placement of the sourcebook therefore translates to 7 ABY. They were assassinated by the former Rebel Alliance sharpshooter Ma'w'shiye&mdash;who had deserted&mdash;using a gun sometime between 4 ABY and 7 ABY. Ma'w'shiye's assassination of Candra was later noted by the New Republic General Airen Cracken in the traitor's entry of the datafile Wanted by Cracken&mdash;the initial version of which was published in 7 ABY. Candra was first mentioned in the "Wanted by Cracken" segment of the November 1994 fourth issue of West End Games' Star Wars Adventure Journal. The segment was authored by C. Robert Carey.
