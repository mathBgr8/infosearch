RQ 550C Dart
2152

The RQ 550C Dart was a small Podracer used by the Podracing champ "Bullseye" Navior. The Dart Podracer and its pilot were originally created for the 1999 film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The Podracer made it as far as the film's animatic stages, which are view-able on the DVD special features of The Phantom Menace.
