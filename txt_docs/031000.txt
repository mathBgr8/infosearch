Adela was a starship owned by Refka Trell. It was registered on Elom with registration number RN32-000439. In 16 ABY, this was one of many ships Luke Skywalker tracked as it passed the inner flight control buoys of the planet Teyr. *Shield of Lies Category: Personal starships Category:Starships
