Bob Carrau
2959

Bob Carrau is a writer most notable in Star Wars for writing the screenplay for the 1984 television film Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure, and for writing the teleplays for some episodes of the 1985&ndash;1987 Ewoks animated television series. Carrau was working for George Lucas as a personal assistant when he got the job to write Caravan of Courage.Empire (UK edition). September 2015 issue, page 102. "Endor's Game" by Owen Williams. He was one of the writers who helped Lucasfilm develop a television adaptation of the video game Maniac Mansion.Miller, Bob (1988). "Saturday Morning 'Star Wars.
