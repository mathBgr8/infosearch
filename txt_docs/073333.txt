Council Center
707

The Council Center was a building in the Agamarian city of Calna Muun. It contained the Agamarian Council. It was done completely in wood and resembled an ocean; the councilors' benches looked like waves, and the speaker's podium resembled a stone washed up from the sea. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught Category:Agamar locations Category:Buildings
