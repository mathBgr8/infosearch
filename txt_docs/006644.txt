Troy Denning
3831

Troy Denning, also known as Richard Awlinson, is a former game designer and author with an efflux of Star Wars novels. He has written over twenty novels, including the New York Times bestseller Waterdeep. The majority of his published writing has been fiction set in the Dungeons and Dragons settings of Dark Sun and Forgotten Realms. Troy Denning occasionally posts on the official Star Wars forums under his name, and on theforce.net forums under TailOne.
