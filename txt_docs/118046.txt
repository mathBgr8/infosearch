thumb|right|250px|[[Anakin Skywalker/Legends|Anakin Skywalker and Aayla Secura repair a hermit crab droid on Kamino.]] Hermit crab droids were small, crablike droids with six legs and two stalked eyes. In 22 BBY, Anakin Skywalker attempted to fix one of these droids that was found on Kamino during the Jedi briefing before the upcoming battle, but Aayla Secura showed Skywalker that the droid simply wasn't turned on. *Republic 50 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid models
