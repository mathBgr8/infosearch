Lungrot
681

Lungrot was a disease contracted on Coruscant. The Rhinnal State Medical Academy released a report of middle-class districts on Coruscant that identified 10 species under the risk of being affected with diseases from the pollution, including Abinyshi, Bovorian, Chadra-Fan, Mriss, Mrlssi, Pa'lowick, Quor'sav, Rishii, Shashay, and Vors.
