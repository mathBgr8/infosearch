Waita Tar dance
1001

The Waita Tar dance was a celebratory ritual dance performed by the Wookees on Yavin. The dance involved torches, yodeling and the females moving in a circle around the males. After Annikin Starkiller impressed the Wookees with his martial prowess, he was welcomed that night by a Waita Tar dance. The Wookees were renamed "Jawas" in the first draft revision, though this was later reversed.
