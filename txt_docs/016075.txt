OrulShai III
1025

OrulShai III was a moon of Gyosha in the Galactic Core. It supported Humans ruled by the Galactic Empire. Wilek Nereus was a native of the moon. Sena Pakkallo once treated him for a parasitic disorder on the moon. *The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook Category:Galactic Empire moons Category:Moons in the Core Worlds
