Terence McGovern
3034

Terence Sean "Terry" McGovern is an American actor, voice actor, television broadcaster, radio personality, and acting instructor who looped the dialogue of several stormtroopers, and provided cantina walla, in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. His most notable dialogue is during the Jedi mind trick scene outside the cantina, uttering the famous phrase "These aren't the droids we are looking for" repeating Alec Guinness. McGovern subsequently provided voices for a number of the LucasArts Star Wars video games.
