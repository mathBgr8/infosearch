Firespray DL-87 stun rifle
1445

The BlasTech Industries Firespray DL-87 stun rifle was an unusual blaster rifle. It was primarily designed to knock out opponents rather than killing them. It had a wide muzzle, with two transceptors mounted in the corners of the barrel.
