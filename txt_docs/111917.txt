Nahra
606

Nahra was a Kaminoan form of ballet in which the species used their bodies to show emotions typically withheld. Using Nahra, Kaminoans mimed their analog of the emotion; outside viewers considered the dance to be somewhat disturbing, due to the Kaminoans' almost total lack of emotion understanding. *Republic Commando: Hard Contact *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dance Category:Kaminoan culture
