As a child, Nell traveled with her gun-runner parents, learning all about the smuggling trade and making a few business contacts along the way. Since she was a member of a smuggling family, she was expected to help out in many ways like running their light freighter. When she was old enough, her parents asked her to go out on her own and make something of herself according to the traditional coming-of-age protocol for Wroonians. Nell apprenticed herself to a Wroonian mercenary and quickly learned the ropes.
