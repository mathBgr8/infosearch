|figures=60 |cards= |isbn=0-7869-3748-6 |upc= |timeline= |preceded by= |followed by= }} Clone Strike was the second set released for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Miniatures game. Released on December 13, 2004, it featured 60 figures from the prequel trilogy era. As with its predecessor, Rebel Storm, miniatures could be purchased in a booster pack of 7 random minis, or in a starter set, containing 8 random minis, as well as General Kenobi and Jango Fett, an updated rulebook, and a map (Muunilinst).
