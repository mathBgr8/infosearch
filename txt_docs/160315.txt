Bastion Defense
752

Bastion Defense was the defense fleet that was assigned to protect Bastion when the emperor-in-exile Roan Fel made it his headquarters. The fleet consisted mainly of Pellaeon-class Star Destroyers and planetary turbolasers placed on the surface of the planet.
