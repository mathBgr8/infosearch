William Morgan Sheppard
2214

William Morgan Sheppard (August 24, 1932 &ndash; January 6, 2019) was a British actor who voiced the Heavy Gun Trooper and the Ruulian Strip Miner in Star Wars: Force Commander. He also voiced the character Ignatius Cheese in the 2000 LucasArts game Escape from Monkey Island. In 2011, he appeared in the sixth series of the new series of Doctor Who in the episode The Impossible Astronaut. He is one of several actors to have appeared in both Star Trek and Star Wars, playing Ira Graves in Star Trek: The Next Generation, the Klingon Commandant in Star Trek VI: The Undiscovered Country, Qatai in Star Trek: Voyager and the Vulcan science minister in the 2009 film Star Trek.
