Fairweld Casino
914

Fairweld Casino was a luxury gambling establishment located in the Commercial district of Estalle Island on Procopia in the Tapani sector. It was a palatial building with numerous gambling halls that catered for the elite of the Tapani sector. Private chambers were available on the upper level for private parties and high-stake games of chance such as sabacc.
