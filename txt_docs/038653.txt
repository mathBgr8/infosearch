thumb|right|Netus, Minister of Defense during the [[Great Sith War/Legends|Great Sith War]] The Minister of Defense was the title used under the Galactic Republic for the civilian head of the Republic Military and the Ministry of Defense. During the Great Sith War, Netus held the position.
