Roolek clan
1853

The Roolek clan was a Rodian clan on the planet Rodia. The clan was decimated during the Rodian Clan Wars were they fought savagely against the Chattza Clan; as a result, the Chattza Navik the Red ordered to exterminate them.Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide By the Galactic Civil War, few clan members survived that genocide attempt and went into hiding on Rodia, keeping their identities secret, open only to their allied clans. Most of them escaped offworld, lest Navik sought them out such as he did with the Tetsu Clan; those would imemdiately attack against any Chattza clan member they would meet on another planet. A small groups of those that stayed behind, were hiding in Iskaayuma, and the Rodmark Weapons Plant operated secretly as a stronghold. The bounty hunter Inntak once hired a group of mercenaries to help capture any Rooleks that would attend the last show of the Rodian Galactic Drama Troupe's tour. Three groups arrived on speeders to watch the show, ten beings in total; after its end, they returned to the factory, followed by the hunters. *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Rodian clans
