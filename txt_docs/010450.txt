S-1 FireHawke Heavy Repulsortank
3125

The S-1 FireHawke Heavy Repulsortank was a tank design used extensively by the Imperial Army. It had armament similar to the Imperial AT-AT. The tank's primary weapon was a turret-mounted heavy laser cannon with a maximum range of five kilometers. This was backed up by a medium blaster cannon with a maximum range of one kilometer. It had a crew of five and enough room for at least one passenger.Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal The Hell's Hammers used this repulsortank in their elite armor unit as well as having its image as part of their unit insignia.
