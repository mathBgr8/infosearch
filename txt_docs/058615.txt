Huutar
1025

Huutar was a Human male who worked as a mechanic on the world of Corellia. He was once hired by a smuggler who owned a YT-1300 freighter; the price for Huutar's services was set at one hundred credits per day. Category:Corellians Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Mechanics
