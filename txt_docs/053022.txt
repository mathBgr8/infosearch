Brolf
1547

Brolfi, singular Brolf, were a species of furry sentients who inhabited the planet of Barlok. Their skin, referred to as hornskin, was colored between different shades of green and yellow, and quivered when the Brolf was feeling emotional. They also had stumpy legs.
