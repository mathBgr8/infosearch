Elin Roe
1519

Elin Roe was a Colonies celestial body in the Elin Roe system, which was patrolled by Hallyn Phlynne during the Galactic Civil War. It had at least five moons, the fifth of which was bombarded with radioactive dust after the Krish mechanic Maniac had tinkered with the hyperdrive of the Yagaran Four's freighter; when the starship attempted to enter hyperspace, it exploded. *Cracken's Rebel Operatives Category:Astronomical objects in the Colonies Category:Terrestrial astronomical objects
