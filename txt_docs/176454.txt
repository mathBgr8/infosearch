Gilded Rebellion

"Gilded Rebellion" was a planned West End Games adventure for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. Set in the Tapani sector, it would have covered the aftermath of that sector's annexation by the Galactic Empire and opportunities for rebellion among the Tapani nobility in the Empire's final days. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Canceled West End Games adventures
