Farrow bird
908

The Farrow bird was indigenous to Aruza. A flying predator, the avian would use its unique bioluminescent chest to blind or stun small airborne mammals long enough to make an easy catch over the planet's valleys. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Birds Category:Creatures of Aruza Category:Creatures of unspecified diet Category:Species of undefined sentience
