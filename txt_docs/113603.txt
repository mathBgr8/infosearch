MREM-02
1093

MREM-02, or Moreemohtwo was a kitchen droid owned by Corpo the Hutt. When Corpo enslaved famous chef Slabba Drewl, Jedi Masters Micah Giiett and Plo Koon came to his rescue. Corpo then bet Drewl in a cooking contest with MREM-02. While Giiett distracted the audience, Koon swapped the ingredients. When Micah's Squirmer Stew won the competition, Corpo started hitting Moreemohtwo, causing it to fall over. Category:Cooking droids Category:Droids with masculine programming
