Nikki Vul
1476

Nikki Vul was a female Human who worked as a HoloNet news reporter near the end of the Clone Wars. Vul reported on the Battle of Coruscant in a HoloNet item broadcast across the galaxy, in which she revealed the outcome of the battle and praised its heroes, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker.
