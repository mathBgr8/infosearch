*Rated 36 RU |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation=*Galactic Empire *Alliance to Restore the Republic *Various private, commercial, and pirate interests }} The Class-A cargo container was a brick-shaped freight container manufactured by Xizor Transport Systems. The container was designed to store cargo during transit in realspace and hyperspace. The BFF-1 bulk freighter utilized four of these containers&mdash;a pair on both port and starboard&mdash;for their cargo storage.
