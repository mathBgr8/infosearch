Jedi (duuvhal)
1134

Jedi was the name of Valara Saar's second pet Duuvhal. Force Adept Valara Saar had left Yashuvhu to try to become a Jedi, but then found the Order too restrictive for her. She was then accompanied by her first pet Duuvhal, Zeev After the violent death of Zeev on the planet Aargau, Valara Saar decided to stop wandering through the galaxy to return to her native world of Yashuvhu. There, she took a second pet Duuvhal, to whom she called Jedi. She would became a Prophetess, a Jedi teacher and a defender of her people from her hut in the Yashaka Mountains.
