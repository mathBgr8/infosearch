thumb|300px|[[Columi/Legends|Columi crime lord Boss Tosk attacking an individual with the help of his Repulsorsuit and his two assassin droids]] A repulsor suit was a type of clothing used for locomotion by the Columi. It could be controlled through mental commands, and allowed the Columi to move without hindrance, and provided them with a measure of extra strength. The crime lord Boss Tosk used a repulsor suit to move around. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Repulsorlift technology
