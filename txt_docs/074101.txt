Pendarran Warriors
1571

The Pendarran Warriors were a military group led by Gendal Delan, who worked closely with the Jedi Knights and Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. The Pendarran Warriors fought at the Battle of Dreighton. During the Great Jedi Purge, the Pendarran Warriors were wiped out by Emperor Palpatine's Imperial troops.
