Cargeloch
1819

Air Marshal Cargeloch was a Human male who served in the Galactic Empire. Cargeloch was responsible for both leading the starfighter wing assigned to the airspace around Imperial Palace and instructing at the Imperial Navy base on Coruscant. The recruits there frequently joked that he slept in his uniform&mdash;their jocular frivolity belying recurrent nightmares of the Air Marshal crinkling the leather on his black flight gloves before slapping the recruits across the face for incompetence in the TIE simulators.
