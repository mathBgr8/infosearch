Spear (frigate)
1557

The Spear was a DP20 gunship that saw service with the Rebel Alliance Fleet during the Galactic Civil War. It was usually seen escorting the Defiance Task Force, and carried a cargo of tauntauns. It managed to escape with the rest of the fleet after Rebel pilots eliminated their pursuers after the disastrous Battle of Hoth. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Defiance Task Force ships Category:DP20 gunships
