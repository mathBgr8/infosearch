Liekas Tendo
1294

Liekas Tendo was a Morath mining engineer hostage. Tendo was taken by the Yevetha during the attack. Along with the other hostages taken during the Yevethan Purge, Tendo was forced to record a message begging the New Republic not to fire on Yevethan ships carrying prisoners. The Yevetha played the messages during the Battle of Doornik-319, causing hesitation from the New Republic ships and forcing them to withdraw.
