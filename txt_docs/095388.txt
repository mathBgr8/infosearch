Old Republic (CR90 corvette)
2027

Old Republic was a CR90 corvette in the Rebel Alliance fleet that fought in the Battle of Endor. In the aftermath of the battle, the Old Republic was destroyed in an encounter with an Imperial system patrol craft (believed to be the Daggerblade). Although it was more heavily armed, Old Republic was unable to consistently hit the Imperial ship. Its drive was disabled, and the Old Republic could do nothing but stand by while the Imperial ship destroyed the sensor drones the Alliance had placed on the edges of the Endor system.
