Pinnace
1584

A pinnace was a fast, short-range starship that was carried aboard a larger vessel. Pinnaces were typically slightly larger than starfighters and had some cargo space; as a result, they were deployed in combat and supply roles. The Ulig Abaha Ltd. #2 Warpod was a pinnace.
