Corint City
1747

Corint City was the planetary capital of Pirik, possibly named after the Corintium ore mined on the planet. It was the base of the Rebel infiltrator Shroud Team, and they spread chaos throughout the galaxy from there. It was 14 kilometers from the Uloitir Sea, and the Jalor Docking Facilities were just outside the city, serving as the main starport for the planet. It was divided into districts, including Overtown District, which was the seat of the government and its center; Talamp Industrial Sector, the industrial section; Lafarn Residential District, the main commercial and residential area; Lowtown District, the seedier section including most of the criminal life; and Strak Tower.
