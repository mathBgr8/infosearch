Universal transceiver package
2205

The Carbanti United Electronics universal transceiver package was a sensor package used on the New Republic's X-wing starfighter. It was comprised of several standard sensor units, including a short-range target-acquiring sensor, an active long-range sensor array, a dedicated energy receptor, and an electro photo receptor. Interface ports also accepted supplemental sensors, making it one of the most flexible and easily upgraded starfighter array systems.
