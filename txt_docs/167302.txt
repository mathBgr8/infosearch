|illustrator=Tim Eldred |editor= |publisher=West End Games |release date=February 1994Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 |pages=9 |published in=Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 |reprinted in= }} "Glah Ubooki's Strange & Wondrous Imports" is an article written by Shane Hensley with illustrations by Tim Eldred. It was published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 in February 1994 by West End Games. This article serves as a supplementary piece for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, introducing Glah Ubooki, a Bimm who, a decade before, stumbled upon an Imperial R&D ship that been had wrecked on Bimmisaari. He managed to sell the prototype weapons and equipment to Rebel agents.
