Benni
901

Benni was a young pack tracker at the Kuat Passenger Port. Using a repulsorcart, he would offer his services as a porter to passengers moving through the space station. Extremely knowledgable about the Port and its working, Benni was friendly and chatty with his customers. *Platt's Starport Guide Category:Humans Category:Males
