Hugo
1590

The Hugo was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by Overlord Ghorin to be part of a convoy to deliver grain to the Imperials. Unfortunately the freighter, along with the Grotius, were disabled along with the rest of the convoy by Rebel Y-wings. They were then boarded by Rebel freighters intending to swap grain cargos in order to discredit Ghorin as a traitor. *Star Wars: X-Wing Tour of Duty: Imperial Pursuit *X-Wing Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
