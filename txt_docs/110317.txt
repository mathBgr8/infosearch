Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuit
1380

thumb|right|A Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuit Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuits were amoeboid creatures created when a TaggeCo chef placed a new form of cuisine, Bantha Breakfast Biscuits into an autochef. The Biscuit Baron preservatives bonded with the bantha genetic material in the meat to create this creature, which promptly consumed the chef. After this incident, TaggeCo restricted shipping of Bantha Breakfast Biscuits to various planets which they claimed were "inadequately prepared to delight in this yummy breakfast delicacy." The Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuits could also mutate in other conditions, such as when the preservatives came into contact with stale ship air and certain environmental conditions. This is what happened to the natives on the planet Gastrula. Giant Amorphous Bantha Breakfast Biscuits were considered for use in biological warfare by the Galactic Empire. The only known way to destroy one of these creatures was by using blue sauce, a popular condiment served with Bantha Breakfast Biscuits. Category:Biscuits and cookies Category:Breakfast foods
