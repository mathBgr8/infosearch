Number Two Honor Squad
1577

Number Two Honor Squad was a squad of 6 Chiss warriors who had been stationed aboard Chaf Envoy to serve as part of an honor guard for the vessel's journey to Outbound Flight in 22 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Survivor's Quest to 22 ABY. *Survivor's Quest Category:Chiss military units
