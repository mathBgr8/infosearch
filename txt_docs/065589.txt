U Wanna Wanna
759

U Wanna Wanna was Mos Eisley's biggest outdoor market, where food and craft could be purchased. Barring when Tatooine's gravel storms reached magnitudes of four or above, it was always open. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Markets Category:Mos Eisley locations
