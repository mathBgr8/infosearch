Reez Andor
3536

Reez Andor was a male Human criminal. By 32 BBY,The events of Star Wars: Bounty Hunter take place in 32 BBY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Andor was wanted dead or alive by Coruscant Police for being a known death stick dealer. His bounty noted his homeworld was unknown but that his last known location was the Uscru Entertainment District on the planet Coruscant, offering a sum of 2,500 credits if he was captured alive or 1,250 if captured dead. It also stated that he was affiliated with fellow death stick dealer Jervis Gloom and that he may be armed. Andor wore a grey eye piece and clothes of varied shades of grey. His shoes were white and his belt had a pink oval in the center. Andor was bald and had tan skin with brown facial hair. While the Mandalorian bounty hunter Jango Fett was hunting for Gloom on Coruscant, Fett encountered Andor waiting for an air taxi in the Entertainment District. Fett captured Andor alive and collected the 2,500 credits. Reez Andor appeared in the 2002 video game Star Wars: Bounty Hunter developed by LucasArts. In the game, Andor is the tenth optional secondary bounty that the player can capture in the Coruscant Entertainment District and is labeled as the thirty-fifth bounty overall. Andor is able to be captured by the player for a higher sum of credits or killed for a lower amount.
