Brillstow
1257

Commander Brillstow was an officer aboard Reprisal in 0.5 ABY. When the Hand of Judgment fled from the Imperial Security Bureau, Daric LaRone contacted him. LaRone simulated Drelfin's voice so that Brillstow believed he was an ISB officer. Knowing that insubordination would bring him into big trouble, he marked LaRone's words and brought the Reprisal out of the hyperspace. *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Humans Category:Imperial Navy officers of the Galactic Empire Category:Males
