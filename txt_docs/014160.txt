Through Ban-Satir II ran the Tingel Route hyperspace lane, which connected the planet with the worlds of Issagra and Gaurick. A lush but dull agriworld, Ban-Satir II is famous for its Novanian grog, an expensive and potent intoxicant. It is produced and marketed from the isle of N'van in the planet's northern hemisphere. The best Norvanian vintages are reserved for the Authority's elite, making Norvanian Reserve a popular cargo for smugglers.
