Siege of an Imperial Galactic Rim shipyard
2568

The siege of an Imperial Galactic Rim shipyard took place during the Operation Shadow Hand in 10 ABY The siege involved a massive New Republic force besieging a great Imperial shipyard under control of the Dark Empire located in the Galactic Rim. The troopship Pelagia was scheduled to rendezvous with an X-wing flight group based in the Ottega system and then proceed to aid the invasion force. The ship and its passengers was destroyed when it was hit by a missile shot from the Galaxy Gun. *Empire's End audio drama *Empire's End 1 Category:Battles of Operation Shadow Hand
