Flangth
1466

Flangth was a kind of food. It was the primary offering of the Flangth-2-Go restaurant chain,Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook and one of the dishes available in the mess at the Rebel base on Arbra.Star Wars (1977) 62 Young Klaus Vandangante found flangth to be rather unpalatable. During the Galactic Civil War, a group of privateers from the Far Orbit were to meet an agent of the Rebel Alliance at Lean Nuuti Bar and Grill on StarForge Station. The group were to identify themselves to the agent by ordering flangth and ootoowergs with Shasa ale. *Star Wars (1977) 62 *Dark Empire Sourcebook *Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook Category:Fast food
