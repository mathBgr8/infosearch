NB-1T Royal Bomber
2319

The Naboo NB-1T Royal Bomber was the successor of the NB-1S Royal Bomber. The NB-1T Royal Bomber was a bomber used by the Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps and incorporated a separate seat for the bombardier. The Naboo Royal Space Fighter Corps preferred Human judgment over that of targeting computers. In an emergency situation when the pilot was knocked out, the bombardier could transfer piloting functions to his station. The craft was equipped with proton bombs.
