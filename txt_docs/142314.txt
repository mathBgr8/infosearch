Harlan Ottekvar
1001

}} Harlan Ottekvar was to portray Lord Baltharog in the holofilm adaptation of the book Quest for Quasar, in 22 BBY. He was criticized by fans of the original work, particularly Groz "QuasarKing327" Niclari. Reasons included Ottekvar being too short to portray Baltharog (1.84 meters, and 1.86 meters in boots) and that he was too famous.
