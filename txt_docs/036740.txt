Several titles with the name Ewok or Ewoks have been used throughout Star Wars: *Ewok, a sentient creature *Ewok, a sentient creature *Ewoks, a 1985 cartoon series *Star Wars: Ewoks, a comic book series loosely based on the above *Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure, a telefilm starring Ewoks *Ewoks: The Battle for Endor, sequel to the above *Ewoks, the opening theme song from the first season of Ewoks *Ewoks, the soundtrack to the above two films *Return of the Ewok, a mockumentary made by Warwick Davis *Star Wars: Ewoks, a canon comic book series
