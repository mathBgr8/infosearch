Corellian governor-general
1185

An unknown governor-general was installed over Corellia by the New Republic following the ouster of Thrackan Sal-Solo in 28 ABY, during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Sometime after the end of the war in 29 ABY, the Galactic Alliance removed the governor-general from Corellia as a reward for the system supporting the Alliance's war effort.
