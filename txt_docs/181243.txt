Akh'laht
1386

}} Akh'laht was a Noghri of Clan Kihm'bar and one of the Noghri Death Commandos. After the Empire's deception to the Noghri was revealed, he informed Rukh, the bodyguard of Grand Admiral Thrawn, of this revelation. Rukh then promised to make the Grand Admiral pay, which he did, during the Battle of Bilbringi. *The Last Command Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Noghri
