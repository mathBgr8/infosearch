Kath subjugation
2179

The Kath subjugation was the conquest of the ocean-covered world Kath II by the Galactic Empire. It used a number of the then-new Aquadon CAVa 400 aquatic attack vehicles to make underwater attacks against the forces of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, who had taken up positions within the Karm Trenches.
