|release date=November 1978A Galaxy Far, Far Away: Exploring Star Wars Comics |pages=63 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Star Wars (Marvel UK) |issue= |next=Star Wars Annual 1979 }} Star Wars Annual No. 1 was a hardcover comic book released in the United Kingdom in 1978 that collected the Star Wars Marvel UK series of comic books of the adaptation of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. The story was first published in the UK in the Star Wars Weekly comic series. A year later, the book was followed up with Star Wars Annual 1979. Category:Legends comic annuals
