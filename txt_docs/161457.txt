Loa Tibeeme
1247

Loa Tibeeme was a Rodian slicer who had been caught by Naescorcom after she had infiltrated their systems. Instead of turning her over for prosecution, they offered her a chance to work for them, in a sort of indentured servitude. Naescorcom had been having problems with slicers, so wanted a skilled one on their side. As part of her work, Loa helped set up the security measures in the computer systems of Ipsus.
