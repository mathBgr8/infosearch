Great Hall (Arbra)
1079

The Great Hall was a large auditorium built by the Rebel Alliance in their hidden base on Arbra. It was used for ceremonies where a large number of Alliance personnel would attend. The Great Hall was used in the ceremony to recognize the heroics of Shira Brie in saving her fellow Rogue Squadron members' lives on Shalyvane.
