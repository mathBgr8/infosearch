|published in=Dark Horse Extra |reprinted in= |pages=4 |timeline= |series= |preceded by=Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 6 |followed by="The Third Time Pays for All" }} "Hard Currency" was a four-part comic appearing in Dark Horse Extra #21-#24 which was published by Dark Horse Comics. Published March 2000. It was a follow-up to Star Wars: Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood, and is a lead-in to Star Wars: Crimson Empire III: Empire Lost. It was written by Randy Stradley, drawn by Isaac Buckminster Owens, lettered by Michael Taylor, and colored by Jason Hvam.
