Battle of Bespin (Imperial Period)
1676

The Battle of Bespin occurred some time after the end of the Clone Wars, when a group of freedom fighters under the Alderaanian Resistance attempted to prevent a shipment of Nazren slaves to the Galactic Empire for use in the Sarlacc Project.
