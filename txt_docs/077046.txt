|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=c. 5.5 BBYStar Wars Omnibus: Droids |series=Star Wars: Droids (1994) |issue=6 |next=last }} Droids 6 is the sixth issue of Star Wars: Droids (1994), a Dark Horse comic book series starring R2-D2 and C-3PO. It was published in September 1994 by Dark Horse Comics. In the conclusion to the adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO in the Kalarba system, a power core rupture on Hosk forces all life forms to be evacuated, but life forms doesn't include droids! As Olag Greck makes his final stand, R2-D2 and C-3PO race against time to save the industrial moon&hellip; but first they'll have to survive Greck's droid armada!
