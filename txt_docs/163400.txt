Drev'starn Spaceport
1801

The Drev'starn Spaceport was the main spaceport in Drev'starn, Bothawui. A mountain plain was expanded and leveled to house the planet's largest starport, not only for practical purposes, but also as a display of prestige. Companies and individuals who could afford to maintain holdings and residents in the expensive districts surrounding the starport gained an instant boost in prestige.
