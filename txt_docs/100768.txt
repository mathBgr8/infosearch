Mos Eisley Inn
1536

The Mos Eisley Inn was an inn located next to Jabba's town house in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was owned and operated by Moth Caralli, a wealthy Mon Calamari. The Inn was run-down and offered only the bare essentials.
