Royal cannon
1834

The Royal cannon was a deployable heavy weapon employed by the Royal Naboo Security Forces. It traveled on the battlefield in a compact egg-shaped configuration, moving on a tripod of repulsor pads. For firing, the cannon was raised up off the tripod, splitting the main structure into three panels.
