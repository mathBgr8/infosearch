|death=July 28, 2021 |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=*Author *Executive editor |companies=LucasBooks |website= }} }} Jonathan W. Rinzler was an author, film historian, and executive editor for Lucas Licensing's book division, LucasBooks. In 2005, Jonathan W. Rinzler was hired to write Star Wars books, beginning with two that were published on the same day, the art book The Art of Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith'The Art of Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith and the real-world reference book The Making of Star Wars Revenge of the Sith which offered behind-the-scenes looks at the 2005 prequel trilogy film, Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith.The Making of Star Wars Revenge of the Sith He went on to write the Star Wars Insider supplements The Art of Revenge of the Sith that same year.Star Wars Insider 83 In 2007, he wrote and published The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film on April 24.The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film Rinzler wrote additional Star Wars reference books in the 2010s, starting with The Sounds of Star Wars'The Sounds of Star Wars and The Making of The Empire Strikes Back, both published in 2010.The Making of The Empire Strikes Back Rinzler wrote and published Star Wars: The Blueprints in 2011Star Wars: The Blueprints and The Making of Return of the Jedi in 2013.The Making of Return of the Jedi Rinzler lobbied to be a writer for both Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Star Wars: Underworld, and ended up being credited as writer of the sixth season episodes "The Disappeared, Part I" and "The Disappeared, Part II."
