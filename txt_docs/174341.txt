Star Wars Weekly 48 is the forty-eighth issue in the Star Wars Weekly series of comic reprints, published on January 3, 1979. It reprinted the Pizzazz comic strips in black and white. *"A Matter of Monsters!" *"Pursuit among the Ruins!" *"Tales of the galaxy: Visitation Part 1" *"The power of Warlock Part 4: Deathship!" *"Tales of the Watcher: Pildorr, the plunderer from outer space!" 48
