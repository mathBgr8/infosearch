Chuka
1200

The chuka was a Bothan hand-to-hand combat weapon. Designed to fit over the hand, the chuka discharged a high-density shock sufficient to disable an opponent. The weapon was so closely associated with Bothan elite, that in some systems, mere possession of a chuka was considered grounds for execution as a spy. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Category:Bothan culture Category:Stun batons
