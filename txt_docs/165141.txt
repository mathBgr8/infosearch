Double-Dip Outer Rim Rumdrop
1994

Double-Dip Outer Rim Rumdrop was a mixed drink invented by Shayne Gerrare for his place of employment, Spirited Spirits, on the planet Cularin. The specialty drink was quite popular and was most responsible for Spirited Spirits' success. It was made with a wide range of alcohol stocks. In 31 BBY, the Rim Rumdrop had been exported to other planets of the system. It was available in the Cabana Bar of the Sanads of Rorkee hotel, on Genarius, where it was the drink of choice of hotel guest Dr. Shilaea Motacc. When Motacc was kidnapped, a stranger went to the Cabana Bar to buy all the available stock of Double-Dip Outer Rim Rumdrops. Category:Cocktails
