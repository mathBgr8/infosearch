The TIE/D Defender was an experimentalThe Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels starfighter, and as testing for its prototype indicated the need for a more powerful hyperdrive, such a hyperdrive was added to Onyx 2's production model. During the Galactic Civil War, Onyx 2 was part of the Imperial Onyx Squadron. The Defender's crew consisted of a TIE pilot. By 4 ABY, is set around the time of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, the events of which are dated to 4 ABY by The New Essential Chronology. Onyx 2 was piloted by Yorr, a respected Imperial test pilot holding the rank of captain by that year.
