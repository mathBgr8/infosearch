Unidentified scout trooper (Battle of Yavin 4)
1600

This unnamed scout trooper was an Imperial scout trooper who served and died during the Evacuation of Yavin. During the battle, he was able to infiltrate the Rebel Alliance's temple base and obtain a datapad containing secret information about the Alliance. Commander Col Serra discovered this and ordered his fighting unit, Renegade Squadron, to kill the scout trooper and bring the datapad back to the base.
