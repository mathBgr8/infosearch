Cree Summer

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American-Canadian |occupation=Voice actor |companies= |website= }} Cree Summer (born July 7, 1969) provided the voice of Princess Kneesaa a Jari Kintaka for the first season of Ewoks and Luminara Unduli for the Star Wars: Clone Wars cartoon. She also portrayed Hylo Visz in Star Wars: The Old Republic: Knights of the Fallen Empire and Marlaa Jinara in Star Wars: Young Jedi Adventures. Some of her most famous non&ndash;Star Wars work includes voicing Penny for the original Inspector Gadget cartoons and voicing Elmyra for Tiny Toon Adventures. She also did the voice of Max in the Batman continuation series entitled Batman Beyond. She has recorded voices for programs from many different studios like Film Roman, Hanna-Barbera, Cartoon Network and Nickelodeon. She has also had a musical career, releasing a solo R&B album and touring as an opening act for Lenny Kravitz.
