The repulsorlift craft ― categorized as a "mini rig" ― featured two open areas that could be used to transport people and cargo within or between installations, two large thrusters on its sides for propulsion, and a tripod mount to stabilize it on the ground. Rebel Alliance personnel and droids, including Han Solo, R2-D2, C-3PO, FX-7, and 2-1B, utilized the craft on the planet Hoth during the Galactic Civil War. The PDT-8 was part of Kenner's Mini-Rigs line. It was discontinued after 1982. Originally the PDT-8 was conceived by Kenner as a transport for droids.
