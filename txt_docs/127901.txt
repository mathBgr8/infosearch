Keenal Dene
2831

|masters= |apprentices= }} Keenal Dene was a leader of the Alliance to Restore the Republic in its early development. According to a report published on Imperial HoloVision in 1 ABY,The Imperial HoloVision report in states that it takes place on 35:9:1 of the Great ReSynchronization calendar, which corresponds to 0 ABY per the reasoning here. Chandrilan senator Mon Mothma resigned from the Imperial Senate and disappeared, although she had actually fled the galactic capital Coruscant to escape execution by the Galactic Empire for her work in building the Alliance.The New Essential Guide to Characters The report stated that Mothma had been secretly meeting Rebel leaders, including Dene amongst others such as Bail Organa, Garm Bel Iblis, and Jan Dodonna. Keenal Dene was mentioned in the Galaxywide NewsNets section written by Paul Sudlow and published in the fourth issue of the Star Wars Adventure Journal magazine, which was published by West End Games in November of 1994.
