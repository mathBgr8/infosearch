Model RLW-77
1612

The Model RLW-77 was a type of wrist laser that caused damage in very short range. These wrist lasers recharged using solar power. The chamber could be overloaded on purpose, transforming the wrist laser into an improvised explosive. The weapon was developed by Koromondain PDS, Incorporated.
