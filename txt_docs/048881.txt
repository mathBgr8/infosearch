Tranese
1066

Tranese was the language of the Iotran species from the world of Iotra. It was a rich language, showing simplicity and accuracy in both its words and its construction. Iotrans considered that Basic was a convoluted and rude language; however, they understood the advantages of learning it, and usually took the effort to do so. *Ultimate Alien Anthology *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Iotran culture Category:Languages
