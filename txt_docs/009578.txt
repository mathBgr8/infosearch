Timothy S. O'Brien
1757

Timothy S. O'Brien was an author for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game produced by West End Games (WEG). He got his start in 1993, when he learned that WEG was creating a new publication, the Star Wars Adventure Journal, which was looking for new writers. His submission, "Rebel Privateers!," was published in the second issue of the Journal. O'Brien went on to write several sourcebooks for WEG, several of which revisited the pirate theme of that first article. *Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal (The Gree Enclave and Stand at Bhir'khi Pass) *Cracken's Threat Dossier (Contributor) *Endgame *Hunter/Hunted *Instant Adventures (The Treasure of Celis Mott) *Pirates & Privateers *Preparing for War *Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide *The Best of the Star Wars Adventure Journal, Issues 1-4 (Rebel Privateers!) *The Far Orbit Project (Hunter/Hunted and Preparing for War) *The Treasure of Celis Mott *The Best of the Star Wars Adventure Journal, Issues 1-4 *Classic Adventures: Volume Four — The Best of the Journal Category:Role-playing game authors
