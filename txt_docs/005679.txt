Mos Osnoe
1935

Mos Osnoe was a small city and spaceport located on the desert planet of Tatooine. It was the site of a Galactic Republic spaceport during the Clone Wars. Separatist commander Sev'rance Tann went to this city to meet Boorka the Hutt, when the latter planned to betray the Republic forces there. Later, when the Jedi General Echuu Shen-Jon and his troops intervened in the partnership between Boorka and the Confederacy, they drove out the Separatists from the Mos Osnoe region.
