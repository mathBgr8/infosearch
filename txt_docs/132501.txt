President's Guests floor
1853

The President's Guest Floor was a floor of the Presidential Palace (later the Imperial Palace) for special guests of the President of the Senate, Galactic Emperor, Chief of State and various other government officials who used the Palace. It had several suites, and the entire floor was done in ornate hand-carved woods and had old-fashioned hinge doors. It was one of the few places that Palpatine left strictly alone when he began to occupy and remodel the palace. He usually reserved it for guests who had fond feelings of days past or could be impressed by such things. At least one suite was done completely in Fijisi wood from Cardooine.
