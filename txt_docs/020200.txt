Miracle Meriko
2244

}} Miracle Meriko was a musician during the Clone Wars, and into the Galactic Civil War whose song "Stardance" reached number two on the charts in 22 BBY. He eventually died while under Imperial custody in 6 BBY with a bounty of 20,000 credits on his head. His death was part of the Empire's sweep to eradicate "questionable" artistic enterprises. *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Musicians
