Droid World
1984

|publisher= |release date= |media type=Song |pages= |published in="Listen Up Meatbags" |genre= }} "Droid World" was a HutteseLeland Chee's karaoke playlist song performed by Sentient 7 and the Clankers, featuring on their album "Listen Up Meatbags." The song was played by the droid DJ R-3X at Oga's Cantina in the town of Black Spire Outpost on the planet Batuu. R-3X considered "Droid World" to be a smashing hitA Crash of Fate and claimed that it was the best thing to ever strike the planet.
