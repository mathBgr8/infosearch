Pesktda Xenobotanical Garden
2310

The Pesktda Xenobotanical Garden was a garden located in the city of Pesktda on the planet Garqi. A set of bafforr trees was located at the gardens. When the Yuuzhan Vong attacked Garqi, the local defenders and the Jedi made a stand at the gardens. The battle was short, though, as the bafforr trees soon unleashed pollen that attacked the warriors' vonduun crab armor.
