Meteor (frigate)
1093

Meteor was a Rebel Alliance Nebulon-B escort frigate. Participating in the Operation: Quick Strike with its squadrons of Z-95 Headhunter starfighters, it launched an attack on Drekker, an Imperial X7 Factory Station in the Yerej system used for the production of valuable crystals. The outcome of that attack is unknown.
