Star Wars Annual Featuring Ewoks
1445

Star Wars Annual Featuring Ewoks, or simply Star Wars Annual, is a Star Wars book that collects the Legends comic books Ewoks (1985) 1 and Star Wars Annual (1977) 3. It was published in hardcover by Marvel Comics, in association with Grandreams, in 1985. *Ewoks (1985) 1 *Star Wars Annual (1977) 3 *; 1985; Marvel Comics/Grandreams; UK hardcover Category:Marvel UK specials and annuals
