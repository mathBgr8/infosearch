They had red hair that fell past their shoulders, green eyes, and light skin that was colored with two vertical pink stripes. Each stripe passed over an eye as it stretched from forehead to cheek. On at least one occasion, Lool wore a tight-fitting yellow necklace and green clothing. While on a public street one day, Loor was asked by a HoloNet News CoCo District Edition journalist for their opinion on the ongoing "Baby Ludi" custody case, which saw the Jedi Order refuse to return a Force-sensitive Human infant named Aris-Del Wari to her mother, Jonava Billane. Lool opined that Billane was being used as a political tool by different factions seeking to push their own agendas.
