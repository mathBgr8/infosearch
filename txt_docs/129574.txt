Fobas worked for the Hutt crime lord Zorba Desilijic Tiure, and on the date 13:3:21, Zorba was convicted of illegally mining ulikuo gemstones, which were prized for their aesthetic, industrial, and&mdash;crucially&mdash;cultural value, on the Outer Rim planet Kip. Fobas attempted to appeal the decision, but was reminded by the case's magistrate that Kip had no appeal process. The case was reported on the same day's issue of HoloNet News' CoCo District Edition. Bequin Fobas was mentioned in the in-universe HoloNet News Vol. 531 #48 issue of HoloNet News, written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens and published on the HoloNet News website on March 21, 2002.
