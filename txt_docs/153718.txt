Danlea Lochner
1867

Danlea Lochner, also known as Tara Warren, was an agent of the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Tara Warren was the daughter of Morellian scoutmaster Barosa Warren and an unidentified mother. She was on bad terms with her father, especially after Tara joined the Rebel Alliance, opposing Barosa's wishes. Angry at Barosa, Tara even changed her legal name. She had three siblings, Roland Warren, Tole Warren, and Alton Lochner (who also changed his surname because of his poor relationship with Barosa). All of them were half-Morellian and they all eventually joined the Rebel Alliance.
