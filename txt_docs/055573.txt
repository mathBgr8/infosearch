Simikarty
2577

Simikarty was a Jedi Master of the Old Republic era. A Jedi Master of some renown, Simikarty produced a set of extremely influential writings about the Jedi Code. Part of these texts were the beliefs that Masters should be limited in their number of apprentices, and that under no circumstances could Padawans over a certain age be taken. Over the years, Simikarty's interpretations began to be accepted as canon by the Jedi Order.
