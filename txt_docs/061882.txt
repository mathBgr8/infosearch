Improcco Company
2990

Improcco Company was a company of clone troopers led by Jedi General Etain Tur-Mukan and clone trooper commander Gett. It was based out of the Acclamator-class assault ship Fearless. During the Skirmish at Dinlo, Improcco Company extracted the 41st Elite Corps' Sarlacc Battalions A and B, saving 1,058 troopers in the process. Improcco Company assisted anti-terror ops on Coruscant by being placed on leave, and providing clone commando squads Delta and Omega cover so the squads could conduct crucial surveillance. However, Kal Skirata noted that the men of Improcco Company did not seem to enjoy their leave. This was attributed to clone troopers having little freedom and few modes of entertainment, and thus were not used to the new found liberty. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Grand Army of the Republic companies
