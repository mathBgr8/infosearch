Korren Starchaser
1414

Korren Starchaser was a scout for the New Republic's Bureau of Scouting and Exploration Services since its inception, and flew his own custom-built starship, the Jedi Dreamer. He was the first to chart the Serias system and recovered artifacts from The Gulch. Among his other discoveries were the Kriekaal system and its native species, the Krieks. In addition to his expertise as an explorer, Starchaser was the developer of the vagabond suit.
