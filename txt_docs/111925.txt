Battle of Sarapin (disambiguation)
900

Several different battles involve Sarapin. *The Battle at Sarapin was a fleet battle at Sarapin involving the Rebel Alliance's Riot Squadron. *The Assault on Sarapin during the Clone Wars was the capture of Sarapin by CIS general Sev'rance Tann. *The Recapture of Sarapin during the Clone Wars was the recapture of Sarapin by Galactic Republic general Echuu Shen-Jon. *The last known Battle of Sarapin was the destruction of Rebel forces by Imperial commander Brenn Tantor.
