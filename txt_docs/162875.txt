Cynabar's Droid Datalog
1261

Cynabar's Droid Datalog was a scandoc regarding droids and their usefulness to smugglers, compiled by Cynabar, notorious maintainer of Cynabar's InfoNet. Version 4.7.1 of the datalog was released on 37:8:1:47. The datalog contained data collated from various newsnets and data archives, including secured files such as those of Galladinium's personnel manual and Imperial Defense Daily that had to be sliced into to be accessed. Comments from readers were appended to the entry on each droid, providing useful additional information and clarifications. A significant portion of the roleplaying book Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids maintains the in-universe conceit that it is Version 4.7.1 of this datalog. *Cynabar's Fantastic Technology: Droids Category:Intelligence reports and documents
