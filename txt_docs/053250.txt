Chedak Communications
2572

Chedak Communications was a communications corporation specializing in shipboard subspace radios, comlinks and holoprojectors. They also owned several broadcast networks and entertainment studios. Chedak made a great deal of credits selling to the entertainment industry, but when the Galactic Empire came down on the media, Chedak started losing profit.
