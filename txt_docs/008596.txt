|line= |model=Protodeka prototype |class=Mobile ground siege weapon |degree= |cost= |length= |width= |height= |mass= |gender= |sensor=RedStar Wars: The Clone Wars video game |plating= |armament=*Claw *Homing concussion missiles *Laser cannons *Seismic wave emitter |equipment= |affiliation=*Confederacy of Independent Systems **Separatist Droid Army |firstmade= |retired= }} The Protodeka was a prototype droid mobile ground siege weapon designed by a Colicoid engineer and manufactured by Geonosians on the planet Raxus Prime that was used by the Confederacy of Independant Systems during the Dark Reaper crisis. A giant, crablike droid prototype, the Protodeka was slow, heavily armored mobile ground siege weapon. It had a slow turn rate, but pursued its target to exhaustion. It was equipped with massive laser cannons and a lethal homing concussion missiles, which had a long seek time and range but were very slow. The droid was also armed with a powerful claw to grab enemy targets that wandered too close. As initial Protodeka prototypes were vulnerable to attack from behind and at close range, Geonosian engineers added a special short-range area-effect seismic pulse weapon that shattered ground units within range. The Protodeka was designed by a Colicoid engineer and manufactured by Geonosians on the planet Raxus Prime. During the Dark Reaper crisis, the Protodeka saw action on Raxus Prime and the planet Thule, where a pair defended both Count Dooku and the ancient Sith city of Kesiak, which contained the Dark Reaper. They were destroyed by Obi-Wan Kenobi on Raxus Prime, and Mace Windu and Luminara Unduli on Thule, where each of the victors utilized the TX-130 Saber-class fighter tanks to win their respective battles against the droid. Designed to turn the tide in favor of the Confederacy, the Protodeka turned out to be too expensive to mass-produce, so only a few were built.
