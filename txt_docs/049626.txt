Sluudrese
711

Sluudrese or Sludese was the native language of the Sludir species from Sluudren. It was mainly formed by grunts, snorts and whinnies, making it harsh-sounding. There was no written form for that language, and ideas had to be conveyed through intricate images. During the Galactic Empire, written Basic replaced this activity. Sludirs also had base-six mathematics. Most Sludir were fluent in both Sludese and Basic, although they were only literate in their own language. *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Languages
