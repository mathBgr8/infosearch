Wes Gevar
1288

Lieutenant Wes Gevar was a Rebel ULAV pilot stationed at Karinda Outpost. He met with a group of rebels who came to the planet to deliver much needed supplies, but was shot by an Imperial biker scout and wounded so badly that he could not participate in the fight to the base, but managed to survive.
