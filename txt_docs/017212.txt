It was situated within the historical Greater Tion region, in the Slice portion of the Outer Rim Territories. Raxus II lay on the hyperlane known as the Tion Trade Route, which connected it to the Endregaad and Tion systems. Raxus II was the second planet orbiting the star Raxus. It was a frozen world covered with ice.
