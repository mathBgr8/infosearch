Ord Klina served as an Ordnance/Regional Depot outpost for the Galactic Republic during the Pius Dea Era, a period of time that lasted between approximately 12,000 BBY and 11,000 BBY, despite it being located outside the borders of Republic space. When the Republic launched the Thirty-Fourth Pius Dea Crusade against the Hutt species in 11,057 BBY, Republic forces moved from Ord Klina toward Hutt Space. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Tyrimm Wyln, a Togorian farmer and the mate of bladesmith Mlatar Thon Gra, used the credits she and her sisters had saved from managing a herd of bists on her native Togoria to tour a portion of the galaxy that included Ord Klina.Alliance Intelligence Reports Ord Klina was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
