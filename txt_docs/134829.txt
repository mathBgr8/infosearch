Ursi
1523

A species known as ursi was domesticated at some point and the creatures were frequently kept as house pets by the Bothans, a sentient species native to the Mid Rim planet Bothawui.Ultimate Alien Anthology The Bothans viewed the creatures to be cuddly. The Bothans also used "Ursi" as a first name for some female members. The ursi was first mentioned in the Galactic Campaign Guide, a 2003 sourcebook for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game, written by Peter Schweighofer and J.D. Wiker.
