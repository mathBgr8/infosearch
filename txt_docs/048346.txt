Vainglorious-class cruiser
2064

The Vainglorious-class cruiser was a warship produced by Rendili StarDrive prior to the Galactic Civil War.Stock Ships Vainglorious-class cruisers were used by the Stark Commercial Combine during the Stark Hyperspace War.The Essential Guide to Warfare As of the time of the Galactic Empire, the Vainglorious-class cruiser was a defunct starship model.
