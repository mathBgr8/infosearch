Charybdis
2170

The Charybdis was a Marauder-class corvette in service to Viraxo Industries. The ship was escorting a convoy to Industrial complex VXO-33274 when it came under attack by Ace Azzameen, who was raiding supplies for a Rebel outpost in the Vergesso Asteroids. Despite having a fighter escort, the corvette was destroyed by Ace Azzameen. Charybdis is a reference to the mythological creature Charybdis.
