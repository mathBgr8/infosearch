Coriolis
2167

Coriolis was a Duros-crewed starship of Galactic Alliance during the latter stages of the Yuuzhan Vong War. It was operated by Commander Yurf Col. It was part of a force commanded by General Wedge Antilles that was tasked with distracting Yuuzhan Vong forces at Duro, as part of a larger effort to liberate Fondor in 28 ABY. Coriolis was lost when it joined with several other warships and Duros freedom fighters in refusing an order to fall back. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starships of the Galactic Alliance
