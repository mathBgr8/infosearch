The Neimoidian oversaw the battle along with other Muuns via hologram. He was captured along with San Hill and the other Muuns after Obi-Wan Kenobi and the Muunilinst 10 secured the InterGalactic Banking Clan's command center. After he was arrested, he held the robe of a Muun while being taken onto a Republic cruiser. The Hyperspace Avatars state that this Neimoidian is Nute Gunray, but Leland Chee has said he's never been named. He is the only Neimoidian to have appeared in the series.
