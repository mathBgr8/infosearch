Aaron McClellan
601

Aaron McClellan (born February 8, 1959) is an artist who illustrated one of the Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game supplements. Along with Michael Manley and Al Williamson, he provided the sketches for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina and Tales from Jabba's Palace. *Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races *Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina *Tales from Jabba's Palace Category:Roleplaying game artists
