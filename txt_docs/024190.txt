Krongbing Interstellar Fashions
837

Krongbing Interstellar Fashions was a company dealing in the manufacture of clothing in the Galactic Republic. Krongbing Interstellar Fashions came under pressure at one point in its history, and struggled to maintain profits. The company entered into a merger with Ayelixe Fabrico, forming Ayelixe/Krongbing Textiles. The former Krongbing Interstellar Fashions viewed this merger as a positive move, and Ayelixe/Krongbing Textiles later became one of the first Signatory Sponsors of the Corporate Sector Authority.
