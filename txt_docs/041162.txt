During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Trag was working behind the bar of the club when Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered, searching for bounty hunter Zam Wesell. Trag was muscular and had light skin, black hair and brown eyes. While working at the club, Trag wore a sleeveless net shirt with black straps.
