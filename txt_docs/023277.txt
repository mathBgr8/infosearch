|birth= |death= |species=Human (clone) |gender=Male |pronouns= |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic **Bacta Company |masters= |apprentices= }} Tyto, designated CT-52/89-9204, was one of the many clones of Jango Fett&mdash;a 1.83 meter&ndash;tall Human male bounty hunter&mdash;created on the planet of Kamino for service in the Grand Army of the Republic. During the Clone Wars, Tyto served the Galactic Republic as a clone trooper captain in command of Bacta Company, a force of four platoons made up of a total of one hundred forty-four troops. Bacta Company was part of the Hawkbat Battalion in the 327th Star Corps, which was, in turn, part of the 7th Legion in the 101st Regiment of the Systems Army Alpha's 2nd Sector Army. Tyto was first mentioned in "Guide to the Grand Army of the Republic," an article written by Karen Traviss and Ryan Kaufman and published in Star Wars Insider 84 in September of 2005.
