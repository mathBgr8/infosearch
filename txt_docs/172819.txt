Nobu 3
1634

Nobu 3 was a Rebel Alliance Cargo ferry which served in the Airam sector during the operations of Task Force Vengeance. When the decision was made to construct a shipyard in Airam space, Nobu 3 and its sister-ships, Nobu 1, Nobu 2, and Nobu 4, were joined with a Rebel convoy charged with bringing the needed supplies and personnel. Nobu 3 itself bore construction equipment. Unfortunately for the Rebels, the Galactic Empire had learned of them and readied a trap in the Rocrin system. Nobu 3 was destroyed with all the others. *Star Wars: X-Wing vs. TIE Fighter: Balance of Power Campaigns Category:Nobu group
