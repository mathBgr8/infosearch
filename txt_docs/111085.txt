Star Roamer
2052

The Star Roamer was a medium freighter manufactured by Damorian Manufacturing Corporation. It was owned by CorDuro Shipping and in 26 ABY was used to deliver a shipment of bacta to the Yuuzhan Vong. They were intrigued by its healing properties, and would later try and sabotage its production using a virus carried by fallen Jedi Bey Gandan. However, the Yuuzhan Vong never received this bacta shipment as the ship was captured by Han Solo and the Wild Knights to acquire bacta tanks to heal the wounded Jedi Master Eelysa. CorDuro was suspected of collaborating with the Yuuzhan Vong so Solo felt no guilt in raiding it.
