Algeran Faction
505

The Algeran Faction was a terrorist group based on Esseles. During the rise to power of Emperor Palpatine, the Algaran Faction were responsible for several atrocities that were considered to be the worst in Esselian history. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Terrorist groups
