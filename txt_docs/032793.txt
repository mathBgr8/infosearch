Rino Romano
1339

Rino Romano (born 1969) is an Italian-Canadian voice actor whose most well-known roles are those of Bruce Wayne/Batman in The Batman, opposite Kevin Michael Richardson, Darien Shields/Tuxedo Mask in the early episodes of the DiC dubbed anime Sailor Moon and Lone Starr in Spaceballs: The Animated Series. In the Star Wars universe, he provided the voices of Kyle Katarn in Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith and Firith Me and the male Revan (taunts and replies in player's commands) in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.
