Star Wars Summer Special 1985
1530

Star Wars Summer Special 1985 was a comic book released in the United Kingdom that reprinted a number of Star Wars stories, including the stories published in the US as Star Wars (1977) 24 and Star Wars (1977) 29. The cover features a portion of the cover art from that original issue. The stories were first published in the UK in issues of Star Wars Weekly. According to an advertisement first published inside Return of the Jedi Weekly 111 and later issues, it was scheluded to be released on "end of june", alongside other summer specials like A-Team, Masters of the Universe, Doctor Who, Transformers and Spiderman. Also included is a story titled R2-D2's tales from the Data Banks, which just reprints a non-Star Wars short story from Marvel Comics' Tales to Astonish #42, titled "I Am Not Human!"
