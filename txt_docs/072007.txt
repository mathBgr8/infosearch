Rengo
1355

"Dusty" Rengo was a male Clantaani member of Longo Two-Guns' gang of criminals, who hid on the desert planet of Tatooine. In 32 BBY, Rengo was wanted by Jabba the Hutt for skimming profits from a "legitimate" protection agency on Tatooine.
