Bor Gracus
2070

Bor Gracus was a male Human Senator who represented Sluis Van and the Sluis sector in the Galactic Senate. He attended the Eriadu Trade Summit in 33 BBY on behalf of both the Galactic Republic and his homeworld. He was in league with Senator Palpatine, who favored him due to the Sluis Van Shipyards. He was succeeded as senator by Corlissi Ludar. Darth Plagueis states that, before entering the Senate, Gracus served as an aide to Ranulph Tarkin.
