Gavron Sil was the son of Drex and the brother of Feleen Bantillian. He was killed by Boba Fett. His father and brother conspired to take revenge on Fett, but their plot was unsuccessful and they soon met the same fate as Sil. Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:Males
