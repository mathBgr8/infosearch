Imperial Exports
871

Imperial Exports, later called Imperial/Republic, was a modestly successful shipping company. In an effort to make their exports more profitable, they engineered a faster hyperspace route that made a stop off on Belsavis. Because the planet's atmosphere was unstable, IE was forced to create a consistently hospitable area on Belsavis for its ships to make berth. A huge dome was constructed over the Plawal Rift to provide a stable atmosphere.
