Clan Shak
1042

thumb|right|200px|[[Koh'shak, prominent member of Clan Shak]] The Shak clan was a Twi'lek family on Ryloth. In the early years of the New Republic, the Shak clan operated the Kala'uun Starport, from which they operated a number of illegal activities, including ryll scams and slave sales. Due to their ryll scams against the New Republic in 6 ABY, the clan was discredited and stripped of its prominence. *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 11 *X-Wing: The Krytos Trap *Platt's Starport Guide Shak
