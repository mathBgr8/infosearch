Rhine was bribed several times by local criminal Kritus Morven. When Morven learned that the Royal Security Forces had started to search for his spice refineries, Morven tasked a spacer with delivering an authorization of credits to Rhine, who would be waiting outside of Keren. In return, Rhine slowed the search down as much as he could, giving Morven the time to evacuate his refinery before it could be discovered. Rhine was a Human who had brown hair and a light skin tone. He wore purple and gray clothing. Dagrus Rhine was a non-player character (NPC) in Star Wars Galaxies who would spawn somewhere outside of Keren during Kritus Morven's third quest. Rhine spawned under the generic "rsf_security_guard" template rather than having a randomized appearance, resulting in a consistent model that was typically assigned to Human female NPCs while also being referred to with he/him pronouns.
