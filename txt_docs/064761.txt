Hyperspace Webcam
869

Hyperspace Webcam was a feature on the Star Wars Hyperspace fan club website. It was a live-action webcam on-set during the production of Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. The webcam image updated online every twenty seconds. Several selections of webcam images were compiled in the Hyperspace Webcam Movies. Category:Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club features
