Senali (species)
1046

Senali were the native species of the Rutan moon of Senali. They were an offshoot of Rutanians from Rutan, and the rivalry between the two species was provoked by the Yuuzhan Vong into war, leaving both Rutan and Senali weak and vulnerable to invasion. *Jedi Apprentice: The Shattered Peace *The Clone Wars: Decide Your Destiny: The Way of the Jedi *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Outer Rim Territories sentient species
