Asteroid Prospector AP-300
3007

The Asteroid Prospector AP-300 was a capital-size asteroid refining starship produced by Kuat Drive Yards. The AP-300 was capable of mining asteroids and refining the metals found in them into high grade ores. The onboard refinery was able to ensure the ore recovered from the asteroids was at least 25% pure.Pirates & Privateers Due to the dangerous environment they worked and their valuable cargo, such ships were equipped with heavy shields, a durable hull, and armament of eight laser cannons and four tractor beams. It was meant for operations involving small to medium-sized asteroid belts. Lant Mining Corporation operated these vessels as part of their mining fleet, including The Pulverizer commanded by Captain Niree. *Flashpoint!
