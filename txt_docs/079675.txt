Cardacs
3662

Cardacs was a lieutenant and pilot in the Rebel Alliance's Red Squadron during the Galactic Civil War. An experienced pilot, Cardacs served in the Outer Rim Territories before transferring to the Independence. His first mission with Red Squadron, under the call sign Red Two, was to rescue Rebel prisoners, including his friend Captain Samuel Raider, who had escaped on the BFF-1 bulk freighter Genue from the Star's End penal colony at Mytus VII. Although the mission was successful, Cardacs was killed in action when his R-22 SpearheadAlthough both the game Star Wars: X-Wing and the official strategy guide referred to the ship as an A-wing, the time period of the game's events would make it the R-22 Spearhead, the A-wing's predecessor. was destroyed by TIE Fighters from the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Intrepid. Although Captain Keyan Farlander, Cardacs' flight leader, had known him for only minutes, Cardacs was the first wingmate he had lost, and the guilt of his death weighed heavily on Farlander. Cardacs' friend Raider initially held Farlander responsible for his death, but later realized that there was nothing he could have done. Cardacs is the wingman (the A-wing that escorts the player) in ToD II: Op I: 'Rescue at the Stars' End' of Star Wars: X-Wing, although he is not mentioned in the game's narrative.
