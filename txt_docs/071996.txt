Mike Cottrell is an actor who played an Ewok warrior in Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. Cottrell also appeared in such films as Flash Gordon and History of the World: Part One. It appears that Mike is wearing the Romba costume, however this Ewok as already been attributed to Debbie Lee Carrington. Category:Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi actors
