Gary M. Sarli
2063

Gary M. Sarli is an author, game designer, developer, and editor who wrote Jedi Counseling from 2004 to 2010, a regular feature on the Wizards of the Coast website that provided rules clarifications for players of the Star Wars Roleplaying Game and Star Wars Miniatures. He also worked as the editor and developer for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game Saga Edition Core Rulebook as well as several other supplements and features for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. *Star Wars Roleplaying Game Saga Edition Core Rulebook *Ultimate Adversaries *Starships of the Galaxy *Scum and Villainy *The Clone Wars Campaign Guide *Jedi Counseling *The Gem of Alderaan Category:Role-playing game authors
