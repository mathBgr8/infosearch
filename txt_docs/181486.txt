The Great Temple Valley was a location on the planet Barkhesh which contained a series of ancient temples of an unknown civilization scattered along it. They were similar to the Massassi Temples on Yavin 4. This area became the site of a battle between Rebel and Imperial forces in 0 BBY. *Star Wars: Rogue Squadron *Strongholds of Resistance Category:Outer Rim Territories structures Category:Valleys
