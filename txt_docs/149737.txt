Slouch hat
591

Slouch hats were "swank" high-class hats worn by Kuati elite such as Viqi Shesh. Usually associated with the Australian armed forces, Slouch hats are sometimes adorned with a plumage of Emu feathers, giving the wearer, usually a higher rank officer, an essence of regality. They are broad brimmed felt hats that have the left side folded and pinned above the headband. The hats depicted here however, seem somewhat more elaborate. *The New Jedi Order: Star by Star Category:Headgear
