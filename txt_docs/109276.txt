Herylcha Baakos
1127

Herylcha Baakos was a female councillor in the Galactic Republic. Herylcha Baakos believed that the Elomin people were neglecting the Elom, and ordered the Elomin to allow their neighbors equal opportunity to live on the surface of their homeworld.
