Gorrlyn
1527

Gorrlyn was a female Wookiee from Kashyyyk. She was Salporin's wife and participated in the Battle of Kashyyyk during the final days of the Clone Wars. After the formation of the Galactic Empire, Gorrlyn and her spouse were both enslaved and separated. Salporin would die defending Leia Organa Solo and Chewbacca, widowing Gorrlyn.
