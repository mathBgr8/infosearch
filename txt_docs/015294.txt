It was the homeworld of the Caarite race, and one of the head offices of the Metatheran Cartel, which was a Caarite-dominated trade organization. Many millennia before the Clone Wars, the axis of the planet shifted dramatically after a tremendous asteroid strike. The seasons drastically shortened, and a new tropical environment changed the world and how the Caarites could live. Their cities now seemed to produce toxins into the land that was slowly killing the planet.
