Nautag d'al' Gargan II
1959

Nautag d'al' Gargan II was an Askajian, the son of Nautag and Yarna d'al' Gargan and brother of Luka and Leia d'al' Gargan. He was named after his father who died trying to prevent his family's enslavement by Jabba the Hutt.
