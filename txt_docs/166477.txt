Star Wars Droids: The Adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO
1710

"Star Wars Droids: The Adventures of R2-D2 and C-3PO" was a source article appearing in Polyhedron 170. It was written by Rich Handley and Abel G. Peña and illustrated by Jeffery Carlisle.
