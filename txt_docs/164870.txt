Michael Dubisch
820

Michael Dubisch is an illustrator who worked on West End Games Star Wars roleplaying game books Alien Encounters and The Far Orbit Project. He also worked for Wizards of the Coast on numerous projects such as The Unknown Regions and The Clone Wars Campaign Guide among others, and designed several miniatures for their line as well. He's the author of the science fiction graphic novel "Weirdling," and co-creator of the award winning comic series, "The People That Melt in The Rain." *Alien Encounters *The Far Orbit Project *Legacy Era Campaign Guide Category:Roleplaying game artists
