Durinium
1078

Durinium was a metal alloy capable of stopping sensor scans. It was often valued by smugglers for its versatility and sensor stopping ability, and was capable of being impregnated into substances ranging from leather to durasteel. As such, it was often used in blaster holsters to conceal illegal weapons, as well as in cargo modules to shield contraband.
