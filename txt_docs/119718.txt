Luke Skywalker's freighter
2237

The freighter Luke Skywalker took on his return to Tatooine seeking blockade runners for the Rebellion was a round ship capable of transporting a X-34 landspeeder. Unfortunately, despite putting a camouflage cover over the freighter, it ended up salvaged by Jawas.
