|designer= |homeworld= |line= |model= |class= |degree= |cost=9,000 credits |length= |width= |height=2 Imperial standard meters |mass= |gender= |sensor=Green |plating=Brown |armament=Variable; heavy blaster pistol, blaster rifle, or light repeating blaster |equipment=*Photoreceptor *Vocabulator |affiliation= |firstmade= |retired= }} K5 Enforcer Droids were a series of security droids developed by Rim Securities. It had a domed head with one sweeping visual sensor and was equipped with a vocabulator speech and sound system. It had two arms and two auto-balance legs, and was equipped with armored body plates. K5 droids were typically armed with a variety of blaster weapons.
