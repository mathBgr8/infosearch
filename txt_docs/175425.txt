Hardscrabble
665

Hardscrabble was a town on Muskree where the planet's tiny population lived during the reign of the Galactic Empire. Hardscrabble would become home to several mining operations, due to several scans indicating a rich vein of minerals and ore. The scans proved false, however, with the settlers remaining taking up nerf herding. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Cities
