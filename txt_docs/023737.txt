H'kig (religious leader)
1242

H'kig was a male Galandan who served as a religious leader and the founder of a religion named for him. Born on Galand, he preached morals and goodness, and that people should live simpler lives. He was also very outspoken against the Ruling Families of Galand, which put him to death after he insulted the Viceroy of Galand. *Dark Force Rising Sourcebook *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Galandans Category:Males Category:Religious workers
