Reesa Doliq
2011

Reesa Doliq was a female Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic during the final decades of peace. A Force-sensitive female, Reesa Doliq was educated in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order; Doliq had attained the rank of Jedi Master by 89 BBY. Appointed as an academy instructor, Master Doliq was stationed at the Coruscant Jedi Temple. At the Temple, a voluntary assignment was open to the Temple's senior Initiates and younger Padawans, which pitted two teams of ten against each other in a fun competition.
