Fibersaw
1304

Fibersaws were silk-thin cutting tools in use by around 22 BBY. These strands could shear through a droideka's casing, dissecting the droid. In combat, a JK-13 security droid's tentacular extremities could thin to nearly invisible dimensions, operating as fibersaws. Fibersaws were mentioned in the 2004 novel The Cestus Deception, written by Steven Barnes. *The Cestus Deception Category:Saws
