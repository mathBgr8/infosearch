Ben Templesmith
1073

Ben Templesmith (b. March 7, 1978) is an Australian graphic artist who inked, colored and penciled the comic "Dark Journey" in Star Wars Tales 17. He has also created various book covers, movie posters, trading cards, and concept work for movies. His most famous work is the comic 30 Days of Night, which was written by Steve Niles, who also worked on a story in Star Wars Tales 17. *"Dark Journey" Category:Comic book colorists Category:Comic book inkers Category:Comic book pencillers
