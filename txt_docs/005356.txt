|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=ActressStar Wars: The Lightsaber Collection |companies=Industrial Light & Magic |website= }} Amy Allen (born October 24, 1976) is an American actress best known for her role as Twi'lek Jedi Aayla Secura in Attack of the Clones and Revenge of the Sith. Aside from portraying Jedi Knight Aayla Secura, she also portrayed three extras in the Outlander Club in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. Namely, within the Legends continuity, these were identified as the Nalle triplets: Mya, Yma, and Lela Mayn (all three names are anagrams of the actress's name). Additionally, she played a Twi'lek senatorial aide Pampy in the DVD version of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, replacing the Human aides of Senator Orn Free Taa. {| ! Year !! Title !!
