Fondor Academy of Engineering and Design
1073

The Fondor Academy of Engineering and Design was a prime source of employee recruits for Kuat Drive Yards and the Techno Union prior to the Clone Wars. When the General Ministry Institute budget was cut, those two organizations began largely funding the Academy.
