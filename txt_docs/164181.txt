Slip 45-J

Slip 45-J was a landing bay located in Kwilaan Starport in the city of Keren on the planet of Naboo. In 23 BBY, Captain Calquad Dominé refused to move his mining transport, Pickaxe from this slip, forcing an arriving refugee transport to divert to Theed. Category:Docking bays Category:Keren locations
