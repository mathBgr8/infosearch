Oolas
1900

Oolas was the male captain of the Steadfast during Operation Flotsam. He came from a planet that had never been occupied by the Galactic Empire. His species possessed upper tentacles and a narrow neck. In 16 ABY, Oolas served in the New Republic Defense Fleet as captain of the Steadfast. Together with Lieutenant Norda Proi, he and his team destroyed a couple of Remnant ships in order to search them for information, for which the Steadfast was equipped with the latest technology. This mission was known as Operation Flotsam.
