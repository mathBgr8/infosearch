Connected to the neighboring planets of Gandle Ott, Shintel and Ivatch by hyperlanes, Peirs supported a small colony whose economy was based around supplying ore and natural resources to Gandle Ott. Peirs was featured in The DarkStryder Campaign, a supplement for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game by West End Games. Peirs was not featured directly in any adventure, but was included to give background information on worlds within the Kathol sector.
