Elite Circle
2011

The Elite Circle was the leading group of assassins in the Assassin's Guild. To get into this elite group, a sentient had to be voted in by their peers. Members of the Circle were assigned special, large-scale missions, killing targets at the request of "big customers."A Guide to the Star Wars Universe During his feud with the Guild, the gunslinger Gallandro killed over half of the Circle's membership, as well as many lower-level members of the Guild.Han Solo and the Lost Legacy The survivors offered Gallandro a position with the Guild in response; though Gallandro refused the offer, the feud did end at that point.Han Solo and the Corporate Sector Sourcebook This marked the only time in their history that the Guild defaulted on a contract.
