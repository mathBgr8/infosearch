Path to the Sky
1487

"Path to the Sky" was an Alderaanian ballad that had been released by 40 ABY. That year, the Solo family used the song's first five notes as the doorbell chime for their apartment on the planet Coruscant. "Path to the Sky" was mentioned in the first installment of Star Wars: Legacy of the Force, the 2006 novel Legacy of the Force: Betrayal, which was written by Aaron Allston. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Ballads Category:Culture of Alderaan
