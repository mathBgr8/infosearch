Greyshade family
1299

The Greyshade family was a family of Human politicians from the planet of Columex. Its members included Senator Jheramahd Greyshade and his cousin, Senator Simon Greyshade, as well as Diedrich Greyshade, a moff of the Commonality. Note: This pedigree chart assumes that the Greyshade's follow the tradition of keeping the male's surname during marriage.
