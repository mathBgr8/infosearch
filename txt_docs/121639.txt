A lav peq pattern was a swooping tactic used by multiple Yuuzhan Vong tsik vai to track and capture prey. It was named after the lav peq netting beetle. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest Category:Airspeeder tactics Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
