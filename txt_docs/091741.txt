|founder= |leader=Head of the School of Torturers |subgroup= |headquarters= |locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} The School of Torturers was a facility set up by Emperor Palpatine during the years of the New Order. It served as the focal point in specialized training of individuals in the fine art of torture. Rebel operative Jan Ors attempted to assassinate the school's Head, taking on an Imperial identity to do so. However, her cover was blown, necessitating her extraction by fellow Rebel agent Kyle Katarn.
