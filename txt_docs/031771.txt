Ronto-class transport
1693

The Ronto-class transport was a blocky cargo vessel built by Damorian Manufacturing Corporation. Ronto-class transports were equipped with at least one dorsal and one ventral laser cannon turret and one escape pod. Their hull could be dented by a Verpine shatter gun, and they had an extremely simple flight deck with a very small viewport. Rontos were considered to be among the slowest, ugliest, and most inefficient ships of the light transport classification in 36 ABY. *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Damorian Manufacturing Corporation products Category:Transport classes
