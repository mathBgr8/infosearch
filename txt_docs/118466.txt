Quarren Sector
821

thumb|250px|[[Han Solo/Legends|Han Solo and Chewbacca in the Quarren Sector.]] The Quarren Sector was a large area in Crevasse City on the planet Kalkovak. The area was populated with Quarren and was a popular settlement for the species outside of Mon Calamari. Han Solo and Lando Calrissian once searched the area for Illyan Webble who knew the location of the Yavin Vassilika.
