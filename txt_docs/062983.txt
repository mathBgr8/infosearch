Brine Eyes-Li
1401

Brine Eyes-Li was a male Gran hitman from Malastare who served the RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate and Sebolto during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, Brine was the originator of the bounty on the head of Meen Burtak.
