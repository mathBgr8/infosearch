|pages=24 |upc= |timeline=0 ABY |series=Classic Star Wars |issue=2 }} Classic Star Wars 2 is the second issue of the Classic Star Wars series, published by Dark Horse Comics. This particular Classic series were reformatted and colored reprints of the Star Wars daily newspaper strip by Archie Goodwin and Al Williamson from the early 1980s, including the second half of The Bounty Hunter of Ord Mantell. Williamson also supplied new panels and art to flow the stories for traditional comic format. Classic Star Wars features the adventures of everyone's favorite Rebels during the period between the first and second Star Wars films. In this issue, Darth Vader plots new ways to trip up the Rebels, but a turncoat Admiral may thwart his plans.
