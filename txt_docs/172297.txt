GEC U47
2225

Galactic Electronics Corporation employed Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transport GEC U47 at their primary research facility Pondut during the Galactic Civil War. GEC U47 job was to run cargo transfers between the containers and the station. After the discovery of Mag-pulse Torpedoes aboard Rebel starfighters, Imperial Intelligence derived Galactic Electronics as the supplier.
