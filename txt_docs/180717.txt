Alliance Veteran's Victory Association
1251

The Alliance Veteran's Victory Association, or AVVA, was a retiree's club for Alliance to Restore the Republic veterans with the ambition to be something more. Its members considered themselves for a reserve role in the the New Republic Defense Fleet. Around the time of the Black Fleet Crisis, they were led by retired Brigadier Bren Derlin, and published a news packet titled Fleet Watch. *Tyrant's Test *Star Wars Encyclopedia *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe, Third Edition, Revised and Expanded *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:New Republic organizations
