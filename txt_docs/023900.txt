Fifth Fleet can refer to: *Fifth Fleet&mdash; a numbered Galactic Republic Navy fleet that participated in the Clone Wars as part of the Open Circle Armada. *Imperial Fifth Fleet&mdash; a fleet in the Imperial Navy of the reconstituted Sith Empire commanded by Grand Moff Rycus Kilran. *5th Fleet Security&mdash; also known as the Fifth Fleet Security, a clone trooper security force. *Fifth Fleet&mdash; a fleet led by Ai-Ari Kuldi. *Fifth Fleet of the Open Circle Fleet&mdash; a major fleet of the Open Circle Armada that participated in the Clone Wars. *New Republic Fifth Fleet&mdash; a Combined Defense Forces of the New Republic fleet. *Galactic Alliance Fifth Fleet&mdash; a fleet of the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances.
