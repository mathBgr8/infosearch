Hira Deboota
810

Hira Deboota was a male physician from Coruscant who worked for the Coruscant State Coroner. After finishing an autopsy on a dead Yaka, he saw a similarity between the Yaka and Ganks. Deboota then forwarded the information he had learned to Senior Anthropologist Mammon Hoole and to proper New Republic scientific authorities. *The Essential Guide to Alien Species *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscanti Category:Healers and doctors Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Males
