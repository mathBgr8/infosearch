Race for Survival
4787

"Race for Survival" is a comic strip written by Archie Goodwin and illustrated by Al Williamson. It was published by the Los Angeles Times Syndicate in 1983. This comic strip details the escape of the Rebels from Yavin 4 in the last stages of the blockade that followed the destruction of the first Death Star.
