Loan-chat

Loan-chat was a manufacturing company that produced ejector seats for starfighters. Their products, such as the "Slingshot" ejection seat, were used in Incom/Subpro Z-95 Headhunters. *The Star Wars Sourcebook *The Star Wars Sourcebook, Second Edition Category:Starship technology companies
