Horace Wyrmyr
1653

Horace Wyrmyr was Academy Dean of the Academy of Carida who participated in the expulsion trial of Cadet Mako Spince. Wyrmyr lived in the Dean's Residence and was the seventeenth and final dean to have ZZ-4Z, whom he disliked, serve as his housekeeper.
