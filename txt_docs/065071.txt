Fury Squadron
1165

Fury Squadron was a Toscan 8-Q starfighter squadron operated by a pirate group in the Outer Rim Territories. Shortly before the Battle of Endor, Fury Squadron and Pirate Squadron accompanied Marauder-class corvette Vulture on a raid against casino owner Dunari.
