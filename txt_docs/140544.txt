Corruption Panel
778

The Corruption Panel was a body of the New Republic Senate that oversaw and reviewed the conduct of senators and other representatives. In 26 ABY it was headed by Kvarm Jia and E'noro sat on the panel. It was charged with investigating the Kuati senator Viqi Shesh prior to the Appeasement Vote.
