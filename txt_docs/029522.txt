Bormterran
1002

The Bormterrans were four-armed, fearsome, loyal, dragon-like, reptiloid sapients indigenous to Bormter. They were fierce melee combatants, which made them perfect for recruitment into Warlord Delurin's dragon troopers. Being from a primitive culture, they were not very comfortable with advanced technology but adapted to using it. They grew to an average height of 1.9 meters.
