Graith
906

Graith was the capital of Goroth Prime. It was the largest and most populous of the planet's o'bekis. It was also the seat of the P'Dar'Ken and the Colonial Government, as well as the location of the corporate headquarters for the various rights holders allowed to operate on Goroth Prime. *Goroth: Slave of the Empire Category:Capital cities
