thumb|250px|[[Teebo/Legends|Teebo tries to juice some dangleberries.]] The dangleberry was a fruit that grew on the forest moon of Endor. The orange-colored fruits, larger than other Endorian berries such as the sunberry or the rainbow berry, grew individually on long vines from which they "dangled." The vines only bloomed once a year, and after they fruited, the Ewoks would extract the juice for consumption."" *Star Wars: Tiny Death Star Category:Berries Category:Ewok food Category:Plants of Endor
