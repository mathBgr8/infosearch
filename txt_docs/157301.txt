Ancestral architecture
392

Ancestral architecture were the type of buildings made by the Yuuzhan Vong to evoke memories of their homeworld. The buildings were rich in plants and wildlife. The Place of Sacrifice was built in the style. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Architecture Category:Yuuzhan Vong culture
