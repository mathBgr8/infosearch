Crotok
800

The Crotok were a sentient alien species. This species' members included the pilot of a Helix-class light interceptor, Kizbon's Box, targeted by a gang of pirates led by Larken. The pirates mistakenly thought the pilot would be unarmed, but much to their surprise, the Crotok managed to outfight them using proton torpedoes. Category:Sentient species
