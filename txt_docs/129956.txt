Po Reggis

Po Reggis was a male Human agricultural technician in the city of Griann on Teyr and the husband of Jiki Reggis who had lived on Teyr for twenty-eight years when Luke Skywalker and Akanah Norand Goss Pell arrived searching for the Fallanassi in 16 ABY. *Shield of Lies Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Technicians Category:Teyr residents
