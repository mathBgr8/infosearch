David Paymer
2226

David Paymer (born August 30, 1954) is an American character actor and voice actor. In Star Wars media, he voiced Luke Skywalker's boyhood friend Deak in the Star Wars radio drama in 1981, appearing in the first episode, "A Wind to Shake the Stars." Outside of Star Wars, Paymer has received acting nominations for Golden Globe, Screen Actors Guild, and Academy Awards.
