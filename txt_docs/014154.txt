Discovered by the Galactic Republic circa 3000 BBY, Alassa Major was established as a resort world, with the wealthiest of the Core escaping here to revitalize and refresh themselves. Loyal to the Republic since the world joined during the blazing of the Enarc Run, it joined the Galactic Empire upon its formation from the ashes of the Republic. A rival resort to Bespin's Cloud City, the planet prospered throughout the Galactic Civil War; though as of 9 ABY it, along with the entire Enarc Run had been secured by the New Republic.
