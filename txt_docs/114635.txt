Solidarity (Marauder-class)
1939

The Solidarity was a Republic Sienar Systems Marauder-class corvette in the Kathol Republic Navy. In 8 ABY, the Solidarity responded to a distress call from the Bravado III, alongside her sister ships Juno II, Mirandine, Quillion, Siroma, and Triumph. Together, they intercepted the modified New Republic corvette FarStar and impounded it on Dayark. *The Kathol Outback Category:Marauder-class Assault Corvettes Category:Starships of the Kathol Republic Navy
