|published in=Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=0 BBY (35 GrS) |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Empire Blues: The Devaronian's Tale" is a short story written by Daniel Keys Moran for Tales from the Mos Eisley Cantina, which was published by Bantam Spectra in August 1995. It takes place in 0 BBY. Kardue'sai'Malloc is a former Imperial army officer who committed atrocities on his home world of Devaron. Now he lives on Tatooine as a fugitive under the alias of Labria. He recall his bloody past while also reflecting upon his true passion: music.
