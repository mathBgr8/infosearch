Hexum'Baz
1472

Hexum'Baz was a male Dug who served under Sebolto's employ during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He was wanted dead by an anonymous person for unknown reasons. In 32 BBY, the person being quoted said that he just wanted the "worthless" Dug dead. His bounty was claimed by Jango Fett.
