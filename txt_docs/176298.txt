Skull-Biters Flightknife
605

Skull-Biters Flightknife was a unit of Adumari Blade series starfighters from the planet Adumar. It participated in the Adumari Civil War and flew in defense of the Cartann nation. They were the first flightknife to surrender to General Wedge Antilles upon receiving the orders from the captured Cartann perator. *X-Wing: Starfighters of Adumar Category:Adumari flightknives
