Imperial Moff's Estate
1460

The Imperial Moff's Estate was the official residence of the Moff of the Tapani sector during the Galactic Civil War. It was previously the estate of the Imperial senator who represented the Tapani Sector. The large building was located on Estalle Island and used was for both entertainment and official Imperial business. It was the official residence of the Tapani sector Moff during the Imperial Era and was previously the estate of Tapani's Imperial senator, prior to the Empire's invasion of the Freeworlds Territory, the said Imperial Moff was Laird Gustavu. The estate was located in the residential district of Estalle Island on Procopia.
