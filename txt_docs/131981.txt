Vhin

Vhin was an island continent on the planet Illarreen, located in the northern hemisphere. It was once rich in resources, but a sudden and intense climate change&mdash;hypothesised to be due to a solar flare&mdash;rendered it uninhabitable. The Poss'Nomin evolved on the eastern shores of the island, but were forced to sail overseas to discover fertile lands. *Alien Encounters Category:Continents Category:Islands
