Time-lock device
594

A time-lock device was a device required on all spaceship docks by Imperial Order D6-66. They were used by spaceship docks, Imperial officials and Spaceship Owners to lock down their ship for a certain amount of time. This was used for impounding ships and securing them. *Inside the Worlds of Star Wars Trilogy *Star Wars: Complete Locations Category:Locking mechanisms
