Kailio Entertainments
1525

Kailio Entertainments was a business set up and owned by the Hutts to serve as a legitimate front for their manufacture and distribution of holographic entertainment in the Core Worlds. On the surface, Kailio Entertainments was seen as an upstanding and respected company, and was known as a charitable patron of the arts.
