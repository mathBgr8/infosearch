It seemed to be used as a staging area and command post. The station appeared to have facilities large enough to handle an influx of starfighters and capital ships. Little is known of the layout of the space station, save that it housed a sizeable holoprojection chamber. A fleet of Acclamator-class assault ships and Jedi starfighters made a stop at this station, following the defeat at the Evacuation of Rhen Var, and the loss of the planet to the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
