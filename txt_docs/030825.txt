|gender=Male |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Pink |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=Galactic Empire |masters= |apprentices= }} Cartariun was a Force-sensitive Imperial technician stationed in an Imperial Garrison on the world of Malrev IV. When Cartariun was a boy, he manifested mild Force-sensitivity. Having seen the fate of the Jedi, he kept his talent hidden. He would eventually join the Empire and served in a construction team that was sent to the planet Malrev IV to construct an outpost in a Sith temple. After the Battle of Endor, his comrades on the planet left to help the Empire in the following conflicts. The Devaronian stayed after discovering the latent powers within the Sith Temple that haunted his former companions. He studied the ways of the Sith with the intention of becoming the next Dark Lord, but he was unaware that his mediocre skills had been augmented by the magics of the temple. thumb|left|180px|Cartariun In isolation for several years, the former Imperial lost his mind but gained enough knowledge of Sith magic to extend his power to the native tribes of the Irrukiine, driving the wolf-like locals into a frenzied bloodlust while controlling their every action through his dark power. Cartariun suffered the fate of all those who claimed power through the dark side when Rogue Squadron, on a rescue mission for a crashed Bothan passenger liner, discovered the reclusive madman scheming to reclaim the Emperor's throne. Although he managed to capture Wedge Antilles and had his servants attack the Rogues, the former escaped and his squadron mates defeated Cartariun's cohorts.
