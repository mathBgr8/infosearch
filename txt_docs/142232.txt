|locations= |cost= |value= |structure= |size= |material= |length= |width= |height= |weight= |protection= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation= }} The Sossen-7 sublight engine was a powerful model of sublight engine that was utilized in Senator Padmé Amidala's personal J-type star skiff during the Clone Wars of 22 BBY. The engines were one facet of the transport that indicated the craft's combat-readiness, and she went on to utilize Sossens in her ship until the end of the war, three years later. According to the official Databank on StarWars.com, Sossen-7s were installed in the skiff used by Amidala in Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith and the Star Wars: The Clone Wars film.
