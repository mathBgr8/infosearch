Birtraub Brothers Storage and Reclamation Center
743

The Birtraub Brothers Storage and Reclamation Center was an immense storage center near Crovna's main spaceport. A mass of gray buildings, it served forty ships at any given time. It was a front for the criminal enterprises of the Birtraub Brothers. The outlet on Crovna housed some of the BloodScar pirate gang's stolen items in warehouse 13 around 0.5 ABY. *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Buildings
