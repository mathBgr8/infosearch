The Ballad of Nomi Sunrider
755

"The Ballad of Nomi Sunrider" was a song composed by Tionne in 11 ABY, shortly after the opening of the Jedi Praxeum. It was composed after weeks of research at the Holocron, and detailed the exploits of Nomi Sunrider during the Great Sith War. During its first performance, it was interrupted by Kyp Durron, who interjected his own pro-Exar Kun viewpoints. *Dark Apprentice *I, Jedi *The Jedi Academy Sourcebook Category:Ballads
