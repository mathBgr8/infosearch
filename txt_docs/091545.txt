Riha Archipelago

The Riha Archipelago was a group of inhabited islands on Rellnas Minor. Nanilas was the root language of the eight different languages spoken by the Rellarin people who lived in the Riha Archipelago. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies Category:Islands Category:Rellnas Minor locations
