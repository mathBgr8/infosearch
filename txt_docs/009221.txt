Bagsho
1353

Bagsho was the largest free port in, and the capital city of, Nim Drovis. It was split into a New Town and an Old Town, with a canal running between the two. Most of the port was made of stone structures, topped with rickety wooden platforms, balconies, and bridges. Near the Old Town was the housing area known as Thousand Stinking Ditches.
