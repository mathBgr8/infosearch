Kell Plath Corporation
491

Kell Plath Corporation was a conglomerate that held many businesses and housing establishments in the city of Sodonna on the planet Teyr. The company also owned the Koqus liner Star Morning which was a huge help during the migration of the Fallanassi to the Koornacht Cluster. *Shield of Lies *Tyrant's Test Category:Conglomerates
