Ministry of War (Five Worlds)
1184

|dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Five Worlds }} The Ministry of War was the civilian ministry that controlled the armed forces of the Five Worlds. Its jurisdiction included the Corellian Defense Force, Corellian Security Force, and a secret starfleet being produced at the Kiris shipyards. It was under the control of the Minister of War, and headquartered in Coronet City. Its successor was the Ministry of Defense. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Corellian military units War
