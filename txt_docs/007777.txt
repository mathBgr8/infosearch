Jamoh Hogra
1812

Jamoh Hogra was a Zabrak mercenary who lived around the time of the Jedi Civil War. Fearful for his life after a raid on a Sith dreadnought, Hogra spent a fortune on his armor, only to be killed while in the bath by one of his many enemies.
