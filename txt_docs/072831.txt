Trix-sor
2589

Trix-sor was a retired Sic-Six. Trix-sor worked as a "fanger" for an Imperial noble, injecting both the aristocrat and his friends with his natural "poison" which caused a state of delirium. A ryll addict with expensive tastes, Trix-sor eventually retired to Cloud City to live a life full of fine food, gambling, and constant celebration. He lived in a private suite at the H'gaard Park Towers hotel overlooking Figg Plaza and refused to become a citizen of Cloud City so that he could openly enjoy the city's casinos and gambling. Trix-sor lived off his fortune and was on good terms with his neighbors, many of whom were members of the Exex. He also had a great number of friends who stopped by for brief visits. Trix-sor was hired to be a spy on Cloud City by the minor Imperial noble Lord Spenz. Trix-sor reports on events and goings-on in Cloud City to Spenz, who hopes to use the information to increase his own power and influence. A polite being, Trix-sor was also sarcastic and found great joy in challenge of beating others in games of chance but was an exceptionally good sport. Trix-sor was fluent in nearly thirty languages but his own speech was high-pitched and peppered with squeaks and chirps. Trix-sor wore fine robes, and carried chemical vials and a small sack of ryll.
