Oracle (Viper)
1610

thumb|right|250px|Oracle launches from the [[Corrupter (Imperial-class)|Corrupter.]] Oracle is the name of a group of viper probe droids that were in the service of the Galactic Empire in the year 3 ABY. Oracle was stationed aboard the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Corrupter and commanded by its captain, Admiral Garreth Holtz. After the Imperial victory at the Battle of Hoth in 3 ABY, Admiral Holtz was assigned to track down and destroy the Defiance Task Force, a Rebel Alliance fleet assigned to escort the survivors from Hoth to safe airspace. Holtz hoped to do this by deploying viper probe droids to scan outer space for the fleet's location, and Probe Group Oracle was one of the probe groups assigned to this task. When the Corrupter Task Force deployed their probes, they were attacked by a pair of A-wings from the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Defiance whose assignment was to eliminate these probes. At least five probes from Oracle were launched from the Corrupter, but all of them were destroyed by Ace Azzameen. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Hyperdrive pods Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
