Frona Zeffla
1514

Frona Zeffla was a female individual who served as an employee in the Imperial Palace on the planet Coruscant. Zeffla died at her desk in her office within the Palace sometime prior to 16 ABY. Following her death, Zeffla's body was not discovered for over a year. Frona Zeffla was mentioned in the 1996 novel, Tyrant's Test, which was written by Michael P.
