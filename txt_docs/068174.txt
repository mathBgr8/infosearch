O. Williams. It was published in 1986 by Happy House, an imprint of Random House. This book shows the Ewoks living at ground level rather than fifty meters up in their tree village. In a flashback scene, Wicket is but a wokling, yet he is wearing a hood not given to him until a later date. Pablo Hidalgo of the Lucasfilm Story Group is seen reading this book in the Rebels Recon segment for the Star Wars Rebels episode "The Holocrons of Fate."
