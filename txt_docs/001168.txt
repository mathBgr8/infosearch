Leeanna Walsman
3345

}} Leeanna Walsman is an Australian actress who portrayed the bounty hunter Zam Wesell in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. She later reprised her role in the video game Star Wars: Bounty Hunter as well as LEGO Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga. Walsman was discovered by Robin Gurland, the casting director for Attack of the Clones, while she was performing with Rose Byrne (Dormé) in the play La Dispute for the Sydney Theatre Company. Walsman shared screen with Star Wars co-star Graeme Blundell (Ruwee Naberrie) in the 2000 film Looking for Alibrandi.
