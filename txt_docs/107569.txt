Crislyn
1472

}} Crislyn was a female reporter who worked for the Galactic News Network at the Indu GNN bureau on Indu San. She was present at the bureau when Kella Rand discovered proof in her holocam recordings of Council Authority officer Kaleb Darme planting a bomb in the midst of the Indu Council. Cryslin was created by Laurie Burns for the 1995 short story "Kella Rand, Reporting..." In it, she had only one line of dialogue.
