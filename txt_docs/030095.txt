|lifespan= |origin=Sonn Vilmari |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Pan-preneur were amphibious sentients indigenous to Sonn Vilmari. They resembled many other aquatic races, and were believed to be distantly related genetically to the Mon Calamari, though many aquatic species shared similar genetics.
