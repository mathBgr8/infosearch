**Rebecca Chastain |publisher=Prima Games |release date=March 8, 2005 |type=Paperback |pages=128 |dimensions=8.3 x 0.3 x 10.7 inches |weight=8.8 ounces |isbn=076154710X |series=Republic Commando |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Republic Commando: Prima Official Game Guide is a computer game strategy guide for Star Wars: Republic Commando, written by Michael Knight and published by Prima Games in 2005. *Detailed strategies for all 3 campaigns of precise military-style operations' *Extensive maps for every mission' *Comprehensive stats for all weapons and equipment' *Enemies exposed: a complete breakdown of each enemy's weapons and weaknesses' *Multiplayer tips and tactics' *Exclusive art gallery—a look behind the graphics' The Game guide opened with the Prologue from the 2004 novel Republic Commando: Hard Contact, a tie-in to the video game Star Wars: Republic Commando. Told from the perspective of the Republic Commando Theta Squad member Darman, who retells their first and last mission to the planet Geonosis&mdash;the squad quickly gets overwhelmed and, save for Darman, they all perish. The prologue is followed by a short "How to use this guide" section, outlining the contents of the guide and the purpose of each section. The guide also includes the one-page long short story The Tale of the Aiwha Pod written by Ryan Kaufman. The game guide, written by Michael Knight, was released by Prima Games on March 8, 2005 in paperback format. The book's dimensions were 8.3 x 0.3 x 10.7 inches and it had 128 pages, weighing at 8.8 ounces. Designed by Simon Olney and Derek Hocking, the product manager was Mario De Govia with Christy Seifert as the editorial supervisor and Rebecca Chastain editing the work.
