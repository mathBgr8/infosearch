Saelt-Marae's mate
978

Saelt-Marae's mate was a female Yarkora who had mated with Saelt-Marae, a member of Jabba Desilijic Tiure's court. Their relationship was documented in a collection of digital recordings made by Marae, which were discovered on an unnamed planet in the Outer Rim Territories. *The New Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Females Category:Yarkora
