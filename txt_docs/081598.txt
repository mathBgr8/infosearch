Striker projectile pistol
3737

*60 meters |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation= }} The Striker projectile pistol was a typical slugthrower pistol manufactured by Oriolanis Defense Systems. Considered to be durable but otherwise unimpressive, the Striker had an ammunition clip of eight slugs. Only a direct hit on a vital area was likely to kill a target. The Striker was readily available, even on planets with strict weapons regulations, and was favored by those in the Outer Rim Territories. Huff Darklighter possessed a Striker pistol, and offered it as a reward to a spacer. The pistol was also favored by spy agents.
