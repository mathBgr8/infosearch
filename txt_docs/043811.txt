Kasa Horansi
1930

The Kasa Horansi were a subspecies of the Horansi species native to Mutanda. Kasa stood on average between 2 and 2.7 meters. Although there was variation as to the reddish-orange to light tan coloring of their fur, their black stripes truly marked their race. They had thick tufts of hair behind their triangular ears. They were the most intelligent of the Horansi subspecies and were considered to be brave and trustworthy. Because of their curiosity, the Kasa acquired off-world knowledge, and some had even left the planet to explore the wider Galaxy. They had developed low level technology but obtained blasters, medicines, and repulsorlift vehicles by trading with representatives on Justa. Their tribes were generally peaceful, but they did occasionally have skirmishes with the Gorvan Horansi. All tribal leaders were albinos which was thought to be an old custom.
