Gravel-maggot
2949

Gravel-maggots were worms found in the badlands of the planet Tatooine. They were known to feed on rotting flesh of other creatures. Despite the obscurity of Tatooine, gravel-maggots were infamous throughout the galaxy, and it was a common if uncreative insult to call someone "gravel-maggot" or the "son of a gravel-maggot."Granta Omega, disguised as Hunti Pereg, uses "son of a gravel-maggot" in Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi. *Legacy of the Jedi *Episode I Adventures 6: The Hunt for Anakin Skywalker *Star Wars: Episode I Racer *Episode I: The Phantom Menace ½ *Star Wars (1998) 7 *Jedi Quest: The Trail of the Jedi *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games *The Last of the Jedi: Against the Empire *Star Wars: A New Hope novelization *Rebel Force: Trapped *Star Wars Galaxies: The Ruins of Dantooine *Splinter of the Mind's Eye 2 *X-Wing: The Bacta War *Legacy (2006) 1 *Legacy (2006) 3 *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe *Galaxy Guide 7: Mos Eisley *Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Creatures of Tatooine Category:Desert creatures Category:Maggots Category:Saprophagous creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
