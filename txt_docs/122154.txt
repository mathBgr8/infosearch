Bowman Gavin
2080

Bowman Gavin was a Human male B-wing pilot who served in the Rebel Alliance and later the New Republic during the Galactic Civil War. In 4 ABY, he became a member of Blue Squadron where he flew during the battle of Endor. The battle was a success when the second Death Star was destroyed, which included the deaths of Emperor Palpatine and Darth Vader.
