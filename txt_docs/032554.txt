She acquired the ship sometime before 11 ABY from a Randoni trader on one of her frequent research trips to Borgo Prime, literally for the price of a song. Tionne often took young Jedi with her on these trips, using it as an opportunity for practical experience. The yacht was an ancient style, deploying solar sails to harness solar wind and power the ship. There is some confusion over when Tionne acquired this ship. In Firestorm, set 11 ABY, Tionne has a ship by this name.
