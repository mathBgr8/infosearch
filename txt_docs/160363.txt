G2-GS
1072

The G2-GS was an industrial beamdrill manufactured by Karflo Corporation. It was a modified G2-GE beamdrill that sported a life support bubble and small ion engines for use in shipyards. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Beamdrills Category:Karflo Corporation products
