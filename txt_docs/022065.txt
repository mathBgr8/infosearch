Hydra (Imperial I-class)
3145

The Hydra was one of four Imperial I-class Star Destroyers stationed at the Maw Installation as part of Admiral Natasi Daala's squadron. The Hydra was destroyed when Han Solo - accompanied by Qwi Xux, Chewbacca, and Kyp Durron - flew the Sun Crusher through her control bridge during the group's escape from the Maw Installation.
