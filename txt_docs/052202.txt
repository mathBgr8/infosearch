Muurian transport
3541

The Muurian transport was a light freighter manufactured by Muurian Interstellar. The 30.5 meter long wedge-shaped freighter was designed to compete with CEC's YT-series and both designs had comparable speeds, which were fast for a freighter and able to match that of a Lambda-class shuttle or Delta-class DX-9 transport. While it had a weaker hull and shields than a stock YT-1300 light freighter, it had a heavier standard armament than the YT-1300 with two double laser cannon turrets, three laser cannons, and two proton torpedo launchers.
