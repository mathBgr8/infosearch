Rampart-class anticoncussion generator
907

The Rampart-class anticoncussion generator was a type of anticoncussion field generator. The Rampart-class anticoncussion generator was used in the Stars' End prison on Mytus VII to supplement the structure's molecularly bonded armor. It absorbed and dispersed kinetic energy, neutralizing blasts, collisions, and impacts.
