Battle of Dellalt
2099

The Battle of Dellalt was one of the last battles of the Clone Wars Outer Rim Sieges. After Order 66, a number of Jedi managed to escape here, but were pursued by Imperial forces. Six of the surviving Jedi stole an SX troop shuttle, disguising it as the Vagabond Trader.
