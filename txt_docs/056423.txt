Stalwart (Victory-class)
3307

Stalwart was a Victory I-class Star Destroyer commanded by Commander Maloc and used by then-Vice Admiral Thrawn as his flagship approximately one year before the Battle of Endor. thumb|left|250px|The Stalwart over [[NL-1.]] From the bridge of the Stalwart, Thrawn supervised the construction of the space station NL-1 in the Pakuuni system during the Battle on the Frontier. It would also serve as the base-of-operations for actions against the Pakuuni Pirates and Rebels who threatened the station, success in no small part due to the actions of ace pilot Maarek Stele.
