|published in=Star Wars Tales 11 |reprinted in= |pages=8 |timeline= |series= |preceded by="The Princess Leia Diaries" |followed by="Ghost" }} "Tall Tales" is an eight-page comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 11 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 3. In a cantina, five patrons play cards and share stories about their own takes on the events of the original trilogy, none of them entirely accurate or truthful. They tell tales of how Obi-Wan Kenobi attacked a squad of stormtroopers in the Mos Eisley cantina and stopped them from taking Luke Skywalker to his father, the Emperor, how Han Solo, after murdering Greedo in cold blood, helped Chewbacca, and C-3PO burn down a cantina full of women and children in their escape from Hoth, and how Princess Leia stole the Millennium Falcon from the first Death Star. As their game draws to a close, a Morseerian working for Black Sun attempts to kidnap one of the players, the son of Senator Amara. Another player, a cloaked stranger, quickly springs into action and kills him with a single shot. As he leaves, the rogueish grin he flashes shows him to be none other than Han Solo.
