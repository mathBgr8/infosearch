Spitfire (shuttle)
1452

thumb|right|250px|Spitfire under attack from an Imperial [[TIE/ad starfighter|TIE Avenger.]] Rebel Alliance Delta-class JV-7 escort shuttle Spitfire served in the Rebel fleet during the Galactic Civil War. Spitfire escorted CR90 corvettes Triumph during the Treachery at Ottega. Shuttle Spitfire futilely attacked Imperial starfighters in attempt to save MC40a light cruiser Warhawk and defecting Victory-class Star Destroyer Protector, only to be destroyed. *Star Wars: TIE Fighter *TIE Fighter: The Official Strategy Guide *TIE Fighter Collector's CD-ROM: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Alliance to Restore the Republic starship groups *
