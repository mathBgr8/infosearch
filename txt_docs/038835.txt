In 22 BBY during the Clone Wars, Lenoan was present at the unveiling ceremony of the Aethersprite-class interceptor at the Kuat Drive Yards, where she symbolically cut a tether tied to the prototype's landing gear. During the test run, the senator lauded its performance, alongside Jedi Knights Aayla Secura and Adi Gallia and the project's Lead Engineer, Walex Blissex. She was removed from office by the Kuati nobility when it was discovered she might have pilfered Kuat Drive Yards funding.
