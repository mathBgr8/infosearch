Death Watch Armor
1766

Death Watch Armor was the armor worn by a break-off of Mandalorian warriors, the Death Watch by 60 BBY. The armor was made of beskar, a metal found on Mandalore, the homeworld of the Mandalorians, which made it almost indestructible.
