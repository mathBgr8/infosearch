Hurana
2393

Hurana was a female student who was a member of a squad of young mercenaries that trained at the elite Leadership School of Andara during the final years of the Galactic Republic before the Clone Wars. She was in charge of arranging transportation for the group. A slight girl with pale gold eyes, Hurana's talent as an aggressive swoop-bike rider belied her shy nature. Hurana was the being placed in charge of transportation for a squad of young mercenaries that studied at the school. During the time that they accepted a mission from the ruler of Ieria, Rana Halion, Hurana was shopping the market for a couple of A-6 Interceptors. The other members of the band were Gillam Tarturi, Rolai Frac, Tulah, Marit Dice, and Ze. During the mission on Ieria, Gillam and the rest of the band pretended to let the young Padawan Anakin Skywalker join the squad. Gillam then attempted to murder Skywalker, but Anakin single-handedly defeated all six mercenaries. Marit convinced Hurana, Tulah, and Ze that Gillam had betrayed them and was using them for revenge, not justice.
