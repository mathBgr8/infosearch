T1-B hovertank
3419

The T1-B hovertank was a tank used by the Rebel Alliance during the early stages of the Galactic Civil War. The T1-B hovertank, known colloquially as a "Rebel hover tank", was manufactured by Yutrane-Trackata for the Alliance to Restore the Republic to use in their fight against the Galactic Empire. Intended for use against ground infantry and aircraft, the tank was armed with laser cannons and flak pods. The T1-B was lightly armored and came equipped with a deflector shield.
