Battle of M2934738
2063

The Battle of M2934738 occurred in 9 ABY. It was the result of an ambush staged by Imperial warlord Delak Krennel against a New Republic's supply convoy en route to Liinade III. As a gesture of good faith, Krennel rescued the New Republic survivors and offered them parole on condition they never again take up arms for the New Republic. *X-Wing: Isard's Revenge *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Battles in the Tragan Cluster Category:Battles of the Hegemony theater
