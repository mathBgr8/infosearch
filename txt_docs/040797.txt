Kaminoan nano-virus vaccine
417

The Kaminoan nano-virus vaccine was a vaccine made by Sayn Ta to protect clone troopers of the Galactic Republic from the Kaminoan nano-virus. After Ta was killed by Kuma Nai, the creator of the virus, the vaccine was injected into all clones. Category:Vaccines and cures
