J'ilk Torlin
1154

J'ilk Torlin was a male Ishi Tib Jedi Master who used a training remote to teach his students. This Jedi was the creation of artist Joe Corroney. Note that, though the character is official, his name is not: it is the name given the character on Joe Corroney's website. The name was given to the character by fan Joshua Schmucker. Thus, the name "J'ilk Torlin" is not a part of strict canon. *Star Wars Gamer 3 Category:Ishi Tib Category:Jedi Masters of the Jedi Order Category:Males
