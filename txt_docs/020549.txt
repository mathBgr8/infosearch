It was the second of two gas giants in the Arbran system, located in the Bon'nyuw-Luq sector, a part of the Trailing Sectors portion of the Outer Rim Territories. The planet was orbited by eleven moons. Arbran V was mentioned in "Hole in the Ground," the first article in Arbra: Sanctuary In the Storm, a series of Planet Hoppers roleplaying articles written by Cory J. Herndon for Wizards of the Coast's Star Wars Roleplaying Game. The article was published on Wizards.com on June 5, 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Arbran system, and therefore Arbran V, in grid square N-17.
