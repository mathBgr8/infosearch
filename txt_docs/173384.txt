Model RDP-12 Rocket Launcher
2013

The Model RDP-12 Rocket Launcher was a model of rocket launcher designed by Locris Syndicates. Designated as a portable self-propelled rocket delivery system, the RDP-12 could be loaded with either Type-12A Anti-Personnel Rockets or Type-12B Hollow-Tip Rockets respectively; with the launcher capable of using either an internal magazine of 4 rockets or an external magazine of 20 rockets. A weapon with a moderate rate of fire, the launcher fired micro rockets; a burst fired rocket in comparison to a study burn rocket, which inflicted not only kinetic damage on impact, but also a small one meter explosion (Incendiary or gas respectively).
