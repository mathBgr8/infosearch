Ixll language
665

The language of the Ixll&mdash;small avian creatures native to Da Soocha V&mdash;consisted of high-pitched chirps, clicks, and whistles that traveled long distances in the thin atmosphere. The language was capable of reprogramming astromech droids. *Dark Empire 2 *Dark Empire 3 *Dark Empire 6 *Dark Empire Sourcebook *The Essential Guide to Alien Species Category:Languages
