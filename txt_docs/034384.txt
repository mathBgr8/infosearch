For these acts, he was wanted by Thesmian authorities. Not long after the Battle of Yavin, Tyne visited the Junction City cantina on the planet Feriae Junction. He was tracked there by the bounty hunter Beilert Valance, who killed him with a blaster shot to the chest, much to the shock of the cantina visitors. Claiming the bounty on Tyne's head, Valance forced two cantina patrons to carry the slaver's body out of the establishment. A brown-haired Zygerrian, Tyne engaged in slavery, the traditional business of his species.Galaxy at War'Star Wars: The Clone Wars: Slaves of the Republic He owned a blaster. Marko Tyne first appeared in the twenty-seventh issue of the Star Wars comic series. The issue was written by Archie Goodwin, illustrated by Carmine Infantino, and released on June 26, 1979 by Marvel Comics.
