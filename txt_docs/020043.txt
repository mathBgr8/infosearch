Emotional Hostages (song)

"Emotional Hostages" was a song by Annadale Fayde that was released on an album of the same name, and on a later album entitled Darkness on the Land. It was given a Scarlet rating by the Imperial Board of Culture. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Songs by Annadale Fayde
