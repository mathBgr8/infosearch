Battle Tactician
947

Battle Tactician was a title for a member of the warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Battle Tacticians were usually more strategy-based than the average warrior, and were usually more prone to statistics and assessments with their enhanced brain.The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial They also had a much calmer temperament than the average warrior. Battle Tacticians were usually found in the command chambers of war vessels, advising their Commanders of the status of their fleets. The warrior Raff and unnamed Nas Choka's tactician served as Battle Tacticians. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Yuuzhan Vong ranks
