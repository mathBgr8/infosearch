Haj Shipping Lines
1117

Haj Shipping Lines was a starline transportation service owned by the Haj family. Two of the ships they owned were the Star of Empire and its sister ship, Queen of Empire. The company stayed neutral in the Galactic Civil War, courting both the Galactic Empire and the Rebel Alliance, allowing it to prosper from multiple sources.
