|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Artist |companies=*Dark Horse Comics *Marvel Comics |website= }} Michael Atiyeh is a colorist who has colored many Star Wars comics over the years, for both Dark Horse and Marvel Comics. He was one of many artists and writers acquired by Dark Horse after CrossGen Comics went bankrupt. Atiyeh has also worked for DC, Image, and Wizard Entertainment.
