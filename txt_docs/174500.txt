Superior Boarding Armor
1425

The MerrWeapons Superior Boarding Armor was a simplified version of spacetrooper armor, yet still adequate for most boarding operations. It was self-repairing and capable of up to 24 hours continuous use. It had a propulsion system powered by a rocket. If this ran out, Gunman discovered that it was possible to vent some air through the rockets to provide some momentum, although the user had to be careful not to deplete their air supply too much.
