Tirsa Wargear
1709

|role=Vehicle manufacturer |products=*LandspeedersStar Wars: The Old Republic: Galactic Strongholds *Leviathan Submersible Carrier |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Tirsa Wargear, headquartered on Tirsa, was a vehicle manufacturer. During the Galactic War, they produced a large number of landspeeder models including the Capital, Champion, Contender, Elite, Paramount, Pinnacle, Premier, Prime, and Victor. During the era of the Galactic Empire's rise to power, Tirsa Wargear manufactured the Leviathan-class Submersible Carrier. They were ordered to shut down their weapons productions in the early years of the New Order, but stayed in business selling spare parts.
