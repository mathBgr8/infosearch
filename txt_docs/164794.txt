Renhoek (transports)
1693

Renhoek was a group of at least six Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports based on the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Stimsenj'kat. In 0 ABY, they participated in the recovery of much needed lonsigar from abandoned cargo containers in an asteroid field, and faced the first combat deployment of the TIE Advanced against the Rebellion. All of the transports completed their mission and fled aboard the Stimsenj'kat. The name Renhoek is similar to "Ren Höek," a lead character from the cartoon series Ren & Stimpy. The name of the transport group's home cruiser Stimsenj'kat is similar to "Stimpson J. Cat," the series' other lead character.
