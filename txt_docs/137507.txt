MD-8 medical droid
1278

The MD-8 medical droid was a model of medical droid and a part of the MD-series that was manufactured by Industrial Automaton. Like the company's MD-7 and MD-9 models, the MD-8 was a prototype medical droid used only in limited sectors. *The New Rebellion *The Essential Guide to Droids MD-08
