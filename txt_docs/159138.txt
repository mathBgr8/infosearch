Jitte
2161

Jitte was an Ton-Falk-class escort carrier under the command of Grand Admiral Zaarin. It carried TIE/LN starfighters that were modified to carry shield generators. It attempted to destroy a TIE/D Defender research and development facility, but it was destroyed by Gamma-class ATR-6 assault transports under escort of Tan Maarek Stele.
