Jen'ari
818

Jen'ari was the word in the Sith for "Dark Lord." It was originally used to delineate the Exiles who survived the Hundred-Year Darkness. Thus, it gave rise to the title of the Dark Lord of the Sith. *Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *Book of Sith: Secrets from the Dark Side Category:Phrases Category:Sith ranks
