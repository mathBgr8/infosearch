The system was centered around the star Doornik-628. Doornik-628F was a gas giant and was circled by eleven moons. Doornik-628F was mentioned in "J't'p'tan: Temples and Tybis," an article written by Jason Fry and published on Wizards.com on January 30, 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Doornik-628 system, and therefore Doornik-628F, in grid square K-10.
