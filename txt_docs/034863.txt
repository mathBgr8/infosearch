|class= |subspecies= |races= |height= |length= |wingspan= |mass= |skincolor= |haircolor= |feathers= |eyecolor= |distinctions=*Leathery scales *Communication via electric field vibrations |lifespan= |origin=Klytonia system |habitat= |diet= |language= }} The Klytonians were a sentient species originating in the Klytonia system of the Outer Rim Territories' Bheriz sector. Their bodies were covered with leathery scales, which produced electric field vibrations that allowed them to communicate with each other. In 0 BBY,The events of take place during Luke Skywalker and Ben Kenobi's visit to Mos Eisley, which is depicted in and dated to 0 BBY by The New Essential Chronology. multiple Klytonians were present at Chalmun's Spaceport Cantina in the Mos Eisley settlement on the Arkanis sector planetThe Essential Atlas Tatooine. The Klytonians communicated with each other across the room they were in. The Klytonians were created by John Chesterman for "Cantina Communications," a one-page vignette included in Star Wars Official Poster Monthly 16, published in 1979 by Paradise Press.
