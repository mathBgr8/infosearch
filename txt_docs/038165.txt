Exoticalia Pet Emporium
1152

Exoticalia Pet Emporium was an exotic pet store in Drev'starn, Bothawui owned by Drend Navett and Klif. The store was set up as a cover for Vengeance's mission to bring down Bothawui's planetary shield before a battle at Bothawui. The shop was near the planetary shield generator. In order to avoid too much attention, they kept polpians, which many Bothans were allergic to, in stock.
