Lon Drasna
474

Lon Drasna was the capital city of the planet Dandrian. It was a sprawling cityport which contained The Watering Hole, among other cantinas. Many illicit swoop races were held in the town. Nothing was said to happen in the city without Sti Groon knowing. Category:Capital cities
