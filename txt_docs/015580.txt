Paul Sudlow
2665

Paul Sudlow was a Star Wars line editor and designer at West End Games from 1995 to 1998. Notable projects include Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game, Second Edition, Revised and Expanded, The Jedi Academy Sourcebook, Lords of the Expanse, Heroes & Rogues, and The DarkStryder Campaign. He also contributed to Decipher's Star Wars CCG and Coruscant and the Core Worlds by Wizards of the Coast.
