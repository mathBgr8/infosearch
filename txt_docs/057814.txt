Kulltu Kutta fought in the Cauldron on sowsquatchback when Asajj Ventress entered the fray and ultimately emerged victorious over the other gladiators. 280px|left|thumb|Kulltu Kutta kills Krutch Kulltu Kutta came from a long line of hired fighters and, shortly after the outbreak of the Clone Wars,The New Essential Chronology became a gladiator, eventually ending up in the The Cauldron arena. It was the largest gladiatorial pit on the planet Rattatak, filled with violent warriors, where war barons and generals sought out the best combatants to win their wars. Therefore, Kulltu Kutta, like many other gladiators, entered a deadly free-for-all battle,The New Essential Guide to Droids but it was a rare gladiator indeed who could survive multiple fights there. In one battle, Kulltu Kutta fought atop a sowsquatch and attacked another gladiator, Krutch, splitting the opponent in half with a blade. However, a short time later, Kulltu Kutta was killed by a droid adversary. Relative to the other gladiators of the Cauldron, Kulltu Kutta was a rather short-statured alien. During combat in the Rattataki arena, Kulltu Kutta wore a mask and brown armor and wielded a sword in the left hand. In battle, Kutta rode a huge, barely tamed sowsquatch, gripping the harness handle with one hand. Kulltu Kutta first appeared as an unnamed character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003.
