Iskalonian language
1079

Iskalonian was the language of the peoples that inhabited the planet Iskalon. In Iskalonian, children were referred to as "fry." When Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa came to Iskalon during the Galactic Civil War, they were affectionately referred to by Primor as both "little fry" and "smally fry." *Star Wars (1977) 74 *Star Wars (1977) 75 *The Essential Guide to Alien Species *Star Wars Gamer 7 Category:Languages
