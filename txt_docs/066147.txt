Golanda
1961

Golanda was a female Human who led the artillery innovations and tactical deployment at the Maw Installation. She died when Tol Sivron flew the Death Star prototype into a black hole while chasing the stolen Sun Crusher during the Battle of the Maw in 11 ABY.
