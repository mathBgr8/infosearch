C-9PO (Brakiss)
1877

C-9PO, pronounced as See-Ninepio or Ninepio for short, was a copper-colored protocol droid that was purchased and modified by Brakiss for use on Telti. C-9PO first appeared in the 1996 novel The New Rebellion, written by Kristine Kathryn Rusch. Another droid designated C-9PO appeared in the 1999 novel The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime, written by R.
