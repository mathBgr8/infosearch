|locations= |role=Megacorporation |products=Assassin droid matrixes and mission specific components |founding= |dissolved=0 ABY Pirates & Privateers |affiliation= }} SGI Systems, Inc. was a megacorporation headquartered on the planet Druckenwell with diverse holdings. They were also know for producing assassin droid matrixes and mission specific components for the black market. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the Galactic Empire nationalized the company alongside Bansche Tech, Reiber Manufacturing, and Sencil Corporation.
