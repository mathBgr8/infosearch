Gann (Ferroan)
1413

Gann was a strong male Ferroan living in the Middle Distance on Zonama Sekot in 29 BBY after immigrating there nearly 60 years prior. He was an instructor and greeter for those who came to Zonama Sekot to obtain a Sekotan Personal Starship.
