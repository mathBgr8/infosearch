Chessa's Doom
5115

"Chessa's Doom" is a short story written by Peter M. Schweighofer with illustrations by John Paul Lona. It was originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 1 in February 1994 by West End Games before being made available online at Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club in January 2008 It is notable in that parts of the narrative are told in prose and parts are told in textless comic book style illustrations. In the Gorkin's Rest cantina on Wroona, Dirk Harkness tells the story of how he became involved with the Alliance to Restore the Republic. Following the death of his lover, Chessa Dohhenty in a firefight with stormtroopers checking their cargo, Harkness attempts to drown his sorrows in alcohol. As he stares at his reflection in the mirror, he realizes how lost he has become. Just then, a man approaches him, claiming to be a friend of Chessa's.
