Force Illusion
1467

Force Illusion was a Force ability that allowed the user to generate illusions. This power was useful in combat, allowing a Force-sensitive individual to make copies of themselves to make it harder to find them or cause their opponent to lose focus by making them see things they feared. When Jedi Knight Cal Kestis faced the fallen Jedi Dagan Gera at the Koboh Observatory, both used this ability, with which Kestis unbalanced Gera, allowing him and his ally to kill him. *Star Wars Jedi: Survivor Category:Force powers
