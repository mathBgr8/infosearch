Inspired by the Cartoon Network's Clone Wars cartoons, this comic-book series uses the television show as a jumping-off point to tell new stories of heroism and villainy in the same stripped-down visual style. Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker Take to the skies to battle a deadly armada! Luminara Unduli and her Padawan Barriss Offee Lead a small army of clone troopers against the dreaded General Grievous! Jedi Master Mace Windu Goes solo to foil a sinister Separatist plot!
