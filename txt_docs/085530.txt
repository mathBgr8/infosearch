Aleson Gray
1288

Aleson Gray was a Hapan male who served as the Dukat and Duch'da to Lady AlGray of the Relephon Moons as of 35 ABY. The ninth cousin of Queen Mother Tenel Ka, he commanded the Kendall during the Swarm War. When he encountered Han Solo and Leia Organa Solo he told them about Jacen Solo's encounter with Tenel Ka; it appeared that this encounter was well known among the Hapans who considered Jacen to be her consort. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Hapans Category:Males
