Desset Fabien
377

Desset Fabien is a Star Wars fan who designed the Pitrillistians and Pitrillistia in the Design An Alien contest held in Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. The entry was given an honorable mention in the 16-years-and-older category. He was 19 at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
