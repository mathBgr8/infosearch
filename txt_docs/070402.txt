Feleema
931

Feleema was the wife of the surgeon Erel Kersos. Erel broke the rules of Corellia marrying an ekster (a non-Corellian person). Erel left his home planet for Feleema. Unfortunately, six years later, Feleema died in a Maglev train accident on Coruscant. *MedStar II: Jedi Healer Category:Females Category:Humans
