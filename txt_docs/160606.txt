The Exotic Exhibit was a bar on Nar Shaddaa located in the Corellian sector and had live dancers and shows. In 5 BBY Han Solo visited the place when he searched for his old friend Mako Spince on the Smugglers' Moon. *The Hutt Gambit Category:Bars and clubs Category:Corellian Sector locations
