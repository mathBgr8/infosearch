Gamine
3391

Gamine was a telepathic Constancian female who served in the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. Gamine was on her way back home with Luke Skywalker after she discovered a way to save her people from the Empire. When they emerged from hyperspace, the Imperials blasted their ship, but not before Luke jettisoned C-3PO and R2-D2 where he knew Han would find them. While Gamine was away, the Empire came to her planet and discovered that the Constancians had telepathic powers. They threatened to destroy their planet if they did not put their ability to their disposal. Luke and Gamine escaped the exploding craft in a lifepod. A person named Sharlee, who was in Outpost 8 at the time, tractor-beamed Luke and Gamine to the Outpost. She agreed to help put Gamine's plan into action. When Han Solo, Chewbacca, C-3PO, R2-D2, and Gyla Petro landed in Outpost 8, Gamine begged Han to help fight off the Empire. He agreed to help, but only because it was convenient for him. Han and Sharlee took a lifepod and made sure it was boarded by Imperials. Then, they fought them off with battle armor. On the Millennium Falcon, Luke, Gamine, C-3PO, R2-D2, and Gyla fought off the Imperials guarding the planet while Han and Sharlee took a TIE/sa bomber and began to shoot down the rest. Two ships made it past Han and close to the Falcon, but Luke got into Gunport 2 and shot down one while Planetary defenses got the other.
