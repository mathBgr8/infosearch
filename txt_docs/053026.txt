Nai created the Kaminoan nano-virus and planned to spread it to all clone troopers. The plan failed when Jedi Kit Fisto and Aayla Secura arrived to investigate the sickness infecting the clones. As the Kaminoan tried to escape, she became involved in a struggle with Secura.
