Captain Dolman's shuttle was shot down on approach to the Emperor's Retreat, on the planet Naboo, by Rebels in 1.5 ABY. Dolman survived and Captain Thrawn then dispatched a spacer to escort Dolman back to the Emperor's Retreat. The Rebels who had attacked him were later eliminated. Captain Dolman was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
