Seifax
578

Seifax was a front company for the Loronar Corporation in 13 ABY which used it to sell its products to both the Galactic Empire and the New Republic. Its plant on Antemeridias had been purchasing miniaturized hyperdrives from the Bith. *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Front companies
