Sarell Harmion
1350

Sarell Harmion was an executive who worked for Gastess' Finance, Inc. Like his employer Sarlim Gastess and his brothers Halleb and K'yne, he operated by taking control of well-run corporations, ruthlessly running them for short-term profit, and leaving the ruins behind once every last credit was gone.
