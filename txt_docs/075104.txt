Trantor Center
523

Trantor Center was an exercise facility located most likely in the Zi-Kree Sector of Coruscant. It contained zero-gravity calisthenics equipment and a spa, among other things. Lorn Pavan was a frequent visitor there. *Darth Maul: Shadow Hunter Category:Coruscant buildings
