Beckman
1899

Beckman was a male Human officer who served as a Commander in the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. In the year 3 ABY, he was in command of the Rebel Hospital and its attending ships. The Hospital was attacked by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Corrupter during a bacta shipment being carried out by the Azzameen family. After the Battle of Hoth, Beckman led the base's evacuation until he was killed as the Imperial seized the hospital.
