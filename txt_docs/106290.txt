SyntheCure
853

SyntheCure was a medical equipment manufacturing company. They specialized in the production of Plasto-Cast. SyntheCure was also involved in producing dispensers for their product, which allowed for plasto-cast to be sprayed onto a broken limb. *Galladinium's Fantastic Technology *The Far Orbit Project Category:Medical companies
