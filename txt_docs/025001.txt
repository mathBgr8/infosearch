Alix Balan
1797

Alix Balan was a general serving the Imperial Coalition in 10 ABY. Following the Imperial Coalition conquest of the galactic capital, Coruscant, in 10 ABY, General Alix Balan led a formal ceremony to reconsecrate Imperial City.
