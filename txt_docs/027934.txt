Floating health spa
1948

Floating health spas were barges located in the habitable Life Zone surrounding the gas giants Bespin and Taloraan. thumb|left|200px|A spa seen from [[Millennium Falcon/Legends|Millennium Falcon cockpit.]] These barges were circular platforms under floating parasols, operated by a barge pilot. Doctors took dozens of patients into the mists of high-flying clouds, having them lie prone on the smooth deck while wearing wispy, sheetlike wraps, in order to receive the supposed therapeutic value of the atmospheric chemical haze in a so-called "cloud bath". The effectiveness of this treatment was never clinically proven, but many visitors to Bespin were lavish in their praise of the technique.
