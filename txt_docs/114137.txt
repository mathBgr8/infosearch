Before the clone wars Anakin Skywalker and his master Obi-Wan Kenobi were sent to a meeting with Sha-Mar Ma-dred. As the Jedi approached the king, Tiarri and the other wives (Kheann, Mirrix and Ashala) were present but were sent away after being introduced to do as they wished. Tiarri was in bed that night when she saw a Skywalker intrude in their room and alarmed the other wives Kheann and Mirrix who tackled him. Skywalker told them that someone stole his lightsaber. Tiarri complimented Skywalkerfor his looks and told him that Ashala took his lightsaber.
