Vincha was a plant with tubercles, which had incredible anesthetic proprieties. Vincha tubercles were white, not bigger than one's thumb, and highly esteemed by Dantari people. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide I: Onslaught Category:Anesthetics Category:Plants of Dantooine
