Batorine
2648

Batorine was the forested homeworld of the Blood Carver species. It joined the Galactic Republic in 110 BBY. The homeworld of the Blood Carver race, the planet Batorine was discovered by the Galactic Republic after the Ruusan Reformations. Embroiled in a terrible, centuries-long war, the planet's natives were nearly driven to extinction by a species known as the Lontars. Intervening in the conflict, the Galactic Senate demanded a cessation of violence on the world and Batorine formally joined the ancient institution in 110 BBY.
