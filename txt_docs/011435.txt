Indrexu Confederation
1863

The Indrexu Confederation was one of the feuding states that rose in the Tion Cluster from the ashes of Xim the Despot's empire, a century after his death. The region, originally called Far Indrexu, was discovered during the expansion of Xim's empire circa 25,100 BBY. It disappeared when the cluster joined the Republic. Millennia after, it was recreated on the same area as the Indrexu sector, when the Galactic Empire split the Tion Hegemony in multiple sectors.
