TB-47 time bomb
1833

The TB-47 time bombs were explosives manufactured by Merr-Sonn, and used by several military organizations, including the armies of the Rebel Alliance and Galactic Empire. They were strong enough to penetrate armor of a vehicle or starfighter and deplete tight forms of infantry. TB-47s played an important role in the destruction of the Endor shield generator and were carried around by pilots for use in boarding missions.
