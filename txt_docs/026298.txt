|pages= |timeline= |campaign= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Hired Guns" is a scenario for the Star Wars Miniatures game, by Sterling Hershey in 2005. It depicts a battle between the vicious Blackstar Raiders and a group of mercenaries hired to eliminate them. The Blackstar Raiders have terrorized planets for months. Anticipating the raid, Medara's Outpost has hired mercenary protection. The Raiders cleverly insert a small force and a spy to find the depot, but has revealed himself to the guards.
