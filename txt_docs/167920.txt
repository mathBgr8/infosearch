ANs-7e sensor unit
1061

The ANs-7e sensor unit was a type of sensor suite that was manufactured by Fabritech and could be found on the RZ-1 A-wing interceptor in service with the Rebel Alliance. The suites were equipped with PA-94 long range phased tachyon detection arrays and PG-7u short range primary threat analysis grids. *Star Wars Screen Entertainment *Star Wars Technical Journal of the Rebel Forces Category:Fabritech products Category:General purpose sensors Category:Starfighter sensors
