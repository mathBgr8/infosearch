Jonsey
2237

Jonsey was a male Human who served as a stormtrooper in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, he was assigned to the stormtrooper contingent aboard the Imperial I-class Star Destroyer Imperator. He was a member of the platoon that had been assigned to Cadet Leader Kyle Katarn for Katarn's Omega Exercise. He participated in the ensuing battle on AX-456 and when the platoon made it to the comm room, Hong ordered him to pull the memory modules from the transmitters before leaving the base.
