Might Squadron
1156

Might Squadron was a starfighter squadron of the Thyferran Home Defense Corps that served under the government instituted by Xucphra Corporation and headed by former Imperial Intelligence chief Ysanne Isard, flying TIE/IN interceptors. A green unit by the standards set by former Rogue Squadron pilot and Imperial agent Erisi Dlarit, the squadron's name became the subject of many jokes within the THDC, the popular refrain being that their name was the answer to the question "Will they fight?" During the Battle of Thyferra, that marked both the climax and resolution of the Bacta War, Might Squadron was annihilated down to the last starfighter by Dlrit's former unit, the Rogues. *X-Wing: The Bacta War *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starfighter squadrons
