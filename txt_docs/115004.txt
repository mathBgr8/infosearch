Virtrol Devin
1073

|masters= |apprentices= }} Virtrol Devin was a pirate serving under Drek Drednar. Virtrol was Drednar's security expert and also the leader of Drednik's boarding team. Devin was typically armed with a blaster pistol and a vibroknife, and carried a breath mask, comlink, and security bypass kit.
