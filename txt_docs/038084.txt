Charm's Way
1782

Charm's Way was a large Naboo freighter captained by Reeve Dallows prior to his disappearance. Charm's Way and its convoy once came under attack from a number of mercenaries, flying Dianoga, Dagger and MorningStar starfighters. The attack was repelled by Bravo Flight pilot Essara Till.
