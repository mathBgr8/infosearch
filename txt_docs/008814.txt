Skent Graff
1518

Skent Graff was a captain in the New Republic Defense Fleet who served during the Yuuzhan Vong War. A broad shouldered male with handsome features who was proud of his Human heritage, Graff's father had served aboard Corbantis during Seti Ashgad's bid for galactic power before dying of the Death Seed plague. In 25 ABY, Graff commanded the cruiser Soothfast on patrol duty in the Meridian sector.
