Allegiant
1675

The Allegiant was an MC80B Star Cruiser in the New Republic Defense Fleet. An aging vessel, Allegiant acted as the mobile base of operations for Nomad Squadron and provided patrol defense for the Meridian sector. The cruiser was still in duty during the Yuuzhan Vong invasion, although it may have been reactivated from a mothballed fleet. *The Last Command *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:MC80B Star Cruisers Category:Starships of the New Republic
