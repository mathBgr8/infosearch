Lucasfilm Archives
4233

The Lucasfilm Archives is a large facility located at Skywalker Ranch designed to preserve and document items from Lucasfilm productions. Currently, the archives contain elements from the Star Wars films, the Indiana Jones films, Willow, American Graffiti, and Tucker: The Man and His Dream.
