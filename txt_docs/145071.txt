Ithor Loman
626

The Ithor Loman was a tapcafe in Rytal Prime presumably owned by Ithorian's. Talon Karrde first met General Jutka there, who warned him about the pirate Rei'Kas. Karrde was later confronted by Xern and several other members of Rei'Kas' gang. Most were killed by Karrde's crew, while Xern was allowed to escape. *Vision of the Future *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Tapcafes
