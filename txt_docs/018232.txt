Most of the tribe lived in its village but many Mokk, including their tribal leader, resided in the Mokk Stronghold. Soothsayers and shamen were also part of the tribe. The Mokk were in conflict with the Kunga and the Janta, another Dantari tribes. The Mokk were usually more hostile and aggressive towards strangers than other tribes. During the Galactic Civil War, the Galactic Empire suspected the Rebel Alliance to have contacts with the Mokk.
