Nervesplicers were tools used to regrow damaged nerves. Balmorra, Kuat, and Coruscant (including its Orowood Medcenter) were some of the only worlds in the galaxy where they could be found. *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Knights of the Fallen Empire *The New Jedi Order: Recovery Category:Medical tools
