Out of Touch
523

The Out of Touch was a ship owned by Tok-Foge Pokresh and registered on Bothawui with registration number RN40-844033. In 16 ABY, it was one of many ships Luke Skywalker tracked as they passed the inner flight control buoys of the planet Teyr. *Shield of Lies Category:Starships
