Chooba
1309

Chooba were small, herd-dwelling slugs from Kirdo III. They were often hunted and eaten by Kitonak. The Kitonak would mimic the creature's favorite dish, the sulfaro plant. They would lie down and wait for a chooba to climb up to its head looking for the real plant's meaty interior. The Kitonak would then swallow the slug whole.
