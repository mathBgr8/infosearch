|pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair= |feathers= |eyes= |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation= |masters= |apprentices= }} Zakrinand Minus was a notable male warlord who lived just prior to the founding of the Galactic Republic. Minus participated in the Unification Wars that marked the establishment of the Galactic Republic. During and after the conflict, he came to be regarded as near-invincible. He was likened to other historical figures, like Xim the Despot of the pre-Republic era and General Grievous of the Clone Wars, in terms of sheer barbarism. Kosh Teradoc, a warlord of the Imperial Remnant after the Battle of Endor, was known to compare himself to Minus, as well as Mandalore the First. Teradoc considered them, as well as himself, warlords guided by providence and sheer, brute strength.
