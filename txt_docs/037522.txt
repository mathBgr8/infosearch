Sayn Ta
1786

Sayn Ta was a female Kaminoan Master Cloner on Kamino. Sayn Ta was the scientist who was given the duty to create a vaccine against the deadly Kaminoan nano-virus, a virus created to decimate the clone troopers of the Galactic Republic when the first clones died a few months after the First Battle of Geonosis. Just after the two Jedi Kit Fisto and Aayla Secura left her laboratory, she was killed by the Separatist Kaminoan Kuma Nai that had developed the nano-virus with an overdose of the substance. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Essential Guide to Warfare Category:Biologists Category:Females Category:Galactic Republic individuals Category:Kaminoans
