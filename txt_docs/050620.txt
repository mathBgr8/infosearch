V-6d mortar launcher
2453

The V-6d mortar launcher was a support weapon manufactured by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc.. Used by the Galactic Empire's TIE pilots and Imperial Army pilots,Star Wars: Battlefront and Imperial commandos during a number of battlefronts throughout the Galactic Civil War,Star Wars: Battlefront II the mortar launcher was capable of firing volleys of mortars into enemy lines, with a capacity of 5 grenades. The grenades could be configured to explode on contact or on a timer. The V-6d appears in the Star Wars: Battlefront video game series as an Imperial-affiliated weapon.
