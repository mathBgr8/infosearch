SeeVee
2585

SeeVee or C-V was an ASP-7 labor droid owned by the smuggler Platt Okeefe. The droid served her onboard the Last Chance. Like many ASP series droids, he was only capable of answering either "affirmative" or "negative". The droid often got on Okeefe's nerves and annoyed many customs inspectors; sometimes with positive results, sometimes negative.Platt's Smugglers Guide At one point while smuggling Imperial-issue heavy blasters, TIE/LN starfighters shot down Platt Okeefe over the planet Dorajan. Crashing in the jungles, Okeefe had to work running repairs on her ship, aided only by SeeVee. However the droid was a cheap model with slow processing speed and proved little practical help. Nevertheless, Okeefe managed the repairs and evaded the Imperial picket.Shadows of the Empire Sourcebook Later, SeeVee was present on the Last Chance when a Brentaalan boy, Darrik, tried to stowaway. The droid traveled with Okeefe and Darrik to the planet Bespin. There, the bounty hunter Beylyssa infiltrated the Last Chance disguised as a Bespin Wing Guard.
