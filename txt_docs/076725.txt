New Republic Obelisk
1762

The New Republic Obelisk was a monument located on the New Republic's capital world of Coruscant by 25 ABY.The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse to 25 ABY. A tall and thin needle made out of stone, the Obelisk was constructed in order to symbolize the unity of the New Republic. Kuati Senator Viqi Shesh's office featured a grand view of both the obelisk and the avenue Commerce Way. The New Republic Obelisk first appeared in James Luceno's novel Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse, which was released in 2000 as an installment of The New Jedi Order series. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant monuments
