Darkk
1837

Darkk was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer in the service of the Galactic Empire during the year 4 ABY. Virtually nothing is known about this ship's service history. The only thing known about it is the fact that it accompanied the Star Destroyer Indiko in order to collect Tan Maarek Stele after he thwarted Grand Admiral Demetrius Zaarin's assassination attempt on Emperor Palpatine while he was riding on his personal luxury liner.
