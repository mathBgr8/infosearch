Baobab Work Placement Bureau
715

The Baobab Work Placement Bureau was a division of the Baobab Archives. Run by the Baobab family, the bureau helped beings of many species obtain successful occupations throughout the galaxy. Under the direction of Ebenn Q3 Baobab, the Work Placement Bureau not only helped many Wookiees find jobs in the business sector, but held evening classes in numerous languages and pushed for equal-employment standards for all species. *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Commerce and labor guilds and unions
