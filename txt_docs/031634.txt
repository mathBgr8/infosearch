Aleen Classic

The Aleen Classic was a podracing event that took place annually on Aleen, during the final years of the Galactic Republic. One race was won by the Veknoid podracer pilot Teemto Pagalies, who used the prize money to free Trax Bonkik from Sebulba. Category:Podracing courses Category:Sporting events
