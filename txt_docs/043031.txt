|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Tasjon was a location on the planet of Corulag that hosted an important annual swoop race in the years before the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, is set on the date 13:5:2, which corresponds to 22 BBY according to the reasoning here. this race was won by Ignar Ominaz and Tasjon was the origin of a HoloNet News article that broadcast his victory. Tasjon was mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #44, an in-universe website issue published on May 2, 2002. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content. Category:Corulag locations
