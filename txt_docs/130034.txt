16th Fighter Squadron
954

The 16th Fighter Squadron was a squadron of E-wing escort starfighters that was attached to the New Republic's Fifth Battle Group. Blue Flight was a part of the squadron. The squadron saw action during the Black Fleet Crisis of 16-17 ABY. *Shield of Lies Category:New Republic starfighter squadrons
