Unidentified green bounty hunter
2224

A green-skinned satyr-like alien worked as a bounty hunter during the Galactic Civil War. This bounty hunter was part of a bounty hunter group on Centares shortly after the Battle of Yavin. When the name of the Rebel pilot who destroyed the first Death Star was revealed, Vader and his stormtroopers killed the hunter and every other individual who heard it. The bounty hunter had green skin with brown fur on their legs. This unidentified individual was created by Star Wars artist Dave Gibbons for Vader's Quest 1 of the Vader's Quest series of comics, published in 1999 by Dark Horse Comics.
