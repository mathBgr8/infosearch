Ministry Council
1689

The Ministry Council was the collective governmental decision-making body that bought together the principal ministers of the New Republic. The Ministry Council was a Senate Council of the New Republic Senate charged with funding the New Republic General Ministry, approving the First Administrator, and general oversight of the various ministries. If the Chief of State should die, be incapacitated, or generally out of contact, the Ministry Council had the right to select an interim Chief of State from the current Minister of State, a former Minister, or a former Chief of State, at least until the Senate could elect a new Chief of State&mdash;a very special power when compared to even the Ruling Council.
