Cadriaan Province
1174

Cadriaan Province was a section of the Tapani sector ruled by House Cadriaan. The Shapani Bypass passed through a small portion of this province. During the Galactic Civil War the province contained seven systems and seven habitable worlds. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Player's Guide to Tapani *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector regions
