Vicria
1133

Vicria was a female Mungra with orange eyes and red-tawny hair. She ran a tapcafe in Makrin City on Shelkonwa. In 0.6 ABY Vicria let the Alliance to Restore the Republic leader, Leia Organa, work in her tapcafe while she was a fugitive on Shelkonwa, wanted by the Galactic Empire. *Allegiance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Mungras Category:Shelkonwans
