Darth Revan's flagship
2795

Darth Revan's flagship was the flagship of Darth Revan, who commanded the military forces of the Sith Empire from this vessel during the Jedi Civil War as of 3957 BBY. thumb|left|200px|[[Bastila Shan and her Jedi companions confront Darth Revan on the bridge, prior to Darth Malak's betrayal]] When Bastila Shan, accompanied by several Jedi Knights and at least one Republic soldier confronted Revan on the bridge of his ship, the latter's apprentice, Darth Malak, betrayed his master by ordering his own forces to open fire, hoping to eliminate the Jedi and Revan both. Not all went according to plan, however, as Bastila survived and was able to use the Force to save the spark of life that still lay within the dying Revan. With his mind nearly destroyed by injuries suffered from the bombardment, he was captured by the Jedi and whisked to the safety of the Dantooine Jedi Enclave.
