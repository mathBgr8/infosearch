All-Clown Squadron of Fun
679

The All-Clown Squadron of Fun was the designation Lando Calrissian, in the guise of "Bescat Offdurmin," gave to the ad hoc squadron accompanying him from Corellia to the Errant Venture in the Kuat system. The other ships in the "squadron" consisted of the X-Wings of Wedge Antilles and Corran Horn and the Pulsar Skate. *Legacy of the Force: Exile Category:Starfighter squadrons
