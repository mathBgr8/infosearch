Sd-77 sonic pistol
1302

The Sd-77 sonic pistol was a ranged weapon produced by Pacnorval Defense Systems, Limited. The sonic pistol fired a wide blast of high-intensity sound waves which could shatter solid objects or stun a sentient. The Sd-77 was popular among private security firms including Santhe Security.
