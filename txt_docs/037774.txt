Doba
1507

Doba was a male Rodian, and a swoop pilot for the Black Vulkars on Taris in 3956 BBY. He was not as skilled as Redros in swoop racing, and was rarely, if ever, favored because of this. Despite being a member, he fled the track when Brejik ordered all Vulkars to attack Revan and Bastila Shan. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Black Vulkars Category:Males Category:Rodians Category:Tarisians
