Iceflake Inn
749

The Iceflake Inn was a vacation resort on the polar ice caps of Coruscant. The resort offered unique and elaborate slopes for turbo-skiing, private glaciers for climbing and exploring, as well as snowfields for children to play in. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscant hotels
