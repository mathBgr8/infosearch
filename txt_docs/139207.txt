Hemalian Barkid
872

Hemalian Barkid was a male businessperson from Drewwa. He worked for Trang Robotics, and as of 40 ABY had been assigned to Kuat. He owned a Y-wing which was stolen by Ben Skywalker during his mission to acquire the Amulet of Kalara. *Legacy of the Force: Exile Category:Males
