Attack Pattern Beta
1083

Attack Pattern Beta was a tactic used by Rebel snowspeeders and Mandalorian Alpha-3 Nimbus-class V-wing starfighters in battle. In Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds, clicking on a snowspeeder might make him announce "Attack Pattern Beta!" In Star Wars: The Clone Wars, at the start of the second mission, Mace Windu orders Luminara Unduli to use Attack Pattern Beta.
