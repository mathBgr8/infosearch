Mika (Farghul)

|gender= |pronouns=She/her |height= |mass= |hair=BrownRepublic 65 |feathers= |eyes=Yellow |skin= |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=Breela |affiliation=Crimson Nova |masters= |apprentices= }} Mika was a female Farghul who was born to a pair of death stick dealers. After her parents went down with the Jedi, she held a grudge against the Jedi while growing up to become a bounty hunter and joined the Crimson Nova. Over time, Mika built a reputation within the underworld for her resourcefulness and tenacity. She was also the mother of Breela, who followed in her mother's footsteps during the Imperial Era and the First Galactic Civil War. thumb|left|Mika Long ago, Mika's parents ran into Jedi trouble when she was very young. Rather than let their business be shut down and submit to capture, her parents tried to kill the Jedi and went down fighting. Mika held a grudge against the Jedi for the rest of her life. Eventually, she was brought into the bounty hunting business by Stroth. Over the years she rose to power in Crimson Nova, and became the group's leader. She developed a romantic bond with fellow Farghul Harll. During the Clone Wars, Mika accepted a bounty for several Jedi from Kh'aris Fenn. Officially she was in it for the credits but unofficially she was out for revenge. Jedi Masters Mace Windu, Agen Kolar, Kit Fisto, and Saesee Tiin came to her hideout to stop this contract, which led to Harll's death. Mika was stunned by Stroth when she refused to surrender to the Jedi, and was then arrested by the Jedi. Mika's daughter, Breela, became a bounty hunter around the time of the Battle of Yavin. Like her mother, she specialized in hunting Jedi, whom she had a personal reason to loathe, and attempted to bring down Kai Justiss. Information from Lucas Licensing originally identified Mika as a Cathar.
