*Black market dealers *Pirates |founder= |leader=Cobral Overlord |subgroup= |headquarters=Cobral Hideout |locations=Rori |formed=The Cobral |founded=c. 40 BBYJedi Apprentice: The Only Witness |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Cobral family }} }} The Neo-Cobral, or Cobral for short, was a criminal organization active on Rori during the Galactic Civil War. The organization was established by former members of the Cobral, a group of smugglers and black market dealers founded by the Cobral family who took over the planetary government of the planet Frego, in the Core Worlds, during the last decades of the Old Republic. The Cobrals lost control over Frego when one of the children of the family, Solan Cobral, killed his brother Rutin to ensure his place as head of the family. After the Cobrals leaders were imprisoned, some of its members relocated to Naboo's moon of Rori which became their base of operations. Their objective was the domination of the moon. In addition to criminal activities, the Cobral tried desperately to get their former leaders out of prison. By the time of the Battle of Yavin, the Neo-Cobrals were based in a bunker located a short distance of the city of Restuss and were led by an individual known as the Cobral Overlord. The Neo-Cobrals were in open conflict with the Restuss Brigade. In 1 ABY, a commoner in Narmle intended to recruit adventurers to assault the Cobral Hideout and eliminate the Cobral Overlord.
