Hammond Ballater
2777

}} Hammond Ballater was a male criminal. He was a member of the Black Sun syndicate on Corellia before he left the organization to work for Shalera the Hutt. Even though Hammond Ballater left Black Sun, the criminal organization never let their members go completely. Sometime following 0 BBY, he was approached by Grobber, the Twi'lek subordinate of Zekka Thyne, who demanded certain information from him. When Ballater refused to cooperate with Black Sun, Grobber sent a well armed individual to find Ballater and bring him back to Coronet, the capital of Corellia. The individual was attacked by two thugs who were attempting to defend Ballater, but they were killed by Grobber's mercenary. Ballater then surrendered, as he did not want the situation to get even worse. 250px|thumb|Possible depictions of Hammond Ballater Hammond Ballater was a non-player character in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Due to the nature of many older quests in Star Wars Galaxies, the appearance of various characters could change each time a player performed the quest. Therefore, the canonical species of Ballater remains unidentified. However, the quest dialogue given by Grobber indicated that Ballater was a male.
