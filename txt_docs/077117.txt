Millie
2336

"Millie" was the name given to a MILL-247-EE emergency service droid on Tem Chesko's freighter. She had a female personality, and was built to resemble a green-skinned humanoid female. She had blue sensors. While on a run to deliver syntonium crystals from Cirus II to Tatooine an asteroid collided with Chesko's ship, causing significant damage to the ships systems, notably the hyperdrive and comlink. Without a hyperdrive the journey, which would take around two weeks with a functional drive, would take 71 years to complete Approximately 8 years into the voyage, the human discovered Millie in a emergency storage compartment and activated her. Over the years, Millie developed a close relationship with Tem, the two being stranded on a ship for seventy years together. Despite Millie's synthetic nature, they developed feelings for each other, to the point that Millie once attempted to destroy herself when she believed he missed his former girlfriend, Maia, more than he cared for her. As her companion aged and his health declined, she took care of him. Eventually he handed her a laser knife and asked her to end his life. Instead, she sacrificed herself to make a cybernetic replacement for his failing heart from her own internal components. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droids with feminine programming Category:MILL-247-EE industrial droids
