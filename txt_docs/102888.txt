Slabba Drewl
944

Slabba Drewl was a chef captured by Corpo the Hutt. He was later rescued by Micah Giiett and Plo Koon. He was very short and had a blunt head with blue skin. He had small beady eyes and a large mouth. The name "Slabba Drewl" is a play on the words "slobber" and "drool". Drewl, Slabba Drewl, Slabba Drewl, Slabba
