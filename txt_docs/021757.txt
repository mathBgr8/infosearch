|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=1 BBYThe date of this story is established internally in relation to the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. |series=Star Wars: Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika |issue=2 }} Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika 2 was the second issue in the Star Wars: Underworld: The Yavin Vassilika series of comics. It was released January 10, 2001 by Dark Horse Comics. The race is on as Han Solo and Chewie search the planet Kalkovak for information leading to the legendary Vassilika of Yavin. Things get lively, though, when hardcore bounty hunters Bossk, Dengar, and IG-88 show up with a laundry list of grudges to settle! And there's nothing more explosive than bad blood between scoundrels! Throw in a swaggering Lando Calrissian, a seductive Jozzel Moffet, and a band of curious Jawas, and you've got yourself the fixings for a visit the locals aren't too likely to forget!
