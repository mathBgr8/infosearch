Book of the Boolmide
1157

The Book of the Boolmide was the holy book of the Skakoan religion. It was kept by the Elders, who read it on the Power Mounds of the Elders. In certain situations, such a reading could open the Gates of Grontessiant. Category:Religious codes and documents Category:Skakoan religion
