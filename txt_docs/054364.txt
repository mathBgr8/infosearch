Stun duel
514

Stun duel was a sport played on Taris after the Sith forbade lethal duels. It was performed with blasters and swords set to stun so the gladiators would not be wounded lethally. Bendak Starkiller was a champion/survivor of old duels and declined to participate in stun duels. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Combat sports
