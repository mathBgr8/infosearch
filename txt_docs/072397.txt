Icknya
1687

Icknya was a tyrant. At some point after she became the Chief of State of the New Republic in 11 ABY,The New Essential Chronology Leia Organa Solo had to deal with Icknya. In 25 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Vector Prime to 25 ABY. the protocol droid C-3PO compared the tyrant to the Yuuzhan Vong agent Nom Anor, the ruler of the planet of Rhommamool. Icknya was first mentioned in the 1999 novel Vector Prime, written by R. A.
