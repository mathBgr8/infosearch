In an interview with Rengor Vanth of Sektor 242 NewsLine, Malis revealed that he hunted members of the Alliance because they were criminals, claiming that Luke Skywalker, in particular, was a vicious murderer for destroying the Death Star and everyone on it. Malis believed that Alderaan's destruction was justified, since it was the homeworld of rebellion against the order of the Empire. Malis also believed that he himself was simply a hunter, not a murderer, despite his record of bringing back dead bounties more often than live captures.
