Lake Weir
1005

Lake Weir was a lake on the planet of Melida/Daan. It was the only body of water near Zehava for a large distance. Originally, a Hall of Evidence was located above the lake, floating on repulsorlifts; however, with the Young's victory and the end of the war, it was destroyed by Nield and members of the Young forces as an initiative for peace.
