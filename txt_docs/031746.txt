Arachnoid
944

Arachnoids were a species of sentient arachnids with a legspan of approximately 2.5 meters. They were covered in dense black and gray fur, and possessed four eyes and powerful hind legs. Ptak-Sok, copilot of the Black Widow, was an Arachnoid. They built the EMRG-50 Rail Guns. Category:Arachnoid sentient species
