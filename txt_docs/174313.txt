Halberd (escort carrier)
2207

Halberd was an Imperial Navy Ton-Falk-class escort carrier. It was stationed near Comm Center and attempted to save that outpost when Rebel Alliance forces raided it as part of a secret operation to contact an Imperial defector. It arrived around the time when Python downloaded the data from Comm Center and launched TIE bombers against her.
