Jedi Killer (disambiguation)
1085

Jedi Killer might refer to: *Kylo Ren, known amongst the First Order ranks as the "Jedi Killer" for preventing the restoration of the Jedi Order. Prior to being officially named, Ren was also known as "Jedi Killer" during pre-production on Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens. *Jedi hunter, a term for an individual who hunted Jedi. *Inquisitorius, Jedi killers who served the Galactic Empire. *Jedi killer, a droid that attacked and killed many Jedi on Cularin. *JK-13 security droids, unofficially known as Jedi Killer droids. *Jedi hunter, the Legends version of the term for an individual who hunted Jedi. *Inquisitorius, the Legends version of the Jedi killers who served the Galactic Empire.
