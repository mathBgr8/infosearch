Underworld Inn
727

The Underworld Inn was a small, run-down hotel located in the lower levels of Coruscant. It had its own cantina as well as a small amount of rooms. Ferus Olin, Trever Flume, and members of the Erased stayed at this inn for a short time while searching for Solace. It was mentioned that it cost extra for towels and water. *The Last of the Jedi: Underworld Category:Coruscant hotels
