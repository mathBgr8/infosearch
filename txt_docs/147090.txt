Five individuals carry the last name Ti: *Sanola Ti, a Human Jedi. *Shaak Ti, a Togruta Jedi. *Kirana Ti, a Human Jedi. *Fionah Ti, a Human Shaper. *Vaash Ti, a Togruta Jedi Initiate related to the previously mentioned Shaak Ti.
