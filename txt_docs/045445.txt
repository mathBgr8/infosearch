Daer'ey'ath
2158

Daer'ey'ath,Dark Nest III: The Swarm War known by her core name Reya, was a Chiss intelligence officer of the Chiss Intelligence. By 35 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion Reya had joined and become the Prime of the Taat Killik nest, from this point on she became known as ReyaTaat. Reya claimed she was found bordering on starvation when the Taat took her into their nest and made her a voluntary Joiner.
