Thrynni Vae
1752

Thrynni Vae was a female Human Jedi apprenticed to Master Tresina Lobi. With Tresina she oversaw the freight transport on Duro in year 26 ABY during the Yuuzhan Vong War. They discovered that CorDuro Shipping held back relief goods from SELCORE at the orbital city Urrdorf City. Thrynni disappeared mysteriously and Tresina went back to Coruscant, where she informed Luke Skywalker about Thrynnis' disappearance.
