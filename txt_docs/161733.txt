Nym's Anniversary Bash

thumb|right|250px|The Modal Nodes playing at Nym's Stronghold Nym's Anniversary Bash was a large anniversary celebration held by pirate warlord Nym on the planet Lok in 1 ABY. Figrin D'an and the Modal Nodes played at Nym's Palace while the sky above the stronghold was illuminated by a giant fireworks display. Nym's quartermaster gave away drinks, cake and small presents to everyone who attended the festivity. Nym's Anniversary Bash was an event in the MMORPG Star Wars Galaxies to celebrate the 30th anniversary of Star Wars. It took place from May 23 to June 30, 2007. Players that visited Nym's stronghold during that time received a unique badge and could get a small present from Nym's Anniversary Quartermaster once every hour. Category:Anniversary celebrations
