The planet had no moons and was completely covered in a methane ocean. Zaur was mentioned in "Three Simple Laws," the first article in the Planet Hoppers series Aargau: For All Your Banking Needs, which was written by Cory J. Herndon and published by Wizards of the Coast on Wizards.com on August 7, 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas later placed the Zug system, and therefore Zaur, in grid square L-10.
