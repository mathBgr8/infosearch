Arachnor
2147

An arachnor was a giant arachnoid that resided on Arzid. Its web was one of the stickiest in the galaxy. Arachnors were large, spider-like creatures that lived in the Macaab mushroom forests of Arzid, feeding mostly on the giant fungi that were plentiful there. However, they also ate the flesh of living creatures. The fungi that they ate contained chemicals which made their webs the stickiest in the galaxy.
