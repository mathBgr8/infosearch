Vulatan
2879

Vulatan was a Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic. A Force-sensitive trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, Vulatan achieved the rank of Jedi Master circa 400 BBY. Vulatan studied as a Consular, specializing in the field of diplomacy and becoming a well-respected diplomat. On a mission assigned by the Jedi High Council, Master Vulatan was part of a relief effort to Dathomir that included fellow Masters Gra'aton and Yoda, as well as some fourteen Padawans. Attempting to salvage the wreck of the lost praxeum ship, the Chu'unthor, the Jedi were met with the Witches of Dathomir, who guard the wreck from the Jedi.
