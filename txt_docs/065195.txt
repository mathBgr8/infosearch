Greeve (Human)
1963

Lieutenant Greeve was a member of the Rebel Alliance strike team lead by General Han Solo on Endor during the Galactic Civil War. He was a scout, and was recruited for General Crix Madine's commandos while working as a forest guide on Kashyyyk.
