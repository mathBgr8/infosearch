Assault (transports)
1486

Assault group was a pair of Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports used by the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War. When the Alliance succeeded in stranding the Imperial EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Priam near Kalla VII, General Jan Dodonna gave the order for a "board and capture" operation against the Priam. A strike by Gold Squadron's Y-wings disabled the ship and Assault group delivered commandos from Alliance Special Forces to capture the vessel. Once captured, Lambda-class shuttles from Panda group delivered the new crew for the ship while transport group Jordi delivered supplies.
