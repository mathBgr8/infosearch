Wrist laser
3561

thumb|[[Boba Fett/Legends|Boba Fett using a Dur-24 wrist laser.]] A wrist laser was a type of compact laser weapon capable of inflicting serious damage at extremely close range. Wrist lasers consisted of rechargeable solar-powered gauntlets. A user had the option of causing the chamber of the gauntlet to be overloaded, which would result in a small explosion. Wrist lasers typically cost 2,000 credits and 100 credits for a power pack.
