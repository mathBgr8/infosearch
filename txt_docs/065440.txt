Gopher
1382

thumb|250px|Launching from the [[Fogger, a TIE/IN interceptor/Legends piloted by Maarek Stele targets the Gopher.]] Gopher was a Xizor Transport Systems Container Transport that hauled supplies for the Rebel Alliance shortly after the Battle of Hoth. During the battle to recapture Outpost D-34 from the Rebels, Maarek Stele scanned the Gopher cargo holds and discovered that it was carrying supplies looted from the station. Stele was congratulated by the Cloaked Figure, who then authorized the launch of stormtrooper transport Omega to board and capture the Gopher.
