Leozi Route
1466

The Leozi Route was a minor hyperlane that was located in the Tapani sector. The route was under the jurisdiction of House Mecetti as it was entirely contained in their province and connected almost a third of the worlds they controlled together. Travel along this route was especially quick due to a minimal amount of debris along the run. The Leozi Route was a spur of the larger Procopian Shipping Lane, splitting of from it at Javis at passing through Tanya, Tanzis and Pozzi before reaching its terminus at Tanda system. *Lords of the Expanse Category:Tapani sector hyperlanes
