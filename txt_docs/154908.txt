Glaze cake
887

Glaze cakes were a sweet dessert. The smuggler Platt Okeefe was carrying a load of glaze cakes when she crashed her starship Pok's Demise on Tatooine. Some local scurriers had been attracted to the smell of the cakes on her ship and ate them; they ended up gnawing on many of her ship systems as well.
