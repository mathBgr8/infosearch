Kolme'starn was a city on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. It was located in the midlands of the planet's western continent, situated between Odve'starn (to the west) and the capital of Drev'starn (to the east). *Shadows of the Empire Planets Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
