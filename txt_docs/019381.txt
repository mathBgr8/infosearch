The Broadside-class shared a common keel design with the Gladiator-class Star Destroyer.The Force Unleashed Campaign Guide Designed for long-range engagements, ships of this class had light armor and unimpressive acceleration and maneuverability. They were armed with missile launchers and two turbolasers. Early models were armed with expensive diamond boron missiles that could be used to deadly effect against formations of tightly-packed ships. The range of the missiles made them very useful in long-range attacks. These weapons were replaced by more economical concussion missiles in later models, though some of the cruisers still kept their lethal armament. The payload was 1,200 missiles. The Broadside-class was first produced by Kuat Drive Yards in the early days of the Galactic Civil War. It was conceived as a long-range missile ship, and was based on the Gladiator-class. The design was stripped of passenger compartments, the hangar bays were turned into torpedo bays and the vessel lost some of its armor. Due to the lack of self-sufficiency, the Broadside-class was redesignated as a "cruiser" instead of a "Star Destroyer". Kuat engineers also decided that great maneuverability was not necessary for the class, thus leaving the Broadside slower than the Gladiator. This was the cause of its vulnerability to other capital warships, and as a result ships of this class were in practice almost never deployed without an escort. However, its missiles proved lethal against fighter squadrons and small space stations. thumb|left|200px|Another view of the Broadside cruiser In addition to its design-problems, a number of Broadsides were stolen or otherwise acquired by the Zann Consortium during the Civil War. This type of ship has many similarities to the Demolisher, a Star Destroyer that appeared in the Star Wars Droids episode "The Pirates of Tarnoonga." In The Force Unleashed Campaign Guide, the two designs were given an in-universe connection. There is some debate in the online community as to the actual size of the Broadside-class.
