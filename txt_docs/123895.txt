Degathem
1794

The Degathem was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by the Galactic Empire during the "Arms Race". The Degathem and the Anterab were delivering supplies critical for the development of the TIE Defender starfighter. Despite rebel attempts to end the phase by raiding the Imperial research station, both ships completed their mission and delivered the supplies.
