Smith *Ken Stephenson |executive producers=*Miki Herman *Elena Lesser *George Lucas *Cliff Ruby *Peter Sauder |production company= |starring=*George Buza *Alyson Court *Anthony Daniels *Don Francks *Jim Henshaw *Taborah Johnson *Cree Summer |music= |timeline=*5 BBY,Star Wars Gamemaster Screen, Revised, p. 56 15 BBY *3.5 ABY }} }} The Ewoks and Droids Adventure Hour was an ABC television programming block that aired from 1985 to 1986. It ran episodes of the Droids and Ewoks animated television series. In Star Wars: Rebel Assault II: The Hidden Empire, pressing Alt-V and then typing "ovres" will turn on theatre mode, where R2-D2, Darth Vader and C-3PO watch the cutscenes and comment MST3K-style. At one point, they imitate Rookie One, saying, "Let's see what's on the telly. It's the Ewoks/Droids cartoon hour! I loved that show! Especially that episode where R2 joined that cult.... and began talking like a hippie..." The code "ovres" is "servo" backwards, which is probably a reference to MST3K's character Tom Servo.
