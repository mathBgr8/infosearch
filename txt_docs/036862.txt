Streln
1423

Corporal Streln was an ISB agent on Imperial Center after to the Battle of Yavin. Along with Gonrey and Vypass, they helped design the YI-5 probe droid. Ysanne Isard ordered the execution of the three when it was revealed that the probes, like her, would have blue and red eyes; however, she later decided to spare them, assuming it was an innocent mistake.
