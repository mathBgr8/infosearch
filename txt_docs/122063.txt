Calian flier
1699

Calian fliers were small atmospheric craft in the Calian Fleet. Each vessel was equipped with two energy cannons. Numerous such vessels were carried aboard a Calian aerial battleship. Aron Peacebringer and Leia Organa took such a ship from Sk'ar's hidden base to attack the Imperial general's battleship. Despite Leia's excellent piloting, the little ship was shot down, but not before Aron and Leia jumped aboard the battleship.
