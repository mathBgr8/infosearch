Belden family
746

The Belden family was one of the original settler families of Bakura. Their members included: *Orn Belden (160 BBY&ndash;4 ABY), eventual Senior Senator of the Bakuran Senate *Eppie Belden (b. 128 BBY), his wife and Bakuran Revolution leader *Roviden Belden (d. 1 ABY), their son, Rebel Alliance agent.
