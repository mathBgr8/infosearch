The planet Malanose was located in the Gree Enclave of the Outer Rim Territories. Several lesser-known hyperspace routes connected it to Asation, Gree, and Te Hasa elsewhere in the Enclave. The average day on Malanose was 18 standard hours, with 320 local days encompassing a year. During the high days of Gree society, Malanose was covered with cities that would produce new devices and technologies. As their society suffered in the decline, the remaining structures were raided for any remaining artifacts, and transported to other planets in the Gree Enclave. Only the largest of relics were left behind in the abandoned cities, which include the Totth'k Lotos. The ruins of Gree hypergates were left on Malanose, among other worlds.
