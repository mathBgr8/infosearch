Tonith family
1619

The Tonith family was a Muun family who had been active in the InterGalactic Banking Clan for several generations prior to the Clone Wars. By 67 BBY, they controlled the Banking Clan. They were known for their ruthlessness, using force against rivals and the conquest of worlds as routine business procedures. Separatist Admiral Pors Tonith was a notable member of the family.
