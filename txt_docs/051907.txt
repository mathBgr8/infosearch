Throm Loro
2850

Throm Loro was a Human male native to the world of Cilpar. He was the founder of the Cilpari Resistance and husband of Elscol Loro. Throm Loro joined the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. He participated in the liberation of a number of prisoners held at an Imperial base on Endor. Throm saved a Wookiee named Groznik from execution during the mission, an action which earned him a life debt from the Wookiee. When the two returned to Cilpar, they led the resistance against the Galactic Empire until Throm was killed in an Imperial raid on one of the resistance's hideouts.
