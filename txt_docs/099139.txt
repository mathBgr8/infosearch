Carapod
1012

Carapods were large red, green, and black insects that lived on Zonama Sekot and helped with the shipbuilding process. Some were cyborgs. Carapods had flat, wide bodies with seven legs: three on each side, and one in the center-front. On either side of the seventh leg grew long gray spurs. Their thick hides contained a high metal content.
