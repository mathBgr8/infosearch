Botiq
2149

|role= |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Botiq was an entity that advertised in multiple issues of the planet Coruscant's CoCo District Edition of HoloNet News in 23 and 22 BBY.A Botiq advertisement appeared in both and , which are set on the dates 13:2:28 and 13:5:16, respectively. The two dates respectively correspond to 23 BBY and 22 BBY, per the reasoning here. Its advertisement was a blue-tinted box that framed a pair of shoes. The name "Botiq," written in the High Galactic alphabet and colored white with a black shadow, lay in the foreground. Botiq's advertisement appeared throughout HoloNet News, an in-universe website created in 2002 to publicize the then-upcoming Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones film. Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens wrote the site content. Category:Society
