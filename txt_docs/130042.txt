Crandor Ijjix
771

Crandor Ijjix was a Norat hostage taken by the Yevetha during the attack. Along with the other hostages taken during the Yevethan Purge, Crandor was forced to record a message begging the New Republic not to fire on Yevethan ships carrying prisoners.
