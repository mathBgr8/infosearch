In 2 BBY, Corellian smuggler Han Solo owed them 2,500 credits for their work on his starship, the Millennium Falcon. Taking advantage of this knowledge, the Corporate Sector Authority executive Odumin&mdash;posing as a skip tracer called "Spray," looking to repossess the vessel for Vinda and D'rag&mdash;got close to Solo and his Wookiee partner Chewbacca in his investigation of a slaving ring in the Corporate Sector.
