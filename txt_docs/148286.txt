Carel Struycken
973

Carel Struycken portrayed King Terak in Ewoks: The Battle for Endor. Some of Struycken's other work includes Men in Black, the Addams Family films, and The Witches of Eastwick, and Star Trek: The Next Generation where he portrayed Mr. Homn. Category:Ewoks: The Battle for Endor actors
