|pages=32 |upc= |timeline=4 ABYStar Wars Omnibus: X-Wing Rogue Squadron Volume 1 |series=Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron |issue=24 }} X-Wing Rogue Squadron 24 was the twenty-fourth issue in the Star Wars: X-Wing Rogue Squadron series of comics. It was released on November 5, 1997 by Dark Horse Comics, and reprinted in 2007 as part of a Hasbro action figure promotion featuring Baron Fel and Hobbie Klivian. Baron Fel's wingmen&mdash;the merciless pilots of the Imperial 181st Fighter Division&mdash;are falling to the firepower of Rogue Squadron. But Fel himself is not down. He has a final card to play, and Wedge Antilles is about to fold!
