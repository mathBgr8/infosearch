Rojene Eborsisk
820

Rojene Eborsisk was a reporter for The Slasher Sun Times who wrote an article on Master Thespian, and former Jedi Knight, Damien Tantrellius' play titled Jedi Men From Baronis for the Sun Times on Imperial Startime 45686.3467. Rojene made note that this play also featured Damien's daughter, Plum Tantrellius, who played Leia. Eborsisk also noted that his daughter was "not too hard to look at either".
