|role=Medical supplies manufacturer |products=*Medical equipment *Medicine |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Delgas Medical, also known as Delgas Corporation, was a medical supplies manufactured located on the planet Kabaira. It manufactured and sold medical equipment and medicines. Del Hunter worked for Delgas as a sales representative. The Delgas Medical Supplies facility on Sel Zonn Station was headed by Dr. Byra Fenn.
