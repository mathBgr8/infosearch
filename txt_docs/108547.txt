Ha'lathin

Ha'lathin was a Bimm poet. An original manuscript by Ha'lathin could be found among the artworks in the yacht Imru Ootmian. It had been a gift from the previous owner of the yacht to the then-current owner, Popara Anjiliac Diresto. *Tempest Feud Category:Bimms Category:Poets
