Mota
1300

Mota was a member of the Workers class who lived on the planet of New Apsolon. He sold weapons illegally, while the leader of the Workers, Lenz preferred obtaining their weapons legally. However, in one case, Lenz told Jedi Master Qui-Gon Jinn and his Padawan Obi-Wan Kenobi that Mota could help them find a probe droid, information about Balog and two speeders. *Jedi Apprentice: The Death of Hope *Jedi Apprentice: The Call to Vengeance *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Humans Category:Males Category:New Apsolon residents
