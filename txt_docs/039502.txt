Sith temple (Hurom)
1536

The city of Hurom on Thule hosted a Sith temple built by the ancient Sith who had first colonized Thule. This imposing structure, older than many galactic civilizations, covered more than a block of the city center and dominated the cityscape for miles. Like most Sith structures on Thule, the temple seethed in the dark side of the Force, and served as the residence of Thule's Sith priests. It served as the meeting place for government leaders and the priesthood and as a commissioning center for all outgoing troops.
