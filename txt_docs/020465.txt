Undervector
840

Undervector were a band whose music was composed entirely of sounds from their home planet, and could also be made into a mathematical equation to define the output of a quantum vector. They became popular following the deaths of Palpatine and Darth Vader at the Battle of Endor. Dllr Nep disliked Undervector's music, claiming "they stink like Tauntaun droppings". *X-Wing Rogue Squadron 14 Category:Bands
