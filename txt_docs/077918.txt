Arthur Adams
1838

Arthur Adams (born April 5, 1963) is a comic book illustrator who drew one of the covers for the Classic Star Wars: A New Hope series and contributed to the Rogue Squadron handbook published by Dark Horse Comics. He started in the industry working for Marvel Comics on the mini-series titled Longshot published in 1985.
