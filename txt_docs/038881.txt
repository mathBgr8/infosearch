C-Gosf
1425

C-Gosf was a female Gosfamling senator of the New Republic who represented Gosfambling c. 17 ABY. She was also a member of the Senate's Inner Council. She ran at great personal risk, because a Gosfamling who failed any contest was forever shunned by other Gosfamlings. *The New Rebellion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Gosfamblings Category:New Republic Senators
