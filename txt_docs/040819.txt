Star Wars: Episode I Adventures
2835

Star Wars: Episode I Adventures was a young-readers series and roleplaying game published by Scholastic to coincide with the 1999 film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The books were written by Ryder Windham, Dave Wolverton, and A. L. Singer. Episode I Adventures was the follow-up to Scholastic's previous Star Wars Missions series. Star Wars: Episode I Adventures originally shipped to subscribers in a black cardboard box featuring diagram illustrations of the Naboo N-1 starfighter on top and the Trade Federation vulture droid starfighter on the bottom. This box contained a Darth Maul carrying case, referred to as the Star Wars: Episode I Adventures Headquarters, which further contained the following items: *12 Adventure Cards *2 Adventure Dice, 10-sided and 20-sided *Adventure Notebook, to keep track of your adventures *Point Pad *1 Adventure Poster *1 Adventure Novel *1 Game Book *1 Adventure Guide *1 Episode I Adventures I.D.
