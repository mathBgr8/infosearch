Alfi
1207

Alfi was a male Jedi Knight. In 40 ABY, whilst still a new Jedi Knight, Alfi was among those Jedi at the Ossus Academy when the Galactic Alliance Guard attempted to "secure" it, holding the students hostage. Alfi was in a cell when GAG major Salle Serpa moved against the Jedi, and was killed there by GAG forces. *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Individuals of unspecified species Category:Jedi Knights of the New Jedi Order Category:Males
