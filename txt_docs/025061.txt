Elite (Yuuzhan Vong)
654

The Elite were the highest ranking members of the four main castes of the Yuuzhan Vong. They were the leaders of the Warrior, Intendant, Shaper, and Priest castes, and made up the court of the Supreme Overlord of the Yuuzhan Vong. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Elite Elite
