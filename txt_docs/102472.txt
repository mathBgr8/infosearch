Hishinu Booj
1242

Hishinu Booj was a male Rodian playwright. Hishinu Booj's most famous work was "The Trickery of Vosdia Nooma," produced in the year prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars. Inspiration for the drama was drawn from the Rodian senator Onaconda Farr, who assisted in writing the drama with some additional help from Ruris Plin. The play was directed by Booj himself and premiered at the Equator City First Stage on Coruscant. Category:Authors Category:Males Category:Rodians
