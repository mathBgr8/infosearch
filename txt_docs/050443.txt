Centrally Controlled Independent Replicant
1341

Centrally Controlled Independent Replicant or CCIR, was a type of remote-control technology used by Loronar Corporation in synthdroids and Needle fighters. CCIR technology allowed multiple automatons to be controlled by large central computers rather than a large number of miniaturized droid brains. The technology relied on near-instantaneous transmission between programmable crystals.
