Geroon Remnant
1876

The Geroon Remnant was a Vagaari military unit posing as the legitimacy Geroon government following their apparent abandonment of their homeworld due to being polluted. In truth however, the Geroon Remnant was a part of a Vagaari Empire plot and the Geroon homeworld was already free, having been liberated by the Chiss Ascendancy. It was supposedly created after the Geroon had gave out after being so polluted by the Vagaari and were forced to abandoned their homeworld, by 22 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion they said they numbered only 60,000, and could fit into a single 300-meter-long transport. The Geroon Remnant was under the leadership of First Steward Bearsh. In reality, however, the Geroon Remnant was merely a front established by a group of Vagaari in an attempt to infiltrate the Chaf Envoy so it could destroy the Outbound Flight. *Survivor's Quest *The New Essential Chronology Category:Fictional organizations Category:Vagaari culture
