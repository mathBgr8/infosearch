Formex
2465

Formex was a polymeric construction material used to build a variety of architectural designs, including columns and roadways. As a fluid, it could be poured into castings on site and manipulated to form various shapes and structures. Formex was commonly used in buildings, including those found on the planet Etti IV, in Bonadan's Spaceport Southeast II area,Han Solo's Revenge on Harridan, in Vandalar City on Reaper's World, in Kavila on Dalos IV, and as support structures and floors in Mesa 291 on Bothawui.
