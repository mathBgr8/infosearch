Mas Missur
2489

Mas Missur was a Jedi Master who served the Jedi Order and the Galactic Republic as a General in the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. A Force-sensitive trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, Mas Missur achieved the rank of Jedi Master by the end of the Separatist Crisis that plagued the Galactic Senate. When the Clone Wars erupted between the Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems, Master Missur took up the rank of Jedi General in the newly created Grand Army of the Republic, leading clone troopers against the Separatist Droid Army on several fronts. When the Republic incited a rebellion on the planet Gaftikar, Master Missur was assigned to lead the Acclamator-class assault ship Leveler to the world to provide support against a CIS invasion.
