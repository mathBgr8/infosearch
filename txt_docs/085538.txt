Maledoth
1071

The Maledoth was a former Yuuzhan Vong slaveship used in the Ramoan relocation project by the Jedi. According to Corran Horn, the Jedi in control of the project was the Wookiee Lowbacca; when he left for the Unknown Regions the project came to a standstill because no others possessed his understanding of Yuuzhan Vong biotechnology. *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Captured starships Category:Slaveships Category:Starships of the New Jedi Order
