The planet had nine moons and occupied the sixth orbital position around the Vulpter star. Vulp Major was mentioned in "Byss and the Deep Core, Part 4: Vulpter," the fourth installment in the Planet Hoppers Byss and the Deep Core article series, written by Rodney Thompson and published on Wizards of the Coast's website on April 28, 2005. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Vulpter system, and therefore Vulp Major, in grid square L-10.
