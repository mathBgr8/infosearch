Wild Force Shard
1075

thumb|right|200px|A red Wild Force Shard Wild Force Shard was a name given to crystals that were imbued with the power of the Force and, in turn, could exponentially enhance the Force powers of an individual. The crystal would resonate in the Force itself and, as such, had no affinity with the dark or light side of the Force; instead, it simply influenced the vicinity it was located in. It was for this reason that the Jedi Order never used such Force crystals, as they could easily amplify dark side or light side powers and potentially turn a Jedi to the dark side. Red and blue variants were known to exist. *Star Wars Galaxies: Trials of Obi-Wan Category:Force crystals
