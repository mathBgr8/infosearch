Khan Bonfils
1800

|listedpronouns= |nationality=British |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Khan Bonfils was an English actor who portrayed Saesee Tiin in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace (and in Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones via archive footage). He died on January 5, 2015 at 42 after collapsing during rehearsals for a theater production of Dante's Inferno.
