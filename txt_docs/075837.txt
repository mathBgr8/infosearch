Conclave on the Plight of the Refugees
1132

The Conclave on the Plight of the Refugees was a conference hosted by Leia Organa Solo in 25.5 ABY, in Government House in the city of Worlport on Ord Mantell. The goal was to convince members of important systems to accept relocation of refugees from captured worlds into hopefully self-reliant communities closer to the Core Worlds. Only several delegates were willing to agree to the plan. Delegates arrived from planets including Ord Mantell, Balmorra, Devaron, Alsakan, Gyndine, a world of the Koornacht Cluster, and Ruan (with Count Borert Harbright representing the latter). *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Meetings
