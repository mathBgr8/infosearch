XX-8
1242

The XX-8 was a model of turbolaser emplacement manufactured by Taim & Bak. The XX-8 was the predecessor of the XX-9 heavy turbolaser. It was similar in design to its successor, though this earlier model featured a taller tower that held both the turbolaser's main turbine and gunnery crew stations. The XX-9's turbine and crew stations were located within the vessel, not the weapon itself. *The Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Shipboard turbolasers Category:Taim & Bak products
