Mandalorian assault rifle
1910

Mandalorian assault rifles were powerful weapons. They were so powerful that they were considered almost overpowered for their size. However, this wouldn't bother their Mandalorian users who, in general, were not known for subtlety during war. The weapon was short and had a thumb through stock with a small scope.
