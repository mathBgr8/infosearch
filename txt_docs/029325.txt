Starhook Ten
2448

The Starhook Ten was a modified space truck owned by Shug Ninx. Ninx used the Starhook Ten to help evacuate Rebels from Byss when their mission was met by stiff resistance. Starhook Ten would later assist Rebel forces during their evacuation from New Alderaan during the Galactic Empire's Operation Shadow Hand. Salvager Three could be another name for Starhook Ten, given that both ships seem to be identical. Additionally, Ninx and Salla Zend enter Byss with the former while using false codes and leave with the latter. However, a more likely explanation may be that Ninx had left the Starhook Ten at Byss prior to the events depicted in Star Wars: Dark Empire and Star Wars: Dark Empire II or that Salvager Three was modified using what was left of the Starlight Intruder to become Starhook Ten as Star Wars Handbook 3: Dark Empire would suggest.
