Lorrd Academy for Aquatic Studies
740

The Lorrd Academy for Aquatic Studies was a branch of the Lorrd University on Lorrd. It contained huge aquariums. In 40 ABY, Movac Arisster took a large crowd hostage there in an attempt to go down in history by killing Jacen Solo.
