TZ-15 shuttle
1971

The TZ-15 shuttle, otherwise known as the Consortium Shuttle, was a starship used by the Zann Consortium during the Galactic Civil War. It was used to transport Defilers and other key personnel across the galaxy. They were also used to transport prisoners, such as planetary officials abducted in the criminal empire's operations.
