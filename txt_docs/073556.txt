Aspiration (Victory-class)
2209

Aspiration was a Victory-class Star Destroyer that came into the Imperial Fleet right after the Battle of Endor and joined Delak Krennel when he installed himself as the Prince-Admiral of the Ciutric Hegemony. It was on station around the fourth planet of the Corvis Minor system.
