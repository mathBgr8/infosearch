Mon Calamari Fish Market
876

The Mon Calamari Fish Market was a market on the planet Coruscant during the times of the Galactic Republic. A young Darth Maul visited the market briefly upon escaping from the Happy Nerf Herder home for young boys. While there, he stole a hoverboard from a bystander and accidentally caused an explosion which threw him off the building. Category:Coruscant commercial buildings Category:Markets
