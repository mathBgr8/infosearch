D'Astan starfighter
2101

The D'Astan starfighter was a starfighter design used by Imperial Baron Ragez D'Asta's private fleet. This force composed the largest privately owned starfighter fleet in the galaxy. thumb|left|100px|Fighters above [[Ord Cantrell/Legends|Ord Cantrell]] It was armed with one very large laser cannon and one smaller laser cannon in the nose, and was propelled by three sublight engines mounted on small aft wings. The D'Astan starfighter was a rather fast craft, capable of outrunning a TIE/IN interceptor in atmospheric flight. This fighter design was instrumental in Baron D'Asta's assault on the estate of self-proclaimed Emperor Xandel Carivus in the Battle of Ord Cantrell. *Crimson Empire II: Council of Blood 6 *Star Wars Handbook 2: Crimson Empire Category:Starfighter classes
