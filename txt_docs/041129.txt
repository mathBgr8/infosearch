Glurrg (pirate)
1097

Glurrg was a male pirate who served under Captain Huba in the Indobok Pirates. Formerly a cook along with the rest of the gang, he was forced to work in a crystal mine after Olag Greck framed the chefs for an attempted poisoning of Boonda the Hutt.
