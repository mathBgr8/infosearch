Peggy Roberts-Hope
1305

Peggy Roberts-Hope provided the voice of Mon Mothma in the 1995 game Star Wars: Dark Forces and in one of its sequels, Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith. She also voiced Mon Mothma and an Alliance Command Center Communications Officer in Star Wars: Rebellion.
