Fazak Tsun
1164

Fazak Tsun was a male Yuuzhan Vong who served as a member of the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong empire. He underwent a simple Escalatier Ceremony under Master Shaper Ch'Gang Hool. However, the Priests found a flaw with his escalation, and declared that the Gods had withdrawn their favor for some fault of Tsun, and he became a Shamed One.
