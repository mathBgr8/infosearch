Morv'vyal
1759

Morv'vyal was a male Bothan tech with the Drev'starn Department of Criminal Discouragement taking fingerprints and other clues in the break-in of the Exoticalia Pet Emporium. When the others of his team smelled smoke, they suspect there was a fire in the building.
