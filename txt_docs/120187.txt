Sabodor
2045

Sabodor was a male Rakririan, one of the few to leave his homeworld of Rakrir. Sabodor settled on Etti IV where he established Sabodor's, an exotic pet-store. He catered to the rich upper class of Etti IV, who desired his acquisitions as a means of showing off their wealth. Of the numerous pet-stores, his was considered the best. Sabodor sold a dinko to Han Solo who planned to use it to repay Ploovo Two-For-One, but only after declining to purchase Chewbacca, initially believing Solo intended to sell the Wookiee.
