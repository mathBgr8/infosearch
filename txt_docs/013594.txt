Womrik
3270

Womrik was a planet located within the Womrik system in the Dufilvian sector of the Mid Rim, near New Cov. The sights of its ice-capped mountain peaks, which were second to none, attracted many tourists and were a source of pride the planet's inhabitants. Garm Bel Iblis used it as a location for Peregrine's Nest. When asked about the number of bases they had used Sena replied the current one was "Fourteenth..." "...That's if you count Womrik and the Mattri asteroid bases." However, Lando Calrissian later doubted the accuracy of those statements when he realized how permanent a structure Peregrine's Nest had become. There may never have, in fact, existed such a base as the one she spoke of, or even the location.
