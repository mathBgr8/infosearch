The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series
6853

"The University of Sanbra Guide to Intelligent Life: The Marvel Series" was an article in Star Wars Gamer 1 that was written by Rich Handley and Joe Bongiorno and illustrated by Joe Corroney. The article was written in an in-universe style and attributed to Tem Eliss.
