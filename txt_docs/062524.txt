The Givin Jujiran Halbeet was a maintenance worker who worked alongside his fellow Givin Gavrilonnis Tejere. While working at the Yag'Dhul Ambassadorial Landing Platform some time just prior to the outbreak of the Clone Wars, Halbeet spotted his co-worker as he attempted to commit an act of sabotage. Upon confronting Tejere, his co-worker shot Halbeet with a concealed blaster, with the force of the impact knocking him off the platform. Fortunately for Halbeet, Jedi Masters Mace Windu and Yoda were passing in an air taxi under the platform and caught him.
