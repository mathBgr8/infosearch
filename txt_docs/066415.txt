Bouncer (starship)
1600

Bouncer was a system patrol craft employed by Dunari. It was hired to protect his casino Dunari's Rest. It was on patrol when Ace Azzameen and his brother Emon went to the casino for a drink, only to be turned back after engaging with Viraxo ships within casino airspace. The Bouncer was not seen again after this. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:IPV-1 System Patrol Craft
