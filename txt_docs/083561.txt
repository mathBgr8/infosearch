SoroSuub Facility
1584

SoroSuub Facility was a manufacturing plant on the planet of Sullust, owned and operated by the SoroSuub Corporation. Prior to the Clone Wars, it also served as a Podracing circuit as part of SoroSuub's sponsorship of the Galactic Trials. The atmosphere on Sullust did not provide breathable air, so the facility was built underground with a manufactured oxygen supply. The Podrace track wound through the facility and into glass tubes protruding from the exterior.
