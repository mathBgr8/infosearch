Scarab droid
1192

Scarab droids were small assassin droids manufactured by Sienar Intelligence Systems. There were eighteen versions of the scarab droid, and all could fit in the palm of a hand. Each version had a different set of weapons and equipment, including spines, claws, and flesh-eating shredders.
