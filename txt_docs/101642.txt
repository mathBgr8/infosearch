Viridian crystal
1111

A Viridian crystal was a rare type of crystal that was used for the color crystal in a lightsaber. This lightsaber crystal produced a lightsaber blade with a silvery-green color. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Knights of the Eternal Throne *Star Wars: The Old Republic: Onslaught Category:Lightsaber crystals
