Fernooda
1585

Fernooda was a male Dug follower of Ashaar Khorda. Fernooda acted as Khorda's intermediary, hiring Jango Fett and Zam Wesell to retrieve the Infant of Shaa from Seylott. Jango later strangled him on Coruscant as he and Wesell foiled the cult's plot along with Jedi Master Yarael Poof. *Star Wars: Jango Fett *Star Wars: Zam Wesell *Star Wars: The Comics Companion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Coruscanti Category:Dugs Category:Information brokers Category:Males
