Thennqora
1116

The Thennqora were a sentient species from the Thennqor system that influenced Saffa paintings after their initial contact, several millennia before the Galactic Civil War. Tamish was a member of this species. The name is misspelled "Thenngora" in the comic adaptation of Heir to the Empire. *Heir to the Empire Category:Mid Rim sentient species
