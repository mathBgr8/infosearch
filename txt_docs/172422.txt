Moosh was originally planned to be part of the 2003Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, but was cut from the final release. This was revealed by one of the game's writers, David Gaider, in a post on the now-defunct Knights of the Old Republic forums. In this and several other posts, he mentioned Moosh while elaborating on the planet Sleheyron, which received only a mention in the final release.
