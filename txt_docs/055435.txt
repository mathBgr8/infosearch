Dreadnaught-Five
2090

Dreadnaught-Five, called D-Five for short, was the fifth of six Dreadnaught-class heavy cruisers used on Outbound Flight. It came directly from the Rendili StarDrive line for use on the project in 27 BBY. Before Outbound Flight departure, Dreadnaught-Five had a minor fuel leak problem. After the crash, it was used as the home of the settlers of the Outbound Flight Colony.
