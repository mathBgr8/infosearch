Michael Heisler
8019

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Letterer |companies=Dark Horse Comics |website= }} Michael Heisler is a letterer for Dark Horse Comics. He did the lettering for most Star Wars comics published from 2007 onwards. Among his works are Star Wars Tales comics "Marooned," "Marked," "Shadows and Light" and "Unseen, Unheard," the last four volumes of Clone Wars Adventures, most issues of Star Wars: Dark Times and Star Wars: Legacy, and the entire Star Wars: Rebellion, Star Wars Adventures, Star Wars: Invasion, Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, Star Wars: The Old Republic, Threat of Peace, Star Wars: The Clone Wars, Star Wars: The Clone Wars graphic novellas, Star Wars: Lost Tribe of the Sith — Spiral and Star Wars: Blood Ties series. Heisler graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania with a double major in drawing and painting in 1985, before becoming a comic book letterer for Marvel Comics in 1987.
