Shock rifle
829

Shock rifles were powerful, medium-range anti-personnel weapons mounted on the NR-N99 Persuader-class droid enforcer. Two weapons could be mounted on either side of the droid's body, and each fired up to ten explosive projectiles in a shotgun-like pattern. The combined shots were powerful enough to waste infantry at relatively close range, and were also effective against small repulsorcraft such as speeder bikes, but lacked the punch to blast away the heavy armor of much larger vehicles. *Star Wars: Battlefront II *Star Wars Battlefront II: Prima Official Game Guide Category:Projectile weaponry Category:Vehicle weaponry
