Ossiki Confederacy Army
3540

The Ossiki Confederacy Army was a military unit on the planet Toydaria in the intersection of the Mid Rim portion of Hutt Space.The Essential Atlas The army served the Ossiki Confederacy and regularly fought in the Toydarian seasonal wars, a cyclical conflict driven by the creation of food shortages by Toydaria's climate. The Ossiki Confederacy's leader once declared that none of their enemies should have access to food if they could not have it. Under this directive, the Ossiki Confederacy Army used chemical weapons to poison and destroy the supplies of rival confederacies, becoming infamous for doing so. During one of the wars caused by drought, the Toydarian Watto joined the Ossiki Confederacy Army in his youth and received a broken left tusk and a lame leg during the conflict. After the drought subsided and the war ended, Watto mustered out of the army by approximately 38 BBY.Jedi vs. Sith: The Essential Guide to the Force states that Watto was present on Tatooine not long after Anakin Skywalker arrived there when he was roughly three years old. The book also states that Skywalker was born in 41 BBY. Therefore, Watto must have left the Ossiki Confederacy Army by approximately 38 BBY.
