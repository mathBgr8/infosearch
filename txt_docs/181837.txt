The Empire Strikes Back Annual
2057

The Empire Strikes Back Annual was a hardcover comic book released in the United Kingdom that collected the Star Wars Marvel UK series of comic books of the adaptation of Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. The story was first published in the UK in The Empire Strikes Back Weekly 118-135 A year later, the book was followed up with The Empire Strikes Back Annual No. 2. Empire Strikes Back Annual
