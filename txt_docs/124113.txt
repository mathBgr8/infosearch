Golan Space Colony 1
1814

The Golan Space Colony 1 was the first model of Golan Arms' line of modular space colonies. This model was in common use throughout the galaxy until later models were introduced that required a smaller crew and had additional cargo space for supplies and other essentials such as extended medical facilities.
