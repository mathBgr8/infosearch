|locations= |formed=0.5 ABY |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Galactic Empire }} Bantha Squad was a training squadron of TIE/LN starfighter pilot cadets at the Academy of Carida around 0.5 ABY. During a training mission in Tarkin's Teeth, Cadet Shira Brie killed half a dozen members of Bantha Squad in her Mountain Terrain Scout Transport; however, the survivors did not seem the least bit fazed.
