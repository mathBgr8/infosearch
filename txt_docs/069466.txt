Slyder's space shuttle
1033

Slyder's space shuttle was a small and heavily armed space vessel, owned by the bounty hunter Slyder, who kept it aboard the Star of Empire. Slyder however operated mainly onboard the Star, tracking and hunting down those who did illegal activities on the ship, such as Rebel agents. Despite its capabilities, it wasn't as large, fast or efficiently armed, compared to other private ships such as Dol Donar's yacht. Indeed, while hunting Jan Ors and her team, Slyder agreed to accompany Dol Donar II and his men on his yacht, after the Rebel vessel Truly Sorry. Slyder's shuttle was left behind on the Star, when Slyder perished in an explosion, and its eventual fate was unknown. *Dark Forces: Soldier for the Empire Category:Bounty hunter starships Category:Shuttles
