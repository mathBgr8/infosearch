Geology
1148

Geology was the study of planetary processes or referred to planetary processes itself. Those applying its concepts were known as geologists. Groundquakes, volcanoes, and geysers were studied in this field. Geology was a field studied by the Svivreni for use in mining. Geology was based on the study of the Earth.
