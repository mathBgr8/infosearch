G-class shuttle
1402

G-class shuttles were small, two-person shuttles sometimes carried aboard Consular-class space cruisers. Jedi Knights Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker used a G-class to infiltrate Krayn's ship and disable his weapons. *Jedi Quest: Path to Truth *Jedi Quest 2 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Shuttle classes Category:Starship classes of the Galactic Republic
