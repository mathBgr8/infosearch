Maria (Lambda-class)
1513

Maria was a Lambda-class shuttle used by the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. The Maria delivered the Death Star plans, recently acquired by AX-235, to Leia Organa on the CR90 corvette Tantive IV. The transfer was interrupted by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Immortal which launched several Assault Gunboats before jumping into hyperspace.
