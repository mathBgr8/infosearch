Dontal
1717

Dontal was a pilot who served in the Rebel Alliance who served aboard the MC80 Home One type Star Cruiser Independence during the Galactic Civil War. In 1 BBY, Dontal was stationed aboard the Independence and was often seen with fellow rebel pilots Misch'an and Karka.
