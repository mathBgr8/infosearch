Corridor
1272

The Corridor was an area on Belsavis' southern hemisphere. It was far enough from the world's volcanic vents to be atmospherically stable every twenty-four hours. Because the authorities could not track ships passing through it, it was used as a haunt for smugglers making the Belsavis Run.
