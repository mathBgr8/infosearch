Trillan Katos
1241

Trillan Katos was a Human male who worked as a banker on a planet during the Clone Wars between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems. He and his family, a wife and two sons, were caught in the crossfire, but were rescued by his co-worker Rayt and his companions.
