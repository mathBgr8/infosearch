|cover artist=Kilian Plunkett |illustrator= |editor= |publisher=Fun Works |early release= |release date=March 1998 |intl release= |rereleased= |media type=Hardcover |pages=10 |isbn=1570828245 |isbn2= |isbn3= |timeline=c. 0.5&ndash;3 ABYThis story is set between the events of and , which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 ABY and 3 ABY respectively. |series=Cruise Along Books |preceded by= |followed by= }} Luke Skywalker's Race Against Time was a young children's book telling a story about Luke Skywalker flying his X-wing against an Imperial ship headed to a Rebel base. It was written by John Whitman and illustrated by Kilian Plunkett. As a Cruise Along Book, the book came packaged with a Micro Machines X-Ray Fleet miniature X-wing fighter. Luke Skywalker leads a squad of fighters to destroy an Imperial Star Destroyer sent to attack a recently discovered Rebel supply base. The fighters fly through a flock of mynocks, and one attaches itself to Luke's X-wing. He shakes the mynock loose after nearly crashing and continues on with the other fighters. They destroy the Star Destroyer and head back to their base. Category:Legends Young Readers
