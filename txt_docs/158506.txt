VicksVisc
725

VicksVisc was a company that manufactured casings for holoprojectors. They were used on the R2-series of astromech droids, namely R2-D2. The company was named for Vickers Viscount, the airplane from which a reading lamp was used for the holoprojector casing. Category:Manufacturing companies
