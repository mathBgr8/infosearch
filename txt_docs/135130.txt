In 1 ABY, the Shifty Prince was transporting a load of phasotron radium in the Naboo system when it was forced to stop by an independent spacer in a heavily armed ship. The spacer, who had been hired by the station master of the Dantooine Space Station, boarded the freighter and stole the phasotron radium. The Shifty Prince appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. The Shifty Prince was added to the game with the release of the expansion pack Jump to Lightspeed on October 27, 2004. It was the first target of a mission that was available to players when communicating with the Dantooine Space Station.
