It was the site of a capital that, at some point by the time shortly after the Battle of Yavin, rebelled against the Galactic Empire and allied itself with the Rebel Alliance. In response, as part of the Galactic Civil War's Hook Nebula campaign, the Imperial Qeimet fleet besieged Nulan VI, and the fleet's Scimitar assault wing carried out deep-atmospheric precision bombing that forced the capital to surrender. The siege of Nulan VI was subsequently mentioned in a compilation of Imperial documents made by Rebel historian Major Arhul Hextrophon for Alliance Supreme Commander Mon Mothma and other Rebel officers.
