HK-58 Besh
1204

HK-58 Besh was a HK-series assassin droid used by HK-47 in his attempts to take over the planet Mustafar. It was destroyed by a group of spacers who went on to defeat HK-47. HK-58 Besh was linked with its twin, Aurek, and if either Aurek or Besh fell, and the other wasn't destroyed within five seconds, its comrade would rise and continue fighting.
