|publisher= |release date= |media type=Song |pages= |published in= |genre= }} "Kick the Ranat" was a song performed as a hit single by The Palpatones, a band formed by Greeata Jendowanian, Rystáll Sant, and Joh Yowza after the crime lord Jabba the Hutt was killed in 4 ABY's Battle of the Great Pit of Carkoon.The New Essential Chronology Because of Jendowanian's beautiful voice, the band became an intergalactic sensation&mdash;also enjoying limited success in the Outer Rim Territories&mdash;through "Kick the Ranat," before Sant left the band. The song shared its name with the Ranat species.Galaxy Guide 4: Alien Races, Second Edition "Kick the Ranat" was introduced in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003. On January 21, 2014, Edward M. Erdelac wrote "Slugthrowers: An Overview of Popular Music and Musicians in a Galaxy Far, Far Away, Part 2," on StarWars.com. In that article, "Kick the Ranat" was again mentioned and was spelled as "Kick The Ranat." This article assumes the original "Kick the Ranat" spelling from "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band" is correct. Category:Songs
