Gilthor Breen
1234

Gilthor Breen was a Balmorran man and the public face of Lyster Innovations, a company on Drewwa. The pale-skinned man met Ben Skywalker when the Jedi apprentice came looking for the Amulet of Kalara. He was a fan of Aliniaca Verr, who also happened to be from Balmorra. Ben was able to distract him with the thought of her or a lookalike long enough for the young man to sneak into the adjacent offices of Tendrando Arms to continue his quest. *Legacy of the Force: Exile Category:Balmorrans Category:Humans Category:Males
