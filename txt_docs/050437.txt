Yal'avi'kema
2230

Chief Engineer Yal'avi'kema was a Chiss officer. In 27 BBY, Chief Yal'avi'kema was the senior engineering officer aboard the cruiser Springhawk, the command ship of Force Commander Mitth'raw'nuruodo and Picket Force Two of the Chiss Expansionary Fleet.
