Golthar's Sky
1479

250px|thumb|The Golthar's Sky illusion approaches the [[Foerost Shipyards.]] Golthar's Sky was a starship conjured up by Sith magic. In 3997 BBY, it approached the Foerost Shipyards within Galactic Republic space, asking for hull repairs. The Foerost technicians were shocked at the immense size of the vessel, and asked them to hold position outside the yards while they sent out an inspection crew to determine what could be done. It was all in vain, however, for the ship was actually a Force illusion created by Ulic Qel-Droma's accomplice, the Sith illusionist Aleema Keto.
