And-Yees
1819

And-Yees was a male Gran wanted by the Republic Security Force. The RSF indicated in their bounty notice that he was a trained assassin and sharpshooter, and that his last known sighting was on Gromas. The bounty hunter Jango Fett eventually claimed Yees' bounty on Outland Transit Station after learning that he was among the hired guns employed by one of his other bounties, Meeko Ghintee. Despite attempting to resist capture with his blaster rifle, the Gran was captured and turned over for his bounty. And-Yees' relation to the similarly named Ree-Yees is unknown. His name was inspired by the lead tester for Bounty Hunter. And-Yees is the third bounty located in the second level of Chapter One.
