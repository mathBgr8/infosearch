It was orbited by five planets. The innermost, Kadok III-A, was a searing-hot rock world with eight moons of its own. The second orbit was occupied by another searing rock planet, Kadok III-B, which had fifteen natural satellites. Kadok III-C, a barren rock planet with a single moon, occupied the third orbital position around its star. Further out, Kadok III-D, commonly known as Guiteica, was a temperate terrestrial world with mountains, glaciers, forests, and huge waterfalls on its surface. It was the homeworld of the j'kuutak and roggwart creaturesStar Wars: The Clone Wars: Episode Guide as well as the sentient Bitthævrian species. The outermost orbit around Kadok III was occupied by the gas giant&ndash;type planet Kadok III-E and its seventy-five moons. Kadok III was mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
