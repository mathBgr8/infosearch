Photoreceptor (Hyperspace)
8024

thumb|300px Photoreceptor was a feature on the Star Wars Hyperspace fan club website. It showcased hundreds of exclusive, sometimes never-before-seen images of the Star Wars saga pulled from the Lucasfilm archives. As of February 9, 2006, Photoreceptor contained 334 images. Many of these were featured in Star Wars Insider Best of Hyperspace articles. Photoreceptor was discontinued from further updates as announced via a blog entitled "Lucas Online by Pabawan" on April 17, 2007.
