Nebulba
1415

Nebulba was a male Dug and a member of the famous Sebulba family, and The Sebolto Clan of Malastare. He had a bounty posted on his head around 32 BBY, which had been specifically ordered by a hateful rival of his podracing relative, the Gran Mawhonic. The bounty was eventually collected by the bounty hunter Jango Fett. The bounty was worth 3000 credits for a live capture, but was reduced to 1500 credits for being taken dead. *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter *Star Wars: Bounty Hunter: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Dugs Category:Males
