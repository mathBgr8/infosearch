|penciller=*Brian Ching *Travel Foreman |inker= |letterer=Michael HeislerStar Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 1: Commencement |colorist=Michael Atiyeh |cover artist=Travis Charest |editor=*Jeremy Barlow *Dave Marshall |publisher=Dark Horse Comics |release date=December 13, 2006 |other date= |media type=Trade paperback |pages=152 |isbn=1593076401 |isbn2= |timeline=3964 BBY |series=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic |issues=0-6 |preceded by= |followed by=Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 2: Flashpoint }} Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Volume 1: Commencement is the first trade paperback of the Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic series of comics. Released on December 13, 2006, the paperback collects the six issues of the Commencement arc as well as the prequel issue #0, Crossroads. Thousands of years before Luke Skywalker would destroy the Death Star in that fateful battle above Yavin 4, one lone Padawan would become a fugitive hunted by his own Masters, charged with murdering every one of his fellow Jedi-in-training! From criminals hiding out in the treacherous under-city of the planet Taris, to a burly, mysterious droid recovered from the desolate landscape of a cratered moon, Padawan Zayne Carrick will find unexpected allies in his desperate race to clear his name before the unmerciful authorities enact swift retribution upon him! *The flagship series of Dark Horse's exciting relaunch of its Star Wars publishing line, celebrating the company's twentieth anniversary. *Set during the era of the smash-hit videogames, Knights of the Old Republic features all-new characters in an action-packed adventure essential to any serious Star Wars devotee and the perfect introduction for anyone interested in exploring the universe beyond the movies! *Knights of the Old Republic 0 *Knights of the Old Republic 1 *Knights of the Old Republic 2 *Knights of the Old Republic 3 *Knights of the Old Republic 4 *Knights of the Old Republic 5 *Knights of the Old Republic 6 01
