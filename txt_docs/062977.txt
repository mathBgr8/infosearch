Command cloak
985

[[Tsavong Lah with his command cloak|200px|right|thumb]] Command cloaks were a variety of living cape that was worn by high-ranking members of the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Command cloaks were connected to the recipient's body via claws that would grip onto their shoulder blades through cuts made into the flesh of a Warrior after their escalation. Such cloaks were only worn by those Yuuzhan Vong who had attained the rank of Commander or higher. *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos II: Jedi Eclipse *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The New Jedi Order: Dark Journey *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Robes and cloaks Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
