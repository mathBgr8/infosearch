Winning Hand
939

The Winning Hand was a pleasure ship the size of a medium transport. In 26 ABY, it made a hyperspace jump from the Corellian sector to the Meridian sector, near Obroa-skai along the Perlemian Trade Route, and vanished after making a garbled transmission. It was located by Uldir Lochett aboard the Pride of Thela. However, it was revealed to be a Peace Brigade ship, and unsuccessfully attempted to capture the Pride of Thela with its tractor beams. *The New Jedi Order: Edge of Victory I: Conquest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Liners Category:Starships of the Peace Brigade
