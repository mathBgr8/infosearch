thumb|170px|[[Rodian/Legends|Rodian head icon marked on shipments of Bantha bile]] Bantha bile was a material harvested from the bantha on the planet Rodia. It was a black-market substance that was used as a medicinal remedy. Han Solo smuggled bantha bile during the Galactic Civil War in containers marked with a Rodian head icon. *Star Wars Insider 89 Category:Drugs and medicine
