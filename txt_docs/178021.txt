Neural disruptor
720

Neural disruptors were devices similar to collars attached to prisoners' necks to incapacitate them. Brejik used this device on Bastila Shan, only to be outwitted by the Jedi, who managed to resist it and take it off with help from the Force. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *The Isis Coordinates Category:Restraining devices
