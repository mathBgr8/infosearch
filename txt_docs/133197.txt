Bharhulai
1189

The Bharhulai were one of several nomadic tribes that inhabited the planet Socorro. The Bharhulai roamed the northern polar regions of Socorro, and used crude weaponry and simple clothing. They were known for fiercely defending their territory and attacking any who dared to enter their lands. The anthropologist Arner Figgis believed that the Bharhulai may have descended from a crashed colony ship and regressed to their savage state, exemplifying his survival-regressive-isolationist theory. *The Black Sands of Socorro *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Culture of Socorro Category:Families
