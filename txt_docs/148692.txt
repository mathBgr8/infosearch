She was a member of House Paramexor. Some time during the war, Imperial Prefect Adar accused Nath of a variety of crimes and, after a trial, she was to be punished according to Guild rules. The trial was overseen by Moff Gorliz and presided over by Janq Paramexor. On learning that the trial involved a battle with Nath, Adar quickly dropped the charges.
