Bargoon Tatooni
948

Bargoon Tatooni was a privately owned trading post in Mos Eisley on Tatooine. It was often the site of an annual Jawa rendezvous and swap meet, and it had the best prices on spacecraft parts and cookware. Bargoon Tatooni was eventually absorbed into the galaxy-wide Barguna Moocha chain. *Adventures in Hyperspace: The Big Switch *Galactic Phrase Book & Travel Guide Category:Markets Category:Mos Eisley locations
