Hiakk tree
937

The hiakk tree was a tree native to New Plympto. Hiakks had silvery trunks, and reached 200 meters in height. The Hajial Chase was a large hiakk forest on New Plympto. Rikknits dwelled in the high branches of hiakk trees, spinning web-nests. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Trees
