|birth=32 BBY, Kamino |death= |species=Human |gender=Male |pronouns=new |donor=Jango Fett |height=1.83 meters |mass= |hair=Black |feathers= |eyes=Brown |skin= |cyber= |families= |parents= |partners= |siblings= |children= |affiliation=*Galactic Republic *Mandalorians **Clan Skirata |masters= |apprentices= }} Kom'rk Skirata (pronounced KOM-or-rohkStar Wars: Republic Commando soundtrack), originally known as Null-6 or N-6, was a Null-class Advanced Recon Commando in the Grand Army of the Republic. A year into the Clone Wars, along with Jaing Skirata and clone shadow troopers, he was one of the Clone Intelligence units responsible for locating General Grievous on Utapau. As Kom'rk and Jaing Skirata prolonged their search for General Grievous, they scouted and observed planets within the outer rim. In 21 BBY, Kom'rk and Jaing came across information regarding General Grievous's affiliation with the Regent of Garis. They felt as though this came to them too easily and that someone wanted them to know that Grievous was based there, so they didn't report this information to their superiors until the end of the Clone Wars. Kom'rk deserted from the Grand Army of the Republic when Order 66 was issued and went to live on Mandalore with the rest of Clan Skirata. Soon after arriving, he discussed Etain Tur-Mukan's cremation and the Imperial Army's improved computer defenses with his Null ARC brother Ordo Skirata. Kom'rk had a good sense of humor, just like all the other Null ARCs. He gave what he called a "crumb" to Kal Skirata to give to Arligan Zey. The chip which he gave Skirata was a recorded conversation between General Grievous and an ally they didn't know Grievous had, the Regent of Garis, minus the location. He told Skirata and his brother Nulls that he didn't want Mace Windu or Obi-Wan Kenobi charging in there and ruining the operation.
