A microsynapse was presumably a droid component, though it is unknown what purpose it might have served. Ca. 15 BBY, following a battle between Kugg's Destroyer and a Ranger X-1 on Dodz, R2-D2 suffered some damage to his microsynapse, which required repairs impossible to perform there, leading the droid and his counterpart to leave Dodz. *Droids (1986) 1 Category:Droid parts
