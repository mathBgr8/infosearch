C-16 fragmentation grenade
2102

The C-16 fragmentation grenade was a model of fragmentation grenade manufactured by Merr-Sonn Munitions, Inc. The grenade contained a detonite core surrounded by a prestressed chrome shell. When detonated, the C-16 sent out a flurry of shrapnel into the target area. The C-16 had a low mass, and incorporated a detonator switch with a present delay of four seconds. The timer could be altered by twisting a dial.
