Vanquisher (Kiltirin-class)
1788

The Vanquisher was a modified Kiltirin-class dungeon ship used as a mobile headquarters for the Mytaranor Slaving Council. It was obtained by Talas Piran, leader of the Slaving Council, during a business trip to Kessel. It was later essential for the Council to obtain its virtual monopoly on slavery on Mytaranor sector. *Alliance Intelligence Reports Category:Enslaver starships Category:Rendili StarDrive products
