Leah Mangue
2307

Leah Battle (formerly Mangue) is an illustrator who has created licensed artwork for Lucasfilm. She is the founder of the Nerf-Herders-Anonymous fansite. In December 2005, her Star Wars Blog became a featured blog on StarWars.com. Her illustration of Boga the veractyl appeared in the Bantha Tracks section of Star Wars Insider 90. Her Star Wars 30th Anniversary tribute artwork appeared in the Bantha Tracks section of Star Wars Insider 97. She was chosen as an artist for Star Wars Celebration V with her print "Have Rifle Will Travel." In May 2007, she was one of the sketch card artists for the 2007 Topps Star Wars 30th Anniversary collectors card set. She has since worked on other Topps collectors card sets, including 2008 Topps Star Wars: The Clone Wars, 2009 Topps Star Wars: The Clone Wars Widevision, 2009 Topps Star Wars Galaxy Series 4, 2010 Topps Star Wars Galaxy Series 5, 2011 Topps Star Wars Galaxy Series 6, 2012 Topps Star Wars Galaxy Series 7, and 2012 Topps Star Wars Galactic Files Series 1.
