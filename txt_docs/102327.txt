Monitor (carrier)
1103

The Monitor was a training carrier in the New Republic Defense Force. The initial trials of the experimental K-wing bombers were conducted on the Monitor. The Monitor was originally mentioned in the online authorial FAQ of Michael P. Kube-McDowell, author of the Black Fleet Crisis trilogy, rather than in the novels of that series, but this information was retrospectively added to the published canon in sources such as The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Carriers Category:Starships of the New Republic
