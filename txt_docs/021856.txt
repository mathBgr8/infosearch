&mdash; Based on corresponding data for Gendius system |system=Gendius system |coordinates=J-17 |xyz= |distance= |suns= |orbited= |position=5 |moons= |routes= |lengthday= |lengthyear= |class= |diameter= |atmosphere= |climate= |gravity= |terrain= |water= |interest= |flora= |fauna= |otherlife= |species= |otherspecies= |socialgroup= |language= |government= |population= |demonym= |cities= |imports= |exports= |affiliation= }} Gendius V was a world that occupied the fifth orbital position within the Gendius system, a part of the Mid Rim's Gendius sector. At some point, the inhabitants of Gendius V were threatened by a plague. A group of individuals visiting the world saved the local people from the disease, and several young adventurers from Gendius V joined the group, becoming the followers of one of their saviors. Gendius V was first mentioned in "Do You Know Who I Am?," an article written by Owen K.C. Stephens and published on March 28, 2002 as part of the Tips and Tactics column on the Wizards of the Coast official website. The world is mentioned in an adventure suggestion for use with the Star Wars Roleplaying Game, which this article plays out as described.
