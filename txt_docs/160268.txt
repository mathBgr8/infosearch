Lumini pirates
1469

The Lumini pirates were a pirate organization. By 35 ABY, the organization had become defunct, having been attacked by the First Order. Kuimi Enissa, a young lieutenant in the organization, was the sole survivor of the attack. *Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker: The Visual Dictionary Category:Pirate organizations
