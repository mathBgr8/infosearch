Malric Blaene
1028

Malric Blaene was a squat, balding male who was a seasoned bartender of the Lost Loves Casino located on Phyrstal Island in the resort city of Le Yer on the planet Abregado-rae during the Galactic Civil War. Being a large gossip, he knew a great deal of questionable information on the rivalry of A'jidre Skrigatov and Malkoi.
