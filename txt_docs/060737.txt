R'Ytha Myr
965

R'Ytha Myr was a female who may have in the past dated Ace Azzameen (or perhaps another individual called "Ace"). Months before the Battle of Endor, she sent an e-mail message to Ace Azzameen, saying that if he was the Ace she dated at Luxiar 7, he should: "DROP DEAD, YOU LOUSE!" She then added that he should disregard the message if he was not the right person. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance Category:Females Category:Individuals of unspecified species
