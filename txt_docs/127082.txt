Unidentified New Republic mammalian diplomat
1260

A female was a diplomat in the New Republic. She had a pet saw-toothed grank. The appearance of this species is one that appeared in the preliminary design stage for Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. Members of this species were designed as slaves and servants for the Hutts on Tatooine as seen in The Art of Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace. *The Wildlife of Star Wars: A Field Guide Category:Diplomats Category:Females Category:Individuals of unidentified species Category:New Republic individuals
