|published in=Star Wars Tales 4 |reprinted in= |pages=4 |timeline=4 ABY |series= |preceded by="A Death Star Is Born" |followed by="Sand Blasted" }} "Spare Parts" is a 4-page comic that first appeared in Star Wars Tales 4. The story was written by Mark Evanier and Sergio Aragonés, art also by Sergio Aragonés, letters by Stan Sakai, colors by Tom Luth and the editor was Scott Allie. On Tatooine, C-3PO and R2-D2 are spotted and captured by Jawas. Seeing that the Jawas are disassembling other droids for spare parts, Threepio knocks two Jawas unconscious and steals their cloaks.
