Kronex spaceport
738

Kronex spaceport was a considerably large spaceport in the Llon Nebulae that served its visitors with a variety of cantinas. The Storm Fleet made a stop in the spaceport during the Clone Wars. Two Jedi, Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker, used their local contact Fizz to covertly learn the purpose and destination of the fleet. *The New Essential Chronology Category:Spaceports
