Battle of Esseles (Galactic Civil War)
1994

A battle occurred near Esseles between the Galactic Empire and the New Republic during the Galactic Civil War. Following the Battle of Endor, Imperial Navy forces were retreating from Esseles when the battle erupted in the outer Essesia system. This battle was the only major battle in this star system of the Galactic Civil War.
