Walk worm
737

Walk worms were large centipede-like, insectile creatures that guarded Uda-Khalid's hidden fortress. They were four meters long and had segmented bodies. Large mandibles protruded from their mouths, used to capture and kill their prey. Category:Invertebrates Category:Predatory creatures Category:Species of undefined sentience
