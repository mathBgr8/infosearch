Fi (disambiguation)
544

Fi can be more than one thing: *Fi was the name of Grand Admiral Peccati Syn's flagship. *Fi was a clone trooper who was killed at Dinlo. *Fi was also the nickname of clone commando RC-8015. *"Fi" was also a High Galactic term used by Sacred Way clergymen.
