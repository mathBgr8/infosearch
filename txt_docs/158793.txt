Offshoot question
2066

The "offshoot question" was the term given by genetically pure Arkanians to the matter over how to deal with the offshoot Arkanians.Knights of the Old Republic 17 By the time of the Mandalorian Wars, most had opted for segregation and relegation of the offshoots to a subjected class.Knights of the Old Republic 18 Arkoh Adasca planned to use Adascorp's genetic engineering expertise to solve the "problem" in a generation by releasing a plague that killed them. Author John Jackson Miller based it on such historical ethnic/religious notions as the "Jewish Question." *Knights of the Old Republic 17 *Knights of the Old Republic 18 *Knights of the Old Republic 20 Category:Arkanian culture
