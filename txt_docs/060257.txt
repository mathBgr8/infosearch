Zoosha fabric was a soluble material available in the galaxy.Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary The Human clubgoer Nyrat Agira wore fashionably impractical clothes made from zoosha fabric. In 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Agira wore a pair of sheer, red-colored zoosha leggings to the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planet Coruscant. In 8 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Cophelion Interlude to 8 ABY. the Rebel Alliance member Leia Organa dressed comfortably but fashionably in a pair of slinky zoosha pants during her and her partner Han Solo's honeymoon to see the Corphelion Comets over the Expansion Region asteroid Corphelion.
