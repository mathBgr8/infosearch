Crystal fern
914

Crystal ferns were plants believed by some to be primitive silicon-based life-forms. The fern could be found in the Hoth asteroid field. It could grow up to several meters in height and propagated when shards of crystal were broken off and took hold on another asteroid. *Young Jedi Knights: Shards of Alderaan *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Ferns
