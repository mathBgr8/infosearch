Its captain was Phulik. The Binder was severely damaged by Aggressor Wing during the battle, but was later repaired and served in the fleet of the Ciutric Hegemony, when the self-proclaimed "Prince-Admiral" took control of the region. In the Battle of Ciutric, the Binder surrendered to the New Republic after Krennel was killed and his flagship Reckoning was destroyed. When the Hegemony entered the New Republic, the ship and its crew were integrated into its defense force and remained at Ciutric IV. The Binder makes an appearance in Star Wars: Empire at War as a Sienar Immobilizer 418 cruiser.
