Ten-Suckers Madoom
1361

Ten-Suckers Madoom, alias Deeto, was a male Rodian criminal noted for his lack of common sense and lack of two fingers/suckers during the Galactic Civil War. He wielded a custom Widowmaker blaster. Madoom was captured by Raal Yorta and Sammie Staable on Ord Mantell, during a game of sabacc. *Rookies: Rendezvous *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Males Category:Rodians
