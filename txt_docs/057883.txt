Ma'Haffee Shipyard
1407

Ma'Haffee Shipyard, known coloquially as The Shipyard by locals, was an influential starship modification and repair company that was located in the Cularin system. It was owned by Trace Polters. The following starships were available in new condition at the Ma'Haffee Shipyard during the Clone Wars: *Barloz-class medium freighter *Firespray-31-class patrol and attack craft *GR-75 medium transport *Gun tug *ILH-KK Citadel-class civilian cruiser *S40K Phoenix Hawk-class light pinnace *YT-1300 light freighter *Z-95 Headhunter *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starship technology companies
