Lin Nunsk
1689

Captain Lin Nunsk was an Imperial Navy captain who had been assigned to the Tapani sector fleet. Leading it from his flagship, the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Gauntlet. He was stationed in the Tapani sector for six years prior to the Battle of Endor and was charged with safeguarding the Galactic Empire's bacta shipments as they made their runs through the Shapani Bypass.
