|habitat= |diet= |language= }} An Ethrani ourobouros was a creatureStar Wars: Revenge of the Sith novelization from the Ethrani system of the Valahari sector, a part of the D'Astan sector of the Outer Rim Territories and the New Territories. places the Ethrani system in grid square N-5, which, according to The Essential Atlas, is located in the New Territories region of the galaxy. Ethrani ourobouroi were known for devouring their own tails. During a battle near the planet Coruscant in 19 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the Battle of Coruscant to 19 BBY the leader of the Confederacy of Independent Systems Dooku&mdash;secretly apprentice to the Dark Lord of the Sith Darth Sidious&mdash;mentally compared his master's plan to convert Jedi Knight Anakin Skywalker to their side by killing his mentor Obi-Wan Kenobi to an Ethrani ourobouros consuming its own tail.
