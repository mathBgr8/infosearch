|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation= }} Galas was a city in Kallistas during the time of the Galactic Civil War. A gem mine settlement, it was eventually abandoned and taken over by sand bears and, later, by Rebel agent Pallas Quintell for a base. Quintell's Z-95 Headhunter fighter was hidden there.
