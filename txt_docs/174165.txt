Weed-eel
1521

Weed-eels were a type of silver-colored eel found in the Braan River on the temperate Mid Rim planetThe Essential Atlas Qiilura in the year 22 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion places the events of Republic Commando: Hard Contact, which include the appearance of weed-eels, in the year 22 BBY.
