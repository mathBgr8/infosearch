Invasion of Ralltiir
2321

The planet Ralltiir was invaded by the Galactic Empire in 10 ABY. As part of the Imperial offensive prior to Operation Shadow Hand, the reborn Emperor Palpatine sent out vast forces to conquer strategic worlds in the Core.
