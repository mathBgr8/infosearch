It was a part of the Koornacht Cluster, itself a region of the Negs portion of the Core Worlds. One of the three gas giants in the system, which was centered around the star Doornik-628, Doornik-628A was orbited by three moons. Doornik-628A was mentioned in "J't'p'tan: Temples and Tybis," an article written by Jason Fry and published on Wizards.com on January 30, 2003. The 2009 reference book The Essential Atlas placed the Doornik-628 system, and therefore Doornik-628A, in grid square K-10.
