Chainly
1751

Chainly was an Imperial captain who assisted Darth Vader on his mission to Samaria in 18 BBY.The Essential Reader's Companion They tried to track down the slicer Astri Oddo Divinian who was only known under the name "Quintus Farel." When Chainly and his stormtrooper team finally discovered Astri's the secret hiding place they unexpectedly encountered the two Jedi Ferus Olin and Fy-Tor-Ana who protected her and her son Lune Oddo Divinian.
