Fey'lya's Pride
2586

Fey'lya's Pride was the name of Borsk Fey'lya's personal Lambda-class shuttle, which he used during the Galactic Civil War. It was aboard this shuttle that Fey'lya escorted a single Bothan escaping from the planet Kothlis with a stolen supercomputer that contained the detailed plans and location of the construction of the Death Star II during the Battle of Kothlis. With Darth Vader aboard his personal flagship Executor and the Imperial II-class Star Destroyer Avenger bearing down on the Kothlis II station, a Rebel orbital platform located above Kothlis, Fey'lya and his shuttle docked with the CR90 corvette Razor in order to safely deliver the plans to Alliance High Command.
