|published in=Dark Horse Presents Annual 2000: Girls Rule! |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=c. 36 BBY is established in Star Wars Omnibus: Rise of the Sith to take place Approx. 36 years before the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY, 36 years after 36 BBY. |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Aurra's Song" is a comic set shortly before The Phantom Menace. It was part of the Dark Horse Presents Annual 2000: Girls Rule! Aurra Sing is on board a Consular-class cruiser, and she has been captured by a former Anzati assassin, Torgo Tahn. The two reminisce about their past and Tahn brags about how he lured her on board. When he is sufficiently distracted, Sing breaks out of her bindings and slips them onto Tahn, revealing that she set up the whole thing, and that his Anzati friends would be interested in seeing him again.
