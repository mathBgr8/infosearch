thumb|right|250px|[[Lando Calrissian/Legends|Lando Calrissian, Luke Skywalker, Leia Organa, and C-3PO in Pavillion]] Pavillion was an underwater city on Iskalon. It was built so water and air breathing visitors could interact with each other.
