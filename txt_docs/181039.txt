Kafleer Tredahl was an amateur holocam operator native to Corellia. When Kafleer's mother, named Zayna Tredahl, was kidnapped in 1 ABY, he appealed to Chertyl Ruluwoor for aid. 250px|thumb|Every possible depiction of Kafleer Tredahl Kafleer Tredahl appeared as a non-player character in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
