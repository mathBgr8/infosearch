Amethyst Shipping & Storage
871

Amethyst Shipping & Storage was a phantom company on New Cov, established by Garm Bel Iblis during the Galactic Civil War. It maintained a warehouse in Ilic, where Han Solo first met Bel Iblis' right and left hand, Sena and Irenez respectively.
