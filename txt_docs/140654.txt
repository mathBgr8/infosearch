Sea Legacy
1488

The Sea Legacy was an Acclamator-class assault ship that deployed the clone forces in the Battle of Pengalan IV. When the battle turned out to be an ambush, the Sea Legacy recovered her troops, and retreated immediately. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Acclamator-class assault ships Category:Starships of the Galactic Republic
