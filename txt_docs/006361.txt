Tavya-class armored picket
3023

The Tavya-class armored picket was a heavy starfighter constructed by Hoersch-Kessel Drive, Inc. and used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars. In contrast to the large capital ships created by HKD for the Trade Federation, the Tavya-class (named after the Tavya system in the Tapani sector) was a small, fast scout, standing in a tradition that stretched back to the Delaya-class courier of four thousand years earlier, and also embodied by the contemporary Seltaya-class fast courier. The Tavya-class carried a single pilot, and was well-armed with energy and missile weapons, including a turret-mounted laser cannon. An additional proton torpedo launcher could be slung below the ventral fuselage for extra firepower. Unlike many of the fighters in use during the Clone Wars, it was equipped with a fully-functional internal hyperdrive. As the Clone Wars intensified, the Confederacy of Independent Systems' fleet began a series of successful surprise attacks against Republic convoys at key hyperspace exit points using intelligence secretly supplied by Palpatine as Darth Sidious.
