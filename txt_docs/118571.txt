Aquatic gundark
1651

The aquatic gundark was an aquatic preator that lived on Yavin 4. It had multiple eyes, making it capable of seeing below and above water simultaneously. They were medium-sized creatures that stalked the shallows of streams and lakes for prey, such as runyips and whisper birds. It is important to note that this and several other animals throughout the Galaxy were called "gundarks," often to be synonymous with "surly".
