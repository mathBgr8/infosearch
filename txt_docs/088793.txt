Zed
2019

Zed was a 501-Z model Level-One security droid on Hosk. Following the evacuation of the spaceport, he met C-3PO and his counterpart, R2-D2. Quickly he gained their support and that of dozens of leftover and broken droids. Together they rerouted a possible explosion on the station and foiled Olag Greck's plan to steal a load of ash ore.
