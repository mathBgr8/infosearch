Paul Danner
2201

Paul M. Danner is an author who wrote six short stories for the Star Wars Adventure Journal: "One of a Kind," "Easy Credits," "Small Favors," "The Last Hand," "No Disintegrations, Please," and "Two for One." "No Disintegrations, Please" and "The Last Hand" were reprinted in Tales from the New Republic in 1999. His short stories could also be found on Hyperspace. He was also the author of Wretched Hives of Scum & Villainy, a sourcebook for Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game. Paul Danner is the co-author and screenwriter of Who Will I Be with in Heaven book. He is the co-screenwriter of If You Only Knew drama film (2010).
