|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters= |locations= |formed= |founded=By 13:3:14 |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation=Greenputt Governing Council }} The Not-Dead-Yet Professional Greenputt Tour, abbreviated NDYPGT, was one of the major professional leagues for greenputt&mdash;a ball sport played on a field&mdash;overseen by the Greenputt Governing Council. The formation of the Non-Sentient Professional Greenputt Tour, which joined the existing professional greenputt leagues, was reported in the news story "Yet Another Greenputt League" in the CoCo District Edition of the HoloNet News on the date 13:3:14. The Not-Dead-Yet Professional Greenputt Tour was first mentioned in HoloNet News Vol. 531 #47, an in-universe issue of the HoloNet news published on the HoloNet News website on March 14, 2002. The site's content was written by Pablo Hidalgo and Paul Ens. Category:Greenputt leagues
