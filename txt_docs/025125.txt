Taieb
1036

Taieb was a Human male artisan who lived in Mos Espa, Tatooine during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. As a gift, he crafted a small podracing helmet for Anakin Skywalker in 32 BBY. *Star Wars: Episode I: The Visual Dictionary *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Artists Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Tatooinians
