thumb|right|200px Environmental bastion armor was an advanced, upgradeable environment suit used around the time of the Mandalorian Wars and Jedi Civil War. The suit was designed to offer complete protection from temperature extremes, sonic extremes, and many forms of toxins&mdash;all while offering solid protection from combat. With additional modifications, the armorsuit could be converted into a fully contained enviro-suit capable of affording the wearer protection from radiation.Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Enviro-suits Category:Medium armor
