Battle of Ando
3281

The Battle of Ando was a skirmish between the Separatist government of Ando and the Republic members of the Andoan Free Colonies during the Clone Wars. As such, it was an Aqualish civil conflict. The Republic sent members of the Jedi Order to assist the Free Colonies, some or all of whom died.
