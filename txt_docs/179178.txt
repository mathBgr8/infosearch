Suicide timer
467

Suicide timers were a type of timed demolition charge that was used by the Lok Revenants/Mere Resistance. Nym and his commando team were known to use this timer on the shield bunkers to deactivate the shield bunkers to render Cavik Toth's Trihexalon production factory vulnerable and destroy it. *Star Wars: Jedi Starfighter Category:Demolition technology
