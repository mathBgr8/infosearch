ITO-9

ITO-9 was an interrogation droid abandoned when its starship was destroyed in space. It was commonly known as a Void Droid that patrolled the Paradise system junkyards. It was equipped with a laser scalpel, power shears, a hypodermic injector, and a grenade. *Scavenger Hunt Category:Interrogation droids
