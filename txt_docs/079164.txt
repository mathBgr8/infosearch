Lonatran Gardens
733

The Lonatran Gardens was a botanical reserve located on the Gree Enclave world of Lonatro and was home to many species of rare and exotic plants. The Lonatran Gardens were maintained through Gree genetic manipulations which allowed for species of plants hitherto thought to be extinct to be present on this world and in the Gardens.
