Shana (Mistryl Shadow Guard)
1131

Shana was a female Human from Emberlene and one of two Mistryl Shadow Guard warrior-women who served as escorts for Mara Jade when she was acting as a Smugglers' Alliance representative in 11 ABY. Her starfighter was destroyed by the Death Star prototype in the Battle of the Maw Installation. *Champions of the Force *Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Mistryl
