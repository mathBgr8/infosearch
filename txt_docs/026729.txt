Battle of the Al'Har system
2425

A battle occurred during the Great Sith War in the Al'Har system. It was fought between at least one Jedi capital ship and a Krath force that came from the Malastare system. From what is known, the Jedi destroyed most or all of their opponents, but their ships sustained heavy damage and crash-landed on the only world in that star system, Haruun Kal. The Krath forces moved on to the Sullust system. Their descendants were undoubtedly the Force-sensitive "Korunnai" people, to whom Jedi Master Mace Windu belonged. The Battle of Al'Har was mentioned in Shatterpoint.
