It was located along the Gamor Run and contained a star orbited by the planet Aridus, homeworld of the Chubbit species. The Flarestar system was originally created for the Star Wars Legends reference book The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons, written by Daniel Wallace, edited by Sue Rostoni and Steve Saffel, and illustrated by Brandon McKinney and Scott Kolins and released In July 1998.
