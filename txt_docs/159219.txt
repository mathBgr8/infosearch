Minister of War (Naboo)
694

The Minister of War was the civilian manager of war related subjects on Naboo. The position had a seat on the Royal Advisory Council, but was dissolved during the Great Time of Peace. *Star Wars: Episode I: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: Episode I Insider's Guide Category:Naboo titles Category:Political titles
