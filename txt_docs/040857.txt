7442

Episode I Adventures 3: The Fury of Darth Maul is a Star Wars Legends junior novel written by Ryder Windham. It is the third volume of the Star Wars: Episode I Adventures series, and it was published by Scholastic in November 1999, with an accompanying gamebook. A sequel, Jedi Emergency, was published the following month. Someone has stolen something that belongs to Darth Sidious. Now he wants it back.
