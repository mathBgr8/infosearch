Toombs assisted in planning Operation Cobolt, and, along with Gideon, led the assault teams assigned to penetrate the Imperial orbital base over Mantooine to recover a new Imperial Intelligence Scandoc decoding computer. Toombs was killed during the operation, and Madine requested permission from Mon Mothma to recommend Toombs for posthumous promotion and commendation.
