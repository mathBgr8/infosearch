Sarylcorp
1405

Sarylcorp was a manufacturing company that produced technological components for starfighters. Among their products were the reactant agitator injector used on Incom Corporation's T-65 X-wing starfighter and the Particle Motivator used in Sienar Fleet Systems' TIE/IN interceptor. *The Star Wars Sourcebook *Starfighter Battle Book: X-wing vs TIE Interceptor *Heir to the Empire Sourcebook *The Star Wars Sourcebook, Second Edition *The Thrawn Trilogy Sourcebook Category:Starship technology companies
