|pages= |published in=Star Wars Insider 74 |reprinted in= }} "The New Jedi Order in 100 Easy Lessons" is a 10-page reference article appearing in Star Wars Insider 74. It was written by Jason Fry, illustrations by Mike Sutfin. The article contains a full list of the New Jedi Order novels, tie-in comics and short stories, with Characters Introduced and Key Events explaining specific details along the series.
