Tempest Scout 3
1776

Tempest Scout 3 was an All Terrain Scout Transport in Tempest Force. It was assigned to the task of searching for potential Rebel traps while operating on the forest moon. Tempest Scout 3 participated in the Battle of Endor, where it was destroyed. Category:All Terrain Scout Transports
