Armored Heartbreak
2090

"Armored Heartbreak" was a song by the band Red Shift Limit. The song was featured on two of the band's compilation albums, Limited Warfare and Thoughts from the Core, both of which were available in the galaxy by the time between the years 0 ABY and 4 ABY.Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim is set in the time period between the Battle of Yavin and the Battle of Endor. Those battles are dated to 0 BBY and 4 ABY, respectively, by The New Essential Chronology, which also establishes the Battle of Yavin as the final event before the beginning of 0 ABY. As a result of Red Shift Limit being openly anti-Imperial, the song was banned by the Imperial Board of Culture. "Armored Heartbreak" was mentioned in Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim, a supplement to West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game written by Simon Smith and Eric Trautmann and published in 1993. *Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Category:Songs by Red Shift Limit
