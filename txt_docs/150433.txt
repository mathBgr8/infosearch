Starfly
568

The Starfly was a small, maneuverable single-person vessel. It was equipped with a tractor beam but carried no life support equipment, hence, Starfly pilots were forced to wear self-contained suits while flying the ships. Starflies were piloted by miners in the asteroid belts near Ithor. *Galaxy of Fear: Spore Category:Starship classes
