Loiric Kaan
1141

Loiric Kaan was the Supreme Commander of the Yammka's Mount, the flagship of Warmaster Nas Choka during the Yuuzhan Vong's assault on Mon Calamari. When he made no useful tactical suggestions but merely assured Yuuzhan Vong victory with empty words, the Warmaster had guards escort Loiric from the command chamber. *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Yuuzhan Vong senior officers
