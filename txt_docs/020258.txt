Swing-bop
501

Swing-bop was a style of music popular circa 0 BBY. Umjing Baab and His Swinging Trio were among the Rughja musicians who played swing-bop. Their style of swing-bop involved each band member playing ten or more instruments at once. *Rebel Dawn *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial Category:Music genres
