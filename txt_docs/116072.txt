Ra-lugg
1319

The ra-lugg was a small, red, six-legged arachnid or crustacean native to Endor. The ra-lugg possessed yellow or black eyes, two grasping pincers, and a web-shooting tail to catch its prey, such as the tiny Fleebogs. One of these creatures in particular terrorized the Fleebog tunnels, until a dose of Ewok growing powder caused it to reach a larger size.
