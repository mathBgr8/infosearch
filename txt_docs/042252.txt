Camp Six
3355

Detention Camp Six was a temporary camp of the Trade Federation, established near Harte Secur on Naboo during the Federation's occupation in 32 BBY. The base consisted of a Command center and two troop centers, and was protected by four laser turrets. Fambaas were captured to be skinned and sold. The camp was visited by the Sith Lord Darth Maul to meet with one of the prisoners, Leika, to find information about the Gungans, a sentient species on Naboo. Camp Six was destroyed by Boss Rugor Nass and the Gungan Grand Army prior to the Battle of Grassy Plains, in order to free the fambaas and use them in the battle. Camp Six appears during the Gungan campaign in the 2001 video game Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds.
