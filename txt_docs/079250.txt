Combat sensor
1524

thumb|right|150px|[[Bao-Dur sporting a Combat sensor.]] A sophisticated visor that was used during the Old Sith Wars, the combat sensor was a modified version of the Spacer's sensor which employed targeting software that had prediction algorithms to direct the user's gaze in combat, increasing efficiency during battle.
