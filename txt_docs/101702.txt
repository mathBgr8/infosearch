It consisted of a young reader novel and an accompanying game book for use with the story, and was sent out with Star Wars Adventures Magazine 6 in March 2003. What Count Dooku wants, Count Dooku gets. Even if it's a Senator. Even if it's a Hutt. He is lucky to have bounty hunters like Jango Fett and Aurra Sing to help him.
