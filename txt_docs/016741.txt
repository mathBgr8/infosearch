Reltooine
2780

Reltooine was a gas giant in the Tingel Arm. It was a part of the Corporate SectorThe Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia and a member world of the Corporate Sector Authority. Reltooine's many moons were popular tourist destinations, replete with breathtaking sights such as rings, erupting cryovolcanoes, and fiery aurorae.
