Kram Ayuk
1737

Kram Ayuk was a male Gran from Hok who worked for RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. He was wanted by the Coruscant Security Force for questioning, involving smuggling of death sticks on Malastare. In 32 BBY, Ayuk had known connections to the RavinsBlud Crime Syndicate, working for them before the Battle of Naboo. Later on, he stayed in the smuggling business by working for Sebolto, a Dug crimelord. He smuggled death sticks and was one of the causes of the sudden rise in Coruscant's death stick dealings. A bounty was posted on him, 5,000 credits alive, 2,500 dead.
