During the Galactic Civil War, Nawnam attempted to eradicate Ganis in order to take over his empire. Ganis, in return, placed a bounty on his head. Nawnam's henchman, F'ej D'aw, hired a group of spacers to smuggle Nawnam off-planet. The Hutt was wounded in the attack and lost his repulsor-sled leaving him immobile. He hid in an old warehouse and waited for D'aw to find him a way off of the planet. The spacers stole a repulsor-truck belonging to Burtiin to move the Hutt crimelord.
