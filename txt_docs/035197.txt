Qang qahsa
1951

Qang qahsas were specialized qahsas used by the Shaper caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. Similar to the qahsas, the Qang qahsas were designed to hold all the information as the protocols of the shaper caste. They normally contained five primary cortexes of knowledge that were accessible to all Shapers with the two additional inner cortexes only being accessed by Master Shapers. The very core of the Qang qahsa contained the eighth cortex which contained the most sacred protocols said to have been bestowed on the species by the gods. The knowledge within them was maintained through use of massive Rikyam brains. What was unknown to the majority of Yuuzhan Vong was that the Qang qahsa did not come from their gods. It did contain all the knowledge that was bestowed upon the Ur-Yuuzhan Vong millennia ago which was used to defeat a machine race back on Yuuzhan'tar. Since that time, the origins of the Qang qahsa was lost to future generations who began to revere the gods above all else. The information from the Qang qahsa was also then bestowed upon the shapers who created the living servants of their race so that they could thrive and prosper.
