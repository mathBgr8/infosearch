LUX-3 landspeeder
1824

The LUX-3 landspeeder was a repulsorlift vehicle manufactured by MandalMotors. The landspeeder had a sleek, rounded frame, an enclosed cockpit and twin, rear-mounted engines. It could hover an unusually high 3 meters and had predictive terrain-following sensors. The Bounty Hunters' Guild had missile equipped models on Tatooine, Terminus and Port Haven. *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels *The Bounty Hunter Code: From the Files of Boba Fett Category:Landspeeder models Category:MandalMotors products
