Leisure Corridor
1326

The Leisure Corridor was a popular trade and tourism hyperlane. The route began at Coruscant and traveled through the Sarin and Parmel sectors, before ending at Ebiwaan.Pirates & Privateers A frequent traveler of this hyperroute was the passenger liner the Culroon Minstrel. Galaxy Guide 9: Fragments from the Rim Ebiwaan was originally located in the Inner Rim.
