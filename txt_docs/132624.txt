Fingal
1803

Fingal was a small Berchestian man who worked overtly as an aide in Governor Staffa's office, and covertly as a spy for Grand Admiral Thrawn. Stationed on Berchest for almost a decade, he had a trade relationship with Governor Staffa for some time. When the Galactic Empire collapsed, he thought his service was over.
