Around the year 10 ABY,Galaxy Guide 11: Criminal Organizations is set around six years after Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi, which takes place in 4 ABY according to The Essential Reader's Companion. it served as the homeworld of the Palkandi Brokerage House, a front organization for the criminal Tenloss Syndicate.
