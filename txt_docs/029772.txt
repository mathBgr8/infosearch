Rellias
1499

Rellias was a city on Naboo ruled by the Gungan Boss Ganne. It was connected to Otoh Gunga through the Rellias channel. During the Invasion of Naboo, Darth Maul attacked the city and forced Boss Ganne to lead him to the Rellias channel in order to prepare the attack of the underwater Gungan cities. *Star Wars: Galactic Battlegrounds Category:Cities on Naboo Category:Gungan Swamp locations
