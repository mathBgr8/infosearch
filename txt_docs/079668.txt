Barid Mesoriaam
2281

Barid Mesoriaam was a high-ranked male Human agent of the Corellian Resistance. He was one of the few who had Bria Tharen's personal holocomm code. In 0 BBY, Mesoriaam was captured by Jabba the Hutt and imprisoned in his Mos Eisley town house. Jabba's men tortured Mesoriaam for information on the Rebel Alliance. Mesoriaam was found by Muftak and Kabe, who had broken into Jabba's mansion.
