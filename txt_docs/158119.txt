Daring (frigate)
1934

The Daring was a frigate operated by the Galactic Alliance in the years following the Yuuzhan Vong War. In 40 ABY, the Daring was part of a Alliance task force monitoring Bothan fleet units in Bothan Space, along with the Alliance warship Bounty. During a mission near the Bothawui system, the Daring successfully engaged a task force of nineteen Bothan frigates, with the aid of the larger Bounty and several squadrons of X-wing starfighters.
