Prosperine Entertainment and Distilleries
951

Prosperine Entertainment and Distilleries was a local company based on the planet Algara II. The company also owned and operated the famous Club Prosperine, an exclusive resort located on the continent of Kreesis. The Chief Executive Officer of the company was Dellin Sorth, a member of the Prosperine social class. *Planets of the Galaxy, Volume Two *The Star Wars Planets Collection Category:Entertainment companies
