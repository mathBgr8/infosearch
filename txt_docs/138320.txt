Seeker Rail Detonator
868

The Seeker Rail Detonator was a Rail detonator which used Seeking rail charges. Its only known difference from a regular Rail Detonator was an ability to fire rail charges which would follow the nearest enemy until it hit. Apart from this ability, it was presumably an identical weapon. *Star Wars: Jedi Knight: Mysteries of the Sith Category:Explosive projectile launchers
