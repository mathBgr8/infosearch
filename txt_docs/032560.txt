Omega (designation)
1038

Omega was a standard designation for Delta-class DX-9 stormtrooper transports found aboard Imperial warships or space stations. A typical duty for these craft was disabling and boarding enemy craft, or heavy rocket attack such as the assault on the Rebel cruiser Lulsla. Another standard designation for Imperial DX-9s was Sigma.
