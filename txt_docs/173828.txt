Algarian Bureau of Planetary Information and Tourism
1390

The Algarian Bureau of Planetary Information and Tourism was an administrative department under the ruling government of the planet Algarian and the Algarian system that oversaw the distribution of public information about the planet and the system. Without the submission of Form AA-23-7022 and a 150 credit processing fee to this department, public information about Algarian and its system was restricted. *Star Wars: Rebellion Category:Bureaucratic and government organizations
