Ooglith
1114

Oogliths were Yuuzhan Vong-bred fungi. When immature, the feeder filaments of an ooglith could transmit germs into the pores of an individual that could kill them. Mature oogliths developed antibacterial enzymes to prevent such an occurrence. Oogliths were shaped into cloakers and masquers. Jacen Solo, while a captive of the Yuuzhan Vong, used the oogliths growing in the Nursery to conceal weapons for an eventual slave revolt and escape attempt. *The New Jedi Order: Traitor Category:Fungi Category:Yuuzhan Vong creatures
