Ashielle Skamini
1381

Ashielle Skamini was a Human female who served as the proprietor of an ore prospecting launch base on Mon Gazza, along with her husband Sembric Skamini and was the mother of Warpoc Skamini, who became Padawan Zett Jukassa. Ashielle and Sembric were murdered by Black Sun operatives seven years after the birth of Zett Jukassa. Zett sensed the murder through the Force, and was involved in the investigation with his master, Mierme Unill.
