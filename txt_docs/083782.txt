Multycorp
567

Multycorp was one of the largest corporations on Vorzyd IV before and around the time of the Clone Wars. Its corporate building had at least twenty-four floors with assembly on floors seven and eight, manufacturing on floors nine and ten, accounting on floor twenty-four and the central computer in the basement. *Jedi Apprentice: The Threat Within *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Companies
