Starboard Broadside Club
1256

The Starboard Broadside Club was a cantina aboard Omze's Incredible Traveling Starport. It was owned by the Wookiee Broadside. It was lit by the large viewports that took in the ambient light created by orbiting a sun or planet. The club was staffed by Twi'lek serving girls who provided drinks and hearty meals to the clientele. The club was outfitted with luxurious leather-covered chairs, shiny metal fixtures, and sparkling glassware. A small stage was in the back, where a band often played music for the patrons.
