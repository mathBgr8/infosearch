Felloux
1800

Felloux was a poet who composed a work detailing the Sith victory at the Battle of Mizra, a battle which took place in 1466 BBY as part of the New Sith Wars. The piece detailed the vast number of Sith Lords plummeting from a sky thick with ash. Felloux noted that each rode a speeder named for a predatory animal that represented their individual personalities, and that the noise produced by the sheer number of repulsorlift vehicles drowned out the screams of those dying in the conflict. The New Essential Chronology&mdash;a book published by the New Republic's Historical Council under the Galactic Federation of Free Alliances&mdash;was published in 36 ABY and detailed aspects of Felloux's piece describing the Battle of Mizra.
