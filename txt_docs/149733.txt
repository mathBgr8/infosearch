Orowood
1160

Orowood was a high-class area on Coruscant that contained Orowood Medcenter and Orowood Tower. It was located twenty kilometers east of West Championne and near the Manarai Mountains. Orowood was named after the oro wood of Alderaan; it had originally been planned to be a living area for those who survived the destruction of Alderaan.
