Euceron (species)
1545

The Eucerons were a species native to the planet Euceron. They were tall humanoids with large, domed heads and delicate limbs. The Eucerons dressed in neutral colors, as if to offset the colorful building structures that filled their cities. Their government, the Ruling Power, hosted the Galactic Games on their world in 26 BBY. *Jedi Quest: The Dangerous Games *The Essential Reader's Companion Category:Mid Rim sentient species
