Aquilaris Grand Arena
736

thumb|250px|The Aquilaris Grand Arena at the start of a podrace. The Aquilaris Grand Arena was a large arena in the capital Floating City on the beautiful ocean world Aquilaris. Thousands of spectators safely watched sports such as dangerous podraces from the stands. Special guests hosted the races from repulsorlift viewing platforms. The Aquilaris Classic, Sunken City, and Bumpy's Breakers were all race courses that began at the arena. *Star Wars: Episode I Racer Category:Aquilaris locations
