Karn
1443

Karn was a large, dark-skinned Human male who joined the Rebel Alliance during the Galactic Civil War. During the Galactic Civil War, he was a member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic loyal to Garm Bel Iblis. Along with his subordinate Lhira, he was in command of Iblis' six cells on the planet Stoga.
