Droids by Janice
2090

Droids by Janice was a small droid shop located in the Upper City of the Outer Rim planet Taris during the time of the Jedi Civil War. It was owned by the Twi'lek Janice Nall, who&mdash;although skilled&mdash;had few customers due to her species. She sold droid upgrades and parts during the Sith Empire's occupation of the ecumenopolis in 3956 BBY.The Essential Atlas places the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, which includes the appearance of Droids by Janice, in 3956 BBY.
