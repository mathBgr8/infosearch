Jinsu Razor
1181

The Jinsu Razor was a unique lightsaber hilt, one which was made by the Emperor's Hand Aralina Silk, who died in Restuss. It is believed her Jinsu Razor hilt was taken by one of the attackers, who then presented it to the Jedi Antaria Wellos. Category:Lightsaber hilts
