The trooper was stationed in a garrison on Bespin's Cloud City after the planet was occupied by Imperial forces led by the Sith Lord Darth Vader in the year 3 ABY.The New Essential Chronology dates the events of , including the occupation of Bespin that AF-119 partook in according to , to 3 ABY. AF-119 was armed with an E-11 blaster rifle. He found his assigned guard duty on Bespin boring, and preferred the excitement of hunting gundarks. AF-119 also found the Cloud City casinos lost their attraction after he was stationed on the gas giant for an extended period.
