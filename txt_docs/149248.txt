Bridge crystal
1192

thumb|250px|[[Teebo/Legends|Teebo uses the Rainbow Crystal to form the Rainbow Bridge.]] The bridge crystal, also called the rainbow crystal, was a magical Ewok artifact. By standing at the edge of the Gorge of Fire and letting the suns rays shine through the crystal, one could form a Rainbow Bridge over it. The bridge was the only passage between the forests of the Ewoks and the land of Zandor, although if the sun stopped shining, the rainbow would disappear. The Ewok Chief Chirpa of Bright Tree Village had stolen the crystal from the Ogre Gantu in his youth, in order to prevent Gantu from raiding Ewok lands. The crystal was later destroyed by accident in a confrontation between Gantu and the Ewok Teebo. *Ewoks (1985) 1 Category:Sacred Ewok items
