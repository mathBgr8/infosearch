Hanto Mountains
1122

The Hanto Mountains were a mountain range on Sulon. Two tall jagged peaks that seemed close together were referred to as "the twins".Dark Forces: Rebel Agent They separated a continental semi-arid region of the planet that included Nefra Canyons, from the farmed regions that included Barons Hed and Fuel City. While the Moldy Crow was fleeing from the Fuel City, Kyle Katarn advised Jan Ors to retreat to the Nefra Canyons in order to lose the pursuing TIE/LN starfighters. While approaching the Mountains, the Crow rolled and passed between the twins, followed by one TIE, but the one following scrapped a side of a peak, exploded, and caused an avalanche that reached the base of the mountain. *Dark Forces: Rebel Agent *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mountain ranges Category:Sulon locations
