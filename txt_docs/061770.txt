Under the Empire, Yzu was an admiral in the 15th Deep Core Reserve Fleet. Foga Brill, the Planetary Governor of Prakith, became his close friend and advisor. Brill cultivated influence under Cinzero Gann, who had became Yzu's political lackey, until Yzu was killed at Tsoss Beacon, a victim of Admiral Daala mass murder of the region's ranking warlords.
