Rampa Rapids
2630

Rampa Rapids was a hyperspace trade route that linked R'alla to Rampa III. This route was used by smugglers to supply Rampa III in water from nearby R'alla. It was strictly controlled by Corporate Sector Authority gunships of the Rampa Skywatch. Smuggler Platt Okeefe found falsified cargo manifests for a run down the Rampa Rapids once while cleaning out the computer files of the ship.
