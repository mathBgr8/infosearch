Karap Valley was located on Hewl, and it featured fog banks that were considered to be infamous by the Rebel Alliance Lieutenant Colonel Palpatine Essex Yerac. At some point by 2 ABY,Alliance Intelligence Reports represents a collection of in-universe reports from "one year before the Battle of Hoth," which corresponds to 2 ABY, according to The New Essential Chronology. Yerac was tracking an independent bounty hunter group through the fog banks of Karap Valley. While there, the Rebel officer unexpectedly encountered the Filar-Nitzan infiltrator Vacander, who told Yerac to cease the pursuit of the bounty hunters. In a subsequent addendum to an Alliance SecuriDex datafile on Vacander, Yerac mentioned the encounter with the Filar-Nitzan on Hewl. Hewl was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
