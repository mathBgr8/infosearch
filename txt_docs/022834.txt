Modirin Mining Concern
1524

|subsid= |headquarters= |locations= |role=Mining |products= |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} The Modirin Mining Concern was a mining corporation owned by the Tenloss Criminal Syndicate. Shortly before the fall of the Galactic Republic, the Modirin Mining Concern was responsible for planting amounts of chromium underneath the surface of the Outer Rim planet Aduba-3. This caused a rush of investors and shipbuilders from many other planets to migrate to Aduba-3 to make their fortunes. Not surprisingly, the chromium dried up in less than a year. Penniless, businesses were forced to abandon any plants or factories they had produced and leave, which Modirin was able to purchase at massive discounts. They then rented out these structures to Aduban merchants, turning a huge profit in the end. Category:Front companies Category:Mining companies Category:Tenloss Syndicate companies
