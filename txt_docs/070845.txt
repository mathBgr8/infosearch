Emanations of Darkness
3665

"Emanations of Darkness" was a short story written by Patricia A. Jackson concerning the final fate of her characters Adalric Cessius Brandl, Jaalib Brandl, and Fable Astin. She submitted it originally for the Star Wars Adventure Journal, but West End Games stopped producing the journal before the story could be printed. It was later submitted to the Star Wars Gamer magazine, where it was turned down for unknown reasons.
