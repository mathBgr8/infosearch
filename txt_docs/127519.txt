Najarkan wilderbeast
1311

Najarkan wilderbeasts were large snakes native to the rain forests of Najarka. They could grow to over thirty meters long, with mouths over two meters in diameter with which they could swallow Humans whole. Their dull red eyes were low-resolution photoreceptors adapted to detect their favored prey, the black-and-white kichicolias.
