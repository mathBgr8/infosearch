Edda Gast
2786

Edda Gast was a Human female and the scientist behind the mutating and "Humanizing" of alien species in an attempt to expand their minds allowing them to think and feel like Humans. Some of the main species that were used in Dr. Gast's experiments included Gamorreans such as Voort saBinring, Ewoks, and Talz. The chief of the Project Chubar was her uncle, Dr. Tuzin Gast, but he began to love his creations and committed suicide with the more violent specimens. Edda then became the main scientist of the project. Gast was captured by Wraith Squadron, a New Republic commando unit, after their raid of the Binring facilities failed. She was promised immunity from prosecution by Nawara Ven of Rogue Squadron after giving all information on Warlord Zsinj to the New Republic. During negotiations with Nawara Ven on board the Mon Ramonda just prior to the battle of Jussafet Four an assassination attempt was made on her by one of Zsinj's own Project Minefield subjects, but thwarted by Ven. She was believed dead, but in reality was saved by Ven, making her new identity seem even more secure. She was arrogant, and speciesist, but believed she would get away without punishment. Ven offered her a chance to do good with her life, but she took her reward of half a million Imperial credits, which she picked over New Republic credits. It was confiscated on Coruscant and she was arrested on the charge of smuggling. Then Ven pointed out that under New Republic law, the smuggling of large sums of Imperial credits brought with it the charge of sedition.
