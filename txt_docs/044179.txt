Ko Hentota
1087

Ko Hentota was a district of Nar Shaddaa. A large ventilation nexus was buried in the lower levels. For a time in 22 BBY, the Circus Horrificus ran out of this nexus until an arqet escaped and attacked 12 spectators. thumb|right|250px|The first reference to "Ko Hentota". The term "Ko Hentota" first appeared in a sketch of a desert skiff by Joe Johnston during the production of Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi. In this context, carved panels "depicting phases of the Ko Hentota" decorated the skiff's hull, which indicate that it may have been originally conceived as an important event or ritual. *Return of the Jedi Sketchbook *Lords of Nal Hutta Category:Nar Shaddaa districts
