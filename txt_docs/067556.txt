Krayt Draggers
1112

The Krayt Draggers was a swoop gang led by Kav Lepkin, with about 15 members. The gang was located in Anchorhead on the planet Tatooine. Their name and gang symbol were based on the Krayt dragon and the binary suns. Category:Swoop gangs
