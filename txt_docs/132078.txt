Bradan
1636

Lieutenant Bradan was an officer of Warlord Zsinj. Bradan was a combat specialist who was originally assigned the point position of the intrusion team assigned to capture the Razor's Kiss. Her job, however, was given to Shalla Nelprin in the guise of Qatya Nassin, as the other woman was smaller and seemed less intimidating. Bradan accompanied the rest of the team to the bridge of the Razor's Kiss and almost caught "Qatya" downloading a tracking program into the ship's main computer, but was fooled by the other's nonchalance.
