Hydrolift
873

Hydrolifts were modes of transportation, for being and objects, such as those used in Zabin Hive. They were also used in space stations, such as in the hyrdolift wells of Fondor Spaceport, to transport cargo between various levels. *Star Wars Adventures 3: The Hostage Princess *Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones junior novelization *Star Wars Missions 3: Attack on Delrakkin *Star Wars Missions 4: Destroy the Liquidator *Star Wars Missions 6: The Search for Grubba the Hutt *Star Wars Missions 7: Ithorian Invasion Category:Transportation technology
