|intl release= |rereleased= |media type= |pages=96 |isbn=none |timeline= |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} The Farlander Papers is a companion book found only in the "Limited Edition" version of the Star Wars: X-Wing video game (the original floppy disk based release) written by Rusel DeMaria. The book contained a novella about Keyan Farlander's assignment in the Alliance to Restore the Republic. In some points the narration breaks when Keyan opens a Rebel pamphlet which contained "A Call to Reason," spec sheets for the various spacecraft in the game, a "who's who" for the races and characters of the galaxy and much more. When he finishes reading, the narrative continues to Farlander's first mission. It serves as a scene-setter and provides helpful information for playing the game. The "Limited Edition" versions are identifiable only by a small round sticker on the front of the box. Much of the story was later included and expanded as part of X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide, though it was largely rewritten and some parts of the story were left out in order to maintain The
