Del Cycer
1532

Del Cycer was a Human male who played sabacc with smuggler Lando Calrissian during Calrissian's second visit to the Oseon system. Middle-aged by the time of the Imperial Period, Cycer&mdash;who came off to Calrissian as a friendly, likable sort&mdash;claimed to be a retired businessman, although Calrissian regarded him as a formidable sabacc opponent and suspected that he was a professional gambler.
