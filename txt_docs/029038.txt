Immodet
3062

Immodet was an elderly male Human general in the Imperial Army, and one of the funders of Carnor Jax's plan to sabotage Palpatine's Clones. After Palpatine's death, Immodet became a member on Jax's Interim Ruling Council. After the death of Redd Wessel, Immodet was the highest-ranking general in the Empire. His troops occupied several entire sectors along the New Republic frontier and provided a first line of defense. A staunch xenophobist, he was very suspicious of the aliens on the council.
