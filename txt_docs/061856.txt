Mod-3

thumb|right|200px|Mod-3 aboard [[Guardian Mantis.]] Mod-3 was an R2-series astromech droid owned by Vana Sage during her time working for the Trade Federation prior to the Battle of Naboo. He served as Sage's co-pilot and in-flight astromech aboard her starship, the Guardian Mantis.
