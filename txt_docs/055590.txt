Darkvenge
3202

The Darkvenge was a Trade Federation Lucrehulk-class battleship under the command of Vicelord Siv Kav. It was the flagship of Darth Sidious' Special Task Force One, with which he intended to destroy Outbound Flight in 27 BBY. However, Force Commander Mitth'raw'nuruodo (core name "Thrawn") of the Chiss Expansionary Fleet attacked and destroyed the entirety of Sidious' Task Force with the exception of the Darkvenge.
