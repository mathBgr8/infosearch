Launch Bay 81
679

Launch Bay 81 was a hangar bay located on a Rebel Alliance EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate located over Hoth. In 3 ABY, during the establishment of Echo Station on the planet, Princess Leia, Han Solo, and Chewbacca launched twin M-class fighters from Launch Bay 81. *A Valentine Story Category:Spacecraft locations
