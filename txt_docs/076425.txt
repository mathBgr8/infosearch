Segnor's Loop
707

Segnor's Loop was a tactical evasive maneuver in which an attacking starfighter makes a quick run away from its target before doubling back for a second pass. Boba Fett used the tactic at least once while pursuing Rivo Xarran. *Starships of the Galaxy, Saga Edition *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Starfighter tactics
