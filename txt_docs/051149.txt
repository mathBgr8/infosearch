Firestorm (Imperial-class)
2939

The Firestorm was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer which became the flagship of Supreme Warlord Harrsk's fleet after the destruction of the Shockwave. It was turned over to Admiral Daala who led the ship in an attack on High Admiral Treuten Teradoc.
