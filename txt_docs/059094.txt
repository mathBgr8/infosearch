Clustership
1027

Clusterships were vessels used to carry yammosks for Yuuzhan Vong war fleets. Clusterships had a full crew of shapers to help the yammosk, members of the priest caste to bless it, and warriors to protect it, but it was the yammosk itself that controlled the ship and kept it alive.
