Scorpion (CR90 corvette)
914

Scorpion was a CR90 corvette in service with the Imperial Navy. The corvette, along with the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Boltthrower, attempted to prevent the Rebel Alliance from boarding the BFF-1 bulk freighter Libom and stealing new surveillance probes for the Relentless. However, the Scorpion was destroyed by Keyan Farlander while flying a B-wing and the Boltthrower attempts to stop the Rebels failed. *Star Wars: X-Wing Category:CR90 corvettes Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
