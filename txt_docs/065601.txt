Spiz
1263

Spiz was a young Human male. He was the leader of Spiz's hoodlums, a gang of young miscreants who terrorized the slums of Coruscant prior to the Clone Wars. In 27 BBY, while they were harassing and trying to kill a child who they called "brat" and was one of their frequent victims for no apparent reason, Spiz and his gang where scared off by a Force illusion of a rancor created by Jedi Master Yarael Poof. *Star Wars: Zam Wesell Category:Coruscanti Category:Criminals Category:Humans Category:Males
