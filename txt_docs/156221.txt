Zam Ghanol
1329

Zam Ghanol was a wiry, old Human woman with gray hair. She was one of the pirates who followed Erli Prann in the Bilbringi system during the Yuuzhan Vong War aboard a Golan II space station. When caught between the Mon Mothma and the Yuuzhan Vong, Ghanol demanded that Prann turn the ship over to the Alliance. Prann initially refused, though after his crew mutinied and subdued him, Jedi Knight Jaina Solo was permitted to take the ship. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Pirates
