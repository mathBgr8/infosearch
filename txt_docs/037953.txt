Star Wars Insider 90 is the 68th issue of Star Wars Insider, released in October 2006. *"The Models of Revenge of the Sith Part 1" *:The first of a three-part series in which Brian Gernano, head of ILM's Model Shop for Episodes II and III, details how the worlds of Revenge of the Sith were brought to life. This issue, Gernand shows how he and his team helped build Kashyyyk. *"Aliens of Episode III" *:Insider speaks with various concept artists and sculptors about how they created the Utapauns, Mustafarians, and Polis Massans, featuring concept art and behind-the-scenes images supplemented with in-universe origins of each species. *"Spaceballs: The Article!" *:2006 marks the 19th anniversary of Spaceballs: The Movie (no, that's not a typo) and Insider catches up with many of the the film's actors to get their recollections of working on the most madcap Star Wars "fan film" of all. *"Cool Trooper: The Star Wars Art of Nathan Cabrera" *:Nathan Cabrera's life-sized Tusken Raider and Stormtrooper Chick sculptures will re-define the way you look at your favorite Star Wars icons. *"Letter from the Editor" *"Comlink" *"Skywalking" **"Steve Sansweet Talks Saga's Future During Star Wars Spectacular" **"Dustin Roberts: Gentleman, Scholar, and New Star Wars Fan Club President" **"Pitching Felux: The 2006 Star Wars Fan Film Awards" **"The 2006 Artoos Awards Ceremony" **"Refresher Reading": 20 Things You Didn't Know About the Tantive IV **Star Wars International: Star Wars Reigns in Spain *"Jedi Library" **"Sculpting History" **"Caught in the Tempest" *"Drawn by the Force" &mdash; Begin the Dark Times *"Jabba's Collection" &mdash; Bad Guys Wear Red *"Technical Readout: Luke's Landspeeder" *"Ask the Master" &mdash; For the Last Time&mdash;No Episodes VII Through IX! *"Scouting the Galaxy" &mdash; Scumming the Galaxy, One Toy at a Time *"Best of Hyperspace" &mdash; Star Wars' Goes to School *Bantha Tracks Vol. 21 090
