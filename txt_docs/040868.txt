Episode I Adventures 14: Podrace to Freedom
5785

Episode I Adventures 14: Podrace to Freedom was the fourteenth installment of Star Wars Episode I Adventures. Anakin Skywalker is a slave on the planet Tatooine. When a band of visitors needs a way to leave Tatooine, he offers to help. His only hope is to win the most dangerous Podrace of his life.
