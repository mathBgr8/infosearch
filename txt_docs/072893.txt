Chu-Gon Dar
2498

}} Chu-Gon Dar was a male Jedi Master of the Old Republic who took his residence on Mustafar, within the Enclave there. His mastery of the Physical Force was unmatched throughout the galaxy, until his death. He created the Chu-Gon Dar cube which had the power to transform certain specific items. Chu-Gon Dar was mentioned in the video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
