Yn
3116

}} Yn was a planet located within the Yn system of the Inner Rim. Around 5300 BBY, establishes that Ergast attempted Tulak Hord's ritual 300 years after Tulak Hord himself. Additionally, states that Ergast was among the Sith who first permanently settled Dromund Kaas, which occurred in 4980 BBY per Star Wars: The Old Republic Encyclopedia. Thus, Hord's use of the ritual at Chabosh, and by extension the battles of Yn and Chabosh, occurred around 5300 BBY. the Jedi laid siege to an Imperial stronghold at the battle of Yn. Tulak Hord, Ortan Cela, Khem Val, and Veshikk Urk broke the siege with their army of dark side warriors. Hord and Khem Val fed on the energies of the fallen Force-users using an ancient Sith ritual.
