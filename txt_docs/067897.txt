YVH-M
833

The YVH-Ms were mouse droids made by Lando Calrissian to seek and follow Yuuzhan Vong infiltrators. They had the special ability to sense Vonglife and the ability to track it as well. Several YVH-Ms were responsible for finding all of Nom Anor's spy rings on the planet Mon Calamari in 28 ABY. *YVH-M-1 *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Droid models of the Galactic Alliance Category:YVH-series battle droid models
