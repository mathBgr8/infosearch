Kane Mihal
1171

Kane Mihal, a former street-thug, was known by his associates for his ostentatious clothing and boisterous demeanor in conversation. During confrontations, he often engaged in risky behavior to impress women he found appealing, if they were present. He booked passage aboard the Ananuru Express during the time the secret Imperial RNA brainwashing virus was being auctioned. Throughout the journey, he demonstrated a lack of familiarity with dining etiquette, using incorrect utensils instead of acknowledging his inexperience. When Secesh Trant attempted to steal the datatape containing the virus's information, Mihal collaborated with members of the Rebels to confront her.
