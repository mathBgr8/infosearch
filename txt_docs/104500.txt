Boultan

Captain Boultan was a Human officer of the Corellian Defense Force. As the First Corellian Insurrection ignited, it became obvious that the groundside CDF remained loyal to the New Republic, while the spaceside CDF, staffed with many ex-Imperial military, had joined with the Human League and the insurgents.
