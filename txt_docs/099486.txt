Chiewab Laboratories
509

Chiewab Laboratories was an aggressively acquisitive biotechnical company that was located within the Corporate Sector as of 40 ABY. The corporation typically dealt with cloning, through many of its associated businesses such as ConCare and SanTech. *Legacy of the Force: Bloodlines *Arms & Equipment Guide Category:Scientific companies
