Century 1
1810

Century 1 was a BFF-1 bulk freighter used by the Galactic Empire during the Galactic Civil War. It was part of a convoy en route an Imperial base under construction in the Ghorman system carrying construction equipment. However, the freighter, along with Century 2 and Century 3 was destroyed by a Rebel R-22 Spearhead and a Y-wing despite the protection of TIE/LN starfighters. This resulted in a delay of the construction of the base for a full year. *Star Wars: X-Wing Category:BFF-1 bulk freighters Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
