Fate's Judges
1300

}} The Fate's Judges was an elite starfighter squadron of the Imperial Starfleet assigned to interdiction duty in the Sevarcos system. They flew TIE/IN interceptors and GAT-12 Skipray Blastboats and were trained extensively in navigating asteroid fields. For pilots to be accepted into the squadron they had to to fly through the Sevarcos system asteroid field without instrumentation. Category:Imperial starfighter squadrons
