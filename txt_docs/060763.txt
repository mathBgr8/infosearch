Agamarian
1002

thumb|right|[[Keyan Farlander/Legends|Keyan Farlander and a female Agamarian.]] Agamarians were the native peoples of the planet Agamar. Agamar was populated mostly by Humans, such as Keyan Farlander. The Agamarians themselves were either evacuated or killed by the Yuuzhan Vong, during their takeover of the galaxy.
