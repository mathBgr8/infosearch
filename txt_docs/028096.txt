House Malreaux
1549

The House Malreaux were one of the oldest and wealthiest families on the planet of Vjun. It was founded by the First Count who was a pirate that extorted his House's noble status from nobles operating around the Bay of Tears in exchange for a loose promise to not plunder their ships. They lived in a castle named the Château Malreaux. The family was known for developing the Malreaux rose. The Viscount Malreaux performed experiments on midi-chlorians that drove the populace of Vjun insane, leading to them murdering each other in 29 BBY.
