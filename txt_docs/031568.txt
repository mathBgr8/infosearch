Great oopik
1518

The great oopik, also known simply as the oopik, was a species native to Paramatan. Found in the planet's deep forests, the oopik's vestigial wings were thought to be the evolutionary link between the planet's reptiles and avians. They used echolocation to seek their food and stun them with powerful ultrasonic waves emitted from their throat sacs.
