Domain Anor
741

Domain Anor was a Domain among the Intendant caste of Yuuzhan Vong society. By far the most famous member of Domain Anor was the Executor and later Prefect Nom Anor, though members of Domain Anor would probably not have considered this a source of pride as he had frequent brushes with disaster during the course of fulfilling his duties. Another known member of this domain was Phaa Anor, a crèche-cousin of Nom. *The New Jedi Order: The Final Prophecy Anor
