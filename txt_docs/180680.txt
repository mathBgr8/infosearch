Oochee was named for a child from Ewok mythology.Galactic Campaign Guide Oochee is an action figure in a Hasbro battle pack released in 2007. The action figure is composed of a repainted Logray body and a Wicket W. Warrick hood from a 1997 Hasbro two-pack. Previously, the name was used in a role-playing game supplement, listing popular Ewok names and their origins. The battle pack also includes an Ewok named Wicket W. Warrick, although the coloring resembles that of Widdle Warrick, his brother.Caravan of Courage: An Ewok Adventure Together, the dark-furred Oochee and the light-furred Wicket W. Warrick resemble the Ewoks who help Chewbacca commandeer an Imperial AT-ST walker in the 1983 film Star Wars: Episode VI Return of the Jedi.
