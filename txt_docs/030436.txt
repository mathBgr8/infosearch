Leslie Schofield
1379

Leslie Schofield (born December 12, 1938) is an English actor who portrayed Chief Moradmin Bast in Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. Footage of Schofield cut from A New Hope was used in The Star Wars Holiday Special, though Bast's death on the Death Star indicate this was a different officer. In 1969 and 1977, he appeared in the sci-fi series Doctor Who in the stories The War Games and The Face of Evil. He has also contributed to the soap opera EastEnders and the sci-fi series Blake's 7.
