Hover Droid (large)
1416

Hover Droids were floating combat droids that were deployed at Cloud City before the Occupation of Bespin. Hover Droid would rotate while levitating. The Hover Droid existed alongside a smaller model. Hover Droids first appeared in the video game Super Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back, and they were later named in the strategy guide Super Empire Strikes Back Official Game Secrets.
