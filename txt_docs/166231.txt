Celestial equator
1138

The celestial equator formed one of the boundaries of the Outlands Region along with the Great Rift. The celestial equator featured in the opening crawl in the rough draft and first two drafts of the screenplay for the original film. It was deleted in the third draft. In the real world, the galactic equator is the middle of the galactic plane, splitting the galaxy in half like two plates.
