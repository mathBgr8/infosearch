Remlout
1296

Remlout was a male X'Ting who served as a loyal bodyguard to Trillot during the Clone Wars. Around 22 BBY, Remlout knew that Asajj Ventress never declined a challenge, and when she met Trillot, the bodyguard challenged the Dark Jedi. She accepted and vowed that she would not kill the X'Ting thug.
