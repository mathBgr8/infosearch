Greenhouse
1263

The Greenhouse was a large tea house located within Ganza Park in the island city of Estalle on the planet Procopia. The building had a picturesque view of the ocean from where it was built, and was named for the fact its walls were made of heavy crystal glass. During the Galactic Civil War the tea house was open to all despite expensive prices, and was considered one of the highlights of the park.
