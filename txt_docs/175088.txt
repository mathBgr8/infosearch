TechMaster II Vacuum Suit
1287

The TechMaster II Vacuum Suit was a type of vacuum suit manufactured by LifeLine Technologies. The suit cost a thousand credits, and traded systems like temperature regulators and waste recyclers for improved mobility. The gloves in particular were tight-fitting to allow for fine manipulation work while wearing the suit. *Platt's Smugglers Guide *The Far Orbit Project Category:Enviro-suits Category:LifeLine Technologies products
