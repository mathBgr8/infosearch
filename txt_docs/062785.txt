|death=January 18, 2002 |listedpronouns= |nationality= |occupation=Actor |companies= |website= }} Roy L. Conrad (November 11, 1940 &ndash; January 18, 2002) was an actor and voice actor, more commonly known among the LucasArts fandom as the voice of Ben in the game Full Throttle, alongside Mark Hamill, who voiced the game's antagonist, Adrian Ripburger. Conrad also voiced Rebel pilot Ace Merrick in Star Wars: Rebel Assault II: The Hidden Empire. Conrad was most known for his deep and heavy voice, and died of lung cancer in 2002.
