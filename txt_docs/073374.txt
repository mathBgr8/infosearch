|published in=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 3 |reprinted in= |pages= |timeline=22 BBY dates the Hunt for the Decimator, the Dark Reaper crisis, the First Battle of Kamino, the battle of Ohma-D'un, the battle of Pengalan IV, Jedi: Mace Windu, and the events of Star Wars: Clone Wars up until the Battle of Hypori to 22 BBY, in that order. |series=Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures graphic novels |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Rogue's Gallery" was a short comic story in Star Wars: Clone Wars Adventures Volume 3. Asajj Ventress and Durge are searching a large spacecraft on which Darth Tyranus has told them he senses some presence. They enter a hall filled with many trophies of battle, and approach one another from opposite directions, having split-up and secured any exits from the hall. Durge is attacked by a deadly combination of baleful life and advanced technology he later learns is named Grievous. Ventress joins the battle, but she too is eventually bested by this new monster. Returning to his mentor, Tyranus, with the pair of battered bodies, he is told he has passed his test and is ordered to put Durge and Ventress in bacta tanks to allow them to heal. Dooku then promotes Grievous to the Supreme Commander of the Confederate Army.
