Imperial Headquarters
1587

Imperial Headquarters was the seat of power for the Imperial Remnant. Located on the planet Bastion in the capital city Ravelin, it was a towering black monolith, with a crystalline starburst at the pinnacle. This starburst could be used as a docking bay for important visitors, and also contained Grand Admiral Gilad Pellaeon's personal arboretum.
