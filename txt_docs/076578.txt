Echani vibrosword
1299

thumb|right|200px|[[Brianna brandishing an Echani vibrosword.]] The Echani vibrosword was a type of long vibroblade that was manufactured by the Echani. Made with a cortosis weave, the blade of this melee weapon was resistant to lightsaber strikes, which enabled them to be used against Force-users. In addition to this, they were sometimes supercooled during production in order to maintain alignment.
