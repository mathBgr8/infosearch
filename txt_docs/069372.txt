Raglath Nur
1489

Raglath Nur was a member of the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. He was assigned to Denua Ku's war party responsible for hunting down any Jeedai who may have survived the Battle of Coruscant. What they found instead, however, was Irek Ismaren. He was killed in the attack on Ismaren. *The New Jedi Order: Enemy Lines II: Rebel Stand *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Yuuzhan Vong warriors
