Form-chair
2124

Form-chair was the generic term used to describe any chair which molded itself to the user's body contours. Klo Merit had a form-chair in his office at RMSU-7 during the Clone Wars. The first Death Star was equipped with form-chairs; and Xizor also had one, which featured cloned leather and a voxchip that was not able to pronounce his name. In 44 ABY after the Lost Tribe of Sith emergence crisis, the Millennium Falcon was fitted with form-chairs as a suggestion from Lando Calrissian, after the previous ones were destroyed during the Assault on the New Jedi Temple by a thermal detonator thrown by Sith apprentice Vestara Khai.Fate of the Jedi: Apocalypse *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *Coruscant Nights III: Patterns of Force *The Last Jedi *Death Star *Rebel Dawn *Star Wars: Shadows of the Empire novel *Dark Nest I: The Joiner King *Dark Nest II: The Unseen Queen *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *Legacy of the Force: Inferno *Legacy of the Force: Invincible *Fate of the Jedi: Omen *Fate of the Jedi: Abyss *Fate of the Jedi: Apocalypse Category:Chairs
