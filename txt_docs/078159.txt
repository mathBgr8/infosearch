Michele Carter
919

Michele Carter is a roleplaying game writer and editor. Carter wrote Star Wars Roleplaying Game articles for the unlicensed Polyhedron magazine issues 93, 94, 97, 99, 103 and 104, and edited the Wizards of the Coast's Hero's Guide, Power of the Jedi Sourcebook and The Dark Side Sourcebook. *Polyhedron magazine **93 **94 **97 **99 **103 **104 *Hero's Guide *Power of the Jedi Sourcebook *The Dark Side Sourcebook Category:Editors Category:Role-playing game authors
