Tusk-cat
1930

Tusk-cats, also spelled as Tusk Cats, were non-sentient carnivorous felines found on the grasslands of the planet Naboo. They used the grass as cover when hunting herbivores. They had a long muscular tail and pair of tusks growing from their lower jaws.
