Red Fang was armed with two laser cannons. The crew was simply Zian himself, although the ship allowed two more passengers. Zian used this ship to take some Kallistan gems offered by Prefect Dengless Rinn in exchange for transporting weapons for Rinn's coup against Governor Linrec. During the same time, Zian also used the ship to take bounty hunter Demos Traxen to Kallistas, where he hoped to capture Pallas Quintell; unfortunately, Zian spent a time "avoiding Imperial entanglements" during the trip and, once they arrived, Quintell had been already captured by other bounty hunters.
