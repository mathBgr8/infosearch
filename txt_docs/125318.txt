Siege of Kyrska
1797

The Siege of Kyrska was an intense three-month-long siege on the planet Kyrska during the Galactic Civil War. The fighting was dragged out due to the high number of house-to-house campaigns. During the battle, a Rebel Urban Combat Specialist pointed out the "Law of Fortifications": "It is always as hard to get out as it is to get in". *Rules of Engagement: The Rebel SpecForce Handbook Category:Battles in the Hevvrol sector Category:Battles of the Galactic Civil War
