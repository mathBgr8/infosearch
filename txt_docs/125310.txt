Ringed moon shadowmoth
1904

The ringed moon shadowmoths, usually called simply shadowmoths, were a species of insects native to Coruscant. They were silicon-based lifeforms, and had tough cocoons. When they first broke from their cocoons, each young shadowmoth's struggles 'activated' their wings, upon which were wingflutes that created songs while flying.The New Jedi Order: Traitor They were a common element in the diet of hawk-bats, who fed upon their larvae.
