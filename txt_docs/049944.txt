Governor of Galidraan
2743

The Governor of Galidraan was a Human male who was appointed to the position by the Galactic Republic in 44 BBY. thumb|left|150px|The governor running That year, he hired the True Mandalorians to deal with political protesters to his rule; at the same time, under the advice of the Death Watch, he told the Jedi that the Mandalorians were butchering civilians. A Jedi task force under Jedi Master Dooku eradicated the True Mandalorians; the Governor took the armor of the survivor, Jango Fett, and sold him into slavery.
