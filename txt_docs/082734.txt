Chuck Bratz
1086

Chuck Bratz was a young boy and the older brother of Donni Bratz. On the ship Reasonable Doubt, they were in the Kidz Arkade. Chuck was playing Wookiee Warpath for his fourth consecutive game, shutting his brother up as he pleaded for a try at the game. He was described as a god when he played the game, and he earned his younger brother's respect from that.
