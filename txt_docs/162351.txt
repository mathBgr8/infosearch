thumb|right|250px|Maarek Stele inspecting Yander 1. Yander group was a group of three YT-1300 light freighters operating near the Hoth system in 3 ABY. Following the Battle of Hoth, the three freighters were inspected by personnel stationed at Outpost D-34, namely Maarek Stele, and found to be carrying foodstuffs and water.
