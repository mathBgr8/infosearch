Sistra Mountain
1064

Sistra was one of the many purple granite mountains on the moon Yavin 8 and was home to a city of Melodies. This mountain was dominated by pools, caves and caverns. These bodies of water were home to the adult Melodies known as Elders, while the young of Melodie society&mdash;who had not yet changed&mdash;resided around these pools in the caves and caverns of the mountain. *Junior Jedi Knights: Lyric's World *The Essential Guide to Planets and Moons Category:Cities Category:Mountains
