Lina Mardec
1308

Lina Mardec was a Human female. She lived in Jalor City with her husband Riic, a cab driver, and two daughters, Sale and Fora. Unfortunately, on 2 ABY, the Mardecs&mdash;except for Riic&mdash;died in a crossfire when the Galactic Empire attacked a Rebel cell.
