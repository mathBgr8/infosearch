An agriworld of lush, green, rolling hills, it was the source of Vaschean rye, and was possibly the homeworld of Sam Heggs. Vasch was a planetary body in the Vasch system of the Outer Rim Territories' Arkanis sector, and was one of that sector's Regency Worlds. It was situated in grid square R-17, and was located along the Vaschean Way hyperspace route, as well as at one end of the Najiba Loop route. It had at least one moon casting moonlight on Vaschean nights. A lush world, Vasch had a temperate climate, and was known as the sector's garden spot with a terrain of green hills. Vasch was settled at the same time as Arkanis, another of the Regency Worlds. During the Galactic War, the government of Vasch sold a large quantity of cheap "rubbish" that was confiscated from criminals. Its hills were dotted with estates owned by wealthy retirees, and had graceful cities visited by tourists from across the sector. While in Mos Eisley on Tatooine, Sam Heggs lamented being on the desert world rather than on Vasch's green hills.
