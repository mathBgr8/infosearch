}} The Tanwa Clan was a Rodian clan on Rodia, directly descended from the Tan Clan. In the aftermath of an ancient civil war, the additional "wa" syllable was added. The most notable member of this clan was Suz Tanwa, treasure hunter and archaeologist. Category:Rodian clans
