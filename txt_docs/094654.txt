|pages= |timeline= |campaign=Living Force |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Destabilization" is an article written by Morrie Mullins as a tie in to the Living Force campaign. It was published on the Wizards of the Coast website on January 5, 2004 and took the form of a succession of HoloNet posts beginning with a sliced order from Jir Tramsig ordering a social and economic destabilization program in the Cularin system. Later posters appeared to include OPS officers, aides to Senator Lavina Wren, and the crimelord Nirama.
