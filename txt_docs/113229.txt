|type=Blaster carbine |culture= |creators= |created=Bobor Kalranoos |destroyed= |discovered= |owners=Sordaan Xris |locations= |cost= |value= |shape= |size= |material= |length= |width= |diam= |height= |weight= |protection= |crew= |capacity= |range= |inscription= |markings= |purpose= |heritage= |affiliation= }} The Kalranoos carbine was a sonic carbine weapon developed by weapons engineer Bobor Kalranoos during the Imperial Era. It was a hybrid design that stemmed from the modifications of a Geonosian carbine. It was equipped with a bio-link module that permitted its user to be directly interfaced with the weapon. One particular Kalranoos carbine was found in Sordaan Xris's collection of exotic weapons. Sordaan claimed to have acquired the weapon in a wager with a Leresai bounty hunter. Another such weapon was found by a spacer on Talus during the Galactic Civil War. The Kalranoos carbine appeared in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011.
