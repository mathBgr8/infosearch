XR808g
2610

XR808g, or the Exxer, was the smuggling vessel owned and operated by Jae Juun, a smuggler, and his Ewok copilot, Tarfang. It was a Corellian Engineering Corporation YT-1000. Its saucer-shaped hull had the cockpit directly on top with four blaster cannons arranged around it. When Juun accompanied Leia Organa and Han Solo to the Killik nest at Qoribu, the ship was destroyed by Gorog dartships while flying via slave circuit, allowing the Millennium Falcon and all present to survive the Gorog attack.
