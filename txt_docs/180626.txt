Revanchist (disambiguation)
507

The term Revanchist could refer to two things: *Revan, a Jedi Knight during the Mandalorian Wars who became Darth Revan, but was redeemed by the conclusion of the Jedi Civil War. *Revanchists, a group of Jedi referred to as the Revanchists who supported facing the Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders against the wishes of the Jedi High Council.
