Eradicator (Imperial-class)
2009

The Eradicator was an Imperial-class Star Destroyer that served in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. In 0 ABY, it was in the process of impounding a starship near the world of Mon Torri when the Millennium Falcon came out of hyperspace.
