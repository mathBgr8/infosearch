Destreg II was a small planet orbited by Destreg Spaceport, a small but expensive-looking Golan Construction Corporation space station. The planet apparently had quite significant amounts of civilian traffic. K'Armyn Viraxo set a trap for Emon and Ace Azzameen here, luring them with the premise of him supposedly being on his yacht, the Highroller. However, the two outlaws managed to both destroy his yacht&mdash;his lure&mdash;and evade Imperial patrols and a group of mercenaries.
