Team Slashrat
1470

Team Slashrat was one of the five Jedi strike-teams sent to Corellia as part of the Galactic Alliance mission code-named Operation Roundabout. Consisting of Corran Horn and a second, unspecified Jedi, their mission was to monitor starfighter activity at Coronet's primary spaceport. Their mission was largely futile, however, as Corellia had already deployed almost every starfighter that could fly to space to ambush the Second Fleet.
