Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 1
2281

Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 1 is the premiere issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in October 1994. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 1, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with the Tales of the Jedi: Dark Lords of the Sith Special Ashcan Edition comic book, and 1995 Topps Star Wars Widevision and Star Wars Topps Finest trading cards.
