Albino Cyclops
906

The Albino Cyclops was a deity of the Skakoan religion which lived in the Gates of Grontessiant. Its Eye was a sacred artifact, and it was guarded by the Lord Being of the Swirblies and four See Seers. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Deities Category:Skakoan religion
