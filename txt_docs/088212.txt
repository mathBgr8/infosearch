Alpha Kentrum
561

Alpha Kentrum was a starship assigned to the Jedi geologist Michael Tandre. After he crash-landed on Orellon II and passed on the ways of the Jedi to the local Kentra, it became the center of a holy temple built in his honor. Category:Starships of the Jedi Order
