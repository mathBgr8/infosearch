Tol-Kachorn
1161

Tol-Kachorn was the capital city of Yinchorr. During the Galactic Civil War, the Galactic Empire discovered that Princess Leia Organa of the Alliance to Restore the Republic was on Yinchorr. The Governor Marcellin Wessel held the Yinchorri Council of Elders hostage, in exchange for the council's release, the Yinchorri attempted to deliver Leia Organa captive to the Empire. In the end, the Empire slaughtered the council, and the Yinchorri rebelled.
