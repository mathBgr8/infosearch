B-1D4, otherwise known as Beewondeefor, or her codename, The Heart of Steel, was an Imperial droid purchased by Leia Organa in 0 ABY. Unbeknownst to them at first, Wondeefor was actually an extremely powerful Imperial weapon devised by Grand Moff 4-8C.
