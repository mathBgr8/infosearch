Finder's Fee
4117

"Finder's Fee" was a short story written by Peter Schweighofer, originally published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 6 by West End Games in May 1995 and later archived online at Hyperspace. Loh'khar arranges transit for some Rebels on the run from his base of operations within Lorana's Labyrinth in exchange for a copy of their freshly acquired Imperial intelligence.
