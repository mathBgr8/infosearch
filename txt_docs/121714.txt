Shola Ruins
1519

The Shola Ruins, also known as the Shola Catacombs, were mysterious ruins and deep crevasses which were mostly unexplorable due to the volatile atmosphere, groundquakes, and heavy magma flows native to Shola. Research from deep scans indicated that these caverns included mining tunnel systems which ran all the way down to the core, which would indicate that the one-time inhabitants of the volcanic world had dug all the way to the core and caused the massive catalysm that left the planet in its current state of tectonic and climatic unrest.
