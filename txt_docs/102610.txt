Slerog Fenn
1457

Slerog Fenn was a male Rodian. Fenn presented himself in flashy clothes, and often adorned himself with earrings. Fenn was a major rival to Loro Ecls in the Gelgelar Free Port, running landing and repair facilities, known respectively as Fenn's Landing and Fenn's Warehouse. He tried to undercut his rival in a bid to take financial control of the port.
