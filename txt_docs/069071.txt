Undercity Access Pod
783

The Undercity Access Pod was a vehicle used to traverse the Undercity of the planet Peekoine when "Sliders" were not acceptable. They had the same general statistics as a standard Cloud Car, but were equipped with only repair lasers instead of the Cloud Car's more powerful weaponry. Some of these were used by a group of Rebels when they were chasing Gamusha and his swoop gang the Gutterwipes in order to rescue Plum Tantrellius whom they had kidnapped. Category:Air vehicle models
