Pada
717

The Pada were a species of round-faced, mammalian bipeds. They had yellow fur, short horns, and large claws that made them excellent at climbing. They also had superb night vision. The Pada were extremely peaceful and honorable and were not allowed to kill another being, even in self-defense. *Jedi's Honor Category:Mammalian sentient species
