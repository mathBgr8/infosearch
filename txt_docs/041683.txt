Daro Willits was a scruffy Human who visited the Outlander Club, a nightclub and gambling hall in the Uscru Entertainment District of the planetStar Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary Coruscant. The socialite Sel Maa spent a great deal of time with him when she visited during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. During a night in 22 BBY,The New Essential Chronology dates the events of to 22 BBY. Willits came to the club hoping to make an impression. He flirted with Maa in a private booth of the club when the Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker entered looking for the bounty hunter Zam Wesell after a failed assassination attempt on Senator Padmé Amidala. The Human Reha Logg joined them, and the trio continued talking when Skywalker started walking through the club. When Wesell was disarmed by Kenobi, Willits walked near the bar with a drink. Willits had brown skin and brown hair styled in meticulously scruffy dreadlocks. During his visit to the Outlander in 22 BBY, he wore a necklace, a black top, a silver harness, a gray jacket with black trimming, gray pants, and black shoes. Daro Willits first appeared in the 2002 prequel trilogy film Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. The Outlander Club scenes including Willits, who was portrayed by an uncredited extra, were shot at Fox Studios, Sydney on July 17, 2000.Mythmaking: Behind the Scenes of Attack of the Clones Prior to the film's release, the character was first pictured in the documentary "A Twinkle Beyond Pluto," released on July 31, 2001. Willits was first identified through the StarWars.com Image Attack feature on May 12, 2003. In the article "Who's Who in the Outlander Club: It's Ladies' Night," published in the seventy-fifth issue of Star Wars Insider on April 6, 2004, author Daniel Wallace mentioned Willits and asked why he resembles an unsigned musician.
