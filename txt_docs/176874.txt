Alaikai Trotter
2008

Alaikai Trotter was a historian who resided on the Outer Rim planet Tatooine during the Galactic Civil War. Trotter had an intimate familiarity with the planet's history and that of its inhabitants. One of Trotter's most notable works was the study of the life and legacy of the famed Tatooinian Jedi Master Sidrona Diath. on Tatooine In 1 ABY, Alaikai Trotter recruited a spacer to fight dangerous creatures that infested the desert of Tatooine and protect the inhabitants of the planet like Sidrona Diath did thousand years before. Alaikai Trotter was first mentioned in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies, a massively multiplayer online roleplaying game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. Alaikai Trotter was mentioned in the mission "Diath's Shadow" that is available to players at mission terminals on Tatooine. Trotter hired the player to follow Diath's example and "free Tatooine from the monsters that infest the desert". The player had to destroy a random creature lair and several creatures to accomplish the mission.
