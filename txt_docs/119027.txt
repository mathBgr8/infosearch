Freebooter-class transport
1820

The Freebooter-class transport was a military transport used by the Galactic Alliance during the Second Galactic Civil War. It was manufactured by Sienar Fleet Systems and was awkward-looking when not in space. 200 meters long, it looked like a crescent moon bisected by a knife blade, orientated in the direction of the crescent tips, and it had a wide curved stern.
