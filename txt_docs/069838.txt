Shae Polikex
1576

Doctor Shae Polikex was a Human female xenobotanist, who was stationed in the Rebel base of Yavin 4. Polikex helped Biggs Darklighter and Nera Dantels find the strange organism that had developed natural defenses against the cause of Derek Klivian's infection, in order to cure Hobbie's disease.
