Wrack
2017

Wrack was an interdictor cruiser that served in the Navy of the Imperial Remnant during the Yuuzhan Vong War. Commanded by Captain Mynar Devis, it was equipped with at least three TIE/D Defender starfighters. In 28 ABY, three of Wrack TIE Defenders participated in the second Battle of Bilbringi.
