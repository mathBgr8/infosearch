Spine of Lok
1618

thumb|right|250px|Obsidian asteroids at the Spine of Lok. The Spine of Lok was a giant asteroid belt in orbit around the planet Lok in the Karthakk system. It was made almost entirely out of obsidian rock, rendering it nearly invisible when viewed against the black backdrop of space.
