Unidentified Knight of the Sith
2111

A Knight of the Sith wore a face mask and black robes and carried a red-bladed lightsaber. Flying a Banta Four starfighter in the Kessil system, he discovered and attacked Kane Starkiller and his sons Annikin Starkiller and Deak Starkiller on the Fourth Moon of Utapau. The Sith was able to surprise the Jedi and swiftly killed Deak. However, his melee skills were no match for Kane, a Jedi Master, who swiftly struck the knight down. The character became the basis for numerous others in later drafts and the films. His appearance seems to be the origin of Darth Vader, though Vader appears as a distinct character in the draft. His ambush of Deak closely resembles the Tusken Raider who got a jump on Luke Skywalker in A New Hope.
