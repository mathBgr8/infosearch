Rixes Antab
1724

Rixes Antab was a male Human thief who worked on the planet Tatooine during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. In 32 BBY, he once stole a shipment of chubas intended for Jabba the Hutt. By doing this, the Hutt posted a bounty on his head. The bounty hunter Jango Fett later captured him alive, when he was on Tatooine attempting to find Gardulla the Hutt involving the connection between Gardulla and the Bando Gora.
