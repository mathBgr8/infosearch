An Inrokini family banner was adorned by a crest. Sometime before the Order of Zildrog campaign Syndic Zenta adopted a force sensitive Valss to the Inrokini family. The two later allied themselves with Theron Shan when he came to Copero seeking a Holomap to the superweapon of the Sith Emperor Vitiate. For that they were considered traitors by Aristocra Saganu. In 28 ABY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic II: Refugee to 28 ABY. a Jedi delegation led by Jedi Master Luke Skywalker visited the planet Csilla in their search for the sentient planet Zonama Sekot.
