TC-9D0
2048

TC-9D0 was a brown-hued TC-series protocol droid wanted by the special branch of the Senatorial Guard for abandoning his post while in possession of "sensitive information" concerning the corrupt dealings of Twi'lek Senator Connus Trell. In 32 BBY, the bounty hunter Jango Fett captured TC-9D0 and claimed his bounty on the streets of Merchant Row while hunting Meeko Ghintee. In the video game Star Wars: Bounty Hunter, TC-9D0 is the first of ten possible secondary bounties which the player can secure in the Merchant Row section during Chapter One. Players have the option of capturing TC-9D0 or destroying him. If the droid is recovered in good condition, his bounty is valued at 2,500 credits. If he were destroyed, however, then the job would only earn the player 1,250 credits.
