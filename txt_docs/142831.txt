}} Illia Setag was the Chairman of Cybot Galactica in 22 BBY, at a time when Galactica was moving from the Universal Socket Interface to their own proprietary Advanced Appendage Bus. "Illia" is William with the first and last letters removed, and Setag is "Gates" spelled backwards, an oblique reference to , chairman and former CEO of Microsoft.
