Dbarian
1165

Dbarians were a worrisome race from the Dbari system. They spent much time weighing the possible outcomes of their actions. Dbarians were characterized by the large amount of flexible, unsegmented tentacles and warts on their body. Their bodies flashed maroon when confused and blue with surprise. These different colors were produced by chromophores, which could also flash pink.
