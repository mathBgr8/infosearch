Zuxu
1845

The zuxu was a creature native to the planet Ganlihk, along with the ludos and the ryless. The zuxu was able to live on land and in the water. It had a special gland that excreted a protective oil over its skin which kept it moist for 3 hours and allowed it to survive on dry land. Their front flippers were padded and could be used to walk on dry ground. Their jaws will filled with sharp teeth. Zuxu were solitary in nature, but would occasionally band together to hunt.
