Tine Felix
1176

Tine Felix was the daughter of the free-trader Vazan Felix and his wife Cyra Felix. She was captured along with her mother by Zhen Mirat, leader of The Fatal Visionaries, as ransom to get both Tine's Future and to ensure that her father would continue to fly the ship under Mirat's command. She was held prisoner by Mirat for at least six months, he had had no intention to give either her or her mother back to Vazan once he had agreed to help, until a group of rebels helped free her and her mother from Mirat's clutches. The family were then re-united. Category:Females Category:Humans
