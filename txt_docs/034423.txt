Rubyflame Lake
1312

thumb|250px|[[Tyler Lucian standing over Rubyflame Lake.]] Rubyflame Lake was a lake located on the planet Centares. Located near Muracie, Rubyflame Lake's waters possessed a red glow as a result of running above an active lava flow. When the Galactic Empire tapped the planet's accessible lava flows to produce geothermal power, the lake suffered.
