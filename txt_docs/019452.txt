*Greedo the Elder |partners= |siblings=Greedo |children= |affiliation=Tetsu Clan |masters= |apprentices= }} Pqweeduk was a Rodian. He was the son of Greedo the Elder and Neela and the younger brother of Greedo. After her husband was murdered by Navik the Red during the clan purges, Neela fled Rodia with her then-two-year-old son, along with other members of her family and Tetsu clan. In 4 BBY, Pqweeduk and Greedo discovered three old Rodian starships in the caves of their homeworld, U-Tendik. Later that night, Navik and Chattza Clan members found the refugees and slaughtered them. Neela was able to escape with Pqweeduk and Greedo.
