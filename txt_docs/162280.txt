Hap lived on Tenoo, where he owned a café called Hap's Sap Tap. He was friends with young pilot Nash Durango. In 232 BBY, his café was repeatedly raided by a gang of pirates. During one of these raids, Hap, who had heard that Durango was transporting Jedi passengers to Tenoo, contected her and asked for help. To his disappointment, the Jedi were only younglings, but they still agreed to help him. The three younglings, Kai Brightstar, Lys Solay and Nubs, confronted the pirates, but their captain, Taborr Val Dorn, caused a giant tree branch to fall on Hap's café.
