|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters=Death Watch bunker, Endor |locations= |formed=Death Watch soldiers |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved=1.5 ABY |restored= |affiliation=Death Watch }} The Ghosts were a unit of elite soldiers within the radical Mandalorian splinter group known as the Death Watch. Clad in Mandalorian armor colored in black, a color traditionally associated with the concept of justice in the Mandalorian culture, the Ghosts were based out of the Death Watch's hidden bunker on the forest moon of Endor. One member of the Ghosts, Rageon Vart, was tasked with overseeing the entrance to Section A of the Death Watch base. Around the year 1 ABY, several Death Watch Ghosts engaged a number of spacers who attempted to intrude into the base. Vart was killed, and his pass key taken by the spacers to gain access to the areas formerly under his supervision.
