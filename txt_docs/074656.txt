Kettemoor
1497

Kettemoor was a Sacheen-class light escort of the New Republic Defense Fleet. During the hunt for the Teljkon Vagabond, Kettemoor was assigned to Colonel Ejagga Pakkpekatt's task force at Gmar Askilon.
