Task Force Token
1282

}} Task Force Token was one of five task forces of the New Republic Defense Fleet's New Republic Second Fleet. During the Black Fleet Crisis, Token was placed under the temporary command of the Fifth Battle Group, paired with Task Force Bellbright and led by Admiral Tolokus.
