Reeza
1738

Reeza was a Human male Mandalorian who fought in the Mandalorian Wars. Following the end of the war, he joined Sherruk's group of bandits. They eventually came to Dantooine, where they terrorized and extorted the local populace. In 3956 BBY, Reeza and Jarg, another of Sherruk's bandits, were killed by Juhani in her rage while the rest of the Mandalorians on Dantooine were killed by Revan. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Criminals Category:Humans Category:Males Category:Mandalorian Neo-Crusaders
