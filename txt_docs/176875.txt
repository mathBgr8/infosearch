on Tatooine}} Madam Prisaal was a female individual from the planet Ichtor 8 who lived during the Galactic Civil War. When her husband was killed by pirates, she vowed to take revenge. After pursuing the pirates through twelve sectors and nearly killing them three separate times, she finally tracked them down on Tatooine. After she found their hideout, in 1 ABY, she hired freelance soldiers to eliminate the murderers. Prisaal was mentioned in the 2003 video game Star Wars Galaxies: An Empire Divided, a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by Sony Online Entertainment and published by LucasArts, prior to its closure on December 15, 2011. "Madam Prisaal of Ichtor 8" was mentioned in the mission "Avenge My Murdered Husband" that was available at mission terminals on Tatooine. Prisaal hired the player to kill her husband's murderers.
