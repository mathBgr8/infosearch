Lia Reicheds
1572

}} Lia Reicheds was a clerk who worked in the Senate Building during the time of the Separatist Crisis. In 22 BBY, before the Clone Wars began, Reicheds witnessed a violent protest against the Military Creation Act outside the Senate which forced the clerk to come through the angry crowds to get into work.
