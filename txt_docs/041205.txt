Crusader (Victory-class)
3752

}} Crusader was a Victory-class Star Destroyer in the Imperial Navy during the Galactic Civil War. Commanded by Captain Zlece Oonaar, this ship, along with her sister ship Bombard, destroyed the Eyttyrmin Batiiv pirate armada at the Battle of Khuiumin. Crusader supported Bombard from a distance during the battle, remaining in position to intercept the fleeing pirates.
