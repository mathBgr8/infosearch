The Raptor was painted with distinctive red bird of prey designs, while its companion Skyclaw had a similar blue scheme. Mazzic often used the unusual but large and intimidating starfighters to frighten his enemies, and to provide protection for his smuggling vessels in dangerous operations. The picture on the back of Specter of the Past has two preybirds.
