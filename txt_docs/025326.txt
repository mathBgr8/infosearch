Crude
659

Crude was an especially potent and illegal drug that came in small pill or capsule form. On Coruscant, the Works were known for their crude peddlers. The drug was a hard psychotropic that created the sense that the user's inhibitions were no longer as large as their insecurities, triggering a selfish pursuit of basely impulses. *Labyrinth of Evil *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Drugs and medicine
