HM-3
1211

HM-3 was a Legal analyst droid active in the halls of the Galactic Alliance Senate during the Second Galactic Civil War. HM-3 helped Jacen Solo change a law about changing laws in order to get more supplies to the GAG, but the law was later used to remove Cal Omas from office in 40 ABY.
