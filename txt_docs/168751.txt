Tarken
1021

Tarken was a Nebulon-B2 frigate belonging to the Rebel Alliance. The Tarken attempted rescue the Rebel MC80 Star Cruiser Knights which was captured during a meeting with Bothans in a Dreadnaught-class heavy cruiser called Doomer.
