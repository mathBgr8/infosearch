Universal jammer
547

The universal jammer was a countermeasure to most types of sensors. It could foil most common types of detectors, but was vulnerable to certain specialized scanners. They were highly expensive. Boba Fett used a suitcase with a universal jammer in it to conceal the armor and weapons he had inside the suitcase. *Rebel Dawn *The New Essential Guide to Weapons and Technology Category:Sensor jammers
