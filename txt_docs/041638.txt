Radark
985

Native to the homeworld of the Bothans and found on many of their colonies, including Mandell and Kothlis, the Radarks were subterrenean serpents that burrowed and tunnel underneath many Bothan structures. When these underground pathways and holes became plentiful, foundations of great institutions usually collapsed.
