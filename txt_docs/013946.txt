Selaggis VI was a gas giant located within the Selaggis system of the Quelii sector, a part of the New Territories portion of the Outer Rim Territories. It lay on the hyperlane known as the Braxant Run, which connected it to the Bandomeer and Vahaba systems. Selaggis VI was one of the seven worlds that orbited the yellow star Selaggis. The planet was orbited by several moons, including Selcaron, and was also circled by a heavy debris ring thought to have been another moon at one time. Selaggis VI was a beautiful yellow-orange gas giant with an atmosphere characterized by constant storm activity. The storms unceasingly changed the planet's patterns of swirls and lines of color, so that each new day offered variations in the worldscape, making it an ever-changing work of art for the colonists on Selcaron. Selaggis VI was the site of the Battle of Selaggis between Warlord Zsinj and General Han Solo in 7 ABY. thumb|right|250px|Selaggis in Star Wars: Rebellion Selaggis was introduced in the 1994 novel The Courtship of Princess Leia, written by Dave Wolverton, which mentioned it as the location of a colony that was obliterated by Warlord Zsinj's Super Star Destroyer Iron Fist. Selaggis then appeared in the 1998 PC video game Star Wars: Rebellion, which described it as a planet with a "favorable climate that has attracted colonists."Star Wars: Rebellion The 1999 novel X-Wing: Solo Command, written by Aaron Allston, later identified the planet as the gas giant Selaggis Six, and changed the location of the colony to one of its moons, Selcaron.
