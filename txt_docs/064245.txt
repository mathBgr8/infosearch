David Jacob Beckett
427

David Jacob Beckett was an inker for several issues of Dark Horse Comics, including Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi — The Golden Age of the Sith, Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi — The Fall of the Sith Empire, Star Wars: Tales of the Jedi — The Sith War, and Jedi Academy: Leviathan.
