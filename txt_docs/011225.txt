Kevin Michael Richardson
6340

Kevin Michael Richardson is an American voice actor who provided the voices of several Star Wars characters, such as Jolee Bindo in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, K'Kruhk in Star Wars: Clone Wars, and Jabba the Hutt in the film Star Wars: The Clone Wars. He also voiced Mace Windu, a character portrayed in the prequel trilogy by Samuel L. Jackson, in video games. He worked with Lucasfilm Animation again on the film Strange Magic, and for LucasArts on the games Indiana Jones and the Emperor's Tomb and RTX Red Rock. He followed in Mark Hamill's footsteps as the voice of The Joker on the WB series The Batman opposite Rino Romano.
