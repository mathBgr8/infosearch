thumb|right|Oliver as Cabbel in The Empire Strikes Back Oliver Maguire (died January 10, 2012) played Cabbel in The Empire Strikes Back. Outside Star Wars, Maguire has appeared in the long running science fiction series Doctor Who where he played a Shrieve in the 1978 story The Ribos Operation starring Tom Baker.
