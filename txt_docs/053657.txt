Cularin Central Broadcasting
1670

Cularin Central Broadcasting was a media corporation located on Cularin, providing news and entertainment to its viewers. Many inhabitants of Cularin felt that the company was biased toward the Thaereian military. One of their reporters was Yara Grugara.
