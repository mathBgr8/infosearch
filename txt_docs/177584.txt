Welt der Spiele
1421

Welt der Spiele GmbH was a German publisher of roleplaying games and board games based in Frankfurt. The company was responsible for printing German translations of several West End Games roleplaying books. *Star Wars: Das Rollenspiel (Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game) *Star Wars: Spielleiterset (Star Wars Gamemaster Screen) *Star Wars: Das Handbuch (The Star Wars Sourcebook) *Handbuch der Rebellenallianz (The Rebel Alliance Sourcebook) *Die Erben des Imperiums Quellenbuch (Heir to the Empire Sourcebook) *Die Dunkle Seite der Macht Quellenbuch (Dark Force Rising Sourcebook) *Das letzte Kommando Quellenbuch (The Last Command Sourcebook) *Das Vermächtnis von Alderaan (Graveyard of Alderaan) *Operation Starfall (Starfall) *Der Todesstern - Technisches Handbuch (Death Star Technical Companion) *Kopfgeldjäger (Galaxy Guide 10: Bounty Hunters) *Angriff auf Hoth (Assault on Hoth) Category:Board game publishing companies
