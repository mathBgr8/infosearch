Kimm systems
2662

The Kimm systems referred to a trio of star systems located in the Mid Rim, each with a shabby industrial planet of the same name: Kimm Aurek, Kimm Besh, and Kimm Cresh. These three worlds belonged to House Sreethyn of the Juvex sector.
