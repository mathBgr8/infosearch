Christopher Cerasi
2013

Christopher Cerasi was an editor for LucasBooks. He edited issues 1&ndash;80 of The Official Star Wars Fact File as well as the entire line of Star Wars comics for Dark Horse Comics, among other titles. He left the employ of Lucas Licensing to return to his home state of New York. In 2003, Cerasi began work as an editor in the Licensed Publishing Division of DC Comics and remained there until October 2010. Cerasi is an unabashed Carrie Fisher fan, and was even featured in the introductory Real Life Tales Of Dave Land story for an edition of Star Wars Tales (wearing Leia's gold metal slave outfit) which featured stories uniquely focusing on Princess Leia and Luke Skywalker. He is also the author of the Star Wars Fandex Deluxe Edition, and wrote the Star Wars: Episode III — Revenge of the Sith (comic series)comic adaptation of Episode III under the pseudonym Miles Lane, as well as the Star Wars Tales story "First Impressions" (as Nathan Walker) about Princess Leia's first visit to Coruscant/meeting with Emperor Palpatine.
