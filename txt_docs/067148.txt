Morgot was a ready source of information for Vos and was a notoriously bad gambler who often got into trouble covering his debts. He was being beaten up by Nastyboy, to whom he owed money, when Aayla Secura and Vos came looking for Morgot. Upon witnessing the scene taking place before them, Aayla proceeded to use a mind trick to persuade the thug to go home.
