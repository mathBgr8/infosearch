Star Wars Insider 34 is the thirty-fourth issue of Star Wars Insider magazine. It was published in Summer 1997. Issue 34 marked the conclusion of Star Wars Insider original quarterly publication schedule; with issue 35, the magazine began publishing bi-monthly.
