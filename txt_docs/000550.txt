|intl release= |pages= |issue= |featured= |series=Star Wars Encyclopedia }} "Emperor Palpatine" is a volume in the Star Wars Encyclopedia series of reference books published by De Agostini. It was published on May 25, 2021. The man born Sheev Palpatine would eventually become one of the most infamous figures in galactic history. Transforming himself into the Sith Lord Darth Sidious, he orchestrated a devious scheme that earned him the position of Supreme Chancellor of the Republic, plunged the galaxy into a conflict that claimed millions of lives, and ultimately allowed him to proclaim himself Emperor.
