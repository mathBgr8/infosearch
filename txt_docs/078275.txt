Tarul wine
1174

Tarul wine was a prestige alcoholic beverage from the planet Naboo. It was a favorite of the Hutt crime lord Suudaa Nem'ro during the Cold War. The Neimoidian merchant, Lik Ankkit of Qiilura, owned a case of this vintage wine, which was destroyed along with his villa during the raid by Omega Squad, a clone commando squad of the Galactic Republic. *Star Wars: The Old Republic *Darth Bane: Rule of Two *Republic Commando: Hard Contact Category:Naboo food Category:Wines
