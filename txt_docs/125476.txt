right|thumb|Rebel Command Fleet at the [[Battle of Endor/Legends|Battle of Endor.]] The Rebel Command Fleet was the main force of the Alliance Fleet and fought at the Battle of Endor. Prior to the battle, it gathered at Sullust with the Sullustan Home Guard.
