Cyclops
1554

The Cyclops, often abbreviated to simply "'Clops", was a smuggling vessel that was active during the time of the Galactic Civil War. The ship was equipped with two shuttles, and was captained by Jerg, whose first mate was Marie Peeno. In 1 BBY, the Cyclops transported several hundred Rebel Alliance sympathizers from Sulon to start a new life in a new colony on Ruusan. *Dark Forces: Rebel Agent *Dark Forces: Rebel Agent audio drama Category:Smuggler starships
