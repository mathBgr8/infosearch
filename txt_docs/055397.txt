Drixo
3438

Drixo was a female Hutt gangster and a rival of Progga the Hutt. Drixo was a Hutt crime lord. In 67 BBY, some of Progga and Drixo's kin were at High Port Space Center, on the MuunDarth Plagueis colonyUltimate Alien Anthology world of Muunilinst, where visitors saw them amongst envoys from Gravlex Med, Kalee and Yagai. That same year, Drixo and Progga made investments with the Muun Hego Damask of the InterGalactic Banking Clan on the planet Comra. Their deal enabled Damask to offer Gardulla the Hutt an investment in Tatooine, which enabled her to fund a podrace that could exceed the ones on Muunilinst and Malastare. Drixo became a rival to Progga during the years after the Battle of Naboo. In 27 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. she hired Dubrak Qennto and his crew to transport furs&mdash;and to smuggle firegems hidden within them&mdash;to Comra, but Qennto's light freighter Bargain Hunter was intercepted by Progga and, eventually, Force Commander Mitth'raw'nuruodo of the Chiss Expansionary Fleet, which caused a delay of the shipment for more than three weeks.
