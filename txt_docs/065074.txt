Pleth Strom
1576

Pleth Strom was a Human male Ensign pilot who served the Galactic Republic and the Praesitlyn Defense Force during the Clone Wars. In 22 BBY, he flew a T-19 starfighter alongside Erk H'Arman when the Confederacy of Independent Systems invaded Praesitlyn.
