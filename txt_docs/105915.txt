Tracker droid
2145

thumb|Kyle Katarn's tracker droid on 8t88's leg. A Tracker droid was a type of Seeker droid; it could be attached or placed on a moving target, and it would then transmit its current location. For practical purposes they were of tiny size and could move around. Some such tracker droids were animal-shaped, moved on their own, and transmitted some audio signal to their user (via a comm unit) according to their distance from the target; the frequency and volume of clicks would increase by proximity, and became unbroken once the user approached the target.Dark Forces: Rebel Agent Kyle Katarn owned a scorpion-shaped tracker droid by 5 ABY. He released when he met 8t88 in the Rimmer's Rest, which, unseen, walked fast and attached itself on the droid's leg. After their unfortunate meeting, Katarn escaped 88's trap, and wore a comm unit to listen its audio signal, aiding him to follow 88 to the correct direction. On his way to Launch Deck Three, 88's bodyguard Grentho noticed it and attempted to pry it off; while resisting at first, the droid released itself and attempted to escape, but Grentho caught it with his fat fingers and notified 88. The droid realised that Katarn was after them and ordered him to destroy it immediately. It squaled as Grentho crushed it, the moment when Katarn appeared. *Dark Forces: Rebel Agent *Dark Forces: Rebel Agent audio drama *
