Yor Millto
1096

Yor Millto was a Toong male who lived on the desert world of Tatooine. Like many others on this planet, he had a few slaves who had to work for him. When Space Pirate Krayn kidnapped his slave Hala and Amee, he considered the loss to be annoying but not tragic.
