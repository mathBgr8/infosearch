Rainbow Berry Pie
3244

Rainbow Berry Bie was an Ewok pie made from rainbow berries. The Ewoks of the Forest Moon of Endor made and consumed the pie for Bright Tree Village's Harvest Moon Feast. The Ewok Zephee baked the pie for one such feast. That year, the rainbow berries had been hard to come by, but Zephee was still able to make her pie. A plethora of the pies were served that night, and all of the Ewoks lined up happily to get some. thumb|right|76px|A rainbow berry pie in Tiny Death Star Rainbow Berry Bie first appeared in the 1984 young readers book The Baby Ewoks' Picnic Surprise, written by Melinda Luke. "Wasaka Berries and Monster Ships," an article written by Craig R. Carey and Vic Wertz and published in the Star Wars Insider magazine's seventy-fourth issue on February 24, 2004, misspells it as "rainbow- berry pie." The pie later made an appearance in the non-canon mobile game Star Wars: Tiny Death Star, which was developed by Disney Mobile in collaboration with LucasArts and NimbleBit and released in 2013.
