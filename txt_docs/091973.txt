Eriadu Spaceport
1006

Eriadu Spaceport was the spaceport serving Eriadu City on the planet of Eriadu. Many of the delegates to the Eriadu Trade Summit including Supreme Chancellor Finis Valorum&mdash;and his would-be assassins&mdash;landed at the spaceport prior to attending the summit.
