Dark Eye (EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate)
890

Dark Eye was a EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate in the service of the Galactic Empire. It attempted to reinforce the System Patrol Squadron above Farfeld II. However, it was attacked by another frigate, the Melisse, which had Y-wings and assistance from Rebel ace Keyan Farlander in a B-wing starfighter.
