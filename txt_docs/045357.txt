Koobis (First Jedi Purge)
1412

Koobis was the door guard of the Bumani Exchange Corporation, the legal face of the Exchange's presence on Citadel Station. When Meetra Surik received a mission from Luxa to speak with Loppak Slusk, he let the former Jedi enter. The Rodian then promptly deserted the area. The Jedi Exile, as the playable character in Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords, has the possibility to kill Koobis for not letting him/her into the Exchange headquarters.
