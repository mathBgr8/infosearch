Psadan (language)
1029

Psadan was the language of the Psadan people from Wayland. The other intelligent species from Wayland, the Myneyrsh, also used Psadan as a second language. Similarly, most Psadans were fluent in both Psadan and Myneyrsh languages. Psadan language consisted of grunts, groans and deep vocalizations.
