David Birney
991

David Birney (April 23, 1939 &ndash; April 27, 2022) voiced Anakin Skywalker in the final episode of the Return of the Jedi radio drama. He also played the Romulan Senator Letant in the Star Trek: Deep Space Nine episode "Tears of the Prophets".
