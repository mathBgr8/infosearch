Shelba 730 Razor
2941

The Shelba 730 Razor was a Podracer that saw use during 32 BBY. Bozzie Baranta bought one, but ended up putting more money into repairing it as he did actually buying it. It lacked a high top speed compared to other podracers, though this suited the Podrace course on Ord Ibanna, which Bozzie frequented.Star Wars: Episode I Racer: Prima's Official Strategy Guide Its also considered one of the most flashy, elegant and beautiful pod ships among the racers of the tournaments due to its royal-blue, silver and gold colors. The Shelba 730 Razor was originally created for use in the 1999 film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, but was ultimately cut before the film's animatic stages. DVD special features The Shelba was recycled for use in the tie-in video game Star Wars Episode I: Racer, which was released during the same year as the film. *Star Wars: Episode I Racer *Star Wars: Episode I Racer: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *Star Wars: Episode I Racer Official Nintendo Player's Guide Category:Podracer models
