Breil
489

Breil was a city located on the Bothan homeworld of Bothawui. This settlement was known for its size, and the word breil was often used as a Bothan family name. Tav Breil'lya, aide to Borsk Fey'lya, was a noted user of this prefix. *Galactic Campaign Guide Category:Cities on Bothawui
