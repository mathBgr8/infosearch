Mutiny on the Rand Ecliptic (short story)
3464

"Mutiny on the Rand Ecliptic" is a short story written by Michael Stern, originally published in the Galaxy Guide 3: The Empire Strikes Back and later reprinted in the sourcebook's second edition as well as The Movie Trilogy Sourcebook. It was subsequently archived on Hyperspace. Derek "Hobbie" Klivian's account of the mutiny on the Rand Ecliptic led by himself and Biggs Darklighter. He is telling the tale to Luke Skywalker onboard the Rebel medical frigate following the Battle of Hoth.
