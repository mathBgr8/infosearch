11-17 was an 11-17-series mining droid on Hosk Station. He assisted Zed, R2-D2, and other droids in stopping Olag Greck's plan to steal a shipment of ash ore, and to destroy Hosk Station to conceal the evidence. 11-17 was instrumental in rescuing Zed from a droid-eating hulgren, and later he distracted the reprogrammed wrecking droid Grozbok, allowing Zed to toss his stun stick into an opened access panel on the wrecking droid, disabling it. *Droids (1994) 6 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:11-17-series mining droids
