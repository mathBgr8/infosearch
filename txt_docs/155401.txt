Shankti Drive Works
1376

|role= |products=Hyperdrive motivators |founding= |dissolved= |affiliation= }} Shankti Drive Works was a technological company that was headquartered in the city of D'larah on the planet Demophon. They specialized in the manufacture and production of hyperdrive motivators and were at the forefront of hyperspace technology. Their hyperdrive motivators were used extensively on Imperial-class Star Destroyers. Their sister corporation, Tumleh Navigation, specialized in the production of navigation systems for hyperspace engines as well as research into hyperspatial programming and navigation.
