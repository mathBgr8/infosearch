Kudos
2037

Kudos was a Rodian who stayed in the settlement Anchorhead on the planet Tatooine when their ship broke down and they had to wait for parts. Kudos did not step foot outside of Anchorhead, and didn't understand why anyone would. Kudos filled their days with the card game pazaak, though there were not many players in Anchorhead. In 3956 BBY,The Essential Atlas dates the events of Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic to 3956 BBY. the Rodian had heard about the destruction of the planet Taris and spent time in Anchorhead's Hunting Lodge when the amnesiac Sith Lord Revan searched for a Star Map on Tatooine. Kudos had copper skin, black eyes and wore a green and teal suit with a utility belt. *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic Category:Gamblers Category:Males Category:Rodians Category:Spacers
