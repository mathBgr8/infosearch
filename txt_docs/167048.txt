Rainbow Bridge
1304

The Rainbow Bridge was a magical phenomenon witnessed on Endor, a forest moon orbiting the planet of Tana. It was created when the artifact known as the Bridge crystal was held toward Ibleam, Endor's sun. By shining through the prismatic crystal, sunlight would create a bridge that appeared to be made of rainbows.
