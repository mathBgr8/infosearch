Emergency cube concentrate
1410

Emergency cube concentrates were small dehydrated edible cubes containing nutrient concentrates.A Guide to the Star Wars Universe They were colored pink and blue, had no flavor, and were used in emergency situations by Rebel operatives. Having crashed on Mimban, Luke Skywalker and Leia Organa survived on emergency cube concentrates until they came upon an Imperial mining colony. *Splinter of the Mind's Eye *A Guide to the Star Wars Universe Category:Synthetic food
