Crix (or Krix) was a personal name. In Star Wars Legends, Crix was a common male name of Corellian origin. It could refer to: *Crix Madine, Rebel Alliance general at the Battle of Endor. *Crix Exohusker, member of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. *Crix Griff, Con artist living on Elrood. *Crix Madine, Rebel Alliance general at the Battle of Endor. *Crix Masst, Rebel Allaince general assassinated by Morteos on Sinded. *Crix Q5 Baobab, instructor of astronomy and astrography at the Baobab Merchant Marine Academy. *Crix Sunburris, Jedi Master and an ace pilot within the ranks of the Jedi Order's Starfighter Corps. *Crix Yarrow, an aide to Mos Eisley mayor Mikdanyell Guh'rantt. *General Crix Madine Military Reserve, aka Crix Base, was a Galactic Alliance military reserve over Coruscant. *Crix-class diplomatic courier shuttle, a diplomatic shuttle used by the Galactic Alliance and later by the Galactic Alliance Core Fleet. **Unidentified Crix-class diplomatic courier shuttle, shuttle aboard which Galactic Alliance Remnant Admiral Gar Stazi traveled to The Wheel. *Crix-class assault shuttle, assault vessel used by the Galactic Alliance and later by the Galactic Alliance Core Fleet. *Krix (disambiguation)
