Swanny and the Rooters
1018

Swanny and the Rooters were a band founded by Swanny Mull shortly after the Battle of Naboo that frequently performed at Decca the Hutt's base. Swanny at one point helped Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker gain entrance to Decca's lair, using them in the band in return. The Rooters were nearly destroyed when a group of Striker's men attacked Decca's compound. *Jedi Quest: The Shadow Trap Category:Bands
