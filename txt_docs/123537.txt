Lucky Lower Thirteen
813

Lucky Lower 13 was an unconverted ballroom on the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Independence, dating back to the ship's time as a pleasure cruiser and located in the aft quadrant of the ship. During the Galactic Civil War, it served as a bar and its proximity to the pilot's lounge made it a popular location for off-duty pilots, as well and many of the engineering and administrative crew who worked in that quadrant. The room was darkened to accommodate the Quarren crew members and tables and chairs were made to glow pink in the dark. *X-Wing: The Official Strategy Guide Category:Independence locations
