Great Mother River Restaurant
953

The Great Mother River Restaurant was located aboard the Ithorian Herdship Bazaar in Isttu Village. It was connected by skywalk to the nearby Great Mother River Hotel. Expensive and opulent, the Great Mother River Restaurant boasted top of the line cuisine, including 200 credit-a-bottle champagne. *Graveyard of Alderaan Category:Bazaar locations Category:Dining establishments
