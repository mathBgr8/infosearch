Sepulchral Ruby
608

The Sepulchral Ruby was a motif used by Utai on P-38 fighters. It originated from early humanoid settlements on Utapau circa 57,000 years before the Clone Wars. Because of this great distance in time, its meaning was long forgotten. *Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith Incredible Cross-Sections *Star Wars: Complete Vehicles Category:Symbols
