Turan
620

The Turan were a sentient species with orange skin. Some Turans pursued careers in music, like the popular band who played in the main hall of a Cloud City casino around 3 ABY. Base nose flutes, electric harps and soft percussion drums gave their music an "insistent, exhilarating tune" that stirred the bounty hunter Dengar deeply. Category:Sentient species
