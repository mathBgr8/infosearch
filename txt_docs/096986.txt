Sideshow Collectibles
8504

Sideshow Collectibles is a California-based toy company that makes licensed action figures and movie collectibles. In 2004 they made an agreement with Lucasfilm to produce Star Wars merchandise, which over the years has taken the form of 12-inch figures, premium-format figures, life-size collectibles, and dioramas.
