Scurry
817

Scurry was a young ferbil native to Kegan. It was one of V-Davi's two pet ferbils. Owning pets was forbidden in Kegan, so when O-Bin discovered it, she ordered V-Tarz to eliminate Scurry, along with V-Davi's other baby ferbil and two lizards. *Jedi Apprentice: The Fight for Truth Category:Males Category:Named pets
