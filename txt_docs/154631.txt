Jewel-fruit (Mon Calamari)
802

Jewel-fruit grew on Mon Calamari and, along with a type of seaweed also native to the world, was used to make a type of candy enjoyed by Vergere. It was also one of the many fruits distilled to make Tihaar. *Republic Commando: Triple Zero *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Fruits and vegetables Category:Mon Calamari culture
