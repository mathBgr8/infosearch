Jovan Station
4085

The Jovan Station was a space station located in the Jovan system. The Jovan Station was the primary Galactic Republic space station in the Gordian Reach during the Clone Wars, and was a stopping point for travelers from Phindar to the Auril sector. It was a very important element in the commerce of the Jovan system.
