Likstro
2158

Likstro was a beverage edible by members of the Aqualish species. In 27 BBY,The Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of Outbound Flight to 27 BBY. after Jedi Padawan Anakin Skywalker bumped into an Aqualish and subsequently spilled his drink in the diner Panky's on the Inner Rim &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Barlok system planet Barlok, Skywalker's Jedi Master, Obi Wan Kenobi, bought a large Likstro as an apology to the Aqualish. Likstro appeared in the 2006 novel Outbound Flight, written by Timothy Zahn. *Outbound Flight Category:Beverages
