Tan pel'trokal
1172

Tan pel'trokal was a Korunnal word that roughly translated to "jungle justice." It was a form of execution done by the Korunnai: the victims would be taken a hundred kilometers from the nearest settlement, removed of all equipment and clothing, and left alone in the jungle, where they faced near-certain death. Very few people were known to have survived tan pel'trokal. Chalk and her ghôsh dealt tan pel'trokal upon the Balawai who had gang-raped her.
