The Corasgh were a sentient species who, under the New Order, established a nest colony located in the Koornacht Cluster of the Core Worlds. This colony was wiped out when its planet was conquered in the Yevethan Purge. *Before the Storm *Before the Storm abridged audiobook Category:Sentient species
