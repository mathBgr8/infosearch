Challenge 35
1511

Challenge 35 was a roleplaying magazine from 1988. It contained many roleplaying articles, including two adventures for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The articles were called "Team Recovery" and "The H-Wing Strike Fighter," and both were written by James B.
