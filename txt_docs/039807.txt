Fred Tatasciore
10388

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Voice actor |companies= |website= }} Fred Tatasciore (born June 13) is an American voice actor from New York City. He has provided the voices for characters in dozens of cartoons, movies, and video games. For Star Wars, he played Qui-Gon Jinn and Oppo Rancisis in the Star Wars: Clone Wars micro TV series, Roos Tarpals in Star Wars: The Clone Wars, Yushyn in Star Wars Rebels, BL-OX in LEGO Star Wars: The Freemaker Adventures and Bolza Grool in Star Wars Resistance. He also voiced more than thirty characters for Star Wars: The Old Republic and its expansions, including Soa and Juvard Illip Oggurobb.Star Wars: The Old Republic Furthermore, he provided additional voices to Star Wars movies since Star Wars: Episode VII The Force Awakens. {| !
