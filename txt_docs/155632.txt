Homthor Deck
718

Homthor Deck was one of many aboard the Mon Calamari designed M-class luxury liner, the Kuari Princess. Passengers' starerooms were situated on Homthor Deck. Along with Wellad Deck, the two were referred to as the "credicruise decks." *Riders of the Maelstrom Category:Kuari Princess locations
