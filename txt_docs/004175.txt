|type=Hardcover |pages=64 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=9780789485885 |series=Star Wars Visual Dictionaries |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary is the third book in the Star Wars Visual Dictionary series. It describes and exhibits the elements of Star Wars: Episode II Attack of the Clones. It was republished as part of Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary. It gives away no spoilers from Attack of the Clones.
