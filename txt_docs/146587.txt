Depot XLS
3522

The Depot XLS was a Telgorn Corporation Repair Yard in the Fondor system. At some point, the repair yard was going to have the EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate Fury, a group of three Assassin-class corvettes called 192, the Ton-Falk-class escort carrier Straasa, the Strike-class medium cruiser Manticore, the Nebulon-B2 frigate Sicorro, and the CR90 corvette AD88 arriving at the depot for repairs. Unfortunately, when the ships had lowered their shields, the Rebels sent a strike force of B-wing starfighters from Rogue Squadron and Green Squadron, intending to destroy the starships at their most weakest condition.
