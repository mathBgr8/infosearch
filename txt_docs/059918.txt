Japor ivory wood
1777

thumb|[[Padmé Amidala/Legends|Queen Amidala's japor snippet was made of Japor ivory wood.]] Japor ivory wood was a type of wood found on the desert planet of Tatooine. Padmé Amidala's japor snippet, made by Anakin Skywalker, was constructed of japor ivory wood.
