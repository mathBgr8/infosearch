Coiling tack
409

Coiling tack were special accountrements that Swokes Swokes would outfit their Schinga mounts with. A set of coiling tack included a gold-inlaid saddle, a bit, bridle and reins, and a long-handled Schinga Electropole. *Geonosis and the Outer Rim Worlds Category:Swokes Swokes culture
