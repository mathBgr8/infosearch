Decree 77-92465-001 was an amendment to the Imperial Charter passed by the Imperial Senate in either 19 or 18 BBY. It read: :"All military offensives shall be considered under law as states of emergency, and subject to the Emergency Military Powers Act of the Senate." : *Children of the Jedi Category:Galactic Empire laws and acts
