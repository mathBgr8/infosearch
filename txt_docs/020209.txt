*Max Rebo Band |publisher= |release date= |media type=Song |pages= |published in= |genre= }} "My Hearts Belong to You" was a song performed by Evar Orbus and His Galactic Jizz-Wailers, a band formed in the final years of the Clone Wars of 22 BBY&ndash;19 BBY.The New Essential Chronology At some point before Evar Orbus, the band's lead musician, was killed in 4 ABYThe Essential Reader's Companion dates the events of "And the Band Played On: The Band's Tale," including the death of Evar Orbus, to 4 ABY. his band made three recordings and a name for themselves with crowd-pleasers such as "My Hearts Belong to You." After Orbus's death, the Max Rebo Band was formed from the remaining band members, and "My Hearts Belong to You" remained popular with the new band. "My Hearts Belong to You" was introduced in "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band," an article Rich Handley wrote for the sixty-seventh issue of the Star Wars Insider magazine, which was published on April 15, 2003. 2008's The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia later referred to the song as "My Heart Belongs to You." This article assumes the "My Hearts Belong to You" name from "Who's Who in the Max Rebo Band" is the correct song title. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Songs by Evar Orbus and His Galactic Wailers Category:Songs by the Max Rebo Band
