Nerf-throwing
723

Nerf-throwing was a sport that involved throwing nerfs for distance or accuracy. Jabrogg Thung was a Gamorrean intergalactic champion in the sport, who then became wanted by the Galactic Humane Society for mortally injuring several nerfs.
