MD-1
1169

MD-1 was an MD-1 medical droid assigned to the Imperial Navy EF76 Nebulon-B escort frigate. Its service carried over once the ship's crew mutined and took over control of the vessel. MD-1 was charged with overseeing the medical lab to handle pathological investigations, occasionally assisted by MD-2 and MD-3. *The Far Orbit Project Category:Droids of the Galactic Empire Category:MD-series medical droids
