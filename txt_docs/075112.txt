Sentinel beetle
843

Sentinel beetles were beetles used by the Yuuzhan Vong. Placed around their slave cities, the beetles would begin to flap their wings and create a steady drone whenever there was an escape attempt or an intruder. That way, they would alert nearby Yuuzhan Vong warriors who would then "deal" with the problem.
