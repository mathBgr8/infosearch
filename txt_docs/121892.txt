This is approximately 1200 times the distance from the Sun to Pluto or three quarters of a light year. The outer extent of the Oort cloud places the boundary of our Solar System at nearly a quarter of the distance to Proxima Centauri, the nearest star to the Sun. *Shield of Lies Category:Stars in the Inner Rim Category:Stars in the Trailing Sectors
