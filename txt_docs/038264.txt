|type=Hardcover reference book |pages=32 |dimensions= |weight= |isbn=0773731822 |series=Incredible Cross-Sections |preceded by= |followed by= }} Star Wars: Episode I Incredible Cross-Sections is the second installment of the Incredible Cross-Sections series of reference books published by Dorling Kindersley on May 10, 1999. Written by David West Reynolds and illustrated by Hans Jenssen and Richard Chasemore, the book provides in-depth cross-sections and illustrations of over a dozen starships and vehicles from the film Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace, including the Consular-class cruiser, tribubble bongo, Armored Assault Tank, and Lucrehulk-class Droid Control Ship.
