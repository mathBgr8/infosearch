Medlifter troop transport
1408

Medlifter troop transports, also know simply as medlifter transports or medlifters, were airspeeders designed to transport wounded soldiers from the battlefield to mobile field hospitals. The Ubrikkian Transports Model 300 was the medlifter most commonly used by the Galactic Republic during the Clone Wars.The Clone Wars Campaign Guide The medlifter model used on Drongar could hold six clone troopers. *MedStar I: Battle Surgeons *MedStar II: Jedi Healer *The New Essential Guide to Droids *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia *The Clone Wars Campaign Guide Category:Repulsorcraft troop transport classes
