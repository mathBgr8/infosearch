New Agamar
1862

New Agamar was a high-gravity planet in the Galactic Republic. At least one native was enlisted in the Republic military by the time of the late Clone Wars.Jedi Trial New Agamar is first and solely mentioned in Jedi Trial, a novel by David Sherman and Dan Cragg, in a short character profile in the latter part of the novel.
