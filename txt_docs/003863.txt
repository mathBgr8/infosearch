Bakura task force
2203

The Bakura task force was a small fleet of Rebel Alliance starships dispatched from the Endor fleet to assist the Imperial world of Bakura in repelling the Ssi-ruuvi invasion force of 4 ABY.The Essential Chronology The task force was not given an operational name or sign, and consisted of the modified starfighter carrier Flurry, one CR90 corvette, five Corellian gunships, the Millennium Falcon and twenty starfighters (including survivors of Rogue Squadron). Because the task force was so hastily assembled, its four B-wings did not have time to complete their maintenance overhauls.The Truce at Bakura Sourcebook At the Invasion of Bakura, the task force lost Flurry and several starfighters.
