Order GAO-1133B was a law passed by the Galactic Alliance stating that major defense firms could only produce weaponry for the Galactic Alliance Defense Force, with limited amounts suitable only for their own planetary defense being the sole exception. *Legacy of the Force: Betrayal Category:Galactic Alliance laws and acts
