Imperial AT-ST manual
1180

The Imperial AT-ST manual was a manual written by the Galactic Empire to guide the reader on the aspects of an Imperial AT-ST. It was updated sometime after 4 ABY, to inform that AT-STs would no longer be used on planets such as Endor, alluding to the fact that many AT-STs were destroyed because of dangers on Endor. *The New Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Manuals and instruction guides Category:Military reports and documents
