Karsems were the currency of the planet Radnor. 5,000 karsems was the equivalent of an average one-year's pay on the planet; it was also the price required to purchase a bio-isolation suit during the panic-riven bioplague crisis that struck Radnor in 27 BBY. *Jedi Quest: The Way of the Apprentice Category:Currency
