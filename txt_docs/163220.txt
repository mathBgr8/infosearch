Furious (Victory II-class)
2343

Furious was a Victory II-class Star Destroyer that, along with several Viraxo ships, including the CR90 corvette Blast Radius took part in the capture of Twin Sun Station. This attack was timed as the station's personnel were evacuating the station due to a warning from Antan Azzameen. Ace and Aeron Azzameen were present as well. Ace, in the Otana, was picking up a container of warheads, while Aeron was getting to the safe to collect some valuables there.
