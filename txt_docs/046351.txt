Jedi courier
1315

Numerous Jedi couriers come to a [[Jedi Convocation|Jedi convocation at Deneba|thumb|200px|right]] Jedi courier was the generic term used for the personal transport of a Jedi. In ancient times, each Jedi chose their own courier, choosing from any number of available classes; following the Exar Kun War, Vanya-class Jedi Couriers were released specifically for the purpose.
