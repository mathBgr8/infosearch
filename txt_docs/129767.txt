Tonas Vueral
897

Tonas Vueral was a male Tunroth. As a Tunroth Hunter, he attained the high rank of chirlan (nineteenth), and was a renowned tracker. He often worked in the Anthos sector, home to many Tunroth on worlds such as Berchest. *Galaxy Guide 12: Aliens — Enemies and Allies *Ultimate Alien Anthology Category:Males Category:Tunroth
