It was situated on a hyperlane that connected it to the Nicandra system as well as the Korvaii system in the Kordu sector. thumb|right|200px|The Ilimardon government was interested in the use of domesticated mynocks as weapons against starships. At some point, the planetary government of Ilimardon, the Regal Hierarchy, became interested in utilizing domesticated mynocks as weapons against starships, as practiced by the Trandoshan pirate Chorssk. To obtain its own flock of domesticated mynocks, the Ilimardon government persuaded the Human Gres Ople, a mynock trainer in Chorssk's pirate organization, to instead become an employee in the wildlife division of the Regal Hierarchy. Ople subsequently raised a flock of mynocks from a breeding pair she had stolen from Chorssk. Six months after Ople's defection, Chorssk had sworn to hunt down and kill his former trainer and was willing to go up against all the starships in the Ilimardon fleet in order to get to her. By 12 ABY, Ilimardon fell within the borders of the New Republic. Ilimardon was governed by the Regal Hierarchy, which maintained a fleet of starships and a wildlife division. At one point, the Human female Greps Ople was employed in the Hierarchy's wildlife division as a trainer of domesticated mynocks. Ilimardon was introduced in "Domesticated Mynocks," an article written by Jeff Quick and published on Wizards.com on April 25, 2002.
