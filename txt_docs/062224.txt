Vew-Grek Holo Images
1137

Vew-Grek Holo Images was a major company located on Bespin's Cloud City. It specialized in a variety of uses for holo technology. The strength of the company was so strong that the business' leaders were given positions on the city's Exex during their time as heads of the company.
