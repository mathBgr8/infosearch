Navigators' Guild
2018

The Navigators' Guild was a guild located in the Unknown Regions of the galaxy that sent navigators to those who hired them. Void Guides, Pathfinders, and Vector One were groups employed by the Guild. The Navigators of the Guild were bound by a strict code of neutrality and an even stricter code of confidentiality. They were forbidden from discussing the operations and locations of any one client with any other client. One such operation was the ability Pathfinders had of finding one another, which was a closely guarded secret among the guild.
