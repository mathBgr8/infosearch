Canny Mandary Bertar
2105

Canny Mandary Bertar was a female Mrlssi senator who served as chair of the Senate Dispensation Committee and represented Mrlsst and the Tapani sector to the Galactic Senate of the Republic during the Separatist Crisis in 23 BBY.
