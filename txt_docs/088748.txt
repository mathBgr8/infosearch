|continent= |location= |climate= |interest= |population= |demonym= |affiliation=Tourist Guild }} Ataria City was located on Ataria Island on the planet Spira. Settled along the beaches of the island's eastern coast, it was a thriving tourist location. The city's marina was the start and end point of the Spira Regatta Open, a famous yacht race.
