Camblian Order
2599

The Camblian Order was a scientific organization that, shortly before the Battle of Yavin in 1 BBY, sent 309 prospective students from the Outer Rim world of Eedoq to be enrolled at the Clarion Scholars Academy on the Expansion Region planet Daemen. However, their transport ship, the Lark, was destroyed en route by the Imperial pilot Tars Nandy, resulting in the deaths of all 309 children. During the Imperial Period in the Outer Rim Territories, a cult known as the Acolytes of Dread Rur stalked the spacelanes, capturing starliners and sending the vessels and all aboard to fiery deaths within nearby suns. The Corellian Security Force dispatched agent Bey to investigate the group, and he foiled an Acolyte plot to destroy a Camblian Order liner carrying hundreds of young scholars departing from Glova. *Empire 9 Category:Scientific organizations
