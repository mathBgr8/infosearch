Knezex Hral Piksoar
1185

Knezex Hral Piksoar was a male Drovian who lived on the swamp planet Nim Drovis. He was a direct descendant of Garnu Hral Eschen, who was killed by Gopso'o warriors. Knezex worked to avenge this death by fighting against Gopso'o terrorists. Knezex worked at the New Republic base on Nim Drovis and was present when Han Solo and Chewbacca dropped off survivors from the Corbantis. *Planet of Twilight *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Drovians Category:Males Category:New Republic individuals
