Kuul milk
423

Kuul milk was a drink that Rodians had at breakfast, served heated. It was thick, creamy and lime-green. Rodian merchant Teeloo cooked it for breakfast for him and his friend before racing through Eskaron in 33 BBY. Category:Breakfast foods Category:Milk Category:Rodian food
