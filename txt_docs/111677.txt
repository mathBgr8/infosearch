Unidentified Mustafarian bounty hunter
2528

This bounty hunter was employed by Jabba Desilijic Tiure in an attempt to execute Tyber Zann just before his prison break. Urai Fen killed him, while he sneaked past the hunter undetected using a cloaking device. thumb|right|200px|The bounty hunter meets [[Tyber Zann.]] It is not known whether the bounty hunter is Mustafarian or Mandalorian, for while he is speaking to Tyber, a gray Mustafarian model is used, whereas, once he is dead, a gray 'dead' model is used wearing armor that looks much more Mandalorian than Mustafarian. To cause greater confusion, in coding, he is identified as Mandalorian.
