Prisoner 934890
930

Prisoner 934890 was a prisoner in Dontamo Prison. In 19 BBY, he gave cellmate Ferus Olin information on the rules of the prison and what to avoid when Olin arrived. However, he went contradictory to his own advice and faked illness in order to be sent to the infirmary. Everybody who was sent there never returned, and it is presumed 934890 met his death also. *The Last of the Jedi: Death on Naboo Category:Criminals Category:Males
