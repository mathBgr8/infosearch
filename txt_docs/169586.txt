East Ryloth Street
593

East Ryloth Street was located within the city of Xakrea on the planet of Darkknell. The roadway was at least five kilometers long and was where Arky's Emporium of Forgotten Treasures was situated. Considered part of a seedy neighborhood, properties on that street were held with lower regard compared to those on its counterpart, West Ryloth Street. Category:Darkknell locations Category:Roadways
