Aeropteryx
1034

The Aeropteryx were a race of avians from a planet in the Q'nithian system. Their wings were too small to power them for flight, so they used them for pulling themselves across the ground instead. Their wings were tipped with talons to help them do this. They had gray feathers and eyes poorly adjusted to vision.
