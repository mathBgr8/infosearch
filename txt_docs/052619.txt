Antha-Kres
1049

Antha-Kres was a male Tiss'shar of considerable influence residing on Tiss'sharl, where he served as the Mayor. An eligible candidate for the position of President of the Tiss'sharl League, he was assassinated by order of the ambitious Geor-Dan-Thi who sought to remove his opponents for the Presidency. *Empire 31 *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Mayors Category:Tiss'shar
