John Jackson Miller
27994

|death= |listedpronouns= |nationality=American |occupation=Writer |companies=*Dark Horse Comics *Del Rey |website= }} John Jackson Miller is an American comic-book writer, novelist and commentator, known for the Knights of the Old Republic and Knight Errant comic series published by Dark Horse Comics. He also wrote the short story Labor Pains, the novel Kenobi, and co-wrote the Knights of the Old Republic Campaign Guide, among many others. Many of Jackson's stories explore the responsibilities held by Jedi. He has edited magazines including Comics & Games Retailer, Comics Buyer's Guide, and Scrye: The Guide to Collectible Card Games, and written books including The Standard Catalog of Comic Books. He also researches comic-book circulation figures on his history site . His first professional comics work appeared in 2003 in Crimson Dynamo for Marvel Comics, which led to a run on Iron Man. His first Star Wars work appeared in 2005 in Star Wars: Empire. Miller also wrote the comic adaptation of Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull in 2009. In 2015, Miller released his first Star Trek novel, Star Trek: The Next Generation: Takedown, to greatly positive reader acclaim. His most recent book was Batman: Resurrection, an original novel set in between the 1989 live action film adaptation of Batman and its 1992 sequel Batman Returns, released on October 15, 2024.
