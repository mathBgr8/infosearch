Makhlouf
1337

Makhlouf was a YT-1300 light freighter belonging to the Rebel Alliance that saw service in the Airam sector. When the decision was made to construct a Rebel shipyard in Airam space. Makhlouf was among the ships that were part of a convoy that was sent there, although the cargo contents were not specified. Unfortunately, the Galactic Empire learned of the convoy and readied a trap for it in the Rocrin system. Constrained by the gravity well projectors of the Interdictor cruiser Compellor, and pinned down by the Imperial-class Star Destroyer Rage, Makhlouf attempted to stop the attack by targeting the TIE/sa bombers.
