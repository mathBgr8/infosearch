Jarril
1222

Jarril was a male smuggler who frequented the Smuggler's Run and was an acquaintance of Han Solo. He met with him on Coruscant in the Crystal Jewel Casino. Jarril was the owner of the Spicy Lady, which he designed after the Millennium Falcon.
