Endorian Grasslands
1541

|terrain= |fauna= |flora=Grass |interest= |affiliation= }} The Endorian Grasslands was an area on the Forest Moon of Endor that was dominated by tall grass. It was inhabited by many species, such as the Grass Trekkers, and it was bordered by the Grass Trekkers' shrine and Gupin stronghold. The Endorian Grasslands first appeared in "The Land of the Gupins," the eighth episode of the animated television series Ewoks. The episode was written by Bob Carrau and premiered on October 26, 1985. *Droids\Ewoks Category:Endor locations
