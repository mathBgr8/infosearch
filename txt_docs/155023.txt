Colada
1978

Colada was a Nebulon-B2 frigate used by the Rebel Alliance. It attempted to evacuate the Goff Platform. Realizing that the most serious danger to the craft evacuating the platform came from the Interdictor Compellor, it made its two starfighter squadrons Azul Squadron and Grez Squadron focus attacks on the Compellor. However, Avenger Squadron managed to destroy both squadrons down to the last starfighter. They then attacked the Colada and reduced its shields.
