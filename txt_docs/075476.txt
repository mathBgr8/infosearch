Quulaar
680

A quulaar was a piece of clothing used by Wookiees to carry heavy loads and younglings who were not yet old enough to climb on their own. The name literally meant "baby-sack". Han Solo and Jarik Solo were once carried in them during a visit to Kashyyyk. *Rebel Dawn *The New Jedi Order: Agents of Chaos I: Hero's Trial *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Apparel for storage Category:Apparel of the Wookiees Category:Bags and backpacks
