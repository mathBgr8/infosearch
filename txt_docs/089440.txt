Brogar
2321

Brogar was a male Advozse who owned Brogar's Safe House and Cantina on the planet Lok, a cantina that doubled as a safe house for those who needed a place to lay low. Brogar used armed droid bouncers to keep the peace within his establishment. By the year 137 ABY, some of his patrons included a compulsive gambler named Naxy Screeger and a fugitive Jedi named Hosk Trey'lis, both of whom were captured in defiance of Brogar's offered sanctuary by Cade Skywalker, Jariah Syn, and Deliah Blue. Brogar denied to the trio of bounty hunters any knowledge that Trey'lis was a Jedi and claimed he must have been affected by a mind trick.
