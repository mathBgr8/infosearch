New Nozho
720

New Nozho was a city on Clak'dor IV that was founded by the Y'bith. It was named for the city of Nozho, which was destroyed by a biological attack around 300 BBY during a civil war on the Clak'dor VII. New Nozho eventually became a notable trade port. Category:Cities
