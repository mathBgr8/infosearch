In later life, Kaj fell in love with Celia Durasha, a smuggler from Lankashiir, and ran several missions with her for underworld kingpins Bwahl the Hutt and Rass M'Guy. When a smuggling deal went sour, however, Bwahl hired the bounty hunter Thune to capture them.
