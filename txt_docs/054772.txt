|gender= |pronouns=He/him |height= |mass= |hair=Brown |feathers= |eyes= |skin=Fair |cyber= |families=new |parents=new |partners=new |siblings=new |children=new |affiliation=Alliance to Restore the Republic |masters= |apprentices= }} Firin Morett was a fair-skinned Human with brown hair who worked as an engineer in the Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War fought by the Alliance against the Galactic Empire. Stationed with General Jan Dodonna at the Great Temple on the moon Yavin 4 prior to 0 BBY's Battle of Yavin,The New Essential Chronology he had final launch authority in the base's hangar and managed ground crews. Responsible for preparing Red and Gold Squadron starships for battle, Morett and his ground crews worked quickly to prepare the squadrons before the Battle of Yavin. Right before the battle, Morett was adding fuel to one of the starfighters as the Rebel pilots were departing. The battle ended with the Alliance destroying the Imperial DS-1 Orbital Battle Station above Yavin 4. Morett wore a gray jumpsuit. Firin Morett first appeared in 1977's Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope, the first film of the original trilogy. His scene was filmed between May 13 and May 18, 1976 at Shepperton Studios's stage H.The Making of Star Wars: The Definitive Story Behind the Original Film He was first named by Decipher in 1998, for their Special Edition Limited expansion of the Star Wars Customizable Card Game.
