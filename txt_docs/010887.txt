He served in the Galactic Empire but eventually defected to the Alliance to Restore the Republic along with Imperial Army storm commando leader&mdash;and later Rebel general&mdash;Crix Madine. By the year 4 ABY,The Death Star II Limited card set takes place primarily during the events of , which The New Essential Chronology dates to 4 ABY. Marmor held the rank of corporal in the Alliance, and he worked as a dock mechanic aboard the MC80 Home One type Star Cruiser Home One. During his service aboard Home One, Marmor managed a staff of seventy-five starfighter and twelve Star Cruiser mechanics in the rear quadrant of the Cruiser. He had fair skin and dark hair.
