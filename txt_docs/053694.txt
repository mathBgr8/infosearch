They were known to be violent and expansionist. The group believed that someday a Man-Hutt would sweep evil from the galaxy. Approximately ten years prior to the Battle of Yavin they sent a battle fleet into twelve systems and virtually wiped out every planet, including the Jiroch system, in a genocide known as the Reslian Purge.
