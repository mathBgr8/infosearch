Oondar
1395

Oondar was a male Duros trader and salesman with close connections to Visquis and the Exchange who was encountered by the Jedi Exile on Nar Shaddaa. He wanted to get rid of fellow merchant Geeda by bribing the Exile. However, the plan backfired and the Exile forced Oondar to move. Oondar then threatened the Exile, saying that he would get the Exchange to attack her. If the player chooses the dark-side option, then the Exile removes Geeda and Oondar gives the player a discount.
