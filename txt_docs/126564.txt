Regulator X-Q2 System Patrol Cruiser
2488

The Regulator X-Q2 System Patrol Cruiser, also known as the Regulator, was a scout starship manufactured by Loronar Corporation. The Regulator X-Q2 was one-hundred-and-fifty meters in length, and could transport up to fifteen troopers and five hundred metric tons of cargo. The vessel required a crew of nine to operate, with five of those onboard serving as gunners for the ship's laser cannons. As with the IPV-1 system patrol craft, the Regulator was not equipped with a hyperdrive. They were used to patrol spaces that were already pacified. Two of the vessels served in the Battle of Bissillirus at the Bissillirus Resupply Base. The X-Q2 appears in West End Games Star Wars Gamemaster Kit.
