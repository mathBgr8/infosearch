The planet occupied the third orbit around its star, the yellow-white Faarlsun, and had a poisonous environment. Weken was first mentioned in the 1993 sourcebook Wanted by Cracken, written by Louis J. Prosperi for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
