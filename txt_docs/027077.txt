Rith Tar'ak was an Ebranite who along with her sisters, Nyik and Tor'ara, were part of the Rull clan on the planet Ebra located in the galaxy's Mid RimUltimate Alien Anthology Tar'ak eventually became a warrior for clan Rull, one of the few Ebranite clans that still had a warring society during Tar'ak's life. Early during its reign, the Galactic Empire took control of the planet and installed Evitch Jenton as its Imperial governor. After the newly appointed governor instituted martial law, At some point during the occupation, Tar'ak and her sisters were the first to volunteer for an assault organized by the Rull clan leader agaisnt an Imperial facility on the planet. However the attack force was slaughtered Imperial forces. The handful of Ebranite survivors, including Tar'ak, Nyik, and Tor'ara were taken captive and were forced to harvest lu-ramin, a substance that grew in caves near the Imperial facility. Soon after the sisters' enslavement, Tar'ak led the group of Ebranites in an escape where they captured an Imperial pilot and forced him to fly them off Ebra in a starship. Soon after, they were captured by a vessel affiliated with the Alliance to Restore the Republic and were given the opportunity to join the Alliance, which they quickly accepted. The sisters served with the Alliance throughout the Kesh sector which contained Ebra. They eventually formed the Sandstorm Unit, a desert infiltration force under the Alliance Special Forces Scandium Team. During its operation, the Sandstorm Unit achieved one of the best records of success of the Scandium units. Around 0 BBYThe article "Cracken's Rebel Operatives" in Star Wars Adventure Journal 10 places the Wellte-ir Massacre before the Battle of Thovinack. The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia places the Battle of Thovinack shortly before the Battle of Yavin, which The New Essential Chronology dates to 0 BBY. twenty-one members of the Scandium Team, including Tar'ak and her sisters, fought a four day battle against two Imperial Armor units on the planet Bresnan in the Wellte-ir system. Tar'ak survived the battle which became known as the Wellte-ir Massacre and resulted in the death of fifteen members of the Scandium Team including Nyik and Tor'ara. After the massacre, the Scandium Team being disbanded and Tar'ak was transfered to Yntrann, a terrestrial object in the Wild Space Yntrann system, &mdash; Based on corresponding data for Yntrann system where she briefly commanded a small yet highly decorated desert task force in the a Special Forces wilderness regiment for the Alliance. However the leadership position did not appeal to Tar'ak despite her success at it. She eventually turned down a promotion to colonel and requested a transfer to position with Alliance recruitment. Her request was granted and she became one of two individuals tasked with recruitment and training on her homeworld of Ebra. While working as a recruitment agent, Tar'ak captured A-5DS, a 5DS Ebranite Relations Droid, which she reprogrammed and later used as a contact among the Ebranite settlements around the Grythsorin canyon on Ebra. Korgath, Tar'ak's friend and former teammate, eventually began trying to convince her to return to field work despite her reluctance after the massacre. Tar'ak was a stocky Ebranite with her clan's insignia on the shoulder of her uppermost left arm. The insignia consisted of a reptilian Hag'thyyr wielding a simple spear while engulfed in flames. Like many Ebranite who served with the Alliance, she was slow to accept the use of high technology except when such items were necessary. She never used blasters preferring to use melee weapons and her Kallinn bow. She was distrustful of droids and was wary of space travel. She was eventually able to overcome the painful memories of the Wellte-ir Massacre and continue to fight the Galactic Empire. Trained as a warrior of the clan Rull, Tar'ak was prepared with a number of necessary skills including beast riding, rock climbing and brawling. She was skilled with a Kallinn bow and a Untarr spear. Tar'ak excelled in leadership and was considered an expert in desert and cavernous survival. While working as a Rebel Alliance recruitment officer Tar'ak used a Kallinn bow with an accompanying quiver and arrows as well as a Untarr spear and natural medpacs.
