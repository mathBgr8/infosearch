A Force-sensitive male trained in the ways of the Force by the Jedi Order, Jaled Dur eventually attained the rank of Jedi Master. Credited with crafting the priceless Heart of the Universe, Master Dur's creation was a large gem imbued with the Force. At some point during his service to the Order, Master Dur took the Sunesi Initiate Aqinos as his Padawan. Instilling in him some forbidden beliefs about droids, technology and the Force, Aqinos would go on to be a controversial Master in the Order himself.
