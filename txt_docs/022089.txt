Berethron e Solo
2848

Berethron e Solo was a King of Corellia. During Solo reign, he initiated democratic reforms in the Corellian empire. In 312 BBY, he abolished his absolute rule and moved the planetary government toward a constitutional monarchy. Han Solo and his cousin Thrackan Sal-Solo were both thought to be Berethron's descendants. In 8 ABY, C-3PO invoked that lineage to prove Solo's worthiness to marry Leia Organa before the Hapan Royal House.
