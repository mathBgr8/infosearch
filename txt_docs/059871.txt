Domain Das
1662

Domain Das was a Domain among the Warrior caste of the Yuuzhan Vong. One warrior of Domain Das, Runck Das, served under Commander Shedao Shai in 25 ABY during the early days of the Yuuzhan Vong War and took part in the Battle of Garqi, where the Jedi Corran Horn, Ganner Rhysode and Jacen Solo successfully escaped despite the efforts of Das' direct superior, Krag Val. When Das returned with news of what happened there for the Commander, Shai killed him for his and Val's failures. Domain Das first appeared in the 2000 novel The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin written by Michael A. Stackpole. *The New Jedi Order: Dark Tide II: Ruin Das
