Tatooine (starship)
750

The Tatooine was a Mon Calamari Star Cruiser of the New Republic Defense Fleet. Tatooine was part of the blockade force under the command of Wedge Antilles dispatched to Almania in 17 ABY. The cruiser was destroyed in battle against Kueller's fleet. *The New Rebellion *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mon Calamari cruisers Category:Starships of the New Republic
