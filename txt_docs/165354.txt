Marduk
1455

The Marduk was an Imperial Carrack-class light cruiser that was assigned to the Celanon system under the command of Systems Sub-Commander Jared Brojtal. Following the attack on the Syndaar Military Prep School, the warship led the search for the Ho'Din terrorist Ort Hoogra-D'En. *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Carrack-class light cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
