Namrhe
2591

Namrhe was originally from Bestine IV but became a nomad when the population were driven from their homeworld by the Galactic Empire and joined the Rebel Alliance soon after. Serving aboard the Mon Calamari Star Cruiser Independence, Namrhe was a talented pilot described by the others as "likely to survive." Namrhe was chosen by Captain Keyan Farlander to serve as his wingman, along with Omin-Oreh, for a seemingly routine escort mission. The three Red Squadron pilots would fly BTL Y-wing starfighters, defending the shuttle Hunter while it captured an Imperial transport carrying officers to the Death Star.
