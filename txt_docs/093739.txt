Before the Helmet
8328

thumb|250px Before the Helmet was a feature on Hyperspace: The Official Star Wars Fan Club that showed production photos of Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith. Before the Helmet began on October 2, 2003 with the image "I Sense Much Fear in You" and ended May 18, 2005 with "The Helmet Lowers". The photo gallery contained eighty-three pictures. For the first time on a Star Wars film, StarWars.com is pleased to present an online gallery of production photos released before the film hits the theaters. A Hyperspace members exclusive!
