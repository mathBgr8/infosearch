Shannur Darkstar
1364

Shannur Darkstar was a Jedi Knight around 3000 BBY. She was one of the investigators of the ruins of Pelgrin, following the archaeological team of Ermin Phin-Mar. The Jedi were especially interested in the Oracle, a structure that emitted tremors in the Force and could only be activated by Force-sensitives. Shannur and her fellow Jedi discovered the Oracle was a device used in combination with the Force to foresee the future.
