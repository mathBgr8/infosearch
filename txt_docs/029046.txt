Kooloota-Fyf
1661

Kooloota-Fyf was the Givin leader of the Shipbuilders and Astromechs Guild and one of the funders of Carnor Jax's plan to sabotage Palpatine's clones. He served on the Imperial Interim Ruling Council. His position on the Council ensured the cooperation of the Guild, and little else. He was imprisoned by Xandel Carivus during Carivus' bid for power.
