SSct power generator
1091

SSct power generators were developed and designed by Santhe/Sienar Technologies for use in their TIE crawler tank. They could power the tank at speeds of up to ninety kilometers per hour. *The Essential Guide to Vehicles and Vessels Category:Reactors and power generators Category:Santhe/Sienar Technologies products
