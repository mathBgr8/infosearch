Starduster
2999

The Starduster was Nera Dantels' personal starship, which assisted the Rebel Alliance on numerous missions. It was the ship that smuggled the first astromech droids for use on Rebel X-wings. The ship was later used to ship Dantels, Leia Organa, Luke Skywalker, and Jorin Sol to Jabiim to take part in a diplomatic mission. Unfortunately, the Jabiimi took the diplomats prisoner due to the fact that Luke's father had betrayed the Jabiimi years earlier.
