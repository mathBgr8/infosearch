Creeve
1958

Creeve was a male Human captain in the Galactic Empire. Captain Creeve was an male Human officer in the Galactic Empire. He was posted to a planet where the locals were poor and willing to do anything for an extra credit, although he found the women to be a little too spirited for his tastes. Creeve was unaware that one of the technicians on the base, Troomis, was secretly a spy for Alliance Intelligence.
