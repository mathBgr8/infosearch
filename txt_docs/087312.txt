Tarrence Chenati
1803

Tarrence Chenati was the alias of a spy who infiltrated the Jedi Starfighter Program in order to sabotage it. A former member of the Senate Bureau of Intelligence, though he was reported to have died in 62 BBY, his identity was reactivated in 42 BBY by the Galactic Senate. It remains unclear whether his "death" was a falsely-reported cover-up to hide his continued existence, or whether the second spy was a different person entirely, merely using the name of Tarrence Chenati. Although discovered by Qui-Gon Jinn and Tahl trying to sabotage Clee Rhara's starfighters, Chenati escaped, but his mission was ultimately successful, as the Jedi starfighter program was soon shut down.
