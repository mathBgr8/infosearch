Armadid
1533

Armadid was a large, Force-using predator that stalked the mountains of planet Nubia. The creature measured about 3 meters in length and had bony, armored plates on its domed back. It had a short, stocky tail with a crablike pincer at the end. Its short legs could retract beneath protective armor. Its head was triangular, with a tooth-filled maw and a pair of curved horns used to gore prey. When hunting, it lay in wait, using its gray armor as camouflage in the rocky terrain.
