Viptiel
596

Viptiel was a plant native to Nal Hutta. It was known for its therapeutic properties to the X'Ting for its hormone-balancing effects during that species' transition from one gender to the other; however, very few could afford it. Vippits also used viptiel as a medicinal supplement, especially during their mating periods. *The Cestus Deception *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Pharmaceutical plants
