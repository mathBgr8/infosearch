Holo faker
1434

thumb|right|220px|G0-T0 and the fake [[Hologram/Legends|hologram of "Goto".]] A holo faker was one who falsified holograms by piecing together holographic footage, usually for personal gain. Many holo fakers got their start as children, playing pranks via their creations.
