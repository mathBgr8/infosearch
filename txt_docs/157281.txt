Its commander was Angela Krin. The Resolute blockaded the Tion Cluster world Endregaad following an outbreak of a plague. This was to ensure that the plague would not spread beyond this planet. During this action, the Resolute intercepted the freighter Shadowfire which had been sent by Popara the Hutt to rescue his child, Mika, who was stuck on the planet.
