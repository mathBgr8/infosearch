Mike Edmonds
2492

Mike Edmonds (born January 13, 1944) played an Ugnaught in the Carbon-freezing chamber scene in Star Wars: Episode V The Empire Strikes Back. Producer Robert Watts liked his performance and promised to keep him in mind for any parts in the next film.
