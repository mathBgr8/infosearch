Bespin Bandit
1818

The Bespin Bandit was a delta-shaped smuggling freighter with an off-set cockpit. It belonged to Uz Bonearm and it fought for the New Republic in the battle of New Alderaan alongside Starhook Ten and Hyperspace Marauder.
