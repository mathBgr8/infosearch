Lojrak Shrag
1232

Lojrak Shrag was a salt-smuggling male Arcona criminal who lived on Cona before the outbreak of the Clone Wars. Godiban Bakoosta was his lieutenant. In 22 BBY, Shrag was arrested by Jedi Tassida Judrelle, Quarmall, and Stass Allie prior to the First Battle of Geonosis. Category:Arcona Category:Males Category:Smugglers
