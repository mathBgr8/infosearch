Corellia's Own
1394

A quadrant of space in the Corellian system, Corellia's Own was perhaps the most peaceful and orderly area in the entire system, including most of the cities on Corellia itself. Patrolled strictly by CorSec under the direct order of the Diktat, it was a sector unsullied by the presence of the Galactic Empire even at its height.
