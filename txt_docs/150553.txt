Kastin
2178

Kastin, also known as OM811, was a barren rock planet with no moons. It occupied the closest orbital position around the red giant star Thrantin Major in the Thrantin OM81-A system, a part of the Ollonir Boundaries in the Negs portion of the Colonies. Kastin was first mentioned in Alliance Intelligence Reports, a 1995 sourcebook for West End Games' Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
