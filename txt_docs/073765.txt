Brendon Fraim
521

Brendon Fraim is a Star Wars fan who designed the Lyco Pibex in the Design An Alien contest held in the Star Wars Galaxy Magazines. The entry was picked second place in the sixteen years and older category. He was twenty-two at the time his entry was published. Category:Design An Alien contest winners
