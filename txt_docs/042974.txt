Titan (Victory-class)
1779

Titan was a Victory-class Star Destroyer which once orbited the planet Harridan. While in orbit, it snagged a Rebel ship in its tractor beam in order to inspect the ship as it was leaving the planet. Shortly afterwards, the Rebel ship was attacked by Egot Pai-4-Yem in the Razorshark. Following his duties, the commander of the Titan moved to intercept the Razorshark, and managed to capture it in its tractor beams, which allowed the Rebel ship to escape into hyperspace.
