Vellian drop ship
514

The Vellian drop ship was a model of starship. One such ship was meant to be in Squarn's Junkyard when a group of Rebels were looking for spare parts to repair their hyperdrive, but in its place was a hummock denoting a partially collapsed cargo container. Category:Dropship classes
