|series=Star Wars (1977) |issue=101 }} Star Wars 101 is the one-hundred-first issue of the Marvel Star Wars series of comics. Han's relationship with Leia is on the rocks&mdash;and that's not all! Han's ready to leave&mdash;but more than just jumping ship, he's going to take off for a whole different universe! And because you demanded it: the return of Femm Syhsa! [sic] "Far, Far Away" is written by Jo Duffy and penciled by Cynthia Martin.Marvel Age #28 His relationship with Leia is not all that's on the rocks for Han Solo. This time it looks like he's not only going to jump ship, he's going to take off for a whole different universe! "Far, Far Away" written by Jo Duffy and penciled by Cynthia Martin. Far, Far Away was originally intended to appear in issue 98 but was pushed back when Supply and Demand was published instead. The events in this comic take place chronologically before the events in Star Wars 99.
