Marvel Movie Showcase 2
1446

Marvel Movie Showcase 2 collects the fourth, fifth, and sixth issues from the Star Wars (1977) series of comics and had a $1.25 U.S. cover price. This issue reprints the cover to Star Wars (1977) 4, but without the original text. This is a comics adaptation of Star Wars: Episode IV A New Hope. *Star Wars (1977) 4 *Star Wars (1977) 5 *Star Wars (1977) 6 02
