|published in=Star Wars Tales 14 |reprinted in= |pages=6 |timeline=22 BBYThe Essential Reader's Companion |series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "The Lesson" was a comic appearing in Star Wars Tales 14 and collected in Star Wars Tales Volume 4. The story revolves around Tu'ala, a Jedi Padawan, during the Battle of Geonosis and her journey in discovering the need for Jedi to be willing to give up their own life to serve the living Force. The story is largely told through flashbacks of Tu'ala learning from her master, Master Zuth.
