Mount Yeroc
674

Mount Yeroc was a tall mesa that was located on the planet Tahlboor, in the Tion Hegemony. Located within the mountain was the Sky cannon, an ancient device that had been constructed by an extinct Tahlboorean civilization. *The Protocol Offensive *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Mesas Category:Tahlboor locations
