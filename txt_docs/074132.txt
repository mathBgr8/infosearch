Aaron McBride
4629

|website= }} Aaron McBride is an artist for Industrial Light & Magic. He wrote and illustrated the Star Wars comic Old Wounds, featuring the first appearance of a resurrected Darth Maul. He drew the Darth Vader and Count Dooku cover art for Star Wars Insider 88 and illustrated the cover for the novels Lords of the Sith and Battlefront: Twilight Company. McBride has worked as a concept artist on the films Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith, Rogue One: A Star Wars Story, Star Wars: Episode VIII The Last Jedi, and Solo: A Star Wars Story, as well as ILM Immersive's Star Wars: Secrets of the Empire and Vader Immortal – Episode I.
