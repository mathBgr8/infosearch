Analysis grid

thumb|right|90px|Dani is subjected to the [[Torture/Legends|tortures of the analysis grid.]] The analysis grid was a device that fired coherent beams of energy through a specimen, revealing much about them. The analysis was an extremely painful process for the specimen, as the energy beams used the same technology as blasters.
