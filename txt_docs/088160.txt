Rebel Privateers!
8873

"Rebel Privateers!" was a Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game article published in Star Wars Adventure Journal 2 written by Timothy S. O'Brien and illustrated by Douglas Shuler. It begins with a letter to Moff Gergris, Halthor Sector Command from Governor Thanis, Noonar. What follows is an article about privateers licensed by the Rebel Alliance to rob from Imperial shipping lanes and Imperial-allied companies. It was reprinted in The Best of the Star Wars Adventure Journal, Issues 1-4 in April 1996.
