The ship played a major role in a number of conflicts during the later stages of the Yuuzhan Vong War. The ship served as the flagship of General Garm Bel Iblis during most of its service in the war. Completed at Coruscant in 27 ABY, the Elegos A'Kla was one of a group of Star Destroyers named after important New Republic leaders. It was also, along with its sister ship Mon Mothma, one of the first Imperial-class Star Destroyers to include gravity well projectors.
