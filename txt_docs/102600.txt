Ehartt Brihnt
1580

Ehartt Brihnt was a wealthy male Rodian hunter. Moving to the Wookiee planet Kashyyyk, Brihnt joined with three other hunters to establish big-game hunting and safari expeditions in the Etyyy or Rodian Hunting Grounds there. Brihnt allied himself with Sordaan Xris, the wealthiest Rodian on Kashyyyk, which made him unpopular with many other safari hunters.
