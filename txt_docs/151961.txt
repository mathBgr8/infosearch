Kanlee Fentetta
1367

Kanlee Fentetta was a prominent citizen of Naboo. Sometime in 0 ABY or 1 ABY his son died in what seemed to be a tragic aquaspeeder accident on Lake Paonga. However, a couple of months later, Fentetta learned that the reason for his son's death had been a botched kidnapping attempt by a group of pirates. He also found out that the murderers were planning to abduct the child of another wealthy family and was determined to stop them. So he offered a handsome reward for a successful assault on the pirate's lair. Fentetta is mentioned in the mission "Avenge murdered Naboo son," which is available at Mission Terminals on the planet Naboo in Star Wars Galaxies.
