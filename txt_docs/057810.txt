Flalios fought the Trogodile Swyy'm-Ee when the Dathomirian Asajj Ventress entered the arena and, in a short time, defeated most of the remaining gladiators. The survivors united for a single attack against Ventress, but she used the the Force to crush stone debris from the ceiling onto their heads. Flalios often fought with chains, whipping them around to deliver stunning blows to the heads of his opponents. Flalios first appeared as an unnamed background character in the sixth chapter of Star Wars: Clone Wars, an animated television micro-series produced by Cartoon Network StudiosStar Wars: Clone Wars Volume One and originally aired on November 14, 2003. The character was later given a name in Ultimate Sticker Book: Star Wars: Clone Wars, a sticker book written by Simon Beecroft and released in 2004. However, in later sources, neither Flalios's backstory nor even origin was revealed. The name of this gladiator was phonetically similar to flail, a weapon consisting of a striking head attached to a handle by a flexible rope, strap, or chain.
