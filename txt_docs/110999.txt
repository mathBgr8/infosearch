Rori Spice Mining Collective
2473

|founder= |leader= |subgroup= |headquarters=Rori |locations= |formed= |founded= |fragmented= |reorganized= |dissolved= |restored= |affiliation= }} The Rori Spice Mining Collective, also known simply as the Spice Collective, was a conglomeration of law-abiding miners who were the only group legally allowed to mine spice on Naboo's moon of Rori during the Galactic Civil War. The miners mostly traded their goods with pharmaceutical companies. By the time of the Battle of Yavin, they would often engage in turf wars with their less than honorable Kobola rivals. They were also in conflict with the extremist Rorgungans as well as with the Narmle Militia.
