Invisible Market
2784

thumb|250px|Smugglers and Invisible Marketeers at work The Invisible Market was the criminal trade that went beyond the black market. Little was known about it, except that Lannik Racto sold numerous assassin droids through the market. Slaves were also sold on the Invisible Market. The Saurin droid trader Hrchek Kal Fas scoured the Invisible Market for the best droid prices.
