Yumfla
878

Yumfla was the capital city of Susevfi. It was a large seaport and contained a fortress built by an Imperial Governor and later used by Leonia Tavira. It also was close to a secret Jensaarai refuge. *I, Jedi *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Capital cities
