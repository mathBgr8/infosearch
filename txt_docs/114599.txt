Xeran
899

Xeran was a Xamster who helped Boba Fett get into Wat Tambor's fortress. Xeran gave Boba an orb of spores from the malvil-tree Malubi that would weaken the Mazariyan around Wat Tambor's fortress. *Boba Fett: A New Threat *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Males Category:Xamsters
