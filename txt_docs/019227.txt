Mebla Dule
1352

Mebla Dule was a Human female. She was an enthusiastic player of Pazaak who encountered Meetra Surik in the cantina on Citadel Station over Telos IV. Mebla Dule is the only character in KotOR II whose dialog you cannot skip through. This may be an inside joke, however, as even after you beat her three times, and she claims that she will quit the game, you can still play her by saying "I'd like to ask you some questions..." She will continue to play. If the player approaches her with B-4D4-GE3 she refuses to play Pazaak saying "Never play pazaak with droids". *Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II: The Sith Lords Category:Females Category:Humans Category:Telosians
