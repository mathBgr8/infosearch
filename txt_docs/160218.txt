Blue Ruin
1955

Blue Ruin was a Nebulon-B2 frigate belonging to the Galactic Empire. It responded to a distress call from an Imperial troop convoy that was under attack by Rebel B-wing starfighters, RZ-1 A-wing interceptors, T-65 X-wing starfighters and BTL Y-wing starfighters in the Mylok system.
