Fantain Mountains
2035

The Fantain Mountains were a location on the sandy planet Zaloriis. Due to the presence of boulders that blocked the planet's high winds, the mountains were home to small forms of animal life, along with Zalorian rock-lions. Prior to the Battle of Hoth in 3 ABY,The New Essential Chronology places the Battle of Hoth in 3 ABY. the reigning government known as the Galactic Empire established a secret base called Camp Culroon near the Fantain Mountains as part of the development of the All Terrain Armored Transport walker&mdash;also known as the AT-AT&mdash;which was led by Colonel Maximilian Veers.
