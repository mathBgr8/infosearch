Battle of Commenor (Clone Wars)
1961

A battle was fought at Commenor between the CIS and the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. The Republic defeated the Separatists and the conflict was one of the key victories that started the Outer Rim Sieges. *The New Essential Chronology *The Essential Atlas Category:Battles of Commenor Category:Battles of the Clone Wars
