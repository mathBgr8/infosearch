}} The Security Tower was a tower in Cloud City on Bespin used as a detention center by the Bespin Wing Guard. When Darth Vader took control of Cloud City following the Battle of Hoth, Baron Administrator Lando Calrissian, Lobot and a squad of Wing Guards set an ambush for the stormtroopers escorting Leia Organa and Chewbacca.
