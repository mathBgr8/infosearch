Multi-detonator
1268

A multi-detonator was a high-powered explosive used for maximum damage potential. Its strength could rip a starship engine into fragments. It was also less susceptible to damper shield effects than a regular thermal detonator. It featured a concussion beam emitter, satellite spin piercer, and a cycle wave ripper surrounding a magno-thermitic charger and fragmentation housing.Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary Boba Fett was known to have used some antiquated multi-detonators. *Star Wars: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary Category:Explosive charges
