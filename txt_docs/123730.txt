**Intelligence Operations Command |masters= |apprentices= }} Commander Daclo Koat-xi was an officer assigned to the New Republic's Intelligence Operations Command. Koat-xi commanded one of the three field command teams reporting to Brigadier Colin Darkmere following Operation Shadow Hand.
