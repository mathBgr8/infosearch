Artificial gravity
3017

Artificial gravity was a mean of generating gravity within a vessel traveling in space such that those traveling aboard it could remain upright. The Moveable Feast was an example of a vessel equipped with artificial gravity. When the ship was damaged while Lina and Milo Graf were traveling aboard it as prisoners, the artificial gravity began to weaken, causing Milo to nearly float away when he stumbled after the ship itself juddered.
