|contributors= |publisher=Topps Publishing |release date=April 1997 |subscribers= |pages=64 |isbn= |issn=1081-1362 |genre= |issue=11 |series=Star Wars Galaxy Magazine }} Star Wars Galaxy Magazine 11 is the eleventh issue of Star Wars Galaxy Magazine. It was published by Topps in April 1997. The issue's theme focused on Darth Vader. The Deluxe Collectors' Edition of issue 11, exclusive to subscribers and comic stores, came polybagged with two promotional trading cards&mdash;a 1997 Topps Star Wars Special Edition hologram card and another highlighting issue 11's cover art by Walter Simonson and Richard Ory&mdash;and a 1997 Topps Star Wars Vehicles promotional postcard.
