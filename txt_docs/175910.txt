Matt Bouchard
1073

Matt Bouchard, an ILM employee, worked as a digital effects artist for The Phantom Menace. Other notable works include being a visual effects technical assistant for Flubber, and digital artist for Shark Tale, War of the Worlds, and Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest.
