Nexus (Carrack-class)
1949

Nexus was a Carrack-class light cruiser belonging to the Empire. Along with its sister ship Xerxes and the Victory-class Star Destroyer Vagrant, it attempted to stop the Defiance Task Force from rendezvousing with the Alliance Fleet at Sullust, but were held back by A-wings, two of which were piloted by Olin Garn and Ace Azzameen. *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance *Star Wars: X-Wing Alliance: Prima's Official Strategy Guide *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Carrack-class light cruisers Category:Starships of the Galactic Empire
