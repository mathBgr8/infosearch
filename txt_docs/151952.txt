Rough and Tundra
6150

|series= |preceded by= |followed by= }} "Rough and Tundra" was a web enhancement published by Wizards of the Coast on March 25, 2004 for the Star Wars Roleplaying Game. Two legendary pirates&mdash;Clabburn the Younger and Salmakk&mdash;are back plying their trade on the Greater Javin during the height of the Galactic Civil War. Interested parties hire a group of spacers to locate the pirates. It is eventually revealed that the pirates were inspired by FiggEx in order to come in and increase their holdings. Salmakk first appeared in the comic Star Wars 3-D 2.
