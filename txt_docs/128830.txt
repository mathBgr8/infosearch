Tanea
1212

Tanea was the Senior Surveyor onboard the Astrogator-class probe ship Astrolabe employed with the Astrographic Survey Institute in 16 ABY. She had nearly three thousand overflights of star systems during her career yet had only set foot on half a dozen worlds.
