Rytal Prime
832

Rytal Prime was the capital city of the moon Dayark. It held the moon's main spaceport. Rytal Prime was also the location of the Kathol Republic senatorial chambers. In 8 ABY, the New Republic corvette FarStar was ordered to land at the spaceport after surrendering to the Kathol Republic Navy. *Vision of the Future *Vision of the Future abridged audiobook *The Kathol Outback Category:Cities Category:Dayark locations
