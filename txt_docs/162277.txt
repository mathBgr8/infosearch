Unidentified Separatist script
1058

A script was used by the Confederacy of Independent Systems on their base on Mustafar. It was used in a warning during the Duel on Mustafar between Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi when they accidentally turned the shields off, allowing the lava to damage the base.
