He was killed on Dantooine by the amnesiac Jedi Padawan Revan at Jon's request. Following the end of the war, he led a group of Mandalorian and Duros bandits (including Reeza and Jarg) to Dantooine, where they terrorized and extorted the local settlers. However, his group of bandits was too small to pose a threat to the Jedi Enclave or any other well-organized defense. Future Mandalore Canderous Ordo was disgusted by him, his followers, and other Mandalorians like him, saying that they were taking scraps when they should be taking worlds. It was rumored he received support from the Sith; this would make sense as he killed Jedi and made the locals distrustful of their supposed protectors. As a Jedi hunter, Sherruk enjoyed eliminating Jedi and taking their lightsabers as trophies. He was known to have killed at least two Jedi; one with a red lightsaber and one with a blue lightsaber. In 3956 BBY, Sherruk and his companions were killed by a reformed Revan, though the former Sith Lord did not find it easy, as Sherruk proved to be a very skilled fighter. Sherruk was described as a "giant man who wielded a lightsaber" by Jon. Sherruk wore red Mandalorian Neo Crusader Armor. Personality wise, Sherruk was a bit sadistic and took great pleasure in killing Jedi and collecting their lightsabers as trophies. He was also confident in his combat abilities and believed that he could kill Revan and take his lightsaber. His name is spelled as both Sherruk and Sheruk in the game. Sherruk is found in the southern end of the Grove after all other Mandalorians and Duros warriors on Dantooine are eliminated, excluding the ones accompanying him.
