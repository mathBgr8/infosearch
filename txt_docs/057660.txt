Brunok
1642

Brunok was a construction worker who was part of a crew on the Imperial superweapon known as the Tarkin around 3 ABY. While on the planet Hockaleg, he and another crew member were subdued by the Rebels Leia Organa, Chewbacca, and Luke Skywalker.
