Vurruk was a fabric used by the Yuuzhan Vong. Made of living material, they were used to make vurruk carpets, which were unusually comfortable. *The New Jedi Order: Force Heretic I: Remnant *The New Jedi Order: The Unifying Force Category:Yuuzhan Vong technology
