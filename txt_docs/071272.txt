Skyhook (Star Destroyer)
2634

Skyhook was an Imperial II-class Star Destroyer under the command of General Han Solo during the hunt for Warlord Zsinj as part of Group Two. It participated in the Battle of Vahaba, defending Admiral Teren Rogriss's Stellar Web along with Solo's other Imperial II-class Star Destroyer, Crynyd, against Warlord Zsinj's Imperial I-class Star Destroyer, Red Gauntlet, and Serpent's Smile, Zsinj's Victory-class Star Destroyer. The attack failed due to mass fire and superior tactics on behalf of the temporarily allied New Republic and Galactic Empire, and Serpent's Smile was destroyed. Red Gauntlet surrendered shortly after, to the combined force of Solo's Star Destroyers, as well as Stellar Web.
