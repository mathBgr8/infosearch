Starshine special
926

"Starshine special" was a popular additive to alcoholic drinks in the galaxy during the last decades of the Galactic Republic. For the right wink, a patron in the Outlander Club on Coruscant could have it added to their cocktail. It may be related to or have similar strength as the Starshine Surprise. *Star Wars: Attack of the Clones: The Visual Dictionary *Star Wars: The Complete Visual Dictionary Category:Food additives
