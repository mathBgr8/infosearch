Airflow car
1318

An airflow car was a small vehicle which traveled through specialized, tubular transportation systems on a cushion of air. These vehicles relied on changing air pressure inside the travel tube to maintain a specific velocity, allowing for rapid transit of beings and cargo. The cars were used on various spacestations.
