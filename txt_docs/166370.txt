Kragg's Fury
1857

Kragg's Fury was the flagship of the Dread Buccaneer Hez Kragg. Roughly the size of a Victory-class Star Destroyer, Kragg's Fury was hidden in the System VV-99-7JE-2N71 shortly before Kragg's death. When a failed attempt led by Jonas Durns to secure the rumored Nijune Treasure Fleet's riches from Kragg's Fury triggered its automated defenses, the ship projected itself into the nearest star. The Kragg's Fury was armed with power turrets, blaster cannons and missile tubes, and was equipped with grappler claws and tractor beams.
