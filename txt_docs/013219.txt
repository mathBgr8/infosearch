Widek Bypass
1284

The Widek Bypass was a hyperspace route located in the Farlax sector. The route was in fact a continuation of Metellos Trade Route from its terminus at Orooturoo. The Bypass was named because the route bypassed the Koornacht Cluster, passing through Wehttam, Thobek, and Galantos, before terminating at Widek. *Coruscant and the Core Worlds Category:Core Worlds hyperlanes
