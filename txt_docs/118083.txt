Drama Korr's space yacht
1337

Drama Korr's space yacht was a sizable silver-and-gray luxury vessel owned by Corellian merchant Drama Korr. Korr was killed aboard the yacht in the Karthakk system by Durge, who later engaged Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker in combat aboard the ship. *Obsession 2 Category:Yachts
