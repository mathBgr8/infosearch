thumb|right|250px|Ksar Hadada Ksar Hadada is a ksar in southeastern Tunisia. Along with two other ksar's around Tataouine, Ksar Hadada was filmed for Mos Espa's Slave Quarters in Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. The Arabic spelling is: قصر حدادة‎. Sometimes it is spelled "Ksar Hedada". * by Mark Dermul * by Gus Lopez and Pamela Gren * by Brady Walters Category:Filming locations
