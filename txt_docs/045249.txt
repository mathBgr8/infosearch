SHADIS Magazine 27
733

SHADIS Magazine 27 was a roleplaying magazine from July/August of 1996. It contained many roleplaying articles, including an adventure for the West End Games Star Wars D6 system. The article was called Corellian Smuggler, and was written by Dan Verssen. Category:Shadis
