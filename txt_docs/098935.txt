Floater-935
2483

The Ubrikkian Floater-935 was an offshoot of the Ubrikkian Desert Sail-20 Skiff. The Floater-935 was a single-occupant vehicle ideal for high altitude. It didn't have any weapons and it was sometimes used in large industrial centers. The Ubrikkian Desert Sail-20 Skiff is a vehicle once produced by Kenner as a Mini-Rig.
