The Palace
923

The Palace in the Historical District of Estalle Island on Procopia was the center of power in the Tapani sector during the Dynastic Era, thousands of years before the Battle of Yavin. During the Galactic Civil War the glittering palace was a museum that presented a somewhat biased version of Tapani history. *Lords of the Expanse Palace Palace Palace
