Wynth
2170

Wynth was an astronomical object located in the Wynth system, a part of the Wyloff sector in the Northern Dependencies portion of the Colonies. Shortly after the Battle of Yavin, the Rebel Alliance operative and Imperial defector Colonel Vin Northal was stationed at Wynth. Wynth was created by David R. Tulo, Jr. and was introduced in Cracken's Rebel Operatives, a 1994 West End Games sourcebook published for use with Star Wars: The Roleplaying Game.
