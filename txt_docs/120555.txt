Vleen Argoe
1476

Vleen Argoe managed the Shvash Gas Cooperative located at Gelgelar Free Port on the planet Gelgelar. She purchased Shvash gas from harvesters at 50 credits per ton and sold to traders for 75 to 120 credits per ton (plus 25 credit deposit per pressurized container). Vleen was a terrible businesswomen and barely managed to earn a living from Shvash gas trading. She had an obsession with the fire that the Shvash gas produced and would often sit in her office all day setting light to small objects and smoking hand-rolled cigarras. Vleen carried a small mini-flamer with her to light her cigarras and would often be distracted by the fire that it produced. *Platt's Starport Guide *Platt's Smugglers Guide Category:Businesspeople Category:Females Category:Humans
