Gabo Tychee
1588

Gabo Tychee was a male Rodian criminal who was employed by Jabba Desilijic Tiure and the Hutt crime syndicate during the waning years of the Galactic Republic. After committing a terrible crime against the Hutts, his bounty was 4,000 credits alive and 2,000 dead. In 32 BBY, he was later found and captured by Jango Fett on Outland Transit Station.
