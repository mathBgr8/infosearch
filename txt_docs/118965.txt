Nerfscourge
497

Nerfscourge was a type of flower that contained pollen which, if overused, was capable of causing nerve damage to most species. Lumiya used it as a trap in her apartment on Coruscant. *Legacy of the Force: Tempest *The Complete Star Wars Encyclopedia Category:Flowers
