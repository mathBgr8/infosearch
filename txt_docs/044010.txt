Treskov 115-W
1332

Treskov 115-W was a main sequence star that was located on the edge of the Deep Core within the Treskov system which included Ebaq 9. For a long time an Imperial supply depot, in 28 ABY it served as the location for a major New Republic ambush of a Yuuzhan Vong fleet. *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way *The New Jedi Order: Destiny's Way abridged audiobook Category:Main-sequence stars Category:Stars in the Deep Core
