Nitrocellulose Incendiary Device 0033X
1577

The Nitrocellulose Incendiary Device 0033X, more commonly known as the 0033X grenade, was a combat explosive produced by Greff-Timms Industrial. The 0033X was the standard-issue grenade of the Old Republic's rocket jumpers. It was the size of a Human fist and could be set to either explode instantaneously or on a ten-second delay.
