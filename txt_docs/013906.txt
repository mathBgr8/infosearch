Its Library of Xer was built 26,000 BBY. Not much was known of the planet, but many historians had expressed interest in gaining access to the Imperial-controlled library. The Pentastar Alignment and the Commerce Master Commissioner of the Velcar Free Commerce Zone had strictly prohibited all entry into the system and landings on the planet.
